आद् रोदसी वितरं विश्कभायात संविव्याणश्चिद्भियसे मृगं कः |जिगर्तिम इन्द्रो अपजर्गुराणः प्रति श्वसन्तम अव दानवं हन् ||
ād rodasī vitaraṁ vi ṣkabhāyat saṁvivyānaś cid bhiyase mṛgaṁ kaḥ | jigartim indro apajargurāṇaḥ prati śvasantam ava dānavaṁ han ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ād: from, out of
    • rodasī: the two worlds (heaven and earth)
    • vitaraṁ: spread, extend
    • vi: apart, asunder
    • ṣkabhāyat: by the power of
    • saṁvivyānaś: having gathered together
    • cid: consciousness, awareness
    • bhiyase: in fear, in awe
    • mṛgaṁ: the deer, symbolizing gentleness or vulnerability
    • kaḥ: who
    • jigartim: the one who conquers
    • indro: Indra, the king of gods
    • apajargurāṇaḥ: the one who is powerful and mighty
    • prati: towards
    • śvasantam: breathing, living
    • ava: down, away
    • dānavaṁ: the demon, representing negativity or obstacles
    • han: to kill, to overcome
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the divine power that spreads across the two worlds, heaven and earth.
    • It emphasizes the importance of consciousness and awareness in overcoming fear and vulnerability.
    • The deer symbolizes gentleness, while Indra represents strength and the ability to conquer challenges.
    • The mantra suggests that through awareness and divine strength, one can overcome obstacles and negativity in life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In moments of fear or anxiety, individuals can draw strength from their awareness and understanding of their situation, much like the mantra suggests.
    • In a workplace setting, when faced with challenges, a team can embody the spirit of Indra by collaborating and supporting each other to overcome obstacles.
    • In personal relationships, being aware of one’s vulnerabilities (like the deer) can lead to deeper connections and the ability to confront issues together.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of awareness and divine strength in overcoming life's challenges.
    • It teaches us that by embracing our vulnerabilities and drawing on our inner strength, we can conquer negativity and obstacles.
    • In real life, this can be applied in various situations, from personal struggles to collective challenges, reminding us of the power of unity and consciousness.
