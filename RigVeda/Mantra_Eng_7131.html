मन्त्र: प्रोऽस्मा उपस्तुतिम् भरतā यज जुजोषति | उक्थैर इन्द्रस्य माहिनं वयो वर्धन्ति सोमिनो भद्रā इन्द्रस्य रातयः ||
pro asmā upastutim bharatā yaj jujoṣati | ukthair indrasya māhinaṁ vayo vardhanti somino bhadrā indrasya rātayaḥ ||
  • Pro asmā upastutim bharatā yaj jujoṣati:
    • Pro asmā: This phrase can be interpreted as "for us" or "for our benefit." It signifies a call for divine assistance and blessings.
    • Upastutim: This means "praise" or "worship." It indicates the act of honoring or venerating the divine.
    • Bharatā: Refers to the people of Bharat (India) or the descendants of Bharata, symbolizing unity and collective identity.
    • Yaj jujoṣati: This translates to "may we engage in sacrifice" or "may we perform our duties." It emphasizes the importance of action and devotion in life.
  • Ukthair indrasya māhinaṁ:
    • Ukthair: This word means "with the help of" or "through the support of." It suggests reliance on divine power.
    • Indrasya: Refers to Indra, the king of gods in Vedic tradition, symbolizing strength, power, and leadership.
    • Māhinaṁ: This can be interpreted as "greatness" or "majesty," indicating the importance of divine qualities.
  • Vayo vardhanti somino:
    • Vayo: This means "age" or "life," referring to vitality and longevity.
    • Vardhanti: Translates to "they increase" or "they grow," indicating the flourishing of life and prosperity.
    • Somino: Refers to "of the soma," which is a sacred drink in Vedic rituals, symbolizing immortality and divine nourishment.
  • Bhadrā indrasya rātayaḥ:
    • Bhadrā: This means "auspicious" or "beneficial," indicating positive outcomes and blessings.
    • Indrasya: Again refers to Indra, emphasizing the connection to divine power.
    • Rātayaḥ: This translates to "the chariots" or "the vehicles," symbolizing the means of achieving goals and aspirations.

Conclusion:

The mantra emphasizes the importance of collective worship and the pursuit of divine blessings for the well-being of society. It highlights the need for action, devotion, and reliance on divine power to achieve greatness and prosperity. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as community gatherings, rituals, and ceremonies where people come together to seek blessings for health, success, and harmony. It serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of individuals and the divine, encouraging a sense of unity and purpose in our actions.
