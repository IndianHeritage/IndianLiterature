या ऋतेन सूर्यं आरोहयन् दिव्य अप्रथयन पृथिवीं मातरं वि |सुप्रजास्त्वम अंगिरसो वो अस्तु प्रति गृभ्णीत मानवं सुमेधसः ||
ya ṛtena sūryam ārohayan divy aprathayan pṛthivīm mātaraṁ vi | suprajāstvam aṅgiraso vo astu prati gṛbhṇīta mānavaṁ sumedhasaḥ ||
  • ya ṛtena: This phrase translates to "by truth" or "through righteousness." It emphasizes the importance of living a life aligned with truth and moral values.
  • sūryam ārohayan: This means "who elevates the sun." The sun is often seen as a symbol of knowledge, enlightenment, and vitality. Elevating the sun signifies the pursuit of knowledge and wisdom.
  • divy aprathayan: This translates to "divine and unbroken." It suggests a connection to the divine and the idea of maintaining continuity in spiritual practices and beliefs.
  • pṛthivīm mātaraṁ vi: This means "the mother Earth." It acknowledges the nurturing aspect of nature and the importance of respecting and caring for our environment.
  • suprajāstvam: This translates to "prosperity" or "good progeny." It signifies the desire for a fruitful and prosperous life, not just for oneself but for future generations.
  • aṅgiraso vo astu: This means "may it be for you, O Angirases." Angirases refers to a group of sages or seers in Vedic literature, symbolizing wisdom and guidance.
  • prati gṛbhṇīta: This translates to "may it be received." It indicates an openness to receiving blessings and wisdom from the divine.
  • mānavaṁ sumedhasaḥ: This means "the wise human." It emphasizes the importance of wisdom and intelligence in navigating life’s challenges.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda encapsulates the essence of living a life rooted in truth, wisdom, and respect for nature. It encourages individuals to seek knowledge and prosperity while being mindful of their responsibilities towards the environment and future generations. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as environmental conservation efforts, educational pursuits, and personal development. By embodying the principles of truth and wisdom, individuals can contribute positively to society and foster a harmonious relationship with the world around them.
