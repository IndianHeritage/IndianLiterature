अस्मांं इह वृणीष्व सख्याय स्वस्तये |महो राये दिवित्मते ||
asmām̐ ihā vṛṇīṣva sakhyāya svastaye | maho rāye divitmate ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • asmām̐: "from us" or "to us"
    • ihā: "here" or "in this place"
    • vṛṇīṣva: "choose" or "select"
    • sakhyāya: "for friendship" or "for companionship"
    • svastaye: "for well-being" or "for prosperity"
    • maho: "great" or "mighty"
    • rāye: "wealth" or "prosperity"
    • divitmate: "to the one who is wise" or "to the enlightened one"
  • Overall Meaning: This mantra can be interpreted as a prayer or invocation asking for the selection of friendship and well-being from the divine, particularly for the purpose of achieving great prosperity and wisdom.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, this mantra can be applied when forming partnerships or collaborations. Businesses often seek alliances that promote mutual growth and well-being, reflecting the essence of "sakhyāya svastaye."
    • In personal relationships, individuals may use this mantra to seek friendships that contribute positively to their lives, emphasizing the importance of surrounding oneself with supportive and wise companions.
    • In community gatherings or social events, invoking this mantra can serve as a reminder to foster connections that lead to collective prosperity and well-being.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its emphasis on the importance of choosing relationships and connections that enhance well-being and prosperity. In real life, it encourages individuals and communities to seek out friendships and partnerships that are mutually beneficial, wise, and supportive. By embodying the principles of this mantra, one can cultivate a life filled with meaningful connections and abundant prosperity.
