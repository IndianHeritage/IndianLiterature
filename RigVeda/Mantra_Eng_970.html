देवानाम् भद्राऽसुमतिर् ऋजुयतां देवानां रातिः अभि नो निवर्तताम् |देवानां सख्यम् उप सेदिमाऽवयं देवाः न आयुः प्र तिरन्तु जीवसे ||
devānām bhadrā sumatir ṛjūyatāṁ devānāṁ rātir abhi no ni vartatām | devānāṁ sakhyam upa sedimā vayaṁ devā na āyuḥ pra tirantu jīvase ||
  • devānām bhadrā sumatir ṛjūyatāṁ: This phrase can be translated as "May the divine beings grant us auspicious and righteous understanding." Here, "devānām" refers to the divine beings or gods, "bhadrā" means auspicious or beneficial, "sumatir" signifies good understanding or wisdom, and "ṛjūyatāṁ" implies righteousness or straightness in thought and action.
  • devānāṁ rātir abhi no ni vartatām: This translates to "May the divine beings' delight not turn away from us." "Rātir" refers to delight or joy, and "ni vartatām" means to not turn away or to remain with us. This line emphasizes the importance of maintaining a connection with divine joy and blessings.
  • devānāṁ sakhyam upa sedimā vayaṁ: This means "May we attain the friendship of the divine beings." "Sakhyam" refers to friendship, "upa" means towards or near, and "sedimā" implies the act of attaining or achieving. This line highlights the desire for a close relationship with the divine.
  • devā na āyuḥ pra tirantu jīvase: This translates to "May the divine beings grant us long life and vitality." "Āyuḥ" means life or longevity, "pra" signifies granting or bestowing, and "tirantu" means to cross over or to achieve. This line expresses a wish for a long and healthy life, supported by divine grace.

Conclusion:

The mantra encapsulates a profound desire for divine blessings in various aspects of life, including wisdom, joy, friendship, and longevity. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applicable in moments of prayer, meditation, or during significant life events such as weddings, births, or new beginnings. By reciting this mantra, individuals seek to align themselves with positive energies and divine support, fostering a sense of community and connection with the divine. It serves as a reminder of the importance of maintaining righteousness and seeking wisdom in our actions, ultimately guiding us towards a fulfilling and harmonious life.
