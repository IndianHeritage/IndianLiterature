सुगः पन्था अनृत्क्षर आदित्यासा ऋतं यते |नात्रावखादो अस्ति वः ||
sugaḥ panthā anṛkṣara ādityāsa ṛtaṁ yate | nātrāvakhādo asti vaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sugaḥ: pleasant, good, or auspicious
    • panthā: path or way
    • anṛkṣara: unchanging, eternal
    • ādityāsa: of the sun or solar
    • ṛtaṁ: truth, order, or cosmic law
    • yate: striving or effort
    • nātrāvakhādo: here there is no obstruction or hindrance
    • asti: there is
    • vaḥ: for you or to you
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of a pleasant path that is eternal and aligned with the cosmic order.
    • The phrase “nātrāvakhādo asti vaḥ” suggests that on this path, there are no obstacles or hindrances for those who strive.
    • It emphasizes the importance of effort and truth in achieving a fulfilling life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, individuals who follow a truthful and ethical path often find success without facing significant obstacles.
    • In personal relationships, striving for honesty and understanding can lead to harmonious connections, free from misunderstandings.
    • In spiritual practices, those who seek enlightenment through sincere effort often find that their journey is smooth and fulfilling.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder that by choosing a path of truth and effort, one can navigate life with ease and grace.
    • Its significance lies in the encouragement to pursue a life aligned with cosmic order, where obstacles are minimized through sincere striving.
    • In various aspects of life—be it career, relationships, or personal growth—this mantra inspires individuals to remain steadfast on their chosen path, ensuring a fulfilling journey.
