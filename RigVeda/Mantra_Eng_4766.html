आ त्वा हरयो वृषणो युजाना वृषरथासो वृषरश्मयो 'त्याः |अस्मत्रांचो वृषणो वज्रवाहो वृष्णे मदाया सुयुजो वहन्तु ||
ā tvā harayo vṛṣaṇo yujānā vṛṣarathāso vṛṣaraśmayo 'tyāḥ | asmatrāñco vṛṣaṇo vajravāho vṛṣṇe madāya suyujo vahantu ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā tvā: To you
    • harayo: The swift ones (often referring to horses or deities)
    • vṛṣaṇo: The strong or powerful
    • yujānā: United or joined
    • vṛṣarathāso: Those who are like chariots (symbolizing strength and speed)
    • vṛṣaraśmayo: The rays of strength
    • 'tyāḥ: May they come
    • asmatrāñco: Our protection
    • vajravāho: The one who carries the thunderbolt (symbolizing power)
    • vṛṣṇe: To Vishnu or the strong
    • madāya: For our joy or happiness
    • suyujo: Well united or harmoniously joined
    • vahantu: May they carry or bring
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the powerful forces represented by the swift and strong, often symbolized by horses and chariots, to come together for the benefit of the speaker.
    • The mention of "vajravāho" signifies a plea for divine strength and protection, akin to how one might seek the support of a strong leader or protector in times of need.
    • The phrase "may they carry" suggests a desire for these forces to bring joy and harmony into one's life, reflecting the importance of unity and strength in achieving personal and communal goals.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a team setting, this mantra can be likened to the collective effort of team members working together harmoniously to achieve a common goal, much like a well-coordinated chariot team.
    • In personal life, one might call upon supportive friends and family during challenging times, seeking their strength and unity to overcome obstacles.
    • In a community context, this mantra reflects the importance of collaboration and support among community members to foster a safe and joyful environment.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its emphasis on the power of unity, strength, and divine support in achieving happiness and protection.
    • It serves as a reminder that in both personal and communal aspects of life, seeking and fostering strong relationships and support systems can lead to greater joy and success.
    • In real-life scenarios, whether in personal challenges, team dynamics, or community efforts, invoking the spirit of this mantra can inspire individuals to work together harmoniously for a common purpose.
