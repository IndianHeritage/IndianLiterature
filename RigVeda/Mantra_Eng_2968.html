मो षूणो अत्र जुहुरन्त देवाः मा पूर्वे अग्ने पितरः पदज्ञाः |पुराण्योः सद्मनोः केतुर्म अन्तर महद देवानाम् असुरत्वम् एकम् ||
mo ṣū ṇo atra juhuranta devā mā pūrve agne pitaraḥ padajñāḥ | purāṇyoḥ sadmanoḥ ketur antar mahad devānām asuratvam ekam ||
  • mo ṣū ṇo atra juhuranta devā: This phrase can be translated as "the gods who are invoked here." It emphasizes the act of calling upon divine beings for assistance or blessings. In real life, this can be likened to seeking guidance or support from mentors or leaders in various fields, such as business or education.
  • mā pūrve agne pitaraḥ padajñāḥ: This translates to "may our forefathers, who are knowledgeable, not be angry." This line highlights the importance of respecting our ancestors and the wisdom they impart. In contemporary life, this can be seen in practices such as honoring family traditions and values, which help guide our decisions and actions.
  • purāṇyoḥ sadmanoḥ ketur antar: This means "the connection between the ancient and the present." It signifies the continuity of knowledge and tradition through generations. In today's context, this can be related to how cultural heritage influences modern society, such as in art, music, and social practices.
  • mahad devānām asuratvam ekam: This translates to "the great unity of the divine beings." It suggests that despite the diversity of deities, there is an underlying oneness. In real life, this can be reflected in the idea of unity in diversity, where different cultures and beliefs coexist harmoniously, promoting peace and understanding among people.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of invoking divine support, respecting ancestral wisdom, recognizing the continuity of traditions, and acknowledging the unity among diverse beliefs. In real life, these principles can guide individuals in their personal and professional lives, fostering a sense of community, respect for heritage, and a commitment to harmony in a diverse world. By applying these teachings, one can cultivate a more meaningful and connected existence, both spiritually and socially.
