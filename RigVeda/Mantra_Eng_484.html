प्रैतु ब्रह्मणस् पतिः प्र देव्य एतु सूनृताः |अच्छा वीरं नार्यं पङ्क्तिराद्हसं देवाः यज्ञं नयन्तु नः ||
praitu brahmaṇas patiḥ pra devy etu sūnṛtā | acchā vīraṁ naryam paṅktirādhasaṁ devā yajñaṁ nayantu naḥ ||
  • Translation: "May the Lord of the Brahmanas (the learned and wise) come to us; may the divine beings, who are embodiments of truth, guide us. Let the powerful heroes, the noble ones, lead us to the sacrificial offerings."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • Praitu: May he come.
    • Brahmaṇas: Refers to the learned or wise individuals, often associated with spiritual knowledge.
    • Patiḥ: Lord or master.
    • Devy: Divine beings or deities.
    • Sūnṛtā: Embodiments of truth or righteousness.
    • Acchā: Powerful or strong.
    • Vīraṁ: Heroes or noble individuals.
    • Naryam: Noble or virtuous.
    • Paṅktirādhasaṁ: Lead us or guide us.
    • Devā: Gods or divine beings.
    • Yajñaṁ: Sacrificial offerings or rituals.
    • Nayantu: May they lead us.
    • Naḥ: To us.
  • Contextual Meaning:
    • This mantra invokes the presence of learned individuals and divine beings, emphasizing the importance of wisdom and truth in guiding one's actions.
    • It highlights the significance of community and collaboration, as it calls upon powerful heroes to lead the way in performing sacred rituals.
    • In a broader sense, it reflects the human desire for guidance and support from higher powers in achieving noble goals.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, leaders often seek the counsel of experienced mentors (the "Brahmanas") to make informed decisions that align with ethical practices (the "Sūnṛtā").
    • During community events or festivals, people come together to perform rituals (the "Yajñaṁ") that foster unity and collective purpose, invoking the support of the divine (the "Devy").
    • In personal development, individuals may look for role models (the "Vīraṁ") who embody virtues and can inspire them to pursue their goals with integrity.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of seeking wisdom and guidance from both learned individuals and divine forces in our lives.
    • It emphasizes the collective effort required to achieve noble objectives and the role of rituals in fostering community spirit.
    • In essence, it encourages individuals to align their actions with truth and righteousness, ensuring that their endeavors are meaningful and impactful.
