त्वं सद्यः अपिबो जात इन्द्र मदाय सोमं परम व्योमन् |यद ध द्यावापृथिवी आविवेशीर अथाभवः पूर्व्यः कारुधायाः ||
tvaṁ sadyo apibo jāta indra madāya somam parame vyoman | yad dha dyāvāpṛthivī āviveśīr athābhavaḥ pūrvyaḥ kārudhāyāḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • tvaṁ: You
    • sadyo: Immediately
    • apibo: Having been born
    • jāta: Born
    • indra: O Indra (the king of gods)
    • madāya: For the sake of joy or intoxication
    • somam: The soma (a sacred drink)
    • parame: In the highest
    • vyoman: In the sky
    • yad: That which
    • dha: Has placed
    • dyāvāpṛthivī: Heaven and Earth
    • āviveśīr: Have entered
    • athābhavaḥ: And thus became
    • pūrvyaḥ: Of the ancients
    • kārudhāyāḥ: Of the one who is strong
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes Indra, the king of gods, emphasizing his immediate birth and the joy associated with the sacred soma drink.
    • The phrase "parame vyoman" suggests a connection to the highest realms, indicating the divine nature of Indra and his role in the cosmos.
    • The mention of "dyāvāpṛthivī" refers to the unity of heaven and earth, symbolizing the interconnectedness of all existence.
    • The mantra reflects the ancient belief in the power of divine forces and their influence on the natural world.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern rituals, the offering of soma or similar substances can be seen as a way to connect with the divine, seeking blessings and joy.
    • The concept of interconnectedness can be applied in environmental movements, emphasizing the need to protect both the earth and the sky for future generations.
    • In personal development, the idea of invoking joy and strength can inspire individuals to seek higher purposes and connect with their inner selves.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of divine joy and the unity of existence, reminding us of the importance of connecting with higher powers and the natural world.
    • Its application in real life can be seen in spiritual practices, environmental awareness, and personal growth, encouraging individuals to seek harmony and strength in their lives.
    • Ultimately, it serves as a reminder of the ancient wisdom that still resonates today, urging us to recognize our place within the cosmos.
