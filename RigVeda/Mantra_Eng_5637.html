एष स्य मित्रावरुणा नृचक्षां उभे उद एति सूर्यो अभि ज्मन |विश्वस्य स्थातुर जगतश्च गोपा ऋजु मार्तेषु वृजिना च पश्यन् ||
eṣa sya mitrāvaruṇā nṛcakṣā ubhe ud eti sūryo abhi jman | viśvasya sthātur jagataś ca gopā ṛju marteṣu vṛjinā ca paśyan ||
  • eṣa sya mitrāvaruṇā: This phrase refers to the divine pair of Mitra and Varuna, who are often associated with the concepts of friendship and cosmic order. Mitra represents light and truth, while Varuna embodies the moral and natural laws of the universe.
  • nṛcakṣā: This term translates to "human eyes" or "the eyes of mankind," indicating the perspective of humanity in relation to the divine and the universe.
  • ubhe ud eti sūryo abhi jman: This part can be interpreted as "the sun rises above both," suggesting the omnipresence and impartiality of the sun, which symbolizes knowledge, enlightenment, and the dispelling of darkness.
  • viśvasya sthātur jagataś ca gopā: This phrase means "the protector of the universe and all beings," emphasizing the role of divine forces in maintaining order and safeguarding life.
  • ṛju marteṣu vṛjinā ca paśyan: This translates to "seeing the straight path among mortals and the obstacles," highlighting the importance of discernment and guidance in navigating life's challenges.

  • Significance: This mantra encapsulates the essence of divine guidance and the interplay between cosmic order and human experience. It emphasizes the need for clarity, truth, and protection in our lives.
  • Real-life Application: In daily life, this mantra can inspire individuals to seek truth and clarity in their actions and decisions. For example, in a professional setting, one might invoke the principles of Mitra and Varuna to foster teamwork and ethical practices, ensuring that all actions align with a greater good.
  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of all beings and the divine forces that guide us. By acknowledging these principles, individuals can strive for a life of integrity, harmony, and purpose, ultimately contributing to the well-being of the community and the world.
