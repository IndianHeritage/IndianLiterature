आ त्वा शुक्रा अचुच्यवुः सुतासा इन्द्र गिरवणः |पिबा त्व अस्यांधस इन्द्र विश्वासु ते हितम् ||
ā tvā śukrā acucyavuḥ sutāsa indra girvaṇaḥ | pibā tv a1syāndhasa indra viśvāsu te hitam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā tvā: To you
    • śukrā: Pure, bright, or auspicious
    • acucyavuḥ: Who has been invoked or called upon
    • sutāsa: Sons or offspring
    • indra: Indra, the king of gods and god of rain and thunderstorms
    • girvaṇaḥ: Of the singers or poets
    • pibā: Drink
    • tv: Your
    • asyāndhasa: Of this offering or sacrifice
    • indra: Indra (repeated for emphasis)
    • viśvāsu: In all or everywhere
    • te: For you
    • hitam: Beneficial or good
  • Overall Meaning: This mantra is a prayer to Indra, invoking his blessings and asking him to partake in the offerings made. It emphasizes the purity and auspiciousness of the offerings and seeks Indra's favor for the well-being of all.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a traditional Hindu ceremony, offerings are made to deities, similar to how this mantra calls upon Indra to accept the offerings. For instance, during a wedding, the couple may invoke blessings for a prosperous life together.
    • In agricultural communities, prayers to Indra are common, especially during the monsoon season, to ensure good rainfall for crops. This reflects the significance of Indra as the god of rain.
  • Significance and Application: The mantra highlights the importance of invoking divine blessings for success and well-being. It teaches us to recognize the interconnectedness of our actions and the divine, encouraging us to seek purity in our intentions and offerings. In modern life, this can be applied in various contexts, such as:
    • In personal development, one can reflect on the purity of their intentions and actions, striving for a life that benefits not just oneself but also the community.
    • In leadership, the mantra serves as a reminder for leaders to act with integrity and to seek the welfare of those they lead, much like how Indra is invoked for the benefit of all.
