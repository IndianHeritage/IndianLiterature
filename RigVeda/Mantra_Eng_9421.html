आयं स्तुतो राजा वन्दि वेधा अपश्च विप्रस्तरति स्वसेतुः |स कक्षीवंतं रेजयत सो अग्निं नेमिं न चक्रं अर्वतो रघुद्रु ||
ayaṁ stuto rājā vandi vedhā apaś ca vipras tarati svasetuḥ | sa kakṣīvantaṁ rejayat so agniṁ nemiṁ na cakram arvato raghudru ||
  • ayaṁ stuto rājā vandi vedhā: This phrase can be translated to "This praised king, the one who is worshipped by the wise." Here, the term "rājā" refers to a king or ruler, symbolizing authority and leadership. In real life, this can be seen in the context of respected leaders in various fields, such as politics, business, or community service, who are acknowledged for their wisdom and guidance.
  • apaś ca vipras tarati svasetuḥ: This translates to "The wise one, who is aware, crosses over the obstacles." The word "vipra" refers to a learned or wise person. This can be related to individuals who, through their knowledge and understanding, navigate challenges in life, such as educators or mentors who help others overcome difficulties.
  • sa kakṣīvantaṁ rejayat: This means "He who shines with brilliance." The term "kakṣīvanta" implies someone who is radiant or influential. In a modern context, this could refer to influential figures in society, such as activists or innovators, who inspire others through their actions and ideas.
  • so agniṁ nemiṁ na cakram arvato raghudru: This can be interpreted as "He who does not turn away from the path of righteousness." "Agni" symbolizes fire, often associated with purity and transformation, while "nemi" refers to a wheel or cycle, indicating the importance of following a righteous path. This can be seen in individuals who uphold ethical standards and integrity in their personal and professional lives, serving as role models for others.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the qualities of wise leadership, the ability to overcome challenges, the brilliance of influential figures, and the importance of adhering to a righteous path. In real life, these principles can be applied in various scenarios, such as in leadership roles, educational settings, and personal development. By embodying these qualities, individuals can inspire others and contribute positively to society, fostering a culture of wisdom, integrity, and resilience.
