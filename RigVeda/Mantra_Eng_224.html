अतो देवाः अवन्तु नो यतो विष्णुर विचक्रमे |पृथिव्याः सप्त धामभिः ||
ato devā avantu no yato viṣṇur vicakrame | pṛthivyāḥ sapta dhāmabhiḥ ||
  • Translation: "May the gods protect us from where Vishnu strides, with the seven realms of the earth."

  • Word Breakdown:
    • Ato: From here, hence, or therefore.
    • Devā: Gods or divine beings.
    • Avantu: May protect or may guard.
    • No: Us or we.
    • Yato: From where or from which.
    • Viṣṇur: Vishnu, the preserver and protector in Hindu mythology.
    • Vicakrame: Strides or moves with purpose.
    • Pṛthivyāḥ: Of the earth.
    • Sapta: Seven.
    • Dhāmabhiḥ: Realms or abodes.

  • Meaning and Explanation:
    • This mantra invokes the protection of the divine, specifically calling upon the gods to safeguard us.
    • It emphasizes the importance of divine presence in our lives, particularly in the context of Vishnu, who represents preservation and protection.
    • The mention of "seven realms" signifies the various aspects of existence and the interconnectedness of life on earth.
    • In real life, this can be interpreted as a call for spiritual guidance and support in our daily endeavors, reminding us to seek protection from negative influences.

  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In times of uncertainty or danger, individuals often turn to prayer or meditation, seeking divine protection similar to the invocation in this mantra.
    • In various cultures, rituals are performed to honor deities and seek their blessings for safety and prosperity, reflecting the essence of this mantra.
    • In personal development, the idea of striving for higher realms of existence can be seen in the pursuit of knowledge, ethics, and community service, aligning with the protective aspect of the divine.

  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the divine support available to us in our lives.
    • It encourages individuals to seek protection and guidance from higher powers, fostering a sense of security and purpose.
    • In a broader context, it highlights the importance of spirituality in navigating life's challenges and the interconnectedness of all beings within the realms of existence.
