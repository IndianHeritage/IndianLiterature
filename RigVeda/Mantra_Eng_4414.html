स तत कृधिषितस तुयम् अग्ने स्पृधो बादस्व सहसा सहस्वान् | यच छस्यसे द्युभिर अक्तो वचोभिस तज जुषस्व जारितुर घोषि मनमा ||
sa tat kṛdhīṣitas tūyam agne spṛdho bādhasva sahasā sahasvān | yac chasyase dyubhir akto vacobhis taj juṣasva jaritur ghoṣi manma ||
  • sa tat kṛdhīṣitas tūyam: This phrase can be translated as "that which is desired by us." It signifies the aspirations and goals that individuals or communities strive to achieve.
  • agne: Referring to Agni, the fire god, who symbolizes energy, transformation, and the divine presence in rituals. Fire is often seen as a purifying force in many cultures.
  • spṛdho bādhasva: This means "remove obstacles." It emphasizes the need for divine assistance in overcoming challenges that hinder progress.
  • sahasā sahasvān: Translated as "with strength and power," this phrase highlights the importance of resilience and determination in the face of adversity.
  • yac chasyase dyubhir: This can be interpreted as "what you cultivate with your efforts." It suggests that success comes from hard work and dedication.
  • akto vacobhis: Meaning "with words that are not spoken," this implies the significance of intentions and thoughts that may not be verbally expressed but are nonetheless powerful.
  • taj juṣasva: This translates to "may you enjoy that." It is a call for the blessings of the divine to be bestowed upon the efforts made.
  • jaritur ghoṣi manma: This phrase can be understood as "may my mind be filled with the sound of joy." It reflects the desire for happiness and fulfillment in life.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking divine guidance and support in achieving one's goals while emphasizing the importance of hard work, resilience, and positive intentions. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various contexts such as personal development, professional endeavors, and spiritual growth. For instance, when facing challenges in a career, one might recite this mantra to invoke strength and clarity, reminding oneself to stay focused on their aspirations and to seek help in overcoming obstacles. Ultimately, it serves as a reminder that with determination and divine support, one can cultivate a fulfilling and joyful life.
