साकं जातः क्रतुनā साकम् ओजसा ववक्षिथ साकं वृद्धो वीर्यैः सासहिर मृधो विचर्षणिः | दाता राधः स्तुवते काम्यं वसु सैणं सश्चद देवो देवं सत्यं इन्द्रं सत्य इन्दुः ||
sākaṁ jātaḥ kratunā sākam ojasā vavakṣitha sākaṁ vṛddho vīryaiḥ sāsahir mṛdho vicarṣaṇiḥ | dātā rādhaḥ stuvate kāmyaṁ vasu sainaṁ saścad devo devaṁ satyam indraṁ satya induḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sākaṁ: Together, in unity
    • jātaḥ: Born, created
    • kratunā: By the power of will or intention
    • ojasā: With strength, energy
    • vavakṣitha: You have spoken, declared
    • vṛddho: The elder, wise
    • vīryaiḥ: With valor, strength
    • sāsahir: With support, assistance
    • mṛdho: From the earth, grounded
    • vicarṣaṇiḥ: Moving, wandering
    • dātā: The giver
    • rādhaḥ: Abundance, prosperity
    • stuvate: Praise, worship
    • kāmyaṁ: Desired, wished for
    • vasu: Wealth, good things
    • sainaṁ: Army, group
    • saścad: And also
    • devo: God, divine
    • devaṁ: The divine one
    • satyam: Truth
    • indraṁ: Indra, the king of gods
    • satya: Truth, reality
    • induḥ: Moon, nectar
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of unity and collaboration in achieving strength and prosperity.
    • It suggests that through collective intention and effort, individuals can create a powerful force that leads to success.
    • The reference to Indra, the king of gods, highlights the significance of divine support in human endeavors.
    • Real-life example: In a team project, when all members work together with a shared goal, they can achieve results that surpass individual efforts.
  • Conclusion:
    • The mantra signifies the power of unity and collective strength in achieving desired outcomes.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, such as teamwork in professional settings, community building, and collaborative efforts in social causes.
    • By invoking the essence of this mantra, individuals and groups can foster a spirit of cooperation and mutual support, leading to greater success and fulfillment.
