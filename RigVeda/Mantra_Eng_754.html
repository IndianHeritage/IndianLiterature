श्वसित्य अप्सु हंसो न सीदन क्रत्वा चेतिष्ठो विश्वाम उषर्भूत ||
śvasity apsu haṁso na sīdan kratvā cetiṣṭho viśām uṣarbhut ||
  • śvasity: This word can be interpreted as "breathing" or "inhaling." In a broader sense, it signifies the essence of life and vitality.
  • apsu: This term refers to "water" or "in the waters." Water is often seen as a source of nourishment and life, essential for all living beings.
  • haṁso: The word "haṁsa" translates to "swan" or "goose," which is often associated with purity and grace. In many cultures, swans symbolize beauty and tranquility.
  • na sīdan: This phrase means "do not sit" or "do not remain inactive." It encourages action and movement, suggesting that one should not be complacent.
  • kratvā: This term refers to "with effort" or "through action." It emphasizes the importance of diligence and hard work in achieving one's goals.
  • cetiṣṭho: This word can be understood as "being aware" or "being conscious." It highlights the significance of mindfulness and awareness in our actions.
  • viśām: This term means "of the community" or "of the people." It signifies the importance of collective effort and unity in achieving common goals.
  • uṣarbhut: This word can be interpreted as "having risen" or "having emerged." It suggests growth, progress, and the realization of potential.

In summary, the mantra "śvasity apsu haṁso na sīdan kratvā cetiṣṭho viśām uṣarbhut" encapsulates a profound message about the essence of life, the importance of action, awareness, and community. It encourages individuals to be active participants in their lives, to strive for growth, and to work together with others for the betterment of society.


In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts:

  • Personal Development: It serves as a reminder to stay active and engaged in personal growth, whether through education, fitness, or self-improvement.
  • Community Engagement: The emphasis on collective effort highlights the importance of community service and collaboration in addressing social issues.
  • Mindfulness Practices: The call for awareness encourages practices such as meditation and mindfulness, which can lead to a more fulfilling and conscious life.

Ultimately, this mantra signifies the interconnectedness of life, the necessity of action, and the power of community, urging individuals to rise and contribute positively to the world around them.
