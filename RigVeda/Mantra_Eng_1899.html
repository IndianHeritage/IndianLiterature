युवां चिद्धि स्माश्विनाव् अनु dyūn विरुद्रस्य प्रसरवणस्य सातौ |अगस्त्यो नरां नृषु प्रशस्तः काराधुनिव चितयत सहस्रैः ||
yuvāṁ cid dhi ṣmāśvināv anu dyūn virudrasya prasravaṇasya sātau | agastyo narāṁ nṛṣu praśastaḥ kārādhunīva citayat sahasraiḥ ||
  • yuvāṁ cid dhi ṣmāśvināv anu dyūn: This phrase refers to the youthful and vibrant nature of the Ashvins, the twin deities associated with health, medicine, and the dawn. The term "yuvāṁ" signifies youthfulness and vitality, suggesting that one should embrace the energy and enthusiasm of life.
  • virudrasya prasravaṇasya sātau: Here, "virudrasya" can be interpreted as the powerful or strong, while "prasravaṇasya" refers to the flow or abundance. This part of the mantra emphasizes the importance of strength and the flow of positive energy in life, akin to how a river nourishes the land around it.
  • agastyo narāṁ nṛṣu praśastaḥ: "Agastyo" refers to the sage Agastya, known for his wisdom and knowledge. "Narāṁ nṛṣu praśastaḥ" translates to being praised among men. This suggests that wisdom and knowledge are highly valued and should be sought after, as they lead to respect and recognition in society.
  • kārādhunīva citayat sahasraiḥ: This phrase can be interpreted as the act of creating or manifesting with great effort and dedication. "Sahasraiḥ" implies doing so with a thousandfold intensity. It encourages individuals to put in their best efforts to achieve their goals, much like an artist dedicating themselves to their craft.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda emphasizes the significance of youth, strength, wisdom, and dedicated effort in achieving success and recognition in life. In real-life scenarios, it can be applied to various aspects such as personal development, career growth, and health. For instance, embracing a youthful spirit can lead to a more vibrant and fulfilling life, while seeking knowledge and wisdom can enhance one's standing in society. Additionally, the mantra encourages individuals to work diligently towards their goals, reminding us that success often requires sustained effort and commitment. Overall, it serves as a powerful reminder of the values that contribute to a meaningful and impactful life.
