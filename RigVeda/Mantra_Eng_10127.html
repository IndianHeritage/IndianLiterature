मन्त्र: न तिग्मानि भ्राशयन भ्राश्याण्य अव स्थिरा तनुहि यातुजूनाम् | उग्राय ते सहो बलं ददामि प्रतीत्या शत्रून् विगदेषु वृश्च ||
ni tigmāni bhrāśayan bhrāśyāny ava sthirā tanuhi yātujūnām | ugrāya te saho balaṁ dadāmi pratītyā śatrūn vigadeṣu vṛśca ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ni: not
    • tigmāni: sharp or fierce
    • bhrāśayan: breaking or shattering
    • bhrāśyāny: those that are broken
    • ava: down or below
    • sthirā: stable or firm
    • tanuhi: body or form
    • yātujūnām: of the enemies or adversaries
    • ugrāya: fierce or powerful
    • te: to you
    • saho: strength or power
    • balaṁ: force or might
    • dadāmi: I give
    • pratītyā: by understanding or realization
    • śatrūn: enemies
    • vigadeṣu: in battles or conflicts
    • vṛśca: to be victorious
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the power to overcome adversities and enemies.
    • It emphasizes the importance of inner strength and stability in the face of challenges.
    • The phrase "not breaking the fierce" suggests resilience and the ability to withstand tough situations.
    • By invoking strength and power, the mantra encourages individuals to confront their fears and adversaries with confidence.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a competitive work environment, this mantra can inspire employees to remain steadfast and not be intimidated by challenges or rivalries.
    • In personal relationships, it can serve as a reminder to maintain stability and not let conflicts break one's spirit.
    • During difficult times, such as health crises or financial struggles, the mantra can motivate individuals to harness their inner strength and seek solutions rather than succumb to despair.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for resilience and empowerment.
    • It teaches that true strength comes from within and that one can face adversities with courage and determination.
    • In real-life scenarios, applying the principles of this mantra can lead to personal growth, improved relationships, and success in various endeavors.
