न सेशे यस्य राम्बतेऽन्तरā शक्त्या कप्रत् |सेद ईशे यस्य रोमा षं निषेदुषो विजृम्भते विश्वस्माद इन्द्र उत्तरः ||
na seśe yasya rambate 'ntarā sakthyā3 kapṛt | sed īśe yasya romaśaṁ niṣeduṣo vijṛmbhate viśvasmād indra uttaraḥ ||
  • Mantra: "na seśe yasya rambate 'ntarā sakthyā3 kapṛt | sed īśe yasya romaśaṁ niṣeduṣo vijṛmbhate viśvasmād indra uttaraḥ ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • na seśe: not remaining, not left behind
    • yasya: whose
    • rambate: moves or vibrates
    • antarā: within
    • sakthyā: with power or strength
    • kapṛt: the one who is capable
    • sed: but
    • īśe: in the lord
    • yasya: whose
    • romaśaṁ: the hair or the fine particles
    • niṣeduṣo: from the place of rest
    • vijṛmbhate: expands or manifests
    • viśvasmād: from the universe
    • indra: Indra, the king of gods
    • uttaraḥ: the supreme or the highest
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra speaks of a divine power that does not remain stagnant but vibrates with strength from within.
    • The phrase "na seśe yasya rambate" suggests that true power is dynamic and ever-moving, much like how a river flows continuously, shaping the landscape around it.
    • The mention of "īśe yasya romaśaṁ" indicates that even the smallest particles or aspects of the divine are capable of manifesting great strength, similar to how a single seed can grow into a mighty tree.
    • Indra, as referenced in the mantra, symbolizes the ultimate authority and power, reminding us that true strength comes from a higher source.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In nature, we see the power of transformation; for instance, a caterpillar becomes a butterfly, illustrating the dynamic nature of life.
    • In personal development, individuals who embrace change and adapt to new circumstances often find greater success, akin to the mantra's emphasis on movement and strength.
    • In leadership, effective leaders inspire their teams to harness their inner strengths, much like the divine power described in the mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of dynamic strength and the ability to adapt and grow.
    • Its significance lies in the reminder that true power is not static but is found in movement, transformation, and connection to a higher source.
    • In real life, this can be applied in various contexts, such as personal growth, leadership, and understanding the natural world, encouraging us to embrace change and seek strength from within and beyond.
