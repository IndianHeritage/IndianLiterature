देवान वा यच चक्रमा कच चिद आगः सखायं वा सदम इज जास्पतिं वा |इयं धीर्भूयाऽअवयानम एषां dyāvā रक्षतं पृथिवी नो अभ्वात ||
devān vā yac cakṛmā kac cid āgaḥ sakhāyaṁ vā sadam ij jāspatiṁ vā | iyaṁ dhīr bhūyā avayānam eṣāṁ dyāvā rakṣatam pṛthivī no abhvāt ||
  • Translation: "O Deities, whatever we have done, whether it is in friendship or in the presence of the Lord of the House, may this understanding be strengthened. May the heavens protect us, and may the earth not abandon us."
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • devān: O Deities
    • vā: or
    • yac: whatever
    • cakṛmā: we have done
    • kac: whether
    • cid: at all
    • āgaḥ: in friendship
    • sakhāyaṁ: or in the presence of the Lord
    • vā: or
    • sadam: the house
    • ij: may we be strengthened
    • jāspatiṁ: the Lord of the House
    • vā: or
    • iyaṁ: this
    • dhīr: understanding
    • bhūyā: may it be strengthened
    • avayānam: of these
    • eṣāṁ: of us
    • dyāvā: may the heavens
    • rakṣatam: protect
    • pṛthivī: the earth
    • no: us
    • abhvāt: not abandon
  • Significance: This mantra emphasizes the importance of divine protection and the strength of community and friendship. It reflects a deep understanding of the interconnectedness of human actions and the divine. The invocation of deities signifies a plea for guidance and support in our endeavors, whether they are personal or communal.
  • Real-Life Application:
    • In personal relationships, this mantra can be a reminder to nurture friendships and seek mutual understanding, ensuring that bonds are strengthened through shared experiences.
    • In community settings, it can serve as a call for collective action, encouraging individuals to work together for a common goal while seeking divine blessings for protection and success.
    • In professional environments, invoking this mantra can inspire teamwork and collaboration, fostering a sense of unity and purpose among colleagues.
  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the need for divine support in our lives and the importance of maintaining strong relationships with others. By recognizing the role of the divine and the strength of community, we can navigate life's challenges with greater resilience and purpose.
