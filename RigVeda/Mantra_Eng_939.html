प्र यद रथेषु पृषतीर अयुग्ध्वं वाजे अद्रिम मरुतो रंहयन्तः |उतारुषस्य वि ष्यन्ति धाराश चरमेवोदभिर व्युन्दन्ति भूम ||
pra yad ratheṣu pṛṣatīr ayugdhvaṁ vāje adrim maruto raṁhayantaḥ | utāruṣasya vi ṣyanti dhārāś carmevodabhir vy undanti bhūma ||
  • Mantra: "pra yad ratheṣu pṛṣatīr ayugdhvaṁ vāje adrim maruto raṁhayantaḥ | utāruṣasya vi ṣyanti dhārāś carmevodabhir vy undanti bhūma ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • pra: for, towards
    • yad: when
    • ratheṣu: in the chariots
    • pṛṣatīr: the strong ones
    • ayugdhvaṁ: not being tied
    • vāje: in the strength
    • adrim: the mountain
    • maruto: the winds
    • raṁhayantaḥ: bringing joy
    • utāruṣasya: of the rising sun
    • vi: indeed
    • ṣyanti: they flow
    • dhārāś: streams
    • carmevodabhir: with the waters of the earth
    • vy: they
    • undanti: they rise
    • bhūma: the earth
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra speaks about the forces of nature, particularly the winds (Maruts) and the rising sun, and their connection to the earth and the chariots.
    • The phrase "pra yad ratheṣu pṛṣatīr" suggests a movement or action that is powerful and unrestrained, akin to how we might see athletes or vehicles racing freely on a track.
    • "Maruto raṁhayantaḥ" indicates the joy and vitality brought by the winds, similar to how a refreshing breeze can uplift our spirits on a hot day.
    • The mention of "utāruṣasya" refers to the rising sun, which symbolizes new beginnings and hope, much like how each morning brings a fresh start in our lives.
    • The "dhārāś carmevodabhir" part illustrates the streams of water that nourish the earth, akin to how rain is essential for agriculture and sustains life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In agriculture, the interplay of winds and sunlight is crucial for crop growth, demonstrating the importance of natural forces in our daily sustenance.
    • In sports, the idea of unrestrained movement can be seen in athletes who push their limits, drawing inspiration from the natural world.
    • In personal life, the rising sun can symbolize new opportunities, encouraging individuals to embrace change and pursue their goals with vigor.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the interconnectedness of nature and human life, highlighting how natural elements influence our existence.
    • Its significance lies in reminding us to respect and harmonize with the forces of nature, as they play a vital role in our well-being and prosperity.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire us to appreciate the beauty of nature, motivate us in our endeavors, and encourage sustainable practices that honor the earth.
