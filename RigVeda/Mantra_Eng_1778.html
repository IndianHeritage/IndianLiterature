अमन्दन माऽ मरुतः स्तोमोऽत्र यन् मे नरः श्रुत्यम् ब्रह्म चक्रः |इन्द्राय वृष्णे सुमखाय मह्यं सख्ये सखायस तन् वे तनुभिः ||
amandan mā marutaḥ stomo atra yan me naraḥ śrutyam brahma cakra | indrāya vṛṣṇe sumakhāya mahyaṁ sakhye sakhāyas tanve tanūbhiḥ ||
  • amandan mā marutaḥ stomo: This phrase can be interpreted as a call for the Maruts, the storm deities, to bring forth their blessings. The word "amandan" suggests a state of bliss or joy, while "mā" indicates a plea or request. The Maruts are often associated with strength and vitality.
  • atra yan me naraḥ śrutyam brahma cakra: Here, "atra" means "here" or "in this place," and "yan me" translates to "what is mine." "Naraḥ" refers to humans or people, while "śrutyam" relates to hearing or knowledge. "Brahma cakra" signifies the cosmic order or the wheel of creation. This part of the mantra emphasizes the importance of human understanding and connection to the divine order.
  • indrāya vṛṣṇe sumakhāya: "Indrāya" refers to Indra, the king of the gods, and "vṛṣṇe" relates to the Vṛṣhṇi clan, which includes notable figures like Krishna. "Sumakhāya" means "of good form" or "auspicious." This line invokes the blessings of Indra and the Vṛṣhṇi lineage, highlighting the importance of divine support in achieving success.
  • mahyaṁ sakhye sakhāyas tanve tanūbhiḥ: "Mahyaṁ" means "to me," while "sakhye" refers to friendship or companionship. "Sakhāyas" indicates friends or allies, and "tanve tanūbhiḥ" suggests the physical body or form. This part of the mantra emphasizes the significance of friendship and community in one's life journey.

  • Conclusion: This mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking divine blessings, understanding the cosmic order, and valuing friendships. In real life, it serves as a reminder to cultivate joy, seek knowledge, and foster strong relationships. Whether in personal endeavors, professional collaborations, or spiritual pursuits, the principles outlined in this mantra can guide individuals toward a harmonious and fulfilling life. By invoking the blessings of the divine and recognizing the importance of community, one can navigate life's challenges with strength and support.
