यो न इन्द्राभितो जनो वृकायुर आदिदेशति |अधस्पदं तं ईं कृत्य विबाधोऽसि सासहिर नभन्ताम् अन्यकेषां ज्याकाद्धि धन्वसु ||
yo na indrābhito jano vṛkāyur ādideśati | adhaspadaṁ tam īṁ kṛdhi vibādho asi sāsahir nabhantām anyakeṣāṁ jyākā adhi dhanvasu ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yo: who
    • na: not
    • indrābhito: surrounded by Indra (the king of gods)
    • jano: people or beings
    • vṛkāyur: with a long life
    • ādideśati: commands or directs
    • adhaspadaṁ: in the lower realms
    • tam: that
    • īṁ: may we achieve
    • kṛdhi: do or create
    • vibādho: you are the protector
    • asi: you are
    • sāsahir: with strength
    • nabhantām: of the heavens
    • anyakeṣāṁ: of others
    • jyākā: in the light
    • adhi: above
    • dhanvasu: in the wealth or prosperity
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the divine protection and guidance provided by Indra, the king of gods, to those who are not surrounded by fear or negativity.
    • It emphasizes the importance of seeking strength and prosperity while being grounded and connected to the earth.
    • The phrase "adhaspadaṁ tam īṁ kṛdhi" suggests a call to action, urging individuals to create or manifest their desires with the support of divine forces.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a challenging situation, such as facing a difficult exam or job interview, one might recite this mantra to invoke confidence and clarity, believing that divine support will guide them through.
    • In times of financial hardship, individuals may reflect on this mantra to inspire them to take proactive steps towards creating wealth and prosperity, trusting that they are not alone in their efforts.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the protective and guiding forces in our lives, encouraging us to take action towards our goals while remaining connected to our roots.
    • Its significance lies in the belief that with divine support, we can overcome obstacles and achieve our aspirations, making it applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal challenges to collective endeavors.
