उद् बुद्ध्यध्वं समनसः सखायः सम अग्निम इन्द्रवम् बहवः सनीलाः |दधिक्राम अग्निम उषसं च देवीं इन्द्रावतोऽवसे निह्वये वः ||
ud budhyadhvaṁ samanasaḥ sakhāyaḥ sam agnim indhvam bahavaḥ sanīḻāḥ | dadhikrām agnim uṣasaṁ ca devīm indrāvato 'vase ni hvaye vaḥ ||
  • ud budhyadhvaṁ: This phrase translates to "Awaken together." It signifies the importance of collective awakening and consciousness. In real life, this can be seen in team efforts where individuals come together to achieve a common goal, such as in a workplace or community service.
  • samanasaḥ sakhāyaḥ: Meaning "friends with a common mind," this emphasizes unity and harmony among individuals. For example, in a sports team, players must work together with a shared mindset to win a game.
  • sam agnim indhvam: This translates to "together we invoke the fire." Fire symbolizes energy, transformation, and knowledge. In practical terms, this can relate to how people come together to share ideas and ignite innovation, such as in brainstorming sessions or collaborative projects.
  • bahavaḥ sanīḻāḥ: This means "many are the offerings." It suggests that there are numerous contributions from various individuals. In a community, this can be seen during festivals where everyone brings their unique offerings, enhancing the collective experience.
  • dadhikrām agnim: This translates to "we have approached the fire." It signifies the act of seeking knowledge and enlightenment. In real life, this can be likened to pursuing education or personal development, where individuals strive to gain wisdom.
  • uṣasaṁ ca devīm: Meaning "and the goddess of dawn," this refers to new beginnings and opportunities. In everyday life, this can be related to starting a new project or phase in life, symbolizing hope and renewal.
  • indrāvato 'vase: This translates to "may we be under the protection of Indra." Indra is a deity associated with strength and protection. In practical terms, this can be interpreted as seeking guidance and support from mentors or leaders in challenging situations.
  • ni hvaye vaḥ: This means "we call upon you." It signifies the act of invoking divine support or assistance. In real life, this can be seen in moments of prayer or meditation where individuals seek help from a higher power during difficult times.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of collective awakening, unity, and the pursuit of knowledge. It highlights the importance of community and collaboration in achieving common goals. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various contexts such as teamwork in professional settings, community gatherings, and personal development endeavors. The mantra serves as a reminder that together, we can invoke positive change and seek guidance in our journeys, fostering a sense of belonging and purpose.
