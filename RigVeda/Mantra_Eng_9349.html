त्वं विश्वा दधिषे केवलानि यानि आविर्व याऽच गुहा वसूनि |कामं इन मे मघवन मा वि तारीष त्वम आज्ञाता त्वम इन्द्रासि दाता ||
tvaṁ viśvā dadhiṣe kevalāni yāny āvir yā ca guhā vasūni | kāmam in me maghavan mā vi tārīs tvam ājñātā tvam indrāsi dātā ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • tvaṁ: You
    • viśvā: All, universal
    • dadhiṣe: You provide, you bestow
    • kevalāni: Only, solely
    • yāny: Whatever
    • āvir: Manifest, appear
    • yā: Which
    • ca: And
    • guhā: Hidden, secret
    • vasūni: Wealth, treasures
    • kāmam: Desire, wish
    • in: In
    • me: Me
    • maghavan: O bountiful one, generous
    • mā: Not
    • vi: Away, far
    • tārīs: To take away, to remove
    • tvam: You
    • ājñātā: Knower, aware
    • tvam: You
    • indrāsi: You are Indra, the king of gods
    • dātā: Giver, bestower
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra addresses a divine entity, acknowledging its universal nature and the ability to provide all that is needed.
    • The speaker expresses a desire for the divine to bestow wealth and treasures, both manifest and hidden.
    • It emphasizes the importance of not having desires taken away, highlighting a plea for abundance and fulfillment.
    • The invocation of Indra signifies a request for strength and support, as Indra is often associated with power and prosperity.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In times of financial difficulty, individuals may recite this mantra to seek divine assistance in overcoming challenges and attracting prosperity.
    • During significant life events, such as weddings or new ventures, people may invoke this mantra to ensure blessings and abundance in their new beginnings.
    • In a corporate setting, leaders may use the essence of this mantra to inspire their teams to work towards collective goals, emphasizing the importance of unity and shared success.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of desires, abundance, and divine support.
    • It encourages individuals to seek not only material wealth but also spiritual richness, fostering a holistic approach to life.
    • In practical terms, it can be applied in various aspects of life, from personal aspirations to communal goals, reinforcing the belief that divine assistance is available to those who seek it with sincerity.
