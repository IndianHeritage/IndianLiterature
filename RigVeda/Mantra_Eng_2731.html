अपश्चिदेष विभ्वोऽदमूनाः प्र सदृचीर असृजद् विश्वचन्द्राः |मध्वः पुनानाः कविभिः पवित्रैर द्युभिर हिन्वन्त्यक्तुभिः धनुत्रीः ||
apaś cid eṣa vibhvo3 damūnāḥ pra sadhrīcīr asṛjad viśvaścandrāḥ | madhvaḥ punānāḥ kavibhiḥ pavitrair dyubhir hinvanty aktubhir dhanutrīḥ ||
  • apaś cid eṣa vibhvo damūnāḥ: This phrase can be interpreted as "This is the essence of the universe, the source of all creation." Here, "apaś" refers to the essence or the fundamental nature, while "cid" signifies consciousness or awareness. "Vibhvo" relates to the vastness or the universe, and "damūnāḥ" can be understood as the source or origin.
  • pra sadhrīcīr asṛjad viśvaścandrāḥ: This part translates to "From this essence, the diverse forms of existence arise." "Pra" means 'from', "sadhrīcīr" refers to the various forms or manifestations, and "asṛjad" means 'to create'. "Viśvaścandrāḥ" can be interpreted as 'the universal moon', symbolizing the light of knowledge and enlightenment that guides all beings.
  • madhvaḥ punānāḥ kavibhiḥ: This phrase means "The wise ones, through their knowledge, purify and elevate." "Madhvaḥ" refers to sweetness or the essence of goodness, while "punānāḥ" means to purify or to make sacred. "Kavibhiḥ" refers to the wise or the poets, indicating that knowledge and wisdom are essential for purification.
  • pavitrair dyubhir hinvanty aktubhir dhanutrīḥ: This translates to "With pure intentions and actions, they achieve their goals." "Pavitrair" means pure, "dyubhir" refers to the heavens or higher realms, and "hinvanty" means to achieve or to reach. "Aktubhir" signifies actions or deeds, while "dhanutrīḥ" can be interpreted as the bow, symbolizing the focus and direction needed to achieve one's aims.

  • Conclusion: This mantra emphasizes the interconnectedness of the universe, the importance of knowledge and wisdom in purifying oneself, and the necessity of pure intentions and actions to achieve one's goals. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios such as personal development, where individuals seek to understand their essence and purpose, or in community building, where collective wisdom and purity of intention can lead to harmonious living. The mantra serves as a reminder that through awareness, knowledge, and purity of action, one can navigate the complexities of life and contribute positively to the world.
