प्र ये गृहाद अममदुस त्वायाऽ पराशरः शतयातुर वसिष्ठः |न ते भोजस्य सख्यम मृषन्ताधा सूरिभ्यः सुदिना व्युच्छान ||
pra ye gṛhād amamadus tvāyā parāśaraḥ śatayātur vasiṣṭhaḥ | na te bhojasya sakhyam mṛṣantādhā sūribhyaḥ sudinā vy ucchān ||
  • Mantra: "pra ye gṛhād amamadus tvāyā parāśaraḥ śatayātur vasiṣṭhaḥ | na te bhojasya sakhyam mṛṣantādhā sūribhyaḥ sudinā vy ucchān ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • pra ye: those who are (or who have)
    • gṛhād: from the home
    • amamadus: not being disturbed
    • tvāyā: by you
    • parāśaraḥ: Parashara (a sage)
    • śatayātur: a hundred ailments
    • vasiṣṭhaḥ: Vasistha (another sage)
    • na te: not for you
    • bhojasya: of the Bhojas (a tribe)
    • sakhyam: friendship
    • mṛṣantādhā: those who are deceitful
    • sūribhyaḥ: from the wise
    • sudinā: with good fortune
    • vy ucchān: may be elevated
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra speaks about the importance of maintaining peace and stability in one's home and life.
    • The reference to sages like Parashara and Vasistha highlights the value of wisdom and guidance in overcoming life's challenges.
    • The phrase "not for you" suggests that deceitful friendships or alliances can lead to misfortune, emphasizing the need for genuine relationships.
    • In real life, this can be seen in the context of personal relationships where trust and honesty are foundational. For example, in a workplace, having a supportive and trustworthy team can lead to better outcomes than working with deceitful colleagues.
    • The mention of "good fortune" indicates that when one aligns with wisdom and truth, they are more likely to experience success and elevation in life.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder to seek wisdom and maintain integrity in our relationships.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, such as personal relationships, professional environments, and community interactions.
    • By fostering genuine connections and aligning with wise counsel, individuals can navigate life's challenges more effectively and achieve greater success.
