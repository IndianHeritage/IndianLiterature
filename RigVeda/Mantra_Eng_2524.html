अध्वर्युभिः पञ्चभिः सप्त विप्राः प्रियं रक्षन्ते निहितं पदं वेः |प्राञ्चो मदन्त्य उक्षणो अजुर्व्या देवाः देवानाम् अनु हि व्रता गुः ||
adhvaryubhiḥ pañcabhiḥ sapta viprāḥ priyaṁ rakṣante nihitam padaṁ veḥ | prāñco madanty ukṣaṇo ajuryā devā devānām anu hi vratā guḥ ||
  • adhvaryubhiḥ pañcabhiḥ: This phrase refers to the five priests (adhvaryus) who perform the rituals. In real life, this can be likened to a team of professionals working together to achieve a common goal, such as a group of doctors collaborating on a complex surgery.
  • sapta viprāḥ: This translates to "seven sages" or "wise ones." In contemporary terms, this can represent a council of advisors or mentors who provide guidance and wisdom in decision-making processes, similar to a board of directors in a company.
  • priyaṁ rakṣante: This means "they protect what is dear." In everyday life, this can be seen in the way families protect their loved ones and values, such as parents safeguarding their children's future through education and moral guidance.
  • nihitam padaṁ veḥ: This phrase means "the hidden path." It signifies the importance of seeking deeper truths and understanding beyond the surface. In real life, this can be compared to the journey of self-discovery, where individuals explore their inner selves to find their true purpose.
  • prāñco madanty: This translates to "the wise ones rejoice." This can be related to moments of achievement and celebration in life, such as when a team successfully completes a project or when individuals reach personal milestones.
  • ukṣaṇo ajuryā: This means "the divine beings are eternal." It emphasizes the idea of immortality and the lasting impact of our actions. In real life, this can be reflected in the legacies we leave behind, such as influential leaders or innovators whose contributions continue to inspire future generations.
  • devā devānām anu hi vratā guḥ: This phrase suggests that the divine beings follow the sacred vows. It highlights the importance of commitment and integrity in our actions. In practical terms, this can be seen in the dedication of individuals to their professions, such as teachers who commit to shaping young minds with integrity and passion.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of collaboration, wisdom, protection of values, and the pursuit of deeper truths. It serves as a reminder of the importance of community and the roles we play in each other's lives. In real-life scenarios, it encourages individuals to work together harmoniously, seek guidance from wise mentors, and remain committed to their values and responsibilities. This mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as in family dynamics, professional environments, and personal growth journeys, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling and meaningful life.
