ये देवानां यज्ञियाः यज्ञियानाम् मनोऽर्यात्रा अमृताः ऋतज्ञाः |ते नो रासन्तां उरुगायम् अद्य युयम् पात स्वस्तिभिः सदा नः ||
ye devānāṁ yajñiyā yajñiyānām manor yajatrā amṛtā ṛtajñāḥ | te no rāsantām urugāyam adya yūyam pāta svastibhiḥ sadā naḥ ||
  • Translation: "Those who are the deities of the sacrifices, who are the essence of the sacrifices, who are the wise and immortal, may they come to us today, and may you protect us always with your blessings."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • ye: Those who
    • devānāṁ: of the deities
    • yajñiyā: of the sacrifices
    • yajñiyānām: of the essence of sacrifices
    • manor: of the mind
    • yajatrā: who are wise
    • amṛtā: immortal
    • ṛtajñāḥ: those who know the truth
    • te: may they
    • no: to us
    • rāsantām: come
    • urugāyam: today
    • adya: today
    • yūyam: you
    • pāta: protect
    • svastibhiḥ: with blessings
    • sadā: always
    • naḥ: us
  • Contextual Meaning: This mantra emphasizes the importance of divine beings associated with rituals and sacrifices. It acknowledges their wisdom and immortality, seeking their presence and protection in our lives. The invocation of these deities signifies a deep respect for the forces that govern the universe and our lives.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern life, this mantra can be recited during significant events such as weddings, housewarming ceremonies, or any auspicious occasion where blessings are sought.
    • In a corporate setting, leaders may invoke similar sentiments during meetings or team-building exercises, emphasizing the importance of collective wisdom and support.
    • In personal development, individuals may reflect on the qualities of wisdom and truth in their decision-making processes, seeking guidance from their inner selves or mentors.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine guidance and protection. It serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of life, the importance of wisdom, and the need for blessings in our endeavors. By invoking these principles, individuals can cultivate a sense of purpose and direction in their lives, fostering a harmonious relationship with the universe and those around them.
