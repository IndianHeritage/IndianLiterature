स नो बोधि पुरेतā सुगेषूता दुर्गेषु पथिकृत विदानः |ये आश्रमासा उरवो वहिṣ्ठास तेभिर न इन्द्राभि वक्षि वाजम् ||
sa no bodhi puraetā sugeṣūta durgeṣu pathikṛd vidānaḥ | ye aśramāsa uravo vahiṣṭhās tebhir na indrābhi vakṣi vājam ||
  • sa no bodhi puraetā: This phrase can be translated as "May the one who is wise and knowledgeable guide us." Here, "bodhi" refers to wisdom or enlightenment, and "puraetā" implies a guide or leader. In real life, this can be seen in the context of seeking mentorship or guidance from experienced individuals in various fields, such as education or career development.
  • sugeṣūta durgeṣu: This part means "may we be protected in difficult situations." "Sugeṣūta" refers to being well-guarded or protected, while "durgeṣu" indicates challenging or perilous circumstances. For example, in times of crisis, such as natural disasters or personal hardships, having a support system or community can provide the necessary protection and resilience.
  • pathikṛd vidānaḥ: This translates to "the one who knows the paths." "Pathikṛd" refers to a traveler or one who knows the way, and "vidānaḥ" means to provide or offer. This can relate to life choices and decisions, where individuals seek advice from those who have navigated similar paths successfully, such as career counselors or life coaches.
  • ye aśramāsa uravo vahiṣṭhās: This phrase means "those who have attained the highest state of being." "Aśramāsa" refers to those who have reached a stage of spiritual or personal development, and "vahiṣṭhās" indicates the best or highest. In real life, this can be seen in individuals who have achieved significant personal growth or enlightenment, serving as role models for others.
  • tebhir na indrābhi vakṣi vājam: This translates to "may we not be defeated by the forces of adversity." "Indrābhi" refers to powerful forces or challenges, and "vakṣi vājam" implies being overwhelmed or defeated. This can be applied in various scenarios, such as facing competition in business or overcoming personal challenges, where resilience and determination are crucial for success.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of wisdom, guidance, protection, and resilience in navigating life's challenges. It highlights the significance of seeking mentorship and support during difficult times and striving for personal growth. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire individuals to cultivate relationships with mentors, build strong support systems, and develop the resilience needed to overcome obstacles, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling and enlightened life.
