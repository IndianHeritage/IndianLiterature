अस्माकम् अत्र पितरो मनुष्याः अभि प्र सेदुर ऋतम् आशुषाणाः |अश्मव्रजाः सुदुघा वव्रे अन्तर उद उष्रा आजन्न उषसो हुवानाः ||
asmākam atra pitaro manuṣyā abhi pra sedur ṛtam āśuṣāṇāḥ | aśmavrajāḥ sudughā vavre antar ud usrā ājann uṣaso huvānāḥ ||
  • Translation: "Our ancestors, the human beings, have come here to us, seeking the truth with their wisdom. They have chosen the path of righteousness, like the strong and powerful, who have invoked the dawn with their prayers."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • asmākam: Our
    • atra: Here
    • pitaro: Ancestors
    • manuṣyā: Human beings
    • abhi: Towards
    • pra: Seeking
    • sedur: Truth
    • ṛtam: Righteousness
    • āśuṣāṇāḥ: With wisdom
    • aśmavrajāḥ: Strong and powerful
    • sudughā: Chosen
    • vavre: Invoked
    • antar: Within
    • ud: Dawn
    • usrā: Light
    • ājann: With prayers
    • uṣaso: Of the dawn
    • huvānāḥ: Those who invoke
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of honoring our ancestors and recognizing their wisdom. For instance, during family gatherings or festivals, people often share stories about their ancestors, reflecting on their values and teachings.
    • The concept of seeking truth and righteousness can be seen in various social movements where individuals strive for justice and equality, inspired by the principles passed down through generations.
    • In personal development, individuals often invoke the 'dawn' of new beginnings, using rituals or affirmations to set intentions for their lives, much like the invocation of the dawn in the mantra.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call to honor our roots and the wisdom of our ancestors while striving for truth and righteousness in our lives. It serves as a reminder that we are part of a larger continuum, where the values and teachings of those who came before us guide our actions today. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire individuals to reflect on their heritage, engage in social justice, and embrace new beginnings with intention and purpose.
