निष्चर्मणो गाम अरिणीत धीतिभिर या जरन्ता युवशा ताकृत्नोतना |सौधन्वना अश्वाद अश्वम् अतक्षत युक्त्वा रथम उप देवाम् आयातना ||
niś carmaṇo gām ariṇīta dhītibhir yā jarantā yuvaśā tākṛṇotana | saudhanvanā aśvād aśvam atakṣata yuktvā ratham upa devām̐ ayātana ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • niś: night
    • carmaṇo: of the skin or covering
    • gām: to the world or to the people
    • ariṇīta: to be led or guided
    • dhītibhir: with wisdom or understanding
    • yā: who
    • jarantā: aging or growing old
    • yuvaśā: youthful or energetic
    • tākṛṇotana: to create or to make
    • saudhanvanā: with wealth or prosperity
    • aśvād: horses
    • aśvam: horse (again)
    • atakṣata: did not abandon or forsake
    • yuktvā: having united or joined
    • ratham: chariot
    • upa: towards
    • devām̐: the divine or gods
    • ayatana: abode or dwelling
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the journey through life, emphasizing the importance of wisdom and youthfulness.
    • The reference to "night" and "skin" suggests the protective layers we have in life, while "guidance" indicates the need for direction.
    • The mention of "wealth" and "horses" symbolizes prosperity and the means to achieve one's goals.
    • It highlights the importance of unity and collaboration, as seen in the phrase "having united the chariot." This can be likened to teamwork in modern organizations.
    • The invocation of the divine suggests seeking higher guidance in our endeavors.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, a team working together towards a common goal can be seen as "uniting the chariot." Each member brings their unique skills, much like the horses pulling the chariot.
    • In personal life, the wisdom gained through experiences can guide decisions, akin to the "wisdom" mentioned in the mantra.
    • Seeking divine guidance or inspiration in challenging times can provide clarity and direction, reflecting the spiritual aspect of the mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra encapsulates the essence of life’s journey, emphasizing the balance between wisdom and youthful energy.
    • It serves as a reminder to seek guidance, work collaboratively, and strive for prosperity while remaining grounded in our values.
    • In real life, it encourages individuals to embrace their experiences, work together towards common goals, and seek higher wisdom in their pursuits.
