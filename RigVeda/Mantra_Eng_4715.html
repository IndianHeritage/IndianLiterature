अर्वाग् रथं विश्ववारं त उग्रेन्द्र युक्तासो हरयो वहन्तु |कीरिश् चिद् धि त्वा हवते स्वर्वान् ऋधिमहि सदहमादस् ते अद्य ||
arvāg rathaṁ viśvavāraṁ ta ugrendra yuktāso harayo vahantu | kīriś cid dhi tvā havate svarvān ṛdhīmahi sadhamādas te adya ||
  • arvāg rathaṁ: This phrase can be translated as "the chariot that moves forward." In a metaphorical sense, it represents the journey of life and the progress one makes. Just as a chariot moves forward, individuals must strive to advance in their personal and professional lives.
  • viśvavāraṁ: This term means "the protector of all." It signifies the importance of guardianship and protection in our lives. In real life, this can be seen in the role of parents, mentors, or leaders who safeguard the well-being of others.
  • ta ugrendra yuktāso harayo vahantu: This part translates to "may the powerful and strong ones carry you." It emphasizes the need for support from those who are capable and strong. In practical terms, this can relate to teamwork in a workplace where strong leaders guide their teams towards success.
  • kīriś cid dhi tvā havate: This phrase means "may the glory of your name be praised." It highlights the significance of reputation and honor. In everyday life, this can be reflected in how individuals strive to build their reputations through hard work and integrity.
  • svarvān ṛdhīmahi: This translates to "we seek the highest prosperity." It signifies the pursuit of success and abundance in life. This can be seen in the aspirations of individuals who work diligently to achieve their goals, whether in their careers or personal lives.
  • sadhamādas te adya: This means "may you be blessed today." It is a call for blessings and positive outcomes in the present moment. In real life, this can be related to the practice of mindfulness and gratitude, where individuals appreciate their current circumstances and seek positivity.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of life's journey, the importance of protection and support, the pursuit of glory and prosperity, and the need for blessings in our daily lives. Its significance lies in reminding us to strive for progress while valuing the support of others, maintaining our reputation, and seeking abundance. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire individuals to work collaboratively, honor their commitments, and remain grateful for the present, ultimately leading to a fulfilling and successful life.
