वृत्राण्य अन्यः समिथेषु जिघ्नते व्रतान्य अन्यो अभि रक्षते सदा |हवामहे वां वृषणा सुवृक्तिभिर अस्मे इन्द्रावरुणा शर्म यच्छताम् ||
vṛtrāṇy anyaḥ samitheṣu jighnate vratāny anyo abhi rakṣate sadā | havāmahe vāṁ vṛṣaṇā suvṛktibhir asme indrāvaruṇā śarma yacchatam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • vṛtrāṇy: enemies or obstacles
    • anyaḥ: another or different
    • samitheṣu: in the gatherings or assemblies
    • jighnate: to strike down or defeat
    • vratāny: vows or commitments
    • anyo: another
    • abhi: towards or in favor of
    • rakṣate: protects or guards
    • sadā: always or continually
    • havāmahe: we invoke or call upon
    • vāṁ: you (plural)
    • vṛṣaṇā: with strength or power
    • suvṛktibhir: with good offerings or well-prepared
    • asme: for us
    • indrāvaruṇā: Indra and Varuna (deities)
    • śarma: protection or comfort
    • yacchatam: may grant or provide
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the duality of forces in life, where one must confront obstacles (vṛtrāṇy) while also seeking protection (rakṣate) from other forces.
    • It emphasizes the importance of community and collective strength in overcoming challenges, as indicated by the references to gatherings (samitheṣu).
    • The invocation of Indra and Varuna signifies the appeal to divine powers for strength and protection, highlighting the belief in a higher force that aids in human endeavors.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, a team may face competition (vṛtrāṇy) from rival companies. By working together (samitheṣu) and invoking the support of mentors or leaders (indrāvaruṇā), they can strategize and overcome these challenges.
    • In personal life, individuals often face various obstacles such as health issues or financial difficulties. By making commitments (vratāny) to improve their situation and seeking support from family and friends, they can find the strength to persevere.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of community, commitment, and divine support in overcoming life's challenges.
    • Its significance lies in the understanding that while obstacles are a part of life, collective strength and the invocation of higher powers can lead to protection and success.
    • In real-life scenarios, whether in personal struggles or professional challenges, this mantra encourages individuals to seek support, remain committed to their goals, and trust in the strength of their community and the divine.
