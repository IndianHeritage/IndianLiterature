स नः सोमेषु सोमपाः सुतेषु शवसस पते |मादयस्व राधसां सूनृतावतेंद्र राया परीनसां ||
sa naḥ someṣu somapāḥ suteṣu śavasas pate | mādayasva rādhasā sūnṛtāvatendra rāyā parīṇasā ||
  • sa naḥ - "May that (divine force) be ours"
  • someṣu - "in the soma (sacred drink)"
  • somapāḥ - "the drinkers of soma"
  • suteṣu - "in the offerings"
  • śavasas pate - "O Lord of the dead"
  • mādayasva - "may you delight us"
  • rādhasā - "with abundance"
  • sūnṛtāvat - "with truthfulness"
  • indra - "O Indra, the king of gods"
  • rāyā - "with wealth"
  • parīṇasā - "with fulfillment"

This mantra from the Rigveda is a prayer invoking the divine for blessings of abundance, truth, and prosperity. It emphasizes the importance of divine connection through offerings and the sacred drink, soma, which symbolizes spiritual nourishment and enlightenment.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various contexts:

  • Spiritual Practices: Individuals can recite this mantra during meditation or rituals to seek divine blessings and enhance their spiritual journey.
  • Community Gatherings: In communal settings, such as festivals or ceremonies, this mantra can be chanted to invoke collective prosperity and harmony.
  • Personal Reflection: The themes of truthfulness and abundance can inspire individuals to reflect on their values and strive for integrity in their actions while seeking fulfillment in their lives.

Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine support in achieving a life filled with abundance, truth, and prosperity. It serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of spiritual and material well-being, encouraging individuals to seek balance and fulfillment in their lives through divine grace and personal integrity.
