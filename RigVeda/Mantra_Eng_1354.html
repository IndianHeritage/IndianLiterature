आयं समह मा तनूहत जनां अनु |सोमपेयं सुखो रथः ||
ayaṁ samaha mā tanūhyāte janām̐ anu | somapeyaṁ sukho rathaḥ ||
  • ayaṁ samaha: This phrase can be translated as "this is the same" or "this is equal." It emphasizes the idea of unity and equality among individuals.
  • mā tanūhyāte: This means "may our bodies not be separated." It reflects a desire for togetherness and harmony among people, suggesting that physical separation should not lead to emotional or spiritual disconnection.
  • janām̐ anu: This translates to "among the people." It indicates a collective aspect, focusing on the community and the relationships within it.
  • somapeyaṁ: This term refers to "the drink of the gods" or "soma," which is often associated with immortality and divine bliss. It symbolizes the pursuit of higher knowledge and spiritual fulfillment.
  • sukho rathaḥ: This means "the chariot of happiness." It signifies the journey towards joy and contentment, suggesting that happiness is a goal that can be achieved through unity and shared experiences.

  • Significance: This mantra encapsulates the essence of community, unity, and the pursuit of happiness. It highlights the importance of staying connected with one another, both physically and emotionally, and emphasizes the collective journey towards joy and fulfillment.
  • Real-life Application: In today's world, where individualism often prevails, this mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of community and relationships. For example, in a workplace setting, fostering teamwork and collaboration can lead to greater productivity and job satisfaction. In personal relationships, maintaining strong bonds with family and friends can enhance emotional well-being and provide support during challenging times.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encourages us to recognize our interconnectedness and strive for unity in our communities. By valuing relationships and working together towards common goals, we can create a more harmonious and fulfilling life. It serves as a timeless reminder that happiness is not just an individual pursuit but a collective journey that thrives on togetherness and shared experiences.
