देवी यदि तविषी त्वावृधोतय इन्द्रं सिषक्त्य उषसं न सूर्यः |यो धृष्णुना शवसा बाधते तम इयर्ति रेणुम बृहद् अरहरिष्वणिः ||
devī yadi taviṣī tvāvṛdhotaya indraṁ siṣakty uṣasaṁ na sūryaḥ | yo dhṛṣṇunā śavasā bādhate tama iyarti reṇum bṛhad arhariṣvaṇiḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • devī: goddess or divine power
    • yadi: if
    • taviṣī: you are powerful
    • tvāvṛdhotaya: you who are the source of strength
    • indraṁ: Indra, the king of gods
    • siṣakty: you have the power
    • uṣasaṁ: of the dawn
    • na: not
    • sūryaḥ: the sun
    • yo: who
    • dhṛṣṇunā: with courage
    • śavasā: with strength
    • bādhate: obstructs
    • tama: darkness
    • iyarti: drives away
    • reṇum: dust or particles
    • bṛhad: great
    • arhariṣvaṇiḥ: the one who is powerful and mighty
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the divine feminine energy, suggesting that if the goddess is powerful and the source of strength, she can influence even the mightiest, like Indra.
    • The reference to dawn signifies new beginnings, hope, and the dispelling of darkness, which can be interpreted as ignorance or negativity in life.
    • The mantra emphasizes the importance of courage and strength in overcoming obstacles and challenges, represented by darkness and dust.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In personal development, this mantra can inspire individuals to harness their inner strength and courage to face challenges, much like how a person might overcome fears to pursue a new career or venture.
    • In a community context, it can symbolize collective strength, where a group comes together to drive away negativity or obstacles, such as a community rallying to support a cause or rebuild after a disaster.
    • In spiritual practices, invoking this mantra can serve as a reminder to seek inner strength and clarity during times of confusion or darkness.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for empowerment through divine strength and courage.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal challenges to collective efforts in communities.
    • By embracing the essence of this mantra, individuals can find the motivation to overcome obstacles and foster a sense of hope and renewal in their lives.
