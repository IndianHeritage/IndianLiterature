ऋष्वा ते पादाः प्र यज जिगास्य अवर्धन वाजाः उता ये चिदात्र |त्वं इन्द्र सालावृकान सहस्रं आसन दधिषे अश्विनां ववृत्याः ||
ṛṣvā te pādā pra yaj jigāsy avardhan vājā uta ye cid atra | tvam indra sālāvṛkān sahasram āsan dadhiṣe aśvinā vavṛtyāḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ṛṣvā: With strength or power
    • te: Your
    • pādā: Feet
    • pra: Forward or towards
    • yaj: Sacrifice or worship
    • jigāsy: To achieve or to conquer
    • avardhan: Increase or growth
    • vājā: Strength or energy
    • uta: And
    • ye: Those who
    • cid: Indeed or certainly
    • atra: Here
    • tvam: You
    • indra: O Indra (the king of gods)
    • sālāvṛkān: The powerful or strong
    • sahasram: Thousands
    • āsan: You have seated
    • dadhiṣe: You have given
    • aśvinā: O Aśvins (the twin gods)
    • vavṛtyāḥ: With your grace or favor
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the divine powers of Indra and the Aśvins, emphasizing the strength and support they provide.
    • The phrase "ṛṣvā te pādā" suggests a request for the strength of the divine to guide one's actions.
    • It highlights the importance of sacrifice and worship ("yaj") in achieving success and growth in life.
    • The mention of "sahasram" indicates the vastness of the blessings and support available from the divine.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a challenging situation, such as preparing for an important exam or presentation, one might recite this mantra to seek strength and guidance from the divine.
    • During a community event or festival, invoking this mantra can symbolize collective strength and the importance of unity in achieving common goals.
    • In personal growth, individuals may reflect on the mantra's message to embrace challenges and seek divine support in their endeavors.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the divine support available to individuals in their pursuits.
    • It emphasizes the significance of strength, sacrifice, and the collective effort in achieving success.
    • In real-life scenarios, it can be applied in moments of challenge, community efforts, and personal growth, reinforcing the belief in divine guidance and support.
