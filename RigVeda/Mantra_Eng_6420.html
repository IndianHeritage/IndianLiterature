अनेहो मित्रार्यमन् नृवद वरुण शंस्यम् |त्रिवरूथम् मरुतो यन्त नश् छर्दिः ||
aneho mitrāryaman nṛvad varuṇa śaṁsyam | trivarūtham maruto yanta naś chardiḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • aneho: without delay, swiftly
    • mitrāryaman: O Mitra and Aryaman (deities associated with friendship and harmony)
    • nṛvad: O Varuna (the god of cosmic order and water)
    • varuṇa: the same as above, emphasizing the importance of Varuna
    • śaṁsyam: we praise or glorify
    • trivarūtham: threefold, referring to the three aspects of existence
    • maruto: O Maruts (the storm deities)
    • yanta: who move or travel
    • naś: our
    • chardiḥ: prosperity or wealth
  • Overall Meaning: This mantra invokes the deities Mitra, Aryaman, Varuna, and the Maruts, seeking their blessings for prosperity and harmony. It emphasizes the importance of divine support in achieving a balanced and prosperous life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community setting, this mantra can be recited during gatherings to foster unity and friendship among members, promoting a sense of belonging and cooperation.
    • In personal life, individuals may chant this mantra during times of uncertainty or conflict, seeking divine guidance and support to navigate challenges and maintain harmony.
    • In business, leaders can use the essence of this mantra to inspire teamwork and collaboration, ensuring that all members work towards a common goal with mutual respect and understanding.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine assistance in achieving harmony and prosperity. It serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of individuals and the importance of seeking support from higher powers in both personal and communal endeavors. By embodying the principles of friendship, cooperation, and respect, individuals can create a more harmonious and prosperous environment in their lives and communities.
