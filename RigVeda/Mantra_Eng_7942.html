अस्मे धेहि द्युमद् यशो मघवद्भ्यश्च मह्यम् च |सनीम् मेधा उता श्रवः ||
asme dhehi dyumad yaśo maghavadbhyaś ca mahyaṁ ca | sanim medhām uta śravaḥ ||
  • asme: This word translates to "to us" or "for us." It signifies a request or a plea directed towards a higher power or the universe.
  • dhehi: Meaning "bestow" or "grant," this word expresses the desire for something valuable to be given.
  • dyumad: This term refers to "brilliance" or "radiance," often associated with light, knowledge, or enlightenment.
  • yaśo: Translated as "fame" or "glory," it indicates a longing for recognition and respect in society.
  • maghavadbhyaś: This word can be understood as "from the powerful" or "from the mighty," referring to those who possess strength and influence.
  • ca: A simple conjunction meaning "and," linking the various elements of the mantra together.
  • mahyaṁ: Meaning "to me" or "to us," it personalizes the request, making it more intimate and direct.
  • ca: Again, this conjunction connects the thoughts expressed in the mantra.
  • sanim: This word means "with" or "together with," suggesting a sense of unity or collaboration.
  • medhām: Translated as "intellect" or "wisdom," it signifies the desire for enhanced understanding and insight.
  • uta: Meaning "or," it presents an alternative or additional request.
  • śravaḥ: This term refers to "fame" or "renown," similar to "yaśo," emphasizing the importance of being well-regarded.

This mantra can be interpreted as a heartfelt plea for the bestowal of brilliance, fame, and wisdom from powerful beings or forces. It reflects a deep yearning for recognition and understanding, both personally and collectively.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as:

  • In academic settings, students may recite this mantra before exams, seeking clarity and wisdom to excel in their studies.
  • Professionals may use it as a motivational tool, wishing for recognition and success in their careers.
  • Individuals facing challenges may invoke this mantra to seek guidance and enlightenment in difficult situations.

Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its universal appeal for wisdom, recognition, and collaboration. It serves as a reminder that we are not alone in our pursuits; we can seek support from the universe and those around us. By embodying the essence of this mantra, individuals can cultivate a mindset that embraces learning, growth, and the pursuit of excellence in all aspects of life.
