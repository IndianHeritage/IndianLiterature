स्वर्ण वस्तोर उषसां अरोचि यज्ञं तन्वाना उशिजो न मनमा |अग्निर जन्मानि देव आ वि विद्वान् द्रवद दूतो देवयावा वनिष्ठः ||
sva1r ṇa vastor uṣasām aroci yajñaṁ tanvānā uśijo na manma | agnir janmāni deva ā vi vidvān dravad dūto devayāvā vaniṣṭhaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sva1r ṇa: Of the heavens
    • vastor: Of the abode
    • uṣasām: Of the dawns
    • aroci: Shining or illuminating
    • yajñaṁ: Sacrifice or offering
    • tanvānā: With a body or form
    • uśijo: The powerful or strong
    • na: Not
    • manma: My mind
    • agnir: Fire or the divine fire
    • janmāni: Births or origins
    • deva: Gods or divine beings
    • ā: Towards
    • vi: Indeed
    • vidvān: Wise or knowledgeable
    • dravad: Flowing or moving
    • dūto: Messenger
    • devayāvā: Of the divine
    • vaniṣṭhaḥ: The best or most excellent
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the divine connection between the heavens and the earthly realm.
    • It emphasizes the importance of sacrifice (yajña) as a means to connect with the divine.
    • The mention of fire (agnir) symbolizes transformation, purification, and the divine presence.
    • The mantra suggests that the wise (vidvān) are those who understand the flow of life and the messages from the divine.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In many cultures, rituals and sacrifices are performed to honor deities, seeking blessings for prosperity and well-being.
    • In modern life, the concept of 'sacrifice' can be seen in acts of charity, where individuals give up their resources for the benefit of others.
    • The idea of transformation through fire can be related to personal growth, where challenges lead to a stronger, more resilient self.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of connecting with the divine through acts of sacrifice and understanding.
    • It teaches us that wisdom comes from recognizing the flow of life and the messages that guide us.
    • In real life, applying these principles can lead to a more fulfilling and harmonious existence, fostering a deeper connection with ourselves and the universe.
