इदं वसो सुतं अन्धः पिबा सुपूर्णम उदरम् |अनाभयिन ररिमा ते ||
idaṁ vaso sutam andhaḥ pibā supūrṇam udaram | anābhayin rarimā te ||
  • idaṁ vaso sutam: This phrase can be translated as "this is the son of Vasu." In Vedic literature, "Vasu" refers to a group of deities associated with wealth and abundance. The term "sutam" means "son" or "offspring." This suggests a connection to prosperity and the blessings that come from divine sources.
  • andhaḥ pibā: "Andhaḥ" means "blind" or "dark," while "pibā" translates to "drink." This part of the mantra can be interpreted as an invitation to consume or absorb something that is hidden or not immediately visible. In a real-life context, this could symbolize the idea of seeking knowledge or wisdom that is not apparent at first glance.
  • supūrṇam udaram: "Supūrṇam" means "well-filled" or "complete," and "udaram" refers to the "belly" or "stomach." Together, this phrase suggests a state of fullness or satisfaction. In practical terms, it can represent the fulfillment of desires or the attainment of goals, akin to achieving a sense of contentment in life.
  • anābhayin rarimā te: "Anābhayin" can be interpreted as "without fear," and "rarimā" means "to be strong" or "to be powerful." "Te" refers to "you." This part of the mantra conveys a message of empowerment and courage, encouraging individuals to embrace their strength without fear. In everyday life, this can be seen in situations where one must confront challenges or uncertainties with confidence.

Conclusion: The mantra "idaṁ vaso sutam andhaḥ pibā supūrṇam udaram | anābhayin rarimā te" encapsulates themes of prosperity, the pursuit of hidden knowledge, fulfillment, and empowerment. Its significance lies in the encouragement to seek abundance and wisdom while embracing one's inner strength without fear. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as personal growth, professional endeavors, and spiritual journeys, reminding individuals to strive for completeness and courage in their pursuits.
