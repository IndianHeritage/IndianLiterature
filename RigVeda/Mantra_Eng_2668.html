ऊर्जो नपातम अध्वरे दीदिवांसम् उप द्यवि |अग्निम ईळे कविक्रतुम् ||
ūrjo napātam adhvare dīdivāṁsam upa dyavi | agnim īḻe kavikratum ||
  • ūrjo: This word translates to "energy" or "vitality." It signifies the life force that sustains all living beings.
  • napātam: This means "descendant" or "offspring." It refers to the generational continuity of energy and life.
  • adhvare: This term is often associated with "sacrifice" or "ritual." It indicates the importance of performing actions with intention and purpose.
  • dīdivāṁsam: This translates to "brilliant" or "radiant." It describes the illuminating quality of the divine or the energy being invoked.
  • upa dyavi: This phrase means "towards the heavens" or "upwards." It signifies the aspiration to connect with higher realms or divine consciousness.
  • agnim: This word means "fire" and symbolizes transformation, purification, and the divine presence in rituals.
  • īḻe: This translates to "I invoke" or "I praise." It indicates an act of reverence and calling upon the divine energy.
  • kavikratum: This term refers to "the creative power of the poet" or "the divine intelligence." It embodies the wisdom and insight that guides actions and thoughts.

The mantra can be interpreted as a call to the divine energy, invoking the brilliance of life and the creative intelligence that guides our actions. It emphasizes the importance of connecting with this energy through purposeful actions and rituals, aiming for a higher understanding and transformation.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios:

  • Personal Growth: Just as the mantra invokes energy and creativity, individuals can harness their inner vitality to pursue personal development and self-improvement.
  • Rituals and Celebrations: The concept of adhvare highlights the significance of rituals in our lives, whether in cultural celebrations or personal milestones, to connect with the divine and the community.
  • Environmental Awareness: The invocation of ūrjo can inspire individuals to respect and nurture the energy of nature, promoting sustainability and ecological balance.

In conclusion, this mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of life, the importance of intention in our actions, and the pursuit of higher consciousness. By invoking the divine energy and creative intelligence, we can transform our lives and contribute positively to the world around us.
