अग्नि दा दाशुषे रयिं वीरवंतं परीनसम् |शिशीहि नः सूनुमतः ||
agne dā dāśuṣe rayiṁ vīravantam parīṇasam | śiśīhi naḥ sūnumataḥ ||
  • Agne: This word refers to Agni, the fire god in Vedic literature. Fire symbolizes purity, energy, and transformation.
  • Dā: This means "give" or "bestow." It is a call for blessings or gifts.
  • Dāśuṣe: This term can be interpreted as "to the one who is worthy" or "to the deserving." It emphasizes the importance of merit and righteousness.
  • Rayiṁ: This translates to "wealth" or "prosperity." It signifies not just material wealth but also spiritual and intellectual richness.
  • Vīravantam: This means "the one who is brave" or "the valiant." It highlights the qualities of courage and strength.
  • Parīṇasam: This can be understood as "full of abundance" or "complete." It suggests a state of fulfillment and completeness.
  • Śiśīhi: This word means "nurture" or "protect." It is a plea for care and safeguarding.
  • Naḥ: This means "us" or "our." It indicates a collective appeal or request.
  • Sūnumataḥ: This translates to "with a good mind" or "with favorable thoughts." It emphasizes the importance of positive intentions and mental clarity.

The mantra can be interpreted as a prayer to Agni, asking for the bestowal of wealth and prosperity to those who are brave and deserving. It seeks protection and nurturing for the collective, with a focus on positive intentions and thoughts.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios:

  • Personal Growth: Individuals can recite this mantra when seeking courage to pursue their goals, emphasizing the importance of bravery and deservingness in achieving success.
  • Community Welfare: It can be used in community gatherings to invoke blessings for collective prosperity and well-being, fostering a sense of unity and shared purpose.
  • Business Ventures: Entrepreneurs may chant this mantra to attract wealth and success in their endeavors, focusing on ethical practices and deservingness.

Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine blessings on those who are brave and deserving. It serves as a reminder of the importance of courage, merit, and positive intentions in achieving prosperity and well-being, both individually and collectively. By invoking Agni's blessings, one can aspire to lead a life filled with abundance and fulfillment.
