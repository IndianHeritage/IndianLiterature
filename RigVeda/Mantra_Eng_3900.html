पपृक्षेण्यम इन्द्र त्वे ह्य ओजो नृम्णानि च नृतमानो अमर्तः |स न एनिं वसवानो रयिं दाह प्रार्यः स्तुषे तुविमघस्य दानम् ||
papṛkṣeṇyam indra tve hy ojo nṛmṇāni ca nṛtamāno amartaḥ | sa na enīṁ vasavāno rayiṁ dāḥ prāryaḥ stuṣe tuvimaghasya dānam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • papṛkṣeṇyam: I seek or I desire.
    • indra: O Indra, the king of gods.
    • tve: You.
    • hy: Indeed.
    • ojo: Strength or energy.
    • nṛmṇāni: The deeds of men.
    • ca: And.
    • nṛtamāno: Not false or deceitful.
    • amartaḥ: Immortal.
    • sa: He or that.
    • na: Not.
    • enīṁ: To lead or guide.
    • vasavāno: The wealth or abundance.
    • rayiṁ: Prosperity or riches.
    • dāḥ: Give.
    • prāryaḥ: Worthy of praise.
    • stuṣe: To be praised.
    • tuvimaghasya: Of the one who is powerful.
    • dānam: Gift or donation.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Indra, the god of rain and thunder, seeking his strength and guidance.
    • The speaker expresses a desire for strength in their actions and seeks to ensure that their deeds are truthful and not deceitful.
    • It emphasizes the importance of integrity in one's actions and the pursuit of immortality through virtuous living.
    • The mantra also calls for the blessings of wealth and prosperity, asking Indra to bestow these gifts upon the devotee.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, this mantra can be applied when seeking guidance in making ethical decisions that require strength and integrity.
    • In personal relationships, it can serve as a reminder to act truthfully and to seek abundance not just in material wealth but in love and support.
    • During challenging times, individuals may recite this mantra to invoke strength and clarity in their actions, ensuring they remain true to their values.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of seeking divine guidance in our endeavors while emphasizing the importance of truthfulness and integrity.
    • It serves as a reminder that true wealth comes from virtuous actions and the blessings of the divine.
    • In real life, it encourages individuals to strive for strength in their character and to seek prosperity through honest means, ultimately leading to a fulfilling and meaningful life.
