भगभक्तस्य ते वयं उद अशेम तवावसाः |मूर्धानं राया आरभे ||
bhagabhaktasya te vayam ud aśema tavāvasā | mūrdhānaṁ rāya ārabhe ||
  • bhagabhaktasya: This term refers to the devotee or follower of the divine. It emphasizes the importance of devotion and surrender to a higher power.
  • te: This word means "to you" or "for you," indicating a sense of offering or dedication towards the divine.
  • vayam: This translates to "we" or "us," suggesting a collective identity or community of devotees.
  • ud aśema: This phrase can be interpreted as "may we attain" or "may we achieve," expressing a desire for spiritual or material fulfillment.
  • tavāvasā: This means "your shelter" or "your protection," highlighting the importance of seeking refuge in the divine.
  • mūrdhānaṁ: This term refers to "the head" or "the top," symbolizing authority, leadership, or the pinnacle of spiritual attainment.
  • rāya: This word means "wealth" or "prosperity," indicating the blessings that come from divine favor.
  • ārabhe: This translates to "I begin" or "I initiate," suggesting the start of a journey or endeavor with divine guidance.

The mantra can be understood as a prayer or invocation where the devotees express their collective desire to attain the blessings and protection of the divine. It emphasizes the importance of devotion and the need for divine guidance in all endeavors.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios:

  • Spiritual Practices: Individuals can recite this mantra during meditation or prayer to seek divine guidance and protection in their spiritual journey.
  • Community Gatherings: Groups can chant this mantra during community events to foster a sense of unity and collective purpose, invoking blessings for the community's well-being.
  • New Beginnings: When starting a new venture, such as a business or project, individuals can use this mantra to seek divine support and ensure a prosperous outcome.

Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its emphasis on devotion, community, and the pursuit of divine blessings. It serves as a reminder that seeking shelter in the divine can lead to prosperity and success in various aspects of life. By incorporating this mantra into daily practices, individuals can cultivate a deeper connection with the divine and navigate life's challenges with faith and assurance.
