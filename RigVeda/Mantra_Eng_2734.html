तं अङ्गिरस्वन् नमसā सपर्णं नव्यं कृत्नोमि संन्यसे पुराजाम् |द्रुहो वि याहि बहुला अदेविः स्वश्च नो मघवन सातये धाः ||
tam aṅgirasvan namasā saparyan navyaṁ kṛṇomi sanyase purājām | druho vi yāhi bahulā adevīḥ svaś ca no maghavan sātaye dhāḥ ||
  • tam aṅgirasvan: This phrase refers to the divine or sacred aspect of the Aṅgiras, a group of sages in Vedic literature known for their wisdom and knowledge.
  • namasā saparyan: "Namasā" means with reverence or salutation, and "saparyan" refers to worship or service. Together, this suggests an act of respectful worship towards the divine.
  • navyaṁ kṛṇomi: "Navyaṁ" means new or fresh, and "kṛṇomi" means I create or I make. This indicates the act of creating something new, possibly in a spiritual or ritualistic context.
  • sanyase purājām: "Sanyase" means to dedicate or offer, and "purājām" refers to the ancient or primordial. This implies a dedication to the ancient wisdom or traditions.
  • druho vi yāhi: "Druho" means enmity or hostility, and "vi yāhi" means to go away or to be removed. This suggests a plea for the removal of negativity or hostility.
  • bahulā adevīḥ: "Bahulā" means abundant or many, and "adevīḥ" refers to the divine or goddesses. This can be interpreted as a call for the presence of many divine energies or blessings.
  • svaś ca no maghavan: "Svaś" means our own or personal, and "maghavan" refers to the one who is bountiful or generous. This indicates a request for personal blessings and abundance.
  • sātaye dhāḥ: "Sātaye" means for the purpose of truth or righteousness, and "dhāḥ" means to bestow or grant. This signifies a desire for the granting of truth and righteousness in life.

Conclusion: This mantra encapsulates a profound spiritual message that emphasizes the importance of reverence, the creation of new beginnings, and the dedication to ancient wisdom. It calls for the removal of negativity and the invocation of divine blessings for personal abundance and truth. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied during rituals, personal meditations, or moments of reflection when seeking guidance, positivity, and a deeper connection to one's spiritual roots. It serves as a reminder to honor the past while embracing the present and future with a sense of purpose and integrity.
