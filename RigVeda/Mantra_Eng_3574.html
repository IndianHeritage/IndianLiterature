बृहस्पत इन्द्र वर्धतं नः सचा सा वां सुमतिर भूत्व अस्मे |अविष्टं धियो जिगृतं पुरंधीर्ज जजस्तम आर्यो वनुषाम अरातिः ||
bṛhaspata indra vardhataṁ naḥ sacā sā vāṁ sumatir bhūtv asme | aviṣṭaṁ dhiyo jigṛtam puraṁdhīr jajastam aryo vanuṣām arātīḥ ||
  • Mantra: "bṛhaspata indra vardhataṁ naḥ sacā sā vāṁ sumatir bhūtv asme | aviṣṭaṁ dhiyo jigṛtam puraṁdhīr jajastam aryo vanuṣām arātīḥ ||"
  • Translation: "May the great lord Indra, the master of wisdom, increase our intelligence and understanding. May we be endowed with good thoughts and may our minds be free from obstacles, leading us to success and prosperity."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • bṛhaspata: Refers to the great lord or master, often associated with wisdom and knowledge.
    • indra: The king of gods, symbolizing strength, power, and leadership.
    • vardhataṁ: To increase or enhance.
    • naḥ: Our or us.
    • sacā: With good or auspiciousness.
    • sā: That or which.
    • vāṁ: You or your.
    • sumatir: Good thoughts or intelligence.
    • bhūtv: Being or becoming.
    • asme: For us.
    • aviṣṭaṁ: Free from obstacles or hindrances.
    • dhiyo: Intellect or understanding.
    • jigṛtam: To be grasped or understood.
    • puraṁdhīr: The one who is wise or knowledgeable.
    • jajastam: To be successful or victorious.
    • aryo: Noble or honorable.
    • vanuṣām: Of humans or mankind.
    • arātīḥ: Obstacles or hindrances.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, this mantra can be invoked during team meetings to encourage collaboration and the generation of innovative ideas, fostering an environment where good thoughts and intelligence thrive.
    • Students can recite this mantra before exams or important presentations to seek clarity of thought and the removal of mental blocks, enhancing their performance.
    • In personal life, individuals may use this mantra during meditation or reflection to cultivate positive thinking and overcome challenges, leading to personal growth and success.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for the enhancement of wisdom, intelligence, and the removal of obstacles. It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from academic pursuits to professional endeavors and personal development. By invoking the blessings of Indra and Bṛhaspati, individuals can aspire to achieve greater clarity of thought, foster good intentions, and navigate through life's challenges with confidence and success.
