प्रत्वक्षसः प्रतवसो विरप्शिनोऽनानता अविथुराऽऋजीषिणः |जुष्टतमासो नृतमासो अञ्जिभिर व्यानज्रे के चिद उष्रा इव स्तृभिः ||
pratvakṣasaḥ pratavaso virapśino 'nānatā avithurā ṛjīṣiṇaḥ | juṣṭatamāso nṛtamāso añjibhir vy ānajre ke cid usrā iva stṛbhiḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • pratvakṣasaḥ: Those who are clear or manifest.
    • pratavaso: Those who are well-formed or well-structured.
    • virapśino: Those who are powerful or strong.
    • ānānatā: Without any limitations or boundaries.
    • avithurā: Those who are expansive or vast.
    • ṛjīṣiṇaḥ: Those who are righteous or just.
    • juṣṭatamāso: Those who are most pleasing or delightful.
    • nṛtamāso: Those who are truthful or honest.
    • añjibhir: With the help of others or through collaboration.
    • vy ānajre: They are protected or safeguarded.
    • ke cid: Anyone or everyone.
    • usrā: Like the sun or radiant.
    • iva: Like or as.
    • stṛbhiḥ: With the help of the stars or celestial bodies.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of individuals who embody clarity, strength, and righteousness.
    • It emphasizes the importance of collaboration and support from others in achieving greatness.
    • The imagery of the sun and stars suggests that these qualities shine brightly and guide others.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace, a leader who is clear in their vision and strong in their decisions can inspire their team to work collaboratively towards a common goal.
    • In community service, individuals who are righteous and truthful can lead initiatives that benefit society, drawing support from others to amplify their impact.
    • In personal relationships, being honest and supportive can create a nurturing environment where everyone feels valued and empowered.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of embodying clarity, strength, and righteousness in our lives.
    • It teaches us the value of collaboration and the impact of our actions on others.
    • By striving to be clear, strong, and truthful, we can create a positive influence in our communities and relationships, much like the sun and stars that guide us.
