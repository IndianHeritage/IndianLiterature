अच्छ ṛषे मारुतं गणं दाना मित्रं न योषणा |दिवो वा धृष्णव ओजसा स्तुतā धीभिर इषण्यत ||
accha ṛṣe mārutaṁ gaṇaṁ dānā mitraṁ na yoṣaṇā | divo vā dhṛṣṇava ojasā stutā dhībhir iṣaṇyata ||
  • accha ṛṣe: This phrase can be translated as "O good sage" or "O wise seer." It addresses a revered figure, acknowledging their wisdom and insight.
  • mārutaṁ gaṇaṁ: "Māruta" refers to the winds or the forces of nature, while "gaṇaṁ" means a group or assembly. Together, this can be understood as "the assembly of the winds" or "the forces of nature."
  • dānā mitraṁ: "Dānā" means "gift" or "offering," and "mitraṁ" translates to "friend." This phrase suggests the idea of friendship through giving or the importance of generosity in relationships.
  • na yoṣaṇā: This can be interpreted as "not through deceit" or "without falsehood." It emphasizes the value of honesty and integrity in interactions.
  • divo vā: "Divo" means "of the sky" or "heaven," and "vā" translates to "or." This phrase can imply a connection to the divine or celestial realms.
  • dhṛṣṇava ojasā: "Dhṛṣṇava" refers to "those who are strong" or "powerful," while "ojasā" means "with energy" or "vigor." This suggests a call for strength and vitality.
  • stutā dhībhir: "Stutā" means "praised" or "worshipped," and "dhībhir" refers to "with wisdom" or "intellect." This indicates that the wise should be honored and revered.
  • iṣaṇyata: This term can be understood as "may it be fulfilled" or "may it come to pass." It expresses a desire for the intentions or prayers to be realized.

Conclusion: This mantra emphasizes the importance of wisdom, strength, and integrity in our lives. It calls upon the forces of nature and the divine, highlighting the significance of friendship and generosity. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various contexts, such as fostering honest relationships, seeking strength in challenging times, and recognizing the value of wisdom in decision-making. By embodying these principles, individuals can cultivate a harmonious and fulfilling life, contributing positively to their communities and the world around them.
