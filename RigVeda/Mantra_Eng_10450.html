यद इन्द्र ब्रह्मणस पते 'भिद्रोहं चरामसि |प्रचेताः न आङ्गिरसो द्विषतां पात्व अंहसः ||
yad indra brahmaṇas pate 'bhidrohaṁ carāmasi | pracetā na āṅgiraso dviṣatām pātv aṁhasaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yad: whatever
    • indra: Indra, the king of gods
    • brahmaṇas: of the Brahman, the ultimate reality or cosmic spirit
    • pate: O lord
    • 'bhidrohaṁ: I do not harbor enmity
    • carāmasi: we move or we act
    • pracetā: with wisdom or intelligence
    • na: not
    • āṅgiraso: of the Angiras, a group of sages
    • dviṣatām: of the enemies
    • pātv: may we be free
    • aṁhasaḥ: from the darkness or ignorance
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra expresses a plea for protection and guidance from the divine, specifically invoking Indra, the king of gods, and the Brahman, the ultimate reality.
    • The speaker asserts that they do not harbor enmity, indicating a desire for peace and harmony in their actions.
    • By invoking wisdom and the guidance of the sages (Angiras), the mantra emphasizes the importance of knowledge and understanding in overcoming ignorance and hostility.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace setting, this mantra can be applied when team members strive to work collaboratively without harboring ill feelings towards each other, promoting a positive work environment.
    • In personal relationships, the essence of this mantra encourages individuals to resolve conflicts peacefully and to seek understanding rather than confrontation.
    • In community settings, it can inspire collective efforts to overcome challenges, emphasizing unity and wisdom in addressing social issues.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for peace, wisdom, and the rejection of enmity. It serves as a reminder that in our actions, we should strive for harmony and understanding.
    • In real-life scenarios, applying the principles of this mantra can lead to healthier relationships, more effective teamwork, and a more cohesive community.
    • Ultimately, it encourages individuals to seek knowledge and wisdom as a means to navigate through life's challenges, fostering a spirit of cooperation and goodwill.
