वायो याहि शिवा दिवो वहस्वा सु स्वश्व्यम् |वहस्व महः पृथुपक्षसां रथे ||
vāyo yāhi śivā divo vahasvā su svaśvyam | vahasva mahaḥ pṛthupakṣasā rathe ||
  • vāyo: This word translates to "O Wind" or "O Vayu," referring to the deity of wind in Vedic literature. In real life, wind is a powerful natural force that can symbolize change and movement.
  • yāhi: This means "come" or "approach." It is an invitation or a call to the wind to come forth, which can be seen as a metaphor for welcoming change or new opportunities in life.
  • śivā: This word means "auspicious" or "good." It signifies positivity and well-being. In everyday life, this can relate to seeking harmony and good fortune in our endeavors.
  • divo: This translates to "of the sky" or "heavenly." It connects the mantra to the celestial realm, suggesting a higher power or divine influence in our lives.
  • vahasvā: This means "carry" or "bear." It implies the act of transporting or bringing forth something significant, akin to how the wind carries seeds for new growth.
  • su: This translates to "good" or "well." It emphasizes the positive aspects of what is being carried or brought forth.
  • svaśvyam: This means "your own" or "self." It suggests a personal connection to the blessings or good fortune being sought.
  • mahaḥ: This translates to "great" or "mighty." It signifies the importance and magnitude of the blessings or forces at play.
  • pṛthupakṣasā: This means "with broad wings." It symbolizes freedom and the ability to soar high, much like birds that can navigate vast skies.
  • rathe: This translates to "in the chariot." The chariot is often a symbol of movement and progress, representing the journey of life and the pursuit of goals.

Conclusion: The mantra "vāyo yāhi śivā divo vahasvā su svaśvyam | vahasva mahaḥ pṛthupakṣasā rathe ||" calls upon the wind to bring forth auspiciousness and blessings from the heavens. It signifies the importance of welcoming change and seeking positivity in our lives. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied when embarking on new ventures, making significant life changes, or seeking guidance and support from higher powers. It encourages individuals to embrace the journey of life with an open heart and mind, allowing the winds of fortune to guide them towards their goals.
