गमन्यस्मे वसून्याहि शंसिषं स्वाशिषं भरमायाहि सोमिनः |त्वम ईशिषे सास्मिन्नासत्सि बरहिष्यानाधृष्याऽतव पात्राणि धर्मणा ||
gamann asme vasūny ā hi śaṁsiṣaṁ svāśiṣam bharam ā yāhi sominaḥ | tvam īśiṣe sāsminn ā satsi barhiṣy anādhṛṣyā tava pātrāṇi dharmaṇā ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • gamann: going or moving
    • asme: we are
    • vasūny: to the wealth or abundance
    • ā hi: indeed
    • śaṁsiṣaṁ: to the one who brings peace or prosperity
    • svāśiṣam: with auspicious blessings
    • bharam: bring or carry
    • ā yāhi: come to us
    • sominaḥ: of the Soma (a sacred drink)
    • tvam: you
    • īśiṣe: the ruler or lord
    • sāsminn: in this
    • ā satsi: in the assembly
    • barhiṣy: in the sacred space
    • anādhṛṣyā: unassailable or invincible
    • tava: your
    • pātrāṇi: vessels or offerings
    • dharmaṇā: by righteousness or duty
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the divine presence, asking for the movement towards abundance and prosperity.
    • It emphasizes the importance of peace and auspiciousness in our lives, suggesting that these qualities should be brought to us.
    • The reference to Soma indicates a connection to spiritual nourishment and the sacred, highlighting the importance of rituals and offerings in achieving harmony.
    • The mantra also acknowledges the role of a ruler or divine force in guiding and protecting the community, emphasizing the need for leadership that is righteous and just.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, this mantra can be related to the importance of leadership that fosters a positive and prosperous work environment.
    • In personal life, it can be seen as a call to seek peace and abundance through righteous actions and community support.
    • During festivals or rituals, invoking this mantra can enhance the spiritual atmosphere, promoting unity and shared blessings among participants.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the quest for abundance, peace, and righteousness in our lives.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal growth to community building and leadership.
    • By embodying the principles of this mantra, individuals and communities can strive towards a harmonious and prosperous existence.
