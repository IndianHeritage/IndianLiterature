उद अस्य शोचिर अस्ताद दीदियुषो व्य अजरम् |तपुरजम्भस्य सुद्युतो गणश्रियः ||
ud asya śocir asthād dīdiyuṣo vy a1jaram | tapurjambhasya sudyuto gaṇaśriyaḥ ||
  • ud asya: This phrase can be interpreted as "from above" or "from the heights." It signifies a divine or elevated source of energy or inspiration.
  • śocir: This word means "radiant" or "brilliant." It suggests a quality of brightness or enlightenment, often associated with knowledge and wisdom.
  • asthād: This term translates to "has established" or "has set." It indicates a sense of permanence or stability in the context of the mantra.
  • dīdiyuṣo: This word refers to "the shining" or "the luminous." It emphasizes the idea of illumination, both in a physical and metaphorical sense.
  • vy a1jaram: This phrase can be understood as "the unchanging" or "the eternal." It points to the idea of something that remains constant despite the changes around it.
  • tapurjambhasya: This term can be interpreted as "of the one who is full of energy" or "of the powerful." It refers to a source of strength and vitality.
  • sudyuto: This word means "brilliant" or "shining." It reinforces the theme of radiance and excellence.
  • gaṇaśriyaḥ: This phrase translates to "the glory of the groups" or "the splendor of the collective." It highlights the importance of community and collaboration in achieving greatness.

In conclusion, this mantra emphasizes the significance of divine inspiration, radiance, and the unchanging nature of truth and knowledge. It suggests that true brilliance comes from a higher source and is established in a stable manner. The mantra also highlights the importance of collective strength and energy, indicating that greatness is often achieved through collaboration and unity.


In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as:

  • Personal Development: Individuals can seek inspiration from higher ideals and strive for enlightenment in their personal and professional lives.
  • Community Building: The emphasis on collective glory encourages teamwork and collaboration in organizations, fostering a sense of unity and shared purpose.
  • Spiritual Growth: The mantra serves as a reminder to connect with the eternal and unchanging truths of existence, guiding individuals on their spiritual journeys.
