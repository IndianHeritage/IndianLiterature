प्र ब्रह्माणो अङ्गिरसो नक्षन्त प्र क्रन्दनुर नभन्यस्य वेतु |प्र धेनव उदप्रुतो नवन्त युज्यताम अद्री अध्वरस्य पेषः ||
pra brahmāṇo aṅgiraso nakṣanta pra krandanur nabhanyasya vetu | pra dhenava udapruto navanta yujyātām adrī adhvarasya peśaḥ ||
  • pra brahmāṇo: This phrase refers to the supreme reality or the ultimate truth, often associated with the concept of Brahman in Hindu philosophy. It signifies the source of all creation and existence.
  • aṅgiraso: Aṅgiras is a sage and a symbol of knowledge and wisdom. This term emphasizes the importance of wisdom in understanding the ultimate truth.
  • nakṣanta: This word can be interpreted as "to shine" or "to illuminate." It suggests the idea of enlightenment and the pursuit of knowledge that leads to understanding the divine.
  • pra krandanur: This can be understood as "to cry out" or "to call." It indicates a yearning or a deep desire for connection with the divine or the ultimate truth.
  • nabhanyasya vetu: This phrase refers to the vastness of the sky or the universe. It symbolizes the infinite nature of existence and the divine.
  • pra dhenava: This term refers to "the cows" or "nourishment." In a broader sense, it symbolizes abundance and the sustenance provided by nature.
  • udapruto: This can be interpreted as "to rise" or "to emerge." It signifies the growth and flourishing of life and knowledge.
  • navanta: This word means "new" or "fresh." It represents renewal and the continuous cycle of life and learning.
  • yujyātām: This term suggests "to be united" or "to come together." It emphasizes the importance of unity and harmony in the pursuit of knowledge and truth.
  • adrī adhvarasya peśaḥ: This phrase can be understood as "the path of the mountains" or "the journey of life." It symbolizes the challenges and experiences one faces in the quest for understanding and enlightenment.

Conclusion: This mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking knowledge and understanding the ultimate truth. It highlights the importance of wisdom, unity, and the continuous journey of life. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied to various aspects such as education, personal growth, and spiritual development. It encourages individuals to seek enlightenment, embrace the abundance of life, and work towards unity and harmony in their pursuits. The significance of this mantra lies in its ability to inspire individuals to connect with the divine and recognize the interconnectedness of all existence.
