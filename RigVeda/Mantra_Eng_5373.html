हवँ त इन्द्र महिमा व्य आनड ब्रह्म यत पासि शवसिन ऋषीणाम् |आ यद वज्रं दधिषे हस्त उग्र घोरः सन् क्रत्वा जनिष्ठा अशाल्हः ||
havaṁ ta indra mahimā vy ānaḍ brahma yat pāsi śavasinn ṛṣīṇām | ā yad vajraṁ dadhiṣe hasta ugra ghoraḥ san kratvā janiṣṭhā aṣāḻhaḥ ||
  • Havaṁ ta indra mahimā vy ānaḍ: This phrase can be translated as "O Indra, your greatness is indeed vast." Indra is a prominent deity in the Rigveda, often associated with rain, thunder, and strength. This line acknowledges the immense power and glory of Indra, which can be likened to the awe we feel in the presence of natural phenomena like thunderstorms or majestic mountains.
  • Brahma yat pāsi śavasinn ṛṣīṇām: This translates to "You protect the sages and their wisdom." Here, 'Brahma' refers to the ultimate reality or cosmic spirit, while 'ṛṣīṇām' refers to the sages or seers. This line emphasizes the importance of wisdom and knowledge in our lives, akin to how we rely on teachers and mentors for guidance in our personal and professional journeys.
  • Ā yad vajraṁ dadhiṣe hasta ugra: This means "You wield the thunderbolt in your powerful hand." The 'vajra' symbolizes strength and indestructibility. In real life, this can be compared to the resilience and determination we need to face challenges, much like a person overcoming obstacles in their career or personal life with unwavering strength.
  • Ghoraḥ san kratvā janiṣṭhā aṣāḻhaḥ: This translates to "You, fierce and mighty, create the world." This line reflects the creative power of Indra, which can be related to the human capacity for innovation and creation. For instance, scientists and artists alike harness their creativity to bring new ideas and inventions into the world, shaping our future.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the reverence for divine power, wisdom, and creativity. It signifies the importance of acknowledging greater forces in our lives, whether they be spiritual, natural, or intellectual. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire individuals to seek strength in adversity, value wisdom from mentors, and embrace their creative potential. By recognizing the interconnectedness of these elements, we can navigate our lives with purpose and resilience, much like the sages who sought knowledge and strength from the divine.
