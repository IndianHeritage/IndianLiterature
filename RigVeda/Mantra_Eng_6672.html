मन्त्र: पुरोळाशं यो अस्मै सोमं ररत आशीर्म् |पाद इति तं शक्रो अम्हसः ||
puroḻāśaṁ yo asmai somaṁ rarata āśiram | pād it taṁ śakro aṁhasaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • puroḻāśaṁ: Refers to the one who is endowed with strength or vigor.
    • yo: Who.
    • asmai: To him or for him.
    • somaṁ: Soma, a sacred drink that symbolizes immortality and divine inspiration.
    • rarata: May he enjoy or may he relish.
    • āśiram: Blessing or prosperity.
    • pād: Foot or step.
    • it: Thus or in this way.
    • taṁ: That.
    • śakro: The powerful one, often referring to Indra, the king of gods.
    • aṁhasaḥ: Of the light or brilliance.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the blessings of strength and prosperity.
    • It emphasizes the importance of divine inspiration (soma) in achieving success.
    • The reference to Indra suggests that one should seek the support of powerful forces in their endeavors.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, this mantra can be applied when seeking success in a project. One might invoke the strength of teamwork and the inspiration of innovative ideas (soma) to achieve their goals.
    • In personal life, individuals may recite this mantra when embarking on a new journey, such as starting a family or moving to a new city, seeking blessings for strength and prosperity.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of seeking divine blessings for strength and prosperity in all aspects of life.
    • It is applicable in various scenarios, from personal growth to professional success, reminding us to harness both inner strength and external support.
    • By invoking the essence of this mantra, individuals can cultivate a mindset that embraces challenges and seeks inspiration, ultimately leading to a fulfilling and prosperous life.
