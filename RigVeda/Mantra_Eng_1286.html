आस्नो वृषस्य वर्तिकाम् अभीक्य युवं नराः नासत्यामुमुक्तम् |उतो कविम पुरुभुजाः युवं ह कृत्पमाणम् अकृतुतं विचक्षे ||
āsno vṛkasya vartikām abhīke yuvaṁ narā nāsatyāmumuktam | uto kavim purubhujā yuvaṁ ha kṛpamāṇam akṛṇutaṁ vicakṣe ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • āsno: of the one who is strong or powerful
    • vṛkasya: of the wolf
    • vartikām: the one who is active or engaged
    • abhīke: in the presence of
    • yuvaṁ: young or youthful
    • narā: men or humans
    • nāsatyām: in the realm of truth or reality
    • umuktam: released or liberated
    • uto: and
    • kavim: the wise or the poet
    • purubhujā: the one who enjoys many things
    • yuvaṁ: young or youthful
    • ha: indeed
    • kṛpamāṇam: the one who is compassionate
    • akṛṇutaṁ: not to be harmed or injured
    • vicakṣe: with wisdom or discernment
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the strength and vitality of youth, symbolized by the wolf, which is often seen as a powerful and active creature in nature.
    • The reference to "nāsatyām" suggests a connection to truth and reality, emphasizing the importance of being grounded in truth while pursuing one's goals.
    • The mention of "kavim purubhujā" highlights the role of wisdom and creativity in navigating life's challenges, suggesting that those who are wise and compassionate can enjoy the fruits of life without harm.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, a young entrepreneur may embody the qualities of strength and wisdom, navigating the challenges of starting a business with creativity and compassion.
    • In personal relationships, being grounded in truth and showing compassion can lead to healthier and more fulfilling connections with others.
    • In education, students who approach their studies with a sense of curiosity and discernment can achieve greater understanding and success.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of strength, wisdom, and compassion in our lives.
    • It encourages individuals to embrace their youthful energy while remaining grounded in truth and using their creativity to navigate life's complexities.
    • In real-life scenarios, applying the principles of this mantra can lead to personal growth, successful relationships, and a fulfilling life.
