सत्वा भरिषो गविषो दुवन्यसच श्रवस्याद इष उषसस्तुरण्यसत् |सत्यो द्रवो द्रवरः पतङ्गरो दधिक्रावेषम् ऊर्जं स्वर जनत ||
satvā bhariṣo gaviṣo duvanyasac chravasyād iṣa uṣasas turaṇyasat | satyo dravo dravaraḥ pataṁgaro dadhikrāveṣam ūrjaṁ svar janat ||
  • satvā bhariṣo gaviṣo: This phrase can be interpreted as "the strength of the mind and body." In real life, this emphasizes the importance of mental and physical well-being. For example, athletes often focus on both physical training and mental resilience to achieve peak performance.
  • duvanyasac chravasyād: This can be understood as "the sound of the divine or the call of the universe." It suggests that there is a higher power or universal truth that guides us. In everyday life, this can be seen in moments of inspiration or intuition that lead individuals to make significant life choices.
  • iṣa uṣasas turaṇyasat: This translates to "the essence of dawn and the arrival of new beginnings." It symbolizes hope and renewal. For instance, every new day offers a chance to start afresh, much like how people often set resolutions at the beginning of a new year.
  • satyo dravo dravaraḥ: This phrase means "truth is the essence of all that flows." It highlights the importance of honesty and integrity in our actions. In real life, businesses that operate transparently and ethically tend to build stronger relationships with their customers and communities.
  • pataṁgaro dadhikrāveṣam: This can be interpreted as "the swift movement of the wind or energy." It signifies the dynamic nature of life and the need to adapt. For example, in the face of challenges, individuals and organizations must be agile and responsive to change to thrive.
  • ūrjaṁ svar janat: This translates to "the energy of the universe is within us." It suggests that we all possess the potential to harness universal energy for our growth and success. In practical terms, this can be seen in practices like meditation and yoga, which help individuals connect with their inner strength and vitality.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of strength, renewal, truth, adaptability, and inner energy. Its significance lies in its application to everyday life, encouraging individuals to cultivate mental and physical well-being, embrace new beginnings, act with integrity, adapt to change, and harness their inner potential. By embodying these principles, one can navigate life's challenges more effectively and lead a fulfilling life.
