एभिर द्युभिः सुमना एभिर इन्दुभिः निरुन्धानो अमतिं गोभिः अश्विनाः | इन्द्रेण दस्युं दारयन्त इन्दुभिः युतद्वेषसः सम इषा रभेमहि ||
ebhir dyubhiḥ sumanā ebhir indubhir nirundhāno amatiṁ gobhir aśvinā | indreṇa dasyuṁ darayanta indubhir yutadveṣasaḥ sam iṣā rabhemahi ||
  • ebhir dyubhiḥ: This phrase can be interpreted as "with the support of the heavens" or "with the help of the celestial bodies." It signifies the importance of divine assistance in our endeavors.
  • sumanā: This word means "with a good mind" or "with a positive attitude." It emphasizes the significance of mental clarity and positivity in achieving success.
  • ebhir indubhir: Here, "indubhir" refers to the "moons" or "lunar energies." This can symbolize the nurturing and calming aspects of life, suggesting that we should draw upon these qualities in our actions.
  • nirundhāno amatiṁ: This phrase translates to "removing obstacles" or "overcoming ignorance." It highlights the need to clear away confusion and barriers that hinder our progress.
  • gobhir aśvinā: "Gobhih" refers to "cows," which are often seen as symbols of wealth and nourishment in Vedic literature. "Aśvinā" refers to the twin deities associated with health and medicine, indicating the importance of well-being and prosperity.
  • indreṇa dasyuṁ darayanta: This can be interpreted as "with Indra, we conquer the enemies." Indra is the king of the gods and symbolizes strength and power, suggesting that divine support is crucial in overcoming challenges.
  • indubhir yutadveṣasaḥ: This phrase suggests "with the lunar energies, we overcome hatred." It emphasizes the need for compassion and understanding in our interactions with others.
  • sam iṣā rabhemahi: This translates to "may we attain our desires together." It signifies the importance of unity and collective effort in achieving goals.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking divine support, maintaining a positive mindset, and overcoming obstacles in life. It emphasizes the importance of unity and compassion in our endeavors. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as teamwork in professional settings, fostering harmonious relationships in communities, and promoting mental well-being. By invoking the principles outlined in this mantra, individuals can navigate challenges more effectively and work towards collective success.
