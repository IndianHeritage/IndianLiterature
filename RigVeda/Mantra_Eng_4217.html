युवं नो येषु वरुण क्षत्रं बृहच्च बिभृतः |उरु णो वाजसातये कृतं राये स्वस्तये ||
yuvaṁ no yeṣu varuṇa kṣatram bṛhac ca bibhṛthaḥ | uru ṇo vājasātaye kṛtaṁ rāye svastaye ||
  • Mantra: "yuvaṁ no yeṣu varuṇa kṣatram bṛhac ca bibhṛthaḥ | uru ṇo vājasātaye kṛtaṁ rāye svastaye ||"
  • Translation: "O Varuna, may the young (yuvaṁ) who possess great power (kṣatram) and strength (bṛhac) protect us. May we be blessed with abundance (rāye) and prosperity (svastaye) through our efforts (vājasātaye)."

  • Word Breakdown:
    • Yuvaṁ: Refers to youth or young individuals, symbolizing energy, potential, and new beginnings.
    • Varuṇa: A deity associated with cosmic order and moral authority, representing the law of nature and the universe.
    • Kṣatram: Denotes power or strength, often associated with leadership and protection.
    • Bṛhac: Means great or vast, indicating something of significant magnitude or importance.
    • Uru: Translates to wide or expansive, suggesting abundance and generosity.
    • Vājasātaye: Refers to efforts or endeavors that lead to success and victory.
    • Rāye: Signifies wealth or prosperity, often linked to material and spiritual abundance.
    • Svastaye: Represents well-being or auspiciousness, indicating a state of health and happiness.

  • Real-Life Examples:
    • Empowerment of Youth: The mantra emphasizes the importance of empowering young individuals in society. For instance, initiatives that support youth education and entrepreneurship can lead to a more prosperous community.
    • Leadership and Protection: In a corporate setting, strong leadership is crucial for guiding teams towards success. Leaders who embody the qualities of strength and moral authority can inspire their teams to achieve great results.
    • Community Prosperity: Community development programs that focus on collective efforts can lead to increased prosperity. For example, cooperative farming initiatives can enhance food security and economic stability in rural areas.

  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a reminder of the significance of youth, strength, and collective efforts in achieving prosperity and well-being. It highlights the role of moral authority and leadership in guiding individuals and communities towards a successful and abundant life. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire initiatives that empower the youth, promote ethical leadership, and foster community collaboration, ultimately leading to a harmonious and prosperous society.
