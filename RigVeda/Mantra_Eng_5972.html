मां नो रक्षो अभि नड यातुमावताम अपोच्छतु मिथुना या किमीदिना |पृथिवी नः पार्थिवात पात्व अंहसो 'न्तरिक्षं दिव्यात पात्व अस्मान ||
mā no rakṣo abhi naḍ yātumāvatām apocchatu mithunā yā kimīdinā | pṛthivī naḥ pārthivāt pātv aṁhaso 'ntarikṣaṁ divyāt pātv asmān ||
  • mā no rakṣo abhi naḍ yātumāvatām apocchatu mithunā yā kimīdinā
    • mā no rakṣo: "May no evil or harm come to us." This phrase emphasizes the desire for protection from negative influences or forces.
    • abhi naḍ yātumāvatām: "Let not the evil spirits approach us." This reflects a plea for safeguarding against malevolent entities.
    • apocchatu mithunā yā kimīdinā: "Let them not disturb our unity." This part highlights the importance of harmony and togetherness, urging that no external forces disrupt our collective strength.
  • pṛthivī naḥ pārthivāt pātv aṁhaso 'ntarikṣaṁ divyāt pātv asmān
    • pṛthivī naḥ: "May the Earth be with us." This signifies a connection to the physical world and the importance of our environment.
    • pārthivāt pātv: "May we be nourished by the Earth." This suggests a reliance on nature for sustenance and well-being.
    • aṁhaso 'ntarikṣaṁ: "May the space above us be filled with light." This indicates a yearning for enlightenment and higher understanding.
    • divyāt pātv asmān: "May we be blessed by the divine." This expresses a desire for spiritual guidance and blessings from higher powers.

Conclusion:

The mantra encapsulates a profound plea for protection, unity, and nourishment from both the Earth and the divine. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various contexts such as:

  • Community Gatherings: When people come together for festivals or important events, invoking this mantra can foster a sense of unity and protection against negative influences.
  • Environmental Awareness: The emphasis on the Earth reminds us of our responsibility to care for the environment, promoting sustainability and respect for nature.
  • Spiritual Practices: In meditation or prayer, this mantra can be used to seek guidance and blessings, enhancing one's spiritual journey.

Overall, the significance of this mantra lies in its holistic approach to life, emphasizing the interconnectedness of protection, unity, and spiritual nourishment.
