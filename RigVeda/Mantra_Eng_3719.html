इति चिन मन्युम अध्रिजस् त्वादातम् आ पशुं ददॆ |आद अग्नॆ अपृणतो 'त्रिः सासह्याद दस्यून इषः सासह्यान नृन ||
iti cin manyum adhrijas tvādātam ā paśuṁ dade | ād agne apṛṇato 'triḥ sāsahyād dasyūn iṣaḥ sāsahyān nṝn ||
  • iti cin manyum: This phrase can be translated as "thus, if I think with my mind." It emphasizes the importance of intention and thought in our actions. In real life, this can be related to the idea that our thoughts shape our reality. For example, a person who maintains a positive mindset is more likely to attract positive experiences.
  • adhrijas tvādātam: This means "you are the one who is unshakable." It refers to a source of strength and stability. In everyday life, this can be likened to a mentor or a leader who remains calm and composed during crises, providing guidance and support to others.
  • ā paśuṁ dade: This translates to "may I give to the creature." It signifies the act of offering or sharing. In practical terms, this can be seen in charitable actions, where individuals or organizations provide help to those in need, such as donating food to the homeless or supporting animal shelters.
  • ād agne apṛṇato 'triḥ: This phrase means "O Agni, the one who is invoked." Agni represents fire and is often seen as a purifying force. In real life, this can be related to the idea of seeking enlightenment or knowledge, akin to how people pursue education or spiritual growth to illuminate their paths.
  • sāsahyād dasyūn: This translates to "may I overcome the enemies." It speaks to the idea of overcoming obstacles or adversaries. In a modern context, this can be related to personal challenges, such as overcoming fears or societal pressures, and striving for success despite difficulties.
  • iṣaḥ sāsahyān nṝn: This means "may I be strong against the wicked." It emphasizes resilience and strength in the face of adversity. In real life, this can be seen in individuals who stand up against injustice or discrimination, advocating for their rights and the rights of others.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates themes of intention, strength, sharing, purification, overcoming obstacles, and resilience. Its significance lies in the reminder that our thoughts and actions can create a positive impact in our lives and the lives of others. In real-life scenarios, it encourages individuals to cultivate a strong mindset, engage in acts of kindness, seek knowledge, and stand firm against challenges. By embodying these principles, one can contribute to a more harmonious and just society.
