मन्त्र: असौ यः पन्था आदित्यो दिवि प्रवाच्यं कृतः | न स देवाः अतिक्रमेत् तं मर्तासो न पश्यथ वित्तं मे अस्य रोदसी ||
asau yaḥ panthā ādityo divi pravācyaṁ kṛtaḥ | na sa devā atikrame tam martāso na paśyatha vittam me asya rodasī ||
  • Mantra: "asau yaḥ panthā ādityo divi pravācyaṁ kṛtaḥ | na sa devā atikrame tam martāso na paśyatha vittam me asya rodasī ||"
  • Translation: "This path, which is declared by the Sun in the heavens, should not be transgressed by the gods; mortals should not see my wealth in this world."
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • asau: this
    • yaḥ: who/which
    • panthā: path
    • ādityo: the Sun
    • divi: in the heavens
    • pravācyaṁ: declared/announced
    • kṛtaḥ: made/created
    • na: not
    • sa: that
    • devā: gods
    • atikrame: transgress
    • tam: that
    • martāso: mortals
    • na: not
    • paśyatha: see
    • vittam: wealth
    • me: my
    • asya: this
    • rodasī: world
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of following a righteous path, as illuminated by the Sun, which symbolizes knowledge, truth, and enlightenment.
    • The phrase "should not be transgressed by the gods" suggests that even divine beings must adhere to certain principles and paths, indicating the universality of moral laws.
    • The mention of "mortals should not see my wealth" reflects a sense of humility and the idea that material wealth should not be the ultimate goal of life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, a leader may follow ethical practices and transparency, akin to following the path of the Sun, ensuring that their actions are just and beneficial for all stakeholders.
    • In personal life, individuals may choose to live simply and focus on spiritual growth rather than accumulating material possessions, embodying the essence of the mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder to adhere to a righteous path in life, guided by wisdom and truth.
    • Its significance lies in promoting ethical behavior and humility, applicable in various aspects of life, including personal relationships, professional conduct, and spiritual pursuits.
    • By following this mantra, individuals can cultivate a life of integrity, leading to personal fulfillment and a positive impact on society.
