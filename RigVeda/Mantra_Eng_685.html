मन्त्र: दिवश्चित्ते बृहतो जातवेदो वैश्वानर प्र रिरिचे महित्वम् | राजा कृष्तीनाम् असि मानवशीनां युधा देविभ्यो वरिवश चकर्थ ||
divaś cit te bṛhato jātavedo vaiśvānara pra ririce mahitvam | rājā kṛṣṭīnām asi mānuṣīṇāṁ yudhā devebhyo varivaś cakartha ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • Divaś: Of the heavens
    • Cit: Knowledge or consciousness
    • Te: Your
    • Bṛhato: Of the great or vast
    • Jātavedo: O fire (or the divine knowledge) that knows all births
    • Vaiśvānara: Universal or cosmic
    • Pra ririce: May you bestow
    • Mahitvam: Greatness or glory
    • Rājā: King or ruler
    • Kṛṣṭīnām: Of the cultivated or the people
    • Asi: You are
    • Mānuṣīṇāṁ: Of humans
    • Yudhā: In battle or conflict
    • Devebhyo: To the gods
    • Varivaś: Bestowing or granting
    • Cakartha: You have done or accomplished
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the divine fire, Jātavedo, representing the universal consciousness and knowledge.
    • It emphasizes the importance of seeking greatness and glory in our endeavors, particularly in the context of human struggles and conflicts.
    • The reference to "king of the cultivated" suggests that leadership and guidance are essential in navigating life's challenges.
    • It acknowledges the role of divine forces in granting success and victory in our pursuits.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, a leader may invoke this mantra to inspire their team to strive for excellence and overcome challenges in a competitive market.
    • In personal life, individuals facing difficulties may reflect on this mantra to seek inner strength and guidance from a higher power.
    • During community events or social movements, this mantra can serve as a reminder of the collective strength and the importance of unity in achieving common goals.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine assistance in achieving greatness and success in human endeavors.
    • It serves as a reminder that while we strive for our goals, we are also part of a larger cosmic order that supports our efforts.
    • In real life, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, from personal challenges to collective efforts, emphasizing the importance of seeking guidance and strength from the divine.
