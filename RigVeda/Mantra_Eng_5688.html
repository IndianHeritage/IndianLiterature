तद् वो अद्य मनामहे सूक्तैः सूर उदिते |यद् ओहते वरुणो मित्रो आर्यमा युयम् ऋतस्य रथ्यः ||
tad vo adya manāmahe sūktaiḥ sūra udite | yad ohate varuṇo mitro aryamā yūyam ṛtasya rathyaḥ ||
  • tad vo adya manāmahe: This phrase translates to "We meditate upon that today." It signifies the importance of focusing our thoughts and intentions on a higher power or truth. In real life, this can be related to setting daily intentions or goals, where individuals take a moment to reflect on what they wish to achieve or align with.
  • sūktaiḥ sūra udite: This means "with hymns, the brave one rises." It emphasizes the power of words and prayers in uplifting one's spirit and courage. For example, athletes often use motivational speeches or mantras to boost their confidence before competitions, illustrating how positive affirmations can lead to success.
  • yad ohate varuṇo mitro aryamā: This translates to "that which Varuna, Mitra, and Aryama uphold." Varuna represents cosmic order, Mitra symbolizes friendship and harmony, and Aryama signifies the path of righteousness. Together, they embody the principles of justice, unity, and moral integrity. In everyday life, this can be seen in community efforts where individuals come together to support justice and harmony, such as in social movements or community service.
  • yūyam ṛtasya rathyaḥ: This means "you are the charioteers of truth." It suggests that individuals have the responsibility to steer their lives towards truth and righteousness. In practical terms, this can be related to ethical decision-making in business or personal life, where one must navigate challenges while adhering to their values and principles.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of meditation, the power of words, the importance of upholding justice and harmony, and the responsibility of individuals to pursue truth. In real life, it serves as a reminder to set intentions, use positive affirmations, engage in community support, and make ethical choices. By embodying these principles, individuals can contribute to a more harmonious and just society, aligning their actions with higher ideals. This mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as personal development, community engagement, and ethical leadership.
