दभ्रेभिः चिच्छशीयांसं हंसि व्राधन्तम् ओजसा |सखिभिर ये त्वे सचा ||
dabhrebhiś cic chaśīyāṁsaṁ haṁsi vrādhantam ojasā | sakhibhir ye tve sacā ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • dabhrebhiś: With the help of the strong or powerful.
    • cic: Indeed, or truly.
    • chaśīyāṁsaṁ: The one who is the essence of brightness or radiance.
    • haṁsi: You are the one who brings forth or manifests.
    • vrādhantam: The one who is full of strength or vigor.
    • ojasā: With energy or vitality.
    • sakhibhir: With friends or companions.
    • ye: Those who.
    • tve: You.
    • sacā: Truly or indeed.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of invoking a powerful and radiant force that brings strength and vitality.
    • It emphasizes the importance of companionship and support from friends in achieving one's goals.
    • The essence of the mantra can be seen as a call to harness inner strength and the collective energy of those around us.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a team setting, the mantra can be applied to highlight the importance of collaboration. For instance, in a workplace, when team members support each other, they can achieve greater success than when working alone.
    • In personal development, individuals often find that their friends and family provide the necessary encouragement and energy to pursue their passions and overcome challenges.
    • In sports, athletes often rely on their teammates for motivation and strength, embodying the spirit of this mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its reminder of the power of collective strength and the importance of surrounding oneself with supportive individuals.
    • It teaches us that true vitality and success come not just from individual effort but also from the energy and encouragement we receive from our companions.
    • In real-life scenarios, whether in personal relationships, professional environments, or community efforts, this mantra serves as a guiding principle to foster collaboration and harness collective energy for greater achievements.
