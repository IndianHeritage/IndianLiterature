उदु ष्य देवः सविता दामून हिरण्यपाणिः प्रतिदोषम अस्तात् |अयोहनुर यजतो मन्द्रजिह्वा आ दाशुषे सुवति भूरी वामम् ||
ud u ṣya devaḥ savitā damūnā hiraṇyapāṇiḥ pratidoṣam asthāt | ayohanur yajato mandrajihva ā dāśuṣe suvati bhūri vāmam ||
  • ud u ṣya devaḥ savitā damūnā hiraṇyapāṇiḥ pratidoṣam asthāt
    • ud u ṣya: This phrase can be interpreted as "arise" or "awaken." It signifies the call for enlightenment and awareness.
    • devaḥ savitā: Refers to Savitā, the Sun God, who is often associated with creation and life-giving energy.
    • damūnā: This term can mean "with restraint" or "with control," suggesting the importance of moderation in our actions.
    • hiraṇyapāṇiḥ: Translates to "one who has golden hands," symbolizing prosperity and the ability to create wealth and abundance.
    • pratidoṣam asthāt: This phrase means "he stands against all obstacles," indicating resilience and strength in overcoming challenges.
  • ayohanur yajato mandrajihva ā dāśuṣe suvati bhūri vāmam
    • ayohanur: This can be interpreted as "the one who moves forward" or "the one who progresses," emphasizing the importance of continuous growth.
    • yajato: Refers to "those who perform sacrifices," highlighting the significance of selfless actions and contributions to society.
    • mandrajihva: This term means "with a gentle tongue," suggesting the value of diplomacy and kindness in communication.
    • ā dāśuṣe: Translates to "for the one who is devoted," indicating the importance of dedication and commitment in our endeavors.
    • suvati bhūri vāmam: This phrase means "may we receive abundant blessings," expressing a desire for prosperity and well-being.

Conclusion:

The mantra emphasizes the importance of awakening to our potential, embracing resilience, and acting with restraint and kindness. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios such as:

  • In personal development, it encourages individuals to rise above challenges and pursue their goals with determination.
  • In professional settings, it highlights the significance of teamwork, selflessness, and effective communication.
  • In community service, it inspires individuals to contribute positively and support one another for collective growth.

Overall, this mantra serves as a reminder of the values of perseverance, kindness, and the pursuit of prosperity through dedicated actions, making it relevant in various aspects of life.
