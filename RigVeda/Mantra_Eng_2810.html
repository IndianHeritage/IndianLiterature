अगन्न इन्द्र श्रवो बृहद् द्युम्नं दधिष्व दुस्तरम् |उत ते शुष्मं तिरामसि ||
agann indra śravo bṛhad dyumnaṁ dadhiṣva duṣṭaram | ut te śuṣmaṁ tirāmasi ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • agann: O Agni (the fire god)
    • indra: O Indra (the king of gods)
    • śravo: of fame or glory
    • bṛhad: great or vast
    • dyumnaṁ: brilliance or splendor
    • dadhiṣva: bestow or grant
    • duṣṭaram: that which is difficult or challenging
    • ut: and
    • te: your
    • śuṣmaṁ: excellence or superiority
    • tirāmasi: we cross over or transcend
  • Overall Meaning: This mantra is a prayer invoking Agni and Indra to grant us great fame and brilliance, helping us to overcome challenges and transcend difficulties. It emphasizes the importance of divine assistance in achieving greatness and overcoming obstacles in life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, individuals often seek guidance and support from mentors or leaders (akin to Agni and Indra) to achieve their career goals. For instance, a young entrepreneur may look for advice from a successful business leader to navigate the challenges of starting a new venture.
    • In personal development, people may turn to spiritual practices or community support to overcome personal struggles, much like seeking the blessings of deities for strength and clarity.
    • In education, students often rely on teachers and educational resources to help them excel academically, paralleling the invocation of divine figures for wisdom and guidance.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine support in the pursuit of greatness and the ability to overcome life's challenges. It serves as a reminder that while personal effort is essential, seeking guidance and support from higher powers or mentors can lead to greater achievements. In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as in professional growth, personal development, and educational pursuits, highlighting the interconnectedness of human effort and divine assistance in achieving success.
