अग्ने नक्षत्रम् अजरम् आ सूर्यं रोहयो दिवि |दधज ज्योतिर जनिभ्यः ||
agne nakṣatram ajaram ā sūryaṁ rohayo divi | dadhaj jyotir janebhyaḥ ||
  • Agne: This word refers to Agni, the fire god in Vedic literature, symbolizing energy, light, and purity.
  • Nakṣatram: This translates to "star" or "constellation," representing the celestial bodies that guide and influence life on Earth.
  • Ajaram: Meaning "immortal" or "eternal," this term signifies the everlasting nature of certain elements in the universe.
  • Ā Sūryaṁ: This phrase means "the sun," which is a source of life, energy, and illumination.
  • Rohayo: This can be interpreted as "to rise" or "to ascend," indicating growth and progress.
  • Divi: Meaning "in the heavens" or "in the sky," it refers to the divine realm or higher consciousness.
  • Dadhaḥ: This means "to hold" or "to support," suggesting the nurturing aspect of the divine.
  • Jyotir: Translated as "light" or "radiance," it symbolizes knowledge, wisdom, and enlightenment.
  • Janebhyaḥ: This means "to the people" or "to humanity," indicating the mantra's relevance to all beings.

The mantra "agne nakṣatram ajaram ā sūryaṁ rohayo divi | dadhaj jyotir janebhyaḥ ||" can be understood as a prayer or invocation to Agni, the fire god, to bestow eternal light and wisdom upon humanity. It emphasizes the connection between the divine, the celestial, and the earthly realms.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various contexts:

  • Spiritual Growth: Just as the sun rises and brings light, individuals can seek enlightenment and knowledge in their lives, striving for personal growth and understanding.
  • Community Welfare: The invocation of light and wisdom can inspire actions that benefit society, encouraging individuals to contribute positively to their communities.
  • Environmental Awareness: The reference to celestial bodies can remind us of our connection to nature and the importance of preserving our environment for future generations.

In conclusion, this mantra signifies the eternal quest for knowledge, light, and growth. It serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of all beings and the divine forces that guide us. By invoking these energies, individuals can aspire to lead lives filled with purpose, wisdom, and compassion, ultimately contributing to the betterment of society and the world at large.
