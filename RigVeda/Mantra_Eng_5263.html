तं त्वा दूतं कृण्महे यशस्तमं देवामां आवीतये वह |विश्वा सूनो सहसो मर्तभोजनाः रास्व तद यत त्वेमहे ||
taṁ tvā dūtaṁ kṛṇmahe yaśastamaṁ devām̐ ā vītaye vaha | viśvā sūno sahaso martabhojanā rāsva tad yat tvemahe ||
  • taṁ tvā dūtaṁ kṛṇmahe: This phrase translates to "We invoke you, O messenger." Here, the term "dūta" refers to a messenger or envoy, symbolizing the importance of communication and connection in our lives. In real life, this can be likened to the role of a mediator or a friend who conveys messages between parties, emphasizing the value of clear communication.
  • yaśastamaṁ devām̐ ā vītaye vaha: This part means "May you bring us fame and glory." The word "yaśas" signifies fame or reputation, while "devām̐" refers to the divine. This can be related to how individuals strive for recognition in their fields, whether in careers, arts, or community service. For instance, a scientist working on groundbreaking research hopes to gain recognition for their contributions, which can inspire others.
  • viśvā sūno sahaso martabhojanā: This translates to "All beings, the sons of the universe, are nourished." The term "viśvā" refers to the universe or all beings, and "sūno" means sons or offspring. This highlights the interconnectedness of all life. In a real-life context, this can be seen in environmental movements where people advocate for the protection of nature, recognizing that all living beings depend on a healthy ecosystem.
  • rāsva tad yat tvemahe: This means "We seek that which is beneficial." The word "rāsva" implies something that is advantageous or favorable. In practical terms, this can relate to personal goals, such as pursuing education or career opportunities that lead to personal growth and societal contribution. For example, choosing a career in healthcare can be seen as seeking to benefit both oneself and the community.

  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of communication, the pursuit of fame and glory through positive actions, the interconnectedness of all beings, and the quest for beneficial outcomes. In real life, these themes resonate in various aspects, such as personal relationships, professional endeavors, and community involvement. By invoking these principles, individuals can strive for a life that not only seeks personal success but also contributes positively to the world around them.
