स्वस्तये वायुमुप ब्रवामहे सोमं स्वस्ति भुवनस्य यस पतिः | बृहस्पतिं सर्वगणं स्वस्तये स्वस्तया आदित्यासो भवन्तु नः ||
svastaye vāyum upa bravāmahai somaṁ svasti bhuvanasya yas patiḥ | bṛhaspatiṁ sarvagaṇaṁ svastaye svastaya ādityāso bhavantu naḥ ||
  • Translation: "We invoke the wind for our well-being; may the soma (nectar) be auspicious for the lord of the universe. May the great lord Bṛhaspati, the protector of all beings, bring us well-being; may the Adityas (solar deities) grant us prosperity."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • Svastaye: Refers to well-being or auspiciousness.
    • Vāyum: Means wind, symbolizing movement, life, and energy.
    • Bravāmahai: A call to invoke or proclaim.
    • Somaṁ: Represents the sacred drink, often associated with immortality and divine inspiration.
    • Bhuvanasya: Pertains to the universe or the world.
    • Yas patiḥ: Refers to the lord or protector.
    • Bṛhaspatiṁ: The deity of wisdom and eloquence, often seen as a guide.
    • Sarvagaṇaṁ: Meaning all beings or all creatures.
    • Ādityāso: Refers to the solar deities, representing light and life.
    • Bhavantu: May they become or may they be.
    • Naḥ: Means 'for us' or 'to us.'
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, invoking the wind can symbolize seeking clarity and direction in our thoughts and actions, much like how a gentle breeze can clear the air.
    • The concept of soma can be related to the pursuit of knowledge and inspiration, akin to how people seek out experiences that enrich their lives, such as education or travel.
    • Bṛhaspati, as a symbol of wisdom, can be seen in the importance of mentorship in our lives, where wise individuals guide us through challenges.
    • The Adityas represent the sun, which is essential for life; this can be reflected in our reliance on natural resources and the importance of sustainability in our modern world.
  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the interconnectedness of well-being, wisdom, and the natural elements that sustain life. It serves as a reminder to seek harmony with the universe and to invoke the forces that promote prosperity and guidance. In real life, this can be applied through practices that enhance our physical, mental, and spiritual health, such as meditation, seeking knowledge, and fostering community relationships. By recognizing the significance of these elements, we can strive for a balanced and fulfilling life.
