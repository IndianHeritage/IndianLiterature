उग्रं व ओजः स्थिरा शवांस्य अधा मरुद्भिर गणस तुविष्मान् ||
ugraṁ va ojaḥ sthirā śavāṁsy adhā marudbhir gaṇas tuviṣmān ||
  • Ugraṁ: This word translates to "fierce" or "powerful." It signifies strength and intensity. In real life, this can be related to individuals or forces that exhibit great determination and vigor, such as athletes pushing their limits or leaders making bold decisions.
  • Va: This is a conjunction meaning "and." It connects ideas, emphasizing the relationship between the qualities being described.
  • Ojaḥ: This term means "vitality" or "energy." It refers to the life force that sustains beings. In practical terms, this can be seen in the energy we derive from nutritious food, exercise, and mental well-being, which contribute to our overall vitality.
  • Sthirā: Meaning "stable" or "firm," this word denotes steadiness and reliability. In everyday life, this can be exemplified by a strong foundation in relationships, careers, or personal values that provide security and support during challenging times.
  • Śavāṁsy: This translates to "bodies" or "forms." It refers to the physical aspect of beings. In a real-world context, this can relate to the importance of physical health and well-being, highlighting the need for care and respect for our bodies.
  • Adhā: Meaning "below" or "under," this word can imply a foundation or something that supports. In life, this can be seen in the underlying principles or values that guide our actions and decisions.
  • Marudbhir: This term refers to "the winds" or "the storm gods." It symbolizes dynamic forces of nature and can represent change and unpredictability in life. For example, the winds of change in our careers or personal lives can lead to new opportunities and growth.
  • Gaṇas: This means "groups" or "multitudes." It signifies community and collective strength. In real life, this can be observed in teamwork, where collaboration among individuals leads to greater achievements than what one could accomplish alone.
  • Tuviṣmān: This word means "powerful" or "mighty." It emphasizes the strength and capability of the forces being described. In practical terms, this can relate to influential leaders or movements that drive significant change in society.

Conclusion: The mantra "ugraṁ va ojaḥ sthirā śavāṁsy adhā marudbhir gaṇas tuviṣmān" encapsulates the essence of strength, vitality, stability, and the dynamic forces of nature. Its significance lies in the recognition of the interconnectedness of these qualities in our lives. By embodying strength and stability while harnessing our vitality, we can navigate the winds of change effectively. This mantra serves as a reminder of the power of community and collaboration, encouraging us to work together to achieve common goals. In real-life scenarios, it can inspire individuals to cultivate their inner strength, maintain stability in their lives, and embrace the changes that come their way, ultimately leading to personal and collective growth.
