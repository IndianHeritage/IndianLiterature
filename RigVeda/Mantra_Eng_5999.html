आ त्वा रथे हिरण्यये हरī मयूरशेव्याः |शितिपृष्ठा वहताम् मध्वो अन्धसो विवक्षणस्य पीतये ||
ā tvā rathe hiraṇyaye harī mayūraśepyā | śitipṛṣṭhā vahatām madhvo andhaso vivakṣaṇasya pītaye ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā: O (a call or invocation)
    • tvā: you (addressing a deity or divine force)
    • rathe: in the chariot (symbolizing movement or journey)
    • hiraṇyaye: golden (representing prosperity and wealth)
    • harī: the one who is green or vibrant (often associated with nature or life)
    • mayūraśepyā: like a peacock (symbolizing beauty and grace)
    • śitipṛṣṭhā: with a sharp back (indicating strength and resilience)
    • vahatām: of those who carry (referring to bearers of burdens or responsibilities)
    • madhvo: of sweetness (representing joy and positivity)
    • andhaso: of the blind (symbolizing ignorance or lack of awareness)
    • vivakṣaṇasya: of the one who desires to speak (indicating the importance of communication)
    • pītaye: for the one who is yellow (often associated with knowledge or enlightenment)
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes a divine presence, asking for guidance and support as one embarks on a journey.
    • The imagery of a chariot suggests movement through life, while the golden aspect symbolizes the pursuit of prosperity.
    • The reference to a peacock highlights the beauty and grace that one should embody during this journey.
    • Strength and resilience are emphasized through the mention of a sharp back, indicating the need to bear responsibilities with courage.
    • The mantra also touches on the importance of communication and awareness, urging one to seek knowledge and understanding.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, this mantra can be applied when starting a new project or venture, invoking the necessary support and guidance.
    • During personal challenges, one might reflect on the strength and resilience needed to overcome obstacles, much like the sharp back of the chariot.
    • In relationships, the emphasis on communication can serve as a reminder to express oneself clearly and seek understanding with others.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the journey of life, emphasizing the importance of guidance, strength, beauty, and communication.
    • Its significance lies in its ability to inspire individuals to seek prosperity and knowledge while navigating the challenges of life.
    • By embodying the qualities represented in the mantra, one can approach life's journey with grace and resilience, ultimately leading to personal growth and fulfillment.
