त्वं ह त्यद अप्रतिमानम् ओजो वज्रेण वज्रिन् धृतितो जगन्थ || त्वं शुष्णस्यावातिरो वधात्रैस् त्वं गा इन्द्र शच्येद अविन्दः ||
tvaṁ ha tyad apratimānam ojo vajreṇa vajrin dhṛṣito jaghantha | tvaṁ śuṣṇasyāvātiro vadhatrais tvaṁ gā indra śacyed avindaḥ ||
  • tvaṁ ha tyad apratimānam: This phrase translates to "You are indeed that which is incomparable." It emphasizes the uniqueness and unparalleled nature of the divine or the ultimate reality. In real life, this can be related to the idea that every individual has their own unique qualities and strengths that set them apart from others.
  • ojo vajreṇa vajrin dhṛṣito jaghantha: This part means "With the strength of the thunderbolt, you are the one who is resolute." The thunderbolt symbolizes immense power and determination. In practical terms, this can be seen in individuals who face challenges with unwavering strength, much like a leader who stands firm in the face of adversity.
  • tvaṁ śuṣṇasyāvātiro vadhatrais: This translates to "You are the one who overcomes the fierce challenges." It signifies the ability to conquer difficulties and obstacles. For example, a student who perseveres through tough academic challenges embodies this spirit of overcoming adversity.
  • tvaṁ gā indra śacyed avindaḥ: This means "You are the one who brings forth strength and power." It highlights the role of the divine in empowering individuals. In everyday life, this can be seen in mentors or leaders who inspire and empower others to achieve their potential.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of strength, uniqueness, and the ability to overcome challenges. Its significance lies in the reminder that each individual possesses inherent power and resilience. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire people to recognize their unique qualities, face challenges with determination, and empower others in their journey. It serves as a motivational reminder that, like the divine, we all have the potential to be strong and impactful in our lives and the lives of others.
