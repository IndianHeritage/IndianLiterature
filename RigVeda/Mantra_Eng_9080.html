देवास आयन परशूम् अभिभ्रन् वनाः वृश्चन्तो अभि विद्भिर आयन |नि सुद्रवं दधतो वक्षाणासु यत्र कृतपीटम् अनु तद दहन्ति ||
devāsa āyan paraśūm̐r abibhran vanā vṛścanto abhi viḍbhir āyan | ni sudrva1ṁ dadhato vakṣaṇāsu yatrā kṛpīṭam anu tad dahanti ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • devāsa: of the gods
    • āyan: coming or approaching
    • paraśūm̐r: with axes or weapons
    • abibhran: bearing or carrying
    • vanā: from the forest
    • vṛścanto: those who are strong or powerful
    • abhi: towards
    • viḍbhir: with knowledge or wisdom
    • āyan: coming or approaching
    • ni sudrvaṁ: with good fortune
    • dadhato: holding or possessing
    • vakṣaṇāsu: in the hearts or minds
    • yatrā: where
    • kṛpīṭam: the protective covering or shield
    • anu: following
    • tad: that
    • dahanti: they burn or destroy
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the divine forces approaching with strength and wisdom.
    • The mention of "axes" symbolizes the power to cut through ignorance and obstacles.
    • The "forest" represents the challenges and complexities of life.
    • The phrase "with good fortune" indicates that those who possess wisdom and strength are blessed.
    • The "protective covering" suggests the importance of safeguarding one's heart and mind from negativity.
    • Overall, the mantra emphasizes the need for divine guidance and inner strength to overcome life's challenges.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, when facing challenges, one can seek guidance from mentors (the divine forces) and approach problems with a strong and wise mindset.
    • In personal life, individuals may encounter emotional turmoil (the forest); by cultivating wisdom and strength, they can navigate through these difficulties.
    • In community service, helping others who are struggling can be seen as carrying the "axes" to cut through societal issues, bringing about positive change.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of divine guidance, inner strength, and wisdom in overcoming life's challenges.
    • It encourages individuals to seek knowledge and support from higher powers while also emphasizing the need to protect one's inner self from negativity.
    • In real-life scenarios, applying the principles of this mantra can lead to personal growth, resilience, and the ability to positively impact others.
