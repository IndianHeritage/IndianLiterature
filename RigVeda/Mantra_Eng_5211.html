मन्त्र: एते द्युम्नेभिर विश्वम् आतिरन्त मन्त्रं ये वारं नार्याः अतक्षन् | प्र ये विशस तिरन्त श्रोषमाणाः आ ये मे अस्य दीधयन्न ऋतस्य ||
ete dyumnebhir viśvam ātiranta mantraṁ ye vāraṁ naryā atakṣan | pra ye viśas tiranta śroṣamāṇā ā ye me asya dīdhayann ṛtasya ||
  • Translation: "These mantras, which transcend the heavens, are the universal hymns that the wise have sung. They have reached the realms of the divine, and those who listen to them are blessed with the truth."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • ete: these
    • dyumnebhir: by the heavenly beings
    • viśvam: universal
    • ātiranta: transcending
    • mantraṁ: mantras or hymns
    • ye: who
    • vāraṁ: the wise
    • naryā: have sung
    • atakṣan: have reached
    • pra: indeed
    • ye: who
    • viśas: realms
    • tiranta: transcending
    • śroṣamāṇā: those who listen
    • ā: and
    • ye: who
    • me: my
    • asya: of this
    • dīdhayann: are blessed
    • ṛtasya: of truth
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, the mantra can be likened to the guiding principles or mission statements that transcend individual roles and unite the organization towards a common goal.
    • In personal life, it reflects the importance of listening to wise counsel from mentors or elders, which can lead to personal growth and understanding of deeper truths.
    • In community gatherings, the collective singing of hymns or mantras can create a sense of unity and shared purpose, transcending individual differences.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its emphasis on the power of collective wisdom and the transcendence of truth. It teaches us that by listening to and embracing universal truths, we can elevate our understanding and connection to the divine. In real life, this mantra encourages us to seek wisdom, listen to the experiences of others, and strive for a greater understanding of our place in the universe. It is applicable in various scenarios, from personal development to community building, reminding us of the importance of unity and shared knowledge in our journey through life.
