मन्तर: नृभिर येमानो हर्यतो विचक्षणो राजा देवः समुद्रियः ||
nṛbhir yemāno haryato vicakṣaṇo rājā devaḥ samudriyaḥ ||
  • nṛbhir: This word refers to "men" or "humans." It signifies the collective strength and wisdom of humanity.
  • yemāno: This term means "who are guided" or "who are led." It indicates the importance of direction and leadership in human endeavors.
  • haryato: This word translates to "by the horses" or "by the swift." It symbolizes speed, agility, and the ability to overcome obstacles.
  • vicakṣaṇo: This means "wise" or "perceptive." It highlights the necessity of wisdom and insight in decision-making processes.
  • rājā: This translates to "king" or "ruler." It represents authority, governance, and the responsibility that comes with leadership.
  • devaḥ: This word means "god" or "divine." It signifies a higher power or spiritual guidance that influences human actions.
  • samudriyaḥ: This term refers to "oceanic" or "related to the sea." It symbolizes vastness, depth, and the interconnectedness of all life.

  • Meaning: The mantra speaks to the relationship between humans and divine guidance, emphasizing the importance of wise leadership and the ability to navigate life's challenges with agility and insight. It suggests that just as horses can swiftly carry a ruler, humans must be guided by wisdom and a higher power to traverse the vast ocean of life.
  • Real-Life Example: In a corporate setting, a CEO (the rājā) must lead their company (nṛbhir) with wisdom (vicakṣaṇo) and agility (haryato) to navigate market challenges (samudriyaḥ). The CEO's decisions are often influenced by a vision or mission (devaḥ) that guides the organization towards success.

  • Conclusion: This mantra emphasizes the significance of wise leadership and the need for guidance in our lives. It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, such as in governance, business, and personal development. By recognizing the importance of wisdom and divine guidance, individuals can better navigate the complexities of life, ensuring that their actions are aligned with a greater purpose.
