नū रोदसी अभिष्टुते वसिष्ठैर ऋतावानो वरुणो मित्रो अग्निः | यच्छन्तु चन्द्रा उपमं नो अर्कं यूयम् पात स्वस्तिभिः सदा नः ||
nū rodasī abhiṣṭute vasiṣṭhair ṛtāvāno varuṇo mitro agniḥ | yacchantu candrā upamaṁ no arkaṁ yūyam pāta svastibhiḥ sadā naḥ ||
  • nū rodasī abhiṣṭute: This phrase can be translated as "Now, the heavens are invoked." It signifies the importance of calling upon the celestial forces, which can be seen as a metaphor for seeking guidance and support from higher powers in our lives.
  • vasiṣṭhair ṛtāvāno: This refers to the wise sages (Vasiṣṭha) who are aligned with the cosmic order (ṛta). In real life, this can be related to seeking wisdom from knowledgeable individuals or mentors who help us navigate through life's challenges.
  • varuṇo mitro agniḥ: Varuna is the god of cosmic order, Mitra represents friendship and harmony, and Agni symbolizes fire and transformation. Together, they represent the balance of natural forces and the importance of maintaining harmony in our relationships and personal growth.
  • yacchantu candrā upamaṁ no arkaṁ: This line suggests a request for the moon (Candra) to provide us with a comparison to the sun (Arka). It highlights the duality of light and darkness, and how both are essential in our lives. For example, just as the moon reflects the sun's light, we often find guidance and inspiration from others.
  • yūyam pāta svastibhiḥ sadā naḥ: This is a plea for protection and well-being. "Pāta" means to protect, and "svastibhiḥ" refers to auspiciousness. In everyday life, this can be interpreted as a desire for safety and prosperity in our endeavors, whether in personal relationships, career, or health.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the interconnectedness of the cosmos, the importance of wisdom, harmony, and protection in our lives. It serves as a reminder to seek guidance from higher powers and wise individuals, to maintain balance in our relationships, and to strive for well-being. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applicable during times of uncertainty or decision-making, encouraging individuals to reflect on their actions and seek support from their community and the universe. By invoking these principles, one can cultivate a more harmonious and fulfilling life.
