मन्त्र: इन्द्राग्नि आगतम् सुतं गीरभिर नभो वरेंयम् |अस्य पातं धियेषिता ||
indrāgnī ā gataṁ sutaṁ gīrbhir nabho vareṇyam | asya pātaṁ dhiyeṣitā ||
  • Indrāgnī: This term refers to the combined powers of Indra, the king of the gods, and Agni, the fire god. Indra symbolizes strength, power, and the ability to overcome obstacles, while Agni represents energy, transformation, and purification.
  • ā gataṁ: This phrase means "having come forth" or "having arrived." It signifies the emergence or manifestation of something significant.
  • sutaṁ: This word translates to "son" or "offspring." In a broader sense, it can refer to the results or fruits of one's actions or efforts.
  • gīrbhir: This term means "with hymns" or "with words." It emphasizes the importance of speech, communication, and the power of words in invoking divine blessings.
  • nabho: This word means "from the sky" or "heaven." It indicates a connection to the divine or celestial realms.
  • vareṇyam: This term translates to "worthy of worship" or "to be desired." It signifies something that is highly esteemed and sought after.
  • asya: This word means "of this" or "of this person." It indicates a personal connection to the subject being discussed.
  • pātaṁ: This term means "protection" or "safeguarding." It emphasizes the need for divine protection and guidance.
  • dhiyeṣitā: This word translates to "by the intellect" or "by wisdom." It highlights the role of intelligence and discernment in achieving one's goals.

Conclusion: The mantra "indrāgnī ā gataṁ sutaṁ gīrbhir nabho vareṇyam | asya pātaṁ dhiyeṣitā ||" encapsulates the essence of seeking divine blessings through the combined powers of Indra and Agni. It emphasizes the importance of communication, wisdom, and the pursuit of worthy goals. In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as during important decisions, rituals, or moments of seeking guidance. By invoking the energies of Indra and Agni, individuals can aspire for strength, transformation, and protection in their endeavors, ultimately leading to success and fulfillment in their personal and professional lives.
