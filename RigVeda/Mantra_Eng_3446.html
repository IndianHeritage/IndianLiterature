आ वाजाऽ यातोप न ऋभुक्षा महो नरो द्रविणसो गृणानाः |आ वः पीतयोऽभिपित्वे अह्नाम इमा अस्तं नवस्व इव गमन ||
ā vājā yātopa na ṛbhukṣā maho naro draviṇaso gṛṇānāḥ | ā vaḥ pītayo 'bhipitve ahnām imā astaṁ navasva iva gman ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā vājā: "O, the powerful ones" or "O, the strong"
    • yātopa: "who are ready" or "who are prepared"
    • na ṛbhukṣā: "do not let us be deprived" or "do not let us go hungry"
    • maho naro: "great men" or "noble beings"
    • draviṇaso: "of wealth" or "of resources"
    • gṛṇānāḥ: "who sing" or "who praise"
    • ā vaḥ: "come to us" or "bring to us"
    • pītayo: "the drinkers" or "the ones who consume"
    • 'bhipitve: "in abundance" or "with plenty"
    • ahnām: "of the days" or "of the time"
    • imā: "these" or "this"
    • astaṁ: "to the setting" or "to the end"
    • navasva: "new" or "fresh"
    • iva: "like" or "as"
    • gman: "to go" or "to move"
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the powerful and noble beings, asking them to bring wealth and resources.
    • It emphasizes the importance of abundance and sustenance, highlighting the need for support in times of need.
    • The reference to "drinkers" suggests a call for nourishment, both physical and spiritual.
    • The phrase "like the new" indicates a desire for renewal and freshness in life, akin to the dawn of a new day.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community setting, this mantra can be applied during harvest festivals where people come together to celebrate abundance and share resources.
    • In personal life, individuals may recite this mantra during times of financial difficulty, seeking support and prosperity.
    • In corporate environments, it can symbolize teamwork and collaboration, where members work together to achieve common goals and share successes.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for abundance, support, and the importance of community.
    • It serves as a reminder that we are not alone in our struggles and that there is strength in unity and collaboration.
    • In real-life scenarios, it encourages individuals and communities to seek help, share resources, and work together towards common goals, fostering a sense of belonging and mutual support.
