पाति प्रियं रिपो अग्रं पदं वेः पाति यह्वश्चराणं सूर्यस्य |पाति नाभा सप्तशीर्षाणम अग्निः पाति देवानाम उपमादम ऋष्वः ||
pāti priyaṁ ripo agram padaṁ veḥ pāti yahvaś caraṇaṁ sūryasya | pāti nābhā saptaśīrṣāṇam agniḥ pāti devānām upamādam ṛṣvaḥ ||
  • pāti: This word translates to "protector" or "nurturer." It signifies a divine force that safeguards and supports.
  • priyaṁ: Meaning "beloved" or "dear," this term emphasizes the importance of love and affection in relationships.
  • ripo: This translates to "enemy" or "opponent," indicating the presence of challenges or adversities in life.
  • agram: Meaning "foremost" or "leading," it suggests the pursuit of excellence and leadership.
  • padaṁ: This means "foot" or "path," symbolizing the journey one takes in life.
  • veḥ: Translates to "who" or "that," referring to the divine or higher power.
  • yahvaś: This term means "who is" or "that which is," further emphasizing the divine presence.
  • caraṇaṁ: Meaning "feet" or "steps," it symbolizes the actions and choices made in life.
  • sūryasya: This translates to "of the sun," representing light, knowledge, and enlightenment.
  • nābhā: Meaning "navel" or "center," it symbolizes the core or essence of existence.
  • saptaśīrṣāṇam: This means "seven heads," which can represent various aspects of life or the universe.
  • agniḥ: Translates to "fire," symbolizing transformation, energy, and purification.
  • devānām: Meaning "of the gods," it signifies divine qualities and celestial beings.
  • upamādam: This translates to "comparison" or "similarity," indicating the importance of understanding through analogy.
  • ṛṣvaḥ: Meaning "strength" or "power," it emphasizes resilience and fortitude.

Conclusion: This mantra encapsulates the essence of protection, love, and the journey of life amidst challenges. It highlights the importance of divine guidance and the pursuit of knowledge and strength. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as seeking protection during difficult times, fostering loving relationships, and striving for personal excellence. It serves as a reminder to embrace the divine presence in our lives, to navigate our paths with courage, and to transform adversities into opportunities for growth.
