एष हितो विनीयतेऽन्तः शुभ्रावतā पथā | यदीतुञ्जन्ति भूर्णयः ||
eṣa hito vi nīyate 'ntaḥ śubhrāvatā pathā | yadī tuñjanti bhūrṇayaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • eṣa: this
    • hito: beneficial or auspicious
    • vi: indeed
    • nīyate: is guided or led
    • 'ntaḥ: within
    • śubhrāvatā: pure or auspicious path
    • pathā: by the way or path
    • yadī: if
    • tuñjanti: they are disturbed or troubled
    • bhūrṇayaḥ: by the obstacles or difficulties
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the guidance towards a beneficial and pure path.
    • It emphasizes that even if one faces disturbances or obstacles, the inner guidance remains steadfast.
    • The mantra suggests that there is a divine or higher force that leads individuals towards their true purpose.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, an individual may face challenges such as job loss or project failures. However, if they remain focused on their goals and seek guidance, they may find new opportunities that align with their true calling.
    • In personal relationships, conflicts may arise, but by adhering to principles of love and understanding, individuals can navigate through difficulties and strengthen their bonds.
    • In spiritual practices, individuals may encounter doubts or distractions, yet by following a disciplined path, they can achieve clarity and inner peace.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its reminder that despite external challenges, one can find strength and direction from within.
    • It encourages individuals to trust in their inner guidance and to remain committed to their path, regardless of the obstacles they may face.
    • This mantra is applicable in various aspects of life, including career, relationships, and personal growth, serving as a source of inspiration and resilience.
