कतरां पूर्वा कतरापरायोः कथा जाते कवयः को विबेद |विश्वं तमनā बिभृतो यद ध नामा विंर्ते अहनी चक्रियेव ||
katarā pūrvā katarāparāyoḥ kathā jāte kavayaḥ ko vi veda | viśvaṁ tmanā bibhṛto yad dha nāma vi vartete ahanī cakriyeva ||
  • katarā pūrvā katarāparāyoḥ - This phrase translates to "Which is the earlier, and which is the later?" It raises questions about the nature of time and existence, prompting us to consider the origins of things and their subsequent developments.
  • kathā jāte kavayaḥ - This means "How do the wise ones know?" It suggests that there are enlightened individuals who possess knowledge beyond ordinary understanding, hinting at the importance of wisdom and insight in navigating life's complexities.
  • ko vi veda - Translated as "Who indeed knows?" This line emphasizes the uncertainty of knowledge and the limitations of human understanding. It reflects the philosophical inquiry into the nature of reality and existence.
  • viśvaṁ tmanā bibhṛto - This phrase means "The universe is upheld by the self." It indicates that the universe and individual consciousness are interconnected, suggesting that our inner state can influence the external world.
  • yad dha nāma vi vartete - This translates to "Whatever exists, exists by name." It points to the significance of language and naming in shaping our reality, highlighting how our perceptions and labels affect our understanding of the world.
  • ahanī cakriyeva - This means "Like the wheel of time." It symbolizes the cyclical nature of existence, where events and experiences are interconnected in a continuous flow, much like the turning of a wheel.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda invites us to reflect on the nature of time, knowledge, and existence. It encourages us to seek wisdom and understand the interconnectedness of all things. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios, such as in decision-making processes where understanding the past and future implications is crucial. It also emphasizes the importance of language in shaping our reality, reminding us to be mindful of our words and their impact. Ultimately, it teaches us that while we may seek answers, the journey of inquiry and understanding is as significant as the answers themselves.
