स इद वने नमस्युभिर वाचस्यते चाऽरु जनेषु प्रब्रुवाण इन्द्रियम् |वृषा चन्दुर भवति हर्यतो वृषा क्षेमेण धेनाम् मगवाऽ यद इन्बति ||
sa id vane namasyubhir vacasyate cāru janeṣu prabruvāṇa indriyam | vṛṣā chandur bhavati haryato vṛṣā kṣemeṇa dhenām maghavā yad invati ||
  • sa id vane namasyubhir vacasyate: This phrase can be translated to "He who is revered in the forest speaks with eloquence." Here, "sa" refers to a divine entity or a revered figure, "id" means "here," "vane" refers to the forest, "namasyubhir" indicates reverence or respect, and "vacasyate" means to speak or express.
  • cāru janeṣu prabruvāṇa indriyam: This translates to "He who speaks beautifully among the charming people." "Cāru" means beautiful or charming, "janeṣu" refers to people, and "prabruvāṇa" means to speak or express oneself. "Indriyam" can refer to the senses or faculties of perception.
  • vṛṣā chandur bhavati haryato: This can be interpreted as "The powerful one becomes radiant." "Vṛṣā" means powerful or strong, "chandur" refers to radiance or brilliance, and "haryato" can be understood as "from the one who is joyful or auspicious."
  • vṛṣā kṣemeṇa dhenām maghavā yad invati: This translates to "The powerful one, through auspiciousness, brings forth abundance." "Kṣemeṇa" refers to auspiciousness or well-being, "dhenām" means abundance or wealth, and "maghavā" refers to a powerful or divine being. "Yad invati" means "that which he brings forth."

Conclusion: This mantra emphasizes the importance of eloquence, respect, and the power of communication in fostering relationships and abundance in life. It suggests that those who are revered and respected, who communicate beautifully, can bring about prosperity and well-being. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various contexts such as leadership, where effective communication can inspire and motivate others, or in personal relationships, where respectful dialogue can strengthen bonds. The mantra serves as a reminder of the significance of our words and actions in creating a harmonious and prosperous environment.
