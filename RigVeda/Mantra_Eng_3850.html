मन्त्र: स्तोमासस त्वा गौरिवीतेर अवर्धन्न अरन्धयो वैदथिनाय पिप्रम् |आ त्वाम ऋजिश्वा सख्याय चक्रे पचन पक्तीर अपिभः सोमम् अस्य ||
stomāsas tvā gaurivīter avardhann arandhayo vaidathināya piprum | ā tvām ṛjiśvā sakhyāya cakre pacan paktīr apibaḥ somam asya ||
  • Stomāsas tvā gaurivīter avardhann: This phrase can be interpreted as a call to the divine, invoking the essence of the sacred. The term "stomāsas" refers to hymns or praises, while "tvā" means "you," indicating a direct address to a deity. "Gaurivīter" suggests a connection to the divine feminine, often associated with prosperity and nourishment.
  • Arandhayo vaidathināya piprum: Here, "arandhayo" can be understood as a reference to the unbroken or uninterrupted flow of blessings. "Vaidathināya" refers to the one who is associated with the Vedic tradition, and "piprum" signifies the act of offering or sharing. This line emphasizes the importance of sharing divine blessings and knowledge.
  • Ā tvām ṛjiśvā sakhyāya cakre: This part translates to "I have made you my friend through righteousness." "Ā tvām" means "to you," while "ṛjiśvā" refers to the path of truth or righteousness. "Sakhyāya" indicates friendship, suggesting that a bond is formed through virtuous actions.
  • Pacan paktīr apibaḥ somam asya: This line can be interpreted as "May the process of cooking (or preparation) bring forth the essence of this offering." "Pacan" refers to cooking, "paktīr" means preparation, and "somam" signifies the sacred drink, often associated with divine ecstasy and enlightenment. This highlights the importance of preparation and intention in rituals.

  • Significance: This mantra encapsulates the essence of devotion, friendship, and the sharing of divine blessings. It emphasizes the importance of righteousness and the sacredness of offerings in spiritual practices.
  • Real-life Application: In everyday life, this mantra can inspire individuals to cultivate friendships based on mutual respect and righteousness. It encourages the sharing of knowledge and blessings within communities, fostering a sense of unity and support. For instance, in a workplace, colleagues can embody the spirit of this mantra by collaborating harmoniously and uplifting each other through shared goals and values.
  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of individuals through shared virtues and the importance of nurturing relationships. By embodying the principles of righteousness and generosity, one can create a positive impact in their surroundings, leading to a more harmonious and prosperous life.
