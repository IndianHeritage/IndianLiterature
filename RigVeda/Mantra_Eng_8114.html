महो नो राया आ भर पवमान जही मृतः |रास्वेन्दो वीरवत् यशः ||
maho no rāya ā bhara pavamāna jahī mṛdhaḥ | rāsvendo vīravad yaśaḥ ||
  • maho - This word translates to "great" or "mighty." It signifies the vastness and importance of the subject being addressed.
  • no - This means "our," indicating a sense of possession or belonging.
  • rāya - This term refers to "wealth" or "prosperity." It emphasizes the importance of abundance in life.
  • ā bhara - This phrase translates to "may you bring" or "may you fill." It is a call for the arrival of prosperity and abundance.
  • pavamāna - This word means "the purifying one" or "the wind." It symbolizes the cleansing and revitalizing forces of nature.
  • jahī - This means "to remove" or "to let go." It signifies the act of discarding negativity or obstacles.
  • mṛdhaḥ - This translates to "harm" or "injury." It refers to anything that may cause damage or distress.
  • rāsvendo - This term can be interpreted as "the one who brings light" or "the radiant one." It symbolizes enlightenment and clarity.
  • vīravad - This means "like a hero" or "with valor." It emphasizes courage and strength in facing challenges.
  • yaśaḥ - This translates to "fame" or "glory." It signifies the recognition and honor that come from virtuous actions.

The mantra can be understood as a prayer or invocation for the arrival of great wealth and prosperity into our lives, while also seeking the removal of harm and negativity. It emphasizes the importance of courage and valor in achieving success and recognition.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios:

  • In personal finance, individuals can recite this mantra while setting financial goals, invoking prosperity and abundance in their lives.
  • In professional settings, it can be used as a motivational tool to encourage teamwork and collaboration, fostering an environment where everyone works towards common goals with courage and valor.
  • In times of adversity, this mantra serves as a reminder to let go of negativity and focus on the positive aspects of life, promoting mental well-being.

Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its holistic approach to life, encompassing wealth, health, and personal growth. By invoking the qualities of prosperity, courage, and clarity, individuals can navigate challenges and strive for success in various aspects of their lives. It serves as a powerful reminder to focus on positivity and the pursuit of greatness.
