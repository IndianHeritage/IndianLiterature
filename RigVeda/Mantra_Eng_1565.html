प्र तव्यसीं नव्यसीं धीतिं अग्नये वाचो मतीं सहसः सूनवे भरे | अपां नपाद यो वसुभिः सह प्रियः होतां पृथिव्यां न्य असीदत ऋत्वियः ||
pra tavyasīṁ navyasīṁ dhītim agnaye vāco matiṁ sahasaḥ sūnave bhare | apāṁ napād yo vasubhiḥ saha priyo hotā pṛthivyāṁ ny asīdad ṛtviyaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • pra: forward, towards
    • tavyasīṁ: the one who is to be praised
    • navyasīṁ: the new, fresh
    • dhītim: intellect, understanding
    • agnaye: to Agni, the fire god
    • vāco: speech, words
    • matiṁ: thought, intention
    • sahasaḥ: with strength, power
    • sūnave: to the son, offspring
    • bhare: may you fill, bestow
    • apāṁ: of waters
    • napād: the one who is born
    • yo: who
    • vasubhiḥ: with the wealth, riches
    • saha: together
    • priyo: dear, beloved
    • hotā: the priest, the one who offers
    • pṛthivyāṁ: in the earth
    • ny: not
    • asīdad: has become, has existed
    • ṛtviyaḥ: in accordance with truth, righteous
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Agni, the fire god, asking for the bestowal of intellect and strength.
    • It emphasizes the importance of clear and powerful speech and thought, which are essential for effective communication and decision-making in life.
    • The reference to "waters" symbolizes the nurturing and life-giving aspects of nature, suggesting a connection between the divine and the natural world.
    • The mantra also highlights the significance of community and collaboration, as it speaks of wealth and resources being shared among people.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, this mantra can be applied to encourage teamwork and collaboration, where individuals are urged to share their ideas and resources for the common good.
    • In personal relationships, it can serve as a reminder to communicate openly and honestly, fostering understanding and connection.
    • In educational environments, it emphasizes the importance of nurturing intellect and creativity, encouraging students to express their thoughts and ideas freely.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for the cultivation of intellect, effective communication, and the sharing of resources.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal relationships to professional environments, highlighting the interconnectedness of individuals and the importance of collaboration.
    • Ultimately, it serves as a reminder to seek wisdom and strength in our actions and interactions, fostering a harmonious and prosperous community.
