सोमारुद्रā वि वृहतं विषूचīm अमीवā यā नो गयम् आविवेश |आरे बाद्थेथां निरृतिम पराचैर अस्मे भद्रā सौश्रवसानि सन्तु ||
somārudrā vi vṛhataṁ viṣūcīm amīvā yā no gayam āviveśa | āre bādhethāṁ nirṛtim parācair asme bhadrā sauśravasāni santu ||
  • Mantra: "somārudrā vi vṛhataṁ viṣūcīm amīvā yā no gayam āviveśa | āre bādhethāṁ nirṛtim parācair asme bhadrā sauśravasāni santu ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • somārudrā: Referring to the divine or celestial beings associated with the moon (Soma) and Rudra (a form of Shiva).
    • vi vṛhataṁ: To increase or expand, often in the context of prosperity or abundance.
    • viṣūcīm: Referring to the path or direction, often associated with the flow of life or energy.
    • amīvā: A term that can imply protection or safeguarding.
    • yā no gayam āviveśa: Invoking the divine to enter into our lives or to bless us with their presence.
    • āre bādhethāṁ: A call to remove obstacles or negativity.
    • nirṛtim: Referring to misfortune or calamity.
    • parācair: From the external or outside forces.
    • asme: For us or to us.
    • bhadrā: Auspicious or beneficial.
    • sauśravasāni: Referring to good fame or reputation.
    • santu: May they be or may they exist.
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra invokes the celestial beings associated with the moon and Rudra, seeking their blessings for prosperity and abundance in life.
    • It emphasizes the importance of removing negativity and obstacles that may hinder our progress, akin to how one might seek guidance and support during challenging times in life.
    • The call for divine presence can be likened to seeking inspiration or support from mentors or spiritual leaders in our lives.
    • By asking for auspiciousness and good reputation, it highlights the significance of living a life of integrity and positive influence on others.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be applied when launching a new venture, where one seeks divine blessings for success and the removal of obstacles.
    • In personal life, individuals may recite this mantra during times of stress or uncertainty, hoping to attract positive energy and dispel negativity.
    • In community gatherings or rituals, this mantra can be used to invoke collective well-being and harmony among participants.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of divine support and human effort in achieving prosperity and well-being.
    • Its significance lies in the belief that by aligning ourselves with positive energies and seeking divine intervention, we can navigate life's challenges more effectively.
    • In real-life scenarios, it encourages individuals to foster a mindset of positivity, seek support from the community, and strive for personal and collective growth.
