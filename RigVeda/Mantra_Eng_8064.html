परि णो देववीतये वाजाम् अर्षसि गोमताḥ | पुनान इन्दव इन्द्रयुः ||
pari ṇo devavītaye vājām̐ arṣasi gomataḥ | punāna indav indrayuḥ ||
  • Mantra: "pari ṇo devavītaye vājām̐ arṣasi gomataḥ | punāna indav indrayuḥ ||"
  • Translation: This mantra can be translated as: "May the divine powers grant us strength and abundance, as we seek the blessings of the gods, and may we be purified and invigorated like the sacred cows."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • pari: Around, encompassing, or in all directions.
    • ṇo: Us, our.
    • devavītaye: For the purpose of divine blessings or powers.
    • vājām̐: Strength, vigor, or abundance.
    • arṣasi: To be invigorated or energized.
    • gomataḥ: Like the sacred cow, which symbolizes nourishment and wealth.
    • punāna: Purifying or cleansing.
    • indav: The divine or celestial beings.
    • indrayuḥ: Strength or vitality.

  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In agriculture, farmers often pray for divine blessings to ensure a bountiful harvest. This mantra reflects the same sentiment, seeking strength and abundance from the divine.
    • In personal development, individuals may use this mantra as a form of affirmation, invoking strength and purification in their lives, similar to how one might seek motivation from a mentor or a spiritual guide.
    • In community gatherings, this mantra can be recited to invoke collective strength and prosperity, much like how teams come together to support each other in achieving common goals.

  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine blessings and strength. It emphasizes the importance of seeking purity and abundance in our lives, akin to the revered status of cows in Vedic culture, which symbolize nourishment and wealth. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts such as agriculture, personal growth, and community support, reminding us of the interconnectedness of our efforts and the divine forces that aid us in our pursuits. By invoking this mantra, individuals and communities can foster a sense of unity, purpose, and abundance in their lives.
