मन्त्र: महि ज्योतिर बिभ्रतं त्वा विचक्षण भास्वन्तं चक्षुषे-चक्षुषे मयः | आरोहन्तं बृहतः पाजसस परि वयं जीवाः प्रति पश्येम सूर्य ||
mahi jyotir bibhrataṁ tvā vicakṣaṇa bhāsvantaṁ cakṣuṣe-cakṣuṣe mayaḥ | ārohantam bṛhataḥ pājasas pari vayaṁ jīvāḥ prati paśyema sūrya ||
  • mahi jyotir bibhrataṁ: This phrase translates to "the great light that carries." Here, "mahi" means great, and "jyotir" refers to light. In real life, this can be likened to the sun, which is a source of light and energy for all living beings.
  • tvā vicakṣaṇa bhāsvantaṁ: This means "to you, the wise and radiant one." "Vicakṣaṇa" refers to wisdom or insight, while "bhāsvantaṁ" means radiant or shining. This can be related to individuals who possess knowledge and share it with others, much like teachers or mentors who illuminate the path for their students.
  • cakṣuṣe-cakṣuṣe mayaḥ: This translates to "with our eyes, we see." "Cakṣuṣe" means eyes, and "mayaḥ" refers to us. This emphasizes the importance of perception and awareness in our lives. For example, being observant in our surroundings can lead to better decision-making and understanding of the world.
  • ārohantam bṛhataḥ pājasas: This means "the one who ascends, the great one." "Ārohantam" refers to ascending or rising, while "bṛhataḥ" means great. This can be seen in the context of personal growth and striving for higher goals, such as pursuing education or career advancement.
  • pari vayaṁ jīvāḥ: This translates to "all of us living beings." "Pari" means all or together, and "jīvāḥ" refers to living beings. This highlights the interconnectedness of life, reminding us that we are all part of a larger ecosystem, much like a community where each individual plays a vital role.
  • prati paśyema sūrya: This means "may we see the sun." "Prati" means may we, "paśyema" means see, and "sūrya" refers to the sun. This is a prayer for enlightenment and clarity, akin to seeking truth and understanding in our lives.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of light, wisdom, perception, and the interconnectedness of all living beings. It serves as a reminder to seek knowledge and enlightenment, much like the sun provides light and energy. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire individuals to pursue education, foster community connections, and strive for personal growth. By embodying the qualities of wisdom and awareness, we can navigate our lives with clarity and purpose, ultimately contributing to the well-being of ourselves and those around us.
