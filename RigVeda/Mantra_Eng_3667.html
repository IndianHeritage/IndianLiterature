त्वं आर्यमा भवसि यत् कनीनां नाम स्वधावन गुह्यं बिभर्षि |अञ्जन्ति मित्रं सुदितं न गोभिर यद् दम्पती समनसा कृतोषि ||
tvam aryamā bhavasi yat kanīnāṁ nāma svadhāvan guhyam bibharṣi | añjanti mitraṁ sudhitaṁ na gobhir yad dampatī samanasā kṛṇoṣi ||
  • tvam aryamā bhavasi: This phrase translates to "You become Aryama." Aryama is often associated with the concept of nobility and righteousness. In real life, this can be seen in individuals who strive to uphold ethical standards and act with integrity in their personal and professional lives.
  • yat kanīnāṁ nāma svadhāvan guhyam bibharṣi: This means "You carry the secret name of the small ones." This can refer to the importance of recognizing and valuing the contributions of those who may be overlooked in society, such as children or marginalized groups. For example, in a workplace, acknowledging the ideas of junior employees can lead to innovative solutions.
  • añjanti mitraṁ sudhitaṁ: This translates to "They bring forth a well-thought-out friend." This emphasizes the value of friendship and collaboration. In real life, having a supportive network of friends can enhance personal growth and provide emotional support during challenging times.
  • na gobhir yad dampatī samanasā kṛṇoṣi: This means "Not with cattle, but with a couple you create." This highlights the significance of human relationships over material possessions. In modern life, this can be seen in the prioritization of family and friendships over wealth, where people find fulfillment in their connections rather than in material success.

  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of nobility, recognizing the contributions of the less visible members of society, the value of friendship, and the significance of human relationships over material wealth. In real life, these principles can guide individuals in their personal and professional lives, encouraging them to act with integrity, appreciate the contributions of others, and prioritize meaningful relationships. By embodying these values, one can foster a more compassionate and connected community.
