मां नो रक्ष आ वेशीद आगृह्णीवसो मां यातुर यातुमावताम् |परोगव्यूत्य अनिराम अप क्षुधम् अग्ने सेध रक्षस्विनः ||
mā no rakṣa ā veśīd āghṛṇīvaso mā yātur yātumāvatām | parogavyūty anirām apa kṣudham agne sedha rakṣasvinaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • mā: not
    • no: our
    • rakṣa: protection
    • ā: towards
    • veśīd: may it come
    • āghṛṇīvaso: of the one who is worthy of praise
    • mā: not
    • yātur: may it go away
    • yātumāvatām: may it not depart
    • parogavyūty: from the external dangers
    • anirām: without any hindrance
    • apa: away
    • kṣudham: hunger
    • agne: O Agni (Fire)
    • sedha: may you direct
    • rakṣasvinaḥ: towards the protectors
  • Overall Meaning: This mantra is a prayer for protection and guidance. It invokes Agni, the fire god, asking for safety from external dangers and hunger. The speaker seeks to ensure that nothing harmful approaches them and that they are shielded by divine protection.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, individuals often seek protection from various forms of harm, whether physical, emotional, or spiritual. For instance, parents pray for the safety of their children as they venture into the world.
    • In times of crisis, such as natural disasters or personal challenges, people may turn to spiritual practices or rituals that echo the sentiments of this mantra, asking for divine intervention and safety.
    • In a corporate setting, leaders may invoke this mantra metaphorically when making decisions that protect the interests of their employees and stakeholders, ensuring that the organization remains safe from external threats.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its universal appeal for safety and protection. It emphasizes the importance of seeking divine guidance in navigating life's challenges. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, from personal safety to organizational well-being, reminding us of the need for vigilance and the power of prayer in seeking protection from the uncertainties of life.
