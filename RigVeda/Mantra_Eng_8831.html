सप्त स्वसृर अरुशीर्वावशानो विद्वान् मध्वा उज जभारा दृष्टे कम् |अन्तर येमे अन्तरिक्षे पुराजा इच्छन् वव्रिम अविदत पूषणस्य ||
sapta svasṝr aruṣīr vāvaśāno vidvān madhva uj jabhārā dṛśe kam | antar yeme antarikṣe purājā icchan vavrim avidat pūṣaṇasya ||
  • Mantra: "sapta svasṝr aruṣīr vāvaśāno vidvān madhva uj jabhārā dṛśe kam | antar yeme antarikṣe purājā icchan vavrim avidat pūṣaṇasya ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • Sapta: Seven
    • Svasṝr: Sisters or streams
    • Aruṣīr: Red or radiant
    • Vāvaśāno: One who is knowledgeable or wise
    • Vidvān: The learned or wise one
    • Madhva: Sweetness or nectar
    • Uj: Bright or shining
    • Jabhārā: Has taken or brought
    • Dṛśe: To the sight or vision
    • Kam: Whom
    • Antar: Within
    • Yeme: Those who
    • Antarikṣe: In the atmosphere or space
    • Purājā: The ancient or primordial
    • Icchan: Wishing or desiring
    • Vavrim: I have come forth
    • Avidat: To know or understand
    • Pūṣaṇasya: Of the nourisher or sustainer
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra speaks of the "seven radiant sisters," which can be interpreted as the seven rivers or streams of knowledge and wisdom that flow through life.
    • The term "vidvān" emphasizes the importance of wisdom and knowledge in navigating life's challenges, akin to how a wise mentor guides a student.
    • "Madhva uj" suggests the sweetness of life that comes from understanding and enlightenment, similar to the joy one feels when achieving a long-sought goal.
    • The phrase "antar yeme antarikṣe purājā" indicates a connection to the cosmos and the ancient wisdom that resides within it, reminding us of our place in the universe.
    • In a practical sense, this mantra encourages individuals to seek knowledge and wisdom, much like how a scientist seeks to understand the laws of nature.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the pursuit of knowledge and the importance of wisdom in our lives.
    • It is applicable in real-life scenarios such as education, personal growth, and decision-making, where understanding and insight lead to better outcomes.
    • By embracing the teachings of this mantra, individuals can cultivate a deeper connection with themselves and the world around them, fostering a life of purpose and fulfillment.
