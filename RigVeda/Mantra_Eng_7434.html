कस्य नूनं परीनसो धियो जिन्वसि दाम्पते |गोषातां यस्य ते गिरः ||
kasya nūnam parīṇaso dhiyo jinvasi dampate | goṣātā yasya te giraḥ ||
  • Mantra: "kasya nūnam parīṇaso dhiyo jinvasi dampate | goṣātā yasya te giraḥ ||"
  • Translation: "Whose indeed are the thoughts that you, O Lord of wealth, inspire? Whose are the words that come forth from you?"
  • Word Breakdown:
    • kasya: Whose
    • nūnam: Indeed
    • parīṇaso: Of the one who is rich or wealthy
    • dhiyo: Thoughts or intellect
    • jinvasi: You inspire or awaken
    • dampate: O Lord of wealth
    • goṣātā: From whom or from which
    • yasya: Whose
    • te: Your
    • giraḥ: Words or speech
  • Contextual Meaning:
    • This mantra addresses a divine figure, often interpreted as a deity associated with wealth and prosperity, such as Lord Kubera or Lord Ganesha.
    • It reflects a deep inquiry into the source of inspiration and wisdom, emphasizing the connection between wealth, intellect, and divine guidance.
    • The mantra suggests that true wealth is not just material but also intellectual and spiritual, highlighting the importance of wisdom in achieving prosperity.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be applied to the importance of ethical leadership. A leader who inspires their team with wisdom and integrity can create a prosperous environment.
    • In personal life, individuals seeking success may reflect on their motivations and the sources of their inspiration, ensuring they align with values that promote both material and spiritual wealth.
    • In education, teachers who inspire students to think critically and creatively embody the essence of this mantra, as they shape the thoughts and words of future generations.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of wealth, wisdom, and divine inspiration.
    • It encourages individuals to seek not just material success but also intellectual and spiritual growth.
    • In real life, applying the principles of this mantra can lead to a more fulfilling and prosperous existence, where one's thoughts and actions are aligned with higher values.
