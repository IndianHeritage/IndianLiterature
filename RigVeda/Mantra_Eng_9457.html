यं देवासोऽवथ वाजसातौ यं शूरसातां मरुतो हिते धनें | प्रातर्यावाणं रथम इन्द्र सानसिं अरिष्यन्तम ā रुहेमā स्वस्तये ||
yaṁ devāso 'vatha vājasātau yaṁ śūrasātā maruto hite dhane | prātaryāvāṇaṁ ratham indra sānasim ariṣyantam ā ruhemā svastaye ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yaṁ: Whom
    • devāso: The gods
    • 'vatha: Have chosen
    • vājasātau: In the context of strength or power
    • yaṁ: Whom
    • śūrasātā: The brave or valiant
    • maruto: The storm gods or winds
    • hite: For the benefit
    • dhane: Wealth or prosperity
    • prātaryāvāṇaṁ: In the morning, the one who drives
    • ratham: The chariot
    • indra: The king of gods
    • sānasim: The one who is powerful
    • ariṣyantam: The one who conquers enemies
    • ā: To
    • ruhemā: We may attain
    • svastaye: For well-being or auspiciousness
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the blessings of the gods, particularly Indra, who is associated with strength, power, and victory.
    • It emphasizes the importance of divine support in achieving success and prosperity.
    • The reference to the morning suggests new beginnings and the potential for growth and achievement.
    • The mention of the storm gods (Maruts) indicates the dynamic forces of nature that can aid in overcoming obstacles.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be seen as a call for divine guidance and support in achieving success and overcoming challenges.
    • In personal life, individuals may recite this mantra during significant life events, such as starting a new job or embarking on a new venture, to seek blessings for prosperity and well-being.
    • In community gatherings or rituals, this mantra can be chanted to invoke collective strength and unity for achieving common goals.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of divine intervention and support in our endeavors.
    • It serves as a reminder that strength, bravery, and the blessings of the gods are essential for overcoming challenges and achieving prosperity.
    • In real life, it encourages individuals and communities to seek divine guidance and work together towards common goals, fostering a sense of unity and purpose.
