आ नो देव शवसां याहि शुष्मिन् भव वृद्ध इन्द्र रायो अस्य |महें नृम्णाय नृपते सुवज्र महि क्षत्राय पौंस्याय शूर ||
ā no deva śavasā yāhi śuṣmin bhavā vṛdha indra rāyo asya | mahe nṛmṇāya nṛpate suvajra mahi kṣatrāya pauṁsyāya śūra ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā no: "Come to us"
    • deva: "O divine beings"
    • śavasā: "with strength"
    • yāhi: "come"
    • śuṣmin: "to the one who is dry or barren"
    • bhavā: "become"
    • vṛdha: "great or powerful"
    • indra: "O Indra (the king of gods)"
    • rāyo: "wealth or prosperity"
    • asya: "of this"
    • mahe: "great"
    • nṛmṇāya: "for the mankind"
    • nṛpate: "O lord of men"
    • suvajra: "with good strength"
    • mahi: "great"
    • kṣatrāya: "for the warrior class"
    • pauṁsyāya: "for the male or masculine"
    • śūra: "brave or valiant"
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to the divine beings, asking them to come to us with strength and to transform the barren into fruitful.
    • It invokes Indra, the king of gods, to bless us with prosperity and wealth.
    • The mantra emphasizes the importance of strength and valor, particularly for leaders and warriors, highlighting the need for courage in facing challenges.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be seen as a call for leaders to seek divine guidance and strength to overcome challenges and achieve prosperity.
    • In personal life, individuals may recite this mantra during times of struggle, seeking strength and support to transform their circumstances.
    • In community settings, it can be a rallying cry for collective strength and bravery in the face of adversity, such as during natural disasters or social challenges.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine assistance in achieving strength, prosperity, and courage.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal struggles to leadership challenges, reminding us of the importance of seeking support and embodying bravery.
    • Ultimately, it encourages individuals and communities to strive for greatness and to transform their lives positively with the help of divine forces.
