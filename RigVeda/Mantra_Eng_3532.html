आ नो यातं दिवो अच्‍छा पृथिव्याः हिरण्येन सुवृतā रथेन |मा वाम अन्ये न यमन देवयन्तः सं यद ददे नाभिः पूर्व्याः वाम ||
ā no yātaṁ divo acchā pṛthivyā hiraṇyayena suvṛtā rathena | mā vām anye ni yaman devayantaḥ saṁ yad dade nābhiḥ pūrvyā vām ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā no yātaṁ: May we go forth
    • dīvo: to the heavens
    • acchā: well
    • pṛthivyā: on the earth
    • hiraṇyayena: with golden (prosperity)
    • suvṛtā: well-directed
    • rathena: with the chariot
    • mā vām: may not (anyone)
    • anye: others
    • ni yaman: lead us away
    • devayantaḥ: by the divine
    • saṁ: together
    • yad: whatever
    • dade: gives
    • nābhiḥ: from the navel
    • pūrvya: from the past
    • vām: to you
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra expresses a desire for collective progress and prosperity.
    • It emphasizes the importance of unity and divine guidance in achieving goals.
    • The reference to "golden chariot" symbolizes a journey towards wealth and success.
    • The plea to not be led astray by others highlights the need for focus and determination.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a team project, members may invoke this mantra to foster collaboration and ensure that they remain focused on their common goal.
    • In personal development, individuals can reflect on this mantra to seek guidance and clarity in their life choices, ensuring they are not distracted by negative influences.
    • In community initiatives, this mantra can serve as a reminder to work together harmoniously towards shared objectives, such as environmental conservation or social welfare.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for unity, prosperity, and divine guidance.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, including teamwork, personal growth, and community efforts.
    • By embodying the principles of this mantra, individuals and groups can navigate challenges effectively and achieve their aspirations.
