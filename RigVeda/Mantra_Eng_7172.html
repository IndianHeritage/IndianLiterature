मन्त्र: सुतावन्तस् त्वा वयम् प्रयस्वन्तो हवामहे | इदं नो बर्हिर आसदे ||
sutāvantas tvā vayam prayasvanto havāmahe | idaṁ no barhir āsade ||
  • Mantra: "sutāvantas tvā vayam prayasvanto havāmahe | idaṁ no barhir āsade ||"
  • Translation: "We, who are endowed with wisdom, invoke you with our efforts; may this offering be accepted by you."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • sutāvantas: Those who are wise or knowledgeable.
    • tvā: You (referring to a deity or divine presence).
    • vayam: We.
    • prayasvanto: With effort or striving.
    • havāmahe: We invoke or call upon.
    • idaṁ: This.
    • no: Our.
    • barhir: Offering or sacrifice.
    • āsade: May it be accepted.
  • Context and Significance:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of wisdom and effort in spiritual practices.
    • It reflects the belief that knowledge and sincere effort are essential for invoking divine blessings.
    • In real life, this can be seen in various contexts such as education, where students must apply themselves diligently to gain knowledge.
    • In professional settings, individuals who strive and apply their skills are more likely to achieve success and recognition.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • A student studying for exams embodies the essence of "prayasvanto" by putting in the effort to understand the material, invoking success in their academic pursuits.
    • A professional working hard on a project demonstrates "sutāvantas" by using their knowledge and skills to achieve a successful outcome, thereby invoking appreciation from peers and superiors.
    • In community service, volunteers who dedicate their time and effort to help others reflect the mantra's call for invoking positive change through wisdom and action.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder that wisdom and effort are crucial in all aspects of life.
    • It encourages individuals to strive for knowledge and to seek divine guidance in their endeavors.
    • By applying the principles of this mantra, one can foster personal growth, achieve goals, and contribute positively to society.
