अहं तष्टेवा वन्धुरम् पर्य आचामि हृदा मतीम् |कुवित सोमस्यापाम इति ||
ahaṁ taṣṭeva vandhuram pary acāmi hṛdā matim | kuvit somasyāpām iti ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ahaṁ: I
    • taṣṭeva: stand firm
    • vandhuram: as a friend
    • pary: around
    • acāmi: I move
    • hṛdā: with my heart
    • matim: my mind
    • kuvit: what is good
    • somasyāpām: of the waters of the moon
    • iti: thus
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra expresses a deep connection between the self and the universe.
    • The speaker asserts their presence and intention, standing firm as a friend, indicating a sense of loyalty and support.
    • The phrase "with my heart" suggests that the actions and thoughts are driven by genuine feelings and intentions.
    • The reference to "the waters of the moon" symbolizes purity, nourishment, and the cyclical nature of life, akin to how water sustains life on Earth.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In personal relationships, standing firm as a friend can mean being there for someone during tough times, offering emotional support and understanding.
    • In a professional setting, this mantra can inspire teamwork, where individuals work together with a shared heart and mind towards a common goal.
    • The concept of "the waters of the moon" can be related to the importance of nurturing our environment, as water is essential for life, and we must protect our natural resources.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of steadfastness, friendship, and heartfelt intentions in our actions.
    • It encourages individuals to connect deeply with themselves and others, fostering a sense of community and support.
    • In real life, applying the principles of this mantra can lead to stronger relationships, better teamwork, and a more sustainable approach to our environment.
