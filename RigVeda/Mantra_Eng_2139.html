यो नर्मरं सहवसुः निहन्तवे पृक्षाय च दासवेशाय चावहः |ऊर्जयन्त्याः अपरिविष्टम आस्यम उतैवाद्य पुरुकृत सास्य उक्त्यः ||
yo nārmaraṁ sahavasuṁ nihantave pṛkṣāya ca dāsaveśāya cāvahaḥ | ūrjayantyā apariviṣṭam āsyam utaivādya purukṛt sāsy ukthyaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yo: who
    • nārmaraṁ: the one who is not mortal
    • sahavasuṁ: the one who is with wealth
    • nihantave: the one who destroys
    • pṛkṣāya: for the purpose of examination
    • ca: and
    • dāsaveśāya: for the one who is the master of gifts
    • cāvahaḥ: and brings forth
    • ūrjayantyā: the one who energizes
    • apariviṣṭam: not surrounded
    • āsyam: the mouth or speech
    • utaivādya: and today
    • purukṛt: the one who performs great deeds
    • sāsy: the one who brings forth abundance
    • ukthyaḥ: the one who is praised
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of a divine entity that is beyond mortality and is associated with wealth and abundance.
    • The phrase "nihantave pṛkṣāya" suggests a purpose of examination or testing, indicating that this divine presence is involved in the evaluation of actions and intentions.
    • The mention of "dāsaveśāya" highlights the importance of generosity and the act of giving, which is a significant virtue in many cultures.
    • The term "ūrjayantyā" refers to the energizing force, suggesting that this divine presence invigorates and empowers individuals.
    • The mantra concludes with a call to recognize and praise the one who performs great deeds and brings forth abundance, emphasizing the importance of gratitude and acknowledgment.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, a leader who embodies the qualities described in this mantra can inspire their team to achieve great results while fostering a culture of generosity and support.
    • In personal life, individuals who practice gratitude and recognize the contributions of others can create a positive environment that encourages collaboration and abundance.
    • In community service, those who give selflessly and uplift others reflect the essence of this mantra, demonstrating the power of collective effort in achieving common goals.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the divine qualities that we can aspire to embody in our lives.
    • It emphasizes the significance of generosity, the importance of recognizing and uplifting others, and the power of collective action.
    • In real-life scenarios, applying the principles of this mantra can lead to personal growth, stronger relationships, and a more harmonious community.
