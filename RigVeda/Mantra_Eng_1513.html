ताम अनु त्वा नवीयसीं नियुतं राया ईमहे |अहेळमाना उरुशंस सरि भव वाजे-वाजे सरि भव ||
tām anu tvā navīyasīṁ niyutaṁ rāya īmahe | aheḻamāna uruśaṁsa sarī bhava vāje-vāje sarī bhava ||
  • tām anu tvā navīyasīṁ: This phrase can be translated as "We seek that which is new and fresh." The word "navīyasīṁ" signifies something that is innovative or rejuvenating. In real life, this can relate to the pursuit of new ideas, technologies, or experiences that enhance our lives.
  • niyutaṁ rāya īmahe: This translates to "We invoke the wealth and prosperity." Here, "niyutaṁ" refers to the abundance or wealth, and "rāya" signifies prosperity. In practical terms, this can be seen in our efforts to achieve financial stability, career growth, or personal development.
  • aheḻamāna uruśaṁsa: This can be interpreted as "the one who is powerful and strong." "Aheḻamāna" suggests strength or might, while "uruśaṁsa" refers to greatness. In everyday life, this can be related to the qualities of leadership, resilience, and the ability to overcome challenges.
  • sarī bhava vāje-vāje: This phrase means "may you become the source of strength and energy." "Sarī" implies being or existence, while "vāje-vāje" refers to the essence of vigor or power. This can be applied in real life by encouraging individuals to harness their inner strength and energy to achieve their goals.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking new opportunities, invoking prosperity, embodying strength, and becoming a source of energy. In a world that is constantly changing, this mantra serves as a reminder to embrace innovation, strive for personal and financial growth, and cultivate resilience. It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, such as in professional settings where creativity and strength are essential for success, or in personal development where one seeks to improve and evolve continuously.
