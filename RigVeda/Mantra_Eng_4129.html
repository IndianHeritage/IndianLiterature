आभूषेण्यं वो मरुतो महित्वनं दिदृक्षेण्यं सूर्यस्येव चक्षणम् |उतो अस्मांऽ अमृतत्वे दधातना शुभं याताम् अनु रथाऽ अवृत्तसत ||
ābhūṣeṇyaṁ vo maruto mahitvanaṁ didṛkṣeṇyaṁ sūryasyeva cakṣaṇam | uto asmām̐ amṛtatve dadhātana śubhaṁ yātām anu rathā avṛtsata ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ābhūṣeṇyaṁ: With brilliance or radiance
    • vo: To you
    • maruto: O Winds (or Maruts, the storm deities)
    • mahitvanaṁ: Of great power or might
    • didṛkṣeṇyaṁ: With the desire to see or witness
    • sūryasyeva: Like the sun
    • cakṣaṇam: Eyes or vision
    • uto: May it be
    • asmām̐: For us
    • amṛtatve: In immortality or eternal life
    • dadhātana: Bestowing or granting
    • śubhaṁ: Auspiciousness or goodness
    • yātām: May it go or come
    • anu: Following or in accordance with
    • rathā: Chariots or vehicles
    • avṛtsata: May it be protected or shielded
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the Maruts, the powerful storm deities, asking them to bestow their brilliance upon us.
    • It likens the vision we seek to that of the sun, symbolizing clarity, enlightenment, and the pursuit of knowledge.
    • The mantra expresses a desire for immortality and auspiciousness, suggesting that these qualities are essential for a fulfilling life.
    • It emphasizes the importance of protection and guidance as we journey through life, akin to a chariot that needs to be shielded from obstacles.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, one might seek the guidance of mentors (akin to the Maruts) to illuminate their path and help them achieve their career goals.
    • In personal development, individuals often strive for clarity and enlightenment, much like seeking the sun's light to dispel darkness in their lives.
    • In relationships, the desire for auspiciousness can be seen in the efforts people make to create harmonious and supportive environments.
    • In times of challenge, individuals may seek protection and guidance, similar to how a chariot is shielded from the elements.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of seeking guidance, clarity, and protection in our lives.
    • Its significance lies in the acknowledgment of the forces that aid us in our journey, encouraging us to strive for greatness while remaining grounded in our quest for knowledge and auspiciousness.
    • In real-life scenarios, it can be applied in various contexts, from personal growth to professional endeavors, emphasizing the interconnectedness of our aspirations and the divine forces that support us.
