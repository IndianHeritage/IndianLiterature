इन्दुः पविष्ट चेतनः प्रियः कवीनाम् मती |सृजद अश्वं रथीर इव ||
induḥ paviṣṭa cetanaḥ priyaḥ kavīnām matī | sṛjad aśvaṁ rathīr iva ||
  • Induḥ: This word translates to "moon" or "nectar." In Vedic literature, it often symbolizes the source of nourishment and bliss.
  • Paviṣṭa: This means "entered" or "pervaded." It indicates a presence that is all-encompassing.
  • Cetanaḥ: This refers to "consciousness" or "awareness." It signifies the essence of being aware and alive.
  • Priyaḥ: This translates to "beloved" or "dear." It denotes something that is cherished and valued.
  • Kavīnām: This means "of the poets" or "of the wise." It refers to those who possess deep insight and creativity.
  • Matī: This translates to "intellect" or "thought." It signifies the mental faculties that guide decision-making and creativity.
  • Sṛjad: This means "to create" or "to produce." It indicates the act of bringing something into existence.
  • Aśvaṁ: This translates to "horse." In Vedic culture, the horse symbolizes strength, speed, and nobility.
  • Rathīr: This means "chariot." The chariot represents the vehicle of progress and movement in life.
  • Iva: This translates to "like" or "as." It is used for comparison, indicating similarity.

The mantra can be interpreted as a call for the divine consciousness (Induḥ) to enter and pervade our awareness (Paviṣṭa cetanaḥ), making it dear (Priyaḥ) to the wise (Kavīnām) and guiding our intellect (Matī). It emphasizes the creation (Sṛjad) of strength and progress (Aśvaṁ rathīr iva) in our lives.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios:

  • Personal Growth: Just as the moon nourishes the earth, we can seek to cultivate our consciousness and intellect to foster personal development.
  • Creative Endeavors: The reference to poets and wisdom highlights the importance of creativity in our lives. Engaging in artistic pursuits can lead to profound insights.
  • Leadership and Progress: The imagery of the horse and chariot symbolizes the drive for progress. In leadership roles, one can draw inspiration from this mantra to guide teams towards success.

Conclusion: This mantra signifies the importance of nurturing our consciousness and intellect, fostering creativity, and striving for progress in our lives. By embodying these principles, we can create a fulfilling and meaningful existence, much like the harmonious balance found in nature.
