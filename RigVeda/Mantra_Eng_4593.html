स ईं स्पृधो वनते अप्रतीतो बिभ्रद् वज्रं वृत्रहणं गभस्तौ |तिष्ठद् धरी अध्य अस्तेवा गर्ते वचोयुजा वहत इन्द्रं ऋष्वम् ||
sa īṁ spṛdho vanate apratīto bibhrad vajraṁ vṛtrahaṇaṁ gabhastau | tiṣṭhad dharī adhy asteva garte vacoyujā vahata indram ṛṣvam ||
  • sa īṁ spṛdho vanate: This phrase can be interpreted as "He who is swift and powerful." It signifies the dynamic and energetic nature of the divine force, akin to how a river flows swiftly, carving its path through the landscape.
  • apratīto bibhrad vajraṁ: This translates to "holding the unapproachable thunderbolt." The thunderbolt (vajra) symbolizes strength and indestructibility, much like a mountain that stands firm against the elements.
  • vṛtrahaṇaṁ gabhastau: This means "the slayer of Vritra, the one who brings forth light." Vritra represents obstacles or ignorance, and the act of slaying him symbolizes overcoming challenges, similar to how a student conquers difficult subjects through perseverance.
  • tiṣṭhad dharī adhy asteva garte: This can be understood as "standing firm, he who supports the heavens." It reflects stability and support, much like a mentor who provides guidance and strength to their mentees.
  • vacoyujā vahata indram ṛṣvam: This translates to "with the power of speech, he carries Indra, the mighty." This emphasizes the importance of communication and expression, akin to how a leader inspires their team through effective dialogue.

  • Significance: This mantra encapsulates the essence of strength, stability, and the power of communication. It highlights the divine qualities that help individuals overcome obstacles and achieve greatness.
  • Real-life Application: In everyday life, this mantra can inspire individuals to harness their inner strength, communicate effectively, and support others in their journey. For instance, a teacher can embody these qualities by guiding students through challenges, much like the divine force described in the mantra.

  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a reminder of the power within each individual to overcome challenges and support others. By embodying the qualities of strength, stability, and effective communication, one can navigate life's obstacles and inspire those around them.
