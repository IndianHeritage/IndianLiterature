सखाय आ शिषामहि ब्रह्मेन्द्राय वज्रीणि |स्तुष उषु वो नृतमाया धृष्णवे ||
sakhāya ā śiṣāmahi brahmendrāya vajriṇe | stuṣa ū ṣu vo nṛtamāya dhṛṣṇave ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sakhāya: O friends or companions
    • ā: may we attain
    • śiṣāmahi: we invoke or we call upon
    • brahmendrāya: to the Lord of Brahman or the supreme being
    • vajriṇe: the one who wields power or strength
    • stuṣa: we praise
    • ū: and
    • ṣu: may we be blessed
    • vo: for you
    • nṛtamāya: for the truth or righteousness
    • dhṛṣṇave: for the one who is steadfast or strong
  • Overall Meaning: This mantra is a call to friends and companions to invoke the supreme power, Brahman, who is strong and righteous. It emphasizes the importance of unity and collective strength in seeking blessings and truth.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • Teamwork: In a workplace, when a team comes together to achieve a common goal, they embody the essence of "sakhāya." By invoking collective strength and righteousness, they can overcome challenges.
    • Community Support: In times of crisis, communities often unite to support one another. This mantra reflects the power of collective action and the importance of standing together for truth and justice.
    • Spiritual Gatherings: During spiritual or religious gatherings, individuals come together to invoke divine blessings, similar to the invocation in this mantra, seeking strength and guidance from a higher power.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its emphasis on unity, strength, and the pursuit of truth. It teaches us that by coming together as friends and invoking the divine, we can achieve greater heights and navigate the complexities of life with righteousness. This mantra is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from teamwork in professional settings to community support during challenging times, reminding us of the power of collective effort and the importance of seeking truth and strength in our endeavors.
