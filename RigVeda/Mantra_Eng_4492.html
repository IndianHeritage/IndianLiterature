जनीष्वा देववीतये सर्वताता स्वस्तये |आ देवान वक्ष्य अमृतां ऋतावृद्धो यज्ञं देवेषु पिष्पृशः ||
janiṣvā devavītaye sarvatātā svastaye | ā devān vakṣy amṛtām̐ ṛtāvṛdho yajñaṁ deveṣu pispṛśaḥ ||
  • janiṣvā: This word translates to "may we be born" or "let us be born." It signifies the desire for new beginnings and the importance of life and creation.
  • devavītaye: This term means "for the sake of the divine" or "in the service of the gods." It emphasizes the connection between human actions and divine will.
  • sarvatātā: This word can be interpreted as "all-pervading" or "universal." It suggests the idea of unity and interconnectedness in the universe.
  • svastaye: This translates to "for well-being" or "for prosperity." It highlights the importance of health, happiness, and success in life.
  • ā devān: This phrase means "from the gods." It indicates a source of blessings and divine support.
  • vakṣy: This word means "I will speak" or "I will declare." It signifies the act of communication and the importance of sharing knowledge.
  • amṛtām̐: This term translates to "immortal" or "eternal." It represents the idea of transcendence and the pursuit of everlasting truth.
  • ṛtāvṛdho: This means "abounding in truth" or "rich in righteousness." It emphasizes the value of living a life aligned with truth and moral principles.
  • yajñaṁ: This word refers to "sacrifice" or "offering." In a broader sense, it signifies the importance of selflessness and contributing to the greater good.
  • deveṣu: This translates to "among the gods." It indicates the presence of divine beings and the significance of their role in human affairs.
  • pispṛśaḥ: This term means "may they touch" or "may they bless." It conveys the idea of divine grace and the blessings that come from the gods.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a profound message about the interconnectedness of life, the divine, and the pursuit of well-being. It emphasizes the importance of being born into a life that serves the divine, promotes universal well-being, and is rich in truth and righteousness. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire individuals to engage in selfless acts, seek divine guidance, and strive for a life that contributes positively to society. It serves as a reminder that our actions have a ripple effect, influencing not only our lives but also the lives of others and the world around us. By embodying the principles of this mantra, individuals can foster a sense of community, purpose, and spiritual fulfillment in their daily lives.
