अनेहसम् प्रतारणं विवक्षणम् मध्वः स्वादिष्टम् ईम पिब |आ यथा मंदसानः किरासि नः प्र क्षुद्रेव तमनā धृतशत ||
anehasam prataraṇaṁ vivakṣaṇam madhvaḥ svādiṣṭham īm piba | ā yathā mandasānaḥ kirāsi naḥ pra kṣudreva tmanā dhṛṣat ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • anehasam: without hesitation or delay
    • prataraṇaṁ: crossing over or overcoming
    • vivakṣaṇam: the desire to express or articulate
    • madhvaḥ: sweet or pleasant
    • svādiṣṭham: that which is well-chosen or desirable
    • īm: may we drink or imbibe
    • piba: to drink
    • ā: as or like
    • yathā: just as
    • mandasānaḥ: one who is slow or lethargic
    • kirāsi: may you become or manifest
    • naḥ: for us
    • pra: before or in front of
    • kṣudreva: like a small or insignificant thing
    • tmanā: with the self or soul
    • dhṛṣat: courage or strength
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the importance of overcoming obstacles without hesitation.
    • It emphasizes the need to articulate our desires and imbibe the sweetness of life.
    • It suggests that just as a lethargic person can become active, we too can manifest our potential.
    • The mantra encourages us to approach challenges with courage and strength, rather than viewing them as insignificant.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, when faced with a challenging project, one might hesitate to take the first step. This mantra encourages individuals to act decisively and embrace the task at hand.
    • In personal relationships, expressing feelings can be daunting. The mantra reminds us to articulate our emotions and seek the sweetness of connection.
    • For students, the mantra serves as a reminder to overcome procrastination and approach studies with enthusiasm and courage.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call to action—encouraging individuals to overcome hesitation and embrace their desires.
    • It is applicable in various life scenarios, from personal growth to professional challenges, reminding us that courage and articulation are key to success.
    • Ultimately, it teaches us that by overcoming our inner lethargy and fears, we can achieve our goals and live a fulfilling life.
