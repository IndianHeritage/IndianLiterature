नū च पुरा च सदनं रयीणां जातस्य च जायमानस्य च क्षाम् |सतश्च गोपाम् भवतश्च भूरेर देवाः अग्निं धारयन द्रविणोदाम् ||
nū ca purā ca sadanaṁ rayīṇāṁ jātasya ca jāyamānasya ca kṣām | sataś ca gopām bhavataś ca bhūrer devā agniṁ dhārayan draviṇodām ||
  • nū ca purā ca sadanaṁ: This phrase can be translated as "now and in the past, the abode." It signifies the continuity of existence and the importance of a stable foundation in life.
  • rayīṇāṁ jātasya ca: This means "of the wealth that is born." It emphasizes the idea that wealth and prosperity are not just material but also spiritual and intellectual.
  • jāyamānasya ca kṣām: This translates to "of the one who is being born." It highlights the potential and opportunities that come with new beginnings, such as the birth of a child or the start of a new venture.
  • sataś ca gopām: This means "and of the hundred protectors." It suggests the presence of guardians or protectors in our lives, whether they are family, friends, or spiritual entities.
  • bhavataś ca bhūrer: This translates to "and of the earth." It signifies our connection to the earth and the importance of nurturing our environment.
  • devā agniṁ dhārayan draviṇodām: This means "the gods who hold the fire of wealth." Fire here symbolizes transformation and energy, indicating that divine forces are at work in the creation and sustenance of wealth.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda encapsulates the essence of life, wealth, and protection. It teaches us that our existence is a continuum, where the past informs the present and the future. In real life, this can be seen in the way families pass down traditions and values, ensuring that wealth—both material and spiritual—is preserved and nurtured. The mention of protectors reminds us of the importance of community and support systems in our lives. Furthermore, the connection to the earth emphasizes our responsibility towards nature and the environment. Overall, this mantra serves as a reminder to honor our roots, embrace new beginnings, and recognize the divine forces that guide us in our pursuit of prosperity and well-being.
