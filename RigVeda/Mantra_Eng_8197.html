मिमाति वह्निर एतशः पदं युजाना ऋक्वभिः |प्र यत समुद्र आहितः ||
mimāti vahnir etaśaḥ padaṁ yujāna ṛkvabhiḥ | pra yat samudra āhitaḥ ||
  • mimāti: This word can be translated as "moves" or "flows." It signifies the dynamic nature of energy or life.
  • vahnir: This refers to "fire." In many cultures, fire symbolizes transformation, purification, and energy.
  • etaśaḥ: This means "from here" or "from this place," indicating a specific origin or source.
  • padaṁ: Translated as "foot" or "step," it can represent a path or journey.
  • yujāna: This word means "to join" or "to unite," suggesting a coming together of forces or elements.
  • ṛkvabhiḥ: This refers to "with hymns" or "with verses," indicating the importance of words and expressions in invoking energy or divinity.
  • pra: This prefix means "forth" or "forward," suggesting movement or progression.
  • yat: This translates to "that" or "which," pointing to a specific subject or object.
  • samudra: This means "ocean" or "sea," symbolizing vastness, depth, and the collective unconscious.
  • āhitaḥ: This word means "set" or "placed," indicating something that has been established or positioned.

The mantra can be interpreted as a poetic expression of the movement of fire (energy) from a specific place, uniting with hymns, and progressing towards the vast ocean of existence. It emphasizes the interconnectedness of energy, expression, and the vastness of life.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various contexts:

  • Personal Growth: Just as fire transforms materials, individuals can transform their lives through knowledge and experience, moving forward on their personal journeys.
  • Community and Collaboration: The idea of uniting with hymns can be seen in collaborative efforts where individuals come together to achieve a common goal, much like a community working towards a shared vision.
  • Spiritual Practices: The invocation of energy through hymns can be related to meditation or prayer, where individuals seek to connect with a higher power or the universe.

In conclusion, this mantra signifies the dynamic interplay of energy, expression, and the vastness of existence. It encourages individuals to recognize their potential for transformation, the importance of collaboration, and the power of spiritual connection. By understanding and applying these principles, one can navigate life's journey with purpose and unity.
