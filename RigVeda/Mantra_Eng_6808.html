स चिकेट सहींयसाग्निश चित्रेण कर्मणा |स होतā शाश्वतीनां दक्षिणाभिर अभिवृत इनोति च प्रतिव्यं नभन्ताम् अन्यके समे ||
sa ciketa sahīyasāgniś citreṇa karmaṇā | sa hotā śaśvatīnāṁ dakṣiṇābhir abhīvṛta inoti ca pratīvya1ṁ nabhantām anyake same ||
  • sa ciketa sahīyasāgniś citreṇa karmaṇā: This phrase can be translated as "He who knows the fire, the one who is wise, through diverse actions." Here, "sa" refers to the divine or the higher power, "ciketa" means to know or understand, "sahīyasāgniś" refers to the fire that symbolizes knowledge and transformation, and "citreṇa karmaṇā" indicates the various actions or deeds that one undertakes in life.
  • sa hotā śaśvatīnāṁ: This part translates to "He is the priest of the eternal." "Hotā" refers to the priest or the one who offers sacrifices, and "śaśvatīnāṁ" means eternal or everlasting. This signifies the role of a guiding force that remains constant through time.
  • dakṣiṇābhir abhīvṛta: This translates to "surrounded by the auspicious." "Dakṣiṇā" refers to the right or auspicious direction, and "abhīvṛta" means surrounded or enveloped. This suggests that the divine presence is surrounded by positive energies and blessings.
  • inoti ca pratīvyaṁ nabhantām anyake same: This can be interpreted as "He who brings forth the offerings to the heavens and to others." "Inoti" means to bring forth or to offer, "pratīvyaṁ" refers to the heavens or the sky, "nabhantām" means the celestial, and "anyake same" indicates the connection to others or the community.

Conclusion:

The mantra emphasizes the importance of knowledge, wisdom, and the role of divine guidance in our actions. It highlights how our deeds, when aligned with higher wisdom, can lead to positive outcomes and blessings in our lives. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied to various aspects such as:

  • Personal Growth: Understanding the importance of knowledge and continuous learning can lead to personal development and transformation.
  • Community Service: Engaging in actions that benefit others and contribute to the community reflects the essence of the mantra.
  • Spiritual Practices: Incorporating rituals and offerings in spiritual practices can enhance one's connection to the divine and foster a sense of purpose.

Overall, this mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of knowledge, action, and divine guidance in our lives, encouraging us to act wisely and with intention.
