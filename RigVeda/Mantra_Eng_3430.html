अरम् मा उष्रयाम्णे 'राम अनुस्रयाम्णे |बभ्रू यामेष्व अस्रिधा ||
aram ma usrayāmṇe 'ram anusrayāmṇe | babhrū yāmeṣv asridhā ||
  • Mantra: "aram ma usrayāmṇe 'ram anusrayāmṇe | babhrū yāmeṣv asridhā ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • aram: peace, comfort, or well-being
    • ma: not, or negation
    • usrayāmṇe: to the one who is radiant or shining
    • 'ram: joy or delight
    • anusrayāmṇe: to the one who is following or accompanying
    • babhrū: possessing or holding
    • yāmeṣv: in the nights or during the night
    • asridhā: with protection or shelter
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of seeking peace and joy in the presence of a radiant being or divine entity.
    • The phrase "aram ma usrayāmṇe" suggests a desire for comfort and well-being, indicating that one should not be deprived of joy.
    • "Anusrayāmṇe" implies a connection or following of this radiant presence, suggesting that joy and peace are found in companionship with the divine.
    • The mention of "babhrū yāmeṣv asridhā" indicates that during the nights, one is under the protection of this divine presence, symbolizing safety and security.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In times of stress or anxiety, individuals often seek solace in meditation or prayer, reflecting the essence of this mantra by connecting with a higher power for peace and joy.
    • During difficult nights, such as when facing personal challenges, people may find comfort in the support of friends or family, paralleling the idea of being under the protective presence of a loved one.
    • In various cultures, rituals are performed at night to invoke peace and protection, demonstrating the universal need for comfort and security during vulnerable times.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking peace and joy through connection with the divine or supportive relationships.
    • It highlights the significance of feeling protected and secure, especially during challenging times, which is a universal human experience.
    • In real life, this mantra can inspire individuals to cultivate relationships that provide comfort and to engage in practices that foster a sense of peace and joy, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling life.
