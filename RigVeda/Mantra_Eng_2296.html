किम ū नु वः कृतवामापरेण किम् सनेन वसव āप्येन |यूयम् नो मित्रावरुणादिते च स्वस्तिम इन्द्रामरुतो दधात ||
kim ū nu vaḥ kṛṇavāmāpareṇa kiṁ sanena vasava āpyena | yūyaṁ no mitrāvaruṇādite ca svastim indrāmaruto dadhāta ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • kim: what
    • ū: indeed
    • nu: now
    • vaḥ: for you
    • kṛṇavāmā: we are doing
    • apareṇa: with another
    • kiṁ: what
    • sanena: with good fortune
    • vasava: the divine beings
    • āpyena: with abundance
    • yūyaṁ: you all
    • no: for us
    • mitrāvaruṇādite: from Mitra, Varuna, and Aditi
    • ca: and
    • svastim: well-being
    • indrāmaruto: Indra and the Maruts
    • dadhāta: may grant
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer seeking blessings and well-being from divine forces.
    • The speaker is inquiring about the actions they should take to receive these blessings.
    • It emphasizes the importance of community and collective well-being, as it addresses "you all" and "for us."
    • Mitra and Varuna represent cosmic order and moral law, while Aditi symbolizes the infinite and nurturing aspect of the universe.
    • Indra and the Maruts are associated with strength, storms, and vitality, indicating a request for dynamic energy and protection.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community gathering, people often come together to seek blessings for prosperity and harmony, similar to the collective prayer in this mantra.
    • During festivals, rituals are performed to invoke divine energies for the well-being of all, reflecting the essence of this mantra.
    • In personal life, individuals may reflect on their actions and seek guidance on how to align with higher principles for a fulfilling life.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the interconnectedness of individuals and the divine, highlighting the importance of seeking blessings for collective well-being.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, such as community prayers, personal reflections, and rituals that aim to foster harmony and abundance.
    • Ultimately, it encourages individuals to act with intention and seek guidance from higher powers to navigate life's challenges and enhance their well-being.
