यवं-यवं नो अन्धसा पुष्टं-पुष्टं परि श्रव || सोम विश्वा च सौभगा ||
yavaṁ-yavaṁ no andhasā puṣṭam-puṣṭam pari srava | soma viśvā ca saubhagā ||
  • Mantra: "yavaṁ-yavaṁ no andhasā puṣṭam-puṣṭam pari srava | soma viśvā ca saubhagā ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yavaṁ-yavaṁ: "whatever" or "all that"
    • no: "for us"
    • andhasā: "from darkness" or "from ignorance"
    • puṣṭam-puṣṭam: "nourished" or "prosperous"
    • pari srava: "flowing all around" or "spreading everywhere"
    • soma: "the divine nectar" or "soma drink"
    • viśvā: "all" or "universal"
    • ca: "and"
    • saubhagā: "auspicious" or "good fortune"
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra expresses a desire for abundance and prosperity in life.
    • The phrase "yavaṁ-yavaṁ no" indicates a request for all that is beneficial and nourishing.
    • "Andhasā" signifies a wish to be freed from ignorance and darkness, seeking enlightenment.
    • "Puṣṭam-puṣṭam" emphasizes the importance of nourishment, both physical and spiritual.
    • "Soma" represents the divine essence that brings joy and vitality, often associated with spiritual experiences.
    • "Viśvā ca saubhagā" conveys a universal appeal for auspiciousness and good fortune in all aspects of life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, this mantra can be invoked during rituals or meditative practices to attract positivity and abundance.
    • For instance, a person starting a new business may recite this mantra to seek prosperity and success.
    • In educational settings, students may use this mantra to seek clarity and understanding, moving away from ignorance.
    • During festivals, families may chant this mantra to invite blessings and good fortune into their homes.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the interconnectedness of nourishment, enlightenment, and prosperity.
    • Its significance lies in the holistic approach to life, encouraging individuals to seek abundance while also striving for knowledge and spiritual growth.
    • In real-life scenarios, it can be applied in various contexts, from personal development to communal celebrations, fostering a sense of unity and shared prosperity.
