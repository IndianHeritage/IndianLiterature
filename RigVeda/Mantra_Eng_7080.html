युवां देवास त्रय एकादशासः सत्याः सत्यस्य ददृशे पुरस्तात् |अस्माकं यज्ञं सवनं जुषाणां पातं सोमं अश्विनā दीद्यग्नि ||
yuvāṁ devās traya ekādaśāsaḥ satyāḥ satyasya dadṛśe purastāt | asmākaṁ yajñaṁ savanaṁ juṣāṇā pātaṁ somam aśvinā dīdyagnī ||
  • yuvāṁ devās traya ekādaśāsaḥ: This phrase refers to the "three young gods" or "three divine beings" who are often associated with the forces of nature and the cosmos. In a real-life context, this can be likened to the three primary forces in nature: creation, preservation, and destruction, which can be seen in various aspects of life, such as the cycle of life and death.
  • satyāḥ satyasya dadṛśe purastāt: This translates to "the truthful ones, who are seen in front." It emphasizes the importance of truth and integrity. In everyday life, this can be related to the value of honesty in relationships and the pursuit of truth in various fields, such as science, law, and personal interactions.
  • asmākaṁ yajñaṁ savanaṁ juṣāṇā: This part speaks about our offerings or sacrifices (yajña) that are pleasing to the divine. In modern terms, this can be understood as the acts of kindness and service we perform for others, which can be seen as a form of sacrifice for the greater good, such as volunteering or helping those in need.
  • pātaṁ somam aśvinā dīdyagnī: This line refers to the "nectar of immortality" (soma) and the divine twins, the Ashvins, who are associated with health and well-being. In real life, this can be interpreted as the pursuit of wellness and the importance of maintaining a healthy lifestyle, which can lead to a fulfilling and vibrant life.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of truth, divine connection, and the importance of our actions in the world. It signifies that by aligning ourselves with truth and performing selfless acts, we can connect with the divine and enhance our well-being. In real-life scenarios, this mantra encourages individuals to live with integrity, serve others, and strive for a balanced and healthy life, ultimately leading to a harmonious existence.
