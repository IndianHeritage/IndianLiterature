तुरण्यवोऽङ्गिरसो नक्षन्त रत्नं देवस्य सवितुर इयानाः |पिताऽच तन् नो महान यजत्रो विश्वे देवाः समनसो जुषन्त ||
turaṇyavo 'ṅgiraso nakṣanta ratnaṁ devasya savitur iyānāḥ | pitā ca tan no mahān yajatro viśve devāḥ samanaso juṣanta ||
  • turaṇyavo 'ṅgiraso: This phrase can be interpreted as "swift and powerful beings" or "those who are quick and energetic." In real life, this can relate to individuals or forces that act with speed and efficiency, such as emergency responders or innovative thinkers who bring about change rapidly.
  • nakṣanta ratnaṁ devasya: This translates to "the precious jewel of the divine." In a practical sense, this can refer to the valuable qualities or virtues that one seeks in life, such as wisdom, compassion, and integrity. For example, a person who embodies these virtues can be seen as a 'jewel' in their community.
  • savituh iyānāḥ: This means "those who are guided by the divine light." In everyday life, this can be likened to individuals who follow their moral compass or spiritual beliefs, leading them to make ethical decisions. For instance, a leader who prioritizes the well-being of their team over personal gain exemplifies this guidance.
  • pitā ca tan no mahān: This phrase translates to "may the great father (or creator) bless us." It signifies a call for divine blessings and support. In real life, this can be seen in rituals or prayers where individuals seek guidance and strength from a higher power, such as during significant life events like weddings or the birth of a child.
  • yajatro viśve devāḥ: This means "the universal deities who are worshipped." It reflects the idea of reverence for the divine in various forms. In practice, this can be observed in diverse religious practices where people come together to honor their beliefs, fostering community and shared values.
  • samanaso juṣanta: This translates to "may they be pleased with our offerings." It emphasizes the importance of gratitude and acknowledgment of the divine. In real life, this can be related to acts of kindness and generosity, where individuals express appreciation for the blessings they receive, such as volunteering or helping those in need.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking divine guidance, embodying virtues, and fostering community through reverence and gratitude. Its significance lies in the reminder to act with integrity, seek wisdom, and acknowledge the interconnectedness of all beings. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire individuals to lead lives that are not only successful but also meaningful, promoting harmony and respect within their communities.
