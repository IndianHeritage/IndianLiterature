यदारम अक्रन्न ऋभवः पितृभ्यां परिविष्टī वेषणा दṁसनाभिः |आद इद देवानाम उप सख्यम आयन धीरासः पुष्टिम अवहन मनायै ||
yadāram akrann ṛbhavaḥ pitṛbhyām pariviṣṭī veṣaṇā daṁsanābhiḥ | ād id devānām upa sakhyam āyan dhīrāsaḥ puṣṭim avahan manāyai ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yadāram: When we are engaged
    • akrann: in the act of creation
    • ṛbhavaḥ: with the wise or enlightened ones
    • pitṛbhyām: from the ancestors or forefathers
    • pariviṣṭī: surrounded or enveloped
    • veṣaṇā: by the manifestations or forms
    • daṁsanābhiḥ: with the power of speech or expression
    • ād: thus
    • id: this
    • devānām: of the divine beings
    • upa: near or close
    • sakhyam: friendship or companionship
    • āyan: the abode or dwelling
    • dhīrāsaḥ: the wise or prudent ones
    • puṣṭim: nourishment or prosperity
    • avahan: to bring or carry
    • manāyai: with the mind or intellect
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the process of creation and the importance of wisdom and guidance from our ancestors.
    • It emphasizes the significance of being surrounded by enlightened beings and the power of expression in manifesting our intentions.
    • The mention of divine beings and friendship suggests that collaboration and support from others are essential in our journey.
    • It highlights the role of the mind in achieving prosperity and nourishment, indicating that our thoughts and intentions shape our reality.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace setting, collaboration among team members can lead to innovative solutions, much like the wise beings mentioned in the mantra.
    • When starting a new project, seeking guidance from mentors or experienced individuals can provide valuable insights, akin to the wisdom of ancestors.
    • In personal relationships, fostering friendships and support systems can enhance our emotional well-being and lead to a more fulfilling life.
    • Practicing mindfulness and positive thinking can help in manifesting our goals, demonstrating the power of the mind as highlighted in the mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of creation, wisdom, and community.
    • It encourages us to seek guidance from those who have come before us and to cultivate meaningful relationships in our lives.
    • By harnessing the power of our minds and surrounding ourselves with supportive individuals, we can achieve prosperity and fulfillment in our endeavors.
    • Ultimately, it teaches us that our thoughts, actions, and connections shape our reality and that we are never alone in our journey.
