अपाधमद अभिशस्तीर अशस्तिहाथेन्द्रो द्युम्न्याभवत् |देवास त इन्द्र सख्याय येमिरे बृहद्भानो मरुद्गण ||
apādhamad abhiśastīr aśastihāthendro dyumny ābhavat | devās ta indra sakhyāya yemire bṛhadbhāno marudgaṇa ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • apādhamad: One who is free from faults or imperfections.
    • abhiśastīr: One who is praised or honored.
    • aśastihāth: One who is strong or powerful.
    • indro: Referring to Indra, the king of gods in Vedic literature.
    • dyumny: Glory or fame.
    • ābhavat: Became or manifested.
    • devās: The gods or divine beings.
    • ta: Those.
    • indra: Again referring to Indra.
    • sakhyāya: For friendship or companionship.
    • yemire: Who have come together or united.
    • bṛhadbhāno: The one with great radiance or light.
    • marudgaṇa: The group of winds or storm gods.
  • Overall Meaning:
    • This mantra speaks of the divine qualities of Indra, who is free from faults and is honored for his strength and glory.
    • It highlights the unity of the gods, particularly Indra, and their friendship with one another, emphasizing the importance of collaboration and support among divine beings.
    • The mention of "bṛhadbhāno" suggests the immense power and radiance that comes from such unity.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace, when team members collaborate effectively, they can achieve greater success, much like the unity of the gods in this mantra.
    • In community service, individuals coming together for a common cause can create a significant impact, reflecting the strength and glory of collective efforts.
    • In personal relationships, fostering friendships based on mutual respect and support can lead to a fulfilling and harmonious life.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of unity, strength, and collaboration among individuals, whether in divine realms or human experiences.
    • It teaches us that by coming together and supporting one another, we can achieve greatness and radiate positivity in our lives.
    • In real-life scenarios, applying the principles of teamwork, friendship, and mutual respect can lead to personal and collective success.
