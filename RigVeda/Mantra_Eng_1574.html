अभीम ऋतस्य दोहनान अनूषत योनौ देवस्य सदने परिवृताḥ |अपाम उपस्थे विभृतो यद आवसद अध स्वधā अधयद याभिर ईयते ||
abhīm ṛtasya dohanā anūṣata yonau devasya sadane parīvṛtāḥ | apām upasthe vibhṛto yad āvasad adha svadhā adhayad yābhir īyate ||
  • abhīm ṛtasya dohanā: This phrase can be interpreted as "the fearless ones who are nourished by truth." Here, "abhīm" signifies fearlessness, while "ṛtasya" refers to truth or cosmic order. In real life, this can be related to individuals who stand firm in their beliefs and values, even in the face of adversity.
  • anūṣata yonau devasya sadane parīvṛtāḥ: This translates to "surrounded by the divine abode, in the womb of the universe." The term "yonau" refers to the source or womb, and "devasya sadane" indicates the dwelling of the divine. This can be likened to the idea of being in a nurturing environment, such as a supportive family or community that fosters growth and development.
  • apām upasthe vibhṛto: This means "spread out in the waters." The "apām" refers to waters, which can symbolize emotions, intuition, and the subconscious. In a practical sense, this can relate to how we navigate our emotional landscapes, drawing strength and wisdom from our experiences.
  • yad āvasad adha svadhā: This phrase translates to "where the self resides below." "Svadhā" refers to the self or one's essence. This can be understood as the importance of self-awareness and understanding one's true nature, which is crucial for personal growth and fulfillment.
  • adhayad yābhir īyate: This can be interpreted as "by which one is led." It emphasizes the guidance that comes from understanding one's truth and essence. In real life, this can be seen in how individuals often seek mentors or guiding principles that align with their values and beliefs.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of living a life rooted in truth, fearlessness, and self-awareness. It highlights the importance of being in a nurturing environment and drawing strength from our emotional experiences. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various contexts, such as personal development, leadership, and community building. By embodying these principles, individuals can navigate life's challenges with resilience and purpose, ultimately contributing to a harmonious existence both for themselves and those around them.
