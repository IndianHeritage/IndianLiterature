मन्त्र: उता नूनं यद इन्द्रियं करिष्यां इन्द्र पौंश्यम् | अद्या नकीष टद आमिनत ||
uta nūnaṁ yad indriyaṁ kariṣyā indra pauṁsyam | adyā nakiṣ ṭad ā minat ||
  • Mantra: "uta nūnaṁ yad indriyaṁ kariṣyā indra pauṁsyam | adyā nakiṣ ṭad ā minat ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • uta: indeed, certainly
    • nūnaṁ: now, at this moment
    • yad: that which
    • indriyaṁ: senses, faculties
    • kariṣyā: will perform, will act
    • indra: a deity, often associated with strength and power
    • pauṁsyam: of manhood, masculinity
    • adyā: today, in this day
    • nakiṣ: not, no
    • ṭad: that, it
    • ā: towards, to
    • minat: may it be fulfilled, may it happen
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra reflects a deep inquiry into the nature of human faculties and their potential.
    • It emphasizes the importance of acting with awareness and intention, particularly in the context of one's senses and capabilities.
    • The invocation of Indra suggests a call for strength and support in harnessing these faculties effectively.
    • The phrase "adyā nakiṣ ṭad ā minat" indicates a desire for fulfillment and realization of one's potential in the present moment.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, this mantra can inspire individuals to utilize their skills and senses to achieve their goals, such as a manager leading a team with clarity and purpose.
    • In personal development, it encourages mindfulness and awareness in daily actions, reminding individuals to act with intention rather than reactively.
    • In sports, athletes may invoke this mantra to channel their physical and mental faculties towards peak performance, drawing on their inner strength.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the power of human faculties and the importance of acting with purpose and strength.
    • Its significance lies in its applicability to various aspects of life, from personal growth to professional success.
    • By embracing the essence of this mantra, individuals can strive for fulfillment and effectiveness in their actions, ultimately leading to a more meaningful and impactful life.
