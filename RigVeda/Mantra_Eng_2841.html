सत्तो होता न ऋत्वियस् तिष्टिरे बर्हिर आनुषक् |अयुज्रन् प्रातर अद्रयः ||
satto hotā na ṛtviyas tistire barhir ānuṣak | ayujran prātar adrayaḥ ||
  • Mantra: "satto hotā na ṛtviyas tistire barhir ānuṣak | ayujran prātar adrayaḥ ||"
  • Translation: This mantra can be translated as: "The one who is true (sattva) is the priest (hotā) who does not deviate from the rituals (ṛtviyas) and who is steadfast in the performance of duties (tistire). They are like the dawn (prātar) that brings light and clarity, dispelling darkness (adrayaḥ)."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • sattva: Refers to truth, purity, and goodness. In real life, this can be seen in individuals who uphold honesty and integrity in their actions.
    • hotā: The priest or the one who performs rituals. This can be likened to a leader or a guide in society who helps others navigate through challenges.
    • ṛtviyas: Refers to the rituals or duties that must be performed. This emphasizes the importance of following one's responsibilities in life.
    • tistire: Means to be steadfast or unwavering. This can be compared to someone who remains committed to their goals despite obstacles.
    • barhir: Refers to the outer world or the environment. It signifies the context in which actions take place.
    • ānuṣak: Indicates the one who is connected or aligned with the truth.
    • prātar: Means dawn or morning, symbolizing new beginnings and enlightenment.
    • adrayaḥ: Refers to darkness or ignorance, which the dawn dispels.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • A teacher who embodies truth and integrity, guiding students through their educational journey, can be seen as a modern-day hotā.
    • A community leader who remains steadfast in their duties, advocating for the welfare of their community, exemplifies the qualities of tistire.
    • In a corporate setting, an employee who adheres to ethical practices and fulfills their responsibilities can be viewed as embodying the principles of ṛtviyas.
    • The dawn symbolizes hope and new opportunities, much like how individuals can start fresh each day, leaving behind past mistakes.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its emphasis on truth, duty, and steadfastness. In real life, it serves as a reminder to uphold integrity in our actions, remain committed to our responsibilities, and strive for enlightenment in our pursuits. By embodying these principles, individuals can contribute positively to their communities and foster an environment of trust and clarity. This mantra encourages us to be like the dawn, bringing light into the lives of others and dispelling the darkness of ignorance and deceit.
