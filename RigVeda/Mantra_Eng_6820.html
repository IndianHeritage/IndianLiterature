यद इन्द्राग्नि जनाः इमे विह्वयन्ते तना गिरा |अस्माकेबhir नृभिः वयं सासह्यामा पृथन्यतो वनुयाम वनुष्यतो नभन्ताम् अन्यके समे ||
yad indrāgnī janā ime vihvayante tanā girā | asmākebhir nṛbhir vayaṁ sāsahyāma pṛtanyato vanuyāma vanuṣyato nabhantām anyake same ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yad: that which
    • indrāgnī: Indra and Agni (the deities representing strength and fire)
    • janā: people or beings
    • ime: these
    • vihvayante: who are engaged in or are performing
    • tanā: with their bodies
    • girā: with words or speech
    • asmākebhir: by us
    • nṛbhir: among men
    • vayaṁ: we
    • sāsahyāma: may we be empowered
    • pṛtanyato: from the earth
    • vanuyāma: may we attain
    • vanuṣyato: from the human realm
    • nabhantām: may we reach the heavens
    • anyake: to others
    • same: in harmony
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the blessings of Indra and Agni, symbolizing strength and energy.
    • It emphasizes the importance of collective effort and unity among people.
    • The phrase "may we be empowered" suggests a desire for strength and capability to achieve goals.
    • It reflects a yearning to connect with both earthly and divine realms, seeking balance and harmony.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a team project, individuals must work together, combining their strengths (like Indra and Agni) to achieve a common goal.
    • In community service, people come together to uplift their surroundings, embodying the spirit of collective action.
    • In personal development, one may seek empowerment through education and self-improvement, striving for a balance between worldly and spiritual pursuits.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the power of unity and collective strength in achieving goals.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, such as teamwork, community building, and personal growth.
    • By invoking the blessings of divine forces, individuals and groups can aspire to reach greater heights while maintaining harmony with one another and the universe.
