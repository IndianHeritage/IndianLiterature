अभिवलग्याऽ cid अद्रिवः शीरषा यातुमतीनाम् |छिन्दि वटूरिणा पदा महावटूरिणा पदा ||
abhivlagyā cid adrivaḥ śīrṣā yātumatīnām | chindhi vaṭūriṇā padā mahāvaṭūriṇā padā ||
  • abhivlagyā: This term can be interpreted as "to be freed" or "to be liberated." It signifies the act of overcoming obstacles or limitations.
  • cid: This word means "consciousness" or "awareness." It represents the state of being aware and enlightened.
  • adrivaḥ: This can be translated as "without obstacles" or "unhindered." It emphasizes the idea of a path that is clear and unobstructed.
  • śīrṣā: This term refers to the "head" or "top." It symbolizes the pinnacle of thought or understanding.
  • yātumatīnām: This phrase means "of those who are wise" or "of the intelligent." It highlights the importance of wisdom in navigating life's challenges.
  • chindhi: This word means "to cut" or "to sever." It suggests the act of removing barriers or difficulties.
  • vaṭūriṇā: This term can be interpreted as "the great" or "the powerful." It denotes strength and capability.
  • padā: This word means "path" or "way." It signifies the journey one takes in life.
  • mahāvaṭūriṇā: This phrase translates to "the great powerful one." It emphasizes the significance of strength and wisdom in overcoming challenges.

The mantra essentially conveys a message of liberation through wisdom and strength. It encourages individuals to cut through obstacles with awareness and intelligence, leading to a clear and unobstructed path in life.


In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts:

  • Personal Growth: Individuals facing personal challenges can use this mantra as a reminder to seek wisdom and awareness in their decisions, helping them to overcome difficulties.
  • Professional Life: In a work environment, this mantra can inspire employees to approach problems with a clear mind and innovative thinking, cutting through bureaucratic obstacles to achieve their goals.
  • Spiritual Journey: For those on a spiritual path, this mantra serves as a guide to seek enlightenment and clarity, encouraging them to remove mental barriers that hinder their progress.

In conclusion, the significance of this mantra lies in its powerful message of overcoming obstacles through wisdom and strength. It serves as a reminder that with awareness and intelligence, one can navigate life's challenges effectively, leading to personal and collective growth.
