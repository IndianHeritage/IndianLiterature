यः पुष्पिणीश च प्रस्वश च धर्मणाधि दाने व्य अवनीर अधारयः |यश चासमाऽ जनो दिद्युतो दिव उरुर ऊर्वाम् अभितः सास्य उक्त्यः ||
yaḥ puṣpiṇīś ca prasvaś ca dharmaṇādhi dāne vy a1vanīr adhārayaḥ | yaś cāsamā ajano didyuto diva urur ūrvām̐ abhitaḥ sāsy ukthyaḥ ||
  • yaḥ puṣpiṇīś ca: This phrase refers to the one who is the lord of flowers or the one who brings forth blossoms. In a real-life context, this can symbolize creativity and the flourishing of ideas, much like how a gardener nurtures plants to bloom.
  • prasvaś ca: This term can be interpreted as the one who is powerful or strong. In everyday life, this can relate to individuals who exhibit strength in character or resilience in the face of challenges, such as a leader guiding their team through difficult times.
  • dharmaṇādhi dāne: This phrase emphasizes the importance of righteousness (dharma) and giving (dāne). It highlights the significance of ethical behavior and generosity in society. For example, charitable acts, such as donating to those in need, embody this principle.
  • vy a1vanīr adhārayaḥ: This can be understood as the one who supports or upholds the world. In practical terms, this could refer to individuals or organizations that work towards social welfare, such as NGOs that provide education and healthcare to underprivileged communities.
  • yaś cāsamā ajano: This part speaks of the one who is unmatched and unique. In real life, this could represent innovators or pioneers in various fields, such as technology or art, who create something entirely new and impactful.
  • didyuto diva urur ūrvām̐: This phrase can be interpreted as the one who shines brightly in the sky, symbolizing hope and inspiration. For instance, influential figures like scientists or activists who illuminate paths for future generations can be seen as embodying this quality.
  • abhitaḥ sāsy ukthyaḥ: This suggests the idea of being surrounded by abundance and prosperity. In a real-world scenario, this can relate to communities that thrive through cooperation and mutual support, leading to a flourishing environment for all.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of creativity, strength, righteousness, support, uniqueness, inspiration, and abundance. Its significance lies in the reminder of the values that contribute to a harmonious and prosperous society. In real-life scenarios, these principles can be applied in various contexts, such as community service, leadership, innovation, and personal development. By embodying these qualities, individuals can contribute positively to their surroundings, fostering a culture of growth and support for all.
