त्वं नश् चित्र ऊत्याऽ वसो राधांसी चोदय |अस्य रायस् त्वम् अग्ने रथीर् असि विदा गाधं तुचे तु नः ||
tvaṁ naś citra ūtyā vaso rādhāṁsi codaya | asya rāyas tvam agne rathīr asi vidā gādhaṁ tuce tu naḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • tvaṁ: You
    • naś: Our
    • citra: Various, colorful
    • ūtyā: Abundance, prosperity
    • vaso: Of wealth
    • rādhāṁsi: To inspire, to motivate
    • codaya: Encourage, stimulate
    • asya: Of this
    • rāyas: Wealth, prosperity
    • tvam: You
    • agne: O Agni (the fire god)
    • rathīr: Chariots, vehicles
    • asi: Are
    • vidā: Knowledge, wisdom
    • gādhaṁ: Deep, profound
    • tuce: To us
    • naḥ: Our
  • Overall Meaning:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Agni, the fire god, asking for inspiration and abundance.
    • The speaker seeks encouragement and motivation to achieve prosperity and success.
    • It emphasizes the importance of divine guidance in attaining wealth and wisdom.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be recited by entrepreneurs seeking inspiration to innovate and grow their ventures.
    • Students may chant this mantra before exams to invoke clarity of thought and wisdom in their studies.
    • During community gatherings, it can be used to foster a spirit of collaboration and shared prosperity among members.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine support in the pursuit of wealth and knowledge.
    • It serves as a reminder that success is often a collective effort, requiring both personal initiative and divine inspiration.
    • In real life, this mantra can be applicable in various scenarios, from personal growth to community development, highlighting the interconnectedness of individual and collective prosperity.
