इन्द्रः किल श्रुत्या अस्य वेद स हि जित्स्णुः पथिकृत सूर्याय |आन मेनां कृत्वन्न अच्युतो भुवद गोः पतिर्दिवः सञ्जना अप्रतीतः ||
indraḥ kila śrutyā asya veda sa hi jiṣṇuḥ pathikṛt sūryāya | ān menāṁ kṛṇvann acyuto bhuvad goḥ patir divaḥ sanajā apratītaḥ ||
  • Indraḥ kila śrutyā asya veda: This phrase refers to Indra, the king of the gods in Vedic literature, who is often associated with strength and power. The term "śrutyā" indicates that this knowledge is revealed or heard, emphasizing the importance of divine wisdom. In real life, this can be likened to the importance of listening to wise counsel or teachings that guide us in our actions.
  • sa hi jiṣṇuḥ pathikṛt sūryāya: Here, "jiṣṇuḥ" means victorious or strong, and "pathikṛt" refers to the one who paves the way. "Sūryāya" relates to the sun, symbolizing enlightenment and clarity. This can be compared to a leader who guides their team towards success, illuminating the path with their vision and strength.
  • ān menāṁ kṛṇvann acyuto bhuvad: "Ān menāṁ" suggests the act of bringing forth or creating. "Acyuto" means infallible or eternal, and "bhuvad" refers to the earthly realm. This implies that through divine guidance, one can manifest their goals in the material world. In practical terms, this can be seen in how individuals strive to achieve their dreams with unwavering determination, often drawing inspiration from spiritual or philosophical teachings.
  • goḥ patir divaḥ sanajā apratītaḥ: "Goḥ patir" translates to the lord of the cows, a symbol of wealth and nourishment, while "divaḥ" means of the heavens. "Sanajā" refers to the one who is born or created, and "apratītaḥ" means unseen or unknown. This can be interpreted as the unseen forces that nurture and support us, akin to the way nature provides for us without our direct awareness. In everyday life, this can be reflected in the gratitude we feel for the resources and support systems that sustain us.

Conclusion: This mantra encapsulates the essence of divine guidance, strength, and the manifestation of goals in the earthly realm. It signifies the importance of listening to wisdom, leading with strength, and recognizing the unseen forces that support our endeavors. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios such as leadership, personal growth, and the pursuit of dreams, reminding us to remain connected to our spiritual roots while navigating the material world.
