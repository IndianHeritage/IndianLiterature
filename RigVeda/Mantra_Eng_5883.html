मां कस्य नो अररुषो धूर्तिः प्रणङ मार्त्यस्य |इन्द्राग्नि शर्म यच्छतम् ||
mā kasya no araruṣo dhūrtiḥ praṇaṅ martyasya | indrāgnī śarma yacchatam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • mā: not
    • kasya: of anyone
    • no: our
    • araruṣo: anger or hostility
    • dhūrtiḥ: cleverness or skill
    • praṇaṅ: to us
    • martyasya: of the mortal
    • indrāgnī: Indra and Agni (deities)
    • śarma: protection or comfort
    • yacchatam: may grant
  • Overall Meaning: This mantra expresses a plea for protection and the avoidance of hostility. It seeks the blessings of the deities Indra and Agni to provide comfort and security to mortals, emphasizing the importance of harmony and skill in navigating life's challenges.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • Conflict Resolution: In situations of conflict, such as workplace disagreements, this mantra can serve as a reminder to seek peaceful resolutions rather than allowing anger to escalate. For instance, a team facing internal strife might benefit from open communication and mediation, embodying the essence of avoiding hostility.
    • Seeking Guidance: When facing difficult decisions, individuals can invoke the qualities of Indra and Agni, representing strength and wisdom, to guide them. For example, a student preparing for exams might meditate on this mantra to find clarity and focus, seeking the protection of these deities against anxiety and distractions.
    • Community Support: In times of crisis, such as natural disasters, communities often come together to support one another. This mantra highlights the importance of collective strength and the need for protection, reminding us to foster unity and compassion in our interactions.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for protection and the avoidance of conflict. It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal relationships to community dynamics. By embodying the principles of harmony, skill, and divine guidance, individuals can navigate challenges more effectively, fostering a sense of security and well-being in their lives and the lives of those around them.
