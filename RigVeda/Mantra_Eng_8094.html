स नः पुनान आ भर रयिं वीरवतिम् इषम् |ईशानः सोम विश्वतः ||
sa naḥ punāna ā bhara rayiṁ vīravatīm iṣam | īśānaḥ soma viśvataḥ ||
  • sa naḥ: This phrase translates to "may he" or "may that" and signifies a call for divine intervention or support.
  • punāna: This word means "purifying" or "cleansing." It suggests a desire for spiritual or physical purification.
  • ā bhara: This translates to "bring" or "bestow." It is an invocation for the divine to grant blessings or resources.
  • rayiṁ: This term refers to "wealth" or "prosperity." It encompasses not just material wealth but also spiritual and emotional abundance.
  • vīravatīm: This word means "with strength" or "heroic." It implies a request for strength and valor in one's endeavors.
  • iṣam: This translates to "power" or "might." It signifies the strength needed to achieve one's goals.
  • īśānaḥ: This term means "lord" or "ruler," often referring to a divine entity or higher power.
  • soma: Soma is a sacred drink in Vedic tradition, symbolizing immortality and divine inspiration.
  • viśvataḥ: This word means "from all directions" or "universally." It indicates a holistic approach to blessings and support.

The mantra "sa naḥ punāna ā bhara rayiṁ vīravatīm iṣam | īśānaḥ soma viśvataḥ ||" is a profound invocation for divine blessings, emphasizing purification, strength, and universal support. It reflects a holistic approach to life, where one seeks not only material wealth but also spiritual and emotional well-being.


In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts:

  • Personal Growth: Individuals can recite this mantra during meditation or prayer to seek purification of thoughts and intentions, fostering a mindset of abundance and strength.
  • Community Support: In communal gatherings, this mantra can be chanted to invoke collective strength and prosperity, encouraging unity and shared goals.
  • Business Ventures: Entrepreneurs may use this mantra to attract success and resilience in their endeavors, reminding themselves of the importance of ethical practices and community support.

In conclusion, this mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of wealth, strength, and divine support. It encourages individuals and communities to seek purification and strength in their pursuits, fostering a holistic approach to prosperity that encompasses all aspects of life. By invoking these qualities, one can navigate challenges with resilience and grace, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling existence.
