आ शुभ्रा यातम अश्विनाऽ स्वश्वा गीरो दास्रा जुजुषाणां युवाकोः |हव्यानि च प्रतिभृतā वीटं नः ||
ā śubhrā yātam aśvinā svaśvā giro dasrā jujuṣāṇā yuvākoḥ | havyāni ca pratibhṛtā vītaṁ naḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā: from
    • śubhrā: bright, pure
    • yātam: may we go, may we proceed
    • aśvinā: O Ashvins (the twin deities of health and medicine)
    • svaśvā: our own, our own horses
    • giro: words, speech
    • dasrā: swift, quick
    • jujuṣāṇā: desiring, wishing
    • yuvākoḥ: of the young, youthful
    • havyāni: offerings, oblations
    • ca: and
    • pratibhṛtā: brought forth, produced
    • vītaṁ: free, liberated
    • naḥ: for us
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the Ashvins, the divine twin horsemen, who symbolize health, vitality, and the swift delivery of blessings.
    • The phrase "may we go" suggests a desire for guidance and support in our endeavors, particularly in the pursuit of health and well-being.
    • The mention of "our own horses" can be interpreted as a metaphor for our own strengths and resources that we must harness to achieve our goals.
    • The reference to "words" and "swift" indicates the importance of communication and quick action in realizing our aspirations.
    • Finally, the invocation of "offerings" signifies the need for gratitude and acknowledgment of the divine forces that assist us in our journey.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, this mantra can be applied when embarking on a new project. One might seek the blessings of the Ashvins for health and success, ensuring that the team communicates effectively and acts swiftly to meet deadlines.
    • In personal life, individuals may recite this mantra when facing health challenges, invoking the Ashvins for support and guidance in their recovery process.
    • During significant life transitions, such as marriage or childbirth, this mantra can serve as a reminder to harness one's inner strengths and seek divine assistance for a smooth journey ahead.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine support in our endeavors, emphasizing the importance of health, communication, and swift action.
    • It serves as a reminder to recognize and utilize our own strengths while remaining grateful for the assistance we receive from higher powers.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be a source of inspiration and motivation, encouraging individuals to pursue their goals with vigor and a sense of purpose.
