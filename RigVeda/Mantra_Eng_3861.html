विषूमृधो जनुषा दानमिन्वन्न अहन गवां मघवन संचकानः |अत्र दासस्य नमुचेश्शिरो यद अवर्तयो मनवे गातुमिच्छन् ||
vi ṣū mṛdho januṣā dānam invann ahan gavā maghavan saṁcakānaḥ | atrā dāsasya namuceḥ śiro yad avartayo manave gātum icchan ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • vi: apart, away
    • ṣū: to be born, to arise
    • mṛdho: from the earth
    • januṣā: by the birth, by the creation
    • dānam: gift, donation
    • invann: invoking, calling upon
    • ahan: day, light
    • gavā: of the cows, of the wealth
    • maghavan: the powerful, the generous
    • saṁcakānaḥ: gathering, accumulating
    • atrā: here, in this place
    • dāsasya: of the servant, of the devotee
    • namuceḥ: of Namuci, a demon
    • śiro: head, mind
    • yad: that, which
    • avartayo: turning, revolving
    • manave: to Manu, the progenitor of mankind
    • gātum: to go, to move
    • icchan: desiring, wishing
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the act of giving and the importance of generosity.
    • The phrase "dānam invann" emphasizes the act of invoking or calling upon the spirit of giving.
    • It highlights the connection between the earth and the wealth represented by cows, which were considered a symbol of prosperity in ancient times.
    • The reference to "dāsasya namuceḥ" suggests the idea of overcoming obstacles or negativity, as Namuci represents a demon that obstructs progress.
    • Ultimately, it conveys a message of seeking enlightenment and progress through acts of kindness and generosity.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern society, acts of charity and community service reflect the essence of this mantra. For instance, donating to food banks or volunteering at shelters embodies the spirit of "dānam."
    • In a corporate context, businesses that engage in corporate social responsibility (CSR) initiatives demonstrate the principle of giving back to the community, aligning with the mantra's emphasis on generosity.
    • On a personal level, helping a neighbor in need or supporting a friend during tough times illustrates the practical application of this mantra in everyday life.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the significance of generosity and the positive impact it can have on individuals and communities.
    • By embracing the values of giving and kindness, we can overcome challenges and foster a sense of unity and progress.
    • In essence, the mantra encourages us to cultivate a spirit of generosity in our lives, leading to personal growth and a harmonious society.
