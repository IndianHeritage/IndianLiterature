आ स्तुतासो मरुतो विश्व ऊती अच्छा सूरीन सर्वताता जिगाता |ये नस् तमनā शतिनो वर्धयन्ति युयम् पात स्वस्तिभिः सदा नः ||
ā stutāso maruto viśva ūtī acchā sūrīn sarvatātā jigāta | ye nas tmanā śatino vardhayanti yūyam pāta svastibhiḥ sadā naḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā: O
    • stutāso: of those who are praised
    • maruto: O Maruts (the storm deities)
    • viśva: all
    • ūtī: strength or energy
    • acchā: good or auspicious
    • sūrīn: O heroes
    • sarvatātā: all-pervading
    • jigāta: may you protect
    • ye: those who
    • nas: us
    • tmanā: with your mind
    • śatino: with strength
    • vardhayanti: who increase
    • yūyam: you
    • pāta: protect
    • svastibhiḥ: with auspiciousness
    • sadā: always
    • naḥ: us
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to the Maruts, the storm deities, who are often associated with strength, vitality, and the forces of nature.
    • The invocation of "ā stutāso maruto" signifies a call to those who are praised, acknowledging their power and influence.
    • The phrase "ye nas tmanā śatino vardhayanti" emphasizes the importance of those who enhance our strength and well-being through their divine energy.
    • It reflects a desire for protection and auspiciousness in our lives, asking the Maruts to safeguard us with their blessings.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In times of personal or communal challenges, such as natural disasters or health crises, people often turn to spiritual practices and prayers for strength and protection.
    • In a corporate setting, leaders may invoke the spirit of teamwork and collaboration, akin to the Maruts, to enhance the collective strength of their teams.
    • During significant life events, such as weddings or new ventures, individuals may seek blessings for auspicious beginnings, paralleling the mantra's call for protection and well-being.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of divine support and the collective strength that can be drawn from spiritual entities.
    • It serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of all beings and the importance of seeking strength from both within and from external sources.
    • In real life, it encourages individuals to foster relationships that enhance their well-being and to seek protection and auspiciousness in their endeavors.
