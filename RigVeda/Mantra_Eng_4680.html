भूय इद वावृधे वीर्यायं एको अजुर्यो दयते वसूनि |प्र रिरिचे दिव इन्द्रः पृथिव्या अर्धम इद अस्य प्रति रोडसी उभे ||
bhūya id vāvṛdhe vīryāyam̐ eko ajuryo dayate vasūni | pra ririce diva indraḥ pṛthivyā ardham id asya prati rodasī ubhe ||
  • bhūya id vāvṛdhe vīryāyam̐: This phrase can be translated as "May we grow in strength and valor." The term "vāvṛdhe" signifies growth or increase, while "vīryāyam" refers to strength or valor. In real life, this can be related to personal development, where individuals strive to enhance their skills and capabilities, much like athletes training to improve their performance.
  • eko ajuryo dayate vasūni: This translates to "One who is immortal bestows wealth." The word "eko" means one, "ajuryo" refers to immortality, and "dayate" means to bestow. This can be seen in the context of philanthropy, where individuals or organizations that have achieved success often share their wealth to uplift others, similar to how successful entrepreneurs invest in community projects.
  • pra ririce diva indraḥ: This means "Indra, the lord of heaven, is invoked." Indra is often associated with power and authority. In a modern context, this can be likened to leaders or influential figures who are called upon to guide and inspire others, much like how a CEO might motivate their team to achieve a common goal.
  • pṛthivyā ardham id asya prati rodasī ubhe: This translates to "Half of this is in the earth, and the other half in the sky." This phrase emphasizes the balance between the material and spiritual realms. In everyday life, this can be reflected in the need for individuals to find harmony between their professional ambitions and personal well-being, akin to maintaining a work-life balance.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of growth, strength, generosity, leadership, and balance. Its significance lies in the encouragement of individuals to strive for personal and communal growth while recognizing the importance of sharing wealth and wisdom. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire people to pursue their goals with vigor, contribute to society, and maintain a balance between their aspirations and responsibilities. It serves as a reminder that true success is not just about personal achievement but also about uplifting others and fostering a harmonious existence.
