मन्त्र: भद्रो नो अग्निराहुतो भद्रā रातिः सुभग भद्रो अध्वरः |भद्रā उता प्रशस्तयः ||
bhadro no agnir āhuto bhadrā rātiḥ subhaga bhadro adhvaraḥ | bhadrā uta praśastayaḥ ||
  • bhadro no agnir āhuto: This phrase can be translated to "May the fire (Agni) that is invoked be auspicious for us." In Vedic culture, Agni represents not just physical fire but also the divine energy that purifies and transforms. For example, in a modern context, this can relate to the idea of setting intentions or goals that are positive and beneficial, akin to lighting a candle for hope and clarity.
  • bhadrā rātiḥ subhaga: This translates to "May our thoughts be auspicious and favorable." Here, "rātiḥ" refers to thoughts or intentions, and "subhaga" means favorable or good. In real life, this can be seen in practices like positive affirmations or mindfulness, where cultivating positive thoughts can lead to better outcomes in personal and professional life.
  • bhadro adhvaraḥ: This means "May our rituals be auspicious." The term "adhvaraḥ" refers to rituals or actions. In contemporary life, this can be related to the importance of performing actions with intention and mindfulness, whether in personal rituals like morning routines or in professional settings like project planning.
  • bhadrā uta praśastayaḥ: This translates to "May we be blessed with auspiciousness and praise." The word "praśastayaḥ" indicates commendation or recognition. In a real-world scenario, this can be linked to the idea of striving for excellence in our endeavors, whether in academics, career, or personal relationships, and seeking acknowledgment for our efforts.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of auspiciousness in various aspects of life—thoughts, actions, and rituals. It serves as a reminder to cultivate positivity and intention in our daily lives. By invoking auspiciousness, we align ourselves with higher energies that can lead to success and fulfillment. This mantra can be applied in real-life scenarios such as setting goals, performing daily rituals, or engaging in community service, where the focus is on creating a positive impact and fostering goodwill.
