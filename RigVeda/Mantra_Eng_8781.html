जरतībhir ओषधībhiḥ पर्णेभिः शकुनानाम् |कर्मारो अश्मभिर द्युभिर हिरण्यवंतम् इच्छतीन्द्रायन्दो परि श्रव ||
jaratībhir oṣadhībhiḥ parṇebhiḥ śakunānām | kārmāro aśmabhir dyubhir hiraṇyavantam icchatīndrāyendo pari srava ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • jaratībhir: by the aged or old ones
    • oṣadhībhiḥ: with the herbs or plants
    • parṇebhiḥ: with the leaves
    • śakunānām: of the auspicious signs or omens
    • kārmāro: the one who performs actions or deeds
    • aśmabhir: with stones
    • dyubhir: with the heavens or sky
    • hiraṇyavantam: the golden or prosperous one
    • icchatī: desiring or wishing
    • ndrāyendo: to Indra, the king of gods
    • pari: around or about
    • srava: to flow or to pour forth
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the blessings of nature, particularly the aged plants and herbs, which are believed to possess wisdom and healing properties.
    • The mention of auspicious signs indicates a desire for positive outcomes and good fortune in endeavors.
    • The reference to performing actions with stones and the heavens suggests a connection between earthly and celestial realms, emphasizing the importance of grounding one's actions in reality while aspiring for higher goals.
    • By addressing Indra, the mantra seeks divine support and guidance in achieving prosperity and success.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In agriculture, farmers often rely on traditional knowledge of herbs and plants to enhance crop yield, reflecting the wisdom of "jaratībhir oṣadhībhiḥ."
    • In decision-making, individuals may look for signs or omens, akin to "śakunānām," to guide their choices, such as interpreting the behavior of animals or natural phenomena.
    • In personal development, people may engage in actions that align with their values and aspirations, similar to "kārmāro," while seeking support from mentors or spiritual guides, akin to invoking "ndrāyendo."
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the interconnectedness of nature, human actions, and divine support.
    • Its significance lies in the recognition of the wisdom of the natural world and the importance of seeking guidance in our endeavors.
    • In real life, it encourages individuals to respect nature, seek auspicious signs, and perform their actions with intention and purpose, ultimately leading to prosperity and fulfillment.
