अहं रन्धयम् मृगयं श्रुतर्वणे यन् माजिहीत वयुनā चनानुशक् |अहं वेषं नम्रम आयवे 'करम् अहं सव्याय पद्गृह्बिम अरन्धयम् ||
ahaṁ randhayam mṛgayaṁ śrutarvaṇe yan mājihīta vayunā canānuṣak | ahaṁ veśaṁ namram āyave 'karam ahaṁ savyāya paḍgṛbhim arandhayam ||
  • ahaṁ randhayam mṛgayaṁ: This phrase translates to "I am the one who hunts." In a real-life context, this can be seen as a metaphor for pursuing one's goals or aspirations. Just as a hunter seeks out their prey, individuals strive to achieve their dreams and ambitions.
  • śrutarvaṇe: This means "in the sound of the call." It signifies the importance of communication and the call to action. In modern life, this can relate to how we respond to opportunities or challenges that come our way, much like how a hunter listens for the sounds of their environment.
  • yan mājihīta vayunā canānuṣak: This translates to "what I have not attained by my efforts." It reflects the idea of striving for what seems unattainable. In real life, this can be related to the pursuit of knowledge, success, or personal growth, where individuals often feel they are reaching for something just out of their grasp.
  • ahaṁ veśaṁ namram āyave 'karam: This means "I am the humble abode of the one who acts." It emphasizes humility and the importance of being grounded while taking action. In everyday life, this can be seen in leaders who remain humble despite their achievements, recognizing the contributions of others.
  • ahaṁ savyāya paḍgṛbhim arandhayam: This translates to "I am the one who grasps the left side." The left side often symbolizes intuition and creativity. In practical terms, this can relate to how individuals use their creative instincts to navigate challenges and make decisions in life.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the journey of self-discovery and the pursuit of one's goals. It highlights the importance of humility, communication, and the balance between effort and intuition. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied to personal and professional growth, encouraging individuals to remain grounded while ambitiously pursuing their dreams. The significance of this mantra lies in its reminder that the path to success is not just about the destination but also about the values and principles we uphold along the way.
