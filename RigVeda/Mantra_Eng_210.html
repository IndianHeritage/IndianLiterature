यā सुरथā रथीतमोभā देवā दिविष्पृशā |अश्विनā तā हवामहे ||
yā surathā rathītamobhā devā divispṛśā | aśvinā tā havāmahe ||
  • yā surathā: This phrase can be translated as "who is the one that is well-equipped" or "who is the one that is well-prepared." In real life, this can refer to individuals who are ready to face challenges, much like a well-prepared athlete before a competition.
  • rathītamobhā: This term means "the one who drives the chariot" or "the one who is the master of the vehicle." In a metaphorical sense, it can represent someone who is in control of their life and direction, similar to a leader guiding a team towards a common goal.
  • devā divispṛśā: This translates to "the gods who touch the heavens." It signifies divine beings or forces that are beyond human reach, representing aspirations and higher ideals. In everyday life, this can be likened to individuals who strive for excellence and aim for lofty goals, such as scientists or artists pushing the boundaries of their fields.
  • aśvinā: Referring to the Aśvins, the twin gods of medicine and health in Vedic mythology, this term symbolizes healing and well-being. In practical terms, this can relate to modern healthcare professionals who work tirelessly to improve the health of individuals and communities.
  • tā havāmahe: This phrase means "we invoke" or "we call upon." It reflects the act of seeking assistance or blessings from the divine. In real life, this can be seen in rituals, prayers, or even moments of reflection where individuals seek guidance in their lives.

Conclusion: The mantra "yā surathā rathītamobhā devā divispṛśā | aśvinā tā havāmahe ||" emphasizes the importance of being well-prepared, taking control of one's life, aspiring for higher ideals, and seeking divine assistance. Its significance lies in the reminder that, like the Aśvins, we can call upon higher powers for support in our endeavors. This mantra is applicable in various real-life scenarios, such as in personal development, leadership, and health, encouraging individuals to strive for excellence while remaining grounded in their efforts to seek help and guidance from the universe or a higher power.
