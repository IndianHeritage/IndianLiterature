पुरूरवो मां मृतः मां प्र पप्तो मां त्वा वृकासो अशिवासा उ क्षण |न वै स्त्रैणानि सख्यानी सन्ति सालावृकाणां हृदयान्य एता ||
purūravo mā mṛthā mā pra papto mā tvā vṛkāso aśivāsa u kṣan | na vai straiṇāni sakhyāni santi sālāvṛkāṇāṁ hṛdayāny etā ||
  • Mantra: "purūravo mā mṛthā mā pra papto mā tvā vṛkāso aśivāsa u kṣan | na vai straiṇāni sakhyāni santi sālāvṛkāṇāṁ hṛdayāny etā ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • purūravo: O, the one who is abundant or rich.
    • mā: do not.
    • mṛthā: be false or untrue.
    • mā pra papto: do not fall into.
    • mā: do not.
    • tvā: you.
    • vṛkāso: O, the one who is like a wolf or fierce.
    • aśivāsa: do not dwell in negativity or misfortune.
    • u: and.
    • kṣan: moment or time.
    • na: not.
    • vai: indeed.
    • straiṇāni: feminine qualities or attributes.
    • sakhyāni: friendships or bonds.
    • sālāvṛkāṇāṁ: of the wolves or fierce beings.
    • hṛdayāny: hearts.
    • etā: these.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra expresses a plea for protection and guidance against negativity and falsehood.
    • The speaker calls upon a powerful entity, possibly a deity, to not let them fall into the traps of deceit or misfortune.
    • The reference to "vṛkāso" (wolf-like) suggests a recognition of fierce challenges or adversities that one may face.
    • The mention of "feminine qualities" and "friendships" indicates the importance of nurturing relationships and positive attributes in overcoming difficulties.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, one might face competition that feels predatory, akin to wolves. This mantra serves as a reminder to stay true to oneself and not succumb to unethical practices.
    • In personal relationships, it emphasizes the importance of surrounding oneself with supportive friends who embody positive qualities, helping to navigate through life's challenges.
    • In times of crisis, such as during a health scare or financial difficulty, this mantra can be a source of strength, encouraging individuals to seek positivity and avoid despair.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra is significant as it encapsulates the struggle against negativity and the importance of maintaining integrity and positive relationships.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, reminding individuals to seek strength in community and to remain steadfast in their values.
    • Ultimately, it encourages a mindset of resilience, urging one to rise above challenges with the support of positive influences.
