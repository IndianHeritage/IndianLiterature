उरुं गभीरं जनुषाभ्य उग्रं विश्वव्यचसम अवतं मतीनाम् |इन्द्रं सोमासः प्रदिवि सुतासः समुद्रं न स्रवत आ विशन्ति ||
uruṁ gabhīraṁ januṣābhy u1graṁ viśvavyacasam avatam matīnām | indraṁ somāsaḥ pradivi sutāsaḥ samudraṁ na sravata ā viśanti ||
  • uruṁ gabhīraṁ: This phrase translates to "deep and vast." It signifies the profound and expansive nature of existence and consciousness.
  • januṣābhy u1graṁ: This can be interpreted as "from the powerful beings." It suggests the influence of strong, divine forces in our lives.
  • viśvavyacasam: Meaning "the one who encompasses all," this term refers to a universal presence that connects everything in the cosmos.
  • avatam matīnām: This translates to "the protector of thoughts." It emphasizes the importance of safeguarding our mental and spiritual well-being.
  • indraṁ: Referring to Indra, the king of gods in Vedic tradition, symbolizing strength, power, and leadership.
  • somāsaḥ: This means "the essence of the moon," representing nourishment, tranquility, and the calming aspects of life.
  • pradivi sutāsaḥ: Translates to "the offspring of the heavens," indicating a connection to divine origins and celestial influences.
  • samudraṁ: Meaning "ocean," it symbolizes vastness, depth, and the source of life, akin to the emotional and spiritual depths we experience.
  • na sravata: This means "not flowing away," suggesting stability and permanence in the face of change.
  • ā viśanti: Translates to "they enter," indicating the process of integration and connection with the divine or universal consciousness.

Conclusion: This mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking protection and guidance from the divine forces that govern our lives. It emphasizes the importance of deep thought, universal connection, and the stability that comes from understanding our place in the cosmos. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied during moments of uncertainty or when seeking clarity in decision-making. By invoking the qualities of Indra and the vastness of the ocean, individuals can find strength and tranquility amidst life's challenges, fostering a sense of inner peace and resilience.
