युवोर दानाय सुभरा असश्चतो रथम आ तस्थुर वचसं न मन्तवे |याभिर धियोऽवथः कर्मन्न इष्टये ताभिर ऊषु ऊतिभिर अश्विनाऽ गतम् ||
yuvor dānāya subharā asaścato ratham ā tasthur vacasaṁ na mantave | yābhir dhiyo 'vathaḥ karmann iṣṭaye tābhir ū ṣu ūtibhir aśvinā gatam ||
  • yuvor dānāya: This phrase translates to "for the young ones' gift." It emphasizes the importance of giving and sharing, particularly with the youth, who represent the future. In real life, this can be seen in mentorship programs where experienced individuals share their knowledge and resources with younger generations.
  • subharā: Meaning "good fortune" or "auspiciousness," this word highlights the positive outcomes that can arise from generosity. For example, charitable organizations that provide education and healthcare to underprivileged communities often lead to improved societal conditions.
  • asaścato ratham ā tasthur vacasaṁ na mantave: This part can be interpreted as "the chariots of those who do not speak ill of others." It suggests that those who maintain a positive and respectful discourse are blessed with success. In everyday life, this can be related to the importance of constructive communication in workplaces, where a positive environment fosters collaboration and innovation.
  • yābhir dhiyo 'vathaḥ: This translates to "with those thoughts that are beneficial." It emphasizes the significance of having constructive and positive thoughts. For instance, in personal development, individuals who focus on positive affirmations and goal-setting often achieve greater success.
  • karmann iṣṭaye: Meaning "for the purpose of action," this phrase underscores the importance of intention behind actions. In real life, this can be seen in social movements where individuals act with a clear purpose to bring about change, such as environmental activism aimed at combating climate change.
  • tābhir ū ṣu ūtibhir aśvinā gatam: This can be interpreted as "with those who have reached their goals through effort." It highlights the value of hard work and perseverance. For example, successful entrepreneurs often share stories of their struggles and the effort they put in to achieve their dreams, inspiring others to follow suit.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of generosity, positive communication, constructive thoughts, purposeful actions, and the value of hard work. In real life, these principles can be applied in various scenarios, such as mentoring the youth, fostering a positive work environment, engaging in social causes, and sharing success stories to inspire others. By embodying these values, individuals can contribute to a more harmonious and prosperous society.
