आशुभिष् चिद् यान् वि मुचाति नूनम् अरीरमद अतमानं चिद् एतोः |अह्यर्षूणां चिन् न् यायाम् अविष्याम् अनु व्रतं सवितुर् मोक्ष्य आगात् ||
āśubhiś cid yān vi mucāti nūnam arīramad atamānaṁ cid etoḥ | ahyarṣūṇāṁ cin ny ayām̐ aviṣyām anu vrataṁ savitur moky āgāt ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • āśubhiś: From the inauspicious or negative influences.
    • cid: Indeed, or certainly.
    • yān: Those who.
    • vi: Away, or from.
    • mucāti: Liberate or free.
    • nūnam: Surely or certainly.
    • arīramad: The one who has not been harmed.
    • atamānaṁ: The highest or supreme.
    • cid: Consciousness or awareness.
    • etoḥ: From this.
    • ahyarṣūṇāṁ: Of those who are enlightened or wise.
    • cin: To gather or collect.
    • ny: Not.
    • ayām̐: To be disturbed or affected.
    • aviṣyām: In the realm of ignorance or darkness.
    • anu: Following or in accordance with.
    • vrataṁ: Vow or commitment.
    • savituh: Of the Sun or the divine light.
    • moky: Liberation or freedom.
    • āgāt: Has come or arrived.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about liberation from negative influences and the importance of consciousness in achieving this freedom.
    • It emphasizes the role of wisdom and enlightenment in navigating through life's challenges.
    • The reference to the Sun symbolizes the ultimate source of light and knowledge, guiding individuals towards liberation.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a challenging situation, such as a personal crisis or conflict, one can reflect on this mantra to seek clarity and liberation from negative emotions.
    • In a professional setting, when faced with unethical practices, invoking the essence of this mantra can inspire individuals to uphold their values and seek a higher path.
    • During meditation or mindfulness practices, focusing on the principles of this mantra can help individuals cultivate awareness and resilience against distractions and negativity.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for self-awareness and the pursuit of enlightenment as a means to overcome life's adversities.
    • It serves as a reminder that through conscious effort and commitment to one's values, individuals can achieve liberation from the inauspicious influences that may hinder their growth.
    • This mantra is applicable in various aspects of life, including personal development, ethical decision-making, and spiritual practices, guiding individuals towards a path of light and wisdom.
