विभ्राजञ् ज्योतिषा स्वर्ग गच्छो रोचनं दिवः |येनेमा विश्वा भुवनानि आभृतā विश्वकर्मणा विश्वदेव्यावतā ||
vibhrājañ jyotiṣā sva1r agaccho rocanaṁ divaḥ | yenemā viśvā bhuvanāny ābhṛtā viśvakarmaṇā viśvadevyāvatā ||
  • vibhrājañ: This word can be translated as "shining" or "illuminating." It signifies the brilliance and radiance that is often associated with divine light.
  • jyotiṣā: This means "by the light" or "of light." It emphasizes the importance of light in understanding and perceiving the world around us.
  • sva1r: This term refers to "heaven" or "the celestial realm." It indicates a higher state of existence or consciousness.
  • agaccho: This translates to "has gone" or "has reached." It suggests a journey or movement towards a destination.
  • rocanaṁ: This means "brightness" or "splendor." It highlights the beauty and allure of the divine light.
  • divaḥ: This word means "of the sky" or "heavenly." It connects the mantra to the celestial and divine aspects of existence.
  • yenemā: This translates to "by which" or "through which." It indicates a means or method of achieving something.
  • viśvā: This means "all" or "universal." It signifies inclusiveness and the interconnectedness of all beings.
  • bhuvanāny: This translates to "worlds" or "realms." It refers to the various dimensions of existence.
  • ābhṛtā: This means "upheld" or "sustained." It suggests the idea of support and continuity in existence.
  • viśvakarmaṇā: This translates to "by all actions" or "through universal deeds." It emphasizes the importance of actions in shaping reality.
  • viśvadevyāvatā: This means "by the universal goddess" or "through the divine feminine." It highlights the nurturing and creative aspects of the divine.

The mantra speaks to the illuminating power of divine light that transcends the physical realm and connects all worlds. It emphasizes the journey towards enlightenment and the importance of actions in sustaining existence. The reference to the universal goddess suggests a nurturing force that upholds and supports all beings.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios:

  • Personal Growth: Just as the light illuminates the path, individuals can seek knowledge and wisdom to navigate their lives effectively.
  • Community Building: The idea of interconnectedness can inspire collective efforts to support and uplift one another in society.
  • Environmental Awareness: Recognizing the various worlds and realms can lead to a greater appreciation for nature and the need to protect our environment.

In conclusion, this mantra serves as a reminder of the divine light that guides us, the importance of our actions, and the interconnectedness of all beings. It encourages us to seek enlightenment, support one another, and honor the nurturing forces in our lives.
