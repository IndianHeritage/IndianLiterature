शत्रूयन्तो अभि ये नस् ततस्रे महि व्राधन्त ओगणासा इन्द्र |अन्धेनामित्रास तमसा सचन्तां सुज्योतिषो अक्तवस् ताम् अभि ष्युः ||
śatrūyanto abhi ye nas tatasre mahi vrādhanta ogaṇāsa indra | andhenāmitrās tamasā sacantāṁ sujyotiṣo aktavas tām̐ abhi ṣyuḥ ||
  • śatrūyanto abhi ye nas tatasre mahi vrādhanta ogaṇāsa indra: This line can be interpreted as a call to Indra, the king of gods, to protect us from our enemies. The term "śatrūyanto" refers to those who are hostile or adversarial, while "mahi vrādhanta" suggests the greatness of Indra's power. In real life, this can be likened to seeking protection and strength in times of conflict or adversity, much like how individuals or nations seek support from allies during challenging times.
  • andhenāmitrās tamasā sacantāṁ: This phrase speaks to the idea of overcoming darkness and ignorance. "Andhenā" refers to blindness or ignorance, while "amitras" means enemies. The line suggests that those who are blinded by ignorance can be led to clarity and understanding. In a real-world context, this can be seen in educational initiatives that aim to enlighten individuals and communities, helping them to overcome prejudices and misconceptions.
  • sujyotiṣo aktavas tām̐ abhi ṣyuḥ: This part emphasizes the importance of light and knowledge. "Sujyotiṣo" translates to "good light" or "auspicious illumination," while "aktavas" refers to the power of action. This can be related to the pursuit of knowledge and wisdom in our lives, where individuals strive to act positively and make informed decisions. For example, in the field of science and technology, the quest for knowledge leads to innovations that improve our quality of life.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a profound message about seeking protection from adversaries, overcoming ignorance, and striving for knowledge and enlightenment. Its significance lies in the universal human experience of facing challenges and the need for guidance and strength. In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as personal struggles, community conflicts, or even global issues where collaboration and understanding are essential for progress. By invoking the essence of this mantra, individuals can find inspiration to seek wisdom, foster unity, and work towards a brighter future.
