यस् ते द्रप्सः स्कन्नो यस् ते अंशुर अवश्च यः परः स्रुचाः |अयं देवो बृहस्पतिः सं तं सिञ्चतु राधसे ||
yas te drapsaḥ skanno yas te aṁśur avaś ca yaḥ paraḥ srucā | ayaṁ devo bṛhaspatiḥ saṁ taṁ siñcatu rādhase ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yas: who
    • te: your
    • drapsaḥ: desires
    • skanno: fulfilled
    • aṁśur: rays
    • avaś: under control
    • ca: and
    • yaḥ: who
    • paraḥ: supreme
    • srucā: with offerings
    • ayaṁ: this
    • devo: deity
    • bṛhaspatiḥ: Brihaspati, the lord of wisdom
    • saṁ: together
    • taṁ: that
    • siñcatu: may pour
    • rādhase: for prosperity
  • Meaning:
    • This mantra invokes the blessings of Brihaspati, the deity associated with wisdom, knowledge, and prosperity.
    • It emphasizes the importance of fulfilling desires and controlling one's aspirations in a righteous manner.
    • The mantra suggests that through offerings and devotion, one can attract the divine blessings of Brihaspati for success and abundance.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, individuals may recite this mantra before important meetings or presentations to seek clarity and wisdom in their decisions.
    • Students preparing for exams might chant this mantra to enhance their focus and understanding, hoping to gain the blessings of knowledge.
    • During rituals or pujas, offerings made while reciting this mantra can symbolize the desire for prosperity and success in various endeavors.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its ability to connect individuals with divine wisdom and prosperity.
    • It serves as a reminder to align one's desires with righteous actions and to seek guidance from higher powers in all aspects of life.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various situations where wisdom, clarity, and prosperity are sought, making it a valuable tool for personal and professional growth.
