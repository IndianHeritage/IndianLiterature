प्र सोमासो अधन्विषुः पवमानास इन्दवः |श्रीणानां अप्सु मृञ्जत ||
pra somāso adhanviṣuḥ pavamānāsa indavaḥ | śrīṇānā apsu mṛñjata ||
  • Pra Somāso Adhanviṣuḥ Pavamānāsa Indavaḥ:
    • Pra: This word can be interpreted as "for" or "towards," indicating a direction or purpose.
    • Somāso: Refers to the "Soma," a sacred drink in Vedic rituals, symbolizing immortality and divine inspiration.
    • Adhanviṣuḥ: This term can be understood as "in the bow," suggesting a connection to archery or the pursuit of goals.
    • Pavamānāsa: Means "the purifying one," indicating a force that cleanses or elevates.
    • Indavaḥ: Refers to "the divine," often associated with the gods or celestial beings.
  • Śrīṇānā Apsu Mṛñjata:
    • Śrīṇānā: This word signifies "those who are prosperous" or "those who are blessed."
    • Apsu: Refers to "in the waters," symbolizing the source of life and nourishment.
    • Mṛñjata: Means "to be made" or "to be created," indicating a process of formation or manifestation.

  • Meaning and Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the essence of the divine Soma, which is believed to purify and elevate the seeker.
    • It emphasizes the importance of striving towards one's goals (represented by the bow) while being blessed by divine forces.
    • The second part highlights the creation of prosperity and abundance in the waters, symbolizing the nurturing aspects of life.

  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a personal context, this mantra can be applied when one is pursuing a career goal. Just as the archer aims for the target, individuals can focus their efforts towards achieving their aspirations while seeking divine guidance.
    • In community settings, this mantra can inspire collective efforts towards prosperity, such as in agricultural practices where water is essential for growth, reminding us of the importance of nurturing our resources.

  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine assistance in the pursuit of goals and the creation of abundance.
    • It serves as a reminder that while we strive for success, we should also seek purity and blessings from higher powers.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be a source of inspiration for individuals and communities alike, encouraging them to work towards their goals with a sense of purpose and divine support.
