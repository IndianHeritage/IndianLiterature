साकामुख्षो मरजयन्त स्वसारो दश धीरस्य धीतयो धनुत्रीः |हरिः पर्य अद्रवज जाः सूर्यस्य ड्रोनं ननक्षे अत्यो न वाजी ||
sākamukṣo marjayanta svasāro daśa dhīrasya dhītayo dhanutrīḥ | hariḥ pary adravaj jāḥ sūryasya droṇaṁ nanakṣe atyo na vājī ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sākamukṣo: With a shared face or aspect.
    • marjayanta: Purifying or cleansing.
    • svasāro: Of one's own kin or family.
    • daśa: Ten.
    • dhīrasya: Of the wise or the patient.
    • dhītayo: Intellects or thoughts.
    • dhanutrīḥ: Bowmen or archers.
    • hariḥ: The divine or the Lord.
    • pary: All around or surrounding.
    • adravaj: Did not move or was steady.
    • jāḥ: Who or which.
    • sūryasya: Of the sun.
    • droṇaṁ: The vessel or container.
    • nanakṣe: Did not fall or was not lost.
    • aty: Beyond or surpassing.
    • na: Not.
    • vājī: The swift or the strong.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the unity and strength found in collective efforts, particularly among those who are wise and patient.
    • The mention of "sākamukṣo" suggests that when individuals come together with a shared purpose, they can achieve greater things.
    • The term "marjayanta" indicates the importance of purification, which can be interpreted as the need to cleanse one's intentions and actions for the greater good.
    • The reference to "daśa dhīrasya" emphasizes the value of wisdom and patience in achieving goals, akin to how a team of ten skilled individuals can accomplish more than one alone.
    • "Dhanutrīḥ" symbolizes the strength and focus required to hit targets, much like archers who must be precise and coordinated in their efforts.
    • The phrase "hariḥ pary adravaj" suggests that divine support is present when one is steadfast in their purpose, akin to how a community can thrive when united in their goals.
    • The imagery of the sun ("sūryasya") represents enlightenment and guidance, indicating that wisdom leads to clarity and success.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace, a team that collaborates effectively, sharing ideas and supporting each other, can achieve remarkable results, much like the "sākamukṣo" aspect of the mantra.
    • In community service, individuals coming together to purify their intentions and work towards a common goal can create significant positive change, reflecting the essence of "marjayanta."
    • In sports, a team that embodies patience and wisdom, like the "daśa dhīrasya," can strategize effectively to win games, demonstrating the power of collective effort.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of unity, wisdom, and collective strength in achieving goals.
    • It serves as a reminder that when individuals come together with a shared purpose and pure intentions, they can overcome challenges and reach greater heights.
    • In real-life scenarios, whether in personal relationships, workplaces, or community efforts, the principles outlined in this mantra can guide individuals towards success and fulfillment.
