विरक्षो विमृधो जहि विवृत्रस्य हनू रुजा |विमन्युम इन्द्र वृत्रहन्न अमित्रस्याभिदासतः ||
vi rakṣo vi mṛdho jahi vi vṛtrasya hanū ruja | vi manyum indra vṛtrahann amitrasyābhidāsataḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • vi: away, apart
    • rakṣo: from the demons or evil forces
    • vi: away, apart
    • mṛdho: from the dead or death
    • jahi: conquer, defeat
    • vi: away, apart
    • vṛtrasya: of Vritra (a demon or obstacle)
    • hanū: jaw or mouth
    • ruja: pain or suffering
    • vi: away, apart
    • manyum: anger or wrath
    • indra: Indra (the king of gods)
    • vṛtrahann: the slayer of Vritra
    • amitrasyābhidāsataḥ: from the enemy's attack
  • Overall Meaning: This mantra is a call for the divine power of Indra to protect and liberate from various forms of evil and suffering. It emphasizes the need to overcome obstacles (symbolized by Vritra) and to be free from pain, anger, and the threats posed by adversaries.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a personal context, when facing challenges such as stress or anxiety, one can recite this mantra to seek mental clarity and emotional strength.
    • In a community setting, this mantra can be invoked during times of conflict or strife, encouraging unity and the overcoming of divisive forces.
    • In a professional environment, it can serve as a reminder to confront and eliminate obstacles that hinder progress, fostering a culture of resilience and determination.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its powerful invocation for protection and strength against negativity and adversity. It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal struggles to collective challenges, reminding individuals and communities to seek divine assistance in overcoming their fears and obstacles. By embracing the essence of this mantra, one can cultivate a mindset of courage and resilience, ultimately leading to personal growth and harmony in society.
