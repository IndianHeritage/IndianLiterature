मन्त्र: पुरोळा इति तुर्वशो यक्षुर आसीद राये मत्स्यासो निश्चिता अपीव | श्रुष्टिं चक्रुर भृगवो द्रुह्यवश च सखा सखायम् अतरत विषूचोः ||
puroḻā it turvaśo yakṣur āsīd rāye matsyāso niśitā apīva | śruṣṭiṁ cakrur bhṛgavo druhyavaś ca sakhā sakhāyam atarad viṣūcoḥ ||
  • puroḻā it turvaśo: This phrase can be interpreted as "the one who is strong and powerful." In real life, this can refer to individuals or leaders who possess strength and resilience, such as a community leader who stands firm in the face of adversity.
  • yakṣur āsīd rāye: This translates to "the one who is a protector of wealth." This can be related to financial advisors or guardians of resources who ensure the prosperity of a community or family, much like a financial planner who helps individuals manage their wealth wisely.
  • matsyāso niśitā apīva: This can be understood as "like the sharpness of a fish." In nature, fish are known for their agility and sharp instincts. This can symbolize the importance of being alert and quick in decision-making, akin to a businessperson who must adapt swiftly to market changes.
  • śruṣṭiṁ cakrur: This means "they created the universe." This highlights the creative power inherent in individuals or groups. For example, artists and innovators who bring new ideas to life can be seen as creators of their own universe, shaping culture and society.
  • bhṛgavo druhyavaś ca: This phrase refers to "the wise and the knowledgeable." In real life, this can be exemplified by educators and mentors who guide others with their wisdom, much like a teacher who inspires students to pursue knowledge and personal growth.
  • sakhā sakhāyam atarad viṣūcoḥ: This translates to "friends who support each other." This emphasizes the importance of community and friendship. In everyday life, this can be seen in support groups or teams where individuals uplift one another, such as friends helping each other through difficult times.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates themes of strength, protection, creativity, wisdom, and friendship. Its significance lies in the reminder of the qualities that contribute to a thriving community and individual success. In real life, these principles can be applied in various scenarios, such as leadership roles, financial management, creative endeavors, educational pursuits, and the importance of supportive relationships. By embodying these qualities, individuals can foster a more harmonious and prosperous environment for themselves and those around them.
