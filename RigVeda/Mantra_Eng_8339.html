उक्षेव यूथा पर्यन्न अरावीद अधि त्विषीर अधिता सूर्यस्य | दिव्यः सुपर्णो 'व चक्षता क्षां सोमः परि क्रतुनā पश्यते जाः ||
ukṣeva yūthā pariyann arāvīd adhi tviṣīr adhita sūryasya | divyaḥ suparṇo 'va cakṣata kṣāṁ somaḥ pari kratunā paśyate jāḥ ||
  • ukṣeva yūthā pariyann arāvīd: This phrase can be interpreted as "like a flock of birds flying together." It signifies unity and collective effort. In real life, this can be seen in teams working towards a common goal, such as a sports team or a project group in a corporate setting, where collaboration leads to success.
  • adhi tviṣīr adhita sūryasya: This translates to "under the brilliance of the sun." The sun is often associated with energy, vitality, and enlightenment. In practical terms, this can relate to how sunlight is essential for agriculture, providing the energy needed for plants to grow, which in turn sustains life.
  • divyaḥ suparṇo 'va cakṣata: This means "the divine, beautiful bird sees." The 'beautiful bird' can symbolize higher consciousness or divine insight. In everyday life, this can be likened to moments of clarity or inspiration that guide individuals in making important decisions, akin to a mentor providing wisdom.
  • kṣāṁ somaḥ pari kratunā: This phrase can be understood as "the moon, through its power, nourishes." The moon is often associated with emotions and intuition. In real life, this can be seen in how the lunar cycle affects tides and even human behavior, highlighting the interconnectedness of nature and human life.
  • paśyate jāḥ: This translates to "witnesses the beings." It emphasizes awareness and observation of life around us. In a practical sense, this can relate to mindfulness practices, where being present and aware of one’s surroundings can lead to a deeper understanding of oneself and others.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates themes of unity, enlightenment, nourishment, and awareness. Its significance lies in the reminder of the interconnectedness of all beings and the importance of collective effort in achieving goals. In real life, these principles can be applied in various scenarios, such as teamwork in professional settings, the importance of nature in our lives, and the value of mindfulness in personal growth. By embodying these teachings, individuals can foster a more harmonious and productive existence.
