न मृषा श्रान्तं यद अवन्ति देवाः विश्वा इति स्पृधो अभ्य अश्नवाव |जयावेद अत्र शतनीतम आजिं यत सम्यञ्चा मिथुनाव अभ्य अजावा ||
na mṛṣā śrāntaṁ yad avanti devā viśvā it spṛdho abhy aśnavāva | jayāved atra śatanītham ājiṁ yat samyañcā mithunāv abhy ajāva ||
  • na mṛṣā: This phrase translates to "not in vain" or "not false." It emphasizes the importance of sincerity and truthfulness in one's actions and intentions.
  • śrāntaṁ: This means "tired" or "weary." It suggests that even in moments of fatigue, one should remain steadfast and focused on their goals.
  • yad avanti devā: This translates to "what the gods desire." It indicates that there is a higher purpose or divine will that guides our actions.
  • viśvā it spṛdho: This means "all beings are inspired." It highlights the interconnectedness of all life and the shared aspirations that bind us together.
  • abhy aśnavāva: This can be interpreted as "we strive for." It reflects the human endeavor to achieve and reach for greater heights.
  • jayāved: This translates to "victory is known." It signifies the recognition of success and the importance of celebrating achievements.
  • atra: This means "here" or "in this place." It emphasizes the present moment and the significance of being aware of our current circumstances.
  • śatanītham: This can be understood as "a hundredfold." It suggests abundance and the idea that efforts can yield great rewards.
  • ājiṁ: This means "today" or "in this moment." It encourages living in the present and making the most of our current opportunities.
  • yat samyañcā: This translates to "what is right." It emphasizes the importance of righteousness and ethical conduct in our actions.
  • mithunāv: This means "in pairs" or "together." It highlights the significance of collaboration and partnership in achieving goals.
  • abhy ajāva: This can be interpreted as "we strive together." It reflects the collective effort required to overcome challenges and achieve success.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of striving for truth, righteousness, and collective effort in the pursuit of success. It serves as a reminder that our actions should be sincere and aligned with a higher purpose, and that even in moments of fatigue, we must remain focused on our goals. In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as teamwork in a professional setting, where collaboration and shared aspirations lead to greater achievements. It also encourages individuals to reflect on their intentions and actions, ensuring they are in harmony with their values and the greater good. Ultimately, it signifies that true success is not just about individual accomplishments but also about the collective journey towards a common goal.
