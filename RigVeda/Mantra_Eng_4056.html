तन् नो अनर्वा सविता वरूथं तट सिंधव इषयन्तो अनु गमन् |उप यद वोच अध्वरस्य होता रायः स्याम पतयो वाजरत्नाः ||
tan no anarvā savitā varūthaṁ tat sindhava iṣayanto anu gman | upa yad voce adhvarasya hotā rāyaḥ syāma patayo vājaratnāḥ ||
  • tan no anarvā savitā varūthaṁ: This phrase can be translated as "May that divine Savitar, the Sun God, illuminate our understanding." Here, "Savitā" refers to the Sun, which is often associated with knowledge, enlightenment, and life-giving energy.
  • tat sindhava iṣayanto anu gman: This part means "May the rivers, which flow with abundance, guide us." Rivers are often seen as symbols of prosperity and nourishment, providing water for agriculture and sustaining life.
  • upa yad voce adhvarasya hotā: This translates to "As we invoke the priest of the sacrifice." In Vedic rituals, the priest plays a crucial role in performing sacrifices and invoking divine blessings, emphasizing the importance of spiritual guidance in our lives.
  • rāyaḥ syāma patayo vājaratnāḥ: This means "May we become strong and prosperous like the powerful ones." The term "vājaratnāḥ" refers to those who are strong and resilient, akin to diamonds, symbolizing strength and durability.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda emphasizes the importance of divine guidance, knowledge, and prosperity in our lives. It highlights the interconnectedness of nature, spirituality, and human existence. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as:

  • In personal development, seeking enlightenment and knowledge to make informed decisions.
  • In community building, fostering cooperation and abundance, much like rivers that nourish the land.
  • In spiritual practices, recognizing the role of mentors and guides in our journeys.
  • In striving for resilience and strength in the face of challenges, akin to the qualities of a diamond.

Overall, this mantra serves as a reminder to seek wisdom, embrace abundance, and cultivate strength in our lives, aligning ourselves with the divine forces that govern the universe.
