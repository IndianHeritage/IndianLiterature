यमेव यतमानं यदैतं प्र वां भरन मानवाः देवयन्तः |आ सीदतं स्वं उ लोकं विदाने स्वास्थे भवतं इन्दवे नः ||
yame iva yatamāne yad aitam pra vām bharan mānuṣā devayantaḥ | ā sīdataṁ svam u lokaṁ vidāne svāsasthe bhavatam indave naḥ ||
  • yame iva yatamāne: This phrase can be translated as "to whom we strive" or "to whom we direct our efforts." It signifies the importance of intention and focus in our endeavors.
  • yad aitam pra vām bharan mānuṣā devayantaḥ: This part means "what is brought forth by the divine beings for you, O humans." It emphasizes the connection between human efforts and divine support, suggesting that our actions are guided by higher powers.
  • ā sīdataṁ svam u lokaṁ vidāne: This translates to "may we attain our own realm of existence." It reflects the aspiration for personal growth and fulfillment, indicating that each individual has a unique path to follow.
  • svāsasthe bhavatam indave naḥ: This means "may you be well and prosperous, O divine one." It is a prayer for well-being and prosperity, acknowledging the role of divine forces in our lives.

The mantra as a whole can be interpreted as a call for guidance and support from the divine while we strive towards our goals. It highlights the interplay between human effort and divine assistance, suggesting that while we work hard, we should also seek blessings from higher powers.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios:

  • Personal Goals: When pursuing personal ambitions, such as education or career advancement, one can reflect on this mantra to remind themselves of the importance of focus and the support they may receive from the universe.
  • Community Efforts: In community service or social initiatives, this mantra can inspire individuals to work together, acknowledging that their collective efforts are supported by a greater purpose.
  • Spiritual Practices: During meditation or prayer, reciting this mantra can help individuals connect with their inner selves and the divine, fostering a sense of peace and direction.

In conclusion, this RigVeda mantra serves as a reminder of the significance of intention, the importance of divine support, and the pursuit of personal and communal growth. It encourages individuals to strive for their goals while remaining open to the guidance and blessings that may come from higher powers, ultimately leading to a fulfilling and prosperous life.
