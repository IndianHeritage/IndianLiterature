समानेव वपुष्यतः कृतवान मानवश युगाः |विदे तद इन्द्रश चेतनम् अध श्रुतो भद्राः इन्द्रस्य रातयः ||
samaneva vapuṣyataḥ kṛṇavan mānuṣā yugā | vide tad indraś cetanam adha śruto bhadrā indrasya rātayaḥ ||
  • Mantra: "samaneva vapuṣyataḥ kṛṇavan mānuṣā yugā | vide tad indraś cetanam adha śruto bhadrā indrasya rātayaḥ ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • samaneva: like one, together
    • vapuṣyataḥ: of the bodies, forms
    • kṛṇavan: creating, making
    • mānuṣā: human beings
    • yugā: ages, epochs
    • vide: know, understand
    • tad: that
    • indraś: of Indra, the king of gods
    • cetanam: consciousness, awareness
    • adha: below, beneath
    • śruto: heard, known
    • bhadrā: auspicious, good
    • indrasya: of Indra
    • rātayaḥ: chariots, vehicles
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the unity and collective strength of humanity across different ages.
    • The phrase "samaneva vapuṣyataḥ" emphasizes the idea of humans coming together, sharing a common purpose and form.
    • "kṛṇavan mānuṣā yugā" suggests that it is the responsibility of humans to create and shape their destinies through collaboration.
    • The reference to "Indra" symbolizes leadership and divine guidance, indicating that with awareness and consciousness, humans can achieve greatness.
    • The mantra encourages individuals to listen and understand the deeper truths ("śruto bhadrā") that lead to auspicious outcomes.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In community service, people from diverse backgrounds come together to address social issues, embodying the essence of "samaneva."
    • In workplaces, teamwork and collaboration are essential for success, reflecting the idea of "kṛṇavan mānuṣā yugā."
    • In leadership, effective leaders inspire collective action, akin to the guidance of "Indra," fostering a sense of shared purpose.
    • Listening to the needs of the community and responding with empathy can lead to positive change, resonating with "śruto bhadrā."
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of unity, collaboration, and consciousness in human endeavors.
    • It serves as a reminder that by working together and being aware of our collective responsibilities, we can create a better world.
    • In various aspects of life—be it personal, professional, or social—applying the principles of this mantra can lead to harmonious and prosperous outcomes.
