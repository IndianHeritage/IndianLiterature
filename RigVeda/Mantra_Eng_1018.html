उषस तं अश्यां यशसं सुवीरं दासप्रवर्गं रयिम अश्वबुध्यम् | सुदंससां श्रवसा या विभासि वाजप्रसूता सुभगे बृहन्तम् ||
uṣas tam aśyāṁ yaśasaṁ suvīraṁ dāsapravargaṁ rayim aśvabudhyam | sudaṁsasā śravasā yā vibhāsi vājaprasūtā subhage bṛhantam ||
  • uṣas: This word refers to the dawn or the first light of day. In many cultures, dawn symbolizes new beginnings and hope.
  • tam: This means 'that' or 'the' and is used to indicate something specific.
  • aśyāṁ: This term can be interpreted as 'darkness' or 'night', representing ignorance or obstacles in life.
  • yaśasaṁ: This translates to 'fame' or 'glory', indicating the pursuit of recognition and success.
  • suvīraṁ: Meaning 'good hero' or 'valiant', it emphasizes the importance of courage and strength in overcoming challenges.
  • dāsapravargaṁ: This can be understood as 'the group of servants' or 'followers', highlighting the significance of community and support.
  • rayim: This word means 'wealth' or 'prosperity', which is essential for a fulfilling life.
  • aśvabudhyam: This term refers to 'the one who understands horses', symbolizing wisdom and the ability to navigate life's challenges effectively.
  • sudaṁsasā: This means 'with good teeth', which can be interpreted as a metaphor for health and vitality.
  • śravasā: This translates to 'with fame' or 'renown', reinforcing the idea of achieving recognition through one's actions.
  • yā: This means 'who' or 'which', used to connect the qualities being described.
  • vibhāsi: This means 'shining' or 'illuminating', symbolizing enlightenment and clarity.
  • vājaprasūtā: This term refers to 'born of strength' or 'produced by power', indicating the importance of strength in achieving goals.
  • subhage: This means 'auspicious' or 'fortunate', suggesting that good fortune accompanies those who strive for greatness.
  • bṛhantam: This translates to 'great' or 'vast', emphasizing the expansive nature of success and achievement.

In conclusion, this mantra from the Rigveda encapsulates the journey from darkness to light, emphasizing the importance of courage, community, and wisdom in achieving fame and prosperity. It serves as a reminder that with the right mindset and support, one can overcome obstacles and attain greatness. In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as pursuing personal goals, fostering teamwork in professional settings, or seeking enlightenment in one's spiritual journey. It encourages individuals to embrace new beginnings, strive for excellence, and recognize the value of collaboration and strength in their endeavors.
