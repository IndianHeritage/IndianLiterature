अधā नो विश्वसौभग हिरण्यवाशीमत्तम |धनानि सुषणā कृधि ||
adhā no viśvasaubhaga hiraṇyavāśīmattama | dhanāni suṣaṇā kṛdhi ||
  • adhā - This word translates to "down" or "below," indicating a grounding or foundational aspect.
  • no - This means "our," suggesting a sense of possession or belonging.
  • viśvasaubhaga - This compound word can be broken down into "viśva" (universal) and "saubhaga" (prosperity or good fortune), implying a universal abundance or well-being.
  • hiraṇyavāśī - This term refers to "golden" or "wealthy," often associated with prosperity and richness.
  • mattama - This means "the best" or "the most excellent," indicating a peak or highest quality.
  • dhanāni - This translates to "wealth" or "riches," referring to material and spiritual abundance.
  • suṣaṇā - This word means "well-formed" or "well-structured," suggesting that the wealth should be organized and beneficial.
  • kṛdhi - This means "create" or "make," indicating an action to be taken.

The mantra can be interpreted as a prayer or invocation for the creation of universal prosperity and well-being. It emphasizes the importance of grounding oneself in abundance and striving for the best forms of wealth, both materially and spiritually.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios:

  • Personal Development: Individuals can use this mantra as a reminder to cultivate a mindset of abundance, focusing on personal growth and the pursuit of excellence in their endeavors.
  • Community Building: In a community context, this mantra encourages collective efforts to create a prosperous environment where everyone can thrive, emphasizing collaboration and shared resources.
  • Business Practices: Entrepreneurs and business leaders can adopt the principles of this mantra by ensuring their ventures are not only profitable but also contribute positively to society, creating wealth that is well-structured and beneficial for all stakeholders.

Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for a holistic approach to wealth and prosperity. It encourages individuals and communities to strive for the best while remaining grounded in the principles of abundance and well-being. By applying these teachings in daily life, one can foster a more prosperous and harmonious existence, both personally and collectively.
