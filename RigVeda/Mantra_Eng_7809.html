ते नः सहस्रिणं रयिम् पवन्ताम् आ सुवीर्यम् |सुवानाः देवासः इन्दवः ||
te naḥ sahasriṇaṁ rayim pavantām ā suvīryam | suvānā devāsa indavaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • te: to you
    • naḥ: our
    • sahasriṇaṁ: of a thousand
    • rayim: wealth or prosperity
    • pavantām: may it flow or come
    • ā: towards
    • suvīryam: excellent strength or valor
    • suvānā: well-formed or auspicious
    • devāsa: of the gods
    • indavaḥ: may they grant or bestow
  • Overall Meaning: This mantra is a prayer for the flow of abundant wealth and strength from the divine. It expresses a desire for the blessings of the gods to bestow prosperity and valor upon us.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be invoked by entrepreneurs seeking success and growth in their ventures. For instance, a startup founder may recite this mantra before a crucial pitch to attract investors and resources.
    • In personal life, individuals may use this mantra during times of financial difficulty, hoping to attract opportunities and support from the universe or divine forces.
    • During community gatherings or festivals, this mantra can be chanted to invoke collective prosperity and strength, fostering a sense of unity and shared purpose among participants.
  • Significance and Application:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking divine assistance in achieving material and spiritual goals. It highlights the belief that true prosperity comes not just from individual effort but also from the blessings of higher powers.
    • In modern life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as during financial planning, career development, or even in personal relationships, where the strength and support of others are crucial.
    • By reciting this mantra, individuals can cultivate a mindset of abundance and gratitude, which can lead to positive outcomes in their endeavors.
