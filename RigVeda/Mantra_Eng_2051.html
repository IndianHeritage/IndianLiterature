आ यन् मे अभ्वं वनदः पनन्तोशिग्भ्यो नामिमीत वर्‍णम् |स चित्रेण चिकीते रंसु भासा जुजुर्वाम् यो मुहुर आ युवां भूत् ||
ā yan me abhvaṁ vanadaḥ panantośigbhyo nāmimīta varṇam | sa citreṇa cikite raṁsu bhāsā jujurvām̐ yo muhur ā yuvā bhūt ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā - from
    • yan - that which
    • me - my
    • abhvaṁ - existence
    • vanadaḥ - of the forest
    • panantośigbhyo - from the depths
    • nāmimīta - I invoke
    • varṇam - color or form
    • sa - he
    • citreṇa - with brilliance
    • cikite - to shine
    • raṁsu - to bring forth
    • bhāsā - light
    • jujurvām̐ - I seek
    • yo - who
    • muhur - repeatedly
    • ā - from
    • yuvā - youth
    • bhūt - existence
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of invoking the essence of existence and the brilliance that comes from the depths of nature.
    • The reference to "vanadaḥ" (forest) symbolizes the richness of life and the interconnectedness of all beings.
    • By calling upon the "varṇam" (color or form), it emphasizes the diversity and beauty of creation.
    • The act of invoking light ("bhāsā") signifies the pursuit of knowledge and enlightenment.
    • The repetition of "muhur" (repeatedly) suggests a continuous quest for understanding and connection with the universe.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In nature, the forest is a source of life, providing resources and a habitat for countless species. This reflects the importance of preserving our environment.
    • In personal growth, seeking knowledge and enlightenment can be likened to a student’s journey through education, where they continuously strive to learn and grow.
    • The diversity in cultures and communities can be seen as the "varṇam" of humanity, where each individual contributes to the richness of society.
    • In relationships, the pursuit of understanding and connection mirrors the mantra's call for light and brilliance in interactions with others.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of existence, the beauty of diversity, and the pursuit of knowledge.
    • It encourages individuals to connect with nature, seek enlightenment, and appreciate the richness of life.
    • In real-life scenarios, it serves as a reminder to cherish our environment, embrace diversity, and continuously strive for personal and collective growth.
