यो देवो देवतमो जायमानो महो वाजेभिर महद्भिः च शुष्मैः | दधानो वज्रम बाह्वोर उषन्तं द्याम अमेन रेजयत प्र भूमा ||
yo devo devatamo jāyamāno maho vājebhir mahadbhiś ca śuṣmaiḥ | dadhāno vajram bāhvor uśantaṁ dyām amena rejayat pra bhūma ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yo: who
    • devo: divine
    • devatamo: the most divine
    • jāyamāno: being born
    • maho: great
    • vājebhir: with strength
    • mahadbhiś: with great ones
    • ca: and
    • śuṣmaiḥ: with the dry
    • dadhāno: holding
    • vajram: thunderbolt
    • bāhvor: of the arms
    • uśantaṁ: powerful
    • dyām: the sky
    • amenā: with strength
    • rejayat: may shine
    • pra: forth
    • bhūma: the earth
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of a divine being who is born with great strength and power.
    • The reference to "vajram" (thunderbolt) symbolizes immense power and the ability to overcome obstacles.
    • The mantra emphasizes the connection between the divine and the earthly realms, suggesting that the divine energy can manifest in the physical world.
    • It highlights the importance of strength, both physical and spiritual, in achieving greatness.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In personal development, individuals often seek to harness their inner strength to overcome challenges, much like the divine energy described in the mantra.
    • In leadership, a strong leader embodies the qualities of power and resilience, inspiring others to achieve greatness.
    • In nature, the thunderbolt represents the raw power of storms, reminding us of the forces beyond our control and the beauty of nature's strength.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of divine strength and power in our lives.
    • It encourages individuals to tap into their inner strength and strive for greatness, both personally and collectively.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as personal growth, leadership, and understanding the forces of nature.
    • Ultimately, it serves as a reminder of the connection between the divine and the earthly, urging us to recognize and harness the power within ourselves.
