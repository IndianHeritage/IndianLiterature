आ वाम अश्वासः सुयुजो वहन्तु यतरश्मय उप यन्त्व अरवाक् |घृतस्य निर्णिग अनु वर्तते वाम उप सिन्धवः प्रदिवि क्षरन्ति ||
ā vām aśvāsaḥ suyujo vahantu yataraśmaya upa yantv arvāk | ghṛtasya nirṇig anu vartate vām upa sindhavaḥ pradivi kṣaranti ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā vām: From us, may you come.
    • aśvāsaḥ: Horses (or energy) that are swift.
    • suyujo: Well-connected or well-yoked.
    • vahantu: May they carry or bring.
    • yatarashmaya: To the place of prosperity or abundance.
    • upa yantv: May they lead us.
    • arvāk: To the front or forward.
    • ghṛtasya: Of ghee (clarified butter), symbolizing nourishment.
    • nirṇig: The one that is pure or clear.
    • anu vartate: Follows or proceeds.
    • vām: To you.
    • upa sindhavaḥ: May the rivers lead us.
    • pradivi: In the sky or heaven.
    • kṣaranti: Flow or move.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the swift energy represented by horses, symbolizing strength and speed.
    • The phrase "may they carry us to the place of prosperity" suggests a desire for guidance towards success and abundance.
    • The mention of "ghee" signifies nourishment and purity, indicating that the path to prosperity should be wholesome and beneficial.
    • The rivers symbolize the flow of life and resources, suggesting that one should be open to the natural currents of opportunity.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be applied when seeking new opportunities. Just as horses carry their riders swiftly, one can seek mentors or partners who can help navigate the path to success.
    • In personal development, the mantra encourages individuals to pursue their goals with vigor and clarity, ensuring that their actions are aligned with their values (symbolized by ghee).
    • In community building, the rivers can represent the collective efforts of individuals working together towards a common goal, emphasizing the importance of collaboration and support.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking guidance and support in our endeavors, whether in personal, professional, or communal aspects of life.
    • It serves as a reminder to pursue paths that are nourishing and beneficial, aligning our actions with our higher goals.
    • Ultimately, it highlights the interconnectedness of our journeys, encouraging us to flow with the opportunities that life presents.
