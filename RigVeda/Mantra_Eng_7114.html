तं हि स्वराजं वृषभं तं ओजसे धिषणे निष्टतक्षतुः |उतोपमानां प्रथमो न शीदसि सोमकामं हि ते मनः ||
taṁ hi svarājaṁ vṛṣabhaṁ tam ojase dhiṣaṇe niṣṭatakṣatuḥ | utopamānām prathamo ni ṣīdasi somakāmaṁ hi te manaḥ ||
  • taṁ hi svarājaṁ vṛṣabhaṁ: This phrase can be translated as "that indeed is the sovereign, the bull." In this context, "svarājaṁ" refers to a state of self-rule or sovereignty, while "vṛṣabhaṁ" symbolizes strength and power, often associated with the bull in Vedic literature. In real life, this can be likened to a leader or a strong individual who embodies authority and control.
  • tam ojase dhiṣaṇe niṣṭatakṣatuḥ: Here, "tam ojase" means "that with brilliance" and "dhiṣaṇe" refers to the act of directing or guiding. "Niṣṭatakṣatuḥ" implies a firm establishment or grounding. This can be interpreted as the need for strong guidance and direction in life, akin to a mentor or a guiding principle that helps one navigate challenges. For example, a teacher who inspires students to achieve their potential can be seen as a "dhiṣaṇe" in their lives.
  • utopamānām prathamo ni ṣīdasi: This translates to "you sit first among those who are like you." It emphasizes the importance of being in the company of like-minded individuals or those who share similar values and goals. In a real-life scenario, this can be related to networking in professional settings, where being surrounded by peers can foster growth and collaboration.
  • somakāmaṁ hi te manaḥ: This phrase means "for your mind desires the essence of the moon." "Somakāmaṁ" refers to the desire for peace, tranquility, and nourishment, often symbolized by the moon in Vedic texts. This can be related to the human pursuit of inner peace and fulfillment, such as through meditation or mindfulness practices that help individuals connect with their inner selves.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of leadership, guidance, community, and the pursuit of inner peace. It signifies the importance of having strong, sovereign leaders who can guide others with brilliance and direction. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios, such as in workplaces where effective leadership is crucial for success, in educational institutions where mentors shape the future of students, and in personal lives where individuals seek peace and fulfillment. By embodying these principles, one can navigate life's challenges more effectively and foster a supportive community around them.
