उन्नत्ति भूमिम पृथिवीं उता द्यां यदा दुग्धं वरुणो वष्ट्यादित् |सम अभ्रेण वसता पर्वतासस तविषीयन्तः श्रथयन्त वीराः ||
unatti bhūmim pṛthivīm uta dyāṁ yadā dugdhaṁ varuṇo vaṣṭy ād it | sam abhreṇa vasata parvatāsas taviṣīyantaḥ śrathayanta vīrāḥ ||
  • Unatti bhūmim pṛthivīm uta dyāṁ: This phrase can be translated as "the elevation of the earth and the sky." It signifies the importance of both terrestrial and celestial realms in our lives. In real life, this can be seen in the way we strive for balance between our earthly responsibilities and our spiritual aspirations.
  • Yadā dugdhaṁ varuṇo vaṣṭy ād it: This translates to "when Varuna, the god of cosmic order, bestows his blessings." Varuna represents the moral and ethical order of the universe. In practical terms, this can be related to moments when we feel a sense of justice or fairness in our lives, such as when we witness acts of kindness or integrity in our communities.
  • Sam abhreṇa vasata parvatāsas: This means "the mountains reside in harmony with the clouds." It symbolizes the interconnectedness of nature and the importance of harmony in our environment. For example, this can be observed in sustainable practices where humans work in tandem with nature, such as organic farming or conservation efforts.
  • Taviṣīyantaḥ śrathayanta vīrāḥ: This translates to "the brave ones who strive and are victorious." It emphasizes the qualities of courage and perseverance. In real life, this can be seen in individuals who overcome challenges, such as athletes who train rigorously to achieve their goals or activists who fight for social justice despite facing adversity.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of striving for balance between the earthly and the divine, the importance of moral order, the harmony of nature, and the valor of individuals. Its significance lies in encouraging us to seek a holistic approach to life, where we respect our environment, uphold justice, and embody courage in our pursuits. In today's world, this mantra can inspire us to engage in sustainable living, advocate for ethical practices, and cultivate resilience in the face of challenges.
