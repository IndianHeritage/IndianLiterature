तं वर्धयन्तो मतीभिः शिवाभिः सिंहम iva नानदतं सधस्थे | बृहस्पतिं वृषणं शूरसातौ भरे-भरे अनु मदेम जिष्णुम ||
taṁ vardhayanto matibhiḥ śivābhiḥ siṁham iva nānadataṁ sadhasthe | bṛhaspatiṁ vṛṣaṇaṁ śūrasātau bhare-bhare anu madema jiṣṇum ||
  • taṁ vardhayanto: This phrase translates to "may we increase him." It signifies the desire to enhance or elevate someone or something, often referring to a deity or a guiding force.
  • matibhiḥ: This means "with our intellects" or "with our thoughts." It emphasizes the importance of wisdom and understanding in the process of growth and elevation.
  • śivābhiḥ: This word refers to "auspicious" or "blessed." It indicates that the growth or elevation should be in a positive and beneficial manner.
  • siṁham iva nānadataṁ: This translates to "like a lion, not being subdued." It symbolizes strength, courage, and the ability to overcome challenges without being defeated.
  • sadhasthe: This means "in the right place" or "in the proper position." It suggests that the elevation or growth should occur in a suitable and appropriate context.
  • bṛhaspatiṁ: Referring to "Bṛhaspati," the deity of wisdom and knowledge, this term highlights the importance of divine guidance in our endeavors.
  • vṛṣaṇaṁ: This word means "the one who is strong" or "the powerful." It signifies the strength and capability required to achieve success.
  • śūrasātau: This translates to "in the presence of the brave." It emphasizes the need for courage and valor in the pursuit of goals.
  • bhare-bhare: This phrase means "with abundance" or "with plenty." It indicates a desire for prosperity and richness in life.
  • anu madema jiṣṇum: This translates to "may we attain victory." It expresses the aspiration for success and triumph in our endeavors.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a profound message about the importance of wisdom, strength, and auspiciousness in the pursuit of growth and success. It encourages individuals to seek divine guidance and to approach their goals with courage and intellect. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as personal development, professional growth, and spiritual pursuits. For instance, when facing challenges in a career, one can invoke the essence of this mantra to seek clarity, strength, and the right opportunities, ultimately leading to success and fulfillment. The significance of this mantra lies in its ability to inspire individuals to strive for excellence while remaining grounded in wisdom and positivity.
