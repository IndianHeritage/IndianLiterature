सपर्णेण्यः स प्रियः विक्ष्व अग्निर होतā मन्द्रो नि षसादā यजीयान् |तं त्वा वयं दमा ā दीदिवांसम् उप जñुबाधो नमसā सदेम ||
saparyeṇyaḥ sa priyo vikṣv a1gnir hotā mandro ni ṣasādā yajīyān | taṁ tvā vayaṁ dama ā dīdivāṁsam upa jñubādho namasā sadema ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • saparyeṇyaḥ: With reverence or worship.
    • sa: He or that.
    • priyo: Dear or beloved.
    • vikṣv: In all directions or everywhere.
    • a1gnir: The fire (Agni), a significant deity in Vedic texts.
    • hotā: The priest or the one who invokes.
    • mandro: The one who is wise or knowledgeable.
    • ni ṣasādā: Has established or has settled.
    • yajīyān: The one who is worthy of worship or sacrifice.
    • taṁ: Him or that.
    • tvā: You.
    • vayaṁ: We.
    • dama: Restraint or control.
    • ā: To or towards.
    • dīdivāṁsam: The one who shines or is radiant.
    • upa: Near or close to.
    • jñubādho: With devotion or with respect.
    • namasā: With reverence or salutation.
    • sadema: May we attain or may we achieve.
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra invokes Agni, the fire god, who is considered a mediator between humans and the divine.
    • It emphasizes the importance of worship and reverence towards Agni, who is seen as a beloved and wise entity.
    • The phrase "we attain" signifies a collective aspiration for spiritual growth and enlightenment.
    • In real life, this can be related to the importance of community and collective efforts in achieving goals, much like how a group of people come together to celebrate a festival or perform a ritual.
  • Significance:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of respect, worship, and the pursuit of knowledge.
    • It teaches us the value of community and the importance of working together towards a common goal.
    • In modern life, this can be applied in various scenarios such as teamwork in workplaces, community service, and even family gatherings where collective efforts lead to greater achievements.
    • Ultimately, it serves as a reminder to honor the divine and the wisdom that guides us in our endeavors.
