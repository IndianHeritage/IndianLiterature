हवन्त उ त्वा हव्यम् विवाचि तनूषु शूराः सूर्यस्य सातौ |त्वं विश्वेषु सेन्यो जनेषु त्वं वृत्राणि रन्धयाऽसुहन्तु ||
havanta u tvā havyaṁ vivāci tanūṣu śūrāḥ sūryasya sātau | tvaṁ viśveṣu senyo janeṣu tvaṁ vṛtrāṇi randhayā suhantu ||
  • Mantra: "havanta u tvā havyaṁ vivāci tanūṣu śūrāḥ sūryasya sātau | tvaṁ viśveṣu senyo janeṣu tvaṁ vṛtrāṇi randhayā suhantu ||"
  • Translation: This mantra can be translated as: "O Agni, we invoke you, the worthy one, who is praised by the brave in the presence of the Sun. You are the one who brings together all beings; may you destroy the obstacles in our path."
  • Word-by-Word Explanation:
    • Havanta: Those who offer or invoke.
    • Tvā: You (referring to Agni, the fire god).
    • Havyaṁ: Worthy of offerings.
    • Vivāci: Praised or spoken of.
    • Tanūṣu: In the bodies or forms.
    • Śūrāḥ: The brave or the strong.
    • Sūryasya: Of the Sun.
    • Sātau: In the presence or in the company.
    • Tvaṁ: You.
    • Viśveṣu: In all beings.
    • Senyo: In the groups or assemblies.
    • Janeṣu: Among people.
    • Vṛtrāṇi: The obstacles or enemies.
    • Randhayā: To destroy or break.
    • Suhantu: May they be favorable or auspicious.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, invoking the spirit of teamwork and collaboration can be likened to calling upon Agni to unite the team (the brave) in the presence of a common goal (the Sun).
    • In personal life, when facing challenges, one might seek the strength to overcome obstacles, similar to how the mantra calls for the destruction of barriers (Vṛtrāṇi) in one’s path.
    • During community gatherings, the collective invocation for peace and harmony can reflect the essence of this mantra, as it emphasizes unity among all beings (Viśveṣu).
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for unity, strength, and the removal of obstacles. It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, such as in personal challenges, team dynamics, and community efforts. By invoking the qualities of Agni, individuals and groups can foster collaboration, overcome difficulties, and strive for collective success. This mantra serves as a reminder of the power of unity and the importance of seeking strength in the face of adversity.
