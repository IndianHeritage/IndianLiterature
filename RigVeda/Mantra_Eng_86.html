अस्मान् सु तत्र चोदयिन्द्र राये रभस्वतः |तुविद्युम्न यशस्वतः ||
asmān su tatra codayendra rāye rabhasvataḥ | tuvidyumna yaśasvataḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • asmān: Us
    • su: Well/Good
    • tatra: There/At that place
    • codayendra: O Indra, inspire/guide
    • rāye: Wealth/Prosperity
    • rabhasvataḥ: With vigor/energy
    • tuvidyumna: One who is knowledgeable/wise
    • yaśasvataḥ: One who is famous/glorious
  • Overall Meaning: This mantra is a prayer to Indra, the king of gods, asking for guidance and inspiration towards prosperity and success. It emphasizes the importance of wisdom and glory in achieving one's goals.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, a team may invoke this mantra to seek inspiration and guidance from their leaders to achieve their targets effectively.
    • Students preparing for exams might recite this mantra to invoke focus and wisdom, hoping to excel in their studies and gain recognition.
    • Entrepreneurs may use this mantra as a motivational tool to inspire themselves and their teams to work energetically towards their business goals.
  • Significance and Application:
    • This mantra highlights the importance of seeking divine guidance in our endeavors, reminding us that success is not solely based on our efforts but also on the wisdom and inspiration we receive.
    • It encourages individuals to pursue their goals with vigor and to be knowledgeable and glorious in their pursuits, which can lead to prosperity.
    • In daily life, this mantra can be applied in various situations where one seeks motivation, whether in personal growth, professional achievements, or academic success.
