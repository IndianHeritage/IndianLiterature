तां वाम अद्य ताव अपरं हुवेमोचन्त्यां उषसि वह्निर उक्थैः |नासत्यā कुह चित संताव आर्यो दिवो नपातā सुदास्तराय ||
tā vām adya tāv aparaṁ huvemocchantyām uṣasi vahnir ukthaiḥ | nāsatyā kuha cit santāv aryo divo napātā sudāstarāya ||
  • tā vām adya tāv aparaṁ huvemocchantyām uṣasi vahnir ukthaiḥ
    • tā vām adya - "We invoke you today" - This phrase signifies the act of calling upon divine forces or energies for assistance and guidance in our daily lives.
    • tāv aparaṁ - "and that which is beyond" - This suggests a connection to the higher realms or the unknown, indicating a desire for enlightenment and understanding of the greater universe.
    • huvemocchantyām - "we offer our prayers" - This emphasizes the importance of prayer and intention in seeking help from the divine.
    • uṣasi vahnir ukthaiḥ - "in the dawn, the fire of the hymn" - This refers to the sacredness of dawn, a time of new beginnings, and the power of hymns or mantras to invoke spiritual energy.
  • nāsatyā kuha cit santāv aryo divo napātā sudāstarāya
    • nāsatyā - "O Nāsatyas" - Nāsatyas are the twin deities associated with health and medicine, symbolizing the importance of well-being in life.
    • kuha cit - "wherever" - This indicates the omnipresence of divine energy, suggesting that help can be sought from anywhere in the universe.
    • santāv - "may they be present" - A call for the divine to be present in our lives, highlighting the need for spiritual guidance.
    • aryo divo napātā - "the noble offspring of heaven" - This refers to the connection between humanity and the divine, emphasizing our shared heritage and the potential for greatness.
    • sudāstarāya - "for the best of the best" - A plea for the highest good, indicating the aspiration for excellence in all aspects of life.

Conclusion:

This mantra from the Rigveda encapsulates the essence of seeking divine guidance and support in our lives. It emphasizes the importance of prayer, the sacredness of new beginnings, and the pursuit of health and well-being. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied during moments of uncertainty or when embarking on new ventures, such as starting a new job, moving to a new place, or facing personal challenges. By invoking the divine and seeking their presence, individuals can find strength, clarity, and inspiration to navigate their paths. Ultimately, this mantra serves as a reminder of our connection to the universe and the potential for greatness that lies within each of us.
