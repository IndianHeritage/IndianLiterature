आ सखायः सबरदुगां धेनुम अजध्वम् उप नव्यसां वचः |सृजध्वम् अनपस्फुराम् ||
ā sakhāyaḥ sabardughāṁ dhenum ajadhvam upa navyasā vacaḥ | sṛjadhvam anapasphurām ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā: from, away
    • sakhāyaḥ: friends, companions
    • sabardughāṁ: with great effort, with strength
    • dhenum: the cow, symbolizing nourishment and wealth
    • ajadhvam: do not be disturbed, do not hesitate
    • upa: towards, near
    • navyasā: new, fresh
    • vacaḥ: words, speech
    • sṛjadhvam: create, produce
    • anapasphurām: without any hindrance, unobstructed
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra encourages individuals to come together as friends and companions.
    • The mention of "sabardughāṁ dhenum" signifies the importance of collective effort in achieving prosperity and nourishment.
    • It advises not to hesitate or be disturbed in the pursuit of goals, emphasizing the need for confidence and determination.
    • The call to "create" and "produce" suggests that through unity and clear communication, new ideas and opportunities can emerge.
    • The phrase "anapasphurām" highlights the importance of an unobstructed path in our endeavors, indicating that clarity and focus are essential for success.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace setting, teamwork is crucial. When colleagues collaborate effectively, they can achieve greater results, much like the mantra suggests.
    • In community projects, individuals coming together to support a common cause can lead to significant positive changes, reflecting the essence of unity and shared effort.
    • In personal relationships, open communication and support among friends can help overcome challenges, embodying the spirit of the mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of unity, collaboration, and clear communication in achieving success and prosperity.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, including workplaces, community initiatives, and personal relationships.
    • By embodying the principles of this mantra, individuals can foster an environment of support and creativity, leading to fruitful outcomes.
