त्रिरन्तरिक्षं सविता महित्वना त्रि राजांसि परिभुः त्रिणि रोचनाः |तिस्रो दिवः पृथिवीस तिस्रा इवति त्रिभिर व्रतैर अभि नो रक्षति तमनः ||
trir antarikṣaṁ savitā mahitvanā trī rajāṁsi paribhus trīṇi rocanā | tisro divaḥ pṛthivīs tisra invati tribhir vratair abhi no rakṣati tmanā ||
  • Mantra: "trir antarikṣaṁ savitā mahitvanā trī rajāṁsi paribhus trīṇi rocanā | tisro divaḥ pṛthivīs tisra invati tribhir vratair abhi no rakṣati tmanā ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • trir: three
    • antarikṣaṁ: the space or atmosphere
    • savitā: the Sun or the divine light
    • mahitvanā: with great power or majesty
    • trī: three
    • rajāṁsi: kings or rulers
    • paribhus: encompassing or surrounding
    • trīṇi: three
    • rocanā: shining or illuminating
    • tisro: three
    • dvaḥ: heavens
    • pṛthivīs: earth
    • tisra: three
    • invati: invoke or call upon
    • tribhir: three
    • vratair: vows or commitments
    • abhi: towards or for
    • no: us
    • rakṣati: protects
    • tmanā: with the self or soul
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the divine power of the Sun (Savitā) and its encompassing nature over the three realms: the heavens, the earth, and the atmosphere.
    • The repetition of "three" signifies the importance of balance and harmony in life, as represented by the three realms and the three kings or rulers.
    • The mantra emphasizes the protective qualities of the divine, invoking the Sun's light to safeguard the self and the community through vows and commitments.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, one can relate to the concept of balance by maintaining harmony between work, family, and personal time, akin to the three realms mentioned in the mantra.
    • The Sun's light can be seen as a metaphor for knowledge and wisdom, guiding individuals in their decisions and actions.
    • Vows or commitments can be reflected in personal relationships, where promises made to loved ones create a protective bond, much like the divine protection invoked in the mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of all aspects of life and the importance of divine guidance and protection.
    • It encourages individuals to seek balance, uphold their commitments, and invoke the light of wisdom in their lives.
    • In a broader context, it highlights the significance of spirituality in fostering a sense of community and personal growth.
