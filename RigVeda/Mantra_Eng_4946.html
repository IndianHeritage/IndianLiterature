सम उपूष्णा गमेमहि यो गृहां अभिशासति |इमा एवति च ब्रवत ||
sam u pūṣṇā gamemahi yo gṛhām̐ abhiśāsati | ima eveti ca bravat ||
  • Mantra: "sam u pūṣṇā gamemahi yo gṛhām̐ abhiśāsati | ima eveti ca bravat ||"
  • Translation: "May we, together with the nourishing one, approach the house of the one who commands us; let us speak this truth."
  • Word-by-Word Explanation:
    • sam: together, in unity
    • u: and, also
    • pūṣṇā: the nourishing one, often referring to the divine or a deity that provides sustenance
    • gamemahi: may we approach, may we go towards
    • yo: who, the one who
    • gṛhām̐: house, abode, or dwelling
    • abhiśāsati: commands, rules, or governs
    • ima: this, these
    • eveti: indeed, truly
    • ca: and
    • bravat: speak, utter, or declare
  • Contextual Significance:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of unity and collective action in seeking nourishment and guidance.
    • The "nourishing one" can be interpreted as a divine force or a community that supports and sustains individuals.
    • The act of approaching the "house" of the one who commands signifies seeking wisdom, authority, or leadership.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community setting, this mantra can be applied when people come together to support a local initiative, such as a food drive or community garden, emphasizing the collective effort to nourish each other.
    • In a workplace, it can represent the importance of teamwork and collaboration, where employees unite to achieve a common goal under the guidance of a leader.
    • In personal relationships, it highlights the significance of mutual support and understanding, where individuals come together to nurture each other's growth.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the power of unity and the importance of seeking nourishment—both physical and spiritual—through collective efforts.
    • It encourages individuals to approach those who provide guidance and support, fostering a sense of community and shared purpose.
    • In today's fast-paced world, the teachings of this mantra can be applied in various aspects of life, promoting collaboration, understanding, and mutual respect.
