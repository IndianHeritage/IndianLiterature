सम्राजाव अस्य भुवनस्य राजथो मित्रावरुणा विदथे स्वर्दृशा |वृष्टिं वां राधो अमृतत्वं ईमहे dyावापृथिवी वि चरन्ति तान्यवः ||
samrājāv asya bhuvanasya rājatho mitrāvaruṇā vidathe svardṛśā | vṛṣṭiṁ vāṁ rādho amṛtatvam īmahe dyāvāpṛthivī vi caranti tanyavaḥ ||
  • samrājāv asya bhuvanasya: This phrase translates to "the rulers of this world." It signifies the authority and governance over the universe, highlighting the importance of leadership and responsibility in maintaining order.
  • rājatho mitrāvaruṇā: Here, "rājatho" means "by the kings" and "mitrāvaruṇā" refers to the divine forces of friendship and cosmic order. This suggests that true leadership is guided by principles of friendship and harmony.
  • vidathe svardṛśā: "Vidathe" means "in the assembly" and "svardṛśā" refers to the divine vision or insight. This emphasizes the need for wise counsel and collective decision-making in governance.
  • vṛṣṭiṁ vāṁ rādho: This translates to "we seek your blessings." It indicates a plea for divine grace and support in achieving prosperity and success.
  • amṛtatvam īmahe: "Amṛtatvam" means "immortality" or "eternal life," and "īmahe" translates to "we aspire." This reflects the human desire for lasting legacy and spiritual fulfillment.
  • dyāvāpṛthivī vi caranti tanyavaḥ: This phrase means "the heavens and the earth move together." It signifies the interconnectedness of all elements of existence and the balance required for harmony in life.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda encapsulates the essence of leadership, divine guidance, and the pursuit of harmony in the universe. It teaches us that effective governance is rooted in friendship, wisdom, and collective effort. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios such as community leadership, organizational management, and even personal relationships. By fostering a spirit of cooperation and seeking divine blessings, individuals and leaders can aspire to create a more harmonious and prosperous world. The mantra serves as a reminder of our interconnectedness and the importance of striving for a balanced existence, both personally and collectively.
