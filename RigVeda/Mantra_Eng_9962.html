क्रतुप्रावाऽजिरिता शाश्वताम् अव इन्द्र इद् भद्रा प्रमतिः सुतावताम् |पूर्णमूर्धर दिव्यं यस्य सिक्तया आ सर्वतातिम् अदितिं वृणीमहे ||
kratuprāvā jaritā śaśvatām ava indra id bhadrā pramatiḥ sutāvatām | pūrṇam ūdhar divyaṁ yasya siktaya ā sarvatātim aditiṁ vṛṇīmahe ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • kratuprāvā: One who possesses wisdom and intellect.
    • jaritā: The one who is capable of action or movement.
    • śaśvatām: Eternal or everlasting.
    • ava: To come down or descend.
    • indra: The king of gods, representing power and authority.
    • id: This or that.
    • bhadrā: Auspicious or beneficial.
    • pramatiḥ: Wisdom or understanding.
    • sutāvatām: Of those who are well-born or noble.
    • pūrṇam: Complete or full.
    • ūdhar: The divine or celestial.
    • divyaṁ: Divine or heavenly.
    • yasya: Whose.
    • siktaya: Sprinkled or infused.
    • ā: From or out of.
    • sarvatātim: All-pervading or universal.
    • aditiṁ: The infinite or boundless.
    • vṛṇīmahe: We choose or we invoke.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the qualities of wisdom, action, and eternal truth.
    • It emphasizes the importance of choosing the auspicious path guided by divine wisdom.
    • The reference to Indra signifies the need for strength and authority in our endeavors.
    • It highlights the aspiration for completeness and the divine essence in our lives.
    • The mention of Aditi represents the infinite possibilities and the boundless nature of existence.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, this mantra can inspire individuals to seek wisdom and act with integrity, ensuring that their decisions are beneficial for all.
    • In personal relationships, invoking the qualities of understanding and compassion can lead to more harmonious interactions.
    • In educational pursuits, the mantra encourages students to strive for completeness in their knowledge and to embrace the infinite possibilities of learning.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of wisdom, action, and the pursuit of the divine in our daily lives.
    • It encourages individuals to make choices that are not only beneficial for themselves but also for the greater good.
    • By invoking these qualities, one can navigate life's challenges with strength and clarity, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling existence.
