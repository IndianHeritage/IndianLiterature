प्रति स्पशो वि सृज तूर्णितमो भवā पायुर वि शो अस्यā अदब्धः |यो नो दूरे अघशंसो यो अन्त्य अग्ने माकिष्टे व्यथिर ā दधर्षीत ||
prati spaśo vi sṛja tūrṇitamo bhavā pāyur viśo asyā adabdhaḥ | yo no dūre aghaśaṁso yo anty agne mākiṣ ṭe vyathir ā dadharṣīt ||
  • Mantra: "prati spaśo vi sṛja tūrṇitamo bhavā pāyur viśo asyā adabdhaḥ | yo no dūre aghaśaṁso yo anty agne mākiṣ ṭe vyathir ā dadharṣīt ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • prati: towards, in response to
    • spaśo: the touch, the sensation
    • vi: indeed, certainly
    • sṛja: create, manifest
    • tūrṇitamo: the swiftest, the most rapid
    • bhavā: existence, being
    • pāyur: protector, guardian
    • viśo: of the community, of the people
    • asyā: of this, of these
    • adabdhaḥ: unscathed, unharmed
    • yo: who
    • no: for us
    • dūre: far away
    • aghaśaṁso: free from sin, without wrongdoing
    • yo: who
    • anty: at the end, ultimate
    • agne: O Fire, O Agni
    • mākiṣ: may you protect
    • ṭe: your
    • vyathir: distress, suffering
    • ā: towards
    • dadharṣīt: may you not allow
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks to the creation and manifestation of sensations and experiences that are swift and protective.
    • It emphasizes the importance of being unharmed and free from wrongdoing, suggesting a moral and ethical dimension to existence.
    • The invocation of Agni (Fire) symbolizes transformation, purification, and the energy that drives life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a personal context, this mantra can be applied when facing challenges or distress. For instance, during a difficult time, one might seek the swift resolution of problems and protection from harm.
    • In a community setting, it can be a reminder for individuals to act ethically and support one another, ensuring that no one is left unprotected or harmed.
    • In a professional environment, it can inspire leaders to create a safe and nurturing atmosphere where employees feel secure and valued.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for swift action towards protection and ethical living.
    • It serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of individuals within a community and the importance of moral integrity.
    • In real life, it encourages us to seek protection from harm, act ethically, and support one another in times of distress, fostering a sense of unity and well-being.
