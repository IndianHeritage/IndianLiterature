विष्वा हि mortalत्वनानुकामाः शतक्रतो |अगन्म वज्रिन्न आशसः ||
viśvā hi martyatvanānukāmā śatakrato | aganma vajrinn āśasaḥ ||
  • Translation: "Indeed, all beings, desiring immortality, have approached you, O Hundred-Powered One; grant us strength and protection."

  • Word Breakdown:
    • viśvā: all, universal
    • hi: indeed, certainly
    • martyatvan: mortality, the state of being mortal
    • ānukāmā: desiring, longing for
    • śatakrato: O Hundred-Powered One, a reference to Indra, the king of gods
    • aganma: we have approached, we have come to
    • vajrinn: with strength, with power
    • āśasaḥ: grant us, bestow upon us

  • Contextual Meaning:
    • This mantra reflects the universal human desire for immortality and strength.
    • It acknowledges the limitations of human existence (mortality) while seeking divine assistance to overcome these limitations.
    • Indra, referred to as "Hundred-Powered," symbolizes immense strength and capability, representing the divine forces that can aid humanity.

  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern life, people often seek ways to enhance their health and longevity, whether through diet, exercise, or medical advancements. This reflects the desire for immortality.
    • In times of crisis, such as natural disasters or personal challenges, individuals and communities often turn to spiritual practices or prayers for strength and protection, mirroring the plea in this mantra.
    • In various cultures, rituals and offerings are made to deities for blessings of health and strength, similar to the invocation of Indra in this mantra.

  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of recognizing our mortal nature while striving for strength and protection through divine support.
    • It serves as a reminder that seeking help from higher powers is a common human experience, applicable in various aspects of life, from personal struggles to collective challenges.
    • Ultimately, it encourages individuals to acknowledge their vulnerabilities and seek empowerment, fostering resilience in the face of life's uncertainties.
