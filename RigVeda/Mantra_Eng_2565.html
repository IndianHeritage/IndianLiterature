परी विश्वानि सुदिताग्नेर अश्यामा मनमभिः |विप्रासो जातवेदसः ||
pari viśvāni sudhitāgner aśyāma manmabhiḥ | viprāso jātavedasaḥ ||
  • Mantra: "pari viśvāni sudhitāgner aśyāma manmabhiḥ | viprāso jātavedasaḥ ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • pari: all, everywhere
    • viśvāni: all beings, all aspects
    • sudhitā: well-thought-out, wise
    • agner: of the fire, of the divine light
    • aśyāma: may we attain, may we achieve
    • manmabhiḥ: with our minds, through our thoughts
    • viprāso: of the wise, the learned
    • jātavedasaḥ: the one who knows all, the all-knowing
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra invokes the divine fire (Agni), representing knowledge, wisdom, and enlightenment.
    • The phrase "pari viśvāni sudhitāgner" suggests a desire for comprehensive understanding and wisdom that encompasses all aspects of life.
    • "Aśyāma manmabhiḥ" indicates the aspiration to achieve this wisdom through our thoughts and intentions.
    • The term "viprāso jātavedasaḥ" refers to the wise individuals who possess deep knowledge and insight, emphasizing the importance of learning and understanding in our lives.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, this mantra can be applied to foster a culture of innovation and collaboration, where all employees are encouraged to share their ideas and insights to achieve collective success.
    • In education, teachers can embody the essence of this mantra by promoting critical thinking and encouraging students to explore various perspectives, leading to a deeper understanding of subjects.
    • In personal development, individuals can reflect on their thoughts and intentions, striving to cultivate wisdom and understanding in their daily lives, whether through meditation, reading, or engaging in meaningful conversations.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of seeking wisdom and understanding in all aspects of life.
    • It encourages individuals to harness their thoughts and intentions to achieve greater knowledge and insight.
    • In a world filled with information, the mantra serves as a reminder to focus on the quality of our understanding and the wisdom we share with others.
    • Ultimately, it emphasizes the interconnectedness of all beings and the collective pursuit of enlightenment.
