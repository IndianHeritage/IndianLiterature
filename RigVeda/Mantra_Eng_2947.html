युवोर ऋतं रोडसी सत्यं अस्तु महे षु णः सुविताय प्र भूतम् |इदं दिवे नमो अग्ने पृथिव्यै सपर्यामि प्रयसां यामि रत्नम् ||
yuvor ṛtaṁ rodasī satyam astu mahe ṣu ṇaḥ suvitāya pra bhūtam | idaṁ dive namo agne pṛthivyai saparyāmi prayasā yāmi ratnam ||
  • yuvor ṛtaṁ rodasī: This phrase can be translated as "the truth of the two (youths) in the sky." Here, "ṛtaṁ" refers to the cosmic order or truth, while "rodaśī" signifies the sky or the heavens. This can be interpreted as the harmony and balance that exists in the universe, akin to the balance we seek in our lives.
  • satyam astu mahe ṣu ṇaḥ: This translates to "let there be truth in the great ones." It emphasizes the importance of truthfulness and integrity, especially among those in positions of power or influence. In real life, this can be seen in leaders who uphold honesty and transparency, fostering trust within their communities.
  • suvitāya pra bhūtam: This means "may it be well-nourished and prosperous." It suggests a desire for abundance and well-being. In practical terms, this can relate to the importance of nurturing relationships, whether in family, friendships, or professional settings, to ensure mutual growth and prosperity.
  • idaṁ dive namo agne: This translates to "to you, O Agni, I offer my homage in the daytime." Agni, the fire god, symbolizes energy, transformation, and purification. In everyday life, this can be likened to the respect we give to the forces that drive our ambitions and aspirations, such as education, hard work, and creativity.
  • pṛthivyai saparyāmi: This means "I worship the Earth." It highlights the significance of nature and our connection to the planet. In contemporary society, this can be reflected in environmental conservation efforts and sustainable living practices that honor and protect our Earth.
  • prayasā yāmi ratnam: This translates to "I strive for the jewel (or treasure)." It signifies the pursuit of valuable goals and aspirations in life. This can be seen in individuals who work diligently towards their dreams, whether in their careers, personal development, or community service.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of truth, prosperity, respect for nature, and the pursuit of valuable goals. Its significance lies in the reminder to uphold integrity, nurture relationships, and honor the Earth while striving for personal and communal growth. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire individuals to lead lives rooted in honesty, respect, and a commitment to the greater good, ultimately contributing to a harmonious and prosperous society.
