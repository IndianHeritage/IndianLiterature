धिभिर हिन्वन्ति वाजिनं वने क्रीळन्तम् अत्यविम् |अभि त्रिपृष्टम् मतयः सम अस्वरन् ||
dhībhir hinvanti vājinaṁ vane krīḻantam atyavim | abhi tripṛṣṭham matayaḥ sam asvaran ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • dhībhir: by the intellects or minds
    • hinvanti: they inspire or lead
    • vājinaṁ: the one who possesses strength or vigor
    • vane: in the forest or wilderness
    • krīḻantam: playing or engaging
    • atyavim: beyond or transcending
    • abhi: towards or in the direction of
    • tripṛṣṭham: threefold or triple
    • matayaḥ: thoughts or ideas
    • sam: together or in harmony
    • asvaran: they sound or resonate
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the power of collective intellect and thought.
    • The phrase "dhībhir hinvanti" suggests that when minds come together, they can inspire and lead.
    • The term "vājinaṁ" refers to strength, indicating that this collective thought can empower individuals.
    • The imagery of "vane krīḻantam" evokes a sense of freedom and playfulness, suggesting that true strength is found in a harmonious and joyful engagement with life.
    • "atyavim" implies transcending limitations, indicating that through collective thought, one can achieve greater heights.
    • The mention of "tripṛṣṭham matayaḥ" emphasizes the importance of diverse thoughts coming together, creating a rich tapestry of ideas.
    • Finally, "sam asvaran" suggests that when these thoughts resonate together, they create a powerful and harmonious sound, akin to a symphony.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a team setting, when individuals share their ideas and collaborate, they can create innovative solutions that none could achieve alone.
    • In community gatherings, diverse perspectives can lead to a more profound understanding of issues, fostering unity and strength.
    • In educational settings, group discussions often lead to deeper insights and learning, as students build on each other's thoughts.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of collective intelligence and the power of collaboration.
    • It teaches us that by coming together and sharing our thoughts, we can transcend individual limitations and achieve greater goals.
    • In real life, this principle is applicable in various scenarios, from workplaces to communities, emphasizing the importance of unity and shared purpose.
