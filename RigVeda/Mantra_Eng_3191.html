यस् त्वां अग्न इन्दते यतस्रुक त्रिस् ते अन्नं कृतवत् सस्मिन्न अहन् |स सु द्युम्नैर अभ्य अस्तु प्रसक्षत तव क्रत्वा जातवेदश् चिकीत्वान् ||
yas tvām agna inadhate yatasruk tris te annaṁ kṛṇavat sasminn ahan | sa su dyumnair abhy astu prasakṣat tava kratvā jātavedaś cikitvān ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yas: who
    • tvām: you
    • agna: O Agni (the fire god)
    • inadhate: who is invoked
    • yata: from where
    • sruk: offerings
    • tris: three
    • te: your
    • annaṁ: food
    • kṛṇavat: who provides
    • sasminn: in that
    • ahan: day
    • sa: he
    • su: well
    • dyumnair: with glory
    • abhy: upon
    • astu: may it be
    • prasakṣat: well established
    • tava: your
    • kratvā: power
    • jātavedaś: O Jātaveda (another name for Agni)
    • cikitvān: knowing
  • Meaning:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Agni, the fire god, who is considered a mediator between humans and the divine.
    • The speaker invokes Agni, asking for his blessings and the provision of food and sustenance.
    • It emphasizes the importance of Agni in rituals and daily life, as he is the one who brings offerings to the gods.
    • The mantra also highlights the glory and power of Agni, wishing for his presence to be well established in the lives of the devotees.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In Hindu rituals, Agni is often invoked during ceremonies such as weddings and homas (fire sacrifices), symbolizing purification and the presence of the divine.
    • In daily life, the act of lighting a lamp or a fire can be seen as a way to invite positivity and prosperity into one’s home, reflecting the essence of this mantra.
    • Communal gatherings often include sharing food, which can be viewed as a modern-day offering to Agni, as it fosters unity and sustenance among people.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the vital role of Agni in both spiritual and material aspects of life.
    • It teaches us to recognize the importance of gratitude for the sustenance we receive and the divine forces that support our existence.
    • In a broader sense, it encourages us to cultivate a sense of community and share our resources, reflecting the interconnectedness of all beings.
