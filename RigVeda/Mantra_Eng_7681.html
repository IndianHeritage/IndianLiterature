स दृषे चिद अभि तृणत्ति वाजम् अरवतā स धत्ते अक्षिति श्रवः |त्वे देवत्रा सदा पुरूवसो विश्वा वामानि धीमहि ||
sa dṛḻhe cid abhi tṛṇatti vājam arvatā sa dhatte akṣiti śravaḥ | tve devatrā sadā purūvaso viśvā vāmāni dhīmahi ||
  • sa dṛḻhe cid abhi tṛṇatti vājam arvatā: This phrase can be interpreted as "He who firmly grasps the essence of the truth, who brings forth the strength of the mighty." Here, "dṛḻhe" signifies firmness or stability, while "tṛṇatti" refers to the act of bringing forth or producing. In real life, this can be likened to a leader who stands firm in their principles and inspires others to harness their strengths for a common goal.
  • sa dhatte akṣiti śravaḥ: This translates to "He who bears the glory of the strong." "Akṣiti" refers to strength or power, and "śravaḥ" means glory or fame. This can be seen in individuals or organizations that uphold their values and ethics, gaining respect and recognition in society. For example, a company that prioritizes sustainability may gain a reputation for being a leader in environmental responsibility.
  • tve devatrā sadā purūvaso: This means "To you, O divine one, we always offer our devotion." "Devatrā" refers to the divine or the gods, and "purūvaso" signifies the many forms or manifestations. This highlights the importance of devotion and respect towards higher powers or principles in life. In practice, this can be observed in various cultures where people express gratitude and reverence through rituals, prayers, or acts of kindness.
  • viśvā vāmāni dhīmahi: This translates to "We contemplate all that is auspicious." "Viśvā" means all or everything, and "vāmāni" refers to auspicious or beneficial things. This encourages a mindset of positivity and the pursuit of good in all aspects of life. For instance, in personal development, focusing on positive affirmations and surrounding oneself with uplifting influences can lead to a more fulfilling life.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of strength, devotion, and the pursuit of auspiciousness in life. It teaches us to be firm in our beliefs, to recognize and honor the divine, and to strive for positivity in our actions. In real-life scenarios, these principles can guide individuals and communities towards achieving their goals, fostering respect, and creating a harmonious environment. By embodying these values, we can contribute to a more compassionate and resilient society.
