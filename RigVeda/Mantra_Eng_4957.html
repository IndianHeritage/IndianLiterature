रायो धारास्य आगृह्णे वसो राशिर अजाश्व |धीवतो-धीवतः सखा ||
rāyo dhārāsy āghṛṇe vaso rāśir ajāśva | dhīvato-dhīvataḥ sakhā ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • rāyo: of wealth or prosperity
    • dhārāsy: of the flow or stream
    • āghṛṇe: to the one who is worthy or deserving
    • vaso: of the dwelling or home
    • rāśir: abundance or collection
    • ajāśva: unimpeded or without obstacles
    • dhīvato: of the wise or intelligent
    • dhīvataḥ: of those who think or contemplate
    • sakhā: friend or companion
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the flow of wealth and prosperity that is directed towards those who are deserving.
    • It emphasizes the importance of a supportive environment, symbolized by the home, where abundance can thrive.
    • The mantra also highlights the value of wisdom and companionship, suggesting that true prosperity comes from thoughtful collaboration and friendship.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be applied to partnerships where mutual respect and shared goals lead to financial success.
    • In personal life, it can reflect the importance of nurturing relationships that foster growth and support, such as family and close friends.
    • Communities that work together towards common objectives, such as local charities or cooperative businesses, exemplify the principles of this mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its holistic approach to prosperity, which is not merely about material wealth but also about the quality of relationships and the wisdom of individuals.
    • It teaches that true abundance is achieved through deservingness, collaboration, and a supportive environment.
    • In real life, applying the principles of this mantra can lead to a more fulfilling and prosperous existence, both individually and collectively.
