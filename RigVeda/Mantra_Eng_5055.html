त इद उग्राः शवसां धृष्णुसेणां उभे युजन्त रोदसी सुमेके | अध स्मैषु रोदसी स्वशोचिर आमवत्सु तस्थौ न रोकाḥ ||
ta id ugrāḥ śavasā dhṛṣṇuṣeṇā ubhe yujanta rodasī sumeke | adha smaiṣu rodasī svaśocir āmavatsu tasthau na rokaḥ ||
  • ta id ugrāḥ: This phrase can be translated as "these powerful ones." The term "ugra" signifies strength and intensity. In real life, this can refer to individuals or forces that exhibit great power, such as leaders, warriors, or natural phenomena like storms.
  • śavasā dhṛṣṇuṣeṇā: This translates to "with the strength of the mighty." Here, "śava" refers to strength or vigor, while "dhṛṣṇu" implies courage or boldness. This can be seen in real-life scenarios where people face challenges with bravery, such as athletes competing in high-stakes events or activists standing up for their beliefs.
  • ubhe yujanta rodasī sumeke: This means "both of them unite in the sky." "Rodasī" refers to the heavens or the sky, and "yujanta" indicates a coming together or uniting. This can be likened to the collaboration of different forces or entities, such as nations working together for peace or scientists collaborating on groundbreaking research.
  • adha smaiṣu rodasī svaśocir: This translates to "beneath them, the heavens shine brightly." The word "adha" means beneath, and "svaśocir" implies a radiant or shining quality. This can be interpreted as the idea that beneath powerful forces, there is a sense of illumination or clarity, much like how a strong leader can inspire hope and positivity in their community.
  • āmavatsu tasthau na rokaḥ: This means "in the midst of the powerful, there is no hindrance." "Āmavatsu" refers to the powerful or mighty, and "na rokaḥ" indicates the absence of obstacles. This can be related to situations where determination and strength lead to overcoming challenges, such as a team achieving success against all odds or an individual breaking through personal barriers to achieve their goals.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the themes of strength, unity, and the overcoming of obstacles. It signifies the importance of powerful forces coming together to create positive outcomes, whether in nature, society, or personal endeavors. In real life, this can be applicable in various scenarios, such as teamwork in professional settings, community efforts for social change, or personal growth through resilience and courage. The mantra serves as a reminder that when powerful entities unite and face challenges with strength and bravery, they can illuminate the path forward and achieve greatness.
