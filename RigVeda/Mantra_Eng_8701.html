परीतो षिञ्चत सुतं सोमो य उत्तमं हविः |दधन्वां यो नार्यो अप्स्व अन्तर आ सुषाव सोमम अद्रिभिः ||
parīto ṣiñcatā sutaṁ somo ya uttamaṁ haviḥ | dadhanvām̐ yo naryo apsv a1ntar ā suṣāva somam adribhiḥ ||
  • Mantra: "parīto ṣiñcatā sutaṁ somo ya uttamaṁ haviḥ | dadhanvām̐ yo naryo apsv a1ntar ā suṣāva somam adribhiḥ ||"
  • Translation: "May the Soma, who is the best of offerings, pour forth the excellent essence. He who is the giver of strength, may he bring forth the Soma from the mountains."
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • parīto: surrounded, encircled
    • ṣiñcatā: pouring, sprinkling
    • sutaṁ: essence, juice (often referring to the juice of the Soma plant)
    • somo: Soma, a sacred plant and drink in Vedic tradition
    • ya: who
    • uttamaṁ: the best, excellent
    • haviḥ: offering, sacrifice
    • dadhanvām̐: who gives, bestows
    • yo: who
    • naryo: men, people
    • apsv: in the waters
    • antar: within
    • ā: from
    • suṣāva: may he bring forth, may he produce
    • somam: Soma
    • adribhiḥ: from the mountains, rocks
  • Context and Significance:
    • This mantra is a prayer for the divine essence of Soma, which is considered a source of strength and vitality.
    • Soma is not just a physical drink but symbolizes the spiritual and cosmic energy that nourishes life.
    • The act of pouring or sprinkling Soma represents the sharing of this divine energy with the community, promoting unity and strength among people.
  • Real-Life Application:
    • In modern life, this mantra can be seen as a call for the sharing of resources and support within communities. Just as Soma nourishes, individuals can contribute to the well-being of others.
    • For example, in a workplace, fostering a culture of collaboration and support can lead to a more productive and harmonious environment.
    • In personal relationships, the essence of this mantra encourages individuals to uplift and empower one another, creating a strong support system.
  • Conclusion:
    • The mantra emphasizes the importance of sharing divine energy and resources for the betterment of society.
    • It serves as a reminder that by nurturing one another, we can create a stronger, more unified community.
    • In essence, it calls for the recognition of the interconnectedness of all beings and the importance of collective strength.
