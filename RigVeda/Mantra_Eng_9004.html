युजानो अश्वा वातस्य धुनि देवो देवस्य वज्रिवः |स्यन्ता पथा विरुक्मता सृजानः स्तोष्य अध्वनाḥ ||
yujāno aśvā vātasya dhunī devo devasya vajrivaḥ | syantā pathā virukmatā sṛjānaḥ stoṣy adhvanaḥ ||
  • yujāno aśvā vātasya dhunī: This phrase can be translated as "the one who is united with the horses of the wind." Here, "yujāno" refers to the act of uniting or joining, while "aśvā" means horses, and "vātasya" refers to the wind. In a real-life context, this can symbolize the importance of harmony and collaboration in achieving goals, much like a team working together to reach a common objective.
  • devo devasya vajrivaḥ: This translates to "the divine one, the powerful one of the gods." "Devo" refers to the divine or godly aspect, while "vajrivaḥ" signifies strength or power. This can be related to the idea of inner strength and resilience that individuals draw upon in challenging situations, akin to how athletes prepare mentally and physically to overcome obstacles.
  • syantā pathā virukmatā: This means "those who are on the path of prosperity." "Syantā" refers to those who are on a journey, "pathā" means path, and "virukmatā" signifies flourishing or thriving. In everyday life, this can be seen in the pursuit of education and career development, where individuals strive to enhance their skills and knowledge to achieve success.
  • sṛjānaḥ stoṣy adhvanaḥ: This translates to "creating and praising the path." "Sṛjānaḥ" means to create, "stoṣy" refers to praise, and "adhvanaḥ" signifies the path or journey. This can be exemplified in the context of innovation and creativity in various fields, such as technology or art, where individuals or groups create new solutions and celebrate the journey of discovery.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of unity, strength, prosperity, and creativity in our lives. It teaches us that by working together harmoniously, drawing upon our inner strength, and pursuing paths of growth and innovation, we can achieve our goals and contribute positively to society. This mantra can be applied in various real-life scenarios, such as teamwork in professional settings, personal development through education, and fostering creativity in artistic endeavors. Ultimately, it serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of our efforts and the divine support that guides us on our journeys.
