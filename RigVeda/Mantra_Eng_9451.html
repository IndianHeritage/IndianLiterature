या ईशिरे भुवनस्य प्रचेतसो विश्वस्य स्थातुर जगतश्च मन्तवः |ते नः कृताद अकृताद एनसस् पर्य अद्य देवासः पिपृताः स्वस्तये ||
ya īśire bhuvanasya pracetaso viśvasya sthātur jagataś ca mantavaḥ | te naḥ kṛtād akṛtād enasas pary adyā devāsaḥ pipṛtā svastaye ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ya: who
    • īśire: are the rulers
    • bhuvanasya: of the world
    • pracetaso: of the wise
    • viśvasya: of all
    • sthātur: of the stable
    • jagatā: of the universe
    • ca: and
    • mantavaḥ: who think
    • te: may you
    • naḥ: for us
    • kṛtād: from the good
    • akṛtād: from the bad
    • enasas: of our enemies
    • pary: all around
    • adyā: today
    • devāsaḥ: O gods
    • pipṛtā: may you grant
    • svastaye: for our well-being
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra addresses the divine forces that govern the universe, acknowledging their wisdom and stability.
    • It seeks blessings from these divine entities to protect us from negativity and harm, both from within and outside.
    • The phrase "may you grant for our well-being" emphasizes the importance of divine support in achieving a harmonious life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In times of crisis, such as natural disasters or personal challenges, people often turn to prayer or meditation, invoking divine protection and guidance.
    • In a corporate setting, leaders may seek the wisdom of experienced mentors (akin to divine forces) to navigate complex decisions, ensuring the stability and success of their ventures.
    • Communities often come together in rituals or gatherings to seek blessings for peace and prosperity, reflecting the collective desire for well-being.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of divine guidance and the importance of seeking protection from negative influences.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal struggles to communal challenges, reminding us of the interconnectedness of wisdom, stability, and well-being.
    • Ultimately, it encourages individuals and communities to cultivate a sense of harmony and seek support from higher powers in their journey through life.
