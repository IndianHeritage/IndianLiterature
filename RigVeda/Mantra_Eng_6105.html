आ नो गोमन्तम अश्विनां सुवीरं सुरथम् रयिम् | वोळ्हम् अश्वावतीर इषः ||
ā no gomantam aśvinā suvīraṁ surathaṁ rayim | voḻham aśvāvatīr iṣaḥ ||
  • Mantra: "ā no gomantam aśvinā suvīraṁ surathaṁ rayim | voḻham aśvāvatīr iṣaḥ ||"
  • Translation: "O Ashvins, may we attain the wealth of the one who possesses cattle, the strong and valiant one, and may we be blessed with abundant prosperity and swift horses."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • ā: A prefix meaning "towards" or "to."
    • no: "our" or "us," indicating a collective desire.
    • gomantam: "one who possesses cattle," symbolizing wealth and prosperity.
    • aśvinā: Referring to the Ashvins, the twin deities associated with health, medicine, and prosperity.
    • suvīraṁ: "the strong and valiant one," representing strength and courage.
    • surathaṁ: "the one who is well-equipped," indicating readiness and capability.
    • rayim: "prosperity" or "wealth," a key aspect of well-being.
    • voḻham: "may we attain" or "may we achieve," a wish for success.
    • aśvāvatīr: "swift horses," symbolizing speed, agility, and progress.
    • iṣaḥ: "blessings" or "powers," indicating divine favor.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In agriculture, possessing cattle is often a sign of wealth and stability. Farmers who have healthy livestock can ensure better yields and financial security.
    • In a corporate setting, a strong and valiant leader can inspire their team to achieve greater heights, much like the Ashvins inspire health and prosperity.
    • In sports, having a well-equipped team with swift players can lead to success in competitions, reflecting the mantra's emphasis on readiness and speed.
  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking divine blessings for prosperity, strength, and readiness in various aspects of life. It serves as a reminder that success is not just about individual effort but also about the collective strength and support from divine forces. In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as in business, personal growth, and community development, where the pursuit of wealth, health, and capability is essential for overall well-being and progress.
