सना दक्षम् उता क्रतुम अप सोम मृधो जहि | अथा नो वस्यसस कृधि ||
sanā dakṣam uta kratum apa soma mṛdho jahi | athā no vasyasas kṛdhi ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sanā: "from the past" or "eternal"
    • dakṣam: "skilled" or "capable"
    • uta: "and"
    • kratum: "intention" or "will"
    • apa: "away" or "remove"
    • soma: "the divine nectar" or "soma drink"
    • mṛdho: "from the earth" or "of the earth"
    • jahi: "overcome" or "destroy"
    • athā: "then" or "therefore"
    • no: "our"
    • vasyasas: "of the controlled" or "of the subdued"
    • kṛdhi: "create" or "make"
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the divine to help us overcome obstacles and challenges.
    • The phrase "sanā dakṣam" suggests a call for eternal skill and capability, emphasizing the importance of wisdom and expertise in our endeavors.
    • "Kratu" refers to our intentions and willpower, highlighting the need for focused and determined efforts.
    • The request to "apa soma mṛdho jahi" indicates a desire to remove earthly limitations and distractions that hinder our progress.
    • Finally, "athā no vasyasas kṛdhi" is a plea for the divine to create a state of control and harmony in our lives.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, this mantra can be applied when facing challenges in a project. By invoking the qualities of skill and intention, one can focus on overcoming obstacles and achieving success.
    • In personal life, when dealing with stress or anxiety, the mantra serves as a reminder to seek inner strength and clarity, allowing one to navigate through difficulties with a calm mind.
    • In educational pursuits, students can use this mantra to cultivate a mindset of determination and focus, helping them to excel in their studies and overcome academic challenges.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of skill, intention, and the ability to overcome earthly challenges.
    • It serves as a powerful reminder that with focused effort and divine support, we can create a harmonious and controlled environment in our lives.
    • By applying the teachings of this mantra, individuals can enhance their personal and professional lives, fostering resilience and success in various endeavors.
