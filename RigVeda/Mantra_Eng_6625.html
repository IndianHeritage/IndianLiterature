तव वायव ऋतस्पते तवष्टुर जामातर अद्भुत |अवांस्य आ वृणीमहे ||
tava vāyav ṛtaspate tvaṣṭur jāmātar adbhuta | avāṁsy ā vṛṇīmahe ||
  • tava vāyav ṛtaspate: This phrase can be translated as "O Wind, Lord of Truth." Here, "vāyav" refers to the wind, which is often associated with life and movement, while "ṛtaspate" signifies the lord of truth or order. In real life, this can be likened to the importance of honesty and integrity in our actions and communications.
  • tvaṣṭur jāmātar adbhuta: This translates to "O Creator, the Wonderful One." "Tvaṣṭur" refers to the creator or the divine craftsman, and "adbhuta" means wonderful or extraordinary. This can be related to the marvels of nature and the universe, reminding us to appreciate the beauty and complexity of creation, such as the intricate patterns in nature or the vastness of the cosmos.
  • avāṁsy ā vṛṇīmahe: This means "We choose your blessings." "Avāṁsy" refers to blessings or boons, and "vṛṇīmahe" means to choose or to seek. This emphasizes the human desire for divine guidance and support in our endeavors. In practical terms, it can be seen in how people often seek blessings before starting new ventures, such as a business or a journey, reflecting a universal hope for success and protection.

  • Significance of the Mantra: This mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking truth, recognizing the wonders of creation, and the desire for divine blessings. It serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of life, the importance of integrity, and the appreciation of the world around us.
  • Real-Life Application: In daily life, this mantra can inspire individuals to act with honesty and integrity, to marvel at the beauty of nature, and to seek guidance in their personal and professional lives. For instance, before making significant decisions, one might reflect on the values of truth and creativity, ensuring that their choices align with their higher purpose.
