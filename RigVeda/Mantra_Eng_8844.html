सिद्ध्रां अग्नि धियोऽस्मे सन्तुत्रीर यं त्रायसे दम आ नित्यहोताः |ऋतावा स रोहिदश्वः पुरुक्शुर द्युभिर अस्मा अहभिर वामम अस्तु ||
sidhrā agne dhiyo asme sanutrīr yaṁ trāyase dama ā nityahotā | ṛtāvā sa rohidaśvaḥ purukṣur dyubhir asmā ahabhir vāmam astu ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sidhrā: those who are accomplished or successful
    • agne: O Agni (the fire god)
    • dhiyo: intellects or thoughts
    • asme: for us
    • sanutrīr: may you be favorable
    • yaṁ: whom
    • trāyase: you protect or save
    • dama: self-control or restraint
    • ā: from
    • nityahotā: the eternal priest or the one who offers sacrifices
    • ṛtāvā: the one who is truthful or righteous
    • sa: he
    • rohidaśvaḥ: the one with red horses (symbolizing strength)
    • purukṣur: the one who is powerful
    • dyubhir: with the heavens
    • asmā: from us
    • ahabhir: with the day
    • vāmam: may it be good or favorable
    • astu: let it be
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Agni, the fire god, who is often seen as a mediator between humans and the divine.
    • It emphasizes the importance of intellect and self-control, suggesting that these qualities are essential for success and protection.
    • The phrase "may you be favorable" indicates a desire for divine blessings in our endeavors.
    • The reference to "nityahotā" highlights the significance of continuous devotion and righteousness in our actions.
    • The imagery of "red horses" symbolizes strength and vitality, suggesting that one should harness these qualities in their life.
    • The mantra concludes with a wish for goodness and favor, reinforcing the idea that positive outcomes are desired in our daily lives.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, individuals can embody the qualities of self-control and intellect by making thoughtful decisions and maintaining composure under pressure.
    • In personal relationships, being righteous and truthful can lead to stronger bonds and mutual respect.
    • During challenging times, invoking the qualities of Agni can serve as a reminder to stay resilient and focused on one's goals.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of intellect, self-control, and righteousness in our lives.
    • It encourages individuals to seek divine guidance and favor in their endeavors, promoting a balanced and successful life.
    • By embodying these qualities, one can navigate the complexities of life with strength and positivity, ultimately leading to personal and communal growth.
