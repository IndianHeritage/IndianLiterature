ऐभिर दद वृष्ण्या पौंस्यानी येभिर औक्षद वृत्रहत्याय वज्री |ये कर्मणः क्रियमाणस्य मह्न ऋतेकर्मम उदजायन्त देवाः ||
aibhir dade vṛṣṇyā pauṁsyāni yebhir aukṣad vṛtrahatyāya vajrī | ye karmaṇaḥ kriyamāṇasya mahna ṛtekarmam udajāyanta devāḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • aibhir: with the help of
    • dade: give
    • vṛṣṇyā: to the Vṛṣṇis (a tribe or clan)
    • pauṁsyāni: the powers of manhood
    • yebhir: by which
    • aukṣad: the strength or vigor
    • vṛtrahatyāya: for the killing of Vṛtra (a demon representing drought)
    • vajrī: the one who wields the thunderbolt (Indra)
    • ye: those who
    • karmaṇaḥ: of actions
    • kriyamāṇasya: being performed
    • mahna: of great power
    • ṛtekarmam: the righteous action
    • udajāyanta: have arisen
    • devāḥ: the gods
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the divine powers and the strength that comes from righteous actions.
    • The Vṛṣṇis, a significant tribe in Vedic literature, are invoked to receive strength and vigor.
    • The reference to Vṛtra symbolizes obstacles or challenges, particularly those that hinder growth and prosperity.
    • Indra, the god of thunder and rain, is depicted as the one who helps in overcoming these challenges.
    • The mantra emphasizes the importance of performing righteous actions, which lead to divine support and empowerment.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, a team working together to overcome a significant challenge can be seen as embodying the spirit of this mantra. Their collective efforts and righteous actions lead to success.
    • In personal life, individuals facing hardships, such as financial difficulties, can find strength in community support and righteous living, akin to the Vṛṣṇis receiving divine help.
    • Environmental activism can also be related to this mantra, where individuals and groups work towards overcoming the 'Vṛtra' of pollution and climate change through righteous actions.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of collective strength and righteous actions in overcoming obstacles.
    • It teaches that divine support is available to those who act with integrity and purpose.
    • In real life, it encourages individuals and communities to unite in their efforts to face challenges, reminding them that their actions can invoke greater powers for good.
