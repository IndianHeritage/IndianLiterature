यत् स्थो दीर्घप्रसद्मनि यद् वादो रोचने दिवः |यद् वा समुद्रे अध्याकृत गृहेऽता आयातम अश्विनाः ||
yat stho dīrghaprasadmani yad vādo rocane divaḥ | yad vā samudre adhy ākṛte gṛhe 'ta ā yātam aśvinā ||
  • yat stho dīrghaprasadmani: This phrase can be translated as "that which is stable and long-lasting." In real life, this can refer to relationships, values, or principles that stand the test of time. For example, a strong friendship that endures through challenges exemplifies this stability.
  • yad vādo rocane divaḥ: This translates to "that which is pleasing in the heavens." This can be interpreted as aspirations or goals that are aligned with higher ideals or virtues. For instance, pursuing a career that contributes positively to society can be seen as a goal that is pleasing to the heavens.
  • yad vā samudre adhy ākṛte: This means "that which is formed in the ocean." The ocean often symbolizes depth, mystery, and the subconscious. In a real-life context, this could refer to the vast potential within each individual, much like the untapped resources of the ocean. For example, a person discovering their hidden talents can be likened to uncovering treasures from the ocean.
  • gṛhe 'ta ā yātam aśvinā: This translates to "may you come to the home with the Ashvins." The Ashvins are twin deities associated with health, medicine, and well-being. This part of the mantra emphasizes the importance of health and harmony in one's home life. In practical terms, it can be seen in the efforts to create a nurturing environment for family and loved ones, ensuring that the home is a place of comfort and healing.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of stability, aspiration, potential, and well-being. Its significance lies in the reminder to cultivate enduring values, pursue noble goals, explore our inner depths, and foster a harmonious home environment. In real life, applying these principles can lead to a fulfilling and balanced life, where one not only seeks personal growth but also contributes positively to the community and nurtures relationships.
