सा आ नो योनिं सदतु प्रेष्ठो बृहस्पतिर विश्ववारो यो अस्ति |कामो रायः सुवीर्यस्य तं दात पार्षण नो अति सश्चतो अरिष्टान् ||
sa ā no yoniṁ sadatu preṣṭho bṛhaspatir viśvavāro yo asti | kāmo rāyaḥ suvīryasya taṁ dāt parṣan no ati saścato ariṣṭān ||
  • sa ā no yoniṁ sadatu: This phrase can be translated as "May the one who is the source of all creation be favorable to us." Here, "yoni" refers to the source or origin, often symbolizing the divine feminine or the creative force in the universe.
  • preṣṭho bṛhaspatir viśvavāro yo asti: This part refers to "the great sage Brihaspati, who is the protector of all." Brihaspati is often associated with wisdom, knowledge, and the guiding force in life. The term "viśvavāro" indicates a universal protector, suggesting that wisdom and guidance are available to all who seek it.
  • kāmo rāyaḥ suvīryasya: This translates to "the desires of the prosperous and the strong." It emphasizes the importance of having noble desires and aspirations that lead to prosperity and strength in life.
  • taṁ dāt parṣan no ati saścato ariṣṭān: This can be interpreted as "May that grant us protection from all adversities." The word "ariṣṭān" refers to obstacles or adversities, indicating a plea for divine protection against challenges that may arise.

  • Significance: This mantra encapsulates a prayer for guidance, prosperity, and protection. It highlights the importance of seeking wisdom and strength while also acknowledging the need for divine support in overcoming life's challenges.
  • Real-life Application: In daily life, this mantra can be invoked during times of uncertainty or when facing challenges. For instance, students may recite it before exams to seek wisdom and clarity, while entrepreneurs might use it when embarking on new ventures to attract prosperity and protection from potential failures.
  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of wisdom, strength, and divine support. By aligning our desires with noble intentions and seeking guidance, we can navigate life's complexities with greater resilience and purpose.
