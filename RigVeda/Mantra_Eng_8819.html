सा आवक्षी महि न आ च सत्त्सि दिवस्पृथिव्योऽरतिर युवत्योः |अग्निः सुतुकः सुतुकैर्भिर अश्वै रभस्वद्भिः रभस्वाम् इह गम्याः ||
sa ā vakṣi mahi na ā ca satsi divaspṛthivyor aratir yuvatyoḥ | agniḥ sutukaḥ sutukebhir aśvai rabhasvadbhī rabhasvām̐ eha gamyāḥ ||
  • sa ā vakṣi mahi na ā ca satsi divaspṛthivyor aratir yuvatyoḥ
    • sa ā vakṣi: This phrase can be interpreted as "let us speak" or "let us express." It emphasizes the importance of communication and sharing thoughts.
    • mahi na ā ca satsi: "mahi" refers to the great or vast, while "satsi" implies existence or being. This suggests that in our expressions, we should acknowledge the vastness of existence.
    • divaspṛthivyor: This term combines "divas" (heaven) and "pṛthivī" (earth), indicating a connection between the celestial and terrestrial realms.
    • aratir yuvatyoḥ: "aratir" means "without obstruction" or "freedom," and "yuvatyoḥ" refers to youth or vitality. This implies that our expressions should be free and vibrant, much like the energy of youth.
  • agniḥ sutukaḥ sutukebhir aśvai rabhasvadbhī rabhasvām̐ eha gamyāḥ
    • agniḥ: Referring to fire, Agni symbolizes transformation, energy, and the divine spark within us.
    • sutukaḥ sutukebhir: "sutukaḥ" means "well-formed" or "noble," while "sutukebhir" suggests being surrounded by good or virtuous things. This indicates that our inner fire should be nurtured by positive influences.
    • aśvai: This term relates to horses, often symbolizing strength and speed. It suggests that our aspirations should be powerful and swift.
    • rabhasvadbhī rabhasvām̐: "rabhasvadbhī" implies being full of energy or vigor, while "rabhasvām̐" suggests a commanding presence. This indicates that we should approach life with enthusiasm and authority.
    • eha gamyāḥ: This phrase means "let us go" or "let us move forward," encouraging action and progress.

Conclusion:

This mantra emphasizes the significance of communication, the connection between the celestial and earthly realms, and the importance of nurturing our inner fire with positive influences. In real life, it encourages individuals to express themselves freely and vibrantly, to pursue their aspirations with strength and enthusiasm, and to move forward with purpose. This can be applied in various scenarios, such as in personal relationships, professional environments, and creative endeavors, where open communication and a positive mindset are essential for growth and success.
