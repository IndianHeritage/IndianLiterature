मां चत्वारा आशवः शविष्ठस्य द्रवितनवः | सुरथासो अभि प्रयो वक्षन वयो न तुग्र्यम ||
māṁ catvāra āśavaḥ śaviṣṭhasya dravitnavaḥ | surathāso abhi prayo vakṣan vayo na tugryam ||
  • māṁ - This word translates to "me" or "to me," indicating a personal connection or invocation.
  • catvāra - Meaning "four," this could symbolize completeness or a group of four elements or aspects.
  • āśavaḥ - This term refers to "vital energies" or "life forces," suggesting a focus on the essence of life.
  • śaviṣṭhasya - Translated as "of the most powerful," it emphasizes strength and potency.
  • dravitnavaḥ - This can be interpreted as "new wealth" or "prosperity," indicating a desire for abundance.
  • surathāso - Meaning "those who are well-armed" or "those who are strong," it suggests readiness and capability.
  • abhi - This word means "towards" or "in the direction of," indicating movement or intention.
  • prayo - Translated as "effort" or "endeavor," it highlights the importance of action.
  • vakṣan - Meaning "to speak" or "to declare," it emphasizes communication and expression.
  • vayo - This term means "age" or "time," suggesting a connection to the passage of time or experience.
  • na - Meaning "not," it negates the following term.
  • tugryam - This can be interpreted as "to be hindered" or "to be obstructed," indicating a desire for freedom from obstacles.

The mantra can be understood as a call for personal empowerment and the pursuit of prosperity through strength and effort. It emphasizes the importance of vital energies and the collective strength of a group, suggesting that when individuals come together with a common purpose, they can overcome obstacles and achieve their goals.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as:

  • Teamwork: In a workplace setting, when a team of four individuals collaborates effectively, leveraging their unique strengths and energies, they can achieve remarkable results.
  • Personal Growth: On a personal level, individuals can reflect on their own strengths and the energies they possess, striving for new opportunities and prosperity in their lives.
  • Community Building: In community initiatives, gathering a group of strong, capable individuals to work towards a common goal can lead to significant positive change.

In conclusion, this mantra serves as a reminder of the power of collective strength, the importance of personal effort, and the pursuit of prosperity. It encourages individuals to harness their energies and work together to overcome challenges, ultimately leading to success and fulfillment in various aspects of life.
