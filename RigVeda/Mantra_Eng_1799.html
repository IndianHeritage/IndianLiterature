आ नो 'वोभिर मरुतो यान्त्व अच्‍छा ज्येष्‍ठेभिर वा बृहद्‍दिवैः सुमायाः |अध यद एषां नियुतः परमाः समुद्रस्य चिद् धनयन्त पारि ||
ā no 'vobhir maruto yāntv acchā jyeṣṭhebhir vā bṛhaddivaiḥ sumāyāḥ | adha yad eṣāṁ niyutaḥ paramāḥ samudrasya cid dhanayanta pāre ||
  • Mantra: "ā no 'vobhir maruto yāntv acchā jyeṣṭhebhir vā bṛhaddivaiḥ sumāyāḥ | adha yad eṣāṁ niyutaḥ paramāḥ samudrasya cid dhanayanta pāre ||"
  • Translation: "May the Maruts, the storm deities, come to us with their powerful and great forces. May they bring us wealth and prosperity from beyond the ocean."
  • Explanation:
    • Maruts: The Maruts are a group of storm deities in Vedic literature, often associated with strength, power, and the forces of nature. They symbolize the dynamic and uncontrollable aspects of the natural world.
    • Wealth and Prosperity: The mantra invokes the Maruts to bring wealth and prosperity, which can be interpreted as both material wealth and spiritual abundance. In real life, this can relate to seeking success in various endeavors, whether in business, personal growth, or community development.
    • Ocean as a Metaphor: The reference to the ocean signifies vastness and the unknown. Just as the ocean holds countless treasures, this mantra encourages us to seek opportunities that may lie beyond our immediate reach. For example, a business might look to expand internationally, tapping into new markets and resources.
  • Real-Life Application:
    • In a business context, companies often seek inspiration from natural forces to drive innovation and growth. The Maruts can symbolize the need for agility and responsiveness to market changes.
    • On a personal level, individuals can use this mantra as a reminder to embrace change and seek new opportunities, much like how one might take risks to achieve personal goals.
    • In community settings, this mantra can inspire collective efforts to harness local resources and talents, fostering a sense of unity and shared purpose.
  • Conclusion: This mantra emphasizes the importance of invoking natural forces for guidance and support in our pursuits. It serves as a reminder that wealth and prosperity are not just material but also encompass spiritual and communal growth. By aligning ourselves with the dynamic energies of the Maruts, we can navigate the challenges of life and seek out the treasures that lie beyond our immediate horizons.
