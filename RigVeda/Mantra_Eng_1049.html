वधैर दुःशंसाम् अप दूद्यो जहि दूरें वा ये अन्ति वा के चिद् अत्रिणः |अथा यज्ञाय गृणते सुगं कृध्य अग्ने सख्ये मा ऋषामाः वयं तव ||
vadhair duḥśaṁsām̐ apa dūḍhyo jahi dūre vā ye anti vā ke cid atriṇaḥ | athā yajñāya gṛṇate sugaṁ kṛdhy agne sakhye mā riṣāmā vayaṁ tava ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • vadhair: by killing or overcoming
    • duḥśaṁsām̐: those who speak ill or have bad intentions
    • apa: away or far
    • dūḍhyo: from us or from our vicinity
    • jahi: destroy or remove
    • dūre: far away
    • vā: or
    • ye: those who
    • anti: who are present
    • vā: or
    • ke cid: anyone
    • atriṇaḥ: who is an enemy or adversary
    • athā: then
    • yajñāya: for the sacrifice or offering
    • gṛṇate: they sing or chant
    • sugaṁ: good or auspicious
    • kṛdhy: may you create or make
    • agne: O Agni (the fire god)
    • sakhye: for friendship or companionship
    • mā: do not
    • riṣāmā: let us be angry or displeased
    • vayaṁ: we
    • tava: your
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer for protection against negativity and ill intentions.
    • It emphasizes the importance of distancing oneself from harmful influences and adversaries.
    • The invocation of Agni signifies the desire for purification and the creation of positive energy.
    • It reflects a communal spirit, asking for collective well-being and harmony.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace, this mantra can be applied when dealing with toxic colleagues. By focusing on positive relationships and teamwork, one can create a more harmonious environment.
    • In personal life, it can be a reminder to distance oneself from negative influences, such as harmful friendships or relationships.
    • During community gatherings or events, invoking this mantra can help foster a spirit of unity and cooperation, ensuring that everyone works towards a common goal.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for the removal of negativity and the promotion of positive energy.
    • It serves as a reminder to seek harmony and friendship, both in personal and communal settings.
    • By applying its teachings, individuals can cultivate a more positive and supportive environment, leading to overall well-being and success.
