त्वेषं रूपं कृतु उत्तरं यत्सम्पृच्छानः सदने गोभिः अद्भिः |कविः बुद्धं परि मर्मृज्यते धीः सा देवताता समितिर् बभूव ||
tveṣaṁ rūpaṁ kṛṇuta uttaraṁ yat sampṛñcānaḥ sadane gobhir adbhiḥ | kavir budhnam pari marmṛjyate dhīḥ sā devatātā samitir babhūva ||
  • tveṣaṁ rūpaṁ kṛṇuta uttaraṁ: This phrase can be translated as "Create a form for us that is superior." It signifies the desire for a higher or divine form, which can be interpreted as seeking guidance or inspiration from a higher power.
  • yat sampṛñcānaḥ sadane gobhir adbhiḥ: This translates to "As we gather in the abode with the waters." Here, "gobhir adbhiḥ" refers to the waters, which can symbolize knowledge, purity, and life. This line emphasizes the importance of community and collective effort in seeking wisdom.
  • kavir budhnam pari marmṛjyate: This means "The wise one, the poet, knows the essence." It highlights the role of wisdom and creativity in understanding deeper truths. The "kavir" or poet is often seen as a visionary who can articulate profound insights.
  • dhīḥ sā devatātā samitir babhūva: This translates to "Intellect, that divine essence, has become a gathering." This line suggests that intellect and divine inspiration come together, forming a collective understanding or enlightenment.

  • Significance: This mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking higher wisdom and understanding through collective efforts and divine inspiration. It reflects the human quest for knowledge and the role of community in achieving enlightenment.
  • Real-life Application: In modern life, this mantra can be applied in various contexts such as:
    • Education: In educational settings, students and teachers can come together to create a learning environment that fosters higher understanding and creativity.
    • Teamwork: In workplaces, collaboration among team members can lead to innovative solutions and a deeper understanding of projects.
    • Spiritual Gatherings: In spiritual or religious contexts, coming together in prayer or meditation can enhance collective consciousness and foster a sense of community.

  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a reminder of the power of collective wisdom and the pursuit of higher knowledge. It encourages individuals to seek inspiration from the divine and to work together in their quest for understanding, making it relevant in various aspects of life, from education to teamwork and spiritual practices.
