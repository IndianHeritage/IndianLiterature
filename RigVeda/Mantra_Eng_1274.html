वīḻुपत्मभिर āशुहेमभिर वā देवानां वā जूतिभिः शाशदानā |तद रासभो नासत्यā सहस्रं āजā यमस्य प्रधानें जिगाय ||
vīḻupatmabhir āśuhemabhir vā devānāṁ vā jūtibhiḥ śāśadānā | tad rāsabho nāsatyā sahasram ājā yamasya pradhane jigāya ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • vīḻupatmabhir: With the swift-moving (or powerful) ones
    • āśuhemabhir: With the quick and bright ones
    • vā: Or
    • devānāṁ: Of the gods
    • vā: Or
    • jūtibhiḥ: With the united forces
    • śāśadānā: Bestowing immortality
    • tad: That
    • rāsabho: The one who is like a horse
    • nāsatyā: Of the Asvins (the twin gods)
    • sahasram: A thousand
    • ājā: Born
    • yamasya: Of Yama (the god of death)
    • pradhane: In the principal (or chief) position
    • jigāya: Has conquered
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the powerful and swift forces, possibly referring to divine energies or deities that are capable of bestowing blessings and immortality.
    • The mention of the Asvins, who are known for their healing and protective qualities, suggests a connection to health and well-being.
    • The reference to Yama, the god of death, indicates a theme of overcoming mortality and achieving a higher state of existence.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern life, this mantra can be related to the pursuit of health and longevity. For instance, people often seek out holistic practices, such as yoga and meditation, which are believed to connect them with divine energies and promote well-being.
    • The idea of "conquering death" can be seen in advancements in medicine and technology, where scientists strive to extend life and improve the quality of life through innovations.
    • In a more metaphorical sense, the mantra can inspire individuals to overcome personal challenges and fears, much like how the Asvins are depicted as overcoming obstacles to help humanity.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of divine support in achieving health, longevity, and overcoming life's challenges.
    • Its significance lies in the reminder that we are not alone in our struggles; there are higher powers and energies that can aid us in our journey.
    • In practical terms, it encourages individuals to seek harmony with the divine, whether through spiritual practices, community support, or personal growth efforts.
