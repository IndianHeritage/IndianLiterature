आ नो अग्नि रयिम भर सत्रासाहं वरेंयम् |विश्वासु पृत्सु दुष्टारम् ||
ā no agne rayim bhara satrāsāhaṁ vareṇyam | viśvāsu pṛtsu duṣṭaram ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā: May (we) approach
    • no: our
    • agne: O Fire (Agni), the divine energy
    • rayim: wealth or prosperity
    • bhara: fill or bestow
    • satrāsāhaṁ: with strength or power
    • vareṇyam: worthy of worship or reverence
    • viśvāsu: in all realms or everywhere
    • pṛtsu: in the world or in the universe
    • duṣṭaram: the difficult or challenging
  • Overall Meaning: This mantra is a prayer to Agni, the divine fire, asking for the bestowal of wealth and prosperity with strength and reverence. It acknowledges the challenges present in the world and seeks Agni's guidance to overcome them.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be invoked by entrepreneurs seeking success and prosperity in their ventures. By recognizing the challenges of the market, they can seek the strength and wisdom to navigate through them.
    • In personal life, individuals may recite this mantra during times of hardship, asking for divine support to overcome difficulties and achieve their goals.
    • In community gatherings, this mantra can be used to inspire collective efforts towards prosperity and well-being, emphasizing the importance of unity and strength in facing challenges.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine assistance in achieving prosperity while acknowledging the challenges of life. It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal struggles to collective endeavors, reminding us of the importance of seeking strength and guidance in our pursuits. By invoking Agni, we align ourselves with the divine energy that fuels our ambitions and helps us overcome obstacles.
