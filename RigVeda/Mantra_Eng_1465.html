स नो नव्येभिर वृषकर्मन् उक्थैः पुरां दर्तः पायुभिः पाहि शग्मैः | दिवोदासेभिर इन्द्र स्तवानो वावृद्धीथा अहोभिर इव द्यौः ||
sa no navyebhir vṛṣakarmann ukthaiḥ purāṁ dartaḥ pāyubhiḥ pāhi śagmaiḥ | divodāsebhir indra stavāno vāvṛdhīthā ahobhir iva dyauḥ ||
  • sa no navyebhir vṛṣakarmann ukthaiḥ: This phrase can be translated to "May he (Indra) come to us with new and powerful deeds." Here, "sa" refers to Indra, the king of gods, and "navyebhir" signifies new or fresh. The term "vṛṣakarmann" relates to powerful actions or deeds, while "ukthaiḥ" refers to hymns or praises. This line emphasizes the importance of invoking divine energy and strength in our endeavors.
  • purāṁ dartaḥ pāyubhiḥ pāhi śagmaiḥ: This translates to "May he protect us from the past dangers and grant us strength." "Purāṁ" means past, "dartaḥ" refers to dangers or obstacles, "pāyubhiḥ" means to protect, and "śagmaiḥ" signifies strength or vigor. This line highlights the need for divine protection from past adversities while seeking strength for future challenges.
  • divodāsebhir indra stavāno: This means "Indra, who is praised by the divine seers." "Divodāsebhir" refers to the divine seers or sages, and "stavāno" means one who is praised. This line underscores the significance of reverence and acknowledgment of divine forces in our lives, as they guide and inspire us.
  • vāvṛdhīthā ahobhir iva dyauḥ: This translates to "May he grow and expand like the heavens." "Vāvṛdhīthā" means to grow or expand, "ahobhir" refers to the heavens, and "iva" means like. This line symbolizes the aspiration for growth and expansion in our lives, akin to the vastness of the sky.

Conclusion:

The mantra encapsulates a profound invocation for strength, protection, and growth. In real-life scenarios, it can be applied in various contexts such as:

  • Personal Development: Individuals can recite this mantra when embarking on new ventures or facing challenges, seeking divine guidance and strength to overcome obstacles.
  • Community and Leadership: Leaders can use this mantra to inspire their teams, fostering a sense of unity and purpose while invoking protection and strength for collective goals.
  • Spiritual Practices: In spiritual gatherings, this mantra can be chanted to create a positive atmosphere, encouraging growth and protection for all participants.

Overall, the significance of this mantra lies in its ability to connect individuals with divine energies, promoting resilience, growth, and a sense of community in the face of life's challenges.
