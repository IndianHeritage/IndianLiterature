सेनेव सृष्टामं दधात्य अस्तुर न दिद्युत त्वेषप्रतिका ||
seneva sṛṣṭāmaṁ dadhāty astur na didyut tveṣapratīkā ||
  • Mantra: "seneva sṛṣṭāmaṁ dadhāty astur na didyut tveṣapratīkā"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • seneva: like an army
    • sṛṣṭāmaṁ: created or produced
    • dadhāty: bestows or grants
    • astur: may it be (a form of blessing)
    • na: not
    • didyut: shine or illuminate
    • tveṣapratīkā: as a symbol of your presence
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra can be understood as a prayer or invocation that likens the divine presence to an army that is well-structured and powerful.
    • The phrase "seneva sṛṣṭāmaṁ" suggests that just as an army is created and organized for a purpose, so too is the divine force that governs the universe.
    • When it states "dadhāty astur," it implies that this divine force bestows blessings and support upon those who seek it.
    • The latter part, "na didyut tveṣapratīkā," indicates that this divine presence should not merely shine as a symbol but should actively engage and manifest in our lives.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, a well-organized team can be likened to an army. Just as an army works together to achieve a common goal, a team must collaborate effectively to succeed in projects.
    • In personal life, when individuals face challenges, invoking this mantra can serve as a reminder to seek support from a higher power or community, much like soldiers rely on each other in battle.
    • In education, teachers can be seen as the guiding force, akin to an army leader, who bestows knowledge and wisdom upon students, helping them to shine in their own right.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of divine support and the structured approach to achieving goals in life.
    • It serves as a reminder that we are not alone in our endeavors; we have the backing of a greater force, much like an army supports its soldiers.
    • In real-life scenarios, whether in personal challenges, professional settings, or educational pursuits, invoking this mantra can inspire individuals to seek collaboration, support, and divine guidance, ultimately leading to success and fulfillment.
