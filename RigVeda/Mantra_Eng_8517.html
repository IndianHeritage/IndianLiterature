तन् नु सत्यं पवमानस्यास्तु यत्र विश्वे कारवः संनसन्त। ज्योतिर यद अह्ने अकृणोद उ लोकं प्रावन मनुं दस्यवे कर अभीकम्॥
tan nu satyam pavamānasyāstu yatra viśve kāravaḥ saṁnasanta | jyotir yad ahne akṛṇod u lokam prāvan manuṁ dasyave kar abhīkam ||
  • tan nu satyam pavamānasyāstu: This phrase can be translated as "Let us be blessed with the truth of the purifying one." Here, "tan" refers to "let us," "satyam" means "truth," and "pavamānasyā" refers to "the purifying one." In real life, this can be understood as a call to seek and embrace truth in our actions and thoughts, akin to striving for honesty in personal and professional relationships.
  • yatra viśve kāravaḥ saṁnasanta: This translates to "where all beings are united in harmony." "Yatra" means "where," "viśve" refers to "all beings," "kāravaḥ" means "united," and "saṁnasanta" implies "in harmony." This can be related to the importance of community and collaboration in our lives, such as working together in a team to achieve common goals, whether in a workplace or a community project.
  • jyotir yad ahne akṛṇod: This means "the light that was created in the day." "Jyotir" translates to "light," "yad" means "that," "ahne" refers to "in the day," and "akṛṇod" means "was created." This can symbolize the pursuit of knowledge and enlightenment, similar to how we seek education and understanding in our daily lives to illuminate our paths.
  • u lokam prāvan manuṁ dasyave kar abhīkam: This translates to "to the world, may we offer our devotion with courage." "U lokam" means "to the world," "prāvan" refers to "may we offer," "manuṁ" means "our devotion," "dasyave" implies "with courage," and "kar abhīkam" means "to act." This can be seen in real life as a call to engage with the world around us, contributing positively and courageously, whether through activism, volunteering, or simply being a responsible citizen.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of truth, unity, enlightenment, and courageous action in our lives. It serves as a reminder to seek truth, foster harmony among all beings, pursue knowledge, and engage with the world with courage. In real-life scenarios, these principles can guide our interactions, decisions, and contributions to society, ultimately leading to a more harmonious and enlightened existence.
