आ मन्द्रं आ वरेण्यं आ विप्रम आ मनीषिणम् |पान्तं आ पुरुष्पृहम् ||
ā mandram ā vareṇyam ā vipram ā manīṣiṇam | pāntam ā puruspṛham ||
  • Word Breakdown:
    • ā mandram: "not slow" or "not dull" - This suggests a call for alertness and quickness in thought and action.
    • ā vareṇyam: "not to be rejected" or "worthy of choice" - This implies the importance of selecting the right path or decisions that are beneficial.
    • ā vipram: "not to be unwise" - This emphasizes the need for wisdom and intelligence in our actions and decisions.
    • ā manīṣiṇam: "not to be without thought" - This encourages deep contemplation and consideration before acting.
    • pāntam: "to protect" - This indicates a desire for safeguarding oneself and others.
    • ā puruspṛham: "not to be without strength" - This highlights the importance of having strength and vigor in our endeavors.
  • Meaning and Interpretation:
    • This mantra calls for a state of alertness and wisdom, urging individuals to be proactive and thoughtful in their actions.
    • It emphasizes the importance of making wise choices and protecting oneself and others from harm.
    • The mantra can be seen as a guide for personal development, encouraging individuals to cultivate strength, intelligence, and mindfulness.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, this mantra can be applied when making important business decisions. For instance, a manager must be alert and wise when choosing a strategy that affects the entire team.
    • In personal relationships, being thoughtful and protective can lead to healthier interactions. For example, being aware of a friend's struggles and offering support reflects the essence of this mantra.
    • In education, students can embody this mantra by being diligent in their studies, making wise choices about their time management, and seeking help when needed.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its holistic approach to life, advocating for alertness, wisdom, and strength.
    • It serves as a reminder to be proactive in our thoughts and actions, ensuring that we make choices that are beneficial not only to ourselves but also to those around us.
    • In various aspects of life, from personal growth to professional success, the principles outlined in this mantra can guide individuals towards a more fulfilling and responsible existence.
