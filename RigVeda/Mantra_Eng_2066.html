तं त्वा गीरभिर गिर्वणसं द्रविणस्युं द्रविणोदः |सपार्येम सपर्यवः ||
taṁ tvā gīrbhir girvaṇasaṁ draviṇasyuṁ draviṇodaḥ | saparyema saparyavaḥ ||
  • taṁ tvā - This phrase translates to "to you" or "that to you." It signifies a direction of devotion or offering towards a divine entity.
  • gīrbhir - This means "with hymns" or "with praises." It emphasizes the importance of vocalizing one's reverence and gratitude through words.
  • girvaṇasaṁ - This term refers to "the one who is worthy of praise." It highlights the qualities of the divine that make it deserving of worship.
  • draviṇasyuṁ - This can be interpreted as "wealth" or "prosperity." It suggests a connection between the divine and material abundance.
  • draviṇodaḥ - This term can mean "the giver of wealth." It reinforces the idea that the divine is a source of prosperity and sustenance.
  • saparyema - This translates to "may we worship" or "let us serve." It indicates an active participation in the act of worship.
  • saparyavaḥ - This means "those who are worthy of worship." It suggests a community or collective that engages in reverence towards the divine.

In essence, this mantra is a call to worship the divine through hymns and praises, recognizing the divine as a source of wealth and prosperity. It emphasizes the importance of vocalizing one's devotion and actively participating in worship, both individually and as a community.


Real-Life Application: In modern life, this mantra can be applied in various contexts. For instance, during a family gathering or a community event, individuals can come together to express gratitude for the blessings they have received. This could be in the form of a prayer or a moment of reflection where they acknowledge the abundance in their lives, whether it be in terms of health, relationships, or material wealth.


Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its reminder of the importance of gratitude and worship in our lives. By recognizing the divine as a source of prosperity and actively participating in acts of devotion, individuals can foster a sense of community and appreciation for the blessings they have. This practice can lead to a more fulfilling and harmonious life, both personally and collectively.
