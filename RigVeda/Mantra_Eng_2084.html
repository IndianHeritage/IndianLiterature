मन्त्र: निहोता हुतृषदने विदानस् त्वेषो दीदिवाम् असदत् सुदक्षः | अदब्धव्रतप्रमतिर्वसिष्ठः सहस्रम्भरः शुचिजिह्वो अग्निः ||
ni hotā hotṛṣadane vidānas tveṣo dīdivām̐ asadat sudakṣaḥ | adabdhavratapramatir vasiṣṭhaḥ sahasrambharaḥ śucijihvo agniḥ ||
  • ni hotā hotṛṣadane vidānas tveṣo dīdivām̐ asadat sudakṣaḥ
    • ni hotā: This term refers to the priest or the one who performs the rituals. In a broader sense, it can symbolize anyone who plays a crucial role in guiding or leading a community.
    • hotṛṣadane: This can be interpreted as the one who is knowledgeable and skilled in performing rituals. It emphasizes the importance of expertise and wisdom in any field.
    • vidānas: This word means 'knowing' or 'understanding.' It highlights the value of knowledge and awareness in making informed decisions.
    • tveṣo: This term can be understood as 'your' or 'of you,' indicating a personal connection or responsibility.
    • dīdivām̐: This word signifies 'shining' or 'radiant,' suggesting the importance of enlightenment and clarity in thought and action.
    • asadat sudakṣaḥ: This phrase can be interpreted as 'not being in the state of ignorance' and 'skilled.' It emphasizes the need for competence and the avoidance of ignorance in any endeavor.
  • adabdhavratapramatir vasiṣṭhaḥ:
    • adabdhavratapramatir: This term refers to someone who is steadfast in their vows and principles. It signifies the importance of commitment and integrity in one's actions.
    • vasiṣṭhaḥ: This word can be interpreted as 'the best' or 'the most excellent.' It highlights the aspiration to achieve excellence in one's pursuits.
  • sahasrambharaḥ śucijihvo agniḥ:
    • sahasrambharaḥ: This term means 'one who has a thousand forms' or 'multifaceted.' It symbolizes versatility and adaptability in various situations.
    • śucijihvo: This word signifies 'pure' or 'having a clean mouth,' which can be interpreted as the importance of speaking truthfully and with integrity.
    • agniḥ: This refers to 'fire,' which is a powerful symbol in many cultures, representing transformation, purification, and energy.

Conclusion:

The mantra emphasizes the significance of knowledge, expertise, commitment, and integrity in both personal and communal life. In real-life scenarios, these qualities are essential for effective leadership, ethical decision-making, and fostering a harmonious community. For instance, in a workplace, a leader who embodies these traits can inspire their team, promote a culture of learning, and navigate challenges with wisdom and integrity. The mantra serves as a reminder that true success is not just about achieving goals but doing so with a sense of responsibility and excellence.
