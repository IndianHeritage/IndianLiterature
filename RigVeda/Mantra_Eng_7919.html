प्रास्य धाराऽक्षरन् वृष्णः सुतस्यौजसा | देवाम् अनु प्रभूषतः ||
prāsya dhārā akṣaran vṛṣṇaḥ sutasyaujasā | devām̐ anu prabhūṣataḥ ||
  • Word Breakdown:
    • prāsya: This can be interpreted as "of the one who is praiseworthy" or "of the one who is worthy of reverence."
    • dhārā: This means "flow" or "stream," often referring to a continuous flow of energy or blessings.
    • akṣaran: Translates to "imperishable" or "eternal," indicating something that is beyond decay or destruction.
    • vṛṣṇaḥ: Refers to "the strong" or "the powerful," often associated with strength and valor.
    • sutasyaujasā: This can be understood as "with the strength of the offspring" or "with the vigor of the progeny."
    • devām̐: This means "gods" or "divine beings," indicating a connection to the divine or spiritual realm.
    • anu: Translates to "following" or "in accordance with," suggesting alignment or harmony.
    • prabhūṣataḥ: This means "may they shine" or "may they be exalted," indicating a desire for the divine to be honored and revered.
  • Meaning and Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks to the flow of divine energy and blessings that are eternal and powerful.
    • It emphasizes the importance of recognizing and honoring the divine forces that guide and support us.
    • The reference to strength and vigor suggests that we draw upon these divine qualities in our own lives, particularly in our endeavors and relationships.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, one might invoke this mantra to seek strength and guidance in challenging projects, recognizing the support of higher powers.
    • In personal relationships, it can serve as a reminder to honor the divine qualities in ourselves and others, fostering respect and love.
    • During times of difficulty, individuals may reflect on this mantra to find inner strength and resilience, drawing upon the eternal energy it represents.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of connecting with the divine and recognizing the flow of energy that sustains us.
    • It is applicable in various aspects of life, from personal growth to professional challenges, reminding us to seek strength and guidance from higher powers.
    • By honoring the divine and acknowledging the imperishable nature of our existence, we can cultivate a deeper sense of purpose and resilience in our lives.
