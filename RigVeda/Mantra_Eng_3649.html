मार्जाल्यो मृज्यते स्वे दामूनाः कविप्रशस्तो अतिथिः शिवो नः | सहस्रशृङ्गो वृषभस तदोजा विश्वाम्ं अग्ने सहसा प्रास्य अन्यान् ||
mārjālyo mṛjyate sve damūnāḥ kavipraśasto atithiḥ śivo naḥ | sahasraśṛṅgo vṛṣabhas tadojā viśvām̐ agne sahasā prāsy anyān ||
  • mārjālyo mṛjyate: This phrase can be interpreted as "the one who is cleansed or purified." In real life, this can relate to the importance of personal hygiene and mental clarity. Just as we cleanse our bodies, we should also strive to purify our thoughts and intentions.
  • sve damūnāḥ: This translates to "in our own abode." It emphasizes the significance of creating a harmonious environment at home. For example, a peaceful home can lead to a more productive and happy life.
  • kavipraśasto: This means "the one who is praised by the wise." It highlights the value of wisdom and knowledge. In a real-world context, this can be seen in how we seek advice from mentors or experts in various fields to make informed decisions.
  • atithiḥ śivo naḥ: This translates to "may the guest be auspicious to us." It reflects the ancient Indian tradition of hospitality. In modern life, this can be applied by treating guests with respect and kindness, fostering goodwill and positive relationships.
  • sahasraśṛṅgo vṛṣabhas: This phrase refers to "the bull with a thousand horns." Symbolically, it represents strength and abundance. In practical terms, it can relate to the idea of harnessing our inner strength to overcome challenges and achieve our goals.
  • tadojā viśvām̐ agne: This means "may that energy, O Agni, be universal." Agni, the fire god, symbolizes transformation and energy. In everyday life, this can be seen in how we channel our energy towards positive actions and endeavors, promoting growth and change.
  • sahasā prāsy anyān: This translates to "may we swiftly consume others." This can be interpreted as the desire to absorb knowledge and experiences from others quickly. In a real-life scenario, this can be applied in learning environments where collaboration and sharing of ideas lead to collective growth.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes purification, wisdom, hospitality, strength, and the transformative power of energy. Its significance lies in the holistic approach to life, encouraging individuals to create a harmonious environment, seek knowledge, and foster positive relationships. In real-life scenarios, these principles can guide us in personal development, community building, and achieving our goals, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling and balanced life.
