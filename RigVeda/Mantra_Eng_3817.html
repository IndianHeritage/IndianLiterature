तव द्युमन्तो अर्चयो ग्रावेवोच्यते बृहत् |उतो ते तान्यतुर यथा स्वानो अर्थ तमनā दिवः ||
tava dyumanto arcayo grāvevocyate bṛhat | uto te tanyatur yathā svāno arta tmanā divaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • Tava: Your
    • Dyumanto: Shining or radiant
    • Arcayo: Invoked or worshipped
    • Grāvevocyate: As if called upon in a gathering
    • Bṛhat: Great or vast
    • Uto: And
    • Te: Your
    • Tanyatur: Expanding or spreading
    • Yathā: As
    • Svāno: Like the sun
    • Arta: Truth or essence
    • Tmanā: With the mind
    • Divaḥ: Of the heavens
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the divine radiance and greatness of a higher power, often interpreted as a reference to the sun or a celestial being.
    • The phrase "tava dyumanto arcayo" suggests that the divine light is worshipped and invoked, highlighting the importance of reverence in spiritual practices.
    • "Grāvevocyate" implies that this divine presence is called upon in gatherings, indicating the communal aspect of worship and the collective seeking of truth.
    • The mention of "bṛhat" emphasizes the vastness of this divine essence, suggesting that it encompasses all aspects of existence.
    • The latter part of the mantra, "uto te tanyatur yathā svāno arta tmanā divaḥ," can be understood as a call for the divine to expand and illuminate our minds with truth, akin to how the sun spreads light across the sky.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, this mantra can be reflected in practices such as meditation or prayer, where individuals seek to connect with a higher power and invite clarity and truth into their lives.
    • In community gatherings, such as festivals or religious ceremonies, the invocation of divine presence can foster a sense of unity and shared purpose among participants.
    • The metaphor of the sun can be seen in the way people seek knowledge and enlightenment, striving to illuminate their minds and lives with wisdom and understanding.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of recognizing and invoking the divine light in our lives, encouraging both individual and collective spiritual practices.
    • Its application in real life can be seen in the pursuit of truth, the fostering of community, and the quest for enlightenment, reminding us of the interconnectedness of all beings under the vastness of the universe.
    • Ultimately, it serves as a reminder to seek the divine presence in our thoughts and actions, allowing it to guide us towards a more meaningful existence.
