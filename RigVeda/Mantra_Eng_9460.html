एवā प्लेटेः सूनुर अवीवृद्धद् वो विश्व आदित्यā अदिते मनिषी |ईशानासो नरो अमर्त्येनास्तावी जनो दिव्यो गयेन ||
evā plateḥ sūnur avīvṛdhad vo viśva ādityā adite manīṣī | īśānāso naro amartyenāstāvi jano divyo gayena ||
  • evā plateḥ: This phrase can be interpreted as "thus, from the plate" or "from the dish." In a broader sense, it signifies the source or origin of nourishment and sustenance.
  • sūnur avīvṛdhad: This translates to "the son who has been nourished." It emphasizes the importance of nurturing and growth, suggesting that a well-nurtured individual is capable of achieving great things.
  • vo viśva ādityā: This means "to you, O all-pervading Adityas." The Adityas are a group of solar deities in Vedic tradition, representing various aspects of the sun and its life-giving properties.
  • adite manīṣī: This can be understood as "the wise one, born of Aditi." Aditi is the mother of the gods and symbolizes infinity and the cosmos. This phrase highlights the connection between wisdom and divine origin.
  • īśānāso naro: This translates to "the lordly man." It refers to individuals who possess authority and leadership qualities, suggesting that true leadership comes from wisdom and divine guidance.
  • amartyenāstāvi: This means "immortal, may he be." It speaks to the aspiration for immortality, not necessarily in a physical sense, but in terms of legacy, impact, and spiritual elevation.
  • jano divyo gayena: This translates to "the divine person, through the song." It suggests that through expression, whether it be through song, poetry, or other forms of art, one can connect with the divine and elevate oneself.

Conclusion:

The mantra encapsulates the essence of nourishment, wisdom, leadership, and the pursuit of immortality through divine connection and expression. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios:

  • In parenting, it emphasizes the importance of nurturing children to help them grow into wise and capable individuals.
  • In leadership, it serves as a reminder that true authority comes from wisdom and a connection to higher ideals.
  • In creative pursuits, it highlights the power of art and expression as a means to connect with the divine and leave a lasting legacy.

Overall, this mantra encourages individuals to seek nourishment for both body and soul, to cultivate wisdom, and to express themselves in ways that resonate with the divine, ultimately leading to a meaningful and impactful life.
