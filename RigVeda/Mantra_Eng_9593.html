प्र ते 'रदद वरुणो यातवे पथः सिन्धो यद वाजाम् अभ्य अद्रवस् त्वम् |भूम्याऽधि प्रवता यासि सानुनā यद एषाम् अग्रं जगताम् इराज्यसि ||
pra te 'radad varuṇo yātave pathaḥ sindho yad vājām̐ abhy adravas tvam | bhūmyā adhi pravatā yāsi sānunā yad eṣām agraṁ jagatām irajyasi ||
  • Translation: This mantra can be translated as: "O Varuna, you who are the guardian of the paths, may you lead us to the waters, where the horses (vājām̐) run swiftly. You who dwell upon the earth, may you grant us the strength to overcome obstacles and lead us to the forefront of the world."
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • pra te: for you
    • ‘radad: to lead or guide
    • varuṇo: O Varuna, the deity of cosmic order and water
    • yātave: who leads or guides
    • pathaḥ: paths or ways
    • sindho: waters or oceans
    • yad: where
    • vājām̐: horses, symbolizing strength and speed
    • abhy: towards
    • adravas: to run or flow
    • tvam: you
    • bhūmyā: upon the earth
    • adhi: above or over
    • pravatā: who dwells or resides
    • yāsi: may you go or lead
    • sānunā: with strength or power
    • yad: where
    • eṣām: of these
    • agraṁ: forefront or best
    • jagatām: of the worlds
    • irajyasi: you may be revered or honored
  • Significance: This mantra invokes Varuna, the deity associated with cosmic order, water, and the moral law. It emphasizes the importance of guidance and strength in navigating life's paths. The reference to horses symbolizes speed and power, suggesting that with divine guidance, one can overcome challenges swiftly.
  • Real-Life Application: In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios:
    • Decision Making: When faced with important decisions, one can seek guidance from a higher power, much like asking for Varuna's assistance in choosing the right path.
    • Overcoming Challenges: In times of difficulty, invoking this mantra can serve as a reminder to seek strength and support from both within and from the universe.
    • Leadership: For leaders, this mantra can inspire them to guide their teams effectively, ensuring they lead with wisdom and strength.

  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the need for guidance and strength in our lives. By invoking Varuna, we acknowledge the importance of cosmic order and the support we can receive in our journeys. It encourages us to seek clarity and power in our actions, ultimately leading us to success and fulfillment in our endeavors.
