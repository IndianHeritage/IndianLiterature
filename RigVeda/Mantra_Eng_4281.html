अश्विनाव एहा गच्छतं नासत्यā मा वि वेनतम् |तिरश्चिद आर्यया परिवर्तिर यातम् अदाभ्यā माध्वी मम श्रुतं हवम् ||
aśvināv eha gacchataṁ nāsatyā mā vi venatam | tiraś cid aryayā pari vartir yātam adābhyā mādhvī mama śrutaṁ havam ||
  • Mantra: "aśvināv eha gacchataṁ nāsatyā mā vi venatam | tiraś cid aryayā pari vartir yātam adābhyā mādhvī mama śrutaṁ havam ||"
  • Translation: "O Ashvins, come here; do not be delayed by the untruthful. May you swiftly come to me, and may my offerings reach you, as I call upon you with my sweet words."
  • Explanation:
    • Ashvins: The Ashvins are twin deities in Vedic mythology, often associated with health, medicine, and the dawn. They symbolize the duality of life and the balance between opposites.
    • Gacchataṁ: This word means "come" or "approach." It signifies a call for divine intervention or assistance.
    • Nāsatyā: Referring to the untruthful or deceitful. This part of the mantra emphasizes the importance of truth and integrity in seeking help.
    • Tiraś cid: This phrase can be interpreted as "swiftly" or "quickly," indicating the urgency of the request for divine help.
    • Adābhyā: This term suggests offerings or sacrifices made with sincerity and devotion, highlighting the importance of intention in rituals.
    • Madhvī mama śrutaṁ havam: This translates to "my sweet words of invocation." It emphasizes the power of words and prayers in connecting with the divine.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In times of crisis, individuals often seek divine help or guidance, similar to how the mantra calls upon the Ashvins for assistance. For instance, during a medical emergency, people may pray for the swift recovery of a loved one.
    • The emphasis on truthfulness can be related to personal integrity in everyday life. For example, in business dealings, honesty fosters trust and leads to better outcomes.
    • The act of making offerings, whether in a religious context or as a gesture of goodwill, reflects the importance of intention and sincerity in human interactions.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine assistance while emphasizing the values of truth, sincerity, and the power of words. In real-life scenarios, it serves as a reminder to seek help with integrity and to approach challenges with a sincere heart. The mantra encourages individuals to maintain a connection with the divine through honest intentions and heartfelt prayers, reinforcing the belief that divine support is available to those who earnestly seek it.
