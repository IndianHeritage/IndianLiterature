महान्तो मह्ना विभ्वोऽ विभूतयो दूरदृशो ये दिव्या इव स्तृभिः |मन्द्राः सुजिह्वाः स्वरितारा आसभिः सम्मिश्ला इन्द्रे मरुतः परिष्टुभः ||
mahānto mahnā vibhvo3 vibhūtayo dūredṛśo ye divyā iva stṛbhiḥ | mandrāḥ sujihvāḥ svaritāra āsabhiḥ sammiślā indre marutaḥ pariṣṭubhaḥ ||
  • Translation: The mantra speaks of the great and powerful beings (mahnā) who are far-reaching (dūredṛśo) and divine (divyā). It highlights the importance of harmony (mandrāḥ) and the auspiciousness (sujihvāḥ) of their actions, invoking the energies of Indra and the Maruts.
  • Word Breakdown:
    • mahānto: Great or powerful.
    • mahnā: By the great ones.
    • vibhvo3: Of the vast or expansive.
    • vibhūtayo: Of the manifestations or forms.
    • dūredṛśo: Those who are far-seeing or far-reaching.
    • ye: Who.
    • divyā: Divine or celestial.
    • iva: Like or as.
    • stṛbhiḥ: With the stars or celestial bodies.
    • mandrāḥ: Harmonious or auspicious.
    • sujihvāḥ: Well-formed or auspicious.
    • svaritāra: Those who are melodious or harmonious.
    • āsabhiḥ: With strength or power.
    • sammiślā: United or combined.
    • indre: To Indra, the king of gods.
    • marutaḥ: The Maruts, storm deities.
    • pariṣṭubhaḥ: Surrounding or encompassing.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • The concept of great beings can be related to leaders or influential figures in society who inspire and guide others towards a common goal.
    • The idea of harmony and auspiciousness can be seen in community gatherings where people come together to celebrate and support one another, creating a positive environment.
    • In nature, the forces of the Maruts can be likened to the powerful storms that bring rain, essential for agriculture and sustaining life.
  • Conclusion: This mantra emphasizes the significance of unity, strength, and divine guidance in our lives. It teaches us the importance of working together harmoniously, drawing inspiration from great leaders and natural forces. In real-life scenarios, it can be applied in community building, leadership, and environmental stewardship, reminding us that we are part of a larger cosmic order that thrives on cooperation and mutual respect.
