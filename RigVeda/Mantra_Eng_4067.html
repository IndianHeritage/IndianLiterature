वायव ā याहि वीतये जुषाणो हव्यदातये |पिबा सुतस्यांधसो अभि प्रयः ||
vāyav ā yāhi vītaye juṣāṇo havyadātaye | pibā sutasyāndhaso abhi prayaḥ ||
  • vāyav: This word refers to the wind or air, symbolizing the vital force or energy that sustains life. In real life, we can relate this to the importance of fresh air and its role in our health and well-being.
  • ā yāhi: This phrase translates to "come here" or "approach," indicating a call for the wind to come closer, which can be seen as an invitation for positive energy or blessings into our lives.
  • vītaye: This term means "for the purpose of liberation" or "to free," suggesting a desire for spiritual or emotional release. In everyday life, this can be related to the need for letting go of stress or negativity to achieve peace.
  • juṣāṇo: This word means "enjoying" or "delighting in," which emphasizes the importance of finding joy in life. For example, taking time to appreciate nature or engage in hobbies can enhance our overall happiness.
  • havyadātaye: This term refers to "offering" or "sacrifice," often in the context of rituals. In modern life, this can be interpreted as the act of giving back to the community or contributing to a cause greater than oneself.
  • pibā: This word means "drink" or "consume," symbolizing the intake of knowledge, wisdom, or nourishment. In real life, this can relate to the importance of continuous learning and personal growth.
  • sutasyāndhaso: This phrase translates to "of the well-prepared offerings," indicating the significance of preparation and intention in our actions. For instance, preparing thoroughly for a presentation or project can lead to success.
  • abhi prayaḥ: This means "towards the goal" or "with purpose," highlighting the importance of direction and intention in our endeavors. In life, setting clear goals and working towards them is crucial for achievement.

Conclusion: The mantra "vāyav ā yāhi vītaye juṣāṇo havyadātaye | pibā sutasyāndhaso abhi prayaḥ ||" encapsulates a profound message about inviting positive energy, seeking liberation, and finding joy in life through intentional actions and offerings. Its significance lies in the reminder to embrace the vital forces around us, to prepare ourselves for our goals, and to engage in acts of giving and joy. In real-life scenarios, this mantra encourages us to cultivate a mindset of positivity, purpose, and community involvement, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling and harmonious existence.
