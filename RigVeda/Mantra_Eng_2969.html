विमे पुरुत्रा पतयन्ति कामाः शम्यच्छा दीदये पूर्व्याणि |समिद्धे अग्नाव ऋतमिद्वदेम महद् देवानाम् असुरत्वम् एकम् ||
vi me purutrā patayanti kāmāḥ śamy acchā dīdye pūrvyāṇi | samiddhe agnāv ṛtam id vadema mahad devānām asuratvam ekam ||
  • vi me purutrā patayanti kāmāḥ: This phrase can be translated to "May my desires be fulfilled from all directions." Here, "vi" signifies a sense of diversity or multiplicity, while "kāmāḥ" refers to desires or wishes. In real life, this can be related to the pursuit of goals in various aspects of life, such as career, relationships, and personal growth. For example, a student may wish to excel in academics, build friendships, and engage in extracurricular activities simultaneously.
  • śamy acchā dīdye pūrvyāṇi: This translates to "May the auspiciousness shine forth from the east." The word "śamy" refers to auspiciousness or good fortune, and "pūrvyāṇi" indicates the east, which is often associated with new beginnings and enlightenment. In practical terms, this can be seen in the way people often start their day with morning rituals or meditation, seeking positivity and clarity for the day ahead.
  • samiddhe agnāv ṛtam id vadema: This means "In the presence of the kindled fire, we speak the truth." Here, "samiddhe" refers to something that is kindled or ignited, often symbolizing knowledge or enlightenment. "ṛtam" signifies truth or order. This can be related to the importance of honesty and integrity in our actions and communications. For instance, in a business meeting, being truthful and transparent can lead to trust and successful collaborations.
  • mahad devānām asuratvam ekam: This translates to "May we attain the greatness of the divine, the unity of all beings." "Mahad" refers to greatness, "devānām" to the divine or gods, and "asuratvam" signifies unity or oneness. This can be applied in real life through the concept of community and collective well-being. For example, when individuals come together for a common cause, such as environmental conservation, they embody this unity and strive for a greater purpose.

  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the fulfillment of desires from various directions, the pursuit of auspiciousness, the importance of truthfulness, and the unity of all beings. In real life, it serves as a reminder to seek balance in our aspirations, to start each day with positivity, to uphold integrity in our interactions, and to work together for the greater good. This mantra can be applicable in personal development, community building, and fostering harmonious relationships.
