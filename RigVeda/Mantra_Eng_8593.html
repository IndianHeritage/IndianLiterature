एव नः सोम परिषिच्यमान आ पवस्व पूयमानः स्वस्ति |इन्द्रं आ विश बृहतā रवेण वर्धयā वाचं जनयā पुरंधिम ||
evā naḥ soma pariṣicyamāna ā pavasva pūyamānaḥ svasti | indram ā viśa bṛhatā raveṇa vardhayā vācaṁ janayā puraṁdhim ||
  • evā naḥ soma pariṣicyamāna: This phrase can be translated as "May the Soma, which is being poured upon us." Soma is a sacred drink in Vedic tradition, often associated with immortality and divine inspiration. In real life, this can symbolize the nourishment we receive from nature and the universe, which sustains our physical and spiritual well-being.
  • ā pavasva pūyamānaḥ svasti: This translates to "May it flow towards us, bringing prosperity." The word "pavasva" implies a flow or movement, suggesting that abundance and good fortune are on their way to us. In practical terms, this can relate to the idea of attracting positive energy and opportunities in our lives through our actions and intentions.
  • indram ā viśa bṛhatā: This means "Indra, may you come to us with great power." Indra is the king of the gods in Vedic mythology, often associated with strength and rain. This can be interpreted as a call for divine support and strength in overcoming challenges. In everyday life, it can be seen as seeking help from higher powers or the universe when facing difficulties.
  • raveṇa vardhayā vācaṁ janayā puraṁdhim: This translates to "With the radiance of the sun, may we increase our speech and wisdom." The sun symbolizes enlightenment and knowledge. This part of the mantra emphasizes the importance of communication and wisdom in achieving success. In real life, it can be applied to the pursuit of knowledge and the sharing of ideas, which can lead to personal and communal growth.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a deep yearning for divine blessings, prosperity, strength, and wisdom. Its significance lies in the holistic approach to life, where one seeks nourishment from nature, attracts positive energy, calls for divine support, and values the power of knowledge and communication. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire individuals to cultivate a positive mindset, seek help when needed, and continuously strive for personal growth and enlightenment. By embodying these principles, one can create a fulfilling and prosperous life.
