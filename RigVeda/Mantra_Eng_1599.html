न यं रिपवो न रिषण्यवो गर्भे सन्तं रेषणा रेषयन्ति |अन्धा अपश्यां न दभन्न अभिख्यां नित्यासा ईं प्रेतारो अरक्षन् ||
na yaṁ ripavo na riṣaṇyavo garbhe santaṁ reṣaṇā reṣayanti | andhā apaśyā na dabhann abhikhyā nityāsa īm pretāro arakṣan ||
  • na yaṁ ripavo - This phrase translates to "those who are not enemies." It emphasizes the idea of peace and harmony, suggesting that one should not harbor animosity towards others.
  • na riṣaṇyavo - This means "nor those who are in distress." It highlights the importance of compassion and understanding towards those who are suffering or in need.
  • garbhe santaṁ - This translates to "in the womb, the peaceful one." It signifies the sanctity of life and the potential that exists within every being, even before birth.
  • reṣaṇā reṣayanti - This means "they do not harm." It reinforces the idea of non-violence and the importance of protecting life and well-being.
  • andhā apaśyā - This translates to "the blind do not see." It can be interpreted as a metaphor for ignorance or lack of awareness, suggesting that those who are unaware of their surroundings may miss the truth.
  • na dabhann abhikhyā - This means "they do not harm or destroy." It emphasizes the importance of preservation and the need to avoid causing harm to others.
  • nityāsa - This translates to "eternal." It signifies the everlasting nature of certain truths and principles that govern life.
  • īm pretāro arakṣan - This means "the spirits do not protect." It suggests that there are forces beyond our understanding that may not always safeguard us, urging individuals to take responsibility for their actions.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates profound teachings about peace, compassion, and the sanctity of life. It encourages individuals to foster harmony, avoid harm, and be aware of their surroundings. In real-life scenarios, these principles can be applied in various contexts, such as conflict resolution, community service, and personal relationships. By embodying these values, individuals can contribute to a more peaceful and compassionate society, ultimately leading to a better world for all.
