मन्त्र: होतā निषत्तो मनोऽपत्यम् स चिन् न्वासाम् पतिः रयिणाम् ||
hotā niṣatto manor apatye sa cin nv āsām patī rayīṇām ||
  • hotā: This term refers to the priest or the one who invokes the deities during a ritual. In a broader sense, it symbolizes the role of a leader or guide in any community or organization.
  • niṣatto: This word means 'one who is alert' or 'vigilant'. It emphasizes the importance of being aware and attentive to one's surroundings and responsibilities.
  • manor: This translates to 'mind' or 'thought'. It signifies the power of intention and the importance of mental clarity in decision-making.
  • apatye: This term means 'offspring' or 'descendants'. It highlights the significance of family and the continuity of values and traditions through generations.
  • sa: This is a conjunction meaning 'he' or 'that'. It connects the previous concepts to the subject of the mantra.
  • cin: This word means 'to know' or 'to perceive'. It emphasizes the importance of knowledge and awareness in achieving one's goals.
  • nv: This is a particle that can mean 'indeed' or 'certainly', reinforcing the certainty of the statement being made.
  • āsām: This term means 'of us' or 'our'. It indicates a sense of belonging and community.
  • patī: This translates to 'lord' or 'master'. It signifies authority and responsibility within a community or family structure.
  • rayīṇām: This word means 'wealth' or 'prosperity'. It highlights the importance of abundance and well-being in life.

In summary, this mantra emphasizes the qualities of leadership, vigilance, mental clarity, and the importance of family and community. It suggests that a true leader is one who is aware, knowledgeable, and responsible for the well-being of their community and descendants. The mantra can be applied in real-life scenarios such as in family dynamics, workplace leadership, and community service, where the qualities of awareness, intention, and responsibility are crucial for success and harmony.


Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for individuals to embody the qualities of a vigilant leader who prioritizes the welfare of their community and future generations. In today's fast-paced world, where distractions are abundant, this mantra serves as a reminder to remain focused, responsible, and connected to our roots, ensuring that we contribute positively to society and foster a sense of belonging and prosperity.
