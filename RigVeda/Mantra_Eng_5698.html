अभि वां नूनम अश्विनाऽ सुहोतā स्तोमैः सिषक्ति नासत्यā विवक्वान् |पूर्वीभिर यातम् पथ्याभिर अर्वाक् स्वरविदा वसुमता रथेन ||
abhi vāṁ nūnam aśvinā suhotā stomaiḥ siṣakti nāsatyā vivakvān | pūrvībhir yātam pathyābhir arvāk svarvidā vasumatā rathena ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • abhi: towards, in the direction of
    • vāṁ: you (plural)
    • nūnam: certainly, indeed
    • aśvinā: O Ashvins (the twin deities)
    • suhotā: well-wisher, benefactor
    • stomaiḥ: with hymns, praises
    • siṣakti: may you be empowered
    • nāsatyā: O Nāsatyas (another name for the Ashvins)
    • vivakvān: speaking, expressing
    • pūrvībhir: by the earlier ones
    • yātam: may you go, may you proceed
    • pathyābhir: by the paths
    • arvāk: openly, clearly
    • svarvidā: knowing the truth, wise
    • vasumatā: with wealth, prosperity
    • rathena: with the chariot
  • Overall Meaning: This mantra is a prayer to the Ashvins, the twin deities of health and medicine in Vedic tradition, asking for their blessings and guidance. It emphasizes the importance of being empowered through hymns and praises, and encourages the deities to lead the way through paths of wisdom and prosperity.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a modern context, this mantra can be related to seeking guidance in difficult times, such as during a health crisis or when making important life decisions. Just as the Ashvins are invoked for their healing powers, individuals today often turn to spiritual practices or community support for strength and clarity.
    • In the workplace, this mantra can symbolize the importance of teamwork and collaboration. Just as the Ashvins are called upon to empower and guide, employees can seek collective wisdom to navigate challenges and achieve success.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine assistance and empowerment. It serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of wisdom, health, and prosperity. In real-life scenarios, invoking such blessings can inspire individuals to seek support, embrace collaboration, and pursue paths that lead to holistic well-being and success. The mantra encourages us to recognize the importance of guidance, whether from divine sources or from our community, as we navigate the complexities of life.
