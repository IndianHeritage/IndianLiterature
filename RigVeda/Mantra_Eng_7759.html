अस्मभ्यं रोदसी रयिम मध्वो वाजस्य सातये |श्रवो वसूनि सं जितम् ||
asmabhyaṁ rodasī rayim madhvo vājasya sātaye | śravo vasūni saṁ jitam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • asmabhyaṁ: for us
    • rodasī: the two worlds (heavens and earth)
    • rayim: wealth or prosperity
    • madhvo: of sweetness or delight
    • vājasya: of strength or power
    • sātaye: for the purpose of nourishment
    • śravo: fame or glory
    • vasūni: the good things or riches
    • saṁ jitam: having been conquered or attained
  • Overall Meaning:
    • This mantra invokes the blessings of the universe for prosperity and strength.
    • It emphasizes the importance of both material wealth and spiritual glory.
    • The mantra suggests that by aligning ourselves with the forces of nature and the universe, we can attain both physical and spiritual abundance.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be seen as a call for success and prosperity in ventures. For instance, entrepreneurs often seek blessings for their startups to thrive and gain recognition.
    • In personal life, individuals may recite this mantra during times of financial difficulty, seeking divine intervention for stability and growth.
    • In community settings, it can be used during festivals or gatherings to invoke collective prosperity and well-being.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its holistic approach to wealth and success, encompassing both material and spiritual dimensions.
    • It serves as a reminder that true prosperity is not just about financial gain but also about achieving fame and glory through righteous actions.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various aspects, from personal aspirations to communal goals, encouraging individuals to seek balance and harmony in their pursuits.
