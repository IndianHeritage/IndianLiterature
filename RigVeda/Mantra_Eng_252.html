आपो अद्यांन्व आचारिषं रसना सम अगस्महि |पयस्वान अग्न आ गहि तं मा सं सृजा वर्चसा ||
āpo adyānv acāriṣaṁ rasena sam agasmahi | payasvān agna ā gahi tam mā saṁ sṛja varcasā ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • āpo: Waters
    • adyānv: Today
    • acāriṣaṁ: To be invoked or called upon
    • rasena: With essence or flavor
    • sam: Together
    • agasmahi: We have come here
    • payasvān: The one who is rich in nourishment
    • agna: O Fire
    • ā gahi: Please come
    • tam: That
    • mā: Do not
    • saṁ: Together
    • sṛja: Create or produce
    • varcasā: With brilliance or radiance
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the waters and the fire, symbolizing the essential elements of life.
    • The phrase "āpo adyānv acāriṣaṁ" suggests a call to the waters to come forth today, emphasizing the importance of water in sustaining life.
    • "Rasena sam agasmahi" indicates a collective gathering, highlighting the unity and harmony among all elements.
    • The invocation of "payasvān agna" refers to the fire as a source of nourishment, representing knowledge, energy, and transformation.
    • The plea "ā gahi tam mā saṁ sṛja varcasā" expresses a desire for the fire to come and not to create anything that lacks brilliance, signifying the importance of purity and positivity in our actions.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In agriculture, the importance of water is paramount. Farmers pray for rain, recognizing that water is essential for crops to thrive, much like the invocation of waters in this mantra.
    • In a community gathering, people come together to share knowledge and resources, reflecting the unity and collective essence mentioned in the mantra.
    • In personal development, individuals seek knowledge and enlightenment (symbolized by fire) to transform their lives positively, aligning with the mantra's call for brilliance in actions.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the interconnectedness of natural elements and the importance of invoking them for sustenance and growth.
    • Its significance lies in the recognition of water and fire as vital forces in life, urging us to respect and harmonize with nature.
    • In real-life scenarios, it serves as a reminder to seek nourishment, knowledge, and positivity in our actions, fostering a balanced and harmonious existence.
