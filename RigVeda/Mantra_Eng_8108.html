जघ्निर्वृत्रम् अमित्रियं सस्निर्वाजं दिवेदिवे |गोषा उ अश्वसा असि ||
jaghnir vṛtram amitriyaṁ sasnir vājaṁ dive-dive | goṣā u aśvasā asi ||
  • Mantra: "jaghnir vṛtram amitriyaṁ sasnir vājaṁ dive-dive | goṣā u aśvasā asi ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • jaghnir: (he) killed
    • vṛtram: Vritra (a serpent or dragon, symbolizing obstacles or ignorance)
    • amitriyaṁ: the one who is not a friend (enemy)
    • sasnir: (he) who has strength
    • vājaṁ: wealth or resources
    • dive-dive: day by day
    • goṣā: (the) herds or cattle
    • u: and
    • aśvasā: (the) horses
    • asi: (you) are
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the victory over Vritra, a representation of obstacles and challenges in life.
    • The phrase "he killed Vritra" symbolizes overcoming difficulties that hinder progress.
    • "Amitriyaṁ" refers to the adversities or enemies that one faces, emphasizing the need to confront and conquer them.
    • "Day by day" suggests a continuous effort in overcoming these challenges, highlighting the importance of persistence.
    • The mention of "wealth" and "resources" indicates that overcoming obstacles leads to prosperity and abundance.
    • The reference to "herds" and "horses" symbolizes the rewards of hard work and the importance of community and support in achieving success.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, an entrepreneur may face numerous challenges (Vritra) such as competition, financial constraints, and market fluctuations. By persistently working to overcome these obstacles (jaghnir), they can achieve success and prosperity (vājaṁ).
    • In personal life, individuals may encounter adversities such as health issues or relationship problems. The mantra encourages them to confront these challenges head-on and seek support from their community (goṣā, aśvasā) to find solutions and improve their situation.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the importance of resilience and determination in the face of challenges.
    • It emphasizes that overcoming obstacles is a continuous process that leads to growth, prosperity, and the strengthening of community ties.
    • In real life, applying the principles of this mantra can inspire individuals to confront their fears and adversities, ultimately leading to personal and collective success.
