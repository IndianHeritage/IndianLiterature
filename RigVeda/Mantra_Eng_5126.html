ऋजीति पर्य वृङ्धि नोऽश्मा भवतु नस् तनूः |सोमो अधि ब्रवीतु नोऽदितिः शर्म यच्छतु ||
ṛjīte pari vṛṅdhi no 'śmā bhavatu nas tanūḥ | somo adhi bravītu no 'ditiḥ śarma yacchatu ||
  • ṛjīte: This word can be interpreted as "may it shine" or "may it be bright." It signifies clarity, purity, and enlightenment.
  • pari vṛṅdhi: This phrase means "may it surround us" or "may it encompass us." It suggests a protective and nurturing presence that envelops individuals.
  • no 'śmā: Translated as "may there be no harm" or "may we be free from obstacles." This emphasizes the desire for safety and well-being.
  • bhavatu: This word means "may it become" or "may it manifest." It indicates a wish for positive outcomes and transformations in life.
  • nas tanūḥ: This translates to "our bodies" or "our physical forms." It highlights the importance of physical health and well-being.
  • somo: Referring to "Soma," a sacred drink in Vedic tradition, symbolizing nourishment, vitality, and divine inspiration.
  • adhi bravītu: This means "may it speak to us" or "may it guide us." It suggests a call for wisdom and guidance from higher powers.
  • no 'ditiḥ: Translated as "may there be no division" or "may we be united." This emphasizes harmony and togetherness among individuals.
  • śarma: This word means "comfort" or "shelter." It signifies the need for security and peace in life.
  • yacchatu: This translates to "may it grant" or "may it bestow." It expresses a desire for blessings and positive gifts in life.

Conclusion: The mantra "ṛjīte pari vṛṅdhi no 'śmā bhavatu nas tanūḥ | somo adhi bravītu no 'ditiḥ śarma yacchatu ||" encapsulates a profound wish for clarity, protection, unity, and well-being. In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as during personal challenges, communal gatherings, or spiritual practices. For instance, individuals may recite this mantra during meditation to seek inner peace and clarity, or in group settings to foster a sense of unity and collective strength. Its significance lies in the holistic approach to life, emphasizing the interconnectedness of body, mind, and spirit, and the importance of seeking guidance and blessings for a harmonious existence.
