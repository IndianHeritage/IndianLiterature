कया शुभा सवयसः सनीलाः समान्या मरुतः सम मिमिक्षुः |कया मती कुत एतासा एते 'र्चन्ति शुष्मं वृषणो वसूया ||
kayā śubhā savayasaḥ sanīḻāḥ samānyā marutaḥ sam mimikṣuḥ | kayā matī kuta etāsa ete 'rcanti śuṣmaṁ vṛṣaṇo vasūyā ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • kayā: By what means
    • śubhā: auspicious
    • savayasaḥ: of the young
    • sanīḻāḥ: well-formed
    • samānyā: common
    • marutaḥ: of the winds
    • sam: together
    • mimikṣuḥ: they are seeking
    • matī: intellect or understanding
    • kuta: from where
    • etāsa: these
    • ete: they
    • 'rcanti: worship or praise
    • śuṣmaṁ: subtle
    • vṛṣaṇo: of the strong
    • vasūyā: of the wealth
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra reflects a quest for understanding and auspiciousness.
    • It poses questions about the source of intellect and the nature of the winds (marutaḥ), which can symbolize change and movement in life.
    • The reference to the young and well-formed suggests a focus on potential and growth.
    • In real life, this can be seen in the pursuit of knowledge and wisdom, where individuals seek to understand their environment and the forces that shape their lives.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In education, students often ask, "By what means can we achieve success?" This reflects the essence of the mantra, as they seek knowledge and understanding.
    • In personal development, individuals may reflect on their strengths and weaknesses, asking themselves how they can grow and improve, akin to the well-formed youth mentioned in the mantra.
    • In the context of teamwork, the winds can symbolize collaboration, where diverse ideas come together to create something greater.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its emphasis on the pursuit of knowledge, understanding, and the importance of collaboration.
    • It encourages individuals to seek auspicious beginnings and to understand the forces that influence their lives.
    • In practical terms, this mantra can be applied in various aspects of life, including education, personal growth, and teamwork, reminding us of the importance of seeking wisdom and working together harmoniously.
