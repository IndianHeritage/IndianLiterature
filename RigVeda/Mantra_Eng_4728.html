आयं रोचयद अरुचो रुचानोऽयं वासयद व्य ऋतेन पूर्वीः |अयम ईयत ऋतयुग्भिर अश्वैः स्वरविदा नाभिना चर्षणिप्राः ||
ayaṁ rocayad aruco rucāno3 'yaṁ vāsayad vy ṛ1tena pūrvīḥ | ayam īyata ṛtayugbhir aśvaiḥ svarvidā nābhinā carṣaṇiprāḥ ||
  • ayaṁ rocayad aruco rucāno: This phrase can be interpreted as "this one shines, the one who is radiant." The term "rocayad" refers to brightness or radiance, while "aruc" signifies beauty or attractiveness. In real life, this can be likened to the way a person's character or actions can shine brightly in a community, drawing others towards them.
  • yaṁ vāsayad vy ṛ1tena pūrvīḥ: This translates to "that which is sustained by the truth of the ancients." Here, "vāsayad" means to sustain or support, and "ṛ1tena" refers to truth or righteousness. This can be related to how traditions and values passed down through generations provide a foundation for current societies, much like how family values shape individual behavior.
  • ayam īyata ṛtayugbhir aśvaiḥ: This means "this one is guided by the righteous paths of the past." The term "īyata" indicates guidance or direction, while "ṛtayug" refers to the age of truth or righteousness. In practical terms, this can be seen in how ethical leadership in organizations often draws from historical precedents to guide decision-making.
  • svarvidā nābhinā carṣaṇiprāḥ: This can be interpreted as "the wise ones who do not stray from the path." "Svarvidā" refers to those who possess knowledge or wisdom, and "carṣaṇiprāḥ" suggests those who remain steadfast. This reflects the importance of wisdom in navigating life's challenges, akin to how mentors or guides help individuals stay true to their goals.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of radiance, truth, guidance, and wisdom in life. It teaches us that by embodying these qualities, we can create a positive impact on ourselves and those around us. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various contexts such as leadership, community building, and personal development. By adhering to the principles of truth and righteousness, we can navigate our paths effectively, ensuring that we shine brightly in our endeavors and contribute positively to society.
