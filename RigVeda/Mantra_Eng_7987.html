नू नो रयिम महाम इन्दोऽस्मभ्यं सोम विश्वतः |आ पवस्व सहस्रिणम् ||
nū no rayim mahām indo 'smabhyaṁ soma viśvataḥ | ā pavasva sahasriṇam ||
  • Mantra: "nū no rayim mahām indo 'smabhyaṁ soma viśvataḥ | ā pavasva sahasriṇam ||"
  • Translation: "O great Indra, may you bestow upon us abundant wealth from all directions; may the Soma flow forth in a thousand streams."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • nū: A particle expressing a wish or desire.
    • no: Our, indicating possession or belonging.
    • rayim: Wealth or prosperity.
    • mahām: Great or mighty.
    • indo: Referring to Indra, the king of gods and god of rain and thunderstorms.
    • 'smabhyaṁ: To us, indicating the recipients of the blessings.
    • soma: A sacred drink, often associated with immortality and divine inspiration.
    • viśvataḥ: From all directions or everywhere.
    • ā: A particle indicating a request or invocation.
    • pavasva: Flow or pour forth.
    • sahasriṇam: A thousand, indicating abundance.
  • Contextual Significance:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Indra, seeking his blessings for wealth and prosperity.
    • In ancient times, wealth was often associated with agricultural abundance, which was crucial for survival.
    • The invocation of Soma signifies the importance of spiritual nourishment and divine inspiration in achieving material success.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern contexts, this mantra can be related to seeking success in business ventures, where individuals pray for prosperity and favorable conditions.
    • During festivals or rituals, people may chant this mantra to invoke blessings for their families and communities, hoping for abundance in their lives.
    • In personal development, individuals may reflect on this mantra as a reminder to seek both material and spiritual wealth, emphasizing a balanced approach to life.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the interconnectedness of material wealth and spiritual well-being.
    • It serves as a reminder that true prosperity comes from a holistic approach, encompassing both physical abundance and spiritual fulfillment.
    • In today's fast-paced world, the essence of this mantra can inspire individuals to seek balance and gratitude in their pursuits, fostering a sense of community and shared prosperity.
