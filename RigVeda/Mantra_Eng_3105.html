मां कस्य यक्षं सदमिद्धुरो गा मां वेशस्य प्रामिनतो मापेः |मां भ्रातुर अग्ने अनृतजोर ऋणं वेर मां सख्युर दक्षं रिपोर भुजेम ||
mā kasya yakṣaṁ sadam id dhuro gā mā veśasya praminato māpeḥ | mā bhrātur agne anṛjor ṛṇaṁ ver mā sakhyur dakṣaṁ ripor bhujema ||
  • mā kasya yakṣaṁ sadam id dhuro gā mā veśasya praminato māpeḥ
    • mā kasya: "Do not let anyone's wealth or possessions be taken away." This phrase emphasizes the importance of respecting others' belongings and the sanctity of personal property.
    • yakṣaṁ: Refers to a spirit or supernatural being, often associated with wealth. In real life, this can symbolize the material wealth that people strive for.
    • sadam id dhuro gā: "Do not let the burden of wealth weigh down on us." This suggests that while wealth is important, it should not become a source of stress or burden.
    • mā veśasya praminato māpeḥ: "Do not let us be overwhelmed by the desires of the world." This highlights the need to maintain balance and not be consumed by material desires.
  • mā bhrātur agne anṛjor ṛṇaṁ ver mā sakhyur dakṣaṁ ripor bhujema
    • mā bhrātur agne: "Do not let us be harmed by our own kin." This line speaks to the importance of harmony within families and communities, urging individuals to avoid conflict and strife.
    • anṛjor ṛṇaṁ: "Do not let us fall into the debt of falsehood." This can be interpreted as a warning against dishonesty and the consequences that arise from it.
    • ver mā sakhyur: "Do not let us befriend those who wish us harm." This emphasizes the importance of choosing friends wisely and surrounding oneself with positive influences.
    • dakṣaṁ ripor bhujema: "Let us be strong against our enemies." This line encourages resilience and strength in the face of adversity.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda serves as a profound reminder of the values of respect, honesty, and strength in relationships. In real life, it is applicable in various scenarios such as family dynamics, friendships, and professional relationships. By adhering to the principles outlined in this mantra, individuals can foster a harmonious environment, avoid unnecessary conflicts, and build a supportive community. The significance lies in its timeless wisdom, urging us to navigate life with integrity and strength, ensuring that we do not let material desires or negative influences dictate our actions.
