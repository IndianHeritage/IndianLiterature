युयं राजानः कं चिच चर्षणीसहः क्षयन्तं मानवशाम् अनु |वयं ते वो वरुण मित्रार्यमन् स्यामेद ऋतस्य रथ्यः ||
yūyaṁ rājānaḥ kaṁ cic carṣaṇīsahaḥ kṣayantam mānuṣām̐ anu | vayaṁ te vo varuṇa mitrāryaman syāmed ṛtasya rathyaḥ ||
  • yūyaṁ rājānaḥ: This phrase translates to "you (plural) are kings." It signifies the inherent leadership qualities present in individuals and the potential for greatness within a community.
  • kaṁ cic carṣaṇīsahaḥ: This means "whoever is in the company of the wise." It emphasizes the importance of surrounding oneself with knowledgeable and wise individuals, as their influence can lead to better decision-making and personal growth.
  • kṣayantam mānuṣām̐ anu: This translates to "following the path of humanity." It suggests that true leadership and wisdom should be aligned with human values and ethics, promoting compassion and understanding.
  • vayaṁ te vo varuṇa: This means "we are yours, Varuna." Varuna is a deity associated with cosmic order and moral authority. This line indicates a surrender to higher principles and the acknowledgment of a greater moral framework guiding one's actions.
  • mitrāryaman: This translates to "friends and foes." It highlights the duality of relationships in life, reminding us that both allies and adversaries play a role in our journey and growth.
  • syāmed ṛtasya rathyaḥ: This means "may we be on the chariot of truth." It signifies the aspiration to live a life guided by truth and righteousness, suggesting that truth is the vehicle that leads to ultimate success and fulfillment.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda encapsulates the essence of leadership, wisdom, and moral integrity. It teaches us the importance of surrounding ourselves with wise individuals, adhering to human values, and recognizing the influence of both friends and foes in our lives. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as in leadership roles, community building, and personal development. By striving to be guided by truth and ethical principles, individuals can foster a harmonious society that values wisdom and compassion, ultimately leading to collective growth and prosperity.
