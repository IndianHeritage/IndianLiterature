इथा यथा त ऊतये सहसावन दिवे-दिवे |राय ऋताया सुक्रतो गोभिः ष्यामा सदहमादो वीरैः स्यामा सदहमादः ||
itthā yathā ta ūtaye sahasāvan dive-dive | rāya ṛtāya sukrato gobhiḥ ṣyāma sadhamādo vīraiḥ syāma sadhamādaḥ ||
  • itthā yathā ta ūtaye sahasāvan dive-dive: This phrase can be translated to "As it is, so may it be, with great speed, day by day." It emphasizes the importance of consistency and the power of intention in manifesting desires. In real life, this can be seen in the practice of setting daily goals and working towards them with determination.
  • rāya ṛtāya sukrato: This part means "for the prosperity of the righteous and the good." It highlights the significance of righteousness and ethical behavior in achieving success. For example, in a business context, companies that prioritize ethical practices often build stronger reputations and customer loyalty.
  • gobhiḥ ṣyāma sadhamādo: This translates to "may we be united with the good and the strong." It suggests the importance of community and collaboration. In everyday life, this can be reflected in teamwork, where individuals come together to achieve a common goal, such as in sports teams or project groups.
  • vīraiḥ syāma sadhamādaḥ: This means "may we be strong and united." It reinforces the idea of strength in unity. In real life, this can be observed in social movements where collective action leads to significant change, such as environmental activism or community service initiatives.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the power of intention, the importance of righteousness, and the strength found in unity. Its significance lies in its applicability to various aspects of life, from personal development to community engagement. By embodying these principles, individuals can work towards their goals while fostering a positive and ethical environment around them. This mantra serves as a reminder that our actions, guided by good intentions and collaboration, can lead to prosperity and strength in our lives and communities.
