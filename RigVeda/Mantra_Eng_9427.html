अध तवम् इन्द्र विद्ध्य अस्मान महो राये नृपतिः वज्रबाहुः |रक्षा च नो मग्नः पाहि सूरिन् अनहसस ते हरिवो अभिष्टौ ||
adha tvam indra viddhy a1smān maho rāye nṛpate vajrabāhuḥ | rakṣā ca no maghonaḥ pāhi sūrīn anehasas te harivo abhiṣṭau ||
  • Adha tvam indra viddhy a1smān: This phrase can be translated as "Know us, O Indra." Here, Indra represents the divine force or the supreme power that governs the universe. In real life, this can be likened to seeking guidance or wisdom from a higher authority or source of inspiration, such as mentors, leaders, or even nature itself.
  • maho rāye nṛpate vajrabāhuḥ: This translates to "O Lord of wealth, with arms like thunder." The term "vajrabāhuḥ" refers to someone who is strong and powerful, akin to a leader or a protector. In a contemporary context, this can be seen in leaders who possess the strength and capability to protect and provide for their people, much like a CEO who safeguards the interests of their company and employees.
  • rakṣā ca no maghonaḥ: This means "Protect us from misfortune." This line emphasizes the need for protection from negative influences or challenges. In everyday life, this can relate to seeking safety and security, whether it be through community support, legal systems, or personal relationships that provide a safety net during tough times.
  • pāhi sūrīn anehasas te harivo abhiṣṭau: This translates to "May the divine protect us and grant us blessings." The term "sūrīn" refers to the divine or heroic beings, while "abhiṣṭau" signifies blessings or auspiciousness. In practical terms, this can be understood as the importance of seeking blessings or positive energies in our endeavors, whether through rituals, positive affirmations, or community support.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a plea for divine guidance, protection, and blessings in our lives. Its significance lies in the recognition of a higher power that can aid us in overcoming challenges and achieving prosperity. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various contexts such as personal growth, leadership, community building, and even in corporate environments where the well-being of individuals and the collective is paramount. By invoking these principles, individuals and communities can foster resilience, strength, and a sense of security in their journeys.
