अस्मिन्पादे परमेतस्थिवांसमध्वस्मभिर्विश्वहादीदिवांसम् |आपो नप्त्रे घृतमन्नं वहन्तीः स्वयमत्कैः परिदीयन्ति यह्वीः ||
asmin pade parame tasthivāṁsam adhvasmabhir viśvahā dīdivāṁsam | āpo naptre ghṛtam annaṁ vahantīḥ svayam atkaiḥ pari dīyanti yahvīḥ ||
  • Mantra: "asmin pade parame tasthivāṁsam adhvasmabhir viśvahā dīdivāṁsam | āpo naptre ghṛtam annaṁ vahantīḥ svayam atkaiḥ pari dīyanti yahvīḥ ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • asmin: in this
    • pade: place or position
    • parame: supreme or highest
    • tasthivāṁsam: standing or residing
    • adhvasmabhir: by the paths or ways
    • viśvahā: all-encompassing or universal
    • dīdivāṁsam: shining or illuminating
    • āpo: waters
    • naptre: for the descendant or progeny
    • ghṛtam: clarified butter or ghee
    • annaṁ: food or sustenance
    • vahantīḥ: carrying or bringing
    • svayam: by themselves
    • atkaiḥ: with offerings or gifts
    • pari: all around or completely
    • dīyanti: they give or bestow
    • yahvīḥ: those who are worthy or deserving
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra speaks of a supreme place where all paths converge, symbolizing unity and the ultimate truth.
    • The mention of "waters" signifies the life-giving and purifying aspects of nature, which are essential for sustenance.
    • “ghṛtam annaṁ” refers to the nourishment provided by nature, emphasizing the importance of food and sustenance in our lives.
    • The phrase "svayam atkaiḥ" suggests that the offerings made are not just material but also spiritual, highlighting the significance of selfless giving.
    • In real life, this mantra can be related to the idea of community and support, where individuals come together to nourish and uplift each other.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the interconnectedness of all beings and the importance of nurturing relationships.
    • It teaches us to recognize the divine in the mundane, encouraging us to appreciate the sustenance provided by nature and the community.
    • In practical terms, it can be applied in our daily lives by fostering a spirit of cooperation, sharing resources, and supporting one another in our journeys.
    • Ultimately, it reminds us that true wealth lies not just in material possessions but in the bonds we create and the love we share.
