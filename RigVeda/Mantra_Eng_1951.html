मन्त्र: प्रो अश्विनाव अवसे कृणुध्वं प्र पूषणं स्वतवसो हि सन्ति | अद्वेषो विष्णुर वाता ऋभुक्षा अच्छा सुम्नाया ववृतीय देवान् ||
pro aśvināv avase kṛṇudhvam pra pūṣaṇaṁ svatavaso hi santi | adveṣo viṣṇur vāta ṛbhukṣā acchā sumnāya vavṛtīya devān ||
  • Mantra: "pro aśvināv avase kṛṇudhvam pra pūṣaṇaṁ svatavaso hi santi | adveṣo viṣṇur vāta ṛbhukṣā acchā sumnāya vavṛtīya devān ||"
  • Translation: "O Aśvins, come here; bring forth the nourishment. O Pūṣan, you who are self-existent, are indeed present. May there be no hostility; may Viṣṇu, the wind, and the Ṛbhukṣa (the giver of food) bring forth good fortune for the gods."

  • Explanation of Key Terms:
    • Aśvins: The twin deities associated with health, medicine, and the dawn. They symbolize the duality of life and the balance between opposites.
    • Pūṣan: A deity representing nourishment and sustenance, often invoked for prosperity and well-being.
    • Viṣṇu: A principal deity in Hinduism, known as the preserver of the universe, embodying protection and harmony.
    • Adveṣo: Refers to the absence of hostility or enmity, promoting peace and goodwill.
    • Ṛbhukṣa: A term that signifies the giver of food, emphasizing the importance of sustenance in life.

  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a healthcare setting, the invocation of Aśvins can be likened to the role of doctors and healthcare professionals who work to restore health and balance in patients' lives.
    • Pūṣan's essence can be seen in agricultural practices where farmers pray for a bountiful harvest, ensuring nourishment for their communities.
    • Viṣṇu's protective qualities can be reflected in community efforts to create safe environments, such as neighborhood watch programs that foster security and harmony.
    • The concept of adveṣo can be applied in conflict resolution, where individuals and groups strive to resolve differences peacefully, promoting understanding and cooperation.
    • Ṛbhukṣa's role in providing sustenance can be observed in food banks and community kitchens that aim to alleviate hunger and support those in need.

  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of harmony, nourishment, and the absence of hostility in our lives. It serves as a reminder to seek balance and support from divine forces while fostering goodwill among individuals. In real-life scenarios, the principles embodied in this mantra can guide us in healthcare, agriculture, community safety, conflict resolution, and social welfare. By invoking these ideals, we can create a more compassionate and prosperous society, reflecting the essence of the divine in our everyday actions.
