स योजते अरुषा विश्वभोजसा स दुद्रवत स्वाहुतः |सुब्रह्मा यज्ञः सुशमी वसूनां देवं राधो जनानाम् ||
sa yojate aruṣā viśvabhojasā sa dudravat svāhutaḥ | subrahmā yajñaḥ suśamī vasūnāṁ devaṁ rādho janānām ||
  • sa yojate aruṣā viśvabhojasā: This phrase can be interpreted as "He who is united with the red (or bright) one, the all-nourishing." Here, "aruṣā" refers to a bright or red entity, often associated with the sun or divine energy. In real life, this can symbolize the importance of connecting with sources of vitality and nourishment, such as nature, community, or spiritual practices that uplift us.
  • sa dudravat svāhutaḥ: This translates to "He who moves swiftly, who is invoked." The term "dudravat" suggests speed and agility, while "svāhutaḥ" indicates a call or invocation. In practical terms, this can relate to the idea of being proactive and responsive in our lives, whether in personal relationships, career pursuits, or spiritual endeavors. For example, a person who actively seeks opportunities and responds quickly to challenges embodies this principle.
  • subrahmā yajñaḥ: This means "the excellent sacrifice." "Subrahmā" signifies something that is auspicious or beneficial, while "yajña" refers to a ritual or offering. In everyday life, this can be seen in acts of kindness, charity, or community service, where individuals contribute positively to society. For instance, volunteering at a local shelter can be viewed as a modern-day yajña, where one offers their time and resources for the greater good.
  • suśamī vasūnāṁ devaṁ rādho janānām: This phrase translates to "the well-being of the divine, the source of prosperity for the people." "Suśamī" refers to well-being or auspiciousness, "vasūnāṁ" relates to wealth or abundance, and "rādho" signifies support or sustenance. This can be applied in real life by recognizing the importance of community and collective well-being. For example, supporting local businesses or engaging in community development initiatives fosters prosperity for all.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the interconnectedness of individuals with divine energy, the importance of proactive engagement in life, and the value of selfless contributions to society. Its significance lies in encouraging us to seek nourishment from various sources, act swiftly in our pursuits, and engage in sacrifices that benefit the community. In real-life scenarios, this can manifest through community service, environmental stewardship, and fostering relationships that promote collective well-being. By embodying these principles, we contribute to a harmonious and prosperous society.
