यद वां कक्षीवाम् उता यद व्यश्व ऋषिर यद वां दीर्घतमां जुहावा |पृथी यद वां वैन्यः साधनेष्व एवेद अतो अश्विनाऽ चेतयेताम् ||
yad vāṁ kakṣīvām̐ uta yad vyaśva ṛṣir yad vāṁ dīrghatamā juhāva | pṛthī yad vāṁ vainyaḥ sādaneṣv eved ato aśvinā cetayethām ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yad: whatever
    • vāṁ: you

    • yad: whatever
    • vyaśva: the seer
    • ṛṣir: sage
    • yad: whatever
    • vāṁ: you
    • dīrghatamā: the longest

    • pṛthī: earth
    • yad: whatever

    • ato: therefore
    • aśvinā: O Ashvins (the twin deities)
    • cetayethām: may inspire
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks to the divine connection between the individual and the cosmos, emphasizing the importance of spiritual guidance and inspiration.
    • The reference to the "womb" symbolizes the source of life and potential, suggesting that all beings originate from a divine source.
    • The mention of "the seer" and "sage" indicates the value of wisdom and insight in navigating life's challenges.
    • The phrase "the longest offered" can be interpreted as the enduring nature of spiritual offerings and the importance of commitment in one's spiritual journey.
    • The invocation of the "Ashvins" highlights the need for divine assistance and inspiration in our endeavors.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a personal context, when facing challenges, one might seek inspiration from wise mentors or spiritual leaders, akin to the "seers" mentioned in the mantra.
    • In a community setting, collective efforts towards a common goal can be seen as a spiritual offering, where the commitment of each individual contributes to the greater good.
    • In times of uncertainty, individuals may turn to prayer or meditation, invoking divine guidance similar to calling upon the "Ashvins" for support.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of life, the importance of seeking wisdom, and the value of divine inspiration.
    • Its significance lies in encouraging individuals to recognize their origins, seek guidance, and commit to their spiritual paths.
    • In real-life scenarios, it can be applied in personal growth, community efforts, and spiritual practices, fostering a deeper connection with oneself and the universe.
