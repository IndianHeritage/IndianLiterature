वयो न ये श्रेणीः पप्तुर ओजसान्तान दिवो बृहतः सानुनस परी |अश्वासा एषाम उभये यथा विदुः प्र पर्वतस्य नभनूमृचुच्यवुः ||
vayo na ye śreṇīḥ paptur ojasāntān divo bṛhataḥ sānunas pari | aśvāsa eṣām ubhaye yathā viduḥ pra parvatasya nabhanūm̐r acucyavuḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • vayo: of age or life
    • na: not
    • ye: those who
    • śreṇīḥ: groups or categories
    • paptur: have fallen or descended
    • ojasāntān: with strength or energy
    • dīvo: of the sky or heaven
    • bṛhataḥ: great or vast
    • sānunas: with affection or care
    • pari: around or about
    • aśvāsa: horses or swift beings
    • eṣām: of them
    • ubhaye: both
    • yathā: as or like
    • viduḥ: they know
    • pra: before or in front of
    • parvatasya: of the mountain
    • nabhanūm̐r: of the sky or atmosphere
    • acucyavuḥ: have descended or fallen
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the essence of life and the interconnectedness of all beings.
    • It emphasizes that those who have fallen or faced challenges are still surrounded by strength and support from the universe.
    • The reference to "horses" symbolizes speed and vitality, suggesting that life continues to move forward despite obstacles.
    • The mention of "mountains" and "sky" indicates the vastness of existence and the importance of perspective in understanding our place in the world.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In times of personal struggle, such as losing a job or facing health issues, individuals often find strength in their community and support systems, reflecting the mantra's message of collective resilience.
    • In nature, we see how ecosystems recover after disasters, illustrating the idea that life persists and thrives even after significant challenges.
    • In sports, teams that face setbacks often rally together, drawing on their collective strength to overcome adversity and achieve success.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the strength found in unity and the importance of resilience in the face of challenges.
    • It encourages individuals to recognize their interconnectedness with others and the universe, fostering a sense of belonging and support.
    • In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios, from personal struggles to community efforts, highlighting the significance of collective strength and perseverance.
