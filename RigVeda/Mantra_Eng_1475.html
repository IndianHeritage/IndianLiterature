वि तद् वोचर अध द्वितान्तः पश्यन्ति रश्मिभिः |स घा विदे अन्व इन्द्रो गवेक्षणो बन्धुक्षिद्भ्यो गवेक्षणः ||
vi tad vocer adha dvitāntaḥ paśyanti raśmibhiḥ | sa ghā vide anv indro gaveṣaṇo bandhukṣidbhyo gaveṣaṇaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • vi: apart, separately
    • tad: that
    • vocer: I speak
    • adha: below, beneath
    • dvitāntaḥ: the second end, the duality
    • paśyanti: they see
    • raśmibhiḥ: with rays, with light
    • sa: he, that
    • ghā: to know, to understand
    • vide: to spread, to expand
    • anv: following, pursuing
    • indro: Indra, the king of gods
    • gaveṣaṇo: seeking, searching
    • bandhukṣidbhyo: from the hidden, from the concealed
    • gaveṣaṇaḥ: the seeker, the one who searches
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the pursuit of knowledge and understanding.
    • The phrase "vi tad vocer adha" suggests a separation or distinction in the act of speaking or expressing knowledge.
    • "Dvitāntaḥ" refers to the duality of existence, indicating that there are multiple perspectives or truths to be acknowledged.
    • The line "paśyanti raśmibhiḥ" implies that through illumination (light or knowledge), one can perceive deeper truths.
    • Indra, as the king of gods, symbolizes strength and wisdom, suggesting that the pursuit of knowledge is a divine endeavor.
    • The search for understanding "from the hidden" emphasizes the importance of uncovering truths that are not immediately visible.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In scientific research, the pursuit of knowledge often involves questioning established theories and seeking new evidence, much like the "seeking" mentioned in the mantra.
    • In personal development, individuals often need to confront their own dualities—strengths and weaknesses—to achieve a deeper understanding of themselves.
    • In education, teachers illuminate complex subjects for students, helping them to "see" the knowledge through various perspectives.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of seeking knowledge and understanding in various aspects of life.
    • It encourages individuals to look beyond the surface and explore the dualities of existence, fostering a deeper comprehension of the world.
    • In real-life scenarios, this pursuit can lead to personal growth, scientific advancements, and a more profound appreciation of diverse perspectives.
