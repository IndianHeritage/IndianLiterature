तं इद गच्छन्ति जुह्वस तं अरवतीर विश्वानि एकः श्रिणवद वचांसि मे |पुरुप्रैषस् ततुरीर यज्ञसाधनो 'च्छिद्रोतिḥ शिशुर आदत्त सं रभः ||
tam id gacchanti juhva1s tam arvatīr viśvāny ekaḥ śṛṇavad vacāṁsi me | purupraiṣas taturir yajñasādhano 'cchidrotiḥ śiśur ādatta saṁ rabhaḥ ||
  • tam id gacchanti juhva1s: This phrase can be translated as "those who invoke (or call upon) him." It signifies the act of seeking divine guidance or support through rituals and prayers.
  • tam arvatīr: This means "that which is worthy of reverence." It refers to the divine or sacred aspects of life that deserve respect and devotion.
  • viśvāny ekaḥ: Translated as "all things are one," this phrase emphasizes the interconnectedness of all existence. It suggests that despite the diversity of life, there is a fundamental unity that binds everything together.
  • śṛṇavad vacāṁsi me: This means "listen to my words." It indicates a plea for attention and understanding, highlighting the importance of communication and the sharing of wisdom.
  • purupraiṣas taturir: This can be interpreted as "the one who is the source of many." It reflects the idea that from a single source, multiple forms and manifestations arise, akin to how a tree grows from a single seed.
  • yajñasādhano 'cchidrotiḥ: This phrase refers to "the one who is the essence of sacrifice." It underscores the significance of selflessness and the act of giving in various forms, whether through rituals, service, or love.
  • śiśur ādatta saṁ rabhaḥ: This translates to "the child who takes (or receives) the essence." It symbolizes the innocence and purity of a child, representing new beginnings and the potential for growth and learning.

Conclusion:

The mantra encapsulates profound philosophical concepts that are applicable in various aspects of life. It teaches us the importance of seeking divine guidance, recognizing the interconnectedness of all beings, and the value of communication. In real life, this can be seen in practices such as community service, where individuals come together to support one another, reflecting the unity and shared purpose emphasized in the mantra. Additionally, it highlights the significance of selflessness and sacrifice, reminding us that our actions can have a positive impact on the world around us. Ultimately, this mantra serves as a reminder to honor the sacredness of life, to listen and learn, and to contribute to the greater good with love and compassion.
