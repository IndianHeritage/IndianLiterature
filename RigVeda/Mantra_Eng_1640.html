भवामित्रो न शेव्यो घृतासुतिर विभूतद्युम्न एवया उ सप्रथाः |अधा ते विष्णो विदुषा चिदर्ध्यः स्तोमो यज्ञश्च राध्यो हविष्मता ||
bhavā mitro na śevyo ghṛtāsutir vibhūtadyumna evayā u saprathāḥ | adhā te viṣṇo viduṣā cid ardhyaḥ stomo yajñaś ca rādhyo haviṣmatā ||
  • bhavā mitro na śevyo: This phrase can be translated as "May you be a friend who is not to be neglected." It emphasizes the importance of friendship and companionship in our lives. In real life, this can be seen in the way we nurture our relationships with family and friends, ensuring that we are there for each other in times of need.
  • ghṛtāsutir vibhūtadyumna evayā u saprathāḥ: This part refers to the "glorious and prosperous" aspects of life. "ghṛtāsutir" can be interpreted as the essence of nourishment and abundance, while "vibhūtadyumna" signifies wealth and success. In practical terms, this can relate to how we strive for prosperity in our careers and personal lives, seeking to create a fulfilling and abundant existence.
  • adhā te viṣṇo viduṣā cid ardhyaḥ: This line speaks to the divine connection and the pursuit of knowledge. "viṣṇo" refers to the preserver and protector in Hinduism, while "viduṣā" means knowledge or wisdom. This suggests that through wisdom, we can connect with the divine. In everyday life, this can be seen in the pursuit of education and self-improvement, where individuals seek to gain knowledge to better themselves and their communities.
  • stomo yajñaś ca rādhyo haviṣmatā: This final part highlights the significance of rituals and offerings. "yajña" refers to sacrifice or worship, and "haviṣmatā" indicates the essence of offerings. This can be related to how we express gratitude and reverence in our lives, whether through religious practices or simply by acknowledging the contributions of others. For example, celebrating festivals or participating in community service can be seen as modern-day yajñas.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of friendship, prosperity, knowledge, and the importance of rituals in our lives. It serves as a reminder to cultivate meaningful relationships, strive for abundance, seek wisdom, and express gratitude through our actions. In real-life scenarios, these principles can guide us in building a harmonious and fulfilling life, fostering connections with others, and contributing positively to society.
