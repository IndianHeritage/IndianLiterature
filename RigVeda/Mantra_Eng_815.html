मन्त्र: मनो न यो 'ध्वनः सद्य एत्य एकः सत्रा सूरो वस्व ईशे | राजानां मित्रावरुणा सुपाणि गोषु प्रियं अमृतं रक्षमााणा ||
mano na yo 'dhvanaḥ sadya ety ekaḥ satrā sūro vasva īśe | rājānā mitrāvaruṇā supāṇī goṣu priyam amṛtaṁ rakṣamāṇā ||
  • mano na yo 'dhvanaḥ sadya ety ekaḥ satrā sūro vasva īśe
    • mano: Refers to the mind or consciousness.
    • na: Means "not".
    • yo: Translates to "who".
    • 'dhvanaḥ: Means "of the path" or "of the journey".
    • sadya: Means "immediately" or "suddenly".
    • ety: Means "comes" or "arrives".
    • ekaḥ: Means "one" or "single".
    • satrā: Refers to "sacrifice" or "ritual".
    • sūro: Means "brave" or "heroic".
    • vasva: Means "all" or "universal".
    • īśe: Refers to "the Lord" or "the divine".
  • rājānā mitrāvaruṇā supāṇī goṣu priyam amṛtaṁ rakṣamāṇā
    • rājānā: Means "kings" or "rulers".
    • mitrāvaruṇā: Refers to "Mitra and Varuna\ deities representing friendship and cosmic order.
    • supāṇī: Means "well-armed" or "strong".
    • goṣu: Refers to "cows\ symbolizing wealth and nourishment.
    • priyam: Means "beloved" or "dear".
    • amṛtaṁ: Refers to "immortality" or "nectar of immortality".
    • rakṣamāṇā: Means "protecting" or "guarding".

The mantra can be interpreted as a call for the mind to focus on the divine path, emphasizing the importance of unity and strength in the pursuit of spiritual and material goals. It highlights the role of divine forces, represented by Mitra and Varuna, in safeguarding prosperity and well-being.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios:

  • Personal Growth: Just as the mantra encourages a focused mind, individuals can apply this by setting clear goals and remaining steadfast in their pursuits, whether in education, career, or personal development.
  • Community and Leadership: The reference to kings and divine protectors can inspire leaders to act with integrity and responsibility, ensuring the welfare of their communities.
  • Spiritual Practices: The invocation of divine protection and guidance can be a part of daily prayers or meditative practices, fostering a sense of connection to higher powers.

Conclusion: This mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of a focused mind, the strength found in unity, and the divine protection that guides us in our endeavors. It encourages individuals to seek balance between spiritual and material pursuits, ensuring that both are nurtured for a fulfilling life.
