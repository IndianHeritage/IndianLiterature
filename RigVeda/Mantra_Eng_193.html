आ ये तंवन्ति रश्मिभिः तिरः समुद्रम ओजसा |मरुद्भिर अग्न आ गहि ||
ā ye tanvanti raśmibhis tiraḥ samudram ojasā | marudbhir agna ā gahi ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā: From
    • ye: Those who
    • tanvanti: Spread out or expand
    • raśmibhis: With rays (often referring to light or energy)
    • tiraḥ: Beyond
    • samudram: The ocean or vastness
    • ojasā: With energy or power
    • marudbhir: By the winds or forces of nature
    • agna: O Fire (often referring to the divine fire or energy)
    • ā: To
    • gahi: Take or grasp
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the divine energy represented by Agni (Fire) and the forces of nature (Maruts). It speaks of the expansive power of light and energy that transcends the vastness of the ocean.
    • The phrase "ā ye tanvanti raśmibhis" suggests the idea of spreading or radiating energy, akin to how sunlight spreads across the earth, nurturing life.
    • "Tiraḥ samudram ojasā" indicates that this energy has the power to reach beyond the vastness of the ocean, symbolizing the limitless potential of divine energy.
    • The invocation of "marudbhir agna" emphasizes the connection between natural forces (winds) and the divine fire, suggesting a harmonious relationship between nature and spirituality.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In nature, the sun's rays can be seen as a metaphor for the divine energy that sustains life. Just as the sun spreads its light, individuals can spread positivity and energy in their communities.
    • The concept of harnessing energy can be related to modern technology, such as solar panels that capture sunlight and convert it into usable energy, reflecting the mantra's theme of utilizing divine energy for practical purposes.
    • In personal development, the idea of reaching beyond one's limits (tiraḥ samudram) can inspire individuals to pursue their goals with vigor and determination, much like the relentless forces of nature.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of recognizing and harnessing the divine energies present in nature and within ourselves. It encourages individuals to connect with these forces to achieve their potential.
    • In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various fields such as environmental sustainability, personal growth, and community building, where the focus is on utilizing natural energies and fostering a harmonious relationship with the environment.
    • Ultimately, the mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of all life and the power of divine energy to inspire and uplift humanity.
