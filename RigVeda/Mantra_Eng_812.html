स्व आ यस् तुभ्यं दमा आ विभाति नमो वा दाशाद उषतो अनु dyūn |वर्धो अग्ने वयो अस्य द्विबर्हा यासद राया सरथं यं जुनासि ||
sva ā yas tubhyaṁ dama ā vibhāti namo vā dāśād uśato anu dyūn | vardho agne vayo asya dvibarhā yāsad rāyā sarathaṁ yaṁ junāsi ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sva: one's own
    • ā: indeed
    • yas: who
    • tubhyaṁ: to you
    • dama: control or restraint
    • ā: indeed
    • vibhāti: shines or radiates
    • namo: homage or salutation
    • vā: or
    • dāśād: from the ten
    • uśato: of the wise
    • anu: after
    • dyūn: the heavens
    • vardho: may you grow
    • agne: O Fire
    • vayo: of strength
    • asya: of this
    • dvibarhā: with two branches
    • yāsad: who is seated
    • rāyā: with wealth
    • sarathaṁ: with chariots
    • yaṁ: whom
    • junāsi: you protect
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Agni, the fire god, who symbolizes energy, transformation, and the divine presence in rituals.
    • The phrase "sva ā yas tubhyaṁ" emphasizes the personal connection and devotion to Agni, indicating that the speaker seeks to align their own energy with that of the divine.
    • "Dama ā vibhāti" suggests that through control and restraint, one can radiate positive energy, akin to how a well-tended fire provides warmth and light.
    • The mention of "dāśād uśato" refers to the wisdom gained from the ten directions, symbolizing a holistic understanding of life and the universe.
    • "Vardho agne" is a plea for growth and strength, reflecting the human desire for progress and prosperity in life.
    • The imagery of "dvibarhā" and "sarathaṁ" evokes the idea of being well-equipped and supported, much like a chariot that is ready for a journey, symbolizing readiness for life's challenges.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In personal development, this mantra can inspire individuals to cultivate self-discipline and focus, leading to personal growth and success.
    • In a business context, the invocation of Agni can symbolize the drive for innovation and the importance of teamwork, akin to a well-coordinated team working towards a common goal.
    • In community rituals, this mantra can be recited to invoke collective strength and unity, encouraging members to support one another in their endeavors.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of aligning personal energy with divine forces, emphasizing the importance of self-control, wisdom, and community support.
    • Its application in real life can be seen in various contexts, from personal growth to teamwork and community building, making it a timeless invocation for strength and prosperity.
    • Ultimately, it serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of individual efforts and collective success, urging us to seek balance and harmony in our pursuits.
