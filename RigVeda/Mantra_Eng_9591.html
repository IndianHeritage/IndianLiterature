यद वावान पुरुतमं पुराशाल् आ वृत्रहेन्द्रो नामान्य अप्राह् |अचेति प्रासहस पतिस तुविष्मान यद ईम उष्मसि कर्तवे करत तत् ||
yad vāvāna purutamam purāṣāḻ ā vṛtrahendro nāmāny aprāḥ | aceti prāsahas patis tuviṣmān yad īm uśmasi kartave karat tat ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yad - whatever
    • vāvāna - is most excellent
    • purutamam - the best among all
    • purāṣāḻ - the ancient or primordial
    • ā - to
    • vṛtrahendro - the slayer of Vritra (a demon)
    • nāmāny - names
    • aprāḥ - not to be neglected
    • aceti - he who knows
    • prāsahas - the lord of strength
    • patis - master or lord
    • tuviṣmān - the one who is powerful
    • yad - whatever
    • īm - these
    • uśmasi - in the warmth or light
    • kartave - to be done
    • karat - to act or perform
    • tat - that
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the supreme power and excellence of the divine, particularly in the context of overcoming obstacles and challenges.
    • The reference to "Vritra" symbolizes the forces of chaos and ignorance that one must confront in life.
    • The mantra emphasizes the importance of recognizing and invoking the strength and wisdom of the divine in our actions.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a challenging situation, such as facing a difficult exam or job interview, one might recite this mantra to seek strength and clarity.
    • During times of personal crisis, individuals may find solace in the idea that they can call upon a higher power to help them navigate their struggles.
    • In leadership roles, invoking the essence of this mantra can inspire confidence and decisiveness in making tough decisions.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its reminder of the divine support available to us in overcoming life's challenges.
    • It encourages individuals to acknowledge their inner strength and the power of the universe in guiding their actions.
    • In practical terms, this mantra can be applied in various life scenarios, from personal growth to professional endeavors, reinforcing the belief that with faith and determination, one can conquer obstacles.
