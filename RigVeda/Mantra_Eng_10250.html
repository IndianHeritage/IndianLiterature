अग्नेर गायत्री अभवत् सायुग्वोष्णिहयाऽ सविता सम बभूव |अनुष्टुभा सोम उक्थैर महस्वान् बृहस्पतेर बृहती वाचम् आवत ||
agner gāyatry abhavat sayugvoṣṇihayā savitā sam babhūva | anuṣṭubhā soma ukthair mahasvān bṛhaspater bṛhatī vācam āvat ||
  • Agner gāyatry abhavat: This phrase refers to the emergence of the Gayatri mantra from Agni (the fire). Agni symbolizes purity, transformation, and the divine spark within us. In real life, this can be likened to the process of personal growth and enlightenment, where one transforms through knowledge and experience.
  • Sayugvoṣṇihayā savitā sam babhūva: Here, Savitā, the Sun God, is invoked. "Sayugvoṣṇihayā" suggests the dual nature of energy—both nurturing and fierce. This can be compared to the sun's role in agriculture, where it provides warmth and light for crops to grow, yet can also scorch them if too intense. In our lives, it reflects the balance of receiving energy and using it wisely.
  • Anuṣṭubhā soma ukthair: This part speaks of the Anuṣṭubh meter and the essence of Soma, which represents the moon and is associated with nourishment and rejuvenation. In practical terms, this can be seen in the importance of rest and rejuvenation in our daily lives, akin to how the moon influences tides and natural cycles.
  • Mahasvān bṛhaspater bṛhatī vācam āvat: This line invokes Bṛhaspati, the deity of wisdom and eloquence. "Bṛhatī vācam" refers to the great speech or wisdom. In real life, this can be related to the importance of communication and knowledge sharing in fostering understanding and collaboration among people.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of transformation, balance, nourishment, and wisdom. Its significance lies in the interconnectedness of these elements in our lives. By recognizing the divine energies represented by Agni, Savitā, Soma, and Bṛhaspati, we can strive for personal growth, maintain balance in our actions, prioritize rejuvenation, and communicate effectively. This mantra serves as a reminder of the holistic approach needed to navigate life's challenges and opportunities, encouraging us to harness these energies for our betterment and the betterment of society.
