पदाः पाणिम् आराधसो न बाधस्व महाम् असि |नहि त्वां कश्चन प्रति ||
padā paṇīm̐r arādhaso ni bādhasva mahām̐ asi | nahi tvā kaś cana prati ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • padā: by the foot
    • paṇīm̐r: by the hand
    • arādhaso: of the one who is worshipped
    • ni: not
    • bādhasva: obstruct
    • mahām̐: great
    • asi: you are
    • nahi: indeed
    • tvā: you
    • kaś: anyone
    • cana: at all
    • prati: against
  • Overall Meaning:
    • This mantra can be interpreted as a plea or invocation to a divine entity, asking not to be obstructed in one's path or endeavors.
    • The speaker acknowledges the greatness of the divine and seeks protection from any hindrances that may arise.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, an individual may recite this mantra before a crucial presentation or meeting, seeking clarity and the removal of obstacles that could hinder their success.
    • Students may use this mantra before exams, asking for divine support to overcome anxiety and distractions, ensuring they can perform to the best of their abilities.
  • Conclusion and Significance:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking divine assistance in overcoming challenges and obstacles in life.
    • It serves as a reminder that while we strive for success, we should also acknowledge the greater forces at play and seek their guidance.
    • In daily life, this mantra can be applicable in various situations, such as during difficult decisions, challenging tasks, or moments of uncertainty, reinforcing the belief that with faith and perseverance, one can navigate through life's hurdles.
