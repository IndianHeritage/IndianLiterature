पूर्वायुषं सुहवम् पुरुष्पृहम् भुज्युं वाजेषु पूर्व्यम् |सचनावन्तं सुमतिभिः शोभरे विद्वेषसम् अनहसम् ||
pūrvāyuṣaṁ suhavam puruspṛham bhujyuṁ vājeṣu pūrvyam | sacanāvantaṁ sumatibhiḥ sobhare vidveṣasam anehasam ||
  • pūrvāyuṣaṁ: This term refers to the "previous life" or "past existence." It signifies the importance of understanding our past experiences and how they shape our present.
  • suhavam: Meaning "auspicious" or "pleasant," this word emphasizes the positive aspects of life and the importance of seeking joy and happiness.
  • puruspṛham: This can be interpreted as "the one who is strong" or "the powerful one." It highlights the strength and resilience that individuals possess.
  • bhujyuṁ: This term refers to "enjoyment" or "pleasure." It suggests that life should be enjoyed and that one should seek fulfillment in their endeavors.
  • vājeṣu: Meaning "in the sacrifices" or "in the offerings," this word indicates the significance of rituals and the act of giving in various forms.
  • pūrvyam: This term can be understood as "from the east" or "the direction of the rising sun," symbolizing new beginnings and opportunities.
  • sacanāvantaṁ: This refers to "those who are wise" or "intelligent beings." It emphasizes the value of wisdom and knowledge in navigating life.
  • sumatibhiḥ: Meaning "with good thoughts" or "with pure intentions," this term highlights the importance of having a positive mindset and intentions in our actions.
  • sobhare: This word signifies "prosperity" or "wealth." It suggests that prosperity is a result of good actions and intentions.
  • vidveṣasam: This term refers to "those who are free from enmity" or "without hatred." It emphasizes the importance of harmony and peace in relationships.
  • anehasam: Meaning "without darkness" or "illuminated," this word symbolizes enlightenment and clarity in thought and action.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of living a fulfilling and prosperous life by reflecting on our past, embracing positivity, and seeking wisdom. It encourages individuals to enjoy life, engage in meaningful rituals, and foster good intentions. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as personal development, conflict resolution, and community building. By embodying the principles outlined in this mantra, individuals can contribute to a harmonious and prosperous society, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling existence.
