मन्त्र: स्वर्णरम् अन्तरिक्षाणि रोचनā द्यावाभूमी पृथिवीं स्कम्भुर ओजसा | पृक्षā इव महयन्तः सुरातयः देवाः स्तवन्ते मनुषाय सूरयः ||
svarṇaram antarikṣāṇi rocanā dyāvābhūmī pṛthivīṁ skambhur ojasā | pṛkṣā iva mahayantaḥ surātayo devāḥ stavante manuṣāya sūrayaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • svarṇaram: Golden or radiant
    • antarikṣāṇi: The heavens or the sky
    • rocanā: Shining or illuminating
    • dyāvābhūmī: The sky and the earth
    • pṛthivīṁ: The earth
    • skambhur: The support or pillar
    • ojasā: With energy or strength
    • pṛkṣā: Like a tree or a support
    • iva: Like or as
    • mahayantaḥ: Great or powerful
    • surātayo: Divine beings or gods
    • devāḥ: The deities
    • stavante: Praise or worship
    • manuṣāya: For humanity
    • sūrayaḥ: The brave or heroic
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the radiant heavens and the earth, emphasizing the interconnectedness of the universe.
    • The phrase "svarṇaram antarikṣāṇi" suggests a vision of a golden sky, symbolizing hope, prosperity, and divine presence.
    • "dyāvābhūmī pṛthivīṁ" refers to the duality of sky and earth, representing the balance of spiritual and material worlds.
    • The term "skambhur ojasā" indicates a strong foundation, suggesting that both the physical and spiritual realms are supported by divine energy.
    • The mention of "devāḥ stavante manuṣāya sūrayaḥ" highlights the role of divine beings in guiding and inspiring humanity, encouraging us to seek strength and bravery in our endeavors.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In moments of challenge, individuals often look to nature, represented by the earth and sky, for inspiration and strength.
    • Communities may come together to celebrate festivals that honor the divine, reflecting the praise of the gods as mentioned in the mantra.
    • In personal development, the idea of seeking balance between spiritual aspirations and material needs can be seen in practices like yoga and meditation.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the divine support that exists in our lives, encouraging us to recognize the beauty and strength in both the spiritual and material worlds.
    • Its significance lies in the call for humanity to acknowledge and praise the divine forces that guide us, fostering a sense of unity and purpose.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire individuals to seek balance, strength, and connection with the universe, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling existence.
