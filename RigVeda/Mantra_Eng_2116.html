नूनं सा ते प्रति वरं जरीत्रे दूहीयद इन्द्र दक्षिणा मगहोनी |शिक्षा स्तोत्रिभ्यो माती धग भगो नो बृहद वदेम विदथे सुवीराः ||
nūnaṁ sā te prati varaṁ jaritre duhīyad indra dakṣiṇā maghonī | śikṣā stotṛbhyo māti dhag bhago no bṛhad vadema vidathe suvīrāḥ ||
  • nūnaṁ sā te prati varaṁ - This phrase can be interpreted as "Indeed, that is your excellent gift." It suggests a recognition of the divine or a higher power's generosity.
  • jaritre duhīyad indra - Here, "Indra" refers to the king of gods in Vedic tradition, and "jaritre" implies the act of nurturing or sustaining. This part emphasizes the importance of divine support in our endeavors.
  • dakṣiṇā maghonī - "Dakṣiṇā" means a gift or offering, often given to priests or sages, while "maghonī" refers to wealth or abundance. This indicates that offerings lead to prosperity and blessings.
  • śikṣā stotṛbhyo - "Śikṣā" means instruction or teaching, and "stotṛbhyo" refers to those who praise or chant. This highlights the value of learning and the power of words in spiritual practices.
  • māti dhag bhago - "Māti" means intellect or wisdom, and "bhago" refers to wealth or prosperity. This suggests that true wealth comes from wisdom and understanding.
  • no bṛhad vadema - "No" means "we," and "bṛhad vadema" translates to "we speak of the great." This indicates a collective effort to express and share knowledge or experiences.
  • vidathe suvīrāḥ - "Vidathe" refers to a gathering or assembly, and "suvīrāḥ" means "the brave." This emphasizes the importance of community and courage in achieving goals.

Conclusion: This mantra encapsulates the essence of divine support, the importance of wisdom, and the value of community in achieving prosperity and success. In real life, it can be applied in various scenarios such as teamwork in professional settings, where collective wisdom and support lead to successful outcomes. It also highlights the significance of gratitude and offerings in our lives, reminding us to acknowledge the contributions of others and the divine in our journey. By fostering a sense of community and valuing wisdom, we can navigate challenges more effectively and achieve our goals with greater ease.
