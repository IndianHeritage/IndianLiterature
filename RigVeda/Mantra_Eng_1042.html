यस्मै त्वं āयजसे स साधत्य अनर्वा क्षेति दधते सुवीर्यम् |स तूताव नैनम अश्नोत्य अंहतिर्ग्ने सख्ये मा ऋषामाऽवयं तव ||
yasmai tvam āyajase sa sādhaty anarvā kṣeti dadhate suvīryam | sa tūtāva nainam aśnoty aṁhatir agne sakhye mā riṣāmā vayaṁ tava ||
  • Mantra: "yasmai tvam āyajase sa sādhaty anarvā kṣeti dadhate suvīryam | sa tūtāva nainam aśnoty aṁhatir agne sakhye mā riṣāmā vayaṁ tava ||"
  • Translation: "To whom you offer your worship, he indeed achieves the unattainable strength; he does not suffer from any harm, O Agni, may we not be angry with you."

  • Word-by-Word Explanation:
    • yasmai: to whom
    • tvam: you
    • āyajase: offer worship
    • sa: he
    • sādhaty: achieves
    • anarvā: unattainable
    • kṣeti: strength
    • dadhate: possesses
    • suvīryam: great power
    • sa: he
    • tūtāva: does not suffer
    • nainam: from him
    • aśnoty: harm
    • aṁhatir: injury
    • agne: O Agni (Fire)
    • sakhye: in friendship
    • mā: may not
    • riṣāmā: be angry
    • vayaṁ: we
    • tava: with you

  • Significance:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of devotion and worship. It suggests that through sincere worship, one can attain strength and resilience against adversities.
    • Agni, representing fire, symbolizes transformation and purification. The mantra invokes Agni's protective qualities, highlighting the belief that true devotion leads to divine support.

  • Real-Life Application:
    • In daily life, this mantra can be applied in situations where individuals face challenges or obstacles. For example, a student preparing for exams may recite this mantra to seek strength and focus, believing that their dedication will lead to success.
    • In professional settings, employees may use this mantra to foster a sense of teamwork and collaboration, reminding themselves that through mutual support and respect, they can achieve their goals without conflict.

  • Conclusion:
    • The mantra serves as a reminder of the power of devotion and the strength that comes from unity and respect. It encourages individuals to seek divine guidance and support in their endeavors, reinforcing the idea that true strength lies in harmony and friendship.
    • By incorporating the principles of this mantra into everyday life, individuals can cultivate resilience, foster positive relationships, and navigate challenges with a sense of purpose and strength.
