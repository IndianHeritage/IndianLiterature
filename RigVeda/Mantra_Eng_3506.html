युवामिद्ध्यवसे पूर्व्याय परिप्रभूतिः गविषः स्वापी |वृणीमहे सख्याय प्रियाय शूरा मंहिष्ठा पितरेव शम्भू ||
yuvām id dhy avase pūrvyāya pari prabhūtī gaviṣaḥ svāpī | vṛṇīmahe sakhyāya priyāya śūrā maṁhiṣṭhā pitareva śambhū ||
  • yuvām id dhy avase pūrvyāya: This phrase can be interpreted as "You two, who are present here, are the ones we focus on." It emphasizes the importance of unity and collaboration between individuals.
  • pari prabhūtī gaviṣaḥ svāpī: This translates to "We seek the abundant wealth and prosperity." It signifies the pursuit of material and spiritual wealth, highlighting the need for abundance in life.
  • vṛṇīmahe sakhyāya priyāya: This means "We choose friendship and love." It underscores the value of relationships and the bonds that tie people together, suggesting that love and friendship are essential for a fulfilling life.
  • śūrā maṁhiṣṭhā pitareva śambhū: This can be interpreted as "Like a father, we seek strength and protection." It reflects the desire for guidance and support, akin to the nurturing role of a father figure in one's life.

  • Significance: This mantra encapsulates the essence of human relationships, the pursuit of prosperity, and the importance of unity. It serves as a reminder that collaboration and love are fundamental to achieving success and happiness.
  • Real-life Application: In everyday life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as in teamwork at the workplace, where collaboration leads to greater achievements. It also resonates in personal relationships, where love and friendship foster a supportive environment. For instance, in a family setting, the nurturing role of parents can be likened to the protective and guiding nature described in the mantra.

  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the interconnectedness of individuals and the importance of seeking prosperity through unity and love. It encourages individuals to build strong relationships and work together towards common goals, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling and prosperous life.
