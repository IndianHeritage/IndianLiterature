नू त्वाम अग्न इमहे वसिष्ठा ईशानं सूनो सहसो वसूनाम् |इषं स्तोत्रिभ्यो मगवद्भ्य आनाḍ यूम पात स्वस्तिभिः सदा नः ||
nū tvām agna īmahe vasiṣṭhā īśānaṁ sūno sahaso vasūnām | iṣaṁ stotṛbhyo maghavadbhya ānaḍ yūyam pāta svastibhiḥ sadā naḥ ||
  • nū tvām agna īmahe: This phrase translates to "We invoke you, O Agni." Agni, the fire god, represents energy, transformation, and the divine messenger between humans and the gods. In real life, invoking Agni can symbolize the importance of seeking guidance and inspiration in our endeavors, much like how we seek motivation from mentors or leaders.
  • vasiṣṭhā īśānaṁ sūno: This means "the best of the best, the lord of the wealth." Here, "Vasiṣṭha" refers to the sage known for his wisdom and wealth of knowledge. In a modern context, this can relate to the pursuit of knowledge and wisdom in our lives, emphasizing the value of education and learning from those who are knowledgeable.
  • sahaso vasūnām: This translates to "the powerful among the wealth." It highlights the idea that true wealth is not just material but also includes strength, wisdom, and virtue. In today's world, this can be seen in successful individuals who use their resources to uplift others, demonstrating that real power lies in sharing and supporting the community.
  • iṣaṁ stotṛbhyo maghavadbhya: This means "to those who praise and to the generous." It emphasizes the importance of gratitude and recognition of those who contribute positively to our lives. In real life, this can be reflected in how we appreciate and acknowledge the efforts of our friends, family, and colleagues, fostering a culture of gratitude.
  • ānaḍ yūyam pāta svastibhiḥ: This translates to "may you protect us with blessings." It signifies a plea for protection and well-being. In contemporary life, this can be related to seeking safety and security in our daily lives, whether through community support, family bonds, or spiritual practices that provide comfort and assurance.
  • sadā naḥ: This means "always for us." It reflects a desire for continuous support and blessings in our lives. In a practical sense, this can be seen in the relationships we cultivate, where we seek enduring connections that provide us with strength and encouragement throughout our journeys.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a deep reverence for Agni, symbolizing the pursuit of wisdom, strength, and community support. Its significance lies in the reminder to seek guidance, appreciate the contributions of others, and strive for continuous growth and protection in our lives. In real-life scenarios, this mantra encourages us to foster relationships, pursue knowledge, and contribute positively to our communities, ultimately leading to a more harmonious and prosperous existence.
