युजानो हरिता रथे भूरी त्वष्टेह राजति |को विश्वाहा द्विषतः पक्ष आसत उतासीनेषु सूरिषु ||
yujāno haritā rathe bhūri tvaṣṭeha rājati | ko viśvāhā dviṣataḥ pakṣa āsata utāsīneṣu sūriṣu ||
  • yujāno: This word can be interpreted as "united" or "joined together." It signifies the importance of collaboration and teamwork in achieving common goals.
  • haritā: This term means "green" or "prosperous." It symbolizes growth, fertility, and the abundance of nature, which are essential for a thriving community.
  • rathe: Translated as "in the chariot," it represents the journey of life and the various paths we take. The chariot can be seen as a metaphor for our ambitions and aspirations.
  • bhūri: This word means "abundant" or "plentiful." It emphasizes the idea of having enough resources and wealth to support oneself and others.
  • tvaṣṭeha: Referring to "the creator" or "the divine craftsman," it highlights the role of a higher power in shaping our destinies and the world around us.
  • rājati: This means "to shine" or "to rule." It suggests the idea of leadership and the importance of guiding others towards a common purpose.
  • ko: This word translates to "who," prompting a question about identity and the nature of existence.
  • viśvāhā: Meaning "all-encompassing" or "universal," it reflects the interconnectedness of all beings and the importance of inclusivity.
  • dviṣataḥ: This term means "of the enemies" or "those who oppose." It acknowledges the presence of challenges and adversities in life.
  • pakṣa: Translated as "wing" or "side," it symbolizes the duality of life, where one must choose between different paths or perspectives.
  • āsata: This means "to sit" or "to dwell," indicating a state of being or existence.
  • utāsīneṣu: This term refers to "those who are seated above," suggesting a higher perspective or wisdom that guides decision-making.
  • sūriṣu: Meaning "in the heroes" or "among the brave," it emphasizes the qualities of courage and valor in facing life's challenges.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of unity, prosperity, and the divine guidance that shapes our lives. It serves as a reminder of the importance of collaboration and the need to embrace both challenges and opportunities. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as teamwork in professional settings, community building, and personal growth. By recognizing the interconnectedness of all beings and the role of a higher power, individuals can navigate their journeys with purpose and resilience, ultimately leading to a more harmonious existence.
