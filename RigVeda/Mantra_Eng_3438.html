रथं ये चक्रुः सुवृतं नरेष्ठां ये धेनुं विश्वजुवं विश्वरूपाम् | त आ तक्षन्त्व ऋभवो रयिं नः स्ववसः स्वपसः सुहस्ताः ||
rathaṁ ye cakruḥ suvṛtaṁ nareṣṭhāṁ ye dhenuṁ viśvajuvaṁ viśvarūpām | ta ā takṣantv ṛbhavo rayiṁ naḥ svavasaḥ svapasaḥ suhastāḥ ||
  • Mantra: "rathaṁ ye cakruḥ suvṛtaṁ nareṣṭhāṁ ye dhenuṁ viśvajuvaṁ viśvarūpām | ta ā takṣantv ṛbhavo rayiṁ naḥ svavasaḥ svapasaḥ suhastāḥ ||"
  • Translation: "Those who have made the well-constructed chariot, those who have created the best of men, those who have produced the universal cow, the one of many forms; may they grant us wealth, may they be our protectors, may they be our helpers."

  • Word-by-Word Explanation:
    • rathaṁ: chariot, symbolizing progress and movement in life.
    • ye: those who.
    • cakruḥ: have made or constructed.
    • suvṛtaṁ: well-constructed or well-formed.
    • nareṣṭhāṁ: the best of men, representing ideal human qualities.
    • ye: those who.
    • dhenuṁ: cow, a symbol of nourishment and wealth.
    • viśvajuvaṁ: universal or all-encompassing.
    • viśvarūpām: of many forms, indicating diversity and adaptability.
    • ta: may they.
    • ā: grant or bestow.
    • takṣantv: may they protect or safeguard.
    • ṛbhavo: wealth or prosperity.
    • rayiṁ: riches or abundance.
    • naḥ: for us.
    • svavasaḥ: our own, indicating personal connection.
    • svapasaḥ: our helpers or supporters.
    • suhastāḥ: those with good hands, symbolizing skill and capability.

  • Real-Life Examples:
    • The concept of a "well-constructed chariot" can be likened to building a solid foundation in one's career or personal life. For instance, a successful entrepreneur invests time and resources into creating a robust business model before launching their venture.
    • The "best of men" can refer to leaders or mentors who inspire and guide others. A teacher who goes above and beyond to support their students exemplifies this ideal.
    • The "universal cow" symbolizes resources that provide sustenance. In modern terms, this could represent sustainable practices in agriculture that ensure food security for communities.

  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of collaboration, skill, and the pursuit of prosperity. It encourages individuals to strive for excellence in their endeavors while recognizing the value of community and support.
    • In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as teamwork in professional settings, nurturing relationships in personal life, and fostering sustainable practices in society. By embodying the principles outlined in this mantra, individuals can contribute to a more prosperous and harmonious world.
