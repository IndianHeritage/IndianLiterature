दूरादिहेव यत् सत्य अरुणाप्सुर अशिश्वितत् |विभानुं विश्वधातनत् ||
dūrād iheva yat saty aruṇapsur aśiśvitat | vi bhānuṁ viśvadhātanat ||
  • Mantra: "dūrād iheva yat saty aruṇapsur aśiśvitat | vi bhānuṁ viśvadhātanat ||"
  • Translation: "From afar, that which is true, the radiant one, has been established; may it shine forth in all directions."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • dūrād: From afar or distant.
    • iheva: Here itself or in this very place.
    • yat: That which.
    • saty: Truth or reality.
    • aruṇapsur: The radiant one, often associated with the sun or light.
    • aśiśvitat: Has been established or made firm.
    • vi: In all or everywhere.
    • bhānuṁ: Light or radiance.
    • viśvadhātanat: May it shine forth or spread in all directions.
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra speaks to the concept of truth and light emanating from a distance, suggesting that truth is not confined to a single location but is universal.
    • The phrase "from afar" indicates that truth can be recognized and appreciated even when it seems distant or unattainable.
    • The "radiant one" symbolizes enlightenment, knowledge, and the positive aspects of existence, akin to the sun that provides light and sustenance to life.
    • Establishing truth in this context means grounding oneself in reality and understanding the importance of honesty and integrity.
    • The call for this truth to "shine forth in all directions" emphasizes the need for truth to be shared and spread, impacting all aspects of life and society.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a personal context, one might reflect on the importance of honesty in relationships. Just as the sun illuminates everything around it, being truthful can foster trust and transparency.
    • In a societal context, this mantra can be applied to the pursuit of justice. Advocating for truth in legal systems ensures that justice is served and that the light of truth prevails over falsehood.
    • In the realm of science, the quest for knowledge and understanding can be seen as a pursuit of truth. Scientific discoveries often come from exploring the unknown, much like seeking light in darkness.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the significance of truth in our lives. It encourages individuals to seek and establish truth, allowing it to radiate and influence their surroundings positively.
    • In various aspects of life—personal relationships, societal justice, and the pursuit of knowledge—this mantra highlights the universal importance of truth and integrity.
    • Ultimately, it calls for a collective effort to ensure that truth is not only recognized but also shared, creating a more enlightened and harmonious existence for all.
