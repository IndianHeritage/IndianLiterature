मन्त्र: न हि वश् चरमं चन वसिष्ठः परिमंसते | अस्माकं अद्य मरुतः सुते सचा विश्वे पिबत कामिनः ||
nahi vaś caramaṁ cana vasiṣṭhaḥ parimaṁsate | asmākam adya marutaḥ sute sacā viśve pibata kāminaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • nahi: not
    • vaś: under control
    • caramaṁ: the last or the ultimate
    • cana: at all
    • vasiṣṭhaḥ: the best or the most excellent
    • parimaṁsate: to be attained or reached
    • asmākam: our
    • adya: today
    • marutaḥ: the winds or the forces of nature
    • sute: in the offerings or sacrifices
    • sacā: and
    • viśve: all
    • pibata: drink or consume
    • kāminaḥ: those who desire
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra emphasizes that the ultimate or the best cannot be controlled or attained by mere desire or effort.
    • It suggests that true fulfillment comes from the natural forces (represented by "marutaḥ") and the collective energy of all beings ("viśve").
    • The phrase "pibata kāminaḥ" indicates that those who have desires should partake in the offerings made to the divine or the natural forces, suggesting a symbiotic relationship between humans and nature.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern life, this mantra can be related to the idea of sustainability. For instance, when we engage in eco-friendly practices, we are acknowledging the power of nature and working in harmony with it rather than trying to dominate it.
    • In personal development, the mantra can be interpreted as a reminder that while we can set goals and desires, true success often comes from being in tune with our environment and the people around us, rather than forcing outcomes.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its reminder of the importance of humility and respect towards the forces of nature and the universe.
    • It teaches us that while we may have desires and ambitions, we should also recognize the limitations of our control and the value of collaboration with the world around us.
    • This perspective is applicable in various aspects of life, including environmental conservation, community building, and personal growth, encouraging a balanced approach to achieving our goals.
