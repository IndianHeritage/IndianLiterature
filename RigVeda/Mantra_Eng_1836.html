येन मानासश्चितयन्त उष्रा व्युष्टिषु शवसां शाश्वतीनाम् |स नो मरुद्भिर् वृषभ श्रवो धा उग्र उग्रेभिः स्थविरः सहोदाः ||
yena mānāsaś citayanta usrā vyuṣṭiṣu śavasā śaśvatīnām | sa no marudbhir vṛṣabha śravo dhā ugra ugrebhiḥ sthaviraḥ sahodāḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yena: by whom
    • mānasaś: of the mind
    • citayanta: who are thinking
    • usrā: of the dawn
    • vyuṣṭiṣu: in the rising
    • śavasā: with strength
    • śaśvatīnām: of the eternal
    • sa: he
    • no: our
    • marudbhir: by the winds
    • vṛṣabha: the strong
    • śravo: of fame
    • dhā: may he bestow
    • ugra: powerful
    • ugrebhiḥ: with great strength
    • sthaviraḥ: the ancient
    • sahodāḥ: with brothers
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the divine force that inspires the mind to think and act with strength and clarity.
    • The reference to "dawn" symbolizes new beginnings and enlightenment, suggesting that the divine presence guides us in our thoughts and actions.
    • The mention of "winds" indicates the dynamic and ever-changing nature of life, where the divine helps us navigate through challenges.
    • The term "strong" and "powerful" emphasizes the importance of resilience and determination in our endeavors.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, when facing a challenging project, invoking this mantra can serve as a reminder to seek clarity and strength in decision-making.
    • During personal struggles, such as health issues or emotional turmoil, reflecting on the essence of this mantra can inspire hope and the courage to overcome obstacles.
    • In educational pursuits, students can use the principles of this mantra to focus their minds and harness their inner strength to achieve their goals.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of divine guidance in our thoughts and actions, encouraging us to seek strength and clarity in our lives.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal challenges to professional endeavors, reminding us of the power of resilience and the support of the divine.
    • By reflecting on this mantra, individuals can cultivate a mindset that embraces new beginnings, fosters determination, and navigates the complexities of life with grace.
