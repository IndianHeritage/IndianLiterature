अनर्वाणो ह्येषां पन्था आदित्यनाम् |अदब्धाः सन्ति पायवः सुगेवृद्धः ||
anarvāṇo hy eṣām panthā ādityānām | adabdhāḥ santi pāyavaḥ sugevṛdhaḥ ||
  • Mantra: "anarvāṇo hy eṣām panthā ādityānām | adabdhāḥ santi pāyavaḥ sugevṛdhaḥ ||"
  • Translation: "The path of the Adityas (the solar deities) is indeed unassailable; the waters are pure and the nourishing ones are well-established."

  • Word-by-Word Explanation:
    • anarvāṇo: Unassailable, invincible, or beyond reach.
    • hy: Indeed, or certainly.
    • eṣām: Of these, referring to the Adityas.
    • panthā: Path or way.
    • ādityānām: Of the Adityas, the solar deities representing various aspects of light and life.
    • adabdhāḥ: Pure, untainted, or uncontaminated.
    • santi: Are, exist, or are present.
    • pāyavaḥ: Nourishing, referring to waters or resources that sustain life.
    • sugevṛdhaḥ: Well-established, strong, or abundant.

  • Real-Life Examples:
    • The concept of an unassailable path can be likened to the pursuit of truth and righteousness in life. For instance, individuals who adhere to ethical principles in their personal and professional lives often find that their path is clear and free from obstacles.
    • The reference to pure waters can be related to the importance of clean drinking water in our daily lives. Just as the mantra emphasizes the purity of waters, communities around the world strive to ensure access to clean water for health and well-being.
    • The nourishing aspect of the waters can be compared to the role of education and knowledge in society. Just as pure waters nourish the body, education nourishes the mind, leading to personal and societal growth.

  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of following a righteous path that is free from impurities and obstacles. It serves as a reminder that purity, whether in thoughts, actions, or resources, is essential for a fulfilling life.
    • In real-life scenarios, the teachings of this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as environmental conservation, ethical living, and the pursuit of knowledge. By striving for purity and righteousness, individuals and communities can create a more harmonious and sustainable world.
