उता त्या मे हवम आ जग्म्यात्म नासत्याऽधीभिर युवम अङ्ग विप्राः |अत्रिं न महस तमसोऽमुमुक्तं तूरवतं नराः दुरिताद अभीक ||
uta tyā me havam ā jagmyātaṁ nāsatyā dhībhir yuvam aṅga viprā | atriṁ na mahas tamaso 'mumuktaṁ tūrvataṁ narā duritād abhīke ||
  • Mantra: "uta tyā me havam ā jagmyātaṁ nāsatyā dhībhir yuvam aṅga viprā | atriṁ na mahas tamaso 'mumuktaṁ tūrvataṁ narā duritād abhīke ||"
  • Translation: "May my invocation reach you, O Nāsatyas, with your wisdom, O divine ones. Free us from the great darkness, and may we be liberated from all difficulties."

  • Word-by-Word Explanation:
    • uta: a particle indicating a wish or desire.
    • tyā: through that, or by that.
    • me: my.
    • havam: invocation or offering.
    • ā: towards or to.
    • jagmyātaṁ: may it reach or come to.
    • nāsatyā: referring to the Nāsatyas, the divine twins associated with healing and truth.
    • dhībhir: with wisdom or intellect.
    • yuvam: you (plural or dual).
    • aṅga: O dear ones or O friends.
    • viprā: the wise or learned ones.
    • atriṁ: from darkness or ignorance.
    • na: not.
    • mahas: great or vast.
    • tamaso: of darkness.
    • 'mumuktaṁ: may we be liberated or freed.
    • tūrvataṁ: from all difficulties or troubles.
    • narā: O humans or people.
    • duritād: from misfortunes or hardships.
    • abhīke: may we be free or liberated.

  • Real-Life Examples:
    • This mantra can be invoked during challenging times, such as when facing personal or professional difficulties. For instance, someone struggling with a health issue might recite this mantra to seek divine assistance and wisdom.
    • In a community setting, this mantra can be used during collective prayers or gatherings to seek guidance and support from the divine, fostering unity and strength among individuals facing common challenges.
    • Students preparing for exams or facing academic pressures can chant this mantra to invoke clarity of thought and wisdom, helping them overcome their fears and difficulties.

  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking divine guidance and wisdom in overcoming life's challenges. It serves as a reminder that, through collective invocation and faith, individuals can find strength and clarity in times of darkness.
    • Its significance lies in its universal applicability; whether in personal struggles, community challenges, or academic pressures, the essence of this mantra encourages individuals to seek help and liberation from difficulties.
    • By reciting this mantra, one can cultivate a sense of hope and resilience, reinforcing the belief that with divine support, one can navigate through life's adversities.
