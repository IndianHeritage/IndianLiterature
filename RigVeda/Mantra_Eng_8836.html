ईशे यो विश्वस्य देववीतर ईशे विश्वायुर उषसो व्युष्टौ |आ यस्मिन मनाः हविष्य अग्नाव् अरिष्टरथः स्कभ्नाति शूषैः ||
īśe yo viśvasyā devavīter īśe viśvāyur uṣaso vyuṣṭau | ā yasmin manā havīṁṣy agnāv ariṣṭarathaḥ skabhnāti śūṣaiḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • īśe: To the Lord or the Divine
    • yo: Who
    • viśvasyā: Of the universe or all
    • devavīter: Of the divine beings or gods
    • viśvāyur: Universal life or vitality
    • uṣaso: Of the dawn
    • vyuṣṭau: Rising or emerging
    • ā: To
    • yasmin: In whom
    • manā: Mind or thought
    • havīṁṣy: Offerings or sacrifices
    • agnāv: In the fire
    • ariṣṭarathaḥ: The one who is free from obstacles
    • skabhnāti: Creates or manifests
    • śūṣaiḥ: With dryness or drought
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the divine presence that governs the universe and all its beings.
    • It emphasizes the importance of the mind and thought in making offerings to the divine, suggesting that our intentions are crucial in spiritual practices.
    • The reference to the dawn symbolizes new beginnings, hope, and the emergence of light from darkness.
    • The phrase "ariṣṭarathaḥ" indicates a state of being free from obstacles, which can be interpreted as a call for divine assistance in overcoming challenges.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, individuals often face obstacles, whether in personal relationships, career challenges, or health issues. This mantra serves as a reminder to seek divine guidance and clarity in such situations.
    • For instance, a student preparing for exams may recite this mantra to focus their mind and seek divine support in overcoming the stress and challenges of studying.
    • In a corporate setting, a team facing a project deadline may use this mantra to foster unity and collective intention, invoking a sense of purpose and clarity in their efforts.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of divine connection, the power of intention, and the importance of overcoming obstacles in life.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal challenges to collective endeavors, reminding us to align our thoughts and actions with a higher purpose.
    • By invoking this mantra, individuals can cultivate a sense of hope, clarity, and resilience, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling and purposeful life.
