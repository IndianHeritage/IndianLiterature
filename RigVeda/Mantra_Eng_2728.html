महī यदि धिषणा शिश्नथे धात सद्योवृद्धं विभ्वं रोडस्योः |गिरो यस्मिन्न अनवद्याः समीचीर विश्वा इन्द्राय तविषीर अनुत्ताः ||
mahī yadi dhiṣaṇā śiśnathe dhāt sadyovṛdhaṁ vibhva1ṁ rodasyoḥ | giro yasminn anavadyāḥ samīcīr viśvā indrāya taviṣīr anuttāḥ ||
  • Translation: The mantra can be translated as: "If the earth, with its vastness, is filled with the divine energy, then may the words that are pure and unblemished come forth, and may they be directed towards Indra, the powerful one."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • mahī: Earth or the vastness of the universe.
    • dhiṣaṇā: Divine energy or consciousness.
    • śiśnathe: Filled or imbued.
    • dhāt: The essence or nature.
    • sadyovṛdhaṁ: Instantly growing or flourishing.
    • vibhvaṁ: Abundant or plentiful.
    • rodasyoḥ: Of the heavens or celestial realms.
    • giro: Words or speech.
    • yasminn: In which or where.
    • anavadyāḥ: Pure or unblemished.
    • samīcīr: To come together or unite.
    • viśvā: All or universal.
    • indrāya: To Indra, the king of gods.
    • taviṣīr: Powerful or strong.
    • anuttāḥ: Unmatched or supreme.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of a harmonious relationship with nature. For instance, when communities engage in sustainable practices, they are acknowledging the earth's abundance and divine energy.
    • The call for pure words can be seen in the context of communication in personal relationships. Honest and unblemished communication fosters trust and understanding.
    • In leadership, directing efforts towards a higher purpose, akin to addressing Indra, can inspire teams to work towards common goals with strength and unity.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for harmony between humanity and the earth, the importance of pure communication, and the pursuit of higher ideals. In real-life scenarios, it encourages individuals and communities to strive for sustainability, integrity in communication, and collective strength in leadership. By embodying these principles, we can create a more balanced and prosperous existence, reflecting the divine energy that the mantra invokes.
