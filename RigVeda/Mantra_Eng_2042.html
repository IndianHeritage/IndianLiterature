दैव्याः होताराः प्रथमā विदुष्टरा ऋजु यक्षतः सम ऋचā वपुष्टरा |देवान् यजन्ताव् ऋतुथā सम अञ्जतो नाभā पृथिव्यā अधि सानुषु त्रिषु ||
daivyā hotārā prathamā viduṣṭara ṛju yakṣataḥ sam ṛcā vapuṣṭarā | devān yajantāv ṛtuthā sam añjato nābhā pṛthivyā adhi sānuṣu triṣu ||
  • daivyā hotārā: This phrase refers to the divine priests or the celestial beings who perform rituals. In real life, this can be likened to spiritual leaders or guides who help individuals connect with the divine through various practices.
  • prathamā viduṣṭara: This means the first among the knowledgeable or wise. It emphasizes the importance of wisdom and knowledge in guiding actions. For example, in a corporate setting, a wise leader who makes informed decisions can lead a team to success.
  • ṛju yakṣataḥ: This translates to "straight and true." It signifies the importance of honesty and integrity in one's actions. In everyday life, being straightforward in communication fosters trust in relationships.
  • sam ṛcā vapuṣṭarā: This phrase can be interpreted as "with the strength of the hymns." It highlights the power of words and intentions. For instance, motivational speeches can inspire individuals to achieve their goals.
  • devān yajantāv: This means "the gods are worshipped." It reflects the idea of reverence and gratitude towards higher powers. In modern life, this can be seen in various forms of gratitude practices, such as mindfulness or meditation.
  • ṛtuthā sam añjato: This translates to "in accordance with truth." It emphasizes the significance of aligning actions with truth and righteousness. For example, ethical business practices are crucial for long-term success.
  • nābhā pṛthivyā adhi sānuṣu triṣu: This phrase refers to the connection between the heavens, earth, and the three realms of existence. It signifies the interconnectedness of all beings. In real life, this can be observed in environmental conservation efforts, where the health of the planet is seen as vital for all life forms.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of divine guidance, wisdom, integrity, and truth in our actions. It serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of all beings and the significance of reverence towards higher powers. In real-life scenarios, these principles can be applied in personal relationships, leadership, ethical practices, and environmental stewardship, fostering a harmonious existence in society.
