त्वं नो अस्याऽ उषसो व्युष्टौ त्वं सूर उदिते बोधि गोपाः |जनमेव नित्यं तनयं जुषस्व स्तोमं मे अग्ने तन्वा सुजात ||
tvaṁ no asyā uṣaso vyuṣṭau tvaṁ sūra udite bodhi gopāḥ | janmeva nityaṁ tanayaṁ juṣasva stomam me agne tanvā sujāta ||
  • tvaṁ no asyā uṣaso vyuṣṭau: This phrase translates to "You, O Dawn, are our light." Here, the speaker is addressing the personification of dawn, symbolizing new beginnings and enlightenment. In real life, dawn represents the start of a new day, a time for fresh opportunities and hope.
  • tvaṁ sūra udite bodhi gopāḥ: This means "You, O Hero, arise and grant us wisdom." The term "sūra" refers to a hero or a powerful being, while "bodhi" signifies wisdom or enlightenment. This can be related to the idea of seeking guidance from those who are knowledgeable or experienced in our lives, such as mentors or leaders.
  • janmeva nityaṁ tanayaṁ juṣasva: This translates to "May you always enjoy the offspring of life." The word "tanaya" refers to offspring or progeny, symbolizing continuity and legacy. In a real-life context, this can be seen in the importance of nurturing future generations, whether through family, education, or community involvement.
  • stomam me agne tanvā sujāta: This means "O Agni, grant me a beautiful body." Here, "Agni" represents the fire god, symbolizing energy, transformation, and purification. The request for a "beautiful body" can be interpreted as a desire for health, vitality, and the ability to perform one's duties effectively. In modern life, this can relate to the importance of physical and mental well-being.

  • Conclusion: This mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking enlightenment, wisdom, and vitality in life. It emphasizes the importance of new beginnings, the pursuit of knowledge, the nurturing of future generations, and the quest for health and well-being. In real-life scenarios, these themes are applicable in various contexts, such as personal development, education, family values, and health awareness. By embodying these principles, individuals can lead more fulfilling and purposeful lives.
