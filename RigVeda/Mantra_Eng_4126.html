प्रयाज्यवो मरुतो भ्राजदृष्टयो बृहद् वयो दधिरे रुक्मवक्षसः |ईयन्ते अश्वैः सुयमेभिर आशुभिः शुभं याताम् अनु रथा अवृत्तसत ||
prayajyavo maruto bhrājadṛṣṭayo bṛhad vayo dadhire rukmavakṣasaḥ | īyante aśvaiḥ suyamebhir āśubhiḥ śubhaṁ yātām anu rathā avṛtsata ||
  • Translation: The mantra speaks of the Maruts, the storm deities, who are described as radiant and powerful. They are invoked to bring auspiciousness and blessings as they ride on their chariots, accompanied by swift horses.
  • Word Breakdown:
    • Prayajyavo: Those who are powerful and capable.
    • Maruto: Referring to the Maruts, the storm gods associated with wind and storms.
    • Bhrājadṛṣṭayo: Those who are radiant and have a bright vision.
    • Bṛhad: Great or vast, indicating the immense power of the Maruts.
    • Vayo: Referring to their strength or might.
    • Dhadhire: They hold or possess.
    • Rukmavakṣasaḥ: With golden or shining bodies, symbolizing their divine nature.
    • Īyante: They move or travel.
    • Aśvaiḥ: With horses, indicating speed and agility.
    • Suyamebhir: With auspiciousness and good fortune.
    • Āśubhiḥ: Free from inauspiciousness or negativity.
    • Śubhaṁ: Auspicious or good.
    • Yātām: May they go or proceed.
    • Anu: Following or in accordance with.
    • Rathā: Chariots, symbolizing movement and progress.
    • Avṛtsata: They have come or arrived.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, invoking the blessings of the Maruts can symbolize the desire for swift progress and success in ventures, akin to a team working together harmoniously to achieve a common goal.
    • In personal life, this mantra can be recited during significant life events, such as weddings or new beginnings, to invite positive energy and auspiciousness into the journey ahead.
    • In environmental discussions, the Maruts can represent the forces of nature, reminding us of the importance of respecting and harmonizing with the natural world.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its invocation of divine forces to bring about auspiciousness and success. It teaches us the importance of harnessing positive energies and working in harmony with the forces around us. In real-life scenarios, whether in personal endeavors, professional pursuits, or environmental consciousness, the essence of this mantra encourages us to seek blessings for progress and positivity, reminding us that we are part of a larger cosmic order that supports our journeys.
