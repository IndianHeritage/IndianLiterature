न सṁस्कृतं प्र मिमीतो गमिष्टान्ति नूनम् अश्विनोपस्तुतेह |दिवाभिपित्वे 'वसागमिष्टा प्रत्य अवर्तिं दाशुषे शम्भविष्टा ||
na saṁskṛtam pra mimīto gamiṣṭhānti nūnam aśvinopastuteha | divābhipitve 'vasāgamiṣṭhā praty avartiṁ dāśuṣe śambhaviṣṭhā ||
  • na saṁskṛtam pra mimīto: This phrase can be interpreted as "do not let the unrefined or unprepared be neglected." It emphasizes the importance of preparation and refinement in any endeavor.
  • gamiṣṭhānti nūnam aśvinopastuteha: This part suggests that those who are prepared will surely reach their goals, akin to the Aśvins (the divine twin horsemen in Vedic mythology) who are known for their swiftness and ability to assist in achieving success.
  • divābhipitve 'vasāgamiṣṭhā: This can be understood as "in the light of day, those who are prepared will find their way." It highlights the significance of clarity and awareness in one's actions.
  • praty avartiṁ dāśuṣe śambhaviṣṭhā: This phrase indicates a return to the source or the original state, suggesting that those who are aligned with their purpose will find fulfillment and success.

The mantra as a whole conveys a profound message about the importance of preparation, clarity, and alignment with one's purpose. It encourages individuals to refine their skills and be aware of their surroundings to achieve their goals effectively.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios:

  • Education: Students who prepare thoroughly for their exams and refine their understanding of subjects are more likely to succeed.
  • Career: Professionals who continuously develop their skills and remain aware of industry trends are better positioned for advancement.
  • Personal Development: Individuals who engage in self-reflection and work on their personal growth are more likely to find fulfillment in their lives.

In conclusion, this mantra serves as a reminder of the significance of preparation, awareness, and alignment with one's goals. By embodying these principles, individuals can navigate their paths more effectively and achieve success in various aspects of life.
