परि णो याह्य अस्मयुर विश्वा वसूनी ओजसा |पाहि नः शर्म वीरवत ||
pari ṇo yāhy asmayur viśvā vasūny ojasā | pāhi naḥ śarma vīravat ||
  • Translation: The mantra "pari ṇo yāhy asmayur viśvā vasūny ojasā | pāhi naḥ śarma vīravat ||" can be translated to mean: "May all the powers of the universe come to us, and may we be protected by the strength of the brave." This invocation seeks the blessings of universal energies for protection and strength.
  • Word Breakdown:
    • pari: Around, encompassing, or all around.
    • ṇaḥ: Us, indicating a collective or community.
    • yāhy: May we go or may we be led.
    • asmayur: Of stone or solid strength, symbolizing stability and resilience.
    • viśvā: All or universal, referring to the entirety of existence.
    • vasūny: Wealth or resources, often interpreted as blessings or abundance.
    • ojasā: With strength or energy, indicating vitality and power.
    • pāhi: Protect or guard, a plea for safeguarding.
    • naḥ: Us, again emphasizing the collective aspect.
    • śarma: Comfort, peace, or shelter, representing security and well-being.
    • vīravat: With bravery or valor, invoking courage and strength.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community setting, this mantra can be recited during gatherings or festivals to invoke collective strength and protection for all members, ensuring a harmonious environment.
    • In personal life, individuals may chant this mantra before embarking on a challenging task, such as starting a new job or undertaking a significant project, to seek strength and protection from universal energies.
    • In times of crisis, such as natural disasters or personal hardships, this mantra can serve as a source of comfort and a reminder of the collective strength that can be harnessed through unity and bravery.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for universal support and protection. It emphasizes the importance of community, strength, and bravery in facing life's challenges. In real-life scenarios, whether in personal endeavors or communal activities, invoking this mantra can foster a sense of unity and resilience, reminding individuals that they are not alone in their struggles and that collective strength can lead to overcoming obstacles. It serves as a powerful reminder of the interconnectedness of all beings and the support that can be drawn from the universe when one seeks it with sincerity and courage.
