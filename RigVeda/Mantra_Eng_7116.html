अप्रामिसत्य मघवन तथेद असद इन्द्र क्रत्वा यथा वशः |सनेमा वाजं तव शिप्रिन्न अवसाम कक्षू चिद यन्तो अद्रिवः ||
aprāmisatya maghavan tathed asad indra kratvā yathā vaśaḥ | sanema vājaṁ tava śiprinn avasā makṣū cid yanto adrivaḥ ||
  • Mantra: "aprāmisatya maghavan tathed asad indra kratvā yathā vaśaḥ | sanema vājaṁ tava śiprinn avasā makṣū cid yanto adrivaḥ ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • aprāmisatya: Not deviating from truth
    • maghavan: O Maghavan (a name for Indra, the king of gods)
    • tathā: Thus
    • asad: Falsehood or untruth
    • indra: Indra, the god of rain and thunderstorms
    • kratvā: By the power or strength
    • yathā: As
    • vaśaḥ: Under control or in command
    • sanema: We may attain
    • vājam: Wealth or strength
    • tava: Your
    • śiprinn: Swift or quick
    • avasā: May we be protected
    • makṣū: From obstacles
    • cid: Indeed
    • yanto: Those who go
    • adrivaḥ: To the path of truth
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of adhering to truth and righteousness.
    • It invokes Indra, symbolizing strength and power, to help overcome falsehood and obstacles.
    • The phrase "not deviating from truth" suggests that one should remain steadfast in their principles, much like a person who stands firm in their beliefs despite societal pressures.
    • The call for protection from obstacles can be likened to seeking guidance in challenging times, such as during a difficult decision in one's career or personal life.
    • In a real-life scenario, this mantra can be applied when facing ethical dilemmas, encouraging individuals to choose the path of honesty and integrity.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the significance of truth and integrity in our lives.
    • It highlights the need for divine support in overcoming challenges and maintaining one's values.
    • In today's world, where misinformation can easily spread, this mantra encourages individuals to seek the truth and act with righteousness.
    • Ultimately, it teaches that by aligning ourselves with truth and seeking strength from higher powers, we can navigate life's complexities with clarity and purpose.
