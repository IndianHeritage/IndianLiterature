स मर्मृजान आयुभिः प्रयस्वान प्रयसे हितः |इन्दुर अत्यो विचक्षणः ||
sa marmṛjāna āyubhiḥ prayasvān prayase hitaḥ | indur atyo vicakṣaṇaḥ ||
  • sa marmṛjāna: This phrase can be interpreted as "he who is the remover of obstacles" or "he who clears the path." In real life, this can refer to individuals or forces that help us overcome challenges, such as mentors, friends, or even technology that simplifies our tasks.
  • āyubhiḥ: This word translates to "with life" or "with longevity." It emphasizes the importance of life and vitality. For example, in health and wellness, practices like yoga and meditation are aimed at enhancing one's life span and quality of life.
  • prayasvān: This term means "the one who is energetic" or "the one who strives." It highlights the value of effort and determination. In a real-world context, this can be seen in athletes who train rigorously to achieve their goals or entrepreneurs who work tirelessly to build their businesses.
  • prayase hitaḥ: This phrase can be understood as "the one who is beneficial through effort." It suggests that the results of hard work lead to positive outcomes. For instance, students who dedicate time to study often achieve better grades, which can lead to future opportunities.
  • indur: This word means "the moon," often symbolizing calmness, serenity, and guidance. In life, the moon can represent a guiding force during difficult times, much like a mentor or a supportive friend who provides clarity and direction.
  • atyo: This term translates to "beyond" or "exceeding." It suggests going beyond the ordinary limits. In practical terms, this can be seen in individuals who push the boundaries of innovation, such as scientists and artists who create groundbreaking work.
  • vicakṣaṇaḥ: This word means "wise" or "perceptive." It emphasizes the importance of wisdom in decision-making. In everyday life, wise choices often lead to better outcomes, whether in personal relationships, career paths, or financial decisions.

Conclusion: The mantra "sa marmṛjāna āyubhiḥ prayasvān prayase hitaḥ | indur atyo vicakṣaṇaḥ ||" encapsulates the essence of overcoming obstacles through energetic effort, leading to beneficial outcomes in life. It signifies the importance of wisdom, vitality, and perseverance in achieving success. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire individuals to strive for their goals, seek guidance, and make wise choices, ultimately leading to a fulfilling and prosperous life. Its significance lies in the reminder that with effort and wisdom, one can navigate life's challenges and emerge victorious.
