आ धूर्ष्व अस्मै दधाताश्वान इन्द्रो न वज्री हिरण्यबाहुः ||
ā dhūrṣv asmai dadhātāśvān indro na vajrī hiraṇyabāhuḥ ||
  • Word Breakdown:
    • ā: A prefix meaning "towards" or "to."
    • dhūrṣv: Derived from "dhū," meaning "to give" or "to bestow."
    • asmai: Meaning "to him" or "to that one."
    • dadhātā: From the root "dadhā," meaning "to place" or "to establish."
    • śvān: Referring to "horses," symbolizing strength and speed.
    • indro: Referring to "Indra," the king of gods, associated with power and rain.
    • na: Meaning "not."
    • vajrī: Meaning "one who wields the thunderbolt," symbolizing strength and protection.
    • hiraṇyabāhuḥ: Meaning "golden-armed," symbolizing wealth and prosperity.
  • Meaning:
    • This mantra can be interpreted as a prayer or invocation to Indra, asking him to bestow strength and resources (symbolized by horses) upon the devotee.
    • It emphasizes the importance of divine support in achieving success and overcoming obstacles.
    • The mention of "golden-armed" Indra signifies the wealth and prosperity that come from divine blessings.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be seen as a call for divine guidance and support in achieving success. Entrepreneurs often seek blessings for strength and resources to navigate challenges.
    • In personal life, individuals may recite this mantra during times of difficulty, seeking the strength to overcome obstacles and the prosperity to improve their circumstances.
    • In sports, athletes may invoke this mantra to gain the strength and speed needed to excel in their performance, symbolizing the desire for divine intervention in achieving victory.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its invocation of divine strength and resources, highlighting the belief in a higher power that supports individuals in their endeavors.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal challenges to professional pursuits, reminding us of the importance of seeking support and strength beyond our own capabilities.
    • Ultimately, it encourages a mindset of resilience and faith, fostering a connection with the divine in our journey towards success and prosperity.
