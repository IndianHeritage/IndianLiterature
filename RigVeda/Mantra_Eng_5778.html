व्यूषा आवः पथ्या जनानाम् पञ्च क्षितीर मानवशीर बोधयन्ती |सुसन्दृग्भिर उक्षभिर भानुम अश्रेद् वि सूर्यो रोधसी चक्षसावः ||
vy u1ṣā āvaḥ pathyā3 janānām pañca kṣitīr mānuṣīr bodhayantī | susaṁdṛgbhir ukṣabhir bhānum aśred vi sūryo rodasī cakṣasāvaḥ ||
  • vy u1ṣā āvaḥ: This phrase can be translated as "O Dawn, please come." Here, "uṣā" refers to the dawn, symbolizing new beginnings and the arrival of light after darkness.
  • pathyā3 janānām: This means "on the path of the people." It emphasizes the importance of guiding humanity along the right path, suggesting that the dawn brings clarity and direction.
  • pañca kṣitīr mānuṣīr bodhayantī: This translates to "the five earthly realms awaken the humans." The "five earthly realms" can be interpreted as the various aspects of life—physical, emotional, intellectual, social, and spiritual—that the dawn illuminates, encouraging human awareness and growth.
  • susaṁdṛgbhir ukṣabhir: This phrase means "with beautiful and auspicious rays." It highlights the positive and nurturing qualities of the dawn, suggesting that the light of the sun brings hope and prosperity.
  • bhānum aśred: This translates to "the sun shines brightly." The sun is often seen as a source of life and energy, representing vitality and strength.
  • vi sūryo rodasī cakṣasāvaḥ: This means "may the sun open our eyes." It signifies the desire for enlightenment and understanding, urging individuals to see the truth and wisdom that the dawn brings.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda encapsulates the essence of dawn as a powerful symbol of renewal, guidance, and enlightenment. In real life, it can be applied to various scenarios such as starting a new day with optimism, seeking clarity in decision-making, or embarking on new ventures. The dawn serves as a reminder that every day is an opportunity for growth and awakening, encouraging individuals to embrace the light of knowledge and understanding in their lives. By invoking the qualities of the dawn, we can aspire to lead lives filled with purpose and awareness, navigating the complexities of existence with clarity and insight.
