अव स्म यस्य वेषणे स्वेदं पथिषु जुह्वति |अभिम् अह स्वजेन्यम भूमाऽपृष्ठेव रुरुहुः ||
ava sma yasya veṣaṇe svedam pathiṣu juhvati | abhīm aha svajenyam bhūmā pṛṣṭheva ruruhuḥ ||
  • Mantra: "ava sma yasya veṣaṇe svedam pathiṣu juhvati | abhīm aha svajenyam bhūmā pṛṣṭheva ruruhuḥ ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ava: down, towards, or in the direction of
    • sma: indeed, certainly
    • yasya: of whom, whose
    • veṣaṇe: in the manifestation, in the form
    • svedam: sweat, moisture, or effort
    • pathiṣu: on the paths, in the journeys
    • juhvati: they offer, they invoke
    • abhīm: fearless, without fear
    • aha: I, me
    • svajenyam: my own, belonging to me
    • bhūmā: the earth, the ground
    • pṛṣṭheva: like the back, as if on the back
    • ruruhuḥ: they roar, they cry out
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks to the idea of invoking strength and fearlessness in the face of challenges.
    • The phrase "ava sma" suggests a call to action, urging one to move forward with determination.
    • "yasya veṣaṇe" indicates the importance of one's form or manifestation, emphasizing the need to embrace one's identity.
    • The mention of "svedam pathiṣu juhvati" reflects the effort and sweat put into one's journey, highlighting the value of hard work.
    • "abhīm aha svajenyam" conveys a sense of personal ownership and confidence, asserting that one is in control of their destiny.
    • Finally, "bhūmā pṛṣṭheva ruruhuḥ" symbolizes the connection to the earth and the power that comes from it, suggesting that one can draw strength from their roots.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, this mantra can inspire individuals to take bold steps in their careers, embracing their unique skills and experiences.
    • In personal development, it encourages people to confront their fears and challenges head-on, fostering resilience and determination.
    • In community building, it highlights the importance of collective effort and support, as individuals work together towards common goals.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the strength that comes from self-awareness, hard work, and fearlessness.
    • Its significance lies in its ability to motivate individuals to embrace their true selves and face life's challenges with courage.
    • In real-life scenarios, it can be applied in various contexts, from personal growth to professional endeavors, encouraging a proactive and fearless approach to life.
