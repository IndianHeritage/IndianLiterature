न ते अन्तः शवसो धाय्य अस्य विं तु बाबधे रोडसी महित्वा |आ ता सूर्यः पृणति तूतुजानो यूथेवाप्सु समीजमान ऊती ||
na te antaḥ śavaso dhāyy asya vi tu bābadhe rodasī mahitvā | ā tā sūriḥ pṛṇati tūtujāno yūthevāpsu samījamāna ūtī ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • na: not
    • te: your
    • antaḥ: end
    • śavaso: of the dead
    • dhāyy: thinking
    • asya: of this
    • vi: indeed
    • tu: but
    • bābadhe: does not bind
    • rodasī: the two worlds
    • mahitvā: greatness
    • ā: from
    • tā: that
    • sūriḥ: the sun
    • pṛṇati: fills
    • tūtujāno: the one who is wise
    • yūthevā: like a group
    • apsu: in the waters
    • samījamāna: gathering
    • ūtī: strength
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the transcendence of the soul beyond the physical realm.
    • It emphasizes that the essence of life and consciousness is not bound by death or the material world.
    • The reference to the sun symbolizes enlightenment, wisdom, and the source of life, suggesting that true understanding comes from a higher perspective.
    • The phrase "like a group in the waters" indicates the interconnectedness of all beings and the collective strength that arises from unity.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In moments of grief, this mantra can serve as a reminder that death is not the end, and the spirit continues to exist in a different form.
    • In a team setting, the idea of collective strength can be applied to workplaces where collaboration leads to greater achievements.
    • In personal development, seeking wisdom and enlightenment can guide individuals to make better life choices and foster a deeper understanding of their purpose.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of understanding life beyond physical existence and encourages individuals to seek wisdom and unity.
    • Its application in real life can be seen in how we cope with loss, work together in teams, and strive for personal growth.
    • Ultimately, it serves as a reminder of the eternal nature of the soul and the importance of collective strength in navigating life's challenges.
