युवोर अश्विनाऽ वपुषे युवायुजं रथं वाणी येमतुर अस्य शर्ध्यम् |आ वाम पतित्वं सख्याय जग्मुषी योषावृणीत जेन्या युवाम पती ||
yuvor aśvinā vapuṣe yuvāyujaṁ rathaṁ vāṇī yematur asya śardhyam | ā vām patitvaṁ sakhyāya jagmuṣī yoṣāvṛṇīta jenyā yuvām patī ||
  • yuvor aśvinā vapuṣe: This phrase refers to the youthful forms of the Aśvins, the twin deities associated with health, medicine, and the dawn. The term "yuvor" signifies youthfulness, which is often associated with vitality and energy.
  • yuvāyujaṁ rathaṁ: Here, "yuvāyujaṁ" means "young and energetic," while "rathaṁ" refers to a chariot. This symbolizes the journey of life, where youth and energy are essential for progress and success.
  • vāṇī yematur asya śardhyam: "Vāṇī" means speech or communication, and "yematur" refers to wisdom or knowledge. This part emphasizes the importance of wise communication in achieving harmony and understanding in relationships.
  • ā vām patitvaṁ sakhyāya: "Ā vām" translates to "to you both," and "patitvaṁ" means "to be united." This suggests a call for unity and friendship, highlighting the significance of companionship in life.
  • jagmuṣī yoṣāvṛṇīta: "Jagmuṣī" means "to go forth," and "yoṣāvṛṇīta" refers to the choice of a woman. This indicates the importance of making choices in life, particularly in relationships and partnerships.
  • jenyā yuvām patī: "Jenyā" means "to be blessed," and "yuvām patī" refers to the youthful lord or protector. This signifies the blessings that come from youthful energy and the protection that comes from strong relationships.

  • Conclusion: This mantra emphasizes the significance of youth, energy, wise communication, unity, and the importance of making choices in relationships. In real life, it can be applied to various scenarios such as:
    • In personal relationships, fostering open communication and unity can lead to stronger bonds.
    • In professional settings, the energy and vitality of youth can drive innovation and progress.
    • In community building, the mantra encourages collaboration and friendship, leading to a harmonious society.
  • Overall, the mantra serves as a reminder of the values that contribute to a fulfilling and successful life, highlighting the interplay between youth, wisdom, and relationships.
