प्र तं इन्द्र नशीमहि रयिं गोमन्तम अश्विनम् |प्र ब्रह्म पूर्वचित्तये ||
pra tam indra naśīmahi rayiṁ gomantam aśvinam | pra brahma pūrvacittaye ||
  • Translation: "We invoke Indra, the powerful one, for wealth and prosperity, the one who is like the Aśvins. We invoke Brahman for the purpose of our previous thoughts."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • pra: A prefix meaning "forth" or "forward," indicating a call or invocation.
    • tam: Refers to "that" or "him," pointing towards a specific deity or concept.
    • indra: The king of gods in Vedic tradition, symbolizing strength, power, and leadership.
    • naśīmahi: A form of the verb meaning "we invoke" or "we call upon."
    • rayiṁ: Refers to wealth, prosperity, or resources.
    • gomantam: Literally means "possessor of cows," symbolizing abundance and prosperity.
    • aśvinam: Refers to the Aśvins, twin deities associated with health, medicine, and well-being.
    • brahma: Represents the ultimate reality or cosmic spirit in Vedic philosophy.
    • pūrvacittaye: Meaning "for the purpose of our previous thoughts," indicating a connection to past intentions or desires.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, invoking Indra for wealth can be likened to seeking divine guidance for success in ventures, much like entrepreneurs often seek inspiration from mentors or role models.
    • The reference to the Aśvins can be seen in modern healthcare practices, where people seek holistic well-being through both traditional and modern medicine, reflecting the duality of health and prosperity.
    • In personal development, the invocation of Brahman for previous thoughts can be related to mindfulness practices, where individuals reflect on their past intentions to align their current actions with their goals.
  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of invoking divine forces for prosperity and well-being. It highlights the interconnectedness of wealth, health, and spiritual awareness. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios such as business, health, and personal growth, reminding us to seek guidance and reflect on our intentions as we strive for success and fulfillment.
