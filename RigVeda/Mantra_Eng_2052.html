आ यो वनातातृषाणो न भाति वार्ण न पथाऽरथ्येव स्वानीत् |कृष्णाध्वा तपूरण्वश्चिकेत द्यौरिव स्मयमानो नभोभिः ||
ā yo vanā tātṛṣāṇo na bhāti vār ṇa pathā rathyeva svānīt | kṛṣṇādhvā tapū raṇvaś ciketa dyaur iva smayamāno nabhobhiḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā yo: He who
    • vanā: from the forest
    • tātṛṣāṇo: who is thirsty
    • na bhāti: does not shine
    • vār: like the rain
    • ṇa: not
    • pathā: on the path
    • rathyeva: like a chariot
    • svānīt: drawn by horses
    • kṛṣṇādhvā: the dark path
    • tapū: the heat
    • raṇvaś: of the battle
    • ciketa: he who knows
    • dyaur: the sky
    • iva: like
    • smayamāno: smiling
    • nabhobhiḥ: with the clouds
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of a person who is in search of fulfillment and sustenance, akin to someone who is thirsty in a forest.
    • The phrase "does not shine like the rain" suggests that without the right conditions or support, one's efforts may go unnoticed or unfulfilled.
    • The imagery of a chariot drawn by horses indicates the importance of guidance and direction in life.
    • The "dark path" and "heat of battle" symbolize the challenges and struggles one faces in pursuit of their goals.
    • Finally, the reference to the sky and clouds evokes a sense of hope and the potential for joy, even amidst difficulties.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • A student striving for academic success may feel overwhelmed and unrecognized, much like the thirsty person in the forest. They need support from teachers and peers to shine.
    • An entrepreneur may face numerous challenges in their journey, akin to the "heat of battle," but with perseverance and the right guidance, they can navigate their path successfully.
    • In personal relationships, individuals may feel unappreciated or lost, but with open communication and understanding, they can find joy and connection, similar to the clouds bringing rain.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking support and guidance in life's journey.
    • It reminds us that challenges are a part of life, but with knowledge and perseverance, we can find fulfillment and joy.
    • In real-life scenarios, whether in education, business, or personal relationships, recognizing the need for support and maintaining hope can lead to success and happiness.
