पाहि नो अग्नि रक्षसो अजुष्टात पाहि धूर्तेर अररुषो अघायोः |त्वा युजा पृथनायूम् अभि श्याम् ||
pāhi no agne rakṣaso ajuṣṭāt pāhi dhūrter araruṣo aghāyoḥ | tvā yujā pṛtanāyūm̐r abhi ṣyām ||
  • pāhi no agne: This phrase translates to "O Agni, protect us." Agni, the fire god, symbolizes energy, transformation, and purification. In real life, this can be seen as a call for protection and guidance in our endeavors, much like how we seek the support of mentors or leaders in our lives.
  • rakṣaso ajuṣṭāt: This means "from the evil ones." It signifies a plea for safety from negative influences or harmful entities. In contemporary terms, this can relate to protecting oneself from toxic relationships or environments that hinder personal growth.
  • pāhi dhūrter araruṣo aghāyoḥ: This translates to "protect us from the dangers of the world." Here, "dhūrter" refers to the challenges and obstacles we face. In everyday life, this can be interpreted as a request for resilience against life's adversities, such as financial struggles or health issues.
  • tvā yujā pṛtanāyūm̐r abhi ṣyām: This means "we seek to unite with you." It expresses a desire for connection and alignment with divine energy. In practical terms, this can be seen as striving for harmony in our lives, whether through community involvement, spiritual practices, or personal relationships.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking protection and guidance from higher powers, represented by Agni. It highlights the need for resilience against negativity and challenges in life. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applicable in moments of uncertainty, encouraging individuals to seek support, foster positive relationships, and maintain a connection with their inner strength and community. By invoking this mantra, one can cultivate a sense of safety and purpose, ultimately leading to personal growth and fulfillment.
