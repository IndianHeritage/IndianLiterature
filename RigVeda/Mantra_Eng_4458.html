आ यस्मिन त्वे स्वपाके यजत्र यक्षद राजन सर्वतातेव नु द्यौः |त्रिषधस्थस ततरुषो न जंहो हव्याः मघानि मानवः यजधै ||
ā yasmin tve sv apāke yajatra yakṣad rājan sarvatāteva nu dyauḥ | triṣadhasthas tataruṣo na jaṁho havyā maghāni mānuṣā yajadhyai ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā - from
    • yasmin - in which
    • tve - you
    • sv - your own
    • apāke - in the sacrifice
    • yajatra - the one who performs the sacrifice
    • yakṣad - may you grant
    • rājan - O King
    • sarvatā - in all ways
    • iteva - indeed
    • nu - surely
    • dyauḥ - the heavens
    • triṣadhasthas - thrice seated
    • tataruṣo - the one who is strong
    • na - not
    • jaṁho - to be disturbed
    • havyā - the offerings
    • maghāni - the great blessings
    • mānuṣā - O humans
    • yajadhyai - may you perform the sacrifice
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of performing sacrifices and rituals with sincerity and devotion.
    • It calls upon the divine to grant blessings and prosperity to those who engage in these sacred acts.
    • The phrase "O King" signifies the importance of leadership and guidance in spiritual practices.
    • The reference to "the heavens" suggests a connection between earthly actions and divine realms.
    • The mantra encourages humans to perform their duties and sacrifices without disturbance, highlighting the need for focus and dedication.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern life, this mantra can be related to the practice of mindfulness and dedication in one's work or personal life.
    • For instance, a leader in a corporate setting can draw inspiration from this mantra to lead their team with integrity and purpose.
    • Individuals engaging in community service or charitable acts can see their efforts as a form of sacrifice that brings blessings to others.
    • In personal relationships, the mantra can remind individuals to invest time and effort into nurturing their connections, akin to performing a sacred ritual.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for dedication, focus, and the performance of righteous actions.
    • It serves as a reminder that our efforts, when aligned with higher principles, can lead to blessings and prosperity.
    • In a world filled with distractions, this mantra encourages individuals to remain steadfast in their commitments, whether in personal, professional, or spiritual realms.
    • Ultimately, it highlights the interconnectedness of human actions and divine blessings, urging us to live with purpose and intention.
