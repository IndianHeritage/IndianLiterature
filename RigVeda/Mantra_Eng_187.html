मन्त्र: न हि देवो न मार्त्यो महास तव क्रतुम् परः |मरुद्भिर अग्न आ गहि ||
nahi devo na martyo mahas tava kratum paraḥ | marudbhir agna ā gahi ||
  • Mantra: "nahi devo na martyo mahas tava kratum paraḥ | marudbhir agna ā gahi ||"
  • Translation: "Neither the gods nor mortals can surpass your great resolve; O Agni, come to us with the Maruts."
  • Explanation:
    • nahi: This word means "not" or "neither," indicating a negation.
    • devo: Refers to "gods," representing divine beings or higher powers.
    • na: Another negation, reinforcing the idea of "not."
    • martyo: Means "mortals," referring to human beings or earthly creatures.
    • mahas: Translates to "great" or "mighty," emphasizing the strength of the resolve being discussed.
    • tava: Means "your," indicating possession or belonging to Agni.
    • kratum: Refers to "resolve" or "intention," highlighting the power of will and determination.
    • paraḥ: Means "beyond" or "surpassing," suggesting that nothing can exceed Agni's resolve.
    • marudbhir: Refers to the "Maruts," the storm deities or winds, symbolizing strength and energy.
    • agna: Means "O Agni," addressing the fire god, who represents transformation and purification.
    • ā gahi: Translates to "come to us," a call for divine presence and assistance.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In challenging situations, individuals often rely on their inner strength and resolve, much like the mantra suggests that nothing can surpass Agni's great resolve. For instance, a student preparing for a difficult exam may draw upon their determination to succeed, believing that their effort will lead to success.
    • In leadership, a strong leader inspires their team by demonstrating unwavering resolve in the face of adversity, akin to how Agni's resolve is unmatched. For example, during a crisis, a leader who remains calm and focused can motivate their team to overcome challenges.
    • In spiritual practices, invoking the energy of Agni and the Maruts can symbolize the call for transformation and support in one's life journey. People may light a fire during rituals to represent the purification of intentions and the arrival of divine energy.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its emphasis on the power of resolve and the divine support that can be called upon in times of need. It teaches us that both divine and human efforts are essential in overcoming challenges. In real life, this mantra can inspire individuals to harness their inner strength and seek support from higher powers, whether through prayer, meditation, or community. It serves as a reminder that with determination and the right intentions, one can navigate through life's adversities successfully.
