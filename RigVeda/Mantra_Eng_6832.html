या आस्व अत्क आशये विश्वा जातान्य एषाम् |परि धामानि मर्मृषद वरुणस्य पुरो गये विश्वे देवाः अनु व्रतं नभन्ताम् अन्यके समे ||
ya āsv atka āśaye viśvā jātāny eṣām | pari dhāmāni marmṛśad varuṇasya puro gaye viśve devā anu vrataṁ nabhantām anyake same ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ya: who
    • āsv: in the horses (or steeds)
    • atka: to the one who is strong
    • āśaye: in the abode
    • viśvā: all
    • jātāny: born or created
    • eṣām: of these
    • pari: around
    • dhāmāni: the abodes
    • marmṛśad: who has perceived
    • varuṇasya: of Varuna (the god of cosmic order)
    • puro: in front
    • gaye: in the song
    • viśve: all
    • devā: the gods
    • anu: following
    • vrataṁ: the vow or commitment
    • nabhantām: to the heavens
    • anyake: to others
    • same: in harmony
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the interconnectedness of all beings and the divine order that governs the universe.
    • It emphasizes the importance of unity and harmony among all creatures, suggesting that all beings are born from the same source and should strive to live in accordance with cosmic principles.
    • The reference to Varuna highlights the significance of moral and ethical conduct, as Varuna is often associated with truth and cosmic law.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community, when individuals work together towards a common goal, they embody the essence of this mantra. For instance, during a natural disaster, people often come together to help each other, reflecting the unity and harmony described.
    • In environmental conservation efforts, the mantra can be seen in action as people recognize their shared responsibility to protect the earth, acknowledging that all life is interconnected.
    • In personal relationships, practicing empathy and understanding aligns with the teachings of this mantra, as it encourages individuals to see themselves in others and act with compassion.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for unity, ethical living, and respect for the cosmic order.
    • It serves as a reminder that our actions impact not only ourselves but also the larger community and the environment.
    • In daily life, applying the principles of this mantra can lead to more harmonious relationships, a greater sense of community, and a deeper understanding of our place in the universe.
