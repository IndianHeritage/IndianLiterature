उषा आ भाहि भानुना चन्द्रेण दुतर दिवः |आवाहन्ती भूय अस्मभ्यं सौभागं व्युचन्ती दिविष्टिषु ||
uṣa ā bhāhi bhānunā candreṇa duhitar divaḥ | āvahantī bhūry asmabhyaṁ saubhagaṁ vyucchantī diviṣṭiṣu ||
  • uṣa: This word refers to the dawn or the early morning light. In many cultures, dawn symbolizes new beginnings and hope.
  • ā bhāhi: This phrase can be translated as "may you shine forth" or "may you illuminate." It expresses a desire for brightness and clarity.
  • bhānunā: This term means "with the light of the sun." It emphasizes the connection between the dawn and the sun's rays, which bring warmth and life.
  • candreṇa: This word means "with the moon." It signifies the balance between the sun and the moon, representing different aspects of life and nature.
  • duhitar divaḥ: This phrase translates to "the daughter of the sky." It personifies the dawn as a divine feminine figure, suggesting nurturing and care.
  • āvahantī: This means "bringing forth" or "conveying." It indicates the act of delivering blessings or good fortune.
  • bhūry: This word means "abundance" or "plenty." It signifies the richness of life and the blessings one can receive.
  • asmabhyaṁ: This translates to "to us." It indicates a personal connection, as if the blessings are being directed towards the speaker and their community.
  • saubhagaṁ: This term means "good fortune" or "prosperity." It reflects the desire for success and well-being.
  • vyucchantī: This means "dispelling" or "removing." It suggests the act of clearing away negativity or obstacles.
  • diviṣṭiṣu: This word refers to "in the heavens" or "in the celestial realms." It connects the blessings to a higher spiritual plane.

Conclusion: The mantra invokes the dawn as a divine feminine force, asking for the blessings of abundance and prosperity to be brought forth into our lives. It emphasizes the importance of light, both from the sun and the moon, symbolizing balance and harmony. In real life, this mantra can be applied during moments of new beginnings, such as starting a new project, entering a new phase in life, or seeking clarity in difficult situations. By reciting this mantra, individuals can cultivate a mindset of hope and positivity, inviting good fortune and dispelling negativity from their lives.
