तुचे तनाया तत् सु नो द्राघीय आयुर जीवसे |आदित्याशः सुमहसः कृणोतन ||
tuce tanāya tat su no drāghīya āyur jīvase | ādityāsaḥ sumahasaḥ kṛṇotana ||
  • tuce tanāya: This phrase can be interpreted as "to you, the body" or "to your body." It signifies a connection to the physical form and the importance of health and vitality.
  • tat su no drāghīya āyur jīvase: This translates to "may that (health) grant us a long life." It emphasizes the desire for longevity and well-being, suggesting that a healthy body is essential for a fulfilling life.
  • ādityāsaḥ: Referring to the "sons of Aditi," this term symbolizes the celestial beings or deities associated with the sun, representing light, energy, and life-giving properties.
  • sumahasaḥ: This means "greatly radiant" or "of great brilliance," indicating the powerful and positive influence of the sun and its energy on life.
  • kṛṇotana: This can be understood as "may they bestow" or "may they grant," suggesting a prayer or invocation for blessings and support from the divine forces.

  • Real-Life Example: Consider the importance of sunlight in our daily lives. Just as the sun provides energy and sustenance to the earth, this mantra reminds us to seek health and vitality through our connection to nature and the universe.
  • Health and Longevity: In modern life, we often overlook the significance of maintaining our physical health. This mantra serves as a reminder to prioritize our well-being through proper nutrition, exercise, and mindfulness, which can lead to a longer, more fulfilling life.
  • Spiritual Connection: The invocation of celestial beings encourages individuals to cultivate a spiritual practice, whether through meditation, yoga, or other forms of self-reflection, to enhance their overall well-being.

  • Conclusion: The mantra "tuce tanāya tat su no drāghīya āyur jīvase | ādityāsaḥ sumahasaḥ kṛṇotana ||" encapsulates the essence of seeking health, longevity, and divine blessings. Its significance lies in the reminder to honor our bodies, connect with the natural world, and embrace the radiant energy of life. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire individuals to adopt healthier lifestyles, foster spiritual growth, and appreciate the interconnectedness of all living beings.
