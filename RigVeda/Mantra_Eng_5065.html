विष्वे यद वाम मंहनाऽ मन्दमानाः क्षत्रं देवासो अदधुः सजोषाः |परी यद भूतो रोधसी चिद उर्वी सन्ति स्पशो अदब्धासो अमूराः ||
viśve yad vām maṁhanā mandamānāḥ kṣatraṁ devāso adadhuḥ sajoṣāḥ | pari yad bhūtho rodasī cid urvī santi spaśo adabdhāso amūrāḥ ||
  • Translation: "All the gods, having given you strength, have bestowed upon you the power of sovereignty. As the earth and the sky are filled with the essence of life, so too are the unblemished beings present everywhere."
  • Word-by-Word Explanation:
    • viśve: All, universal
    • yad: That which
    • vām: You (plural)
    • maṁhanā: Strength, power
    • mandamānāḥ: Bestowed, conferred
    • kṣatraṁ: Sovereignty, authority
    • devāso: Gods, divine beings
    • adadhuḥ: They have given
    • sajoṣāḥ: With joy, in harmony
    • pari: Around, in all directions
    • yad: That which
    • bhūtho: Existing, being
    • rodasī: Sky, atmosphere
    • cid: Consciousness, awareness
    • urvī: Earth, land
    • santi: Are, exist
    • spaśo: Unblemished, pure
    • adabdhāso: Unblemished, without defect
    • amūrāḥ: Beings, entities
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of community and collective strength. For instance, in a workplace, when team members support each other, they can achieve greater success together than individually.
    • The idea of divine beings bestowing power can be seen in various cultures where leaders are viewed as chosen or blessed by a higher power, reinforcing their authority and responsibility.
    • The connection between the earth and sky symbolizes the interdependence of nature and humanity. For example, environmental conservation efforts highlight how protecting the earth ensures a sustainable future for all living beings.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its reminder of the interconnectedness of all beings and the collective strength that comes from unity. It encourages individuals to recognize their power and responsibility within their communities and the larger world. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various contexts, such as teamwork, leadership, and environmental stewardship, promoting harmony and collaboration for a better future.
