इमं अञ्जस्पां उभये अकृण्वत धर्माणम् अग्निं विदथस्य साधनम् |अक्तुं न यह्वम् उषसः पुरोहितं तनूनपतं अरुषस्य निंसते ||
imam añjaspām ubhaye akṛṇvata dharmāṇam agniṁ vidathasya sādhanam | aktuṁ na yahvam uṣasaḥ purohitaṁ tanūnapātam aruṣasya niṁsate ||
  • Translation: This mantra can be translated as: "May we, through our actions, create the twofold path of righteousness, O Agni, the facilitator of our endeavors. May we not be hindered by the dawn, O priest, who brings forth the light of the red dawn."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • imam añjaspām: "this twofold path" - refers to the dual nature of righteousness and duty.
    • ubhaye akṛṇvata: "may we create both" - emphasizes the importance of action in creating a balanced life.
    • dharmāṇam: "of righteousness" - signifies the moral and ethical duties one must follow.
    • agniṁ vidathasya sādhanam: "O Agni, the facilitator of our endeavors" - Agni represents the divine fire, symbolizing transformation and the energy needed to achieve goals.
    • aktuṁ na yahvam: "may we not be hindered" - a plea for strength and perseverance.
    • uṣasaḥ purohitaṁ: "O priest of the dawn" - refers to the guiding force that leads us into new beginnings.
    • tanūnapātam: "who brings forth the light" - symbolizes enlightenment and knowledge.
    • aruṣasya niṁsate: "of the red dawn" - signifies hope and the promise of a new day.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, this mantra can be applied when embarking on a new project. The dual paths of righteousness could represent ethical business practices and innovative strategies.
    • In personal life, it can be reflected in the balance between personal desires and social responsibilities, encouraging individuals to act in ways that benefit both themselves and their communities.
    • During challenging times, invoking the strength of Agni can inspire individuals to transform their struggles into opportunities for growth and learning.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for action towards righteousness and the pursuit of goals with integrity. It serves as a reminder that while we strive for success, we must remain grounded in our ethical responsibilities. In real life, this mantra encourages individuals to embrace change, seek enlightenment, and act with purpose, ensuring that their endeavors contribute positively to the world around them.
