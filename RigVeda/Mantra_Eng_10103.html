त्वं पुरूण्य आ भरā स्वश्व्या येभिर मंसा निवचनानि शंसन |सुगेभिर विश्वा दुरिता तरेमा विदो षु ण उर्वियā गाधम अद्य ||
tvam purūṇy ā bharā svaśvyā yebhir maṁsai nivacanāni śaṁsan | sugebhir viśvā duritā tarema vido ṣu ṇa urviyā gādham adya ||
  • tvam purūṇy ā bharā svaśvyā: This phrase can be translated as "You, who are the embodiment of completeness, fill us with your essence." Here, "tvam" refers to the divine or the ultimate reality, while "purūṇy" signifies completeness or wholeness. In real life, this can be related to the idea of seeking fulfillment and purpose in life, where individuals strive to connect with something greater than themselves.
  • yebhir maṁsai nivacanāni śaṁsan: This translates to "By which we are guided and protected." The term "maṁsai" refers to the essence or substance, and "nivacanāni" means guidance or direction. This can be likened to the guidance we receive from mentors, teachers, or spiritual leaders in our lives, helping us navigate challenges and make wise decisions.
  • sugebhir viśvā duritā tarema: This means "With auspiciousness, may we overcome all obstacles." "Sugebhir" refers to auspiciousness or good fortune, while "duritā" signifies obstacles or difficulties. In a real-life context, this can be seen in the pursuit of goals, where individuals often face challenges but can find strength and support through positive thinking and community.
  • vido ṣu ṇa urviyā gādham adya: This translates to "May we attain the vastness of the earth today." "Urvi" refers to the earth or the world, and "gādham" signifies depth or richness. This can be interpreted as a call to embrace the richness of life and the world around us, encouraging individuals to explore and appreciate the diversity of experiences available to them.

  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking completeness, guidance, and auspiciousness in overcoming life's challenges. It encourages individuals to connect with the divine or a higher purpose, seek mentorship, and embrace the richness of life. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as during personal growth journeys, in educational settings, or in community-building efforts, where individuals come together to support one another in achieving their goals and overcoming obstacles.
