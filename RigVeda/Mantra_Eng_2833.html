इन्द्र प्राणो धितावानं यज्ञं विश्वेभिर देवभिः |तिर स्तवाना विश्वते ||
indra pra ṇo dhitāvānaṁ yajñaṁ viśvebhir devebhiḥ | tira stavāna viśpate ||
  • Indra: Refers to the king of the gods in Vedic tradition, symbolizing strength, power, and leadership.
  • Pra ṇo: This term can be interpreted as "of the breath" or "of the life force," indicating vitality and energy.
  • Dhitāvānaṁ: This word suggests "one who is wise" or "one who has understanding," emphasizing the importance of wisdom in leadership.
  • Yajñaṁ: Refers to a ritual or sacrifice, often performed to please the gods and ensure prosperity and harmony.
  • Viśvebhir: This means "by all" or "with all," indicating inclusivity and collective participation.
  • Devebhiḥ: Refers to "the gods," highlighting the divine aspect of the ritual and the need for divine blessings.
  • Tira: This can be interpreted as "to cross over" or "to transcend," suggesting overcoming obstacles.
  • Stavāna: This term means "praise" or "hymn," indicating the importance of expressing gratitude and reverence.
  • Viśpate: Refers to "the lord of all," symbolizing a universal authority or the ultimate reality.

The mantra can be understood as a call to Indra, the powerful leader, to bless the rituals performed with wisdom and collective effort. It emphasizes the importance of divine support in overcoming challenges and achieving success through unity and reverence.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as:

  • Leadership: Leaders can draw inspiration from Indra's qualities, striving to be wise and inclusive in their decision-making.
  • Community Efforts: The mantra encourages collective participation in community rituals or projects, fostering unity and shared purpose.
  • Overcoming Challenges: The idea of transcending obstacles can be applied in personal and professional life, motivating individuals to seek divine guidance and support.

Conclusion: This mantra signifies the importance of wisdom, collective effort, and divine blessings in achieving success and overcoming challenges. It serves as a reminder to seek guidance from higher powers while working together harmoniously in our endeavors.
