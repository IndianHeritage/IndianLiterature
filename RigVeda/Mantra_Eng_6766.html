जयतं च प्र स्तुतं च प्र चावतं प्रजां च धत्तं द्रविणं च धत्तम् |सजोषसां उषसा सूर्येण चोर्जं नो धत्तम् अश्विनाः ||
jayataṁ ca pra stutaṁ ca pra cāvatam prajāṁ ca dhattaṁ draviṇaṁ ca dhattam | sajoṣasā uṣasā sūryeṇa corjaṁ no dhattam aśvinā ||
  • Mantra: "jayataṁ ca pra stutaṁ ca pra cāvatam prajāṁ ca dhattaṁ draviṇaṁ ca dhattam | sajoṣasā uṣasā sūryeṇa corjaṁ no dhattam aśvinā ||"
  • Translation: This mantra can be translated as: "May we be victorious and praised, may we be protected and may we be endowed with progeny and wealth. With the dawn and the sun, may we not be deprived of the blessings of the Ashvins."
  • Word-by-Word Explanation:
    • jayataṁ: victorious, successful
    • pra stutaṁ: praised, honored
    • pra cāvatam: protected, safeguarded
    • prajāṁ: progeny, offspring
    • draviṇaṁ: wealth, resources
    • sajoṣasā: with joy, happiness
    • uṣasā: dawn, new beginnings
    • sūryeṇa: with the sun, light, energy
    • corjaṁ: deprived, taken away
    • no: not
    • dhattam: may we receive, may we be granted
    • aśvinā: the Ashvins, divine twin horsemen, deities of health and medicine
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a competitive environment, such as a workplace or academic setting, individuals often seek victory and recognition. This mantra emphasizes the importance of striving for success while also being acknowledged for one's efforts.
    • In family life, the desire for progeny and wealth reflects common aspirations. Many people work hard to provide for their families and ensure a prosperous future for their children.
    • The mention of dawn and the sun symbolizes new beginnings and hope. For instance, starting a new project or entering a new phase in life can be seen as a 'dawn' where one seeks blessings for success.
    • The Ashvins, known for their healing powers, remind us of the importance of health and well-being. In modern life, this can relate to the pursuit of a healthy lifestyle, seeking medical help, or engaging in wellness practices.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its holistic approach to life, encompassing success, protection, family, and health. It serves as a reminder to seek balance in our pursuits and to acknowledge the divine forces that aid us in our journey. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be invoked during personal challenges, family gatherings, or new ventures, reinforcing the belief that with effort and divine support, one can achieve their goals and lead a fulfilling life.
