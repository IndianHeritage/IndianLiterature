प्राचīm उ देवाश्विनā धियं मे 'मृत्ध्रां सातये कृतं वसूयुम् |विश्वा अविष्टं वाज आ पुरंधीस ता नः शक्तं शचीपति शचीभिः ||
prācīm u devāśvinā dhiyam me 'mṛdhrāṁ sātaye kṛtaṁ vasūyum | viśvā aviṣṭaṁ vāja ā puraṁdhīs tā naḥ śaktaṁ śacīpatī śacībhiḥ ||
  • Mantra: "prācīm u devāśvinā dhiyam me 'mṛdhrāṁ sātaye kṛtaṁ vasūyum | viśvā aviṣṭaṁ vāja ā puraṁdhīs tā naḥ śaktaṁ śacīpatī śacībhiḥ ||"
  • Translation: "O Ashvins, grant me the wisdom that is beneficial and leads to prosperity. May all my desires be fulfilled, and may we be empowered by the divine strength of the Shakti."

  • Explanation of Key Terms:
    • prācīm: Refers to the ancient or traditional wisdom.
    • devāśvinā: The Ashvins, twin deities associated with health, medicine, and the dawn.
    • dhiyam: Wisdom or intellect.
    • mṛdhrāṁ: Beneficial or auspicious.
    • sātaye: Leading to prosperity or success.
    • vasūyum: Wealth or abundance.
    • viśvā: All or universal.
    • aviṣṭaṁ: Fulfilled or accomplished.
    • vāja: Strength or power.
    • puraṁdhīs: Divine intelligence or wisdom.
    • śaktaṁ: Empowered or capable.
    • śacīpatī: Lord of power or strength.
    • śacībhiḥ: With the help of divine energies.

  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, individuals often seek wisdom and guidance to make decisions that lead to success. This mantra can be invoked to seek clarity and beneficial outcomes in business ventures.
    • In personal life, when facing challenges, one might recite this mantra to attract positive energy and support from the universe, similar to how people often turn to meditation or prayer for strength.
    • In healthcare, the Ashvins symbolize healing. This mantra can be used by those seeking health and wellness, invoking the divine for assistance in recovery or maintaining good health.

  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine wisdom and strength. It emphasizes the importance of seeking guidance from higher powers to achieve prosperity and fulfillment in various aspects of life. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applicable in personal growth, professional success, and health, serving as a reminder to connect with the divine for support and empowerment. By reciting this mantra, individuals can cultivate a mindset of abundance and resilience, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling life.
