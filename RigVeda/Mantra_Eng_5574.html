उतो हि वां रत्नधेयानि सन्ति पुरूणि dyāvāpṛthivī सुदासे |अस्मे धत्तं यद असद अस्कृधोयु यूम् पात स्वस्तिभिः सदा नः ||
uto hi vāṁ ratnadheyāni santi purūṇi dyāvāpṛthivī sudāse | asme dhattaṁ yad asad askṛdhoyu yūyam pāta svastibhiḥ sadā naḥ ||
  • Translation: "Indeed, there are many treasures (ratnadheyāni) that exist in the ancient (purūṇi) realms of the sky (dyāvā) and the earth (pṛthivī). May you (yūyam) bestow upon us (asme) that which is beneficial (svastibhiḥ) and may we always be protected (pāta) from harm (asad askṛdho)."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • uto hi: Indeed, truly
    • vāṁ: you (plural)
    • ratnadheyāni: treasures, valuable things
    • santi: exist, are
    • purūṇi: ancient, old
    • dyāvāpṛthivī: sky and earth
    • sudāse: beneficial, good
    • asme: for us
    • dhattaṁ: may you bestow
    • yad: that which
    • asad askṛdhoyu: from harm, from negativity
    • yūyam: you (plural)
    • pāta: protect
    • svastibhiḥ: with auspiciousness, well-being
    • sadā: always
    • naḥ: us
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • This mantra emphasizes the existence of treasures in both the physical and spiritual realms. For instance, in our daily lives, we often seek knowledge and wisdom, which can be seen as treasures that enrich our lives.
    • The call for protection and well-being can be related to the importance of community support. Just as we seek the blessings of others for our safety and prosperity, this mantra invokes a collective wish for auspiciousness.
    • In a corporate setting, this mantra can be applied when teams come together to achieve a common goal, emphasizing the importance of collaboration and the sharing of resources (treasures) for mutual benefit.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its universal appeal for well-being, protection, and the acknowledgment of the treasures that exist in our lives. It serves as a reminder to seek out and appreciate the valuable aspects of our existence, whether they be material, intellectual, or spiritual. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, from personal relationships to professional environments, highlighting the importance of community, collaboration, and the pursuit of a prosperous and harmonious life.
