उतो पितृभ्यां प्रवीदनु घोषं महो महद्भ्यां अनयन्त शूषम् |उक्षाः ह यत्र परि धानम् अक्तोर अनु स्वं धाम जरितुर ववक्ष ||
uto pitṛbhyām pravidānu ghoṣam maho mahadbhyām anayanta śūṣam | ukṣā ha yatra pari dhānam aktor anu svaṁ dhāma jaritur vavakṣa ||
  • Mantra: "uto pitṛbhyām pravidānu ghoṣam maho mahadbhyām anayanta śūṣam | ukṣā ha yatra pari dhānam aktor anu svaṁ dhāma jaritur vavakṣa ||"
  • Translation: This mantra can be interpreted as a call to the ancestors (pitṛ) and the great forces of the universe (maha) to bestow their blessings and guidance. It speaks of the importance of connection to our roots and the wisdom that comes from the past.
  • Word Breakdown:
    • uto: a call or invocation
    • pitṛbhyām: to the ancestors
    • pravidānu: to reveal or to make known
    • ghoṣam: the sound or call
    • maho: great
    • mahadbhyām: from the great ones
    • anayanta: to lead or guide
    • śūṣam: the dry or barren
    • ukṣā: the seed or potential
    • yatra: where
    • pari dhānam: the surrounding or encompassing wealth
    • aktor: of the act or action
    • anu: following
    • svaṁ: one's own
    • dhāma: abode or home
    • jaritur: of the aging or the old
    • vavakṣa: to express or to declare
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In many cultures, honoring ancestors is a common practice. For instance, during the Day of the Dead in Mexico, families create altars to remember and celebrate their deceased loved ones, reflecting the essence of this mantra.
    • In modern life, individuals often seek guidance from their elders or mentors, recognizing the wisdom that comes from experience, akin to invoking the blessings of the ancestors.
    • In agriculture, the concept of planting seeds (ukṣā) and nurturing them to fruition can be seen as a metaphor for personal growth and development, where one must cultivate their potential to achieve success.
  • Conclusion: This mantra emphasizes the significance of connecting with our roots and the wisdom of our ancestors. It serves as a reminder that our actions today are influenced by the past, and by honoring those who came before us, we can navigate our present and future with greater clarity and purpose. In real life, this can be applied in various ways, such as through family traditions, seeking mentorship, and recognizing the importance of heritage in shaping our identities.
