युñजाथां रासभं रथे वीढवाङ्गे वृषण्वसू |मध्वः सोमस्य पीतये ||
yuñjāthāṁ rāsabhaṁ rathe vīḍvaṅge vṛṣaṇvasū | madhvaḥ somasya pītaye ||
  • yuñjāthāṁ: This word can be translated as "may you unite" or "may you join together." It signifies the importance of unity and collaboration in achieving common goals.
  • rāsabhaṁ: This term refers to a "chariot" or "vehicle." In a metaphorical sense, it represents the means or tools we use to navigate through life.
  • rathe: This word also means "in the chariot." It emphasizes the journey we undertake in life, suggesting that we are all on a path that requires guidance and support.
  • vīḍvaṅge: This can be interpreted as "with strength" or "with vigor." It highlights the necessity of strength and resilience in our endeavors.
  • vṛṣaṇvasū: This term can be understood as "the one who brings prosperity" or "the one who is powerful." It signifies the importance of having a strong foundation and support system in our lives.
  • madhvaḥ: This word translates to "sweet" or "pleasant." It suggests that the outcomes of our efforts should be rewarding and fulfilling.
  • somasya: Referring to "of the soma," which is often associated with a divine drink that grants immortality and enlightenment. It symbolizes the pursuit of higher knowledge and spiritual growth.
  • pītaye: This means "for drinking" or "to consume." It indicates the act of taking in knowledge, experiences, and the essence of life.

In summary, this mantra emphasizes the significance of unity, strength, and the pursuit of knowledge in our lives. It encourages individuals to come together, support one another, and embark on their journeys with vigor and resilience. The metaphor of the chariot represents the path we take, while the references to sweetness and soma highlight the importance of seeking rewarding and enriching experiences.


In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as:

  • Teamwork: In a workplace setting, this mantra can inspire collaboration among team members, encouraging them to unite their strengths to achieve common objectives.
  • Community Building: In social settings, it can serve as a reminder of the importance of coming together as a community to support one another and work towards shared goals.
  • Personal Growth: On an individual level, it encourages people to seek knowledge and experiences that enrich their lives, fostering a sense of fulfillment and purpose.

Ultimately, the mantra serves as a guiding principle for living a harmonious and purposeful life, emphasizing the interconnectedness of individuals and the importance of collective strength in achieving success.
