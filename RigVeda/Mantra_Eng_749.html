वर्धन्तीम् आपः पन्बा सुशिष्विम ऋतस्य योनाः गर्भे सुजातम् ||
vardhantīm āpaḥ panvā suśiśvim ṛtasya yonā garbhe sujātam ||
  • Vardhantīm: This word means "nourishing" or "promoting growth." It signifies the idea of fostering development and abundance.
  • Āpaḥ: This translates to "waters." In the context of the mantra, it refers to the life-giving and purifying qualities of water, which are essential for growth and sustenance.
  • Panvā: This term means "pure" or "clean." It emphasizes the importance of purity in both physical and spiritual contexts.
  • Suśiśvim: This word means "well-formed" or "beautiful." It suggests the idea of perfection and harmony in creation.
  • Ṛtasya: This translates to "of truth" or "of order." It refers to the cosmic order and the principles that govern the universe.
  • Yonā: This means "womb" or "source." It symbolizes the origin of life and the nurturing aspect of creation.
  • Garbhe: This word means "in the womb." It signifies the process of gestation and the development of life within a protective environment.
  • Su-jātam: This translates to "well-born" or "noble." It indicates the idea of a life that is born out of purity, truth, and harmony.

The mantra "vardhantīm āpaḥ panvā suśiśvim ṛtasya yonā garbhe sujātam" can be interpreted as a prayer for the nourishing waters that promote growth and purity, leading to the birth of a noble and well-formed life. It highlights the interconnectedness of water, purity, truth, and the nurturing aspects of creation.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various contexts:

  • Agriculture: Farmers can invoke this mantra to seek blessings for abundant rainfall and fertile soil, ensuring a good harvest.
  • Health and Well-being: The emphasis on purity can be a reminder to maintain a healthy lifestyle, focusing on clean water and nutritious food for physical and mental well-being.
  • Environmental Conservation: The mantra can inspire individuals and communities to protect water sources and promote sustainable practices that honor the purity of nature.
  • Personal Growth: On a personal level, it encourages individuals to cultivate qualities of truth, purity, and harmony in their lives, leading to a more fulfilling existence.

In conclusion, this mantra serves as a reminder of the vital role that water and purity play in the cycle of life. It encourages us to seek growth and harmony in our lives while respecting the natural order of the universe. By embodying these principles, we can contribute to a more balanced and nurturing world.
