ओ त्यं अह्व आ रथम अद्य दंषिष्ठम् ऊतये |यम् अश्विनā सुहवā रुद्रवर्तनी आ सूर्यायै तस्थतुः ||
o tyam ahva ā ratham adyā daṁsiṣṭham ūtaye | yam aśvinā suhavā rudravartanī ā sūryāyai tasthathuḥ ||
  • Mantra: "o tyam ahva ā ratham adyā daṁsiṣṭham ūtaye | yam aśvinā suhavā rudravartanī ā sūryāyai tasthathuḥ ||"
  • Translation: "O, that which is called upon, may the best chariot be ready today; may the Aśvins, who are beautiful and swift, come to us, as they did for the Sun."
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • O: A call or invocation.
    • tyam: That (referring to a divine presence or energy).
    • ahva: Called upon or invoked.
    • ā: Towards or for.
    • ratham: Chariot, symbolizing movement, progress, or journey.
    • adyā: Today, indicating immediacy.
    • daṁsiṣṭham: The best or most excellent.
    • ūtaye: For the purpose of support or assistance.
    • yam: Who or that which.
    • aśvinā: Aśvins, the twin deities associated with health and medicine.
    • suhavā: Beautiful or pleasant.
    • rudravartanī: Those who move in the path of Rudra, often associated with transformation.
    • ā: Towards or for.
    • sūryāyai: To the Sun, representing light, energy, and life.
    • tasthathuḥ: May they stand or may they be present.
  • Significance:
    • This mantra is an invocation for divine assistance and support in our endeavors.
    • The chariot symbolizes our journey in life, and invoking the best chariot signifies the desire for the best possible path or outcome.
    • The Aśvins represent health, vitality, and swiftness, suggesting that we seek not just any help, but the most effective and beautiful kind.
    • By calling upon the Sun, we acknowledge the source of life and energy, emphasizing the importance of light and positivity in our lives.
  • Real-Life Application:
    • In daily life, this mantra can be recited before embarking on important tasks, such as starting a new job, beginning a journey, or making significant decisions.
    • It serves as a reminder to seek the best resources and support available, whether through community, family, or personal strengths.
    • In times of challenge, invoking the Aśvins can inspire individuals to focus on health and well-being, encouraging a proactive approach to personal care.
    • The mantra can also be used in rituals or meditative practices to cultivate a sense of purpose and direction, aligning oneself with positive energies.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking divine guidance and support in our life's journey.
    • It emphasizes the importance of striving for excellence and surrounding ourselves with positive influences.
    • By integrating this mantra into our lives, we can foster a mindset of growth, health, and purposeful action.
