त्वया वयं सुवृद्धा ब्रह्मणस पते स्पार्हा वसु मनुष्याः ददिमहि |या नो दूरि तळितो या अरातयोऽभि सन्ति जम्बयाः ता अनप्नसः ||
tvayā vayaṁ suvṛdhā brahmaṇas pate spārhā vasu manuṣyā dadīmahi | yā no dūre taḻito yā arātayo 'bhi santi jambhayā tā anapnasaḥ ||
  • tvayā vayaṁ suvṛdhā: This phrase translates to "We are nourished by you." It signifies the importance of divine support and sustenance in our lives. In real life, this can be compared to how individuals rely on their families, communities, or mentors for emotional and spiritual nourishment.
  • brahmaṇas pate: This means "O Lord of Creation." It acknowledges a higher power or divine force that governs the universe. In everyday life, this can be seen in the respect and reverence people have for nature, the universe, or a higher purpose that guides their actions.
  • spārhā vasu manuṣyā dadīmahi: This translates to "We offer our desires to you, O wealth of humanity." It reflects the idea of sharing one's aspirations and desires with the divine, seeking guidance and blessings. For instance, when people pray for success in their endeavors, they are essentially offering their desires to a higher power.
  • yā no dūre taḻito: This means "That which is far away." It suggests the pursuit of goals or aspirations that may seem distant or challenging. In real life, this can be likened to individuals striving for their dreams, such as pursuing higher education or career advancements that require dedication and perseverance.
  • yā arātayo 'bhi santi: This translates to "That which is free from obstacles." It emphasizes the importance of overcoming challenges in the pursuit of one's goals. For example, athletes training for competitions often face physical and mental barriers, yet they strive to overcome them to achieve success.
  • jambhayā tā anapnasaḥ: This means "May we be free from fear." It highlights the desire for safety and security in life. In a real-world context, this can relate to individuals seeking peace of mind in their personal and professional lives, whether through financial stability, healthy relationships, or mental well-being.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking divine support, sharing aspirations, overcoming obstacles, and striving for a life free from fear. Its significance lies in the reminder that we are not alone in our journeys; we have the support of a higher power and the strength to pursue our dreams. In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as during personal challenges, career pursuits, or moments of uncertainty, encouraging individuals to seek guidance, remain resilient, and foster a sense of community and support.
