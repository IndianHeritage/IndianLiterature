तं त्वा नावं न पर्षणिं शूषस्य धुरी धीमहि | इन्द्रं न यज्ञैश्चितयन्त आयवः स्तोमेभिर इन्द्रम आयवः ||
taṁ tvā nāvaṁ na parṣaṇiṁ śūṣasya dhuri dhīmahi | indraṁ na yajñaiś citayanta āyavaḥ stomebhir indram āyavaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • taṁ: that
    • tvā: you
    • nāvaṁ: boat or vessel
    • na: not
    • parṣaṇiṁ: the one who is to be praised
    • śūṣasya: of the dry or barren
    • dhuri: we meditate or contemplate
    • dhīmahi: we think or reflect
    • indraṁ: Indra, the king of gods
    • na: not
    • yajñaiś: with sacrifices
    • citayanta: those who are invoking
    • āyavaḥ: those who are seeking
    • stomebhir: with hymns
    • indram: Indra
    • āyavaḥ: those who are seeking
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra expresses a plea for guidance and support from Indra, the deity associated with strength, rain, and thunder.
    • The phrase "taṁ tvā nāvaṁ" suggests a desire for a vessel or means to navigate through life's challenges.
    • By stating "na parṣaṇiṁ śūṣasya," it emphasizes the need for sustenance and support in times of drought or difficulty.
    • The invocation of Indra through "indraṁ na yajñaiś citayanta" highlights the importance of rituals and collective efforts in seeking divine assistance.
    • The mantra concludes with a call for unity and strength through hymns, indicating that communal worship and praise can lead to divine favor.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In times of personal crisis, individuals often seek support from friends and family, akin to invoking Indra for strength and guidance.
    • Communities facing drought may come together for rituals and prayers, reflecting the collective effort to seek divine intervention for rain.
    • In corporate settings, teams may engage in brainstorming sessions to navigate challenges, similar to the metaphor of a vessel guiding them through turbulent waters.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of seeking divine guidance and support in overcoming life's challenges.
    • It highlights the value of community and collective efforts in invoking strength and sustenance.
    • In real-life scenarios, whether personal or communal, the principles of unity, support, and seeking higher guidance remain relevant and applicable.
