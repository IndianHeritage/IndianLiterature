मन्त्र: व्य आर्यमा वरुणश्चेति पन्थामिषस्पतिः सुवितं गातुमाग्निः | इन्द्राविष्णूनृवदु षु स्तवानां शर्म नो यन्तममवद वरूथम ||
vy aryamā varuṇaś ceti panthām iṣas patiḥ suvitaṁ gātum agniḥ | indrāviṣṇū nṛvad u ṣu stavānā śarma no yantam amavad varūtham ||
  • vy aryamā varuṇaś ceti: This phrase can be translated as "O Aryama and Varuna, the path is yours." Here, Aryama is often associated with the sun and the concept of friendship, while Varuna represents the cosmic order and moral authority. In real life, this can be seen as a call for guidance and support from higher powers or principles when navigating life's challenges.
  • panthām iṣas patiḥ suvitaṁ gātum agniḥ: This translates to "May the Lord of the path, the one who is powerful, lead us to the auspicious." The term "Agni" refers to fire, which symbolizes transformation and purification. In practical terms, this can relate to seeking clarity and direction in our endeavors, much like how fire can illuminate a dark path.
  • indrāviṣṇū nṛvad u ṣu stavānā: This means "Indra and Vishnu, we sing your praises." Indra is the king of the gods and represents strength and power, while Vishnu embodies preservation and protection. In everyday life, this can be interpreted as recognizing and honoring the forces that help us maintain balance and strength in our lives, whether they be personal relationships, community support, or spiritual beliefs.
  • śarma no yantam amavad varūtham: This translates to "May we be protected and may we not stray from the path." The word "śarma" signifies comfort and safety. In a real-world context, this can be seen as a prayer for protection from negative influences and distractions, allowing individuals to stay focused on their goals and values.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking guidance from higher powers and recognizing the forces that support us in our journey. It highlights the need for protection and clarity as we navigate life's challenges. In real-life scenarios, this can be applicable in various situations such as during difficult decisions, when facing uncertainties, or when striving for personal growth. By invoking these divine energies, individuals can find strength, direction, and a sense of security in their pursuits.
