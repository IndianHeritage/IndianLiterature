सप्ती चिद्घा मदाच्युता मिथुनā वहतो रथम् |एवेद धूर वृष्ण उttarā ||
saptī cid ghā madacyutā mithunā vahato ratham | eved dhūr vṛṣṇa uttarā ||
  • Mantra: "saptī cid ghā madacyutā mithunā vahato ratham | eved dhūr vṛṣṇa uttarā ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • saptī: Seven
    • cid: Consciousness or awareness
    • ghā: To be or to exist
    • madacyutā: The one who is not fallen from bliss or ecstasy
    • mithunā: Coupled or united
    • vahato: Carrying or bearing
    • ratham: Chariot
    • eved: This indeed
    • dhūr: The one who is strong or powerful
    • vṛṣṇa: A term often associated with strength or the Vrishni clan
    • uttarā: The one who is superior or higher
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of a powerful chariot that is carried by a united force, symbolizing strength and consciousness.
    • The mention of "seven" can be interpreted as the seven chakras or energy centers in the body, indicating a holistic approach to strength and awareness.
    • The phrase "madacyutā" suggests a state of bliss that is unshakeable, which can be related to achieving a state of mental peace and stability in life.
    • The "mithunā" signifies unity, which can be seen in partnerships, teamwork, or community efforts where individuals come together for a common goal.
    • The "ratham" or chariot can be viewed as the journey of life, where the strength and unity of the individuals propel the chariot forward.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, a team that works in harmony, leveraging each member's strengths, can be likened to the chariot being carried by united forces.
    • In personal relationships, couples who support each other and maintain a blissful connection exemplify the "madacyutā" aspect of the mantra.
    • In community service, volunteers coming together for a cause demonstrate the power of unity and collective strength, akin to the "vahato ratham."
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of unity, strength, and consciousness in our lives.
    • It serves as a reminder that when individuals come together with a shared purpose, they can achieve great things, much like a chariot that moves forward with the combined effort of its bearers.
    • In real-life scenarios, whether in personal relationships, professional environments, or community efforts, the principles encapsulated in this mantra can guide us towards achieving harmony and success.
