वि श्रयन्ताम ऋतावृद्धः प्रयै देविभ्यो महीः |पावकासः पुरुष्पृहो द्वारो देवीर असश्चतः ||
vi śrayantām ṛtāvṛdhaḥ prayai devebhyo mahīḥ | pāvakāsaḥ puruspṛho dvāro devīr asaścataḥ ||
  • vi śrayantām: This phrase can be interpreted as "may we be supported" or "may we find refuge." It emphasizes the importance of seeking support and guidance from higher powers or the universe.
  • ṛtāvṛdhaḥ: This term refers to "the one who increases truth" or "the one who promotes righteousness." It signifies the value of truth and moral integrity in our lives.
  • prayai: This word means "with devotion" or "with earnestness." It highlights the necessity of sincere effort and dedication in our pursuits.
  • devebhyo: This translates to "from the gods" or "from divine beings." It indicates the source of support and blessings that we seek in our lives.
  • mahīḥ: This word means "the great" or "the vast." It can refer to the earth or the universe, symbolizing the expansive nature of existence.
  • pāvakāsaḥ: This term can be understood as "the one who is pure" or "the one who brings purity." It emphasizes the importance of purity in thoughts, actions, and intentions.
  • puruspṛho: This phrase means "the one who desires the best" or "the one who seeks fulfillment." It reflects the human aspiration for growth and achievement.
  • dvāro: This word translates to "the door." It symbolizes opportunities and pathways that lead to new experiences and knowledge.
  • devīr: This term refers to "goddesses" or "divine beings." It signifies the feminine aspect of divinity and the nurturing qualities associated with it.
  • asaścataḥ: This phrase means "may they be auspicious" or "may they bring good fortune." It expresses a desire for positive outcomes and blessings in our endeavors.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a profound message about seeking support from divine forces while striving for truth and purity in our lives. It encourages us to approach our goals with devotion and earnestness, recognizing the vast opportunities that lie before us. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as during personal challenges, professional endeavors, or spiritual journeys. By invoking the essence of this mantra, individuals can cultivate a mindset of positivity, integrity, and openness to divine guidance, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling and harmonious life.
