मन्त्र: चित्रो यद अभ्राट् च्वेतो न विक्षु रथो न रुक्मी त्वेषः समत्सु ||
citro yad abhrāṭ chveto na vikṣu ratho na rukmī tveṣaḥ samatsu ||
  • Word Breakdown:
    • citro: This word can be interpreted as "brilliant" or "radiant," often associated with light or clarity.
    • yad: This translates to "that" or "which," indicating a connection or reference to something specific.
    • abhrāṭ: This term means "cloud" or "mist," symbolizing something that can obscure or cover.
    • chveto: This word refers to "shining" or "brilliant," emphasizing brightness.
    • na vikṣu: This phrase means "not scattered" or "not dispersed," suggesting unity or coherence.
    • ratho: This translates to "chariot," a symbol of movement, progress, and the journey of life.
    • na rukmī: This means "not golden," which can imply something that is not superficial or merely ornamental.
    • tveṣaḥ: This word means "form" or "appearance," indicating the essence of something.
    • samatsu: This translates to "in harmony" or "together," suggesting unity and balance.
  • Meaning:
    • The mantra speaks of a radiant essence that is not obscured by clouds, emphasizing clarity and brilliance.
    • It suggests that this brilliance is not scattered or lost, but rather remains unified and coherent.
    • The reference to the chariot symbolizes the journey of life, indicating that this essence guides us through our experiences.
    • By stating "not golden," it implies that true value lies beyond superficial appearances, focusing on deeper truths.
    • Finally, the mantra emphasizes harmony, suggesting that this essence thrives in unity and balance.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In personal development, individuals often seek clarity in their goals and aspirations. This mantra can inspire one to pursue a path that is true to their essence, avoiding distractions that cloud their vision.
    • In relationships, the idea of unity and harmony can be applied to foster deeper connections, encouraging individuals to look beyond superficial traits and appreciate the true essence of their partners.
    • In leadership, the concept of guiding others through a journey can be seen in effective leaders who illuminate the path for their teams, ensuring that everyone remains focused and united towards a common goal.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of clarity, unity, and deeper understanding in our lives.
    • It encourages individuals to seek the brilliance within themselves and others, fostering a sense of harmony and purpose.
    • By applying the teachings of this mantra, one can navigate life's challenges with a clear vision, ensuring that they remain true to their essence while promoting unity and balance in their surroundings.
