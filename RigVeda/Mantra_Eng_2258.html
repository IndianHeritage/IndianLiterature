वीरेभिर वीरान वनवत वनुष्यतो गोभी रयिम पप्रथद बोधति त्मना |तोकं च तस्य तनयं च वर्धते यं-यं युजं कृतुते ब्रह्मणस पतिः ||
vīrebhir vīrān vanavad vanuṣyato gobhī rayim paprathad bodhati tmanā | tokaṁ ca tasya tanayaṁ ca vardhate yaṁ-yaṁ yujaṁ kṛṇute brahmaṇas patiḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • vīrebhir: by the brave ones
    • vīrān: the heroes
    • vanavad: like a forest
    • vanuṣyato: from the human beings
    • gobhī: with the cows
    • rayim: wealth
    • paprathad: he spreads
    • bodhati: he awakens
    • tmanā: with the self
    • tokaṁ: the offspring
    • ca: and
    • tasya: of that
    • tanayaṁ: the progeny
    • ca: and
    • vardhate: grows
    • yaṁ-yaṁ: whoever
    • yujaṁ: joins
    • kṛṇute: creates
    • brahmaṇas: of the divine
    • patiḥ: lord
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the power and influence of brave individuals (vīrebhir vīrān) who, like a forest, provide shelter and support to humanity (vanavad vanuṣyato).
    • It emphasizes the importance of wealth (rayim) and prosperity, which is spread and awakened through the efforts of these heroes.
    • The mantra also highlights the significance of progeny (tokaṁ ca tanayaṁ), suggesting that the growth and success of future generations are tied to the actions and virtues of the present.
    • It concludes with the idea that those who align themselves with the divine (brahmaṇas patiḥ) will create and nurture a prosperous environment.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern society, leaders who exhibit bravery and integrity can inspire others to follow suit, creating a ripple effect of positive change.
    • Communities that support local agriculture and livestock (gobhī) can enhance their wealth and sustainability, much like the mantra suggests.
    • Parents and educators play a crucial role in nurturing the next generation (tokaṁ ca tanayaṁ), ensuring that they are equipped to face future challenges.
    • Collaborative efforts in businesses or social initiatives (yaṁ-yaṁ yujaṁ) can lead to innovative solutions that benefit society as a whole.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of bravery, prosperity, and the nurturing of future generations.
    • Its significance lies in the encouragement of individuals to act heroically and selflessly for the greater good, fostering a supportive community.
    • In real life, applying the principles of this mantra can lead to a more harmonious and prosperous society, where the actions of a few can uplift many.
