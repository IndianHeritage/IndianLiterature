अन्तश्च प्रागादितिर्भवस्य अवयाता हरसो दैव्यस्य |इन्दव इन्द्रस्य सख्यं जुषाणः श्रौष्टीव धुरम् अनु राया ऋध्याः ||
antaś ca prāgā aditir bhavāsy avayātā haraso daivyasya | indav indrasya sakhyaṁ juṣāṇaḥ śrauṣṭīva dhuram anu rāya ṛdhyāḥ ||
  • antaś ca prāgā: This phrase can be translated as "the inner and the outer." It signifies the duality of existence, encompassing both the internal world of thoughts and feelings and the external world of actions and interactions.
  • aditir bhavāsy: "Aditi" refers to the infinite or the boundless, often associated with the mother of gods in Vedic literature. This part suggests the nurturing aspect of the universe that supports all beings.
  • avayātā haraso daivyasya: This can be interpreted as "the divine essence that is ever-present." It emphasizes the omnipresence of divine energy in all aspects of life, suggesting that divinity is not separate from the material world but is interwoven within it.
  • indav indrasya sakhyaṁ: "Indra" is the king of gods and represents strength and power. This phrase speaks to the friendship or alliance with Indra, symbolizing the importance of community and support in achieving strength and success.
  • juṣāṇaḥ: This word means "enjoying" or "delighting in." It indicates the joy that comes from recognizing and embracing the divine presence in life.
  • śrauṣṭīva dhuram anu rāya ṛdhyāḥ: This can be understood as "following the path of righteousness and prosperity." It suggests that by adhering to moral and ethical principles, one can attain success and fulfillment.

Conclusion: This mantra encapsulates the essence of interconnectedness between the inner self and the outer world, the nurturing aspect of the universe, and the importance of divine presence in our lives. It teaches us that by fostering relationships, embracing joy, and following a righteous path, we can achieve both personal and communal prosperity. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as in personal development, community building, and ethical decision-making, reminding us to seek balance and harmony in all aspects of life.
