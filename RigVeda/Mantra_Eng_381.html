नास्मै विद्युः न तन्यतुः सिषेध न याम मिहम् अकिरद ध्रादुनिं च | इन्द्रश्च यद युयुधाते अहिश्चोतापरीभ्यो मगवाऽऽवि जिग्ये ||
nāsmai vidyun na tanyatuḥ siṣedha na yām miham akirad dhrāduniṁ ca | indraś ca yad yuyudhāte ahiś cotāparībhyo maghavā vi jigye ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • nāsmai: to him (Indra)
    • vidyun: lightning
    • na: not
    • tanyatuḥ: let it spread
    • siṣedha: let it not be restrained
    • na: not
    • yām: that which
    • miham: I have made
    • akirad: unmade
    • dhrāduniṁ: the firm support
    • ca: and
    • indraś: Indra
    • ca: and
    • yad: that which
    • yuyudhāte: fights
    • ahiś: the serpent
    • cotāparībhyo: from the other beings
    • maghavā: the powerful one
    • vi: indeed
    • jigye: has conquered
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the deity Indra, who is often associated with storms, rain, and strength.
    • The phrase "nāsmai vidyun na tanyatuḥ" suggests a plea for the lightning not to spread or cause harm, indicating a desire for protection.
    • "siṣedha na yām miham akirad" reflects the idea of maintaining stability and support in the face of chaos.
    • The latter part of the mantra highlights Indra's role as a warrior who fights against adversaries, particularly the serpent, symbolizing challenges or obstacles.
    • Indra's victory over these challenges ("maghavā vi jigye") signifies the triumph of strength and resilience.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In times of natural disasters, such as storms or floods, people often seek protection and stability, much like the plea in this mantra.
    • In personal challenges, individuals may invoke strength and resilience to overcome obstacles, akin to Indra's battles against adversaries.
    • Communities coming together to support each other during crises reflect the essence of maintaining firm support amidst chaos.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking protection, stability, and strength in the face of challenges.
    • It serves as a reminder that, like Indra, individuals can confront their adversities with courage and resilience.
    • In a broader context, it highlights the significance of community support and the collective strength that can be harnessed during difficult times.
