स त्वं नो अग्नि 'वमो भवोती नेदिष्टो अस्य उषसो व्युष्टौ |अव यक्ष्व नो वरुणं रराणो वीहि मृळीकं सुहवो न एधि ||
sa tvaṁ no agne 'vamo bhavotī nediṣṭho asyā uṣaso vyuṣṭau | ava yakṣva no varuṇaṁ rarāṇo vīhi mṛḻīkaṁ suhavo na edhi ||
  • sa tvaṁ no agne: This phrase translates to "O Agni, you are with us." Agni, the fire god, represents energy, transformation, and the divine spark within us. In real life, this can be seen as a call to harness our inner strength and passion to achieve our goals.
  • 'vamo bhavotī: This means "may you be our protector." It signifies the need for guidance and protection in our endeavors. For example, in a challenging situation at work, seeking mentorship or support from a colleague can be seen as invoking this protective energy.
  • nediṣṭho asyā uṣaso vyuṣṭau: This translates to "may you lead us to the dawn." The dawn symbolizes new beginnings and opportunities. In everyday life, this can relate to starting a new project or phase in life, where we seek clarity and direction.
  • ava yakṣva no varuṇaṁ: This means "may Varuna, the god of cosmic order, protect us." Varuna represents the moral and ethical order of the universe. In practical terms, this can be applied to maintaining integrity in our actions and decisions, ensuring that we align with our values.
  • rarāṇo vīhi mṛḻīkaṁ: This translates to "may you grant us strength and prosperity." It emphasizes the importance of resilience and abundance in our lives. For instance, during tough economic times, focusing on personal development and skills can lead to new opportunities and financial stability.
  • suhavo na edhi: This means "may we not be harmed." It reflects a desire for safety and well-being. In real life, this can be seen in the context of community support, where individuals come together to ensure each other's safety and welfare.

  • Conclusion: This mantra encapsulates a profound plea for guidance, protection, and strength from divine forces. Its significance lies in the recognition of our interconnectedness with the universe and the divine. In real-life scenarios, it serves as a reminder to seek support, maintain integrity, and strive for personal growth. By invoking these energies, we can navigate challenges and embrace new beginnings with confidence and resilience.
