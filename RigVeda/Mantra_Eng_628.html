न यस्य द्यावापृथिवी अनु व्याचो न सिन्धवो रजसो अन्तम आनाśुḥ |न त स्ववृष्टिम मदे अस्य युध्यत एको अन्यच चक्रिषे विश्वम् आनुशक ||
na yasya dyāvāpṛthivī anu vyaco na sindhavo rajaso antam ānaśuḥ | nota svavṛṣṭim made asya yudhyata eko anyac cakṛṣe viśvam ānuṣak ||
  • Translation: "He who does not have the sky and the earth as his boundary, who does not have the rivers as his end, he who does not have his own rain, he who fights alone, I have created the universe for him."
  • Word-by-Word Explanation:
    • na yasya: "He who does not have"
    • dyāvāpṛthivī: "the sky and the earth"
    • anu vyaco: "as his boundary"
    • na sindhavo: "who does not have the rivers"
    • rajaso: "as his end"
    • antam: "end"
    • ānaśuḥ: "he who does not have his own rain"
    • nota: "he who fights"
    • svavṛṣṭim: "his own rain"
    • made: "in my creation"
    • asya: "for him"
    • yudhyata: "who fights"
    • eko: "alone"
    • anyac: "other"
    • cakṛṣe: "I have created"
    • viśvam: "the universe"
    • ānuṣak: "for him"
  • Significance:
    • This mantra emphasizes the idea of limitless existence and the boundless nature of the universe.
    • It suggests that true strength and creation come from within, rather than from external boundaries or resources.
    • The concept of fighting alone can be interpreted as the individual journey of self-discovery and personal growth.
  • Real-Life Application:
    • In a world where people often feel constrained by societal norms and expectations, this mantra encourages individuals to break free from limitations and explore their true potential.
    • For example, an entrepreneur who starts a business without external funding embodies the spirit of this mantra, relying on their own resources and creativity.
    • In personal development, individuals who pursue their passions despite challenges reflect the essence of fighting alone and creating their own path.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder that the universe is vast and full of possibilities, and that true power lies within oneself.
    • It encourages individuals to embrace their uniqueness and to create their own destiny, regardless of external circumstances.
    • Ultimately, it inspires a sense of independence and self-reliance, which are crucial for personal and spiritual growth.
