देवैर नो देव्य अदितिः न पातु देवस् त्राताऽत्रायताम् अप्रयुच्छन् |तन नो मित्रो वरुणो मामहन्ताम् अदितिः सिन्धुः पृथिवी उता द्यौः ||
devair no devy aditir ni pātu devas trātā trāyatām aprayucchan | tan no mitro varuṇo māmahantām aditiḥ sindhuḥ pṛthivī uta dyauḥ ||
  • Translation: "May the divine beings protect us; may Aditi, the mother of the gods, safeguard us. May the deities be our protectors, and may they not withhold their blessings. May Mitra and Varuna, the great deities, along with Aditi, the ocean, the earth, and the sky, be with us."
  • Explanation of Key Terms:
    • Devair: Refers to the divine beings or gods, symbolizing higher powers that guide and protect humanity.
    • Aditi: The mother of the gods in Vedic tradition, representing boundless space and the nurturing aspect of the universe.
    • Mitra and Varuna: Two significant deities in Vedic literature; Mitra represents friendship and harmony, while Varuna embodies cosmic order and moral authority.
    • Sindhu: Refers to the ocean, symbolizing vastness and the source of life.
    • Pṛthivī: Represents the earth, the foundation of life and sustenance.
    • Dyauḥ: Refers to the sky or heaven, representing the realm of the divine and the infinite.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In times of uncertainty or crisis, individuals often seek protection and guidance from higher powers, similar to how the mantra invokes divine protection.
    • In community gatherings or rituals, people may call upon the blessings of deities like Aditi, Mitra, and Varuna to foster unity and harmony among participants.
    • Environmental movements often draw parallels with the elements mentioned in the mantra, emphasizing the need to protect the earth (Pṛthivī) and oceans (Sindhu) for future generations.
  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of divine protection and the interconnectedness of all elements of life—earth, ocean, and sky. It serves as a reminder of the need for harmony, moral order, and the nurturing aspect of the universe in our daily lives. In a world filled with challenges, invoking such blessings can inspire individuals and communities to seek guidance, foster unity, and protect the environment, ultimately leading to a more balanced and harmonious existence.
