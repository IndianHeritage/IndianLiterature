आयं यः सृञ्जये पुरो दैववाते समिध्यते |द्यमाम् अमित्रदम्भनः ||
ayaṁ yaḥ sṛñjaye puro daivavāte samidhyate | dyumām̐ amitradambhanaḥ ||
  • ayaṁ yaḥ sṛñjaye puro: This phrase can be translated to "This one who is invoked in the sacred place." Here, "ayaṁ" refers to "this," "yaḥ" means "who," "sṛñjaye" translates to "is invoked," and "puro" means "in the sacred place." This indicates the importance of a specific location for spiritual practices, akin to how people gather in places of worship or meditation to connect with the divine.
  • daivavāte samidhyate: This part means "is kindled by divine winds." "Daivavāte" refers to "divine winds" or "spiritual forces," and "samidhyate" means "is kindled." This suggests that spiritual energy or divine presence is activated in the sacred space, similar to how a fire is ignited by wind. In real life, this can be likened to the inspiration one feels in nature or during moments of deep reflection.
  • dyumām̐ amitradambhanaḥ: This translates to "the radiant one, the destroyer of enemies." "Dyumām̐" means "radiant" or "brilliant," while "amitradambhanaḥ" refers to "the one who destroys enemies." This can symbolize the triumph of good over evil, or the overcoming of obstacles in life. For example, when individuals face challenges, their inner strength and resilience can be seen as the "radiant" force that helps them prevail.

  • Significance: This mantra emphasizes the importance of sacred spaces and the divine energies that can be invoked within them. It highlights the connection between the physical and spiritual realms, suggesting that when one engages in spiritual practices in a dedicated space, they can access greater strength and clarity.
  • Real-life Application: In modern life, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as during meditation, prayer, or any form of spiritual practice. Creating a dedicated space for these activities can enhance focus and connection to the divine. Additionally, it serves as a reminder that inner strength can help overcome life's challenges, much like the radiant force that dispels darkness.
