शुनं अस्मभ्यम् ऊतये वरुणो मित्रो आर्यमाः |शर्म यच्छन्तु सप्रथा आदित्यासो यद इमहे अति द्विषः ||
śunam asmabhyam ūtaye varuṇo mitro aryamā | śarma yacchantu sapratha ādityāso yad īmahe ati dviṣaḥ ||
  • śunam asmabhyam ūtaye: This phrase can be translated as "May the divine grant us strength." Here, "śunam" refers to strength or vigor, while "asmabhyam" means "to us." This highlights the importance of seeking divine support for personal and communal strength.
  • varuṇo mitro aryamā: This part refers to the deities Varuna, Mitra, and Aryama. Varuna is associated with cosmic order and moral authority, Mitra represents friendship and harmony, and Aryama is linked to the ancestors and the afterlife. Together, they symbolize the interconnectedness of strength, friendship, and moral integrity.
  • śarma yacchantu sapratha: "śarma" means protection or shelter, and "yacchantu" translates to "may they grant." "sapratha" implies togetherness or unity. This line emphasizes the need for collective protection and support from the divine, suggesting that unity among people is essential for safety and well-being.
  • ādityāso yad īmahe: "ādityāso" refers to the Adityas, a group of solar deities, and "yad īmahe" means "we invoke." This indicates a call to the solar deities for guidance and enlightenment, symbolizing the pursuit of knowledge and clarity in life.
  • ati dviṣaḥ: This phrase translates to "against those who wish us harm." It highlights the need for protection against negativity and adversaries, emphasizing the importance of resilience and vigilance in the face of challenges.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a profound appeal for strength, protection, and unity from divine forces. In real-life scenarios, it can be applied in various contexts such as community gatherings, team-building exercises, or personal challenges where individuals seek support and strength. The invocation of deities signifies the importance of moral integrity, friendship, and collective effort in overcoming adversities. By embracing these values, individuals and communities can foster a harmonious environment that promotes resilience and well-being.
