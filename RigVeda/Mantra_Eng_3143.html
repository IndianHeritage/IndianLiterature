परी तमनā मित्रद्रुति एति होताग्निर मन्द्रो मधुवचā ऋतावā |द्रवन्त्य अस्य वाजिनो न शोकā भयन्ते विश्वा भुवना यद अभ्राट ||
pari tmanā mitadrur eti hotāgnir mandro madhuvacā ṛtāvā | dravanty asya vājino na śokā bhayante viśvā bhuvanā yad abhrāṭ ||
  • Mantra: "pari tmanā mitadrur eti hotāgnir mandro madhuvacā ṛtāvā | dravanty asya vājino na śokā bhayante viśvā bhuvanā yad abhrāṭ ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • pari: around, encompassing
    • tmanā: with the self, with one's own essence
    • mitadrur: the one who is benevolent or kind
    • eti: goes, moves towards
    • hotā: the priest or the one who invokes
    • agnir: fire, symbolizing energy and transformation
    • mandro: sweet, pleasant
    • madhuvacā: with sweet words or speech
    • ṛtāvā: the one who is truthful, righteous
    • dravanty: flows, moves forward
    • asya: of this, of it
    • vājino: of the strong, powerful
    • na: not
    • śokā: sorrows, grief
    • bhayante: are afraid, fear
    • viśvā: all, universal
    • bhuvanā: worlds, realms
    • yad: that, which
    • abhrāṭ: is free from clouds, clear
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra speaks of the benevolent nature of the self that moves towards the divine energy represented by fire (Agni).
    • The invocation of Agni signifies transformation and the power of positive energy in our lives.
    • The phrase "mandro madhuvacā" emphasizes the importance of sweet and kind speech, suggesting that our words have the power to uplift and transform situations.
    • The mantra reassures that those who embody truth and righteousness will not be affected by sorrows or fears, as they are supported by the universal energies.
    • In real life, this can be seen in how positive affirmations and kind words can change our mindset and the atmosphere around us.
    • For example, in a workplace, a leader who communicates positively and truthfully can foster a supportive environment, reducing stress and enhancing productivity.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its reminder of the power of positivity, truth, and the transformative energy of our words and actions.
    • It encourages individuals to cultivate a benevolent nature and to communicate with kindness, which can lead to a harmonious existence.
    • In various life scenarios, such as personal relationships, professional settings, or community interactions, embodying the principles of this mantra can lead to a more fulfilling and less fearful life.
    • Ultimately, it teaches us that by aligning ourselves with truth and positivity, we can navigate through life's challenges with grace and strength.
