यद उत्तमे मरुतो मध्यमे वा यद वावमे सुभगासो दिवि ष्ठा |अतो नो रुद्राऽउता वा nv अ1स्याग्ने वित्ताद धविषो यद यजाम ||
yad uttame maruto madhyame vā yad vāvame subhagāso divi ṣṭha | ato no rudrā uta vā nv a1syāgne vittād dhaviṣo yad yajāma ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yad: whatever
    • uttame: the highest
    • maruto: of the Maruts (storm deities)
    • madhyame: in the middle
    • vā: or
    • yad: whatever
    • vāvame: that which is pleasing
    • subhagāso: auspicious
    • divi: in the heavens
    • ṣṭha: may reside
    • ato: therefore
    • no: to us
    • rudrā: O Rudra (a form of Shiva)
    • uta: and
    • vā: or
    • nv: may we
    • a1syāgne: O Agni (the fire god)
    • vittād: from wealth
    • dhaviṣo: of the riches
    • yad: whatever
    • yajāma: we worship
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the highest aspects of the Maruts, the storm deities, and Agni, the fire god.
    • It emphasizes the importance of seeking the highest and most auspicious blessings from divine forces.
    • The mention of "wealth" and "riches" signifies the desire for prosperity and abundance in life.
    • In real life, this can be related to seeking guidance and blessings in endeavors that lead to success and fulfillment.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, entrepreneurs often seek divine blessings for success and prosperity, similar to how this mantra seeks the favor of the deities.
    • During significant life events, such as weddings or new ventures, people perform rituals invoking divine blessings for a prosperous future.
    • In personal development, individuals may meditate or pray for guidance and strength to achieve their highest potential, akin to invoking the highest aspects of the divine.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of seeking divine blessings for prosperity, success, and auspiciousness in life.
    • It serves as a reminder to connect with higher powers and seek their guidance in our endeavors.
    • In contemporary life, the principles of this mantra can be applied in various situations, from personal growth to professional success, emphasizing the importance of spirituality in achieving our goals.
