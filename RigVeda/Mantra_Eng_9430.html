युवोर यदि सख्यायास्मे शर्धाय स्तोमं जुजुषे नमस्वान् |विश्वत्र यस्मिन्न आ गिरः समीचिः पूर्वीव गातुर दाशत सूनृतायै ||
yuvor yadi sakhyāyāsme śardhāya stomaṁ jujuṣe namasvān | viśvatra yasminn ā giraḥ samīcīḥ pūrvīva gātur dāśat sūnṛtāyai ||
  • Mantra: "yuvor yadi sakhyāyāsme śardhāya stomaṁ jujuṣe namasvān | viśvatra yasminn ā giraḥ samīcīḥ pūrvīva gātur dāśat sūnṛtāyai ||"
  • Translation: "If you two are friends, we offer this hymn with devotion. In that, where all words are united, may the ancient song be sung for the sake of truth."
  • Word-by-Word Explanation:
    • yuvor: of the two (referring to friends or companions)
    • yadi: if
    • sakhyāyā: in friendship
    • asme: we are
    • śardhāya: with devotion
    • stomaṁ: hymn or praise
    • jujuṣe: we offer
    • namasvān: with reverence
    • viśvatra: everywhere
    • yasmin: in that
    • ā: to
    • giraḥ: words or sounds
    • samīcīḥ: united or harmonious
    • pūrvīva: like the ancient
    • gātur: song or chant
    • dāśat: may it be sung
    • sūnṛtāyai: for the sake of truth
  • Significance:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of friendship and unity in achieving a common goal.
    • It highlights the power of collective effort in the pursuit of truth and harmony.
    • The invocation of hymns and reverence suggests that spiritual practices can enhance relationships and foster a sense of community.
  • Real-Life Application:
    • In personal relationships, this mantra can serve as a reminder to nurture friendships and work together towards shared aspirations.
    • In professional settings, it encourages collaboration and teamwork, emphasizing that united efforts yield better results.
    • In community initiatives, it inspires collective action for social causes, reinforcing the idea that together, we can achieve greater truths and positive change.
  • Conclusion:
    • The mantra serves as a timeless reminder of the strength found in unity and the importance of working together with respect and devotion.
    • By applying its teachings in our daily lives, we can foster deeper connections, promote harmony, and strive for truth in our interactions.
