इह प्रियं प्रजया ते समृद्ध्यताम् अस्मिन् गृहे गार्हपत्याय जागृहि |एनā पत्या तन्वं सं सृजस्वाधा जीव्री विदथम् आ वदाथः ||
iha priyam prajayā te sam ṛdhyatām asmin gṛhe gārhapatyāya jāgṛhi | enā patyā tanva1ṁ saṁ sṛjasvādhā jivrī vidatham ā vadāthaḥ ||
  • Mantra: "iha priyam prajayā te sam ṛdhyatām asmin gṛhe gārhapatyāya jāgṛhi | enā patyā tanvaṁ saṁ sṛjasvādhā jivrī vidatham ā vadāthaḥ ||"
  • Translation: "May what is dear to you be fulfilled here; may you prosper in this home, O Gārhapatya (the domestic fire). With this, may you create a body (or form) for your husband, and may you speak of the wealth of life."
  • Explanation:
    • iha priyam prajayā te sam ṛdhyatām: This phrase expresses a wish for the fulfillment of what is dear to the individual. In real life, this can be seen in the context of family and relationships, where one hopes for the happiness and success of loved ones.
    • asmin gṛhe gārhapatyāya jāgṛhi: Here, the reference to Gārhapatya, the domestic fire, symbolizes the sanctity of the home. In many cultures, the hearth is considered the heart of the home, representing warmth, safety, and nourishment. For example, in modern households, the kitchen often serves as a gathering place for family, where meals are shared and bonds are strengthened.
    • enā patyā tanvaṁ saṁ sṛjasvādhā: This line speaks to the creation of a form or body for the husband, which can be interpreted as nurturing relationships and supporting one's partner. In contemporary life, this can relate to the roles individuals play in partnerships, where mutual support and understanding are essential for a healthy relationship.
    • jivrī vidatham ā vadāthaḥ: This part emphasizes the importance of speaking about the wealth of life, which can be understood as sharing experiences, knowledge, and resources. In practical terms, this can be seen in community gatherings, where people come together to share stories, wisdom, and support, fostering a sense of belonging and collective growth.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its emphasis on the importance of home, relationships, and community. It highlights the interconnectedness of personal desires and collective well-being. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in family rituals, community gatherings, and personal reflections, reminding individuals to cherish their relationships, nurture their homes, and contribute positively to their communities. By embodying these principles, one can create a harmonious and fulfilling life, both personally and socially.
