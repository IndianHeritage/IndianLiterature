क्व स्विद आसां कतमā पुराणी यया विधाना विदधुर ऋभूणाम् |शुभं यच्छुभ्राऽउषसश्चरन्ति न वि ज्ञायन्ते सदृशीर्जन्याः ||
kva svid āsāṁ katamā purāṇī yayā vidhānā vidadhur ṛbhūṇām | śubhaṁ yac chubhrā uṣasaś caranti na vi jñāyante sadṛśīr ajuryāḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • kva svid: Where indeed
    • āsāṁ: of the hopes
    • katamā: which one
    • purāṇī: ancient
    • yayā: by which
    • vidhānā: arrangements
    • vidadhur: they have made
    • ṛbhūṇām: of the wise
    • śubhaṁ: auspicious
    • yac: that which
    • shubhrā: pure
    • uṣasaś: of the dawns
    • caranti: they move
    • na: not
    • vi jñāyante: are known
    • sadṛśīr: similar
    • ajuryāḥ: unblemished
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra reflects a quest for understanding the origins and nature of hopes and aspirations.
    • It questions the source of ancient wisdom and the arrangements made by the wise.
    • The reference to "auspicious dawns" symbolizes new beginnings and opportunities that are pure and unblemished.
    • It suggests that the true nature of these auspicious moments is often beyond human comprehension.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a personal context, when embarking on a new venture, such as starting a business, one may reflect on the hopes and aspirations that drive them.
    • In a broader societal context, this mantra can be applied to the pursuit of knowledge and wisdom in various fields, such as science or philosophy, where the origins of ideas are often complex and multifaceted.
    • In moments of uncertainty, such as during a crisis, individuals may seek guidance from ancient wisdom or traditions that have stood the test of time.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of understanding the roots of our aspirations and the wisdom of the past.
    • It encourages individuals to seek clarity and purity in their pursuits while acknowledging that some aspects of life remain mysterious.
    • In real life, this can inspire a reflective approach to decision-making, urging us to consider the deeper meanings behind our goals and the influences of ancient wisdom.
