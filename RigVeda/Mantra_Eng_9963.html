मन्त्र: चित्रस ते भानुः क्रतुप्रā अभिष्टिः सन्ति स्पृधो जरणिप्रā अधृष्टाः | रजिष्ठया राज्यā पश्व ā गोस तूतूर्षति पर्य अग्रं दुवास्युः ||
citras te bhānuḥ kratuprā abhiṣṭiḥ santi spṛdho jaraṇiprā adhṛṣṭāḥ | rajiṣṭhayā rajyā paśva ā gos tūtūrṣati pary agraṁ duvasyuḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • citras: colorful, diverse
    • te: your
    • bhānuḥ: sun, light
    • kratuprā: endowed with intelligence, wisdom
    • abhiṣṭiḥ: blessings, prosperity
    • santi: exist, are
    • spṛdho: desires, aspirations
    • jaraṇiprā: those who are wise, knowledgeable
    • adhṛṣṭāḥ: fearless, bold
    • rajiṣṭhayā: with great effort
    • rajyā: rule, governance
    • paśva: cattle, wealth
    • ā: towards
    • gos: cows, symbol of wealth
    • tūtūrṣati: to be fulfilled, accomplished
    • pary: around, in all directions
    • agraṁ: foremost, best
    • duvasyuḥ: those who are prosperous
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the diverse and colorful nature of life, represented by the sun (bhānuḥ) that brings light and wisdom (kratuprā).
    • It emphasizes the importance of blessings (abhiṣṭiḥ) and the existence of aspirations (spṛdho) that guide individuals towards knowledge and fearlessness (adhṛṣṭāḥ).
    • The mantra suggests that through great effort (rajiṣṭhayā), one can achieve governance (rajyā) and prosperity (paśva) in life.
    • The mention of cows (gos) symbolizes wealth and abundance, indicating that true prosperity is fulfilled when one is surrounded by wealth in all directions (pary).
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, the mantra can be applied to the journey of an entrepreneur who, through diverse strategies and hard work, brings light (wisdom) to their venture, leading to prosperity.
    • In personal development, individuals can relate to the idea of pursuing their aspirations fearlessly, using their knowledge to navigate challenges and achieve their goals.
    • In community building, the mantra reflects the importance of collective effort in governance and prosperity, where diverse contributions lead to a thriving society.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the interconnectedness of wisdom, effort, and prosperity in life.
    • It teaches that by embracing diversity and working diligently towards our aspirations, we can achieve a fulfilling and prosperous existence.
    • In real-life scenarios, it encourages individuals and communities to harness their collective strengths to create a brighter future.
