यच चिद्धि सत्य सोमपा अनाशस्ता इव स्मसि |आ तु न इन्द्र शंसय गोष्व अश्वेषु शुभ्रिषु सहस्रेषु तुवीमघ ||
yac cid dhi satya somapā anāśastā iva smasi | ā tū na indra śaṁsaya goṣv aśveṣu śubhriṣu sahasreṣu tuvīmagha ||
  • Translation: "What is true, like the essence of the moon, is indeed eternal; O Indra, may we not doubt you, as you are the one who grants us abundance in cattle and horses, in thousands."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • yac cid dhi: "What is true" - This phrase emphasizes the importance of truth and reality in our lives.
    • satya: "truth" - The concept of truth is central to many philosophies and religions, highlighting its significance in ethical living.
    • somapā: "like the essence of the moon" - The moon is often associated with calmness, reflection, and the cyclical nature of life.
    • anāśastā: "eternal" - This suggests that truth is unchanging and everlasting, regardless of circumstances.
    • iva smasi: "indeed" - A confirmation of the previous statements, reinforcing their validity.
    • ā tū na indra: "O Indra, may we not doubt you" - Indra is a deity associated with strength and abundance, representing trust in divine support.
    • śaṁsaya: "doubt" - This word highlights the importance of faith and confidence in the face of uncertainty.
    • goṣv aśveṣu: "in cattle and horses" - Symbolizing wealth and prosperity, these animals represent the fruits of hard work and divine blessings.
    • śubhriṣu: "in thousands" - This indicates abundance and the idea of receiving blessings in large quantities.
    • tuvīmagha: "may we receive" - A prayerful request for blessings and prosperity.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In personal relationships, valuing truth can lead to stronger bonds and trust among friends and family.
    • In business, transparency and honesty can foster a positive work environment and build customer loyalty.
    • In spiritual practices, reflecting on the eternal nature of truth can provide comfort during challenging times, reminding individuals of their core values.
  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of truth and the importance of trusting in divine support for abundance and prosperity. In real life, this can be applied in various contexts, such as personal integrity, ethical business practices, and spiritual growth. By embracing truth and maintaining faith, individuals can navigate life's challenges with confidence and receive blessings in their endeavors.
