आयं नाभा वदति वल्गु वो गृहे देवपुत्रा ऋषयः तच्छृणोतन |सुब्रह्मण्यं अंगिरसो वो अस्तु प्रति गृभ्णीत मानवं सुमेधसः ||
ayaṁ nābhā vadati valgu vo gṛhe devaputrā ṛṣayas tac chṛṇotana | subrahmaṇyam aṅgiraso vo astu prati gṛbhṇīta mānavaṁ sumedhasaḥ ||
  • ayaṁ nābhā vadati: This phrase can be translated as "this navel speaks." In a metaphorical sense, the navel represents the center of life and energy. It is often associated with the source of nourishment and connection to the universe.
  • valgu vo gṛhe: This means "beautifully in your home." It emphasizes the importance of beauty and harmony within one's living space, suggesting that a well-maintained home contributes to overall well-being.
  • devaputrā ṛṣayas: Translated as "the sages, sons of the gods," this refers to enlightened beings or wise individuals who possess divine knowledge. They are often seen as guides or mentors in life.
  • tac chṛṇotana: This means "listen to that." It encourages individuals to pay attention to the wisdom and teachings of the sages, highlighting the importance of learning from those who are knowledgeable.
  • subrahmaṇyam aṅgiraso: This phrase translates to "may auspiciousness come from the Angiras." Angiras is a sage known for his wisdom and knowledge. The invocation of auspiciousness suggests a desire for positive outcomes and blessings in life.
  • vo astu prati gṛbhṇīta: This means "may it come to you." It expresses a wish for the blessings and wisdom to be received by the listener, reinforcing the idea of openness to divine guidance.
  • mānavaṁ sumedhasaḥ: Translated as "the wise human," this phrase emphasizes the importance of wisdom and intelligence in human life. It suggests that one should strive to be wise and thoughtful in their actions and decisions.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of wisdom, beauty, and divine guidance in our lives. It encourages individuals to create a harmonious environment at home, listen to the teachings of wise individuals, and seek auspiciousness in their endeavors. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied by fostering a positive home environment, engaging in continuous learning, and being open to the wisdom of mentors. By embodying these principles, individuals can lead more fulfilling and meaningful lives, ultimately contributing to their personal growth and the well-being of their communities.
