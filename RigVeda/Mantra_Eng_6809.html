मन्त्र: अग्निर्जातादेवानाम् अग्निर्वेदमार्तानाम् अपीच्यम् |अग्निः स द्रविणोदाऽ अग्निर्द्वाराव्यूर्णुते स्वाहुतो नवीयसां नभन्ताम् अन्यके समे ||
agnir jātā devānām agnir veda martānām apīcyam | agniḥ sa draviṇodā agnir dvārā vy ūrṇute svāhuto navīyasā nabhantām anyake same ||
  • Agnir jātā devānām: This phrase translates to "Agni, born of the gods." Agni, the fire god, is considered a divine entity in Vedic literature, symbolizing purity, energy, and transformation. In real life, fire is essential for cooking, warmth, and energy, representing the transformative power of nature.
  • Agnir veda martānām apīcyam: This means "Agni knows the mortals." Here, Agni is seen as a witness to human actions and a guide for mortals. In our lives, this can be likened to the moral compass or conscience that guides our decisions and actions, reminding us of accountability.
  • Agniḥ sa draviṇodā: This translates to "Agni, the giver of wealth." Fire is often associated with prosperity and abundance. In practical terms, this can be related to the idea that hard work and dedication (symbolized by fire) lead to success and wealth in various endeavors, such as business or personal growth.
  • Agnir dvārā vy ūrṇute svāhuto: This means "Agni opens the doors." Agni is seen as a facilitator of opportunities and pathways. In real life, this can be interpreted as the importance of being open to new experiences and opportunities, much like how fire can clear a path through obstacles.
  • Navīyasā nabhantām anyake same: This translates to "With newness, it brings forth the other." This suggests that Agni brings forth new beginnings and transformations. In our lives, this can be related to the idea of renewal and change, such as starting a new job, moving to a new place, or embarking on a new journey.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of Agni as a divine force that embodies transformation, guidance, and prosperity. In real life, it serves as a reminder of the importance of our actions, the opportunities we create, and the potential for renewal and growth. By acknowledging the role of Agni in our lives, we can strive to harness its energy for positive change and success in our personal and professional endeavors.
