तव वाजी वाजम्भरो विहायाऽभिष्टकृत् जायते सत्यशुष्मः |तव रयिः देवजूतो मयोभुः तव आशुर् जूजुवाम् अग्ने अर्वा ||
tvad vājī vājambharo vihāyā abhiṣṭikṛj jāyate satyaśuṣmaḥ | tvad rayir devajūto mayobhus tvad āśur jūjuvām̐ agne arvā ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • tvad: Your
    • vājī: Powerful, strong
    • vājambharo: One who brings forth strength
    • vihāyā: By abandoning
    • abhiṣṭikṛj: One who fulfills desires
    • jāyate: Is born
    • satyaśuṣmaḥ: With true brilliance
    • rayir: Wealth
    • devajūto: Divine being
    • mayobhus: Abundant
    • tvad: Your
    • āśur: Strength
    • jūjuvām̐: We invoke
    • agne: O Fire
    • arvā: To you
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra addresses Agni, the fire god, symbolizing energy, transformation, and purity.
    • The phrase "tvad vājī vājambharo" emphasizes the strength and power that comes from Agni.
    • By saying "vihāyā abhiṣṭikṛj," it suggests that by letting go of negativity or obstacles, one can achieve their desires.
    • "jāyate satyaśuṣmaḥ" indicates that true brilliance and success arise from aligning with truth and righteousness.
    • The invocation of "tvad rayir devajūto mayobhus" highlights the importance of divine wealth and abundance in our lives.
    • Finally, "tvad āśur jūjuvām̐ agne arvā" is a call to Agni for strength and support in our endeavors.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, a team may invoke the essence of this mantra by letting go of past failures (vihāyā) and focusing on their strengths (vājī) to achieve their goals (abhiṣṭikṛj).
    • In personal development, individuals can reflect on their true values (satyaśuṣmaḥ) to guide their decisions and actions, leading to a more fulfilling life.
    • During challenging times, people may seek the strength of Agni (āśur) to overcome obstacles and emerge victorious.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of harnessing inner strength and aligning with truth to achieve one's desires.
    • It teaches us that by letting go of negativity and invoking divine support, we can transform our lives and attain abundance.
    • In real life, this mantra can be applied in various situations, from personal growth to professional success, reminding us of the power of intention and the divine forces that support us.
