मन्त्र: नीत्वा वसिष्ठा अह्वन्त वाजिनं गृणन्तो अग्ने विदथेṣu वेधसः |रायस पोषं यजमानेषु धारय युयम् पात स्वस्तिभिः सदा नः ||
ni tvā vasiṣṭhā ahvanta vājinaṁ gṛṇanto agne vidatheṣu vedhasaḥ | rāyas poṣaṁ yajamāneṣu dhāraya yūyam pāta svastibhiḥ sadā naḥ ||
  • ni tvā vasiṣṭhā ahvanta vājinaṁ: This phrase can be translated as "We invoke you, O Agni, the most excellent one." Here, Agni represents the divine fire, a symbol of purity and transformation. In real life, invoking Agni can be likened to seeking inspiration or guidance in challenging situations, much like how people turn to mentors or leaders for wisdom.
  • gṛṇanto agne vidatheṣu vedhasaḥ: This means "We sing praises to you, Agni, in the gatherings of the wise." This highlights the importance of community and collective wisdom. In modern contexts, this can be seen in collaborative efforts, such as team meetings or brainstorming sessions, where diverse ideas are shared to achieve a common goal.
  • rāyas poṣaṁ yajamāneṣu: Translated, this means "Nourisher of wealth, while we perform our sacrifices." This emphasizes the idea that through our efforts and sacrifices, we seek prosperity and abundance. In everyday life, this can be related to hard work and dedication in one's career or personal projects, where the fruits of labor lead to success.
  • dhāraya yūyam pāta svastibhiḥ sadā naḥ: This translates to "May you, O Agni, always protect us with your auspiciousness." This is a plea for divine protection and blessings. In real life, this can be compared to the protective instincts of parents or guardians who wish for the safety and well-being of their loved ones, as well as the importance of positive energy and good intentions in our lives.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking guidance, wisdom, and protection from the divine. It emphasizes the importance of community, hard work, and the pursuit of prosperity while invoking the blessings of Agni. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as during important decisions, communal gatherings, or personal endeavors where one seeks success and well-being. It serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of our actions, the support we seek from others, and the divine forces that guide us in our journey.
