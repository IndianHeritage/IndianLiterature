ऋतावाऽस्य रोदसी दक्षं साचन्त ऊतयः |हविष्मन्तस्तं ईळते तं सनीष्यन्तोऽवसे ||
ṛtāvā yasya rodasī dakṣaṁ sacanta ūtayaḥ | haviṣmantas tam īḻate taṁ saniṣyanto 'vase ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ṛtāvā: One who is established in truth or righteousness.
    • yasya: Whose.
    • rodasī: The two worlds or the sky and the earth.
    • dakṣaṁ: Skillful or capable.
    • sacanta: They who are united or come together.
    • ūtayaḥ: Those who are elevated or uplifted.
    • haviṣmantas: Those who offer oblations or sacrifices.
    • tam: That.
    • īḻate: Worship or praise.
    • taṁ: That.
    • saniṣyanto: Those who are devoted or dedicated.
    • 'vase: To be under control or to be governed.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the importance of truth and righteousness in the universe.
    • It emphasizes the unity of the two worlds (heaven and earth) and the skillful actions of those who are aligned with truth.
    • The mantra suggests that those who are dedicated to offering sacrifices and living a life of righteousness are uplifted and praised.
    • It highlights the idea that true devotion and dedication lead to a harmonious existence governed by higher principles.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace, a team that operates with transparency and integrity often achieves greater success and harmony compared to one that does not.
    • In personal relationships, individuals who communicate openly and honestly tend to build stronger bonds and trust.
    • Communities that come together for a common cause, such as environmental conservation, exemplify the unity and upliftment described in the mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for individuals to align themselves with truth and righteousness.
    • It serves as a reminder that our actions, when rooted in integrity, can lead to personal and collective upliftment.
    • In real life, applying the principles of this mantra can foster better relationships, enhance teamwork, and contribute to a more harmonious society.
