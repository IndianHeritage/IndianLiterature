भूरि दाक्षेभिर वचनैभिर ऋक्वभिः सख्येभिः सख्यानि प्र वोचत | इन्द्रो धुनिं च चुमुरीं च दम्भयञ श्रद्धामनस्या श्रिणुते दभीतये ||
bhūri dakṣebhir vacanebhir ṛkvabhiḥ sakhyebhiḥ sakhyāni pra vocata | indro dhuniṁ ca cumuriṁ ca dambhayañ chraddhāmanasyā śṛṇute dabhītaye ||
  • bhūri dakṣebhir vacanebhir ṛkvabhiḥ: This phrase can be translated as "with abundant skillful words and verses." Here, "bhūri" means abundant or plentiful, "dakṣebhir" refers to skillful or capable, "vacanebhir" means words, and "ṛkvabhiḥ" refers to the verses of the Rigveda. This emphasizes the importance of using well-crafted language and knowledge in communication.
  • sakhyebhiḥ sakhyāni pra vocata: This translates to "let us speak together with friendship." "Sakhyebhiḥ" means with friends, and "sakhyāni" refers to friendship. This part of the mantra highlights the value of unity and collaboration among individuals, suggesting that collective efforts lead to greater outcomes.
  • indro dhuniṁ ca cumuriṁ ca: This can be interpreted as "Indra, the powerful, and the great." "Indro" refers to Indra, the king of gods in Vedic tradition, while "dhuniṁ" and "cumuriṁ" signify strength and greatness. This invokes the idea of seeking strength and support from divine powers in our endeavors.
  • dambhayañ chraddhāmanasyā: This phrase means "with faith and sincerity." "Dambhayañ" refers to sincerity or truthfulness, and "chraddhāmanasyā" means with a heart full of faith. This emphasizes the importance of approaching tasks and relationships with genuine intent and belief.
  • śṛṇute dabhītaye: This translates to "may they listen for protection." "Śṛṇute" means to listen, and "dabhītaye" refers to protection or safety. This part of the mantra calls for attentiveness and the need for safeguarding oneself and others through understanding and support.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of effective communication, collaboration, and the importance of sincerity in relationships. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various contexts such as teamwork in a workplace, where clear and skillful communication is essential for success. It also highlights the significance of building friendships and working together towards common goals, fostering a sense of community. Furthermore, invoking strength and protection through faith and sincerity can be crucial in personal relationships and during challenging times, reminding us to support one another and listen actively. Overall, this mantra serves as a guiding principle for harmonious living and effective collaboration in our daily lives.
