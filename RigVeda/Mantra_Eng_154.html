द्रविणोदाद ददातु नो वसूनि यानि श्रिण्विरे |देवेषु ता वनामहे ||
draviṇodā dadātu no vasūni yāni śṛṇvire | deveṣu tā vanāmahe ||
  • Translation: "May the wealth (draviṇodā) be given to us, the riches (vasūni) that we hear of. We invoke (vanāmahe) the deities (deveṣu)."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • draviṇodā: Refers to wealth or resources, often associated with prosperity and abundance.
    • dadātu: Means "may it be given" or "let it be bestowed," indicating a request or prayer for blessings.
    • no: Translates to "to us," emphasizing the personal aspect of the prayer.
    • vasūni: Refers to riches or valuable possessions, often linked to material and spiritual wealth.
    • yāni: Means "that which" or "those which," connecting the wealth to the things we hear about.
    • śṛṇvire: Translates to "we hear," indicating awareness and acknowledgment of wealth and blessings.
    • deveṣu: Refers to the deities or divine beings, highlighting the spiritual aspect of the invocation.
    • tā: Means "those," referring back to the riches and blessings being invoked.
    • vanāmahe: Means "we invoke" or "we call upon," indicating a plea for divine intervention.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be seen as a prayer for prosperity and success. Entrepreneurs often seek blessings for their ventures, hoping for wealth and resources to flow into their businesses.
    • In personal life, individuals may recite this mantra during times of financial difficulty, seeking divine assistance to overcome challenges and attract abundance.
    • Communities may come together to chant this mantra during festivals or rituals, invoking the blessings of deities for collective prosperity and well-being.
  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking divine blessings for wealth and prosperity. It serves as a reminder that while hard work is essential, invoking spiritual support can enhance one's efforts. In real life, this mantra can be applicable in various scenarios, such as business endeavors, personal financial challenges, and community gatherings, where the collective intention is to attract abundance and well-being. By acknowledging the divine and expressing gratitude for the wealth we seek, we align ourselves with the flow of prosperity in our lives.
