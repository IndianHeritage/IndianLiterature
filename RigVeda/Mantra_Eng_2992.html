अभीका आसाम पदवीर अबोध्य आदित्यनाम अह्वे चारु नाम |आपश्चिद अस्मा अरमन्त देविः पृथग्व्रजन्ति परि सीम अवृञ्जन ||
abhīka āsām padavīr abodhy ādityānām ahve cāru nāma | āpaś cid asmā aramanta devīḥ pṛthag vrajantīḥ pari ṣīm avṛñjan ||
  • abhīka āsām padavīr: This phrase can be interpreted as "may we be endowed with the strength and courage to overcome obstacles." In real life, this can relate to facing challenges in personal or professional life, such as overcoming fears or striving for success in a competitive environment.
  • abodhy ādityānām: This translates to "may we be enlightened by the wisdom of the sun." The sun symbolizes knowledge, clarity, and enlightenment. In practical terms, this can be seen in the pursuit of education and self-improvement, where individuals seek knowledge to illuminate their paths.
  • ahve cāru nāma: This means "I call upon the beautiful name." This can refer to invoking positive qualities or virtues in our lives. For example, when we strive to embody kindness, compassion, or integrity, we are calling upon these beautiful names in our actions and interactions with others.
  • āpaś cid asmā aramanta devīḥ: This can be interpreted as "the divine energies that flow through us." It emphasizes the connection between the individual and the divine. In real life, this can be seen in moments of inspiration or creativity, where individuals feel a surge of energy that drives them to create or innovate.
  • pṛthag vrajantīḥ pari ṣīm avṛñjan: This translates to "may we move forward together, united in purpose." This highlights the importance of community and collaboration. In practical terms, this can be applied in teamwork settings, where collective efforts lead to greater achievements than individual endeavors.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of strength, enlightenment, beauty, divine energy, and unity. In real life, these themes are applicable in various scenarios, such as personal growth, education, community building, and collaborative efforts. By embodying these principles, individuals can navigate life's challenges more effectively and contribute positively to society.
