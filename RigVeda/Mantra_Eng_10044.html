भोजम अश्वाः सुष्ठुवाहो वहन्ति सुवृद रथो वर्तते दक्षिणायाः |भोजन देवासो 'वत भरेषु भोजः शत्रून समनीकेषु जेता ||
bhojam aśvāḥ suṣṭhuvāho vahanti suvṛd ratho vartate dakṣiṇāyāḥ | bhojaṁ devāso 'vatā bhareṣu bhojaḥ śatrūn samanīkeṣu jetā ||
  • Translation: The mantra can be translated as: "The horses that are well-trained carry the chariot that moves towards the south. The gods bestow abundance upon us; may we conquer our enemies in battle."
  • Word-by-Word Explanation:
    • bhojam: Refers to nourishment or abundance.
    • aśvāḥ: Means horses, symbolizing strength and speed.
    • suṣṭhuvāho: Indicates well-trained or well-prepared.
    • vahanti: Means to carry or transport.
    • suvṛd: Refers to a well-constructed or well-formed chariot.
    • ratho: Means chariot, a symbol of progress and movement.
    • vartate: Means to move or to proceed.
    • dakṣiṇāyāḥ: Refers to the direction south, often associated with prosperity.
    • devāso: Refers to the gods or divine beings.
    • Means to bestow or grant.
    • bhareṣu: Refers to abundance or wealth.
    • śatrūn: Means enemies or adversaries.
    • samanīkeṣu: Refers to being in a group or together.
    • jetā: Means to conquer or to win.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • The well-trained horses symbolize the importance of preparation and skill in achieving success, much like athletes who train rigorously for competitions.
    • The chariot represents the journey of life, where direction and purpose are crucial, similar to how individuals set goals and work towards them.
    • The concept of divine blessings can be seen in various cultures where people seek guidance and support from higher powers in their endeavors, such as praying for success before an important event.
    • Conquering enemies can be interpreted as overcoming challenges in life, such as facing personal fears or professional obstacles, akin to how businesses strategize to outmaneuver competitors.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its emphasis on preparation, divine support, and the pursuit of success. It teaches us that with the right training and mindset, we can navigate the challenges of life effectively. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire individuals to prepare diligently for their goals, seek support from their communities or spiritual beliefs, and strive to overcome obstacles, ultimately leading to personal and collective victories.
