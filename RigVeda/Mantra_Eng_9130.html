मूषो न शिष्णा व्य अदन्ति माध्यः स्तोतारं ते शतक्रतो |सकृत सु नो मगवन्न इन्द्र मृळयाधा पितेव नो भव ||
mūṣo na śiśnā vy adanti mādhyaḥ stotāraṁ te śatakrato | sakṛt su no maghavann indra mṛḻayādhā piteva no bhava ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • mūṣo: of the mouse
    • na: not
    • śiśnā: to be frightened
    • vy: indeed
    • adanti: they speak
    • mādhyaḥ: in the middle
    • stotāraṁ: the one who is praised
    • te: your
    • śatakrato: O hundred-fold (referring to Indra)
    • sakṛt: once
    • su: well
    • no: for us
    • maghavann: O powerful one
    • indra: Indra, the king of gods
    • mṛḻayādhā: may you protect
    • piteva: like a father
    • no: us
    • bhava: become
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the deity Indra, asking for his protection and guidance.
    • The reference to "mūṣo" (mouse) symbolizes vulnerability, suggesting that even the smallest creatures deserve protection.
    • The phrase "like a father" emphasizes the nurturing and protective qualities that are sought from Indra.
    • It highlights the importance of divine support in overcoming fears and challenges in life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In times of personal crisis, individuals often seek guidance and support from higher powers or mentors, similar to how this mantra seeks Indra's protection.
    • Parents often act as protectors, guiding their children through fears and uncertainties, akin to the plea for Indra to act like a father.
    • In a workplace setting, employees may look to their leaders for support and reassurance during challenging projects, reflecting the need for divine-like guidance.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the universal need for protection and guidance, regardless of one's status or strength.
    • It teaches us to seek help from higher powers or supportive figures in our lives, especially when facing fears or challenges.
    • In a broader sense, it encourages a sense of community and interconnectedness, reminding us that we are all vulnerable and in need of support.
