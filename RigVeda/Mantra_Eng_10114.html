मन्त्र: चित्र इच्छिषोऽतरुणस्य वक्षठो न यो मातराव अप्येति धातवे | अनूधā यदि जीवजनद अधा च नु ववक्ष सद्यो महि दूत्यं चरन् ||
citra ic chiśos taruṇasya vakṣatho na yo mātarāv apyeti dhātave | anūdhā yadi jījanad adhā ca nu vavakṣa sadyo mahi dūtya1ṁ caran ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • citra: colorful, diverse
    • ic: this
    • chiśos: of the young one
    • taruṇasya: of the youthful
    • vakṣatho: in the heart
    • na: not
    • yo: who
    • mātarāv: of the mothers
    • apyeti: goes away
    • dhātave: to the creator
    • anūdhā: without delay
    • yadi: if
    • jījanad: gives birth
    • adhā: below
    • ca: and
    • nu: indeed
    • vavakṣa: will speak
    • sadyo: immediately
    • mahi: great
    • dūtyaṁ: messenger
    • caran: moving
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the vibrant and diverse nature of youth and the heart's connection to creation.
    • It emphasizes the importance of nurturing and protecting the young, as they are the future.
    • The phrase "who does not go away from the mothers" suggests a bond that should not be severed, highlighting the role of maternal care.
    • The idea of "without delay" indicates the urgency in addressing the needs of the young and ensuring their well-being.
    • The mention of a "great messenger" signifies the importance of communication and guidance in the growth and development of youth.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern society, this mantra can be related to the importance of education and mentorship for the youth.
    • Programs that support young people, such as after-school initiatives or youth mentorship programs, embody the essence of nurturing the young.
    • In family dynamics, the role of parents and guardians in providing emotional and physical support reflects the mantra's emphasis on maternal care.
    • Community efforts to protect and empower youth, such as anti-bullying campaigns, resonate with the call to safeguard the young from harm.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the responsibility we have towards the younger generation.
    • It highlights the significance of nurturing, protecting, and guiding youth to ensure a vibrant future.
    • In real life, applying the principles of this mantra can lead to stronger communities and a more supportive environment for growth.
    • Ultimately, it calls for immediate action and communication to foster a healthy and thriving youth population.
