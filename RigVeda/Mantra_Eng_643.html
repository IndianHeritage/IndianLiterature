मन्त्र: आर्चा दिवे बृहते शूष्यं वचः स्वकक्षत्रं यस्य धृषतो धृषण मनः | बृहच्छ्रवा असुरो बरहणा कृतः पुरो हरिभ्यां वृषभो रथो हि षः ||
arcā dive bṛhate śūṣya1ṁ vacaḥ svakṣatraṁ yasya dhṛṣato dhṛṣan manaḥ | bṛhacchravā asuro barhaṇā kṛtaḥ puro haribhyāṁ vṛṣabho ratho hi ṣaḥ ||
  • arcā dive bṛhate: This phrase can be translated as "the great invocation in the heavens." It signifies the importance of calling upon divine forces or energies that exist in the universe. In real life, this can be likened to the practice of prayer or meditation, where individuals seek connection with a higher power or the universe.
  • śūṣyaṁ vacaḥ: This means "the words that are powerful." It emphasizes the significance of speech and communication. In everyday life, this can be related to the impact of our words on others, highlighting the importance of speaking positively and constructively.
  • svakṣatraṁ yasya dhṛṣato dhṛṣan manaḥ: This translates to "whose mind is steadfast and focused." It suggests the value of determination and clarity of thought. In practical terms, this can be seen in individuals who set goals and work diligently towards achieving them, demonstrating mental strength and focus.
  • bṛhacchravā asuro barhaṇā: This phrase means "the great sound of the divine." It refers to the powerful vibrations or energies that can be felt in the presence of the divine. In real life, this can be compared to moments of inspiration or profound realization that can change one's perspective or life path.
  • kṛtaḥ puro haribhyāṁ: This translates to "created for the divine beings." It signifies the idea that our actions and creations should be aligned with higher principles or values. In a practical sense, this can be seen in acts of service or creativity that aim to uplift others or contribute positively to society.
  • vṛṣabho ratho hi ṣaḥ: This means "the powerful chariot." It symbolizes strength and the ability to move forward with purpose. In real life, this can be likened to the journey of life where individuals harness their inner strength to overcome challenges and achieve their aspirations.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of invoking divine energies, the power of words, mental focus, and the importance of aligning our actions with higher values. Its significance lies in encouraging individuals to cultivate a strong mind, communicate positively, and act with purpose. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire personal growth, effective communication, and a commitment to serving the greater good, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling and harmonious life.
