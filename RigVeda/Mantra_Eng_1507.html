ऊती देवानां वयम् इन्द्रवन्तो मंसिमहि स्वयशसो मरुद्भिः |अग्निर मित्रो वरुणः शर्म यंशन तद अश्याम मगवाणो वयं च ||
ūtī devānāṁ vayam indravanto maṁsīmahi svayaśaso marudbhiḥ | agnir mitro varuṇaḥ śarma yaṁsan tad aśyāma maghavāno vayaṁ ca ||
  • ūti devānāṁ: This phrase translates to "the strength of the gods." It signifies the divine power and energy that the deities possess.
  • vayam indravanto: This means "we are endowed with the qualities of Indra." Indra is the king of the gods and represents strength, courage, and leadership.
  • maṁsīmahi: This translates to "we aspire" or "we strive." It indicates a desire to achieve greatness and excellence.
  • svayaśaso: This means "of our own glory." It emphasizes the importance of self-earned respect and honor.
  • marudbhiḥ: Referring to the Maruts, the storm gods, this word symbolizes the forces of nature and their power.
  • agnir mitro varuṇaḥ: This phrase translates to "Agni (the fire god), Mitra (the god of friendship), and Varuna (the god of cosmic order)." These deities represent different aspects of life and the universe.
  • śarma yaṁsan: This means "the shelter that we seek." It signifies the need for protection and safety in life.
  • tad aśyāma: This translates to "that we may attain." It expresses the aspiration to reach a higher state of being or understanding.
  • maghavāno vayaṁ ca: This means "we are also endowed with abundance." It highlights the blessings and prosperity that come from divine favor.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda encapsulates the essence of seeking divine strength, protection, and prosperity. It emphasizes the importance of striving for greatness while acknowledging the support of the gods. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios such as personal development, leadership roles, and community building. For instance, when individuals or groups face challenges, invoking the qualities of strength and protection can inspire confidence and resilience. The mantra serves as a reminder that with divine support and personal effort, one can achieve their goals and contribute positively to society.
