प्रचेतसां त्वा कव एग्ने दूतं वरेण्यं |हव्यवाहं निषेदिरे ||
pracetasaṁ tvā kave 'gne dūtaṁ vareṇyam | havyavāhaṁ ni ṣedire ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • pracetasaṁ: With wisdom or intelligence
    • tvā: You
    • kave: O poet or seer
    • agne: O Agni (the fire god)
    • dūtaṁ: Messenger
    • vareṇyam: Worthy of worship or reverence
    • havyavāhaṁ: One who carries offerings
    • ni ṣedire: They have settled or established
  • Overall Meaning:
    • This mantra addresses Agni, the fire god, as a wise and revered messenger.
    • It acknowledges Agni's role in carrying offerings to the divine and emphasizes the importance of wisdom in communication and connection with the divine.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In many cultures, fire is seen as a purifying element. For instance, in Hindu rituals, offerings are made to Agni during ceremonies, symbolizing the connection between the physical and spiritual realms.
    • In modern life, the concept of a "messenger" can be seen in various forms, such as communication technologies that help convey messages and ideas effectively, much like Agni conveys offerings to the gods.
  • Significance and Application:
    • This mantra highlights the importance of wisdom and reverence in our actions and communications.
    • In real life, it encourages individuals to approach their endeavors with intelligence and respect, whether in personal relationships, professional settings, or spiritual practices.
    • By recognizing the role of wisdom in our interactions, we can foster better understanding and harmony in our lives.
