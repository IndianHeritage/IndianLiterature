त्वं अग्नि पुरुरूपो विशे-विशे वयो दधासि प्रत्नथा पुरुष्टुत | पुरूण्यन्ना सहसा वि राजसि त्विषिः सा ते तित्विषाणस्य नाधृषे ||
tvam agne pururūpo viśe-viśe vayo dadhāsi pratnathā puruṣṭuta | purūṇy annā sahasā vi rājasi tviṣiḥ sā te titviṣāṇasya nādhṛṣe ||
  • tvam agne - "You, O Agni" - This refers to Agni, the fire god, who is a central figure in Vedic rituals and symbolizes energy, transformation, and purification.
  • pururūpo - "of many forms" - Agni is described as having multiple forms, representing the various manifestations of energy and fire in the universe.
  • viśe-viśe - "in every community" - This indicates that Agni is present in all aspects of life and society, signifying the universal nature of fire and energy.
  • vayo dadhāsi - "you provide strength" - Agni is seen as a source of strength and vitality, essential for life and growth.
  • pratnathā puruṣṭuta - "you are praised by the wise" - This highlights the reverence and respect that wise individuals have for Agni, acknowledging its importance in rituals and daily life.
  • purūṇy annā - "the many foods" - Agni is associated with nourishment, as fire is essential for cooking and preparing food.
  • sahasā vi rājasi - "you rule with great power" - This emphasizes Agni's dominion and influence over the natural and spiritual realms.
  • tviṣiḥ - "in brilliance" - Agni is characterized by its brightness and radiance, symbolizing knowledge and enlightenment.
  • sā te titviṣāṇasya - "that which is of the radiant one" - This refers to the divine qualities of Agni, linking it to higher spiritual truths.
  • nādhṛṣe - "may we not be forsaken" - A plea for protection and guidance from Agni, ensuring that one is not abandoned in times of need.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda emphasizes the multifaceted nature of Agni as a source of strength, nourishment, and enlightenment. In real life, the significance of this mantra can be seen in various contexts, such as:

  • Rituals and Ceremonies: Agni is central to many Hindu rituals, where offerings are made to the fire, symbolizing the connection between the material and spiritual worlds.
  • Personal Growth: Just as Agni transforms raw materials into something useful, individuals can harness their inner strength and energy to transform their lives and achieve their goals.
  • Community and Society: The presence of Agni in every community signifies the importance of collective strength and unity in overcoming challenges.

Overall, this mantra serves as a reminder of the vital role that energy, transformation, and community play in our lives, encouraging us to seek strength and enlightenment through our actions and connections with others.
