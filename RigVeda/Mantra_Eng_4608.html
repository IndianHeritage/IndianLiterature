नू मा आ वाचम उप याहि विद्वान् विश्वेभिः सूनो सहसो यजत्रैः |ये अग्निजिह्वा ऋतसाप आसुर ये मनुं चक्रुर उपरं दसाय ||
nū ma ā vācam upa yāhi vidvān viśvebhiḥ sūno sahaso yajatraiḥ | ye agnijihvā ṛtasāpa āsur ye manuṁ cakrur uparaṁ dasāya ||
  • nū ma ā vācam upa yāhi vidvān: This phrase can be translated as "O wise one, come to my speech." Here, the speaker is invoking a knowledgeable entity or person to lend their wisdom to the conversation. In real life, this can be seen in situations where individuals seek guidance from mentors or experts in various fields, such as a student asking a teacher for advice on a complex topic.
  • viśvebhiḥ sūno sahaso yajatraiḥ: This part means "with all the sons of the universe, together with the powerful ones." It emphasizes the importance of community and collaboration. In modern contexts, this can relate to teamwork in workplaces or collaborative projects where diverse talents come together to achieve a common goal, such as a group of scientists working together on a groundbreaking research project.
  • ye agnijihvā ṛtasāpa āsur: This translates to "those who have the fire-tongue, who are truthful and divine." The "fire-tongue" symbolizes the power of speech and communication, while "truthful and divine" refers to the importance of honesty and integrity. In everyday life, this can be seen in leaders who inspire others through their truthful communication, such as activists who speak out for justice and equality.
  • ye manuṁ cakrur uparaṁ dasāya: This means "those who have created the human being, who have brought forth the ten." This can be interpreted as a reference to the creators of humanity and the various aspects of life. In a contemporary context, this could relate to the role of educators and parents in shaping the future generations, emphasizing the responsibility of nurturing and guiding young minds.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking wisdom, the power of community, the importance of truthful communication, and the responsibility of nurturing future generations. Its significance lies in the reminder that collaboration and integrity are essential in all aspects of life, whether in personal relationships, professional environments, or societal structures. By embodying these principles, individuals can contribute to a more harmonious and enlightened world.
