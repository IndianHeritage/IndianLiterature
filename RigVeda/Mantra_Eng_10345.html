यं ते श्येनश्चारुम अवृकम् पदाभरद अरुणं मानम् अन्धसः |एनां वयो विटारी आयुर जीवस एना जागार बन्धुताः ||
yaṁ te śyenaś cārum avṛkam padābharad aruṇam mānam andhasaḥ | enā vayo vi tāry āyur jīvasa enā jāgāra bandhutā ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yaṁ: whom
    • te: your
    • śyenaś: of the eagle
    • cārum: beautiful
    • avṛkam: of the wolf
    • padābharad: has placed on the path
    • aruṇam: the reddish dawn
    • mānam: respect or honor
    • andhasaḥ: of the blind
    • enā: by this
    • vayo: our age
    • vi: indeed
    • tāry: may be saved
    • āyur: life
    • jīvasa: of the living
    • enā: by this
    • jāgāra: vigilance
    • bandhutā: kinship or connection
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the divine connection and protection that one seeks from the higher powers.
    • The reference to the eagle and the wolf symbolizes strength and vigilance, suggesting that one should embody these qualities in life.
    • The "reddish dawn" signifies new beginnings and hope, indicating that every day brings a chance for renewal and growth.
    • The mention of "blindness" can be interpreted as ignorance or lack of awareness, urging individuals to seek knowledge and enlightenment.
    • The mantra emphasizes the importance of kinship and community, highlighting that our lives are interconnected.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, embodying the qualities of the eagle (vision) and the wolf (teamwork) can lead to successful collaborations and projects.
    • In personal life, the mantra encourages individuals to support one another, fostering a sense of community and belonging.
    • During challenging times, reflecting on the "reddish dawn" can inspire hope and motivate individuals to persevere through difficulties.
    • Seeking knowledge and awareness can be seen in educational pursuits, where individuals strive to overcome ignorance and enhance their understanding of the world.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the strength found in community and the importance of vigilance in our lives.
    • It encourages individuals to seek knowledge, embrace new beginnings, and honor the connections we share with others.
    • In essence, it highlights the significance of living a life filled with purpose, awareness, and mutual support.
