यो मे शतां च विष्टिं च गोनाṁ हरī च युक्ता सुदुरā ददाति |वैश्वानर सुष्टुतो वावृद्धानो 'ग्ने यच्च त्र्यरुणाय शरमा ||
yo me śatā ca viṁśatiṁ ca gonāṁ harī ca yuktā sudhurā dadāti | vaiśvānara suṣṭuto vāvṛdhāno 'gne yaccha tryaruṇāya śarma ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yo: who
    • me: to me
    • śatā: of hundreds
    • ca: and
    • viṁśatiṁ: of twenty
    • ca: and
    • gonāṁ: of the cows
    • harī: the divine
    • ca: and
    • yuktā: connected
    • sudhurā: very difficult
    • dadāti: gives
    • vaiśvānara: the universal fire
    • suṣṭuto: well praised
    • vāvṛdhāno: increasing
    • agne: O fire
    • yaccha: grant
    • tryaruṇāya: to the threefold
    • śarma: protection
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the divine forces represented by fire (Agni) and seeks blessings for abundance and protection.
    • The mention of "hundreds" and "twenties" signifies a request for prosperity and wealth, often symbolized by cattle in ancient times.
    • The term "harī" refers to the divine, indicating a connection to higher spiritual powers.
    • The phrase "sudhurā dadāti" suggests that the blessings being sought are not easily attained, emphasizing the need for sincere devotion and effort.
    • "Vaiśvānara" represents the universal aspect of fire, which is essential for life, symbolizing nourishment and energy.
    • The request for "protection" (śarma) highlights the importance of safety and security in one's life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In agricultural societies, the prosperity of livestock (cows) is crucial for sustenance, making this mantra relevant for farmers seeking blessings for their herds.
    • In modern contexts, individuals may recite this mantra during times of financial difficulty, seeking divine intervention for abundance and stability.
    • In spiritual practices, this mantra can be used in rituals to invoke protection and blessings for families and communities.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the interconnectedness of divine blessings, prosperity, and protection.
    • Its significance lies in the recognition of the challenges in attaining wealth and security, encouraging individuals to seek higher spiritual guidance.
    • In real-life scenarios, it serves as a reminder to cultivate gratitude and connection with the divine while striving for material and spiritual well-being.
