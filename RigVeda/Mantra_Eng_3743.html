असम्मृष्टो जायसे मात्रोः शुचिर मन्द्रः कविर उद अतिष्ठो विवस्वतः |घृतेन त्वावर्धयन्न अग्न आहुत धूमस ते केतुर्भवद दिवि श्रितः ||
asammṛṣṭo jāyase mātroḥ śucir mandraḥ kavir ud atiṣṭho vivasvataḥ | ghṛtena tvāvardhayann agna āhuta dhūmas te ketur abhavad divi śritaḥ ||
  • asammṛṣṭo jāyase mātroḥ: This phrase can be translated to "You are born from the untainted (pure) mother." It signifies the idea of purity and the importance of a clean origin. In real life, this can be related to the concept of nurturing environments, such as a child being raised in a loving and supportive family, which shapes their character and future.
  • śucir mandraḥ: This means "pure and gentle." It emphasizes the qualities of purity and gentleness, which are essential in various aspects of life, such as in relationships and communication. For example, a gentle approach in conflict resolution can lead to more harmonious outcomes.
  • kavir ud atiṣṭho vivasvataḥ: Translated, this means "the wise one rises above the sun." This suggests enlightenment and wisdom that transcends ordinary understanding. In practical terms, it can refer to individuals who seek knowledge and understanding beyond superficial appearances, such as scientists or philosophers who challenge existing paradigms.
  • ghṛtena tvāvardhayann agna: This phrase translates to "by ghee (clarified butter), we nourish you, O Agni (the fire)." Ghee is often used in rituals and symbolizes nourishment and sustenance. In everyday life, this can be seen in the importance of proper nutrition and care for one's body and mind, akin to how we fuel our passions and ambitions.
  • āhuta dhūmas te ketur abhavad divi śritaḥ: This means "the smoke of your offerings has become your banner in the sky." It signifies the idea that our actions and offerings create a legacy that is visible to the world. In real life, this can be related to how our contributions to society, whether through charity, art, or innovation, leave a lasting impact and can inspire others.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of purity, wisdom, nourishment, and the legacy of our actions. Its significance lies in the reminder to cultivate purity in our thoughts and actions, seek wisdom, nourish our passions, and recognize that our contributions shape our identity and influence the world around us. In real-life scenarios, this mantra encourages individuals to strive for personal growth, maintain integrity, and leave a positive mark on society, much like the guiding principles of many ethical frameworks and philosophies.
