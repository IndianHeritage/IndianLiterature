चित्रो वोऽस्तु यामश्चित्र ऊती सुदानवः |मरुतो अहिभानवः ||
citro vo 'stu yāmaś citra ūtī sudānavaḥ | maruto ahibhānavaḥ ||
  • Word Breakdown:
    • citro: This word can be interpreted as "brilliant" or "illuminated." It signifies clarity and enlightenment.
    • vo: This is a pronoun meaning "your" or "of you," indicating a connection to the audience or the divine.
    • stu: This means "may I praise" or "let us praise," suggesting an act of reverence or acknowledgment.
    • yāmaś: This word translates to "together" or "in unison," emphasizing the importance of collective action or thought.
    • citra: Similar to "citro," it means "varied" or "diverse," highlighting the beauty in diversity.
    • ūtī: This refers to "wealth" or "prosperity," indicating a desire for abundance.
    • sudānavaḥ: This can be interpreted as "good leaders" or "noble ones," referring to those who guide and inspire others.
    • maruto: This word refers to the "Maruts," the storm deities, symbolizing power and energy.
    • ahibhānavaḥ: This translates to "those who are like serpents," which can symbolize transformation and the dynamic nature of life.
  • Meaning and Interpretation:
    • The mantra begins with a call for brilliance and illumination, suggesting that we seek clarity in our thoughts and actions.
    • It emphasizes the importance of collective praise and acknowledgment, reminding us that we are stronger together.
    • The mention of diversity and prosperity indicates that we should celebrate our differences and strive for abundance in our lives.
    • By invoking the Maruts, the mantra connects us to the forces of nature, urging us to harness energy and power in our endeavors.
    • The reference to transformation through the imagery of serpents suggests that change is a natural part of life, and we should embrace it.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace setting, this mantra can inspire teamwork and collaboration, where diverse talents come together to achieve common goals.
    • In community initiatives, it encourages individuals to celebrate their unique backgrounds while working towards shared prosperity.
    • In personal development, it serves as a reminder to seek clarity in our thoughts and embrace change as a pathway to growth.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of unity, diversity, and the pursuit of clarity and prosperity in our lives.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal growth to community building, reminding us of the power of collective action and the beauty of diversity.
    • By embracing these principles, we can foster a more harmonious and prosperous existence, both individually and collectively.
