व्रजं वज्री गवामिव सिषासन्न अङ्गिरस्तमः |अपावृणोद इष इन्द्रः परिवृताः द्वार इषः परिवृताः ||
vrajaṁ vajrī gavām iva siṣāsann aṅgirastamaḥ | apāvṛṇod iṣa indraḥ parīvṛtā dvāra iṣaḥ parīvṛtāḥ ||
  • Mantra: "vrajaṁ vajrī gavām iva siṣāsann aṅgirastamaḥ | apāvṛṇod iṣa indraḥ parīvṛtā dvāra iṣaḥ parīvṛtāḥ ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • vrajaṁ: herd or group
    • vajrī: one who wields a thunderbolt (Indra)
    • gavām: of the cows
    • iva: like or as
    • siṣāsann: having approached or come near
    • aṅgirastamaḥ: the most excellent or supreme
    • apāvṛṇod: may he open or remove
    • iṣa: the command or will
    • indraḥ: Indra, the king of gods
    • parīvṛtā: surrounded or enveloped
    • dvāra: door or gateway
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes Indra, the deity associated with strength, rain, and the protection of the herd (cows).
    • The imagery of a herd of cows suggests abundance, nourishment, and prosperity.
    • Indra is depicted as a powerful figure who can remove obstacles and grant access to prosperity and well-being.
    • The phrase "parīvṛtā dvāra" indicates the idea of being surrounded by challenges or barriers, which Indra is called upon to help overcome.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In agriculture, farmers often pray to Indra for rain, which is essential for the growth of crops and the well-being of livestock.
    • In a business context, this mantra can symbolize the need for overcoming obstacles to achieve success, much like a company seeking to break into a new market.
    • In personal life, individuals may face challenges that seem insurmountable, and invoking the strength of Indra can be a metaphor for seeking support and guidance to navigate through difficulties.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of divine support in overcoming challenges and achieving prosperity.
    • It serves as a reminder that, like Indra, we can seek strength and guidance in times of need, whether in personal endeavors, professional pursuits, or communal efforts.
    • By recognizing the barriers we face and calling upon the strength to overcome them, we can foster a sense of resilience and hope in our lives.
