मन्त्र: तिस्रो भूमीर धारयन त्रिम् उता dyūn त्रिणि व्रता विदथे अन्तर एषाम् | ऋतेनादित्य महि वो महित्वं तद् आर्यमन् वरुण मित्र चारु ||
tisro bhūmīr dhārayan trīm̐r uta dyūn trīṇi vratā vidathe antar eṣām | ṛtenādityā mahi vo mahitvaṁ tad aryaman varuṇa mitra cāru ||
  • tisro bhūmīr dhārayan: This phrase translates to "holding the three realms of the earth." In a real-life context, this can be understood as the interconnectedness of our physical environment, society, and individual consciousness. For example, sustainable living practices can be seen as a way to honor and maintain the balance of these three realms.
  • trīm̐r uta dyūn: This means "the three heavens." It signifies the spiritual dimensions that exist beyond our physical reality. In modern life, this can relate to the pursuit of higher knowledge, wisdom, and spiritual growth, akin to seeking enlightenment through education or meditation.
  • trīṇi vratā vidathe antar eṣām: This translates to "the three vows within the assembly." It emphasizes the importance of commitment and integrity in our actions and relationships. In everyday life, this can be reflected in personal commitments, such as family responsibilities, professional ethics, and community service.
  • ṛtenādityā mahi vo mahitvaṁ: This phrase means "by truth, O Adityas, may your greatness be." It highlights the significance of truth and righteousness in achieving greatness. In practical terms, this can be seen in the importance of honesty in business practices, which fosters trust and long-term success.
  • tad aryaman varuṇa mitra cāru: This translates to "that Aryaman, Varuna, and the beautiful Mitra." These deities symbolize various aspects of life, such as friendship, cosmic order, and moral integrity. In real life, this can be interpreted as the importance of building strong, ethical relationships and maintaining harmony in our interactions with others.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of balance, commitment, truth, and ethical relationships in life. Its significance lies in the reminder to honor our responsibilities towards the environment, society, and ourselves. By applying these principles, we can foster a harmonious existence that promotes personal growth and collective well-being. In today's world, this mantra serves as a guiding philosophy for sustainable living, ethical practices, and the pursuit of spiritual and moral integrity.
