मन्त्र: परि त्वा गिरवणो गिर इमा भवन्तु विश्वतः | वृद्धायुम् अनु वृद्धयः जुष्टा भवन्तु जुष्टयः ||
pari tvā girvaṇo gira imā bhavantu viśvataḥ | vṛddhāyum anu vṛddhayo juṣṭā bhavantu juṣṭayaḥ ||
  • Mantra: "pari tvā girvaṇo gira imā bhavantu viśvataḥ | vṛddhāyum anu vṛddhayo juṣṭā bhavantu juṣṭayaḥ ||"
  • Translation: "May these words of praise come to you from all directions; may they be filled with abundance and growth, and may they bring forth prosperity and well-being."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • pari: around, all around
    • tvā: you
    • girvaṇo: of the singers, of the poets
    • gira: words, speech
    • imā: these
    • bhavantu: may they become, may they manifest
    • viśvataḥ: from all sides, universally
    • vṛddhāyum: growth, increase
    • anu: following, in accordance with
    • vṛddhayo: those who are growing, those who increase
    • juṣṭā: filled, endowed
    • bhavantu: may they become
    • juṣṭayaḥ: prosperity, abundance
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, this mantra can be invoked during meetings to encourage collaboration and creativity among team members, fostering an environment where ideas flow freely from all directions.
    • In personal life, families can recite this mantra during gatherings to promote harmony and growth, ensuring that every member feels valued and contributes to the collective well-being.
    • In community events, this mantra can serve as a reminder to embrace diversity and inclusivity, allowing voices from all backgrounds to be heard and appreciated.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for collective growth and prosperity. It emphasizes the importance of unity and the power of words in shaping our reality. In real-life scenarios, whether in personal relationships, professional environments, or community interactions, invoking this mantra can inspire individuals to work together towards common goals, fostering an atmosphere of support and abundance. By recognizing the value of contributions from all sides, we can create a more harmonious and prosperous existence for ourselves and those around us.
