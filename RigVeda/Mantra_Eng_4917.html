अपि पन्थाम् अगन्महि स्वस्तिगाम् अनहसम् |येन विश्वाः परि द्विषो वृणक्ति विन्दते वसु ||
api panthām aganmahi svastigām anehasam | yena viśvāḥ pari dviṣo vṛṇakti vindate vasu ||
  • api - This word translates to "even" or "also," indicating inclusivity or a broader perspective.
  • panthām - This means "path" or "way," referring to the journey or direction one takes in life.
  • aganmahi - This translates to "we have come" or "we have arrived," suggesting a sense of achievement or reaching a destination.
  • svastigām - This means "to auspiciousness" or "to well-being," indicating a positive outcome or a state of prosperity.
  • anehasam - This translates to "without darkness" or "illuminated," symbolizing clarity, knowledge, and enlightenment.
  • yena - This means "by which," indicating a means or method to achieve something.
  • viśvāḥ - This translates to "all" or "universal," referring to the entirety of existence or the collective.
  • pari - This means "around" or "in all directions," suggesting a comprehensive or holistic approach.
  • dviṣo - This translates to "enemies" or "those who oppose," indicating challenges or adversities one may face.
  • vṛṇakti - This means "chooses" or "selects," referring to the act of making decisions or taking actions.
  • vindate - This translates to "obtains" or "achieves," indicating success or attainment.
  • vasu - This means "wealth" or "prosperity," symbolizing material and spiritual abundance.

The mantra can be interpreted as a prayer or invocation for guidance on the right path that leads to well-being and prosperity, free from darkness and ignorance. It emphasizes the importance of choosing the right actions to overcome adversities and achieve success in life.


In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts:

  • Personal Growth: Individuals can reflect on their life choices and paths, seeking clarity and enlightenment to make decisions that lead to personal and professional success.
  • Conflict Resolution: In situations of conflict or opposition, this mantra encourages individuals to choose paths that promote understanding and resolution, rather than hostility.
  • Community Well-being: On a larger scale, communities can adopt this mantra to foster unity and collective prosperity, working together to overcome challenges and achieve common goals.

Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for a conscious and enlightened approach to life. It serves as a reminder to seek auspicious paths, make wise choices, and strive for collective well-being, ultimately leading to a prosperous and harmonious existence.
