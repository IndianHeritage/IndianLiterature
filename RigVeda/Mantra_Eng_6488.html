वयं हि त्वा बन्धुमन्तम अबन्धवो विप्रास इन्द्र येमिम |या ते धामानि वृषभ तेभिर आगहि विश्वेभिः सोमपीतये ||
vayaṁ hi tvā bandhumantam abandhavo viprāsa indra yemima | yā te dhāmāni vṛṣabha tebhir ā gahi viśvebhiḥ somapītaye ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • vayaṁ: We
    • hi: indeed
    • tvā: you
    • bandhumantam: the one who is connected (to us)
    • abandhavo: without bonds
    • viprāsa: O wise one
    • indra: O Indra (the king of gods)
    • yemima: who is here
    • yā: who
    • te: your
    • dhāmāni: abodes
    • vṛṣabha: O bull (symbol of strength)
    • tebhir: with these
    • ā gahi: come forth
    • viśvebhiḥ: with all
    • somapītaye: for the enjoyment of Soma (the divine nectar)
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra addresses Indra, the chief deity in Vedic tradition, invoking his presence and strength.
    • The phrase "vayaṁ hi tvā bandhumantam" suggests a deep connection and relationship with the divine, emphasizing that we are all interconnected.
    • The term "abhandhavo" indicates a state of being free from worldly bonds, highlighting the importance of spiritual liberation.
    • By calling Indra "vṛṣabha," the mantra acknowledges his power and strength, which can be seen as a metaphor for overcoming challenges in life.
    • The request for Indra to "come forth" with "viśvebhiḥ" signifies a collective call for divine assistance, emphasizing community and unity in seeking spiritual nourishment.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In times of personal or communal crisis, individuals often turn to spiritual practices or rituals, similar to invoking Indra for strength and guidance.
    • The concept of interconnectedness can be observed in community service, where individuals come together to support one another, reflecting the idea of being "bandhumantam."
    • In modern contexts, the pursuit of spiritual liberation can be seen in mindfulness practices, yoga, and meditation, which aim to free individuals from the bonds of stress and anxiety.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of seeking divine guidance and strength in our lives.
    • It emphasizes the interconnectedness of all beings and the need for community support in overcoming life's challenges.
    • In a broader sense, it encourages individuals to pursue spiritual growth and liberation, fostering a sense of unity and purpose in the journey of life.
