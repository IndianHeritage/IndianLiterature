मन्त्र: सूरो न यस्य दृष्टिरarepā भीमा यद एति शुचतस् त आ धीः |हेषस्वतः शुरुधो नायमक्तोः कुत्राचिद रण्वो वसतिर वनेजाः ||
sūro na yasya dṛśatir arepā bhīmā yad eti śucatas ta ā dhīḥ | heṣasvataḥ śurudho nāyam aktoḥ kutrā cid raṇvo vasatir vanejāḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sūro: The brave or the hero.
    • na: Not.
    • yasya: Whose.
    • dṛśatir: Vision or sight.
    • arepā: Is not disturbed or shaken.
    • bhīmā: Fearsome or formidable.
    • yad: That which.
    • eti: Goes or moves.
    • śucatas: Purely or with purity.
    • ta: Those.
    • ā: To or towards.
    • dhīḥ: Intellect or understanding.
    • heṣasvataḥ: Of the one who is steadfast.
    • śurudho: Of the brave or the strong.
    • nāyam: This is not.
    • aktoḥ: Of the one who is not engaged.
    • kutrā: Where.
    • cid: At all.
    • raṇvo: In battle.
    • vasatir: Dwelling or residing.
    • vanejāḥ: In the forest or wilderness.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the qualities of a true hero or brave individual.
    • It emphasizes the importance of having a clear vision and being undisturbed by fear.
    • The mantra suggests that true strength lies in the purity of one's intentions and intellect.
    • It also implies that a true warrior does not engage in battle without purpose or understanding.
    • The reference to dwelling in the wilderness can symbolize the need for solitude and reflection in the pursuit of wisdom.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • A leader who remains calm and focused during a crisis exemplifies the qualities described in this mantra.
    • Individuals who pursue their goals with clarity and integrity, despite challenges, embody the essence of a true hero.
    • In personal development, taking time for self-reflection and understanding one's motivations can lead to greater wisdom and strength.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of inner strength, clarity of vision, and the importance of pure intentions.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, such as leadership, personal growth, and ethical decision-making.
    • By embodying these qualities, individuals can navigate challenges with courage and wisdom, ultimately contributing to their personal and communal well-being.
