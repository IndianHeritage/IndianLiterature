एना विश्वानि आर्य आद्युम्नानि मानवशाणाम् |सिषासन्तो वनामहे ||
enā viśvāny arya ā dyumnāni mānuṣāṇām | siṣāsanto vanāmahe ||
  • Mantra: "enā viśvāny arya ā dyumnāni mānuṣāṇām | siṣāsanto vanāmahe ||"
  • Translation: "With these, O Aryans, we invoke the glorious achievements of mankind; may we be blessed with abundance."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • enā: with these
    • viśvāny: all, universal
    • arya: noble, honorable
    • ā: towards
    • dyumnāni: glorious achievements, fame
    • mānuṣāṇām: of mankind
    • siṣāsanto: may we be blessed
    • vanāmahe: we invoke, we call upon
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the collective achievements and aspirations of humanity.
    • The term "Arya" refers to those who are noble and virtuous, suggesting that the invocation is directed towards individuals who embody these qualities.
    • The phrase "glorious achievements of mankind" highlights the importance of striving for excellence and success in various fields, such as science, art, and community service.
    • In real life, this can be seen in the efforts of individuals and groups working towards social justice, environmental sustainability, and technological advancements.
    • The invocation for blessings signifies a desire for prosperity and well-being, not just for oneself but for the community as a whole.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of humanity and the importance of working together towards common goals.
    • It encourages individuals to aspire for greatness while also uplifting others, fostering a sense of community and shared purpose.
    • In practical terms, this can be applied in various scenarios, such as collaborative projects, community service initiatives, and even in personal relationships where mutual support and encouragement are vital.
    • Ultimately, the mantra inspires a vision of a harmonious society where the achievements of one contribute to the well-being of all.
