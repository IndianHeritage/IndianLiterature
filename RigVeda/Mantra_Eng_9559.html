मन्त्र: उता त्वं सख्ये स्थिरपीतमाहुर नैनं हिन्वन्त्यपि वाजिनेषु |अधेन्वा चरति माययैष वाचं शुश्रुवाम् aphalाम् अपुष्पाम् ||
uta tvaṁ sakhye sthirapītam āhur nainaṁ hinvanty api vājineṣu | adhenvā carati māyayaiṣa vācaṁ śuśruvām̐ aphalām apuṣpām ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • uta: indeed, or also
    • tvaṁ: you
    • sakhye: in friendship
    • sthirapītam: stable, firm, or steadfast
    • āhur: they say
    • nainaṁ: this (one)
    • hinvanty: can harm or diminish
    • api: even
    • vājineṣu: among the strong or powerful
    • adhenvā: indeed, or truly
    • carati: moves or acts
    • māyayaiṣa: this is an illusion
    • vācaṁ: speech or words
    • śuśruvām̐: I have heard
    • aphalām: without fruit or result
    • apuṣpām: without flowers or beauty
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the strength of friendship and the power of words.
    • It emphasizes that true friendship is steadfast and cannot be diminished by external forces, even among the powerful.
    • The phrase "this is an illusion" suggests that the challenges we face in relationships or communication may not be as significant as they seem.
    • It highlights the importance of meaningful communication, as words without substance (aphalām apuṣpām) lack true value.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace, a strong team built on trust and friendship can withstand pressures and challenges, demonstrating that unity is a source of strength.
    • In personal relationships, friends who support each other through difficult times exemplify the idea that true friendship remains unshaken by external circumstances.
    • In public speaking or communication, delivering impactful messages requires substance; mere words without depth do not resonate with the audience.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the enduring nature of true friendship and the importance of meaningful communication.
    • It teaches us that while challenges may arise, the bonds we form can provide stability and strength.
    • In real life, it encourages us to cultivate genuine relationships and to ensure that our words carry weight and significance.
    • Ultimately, it reminds us that the essence of our connections and the authenticity of our communication are what truly matter.
