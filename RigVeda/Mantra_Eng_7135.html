धृततश्चिद्धृतशनमनः कृणोषीन्द्र यत् त्वम् |तीव्रैः सोमैः सपर्यतो नमोभिः प्रतिभूषतो भद्राः इन्द्रस्य रातयः ||
dhṛṣataś cid dhṛṣan manaḥ kṛṇoṣīndra yat tvam | tīvraiḥ somaiḥ saparyato namobhiḥ pratibhūṣato bhadrā indrasya rātayaḥ ||
  • dhṛṣataś cid: This phrase can be interpreted as "with courage and awareness." It emphasizes the importance of being bold and conscious in our actions and thoughts.
  • dhṛṣan manaḥ: This translates to "the mind that is courageous." It suggests that a strong and fearless mindset is essential for achieving one's goals.
  • kṛṇoṣīndra: This can be understood as "O Indra, you who create." Indra is often regarded as the king of the gods in Vedic literature, symbolizing power and authority.
  • yat tvam: This means "whatever you are." It acknowledges the divine presence and the qualities of Indra that inspire strength and valor.
  • tīvraiḥ somaiḥ: This phrase refers to "with intense offerings." It highlights the significance of dedication and devotion in our pursuits.
  • saparyato: This means "with reverence." It indicates that respect and honor are crucial in our interactions, especially with the divine.
  • namobhiḥ: This translates to "with salutations." It emphasizes the importance of humility and gratitude in our approach to life.
  • pratibhūṣato: This can be interpreted as "shining forth." It suggests that the qualities we cultivate within ourselves will manifest outwardly.
  • bhadrā indrasya rātayaḥ: This means "the auspicious paths of Indra." It signifies the favorable directions and opportunities that arise when we align ourselves with divine qualities.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of courage, awareness, and reverence in our lives. It teaches us that by cultivating a fearless mindset and dedicating ourselves to our goals with respect and humility, we can attract auspicious opportunities. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as facing challenges in personal or professional life, where a courageous approach and a respectful attitude can lead to success and fulfillment. By embodying these principles, we align ourselves with the divine energies represented by Indra, paving the way for a prosperous and meaningful existence.
