मन्त्र: न त्वा दधे वरेण्यं दक्षस्येळा सहस्कृत |अग्नि सुदीतिम उषिजम् ||
ni tvā dadhe vareṇyaṁ dakṣasyeḻā sahaskṛta | agne sudītim uśijam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ni: a prefix indicating negation or emphasis.
    • tvā: you (referring to Agni, the fire god).
    • dadhe: I place or I establish.
    • vareṇyaṁ: the most excellent or worthy.
    • dakṣasyeḻā: the skill or ability (often associated with dexterity and competence).
    • sahaskṛta: accomplished or achieved with effort.
    • agne: O Agni (the fire god).
    • Sudītim: clarity or brilliance.
    • uśijam: the one who is pure or the purifier.
  • Overall Meaning: This mantra is a prayer to Agni, the fire god, expressing the speaker's intention to establish a connection with the divine through the offering of their best qualities and efforts. It emphasizes the importance of skill, clarity, and purity in one's actions and intentions.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, when someone takes on a project, they may invoke the spirit of this mantra by dedicating their best efforts and skills to achieve excellence, much like a chef who meticulously prepares a meal to honor their guests.
    • In personal relationships, individuals can embody the essence of this mantra by striving for clarity and purity in their communication, ensuring that their intentions are understood and appreciated, similar to how a mentor guides a mentee with wisdom and care.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for individuals to recognize and harness their inner strengths and virtues. By dedicating oneself to excellence and purity in thought and action, one can foster a deeper connection with the divine and contribute positively to the world around them. This mantra is applicable in various life scenarios, from personal growth to professional endeavors, encouraging a mindset of dedication, clarity, and skillful action.
