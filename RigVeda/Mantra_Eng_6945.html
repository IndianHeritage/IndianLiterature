यद वीजाव इन्द्र यत स्थिरे यत पार्शाने पराभृतम् |वसु स्पार्हं तद आ भर ||
yad vīḻāv indra yat sthire yat parśāne parābhṛtam | vasu spārhaṁ tad ā bhara ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yad: whatever
    • vīḻāv: is powerful or strong
    • indra: O Indra (the king of gods)
    • yat: whatever
    • sthire: is stable or firm
    • yat: whatever
    • parśāne: in the realms of the heavens
    • parābhṛtam: that which is supreme or exalted
    • vasu: wealth or prosperity
    • spārhaṁ: desire or aspiration
    • tad: that
    • ā: may it come
    • bhara: bring or bestow
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Indra, the god of rain and thunderstorms, asking for strength and stability in life.
    • The repetition of "yad" emphasizes the various aspects of life that one seeks to be strong and stable.
    • The mention of "vasu" indicates a desire for wealth and prosperity, which is a common aspiration in human life.
    • It reflects the human quest for stability in both material and spiritual realms.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be applied to the pursuit of a stable and prosperous enterprise. Entrepreneurs often seek the strength to overcome challenges and the stability to grow their businesses.
    • In personal life, individuals may recite this mantra when facing uncertainties, seeking the strength to maintain their relationships and the stability to provide for their families.
    • In community settings, leaders may invoke this mantra to inspire collective strength and stability, fostering a sense of unity and shared prosperity among members.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of seeking strength and stability in all aspects of life, whether personal, professional, or communal.
    • It serves as a reminder that the pursuit of wealth and prosperity should be grounded in a firm foundation of strength and stability.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be a source of inspiration and motivation, encouraging individuals and communities to strive for a balanced and prosperous existence.
