धन्वना गा धन्वनाजीं जयेम धन्वना तीव्राः समदो जयेम |धनुः शत्रोर अपकामं कृतोति धन्वना सर्वाः प्रदिशो जयेम ||
dhanvanā gā dhanvanājiṁ jayema dhanvanā tīvrāḥ samado jayema | dhanuḥ śatror apakāmaṁ kṛṇoti dhanvanā sarvāḥ pradiśo jayema ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • dhanvanā: By the bow (or the one who wields the bow)
    • gā: Let us sing or praise
    • dhanvanājiṁ: The one who is skilled in archery
    • jayema: May we conquer or triumph
    • tīvrāḥ: Fierce or intense
    • samado: Equally or uniformly
    • dhanuḥ: The bow
    • śatror: Of the enemy
    • apakāmaṁ: That which is desired or sought
    • kṛṇoti: Creates or brings forth
    • sarvāḥ: All or every
    • pradiśo: Directions or realms
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the power and skill associated with archery, symbolizing strength, focus, and determination.
    • The bow represents the ability to achieve goals and overcome obstacles, while the archer symbolizes the individual who is skilled and determined.
    • The repetition of "jayema" signifies a call for victory, not just in battle but in all aspects of life.
    • The phrase "dhanvanā tīvrāḥ samado" suggests that one should be fierce yet balanced in their approach to challenges.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In sports, an athlete must harness their skills (the bow) and maintain focus (the archer) to achieve victory (jayema).
    • In business, a leader must navigate challenges with intensity and balance, ensuring that all team members are aligned towards a common goal.
    • In personal development, individuals can apply the principles of this mantra by setting clear goals (the target) and working diligently (the bow) to achieve them.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of skill, focus, and determination in achieving success.
    • It encourages individuals to harness their inner strength and approach challenges with both intensity and balance.
    • In various aspects of life—be it personal, professional, or spiritual—this mantra can inspire one to strive for victory and overcome obstacles.
