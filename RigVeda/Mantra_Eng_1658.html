उत मन्ये पितुर अद्रुहो मनो मातुर महि स्वतवस् तद धवीमभिः |सुरेतसā पितरā भूम चक्रतुर उरु प्रजायā अमृतं वरīmभिः ||
uta manye pitur adruho mano mātur mahi svatavas tad dhavīmabhiḥ | suretasā pitarā bhūma cakratur uru prajāyā amṛtaṁ varīmabhiḥ ||
  • Translation: "I invoke the ungrudging mind of my father and the great mind of my mother; may they, with their wisdom, bestow upon us the immortal wealth of progeny."

  • Word Breakdown:
    • uta: indeed, or also
    • manye: I think or I consider
    • pitur: of the father
    • adruho: ungrudging or without malice
    • mano: mind
    • mātur: of the mother
    • mahi: great or vast
    • svatavas: with their wisdom or intelligence
    • tad: that
    • dhavīmabhiḥ: may they bestow
    • suretasā: with wisdom or intelligence
    • pitarā: fathers or ancestors
    • bhūma: may they create
    • cakratur: may they accomplish
    • uru: vast or abundant
    • prajāyā: of progeny or offspring
    • amṛtaṁ: immortal or eternal
    • varīmabhiḥ: with wealth or abundance

  • Contextual Significance:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of parental blessings and wisdom in the journey of life.
    • It reflects the deep respect and reverence for one's parents, acknowledging their role in shaping one's character and destiny.
    • The invocation of "immortal wealth" signifies the desire for not just material prosperity but also spiritual and intellectual growth.

  • Real-Life Application:
    • In modern society, this mantra can be applied in family dynamics, where the wisdom of parents is sought in making important life decisions.
    • It can also be relevant in educational settings, where the guidance of mentors and elders is crucial for personal development.
    • In a broader sense, it encourages individuals to seek knowledge and wisdom from their heritage and to honor their roots.

  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a reminder of the invaluable contributions of parents and ancestors in our lives. It highlights the significance of seeking their wisdom and blessings to achieve a fulfilling and prosperous life. By acknowledging and respecting our lineage, we can cultivate a sense of gratitude and responsibility towards future generations, ensuring the continuity of wisdom and values.
