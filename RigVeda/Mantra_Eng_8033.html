संवृक्तधृष्णुम उक्त्यम महामहिव्रतम मदम् |शतम पुरो रुरुक्षाणिम ||
saṁvṛktadhṛṣṇum ukthyam mahāmahivratam madam | śatam puro rurukṣaṇim ||
  • saṁvṛktadhṛṣṇum: This term can be broken down into two parts: "saṁvṛkta" meaning "enveloped" or "surrounded," and "dhṛṣṇum," which refers to "strength" or "power." Together, it suggests a state of being surrounded by strength or power.
  • ukthyam: This word translates to "that which is spoken" or "the utterance." It signifies the importance of words and their power in shaping reality and invoking divine energies.
  • mahāmahivratam: This term can be understood as "the great vow of the supreme." "Mahā" means "great," "mahi" refers to "the supreme," and "vratam" means "vow" or "commitment." This indicates a profound commitment to a higher purpose or spiritual path.
  • madam: This word translates to "intoxication" or "ecstasy." It can refer to a state of bliss or heightened awareness that comes from spiritual practices or experiences.
  • śatam: This means "hundred" or "many," often used to denote abundance or a multitude of blessings.
  • puro: This term means "in front" or "before," indicating a position of prominence or priority.
  • rurukṣaṇim: This word can be interpreted as "the one who is to be invoked" or "the one who is desired." It suggests a longing or aspiration towards a divine presence or energy.

Conclusion: The mantra "saṁvṛktadhṛṣṇum ukthyam mahāmahivratam madam | śatam puro rurukṣaṇim ||" encapsulates the essence of invoking strength and divine energy through powerful words and commitments. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios such as:


  • Personal Development: By surrounding oneself with positive influences and committing to personal growth, individuals can harness their inner strength and achieve their goals.
  • Spiritual Practices: Engaging in meditation or prayer can create a state of bliss and connection to the divine, enhancing one's spiritual journey.
  • Community Building: The collective strength of a community can be invoked through shared intentions and commitments, leading to a supportive environment for all members.

This mantra serves as a reminder of the power of words, intentions, and the strength that comes from a committed spiritual path, encouraging individuals to seek abundance and divine connection in their lives.
