विश्वं प्रतिची सप्रथā उद अस्ताद् रुशद् वासो बिभ्रती शुक्रम् अश्वैत् |हिरण्यवर्णा सुदृशीकसंदृग् गावां माता नेत्र्य अह्नां अरोचि ||
viśvam pratīcī saprathā ud asthād ruśad vāso bibhratī śukram aśvait | hiraṇyavarṇā sudṛśīkasaṁdṛg gavām mātā netry ahnām aroci ||
  • viśvam pratīcī saprathā: This phrase can be translated as "the whole universe, with its diverse aspects." It signifies the interconnectedness of all beings and elements in the cosmos. In real life, this can be seen in the way ecosystems function, where every organism plays a role in maintaining balance.
  • ud asthād ruśad vāso: This means "it has risen, shining brightly." This can be interpreted as the emergence of knowledge or enlightenment. For example, when a person gains insight or understanding about a complex issue, it can be likened to a light illuminating a dark room.
  • bibhratī śukram aśvait: This translates to "it carries the pure and the swift." Here, 'pure' can refer to truth or righteousness, while 'swift' can denote the speed of thought or action. In everyday life, this can be observed in individuals who act with integrity and decisiveness, leading to positive outcomes.
  • hiraṇyavarṇā: This term means "golden-colored" or "radiant." It symbolizes prosperity and abundance. In practical terms, this can relate to the pursuit of wealth or success in one's career, where hard work and dedication lead to fruitful results.
  • sudṛśīkasaṁdṛg: This phrase translates to "having a beautiful vision." It emphasizes the importance of perspective and clarity in life. For instance, a leader with a clear vision can inspire and guide their team towards achieving common goals.
  • gavām mātā: This means "the mother of cows," which symbolizes nourishment and sustenance. In a broader sense, it can represent the nurturing aspects of life, such as family and community support, which are essential for personal growth and well-being.
  • netry ahnām aroci: This translates to "the eye of the day has shone." It signifies the arrival of a new dawn or opportunity. In real life, this can be seen when individuals seize new opportunities for growth, such as starting a new job or embarking on a new project.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of universal interconnectedness, enlightenment, purity, prosperity, vision, nourishment, and new opportunities. Its significance lies in reminding us of the holistic nature of existence and the importance of striving for knowledge, integrity, and community support. In real-life scenarios, these principles can guide individuals in their personal and professional lives, encouraging them to seek balance, act with purpose, and embrace new beginnings.
