एकं नु त्वा सत्पतिम पाञ्चजन्यं जातं श्रिणोमि यशसं जनेषु |तम मे जगृभ्र आशसो नविष्ठं दोषा वस्तोर हवमानासा इन्द्रम् ||
ekaṁ nu tvā satpatim pāñcajanyaṁ jātaṁ śṛṇomi yaśasaṁ janeṣu | tam me jagṛbhra āśaso naviṣṭhaṁ doṣā vastor havamānāsa indram ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ekaṁ: one
    • nu: indeed
    • tvā: you
    • satpatim: the true path or the righteous leader
    • pāñcajanyaṁ: the conch (symbolizing victory and auspiciousness)
    • jātaṁ: born or arisen
    • śṛṇomi: I hear or I listen
    • yaśasaṁ: of fame or glory
    • janeṣu: among people
    • tam: that
    • me: my
    • jagṛbhra: may you grasp or hold
    • āśaso: of the auspicious
    • naviṣṭhaṁ: the best or the most excellent
    • doṣā: faults or imperfections
    • vastor: of the substance or the essence
    • havamānāsa: invoking or calling upon
    • indram: Indra, the king of gods
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra expresses a deep reverence for the divine and the pursuit of righteousness.
    • The speaker acknowledges the singularity of the divine presence and seeks guidance on the true path.
    • The mention of "pāñcajanyaṁ" symbolizes the call to action and the awakening of one's inner strength.
    • By listening to the divine, one can attain fame and glory among people, emphasizing the importance of spiritual connection.
    • The plea to grasp the auspicious and the best reflects a desire for purity and excellence in one's life.
    • Finally, invoking Indra signifies a call for strength and protection against imperfections and challenges.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a challenging situation, such as a job interview, one might recite this mantra to seek clarity and confidence.
    • During a significant life decision, individuals may reflect on the true path and invoke divine guidance to ensure they make the right choice.
    • In community gatherings, the use of the conch (pāñcajanyaṁ) can symbolize unity and the call for collective action towards a common goal.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking divine guidance and the pursuit of righteousness in everyday life.
    • It serves as a reminder to listen to the inner voice and strive for excellence while overcoming imperfections.
    • In a world filled with challenges, invoking the divine can provide strength and clarity, making this mantra relevant in various real-life scenarios.
