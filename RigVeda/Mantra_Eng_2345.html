पृक्षे ता विश्वा भुवना ववक्षिरे मित्राय वा सदम आ जीरददानवः | पृषदश्वासो अनवभ्रराधस ऋजिप्यासो न वयुनेषु धूर्षदः ||
pṛkṣe tā viśvā bhuvanā vavakṣire mitrāya vā sadam ā jīradānavaḥ | pṛṣadaśvāso anavabhrarādhasa ṛjipyāso na vayuneṣu dhūrṣadaḥ ||
  • pṛkṣe: This word can be interpreted as "to be aware" or "to perceive." It emphasizes the importance of awareness in our lives.
  • tā: This means "that" or "those," indicating a connection to the subject being discussed.
  • viśvā: Translated as "all" or "universal," it signifies inclusivity and the interconnectedness of all beings.
  • bhuvanā: This refers to "worlds" or "realms," suggesting the various dimensions of existence.
  • vavakṣire: This means "they have revealed" or "they have shown," indicating the act of sharing knowledge or wisdom.
  • mitrāya: Translated as "to the friend," it highlights the importance of friendship and companionship in our lives.
  • vā: This means "or," presenting an alternative or choice.
  • sadam: This can be interpreted as "the abode" or "the dwelling," referring to a place of comfort and safety.
  • ā: This is a preposition meaning "from" or "out of," indicating a source or origin.
  • jīradānavaḥ: This term can be understood as "those who give life" or "life-givers," emphasizing the nurturing aspect of existence.
  • pṛṣadaśvāso: This can be interpreted as "those who are swift" or "those who are agile," suggesting the importance of adaptability.
  • anavabhrarādhasa: This term refers to "without obstacles" or "unhindered," indicating a state of freedom and ease.
  • ṛjipyāso: This means "those who are righteous" or "those who follow the path of truth," emphasizing moral integrity.
  • na: This means "not," indicating negation or absence.
  • vayuneṣu: This can be interpreted as "in the forces" or "in the energies," referring to the various influences in life.
  • dhūrṣadaḥ: This term means "those who are strong" or "those who are powerful," highlighting the importance of strength in overcoming challenges.

In summary, this mantra emphasizes the significance of awareness, friendship, adaptability, and moral integrity in navigating the various realms of existence. It encourages individuals to be life-givers and to embrace a righteous path while remaining strong and unhindered by obstacles.


Conclusion: The mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of all beings and the importance of nurturing relationships and moral values in our lives. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in personal relationships, community building, and even in professional environments where collaboration and integrity are essential for success. By embodying the principles outlined in this mantra, individuals can contribute to a more harmonious and supportive world.
