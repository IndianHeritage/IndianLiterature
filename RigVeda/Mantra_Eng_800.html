स हि क्षपावां अग्नि रयीणां दाशद यो अस्मा अरं सूक्तैः ||
sa hi kṣapāvām̐ agnī rayīṇāṁ dāśad yo asmā araṁ sūktaiḥ ||
  • sa hi kṣapāvām̐: This phrase can be translated as "Indeed, he is the one who brings forth the dawn." Here, "sa" means "he," "hi" emphasizes certainty, and "kṣapāvām̐" refers to the dawn or the light that dispels darkness.
  • agnī: This word means "fire" or "the divine fire." In Vedic literature, Agni is often personified as a deity representing the sacred fire, which is central to rituals and symbolizes transformation and purification.
  • rayīṇāṁ: This term translates to "of wealth" or "of abundance." It signifies prosperity and the blessings that come from the divine.
  • dāśad: This word means "he who gives" or "the giver." It emphasizes the act of providing or bestowing something valuable.
  • yo asmā: This phrase translates to "who among us." It indicates a sense of inclusivity and collective experience.
  • araṁ: This term can mean "to lead" or "to guide." It suggests a direction or purpose in the context of the mantra.
  • sūktaiḥ: This word refers to "hymns" or "sacred verses." It highlights the importance of recitation and the power of words in invoking divine blessings.

  • Meaning and Interpretation: The mantra speaks to the divine qualities of Agni, the fire god, who brings forth light and prosperity. It emphasizes the importance of invoking divine energy through hymns and rituals to receive blessings and abundance in life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, this mantra can be applied during rituals or ceremonies where fire is present, such as weddings or housewarming ceremonies, symbolizing the purification and sanctification of the space.
    • In a metaphorical sense, it can be related to personal growth and transformation, where individuals seek to dispel the darkness of ignorance and invite the light of knowledge and prosperity into their lives.

  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call to recognize the divine forces that guide and provide for us. By acknowledging and invoking these energies through sacred hymns, individuals can cultivate a sense of abundance and clarity in their lives. It serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of all beings and the importance of seeking divine guidance in our pursuits.
