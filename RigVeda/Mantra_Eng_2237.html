मां नः स्तेनिभ्यो ये अभि द्रुहस पदे निरामिणो रिपवोऽन्नेषु जागृधुः |आ देवानाम् ओहते वि व्रयो हृदि बृहस्पते न परः साम्नो विदुः ||
mā naḥ stenebhyo ye abhi druhas pade nirāmiṇo ripavo 'nneṣu jāgṛdhuḥ | ā devānām ohate vi vrayo hṛdi bṛhaspate na paraḥ sāmno viduḥ ||
  • mā naḥ stenebhyo ye abhi druhas pade: This phrase translates to "May we not be harmed by those who wish to harm us." It emphasizes the importance of protection from malevolent forces. In real life, this can be related to the need for safety and security in our daily lives, whether it be physical safety or emotional well-being.
  • nirāmiṇo ripavo 'nneṣu jāgṛdhuḥ: This means "Let the enemies who are lurking in the shadows be vigilant." It suggests that we should be aware of potential threats and remain alert. For example, in a workplace, being aware of office politics and maintaining good relationships can help in avoiding conflicts.
  • ā devānām ohate vi vrayo: This translates to "The divine powers protect us." It signifies the belief in higher forces that guide and safeguard us. In real life, this can be seen in the way people seek blessings or guidance from spiritual leaders or through prayer during challenging times.
  • hṛdi bṛhaspate na paraḥ sāmno viduḥ: This means "In the heart of the wise, there is no greater knowledge." It highlights the value of wisdom and understanding. In practical terms, this can be applied in decision-making processes, where informed choices lead to better outcomes.

  • Significance: This mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking protection from negativity, being aware of potential threats, and valuing wisdom. It serves as a reminder to remain vigilant and to trust in the divine for guidance.
  • Real-life Application: The teachings of this mantra can be applied in various aspects of life, such as personal safety, workplace dynamics, and spiritual practices. By embodying the principles of awareness, protection, and wisdom, individuals can navigate challenges more effectively.
