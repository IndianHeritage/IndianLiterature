मन्त्र: चित्रं तद् वो मरुतो याम चेकिते पृष्ण्या यद् ऊधर अप्य आपयो दूहुः |यद् वा निदे नवमानस्य रुद्रियास त्रितं जराया जुरताम अदाभ्याः ||
citraṁ tad vo maruto yāma cekite pṛśnyā yad ūdhar apy āpayo duhuḥ | yad vā nide navamānasya rudriyās tritaṁ jarāya juratām adābhyāḥ ||
  • citraṁ tad vo maruto: This phrase can be translated as "O Maruts, that which is wonderful or extraordinary." The Maruts are a group of storm deities in Vedic literature, often associated with the forces of nature. In real life, this can be likened to the awe-inspiring power of natural phenomena, such as thunderstorms or hurricanes, which can be both beautiful and destructive.
  • yāma cekite: This means "let us go forth." It signifies a call to action or movement. In everyday life, this can represent the importance of taking initiative and moving forward in our endeavors, whether in personal goals or community efforts.
  • pṛśnyā yad ūdhar apy āpayo duhuḥ: This translates to "what is drawn forth from the depths." It suggests the idea of extracting something valuable from within, akin to how individuals often need to dig deep within themselves to find strength or creativity. For example, artists often draw inspiration from their personal experiences and emotions.
  • yad vā nide navamānasya: This can be interpreted as "what is new and fresh." It emphasizes the importance of innovation and new beginnings. In a practical sense, this can relate to the launch of new projects or ideas in business or technology, where fresh perspectives lead to growth and success.
  • rudriyās tritaṁ jarāya juratām adābhyāḥ: This phrase refers to "the strength of the Rudras, the fierce ones." The Rudras are often associated with transformation and change. In real life, this can be seen in the context of overcoming challenges and adversities, where individuals harness their inner strength to transform their circumstances.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates themes of wonder, action, depth, innovation, and strength. Its significance lies in the encouragement to embrace the extraordinary aspects of life, take initiative, and draw upon inner resources to navigate challenges. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire individuals to pursue their goals with vigor, seek new opportunities, and transform their lives through resilience and creativity. It serves as a reminder that within the depths of our experiences lies the potential for greatness and renewal.
