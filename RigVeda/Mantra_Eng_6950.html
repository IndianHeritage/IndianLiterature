मन्त्र: सुनीतो घा स मार्त्यो यं मरुतो यं आर्यमाः | मित्रः पान्त्य अद्रुहः ||
sunītho ghā sa martyo yam maruto yam aryamā | mitraḥ pānty adruhaḥ ||
  • sunītho: This word can be translated as "well-behaved" or "well-conducted." It emphasizes the importance of good conduct and moral integrity in one's actions.
  • ghā: This term means "to speak" or "to express." It highlights the significance of communication and the power of words in shaping relationships and society.
  • sa martyo: "sa" means "that," and "martyo" refers to "mortal" or "human." This phrase points to the human condition and the shared experiences of mortality.
  • yam maruto: "yam" translates to "whom," and "maruto" refers to the "winds" or "storm gods." This can symbolize the forces of nature and their impact on human life.
  • yam aryamā: "aryamā" is often associated with the concept of righteousness or moral order. This phrase suggests a connection to higher principles and the pursuit of justice.
  • mitraḥ: This word means "friend" or "ally." It signifies the importance of friendship, cooperation, and support among individuals and communities.
  • pānty: This term can be interpreted as "to guide" or "to lead." It emphasizes the role of guidance in navigating life's challenges.
  • adruhaḥ: This word means "without enmity" or "non-hostile." It underscores the value of peace, harmony, and the absence of conflict in relationships.

In summary, this mantra from the Rigveda encapsulates essential values such as good conduct, effective communication, the acknowledgment of human mortality, respect for natural forces, the pursuit of righteousness, the importance of friendship, guidance, and the promotion of peace. These principles are applicable in various real-life scenarios, such as:


  • In personal relationships, practicing good conduct and effective communication can lead to stronger bonds and mutual understanding.
  • In community settings, fostering friendships and cooperation can create a supportive environment that benefits everyone.
  • In professional life, guiding others with integrity and promoting a non-hostile atmosphere can enhance teamwork and productivity.
  • In environmental contexts, recognizing the power of nature and acting with respect towards it can lead to sustainable practices.

Overall, the significance of this mantra lies in its timeless wisdom, encouraging individuals to embody these values in their daily lives, thereby contributing to a more harmonious and just society.
