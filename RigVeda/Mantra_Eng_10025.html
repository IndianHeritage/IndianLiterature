साकंयुजा शकुनस्येव पक्षाः पश्वेव चित्रा यजुर आगमिष्टम् |अग्निर इव देवयोः दीदिवांसः परिज्मानेव यजथः पुरुत्रा ||
sākaṁyujā śakunasyeva pakṣā paśveva citrā yajur ā gamiṣṭam | agnir iva devayor dīdivāṁsā parijmāneva yajathaḥ purutrā ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sākaṁyujā: united with the purpose
    • śakunasyeva: like the birds
    • pakṣā: wings
    • paśveva: like the animals
    • citrā: colorful or diverse
    • yajur: the act of worship or sacrifice
    • ā gamiṣṭam: to attain or reach
    • agnir: fire
    • iva: like
    • devayor: of the gods
    • dīdivāṁsā: shining or radiant
    • parijmāneva: like the encompassing
    • yajathaḥ: you who perform the sacrifice
    • purutrā: in many places
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of unity and collaboration, akin to how birds use their wings to soar together.
    • It draws a parallel between the diversity of life (like colorful animals) and the collective effort in worship or sacrifice.
    • The reference to fire symbolizes transformation and purity, suggesting that the divine presence is radiant and all-encompassing.
    • It encourages individuals to come together in various places to perform acts of devotion and sacrifice, highlighting the significance of community in spiritual practices.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace, teamwork can be seen as a manifestation of this mantra, where diverse skills and backgrounds come together to achieve a common goal.
    • In community service, individuals from different walks of life unite to help those in need, reflecting the essence of collective worship and sacrifice.
    • During festivals, people gather to celebrate, showcasing the colorful diversity of cultures while performing rituals that strengthen community bonds.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the power of unity in diversity, encouraging individuals to collaborate for a greater purpose.
    • Its application in real life can be seen in various communal activities, teamwork, and collective spiritual practices.
    • By embracing the essence of this mantra, individuals can foster harmony and achieve transformative outcomes in their personal and communal lives.
