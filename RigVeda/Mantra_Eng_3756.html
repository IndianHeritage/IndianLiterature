त्वं अग्नि सप्रथā असि जुष्टो होतā वरेण्यः | त्वया यज्ञं वि तन्वते ||
tvam agne saprathā asi juṣṭo hotā vareṇyaḥ | tvayā yajñaṁ vi tanvate ||
  • tvam agne: This phrase translates to "You, O Agni." Agni is the Vedic fire god, symbolizing energy, transformation, and the divine messenger between humans and the gods. In real life, fire represents not only physical fire but also the transformative power of energy in various forms, such as passion and creativity.
  • saprathā asi: This means "You are the one who is invoked." It emphasizes the importance of Agni in rituals and prayers. In everyday life, this can be likened to the way we invoke inspiration or motivation in our endeavors, whether in art, work, or personal growth.
  • juṣṭo hotā: This translates to "the one who is pleased and is the priest." Agni is seen as a facilitator of offerings and prayers. In a modern context, this can be related to the role of leaders or mentors who guide and support us in our journeys, ensuring that our efforts are recognized and appreciated.
  • vareṇyaḥ: This means "the most excellent" or "worthy of worship." It signifies the supreme nature of Agni. In real life, this can be associated with the pursuit of excellence in our actions and the respect we give to those who embody such qualities, like role models or leaders in various fields.
  • tvayā yajñaṁ vi tanvate: This translates to "by you, the sacrifice is performed." It highlights the idea that all actions, especially those aimed at the greater good, require effort and dedication. In contemporary life, this can be seen in community service, environmental efforts, or any initiative that requires collective sacrifice for a common goal.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of Agni as a divine force that transforms and connects us to higher ideals. It teaches us the importance of invoking inspiration, recognizing excellence, and dedicating ourselves to meaningful actions. In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as in personal development, community engagement, and leadership, reminding us to strive for excellence and to honor the transformative power of our efforts.
