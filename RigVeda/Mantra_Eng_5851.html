रायेनु यं जजñतू रोडसीमे राय देवī धिषणा धाति देवम् |अध वायुं नियुतः सश्चत स्वा उता श्वेतं वसुधितिं निरेक ||
rāye nu yaṁ jajñatū rodasīme rāye devī dhiṣaṇā dhāti devam | adha vāyuṁ niyutaḥ saścata svā uta śvetaṁ vasudhitiṁ nireke ||
  • Translation: The mantra can be translated as: "May the divine power that is born in the heavens grant us prosperity. May the goddess of fortune bestow her blessings upon us. May we be united with the vital energy and may we attain the pure wealth of the earth."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • rāye: Refers to prosperity or wealth.
    • jajñatū: Means "that which is born" or "that which is generated."
    • rodasīme: Refers to the heavens or the sky.
    • devī: Denotes the goddess, often associated with divine feminine energy.
    • dhiṣaṇā: Means "blessings" or "grace."
    • adhā: Means "below" or "under."
    • vāyuṁ: Refers to the vital energy or life force.
    • niyutaḥ: Means "united" or "connected."
    • svā: Refers to "wealth" or "prosperity."
    • uta: Means "and" or "also."
    • śvetaṁ: Refers to "pure" or "white," symbolizing purity.
    • vasudhitiṁ: Refers to the earth or the land.
    • nireke: Means "apart from" or "besides."
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be invoked during the opening of a new venture, seeking divine blessings for prosperity and success.
    • During festivals or rituals, individuals may recite this mantra to attract wealth and abundance into their lives, emphasizing the importance of divine grace in achieving material success.
    • In personal development, one might meditate on this mantra to align themselves with positive energies and attract opportunities for growth and prosperity.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its invocation of divine blessings for prosperity and well-being. It emphasizes the interconnectedness of spiritual and material wealth, suggesting that true prosperity comes from a harmonious relationship with the divine. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as business ventures, personal growth, and during rituals, serving as a reminder to seek divine guidance and blessings in all endeavors. By reciting this mantra, individuals can cultivate a mindset of abundance and gratitude, attracting positive energies into their lives.
