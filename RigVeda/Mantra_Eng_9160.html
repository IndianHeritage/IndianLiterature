आ नो बर्हिः सदहमादे बृहद् दिवि देवाम् ईळे सादयाऽ सप्त होत्रन् | इन्द्रं मित्रं वरुणं सातये भगं स्वस्त्य अग्निं समिधानम् ईमहे ||
ā no barhiḥ sadhamāde bṛhad divi devām̐ īḻe sādayā sapta hotṝn | indram mitraṁ varuṇaṁ sātaye bhagaṁ svasty a1gniṁ samidhānam īmahe ||
  • Mantra: "ā no barhiḥ sadhamāde bṛhad divi devām̐ īḻe sādayā sapta hotṝn | indram mitraṁ varuṇaṁ sātaye bhagaṁ svasty a1gniṁ samidhānam īmahe ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā no: May we be blessed
    • barhiḥ: with the sacred grass (barhis)
    • sadhamāde: in harmony
    • bṛhad: great
    • divi: in the heavens
    • devām̐: the divine beings
    • īḻe: I invoke
    • sādayā: with the help of
    • sapta: the seven
    • hotṝn: priests or seers
    • indram: Indra (the king of gods)
    • mitraṁ: Mitra (the god of friendship)
    • varuṇaṁ: Varuna (the god of cosmic order)
    • sātaye: for prosperity
    • bhagaṁ: wealth or fortune
    • svasty: auspiciousness
    • a1gniṁ: Agni (the fire god)
    • samidhānam: the one who is kindled
    • īmahe: we meditate or invoke
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra is a prayer for blessings and harmony. It invokes the divine beings in the heavens, asking for their guidance and support.
    • The mention of "barhiḥ" refers to the sacred grass used in rituals, symbolizing purity and sanctity.
    • The "seven priests" represent the various aspects of spiritual guidance and wisdom that one can seek in life.
    • Indra, Mitra, and Varuna are significant deities representing strength, friendship, and cosmic order, respectively. Their invocation signifies a holistic approach to life, encompassing strength, relationships, and adherence to universal laws.
    • The mantra emphasizes the importance of prosperity, wealth, and auspiciousness, which are essential for a fulfilling life.
    • Agni, the fire god, symbolizes transformation and the energy needed to achieve one's goals.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, this mantra can be applied when forming a new team. Invoking harmony and cooperation among team members can lead to better productivity and success.
    • During personal challenges, one might meditate on this mantra to seek guidance and support from friends and family, fostering stronger relationships.
    • In community gatherings or rituals, this mantra can be recited to invoke blessings for collective prosperity and well-being.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of strength, relationships, and cosmic order in achieving a prosperous life.
    • Its significance lies in its ability to inspire individuals to seek harmony and divine guidance in their personal and communal endeavors.
    • By applying the principles of this mantra in daily life, one can cultivate a sense of purpose, unity, and fulfillment.
