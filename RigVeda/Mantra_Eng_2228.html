मन्त्र: उता वा यो नो मर्चयाद अनागसः 'रातिवा मर्तः सानुको वृकः | बृहस्पते अप तं वर्तयापथः सुगं नो अस्यै देववीतये कृधि ||
uta vā yo no marcayād anāgaso 'rātīvā martaḥ sānuko vṛkaḥ | bṛhaspate apa taṁ vartayā pathaḥ sugaṁ no asyai devavītaye kṛdhi ||
  • Mantra: "uta vā yo no marcayād anāgaso 'rātīvā martaḥ sānuko vṛkaḥ | bṛhaspate apa taṁ vartayā pathaḥ sugaṁ no asyai devavītaye kṛdhi ||"
  • Translation: This mantra can be translated as a plea for guidance and protection from the divine, invoking the blessings of Bṛhaspati, the deity of wisdom and eloquence.
  • Word-by-word Meaning:
    • uta vā: or indeed
    • yo: who
    • no: us
    • marcayād: may not harm
    • anāgaso: without sin
    • 'rātīvā: in the night
    • martaḥ: mortal
    • sānuko: with a small amount
    • vṛkaḥ: wolf
    • bṛhaspate: O Bṛhaspati
    • apa: away
    • taṁ: that
    • vartayā: may lead
    • pathaḥ: on the path
    • sugaṁ: good
    • no: for us
    • asyai: this
    • devavītaye: for the sake of the divine
    • kṛdhi: do
  • Context and Significance:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking divine guidance and protection in our lives.
    • It reflects the human condition of vulnerability, where we often face challenges and threats, symbolized by the "wolf" (vṛkaḥ) that represents danger.
    • The invocation of Bṛhaspati signifies the need for wisdom and clarity in navigating life's complexities.
  • Real-Life Application:
    • In times of uncertainty, such as during a job search or a personal crisis, individuals can recite this mantra to seek clarity and guidance.
    • It can be used in rituals or meditative practices to foster a sense of protection and to invoke wisdom in decision-making.
    • For example, a student preparing for exams may chant this mantra to seek focus and the right path to success.
  • Conclusion:
    • The mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of divine support in our endeavors.
    • It encourages individuals to seek wisdom and protection, reinforcing the belief that we are not alone in our struggles.
    • Ultimately, it highlights the interconnectedness of human experience and the divine, urging us to remain open to guidance in our journey through life.
