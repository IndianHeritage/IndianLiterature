वृषणस ते अभिषवो वृषा कशा हिरण्ययी |वृषा रथो मघवन वृषणाऽ हरि वृषा त्वं शतक्रतो ||
vṛṣaṇas te abhīśavo vṛṣā kaśā hiraṇyayī | vṛṣā ratho maghavan vṛṣaṇā harī vṛṣā tvaṁ śatakrato ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • vṛṣaṇas: The strong or powerful ones.
    • te: Your.
    • abhīśavo: Those who are worthy of worship or reverence.
    • vṛṣā: The one who is strong or powerful.
    • kaśā: The one who is like a horse or swift.
    • hiraṇyayī: Golden or precious.
    • ratho: Chariot.
    • maghavan: The one who is generous or bountiful.
    • harī: The one who is swift or quick.
    • tvaṁ: You.
    • śatakrato: The one who has a hundred powers or capabilities.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the powerful and revered forces of nature and divinity.
    • The mention of "vṛṣaṇas" and "abhīśavo" suggests a call to the strong and worthy, emphasizing the importance of strength and reverence in our lives.
    • The imagery of a chariot ("ratho") signifies movement, progress, and the journey of life, where one must harness their inner strength and capabilities.
    • The term "maghavan" highlights the importance of generosity and abundance, reminding us to be bountiful in our actions and thoughts.
    • The phrase "śatakrato" suggests that we all possess multiple capabilities and strengths, encouraging us to recognize and utilize them.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, this mantra can inspire individuals to harness their strengths and work collaboratively, much like a chariot that moves forward with the combined effort of its horses.
    • In personal development, recognizing one's "hundred powers" can motivate individuals to pursue their goals with confidence and determination.
    • In community service, embodying the qualities of "maghavan" can lead to acts of kindness and generosity, fostering a sense of unity and support among individuals.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the strength, reverence, and capabilities that reside within each individual.
    • It encourages us to recognize our potential and to act with generosity and purpose in our lives.
    • By embodying the qualities of strength and reverence, we can navigate the journey of life with confidence and contribute positively to our communities.
