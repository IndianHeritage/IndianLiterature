अपाय्य अस्यांधसो मदाय मनिषिणः सुवानस्य प्रयसः |यस्मिन्न इन्द्रः प्रदिवि वावृद्धान ओको दधे ब्रह्मण्यन्तश्च नरः ||
apāyy asyāndhaso madāya manīṣiṇaḥ suvānasya prayasaḥ | yasminn indraḥ pradivi vāvṛdhāna oko dadhe brahmaṇyantaś ca naraḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • apāyy: to be free from, to be liberated
    • asyāndhaso: of this darkness or ignorance
    • madāya: for the sake of intoxication or bliss
    • manīṣiṇaḥ: of the wise or the enlightened
    • suvānasya: of the good or auspicious
    • prayasaḥ: of efforts or endeavors
    • yasminn: in which
    • indraḥ: Indra, the king of gods
    • pradivi: in the sky or heaven
    • vāvṛdhāna: expanding or increasing
    • oko: the abode or dwelling
    • dadhe: establishes or creates
    • brahmaṇyantaś: in the realm of the ultimate reality or Brahman
    • ca: and
    • naraḥ: the human beings or men
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about liberation from ignorance and the pursuit of bliss through wisdom.
    • It emphasizes the importance of good efforts and auspicious actions in achieving a higher state of existence.
    • Indra, as a symbol of strength and power, represents the divine force that aids in the expansion of consciousness.
    • The mantra suggests that through wise actions and efforts, humans can create a dwelling in the realm of ultimate reality.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a personal context, one might seek to overcome ignorance by pursuing education and self-awareness, leading to a more fulfilling life.
    • In a community setting, collective efforts towards social justice and environmental sustainability can be seen as striving for the good and auspicious.
    • Professionally, individuals can apply the principles of this mantra by engaging in ethical practices and striving for excellence in their work, thereby contributing to a greater purpose.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for enlightenment and the pursuit of a higher state of being through wise actions.
    • It is applicable in various aspects of life, including personal growth, community development, and professional integrity.
    • By embodying the principles of this mantra, individuals can contribute to a more harmonious and enlightened society.
