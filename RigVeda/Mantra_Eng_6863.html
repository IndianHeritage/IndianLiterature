तं īḻiṣ्व य आहुतो 'ग्निर विभ्राजते घृतैः |इमं नः श्रिणवद धवम् ||
tam īḻiṣva ya āhuto 'gnir vibhrājate ghṛtaiḥ | imaṁ naḥ śṛṇavad dhavam ||
  • tam īḻiṣva: This phrase can be interpreted as "that which is to be invoked" or "that which is desired." It signifies the importance of intention and focus in our prayers and actions.
  • ya āhuto: This means "that which is invoked" or "that which is called upon." It emphasizes the act of calling upon divine forces or energies for assistance and guidance.
  • 'gnir: This word translates to "fire," which in many cultures symbolizes purification, transformation, and energy. In the context of the mantra, it represents the divine energy that is being invoked.
  • vibhrājate: This means "shines" or "radiates." It indicates the presence of divine light and energy that is brought forth through the invocation.
  • ghṛtaiḥ: This word refers to "ghee" or "clarified butter," which is often used in rituals and offerings. It symbolizes nourishment, purity, and the essence of life.
  • imaṁ naḥ: This translates to "this for us," indicating a personal connection and the desire for the invoked energy to be beneficial to the individual or community.
  • śṛṇavad: This means "listen" or "hear." It signifies the importance of being receptive to divine guidance and wisdom.
  • dhavam: This word can be interpreted as "to the one who is swift" or "to the one who moves quickly." It suggests the dynamic nature of divine energy and its ability to respond to our calls.

In conclusion, this mantra encapsulates the essence of invoking divine energy for personal and communal benefit. It highlights the significance of intention, the act of calling upon higher powers, and the importance of receptivity to divine guidance. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as during rituals, meditation, or moments of seeking clarity and support in challenging situations. By focusing our intentions and invoking divine energies, we can create a transformative experience that nourishes our spirit and guides us on our path.
