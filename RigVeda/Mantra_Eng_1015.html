प्रत्यर्ची रुशद अस्य अदर्शि विथिष्ठते बाधते कृष्णम् अभ्वम् |स्वरुं न पेयो विदथेष्व अञ्जन चित्रम दिवो दुहिताऽभानुम अश्रेत ||
praty arcī ruśad asyā adarśi vi tiṣṭhate bādhate kṛṣṇam abhvam | svaruṁ na peśo vidatheṣv añjañ citraṁ divo duhitā bhānum aśret ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • praty: towards
    • arcī: light or radiance
    • ruśad: shining or bright
    • asyā: of this
    • adarśi: does not see
    • vi: apart or separate
    • tiṣṭhate: stands or exists
    • bādhate: obstructs or hinders
    • kṛṣṇam: the dark or black
    • abhvam: non-existent or absent
    • svaruṁ: essence or nature
    • na: not
    • peśo: of the body or form
    • vidatheṣv: in the assemblies or gatherings
    • añjañ: quickly or swiftly
    • citraṁ: diverse or colorful
    • divo: of the sky or heaven
    • duhitā: daughter
    • bhānum: of the sun
    • aśret: has taken or accepted
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the radiant light that stands apart from darkness and does not see the obstacles that hinder it.
    • The mention of "kṛṣṇam abhvam" suggests the absence of darkness, symbolizing the triumph of light over ignorance and negativity.
    • The "duhitā bhānum" refers to the daughter of the sun, which can be interpreted as the nurturing and illuminating aspects of knowledge and wisdom.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a personal context, this mantra can be applied to the journey of self-discovery and enlightenment. Just as light dispels darkness, knowledge and awareness can help individuals overcome challenges and obstacles in their lives.
    • In a societal context, the mantra can be seen as a call to action for communities to embrace diversity ("citraṁ") and work together towards a common goal, illuminating the path for future generations.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its emphasis on the power of light (knowledge) to overcome darkness (ignorance).
    • It serves as a reminder that despite the obstacles we may face, the essence of truth and wisdom will always prevail.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra encourages individuals and communities to seek knowledge, embrace diversity, and work towards enlightenment, fostering a brighter future for all.
