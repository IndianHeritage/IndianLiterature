आ यद इन्द्रश्च दद्वहे सहस्रं वसुरोचिषः |ओजिष्टम अश्व्यम पशुम ||
ā yad indraś ca dadvahe sahasraṁ vasurociṣaḥ | ojiṣṭham aśvyam paśum ||
  • Mantra: "ā yad indraś ca dadvahe sahasraṁ vasurociṣaḥ | ojiṣṭham aśvyam paśum ||"
  • Translation: "We invoke Indra, the mighty one, who grants us a thousand riches; the most powerful, the best of steeds, and the best of cattle."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • ā yad: "As we invoke" - This phrase indicates the act of calling upon a deity or power.
    • indraś: "Indra" - The king of gods in Vedic tradition, symbolizing strength, power, and leadership.
    • ca: "and" - A conjunction that connects thoughts or entities.
    • dadvahe: "we offer" - This signifies the act of giving or presenting something valuable.
    • sahasraṁ: "thousand" - Represents abundance and prosperity.
    • vasurociṣaḥ: "riches" - Refers to wealth, resources, and blessings.
    • ojiṣṭham: "the most powerful" - Indicates superiority and strength.
    • aśvyam: "of steeds" - Refers to horses, which symbolize speed, power, and nobility.
    • paśum: "cattle" - Represents livestock, a symbol of wealth and sustenance in agrarian societies.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern agriculture, invoking the blessings of prosperity can be likened to farmers praying for a bountiful harvest, ensuring their livestock is healthy and their crops are abundant.
    • In business, leaders often seek inspiration and strength, akin to invoking Indra, to guide their decisions and lead their teams towards success and wealth.
    • In personal life, individuals may seek strength and support during challenging times, similar to calling upon divine powers for guidance and abundance.
  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of invoking divine strength and abundance in various aspects of life. It signifies the aspiration for prosperity, health, and power, which are essential for personal and communal growth. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in contexts such as agriculture, business, and personal development, reminding individuals to seek strength and blessings in their endeavors. By recognizing the significance of abundance and power, one can cultivate a mindset that attracts success and well-being.
