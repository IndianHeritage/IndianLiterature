यथा पूर्वेभ्यः शतसां अमृतध्रः सहस्रसां पर्यया वाजं इन्दो |एवा पवस्व सुविताय नव्यसे तव व्रतम अन्व आपः साचन्ते ||
yathā pūrvebhyaḥ śatasā amṛdhraḥ sahasrasāḥ paryayā vājam indo | evā pavasva suvitāya navyase tava vratam anv āpaḥ sacante ||
  • yathā pūrvebhyaḥ - "As it was for the ancients" - This phrase refers to the wisdom and practices of the ancestors, emphasizing the importance of tradition and the knowledge passed down through generations.
  • śatasā amṛdhraḥ - "With a hundredfold abundance" - This signifies the idea of prosperity and the blessings that come from following the right path, suggesting that abundance is a result of righteous living.
  • sahasrasāḥ - "With thousands" - This indicates a vastness or multitude, reinforcing the concept of collective strength and the power of community in achieving goals.
  • paryayā vājam indo - "By the means of the divine" - This phrase highlights the role of divine intervention or support in human endeavors, suggesting that success is often aided by higher powers.
  • evā pavasva - "Thus, may you purify" - This is a call for purification, both of the mind and spirit, indicating the need for clarity and focus in one's intentions and actions.
  • suavitāya - "For the well-being" - This emphasizes the importance of actions that lead to the welfare of oneself and others, promoting a sense of responsibility towards the community.
  • navyase - "In the new" - This suggests embracing new beginnings and innovations while respecting the past, indicating a balance between tradition and modernity.
  • tava vratam - "Your vow" - This refers to the commitments and promises made, highlighting the significance of dedication and integrity in one's pursuits.
  • anv āpaḥ sacante - "May the waters flow" - This symbolizes the flow of life and abundance, suggesting that when one follows the right path, blessings will naturally come.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of living a life rooted in tradition while embracing new opportunities. It emphasizes the importance of community, divine support, and personal integrity in achieving prosperity and well-being. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various contexts such as community service, where individuals come together to support one another, or in personal endeavors where one seeks to balance respect for heritage with innovation. The mantra serves as a reminder that success is not solely an individual pursuit but a collective journey enriched by shared values and divine guidance.
