देवं-देवं वो 'वसे देवं-देवम् अभिष्टये |देवं-देवं हुवेम वाजसातये गृणन्तो देव्या धियाः ||
devaṁ-devaṁ vo 'vase devaṁ-devam abhiṣṭaye | devaṁ-devaṁ huvema vājasātaye gṛṇanto devyā dhiyā ||
  • devaṁ-devaṁ: This phrase translates to "the divine" or "the god of gods." It emphasizes the supreme nature of divinity, suggesting that there is a higher power that encompasses all other deities.
  • vo 'vase: This means "we invoke you" or "we call upon you." It indicates a plea or a request for the divine presence to be with us.
  • devaṁ-devam abhiṣṭaye: This can be interpreted as "for the purpose of worshipping the divine." It highlights the intention behind the invocation, which is to seek blessings and guidance from the divine.
  • devaṁ-devaṁ huvema: This translates to "we chant to the divine." It signifies the act of prayer or chanting as a means to connect with the divine.
  • vājasātaye: This means "for the purpose of strength or power." It suggests that the invocation is aimed at gaining strength, whether it be physical, mental, or spiritual.
  • gṛṇanto devyā dhiyā: This translates to "with the wisdom of the divine." It implies that the wisdom and guidance of the divine are sought in our endeavors.

In essence, this mantra is a powerful invocation that seeks the presence and blessings of the divine. It emphasizes the importance of connecting with a higher power for strength, wisdom, and guidance in our lives.


Real-Life Application:

  • In daily life, individuals often face challenges that require strength and wisdom. This mantra can be recited during times of difficulty, such as preparing for an important exam or facing a personal crisis, to invoke divine support.
  • In communal settings, such as during festivals or rituals, this mantra can be chanted to foster a sense of unity and collective strength among participants, reminding them of the divine presence that guides them.
  • Professionals may use this mantra as a form of meditation before important meetings or decisions, seeking clarity and divine guidance in their actions.

Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its ability to connect individuals with the divine, providing them with strength, wisdom, and guidance. It serves as a reminder of the importance of seeking higher support in our endeavors, whether personal or communal. By invoking the divine, we align ourselves with a greater purpose and find the courage to navigate life's challenges.
