अस्येदु प्र ब्रूहि पूर्व्याणि तुरस्य कर्माणि नव्य उक्थैः |युधे यद इष्णान आयुधान्य ऋघायमानो निरिणाति शत्रून् ||
asyed u pra brūhi pūrvyāṇi turasya karmāṇi navya ukthaiḥ | yudhe yad iṣṇāna āyudhāny ṛghāyamāṇo niriṇāti śatrūn ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • asyed: this (referring to the speaker or the context)
    • u: indeed, or certainly
    • pra: forth, or forward
    • brūhi: speak, or declare
    • pūrvyāṇi: the ancient, or previous
    • turasya: of the swift, or the powerful
    • karmāṇi: deeds, or actions
    • navya: new, or fresh
    • ukthaiḥ: with hymns, or praises
    • yudhe: in battle, or in conflict
    • yad: that which
    • iṣṇāna: striving, or effort
    • āyudhāny: weapons, or tools
    • ṛghāyamāṇo: being invoked, or called upon
    • niriṇāti: overcomes, or defeats
    • śatrūn: enemies, or adversaries
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of declaring and invoking the deeds of the past while also embracing new actions and praises.
    • It suggests that in times of conflict or battle, one should strive with determination and utilize their tools or weapons effectively.
    • The invocation of strength and the remembrance of ancient deeds can empower individuals to overcome their adversaries.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a competitive work environment, professionals often reflect on past successes (pūrvyāṇi) to inspire new strategies (navya) that can lead to success in current projects.
    • Sports teams often invoke the spirit of past victories while preparing for new matches, using motivational speeches (ukthaiḥ) to boost morale and performance.
    • In personal challenges, individuals may draw upon their past experiences and lessons learned to tackle new obstacles, much like preparing for a battle (yudhe).
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the power of reflection and invocation in overcoming challenges.
    • It teaches that by acknowledging past achievements and embracing new efforts, one can effectively confront and defeat adversaries in various aspects of life.
    • Its application is relevant in personal growth, professional development, and competitive scenarios, reminding us that our history can guide our future actions.
