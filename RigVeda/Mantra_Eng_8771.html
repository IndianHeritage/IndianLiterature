तवे सोम प्रथमा वृक्तबरहिषो महे वाजाय श्रवसे धियं दधुः |स तवं नो वीर वीर्याय चोदय ||
tve soma prathamā vṛktabarhiṣo mahe vājāya śravase dhiyaṁ dadhuḥ | sa tvaṁ no vīra vīryāya codaya ||
  • tve soma prathamā: This phrase translates to "You, Soma, are the first." Soma is often considered a divine drink or a deity associated with inspiration and vitality. In real life, this can be likened to the importance of foundational elements in our lives, such as education or health, which set the stage for future success.
  • vṛktabarhiṣo: This term refers to "the one who is praised or celebrated." It emphasizes the importance of recognition and appreciation in our lives. For example, in a workplace, acknowledging the efforts of team members can boost morale and productivity.
  • mahe vājāya: This means "for great strength or power." It signifies the pursuit of strength, whether physical, mental, or emotional. In real life, this can be seen in athletes training for competitions or individuals overcoming personal challenges to achieve their goals.
  • śravase dhiyaṁ dadhuḥ: This translates to "they have given us wisdom and intelligence." It highlights the value of knowledge and insight. In everyday life, this can be related to the importance of education and learning from experiences to make informed decisions.
  • sa tvaṁ no vīra vīryāya codaya: This phrase means "O hero, inspire us towards strength." It is a call for motivation and encouragement. In real life, this can be seen in leaders who inspire their teams to strive for excellence or in mentors who guide their mentees to reach their full potential.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of foundational elements such as inspiration, recognition, strength, wisdom, and motivation in our lives. It serves as a reminder that these qualities are essential for personal and collective growth. In various real-life scenarios, whether in education, the workplace, or personal development, the principles outlined in this mantra can guide individuals and communities towards achieving their goals and fostering a supportive environment for success.
