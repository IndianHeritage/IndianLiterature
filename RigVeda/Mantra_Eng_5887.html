ताविद दु:शंसं मर्त्यं दुर्विद्वांसं रक्षस्विनम् |आभोगं हन्मना हतमुदधिं हन्मना हतम् ||
tāv id duḥśaṁsam martyaṁ durvidvāṁsaṁ rakṣasvinam | ābhogaṁ hanmanā hatam udadhiṁ hanmanā hatam ||
  • tāv id duḥśaṁsam martyaṁ durvidvāṁsaṁ rakṣasvinam
    • tāv id: This phrase can be interpreted as "these" or "those," indicating a focus on specific entities or situations.
    • duḥśaṁsam: This term refers to "difficult" or "troublesome," often associated with challenges or adversities in life.
    • martyaṁ: This word means "mortal" or "human," emphasizing the human condition and its inherent struggles.
    • durvidvāṁsaṁ: This translates to "difficult to understand" or "hard to comprehend," suggesting complexities in life or situations that are not easily navigable.
    • rakṣasvinam: This term refers to "those who are powerful" or "those who protect," often associated with forces that can either be benevolent or malevolent.
  • ābhogaṁ hanmanā hatam
    • ābhogaṁ: This word means "enjoyment" or "pleasure," indicating the pursuit of happiness or fulfillment.
    • hanmanā: This term can be interpreted as "with the mind" or "through thought," suggesting that our mental state plays a crucial role in our experiences.
    • hatam: This means "destroyed" or "overcome," indicating the idea of overcoming obstacles or challenges.
  • udadhiṁ hanmanā hatam
    • udadhiṁ: This word translates to "ocean" or "vastness," symbolizing the depth and breadth of experiences in life.
    • hanmanā: Again, this emphasizes the role of the mind in navigating through life's challenges.
    • hatam: Reiterating the concept of overcoming or conquering difficulties.

Conclusion:

The mantra encapsulates the essence of human struggles and the complexities of life. It acknowledges the presence of challenges that are difficult to understand and navigate, particularly in the context of human existence. The invocation of powerful forces suggests that there are both protective and adversarial elements in our lives. The emphasis on the mind indicates that our thoughts and mental states are crucial in overcoming these challenges.


In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied to various situations such as:

  • Facing personal challenges, such as health issues or emotional struggles, where understanding and mental resilience are key to overcoming difficulties.
  • Dealing with complex relationships, where communication and mental clarity can help navigate misunderstandings.
  • Confronting societal issues, where collective understanding and mental fortitude can lead to positive change.

Ultimately, the significance of this mantra lies in its reminder that while life presents us with difficulties, it is our mindset and approach that determine our ability to overcome them and find joy amidst adversity.
