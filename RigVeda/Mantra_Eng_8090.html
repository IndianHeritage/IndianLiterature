मन्त्र: पुरः सद्य इत्ताधिये दिवोदासाय शम्बरम् |अध त्यम् तुर्वशं यदुम् ||
puraḥ sadya itthādhiye divodāsāya śambaram | adha tyaṁ turvaśaṁ yadum ||
  • puraḥ sadya itthādhiye: This phrase can be translated as "forward, immediately, let it be established." The word "puraḥ" means "in front" or "forward," while "sadya" means "immediately" or "right now." "Itthādhiye" suggests the idea of establishing or manifesting something.
  • divodāsāya: This term refers to "Divodasa," a name that can be associated with a divine or celestial being. In the context of the mantra, it signifies a connection to the divine or higher powers that govern the universe.
  • śambaram: This word can be interpreted as "auspiciousness" or "blessing." It denotes a state of well-being and prosperity that one seeks to achieve through divine intervention.
  • adha tyaṁ: This phrase translates to "below that" or "underneath that." It indicates a grounding or foundational aspect, suggesting that the blessings or auspiciousness sought should be rooted in something substantial.
  • turvaśaṁ: This term refers to "Turvaśa," which can be understood as a reference to a powerful or noble being. It implies a connection to strength, valor, and nobility.
  • yadum: This word can be interpreted as "that which is." It signifies the essence or the reality of the situation being addressed in the mantra.

  • Significance: The mantra emphasizes the importance of immediate action and the establishment of auspiciousness through divine connection. It calls for grounding one's aspirations in strength and nobility, suggesting that true blessings come from a solid foundation.
  • Real-life Application: In practical terms, this mantra can be applied in various aspects of life, such as:
    • Personal Goals: When pursuing personal or professional goals, one can invoke this mantra to remind themselves to take immediate action and establish a strong foundation for their aspirations.
    • Spiritual Practices: In spiritual practices, reciting this mantra can help individuals connect with divine energies, seeking blessings and auspiciousness in their lives.
    • Community Initiatives: When starting community projects, this mantra can serve as a reminder to act swiftly and ensure that the initiatives are rooted in noble intentions and collective strength.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of taking immediate action towards establishing auspiciousness while being grounded in strength and nobility. Its significance lies in its ability to inspire individuals to pursue their goals with determination and a sense of divine connection, making it applicable in various real-life scenarios.
