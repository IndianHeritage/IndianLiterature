स्वस्ति न इन्द्रो वृद्धश्रवाः स्वस्ति नः पूषा विश्ववेदाः |स्वस्ति नस् तार्क्ष्यो अरिष्टनेमिः स्वस्ति नो बृहस्पतिर दधातु ||
svasti na indro vṛddhaśravāḥ svasti naḥ pūṣā viśvavedāḥ | svasti nas tārkṣyo ariṣṭanemiḥ svasti no bṛhaspatir dadhātu ||
  • svasti na indro vṛddhaśravāḥ: This phrase translates to "May there be well-being from Indra, the one of great fame." Indra is a prominent deity in the Rigveda, often associated with rain, thunder, and strength. In real life, this can be interpreted as a prayer for prosperity and protection from challenges, much like how people seek blessings for success in their endeavors.
  • svasti naḥ pūṣā viśvavedāḥ: This means "May there be well-being from Pūṣa, the all-knowing." Pūṣa is a deity associated with nourishment and prosperity. In practical terms, this can be seen as a call for abundance and wisdom in decision-making, akin to how individuals seek guidance in their personal and professional lives to ensure growth and sustenance.
  • svasti nas tārkṣyo ariṣṭanemiḥ: This translates to "May there be well-being from Tārkṣya, the one who removes obstacles." Tārkṣya is often identified with the eagle, symbolizing strength and the ability to overcome challenges. In everyday life, this can be related to the pursuit of overcoming difficulties, such as striving for success in a career or personal goals, where one seeks the strength to navigate through adversities.
  • svasti no bṛhaspatir dadhātu: This means "May the great teacher, Bṛhaspati, grant us well-being." Bṛhaspati is the deity of wisdom and knowledge. This can be applied in real life as a reminder of the importance of education and learning, where individuals seek knowledge to make informed decisions and lead fulfilling lives.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a universal prayer for well-being, prosperity, wisdom, and the removal of obstacles. It signifies the importance of divine blessings in achieving success and harmony in life. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be invoked during significant events such as weddings, new ventures, or any situation where individuals seek guidance and support. It serves as a reminder that while we strive for our goals, seeking blessings from higher powers can provide the strength and wisdom needed to navigate life's challenges.
