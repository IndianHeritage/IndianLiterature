क उ श्रवत् कतमो यज्ञियानां वन्दारु देवः कतमो जुषाते |कस्येमां देवीं अमृतेषु प्रेष्ठां हृदि श्रेषाम सुष्टुतिं सुहव्यां ||
ka u śravat katamo yajñiyānāṁ vandāru devaḥ katamo juṣāte | kasyemāṁ devīm amṛteṣu preṣṭhāṁ hṛdi śreṣāma suṣṭutiṁ suhavyām ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ka u: Who indeed
    • śravat: is to be praised
    • katamo: which one
    • yajñiyānāṁ: of those who perform sacrifices
    • vandāru: is worthy of worship
    • devaḥ: the divine
    • katamo: which one
    • juṣāte: is to be enjoyed
    • kasyemāṁ: whose
    • devīm: goddess
    • amṛteṣu: among the immortal
    • preṣṭhāṁ: the most beloved
    • hṛdi: in the heart
    • śreṣāma: the best
    • suṣṭutiṁ: good praise
    • suhavyām: of the auspicious
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra poses a series of questions about divinity and the nature of worship.
    • It seeks to identify which deity among the divine beings is most worthy of praise and enjoyment.
    • The reference to "yajñiyānāṁ" indicates a connection to rituals and sacrifices, highlighting the importance of these practices in Vedic culture.
    • The phrase "amṛteṣu preṣṭhāṁ" suggests a search for the most beloved goddess among the immortal beings, emphasizing the significance of divine love and connection.
    • In a real-life context, this can be related to how individuals seek guidance and blessings from deities in their daily lives, often through rituals and prayers.
  • Significance and Real-Life Application:
    • This mantra reflects the human quest for understanding the divine and the importance of recognizing the qualities of deities that resonate with us.
    • In modern life, it can be applied in the context of seeking inspiration and guidance from higher powers, whether through meditation, prayer, or personal reflection.
    • It encourages individuals to contemplate their values and the qualities they admire in others, fostering a deeper connection with the divine and with their own spiritual journey.
    • Ultimately, this mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of devotion, reverence, and the pursuit of a meaningful relationship with the divine in our lives.
