वाच्यन्ते वां ककुहासो जूर्णायाम अधि विष्टपि |यद वां रथो विभिष पतात ||
vacyante vāṁ kakuhāso jūrṇāyām adhi viṣṭapi | yad vāṁ ratho vibhiṣ patāt ||
  • Mantra: "vacyante vāṁ kakuhāso jūrṇāyām adhi viṣṭapi | yad vāṁ ratho vibhiṣ patāt ||"
  • Translation: This mantra can be interpreted as a call for the divine to manifest and provide strength and support in times of need. It speaks to the idea of being uplifted and guided by a higher power.
  • Word Breakdown:
    • vacyante: to be spoken or declared
    • vāṁ: you (plural or respectful form)
    • kakuhāso: refers to the heart or the core of being
    • jūrṇāyām: in the state of being worn or aged
    • adhi: upon or above
    • viṣṭapi: a place of support or foundation
    • yad: that which
    • vāṁ: you (again, plural or respectful)
    • ratho: chariot or vehicle
    • vibhiṣ: to be protected or safeguarded
    • patāt: to fall or descend
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In times of personal crisis, such as losing a job or facing health issues, individuals often seek guidance and support from their community or spiritual beliefs, reflecting the essence of this mantra.
    • During significant life events, such as weddings or births, people invoke blessings for strength and protection, similar to the call for divine support in this mantra.
    • In a broader context, communities come together in times of disaster, such as natural calamities, to support one another, embodying the idea of being uplifted and guided by a collective spirit.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine support and strength during challenging times. It emphasizes the importance of community, spiritual guidance, and the need for a solid foundation in life. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied when seeking help, protection, and encouragement, reminding individuals that they are not alone in their struggles and that there is a higher power or collective spirit that can provide support.
