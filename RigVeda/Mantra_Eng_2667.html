अग्निं यन्तुरम apturam ऋतस्य योगे वनुषः |विप्राः वाजैः सम इन्दते ||
agniṁ yanturam apturam ṛtasya yoge vanuṣaḥ | viprā vājaiḥ sam indhate ||
  • Agniṁ: This word refers to "Agni," the fire god in Vedic literature, symbolizing energy, transformation, and purification.
  • Yanturam: This can be interpreted as "the one who moves" or "the one who leads," indicating a guiding force or energy.
  • Apturam: This term means "the one who is fit" or "the one who is capable," suggesting readiness and suitability for a task.
  • ṛtasya: This word translates to "of truth" or "of order," representing the cosmic order and the principles of righteousness.
  • Yoge: This refers to "union" or "connection," often associated with spiritual practices that lead to enlightenment.
  • Vanuṣaḥ: This term means "among men" or "in the human realm," indicating the relevance of the mantra to human life.
  • Viprā: This word signifies "the wise" or "the learned," referring to those who possess knowledge and insight.
  • Vājaiḥ: This translates to "with strength" or "with vigor," emphasizing the importance of power and energy in actions.
  • Sam Indhate: This phrase means "they unite" or "they come together," indicating collaboration and collective effort.

The mantra can be interpreted as a call to Agni, the divine force, to guide and empower individuals who are wise and knowledgeable. It emphasizes the importance of truth and righteousness in human actions and the need for unity and collaboration among people.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios:

  • Leadership: Just as Agni leads and transforms, effective leaders guide their teams with wisdom and integrity, fostering a sense of purpose and direction.
  • Community Building: The emphasis on unity and collaboration can be seen in community initiatives where individuals come together to achieve common goals, such as environmental conservation or social justice.
  • Personal Growth: The mantra encourages individuals to seek knowledge and truth, promoting lifelong learning and self-improvement.

In conclusion, this mantra signifies the importance of divine guidance, the pursuit of truth, and the power of collective action. It serves as a reminder that by aligning ourselves with higher principles and working together, we can achieve meaningful transformation in our lives and communities.
