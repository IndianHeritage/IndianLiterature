वव्रासो न ये स्वजाः स्वतवसा इषं स्वर अभिजायन्त धूतयः |सहस्रियासो अपां नोरमय आस गावो वन्द्यासो नोक्षणः ||
vavrāso na ye svajāḥ svatavasa iṣaṁ svar abhijāyanta dhūtayaḥ | sahasriyāso apāṁ normaya āsā gāvo vandyāso nokṣaṇaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • vavrāso: Those who are united or connected.
    • na: Not.
    • ye: Who.
    • svajāḥ: Their own kind or relatives.
    • svatavasa: By their own strength or power.
    • iṣaṁ: Desire or wish.
    • svar: Sound or voice.
    • abhijāyanta: Born or generated.
    • dhūtayaḥ: Purified or cleansed.
    • sahasriyāso: Those who are numerous or abundant.
    • apāṁ: Of waters.
    • normaya: Not to be obstructed or hindered.
    • āsā: Hope or expectation.
    • gāvo: Cows or wealth.
    • vandyāso: Worthy of worship or respect.
    • nokṣaṇaḥ: Not to be diminished or reduced.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the importance of unity and the strength that comes from being connected with one's own kind.
    • It emphasizes that those who are not united with their own kind cannot achieve their desires or wishes effectively.
    • The mantra also highlights the significance of purity and the abundance of resources, symbolized by water and cows, which are essential for sustenance and prosperity.
    • It suggests that hope and respect for the abundant resources should not be diminished, as they are vital for a fulfilling life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community, when people come together and support each other, they can achieve common goals, such as organizing a successful event or helping those in need.
    • In a workplace, teamwork and collaboration lead to better outcomes, as individuals contribute their strengths towards a shared objective.
    • In personal relationships, maintaining strong bonds with family and friends fosters emotional support and resilience during challenging times.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its reminder of the power of unity and the importance of nurturing relationships.
    • It teaches us that by working together and respecting our resources, we can create a prosperous and harmonious life.
    • This mantra is applicable in various aspects of life, including community building, workplace dynamics, and personal relationships, encouraging us to value connection and collaboration.
