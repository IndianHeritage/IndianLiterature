अदृष्टान् हन्त्यायत्यथो हन्ति परायती |अथो अवघ्नती हन्त्यअथो पिनष्टि पिम्षती ||
adṛṣṭān hanty āyaty atho hanti parāyatī | atho avaghnatī hanty atho pinaṣṭi piṁṣatī ||
  • adṛṣṭān: This word translates to "the unseen" or "the invisible." It refers to forces or entities that are not visible to the naked eye but have a significant impact on our lives.
  • hanty: This means "kills" or "destroys." It indicates a force that can eliminate or remove obstacles or challenges.
  • āyaty: This can be interpreted as "it comes" or "it arrives." It signifies the arrival of something, possibly a new opportunity or challenge.
  • atho: This word means "then" or "therefore," indicating a transition or consequence of the previous statement.
  • hanti: Again, this means "kills" or "destroys," reinforcing the idea of removal or elimination.
  • parāyatī: This translates to "the one who is beyond" or "the supreme." It refers to a higher power or force that transcends ordinary existence.
  • avaghnatī: This means "it strikes" or "it hits." It suggests a forceful action that can have a profound effect.
  • pinaṣṭi: This translates to "it annihilates" or "it completely destroys." It emphasizes the totality of destruction that can occur.
  • piṁṣatī: This means "it crushes" or "it grinds." It indicates a process of breaking down or reducing something to its basic elements.

The mantra speaks to the power of unseen forces in our lives, suggesting that there are elements beyond our perception that can influence our existence. It highlights the dual nature of these forces: they can both destroy and create, remove obstacles, and bring new opportunities.
For example, consider the impact of a sudden change in life circumstances, such as losing a job. While this may seem like a destructive force (hanty), it can also lead to new opportunities (āyaty) that one may not have considered otherwise. Similarly, the unseen forces of fate or destiny (adṛṣṭān) can guide individuals toward paths they never anticipated.
In a broader context, this mantra can be applied to various aspects of life, including personal growth, relationships, and career changes. It serves as a reminder that while we may not always see the forces at play in our lives, they are there, shaping our experiences and guiding us toward our ultimate purpose.
In conclusion, the significance of this mantra lies in its acknowledgment of the unseen forces that govern our lives. It encourages us to remain open to change and to recognize that destruction can often lead to new beginnings. By understanding and embracing these dynamics, we can navigate life's challenges with greater awareness and resilience.
