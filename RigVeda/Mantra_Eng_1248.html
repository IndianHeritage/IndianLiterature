शाश्वत पुरुषा व्य उवासा देव्य अथो अद्येदं व्य आवो मघोनि |अथो व्य उच्छाद उत्तराम् अनु dyūn अजरामृतā चरति स्वधाभिः ||
śaśvat puroṣā vy uvāsa devy atho adyedaṁ vy āvo maghonī | atho vy ucchād uttarām̐ anu dyūn ajarāmṛtā carati svadhābhiḥ ||
  • śaśvat puroṣā vy uvāsa devy: This phrase can be translated as "the eternal beings reside in the divine." Here, "śaśvat" means eternal, "puroṣā" refers to beings or persons, and "devy" indicates the divine or godly aspect. This suggests that the essence of life and existence is rooted in a divine presence.
  • atho adyedaṁ vy āvo maghonī: This translates to "and today, we invoke the abundance." "Atho" means 'and', "adyedaṁ" refers to 'today', and "maghonī" signifies 'abundance' or 'wealth'. This line emphasizes the importance of recognizing and invoking prosperity in our lives, suggesting that we should actively seek and appreciate the abundance around us.
  • atho vy ucchād uttarām̐ anu dyūn: This can be interpreted as "and we rise towards the higher realms." "Ucchād" means 'to rise', "uttarām̐" refers to 'higher', and "anu dyūn" indicates 'towards the heavens'. This line encourages individuals to aspire for higher goals and spiritual enlightenment, suggesting a journey towards greater understanding and existence.
  • ajarāmṛtā carati svadhābhiḥ: This translates to "the immortal ones move with their own essence." "Ajarāmṛtā" means 'immortal', "carati" refers to 'move', and "svadhābhiḥ" indicates 'with their own essence'. This line highlights the idea of living in harmony with one's true self and the eternal nature of the soul, suggesting that true fulfillment comes from aligning with our inner essence.

  • Significance: This mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking divine connection, abundance, higher aspirations, and living in alignment with one's true self. It serves as a reminder of the eternal nature of existence and the importance of spiritual growth.
  • Real-life Application: In practical terms, this mantra can be applied in various aspects of life. For instance, in personal development, one can reflect on their goals and aspirations, seeking to rise above challenges and connect with their inner self. In professional settings, invoking abundance can translate to recognizing opportunities for growth and success. Additionally, in spiritual practices, this mantra can be used as a meditative focus to cultivate a deeper connection with the divine and one's true essence.
