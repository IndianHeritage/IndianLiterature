उरु वां रथः परि नक्षति द्याम आ यत समुद्राद अभि वर्तते वाम् |मध्वा माध्वी मधु वां पृथस्यान यत सीं वां पृथ्क्षो भुरजन्त पक्वाः ||
uru vāṁ rathaḥ pari nakṣati dyām ā yat samudrād abhi vartate vām | madhvā mādhvī madhu vām pruṣāyan yat sīṁ vām pṛkṣo bhurajanta pakvāḥ ||
  • uru vāṁ rathaḥ pari nakṣati dyām: This phrase can be translated to "the great chariot moves around the sky." Here, "uru" means great or vast, "vāṁ" refers to you, "rathaḥ" means chariot, "pari" means around, "nakṣati" means moves, and "dyām" refers to the sky or heavens. This can symbolize the journey of life and the vastness of the universe.
  • ā yat samudrād abhi vartate vām: This translates to "from the ocean, it comes to you." "Ā" means from, "yat" means that which, "samudrād" refers to the ocean, "abhi" means towards, and "vartate" means comes. This can represent the idea of abundance and the flow of resources or knowledge from a vast source to an individual.
  • madhvā mādhvī madhu vām pruṣāyan: This can be interpreted as "sweetness, sweet, and honey come to you." "Madhvā" means sweetness, "mādhvī" refers to sweet, "madhu" means honey, and "vām" again refers to you. This emphasizes the idea of receiving joy, nourishment, and positivity in life.
  • yat sīṁ vām pṛkṣo bhurajanta pakvāḥ: This translates to "that which is strong and brings you the ripened fruits." "Yat" means that which, "sīṁ" refers to strength, "vām" is you, "pṛkṣo" means to bring, "bhurajanta" refers to abundance, and "pakvāḥ" means ripened or matured. This signifies the importance of strength and resilience in achieving the fruits of one's labor.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda encapsulates the journey of life, the flow of resources, the sweetness of experiences, and the strength required to attain one's goals. In real life, it can be applied to various scenarios such as personal growth, professional endeavors, and relationships. For instance, just as a chariot moves through the vast sky, individuals navigate through life's challenges. The ocean symbolizes the vast opportunities available, while the sweetness represents the joy and fulfillment that come from hard work and perseverance. Ultimately, this mantra serves as a reminder to embrace the journey, seek abundance, and cultivate strength to reap the rewards of life.
