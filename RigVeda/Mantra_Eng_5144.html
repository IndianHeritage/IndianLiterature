मां शूने अग्नि नि षदाम नृणाम मांशेषसो 'वीरता परि त्वा | प्रजावतिषु दुर्यासु दुर्य ||
mā śūne agne ni ṣadāma nṛṇām māśeṣaso 'vīratā pari tvā | prajāvatīṣu duryāsu durya ||
  • mā śūne agne: This phrase translates to "O Agni, do not let us be without." Agni, the fire god, symbolizes energy, transformation, and the divine presence in rituals. In real life, this can be interpreted as a plea for guidance and support in our endeavors.
  • ni ṣadāma nṛṇām: This means "let us not be deprived of our strength." It emphasizes the importance of resilience and strength in human life. For example, in challenging situations like job loss or personal crises, maintaining inner strength is crucial for overcoming adversity.
  • māśeṣaso 'vīratā: This translates to "may we not be devoid of valor." Valor is essential in facing life's challenges. In real life, this can be seen in individuals who stand up for justice or fight against oppression, embodying courage in their actions.
  • pari tvā: This means "we turn to you." It signifies seeking help and guidance from a higher power or the universe. In practical terms, this can be related to seeking mentorship or support from experienced individuals during difficult times.
  • prajāvatīṣu duryāsu durya: This phrase can be interpreted as "in the midst of difficulties, may we have progeny." It highlights the importance of continuity and growth even in challenging circumstances. For instance, families often find ways to thrive and grow despite economic hardships, emphasizing resilience and hope for the future.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a profound message of seeking strength, valor, and guidance from the divine, especially in times of difficulty. Its significance lies in the universal human experience of facing challenges and the need for support and resilience. In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as personal struggles, community challenges, or even in professional settings where teamwork and support are essential for overcoming obstacles. By invoking the essence of this mantra, individuals can find inspiration to persevere and thrive amidst adversity.
