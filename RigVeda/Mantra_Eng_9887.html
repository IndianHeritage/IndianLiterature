सुदेवो अद्य प्रपतेद अनावृत परावतं परमां गन्तवा उ |अधā शयीत निरृतेर उपस्थे 'धैनें वृका रभसासो अद्युः ||
sudevo adya prapated anāvṛt parāvatam paramāṁ gantavā u | adhā śayīta nirṛter upasthe 'dhainaṁ vṛkā rabhasāso adyuḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sudevo: The one who is auspicious or good.
    • adya: Today or in the present moment.
    • prapated: Has reached or attained.
    • anāvṛt: Unobstructed or without hindrance.
    • parāvatam: The highest or supreme place.
    • paramāṁ: The ultimate or the best.
    • gantavā: To be reached or attained.
    • adhā: Below or beneath.
    • śayīta: To lie down or rest.
    • nirṛter: Of the destructive or negative forces.
    • upasthe: In the presence or vicinity.
    • 'dhainaṁ: This or that (referring to a specific entity).
    • vṛkā: Wolves or predators.
    • rabhasāso: With great force or intensity.
    • adyuḥ: Today or in the present time.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of reaching a state of auspiciousness and enlightenment in the present moment.
    • It emphasizes the importance of overcoming obstacles to attain the highest spiritual goals.
    • The reference to "lying down" in the presence of destructive forces suggests a need for vigilance and awareness.
    • The mention of "wolves" symbolizes challenges or adversities that may arise with intensity.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, one may face challenges (the "wolves") that require resilience and strategic thinking to overcome.
    • In personal life, individuals may encounter negative influences or distractions that hinder their spiritual or personal growth.
    • Practicing mindfulness and being present (as suggested by "adya") can help individuals navigate through life's challenges effectively.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder to strive for auspiciousness and clarity in our lives.
    • It encourages individuals to confront and overcome obstacles with awareness and strength.
    • In a broader sense, it highlights the importance of being present and mindful in the face of life's adversities, ultimately guiding us towards our highest potential.
