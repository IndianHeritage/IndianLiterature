अच्छा गीरो मतयो देवयन्तीर अग्निं यान्ति द्रविणं भिक्षमाणाः |सुसन्दृशं सुप्रतीकं स्वञ्चं हव्यवाहम् अरतिम मानवाणाम् ||
acchā giro matayo devayantīr agniṁ yanti draviṇam bhikṣamāṇāḥ | susaṁdṛśaṁ supratīkaṁ svañcaṁ havyavāham aratim mānuṣāṇām ||
  • acchā giro matayo: This phrase can be translated as "good thoughts and intentions." It emphasizes the importance of having positive and constructive thoughts in our lives. For example, when we approach challenges with a positive mindset, we are more likely to find solutions and overcome obstacles.
  • devayantīr agniṁ: This refers to the divine fire or energy that is believed to guide and purify our actions. In real life, this can be likened to the passion and motivation we feel when pursuing our goals. For instance, a person who is passionate about their work often inspires others and creates a positive environment.
  • yanti draviṇam bhikṣamāṇāḥ: This translates to "those who seek wealth and resources." It highlights the pursuit of material and spiritual wealth. In today's context, this can be seen in individuals who strive for financial stability while also seeking personal growth and fulfillment.
  • susaṁdṛśaṁ supratīkaṁ: This means "well-seen and well-formed." It suggests the importance of clarity and vision in our endeavors. For example, having a clear plan or vision for a project can lead to successful outcomes, as it allows individuals to focus their efforts effectively.
  • svañcaṁ havyavāham: This phrase can be interpreted as "the one who carries offerings." It signifies the act of giving and sharing with others. In real life, this can be exemplified by charitable actions, where individuals or organizations contribute to the welfare of society, thus creating a sense of community and support.
  • aratim mānuṣāṇām: This translates to "the absence of sorrow for humanity." It emphasizes the importance of joy and contentment in life. For instance, engaging in activities that bring happiness, such as spending time with loved ones or pursuing hobbies, can lead to a more fulfilling life.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of positive thinking, divine guidance, the pursuit of wealth, clarity of vision, the importance of giving, and the pursuit of joy. In real life, these principles can be applied in various scenarios, such as personal development, professional endeavors, and community service. By embodying these values, individuals can lead more meaningful and impactful lives, contributing to their own well-being and that of others around them.
