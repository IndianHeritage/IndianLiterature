मन्त्र: उतो नो अस्य कस्य चिद् दाक्षस्य तव वृत्रहन् |अस्मभ्यं नृम्णम आ भरास्मभ्यं नृमणस्यसे ||
uto no asya kasya cid dakṣasya tava vṛtrahan | asmabhyaṁ nṛmṇam ā bharāsmabhyaṁ nṛmaṇasyase ||
  • Mantra: "uto no asya kasya cid dakṣasya tava vṛtrahan | asmabhyaṁ nṛmṇam ā bharāsmabhyaṁ nṛmaṇasyase ||"
  • Translation: "O Vritra-han, who is capable and powerful, may you bestow upon us the strength of manliness; grant us the vigor of life and the ability to overcome challenges."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • Uto: A call for attention or invocation.
    • No: Our, indicating possession or belonging.
    • Asya: This, referring to the current situation or context.
    • Kasya: Whose, indicating a connection to a powerful entity.
    • Cid: Any, suggesting inclusivity or universality.
    • Dakṣasya: Capable or skilled, referring to the qualities of the deity invoked.
    • Tava: Your, indicating the deity's attributes.
    • Vṛtrahan: Slayer of Vritra, a metaphor for overcoming obstacles and challenges.
    • Asmabhyaṁ: To us, indicating the recipients of the blessings.
    • Nṛmṇam: Strength or manliness, referring to vigor and vitality.
    • Ā: To, indicating direction or purpose.
    • Bharā: Bestow or grant, a request for divine assistance.
    • Nṛmaṇasyase: Of life or existence, referring to the essence of being.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a competitive environment, such as a workplace, individuals often face challenges that require them to summon their inner strength and capabilities. This mantra can serve as a reminder to seek the strength to overcome obstacles.
    • In sports, athletes often invoke a sense of power and capability to push through physical and mental barriers. The essence of this mantra can inspire them to harness their skills and overcome their opponents.
    • During personal struggles, such as health issues or emotional challenges, individuals can reflect on this mantra to find the strength to persevere and seek support from their community.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its invocation of strength and capability. It emphasizes the importance of overcoming challenges and seeking support from divine forces. In real-life scenarios, whether in personal struggles, professional challenges, or competitive environments, this mantra serves as a powerful reminder to harness one's inner strength and capabilities. It encourages individuals to face their obstacles with courage and determination, ultimately leading to personal growth and success.
