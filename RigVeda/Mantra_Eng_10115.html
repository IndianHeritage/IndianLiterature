अग्निः ह नामा धायि दन्न अपस्तमः सं यो वनाय युवते भस्मना दता |अभिप्रमुरा जुह्वा स्वध्वरा इनो न प्रोथमानो यवसे वृषा ||
agnir ha nāma dhāyi dann apastamaḥ saṁ yo vanā yuvate bhasmanā datā | abhipramurā juhvā svadhvara ino na prothamāno yavase vṛṣā ||
  • Agnir ha nāma dhāyi: This phrase translates to "Agni, indeed, is the name that supports." Here, Agni represents the divine fire, symbolizing energy, transformation, and purification. In real life, we can relate this to the role of fire in cooking, warmth, and even in rituals that purify and transform our intentions.
  • Dann apastamaḥ: This means "the one who gives wealth." It signifies the abundance and prosperity that Agni can bestow upon us. In modern terms, this can be seen in how we seek opportunities for growth and success in our careers or businesses, where hard work and dedication can lead to wealth.
  • Saṁ yo vanā yuvate bhasmanā datā: This translates to "who, like a young man, gives wealth with brilliance." This line emphasizes the youthful energy and vibrancy associated with Agni. In our lives, this can be reflected in the enthusiasm and creativity of youth, which often leads to innovative ideas and solutions in various fields.
  • Abhipramurā juhvā svadhvara: This means "the one who is invoked with offerings." It highlights the importance of rituals and offerings in connecting with the divine. In contemporary life, this can be seen in how we express gratitude and seek blessings through various forms of offerings, whether material or through acts of kindness.
  • Ino na prothamāno yavase vṛṣā: This translates to "may we not be deprived of the wealth that is ours." It is a plea for abundance and protection from loss. In real life, this can resonate with our desire for security and stability in our lives, whether it be financial, emotional, or spiritual.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of Agni as a source of energy, wealth, and transformation. It teaches us the importance of invoking divine support through our actions and intentions. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire us to seek abundance, embrace our youthful energy, and express gratitude, ultimately leading to a more fulfilled and prosperous life. By recognizing the transformative power of our actions and the importance of connection with the divine, we can navigate our lives with purpose and intention.
