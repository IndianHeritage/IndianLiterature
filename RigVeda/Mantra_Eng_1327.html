आ वां रथं युवतिस तिष्ठद अत्र जुष्ट्वी नराऽ दूहिता सूर्यस्य |परि वाम अश्वा वपुषः पतङ्गा वयो वहन्तु अरुषा अभीक ||
ā vāṁ rathaṁ yuvatis tiṣṭhad atra juṣṭvī narā duhitā sūryasya | pari vām aśvā vapuṣaḥ pataṁgā vayo vahantv aruṣā abhīke ||
  • Mantra: "ā vāṁ rathaṁ yuvatis tiṣṭhad atra juṣṭvī narā duhitā sūryasya | pari vām aśvā vapuṣaḥ pataṁgā vayo vahantv aruṣā abhīke ||"
  • Translation: "May the youthful maiden, the daughter of the sun, stand here on the chariot. May the horses, with their beautiful forms, carry us swiftly and safely."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • ā vāṁ: "May we" - a call for collective well-being.
    • rathaṁ: "chariot" - symbolizes the journey of life.
    • yuvatis: "youthful maiden" - represents vitality and energy.
    • tiṣṭhad: "stand" - signifies stability and presence.
    • atra: "here" - indicates the importance of the present moment.
    • juṣṭvī: "pleasing" - denotes harmony and joy.
    • narā: "men" - refers to humanity.
    • duhitā: "daughter" - symbolizes nurturing and care.
    • sūryasya: "of the sun" - represents enlightenment and energy.
    • pari vām: "may surround us" - a call for protection and support.
    • aśvā: "horses" - symbolize strength and speed.
    • vapuṣaḥ: "with beautiful forms" - signifies the importance of aesthetics and beauty in life.
    • pataṁgā: "swiftly" - denotes the desire for quick progress.
    • vayo: "age" - refers to the passage of time.
    • vahantv: "may carry" - a wish for support in our endeavors.
    • aruṣā: "red" - often symbolizes auspiciousness and vitality.
    • abhīke: "may they be" - a hopeful expression for the future.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a wedding ceremony, invoking blessings for the couple to have a joyful and prosperous journey together can be likened to the essence of this mantra.
    • In a graduation ceremony, students may seek the blessings of their mentors and family to support them as they embark on their professional journeys, similar to the chariot metaphor.
    • In sports, athletes often call upon their team spirit and the support of their coaches and fans to achieve victory, reflecting the desire for strength and speed in the mantra.
  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of support, vitality, and the journey of life. It serves as a reminder to seek harmony and strength in our endeavors, whether in personal relationships, professional pursuits, or communal activities. By invoking the blessings of energy and protection, we can navigate the challenges of life with grace and speed, making it applicable in various real-life scenarios such as celebrations, transitions, and collective efforts.
