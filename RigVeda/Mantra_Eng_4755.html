ऋतस्य पथि वेधाऽपायि श्रीये मनांसि देवासो अकृन्। दधानो नाम महो वचोभिर वपुर दृषये वेन्यो व्य आवः॥
ṛtasya pathi vedhā apāyi śriye manāṁsi devāso akran | dadhāno nāma maho vacobhir vapur dṛśaye venyo vy āvaḥ ||
  • ṛtasya pathi: This phrase translates to "on the path of truth." In real life, this can be understood as following a moral and ethical path in our actions and decisions. For example, a person who chooses honesty in their business dealings exemplifies this principle.
  • vedhā apāyi: This means "may the knowledge be revealed." It signifies the importance of gaining wisdom and understanding. In a practical context, this can relate to the pursuit of education and learning, where individuals seek to uncover truths about the world around them.
  • śriye manāṁsi: This translates to "for the prosperity of the mind." It emphasizes the need for mental well-being and clarity. For instance, engaging in mindfulness practices or meditation can enhance mental health and lead to a more prosperous life.
  • devāso akran: This means "the divine beings have gathered." It suggests the presence of higher powers or energies in our lives. In real life, this can be seen in moments of inspiration or creativity, where individuals feel a connection to something greater than themselves.
  • dadhāno nāma maho vacobhir: This translates to "holding the great name with powerful words." It highlights the significance of language and communication. For example, a leader who uses inspiring rhetoric can motivate and unite people towards a common goal.
  • vapur dṛśaye: This means "to manifest the form." It signifies the importance of bringing ideas into reality. In practical terms, this can be seen in entrepreneurship, where individuals take their visions and turn them into tangible products or services.
  • venyo vy āvaḥ: This translates to "may we be surrounded by good." It emphasizes the desire for positive influences and environments. In real life, this can relate to building supportive communities and relationships that foster growth and happiness.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of living a life aligned with truth, wisdom, and prosperity. It encourages individuals to seek knowledge, maintain mental well-being, and communicate effectively while being surrounded by positive influences. In today's world, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as in personal development, leadership, and community building, ultimately guiding individuals towards a fulfilling and meaningful life.
