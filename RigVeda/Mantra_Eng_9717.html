यम इमं त्वं वृषाकपिम प्रियं इन्द्राभिरक्षसि |श्वा nv अस्य जम्बिषद अपि कर्णे वराहयुर विश्वस्माद इन्द्र उत्तरः ||
yam imaṁ tvaṁ vṛṣākapim priyam indrābhirakṣasi | śvā nv asya jambhiṣad api karṇe varāhayur viśvasmād indra uttaraḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yam: who
    • imaṁ: this
    • tvaṁ: you
    • vṛṣākapim: O Indra, the one who is strong and powerful
    • priyam: dear
    • indrābhirakṣasi: you protect with the strength of Indra
    • śvā: may this
    • nv: indeed
    • asya: of this
    • jambhiṣad: from the obstacles
    • api: even
    • karṇe: in the ears
    • varāhayur: may they be removed
    • viśvasmād: from all
    • indra: O Indra
    • uttaraḥ: the best
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Indra, the king of the gods, asking for protection and the removal of obstacles.
    • The phrase "O Indra, the one who is strong and powerful" emphasizes the might and authority of Indra, suggesting that he is capable of providing the necessary strength to overcome challenges.
    • The request for protection "you protect with the strength of Indra" indicates a reliance on divine power in times of need.
    • The mention of "may they be removed from the ears" symbolizes the desire to eliminate distractions or negative influences that hinder one's progress.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, individuals may face various challenges such as competition or workplace politics. This mantra can be invoked as a reminder to seek strength and clarity in decision-making.
    • Students preparing for exams can recite this mantra to focus their minds and eliminate distractions, thereby enhancing their concentration and performance.
    • During personal struggles, such as health issues or emotional turmoil, individuals can find solace in this mantra, seeking divine intervention for strength and protection.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of seeking divine support in overcoming life's challenges.
    • It serves as a reminder that strength and protection can come from a higher power, encouraging individuals to remain resilient in the face of adversity.
    • In real-life scenarios, invoking this mantra can provide comfort, focus, and a sense of empowerment, making it applicable in various aspects of life, from personal struggles to professional challenges.
