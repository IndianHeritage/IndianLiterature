इमे त इन्द्र ते वयं पुरुष्टुत ये त्वारभ्य चरामसि प्रभूवसो |नहि त्वद अन्यो गिरवणो गिरः सघात क्षोणीर्व इव प्रति नो हर्य तद वचः ||
ime ta indra te vayam puruṣṭuta ye tvārabhya carāmasi prabhūvaso | nahi tvad anyo girvaṇo giraḥ saghat kṣoṇīr iva prati no harya tad vacaḥ ||
  • ime ta indra te vayam puruṣṭuta: This phrase can be translated to "These are our offerings to you, Indra, the great one." Here, Indra is recognized as a powerful deity, often associated with rain, thunder, and strength. In real life, this can be likened to how people offer gratitude or seek blessings from higher powers or nature for prosperity and well-being.
  • ye tvārabhya carāmasi prabhūvaso: This translates to "We are those who move towards you, O Lord." It signifies a sense of devotion and the act of seeking guidance or support from a higher power. In everyday life, this can be seen in how individuals seek mentors or leaders who can guide them in their personal or professional journeys.
  • nahi tvad anyo girvaṇo giraḥ: This means "There is no other like you, O Indra, in the world of sounds." It emphasizes the uniqueness and supremacy of Indra among all deities. In a modern context, this can be compared to how people often look up to unique leaders or innovators who stand out in their fields, such as a groundbreaking scientist or a visionary entrepreneur.
  • saghat kṣoṇīr iva prati no harya tad vacaḥ: This translates to "Like the earth, may your words come to us." This line suggests a desire for the blessings and wisdom of Indra to be as abundant and nurturing as the earth itself. In real life, this can be related to the importance of communication and the sharing of knowledge, akin to how communities thrive on shared wisdom and support.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a deep sense of reverence towards Indra, acknowledging his power and seeking his guidance. Its significance lies in the universal themes of devotion, the search for wisdom, and the recognition of unique leadership. In real life, this mantra can inspire individuals to seek guidance from mentors, appreciate the uniqueness of influential figures, and foster a community where knowledge and support are shared abundantly. It serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of all beings and the importance of seeking higher wisdom in our journeys.
