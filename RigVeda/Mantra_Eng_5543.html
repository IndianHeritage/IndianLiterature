उद अस्य बाहू शिथिरा बृहन्ता हिरण्यया दिवो अन्ताम् अनष्टाम् |नूनं सो अस्य महिमा पनिष्ट सूरश्चिद अस्मानु दाद अपस्याम् ||
ud asya bāhū śithirā bṛhantā hiraṇyayā divo antām̐ anaṣṭām | nūnaṁ so asya mahimā paniṣṭa sūraś cid asmā anu dād apasyām ||
  • ud asya bāhū: This phrase translates to "the arms of this (being) are raised." It signifies strength and readiness for action. In real life, this can be likened to a person preparing to take on challenges, such as an athlete getting ready for a competition.
  • śithirā bṛhantā: This means "strong and powerful." It emphasizes the idea of resilience and capability. For example, a community coming together to support each other during a crisis demonstrates collective strength.
  • hiraṇyayā divo antām̐ anaṣṭām: This translates to "the golden (light) of the sky is not lost." It symbolizes hope and the eternal nature of light and positivity. In everyday life, this can be seen in how people find hope in difficult situations, like a student persevering through challenges to achieve their dreams.
  • nūnaṁ so asya mahimā paniṣṭa: This means "indeed, the greatness of this (being) is established." It reflects the recognition of one's potential and achievements. For instance, a leader who inspires others through their vision and actions embodies this greatness.
  • sūraś cid asmā anu dād apasyām: This translates to "the brave one gives us strength." It highlights the importance of courage and support from others. In real life, this can be seen in how mentors or role models empower individuals to overcome their fears and pursue their goals.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the themes of strength, resilience, hope, recognition of greatness, and the importance of courage. Its significance lies in inspiring individuals to harness their inner strength and support one another in the face of challenges. In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as personal development, community support, and leadership, encouraging people to rise above difficulties and strive for greatness together.
