मन्त्र: अतिविद्धा विथुरेणाऽचिद् अस्त्रā त्रिः सप्त सानु संहितā गिरीणाम् | न तद् देवो न मार्त्यस् तुतुर्याद् यानि प्रवृद्धो वृषभश्चकार ||
atividdhā vithureṇā cid astrā triḥ sapta sānu saṁhitā girīṇām | na tad devo na martyas tuturyād yāni pravṛddho vṛṣabhaś cakāra ||
  • Atividdhā vithureṇā: This phrase can be interpreted as "having transcended the limitations of the material world." It suggests a state of enlightenment or a higher understanding that goes beyond ordinary perception.
  • Cid astrā: "Cid" refers to consciousness or awareness, while "astrā" can mean weapons or tools. Together, this implies that true awareness is a powerful tool that can be used to navigate life's challenges.
  • Triḥ sapta sānu saṁhitā girīṇām: This part can be understood as "the three and seven together form a unity among the mountains." It symbolizes the interconnectedness of various elements in nature and life, emphasizing harmony and balance.
  • Na tad devo na martyas tuturyād: This translates to "neither the divine nor the mortal can escape it." It highlights the inevitability of certain truths or laws that govern existence, applicable to both gods and humans.
  • Yāni pravṛddho vṛṣabhaś cakāra: This phrase means "whatever the powerful bull has created." The bull often symbolizes strength and fertility, representing the creative forces in the universe that drive progress and evolution.

  • Significance: This mantra encapsulates profound philosophical ideas about consciousness, the nature of existence, and the interconnectedness of all things. It encourages individuals to seek a higher understanding and to recognize the unity in diversity.
  • Real-life Application: In practical terms, this mantra can be applied in various aspects of life, such as in personal development, where one strives to transcend limitations and cultivate awareness. It can also be relevant in environmental consciousness, reminding us of our connection to nature and the importance of harmony within ecosystems.

  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a reminder of the deeper truths that govern our lives. By embracing the principles of interconnectedness and awareness, individuals can navigate their journeys with greater wisdom and purpose, ultimately contributing to a more harmonious existence.
