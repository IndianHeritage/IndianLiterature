तवे वसूनि पूर्वणीक होतर दोषा वस्तोर एरिरे यज्ञियासः |क्षामेव विश्वा भुवनानि यस्मिन सं सौभागानि दधिरे पावके ||
tve vasūni purvaṇīka hotar doṣā vastor erire yajñiyāsaḥ | kṣāmeva viśvā bhuvanāni yasmin saṁ saubhagāni dadhire pāvake ||
  • tve vasūni purvaṇīka: This phrase can be translated as "to you, the ancient deities." Here, "tve" refers to "you," "vasūni" signifies "the deities" or "the divine forces," and "purvaṇīka" means "ancient" or "of the past." This highlights the reverence for the timeless and eternal aspects of divinity.
  • hotar doṣā vastor erire: This part translates to "the priest (hotar) who is free from faults (doṣā) in the offerings (vastor)." The term "hotar" refers to the priest or the one who performs the rituals, while "doṣā" indicates any faults or imperfections. This emphasizes the importance of purity and sincerity in spiritual practices.
  • yajñiyāsaḥ: This word means "the offerings" or "sacrifices." It signifies the act of giving and the importance of rituals in connecting with the divine.
  • kṣāmeva viśvā bhuvanāni: This translates to "in whom all worlds (bhuvanāni) are established (kṣāmeva)." It suggests that the divine presence is the foundation of all existence, encompassing all realms of life.
  • yasmin saṁ saubhagāni dadhire pāvake: This phrase means "in whom the auspicious (saubhagāni) things are established (dadhire) in the fire (pāvake)." Here, "pāvake" refers to fire, which is often seen as a purifying element in rituals. This indicates that the divine presence brings forth prosperity and auspiciousness in life.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of connecting with the ancient divine forces through sincere rituals and offerings. It highlights the importance of purity in spiritual practices and acknowledges the divine as the foundation of all existence. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire individuals to engage in selfless acts, rituals, and community service, fostering a sense of connection with the divine and promoting harmony and prosperity in their lives and surroundings. By recognizing the divine in all aspects of life, one can cultivate a deeper understanding of existence and contribute positively to the world.
