गीर्णं भुवनं तमसापगूढम् आविः स्वर अभवज जाते अग्नौ |तस्य देवाः पृथिवी द्यौर उतापो 'रणयन्न ओषधिः सख्ये अस्य ||
gīrṇam bhuvanaṁ tamasāpagūḻham āviḥ svar abhavaj jāte agnau | tasya devāḥ pṛthivī dyaur utāpo 'raṇayann oṣadhīḥ sakhye asya ||
  • gīrṇam bhuvanaṁ: This phrase can be translated as "the world that is filled with sound" or "the universe that resonates." It signifies the vibrational nature of existence, where everything is interconnected through sound and energy.
  • tamasāpagūḻham: This means "overcome by darkness" or "enveloped in ignorance." It reflects the challenges and obstacles that individuals face in life, often represented by ignorance or lack of awareness.
  • āviḥ svar: Translated as "the light of the sun" or "the radiant sound," this phrase symbolizes enlightenment, knowledge, and the awakening of consciousness. It suggests the importance of seeking truth and clarity in life.
  • abhavaj jāte agnau: This can be interpreted as "born from the fire" or "emerging from the flames." Fire is often associated with transformation and purification, indicating that through challenges, one can emerge stronger and more enlightened.
  • tasya devāḥ: This means "the gods of that" or "the divine forces." It emphasizes the role of divine energies in guiding and supporting individuals through their journeys.
  • pṛthivī dyaur utāpo: Translated as "the earth and the sky," this phrase represents the duality of existence and the balance between the material and spiritual realms. It highlights the interconnectedness of all elements of life.
  • 'raṇayann oṣadhīḥ: This can be understood as "the herbs that nourish" or "the plants that provide sustenance." It signifies the importance of nature and its resources in sustaining life and promoting well-being.
  • sakhye asya: This phrase translates to "in friendship with this" or "in harmony with this." It emphasizes the significance of relationships and community in achieving balance and fulfillment in life.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the journey of life, highlighting the interplay between sound, light, darkness, and transformation. It serves as a reminder that despite the challenges and ignorance we may face, there is always the potential for enlightenment and growth. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied to personal development, encouraging individuals to seek knowledge, embrace transformation, and foster harmonious relationships with others and nature. It signifies the importance of community and divine guidance in navigating the complexities of existence, ultimately leading to a more fulfilled and enlightened life.
