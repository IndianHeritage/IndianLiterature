अभ्यूर्णोति यन् नग्नं भीषक्ति विश्वं यत तुरम् |प्रेम अन्धः ख्यन निः श्रोणो भूत ||
abhy ūrṇoti yan nagnam bhiṣakti viśvaṁ yat turam | prem andhaḥ khyan niḥ śroṇo bhūt ||
  • Word Breakdown:
    • abhy - towards, in the direction of
    • ūrṇoti - to cover, to envelop
    • yan - that which
    • nagnam - naked, bare
    • bhiṣakti - power, strength
    • viśvaṁ - all, the universe
    • yat - that which
    • turam - swift, quick
    • prem - love, affection
    • andhaḥ - blind
    • khyan - to know, to understand
    • niḥ - without
    • śroṇo - hips, loins
    • bhūt - being, existence
  • Meaning:
    • This mantra speaks about the power of love and the universal strength that envelops all beings.
    • It suggests that love can cover and protect those who are vulnerable or exposed (naked).
    • The phrase "bhiṣakti viśvaṁ" indicates that this love is a universal force, capable of swift action ("turam").
    • It also highlights the idea that love can be blind ("prem andhaḥ"), meaning it does not discriminate and is unconditional.
    • The mention of "niḥ śroṇo bhūt" implies that true understanding and existence come from a place of love, free from attachment or ego.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In relationships, love often acts as a protective shield, helping individuals feel secure and valued, even in their most vulnerable states.
    • In community service, acts of love and kindness can uplift those who are struggling, providing them with strength and support.
    • In leadership, a leader who embodies love and compassion can inspire their team, fostering a sense of unity and purpose.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of love as a powerful, universal force that can protect and uplift individuals.
    • It teaches us that love is essential for understanding and existence, encouraging us to act with compassion and empathy in our daily lives.
    • In real-life scenarios, applying the principles of this mantra can lead to stronger relationships, healthier communities, and more effective leadership.
