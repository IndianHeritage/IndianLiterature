मन्त्र: तिस्रो द्यावो निहिता अन्तर अस्मिन तिस्रो भूमीर उपराः षड्विधानाः | गृत्सो राजा वरुणश चक्र एतं दिवि प्रेङ्खं हिरण्ययं शुभे कम् ||
tisro dyāvo nihitā antar asmin tisro bhūmīr uparāḥ ṣaḍvidhānāḥ | gṛtso rājā varuṇaś cakra etaṁ divi preṅkhaṁ hiraṇyayaṁ śubhe kam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • tisro: three
    • dyāvo: heavens
    • nihitā: placed or situated
    • antar: within
    • asmin: this
    • bhūmīr: earth
    • uparāḥ: above
    • ṣaḍvidhānāḥ: sixfold
    • gṛtso: the one who is rich or prosperous
    • rājā: king
    • varuṇaś: Varuna, the god of cosmic order
    • cakra: wheel or cycle
    • etaṁ: this
    • divi: in the sky or heaven
    • preṅkhaṁ: to be established or set
    • hiraṇyayaṁ: golden or auspicious
    • śubhe: auspicious or good
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the three heavens that are situated within this world, indicating a connection between the earthly realm and the celestial.
    • The mention of "three" can symbolize various triads found in life, such as the past, present, and future, or the mind, body, and spirit.
    • The "sixfold" aspect refers to the various dimensions or aspects of existence, suggesting a holistic view of life.
    • The invocation of Varuna as the king emphasizes the importance of cosmic order and righteousness in governance and personal conduct.
    • The "golden" aspect signifies prosperity, success, and auspiciousness, which are desirable in both personal and communal life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In personal development, one can reflect on the triads mentioned in the mantra to create a balanced life, focusing on mental, physical, and spiritual well-being.
    • In leadership, the qualities of Varuna can be emulated by leaders who strive for justice, integrity, and the welfare of their people.
    • In community building, the mantra can inspire collective efforts towards creating a harmonious environment that respects both earthly and spiritual values.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of all aspects of life, urging individuals to seek balance and harmony.
    • Its significance lies in promoting a holistic approach to life, where spiritual, ethical, and material dimensions are acknowledged and nurtured.
    • In real-life scenarios, it encourages individuals and communities to strive for prosperity while adhering to cosmic and moral principles, ultimately leading to a fulfilling existence.
