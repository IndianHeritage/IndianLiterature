यत तृतीयं सवनं रत्नधेयम् अकृतुध्वं स्वपस्याऽ सुहस्ताः |तद ऋभवः परिषिक्तं व एतत सम मदेभिर इन्द्रियैः पिबध्वम् ||
yat tṛtīyaṁ savanaṁ ratnadheyam akṛṇudhvaṁ svapasyā suhastāḥ | tad ṛbhavaḥ pariṣiktaṁ va etat sam madebhir indriyebhiḥ pibadhvam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yat: that which
    • tṛtīyaṁ: third
    • savanaṁ: offering or sacrifice
    • ratnadheyam: endowed with riches or treasures
    • akṛṇudhvaṁ: do not neglect or do not forsake
    • svapasyā: with a clear mind or vision
    • suhastāḥ: with good hands or auspicious actions
    • tad: that
    • ṛbhavaḥ: be strong or be powerful
    • pariṣiktaṁ: consecrated or purified
    • va: indeed
    • etat: this
    • sam: together or in harmony
    • madebhir: with joy or bliss
    • indriyebhiḥ: with the senses
    • pibadhvam: drink or consume
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of the third offering, which is rich in treasures and should not be neglected.
    • It encourages individuals to approach life with clarity and auspicious actions, suggesting that one should be mindful and purposeful in their endeavors.
    • The call to be strong and to partake in the blessings of life with joy and harmony highlights the significance of community and shared experiences.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, this mantra can be applied to teamwork. Just as the mantra advises not to neglect the third offering, team members should not overlook their contributions and the importance of collaboration.
    • In personal relationships, approaching interactions with clarity and good intentions can lead to stronger bonds, much like the mantra suggests auspicious actions.
    • During celebrations or communal gatherings, the idea of sharing joy and blessings resonates with the mantra's call to drink from the well of happiness together.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its holistic approach to life, urging individuals to recognize the value of their contributions, act with purpose, and share joy with others.
    • In real-life scenarios, it serves as a reminder to maintain clarity in our actions, foster strong relationships, and celebrate the richness of life together.
    • Ultimately, it encourages a balanced and harmonious existence, where each individual plays a vital role in the collective well-being.
