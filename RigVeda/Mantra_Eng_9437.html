विरूपास इद ऋषयस् त इद गम्भीरवेपसः |ते अङ्गिरसः सूनवस् ते अग्नेः परि जज्निरे ||
virūpāsa id ṛṣayas ta id gambhīravepasaḥ | te aṅgirasaḥ sūnavas te agneḥ pari jajñire ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • virūpāsa: Those who have diverse forms or appearances.
    • id: Here, in this context.
    • ṛṣayas: The sages or seers, those who have attained knowledge.
    • ta: They.
    • id: Here, in this context.
    • gambhīravepasaḥ: Those who are profound and deep in their understanding.
    • te: They.
    • aṅgirasaḥ: The ones who are associated with the divine fire or the Agni.
    • sūnavas: The offspring or progeny.
    • te: They.
    • agneḥ: Of Agni, the fire god.
    • pari: All around or in every direction.
    • jajñire: They were born or manifested.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the diverse forms of sages who possess profound wisdom.
    • It highlights the connection between these sages and Agni, the divine fire, suggesting that their knowledge is a result of divine inspiration.
    • The mention of "sūnavas" indicates that these sages have given rise to knowledge and wisdom that benefits humanity.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern times, we can see this in the way diverse thinkers and innovators contribute to society. For instance, scientists, philosophers, and artists all bring unique perspectives that enrich our understanding of the world.
    • Just as the sages are connected to Agni, many contemporary leaders and thinkers draw inspiration from their cultural and spiritual roots, using that knowledge to guide their actions and decisions.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of diverse perspectives and profound wisdom in shaping our understanding of life.
    • It serves as a reminder that knowledge is not static; it evolves through the contributions of many, much like the sages who were inspired by the divine.
    • In real life, this mantra encourages us to seek wisdom from various sources and to appreciate the richness that different viewpoints bring to our collective knowledge.
