आ रोडसी अपृणा जायमान उता प्र रिख्ता अध नु प्रयज्यो |दिवश चिद अग्ने महिना पृथिव्याः वच्यन्तां ते वह्नयः सप्तजिव्हवः ||
ā rodasī apṛṇā jāyamāna uta pra rikthā adha nu prayajyo | divaś cid agne mahinā pṛthivyā vacyantāṁ te vahnayaḥ saptajihvāḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā rodasī: From the two worlds (heaven and earth)
    • apṛṇā: Unbounded or limitless
    • jāyamāna: Being born or generated
    • uta: And
    • pra rikthā: Well-established or firmly grounded
    • adha: Below or beneath
    • nu: Now or indeed
    • prayajyo: Worthy of worship or reverence
    • divaś: Of the heavens
    • cid: Indeed or truly
    • agne: O Fire (Agni)
    • mahinā: With greatness or power
    • pṛthivyā: Of the earth
    • vacyantāṁ: May they speak or manifest
    • te: Your
    • vahnayaḥ: O flames or fires
    • saptajihvāḥ: With seven tongues
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the divine fire, Agni, representing the connection between the earthly and the celestial realms.
    • The phrase "from the two worlds" signifies the unity of heaven and earth, suggesting that all creation is interconnected.
    • The mention of "seven tongues" symbolizes the multifaceted nature of fire, which can represent various forms of energy and transformation.
    • In real life, this can be likened to the diverse applications of fire, from cooking and warmth to industrial processes and energy generation.
  • Significance:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of Agni as a purifying and transformative force in our lives.
    • It serves as a reminder of the need to respect and harness natural elements for our benefit while maintaining harmony with the environment.
    • In modern contexts, this can be applied to sustainable practices, such as using renewable energy sources and promoting ecological balance.
  • Conclusion:
    • The mantra encapsulates the essence of creation, transformation, and reverence for nature.
    • By acknowledging the power of Agni, we are encouraged to seek balance in our lives and to recognize the interconnectedness of all existence.
    • In daily life, this can inspire us to engage in practices that honor the environment and promote sustainability, ensuring a harmonious relationship with the world around us.
