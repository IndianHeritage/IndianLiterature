सुपर्णा वाचम् अक्रतोपा द्यव्य आखरे कृष्णा इषिरा अनर्तिषुः |न्यङ् न यन्त्य उपरस्य निष्कृतम् पुरू रेतो दधिरे सूर्यश्वितः ||
suparṇā vācam akratopa dyavy ākhare kṛṣṇā iṣirā anartiṣuḥ | nya1ṅ ni yanty uparasya niṣkṛtam purū reto dadhire sūryaśvitaḥ ||
  • Mantra: "suparṇā vācam akratopa dyavy ākhare kṛṣṇā iṣirā anartiṣuḥ | nya1ṅ ni yanty uparasya niṣkṛtam purū reto dadhire sūryaśvitaḥ ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • suparṇā: well-winged or auspicious
    • vācam: speech or voice
    • akratopa: without any hindrance or obstruction
    • dyavy: heavenly or celestial
    • ākharē: in the sky or atmosphere
    • kṛṣṇā: black or dark, often referring to the color of the night or the unknown
    • iṣirā: with arrows or sharpness
    • anartiṣuḥ: in the midst of troubles or difficulties
    • nyaṅ: they go or they move
    • ni yanty: they lead or guide
    • uparasya: of the higher realms or above
    • niṣkṛtam: liberation or freedom
    • purū: many or abundant
    • reto: energy or vitality
    • dadhire: they hold or possess
    • sūryaśvitaḥ: shining like the sun
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra speaks of auspicious voices that are free from obstruction, suggesting the importance of clear and positive communication.
    • The reference to "heavenly" and "in the sky" indicates a connection to higher consciousness or divine guidance.
    • The mention of "darkness" and "arrows" symbolizes the challenges and adversities one may face in life.
    • It emphasizes that despite these challenges, there is a path to liberation and abundance of energy that can be attained.
    • For example, in a real-life scenario, when facing difficulties in a project at work, clear communication and seeking guidance can lead to successful outcomes.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the power of positive communication and the importance of seeking higher guidance in times of trouble.
    • It teaches us that even in the face of adversity, there is a way to achieve liberation and harness our inner energy.
    • In real life, this can be applied in various situations such as personal relationships, professional challenges, or spiritual journeys, reminding us to stay connected to our higher selves and communicate effectively.
