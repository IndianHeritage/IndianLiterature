मन्त्र: सरूपैर आ सु नो गहि संभरितैः संभरिताश्वः | दिवो अमुष्य शासतो दिवं यय दिवावसो ||
sarūpair ā su no gahi sambhṛtaiḥ sambhṛtāśvaḥ | divo amuṣya śāsato divaṁ yaya divāvaso ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sarūpair: with beautiful forms
    • ā: towards
    • su: good or auspicious
    • no: our
    • gahi: may you grasp or hold
    • sambhṛtaiḥ: with abundance or wealth
    • sambhṛtāśvaḥ: well-equipped horses
    • divo: of the heavens
    • amuṣya: of this (or that) world
    • śāsato: ruling or governing
    • divaṁ: the sky or heaven
    • yaya: by which
    • divāvaso: dwelling in the day or daytime
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the divine to bestow upon us the beauty and auspiciousness of life.
    • The phrase "with beautiful forms" suggests a desire for harmony and aesthetic appeal in our surroundings.
    • By asking the divine to "grasp our abundance," it emphasizes the importance of wealth and resources in achieving a fulfilling life.
    • The mention of "well-equipped horses" symbolizes strength, readiness, and the ability to move forward in life.
    • It acknowledges the connection between the earthly realm and the heavens, suggesting that our actions and intentions are guided by a higher power.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be applied to the pursuit of success and prosperity. Just as one seeks to create a beautiful and harmonious workplace, the mantra encourages leaders to foster an environment that attracts talent and resources.
    • In personal life, individuals may reflect on their relationships and surroundings, striving to cultivate beauty and positivity, akin to the "beautiful forms" mentioned in the mantra.
    • In community development, the idea of "well-equipped horses" can be seen in initiatives that empower individuals with skills and resources, enabling them to contribute effectively to society.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of our lives with the divine and the importance of seeking beauty, abundance, and strength.
    • Its significance lies in the encouragement to strive for a harmonious existence, both personally and collectively, while recognizing the support of higher powers in our endeavors.
    • In real-life scenarios, it can inspire individuals and communities to pursue goals that enhance their quality of life, promote well-being, and foster a sense of unity with the universe.
