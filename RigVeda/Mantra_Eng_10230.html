उप ते गा इवाकरं वृणीष्व दूहितर दिवः |रात्रि स्तोमं न जिग्युषे ||
upa te gā ivākaraṁ vṛṇīṣva duhitar divaḥ | rātri stomaṁ na jigyuṣe ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • upa te: "to you" or "towards you"
    • gā: "song" or "hymn"
    • ivākaraṁ: "like a creator" or "as if creating"
    • vṛṇīṣva: "choose" or "select"
    • duhitar: "daughter"
    • divaḥ: "of the heavens" or "of the sky"
    • rātri: "night"
    • stomaṁ: "praise" or "offering"
    • na: "not"
    • jigyuṣe: "to be defeated" or "to be overcome"
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra can be seen as a prayer or invocation directed towards a divine entity, asking for guidance and support.
    • The phrase "upa te gā ivākaraṁ" suggests a call to the divine to inspire creativity and harmony, akin to how a song can uplift spirits.
    • By asking to "choose" a "daughter of the heavens," it implies a desire for divine blessings and protection, particularly in the context of familial or societal relationships.
    • The mention of "rātri stomaṁ" indicates a reverence for the night, which can symbolize rest, reflection, and the nurturing aspects of life.
    • The concluding part "na jigyuṣe" emphasizes a plea for strength and resilience, seeking to overcome challenges and adversities.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a family setting, this mantra can be recited during a ceremony to bless a daughter or a young woman, invoking divine protection and guidance as she embarks on new phases of life.
    • In a community gathering, it can serve as a reminder to support one another, fostering creativity and collaboration, much like how a song brings people together.
    • During challenging times, individuals may reflect on this mantra to find inner strength and resilience, reminding themselves that they can overcome obstacles with divine support.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking divine guidance and support in various aspects of life, particularly in relationships and personal growth.
    • Its significance lies in the reminder of the interconnectedness of creativity, familial bonds, and the strength to face challenges.
    • In real-life scenarios, it encourages individuals to embrace their creative potential, nurture relationships, and cultivate resilience, making it a timeless invocation for personal and communal harmony.
