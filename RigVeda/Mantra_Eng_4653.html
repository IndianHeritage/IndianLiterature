त्वं रथम प्र भरो योधम् ऋष्वम् आवो युध्यन्तं वृषभं दशद्युम् |त्वं तुग्रं वेतसवे सचाहन त्वं तुजिं गृणन्तम् इन्द्र तूतोः ||
tvaṁ ratham pra bharo yodham ṛṣvam āvo yudhyantaṁ vṛṣabhaṁ daśadyum | tvaṁ tugraṁ vetasave sacāhan tvaṁ tujiṁ gṛṇantam indra tūtoḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • tvaṁ: You
    • ratham: Chariot
    • pra: Forward
    • bharo: Carry
    • yodham: Warrior
    • ṛṣvam: Strong
    • āvo: We invoke
    • yudhyantaṁ: Engaged in battle
    • vṛṣabhaṁ: The bull (symbol of strength)
    • daśadyum: Tenfold (or powerful)
    • tugraṁ: Powerful
    • vetasave: Swift
    • sacāhan: With strength
    • tujiṁ: Victorious
    • gṛṇantam: Praising
    • indra: Indra (the king of gods)
    • tūtoḥ: For your protection
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a call to the divine, specifically to Indra, the god of thunder and rain, who is often associated with strength and victory in battle.
    • The invocation of the chariot symbolizes the journey of life, where one must be prepared to face challenges and adversities.
    • The mention of the warrior and the bull signifies the strength and courage needed to overcome obstacles.
    • By calling upon Indra, the mantra emphasizes the importance of divine support in achieving success and protection in difficult times.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a competitive environment, such as a sports event, athletes often invoke their inner strength and seek motivation from their coaches or mentors, similar to how this mantra seeks divine support.
    • During challenging times, individuals may turn to spiritual practices or prayers, asking for guidance and strength to navigate through their struggles, akin to invoking Indra for protection and victory.
    • In leadership roles, a leader may embody the qualities of a warrior, leading their team with strength and courage, while also seeking the support of their community or higher powers for success.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the strength and courage required to face life's battles, while also highlighting the importance of seeking divine support.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal challenges to professional endeavors, where invoking strength and protection can lead to success.
    • Ultimately, it teaches us that while we must strive for our goals, we can also seek help from higher powers or the community around us to achieve victory.
