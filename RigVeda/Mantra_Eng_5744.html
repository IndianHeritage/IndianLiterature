उप त्या वह्नी गमतो विषं नो रक्षोहनā संभरता वीलुपाणि |सम अन्धांस्य अगमत मत्सराणि मा नो मर्दिष्टम् आगतं शिवेन ||
upa tyā vahnī gamato viśaṁ no rakṣohaṇā sambhṛtā vīḻupāṇī | sam andhāṁsy agmata matsarāṇi mā no mardhiṣṭam ā gataṁ śivena ||
  • upa tyā vahnī gamato viśaṁ: This phrase can be translated as "May the fire (vahnī) lead us to the community (viśaṁ)." Here, fire symbolizes knowledge, purity, and transformation. In real life, this can be likened to the pursuit of education and enlightenment that guides individuals towards a collective understanding and harmony.
  • no rakṣohaṇā sambhṛtā vīḻupāṇī: This translates to "May we be protected from harm (rakṣohaṇā) by the well-prepared (sambhṛtā) hands (vīḻupāṇī)." This emphasizes the importance of being safeguarded by those who are equipped and ready to protect. In a modern context, this can relate to the role of community leaders, law enforcement, or even family members who provide support and security.
  • sam andhāṁsy agmata matsarāṇi: This means "May we not be led into darkness (andhāṁsy) and may jealousy (matsarāṇi) not harm us." This highlights the need for guidance away from ignorance and negativity. In everyday life, this can be seen in the importance of mentorship and positive relationships that foster growth and understanding, steering clear of envy and competition.
  • mā no mardhiṣṭam ā gataṁ śivena: This translates to "May we not be struck down (mardhiṣṭam) by what has come (ā gataṁ) through auspiciousness (śivena)." This suggests a plea for protection from adverse circumstances that may arise, even those that seem fortunate. In practical terms, this can be related to the idea of being cautious about opportunities that may seem beneficial but could lead to negative outcomes.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a profound message about the importance of guidance, protection, and the pursuit of knowledge. It serves as a reminder to seek enlightenment and community support while being wary of negativity and potential pitfalls. In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as in educational settings where students seek knowledge, in community gatherings where support systems are built, and in personal relationships where mentorship and positive influences are crucial. Ultimately, it encourages individuals to strive for a harmonious existence, safeguarded by wisdom and collective strength.
