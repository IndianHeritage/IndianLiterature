नितिग्मम् अभ्य अंशुं सीदद धोताम मनाव अधि |जुषाणो अस्य सख्यम् ||
ni tigmam abhy a1ṁśuṁ sīdad dhotā manāv adhi | juṣāṇo asya sakhyam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ni: not
    • tigmam: sharp or intense
    • abhy: towards
    • a1ṁśuṁ: the light or radiance
    • sīdad: to sit or to settle
    • dhotā: the one who purifies or cleanses
    • manāv: the mind or intellect
    • adhi: above or over
    • juṣāṇo: enjoying or cherishing
    • asya: of this
    • sakhyam: friendship or companionship
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the importance of not allowing intense or sharp thoughts to dominate the mind.
    • It emphasizes the need for purification of the mind, suggesting that one should strive for clarity and peace.
    • The phrase "enjoying this friendship" indicates the value of companionship and support in achieving mental tranquility.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a stressful work environment, individuals can often feel overwhelmed by sharp, intense thoughts. Practicing mindfulness and meditation can help in settling these thoughts, leading to a more peaceful state of mind.
    • In personal relationships, fostering a supportive environment where friends can share their thoughts and feelings can lead to better mental health and emotional stability.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of mental clarity and the importance of companionship in achieving a peaceful mind.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, such as in workplaces, personal relationships, and during times of stress, reminding us to seek purification of thoughts and cherish the bonds we share with others.
