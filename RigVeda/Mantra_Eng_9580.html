सनामाना चिद्ध्वसयो न्‍य अस्मा अवाहन् इन्द्र उषसो यथानः |ऋष्वैर अगच्छः सखिभिर निकामैः साकं प्रतिष्ठा हृद्या जगन्थ ||
sanāmānā cid dhvasayo ny asmā avāhann indra uṣaso yathānaḥ | ṛṣvair agacchaḥ sakhibhir nikāmaiḥ sākam pratiṣṭhā hṛdyā jaghantha ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sanāmānā: With auspiciousness or well-being
    • cid: Consciousness or awareness
    • dhvasayo: To remove or dispel
    • ny: Not or without
    • asmā: Us or our
    • avāhann: To bring or lead
    • indra: The deity Indra, often associated with strength and power
    • uṣaso: Dawn or light
    • yathānaḥ: As it is or in the same way
    • ṛṣvair: By the sages or seers
    • agacchaḥ: To go or to move
    • sakhibhir: With friends or companions
    • nikāmaiḥ: With offerings or gifts
    • sākam: Together or in unity
    • pratiṣṭhā: Establishment or foundation
    • hṛdyā: With heart or affection
    • jaghantha: To be praised or honored
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the divine presence of Indra, asking for the removal of obstacles and the dispelling of negativity.
    • It emphasizes the importance of consciousness and awareness in achieving well-being.
    • The reference to dawn symbolizes new beginnings, hope, and enlightenment.
    • By mentioning sages and friends, it highlights the significance of community and collective effort in spiritual and personal growth.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a challenging situation, such as a difficult project at work, invoking the spirit of this mantra can remind individuals to seek support from colleagues and work together towards a common goal.
    • During personal struggles, such as health issues, this mantra can serve as a reminder to maintain a positive mindset and seek help from loved ones, fostering a sense of community.
    • In educational settings, students can use the essence of this mantra to encourage collaboration and mutual support, leading to better outcomes.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for unity, awareness, and the dispelling of negativity through collective effort.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, such as teamwork, personal challenges, and community building.
    • By embodying the principles of this mantra, individuals can foster a supportive environment that promotes well-being and success.
