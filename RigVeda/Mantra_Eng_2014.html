त्वाम् अग्नि दाम आ विश्वतिं विश्वस त्वां राजानं सुविदत्रं ऋञ्जते |त्वं विश्वानि स्वनीक पत्यासे त्वं सहस्राणि शतां दश प्रतिः ||
tvām agne dama ā viśpatiṁ viśas tvāṁ rājānaṁ suvidatram ṛñjate | tvaṁ viśvāni svanīka patyase tvaṁ sahasrāṇi śatā daśa prati ||
  • tvām agne - "O Agni,"
  • dama ā viśpatiṁ - "bring us to the abode of the Lord,"
  • viśas tvāṁ rājānaṁ - "we seek your guidance, O King,"
  • suvidatram ṛñjate - "who is well-versed in the ways of righteousness,"
  • tvaṁ viśvāni svanīka patyase - "you are the master of all beings,"
  • tvaṁ sahasrāṇi - "you are the one who possesses thousands,"
  • śatā daśa prati - "and hundreds and tens."

This mantra from the Rigveda is a prayer to Agni, the fire god, who symbolizes purity, transformation, and the divine presence in rituals. The invocation of Agni is significant as he acts as a mediator between humans and the divine, facilitating communication and the offering of sacrifices.


In real life, this mantra can be interpreted as a call for guidance and support in our endeavors. For instance, when embarking on a new project or facing challenges, one might seek the wisdom and clarity that Agni represents. The phrase "bring us to the abode of the Lord" can be seen as a desire for spiritual growth and enlightenment, akin to seeking mentorship or guidance from a wise leader in a professional setting.


The significance of this mantra lies in its emphasis on righteousness and the pursuit of knowledge. In today's world, where ethical dilemmas and moral choices are prevalent, invoking the qualities of Agni can inspire individuals to act with integrity and wisdom. The reference to "thousands" and "hundreds" can symbolize the abundance of opportunities and resources available to us when we align ourselves with higher principles.


In conclusion, this mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of seeking guidance, acting righteously, and recognizing the abundance in our lives. It encourages us to connect with the divine and to strive for excellence in our personal and professional journeys.
