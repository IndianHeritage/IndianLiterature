स रेवाम् इव विश्वपितर दैव्यः केतुः श्रिणोतु नः |उक्तैः अग्निः बृहद्भानुः ||
sa revām̐ iva viśpatir daivyaḥ ketuḥ śṛṇotu naḥ | ukthair agnir bṛhadbhānuḥ ||
  • sa revām̐ iva viśpatir daivyaḥ ketuḥ śṛṇotu naḥ
    • sa - This word translates to "he" or "that," indicating a divine presence or entity.
    • revām̐ - This term can be interpreted as "radiant" or "brilliant," often associated with light and positivity.
    • iva - Meaning "like" or "as," it draws a comparison to something else.
    • viśpatir - This refers to a "lord of the universe" or "cosmic ruler," suggesting a higher power overseeing all.
    • daivyaḥ - Meaning "divine," it emphasizes the celestial or spiritual nature of the subject.
    • ketuḥ - This word can mean "banner" or "sign," often symbolizing victory or guidance.
    • śṛṇotu - Translates to "may he hear," indicating a plea for attention or acknowledgment from the divine.
    • naḥ - Meaning "us," it personalizes the mantra, making it a collective invocation.
  • ukthair agnir bṛhadbhānuḥ
    • ukthair - This word means "with the words" or "through speech," suggesting the power of verbal invocation.
    • agnir - Refers to "fire," which in many cultures symbolizes purification, transformation, and energy.
    • bṛhadbhānuḥ - This term translates to "the great sun" or "the vast light," representing enlightenment and knowledge.

Conclusion:

This mantra invokes the divine presence, asking for guidance and enlightenment. It emphasizes the importance of communication with the divine through words and the transformative power of fire and light. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios such as:

  • Seeking clarity and guidance in difficult situations, much like asking for help from a mentor or a wise friend.
  • Using the power of positive affirmations and prayers to manifest one's desires and intentions.
  • Recognizing the importance of community and collective efforts in achieving common goals, akin to how a team works together towards success.

Overall, the mantra serves as a reminder of the divine support available to us and the significance of our intentions and words in shaping our reality.
