व्य अनिनस्य धनिनः प्रहोषे चिद् अररुषः |कदाचित् प्रजिगतो अदेवयोः ||
vy aninasya dhaninaḥ prahoṣe cid araruṣaḥ | kadā cana prajigato adevayoḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • vy: a prefix indicating 'through' or 'by means of'
    • aninasya: of the one who is not obstructed or hindered
    • dhaninaḥ: of the wealthy or prosperous
    • prahoṣe: in the act of speaking or proclaiming
    • cid: consciousness or awareness
    • araruṣaḥ: not angry or not disturbed
    • kadā: when
    • cana: at any time or ever
    • prajigato: having been born or having come into being
    • adevayoḥ: of the divine or spiritual beings
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the nature of wealth and prosperity.
    • It suggests that true wealth is not just material but also involves a state of consciousness that is free from anger and disturbance.
    • The phrase "when the wealthy speak" implies that their words carry weight and significance, especially when they are in a state of calm and awareness.
    • It emphasizes the importance of a peaceful mind in achieving and maintaining prosperity.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In business, leaders who approach challenges with a calm demeanor and clear consciousness are often more successful than those who react with anger or frustration.
    • Wealthy individuals who practice mindfulness and emotional intelligence tend to have better relationships and a more fulfilling life.
    • In personal finance, maintaining a peaceful mindset can lead to better decision-making, such as investing wisely rather than impulsively.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of a balanced mind in the pursuit of wealth and prosperity.
    • It teaches that true richness comes from a state of awareness and tranquility, rather than mere accumulation of material possessions.
    • In real life, applying this wisdom can lead to more meaningful success, better relationships, and a deeper sense of fulfillment.
