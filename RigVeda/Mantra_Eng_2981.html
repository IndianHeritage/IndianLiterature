मन्त्र: पदे इव निहिते दसमे अन्तस् तयोः अन्यद् गुह्यं आविर्भूतम् अन्यत् | सदृचीनां पथ्या सा विषूचि महद् देवानां असुरत्वम् एकम् ||
pade iva nihite dasme antas tayor anyad guhyam āvir anyat | sadhrīcīnā pathyā3 sā viṣūcī mahad devānām asuratvam ekam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • pade: in the step or path
    • iva: like
    • nihite: placed or established
    • dasme: in the tenth
    • antas: within
    • tayor: of those two
    • anyad: other
    • guhyam: secret or hidden
    • āvir: manifest or revealed
    • anyat: another
    • sadhrīcīnā: in harmony or similarity
    • pathyā: path or way
    • sā: that
    • viṣūcī: the great or vast
    • mahad: great
    • devānām: of the gods
    • asuratvam: the quality of being divine or celestial
    • ekam: one
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the interconnectedness of paths and the hidden truths that lie within them.
    • The phrase "pade iva nihite" suggests that every step we take is significant and has a purpose, much like the steps in a journey.
    • The mention of "guhyam āvir" indicates that there are deeper, often hidden meanings or truths that can be revealed through exploration and understanding.
    • In real life, this can be likened to personal growth; as we navigate through life, we often uncover hidden strengths and insights that guide us.
    • The idea of "sadhrīcīnā pathyā" emphasizes the importance of finding a path that resonates with our true selves, leading to harmony and fulfillment.
    • For example, in career choices, individuals often find that pursuing a path aligned with their passions leads to greater satisfaction and success.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of understanding the paths we choose and the hidden truths that can be revealed through introspection and exploration.
    • It encourages individuals to seek harmony in their choices and to be open to discovering deeper meanings in their experiences.
    • In a broader context, it can be applied to various aspects of life, including personal development, relationships, and spiritual journeys, reminding us that every step we take is part of a greater whole.
