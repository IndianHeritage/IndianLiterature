प्र सū महे सुशरणाया मेधाṁ गिरं भरे नव्यसीṁ जायमानाम् |य आहनā दुधितुर वक्षाणासु रूपा मिनानो अकृणोद इदं नः ||
pra sū mahe suśaraṇāya medhāṁ giram bhare navyasīṁ jāyamānām | ya āhanā duhitur vakṣaṇāsu rūpā mināno akṛṇod idaṁ naḥ ||
  • pra sū mahe suśaraṇāya medhāṁ: This phrase can be translated as "We invoke the great intellect and wisdom." Here, "pra sū" signifies the act of invoking or calling forth, while "mahe" means great, and "medhāṁ" refers to intellect or wisdom. In real life, this can be seen as a call for clarity and understanding in decision-making processes, whether in personal life or in professional settings.
  • giram bhare navyasīṁ jāyamānām: This translates to "Let us fill our speech with new and fresh ideas." "Giram" refers to speech or words, "bhare" means to fill, and "navyasīṁ" indicates something new or fresh. This can be applied in contexts such as brainstorming sessions in workplaces, where innovative ideas are encouraged to foster creativity and progress.
  • ya āhanā duhitur vakṣaṇāsu: This means "That which is born from the nurturing of the mind." "Ya" means that, "āhanā" refers to birth or creation, "duhitur" relates to nurturing, and "vakṣaṇāsu" pertains to the mind or intellect. In everyday life, this can be related to the importance of nurturing our thoughts and ideas, which can lead to personal growth and development.
  • rūpā mināno akṛṇod idaṁ naḥ: This translates to "May it manifest in beautiful forms for us." "Rūpā" means form or shape, "mināno" refers to manifestation, "akṛṇod" means to create or bring forth, and "idaṁ naḥ" means for us. This can be seen in the context of artistic expression, where nurturing ideas can lead to beautiful creations, whether in art, literature, or any other form of expression.

  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of invoking wisdom, nurturing fresh ideas, and manifesting them into beautiful forms. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios such as education, creativity, and personal development. By focusing on these aspects, individuals and communities can foster an environment of growth, innovation, and beauty, ultimately leading to a more enlightened and harmonious existence.
