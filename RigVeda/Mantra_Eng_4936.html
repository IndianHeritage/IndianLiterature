अभि नो नार्यं वसु वीरम् प्रयतदक्षिणम् |वामं गृहपतिं नय ||
abhi no naryaṁ vasu vīram prayatadakṣiṇam | vāmaṁ gṛhapatiṁ naya ||
  • Mantra: "abhi no naryaṁ vasu vīram prayatadakṣiṇam | vāmaṁ gṛhapatiṁ naya ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • abhi: towards, for
    • no: us, our
    • naryaṁ: noble, virtuous
    • vasu: wealth, prosperity
    • vīram: hero, strength
    • prayatadakṣiṇam: with effort, skillfully
    • vāmaṁ: the left, auspicious
    • gṛhapatiṁ: lord of the house, master
    • naya: lead, guide
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer for guidance and support towards noble and virtuous actions.
    • It emphasizes the importance of wealth and strength that is achieved through skillful efforts.
    • The mention of "gṛhapatiṁ" signifies the role of a leader or head of a household, highlighting the need for wise leadership.
    • The invocation of "vāmaṁ" suggests seeking auspiciousness and positivity in our endeavors.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, a manager may embody the qualities of "gṛhapatiṁ" by leading their team with integrity and wisdom, ensuring that their efforts are directed towards achieving collective goals.
    • In personal life, individuals may strive to embody "naryaṁ" by engaging in community service, thereby contributing to the prosperity and well-being of society.
    • During challenging times, people can draw strength from "vīram" by facing difficulties with courage and resilience, thus inspiring others around them.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the virtues of leadership, strength, and the pursuit of prosperity through noble actions.
    • Its significance lies in its applicability to various aspects of life, including personal development, community engagement, and professional leadership.
    • By embodying the principles outlined in this mantra, individuals can contribute positively to their families, workplaces, and society at large.
