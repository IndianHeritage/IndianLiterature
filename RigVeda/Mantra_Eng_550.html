मन्त्र: दिवस कण्वास इन्दवो वसु सिन्धूनाम् पदे |स्वं ववृं कुहा धित्सथः ||
divas kaṇvāsa indavo vasu sindhūnām pade | svaṁ vavriṁ kuha dhitsathaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • divas: of the sky or heaven
    • kaṇvāsa: of the Kanva family or followers
    • indavo: of the Indus or the divine beings
    • vasu: wealth or prosperity
    • sindhūnām: of the rivers
    • pade: in the path or way
    • svaṁ: our own
    • vavriṁ: we have chosen
    • kuha: where
    • dhitsathaḥ: you desire or wish
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the divine beings associated with the sky and the rivers, particularly the Indus, and emphasizes the importance of wealth and prosperity.
    • The phrase "svaṁ vavriṁ" indicates a choice or selection made by the speaker, suggesting a conscious decision to pursue a certain path or goal.
    • The question "kuha dhitsathaḥ" implies a search for direction or purpose, asking where one’s desires and aspirations lie.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be interpreted as a call to seek prosperity and success in one's endeavors. For instance, a startup may reflect on this mantra when deciding on its mission and vision, ensuring that it aligns with the wealth of opportunities available in the market.
    • In personal life, individuals may use this mantra as a reminder to reflect on their aspirations and the paths they choose to achieve their goals, whether in education, career, or personal development.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its encouragement to seek divine guidance in the pursuit of prosperity and to be mindful of the paths we choose in life.
    • It serves as a reminder to align our desires with our actions, ensuring that we are consciously choosing paths that lead to fulfillment and success.
    • In contemporary life, this mantra can be applicable in various scenarios, such as setting personal goals, making career choices, or even in community planning, where the focus is on collective prosperity and well-being.
