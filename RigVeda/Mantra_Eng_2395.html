ग्रावाणेव तदिदर्थं जरेथे गृध्रेवं वृक्षं निधिमन्तमच्छ || ब्रह्माणेव विदथ उक्तशासादूतिव हव्यां जन्यां पुरुत्रा ||
grāvāṇeva tad id arthaṁ jarethe gṛdhreva vṛkṣaṁ nidhimantam accha | brahmāṇeva vidatha ukthaśāsā dūteva havyā janyā purutrā ||
  • Translation: The mantra can be translated as: "Like a bird that seeks its nest, like a vulture that finds a tree with treasure, like a Brahman who knows the sacred verses, may the offerings be fruitful and may they be received from all directions."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • grāvāṇeva: Like a bird or a creature that seeks.
    • tad id arthaṁ: That which is the purpose or meaning.
    • jarethe: To seek or to search.
    • gṛdhreva: Like a vulture.
    • vṛkṣaṁ: A tree.
    • nidhimantam: One that has treasure or wealth.
    • accha: To receive or to accept.
    • brahmāṇeva: Like a Brahman or a learned person.
    • vidatha: Knowledge or wisdom.
    • ukthaśāsā: Sacred verses or hymns.
    • dūteva: Like a messenger or a deity.
    • havyā: Offerings or sacrifices.
    • janyā: Born or generated.
    • purutrā: From all directions.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • Just as a bird instinctively searches for its nest, individuals often seek their purpose or place in life, whether it be in their careers, relationships, or personal growth.
    • The vulture, known for its keen eyesight, symbolizes the ability to find opportunities or resources that others may overlook, akin to entrepreneurs who identify market gaps.
    • A learned person, like a Brahman, represents the pursuit of knowledge and wisdom, which is essential in making informed decisions in various aspects of life, such as education and career choices.
    • Offerings made in rituals can be seen as acts of gratitude and acknowledgment of the forces that support us, similar to how people express appreciation in their communities or workplaces.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its emphasis on the pursuit of purpose, the recognition of opportunities, and the importance of knowledge. In real life, it encourages individuals to seek their goals with determination, to be aware of the resources available to them, and to value the wisdom gained through experience. This mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as personal development, professional endeavors, and community engagement, reminding us to be proactive in our pursuits and to appreciate the support we receive from our surroundings.
