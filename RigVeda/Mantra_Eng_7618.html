त्वां शुष्मिन् पुरुहूत वाजयन्तमुप ब्रुवे शतक्रतो |स नो रास्व सुवीर्यम् ||
tvāṁ śuṣmin puruhūta vājayantam upa bruve śatakrato | sa no rāsva suvīryam ||
  • tvāṁ - This word translates to "you" and signifies a direct address to a divine entity or a higher power.
  • śuṣmin - This term means "the one who is full of energy" or "the one who is vibrant." It indicates a state of vitality and strength.
  • puruhūta - This word can be interpreted as "the one who is invoked frequently" or "the one who is called upon." It reflects the idea of a deity who is often worshipped and revered.
  • vājayantam - This term means "the one who bestows strength" or "the one who grants victory." It emphasizes the power and support that the divine provides to devotees.
  • upa bruve - This phrase translates to "I speak to you" or "I call upon you." It signifies a personal connection and communication with the divine.
  • śatakrato - This word refers to "the one with a hundred powers" or "the one who has achieved great feats." It highlights the omnipotence and capability of the divine being addressed.
  • sa no - This phrase means "may he" or "let him." It is a request or invocation for blessings.
  • rāsva - This term translates to "short" or "swift," indicating a desire for quick results or immediate blessings.
  • suvīryam - This word means "good strength" or "excellent power." It reflects the aspiration for strength and vitality in life.

The mantra can be understood as a heartfelt invocation to a divine power, asking for strength, vitality, and support in achieving one's goals. The speaker acknowledges the divine's energy and capability, expressing a desire for blessings that lead to success and well-being.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as:

  • Personal Challenges: When facing difficulties, individuals can recite this mantra to seek strength and resilience, much like athletes who invoke their inner strength before a competition.
  • Professional Aspirations: In the workplace, professionals can use this mantra to inspire confidence and determination, similar to how leaders motivate their teams to achieve collective goals.
  • Health and Wellness: Those seeking to improve their physical health can recite this mantra to invoke energy and vitality, akin to how individuals engage in positive affirmations during fitness routines.

In conclusion, this mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the connection between the divine and human aspirations. It emphasizes the importance of seeking strength and support from a higher power in various aspects of life, encouraging individuals to strive for excellence and resilience in their endeavors.
