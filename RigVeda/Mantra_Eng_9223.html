क्व स्विद अद्य कटमास्व अश्विनां विक्षु दश्रां मादयेत शुभस पती |का ईं नि येमे कटमस्य जग्मतुर विप्रस्य वा यजमानस्य वा गृहम् ||
kva svid adya katamāsv aśvinā vikṣu dasrā mādayete śubhas patī | ka īṁ ni yeme katamasya jagmatur viprasya vā yajamānasya vā gṛham ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • kva svid: Where indeed
    • adya: Today
    • katamāsv: Which of the two
    • aśvinā: O Ashvins (the twin deities)
    • vikṣu: In the midst of the people
    • dasrā: With a clear vision
    • mādayete: May they delight
    • śubhas patī: O Lord of auspiciousness
    • ka: Who
    • īṁ: May I
    • ni: To
    • yeme: Who are they
    • katamasya: Which of them
    • jagmatur: Have gone
    • viprasya: Of the wise
    • vā: Or
    • yajamānasya: Of the worshipper
    • vā: Or
    • gṛham: Home
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a call to the Ashvins, the divine twin horsemen in Vedic mythology, who are often associated with health, medicine, and the dawn.
    • The speaker is inquiring about the presence of the Ashvins today, seeking their blessings and auspiciousness in the midst of the community.
    • It reflects a desire for clarity and vision, as indicated by the term "dasrā," which suggests a longing for insight and understanding in one's life.
    • The mention of "viprasya" and "yajamānasya" indicates a connection to the wise and the worshippers, emphasizing the importance of community and shared spiritual practices.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern life, this mantra can be applied during significant events such as weddings or community gatherings, where blessings and auspiciousness are sought for the collective well-being.
    • Healthcare practitioners may invoke the Ashvins for guidance and healing, reflecting the mantra's connection to health and clarity.
    • In educational settings, students may recite this mantra before exams or important presentations, seeking the clarity and insight represented by the Ashvins.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of seeking divine guidance and auspiciousness in our daily lives.
    • It highlights the interconnectedness of individuals within a community and the shared pursuit of wisdom and clarity.
    • By invoking the Ashvins, we remind ourselves of the need for support and insight in our endeavors, whether personal or communal.
