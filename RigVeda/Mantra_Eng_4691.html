स मातरā सूर्येण कवीनाम आवासयद रुजद अद्रिं गृणानः |स्वाधीभिर ऋक्वभिर वावशान उद उष्रियाणाम असृजन निदानम् ||
sa mātarā sūryeṇā kavīnām avāsayad rujad adriṁ gṛṇānaḥ | svādhībhir ṛkvabhir vāvaśāna ud usriyāṇām asṛjan nidānam ||
  • sa mātarā sūryeṇā: This phrase can be translated as "the mother (or source) of the sun." In many cultures, the sun is often personified as a nurturing figure, providing light and life. In real life, we can relate this to the sun's essential role in agriculture, where sunlight is crucial for the growth of crops.
  • kavīnām avāsayad: This means "the one who inspires the poets." Here, the term "kavi" refers to poets or seers, indicating a source of inspiration. In modern life, this can be seen in how artists, writers, and musicians draw inspiration from nature, experiences, and emotions to create their works.
  • rujad adriṁ gṛṇānaḥ: This translates to "who removes the pain from the mountains." Mountains often symbolize challenges or obstacles in life. The idea of removing pain can be related to overcoming difficulties, such as personal struggles or societal issues, through perseverance and support from others.
  • svādhībhir ṛkvabhir vāvaśāna: This phrase means "with the knowledge of the wise." It emphasizes the importance of wisdom and knowledge in guiding actions. In real life, this can be applied in educational settings where learning from experienced individuals helps shape the future of students and communities.
  • ud usriyāṇām asṛjan nidānam: This translates to "who creates the foundation for the seekers." This highlights the idea of providing a base or support for those who are searching for truth or knowledge. In contemporary society, this can be seen in mentorship programs, where experienced individuals guide newcomers in their respective fields.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the interconnectedness of nature, knowledge, and human experience. It signifies the importance of nurturing sources of inspiration, overcoming challenges, and the role of wisdom in guiding individuals. In real life, these themes are applicable in various scenarios, such as education, personal development, and community support. By recognizing the significance of these elements, individuals can foster a more harmonious and enlightened society.
