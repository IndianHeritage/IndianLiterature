अपादित उदु नश्चित्रतमो महीम् भरषद द्युमतिम् इन्द्रहूतिम् |पण्यसीं धीतिं दैवस्य यामञ्ज जनस्य रातिं वनते सुदानुः ||
apād ita ud u naś citratamo mahīm bharṣad dyumatīm indrahūtim | panyasīṁ dhītiṁ daivyasya yāmañ janasya rātiṁ vanate sudānuḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • apād: from the feet
    • ita: here
    • ud: up
    • u: and
    • naś: of us
    • citratamo: most colorful or diverse
    • mahīm: the earth or the great expanse
    • bharṣad: may it be filled or nourished
    • dyumatīm: shining or radiant
    • indrahūtim: the offering to Indra
    • panyasīṁ: the wealth or abundance
    • dhītiṁ: intelligence or wisdom
    • daivyasya: divine
    • yāmañ: the one who brings
    • janasya: of the people
    • rātiṁ: joy or happiness
    • vanate: may it flourish
    • sudānuḥ: the one who is well-nourished or prosperous
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the divine forces to bless the earth and its inhabitants.
    • It emphasizes the importance of abundance, wisdom, and joy in life.
    • By calling upon Indra, the deity associated with rain and thunder, it seeks to ensure prosperity and nourishment for all.
    • The mantra suggests that when the earth is filled with diversity and radiance, it leads to the flourishing of society.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In agriculture, this mantra can be related to the importance of rain and fertile land for a good harvest.
    • In personal life, it can be seen as a call for wisdom and joy in relationships, encouraging individuals to seek happiness and prosperity.
    • In community settings, it highlights the need for collective well-being and the flourishing of society through shared resources and knowledge.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the interconnectedness of nature, wisdom, and human joy.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from agriculture to personal relationships, emphasizing the need for abundance and prosperity.
    • By invoking divine blessings, it encourages individuals and communities to strive for a harmonious and flourishing existence.
