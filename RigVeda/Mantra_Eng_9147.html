स्त्रियं दृष्ट्वा य किटवं तातापान्येषां जायाṁ सुखृतं च योनिṁ |पूर्वाह्णे अश्वान युयुजे हि बभ्रूण सो अग्नेर अन्ते वृषलः पपाद ||
striyaṁ dṛṣṭvāya kitavaṁ tatāpānyeṣāṁ jāyāṁ sukṛtaṁ ca yonim | pūrvāhṇe aśvān yuyuje hi babhrūn so agner ante vṛṣalaḥ papāda ||
  • striyaṁ dṛṣṭvāya: This phrase translates to "seeing the woman." It emphasizes the importance of recognizing and respecting women in society. In real life, this can be related to gender equality and the empowerment of women in various fields, such as education and employment.
  • kitavaṁ tatāpānyeṣāṁ: "The deceitful one, who is not to be trusted." This part warns against those who may have ulterior motives or who are dishonest. In everyday life, this can be seen in the context of relationships, business dealings, or politics, where trust is crucial.
  • jāyāṁ sukṛtaṁ ca yonim: "The wife, and the good deeds associated with her." This highlights the significance of a virtuous partner and the positive actions that stem from a good relationship. In modern contexts, this can relate to the importance of supportive partnerships in personal and professional growth.
  • pūrvāhṇe aśvān yuyuje hi babhrūn: "In the early morning, they harness the horses." This symbolizes the beginning of a new day and the preparation for the tasks ahead. It can be likened to the importance of planning and setting goals at the start of any endeavor, whether it be in business, education, or personal life.
  • so agner ante vṛṣalaḥ papāda: "So, at the end of the fire, the lowly one fell." This can be interpreted as a caution against those who do not respect the sacredness of life and relationships. In real life, it serves as a reminder that dishonesty and deceit can lead to downfall, while integrity and respect lead to success.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda emphasizes the importance of respect for women, the dangers of deceit, the value of virtuous partnerships, the significance of preparation and planning, and the consequences of dishonesty. In today's world, these teachings are applicable in various scenarios, such as promoting gender equality, fostering trustworthy relationships, and encouraging ethical behavior in personal and professional settings. By embodying these principles, individuals can contribute to a more harmonious and respectful society.
