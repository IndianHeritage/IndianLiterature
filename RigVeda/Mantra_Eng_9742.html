यत्रेदानीं पश्यसि जातवेदस् तिष्ठन्तम् अग्न उता वा चरन्तम् |यद वान्तरिक्षे पथिभिः पतन्तं तं अस्तां विद्ध्य शर्वा शिषाणः ||
yatredānīm paśyasi jātavedas tiṣṭhantam agna uta vā carantam | yad vāntarikṣe pathibhiḥ patantaṁ tam astā vidhya śarvā śiśānaḥ ||
  • Mantra: "yatredānīm paśyasi jātavedas tiṣṭhantam agna uta vā carantam | yad vāntarikṣe pathibhiḥ patantaṁ tam astā vidhya śarvā śiśānaḥ ||"
  • Translation: "Wherever you see, O Jātavedas (the one who knows all births), standing or moving, O Agni (the divine fire), and wherever you see him flying in the sky with the paths, may you know him, O Śarvā (the all-pervading one), as the one who is everywhere."

  • Word Breakdown:
    • yatredānīm: Wherever you see
    • paśyasi: You see
    • jātavedas: The one who knows all births
    • tiṣṭhantam: Standing
    • agna: O Agni, the divine fire
    • uta: Or
    • vā: And
    • carantam: Moving
    • yad: Wherever
    • vāntarikṣe: In the sky
    • pathibhiḥ: With the paths
    • patantaṁ: Flying
    • tam: Him
    • astā: May you know
    • vidhya: Know
    • śarvā: The all-pervading one
    • śiśānaḥ: As the one who is everywhere

  • Significance:
    • This mantra emphasizes the omnipresence of Agni, representing the divine fire that symbolizes knowledge, transformation, and purity.
    • It highlights the importance of awareness and perception in recognizing the divine in all aspects of life, whether stationary or in motion.
    • Agni is not just a physical fire but also a metaphor for the inner light of consciousness and wisdom.

  • Real-Life Application:
    • In daily life, this mantra encourages individuals to be mindful and observant of their surroundings, recognizing the divine presence in nature, relationships, and experiences.
    • For example, when one witnesses a beautiful sunset or a moment of kindness, it serves as a reminder of the divine energy that connects all beings.
    • In moments of challenge or change, recalling this mantra can inspire resilience and transformation, as it acknowledges the constant presence of divine guidance.

  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a profound reminder of the interconnectedness of all life and the divine presence that permeates existence.
    • By cultivating awareness of this omnipresence, individuals can foster a deeper connection to themselves, others, and the universe.
    • Ultimately, it encourages a life of mindfulness, gratitude, and recognition of the sacred in everyday moments.
