ते अद्रयो दाशयन्त्रास आषवस् तेषां आधानं पर्य एति हर्यतम् |त उ सुतस्य सोम्यस्यान्धसोऽंशोः पीयूषं प्रथमस्य भेजिरे ||
te adrayo daśayantrāsa āśavas teṣām ādhānam pary eti haryatam | ta ū sutasya somyasyāndhaso 'ṁśoḥ pīyūṣam prathamasya bhejire ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • te - those
    • adrayo - mountains
    • daśayantrāsa - tenfold (or multiple) support
    • āśavas - swift or powerful
    • teṣām - of them
    • ādhānam - foundation or base
    • pary eti - surrounds or encompasses
    • haryatam - the most auspicious or beneficial
    • ta - that
    • ū - and
    • sutasya - of the son
    • somyasyāndhaso - of the moon-like (or gentle) one
    • ’ṁśoḥ - of the essence
    • pīyūṣam - nectar or essence
    • prathamasya - of the first
    • bhejire - they have broken or released
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the mountains as a source of strength and support, symbolizing stability and endurance in life.
    • The phrase "tenfold support" suggests that there are multiple layers of support that one can rely on, much like the various aspects of life that provide us with strength—family, friends, community, and nature.
    • The term "swift" indicates the dynamic nature of these supports, which can change and adapt to our needs.
    • The "foundation" signifies the importance of having a solid base in life, whether it be moral values, education, or emotional stability.
    • The "most auspicious" aspect highlights the positive outcomes that arise from these supports, suggesting that they lead to prosperity and well-being.
    • The reference to the "son of the moon-like one" can be interpreted as the nurturing qualities of a mother or a guiding figure, emphasizing the importance of guidance and care in our lives.
    • The "nectar" symbolizes the essence of life, the sweetness that comes from fulfilling relationships and experiences.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a family setting, the support of parents and siblings can be seen as the "mountains" that provide stability during challenging times.
    • In a professional context, mentors and colleagues can serve as the "tenfold support," helping individuals navigate their careers.
    • Community support during crises, such as natural disasters, exemplifies the "auspicious" nature of collective strength.
    • In personal development, having a strong moral foundation can guide decisions and actions, leading to a fulfilling life.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of support systems in our lives, highlighting the importance of stability, guidance, and nurturing relationships.
    • It serves as a reminder to seek and appreciate the various forms of support available to us, whether they come from nature, family, friends, or community.
    • In real-life scenarios, recognizing and valuing these supports can lead to a more balanced and fulfilling existence, ultimately contributing to personal and collective well-being.
