आ नूनं यातम अश्विनेमाऽ हव्यानि वां हिता |इमे सोमासो अधि तुर्वशे यदाव इमे कण्वेषु वाम अथ ||
ā nūnaṁ yātam aśvinemā havyāni vāṁ hitā | ime somāso adhi turvaśe yadāv ime kaṇveṣu vām atha ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā nūnaṁ: Indeed, now
    • yātam: You have come
    • aśvinemā: O Ashvins (the twin deities)
    • havyāni: Offerings
    • vāṁ: Your
    • hitā: Beneficial
    • ime: These
    • somāso: Soma (a sacred drink)
    • adhi: Upon
    • turvaśe: The strong
    • yadāv: When
    • ime: These
    • kaṇveṣu: In the Kanva family
    • vām: You
    • atha: Then
  • Contextual Meaning:
    • This mantra is a prayer to the Ashvins, who are considered divine physicians and bringers of health and prosperity.
    • The speaker acknowledges the presence of the Ashvins and offers them beneficial offerings, seeking their blessings.
    • The mention of "Soma" indicates a connection to rituals that involve this sacred drink, which is believed to confer immortality and divine insight.
    • The reference to the "Kanva family" suggests a lineage or tradition that is invoking the blessings of the Ashvins, emphasizing the importance of heritage in spiritual practices.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern times, people often seek blessings for health and well-being through various rituals and prayers, similar to how the Ashvins are invoked in this mantra.
    • Health practitioners and caregivers can be seen as modern-day Ashvins, providing care and healing to those in need.
    • Communities often come together to celebrate festivals that honor deities, reflecting the collective spirit of seeking divine blessings for prosperity and health.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of divine intervention in human affairs, particularly in health and well-being.
    • It serves as a reminder of the importance of community, tradition, and the pursuit of beneficial outcomes through spiritual practices.
    • In real life, the invocation of divine blessings can inspire individuals and communities to strive for better health, prosperity, and harmony in their lives.
