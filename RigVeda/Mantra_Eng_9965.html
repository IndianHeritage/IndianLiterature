मन्द्रा कृत्नुध्वं धिया आ तनुध्वं नावम् अरित्रपरणीं कृत्नुध्वम् |इष्कृत्नुध्वम् आयुधारं कृत्नुध्वम् प्राञ्चं यज्ञं प्र णयतां सखायः ||
mandrā kṛṇudhvaṁ dhiya ā tanudhvaṁ nāvam aritraparaṇīṁ kṛṇudhvam | iṣkṛṇudhvam āyudhāraṁ kṛṇudhvam prāñcaṁ yajñam pra ṇayatā sakhāyaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • mandrā: a call or invocation
    • kṛṇudhvaṁ: you should create or make
    • dhiya: with intellect or understanding
    • ā: towards or in the direction of
    • tanudhvaṁ: you should make light or subtle
    • nāvam: a boat or vessel
    • aritraparaṇīṁ: free from obstacles or difficulties
    • kṛṇudhvam: you should create or make
    • iṣkṛṇudhvam: you should also create or make
    • āyudhāraṁ: a holder of weapons or strength
    • kṛṇudhvam: you should create or make
    • prāñcaṁ: the vital or life force
    • yajñam: a sacrifice or offering
    • pra ṇayatā: by the act of leading or guiding
    • sakhāyaḥ: friends or companions
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of collective effort and the power of intention in achieving goals.
    • The invocation to create a vessel (nāvam) signifies the need for a supportive structure or environment to navigate through life's challenges.
    • By calling upon friends (sakhāyaḥ) and guiding each other, the mantra highlights the significance of community and collaboration.
    • The mention of making light (tanudhvaṁ) suggests the importance of maintaining a positive and light-hearted approach in difficult situations.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a team project at work, individuals can invoke this mantra by collaborating effectively, sharing ideas, and supporting each other to overcome obstacles.
    • In personal relationships, friends can embody the essence of this mantra by being there for one another, creating a safe space to express thoughts and feelings.
    • In community service, groups can come together to create solutions for social issues, embodying the spirit of collective action and support.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for unity, creativity, and support in overcoming life's challenges.
    • It serves as a reminder that with collective effort and a positive mindset, individuals can navigate through difficulties and achieve their goals.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as teamwork, friendships, and community initiatives, fostering a sense of togetherness and shared purpose.
