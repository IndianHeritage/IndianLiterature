तं वो विं न दृषदं देवम् अन्धस इन्दुम प्रोतन्तं प्रवापन्तं अर्णवम् |आसा वह्निं न शोचिषा विरप्शिनं महिव्रतं न सरजन्तं अध्वनः ||
taṁ vo viṁ na druṣadaṁ devam andhasa indum prothantam pravapantam arṇavam | āsā vahniṁ na śociṣā virapśinam mahivrataṁ na sarajantam adhvanaḥ ||
  • taṁ vo viṁ na druṣadaṁ devam: This phrase can be interpreted as a call to the divine, asking for the presence of a deity that is not easily perceived or understood. It signifies the search for deeper truths and the unseen forces that govern our lives.
  • andhasa indum prothantam pravapantam arṇavam: Here, "andhasa" refers to darkness or ignorance, while "indum" signifies the moon, often associated with knowledge and enlightenment. This part of the mantra suggests the emergence of light from darkness, symbolizing the journey from ignorance to knowledge. "Arṇavam" refers to the ocean, representing the vastness of knowledge and experience.
  • āsā vahniṁ na śociṣā virapśinam: "Vahni" means fire, which symbolizes transformation and purification. This line emphasizes the importance of seeking the transformative power of knowledge and wisdom, which can illuminate our paths and help us overcome challenges.
  • mahivrataṁ na sarajantam adhvanaḥ: "Mahivrata" refers to a great vow or commitment, indicating the importance of dedication in the pursuit of knowledge and truth. "Adhvanaḥ" means paths, suggesting that there are various routes to enlightenment, and one must choose wisely.

Conclusion: This mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking knowledge and enlightenment amidst ignorance and darkness. It emphasizes the transformative power of wisdom and the importance of commitment in our journey towards understanding. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios such as education, personal development, and spiritual growth. By recognizing the unseen forces that influence our lives and committing to a path of learning and transformation, we can navigate through challenges and emerge enlightened, much like the moon illuminating the night sky. This mantra serves as a reminder to seek knowledge, embrace transformation, and remain dedicated to our personal and spiritual journeys.
