विषो-विषो वो अतिथिं वाजयन्तः पुरुप्रियम् | अग्निं वो दुर्यं वचः स्तुषे शूषस्य मन्मभिः ||
viśo-viśo vo atithiṁ vājayantaḥ purupriyam | agniṁ vo duryaṁ vacaḥ stuṣe śūṣasya manmabhiḥ ||
  • viśo-viśo: This term can be interpreted as "of the people" or "of the community." It emphasizes the importance of collective identity and unity among individuals.
  • vo: This means "to you," indicating a direct address to the audience or the divine.
  • atithiṁ: This translates to "guest" or "host." In many cultures, guests are treated with great respect, symbolizing hospitality and the importance of welcoming others.
  • vājayantaḥ: This word means "those who bring strength" or "those who are powerful." It signifies the importance of strength and support within a community.
  • purupriyam: This can be understood as "beloved by the people" or "dear to the community." It highlights the value of being cherished and respected within a society.
  • agniṁ: This refers to "fire," which is a significant element in Vedic rituals and symbolizes purity, transformation, and energy.
  • vo: Again, this means "to you," reinforcing the direct address to the audience or divine forces.
  • duryaṁ: This translates to "difficult" or "challenging." It acknowledges the struggles and obstacles that may arise in life.
  • vacaḥ: This means "words" or "speech," emphasizing the power of communication and expression.
  • stuṣe: This translates to "praise" or "worship," indicating the act of honoring and recognizing the divine or the strengths within the community.
  • śūṣasya: This means "of the dry" or "of the barren," which can symbolize challenges or hardships that need to be overcome.
  • manmabhiḥ: This translates to "with my thoughts" or "with my mind," emphasizing the importance of intention and mental focus in achieving goals.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of community, hospitality, and the power of words and intentions. It highlights the importance of supporting one another through challenges and celebrating the strengths within a community. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various contexts such as fostering teamwork in workplaces, promoting inclusivity in social gatherings, and encouraging open communication in relationships. By embodying the principles of unity, respect, and support, individuals can create a harmonious environment that benefits everyone involved.
