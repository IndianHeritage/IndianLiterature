तं उ स्तोतारः पूर्व्यं यथा विद ऋतस्य गर्भं जनुषा पिपर्तन |आस्य जानन्तो नाम चिद् विवक्तन महस् ते विष्णो सुमतिम भजामहे ||
tam u stotāraḥ pūrvyaṁ yathā vida ṛtasya garbhaṁ januṣā pipartana | āsya jānanto nāma cid vivaktana mahas te viṣṇo sumatim bhajāmahe ||
  • tam u stotāraḥ pūrvyaṁ: This phrase can be translated as "We invoke the ancient praise." It signifies the importance of tradition and the reverence for the wisdom passed down through generations.
  • yathā vida ṛtasya garbhaṁ: This means "as one who knows the essence of truth." Here, 'ṛta' refers to the cosmic order or truth, and 'garbha' signifies the womb or source. It emphasizes the understanding of fundamental truths that govern existence.
  • januṣā pipartana: This translates to "which is nurtured by birth." It suggests that truth and wisdom are inherent in life and are nurtured through experiences and existence.
  • āsya jānanto nāma: This means "those who know this name." It indicates the importance of knowledge and awareness in recognizing the divine or the essence of life.
  • cid vivaktana: This can be interpreted as "the consciousness that reveals." It highlights the role of consciousness in understanding and revealing deeper truths.
  • mahas te viṣṇo: This translates to "O great Vishnu." Vishnu is often associated with preservation and protection in Hinduism, symbolizing the sustaining force of the universe.
  • sumatim bhajāmahe: This means "we seek good understanding." It reflects the aspiration for wisdom and clarity in thought and action.

Conclusion: This mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking truth and wisdom through the acknowledgment of ancient knowledge and the divine. It emphasizes the importance of nurturing understanding and consciousness in our lives. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various contexts such as education, where the pursuit of knowledge is paramount, or in personal development, where understanding oneself and the universe leads to a more fulfilling life. By invoking the qualities of Vishnu, we aspire to maintain balance and harmony in our lives, seeking clarity and wisdom in our decisions and actions.
