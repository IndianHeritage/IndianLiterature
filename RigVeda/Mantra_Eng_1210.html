ऋभुर भराय सं शिशातु सातीं समर्यजिद वाजो अस्मांं अविष्टु |तन नो मित्रो वरुणो मामहन्ताम् अदितिः सिन्धुः पृथिवी उता द्यौः ||
ṛbhur bharāya saṁ śiśātu sātiṁ samaryajid vājo asmām̐ aviṣṭu | tan no mitro varuṇo māmahantām aditiḥ sindhuḥ pṛthivī uta dyauḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ṛbhur: The one who is wise or knowledgeable.
    • bharāya: May he nourish or support.
    • saṁ: Together or in unity.
    • śiśātu: May he protect or preserve.
    • sātiṁ: The truth or righteousness.
    • samaryajid: The one who is victorious in battles or conflicts.
    • vājo: The one who is powerful or strong.
    • asmām̐: For us or among us.
    • aviṣṭu: May he not be absent or may he be present.
    • tan: That or this.
    • no: Our or us.
    • mitro: Friend or ally.
    • varuṇo: The divine or cosmic order.
    • māmahantām: May he be great or significant.
    • aditiḥ: The infinite or boundless.
    • sindhuḥ: The ocean or river.
    • pṛthivī: The earth.
    • uta: And or also.
    • dyauḥ: The sky or heavens.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the divine forces to provide nourishment and protection.
    • It emphasizes the importance of unity and righteousness in our lives.
    • The mention of "vājo" signifies the strength and power that we seek in our endeavors.
    • By calling upon "mitro" and "varuṇo," it highlights the need for friendship and adherence to cosmic order.
    • The reference to "aditiḥ" suggests a connection to the infinite possibilities that life offers.
    • The elements of nature, such as "sindhuḥ," "pṛthivī," and "dyauḥ," remind us of our relationship with the environment.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community setting, this mantra can be applied to foster collaboration and support among members, ensuring that everyone works together for a common goal.
    • In personal relationships, invoking the principles of truth and righteousness can help resolve conflicts and strengthen bonds.
    • In professional environments, the call for strength and power can inspire individuals to pursue their goals with determination and resilience.
    • Environmental awareness can be enhanced by recognizing our connection to nature, as highlighted by the references to earth, ocean, and sky.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of all beings and the importance of seeking support and strength from the divine and from each other.
    • Its significance lies in promoting unity, righteousness, and respect for nature, which are essential for a harmonious existence.
    • Incorporating the teachings of this mantra into daily life can lead to personal growth, stronger relationships, and a more sustainable world.
