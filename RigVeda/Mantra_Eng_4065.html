विप्रेभिर् विप्र सन्त्य प्रातर्यावभिर् आगहि |देवेभिः सोमपीतये ||
viprebhir vipra santya prātaryāvabhir ā gahi | devebhiḥ somapītaye ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • viprebhir: By the learned or wise ones (vipra refers to a sage or learned person).
    • vipra: The wise or learned individual.
    • santya: Peace or tranquility.
    • prātaryāvabhir: By the dawn or the early morning light.
    • ā gahi: Come forth or approach.
    • devebhiḥ: By the gods or divine beings.
    • somapītaye: For the drinking of Soma (a sacred drink in Vedic rituals).
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the wise individuals to come together in peace and harmony, especially at the dawn of a new day.
    • It emphasizes the importance of collective wisdom and the role of learned individuals in guiding society.
    • The mention of the gods and the drinking of Soma signifies a spiritual connection and the importance of divine blessings in achieving peace and wisdom.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community setting, this mantra can be applied during gatherings where leaders and knowledgeable individuals come together to discuss and resolve issues, fostering a peaceful environment.
    • In educational institutions, the collaboration of teachers and students can be seen as a modern-day interpretation of this mantra, where wisdom is shared and peace is cultivated through learning.
    • In corporate environments, team-building exercises that promote collaboration and understanding among employees reflect the essence of this mantra, leading to a harmonious workplace.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for unity, wisdom, and divine guidance in achieving peace.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from community gatherings to educational and corporate settings, where the collective wisdom of individuals can lead to positive outcomes.
    • Ultimately, it serves as a reminder of the importance of collaboration and the pursuit of knowledge in fostering a peaceful and harmonious society.
