एष उ स्य पूर्व्रतो जज्ञानो जनयन्न इषः |धारया पवते सुतः ||
eṣa u sya puruvrato jajñāno janayann iṣaḥ | dhārayā pavate sutaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • eṣa: this
    • u: indeed
    • sya: of him
    • puruvrato: who has taken a vow (or commitment) in the past
    • jajñāno: knowing (or having knowledge)
    • janayann: generating (or creating)
    • iṣaḥ: power (or authority)
    • dhārayā: by sustaining
    • pavate: he who moves (or flows)
    • sutaḥ: the son (or offspring)
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of a divine entity or force that is both knowledgeable and powerful.
    • The phrase "puruvrato" suggests a commitment or vow taken in the past, indicating a sense of responsibility and dedication.
    • The term "janayann" implies the act of creation or generation, highlighting the creative aspect of this divine force.
    • "Dhārayā pavate" indicates that this force sustains and moves, suggesting a dynamic and nurturing quality.
    • Overall, the mantra reflects the interconnectedness of knowledge, power, and the nurturing aspect of creation.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a leadership context, a leader who has a vision (knowledge) and is committed to their goals (puruvrato) can inspire and generate positive change (janayann) within their team.
    • In parenting, a nurturing parent (dhārayā) who provides guidance and support (pavate) to their children (sutaḥ) embodies the essence of this mantra.
    • In environmental conservation, individuals and organizations that take a vow to protect nature (puruvrato) and actively work towards sustainability (janayann) demonstrate the principles of this mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of knowledge, commitment, and nurturing in the process of creation and sustenance.
    • Its significance lies in the reminder that true power comes from a combination of wisdom and responsibility.
    • In real-life scenarios, whether in leadership, parenting, or environmental efforts, embodying these principles can lead to positive outcomes and a harmonious existence.
