इमं नो अग्नि अध्वरं जुषस्व मरुत्स्व इन्द्रे यशसं कृधī नः |आ नक्तā बर्हिः सदताम उषासोशन्ता मित्रावरुणा यजेह ||
imaṁ no agne adhvaraṁ juṣasva marutsv indre yaśasaṁ kṛdhī naḥ | ā naktā barhiḥ sadatām uṣāsośantā mitrāvaruṇā yajeha ||
  • imaṁ no agne adhvaraṁ juṣasva: This phrase can be translated as "O Agni, may you accept this sacrifice." Here, Agni represents the fire god, who is a vital element in Vedic rituals. In real life, this can be seen as an appeal to the divine for acceptance of one's efforts or endeavors, much like seeking approval from a mentor or authority figure.
  • marutsv indre yaśasaṁ kṛdhī naḥ: This translates to "O Maruts, and Indra, may you bestow upon us glory." The Maruts are storm deities, and Indra is the king of the gods, often associated with strength and rain. In a contemporary context, this can symbolize the importance of teamwork and collaboration in achieving success, akin to how a sports team works together to win a championship.
  • ā naktā barhiḥ sadatām uṣāsośantā: This means "May the sacred grass be ever present at night." The barhiḥ refers to the sacred grass used in rituals, symbolizing purity and sanctity. In everyday life, this can be interpreted as the need for maintaining purity in our thoughts and actions, similar to how one might strive for integrity in personal and professional relationships.
  • mitrāvaruṇā yajeha: This translates to "May we worship the Mitra and Varuna." Mitra and Varuna are deities representing friendship and cosmic order, respectively. This can be related to the importance of maintaining harmony and balance in our lives, much like how we seek to build strong friendships and uphold ethical standards in society.

  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of seeking divine blessings for our actions and endeavors. It highlights the importance of collaboration, purity, and harmony in our lives. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied to various aspects such as teamwork in professional settings, maintaining integrity in personal relationships, and striving for a balanced life. By invoking these principles, individuals can foster a sense of community and purpose, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling existence.
