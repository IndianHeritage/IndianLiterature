स प्रकेत उभयस्य प्रवीद्वान सहस्रदाना उता वा सदानः |यमेना ततम परिधिं वयिष्यन्न अप्सरसः परि जजñe वसिष्ठः ||
sa praketa ubhayasya pravidvān sahasradāna uta vā sadānaḥ | yamena tatam paridhiṁ vayiṣyann apsarasaḥ pari jajñe vasiṣṭhaḥ ||
  • sa praketa ubhayasya: This phrase can be translated as "he who knows both sides." It signifies a person who possesses wisdom and understanding of dualities in life, such as good and evil, joy and sorrow. In real life, this can be seen in leaders or mediators who can navigate complex situations by understanding different perspectives.
  • pravidvān sahasradāna uta vā sadānaḥ: This part means "the one who is knowledgeable and generous." It emphasizes the importance of knowledge and the willingness to share it with others. For example, a teacher who imparts knowledge to students embodies this quality, enriching their lives and fostering growth.
  • yamena tatam paridhiṁ: This translates to "by the one who has established the boundary." It suggests the importance of setting limits and boundaries in life. In a practical sense, this can relate to personal relationships where healthy boundaries are essential for mutual respect and understanding.
  • vayiṣyann apsarasaḥ: This phrase can be interpreted as "the one who is surrounded by celestial beings." It symbolizes the idea of being in harmony with the universe and the divine. In real life, this can be reflected in moments of inspiration or creativity, where individuals feel connected to something greater than themselves, such as artists or musicians during their creative processes.
  • pari jajñe vasiṣṭhaḥ: This means "the one who is born from the best." It signifies the idea of excellence and striving for the highest ideals. In practical terms, this can be seen in individuals who pursue their passions and aim for excellence in their fields, such as scientists making groundbreaking discoveries or athletes achieving new records.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of wisdom, generosity, boundary-setting, harmony with the universe, and the pursuit of excellence. In real life, these qualities are applicable in various scenarios, such as leadership, education, personal relationships, creative endeavors, and professional excellence. By embodying these principles, individuals can lead more fulfilling and impactful lives, contributing positively to society and fostering a sense of community and understanding among diverse perspectives.
