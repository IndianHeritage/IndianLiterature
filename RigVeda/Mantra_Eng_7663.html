आयं विश्वा अभि श्रीयो अग्निर देवेषु पत्यते |आ वाजैर उप नो गमत ||
ayaṁ viśvā abhi śriyo 'gnir deveṣu patyate | ā vājair upa no gamat ||
  • ayaṁ viśvā abhi śriyo 'gnir deveṣu patyate
    • ayaṁ - This word translates to "this" or "this one," indicating a specific focus or subject.
    • viśvā - Meaning "all" or "universal," it signifies inclusiveness and the entirety of existence.
    • abhi - This can mean "towards" or "in the direction of," suggesting movement or intention.
    • śriyo - This word refers to "prosperity" or "wealth," often in a spiritual or material sense.
    • agnir - Meaning "fire," it symbolizes energy, transformation, and purification.
    • deveṣu - This translates to "among the gods," indicating a divine or spiritual context.
    • patyate - Meaning "is worshipped" or "is revered," it denotes respect and acknowledgment.
  • ā vājair upa no gamat
    • ā - This word means "towards" or "to," indicating direction or purpose.
    • vājair - Referring to "strength" or "power," it often relates to the vigor needed to achieve goals.
    • upa - Meaning "near" or "close," it suggests proximity or approach.
    • no - This translates to "us," indicating a collective or community aspect.
    • gamat - Meaning "may come" or "may approach," it implies a hopeful or aspirational movement.

Meaning and Interpretation:

This mantra emphasizes the universal nature of prosperity and the reverence for divine energy, represented by fire. It suggests that all forms of wealth and success are directed towards the divine, and through this acknowledgment, one can draw strength and power into their lives. The invocation of fire symbolizes transformation and the potential for growth, urging individuals to approach their goals with vigor and intention.


Real-Life Examples:

  • In a business context, this mantra can be applied to the pursuit of success. By recognizing the divine or ethical aspects of business, entrepreneurs can create a culture of respect and integrity, leading to sustainable growth.
  • In personal development, individuals can use this mantra as a reminder to focus on their goals with determination and to seek guidance from higher principles or values, ensuring that their journey is aligned with a greater purpose.
  • In community initiatives, this mantra can inspire collective efforts towards prosperity, encouraging collaboration and shared strength to achieve common goals.

Conclusion:

The significance of this mantra lies in its call for reverence towards the divine while pursuing prosperity. It teaches that true wealth is not just material but also spiritual, and by aligning our intentions with higher values, we can attract strength and success into our lives. This mantra is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal growth to community development, reminding us to approach our aspirations with respect, energy, and a sense of unity.
