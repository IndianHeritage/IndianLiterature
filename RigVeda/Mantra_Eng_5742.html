न्युप्रियो मनुष्यः सादि होता नासत्याः यो यजते वन्दते च |अश्नीतम मध्वो अश्विनाः उपाक आ वां वोच विदथेṣu प्रयस्वान ||
ny u priyo manuṣaḥ sādi hotā nāsatyā yo yajate vandate ca | aśnītam madhvo aśvinā upāka ā vāṁ voce vidatheṣu prayasvān ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • nyu: not
    • u: indeed
    • priyo: dear
    • manuṣaḥ: human being
    • sādi: who is the best
    • hotā: the priest or the one who offers
    • nāsatyā: the one who is truthful
    • yo: who
    • yajate: worships or offers
    • vandate: praises
    • ca: and
    • aśnītam: enjoys or receives
    • madhvo: sweetness or bliss
    • aśvinā: with the Aśvins (divine twin horsemen)
    • upāka: with the help
    • ā: indeed
    • vāṁ: your
    • voce: voice or speech
    • vidatheṣu: in the assemblies or gatherings
    • prayasvān: the one who is active or diligent
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of being a truthful and devoted individual.
    • It suggests that a person who worships sincerely and praises the divine will receive blessings and enjoy the sweetness of life.
    • The mention of the Aśvins symbolizes the duality of life, representing health, wealth, and well-being.
    • It encourages active participation in community gatherings and the importance of voice in collective worship.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community setting, a person who actively participates in rituals and helps others is often respected and valued.
    • In workplaces, those who contribute positively and support their colleagues tend to create a harmonious environment, leading to collective success.
    • In personal relationships, being truthful and supportive fosters trust and strengthens bonds.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the value of truthfulness, devotion, and active participation in both spiritual and social contexts.
    • It is applicable in real-life scenarios where community, relationships, and personal integrity are essential for a fulfilling life.
    • By embodying these principles, individuals can enhance their own lives and contribute positively to the lives of others.
