वृषा सोतā सुनोतु ते वृषन्न ऋजीपिन्न आ भर |वृषा दधन्वे वृषणं नदिष्व आ तुभ्यं स्थातर हरिणाम् ||
vṛṣā sotā sunotu te vṛṣann ṛjīpinn ā bhara | vṛṣā dadhanve vṛṣaṇaṁ nadīṣv ā tubhyaṁ sthātar harīṇām ||
  • vṛṣā sotā sunotu te: This phrase can be translated as "May the strong one (vṛṣā) hear you." Here, "vṛṣā" refers to strength or power, often associated with deities or forces of nature. In real life, this can be likened to seeking the support of powerful allies or mentors in our endeavors.
  • vṛṣann ṛjīpinn ā bhara: This translates to "May the strong one (vṛṣa) bring forth righteousness." The term "ṛjīpinn" relates to righteousness or truth. This can be seen in situations where individuals or communities strive for justice, such as social movements advocating for equality and fairness.
  • vṛṣā dadhanve vṛṣaṇaṁ: This means "The strong one (vṛṣā) who wields power." It emphasizes the idea of strength being used responsibly. In a real-life context, this can be related to leaders who use their power to uplift others rather than for personal gain, such as philanthropic leaders who invest in community development.
  • nadīṣv ā tubhyaṁ: This translates to "In the rivers (nadīṣv), may it flow to you." Rivers symbolize the flow of life and abundance. This can be compared to the idea of resources and opportunities flowing towards those who are deserving, much like how a river nourishes the land around it.
  • sthātar harīṇām: This means "The one who supports the swift ones (harīṇām)." Here, "harīṇām" refers to the swift or agile, often symbolizing progress and movement. In real life, this can be seen in how innovation and technology support rapid advancements in society, helping those who are quick to adapt and evolve.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of strength, righteousness, and the flow of resources in our lives. It signifies that true power should be wielded with responsibility and that support should be given to those who strive for justice and progress. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire individuals to seek strength in their pursuits, advocate for righteousness, and create a supportive environment for innovation and growth. It serves as a reminder that our actions should contribute to the greater good, fostering a community where everyone can thrive.
