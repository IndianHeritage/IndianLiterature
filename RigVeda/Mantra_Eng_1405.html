अभ्रातेव पुंसा एति प्रतिची गर्तारुग इव सनये धनानाम् |जायेव पत्या उषती सुवासा उषा हस्रेवा नि रिणीते अप्सः ||
abhrāteva puṁsa eti pratīcī gartārug iva sanaye dhanānām | jāyeva patya uśatī suvāsā uṣā hasreva ni riṇīte apsaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • abhrāteva: like a cloud
    • puṁsa: of a man
    • eti: goes
    • pratīcī: towards the west
    • gartārug: like a swift-moving chariot
    • iva: like
    • sanaye: to the one who is rich
    • dhanānām: of wealth
    • jāyeva: like a mother
    • patya: of a husband
    • uśatī: the one who is auspicious
    • suvāsā: with good fragrance
    • uṣā: dawn
    • hasreva: like a beautiful one
    • ni riṇīte: brings forth
    • apsaḥ: of the waters
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra describes the beauty and richness of life, comparing it to the elements of nature.
    • The imagery of a cloud suggests abundance and nourishment, akin to how clouds bring rain, which is essential for agriculture.
    • The reference to a swift-moving chariot symbolizes progress and the journey of life, emphasizing the importance of moving forward with purpose.
    • The mention of dawn (uṣā) signifies new beginnings, hope, and the promise of a new day, which is relatable in our daily lives as we face challenges and opportunities.
    • The auspiciousness of a mother and the fragrance of dawn highlight the nurturing aspects of life, reminding us of the importance of care and positivity in our relationships.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In agriculture, the arrival of rain (symbolized by clouds) is crucial for crop growth, representing how nature supports human life.
    • In personal development, the idea of moving forward like a chariot can be seen in the pursuit of education or career goals, where one must continuously strive for progress.
    • The dawn represents new opportunities; for instance, starting a new job or project can be seen as a fresh start, filled with potential.
    • In relationships, nurturing and positive interactions, akin to the fragrance of dawn, can strengthen bonds and create a supportive environment.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra encapsulates the essence of life, emphasizing abundance, progress, and nurturing relationships.
    • Its significance lies in reminding us to appreciate the natural elements that support our existence and to embrace new beginnings with hope and positivity.
    • In real-life scenarios, it encourages us to be proactive in our pursuits, cherish our relationships, and recognize the beauty in everyday experiences.
