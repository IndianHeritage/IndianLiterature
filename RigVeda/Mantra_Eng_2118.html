यः पृथिवीं व्यथमानाम् अदृंहद यः पर्वतान् प्रकुपिताम् अरम्णात् |यो अन्तरिक्षं विममे वरीयो यो द्यां अस्तभ्नात् स जनास इन्द्रः ||
yaḥ pṛthivīṁ vyathamānām adṛṁhad yaḥ parvatān prakupitām̐ aramṇāt | yo antarikṣaṁ vimame varīyo yo dyām astabhnāt sa janāsa indraḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yaḥ: who
    • pṛthivīm: the earth
    • vyathamānām: trembling or shaking
    • adṛṁhad: great or vast
    • yaḥ: who
    • parvatān: mountains
    • prakupitām: angry or disturbed
    • aramṇāt: from the disturbance
    • yo: who
    • antarikṣaṁ: the space or atmosphere
    • vimame: measured or understood
    • varīyo: the most powerful
    • yo: who
    • dyām: the sky
    • astabhnāt: established or set
    • sa: he
    • janāsa: of the people
    • indraḥ: Indra, the king of gods
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the divine power of Indra, who is capable of calming the trembling earth and the angry mountains.
    • It highlights Indra's ability to measure the vastness of the atmosphere and establish order in the sky.
    • Indra is portrayed as a protector and a powerful force that ensures the well-being of the people.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In times of natural disasters, such as earthquakes or landslides, the mantra can be seen as a call for divine intervention to restore balance and safety.
    • In a metaphorical sense, when individuals face turmoil in their lives, invoking the essence of this mantra can serve as a reminder to seek strength and stability.
    • In environmental discussions, the mantra can symbolize the need for harmony between nature and humanity, urging us to respect and protect our planet.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its representation of divine strength and the ability to restore order amidst chaos.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal challenges to broader environmental issues, reminding us of the importance of seeking balance and protection.
    • Ultimately, it encourages individuals and communities to connect with the divine for guidance and support in overcoming obstacles.
