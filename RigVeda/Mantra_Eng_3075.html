अत्यā वृधस्नू रोहितā घृतस्नू ऋतस्य मन्ये मनसā जवीष्ठā |अन्तर īयसे अरुṣā युजāनो युष्मांś च देवān विशā ā च मर्तān ||
atyā vṛdhasnū rohitā ghṛtasnū ṛtasya manye manasā javiṣṭhā | antar īyase aruṣā yujāno yuṣmām̐ś ca devān viśa ā ca martān ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • atyā: beyond, surpassing
    • vṛdhasnū: the one who is strong, powerful
    • rohitā: red, often associated with vitality and energy
    • ghṛtasnū: one who is nourished, often referring to the essence of life
    • ṛtasya: of truth, righteousness
    • manye: I think, I believe
    • manasā: with the mind
    • javiṣṭhā: the swiftest, the most agile
    • antar: within
    • īyase: you move, you go
    • aruṣā: red, bright, often symbolizing auspiciousness
    • yujāno: joining, connecting
    • yuṣmām̐ś: to you, to your essence
    • ca: and
    • devān: the gods
    • viśa: to the world, to the universe
    • ā: from, out of
    • ca: and
    • martān: mortals, human beings
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of a powerful and vibrant force that transcends ordinary strength.
    • The imagery of "red" and "nourished" suggests a life force that is both dynamic and sustaining.
    • The reference to "truth" and "mind" indicates a connection between our thoughts and the universal principles of righteousness.
    • It emphasizes the importance of agility and swiftness in thought and action, suggesting that one should be quick to adapt and respond to life's challenges.
    • The invocation of the gods and the connection to both the divine and the mortal realm highlights the interdependence of all beings.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, a leader who embodies the qualities of strength, agility, and righteousness can inspire their team to achieve greater heights.
    • In personal relationships, being nourishing and supportive while also being truthful can strengthen bonds and foster trust.
    • In sports, athletes often need to be swift and agile, adapting quickly to the dynamics of the game, much like the mantra suggests.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of strength, truth, and agility in our lives.
    • It encourages us to cultivate these qualities within ourselves and recognize their significance in our interactions with others.
    • By embodying these principles, we can navigate the complexities of life with grace and purpose, ultimately contributing to a harmonious existence.
