प्र वो भ्रियन्त इन्दवो मत्सराः मादयिष्णवः |द्रप्सा मध्वश चामूषदः ||
pra vo bhriyanta indavo matsarā mādayiṣṇavaḥ | drapsā madhvaś camūṣadaḥ ||
  • Translation: The mantra can be translated as: "May the Indavas (the divine beings) bring us abundance, free from envy, and may we be filled with joy and sweetness."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • pra: A prefix meaning "forth" or "forward," indicating a sense of abundance or increase.
    • vo: A form of "you," referring to the divine beings or gods.
    • bhriyanta: Derived from the root "bhr" meaning "to carry" or "to bring," suggesting the act of providing or bestowing.
    • indavo: Referring to the divine beings or gods, often associated with inspiration and enlightenment.
    • matsarā: Meaning "free from envy," emphasizing the importance of harmony and goodwill.
    • mādayiṣṇavaḥ: Meaning "filled with joy," indicating a state of happiness and fulfillment.
    • drapsā: Referring to sweetness or pleasantness, often associated with the joys of life.
    • madhvaś: Meaning "sweet," reinforcing the idea of joy and positivity.
    • camūṣadaḥ: Referring to the act of gathering or coming together, often in a communal or collective sense.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace setting, fostering an environment free from envy can lead to better collaboration and productivity. When team members support each other, they can achieve greater success together.
    • In personal relationships, promoting joy and sweetness can enhance bonds. Celebrating each other's achievements without jealousy can create a more fulfilling connection.
    • In community gatherings, encouraging a spirit of togetherness and joy can lead to memorable experiences. Events that focus on collective happiness can strengthen community ties.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for abundance, joy, and harmony among individuals and communities. It emphasizes the importance of fostering positive relationships free from envy, which is applicable in various aspects of life, including personal relationships, workplaces, and community interactions. By embodying the principles of this mantra, individuals can contribute to a more harmonious and joyful existence, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling life experience.
