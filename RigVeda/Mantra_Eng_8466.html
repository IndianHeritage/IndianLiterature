त्वं नृचक्षाऽसि सोम विश्वतः पवमान वृषभ ता विघावसि |स नः पवस्व वसुमद धिरण्यवद वयं स्याम भुवनेषु जीवसे ||
tvaṁ nṛcakṣā asi soma viśvataḥ pavamāna vṛṣabha tā vi dhāvasi | sa naḥ pavasva vasumad dhiraṇyavad vayaṁ syāma bhuvaneṣu jīvase ||
  • tvaṁ nṛcakṣā asi: This phrase translates to "You are the one with human eyes." Here, the term "nṛcakṣā" refers to the divine perception that allows one to see beyond the physical realm. In real life, this can be likened to the ability of individuals to empathize and understand others' perspectives, fostering compassion and connection.
  • soma viśvataḥ pavamāna: "Soma" represents the divine nectar or essence, while "pavamāna" means "the one who flows." This can be interpreted as the universal life force that nourishes all beings. In practical terms, this can be seen in the way nature provides sustenance through food, water, and air, which are essential for life.
  • vṛṣabha tā vi dhāvasi: "Vṛṣabha" means "the bull," symbolizing strength and vitality. "Tā vi dhāvasi" translates to "you run forth." This can be related to the pursuit of goals and ambitions in life, where individuals harness their inner strength to overcome challenges and achieve success.
  • sa naḥ pavasva vasumad: This phrase means "may you purify us with wealth." Here, "vasumad" refers to prosperity and abundance. In a real-life context, this can be understood as a prayer for spiritual and material wealth, encouraging individuals to seek not just financial success but also richness in experiences and relationships.
  • dhiraṇyavad vayaṁ syāma bhuvaneṣu jīvase: This translates to "may we be strong like gold and live in the world." The comparison to gold signifies resilience and value. In everyday life, this can be interpreted as a call to cultivate inner strength and integrity, allowing individuals to navigate life's challenges with grace and purpose.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of divine perception, the flow of life, inner strength, and the pursuit of both material and spiritual wealth. Its significance lies in the holistic approach to life, encouraging individuals to seek balance between their physical needs and spiritual growth. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire people to develop empathy, pursue their goals with determination, and strive for a life rich in experiences and relationships, ultimately leading to a fulfilling existence.
