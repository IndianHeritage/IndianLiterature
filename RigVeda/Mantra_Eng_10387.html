विन इन्द्र मृधो जहि नीचां यच्छ पृतन्यतः |यो अस्मां अभिदासत्य अधरं गमयात तमः ||
vi na indra mṛdho jahi nīcā yaccha pṛtanyataḥ | yo asmām̐ abhidāsaty adharaṁ gamayā tamaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • vi: away, apart
    • na: not
    • indra: Indra, the king of gods
    • mṛdho: from the mortal realm
    • jahi: conquer, defeat
    • nīcā: low, inferior
    • yaccha: grant, give
    • pṛtanyataḥ: from the earth
    • yo: who
    • asmām̐: among us
    • abhidāsaty: who speaks truthfully
    • adharaṁ: the lower, the base
    • gamayā: by leading, guiding
    • tamaḥ: darkness, ignorance
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the divine power of Indra to help overcome the challenges posed by lower, inferior forces.
    • It emphasizes the importance of truth and integrity, suggesting that those who speak the truth will be guided away from darkness and ignorance.
    • The plea to Indra signifies a desire for divine assistance in overcoming obstacles that hinder progress and enlightenment.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, when facing unethical practices or corruption, one might invoke the spirit of this mantra to seek the strength to stand against such forces.
    • In personal relationships, when dealing with dishonesty or betrayal, this mantra can serve as a reminder to uphold truth and integrity, guiding one away from negativity.
    • In educational pursuits, students may find inspiration in this mantra to seek knowledge and truth, overcoming ignorance and striving for enlightenment.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the importance of truth and the need for divine support in overcoming challenges.
    • Its significance lies in the belief that by aligning oneself with truth and seeking guidance from higher powers, one can navigate through life's difficulties and emerge victorious.
    • In various aspects of life—be it personal, professional, or spiritual—this mantra encourages individuals to strive for integrity and seek enlightenment, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling existence.
