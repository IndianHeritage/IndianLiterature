अरā इवेद अचरमा अहेव प्र-प्र जायतें अकाश महोभिः | पृष्णेः पुत्रा उपमासो रभिष्ठाः स्वया मत्यā मरुतः सम मिमिक्षुः ||
arā ived acaramā aheva pra-pra jāyante akavā mahobhiḥ | pṛśneḥ putrā upamāso rabhiṣṭhāḥ svayā matyā marutaḥ sam mimikṣuḥ ||
  • arā ived acaramā aheva pra-pra jāyante akavā mahobhiḥ: This line can be interpreted as a call to the divine forces that govern the universe. The term "arā" refers to the essence or the core of existence, while "ived" suggests knowledge or understanding. "Acaramā" implies the movement or the dynamic aspect of life. The phrase "pra-pra jāyante" indicates the emergence or birth of something significant, and "akavā mahobhiḥ" refers to the great powers or energies that facilitate this process.
  • pṛśneḥ putrā upamāso rabhiṣṭhāḥ: Here, "pṛśneḥ" refers to the offspring or the progeny of the divine. "Putrā" means sons or children, symbolizing the continuation of life and legacy. "Upamāso" suggests a comparison or a metaphorical relationship, while "rabhiṣṭhāḥ" indicates those who are endowed with strength or power. This line emphasizes the importance of lineage and the strength derived from divine connections.
  • svayā matyā marutaḥ sam mimikṣuḥ: In this line, "svayā matyā" refers to the wisdom or intelligence that comes from within. "Marutaḥ" signifies the winds or the forces of nature, which can be seen as the driving energies of life. "Sam mimikṣuḥ" suggests a gathering or a coming together of these forces. This line highlights the importance of inner wisdom and the harmony between human beings and the natural world.

  • Significance: This mantra encapsulates the essence of creation, the importance of divine lineage, and the harmony between inner wisdom and the forces of nature. It serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of all life and the divine energies that influence our existence.
  • Real-life Application: In practical terms, this mantra can be applied in various aspects of life. For instance, in family settings, it emphasizes the importance of nurturing relationships and understanding the legacy we pass on to future generations. In professional environments, it can inspire teamwork and collaboration, recognizing the strengths that each individual brings to the collective effort. Additionally, it encourages individuals to seek inner wisdom and align themselves with the natural rhythms of life, promoting a balanced and harmonious existence.
