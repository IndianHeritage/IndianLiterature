अधि श्रीयं नि दधुश्चारुम अस्मिन् दिवो यदक्षी अमृताऽकृण्वन् |अध क्षरन्ति सिन्धवो न सृष्टाः प्र निचीर अग्ने अरुषीर अजानन् ||
adhi śriyaṁ ni dadhuś cārum asmin divo yad akṣī amṛtā akṛṇvan | adha kṣaranti sindhavo na sṛṣṭāḥ pra nīcīr agne aruṣīr ajānan ||
  • adhi śriyaṁ ni dadhuś: This phrase can be translated as "they who have placed the supreme glory." Here, "adhi" means supreme or higher, and "śriyaṁ" refers to glory or prosperity. This suggests the importance of striving for higher ideals and values in life.
  • cārum asmin divo: This translates to "in this beautiful sky." The word "cārum" means beautiful, and "divo" refers to the sky or heavens. This can symbolize the vast potential and beauty of the universe, encouraging individuals to appreciate the world around them.
  • yad akṣī amṛtā akṛṇvan: This means "those who have created the immortal eyes." "Akṣī" refers to eyes, and "amṛtā" means immortal or eternal. This can be interpreted as the pursuit of wisdom and enlightenment, suggesting that true vision comes from understanding and knowledge.
  • adha kṣaranti sindhavo: This translates to "the rivers flow downwards." "Kṣaranti" means to flow or move, and "sindhavo" refers to rivers. This can be seen as a metaphor for the natural flow of life and the inevitability of change, reminding us to adapt and go with the flow.
  • na sṛṣṭāḥ pra nīcīr: This means "not created for the lowly." "Sṛṣṭāḥ" refers to creation, and "nīcīr" means lowly or inferior. This suggests that higher ideals and values are not meant for those who do not aspire to them, emphasizing the importance of striving for greatness.
  • agne aruṣīr ajānan: This translates to "O Agni, the red one, who knows." "Agni" refers to the fire god, symbolizing energy and transformation, while "aruṣīr" means red, often associated with vitality. This invokes the idea of seeking guidance from a higher power or source of energy in our pursuits.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda emphasizes the pursuit of higher ideals, the beauty of the universe, the importance of wisdom, and the natural flow of life. It encourages individuals to strive for greatness and seek guidance from higher powers. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various ways, such as in personal development, leadership, and community building. By aspiring to higher values and understanding the world around us, we can create a more meaningful and fulfilling life.
