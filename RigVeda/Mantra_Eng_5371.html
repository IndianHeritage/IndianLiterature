नū इन्द्र राये वरिवस कृधī न ā ते मनो ववृत्याम मग्हाय |गॊमद अश्वावद रथवद व्यन्तो यūयम पात स्वस्तिभिः सदा नः ||
nū indra rāye varivas kṛdhī na ā te mano vavṛtyāma maghāya | gomad aśvāvad rathavad vyanto yūyam pāta svastibhiḥ sadā naḥ ||
  • nū indra rāye varivas kṛdhī: This phrase can be translated as "O Indra, may you be pleased with our offerings." Indra is a prominent deity in the Rigveda, often associated with rain, thunder, and war. In real life, this can be likened to seeking favor from a higher power or authority in various aspects of life, such as asking for blessings before an important event or decision.
  • na ā te mano vavṛtyāma maghāya: This translates to "May our minds not stray from your greatness." It emphasizes the importance of focus and devotion. In everyday life, this can be seen in the context of maintaining dedication to one's goals or values, ensuring that distractions do not lead one away from their purpose.
  • gomad aśvāvad rathavad vyanto: This part means "With wealth like cows, horses, and chariots." Cows and horses symbolize prosperity and strength in Vedic culture. In modern terms, this can represent the various resources and assets one accumulates, such as financial stability, education, and skills, which contribute to personal and professional growth.
  • yūyam pāta svastibhiḥ sadā naḥ: This translates to "May you protect us always with auspiciousness." It reflects a plea for safety and well-being. In real life, this can be related to the desire for protection and good fortune in our daily lives, whether it be through community support, family, or spiritual beliefs.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a deep reverence for divine protection and guidance. It signifies the importance of seeking blessings, maintaining focus on one's goals, and recognizing the value of resources in achieving prosperity. In real life, this mantra can be applicable in various scenarios, such as during rituals, personal reflections, or even in moments of decision-making where one seeks clarity and support from a higher power. It serves as a reminder to remain grounded in one's values while striving for success and well-being.
