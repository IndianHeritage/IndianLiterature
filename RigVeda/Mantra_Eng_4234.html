ते हि सत्याः ऋतस्पृश ऋतावानो जन-जनैः | सुनीतासः सुदानवोऽंहोश्चिदुरुचक्रयः ||
te hi satyā ṛtaspṛśa ṛtāvāno jane-jane | sunīthāsaḥ sudānavo 'ṁhoś cid urucakrayaḥ ||
  • te hi satyā: This phrase translates to "they are indeed the truth." It emphasizes the importance of truthfulness and integrity in life. In real life, this can be seen in the actions of individuals who uphold honesty in their personal and professional relationships, fostering trust and respect.
  • ṛtaspṛśa: This term means "those who touch the truth." It signifies the connection between individuals and the ultimate reality or cosmic order (ṛta). For example, scientists who seek to uncover the truths of nature through research and experimentation embody this principle, as they strive to understand the universe's laws.
  • ṛtāvāno jane-jane: This translates to "the one who possesses truth in every person." It suggests that truth is inherent in all individuals. In a practical context, this can be observed in community leaders who inspire others to act with integrity and honesty, promoting a culture of truthfulness within their communities.
  • sunīthāsaḥ: This word means "those who are well-directed." It refers to individuals who are guided by wisdom and righteousness. For instance, a mentor who provides guidance to students, helping them navigate their educational and personal challenges, exemplifies this quality.
  • sudānavo: This term means "those who are good in their actions." It highlights the importance of performing good deeds and contributing positively to society. An example can be seen in volunteers who dedicate their time to help the less fortunate, demonstrating compassion and kindness.
  • 'ṁhoś cid urucakrayaḥ: This phrase can be interpreted as "those who are powerful and have a broad vision." It signifies individuals who possess strength and insight. Leaders who make impactful decisions for the betterment of society, such as environmental activists advocating for sustainable practices, embody this idea.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of truth, integrity, and righteous action in human life. It encourages individuals to seek and uphold truth, act with good intentions, and guide others towards a path of wisdom and compassion. In real-life scenarios, these principles can be applied in various contexts, such as personal relationships, community service, and leadership roles. By embodying these values, individuals contribute to a more harmonious and truthful society, ultimately leading to collective well-being and progress.
