त्रीण्य एक उरुगायो वि चक्रमे यत्र देवासो मदन्ति ||
trīṇy eka urugāyo vi cakrame yatra devāso madanti ||
  • Mantra: "trīṇy eka urugāyo vi cakrame yatra devāso madanti"
  • Translation: "Three are the one who moves in many ways; where the gods rejoice."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • trīṇy: Three
    • eka: One
    • urugāyo: Moves in many ways or has many forms
    • vi: In various ways
    • cakrame: Moves or revolves
    • yatra: Where
    • devāso: Gods or divine beings
    • madanti: Rejoice or are delighted
  • Meaning and Explanation:
    • This mantra speaks of the concept of unity in diversity. The phrase "Three are the one" suggests that while there may be multiple forms or manifestations, they all originate from a singular source.
    • The term "urugāyo" indicates the adaptability and versatility of this singular entity, which can take on various forms and functions, much like how water can exist as ice, liquid, or vapor.
    • The mention of "where the gods rejoice" implies that this unity and diversity is a source of joy and celebration among the divine, suggesting that harmony and balance are essential for happiness.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In nature, we see this principle in ecosystems where different species coexist and contribute to the overall health of the environment. For instance, bees, flowers, and trees all play distinct roles but are interconnected, showcasing the idea of "one in many."
    • In human society, diverse cultures and communities can come together to create a vibrant society. For example, cities like New York or London thrive on the contributions of various ethnic groups, each bringing unique traditions and perspectives, yet all contributing to a shared identity.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its reminder of the importance of unity amidst diversity. It encourages us to recognize and celebrate the different roles and forms that exist in our lives and the world around us.
    • In practical terms, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as promoting inclusivity in workplaces, fostering collaboration in teams, and appreciating the richness of cultural diversity in communities.
    • Ultimately, it teaches us that true joy and fulfillment come from embracing the interconnectedness of all beings and recognizing that, despite our differences, we are part of a greater whole.
