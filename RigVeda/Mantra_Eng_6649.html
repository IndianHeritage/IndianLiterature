यद्वाभिपित्वे असुराः ऋतं यते चर्धिर्येमा विदाशुषे |वयं तद्वो वसवो विश्ववेदस उप स्थेयाम मध्य आ ||
yad vābhipitve asurā ṛtaṁ yate chardir yema vi dāśuṣe | vayaṁ tad vo vasavo viśvavedasa upa stheyāma madhya ā ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yad: whatever
    • vā: indeed
    • abhipitve: in the presence of
    • asurā: the powerful beings (often interpreted as deities or divine forces)
    • ṛtaṁ: truth, order, or cosmic law
    • yate: that which is sought
    • chardir: energy or strength
    • yema: we may attain
    • vi: in various ways
    • dāśuṣe: to the one who gives
    • vayaṁ: we
    • tad: that
    • vo: to you
    • vasavo: the divine beings or forces
    • viśvavedasa: all-knowing
    • upa: near
    • stheyāma: may we stand
    • madhya: in the midst
    • ā: indeed
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks to the pursuit of truth and strength in the presence of divine forces.
    • It emphasizes the importance of seeking knowledge and support from higher powers.
    • The phrase "we may attain" suggests a collective effort towards achieving a common goal.
    • It highlights the significance of standing together in the presence of wisdom and divine guidance.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a team setting, individuals often rely on each other's strengths and knowledge to achieve a common objective, much like the collective pursuit mentioned in the mantra.
    • In spiritual practices, individuals seek guidance from higher powers or deities, reflecting the idea of standing in the presence of divine forces.
    • In community service, people come together to support one another, embodying the essence of unity and shared purpose as expressed in the mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of truth, strength, and unity in the pursuit of knowledge and divine guidance.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, such as teamwork, spiritual practices, and community efforts.
    • By embodying the principles of this mantra, individuals and groups can foster a sense of purpose and connection to higher ideals.
