बृहस्पतिर्नः परि पातु पश्चादुतोत्तरस्मादधरादघायोः | इन्द्रः पुरस्तादुत मध्यतो नः सखा सकिभ्यो वरिवः कृणोतु ||
bṛhaspatir naḥ pari pātu paścād utottarasmād adharād aghāyoḥ | indraḥ purastād uta madhyato naḥ sakhā sakhibhyo varivaḥ kṛṇotu ||
  • Mantra: "bṛhaspatir naḥ pari pātu paścād utottarasmād adharād aghāyoḥ | indraḥ purastād uta madhyato naḥ sakhā sakhibhyo varivaḥ kṛṇotu ||"
  • Translation: "May Brihaspati protect us from behind, from above, and from below, from all evils. May Indra be our friend in front and in the middle, and may he grant us strength and support from our companions."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • Brihaspati: The deity of wisdom and knowledge, often associated with Jupiter.
    • Naḥ: Our.
    • Pari pātu: Protect us.
    • Paścād: From behind.
    • Utottarasmād: From above.
    • Adharād: From below.
    • Aghāyoḥ: From all evils or obstacles.
    • Indraḥ: The king of gods, representing strength and power.
    • Purastād: In front.
    • Madhyato: In the middle.
    • Sakhā: Friend.
    • Sakhibhyo: From companions.
    • Varivaḥ: Grant us or provide.
    • Kṛṇotu: May he do or may he make.
  • Significance:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of protection and support from various directions—behind, above, below, in front, and in the middle.
    • It highlights the need for wisdom (Brihaspati) and strength (Indra) in overcoming challenges and evils in life.
    • The invocation of these deities signifies a holistic approach to seeking guidance and protection in all aspects of life.
  • Real-Life Application:
    • In daily life, individuals often face challenges that require wisdom and strength. For example, a student preparing for exams may recite this mantra to seek clarity and focus.
    • In professional settings, teams may invoke this mantra to foster collaboration and support among colleagues, ensuring that everyone is protected from negativity and obstacles.
    • During significant life events, such as weddings or new ventures, this mantra can be recited to seek blessings for protection and success from all directions.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of wisdom, strength, and support in navigating life's challenges.
    • By invoking the protection of Brihaspati and Indra, individuals can cultivate a sense of security and confidence in their endeavors.
    • Ultimately, it encourages a proactive approach to seeking guidance and fostering positive relationships in all areas of life.
