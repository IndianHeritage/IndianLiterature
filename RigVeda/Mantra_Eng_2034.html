उभयासो जातवेदः स्याम ते स्तोतारो अग्ने सूरयश्च शर्मणि |वास्वो रायः पुरुषचन्द्रस्य भूयसः प्रजावतः स्वपत्यमस्य शग्धि नः ||
ubhayāso jātavedaḥ syāma te stotāro agne sūrayaś ca śarmaṇi | vasvo rāyaḥ puruścandrasya bhūyasaḥ prajāvataḥ svapatyasya śagdhi naḥ ||
  • Ubhayāso jātavedaḥ: This phrase can be interpreted as "may we be endowed with both kinds of knowledge." In real life, this can refer to the importance of having both practical knowledge and spiritual wisdom. For example, a successful entrepreneur not only needs business acumen but also emotional intelligence to lead effectively.
  • Syāma te stotāro agne: This translates to "may we be your praise-singers, O Agni." Agni, the fire god, symbolizes energy and transformation. In a modern context, this can relate to how we honor and respect the forces that drive our lives, such as our passions and ambitions. For instance, a person who dedicates their life to a cause, like environmental conservation, embodies this praise of Agni through their actions.
  • Sūrayaś ca śarmaṇi: This means "and may we find protection and prosperity." In everyday life, this can be seen in the pursuit of safety and well-being, whether it’s through financial stability, health, or community support. For example, a family that invests in health insurance is seeking the protection and prosperity mentioned in this mantra.
  • Vasvo rāyaḥ puruścandrasya: This phrase can be interpreted as "may we attain the wealth of the sun and the moon." This symbolizes the balance of material and spiritual wealth. In practical terms, it can refer to individuals striving for a balanced life, where they seek both financial success and inner peace, like a person who works hard in their career while also dedicating time to meditation and self-care.
  • Bhūyasaḥ prajāvataḥ: This translates to "may we be blessed with abundant progeny." This can be understood as a desire for growth and continuity, whether in a family or in a community. In real life, this can be seen in the efforts of communities to nurture the next generation through education and mentorship programs.
  • Svapatyasya śagdhi naḥ: This means "may we be united in our endeavors." This emphasizes the importance of collaboration and unity in achieving common goals. In a modern context, this can be reflected in teamwork within organizations, where diverse individuals come together to achieve a shared vision, such as a group of scientists working collaboratively on climate change solutions.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking knowledge, honoring transformative forces, and striving for protection, prosperity, and unity. Its significance lies in its holistic approach to life, encouraging individuals to balance material and spiritual pursuits while fostering community and collaboration. In real-life scenarios, these principles can guide personal development, professional success, and social responsibility, ultimately leading to a more harmonious existence.
