ओ श्रुष्टिर विदथ्या सम एतु प्रतिस्तोमं दधिमहि तुराणाम् |यद अद्य देवः सविता सुवाति स्यामास्य रत्निनो विभागे ||
o śruṣṭir vidathyā3 sam etu prati stomaṁ dadhīmahi turāṇām | yad adya devaḥ savitā suvāti syāmāsya ratnino vibhāge ||
  • o śruṣṭir vidathyā3 sam etu prati stomaṁ dadhīmahi turāṇām
    • o: An invocation, often used to call upon divine presence or consciousness.
    • śruṣṭir: Refers to creation or manifestation, indicating the act of bringing forth.
    • vidathyā: Suggests the arrangement or order of things, often associated with divine planning.
    • sam etu: Implies a coming together or unity, suggesting harmony in creation.
    • prati stomaṁ: Refers to the praise or hymn that is directed towards the divine.
    • dadhīmahi: Means "we meditate" or "we contemplate," indicating a focus on the divine.
    • turāṇām: Refers to the swift or rapid, suggesting the urgency or immediacy of the divine presence.
  • yad adya devaḥ savitā suvāti syāmāsya ratnino vibhāge
    • yad: Means "that" or "which," indicating a specific focus or subject.
    • adya: Refers to "today," emphasizing the present moment.
    • devaḥ: Means "god" or "divine being," referring to a higher power.
    • savitā: Refers to the Sun God, symbolizing illumination, life, and energy.
    • suvāti: Means "may it prosper" or "may it be well," indicating a wish for well-being.
    • syāmāsya: Refers to "may we be" or "let us be," suggesting a collective aspiration.
    • ratnino: Means "of the jewels" or "of the treasures," symbolizing valuable aspects of life.
    • vibhāge: Refers to "in the distribution" or "in the sharing," indicating the importance of sharing blessings.

Conclusion:

This mantra emphasizes the importance of creation, unity, and divine presence in our lives. It calls upon the divine to bless us with prosperity and well-being, particularly in the context of the Sun God, who represents life and energy. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied during rituals, meditation, or moments of reflection to invoke positivity and harmony. It serves as a reminder to appreciate the treasures in our lives and to share them with others, fostering a sense of community and interconnectedness.
