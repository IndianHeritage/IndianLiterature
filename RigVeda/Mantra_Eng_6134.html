माकिर एनā पथā गाद येनेमे यान्ति चेदयः |अन्यो नेत सूरीर ओहते भूридावत्तरो जनः ||
mākir enā pathā gād yeneme yanti cedayaḥ | anyo net sūrir ohate bhūridāvattaro janaḥ ||
  • mākir enā pathā: This phrase can be translated as "the wise one on this path." Here, "mākir" refers to a wise or knowledgeable person, while "pathā" means the path or way. In real life, this can be likened to individuals who guide others through their wisdom, such as teachers or mentors who help students navigate their educational journeys.
  • gād yeneme yanti cedayaḥ: This part translates to "who leads others along the way." "Gād" implies leading or guiding, and "yanti cedayaḥ" refers to those who follow or are led. An example of this can be seen in leadership roles in organizations where leaders inspire and guide their teams towards achieving common goals.
  • anyo net sūrir ohate: This translates to "another wise one who is not obstructed." "Anyo" means another, "net" refers to a leader or guide, and "sūrir" means wise. This can be related to the idea of collaboration, where different leaders or wise individuals come together to support each other without hindrance, much like how different departments in a company work together to achieve success.
  • bhūridāvattaro janaḥ: This phrase means "the one who is most generous and capable." "Bhūridāvattaro" refers to someone who is abundant in giving and has great capabilities, while "janaḥ" means people or individuals. In a real-life context, this can be seen in philanthropists who use their resources and knowledge to uplift communities and support those in need.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of wisdom, guidance, collaboration, and generosity in life. It signifies that a wise leader not only guides others but also collaborates with fellow wise individuals to create a supportive environment. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various contexts such as education, leadership, and community service, where the collective efforts of knowledgeable and generous individuals can lead to significant positive change. The mantra serves as a reminder of the value of wisdom and the impact of collaborative efforts in achieving common goals.
