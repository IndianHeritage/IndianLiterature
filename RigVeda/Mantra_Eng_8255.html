या उग्रेभ्यश्चिदोजीयां चूरिभ्यश्चिच्चूरतरः |भूरिदाभ्यश्चिन मंहीयान् ||
ya ugrebhyaś cid ojīyāñ chūrebhyaś cic chūrataraḥ | bhūridābhyaś cin maṁhīyān ||
  • Mantra: "ya ugrebhyaś cid ojīyāñ chūrebhyaś cic chūrataraḥ | bhūridābhyaś cin maṁhīyān ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ya: who
    • ugrebhyaś: from the powerful or fierce
    • cid: indeed
    • ojīyāñ: the one who is full of energy or strength
    • chūrebhyaś: from the swift or quick
    • cic: indeed
    • chūrataraḥ: the one who is swifter
    • bhūridābhyaś: from the abundant or generous
    • cin: indeed
    • maṁhīyān: the one who is most revered or esteemed
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of a divine entity or force that is both powerful and swift.
    • The phrase "from the powerful or fierce" suggests a source of strength that can overcome challenges.
    • The mention of "the one who is full of energy" indicates a dynamic force that is essential for progress and success.
    • By referring to "the one who is swifter," it emphasizes the importance of speed and agility in achieving goals.
    • The term "from the abundant" highlights the idea of generosity and the importance of sharing resources.
    • Finally, "the one who is most revered" signifies the ultimate respect and honor given to this divine force.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be applied to the qualities of a successful leader who is both powerful in decision-making and swift in execution.
    • In sports, athletes often embody the qualities of energy and speed, striving to be the best in their field, much like the revered figures in this mantra.
    • In personal development, individuals who harness their inner strength and act quickly on opportunities can achieve great success, reflecting the essence of this mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the qualities that lead to success: strength, speed, generosity, and reverence.
    • It encourages individuals to cultivate these attributes in their lives, whether in personal endeavors, professional pursuits, or community involvement.
    • By embodying the essence of this mantra, one can navigate challenges effectively and achieve greatness in various aspects of life.
