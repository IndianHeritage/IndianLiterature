नेषि णो यथा पुराणेन: शूर मण्यसे |विश्वानि पूरोर अप पर्षि वह्निर आस वह्निर नो अच् छा ||
neṣi ṇo yathā purānenāḥ śūra manyase | viśvāni pūror apa parṣi vahnir āsā vahnir no accha ||
  • neṣi ṇo yathā purānenāḥ: This phrase can be translated as "Lead us as in the ancient times." Here, the term "neṣi" refers to guidance or leadership, while "purānenāḥ" indicates a connection to the wisdom and practices of the past. This suggests a desire to draw upon the knowledge and experiences of previous generations.
  • śūra manyase: This translates to "You who are considered brave." The word "śūra" means brave or valiant, and "manyase" implies being esteemed or regarded. This part of the mantra acknowledges the valor and strength of those who have come before us, encouraging us to embody similar qualities in our lives.
  • viśvāni pūror apa parṣi: This can be interpreted as "May all the energies of the universe be favorable to us." "Viśvāni" refers to all or universal, "pūror" means of the past, and "apa parṣi" suggests a request for blessings or support. This line emphasizes the interconnectedness of all beings and the importance of seeking harmony with the universe.
  • vahnir āsā vahnir no accha: This translates to "May the fire (energy) be with us, may it not abandon us." "Vahnir" refers to fire, which symbolizes energy, passion, and transformation. The repetition of "vahnir" underscores the significance of maintaining this energy in our lives, ensuring that we remain motivated and driven.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a profound message about seeking guidance from the wisdom of the past, embracing bravery, and fostering a harmonious relationship with the universe. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various contexts, such as:

  • Personal Development: Individuals can reflect on the teachings and experiences of their ancestors to navigate challenges in their lives, drawing strength from their legacies.
  • Community Leadership: Leaders can embody the qualities of bravery and wisdom, guiding their communities with a sense of purpose and connection to their history.
  • Environmental Awareness: The call for universal energies encourages a collective responsibility towards nature, promoting sustainable practices that honor the earth.

Overall, this mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of heritage, courage, and the continuous pursuit of energy and motivation in our lives.
