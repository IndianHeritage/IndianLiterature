मन्त्र: कद धिष्ण्यासु वृधसानो अग्ने कद वाताय प्रतवसे शुभंये | परिज्मने नासत्याय क्षे ब्रवः कद अग्ने रुद्राय नृघ्ने ||
kad dhiṣṇyāsu vṛdhasāno agne kad vātāya pratavase śubhaṁye | parijmane nāsatyāya kṣe bravaḥ kad agne rudrāya nṛghne ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • kad - when
    • dhiṣṇyāsu - in the dwelling places
    • vṛdhasāno - the one who is strong
    • agne - O Agni (the fire god)
    • kad - when
    • vātāya - to the winds
    • pratavase - may you come
    • śubhaṁye - auspiciously
    • parijmane - in the gathering
    • nāsatyāya - to the Nasatyas (the twin deities)
    • kṣe - in the field
    • bravaḥ - we call upon
    • kad - when
    • agne - O Agni
    • rudrāya - to Rudra (the storm god)
    • nṛghne - the one who is fierce
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes Agni, the fire god, and expresses a desire for auspiciousness in various aspects of life.
    • It highlights the importance of strength and protection in our dwelling places, symbolizing safety and security.
    • The mention of winds signifies the dynamic nature of life and the need for adaptability.
    • Calling upon the Nasatyas emphasizes the importance of duality and balance in life, as they represent health and well-being.
    • Rudra's invocation suggests a recognition of the fierce and transformative aspects of nature, urging us to embrace change.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a household, ensuring a safe and secure environment for family members can be seen as a reflection of the strength invoked in the mantra.
    • In professional settings, adaptability to changing circumstances, much like the winds, is crucial for success.
    • Health and wellness practices, such as yoga and meditation, can be linked to the invocation of the Nasatyas, promoting balance in life.
    • Embracing challenges and changes in life, akin to the fierce nature of Rudra, can lead to personal growth and transformation.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of strength, adaptability, balance, and transformation in our lives.
    • Its significance lies in the holistic approach it advocates, encouraging individuals to seek auspiciousness in their surroundings while being prepared for change.
    • In real-life scenarios, applying the principles of this mantra can lead to a more harmonious and fulfilling existence, fostering resilience and well-being.
