तां वां गीरभिर विपण्यवः प्रयस्वन्तो हवामहे | मेधसातां सनीष्यवः ||
tā vāṁ gīrbhir vipanyavaḥ prayasvanto havāmahe | medhasātā saniṣyavaḥ ||
  • tā vāṁ gīrbhir vipanyavaḥ: This phrase can be translated as "We invoke you with our hymns." Here, "tā" refers to the divine or the deity being addressed, "vāṁ" means "you," "gīrbhir" refers to "hymns" or "words," and "vipanyavaḥ" implies "we are invoking" or "we are calling upon." This highlights the importance of prayer and the power of words in connecting with the divine.
  • prayasvanto havāmahe: This translates to "We strive to invoke you." "Prayasvanto" means "striving" or "making an effort," and "havāmahe" means "we invoke" or "we call upon." This emphasizes the active role of individuals in seeking divine blessings through their efforts and intentions.
  • medhasātā saniṣyavaḥ: This can be interpreted as "May we be endowed with wisdom." "Medhasātā" refers to "wisdom" or "intellect," and "saniṣyavaḥ" means "may we attain" or "may we be blessed with." This part of the mantra expresses a desire for enlightenment and understanding, which is essential for personal and spiritual growth.

  • Real-Life Example: In a modern context, this mantra can be related to the practice of mindfulness and meditation. Just as the mantra calls upon the divine with intention and effort, individuals today strive to connect with their inner selves through meditation, seeking clarity and wisdom in their lives.
  • Significance: The mantra emphasizes the power of words and intentions in spiritual practice. It serves as a reminder that invoking the divine requires sincere effort and a desire for wisdom. In everyday life, this can translate to the importance of setting intentions in our actions, whether in personal relationships, professional endeavors, or spiritual pursuits.
  • Application: This mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as during prayer, meditation, or even in moments of decision-making. By invoking the divine with sincerity and striving for wisdom, individuals can navigate challenges with greater clarity and purpose.

  • Conclusion: The mantra "tā vāṁ gīrbhir vipanyavaḥ prayasvanto havāmahe | medhasātā saniṣyavaḥ" encapsulates the essence of seeking divine connection through words and effort. It highlights the importance of wisdom in our lives and encourages individuals to actively pursue enlightenment. By integrating the principles of this mantra into daily life, one can foster a deeper connection with the divine and enhance personal growth.
