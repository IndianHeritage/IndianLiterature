आ नो अग्नि सुचेतुना रयिं विश्वायुपोषसम् |मार्दीकं धेहि जीवसे ||
ā no agne sucetunā rayiṁ viśvāyupoṣasam | mārḍīkaṁ dhehi jīvase ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā: towards
    • no: our
    • agne: O Agni (the divine fire)
    • sucetunā: with clarity or purity
    • rayiṁ: wealth or prosperity
    • viśvāyupoṣasam: for all beings or universally
    • mārḍīkaṁ: the essence or the best
    • dhehi: grant or bestow
    • jīvase: for living or for life
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Agni, the fire god, who symbolizes energy, transformation, and purity.
    • The invocation is for Agni to bestow wealth and prosperity that is clear and beneficial for all beings.
    • The term "mārḍīkaṁ" suggests a desire for the best or the essence of wealth, indicating a pursuit of not just material wealth but also spiritual and emotional richness.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, this mantra can be applied when a team seeks clarity and purpose in their projects, aiming for success that benefits not just the company but also the community.
    • In personal life, individuals can reflect on this mantra when making decisions about their careers or relationships, ensuring that their choices lead to prosperity that is wholesome and beneficial for themselves and others.
    • During community gatherings or rituals, this mantra can be recited to invoke collective prosperity and harmony, emphasizing the importance of shared wealth and well-being.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its holistic approach to wealth and prosperity, urging individuals and communities to seek clarity and purity in their pursuits.
    • It highlights the interconnectedness of all beings, reminding us that true wealth is not just material but also encompasses emotional and spiritual well-being.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra serves as a guiding principle for ethical living, encouraging us to strive for success that uplifts not only ourselves but also those around us.
