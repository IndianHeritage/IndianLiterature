आ तिष्ठ रथं वृषणं वृषा ते सुतः सोमः परिषिक्त मधूनी |युक्त्वा वृषभ्यां वृषभ क्षितीनां हरिभ्यां याहि प्रवतोप मद्रिक ||
ā tiṣṭha rathaṁ vṛṣaṇaṁ vṛṣā te sutaḥ somaḥ pariṣiktā madhūni | yuktvā vṛṣabhyāṁ vṛṣabha kṣitīnāṁ haribhyāṁ yāhi pravatopa madrik ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā tiṣṭha: "Arise" or "Stand up"
    • rathaṁ: "Chariot"
    • vṛṣaṇaṁ: "Strong" or "Powerful"
    • vṛṣā: "Strength" or "Might"
    • te: "Your"
    • sutaḥ: "Son" or "Offspring"
    • somaḥ: "Soma" (a sacred drink)
    • pariṣiktā: "Anointed" or "Blessed"
    • madhūni: "Sweet" or "Honey"
    • yuktvā: "Having united" or "Joined"
    • vṛṣabhyāṁ: "With the strong ones"
    • vṛṣabha: "Bull" or "Strength"
    • kṣitīnāṁ: "Of the earth" or "Earthly"
    • haribhyāṁ: "With the horses" or "Equine"
    • yāhi: "Go" or "Proceed"
    • pravatopa: "To the place of action"
    • madrik: "The one who is powerful" or "Mighty"
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra calls for the awakening of strength and power, symbolized by the chariot and the bull.
    • The chariot represents the journey of life, where one must harness their inner strength to move forward.
    • The mention of "Soma" signifies the importance of spiritual nourishment and the sweetness of life.
    • It emphasizes unity and collaboration, as seen in the phrase "having united with the strong ones," which can be interpreted as working together with others to achieve common goals.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a team setting, this mantra can be applied to motivate individuals to come together, harness their strengths, and work towards a shared objective, much like a sports team striving for victory.
    • In personal development, it encourages individuals to rise up, embrace their inner strength, and pursue their goals with determination, akin to someone overcoming obstacles to achieve their dreams.
    • In community service, the mantra can inspire collective action, where individuals unite to support a cause, reflecting the idea of "going to the place of action" for the betterment of society.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the importance of strength, unity, and purpose in our lives.
    • Its significance lies in its ability to inspire individuals to rise, collaborate, and take action, whether in personal endeavors, professional settings, or community initiatives.
    • By embodying the principles of this mantra, we can navigate the challenges of life with resilience and determination, ultimately leading to personal and collective growth.
