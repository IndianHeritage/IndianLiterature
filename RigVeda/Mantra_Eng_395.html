न्य आविध्यद इल्लीबिशस्य दृढा विशृङ्गिणम् अभिनच छुष्णम् इन्द्रः |यावत तरो मगवन् यावद ओजो वज्रेण शत्रुम अवधीः पृथन्युम् ||
ny āvidhyad ilībiśasya dṛḻhā vi śṛṅgiṇam abhinac chuṣṇam indraḥ | yāvat taro maghavan yāvad ojo vajreṇa śatrum avadhīḥ pṛtanyum ||
  • Mantra: "ny āvidhyad ilībiśasya dṛḻhā vi śṛṅgiṇam abhinac chuṣṇam indraḥ | yāvat taro maghavan yāvad ojo vajreṇa śatrum avadhīḥ pṛtanyum ||"
  • Translation: This mantra speaks of the strength and power of Indra, the king of gods, who is invoked to overcome obstacles and enemies with his mighty weapon, the thunderbolt (vajra).
  • Word Breakdown:
    • ny āvidhyad: "not to be hindered" or "not to be obstructed."
    • ilībiśasya: "of the powerful" or "of the strong."
    • dṛḻhā: "firm" or "steadfast."
    • vi śṛṅgiṇam: "with the horn" or "with the strength."
    • abhinac: "to overcome" or "to conquer."
    • chuṣṇam: "the one who is nourished" or "the one who is sustained."
    • indraḥ: "Indra," the god of rain and thunderstorms, symbolizing power and strength.
    • yāvat: "as long as" or "until."
    • taro: "of the trees" or "of the strong."
    • maghavan: "the bountiful" or "the generous."
    • ojo: "strength" or "energy."
    • vajreṇa: "with the thunderbolt."
    • śatrum: "enemy" or "adversary."
    • avadhīḥ: "to strike down" or "to defeat."
    • pṛtanyum: "the earth" or "the ground."
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a competitive environment, such as a workplace, individuals often face challenges and obstacles. This mantra can be a source of inspiration to remain steadfast and overcome difficulties with strength and determination.
    • In sports, athletes invoke the spirit of Indra to channel their inner strength and resilience, pushing through challenges to achieve victory.
    • In personal life, when facing adversities, such as health issues or financial struggles, one can draw upon the essence of this mantra to find the courage to confront and overcome these challenges.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its invocation of Indra's strength and power to overcome obstacles and adversaries. It serves as a reminder that with determination and resilience, one can face challenges head-on and emerge victorious. In real-life scenarios, whether in personal struggles, professional challenges, or competitive environments, this mantra encourages individuals to harness their inner strength and confront difficulties with confidence and courage.
