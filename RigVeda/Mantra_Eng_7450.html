ऋतेन देवः सविता शमायत ऋतस्य श्रिङ्गम उर्वियā वि पप्रथे |ऋतं सासाह महि चित पृथन्यतो मा नो वि यौष्टं सख्या मुमोचतम् ||
ṛtena devaḥ savitā śamāyata ṛtasya śṛṅgam urviyā vi paprathe | ṛtaṁ sāsāha mahi cit pṛtanyato mā no vi yauṣṭaṁ sakhyā mumocatam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ṛtena: By truth or righteousness
    • devaḥ: The divine or gods
    • savitā: The sun or the source of life
    • śamāyata: May bring peace or harmony
    • ṛtasya: Of truth or cosmic order
    • śṛṅgam: The peak or summit
    • urviyā: With vastness or expansiveness
    • vi: In various ways
    • paprathe: Has spread or manifested
    • ṛtam: The truth or cosmic order
    • sāsāha: With strength or power
    • mahi: Great or vast
    • cit: Consciousness or awareness
    • pṛtanyato: From the earth or the ground
    • mā: May not
    • no: Us
    • vi yauṣṭaṁ: Be deprived of strength
    • sakhyā: By friendship or companionship
    • mumocatam: May liberate or free
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of truth and righteousness as foundational elements of life.
    • The invocation of the divine (devaḥ) and the sun (savitā) suggests a connection to higher powers that govern the universe.
    • The phrase "may bring peace" indicates a desire for harmony in the world, which can be achieved through adherence to truth.
    • The mention of "the peak of truth" signifies the ultimate goal of human existence, which is to reach a state of enlightenment and understanding.
    • The vastness (urviyā) implies that truth and righteousness are not limited but are expansive and universal.
    • The call for liberation from deprivation of strength (vi yauṣṭaṁ) highlights the importance of community and friendship in overcoming challenges.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace, fostering a culture of honesty and integrity can lead to a harmonious environment where employees feel valued and empowered.
    • In personal relationships, practicing truthfulness can strengthen bonds and create a sense of trust and security.
    • In community settings, collective efforts towards truth and righteousness can lead to social justice and equality.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the significance of truth and righteousness in our lives.
    • It encourages individuals to seek harmony and peace through their actions and relationships.
    • By embodying these principles, we can contribute to a more just and compassionate world, reflecting the cosmic order that the mantra speaks of.
