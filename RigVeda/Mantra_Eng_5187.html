नहि ग्रभायारणः सुशेवं अन्योदaryo मनसा मन्तवाऽउ |अधाऽचिद ओकः पुनरित स एत्य आ नो वाज्य अभिषाल् एतु नव्यः ||
nahi grabhāyāraṇaḥ suśevo 'nyodaryo manasā mantavā u | adhā cid okaḥ punar it sa ety ā no vājy abhīṣāḻ etu navyaḥ ||
  • Translation: The mantra can be translated as: "Indeed, the one who is not grasped by the mind, who is well-versed in the essence of things, may come to us with his blessings, and may we be endowed with new insights."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • nahi: Indeed, not
    • grabhāyāraṇaḥ: One who is not grasped or understood
    • suśevo: Well-versed, knowledgeable
    • anyodaryo: May come to us
    • manasā: By the mind
    • mantavā: One who is insightful or wise
    • adhā: If
    • cid: Indeed
    • okaḥ: Blessings or insights
    • punar: Again, anew
    • it: Thus, in this way
    • sa: He, that one
    • ety: May come
    • ā: To us
    • no: Us
    • vājy: Endowed, blessed
    • abhīṣāḻ: With new insights
    • etu: May come
    • navyaḥ: New, fresh
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In the context of education, this mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking knowledge that transcends mere memorization. For instance, a student who understands the underlying principles of physics rather than just memorizing formulas is embodying the essence of this mantra.
    • In professional settings, a leader who is not just focused on the immediate tasks but is also visionary and insightful can inspire their team to innovate and think creatively, reflecting the wisdom of the mantra.
    • In personal development, individuals who seek self-awareness and deeper understanding of their emotions and thoughts can find new insights that lead to personal growth, aligning with the call for blessings of new insights.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for deeper understanding and insight beyond superficial knowledge. It encourages individuals to seek wisdom that is not easily grasped by the mind but is essential for true growth and enlightenment. In real-life scenarios, whether in education, leadership, or personal development, the application of this mantra can lead to transformative experiences, fostering a mindset that values depth over breadth and encourages continuous learning and insight. By inviting blessings of new insights, we open ourselves to the possibilities of growth and understanding in various aspects of life.
