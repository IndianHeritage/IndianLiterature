इषे पवस्व धारयामानो मृज्यमानो मनिषिभिः |इन्दो रुचाभि गा इहि ||
iṣe pavasva dhārayā mṛjyamāno manīṣibhiḥ | indo rucābhi gā ihi ||
  • iṣe pavasva: This phrase can be translated as "may you be empowered" or "may you be strengthened." It signifies the invocation of energy and power, encouraging individuals to harness their inner strength.
  • dhārayā: This word means "to hold" or "to sustain." It emphasizes the importance of stability and support in one's life, suggesting that we should hold onto our values and principles.
  • mṛjyamāno: This term translates to "being purified" or "being cleansed." It indicates the process of purification, both physically and spiritually, which is essential for personal growth and development.
  • manīṣibhiḥ: This word refers to "wise individuals" or "those who have knowledge." It highlights the importance of wisdom and guidance from learned individuals in our journey of life.
  • indo: This term can be interpreted as "the moon" or "the divine." It represents a source of light and inspiration, guiding us through darkness.
  • rucābhi: This word means "with brilliance" or "with radiance." It signifies the beauty and positivity that comes from enlightenment and knowledge.
  • gā ihi: This phrase translates to "come forth" or "move forward." It encourages action and progress, urging individuals to take steps towards their goals and aspirations.

Conclusion: The mantra "iṣe pavasva dhārayā mṛjyamāno manīṣibhiḥ | indo rucābhi gā ihi ||" encapsulates the essence of empowerment, stability, purification, wisdom, and progress. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as personal development, leadership, and community building. For instance, in a workplace setting, leaders can embody the principles of this mantra by empowering their team members, providing guidance, and fostering a culture of continuous learning and improvement. Ultimately, this mantra serves as a reminder to seek strength from within, uphold our values, and strive for enlightenment as we navigate through life's challenges.
