दिव्याऽआपोऽभि यद् एनम् आयन दृतिं न शुष्कं सरसी शयानम् |गवाम् अह न मायुर वत्सिनीनाम् मण्डूकानां वाग्नुर अत्र सम एति ||
divyā āpo abhi yad enam āyan dṛtiṁ na śuṣkaṁ sarasī śayānam | gavām aha na māyur vatsinīnām maṇḍūkānāṁ vagnur atrā sam eti ||
  • Divyā āpo abhi yad enam āyan dṛtiṁ na śuṣkaṁ sarasī śayānam:
    • Divyā āpo: Refers to the divine waters or celestial waters, symbolizing purity and life-giving properties.
    • Abhi: Means 'towards' or 'in the direction of', indicating a movement or flow.
    • Yad enam āyan: Translates to 'that which brings forth' or 'that which leads to', suggesting a source of nourishment or support.
    • Dṛtiṁ: Means 'steadfastness' or 'courage', emphasizing the importance of resilience in life.
    • Na śuṣkaṁ: Means 'not dry', indicating the necessity of sustenance and vitality.
    • Sarasī śayānam: Refers to 'the lake or water body', symbolizing a place of abundance and tranquility.
  • Gavām aha na māyur vatsinīnām maṇḍūkānāṁ vagnur atrā sam eti:
    • Gavām: Refers to 'cows', which are often seen as symbols of wealth and nourishment in Vedic culture.
    • Aha: Means 'I' or 'my', indicating personal connection or ownership.
    • Na māyur: Translates to 'not a peacock', suggesting a contrast between the humble and the ostentatious.
    • Vatsinīnām: Refers to 'the calves', symbolizing new beginnings and growth.
    • Maṇḍūkānāṁ: Means 'frogs', which can symbolize transformation and adaptability.
    • Vagnur atrā sam eti: Indicates 'the fire here is present', suggesting the presence of energy and vitality in the environment.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of divine nourishment and the need for resilience in life.
    • It highlights the significance of maintaining a connection with nature and the resources it provides, such as water and livestock.
    • In real life, this can be applied to the importance of sustainable living, where we respect and utilize natural resources wisely.
    • The mantra also encourages us to embrace humility and growth, reminding us that true wealth lies in nurturing relationships and fostering a supportive community.
    • Overall, it serves as a reminder to seek balance, resilience, and connection with the world around us.
