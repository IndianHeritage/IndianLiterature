द्रप्सम् अपश्यं विषुणे चरन्तम् उपह्वरे नद्यो अंशुमत्याः |नभो न कृष्णम् अवतस्थिवांसम् इष्यामि वो वृषणो युध्यताजौ ||
drapsam apaśyaṁ viṣuṇe carantam upahvare nadyo aṁśumatyāḥ | nabho na kṛṣṇam avatasthivāṁsam iṣyāmi vo vṛṣaṇo yudhyatājau ||
  • Mantra: "drapsam apaśyaṁ viṣuṇe carantam upahvare nadyo aṁśumatyāḥ | nabho na kṛṣṇam avatasthivāṁsam iṣyāmi vo vṛṣaṇo yudhyatājau ||"
  • Translation: This mantra speaks of the unseen forces that move through the waters and the heavens, invoking the strength and valor of the divine to aid in overcoming challenges.
  • Word Breakdown:
    • drapsam: Refers to the unseen or hidden aspects of nature.
    • apaśyaṁ: Means "not seen" or "invisible."
    • viṣuṇe: Pertains to the divine or celestial beings.
    • carantam: Indicates movement or action.
    • upahvare: To invoke or call upon.
    • nadyo: Refers to rivers or waters.
    • aṁśumatyāḥ: Associated with light or radiance.
    • nabho: Refers to the sky or heavens.
    • na kṛṣṇam: Not black or dark, symbolizing clarity or purity.
    • avatasthivāṁsam: Refers to the one who resides or stands firm.
    • iṣyāmi: I invoke or call upon.
    • vo: To you (the divine or celestial beings).
    • vṛṣaṇo: Refers to strength or valor.
    • yudhyatājau: To engage in battle or struggle.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In times of personal struggle, such as facing a difficult decision or challenge, individuals may invoke their inner strength and seek guidance from higher powers, similar to the invocation in this mantra.
    • In nature, rivers symbolize the flow of life and the unseen forces that shape our environment, reminding us of the interconnectedness of all things.
    • In a competitive scenario, such as sports or business, individuals often call upon their inner strength and resilience to overcome obstacles, reflecting the essence of this mantra.
  • Conclusion: This mantra emphasizes the importance of recognizing and invoking the unseen forces that guide and support us in our endeavors. It teaches us to seek strength and clarity in challenging times, reminding us that we are not alone in our struggles. The significance of this mantra lies in its applicability to various aspects of life, encouraging individuals to harness their inner strength and connect with the divine for guidance and support.
