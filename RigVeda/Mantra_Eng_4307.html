यच चिद्धि ते गणाः इमे छदायन्ति मघत्तये |परि चिद्वष्टयः दधुर ददतो राधो अह्रयम् सुजाते अश्वसूत्रे ||
yac cid dhi te gaṇā ime chadayanti maghattaye | pari cid vaṣṭayo dadhur dadato rādho ahrayaṁ sujāte aśvasūnṛte ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yac - whatever
    • cid - indeed
    • dhi - thought or intellect
    • te - your
    • gaṇā - groups or assemblies
    • ime - these
    • chadayanti - cover or protect
    • maghattaye - for greatness or abundance
    • pari - around or in all directions
    • cid - indeed
    • vaṣṭayo - the powers or energies
    • dadhur - they have held or maintained
    • dadato - giving or bestowing
    • rādho - wealth or prosperity
    • ahrayaṁ - the one who brings forth
    • sujāte - well-born or noble
    • aśvasūnṛte - in the context of horses or strength
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the divine intellect and the groups that protect and promote abundance.
    • It emphasizes the importance of collective strength and the energies that surround us, which contribute to prosperity.
    • The reference to "well-born" suggests that noble intentions and actions lead to fruitful outcomes.
    • The mention of "horses" symbolizes strength, speed, and the ability to overcome obstacles, which can be applied to various aspects of life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, teamwork and collaboration can be seen as the "groups" that protect and promote the success of a project.
    • In personal life, surrounding oneself with supportive friends and family can lead to a more abundant and fulfilling life.
    • In community initiatives, collective efforts to uplift the community reflect the essence of this mantra, where shared goals lead to prosperity.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of collective strength, noble intentions, and the protective energies that foster abundance.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal relationships to professional environments, emphasizing the importance of collaboration and shared goals.
    • Ultimately, it serves as a reminder that our thoughts, actions, and the company we keep can significantly influence our journey towards prosperity and fulfillment.
