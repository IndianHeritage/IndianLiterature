तां ह त्यद्वर्तिर्यद् अराध्रमुग्रेच्छा धियूहथुः शाश्वद् अश्वैः |मनोजवेभिरिषिरैः शयध्यै परिव्यथिर्दाशुषो मार्त्यस्य ||
tā ha tyad vartir yad aradhram ugretthā dhiya ūhathuḥ śaśvad aśvaiḥ | manojavebhir iṣiraiḥ śayadhyai pari vyathir dāśuṣo martyasya ||
  • tā ha tyad vartir: This phrase can be interpreted as "that which is the essence of existence." It emphasizes the fundamental nature of reality and the interconnectedness of all beings.
  • yad aradhram: This translates to "what is beyond the ordinary." It suggests a pursuit of higher knowledge or understanding that transcends everyday experiences.
  • ugretthā dhiya ūhathuḥ: This means "may the powerful thoughts inspire us." It highlights the importance of strong, positive thoughts in shaping our actions and destinies.
  • śaśvad aśvaiḥ: This can be understood as "with constant energy." It signifies the need for sustained effort and vitality in our endeavors.
  • manojavebhir iṣiraiḥ: This translates to "with the swiftness of the mind." It emphasizes the power of mental agility and quick thinking in overcoming challenges.
  • śayadhyai pari vyathir: This means "to overcome obstacles." It speaks to the necessity of resilience and determination in the face of difficulties.
  • dāśuṣo martyasya: This can be interpreted as "for the benefit of humanity." It underscores the importance of actions that contribute to the welfare of all beings.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda encapsulates the essence of striving for higher knowledge, the power of positive thinking, and the importance of resilience. In real life, it can be applied in various scenarios such as personal development, leadership, and community service. For instance, in a workplace setting, fostering a culture of positive thinking and resilience can lead to improved teamwork and productivity. In personal life, embracing these principles can help individuals navigate challenges and contribute positively to society. Ultimately, the mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of existence and the impact of our thoughts and actions on the world around us.
