विष्वस्य हि प्रेषितो रक्षसि व्रतम् अहेलयन्न उच्चरस्य स्वधा अनु |यद अद्य त्वां सूर्योपब्रवामहि तं नो देवाऽनु मंसीरत क्रतुम् ||
viśvasya hi preṣito rakṣasi vratam aheḻayann uccarasi svadhā anu | yad adya tvā sūryopabravāmahai taṁ no devā anu maṁsīrata kratum ||
  • Translation: "Indeed, you are the one who is sent forth to protect the universe, O Rakṣasi, who is the embodiment of the vow. You rise up with your own essence. Today, we invoke you, O Sun, so that the gods may grant us strength and wisdom."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • viśvasya: of the universe, all-encompassing
    • hi: indeed, certainly
    • preṣito: sent forth, dispatched
    • rakṣasi: protector, guardian
    • vratam: vow, commitment
    • aheḻayann: rising, elevating
    • uccarasi: you rise, you ascend
    • svadhā: essence, self
    • yad: that which
    • adya: today, in this moment
    • tvā: you
    • sūrya: Sun
    • upabravāmahai: we invoke, we call upon
    • taṁ: that
    • no: for us
    • devā: gods, divine beings
    • anu: following, in accordance with
    • maṁsīrata: grant us, bestow upon us
    • kratum: strength, wisdom, resolve
  • Significance: This mantra emphasizes the importance of protection and guidance from divine forces. It acknowledges the role of the Sun as a source of energy and wisdom, invoking its power to bestow strength upon us. The mention of "Rakṣasi" signifies the protective aspect of the divine, suggesting that there are forces in the universe that guard and support us in our endeavors.
  • Real-Life Application: In daily life, this mantra can be applied in various situations where one seeks protection and guidance. For instance:
    • Before embarking on a new venture, such as starting a business, individuals can recite this mantra to seek divine support and protection against challenges.
    • During times of uncertainty or fear, invoking this mantra can provide a sense of security and strength, reminding individuals that they are supported by greater forces.
    • In moments of decision-making, the mantra serves as a reminder to seek wisdom and clarity, aligning one's actions with a higher purpose.
  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking divine protection and wisdom in our lives. By recognizing the interconnectedness of the universe and the support we receive from divine forces, we can navigate our challenges with confidence and strength. It serves as a reminder that we are not alone in our journeys and that invoking the powers of the universe can lead to greater clarity and success.
