रथेन पृथुपाजसा दाश्वांसम् उप गच्छतम् |इन्द्रवायू इहागतम् ||
rathena pṛthupājasā dāśvāṁsam upa gacchatam | indravāyū ihā gatam ||
  • Mantra: "rathena pṛthupājasā dāśvāṁsam upa gacchatam | indravāyū ihā gatam ||"
  • Translation: "May the chariot, with its broad glory, carry the ten horses; may it go forth with the power of Indra and Vayu."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • Rathena: Refers to a chariot, symbolizing movement, progress, and the journey of life.
    • Pṛthupājasā: Means "with broad glory," indicating strength, magnificence, and the ability to overcome obstacles.
    • Dāśvāṁsam: Refers to "ten horses," which can symbolize energy, vitality, and the various forces that drive us forward.
    • Upa gacchatam: Translates to "may it go forth," suggesting a desire for progress and movement towards a goal.
    • Indravāyū: Combines the names of two powerful deities, Indra (the god of rain and thunderstorms) and Vayu (the god of wind), representing strength and the forces of nature.
    • ihā gatam: Means "has come here," indicating presence and the realization of potential.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a personal context, this mantra can be seen as a call for motivation and support in pursuing one's goals, much like how a student prepares for exams with the hope of success.
    • In a professional setting, it can symbolize teamwork and collaboration, where each member contributes their strengths (the horses) to achieve a common objective (the chariot).
    • In a broader societal context, it can represent the collective effort of a community coming together to overcome challenges, akin to how a city mobilizes resources during a natural disaster.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its invocation of strength, unity, and progress. It emphasizes the importance of harnessing collective energy and divine support to move forward in life. In real-life scenarios, whether in personal ambitions, professional endeavors, or community efforts, this mantra serves as a reminder that with the right mindset and collaboration, we can overcome obstacles and achieve our goals. It encourages us to seek the support of greater forces, both within ourselves and in the universe, as we navigate our journeys.
