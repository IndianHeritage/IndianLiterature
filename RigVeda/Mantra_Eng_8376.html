वृषेवं यूथा परि कोशम् अर्षस्य अपाम उपस्थे वृषभः कणिक्रदत् |स इन्द्राय पवसे मत्सरिण्तमो यथा जेषाम समिथे त्वोतयः ||
vṛṣeva yūthā pari kośam arṣasy apām upasthe vṛṣabhaḥ kanikradat | sa indrāya pavase matsarintamo yathā jeṣāma samithe tvotayaḥ ||
  • Mantra: "vṛṣeva yūthā pari kośam arṣasy apām upasthe vṛṣabhaḥ kanikradat | sa indrāya pavase matsarintamo yathā jeṣāma samithe tvotayaḥ ||"
  • Translation: This mantra can be interpreted as a call to the divine, invoking the strength and unity of a group, akin to a herd of bulls, to overcome obstacles and achieve success.
  • Word Breakdown:
    • vṛṣeva: Like a bull, symbolizing strength and power.
    • yūthā: A group or herd, indicating unity and collective strength.
    • pari: Around or surrounding, suggesting protection and support.
    • kośam: A covering or sheath, representing the barriers or challenges faced.
    • arṣasy: Of the heavens or divine, indicating a higher power.
    • apām: Of the waters, symbolizing life and nourishment.
    • upasthe: At the feet or base, suggesting humility and grounding.
    • vṛṣabhaḥ: The bull, again emphasizing strength and leadership.
    • kanikradat: Roaring or making a sound, representing the call to action.
    • sa: He or that, referring to the divine or the collective strength.
    • indrāya: To Indra, the king of gods, symbolizing authority and power.
    • pavase: To purify or cleanse, indicating the need for clarity and focus.
    • matsarintamo: The most envious or competitive, highlighting the challenges faced.
    • yathā: As or like, indicating a comparison or example.
    • jeṣāma: We shall conquer, representing determination and victory.
    • samithe: In the assembly or gathering, emphasizing community and collaboration.
    • tvotayaḥ: Your praises or offerings, indicating devotion and respect.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, a team working together to achieve a common goal can be likened to the herd of bulls, where each member contributes their strength to overcome challenges.
    • In sports, a team that supports each other and works in unison can achieve victory against more formidable opponents, embodying the essence of this mantra.
    • In community service, individuals coming together to support a cause demonstrate the power of collective action, akin to the unity expressed in the mantra.
  • Conclusion: This mantra emphasizes the significance of unity, strength, and collective action in overcoming obstacles. It serves as a reminder that when individuals come together with a common purpose, they can achieve great things. In real-life scenarios, whether in professional, athletic, or community contexts, the principles of collaboration and mutual support highlighted in this mantra can lead to success and fulfillment.
