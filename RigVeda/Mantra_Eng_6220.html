आ नूनं यातम अश्विनाऽ रथेन सूर्यत्वचा |भुजी हिरण्यपेक्षसा कवि गम्भीरचेतसा ||
ā nūnaṁ yātam aśvinā rathena sūryatvacā | bhujī hiraṇyapeśasā kavī gambhīracetasā ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā nūnaṁ: Indeed, surely
    • yātam: You have come
    • aśvinā: O Ashvins (the twin gods of medicine and health)
    • rathena: With the chariot
    • sūryatvacā: With the brilliance of the sun
    • bhujī: Enjoy
    • hiraṇyapeśasā: With golden adornments
    • kavī: O wise one
    • gambhīracetasā: With profound intellect
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the Ashvins, who are revered as divine physicians and bringers of health and prosperity.
    • The phrase "with the chariot" symbolizes the swift and effective means by which the Ashvins deliver their blessings.
    • The "brilliance of the sun" signifies enlightenment, vitality, and the life-giving force that the Ashvins embody.
    • The mention of "golden adornments" suggests wealth, beauty, and the richness of life that comes from divine blessings.
    • Calling upon the "wise one" with "profound intellect" emphasizes the importance of wisdom and understanding in achieving health and prosperity.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern medicine, the Ashvins can be likened to healthcare professionals who work tirelessly to restore health and well-being to individuals.
    • The concept of "chariots" can be seen in the advancements of medical technology, such as ambulances and telemedicine, which facilitate swift access to healthcare.
    • The "brilliance of the sun" can be related to the importance of mental and physical health, where sunlight is known to improve mood and overall well-being.
    • Wealth and prosperity can be represented by the pursuit of education and knowledge, which leads to better job opportunities and financial stability.
    • Wisdom and intellect are crucial in decision-making processes, whether in personal life or in business, where informed choices lead to success.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the divine support available to us in our pursuit of health, wealth, and wisdom.
    • It encourages individuals to seek knowledge and understanding, as these are essential for navigating life's challenges.
    • Incorporating the essence of this mantra into daily life can inspire a proactive approach to health and well-being, fostering a balanced and prosperous existence.
    • Ultimately, the invocation of the Ashvins highlights the interconnectedness of physical health, mental clarity, and material success, urging us to strive for harmony in all aspects of life.
