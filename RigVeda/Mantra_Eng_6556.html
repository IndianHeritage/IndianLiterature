आ त्वा गोभिर इव व्रजं गीरभिर ऋणोम्य अद्रिवः |आ स्मा कामं जारितुर आ मनः पृण ||
ā tvā gobhir iva vrajaṁ gīrbhir ṛṇomy adrivaḥ | ā smā kāmaṁ jaritur ā manaḥ pṛṇa ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā tvā: To you
    • gobhir: With the cows (or wealth)
    • iva: Like
    • vrajaṁ: The pasture or abode
    • gīrbhir: With hymns or words
    • ṛṇomy: I offer or I invoke
    • adrivaḥ: Without obstacles or difficulties
    • ā smā: From us
    • kāmaṁ: Desire or wish
    • jaritur: To be fulfilled or to come to fruition
    • ā manaḥ: To the mind
    • pṛṇa: To be satisfied or to be filled
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra expresses a prayer or invocation to a divine entity, asking for blessings and fulfillment of desires.
    • The imagery of cows symbolizes wealth, prosperity, and nourishment, which are essential for a fulfilling life.
    • The reference to hymns indicates the importance of words and prayers in connecting with the divine.
    • The phrase "without obstacles" suggests a desire for a smooth path in life, free from hindrances.
    • Ultimately, the mantra seeks to align one's desires with the divine will, ensuring that one's mind is satisfied and fulfilled.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be applied when seeking success and prosperity. Entrepreneurs often invoke blessings for their ventures, hoping for a smooth journey and fruitful outcomes.
    • In personal life, individuals may recite this mantra when pursuing personal goals, such as education or relationships, wishing for clarity and fulfillment in their endeavors.
    • During significant life events, such as weddings or new beginnings, people may use this mantra to seek divine blessings for a harmonious and prosperous future.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking divine guidance and blessings in all aspects of life.
    • It serves as a reminder that our desires should align with a higher purpose, and that through prayer and intention, we can navigate life's challenges more effectively.
    • In essence, it encourages individuals to cultivate a mindset of abundance, gratitude, and connection with the divine, leading to a more fulfilling and prosperous life.
