ये ते शुक्रासः शुचयः शुचिष्मः क्षां वपन्ति विषितासो अश्वाः |अध भ्रमस त उर्वियā वि भाति यातयमानो अधि सानु पृथ्नेः ||
ye te śukrāsaḥ śucayaḥ śuciṣmaḥ kṣāṁ vapanti viṣitāso aśvāḥ | adha bhramas ta urviyā vi bhāti yātayamāno adhi sānu pṛśneḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ye: those who
    • te: you
    • śukrāsaḥ: pure, bright, or luminous
    • śucayaḥ: those who are clean or sacred
    • śuciṣmaḥ: the most pure or the purest
    • kṣāṁ: who are endowed with strength
    • vapanti: they manifest or they appear
    • viṣitāso: those who are well-versed or knowledgeable
    • aśvāḥ: horses (symbolizing speed, power, and energy)
    • adha: below or beneath
    • bhramas: the one who moves or wanders
    • ta: those
    • urviyā: with great strength or vastness
    • vi: in or with
    • bhāti: shines or appears
    • yātayamāno: moving or traveling
    • adhi: above or over
    • sānu: the earth or the ground
    • pṛśneḥ: of the vastness or the expanse
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of those who are pure and knowledgeable, symbolized by the horses that are strong and swift.
    • The imagery of brightness and purity suggests a state of enlightenment and clarity of thought.
    • The mention of movement and vastness indicates the dynamic nature of life and the universe, where knowledge and purity lead to progress and growth.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, individuals who embody purity of intention and knowledge often rise to leadership positions, guiding others with clarity and strength.
    • In personal development, striving for purity in thoughts and actions can lead to a more fulfilling and meaningful life, akin to the horses that symbolize energy and progress.
    • In environmental contexts, those who advocate for purity in nature and sustainability reflect the essence of this mantra, promoting a healthier planet for future generations.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of purity, knowledge, and strength in both personal and collective journeys.
    • It serves as a reminder that by embodying these qualities, individuals can navigate the complexities of life with grace and purpose.
    • In real-life scenarios, applying the principles of this mantra can lead to personal growth, effective leadership, and a positive impact on society and the environment.
