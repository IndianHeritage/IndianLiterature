स्वादुष किलायम् मधुमाम् उतायं तीव्रः किलायं रसवाम् उतायम् |उतो nv अ1स्य पपिवांसम् इन्द्रं न कश्चन सहत आहवेषु ||
svāduṣ kilāyam madhumām̐ utāyaṁ tīvraḥ kilāyaṁ rasavām̐ utāyam | uto nv a1sya papivāṁsam indraṁ na kaś cana sahata āhaveṣu ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • svāduṣ: sweet, pleasant
    • kilāyam: indeed, truly
    • madhumām̐: honey-like, sweet
    • utāyaṁ: to be taken, to be consumed
    • tīvraḥ: intense, powerful
    • rasavām̐: possessing flavor, essence
    • uto: or, also
    • nv: indeed, truly
    • a1sya: of this, of that
    • papivāṁsam: drinking, consuming
    • indraṁ: Indra, the king of gods
    • na: not
    • kaś cana: anyone, any
    • sahata: can endure, withstand
    • āhaveṣu: in battles, in conflicts
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the sweetness and richness of life, symbolized by honey and intense flavors.
    • It emphasizes the importance of consuming the essence of life, which can be interpreted as enjoying the good things in life.
    • The reference to Indra suggests a connection to divine strength and power, indicating that one should seek the blessings of the divine in their endeavors.
    • The phrase "no one can endure in battles" implies that without the sweetness and essence of life, one may struggle in the face of challenges.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, this mantra can be applied to the importance of enjoying one's work and finding fulfillment in it. For instance, a teacher who finds joy in educating students is more likely to inspire them.
    • In personal relationships, the sweetness of love and companionship can be seen as essential for overcoming life's challenges. Couples who nurture their bond often find strength in adversity.
    • In health and wellness, the mantra can remind individuals to savor the flavors of nutritious foods, promoting a balanced diet that enhances overall well-being.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder to appreciate the sweetness of life and to seek the divine's blessings in our endeavors.
    • It encourages individuals to embrace the richness of experiences, whether in work, relationships, or personal growth.
    • Ultimately, the mantra highlights that the essence of life, much like honey, is vital for overcoming challenges and achieving fulfillment.
