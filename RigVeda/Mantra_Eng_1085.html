वैश्वानरस्य सुमतौ स्याम राजा हि कम भुवनानाम् अभिश्रीः |इतो जातो विश्वं इदं विचष्टे वैश्वानरो यतते सूर्येण ||
vaiśvānarasya sumatau syāma rājā hi kam bhuvanānām abhiśrīḥ | ito jāto viśvam idaṁ vi caṣṭe vaiśvānaro yatate sūryeṇa ||
  • Vaiśvānarasya: This term refers to the universal or cosmic aspect of fire, symbolizing the divine presence that pervades all existence.
  • Sumatau: This means "with good thoughts" or "with auspicious intentions," indicating a mindset that is positive and constructive.
  • Syāma: Translated as "may we be," it expresses a desire or aspiration for something to manifest in our lives.
  • Rājā hi: This phrase means "the king indeed," suggesting a supreme authority or a central figure that governs or influences.
  • Kam bhuvanānām: This translates to "of all the worlds," indicating a universal significance that transcends individual realms.
  • Abhiśrīḥ: Meaning "glory" or "prosperity," it signifies the abundance and success that comes from divine blessings.
  • Ito jāto: This means "born from here," suggesting a connection to the present moment or the current state of existence.
  • Viśvam idaṁ: Translated as "this entire universe," it emphasizes the all-encompassing nature of the divine presence.
  • Vi caṣṭe: This means "moves" or "acts," indicating the dynamic nature of the universe and the divine force within it.
  • Vaiśvānaro: Referring again to the universal fire, it symbolizes transformation and the energy that fuels life.
  • Yatate: This means "strives" or "exerts effort," highlighting the importance of action and determination in achieving goals.
  • Sūryeṇa: Translated as "with the sun," it signifies the life-giving energy and illumination that the sun provides, representing knowledge and enlightenment.

Conclusion: This mantra encapsulates the essence of universal consciousness and the interconnectedness of all beings. It emphasizes the importance of positive intentions, the pursuit of glory and prosperity, and the dynamic nature of existence. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios such as leadership, where one must embody good thoughts and strive for the betterment of all. It also serves as a reminder to harness the energy of the universe, much like the sun, to illuminate our paths and inspire others. By aligning ourselves with these principles, we can create a harmonious and prosperous life, contributing positively to the world around us.
