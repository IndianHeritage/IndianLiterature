सं वां कर्मणा सम इषा हिनोमीन्द्राविष्णू अपसस पारें अस्य |जुषेथां यज्ञं द्रविणं च धत्तम अरिष्टैर नः पथिभिः पारयन्ता ||
saṁ vāṁ karmaṇā sam iṣā hinomīndrāviṣṇū apasas pāre asya | juṣethāṁ yajñaṁ draviṇaṁ ca dhattam ariṣṭair naḥ pathibhiḥ pārayantā ||
  • saṁ vāṁ karmaṇā: This phrase can be translated as "together with our actions." It emphasizes the importance of collective effort and collaboration in achieving goals. In real life, this can be seen in team projects at work or community service initiatives where individuals come together to accomplish a common objective.
  • sam iṣā hinomīndrāviṣṇū: This part refers to the divine powers of Indra and Vishnu, suggesting that we seek their guidance and support. Indra is often associated with strength and leadership, while Vishnu represents preservation and protection. In practical terms, this can be related to seeking inspiration from leaders or mentors in our lives who guide us through challenges.
  • apasas pāre asya: This translates to "beyond this, may we attain." It signifies the aspiration to reach higher goals and aspirations. In everyday life, this can be reflected in personal development, such as pursuing education or skills that help us grow beyond our current state.
  • juṣethāṁ yajñaṁ: This phrase means "may we enjoy the sacrifice." It highlights the importance of selflessness and the value of contributing to a greater cause. In modern contexts, this can be seen in charitable acts or volunteering, where individuals find fulfillment in giving back to society.
  • draviṇaṁ ca dhattam: This translates to "and may we hold wealth." It suggests a balance between spiritual and material wealth. In real life, this can be interpreted as the pursuit of financial stability while also nurturing spiritual or emotional well-being, such as maintaining relationships and personal values.
  • ariṣṭair naḥ pathibhiḥ pārayantā: This means "may we be protected from obstacles on our paths." It emphasizes the need for guidance and protection from challenges. In practical terms, this can relate to seeking support from friends, family, or community resources when facing difficulties in life.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of collaboration, divine guidance, aspiration, selflessness, balance, and protection. Its significance lies in its applicability to various aspects of life, from personal growth to community involvement. By embodying the principles outlined in this mantra, individuals can navigate their paths with purpose and resilience, fostering a harmonious existence that benefits both themselves and society at large.
