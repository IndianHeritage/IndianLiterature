यद आचरस् तन्मा वावृद्धानो बलानिंद्र प्रब्रुवाणो जनेषु |मायेत सा ते यानि युद्धान्य आहुर नाद्य शत्रुं ननु पुरा विवित्से ||
yad acaras tanvā vāvṛdhāno balānīndra prabruvāṇo janeṣu | māyet sā te yāni yuddhāny āhur nādya śatruṁ nanu purā vivitse ||
  • Mantra: "yad acaras tanvā vāvṛdhāno balānīndra prabruvāṇo janeṣu | māyet sā te yāni yuddhāny āhur nādya śatruṁ nanu purā vivitse ||"
  • Translation: "Whatever actions are performed by the strong, O Indra, who is the leader among the powerful, may those actions be directed towards the people. Those who are engaged in battle, may they not be your enemies, but rather may they be your allies."

  • Word Breakdown:
    • yad acaras: Whatever actions are performed
    • tanvā: By the strong or powerful
    • vāvṛdhāno: O Indra, who is the leader
    • balānīndra: Among the powerful
    • prabruvāṇo: May those actions be directed
    • janeṣu: Towards the people
    • māyet: May they not be
    • sā te: Your enemies
    • yāni: Those who are engaged
    • yuddhāny: In battle
    • āhur: May they be
    • nādya: Not your enemies
    • śatruṁ: Enemies
    • nanu: But rather
    • purā: May they be your allies
    • vivitse: Engaged in battle

  • Significance: This mantra emphasizes the importance of unity and collaboration among individuals, especially in times of conflict. It calls upon Indra, the king of gods, to guide the actions of the strong towards the welfare of the people rather than engaging in destructive battles.
  • Real-Life Application:
    • In a workplace setting, this mantra can be applied to encourage teamwork. When strong leaders focus their efforts on collaboration rather than competition, the entire organization benefits.
    • In community service, individuals with resources and power can use their influence to uplift others, fostering a sense of unity rather than division.
    • In international relations, this mantra serves as a reminder for nations to work together for peace rather than engaging in conflicts that harm the global community.

  • Conclusion: The essence of this mantra lies in the call for strength to be used for the greater good. It highlights the significance of directing one's power and actions towards constructive purposes, fostering harmony and cooperation in society. By embodying these principles, individuals and communities can create a more peaceful and supportive environment.
