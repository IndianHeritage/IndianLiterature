प्रत्यस्य श्रेणयो ददृश्र एकं नियानं बहवो रथासः | बाहू यद अग्ने अनुमर्मृजानो न्यङ्ग उत्तानाम अन्वेषि भूमिम् ||
praty asya śreṇayo dadṛśra ekaṁ niyānam bahavo rathāsaḥ | bāhū yad agne anumarmṛjāno nyaṅṅ uttānām anveṣi bhūmim ||
  • Mantra: "praty asya śreṇayo dadṛśra ekaṁ niyānam bahavo rathāsaḥ | bāhū yad agne anumarmṛjāno nyaṅṅ uttānām anveṣi bhūmim ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • praty: towards
    • asya: this
    • śreṇayo: of the groups or paths
    • dadṛśra: having seen or perceived
    • ekaṁ: one
    • niyānam: guidance or direction
    • bahavo: many
    • rathāsaḥ: chariots or vehicles
    • bāhū: arms or strength
    • yad: which
    • agne: O Agni (the fire god)
    • anumarmṛjāno: having been purified or made clear
    • nyaṅṅ: to the one who
    • uttānām: of the elevated or exalted
    • anveṣi: seeking or searching
    • bhūmim: the earth or ground
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the unity and guidance found in the collective paths of life.
    • It emphasizes the importance of a singular direction amidst the many vehicles (or paths) we may take.
    • The invocation of Agni symbolizes the purification and clarity needed to navigate these paths effectively.
    • In real life, this can be likened to the journey of individuals in a community working towards a common goal, where each person contributes their strength (bāhū) to achieve a unified purpose.
  • Real-Life Example:
    • In a corporate setting, a team may have various roles (rathāsaḥ) but must work together under a common vision (niyānam) to achieve success.
    • In a family, each member may have different responsibilities, yet they all contribute to the well-being of the household, guided by shared values and goals.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of unity and collective effort in achieving goals.
    • It teaches us that while we may have different paths and roles, a singular direction and purpose can lead to greater achievements.
    • In everyday life, this principle can be applied in various contexts, such as teamwork, community service, and family dynamics, reminding us of the power of collaboration and shared vision.
