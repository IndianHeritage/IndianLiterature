अग्नि बाधस्व विमृधो विडुर्गहापामिवाम् अप रक्षांसी सेध || अस्मात् समुद्राद् बृहतो दिवो नोऽपाम् भूमानम् उप नः सृजेह ||
agne bādhasva vi mṛdho vi durgahāpāmīvām apa rakṣāṁsi sedha | asmāt samudrād bṛhato divo no 'pām bhūmānam upa naḥ sṛjeha ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • Agne: O Agni (the fire god)
    • Bādhasva: Remove or dispel
    • Vi: Completely or thoroughly
    • Mṛdho: From the enemies or obstacles
    • Vi: Completely or thoroughly
    • Durghāpām: From the difficult or dangerous situations
    • Iivām: These
    • Apa: Away or from
    • Rakṣāṁsi: The protections or defenses
    • Sedha: Make them effective or strong
    • Asmāt: From us
    • Samudrād: From the ocean or vastness
    • Bṛhato: Of the great or vast
    • Divo: Of the sky or heavens
    • No: For us
    • 'Pām: May we receive
    • Bhūmānam: The earthly or material
    • Upa: Near or close
    • Naḥ: To us
    • Sṛjeha: May you create or manifest
  • Overall Meaning: This mantra is a prayer to Agni, the fire god, seeking protection and the removal of obstacles. It emphasizes the desire for safety from dangers and the creation of favorable conditions in life. The invocation of Agni symbolizes the transformative power of fire, which can purify and protect.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In times of personal crisis, individuals often seek spiritual guidance or perform rituals to invoke divine protection, similar to how this mantra seeks the favor of Agni.
    • In a corporate setting, teams may face challenges that seem insurmountable. By fostering a collaborative environment and invoking a spirit of teamwork (akin to invoking Agni), they can overcome these obstacles.
    • During natural disasters, communities come together to protect one another, reflecting the essence of seeking protection and safety as expressed in this mantra.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its universal appeal for protection and the removal of obstacles. It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal challenges to communal efforts in times of crisis. By invoking the divine, individuals and communities can find strength and resilience, reminding us of the importance of seeking support and protection in our lives.
