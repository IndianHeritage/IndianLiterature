बह्वीः समा अकरम अन्तर अस्मिन् इन्द्रं वृणानः पितरं जहामि | अग्निः सोमो वरुणस ते च्यवन्ते पर्यावर्द राष्ट्रं तद अवाम्य आयन ||
bahvīḥ samā akaram antar asminn indraṁ vṛṇānaḥ pitaraṁ jahāmi | agniḥ somo varuṇas te cyavante paryāvard rāṣṭraṁ tad avāmy āyan ||
  • bahvīḥ samā akaram: This phrase can be translated as "many are the actions" or "various are the deeds." It signifies the diversity of actions and choices available to individuals in life.
  • antar asminn indraṁ vṛṇānaḥ: Here, "antar asminn" means "within this," and "indraṁ vṛṇānaḥ" refers to "we choose Indra." Indra is often seen as the king of the gods and a symbol of strength and power. This part emphasizes the importance of making wise choices in life, particularly in selecting strong and virtuous leaders or ideals to follow.
  • pitaraṁ jahāmi: This translates to "I offer to the ancestors." It highlights the significance of honoring and remembering one's forebears, acknowledging their contributions and wisdom in shaping our lives.
  • agniḥ somo varuṇas te cyavante: "Agniḥ" refers to fire, "somo" to the moon, and "varuṇas" to the god Varuna, who represents cosmic order. "Te cyavante" means "they depart." This line suggests the transient nature of life and the elements, reminding us that everything is subject to change and impermanence.
  • paryāvard rāṣṭraṁ tad avāmy āyan: "Paryāvard" means "to protect," "rāṣṭraṁ" refers to the realm or community, and "tad avāmy āyan" translates to "I bring forth." This emphasizes the responsibility of individuals to protect and nurture their communities and environments.

  • Conclusion: This mantra encapsulates the essence of life, emphasizing the importance of diverse actions, wise choices, honoring our ancestors, recognizing the impermanence of existence, and the responsibility to protect our communities. In real-life scenarios, it can be applied in various contexts such as leadership selection, community service, environmental conservation, and personal growth. By reflecting on these principles, individuals can lead more meaningful and responsible lives, contributing positively to society.
