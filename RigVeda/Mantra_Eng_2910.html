इदं ह्य अन्व ओजसा सुतं राधानाम् पते |पिबा त्व अस्य गिरवणः ||
idaṁ hy anv ojasā sutaṁ rādhānām pate | pibā tv a1sya girvaṇaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • idaṁ: this
    • hy: indeed
    • anv: following
    • ojasā: with energy or strength
    • sutaṁ: the offspring or child
    • rādhānām: of the prosperous or wealthy
    • pate: O Lord or master
    • pibā: drink or consume
    • tv: you
    • a1sya: of this
    • girvaṇaḥ: of the song or hymn
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra expresses a deep reverence for a divine entity, often interpreted as a call to the Lord to partake in the offerings made with strength and devotion.
    • The phrase "this indeed, following with energy, the offspring of the prosperous" suggests a connection between divine blessings and human prosperity.
    • The invocation to "drink of this hymn" signifies the importance of spiritual nourishment and the power of sacred words in enhancing one's life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a modern context, this mantra can be related to the practice of meditation and mindfulness, where individuals seek to connect with their inner strength and divine energy.
    • During rituals or ceremonies, such as weddings or festivals, invoking blessings for prosperity and well-being aligns with the essence of this mantra.
    • In personal development, the idea of consuming the "hymn" can be likened to absorbing positive affirmations or motivational teachings that uplift and empower individuals.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine energy and blessings, emphasizing the relationship between spiritual practices and material prosperity.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal growth to communal celebrations, reminding us of the importance of seeking divine guidance and strength in our endeavors.
    • Ultimately, it encourages individuals to recognize the power of words and intentions in shaping their reality and achieving their goals.
