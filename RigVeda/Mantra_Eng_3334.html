यदा समर्यं व्य अचेद ऋघावā दीर्घं यद āजिम अभ्य अख्यद आर्यः |अचिक्रदद वृषणं पत्न्य अच्छा दुरोण ā निśितं सोमसुद्भिः ||
yadā samaryaṁ vy aced ṛghāvā dīrghaṁ yad ājim abhy akhyad aryaḥ | acikradad vṛṣaṇam patny acchā duroṇa ā niśitaṁ somasudbhiḥ ||
  • yadā samaryaṁ vy aced ṛghāvā: This phrase can be interpreted as "when the strong one (ṛghāvā) overcomes the obstacles (samaryaṁ)." It signifies the importance of strength and resilience in facing challenges. In real life, this can be seen in athletes who train rigorously to overcome physical and mental barriers to achieve their goals.
  • dīrghaṁ yad ājim abhy akhyad aryaḥ: Here, "dīrghaṁ" means "long" or "enduring," and "ājim" refers to "the one who is swift or agile." This part suggests that the enduring qualities of a person or entity are recognized and praised. For example, in a business context, companies that have a long-standing reputation for quality and service are often celebrated and trusted by consumers.
  • acikradad vṛṣaṇam patny acchā: This can be translated to "the one who is powerful (vṛṣaṇam) and has a good partner (patny)." It emphasizes the significance of having strong alliances and partnerships. In personal relationships, this can be seen in successful marriages or friendships where both partners support and uplift each other.
  • duroṇa ā niśitaṁ somasudbhiḥ: "duroṇa" refers to "the strong or firm," and "niśitaṁ" means "sharp or keen." This suggests that strength combined with sharpness or intelligence leads to success. In a real-world scenario, this can be observed in leaders who not only possess strength but also the wisdom to make strategic decisions that benefit their organizations.

  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the virtues of strength, resilience, enduring qualities, powerful partnerships, and the combination of strength with intelligence. In real life, these principles can be applied in various scenarios, such as sports, business, and personal relationships. By embodying these qualities, individuals can navigate challenges effectively and achieve success in their endeavors.
