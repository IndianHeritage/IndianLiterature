पुनर्दाय ब्रह्मजायां कृत्वि देवैर निकिल्बिषम् |ऊर्जं पृथिव्या भक्त्वायोरुगायम् उपासते ||
punardāya brahmajāyāṁ kṛtvī devair nikilbiṣam | ūrjam pṛthivyā bhaktvāyorugāyam upāsate ||
  • Mantra: "punardāya brahmajāyāṁ kṛtvī devair nikilbiṣam | ūrjam pṛthivyā bhaktvāyorugāyam upāsate ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • punardāya: Rebirth or renewal
    • brahmajāyāṁ: In the realm of the ultimate reality or Brahman
    • kṛtvī: Having made or created
    • devair: By the gods
    • nikilbiṣam: All afflictions or obstacles
    • ūrjam: Energy or strength
    • pṛthivyā: Of the earth
    • bhaktvā: By worshipping or devoting
    • yorugāyam: The great or vast
    • upāsate: They worship or meditate
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra speaks about the concept of renewal and the divine energy that is present in the universe.
    • The phrase "punardāya" signifies the idea of rebirth, which can be related to personal growth and transformation in our lives. For example, after facing a significant challenge, an individual may experience a rebirth in their perspective, leading to a more positive outlook.
    • "brahmajāyāṁ" refers to the ultimate reality, suggesting that through understanding and connecting with this reality, one can transcend worldly troubles.
    • The mention of "devair nikilbiṣam" indicates that by invoking the divine, one can overcome all obstacles. In real life, this can be seen in practices such as prayer or meditation, where individuals seek divine assistance to navigate difficulties.
    • "ūrjam pṛthivyā" emphasizes the energy of the earth, reminding us of the importance of grounding ourselves in nature. For instance, spending time outdoors can rejuvenate our spirits and provide clarity.
    • The phrase "bhaktvāyorugāyam upāsate" highlights the act of worship and devotion, suggesting that through sincere devotion, one can access vast energies that aid in overcoming life's challenges.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of renewal, divine connection, and the power of devotion in overcoming life's obstacles.
    • In real-life scenarios, it encourages individuals to seek spiritual growth, connect with nature, and practice devotion to navigate challenges effectively.
    • Ultimately, it serves as a reminder that through understanding the ultimate reality and embracing renewal, one can harness the energy needed to thrive in life.
