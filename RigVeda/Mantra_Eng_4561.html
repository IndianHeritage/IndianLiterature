तन् नः प्रातनं सख्यम अस्तु युष्मे इत्त्था वदद्भिर वलम् अंगिरोभिः |हनन अच्युताच्युद दसमेषयन्तम् ऋणोः पुरो वि दुरो अस्य विश्वाः ||
tan naḥ pratnaṁ sakhyam astu yuṣme itthā vadadbhir valam aṅgirobhiḥ | hann acyutacyud dasmeṣayantam ṛṇoḥ puro vi duro asya viśvāḥ ||
  • tan naḥ pratnaṁ sakhyam astu: This phrase translates to "May our friendship be strong." It emphasizes the importance of camaraderie and unity among individuals. In real life, this can be seen in various contexts such as teamwork in a workplace, where collaboration leads to success.
  • yuṣme itthā vadadbhir: This means "May you speak thus." It suggests the value of communication and the sharing of thoughts and ideas. For example, in a family setting, open communication fosters understanding and strengthens relationships.
  • valam aṅgirobhiḥ: This translates to "with the strength of the Angiras." The Angiras are considered wise sages in Vedic literature. This part of the mantra highlights the importance of wisdom and guidance in our lives. In a practical sense, seeking advice from mentors or experienced individuals can lead to better decision-making.
  • hann acyutacyud: This phrase can be interpreted as "may we overcome obstacles." It reflects the human desire to triumph over challenges. In everyday life, this can be related to personal struggles, such as overcoming health issues or professional setbacks, where resilience is key.
  • dasmeṣayantam ṛṇoḥ puro: This means "may we be free from debts." It signifies the aspiration for financial stability and freedom from obligations. In modern society, this is relevant as individuals strive for financial independence and security through responsible budgeting and saving.
  • vi duro asya viśvāḥ: This translates to "may all beings be free from fear." It emphasizes the universal desire for safety and peace. In real life, this can be seen in community efforts to create safe environments, such as neighborhood watch programs or initiatives aimed at reducing crime.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of friendship, communication, wisdom, resilience, financial stability, and the universal quest for safety. Its significance lies in its applicability to various aspects of life, encouraging individuals to foster strong relationships, seek guidance, overcome challenges, achieve financial independence, and promote peace within their communities. By embodying these principles, one can lead a more harmonious and fulfilling life.
