अग्नि स्तोमं जुषस्व मे वर्धस्वानेन मनमनाः |प्रतिसूक्तानि हर्य नः ||
agne stomaṁ juṣasva me vardhasvānena manmanā | prati sūktāni harya naḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • Agne: O Agni (the Fire God)
    • Stomaṁ: Praise or hymn
    • Juṣasva: May you accept or enjoy
    • Me: My
    • Vardhasvā: May you increase or enhance
    • Anena: By this
    • Manmanā: With my mind or thoughts
    • Prati: In response to
    • Sūktāni: Well-spoken verses or hymns
    • Harya: May they be pleasing
    • Naḥ: To us
  • Overall Meaning: This mantra is a prayer to Agni, the deity representing fire, seeking his acceptance of the hymns offered and asking for his blessings to enhance the speaker's life through the power of these well-spoken verses.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a traditional Hindu home, during rituals, offerings are made to Agni, symbolizing the divine presence in fire. This mantra can be recited to invoke blessings for prosperity and well-being.
    • In a corporate setting, a team might use this mantra metaphorically to seek collective growth and success, emphasizing the importance of collaboration and shared goals.
    • In personal development, individuals may recite this mantra as a form of affirmation, focusing their thoughts on positive outcomes and personal growth.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its invocation of Agni as a symbol of transformation and purification. It emphasizes the importance of intention and collective effort in achieving personal and communal goals. In real-life scenarios, whether in spiritual practices, professional environments, or personal growth journeys, this mantra serves as a reminder of the power of words and thoughts in shaping our reality. By seeking the blessings of Agni, individuals and communities can aspire to enhance their lives and foster a spirit of unity and progress.
