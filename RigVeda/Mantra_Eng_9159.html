अद्वेषोऽद्या बर्हिषः स्तरीमणि ग्राव्णां योगे मनमनः साधा ईमहे |आदित्यानां शर्मणि स्था भुरण्यसि स्वस्त्यग्निं समिधानम् ईमहे ||
adveṣo adya barhiṣaḥ starīmaṇi grāvṇāṁ yoge manmanaḥ sādha īmahe | ādityānāṁ śarmaṇi sthā bhuraṇyasi svasty a1gniṁ samidhānam īmahe ||
  • Adveṣo adya barhiṣaḥ: This phrase can be translated as "Today, let there be no enmity." It emphasizes the importance of harmony and peace among individuals. In real life, this can be seen in community gatherings where people come together to resolve conflicts and promote understanding.
  • Starīmaṇi grāvṇāṁ: This means "the shining light of the community." It signifies the collective strength and brilliance of a group. For example, in a workplace, teamwork and collaboration can lead to innovative solutions, showcasing the power of collective effort.
  • Yoge manmanaḥ sādha īmahe: This translates to "We strive for unity of mind and purpose." It highlights the significance of shared goals and mutual understanding. In a family setting, when members work together towards a common objective, such as planning a celebration, it fosters stronger bonds.
  • Ādityānāṁ śarmaṇi: This phrase means "In the shelter of the sun." The sun symbolizes life, energy, and positivity. In real life, this can be related to seeking guidance and inspiration from positive role models or mentors who illuminate our paths.
  • Sthā bhuraṇyasi: This translates to "May you be established in strength." It encourages individuals to find their inner strength and resilience. For instance, during challenging times, such as a personal loss or professional setback, drawing on one's inner strength can lead to recovery and growth.
  • Svasty a1gniṁ samidhānam īmahe: This means "We invoke the auspicious fire." Fire represents transformation and purification. In a practical sense, this can be seen in rituals or ceremonies where fire is used to symbolize new beginnings, such as weddings or housewarming ceremonies.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of unity, strength, and positivity. Its significance lies in promoting harmony and collective well-being in various aspects of life, from personal relationships to community interactions. By embodying the principles of this mantra, individuals can foster a more peaceful and collaborative environment, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling and harmonious existence.
