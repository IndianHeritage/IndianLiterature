मन्त्र: शं नो धाता शम् उ धार्ता नो अस्तु शं न उरूचī भवतु स्वधाभिः |शं रोदसी बृहतī शं नो अद्रिः शं नो देवानां सुहवानी सन्तु ||
śaṁ no dhātā śam u dhartā no astu śaṁ na urūcī bhavatu svadhābhiḥ | śaṁ rodasī bṛhatī śaṁ no adriḥ śaṁ no devānāṁ suhavāni santu ||
  • śaṁ no dhātā: This phrase translates to "May the giver of prosperity be auspicious to us." Here, "dhātā" refers to the divine force or deity that provides sustenance and abundance.
  • śam u dhartā no astu: This means "May the supporter of peace be with us." "Dhartā" signifies the one who upholds or supports, emphasizing the importance of stability and peace in our lives.
  • śaṁ na urūcī bhavatu svadhābhiḥ: This translates to "May there be auspiciousness in our actions." "Urūcī" refers to the brilliance or radiance of our deeds, suggesting that our actions should be guided by positivity and good intentions.
  • śaṁ rodasī bṛhatī: This means "May the vastness of the sky be auspicious." It highlights the connection between the divine and the expansive nature of the universe, encouraging us to seek harmony with the cosmos.
  • śaṁ no adriḥ: This translates to "May the mountains be auspicious to us." Mountains symbolize strength and stability, reminding us to seek support from strong foundations in our lives.
  • śaṁ no devānāṁ suhavāni santu: This means "May the divine beings bring us good fortune." It emphasizes the importance of divine blessings and the role of higher powers in our well-being.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a prayer for peace, prosperity, and auspiciousness in various aspects of life. It serves as a reminder to seek harmony with the universe, to act with good intentions, and to rely on divine support. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied during significant events such as weddings, new ventures, or any occasion where blessings and positive energy are sought. By reciting this mantra, individuals can cultivate a mindset focused on peace, abundance, and the interconnectedness of all beings, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling and harmonious life.
