मन्त्र: पुरोळाशं च नो घसो जोषयासे गिरश्च नः |वधूयुर इव योषणाम् ||
puroḻāśaṁ ca no ghaso joṣayāse giraś ca naḥ | vadhūyur iva yoṣaṇām ||
  • puroḻāśaṁ: This term can be broken down into two parts: "puro" meaning "in front" or "before," and "ḻāśaṁ" which refers to "prosperity" or "wealth." Thus, it signifies the prosperity that lies ahead.
  • ca: This is a conjunction meaning "and," indicating a connection between the ideas presented in the mantra.
  • no: This word means "our," suggesting a sense of possession or belonging.
  • ghaso: This term can be interpreted as "food" or "nourishment," which is essential for life and sustenance.
  • joṣayāse: This word derives from "joṣa," meaning "to delight" or "to please," and "āse," which can mean "to grant" or "to bestow." Together, it implies the act of bestowing delight or nourishment.
  • giraś: This term refers to "speech" or "words," which can be seen as a means of communication and expression.
  • ca: Again, this conjunction connects the previous and following ideas.
  • naḥ: This means "to us," reinforcing the personal aspect of the mantra.
  • vadhūyur: This term can be understood as "like a bride," symbolizing beauty, grace, and auspiciousness.
  • iva: This means "like" or "as," indicating a comparison.
  • yoṣaṇām: This word refers to "women" or "feminine energy," which is often associated with nurturing and creation.

The mantra can be interpreted as a prayer or invocation for prosperity and nourishment, both physically and spiritually. It emphasizes the importance of communication and the role of feminine energy in bringing beauty and grace into our lives.


In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts:

  • Personal Growth: Just as the mantra calls for prosperity and nourishment, individuals can seek to cultivate their own personal and professional growth by setting clear goals and nurturing their talents.
  • Relationships: The reference to "like a bride" suggests the importance of nurturing relationships. In personal relationships, one can strive to bring beauty and grace into interactions, fostering love and understanding.
  • Community Well-being: The emphasis on nourishment can extend to community efforts, where individuals work together to ensure that everyone has access to food, resources, and support, creating a thriving environment.

Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its holistic approach to life, emphasizing the interconnectedness of prosperity, nourishment, communication, and the nurturing aspects of femininity. By embodying these principles, individuals and communities can create a more harmonious and prosperous existence.
