एवेन्द्रं वृषणं वज्रबाहुं वसिष्ठासो अभ्यर्चन्त्यर्कैः |स नः स्तुतो वीरवत् धातु गोमद् यूयम् पात स्वस्तिभिः सदा नः ||
eved indraṁ vṛṣaṇaṁ vajrabāhuṁ vasiṣṭhāso abhy arcanty arkaiḥ | sa naḥ stuto vīravad dhātu gomad yūyam pāta svastibhiḥ sadā naḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • eved: Indeed
    • indraṁ: Indra (the king of gods)
    • vṛṣaṇaṁ: The one who is powerful
    • vajrabāhuṁ: The one with a thunderbolt in his arm
    • vasiṣṭhāso: The wise or learned ones
    • abhy: With devotion
    • arcanty: Worship
    • arkaiḥ: With hymns or praises
    • sa: He
    • naḥ: Our
    • stuto: Praised
    • vīravad: Like a hero
    • dhātu: May he grant
    • gomad: Wealth or prosperity
    • yūyam: You all
    • pāta: Protect
    • svastibhiḥ: With auspiciousness
    • sadā: Always
    • naḥ: Our
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Indra, the god of rain and thunderstorms, who is often associated with strength and power.
    • The phrase "vajrabāhuṁ" signifies Indra's might, as he wields a thunderbolt, symbolizing his ability to protect and provide.
    • The worshippers, referred to as "vasiṣṭhāso," are invoking Indra with hymns, seeking his blessings for prosperity and protection.
    • The mantra emphasizes the importance of collective worship and the need for divine protection in achieving success and well-being.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In agricultural communities, invoking Indra is common during the monsoon season, as rain is crucial for crops. Farmers may chant this mantra to seek favorable weather conditions.
    • In modern contexts, this mantra can be recited during significant life events, such as weddings or new ventures, to invoke blessings for prosperity and protection.
    • Corporate leaders may use the essence of this mantra in team meetings, emphasizing the importance of collective effort and seeking guidance for success.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine intervention and support in achieving goals.
    • It highlights the importance of strength, wisdom, and collective effort in overcoming challenges.
    • In real life, this mantra serves as a reminder to seek guidance and protection from higher powers, fostering a sense of community and shared purpose.
