माता पितरं ऋत आ बभाज धीत्य अग्रे मनसा सं हि जग्मे |सा बीभत्सुर गर्भरसā निविद्धा नमस्वन्त इद उपवाकम ईयुः ||
mātā pitaram ṛta ā babhāja dhīty agre manasā saṁ hi jagme | sā bībhatsur garbharasā nividdhā namasvanta id upavākam īyuḥ ||
  • mātā pitaram: This phrase translates to "mother and father." It signifies the importance of parental figures in one's life, representing love, care, and guidance.
  • ṛta: This term refers to "truth" or "cosmic order." It embodies the idea of living in harmony with the universe and adhering to moral principles.
  • ā babhāja: This means "they have obtained" or "they have achieved." It indicates the attainment of wisdom or understanding through the guidance of parents and adherence to truth.
  • dhīty agre: This translates to "with wisdom in the forefront." It emphasizes the importance of prioritizing wisdom in decision-making and life choices.
  • manasā: Meaning "with the mind," this word highlights the role of mental focus and intention in achieving one's goals.
  • saṁ hi jagme: This phrase means "indeed, in this world." It suggests that the principles discussed are applicable in the real world and should be practiced in daily life.
  • sā bībhatsur: This translates to "she who is fierce" or "powerful." It can refer to the strength and resilience that comes from following the path of truth and wisdom.
  • garbharasā nividdhā: This means "bound by the essence of creation." It signifies the connection to the source of life and the importance of nurturing that connection.
  • namasvanta: This term refers to "those who are worthy of respect." It highlights the value of respecting those who embody wisdom and truth.
  • id upavākam īyuḥ: This translates to "may they speak this truth." It calls for the expression and sharing of wisdom and truth in society.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of parental guidance, the pursuit of truth, and the importance of wisdom in navigating life. It teaches that by honoring our parents and adhering to moral principles, we can achieve a deeper understanding of ourselves and the world around us. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in family dynamics, education, and personal development, encouraging individuals to seek wisdom and truth in their actions and decisions. By fostering respect for those who embody these values, we contribute to a more harmonious and enlightened society.
