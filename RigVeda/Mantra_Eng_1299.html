यो वाम् अश्विनाऽ मनसो जवीयान् रथः स्वश्वो विश आजीगाति |येना गच्छथः सुक्रतो दुरोणं तेन नराः वर्तिर अस्मभ्यं यातम् ||
yo vām aśvinā manaso javīyān rathaḥ svaśvo viśa ājigāti | yena gacchathaḥ sukṛto duroṇaṁ tena narā vartir asmabhyaṁ yātam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yo: who
    • vām: you
    • aśvinā: O Ashvins (the twin deities)
    • manaso: of the mind
    • javīyān: the swift
    • rathaḥ: chariot
    • svaśvo: your own horse
    • viśa: to the world
    • ājigāti: who moves
    • yena: by whom
    • gacchathaḥ: you go
    • sukṛto: the good deeds
    • duroṇaṁ: the difficult path
    • tena: by that
    • narā: men
    • vartir: may live
    • asmabhyaṁ: for us
    • yātam: may go
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the Ashvins, who are known as the divine physicians and symbolize swiftness and agility.
    • The phrase "manaso javīyān" emphasizes the importance of a swift mind, suggesting that mental agility is as crucial as physical speed.
    • The "rathaḥ svaśvo" refers to the chariot and horse, symbolizing the journey of life and the means by which we navigate it.
    • The mantra encourages individuals to pursue good deeds ("sukṛto") even when faced with challenges ("duroṇaṁ").
    • It highlights the idea that by following the right path and engaging in virtuous actions, one can achieve a fulfilling life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, a swift mind can lead to innovative solutions and quick decision-making, much like the Ashvins who are known for their speed.
    • When faced with difficult challenges, such as a demanding project or personal crisis, the mantra serves as a reminder to focus on good deeds and ethical choices, which can lead to positive outcomes.
    • In sports, athletes often embody the spirit of the Ashvins, demonstrating both physical prowess and mental agility to overcome obstacles and achieve their goals.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of mental agility and virtuous living in navigating the complexities of life.
    • It serves as a reminder that by aligning our actions with good deeds and maintaining a swift mind, we can successfully traverse the challenges we face.
    • In essence, it encourages us to seek divine guidance and support in our endeavors, ensuring that we remain on the right path.
