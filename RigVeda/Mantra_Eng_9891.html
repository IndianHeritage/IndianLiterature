इति त्वा देवाः इमा आहुर ऐळ यथेम् एतद्भवसि मृत्युबन्धुः |प्रजा ते देवान् हविषा यजति स्वर्ग उ त्वम् अपि मादयासे ||
iti tvā devā ima āhur aiḻa yathem etad bhavasi mṛtyubandhuḥ | prajā te devān haviṣā yajāti svarga u tvam api mādayāse ||
  • iti tvā devā ima āhur aiḻa: This phrase translates to "Thus, the gods have declared you, O Aila." Here, "Aila" refers to a divine or exalted being, indicating a connection to the divine or a higher state of existence.
  • yathem etad bhavasi mṛtyubandhuḥ: This means "As you become this, O one bound by death." It suggests a transformation or a journey from mortality to a higher state, emphasizing the transient nature of life and the potential for spiritual elevation.
  • prajā te devān haviṣā yajāti: This translates to "Your progeny, O divine one, are offered to the gods." It highlights the importance of offerings and rituals in connecting with the divine, suggesting that one's actions and offerings can influence their spiritual journey and the well-being of their descendants.
  • svarga u tvam api mādayāse: This means "You, too, may rejoice in heaven." It implies that through righteous actions and devotion, one can attain a state of bliss or heaven, reinforcing the idea of reward for virtuous living.

Conclusion:

The mantra encapsulates the essence of divine connection, the journey from mortality to spiritual elevation, and the significance of offerings in Hindu rituals. In real life, this can be applied through practices such as:

  • Engaging in community service and charitable acts, which can be seen as offerings to the divine and a way to uplift society.
  • Participating in rituals and prayers that honor ancestors and seek blessings for future generations, thereby acknowledging the cycle of life and death.
  • Striving for personal growth and spiritual development, which aligns with the idea of transforming oneself from a state of ignorance (mṛtyubandhuḥ) to enlightenment.

Ultimately, this mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of life, the importance of our actions, and the potential for spiritual fulfillment through devotion and righteous living.
