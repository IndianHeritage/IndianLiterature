यद अङ्ग दाशुषे त्वम् अग्नि भद्रं करिष्यसि |तवे तद सत्यं अङ्गिरः ||
yad aṅga dāśuṣe tvam agne bhadraṁ kariṣyasi | tavet tat satyam aṅgiraḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yad: whatever
    • aṅga: O Agni (the divine fire)
    • dāśuṣe: for the benefit
    • tvam: you
    • agne: O Agni
    • bhadraṁ: auspiciousness
    • kariṣyasi: you will perform
    • tavet: that is your truth
    • tat: that
    • satyam: truth
    • aṅgiraḥ: O Angiras (a sage)
  • Overall Meaning: This mantra addresses Agni, the sacred fire, and expresses a prayer for auspiciousness and truth. It emphasizes that whatever actions Agni performs, they are for the benefit of all and are rooted in truth.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, we often seek guidance and support from forces greater than ourselves, much like how this mantra seeks the blessings of Agni. For instance, when starting a new venture, individuals may perform rituals to invoke divine blessings for success and auspiciousness.
    • In relationships, the essence of this mantra can be reflected in the commitment to act with truth and integrity, ensuring that our actions benefit not just ourselves but also those around us.
    • In environmental practices, the reverence for Agni can be seen in the way we treat fire and energy sources, promoting sustainable practices that honor the earth and its resources.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for auspiciousness and truth in our actions. It serves as a reminder to align our intentions with the greater good, ensuring that our endeavors are beneficial to ourselves and society. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire individuals to act with integrity, seek blessings for their undertakings, and foster a sense of responsibility towards the environment and community.
