मन्त्र: त्रिकद्रुकेषु चेतनं देवासो यज्ञं अत्नत || तं इद वर्धन्तु नो गिरः ||
trikadrukeṣu cetanaṁ devāso yajñam atnata | tam id vardhantu no giraḥ ||
  • Trikadrukeṣu: This term can be broken down into "tri" meaning three and "kadrukeṣu" which refers to the three realms or aspects of existence. In many interpretations, these realms are often understood as the physical, mental, and spiritual dimensions of life.
  • Cetanaṁ: This word translates to consciousness or awareness. It signifies the active, aware state of being that allows individuals to perceive and interact with the world around them.
  • Devāso: This term refers to the divine beings or deities. In the context of the mantra, it suggests the presence and influence of higher powers or spiritual entities that guide and support human endeavors.
  • Yajñam: Yajña is a Vedic term that denotes sacrifice or offering, often in the context of rituals performed to appease the gods. It symbolizes the act of giving and the importance of selflessness in spiritual practices.
  • Atnata: This word can be interpreted as 'eternal' or 'everlasting.' It emphasizes the timeless nature of the divine and the enduring impact of spiritual practices.
  • Tam id vardhantu: This phrase translates to "may that (divine influence) increase or grow." It expresses a desire for the blessings and support of the divine to flourish in one’s life.
  • No giraḥ: This means "our words" or "our expressions." It indicates a plea for the divine to enhance the quality and effectiveness of our communication and expressions.

Conclusion:

The mantra "trikadrukeṣu cetanaṁ devāso yajñam atnata | tam id vardhantu no giraḥ ||" encapsulates the essence of seeking divine support in the three realms of existence—physical, mental, and spiritual. It emphasizes the importance of consciousness and awareness in our actions and the need for selfless offerings to the divine. In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as during personal challenges, community service, or spiritual practices where one seeks to align with higher principles and enhance their communication and relationships. By invoking the divine presence and expressing a desire for growth and clarity, individuals can foster a deeper connection with their spiritual selves and the world around them, ultimately leading to a more harmonious and fulfilling life.
