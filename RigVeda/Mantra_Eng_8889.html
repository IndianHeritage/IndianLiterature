दिवा ह क्षामा प्रथम ऋतेनाभिश्रावे भवतः सत्यवाचा |देवो यन् मर्तान यजथाय कृत्वान सीदद धोताऽ प्रत्यङ् स्वम् असुं यन् ||
dyāvā ha kṣāmā prathame ṛtenābhiśrāve bhavataḥ satyavācā | devo yan martān yajathāya kṛṇvan sīdad dhotā pratyaṅ svam asuṁ yan ||
  • dyāvā ha kṣāmā: This phrase translates to "the heavens are indeed vast." It signifies the expansive nature of the universe and the infinite possibilities it holds. In real life, this can be related to the limitless opportunities we encounter in our personal and professional lives.
  • prathame ṛtenābhiśrāve: This means "first, let the truth flow." It emphasizes the importance of truth and righteousness as foundational principles. In everyday scenarios, this can be seen in the importance of honesty in relationships and business dealings, where truth fosters trust and integrity.
  • bhavataḥ satyavācā: This translates to "may your words be truthful." It highlights the significance of speaking with honesty and integrity. In real life, this can be applied in situations such as public speaking, where the speaker's credibility is built on their commitment to truthfulness.
  • devo yan martān yajathāya kṛṇvan: This phrase means "the divine creates for the mortals who worship." It suggests that the divine provides for those who seek and honor it through their actions. In practical terms, this can be seen in how communities come together to support one another, reflecting a sense of shared responsibility and care.
  • sīdad dhotā pratyaṅ svam asuṁ yan: This translates to "may the divine grant us our desires." It signifies the hope and aspiration for fulfillment of one's needs and wishes. In real life, this can be related to setting goals and working towards them, believing that with effort and devotion, one can achieve their dreams.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of truth, divine support, and the vastness of possibilities. Its significance lies in the reminder to uphold truthfulness in our words and actions, to seek divine guidance, and to recognize the infinite opportunities available to us. In real life, this mantra can inspire individuals to live with integrity, foster community support, and pursue their aspirations with faith and dedication.
