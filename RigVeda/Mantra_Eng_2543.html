ईयिवांसम अति श्रीधः शाश्वतीर अति सश्चतः |अन्व ईम अविन्दन निचिरासो अद्रुहो 'प्सु सिंहम इव श्रितम् ||
īyivāṁsam ati sridhaḥ śaśvatīr ati saścataḥ | anv īm avindan nicirāso adruho 'psu siṁham iva śritam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • īyivāṁsam: Those who are moving or progressing.
    • ati: Beyond or surpassing.
    • sridhaḥ: Wealth or prosperity.
    • śaśvatīr: Eternal or everlasting.
    • ati saścataḥ: Beyond all that is good or beneficial.
    • anv: Following or pursuing.
    • īm: They have attained or achieved.
    • avindan: They found or discovered.
    • nicirāso: Those who are steadfast or resolute.
    • adruho: Without enmity or malice.
    • 'psu: In the waters or in the flow.
    • siṁham: Like a lion.
    • iva: Like or as.
    • śritam: Residing or dwelling.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of individuals who are progressing towards prosperity and eternal goodness.
    • It emphasizes the importance of pursuing wealth and success while maintaining a spirit of goodwill and without malice.
    • The imagery of a lion suggests strength, courage, and leadership, indicating that those who follow this path are powerful and noble.
    • The reference to "waters" symbolizes the flow of life and the importance of being adaptable and resilient in the face of challenges.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, a team that works collaboratively and supports each other while striving for success exemplifies the essence of this mantra.
    • In personal relationships, individuals who pursue their goals while fostering kindness and understanding reflect the teachings of this mantra.
    • Community leaders who advocate for progress and prosperity without harboring resentment or hostility towards others embody the spirit of this verse.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of pursuing prosperity and success with a positive and benevolent attitude.
    • It teaches that true strength lies in resilience, adaptability, and the ability to uplift others while striving for personal goals.
    • In real life, applying the principles of this mantra can lead to harmonious relationships, successful endeavors, and a fulfilling life.
