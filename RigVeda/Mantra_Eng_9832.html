बळ अस्य नीताऽ विपणेश च मनमहै वयाऽ अस्य प्रहुताऽ आसुर अत्तवे |यदा घोरा सो अमृतत्वम् आशताद इज जनस्य दैव्यस्य चर्किरन् ||
baḻ asya nīthā vi paṇeś ca manmahe vayā asya prahutā āsur attave | yadā ghorāso amṛtatvam āśatād ij janasya daivyasya carkiran ||
  • baḻ asya nīthā: This phrase can be interpreted as "the strength of this path." It signifies the importance of following a righteous and strong path in life. For example, in a career, choosing a path that aligns with one's values and ethics can lead to long-term success.
  • vi paṇeś ca manmahe: This translates to "and the wisdom of the mind." It emphasizes the role of intellect and wisdom in decision-making. In real life, this can be seen in leaders who make informed decisions based on knowledge and experience, rather than impulsive reactions.
  • vayā asya prahutā: This means "the power of this abundance." It suggests that there is a wealth of resources and opportunities available. For instance, in a community, when individuals come together and share their skills and resources, they can create a thriving environment for all.
  • āsur atende: This phrase can be interpreted as "the divine connection." It highlights the importance of recognizing a higher power or purpose in life. Many people find strength and guidance through spirituality or religion, which can help them navigate challenges.
  • yadā ghorāso: This translates to "when the fierce ones." It refers to facing challenges or adversities. In real life, this can be likened to overcoming obstacles in personal or professional life, such as dealing with a difficult project at work or personal struggles.
  • amṛtatvam āśatād: This means "the attainment of immortality." It symbolizes the pursuit of lasting impact and legacy. For example, individuals who contribute to society through philanthropy or innovation often leave a lasting mark that transcends their lifetime.
  • ij janasya daivyasya carkiran: This translates to "the divine light of the people." It signifies the collective strength and wisdom of the community. In practice, this can be seen in social movements where the collective voice of the people brings about significant change.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of strength, wisdom, abundance, divine connection, resilience, immortality, and collective power. Its significance lies in guiding individuals and communities to navigate life's challenges with integrity and purpose. In real-life scenarios, it encourages us to make informed decisions, recognize the resources around us, and work together towards a common goal, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling and impactful life.
