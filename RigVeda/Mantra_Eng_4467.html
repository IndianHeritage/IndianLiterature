तां नृभ्य आ सौश्रवसा सुवीराग्ने सूनो सहसः पुष्यसे धाः |कृतोषि यच छवसां भूरी पश्वो वयो वृकायारये जसुरये ||
tā nṛbhya ā sauśravasā suvīrāgne sūno sahasaḥ puṣyase dhāḥ | kṛṇoṣi yac chavasā bhūri paśvo vayo vṛkāyāraye jasuraye ||
  • tā nṛbhya ā sauśravasā: This phrase can be translated as "those who are endowed with good fame." Here, "tā" refers to "those," "nṛbhya" means "to men," and "sauśravasā" signifies "having good reputation." In real life, this can relate to individuals who are respected in their communities for their integrity and contributions.
  • suvīrāgne sūno sahasaḥ puṣyase dhāḥ: This part translates to "O Agni, the strong one, may you nourish us." "Suvīrāgne" refers to "O strong Agni," "sūno" means "of the son," and "sahasaḥ puṣyase dhāḥ" implies "may you provide nourishment." In practical terms, this can be seen as a prayer for strength and sustenance, akin to seeking support from a mentor or leader in challenging times.
  • kṛṇoṣi yac chavasā bhūri paśvo: This translates to "You who create abundance and wealth." "Kṛṇoṣi" means "you create," "yac" refers to "whatever," "chavasā" means "of the body," and "bhūri paśvo" signifies "of abundant wealth." This can be related to the efforts we put into our work and the rewards that follow, emphasizing the importance of hard work and dedication in achieving success.
  • vayo vṛkāyāraye jasuraye: This phrase means "may we be protected and victorious." "Vayo" refers to "our age," "vṛkāyāraye" means "may we be protected," and "jasuraye" signifies "may we be victorious." In a real-life context, this can be interpreted as a wish for safety and success in our endeavors, whether in personal challenges or professional pursuits.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of good reputation, strength, nourishment, abundance, protection, and victory. In real life, it serves as a reminder to cultivate positive qualities, seek support from strong figures, work diligently towards our goals, and aspire for success while ensuring our safety. This mantra can be applicable in various scenarios, such as during personal challenges, professional endeavors, or community service, where the values of integrity, strength, and success are paramount.
