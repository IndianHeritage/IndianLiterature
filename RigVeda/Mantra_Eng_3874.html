वृष्णे यत् ते वृषणो अर्कम् आर्चान इन्द्र ग्रावाणो अदितिः सजोषाः |अनश्वासो ये पवयो 'रथाः इन्द्रेषिताः अभ्य अवर्तन्त दस्यून् ||
vṛṣṇe yat te vṛṣaṇo arkam arcān indra grāvāṇo aditiḥ sajoṣāḥ | anaśvāso ye pavayo 'rathā indreṣitā abhy avartanta dasyūn ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • vṛṣṇe: To Vishnu or the divine.
    • yat: That which.
    • te: Your.
    • vṛṣaṇo: Of the strong or powerful.
    • arkam: Light or radiance.
    • arcān: Worship or offerings.
    • indra: The king of gods, representing strength and power.
    • grāvāṇo: The one who brings together or unites.
    • aditiḥ: The infinite or boundless.
    • sajoṣāḥ: With joy or happiness.
    • anaśvāso: Without fatigue or weariness.
    • ye: Those who.
    • pavayo: Of the pure or sacred.
    • 'rathā: Chariots or vehicles.
    • indreṣitā: Directed by Indra.
    • abhy: Towards.
    • avartanta: They turned or revolved.
    • dasyūn: The enemies or obstacles.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the divine energy of Vishnu, emphasizing the importance of strength and unity in overcoming challenges.
    • The reference to Indra highlights the need for leadership and guidance in our endeavors.
    • The phrase "without fatigue" suggests that with divine support, one can achieve their goals without exhaustion.
    • The mention of "enemies" symbolizes the obstacles we face in life, which can be overcome through collective effort and divine assistance.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a team project at work, invoking the spirit of unity and strength can lead to successful collaboration, much like the divine support mentioned in the mantra.
    • During challenging times, such as a personal crisis, seeking guidance from a mentor or leader can provide the necessary direction to overcome obstacles.
    • In sports, a team that works together harmoniously, drawing on each member's strengths, can achieve victory against formidable opponents.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of divine support, unity, and strength in overcoming life's challenges.
    • It teaches us that with collective effort and guidance, we can navigate through obstacles and achieve our goals.
    • In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios, from personal struggles to professional teamwork, reminding us to seek support and work together for success.
