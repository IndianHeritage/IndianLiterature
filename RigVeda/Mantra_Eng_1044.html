भरामेध्मं कृणवामाः हविषं ते चितयन्तः पर्वणा-पर्वणा वयं | जीवातवे प्रतरं साधयाधियः अग्नि सख्ये मा ऋषामाः वयं तव ||
bharāmedhmaṁ kṛṇavāmā havīṁṣi te citayantaḥ parvaṇā-parvaṇā vayam | jīvātave prataraṁ sādhayā dhiyo 'gne sakhye mā riṣāmā vayaṁ tava ||
  • Mantra: "bharāmedhmaṁ kṛṇavāmā havīṁṣi te citayantaḥ parvaṇā-parvaṇā vayam | jīvātave prataraṁ sādhayā dhiyo 'gne sakhye mā riṣāmā vayaṁ tava ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • bharāmedhmaṁ: May we be endowed with strength and vigor.
    • kṛṇavāmā: We offer or create.
    • havīṁṣi: Offerings or oblations.
    • te: To you (referring to the divine).
    • citayantaḥ: Those who are thoughtful or wise.
    • parvaṇā-parvaṇā: In every moment or occasion.
    • vayam: We.
    • jīvātave: For the sake of life.
    • prataraṁ: To cross over or to achieve.
    • sādhayā: By means of accomplishment.
    • dhiyo: Intellects or minds.
    • 'gne: O fire (often referring to Agni, the fire god).
    • sakhye: In friendship.
    • mā: Do not.
    • riṣāmā: Anger or wrath.
    • vayaṁ: We.
    • tava: Your.
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra is a prayer for strength and wisdom, invoking the divine to bless us with the ability to make meaningful offerings.
    • It emphasizes the importance of thoughtful action in every moment of our lives, suggesting that we should be mindful and intentional in our endeavors.
    • The reference to Agni, the fire god, symbolizes transformation and purification, indicating that our actions should lead to positive change.
    • In real life, this can be seen in various contexts, such as in teamwork where individuals contribute their strengths for a common goal, or in personal development where one seeks to improve oneself through knowledge and wisdom.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for unity, strength, and wisdom in our actions.
    • It teaches us to approach life with a sense of purpose and mindfulness, ensuring that our contributions are meaningful and beneficial.
    • In practical terms, this mantra can be applied in daily life by fostering collaboration in communities, promoting personal growth, and encouraging a harmonious relationship with others.
    • Ultimately, it serves as a reminder to act with integrity and to seek the greater good in all our endeavors.
