यद अग्ने अद्य मिथुनā शपातो यद वाचस तृष्टं जनयन्त रेब्हाः |मन्योः मनसः शरव्याः जायत यā तया विध्य हृदये यातुधानान ||
yad agne adya mithunā śapāto yad vācas tṛṣṭaṁ janayanta rebhāḥ | manyor manasaḥ śaravyā3 jāyate yā tayā vidhya hṛdaye yātudhānān ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yad - whatever
    • agne - O Agni (the fire god)
    • adya - today
    • mithunā - in pairs
    • śapāto - by the power of the oath
    • yad - whatever
    • vācas - words
    • tṛṣṭaṁ - that which is desired
    • janayanta - they generate
    • rebhāḥ - the powerful
    • manyor - of the mind
    • manasaḥ - of the thoughts
    • śaravyā - of the arrows
    • jāyate - is born
    • yā - that
    • tayā - by that
    • vidhya - knowledge
    • hṛdaye - in the heart
    • yātudhānān - of the evil spirits
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes Agni, the fire god, emphasizing the importance of fire in rituals and as a symbol of purity and transformation.
    • The phrase "in pairs" suggests the significance of duality and partnership in life, reflecting the balance between opposites.
    • The mention of "by the power of the oath" indicates the importance of commitment and integrity in one's actions and words.
    • The mantra speaks to the generation of desires and thoughts, highlighting the power of the mind in shaping reality.
    • It also addresses the need for knowledge and wisdom to navigate through challenges, particularly those posed by negative influences or evil spirits.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In relationships, the idea of "in pairs" can be seen in partnerships where mutual respect and understanding are essential for harmony.
    • In professional settings, the commitment to one's word and integrity can lead to trust and successful collaborations.
    • The power of the mind is evident in practices like meditation and positive thinking, which can help individuals overcome obstacles and achieve their goals.
    • Knowledge and wisdom are crucial in decision-making processes, whether in personal life or in business, to avoid pitfalls and navigate complexities.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the interconnectedness of thoughts, words, and actions, urging individuals to cultivate integrity and wisdom.
    • Its significance lies in the reminder that our desires and intentions shape our reality, and that knowledge is essential in overcoming challenges.
    • In real life, applying the principles of this mantra can lead to more meaningful relationships, successful endeavors, and a deeper understanding of oneself and the world.
