इयं वाम ब्रह्मणस पते सुवृक्तिर ब्रह्मेन्द्राय वज्रीणि अकारी |अविष्टं धियो जिगृतं पुरंधीर्ज जजस्तं आर्यो वनुषां अरातिः ||
iyaṁ vām brahmaṇas pate suvṛktir brahmendrāya vajriṇe akāri | aviṣṭaṁ dhiyo jigṛtam puraṁdhīr jajastam aryo vanuṣām arātīḥ ||
  • iyaṁ vām brahmaṇas pate: This phrase can be translated as "This is your, O Lord of Brahman." Here, "Brahman" refers to the ultimate reality or cosmic spirit in Hindu philosophy. The invocation acknowledges the divine presence and seeks guidance.
  • suvṛktir brahmendrāya vajriṇe akāri: This translates to "I have created good conduct for the Lord of Brahman, the powerful one." The term "suvṛkti" signifies virtuous actions or good conduct, emphasizing the importance of ethical living and righteousness in one's life.
  • aviṣṭaṁ dhiyo jigṛtam: This means "the intellect that is not obstructed." It suggests the pursuit of clarity and wisdom, encouraging individuals to seek knowledge and understanding without distractions or hindrances.
  • puraṁdhīr jajastam: This can be interpreted as "the one who has created the universe." It acknowledges the divine as the creator and sustainer of all existence, reinforcing the belief in a higher power that governs the cosmos.
  • aryo vanuṣām arātīḥ: This translates to "the noble one who is free from enmity." It highlights the value of compassion and non-violence, urging individuals to cultivate harmonious relationships with others and to act with kindness.

  • Significance: This mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking divine guidance, promoting virtuous conduct, and striving for wisdom. It serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of all beings and the importance of ethical living.
  • Real-life Application: In daily life, this mantra can inspire individuals to engage in self-reflection, practice compassion, and pursue knowledge. For instance, in a workplace setting, one might apply the principles of good conduct and non-violence by fostering a collaborative environment and resolving conflicts amicably.
  • Conclusion: The mantra encourages individuals to align their actions with higher principles, seek wisdom, and cultivate positive relationships. By embodying these values, one can contribute to a more harmonious and enlightened society.
