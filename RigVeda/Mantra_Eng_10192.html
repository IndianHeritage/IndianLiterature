समानम् पूर्वीर अभि वावशानास तिष्ठन् वत्सस्य मातरः सनीलाः |ऋतस्य सानाव अधि चक्रमाणा रिहन्ति मध्वो अमृतस्य वाणिः ||
samānam pūrvīr abhi vāvaśānās tiṣṭhan vatsasya mātaraḥ sanīḻāḥ | ṛtasya sānāv adhi cakramāṇā rihanti madhvo amṛtasya vāṇīḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • samānam: similar, equal
    • pūrvīr: of the ancients, predecessors
    • abhi: towards, in the direction of
    • vāvaśānās: having been made to stand, established
    • tiṣṭhan: standing, existing
    • vatsasya: of the calf, or young one
    • mātaraḥ: mothers
    • sanīḻāḥ: who are nourishing, supportive
    • ṛtasya: of truth, order
    • sānāv: together, in harmony
    • adhi: upon, over
    • cakramāṇā: revolving, turning
    • rihanti: they enjoy, they partake
    • madhvo: of sweetness, pleasantness
    • amṛtasya: of immortality, eternal
    • vāṇīḥ: speech, voice
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of unity and harmony among beings, emphasizing the importance of collective existence.
    • The reference to "mothers" signifies nurturing and support, suggesting that a community thrives when its members care for one another.
    • The mention of "truth" and "immortality" indicates that living in accordance with universal principles leads to lasting fulfillment and joy.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a family, the nurturing role of parents (mātaraḥ) is crucial for the development of children (vatsasya). When parents support each other and work together, they create a harmonious environment.
    • In a workplace, collaboration and mutual respect among colleagues can lead to a productive atmosphere where everyone enjoys their work (rihanti) and contributes to the success of the organization.
    • In society, communities that prioritize truth and support (ṛtasya sānāv) often experience greater social cohesion and resilience, leading to a more peaceful coexistence.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of unity, support, and truth in various aspects of life.
    • It teaches us that by nurturing relationships and fostering a sense of community, we can achieve a harmonious existence.
    • In real-life scenarios, whether in families, workplaces, or communities, applying the principles of this mantra can lead to a more fulfilling and joyful life.
