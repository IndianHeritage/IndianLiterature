विषो यद अह्वे नृभिः सनीळा अग्निर देवत्वा विश्वान्य अश्याः ||
viśo yad ahve nṛbhiḥ sanīḻā agnir devatvā viśvāny aśyāḥ ||
  • viśo: This term refers to the collective or community, often implying a sense of unity among people.
  • yad: This means "that" or "which," indicating a connection or reference to something specific.
  • ahve: This translates to "invoke" or "call upon," suggesting a plea or request for divine assistance.
  • nṛbhiḥ: This word means "by men" or "by people," emphasizing the human aspect of the invocation.
  • sanīḻā: This term can be interpreted as "with strength" or "with vigor," indicating a powerful or earnest invocation.
  • agnir: This refers to "fire," which in Vedic literature symbolizes purity, transformation, and the divine presence.
  • devatvā: This means "divinity" or "godliness," suggesting the sacred nature of the invocation.
  • viśvāny: This translates to "all" or "every," indicating inclusiveness in the invocation.
  • aśyāḥ: This term means "prosperity" or "well-being," referring to the desired outcomes of the invocation.

The mantra can be understood as a collective invocation by people, calling upon the divine fire (Agni) with strength, seeking prosperity and well-being for all. It emphasizes the importance of community and the shared human experience in seeking divine blessings.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as:

  • Community Gatherings: During festivals or communal events, people can come together to invoke blessings for prosperity and harmony.
  • Teamwork in Organizations: In a workplace, teams can embody the spirit of this mantra by working together with strength and unity to achieve common goals.
  • Environmental Initiatives: Communities can invoke the spirit of this mantra when engaging in collective efforts to protect the environment, calling upon the divine for support in their endeavors.

In conclusion, this mantra signifies the power of collective intention and the importance of invoking divine support for the well-being of the community. It serves as a reminder that when people unite with a common purpose, they can achieve greater prosperity and harmony in their lives.
