मन्त्र: मन्युर इन्द्रो मन्युर एवास देवो मन्युर होतā वरुणो जातवेदाः | मन्युः विशा ईळते मानवशीर याः पाहि नो मन्यो तपसा सजोषाः ||
manyur indro manyur evāsa devo manyur hotā varuṇo jātavedāḥ | manyuṁ viśa īḻate mānuṣīr yāḥ pāhi no manyo tapasā sajoṣāḥ ||
  • Mantra: "manyur indro manyur evāsa devo manyur hotā varuṇo jātavedāḥ | manyuṁ viśa īḻate mānuṣīr yāḥ pāhi no manyo tapasā sajoṣāḥ ||"
  • Translation: This mantra speaks of the concept of "Manyu," which can be understood as anger or wrath. It invokes Indra, the king of gods, and Varuna, the god of cosmic order, to protect us from anger and to channel it positively.
  • Word Breakdown:
    • Manyur: Anger or wrath.
    • Indro: Referring to Indra, the king of gods, symbolizing strength and power.
    • Devo: Divine or godly presence.
    • Hotā: The one who offers sacrifices or prayers.
    • Varuṇo: Referring to Varuna, the god of water and cosmic order.
    • Jātavedāḥ: The one who knows all births, often associated with Agni, the fire god.
    • Viśa: To pervade or to spread.
    • Mānuṣīr: Human beings.
    • Pāhi: Protect or guard.
    • Tapasā: Through austerity or penance.
    • Sajoṣāḥ: With harmony or togetherness.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace, when conflicts arise, it is essential to manage anger constructively. This mantra reminds us to seek divine guidance to channel our emotions positively.
    • In personal relationships, understanding and managing anger can lead to healthier interactions. The invocation of divine forces can be seen as a call for patience and understanding.
    • In community settings, this mantra can be a reminder to work together harmoniously, especially when faced with challenges that may provoke anger or frustration.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine intervention in managing human emotions, particularly anger. It emphasizes the importance of channeling our feelings positively and seeking protection from destructive emotions. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as personal relationships, workplaces, and community interactions, promoting harmony and understanding in the face of challenges.
