ये नः पूर्वे पितरः सोम्यासो 'नूहिरे सोमपीथं वसिष्ठाः |तेभिर यमः संराणो हविंष्य उशन्न उशद्भिः प्रतिकामम अत्तु ||
ye naḥ pūrve pitaraḥ somyāso 'nūhire somapīthaṁ vasiṣṭhāḥ | tebhir yamaḥ saṁrarāṇo havīṁṣy uśann uśadbhiḥ pratikāmam attu ||
  • Translation: "Those who are our forefathers, the wise ones, who have gone before us, may they grant us the nectar of immortality. May Yama, the lord of death, who is nourished by offerings, accept our offerings and fulfill our desires."
  • Meaning of Key Terms:
    • pūrve pitaraḥ: Refers to our ancestors or forefathers, emphasizing the connection to our lineage and heritage.
    • somyāso: Represents the wise and virtuous, indicating the qualities we aspire to inherit from our ancestors.
    • anūhire: Suggests the act of granting or bestowing, highlighting the role of ancestors in our lives.
    • somapīthaṁ: Refers to the nectar of immortality, symbolizing the ultimate goal of life and spiritual fulfillment.
    • yamaḥ: The lord of death, representing the inevitable nature of mortality and the cycle of life.
    • havīṁṣy: Refers to offerings, indicating the importance of rituals and gratitude in our lives.
    • uśann uśadbhiḥ: Suggests the fulfillment of desires, emphasizing the connection between our actions and their outcomes.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In many cultures, honoring ancestors through rituals and remembrance is a common practice. For instance, during the Day of the Dead in Mexico, families create altars to celebrate and remember their deceased loved ones, reflecting the idea of seeking blessings from those who have passed.
    • In modern life, the concept of legacy is significant. Individuals often strive to leave a positive impact on future generations, akin to the blessings sought from ancestors in this mantra.
    • In personal development, the pursuit of wisdom and virtue, as embodied by our forefathers, can guide individuals in making ethical decisions and leading fulfilling lives.
  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of honoring our ancestors and recognizing their influence on our lives. It serves as a reminder to seek wisdom, virtue, and fulfillment in our actions. In real life, this can manifest through rituals, personal development, and the pursuit of a meaningful legacy. By acknowledging our roots and the cycle of life, we can cultivate a deeper understanding of our place in the world and the interconnectedness of all beings.
