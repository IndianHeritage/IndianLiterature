यं यज्ञं नयथा नर आदित्याः ऋजुना पथाः |प्र वह स धीतये नशत ||
yaṁ yajñaṁ nayathā nara ādityā ṛjunā pathā | pra vaḥ sa dhītaye naśat ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yaṁ: who/that
    • yajñaṁ: sacrifice/ritual
    • nayathā: lead/guide
    • nara: man/human
    • ādityā: O Adityas (the solar deities)
    • ṛjunā: straight/right
    • pathā: path/way
    • pra: forth/forward
    • vaḥ: your
    • sa: that
    • dhītaye: for understanding/knowledge
    • naśat: may we attain
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks to the importance of guidance in the pursuit of knowledge and understanding.
    • It emphasizes the role of rituals (yajña) in connecting humans (nara) with divine forces (ādityā) to find the right path (ṛjunā pathā).
    • The phrase "pra vaḥ sa dhītaye naśat" suggests a collective aspiration for wisdom and enlightenment.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern life, this mantra can be related to the importance of mentorship and guidance in personal and professional development.
    • For instance, students often seek guidance from teachers or mentors to navigate their educational paths, much like the guidance sought from the divine in the mantra.
    • In corporate settings, leaders often emphasize the importance of teamwork and collective wisdom to achieve organizational goals, reflecting the essence of "pra vaḥ sa dhītaye."
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine guidance in the pursuit of knowledge and the right path in life.
    • It serves as a reminder that while individual effort is important, seeking guidance and wisdom from others, including spiritual forces, can lead to greater understanding and success.
    • In various aspects of life, from education to career, the principles embodied in this mantra encourage individuals to seek out mentors and to engage in collective efforts for knowledge and growth.
