मन्त्र: उता वाजिनं पुरुनिष्किध्वानं दधिक्राम उ ददथुर विश्वकृष्टिम् | ऋजिप्यं श्येनं पुरुषिताप्सुम् आशुं चर्कृत्यम् आर्यो नृपतिं न शूरम् ||
uta vājinam puruniṣṣidhvānaṁ dadhikrām u dadathur viśvakṛṣṭim | ṛjipyaṁ śyenam pruṣitapsum āśuṁ carkṛtyam aryo nṛpatiṁ na śūram ||
  • uta vājinam puruniṣṣidhvānaṁ: This phrase can be interpreted as a call to the powerful and swift (vājinam) forces of nature or deities (puruniṣṣidhvānaṁ). In real life, this can be likened to the forces that drive success and progress, such as innovation and determination.
  • dadhikrām u dadathur viśvakṛṣṭim: Here, "dadhikrām" suggests the act of moving forward or advancing, while "viśvakṛṣṭim" refers to universal prosperity or well-being. This can be related to the idea of collective growth in society, where the advancement of one contributes to the welfare of all, similar to community development projects that uplift entire neighborhoods.
  • ṛjipyaṁ śyenam pruṣitapsum: "ṛjipyaṁ" indicates righteousness or truth, and "śyenam" refers to the eagle, a symbol of vision and clarity. This can be seen in real life through leaders who embody integrity and have a clear vision for their communities, much like a mentor guiding others towards ethical paths.
  • āśuṁ carkṛtyam aryo nṛpatiṁ na śūram: "āśuṁ" means swift or quick, while "carkṛtyam" refers to the act of action or doing. "nṛpatiṁ" signifies a ruler or leader, and "na śūram" implies not being cowardly. This can be exemplified by leaders who take decisive actions in times of crisis, demonstrating courage and quick thinking, akin to emergency responders during natural disasters.

  • Conclusion: This mantra emphasizes the importance of strength, righteousness, and collective progress. It highlights the role of leaders who act swiftly and with integrity for the betterment of society. In real life, these principles can be applied in various scenarios, such as community leadership, corporate governance, and personal development. By embodying these qualities, individuals and leaders can inspire others and contribute to a more prosperous and harmonious world.
