नू मे ब्रह्माण्य अग्न उच छशाधि त्वं देव मगवद्भ्यः सुषूदः |रातौ स्यामोभयासा आ ते यूयम् पात स्वस्तिभिः सदा नः ||
nū me brahmāṇy agna uc chaśādhi tvaṁ deva maghavadbhyaḥ suṣūdaḥ | rātau syāmobhayāsa ā te yūyam pāta svastibhiḥ sadā naḥ ||
  • nū me brahmāṇy agna: This phrase can be translated as "O Agni, the divine fire, I invoke you." Here, Agni represents the sacred fire, which is a significant element in Vedic rituals and symbolizes purity and the divine presence.
  • uc chaśādhi tvaṁ deva: This means "may you shine forth, O God." It emphasizes the brilliance and illuminating nature of Agni, akin to how a guiding light helps us navigate through darkness in our lives.
  • maghavadbhyaḥ suṣūdaḥ: This translates to "may you bestow blessings upon us." It reflects the desire for divine grace and abundance, similar to how we seek support and blessings in our personal and professional endeavors.
  • rātau syāmobhayāsa: This phrase means "may we be protected both day and night." It signifies the need for constant protection and guidance, akin to how we rely on our loved ones and community for support in our daily lives.
  • ā te yūyam pāta: This translates to "may you always protect us." It expresses a plea for ongoing safety and well-being, similar to how we seek security in our homes and relationships.
  • svastibhiḥ sadā naḥ: This means "may we always be in a state of well-being." It highlights the importance of health and prosperity, reflecting our universal desire for a fulfilling and happy life.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a deep yearning for divine guidance, protection, and blessings in our lives. It emphasizes the significance of Agni as a symbol of purity and illumination, reminding us of the importance of seeking support from the divine and our community. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applicable during rituals, personal prayers, or moments of reflection when we seek strength and guidance in our endeavors. It serves as a reminder to cultivate a sense of gratitude and connection with the divine, fostering a sense of well-being and security in our lives.
