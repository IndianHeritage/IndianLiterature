मन्त्र: प्रोग्राम पीतिं वृष्ण इयर्मि सत्यां प्रयै सुतस्य हर्यश्व तुभ्यम् |इन्द्र धेनाभिर इह मादयस्व धीभिर विश्वाभिः शच्याः गृणानः ||
progrām pītiṁ vṛṣṇa iyarmi satyām prayai sutasya haryaśva tubhyam | indra dhenābhir iha mādayasva dhībhir viśvābhiḥ śacyā gṛṇānaḥ ||
  • Progrām pītiṁ vṛṣṇa iyarmi satyām prayai sutasya haryaśva tubhyam:
    • Progrām: This word can be interpreted as "to move forward" or "to progress." It signifies the importance of advancement in life.
    • Pītiṁ: Refers to "joy" or "happiness." It emphasizes the pursuit of joy in our endeavors.
    • Vṛṣṇa: This term is often associated with strength and vitality, representing the power of the divine.
    • Iyarmi: Means "I invoke" or "I call upon," indicating a plea or request for divine assistance.
    • Satyām: Translates to "truth," highlighting the significance of honesty and integrity in our actions.
    • Prayai: This word means "to strive" or "to work towards," suggesting the need for effort in achieving goals.
    • Sutasya: Refers to "the offspring" or "the progeny," which can symbolize the fruits of our labor or the results of our actions.
    • Haryaśva: This term can be interpreted as "the one who brings happiness," often associated with divine blessings.
    • Tubhyam: Means "to you," indicating a direct address to the divine or a higher power.
  • Indra dhenābhir iha mādayasva:
    • Indra: The king of gods, representing power and authority.
    • Dhenābhir: Refers to "with the cows," symbolizing abundance and prosperity.
    • Iha: Means "here," indicating the present moment or our current situation.
    • Mādayasva: Translates to "may you be pleased," a request for divine favor and joy.
  • Dhībhir viśvābhiḥ śacyā gṛṇānaḥ:
    • Dhībhir: Refers to "with wisdom" or "intellect," emphasizing the importance of knowledge in our pursuits.
    • Viśvābhiḥ: Means "with all," suggesting inclusivity and unity in our efforts.
    • Śacyā: Refers to "strength" or "power," indicating the need for resilience.
    • Gṛṇānaḥ: Means "singing" or "praising," highlighting the importance of gratitude and acknowledgment of divine blessings.

Conclusion: This mantra emphasizes the significance of striving for joy and truth while invoking divine blessings for strength and wisdom. In real life, it encourages individuals to pursue their goals with integrity and gratitude, recognizing the importance of community and divine support. Whether in personal endeavors, professional pursuits, or spiritual growth, the principles outlined in this mantra can guide individuals towards a fulfilling and prosperous life.
