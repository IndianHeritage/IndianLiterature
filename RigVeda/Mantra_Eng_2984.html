वीरस्य नु स्वश्व्यं जनासः प्र नु वोचाम विदुर अस्य देवाः |षोल्हा युक्ताः पञ्च-पञ्चा वहन्ति महद देवानाम असुरत्वम् एकम् ||
vīrasya nu svaśvyaṁ janāsaḥ pra nu vocāma vidur asya devāḥ | ṣoḻhā yuktāḥ pañca-pañcā vahanti mahad devānām asuratvam ekam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • vīrasya: of the hero
    • nu: indeed
    • svaśvyaṁ: of one's own
    • janāsaḥ: people or beings
    • pra: forth or forward
    • nu: indeed
    • vocāma: we speak or we declare
    • vidur: know or understand
    • asya: of this
    • devāḥ: the gods
    • ṣoḻhā: sixteen
    • yuktāḥ: united or connected
    • pañca-pañcā: five and five
    • vahanti: carry or bear
    • mahad: great
    • devānām: of the gods
    • asuratvam: the power or strength
    • ekam: one
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the strength and unity of the people, particularly in relation to the divine.
    • It emphasizes the importance of collective strength and the acknowledgment of divine guidance.
    • The reference to "sixteen united" suggests a harmonious collaboration, which can be seen in various aspects of life, such as teamwork in organizations or communities working together for a common goal.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, a team of sixteen individuals from different departments can come together to launch a new product, showcasing the power of collaboration.
    • In community service, various groups may unite to address local issues, demonstrating how collective efforts can lead to significant change.
    • In sports, a team working in unison, each player contributing their unique skills, exemplifies the essence of this mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for unity and strength among people, guided by a higher power.
    • It serves as a reminder that when individuals come together with a shared purpose, they can achieve greatness.
    • This principle is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal relationships to professional environments, highlighting the importance of collaboration and mutual support.
