आ नारीस्य दक्षिणा व्यश्वाम् एतु सोमिनः |स्थूरं च राधः शतवत सहस्रवत ||
ā nāryasya dakṣiṇā vyaśvām̐ etu sominaḥ | sthūraṁ ca rādhaḥ śatavat sahasravat ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā: from
    • nāryasya: of the woman
    • dakṣiṇā: the right or auspicious
    • vyaśvām̐: may it come
    • etu: let it arrive
    • sominaḥ: of the moon or soma (nectar)
    • sthūraṁ: substantial or solid
    • ca: and
    • rādhaḥ: prosperity or wealth
    • śatavat: of hundreds
    • sahasravat: of thousands
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the auspiciousness associated with women and the blessings of prosperity.
    • The phrase "ā nāryasya dakṣiṇā" emphasizes the importance of women in society, suggesting that their well-being is crucial for the overall prosperity.
    • "Vyaśvām̐ etu sominaḥ" indicates a desire for the arrival of blessings, akin to the nourishing qualities of the moon or soma, which is often associated with fertility and abundance.
    • The latter part, "sthūraṁ ca rādhaḥ," signifies a call for substantial wealth and prosperity, highlighting the need for both material and spiritual richness.
    • The terms "śatavat" and "sahasravat" suggest that the blessings sought are not just for the individual but are to be multiplied, indicating a wish for collective prosperity.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern society, this mantra can be reflected in initiatives that empower women, recognizing their role in fostering economic growth and community well-being.
    • For instance, microfinance programs that support women entrepreneurs can be seen as a practical application of this mantra, as they help in creating substantial wealth for families and communities.
    • Additionally, community gatherings that celebrate women's contributions can serve as a reminder of the importance of nurturing and supporting women for overall societal prosperity.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of women in the fabric of society and the need for their empowerment to achieve collective prosperity.
    • It serves as a reminder that true wealth is not just material but also encompasses the well-being and upliftment of all members of society, particularly women.
    • In real-life scenarios, it encourages actions and policies that promote gender equality and support for women, ultimately leading to a more prosperous and harmonious society.
