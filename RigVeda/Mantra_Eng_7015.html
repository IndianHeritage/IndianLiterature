आ त्वा सुतास इन्दवो मदा य इन्द्र गिर्वणः |आपो न वज्रिन्न अन्व ओक्यं सरः पृणन्ति शूर राधसे ||
ā tvā sutāsa indavo madā ya indra girvaṇaḥ | āpo na vajrinn anv okya1ṁ saraḥ pṛṇanti śūra rādhase ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā tvā: To you
    • sutāsa: Sons or offspring
    • indavo: O Indra (the king of gods)
    • madā: Joy or intoxication
    • ya: Who
    • indra: O Indra
    • girvaṇaḥ: Of the songs or hymns
    • āpo: Waters
    • na: Not
    • vajrinn: With the thunderbolt
    • anv: Following
    • okyaṁ: The abode or dwelling
    • saraḥ: The waters or streams
    • pṛṇanti: Fill or nourish
    • śūra: Brave or heroic
    • rādhase: For the one who is worthy or deserving
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes Indra, the king of gods, and speaks of the joy and intoxication that his sons (the divine forces) bring.
    • It emphasizes the importance of the waters, which are essential for life, and how they nourish the brave and deserving.
    • The mention of "vajrinn" (thunderbolt) signifies strength and power, suggesting that the divine forces protect and empower those who are worthy.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In agriculture, the invocation of water is crucial. Just as the mantra highlights the importance of water in nourishing life, farmers pray for rain to ensure a good harvest.
    • In personal development, individuals often seek inspiration and strength from higher powers or ideals, similar to how the mantra seeks the blessings of Indra for courage and success.
    • In community settings, leaders may embody the qualities of bravery and worthiness, drawing on the support of their community (the "waters") to achieve common goals.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the interconnectedness of divine blessings, nature, and human effort.
    • It teaches us to seek strength and nourishment from higher powers while recognizing our own role in cultivating bravery and worthiness.
    • In real life, it serves as a reminder to honor the forces that sustain us and to strive for excellence in our endeavors, ensuring that we are deserving of the blessings we seek.
