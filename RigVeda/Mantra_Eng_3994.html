असावी ते जुजुषाणाय सोमः क्रत्वे दक्षाय बृहत मदाय |हरि रथे सुदुरा योगे अर्वाग इन्द्र प्रिय कृत्नुहि हूयमानः ||
asāvi te jujuṣāṇāya somaḥ kratve dakṣāya bṛhate madāya | harī rathe sudhurā yoge arvāg indra priyā kṛṇuhi hūyamānaḥ ||
  • asāvi te jujuṣāṇāya somaḥ: This phrase refers to the "Soma," a sacred drink in Vedic rituals, which is believed to bestow strength and vitality. The term "asāvi" implies a call for the Soma to come forth for the benefit of the worshippers.
  • kratve dakṣāya bṛhate madāya: Here, "kratve" signifies the power of action or sacrifice, while "dakṣāya" means skill or competence. "Bṛhate" refers to greatness, and "madāya" indicates intoxication or bliss. Together, this line emphasizes the importance of skilled action and the pursuit of greatness, leading to a state of bliss.
  • harī rathe sudhurā yoge: "Harī" refers to the divine or the auspicious, while "rathe" means chariot. "Sudhurā" suggests something that is difficult to attain, and "yoge" refers to union or connection. This line symbolizes the journey of life as a chariot ride, where the divine guides us through challenging paths.
  • arvāg indra priyā kṛṇuhi hūyamānaḥ: "Arvāg" means forward or ahead, while "indra" refers to the king of gods, representing power and authority. "Priyā" means beloved, and "kṛṇuhi" is a call to action. "Hūyamānaḥ" indicates being invoked or called upon. This line expresses a plea for divine assistance in moving forward in life, invoking the blessings of Indra.

  • Significance: This mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking divine support in our endeavors. It highlights the importance of skilled action, the pursuit of greatness, and the need for divine guidance in overcoming life's challenges.
  • Real-life Application: In practical terms, this mantra can be applied in various aspects of life, such as:
    • Career Growth: When facing challenges at work, one can recite this mantra to seek clarity and strength in decision-making.
    • Personal Development: It serves as a reminder to pursue greatness through skillful actions, encouraging individuals to strive for excellence in their personal goals.
    • Spiritual Journey: The mantra can be used in meditation to invoke divine guidance, helping individuals navigate through difficult times with faith and resilience.

  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the interconnectedness of divine support, skilled action, and the pursuit of greatness. By invoking these principles, individuals can find strength and guidance in their personal and professional lives, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling existence.
