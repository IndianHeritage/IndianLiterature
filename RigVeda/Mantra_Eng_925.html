तां अस्य पृषणायुवः सोमं श्रीणन्ति पृष्णयः |प्रियā इन्द्रस्य धेनवो वज्रं हिन्वन्ति सायकम् वस्वीर अनु स्वराज्यम् ||
tā asya pṛśanāyuvaḥ somaṁ śrīṇanti pṛśnayaḥ | priyā indrasya dhenavo vajraṁ hinvanti sāyakaṁ vasvīr anu svarājyam ||
  • tā asya pṛśanāyuvaḥ somaṁ śrīṇanti pṛśnayaḥ: This line refers to the divine qualities of the "Soma," a sacred drink in Vedic tradition, which is associated with immortality and divine inspiration. The term "pṛśanāyuvaḥ" can be interpreted as those who are endowed with strength and vitality. In real life, this can be likened to individuals who possess a strong spirit and resilience, often inspiring others around them.
  • priyā indrasya dhenavo vajraṁ hinvanti: Here, "Indra" is the king of the gods, and "dhenavo" refers to the cows, which symbolize nourishment and abundance. The "vajraṁ" or thunderbolt signifies strength and power. This line suggests that the beloved of Indra, or those who are favored by the divine, are capable of wielding great strength and influence. In practical terms, this can be seen in leaders or mentors who guide and empower others, much like a strong leader who inspires their team to achieve greatness.
  • sāyakaṁ vasvīr anu svarājyam: The term "sāyakaṁ" refers to weapons or tools, while "vasvīr" implies those who are prosperous or wealthy. "Anu svarājyam" can be interpreted as following the path of sovereignty or authority. This line emphasizes the importance of having the right tools and resources to achieve success and prosperity. In everyday life, this can be related to the importance of education, skills, and resources in achieving one's goals, much like how a well-equipped individual can navigate challenges effectively.

  • Conclusion: This mantra encapsulates the essence of divine blessings, strength, and the importance of resources in achieving success. It signifies that with divine favor, resilience, and the right tools, individuals can navigate life's challenges and attain prosperity. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various contexts such as leadership, personal development, and community building, where the combination of inspiration, strength, and resources leads to collective success and well-being.
