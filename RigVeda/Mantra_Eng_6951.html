दधानो गोमद अश्ववत सुवीर्यम आदित्यजूत एधते |सदा रायाऽपुरुष्पृहा ||
dadhāno gomad aśvavat suvīryam ādityajūta edhate | sadā rāyā puruspṛhā ||
  • Translation: The mantra can be translated as: "He who possesses strength like a horse, who is endowed with the brilliance of the sun, may he always bring us wealth and fulfill our desires."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • dadhāno: One who possesses or holds.
    • gomad: Like a cow, symbolizing nourishment and wealth.
    • aśvavat: Like a horse, representing strength and vitality.
    • suvīryam: Excellent strength or valor.
    • ādityajūta: Endowed with the brilliance of the sun, symbolizing radiance and energy.
    • edhate: May he bring or may he fulfill.
    • sadā: Always.
    • rāyā: Wealth or prosperity.
    • puruspṛhā: The desire of a person or the longing for fulfillment.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be invoked by entrepreneurs seeking strength and prosperity in their ventures. For instance, a startup founder may recite this mantra to attract success and resources.
    • In personal life, individuals may use this mantra during times of struggle, seeking the strength and vitality to overcome challenges, much like athletes who draw on their inner strength to achieve their goals.
    • In community gatherings, this mantra can be recited to inspire collective strength and unity, encouraging members to work together towards common goals, similar to how a sports team rallies for victory.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its invocation of strength, prosperity, and fulfillment of desires. It serves as a reminder that with the right mindset and energy, individuals can attract wealth and success into their lives. In real-life scenarios, whether in personal endeavors, business pursuits, or community efforts, this mantra encourages individuals to harness their inner strength and work towards their aspirations, embodying the qualities of vitality and brilliance akin to a horse and the sun. By integrating this mantra into daily practices, one can cultivate a mindset geared towards achieving their goals and fulfilling their desires.
