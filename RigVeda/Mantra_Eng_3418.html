अवीवृद्धन्त गोतमा इन्द्र त्वे स्तोमवाहसः |ऐषु धा वीरवत् यशः ||
avīvṛdhanta gotamā indra tve stomavāhasaḥ | aiṣu dhā vīravad yaśaḥ ||
  • Mantra: "avīvṛdhanta gotamā indra tve stomavāhasaḥ | aiṣu dhā vīravad yaśaḥ ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • avīvṛdhanta: Those who do not hold back or restrain.
    • gotamā: Referring to the sage Gotama or the lineage of Gotama.
    • indra: The king of gods, representing strength and power.
    • tve: To you (Indra).
    • stomavāhasaḥ: Those who carry praises or hymns.
    • aiṣu: In this context, it can mean "in this way" or "thus."
    • dhā: To bestow or grant.
    • vīravad: Like a hero or with valor.
    • yaśaḥ: Fame or glory.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes Indra, the powerful deity, asking him to bestow strength and glory upon those who do not hold back in their pursuits.
    • The reference to "gotamā" suggests a connection to wisdom and knowledge, emphasizing the importance of these qualities in achieving success.
    • The phrase "stomavāhasaḥ" highlights the significance of praise and recognition, indicating that those who are celebrated and honored are worthy of divine blessings.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, a team that works diligently and does not shy away from challenges often receives recognition and rewards, akin to the blessings of Indra.
    • In sports, athletes who train hard and push their limits are celebrated for their achievements, reflecting the valor and glory mentioned in the mantra.
    • In education, students who actively participate and seek knowledge are often recognized for their efforts, embodying the spirit of "gotamā."
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of courage, dedication, and the pursuit of knowledge in achieving success and recognition.
    • It serves as a reminder that those who strive without restraint and seek to uplift themselves and others will be rewarded with strength and glory.
    • In real life, this mantra can inspire individuals to pursue their goals with determination and to recognize the value of hard work and valor in their endeavors.
