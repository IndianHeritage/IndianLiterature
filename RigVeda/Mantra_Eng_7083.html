यम् ऋत्विजो बहुधा कल्पयन्तः सचेतसो यज्ञं इमं वहन्ति |यो अनूचानो ब्राह्मणो युक्त आसीत का स्वित तत्र यजमानस्य संवीत ||
yam ṛtvijo bahudhā kalpayantaḥ sacetaso yajñam imaṁ vahanti | yo anūcāno brāhmaṇo yukta āsīt kā svit tatra yajamānasya saṁvit ||
  • yam ṛtvijo bahudhā kalpayantaḥ: This phrase refers to the various ṛtvijas (priests or seers) who, with their diverse knowledge and skills, conceptualize and prepare for the yajña (sacred ritual). In real life, this can be likened to a team of professionals working together to plan and execute a significant project, each contributing their expertise.
  • sacetaso yajñam imaṁ vahanti: Here, it indicates that those who are mindful and focused carry out the yajña. This can be compared to individuals in a workplace who are dedicated and fully engaged in their tasks, ensuring that the project is successful through their commitment and attention to detail.
  • yo anūcāno brāhmaṇo yukta āsīt: This part speaks of a brāhmaṇa (a learned person or priest) who is well-versed and aligned with the principles of the yajña. In modern terms, this could represent a mentor or leader who guides a team with wisdom and knowledge, ensuring that everyone is on the right path.
  • kā svit tatra yajamānasya saṁvit: This phrase questions the awareness or understanding of the yajamāna (the person performing the ritual). It emphasizes the importance of being conscious and informed about the actions being taken. In everyday life, this can relate to the necessity of being aware of one’s responsibilities and the impact of one’s actions on the larger community or organization.

  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of collaboration, mindfulness, and awareness in any endeavor, particularly in rituals or projects that require collective effort. In real-life scenarios, it highlights the importance of teamwork, the role of knowledgeable leaders, and the necessity of being conscious of one’s actions and their implications. This mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as in workplaces, community projects, or any situation where collective effort and awareness are crucial for success.
