स हि स्वसृत पृषदश्वो युवा गणोऽयां ईशानस् तविषीभिर आवृतः |असि सत्य ऋणयावानेद्योऽस्याः धियः प्राविताथा वृषा गणः ||
sa hi svasṛt pṛṣadaśvo yuvā gaṇo3 'yā īśānas taviṣībhir āvṛtaḥ | asi satya ṛṇayāvānedyo 'syā dhiyaḥ prāvitāthā vṛṣā gaṇaḥ ||
  • sa hi svasṛt pṛṣadaśvo yuvā gaṇo: This phrase can be interpreted as "Indeed, the youthful assembly of horses." Here, "svasṛt" refers to the idea of being born or emerging, while "pṛṣadaśvo" signifies the strength and vitality of horses, often symbolizing energy and movement in life.
  • yā īśānas taviṣībhir āvṛtaḥ: This translates to "which is enveloped by the powerful." The term "īśānas" refers to the divine or the powerful forces that govern the universe, suggesting that the youthful energy is protected and guided by higher powers.
  • asi satya ṛṇayāvānedyo: This means "you are the truth, the one who is not to be denied." Here, "satya" represents truth, emphasizing the importance of honesty and integrity in our lives. It suggests that truth is an essential foundation for any endeavor.
  • 'syā dhiyaḥ prāvitāthā vṛṣā gaṇaḥ: This can be interpreted as "the thoughts that are directed towards the powerful assembly." "Dhiyaḥ" refers to intellect or thought, indicating that our thoughts should be aligned with strength and purpose, leading to fruitful outcomes.

  • Significance: This mantra emphasizes the importance of youthful energy, the guidance of higher powers, the value of truth, and the alignment of thoughts with strength. It reflects the interconnectedness of these elements in achieving success and fulfillment in life.
  • Real-life Application: In practical terms, this mantra can be applied in various aspects of life, such as:
    • Personal Development: Embracing youthful energy and vitality can inspire individuals to pursue their goals with enthusiasm and vigor.
    • Leadership: Acknowledging the guidance of higher powers or mentors can help leaders make informed decisions and inspire their teams.
    • Integrity in Actions: Upholding truth in personal and professional relationships fosters trust and respect, essential for collaboration and success.
    • Focused Intentions: Aligning thoughts with strength and purpose can lead to clearer goals and more effective actions in achieving them.

  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a reminder of the vital elements that contribute to a successful and meaningful life. By harnessing youthful energy, seeking guidance, valuing truth, and aligning our thoughts with strength, we can navigate life's challenges and achieve our aspirations.
