आपī वो अस्मे पितरेव पुत्रोgreव रुचā नृपतīव तुर्वै | इर्येव पुष्ट्यै किरणेव भुज्यै श्रुष्टīvानेव हवम् ā गमिष्टम् ||
āpī vo asme pitareva putrogreva rucā nṛpatīva turyai | iryeva puṣṭyai kiraṇeva bhujyai śruṣṭīvāneva havam ā gamiṣṭam ||
  • Transliteration: āpī vo asme pitareva putrogreva rucā nṛpatīva turyai | iryeva puṣṭyai kiraṇeva bhujyai śruṣṭīvāneva havam ā gamiṣṭam ||
  • Meaning:
    • āpī: May we be nourished or sustained.
    • vo: By you (the divine or the gods).
    • asme: We are.
    • pitareva: Like a father.
    • putrogreva: Like a son who is dear.
    • rucā: With brilliance or radiance.
    • nṛpatīva: Like a king.
    • turyai: With the sound of the drum (symbolizing victory or celebration).
    • iryeva: Like a path or way.
    • puṣṭyai: For nourishment or prosperity.
    • kiraṇeva: Like rays (of light).
    • bhujyai: For enjoyment or consumption.
    • śruṣṭīvāneva: Like a creation or manifestation.
    • havam: The offering or invocation.
    • ā gamiṣṭam: May it come to us or may it be attained.
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra expresses a deep yearning for sustenance and blessings from the divine.
    • It likens the relationship between the devotee and the divine to that of a father and son, emphasizing love, care, and protection.
    • The mention of "nṛpatīva" suggests a desire for leadership qualities, wisdom, and the ability to rule justly, akin to a king.
    • The imagery of light and nourishment indicates a quest for knowledge, prosperity, and fulfillment in life.
    • In real life, this mantra can be seen as a call for support in personal and professional endeavors, seeking guidance and strength to overcome challenges.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its holistic approach to life, encompassing spiritual, emotional, and material aspects.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, such as during times of uncertainty, when seeking guidance in leadership roles, or when striving for personal growth and prosperity.
    • By reciting this mantra, individuals can cultivate a sense of connection with the divine, fostering a supportive environment for their aspirations and endeavors.
