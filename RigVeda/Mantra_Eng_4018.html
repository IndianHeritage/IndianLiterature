सदापृणो यजतो विद्विषो वधीद बाहुवृक्तः श्रुतवित तर्यो वः सचा |उभा स वराः प्रत्य एति भाति च यद ईं गणं भजते सुप्रयावभिः ||
sadāpṛṇo yajato vi dviṣo vadhīd bāhuvṛktaḥ śrutavit taryo vaḥ sacā | ubhā sa varā praty eti bhāti ca yad īṁ gaṇam bhajate suprayāvabhiḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sadāpṛṇo: Always fulfilled or satisfied.
    • yajato: Those who perform sacrifices or rituals.
    • vi dviṣo: Against the enemies or those who oppose.
    • vadhīd: May they be vanquished or defeated.
    • bāhuvṛktaḥ: With strong arms or powerful strength.
    • śrutavit: Knowledgeable or wise.
    • taryo: May you be protected or safeguarded.
    • vaḥ: For you or your.
    • sacā: And also.
    • ubhā: Both or together.
    • sa: That or he.
    • varā: Excellent or auspicious.
    • praty: Towards or in the direction of.
    • eti: Comes or approaches.
    • bhāti: Shines or manifests.
    • ca: And.
    • yad: Whatever or that which.
    • īṁ: The divine or the sacred.
    • gaṇam: Group or assembly.
    • bhajate: Worships or serves.
    • suprayāvabhiḥ: With excellent offerings or well-prepared gifts.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of performing rituals and sacrifices with a pure heart and intention.
    • It suggests that those who engage in such practices will be fulfilled and protected from adversaries.
    • The phrase "may they be vanquished" indicates a desire for overcoming obstacles and negativity in life.
    • The mantra also highlights the significance of knowledge and wisdom in achieving success and protection.
    • It encourages unity and collective worship, suggesting that when a group comes together with a common purpose, they can achieve greater results.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, teams that work together harmoniously and support each other often achieve their goals more effectively than those that do not.
    • In personal life, individuals who engage in community service or collective rituals often find a sense of fulfillment and protection from negative influences.
    • Educational institutions that promote collaborative learning and knowledge sharing create an environment where students thrive and succeed.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the power of collective effort, knowledge, and the importance of maintaining a positive and supportive environment.
    • It is applicable in various aspects of life, including personal relationships, professional settings, and community engagements.
    • By embodying the principles of this mantra, individuals and groups can navigate challenges more effectively and achieve their aspirations.
