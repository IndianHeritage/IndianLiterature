विः ये चृतन्त्य ऋता सपन्त आद इद वसूनी प्र ववाचास्मै ||
vi ye cṛtanty ṛtā sapanta ād id vasūni pra vavācāsmai ||
  • Word Breakdown:
    • vi: a prefix that can mean "apart" or "in different ways."
    • ye: means "those who."
    • cṛtanty: derived from the root "cṛ," meaning "to create" or "to accomplish."
    • ṛtā: refers to "truth" or "cosmic order."
    • sapanta: means "those who are wise" or "intelligent."
    • ād: means "first" or "beginning."
    • id: translates to "this" or "here."
    • vasūni: refers to "wealth" or "prosperity."
    • pra: means "forth" or "forward."
    • vavāc: means "to speak" or "to declare."
    • asmai: means "to him" or "to that person."
  • Meaning:
    • This mantra speaks about those who create and uphold the truth and cosmic order.
    • It emphasizes the importance of wisdom and intelligence in achieving prosperity.
    • The phrase suggests that those who are wise and truthful will be able to declare or manifest wealth and abundance.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, a company that operates with integrity and transparency is more likely to succeed and attract loyal customers.
    • In personal relationships, individuals who communicate honestly and wisely tend to build stronger, more fulfilling connections.
    • In education, students who seek knowledge and truth are often more successful in their academic and professional pursuits.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its emphasis on the relationship between truth, wisdom, and prosperity.
    • It teaches that by adhering to truth and wisdom, individuals can manifest abundance in their lives.
    • This principle is applicable in various aspects of life, including personal growth, professional success, and social relationships.
