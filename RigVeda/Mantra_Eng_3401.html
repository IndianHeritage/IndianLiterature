अस्मां अवन्तु ते शतम् अस्मान् सहस्रं ऊतयः |अस्मान् विश्वा अभिष्टयः ||
asmām̐ avantu te śatam asmān sahasram ūtayaḥ | asmān viśvā abhiṣṭayaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • asmām̐: "us" or "to us"
    • avantu: "may protect" or "may guard"
    • te: "your" or "of you"
    • śatam: "hundred"
    • asmān: "us" or "to us" (repeated for emphasis)
    • sahasram: "thousand"
    • ūtayaḥ: "may be prosperous" or "may be enriched"
    • viśvā: "all" or "universal"
    • abhiṣṭayaḥ: "may bless" or "may grant wishes"
  • Overall Meaning:
    • This mantra is a prayer for protection and prosperity.
    • It expresses a desire for the blessings of the divine to be upon the speaker and their community.
    • The repetition of "us" emphasizes the collective nature of the prayer, highlighting the importance of community and shared well-being.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a family setting, this mantra can be recited during gatherings to invoke protection and prosperity for all members.
    • In a workplace, teams may chant this mantra before starting a project to ensure collective success and safeguard against challenges.
    • During community events or festivals, this mantra can be used to foster unity and a sense of shared purpose among participants.
  • Conclusion and Significance:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking divine protection and blessings not just for oneself, but for the entire community.
    • It serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of individuals and the collective responsibility towards each other's well-being.
    • In real life, it can be applied in various scenarios where teamwork, collaboration, and mutual support are essential for success and harmony.
