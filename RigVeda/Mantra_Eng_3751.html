सखायास ते विषुणा अग्न एते शिवासः सन्तो अशिवा अभूवन् |अधूर्षत स्वयम् एते वचोभिर ऋजूयते वृजिनानि ब्रुवन्तः ||
sakhāyas te viṣuṇā agna ete śivāsaḥ santo aśivā abhūvan | adhūrṣata svayam ete vacobhir ṛjūyate vṛjināni bruvantaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sakhāyas: friends or companions
    • te: your
    • viṣuṇā: with the power of speech
    • agna: O Agni (the fire god)
    • ete: these
    • śivāsaḥ: auspicious or benevolent
    • santo: those who are peaceful
    • aśivā: those who are not auspicious
    • abhūvan: have not become
    • adhūrṣata: they have not been deceived
    • svayam: themselves
    • ete: these
    • vacobhir: with words
    • ṛjūyate: are made straight or correct
    • vṛjināni: obstacles or sins
    • bruvantaḥ: speaking or declaring
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra addresses Agni, the fire god, and speaks of the importance of companionship and communication.
    • It emphasizes the role of friends who, through their words, can help in overcoming obstacles and ensuring auspiciousness in life.
    • The mantra suggests that those who are peaceful and benevolent do not become entangled in negativity or deceit.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace, having supportive colleagues (sakhāyas) can lead to a more productive environment. Their encouragement can help overcome challenges (vṛjināni) and foster a positive atmosphere.
    • In personal relationships, friends who communicate openly (vacobhir) can help each other navigate through difficult times, ensuring that negativity does not take root.
    • In community settings, leaders who promote dialogue and understanding can create a harmonious society, where the auspicious (śivāsaḥ) prevails over the inauspicious (aśivā).
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of friendship and communication in achieving a harmonious and auspicious life.
    • It teaches us that through supportive relationships and clear communication, we can navigate life's challenges and foster positivity.
    • In essence, it serves as a reminder of the power of words and companionship in overcoming obstacles and promoting well-being in various aspects of life.
