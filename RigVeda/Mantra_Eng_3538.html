हंसासो ये वाम मधुमन्तो अस्रिधो हिरण्यपर्णा उहुव उषर्बुधः | उदप्रुतो मन्दिनो मन्दिनिष्पृशो मध्वो न मक्षः सवना गच्छथः ||
haṁsāso ye vām madhumanto asridho hiraṇyaparṇā uhuva uṣarbudhaḥ | udapruto mandino mandinispṛśo madhvo na makṣaḥ savanāni gacchathaḥ ||
  • haṁsāso: This term refers to "swans" or "birds," often symbolizing purity and spiritual elevation. In real life, swans are known for their grace and beauty, representing the pursuit of higher ideals.
  • ye: This means "those who," indicating a group or collective.
  • vām: Translates to "your," suggesting a personal connection or address to the audience.
  • madhumanto: This word means "sweet" or "honeyed," often associated with pleasant experiences or the sweetness of life.
  • asridho: This can be interpreted as "those who are protected" or "sheltered," indicating safety and security.
  • hiraṇyaparṇā: This term means "golden-winged," symbolizing wealth, prosperity, and auspiciousness.
  • uhuva: This means "they have spoken" or "they have declared," indicating communication or expression of thoughts.
  • uṣarbudhaḥ: This can be interpreted as "those who are awakened" or "those who have realized," suggesting enlightenment or awareness.
  • udapruto: This means "having risen" or "having emerged," indicating growth or ascension.
  • mandino: This term refers to "gentle" or "calm," suggesting tranquility and peace.
  • mandinispṛśo: This can be interpreted as "touched by the gentle," indicating a soft influence or gentle touch in life.
  • madhvo: This means "of sweetness," again emphasizing the pleasant aspects of life.
  • na makṣaḥ: This translates to "not to be obstructed," indicating freedom and the absence of barriers.
  • savanāni: This means "offerings" or "sacrifices," often related to rituals and the act of giving.
  • gacchathaḥ: This translates to "go" or "move forward," indicating progress and action.

Conclusion: This mantra emphasizes the importance of spiritual elevation, protection, and the pursuit of sweetness in life. It encourages individuals to rise above challenges, embrace tranquility, and move forward with purpose. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various contexts such as personal growth, community building, and fostering harmonious relationships. By embodying the qualities of purity, gentleness, and awareness, individuals can create a positive impact in their lives and the lives of others, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling existence.
