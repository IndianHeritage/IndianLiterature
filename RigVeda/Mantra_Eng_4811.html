यद इन्द्र नाहुषीष्व आम̐ ओजो नृम्णं च कृष्टिषु |यद वा पञ्च क्षितीनां द्युम्नम आ भर सत्रा विश्वानि पौंस्याः ||
yad indra nāhuṣīṣv ām̐ ojo nṛmṇaṁ ca kṛṣṭiṣu | yad vā pañca kṣitīnāṁ dyumnam ā bhara satrā viśvāni pauṁsyā ||
  • Mantra: "yad indra nāhuṣīṣv ām̐ ojo nṛmṇaṁ ca kṛṣṭiṣu | yad vā pañca kṣitīnāṁ dyumnam ā bhara satrā viśvāni pauṁsyā ||"
  • Translation: This mantra invokes Indra, the king of gods, asking for strength and vitality in human endeavors. It also seeks the blessings of prosperity and success in all aspects of life.
  • Word Breakdown:
    • yad: that which
    • indra: Indra, the king of gods
    • nāhuṣīṣv: in the context of the Nāhuṣa, a legendary king
    • ām̐: a particle indicating emphasis
    • ojo: strength or energy
    • nṛmṇaṁ: human beings
    • kṛṣṭiṣu: in endeavors or efforts
    • vā: or
    • pañca: five
    • kṣitīnāṁ: of the earth or realms
    • dyumnam: glory or prosperity
    • ā bhara: may you bring
    • satrā: offerings or sacrifices
    • viśvāni: all
    • pauṁsyā: of mankind or humanity
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, this mantra can be recited before a major project launch, invoking the strength of Indra to ensure the success of the endeavor.
    • During personal challenges, individuals may reflect on this mantra to seek inner strength and resilience, drawing inspiration from the divine energy it represents.
    • In community gatherings, this mantra can be used to foster unity and collective strength, encouraging participants to work together towards common goals.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine support in human efforts. It emphasizes the importance of strength, unity, and prosperity in all endeavors. In real life, it serves as a reminder to seek help from higher powers during challenging times and to work collaboratively towards achieving success. By invoking the blessings of Indra, individuals and communities can find the motivation and energy needed to overcome obstacles and achieve their goals.
