अग्नि त्रै ते वाजिना त्रि षधस्थाऽतिस्रस् ते जिव्हा ऋतजात पूर्वीः |तिस्र उ ते तन्भो देववातास ताभिर नः पाहि गीरः अप्रयुच्छन् ||
agne trī te vājinā trī ṣadhasthā tisras te jihvā ṛtajāta pūrvīḥ | tisra u te tanvo devavātās tābhir naḥ pāhi giro aprayucchan ||
  • Agne: This word refers to Agni, the fire god, who symbolizes energy, light, and purity. In real life, fire is essential for cooking, warmth, and various rituals.
  • Trī te vājinā trī: This phrase can be interpreted as "threefold strength" or "threefold energy." It signifies the importance of having multiple sources of strength in life, such as physical, mental, and spiritual resilience.
  • Ṣadhasthā: Meaning "sixfold," this term emphasizes the multifaceted nature of existence. In practical terms, it can relate to the six senses (sight, hearing, touch, taste, smell, and intuition) that guide our experiences.
  • Tisras te jihvā: This translates to "three tongues," which can symbolize communication and expression. In everyday life, effective communication is crucial in personal and professional relationships.
  • Ṛtajāta pūrvīḥ: This phrase means "born of truth," highlighting the significance of truthfulness and integrity in our actions and thoughts. In real life, honesty fosters trust and strengthens relationships.
  • Tisra u te tanvo: This can be interpreted as "threefold bodies," referring to the physical, mental, and spiritual aspects of a person. Balancing these aspects is essential for holistic well-being.
  • Devavātās: This term means "divine winds" or "spiritual energies," suggesting the influence of higher powers in our lives. In real life, many people seek guidance from spiritual practices or beliefs.
  • Tābhir naḥ pāhi: This translates to "protect us with these," indicating a plea for divine protection and guidance. In times of uncertainty, many individuals turn to prayer or meditation for comfort and support.
  • Giro aprayucchan: This means "do not let our words go astray," emphasizing the importance of mindful speech. In real life, being careful with our words can prevent misunderstandings and conflicts.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking strength, truth, and divine guidance in our lives. It highlights the importance of communication, integrity, and balance among the physical, mental, and spiritual aspects of our existence. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can serve as a reminder to cultivate resilience, practice honesty, and seek protection from higher powers, ultimately leading to a more harmonious and fulfilling life.
