यत्र क्व च ते मनो दक्षं दधसा उत्तराम् |तत्रा सदः कृत्नवसे ||
yatra kva ca te mano dakṣaṁ dadhasa uttaram | tatrā sadaḥ kṛṇavase ||
  • Mantra: "yatra kva ca te mano dakṣaṁ dadhasa uttaram | tatrā sadaḥ kṛṇavase ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yatra: wherever
    • kva: wherever
    • ca: and
    • te: your
    • mano: mind
    • dakṣaṁ: skillful
    • dadhasa: you bestow
    • uttaram: superior
    • tatrā: there
    • sadaḥ: always
    • kṛṇavase: may we attain
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks to the omnipresence of the mind and its capabilities.
    • It emphasizes the idea that wherever one directs their skillful mind, they can achieve superior outcomes.
    • The phrase "you bestow" suggests that the ability to harness the mind's potential is a gift or a blessing.
    • The concluding part, "may we attain," indicates a collective aspiration for success and enlightenment.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, when a team focuses their collective skills and creativity on a project, they can achieve remarkable results, demonstrating the power of a unified and skillful mind.
    • In personal development, individuals who cultivate mindfulness and direct their thoughts towards positive goals often find themselves achieving greater success and fulfillment in life.
    • In education, students who apply their minds diligently to their studies tend to excel, showcasing the mantra's message about the importance of a focused and skillful mind.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of directing one's mind skillfully towards goals, suggesting that success is attainable wherever one applies their mental faculties.
    • It serves as a reminder of the power of intention and focus in achieving superior outcomes in various aspects of life, including personal growth, professional endeavors, and collective efforts.
    • In essence, it encourages individuals and groups to harness their mental capabilities to create positive change and attain their aspirations.
