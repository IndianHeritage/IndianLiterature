सम मातृभिर न शिशुर वावशानो वृषा दधन्वे पुरुवारो अद्भिः |मर्यो न योषाम अभि निष्कृतं यन् सं गच्छते कलश उष्रियाभिः ||
sam mātṛbhir na śiśur vāvaśāno vṛṣā dadhanve puruvāro adbhiḥ | maryo na yoṣām abhi niṣkṛtaṁ yan saṁ gacchate kalaśa usriyābhiḥ ||
  • Translation: The mantra can be translated as: "May we, with the help of the mothers, be protected from all dangers. May we be strong and courageous, and may we attain the blessings of the divine."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • sam: together, in harmony.
    • mātṛbhir: by the mothers, referring to nurturing and protective forces.
    • na: not, indicating negation of danger.
    • śiśur: child, symbolizing innocence and vulnerability.
    • vāvaśāno: may we be protected, a plea for safety.
    • vṛṣā: strong, representing strength and courage.
    • dadhanve: wielding, indicating action and readiness.
    • puruvāro: the one who is blessed, indicating divine favor.
    • adbhiḥ: with waters, symbolizing nourishment and life.
    • maryo: mortal, referring to human beings.
    • na: not, again indicating negation.
    • yoṣām: of women, highlighting the role of femininity.
    • abhi: towards, indicating direction or focus.
    • niṣkṛtaṁ: liberation, suggesting freedom from troubles.
    • yan: that which, referring to the blessings sought.
    • saṁ: together, indicating unity.
    • gacchate: may go, suggesting movement towards a goal.
    • kalaśa: pot, symbolizing abundance and prosperity.
    • usriyābhiḥ: with blessings, indicating divine grace.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a family setting, this mantra can be invoked during a child's naming ceremony, emphasizing the protective and nurturing role of mothers in a child's life.
    • In a community gathering, it can be recited to foster unity and strength among members, encouraging them to support one another in times of need.
    • During challenging times, such as natural disasters or personal crises, individuals may reflect on this mantra to seek courage and protection from their loved ones and the divine.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its emphasis on protection, strength, and the nurturing aspects of life. It serves as a reminder of the importance of community, familial bonds, and divine blessings in overcoming challenges. In real-life scenarios, it can be applied in various contexts, such as family ceremonies, community support systems, and personal reflections during difficult times. By invoking this mantra, individuals can cultivate a sense of safety and empowerment, fostering resilience in their lives.
