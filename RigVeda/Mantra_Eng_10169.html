नि तद दधिषे 'वरं परं च यस्मिन्न आविथावसā दुरोणे |आ मातरā स्थापयसे जिगत्नू अता इनोषि करवरā पुरूणि ||
ni tad dadhiṣe 'varam paraṁ ca yasminn āvithāvasā duroṇe | ā mātarā sthāpayase jigatnū ata inoṣi karvarā purūṇi ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ni: not
    • tad: that
    • dadhiṣe: you give
    • varam: the best
    • paraṁ: supreme
    • ca: and
    • yasmin: in which
    • āvithāvasā: enveloped
    • duroṇe: in the great strength
    • ā: from
    • mātarā: the mothers
    • sthāpayase: you establish
    • jigatnū: the powerful
    • ata: thus
    • inoṣi: you protect
    • karvarā: the deeds
    • purūṇi: the many
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the divine power that grants the best and supreme qualities.
    • It emphasizes the importance of being enveloped in strength and protection.
    • The reference to "mothers" signifies nurturing and the foundational support in life.
    • The term "jigatnū" refers to the powerful forces that help in establishing stability and strength.
    • It highlights the significance of protecting one's actions and deeds, ensuring they are righteous and beneficial.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a family setting, parents (mothers) provide the nurturing environment that helps children grow strong and capable.
    • In a workplace, a supportive team can envelop an individual in strength, allowing them to perform at their best.
    • In community service, protecting the deeds of kindness ensures that the impact of those actions is felt widely.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of divine support and strength in our lives.
    • It teaches us to seek the best qualities and to protect our actions, ensuring they contribute positively to the world.
    • In real-life scenarios, it encourages us to create nurturing environments, whether at home, work, or in our communities, fostering growth and strength.
