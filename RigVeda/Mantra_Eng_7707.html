गिरस्त इन्द ओजसा मर्मृज्यन्ते अपस्युवः |याभिर मदाय शुम्भसे ||
giras ta inda ojasā marmṛjyante apasyuvaḥ | yābhir madāya śumbhase ||
  • giras: This word refers to "speech" or "words." In a broader sense, it can signify communication and the power of expression.
  • ta: This is a demonstrative pronoun meaning "those" or "that." It indicates something specific that is being referred to.
  • inda: This term can be interpreted as "inspired" or "enlightened." It suggests a state of heightened awareness or divine influence.
  • ojasā: This word means "with energy" or "with strength." It implies vitality and the power that comes from within.
  • marmṛjyante: This term can be translated as "are purified" or "are cleansed." It indicates a process of refinement or elevation.
  • apasyuvaḥ: This word means "those who are not seen" or "invisible." It can refer to the unseen forces or energies that influence our lives.
  • yābhir: This word means "by which" or "through which." It connects the previous concepts to the means or methods of achieving something.
  • madāya: This term translates to "for the purpose of joy" or "for delight." It emphasizes the intention behind actions or words.
  • śumbhase: This word can be interpreted as "to the one who is glorious" or "to the one who is celebrated." It signifies reverence towards a higher power or divine entity.

  • Meaning and Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks to the power of words and communication, suggesting that through inspired and energetic speech, one can purify and elevate their existence.
    • The reference to "those who are not seen" can be understood as the unseen influences in our lives, such as thoughts, emotions, and spiritual energies that shape our reality.
    • The mantra emphasizes the importance of intention, suggesting that when we communicate with joy and purpose, we connect with something greater than ourselves.

  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace setting, effective communication can lead to a more harmonious environment. When team members express their ideas with clarity and enthusiasm, it can inspire collaboration and innovation.
    • In personal relationships, the way we communicate can significantly impact our connections. Speaking with love and intention can strengthen bonds and foster understanding.
    • In spiritual practices, many people use mantras or affirmations to align their thoughts and energies. This practice can lead to a sense of peace and clarity, reflecting the idea of purification through inspired speech.

  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its reminder of the power of words and the importance of intention behind our communication.
    • It encourages us to be mindful of how we express ourselves and to recognize the unseen energies that influence our lives.
    • In real-life scenarios, applying the principles of this mantra can lead to improved relationships, enhanced creativity, and a deeper connection to the divine or the universe.
