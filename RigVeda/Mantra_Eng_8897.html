श्रुधी नो अग्नि सदने सधस्थे युक्ष्वा रथम अमृतस्य द्रवित्नुम् |आ नो वह रोडसी देवपुत्रे माकिर देवानाम् अप भूर इह स्याः ||
śrudhī no agne sadane sadhasthe yukṣvā ratham amṛtasya dravitnum | ā no vaha rodasī devaputre mākir devānām apa bhūr iha syāḥ ||
  • śrudhī no agne: This phrase translates to "O Agni, listen to us." Agni, the fire god, represents energy, transformation, and the divine messenger. In real life, this can be seen as a call for guidance and support in our endeavors, much like seeking advice from a mentor or a trusted friend.
  • sadane sadhasthe: This means "in the abode of prosperity." It signifies a place where abundance and success reside. In practical terms, this could refer to a successful business, a thriving community, or a harmonious family environment where everyone flourishes.
  • yukṣvā ratham amṛtasya dravitnum: This translates to "let us harness the chariot of immortality." The chariot symbolizes the journey of life, and "immortality" can be interpreted as achieving lasting impact or legacy. In real life, this could relate to pursuing goals that contribute to society, such as environmental sustainability or social justice, ensuring that our actions have a long-term positive effect.
  • ā no vaha rodasī: This means "bring us together, O heavens." It emphasizes unity and collaboration. In today's world, this can be applied to teamwork in workplaces, community service, or global cooperation to tackle issues like climate change or poverty.
  • devaputre mākir devānām: This translates to "O son of the gods, may we be blessed by the divine." It reflects the desire for divine blessings and support in our endeavors. In real life, this can be seen in rituals or practices where individuals seek blessings for success, health, and happiness, such as weddings or new ventures.
  • apa bhūr iha syāḥ: This means "may we not be deprived of abundance here." It expresses a wish for prosperity and fulfillment in our current lives. This can relate to the importance of gratitude and recognizing the abundance we have, whether it be in relationships, health, or opportunities.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a profound message about seeking guidance, unity, and divine blessings while striving for prosperity and a lasting legacy. Its significance lies in its applicability to various aspects of life, from personal growth to community building. By invoking these principles, individuals can foster a sense of purpose and connection, ultimately leading to a more harmonious and fulfilling existence.
