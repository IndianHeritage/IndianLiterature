य इन्द्र सस्त्य अव्रतो नुṣ्वापम अदेवयुः |स्वैः षा एवैर मुमुरत पोष्यं रयिं सन्तर धेहि तं ततः ||
ya indra sasty avrato 'nuṣvāpam adevayuḥ | svaiḥ ṣa evair mumurat poṣyaṁ rayiṁ sanutar dhehi taṁ tataḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ya - who
    • indra - Indra, the king of gods
    • sasty - of good fortune
    • avrato - with vows or promises
    • 'nuṣvāpam - may he awaken
    • adevayuḥ - the divine energy
    • svaiḥ - with his own
    • ṣa - six
    • evair - indeed
    • mumurat - may he bestow
    • poṣyaṁ - nourishment
    • rayiṁ - wealth or prosperity
    • sanutar - with great abundance
    • dhehi - grant or bestow
    • taṁ - that
    • tataḥ - thereafter
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes Indra, the god of rain and thunder, symbolizing strength and prosperity.
    • It emphasizes the importance of good fortune and the fulfillment of vows, suggesting that one should remain committed to their promises.
    • The phrase "may he awaken the divine energy" indicates a desire for spiritual awakening and the realization of one's potential.
    • The mention of "six" could symbolize completeness or the six directions (north, south, east, west, above, below), representing a holistic approach to life.
    • The mantra seeks the bestowal of nourishment and wealth, highlighting the need for both physical sustenance and spiritual richness.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be applied when seeking success and prosperity in ventures, emphasizing the importance of ethical practices and commitment to promises made to clients and partners.
    • In personal life, it can serve as a reminder to uphold one's vows in relationships, ensuring that love and support are consistently provided.
    • In spiritual practices, chanting this mantra can help individuals focus on their goals, awaken their inner strength, and attract positive energies into their lives.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of invoking divine support for prosperity and well-being.
    • It teaches the value of commitment and the pursuit of both material and spiritual nourishment.
    • In real-life scenarios, it encourages individuals to align their actions with their promises, fostering a sense of integrity and abundance.
    • Ultimately, it serves as a reminder that true wealth encompasses not just material success but also spiritual fulfillment and ethical living.
