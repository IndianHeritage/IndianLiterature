अग्निर्दाद्द्रविणं वीरपेक्षाऽग्निः ऋषिं यः सहस्रां सनोति। अग्निर्दिवि हव्यं आ ततानाग्नेर धामानि विभृतां पुरुत्रा॥
agnir dād draviṇaṁ vīrapeśā agnir ṛṣiṁ yaḥ sahasrā sanoti | agnir divi havyam ā tatānāgner dhāmāni vibhṛtā purutrā ||
  • Agnir dād draviṇaṁ vīrapeśā: This phrase can be translated to "May Agni (the fire god) grant us wealth and strength." Agni is often seen as a divine force that purifies and transforms. In real life, this can be related to the idea of hard work and dedication leading to prosperity. For example, a person who diligently works on their career or business may find success and financial stability.
  • Agnir ṛṣiṁ yaḥ sahasrā sanoti: This means "Agni, who supports the sage with a thousand resources." Here, Agni is depicted as a supporter of wisdom and knowledge. In practical terms, this can be seen in the way education and mentorship can provide individuals with the tools they need to succeed. For instance, a student who seeks guidance from a knowledgeable teacher may gain insights that lead to personal and professional growth.
  • Agnir divi havyam ā tatānāgner: This translates to "Agni, who is in the heavens, receives our offerings." This highlights the importance of rituals and offerings in connecting with the divine. In modern life, this can be likened to the practice of gratitude and recognition of the forces that contribute to our well-being. For example, taking time to appreciate the support of family and friends can strengthen those relationships.
  • Dhāmāni vibhṛtā purutrā: This means "Agni, who holds the realms everywhere." It signifies the omnipresence of Agni and the idea that divine energy is present in all aspects of life. In a real-world context, this can be interpreted as recognizing the interconnectedness of all things. For instance, understanding that our actions have consequences on the environment and society can lead to more responsible living.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of Agni as a symbol of wealth, wisdom, divine connection, and universal presence. In real life, it teaches us the importance of hard work, the value of knowledge, the practice of gratitude, and the awareness of our interconnectedness. By embodying these principles, individuals can lead more fulfilling and meaningful lives, fostering both personal growth and a positive impact on the world around them.
