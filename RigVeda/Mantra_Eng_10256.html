मन्त्र: न हि स्थूर्य ऋतुथा यातम् अस्ति नोत श्रवो विविधे संगमेषु | गव्यन्त इन्द्रं सख्याय विप्राऽश्वायन्तो वृषणं वाजयन्तः ||
nahi sthūry ṛtuthā yātam asti nota śravo vivide saṁgameṣu | gavyanta indraṁ sakhyāya viprā aśvāyanto vṛṣaṇaṁ vājayantaḥ ||
  • Translation: "There is no one who does not have the strength of the sun; there is no one who does not have the glory in the diverse gatherings. The wise ones, united in friendship, bring forth Indra, the powerful one, like horses that are driven to victory."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • nahi: not
    • sthūry: strength or power
    • ṛtuthā: of the sun
    • yātam: to be
    • asti: there is
    • nota: glory
    • śravo: fame or renown
    • vivide: diverse
    • saṁgameṣu: in gatherings
    • gavyanta: bringing forth
    • indraṁ: Indra, the king of gods
    • sakhyāya: in friendship
    • viprā: wise ones or seers
    • aśvāyanto: like horses
    • vṛṣaṇaṁ: the powerful one
    • vājayantaḥ: driving to victory
  • Meaning and Explanation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the inherent strength and potential within every individual, likening it to the strength of the sun.
    • The phrase "there is no one who does not have the strength of the sun" suggests that everyone possesses unique abilities and strengths that can be harnessed.
    • The mention of "diverse gatherings" highlights the importance of community and collaboration, where individuals come together to share their strengths.
    • Indra, as the king of gods, symbolizes power and success. The mantra suggests that through unity and friendship, individuals can invoke this power and achieve great things.
    • The imagery of horses being driven to victory represents the collective effort and determination needed to overcome challenges and reach goals.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace setting, team projects often require individuals to come together, leveraging their unique skills to achieve a common goal, much like the wise ones in the mantra.
    • In sports, a team that works in harmony, supporting each other, can achieve victory, reflecting the idea of driving towards success together.
    • Community initiatives, such as charity events, demonstrate how diverse individuals can unite their strengths for a greater cause, embodying the essence of this mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the power of unity and the potential within each individual.
    • It encourages collaboration and friendship, suggesting that together, we can invoke greater strength and achieve success.
    • In real life, whether in personal relationships, professional environments, or community efforts, the principles outlined in this mantra can guide us towards collective achievement and fulfillment.
