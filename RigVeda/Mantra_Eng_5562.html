आ माम मित्रावरुणेह रक्षतं कुलाययद विश्वयन मा न आ गण |अजकावं दुरदृष्टीकं तिरो दधे मा माम पद्येन रपसां विदत्सरुः ||
ā mām mitrāvaruṇeha rakṣataṁ kulāyayad viśvayan mā na ā gan | ajakāvaṁ durdṛśīkaṁ tiro dadhe mā mām padyena rapasā vidat tsaruḥ ||
  • Transliteration: "ā mām mitrāvaruṇeha rakṣataṁ kulāyayad viśvayan mā na ā gan | ajakāvaṁ durdṛśīkaṁ tiro dadhe mā mām padyena rapasā vidat tsaruḥ ||"
  • Meaning:
    • ā mām: Protect me
    • mitrāvaruṇe: O Mitra and Varuna (the deities representing friendship and cosmic order)
    • rakṣataṁ: safeguard
    • kulāyayad: from the family or lineage
    • viśvayan: from all directions
    • mā na ā gan: may I not be harmed
    • ajakāvaṁ: like a goat (symbolizing innocence)
    • durdṛśīkaṁ: difficult to see or perceive
    • tiro dadhe: may it be removed
    • mā mām: do not let me
    • padyena: by my foot or path
    • rapasā: by the power of the divine
    • vidat: knowledge or wisdom
    • tsaruḥ: may it be swift or quick
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra is a plea for protection from the deities Mitra and Varuna, who symbolize the principles of friendship, harmony, and cosmic order.
    • The speaker seeks to be safeguarded from harm that may come from their lineage or family, indicating a desire for protection from familial or ancestral issues.
    • The reference to being like a goat suggests a wish to remain innocent and untainted by negativity or malice.
    • The mantra also expresses a desire for the removal of obstacles that are difficult to perceive, which can be likened to unseen challenges in life, such as mental health issues or societal pressures.
    • The invocation of divine power to guide one's path emphasizes the importance of spiritual support in navigating life's challenges.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a family setting, individuals may face conflicts or issues that stem from generational patterns. This mantra can be a reminder to seek protection and healing from these familial influences.
    • In professional life, one may encounter unseen challenges, such as office politics or competition. The mantra serves as a reminder to remain focused and seek divine guidance to navigate these complexities.
    • During times of personal crisis, such as mental health struggles, reciting this mantra can provide comfort and a sense of protection, encouraging individuals to seek help and support.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of seeking protection and guidance from higher powers in various aspects of life.
    • It highlights the importance of maintaining innocence and purity in one's actions while navigating the complexities of relationships and societal expectations.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied as a source of strength and reassurance, reminding individuals to seek divine support in overcoming challenges and maintaining harmony in their lives.
