उप त्वाग्ने दिवे-दिवे दोषावस्तर धियā वयम् |नमो भरन्त एमसि ||
upa tvāgne dive-dive doṣāvastar dhiyā vayam | namo bharanta emasi ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • upa: Towards, near
    • tvāgne: O Agni (the Fire God)
    • dive-dive: Day by day, in every moment
    • doṣāvastar: Free from faults, without defects
    • dhiyā: With intellect, with understanding
    • vayam: We
    • namo: Salutations, reverence
    • bharanta: Carrying, bearing
    • emasi: We are, we exist
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Agni, the deity representing fire, which is often associated with purity, transformation, and the divine presence in rituals.
    • The phrase "upa tvāgne" signifies a call to Agni, inviting his presence and blessings into our lives.
    • "dive-dive" emphasizes the importance of daily devotion and the continuous nature of our relationship with the divine.
    • "doṣāvastar" suggests a desire for purity and the removal of imperfections, both in ourselves and in our actions.
    • "dhiyā vayam" indicates that we seek wisdom and understanding, highlighting the importance of intellect in our spiritual journey.
    • The concluding part, "namo bharanta emasi," expresses our reverence and acknowledgment of the divine force that sustains us.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, this mantra can be recited during morning rituals to invoke clarity and purity for the day ahead, much like how one might set intentions for a productive day.
    • In a corporate setting, leaders can reflect on this mantra to inspire a culture of integrity and continuous improvement, ensuring that their actions are free from faults and guided by wisdom.
    • In personal relationships, individuals can use the essence of this mantra to foster understanding and communication, striving to be free from misunderstandings and to approach each interaction with clarity and respect.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of seeking divine guidance in our daily lives, encouraging us to strive for purity, wisdom, and continuous growth.
    • Its significance lies in its ability to inspire individuals to cultivate a deeper connection with the divine, while also promoting self-improvement and ethical living.
    • In essence, it teaches us that by inviting the divine into our daily actions and decisions, we can lead more meaningful and fulfilling lives.
