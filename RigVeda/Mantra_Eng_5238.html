त्वं वरुण उता मित्रो अग्ने त्वां वर्धन्ति मतीभिर वसिष्ठाः |त्वे वसु सुषणानानि सन्तु यूयम् पात स्वस्तिभिः सदा नः ||
tvaṁ varuṇa uta mitro agne tvāṁ vardhanti matibhir vasiṣṭhāḥ | tve vasu suṣaṇanāni santu yūyam pāta svastibhiḥ sadā naḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • tvaṁ: You
    • varuṇa: Varuna, the god of cosmic order and water
    • uta: and
    • mitra: Mitra, the god of friendship and contracts
    • agne: O Agni, the fire god
    • tvāṁ: you
    • vardhanti: nourish or increase
    • matibhir: with thoughts or intentions
    • vasiṣṭhāḥ: the best or the most excellent
    • tve: you
    • vasu: wealth or good things
    • suṣaṇanāni: auspicious or beneficial
    • santu: may they be
    • yūyam: you all
    • pāta: protect
    • svastibhiḥ: with auspiciousness
    • sadā: always
    • naḥ: us
  • Overall Meaning:
    • This mantra invokes the divine powers of Varuna, Mitra, and Agni, asking for their blessings and protection.
    • It emphasizes the importance of thoughts and intentions in nourishing and increasing one's well-being.
    • The mantra seeks the presence of auspiciousness and wealth in life, ensuring that the divine entities protect and guide us always.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, individuals often seek guidance and protection from higher powers during significant events, such as weddings or new ventures, similar to how this mantra seeks blessings.
    • In a corporate setting, leaders may invoke positive intentions and teamwork (akin to the thoughts mentioned in the mantra) to foster a collaborative environment.
    • During challenging times, people may recite this mantra or similar prayers to invoke a sense of security and auspiciousness in their lives.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of divine support in achieving prosperity and well-being.
    • It serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of thoughts, intentions, and the divine in manifesting a fulfilling life.
    • In real-life scenarios, it encourages individuals to seek blessings and maintain positive intentions, fostering a harmonious existence.
