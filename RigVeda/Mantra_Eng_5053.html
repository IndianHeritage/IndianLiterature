न य ईषन्ते जनुषो 'यां व अन्तः सन्तो 'वद्यानी पुनानाः |निर्यद दुहरे शुचयो 'नु जोषम् अनु श्रीया तन्वम् उक्षमानाः ||
na ya īṣante januṣo 'yā nv a1ntaḥ santo 'vadyāni punānāḥ | nir yad duhre śucayo 'nu joṣam anu śriyā tanvam ukṣamāṇāḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • na - not
    • ya - who
    • īṣante - desire
    • januṣo - of beings
    • 'yā - who
    • nv - indeed
    • antaḥ - within
    • santo - the good
    • 'vadyāni - pure
    • punānāḥ - purifying
    • nir - without
    • yad - that
    • duhre - which is heavy
    • śucayo - of the pure
    • 'anu - following
    • joṣam - delight
    • anu - following
    • śriyā - prosperity
    • tanvam - body
    • ukṣamāṇāḥ - those who are nourished
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the essence of purity and the qualities of those who are virtuous.
    • It emphasizes that those who do not desire the heavy burdens of life, but instead seek purity and goodness, are the ones who truly thrive.
    • The mantra suggests that by following a path of righteousness and purity, one can attain true prosperity and nourishment.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate environment, individuals who focus on ethical practices and integrity often find themselves in positions of leadership and respect, as opposed to those who may seek shortcuts for immediate gains.
    • In personal relationships, individuals who prioritize honesty and kindness tend to build stronger, more fulfilling connections, leading to a more enriched life.
    • In community service, those who dedicate their time to helping others without seeking recognition often find a deeper sense of fulfillment and purpose.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for individuals to embrace purity and goodness in their lives.
    • It serves as a reminder that true prosperity comes from within and is achieved through virtuous actions and intentions.
    • In real-life scenarios, applying the principles of this mantra can lead to personal growth, stronger relationships, and a more harmonious society.
