यद आपीतासो अंशवो गावो न दुह्र ऊधभिः |यद वा वाणीर अनूषत प्र देवयन्तो अश्विनाः ||
yad āpītāso aṁśavo gāvo na duhra ūdhabhiḥ | yad vā vāṇīr anūṣata pra devayanto aśvinā ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yad: whatever
    • āpītāso: those who are endowed
    • aṁśavo: with parts or portions
    • gāvo: cows (symbolizing wealth and nourishment)
    • na: not
    • duhra: difficult or hard
    • ūdhabhiḥ: to be obtained or achieved
    • yad: whatever
    • vā: or
    • vāṇīr: speech or words
    • anūṣata: that which is followed or adhered to
    • pra: towards
    • devayanto: divine or godly
    • aśvinā: the Ashvins, the twin gods of medicine and health
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the abundance and ease of obtaining what is essential for life, represented by cows and divine speech.
    • The mention of "cows" symbolizes not just physical wealth but also nourishment and sustenance, which are vital for survival.
    • The phrase "not difficult to obtain" suggests that the blessings of life, whether material or spiritual, are accessible to those who seek them with sincerity.
    • The reference to "speech" indicates the importance of communication and the divine guidance that comes through words, emphasizing the role of wisdom and knowledge in achieving one's goals.
    • The invocation of the Ashvins highlights the connection to health and well-being, suggesting that divine assistance is available for those who pursue their needs with faith.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In agriculture, farmers often pray for a bountiful harvest, reflecting the desire for abundance and sustenance, akin to the blessings of cows in the mantra.
    • In personal development, individuals seek knowledge and wisdom through education and mentorship, paralleling the importance of divine speech and guidance.
    • In healthcare, people turn to medical professionals (the modern-day Ashvins) for assistance in maintaining their health, embodying the pursuit of well-being mentioned in the mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of seeking abundance and nourishment in life, both materially and spiritually.
    • It teaches that with sincere effort and divine guidance, what we need is not far from our reach.
    • In real-life scenarios, it encourages individuals to pursue their goals with faith, seek knowledge, and maintain their health, knowing that support is available from both the divine and the community.
