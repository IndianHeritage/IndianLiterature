एव ते हारियोजनā सुवृक्तीन्द्र ब्रह्माणि गोतमासो अक्रन |ऐषु विश्वपेśसँ धियं धाह प्रातर मक्षू धियावसुर जगम्यāt ||
evā te hāriyojanā suvṛktīndra brahmāṇi gotamāso akran | aiṣu viśvapeśasaṁ dhiyaṁ dhāḥ prātar makṣū dhiyāvasur jagamyāt ||
  • evā te hāriyojanā: This phrase can be interpreted as "may your paths be clear" or "may your journey be successful." It emphasizes the importance of having a clear direction in life.
  • suvṛktīndra: This term refers to "well-spoken" or "good speech." It highlights the significance of communication and the power of words in shaping our reality.
  • brahmāṇi gotamāso akran: This can be understood as "the divine essence of the universe." It suggests a connection to the greater cosmic order and the importance of aligning oneself with it.
  • aiṣu viśvapeśasaṁ dhiyaṁ dhāḥ: This phrase means "may we be endowed with wisdom and understanding." It emphasizes the need for knowledge and insight in navigating life's challenges.
  • prātar makṣū dhiyāvasur jagamyāt: This translates to "may we rise in the morning with clarity of thought." It signifies the importance of starting each day with a fresh perspective and a clear mind.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking clarity, wisdom, and good communication in our lives. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various contexts such as personal development, professional communication, and spiritual growth. For instance, when embarking on a new project, having a clear plan (evā te hāriyojanā) and communicating effectively (suvṛktīndra) can lead to success. Additionally, aligning oneself with the greater cosmic order (brahmāṇi gotamāso akran) can provide a sense of purpose and direction. Ultimately, this mantra serves as a reminder to approach each day with clarity and wisdom, fostering a more fulfilling and meaningful life.
