इमे चेतारो अनृतस्य भूरेर मित्रो आर्यमा वरुणो हि सन्ति |इमा ऋतस्य वावृद्धुर दुरोणे शग्मासः पुत्रा अदितेर अदब्धाः ||
ime cetāro anṛtasya bhūrer mitro aryamā varuṇo hi santi | ima ṛtasya vāvṛdhur duroṇe śagmāsaḥ putrā aditer adabdhāḥ ||
  • ime cetāro anṛtasya bhūrer: This phrase can be translated to "these are the thoughts of the truth of the earth." Here, "cetāro" refers to thoughts or consciousness, while "anṛtasya" means truth or reality, and "bhūrer" refers to the earth. This suggests that our thoughts should align with the truth of our existence and the world around us.
  • mitro aryamā varuṇo hi santi: This translates to "the friends, Aryama and Varuna, indeed exist." In this context, "mitro" means friends, "Aryama" is a deity associated with friendship and hospitality, and "Varuna" is the god of cosmic order and water. This line emphasizes the importance of friendship and divine support in our lives, suggesting that we are never alone in our journey.
  • ima ṛtasya vāvṛdhur duroṇe: This means "these are the ones who have grown in the truth." "Ṛtasya" refers to truth or cosmic order, and "vāvṛdhur" means to grow or develop. This line highlights the idea that those who adhere to truth and righteousness will flourish and prosper.
  • śagmāsaḥ putrā aditer adabdhāḥ: This translates to "the sons of Aditi, who are unblemished." "Aditi" is the mother of the gods in Vedic tradition, and "putrā" means sons. "Adabdhāḥ" implies purity or being unblemished. This line signifies the importance of purity and righteousness in one's character, suggesting that those who embody these qualities are blessed.

  • Significance: This mantra emphasizes the importance of aligning our thoughts with truth, the value of friendship and divine support, the growth that comes from adhering to righteousness, and the purity of character. It serves as a reminder that we are part of a larger cosmic order and that our actions and thoughts have significance.
  • Real-life Application: In everyday life, this mantra can inspire individuals to cultivate honesty in their thoughts and actions, seek supportive relationships, and strive for personal growth through ethical living. For example, in a workplace setting, fostering a culture of honesty and support can lead to a more productive and harmonious environment. Similarly, in personal relationships, being truthful and supportive can strengthen bonds and create a sense of community.
