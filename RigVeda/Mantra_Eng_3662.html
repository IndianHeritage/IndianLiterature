विज्योतिषा बृहता भात्य अग्निर आविर्विश्वानि कृत्नुते महित्वा |प्रादेवीर मायः सहते दुरेवाः शिशीते शृङ्गे रक्षसे विनिक्षे ||
vi jyotiṣā bṛhatā bhāty agnir āvir viśvāni kṛṇute mahitvā | prādevīr māyāḥ sahate durevāḥ śiśīte śṛṅge rakṣase vinikṣe ||
  • vi jyotiṣā: This phrase translates to "by the light" or "through illumination." It signifies the importance of knowledge and enlightenment in our lives.
  • bṛhatā bhāty: This means "the great one shines." It refers to the vastness and brilliance of the divine or universal truth that guides us.
  • agnir āvir: "Agni" refers to fire, which symbolizes energy, transformation, and purification. "Āvir" means "to manifest" or "to appear." Together, they suggest the emergence of divine energy or inspiration.
  • viśvāni kṛṇute mahitvā: This translates to "he (Agni) creates all great things." It emphasizes the role of divine energy in the creation and sustenance of the universe.
  • prādevīr māyāḥ: "Prādevīr" refers to the divine powers or deities, while "māyāḥ" means "illusions" or "the power of creation." This indicates the interplay between divine forces and the material world.
  • sahate durevāḥ: This means "the distant gods endure." It suggests that even the far-off divine entities are present and influential in our lives.
  • śiśīte: This word means "to shine" or "to illuminate." It reinforces the theme of enlightenment and the presence of divine light.
  • śṛṅge rakṣase vinikṣe: "Rakṣase" refers to protection or guardianship, while "vinikṣe" means "to remove" or "to dispel." This phrase implies the removal of obstacles and the protection from negative forces.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda emphasizes the significance of divine light and energy in our lives. It highlights the importance of seeking knowledge and enlightenment, as well as the protective forces that guide us through challenges. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in moments of uncertainty or fear, reminding us to seek clarity and protection through spiritual practices, meditation, or prayer. It encourages individuals to embrace the transformative power of knowledge and the divine, fostering a sense of security and purpose in their journey through life.
