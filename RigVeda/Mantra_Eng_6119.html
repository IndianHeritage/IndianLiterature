ताभिर ā यातम ऊतिभिर नव्यसीभीः सुशस्तिभिः |यद वां वृषण्वसू हुवे ||
tābhir ā yātam ūtibhir navyasībhiḥ suśastibhiḥ | yad vāṁ vṛṣaṇvasū huve ||
  • tābhir: This word can be translated as "by them" or "through them." It indicates a connection or reliance on certain forces or entities.
  • ā yātam: This phrase means "may we be led" or "may we be guided." It expresses a desire for direction and support in our endeavors.
  • ūtibhir: This term refers to "strengths" or "powers." It signifies the various strengths that can aid in achieving goals.
  • navyasībhiḥ: This word translates to "new" or "fresh." It suggests the importance of innovation and new beginnings in our lives.
  • suśastibhiḥ: This means "with good qualities" or "with excellence." It emphasizes the need for virtuous attributes in our actions and decisions.
  • yad: This word means "that" or "which," indicating a reference to something specific.
  • vāṁ: This translates to "your" or "of you," suggesting a personal connection or relationship.
  • vṛṣaṇvasū: This term can be interpreted as "the one who is strong" or "the powerful one." It denotes strength and capability.
  • huve: This means "I invoke" or "I call upon." It indicates a plea or request for assistance or blessings.

The mantra can be understood as a prayer or invocation for guidance and strength through the support of powerful and virtuous forces. It emphasizes the importance of being led by strengths that are new and excellent, while also invoking the presence of a strong entity or force to assist in our endeavors.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as:

  • Personal Growth: When embarking on a new journey, whether it be a career change or personal development, one can reflect on this mantra to seek guidance and strength from positive influences.
  • Teamwork: In a collaborative environment, invoking the strengths of the team and focusing on new ideas can lead to innovative solutions and success.
  • Spiritual Practices: During meditation or prayer, this mantra can serve as a reminder to seek guidance from higher powers and to embody virtuous qualities in daily life.

Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for guidance and strength through virtuous and powerful forces. It encourages individuals to seek new beginnings and to embody excellence in their actions. By applying this mantra in real-life scenarios, one can foster personal growth, enhance teamwork, and deepen spiritual practices, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling and purposeful life.
