आ नो विश्वाभिर ऊतिभिर अश्विनां गच्छतं युवम् |दश्रा हिरण्यवर्तनी पिबतं सोम्यम् मधु ||
ā no viśvābhir ūtibhir aśvinā gacchataṁ yuvam | dasrā hiraṇyavartanī pibataṁ somyam madhu ||
  • Mantra: "ā no viśvābhir ūtibhir aśvinā gacchataṁ yuvam | dasrā hiraṇyavartanī pibataṁ somyam madhu ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā: May (we) approach
    • no: us
    • viśvābhir: with all (the) energies
    • ūtibhir: with (the) powers
    • aśvinā: O Ashvins (the divine twin horsemen)
    • gacchataṁ: may you go
    • yuvam: you two (the Ashvins)
    • dasrā: with the light
    • hiraṇyavartanī: golden, flowing (like honey)
    • pibataṁ: may you drink
    • somyam: the soma (sacred drink)
    • madhu: sweet (honey)
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to the Ashvins, who are considered the divine physicians and bringers of health and prosperity.
    • The invocation "ā no viśvābhir ūtibhir" suggests a desire for the Ashvins to come to us with all their powers and energies, highlighting the importance of divine assistance in our lives.
    • The phrase "gacchataṁ yuvam" emphasizes the duality of the Ashvins, representing balance and harmony in life.
    • "Dasrā hiraṇyavartanī" refers to the light and abundance that the Ashvins bring, akin to the golden rays of the sun that nourish the earth.
    • The request to "pibataṁ somyam madhu" signifies a longing for the sweet, nourishing essence of life, akin to the joy and vitality we seek in our daily experiences.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In times of illness or distress, people often seek divine intervention or support, much like the prayer to the Ashvins for health and healing.
    • The concept of balance and harmony represented by the Ashvins can be seen in modern wellness practices, where individuals strive for a balanced lifestyle through diet, exercise, and mental health care.
    • The idea of seeking sweetness in life can be related to the pursuit of happiness and fulfillment, whether through relationships, career, or personal passions.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking divine support for health, prosperity, and balance in life.
    • Its significance lies in the acknowledgment of higher powers that guide and assist us in our journey, reminding us of the interconnectedness of all energies.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra encourages individuals to seek harmony, health, and joy, fostering a holistic approach to living.
