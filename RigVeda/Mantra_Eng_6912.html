विषु विश्वा अभियुजो वज्रिन विश्वग यथा वृहा |भव नः सुश्रवस्तमः ||
vi ṣu viśvā abhiyujo vajrin viṣvag yathā vṛha | bhavā naḥ suśravastamaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • vi ṣu: "in all" or "in every"
    • viśvā: "universal" or "all-encompassing"
    • abhiyujo: "those who are united" or "those who are connected"
    • vajrin: "the one who wields power" or "the strong one"
    • viṣvag: "in all directions" or "everywhere"
    • yathā: "as" or "like"
    • vṛha: "great" or "vast"
    • bhavā: "may you become" or "may you be"
    • naḥ: "our" or "to us"
    • suśravastamaḥ: "the most renowned" or "the best of fame"
  • Overall Meaning: This mantra can be interpreted as a prayer or invocation for universal strength and unity. It emphasizes the importance of collective power and greatness, urging that we may become renowned and respected in all aspects of life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • Community Strength: In a community, when individuals come together for a common cause, such as environmental conservation, they embody the essence of "viṣu viśvā abhiyujo." Their collective efforts can lead to significant change, showcasing the power of unity.
    • Teamwork in Organizations: In a workplace, when team members collaborate effectively, they harness the "vajrin" aspect of the mantra. This collaboration can lead to innovative solutions and successful projects, demonstrating how unity can enhance productivity.
    • Global Movements: Movements like climate change activism reflect the mantra's call for universal action ("viṣvag"). When people from diverse backgrounds unite for a common goal, they amplify their voices and impact, striving for a "suśravastamaḥ" legacy.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for unity, strength, and recognition. In real-life scenarios, it serves as a reminder that collective efforts can lead to greatness and that working together can yield powerful results. Whether in communities, workplaces, or global movements, the principles encapsulated in this mantra encourage individuals to strive for a common purpose, fostering a sense of belonging and shared achievement.
