सामन नु राये निधिमान nv अन्नं करामहे सु पुरुध श्रवांसि |तां नो विश्वानि जरताम ममत्तु परातरं सु निरृतिर जिहीताम ||
sāman nu rāye nidhiman nv annaṁ karāmahe su purudha śravāṁsi | tā no viśvāni jaritā mamattu parātaraṁ su nirṛtir jihītām ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sāman: song or melody
    • nu: indeed or surely
    • rāye: for wealth or prosperity
    • nidhiman: the one who is a treasure or source
    • nv: new or fresh
    • annaṁ: food or nourishment
    • karāmahe: we make or create
    • su: good or auspicious
    • purudha: the one who is strong or powerful
    • śravāṁsi: fame or glory
    • tā: that
    • no: for us
    • viśvāni: all or universal
    • jaritā: the one who brings forth or creates
    • mamattu: may it be ours
    • parātaraṁ: the highest or supreme
    • su: good or auspicious
    • nirṛtir: misfortune or adversity
    • jihītām: may it be removed or dispelled
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra expresses a desire for prosperity and nourishment.
    • It emphasizes the importance of creating a harmonious environment that fosters wealth and abundance.
    • The invocation of strength and fame suggests a call for recognition and success in endeavors.
    • It also seeks the removal of misfortune, highlighting the need for protection against adversity.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be recited during meetings or planning sessions to invoke success and prosperity.
    • Families may use this mantra during meals to express gratitude for food and to seek blessings for abundance.
    • In personal development, individuals can reflect on this mantra to motivate themselves towards achieving their goals and dispelling negativity.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of prosperity, nourishment, and well-being.
    • Its significance lies in the holistic approach to life, where one seeks not only material wealth but also spiritual and emotional fulfillment.
    • By applying the principles of this mantra in daily life, individuals can cultivate a positive mindset, attract abundance, and overcome challenges.
