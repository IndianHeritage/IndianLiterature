विषाम आसाम अभयानाम अधिक्षितं गीरभिरु स्वयशसं गृणीमसि |ज्ञाभिर विश्वाभिर अदितिम अनर्वणम अक्तोर युवानं नृमणा अधा पतिम ||
viśām āsām abhayānām adhikṣitaṁ gīrbhir u svayaśasaṁ gṛṇīmasi | gnābhir viśvābhir aditim anarvaṇam aktor yuvānaṁ nṛmaṇā adhā patim ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • viśām: of the communities or groups
    • āsām: of those who are present
    • abhayānām: of the fearless
    • adhikṣitaṁ: governed or protected
    • gīrbhir: by the hymns or praises
    • u: and
    • svayaśasaṁ: of one's own glory
    • gṛṇīmasi: we sing or chant
    • gnābhir: with knowledge or wisdom
    • viśvābhir: by all beings
    • aditim: to the infinite or boundless
    • anarvaṇam: without any hindrance
    • aktor: of the powerful
    • yuvānaṁ: the youthful
    • nṛmaṇā: by the human beings
    • adhā: down or towards
    • patim: the lord or master
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the collective strength and unity of communities that are fearless and protected.
    • It emphasizes the importance of singing praises and acknowledging the glory that comes from wisdom and knowledge.
    • The reference to "aditim" suggests a connection to the infinite, indicating that the power and protection we seek are boundless.
    • The mention of "yuvānaṁ" highlights the vitality and energy of youth, suggesting that the strength of the community is rejuvenated by its younger members.
    • Overall, the mantra reflects a harmonious relationship between individuals and the collective, where wisdom and fearlessness lead to a prosperous existence.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace, a team that operates fearlessly and supports each other can achieve greater success. For instance, a project team that collaborates openly and shares knowledge can innovate more effectively.
    • In educational settings, students who work together fearlessly, sharing their insights and learning from one another, create a vibrant learning environment that fosters growth.
    • Communities that come together to support each other during crises, such as natural disasters, exemplify the essence of this mantra. Their collective strength and wisdom can lead to effective recovery and resilience.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the power of unity, fearlessness, and wisdom in achieving collective goals.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from workplaces to communities, where collaboration and mutual support lead to success.
    • By embracing the principles outlined in this mantra, individuals and groups can foster an environment of growth, resilience, and prosperity.
