आयुक्त सप्त हरितः सदस्थाद या ईं वहन्ति सूर्यं घृताचिः |धामानि मित्रावरुणा युवाकुः सं यो यूथेव जनिमानि चष्टे ||
ayukta sapta haritaḥ sadhasthād yā īṁ vahanti sūryaṁ ghṛtācīḥ | dhāmāni mitrāvaruṇā yuvākuḥ saṁ yo yūtheva janimāni caṣṭe ||
  • Mantra: "ayukta sapta haritaḥ sadhasthād yā īṁ vahanti sūryaṁ ghṛtācīḥ | dhāmāni mitrāvaruṇā yuvākuḥ saṁ yo yūtheva janimāni caṣṭe ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ayukta: unconnected or disjointed
    • sapta: seven
    • haritaḥ: green or flourishing
    • sadhasthād: from the assembly or gathering
    • yā: who
    • īṁ: indeed
    • vahanti: carry or bring
    • sūryaṁ: the sun
    • ghṛtācīḥ: with ghee or clarified butter
    • dhāmāni: abodes or places
    • mitrāvaruṇā: associated with the deities Mitra and Varuna
    • yuvākuḥ: youthful or energetic
    • saṁ: together
    • yo: who
    • yūtheva: like a group or assembly
    • janimāni: births or creations
    • caṣṭe: moves or acts
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of a collective energy that is vibrant and flourishing, represented by the "seven green" aspects that come together in unity.
    • The reference to the sun indicates a source of life and vitality, suggesting that this energy is essential for growth and sustenance.
    • The mention of "Mitra and Varuna" signifies harmony and balance, as these deities represent friendship and cosmic order.
    • The idea of youthfulness and energy implies that this collective force is dynamic and ever-evolving, capable of bringing forth new creations and opportunities.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community setting, when individuals come together with a shared purpose, they can create a flourishing environment, much like the "seven green" aspects of the mantra.
    • In nature, the sun is essential for the growth of plants, symbolizing how vital energy sources are for life, paralleling the mantra's emphasis on the sun's role.
    • In teamwork, the collaboration of diverse talents can lead to innovative solutions, reflecting the unity and collective action described in the mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of unity, collective energy, and the vital role of natural forces in fostering growth and harmony.
    • It serves as a reminder that when individuals come together with a common goal, they can create a powerful and positive impact, much like the sun nurturing life on Earth.
    • In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as community building, teamwork, and environmental sustainability, highlighting the interconnectedness of all beings and the importance of collaboration.
