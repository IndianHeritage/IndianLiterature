मन्त्र: न हि ष्म यद ध वः पुरा स्तोमेभिर वृक्तबरिषः |शर्धां ऋतस्य जिन्वथ ||
nahi ṣma yad dha vaḥ purā stomebhir vṛktabarhiṣaḥ | śardhām̐ ṛtasya jinvatha ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • nahi: not
    • ṣma: peace or tranquility
    • yad: that which
    • dha: to hold or to support
    • vaḥ: you (plural)
    • purā: in the past
    • stomebhir: with hymns or praises
    • vṛktabarhiṣaḥ: those who are engaged in the sacred fire or rituals
    • śardhām̐: with devotion or sincerity
    • ṛtasya: of truth or righteousness
    • jinvatha: may you thrive or prosper
  • Overall Meaning: This mantra emphasizes the importance of maintaining peace and righteousness through collective efforts and devotion. It suggests that by engaging in sacred practices and supporting one another, individuals can thrive in truth and harmony.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • Community Support: In a community, when individuals come together to support each other during difficult times, they create an environment of peace and stability. For instance, during natural disasters, communities often unite to help those affected, embodying the essence of this mantra.
    • Workplace Collaboration: In a professional setting, when team members collaborate sincerely and support each other’s efforts, they can achieve greater success. This reflects the idea of thriving together in truth and righteousness.
    • Family Unity: Within families, maintaining harmony and supporting each other’s aspirations leads to a nurturing environment. This aligns with the mantra’s call for collective devotion and support.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for unity, support, and the pursuit of truth. It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, such as community building, workplace dynamics, and family relationships. By embodying the principles of this mantra, individuals can foster an environment of peace and prosperity, ultimately leading to a more harmonious society.
