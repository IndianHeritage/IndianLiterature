पुनः समव्यद् विततं वयन्ती मध्याकर्तोर्न्यधाच्छक्म धीरः |उत्संहायास्थाद् व्यृतूम् अदर्दर अरमतिḥ सविता देव आगात् ||
punaḥ sam avyad vitataṁ vayantī madhyā kartor ny adhāc chakma dhīraḥ | ut saṁhāyāsthād vy ṛ1tūm̐r adardhar aramatiḥ savitā deva āgāt ||
  • Mantra: "punaḥ sam avyad vitataṁ vayantī madhyā kartor ny adhāc chakma dhīraḥ | ut saṁhāyāsthād vy ṛ1tūm̐r adardhar aramatiḥ savitā deva āgāt ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • punaḥ: again
    • sam: together, in harmony
    • avyad: spread out, expanded
    • vitataṁ: vast, extensive
    • vayantī: flowing, moving
    • madhyā: in the middle, central
    • kartor: of the doer, creator
    • ny: not
    • adhāc: below, beneath
    • chakma: wisdom, intelligence
    • dhīraḥ: the wise, the courageous
    • ut: up, towards
    • saṁhāyāsthād: having gathered together
    • vy: in the direction of
    • ṛ1tūm̐r: seasons, cycles
    • adardhar: not to be harmed
    • aramatiḥ: the one who delights, enjoys
    • savitā: the sun, the divine light
    • deva: god, divine being
    • āgāt: has come, arrived
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra speaks of the cyclical nature of existence and the importance of harmony and wisdom in navigating life's challenges.
    • The phrase "punaḥ sam avyad vitataṁ" suggests a return to a state of unity and balance, which can be likened to the way nature rejuvenates itself after a season.
    • In real life, this can be seen in the changing seasons, where after winter, spring brings new life and growth, symbolizing renewal and hope.
    • The term "dhīraḥ" refers to those who possess wisdom and courage, reminding us that in times of difficulty, it is essential to remain steadfast and composed.
    • For example, during a personal crisis, a wise individual may approach the situation with calmness and clarity, leading to better decision-making.
    • The mantra also emphasizes the importance of gathering together ("saṁhāyāsthād") to face challenges, highlighting the value of community and support.
    • In a workplace setting, teamwork and collaboration can lead to innovative solutions and a more resilient organization.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the cyclical nature of life and the importance of wisdom, unity, and resilience.
    • Its significance lies in encouraging individuals to embrace challenges with courage and to seek harmony in their surroundings.
    • In real-life scenarios, whether in personal relationships, community efforts, or professional environments, the principles outlined in this mantra can guide individuals towards a more fulfilling and balanced existence.
