तं इद धनेषु हितेष्व अधिवाकाय हवन्ते |येषाम इन्द्रस ते जयन्ति ||
tam id dhaneṣu hiteṣv adhivākāya havante | yeṣām indras te jayanti ||
  • tam id dhaneṣu hiteṣv adhivākāya havante
    • tam - that
    • id - this
    • dhaneṣu - in wealth or riches
    • hiteṣv - in what is beneficial or good
    • adhivākāya - for the one who speaks or invokes
    • havante - they who offer or invoke
  • yeṣām indras te jayanti
    • yeṣām - of whom
    • indras - Indra, the king of gods
    • te - they
    • jayanti - conquer or succeed

The mantra can be translated as: "Those who invoke the divine in their wealth and what is beneficial, Indra grants them victory." This mantra emphasizes the importance of invoking divine blessings in the pursuit of wealth and prosperity, suggesting that true success comes from aligning one's efforts with higher principles and values.


In real life, this can be seen in various contexts:

  • Business Ethics: Entrepreneurs who prioritize ethical practices and social responsibility often find greater success. For example, companies that focus on sustainability and community welfare tend to build stronger brand loyalty and customer trust.
  • Personal Finance: Individuals who manage their finances wisely, investing in education and health, often experience long-term prosperity. This reflects the idea of seeking what is beneficial, not just for oneself but for the community.
  • Community Service: Organizations that engage in charitable activities and support local communities often receive support and success in their ventures, as they are seen as contributing positively to society.

Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its reminder that invoking divine support in our endeavors, particularly in wealth and prosperity, is essential for achieving true success. It teaches us that our actions should be aligned with what is beneficial not only for ourselves but also for others. In a world where competition is fierce, this mantra encourages a holistic approach to success, where ethical considerations and community welfare play a crucial role in achieving lasting victory.
