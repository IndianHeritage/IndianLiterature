आ ते महा इन्द्रोत्य उग्र समन्यवो यत समरन्त सेनाः |पताति दिद्युन नार्यस्य बाह्वोर मा ते मनो विश्वद्र्यं वि चारीत ||
ā te maha indroty ugra samanyavo yat samaranta senāḥ | patāti didyun naryasya bāhvor mā te mano viṣvadrya1g vi cārīt ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā te: To you
    • maha: Great
    • indroty: O Indra (the king of gods)
    • ugra: Powerful
    • samanyavo: United
    • yat: Who
    • samaranta: In battle
    • senāḥ: Armies
    • patāti: Fall
    • didyun: Shine
    • naryasya: Of the man
    • bāhvor: Of the arms
    • mā: Not
    • te: Your
    • mano: Mind
    • viṣvadrya: All directions
    • vi: In
    • cārīt: Move
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Indra, the god of rain and thunder, who is often associated with strength and power.
    • The phrase "ā te maha indroty ugra" invokes the great and powerful Indra, asking for his blessings.
    • "samanyavo yat samaranta senāḥ" refers to the united armies that are engaged in battle, highlighting the importance of unity and strength in facing challenges.
    • The line "patāti didyun naryasya bāhvor" suggests that the strength of a man’s arms should not falter, emphasizing resilience and determination.
    • "mā te mano viṣvadrya vi cārīt" indicates a plea for the mind to remain focused and steadfast in all directions, symbolizing the need for clarity and purpose in one’s actions.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, this mantra can be applied to team dynamics where unity and collective strength are essential for overcoming challenges and achieving goals.
    • In sports, a team that works together harmoniously, much like the "united armies," is more likely to succeed than one that does not collaborate effectively.
    • In personal life, maintaining mental focus and resilience in the face of adversity can lead to personal growth and success, reflecting the essence of this mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of strength, unity, and mental clarity in overcoming challenges.
    • It serves as a reminder that invoking divine support, like that of Indra, can empower individuals and groups to face adversities with courage and determination.
    • In real-life scenarios, whether in personal endeavors, professional settings, or communal efforts, the principles encapsulated in this mantra can guide individuals towards success and resilience.
