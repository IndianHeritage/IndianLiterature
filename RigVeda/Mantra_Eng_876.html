यो नो अग्ने 'भिदासत्य अन्ति दूरि पदिष्ट सः |अस्माकं इद वृद्धे भव ||
yo no agne 'bhidāsaty anti dūre padīṣṭa saḥ | asmākam id vṛdhe bhava ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yo: who
    • no: our
    • agne: O Agni (the fire god)
    • bhidā: separation or division
    • saty: truth
    • anti: exists or is present
    • dūre: far away
    • padīṣṭa: established or set
    • saḥ: he or that
    • asmākam: our
    • id: this
    • vṛdhe: in growth or prosperity
    • bhava: may you become
  • Overall Meaning:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Agni, the fire god, asking for his presence and support in our lives.
    • It emphasizes the importance of truth and unity, even when faced with separation or distance.
    • The mantra seeks blessings for growth and prosperity in our endeavors.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a family setting, when members are physically apart due to work or studies, this mantra can be a reminder to maintain truth and unity in relationships.
    • In a business context, leaders can invoke this mantra to foster a culture of honesty and collaboration, ensuring that all team members work towards common goals despite challenges.
    • During personal challenges, individuals can recite this mantra to seek inner strength and clarity, promoting personal growth and resilience.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for unity, truth, and growth, which are essential for personal and communal well-being.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from family dynamics to professional environments, encouraging individuals to seek connection and integrity.
    • By invoking Agni's blessings, one can aspire for a life filled with truth, prosperity, and harmonious relationships.
