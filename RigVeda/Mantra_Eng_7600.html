न त्वा देवासा आशत न मर्त्यासो अद्रिवः |विश्वा जातानि शवसाभिभूर असि न त्वा देवासा आशत ||
na tvā devāsa āśata na martyāso adrivaḥ | viśvā jātāni śavasābhibhūr asi na tvā devāsa āśata ||
  • na tvā devāsa āśata: This phrase translates to "The gods do not approach you." It suggests a sense of distance or separation from divine beings. In real life, this can be interpreted as moments when individuals feel abandoned or disconnected from spiritual guidance or support.
  • na martyāso adrivaḥ: This means "nor do mortals approach you." Here, the term "mortal" refers to human beings, indicating that there is a barrier between the divine and the human realm. This can resonate with people who feel isolated in their struggles, as if they are not worthy of divine attention or help.
  • viśvā jātāni śavasābhibhūr asi: This translates to "You are the one who encompasses all beings." It emphasizes the omnipresence and all-encompassing nature of the divine. In everyday life, this can be seen in the interconnectedness of all living beings, where one’s actions can impact others, highlighting the importance of compassion and empathy.
  • na tvā devāsa āśata: The repetition of this line reinforces the idea of the divine being unapproachable or distant. It serves as a reminder that while one may seek divine intervention, there are times when it feels as though the divine is silent or absent, prompting individuals to rely on their inner strength and resilience.

Conclusion: This mantra reflects the complex relationship between the divine and humanity. It acknowledges feelings of separation and the search for connection, while also affirming the omnipresence of the divine in all beings. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can serve as a reminder to seek inner strength during times of struggle, to recognize the interconnectedness of life, and to foster a sense of community and support among individuals. It encourages individuals to find solace in the idea that, despite feelings of isolation, they are part of a larger whole, and that the divine essence is present in all aspects of life.
