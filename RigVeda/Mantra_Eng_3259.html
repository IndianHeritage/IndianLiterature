आयम् पन्थाः अनुवित्तः पुराणो यतो देवाः उदजायन्त विश्वे |अतश्चिद् आ जनिषीष्ट प्रवरद्धो मा मातरम् अमुयā पट्टवे कः ||
ayam panthā anuvittaḥ purāṇo yato devā udajāyanta viśve | ataś cid ā janiṣīṣṭa pravṛddho mā mātaram amuyā pattave kaḥ ||
  • ayam panthā anuvittaḥ purāṇo: This phrase translates to "this path is ancient and has been followed." It signifies the continuity of wisdom and knowledge that has been passed down through generations. In real life, this can be likened to the traditions and practices that families or cultures maintain over time, such as rituals, festivals, or even moral values that guide behavior.
  • yato devā udajāyanta viśve: This means "from where all the gods have arisen." It emphasizes the source of divine inspiration and the interconnectedness of all beings. In a modern context, this can be related to the idea that creativity and innovation often stem from a collective consciousness or shared experiences, much like how collaborative efforts in teams lead to groundbreaking ideas.
  • ataś cid ā janiṣīṣṭa pravṛddho: This translates to "therefore, one should strive to attain that which is growing." It suggests the importance of growth and progress in life. In practical terms, this can be seen in personal development, such as pursuing education, skills, or self-improvement, which are essential for success in various fields.
  • mā mātaram amuyā pattave kaḥ: This phrase can be interpreted as "who can attain that without the mother?" It highlights the significance of nurturing and support in achieving one's goals. In real life, this can be reflected in the role of family, mentors, or communities that provide the necessary encouragement and resources for individuals to thrive.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of ancient wisdom, the importance of divine inspiration, the pursuit of growth, and the necessity of support systems in life. Its significance lies in reminding us that while we strive for progress and success, we must also honor our roots, seek inspiration from collective experiences, and acknowledge the vital role of nurturing relationships. In today's fast-paced world, this mantra serves as a guiding principle for personal and communal development, encouraging us to remain connected to our heritage while aspiring for a brighter future.
