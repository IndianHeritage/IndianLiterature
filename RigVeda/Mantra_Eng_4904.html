मन्त्र: तुषा उ वो मह ऋतस्य गोपान अदितिम मित्रं वरुणं सुजातान् | आर्यमणं भागं अदब्धधीतीन् अच्छा वोचे सधन्यः पावकान् ||
stuṣa u vo maha ṛtasya gopān aditim mitraṁ varuṇaṁ sujātān | aryamaṇam bhagam adabdhadhītīn acchā voce sadhanyaḥ pāvakān ||
  • stuṣa u vo maha ṛtasya gopān: This phrase can be translated as "We praise you, O great guardians of truth." Here, "stuṣa" means to praise, "maha" means great, and "ṛtasya" refers to truth or cosmic order. In real life, this can be seen in the context of leaders or guardians who uphold justice and truth in society, such as judges or community leaders.
  • aditim mitraṁ varuṇaṁ sujātān: This translates to "Aditi, the friend, Varuna, the well-born." Aditi is often associated with the infinite and the mother of gods, while Varuna represents the cosmic order and moral authority. In modern terms, this can relate to the importance of friendship and moral integrity in our relationships, emphasizing the need for trustworthy companions in our lives.
  • aryamaṇam bhagam adabdhadhītīn: This can be interpreted as "the noble and prosperous ones who are unblemished." "Aryamaṇam" refers to nobility, and "bhagam" signifies prosperity. This highlights the value of integrity and ethical behavior in achieving success. In a real-life scenario, this can be seen in business practices where ethical companies thrive due to their commitment to honesty and fairness.
  • acchā voce sadhanyaḥ pāvakān: This means "may we speak well of the pure ones." It emphasizes the importance of speaking positively about those who embody purity and virtue. In everyday life, this can be applied in our interactions, where promoting positivity and recognizing the good in others fosters a supportive community.

  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of truth, integrity, friendship, and positive communication. In real-life scenarios, these values are crucial for building strong relationships, fostering a just society, and achieving personal and communal success. By embodying these principles, individuals can contribute to a harmonious and prosperous environment, reflecting the essence of the mantra in their daily lives.
