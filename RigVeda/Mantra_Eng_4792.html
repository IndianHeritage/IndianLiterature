स नो नियुद्भिर ā पृण कामं वाजेभिर अश्विभिः |गोमद्भिर गोपते धृतत ||
sa no niyudbhir ā pṛṇa kāmaṁ vājebhir aśvibhiḥ | gomadbhir gopate dhṛṣat ||
  • sa no niyudbhir: This phrase can be translated as "may he (the divine) guide us." The term "niyud" refers to guidance or direction, suggesting a plea for divine support in our endeavors.
  • ā pṛṇa kāmaṁ: This translates to "fulfill our desires." Here, "pṛṇa" means to fulfill or satisfy, and "kāma" refers to desires or wishes. This part of the mantra expresses a yearning for the fulfillment of our aspirations.
  • vājebhir aśvibhiḥ: This can be interpreted as "with the strength of horses." "Vāja" means strength or vigor, and "aśvibhiḥ" refers to horses, which are often symbolic of power and speed. This suggests a desire for robust energy and capability in achieving our goals.
  • gomadbhir: This word translates to "with the wealth of cows." In ancient Vedic society, cows were considered a symbol of wealth and prosperity. This indicates a wish for abundance and resources.
  • gopate: This term means "O Lord of the cows" or "O protector." It is a respectful address to a divine figure, emphasizing the importance of divine protection and guidance.
  • dhṛṣat: This word translates to "may we be strong" or "may we have courage." It signifies a request for strength and resilience in the face of challenges.

Conclusion: The mantra "sa no niyudbhir ā pṛṇa kāmaṁ vājebhir aśvibhiḥ gomadbhir gopate dhṛṣat" encapsulates a profound plea for divine guidance, fulfillment of desires, strength, and abundance. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts such as:

  • In personal endeavors, individuals can recite this mantra when embarking on new projects or goals, seeking divine support and strength to overcome obstacles.
  • In community settings, it can be used during gatherings or rituals to invoke blessings for collective prosperity and well-being.
  • In times of uncertainty or challenge, this mantra serves as a reminder to seek guidance and maintain courage, reinforcing the belief that divine support is available to those who ask.

Overall, this mantra emphasizes the interconnectedness of divine guidance, personal aspirations, and the strength needed to achieve them, making it relevant in both spiritual and practical aspects of life.
