समानमञ्ज्येषां विभ्राजन्ते रुक्मासोऽधि बाहुषु |दविद्युतत्यृष्टयः ||
samānam añjy eṣāṁ vi bhrājante rukmāso adhi bāhuṣu | davidyutaty ṛṣṭayaḥ ||
  • Mantra: "samānam añjy eṣāṁ vi bhrājante rukmāso adhi bāhuṣu | davidyutaty ṛṣṭayaḥ ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • samānam: similar, equal, or harmonious
    • añjy: oil or unguent, often symbolizing nourishment or enhancement
    • eṣāṁ: of these, referring to a group or collective
    • vi: in this context, it can mean 'in the midst of' or 'among'
    • bhrājante: shine, radiate, or illuminate
    • rukmāso: golden, often symbolizing wealth or prosperity
    • adhi: upon or over
    • bāhuṣu: arms, symbolizing strength or capability
    • davidyutaty: of the brilliant or radiant
    • ṛṣṭayaḥ: the desires or aspirations
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of harmony and unity among individuals or elements.
    • The imagery of "rukmāso adhi bāhuṣu" suggests that there is a golden radiance that shines upon the arms, symbolizing strength and capability enhanced by unity.
    • The phrase "davidyutaty ṛṣṭayaḥ" indicates that the aspirations or desires are illuminated by this brilliance, suggesting that when individuals come together in harmony, their collective goals become clearer and more achievable.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace, when team members collaborate harmoniously, their combined efforts can lead to innovative solutions and successful projects, much like the golden radiance enhancing their capabilities.
    • In a community, when people unite for a common cause, such as environmental conservation, their collective strength can lead to significant positive changes, illuminating their shared aspirations.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of unity and collaboration in achieving common goals.
    • It signifies that when individuals come together with a shared purpose, their combined strengths can lead to greater achievements and illuminate their paths.
    • In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios, from personal relationships to professional environments, highlighting the power of teamwork and collective effort.
