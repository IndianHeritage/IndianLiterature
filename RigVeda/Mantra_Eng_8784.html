शरणावति सोमं इन्द्रः पिबतु वृत्रहा |बलं दधाना आत्मनि करिष्यन् वीर्यं महद इन्द्रायन्दो परि श्रव ||
śaryaṇāvati somam indraḥ pibatu vṛtrahā | balaṁ dadhāna ātmani kariṣyan vīryam mahad indrāyendo pari srava ||
  • śaryaṇāvati somam: This phrase can be translated as "may Indra drink the Soma that is rich in nourishment." In Vedic tradition, Soma is a sacred drink that is believed to confer immortality and strength. It symbolizes divine inspiration and the essence of life.
  • indraḥ pibatu: This means "may Indra drink." Indra is the king of the gods in Vedic mythology, often associated with thunder, rain, and war. His drinking of Soma signifies the acquisition of strength and power.
  • vṛtrahā: This term refers to "the slayer of Vritra." Vritra is a serpent or dragon that represents chaos and drought. Indra's victory over Vritra symbolizes the triumph of order over chaos, and the restoration of fertility and abundance.
  • balaṁ dadhāna ātmani: This translates to "establishing strength within oneself." It emphasizes the importance of inner strength and resilience, suggesting that true power comes from within.
  • kariṣyan vīryam mahad: This means "may I perform great deeds of valor." It calls for the pursuit of courage and heroic actions, encouraging individuals to strive for greatness in their endeavors.
  • indrāyendo pari srava: This phrase can be interpreted as "may the strength flow towards Indra." It signifies the importance of channeling one's energy and efforts towards a higher purpose or divine entity.

Conclusion:

The mantra encapsulates themes of strength, divine inspiration, and the triumph of order over chaos. In real life, it serves as a reminder to cultivate inner strength and resilience, much like athletes who prepare mentally and physically for competition. It encourages individuals to strive for greatness and to channel their efforts towards noble causes, similar to leaders who inspire their teams to achieve collective goals. The significance of this mantra lies in its ability to motivate individuals to overcome challenges and pursue their aspirations with courage and determination.
