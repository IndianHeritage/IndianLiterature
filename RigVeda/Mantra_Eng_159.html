आ त्वा वहन्तु हरयो वृषणं सोमपीतये |इन्द्र त्वा सूरचक्षसः ||
ā tvā vahantu harayo vṛṣaṇaṁ somapītaye | indra tvā sūracakṣasaḥ ||
  • Mantra: "ā tvā vahantu harayo vṛṣaṇaṁ somapītaye | indra tvā sūracakṣasaḥ ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā: to you
    • tvā: you
    • vahantu: may they carry or bring
    • harayo: the shining ones or the divine beings
    • vṛṣaṇaṁ: strength or power
    • somapītaye: for the sake of the drinker of Soma (a sacred drink)
    • indra: Indra, the king of gods
    • tvā: you
    • sūracakṣasaḥ: with the eyes of the divine or the powerful
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra is a prayer directed towards Indra, the chief deity in Vedic tradition, who represents strength, power, and the forces of nature.
    • The phrase "ā tvā vahantu harayo" expresses a wish that divine beings may carry or bring strength to the individual. This can be likened to seeking support from mentors or leaders in our lives who guide us towards our goals.
    • "vṛṣaṇaṁ somapītaye" indicates a desire for strength for the purpose of enjoying the benefits of life, symbolized by the sacred drink Soma, which represents vitality and spiritual enlightenment. In real life, this can be compared to the pursuit of knowledge and experiences that enrich our lives.
    • The invocation of Indra with "indra tvā sūracakṣasaḥ" emphasizes the need for divine insight and clarity in our actions. This can be related to seeking wisdom and guidance in decision-making processes, whether in personal or professional contexts.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of seeking divine support and strength in our endeavors. It highlights the interconnectedness of spiritual and material pursuits.
    • In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios such as during challenging times when one seeks guidance, or when embarking on new ventures that require courage and insight.
    • Ultimately, it serves as a reminder to acknowledge the divine forces at play in our lives and to seek their blessings for strength and clarity in our actions.
