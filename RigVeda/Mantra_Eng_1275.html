तुग्रो ह भुज्युम अश्विनोदमेघे रयिं न कश्चित् ममृवाम् अवाहाः |तम ऊहथुर नौभिर आत्मन्वतीभिर अन्तरिक्षप्रुद्भिर अपोदकाभिः ||
tugro ha bhujyum aśvinodameghe rayiṁ na kaś cin mamṛvām̐ avāhāḥ | tam ūhathur naubhir ātmanvatībhir antarikṣaprudbhir apodakābhiḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • tugro: strong, powerful
    • ha: indeed
    • bhujyum: to the one who is capable
    • aśvinodameghe: O Ashvins, the divine twin horsemen
    • rayiṁ: wealth, prosperity
    • na: not
    • kaś: anyone
    • cin: at all
    • mamṛvām̐: may I not be deprived
    • avāhāḥ: of the blessings
    • tam: that
    • ūhathur: they have brought
    • naubhir: with ships
    • ātmanvatībhir: with self-sufficient beings
    • antarikṣaprudbhir: from the celestial realms
    • apodakābhiḥ: with waters that are pure
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to the Ashvins, who are considered the divine physicians and bringers of prosperity in Vedic literature.
    • The speaker expresses a desire for strength and capability, seeking the blessings of the Ashvins to ensure that they are not deprived of wealth and prosperity.
    • The mention of "ships" and "self-sufficient beings" suggests a connection to trade, travel, and the importance of community and collaboration in achieving success.
    • The reference to "pure waters" symbolizes clarity, purity, and the essential nature of resources that sustain life and prosperity.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern business, this mantra can be likened to seeking the support of mentors and partners who can help navigate challenges and bring prosperity.
    • In personal life, it reflects the importance of community support and the need for clear communication and collaboration to achieve common goals.
    • In environmental contexts, the emphasis on "pure waters" can relate to the significance of clean water resources for health and prosperity in communities.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of seeking divine support in our endeavors, emphasizing the importance of strength, community, and purity in achieving prosperity.
    • It serves as a reminder that success is often a collective effort, requiring collaboration and the nurturing of resources that sustain life.
    • In real-life scenarios, it encourages individuals to seek help, maintain clarity in their pursuits, and value the contributions of others in their journey towards prosperity.
