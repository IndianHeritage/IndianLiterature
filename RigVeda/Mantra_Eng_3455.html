व्य अकृṇोता चमसं चतुर्धा सखे वि शिक्षेति अब्रवीत् |अथैता वाजां अमृतस्य पन्थां गाणं देवानां ऋभवः सुहस्ताः ||
vy akṛṇota camasaṁ caturdhā sakhe vi śikṣety abravīta | athaita vājā amṛtasya panthāṁ gaṇaṁ devānām ṛbhavaḥ suhastāḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • vy akṛṇota: He (the creator) made or created.
    • camasaṁ: The cup or vessel.
    • caturdhā: In four ways or forms.
    • sakhe: O friend.
    • vi śikṣety: He instructed or taught.
    • abravīta: He spoke or declared.
    • athaita: And then, this.
    • vājā: The prize or reward.
    • amṛtasya: Of immortality.
    • panthāṁ: The path or way.
    • gaṇaṁ: The group or assembly.
    • devānām: Of the gods.
    • ṛbhavaḥ: The wise or enlightened ones.
    • suhastāḥ: With good hands or auspicious hands.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the creation of a vessel (camasa) in four forms, which can symbolize the multifaceted nature of existence and the various paths one can take in life.
    • The phrase "O friend" indicates a sense of camaraderie and shared journey, suggesting that we are not alone in our pursuits.
    • The instruction to seek the "path of immortality" implies a quest for knowledge, wisdom, and spiritual enlightenment.
    • The mention of the "assembly of the gods" signifies the importance of community and collaboration in achieving higher goals.
    • The "wise ones" with "auspicious hands" can be seen as mentors or guides who help us navigate our paths effectively.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, the creation of a project (the vessel) can take various forms (caturdhā) depending on the team's approach and collaboration (sakhe).
    • Mentorship programs in workplaces exemplify the "wise ones" guiding newcomers towards success and growth.
    • In personal development, individuals often seek paths (panthāṁ) that lead to self-improvement and fulfillment, much like the quest for immortality (amṛtasya).
    • Community service initiatives can reflect the assembly of the gods (gaṇaṁ devānām), where collective efforts lead to significant societal impact.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of creation, collaboration, and guidance in our lives.
    • It teaches us that the journey towards wisdom and immortality is not solitary; we must engage with others and learn from those who have walked the path before us.
    • In real life, applying the principles of this mantra can lead to personal growth, successful teamwork, and a deeper understanding of our purpose.
    • Ultimately, it encourages us to seek knowledge and support from our community as we navigate the complexities of life.
