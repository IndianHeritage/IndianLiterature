सदा सुगः पितुमां अस्तु पन्था मध्वा देवाः ओषधिः सम पिपृक्त |भागो मे अग्नि सख्ये न मृध्या उद रायो अश्यां सदनं पुरुक्षोः ||
sadā sugaḥ pitumām̐ astu panthā madhvā devā oṣadhīḥ sam pipṛkta | bhago me agne sakhye na mṛdhyā ud rāyo aśyāṁ sadanam purukṣoḥ ||
  • Translation: "May I always have the sweet taste of the divine nectar, as the gods have prepared it for me. O Agni, may your blessings not be diminished, and may I attain the abode of the prosperous."
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sadā: always
    • sugaḥ: sweet or pleasant
    • pitumām̐: nectar or essence
    • astu: may it be
    • panthā: path or way
    • madhvā: sweet or honey-like
    • devā: gods
    • oṣadhīḥ: herbs or plants
    • sam pipṛkta: have prepared or made
    • bhago: prosperity or wealth
    • me: my
    • agne: O Agni (the fire god)
    • sakhye: in friendship
    • na: not
    • mṛdhyā: diminished or reduced
    • ud: upward or higher
    • rāyo: wealth or prosperity
    • aśyāṁ: abode or dwelling
    • sadanam: home or residence
    • purukṣoḥ: of the many or the prosperous
  • Significance:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking sweetness and positivity in life, akin to the divine nectar that nourishes the soul.
    • It reflects a desire for a prosperous path, suggesting that one should strive for a life filled with abundance and joy.
    • Agni, as the fire god, symbolizes transformation and purification, indicating that one should seek blessings for personal growth and success.
  • Real-Life Application:
    • In daily life, this mantra can inspire individuals to pursue their goals with a positive mindset, focusing on the sweetness of experiences rather than challenges.
    • It can be recited during times of transition, such as starting a new job or moving to a new place, to invoke blessings for prosperity and success.
    • In relationships, it encourages fostering friendships and connections that are enriching and supportive, much like the blessings of Agni.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder to seek the sweetness in life and to cultivate a prosperous path through positive actions and relationships.
    • By invoking the blessings of Agni, one can aspire to achieve personal growth and a fulfilling life, making it relevant in various aspects of modern living.
