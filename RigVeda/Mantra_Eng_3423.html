सहस्रं व्यतीनां युक्तानाम इन्द्रं ईमहे |शतम् सोमस्य खार्यः ||
sahasraṁ vyatīnāṁ yuktānām indram īmahe | śataṁ somasya khāryaḥ ||
  • Mantra: "sahasraṁ vyatīnāṁ yuktānām indram īmahe | śataṁ somasya khāryaḥ ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sahasraṁ: thousand
    • vyatīnāṁ: of those who have crossed over
    • yuktānām: of the wise or those who are skilled
    • indram: Indra, the king of gods
    • īmahe: we invoke or we meditate upon
    • śataṁ: hundred
    • somasya: of the soma (a sacred drink)
    • khāryaḥ: the essence or the best part
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a call to Indra, the deity associated with strength, rain, and the heavens, to bless those who are wise and have successfully crossed challenges.
    • The phrase "sahasraṁ vyatīnāṁ" signifies the multitude of individuals who have overcome obstacles, suggesting a collective strength and wisdom.
    • By invoking Indra, the mantra seeks divine support and guidance for those who are skilled and knowledgeable.
    • The reference to "śataṁ somasya khāryaḥ" emphasizes the importance of the essence of soma, which symbolizes vitality, nourishment, and spiritual enlightenment.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, a team that has successfully navigated challenges can be seen as "vyatīnāṁ yuktānām." They may invoke the spirit of collaboration and wisdom to achieve greater goals.
    • In personal development, individuals who have overcome significant life challenges can reflect on their journey and seek inspiration from the essence of their experiences, akin to the "soma" that nourishes their growth.
    • In community gatherings, invoking a higher power or collective wisdom can unify individuals, much like the invocation of Indra in this mantra, fostering a sense of shared purpose and strength.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of collective wisdom and the strength derived from overcoming challenges.
    • It serves as a reminder to seek divine guidance and support in our endeavors, whether in personal, professional, or communal contexts.
    • By recognizing the essence of our experiences and invoking higher powers, we can navigate life's complexities with greater resilience and purpose.
