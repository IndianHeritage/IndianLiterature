आ यद रुहाव वरुणश्च नावम् प्र यत समुद्रम ईरयाव मध्यम् |अधि यद अपां स्नुभिः चराव प्र प्रेङ्ख ईङ्खयावहै शुभे कम् ||
ā yad ruhāva varuṇaś ca nāvam pra yat samudram īrayāva madhyam | adhi yad apāṁ snubhiś carāva pra preṅkha īṅkhayāvahai śubhe kam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā yad: Whatever
    • ruhāva: rises or ascends
    • varuṇaś: Varuna (the god of water and celestial ocean)
    • ca: and
    • nāvam: the boat or vessel
    • pra: towards
    • yat: which
    • samudram: the ocean
    • īrayāva: we navigate or steer
    • madhyam: in the middle
    • adhi: above or over
    • yad: whatever
    • apāṁ: of waters
    • snubhiś: with the currents
    • carāva: we move or flow
    • pra: towards
    • preṅkha: the path or direction
    • īṅkhayāvahai: we navigate or steer
    • śubhe: auspicious or good
    • kam: to whom or what
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the journey of life and the divine guidance we seek while navigating through it.
    • It emphasizes the importance of understanding the forces around us, represented by Varuna, the god of water, who symbolizes the vastness and unpredictability of life.
    • The "boat" represents our life, and the "ocean" signifies the challenges and experiences we encounter.
    • Moving "in the middle" suggests finding balance amidst chaos, while "currents" symbolize the various influences and circumstances that affect our journey.
    • The mantra encourages us to seek auspicious paths and divine guidance in our endeavors.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional context, when starting a new project, one might feel overwhelmed by the challenges ahead. This mantra serves as a reminder to seek guidance and navigate through uncertainties with confidence.
    • In personal relationships, when facing conflicts, the mantra encourages individuals to find a balanced approach and seek a harmonious resolution, much like steering a boat through turbulent waters.
    • During times of decision-making, such as choosing a career path or making significant life changes, the mantra inspires individuals to trust in divine guidance and remain open to the auspicious opportunities that arise.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of seeking divine guidance and maintaining balance in life's journey.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, reminding us to navigate through challenges with wisdom and trust in the auspicious paths that unfold before us.
    • Ultimately, it encourages a harmonious relationship with the forces of nature and the divine, fostering resilience and clarity in our pursuits.
