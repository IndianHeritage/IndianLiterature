मन्त्र: कदात्विषन्त सूरयस् तिर आप इव श्रिधः | अर्षन्ति पूतदक्षसः ||
kad atviṣanta sūrayas tira āpa iva sridhaḥ | arṣanti pūtadakṣasaḥ ||
  • Mantra: "kad atviṣanta sūrayas tira āpa iva sridhaḥ | arṣanti pūtadakṣasaḥ ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • kad: when
    • atviṣanta: they shine or illuminate
    • sūrayas: the sun or solar beings
    • tira: across or through
    • āpa: waters or the essence of life
    • iva: like or as
    • sridhaḥ: in harmony or in a state of abundance
    • arṣanti: they are inspired or they rise
    • pūtadakṣasaḥ: those who are pure and skilled
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of a moment when the solar beings shine brightly, illuminating the world around them.
    • The comparison of the sun to waters signifies the life-giving and nurturing qualities of both light and water.
    • The phrase "in harmony" suggests a state of balance and abundance, where all elements coexist peacefully.
    • The mention of "pure and skilled" beings indicates that those who are virtuous and knowledgeable are inspired and uplifted in this harmonious state.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In nature, the sun's rays nourish plants, allowing them to grow and thrive, much like how knowledge and purity can help individuals flourish in society.
    • In a community, when people work together harmoniously, sharing their skills and knowledge, they create an environment that is nurturing and supportive, akin to the sun and water sustaining life.
    • In personal development, individuals who cultivate purity of thought and action often find themselves inspired and motivated, leading to personal growth and success.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of harmony, purity, and the nurturing aspects of life.
    • It serves as a reminder that when we align ourselves with virtuous principles and work together, we can create a thriving environment for ourselves and others.
    • In real life, this can be applied in various contexts, such as community building, personal development, and environmental sustainability, highlighting the interconnectedness of all life.
