परी प्र सोम ते रसः 'सरजी कलशे सुतः |श्येनो न तक्तो अर्षति ||
pari pra soma te raso 'sarji kalaśe sutaḥ | śyeno na takto arṣati ||
  • Mantra: "pari pra soma te raso 'sarji kalaśe sutaḥ | śyeno na takto arṣati ||"
  • Translation: "May the essence of the Soma, which is poured into the vessel, be abundant; like the falcon that does not falter."
  • Word-by-Word Explanation:
    • pari: around, in all directions
    • pra: forth, forward
    • soma: a sacred drink, often associated with immortality and divine inspiration
    • te: your
    • raso: essence, flavor, or juice
    • 'sarji: poured, released
    • kalaśe: in the vessel or pot
    • sutaḥ: son, offspring, or that which is produced
    • śyeno: falcon, a symbol of strength and swiftness
    • na: not
    • takto: falter, waver, or hesitate
    • arṣati: to rise, to soar
  • Contextual Significance:
    • The mantra invokes the essence of Soma, which is often seen as a metaphor for divine inspiration and the vital energy that sustains life.
    • The reference to the falcon symbolizes the ability to rise above challenges and maintain focus and determination.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, the essence of Soma can be likened to the motivation and creativity that drive innovation. For instance, a team brainstorming session can lead to the 'pouring' of ideas that fuel a successful project.
    • The falcon's unwavering flight can represent an athlete's determination to succeed despite obstacles, such as a runner pushing through fatigue to finish a race.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of harnessing inner strength and inspiration to achieve one's goals.
    • It serves as a reminder to remain steadfast and focused, much like the falcon, in the pursuit of our aspirations.
    • In daily life, this can be applied in various scenarios, from personal growth to professional endeavors, encouraging individuals to seek their inner essence and rise above challenges.
