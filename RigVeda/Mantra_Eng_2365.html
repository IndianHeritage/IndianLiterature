अपां नपाद आ ह्य अस्ताद उपस्थं जिह्मानाम ऊर्ध्वो विद्युतं वसानः |तस्य ज्येष्ठम् महिमानं वहन्तीर हिरण्यवर्णाः परि यन्ति यह्वीः ||
apāṁ napād ā hy asthād upasthaṁ jihmānām ūrdhvo vidyutaṁ vasānaḥ | tasya jyeṣṭham mahimānaṁ vahantīr hiraṇyavarṇāḥ pari yanti yahvīḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • apāṁ: of waters
    • napād: the foot or the path
    • ā hy: indeed
    • asthād: has established
    • upasthaṁ: the presence or the approach
    • jihmānām: of the swift or the quick
    • ūrdhvo: upward
    • vidyutaṁ: lightning or brilliance
    • vasānaḥ: dwelling or residing
    • tasya: of that
    • jyeṣṭham: the greatest or the elder
    • mahimānaṁ: of glory or greatness
    • vahantīr: carrying or bearing
    • hiraṇyavarṇāḥ: golden-colored or radiant
    • pari: all around
    • yanti: they go or they move
    • yahvīḥ: the swift or the quick ones
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the waters that have established their path and presence, symbolizing the flow of life and energy.
    • The mention of "jihmānām" refers to the swift and dynamic nature of these waters, akin to how opportunities in life can come quickly and unexpectedly.
    • "ūrdhvo vidyutaṁ" suggests a connection to the upward movement of energy, similar to how aspirations and ambitions can elevate one's life.
    • The "hiraṇyavarṇāḥ" indicates a radiant quality, representing the potential for greatness and success that can be achieved through the right actions and mindset.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In nature, rivers and streams symbolize the flow of life, reminding us to adapt and move with the currents of change.
    • In personal development, the mantra encourages individuals to seize opportunities as they arise, much like how one must be ready to catch a wave when surfing.
    • In professional settings, the idea of upward energy can be seen in career advancements, where individuals strive for higher positions and greater responsibilities.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of recognizing and harnessing the dynamic forces of life, represented by water and energy.
    • Its significance lies in the reminder to be aware of the opportunities that flow around us and to act swiftly to embrace them.
    • In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios, from personal growth to professional success, encouraging a proactive and positive approach to life's challenges.
