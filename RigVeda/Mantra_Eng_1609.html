यद धा त्यद वाम पुरुमीळ्हस्य सोमिनः प्र मित्रासो न दधिरे स्वाभुवः |अध क्रतुं विदतं गातुम आर्चत उता श्रुतं वृषणा पस्त्यावतः ||
yad dha tyad vām purumīḻhasya sominaḥ pra mitrāso na dadhire svābhuvaḥ | adha kratuṁ vidataṁ gātum arcata uta śrutaṁ vṛṣaṇā pastyāvataḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yad dha: whatever
    • tyad: that
    • vām: your
    • purumīḻhasya: of the great one
    • sominaḥ: of the moon or soma (nectar)
    • pra: before
    • mitrāso: of friends
    • na: not
    • dadhire: they hold
    • svābhuvaḥ: self-born or self-existent
    • adha: below
    • kratuṁ: the will or intention
    • vidataṁ: to be made
    • gātum: to go or to move
    • arcata: to worship or to honor
    • uta: or
    • śrutaṁ: heard or known
    • vṛṣaṇā: of the strong or powerful
    • pastyāvataḥ: of the one who is capable
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the interconnectedness of existence and the divine.
    • It emphasizes the importance of intentions and the power of collective will.
    • The reference to "soma" symbolizes nourishment and the essence of life, suggesting that our actions should be in harmony with the greater cosmos.
    • The phrase "self-born" indicates that true wisdom and strength come from within, and we must recognize our own potential.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a team setting, the mantra can be applied to emphasize collaboration. Just as friends (mitrāso) work together, individuals should align their intentions (kratuṁ) to achieve a common goal.
    • In personal development, recognizing one's own strength (vṛṣaṇā) and potential (svābhuvaḥ) can lead to greater self-awareness and empowerment.
    • In spiritual practices, the act of worship (arcata) can be seen as a way to honor the interconnectedness of all beings and the divine essence within.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of unity, intention, and self-awareness in our lives.
    • Its significance lies in the understanding that our actions and thoughts are interconnected with the universe, and by aligning them, we can create a harmonious existence.
    • In real-life scenarios, whether in personal growth, teamwork, or spiritual practices, the principles outlined in this mantra can guide us towards a more fulfilling and purposeful life.
