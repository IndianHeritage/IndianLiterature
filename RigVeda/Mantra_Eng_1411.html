अस्तोढ्वं स्तोम्या ब्रह्मणा मे 'विवृद्धध्वम् उषतीर उषासः |युष्माकं देवीर अवसा सनेमा सहस्रिणं च शतिनं च वाजम् ||
astoḍhvaṁ stomyā brahmaṇā me 'vīvṛdhadhvam uśatīr uṣāsaḥ | yuṣmākaṁ devīr avasā sanema sahasriṇaṁ ca śatinaṁ ca vājam ||
  • Mantra: "astoḍhvaṁ stomyā brahmaṇā me 'vīvṛdhadhvam uśatīr uṣāsaḥ | yuṣmākaṁ devīr avasā sanema sahasriṇaṁ ca śatinaṁ ca vājam ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • astoḍhvaṁ: "May you be elevated" or "May you rise high."
    • stomyā: "With praise" or "through hymns."
    • brahmaṇā: "By the divine" or "through the supreme consciousness."
    • me: "My" or "to me."
    • 'vīvṛdhadhvam: "May you grow" or "may you expand."
    • uśatīr: "The auspicious" or "the beneficial."
    • uṣāsaḥ: "Of the dawn" or "of the new beginnings."
    • yuṣmākaṁ: "Your" or "of you."
    • devīr: "Goddesses" or "divine beings."
    • avasā: "May we be protected" or "may we be sheltered."
    • sanema: "We may attain" or "we may achieve."
    • sahasriṇaṁ: "With a thousand" or "with many."
    • ca: "And."
    • śatinaṁ: "Of strength" or "of power."
    • vājam: "The wealth" or "the resources."
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra is a prayer for elevation and growth, invoking divine blessings for prosperity and auspicious beginnings.
    • The phrase "May you be elevated" suggests a desire for spiritual and material upliftment, akin to striving for personal growth in one's career or relationships.
    • When we say "with praise," it emphasizes the importance of gratitude and acknowledgment of the divine forces in our lives, similar to how we express appreciation to mentors or guides who help us succeed.
    • The invocation of "goddesses" symbolizes the feminine divine energy, representing nurturing, wisdom, and strength, which can be seen in real-life examples of women leading communities or organizations.
    • The call for protection and achievement reflects a universal human desire for safety and success, much like how individuals seek support from family and friends during challenging times.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of seeking divine guidance and support in our endeavors, reminding us that we are not alone in our journeys.
    • It is applicable in real-life scenarios such as starting a new project, entering a new phase in life, or seeking strength during difficult times.
    • By reciting this mantra, individuals can cultivate a mindset of gratitude, resilience, and openness to divine blessings, fostering a sense of community and shared purpose.
