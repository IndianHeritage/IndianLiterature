पुराणां वां वीर्याः प्र ब्रवाम जन एथो हासथुर भिषजामयोभुवा |तां वां नु नव्याव अवसे करामहे यं नासत्याः श्रद अरिर यथा दधत ||
purāṇā vāṁ vīryā3 pra bravā jane 'tho hāsathur bhiṣajā mayobhuvā | tā vāṁ nu navyāv avase karāmahe 'yaṁ nāsatyā śrad arir yathā dadhat ||
  • Mantra: "purāṇā vāṁ vīryā3 pra bravā jane 'tho hāsathur bhiṣajā mayobhuvā | tā vāṁ nu navyāv avase karāmahe 'yaṁ nāsatyā śrad arir yathā dadhat ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • purāṇā: ancient, old
    • vāṁ: your
    • vīryā: strength, power
    • pra: forth, out
    • bravā: I speak, I declare
    • jane: to people, to the world
    • 'tho: indeed, truly
    • hāsathur: healer, physician
    • bhiṣajā: of the healer, of the physician
    • mayobhuvā: born of the mind, mental
    • tā: those
    • vāṁ: your
    • nu: indeed, truly
    • navyāv: new, fresh
    • avase: to bring, to lead
    • karāmahe: we perform, we do
    • 'yaṁ: that
    • nāsatyā: of the Asvins, the twin deities
    • śrad: faith, trust
    • arir: enemy, adversary
    • yathā: as, like
    • dadhat: they hold, they maintain
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra speaks of invoking the ancient strength and power that is inherent in the divine.
    • It emphasizes the role of healers and physicians, suggesting that they possess a unique ability to channel this ancient wisdom for the benefit of humanity.
    • The reference to "new" indicates the continuous renewal of this strength and wisdom, suggesting that it is always available to those who seek it.
    • The mention of the Asvins, who are known as divine physicians, highlights the importance of faith and trust in the healing process.
    • In real life, this can be seen in the way people turn to doctors and healers, placing their trust in their expertise and the ancient knowledge they carry.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of ancient wisdom and the role of healers in society.
    • It teaches us to trust in the healing process and the knowledge that has been passed down through generations.
    • In a modern context, it can be applied in healthcare, where the integration of traditional and modern medicine can lead to holistic healing.
    • Ultimately, it reminds us that strength and healing are not just physical but also mental and spiritual, urging us to seek balance in all aspects of life.
