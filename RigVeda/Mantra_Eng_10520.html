बृहस्पतिर्नयतु दुर्गहातिरः पुनर्नेषदघशंसाय मनमा |क्षिपदशस्तिमअपदुर्मतिं हन्न अथाकरदयजमानाय शं योः ||
bṛhaspatir nayatu durgahā tiraḥ punar neṣad aghaśaṁsāya manma | kṣipad aśastim apa durmatiṁ hann athā karad yajamānāya śaṁ yoḥ ||
  • Mantra: "bṛhaspatir nayatu durgahā tiraḥ punar neṣad aghaśaṁsāya manma | kṣipad aśastim apa durmatiṁ hann athā karad yajamānāya śaṁ yoḥ ||"
  • Translation: "May Brihaspati lead us across the difficult path, may he remove the obstacles and grant us wisdom. May he destroy the evil thoughts and grant prosperity to the one who offers sacrifices."

  • Word-by-Word Explanation:
    • Brihaspati: The deity representing wisdom, knowledge, and eloquence.
    • Nayatu: May he lead or guide.
    • Durgahā: Difficult or challenging.
    • Tiraḥ: Across or beyond.
    • Punar: Again or anew.
    • Neṣad: Remove or eliminate.
    • Aghaśaṁsāya: Evil thoughts or negative influences.
    • Manma: Grant me or bless me.
    • Kṣipad: Destroy or eliminate.
    • Aśastim: Obstacles or difficulties.
    • Apa: Away or from.
    • Durmatiṁ: Bad or misguided thoughts.
    • Hann: To destroy.
    • Athā: Therefore or thus.
    • Karad: To the one who performs or offers.
    • Yajamānāya: To the one who is performing the sacrifice.
    • Śaṁ: Peace or prosperity.
    • Yoḥ: Of whom or to whom.

  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a challenging situation, such as preparing for an important exam, students often seek guidance and wisdom to navigate their studies effectively. This mantra can be a source of inspiration, encouraging them to overcome obstacles and focus on their goals.
    • In professional settings, when facing tough decisions or ethical dilemmas, individuals can invoke the essence of this mantra to seek clarity and wisdom, helping them to make informed choices that lead to positive outcomes.
    • During rituals or ceremonies, individuals may recite this mantra to invoke blessings for prosperity and success, ensuring that their efforts are fruitful and free from negative influences.

  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine guidance and the removal of obstacles in our lives. It emphasizes the importance of wisdom and clarity in decision-making, urging individuals to seek higher knowledge and understanding. In real-life scenarios, whether in education, professional endeavors, or personal challenges, this mantra serves as a reminder to remain focused, seek guidance, and strive for prosperity while overcoming negativity. By embodying the principles of this mantra, individuals can navigate life's difficulties with resilience and purpose.
