न तं अशनति कश्चन दिव इव सान्वारभम् |सावरण्यस्य दक्षिणा वि सिन्धुर इव पप्रथे ||
na tam aśnoti kaś cana diva iva sānv ārabham | sāvarṇyasya dakṣiṇā vi sindhur iva paprathe ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • na: not
    • tam: him/that
    • aśnoti: achieves/attains
    • kaś cana: anyone
    • diva: like the sky
    • iva: like
    • sānv: with a firm foundation
    • ārabham: beginning/start
    • sāvarṇyasya: of the universal order
    • dakṣiṇā: southern
    • vi: like
    • sindhur: ocean
    • iva: like
    • paprathe: flows/expands
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra suggests that no one can attain or achieve anything without a solid foundation or support, much like the vastness of the sky.
    • The reference to the "southern" aspect may symbolize a direction of prosperity or abundance, indicating that true success comes from aligning oneself with universal principles.
    • The comparison to the ocean emphasizes the idea of vastness and depth, suggesting that true achievements are profound and expansive.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In education, students who build a strong foundation in their early years tend to achieve greater success in higher studies, much like how a building requires a solid base.
    • In business, companies that adhere to ethical practices and align with universal values often find long-term success, akin to the ocean's steady flow.
    • In personal relationships, individuals who cultivate strong, supportive connections are more likely to experience fulfilling and lasting bonds, similar to the stability provided by the sky.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of a strong foundation and alignment with universal principles in achieving success.
    • It serves as a reminder that true accomplishments are not just about individual effort but also about the support and values that guide us.
    • In real life, applying this wisdom can lead to more meaningful achievements in various aspects, including education, business, and personal relationships.
