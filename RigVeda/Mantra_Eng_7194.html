त्यान नु क्षत्रियाम् अव आदित्यान याचिषामहे |सुमृळीकाम् अभिष्टये ||
tyān nu kṣatriyām̐ ava ādityān yāciṣāmahe | sumṛḻīkām̐ abhiṣṭaye ||
  • Mantra: "tyān nu kṣatriyām̐ ava ādityān yāciṣāmahe | sumṛḻīkām̐ abhiṣṭaye ||"
  • Translation: "We invoke the Kshatriyas (warriors) and the Adityas (solar deities) for blessings and prosperity."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • tyān: "those" or "them," referring to the Kshatriyas.
    • nu: "indeed" or "truly," emphasizing the invocation.
    • kṣatriyām̐: "Kshatriyas," the warrior class in Vedic society, symbolizing strength, protection, and valor.
    • ava: "down" or "towards," indicating a direction of invocation.
    • ādityān: "the Adityas," representing the solar deities, associated with light, truth, and cosmic order.
    • yāciṣāmahe: "we invoke" or "we seek," a call for divine assistance.
    • sumṛḻīkām̐: "for prosperity" or "for well-being," indicating the desired outcome of the invocation.
    • abhiṣṭaye: "for blessings" or "for fulfillment," emphasizing the intention behind the invocation.
  • Contextual Significance:
    • This mantra highlights the importance of invoking both strength (Kshatriyas) and divine guidance (Adityas) in achieving success and prosperity.
    • In real life, this can be seen in various contexts such as leadership, where a leader may seek the support of their team (Kshatriyas) and the guidance of higher principles (Adityas) to achieve goals.
    • For example, in a business setting, a manager may rely on their team's skills and dedication while also adhering to ethical practices and values to ensure the success of a project.
  • Conclusion:
    • The mantra serves as a reminder of the balance between strength and wisdom, urging individuals to seek both physical prowess and moral integrity in their endeavors.
    • It is applicable in various life scenarios, including personal growth, leadership, and community building, where invoking the right qualities can lead to prosperity and fulfillment.
    • Ultimately, it emphasizes the interconnectedness of human effort and divine support in achieving one's aspirations.
