कथा सबाधः शशमानो अस्य नशद अभि द्रविणं दीध्यानाḥ |देवो भुवन नवेद मा ऋतानां नमो जगृभ्वां अभि यज जुजोषत ||
kathā sabādhaḥ śaśamāno asya naśad abhi draviṇaṁ dīdhyānaḥ | devo bhuvan navedā ma ṛtānāṁ namo jagṛbhvām̐ abhi yaj jujoṣat ||
  • kathā sabādhaḥ: This phrase can be interpreted as "how the sound resonates" or "how the words are articulated." It emphasizes the importance of communication and expression in our lives.
  • śaśamāno asya: This translates to "the one who is shining or illuminating." It signifies the presence of knowledge and wisdom that enlightens our path.
  • naśad abhi draviṇaṁ: This means "may we attain wealth or resources." It reflects the human desire for prosperity and abundance in life.
  • dīdhyānaḥ: This word can be understood as "to contemplate" or "to meditate." It suggests the importance of reflection and mindfulness in our daily activities.
  • devo bhuvan navedā: This phrase translates to "the divine being who governs the universe." It acknowledges the presence of a higher power that oversees all creation.
  • ma ṛtānāṁ: This means "of the seasons" or "of the cycles of life." It highlights the natural rhythms and changes that occur in our environment.
  • namo jagṛbhvām̐: This translates to "we bow down to the one who has grasped." It signifies respect and reverence towards those who have achieved understanding and mastery.
  • abhi yaj jujoṣat: This means "may we engage in sacrifice or offering." It emphasizes the importance of selflessness and contributing to the greater good.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of communication, wisdom, prosperity, mindfulness, reverence, and selflessness. In real life, it serves as a reminder to articulate our thoughts clearly, seek knowledge, strive for abundance, reflect on our actions, respect the forces that govern our lives, and contribute positively to society. By embodying these principles, we can lead a more fulfilling and harmonious life, aligning ourselves with the natural rhythms of the universe and fostering a sense of community and connection with others.
