जगृभ्मा दूरादिशं श्लोकम अद्रेर अध तमनā |अधारयद अररिन्दानि सुक्रतुः पुरू सद्मानि सुक्रतुः ||
jagṛbhmā dūraādiśaṁ ślokam adrer adha tmanā | adhārayad ararindāni sukratuḥ purū sadmāni sukratuḥ ||
  • jagṛbhmā: This word translates to "I have grasped" or "I have understood." It signifies the act of comprehension or realization.
  • dūraādiśaṁ: This means "from a distance" or "far away." It suggests the idea of reaching out or connecting with something that is not immediately present.
  • ślokam: Refers to a "verse" or "mantra." In this context, it indicates the importance of sacred words or hymns in spiritual practice.
  • adreḥ: This translates to "of the mountain" or "of the earth." It symbolizes stability, strength, and grounding.
  • adha: Meaning "below" or "beneath," it can imply humility or the foundation upon which one builds their understanding.
  • tmanā: This word means "with the mind" or "by the self." It emphasizes the role of consciousness and intention in the process of understanding.
  • adhārayad: This translates to "supported" or "held up." It indicates the idea of nurturing or sustaining something important.
  • ararindāni: This can be interpreted as "the one who conquers enemies" or "the victorious." It represents overcoming challenges and adversities.
  • sukratuḥ: Meaning "good intention" or "noble purpose," it highlights the importance of having a positive mindset and ethical motivations.
  • purū: This translates to "many" or "abundant." It signifies richness in experience or resources.
  • sadmāni: Refers to "homes" or "abodes." It symbolizes the places of comfort, safety, and belonging.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of understanding and grasping knowledge from a distance, suggesting that wisdom can be sought beyond immediate surroundings. It highlights the significance of grounding oneself in humility and intention, while also recognizing the power of good intentions and the ability to overcome challenges. In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios such as personal growth, conflict resolution, and the pursuit of knowledge. By fostering a mindset of understanding and support, individuals can create a nurturing environment that leads to success and fulfillment in their endeavors.
