अचेत्य अग्निश्चिकितुर हव्यवाट स सुमद्रथः |अग्निः शुक्रेण शोचिषा बृहत् सूरो अरोचत दिवि सूर्यो अरोचत ||
acety agniś cikitur havyavāṭ sa sumadrathaḥ | agniḥ śukreṇa śociṣā bṛhat sūro arocata divi sūryo arocata ||
  • acety agniś cikitur havyavāṭ sa sumadrathaḥ: This line can be translated to "The fire, which is the essence of offerings, has been kindled by the wise one." Here, "agni" refers to fire, which is a central element in Vedic rituals, symbolizing purity and the divine. The term "havyavāṭ" indicates the fire that consumes offerings, suggesting the importance of sacrifice and devotion in spiritual practices.
  • agniḥ śukreṇa śociṣā bṛhat sūro arocata: This translates to "The fire, shining brightly with purity, is the great hero that illuminates." The word "śukreṇa" signifies purity, while "bṛhat" means great or vast. This line emphasizes the transformative power of fire, which not only purifies but also provides light and guidance, akin to how knowledge and wisdom illuminate our lives.
  • divi sūryo arocata: This means "The sun shines in the sky." The sun, or "sūrya," is often seen as a symbol of life, energy, and vitality. This line connects the earthly element of fire with the celestial body of the sun, suggesting a unity between the two and the importance of both in sustaining life.

  • Significance: This mantra highlights the essential role of fire in Vedic rituals and its symbolic representation of purity, transformation, and divine presence. It reflects the interconnectedness of earthly and celestial elements, emphasizing the importance of both in spiritual practices.
  • Real-life Application: In modern life, this mantra can be applied to the concept of personal transformation and the pursuit of knowledge. Just as fire purifies and illuminates, individuals can seek to purify their intentions and illuminate their paths through education, self-reflection, and spiritual practices. For example, engaging in meditation or community service can be seen as a way to 'kindle the fire' within oneself, leading to personal growth and a positive impact on society.
