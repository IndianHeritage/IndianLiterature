सहस्रं त इन्द्रोतयो नः सहस्रं इषो हरिवो गूर्ततमाः |सहस्रं रायो मादयध्यै सहस्रीण उप नो यान्तु वाजाः ||
sahasraṁ ta indrotayo naḥ sahasram iṣo harivo gūrtatamāḥ | sahasraṁ rāyo mādayadhyai sahasriṇa upa no yantu vājāḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sahasraṁ: thousand
    • ta: those
    • indrotayo: of Indra (the king of gods)
    • naḥ: to us
    • sahasram: thousand
    • iṣo: of strength
    • harivo: of the horses (symbolizing speed and power)
    • gūrtatamāḥ: the best or most excellent
    • sahasraṁ: thousand
    • rāyo: of wealth
    • mādayadhyai: may we be delighted
    • sahasriṇa: with thousands
    • upa: may come to us
    • no: our
    • yantu: may they go
    • vājāḥ: steeds or horses (representing strength and victory)
  • Overall Meaning: This mantra is a prayer for abundance and strength. It invokes the blessings of Indra, the god of rain and thunder, asking for a thousandfold strength and wealth to come to the devotees. The repetition of "sahasraṁ" emphasizes the desire for abundance in various aspects of life, including strength, wealth, and success.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be seen as a call for prosperity and success in ventures. Entrepreneurs may recite this mantra to attract wealth and favorable outcomes in their endeavors.
    • In personal life, individuals may use this mantra during times of struggle, seeking strength and resilience to overcome challenges, much like athletes preparing for a competition.
    • In community gatherings, this mantra can be chanted to invoke collective strength and unity, encouraging members to work together towards common goals, similar to a team rallying before a big game.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its powerful invocation for abundance and strength. It serves as a reminder that through collective effort and divine blessings, individuals and communities can achieve great heights. In real-life scenarios, whether in personal struggles, business ventures, or community efforts, this mantra encourages a mindset of abundance and resilience, fostering a spirit of collaboration and success. By embracing the essence of this mantra, one can cultivate a life filled with strength, wealth, and joy.
