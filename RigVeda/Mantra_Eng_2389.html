समाववर्ति विष्ठितो जिगीषुर विश्वेषां कामश चरताम अमाभूत |शश्वाम् अपो विकृतं हित्व्य आगाद अनु व्रतं सवितुर्दैवस्य ||
samāvavarti viṣṭhito jigīṣur viśveṣāṁ kāmaś caratām amābhūt | śaśvām̐ apo vikṛtaṁ hitvy āgād anu vrataṁ savitur daivyasya ||
  • samāvavarti: This term can be understood as "to come together" or "to unite." It signifies the importance of unity and collaboration in achieving common goals.
  • viṣṭhito: Meaning "established" or "settled," this word emphasizes the need for a stable foundation in any endeavor.
  • jigīṣur: This translates to "the one who desires to conquer" or "the aspirant." It reflects the human spirit's innate desire to achieve and succeed.
  • viśveṣāṁ: This word means "of all" or "universal." It indicates inclusivity and the interconnectedness of all beings.
  • kāmaś: This term refers to "desires" or "wishes." It highlights the significance of aspirations in human life.
  • caratām: Meaning "to move" or "to act," it suggests the importance of action in the pursuit of desires.
  • amābhūt: This can be interpreted as "may it not be unfulfilled" or "may it come to fruition." It expresses the hope for the realization of desires.
  • śaśvām̐: This word means "everlasting" or "eternal," indicating the timeless nature of certain truths and aspirations.
  • apo: Meaning "water," it symbolizes nourishment and the essential elements of life.
  • vikṛtaṁ: This translates to "transformed" or "changed," suggesting the dynamic nature of existence and the potential for growth.
  • hitvy: This term means "for the benefit" or "for the good," emphasizing the importance of actions that lead to positive outcomes.
  • āgād: Meaning "to go" or "to proceed," it signifies movement towards a goal or aspiration.
  • anu: This word means "following" or "after," indicating a sequential or progressive approach to achieving desires.
  • vrataṁ: This translates to "vow" or "commitment," highlighting the importance of dedication in the pursuit of goals.
  • savituh: Referring to "the divine" or "the sun," it symbolizes enlightenment and higher consciousness.
  • daivyasya: This means "of the divine" or "of the gods," indicating a connection to spiritual or higher powers.

In conclusion, this mantra encapsulates the essence of human aspiration, unity, and the pursuit of desires. It emphasizes the importance of establishing a stable foundation, taking action, and remaining committed to one's goals while being inclusive of all beings. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios such as teamwork in professional settings, community building, and personal development. By fostering collaboration and a shared vision, individuals can work together to achieve common aspirations, leading to a more harmonious and fulfilling existence.
