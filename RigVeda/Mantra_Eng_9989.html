सङ्क्रन्दनेनानिमिषेण जित्ष्णुना युत्कारेण दुष्च्यवनेन धृष्णुना |तद इन्द्रेण जयत तत सहध्वं युधो नर इषुहस्तेन वृष्णा ||
saṁkrandanenānimiṣeṇa jiṣṇunā yutkāreṇa duścyavanena dhṛṣṇunā | tad indreṇa jayata tat sahadhvaṁ yudho nara iṣuhastena vṛṣṇā ||
  • saṁkrandanenānimiṣeṇa: This term can be broken down into "saṁkrandana," which means a loud cry or shout, and "animiṣeṇa," which refers to something that is done without blinking or hesitating. This suggests a call to action that is urgent and requires immediate attention.
  • jiṣṇunā: This word translates to "with strength" or "with power." It emphasizes the importance of strength in overcoming challenges.
  • yutkāreṇa: This term means "with a united effort" or "in collaboration." It highlights the significance of teamwork and collective action in achieving goals.
  • duścyavanena: This word can be interpreted as "with difficulty" or "with obstacles." It acknowledges that challenges and adversities are a part of life.
  • dhṛṣṇunā: This translates to "with determination" or "with courage." It signifies the need for bravery and resolve in the face of difficulties.
  • tad indreṇa: "Tad" means "that," and "indreṇa" refers to "by Indra," the king of gods in Vedic literature. This suggests invoking divine support or strength in our endeavors.
  • jayata: This word means "may we conquer" or "may we triumph." It is a call for victory and success.
  • tat sahadhvaṁ: "Tat" means "that," and "sahadhvaṁ" translates to "together." This emphasizes the importance of unity in achieving success.
  • yudho: This term means "in battle" or "in conflict." It signifies the struggles we face in life.
  • nara: This translates to "man" or "human." It represents humanity and our collective experiences.
  • iṣuhastena: "Iṣu" means "arrow," and "hastena" means "by the hand." This symbolizes the tools or means we use to achieve our goals.
  • vṛṣṇā: This term refers to the "Vrishni" clan, often associated with strength and valor. It signifies the qualities we aspire to embody.

  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of collective strength, determination, and divine support in overcoming challenges. It serves as a reminder that while obstacles may arise, unity and courage can lead to victory. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as teamwork in professional settings, community efforts in social causes, or personal challenges where support from others is crucial. By invoking the spirit of collaboration and resilience, individuals and groups can navigate difficulties and achieve their goals.
