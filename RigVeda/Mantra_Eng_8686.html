सनेमि त्वम् अस्मद आमं अदेवं कं चिद अत्रिणम् |साह्वाम् इन्दो परि बाधो अप द्वयुम् ||
sanemi tvam asmad ām̐ adevaṁ kaṁ cid atriṇam | sāhvām̐ indo pari bādho apa dvayum ||
  • Sanemi: This word translates to "we approach" or "we seek." It signifies the act of seeking knowledge or divine guidance.
  • Tvam: This means "you," referring to a divine entity or a higher power that is being addressed.
  • Asmad: This translates to "us" or "our," indicating a collective identity or community.
  • Ām̐: This is an affirmation, often used to emphasize the sincerity of the request or prayer.
  • Adevaṁ: This means "divine" or "godly," referring to the sacred nature of the entity being invoked.
  • Kaṁ cid: This translates to "anyone" or "anyone at all," suggesting an openness to the divine presence.
  • Atriṇam: This can be interpreted as "to protect" or "to guide," indicating a desire for divine protection or guidance.
  • Sāhvām̐: This word means "together" or "in unity," emphasizing the importance of collective strength and harmony.
  • Indo: This refers to "Indra," the king of gods in Vedic literature, symbolizing power, strength, and leadership.
  • Pari bādho: This translates to "may you remove" or "may you protect us from," indicating a plea for protection from obstacles or negativity.
  • Apa dvayum: This means "the two" or "the duality," which can refer to the dual nature of existence, such as good and evil, or light and darkness.

The mantra essentially expresses a collective plea for divine guidance and protection. It emphasizes the importance of seeking unity and strength in the face of challenges. The invocation of Indra signifies a call for leadership and strength to overcome obstacles.


In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as:

  • Community Gatherings: When a community comes together for a common purpose, invoking this mantra can foster unity and collective strength.
  • Personal Challenges: Individuals facing difficulties can recite this mantra to seek guidance and protection from higher powers.
  • Leadership Situations: Leaders can use this mantra to inspire their teams, emphasizing the importance of unity and strength in overcoming challenges.

In conclusion, this mantra serves as a reminder of the power of collective seeking and the importance of divine guidance in navigating life's challenges. It encourages individuals and communities to come together in unity, seeking strength and protection from higher powers.
