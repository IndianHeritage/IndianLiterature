प्र नु यद एषां महिना चिकीर्ते प्र युञ्जते प्रयुजस ते सुवृक्ति |अध यद एषां सुदिने न शरुर विश्वं एरीणं पुरुषायन्त सेनाः ||
pra nu yad eṣām mahinā cikitre pra yuñjate prayujas te suvṛkti | adha yad eṣāṁ sudine na śarur viśvam eriṇam pruṣāyanta senāḥ ||
  • Translation: "Let us invoke the strength of those who are powerful; may they bestow upon us their auspiciousness. When the forces of the world unite, may they protect us from harm and lead us to victory."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • pra nu: An invocation or call to action.
    • yad: That which.
    • eṣām: Of those.
    • mahinā: With strength or power.
    • cikitre: To create or manifest.
    • pra yuñjate: May they bestow or grant.
    • prayujas: To use or apply.
    • te: You or those.
    • suvṛkti: Auspiciousness or good fortune.
    • adha: Below or in the face of.
    • yad: That which.
    • eṣāṁ: Of these.
    • sudine: In good times or auspicious moments.
    • na: Not.
    • śarur: Harm or injury.
    • viśvam: The universe or all.
    • eriṇam: Enemies or adversaries.
    • pruṣāyanta: To protect or guard.
    • senāḥ: Armies or forces.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, a team may invoke the collective strength of its members to achieve a common goal, ensuring that everyone contributes their unique skills for success.
    • During a sports event, a coach may rally the team to unite their strengths, emphasizing teamwork to overcome the opposing team and achieve victory.
    • In community service, individuals may come together, invoking their collective power to address social issues, ensuring that their efforts lead to positive change.
  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of unity and collective strength in overcoming challenges and achieving success. It serves as a reminder that when individuals come together with a common purpose, they can create a powerful force that protects against adversity and leads to auspicious outcomes. This principle is applicable in various aspects of life, including personal relationships, professional environments, and community initiatives, highlighting the significance of collaboration and mutual support in achieving shared goals.
