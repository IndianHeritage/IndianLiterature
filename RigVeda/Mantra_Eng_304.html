आ नो भज परमेष्वा वाजेषु मध्यमेषु |शिक्षा वस्वो अन्तमस्य ||
ā no bhaja parameṣv ā vājeṣu madhyameṣu | śikṣā vasvo antamasya ||
  • Mantra: "ā no bhaja parameṣv ā vājeṣu madhyameṣu | śikṣā vasvo antamasya ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā: towards us
    • no: our
    • bhaja: may you serve or worship
    • parameṣv: in the highest or supreme
    • ā: towards
    • vājeṣu: in the midst of the powerful or strong
    • madhyameṣu: in the middle or among
    • śikṣā: instruction or teaching
    • vasvo: of the wealth or prosperity
    • antamasya: of the darkness or ignorance
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra calls for divine guidance and support in our endeavors.
    • It emphasizes the importance of seeking knowledge and instruction to overcome ignorance.
    • The phrase "in the midst of the powerful" suggests that we should strive to be among those who are strong and capable, learning from them.
    • It highlights the need for collaboration and community in achieving our goals.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace setting, this mantra can be applied by encouraging teamwork and collaboration among colleagues to achieve common goals.
    • In education, students can benefit from seeking guidance from teachers and mentors, thereby overcoming challenges and ignorance.
    • In personal development, individuals can strive to surround themselves with positive influences and learn from those who have achieved success.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of seeking divine support and wisdom in our lives.
    • It teaches us that knowledge and collaboration are essential in overcoming obstacles and achieving success.
    • By applying the principles of this mantra, individuals can foster a sense of community, enhance their learning, and ultimately lead a more fulfilling life.
