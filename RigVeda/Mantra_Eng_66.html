स नो वृषन्न अमुं चरुं सत्रादावन्न अपा वृधि |अस्मभ्यम अप्रतिष्कुतः ||
sa no vṛṣann amuṁ caruṁ satrādāvann apā vṛdhi | asmabhyam apratiṣkutaḥ ||
  • sa no vṛṣann: This phrase can be translated as "may he (the divine) protect us." The term "vṛṣan" refers to a powerful or strong entity, often associated with deities or divine forces. In real life, this can be understood as a call for protection and guidance in our endeavors.
  • amuṁ caruṁ: This translates to "this offering" or "this sacrifice." The word "caru" refers to a type of food or offering made during rituals. In a practical sense, it signifies the importance of giving and sharing, whether it be in the form of resources, time, or kindness towards others.
  • satrādāvann: This can be interpreted as "in the beginning of the sacrifice." The term "satra" refers to a ritual or sacrifice that is performed for a specific purpose. This highlights the significance of starting any endeavor with intention and purpose, much like how we begin important projects or relationships in our lives.
  • apā vṛdhi: This phrase means "may we not be deprived of growth." "Vṛdhi" signifies growth, prosperity, or increase. In everyday life, this can relate to personal development, career advancement, or the flourishing of relationships. It emphasizes the need for continuous growth and improvement.
  • asmabhyam apratiṣkutaḥ: This translates to "may we not be obstructed." The term "apratiṣkutaḥ" suggests the removal of obstacles. In real life, this can be seen as a prayer or wish for the removal of challenges that hinder our progress, whether they are personal, professional, or spiritual.

Conclusion: The mantra "sa no vṛṣann amuṁ caruṁ satrādāvann apā vṛdhi | asmabhyam apratiṣkutaḥ ||" encapsulates a profound message of seeking divine protection, the importance of offerings, the significance of purposeful beginnings, the desire for growth, and the removal of obstacles. In our daily lives, we can apply this mantra by being mindful of our intentions, engaging in acts of kindness, striving for personal and professional growth, and seeking support in overcoming challenges. It serves as a reminder that with the right mindset and support, we can navigate through life's complexities and achieve our goals.
