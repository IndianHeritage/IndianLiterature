उत तिष्ठ नूनम एषां स्तोमैः समुक्षितानाम् |मरुताम् पुरुतमम् अपूर्व्यं गवां सर्गम् इव ह्वये ||
ut tiṣṭha nūnam eṣāṁ stomaiḥ samukṣitānām | marutām purutamam apūrvyaṁ gavāṁ sargam iva hvaye ||
  • ut tiṣṭha nūnam eṣāṁ stomaiḥ samukṣitānām: This phrase can be translated as "Arise, indeed, from these hymns that are gathered together." Here, "ut tiṣṭha" means "arise" or "get up," indicating a call to action or awakening. "nūnam" emphasizes certainty, suggesting that this is a definite call. "eṣāṁ stomaiḥ" refers to the hymns or praises that are being invoked, and "samukṣitānām" means "gathered together," indicating a collective effort or unity in purpose.
  • marutām purutamam apūrvyaṁ gavāṁ sargam iva hvaye: This part translates to "I invoke the most excellent Maruts, the unique creation of the cows." "Marutām" refers to the Maruts, the storm deities associated with wind and storms in Vedic literature. "purutamam" means "the most excellent" or "the best," highlighting their supreme nature. "apūrvyaṁ" signifies something unique or unprecedented, while "gavāṁ sargam" refers to the creation or manifestation of cows, which are considered sacred in Vedic culture. "iva hvaye" means "I invoke," indicating a prayer or supplication.

  • Significance: This mantra emphasizes the importance of collective action and the invocation of divine forces for achieving goals. The Maruts symbolize strength, vitality, and the power of nature, which can be harnessed for positive outcomes. The reference to cows highlights the significance of agriculture and sustenance in Vedic society, where cows were seen as a source of wealth and nourishment.
  • Real-life Application: In contemporary life, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as community gatherings, team projects, or any collective endeavor where unity and strength are required. For instance, in a corporate setting, a team leader might invoke the spirit of collaboration and collective effort to motivate the team towards achieving a common goal. Similarly, in social movements, the call for unity and collective action can lead to significant change.

  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a reminder of the power of unity and the importance of invoking higher energies to support our endeavors. It encourages individuals and communities to rise together, harnessing their collective strengths to achieve greatness. By recognizing the divine forces at play and aligning with them, we can navigate challenges and create a more harmonious existence.
