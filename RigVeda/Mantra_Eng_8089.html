आयā वीतī परि श्रव यस् त इन्दो मदेष्व ā |अवाहन नवतीर नव ||
ayā vītī pari srava yas ta indo madeṣv ā | avāhan navatīr nava ||
  • ayā vītī: This phrase can be interpreted as "may the path be clear" or "may the way be unobstructed." It signifies the desire for clarity and guidance in one's journey through life.
  • pari srava: This means "to flow around" or "to surround." It suggests the idea of being enveloped by positive influences or energies, akin to being in a supportive environment.
  • yas ta indo: Translated as "that which is pleasing to the mind," this phrase refers to the pursuit of knowledge and wisdom that brings joy and satisfaction. It emphasizes the importance of mental clarity and enlightenment.
  • madeṣv ā: This can be understood as "in the intoxication of joy" or "in the bliss of happiness." It highlights the significance of experiencing joy and fulfillment in life.
  • avāhan: This word means "to invoke" or "to call upon." It signifies the act of inviting positive energies or divine blessings into one's life.
  • navatīr nava: This phrase translates to "newness" or "freshness." It represents the idea of renewal and the continuous cycle of life, encouraging individuals to embrace change and new beginnings.

Conclusion: The mantra "ayā vītī pari srava yas ta indo madeṣv ā | avāhan navatīr nava ||" encapsulates the essence of seeking clarity, joy, and renewal in life. It serves as a reminder to invoke positive energies and embrace new experiences. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied during significant life transitions, such as starting a new job, moving to a new place, or embarking on a personal journey of self-discovery. By focusing on clarity and joy, individuals can navigate challenges with a positive mindset, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling and enriched life.
