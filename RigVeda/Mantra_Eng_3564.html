यस्तस्तम्भ सहसा वि ज्मो अन्तान बृहस्पतिस त्रिषधस्थो रवेण |तम प्रात्नास ऋषयो दिद्यानाः पुरो विप्राः दधिरे मन्द्रजिह्वम् ||
yas tastambha sahasā vi jmo antān bṛhaspatis triṣadhastho raveṇa | tam pratnāsa ṛṣayo dīdhyānāḥ puro viprā dadhire mandrajihvam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yas: who
    • tastambha: has established
    • sahasā: with great force
    • vi: in the midst of
    • jmo: the one who knows
    • antān: the ends
    • bṛhaspatis: of the great sage or deity
    • triṣadhastho: who is in three places
    • raveṇa: with the sun
    • tam: that
    • pratnāsa: the one who is invoked
    • ṛṣayo: the sages
    • dīdhyānāḥ: who are meditating
    • puro: in the front
    • viprā: the learned ones
    • dadhire: have held
    • mandrajihvam: with a gentle tongue
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of a powerful entity or force that has established itself with great strength and knowledge.
    • The reference to "bṛhaspatis" suggests a connection to wisdom and guidance, often associated with the planet Jupiter in Vedic astrology.
    • The phrase "triṣadhastho raveṇa" indicates a presence that is connected to the sun, symbolizing illumination, energy, and life.
    • The invocation of sages ("ṛṣayo") highlights the importance of wisdom and meditation in understanding and connecting with this force.
    • The "gentle tongue" signifies the importance of communication and expression in sharing knowledge and wisdom.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, a leader who embodies the qualities of strength, wisdom, and effective communication can inspire and guide their team towards success.
    • In personal relationships, individuals who practice active listening and gentle communication can foster deeper connections and understanding.
    • In education, teachers who invoke wisdom and encourage meditation or reflection can help students grasp complex concepts more effectively.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of strength, wisdom, and effective communication in various aspects of life.
    • It serves as a reminder to seek knowledge and understanding while also being mindful of how we express ourselves.
    • In real-life scenarios, applying these principles can lead to personal growth, improved relationships, and successful endeavors.
