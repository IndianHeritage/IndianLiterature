प्र वो रयिं युक्ताश्वं भरध्वं राया एषेऽवसे दधीत धिः |सुशेवा एवैर औशिजस्य होताः ये वा एवामरुतस्तुराणाम् ||
pra vo rayiṁ yuktāśvam bharadhvaṁ rāya eṣe 'vase dadhīta dhīḥ | suśeva evair auśijasya hotā ye va evā marutas turāṇām ||
  • pra vo rayiṁ yuktāśvam bharadhvaṁ: This phrase can be translated as "May you bring forth wealth, harnessing the power of the horses." Here, "rayiṁ" refers to wealth or prosperity, while "yuktāśvam" signifies the idea of utilizing horses, which are often symbolic of strength and speed in Vedic literature. In real life, this can be likened to leveraging resources effectively to achieve success, much like a business utilizing its assets to maximize profits.
  • rāya eṣe 'vase dadhīta dhīḥ: This part translates to "May the wealth that is here be under your control." The term "rāya" refers to wealth or riches, and "vase dadhīta" implies the idea of controlling or possessing that wealth. In a modern context, this can be seen in the way individuals or organizations manage their finances and investments to ensure stability and growth.
  • suśeva evair auśijasya hotā: This translates to "The one who is a good priest of the well-being." "Suśeva" means good or beneficial, while "auśijasya" refers to well-being or health. This can be related to the role of leaders or mentors in our lives who guide us towards making positive choices for our health and prosperity, similar to how a good manager leads a team towards success.
  • ye va evā marutas turāṇām: This phrase means "Those who are the swift ones among the Maruts." The Maruts are storm deities associated with strength and speed. This can be interpreted as the importance of agility and quick decision-making in today's fast-paced world, where being able to adapt and respond swiftly can lead to success in various endeavors.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of harnessing resources wisely, controlling wealth, promoting well-being, and being agile in decision-making. In real life, these principles can be applied in various scenarios, such as in business, personal finance, health management, and leadership. By embodying these values, individuals and organizations can strive for prosperity and success, ensuring that they not only achieve their goals but also contribute positively to the well-being of their communities.
