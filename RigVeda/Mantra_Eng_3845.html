नव यद अस्य नवतिं च भोगान साकं वज्रेण मगवां विवृश्चत् |आर्चन्तीन्द्रं मरुतः सधस्थे त्रैषṭुभेन वचसा बाधत द्यां ||
nava yad asya navatiṁ ca bhogān sākaṁ vajreṇa maghavā vivṛścat | arcantīndram marutaḥ sadhasthe traiṣṭubhena vacasā bādhata dyām ||
  • Translation: "Whatever is new and whatever is the source of enjoyment, may the powerful Maghavan (Indra) bestow it upon us with his thunderbolt. The Maruts, who are the storm deities, worship Indra, and with their powerful words, they strengthen the heavens."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • nava: new, fresh, or novel.
    • yad: whatever.
    • asya: of this.
    • navatiṁ: source of enjoyment or pleasure.
    • bhogān: enjoyments or pleasures.
    • sākaṁ: together with.
    • vajreṇa: with the thunderbolt (symbolizing power and strength).
    • maghavā: Maghavan, another name for Indra, the king of gods.
    • vivṛścat: may he bestow or grant.
    • arcantīndram: worshipping Indra.
    • marutaḥ: the Maruts, storm deities.
    • sadhasthe: who are present or established.
    • traiṣṭubhena: with the powerful words.
    • vacasā: with speech or words.
    • bādhata: may they strengthen.
    • dyām: the heavens or sky.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of new beginnings and the pursuit of enjoyment in life. For instance, when starting a new job, one may seek the blessings of Indra for success and fulfillment.
    • The reference to the Maruts worshipping Indra can be likened to teamwork in a corporate environment, where individuals come together to support a leader or a common goal, enhancing the overall strength of the group.
    • The metaphor of the thunderbolt symbolizes the power of determination and action. In personal development, one might harness this energy to overcome obstacles and achieve their aspirations.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its invocation of divine support for new ventures and the pursuit of joy. It teaches us the value of collaboration and the strength found in unity. In real-life scenarios, whether in personal growth, professional endeavors, or community efforts, this mantra serves as a reminder to seek blessings for success and to work together harmoniously to achieve common goals. By embracing new opportunities and supporting one another, we can create a fulfilling and prosperous life.
