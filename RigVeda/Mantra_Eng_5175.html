मन्त्र: सुसन्दृक ते स्वनीक प्रतीकं वियद् रुक्मो न रोचसा उपाके |दिवो न ते तान्यतुर एति शुष्मश् चित्रो न सूरः प्रति चक्षि भानुम् ||
susaṁdṛk te svanīka pratīkaṁ vi yad rukmo na rocasa upāke | divo na te tanyatur eti śuṣmaś citro na sūraḥ prati cakṣi bhānum ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • susaṁdṛk: Well-formed or well-structured
    • te: Your
    • svanīka: Sound or voice
    • pratīkaṁ: Symbol or representation
    • vi: Indeed
    • yad: That which
    • rukmo: Shining or radiant
    • na: Not
    • rocasa: Brightness or light
    • upāke: In the context of
    • divo: Of the heavens
    • na: Not
    • te: Your
    • tanyatur: Stretching or extending
    • eti: Goes or moves
    • śuṣmaś: Subtle or fine
    • citro: Colorful or diverse
    • na: Not
    • sūraḥ: Brave or heroic
    • prati: Towards
    • cakṣi: Eye or vision
    • bhānum: Sun or light
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the essence of clarity and brilliance in communication and representation.
    • The phrase "susaṁdṛk te svanīka" emphasizes the importance of having a well-structured voice or message that resonates with others.
    • It suggests that true brilliance ("rukmo") is not merely about outward appearances ("rocasa") but about the depth and substance of what is being communicated.
    • The reference to the heavens ("divo") indicates a higher purpose or divine connection in our expressions.
    • The mantra also highlights the need for a clear vision ("cakṣi") to perceive the true light ("bhānum") in our endeavors.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In public speaking, a well-structured message can captivate an audience, much like how a radiant sun captures attention.
    • In personal relationships, clear and honest communication fosters deeper connections, reflecting the essence of "susaṁdṛk."
    • In leadership, a leader's vision can inspire others, akin to how the sun illuminates the path forward.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the significance of clarity, substance, and purpose in our expressions and communications.
    • It encourages individuals to strive for a deeper understanding and representation of their thoughts and ideas, ensuring they resonate with others.
    • In various aspects of life, from personal relationships to professional endeavors, embodying the principles of this mantra can lead to more meaningful interactions and outcomes.
