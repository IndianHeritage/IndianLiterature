निवेवेति पलितो दूत आस्व अन्तर महाम्श्चरति रोचनैना |वपुंषि बिभ्रद अभि नो विचष्टे महद देवानाम असुरत्वम् एकम् ||
ni veveti palito dūta āsv antar mahām̐ś carati rocanena | vapūṁṣi bibhrad abhi no vi caṣṭe mahad devānām asuratvam ekam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ni veveti: does not move away
    • palito: protected or guarded
    • dūta: messenger or envoy
    • āsv: within or inside
    • antar: between or among
    • mahām̐ś: great or mighty
    • carati: moves or travels
    • rocanena: with brilliance or light
    • vapūṁṣi: bodies or forms
    • bibhrad: holding or bearing
    • abhi: towards or in the direction of
    • no: our
    • vi caṣṭe: does not waver or shake
    • mahad: great or vast
    • devānām: of the gods
    • asuratvam: the quality of being powerful or divine
    • ekam: one or singular
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of a divine messenger who remains steadfast and does not waver, embodying the qualities of protection and brilliance.
    • The "greatness" mentioned refers to the divine qualities that are inherent in the universe, suggesting a unity among all powerful beings.
    • In real life, this can be likened to a leader or a guiding figure who remains resolute in their purpose, providing direction and support to those they lead.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • A teacher who inspires students by being a constant source of knowledge and support, guiding them through challenges without faltering.
    • A mentor in a professional setting who provides unwavering support and guidance, helping their mentees navigate their careers with confidence.
    • In a community, a leader who stands firm in their values and beliefs, rallying people together for a common cause, embodying the qualities of strength and unity.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of steadfastness, protection, and the embodiment of divine qualities in our lives.
    • It serves as a reminder that in times of uncertainty, we can look to those who exhibit these qualities for guidance and support.
    • In a broader sense, it encourages individuals to cultivate these qualities within themselves, becoming a source of strength and inspiration for others.
