आ चिकीतान सुक्रतू देवौ मर्त ऋशादसाः |वरुणाय ऋतपेक्षसे दधीत प्रयसे महे ||
ā cikitāna sukratū devau marta riśādasā | varuṇāya ṛtapeśase dadhīta prayase mahe ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā cikitāna: "O wise one" or "O knowledgeable one"
    • sukratū: "with good deeds" or "one who performs righteous actions"
    • devau: "gods" or "divine beings"
    • marta: "mortal" or "human beings"
    • riśādasā: "who are in distress" or "who are suffering"
    • varuṇāya: "to Varuna" (the god of cosmic order and water)
    • ṛtapeśase: "who embodies truth and order"
    • dadhīta: "may you grant" or "may you bestow"
    • prayase: "with effort" or "with diligence"
    • mahe: "great" or "mighty"
  • Overall Meaning:
    • This mantra is a prayer directed towards the divine, specifically to Varuna, who represents the principles of truth and cosmic order.
    • The speaker acknowledges the wisdom of the divine and seeks assistance for mortals who are suffering or in distress.
    • It emphasizes the importance of righteous actions and the need for divine support in overcoming challenges.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In times of personal crisis, individuals often turn to prayer or meditation, seeking guidance and support from a higher power, similar to the invocation of Varuna in this mantra.
    • Community service and charitable actions can be seen as modern-day expressions of "sukratū," where individuals perform good deeds to alleviate the suffering of others.
    • In a corporate setting, leaders who embody truth and integrity can be likened to Varuna, as they create an environment of trust and order, benefiting all employees.
  • Conclusion and Significance:
    • This mantra highlights the interconnectedness of divine wisdom and human action, reminding us that seeking help from a higher power can be essential in overcoming life's challenges.
    • It serves as a reminder to engage in righteous actions and to support those who are suffering, fostering a sense of community and compassion.
    • In a broader context, the mantra encourages individuals to strive for truth and order in their lives, which can lead to personal growth and societal harmony.
