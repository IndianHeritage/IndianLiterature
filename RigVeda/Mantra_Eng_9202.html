युवं रथेन विमदाय शुन्ध्युवं न्यूहतुः पुरुमित्रस्य योषणाम् |युवं हवं वध्रिमत्यागच्छतं युवं सुषुतिं चक्रतुः पुरंढये ||
yuvaṁ rathena vimadāya śundhyuvaṁ ny ūhathuḥ purumitrasya yoṣaṇām | yuvaṁ havaṁ vadhrimatyā agacchataṁ yuvaṁ suṣutiṁ cakrathuḥ puraṁdhaye ||
  • Mantra: "yuvaṁ rathena vimadāya śundhyuvaṁ ny ūhathuḥ purumitrasya yoṣaṇām | yuvaṁ havaṁ vadhrimatyā agacchataṁ yuvaṁ suṣutiṁ cakrathuḥ puraṁdhaye ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yuvaṁ: young, youthful
    • rathena: by the chariot
    • vimadāya: with wisdom or intelligence
    • śundhyuvaṁ: may they be united
    • ny ūhathuḥ: may they be guided
    • purumitrasya: of the good friend
    • yoṣaṇām: of the women
    • havaṁ: invocation or offering
    • vadhrimatyā: with strength or power
    • agacchataṁ: may they go
    • suṣutiṁ: good thoughts or intentions
    • cakrathuḥ: may they create
    • puraṁdhaye: for the one who is a protector or guardian
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of youth and vitality, symbolized by "yuvaṁ," which represents the energy and potential of young individuals.
    • The mention of "rathena" (by the chariot) signifies the journey of life, where wisdom ("vimadāya") plays a crucial role in guiding one's path.
    • The phrase "purumitrasya yoṣaṇām" highlights the significance of relationships, particularly the bond between friends and the support of women in society.
    • It calls for unity and collaboration ("śundhyuvaṁ ny ūhathuḥ"), suggesting that collective efforts lead to greater achievements.
    • The invocation of strength ("vadhrimatyā") and good intentions ("suṣutiṁ") indicates that success is not just about power but also about having a positive mindset and ethical values.
    • Finally, "puraṁdhaye" refers to the protector, suggesting that the outcomes of these efforts should benefit those who safeguard and nurture the community.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, a young team working together on a project can embody the essence of this mantra. Their collective wisdom and support can lead to innovative solutions.
    • In community service, youth-led initiatives that focus on helping others reflect the mantra's call for unity and strength, showcasing how young individuals can make a significant impact.
    • In personal relationships, the mantra encourages individuals to support one another, especially in challenging times, fostering a sense of community and friendship.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the power of youth, wisdom, and collaboration in achieving success and fostering positive relationships.
    • Its significance lies in its applicability across various aspects of life, from personal relationships to professional endeavors, emphasizing that collective efforts and good intentions lead to a harmonious and prosperous society.
    • By embodying the principles outlined in this mantra, individuals can contribute to a supportive and thriving community, ensuring that the values of friendship, strength, and wisdom are upheld.
