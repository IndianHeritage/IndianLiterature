सम्मिश्लो अरुषो भव सूपस्थाभिर न धेनुभिः |सीदञ छ्येनो न योनिम आ ||
sammiślo aruṣo bhava sūpasthābhir na dhenubhiḥ | sīdañ chyeno na yonim ā ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sammiślo: One who is united or harmonious.
    • aruṣo: The one who is red or bright, often associated with the sun.
    • bhava: Become or manifest.
    • sūpasthābhir: With auspicious or favorable conditions.
    • na: Not.
    • dhenubhiḥ: By the cows or nurturing entities.
    • sīdañ: Sit or reside.
    • chyeno: Without any obstruction or hindrance.
    • na: Not.
    • yonim: The womb or source.
    • ā: To or towards.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of a harmonious and auspicious state of being.
    • The imagery of "aruṣo" suggests brightness and positivity, akin to the rising sun that brings light and warmth.
    • The phrase "sūpasthābhir" indicates that this state is achieved under favorable conditions, emphasizing the importance of a supportive environment.
    • The mention of "dhenubhiḥ" can be interpreted as nurturing forces, akin to the nurturing qualities of a mother or nature that provide sustenance and support.
    • The latter part of the mantra, "sīdañ chyeno na yonim ā," suggests a desire to reside in a state free from obstacles, moving towards a source of creation or potential.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace, fostering a harmonious environment can lead to increased productivity and creativity. Just as the mantra suggests auspicious conditions, a supportive team can help individuals thrive.
    • In personal relationships, nurturing and supportive dynamics can lead to deeper connections, much like the nurturing qualities of "dhenubhiḥ" that promote growth and understanding.
    • In education, creating a positive and encouraging atmosphere allows students to flourish, reflecting the essence of "sūpasthābhir" where favorable conditions lead to success.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of harmony, positivity, and nurturing environments in achieving personal and collective growth.
    • Its application in real life can be seen in various domains such as work, relationships, and education, where supportive conditions lead to success and fulfillment.
    • Ultimately, it serves as a reminder to cultivate positivity and support in our surroundings to foster growth and creativity.
