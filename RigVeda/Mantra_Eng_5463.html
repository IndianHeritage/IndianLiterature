अनु तद उर्वी रोदसी जिहाताम् अनु द्युक्षो वरुण इन्द्रसखा |अनु विश्वे मरुतो ये सहासो रायः स्याम धरणं धियधै ||
anu tad urvī rodasī jihātām anu dyukṣo varuṇa indrasakhā | anu viśve maruto ye sahāso rāyaḥ syāma dharuṇaṁ dhiyadhyai ||
  • anu tad urvī rodasī jihātām: This phrase can be translated to "May that earth and sky be favorable to us." Here, "anu" means "may," "tad" refers to "that," "urvī" means "earth," "rodasī" refers to "sky," and "jihātām" means "be favorable." This line emphasizes the importance of harmony between the earth and the sky, which can be seen in our daily lives as the balance between nature and human activities.
  • anu dyukṣo varuṇa indrasakhā: This translates to "May Varuna, the god of cosmic order, and Indra, the god of rain and thunder, be our allies." "Dyukṣo" refers to "of the heavens," "Varuṇa" is the deity representing the cosmic order, and "indrasakhā" means "friend of Indra." This line highlights the significance of divine support in our endeavors, akin to seeking guidance from mentors or leaders in our personal and professional lives.
  • anu viśve maruto ye sahāso: This means "May all the Maruts, the storm deities, who are powerful, be with us." "Viśve" means "all," "maruto" refers to the Maruts, and "sahāso" means "powerful." This line signifies the need for strength and support from various forces in our lives, similar to how teamwork and collaboration can lead to success in projects or community efforts.
  • rāyaḥ syāma dharuṇaṁ dhiyadhyai: This translates to "May we attain wealth and prosperity through our intellect." "Rāyaḥ" means "wealth," "syāma" means "may we attain," "dharuṇaṁ" refers to "through strength," and "dhiyadhyai" means "through intellect." This line emphasizes the importance of wisdom and intelligence in achieving success, reflecting the idea that knowledge and strategic thinking are crucial in real-life scenarios, such as in business or personal growth.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a holistic approach to life, emphasizing the interconnectedness of nature, divine support, collective strength, and the importance of intellect in achieving prosperity. In real-life scenarios, it serves as a reminder to seek balance in our environment, to value mentorship and collaboration, and to prioritize knowledge and wisdom in our pursuits. This mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as in personal development, community building, and professional endeavors, guiding individuals towards a harmonious and prosperous life.
