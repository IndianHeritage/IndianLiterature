प्र ते अग्नि हविष्मतीं इयर्म्य अच् छा सुद्युम्नां रातिनीं घृताचीं | प्रदक्षिणिद देवतातिम उराणः सं रातिभिर वसुभिर यज्ञं अश्रेत ||
pra te agne haviṣmatīm iyarmy acchā sudyumnāṁ rātinīṁ ghṛtācīm | pradakṣiṇid devatātim urāṇaḥ saṁ rātibhir vasubhir yajñam aśret ||
  • pra te agne haviṣmatīm: This phrase can be translated as "to you, O Agni, who is worthy of offerings." Agni, the fire god, represents the divine energy that transforms offerings into spiritual nourishment. In real life, this can be likened to the way we channel our efforts and intentions into meaningful actions, much like how fire transforms raw materials into something valuable.
  • iyarmy acchā sudyumnāṁ rātinīṁ ghṛtācīm: This part means "may we obtain the excellent and prosperous wealth that is pleasing and nourishing." Here, "sudyumnāṁ" refers to prosperity and "ghṛtācīm" signifies nourishment. In everyday life, this can be seen in our pursuit of knowledge, relationships, and resources that enrich our lives and contribute to our well-being.
  • pradakṣiṇid devatātim urāṇaḥ: This translates to "the divine being who is surrounded by the offerings." The term "pradakṣiṇid" refers to the act of circumambulation, which is a common practice in many cultures as a form of respect and devotion. In a real-life context, this can be related to the respect we show towards our traditions, values, and the people we honor in our lives.
  • saṁ rātibhir vasubhir yajñam aśret: This means "may we perform the sacrifice with the help of the divine beings." The word "yajñam" refers to sacrifice or offering, which is a central theme in many spiritual practices. In practical terms, this can be understood as the importance of community and collaboration in achieving common goals, whether in family, work, or society.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of devotion, prosperity, and community in our lives. It teaches us to channel our energies towards meaningful pursuits, seek nourishment in our endeavors, and honor the divine and the community around us. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various ways, such as through acts of kindness, community service, and the pursuit of knowledge and personal growth. By embodying these principles, we can create a more harmonious and fulfilling existence, much like the offerings made to Agni that transform into blessings for all.
