देवानां दूतः पुरुधा प्रसूतो 'नागान नो वोकतु सर्वताता |श्रिणोतु नः पृथिवी द्यौर उतापः सूर्यो नक्षत्रैर उर्व अन्तरिक्षम् ||
devānāṁ dūtaḥ purudha prasūto 'nāgān no vocatu sarvatātā | śṛṇotu naḥ pṛthivī dyaur utāpaḥ sūryo nakṣatrair urv a1ntarikṣam ||
  • Mantra: "devānāṁ dūtaḥ purudha prasūto 'nāgān no vocatu sarvatātā | śṛṇotu naḥ pṛthivī dyaur utāpaḥ sūryo nakṣatrair urv a1ntarikṣam ||"
  • Translation: "May the messenger of the gods, born of the divine, speak to us without hindrance. May the Earth, the Sky, the Sun, and the Stars listen to us from all directions."
  • Word-by-Word Explanation:
    • devānāṁ: of the gods
    • dūtaḥ: messenger
    • purudha: born of the divine
    • prasūto: produced or generated
    • nāgān: without hindrance
    • no: to us
    • vocatu: may speak
    • sarvatātā: from all directions
    • śṛṇotu: may listen
    • naḥ: to us
    • pṛthivī: Earth
    • dyaur: Sky
    • utāpaḥ: and the heavens
    • sūryo: Sun
    • nakṣatrair: with the stars
    • urv: vast
    • antarikṣam: space or atmosphere
  • Significance:
    • This mantra emphasizes the connection between humans and the divine, highlighting the importance of communication with higher powers.
    • It reflects the belief that the universe is listening to our thoughts and prayers, urging us to be mindful of our intentions.
    • In real life, this can be seen in practices such as meditation, prayer, or rituals where individuals seek guidance and support from the universe or divine entities.
  • Real-Life Application:
    • In moments of decision-making, individuals often seek divine guidance, whether through prayer or reflection, embodying the essence of this mantra.
    • In community gatherings or spiritual ceremonies, invoking the presence of the divine and the elements of nature can foster a sense of unity and purpose.
    • In personal development, the mantra serves as a reminder to align one's actions with higher values and to remain open to the wisdom of the universe.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking divine communication and the interconnectedness of all beings.
    • It encourages individuals to be aware of their surroundings and the energies they invoke, promoting a harmonious relationship with the universe.
    • Ultimately, it serves as a reminder that we are never alone; the divine and the cosmos are always listening and guiding us.
