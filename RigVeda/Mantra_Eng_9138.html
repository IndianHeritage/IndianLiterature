न मां मिमेथा न जिहीळा एषा शिवा सकिभ्य उता मह्यम आसीत |अक्षस्याहं एकपरस्य हेतोर अनुव्रताम् अप जायााम् अरोधम् ||
na mā mimetha na jihīḻa eṣā śivā sakhibhya uta mahyam āsīt | akṣasyāham ekaparasya hetor anuvratām apa jāyām arodham ||
  • na mā mimetha: This phrase can be interpreted as "do not deceive me" or "do not mislead me." It emphasizes the importance of honesty and integrity in relationships.
  • na jihīḻa: This means "do not harm" or "do not cause injury." It reflects the value of compassion and non-violence towards others.
  • eṣā śivā: Translated as "this is auspicious" or "this is good." It signifies the presence of positive energy and blessings in one's life.
  • sakhibhya uta mahyam āsīt: This can be understood as "may it be for my friends and for me." It highlights the importance of community and shared well-being.
  • akṣasyāham: This translates to "I am imperishable" or "I am eternal." It speaks to the idea of the soul's immortality and the enduring nature of one's true self.
  • ekaparasya hetor: This means "of the one cause" or "of the singular origin." It suggests a unity in existence and the interconnectedness of all beings.
  • anuvratām: This can be interpreted as "following the path" or "adhering to the principles." It emphasizes the importance of commitment to one's values and duties.
  • apa jāyām arodham: This translates to "do not let me fall" or "do not let me be hindered." It expresses a desire for protection and support in one's endeavors.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates profound teachings on integrity, compassion, community, and the eternal nature of the soul. In real-life scenarios, these principles can be applied in various contexts, such as fostering honest relationships, promoting non-violence in interactions, and supporting one another in a community. By adhering to these values, individuals can create a harmonious environment that nurtures personal growth and collective well-being. The mantra serves as a reminder to seek auspiciousness in our actions and to remain steadfast in our commitments, ultimately leading to a fulfilling and meaningful life.
