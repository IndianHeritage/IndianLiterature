किम इच्छन्ती सरमा प्रेदम् आनड दूरे ह्य अध्वा जगुरिः पराचैः |कास्मेहितिः का परितकम्यासीद कथं रसायाऽतरः पयांसि ||
kim icchantī saramā predam ānaḍ dūre hy adhvā jaguriḥ parācaiḥ | kāsmehitiḥ kā paritakmyāsīt kathaṁ rasāyā ataraḥ payāṁsi ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • kim icchantī: What do they desire?
    • saramā: Saramā refers to a female dog, often symbolizing loyalty and guidance.
    • predam: The dead or the departed.
    • ānaḍ: To bring forth or to lead.
    • dūre: Far away.
    • hy: Indeed.
    • adhvā: The path or journey.
    • jaguriḥ: To awaken or to be aware.
    • parācaiḥ: With the help of others or through external means.
    • kāsmehitiḥ: What is the purpose or intention?
    • kā: What?
    • paritakmyāsīt: To be surrounded or enveloped.
    • kathaṁ: How?
    • rasāyā: Of the essence or the taste.
    • ataraḥ: Beyond or transcending.
    • payāṁsi: Waters or fluids.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra poses a series of questions that reflect on desires, intentions, and the journey of life.
    • The mention of "saramā" symbolizes the loyalty and guidance we seek in our lives, akin to how a loyal dog guides its owner.
    • The reference to the "dead" suggests a contemplation of life and death, urging us to consider what we truly desire in our existence.
    • The phrase "dūre hy adhvā" emphasizes the distance we often feel from our goals or desires, highlighting the challenges in our journey.
    • Asking "kāsmehitiḥ" and "kā" reflects the need for clarity in our intentions and the purpose behind our actions.
    • The concluding part about "rasāyā ataraḥ payāṁsi" suggests that there are deeper essences and experiences that lie beyond the surface, much like the waters that nourish life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, one might ask themselves "What do I truly desire in my career?" This reflection can lead to a more fulfilling path.
    • In relationships, the loyalty symbolized by "saramā" can remind us of the importance of trust and guidance in our connections with others.
    • When facing challenges, the mantra encourages us to seek clarity in our intentions and to understand the deeper motivations behind our actions.
    • In moments of self-doubt, contemplating the essence of our desires can help us transcend obstacles and find our true path.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a profound reminder to reflect on our desires, intentions, and the journey of life.
    • It encourages us to seek clarity and understanding in our pursuits, fostering a deeper connection with ourselves and the world around us.
    • By contemplating the essence of our experiences, we can navigate life's challenges with greater awareness and purpose.
    • Ultimately, it highlights the importance of loyalty, guidance, and the quest for deeper meaning in our lives.
