देवैर नो देव्य अदितिर न पातु देवस् त्रातात्रायताम् अप्रयुच्छन् |न हि मित्रस्य वरुणस्य धासिमर्हामसि प्रामियम् सान्व अग्नेः ||
devair no devy aditir ni pātu devas trātā trāyatām aprayucchan | nahi mitrasya varuṇasya dhāsim arhāmasi pramiyaṁ sānv agneḥ ||
  • Translation: "May the divine beings protect us; may Aditi, the mother of the gods, safeguard us. May the deities be our protectors, and may we not be deprived of their grace. We are not the servants of Mitra and Varuna; we are worthy of the protection of Agni."
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • devair: by the gods
    • no: us
    • devy: divine
    • aditir: Aditi, the mother of the gods
    • ni: may
    • pātu: protect
    • devas: deities
    • trātā: protector
    • trāyatām: may protect
    • aprayucchan: not deprived
    • nahi: not
    • mitrasya: of Mitra
    • varuṇasya: of Varuna
    • dhāsim: servants
    • arhāmasi: we are worthy
    • pramiyaṁ: protection
    • sānv: of Agni
    • agneḥ: of Agni
  • Contextual Significance:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of divine protection in our lives. It acknowledges the role of various deities, particularly Aditi and Agni, in safeguarding individuals and communities.
    • Aditi represents the infinite and the nurturing aspect of the universe, while Agni symbolizes transformation and purification. Together, they embody the protective forces that guide and support us.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In times of crisis, such as natural disasters or personal challenges, people often turn to spiritual practices, prayers, or rituals seeking divine protection and guidance, reflecting the essence of this mantra.
    • In a corporate setting, leaders may invoke the spirit of teamwork and collaboration, akin to the divine protection sought in the mantra, to foster a supportive work environment.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of humanity and the divine. It encourages individuals to seek protection and guidance from higher powers while recognizing their own worthiness of such support.
    • In everyday life, it can inspire individuals to cultivate a sense of community, support one another, and acknowledge the divine presence in their endeavors, ultimately leading to a more harmonious existence.
