आ नो देवः सविता सावीषद वय ऋजूयते यजमानाय सुन्वते |यथा देवान प्रतिभूषेम पाकवद आ सर्वतातिम अदितिं वृणीमहे ||
ā no devaḥ savitā sāviṣad vaya ṛjūyate yajamānāya sunvate | yathā devān pratibhūṣema pākavad ā sarvatātim aditiṁ vṛṇīmahe ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā no: May we be blessed
    • devaḥ: by the divine
    • savitā: the Sun God
    • sāviṣad: who bestows wisdom
    • vaya: our
    • ṛjūyate: may it be straight or righteous
    • yajamānāya: for the worshipper
    • sunvate: who is performing the sacrifice
    • yathā: as
    • devān: the gods
    • pratibhūṣema: may we be adorned or blessed
    • pākavad: with purity
    • ā: and
    • sarvatātim: all-pervading
    • aditiṁ: the infinite or boundless
    • vṛṇīmahe: we choose or invoke
  • Overall Meaning:
    • This mantra is a prayer for divine blessings and guidance from Savitā, the Sun God, who represents enlightenment and wisdom.
    • It emphasizes the importance of righteousness and purity in one's actions, particularly in the context of performing rituals or sacrifices.
    • The invocation of the gods signifies a desire for divine support and the aspiration to embody their qualities.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, this mantra can be applied when a team seeks guidance and clarity in their projects. They may invoke the spirit of collaboration and righteousness to achieve their goals.
    • In personal life, individuals may recite this mantra during challenging times, seeking wisdom and purity of thought to make the right decisions.
    • During community gatherings or rituals, this mantra can be chanted to invoke collective blessings and to ensure that the intentions behind the gathering are pure and righteous.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine guidance and the pursuit of righteousness in all endeavors.
    • It serves as a reminder to seek wisdom and purity in our actions, fostering a sense of community and shared purpose.
    • In real-life scenarios, whether in personal, professional, or communal contexts, this mantra encourages individuals to align their intentions with higher values, ultimately leading to a more harmonious existence.
