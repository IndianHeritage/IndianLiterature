यो नन्त्वान्य अनमन्य ओजसोतादर्दर मण्युनā शम्बराणि वि |प्राच्यावयद अच्युतā ब्रह्मणस पतिर ā चाविशद वसुमन्तं वि पर्वतम् ||
yo nantvāny anaman ny ojasotādardar manyunā śambarāṇi vi | prācyāvayad acyutā brahmaṇas patir ā cāviśad vasumantaṁ vi parvatam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yo: who
    • nantvāny: having no rival
    • anaman: unshaken
    • ojasotādardar: with great energy
    • manyunā: with anger
    • śambarāṇi: the powerful
    • vi: indeed
    • prācyāvayad: he who has been invoked
    • acyutā: imperishable
    • brahmaṇas: of the Brahman
    • patir: lord
    • ā: towards
    • cāviśad: has entered
    • vasumantaṁ: the wealth-giving
    • vi: indeed
    • parvatam: the mountain
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of a divine entity who is unparalleled and unshaken, embodying immense energy and power.
    • The reference to "having no rival" suggests a supreme being, often interpreted as a representation of the ultimate reality or Brahman in Hindu philosophy.
    • The phrase "with anger" indicates the protective and fierce aspect of this divine force, which can be seen as a guardian against negativity and evil.
    • The invocation of "the wealth-giving mountain" symbolizes stability, abundance, and the nurturing aspects of nature, which are essential for life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In times of personal crisis, individuals often seek strength and resilience, akin to the unshaken nature of the divine described in this mantra. For instance, someone facing a significant life challenge may draw inspiration from this mantra to find inner strength.
    • The concept of a protective force can be seen in various cultures where people invoke deities or spiritual entities for safety and guidance, such as during rituals or prayers for protection against harm.
    • The metaphor of the mountain as a source of wealth can be related to environmental conservation efforts, where communities recognize the importance of preserving natural resources for future generations.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of seeking strength, protection, and abundance in life.
    • It serves as a reminder of the imperishable nature of the divine and the importance of connecting with this energy for personal empowerment.
    • In real-life scenarios, individuals can apply the teachings of this mantra by cultivating resilience, seeking guidance in challenging times, and appreciating the abundance provided by nature.
