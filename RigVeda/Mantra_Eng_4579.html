आ नो भरा वृषणं शुष्मं इन्द्र धनस्पृतं शूशुवांसं सुदक्षम् |येन वंसाम पृतनासु शत्रून तवोतिभिर उता जामींंर अजामीन् ||
ā no bhara vṛṣaṇaṁ śuṣmam indra dhanaspṛtaṁ śūśuvāṁsaṁ sudakṣam | yena vaṁsāma pṛtanāsu śatrūn tavotibhir uta jāmīm̐r ajāmīn ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā no bhara: "May you bring to us" - a call for abundance and support.
    • vṛṣaṇaṁ: "the strong" - referring to strength or power.
    • śuṣmam: "the dry" - symbolizing a state of lack or need.
    • indra: "O Indra" - invoking the king of gods, representing power and authority.
    • dhanaspṛtaṁ: "who is rich" - referring to wealth and prosperity.
    • śūśuvāṁsaṁ: "the one who is wise" - indicating intelligence and skill.
    • sudakṣam: "well-skilled" - emphasizing expertise and capability.
    • yena: "by whom" - indicating the means or agency.
    • vaṁsāma: "our lineage" - referring to family or community.
    • pṛtanāsu: "in battles" - symbolizing challenges or conflicts.
    • śatrūn: "enemies" - representing obstacles or adversaries.
    • tavotibhir: "with your strength" - invoking the power of Indra.
    • uta: "and" - a conjunction linking ideas.
    • jāmīm̐r: "our offspring" - referring to future generations.
    • ajāmīn: "and those who are not born" - symbolizing potential and future possibilities.
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra is a prayer for strength, wisdom, and prosperity, invoking the divine support of Indra.
    • It emphasizes the importance of community and lineage, seeking protection and guidance in times of conflict.
    • In real life, this can be seen in the context of families and communities coming together to support one another during difficult times, such as natural disasters or economic hardships.
    • For example, during a community crisis, individuals may invoke the spirit of unity and strength to overcome challenges, much like the mantra calls for divine assistance.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for collective strength and wisdom in facing adversities.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, such as community gatherings, family prayers, or even in personal reflections when seeking guidance and support.
    • Ultimately, it serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of individuals and the importance of seeking help from a higher power in times of need.
