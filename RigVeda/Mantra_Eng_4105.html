शर्धं-शर्धं वा एषां व्रातं-व्रातं गणं-गणं सुशस्तिभिः |अनु क्रामेम धीतिभिः ||
śardhaṁ-śardhaṁ va eṣāṁ vrātaṁ-vrātaṁ gaṇaṁ-gaṇaṁ suśastibhiḥ | anu krāmema dhītibhiḥ ||
  • śardhaṁ-śardhaṁ: This term can be interpreted as "together" or "in unity." It emphasizes the importance of collective effort and harmony among individuals.
  • va eṣāṁ: This phrase translates to "this group" or "these people," indicating a specific community or gathering.
  • vrātaṁ-vrātaṁ: The word "vrāta" refers to a group or assembly, often with a connotation of a sacred or purposeful gathering. The repetition emphasizes the significance of the assembly.
  • gaṇaṁ-gaṇaṁ: Similar to "vrāta," "gaṇa" means a group or multitude. The repetition again highlights the importance of community.
  • suśastibhiḥ: This term can be understood as "with good intentions" or "with auspiciousness." It suggests that the actions and thoughts of the group should be positive and beneficial.
  • anu krāmema: This phrase means "we shall proceed" or "we shall move forward," indicating a sense of direction and purpose in the actions of the group.
  • dhītibhiḥ: This word translates to "with wisdom" or "with understanding," emphasizing the need for knowledge and insight in the group's endeavors.

  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of unity, collective purpose, and positive intentions within a community. It highlights the importance of moving forward together with wisdom and understanding. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various contexts such as team projects, community service, or any collaborative effort where harmony and shared goals are essential. By fostering a spirit of cooperation and wisdom, individuals can achieve greater outcomes and contribute positively to society.
