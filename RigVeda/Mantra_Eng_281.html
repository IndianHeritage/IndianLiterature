मन्त्र: बिभ्रद् द्रापिं हिरण्ययं वरुणो वस्त निर्णिजम् |परि स्पशो नि षेदिरे ||
bibhrad drāpiṁ hiraṇyayaṁ varuṇo vasta nirṇijam | pari spaśo ni ṣedire ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • bibhrad: to hold or support
    • drāpiṁ: the earth or the ground
    • hiraṇyayaṁ: golden or precious
    • varuṇo: Varuna, the god of water and cosmic order
    • vasta: dwelling or abode
    • nirṇijam: that which is unshaken or stable
    • pari: around or surrounding
    • spaśo: touch or contact
    • ni: not
    • ṣedire: to be disturbed or shaken
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the divine support that Varuna provides to the earth, emphasizing stability and protection.
    • The phrase "bibhrad drāpiṁ" suggests that the earth is held firmly, indicating a sense of security and grounding.
    • "Hiraṇyayaṁ" implies that this support is not just ordinary but precious, symbolizing the value of stability in our lives.
    • Varuna, as a deity, represents the cosmic order and the natural laws that govern the universe, reminding us of the importance of harmony and balance.
    • The mention of "vasta nirṇijam" indicates that this stability is unshaken, which can be related to the idea of resilience in the face of challenges.
    • The concluding part, "pari spaśo ni ṣedire," reinforces the idea that true stability is not easily disturbed, highlighting the importance of maintaining inner peace amidst external chaos.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In personal life, this mantra can be applied to the concept of emotional stability. Just as the earth is held firm, individuals can cultivate resilience through mindfulness and self-awareness.
    • In a community context, the mantra can inspire collective efforts to create a supportive environment where individuals feel secure and valued, much like Varuna's protective embrace over the earth.
    • In environmental discussions, the mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of preserving our natural resources and maintaining the balance of ecosystems, akin to Varuna's role in cosmic order.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of stability, support, and resilience in our lives.
    • It encourages individuals and communities to seek harmony and balance, drawing inspiration from the divine protection offered by Varuna.
    • In a world filled with uncertainties, this mantra serves as a guiding principle to cultivate inner strength and foster a nurturing environment for all.
