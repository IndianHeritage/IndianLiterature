स त्वं न इन्द्र वाजेभिर दशस्याऽ च गातुया च |अच्छा च नः सुम्नं नेषि ||
sa tvaṁ na indra vājebhir daśasyā ca gātuyā ca | acchā ca naḥ sumnaṁ neṣi ||
  • sa tvaṁ: This phrase translates to "you" or "that you." It signifies a direct address to a divine entity, in this case, Indra, the king of gods in Vedic literature.
  • na indra: "Na" means "not," and "Indra" refers to the deity. This part can be interpreted as a plea or a statement regarding Indra's presence or influence.
  • vājebhir: This word means "with strength" or "with vigor." It indicates the power and might associated with Indra.
  • daśasyā ca: "Daśasyā" refers to "of the ten," which could symbolize completeness or wholeness. "Ca" means "and," linking it to the next part of the mantra.
  • gātuyā ca: "Gātuyā" can be interpreted as "with song" or "with praise." This suggests a connection to devotion and the act of worship through music or hymns.
  • acchā ca naḥ: "Acchā" means "good" or "auspicious," and "naḥ" means "to us." This part expresses a desire for goodness or blessings to be bestowed upon the worshippers.
  • sumnaṁ neṣi: "Sumnaṁ" translates to "well-being" or "prosperity," and "neṣi" means "to lead" or "to guide." This indicates a request for guidance towards a prosperous and fulfilling life.

Conclusion:

This mantra is a prayer for strength, guidance, and well-being, invoking the blessings of Indra. In real-life scenarios, it can be applicable in various contexts such as:

  • Personal Growth: Individuals can recite this mantra during times of challenge, seeking strength and guidance to overcome obstacles.
  • Community Gatherings: It can be used in communal prayers or gatherings to invoke collective strength and prosperity for the community.
  • Rituals and Ceremonies: The mantra can be incorporated into rituals aimed at achieving auspicious beginnings, such as weddings or new ventures.

Overall, the significance of this mantra lies in its ability to connect individuals with divine strength and guidance, fostering a sense of community and well-being in various aspects of life.
