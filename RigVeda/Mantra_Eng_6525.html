उद उ तिष्ठ svadhvara स्तवाणो देव्या कृपा |अभिख्या भासा बृहतः शुशुक्वनिः ||
ud u tiṣṭha svadhvara stavāno devyā kṛpā | abhikhyā bhāsā bṛhatā śuśukvaniḥ ||
  • ud u tiṣṭha: This phrase translates to "arise" or "get up." It signifies the importance of awakening, both physically and spiritually. In real life, this can be seen as a call to action, encouraging individuals to rise from complacency and take charge of their lives.
  • svadhvara: This term can be interpreted as "one's own strength" or "self-power." It emphasizes the idea of relying on one's inner strength and capabilities. For example, in a challenging situation at work, instead of waiting for help, one should harness their own skills and knowledge to overcome obstacles.
  • stavāno devyā kṛpā: This part means "the grace of the goddess." It highlights the significance of divine support and blessings in our endeavors. In everyday life, people often seek guidance or blessings from a higher power when facing difficulties, such as praying for success before an important exam or presentation.
  • abhikhyā: This word refers to "knowledge" or "wisdom." It suggests that understanding and awareness are crucial for progress. For instance, in education, acquiring knowledge is essential for personal and professional growth, enabling individuals to make informed decisions.
  • bhāsā bṛhatā: This translates to "the great light" or "the vast illumination." It symbolizes enlightenment and the pursuit of truth. In real life, this can be related to the quest for knowledge and understanding, such as engaging in discussions, reading, or exploring new ideas to broaden one's perspective.
  • śuśukvaniḥ: This term can be interpreted as "the sound of joy" or "the voice of happiness." It signifies the importance of positivity and joy in life. For example, celebrating achievements, whether big or small, fosters a positive environment and encourages further success.

Conclusion: The mantra "ud u tiṣṭha svadhvara stavāno devyā kṛpā | abhikhyā bhāsā bṛhatā śuśukvaniḥ ||" serves as a powerful reminder of the importance of awakening to one's potential, relying on inner strength, seeking divine guidance, and pursuing knowledge. In real-life scenarios, it encourages individuals to take proactive steps towards their goals, embrace positivity, and continuously seek wisdom. This mantra can be applied in various aspects of life, including personal development, education, and professional growth, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling and enlightened existence.
