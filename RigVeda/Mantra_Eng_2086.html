विधेम ते परम जनन्मन् अग्ने विधेम स्तोमैर अवरे साधस्ते |यस्माद् योनेर उदारिता यजेतम् प्र तवे हविषं जुहुरे समिद्धे ||
vidhema te parame janmann agne vidhema stomair avare sadhasthe | yasmād yoner udārithā yaje tam pra tve havīṁṣi juhure samiddhe ||
  • Vidhema te parame janmann: This phrase translates to "We meditate upon you, O Agni, in the highest birth." Here, "Agni" refers to the fire god, symbolizing energy, transformation, and the divine presence in the world. The term "parame janmann" signifies the highest or most exalted state of existence, suggesting a connection to spiritual enlightenment.
  • Agne vidhema stomair avare sadhasthe: This means "O Agni, we meditate upon you with our hymns, as you are present in the lower realms." This line emphasizes the omnipresence of Agni, indicating that the divine energy is accessible in both the spiritual and material worlds. It suggests that through devotion and worship, one can connect with this divine energy.
  • Yasmād yoner udārithā: This translates to "From whom the great energies are born." This line highlights the idea that all forms of energy and creation stem from the divine source, Agni. It reflects the interconnectedness of all life and the importance of recognizing the divine in every aspect of existence.
  • Yaje tam pra tve havīṁṣi juhure samiddhe: This means "To that Agni, we offer our oblations, as he is fully kindled." This line signifies the act of offering and devotion, suggesting that when one is fully engaged in worship, the divine energy is activated and becomes a source of blessings and transformation in life.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of Agni as a divine force that connects the spiritual and material realms. It teaches us the importance of meditation, devotion, and recognizing the divine energy in our lives. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied through practices such as meditation, prayer, and rituals that honor the divine presence in our daily activities. By acknowledging and connecting with this energy, individuals can experience transformation, guidance, and a deeper understanding of their purpose in life. The mantra serves as a reminder of the sacredness of existence and the potential for spiritual growth through devotion and awareness.
