ऋतेन मित्रावरुणाव ऋतावृद्धाव ऋतस्पृशा |क्रतुम् बृहन्तम् आशाथे ||
ṛtena mitrāvaruṇāv ṛtāvṛdhāv ṛtaspṛśā | kratum bṛhantam āśāthe ||
  • ṛtena: This word translates to "by truth" or "through righteousness." It emphasizes the importance of living a life based on truth and moral integrity.
  • mitrāvaruṇāv: This refers to the deities Mitra and Varuna, who symbolize friendship and cosmic order, respectively. Mitra represents the light of day and social harmony, while Varuna embodies the moral and natural laws of the universe.
  • ṛtāvṛdhāv: This can be understood as "those who increase righteousness." It suggests a focus on promoting and enhancing truth and ethical behavior in society.
  • ṛtaspṛśā: This means "touched by truth." It indicates a connection or alignment with the principles of truth and righteousness.
  • kratum: This word means "intention" or "resolve." It signifies the mental determination to act in accordance with one's values and beliefs.
  • bṛhantam: This translates to "great" or "vast." It implies a sense of magnitude and importance in the intentions we hold.
  • āśāthe: This can be interpreted as "may we be protected" or "may we be guided." It expresses a desire for divine support in our endeavors.

The mantra as a whole can be understood as a prayer or invocation for guidance and support from the divine, particularly in the pursuit of truth and righteousness. It emphasizes the importance of aligning one's intentions with moral values and seeking the blessings of the deities Mitra and Varuna to enhance one's journey towards a virtuous life.


In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts:

  • Personal Integrity: Individuals can reflect on their actions and decisions, ensuring they are rooted in truth and ethical principles. For example, a business leader might use this mantra to guide their decisions, promoting transparency and fairness in their company.
  • Community Harmony: In social settings, this mantra encourages fostering relationships based on trust and mutual respect. Community leaders can invoke these principles to resolve conflicts and build a cohesive environment.
  • Environmental Responsibility: The connection to cosmic order can inspire individuals and organizations to act responsibly towards nature, promoting sustainability and ethical stewardship of the earth.

In conclusion, this mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the significance of truth and righteousness in our lives. By aligning our intentions with these values and seeking divine guidance, we can navigate our paths with integrity and purpose, ultimately contributing to a more harmonious and just society.
