कयā नो अग्ने वि वसः सुवृक्तिं कामु स्वधाम ऋणवः शस्यमानः |कदā भवेम पतयः सुदात्र रयो वन्तारो दुष्टरस्य साधोः ||
kayā no agne vi vasaḥ suvṛktiṁ kām u svadhām ṛṇavaḥ śasyamānaḥ | kadā bhavema patayaḥ sudatra rāyo vantāro duṣṭarasya sādhoḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • kayā: By what means
    • no: Our
    • agne: O Fire (Agni, the divine fire)
    • vi vasaḥ: You who dwell in all
    • suvṛktiṁ: Good thoughts or auspiciousness
    • kām: Desires
    • u: And
    • svadhām: By your own power
    • ṛṇavaḥ: The one who is worthy
    • śasyamānaḥ: Who is being nourished
    • kadā: When
    • bhavema: May we become
    • patayaḥ: The lords or rulers
    • sudatra: Of good fortune
    • rāyo: Wealth
    • vantāro: Those who possess
    • duṣṭarasya: Of the wicked
    • sādhoḥ: Of the righteous
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Agni, the divine fire, seeking guidance and auspiciousness in our thoughts and actions.
    • It emphasizes the importance of aligning our desires with righteousness and the pursuit of good fortune.
    • The phrase "by what means" suggests a quest for understanding how to achieve our goals through virtuous actions.
    • It reflects a desire to be leaders or rulers of good fortune, indicating a collective aspiration for prosperity and well-being.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, this mantra can be applied when a team seeks to align their goals with ethical practices, ensuring that their success is built on a foundation of integrity.
    • In personal life, individuals may reflect on this mantra when making decisions about their careers or relationships, striving to ensure that their desires do not lead them astray from their values.
    • Communities can invoke this mantra during gatherings or festivals, seeking blessings for collective prosperity and harmony.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for introspection and alignment of desires with righteousness.
    • It serves as a reminder that true success and leadership come from virtuous actions and the pursuit of good fortune.
    • In real life, it encourages individuals and communities to seek guidance from higher principles, ensuring that their paths are illuminated by wisdom and integrity.
