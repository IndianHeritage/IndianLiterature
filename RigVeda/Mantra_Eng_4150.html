ऋष्टयो वो मरुतो अंशयोर्धि सह ओजो बाह्वोः वो बलं हितम् |नृम्णा शीरषस्व आयुधा रथेषु वो विश्वा वः श्रीर अधि तनुषु पिपीषे ||
ṛṣṭayo vo maruto aṁsayor adhi saha ojo bāhvor vo balaṁ hitam | nṛmṇā śīrṣasv āyudhā ratheṣu vo viśvā vaḥ śrīr adhi tanūṣu pipiśe ||
  • ṛṣṭayo vo maruto: This phrase refers to the "Maruts," the storm deities in Vedic literature, who are often associated with strength and vitality. The term "ṛṣṭayo" can be interpreted as "blessings" or "good fortune." In real life, this can be seen as the positive energy or support we receive from nature or the universe during challenging times.
  • aṁsayor adhi saha: This translates to "together with your strength." It emphasizes the importance of unity and collaboration. In a practical context, this can be related to teamwork in workplaces or communities where collective effort leads to greater achievements.
  • ojo bāhvor vo balaṁ hitam: Here, "ojo" means "energy" or "vigor," and "bāhvor" refers to "arms." This line suggests that the strength and energy of the Maruts are beneficial for the people. In everyday life, this can be likened to the support we receive from friends and family that empowers us to overcome obstacles.
  • nṛmṇā śīrṣasv āyudhā: This part can be interpreted as "the weapons of men." It signifies the tools or skills that individuals possess. In a modern context, this could refer to education, technology, or any resources that empower individuals to succeed in their endeavors.
  • ratheṣu vo viśvā vaḥ: "Ratheṣu" means "in chariots," and "viśvā" refers to "all." This line suggests that the blessings of the Maruts are present in all aspects of life, symbolized by the chariot, which represents progress and movement. In real life, this can be seen in how we navigate through various phases of life, whether in personal or professional journeys.
  • śrīr adhi tanūṣu pipiśe: "Śrīr" means "prosperity" or "wealth," and "adhi tanūṣu" refers to "in your bodies." This indicates that prosperity is bestowed upon individuals. In practical terms, this can relate to health, well-being, and the abundance we experience in life.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of strength, unity, and prosperity. It highlights the importance of collaboration and the support we receive from the universe and our community. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can serve as a reminder to seek strength in unity, to harness our skills and resources, and to recognize the blessings that contribute to our well-being. It encourages individuals to work together towards common goals, fostering a sense of community and shared purpose, ultimately leading to a more prosperous and fulfilling life.
