ये अग्निः परि जज्निरे विरूपासो दिवस परि | नवग्वो नु दशग्वो अंगिरस्तमः सचा देवेषु मंहते ||
ye agneḥ pari jajñire virūpāso divas pari | navagvo nu daśagvo aṅgirastamaḥ sacā deveṣu maṁhate ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ye: those who
    • agneḥ: of Agni (the fire god)
    • pari: around
    • jajñire: were born or manifested
    • virūpāso: having diverse forms
    • divas: in the heavens
    • pari: around
    • navagvo: new cows (or wealth)
    • nu: indeed
    • daśagvo: ten cows (or wealth)
    • aṅgirastamaḥ: the best of the Angiras (a group of sages)
    • sacā: and with
    • deveṣu: among the gods
    • maṁhate: they are honored or revered
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the divine fire, Agni, who is considered a mediator between humans and the gods.
    • The phrase "those who were born around Agni" suggests the importance of fire in various aspects of life, including rituals and sacrifices.
    • The mention of "diverse forms" indicates that Agni manifests in various ways, symbolizing transformation and change.
    • The reference to "new cows" and "ten cows" can be interpreted as a metaphor for wealth and prosperity, emphasizing the blessings that come from honoring the divine.
    • The term "the best of the Angiras" highlights the significance of wisdom and knowledge in achieving success and reverence among the gods.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern rituals, fire is still a central element, symbolizing purification and the connection between the physical and spiritual realms.
    • In business, the idea of "new wealth" can be seen in startups that innovate and create new markets, reflecting the transformative power of ideas.
    • Honoring wisdom and knowledge, as suggested by the Angiras, is applicable in educational pursuits, where learning is revered and sought after.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of Agni as a source of transformation, wealth, and divine connection.
    • It teaches us to honor the diverse forms of prosperity and wisdom in our lives, encouraging us to seek knowledge and maintain a connection with the divine.
    • In real life, the principles derived from this mantra can guide individuals in their personal and professional journeys, fostering growth, respect for knowledge, and a deeper understanding of the interconnectedness of life.
