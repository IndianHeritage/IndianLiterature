ये ते विप्र ब्रह्मकृतः सुते सचा वसूनां च वसनश्च दावने |प्र ते सुम्नस्य मनसा पथा bhuvan मादे सुतस्य सोम्यस्यांधसः ||
ye te vipra brahmakṛtaḥ sute sacā vasūnāṁ ca vasunaś ca dāvane | pra te sumnasya manasā pathā bhuvan made sutasya somyasyāndhasaḥ ||
  • ye te vipra brahmakṛtaḥ: This phrase refers to those wise individuals (vipra) who are engaged in the pursuit of knowledge and truth (brahmakṛta). In real life, this can be likened to scholars, philosophers, or spiritual leaders who dedicate their lives to understanding the deeper meanings of existence.
  • sute sacā vasūnāṁ ca vasunaś ca dāvane: Here, "sute" means "in the offerings," and "vasūnāṁ" refers to the wealth or abundance. This part emphasizes the importance of sharing and giving, akin to charitable acts in modern society where individuals or organizations contribute to the welfare of others, such as food banks or educational programs.
  • pra te sumnasya manasā pathā: This translates to "may we find the path of happiness with our minds." It highlights the significance of mental well-being and the pursuit of joy. In contemporary life, this can be related to practices like mindfulness, meditation, or therapy, which aim to enhance mental health and overall happiness.
  • bhuvan made sutasya somyasyāndhasaḥ: This phrase can be interpreted as "in the world, may we enjoy the fruits of our labor." It signifies the rewards of hard work and the joy that comes from achieving one's goals. In real life, this can be seen in the success stories of individuals who have worked diligently to achieve their dreams, such as entrepreneurs or artists who find fulfillment in their creations.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of wisdom, generosity, mental well-being, and the rewards of hard work. Its significance lies in encouraging individuals to seek knowledge, share their resources, cultivate happiness, and appreciate the fruits of their labor. In today's world, these principles can be applied in various scenarios, such as community service, personal development, and professional achievements, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling and harmonious life.
