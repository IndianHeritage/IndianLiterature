प्र मित्रयोः वरुणयोः स्तोमो न एतु शूष्यः |नमस्वान तुविजातयोः ||
pra mitrayor varuṇayoḥ stomo na etu śūṣyaḥ | namasvān tuvijātayoḥ ||
  • Mantra: "pra mitrayor varuṇayoḥ stomo na etu śūṣyaḥ | namasvān tuvijātayoḥ ||"
  • Translation: "May the hymn (stoma) of the two deities, Mitra and Varuna, not become dry (śūṣyaḥ). I bow to you, O divine beings, who are of different kinds (tuvijātayoḥ)."
  • Explanation:
    • Mitra and Varuna: In Vedic literature, Mitra represents friendship, harmony, and the social order, while Varuna embodies cosmic order, truth, and moral authority. Together, they symbolize the balance between social and cosmic laws.
    • Stoma: The term 'stoma' refers to a hymn or praise. In this context, it signifies the importance of maintaining the flow of devotion and reverence towards these deities.
    • Dryness (śūṣyaḥ): The phrase "not become dry" suggests that the devotion and connection to these divine principles should remain vibrant and alive, not stagnant or lifeless.
    • Bow (namasvān): The act of bowing signifies humility and respect towards the divine forces, acknowledging their significance in our lives.
    • Diversity (tuvijātayoḥ): The mention of different kinds indicates the acceptance of various paths and forms of divinity, promoting inclusivity and understanding in spiritual practices.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community setting, fostering friendship and cooperation (Mitra) can lead to a harmonious environment, while upholding truth and justice (Varuna) ensures that moral values are respected.
    • In personal relationships, maintaining open communication and trust (Mitra) alongside honesty and integrity (Varuna) can strengthen bonds and create lasting connections.
    • In a workplace, collaboration and teamwork (Mitra) combined with ethical practices and accountability (Varuna) can lead to a productive and positive organizational culture.
  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of maintaining a vibrant spiritual connection with the divine principles of friendship and cosmic order. It serves as a reminder to keep our devotion alive and to respect the diverse paths of spirituality. In real life, applying these principles can lead to harmonious relationships, ethical practices, and a balanced community, ultimately contributing to a more peaceful and just society.
