इमां ते धियम् प्र भरे महो महीम् अस्य स्तोत्रे धिषणा यत त आनजे |तमुत्सवे च प्रसवे च सासहिम इन्द्रं देवासः शवसामदन्न अनु ||
imāṁ te dhiyam pra bhare maho mahīm asya stotre dhiṣaṇā yat ta ānaje | tam utsave ca prasave ca sāsahim indraṁ devāsaḥ śavasāmadann anu ||
  • imāṁ te dhiyam pra bhare: This phrase translates to "I offer you this intellect." Here, the speaker is addressing a divine entity, likely Indra, and is expressing a desire to dedicate their intellect and wisdom to this deity.
  • maho mahīm asya stotre: This means "the great glory of this hymn." It emphasizes the significance and power of the hymn being recited, suggesting that it holds immense value and strength.
  • dhiṣaṇā yat ta ānaje: This can be interpreted as "the inspiration that comes from you." It indicates that the wisdom and guidance sought are derived from the divine, highlighting the importance of divine inspiration in human endeavors.
  • tam utsave ca prasave ca: This translates to "in celebration and in birth." It signifies the dual aspects of life—celebration and creation—implying that the divine presence is essential in both joyous and significant moments.
  • sāsahim indraṁ devāsaḥ: This means "the powerful Indra, the god." Indra is often associated with strength, rain, and thunder, representing the force of nature and the divine protector.
  • śavasāmadann anu: This translates to "who nourishes the dead." It suggests that the divine also plays a role in the cycle of life and death, providing sustenance and support even in the afterlife.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda encapsulates the essence of devotion, wisdom, and the divine's role in human life. It signifies the importance of seeking divine guidance in our intellectual pursuits and recognizing the divine presence in both joyous celebrations and significant life events. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applicable in moments of decision-making, celebrations such as weddings or births, and during times of reflection on life and death. By invoking the divine, individuals can find strength, clarity, and purpose in their actions, fostering a deeper connection with the spiritual realm and the universe around them.
