एष उ स्य वृषा रथोऽव्यो वारेभिरर्षति |गच्छन् वाजं सहस्रिणम् ||
eṣa u sya vṛṣā ratho 'vyo vārebhir arṣati | gacchan vājaṁ sahasriṇam ||
  • Word Breakdown:
    • eṣa: This means "this" or "he" and indicates a specific subject or entity.
    • u: A particle that can mean "also" or "indeed," adding emphasis.
    • syā: This translates to "may be" or "is," suggesting potential or existence.
    • vṛṣā: This word means "bull" or "strength," often symbolizing power and vitality.
    • ratho: Meaning "chariot," it represents movement, progress, and the journey of life.
    • 'vyo: A form of "to go" or "to move," indicating action or direction.
    • vārebhir: This can be interpreted as "with the best" or "with excellence," suggesting quality.
    • arṣati: Meaning "to rise" or "to ascend," indicating growth or elevation.
    • gacchan: This means "going" or "moving," reinforcing the theme of progress.
    • vājaṁ: Translates to "wealth" or "prosperity," often associated with success and abundance.
    • sahasriṇam: Meaning "thousand," it emphasizes abundance and multiplicity.
  • Meaning and Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of a powerful entity or force that is on a journey, moving with strength and purpose.
    • The "chariot" symbolizes the vehicle of life, suggesting that we are all on a path, driven by our desires and ambitions.
    • The emphasis on "wealth" and "thousand" indicates that this journey is not just about personal growth but also about achieving prosperity and abundance.
    • In real life, this can be likened to an individual pursuing their career goals, where the "chariot" represents their efforts and the "wealth" symbolizes the rewards of hard work.
  • Conclusion and Significance:
    • This mantra highlights the importance of strength, direction, and the pursuit of prosperity in our lives.
    • It serves as a reminder that our journey is significant, and with the right mindset and effort, we can achieve our goals.
    • In practical terms, it encourages individuals to embrace their ambitions, work diligently, and strive for excellence in all endeavors.
    • Ultimately, it signifies that the journey towards success is as important as the destination itself, urging us to rise and move forward with purpose.
