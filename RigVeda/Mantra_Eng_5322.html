यस्त इन्द्र प्रियः जनो ददाशद आसन निरके अद्रिवः सखा ते |वयं ते अस्यां सुमतौ चनिष्ठाः स्याम वरूथे अग्नतो नृपीतौ ||
yas ta indra priyo jano dadāśad asan nireke adrivaḥ sakhā te | vayaṁ te asyāṁ sumatau caniṣṭhāḥ syāma varūthe aghnato nṛpītau ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yas: who
    • ta: that
    • indra: Indra, the king of gods
    • priyo: dear
    • jano: person or people
    • dadāśad: has given
    • asan: seat or place
    • nireke: without any hindrance
    • adrivaḥ: those who are free from obstacles
    • sakhā: friend
    • te: your
    • vayaṁ: we
    • te: your
    • asyāṁ: in this
    • sumatau: with good thoughts
    • caniṣṭhāḥ: may we be
    • syāma: may we become
    • varūthe: in the protection
    • aghnato: of the non-violent
    • nṛpītau: in the presence of the ruler
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra expresses a plea for divine support and friendship from Indra, the king of gods.
    • It emphasizes the importance of community and collective strength, as it addresses the people who are dear to Indra.
    • The phrase "may we be in this with good thoughts" suggests a focus on positive intentions and unity among individuals.
    • The mention of "protection" indicates a desire for safety and security in the presence of a ruler or leader.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace, this mantra can be likened to a team coming together to support each other under the guidance of a manager, fostering a positive and collaborative environment.
    • In a community setting, it reflects how neighbors can unite to create a safe and supportive neighborhood, looking out for one another.
    • In personal relationships, it highlights the importance of having friends who provide emotional support and encouragement during challenging times.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the value of friendship, community, and divine support in overcoming obstacles.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, such as teamwork, community building, and personal relationships, where collective strength and positive intentions lead to success and harmony.
    • Ultimately, it serves as a reminder to seek and offer support, fostering a sense of unity and protection in our lives.
