अबोध्य अग्निर ज्म उद एति सूर्यो व्य उषाश चन्द्रा मह्य आवो आर्चिषा |आयुक्षाताम अश्विनाऽ यातवे रथम प्रासवीद देवः सविता जगत पृथक् ||
abodhy agnir jma ud eti sūryo vy u1ṣāś candrā mahy āvo arciṣā | āyukṣātām aśvinā yātave ratham prāsāvīd devaḥ savitā jagat pṛthak ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • abodhy: awaken or inspire
    • agnir: fire
    • jma: born or generated
    • ud: upward or rising
    • eti: goes or moves
    • sūryo: the sun
    • vy: in the same way
    • uṣāś: dawn or morning light
    • candrā: moon
    • mahy: for us or for our benefit
    • āvo: may we be protected
    • arciṣā: by the light or radiance
    • āyukṣātām: for long life
    • aśvinā: O Ashvins (the twin gods)
    • yātave: who travel or move
    • ratham: chariot
    • prāsāvīd: may he grant or bestow
    • devaḥ: the divine or god
    • savitā: the sun god
    • jagat: the world
    • pṛthak: separately or distinctly
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the divine forces of fire, the sun, and the dawn, symbolizing enlightenment, energy, and new beginnings.
    • The mention of the Ashvins, who are known as the divine physicians, suggests a plea for health and vitality.
    • The phrase "may we be protected by the light" emphasizes the importance of guidance and safety in our lives.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, this mantra can be recited during the morning to seek inspiration and energy for the day ahead, similar to how the rising sun brings light and warmth.
    • During health-related rituals or prayers, invoking the Ashvins can be a way to seek healing and well-being, reflecting the importance of health in our lives.
    • In moments of uncertainty or fear, reciting this mantra can serve as a reminder to seek guidance and protection from higher powers.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of light, health, and divine guidance in our lives.
    • It serves as a reminder to seek inspiration and protection from the divine forces that govern our existence.
    • Incorporating this mantra into daily practices can foster a sense of connection to the universe and promote a positive outlook on life.
