सातिर न वो 'मवतī स्वरवती तवेषा विपाकā मरुतः पिपिष्वती |भद्रा वो रातिः पृणतो न दक्षिणा पृथुज्रयी असुर्येव जन्जती ||
sātir na vo 'mavatī svarvatī tveṣā vipākā marutaḥ pipiṣvatī | bhadrā vo rātiḥ pṛṇato na dakṣiṇā pṛthujrayī asuryeva jañjatī ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sātir: strength or power
    • na: not
    • vo: to you (plural)
    • 'mavatī: may it protect
    • svarvatī: celestial or heavenly
    • tveṣā: with this
    • vipākā: the result or outcome
    • marutaḥ: of the winds or storm deities
    • pipiṣvatī: may it nourish or sustain
  • Contextual Meaning:
    • This mantra invokes the strength and protection of divine forces, particularly the Maruts, who are associated with storms and winds.
    • It emphasizes the importance of celestial support in achieving success and well-being.
    • The phrase "bhadrā vo rātiḥ" suggests a desire for auspiciousness and favorable conditions in life.
    • The mantra also highlights the need for nourishment and sustenance, both physically and spiritually.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In times of personal crisis, individuals often seek strength and protection through prayer or meditation, similar to the invocation in this mantra.
    • Communities may come together to perform rituals or ceremonies to invoke divine blessings for prosperity and safety, reflecting the collective desire for auspiciousness.
    • In professional settings, teams may seek guidance and support from mentors or leaders, akin to seeking the nourishment and strength from divine forces.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of divine support and human effort.
    • It emphasizes the significance of seeking strength and protection in various aspects of life, whether personal, communal, or professional.
    • By invoking the blessings of the Maruts, individuals and communities can aspire for auspiciousness and nourishment in their endeavors.
    • Ultimately, the mantra encourages a holistic approach to life, recognizing the importance of both spiritual and material well-being.
