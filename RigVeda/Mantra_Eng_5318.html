उभे चिद इन्द्र रोदसी महित्वा पप्राथ तविषीभिस तुविष्मः |नि वज्रम इन्द्रो हरिवान मिमिक्षन सम अन्धसा मदेषु वा उवोच ||
ubhe cid indra rodasī mahitvā paprātha taviṣībhis tuviṣmaḥ | ni vajram indro harivān mimikṣan sam andhasā madeṣu vā uvoca ||
  • ubhe cid indra rodasī: This phrase translates to "Both the heavens and the earth, O Indra." Here, "Indra" refers to the king of the gods in Vedic tradition, symbolizing power and authority. The mention of "heavens" and "earth" signifies the duality of existence and the interconnectedness of all realms.
  • mahitvā paprātha taviṣībhis tuviṣmaḥ: This can be interpreted as "With great power, you have spread forth with your might." It emphasizes the strength and influence of Indra, who is often associated with storms and rain, vital for agriculture. This can be related to real-life scenarios where leadership and strength are required to bring about change or progress.
  • ni vajram indro harivān: This means "Indra, the one who wields the thunderbolt." The "vajra" symbolizes both a weapon and a metaphor for indomitable strength. In real life, this can be likened to individuals or leaders who face challenges head-on, using their skills and resources to overcome obstacles.
  • mimikṣan sam andhasā madeṣu vā uvoca: This translates to "He who sees everything, whether in darkness or in the midst of intoxication, speaks." This line suggests that true wisdom and insight come from the ability to perceive beyond superficial appearances, even in challenging situations. In everyday life, this can be applied to decision-making processes where clarity and foresight are crucial.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of strength, leadership, and wisdom. It signifies the importance of having a powerful presence in both the physical and spiritual realms, as well as the ability to see through illusions and challenges. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire individuals to embrace their inner strength, lead with authority, and maintain clarity in their thoughts and actions, especially during difficult times. It serves as a reminder that true power lies not just in physical might but also in the wisdom to navigate life's complexities.
