स तु पवस्व परि पार्थिवं रजो दिव्या च सोम धर्मभिः |त्वां विप्रासो मतीभिर् विचक्षण शुभ्रं हिन्वन्ति धीतिभिः ||
sa tū pavasva pari pārthivaṁ rajo divyā ca soma dharmabhiḥ | tvāṁ viprāso matibhir vicakṣaṇa śubhraṁ hinvanti dhītibhiḥ ||
  • sa tū pavasva: This phrase can be translated as "May you, O divine one, be invigorated." It signifies a call for the divine energy to be activated and to flow into the world.
  • pari pārthivaṁ rajo: This means "the earthly dust." It represents the material world and the physical aspects of life that we experience daily.
  • divyā ca soma: "And the divine Soma." Soma is often associated with a sacred drink that grants immortality and divine insight, symbolizing spiritual nourishment and enlightenment.
  • dharmabhiḥ: This translates to "by the principles of righteousness." It emphasizes the importance of living a life aligned with dharma, or moral and ethical duties.
  • tvāṁ viprāso: "The wise ones invoke you." This indicates that those who are knowledgeable and enlightened seek the presence and blessings of the divine.
  • matibhir vicakṣaṇa: "With their intellect and wisdom." This highlights the role of intelligence and discernment in understanding and connecting with the divine.
  • śubhraṁ hinvanti: "They bring forth the pure." This suggests that through righteous actions and wisdom, one can manifest purity and goodness in the world.
  • dhītibhiḥ: "With their understanding." This reinforces the idea that comprehension and insight are essential in the pursuit of spiritual and moral goals.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda encapsulates the essence of invoking divine energy and wisdom to navigate the material world while adhering to principles of righteousness. In real life, it serves as a reminder to seek spiritual nourishment and clarity through ethical living and intellectual engagement. Whether in personal decision-making, community leadership, or spiritual practices, the mantra encourages individuals to align their actions with higher principles, fostering a harmonious existence that benefits both themselves and society at large.
