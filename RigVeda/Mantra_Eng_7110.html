केतेन शर्मन् सचते सुशामण्य अग्ने तुभ्यं चिकीत्वना |इषणयया नः पुरुरूपम् आ भर वाजं नेदिष्टम् ऊतये ||
ketena śarman sacate suṣāmaṇy agne tubhyaṁ cikitvanā | iṣaṇyayā naḥ pururūpam ā bhara vājaṁ nediṣṭham ūtaye ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ketena: By what (means)
    • śarman: Happiness or joy
    • sacate: May it be attained
    • suṣāmaṇy: With good fortune
    • agne: O Agni (the fire god)
    • tubhyaṁ: To you
    • cikitvanā: With understanding or wisdom
    • iṣaṇyayā: With strength or power
    • naḥ: Our
    • pururūpam: Many forms or manifestations
    • ā bhara: May you bring
    • vājaṁ: Wealth or prosperity
    • nediṣṭham: The best or most desirable
    • ūtaye: For our well-being
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Agni, the fire god, seeking guidance and prosperity.
    • It emphasizes the importance of wisdom and understanding in achieving happiness and success.
    • The phrase "by what means may happiness be attained" reflects a quest for knowledge and the right approach to life.
    • The invocation of Agni symbolizes the transformative power of fire, which can purify and energize.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, this mantra can be applied when seeking innovative solutions to challenges. Just as the mantra calls for wisdom, businesses often rely on strategic thinking to navigate complexities.
    • In personal life, individuals may reflect on this mantra when making significant life decisions, such as career changes or relationships, emphasizing the need for understanding and clarity.
    • In community gatherings, invoking Agni can symbolize unity and collective strength, as people come together to share knowledge and resources for mutual benefit.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of seeking wisdom and understanding in all aspects of life.
    • It serves as a reminder that true happiness and prosperity come from thoughtful actions and the right intentions.
    • In contemporary life, the principles embedded in this mantra can guide individuals and communities towards achieving their goals while fostering a sense of unity and purpose.
