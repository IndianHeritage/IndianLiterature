वेत्था हि निरृतीनां वज्रहस्त परिवृजम् |अहर-आहः शुन्ध्युः परिपदाम iva ||
vetthā hi nirṛtīnāṁ vajrahasta parivṛjam | ahar-ahaḥ śundhyuḥ paripadām iva ||
  • vetthā hi nirṛtīnāṁ: This phrase translates to "the one who knows the ways of the unyielding." Here, "vetthā" means "one who knows" or "knower," and "nirṛtīnāṁ" refers to "the unyielding" or "those who do not relent." In real life, this can be likened to individuals who possess deep wisdom and understanding of life's challenges, akin to a seasoned mentor or a wise elder who guides others through difficult times.
  • vajrahasta parivṛjam: This part means "with a hand like a thunderbolt." "Vajra" symbolizes strength and indestructibility, while "hasta" means "hand." This can be compared to a leader or a figure of authority who wields power with both strength and responsibility, much like a judge who makes tough decisions that can have lasting impacts on people's lives.
  • ahar-ahaḥ śundhyuḥ: This translates to "day after day." It emphasizes the continuity and persistence of actions or events. In everyday life, this can be seen in the dedication of individuals who work tirelessly towards their goals, such as a student studying diligently for exams or an athlete training consistently for a competition.
  • paripadām iva: This phrase means "as if in a circle." It suggests a cyclical nature of life and experiences. This can be related to the concept of karma, where actions have consequences that come back around, much like how a person’s good deeds can lead to positive outcomes in their life.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of wisdom, strength, persistence, and the cyclical nature of life. It signifies the importance of understanding life's challenges and facing them with strength and resilience. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire individuals to seek knowledge, act with integrity, and remain steadfast in their pursuits, recognizing that their actions will ultimately shape their future. It serves as a reminder that wisdom and strength are essential in navigating the complexities of life, and that every day presents an opportunity to grow and learn.
