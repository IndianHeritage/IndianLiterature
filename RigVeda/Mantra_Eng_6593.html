तद्वार्यं वृणीमहे वरिष्टं गोपयत्यं |मित्रो यत् पान्ति वरुणो यद् आर्यमाः ||
tad vāryaṁ vṛṇīmahe variṣṭhaṁ gopayatyam | mitro yat pānti varuṇo yad aryamā ||
  • tad vāryaṁ vṛṇīmahe: This phrase translates to "We choose that which is excellent." Here, "tad" means "that," "vāryaṁ" refers to "excellent" or "worthy," and "vṛṇīmahe" means "we choose" or "we select." This emphasizes the importance of making conscious choices in life, selecting what is beneficial and virtuous.
  • variṣṭhaṁ gopayatyam: This part means "the best protector." "Variṣṭhaṁ" translates to "the best" or "the highest," and "gopayatyam" refers to "one who protects." This can be interpreted as seeking the highest form of protection, which can be understood in various contexts, such as spiritual guidance, moral support, or even physical safety.
  • mitro yat pānti: This translates to "the friend who nourishes." "Mitro" means "friend," and "yat pānti" means "who nourishes" or "who sustains." This highlights the significance of friendship and companionship in our lives, emphasizing that true friends provide support and nourishment, both emotionally and spiritually.
  • varuṇo yad aryamā: This means "as Varuna, who is the upholder of cosmic order." "Varuṇo" refers to the deity Varuna, associated with water and cosmic order, while "yad aryamā" translates to "who is the upholder." This signifies the importance of maintaining harmony and balance in life, akin to the natural order of the universe.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of making wise choices, seeking protection, valuing friendships, and upholding cosmic order. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios such as:

  • In decision-making, one should strive to choose paths that are beneficial and aligned with higher values.
  • In relationships, it is essential to surround oneself with friends who provide support and nourishment, fostering a positive environment.
  • In community and societal roles, individuals can work towards maintaining harmony and balance, contributing to the greater good.

This mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of our choices, relationships, and the larger universe, encouraging us to live with intention and purpose.
