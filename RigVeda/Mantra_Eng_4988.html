तां योद्धिष्टम अभि गा इन्द्र नूनम अपः स्वर उषसो अग्न ऊष्हाḥ |दिशः स्वर उषसा इन्द्र चित्रा अपो गा अग्ने युवसे नियुत्वान ||
tā yodhiṣṭam abhi gā indra nūnam apaḥ svar uṣaso agna ūḻhāḥ | diśaḥ svar uṣasa indra citrā apo gā agne yuvase niyutvān ||
  • tā yodhiṣṭam abhi gā indra nūnam apaḥ svar uṣaso agna ūḻhāḥ
    • tā - that
    • yodhiṣṭam - the one who is steadfast or firm
    • abhi - towards
    • gā - to go or to move
    • indra - the deity Indra, often associated with strength and power
    • nūnam - indeed or surely
    • apaḥ - waters
    • svar - heaven or sky
    • uṣaso - of dawn
    • agna - fire
    • ūḻhāḥ - to illuminate or to shine
  • diśaḥ svar uṣasa indra citrā apo gā agne yuvase niyutvān
    • diśaḥ - directions
    • svar - heaven or sky
    • uṣasa - of dawn
    • indra - Indra, the powerful deity
    • citrā - colorful or diverse
    • apo - waters
    • gā - to go or to move
    • agne - O fire
    • yuvase - to you, the youthful
    • niyutvān - who moves swiftly

This mantra invokes the divine energies of Indra and Agni, emphasizing the importance of strength, illumination, and the vital role of water and fire in life. It suggests a connection between the heavens, the dawn, and the natural elements that sustain life.


In real life, this mantra can be seen as a reminder of the importance of resilience and steadfastness in the face of challenges. For example, when individuals face difficulties in their careers or personal lives, they can draw inspiration from the idea of being "yodhiṣṭam" or steadfast, much like a warrior who does not waver in battle.


The reference to "waters" and "fire" symbolizes the essential elements that nourish and sustain life. Just as water is crucial for survival and fire represents energy and transformation, this mantra encourages individuals to seek balance in their lives by nurturing both their emotional and physical well-being.


In conclusion, this mantra serves as a powerful invocation for strength, resilience, and the harmonious interplay of natural elements. It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, such as personal growth, overcoming obstacles, and fostering a deeper connection with nature. By embodying the qualities of Indra and Agni, individuals can navigate life's challenges with courage and clarity.
