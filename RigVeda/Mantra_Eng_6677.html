न देवानाम् अपि ह्नुतः सुमतिं न जुगक्षतः |श्रवो बृहद् विवासतः ||
na devānām api hnutaḥ sumatiṁ na jugukṣataḥ | śravo bṛhad vivāsataḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • na: not
    • devānām: of the gods
    • api: even
    • hnutaḥ: those who are sacrificed
    • sumatiṁ: good understanding or wisdom
    • na: not
    • jugukṣataḥ: those who are restrained or controlled
    • śravo: of fame or glory
    • bṛhad: great
    • vivāsataḥ: those who are liberated or free
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra suggests that even the gods do not attain wisdom through mere sacrifices.
    • It emphasizes that true understanding and wisdom cannot be achieved through superficial means or rituals.
    • The phrase "those who are restrained" indicates that control over one's desires and actions is essential for gaining true knowledge.
    • The mention of "great fame" implies that true liberation and recognition come from deeper understanding rather than external accolades.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern education, students who focus solely on rote learning (akin to mere sacrifices) often struggle to grasp concepts deeply, while those who engage critically with the material (showing restraint and control) tend to excel.
    • In the workplace, employees who seek recognition through superficial means (like showing off) may not achieve true respect, while those who demonstrate genuine skill and understanding earn lasting admiration.
    • In personal relationships, individuals who prioritize genuine communication and understanding over mere appearances foster deeper connections, illustrating the mantra's message about the importance of true wisdom.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of seeking true wisdom and understanding rather than relying on external rituals or superficial actions.
    • It teaches that control over one's desires and a focus on genuine knowledge lead to liberation and true recognition.
    • In real life, this principle can be applied in various fields such as education, professional development, and personal relationships, encouraging individuals to seek depth and authenticity in their pursuits.
