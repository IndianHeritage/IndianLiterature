मन्त्र: एं आशुम् आशवे भर यज्ञश्रियं नृमादनम् |पतयन मन्दयत्सखम् ||
em āśum āśave bhara yajñaśriyaṁ nṛmādanam | patayan mandayatsakham ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • em: This is an invocation or a call to the divine.
    • āśum: Refers to speed or swiftness.
    • āśave: Pertains to the one who is swift or powerful.
    • bhara: Means to fill or to bring forth.
    • yajñaśriyaṁ: Refers to the glory or wealth of sacrifice (yajña).
    • nṛmādanam: Refers to the joy or happiness of mankind.
    • patayan: Means to fall or to descend.
    • mandayatsakham: Refers to the friend or companion who is gentle or kind.
  • Overall Meaning:
    • This mantra is a prayer for the swift and powerful forces of nature to be filled with the glory of sacrifice, bringing joy and happiness to humanity.
    • It emphasizes the importance of sacrifice (yajña) in achieving prosperity and well-being.
    • The invocation calls upon the divine to ensure that these forces descend gently, like a kind friend, into our lives.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern life, this mantra can be related to the concept of teamwork and collaboration. Just as the forces of nature work together for the benefit of humanity, individuals can come together to achieve common goals.
    • In a corporate setting, when a team works harmoniously, contributing their unique skills (akin to yajña), they create a productive environment that fosters joy and success.
    • In personal relationships, the idea of being a gentle friend who supports others can lead to stronger bonds and a happier community.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for unity, sacrifice, and the gentle support of one another in the pursuit of happiness and prosperity.
    • It teaches us that by working together and supporting each other, we can create a positive impact in our lives and the lives of others.
    • This mantra is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal relationships to professional environments, reminding us of the power of collaboration and kindness.
