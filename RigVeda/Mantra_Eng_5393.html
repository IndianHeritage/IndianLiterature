महाम् उतासि यस्य ते 'नु स्वधावरī सहः | मम्नाते इन्द्र रोदसी ||
mahām̐ utāsi yasya te 'nu svadhāvarī sahaḥ | mamnāte indra rodasī ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • mahām̐: Great or powerful
    • utāsi: You have risen or you have come forth
    • yasya: Whose
    • te: Your
    • anu: Following or in accordance with
    • svadhāvarī: The essence of self or one's own nature
    • sahaḥ: Together or in union
    • mamnāte: May I be nourished or sustained
    • indra: Indra, the king of gods
    • rodasī: The two worlds or realms
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the powerful presence of Indra, the king of gods, emphasizing the importance of divine support in our lives.
    • The phrase "mahām̐ utāsi" signifies the arrival of great energy or strength, suggesting that we should seek and embrace powerful forces that uplift us.
    • "Svadhāvarī sahaḥ" indicates the importance of aligning with our true nature and essence, which can lead to harmony and balance in our lives.
    • The request for nourishment from Indra highlights the need for spiritual and emotional sustenance, which can be found through connection with the divine and the universe.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In times of personal struggle, individuals often seek strength from their beliefs or spiritual practices, similar to invoking Indra for support.
    • People may engage in meditation or yoga to connect with their true selves, aligning with the essence of "svadhāvarī" to find inner peace.
    • Communities often come together in rituals or gatherings to celebrate and seek blessings, reflecting the idea of collective nourishment and support.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the power of divine support and the importance of aligning with our true selves.
    • It encourages individuals to seek strength from higher powers and to nourish their spirits through connection with the universe.
    • In real life, this can manifest through spiritual practices, community support, and personal reflection, ultimately leading to a more balanced and fulfilling existence.
