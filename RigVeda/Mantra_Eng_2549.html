त्वां अग्नि मनिषिणः सम्राजं चर्षणीनाम् |देवम् मर्तास इन्द्रते सम अध्वरे ||
tvām agne manīṣiṇaḥ samrājaṁ carṣaṇīnām | devam martāsa indhate sam adhvare ||
  • tvām - This word translates to "you," referring to Agni, the fire god in Vedic literature.
  • agne - This is the vocative form of Agni, emphasizing the invocation of the fire deity.
  • manīṣiṇaḥ - This term means "wise" or "intelligent," indicating the revered qualities of Agni.
  • samrājaṁ - This translates to "the ruler" or "king," suggesting Agni's supreme authority among the deities.
  • carṣaṇīnām - This word refers to "the seers" or "the wise ones," highlighting the connection between Agni and those who possess knowledge.
  • devam - Meaning "divine" or "god," it reinforces Agni's status as a deity.
  • martāsa - This translates to "mortals," indicating the human beings who seek the blessings of Agni.
  • indhate - This means "to inspire" or "to enlighten," suggesting Agni's role in guiding and uplifting humanity.
  • sam - This word means "together" or "in harmony," indicating unity in worship and purpose.
  • adhvare - This translates to "in the sacrifice," referring to the rituals performed in honor of Agni.

The mantra can be interpreted as a prayer to Agni, the fire god, who is revered for his wisdom and authority. It acknowledges Agni as the ruler of the wise and seeks his blessings for enlightenment and guidance in the rituals performed by mortals.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various contexts:

  • Spiritual Practices: Individuals can recite this mantra during meditation or rituals to invoke the qualities of wisdom and guidance in their lives.
  • Leadership: Leaders can reflect on the qualities of Agni as a ruler and strive to embody wisdom and enlightenment in their decision-making processes.
  • Community Gatherings: In communal rituals or gatherings, this mantra can be chanted to foster unity and collective purpose among participants.

Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its invocation of Agni as a source of wisdom and guidance. It emphasizes the importance of seeking divine inspiration in our actions and decisions, particularly in communal and spiritual contexts. By recognizing Agni's role in our lives, we can cultivate a deeper connection to the divine and strive for enlightenment in our daily endeavors.
