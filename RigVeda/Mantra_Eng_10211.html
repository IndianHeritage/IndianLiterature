अहम् एव स्वयम् इदं वदामि जुष्टं देवैः उता मानवैः |यं कामये तं-तम् उग्रं कृत्नोमि तं ब्रह्माणं तं ऋषिं तं सुमेधाम् ||
aham eva svayam idaṁ vadāmi juṣṭaṁ devebhir uta mānuṣebhiḥ | yaṁ kāmaye taṁ-tam ugraṁ kṛṇomi tam brahmāṇaṁ tam ṛṣiṁ taṁ sumedhām ||
  • Aham eva svayam idaṁ vadāmi: This phrase translates to "I alone speak this." It emphasizes the speaker's authority and confidence in the knowledge being shared. In real life, this can be likened to a leader or an expert in a field who confidently shares their insights, knowing that their experience and understanding are valid.
  • Juṣṭaṁ devebhir uta mānuṣebhiḥ: This means "that which is pleasing to the gods and to humans." It highlights the importance of aligning one's actions and words with what is beneficial and acceptable to both divine and human realms. For example, a community leader who promotes sustainable practices is acting in a way that pleases both the environment (often seen as divine) and the people who depend on it.
  • Yaṁ kāmaye taṁ-tam ugraṁ kṛṇomi: This translates to "Whatever I desire, I create that powerful thing." It speaks to the power of intention and manifestation. In practical terms, this can be seen in entrepreneurs who visualize their goals and work diligently to bring their visions to life, demonstrating the strength of focused desire and effort.
  • Tam brahmāṇaṁ tam ṛṣiṁ taṁ sumedhām: This means "that which is the ultimate reality, that which is the sage, that which is the wise." It refers to the pursuit of higher knowledge and wisdom. In everyday life, this can be reflected in individuals who seek knowledge through education, meditation, or philosophical inquiry, striving to become more enlightened and wise in their decisions.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of self-assertion, the importance of aligning with higher principles, the power of intention, and the pursuit of wisdom. Its significance lies in encouraging individuals to speak their truth, act in harmony with the greater good, manifest their desires through focused effort, and seek knowledge and wisdom. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire leaders, educators, and individuals to strive for authenticity, ethical practices, and continuous personal growth, ultimately contributing to a more harmonious society.
