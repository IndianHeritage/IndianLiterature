अस्मे रुद्राः मेहनाः पर्वतासो वृत्रहत्ये भरहूतौ सजोषाः |यः शंसते स्तुवते धायि पज्र इन्द्रज्येष्ठा अस्मां अवन्तु देवाः ||
asme rudrā mehanā parvatāso vṛtrahatye bharahūtau sajoṣāḥ | yaḥ śaṁsate stuvate dhāyi pajra indrajyeṣṭhā asmām̐ avantu devāḥ ||
  • Mantra: "asme rudrā mehanā parvatāso vṛtrahatye bharahūtau sajoṣāḥ | yaḥ śaṁsate stuvate dhāyi pajra indrajyeṣṭhā asmām̐ avantu devāḥ ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • asme: for us
    • rudrā: O Rudra (a form of Shiva, representing storm and destruction)
    • mehanā: with great strength
    • parvatāso: from the mountains
    • vṛtrahatye: who destroys Vritra (the demon of drought)
    • bharahūtau: with great abundance
    • sajoṣāḥ: with joy
    • yaḥ: who
    • śaṁsate: praises
    • stuvate: worships
    • dhāyi: the giver
    • pajra: the strong
    • indrajyeṣṭhā: the best among the powerful
    • asmām̐: for us
    • avantu: may protect
    • devāḥ: the gods
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra invokes Rudra, a powerful deity associated with storms and destruction, asking for his strength and protection.
    • The reference to "vṛtrahatye" signifies the destruction of obstacles and negativity, akin to overcoming challenges in life.
    • The phrase "bharahūtau sajoṣāḥ" emphasizes the joy and abundance that comes from divine blessings, similar to the joy one feels when achieving personal goals or receiving support from loved ones.
    • By praising and worshiping the divine, as mentioned in "yaḥ śaṁsate stuvate," individuals can cultivate a sense of gratitude and connection to a higher power, which can be seen in practices like meditation or community worship.
    • The mantra concludes with a plea for protection from the gods, highlighting the importance of divine guidance in navigating life's challenges.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of seeking strength and support from divine forces in our lives.
    • It is applicable in real-life scenarios where individuals face challenges, such as personal struggles, health issues, or professional obstacles, reminding them to seek help and remain hopeful.
    • By invoking the blessings of the divine, individuals can foster resilience and a positive outlook, ultimately leading to personal growth and fulfillment.
