एष ग्रावेव जरीता त इन्द्र्यर्ति वाचं बृहद् आशुषाणः | प्र सव्येन मघवन यंसी रायाḥ प्र दक्षिणिद धरीवो मा वि वेनः ||
eṣa grāveva jaritā ta indreyarti vācam bṛhad āśuṣāṇaḥ | pra savyena maghavan yaṁsi rāyaḥ pra dakṣiṇid dharivo mā vi venaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • eṣa: this
    • grāveva: like a call or invocation
    • jaritā: the one who stirs or awakens
    • ta: that
    • indreyarti: the one who inspires or empowers
    • vācam: speech or voice
    • bṛhad: great or vast
    • āśuṣāṇaḥ: the one who brings forth or manifests
    • pra: forward or towards
    • savyena: with the left hand or direction
    • maghavan: the one who is rich or bountiful
    • yaṁsi: who
    • rāyaḥ: wealth or prosperity
    • pra: forward or towards
    • dakṣiṇid: with the right hand or direction
    • dhari: to hold or bear
    • vo: your
    • mā: not
    • vi: apart or away
    • venaḥ: to be separated or divided
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of a divine invocation that stirs the powers of inspiration and speech.
    • It emphasizes the importance of both the left and right directions, symbolizing balance and harmony in life.
    • The mention of "maghavan" refers to the abundance and richness that can be attained through proper invocation and alignment with divine energies.
    • The phrase "mā vi venaḥ" suggests a plea for unity and the avoidance of division, highlighting the importance of community and togetherness.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, this mantra can be applied to inspire teamwork and collaboration, ensuring that all voices are heard and valued.
    • In personal relationships, it can serve as a reminder to communicate openly and avoid misunderstandings, fostering a sense of unity.
    • In community gatherings, invoking this mantra can help create an atmosphere of abundance and shared prosperity, encouraging collective efforts towards common goals.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for unity, inspiration, and the manifestation of abundance.
    • It teaches us the importance of balance in our actions and thoughts, urging us to work together towards common objectives.
    • In real life, applying the principles of this mantra can lead to enriched relationships, successful collaborations, and a harmonious community.
