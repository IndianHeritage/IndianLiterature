मां त्वा तपत प्रिय आत्मापियन्तं मां स्वधितिस तन्बव1 आ तिष्ठिपत ते |मां ते गृध्नुर अविशस्तातिहाय चिद्रā गात्राण्य असिना मिथुं कः ||
mā tvā tapat priya ātmāpiyantam mā svadhitis tanva1 ā tiṣṭhipat te | mā te gṛdhnur aviśastātihāya chidrā gātrāṇy asinā mithū kaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • mā: not
    • tvā: you
    • tapat: to burn or to be heated
    • priya: dear or beloved
    • ātma: self or soul
    • piyantam: to be cherished or loved
    • mā: not
    • svadhitis: your own nature or essence
    • tanva: body or form
    • ā: towards
    • tiṣṭhipat: to stand or to remain
    • te: your
    • mā: not
    • te: your
    • gṛdhnur: to covet or desire
    • aviśastātihāya: without being disturbed or harmed
    • chidrā: holes or gaps
    • gātrāṇy: limbs or body parts
    • asinā: by a weapon or knife
    • mithū: together or mutually
    • kaḥ: who
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra expresses a plea for protection and preservation of the self and one's essence.
    • It emphasizes the importance of not allowing external forces to harm or disturb one's inner nature.
    • The reference to "not coveting" suggests a call for contentment and the avoidance of greed.
    • The imagery of "gaps in the body" and "being harmed by a weapon" symbolizes vulnerability and the need for safeguarding oneself from negative influences.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace setting, this mantra can be applied to maintain one's integrity and self-worth amidst competition and envy.
    • In personal relationships, it serves as a reminder to protect one's emotional well-being from toxic influences.
    • In spiritual practices, it encourages individuals to focus on their true essence and not be swayed by external judgments or desires.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for self-preservation and the importance of maintaining one's true nature.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, reminding individuals to stay grounded and not be influenced by negativity or greed.
    • Ultimately, it encourages a harmonious existence where one cherishes their essence while navigating the complexities of life.
