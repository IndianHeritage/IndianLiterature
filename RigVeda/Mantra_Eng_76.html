समोहे वा य आषत नरस्तोकस्य सानितौ |विप्रासो वा धियायवः ||
samohe vā ya āśata naras tokasya sanitau | viprāso vā dhiyāyavaḥ ||
  • Mantra: "samohe vā ya āśata naras tokasya sanitau | viprāso vā dhiyāyavaḥ ||"
  • Translation: This mantra can be translated as: "May those who are wise and learned, who are endowed with intelligence, be united in thought and action, and may they be blessed with prosperity."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • samohe: united, harmonious
    • vā: or
    • ya: who
    • āśata: are endowed, blessed
    • naras: men, people
    • tokasya: of prosperity, wealth
    • sanitau: in thought, in action
    • viprāso: wise, learned individuals
    • dhiyāyavaḥ: endowed with intelligence, wisdom
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of unity among individuals, particularly those who are wise and knowledgeable.
    • It suggests that when people come together with a common purpose and shared wisdom, they can achieve greater prosperity and success.
    • In real life, this can be seen in various scenarios such as teamwork in a corporate environment, where collaboration and shared knowledge lead to innovative solutions and business growth.
    • Another example is in community initiatives, where individuals with different skills and knowledge unite to address social issues, leading to positive change.
  • Significance:
    • The mantra highlights the value of collective intelligence and the strength that comes from unity.
    • It serves as a reminder that wisdom is not just an individual trait but can be amplified when shared among a group.
    • In today's world, where division and conflict are prevalent, this mantra encourages individuals to seek common ground and work together for mutual benefit.
    • It is applicable in various aspects of life, including personal relationships, professional settings, and community engagements, promoting harmony and shared success.
