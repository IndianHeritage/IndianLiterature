एष देवो अमर्त्यः पर्णवीर इव दीयति |अभि द्रोणान्य आसदाम् ||
eṣa devo amartyaḥ parṇavīr iva dīyati | abhi droṇāny āsadam ||
  • Mantra: "eṣa devo amartyaḥ parṇavīr iva dīyati | abhi droṇāny āsadam ||"
  • Translation: "This divine being, immortal like the Parṇavīr, bestows (gifts) abundantly; may he grant us a seat (or place) in the assembly."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • eṣa: This
    • devo: divine being or god
    • amartyaḥ: immortal
    • parṇavīr: like the Parṇavīr (a term often associated with strength or vitality)
    • dīyati: bestows or gives
    • abhi: towards or for
    • droṇāny: seats or places
    • āsadam: assembly or gathering
  • Contextual Explanation:
    • The mantra speaks of a divine being who is immortal and generous, akin to the strength and vitality represented by the Parṇavīr.
    • In real life, this can be likened to leaders or mentors who provide guidance and support, helping individuals find their place in society or a community.
    • For example, a teacher who nurtures students and helps them realize their potential embodies the essence of this mantra.
    • The reference to "granting a seat in the assembly" signifies the importance of inclusion and recognition in social or communal settings.
  • Significance:
    • This mantra emphasizes the value of divine support and the importance of community.
    • It encourages individuals to seek guidance from higher powers or mentors while also recognizing the need for belonging and acceptance in a group.
    • In practical terms, it can be applied in various scenarios such as workplaces, educational institutions, and social organizations where collaboration and support are essential for growth and success.
