अग्निर इषां सख्ये ददातु न ईशे यो वार्याणाम् |अग्निं टोके तनये शाश्वद इमहे वसुं संतं तनूपाम् ||
agnir iṣāṁ sakhye dadātu na īśe yo vāryāṇām | agniṁ toke tanaye śaśvad īmahe vasuṁ santaṁ tanūpām ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • agnir: Fire
    • iṣāṁ: of the desires
    • sakhye: in friendship
    • dadātu: may he give
    • na: not
    • īśe: to the lord
    • yo: who
    • vāryāṇām: of the strong
    • agniṁ: fire
    • toke: in offspring
    • tanaye: in children
    • śaśvad: always
    • īmahe: we meditate
    • vasuṁ: wealth
    • santaṁ: peaceful
    • tanūpām: of the body
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes Agni, the fire god, symbolizing purity, transformation, and the divine spark of life.
    • The first part expresses a desire for Agni to grant us our wishes in friendship, emphasizing the importance of community and support.
    • The second part focuses on the blessings of Agni in our offspring and family, highlighting the significance of lineage and continuity.
    • It reflects a yearning for wealth and peace, both materially and spiritually, suggesting that true wealth comes from harmony and well-being.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern society, this mantra can be related to the importance of building strong relationships and networks, akin to how friendships and alliances can lead to personal and professional success.
    • In family life, the emphasis on blessings for children resonates with the universal desire for a prosperous and harmonious family environment.
    • The pursuit of peace and wealth can be seen in various cultural practices, such as rituals and celebrations that honor family and community, fostering a sense of belonging and shared prosperity.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of our desires, relationships, and the blessings we seek in life.
    • It highlights the significance of nurturing friendships, valuing family, and striving for peace and prosperity.
    • In real life, it encourages us to cultivate positive relationships and seek harmony in our pursuits, ultimately leading to a fulfilling and enriched life.
