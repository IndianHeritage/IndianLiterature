अर्वाम् इव श्रवसे सातिमच्छेन्द्रस्य वायोः अभि वीतीं अर्षा |स नः सहस्राः बृहतीर् इषो दा भव Soma द्रविणोवित पुनानः ||
arvām̐ iva śravase sātim acchendrasya vāyor abhi vītim arṣa | sa naḥ sahasrā bṛhatīr iṣo dā bhavā soma draviṇovit punānaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • arvām̐ - like the vastness
    • iva - as if
    • śravase - of fame or glory
    • sātim - strength or power
    • acchendrasya - of the unbroken or undivided
    • vāyor - of the wind
    • abhi - towards
    • vītim - the limit or boundary
    • arṣa - of the seer or sage
    • sa - may he
    • naḥ - our
    • sahasrā - thousands
    • bṛhatīr - of great or vast
    • iṣo - of the arrow or aim
    • dā - give
    • bhavā - may you become
    • soma - the divine nectar or moon
    • draviṇovit - of wealth or abundance
    • punānaḥ - purifying or cleansing
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the vastness and glory of the universe, likening it to the strength of the wind and the wisdom of the sages.
    • It emphasizes the importance of seeking strength and abundance in life, akin to aiming for a target with precision.
    • The reference to "soma" symbolizes the divine essence that nourishes and purifies, suggesting a connection to spiritual wealth and well-being.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In personal development, one might strive for greatness and clarity in their goals, much like aiming for a target with a well-aimed arrow.
    • In community building, the idea of sharing wealth and resources can be seen in initiatives that promote collective well-being, reflecting the essence of "soma" as a purifying force.
    • In environmental conservation, the vastness of nature and the strength of the wind can inspire efforts to protect and sustain our planet, recognizing the interconnectedness of all life.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of aiming for strength, abundance, and purity in our lives.
    • Its significance lies in the encouragement to seek wisdom and clarity in our pursuits, while also fostering a sense of community and environmental responsibility.
    • In essence, it calls for a harmonious balance between personal aspirations and collective well-being, making it applicable in various aspects of life, from personal growth to social responsibility.
