मायाभिरुत्सिसृप्सत इन्द्र द्यां आरुरुक्षतः |अव दस्यूम् अधूनुथाः ||
māyābhir utsisṛpsata indra dyām ārurukṣataḥ | ava dasyūm̐r adhūnuthāḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • māyābhir: By illusions or through the power of illusion.
    • utsisṛpsata: They (the forces or beings) are striving or moving forward.
    • indra: Indra, the king of gods, often associated with strength and power.
    • dyām: The sky or the heavens.
    • ārurukṣataḥ: They are ascending or rising.
    • ava: Down or away.
    • dasyūm̐r: The enemies or obstacles.
    • adhūnuthāḥ: They are being driven away or removed.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the power of Indra, who is depicted as overcoming illusions and obstacles.
    • The phrase "māyābhir utsisṛpsata" suggests that despite the illusions that may cloud judgment or reality, there is a movement towards clarity and truth.
    • Indra's ascent into the heavens symbolizes the pursuit of higher knowledge and understanding, transcending the mundane.
    • The latter part of the mantra, "ava dasyūm̐r adhūnuthāḥ," emphasizes the removal of obstacles, which can be interpreted as the triumph over challenges in life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In personal development, individuals often face self-doubt and illusions about their capabilities. This mantra can inspire one to rise above these doubts, much like Indra rising to the heavens.
    • In a professional context, teams may encounter challenges that seem insurmountable. The essence of this mantra encourages collaboration and the collective effort to overcome these obstacles.
    • In spiritual practices, the mantra can serve as a reminder to seek clarity and truth, pushing through the distractions and illusions of daily life.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of overcoming illusions and obstacles through strength and determination.
    • It serves as a powerful reminder that, like Indra, we have the ability to rise above challenges and achieve higher understanding.
    • In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios, from personal growth to professional challenges, encouraging individuals to strive for clarity and success.
