मन्त्र: अस्ति हि वः सजात्यं ऋशादसो देवासो अस्त्य आप्यम् |प्र णः पूर्वस्मै सुविताय वोचत मक्षू सुम्नाय नव्यसे ||
asti hi vaḥ sajātyaṁ riśādaso devāso asty āpyam | pra ṇaḥ pūrvasmai suvitāya vocata makṣū sumnāya navyase ||
  • asti hi vaḥ sajātyaṁ: This phrase translates to "Indeed, there is a unity among you." It emphasizes the importance of community and togetherness. In real life, this can be seen in various social groups, such as families, teams, or organizations, where collaboration and mutual support lead to success.
  • riśādaso devāso asty āpyam: This means "the divine beings are present and accessible." It suggests that the divine or spiritual presence is always around us, ready to guide and support us. For example, in moments of crisis or decision-making, many people turn to prayer or meditation, seeking guidance from a higher power.
  • pra ṇaḥ pūrvasmai: This translates to "to the one who is before us." It signifies respect for those who have come before us, such as ancestors or mentors. In real life, this can be reflected in traditions where people honor their forebears, learning from their experiences and wisdom.
  • suvitāya vocata: This means "speak to the well-being." It encourages communication that promotes health and prosperity. In practice, this can be seen in positive affirmations and supportive conversations that uplift individuals and communities.
  • makṣū sumnāya navyase: This translates to "for the new beginnings." It signifies the importance of fresh starts and new opportunities. In real life, this can be related to various life events such as starting a new job, moving to a new place, or embarking on a new relationship, where one seeks blessings for success and happiness.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of unity, divine presence, respect for the past, positive communication, and the significance of new beginnings. Its application in real life is profound, as it encourages individuals to foster community, seek spiritual guidance, honor their roots, communicate positively, and embrace new opportunities. By embodying these principles, one can lead a more harmonious and fulfilling life.
