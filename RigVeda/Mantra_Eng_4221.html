तां वाम इयानोऽवसे पूर्वा उप ब्रुवे सचा |स्वश्वासः सु चेतुना वाजाम् अभि प्र दावने ||
tā vām iyāno 'vase pūrvā upa bruve sacā | svaśvāsaḥ su cetunā vājām̐ abhi pra dāvane ||
  • tā vām iyāno 'vase pūrvā upa bruve sacā
    • tā - that
    • vām - you
    • iyāno - may I approach
    • 'vase - in your control
    • pūrvā - the earlier
    • upa - near
    • bruve - I speak
    • sacā - with truth
  • svaśvāsaḥ su cetunā vājām̐ abhi pra dāvane
    • svaśvāsaḥ - with our own breath
    • su - good
    • cetunā - with thought
    • vājām̐ - of strength
    • abhi - towards
    • pra - to
    • dāvane - the offering

This mantra can be interpreted as a prayer or invocation where the speaker seeks to approach a higher power or divine presence with sincerity and truth. The speaker expresses a desire to be in control and to communicate effectively, emphasizing the importance of truthfulness in their intentions.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios:

  • Personal Growth: Just as the speaker seeks to approach the divine with truth, individuals can strive for honesty in their personal development. For example, when setting goals, being truthful about one's strengths and weaknesses can lead to more effective planning and execution.
  • Communication: The emphasis on speaking with truth can be applied in everyday conversations. For instance, in a workplace setting, clear and honest communication fosters trust and collaboration among team members.
  • Mindfulness: The reference to "svaśvāsaḥ" (with our own breath) highlights the importance of being present and mindful. Practicing mindfulness can enhance one's ability to focus and make thoughtful decisions, whether in personal relationships or professional endeavors.

Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for sincerity, control, and mindful communication. By embodying these principles, individuals can enhance their relationships, foster personal growth, and contribute positively to their communities. The mantra serves as a reminder that approaching life with truth and intention can lead to greater fulfillment and harmony.
