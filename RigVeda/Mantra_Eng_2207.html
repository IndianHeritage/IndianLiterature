सो अङ्गिरसाम् उचथाऽजुजुष्वान् ब्रह्मा तूतोद इन्द्रो गातुम् इष्णन् |मुष्णन्न् उषसः सूर्येण स्तवान् अशनस्य चिच्छिष्णथत् पूर्व्याणि ||
so aṅgirasām ucathā jujuṣvān brahmā tūtod indro gātum iṣṇan | muṣṇann uṣasaḥ sūryeṇa stavān aśnasya cic chiśnathat pūrvyāṇi ||
  • so aṅgirasām ucathā: This phrase can be interpreted as "thus, from the Angiras (a group of sages or seers), I speak." It emphasizes the importance of wisdom and knowledge passed down through generations.
  • jujuṣvān brahmā: This translates to "the one who desires the ultimate reality or Brahman." It signifies the pursuit of spiritual truth and understanding, akin to how individuals seek purpose and meaning in their lives.
  • tūtod indro gātum iṣṇan: Here, "Indra" refers to the king of gods, and "gātum iṣṇan" means "to go forth." This can be seen as a call to action, encouraging individuals to strive for greatness and leadership in their own lives, much like how leaders inspire their communities.
  • muṣṇann uṣasaḥ sūryeṇa: This phrase means "seizing the dawn with the sun." It symbolizes new beginnings and opportunities, similar to how each day presents a chance for renewal and growth.
  • stavān aśnasya: This translates to "the one who praises the eater." It can be interpreted as recognizing and honoring those who contribute to the sustenance of life, akin to how we appreciate the efforts of farmers and providers in our society.
  • cic chiśnathat pūrvyāṇi: This means "may you attain the ancient knowledge." It highlights the importance of learning from the past and applying that wisdom to present challenges, much like how historical lessons inform modern decision-making.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda encapsulates the essence of seeking knowledge, striving for greatness, and honoring the contributions of others. Its significance lies in the encouragement to pursue spiritual and personal growth while recognizing the interconnectedness of all beings. In real life, this mantra can inspire individuals to lead with purpose, appreciate the efforts of those around them, and learn from history to navigate the complexities of modern life. It serves as a reminder that wisdom and action are essential in creating a meaningful existence.
