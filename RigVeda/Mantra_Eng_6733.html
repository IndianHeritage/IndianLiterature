अस्माकम् अद्यान्तमं स्तोमं धिष्व महामह | अस्माकं ते सवना सन्तु शान्तमाऽ मदाय द्युक्ष सोमपाः ||
asmākam adyāntamaṁ stomaṁ dhiṣva mahāmaha | asmākaṁ te savanā santu śaṁtamā madāya dyukṣa somapāḥ ||
  • Translation: "Let our hymn today be powerful; may it be great and glorious. May our offerings be pleasing to you, O Soma, and may they bring us peace and joy."

  • Word Breakdown:
    • asmākam: Our
    • adyāntamaṁ: Today
    • stomaṁ: Hymn or praise
    • dhiṣva: Be powerful or strong
    • mahāmaha: Great and glorious
    • asmākaṁ: Our
    • te: To you
    • savanā: Offerings
    • santu: May they be
    • śaṁtamā: Peaceful
    • madāya: For joy or delight
    • dyukṣa: May they bring
    • somapāḥ: O Soma (a deity associated with the moon and intoxicating drink)

  • Contextual Explanation:
    • This mantra is a prayer for strength and glory in the hymns offered to the divine. It emphasizes the importance of making offerings that are not only powerful but also bring peace and joy.
    • In real life, this can be likened to the efforts we put into our work or personal projects. Just as the mantra seeks to make the hymn powerful, individuals strive to make their contributions impactful and meaningful.
    • The reference to Soma highlights the importance of joy and celebration in our lives. Just as Soma is associated with nourishment and delight, we should seek to find joy in our daily activities and relationships.

  • Significance:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the power of words and intentions. It encourages individuals to focus on creating positive and impactful contributions in their lives.
    • In a broader sense, it reflects the human desire for connection with the divine and the importance of rituals in fostering a sense of community and shared purpose.
    • In practical terms, this mantra can be applied in various life scenarios, such as during personal achievements, community gatherings, or even in professional settings where teamwork and collaboration are essential.
