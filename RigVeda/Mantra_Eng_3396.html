प्रवता हि क्रतूनाम् आ हा पदेव गच्छसि |अभक्षि सूर्ये सचा ||
pravatā hi kratūnām ā hā padeva gacchasi | abhakṣi sūrye sacā ||
  • Mantra: "pravatā hi kratūnām ā hā padeva gacchasi | abhakṣi sūrye sacā ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • pravatā: flowing or moving forward
    • hi: indeed or surely
    • kratūnām: of the sacrifices or intentions
    • ā: towards or to
    • hā: a call or invocation
    • padeva: like a footstep or path
    • gacchasi: you go or you move
    • abhakṣi: not consuming or not taking
    • sūrye: in the sun or sunlight
    • sacā: with truth or in truth
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks to the idea of moving forward with intention and purpose.
    • The phrase "pravatā hi kratūnām" suggests that the flow of one's intentions or sacrifices is essential for progress.
    • The invocation "ā hā padeva gacchasi" indicates a call to follow a path that is illuminated by truth and sincerity.
    • The latter part, "abhakṣi sūrye sacā," emphasizes the importance of not consuming or taking from the sun, which can be interpreted as a metaphor for living in harmony with nature and truth.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In personal development, this mantra can inspire individuals to pursue their goals with clear intentions, much like setting a path in life that is guided by their values.
    • In environmental practices, the idea of not consuming from the sun can relate to sustainable living, where one respects natural resources and seeks to live in balance with the environment.
    • In community building, the mantra encourages collective efforts where intentions are aligned towards a common goal, fostering unity and purpose.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of intentional living and the pursuit of truth in all actions.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal growth to environmental sustainability and community engagement.
    • By embodying the principles of this mantra, individuals and communities can create a more harmonious and purposeful existence.
