अच्छा वो अग्निम अवसे देवं गासि स नो वसुः |रासत पुत्र ऋषूणाम ऋतावा पर्षति द्विषः ||
acchā vo agnim avase devaṁ gāsi sa no vasuḥ | rāsat putra ṛṣūṇām ṛtāvā parṣati dviṣaḥ ||
  • acchā vo agnim avase devaṁ gāsi sa no vasuḥ
    • acchā: This word can be interpreted as "good" or "auspicious." It sets a positive tone for the mantra.
    • vo: This means "to you," indicating a direction of reverence towards the divine.
    • agnim: Refers to "Agni," the fire god, symbolizing energy, transformation, and purification.
    • avase: This translates to "may we invoke" or "may we call upon," suggesting a plea for divine presence.
    • devaṁ: Meaning "the divine" or "god," it emphasizes the spiritual aspect of the invocation.
    • gāsi: This means "sing" or "praise," indicating an act of devotion and acknowledgment of the divine.
    • sa no vasuḥ: Translates to "may he bring us wealth," where "vasuḥ" refers to prosperity and abundance.
  • rāsat putra ṛṣūṇām ṛtāvā parṣati dviṣaḥ
    • rāsat: This can mean "from the essence" or "from the core," suggesting a deep connection to the fundamental truths.
    • putra: Meaning "son," it symbolizes lineage, continuity, and the importance of family.
    • ṛṣūṇām: Refers to "the sages" or "the wise ones," indicating respect for knowledge and wisdom.
    • ṛtāvā: This translates to "the one who is righteous" or "truthful," emphasizing the importance of righteousness in life.
    • parṣati: Meaning "to protect" or "to guard," it suggests a divine safeguarding of the righteous.
    • dviṣaḥ: This means "from enemies" or "from those who wish harm," indicating a plea for protection from negativity.

Conclusion:

This mantra is a powerful invocation for divine presence, prosperity, and protection. It emphasizes the importance of righteousness, wisdom, and the connection to the divine. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied during rituals, prayers, or personal meditations to seek guidance and blessings. For instance, individuals may recite this mantra during significant life events such as weddings, new ventures, or when facing challenges, to invoke positive energy and divine support. Its significance lies in fostering a sense of connection to the divine, promoting inner peace, and encouraging a life of righteousness and abundance.
