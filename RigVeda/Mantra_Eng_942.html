मन्त्र: शूरा इवेद युयुधयो न जग्मयः श्रवस्यवो न पृतनासु यतिरे | भयन्ते विश्वा भुवना मरुद्भ्यो राजान इव त्वेषसंदृशो नरः ||
śūrā ived yuyudhayo na jagmayaḥ śravasyavo na pṛtanāsu yetire | bhayante viśvā bhuvanā marudbhyo rājāna iva tveṣasaṁdṛśo naraḥ ||
  • śūrā ived yuyudhayo na jagmayaḥ: This phrase translates to "The brave ones engage in battle, they do not retreat." It emphasizes the valor and courage of individuals who face challenges head-on. In real life, this can be seen in soldiers who fight for their country, athletes who compete fiercely, or anyone who stands up for their beliefs despite adversity.
  • śravasyavo na pṛtanāsu yetire: This means "Those who are renowned do not falter in their endeavors." It highlights the importance of perseverance and determination in achieving greatness. For example, successful entrepreneurs like Steve Jobs or Elon Musk faced numerous failures but continued to pursue their visions, ultimately leading to significant achievements.
  • bhayante viśvā bhuvanā marudbhyo: This translates to "All worlds are fearful of the mighty winds." It signifies the power of nature and the forces that can instill fear. In real life, this can be related to natural disasters like hurricanes or earthquakes, which remind humanity of its vulnerability and the need for respect towards nature.
  • rājāna iva tveṣasaṁdṛśo naraḥ: This means "A man who is like a king is seen in his strength." It suggests that true leadership is demonstrated through strength and resilience. In contemporary society, leaders in various fields—be it politics, business, or community service—who exhibit strength in decision-making and moral integrity are often revered and followed.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of bravery, perseverance, respect for nature, and the qualities of true leadership. In real life, these principles can be applied in various scenarios, such as facing personal challenges, pursuing professional goals, respecting the environment, and embodying leadership qualities in our communities. By embracing these values, individuals can contribute positively to society and inspire others to do the same.
