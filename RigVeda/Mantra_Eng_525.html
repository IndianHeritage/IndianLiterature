सवितारं उषसम् अश्विनां भागं अग्निं व्युष्टिषु क्षपः |कण्वासस् त्वा सुतसोमास इन्द्रते हव्यवाहं स्वध्वर ||
savitāram uṣasam aśvinā bhagam agniṁ vyuṣṭiṣu kṣapaḥ | kaṇvāsas tvā sutasomāsa indhate havyavāhaṁ svadhvara ||
  • Mantra: "savitāram uṣasam aśvinā bhagam agniṁ vyuṣṭiṣu kṣapaḥ | kaṇvāsas tvā sutasomāsa indhate havyavāhaṁ svadhvara ||"
  • Translation: This mantra invokes the divine energies of Savitar, the Sun God, and the Aśvins, the twin deities associated with health and medicine. It seeks blessings for prosperity and well-being, represented by Agni, the fire god, and emphasizes the importance of offerings (havyavāha) to the divine.
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • Savitāram: Referring to Savitar, the Sun God, symbolizing illumination and life.
    • Uṣasam: Dawn, representing new beginnings and hope.
    • Aśvinā: The Aśvins, divine horsemen, embodying health and vitality.
    • Bhagam: Blessings or prosperity.
    • Agniṁ: Agni, the fire god, symbolizing transformation and purification.
    • Vyuṣṭiṣu: In the context of offerings or rituals.
    • Kṣapaḥ: Night or darkness, representing the end of ignorance.
    • Kaṇvāsas: A reference to the divine or sacred.
    • Sutasomāsa: Those who are blessed with Soma, a drink of immortality.
    • Indhate: To invoke or call upon.
    • Havyavāhaṁ: The one who carries offerings to the gods.
    • Svadhvara: The one who is self-illuminated or self-sustaining.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, the invocation of Savitar can be seen in the practice of morning rituals, where individuals seek the blessings of the Sun for health and prosperity.
    • The Aśvins are often called upon in Ayurvedic practices, where their association with health is invoked for healing and wellness.
    • Agni represents the transformative power of fire, which can be related to cooking, where food is transformed and purified through fire, symbolizing nourishment and sustenance.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its holistic approach to life, emphasizing the interconnectedness of health, prosperity, and divine blessings. It serves as a reminder to seek illumination and guidance from the divine forces in our daily lives. By invoking these energies, individuals can cultivate a sense of well-being and purpose, applicable in various aspects of life, from personal health to community welfare.
