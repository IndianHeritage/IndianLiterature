उत मे 'रपद युवतिर ममन्दुशी प्रति श्यावाय वर्तनिम् |वि रोहितā पुरुमीळ्हाय येमतुर विप्राय दीर्घयशसे ||
uta me 'rapad yuvatir mamanduṣī prati śyāvāya vartanim | vi rohitā purumīḻhāya yematur viprāya dīrghayaśase ||
  • uta me 'rapad yuvatir mamanduṣī prati śyāvāya vartanim
    • uta - This word can be interpreted as "and" or "also," indicating a connection or addition.
    • me - Translates to "my," indicating possession or personal connection.
    • 'rapad - Refers to "the path" or "the way," symbolizing guidance or direction in life.
    • yuvatir - Means "young" or "youthful," often associated with vitality and energy.
    • mamanduṣī - This can be understood as "my desires" or "my aspirations," reflecting personal goals.
    • prati - Translates to "towards" or "in the direction of," indicating movement or intention.
    • śyāvāya - Refers to "dark" or "black," which can symbolize challenges or obstacles.
    • vartanim - Means "I am moving" or "I am progressing," indicating action and forward movement.
  • vi rohitā purumīḻhāya yematur viprāya dīrghayaśase
    • vi - This word means "in" or "within," suggesting an internal or intrinsic quality.
    • rohitā - Refers to "red" or "bright," often symbolizing strength, passion, or vitality.
    • purumīḻhāya - Can be interpreted as "to the great one" or "to the powerful," indicating respect or reverence.
    • yematur - Means "who is wise" or "who has knowledge," reflecting the importance of wisdom in life.
    • viprāya - Refers to "the learned" or "the wise," emphasizing the value of education and intellect.
    • dīrghayaśase - Translates to "long-lasting glory" or "enduring fame," indicating the desire for a legacy or lasting impact.

Conclusion:

This mantra encapsulates the journey of life, emphasizing the importance of personal aspirations, the challenges faced, and the pursuit of wisdom and glory. It reflects the duality of youth and the obstacles that may arise, while also highlighting the significance of knowledge and respect for the wise. In real life, this mantra can be applied to various scenarios, such as:

  • Setting personal goals and striving towards them despite challenges.
  • Valuing education and the wisdom of others in decision-making processes.
  • Understanding the importance of leaving a positive legacy through one's actions and contributions to society.

Ultimately, this mantra serves as a reminder to embrace the journey of life with determination, seek knowledge, and aspire for a lasting impact.
