युवाभ्यां वाजिनीवासू प्रति स्तोमा अदृक्षत |वाचं दूतो यथोहिषे ||
yuvābhyāṁ vājinīvasū prati stomā adṛkṣata | vācaṁ dūto yathohiṣe ||
  • yuvābhyāṁ: This word translates to "two young ones" or "two youthful beings." In a broader context, it can symbolize the potential and energy of youth, representing new beginnings and fresh perspectives.
  • vājinīvasū: This term can be interpreted as "the one who possesses strength" or "the powerful." It signifies vigor, vitality, and the ability to overcome challenges.
  • prati: This word means "towards" or "in the direction of." It indicates movement or intention towards a goal or purpose.
  • stomā: This refers to "praises" or "hymns." It highlights the importance of expression, communication, and the sharing of positive sentiments.
  • adṛkṣata: This translates to "they beheld" or "they observed." It suggests awareness, perception, and the act of witnessing something significant.
  • vācaṁ: This means "speech" or "voice." It emphasizes the power of words and communication in shaping our reality.
  • dūto: This term means "messenger." It represents the idea of conveying messages, whether they are personal, spiritual, or communal.
  • yathohiṣe: This can be interpreted as "as it is desired" or "according to one's wish." It reflects the alignment of intentions with actions and outcomes.

In summary, this mantra speaks to the dynamic interplay between youth, strength, communication, and intention. It emphasizes the importance of being aware of our surroundings and the messages we convey through our words and actions. The imagery of two youthful beings suggests collaboration and partnership, while the notion of a messenger highlights the significance of sharing knowledge and intentions.


Significance and Real-Life Application:

  • This mantra can be applied in various real-life scenarios, such as teamwork in professional settings where collaboration and communication are key to success.
  • In personal relationships, it serves as a reminder of the importance of expressing our thoughts and feelings clearly, ensuring that our intentions align with our actions.
  • In educational contexts, it encourages the sharing of knowledge and the nurturing of young minds, fostering an environment where ideas can flourish.
  • On a spiritual level, it can inspire individuals to be mindful of their words and the impact they have on others, promoting a culture of positivity and support.

Ultimately, this mantra encourages us to harness the energy of youth and strength, communicate effectively, and act with intention, leading to a more harmonious and fulfilling life.
