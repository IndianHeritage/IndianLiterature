अश्याम तं कामम् अग्ने तवोती अश्याम रयिं रयिवः सुवीरम् |अश्याम वाजम् अभि वाजयन्तो अश्याम द्युम्नम् अजराजरं ते ||
aśyāma taṁ kāmam agne tavotī aśyāma rayiṁ rayivaḥ suvīram | aśyāma vājam abhi vājayanto 'śyāma dyumnam ajarājaraṁ te ||
  • Mantra: "aśyāma taṁ kāmam agne tavotī aśyāma rayiṁ rayivaḥ suvīram | aśyāma vājam abhi vājayanto 'śyāma dyumnam ajarājaraṁ te ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • aśyāma: dark, black, or mysterious
    • taṁ: that
    • kāmam: desire or wish
    • agne: O Agni (the fire god)
    • tavotī: your (of you)
    • rayiṁ: wealth or prosperity
    • rayivaḥ: like wealth
    • suvīram: good hero or valiant
    • vājam: strength or power
    • abhi: towards or for
    • vājayanto: those who bring strength
    • dyumnam: glory or fame
    • ajarājaraṁ: imperishable or eternal
    • te: your
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Agni, the fire god, who symbolizes energy, transformation, and the divine spark within us.
    • The repetition of "aśyāma" suggests a focus on the mysterious and profound aspects of desire and wealth.
    • The mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking not just material wealth but also the strength and valor that come from spiritual and moral integrity.
    • In real life, this can be related to the pursuit of goals where one seeks not only success but also the qualities that make one a good leader or a hero in their community.
    • For example, a business leader may strive for financial success (rayiṁ) but should also cultivate qualities like courage (suvīram) and integrity (dyumnam) to inspire their team.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its holistic approach to success, urging individuals to balance material desires with ethical and moral values.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, such as in leadership, personal development, and community service, where the focus should be on building a legacy that is both prosperous and virtuous.
    • Ultimately, this mantra serves as a reminder that true wealth encompasses not just material gains but also the strength of character and the impact one has on others.
