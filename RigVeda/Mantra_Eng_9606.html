भुरन्तु नो यशसः सोत्व अन्धसो ग्रावाणो वाचा दिविता दिवितमता |नरो यत्र दूहते काम्यम् मध्वाघोषयन्तो अभितो मिथस्तुरः ||
bhurantu no yaśasaḥ sotv andhaso grāvāṇo vācā divitā divitmatā | naro yatra duhate kāmyam madhv āghoṣayanto abhito mithasturaḥ ||
  • bhurantu no yaśasaḥ: This phrase can be translated as "may our fame and glory increase." It emphasizes the importance of reputation and recognition in society. In real life, this can be seen in the context of individuals striving for excellence in their careers, such as a student aiming for high grades or an athlete training hard to win competitions.
  • sotv andhaso grāvāṇo vācā: This part means "may we be free from darkness and speak with clarity." It highlights the value of knowledge and communication. For example, in a workplace, clear communication can lead to better teamwork and project success, while ignorance can lead to misunderstandings and failures.
  • divitā divitmatā: This translates to "may we attain wealth and prosperity." It reflects the aspiration for material and spiritual abundance. In everyday life, this can relate to financial stability, such as saving for a home or investing in education for a better future.
  • naro yatra duhate kāmyam: This means "where men fulfill their desires." It speaks to the pursuit of goals and aspirations. For instance, in a community, people may come together to support each other in achieving their dreams, such as starting a business or pursuing artistic endeavors.
  • madhv āghoṣayanto: This translates to "enjoying the sweetness of life." It suggests the importance of savoring life's pleasures and joys. In real life, this can be seen in moments of celebration, such as family gatherings, festivals, or personal achievements that bring happiness.
  • abhito mithasturaḥ: This phrase means "may we be surrounded by good company." It emphasizes the significance of community and relationships. In practice, this can be observed in the support systems we build, such as friendships, family ties, and professional networks that uplift and encourage us.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a holistic approach to life, emphasizing the pursuit of fame, knowledge, wealth, fulfillment of desires, enjoyment of life, and the importance of community. Its significance lies in guiding individuals towards a balanced and prosperous life. In real-life scenarios, it encourages people to strive for excellence, communicate effectively, seek prosperity, cherish relationships, and enjoy the journey of life. By embodying these principles, one can create a fulfilling and meaningful existence.
