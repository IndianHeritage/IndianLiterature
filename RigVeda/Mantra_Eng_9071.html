देवानाम् माने प्रथमाऽतिष्ठन् कृतान्त्रादेषाम् उपराद् उदायन् |त्रयस्तपन्ति पृथिवीमनूपाद्वा बृबूकं वहतः पुरिषम् ||
devānām māne prathamā atiṣṭhan kṛntatrād eṣām uparā ud āyan | trayas tapanti pṛthivīm anūpā dvā bṛbūkaṁ vahataḥ purīṣam ||
  • devānām māne prathamā atiṣṭhan: This phrase translates to "The first among the gods stands in honor." It signifies the importance of divine presence and the respect that should be accorded to higher powers. In real life, this can be related to the respect we give to leaders or mentors in our lives, acknowledging their guidance and wisdom.
  • kṛntatrād eṣām uparā ud āyan: This means "They who cut through the obstacles rise above." It emphasizes overcoming challenges and rising to new heights. For example, in a professional setting, an individual who faces setbacks but continues to strive for success embodies this principle by breaking through barriers to achieve their goals.
  • trayas tapanti pṛthivīm anūpā: This translates to "The three (elements) heat the earth." This can be interpreted as the fundamental forces or elements that sustain life and energy on Earth. In a practical sense, this could refer to the balance of nature, such as the sun, water, and air, which are essential for agriculture and sustaining life.
  • dvā bṛbūkaṁ vahataḥ purīṣam: This means "Two carry the burden of the city." This can symbolize teamwork and collaboration in society. In everyday life, this can be seen in community efforts where individuals come together to support a common cause, such as volunteering for local charities or working in teams to achieve a project goal.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of divine respect, overcoming challenges, the importance of natural elements, and the value of teamwork. Its significance lies in its applicability to various aspects of life, encouraging individuals to honor their mentors, strive against obstacles, appreciate the natural world, and work collaboratively with others. In a world that often presents challenges, this mantra serves as a reminder of the strength found in unity and the divine guidance that supports our endeavors.
