प्र ते वोकाम वीर्याः या मंदसाना आरुजः |पुरो दासीर अभीत्य ||
pra te vocāma vīryā3 yā mandasāna ārujaḥ | puro dāsīr abhītya ||
  • Mantra: "pra te vocāma vīryā3 yā mandasāna ārujaḥ | puro dāsīr abhītya ||"
  • Translation: "We call upon your strength, O one who is seated in the assembly; may the servants of the house be free from fear."

  • Word-by-Word Explanation:
    • pra: A prefix meaning "forth" or "outward," indicating a call or invocation.
    • te: "Your," referring to the divine or a higher power.
    • vocāma: "We call" or "we invoke," suggesting a plea or request.
    • vīryā: "Strength" or "power," often associated with valor and courage.
    • yā: "Who," referring to the subject of the invocation.
    • mandasāna: "Seated in the assembly," indicating a position of authority or importance.
    • ārujaḥ: "One who is elevated" or "one who rises," suggesting a higher state of being or consciousness.
    • puro: "In front" or "before," indicating a place of prominence.
    • dāsīr: "Servants" or "attendants," referring to those who serve in a household or community.
    • abhītya: "Free from fear," indicating a state of safety and security.

  • Real-Life Examples:
    • This mantra can be applied in a leadership context where a leader invokes the strength and support of their team to overcome challenges.
    • In a community gathering, this invocation can be used to foster a sense of unity and courage among members, encouraging them to face difficulties together.
    • In personal life, one might recite this mantra during times of stress or fear, seeking strength and reassurance from a higher power or inner self.

  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of invoking strength and courage, not just for oneself but for the community and those who serve.
    • Its significance lies in the recognition of collective strength and the need for support in overcoming fears and challenges.
    • In real-life scenarios, it serves as a reminder to seek help and to empower others, fostering a sense of safety and unity in various aspects of life.
