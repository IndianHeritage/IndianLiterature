यस्य त्वं ऊर्ध्वो अध्वराय तिष्ठसि क्षयद्वीरः स साधते |सो अर्वद्भिः सानितā स विपण्युभिः स शूरैः सानितā कृतम् ||
yasya tvam ūrdhvo adhvarāya tiṣṭhasi kṣayadvīraḥ sa sādhate | so arvadbhiḥ sanitā sa vipanyubhiḥ sa śūraiḥ sanitā kṛtam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yasya: of whom
    • tvam: you
    • ūrdhvo: upward
    • adhvarāya: to the sacrifice
    • tiṣṭhasi: you stand
    • kṣayadvīraḥ: the one who is a hero in destruction
    • sa: he
    • sādhate: accomplishes
    • so: he
    • arvadbhiḥ: with the brave
    • sanitā: is accompanied
    • sa: he
    • vipanyubhiḥ: with the powerful
    • sa: he
    • śūraiḥ: with the strong
    • sanitā: is accompanied
    • kṛtam: done
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of a divine presence that stands firm and strong in the face of challenges.
    • The phrase "you stand upward to the sacrifice" suggests a readiness to engage in spiritual or personal endeavors that require effort and commitment.
    • The term "kṣayadvīraḥ" refers to a hero who is capable of overcoming destruction, symbolizing resilience and strength in adversity.
    • The mantra emphasizes the importance of companionship with the brave and powerful, indicating that success is often achieved through collaboration and support from others.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, a leader who stands firm during a crisis and inspires their team to work together can be seen as embodying the qualities described in this mantra.
    • In personal life, individuals facing challenges such as illness or loss may find strength in the support of friends and family, reflecting the idea of being accompanied by the brave and strong.
    • In community service, volunteers who come together to support a cause demonstrate the power of collective action, resonating with the mantra's emphasis on collaboration.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of resilience, collaboration, and the strength found in community.
    • It serves as a reminder that in both personal and collective endeavors, standing firm and supporting one another can lead to significant accomplishments.
    • In real life, applying the principles of this mantra can help individuals and groups navigate challenges effectively, fostering a sense of unity and purpose.
