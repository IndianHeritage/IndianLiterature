अग्निं विश्वा अभि पृक्षः साचन्ते समुद्रं न स्रवतः सप्त यह्वीः |न जामिभिर वि चिकीते वयो नो विदा देवेषु प्रमतिं चिकीत्वान ||
agniṁ viśvā abhi pṛkṣaḥ sacante samudraṁ na sravataḥ sapta yahvīḥ | na jāmibhir vi cikite vayo no vidā deveṣu pramatiṁ cikitvān ||
  • Agniṁ viśvā abhi pṛkṣaḥ: This phrase refers to Agni, the fire god, who is considered the universal energy or the divine force that pervades all existence. In real life, Agni symbolizes transformation and purification, much like how fire transforms materials through combustion.
  • Sacante samudraṁ na sravataḥ sapta yahvīḥ: This part speaks of the seven streams or currents that flow into the ocean, representing the various paths of life and the interconnectedness of all beings. In a practical sense, this can be likened to the different cultures, ideas, and experiences that converge in a globalized world, enriching our collective existence.
  • Na jāmibhir vi cikite vayo: This line suggests that the divine or the higher consciousness is not limited by human understanding or perception. It emphasizes the importance of seeking knowledge beyond conventional wisdom. For example, in scientific exploration, breakthroughs often come from questioning established norms and venturing into the unknown.
  • No vidā deveṣu pramatiṁ cikitvān: This phrase highlights the need for awareness and mindfulness in our actions and thoughts. It encourages individuals to be conscious of their decisions and their impact on the world around them. In everyday life, this can be seen in sustainable practices, where individuals and communities strive to make choices that benefit the environment and society.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking knowledge, understanding the interconnectedness of life, and the transformative power of divine energy. Its significance lies in encouraging individuals to pursue wisdom, embrace diversity, and act mindfully in their daily lives. In a world that often feels fragmented, this mantra serves as a reminder of our shared humanity and the importance of unity in diversity.
