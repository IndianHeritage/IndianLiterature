सुमङ्गलीर इयं वधूर इमां समेता पश्यत |सौभाग्यम अस्यै दत्त्वायाथास्तं विपरेतन ||
sumaṅgalīr iyaṁ vadhūr imāṁ sameta paśyata | saubhāgyam asyai dattvāyāthāstaṁ vi paretana ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sumaṅgalīr: auspicious, bringing good fortune
    • iyaṁ: this
    • vadhūr: bride, woman
    • imāṁ: this
    • sameta: together, united
    • paśyata: may you see, may you perceive
    • saubhāgyam: good fortune, prosperity
    • asyai: to her
    • dattvā: having given
    • yāthā: as it is, in the same manner
    • astaṁ: to the setting, to the end
    • vi: in, with
    • paretana: the other, the external
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a blessing for a bride, wishing her auspiciousness and good fortune.
    • It emphasizes the importance of unity and perception in relationships, particularly in marriage.
    • The phrase "may you see" suggests that the bride should be aware of the blessings and opportunities around her.
    • By giving her good fortune, it implies that the community or family plays a role in her happiness and success.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a wedding ceremony, this mantra can be recited to invoke blessings for the bride, ensuring she embarks on her new journey with positivity.
    • In family gatherings, sharing this mantra can foster a sense of unity and support among family members, reinforcing the idea that collective blessings contribute to individual success.
    • In personal development, individuals can reflect on the mantra's message to recognize and appreciate the good fortune in their lives, encouraging a mindset of gratitude and awareness.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its emphasis on auspiciousness and the collective support of family and community in a person's life.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, especially during significant life events like weddings, where blessings and good wishes are paramount.
    • Ultimately, it serves as a reminder that our perceptions and the support we receive from others play a crucial role in our journey towards happiness and success.
