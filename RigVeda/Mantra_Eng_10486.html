आ त्वाहर्षम् अन्तर एधि ध्रुवस तिष्ठाविचाचलिः |विशस त्वा सर्वा वाञ्छन्तु मा त्वद राष्ट्रम् अधि भ्रशत ||
ā tvāhārṣam antar edhi dhruvas tiṣṭhāvicācaliḥ | viśas tvā sarvā vāñchantu mā tvad rāṣṭram adhi bhraśat ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā: from
    • tvā: you
    • hārṣam: joy, happiness
    • antar: within
    • edhi: may you reside
    • dhruvas: stable, firm
    • tiṣṭhā: stand, remain
    • vicācaliḥ: unshaken, unwavering
    • viśas: may you be known
    • tvā: you
    • sarvā: all
    • vāñchantu: may desire
    • mā: not
    • tvad: your
    • rāṣṭram: realm, kingdom
    • adhi: above
    • bhraśat: fall, decline
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes a sense of stability and joy, asking for the presence of happiness to reside within.
    • It emphasizes the importance of being unwavering and firm in one's principles and actions.
    • The latter part of the mantra expresses a desire for all beings to be fulfilled in their aspirations, while also wishing that the realm or kingdom does not fall into decline.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace setting, this mantra can be applied to foster a positive environment where employees feel stable and joyful, leading to increased productivity.
    • In personal relationships, it encourages individuals to remain steadfast and supportive, ensuring that the bonds do not weaken over time.
    • In community leadership, it serves as a reminder for leaders to maintain a strong and stable governance that supports the aspirations of all community members.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for inner joy and stability, which are essential for personal and communal well-being.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal relationships to professional environments, emphasizing the need for unwavering support and fulfillment of aspirations.
    • By embodying the principles of this mantra, individuals and communities can work towards a harmonious existence, ensuring that their realms do not fall into decline.
