यो मृळयाति चक्रुषे चिदागो वयं स्याम वरुणे अनागाः |अनु व्रतान्य अदितेर ऋधन्तो युयम पात स्वस्तिभिः सदा नः ||
yo mṛḻayāti cakruṣe cid āgo vayaṁ syāma varuṇe anāgāḥ | anu vratāny aditer ṛdhanto yūyam pāta svastibhiḥ sadā naḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yo: who
    • mṛḻayāti: brings to life or revives
    • cakruṣe: you have made or created
    • cid: consciousness or awareness
    • āgo: the past or what has been
    • vayaṁ: we
    • syāma: may we be
    • varuṇe: to Varuna (the deity of cosmic order)
    • anāgāḥ: free from sin or guilt
    • anu: following or in accordance with
    • vratāny: vows or commitments
    • aditer: of Aditi (the mother of gods)
    • ṛdhanto: flourishing or prosperous
    • yūyam: you (plural)
    • pāta: protect or safeguard
    • svastibhiḥ: with auspiciousness or well-being
    • sadā: always
    • naḥ: for us
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the divine presence of Varuna, who represents the cosmic order and moral authority.
    • It emphasizes the importance of living a life free from sin and adhering to one's commitments and vows.
    • The phrase "may we be free from sin" suggests a desire for purity and righteousness in our actions.
    • By seeking protection from Varuna, the mantra highlights the need for divine guidance in maintaining harmony and well-being in life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, individuals may face moral dilemmas where they must choose between right and wrong. This mantra serves as a reminder to seek guidance and strive for righteousness.
    • In a community setting, leaders can use the principles of this mantra to promote ethical behavior and collective well-being, ensuring that their actions align with the greater good.
    • During significant life events, such as weddings or new ventures, invoking this mantra can symbolize a commitment to uphold values and seek blessings for a prosperous journey.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for moral integrity and the pursuit of a life aligned with cosmic order.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal decision-making to community leadership, emphasizing the importance of ethical conduct and divine guidance.
    • By reciting this mantra, individuals can cultivate a sense of responsibility towards themselves and society, fostering a harmonious and prosperous existence.
