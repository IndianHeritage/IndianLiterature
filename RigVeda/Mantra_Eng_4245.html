पुरूरुणा चिद्ध्यस्त्यवो नूनं वां वरुण |मित्र वंसी वां सुमतिम् ||
purūruṇā cid dhy asty avo nūnaṁ vāṁ varuṇa | mitra vaṁsi vāṁ sumatim ||
  • Word Breakdown:
    • purūruṇā: This term can be interpreted as "with great effort" or "with abundant strength." It signifies the importance of putting in significant effort to achieve one's goals.
    • cid: This word means "indeed" or "certainly," emphasizing certainty in the statement that follows.
    • dhy: This can be understood as "meditation" or "contemplation," indicating a state of focused thought or reflection.
    • asty: Meaning "there is" or "exists," it asserts the presence of something significant.
    • avo: This word translates to "O," often used as a form of address or invocation.
    • nūnaṁ: This means "certainly" or "indeed," reinforcing the certainty of the statement.
    • vāṁ: This is a form of "you," indicating a direct address to the divine or a higher power.
    • varuṇa: Varuṇa is a deity associated with cosmic order and moral authority, representing the principles of truth and law.
    • mitra: This term means "friend" and is often associated with harmony and companionship.
    • vaṁsi: This can be interpreted as "you who are" or "you who belong to," indicating a connection to the divine.
    • sumatim: Meaning "good thought" or "wisdom," it refers to the pursuit of knowledge and understanding.
  • Meaning and Interpretation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of effort and contemplation in achieving wisdom and understanding.
    • It invokes Varuṇa, the deity of cosmic order, suggesting that one should seek guidance from higher principles in their endeavors.
    • The mention of "friend" (Mitra) indicates the value of companionship and support in the pursuit of knowledge.
    • Overall, the mantra encourages individuals to engage in deep thought and reflection, while also recognizing the importance of divine guidance and friendship.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, this mantra can be applied to teamwork. Just as the mantra emphasizes the importance of friendship and support, successful projects often rely on collaboration and mutual respect among team members.
    • In personal development, individuals can reflect on their goals and the efforts required to achieve them. The mantra serves as a reminder to meditate on one's intentions and seek wisdom from trusted mentors or spiritual guides.
    • In educational contexts, students can embody the principles of this mantra by engaging in deep study and seeking knowledge while also fostering friendships that support their learning journey.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of effort, contemplation, and the pursuit of wisdom in life.
    • It serves as a reminder to seek guidance from higher principles and to value the support of friends and companions.
    • In real-life scenarios, applying the teachings of this mantra can lead to personal growth, successful collaborations, and a deeper understanding of one's purpose.
