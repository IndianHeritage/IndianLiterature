तां पूषञ्च शिवतमाम् एरयस्व यस्यां बीजम् मनुष्याः वपन्ति |या न ऊरू उषती विश्रयाते यस्यां उषन्तः प्रहराम शेपम् ||
tām pūṣañ chivatamām erayasva yasyām bījam manuṣyā3 vapanti | yā na ūrū uśatī viśrayāte yasyām uśantaḥ praharāma śepam ||
  • tām pūṣañ chivatamām: This phrase can be translated as "O Pusha, the most nourishing one." Pusha is a deity associated with nourishment and prosperity. In real life, this can be related to the importance of seeking sustenance and support from nature and the universe, much like how we rely on food and resources for our well-being.
  • erayasva: This means "may you come forth" or "may you manifest." It signifies a call for the divine to reveal its presence and blessings in our lives. For example, when we seek guidance in difficult times, we often hope for a sign or support from a higher power.
  • yasyām bījam manuṣyā3 vapanti: This translates to "in which the seeds of humanity are sown." It emphasizes the connection between the divine and human existence. Just as seeds need nurturing to grow, humans require care and support to thrive. This can be seen in community efforts to support education and health, ensuring that future generations can flourish.
  • yā na ūrū uśatī viśrayāte: This means "where the strong do not suffer." It highlights the idea of strength and resilience. In real life, this can be related to the importance of building a supportive environment where individuals can thrive without fear of oppression or hardship, such as in a healthy workplace or community.
  • yasyām uśantaḥ praharāma śepam: This translates to "where the peaceful ones are protected." It signifies the need for safety and security for those who promote peace and harmony. In contemporary society, this can be reflected in the protection of human rights activists and those who work towards social justice, ensuring they can continue their vital work without fear.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the interconnectedness of nourishment, strength, and peace in human life. It calls upon divine support for the growth and protection of humanity. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied to community building, social justice efforts, and the pursuit of a harmonious existence. By recognizing the importance of nurturing our environment and supporting one another, we can create a society where everyone has the opportunity to thrive and live peacefully.
