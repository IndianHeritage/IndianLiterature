यम आस कृतपनीळम् भासाकेतुं वर्धयन्ति |भ्राजते श्रेणिदान ||
yam āsā kṛpanīḻam bhāsāketuṁ vardhayanti | bhrājate śreṇidan ||
  • yam: This word translates to "whom" or "that which." It indicates a subject or a person of focus.
  • āsā: This means "hope" or "expectation." It signifies the aspirations or desires that one holds.
  • kṛpanīḻam: This term can be interpreted as "the one who is in a state of poverty" or "the one who is miserly." It reflects a condition of lack or scarcity.
  • bhāsāketuṁ: This can be broken down into "bhāsā," meaning "light" or "radiance," and "ketuṁ," which refers to "flag" or "banner." Together, it symbolizes a beacon of hope or enlightenment.
  • vardhayanti: This means "they increase" or "they promote." It indicates the act of nurturing or enhancing something.
  • bhrājate: This translates to "shines" or "illuminates." It signifies the act of radiating or being prominent.
  • śreṇidan: This term can be understood as "the one who is in a state of prosperity" or "the one who is wealthy." It represents abundance and success.

  • Meaning of the Mantra: The mantra speaks about the transformative power of hope and enlightenment. It suggests that those who nurture their hopes and aspirations, even in the face of scarcity or poverty, will eventually shine and attain prosperity.
  • Real-Life Example: Consider an individual who is struggling financially but holds onto the hope of starting a small business. By nurturing this hope and working diligently towards it, they may eventually find success and prosperity, illuminating their life and the lives of those around them.

  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its encouragement to maintain hope and strive for improvement, regardless of current circumstances. It teaches that nurturing one's aspirations can lead to personal growth and success. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various situations, such as pursuing education, starting a new venture, or overcoming personal challenges. It serves as a reminder that hope and effort can lead to a brighter future.
