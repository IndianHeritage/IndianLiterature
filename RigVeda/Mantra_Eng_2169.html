न क्षोणीभ्यां परिभवे त इन्द्रियं न समुद्रैः पर्वतैर इन्द्र ते रथः |न ते वज्रम अन्व अश्नोति कश्चन यद आशुभिः पतसि योजनां पुरु ||
na kṣoṇībhyām paribhve ta indriyaṁ na samudraiḥ parvatair indra te rathaḥ | na te vajram anv aśnoti kaś cana yad āśubhiḥ patasi yojanā puru ||
  • na kṣoṇībhyām paribhve ta indriyaṁ: This phrase can be translated to "May your senses not be overwhelmed by the earth." Here, "kṣoṇī" refers to the earth, and "indriyaṁ" refers to the senses. It suggests the importance of maintaining control over one's senses and not being swayed by worldly distractions.
  • na samudraiḥ parvatair indra te rathaḥ: This translates to "Your chariot is not obstructed by the oceans or mountains." "Samudraiḥ" means oceans, and "parvatair" means mountains. This line emphasizes the idea that true strength and determination can overcome any obstacles, whether they are physical or metaphorical.
  • na te vajram anv aśnoti kaś cana: This means "No one can seize your thunderbolt." "Vajram" refers to a thunderbolt, symbolizing power and strength. This line conveys the idea that one's inner strength and power are invulnerable to external forces.
  • yad āśubhiḥ patasi yojanā puru: This translates to "When you soar above the evil, you become great." "Āśubhiḥ" refers to evil or negativity, and "yojanā puru" suggests the act of rising above. This line encourages individuals to transcend negativity and challenges to achieve greatness.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of self-control, resilience, and inner strength. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios such as facing challenges in personal or professional life. For instance, when dealing with stress or distractions, one can remember to maintain focus and not let external circumstances dictate their emotions or actions. The mantra serves as a reminder that true power lies within, and by rising above negativity, individuals can achieve their goals and aspirations. It encourages a mindset of perseverance and strength, applicable in everyday situations, from academic challenges to personal relationships.
