प्र घा nv अस्य महतो महानि सत्याः सत्यस्य करणानि वोचम् |त्रिकद्रुकेष्व अपिबत सुतस्यास्य मदे अहिम इन्द्रो जग्हान ||
pra ghā nv asya mahato mahāni satyā satyasya karaṇāni vocam | trikadrukeṣv apibat sutasyāsya made ahim indro jaghāna ||
  • pra ghā nv asya mahato mahāni: This phrase can be translated as "Let us speak of the great and mighty." Here, "pra" signifies a call to action or invocation, while "mahato" refers to greatness or vastness. In real life, this can be seen as a call to recognize and appreciate the significant aspects of life, such as nature, relationships, and achievements.
  • satyā satyasya karaṇāni vocam: This translates to "I speak of the truths and the actions of truth." The repetition of "satyā" emphasizes the importance of truthfulness. In everyday life, this can be related to the value of honesty in personal and professional relationships, where truth serves as the foundation for trust and integrity.
  • trikadrukeṣv apibat sutasyāsya made: This can be interpreted as "In the three realms, let us partake of the essence of this truth." The "three realms" may refer to the physical, mental, and spiritual dimensions of existence. This suggests that truth should permeate all aspects of life, encouraging individuals to seek balance and harmony across these dimensions.
  • ahim indro jaghāna: This phrase means "Indra, the lord of strength, has vanquished the adversary." Indra symbolizes power and protection. In a real-life context, this can be seen as a metaphor for overcoming challenges and adversities through strength and resilience, much like how individuals face personal struggles or societal issues.

  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of truth, strength, and the recognition of greatness in life. It serves as a reminder to uphold honesty in our actions, seek balance across different aspects of our existence, and draw upon our inner strength to overcome challenges. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as personal development, leadership, and community building, where truth and strength are essential for growth and harmony.
