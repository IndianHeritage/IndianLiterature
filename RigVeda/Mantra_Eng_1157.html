सुपर्णा एता आसते मध्य आरोधने दिवः |ते सेधन्ति पथो वृकम् तरन्तं यःवतीर अपो वित्तम् मे अस्य रोडसी ||
suparṇā eta āsate madhya ārodhane divaḥ | te sedhanti patho vṛkaṁ tarantaṁ yahvatīr apo vittam me asya rodasī ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • suparṇā: Well-winged or auspicious birds.
    • eta: These.
    • āsate: Sit or reside.
    • madhya: In the middle.
    • ārodhane: In the ascent or elevation.
    • divaḥ: Of the sky or heaven.
    • te: They.
    • sedhanti: They support or uphold.
    • patho: The paths or ways.
    • vṛkaṁ: The wolf or predator.
    • tarantaṁ: Crossing or overcoming.
    • yahvatīr: Those who are strong or powerful.
    • apo: Waters.
    • vittam: Wealth or resources.
    • me: My.
    • asya: This.
    • rodasī: The two worlds or realms.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of auspicious birds that reside in the middle of the sky, symbolizing divine guidance and protection.
    • The mention of paths suggests the various journeys we undertake in life, and the support we receive from the divine in navigating these paths.
    • The reference to overcoming predators (vṛkaṁ) can be seen as a metaphor for overcoming challenges and adversities in life.
    • The strong (yahvatīr) represent those who possess the strength to face life's challenges, while the waters (apo) symbolize the flow of resources and wealth that can aid in this journey.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, the mantra can be applied to teamwork, where individuals support each other (the birds) in achieving common goals (the paths).
    • In personal life, it can represent the strength one finds in family and friends when facing difficulties (overcoming the wolf).
    • In spiritual practice, it can signify the guidance one receives from higher powers or the universe in navigating life's complexities.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of divine support and guidance in our lives, encouraging us to seek strength and resources to overcome challenges.
    • It serves as a reminder that we are not alone in our journeys; we have the support of the universe and those around us.
    • In essence, it teaches us to trust in the process of life, to seek help when needed, and to recognize the strength within ourselves and our communities.
