इमा ब्रह्म सरस्वती जुषस्व वाजिनीवति |या ते मनमा गृत्समदा ऋतावरी प्रियā देवेषु जुह्वति ||
imā brahma sarasvati juṣasva vājinīvati | yā te manma gṛtsamadā ṛtāvari priyā deveṣu juhvati ||
  • imā brahma sarasvati juṣasva vājinīvati - This line can be translated as "O Sarasvati, may these offerings be pleasing to you, the embodiment of Brahman." Here, Sarasvati is revered as the goddess of knowledge, music, art, wisdom, and learning. The term "Brahman" refers to the ultimate reality or cosmic spirit in Hindu philosophy.
  • yā te manma gṛtsamadā ṛtāvari priyā deveṣu juhvati - This translates to "May those who are dear to you, O goddess, who are endowed with truth and righteousness, be the ones who offer to the divine." This line emphasizes the importance of truth and righteousness in one's actions and offerings to the divine.

  • Significance: This mantra highlights the importance of knowledge and wisdom in our lives. Sarasvati, as the goddess of learning, represents the pursuit of knowledge and the importance of education. The invocation of her blessings signifies the need for clarity of thought and purity of intention in our endeavors.
  • Real-life Application: In today's world, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as:
    • Education: Students can recite this mantra before exams or while studying to seek blessings for clarity and understanding.
    • Creative Pursuits: Artists and musicians may invoke Sarasvati's blessings to inspire creativity and enhance their skills.
    • Professional Life: In the workplace, individuals can use this mantra to promote ethical practices and truthfulness in their professional dealings.

  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a reminder of the significance of knowledge, truth, and righteousness in our lives. By invoking the blessings of Sarasvati, we are encouraged to pursue wisdom and act with integrity, which ultimately leads to personal and collective growth. In a world filled with distractions and misinformation, this mantra is a guiding light, urging us to seek knowledge and uphold truth in all our endeavors.
