जघन्वां इन्द्र मित्रेरूञ् चोदप्रवृद्धो हरिवो अदाशून् |प्र ये पश्यन्न आर्यमणं सचायोस त्वया शूर्ता वहमानā अपत्यं ||
jaghanvām̐ indra mitrerūñ codapravṛddho harivo adāśūn | pra ye paśyann aryamaṇaṁ sacāyos tvayā śūrtā vahamānā apatyam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • jaghanvām̐: We are invoking or calling upon.
    • indra: Indra, the king of gods, associated with rain and thunderstorms.
    • mitrerūñ: As a friend or ally.
    • codapravṛddho: Inspired or motivated.
    • harivo: The divine beings or gods.
    • adāśūn: Those who are swift or quick.
    • pra: For or towards.
    • ye: Those who.
    • paśyann: Seeing or perceiving.
    • aryamaṇaṁ: The noble or revered one.
    • sacāyos: With your strength or power.
    • tvayā: By you.
    • śūrtā: The strong or valiant ones.
    • vahamānā: Carrying or bearing.
    • apatyam: Offspring or progeny.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a call to Indra, the powerful deity, to inspire and motivate us as friends and allies.
    • It emphasizes the importance of divine support in our endeavors, particularly in the pursuit of strength and success.
    • The mention of "seeing" suggests awareness and perception, indicating that we should be mindful of our actions and their consequences.
    • It also highlights the role of strength and valor in achieving our goals, as we seek to carry forward our lineage or legacy.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, this mantra can be applied when teams come together to achieve a common goal, invoking the spirit of collaboration and support.
    • In personal life, it can be seen in the context of family, where the strength of relationships and shared values help in nurturing the next generation.
    • In sports, athletes often call upon their inner strength and the support of their teammates, akin to invoking divine assistance for victory.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of divine inspiration and support in our lives.
    • It teaches us to recognize the strength within ourselves and the value of community and relationships.
    • In real-life scenarios, whether in personal, professional, or communal settings, invoking such strength and support can lead to success and fulfillment.
