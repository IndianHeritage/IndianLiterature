अध त्वष्टा ते मह उग्र वज्रं सहस्रभृष्टिं ववृतच छताश्रिम् |निकामम अरमणसं येन नवन्तम अहिं सम पिणग ऋजीषिन ||
adha tvaṣṭā te maha ugra vajraṁ sahasrabhṛṣṭiṁ vavṛtac chatāśrim | nikāmam aramaṇasaṁ yena navantam ahiṁ sam piṇag ṛjīṣin ||
  • Adha tvaṣṭā te maha ugra vajraṁ: This phrase can be translated as "O Tvaṣṭā, the great and powerful one, wielding the thunderbolt." Tvaṣṭā is a divine craftsman and architect in Vedic mythology, symbolizing creativity and the power of creation. In real life, this can be likened to the role of engineers and architects who design and build structures that shape our environment.
  • Sahasrabhṛṣṭiṁ vavṛtac chatāśrim: This translates to "who has manifested a thousand forms of strength." This can be interpreted as the diverse capabilities and strengths that individuals possess. For example, a community may come together, each member contributing their unique skills—be it in healthcare, education, or technology—to achieve a common goal.
  • Nikāmam aramaṇasaṁ: This means "by which the unyielding and steadfast are made." It emphasizes the importance of resilience and determination. In everyday life, this can be seen in individuals who face challenges, such as students overcoming academic difficulties or athletes training rigorously to achieve their goals.
  • Yena navantam ahiṁ sam piṇag ṛjīṣin: This can be interpreted as "by which the newly born are nurtured and protected." This highlights the significance of care and protection in nurturing new life, akin to how parents and communities support children as they grow. For instance, educational programs and mentorship initiatives play a crucial role in guiding the youth towards a successful future.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of creation, strength, resilience, and nurturing. It signifies the interconnectedness of various roles in society, from creators and builders to nurturers and protectors. In real life, this mantra can inspire individuals to recognize their unique contributions and the importance of collaboration in achieving collective goals. Whether in personal endeavors, community projects, or professional environments, the principles embodied in this mantra encourage us to harness our strengths and support one another in the journey of life.
