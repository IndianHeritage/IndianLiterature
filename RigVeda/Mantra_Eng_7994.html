आ पवस्व महीम इषं गोमद इन्दो हिरण्यवत् |अश्वावद वाजवत सुतः ||
ā pavasva mahīm iṣaṁ gomad indo hiraṇyavat | aśvāvad vājavat sutaḥ ||
  • ā pavasva: This phrase can be interpreted as "come forth" or "approach." It signifies an invitation or a call to a divine presence or energy.
  • mahīm: This word means "greatness" or "majesty." It refers to the vastness of the universe and the power of the divine.
  • iṣaṁ: This translates to "the essence" or "the power." It denotes the fundamental energy or force that drives creation and existence.
  • gomad: This term means "rich in cows" or "wealthy." In the Vedic context, cows symbolize prosperity and abundance.
  • indo: This word refers to "Indra," the king of gods in Vedic mythology, representing strength, power, and the ability to overcome obstacles.
  • hiraṇyavat: This means "golden" or "radiant." It symbolizes wealth, purity, and the divine light that guides and protects.
  • aśvāvad: This translates to "like horses." Horses in Vedic culture symbolize speed, strength, and vitality.
  • vājavat: This means "like a chariot" or "powerful." It signifies the ability to move forward with strength and purpose.
  • sutaḥ: This word means "son" or "offspring." It represents the continuation of lineage, knowledge, and values through generations.

The mantra as a whole can be understood as a call to the divine for the manifestation of greatness, power, and abundance in life. It invokes the essence of prosperity and strength, asking for the blessings of wealth and vitality, much like the blessings of Indra, who is known for his might and ability to bestow riches.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as:

  • Personal Growth: When seeking to achieve personal goals, one can recite this mantra to invoke the energy of strength and abundance, encouraging a mindset of prosperity.
  • Business Ventures: Entrepreneurs may use this mantra to attract success and wealth in their endeavors, aligning their efforts with the divine forces of abundance.
  • Spiritual Practices: In meditation or prayer, this mantra can serve as a reminder of the interconnectedness of all beings and the importance of seeking divine guidance in one's life journey.

Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its ability to connect individuals with the divine energies of strength, prosperity, and abundance. By invoking these qualities, one can enhance their personal and professional lives, fostering a sense of purpose and fulfillment. It serves as a reminder that we are part of a larger universe, and by aligning ourselves with its energies, we can achieve our highest potential.
