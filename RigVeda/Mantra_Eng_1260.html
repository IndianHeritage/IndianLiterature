मन्त्र: दिवो वराहं अरुषं कपर्दिनं त्वेषं रूपं नमसां न ह्वयामहे | हस्ते बिभ्रद् भेषजाः वार्याणि शर्मा वर्म च्छर्दिर अस्मभ्यं यंषत ||
divo varāham aruṣaṁ kapardinaṁ tveṣaṁ rūpaṁ namasā ni hvayāmahe | haste bibhrad bheṣajā vāryāṇi śarma varma cchardir asmabhyaṁ yaṁsat ||
  • Translation: The mantra can be translated as: "We invoke the divine Varaha, the red one with matted hair, with this form, we bow down. May he, who holds the healing herbs in his hands, grant us protection and shelter."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • Divo: Of the heavens or divine.
    • Varāham: Varaha, a divine boar, symbolizing strength and protection.
    • Aruṣaṁ: The red one, often associated with vitality and energy.
    • Kapardinaṁ: One with matted hair, representing asceticism and spiritual power.
    • Tveṣaṁ: This form, indicating the specific manifestation of the divine.
    • Namasā: With reverence or bowing down.
    • Hvāyāmahe: We invoke or call upon.
    • Haste: In the hands.
    • Bibhrad: Holding or carrying.
    • Bheṣajā: Healing herbs or medicines.
    • Vāryāṇi: Protective or safeguarding.
    • Śarma: Shelter or comfort.
    • Varma: Armor or protection.
    • Cchardir: Granting or bestowing.
    • Asmabhyaṁ: To us or for us.
    • Yaṁsat: Who is worthy or deserving.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In times of illness, people often seek healing through natural remedies, much like invoking the healing herbs mentioned in the mantra.
    • During challenging situations, individuals may turn to spiritual practices or rituals for protection and guidance, reflecting the essence of seeking shelter and safety.
    • In various cultures, the symbolism of animals, such as the boar, is used to represent strength and resilience, which can be seen in totemic practices or in sports teams adopting animal mascots.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its invocation of divine protection and healing. It emphasizes the importance of seeking strength and safety in both physical and spiritual realms. In real life, this mantra can be applicable in various scenarios, such as during health crises, personal challenges, or when one seeks guidance and support. By recognizing the divine presence in our lives, we can cultivate a sense of security and well-being, drawing upon the strength of the universe to navigate our journeys.
