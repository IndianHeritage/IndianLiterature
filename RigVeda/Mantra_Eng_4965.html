इमं च नो गवेषणं सातये सीषधो गणम् |आरात पूषन्न असि श्रुतः ||
imaṁ ca no gaveṣaṇaṁ sātaye sīṣadho gaṇam | ārāt pūṣann asi śrutaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • imaṁ: this
    • ca: and
    • no: our
    • gaveṣaṇaṁ: search or inquiry
    • sātaye: for the purpose of truth
    • sīṣadho: in the right direction
    • gaṇam: group or assembly
    • ārāt: from the enemy or obstacle
    • pūṣann: O Pusha (the nourisher)
    • asi: you are
    • śrutaḥ: known or heard
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer for guidance and support in our search for truth.
    • It emphasizes the importance of seeking knowledge and understanding in a collective manner.
    • The invocation of Pūṣan, the deity associated with nourishment and prosperity, signifies the need for divine assistance in overcoming obstacles.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a team project, members often come together to seek the truth of their objectives, ensuring that everyone is aligned and working towards a common goal.
    • In educational settings, students collaborate to explore complex subjects, fostering a deeper understanding through shared inquiry.
    • In personal development, individuals may seek guidance from mentors or spiritual leaders to navigate challenges and find their true path.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of collective inquiry and the pursuit of truth in overcoming challenges.
    • It serves as a reminder that seeking knowledge is not a solitary journey; rather, it is enriched through collaboration and divine guidance.
    • In real-life scenarios, whether in professional, educational, or personal contexts, the principles of this mantra can be applied to foster unity, understanding, and growth.
