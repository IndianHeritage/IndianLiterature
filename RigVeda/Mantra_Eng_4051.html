ताम् अस्य रीतिम् परशोर इव प्रत्य अनीकम् अख्यं भुजे अस्य वर्पसः |सचाऽ यदि पितुमन्तम् इव क्षयं रत्नं दधाति भरहूतये विशे ||
tām asya rītim paraśor iva praty anīkam akhyam bhuje asya varpasaḥ | sacā yadi pitumantam iva kṣayaṁ ratnaṁ dadhāti bharahūtaye viśe ||
  • tām asya rītim - This phrase can be translated as "that path of this" or "that way of this." It signifies a direction or a method that is being referred to.
  • paraśor iva - This means "like a sharp axe." The sharpness of an axe symbolizes clarity, precision, and the ability to cut through confusion or obstacles.
  • praty anīkam - This translates to "not moving away" or "not deviating." It emphasizes the importance of staying true to one's path or purpose.
  • akhyam bhuje asya varpasaḥ - This can be interpreted as "the one who is known, who bears the burden." It suggests a person who is recognized for their strength and ability to carry responsibilities.
  • sacā yadi - This means "and if." It introduces a conditional aspect, indicating that what follows is dependent on a certain situation.
  • pitumantam iva - This translates to "like a father." The father figure often represents guidance, protection, and wisdom.
  • kṣayaṁ ratnaṁ dadhāti - This means "gives the jewel of destruction." Here, "jewel" symbolizes something precious, while "destruction" can refer to the removal of negativity or obstacles.
  • bharahūtaye viśe - This translates to "for the benefit of the community." It emphasizes the importance of collective well-being and the role of individuals in contributing to society.

Conclusion: This mantra encapsulates the essence of following a clear and precise path in life, akin to the sharpness of an axe that cuts through confusion. It highlights the importance of staying true to one's purpose and responsibilities, much like a father who guides and protects. The mention of giving a "jewel of destruction" signifies the transformative power of removing obstacles for the greater good of the community. In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as leadership, personal development, and community service, where clarity of purpose and collective benefit are paramount.
