मन्त्र: स्वर्भानोर अध यद इन्द्र मायाऽवो दिवो वर्तमानाऽवाहन् |गूढं सूर्यं तमसापवृतेन तुरीयेण ब्रह्मणाविन्दद आत्रिः ||
svarbhānor adha yad indra māyā avo divo vartamānā avāhan | gūḻhaṁ sūryaṁ tamasāpavratena turīyeṇa brahmaṇāvindad atriḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • svarbhānor: of the radiant one (often referring to the sun or light)
    • adha: beneath or below
    • yad: that which
    • indra: Indra, the king of gods, associated with power and strength
    • māyā: illusion or magic
    • avo: may we invoke or call upon
    • dīvo: of the heavens
    • vartamānā: existing or present
    • avāhan: invocation or calling
    • gūḻhaṁ: hidden or concealed
    • sūryaṁ: the sun
    • tamasāpavratena: by the removal of darkness
    • turīyeṇa: in the fourth state (often referring to a higher state of consciousness)
    • brahmaṇā: by the ultimate reality or the divine
    • vindad: to find or attain
    • atriḥ: Atri, a sage or seer
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of invoking the divine light and wisdom that exists beyond the illusions of the material world.
    • It emphasizes the importance of seeking knowledge and enlightenment, represented by the sun, which dispels darkness and ignorance.
    • Indra, as the king of gods, symbolizes strength and power, suggesting that one should harness these qualities to overcome challenges.
    • The reference to "the fourth state" indicates a higher state of consciousness, which can be achieved through meditation and spiritual practices.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, this mantra can inspire individuals to seek clarity and understanding in complex situations, much like how the sun illuminates the world.
    • In moments of confusion or doubt, one can meditate on this mantra to invoke inner strength and wisdom, akin to how a leader draws upon their resources to guide others.
    • Practicing mindfulness and meditation can help individuals reach that "fourth state," leading to greater peace and insight in their lives.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of seeking truth and enlightenment in a world filled with distractions and illusions.
    • Its significance lies in the encouragement to invoke divine guidance and wisdom, helping individuals navigate their lives with clarity and purpose.
    • By applying the teachings of this mantra, one can cultivate a deeper understanding of themselves and the universe, leading to a more fulfilling and enlightened existence.
