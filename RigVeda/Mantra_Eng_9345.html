तां सु ते कीर्तिम मघवन महित्वा यत त्वा भीते रोदसी अह्वेताम् |प्रावो देवाम् आतिरो दासम् ओजः प्रजायै त्वस्यै यद अशिक्ष इन्द्र ||
tāṁ su te kīrtim maghavan mahitvā yat tvā bhīte rodasī ahvayetām | prāvo devām̐ ātiro dāsam ojaḥ prajāyai tvasyai yad aśikṣa indra ||
  • tāṁ su te kīrtim: This phrase translates to "that good fame of yours." It emphasizes the importance of reputation and the positive recognition one receives in society.
  • maghavan mahitvā: "O Maghavan, with great power." Maghavan refers to Indra, the king of gods, symbolizing strength and authority. This highlights the idea that true power comes with responsibility.
  • yat tvā bhīte rodasī ahvayetām: "That which the two worlds (heaven and earth) call upon you in fear." This suggests that even the celestial and earthly realms recognize and respect the power of Indra, indicating the universal acknowledgment of strength.
  • prāvo devām̐ ātiro dāsam ojaḥ: "I invoke the divine energy that surpasses the strength of the servants." This line speaks to the idea of seeking divine assistance that transcends ordinary capabilities, emphasizing the need for higher support in challenging situations.
  • prajāyai tvasyai yad aśikṣa indra: "For the sake of the progeny, what you have taught, O Indra." This reflects the importance of wisdom and knowledge passed down through generations, highlighting the role of guidance in nurturing future generations.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda encapsulates the essence of seeking divine strength and wisdom while acknowledging the importance of reputation and the legacy one leaves behind. In real-life scenarios, it can be applied in various contexts such as leadership, where a leader must maintain a good reputation and seek higher guidance to make impactful decisions. It also emphasizes the responsibility that comes with power and the importance of imparting knowledge to future generations, making it relevant in educational and familial settings. Ultimately, it serves as a reminder to strive for greatness while being mindful of the legacy we create for those who follow us.
