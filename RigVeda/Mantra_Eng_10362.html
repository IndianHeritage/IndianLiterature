स इनु रायः सुभृतस्य चाकनन मदं यो अस्य रंह्यं चिकेटति |त्वावृधो मघवन दाश्वध्वरो मक्षू स वाजम् भरते धनाऽ नृभिः ||
sa in nu rāyaḥ subhṛtasya cākanan madaṁ yo asya raṁhyaṁ ciketati | tvāvṛdho maghavan dāśvadhvaro makṣū sa vājam bharate dhanā nṛbhiḥ ||
  • sa in nu rāyaḥ subhṛtasya cākanan: This phrase can be interpreted as "He who is the lord of wealth and prosperity." In real life, this can refer to individuals or entities that possess great resources or wealth, such as successful entrepreneurs or philanthropists who use their wealth for the betterment of society.
  • madaṁ yo asya raṁhyaṁ ciketati: This translates to "He who contemplates the joy and abundance." This can be related to people who appreciate the richness of life, such as those who practice gratitude and mindfulness, recognizing the abundance around them, whether it be in nature, relationships, or opportunities.
  • tvāvṛdho maghavan dāśvadhvaro: This can be understood as "You, who are endowed with great power and strength." In a modern context, this could refer to leaders or influential figures who have the ability to effect change, such as activists or community leaders who work tirelessly for social justice.
  • makṣū sa vājam bharate dhanā nṛbhiḥ: This means "May he who brings forth wealth and prosperity be revered by men." This highlights the importance of recognizing and honoring those who contribute positively to society, such as educators, healthcare workers, and innovators who enhance the quality of life for others.

  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of wealth, prosperity, and the joy that comes from recognizing and appreciating abundance in life. It encourages individuals to acknowledge the power and influence of those who contribute to the welfare of society. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied by fostering a culture of gratitude, supporting community leaders, and striving for personal growth and abundance in various aspects of life. By doing so, we not only enhance our own lives but also contribute to the collective prosperity of our communities.
