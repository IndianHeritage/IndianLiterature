अच्छा नः शीरशोचिषं गिरो यन्तु दर्शतम् |अच्छा यज्ञासो नमसा पुरूवसुम पुरुप्रशस्तम् ऊतये ||
acchā naḥ śīraśociṣaṁ giro yantu darśatam | acchā yajñāso namasā purūvasum purupraśastam ūtaye ||
  • acchā naḥ: This phrase translates to "may good things come to us." It expresses a desire for positivity and well-being in life.
  • śīraśociṣaṁ: This word refers to "brilliance" or "radiance." It signifies the importance of clarity and enlightenment in thoughts and actions.
  • giro yantu darśatam: This means "let our words be directed towards the truth." It emphasizes the value of truthful communication and the pursuit of knowledge.
  • acchā yajñāso: This translates to "may our sacrifices be good." It highlights the significance of selfless actions and contributions to society.
  • namasā: This word means "with reverence" or "with respect." It indicates the importance of humility and gratitude in our endeavors.
  • purūvasum: This refers to "the one who is well-known" or "the one who is esteemed." It signifies the value of reputation and the impact of our actions on how we are perceived.
  • purupraśastam: This means "the one who is praised." It emphasizes the importance of striving for excellence and being recognized for our contributions.
  • ūtaye: This translates to "for the purpose of nourishment." It signifies the ultimate goal of our actions, which is to foster growth and sustenance in life.

In conclusion, this mantra from the Rigveda encapsulates the essence of seeking goodness, truth, and respect in our lives. It encourages individuals to engage in selfless actions, communicate truthfully, and strive for excellence while remaining humble. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as:


  • In personal relationships, fostering open and honest communication can lead to stronger bonds and mutual respect.
  • In professional settings, engaging in ethical practices and contributing positively to the workplace can enhance one's reputation and lead to career growth.
  • In community service, selfless actions aimed at helping others can create a more harmonious society and inspire others to contribute as well.

The significance of this mantra lies in its timeless wisdom, reminding us that our thoughts, words, and actions should align with the principles of goodness, truth, and respect, ultimately leading to a fulfilling and meaningful life.
