अधि यद अस्मिन वाजिनीव शुभः स्पर्धन्ते धियः सूर्ये न विशः |अपो वृणानः पवते कवीयन व्रजं न पशुवर्धनाया मन्म ||
adhi yad asmin vājinīva śubhaḥ spardhante dhiyaḥ sūrye na viśaḥ | apo vṛṇānaḥ pavate kavīyan vrajaṁ na paśuvardhanāya manma ||
  • adhi yad asmin vājinīva śubhaḥ: This phrase can be translated to "Whatever is auspicious in this world, like a swift horse." Here, the term "vājinīva" refers to a horse, which is often a symbol of strength, speed, and nobility. In real life, this can be likened to the pursuit of excellence and the importance of striving for the best in our endeavors, much like a racehorse that is trained to win.
  • spardhante dhiyaḥ sūrye na viśaḥ: This translates to "The minds compete like the sun, not like the weak." The sun is a powerful symbol of energy, vitality, and clarity. This line emphasizes the idea of healthy competition and the pursuit of knowledge and wisdom. In a real-life context, this can be seen in academic or professional settings where individuals strive to outdo each other in innovation and creativity, much like how the sun outshines all other celestial bodies.
  • apo vṛṇānaḥ pavate kavīyan: This means "The waters, choosing, flow with wisdom." Water is often associated with life, nourishment, and adaptability. This line suggests that wisdom flows like water, adapting to circumstances and nourishing those who seek it. In everyday life, this can be observed in how individuals learn from experiences and adapt their knowledge to different situations, much like how rivers carve their paths through landscapes.
  • vrajaṁ na paśuvardhanāya manma: This translates to "May we not be led astray from the path of growth." This line serves as a reminder to stay focused on our goals and not to be distracted by trivial matters. In practical terms, this can be applied to personal development, where one must remain committed to their aspirations and not be swayed by negativity or setbacks.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of striving for excellence, engaging in healthy competition, flowing with wisdom, and remaining steadfast on the path of growth. Its significance lies in its timeless message that encourages individuals to pursue their goals with vigor and clarity, adapt to life's challenges with wisdom, and stay true to their purpose. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire students, professionals, and anyone seeking personal development to embrace these values in their daily lives.
