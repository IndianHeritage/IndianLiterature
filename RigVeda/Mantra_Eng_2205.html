स नो युवेन्द्रो जोहूत्रः सखा शिवो नराम अस्तु पाताः |यः शंसन्तं यः शशमानं ऊती पचन्तं च स्तुवन्तं च प्रणेषत ||
sa no yuvendro johūtraḥ sakhā śivo narām astu pātā | yaḥ śaṁsantaṁ yaḥ śaśamānam ūtī pacantaṁ ca stuvantaṁ ca praṇeṣat ||
  • sa no yuvendro johūtraḥ: This phrase can be translated as "May the youthful Indra, the lord of strength, be invoked." Here, "Indra" represents the divine force of strength and power, often associated with leadership and courage. In real life, this can be seen in leaders who inspire and motivate others to achieve greatness.
  • sakhā śivo narām astu pātā: This translates to "May he be a friend, auspicious and protective to men." The term "sakhā" means friend, and "śivo" signifies auspiciousness. This emphasizes the importance of friendship and support in human relationships. For example, in a workplace, having supportive colleagues can lead to a more productive and harmonious environment.
  • yaḥ śaṁsantaṁ yaḥ śaśamānam: This means "He who praises and he who is praised." It highlights the reciprocal nature of appreciation and recognition in relationships. In everyday life, this can be seen in how people uplift each other through compliments and encouragement, fostering a positive atmosphere.
  • ūtī pacantaṁ ca stuvantaṁ ca praṇeṣat: This translates to "He who nourishes, who is praised, and who is invoked." This part emphasizes the importance of nourishment—both physical and emotional. For instance, a nurturing parent or mentor plays a crucial role in the development of an individual, providing guidance and support.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of strength, friendship, and mutual appreciation. It signifies the importance of invoking divine qualities in our lives, such as courage, support, and nourishment. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as leadership, teamwork, and personal relationships. By embodying these qualities, individuals can create a more harmonious and supportive environment, ultimately leading to collective growth and well-being.
