स नो अद्य वसुत्तये क्रतुविद गातुवित्तमः |वाजं जेषि श्रवो बृहत् ||
sa no adya vasuttaye kratuvid gātuvittamaḥ | vājaṁ jeṣi śravo bṛhat ||
  • sa no adya vasuttaye: This phrase can be translated as "May we today attain wealth." The word "sa" means "may we," "adya" means "today," and "vasuttaye" refers to "wealth" or "prosperity." In a real-life context, this can be understood as a prayer or intention to achieve success and abundance in our endeavors, whether in business, personal life, or spiritual pursuits.
  • kratuvid gātuvittamaḥ: This part translates to "the best of those who know the mind." Here, "kratu" refers to "intellect" or "will," "vid" means "to know," and "gātuvittamaḥ" means "the best of those who sing or chant." This can be interpreted as a call to those who possess wisdom and understanding, encouraging them to share their knowledge and insights. In practical terms, this could relate to seeking guidance from mentors or wise individuals in our lives.
  • vājaṁ jeṣi: This phrase means "may we conquer the wealth." "Vājaṁ" refers to "wealth" or "prosperity," and "jeṣi" means "to conquer" or "to achieve." This signifies the aspiration to overcome obstacles and attain success. In everyday life, this can be seen in the pursuit of career goals, financial stability, or personal growth.
  • śravo bṛhat: This translates to "the great fame." "Śravo" means "fame" or "renown," and "bṛhat" means "great" or "vast." This part of the mantra emphasizes the importance of achieving not just material wealth but also recognition and respect in society. In real life, this can be related to striving for excellence in one’s field, contributing positively to the community, and leaving a lasting legacy.

Conclusion: The mantra "sa no adya vasuttaye kratuvid gātuvittamaḥ | vājaṁ jeṣi śravo bṛhat ||" encapsulates a holistic approach to prosperity, wisdom, and recognition. It encourages individuals to seek wealth and success while also valuing knowledge and the guidance of wise individuals. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various aspects, such as setting intentions for personal and professional growth, seeking mentorship, and striving for excellence in one’s endeavors. Ultimately, it serves as a reminder that true wealth encompasses not only material gains but also wisdom, respect, and a positive impact on society.
