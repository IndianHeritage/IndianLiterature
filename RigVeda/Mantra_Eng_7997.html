जनयन रोचनā दिवो जनयन्न अप्सु सूर्यं |वासानो गा अपो हरिः ||
janayan rocanā divo janayann apsu sūryam | vasāno gā apo hariḥ ||
  • Mantra: "janayan rocanā divo janayann apsu sūryam | vasāno gā apo hariḥ ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • janayan: creating or generating
    • rocanā: brightness or radiance
    • dīvo: of the sky or heaven
    • janayann: producing or giving birth
    • apsu: in the waters
    • sūryam: the sun
    • vasāno: dwelling or residing
    • gā: the waters or streams
    • apo: of the waters
    • hariḥ: the remover or the one who takes away
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the creation of brightness in the heavens and the birth of the sun from the waters.
    • The phrase "janayan rocanā divo" suggests the act of generating light and radiance in the sky, symbolizing enlightenment and knowledge.
    • "janayann apsu sūryam" indicates that the sun, a source of life and energy, emerges from the waters, which can be interpreted as the primordial source of creation.
    • The latter part, "vasāno gā apo hariḥ," implies that the divine presence resides in the waters, which can be seen as a metaphor for the nurturing and life-giving aspects of nature.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In nature, the sun is essential for life on Earth, providing energy for plants to grow, which in turn sustains the food chain. This reflects the mantra's emphasis on the sun's role in creation.
    • In many cultures, water is seen as a sacred element, often associated with purification and life. The mantra highlights the connection between water and the sun, symbolizing the balance of elements necessary for life.
    • In personal development, the idea of generating light and knowledge can be applied to the pursuit of education and self-improvement, where one seeks to illuminate their mind and spirit.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the interconnectedness of elements in the universe—light, water, and life. It serves as a reminder of the importance of balance and harmony in our lives.
    • In real-life scenarios, it encourages individuals to seek knowledge and enlightenment while recognizing the nurturing aspects of nature.
    • Ultimately, it teaches us to appreciate the sources of life and energy around us, fostering a sense of gratitude and responsibility towards the environment.
