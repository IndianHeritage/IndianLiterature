युवं रेबहम परिषूतेर उरुष्यथो हिमेन घर्मं परितप्तम अत्रये |युवं शयोर अवसाम पिप्यथुर गवि प्र दीर्घेण वन्दनस ताऱ्य आयुषा ||
yuvaṁ rebham pariṣūter uruṣyatho himena gharmam paritaptam atraye | yuvaṁ śayor avasam pipyathur gavi pra dīrgheṇa vandanas tāry āyuṣā ||
  • yuvaṁ rebham: This phrase can be interpreted as "young and vibrant." It signifies the energy and potential of youth, which is often associated with new beginnings and opportunities.
  • pariṣūter: This term refers to "in the midst of the gathering." It suggests a communal aspect, where individuals come together, highlighting the importance of community and collaboration in achieving goals.
  • uruṣyatho: This word means "with great strength." It emphasizes the power and vigor that youth possesses, which can be harnessed for constructive purposes.
  • himena: Translated as "with the cold," it can symbolize challenges or adversities that one may face in life. It reminds us that even in the face of difficulties, there is potential for growth.
  • gharmam paritaptam: This phrase means "the heat that is felt all around." It represents the warmth of life experiences and the lessons learned through them, suggesting that challenges can lead to personal development.
  • atraye: This term can be understood as "in this context." It indicates the importance of situational awareness and understanding the environment in which one operates.
  • yuvaṁ śayor: This translates to "young in the abode." It signifies the idea of youth being a state of mind and spirit, not just an age.
  • avasam pipyathur: This phrase means "thirsting for sustenance." It reflects the innate desire for growth, knowledge, and fulfillment that drives individuals, especially the youth.
  • gavi pra dīrgheṇa: This can be interpreted as "in the long-lasting abode." It suggests the importance of stability and longevity in one's pursuits and relationships.
  • vandanas tāry āyuṣā: This phrase means "with reverence, may we attain longevity." It emphasizes the value of respect and gratitude in life, which can lead to a fulfilling and extended life.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of youth, community, strength, and the journey of life. It highlights the importance of harnessing youthful energy in a supportive environment while acknowledging the challenges that come with it. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied to various aspects such as education, teamwork, and personal development. For instance, in a workplace setting, fostering a collaborative atmosphere where young professionals can thrive and learn from challenges can lead to innovation and growth. Ultimately, the mantra serves as a reminder to embrace youthfulness, respect our experiences, and strive for longevity in our endeavors.
