भग प्राणेतर भग सत्यराधो भगेमां धियम उद अवा ददन नः |भग प्र णो जनय गोभिर अश्वैर भग प्र नृभिर नृवंतः स्याम ||
bhaga praṇetar bhaga satyarādho bhagemāṁ dhiyam ud avā dadan naḥ | bhaga pra ṇo janaya gobhir aśvair bhaga pra nṛbhir nṛvantaḥ syāma ||
  • bhaga praṇetar: This phrase can be interpreted as "O Lord of Wealth" or "O Divine Benefactor." It signifies the importance of divine blessings in our lives, particularly in terms of prosperity and abundance.
  • bhaga satyarādho: This translates to "the one who is worthy of praise and truth." It emphasizes the value of truthfulness and integrity in our actions and thoughts, suggesting that true wealth comes from living a life of honesty.
  • bhagemāṁ dhiyam ud avā dadan naḥ: This means "may you bestow upon us the wealth of wisdom." Here, wisdom is portrayed as a form of wealth that is essential for making sound decisions and leading a fulfilling life.
  • bhaga pra ṇo janaya gobhir aśvair: This can be understood as "may you generate for us prosperity through our efforts." It highlights the importance of hard work and diligence in achieving success and abundance.
  • bhaga pra nṛbhir nṛvantaḥ syāma: This translates to "may we be enriched by the blessings of the divine." It suggests that while personal effort is crucial, divine grace plays a significant role in our achievements.

  • Real-Life Example: Consider a person striving for success in their career. They work hard (bhaga pra ṇo janaya) and maintain integrity (bhaga satyarādho), while also seeking wisdom (bhagemāṁ dhiyam ud avā dadan naḥ) to make informed decisions. Their efforts, combined with a sense of gratitude for the divine blessings they receive, can lead to a prosperous and fulfilling life.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking divine blessings for prosperity, wisdom, and integrity. It teaches us that while personal effort is vital, recognizing and appreciating the role of divine grace can lead to a more enriched life. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various aspects, such as career growth, personal relationships, and overall well-being, reminding us to balance hard work with ethical living and gratitude.
