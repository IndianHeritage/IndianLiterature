तन्तुं तन्‍वन रजसो भानुम अन्व इहि ज्योतिष्मतः पथो रक्ष धियā कृतान् |अनुल्बणं वयत जोगुवाम अपो मनुर भव जनया दैव्यम् जनम् ||
tantuṁ tanvan rajaso bhānum anv ihi jyotiṣmataḥ patho rakṣa dhiyā kṛtān | anulbaṇaṁ vayata joguvām apo manur bhava janayā daivyaṁ janam ||
  • tantuṁ tanvan rajaso bhānum: This phrase can be interpreted as a call to the divine light (bhānum) that illuminates the path of life (tantuṁ tanvan). It signifies the importance of seeking guidance from a higher power in our endeavors.
  • anv ihi jyotiṣmataḥ patho: Here, "anv ihi" means "come here" or "approach," while "jyotiṣmataḥ" refers to the wise or enlightened ones. This suggests that we should seek the wisdom of those who are enlightened to guide us on our journey.
  • rakṣa dhiyā kṛtān: This part translates to "protect our intellect and actions." It emphasizes the need for divine protection over our thoughts and deeds, ensuring that they are aligned with righteousness.
  • anulbaṇaṁ vayata: "Anulbaṇaṁ" means "to be free from obstacles," and "vayata" refers to the divine or the higher self. This indicates a desire for liberation from hindrances in our spiritual and worldly pursuits.
  • joguvām apo manur bhava: This phrase can be understood as a request for the divine to manifest (bhava) in our lives, guiding us towards our true purpose (manur). It reflects the aspiration for a deeper connection with the divine.
  • janayā daivyaṁ janam: This final part translates to "may we generate a divine community." It highlights the importance of fostering a community that is spiritually inclined and supportive of each other’s growth.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a profound message about seeking divine guidance, protection, and the importance of community in our spiritual journey. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios such as:

  • In personal development, individuals can reflect on their actions and seek wisdom from mentors or spiritual leaders to ensure they are on the right path.
  • In community settings, fostering a supportive environment where individuals uplift each other can lead to collective growth and enlightenment.
  • In times of uncertainty or challenges, invoking this mantra can serve as a reminder to seek divine guidance and protection, helping individuals navigate through difficulties with clarity and purpose.

Overall, this mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of our actions, thoughts, and the divine, encouraging us to strive for a life that is not only successful but also spiritually fulfilling.
