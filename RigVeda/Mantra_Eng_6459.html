आ गन्ता मा रिषण्यत प्रस्थानवानो माप स्थाता समन्यवः |स्थिरा चिन् नमयिष्णवः ||
ā gantā mā riṣaṇyata prasthāvāno māpa sthātā samanyavaḥ | sthirā cin namayiṣṇavaḥ ||
  • Word Breakdown:
    • ā gantā: "to go" or "to move forward."
    • mā: "not" or "do not."
    • riṣaṇyata: "be disturbed" or "be agitated."
    • prasthāvāno: "one who is setting out" or "initiator."
    • māpa: "do not" or "let not."
    • sthātā: "to remain" or "to stay."
    • samanyavaḥ: "common" or "ordinary."
    • sthirā: "steady" or "firm."
    • cin: "to be" or "to exist."
    • namayiṣṇavaḥ: "to be worshipped" or "to be revered."
  • Meaning:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of moving forward in life without being disturbed by external factors.
    • It encourages individuals to initiate their journeys or endeavors while maintaining a sense of calm and steadiness.
    • The phrase "do not be agitated" serves as a reminder to remain composed, even in challenging situations.
    • It highlights the significance of being firm and steady in one's purpose, suggesting that true reverence comes from a stable and unwavering spirit.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, when facing a challenging project, one can apply this mantra by focusing on the task at hand without getting overwhelmed by stress or distractions.
    • During personal challenges, such as a health issue or a family crisis, maintaining a calm demeanor and a steady approach can lead to better decision-making and outcomes.
    • In sports, athletes often use mental techniques to stay focused and composed, embodying the essence of this mantra by not letting pressure affect their performance.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its timeless wisdom about the importance of composure and steadiness in the face of life's challenges.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal struggles to professional endeavors, reminding us to remain focused and calm.
    • By embodying the principles of this mantra, individuals can navigate their journeys with grace and purpose, ultimately leading to success and fulfillment.
