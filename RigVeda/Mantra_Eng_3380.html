मन्त्र: उता शुष्णस्य धृष्णुया प्र मृक्षो अभि वेदनम् |पुरो यद अस्य सम्पिणक ||
uta śuṣṇasya dhṛṣṇuyā pra mṛkṣo abhi vedanam | puro yad asya sampiṇak ||
  • uta - This word can be translated as "and" or "also," indicating a connection or addition to what has been previously mentioned.
  • śuṣṇasya - This term refers to "the one who is powerful" or "the one who is strong." It signifies strength and capability.
  • dhṛṣṇuyā - This word means "with determination" or "with courage." It emphasizes the importance of resolve in achieving one's goals.
  • pra - This prefix often means "forward" or "towards," suggesting movement or progression.
  • mṛkṣo - This term can be interpreted as "the one who is seeking" or "the one who is striving." It denotes an active pursuit of knowledge or truth.
  • abhi - This word means "towards" or "against," indicating direction or focus in one's efforts.
  • vedanam - This term refers to "knowledge" or "wisdom," particularly in the context of the Vedas, which are ancient Indian scriptures.
  • puro - This word means "in front" or "before," suggesting a position of leadership or precedence.
  • yad - This translates to "that" or "which," referring to something specific that has been mentioned or is known.
  • asya - This term means "of this" or "of it," indicating possession or relation to something previously stated.
  • sampiṇak - This word can be interpreted as "the one who is complete" or "the one who is fulfilled," suggesting a state of wholeness or achievement.

The mantra emphasizes the importance of strength, determination, and the pursuit of knowledge. It suggests that with courage and a focused approach, one can achieve completeness and fulfillment in life. The connection between strength and knowledge is crucial, as it highlights that true power comes from understanding and wisdom.


In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts:

  • Personal Development: Individuals striving for personal growth can draw inspiration from this mantra. By cultivating strength and determination, they can overcome challenges and achieve their goals.
  • Education: Students can relate to the pursuit of knowledge as a journey that requires courage and effort. This mantra encourages them to seek wisdom actively and with resolve.
  • Leadership: Leaders can embody the qualities of strength and determination, guiding their teams towards success. The mantra serves as a reminder that effective leadership is rooted in knowledge and the ability to inspire others.

In conclusion, this RigVeda mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the interconnectedness of strength, determination, and knowledge. It encourages individuals to pursue their goals with courage and to seek wisdom in their endeavors, ultimately leading to a sense of fulfillment and completeness in life.
