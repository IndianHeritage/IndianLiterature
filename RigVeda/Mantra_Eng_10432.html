सहस्राक्षेण शतशारदेन शतायुषा हविषाहर्षम एनम् |शतम् यथेयं शरदो नयातिंद्रो विश्वस्य दुरितस्य पारम् ||
sahasrākṣeṇa śataśāradena śatāyuṣā haviṣāhārṣam enam | śataṁ yathemaṁ śarado nayātīndro viśvasya duritasya pāram ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sahasrākṣeṇa: With a thousand eyes
    • śataśāradena: With a hundred autumns
    • śatāyuṣā: With a hundred years of life
    • haviṣāhārṣam: With offerings and sacrifices
    • enam: This being
    • śataṁ: A hundred
    • yathemaṁ: That which is here
    • śarado: Autumns
    • nayātīndro: May Indra lead
    • viśvasya: Of the universe
    • duritasya: Of difficulties or obstacles
    • pāram: Beyond
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the divine with a thousand eyes, symbolizing omniscience and the ability to see all aspects of life.
    • The mention of "a hundred autumns" and "a hundred years of life" signifies longevity, prosperity, and the cyclical nature of life.
    • By offering sacrifices and performing rituals, one seeks blessings and guidance from the divine.
    • The plea for Indra, the king of gods, to lead one beyond the difficulties of life emphasizes the need for divine intervention in overcoming challenges.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, a team may face numerous challenges in a project. By invoking the spirit of collaboration and seeking guidance from experienced mentors (akin to the divine), they can navigate through obstacles effectively.
    • During personal hardships, individuals often turn to spiritual practices, such as meditation or prayer, to seek clarity and strength, reflecting the essence of this mantra.
    • In community gatherings, rituals and offerings are made to seek blessings for prosperity and harmony, mirroring the sacrificial aspect of the mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of divine guidance and the power of collective effort in overcoming life's challenges.
    • It serves as a reminder to seek wisdom and support from higher powers, whether through spirituality, community, or mentorship.
    • In essence, it teaches us that with the right intentions and support, we can transcend difficulties and achieve a fulfilling life.
