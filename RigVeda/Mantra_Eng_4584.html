वयं त एभिः पुरुहूत सख्यैः शत्रोः-शत्रोर उत्तर इति स्याम |घ्नन्तो वृत्राण्य उभयानी शूर राया मदेम बृहतā त्वोताः ||
vayaṁ ta ebhiḥ puruhūta sakhyaiḥ śatroḥ-śatror uttara it syāma | ghnanto vṛtrāṇy ubhayāni śūra rāyā madema bṛhatā tvotāḥ ||
  • Mantra: vayaṁ ta ebhiḥ puruhūta sakhyaiḥ śatroḥ-śatror uttara it syāma | ghnanto vṛtrāṇy ubhayāni śūra rāyā madema bṛhatā tvotāḥ ||
  • Translation: "May we, with the help of these many friends, overcome our enemies. Let us, the brave, strike down both the foes and the obstacles, and may we attain great wealth and prosperity."

  • Word-by-Word Explanation:
    • vayaṁ: We
    • ta: Those
    • ebhiḥ: With these
    • puruhūta: Many friends or allies
    • sakhyaiḥ: With friendship
    • śatroḥ-śatror: Of the enemy and the adversary
    • uttara: Overcome or surpass
    • it: Thus
    • syāma: May we become
    • ghnanto: Striking down
    • vṛtrāṇy: The obstacles or foes
    • ubhayāni: Both
    • śūra: Brave or valiant
    • rāyā: Wealth or prosperity
    • madema: May we attain
    • bṛhatā: Great or vast
    • tvotāḥ: Blessings or boons

  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, this mantra can be applied when a team collaborates to overcome challenges in a project. The "many friends" represent team members working together to achieve a common goal, overcoming competition and obstacles.
    • In personal life, individuals may face various challenges such as health issues or financial difficulties. By seeking support from friends and family (the "many friends"), one can find the strength to overcome these adversities.
    • In community initiatives, this mantra reflects the power of collective action. When a community comes together to address social issues, they embody the spirit of overcoming obstacles and achieving prosperity for all.

  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its emphasis on unity, strength, and the pursuit of prosperity. It teaches us that with the support of friends and allies, we can overcome challenges and adversaries in various aspects of life. Whether in personal struggles, professional endeavors, or community efforts, the essence of this mantra encourages collaboration and bravery in the face of difficulties. By embodying these principles, we can strive for greater achievements and a prosperous life.
