युवो राष्ट्रं बृहद् इन्भति द्यौर यौ सेत्रिभिर अरज्जुभिः सिनीथः |परि नो हेळो वरुणस्य वृज्या उरुं न इन्द्रः कृत्वाद् उ लोकम् ||
yuvo rāṣṭram bṛhad invati dyaur yau setṛbhir arajjubhiḥ sinīthaḥ | pari no heḻo varuṇasya vṛjyā uruṁ na indraḥ kṛṇavad u lokam ||
  • yuvo rāṣṭram: This phrase translates to "the youthful state of the nation." It signifies the vigor and potential of a society or community, emphasizing the importance of youth in shaping the future.
  • bṛhad invati dyaur: This means "the vast sky supports." It suggests that the universe or the divine provides support and sustenance to those who strive for greatness.
  • yau setṛbhir arajjubhiḥ sinīthaḥ: This can be interpreted as "those who are guided by the wise." It highlights the importance of wisdom and guidance in achieving success and prosperity.
  • pari no heḻo varuṇasya vṛjyā: This translates to "may the protection of Varuna surround us." Varuna is the deity associated with cosmic order and moral authority, indicating the need for divine protection and ethical living.
  • uruṁ na indraḥ kṛṇavad u lokam: This means "may Indra, the king of gods, grant us a vast realm." Indra represents strength and leadership, suggesting that with divine blessings, one can achieve greatness and expand their horizons.

  • Significance: This mantra encapsulates the essence of youth, wisdom, divine protection, and the pursuit of greatness. It serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of these elements in building a prosperous society.
  • Real-life Application: In contemporary society, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as:
    • Education: Emphasizing the role of youth in education and the importance of wise mentors to guide them.
    • Community Development: Encouraging young leaders to take initiative in community projects, supported by the wisdom of experienced individuals.
    • Spiritual Growth: Seeking divine guidance and protection in personal and communal endeavors, fostering a sense of moral responsibility.

  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a powerful invocation for the youth to harness their potential, seek wisdom, and strive for greatness with the support of divine forces. It is applicable in various aspects of life, from personal development to community engagement, reminding us of the importance of collaboration, guidance, and ethical living in achieving a harmonious and prosperous society.
