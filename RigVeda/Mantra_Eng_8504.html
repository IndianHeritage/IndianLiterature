मन्त्र: उरुगव्यूतिर्भयानी कृत्वान समीचीन ए पवस्वा पुरंन्धी |अपः सिषासन्न उषसः स्वर्ण गाः सं चक्रदो महो अस्मभ्यं वाजान ||
urugavyūtir abhayāni kṛṇvan samīcīne ā pavasvā puraṁdhī | apaḥ siṣāsann uṣasaḥ sva1r gāḥ saṁ cikrado maho asmabhyaṁ vājān ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • urugavyūtir: One who has vast and expansive qualities.
    • abhayāni: Bestowing fearlessness or safety.
    • kṛṇvan: Creating or manifesting.
    • samīcīne: In a proper or suitable manner.
    • ā: Towards or in the direction of.
    • pavasvā: Purifying or cleansing.
    • puraṁdhī: The one who is the master of the house or the creator.
    • apaḥ: Waters or the essence of life.
    • siṣāsann: Approaching or coming near.
    • uṣasaḥ: Of the dawn or the new beginning.
    • sva1r: The celestial or heavenly realm.
    • gāḥ: Cows or wealth.
    • saṁ: Together or in union.
    • cikrado: Bestowing or granting.
    • maho: Great or significant.
    • asmabhyaṁ: For us or to us.
    • vājān: Strength or vigor.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of a divine force that is expansive and protective, creating a safe environment for all.
    • The reference to "waters" symbolizes the essence of life, suggesting that purity and nourishment are essential for growth.
    • The dawn represents new beginnings, indicating that every day brings opportunities for renewal and strength.
    • The mention of "cows" symbolizes wealth and prosperity, emphasizing the importance of abundance in life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, a leader who fosters a safe and inclusive environment can be seen as creating an "urugavyūtir," allowing employees to thrive without fear.
    • In personal life, practicing mindfulness and purification through meditation can help individuals approach their challenges with a clear mind, akin to the "pavasvā" aspect of the mantra.
    • Community initiatives that focus on sustainability and abundance, such as community gardens, reflect the essence of "gāḥ" by promoting shared resources and wealth.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of creating a safe, abundant, and nurturing environment for growth and prosperity.
    • Its teachings can be applied in various aspects of life, including personal development, leadership, and community building.
    • By embodying the principles of fearlessness, purity, and abundance, individuals and communities can foster a harmonious existence that benefits all.
