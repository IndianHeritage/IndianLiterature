आद्यā देवā उditā सूर्यस्य निर अṁहसः पिपृतā निर अवद्यāt |तन नो मित्रो वरुणो माऽमहन्तām अदितिः सिन्धुः पृथिवī उता द्यौः ||
adyā devā uditā sūryasya nir aṁhasaḥ pipṛtā nir avadyāt | tan no mitro varuṇo māmahantām aditiḥ sindhuḥ pṛthivī uta dyauḥ ||
  • adyā - Today
  • devā - The gods
  • uditā - Have arisen
  • sūryasya - Of the sun
  • nir aṁhasaḥ - Without any obstruction
  • pipṛtā - Have filled
  • nir avadyāt - Without any fault
  • tan - That
  • no - To us
  • mitro - O Mitra
  • varuṇo - O Varuna
  • māmahantām - May you grant us
  • aditiḥ - Aditi (the infinite)
  • sindhuḥ - The ocean
  • pṛthivī - The earth
  • uta - And
  • dyauḥ - The sky

This mantra from the Rigveda invokes the deities Mitra and Varuna, seeking their blessings for a life filled with abundance and harmony. The opening lines express gratitude for the divine presence that has arisen today, symbolized by the sun, which is a universal source of light and life. The phrase "nir aṁhasaḥ" emphasizes the unobstructed nature of this divine energy, suggesting that it is pure and faultless.


The invocation of Mitra and Varuna highlights the importance of friendship, truth, and cosmic order. In real life, this can be related to the significance of maintaining honest relationships and upholding moral values in our interactions. For instance, in a workplace setting, fostering a culture of transparency and trust can lead to better teamwork and productivity.


The mention of Aditi, the infinite, along with the elements of nature—ocean, earth, and sky—reflects the interconnectedness of all existence. This can be seen in environmental conservation efforts, where recognizing the importance of preserving our natural resources is crucial for sustaining life on Earth.


Conclusion: This mantra serves as a reminder of the divine forces that govern our lives and the importance of living in harmony with nature and each other. It encourages us to seek blessings for a life free from obstacles and filled with truth and abundance. In practical terms, it can inspire individuals to cultivate positive relationships, engage in ethical practices, and contribute to the well-being of the environment, ultimately leading to a more balanced and fulfilling life.
