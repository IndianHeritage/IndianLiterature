देवो वो द्रविणोदाः पूर्णां विवष्ट्य आसिचम् |उद वा सिञ्चध्वम् उप वा प्रिणध्वम् आद इद् वो देव ओहते ||
devo vo draviṇodāḥ pūrṇāṁ vivaṣṭy āsicam | ud vā siñcadhvam upa vā pṛṇadhvam ād id vo deva ohate ||
  • Devo vo draviṇodāḥ: This phrase translates to "O Gods, you who are the givers of wealth." It acknowledges the divine as the source of abundance and prosperity.
  • Pūrṇāṁ vivaṣṭy āsicam: This means "May you pour forth fullness." It signifies the desire for completeness and fulfillment in life, suggesting that the divine blessings should lead to a state of abundance.
  • Ud vā siñcadhvam: This translates to "Pour forth from above." It is a call to the divine to shower blessings and prosperity from the heavens.
  • Upa vā pṛṇadhvam: This means "Fill us up." It expresses a yearning for the divine to fill our lives with richness and satisfaction.
  • Ād id vo deva ohate: This translates to "Thus, O Gods, we invoke you." It is a prayerful invocation, seeking the attention and blessings of the divine.

  • Real-Life Example: In many cultures, rituals are performed to invoke blessings for wealth and prosperity. For instance, during Diwali in India, people perform Lakshmi Puja to invite the goddess of wealth into their homes, reflecting the essence of this mantra.
  • Significance: This mantra emphasizes the importance of divine blessings in achieving prosperity and fulfillment. It serves as a reminder that while hard work is essential, seeking spiritual support can enhance our efforts.
  • Application in Real Life: This mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as during business ventures, personal projects, or even in daily life when one seeks abundance. It encourages individuals to remain open to receiving blessings and to acknowledge the role of the divine in their successes.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the relationship between the divine and human endeavors, highlighting the need for spiritual support in achieving material and emotional fulfillment. By invoking the divine, individuals can cultivate a mindset of abundance and gratitude, which can lead to a more prosperous and satisfying life.
