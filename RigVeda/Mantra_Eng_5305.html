त्वं ह त्यद इन्द्र कुत्सम आवः शुश्रूषमाणस तंवा समर्ये | दासं यच छुष्णं कुयवं न्य अस्मा अरन्धय आरजुनेयाय शिक्षन् ||
tvaṁ ha tyad indra kutsam āvaḥ śuśrūṣamāṇas tanvā samarye | dāsaṁ yac chuṣṇaṁ kuyavaṁ ny asmā arandhaya ārjuneyāya śikṣan ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • tvaṁ: You
    • ha: indeed
    • tyad: that
    • indra: O Indra (the king of gods)
    • kutsam: the one who is worthy of respect
    • āvaḥ: bring forth
    • śuśrūṣamāṇas: who is attentive to our needs
    • tanvā: with your body
    • samarye: in the battle
    • dāsaṁ: the servant
    • yac: who
    • chuṣṇaṁ: is thirsty
    • kuyavaṁ: the one who is deceitful
    • ny: not
    • asmā: from us
    • arandhaya: do not let go
    • ārjuneyāya: to the one who is strong
    • śikṣan: teach or instruct
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Indra, the god of rain and thunderstorms, asking for his protection and guidance.
    • The speaker acknowledges Indra's power and requests him to bring forth his strength in times of need.
    • It emphasizes the importance of being attentive to the needs of others and the community, as well as the need to confront deceit and negativity.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace setting, this mantra can be applied when a team faces challenges. The leader can invoke the spirit of Indra by encouraging teamwork and vigilance against deceitful practices.
    • In personal relationships, it serves as a reminder to be attentive to the needs of loved ones and to confront any dishonesty that may arise.
    • In community service, it can inspire individuals to support those who are vulnerable and to stand against any form of injustice.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of divine guidance in overcoming challenges and the need for vigilance against deceit.
    • It teaches us to be proactive in our relationships and communities, ensuring that we support one another and confront negativity.
    • In essence, it encourages a collective strength and awareness that can lead to a more harmonious and just society.
