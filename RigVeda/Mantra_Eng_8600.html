ऋजुः पवस्व वृजिनस्य हन्तापामिवाम् बाधमानो मृधश्च | अभिश्रीणन् पयः पयसाभिगोनाम इन्द्रस्य त्वं तव वयं सखायः ||
ṛjuḥ pavasva vṛjinasya hantāpāmīvām bādhamāno mṛdhaś ca | abhiśrīṇan payaḥ payasābhi gonām indrasya tvaṁ tava vayaṁ sakhāyaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ṛjuḥ: Straight, honest, or upright.
    • pavasva: May you purify or cleanse.
    • vṛjinasya: Of the impurities or obstacles.
    • hantāpāmīvām: May you destroy or remove them.
    • bādhamāno: Being troubled or afflicted.
    • mṛdhaś: With strength or power.
    • ca: And.
    • abhiśrīṇan: Bestowing prosperity or abundance.
    • payaḥ: Milk or nourishment.
    • payasābhi: With the essence of nourishment.
    • gonām: Of the cows or creatures.
    • indrasya: Of Indra, the king of gods.
    • tvaṁ: You.
    • tava: Your.
    • vayaṁ: We.
    • sakhāyaḥ: Friends or companions.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the divine energy to cleanse and purify the individual from impurities and obstacles in life.
    • The term "ṛjuḥ" emphasizes the importance of honesty and integrity in one's actions.
    • It calls upon the strength to overcome afflictions and challenges, suggesting that with divine support, one can rise above difficulties.
    • The reference to "abhiśrīṇan payaḥ" signifies the nourishment and abundance that comes from a pure and honest life, akin to how milk nourishes the body.
    • By invoking Indra, the mantra seeks the blessings of the king of gods, who is often associated with strength, rain, and prosperity.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, maintaining integrity (ṛjuḥ) can lead to a trustworthy reputation, which in turn attracts opportunities and success.
    • When facing personal challenges, such as health issues or emotional distress, this mantra serves as a reminder to seek strength (mṛdhaś) and support from friends and community (sakhāyaḥ).
    • In times of scarcity, the invocation of abundance (abhiśrīṇan payaḥ) can inspire individuals to focus on gratitude and the positive aspects of their lives, fostering a mindset of abundance rather than lack.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of purity, strength, and community support in overcoming life's challenges.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal struggles to professional integrity, reminding us of the importance of honesty and the power of divine blessings.
    • By reciting this mantra, individuals can cultivate a mindset that embraces positivity, resilience, and the pursuit of a fulfilling life.
