मन्त्र: कदा क्षत्रश्रियं नरं आवरुणं करामहे | मृलीकायोरुचक्षसम् ||
kadā kṣatraśriyaṁ naram ā varuṇaṁ karāmahe | mṛḻīkāyorucakṣasam ||
  • Mantra: "kadā kṣatraśriyaṁ naram ā varuṇaṁ karāmahe | mṛḻīkāyorucakṣasam ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • kadā: When
    • kṣatraśriyaṁ: the glory of sovereignty or power
    • naram: a man or human being
    • ā: towards
    • varuṇaṁ: Varuna, the god of cosmic order and water
    • karāmahe: we perform or we invoke
    • mṛḻīkāyorucakṣasam: of the two worlds or realms (often interpreted as the earthly and the divine)
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra expresses a desire for the glory and power of a human being to be directed towards Varuna, the deity representing cosmic order and moral authority.
    • The invocation suggests a yearning for divine guidance and support in achieving sovereignty and righteousness.
    • The mention of "mṛḻīkāyorucakṣasam" indicates a connection between the earthly realm and the divine, emphasizing the importance of balance between the two.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In leadership roles, individuals often seek guidance from higher principles or values, akin to invoking Varuna for wisdom and moral clarity.
    • In community decision-making, leaders may reflect on the greater good, striving for a balance between personal ambition and collective welfare, similar to the dual realms mentioned in the mantra.
    • In personal development, individuals may meditate on their aspirations and seek alignment with their values, invoking a sense of purpose and direction in their lives.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of seeking divine support in the pursuit of power and righteousness.
    • It serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of the earthly and divine realms, encouraging individuals to act with integrity and purpose.
    • In contemporary life, the principles embodied in this mantra can guide ethical leadership, personal growth, and community engagement, fostering a harmonious balance between ambition and moral responsibility.
