शुम्भमानाऽऋतायुभिर् मृज्यमानाऽगभस्त्योः |पवन्ते वारे अव्यये ||
śumbhamānā ṛtāyubhir mṛjyamānā gabhastyoḥ | pavante vāre avyaye ||
  • śumbhamānā: This term can be interpreted as "being praised" or "being celebrated." It signifies the importance of recognition and appreciation in our lives.
  • ṛtāyubhir: This word refers to "truth" or "righteousness." It emphasizes the value of living a life aligned with truth and moral principles.
  • mṛjyamānā: This means "being purified" or "being cleansed." It suggests the process of purification, both physically and spiritually, which is essential for personal growth.
  • gabhastyoḥ: This term relates to "light" or "radiance." It symbolizes knowledge, wisdom, and enlightenment that guide individuals in their journey.
  • pavante: This word means "to blow" or "to move." It can be associated with the forces of nature or the winds of change that influence our lives.
  • vāre: This translates to "in the atmosphere" or "in the space." It indicates the environment or context in which we exist and operate.
  • avyaye: This term means "imperishable" or "eternal." It signifies the everlasting nature of truth and righteousness, which transcends time and space.

  • Significance: This mantra encapsulates the essence of living a life grounded in truth, righteousness, and purity. It highlights the importance of being recognized for our virtues and the need for continuous self-improvement.
  • Real-life Application: In everyday life, this mantra can inspire individuals to strive for honesty and integrity in their personal and professional relationships. For example, a business leader who prioritizes ethical practices and transparency can create a positive work environment that fosters trust and collaboration.
  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a reminder of the values that should guide our actions and decisions. By embracing truth, righteousness, and the pursuit of knowledge, we can navigate the complexities of life with clarity and purpose. It encourages us to seek purification of our thoughts and actions, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling and meaningful existence.
