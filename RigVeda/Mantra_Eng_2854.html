मन्त्र: विद्मा हि त्वा धनञ्जयं वाजेषु दधृषं कवे | अधा ते सुम्नम इमहे ||
vidmā hi tvā dhanaṁjayaṁ vājeṣu dadhṛṣaṁ kave | adhā te sumnam īmahe ||
  • vidmā: This word translates to "we know" or "we are aware." It signifies knowledge and understanding.
  • hi: This is an emphatic particle, often used to emphasize the preceding word, in this case, "vidmā."
  • tvā: This means "you" or "to you," indicating a direct address to a deity or a higher power.
  • dhanaṁjayaṁ: This term can be interpreted as "the one who conquers wealth" or "the victorious one." It refers to a deity associated with prosperity and success.
  • vājeṣu: This word means "in the offerings" or "in the sacrifices," suggesting a context of rituals and devotion.
  • dadhṛṣaṁ: This translates to "who holds" or "who possesses," indicating a sense of control or mastery.
  • kave: This means "O poet" or "O sage," addressing a wise figure, often associated with inspiration and creativity.
  • adhā: This word means "therefore" or "thus," indicating a conclusion or a result of the previous statements.
  • te: This means "your," indicating possession or belonging.
  • sumnam: This translates to "auspiciousness" or "good fortune," referring to positive outcomes and blessings.
  • īmahe: This means "we invoke" or "we call upon," indicating a plea or request for divine intervention.

The mantra can be understood as a prayer or invocation where the speaker expresses their knowledge and awareness of a divine presence, specifically addressing a deity associated with wealth and prosperity. The speaker acknowledges the deity's power and control over offerings and sacrifices, and they seek auspiciousness and blessings through this invocation.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as:

  • Financial Success: Individuals or businesses may recite this mantra during rituals or prayers to seek blessings for prosperity and success in their financial endeavors.
  • Creative Pursuits: Artists, writers, and creators may invoke this mantra to seek inspiration and guidance in their creative processes, recognizing the role of divine influence in their work.
  • Rituals and Offerings: During religious ceremonies or personal rituals, this mantra can be recited to honor the divine and express gratitude for the blessings received.

In conclusion, this mantra emphasizes the importance of knowledge, divine connection, and the pursuit of auspiciousness in various aspects of life. It serves as a reminder to seek guidance and blessings from higher powers, particularly in matters related to wealth, creativity, and overall well-being.
