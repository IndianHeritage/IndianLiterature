अस्मिन्स्व एतच चकपूत एनो हिते मित्रे निगतान हन्ति वीरान् |अवोर्वा यद धात तनूष्व अवः प्रियासु यज्ञियास्व अर्वा ||
asmin sv e3tac chakapūta eno hite mitre nigatān hanti vīrān | avor vā yad dhāt tanūṣv avaḥ priyāsu yajñiyāsv arvā ||
  • Mantra: "asmin sv e3tac chakapūta eno hite mitre nigatān hanti vīrān | avor vā yad dhāt tanūṣv avaḥ priyāsu yajñiyāsv arvā ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • asmin: in this
    • sv: self
    • e3tac: this (referring to the divine or sacred)
    • chakapūta: the one who is covered or protected
    • eno: may he (the divine) grant
    • hite: in welfare or well-being
    • mitre: O friend (often referring to the divine as a friend)
    • nigatān: those who are in distress or trouble
    • hanti: may he destroy or remove
    • vīrān: the brave or the heroes
    • avor: or (in the sense of 'also')
    • vā: or
    • yad: whatever
    • dhāt: is established or created
    • tanūṣv: in bodies or forms
    • avaḥ: may it come down or descend
    • priyāsu: to those who are dear
    • yajñiyāsv: in the sacrifices or offerings
    • arvā: may it be fulfilled or accomplished
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra invokes the divine presence to provide protection and welfare.
    • The phrase "O friend" signifies a personal relationship with the divine, suggesting that the divine is approachable and supportive.
    • The mantra emphasizes the importance of community and support, as it seeks to protect not just individuals but also the brave and those in distress.
    • In real life, this can be seen in the context of seeking help during difficult times, whether through friends, family, or community support systems.
    • The mention of "sacrifices or offerings" indicates that actions taken with good intentions and dedication can lead to positive outcomes.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of divine support in overcoming challenges and achieving well-being.
    • It teaches us the value of community, friendship, and the importance of seeking help when needed.
    • In practical terms, it encourages individuals to engage in acts of kindness and support for others, fostering a sense of unity and strength.
    • Ultimately, it serves as a reminder that through devotion, community, and selfless actions, one can navigate life's difficulties and achieve fulfillment.
