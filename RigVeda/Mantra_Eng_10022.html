शतं वा यद असूर्य प्रति त्वा सुमित्र इत्तास्थौद दुरमित्र इत्तास्थौत् |आवो यद दस्युहत्ये कुत्सपुत्रम् प्रावो यद दस्युहत्ये कुत्सवत्सम् ||
śataṁ vā yad asurya prati tvā sumitra itthāstaud durmitra itthāstaut | āvo yad dasyuhatye kutsaputram prāvo yad dasyuhatye kutsavatsam ||
  • śataṁ vā yad asurya prati tvā: This phrase can be translated to "A hundred times, O Asura, I offer my homage to you." Here, "Asura" refers to a powerful being or deity, often associated with strength and might. In real life, this can be likened to the respect and reverence we give to leaders or influential figures who guide us.
  • sumitra itthāstaud durmitra itthāstaut: This part means "May the good friend be with us, and may the bad friend be kept away." It emphasizes the importance of surrounding ourselves with positive influences while avoiding negativity. In everyday life, this can be seen in the choice of friends and associates, where positive relationships foster growth and well-being.
  • āvo yad dasyuhatye kutsaputram: This translates to "Come, O one who destroys the wicked, and protect us from the evil." This line calls for divine protection against malevolent forces. In practical terms, it reflects our desire for safety and security in our lives, whether it be from harmful individuals or negative situations.
  • prāvo yad dasyuhatye kutsavatsam: This means "We invoke you, O protector, to shield us from the wicked." It reinforces the plea for safeguarding against harm. In real life, this can be related to seeking help from authorities or community support systems when faced with threats or challenges.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a profound message about the importance of seeking protection from negativity and fostering positive relationships. It serves as a reminder to be mindful of the influences we allow into our lives and to actively seek the company of those who uplift us. In a world filled with challenges, this mantra encourages us to invoke higher powers for guidance and protection, making it applicable in various real-life scenarios, such as personal relationships, community interactions, and even in professional environments where collaboration and support are essential for success.
