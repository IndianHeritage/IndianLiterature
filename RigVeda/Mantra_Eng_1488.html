प्र बोधयाऽपुरंढिं जार आ ससतीं इव |प्र चक्षय रोदसी वासयोषसः श्रवसे वासयोषसः ||
pra bodhayā puraṁdhiṁ jāra ā sasatīm iva | pra cakṣaya rodasī vāsayoṣasaḥ śravase vāsayoṣasaḥ ||
  • Mantra: "pra bodhayā puraṁdhiṁ jāra ā sasatīm iva | pra cakṣaya rodasī vāsayoṣasaḥ śravase vāsayoṣasaḥ ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • pra: forward, towards
    • bodhayā: awakening, enlightening
    • puraṁdhiṁ: the mind or intellect
    • jāra: like a flame or light
    • ā: towards, to
    • sasatīm: the one who is full of knowledge
    • iva: like, as if
    • cakṣaya: with vision, seeing
    • rodasī: the two worlds, the heavens and the earth
    • vāsayoṣasaḥ: of the radiant, shining beings
    • śravase: of fame, glory
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra calls for the awakening of the intellect and mind, urging it to shine brightly like a flame.
    • It emphasizes the importance of enlightenment and knowledge, suggesting that one should strive to gain wisdom.
    • The phrase "cakṣaya rodasī" indicates a desire to see and understand the duality of existence, both the material and spiritual realms.
    • It also highlights the significance of being surrounded by radiant beings, which can be interpreted as wise individuals or enlightened souls.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, this mantra can be applied to the pursuit of knowledge and continuous learning. For instance, a scientist may seek to enlighten their mind through research and collaboration with others.
    • In personal development, individuals may use this mantra as a reminder to seek wisdom from mentors or books, fostering a mindset of growth and enlightenment.
    • In spiritual practices, one might meditate on this mantra to enhance their understanding of the universe and their place within it, striving for a balance between the material and spiritual aspects of life.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the importance of awakening the intellect and seeking knowledge.
    • Its significance lies in the encouragement to pursue enlightenment and to surround oneself with wisdom and radiance.
    • In real life, it can be applied in various contexts, from personal growth to professional development, emphasizing the continuous journey towards understanding and enlightenment.
