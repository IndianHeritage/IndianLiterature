आ नो राधांसी सवितः स्तवध्या आ रयो यन्तु पर्वतस्य रातौ |सदा नो दिव्यः पायुः सिषक्तु यूयम् पात स्वस्तिभिः सदा नः ||
ā no rādhāṁsi savitaḥ stavadhyā ā rāyo yantu parvatasya rātau | sadā no divyaḥ pāyuḥ siṣaktu yūyam pāta svastibhiḥ sadā naḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā no: May we be blessed
    • rādhāṁsi: with prosperity
    • savitāḥ: by the Sun
    • stavadhyā: through praise
    • ā: may
    • rāyo: wealth
    • yantu: come to us
    • parvatasya: of the mountains
    • rātau: in the night
    • sadā: always
    • no: for us
    • divyaḥ: divine
    • pāyuḥ: strength
    • siṣaktu: may it bestow
    • yūyam: you all
    • pāta: protect
    • svastibhiḥ: with auspiciousness
    • sadā: always
    • naḥ: for us
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer for blessings and prosperity.
    • It invokes the Sun, a symbol of life and energy, to bestow wealth and strength upon us.
    • The reference to mountains signifies stability and the abundance of resources.
    • The mantra emphasizes the importance of divine protection and auspiciousness in our lives.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be recited to seek success and prosperity in ventures.
    • Individuals may chant this during personal challenges to invoke strength and protection.
    • Communities can use this mantra during festivals or gatherings to promote collective well-being and harmony.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its holistic approach to seeking blessings for prosperity, strength, and protection.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal growth to community welfare.
    • By invoking divine energies, individuals and communities can foster a sense of unity and purpose, leading to a more prosperous and harmonious existence.
