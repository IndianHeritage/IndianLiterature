याभिः पक्थम् अवथो याभिर अध्रिगुं याभिर बभ्रुं विजोषसम् |ताभिर नो मक्षू तुयम् अश्विनाऽ गतं भीषज्यतं यद आतुरम् ||
yābhiḥ paktham avatho yābhir adhriguṁ yābhir babhruṁ vijoṣasam | tābhir no makṣū tūyam aśvinā gatam bhiṣajyataṁ yad āturam ||
  • yābhiḥ paktham avatho: This phrase can be interpreted as "with those who are well-prepared" or "with those who are skilled." It emphasizes the importance of being surrounded by capable individuals who can support and uplift us.
  • yābhir adhriguṁ: This translates to "with those who are strong" or "with those who are resilient." It highlights the value of strength and resilience in overcoming challenges in life.
  • yābhir babhruṁ vijoṣasam: This can be understood as "with those who are wise and knowledgeable." Wisdom is a crucial aspect of making informed decisions and navigating life's complexities.
  • tābhir no makṣū tūyam: This phrase means "may we be protected by them." It signifies the desire for safety and well-being through the support of those around us.
  • aśvinā gatam bhiṣajyataṁ: This translates to "may the divine heal us." The Aśvins are considered divine physicians in Vedic literature, symbolizing healing and restoration.
  • yad āturam: This means "from whatever is ailing us." It acknowledges the presence of suffering or difficulties in life and the need for healing.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of community, strength, wisdom, and divine support in overcoming life's challenges. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various contexts such as:

  • In a workplace, surrounding oneself with skilled and resilient colleagues can lead to better teamwork and success.
  • In personal relationships, having wise friends or family members can provide guidance during tough times.
  • In health, seeking help from medical professionals (symbolized by the Aśvins) can lead to recovery from ailments.

This mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of individuals and the importance of seeking support and wisdom from others, as well as the divine, in our journey through life.
