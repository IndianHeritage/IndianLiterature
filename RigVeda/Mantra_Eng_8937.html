अव सृज पुनर अग्ने पितृभ्यो यस् त आहुतश्चरति स्वधाभिः |आयुर वसाना उप वेतु शेषः सं गच्छतां तन्‍वा जातवेदः ||
ava sṛja punar agne pitṛbhyo yas ta āhutaś carati svadhābhiḥ | āyur vasāna upa vetu śeṣaḥ saṁ gacchatāṁ tanvā jātavedaḥ ||
  • ava sṛja punar agne: This phrase translates to "O Agni, the divine fire, we invoke you again." Agni is often seen as a mediator between humans and the divine, representing energy and transformation. In real life, this can be likened to the way we seek inspiration or motivation in our daily lives, calling upon our inner strength to overcome challenges.
  • pitṛbhyo yas ta āhutaś carati svadhābhiḥ: This means "who moves with the offerings made to the ancestors." This highlights the importance of honoring our ancestors and the wisdom they impart. In modern life, this can be seen in practices such as family gatherings, where stories and traditions are shared, keeping the memory of ancestors alive.
  • āyur vasāna upa vetu śeṣaḥ: This translates to "may longevity dwell with us." It emphasizes the desire for a long and fulfilling life. In contemporary terms, this can relate to health and wellness practices, where individuals strive for a balanced lifestyle to ensure longevity and vitality.
  • saṁ gacchatāṁ tanvā jātavedaḥ: This means "may the body of the one who knows the truth go forth." It signifies the pursuit of knowledge and truth. In real life, this can be reflected in the quest for education and self-improvement, where individuals seek to expand their understanding of the world around them.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking divine guidance, honoring our roots, aspiring for longevity, and pursuing knowledge. Its significance lies in the holistic approach to life it advocates, reminding us to connect with our spiritual selves, respect our heritage, maintain our health, and continuously seek wisdom. In today's fast-paced world, these principles can be applied through mindfulness practices, family traditions, health-conscious living, and lifelong learning, fostering a balanced and meaningful existence.
