इयं सा वो अस्मे दीधितिर यजत्रा अपिप्राणि च सदनी च भूयः |नि या देवेषु यतते वसूयुर विद्यामेषं वृजनं जीरदानुम ||
iyaṁ sā vo asme dīdhitir yajatrā apiprāṇī ca sadanī ca bhūyāḥ | ni yā deveṣu yatate vasūyur vidyāmeṣaṁ vṛjanaṁ jīradānum ||
  • iyaṁ sā vo asme dīdhitir: This phrase can be translated as "This is the light that shines upon you." Here, "dīdhitir" refers to illumination or enlightenment. In real life, this can be likened to moments of inspiration or clarity that guide individuals in their decisions and actions.
  • yajatrā apiprāṇī ca sadanī ca bhūyāḥ: This part means "the one who performs the sacrifice, who is full of life and is a great abode." It emphasizes the importance of sacrifice and the vitality that comes from it. In practical terms, this can be seen in community service or charitable acts where individuals contribute for the greater good, creating a supportive environment.
  • ni yā deveṣu yatate vasūyur: This translates to "who strives for the divine and seeks wealth." It highlights the pursuit of spiritual and material wealth. In everyday life, this can be reflected in the balance between seeking personal success and maintaining spiritual integrity, such as pursuing a career while also engaging in spiritual practices.
  • vidyāmeṣaṁ vṛjanaṁ jīradānum: This means "this knowledge is the source of life." It underscores the significance of knowledge and wisdom as essential for a fulfilling life. In real-world scenarios, this can be seen in the value placed on education and lifelong learning, where knowledge empowers individuals to make informed choices and improve their circumstances.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of enlightenment, sacrifice, the pursuit of wealth, and the importance of knowledge. Its significance lies in the holistic approach it advocates for living a balanced life that integrates spiritual and material pursuits. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire individuals to seek clarity in their lives, engage in selfless acts for the community, strive for personal and spiritual growth, and value education as a means to enhance their existence. By embodying these principles, one can lead a more meaningful and enriched life.
