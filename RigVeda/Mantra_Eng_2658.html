ईळे अग्निं विपश्चितं गिरा यज्ञस्य साधनम् |श्रुष्टिवानं धितावानम् ||
īḻe agniṁ vipaścitaṁ girā yajñasya sādhanam | śruṣṭīvānaṁ dhitāvānam ||
  • īḻe: This word translates to "I invoke" or "I praise." It signifies the act of calling upon a divine presence or energy.
  • agniṁ: Refers to "Agni," the fire god in Vedic tradition. Agni symbolizes transformation, purification, and the divine messenger between humans and the gods.
  • vipaścitaṁ: This means "the wise" or "the knowledgeable." It highlights the importance of wisdom and understanding in our actions and rituals.
  • girā: Translates to "with words" or "through speech." This emphasizes the power of words and mantras in invoking divine energies.
  • yajñasya: Refers to "of the sacrifice" or "of the ritual." Yajna is a central concept in Vedic rituals, representing offerings made to the divine.
  • sādhanam: This means "the means" or "the instrument." It indicates the tools or methods used to achieve a spiritual goal.
  • śruṣṭīvānaṁ: This can be interpreted as "the one who creates" or "the creator." It signifies the creative force behind existence.
  • dhitāvānam: This translates to "the one who is established" or "the one who is firm." It represents stability and strength in the pursuit of spiritual goals.

The mantra can be understood as an invocation to Agni, the wise and knowledgeable fire god, to assist in the rituals and sacrifices we perform. It emphasizes the importance of wisdom, the power of words, and the means through which we connect with the divine. The mention of creation and stability suggests that through our rituals and offerings, we not only seek blessings but also contribute to the ongoing process of creation and maintain a firm foundation in our spiritual practices.


Conclusion: This mantra holds significant relevance in real-life scenarios, particularly in the context of rituals, personal growth, and spiritual practices. It teaches us the importance of intention, wisdom, and the power of our words in shaping our reality. By invoking Agni, we are reminded to approach our endeavors with knowledge and clarity, ensuring that our actions are aligned with our higher purpose. In modern life, this can be applied in various ways, such as in meditation, goal-setting, and even in our daily communications, where the intention behind our words can create transformative effects in our lives and the lives of others.
