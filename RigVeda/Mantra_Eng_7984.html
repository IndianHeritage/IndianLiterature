समीचीनानूनषत हरिं हिन्वन्त्य अद्रिभिः |योना ऋतस्य सीदत ||
samīcīnā anūṣata hariṁ hinvanty adribhiḥ | yonāv ṛtasya sīdata ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • samīcīnā: Those who are united or in harmony.
    • anūṣata: They follow or pursue.
    • hariṁ: The divine or the one who brings joy.
    • hinvanty: They drive away or remove.
    • adribhiḥ: By the mountains or obstacles.
    • yonāv: In the womb or source.
    • ṛtasya: Of truth or cosmic order.
    • sīdata: They become established or firm.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the importance of unity and harmony among individuals.
    • It suggests that when people come together in pursuit of a common goal, they can overcome obstacles (symbolized by mountains).
    • The divine presence (hariṁ) is invoked to help remove these obstacles, indicating that spiritual support is essential in our endeavors.
    • Furthermore, it emphasizes that through this unity and pursuit of truth (ṛtasya), one can establish a firm foundation for success and growth.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace, when team members collaborate effectively, they can achieve their targets more efficiently, overcoming challenges that may arise.
    • In community service, when individuals unite for a cause, such as environmental conservation, they can drive significant change, removing barriers to progress.
    • In personal relationships, open communication and mutual support can help partners navigate difficulties, reinforcing their bond.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of unity, collaboration, and the pursuit of truth in overcoming life's challenges.
    • It serves as a reminder that spiritual and communal support is vital in establishing a strong foundation for success.
    • In real-life scenarios, whether in personal, professional, or social contexts, the principles outlined in this mantra can guide individuals towards achieving their goals while fostering harmony and cooperation.
