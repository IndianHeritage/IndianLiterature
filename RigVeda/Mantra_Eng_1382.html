एतं शर्धं धाम यस्य सूरेर इत्य अवोचन दशतयस्य नंशे |द्युम्नानि येषु वसुतातीर रारण विश्वे संवन्तु प्रभृतेषु वाजम् ||
etaṁ śardhaṁ dhāma yasya sūrer ity avocan daśatayasya naṁśe | dyumnāni yeṣu vasutātī rāran viśve sanvantu prabhṛtheṣu vājam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • etaṁ: this
    • śardhaṁ: abundance or wealth
    • dhāma: abode or dwelling
    • yasya: of whom
    • sūrer: the wise or learned
    • ity: thus
    • avocan: they have spoken
    • daśatayasya: of the tenfold
    • naṁśe: portion or share
    • dyumnāni: glories or fame
    • yeṣu: in whom
    • vasutātī: wealth or prosperity
    • rāran: may they shine
    • viśve: all
    • sanvantu: may they come together
    • prabhṛtheṣu: in the great or vast
    • vājam: strength or power
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the abundance and wealth that belongs to the wise and learned individuals.
    • It emphasizes the importance of sharing and recognizing the contributions of all, particularly in the context of prosperity and strength.
    • The phrase "may they shine" suggests a call for collective success and recognition of achievements.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, this mantra can be applied to teamwork, where the contributions of each member are acknowledged, leading to a stronger and more successful organization.
    • In community service, it reflects the idea that when individuals come together to share their resources and knowledge, they can create a more prosperous environment for all.
    • In education, it highlights the importance of collaboration among students and teachers, where shared knowledge leads to greater learning outcomes.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for unity, recognition, and the sharing of wealth and knowledge.
    • It serves as a reminder that collective efforts and shared wisdom lead to greater achievements and prosperity.
    • In real-life scenarios, applying the principles of this mantra can foster collaboration, enhance community spirit, and promote a culture of appreciation and support.
