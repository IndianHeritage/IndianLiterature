वाजेषु सासहिर भव त्वाम इमहे शतक्रतो |इन्द्र वृत्राय हन्तवे ||
vājeṣu sāsahir bhava tvām īmahe śatakrato | indra vṛtrāya hantave ||
  • vājeṣu: This word translates to "in the context of strength or power." It signifies the importance of vigor and energy in our endeavors.
  • sāsahir: This term means "with the help of." It emphasizes the need for support and collaboration in achieving goals.
  • bhava: This means "become" or "may you become." It is a call to action, urging the recipient to embody certain qualities.
  • tvām: This translates to "you," indicating a direct address to a deity or a powerful force.
  • īmahe: This means "we meditate upon" or "we invoke." It reflects the act of contemplation and reverence.
  • śatakrato: This term refers to "the one with a hundred powers," often associated with the deity Indra, who is known for his strength and prowess.
  • indra: Indra is the king of the gods in Vedic tradition, symbolizing power, rain, and thunder.
  • vṛtrāya: This means "to Vritra," a serpent or dragon that represents obstacles or challenges.
  • hantave: This translates to "to be defeated" or "to be vanquished," indicating the overcoming of challenges.

The mantra can be interpreted as a prayer or invocation for strength and support in overcoming obstacles, particularly those represented by Vritra. It emphasizes the importance of collaboration and the invocation of divine powers to achieve success.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as:

  • Personal Challenges: When facing difficulties in life, one can meditate on this mantra to seek strength and support from others, as well as from a higher power.
  • Teamwork: In a professional setting, this mantra can serve as a reminder of the importance of collaboration and collective effort in overcoming challenges.
  • Spiritual Growth: It encourages individuals to reflect on their inner strength and the support they can draw from their beliefs and community.

In conclusion, this mantra signifies the importance of invoking strength and support in the face of challenges. It serves as a reminder that with collaboration and divine assistance, one can overcome obstacles and achieve their goals. Whether in personal struggles, professional teamwork, or spiritual journeys, the essence of this mantra is applicable in various aspects of life, encouraging resilience and unity.
