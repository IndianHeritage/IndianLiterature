जानत्य अहनः प्रथमस्य नाम शुक्रा कृष्णाद अजनिष्ट श्वितीची |ऋतस्य योषा न मिनाति धामाहर-हर निष्कृतम् आचरन्ती ||
jānaty ahnaḥ prathamasya nāma śukrā kṛṣṇād ajaniṣṭa śvitīcī | ṛtasya yoṣā na mināti dhāmāhar-ahar niṣkṛtam ācarantī ||
  • jānaty ahnaḥ prathamasya nāma: This phrase translates to "knows the name of the first day." Here, "ahnaḥ" refers to the day, and "prathamasya" means the first. This can be interpreted as the importance of recognizing beginnings, whether in a day or in life events.
  • śukrā kṛṣṇād ajaniṣṭa: This part means "the bright one was born from the dark." "Śukrā" signifies brightness or purity, while "kṛṣṇād" refers to darkness. This duality can be seen in real life, where positive outcomes often arise from challenging or dark situations, such as personal growth following adversity.
  • śvitīcī: This term can be understood as "the one who shines." It emphasizes the idea of enlightenment and knowledge, suggesting that wisdom and clarity emerge from understanding the dualities of life.
  • ṛtasya yoṣā na mināti: This translates to "the woman of truth does not falter." "Rta" signifies truth or cosmic order, and "yoṣā" refers to a woman. This can be related to the strength of character and integrity in individuals, particularly women, who uphold truth in their actions and decisions.
  • dhāmāhar-ahar niṣkṛtam ācarantī: This means "day after day, they perform the righteous deeds." "Dhāmāhar" refers to the act of gathering or collecting, while "niṣkṛtam" signifies righteous actions. This highlights the importance of consistent ethical behavior and the impact of daily actions on the greater good.

  • Conclusion: This mantra emphasizes the significance of recognizing beginnings, the interplay of light and darkness, the strength of truth, and the importance of consistent righteous actions. In real life, it serves as a reminder to embrace challenges as opportunities for growth, to uphold integrity, and to engage in daily practices that contribute positively to society. Whether in personal development, community service, or professional ethics, the teachings of this mantra can guide individuals toward a more meaningful and impactful life.
