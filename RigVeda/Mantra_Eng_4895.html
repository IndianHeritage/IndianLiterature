मन्त्र: उता त्वं सूनो सहसो नो अद्यā देवाम् अस्मिन् अध्वरे ववृत्याः |स्याम अहं ते सदमिद् रातौ तव स्याम अग्ने 'वसā सुवीरः ||
uta tvaṁ sūno sahaso no adyā devām̐ asminn adhvare vavṛtyāḥ | syām ahaṁ te sadam id rātau tava syām agne 'vasā suvīraḥ ||
  • Mantra: "uta tvaṁ sūno sahaso no adyā devām̐ asminn adhvare vavṛtyāḥ | syām ahaṁ te sadam id rātau tava syām agne 'vasā suvīraḥ ||"
  • Translation: "O Agni, may we be strong today, as we invoke the divine powers in this sacrifice. May I be with you in the night, and may I be a worthy companion to you, O Agni, who is rich in strength."

  • Word-by-Word Explanation:
    • uta: a particle indicating a wish or desire.
    • tvaṁ: you (referring to Agni, the fire god).
    • sūno: strong or powerful.
    • sahaso: together or in unity.
    • no: our.
    • adyā: today.
    • devām̐: divine beings or gods.
    • asminn: in this (context of the sacrifice).
    • adhvare: in the ritual or sacrifice.
    • vavṛtyāḥ: may manifest or come forth.
    • syām: may I be.
    • ahaṁ: I.
    • te: your.
    • sadam: a place of dwelling or abode.
    • id: this.
    • rātau: in the night.
    • tava: your.
    • syām: may I be.
    • agne: O Agni (fire).
    • 'vasā: rich or abundant.
    • suvīraḥ: strong or valiant.

  • Significance:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of invoking divine strength and unity in our endeavors.
    • Agni, as the fire god, symbolizes transformation, energy, and the divine spark within us.
    • The mantra reflects a desire for strength and companionship in both spiritual and worldly pursuits.
  • Real-Life Application:
    • In daily life, this mantra can be a reminder to seek strength and support from others when facing challenges.
    • It encourages individuals to come together in unity, whether in family, community, or workplace settings, to achieve common goals.
    • In moments of difficulty, reciting this mantra can serve as a source of motivation and a call for divine assistance.

  • Conclusion:
    • The mantra serves as a powerful invocation for strength, unity, and divine support in our lives.
    • By recognizing the importance of collaboration and seeking divine guidance, we can navigate life's challenges more effectively.
    • Ultimately, it teaches us that we are not alone in our struggles and that together, with divine assistance, we can achieve greatness.
