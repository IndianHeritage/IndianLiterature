कूष्ठो देवाव अश्विनाद्याः दिवो मनावसू |तच्छ्रवथो वृषणवसू अत्रीर वाम आ विवासति ||
kūṣṭho devāv aśvinādyā divo manāvasū | tac chravatho vṛṣaṇvasū atrir vām ā vivāsati ||
  • kūṣṭho: This term refers to a person who is afflicted or suffering, often associated with ailments or diseases.
  • devāv: This means 'gods' or 'divine beings', indicating a higher power or celestial entities.
  • aśvinādyā: Refers to the Aśvins, the twin gods in Vedic mythology known for their healing abilities and association with health and medicine.
  • dīvo: This translates to 'of the sky' or 'heaven', suggesting a connection to the divine or celestial realm.
  • manāvasū: This term can be interpreted as 'the thoughts or intentions of the mind', indicating the mental aspect of well-being.
  • tac: This means 'that' or 'it', referring to the subject of the mantra.
  • chravatho: This term can be understood as 'of the one who hears' or 'listening', emphasizing the importance of attentiveness.
  • vṛṣaṇvasū: This can be interpreted as 'the one who brings strength or vigor', indicating a source of power or energy.
  • atrir: Refers to a sage or seer, often associated with wisdom and knowledge.
  • vām: This means 'your' or 'of you', indicating a personal connection.
  • ā vivāsati: This translates to 'may it be revealed' or 'may it come forth', suggesting a desire for enlightenment or understanding.

The mantra essentially invokes the divine Aśvins to aid those who are suffering, emphasizing the importance of mental clarity and attentiveness in the healing process. It highlights the connection between the divine and human experience, suggesting that through divine intervention and personal intention, one can overcome ailments and achieve well-being.


In real life, this mantra can be applicable in various scenarios, such as:

  • Health and Healing: Individuals facing health challenges can recite this mantra to seek divine assistance and healing, fostering a mindset of hope and recovery.
  • Mental Clarity: The emphasis on mental intentions encourages individuals to focus on positive thoughts and clarity, which can be beneficial in stressful situations.
  • Spiritual Connection: It serves as a reminder of the connection between the divine and human experience, encouraging individuals to seek guidance and support from higher powers.

Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine intervention in times of suffering, promoting healing and mental clarity. It is applicable in everyday life as a source of strength and encouragement, reminding individuals of the power of intention and the support available from the divine realm.
