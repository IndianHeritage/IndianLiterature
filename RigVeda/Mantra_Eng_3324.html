ऋतस्य दृढा धरुणानि सन्ति पुरूणि चन्द्रा वपुषे वपूंषि |ऋतेन दीर्घम् इषणन्त पृक्ष ऋतेन गावा ऋतम् आ विवेशुः ||
ṛtasya dṛḻhā dharuṇāni santi purūṇi candrā vapuṣe vapūṁṣi | ṛtena dīrgham iṣaṇanta pṛkṣa ṛtena gāva ṛtam ā viveśuḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ṛtasya: of truth or order
    • dṛḻhā: firm or strong
    • dharuṇāni: supports or pillars
    • santi: exist or are
    • purūṇi: ancient or old
    • candrā: moon or lunar
    • vapuṣe: form or body
    • vapūṁṣi: bodies or forms
    • ṛtena: through truth
    • dīrgham: long or extended
    • iṣaṇanta: to be desired or sought
    • pṛkṣa: to be perceived or observed
    • gāva: cows or wealth
    • ṛtam: truth or order
    • ā viveśuḥ: enter or come into
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the enduring nature of truth (ṛta) and its foundational role in the universe.
    • The "strong supports" (dṛḻhā dharuṇāni) symbolize the principles and values that uphold society and life.
    • The reference to the "ancient moon" (purūṇi candrā) suggests the timelessness of these truths, which have been recognized since ancient times.
    • The mantra emphasizes that through truth (ṛtena), one can achieve long-lasting desires (dīrgham iṣaṇanta) and gain insight (pṛkṣa).
    • The mention of "cows" (gāva) symbolizes wealth and prosperity, indicating that truth leads to abundance.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In personal relationships, honesty and integrity serve as the "strong supports" that maintain trust and connection.
    • In business, companies that adhere to ethical practices often find long-term success and loyalty from customers.
    • In governance, transparency and truthfulness in leadership foster a healthy society and promote civic engagement.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of truth as a guiding principle in all aspects of life.
    • It teaches that by embracing truth, individuals and societies can achieve stability, prosperity, and harmony.
    • In a world often filled with uncertainty, this mantra serves as a reminder to seek and uphold truth in our actions and decisions.
