हरī नु कं रथ इन्द्रस्य योजम् आयै सूक्तेन वचसā नवेन |मो षु त्वां अत्र बहवो हि विप्रā नि रीर्मन यजमानासो अन्ये ||
harī nu kaṁ ratha indrasya yojam āyai sūktena vacasā navena | mo ṣu tvām atra bahavo hi viprā ni rīraman yajamānāso anye ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • harī: O Lord, often referring to a divine being or God.
    • nu: indeed, or surely.
    • kaṁ: who or what.
    • ratha: chariot, symbolizing movement or progress.
    • indrasya: of Indra, the king of gods, representing power and authority.
    • yojam: connection or union.
    • āyai: to come or arrive.
    • sūktena: through the hymn or sacred verse.
    • vacasā: with words or speech.
    • navena: new or fresh.
    • mo: may you.
    • ṣu: be or become.
    • tvām: you.
    • atra: here or in this place.
    • bahavo: many or numerous.
    • hi: indeed or surely.
    • viprā: the wise or learned ones.
    • ni: not or without.
    • rīraman: to be pleased or satisfied.
    • yajamānāso: those who are performing the sacrifice.
    • anye: others or different ones.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a call to the divine, specifically to Indra, seeking guidance and connection.
    • The reference to the chariot symbolizes the journey of life and the need for divine support in our endeavors.
    • The use of "new words" suggests the importance of fresh perspectives and innovative approaches in our prayers and actions.
    • The mention of "many wise ones" indicates that collective wisdom and community support are essential in achieving our goals.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, when launching a new project, teams often seek the input of experienced colleagues (the "wise ones") to ensure success.
    • In personal life, individuals may turn to mentors or spiritual leaders for guidance during significant life changes, akin to calling upon Indra for support.
    • Innovative ideas in technology often arise from collaborative discussions, reflecting the mantra's emphasis on new words and collective effort.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking divine guidance and the value of community wisdom in our pursuits.
    • It encourages individuals to approach challenges with a fresh perspective and to recognize the power of collaboration.
    • In real life, whether in personal growth, professional endeavors, or spiritual journeys, invoking this mantra can serve as a reminder to remain open to new ideas and the support of others.
