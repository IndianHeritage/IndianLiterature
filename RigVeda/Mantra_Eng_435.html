ऊर्ध्वो नः पाह्य अंहसो नि केतुनā विश्वं सम अत्रिणं दह | कृधī न ऊर्ध्वाण् चरथाय जीवसे विदा देवेषु नो दुवः ||
ūrdhvo naḥ pāhy aṁhaso ni ketunā viśvaṁ sam atriṇaṁ daha | kṛdhī na ūrdhvāñ carathāya jīvase vidā deveṣu no duvaḥ ||
  • ūrdhvo naḥ pāhy aṁhaso: This phrase can be translated as "May the higher powers protect us from all harm." The term "ūrdhvo" refers to the higher realms or spiritual elevation, while "pāhy" means to protect. In real life, this can be seen as a call for divine protection in our endeavors, whether in personal challenges or professional pursuits.
  • ni ketunā viśvaṁ sam atriṇaṁ daha: This part translates to "With the guiding light, may we burn away all obstacles." "Ketunā" refers to a guiding light or a beacon, and "daha" means to burn. This can be likened to the way a lighthouse guides ships safely to shore, symbolizing the importance of guidance in overcoming life's challenges.
  • kṛdhī na ūrdhvāñ carathāya: This means "May we strive towards the higher path." "Kṛdhī" implies effort or striving, while "carathāya" refers to the path or journey. In everyday life, this can be related to the pursuit of personal growth, education, or spiritual enlightenment, emphasizing the importance of continuous effort in achieving our goals.
  • jīvase vidā deveṣu no duvaḥ: This translates to "May we live in harmony with the divine." "Jīvase" means to live, "vidā" refers to knowledge or wisdom, and "deveṣu" means in the divine or with the gods. This can be interpreted as a reminder to align our actions with higher principles and values, fostering a sense of community and respect for the divine in our lives.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a profound message of seeking protection, guidance, and striving for higher ideals while living in harmony with the divine. Its significance lies in its applicability to various aspects of life, such as personal development, ethical living, and the pursuit of knowledge. In real-life scenarios, individuals can invoke this mantra during challenging times, seeking strength and clarity in their decisions, and fostering a sense of purpose and direction in their lives. By embodying the principles of this mantra, one can cultivate resilience, wisdom, and a deeper connection to the world around them.
