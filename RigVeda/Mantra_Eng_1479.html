उभे पुनामि रोडसी ऋतेन द्रुहो दहामि सम महीर अनिन्द्राः | अभिवलग्य यत्र हता अमित्रा वैलस्थाणं परि तृझा अशेरन् ||
ubhe punāmi rodasī ṛtena druho dahāmi sam mahīr anindrāḥ | abhivlagya yatra hatā amitrā vailasthānam pari tṛḻhā aśeran ||
  • Mantra: "ubhe punāmi rodasī ṛtena druho dahāmi sam mahīr anindrāḥ | abhivlagya yatra hatā amitrā vailasthānam pari tṛḻhā aśeran ||"
  • Translation: "I invoke both the heavens and the earth with the truth; I burn away the deceitful ones. May the great ones, who are free from sin, protect me. Where the enemies are defeated, may I find a place of strength and stability."

  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ubhe: both
    • punāmi: I invoke or I purify
    • rodasī: heavens and earth
    • ṛtena: with truth
    • druho: deceitful or harmful
    • dahāmi: I burn or I destroy
    • sam: together or in harmony
    • mahīr: great ones or powerful beings
    • anindrāḥ: free from sin or faultless
    • abhivlagya: having been defeated
    • yatra: where
    • hatā: defeated
    • amitrā: enemies
    • vailasthānam: place of strength or stability
    • pari: all around
    • tṛḻhā: may I find or may I attain
    • aśeran: may I be protected or may I be safe

  • Significance: This mantra emphasizes the importance of truth and the power of invoking divine protection against deceit and negativity. It reflects a desire for harmony between the heavens and the earth, suggesting that our actions should align with universal truths.
  • Real-Life Application:
    • In personal relationships, this mantra can serve as a reminder to seek honesty and transparency, helping to build trust and eliminate deceit.
    • In professional settings, it encourages integrity in business practices, promoting a culture where truth prevails over manipulation.
    • In times of conflict, it can inspire individuals to seek resolution through understanding and truth rather than aggression, fostering peace and stability.

  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a powerful invocation for truth and protection. It encourages individuals to align their actions with higher principles, seek strength in unity, and create a stable environment free from deceit. By embodying these values, one can navigate life's challenges with integrity and resilience.
