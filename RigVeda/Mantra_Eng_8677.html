पुनातादक्षसाधनं यथा शर्धाय वीतये |यथा मित्राय वरुणाय शान्तमः ||
punātā dakṣasādhanaṁ yathā śardhāya vītaye | yathā mitrāya varuṇāya śaṁtamaḥ ||
  • punātā: This word translates to "purification" or "cleansing." It signifies the importance of mental and spiritual clarity.
  • dakṣasādhanaṁ: This refers to "skillful means" or "capable actions." It emphasizes the need for competence and effectiveness in our endeavors.
  • yathā: Meaning "as" or "in the way that," it indicates a comparison or a method.
  • śardhāya: This word can be interpreted as "for the purpose of faith" or "in devotion." It highlights the role of belief and trust in our actions.
  • vītaye: This translates to "for the sake of liberation" or "to achieve freedom." It points towards the ultimate goal of spiritual and personal growth.
  • mitrāya: Meaning "to the friend," it signifies the importance of relationships and community.
  • varuṇāya: This refers to "to Varuna," the deity associated with cosmic order and moral authority, symbolizing the need for righteousness.
  • śaṁtamaḥ: This translates to "peace" or "tranquility," indicating the desired outcome of the actions taken.

The mantra emphasizes the significance of purification and skillful actions in the pursuit of faith and liberation. It suggests that by acting with competence and devotion, one can achieve peace and harmony in relationships, guided by moral principles.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios:

  • In personal development, individuals can focus on cleansing their thoughts and actions to enhance their skills and achieve their goals.
  • In professional settings, teamwork and collaboration can be improved by fostering trust and competence among colleagues, leading to a harmonious work environment.
  • In community service, acting with a sense of duty and moral responsibility can help in building stronger relationships and promoting peace.

In conclusion, this mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of purification, skillful action, faith, and peace. It encourages individuals to strive for personal and communal growth while adhering to moral values, ultimately leading to a more harmonious existence.
