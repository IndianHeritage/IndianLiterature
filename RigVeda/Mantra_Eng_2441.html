वव्राजाऽसिम् अनदतीर् अदब्धा दिवो यह्वीः अवसाना अनग्नाः |सनाऽत्र युवतयः सयोनिः एकं गर्भं दधिरे सप्त वाणयः ||
vavrājā sīm anadatīr adabdhā divo yahvīr avasānā anagnāḥ | sanā atra yuvatayaḥ sayonīr ekaṁ garbhaṁ dadhire sapta vāṇīḥ ||
  • vavrājā sīm anadatīr adabdhā: This phrase can be interpreted as "the one who has crossed the boundaries, untainted and unblemished." In real life, this can refer to individuals who strive to maintain their integrity and purity in their actions and thoughts, despite the challenges they face. For example, a whistleblower in a corporate setting who exposes wrongdoing while maintaining their ethical standards embodies this idea.
  • dīvo yahvīr avasānā anagnāḥ: This translates to "those who are free from the constraints of the heavens, untouched." This can symbolize people who rise above societal expectations and limitations. A real-life example could be an artist who breaks conventional norms to express their creativity, thereby inspiring others to pursue their passions without fear of judgment.
  • sanā atra yuvatayaḥ sayonīr: This means "the young ones here, who are full of life and energy." It highlights the vitality and potential of youth. In a practical context, this can be seen in youth-led movements for social change, such as climate activism, where young individuals passionately advocate for a sustainable future.
  • ekaṁ garbhaṁ dadhire sapta vāṇīḥ: This phrase can be interpreted as "they hold one essence, with seven voices." This signifies unity in diversity, where different perspectives come together for a common purpose. An example of this is a collaborative project where individuals from various backgrounds contribute their unique skills to achieve a shared goal, such as a community development initiative.

  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of integrity, the courage to transcend limitations, the vitality of youth, and the power of unity in diversity. In real life, these principles can guide individuals and communities in their pursuit of truth, creativity, and collective progress. By embodying these values, we can create a more harmonious and just society, where everyone has the opportunity to thrive and contribute meaningfully.
