आ धरणसिर बृहद्दिवो रराणो विश्वेभिर गन्त्वोमभिर हुवानः |ज्ञा वसाना ओषधीर अमृतध्रस त्रिधातुशृङ्गो वृषभो वयोधाः ||
ā dharṇasir bṛhaddivo rarāṇo viśvebhir gantv omabhir huvānaḥ | gnā vasāna oṣadhīr amṛdhras tridhātuśṛṅgo vṛṣabho vayodhāḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā: from, out of
    • dharṇasir: the one who supports or holds
    • bṛhad: great, vast
    • divo: of the sky, heavenly
    • rarāṇo: moving, going
    • viśvebhir: with all, universal
    • gantv: going, moving towards
    • omabhir: with offerings, prayers
    • huvānaḥ: invoking, calling upon
    • gnā: knowledge, wisdom
    • vasāna: dwelling, residing
    • oṣadhīr: herbs, plants
    • amṛdhras: immortal, eternal
    • tridhātuśṛṅgo: three realms, three worlds
    • vṛṣabho: the bull, symbol of strength
    • vayodhāḥ: the warriors, those who strive
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the vastness and support of the universe, suggesting that there is a divine force that holds everything together.
    • The phrase "with all, universal" indicates a collective movement towards a common goal, emphasizing unity and cooperation.
    • Invoking knowledge and wisdom signifies the importance of understanding and enlightenment in our lives.
    • The mention of "herbs" and "immortal" reflects the connection between nature and the eternal aspects of life, suggesting that wisdom can be found in the natural world.
    • The "three realms" can be interpreted as the physical, mental, and spiritual dimensions of existence, highlighting the need for balance in all aspects of life.
    • The "bull" symbolizes strength and determination, while "the warriors" represent those who strive for their goals, indicating that perseverance is essential for success.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a team project, the mantra's emphasis on unity can be seen when team members collaborate effectively, each contributing their strengths towards a common goal.
    • In personal growth, seeking knowledge and wisdom can be likened to reading books or attending workshops that enhance one's understanding of the world.
    • In environmental conservation, recognizing the connection between nature and immortality can inspire individuals to protect the earth's resources for future generations.
    • In sports, the determination of athletes to overcome challenges reflects the "warriors" striving for victory, showcasing the importance of resilience.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of all beings and the importance of wisdom, strength, and unity in our lives.
    • Its significance lies in encouraging individuals to seek knowledge, work together, and strive for their goals while respecting the natural world.
    • In real-life scenarios, applying the principles of this mantra can lead to personal growth, successful teamwork, and a deeper appreciation for the environment.
