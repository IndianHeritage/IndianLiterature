मन्दिष्ट यद उषाणे काव्ये सच्चाम् इन्द्रो वङ्कू वङ्कुतराधि तिष्ठति |उग्रो ययिं निर अपः स्रोतसासृजद् विशुष्णस्य दृष्मिता ऐरयत पुरः ||
mandiṣṭa yad uśane kāvye sacām̐ indro vaṅkū vaṅkutarādhi tiṣṭhati | ugro yayiṁ nir apaḥ srotasāsṛjad vi śuṣṇasya dṛṁhitā airayat puraḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • mandiṣṭa: that which is desired or sought after
    • yad: which
    • uśane: in the light or brilliance
    • kāvye: in poetry or art
    • sacām̐: with truth or reality
    • indro: Indra, the king of gods
    • vaṅkū: the one who is powerful or strong
    • vaṅkutarādhi: the one who is exalted or elevated
    • tiṣṭhati: stands or resides
    • ugro: fierce or strong
    • yayiṁ: who
    • nir: without
    • apaḥ: water
    • srotasāsṛjad: has created streams or flows
    • vi: in the presence of
    • śuṣṇasya: of the scorching or burning
    • dṛṁhitā: has been established or fixed
    • airayat: has brought forth or produced
    • puraḥ: in front or ahead
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the pursuit of desires that are illuminated by truth and creativity.
    • It invokes the presence of Indra, symbolizing strength and leadership, suggesting that true power comes from a foundation of truth.
    • The imagery of water and streams indicates the flow of life and knowledge, essential for growth and sustenance.
    • The mention of scorching suggests challenges or trials that one must face, yet it also emphasizes resilience and the ability to thrive despite adversity.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In the realm of art and literature, creators often seek inspiration that is both truthful and profound, much like the light mentioned in the mantra.
    • In leadership, a strong leader (like Indra) must stand firm in their values and lead with integrity, inspiring others to follow.
    • In personal growth, individuals may face challenges (the scorching) but can emerge stronger by embracing their truths and learning from their experiences.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of seeking truth and creativity in all endeavors.
    • It teaches that strength and resilience are essential in overcoming life's challenges.
    • In real life, it encourages individuals to pursue their passions with integrity and to remain steadfast in the face of adversity, ultimately leading to personal and collective growth.
