अयं स्वादुर इह मदीष्ट आस यस्येन्द्रो वृत्रहत्ये ममाद || पुरूणि यश च्यौत्ना शम्बरस्य विनवतीं नव च देह्योऽहन ||
ayaṁ svādur iha madiṣṭha āsa yasyendro vṛtrahatye mamāda | purūṇi yaś cyautnā śambarasya vi navatiṁ nava ca dehyo3 han ||
  • ayaṁ svādur iha madiṣṭha āsa: This phrase can be translated to "This is sweet, here is the best place." It signifies the importance of finding joy and comfort in one's surroundings. In real life, this can relate to creating a nurturing environment at home or in the workplace where individuals feel valued and happy.
  • yasyendro vṛtrahatye mamāda: This part means "Whose Indra (the king of gods) has vanquished the Vritra." Vritra symbolizes obstacles or challenges. This can be interpreted as the triumph of good over evil or the overcoming of difficulties. For example, in personal life, it can refer to overcoming fears or challenges, much like a student conquering their anxiety before an important exam.
  • purūṇi yaś cyautnā śambarasya: This translates to "The many glories of the one who has defeated Shambara." Shambara represents ignorance or delusion. This line emphasizes the importance of wisdom and knowledge in achieving success. In a real-world context, this can be seen in the pursuit of education and self-improvement, where individuals strive to gain knowledge to overcome ignorance.
  • vi navatiṁ nava ca dehyo3 han: This means "He who has attained the new and the old." It suggests the importance of balance between tradition and innovation. In modern society, this can be applied to businesses that respect traditional values while also embracing new technologies and ideas to stay relevant and competitive.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of overcoming challenges, the pursuit of knowledge, and the balance between tradition and innovation. Its significance lies in its applicability to various aspects of life, from personal growth to professional success. By embracing the teachings of this mantra, individuals can strive for a fulfilling life, characterized by joy, wisdom, and resilience in the face of adversity.
