अप त्या अस्तुर अनिरा अमीवा निर अतृसन् तमिषीचीर अभैषुः |आ सोमो अस्मां अरुहद विहायाऽगन्म यत्र प्रतिरन्त आायुः ||
apa tyā asthur anirā amīvā nir atrasan tamiṣīcīr abhaiṣuḥ | ā somo asmām̐ aruhad vihāyā aganma yatra pratiranta āyuḥ ||
  • apa tyā asthur anirā amīvā nir atrasan tamiṣīcīr abhaiṣuḥ
    • apa - away, from
    • tyā - that, which
    • asthur - is not to be harmed
    • anirā - without fear
    • amīvā - without obstacles
    • nir - without
    • atrasan - any hindrance
    • tamiṣīcīr - in darkness
    • abhaiṣuḥ - free from fear
  • ā somo asmām̐ aruhad vihāyā aganma yatra pratiranta āyuḥ
    • ā - from
    • somo - the moon, or the essence of life
    • asmām̐ - from us
    • aruhad - has ascended
    • vihāyā - having left
    • aganma - has come
    • yatra - where
    • pratiranta - the end, or limit
    • āyuḥ - life, longevity

This mantra from the Rigveda speaks to the themes of protection, fearlessness, and the pursuit of life and longevity. The first part emphasizes a state of being free from harm and fear, suggesting a life lived without obstacles and in the light, away from darkness. This can be interpreted as a call for inner strength and resilience in the face of challenges.


The second part of the mantra invokes the essence of life, symbolized by the moon, which is often associated with nourishment and growth. It suggests that this essence has ascended from us, indicating a connection to a higher power or the universe, and that it brings life and longevity to those who embrace it.


Real-Life Examples:

  • In personal development, individuals often seek to overcome fears and obstacles in their lives. This mantra can serve as a reminder to cultivate courage and resilience, allowing one to navigate life's challenges with confidence.
  • In health and wellness, the pursuit of longevity and a fulfilling life can be seen in practices such as meditation, yoga, and healthy living. The essence of life, as represented by the moon, can symbolize the importance of balance and harmony in one's lifestyle.
  • In community and relationships, fostering an environment free from fear and harm can lead to stronger bonds and a supportive network. This mantra encourages individuals to create safe spaces for themselves and others.

Conclusion:

The significance of this mantra lies in its powerful message of fearlessness, protection, and the pursuit of a fulfilling life. It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal growth to community building, reminding us to seek light over darkness and to embrace the essence of life that nourishes our existence. By embodying these principles, we can lead lives that are not only longer but also richer and more meaningful.
