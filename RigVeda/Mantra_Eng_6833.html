स समुद्रो अपीच्यस तुरो द्यां इव रोहति न यद आसु यजुर दधे |स मायā आर्चिनā पदास्तृणान नाकम आरुहन नभन्ताम् अन्यके समे ||
sa samudro apīcyas turo dyām iva rohati ni yad āsu yajur dadhe | sa māyā arcinā padāstṛṇān nākam āruhan nabhantām anyake same ||
  • sa samudro apīcyas: This phrase can be translated as "that ocean, indeed, is vast." Here, the ocean symbolizes the infinite and boundless nature of existence. In real life, we often encounter vastness in nature, such as the ocean itself or the sky, which can evoke feelings of awe and humility.
  • turo dyām iva rohati: This translates to "like a swift chariot, it ascends to the sky." The imagery of a chariot suggests speed and movement, representing the journey of life and the pursuit of goals. In our daily lives, we often strive to achieve our aspirations, much like a chariot racing towards its destination.
  • ni yad āsu yajur dadhe: This means "wherever the sacrifice is placed." Sacrifice here refers to the efforts and dedication we put into our endeavors. In real life, this can be seen in various contexts, such as the hard work put into a career, education, or personal relationships, where we often have to make sacrifices to achieve our goals.
  • sa māyā arcinā padāstṛṇān: This translates to "that illusion, with its radiant light, covers the paths." The term "illusion" can refer to the distractions and challenges we face in life. The radiant light symbolizes knowledge and awareness that can help us navigate through these distractions. For example, in a world filled with information overload, being discerning and focused can help us find our true path.
  • nākam āruhan nabhantām: This means "ascending to the heavens, those who are not bound." This phrase suggests liberation and transcendence. In real life, this can be interpreted as the pursuit of spiritual growth or personal freedom, where individuals seek to rise above their limitations and societal constraints.
  • anyake same: This translates to "like others." It implies a sense of unity and interconnectedness among all beings. In our daily lives, recognizing our shared experiences and challenges can foster empathy and compassion towards others.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates profound themes of vastness, aspiration, sacrifice, illusion, liberation, and interconnectedness. Its significance lies in encouraging individuals to recognize the boundless nature of existence, pursue their goals with dedication, and navigate through life's distractions with awareness. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire us to strive for personal growth, foster connections with others, and remain focused on our true aspirations amidst the chaos of modern life.
