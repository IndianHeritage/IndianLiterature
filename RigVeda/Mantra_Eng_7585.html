अध द्रप्सो अंशुमत्याऽ उपस्थे धारणत तंवं तित्विषाणः |विशो अदेवीर अभ्य आचारन्तीर बृहस्पतिना युजेंद्रः ससाहे ||
adha drapso aṁśumatyā upasthe 'dhārayat tanvaṁ titviṣāṇaḥ | viśo adevīr abhy ā3carantīr bṛhaspatinā yujendraḥ sasāhe ||
  • Adha drapso aṁśumatyā: This phrase can be translated as "with the light of the sun." Here, "adha" means "below" or "under," "drapso" refers to "the light" or "radiance," and "aṁśumatyā" means "of the sun." This signifies the importance of sunlight in our lives, representing knowledge, clarity, and enlightenment.
  • Upasthe 'dhārayat: This translates to "holding or supporting." "Upasthe" means "in the presence of" or "near," and "dhārayat" means "to hold" or "to support." This can be interpreted as the support we receive from our surroundings, including nature and community, which helps us thrive.
  • Tanvaṁ titviṣāṇaḥ: This means "with a radiant body." "Tanvaṁ" refers to "body" or "form," and "titviṣāṇaḥ" means "radiant" or "shining." This emphasizes the idea of being vibrant and full of life, suggesting that a healthy body and mind are essential for achieving our goals.
  • Viśo adevīr abhy ā3carantīr: This can be translated as "the divine beings who act in the world." "Viśo" means "of the world" or "universe," "adevīr" refers to "divine beings," and "abhy ā3carantīr" means "who perform actions." This highlights the interconnectedness of all beings and the divine forces that guide our actions.
  • Bṛhaspatinā yujendraḥ sasāhe: This translates to "with the guidance of the great teacher." "Bṛhaspatinā" refers to "Bṛhaspati," the deity of wisdom and knowledge, "yujendraḥ" means "the lord of yoga" or "the one who unites," and "sasāhe" means "to endure" or "to support." This signifies the importance of wisdom and guidance in navigating life's challenges.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of light, support, vibrancy, divine guidance, and wisdom in our lives. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied to various aspects such as personal growth, community support, and the pursuit of knowledge. For instance, when facing challenges, seeking guidance from mentors or wise individuals can illuminate the path forward. Additionally, fostering a supportive community can enhance our well-being and help us thrive. Ultimately, this mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of life and the importance of nurturing our physical, mental, and spiritual selves.
