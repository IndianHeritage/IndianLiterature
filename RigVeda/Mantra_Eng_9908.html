ओषधीः इति मातरस् तद् वो देवीर उप ब्रुवे |सनेयम् अश्वं गां वास आत्मानं तव पुरुष ||
oṣadhīr iti mātaras tad vo devīr upa bruve | saneyam aśvaṁ gāṁ vāsa ātmānaṁ tava pūruṣa ||
  • Oṣadhīr iti mātaras: This phrase can be translated as "O herbs, O plants." Here, "oṣadhīr" refers to medicinal plants and herbs that are essential for healing and nourishment. In real life, this can be related to the use of herbal medicine and natural remedies in various cultures, emphasizing the importance of nature in our health.
  • Tad vo devīr upa bruve: This translates to "I speak to you, O divine ones." This line acknowledges the presence of divine beings or deities, suggesting a connection between humans and the divine. In modern life, this can be seen in practices such as prayer or meditation, where individuals seek guidance or blessings from a higher power.
  • Saneyam aśvaṁ gāṁ: This means "to bring forth the horse and the cow." The horse and cow are significant in Vedic culture, symbolizing strength, wealth, and sustenance. In contemporary terms, this can relate to agriculture and livestock farming, where animals are vital for food production and economic stability.
  • Vāsa ātmānaṁ tava pūruṣa: This translates to "may your spirit dwell within us." This line emphasizes the idea of divine presence within individuals, suggesting that the essence of the divine can guide and inspire human actions. In real life, this can be reflected in the pursuit of inner peace and self-awareness, where individuals strive to connect with their higher selves.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the interconnectedness of nature, divinity, and humanity. It highlights the significance of herbal medicine, the divine guidance we seek, the importance of livestock in sustenance, and the aspiration for inner spiritual connection. In today's world, this mantra can inspire individuals to respect nature, seek spiritual growth, and recognize the importance of community and agriculture in sustaining life. It serves as a reminder of our roots and the holistic approach to living harmoniously with the environment and the divine.
