विमच छ्रथाय रशनामिवाग ऋध्याम ते वरुण खाम ऋतस्य |मा तन्तुश्चेदी वयतो धियम मे मा मात्रा शारी अपसः पुर ऋतोः ||
vi mac chrathāya raśanām ivāga ṛdhyāma te varuṇa khām ṛtasya | mā tantuś chedi vayato dhiyam me mā mātrā śāry apasaḥ pura ṛtoḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • vi: apart, away
    • mac: my
    • chrathāya: to the one who is powerful
    • raśanām: of the rope or bond
    • ivāga: like a thread
    • ṛdhyāma: may we be freed
    • te: your
    • varuṇa: O Varuna (the god of cosmic order)
    • khām: the sky or space
    • ṛtasya: of truth or order
    • mā: not
    • tantuś: the thread or bond
    • chedi: may it be cut
    • vayato: of our desires
    • dhiyam: intellect or understanding
    • me: my
    • mā: not
    • mātrā: in measure
    • śāry: of the truth
    • apasaḥ: from the falsehood
    • pura: from the past
    • ṛtoḥ: of the truth
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Varuna, the deity associated with cosmic order and truth.
    • The speaker seeks liberation from the bonds of ignorance and falsehood, represented by the metaphor of a rope or thread.
    • It emphasizes the importance of clarity in thought and the pursuit of truth, urging the divine to help cut through the distractions and misconceptions that cloud understanding.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, this mantra can be applied when seeking clarity in decision-making. For instance, a manager may feel overwhelmed by conflicting information and can invoke this principle to seek a clear path forward.
    • In personal relationships, individuals may face misunderstandings. This mantra serves as a reminder to seek truth and understanding, cutting through the 'threads' of miscommunication.
    • In the context of education, students can use this mantra to focus on their studies, asking for clarity and understanding in their pursuit of knowledge.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for liberation from ignorance and the pursuit of truth.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, encouraging individuals to seek clarity in their thoughts and actions.
    • By invoking the divine assistance of Varuna, one can strive to overcome obstacles and achieve a deeper understanding of themselves and the world around them.
