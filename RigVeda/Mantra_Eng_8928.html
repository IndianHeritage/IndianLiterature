ये सत्यासो हविरादो हविष्पा इन्द्रेण देवैः सरथं दधानाḥ |आग्ने याहि सहस्रं देववन्दैः परैः पूर्वैः पितृभिर घर्मसद्भिः ||
ye satyāso havirado haviṣpā indreṇa devaiḥ sarathaṁ dadhānāḥ | āgne yāhi sahasraṁ devavandaiḥ paraiḥ pūrvaiḥ pitṛbhir gharmasadbhiḥ ||
  • Translation: "Those who are truthful, who offer their oblations, who are endowed with strength, may they, with Indra and the gods, bring forth the offerings. O Agni, come with a thousand divine beings, with the ancestors, who are in the warm abode."
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ye: those who
    • satyāso: who are truthful
    • havirado: who offer oblations
    • haviṣpā: endowed with strength
    • indreṇa: with Indra
    • devaiḥ: with the gods
    • sarathaṁ: bringing forth
    • dadhānāḥ: may they bring
    • āgne: O Agni
    • yāhi: come
    • sahasraṁ: with a thousand
    • devavandaiḥ: divine beings
    • paraiḥ: with the ancestors
    • pūrvaiḥ: who are in the past
    • pitṛbhir: with the ancestors
    • gharmasadbhiḥ: who are in the warm abode
  • Context and Explanation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of truthfulness and the act of offering. In many cultures, including modern society, honesty is a valued trait that fosters trust and integrity.
    • The invocation of Indra and the gods signifies the need for divine support in our endeavors. For example, in a business setting, one might seek the blessings of higher powers for success and ethical conduct.
    • Agni, the fire god, represents transformation and purification. In real life, this can be likened to the process of personal growth, where one seeks to improve oneself through honest efforts and the support of others.
    • The mention of ancestors highlights the importance of lineage and heritage. In many cultures, honoring one's ancestors is a way to connect with the past and seek guidance for the future.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the values of truth, integrity, and the importance of seeking divine guidance in our actions.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, such as personal development, ethical business practices, and honoring family traditions.
    • By embodying these principles, individuals can create a positive impact in their lives and the lives of those around them.
