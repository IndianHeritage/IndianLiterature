ये नाकस्याधि रोचने दिवि देवास आसते |मरुद्भिर अग्न आ गहि ||
ye nākasyādhi rocane divi devāsa āsate | marudbhir agna ā gahi ||
  • Mantra: "ye nākasyādhi rocane divi devāsa āsate | marudbhir agna ā gahi ||"
  • Translation: "Those who dwell in the sky, the divine beings, who shine brightly; come, O Agni, with the Maruts."
  • Word-by-Word Explanation:
    • ye: Those who
    • nākasyādhi: in the sky
    • rocane: shining or illuminating
    • divi: in the heavens
    • devāsa: divine beings or gods
    • āsate: dwell or reside
    • marudbhir: with the Maruts (the storm deities)
    • agna: O Agni (the fire god)
    • ā gahi: come or approach
  • Contextual Significance:
    • This mantra invokes Agni, the fire god, and the Maruts, who are associated with storms and winds. It emphasizes the connection between the divine and the natural elements.
    • In real life, this can be seen as a call for divine guidance and support in times of need, especially during challenges that require strength and resilience.
    • For example, during a difficult phase in life, one might seek inspiration and strength from the divine, much like how the mantra calls upon Agni and the Maruts for assistance.
  • Real-Life Application:
    • This mantra can be recited during rituals or personal meditation to invoke strength and clarity in decision-making.
    • It can also be relevant in situations where one seeks to overcome obstacles, such as during exams, job interviews, or personal challenges.
    • In a broader sense, it serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of nature and the divine, encouraging individuals to respect and honor both.
  • Conclusion:
    • The mantra highlights the importance of seeking divine support in our endeavors and recognizing the power of nature.
    • It encourages individuals to cultivate a relationship with the divine, drawing strength from it in their daily lives.
    • Ultimately, it serves as a reminder that we are not alone in our struggles; the divine and natural forces are always present to guide and support us.
