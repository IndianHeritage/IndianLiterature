तद् राधो अद्य सवितुर्वरेण्यं वयं देवस्य प्रासवे मनामहे |अस्मभ्यं dyāvāpṛthivī सुचेतुनā रयिं धत्तं वसुमन्तं शतग्विनम् ||
tad rādho adya savitur vareṇyaṁ vayaṁ devasya prasave manāmahe | asmabhyaṁ dyāvāpṛthivī sucetunā rayiṁ dhattaṁ vasumantaṁ śatagvinam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • tad: that
    • rādho: may we be blessed
    • adya: today
    • savituh: of the Sun
    • vareṇyaṁ: the most excellent
    • vayaṁ: we
    • devasya: of the divine
    • prasave: in the birth
    • manāmahe: we meditate
    • asmabhyaṁ: for us
    • dyāvāpṛthivī: heaven and earth
    • sucetunā: with pure intellect
    • rayiṁ: wealth
    • dhattaṁ: may you bestow
    • vasumantaṁ: the prosperous
    • śatagvinam: with a hundred blessings
  • Meaning and Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer for blessings and prosperity.
    • It invokes the divine essence of the Sun, Savitur, symbolizing enlightenment and life.
    • The phrase "may we be blessed today" emphasizes the immediacy of the request for divine grace.
    • It highlights the importance of pure intellect and wisdom in achieving success and wealth.
    • The mention of "heaven and earth" signifies the holistic nature of existence and the interconnectedness of all life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, individuals often seek blessings for new ventures, such as starting a business or a new job, reflecting the desire for divine guidance.
    • Students may recite this mantra before exams, hoping for clarity of thought and success in their endeavors.
    • During significant life events, such as weddings or housewarming ceremonies, this mantra can be chanted to invoke prosperity and harmony.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of seeking divine guidance and wisdom in our pursuits.
    • It emphasizes the need for a pure mind and heart in attracting prosperity and blessings.
    • In a broader sense, it encourages individuals to connect with the divine and recognize the interdependence of all aspects of life, fostering a sense of gratitude and mindfulness.
