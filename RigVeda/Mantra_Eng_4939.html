परी तृन्धि पणीनाम आरय हृदया कवे |अथेम अस्मभ्यं रन्धय ||
pari tṛndhi paṇīnām ārayā hṛdayā kave | athem asmabhyaṁ randhaya ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • pari: around, surrounding
    • tṛndhi: to cut, to sever
    • paṇīnām: of the skilled, the wise
    • ārayā: with the help, with the support
    • hṛdayā: heart, mind
    • kave: of the poet, the seer
    • athem: here, in this place
    • asmabhyaṁ: for us, for our benefit
    • randhaya: to nourish, to provide
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks to the idea of seeking support and nourishment from the wise and skilled individuals around us.
    • The phrase "pari tṛndhi paṇīnām" suggests the importance of cutting through ignorance or obstacles with the help of those who possess knowledge.
    • "ārayā hṛdayā kave" emphasizes the role of the heart and mind in this process, indicating that true wisdom comes from a place of emotional and intellectual understanding.
    • The concluding part "athem asmabhyaṁ randhaya" highlights the desire for nourishment and support for our collective well-being.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace setting, this mantra can be applied when teams collaborate. Skilled team members can help cut through challenges, providing support and guidance to achieve common goals.
    • In educational environments, teachers and mentors play a crucial role in guiding students. Their wisdom helps students navigate their learning journeys, fostering both intellectual and emotional growth.
    • In personal relationships, seeking advice from wise friends or family members can help individuals overcome personal challenges, leading to emotional nourishment and support.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of community and the interconnectedness of individuals in the pursuit of knowledge and well-being.
    • It teaches us to value the wisdom of those around us and to seek their support in overcoming obstacles.
    • In real life, applying the principles of this mantra can lead to stronger relationships, better teamwork, and a more supportive environment, ultimately contributing to personal and collective growth.
