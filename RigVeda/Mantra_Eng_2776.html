मन्त्र: ससानात्यां उता सूर्यं ससानेंद्रः ससाना पुरुभोजसां गाम् |हिरण्ययं उता भोगं ससाना हत्वी दस्यून प्रार्यं वर्णम आवत ||
sasānātyām̐ uta sūryaṁ sasānendraḥ sasāna purubhojasaṁ gām | hiraṇyayam uta bhogaṁ sasāna hatvī dasyūn prāryaṁ varṇam āvat ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sasānātyām̐: He who rules or governs.
    • uta: and.
    • sūryaṁ: the sun.
    • sasānendraḥ: the ruler or lord.
    • sasāna: he who governs.
    • purubhojasaṁ: the one who enjoys great wealth.
    • gām: the earth or the world.
    • hiraṇyayam: golden or prosperous.
    • uta: and.
    • bhogaṁ: enjoyment or wealth.
    • sasāna: he who governs.
    • hatvī: having defeated.
    • dasyūn: the enemies or the wicked.
    • prāryaṁ: the one who is to be praised.
    • varṇam: the color or the quality.
    • āvat: may protect or shelter.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of a divine ruler, often interpreted as the sun or a supreme deity, who governs the world with authority and wealth.
    • The mention of "purubhojasaṁ" indicates the abundance and prosperity that comes from this governance, suggesting that a just ruler brings wealth to the land.
    • The phrase "hatvī dasyūn" implies the defeat of evil or wickedness, highlighting the importance of righteousness in leadership.
    • In a real-life context, this can be related to leaders or governments that work towards the welfare of their people, ensuring prosperity while combating corruption and injustice.
  • Significance and Real-Life Application:
    • This mantra emphasizes the qualities of good governance, such as justice, prosperity, and the protection of the people from evil.
    • In modern society, it can be applied to the principles of effective leadership, where leaders are expected to act in the best interest of their constituents, promoting economic growth and social justice.
    • It serves as a reminder for individuals in positions of power to uphold their responsibilities and to strive for a society that is free from corruption and injustice.
    • In personal life, it encourages individuals to embody these qualities in their own actions, promoting fairness and integrity in their interactions with others.
