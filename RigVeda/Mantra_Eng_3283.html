आ न इन्द्रो दूराद् आ न आसाद् अभिष्टकृत् अवसे यासद् उग्रः |ओजिष्टेभिर् नृपतिः वज्रबाहुः सङ्गे समत्सु तुर्वणिः पृथन्यून् ||
ā na indro dūrād ā na āsād abhiṣṭikṛd avase yāsad ugraḥ | ojiṣṭhebhir nṛpatir vajrabāhuḥ saṁge samatsu turvaṇiḥ pṛtanyūn ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā na: May we not be far away
    • indro: O Indra (the king of gods)
    • dūrād: From afar
    • ā na: May we not be hindered
    • āsād: May we attain
    • abhiṣṭikṛd: The one who fulfills desires
    • avase: May we be protected
    • yāsad: Who is powerful
    • ugraḥ: Fierce or strong
    • ojiṣṭhebhir: With the strongest
    • nṛpatir: The lord of men
    • vajrabāhuḥ: With arms like thunderbolts
    • saṁge: In unity
    • samatsu: In battles
    • turvaṇiḥ: The one who is swift
    • pṛtanyūn: In the midst of the earth
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Indra, the god of rain and thunderstorms, who is often invoked for strength and protection.
    • The phrase "ā na indro dūrād" emphasizes the desire for closeness to divine support, suggesting that one should not feel distant from the divine, especially in times of need.
    • “abhiṣṭikṛd avase” indicates a plea for the fulfillment of desires and protection, highlighting the importance of divine intervention in achieving goals.
    • The mention of “vajrabāhuḥ” symbolizes strength and resilience, akin to a leader or a warrior who possesses the might to overcome challenges.
    • In real life, this mantra can be related to situations where individuals seek guidance and strength, such as during difficult times in personal or professional life, or when facing challenges that require collective effort.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its invocation of divine strength and unity in overcoming obstacles.
    • It serves as a reminder that in times of struggle, one can seek support from higher powers and from the community, emphasizing the importance of collaboration and shared strength.
    • This mantra can be applicable in various real-life scenarios, such as team projects, community initiatives, or personal challenges, where the collective effort and divine guidance can lead to success.
