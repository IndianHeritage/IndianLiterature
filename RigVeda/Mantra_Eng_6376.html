यस्यानूनाऽगभीरामदाऽउरवस्तरुत्राः |हर्षुमन्तः शूरसातौ ||
yasyānūnā gabhīrā madā uravas tarutrāḥ | harṣumantaḥ śūrasātau ||
  • yasyānūnā: This term can be broken down into "yasy" meaning "whose" and "ānūnā" which refers to "the ones who are not small" or "the great." This suggests a connection to greatness or significance.
  • gabhīrā: This word translates to "deep" or "profound." It indicates something that has depth, whether in thought, emotion, or physicality.
  • madā: This term means "intoxication" or "joy." It can refer to a state of bliss or ecstasy that comes from a deep understanding or experience.
  • uravas: This word means "broad" or "wide." It can signify expansiveness, whether in terms of space, thought, or influence.
  • tarutrāḥ: This term refers to "trees" or "plants." In a metaphorical sense, it can represent growth, stability, and life.
  • harṣumantaḥ: This word translates to "joyful" or "full of delight." It indicates a state of happiness and contentment.
  • śūrasātau: This term can be understood as "in the presence of the brave" or "among the heroes." It emphasizes the importance of courage and valor in life.

The mantra speaks to the essence of greatness, depth, joy, and the presence of bravery. It suggests that those who embody these qualities are connected to a profound experience of life. The imagery of deep joy and broad expansiveness, alongside the metaphor of trees, evokes a sense of stability and growth that is essential for a fulfilling life.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios:

  • Personal Growth: Just as trees grow deep roots and expand their branches, individuals can strive for personal development by cultivating deep knowledge and broad experiences.
  • Community Building: The idea of being among the brave can inspire individuals to come together in support of one another, fostering a community that values courage and joy.
  • Emotional Well-being: The state of joy and intoxication can be seen in moments of happiness, such as celebrating achievements or enjoying nature, reminding us to appreciate the depth of our experiences.

In conclusion, this mantra emphasizes the significance of embracing greatness, depth, joy, and bravery in our lives. It encourages individuals to seek profound experiences, cultivate personal growth, and build supportive communities, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling and enriched life.
