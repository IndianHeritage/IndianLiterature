स इद दासं तुवीरवम् पतिर दन षलक्षं त्रिशीर्षाणं दमन्यत् |अस्य तृतो nv ओजसा वृद्धानो विपा वराहम् अयोग्रयां हन् ||
sa id dāsaṁ tuvīravam patir dan ṣaḻakṣaṁ triśīrṣāṇaṁ damanyat | asya trito nv ojasā vṛdhāno vipā varāham ayoagrayā han ||
  • sa id dāsaṁ tuvīravam: This phrase can be translated as "He who is the master of the strong servant." In a real-life context, this can refer to a leader or a person in authority who guides and manages those who work under them, emphasizing the importance of strong leadership.
  • patir dan ṣaḻakṣaṁ: This translates to "the lord with six eyes." The six eyes can symbolize perception, awareness, and the ability to see beyond the obvious. In modern life, this can relate to individuals who possess insight and foresight, such as successful entrepreneurs or visionary leaders who can anticipate market trends.
  • triśīrṣāṇaṁ damanyat: This means "the one with three heads." This can represent multifaceted wisdom or the ability to approach problems from different angles. In practical terms, this can be seen in professionals who are adept in various fields, allowing them to tackle challenges creatively and effectively.
  • asya trito nv ojasā vṛdhāno: This translates to "the third one, growing in strength." This can signify the importance of continuous growth and development. In real life, this can be applied to personal development, where individuals strive to improve their skills and knowledge over time.
  • vipā varāham ayoagrayā han: This phrase can be interpreted as "the one who conquers with great strength." This highlights the value of resilience and determination in overcoming obstacles. In everyday life, this can be seen in athletes who train rigorously to achieve their goals or individuals who persist through challenges to succeed in their careers.

  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the qualities of strong leadership, multifaceted wisdom, continuous growth, and resilience. In real-life scenarios, these attributes are essential for success in various fields, whether in business, personal development, or overcoming life's challenges. By embodying these principles, individuals can navigate their paths more effectively and achieve their goals.
