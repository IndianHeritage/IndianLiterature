पद्यां वस्ते पुरुरूपा वपुंष्य ऊर्ध्वा तस्थौ त्र्यविं रेरिहाणा |ऋतस्य सद्म विचरामि विद्वान् महद् देवानाम् असुरत्वम् एकम् ||
padyā vaste pururūpā vapūṁṣy ūrdhvā tasthau tryaviṁ rerihāṇā | ṛtasya sadma vi carāmi vidvān mahad devānām asuratvam ekam ||
  • padyā vaste pururūpā: This phrase can be translated as "the one who is manifold in form." It signifies the diverse manifestations of the divine or the universe. In real life, this can be seen in nature, where a single entity, like a tree, can take on various forms and shapes depending on its environment.
  • vapūṁṣy ūrdhvā tasthau: This means "the body stands upright." It symbolizes strength, stability, and the ability to rise above challenges. For example, a person who faces adversity but remains resilient and upright in their values exemplifies this idea.
  • tryaviṁ rerihāṇā: This can be interpreted as "the three realms are in harmony." It refers to the balance between the physical, mental, and spiritual aspects of life. In practical terms, this can be observed in individuals who maintain a healthy lifestyle, balancing work, relationships, and personal growth.
  • ṛtasya sadma vi carāmi vidvān: This translates to "the wise one moves in the abode of truth." It emphasizes the importance of wisdom and truth in one's journey. In everyday life, this can be seen in leaders or mentors who guide others with integrity and knowledge.
  • mahad devānām asuratvam ekam: This phrase means "the greatness of the divine is one." It highlights the unity of all divine forces and the interconnectedness of all beings. In real life, this can be reflected in the idea of community and collaboration, where diverse individuals come together for a common purpose.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates profound philosophical concepts that are applicable in various aspects of life. It teaches us about the diversity of existence, the importance of resilience, the need for balance, the value of wisdom, and the unity of all beings. In a world that often emphasizes division and conflict, this mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of life and the strength found in harmony and truth. By embodying these principles, individuals can lead more fulfilling and meaningful lives, contributing positively to their communities and the world at large.
