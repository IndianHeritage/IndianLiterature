श्रुधी नो अग्नि सदने सधस्थे युक्ष्वा रथम अमृतस्य द्रवित्नुम् |आ नो वह रोडसी देवपुत्रे माकिर देवानाम् अप भूर इह स्याः ||
śrudhī no agne sadane sadhasthe yukṣvā ratham amṛtasya dravitnum | ā no vaha rodasī devaputre mākir devānām apa bhūr iha syāḥ ||
  • śrudhī no agne: This phrase translates to "O Agni, listen to us." Agni, the fire god, represents energy, transformation, and the divine messenger. In real life, this can be seen as a call for attention to our inner fire or passion, urging us to focus on our goals.
  • sadane sadhasthe: This means "in the abode of the prosperous." It signifies a place of abundance and success. In practical terms, this could refer to a supportive environment, such as a nurturing home or a thriving workplace where individuals can flourish.
  • yukṣvā ratham amṛtasya dravitnum: This translates to "let us harness the chariot of immortality." The chariot symbolizes the journey of life, and "immortality" can be interpreted as achieving lasting impact or legacy. In everyday life, this could relate to pursuing endeavors that leave a positive mark on society, such as community service or creative projects.
  • ā no vaha rodasī: This means "bring us together, O heavens." It emphasizes unity and collaboration. In real life, this can be applied to teamwork in various settings, such as in a family, workplace, or community, where collective efforts lead to greater achievements.
  • devaputre mākir devānām: This translates to "O son of the gods, may we be blessed." It reflects a desire for divine blessings and guidance. In practical terms, this can be seen in rituals or practices where individuals seek inspiration and support from higher powers, such as prayer or meditation.
  • apa bhūr iha syāḥ: This means "may we not be deprived of abundance here." It expresses a wish for prosperity and fulfillment in our current lives. In real life, this can relate to striving for personal and communal well-being, ensuring that resources and opportunities are accessible to all.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a profound message about seeking guidance, unity, and prosperity in our lives. It emphasizes the importance of harnessing our inner strengths and working together towards common goals. In today's world, this mantra can inspire individuals and communities to pursue their passions, foster collaboration, and strive for a legacy that benefits future generations. By invoking the essence of this mantra, we can cultivate a life filled with purpose, connection, and abundance.
