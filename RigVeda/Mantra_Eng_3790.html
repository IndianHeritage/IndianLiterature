यम् अग्ने वाजसातम त्वं चिन् मन्यसे रयिम् | तं नो गीरभिः श्रवायं देवत्रा पनया युजम् ||
yam agne vājasātama tvaṁ cin manyase rayim | taṁ no gīrbhiḥ śravāyyaṁ devatrā panayā yujam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yam: whom
    • agne: O Agni (the fire god)
    • vājasātama: the bestower of strength or wealth
    • tvaṁ: you
    • cin: if you consider
    • manyase: you think
    • rayim: wealth or prosperity
    • taṁ: that
    • no: for us
    • gīrbhiḥ: with hymns or praises
    • śravāyyaṁ: worthy of fame
    • devatrā: by the divine
    • panayā: through the offering
    • yujam: may we be united
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Agni, the fire god, who is often seen as a mediator between humans and the divine.
    • The speaker is asking Agni to bestow wealth and prosperity upon them, emphasizing the importance of divine blessings in achieving success.
    • The phrase "if you consider" indicates a personal relationship with Agni, suggesting that the divine is attentive to human thoughts and intentions.
    • The use of "with hymns" highlights the significance of devotion and praise in spiritual practices, reinforcing the idea that worship can attract divine favor.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern life, individuals often seek blessings for success in their careers or businesses. This mantra can be recited during rituals or prayers to invoke prosperity.
    • In corporate settings, leaders may emphasize the importance of teamwork and unity, akin to the idea of being "united" through divine blessings for collective success.
    • During festivals or significant life events, people may perform rituals to honor deities, similar to how this mantra seeks the favor of Agni for wealth and prosperity.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of divine intervention in human endeavors, particularly in the pursuit of wealth and prosperity.
    • It teaches that devotion, praise, and a sincere heart can attract blessings from the divine, making it applicable in various aspects of life, from personal aspirations to communal efforts.
    • By recognizing the role of the divine in our lives, we can cultivate a sense of gratitude and responsibility towards our actions, ensuring that our pursuits are aligned with higher values.
