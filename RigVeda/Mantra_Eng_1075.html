द्रविणोदाऽ द्रविणसस् तुरस्य द्रविणोदाः सनरस्य प्र यंषत् |द्रविणोदाऽ वीरवतīm इषं नो द्रविणोदाऽ रासते दीर्घम आयुः ||
draviṇodā draviṇasas turasya draviṇodāḥ sanarasya pra yaṁsat | draviṇodā vīravatīm iṣaṁ no draviṇodā rāsate dīrgham āyuḥ ||
  • Translation: The mantra can be translated as: "May the wealth (draviṇa) of the brave (vīravatī) be abundant; may it bring us long life (dīrgham āyuḥ) and prosperity (pra yaṁsat)."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • draviṇodā: Refers to wealth or resources, often associated with prosperity and abundance.
    • draviṇasas: Indicates the essence or nature of wealth.
    • turasy: Suggests a sense of urgency or importance.
    • sanarasya: Implies a connection to vitality or life.
    • pra yaṁsat: A call for blessings or success.
    • vīravatīm: Relates to bravery or valor.
    • iṣaṁ: Refers to desires or aspirations.
    • rāsate: Indicates the act of flourishing or thriving.
    • dīrgham āyuḥ: Means long life or longevity.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be applied to the pursuit of success and prosperity. For instance, a startup may invoke this mantra during a launch event, seeking blessings for growth and abundance.
    • In personal life, individuals may recite this mantra during significant life events, such as weddings or the birth of a child, to wish for a prosperous and long life for their loved ones.
    • In community gatherings, this mantra can be used to inspire collective efforts towards achieving common goals, emphasizing the importance of bravery and resourcefulness in overcoming challenges.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its invocation of wealth, bravery, and longevity. It serves as a reminder that prosperity is not just about material wealth but also about the courage to pursue one's goals and the vitality to enjoy life. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, from personal aspirations to communal endeavors, encouraging individuals and groups to strive for abundance and success while fostering a spirit of bravery and resilience.
