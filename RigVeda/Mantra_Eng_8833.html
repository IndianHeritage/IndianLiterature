असच्च च सच्च च परम व्योमन दाक्षस्य जनन्मन् अदितेर उपस्थे |अग्निः ह नः प्रथमजा ऋतस्य पूर्व आयुनी वृषभश च धेनुः ||
asac ca sac ca parame vyoman dakṣasya janmann aditer upasthe | agnir ha naḥ prathamajā ṛtasya pūrva āyuni vṛṣabhaś ca dhenuḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • asac ca sac ca: "both the untruth and the truth"
    • parame vyoman: "in the supreme sky"
    • dakṣasya: "of the capable one"
    • janmann: "from birth"
    • aditer: "of Aditi" (the mother of gods)
    • upasthe: "in the presence"
    • agnir: "the fire"
    • ha naḥ: "may it be ours"
    • prathamajā: "the first-born"
    • ṛtasya: "of truth or order"
    • pūrva: "the ancient"
    • āyuni: "in life"
    • vṛṣabhaś ca: "and the bull"
    • dhenuḥ: "the cow"
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks to the duality of existence, acknowledging both truth and falsehood.
    • The "supreme sky" symbolizes a higher consciousness or ultimate reality.
    • The reference to Aditi signifies the nurturing aspect of the universe, representing the source of all creation.
    • Fire (Agni) is often seen as a purifying force, representing knowledge and transformation.
    • The "first-born" and "ancient truth" suggest the importance of foundational principles in life.
    • The bull and cow symbolize strength and nourishment, respectively, indicating the balance of power and sustenance in life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In personal development, recognizing both our strengths (truth) and weaknesses (falsehood) can lead to growth.
    • In relationships, understanding the dual nature of communication can help in resolving conflicts.
    • In environmental sustainability, the balance between consumption (cow) and production (bull) is crucial for a healthy ecosystem.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of understanding the dualities of life and the need for balance.
    • It encourages individuals to seek truth while acknowledging the existence of falsehood, promoting a holistic approach to life.
    • In practical terms, it can be applied in various fields such as personal growth, conflict resolution, and environmental stewardship.
