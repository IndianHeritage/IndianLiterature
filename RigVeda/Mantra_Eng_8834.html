अयं स यस्य शर्मन् अवोभिर अग्नेर एधते जारिताभिष्टौ |ज्येष्ठेभिर यो भानुभिर ऋषूणाम पर्येति परिवीतो विभावा ||
ayaṁ sa yasya śarmann avobhir agner edhate jaritābhiṣṭau | jyeṣṭhebhir yo bhānubhir ṛṣūṇām paryeti parivīto vibhāvā ||
  • ayaṁ sa yasya śarmann: This phrase can be translated to "This is he who is protected." It signifies the idea of safety and security, suggesting that there is a divine protection surrounding an individual.
  • avobhir agner edhate: This translates to "who is nourished by the flames." Here, fire symbolizes energy, transformation, and purification. In real life, this can be related to the warmth and energy we derive from our passions and pursuits.
  • jaritābhiṣṭau: This means "with the blessings of the ancients." It emphasizes the importance of wisdom and guidance from those who came before us, suggesting that we should seek knowledge from our elders and ancestors.
  • jyeṣṭhebhir yo bhānubhir: This translates to "who is surrounded by the elder seers." It indicates the presence of wise individuals who guide and support us in our journey, akin to mentors or teachers in our lives.
  • ṛṣūṇām paryeti: This means "who moves among the sages." It highlights the importance of being in the company of wise and enlightened individuals, which can lead to personal growth and understanding.
  • parivīto vibhāvā: This translates to "surrounded by the manifestations of glory." It suggests that when one is in the right company and under divine protection, they are likely to experience success and fulfillment in their endeavors.

Conclusion: This mantra emphasizes the significance of divine protection, the importance of wisdom from the past, and the value of being surrounded by enlightened individuals. In real life, it can be applied in various scenarios such as seeking guidance from mentors, valuing the teachings of our ancestors, and pursuing our passions with the understanding that we are supported by a greater force. It serves as a reminder to cultivate a supportive environment and to honor the wisdom that has been passed down through generations, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling and successful life.
