एतं ते स्तोमं तुविजात विप्रो रथं न धीराḥ स्वपā अतक्षम् |यदीद अग्ने प्रति त्वं देव हर्याः स्वर्वतीर अप एनā जयेम ||
etaṁ te stomaṁ tuvijāta vipro rathaṁ na dhīraḥ svapā atakṣam | yadīd agne prati tvaṁ deva haryāḥ svarvatīr apa enā jayema ||
  • Translation: This mantra can be translated as: "O Agni, we invoke your praise, the wise one who is not hindered, may we conquer the waters that flow towards you."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • etaṁ: this
    • te: your
    • stomaṁ: praise or hymn
    • tuvijāta: invoked or called upon
    • vipro: wise or learned one
    • rathaṁ: chariot or vehicle
    • na: not
    • dhīraḥ: hindered or obstructed
    • svapā: may we conquer
    • atakṣam: the flowing waters
    • yadīd: that which
    • agne: O Agni (the fire god)
    • prati: towards
    • tvaṁ: you
    • deva: deity or god
    • haryāḥ: of the flowing waters
    • svarvatīr: celestial or heavenly
    • apa: waters
    • enā: these
    • jayema: may we conquer or triumph
  • Contextual Significance:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Agni, the fire god, who is often seen as a mediator between humans and the divine. In many cultures, fire symbolizes transformation and purification.
    • The invocation of Agni signifies the importance of seeking guidance and strength from higher powers in times of need.
    • The reference to "conquering the waters" can be interpreted as overcoming challenges or obstacles in life, much like how one might navigate through turbulent waters.
  • Real-Life Application:
    • In daily life, this mantra can be applied when facing difficulties or uncertainties. For instance, when starting a new job or moving to a new city, one might invoke the spirit of Agni for courage and guidance.
    • In moments of stress or anxiety, reciting this mantra can serve as a reminder to seek inner strength and clarity, much like how fire provides light in darkness.
    • It can also be relevant in communal settings, such as during festivals or rituals, where the collective invocation of divine support can foster unity and strength among participants.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of seeking divine assistance in overcoming life's challenges. It serves as a reminder of the power of faith and the importance of invoking higher energies to navigate through difficulties.
    • By understanding and applying the teachings of this mantra, individuals can cultivate resilience and a sense of purpose, ultimately leading to personal growth and transformation.
