मां नो वृकाय वृक्ये समस्मा अघायते रीरधता यजत्राः |यूयं हि ष्ठा रथ्यो नस् तनूनां यूयं दक्षस्य वचसो बभूव ||
mā no vṛkāya vṛkye samasmā aghāyate rīradhatā yajatrāḥ | yūyaṁ hi ṣṭhā rathyo nas tanūnāṁ yūyaṁ dakṣasya vacaso babhūva ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • mā: not
    • no: us
    • vṛkāya: to the wolves
    • vṛkye: in the form of wolves
    • samasmā: from all sides
    • aghāyate: may it not harm
    • rīradhatā: may it be prosperous
    • yajatrāḥ: those who perform sacrifices
    • yūyaṁ: you all
    • hi: indeed
    • ṣṭhā: stand firm
    • rathyo: on the chariots
    • nas: of us
    • tanūnāṁ: of our bodies
    • yūyaṁ: you all
    • dakṣasya: of the skilled
    • vacaso: of the words
    • babhūva: may it become
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra expresses a plea for protection and prosperity.
    • The reference to "wolves" symbolizes dangers or adversities that may threaten individuals or communities.
    • The phrase "may it not harm" indicates a desire for safety and security from these threats.
    • The invocation of "those who perform sacrifices" highlights the importance of rituals and community efforts in seeking divine favor and protection.
    • The call to "stand firm" suggests resilience and unity in the face of challenges.
    • The mention of "the skilled" emphasizes the value of wisdom and effective communication in overcoming obstacles.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community facing natural disasters, this mantra can be a reminder for collective action and support, ensuring that everyone stands together to face the challenges.
    • In personal life, individuals may recite this mantra during times of uncertainty, such as job loss or health issues, to invoke strength and protection.
    • In a corporate setting, teams can use the essence of this mantra to foster collaboration and resilience, especially during challenging projects or market conditions.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the importance of community, resilience, and the need for protection against adversities.
    • Its significance lies in its ability to inspire individuals and groups to come together, support one another, and seek divine guidance in their endeavors.
    • In real-life scenarios, whether in personal challenges or collective struggles, the principles embodied in this mantra can guide actions and foster a sense of unity and strength.
