अस्माकं इन्द्र दुष्टारं पुरोयावानम् आजिषु |सयावानं धन-धनं वाजयन्तम् अवा रथम ||
asmākam indra duṣṭaram puroyāvānam ājiṣu | sayāvānaṁ dhane-dhane vājayantam avā ratham ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • asmākam: our
    • indra: Indra, the king of gods and god of rain and thunderstorms
    • duṣṭaram: the one who is difficult to conquer or overcome
    • puroyāvānam: the one who is present in the forefront or leading
    • ājiṣu: in battles or conflicts
    • sayāvānam: the one who is ready to help or assist
    • dhane-dhane: in wealth or prosperity
    • vājayantam: the one who is victorious or triumphant
    • avā: protect or guard
    • ratham: the chariot or vehicle
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes Indra, asking for his assistance in overcoming challenges and achieving victory.
    • It emphasizes the importance of divine support in both personal and communal struggles.
    • The reference to wealth suggests that prosperity is also a form of victory, and that divine blessings are essential for success.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, a company may face fierce competition (duṣṭaram) and seek divine guidance (Indra) to navigate challenges and emerge victorious (vājayantam).
    • In personal life, individuals may face hardships or conflicts (ājiṣu) and call upon spiritual beliefs or practices to find strength and support (sayāvānam).
    • Communities may come together in times of crisis, invoking collective strength and support to overcome difficulties, much like seeking the protection of a divine force (avā ratham).
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of seeking divine assistance in overcoming obstacles and achieving success.
    • It serves as a reminder that in both personal and communal endeavors, the support of a higher power can provide strength and guidance.
    • In real life, this can be applicable in various scenarios such as business challenges, personal conflicts, or community crises, where invoking a sense of divine support can inspire resilience and determination.
