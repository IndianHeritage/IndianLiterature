दधन्न ऋतं धनयन्न अस्य धीतिṁ आद इद आर्यो दिधिष्वो विभृत्राः |अतृष्यन्तीर अपसो यन्त्य अच्छा देवाञ जन्म प्रयसा वर्धयन्तीः ||
dadhann ṛtaṁ dhanayann asya dhītim ād id aryo didhiṣvo3 vibhṛtrāḥ | atṛṣyantīr apaso yanty acchā devāñ janma prayasā vardhayantīḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • dadhann: Giving or bestowing
    • ṛtaṁ: Truth or righteousness
    • dhanayann: Enriching or providing wealth
    • asya: Of this
    • dhītim: Intelligence or wisdom
    • ād: Thus or in this way
    • id: Here or now
    • aryo: Noble or honorable
    • didhiṣvo: May you strive or endeavor
    • vibhṛtrāḥ: Those who uphold or support
    • atṛṣyantīr: Those who are not thirsty or unsatisfied
    • apaso: Of the waters or streams
    • yanty: Going or moving
    • acchā: Good or auspicious
    • devāñ: The gods
    • janma: Birth or creation
    • prayasā: By effort or endeavor
    • vardhayantīḥ: Increasing or enhancing
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of truth and righteousness in life.
    • It suggests that by bestowing wisdom and wealth, one can achieve a noble life.
    • The phrase "may you strive" indicates the need for effort in achieving one's goals.
    • It highlights the idea that true satisfaction comes from a state of abundance and fulfillment.
    • The reference to the gods signifies the divine support available to those who pursue righteousness.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, a company that operates with integrity and provides value to its customers is likely to thrive.
    • Individuals who seek knowledge and wisdom often find themselves in positions of leadership and respect.
    • Efforts in community service can lead to a more fulfilling life, as one contributes to the well-being of others.
    • In personal relationships, being truthful and supportive fosters deeper connections and satisfaction.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the significance of truth, wisdom, and effort in achieving a fulfilling life.
    • It encourages individuals to strive for righteousness and to support one another in their endeavors.
    • In a world where challenges are abundant, the principles outlined in this mantra can guide us toward a more meaningful existence.
    • Ultimately, it teaches that by aligning our actions with truth and wisdom, we can enhance our lives and the lives of those around us.
