न्य अकृतून ग्रथिनो मृध्रवाचः पणींर अश्रद्धाम् अवृद्धाम् अयज्ञान् |प्र-प्र तान दस्यूम् अग्निर विवाय पूर्वश चकारापराम् अयज्यून् ||
ny akratūn grathino mṛdhravācaḥ paṇīm̐r aśraddhām̐ avṛdhām̐ ayajñān | pra-pra tān dasyūm̐r agnir vivāya pūrvaś cakārāparām̐ ayajyūn ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ny akratūn: Not having any deceit or falsehood.
    • grathino: Those who are bound or tied.
    • mṛdhravācaḥ: With gentle speech or kind words.
    • paṇīm̐r: Hands or actions.
    • aśraddhām̐: Without faith or disbelief.
    • avṛdhām̐: Without growth or development.
    • ayajñān: Without sacrifice or offerings.
    • pra-pra: To those who are deserving or worthy.
    • tān: Those.
    • dasyūm̐r: The wicked or harmful.
    • agnir: Fire, often symbolizing energy or transformation.
    • vivāya: By joining or uniting.
    • pūrvaś: From the past or earlier.
    • cakārāparām̐: Created or established something supreme.
    • ayajyūn: Those who are not engaged in sacrifice.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of honesty and integrity in our actions and speech.
    • It suggests that without faith and growth, our efforts may be in vain, particularly in the context of spiritual or communal sacrifices.
    • The reference to "the wicked" indicates a warning against negative influences and the need to align with positive energies.
    • The mention of fire symbolizes transformation and the necessity of purification through sacrifice, both literally and metaphorically.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace, fostering an environment of honesty and open communication can lead to better teamwork and productivity.
    • In personal relationships, being truthful and supportive can strengthen bonds and promote mutual growth.
    • In community service, engaging in selfless acts and sacrifices can uplift those in need and create a positive impact.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the values of integrity, faith, and the importance of positive actions in our lives.
    • It highlights the significance of engaging in meaningful sacrifices that contribute to personal and communal growth.
    • By embodying these principles, individuals can transform their lives and the lives of those around them, creating a harmonious and supportive environment.
