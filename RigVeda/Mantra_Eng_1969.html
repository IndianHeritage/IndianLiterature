मन्त्र: सुरुक्मे हि सुपेशसाधि श्रियाऽविराजतः | उषासाव् एष सिदताम् ||
surukme hi supeśasādhi śriyā virājataḥ | uṣāsāv eha sīdatām ||
  • Surukme: This term can be interpreted as "with beauty" or "with grace." It signifies the aesthetic and harmonious aspects of life.
  • Hi: A particle that emphasizes the preceding word, often translated as "indeed" or "certainly."
  • Supeśasādhi: This compound word can be broken down into "su" (good) and "peśasādhi" (the one who has attained or achieved). It refers to someone who has achieved great success or prosperity.
  • Śriyā: This word means "wealth" or "prosperity." It represents not just material wealth but also spiritual richness and abundance in life.
  • Virājataḥ: This term means "shining" or "radiating." It indicates a state of brilliance or glory that comes from success and prosperity.
  • Uṣāsāv: This word refers to "dawn" or "the morning." It symbolizes new beginnings, hope, and the arrival of light after darkness.
  • Eha: This means "here" or "in this place," indicating the immediacy of the experience being described.
  • Sīdatām: This term can be interpreted as "may it shine" or "may it radiate." It is a wish or blessing for the qualities mentioned to manifest.

The mantra can be understood as a prayer or invocation for beauty, prosperity, and brilliance to manifest in one's life, particularly at the dawn of new opportunities. It emphasizes the importance of grace and success, suggesting that these qualities should shine forth in our lives.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios:

  • In personal development, one might meditate on this mantra to attract positive energy and success in their endeavors.
  • In business, entrepreneurs can use the essence of this mantra to inspire their teams to work harmoniously towards achieving prosperity.
  • In relationships, it can serve as a reminder to cultivate beauty and grace in interactions, fostering a supportive and loving environment.

Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for the manifestation of beauty, prosperity, and brilliance in our lives. It encourages individuals to embrace new beginnings with hope and positivity, reminding us that success is not just about material wealth but also about the richness of our experiences and relationships. By embodying these qualities, we can create a more fulfilling and radiant life.
