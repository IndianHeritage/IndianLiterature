उत न ईं तवष्टा गन्त्व अच् छा स्मत सूरिभिर अभिपित्वे सजोषाः |आ वृत्रहेन्द्रश चर्षणिप्रास तुविष्टमो नरां न इह गम्याः ||
uta na īṁ tvaṣṭā gantv acchā smat sūribhir abhipitve sajoṣāḥ | ā vṛtrahendraś carṣaṇiprās tuviṣṭamo narāṁ na iha gamyāḥ ||
  • Mantra: "uta na īṁ tvaṣṭā gantv acchā smat sūribhir abhipitve sajoṣāḥ | ā vṛtrahendraś carṣaṇiprās tuviṣṭamo narāṁ na iha gamyāḥ ||"
  • Word-by-Word Explanation:
    • uta: indeed, or so be it
    • na: not
    • īṁ: a sound or invocation
    • tvaṣṭā: the divine craftsman or creator
    • gantv: to go or to move
    • acchā: good or auspicious
    • smat: from us or our
    • sūribhir: by the heroes or the strong
    • abhipitve: by approaching or invoking
    • sajoṣāḥ: with joy or happiness
    • ā: to or towards
    • vṛtrahendra: the lord of obstacles or the powerful
    • carṣaṇiprās: the one who is invoked or called upon
    • tuviṣṭamo: the most excellent or supreme
    • narāṁ: of men or humans
    • na: not
    • iha: here or in this world
    • gamyāḥ: to be approached or reached
  • Meaning:
    • This mantra invokes the divine craftsman, Tvaṣṭā, asking for auspiciousness and support from the strong and heroic.
    • It emphasizes the importance of joy and collective effort in overcoming obstacles, represented by Vṛtrahendra.
    • The mantra suggests that the supreme power is not easily accessible to those who do not strive or seek it earnestly.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a team project, the success of the endeavor often relies on the collective strength and collaboration of all members, akin to the heroes mentioned in the mantra.
    • When facing challenges in life, such as personal or professional obstacles, invoking a sense of community and support can lead to overcoming these hurdles.
    • In spiritual practices, individuals often seek divine guidance and support through prayers and rituals, reflecting the essence of this mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of collective strength, joy, and the pursuit of divine support in overcoming life's challenges.
    • It serves as a reminder that while obstacles may be formidable, with unity and earnest effort, one can seek and attain higher goals.
    • In real life, it encourages individuals to work together, support one another, and seek divine guidance in their endeavors.
