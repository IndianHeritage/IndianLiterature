प्र य आरुः शितिपृष्ठस्य धासेर आ मातराः विविशुः सप्त वाणिः |परिक्षिता पितरा सं चरते प्र सर्स्राते दीर्घम आयुः प्रयक्षे ||
pra ya āruḥ śitipṛṣṭhasya dhāser ā mātarā viviśuḥ sapta vāṇīḥ | parikṣitā pitarā saṁ carete pra sarsrāte dīrgham āyuḥ prayakṣe ||
  • Mantra: "pra ya āruḥ śitipṛṣṭhasya dhāser ā mātarā viviśuḥ sapta vāṇīḥ | parikṣitā pitarā saṁ carete pra sarsrāte dīrgham āyuḥ prayakṣe ||"
  • Translation: This mantra speaks of the divine energies and the blessings of the mothers (mātṛ) that enter into the being, invoking the seven voices (vāṇīḥ) that guide and protect. It emphasizes the importance of parental guidance (pitarā) and the pursuit of a long life (dīrgham āyuḥ).
  • Word Breakdown:
    • pra ya āruḥ: "those who have risen" - signifies awakening or enlightenment.
    • śitipṛṣṭhasya: "of the one with a sharp back" - can symbolize strength and resilience.
    • dhāser: "of the one who holds" - refers to the nurturing aspect of life.
    • ā mātarā: "O mothers" - invokes the protective and nurturing qualities of motherhood.
    • viviśuḥ: "have entered" - indicates the presence of divine energies.
    • sapta vāṇīḥ: "seven voices" - represents various forms of guidance and wisdom.
    • parikṣitā: "those who are examined" - suggests the importance of discernment and wisdom.
    • pitarā: "parents" - highlights the role of parental guidance.
    • saṁ carete: "they move together" - signifies unity and harmony.
    • pra sarsrāte: "may they flow forth" - indicates the flow of life and energy.
    • dīrgham āyuḥ: "long life" - a blessing for longevity and health.
    • prayakṣe: "may it be realized" - a call for manifestation of these blessings.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a family setting, the nurturing role of mothers can be seen in how they provide emotional support and guidance to their children, helping them to grow into resilient individuals.
    • The concept of "seven voices" can be related to the various forms of wisdom we encounter in life, such as advice from elders, teachings from books, or insights gained through personal experiences.
    • In a community, the importance of parental guidance can be observed in how families work together to raise children, ensuring they are equipped with the values and skills needed for a successful life.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its emphasis on the interconnectedness of life, the importance of parental guidance, and the blessings of longevity. In real life, it serves as a reminder to honor our roots, seek wisdom from various sources, and cherish the nurturing relationships that shape our existence. By acknowledging these elements, we can cultivate a life filled with purpose, resilience, and harmony.
