यद देवस्य शवसां प्रारीण आसुं रिणन्न अपः |भुवद विश्वम् अभ्यादेवम् ओजसा विदाद ऊर्जं शतक्रतुर विदाद इषम् ||
yad devasya śavasā prāriṇā asuṁ riṇann apaḥ | bhuvad viśvam abhy ādevam ojasā vidād ūrjaṁ śatakratur vidād iṣam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yad: that which
    • devasya: of the divine
    • śavasā: with strength
    • prāriṇā: with the power of life
    • asuṁ: the vital force
    • riṇann: calling forth
    • apaḥ: waters
    • bhuvad: may it be
    • viśvam: the universe
    • abhy: towards
    • ādevam: the divine
    • ojasā: with energy
    • vidād: may it grant
    • ūrjaṁ: strength
    • śatakratur: the one with a hundred powers
    • vidād: may it grant
    • iṣam: desires
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the divine forces to bestow strength and vitality.
    • It emphasizes the interconnectedness of life and the universe, suggesting that the divine energy flows through all aspects of existence.
    • The reference to "waters" symbolizes purification and nourishment, essential for life.
    • The phrase "the one with a hundred powers" refers to the divine's ability to fulfill various needs and desires.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, individuals often seek strength and vitality through various means such as exercise, nutrition, and meditation. This mantra can serve as a reminder to connect with the divine energy that supports these efforts.
    • In times of stress or uncertainty, people may turn to spiritual practices or rituals to invoke strength and clarity, reflecting the essence of this mantra.
    • Environmental conservation efforts can be seen as a way to honor the "waters" and the interconnectedness of life, aligning with the mantra's call for divine support in nurturing the universe.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of divine energy in our lives, encouraging us to seek strength and vitality from the universe.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal growth to environmental awareness, reminding us of our connection to the divine and the importance of nurturing our surroundings.
    • By reciting this mantra, individuals can cultivate a sense of empowerment and purpose, aligning their desires with the greater cosmic order.
