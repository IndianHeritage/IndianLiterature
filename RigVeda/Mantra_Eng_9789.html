प्र ये मित्रं प्रार्यमाणं दुरेवाः प्र संगिरः प्र वरुणं मिनन्ति |न्य अमित्रेषु वधं इन्द्र तुंरं वृषन् वृषाणं अरुषं शिशीहि ||
pra ye mitram prāryamaṇaṁ durevāḥ pra saṁgiraḥ pra varuṇam minanti | ny a1mitreṣu vadham indra tumraṁ vṛṣan vṛṣāṇam aruṣaṁ śiśīhi ||
  • Translation: The mantra can be translated as: "Those who are far away, may they come together with us, O Mitra. May they unite with Varuna, and may we not be harmed by our enemies, O Indra, the powerful one, the red one, protect us."
  • Key Terms:
    • Mitra: Represents friendship, harmony, and the divine aspect of social order.
    • Varuna: Symbolizes cosmic order and moral authority, often associated with the waters and the sky.
    • Indra: The king of gods, associated with strength, power, and protection.
    • Vṛṣan: Refers to the powerful or strong, often used to denote a protector.
    • Aruṣa: Means red, symbolizing vitality and energy.
  • Contextual Meaning:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of unity and protection in the face of adversity.
    • It calls upon divine forces to bring together those who are distant, fostering a sense of community and cooperation.
    • The invocation of Indra highlights the need for strength and protection against enemies or challenges.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace setting, this mantra can be applied to encourage teamwork and collaboration among employees who may be working in different locations.
    • In community building, it serves as a reminder to unite diverse groups for a common cause, promoting harmony and understanding.
    • In personal relationships, it can inspire individuals to reach out and mend distances, fostering connections and support.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for unity, protection, and the importance of divine support in overcoming challenges.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal relationships to community efforts, emphasizing the need for collaboration and strength in adversity.
    • By invoking these divine qualities, individuals and groups can strive for a harmonious existence, ensuring that they are supported and protected in their endeavors.
