अगच्छतं कृपमाणं परावती पितुः स्वस्य त्यजसां निबाधितम् |स्वर्वतीर इता ऊतीर युवोर अह चित्रा अभीक अभवन् अभिष्टयः ||
agacchataṁ kṛpamāṇam parāvati pituḥ svasya tyajasā nibādhitam | svarvatīr ita ūtīr yuvor aha citrā abhīke abhavann abhiṣṭayaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • agacchataṁ: "to go" or "to move towards"
    • kṛpamāṇam: "with compassion" or "with mercy"
    • parāvati: "the highest" or "supreme"
    • pituḥ: "of the father"
    • svasya: "of one's own" or "one's own self"
    • tyajasā: "by abandoning" or "by letting go"
    • nibādhitam: "bound" or "tied"
    • svarvatīr: "the one who is in the sky" or "heavenly"
    • ita: "here" or "in this place"
    • ūtīr: "to rise" or "to ascend"
    • yuvor: "of the young" or "of the youth"
    • aha: "today" or "this day"
    • citrā: "colorful" or "varied"
    • abhīke: "to be seen" or "to be perceived"
    • abhavann: "becoming" or "manifesting"
    • abhiṣṭayaḥ: "the desired" or "the wished for"
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of a journey towards compassion and the abandonment of ties that bind us.
    • The reference to "the father" suggests a connection to lineage and the importance of familial bonds.
    • It emphasizes the need to rise above earthly attachments and to seek a higher, more colorful existence.
    • The phrase "of the young" indicates a focus on youth and the potential they hold for change and growth.
    • Overall, it encourages individuals to perceive the beauty in life and to strive for their desires with a compassionate heart.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a personal context, this mantra can inspire someone to let go of toxic relationships that hinder their growth.
    • In a professional setting, it can motivate individuals to pursue their passions and ambitions, despite societal expectations.
    • In community service, it can encourage acts of kindness and compassion towards those in need, fostering a sense of unity and support.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for compassion, self-awareness, and the pursuit of higher ideals.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal growth to community engagement, reminding us to rise above our limitations.
    • Ultimately, it encourages us to embrace the colorful aspects of life and to manifest our deepest desires with a compassionate approach.
