इच्छन्न अश्वस्य यच चिरः पर्वतेष्व अपश्रितम् |तद विदच्छरणावतिः ||
icchann aśvasya yac chiraḥ parvateṣv apaśritam | tad vidac charyaṇāvati ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • icchann: wishing or desiring
    • aśvasya: of the horse
    • yac: that which
    • chiraḥ: for a long time
    • parvateṣv: in the mountains
    • apaśritam: sheltered or protected
    • tad: that
    • vidac: knowing or understanding
    • charyaṇāvati: is worthy of worship or reverence
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the desires associated with the horse, which is often a symbol of strength, speed, and freedom in Vedic literature.
    • The phrase "for a long time" suggests a deep-rooted aspiration or longing that has been nurtured over time.
    • The mention of "mountains" indicates a place of stability and permanence, where aspirations can find shelter and protection.
    • Ultimately, the mantra emphasizes that understanding and revering these desires is essential for spiritual growth and fulfillment.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern life, the "horse" can symbolize our ambitions and goals. For instance, a person may have a long-standing desire to achieve a particular career milestone, akin to the horse's strength and speed.
    • The "mountains" can represent the challenges and obstacles one faces in pursuit of these goals. Just as the horse seeks shelter in the mountains, individuals often seek support and guidance from mentors or communities.
    • Understanding and revering one's desires can lead to a more fulfilling life. For example, someone who recognizes their passion for art may dedicate time to develop their skills, leading to personal satisfaction and possibly a career in the arts.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the importance of recognizing and nurturing our desires while seeking stability and support in our pursuits.
    • It serves as a reminder that our aspirations, when understood and revered, can lead to significant personal and spiritual growth.
    • In real life, this can be applied by setting clear goals, seeking mentorship, and remaining committed to our passions, ultimately leading to a more meaningful existence.
