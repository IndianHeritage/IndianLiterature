मन्त्र: भद्रं नो अपि वातय मनो दक्षम् उता क्रतुम् |अधा ते सख्ये अन्धसो वि वो मदे रणन् गावो न यवसे विवक्षसे ||
bhadraṁ no api vātaya mano dakṣam uta kratum | adhā te sakhye andhaso vi vo made raṇan gāvo na yavase vivakṣase ||
  • bhadraṁ no api vātaya: This phrase translates to "May the winds be auspicious to us." Here, "bhadraṁ" signifies auspiciousness or goodness, while "vātaya" refers to the winds. In real life, this can be interpreted as a prayer for favorable conditions in our endeavors, akin to seeking good fortune in our daily activities.
  • mano dakṣam uta kratum: This means "May our minds be skilled and our intentions strong." "Mano" refers to the mind, "dakṣam" means skilled or capable, and "kratum" signifies intention or resolve. In practical terms, this can be seen as a call to cultivate mental agility and determination, essential qualities for achieving success in any field, whether in academics, sports, or personal goals.
  • adhā te sakhye andhaso: This translates to "We are united in friendship." "Adhā" means united, "te" refers to you (the divine or the universe), "sakhye" means friendship, and "andhaso" implies darkness or ignorance. This line emphasizes the importance of unity and camaraderie, suggesting that together we can overcome ignorance and challenges. In real life, this can be reflected in teamwork and collaboration in workplaces or communities.
  • vi vo made raṇan gāvo: This means "May we be filled with joy and strength." "Vi vo" translates to "with you," "made" refers to joy or delight, "raṇan" means strength, and "gāvo" signifies cows, which are often symbolic of wealth and prosperity in Vedic literature. This line can be interpreted as a wish for abundance and vitality in our lives, akin to striving for happiness and prosperity in our personal and professional lives.
  • na yavase vivakṣase: This translates to "May we not be hindered in our pursuits." "Na" means not, "yavase" refers to obstacles, and "vivakṣase" means to express or pursue. This line serves as a reminder to remain steadfast in our goals and aspirations, encouraging resilience in the face of challenges. In real life, this can be seen in the determination of individuals who overcome obstacles to achieve their dreams, such as entrepreneurs or athletes.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a holistic approach to life, emphasizing the importance of auspicious beginnings, mental acuity, unity, joy, and resilience. Its significance lies in its applicability to various aspects of life, from personal growth to community building. By invoking these qualities, individuals can navigate their journeys with a sense of purpose and strength, fostering a positive environment for themselves and those around them. This mantra serves as a timeless reminder of the values that lead to a fulfilling and prosperous life.
