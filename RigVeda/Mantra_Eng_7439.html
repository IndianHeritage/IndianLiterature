आयं वां कृष्णो अश्विनाः हवते वाजिनीवासु |मध्वः सोमस्य पीतये ||
ayaṁ vāṁ kṛṣṇo aśvinā havate vājinīvasū | madhvaḥ somasya pītaye ||
  • ayaṁ vāṁ: This phrase translates to "this is for you." It signifies a direct address, indicating a personal connection or offering.
  • kṛṣṇo: Refers to Lord Krishna, a central figure in Hinduism known for his wisdom, compassion, and divine playfulness. He represents the ideal of dharma (righteousness) and is often invoked for guidance.
  • aśvinā: This refers to the Ashvins, the twin Vedic deities associated with health, medicine, and the dawn. They symbolize the duality of life and the balance between opposites.
  • havate: This word means "to invoke" or "to call upon." It indicates a ritualistic aspect, where the speaker is seeking the blessings or presence of the deities.
  • vājinīvasū: This term can be interpreted as "the one who brings wealth" or "the one who provides abundance." It emphasizes prosperity and the fulfillment of desires.
  • madhvaḥ: This word means "sweet" or "pleasant." It often refers to the sweetness of life, joy, and the positive aspects of existence.
  • somasya: Refers to "Soma," a sacred drink in Vedic rituals believed to confer immortality and divine inspiration. It symbolizes the essence of life and spiritual enlightenment.
  • pītaye: This means "for drinking" or "to be consumed." It indicates the act of partaking in the divine essence or blessings offered by the deities.

Conclusion: The mantra "ayaṁ vāṁ kṛṣṇo aśvinā havate vājinīvasū | madhvaḥ somasya pītaye ||" encapsulates a profound invocation for divine blessings, prosperity, and spiritual nourishment. In real life, this mantra can be applied during rituals, prayers, or personal meditations where one seeks guidance, health, and abundance. It serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of life, the importance of seeking divine support, and the pursuit of a balanced and fulfilling existence. By invoking the energies of Krishna and the Ashvins, individuals can cultivate a sense of peace, joy, and prosperity in their daily lives.
