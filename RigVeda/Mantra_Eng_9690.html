प्र त्वा मुचामि वरुणस्य पाशाद येन त्वाबध्नात सविता सुशेवः |ऋतस्य योनौ सुखृतस्य लोके 'रिष्टां त्वा सह पत्या दधामि ||
pra tvā muñcāmi varuṇasya pāśād yena tvābadhnāt savitā suśevaḥ | ṛtasya yonau sukṛtasya loke 'riṣṭāṁ tvā saha patyā dadhāmi ||
  • pra tvā muñcāmi varuṇasya pāśād: This phrase translates to "I release you from the bonds of Varuna." Varuna is the Vedic deity associated with cosmic order and moral authority. In real life, this can be understood as a plea for liberation from constraints or negative influences that bind us, such as fear, guilt, or societal expectations.
  • yena tvābadhnāt savitā suśevaḥ: This means "by whom you are bound, may Savita (the Sun) grant you good." Savita represents the life-giving energy of the sun. This part emphasizes the idea that while we may be bound by certain forces, there is also a divine energy that can help us achieve goodness and prosperity. For example, in our daily lives, we often face challenges that can feel binding, but through positive actions and seeking guidance, we can find our way to success.
  • ṛtasya yonau sukṛtasya loke: This translates to "in the womb of truth, in the world of good deeds." Here, 'ṛta' refers to the cosmic order and truth, while 'sukṛta' refers to good deeds. This suggests that truth and righteousness are foundational to a harmonious life. In practical terms, this can be seen in how honesty and integrity in our actions lead to trust and respect in relationships, whether personal or professional.
  • 'riṣṭāṁ tvā saha patyā dadhāmi: This means "I place you with your husband." This can symbolize the idea of unity and partnership. In real life, this can be interpreted as the importance of relationships and support systems in overcoming life's challenges. For instance, in a marriage or partnership, mutual support can help individuals navigate difficulties together.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a profound message of liberation, the power of divine energy, the importance of truth and good deeds, and the significance of supportive relationships. In real life, it serves as a reminder that while we may face constraints, we have the ability to seek liberation through positive actions and the support of others. It encourages us to live truthfully and to foster relationships that uplift and empower us, ultimately leading to a more harmonious and fulfilling life.
