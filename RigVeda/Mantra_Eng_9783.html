समानम अस्मान् अनपावृद् अर्च क्ष्मयā दिवो असमम् ब्रह्म नव्यम् |विज यः पृष्ठेव जनिमान्य आर्य इन्द्रश् चिकाय न सखायम् ईषे ||
samānam asmā anapāvṛd arca kṣmayā divo asamam brahma navyam | vi yaḥ pṛṣṭheva janimāny arya indraś cikāya na sakhāyam īṣe ||
  • Translation: The mantra can be translated as: "May we be united in our thoughts and actions, may we be free from obstacles, and may we attain the divine knowledge that leads us to the ultimate truth. Just as the sun rises in the sky, may we also rise in our understanding and wisdom, guided by the divine."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • samānam: united, together
    • asmā: us, we
    • anapāvṛd: free from obstacles, unimpeded
    • arca: worship, reverence
    • kṣmayā: with grace, with kindness
    • divo: of the heavens, divine
    • asamam: equal, balanced
    • brahma: ultimate reality, truth
    • navyam: new, fresh
    • vi: who
    • yaḥ: that
    • pṛṣṭheva: like the back, as a support
    • janimāny: born, arising
    • arya: noble, honorable
    • indraś: of Indra, the king of gods
    • cikāya: may he guide, may he lead
    • na: not
    • sakhāyam: friend, companion
    • īṣe: may we strive, may we aim
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a team setting, this mantra emphasizes the importance of unity and collaboration. For instance, in a workplace, when team members work together harmoniously, they can overcome challenges and achieve common goals more effectively.
    • In personal relationships, the mantra encourages individuals to support each other and grow together. Couples who communicate openly and work through obstacles together often find deeper connections and understanding.
    • In community service, this mantra can inspire collective efforts to address social issues. When people come together with a shared purpose, they can create significant positive change in their communities.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for unity, support, and the pursuit of higher knowledge. It reminds us that by working together and striving for a common purpose, we can overcome obstacles and achieve greater understanding. In real life, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as in teamwork, relationships, and community efforts, fostering a spirit of collaboration and mutual respect. Ultimately, it encourages us to rise above challenges and seek the divine wisdom that guides our actions and thoughts.
