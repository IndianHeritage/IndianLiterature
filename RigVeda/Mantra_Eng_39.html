तं त्वा वाजेषु वाजिनं वाजयामः शतक्रतो |धनानां इन्द्र सातये ||
taṁ tvā vājeṣu vājinaṁ vājayāmaḥ śatakrato | dhanānām indra sātaye ||
  • taṁ tvā - This phrase translates to "to you" or "to that," indicating a direction of devotion or focus towards a divine entity.
  • vājeṣu - This word means "in the riches" or "in the wealth," referring to abundance and prosperity.
  • vājinaṁ - This term signifies "the one who bestows wealth" or "the giver of riches," often associated with deities who provide material and spiritual wealth.
  • vājayāmaḥ - This translates to "we invoke" or "we call upon," indicating an act of prayer or supplication.
  • śatakrato - This is a name of Indra, meaning "the one with a hundred powers," symbolizing strength, might, and the ability to overcome challenges.
  • dhanānām - This means "of wealth" or "of riches," emphasizing the importance of material prosperity.
  • indra sātaye - This phrase translates to "for the satisfaction of Indra," indicating a desire to please or gain favor from the deity.

The mantra can be understood as a prayer to Indra, the god of rain and thunderstorms, who is also associated with strength and prosperity. The devotee is invoking Indra to bestow wealth and abundance upon them, recognizing his power and influence in the material world.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as:

  • Business Ventures: Entrepreneurs may recite this mantra before starting a new business or project, seeking Indra's blessings for success and prosperity.
  • Financial Challenges: Individuals facing financial difficulties might use this mantra as a form of meditation or prayer, hoping to attract wealth and overcome their struggles.
  • Community Prosperity: Communities can come together to chant this mantra during festivals or gatherings, invoking collective prosperity and abundance for all members.

Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its invocation of divine support for wealth and prosperity. It serves as a reminder of the importance of seeking blessings from higher powers in our endeavors. By recognizing the role of divine forces in our material pursuits, we cultivate a sense of gratitude and responsibility towards the wealth we seek and receive. This mantra encourages individuals and communities to strive for abundance while acknowledging the spiritual dimensions of prosperity.
