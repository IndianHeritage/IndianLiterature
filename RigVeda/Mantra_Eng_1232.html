याभिर नरं गोषुयुधं नृषाह्ये क्षेत्रस्य साता तनयस्य जिन्वथः |याभी रथां अवथो याभिर अर्वतस ताभिर ऊषु ऊतिभिर अश्विनाऽ गतम् ||
yābhir naraṁ goṣuyudhaṁ nṛṣāhye kṣetrasya sātā tanayasya jinvathaḥ | yābhī rathām̐ avatho yābhir arvatas tābhir ū ṣu ūtibhir aśvinā gatam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yābhir: By whom
    • naraṁ: Man
    • goṣuyudhaṁ: In the battle of cattle
    • nṛṣāhye: In the contest of men
    • kṣetrasya: Of the field
    • sātā: Of the progeny
    • tanayasya: Of the offspring
    • jinvathaḥ: May you conquer
    • yābhī: By whom
    • rathām̐: Chariots
    • avatho: May you protect
    • yābhir: By whom
    • arvatas: Of the swift
    • tābhir: By those
    • ū: And
    • ṣu: Good
    • ūtibhir: With strength
    • aśvinā: O Ashvins (the twin deities)
    • gatam: Having gone
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the divine powers of the Ashvins, who are known as the twin gods of health and medicine in Vedic literature.
    • The phrase "yābhir naraṁ goṣuyudhaṁ" suggests a plea for protection and victory in conflicts, whether they be physical battles or struggles in life.
    • The mention of "kṣetrasya sātā tanayasya" emphasizes the importance of progeny and the continuation of lineage, highlighting the value of family and community.
    • Furthermore, "yābhī rathām̐ avatho" indicates a desire for safeguarding one's chariots, which can symbolize one's resources, ambitions, and endeavors.
    • The overall tone of the mantra is one of seeking divine assistance in overcoming challenges and achieving success.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a competitive work environment, individuals often face challenges that require them to seek support from colleagues or mentors, akin to invoking the Ashvins for guidance and strength.
    • Families may recite this mantra during significant life events, such as weddings or childbirth, to bless the new beginnings and ensure prosperity for future generations.
    • In sports, teams may use similar invocations to rally together, seeking strength and unity to overcome their opponents.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of seeking divine support in our endeavors, whether they are personal, familial, or communal.
    • It highlights the interconnectedness of individuals within a community and the shared responsibility to protect and uplift one another.
    • In essence, the mantra encourages us to strive for victory in our pursuits while remaining grounded in our values and relationships.
