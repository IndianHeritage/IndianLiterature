स īṁ रेबो न प्रति वस्त उष्राः शोचिषा रारपीति मित्रमहाः |नक्तं य īम अरुषो यो दिवा नृन अमर्त्यो अरुषो यो दिवा नृन ||
sa īṁ rebho na prati vasta usrāḥ śociṣā rārapīti mitramahāḥ | naktaṁ ya īm aruṣo yo divā nṝn amartyo aruṣo yo divā nṝn ||
  • sa īṁ rebho na prati vasta usrāḥ: This phrase can be interpreted as a call to the divine forces, asking for their presence and support. The term "rebho" refers to the energies or forces that are beneficial and protective.
  • śociṣā rārapīti mitramahāḥ: Here, "śociṣā" signifies brightness or purity, while "mitramahāḥ" refers to the great friend or ally. This suggests that the divine forces are not only protective but also bring light and positivity into our lives.
  • naktaṁ ya īm aruṣo: This part speaks to the duality of existence, referencing both night ("naktaṁ") and day ("divā"). "Arusho" can be understood as the radiant or fiery aspect of the divine, indicating that both night and day have their own significance and beauty.
  • yo divā nṝn amartyo: This line emphasizes the idea of immortality and the divine connection to humanity. "Amartyo" suggests a state beyond death, hinting at the eternal nature of the soul and its relationship with the divine.
  • aruṣo yo divā nṝn: This reiterates the connection between the divine and human beings, emphasizing that the divine presence is always with us, whether in the light of day or the darkness of night.

  • Significance: This mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking divine guidance and support in all aspects of life. It highlights the importance of recognizing the duality of existence and the eternal connection between the divine and humanity.
  • Real-life Application: In practical terms, this mantra can be invoked during times of uncertainty or difficulty, serving as a reminder that one is never alone. For instance, during challenging times at work or in personal relationships, reciting this mantra can provide comfort and a sense of connection to a higher power.
  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the protective and guiding forces in our lives. By acknowledging the divine presence, we can cultivate a sense of peace and resilience, allowing us to navigate the complexities of life with greater ease and confidence.
