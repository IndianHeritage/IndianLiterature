मन्त्र: पदं देवस्य मीळ्हुषो 'नाधृष्टाभिर ऊतिभिः |भद्रा सूर्य इवोपदृक ||
padaṁ devasya mīḻhuṣo 'nādhṛṣṭābhir ūtibhiḥ | bhadrā sūrya ivopadṛk ||
  • padaṁ - This word translates to "foot" or "path." In a spiritual context, it can signify the path of righteousness or the way to enlightenment.
  • devasya - Meaning "of the divine" or "of the god." This refers to the higher powers or deities that guide and protect us.
  • mīḻhuṣo - This term can be interpreted as "of the wise" or "of the learned." It emphasizes the importance of wisdom in understanding the divine.
  • ānādhṛṣṭābhir - This word means "by those who are not discouraged" or "by those who do not lose hope." It highlights the significance of perseverance and resilience in the face of challenges.
  • ūtibhiḥ - This translates to "with strength" or "with vigor." It suggests that one should approach life with energy and determination.
  • bhadrā - Meaning "auspicious" or "beneficial." This word conveys the idea of seeking good fortune and positive outcomes.
  • sūrya - This refers to the "sun," which symbolizes light, knowledge, and life. The sun is often seen as a source of energy and vitality.
  • ivopadṛk - This can be interpreted as "like a guide" or "as a protector." It suggests that the divine presence acts as a guiding force in our lives.

The mantra can be understood as a call to follow the divine path with wisdom and strength, remaining undeterred by obstacles, and seeking auspiciousness in life. It emphasizes the importance of resilience and the pursuit of knowledge, likening the divine guidance to the illuminating power of the sun.


In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts:

  • Personal Growth: When facing challenges in personal or professional life, one can draw inspiration from this mantra to remain steadfast and seek wisdom in decision-making.
  • Education: Students can use this mantra as a reminder to pursue knowledge with vigor and not to be discouraged by setbacks in their academic journey.
  • Spiritual Practice: Individuals on a spiritual path can recite this mantra to invoke divine guidance and strength, fostering a deeper connection with the divine.

In conclusion, this mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the importance of following a righteous path, seeking wisdom, and maintaining resilience in the face of adversity. It encourages individuals to embrace the light of knowledge and the protective guidance of the divine, making it applicable in various aspects of life.
