दानाय मनः सोमपावन्न अस्तु ते 'र्वाञ्चा हरि वन्दनश्रुद आ कृतहि | यमिष्टासः सारथयो य इन्द्र ते न त्वा केता आ दभ्नुवन्ति भूर्णयः ||
dānāya manaḥ somapāvann astu te 'rvāñcā harī vandanaśrud ā kṛdhi | yamiṣṭhāsaḥ sārathayo ya indra te na tvā ketā ā dabhnuvanti bhūrṇayaḥ ||
  • Translation: The mantra can be translated as: "May your mind be purified by the soma, and may you, O Indra, be worshipped by the offerings. Those who are your charioteers, may they not be hindered by the obstacles that come in their way."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • dānāya: For the purpose of giving or offering.
    • manaḥ: Mind or consciousness.
    • somapāvann: Purified by the soma (a sacred drink in Vedic rituals).
    • astu: May it be or let it be.
    • te: Your.
    • 'rvāñcā: Worship or reverence.
    • harī: A name for Indra, often associated with strength and power.
    • vandanaśrud: The act of worship or offering.
    • ā: To or towards.
    • kṛdhi: Do or make.
    • yamiṣṭhāsaḥ: Those who are your charioteers or guides.
    • sārathayo: Charioteers or those who drive the chariot.
    • ya: Who.
    • indra: The king of gods, representing strength and leadership.
    • te: Your.
    • na: Not.
    • tvā: You.
    • ketā: Obstacles or hindrances.
    • ā: To or towards.
    • dabhnuvanti: Hinder or obstruct.
    • bhūrṇayaḥ: Those who are strong or powerful.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, leaders often seek to purify their intentions and actions (similar to the purification by soma) to ensure that their decisions benefit the organization and its employees.
    • In personal relationships, individuals may strive to offer love and support to their partners, akin to the worship and offerings made in the mantra.
    • In sports, coaches and players work together as a team (the charioteers) to overcome challenges and obstacles, embodying the essence of the mantra.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for purification of the mind and intentions, the importance of offering and worship, and the need for teamwork in overcoming obstacles. In real life, it encourages individuals to strive for clarity in their thoughts, to support one another in their endeavors, and to work collaboratively to achieve common goals. This mantra can be applied in various aspects of life, including personal growth, professional environments, and community building, emphasizing the interconnectedness of individual actions and collective success.
