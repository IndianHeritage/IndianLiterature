आ पवमान धारय रयिं सहस्रवर्चसम् |अस्मे इन्दो स्वाभुवम् ||
ā pavamāna dhāraya rayiṁ sahasravarcasam | asme indo svābhuvam ||
  • Mantra: "ā pavamāna dhāraya rayiṁ sahasravarcasam | asme indo svābhuvam ||"
  • Translation: "O Purifier, hold the wealth that is full of brilliance for us; may it be of the nature of the divine, O Indra."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • ā pavamāna: "O Purifier" - Refers to a divine entity that cleanses and purifies.
    • dhāraya: "hold" - A request to sustain or maintain something valuable.
    • rayiṁ: "wealth" - Represents prosperity, abundance, and resources.
    • sahasravarcasam: "full of brilliance" - Indicates wealth that is not just material but also radiant and auspicious.
    • asme: "for us" - A plea for personal benefit and collective good.
    • indo: "O Indra" - Indra is a prominent deity in Vedic literature, symbolizing strength and power.
    • svābhuvam: "of the nature of the divine" - Suggests that the wealth sought is not just material but also spiritually uplifting.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be interpreted as a call for ethical wealth generation, where the focus is not only on profits but also on the positive impact on society.
    • In personal life, it can be seen as a reminder to seek not just material possessions but also spiritual and emotional wealth, such as love, happiness, and fulfillment.
    • In community settings, it encourages collective efforts towards prosperity that benefits all, akin to community service or cooperative businesses.
  • Significance:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of purity and intention behind the pursuit of wealth.
    • It highlights the need for a balance between material and spiritual wealth, suggesting that true prosperity encompasses both.
    • By invoking Indra, it connects the pursuit of wealth with divine blessings, indicating that success should be aligned with higher values.
  • Conclusion:
    • The mantra serves as a guiding principle for individuals and communities to seek wealth that is not only abundant but also beneficial and uplifting.
    • It is applicable in various aspects of life, including personal growth, business ethics, and community development.
    • Ultimately, it encourages a holistic approach to prosperity, where the pursuit of wealth is intertwined with moral and spiritual values.
