शंसेद उक्तं सुदानव उता द्युक्षं यथा नरः |चक्रिमा सत्यराधसे ||
śaṁsed ukthaṁ sudānava uta dyukṣaṁ yathā naraḥ | cakṛmā satyarādhase ||
  • śaṁsed: This term can be interpreted as "to be fulfilled" or "to be completed." It signifies the importance of achieving one's goals and aspirations.
  • ukthaṁ: This word refers to "speech" or "utterance." It emphasizes the power of words and the significance of communication in human interactions.
  • sudānava: This term can be understood as "good nourishment" or "beneficial sustenance." It highlights the importance of nurturing oneself and others, both physically and spiritually.
  • uta: This word means "and" or "also," indicating a connection or addition to the previous ideas.
  • dyukṣaṁ: This term can be interpreted as "to be bright" or "to shine." It symbolizes the pursuit of knowledge, wisdom, and enlightenment.
  • yathā: This word means "as" or "like," suggesting a comparison or analogy.
  • naraḥ: This term refers to "man" or "human being," emphasizing the human experience and the journey of life.
  • cakṛmā: This word can be understood as "to create" or "to make." It signifies the act of bringing ideas and dreams into reality through effort and determination.
  • satyarādhase: This term means "to the one who is established in truth." It highlights the importance of truthfulness and integrity in one's actions and thoughts.

In summary, this mantra emphasizes the significance of fulfilling one's goals through effective communication, nurturing oneself and others, and pursuing knowledge and enlightenment. It encourages individuals to create their own paths while remaining grounded in truth and integrity.


Conclusion: The mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of our actions, words, and aspirations. In real-life scenarios, it can be applied in various contexts, such as personal development, leadership, and community building. By embodying the principles outlined in this mantra, individuals can strive for a balanced and meaningful life, contributing positively to their surroundings and fostering a sense of unity and purpose.
