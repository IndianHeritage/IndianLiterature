स हि ष्मा दानम् इन्बति वसूनां च मजनना |स नस् त्रासते दुरिताद अभिहृतः शंसाद अघाद अभिहृतः ||
sa hi ṣmā dānam invati vasūnāṁ ca majmanā | sa nas trāsate duritād abhihrutaḥ śaṁsād aghād abhihrutaḥ ||
  • sa hi ṣmā dānam invati vasūnāṁ ca majmanā: This line can be translated to mean "Indeed, the gift (or donation) brings forth wealth and prosperity." Here, "dānam" refers to the act of giving or charity, while "vasūnāṁ" signifies wealth or resources. The mantra emphasizes the importance of generosity in attracting abundance.
  • sa nas trāsate duritād: This part translates to "It protects us from misfortunes." The word "trāsate" means to protect or shield, and "duritād" refers to difficulties or misfortunes. This suggests that acts of kindness and generosity can serve as a safeguard against life's challenges.
  • abhihrutaḥ śaṁsād aghād abhihrutaḥ: This can be interpreted as "Those who are endowed with blessings are free from sin." "Abhihrutaḥ" means to be endowed or blessed, while "aghād" refers to sin or negativity. This line reinforces the idea that those who engage in virtuous acts, such as giving, are blessed and liberated from negative influences.

  • Real-Life Examples:
    • Charitable Donations: When individuals or organizations donate to charities, they not only help those in need but also often find themselves experiencing a sense of fulfillment and increased prosperity in their own lives.
    • Community Support: In communities where people support each other through acts of kindness, such as volunteering or helping neighbors, there tends to be a stronger sense of unity and resilience against hardships.
    • Personal Growth: Engaging in acts of generosity can lead to personal growth and a positive mindset, which can help individuals navigate challenges more effectively.

  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its profound message about the power of generosity and its protective qualities. In real life, it serves as a reminder that giving not only benefits others but also enriches our own lives. By practicing generosity, we can create a positive cycle of abundance and protection against misfortunes, fostering a harmonious and supportive community. This mantra encourages us to embrace the spirit of giving, highlighting its transformative impact on both the giver and the receiver.
