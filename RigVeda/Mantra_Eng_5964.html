अद्यā मुरीय यदि यातुधानोऽस्मि यदि वायुस् तातप पूरुषस्य |अधā स वीरैर दशभिर् वि यūयā यो मां मोघं यातुधानेत्याह ||
adyā murīya yadi yātudhāno asmi yadi vāyus tatapa pūruṣasya | adhā sa vīrair daśabhir vi yūyā yo mā moghaṁ yātudhānety āha ||
  • adyā: Today
  • murīya: I am free from all obstacles
  • yadi: If
  • yātudhāno: The evil spirits or obstacles
  • asmi: I am
  • yadi vāyus: If the wind
  • tatapa: Burns or torments
  • pūruṣasya: Of the person
  • adhā: Below or beneath
  • sa: That
  • vīrair: By the heroes
  • daśabhir: With ten
  • vi yūyā: You all
  • yo: Who
  • mā: Not
  • moghaṁ: In vain
  • yātudhānety: Against the evil spirits
  • āha: Speaks

This mantra can be interpreted as a powerful invocation for protection and strength against obstacles and negative forces. The speaker asserts their freedom from hindrances and calls upon the strength of heroes to combat any evil spirits or challenges that may arise.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios such as:

  • Personal Challenges: When facing difficulties in life, such as health issues or personal conflicts, reciting this mantra can serve as a reminder of one's inner strength and the support available from others.
  • Professional Obstacles: In a work environment, when encountering challenges or competition, this mantra can inspire confidence and resilience, encouraging individuals to overcome barriers with the help of their colleagues.
  • Spiritual Protection: Many people use mantras for spiritual protection against negative energies or influences. This mantra emphasizes the importance of community and collective strength in overcoming such challenges.

Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its empowering message of overcoming obstacles with the support of others. It teaches us that we are not alone in our struggles and that collective strength can help us face and conquer challenges. In everyday life, whether in personal, professional, or spiritual contexts, this mantra serves as a reminder to seek support and harness our inner strength to navigate through difficulties.
