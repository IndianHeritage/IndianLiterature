इमं यज्ञं त्वम अस्माकं इन्द्र पुरो दधत सनीष्यसि क्रतुं नः |श्वघ्निवा वज्रिन सनये धनानां त्वया वयं आर्य आजिं जयेम ||
imaṁ yajñaṁ tvam asmākam indra puro dadhat saniṣyasi kratuṁ naḥ | śvaghnīva vajrin sanaye dhanānāṁ tvayā vayam arya ājiṁ jayema ||
  • Translation: "O Indra, you who are the bestower of our sacrifices, may you grant us strength and wisdom. Like the powerful thunderbolt, may you bestow upon us wealth and victory."
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • imaṁ yajñaṁ: this sacrifice
    • tvam: you
    • asmākam: of us
    • indra: O Indra (the king of gods)
    • puro: in front
    • dadhat: may you place
    • saniṣyasi: may you grant
    • kratuṁ: strength, wisdom
    • naḥ: to us
    • śvaghnīva: like the powerful
    • vajrin: thunderbolt
    • sanaye: may you bestow
    • dhanānāṁ: of wealth
    • tvayā: by you
    • vayam: we
    • arya: noble
    • ājiṁ: victory
    • jayema: may we achieve
  • Context and Significance:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Indra, the god of rain and thunderstorms, who is often associated with strength and power.
    • It emphasizes the importance of sacrifices (yajñas) in seeking blessings from the divine for strength and prosperity.
    • The metaphor of the thunderbolt (vajra) signifies the power and impact of divine blessings in overcoming challenges.
  • Real-Life Applications:
    • In modern life, this mantra can be seen as a call for seeking guidance and strength in times of difficulty, such as during personal challenges or societal issues.
    • It can be applied in various contexts, such as in business ventures where individuals seek success and prosperity through hard work and ethical practices.
    • In community gatherings or rituals, this mantra can be recited to invoke collective strength and unity for achieving common goals.
  • Conclusion:
    • The mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of seeking divine support in our endeavors.
    • It highlights the interconnectedness of individual efforts and divine blessings in achieving success and prosperity.
    • By invoking Indra's strength, we are encouraged to pursue our goals with determination and integrity, fostering a sense of community and shared purpose.
