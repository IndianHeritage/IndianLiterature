अस्मा इद उ स्तोमं सं हिनोमि रथं न तष्टेव तात्सिनाय |गिरश च गिर्वाहसे सुवृक्तीन्द्राय विश्वमिन्वम मेधिराय ||
asmā id u stomaṁ saṁ hinomi rathaṁ na taṣṭeva tatsināya | giraś ca girvāhase suvṛktīndrāya viśvaminvam medhirāya ||
  • Translation: "I offer this hymn to the one who is the embodiment of strength and power, who drives the chariot of life, and who is the source of all wisdom and prosperity."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • asmā: "we" or "us" - indicating a collective offering or prayer.
    • id: "this" - referring to the hymn or mantra being recited.
    • u: "also" - adding emphasis to the offering.
    • stomaṁ: "hymn" or "praise" - the act of worship through words.
    • saṁ hinomi: "I offer" - a personal commitment to the act of devotion.
    • rathaṁ: "chariot" - symbolizing the journey of life and the forces that drive it.
    • na taṣṭeva: "not to be abandoned" - emphasizing the importance of steadfastness and support.
    • tatsināya: "to the one who is the source" - acknowledging the divine source of strength.
    • giraś: "speech" or "words" - the power of communication and expression.
    • ca: "and" - connecting ideas and concepts.
    • girvāhase: "to the one who is praised" - recognizing the divine being worthy of worship.
    • suvṛktīndrāya: "to the one who grants prosperity" - invoking blessings of abundance and success.
    • viśvaminvam: "to all beings" - a universal appeal for blessings.
    • medhirāya: "to the one who is wise" - seeking wisdom and understanding.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, this mantra can be recited during team meetings to invoke collective strength and wisdom, ensuring that all team members are aligned and motivated towards a common goal.
    • During personal challenges, individuals can use this mantra as a form of meditation to seek guidance and support, reminding themselves of the strength they possess and the wisdom they can draw upon.
    • In community gatherings, this hymn can serve as a reminder of the importance of unity and collective effort in achieving prosperity and well-being for all members.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its emphasis on collective strength, wisdom, and the importance of support in our lives. It serves as a reminder that we are not alone in our journeys; we have the power of community and the divine to guide us. In real-life scenarios, whether in personal struggles, professional endeavors, or community efforts, invoking this mantra can inspire resilience, unity, and a shared vision for prosperity and success.
