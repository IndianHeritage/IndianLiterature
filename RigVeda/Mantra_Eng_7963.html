स नो ज्योतिंषि पूर्व्य पवमान विग रोचय |क्रतवे दक्षाय नो हिनु ||
sa no jyotīṁṣi pūrvya pavamāna vi rocaya | kratve dakṣāya no hinu ||
  • sa no: This phrase translates to "may that (energy) be ours." It signifies a collective aspiration for a shared experience or benefit.
  • jyotīṁṣi: This word means "lights" or "radiances." It symbolizes knowledge, wisdom, and enlightenment.
  • pūrvya: This term refers to "the ancient" or "the previous." It connects to the wisdom and knowledge passed down through generations.
  • pavamāna: This word means "the purifying one." It represents the forces that cleanse and elevate our consciousness.
  • vi rocaya: This phrase translates to "may it shine forth." It is a call for illumination and clarity in our lives.
  • kratve: This term means "for the purpose" or "for the action." It emphasizes intention and the drive behind our actions.
  • dakṣāya: This word translates to "skill" or "competence." It highlights the importance of being adept and capable in our endeavors.
  • no hinu: This phrase means "for us." It signifies a personal and communal plea for guidance and support.

In essence, this mantra is a prayer for the illumination of knowledge and wisdom that has been cherished through time. It seeks the purifying energy that can help us shine in our pursuits, emphasizing the importance of skill and intention in our actions.


For example, in a real-life scenario, this mantra can be applied in educational settings where students seek knowledge and clarity in their studies. When students recite this mantra, they invoke the ancient wisdom of their predecessors, aiming to enhance their understanding and skills in their respective fields.


In conclusion, the significance of this mantra lies in its call for enlightenment, purification, and skillful action. It serves as a reminder that in our quest for knowledge and success, we should remain connected to the wisdom of the past while striving to shine brightly in our present endeavors. This mantra can be applicable in various aspects of life, including education, professional development, and personal growth, guiding individuals and communities towards a more enlightened and skillful existence.
