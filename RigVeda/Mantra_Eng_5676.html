एष स्तोमो वरुण मित्र तुभ्यं सोमः शुक्रो न वायवे 'यामि |अविष्टं धियो जिगृतं पुरंधीः यूयम् पात स्वस्तिभिः सदा नः ||
eṣa stomo varuṇa mitra tubhyaṁ somaḥ śukro na vāyave 'yāmi | aviṣṭaṁ dhiyo jigṛtam puraṁdhīr yūyam pāta svastibhiḥ sadā naḥ ||
  • eṣa stomo varuṇa mitra tubhyaṁ: This phrase can be translated as "This hymn is dedicated to you, Varuna and Mitra." Varuna and Mitra are deities associated with cosmic order and friendship, respectively. In real life, this can be seen as a call for harmony and balance in relationships, emphasizing the importance of trust and cooperation among individuals.
  • somaḥ śukro na vāyave 'yāmi: Here, "somaḥ" refers to the sacred drink that symbolizes immortality and divine inspiration, while "śukro" means bright or pure. The phrase suggests a desire to attain purity and enlightenment, akin to seeking knowledge and wisdom in our daily lives. For example, pursuing education or spiritual practices can be seen as a modern-day interpretation of this quest for purity and enlightenment.
  • aviṣṭaṁ dhiyo jigṛtam puraṁdhīr: This translates to "May our intellects be unclouded and our understanding be sharp." It emphasizes the importance of clarity of thought and wisdom. In practical terms, this can relate to decision-making processes in our lives, where clear thinking leads to better outcomes, whether in personal relationships or professional endeavors.
  • yūyam pāta svastibhiḥ sadā naḥ: This line means "May you protect us always with your blessings." It reflects a plea for divine protection and well-being. In everyday life, this can be interpreted as seeking support from a higher power or the universe, especially during challenging times, reminding us of the importance of faith and hope.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates themes of harmony, purity, clarity, and divine protection. Its significance lies in its call for balance in relationships, the pursuit of knowledge, and the need for spiritual support. In real-life scenarios, these principles can guide individuals in fostering healthy relationships, making informed decisions, and maintaining a hopeful outlook in the face of adversity. By embodying these values, one can navigate life's challenges with grace and wisdom.
