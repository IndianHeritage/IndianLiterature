प्र यात शीभम आशुभिः सन्ति कण्वेषु वो दुवाह् | तत्रो षु मादयाध्वै ||
pra yāta śībham āśubhiḥ santi kaṇveṣu vo duvaḥ | tatro ṣu mādayādhvai ||
  • Mantra: "pra yāta śībham āśubhiḥ santi kaṇveṣu vo duvaḥ | tatro ṣu mādayādhvai ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • pra: forward, towards
    • yāta: go, proceed
    • śībham: auspiciousness, good fortune
    • āśubhiḥ: from inauspiciousness, negativity
    • santi: there are, exist
    • kaṇveṣu: in the actions, deeds
    • vo: for you, to you
    • duvaḥ: offerings, blessings
    • tatro: there, in that place
    • ṣu: good, auspicious
    • mādayādhvai: may you rejoice, be happy
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the journey towards auspiciousness and the removal of negativity.
    • It emphasizes the importance of moving forward towards good fortune while leaving behind inauspiciousness.
    • The phrase "for you" indicates a personal connection, suggesting that the blessings and offerings are directed towards the individual or group reciting the mantra.
    • The concluding part encourages joy and happiness in the journey, highlighting the significance of a positive mindset.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, this mantra can be applied when embarking on a new project. By focusing on positive outcomes and leaving behind past failures, individuals can foster a productive environment.
    • In personal life, during times of stress or negativity, reciting this mantra can serve as a reminder to seek joy and positivity, encouraging individuals to move forward with hope.
    • In community gatherings or rituals, this mantra can be recited to invoke blessings for collective well-being, promoting a sense of unity and shared joy.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its ability to inspire individuals to pursue auspiciousness while overcoming negativity.
    • It serves as a reminder that joy and positivity are essential components of any journey, whether personal or communal.
    • By applying the teachings of this mantra in daily life, individuals can cultivate a more optimistic outlook, leading to better outcomes in various aspects of life.
