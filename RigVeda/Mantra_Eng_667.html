अध ते विश्वम् अनु हासद इष्टय आपो निम्नेव सवनाः हविष्मतः |यत पर्वते न समशीत हर्यत इन्द्रस्य वज्रः शनथिता हिरण्ययः ||
adha te viśvam anu hāsad iṣṭaya āpo nimneva savanā haviṣmataḥ | yat parvate na samaśīta haryata indrasya vajraḥ śnathitā hiraṇyayaḥ ||
  • Adha te viśvam anu hāsad iṣṭaya: This phrase can be translated to "May all beings be in harmony with you." It emphasizes the importance of unity and cooperation among all living entities. In real life, this can be seen in community efforts where individuals come together for a common cause, such as environmental conservation or social justice movements.
  • Āpo nimneva savanā haviṣmataḥ: This part means "Like the waters that flow together, may our offerings be accepted." It signifies the idea of collective effort and the importance of sharing resources. For example, in a workplace, when team members collaborate and share their skills, they can achieve greater success than working in isolation.
  • Yat parvate na samaśīta haryata: This translates to "Where the mountains do not obstruct the flow." It suggests the idea of overcoming obstacles and challenges. In real life, this can be related to personal growth, where individuals face and conquer their fears or difficulties, much like a river that carves its path through mountains.
  • Indrasya vajraḥ śnathitā hiraṇyayaḥ: This phrase means "The thunderbolt of Indra, made of gold." Indra is often associated with strength and power. This can be interpreted as the strength derived from unity and collaboration, akin to how a strong team can achieve remarkable results. For instance, in sports, a well-coordinated team can outperform a group of individually talented players.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of unity, collaboration, and overcoming obstacles. Its significance lies in the reminder that when individuals come together with a common purpose, they can achieve greatness, much like the flowing waters or the strength of a united team. In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as community service, teamwork in professional settings, and personal development, encouraging individuals to work together harmoniously for a greater good.
