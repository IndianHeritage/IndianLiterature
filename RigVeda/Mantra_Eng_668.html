अस्मै भीमाय नमसā सम अध्वर उषो न शुभ्र ā भरā पानीयसे |यस्य धाम श्रवसे नामेन्द्रियं ज्योतिर अकारी हरितो नायसे ||
asmai bhīmāya namasā sam adhvara uṣo na śubhra ā bharā panīyase | yasya dhāma śravase nāmendriyaṁ jyotir akāri harito nāyase ||
  • Asmai bhīmāya namasā: This phrase can be translated as "To that great one, we bow." It signifies respect and reverence towards a powerful entity, often interpreted as a divine force or energy.
  • Sam adhvara uṣo na śubhra: This means "In the sacred act, may the dawn be auspicious." Here, "adhvara" refers to a sacred ritual or act, while "uṣo" represents the dawn, symbolizing new beginnings and enlightenment.
  • Ā bharā panīyase: This translates to "Bring forth the waters." Water is often associated with life, purity, and sustenance, indicating a plea for abundance and nourishment.
  • Yasya dhāma śravase: This means "Whose abode is renowned." It refers to the dwelling of the divine or the source of all creation, emphasizing the importance of seeking connection with the divine.
  • Nāmendriyaṁ jyotir akāri: This translates to "The light that is beyond the senses." It suggests a higher consciousness or spiritual awareness that transcends ordinary perception.
  • Harito nāyase: This means "May it lead us to the green pastures." Green pastures symbolize prosperity, growth, and well-being, indicating a desire for a fruitful and fulfilling life.

  • Significance: This mantra encapsulates a deep reverence for the divine, the importance of rituals, and the quest for spiritual enlightenment. It emphasizes the need for sustenance, both physical and spiritual, and the aspiration for a prosperous life.
  • Real-life Application: In daily life, this mantra can be invoked during rituals, prayers, or meditation to seek blessings for health, prosperity, and spiritual growth. For instance, during a family gathering or a significant life event, reciting this mantra can create a sacred atmosphere, inviting positive energies and blessings.

  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of life, the importance of seeking divine guidance, and the pursuit of a meaningful existence. By incorporating its teachings into our lives, we can foster a sense of gratitude, purpose, and harmony with the universe.
