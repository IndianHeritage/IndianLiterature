आ यः स्वर्ण भानुना चित्रो विभाति अर्किषा |अञ्जानो अजरैर अभि ||
ā yaḥ sva1r ṇa bhānunā citro vibhāty arciṣā | añjāno ajarair abhi ||
  • Word Breakdown:
    • ā yaḥ: This can be interpreted as "that which is" or "that which exists."
    • sva1r: Refers to "light" or "radiance," often associated with the sun or divine illumination.
    • ṇa: A particle that can denote negation or absence.
    • bhānunā: Translates to "by the sun" or "through the light of the sun."
    • citro: Means "variegated" or "colorful," suggesting diversity and richness.
    • vibhāty: Means "shines" or "illuminates," indicating the act of bringing light or clarity.
    • arciṣā: Refers to "by the rays" or "through the beams," emphasizing the source of illumination.
    • añjāno: Can be interpreted as "the one who is adorned" or "the one who is decorated."
    • ajarair: Means "immortal" or "eternal," suggesting a state beyond decay.
    • abhi: A preposition meaning "towards" or "upon," indicating direction or focus.
  • Meaning:
    • This mantra speaks of the radiant light of the sun, which brings clarity and illumination to the world.
    • It emphasizes the beauty and diversity of existence, as represented by the colorful rays of sunlight.
    • The mention of "immortal" suggests a connection to eternal truths or principles that transcend the physical realm.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, the sun is a source of energy and life. Just as sunlight nourishes plants, this mantra reminds us of the importance of seeking knowledge and enlightenment.
    • The diversity of colors in nature, such as in a sunset or a blooming garden, reflects the "citro" aspect, encouraging us to appreciate the variety in our surroundings.
    • In moments of confusion or darkness, seeking clarity through knowledge and wisdom can be likened to finding light in the darkness, as suggested by the mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of light, knowledge, and diversity in our lives.
    • It encourages us to seek illumination in our thoughts and actions, promoting a deeper understanding of ourselves and the world around us.
    • In practical terms, it can be applied in various aspects of life, such as education, personal growth, and fostering appreciation for the beauty of diversity in our communities.
