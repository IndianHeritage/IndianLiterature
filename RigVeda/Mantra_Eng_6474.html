यस्य वा युयम् प्रति वाजिनो नर आ हव्यā वीतये गथा |अभि षद्युम्नैर उता वाजसातिभिः सुम्ना वो धूतयो नशत ||
yasya vā yūyam prati vājino nara ā havyā vītaye gatha | abhi ṣa dyumnair uta vājasātibhiḥ sumnā vo dhūtayo naśat ||
  • Mantra: "yasya vā yūyam prati vājino nara ā havyā vītaye gatha | abhi ṣa dyumnair uta vājasātibhiḥ sumnā vo dhūtayo naśat ||"
  • Translation: This mantra can be interpreted as a call for blessings and prosperity. It speaks to the divine forces that guide and protect individuals, urging them to bestow their grace upon those who are worthy.
  • Word Breakdown:
    • yasya: of whom
    • vā: or
    • yūyam: you (plural)
    • prati: towards
    • vājino: the strong or powerful
    • nara: man or human
    • ā: to
    • havyā: offerings or sacrifices
    • vītaye: for the purpose of liberation or attainment
    • gatha: to go or to move
    • abhi: towards
    • ṣa: six (often used in a context of completeness)
    • dyumnair: with glory or brilliance
    • uta: and
    • vājasātibhiḥ: with the strength of horses (symbolizing power)
    • sumnā: auspiciously
    • vo: to you
    • dhūtayo: those who are purified or cleansed
    • naśat: may they prosper or thrive
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, this mantra can be invoked during meetings or decision-making processes to seek clarity and strength in leadership, ensuring that the team moves towards success with collective effort.
    • During personal challenges, individuals may recite this mantra to seek guidance and support from higher powers, fostering a sense of hope and determination to overcome obstacles.
    • In community gatherings or rituals, this mantra can be used to invoke blessings for collective prosperity and harmony, encouraging unity and shared goals among participants.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine blessings and the strength to achieve one's goals. It emphasizes the importance of collective effort, purity of intention, and the pursuit of prosperity. In real-life scenarios, it can be applied in various contexts, from personal growth to community development, serving as a reminder of the power of unity and the support of higher forces in our endeavors.
