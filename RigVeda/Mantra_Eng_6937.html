तवेद उ ताः सुखीरतयोऽसन्नुत प्रशस्तयः |यद इन्द्र मृळयासि नः ||
taved u tāḥ sukīrtayo 'sann uta praśastayaḥ | yad indra mṛḻayāsi naḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • taved: By your power
    • u: and
    • tāḥ: those
    • sukīrtayo: of good fame
    • 'sann: being
    • uta: or
    • praśastayaḥ: of praise
    • yad: which
    • indra: O Indra (the king of gods)
    • mṛḻayāsi: you bestow
    • naḥ: upon us
  • Overall Meaning: This mantra is a prayer to Indra, seeking his blessings and the power to achieve good fame and praise. It emphasizes the importance of divine support in attaining success and recognition in life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • Career Success: In a professional setting, individuals often seek the blessings of higher powers or mentors to achieve recognition and success in their careers. For instance, a student may pray for guidance and support before an important exam or job interview.
    • Community Recognition: Community leaders or activists may invoke this mantra when seeking support for their initiatives, hoping to gain recognition for their efforts in improving society.
    • Personal Achievements: Athletes often seek blessings before competitions, hoping to achieve personal bests and gain accolades, similar to how this mantra seeks divine favor for good fame.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its acknowledgment of the need for divine assistance in achieving success and recognition. It teaches us that while personal effort is crucial, the support of higher powers or the community can play a vital role in our accomplishments. In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios where individuals seek to excel, whether in academics, careers, or personal endeavors, reminding us to remain humble and seek support from the universe or our community.
