मन्त्र: हिरण्यशृङ्गो यो अस्य पादाः मनोजवाः अवर इन्द्र आसीत् | देवाः इद अस्य हविराद्यम् आयन यो अर्वन्तम् प्रथमो अध्यतिष्ठत् ||
hiraṇyaśṛṅgo 'yo asya pādā manojavā avara indra āsīt | devā id asya haviradyam āyan yo arvantam prathamo adhyatiṣṭhat ||
  • hiraṇyaśṛṅgo: This term translates to "golden horn" or "golden peak." In a metaphorical sense, it can represent prosperity, abundance, and the heights of achievement. For example, in modern life, a successful business or a person who has reached the pinnacle of their career can be seen as having a "golden horn."
  • yo asya pādā: This phrase means "who is the support of this." It signifies the foundation or the basis upon which something stands. In real life, this could refer to the values and principles that support an individual's actions or a community's culture.
  • manojavā: This word means "swift as the mind." It emphasizes the speed and agility of thought and action. In today's fast-paced world, the ability to think quickly and adapt is crucial, whether in business decisions or personal life choices.
  • avara indra āsīt: This translates to "the lower Indra was present." Indra is often seen as a king of gods and a symbol of power. This could imply that even in lower states or situations, there is a presence of strength and authority. For instance, a leader in a small community can have a significant impact, similar to Indra's influence.
  • devā id asya haviradyam āyan: This means "the gods have come to this offering." It suggests that divine forces are present in our actions and offerings. In real life, this can be seen in rituals or acts of kindness where people feel a connection to something greater, such as community service or charitable acts.
  • yo arvantam prathamo adhyatiṣṭhat: This translates to "who first established the one who is moving." It signifies the importance of being a pioneer or a leader in any endeavor. In modern contexts, this could refer to innovators in technology or social movements who pave the way for others.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of prosperity, foundational values, swift thinking, and the presence of divine support in our actions. It highlights the importance of leadership and pioneering spirit in achieving success. In real life, these principles can be applied in various scenarios, such as in business, community service, and personal development, reminding us that our actions are supported by a greater purpose and that we have the potential to lead and inspire others.
