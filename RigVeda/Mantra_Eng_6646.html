ऋते स विनदते युधः सुगेभिर यात्य अध्वनः |आर्यमा मित्रो वरुणः सरातयो यं त्रायन्ते सजोषसः ||
ṛte sa vindate yudhaḥ sugebhir yāty adhvanaḥ | aryamā mitro varuṇaḥ sarātayo yaṁ trāyante sajoṣasaḥ ||
  • ṛte: This word translates to "without" or "in the absence of." It signifies the importance of truth and righteousness in actions.
  • sa: Meaning "he" or "that," it refers to the divine or the higher power that guides us.
  • vindate: This means "to find" or "to attain." It suggests the pursuit of goals or truths.
  • yudhaḥ: Translated as "battle" or "struggle," it represents the challenges and conflicts we face in life.
  • sugebhir: This word means "with good qualities" or "with auspiciousness," indicating the importance of virtue in our endeavors.
  • yāty: Meaning "goes" or "moves," it signifies the journey or path one takes in life.
  • adhvanaḥ: This translates to "of the path," emphasizing the significance of the journey we undertake.
  • aryamā: A name of a deity, representing the divine forces that protect and guide us.
  • mitro: Meaning "friend," it signifies companionship and support in our endeavors.
  • varuṇaḥ: Another deity, associated with cosmic order and moral authority, representing the law of nature.
  • sarātayo: This means "those who are in harmony," indicating the importance of unity and cooperation.
  • yaṁ: Meaning "whom," it refers to the divine or the guiding principles.
  • trāyante: This translates to "protect" or "safeguard," emphasizing the protective nature of divine forces.
  • sajoṣasaḥ: Meaning "with joy" or "with happiness," it signifies the positive outcomes of righteous actions.

The mantra emphasizes the significance of truth, virtue, and divine guidance in overcoming life's struggles. It suggests that by adhering to righteousness and seeking the support of divine forces, one can navigate the challenges of life successfully.


In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts:

  • Personal Struggles: When facing challenges, individuals can reflect on the importance of truth and virtue, seeking guidance from their inner selves or higher powers.
  • Community Support: The mantra highlights the importance of friendship and unity, encouraging individuals to seek support from their community during difficult times.
  • Professional Challenges: In the workplace, adhering to ethical practices and seeking guidance can lead to success and harmony among colleagues.

In conclusion, this mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of righteousness, divine guidance, and the support of others in overcoming life's challenges. It encourages individuals to pursue their goals with virtue and to seek protection and harmony in their journeys.
