विष्वेद अनु रोधना अस्य पौंस्यं ददुर अस्मै दधिरे कृत्नवे धनम् |षट् अस्तभ्ना विष्टिरः पाँच संदृशः परि परो अभवः सास्य उक्त्यः ||
viśved anu rodhanā asya pauṁsyaṁ dadur asmai dadhire kṛtnave dhanam | ṣaḻ astabhnā viṣṭiraḥ pañca saṁdṛśaḥ pari paro abhavaḥ sāsy ukthyaḥ ||
  • viśved anu rodhanā: This phrase can be interpreted as "the universal forces or energies that are in harmony." In real life, this can be related to the idea of teamwork and collaboration, where different individuals come together to achieve a common goal.
  • asya pauṁsyaṁ dadur asmai: This translates to "they have given him strength." This can be seen in scenarios where mentorship or support from others empowers an individual, such as a teacher guiding a student or a coach motivating an athlete.
  • dadhire kṛtnave dhanam: This means "they have bestowed upon him wealth." In practical terms, this can refer to the abundance that comes from hard work and dedication, such as a farmer reaping a bountiful harvest after a season of labor.
  • ṣaḻ astabhnā viṣṭiraḥ: This can be understood as "sixfold support or stability." In everyday life, this could relate to the various forms of support systems we have, such as family, friends, community, and professional networks that provide stability during challenging times.
  • pañca saṁdṛśaḥ: This translates to "fivefold vision." This can be likened to having multiple perspectives or approaches to problem-solving, similar to how a team might brainstorm different solutions to a challenge.
  • pari paro abhavaḥ: This means "they have become one with the universe." This reflects the idea of interconnectedness, where individuals recognize their place within a larger community or ecosystem, akin to environmental awareness and sustainability efforts.
  • sāsy ukthyaḥ: This can be interpreted as "the chant of prosperity." In real life, this could relate to the positive affirmations and intentions we set for ourselves, which can lead to personal growth and success.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of unity, support, and the interconnectedness of all beings. It highlights how collaboration and shared strength can lead to prosperity and stability. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various contexts, such as teamwork in professional settings, community support during crises, and the importance of mentorship in personal development. By recognizing the value of collective effort and the strength derived from it, individuals can foster a more harmonious and prosperous existence.
