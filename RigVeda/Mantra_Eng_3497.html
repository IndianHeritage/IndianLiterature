उत स्मास्य द्रवतस् तुरण्यतः पर्णं न वेर अनु वाति प्रगर्धिनः |श्येनस्येव ध्रजतो अङ्कसम परि दधिक्राव्णः सहोर्जा तरित्रतः ||
uta smāsya dravatas turaṇyataḥ parṇaṁ na ver anu vāti pragardhinaḥ | śyenasyeva dhrajato aṅkasam pari dadhikrāvṇaḥ sahorjā taritrataḥ ||
  • Translation: The mantra can be translated as: "From the swift movement of the waters, the leaves do not tremble; like the eagle that soars, the powerful one moves with strength and energy." This mantra reflects the themes of strength, resilience, and the natural order of movement in the universe.
  • Word Breakdown:
    • uta: and, also
    • smāsya: of the swift, quick
    • dravatas: of the flowing, moving (often referring to water)
    • turaṇyataḥ: from the swift movement
    • parṇaṁ: leaves
    • na: not
    • ver: tremble, shake
    • anu: after, following
    • vāti: blows, moves (as in wind)
    • pragardhinaḥ: powerful, strong
    • śyenasyeva: like the eagle
    • dhrajato: of the strong, mighty
    • aṅkasam: with strength, energy
    • pari: around, surrounding
    • dadhikrāvṇaḥ: the one who moves with strength
    • sahorjā: with energy, vigor
    • taritrataḥ: from the heights, elevated
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • This mantra can be related to the resilience of nature. For instance, during a storm, trees may sway but do not break, symbolizing strength in adversity.
    • In human life, this can be seen in athletes who train rigorously. They face challenges and setbacks but continue to push forward, embodying the spirit of the eagle soaring high.
    • In a corporate environment, a leader who navigates through turbulent times without losing composure can be likened to the powerful movement described in the mantra.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its emphasis on strength, resilience, and the ability to remain steadfast in the face of challenges. It teaches us that, like the eagle and the flowing waters, we can harness our inner strength and navigate through life's adversities with grace and power. This mantra is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal challenges to professional hurdles, reminding us to remain strong and composed, no matter the circumstances we face.
