एष स्य मद्यो रसोऽव चष्टे दिवः शिशुः |य इन्दुर वारम् आविशत् ||
eṣa sya madyo raso 'va caṣṭe divaḥ śiśuḥ | ya indur vāram āviśat ||
  • Mantra: "eṣa sya madyo raso 'va caṣṭe divaḥ śiśuḥ | ya indur vāram āviśat ||"
  • Translation: This mantra can be translated as: "This is the essence of the divine nectar that moves in the heavens like a child; it is the moon that has entered the realm of the strong."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • eṣa: This
    • sya: of this
    • madyo: nectar or essence
    • raso: taste or flavor
    • va: or
    • caṣṭe: moves or flows
    • divaḥ: of the heavens
    • śiśuḥ: child
    • ya: who
    • indur: moon
    • vāram: strong or powerful
    • āviśat: has entered
  • Meaning and Explanation:
    • This mantra speaks of a divine essence, likened to nectar, which is both sweet and nourishing.
    • The reference to "moving in the heavens like a child" suggests a sense of innocence and purity, indicating that this essence is both playful and vital.
    • The mention of the moon signifies calmness, beauty, and a source of light, which has entered the realm of strength, suggesting that this essence is not only gentle but also powerful.
    • In real life, this can be interpreted as the balance between gentleness and strength, where one can be nurturing yet assertive.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In parenting, a nurturing approach (the essence of nectar) combined with the ability to set boundaries (the strength of the moon) creates a balanced environment for children to thrive.
    • In leadership, a leader who embodies compassion (the child-like essence) while also being decisive and strong (the moon's strength) can inspire and motivate their team effectively.
    • In personal relationships, being gentle and understanding while also standing firm in one’s values can lead to healthier and more fulfilling connections.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of balancing gentleness with strength in various aspects of life.
    • It serves as a reminder that true power lies not just in force but in the ability to nurture and support while being resolute.
    • By embodying these qualities, individuals can create harmonious relationships and environments, fostering growth and positivity in their lives and the lives of others.
