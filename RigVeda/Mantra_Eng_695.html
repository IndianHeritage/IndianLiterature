अस्मा इद उ त्यं उपमं स्वर्षाम् भराम्य आङ्गूषम् आस्येन |मंहिष्ठम् अच्चोक्तिभिर मतीनां सुवृक्तिभिः सूरीं वावृद्धध्यै ||
asmā id u tyam upamaṁ svarṣām bharāmy āṅgūṣam āsyena | maṁhiṣṭham acchoktibhir matīnāṁ suvṛktibhiḥ sūriṁ vāvṛdhadhyai ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • asmā: from us
    • id: this
    • u: indeed
    • tyam: that
    • upamaṁ: comparison or analogy
    • svarṣām: of the shining or radiant
    • bharāmy: I carry or I bear
    • āṅgūṣam: with the thumb
    • āsyena: with the mouth
    • maṁhiṣṭham: the best or the most excellent
    • acchoktibhir: with good speech or utterances
    • matīnāṁ: of the wise or intelligent
    • suvṛktibhiḥ: with good sayings or expressions
    • sūriṁ: the hero or the brave one
    • vāvṛdhadhyai: may he be nourished or increased
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the power of words and the importance of wise speech.
    • It emphasizes the idea of carrying forth the best qualities and expressions, akin to how one might carry a precious object with care.
    • The reference to "the shining" suggests that wisdom and good speech illuminate our path, much like light dispels darkness.
    • In real life, this can be seen in how effective communication can lead to better relationships, understanding, and collaboration.
    • For example, in a workplace, a leader who communicates clearly and positively can inspire their team, leading to increased productivity and morale.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its reminder of the power of words and the impact they have on our lives and the lives of others.
    • It encourages us to be mindful of our speech, to express ourselves wisely, and to uplift those around us.
    • In various scenarios, such as conflict resolution, teaching, or even casual conversations, applying the principles of this mantra can lead to more harmonious interactions.
    • Ultimately, it teaches us that by nurturing our own wisdom and the wisdom of others through our words, we contribute to a more enlightened and supportive community.
