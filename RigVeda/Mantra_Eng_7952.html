भुवत त्रितस्य मार्ज्यो भुवद इन्द्राय मत्सरः |सं रूपैर अज्ञते हरिः ||
bhuvat tritasya marjyo bhuvad indrāya matsaraḥ | saṁ rūpair ajyate hariḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • bhuvat: Of the earth or worldly existence.
    • tritasya: Of the third (often interpreted as a reference to the three realms: earth, atmosphere, and heaven).
    • marjyo: The one who is to be worshipped or revered.
    • bhuvad: Related to the earth or worldly matters.
    • indrāya: To Indra, the king of gods, representing power and authority.
    • matsaraḥ: Envy or jealousy.
    • saṁ: Together or in union.
    • rūpair: With forms or appearances.
    • ajyate: Is invoked or called upon.
    • hariḥ: The divine or the one who removes obstacles.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks to the interconnectedness of the earthly realm and the divine.
    • It emphasizes the importance of worshipping the divine (Indra) while acknowledging the worldly existence.
    • The mention of "matsaraḥ" suggests a warning against envy, highlighting the need for unity and harmony.
    • The invocation of "hariḥ" signifies the call for divine assistance in overcoming obstacles and achieving peace.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace setting, this mantra can be applied to foster teamwork and collaboration, where individuals are encouraged to work together without jealousy.
    • In community gatherings, it serves as a reminder to celebrate collective achievements rather than harboring envy towards others' successes.
    • In personal relationships, it encourages individuals to support one another and seek divine guidance in resolving conflicts.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of recognizing the divine in our daily lives while navigating the complexities of human emotions like envy.
    • It serves as a reminder to seek unity and support from the divine, promoting a harmonious existence.
    • By applying its teachings, individuals can cultivate a more peaceful and cooperative environment in various aspects of life.
