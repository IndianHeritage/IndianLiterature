अभि स्ववृष्टिम् मादे अस्य युध्यतो राघवीर इव प्रवणेषु सस्रुर ऊतयः |इन्द्रो यद् वज्री धृषमाणो अन्धसा भिनद् वलस्य परिधीर्म् इव त्रितः ||
abhi svavṛṣṭim made asya yudhyato raghvīr iva pravaṇe sasrur ūtayaḥ | indro yad vajrī dhṛṣamāṇo andhasā bhinad valasya paridhīm̐r iva tritaḥ ||
  • Mantra: "abhi svavṛṣṭim made asya yudhyato raghvīr iva pravaṇe sasrur ūtayaḥ | indro yad vajrī dhṛṣamāṇo andhasā bhinad valasya paridhīm̐r iva tritaḥ ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • abhi: towards, for
    • svavṛṣṭim: one's own rain, abundance
    • made: in this, in this context
    • asya: of this
    • yudhyato: fighting, engaged in battle
    • raghvīr: like the brave Raghus, a lineage of warriors
    • iva: like, as if
    • pravaṇe: in the flow, in the course
    • sasrur: they have poured forth, they have released
    • ūtayaḥ: the rains, the blessings
    • indro: Indra, the king of gods
    • yad: when
    • vajrī: wielding the thunderbolt
    • dhṛṣamāṇo: being courageous, bold
    • andhasā: in darkness
    • bhinad: breaking, piercing
    • valasya: of the strong, powerful
    • paridhīm̐r: the covering, the veil
    • iva: like, as if
    • tritaḥ: the third, a reference to a deity or aspect
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra speaks of the abundance and blessings that come from the divine, particularly in the context of struggle and battle.
    • The phrase "abhi svavṛṣṭim" suggests that one should seek their own abundance, which can be interpreted as striving for personal growth and success.
    • The reference to "raghvīr" indicates the valor and courage of the Raghus, symbolizing the importance of bravery in facing challenges.
    • Indra, as the king of gods, represents strength and the ability to overcome obstacles, especially when he "breaks through darkness" to bring forth light and blessings.
    • This can be likened to a person facing difficulties in life, such as a student preparing for exams or an athlete training for a competition, where perseverance and courage lead to success.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its encouragement to seek one's own potential and to face challenges with courage and determination.
    • In real-life scenarios, it can be applied to various situations such as personal development, professional challenges, and overcoming adversities.
    • By embodying the qualities of bravery and resilience, individuals can navigate through life's battles and emerge victorious, much like the divine blessings that Indra bestows upon those who strive.
