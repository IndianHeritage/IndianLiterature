अपाद इन्द्रो अपाद अग्निर विश्वे देवाः अमत्सत |वरुण इद इह क्षयात तं आपो अभ्य अनूषत वत्सं संशिश्वरीर इव ||
apād indro apād agnir viśve devā amatsata | varuṇa id iha kṣayat tam āpo abhy anūṣata vatsaṁ saṁśiśvarīr iva ||
  • apād indro: This phrase refers to Indra, the king of the gods in Vedic mythology, who is often associated with strength and power. "Apād" can be interpreted as "without obstacles" or "unhindered," suggesting that Indra is capable of overcoming challenges.
  • apād agnir: Here, "Agnir" refers to Agni, the fire god, symbolizing energy, transformation, and purification. "Apād" again implies the absence of barriers, indicating that the divine energy of Agni is accessible and potent.
  • viśve devā amatsata: This translates to "all the gods have become immortal." "Viśve devā" refers to all deities, while "amatsata" suggests a state of eternal existence. This can be seen as a reminder of the divine presence in all aspects of life.
  • varuṇa id iha kṣayat: Varuṇa is the god of cosmic order and water. "Id iha" means "here and now," and "kṣayat" refers to the act of diminishing or reducing. This line emphasizes the importance of maintaining cosmic order in our current lives.
  • tam āpo abhy anūṣata: "Tam" refers to that divine essence, while "āpo" means "waters." "Abhy anūṣata" can be interpreted as "flowing towards." This suggests that divine qualities and wisdom flow like water, nourishing life and guiding us.
  • vatsaṁ saṁśiśvarīr iva: "Vatsaṁ" means "calf" or "young one," and "saṁśiśvarīr" refers to the nurturing aspect of divinity. This line implies that just as a mother nurtures her young, the divine provides care and sustenance to all beings.

Conclusion: This mantra encapsulates the essence of divine support and guidance in our lives. It highlights the importance of overcoming obstacles with the strength of Indra and the transformative power of Agni. The immortality of the gods serves as a reminder of the eternal nature of the divine, while Varuṇa's presence emphasizes the need for harmony and order in our lives. The flowing waters symbolize the continuous nourishment of wisdom and guidance, akin to a nurturing mother caring for her child. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire individuals to seek strength in adversity, embrace transformation, and recognize the divine support that surrounds them, ultimately leading to a more harmonious and fulfilling existence.
