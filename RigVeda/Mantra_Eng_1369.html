त्वं नो अस्य इन्द्र दुर्हणायाः पाहि वज्रिवो दुरिताद अभीक |प्र नो वाजान रथ्योऽ अश्वबुध्यान इषे यन्धि श्रवसे सूनृतायै ||
tvaṁ no asyā indra durhaṇāyāḥ pāhi vajrivo duritād abhīke | pra no vājān rathyo3 aśvabudhyān iṣe yandhi śravase sūnṛtāyai ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • tvaṁ: You
    • no: our
    • asyā: of this
    • indra: O Indra (the king of gods)
    • durhaṇāyāḥ: of the difficult (or challenging)
    • pāhi: protect
    • vajrivo: with your thunderbolt
    • duritād: from misfortunes
    • abhīke: may you remove
    • pra: may we
    • no: our
    • vājān: wealth
    • rathyo: of the chariots
    • aśvabudhyān: of the horses
    • iṣe: may we attain
    • yandhi: that which
    • śravase: is glorious
    • sūnṛtāyai: for the sake of truth
  • Overall Meaning: This mantra is a prayer to Indra, the god of rain and thunder, asking for protection from difficulties and misfortunes. It seeks blessings for wealth and success, particularly in endeavors that require strength and valor, symbolized by chariots and horses.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In times of personal or professional challenges, individuals often seek divine intervention or guidance, similar to how this mantra calls upon Indra for protection.
    • In competitive environments, such as sports or business, people may invoke the spirit of this mantra to gain strength and overcome obstacles, much like athletes preparing for a big game.
    • During significant life events, such as weddings or new ventures, families may recite prayers or mantras to seek blessings for prosperity and success, reflecting the essence of this invocation.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its universal appeal for protection and success in the face of adversity. It emphasizes the importance of seeking divine support in our endeavors, reminding us that while we strive for our goals, we are not alone in our struggles. This mantra can be applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal challenges to collective efforts in communities, reinforcing the belief in a higher power that aids us in our journey towards truth and prosperity.
