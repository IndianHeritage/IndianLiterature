सिन्धुर न क्षोढः शिमिवाम् ऋघायतो वृषेवा वध्रींर अभि वष्ट्य ओजसा |अग्नेर इव प्रसितिर नाह वर्तवे यं-यं युजं कृतुते ब्रह्मणस पतिः ||
sindhur na kṣodaḥ śimīvām̐ ṛghāyato vṛṣeva vadhrīm̐r abhi vaṣṭy ojasā | agner iva prasitir nāha vartave yaṁ-yaṁ yujaṁ kṛṇute brahmaṇas patiḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sindhur: ocean or river
    • na: not
    • kṣodaḥ: to be disturbed or agitated
    • śimīvām̐: like a deer
    • ṛghāyato: in the act of running
    • vṛṣeva: like a bull
    • vadhrīm̐r: strong or powerful
    • abhi: towards
    • vaṣṭy: to protect or guard
    • ojasā: with energy or strength
    • agner: of fire
    • iva: like
    • prasitir: the path or direction
    • nāha: not
    • vartave: to flow or move
    • yaṁ-yaṁ: whatever
    • yujaṁ: is connected or joined
    • kṛṇute: creates or establishes
    • brahmaṇas: of the divine or the ultimate reality
    • patiḥ: lord or master
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the stability and strength of the divine forces, comparing them to the ocean that remains undisturbed despite external disturbances.
    • The imagery of a deer running and a powerful bull signifies agility and strength, suggesting that one should embody both qualities in life.
    • The reference to fire indicates the transformative power of energy and the importance of direction in one’s actions.
    • It emphasizes that whatever is connected to the divine will flourish and be protected, highlighting the significance of spiritual alignment.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, maintaining composure like the ocean amidst challenges can lead to better decision-making and leadership.
    • In personal relationships, embodying the agility of a deer and the strength of a bull can help navigate conflicts effectively.
    • In spiritual practices, aligning oneself with divine energy can lead to personal growth and fulfillment.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of inner strength and stability in the face of life's challenges.
    • It encourages individuals to seek alignment with the divine, ensuring protection and guidance in their endeavors.
    • By embodying the qualities of agility and strength, one can navigate life's complexities with grace and resilience.
