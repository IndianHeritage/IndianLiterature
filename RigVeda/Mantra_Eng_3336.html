मन्त्र: का इमं दशभिर ममेन्द्रं क्रिणाति धेनुभिः |यदा वृत्राणि जङ्घनद अथैनम मे पुनर ददत ||
ka imaṁ daśabhir mamendraṁ krīṇāti dhenubhiḥ | yadā vṛtrāṇi jaṅghanad athainam me punar dadat ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ka: Who
    • imaṁ: this
    • daśabhir: with ten
    • mamendraṁ: my lord (Indra)
    • krīṇāti: purchases or acquires
    • dhenubhiḥ: with cows
    • yadā: when
    • vṛtrāṇi: the obstacles or enemies
    • jaṅghanad: are defeated
    • athainam: then him (Indra)
    • me: my
    • punar: again
    • dadat: gives
  • Contextual Meaning:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Indra, the king of gods in Vedic literature, who is often associated with rain, thunder, and strength.
    • The speaker is inquiring about who can acquire Indra with ten cows, symbolizing wealth and prosperity.
    • The mention of defeating obstacles (vṛtrāṇi) signifies overcoming challenges in life.
    • It reflects a desire for divine assistance in achieving success and prosperity.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be interpreted as seeking divine support to overcome competition (vṛtrāṇi) and achieve success (krīṇāti) through hard work and ethical practices.
    • In personal life, it can represent the struggle against personal challenges, such as health issues or financial difficulties, and the hope for divine intervention to restore balance and prosperity.
    • In agriculture, invoking Indra for rain and favorable weather conditions can be seen as a practical application of this mantra, as farmers often pray for good harvests.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking divine guidance and support in overcoming life's challenges.
    • It highlights the relationship between material wealth (represented by cows) and spiritual well-being, suggesting that true prosperity comes from a harmonious balance of both.
    • In modern life, this mantra can inspire individuals to seek help from higher powers or the universe when facing difficulties, reinforcing the belief that perseverance and faith can lead to success.
