उदु त्यद दर्षतं वपुर दिव एति प्रतिह्वरे |यद ईम आशुर वहति देव एतशो विश्वस्मै चक्षसे अरम् ||
ud u tyad darśataṁ vapur diva eti pratihvare | yad īm āśur vahati deva etaśo viśvasmai cakṣase aram ||
  • ud u tyad darśataṁ vapur diva eti pratihvare
    • ud u tyad: This phrase can be interpreted as "upward" or "higher." It signifies the aspiration to rise above the mundane and reach a higher state of consciousness or existence.
    • darśataṁ: This means "to see" or "to perceive." It emphasizes the importance of vision and understanding in our lives.
    • vapur: Refers to "form" or "body." It indicates the physical manifestation of existence.
    • diva eti pratihvare: This can be translated as "the form that goes towards the heavens." It suggests a journey or progression towards a divine or elevated state.
  • yad īm āśur vahati deva etaśo viśvasmai cakṣase aram
    • yad: This means "that" or "which," referring to a specific quality or action.
    • īm: This can be interpreted as "that which is desired" or "aspired to."
    • āśur: Refers to "the divine" or "the godly." It indicates a connection to higher powers or spiritual entities.
    • vahati: This means "carries" or "brings forth." It signifies the act of delivering or manifesting something.
    • deva: Refers to "god" or "deity," emphasizing the divine aspect of existence.
    • etaśo: This means "from this" or "from here," indicating a source or origin.
    • viśvasmai: Refers to "to all" or "universal," suggesting inclusivity and a broader perspective.
    • cakṣase: This means "to the eye" or "to vision," emphasizing perception and awareness.
    • aram: This can be interpreted as "the path" or "the way," indicating a journey or direction.

Conclusion:

The mantra speaks to the journey of elevating oneself towards a higher state of consciousness and understanding. It emphasizes the importance of vision, both literal and metaphorical, in perceiving the divine and the universal truths of existence. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios such as:

  • Personal Growth: Striving for self-improvement and higher understanding in one's life.
  • Spiritual Practices: Engaging in meditation or mindfulness to connect with the divine and gain deeper insights.
  • Community Engagement: Working towards the betterment of society by sharing knowledge and fostering inclusivity.

Ultimately, this mantra encourages individuals to seek higher truths and to carry those insights into their daily lives, promoting a more enlightened and compassionate existence.
