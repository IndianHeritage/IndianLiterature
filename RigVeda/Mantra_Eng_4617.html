आ जनाय द्रुह्वाणे पार्थिवानि दिव्यानी दीपयोऽन्तरिक्षा |तपा वृषन् विश्वतः शोचिषा तान ब्रह्मद्विषे शोचय क्षाम अपश्च ||
ā janāya druhvaṇe pārthivāni divyāni dīpayo 'ntarikṣā | tapā vṛṣan viśvataḥ śociṣā tān brahmadviṣe śocaya kṣām apaś ca ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā: from
    • janāya: for the people
    • druhvaṇe: who are hostile
    • pārthivāni: earthly
    • divyāni: divine
    • dīpayo: illuminate
    • 'ntarikṣā: the sky
    • tapā: through penance
    • vṛṣan: the strong
    • viśvataḥ: from all sides
    • śociṣā: with brightness
    • tān: those
    • brahmadviṣe: who are opposed to the divine
    • śocaya: make them shine
    • kṣām: the earth
    • apaś: and the unseen
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks to the need for illumination and clarity in the face of hostility and negativity.
    • The phrase "ā janāya druhvaṇe" suggests a call to protect and uplift the people who may be facing adversities or hostility.
    • By invoking "pārthivāni divyāni," it emphasizes the connection between earthly and divine elements, suggesting that divine support is essential for overcoming earthly challenges.
    • The term "tapā" indicates that through penance or disciplined effort, one can achieve strength and clarity.
    • "viśvataḥ śociṣā" implies that this illumination should come from all directions, highlighting the need for comprehensive understanding and awareness.
    • Finally, the mantra calls for the transformation of negativity ("brahmadviṣe") into positivity, urging the divine to help those who are struggling.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace setting, this mantra can be applied when teams face conflicts or hostility. By fostering a culture of understanding and support, teams can illuminate the path to collaboration.
    • In personal relationships, when misunderstandings arise, invoking the spirit of this mantra can encourage individuals to seek clarity and resolution through open communication and empathy.
    • In community settings, this mantra can inspire collective efforts to uplift those who are marginalized or facing challenges, promoting a sense of unity and shared purpose.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for illumination and support in the face of adversity.
    • It teaches us the importance of seeking divine guidance and strength through disciplined efforts, especially when confronted with negativity.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra encourages individuals and communities to work together to transform challenges into opportunities for growth and understanding.
