उच्छ्रयस्व वनस्पते वर्ष्मन् पृथिव्याऽधि |सुमिती मीयमानो वर्चो धा यज्ञवाहसे ||
uc chrayasva vanaspate varṣman pṛthivyā adhi | sumitī mīyamāno varco dhā yajñavāhase ||
  • uc chrayasva: This phrase can be translated as "rise up" or "ascend." It signifies the call for elevation, both in a physical and spiritual sense.
  • vanaspate: This term refers to "the lord of the forest" or "the tree." In a broader context, it symbolizes nature, growth, and the interconnectedness of life.
  • varṣman: This word means "with strength" or "power." It emphasizes the importance of vigor and resilience in our endeavors.
  • pṛthivyā adhi: This translates to "upon the earth." It indicates a grounding aspect, reminding us of our connection to the physical world and the importance of nurturing it.
  • sumitī: This term means "good thought" or "wise counsel." It highlights the value of wisdom and positive intentions in our actions.
  • mīyamāno: This word can be interpreted as "being united" or "coming together." It signifies the importance of collaboration and harmony among individuals.
  • varco: This translates to "radiance" or "brilliance." It represents the light of knowledge and enlightenment that guides us.
  • dhā: This word means "to hold" or "to support." It emphasizes the role of stability and strength in our pursuits.
  • yajñavāhase: This term refers to "the one who carries the sacrifice" or "the one who performs rituals." It signifies the importance of dedication and commitment to a higher purpose.

In conclusion, this mantra from the Rigveda encapsulates the essence of growth, unity, and the pursuit of wisdom. It calls upon the forces of nature to rise and support us as we strive for strength and enlightenment. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as:

  • In personal development, it encourages individuals to rise above challenges and seek wisdom in their decisions.
  • In environmental conservation, it serves as a reminder of our responsibility to nurture and protect the earth.
  • In community building, it emphasizes the importance of collaboration and collective efforts towards a common goal.

Overall, the significance of this mantra lies in its call for a harmonious existence, where individuals rise together with strength and wisdom, contributing to the greater good of society and the environment.
