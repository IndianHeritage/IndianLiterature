याभिर विष्पलां धनसाम अथर्व्यं सहस्रमील्ह आजाव अजन्वतम् |याभिर वशम अश्व्यम प्रेणिम आवतं ताभिर ऊषु ऊतिभिर अश्विनां गतम् ||
yābhir viśpalāṁ dhanasām atharvyaṁ sahasramīḻha ājāv ajinvatam | yābhir vaśam aśvyam preṇim āvataṁ tābhir ū ṣu ūtibhir aśvinā gatam ||
  • yābhir: This word translates to "by whom" or "through which." It indicates a means or method.
  • viśpalāṁ: This term refers to "wealth" or "prosperity." It signifies abundance and the resources one possesses.
  • dhanasām: This means "of the wealthy" or "of riches." It emphasizes the importance of wealth in achieving goals.
  • atharvyaṁ: This word can be interpreted as "the one who is knowledgeable" or "wise." It suggests the need for wisdom in managing wealth.
  • sahasramīḻha: This term means "thousandfold" or "multiplied." It indicates the desire for wealth to grow exponentially.
  • ājāv: This translates to "the one who is swift" or "quick." It implies the need for speed in achieving one's goals.
  • ajinvatam: This word means "the one who is protected" or "sheltered." It suggests the importance of security in wealth.
  • vaśam: This means "control" or "influence." It indicates the ability to manage one's resources effectively.
  • aśvyam: This term refers to "the horse" or "swift movement." It symbolizes progress and the journey towards success.
  • preṇim: This translates to "the one who inspires" or "motivator." It signifies the role of inspiration in achieving goals.
  • āvataṁ: This means "to protect" or "to shelter." It emphasizes the need for safeguarding one's wealth and resources.
  • ū: This word can be interpreted as "with" or "together." It suggests collaboration and unity in efforts.
  • ṣu: This translates to "good" or "beneficial." It indicates the positive outcomes of wise management of resources.
  • ūtibhir: This term means "by efforts" or "through actions." It emphasizes the importance of hard work in achieving success.
  • aśvinā: This refers to "the twin gods" or "the divine." It symbolizes divine support and guidance in one's endeavors.
  • gatam: This means "gone" or "achieved." It signifies the attainment of goals and the realization of aspirations.

Conclusion: This mantra emphasizes the significance of wealth, wisdom, and protection in achieving success. It highlights the importance of swift action, control over resources, and the role of inspiration and divine guidance in one's journey. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios such as financial planning, career development, and personal growth. By understanding and implementing these principles, individuals can work towards achieving their goals while ensuring their resources are well-managed and protected.
