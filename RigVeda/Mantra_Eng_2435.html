आवदंस्त्वं शकुने भद्रम् आवद तुष्णीं आसीनः सुमतिं चिकीद्धि नः |यद उत्पतन वदसि कर्करिर यथा बृहद् वदेम विदथे सुवीराः ||
āvadam̐s tvaṁ śakune bhadram ā vada tūṣṇīm āsīnaḥ sumatiṁ cikiddhi naḥ | yad utpatan vadasi karkarir yathā bṛhad vadema vidathe suvīrāḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • āvadam̐s: Speak or declare
    • tvaṁ: You
    • śakune: O bird (often symbolizing a messenger or divine being)
    • bhadram: Goodness or auspiciousness
    • ā vada: Speak forth
    • tūṣṇīm: In silence
    • āsīnaḥ: Sitting or being seated
    • sumatiṁ: Good thought or wisdom
    • cikiddhi: May you inspire or create
    • naḥ: For us
    • yad: Whatever
    • utpatan: You speak or arise
    • vadasi: You say
    • karkarir: Like a crow (often associated with communication)
    • yathā: As
    • bṛhad: Great or vast
    • vadema: We may speak
    • vidathe: In the assembly or gathering
    • suvīrāḥ: The brave or the strong
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a call for communication and the sharing of wisdom.
    • The speaker is addressing a divine messenger (symbolized by the bird) to convey auspiciousness and good thoughts.
    • It emphasizes the importance of speaking out in gatherings and the power of collective wisdom.
    • The reference to silence suggests that even in quiet moments, profound thoughts can arise.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate meeting, encouraging open communication can lead to innovative ideas and solutions.
    • In educational settings, fostering an environment where students feel comfortable sharing their thoughts can enhance learning.
    • In community gatherings, collective discussions can lead to better decision-making and unity.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of communication, wisdom, and collective thought in various aspects of life.
    • It teaches us to value the voices of others and to seek auspiciousness in our interactions.
    • In real-life scenarios, applying the principles of this mantra can lead to better relationships, enhanced creativity, and a more harmonious community.
