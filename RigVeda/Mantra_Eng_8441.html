अयम् मातवाँ चकुनो यथा हितोऽव्ये ससार पवमान उर्मिणा |तव क्रत्वा रोधसी अन्तरā कवे शुचिर धियā पवते सोम इन्द्र ते ||
ayam matavāñ chakuno yathā hito 'vye sasāra pavamāna ūrmiṇā | tava kratvā rodasī antarā kave śucir dhiyā pavate soma indra te ||
  • ayam matavāñ chakuno: This phrase can be translated as "this one who is wise like a bird." Here, the term "matavāñ" refers to wisdom or intelligence, while "chakuno" relates to a bird, symbolizing freedom and perspective. In real life, this can be likened to individuals who possess the ability to see beyond immediate circumstances, much like a bird soaring high above the ground, gaining a broader view of the world.
  • yathā hito 'vye sasāra pavamāna ūrmiṇā: This part means "as the wind moves through the waves." The word "pavamāna" refers to the wind, and "ūrmiṇā" means waves. This imagery evokes the idea of movement and change, suggesting that just as the wind influences the waves, our thoughts and actions can shape our environment. For example, a leader's vision can inspire a team to achieve great heights, much like the wind guiding the waves.
  • tava kratvā rodasī antarā: Translated, this means "by your power, O Indra, the two worlds are connected." "Kratu" refers to power or strength, and "rodasī" signifies the two realms or worlds. This highlights the interconnectedness of different aspects of existence. In practical terms, this can be seen in how our actions in one area of life (like work) can impact another (like family), emphasizing the importance of balance and harmony.
  • kave śucir dhiyā pavate soma indra te: This translates to "the wise one, pure in thought, moves with the essence of the divine." "Kave" refers to a sage or wise person, "śucir" means pure, and "soma" symbolizes the divine essence or nectar. This suggests that purity of thought and wisdom leads to divine inspiration. In everyday life, this can be reflected in how mindfulness and clarity in our intentions can lead to more meaningful and fulfilling experiences.

  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of wisdom, the interconnectedness of our actions, and the purity of thought. In real life, it serves as a reminder to cultivate a broader perspective, to recognize the impact of our actions on different aspects of our lives, and to strive for clarity and purity in our intentions. By embodying these principles, we can navigate life's challenges with grace and purpose, ultimately leading to a more harmonious existence.
