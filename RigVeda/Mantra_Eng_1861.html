सनā ता त इन्द्र नव्यā आगुḥ सहो नभो 'विरणāय पूर्वीḥ |भिनत पुरो न भिदो अदेवीर ननमो वधर अदेवस्य पीयोḥ ||
sanā tā ta indra navyā āguḥ saho nabho 'viraṇāya pūrvīḥ | bhinat puro na bhido adevīr nanamo vadhar adevasya pīyoḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sanā: Together, in harmony
    • tā: That, those
    • ta: You, your
    • indra: O Indra, the king of gods
    • navyā: New, fresh
    • āguḥ: They have called, invoked
    • saho: Together, united
    • nabho: Sky, atmosphere
    • 'viraṇāya: For the purpose of prosperity
    • pūrvīḥ: The earlier, previous
    • bhinat: Breaking, separating
    • puro: The front, the beginning
    • na: Not
    • bhido: To be broken, divided
    • adevīr: By the divine beings
    • nanamo: We bow, we salute
    • vadhar: To the one who protects
    • adevasya: Of the divine
    • pīyoḥ: Of the nectar, the essence
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the blessings of Indra, the king of gods, emphasizing unity and harmony among people.
    • The phrase "together in harmony" suggests the importance of collective effort and cooperation in achieving prosperity.
    • It highlights the need to break down barriers and divisions that may hinder progress, urging individuals to work together for a common goal.
    • The reference to "the divine" signifies the spiritual aspect of this unity, suggesting that divine support is essential for success.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace, teamwork is crucial for achieving project goals. When employees collaborate effectively, they can innovate and solve problems more efficiently.
    • In community service, individuals coming together to support a cause can create significant positive change, such as organizing a food drive or a clean-up event.
    • In family settings, unity and support among family members can lead to a nurturing environment, fostering growth and resilience.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of unity, cooperation, and divine support in achieving prosperity and success.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, including workplaces, communities, and families, where collective efforts lead to greater outcomes.
    • By invoking the spirit of togetherness and seeking divine blessings, individuals and groups can overcome challenges and thrive in their endeavors.
