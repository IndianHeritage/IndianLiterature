कुत्रा चिद यस्य समृतौ रण्वा नरो नृषदने |अर्हन्तश चिद यं इन्दते संजनयन्ति जन्तवः ||
kutrā cid yasya samṛtau raṇvā naro nṛṣadane | arhantaś cid yam indhate saṁjanayanti jantavaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • kutrā cid: somewhere, anywhere
    • yasya: of whom
    • samṛtau: in prosperity, in wealth
    • raṇvā: a warrior, a man of valor
    • naro: man
    • nṛṣadane: in the battlefield
    • arhantaś: worthy, deserving
    • cid: indeed
    • yam: whom
    • indhate: they praise, they honor
    • saṁjanayanti: they inspire, they awaken
    • jantavaḥ: living beings, creatures
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the recognition and honor given to a warrior or a man of valor who is prosperous and brave.
    • It suggests that wherever such a person exists, they are praised and respected by others.
    • The term "saṁjanayanti jantavaḥ" implies that the presence of such a person inspires and awakens courage in others.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern society, leaders in various fields, such as sports, politics, or social activism, can be seen as "warriors" who inspire others through their actions and achievements.
    • For instance, a sports figure like Muhammad Ali, who fought not only in the boxing ring but also for civil rights, embodies the qualities of a warrior who inspires others to stand up for their beliefs.
    • Similarly, in the context of social movements, figures like Malala Yousafzai represent individuals who, through their courage and determination, inspire others to pursue education and equality.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its emphasis on the qualities of bravery, prosperity, and the ability to inspire others.
    • It highlights the importance of recognizing and honoring those who contribute positively to society, encouraging a culture of respect and admiration for valorous deeds.
    • In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as leadership, mentorship, and community service, where individuals are encouraged to uplift and inspire others through their actions and character.
