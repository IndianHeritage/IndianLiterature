अरित्रं वां दिवस पृथु तीर्थे सिंधूनां रथः |धिया युयुज्र इन्दवः ||
aritraṁ vāṁ divas pṛthu tīrthe sindhūnāṁ rathaḥ | dhiyā yuyujra indavaḥ ||
  • Mantra: "aritraṁ vāṁ divas pṛthu tīrthe sindhūnāṁ rathaḥ | dhiyā yuyujra indavaḥ ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • aritraṁ: the chariot or vehicle
    • vāṁ: of you (plural or addressing a group)
    • divas: of the heavens or sky
    • pṛthu: vast or wide
    • tīrthe: in the sacred places or waters
    • sindhūnāṁ: of the rivers
    • rathaḥ: the chariot
    • dhiyā: with intellect or understanding
    • yuyujra: may you unite or connect
    • indavaḥ: the moon or the divine
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of a chariot that is vast and is associated with the heavens and sacred waters.
    • The reference to "rivers" suggests a connection to life, nourishment, and the flow of energy.
    • The invocation of "intellect" indicates a desire for wisdom and understanding in our pursuits.
    • The term "indavaḥ" refers to the moon, symbolizing calmness, reflection, and the divine feminine energy.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a practical sense, this mantra can be applied in the context of teamwork and collaboration. Just as a chariot requires a team of horses to move forward, individuals must work together harmoniously to achieve common goals.
    • In personal development, the emphasis on intellect and understanding can be seen in the pursuit of education and self-improvement. For instance, engaging in discussions, reading, and reflecting on experiences can help individuals grow.
    • The connection to rivers can be likened to the flow of life and the importance of adaptability. Just as rivers carve their paths through landscapes, individuals must navigate challenges and changes in their lives.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of unity, wisdom, and adaptability in our lives.
    • It encourages individuals to seek knowledge and work collaboratively, much like the harmonious movement of a chariot.
    • In a broader sense, it reminds us to connect with the natural flow of life, embracing change and seeking understanding in our journeys.
