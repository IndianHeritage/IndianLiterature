यद इन्द्र पूर्वो अपरा य शिक्षन्न अयज ज्यायान कनीयसो देश्णम् |अमृत इति पर्य आसीत दूरम् आ चित्र चित्र्यम् भरā रयिं नः ||
yad indra pūrvo aparāya śikṣann ayaj jyāyān kanīyaso deṣṇam | amṛta it pary āsīta dūram ā citra citryam bharā rayiṁ naḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yad: that which
    • indra: Indra, the king of gods
    • pūrvo: ancient or previous
    • aparāya: not to be surpassed
    • śikṣann: teaching or instructing
    • ayaj: worship or sacrifice
    • jyāyān: greater or superior
    • kanīyaso: lesser or inferior
    • deṣṇam: vision or sight
    • amṛta: immortal or eternal
    • it: thus
    • pary: around or about
    • āsīta: may it be
    • dūram: far or distant
    • ā: from
    • citra: colorful or diverse
    • citryam: variety or richness
    • bharā: may it bring
    • rayiṁ: wealth or prosperity
    • naḥ: to us
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the blessings of Indra, the god of rain and thunder, who is often associated with strength and power.
    • It emphasizes the importance of ancient wisdom and teachings that guide us in our worship and sacrifices.
    • The mantra suggests that through these teachings, we can attain a greater vision and understanding, surpassing the limitations of the lesser beings.
    • It also speaks of the eternal nature of existence, encouraging us to seek out the richness and diversity of life.
    • Ultimately, it is a prayer for prosperity and abundance, asking for wealth to come to us from all directions.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, this mantra can be applied to the importance of mentorship and learning from experienced leaders (Indra) to achieve greater success.
    • In personal development, it encourages individuals to seek knowledge from ancient philosophies and teachings that can guide them in their life choices.
    • In community building, it highlights the need for diverse perspectives and collaboration to create a prosperous environment for all.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for wisdom, guidance, and the pursuit of prosperity through collective efforts and ancient teachings.
    • It is applicable in various aspects of life, including personal growth, professional success, and community development.
    • By embracing the teachings of the past and seeking the blessings of higher powers, we can navigate our paths towards a fulfilling and abundant life.
