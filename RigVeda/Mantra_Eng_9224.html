समानमुत्यं पुरुहूतमुक्त्यं रथं त्रिचक्रं सवना गनिग्मतम् |परिज्मानं विदथ्यं सुवृक्तिभिर वयं व्युष्टा उषसो हवामहे ||
samānam u tyam puruhūtam ukthya1ṁ rathaṁ tricakraṁ savanā ganigmatam | parijmānaṁ vidathyaṁ suvṛktibhir vayaṁ vyuṣṭā uṣaso havāmahe ||
  • Mantra: "samānam u tyam puruhūtam ukthyaṁ rathaṁ tricakraṁ savanā ganigmatam | parijmānaṁ vidathyaṁ suvṛktibhir vayaṁ vyuṣṭā uṣaso havāmahe ||"
  • Word-by-Word Explanation:
    • samānam: Similar or equal
    • u: Indeed
    • tyam: That
    • puruhūtam: Invoked by many
    • ukthyaṁ: Through hymns or praises
    • rathaṁ: Chariot
    • tricakraṁ: Three-wheeled
    • savanā: Offering or sacrifice
    • ganigmatam: Gathered or assembled
    • parijmānaṁ: Encompassing or surrounding
    • vidathyaṁ: Well-structured or well-formed
    • suvṛktibhir: With good words or expressions
    • vayaṁ: We
    • vyuṣṭā: Having risen or emerged
    • uṣaso: Of the dawn
    • havāmahe: We invoke or call upon
  • Meaning:
    • This mantra speaks of unity and the collective strength of individuals coming together to invoke divine energies.
    • The "chariot" symbolizes the journey of life, which is supported by the "three wheels" representing stability, progress, and direction.
    • The act of invoking through hymns signifies the importance of communication and expression in connecting with the divine.
    • The "dawn" represents new beginnings, hope, and enlightenment, suggesting that through collective effort and good intentions, we can achieve a brighter future.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community project, individuals come together to build a playground. Each person's contribution, whether it be time, resources, or skills, symbolizes the unity and collective strength emphasized in the mantra.
    • In a corporate setting, a team working collaboratively on a project can be seen as a "chariot" moving towards a common goal, with each member's unique skills acting as the "wheels" that drive success.
    • During a cultural festival, people from diverse backgrounds come together to celebrate, showcasing the power of unity and shared purpose, akin to the invocation of divine energies in the mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of unity, collaboration, and the power of collective intention in achieving goals.
    • It serves as a reminder that through harmonious efforts and good communication, we can navigate the challenges of life and emerge into new beginnings.
    • In real-life scenarios, whether in personal relationships, community efforts, or professional environments, the principles embodied in this mantra can guide us towards success and fulfillment.
