सहस्व मण्यो अभिमातिम अस्मे रुजन मृणन प्रमृणन प्रेहि शत्रून् |उग्रं ते पाजो नन्व् ā रुरुध्रे वशी वशं नयसा एकज त्वम् ||
sahasva manyo abhimātim asme rujan mṛṇan pramṛṇan prehi śatrūn | ugraṁ te pājo nanv ā rurudhre vaśī vaśaṁ nayasa ekaja tvam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sahasva: Be strong or powerful
    • manyo: With great thought or consideration
    • abhimātim: With a favorable intention or desire
    • asme: To us
    • rujan: Pain or suffering
    • mṛṇan: To be freed or liberated
    • pramṛṇan: To be protected or saved
    • prehi: Send or direct
    • śatrūn: Enemies or adversaries
    • ugraṁ: Fierce or powerful
    • te: Your
    • pājo: Strength or might
    • nanv: Indeed
    • ā: To
    • rurudhre: To be invoked or called upon
    • vaśī: One who is in control or has mastery
    • vaśaṁ: Under control or in command
    • nayasa: Lead or guide
    • ekaja: The one who is unique or singular
    • tvam: You
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra calls upon a powerful force or deity to grant strength and protection.
    • It emphasizes the importance of having a strong intention and favorable desires in overcoming challenges.
    • The plea for liberation from pain and suffering reflects a universal human experience, where individuals seek relief from adversities.
    • By invoking the strength of a higher power, the mantra suggests that one can gain control over their circumstances and adversaries.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a challenging work environment, an employee may recite this mantra to seek strength and clarity in decision-making, aiming to overcome obstacles and adversaries.
    • A student facing academic pressure might use this mantra to focus their thoughts and intentions, seeking liberation from stress and the ability to perform well.
    • During personal conflicts, individuals may invoke this mantra to find the strength to resolve issues peacefully and assertively.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for inner strength, clarity of thought, and the ability to overcome challenges.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal struggles to professional challenges, reminding individuals of the power of intention and the support of higher forces.
    • By embracing the essence of this mantra, one can cultivate resilience and mastery over their circumstances, leading to a more empowered and fulfilling life.
