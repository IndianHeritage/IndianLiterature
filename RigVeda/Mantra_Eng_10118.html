स इद अग्निः कण्वतमः कण्वसखार्यः परस्यान्तरस्य तरुषः | अग्निः पातु गृणतो अग्निः सूरीन अग्निर ददातु तेषाम् अवो नः ||
sa id agniḥ kaṇvatamaḥ kaṇvasakhāryaḥ parasyāntarasya taruṣaḥ | agniḥ pātu gṛṇato agniḥ sūrīn agnir dadātu teṣām avo naḥ ||
  • sa id agniḥ: This phrase translates to "this is indeed Agni." Agni is the Vedic fire god, symbolizing energy, transformation, and the divine spark within all beings.
  • kaṇvatamaḥ: This means "the most powerful" or "the strongest." It emphasizes Agni's supreme strength and capability to transform and purify.
  • kaṇvasakhāryaḥ: This can be interpreted as "the one who is associated with the wise." It suggests that Agni is not just a physical fire but also a source of wisdom and knowledge.
  • parasyāntarasya taruṣaḥ: This phrase means "of the tree that is beyond." It symbolizes the interconnectedness of all life and the idea that Agni resides in all things, even those that seem distant or separate.
  • agniḥ pātu: This translates to "may Agni protect." It is a plea for the divine fire to safeguard and guide us in our endeavors.
  • gṛṇato agniḥ: This means "Agni who is praised." It highlights the importance of honoring and acknowledging the divine forces in our lives.
  • sūrīn: This word refers to "the noble ones" or "the wise." It indicates that Agni is a source of inspiration for those who seek knowledge and enlightenment.
  • agnir dadātu: This translates to "may Agni give." It is a request for blessings and gifts from the divine fire, emphasizing the importance of receiving guidance and support.
  • teṣām avo naḥ: This means "may he grant us." It is a collective plea for the blessings of Agni to be bestowed upon all.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of Agni as a powerful, transformative force that embodies wisdom and protection. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be invoked during rituals, personal meditations, or moments of seeking guidance and strength. It serves as a reminder of the divine presence in our lives, encouraging us to honor the wisdom within ourselves and the interconnectedness of all beings. By acknowledging Agni, we invite protection, inspiration, and blessings into our lives, fostering a deeper connection with the universe and our own inner fire.
