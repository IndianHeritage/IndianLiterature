प्रेन्द्राग्निभ्यां सुवचस्याम इयर्मि सिन्धाव इव प्रेयरयं नावम् अर्कैः |अयां इव परि चरन्ति देवाः ये अस्मभ्यं धनदां उdbhिदश्च ||
prendrāgnibhyāṁ suvacasyām iyarmi sindhāv iva prerayaṁ nāvam arkaiḥ | ayā iva pari caranti devā ye asmabhyaṁ dhanadā udbhidaś ca ||
  • Mantra: "prendrāgnibhyāṁ suvacasyām iyarmi sindhāv iva prerayaṁ nāvam arkaiḥ | ayā iva pari caranti devā ye asmabhyaṁ dhanadā udbhidaś ca ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • prendrāgnibhyāṁ: To the powerful Agni (fire) and the divine forces.
    • suvacasyām: In the realm of good speech or auspiciousness.
    • iyarmi: I invoke or I call upon.
    • sindhāv: Like the rivers.
    • iva: As if.
    • prerayaṁ: To guide or to lead.
    • nāvam: The boat or vessel.
    • arkaiḥ: With the rays (of light or blessings).
    • ayā: Like that or in this manner.
    • pari caranti: They move around or travel.
    • devā: The gods or divine beings.
    • ye: Who.
    • asmabhyaṁ: For us.
    • dhanadā: Bestowers of wealth or prosperity.
    • udbhidaś: Those who are born or arise.
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra invokes the divine forces, particularly Agni, the fire god, who symbolizes energy, transformation, and purification.
    • The phrase "suvacasyām" emphasizes the importance of good communication and auspiciousness in our endeavors.
    • By comparing the guidance of these divine forces to rivers, the mantra suggests that just as rivers flow and nourish the land, divine blessings can guide us through life's challenges.
    • The mention of "nāvam arkaiḥ" symbolizes the vessel of life that is supported by the rays of divine blessings, indicating that with the right guidance, we can navigate through difficulties.
    • The concluding part of the mantra highlights the role of the gods in bestowing wealth and prosperity upon us, suggesting that divine support is essential for success and abundance.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, invoking this mantra can be seen as a call for ethical practices and good communication, which are essential for success.
    • In personal life, it can be a reminder to seek guidance from wise mentors or spiritual leaders, akin to the rivers that guide us.
    • During challenging times, individuals may recite this mantra to seek divine intervention and support, much like a sailor relies on the stars for navigation.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the importance of divine guidance, ethical communication, and the pursuit of prosperity.
    • Its significance lies in the belief that with the right intentions and divine support, we can navigate the complexities of life successfully.
    • In real-life scenarios, it encourages individuals to seek wisdom, maintain good relationships, and remain open to the blessings that come from higher powers.
