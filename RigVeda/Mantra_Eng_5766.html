एषा नेत्री राधसः सूनृतानाम उषा उचन्ती रिभ्यते वसिष्ठैः |दीर्घश्रुतं रयिम अस्मे दधाना यूम पात स्वस्तिभिः सदा नः ||
eṣā netrī rādhasaḥ sūnṛtānām uṣā ucchantī ribhyate vasiṣṭhaiḥ | dīrghaśrutaṁ rayim asme dadhānā yūyam pāta svastibhiḥ sadā naḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • eṣā: this
    • netrī: eye or vision
    • rādhasaḥ: of the radiant or bright
    • sūnṛtānām: of those who speak the truth
    • uṣā: dawn or light
    • ucchantī: rising or emerging
    • ribhyate: is invoked or called upon
    • vasiṣṭhaiḥ: by the best or the most excellent
    • dīrghaśrutaṁ: long-lasting or enduring
    • rayim: wealth or prosperity
    • asme: for us
    • dadhānā: holding or bestowing
    • yūyam: you (plural)
    • pāta: protect or preserve
    • svastibhiḥ: with auspiciousness or well-being
    • sadā: always
    • naḥ: for us
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the dawn, symbolizing new beginnings and enlightenment.
    • It emphasizes the importance of truthfulness and the pursuit of knowledge.
    • The mention of "wealth" signifies not just material prosperity but also spiritual and intellectual richness.
    • The call for protection with auspiciousness highlights the need for safety and well-being in our endeavors.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, this mantra can be applied when starting a new project, invoking clarity and truthfulness among team members.
    • In personal life, it can be a reminder to seek knowledge and wisdom, ensuring that our actions are aligned with truth.
    • During challenging times, reciting this mantra can serve as a source of strength, encouraging individuals to remain optimistic and seek auspicious outcomes.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for enlightenment, truth, and protection.
    • It serves as a reminder to embrace new beginnings with integrity and to seek prosperity in all aspects of life.
    • In real-life scenarios, it encourages individuals to foster a positive environment, whether in personal relationships or professional endeavors, ensuring that actions are guided by truth and auspiciousness.
