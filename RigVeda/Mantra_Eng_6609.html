जुहुराणां चिद अश्विनां मन्येथां वृषण्वसू |युवं हि रुद्राः पर्वतः अति द्विषः ||
juhurāṇā cid aśvinā manyethāṁ vṛṣaṇvasū | yuvaṁ hi rudrā parṣatho ati dviṣaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • juhurāṇā: One who is invoked or called upon.
    • cid: Indeed or surely.
    • aśvinā: Referring to the Aśvins, the twin deities associated with health and medicine.
    • manyethāṁ: May they think or consider.
    • vṛṣaṇvasū: The one who is powerful and possesses wealth or resources.
    • yuvaṁ: Young or youthful.
    • hi: Indeed or surely.
    • rudrā: Referring to Rudra, a form of Shiva, associated with storms and the hunt.
    • parṣatho: In the assembly or gathering.
    • ati: Beyond or exceedingly.
    • dviṣaḥ: Enemies or those who harbor ill will.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the Aśvins, asking them to consider the needs and well-being of the speaker.
    • It emphasizes the importance of youthfulness and vitality, suggesting that these qualities are essential for overcoming challenges.
    • The mention of Rudra indicates a connection to strength and protection, especially in the face of adversaries.
    • The mantra can be seen as a plea for divine assistance in navigating life's difficulties, particularly against those who may wish harm.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a medical context, one might invoke the Aśvins for healing and health, especially during times of illness.
    • In personal challenges, such as facing competition in the workplace, individuals may seek the strength of Rudra to overcome obstacles.
    • In community gatherings, this mantra can serve as a reminder to support one another against common adversaries, fostering unity and resilience.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of invoking divine powers for support and guidance in challenging times.
    • It teaches the importance of youthfulness, vitality, and community in overcoming adversities.
    • In real life, it encourages individuals to seek help from higher powers while also fostering a sense of unity and strength among peers.
