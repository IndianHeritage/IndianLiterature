प्र ये शुम्भन्ते जनयो न सप्तयो यामन रुद्रस्य सूनवः सुदंससः |रोदसी हि मरुतश चक्रिरे वृधे मदन्ति वीराः विदथेṣu घृष्वयः ||
pra ye śumbhante janayo na saptayo yāman rudrasya sūnavaḥ sudaṁsasaḥ | rodasī hi marutaś cakrire vṛdhe madanti vīrā vidatheṣu ghṛṣvayaḥ ||
  • pra ye śumbhante: This phrase can be interpreted as "those who come together" or "those who unite." It emphasizes the importance of unity and collaboration among individuals or groups.
  • janayo na saptayo: This translates to "the people are not seven." The number seven often symbolizes completeness or wholeness in various cultures. Here, it may suggest that the community is more than just a complete set; it is dynamic and evolving.
  • yāman rudrasya sūnavaḥ sudaṁsasaḥ: This part refers to "the sons of Rudra, the auspicious ones." Rudra is a deity associated with storms and the hunt, representing both destruction and regeneration. The phrase highlights the connection between divine forces and human endeavors.
  • rodasī hi marutaś: This means "the heavens and the winds." It signifies the natural elements that influence life on Earth, reminding us of the interconnectedness of nature and humanity.
  • cakrire vṛdhe: This translates to "they have created abundance." It suggests that through unity and divine blessings, prosperity and growth can be achieved.
  • madanti vīrā: This means "the brave ones rejoice." It emphasizes the joy and fulfillment that comes from courage and strength in facing challenges.
  • vidatheṣu ghṛṣvayaḥ: This phrase can be interpreted as "in the gatherings of the wise." It highlights the importance of wisdom and knowledge in communal settings, where decisions are made and futures are shaped.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda encapsulates the essence of unity, divine connection, and the importance of wisdom in community life. In real-life scenarios, it can be applied to various contexts such as teamwork in organizations, community building, and collaborative efforts in social movements. The significance lies in recognizing that through collective strength and wisdom, individuals can overcome challenges and create a prosperous future. It serves as a reminder that our actions, when aligned with higher principles and in harmony with nature, can lead to abundance and joy for all.
