आ नो गव्येभिर अश्व्यैः सहस्रैर उप गच्छताम् |अन्ति षड् भूत्वाम् अवः ||
ā no gavyebhir aśvyaiḥ sahasrair upa gacchatam | anti ṣad bhūtu vām avaḥ ||
  • Mantra: "ā no gavyebhir aśvyaiḥ sahasrair upa gacchatam | anti ṣad bhūtu vām avaḥ ||"
  • Translation: "May we, with the help of countless cows and horses, reach the highest goal; may the six elements of existence come to us."

  • Word-by-Word Explanation:
    • ā: A preposition meaning "towards" or "to."
    • no: A pronoun meaning "us" or "our."
    • gavyebhir: Derived from "gavya," meaning "cows," indicating abundance and prosperity.
    • aśvyaiḥ: From "aśva," meaning "horses," symbolizing strength and power.
    • sahasrair: Meaning "thousands," suggesting a large quantity or multitude.
    • upa: Meaning "near" or "approaching," indicating a journey or progress.
    • gacchatam: A verb meaning "to go" or "to reach," implying the attainment of a goal.
    • anti: Meaning "may come" or "may arrive," expressing a wish or desire.
    • ṣad: Meaning "six," referring to the six elements of existence.
    • bhūtu: Meaning "elements" or "beings," indicating the fundamental aspects of life.
    • vām: Meaning "to you" or "to us," indicating a collective aspiration.
    • avaḥ: A verb meaning "to bring" or "to lead," suggesting an invitation or call.

  • Real-Life Examples:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of community and collaboration. For instance, in a business setting, a team working together with diverse skills (symbolized by cows and horses) can achieve greater success than individuals working in isolation.
    • In agriculture, the abundance of resources (cows for dairy, horses for labor) is crucial for prosperity. Farmers often rely on livestock to enhance productivity and ensure sustainability.
    • In personal development, the "six elements" can represent various aspects of life such as physical health, mental well-being, emotional stability, social connections, spiritual growth, and financial security. Striving for balance among these elements leads to a fulfilling life.

  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of collective effort and the pursuit of holistic well-being. It encourages individuals to seek abundance and strength through collaboration and to aspire for a balanced existence.
    • In real-life scenarios, whether in personal relationships, professional environments, or community initiatives, the principles embodied in this mantra can guide individuals towards achieving their goals while fostering a sense of unity and purpose.
