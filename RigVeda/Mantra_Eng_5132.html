मर्माणि ते वर्मणा छादयामि सोमस त्वा राजामृतेनानु वस्ताम् |उरोर वरियो वरुणस ते कृणोतु जयन्तं त्वानु देवाः मदन्तु ||
marmāṇi te varmaṇā chādayāmi somas tvā rājāmṛtenānu vastām | uror varīyo varuṇas te kṛṇotu jayantaṁ tvānu devā madantu ||
  • marmāṇi te varmaṇā chādayāmi: This phrase translates to "I cover your vital points with protection." In a real-life context, this can be understood as a metaphor for safeguarding one's vulnerabilities. For example, in a corporate environment, a manager might protect their team's weaknesses by providing training and resources, ensuring that they can perform effectively without exposing their shortcomings.
  • somas tvā rājāmṛtenānu vastām: This means "May you, O Soma, dwell with the king of immortality." Soma is often associated with the moon and is considered a source of nourishment and immortality. In practical terms, this can be related to the idea of seeking wisdom and enlightenment, akin to how individuals pursue knowledge and personal growth to achieve a lasting impact in their lives.
  • uror varīyo varuṇas te kṛṇotu: This translates to "May Varuna, the lord of the waters, grant you the best of blessings." Varuna represents the cosmic order and moral authority. In everyday life, this can be seen in the way people seek guidance from ethical principles or mentors to navigate challenges, ensuring that their actions align with a greater good.
  • jayantaṁ tvānu devā madantu: This means "May the gods inspire you to victory." This can be interpreted as a call for divine support in achieving success. In real life, this resonates with the idea of teamwork and collaboration, where individuals come together, drawing on each other's strengths to overcome obstacles and achieve common goals.

  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of protection, wisdom, ethical guidance, and divine inspiration in achieving success and well-being. In real-life scenarios, it serves as a reminder to safeguard our vulnerabilities, seek knowledge, adhere to moral principles, and collaborate with others. By embodying these values, individuals can navigate life's challenges more effectively and strive for a fulfilling existence.
