अधā गावा उपमातिं कनायā अनु श्वान्तस्य कस्य चित परेयुः |श्रुधि त्वं सुद्रविणो नस् त्वं याल् आश्वघ्नस्य वावृधे सूनृताभिः ||
adhā gāva upamātiṁ kanāyā anu śvāntasya kasya cit pareyuḥ | śrudhi tvaṁ sudraviṇo nas tvaṁ yāḻ āśvaghnasya vāvṛdhe sūnṛtābhiḥ ||
  • adhā gāva upamātiṁ kanāyā anu śvāntasya kasya cit pareyuḥ
    • adhā - down or below
    • gāva - cows, which symbolize wealth and nourishment
    • upamātiṁ - the measure or the way of understanding
    • kanāyā - in the context of the divine or the sacred
    • anu - following or in accordance with
    • śvāntasya - of the one who is peaceful or tranquil
    • kasya cit - of anyone or of any person
    • pareyuḥ - to go beyond or to transcend
  • śrudhi tvaṁ sudraviṇo nas tvaṁ yāḻ āśvaghnasya vāvṛdhe sūnṛtābhiḥ
    • śrudhi - listen or pay attention
    • tvaṁ - you
    • sudraviṇo - of the one who possesses good wealth or resources
    • nas - to us
    • yāḻ - the one who is swift or quick
    • āśvaghnasya - of the one who is a slayer of horses, symbolizing strength and power
    • vāvṛdhe - may you grow or increase
    • sūnṛtābhiḥ - with good truths or righteous words

This mantra speaks to the themes of wealth, tranquility, and the pursuit of truth. It emphasizes the importance of understanding the divine measure of wealth and nourishment, represented by cows, and the need to transcend beyond the ordinary through peaceful means. The call to listen and pay attention to the divine guidance is a reminder of the importance of being receptive to wisdom and truth.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios:

  • In personal development, it encourages individuals to seek inner peace and tranquility as a foundation for growth and success.
  • In business, it highlights the importance of ethical practices and the pursuit of wealth that is aligned with truth and righteousness.
  • In community building, it serves as a reminder to listen to the needs of others and to work towards collective well-being.

Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its holistic approach to life, urging individuals to cultivate peace, seek ethical wealth, and embrace truth. It is applicable in various aspects of life, from personal growth to community engagement, reminding us that true prosperity is rooted in righteousness and understanding.
