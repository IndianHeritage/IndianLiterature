ते नूनं नोऽयम् ऊतये वरुणो मित्रो आर्यमा |नयिष्ठा उ नो नेषाणि परषिष्ठा उ नः परषाण्यति द्विषः ||
te nūnaṁ no 'yam ūtaye varuṇo mitro aryamā | nayiṣṭhā u no neṣaṇi parṣiṣṭhā u naḥ parṣaṇy ati dviṣaḥ ||
  • Mantra: "te nūnaṁ no 'yam ūtaye varuṇo mitro aryamā | nayiṣṭhā u no neṣaṇi parṣiṣṭhā u naḥ parṣaṇy ati dviṣaḥ ||"
  • Translation: "Indeed, this Varuna, the friend Aryama, is not to be neglected by us; may he lead us on the right path, and may we not be harmed by those who wish us ill."

  • Word Breakdown:
    • te: "you" or "to you"
    • nūnaṁ: "indeed" or "certainly"
    • no: "us" or "our"
    • 'yam: "this"
    • ūtaye: "for protection" or "for guidance"
    • varuṇo: "Varuna," the deity associated with cosmic order and water
    • mitro: "friend," indicating a benevolent force
    • aryamā: "Aryama," another deity representing friendship and protection
    • nayiṣṭhā: "may lead" or "may guide"
    • u: "and"
    • neṣaṇi: "not be harmed" or "not be led astray"
    • parṣiṣṭhā: "those who wish us ill"
    • naḥ: "us" or "to us"
    • parṣaṇy: "by those who wish harm"
    • ati: "beyond" or "exceeding"
    • dviṣaḥ: "enemies" or "those who harbor ill will"

  • Contextual Explanation:
    • This mantra invokes the deities Varuna and Aryama, seeking their guidance and protection.
    • Varuna is often associated with the moral and cosmic order, representing the laws of nature and the universe.
    • Aryama symbolizes friendship and support, emphasizing the importance of community and relationships.
    • The plea for guidance reflects a universal human desire for direction in life, especially in challenging times.

  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In times of uncertainty, such as during a job search or a personal crisis, individuals often seek guidance from mentors or spiritual leaders, akin to invoking Varuna and Aryama for support.
    • Communities come together in times of need, demonstrating the essence of friendship and support, much like the invocation of Aryama.
    • In conflict situations, the desire to avoid harm from adversaries resonates with the mantra's plea for protection against those who wish ill.

  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of seeking divine guidance and protection in our lives.
    • It highlights the importance of friendship, community, and moral integrity in navigating life's challenges.
    • In a modern context, it serves as a reminder to seek support from others and to remain steadfast in the face of adversity, fostering a sense of unity and resilience.
