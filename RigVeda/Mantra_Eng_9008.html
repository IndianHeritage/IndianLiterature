अकर्मा दस्युर अभि नो अमन्तुर अन्यव्रतो अमानुषः |त्वं तस्यामित्रहन वधार दासस्य दम्भय ||
akarmā dasyur abhi no amantur anyavrato amānuṣaḥ | tvaṁ tasyāmitrahan vadhar dāsasya dambhaya ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • akarmā: without action or inaction
    • dasyur: the one who is a thief or a rogue
    • abhi: towards or against
    • no: our
    • amantur: may they not harm
    • anyavrato: one who has different vows or intentions
    • amānuṣaḥ: inhuman or non-human
    • tvaṁ: you
    • tasyā: of that
    • amitrahan: the one who destroys enemies
    • vadhar: to kill or to remove
    • dāsasya: of the servant or devotee
    • dambhaya: to deceive or to trick
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the protection from those who act without righteousness.
    • It emphasizes the need for divine intervention against deceitful and harmful individuals.
    • The phrase "may they not harm" reflects a plea for safety and security from malevolent forces.
    • The reference to "inhuman" suggests that the threats may come from those who lack moral integrity.
    • It calls upon a higher power to eliminate deceit and protect the faithful or devoted individuals.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace, this mantra can be applied when dealing with colleagues who may engage in unethical practices to undermine others.
    • In personal relationships, it serves as a reminder to be cautious of individuals who may not have genuine intentions.
    • In broader societal contexts, it can be invoked in situations where corruption and dishonesty threaten the well-being of communities.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the importance of seeking protection from negative influences and deceitful individuals.
    • It serves as a reminder to remain vigilant and to call upon higher powers for guidance and safety.
    • In real life, it encourages individuals to foster integrity and righteousness while being aware of the potential for harm from those who act without moral consideration.
    • Ultimately, it reinforces the belief in the triumph of good over evil and the importance of community support in overcoming challenges.
