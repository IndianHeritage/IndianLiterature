पवमान ऋतम् बृहच्छुक्रं ज्योतिर्जजीजनत् |कृष्णा तमांसि जङ्घनत् ||
pavamāna ṛtam bṛhac chukraṁ jyotir ajījanat | kṛṣṇā tamāṁsi jaṅghanat ||
  • pavamāna: This term refers to the "purifying" or "sublime" aspect of the divine. It signifies the essence of purity and the cleansing of the mind and spirit.
  • ṛtam: This word means "truth" or "cosmic order." It represents the fundamental principle that governs the universe, emphasizing the importance of living in harmony with this order.
  • bṛhac: Translated as "great" or "vast," this term indicates the expansive nature of the universe and the divine presence that encompasses all.
  • chukraṁ: This means "brilliant" or "radiant." It symbolizes the light of knowledge and wisdom that dispels ignorance and darkness.
  • jyotir: Meaning "light" or "illumination," this term signifies enlightenment and the pursuit of knowledge, guiding individuals on their spiritual journey.
  • ajījanat: This word translates to "brought forth" or "created." It indicates the act of manifestation, where the divine brings forth creation from the cosmic order.
  • kṛṣṇā: This term refers to "darkness" or "blackness," symbolizing ignorance, confusion, or the challenges one faces in life.
  • tamāṁsi: This means "the dark" or "the obscured." It emphasizes the presence of obstacles and the need to overcome them to attain clarity and understanding.
  • jaṅghanat: This word means "to dispel" or "to drive away." It signifies the action of removing darkness and ignorance, leading to enlightenment.

In summary, this mantra encapsulates the journey from darkness to light, emphasizing the importance of truth, purity, and the pursuit of knowledge. It highlights the divine's role in creating and sustaining the cosmic order while encouraging individuals to seek enlightenment and overcome ignorance.


Significance and Real-Life Application:

  • This mantra serves as a reminder to strive for purity in thoughts and actions, promoting mental and spiritual well-being.
  • It encourages individuals to seek truth and live in accordance with the cosmic order, fostering harmony in personal and social relationships.
  • The emphasis on dispelling darkness can be applied in real life by addressing ignorance and misinformation, promoting education and awareness in communities.
  • In moments of confusion or challenges, this mantra can inspire individuals to seek clarity and understanding, guiding them towards informed decisions.
