कुर्मस्तु आयुरजरं यदग्ने यथा युक्तो जातवेदो न ऋष्याः |अथा वहासि सुमनस्यमानो भागं देवभ्यो हविषः सुजात ||
kurmas ta āyur ajaraṁ yad agne yathā yukto jātavedo na riṣyāḥ | athā vahāsi sumanasyamāno bhāgaṁ devebhyo haviṣaḥ sujāta ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • kurmas: "may you protect" or "may you sustain"
    • ta: "that" or "this"
    • āyur: "life" or "longevity"
    • ajaraṁ: "immortal" or "without decay"
    • yad: "that which"
    • agne: "O Agni" (the fire god)
    • yathā: "as" or "in the way that"
    • yukto: "connected" or "united"
    • jātavedo: "the one who knows all births" (another name for Agni)
    • na: "not"
    • riṣyāḥ: "become weak" or "decay"
    • athā: "then" or "thereafter"
    • vahāsi: "you carry" or "you bring"
    • sumanasyamāno: "with a pleasing mind" or "with good intentions"
    • bhāgaṁ: "portion" or "share"
    • devebhyo: "to the gods"
    • haviṣaḥ: "with offerings" or "with oblations"
    • sujāta: "well-born" or "auspiciously born"
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Agni, the fire god, seeking protection and longevity.
    • It emphasizes the importance of being connected to divine energies and maintaining a pure intention in our actions.
    • The phrase "may you protect that life which is immortal" suggests a desire for spiritual and physical well-being.
    • In real life, this can be related to practices such as meditation, yoga, and rituals that promote health and longevity.
    • The invocation of Agni signifies the importance of fire in purification and transformation, akin to how we use fire in cooking and rituals to transform raw ingredients into nourishing meals.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of seeking divine blessings for health and longevity.
    • It teaches us the value of maintaining a connection with the divine through our intentions and actions.
    • In modern life, this can be applied through mindfulness practices, healthy living, and rituals that honor our connection to nature and the universe.
    • Ultimately, it serves as a reminder to cultivate a life that is not only long but also meaningful and aligned with higher principles.
