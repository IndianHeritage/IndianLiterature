मक्षू न येषु दोहसे चिद आयā ā नाम धृष्णु मारुतं दधानाः |न ये स्तौना आयासो मह्ना नू चित सुदानुर अव यासद उग्रान ||
makṣū na yeṣu dohase cid ayā ā nāma dhṛṣṇu mārutaṁ dadhānāḥ | na ye staunā ayāso mahnā nū cit sudānur ava yāsad ugrān ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • makṣū: those who are strong or powerful
    • na: not
    • yeṣu: in whom
    • dohase: in the act of milking or extracting
    • cid: indeed
    • ayā: this or that
    • ā: to or towards
    • nāma: name or fame
    • dhṛṣṇu: those who are steadfast or determined
    • mārutaṁ: the wind or the force of nature
    • dadhānāḥ: holding or possessing
    • na: not
    • ye: those who
    • staunā: who praise or worship
    • ayāso: with strength or vigor
    • mahnā: with great power
    • nū: indeed
    • cit: certainly
    • sudānur: with good intentions or auspiciousness
    • ava: down or away
    • yāsad: to be achieved or attained
    • ugrān: fierce or intense
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the strength and determination of those who are not easily swayed or distracted.
    • It emphasizes the importance of steadfastness in the face of challenges, akin to how a strong tree withstands the fiercest winds.
    • The reference to "mārutaṁ" (the wind) symbolizes the forces of nature and life that can be both nurturing and destructive.
    • In real life, this can be related to individuals who remain resolute in their goals despite external pressures or adversities, much like a student who perseveres through difficult studies to achieve academic success.
  • Significance:
    • The mantra serves as a reminder of the power of resilience and the importance of maintaining one's focus and determination.
    • It encourages individuals to cultivate inner strength and to remain grounded, even when faced with challenges that may seem overwhelming.
    • In practical terms, this can be applied in various scenarios, such as in professional settings where one must navigate through competition and stress, or in personal life when dealing with hardships.
