इन्द्रं इद् विमहीनां मेधे वृणीत मर्त्यः |इन्द्रं सनीष्युर ऊतये ||
indram id vimahīnām medhe vṛṇīta martyaḥ | indraṁ saniṣyur ūtaye ||
  • Indram: Refers to Indra, the king of the gods in Vedic mythology, symbolizing power, strength, and leadership.
  • id: This word can be interpreted as "this" or "here," indicating a focus on the present moment or situation.
  • vimahīnām: This term means "of the mighty" or "of those who are powerful," suggesting a connection to strength and capability.
  • medhe: Translates to "intellect" or "wisdom," emphasizing the importance of mental clarity and understanding.
  • vṛṇīta: Means "to choose" or "to select," indicating an active decision-making process.
  • martyaḥ: Refers to "mortals" or "human beings," highlighting the human experience and the need for guidance.
  • saniṣyur: This word can be understood as "to be established" or "to be firm," suggesting stability and assurance.
  • ūtaye: Means "for prosperity" or "for well-being," indicating a desire for growth and success.

The mantra can be interpreted as a call to Indra, asking for his guidance and strength in making wise choices for the benefit of humanity. It emphasizes the importance of intellect and decision-making in achieving prosperity and stability.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as:

  • Leadership: Leaders can invoke the qualities of Indra to inspire strength and wisdom in their decision-making processes.
  • Personal Growth: Individuals can reflect on their choices and seek clarity in their thoughts to achieve their goals.
  • Community Development: Communities can come together to make collective decisions that promote well-being and prosperity for all members.

Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine guidance in the pursuit of wisdom and strength. It serves as a reminder that, as mortals, we must actively choose paths that lead to prosperity and stability, both individually and collectively. By embodying the qualities of Indra, we can navigate life's challenges with clarity and purpose.
