याभिः परिज्मा तनयस्य मञ्जनā द्विमाता तूरषु तरणिर विभूषति |याभिस त्रिमन्तुर अभवद् विचक्षणस ताभिर ūषु ऊतिभिर अश्विनā गतम् ||
yābhiḥ parijmā tanayasya majmanā dvimātā tūrṣu taraṇir vibhūṣati | yābhis trimantur abhavad vicakṣaṇas tābhir ū ṣu ūtibhir aśvinā gatam ||
  • yābhiḥ parijmā tanayasya majmanā: This phrase can be interpreted as a call to the divine for protection and support. The term "parijmā" refers to the act of embracing or surrounding, while "tanayasya" means offspring or progeny. In real life, this can be seen as a plea for the well-being of one's children or future generations.
  • dvimātā tūrṣu taraṇir vibhūṣati: Here, "dvimātā" suggests duality or two mothers, which can symbolize the nurturing aspects of nature and the divine. "Taraṇir" refers to the one who crosses over or transcends, and "vibhūṣati" means to adorn or embellish. This can be related to the idea of finding balance in life, drawing from both earthly and spiritual sources for guidance and strength.
  • yābhis trimantur abhavad vicakṣaṇas: This line speaks of the "trimantur," which can be understood as the threefold wisdom or knowledge. "Vicakṣaṇas" refers to the wise or discerning ones. In practical terms, this emphasizes the importance of wisdom in decision-making and the need to seek knowledge from various sources to navigate life's challenges.
  • tābhir ū ṣu ūtibhir aśvinā gatam: The "aśvinā" refers to the twin deities associated with health and medicine. "Ū ṣu ūtibhir" can be interpreted as the blessings or benefits derived from their grace. This highlights the significance of health and well-being in our lives, reminding us to seek divine assistance in maintaining our physical and mental health.

  • Conclusion: This mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking divine protection, wisdom, and health. It emphasizes the interconnectedness of life, the importance of nurturing future generations, and the need for balance between earthly and spiritual guidance. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in family prayers, during times of uncertainty, or when seeking clarity in decision-making. It serves as a reminder to honor both our earthly responsibilities and our spiritual connections, ultimately guiding us towards a harmonious and fulfilling life.
