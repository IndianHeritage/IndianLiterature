नक्षन्त इन्द्रं अवसे सुखृत्यया येषां सुतेषु मन्दसे |यथा संवरते अमदो यथा कृश एवास्मे इन्द्र मत्स्व ||
nakṣanta indram avase sukṛtyayā yeṣāṁ suteṣu mandase | yathā saṁvarte amado yathā kṛśa evāsme indra matsva ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • nakṣanta: Those who are shining or bright.
    • indram: Indra, the king of gods, often associated with rain and thunderstorms.
    • avase: To be invoked or called upon.
    • sukṛtyayā: By good deeds or righteous actions.
    • yeṣāṁ: Whose.
    • suteṣu: In the offerings or sacrifices.
    • mandase: With a gentle or pleasing disposition.
    • yathā: Just as.
    • saṁvarte: In the midst of or during the occurrence.
    • amado: The nectar or bliss.
    • yathā: Just as.
    • kṛśa: Weak or feeble.
    • evāsme: We are indeed.
    • indra: Indra, again referring to the deity.
    • matsva: May you be pleased or may you grant us your favor.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes Indra, asking him to be present and favorably disposed towards those who perform good deeds.
    • It emphasizes the importance of righteousness and good actions in attracting divine blessings.
    • The reference to offerings suggests that rituals and sacrifices are a means to connect with the divine and seek assistance.
    • The mention of "nectar" symbolizes the bliss and joy that comes from divine grace, while "weak" indicates the human condition that often requires support from higher powers.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community setting, individuals who engage in charitable acts often find themselves receiving support and goodwill from others, akin to invoking divine favor.
    • In personal life, someone who consistently acts with integrity and kindness may experience a sense of fulfillment and happiness, reflecting the "nectar" of good deeds.
    • During festivals or religious ceremonies, people perform rituals to seek blessings, demonstrating the relevance of this mantra in contemporary practices.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of good deeds and the importance of invoking divine support in our lives.
    • It serves as a reminder that our actions can attract positive outcomes and blessings, both from the divine and within our communities.
    • In real life, it encourages individuals to engage in righteous behavior, fostering a sense of connection with the divine and promoting harmony in society.
