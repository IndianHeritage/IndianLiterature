अर्चामि ते सुमतिं घोष्यर्वाक् सं ते वावातां जरताम् इयं गीः |स्वश्वास त्वा सुरथा मरजयेमास्मे क्षत्राणि धारयेर अनु dyūn ||
arcāmi te sumatiṁ ghoṣy arvāk saṁ te vāvātā jaratām iyaṁ gīḥ | svaśvās tvā surathā marjayemāsme kṣatrāṇi dhārayer anu dyūn ||
  • arcāmi te: This phrase translates to "I worship you" or "I offer my prayers to you." It signifies the act of devotion and reverence towards a higher power or deity.
  • sumatiṁ: This word means "good thought" or "wisdom." It emphasizes the importance of having a clear and positive mindset in life.
  • ghoṣy: This term refers to "sound" or "voice." It can symbolize communication, expression, and the sharing of ideas.
  • arvāk: This means "from afar" or "distant." It suggests the idea of reaching out beyond immediate surroundings or limitations.
  • saṁ te vāvātā: This phrase can be interpreted as "may the winds be favorable to you." It reflects the desire for positive circumstances and support in one's endeavors.
  • jaratām: This word means "of the aged" or "of the wise." It highlights the value of experience and wisdom that comes with age.
  • iyaṁ gīḥ: This translates to "this song" or "this hymn." It signifies the power of music and poetry in expressing devotion and emotions.
  • svaśvās: This term means "self-confidence" or "self-assurance." It emphasizes the importance of believing in oneself.
  • tvā surathā: This phrase translates to "you who are auspicious." It refers to the divine qualities that bring good fortune and success.
  • marjayemāsme: This means "we may purify" or "we may cleanse." It signifies the act of seeking purity in thoughts, actions, and intentions.
  • kṣatrāṇi: This word refers to "warriors" or "nobility." It symbolizes strength, courage, and the protective qualities of leadership.
  • dhārayer: This means "to uphold" or "to support." It emphasizes the importance of maintaining values and principles.
  • anu dyūn: This translates to "after the heavens" or "following the celestial." It suggests a connection to the divine and the pursuit of higher ideals.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a deep sense of devotion, wisdom, and the pursuit of positive thoughts and actions. It encourages individuals to seek purity in their intentions, uphold noble values, and maintain self-confidence while navigating life's challenges. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as during personal challenges, in leadership roles, or while striving for personal growth. By embodying the principles of this mantra, individuals can foster a positive mindset, enhance their communication, and create a supportive environment for themselves and others.
