यद आयातं शुभस पती वरेयं सूर्यां उप |क्वैकं चक्रं वाम आसीत क्व देष्ट्राय तस्थतुः ||
yad ayātaṁ śubhas patī vareyaṁ sūryām upa | kvaikaṁ cakraṁ vām āsīt kva deṣṭrāya tasthathuḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yad: that which
    • ayātaṁ: has come forth
    • śubhas: auspicious
    • patī: lord or master
    • vareyaṁ: I seek or desire
    • sūryām: the sun
    • upa: near or towards
    • kvaikaṁ: where is
    • cakraṁ: the wheel or cycle
    • vām: of you
    • āsīt: was or existed
    • kva: where
    • deṣṭrāya: for the one who is to be seen
    • tasthathuḥ: stood or remained
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra expresses a yearning for the auspicious qualities of the sun, which is often seen as a symbol of enlightenment, vitality, and clarity.
    • The phrase "yad ayātaṁ" suggests a recognition of the blessings that have come into one's life, emphasizing gratitude.
    • By seeking the "śubhas patī" or the lord of auspiciousness, the mantra highlights the importance of aligning oneself with positive energies and influences.
    • The reference to "kvaikaṁ cakraṁ" can be interpreted as a quest for understanding the cycles of life and existence, prompting reflection on one's journey and purpose.
    • In a practical sense, this mantra encourages individuals to seek clarity and positivity in their lives, much like how the sun brings light and warmth.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, one might recite this mantra during meditation to cultivate a sense of peace and to invite positive energy into their surroundings.
    • In moments of uncertainty or confusion, reflecting on the cycles of life, as suggested by the mantra, can help individuals find direction and purpose.
    • For instance, a student preparing for exams might use this mantra to focus their mind and seek clarity, much like the sun illuminating a path.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of seeking auspiciousness and clarity in our lives.
    • It encourages individuals to reflect on their journey, recognize the blessings they have received, and strive for positivity.
    • In a broader context, it can be applied in various life situations, such as decision-making, personal growth, and spiritual practices, reinforcing the idea that aligning with positive energies can lead to a more fulfilling life.
