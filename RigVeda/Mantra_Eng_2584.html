आयामि ते नमुक्तिं जुषस्व ऋतावस तुभ्यं चेतते सहस्वः |विद्वां आ वक्षि विदुषो नि षट्सि मध्य आ बर्हिर ऊतये यजत्र ||
ayāmi te namaüktiṁ juṣasva ṛtāvas tubhyaṁ cetate sahasvaḥ | vidvām̐ ā vakṣi viduṣo ni ṣatsi madhya ā barhir ūtaye yajatra ||
  • ayāmi te namaüktiṁ: This phrase can be translated as "I come to you with a prayer." It signifies the act of approaching the divine with humility and reverence.
  • juṣasva ṛtāvas tubhyaṁ: This means "may you be pleased with the truth." Here, the emphasis is on the importance of truthfulness and righteousness in one's actions and thoughts.
  • cetate sahasvaḥ: This translates to "may you be aware and strong." It suggests a call for awareness and strength in facing life's challenges.
  • vidvām̐ ā vakṣi viduṣo: This can be interpreted as "I am knowledgeable and I speak with wisdom." It highlights the value of knowledge and wisdom in guiding one's actions.
  • ni ṣatsi madhya: This means "may you sit in the center." It symbolizes stability and balance, suggesting that one should remain grounded amidst chaos.
  • ā barhir ūtaye yajatra: This translates to "may you be the support for the offerings." It indicates the importance of being a foundation for others, especially in communal or spiritual practices.

  • Significance: This mantra encapsulates the essence of devotion, truth, strength, knowledge, and community support. It serves as a reminder to approach life with humility and integrity.
  • Real-life Application: In daily life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios such as:
    • In personal relationships, approaching others with respect and truthfulness fosters trust and harmony.
    • In professional settings, being knowledgeable and wise in decision-making can lead to better outcomes and leadership.
    • In community service, being a support for others during challenging times exemplifies the spirit of togetherness and compassion.

  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a guiding principle for living a life of integrity, awareness, and support for others. By embodying these values, individuals can contribute positively to their communities and lead fulfilling lives.
