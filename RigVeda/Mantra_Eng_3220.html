स्वर यद वेदि सुदृशीकम् अर्कैर महि ज्योती रुचुर यद ध वस्तोः |अन्धा तमांसि दुधिता विचक्षे नृभ्यश चकार नृतमो अभिष्टौ ||
sva1r yad vedi sudṛśīkam arkair mahi jyotī rurucur yad dha vastoḥ | andhā tamāṁsi dudhitā vicakṣe nṛbhyaś cakāra nṛtamo abhiṣṭau ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sva1r: "of the self" or "one's own"
    • yad: "that which"
    • vedi: "knows" or "is known"
    • Sudṛśīkam: "beautiful" or "well-formed"
    • arkair: "with rays" or "with light"
    • mahi: "great" or "vast"
    • jyotī: "light" or "brightness"
    • rurucur: "shining" or "illuminating"
    • yad: "that which"
    • dha: "holds" or "contains"
    • vastuḥ: "substance" or "thing"
    • andhā: "blind" or "darkness"
    • tamāṁsi: "the dark" or "the obscured"
    • dudhitā: "illuminated" or "made bright"
    • vicakṣe: "to see" or "to perceive"
    • nṛbhyaś: "to humans" or "to people"
    • cakāra: "created" or "made"
    • nṛtamo: "the best of men" or "the most excellent"
    • abhiṣṭau: "in the desires" or "in the aspirations"
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the self and the knowledge that one possesses.
    • It emphasizes the beauty and brilliance of knowledge, likening it to light that dispels darkness.
    • The mantra suggests that true understanding allows one to perceive beyond the obscured and the blind spots of ignorance.
    • It highlights the role of knowledge in shaping human aspirations and desires, guiding individuals towards excellence.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In education, the pursuit of knowledge can illuminate paths for students, helping them to overcome challenges and achieve their goals.
    • In personal development, self-awareness and understanding can lead to better decision-making and improved relationships.
    • In leadership, a leader who possesses knowledge and insight can inspire and guide their team towards success.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its celebration of knowledge as a transformative force.
    • It teaches us that by seeking knowledge and understanding, we can dispel the darkness of ignorance and enhance our lives.
    • This mantra is applicable in various aspects of life, including education, personal growth, and leadership, reminding us of the importance of wisdom in our journey.
