अधā होतā न्य असीदो यजीयान इḻस पद इषयन्न īḍ्यः सन |तं त्वा नरः प्रथमं देवयन्तो महो राये चितयन्तो अनु गमन ||
adhā hotā ny asīdo yajīyān iḻas pada iṣayann īḍyaḥ san | taṁ tvā naraḥ prathamaṁ devayanto maho rāye citayanto anu gman ||
  • adhā hotā ny asīdo: This phrase can be interpreted as "the one who is invoked (hotā) does not become distant (asīdo)." In a real-life context, this can refer to the idea that when we call upon help or guidance, it is always available to us, much like how we can reach out to friends or mentors in times of need.
  • yajīyān iḻas pada: This translates to "the one who performs sacrifices (yajīyān) is in a position of strength (iḻas pada)." In modern terms, this can be likened to the concept of hard work and dedication leading to success. For example, a student who diligently studies for exams is more likely to achieve good results.
  • iṣayann īḍyaḥ san: This means "being worthy of worship (īḍyaḥ) and possessing power (iṣayann)." In everyday life, this can be seen in leaders or individuals who earn respect and admiration through their actions and integrity, such as community leaders who work tirelessly for the welfare of others.
  • taṁ tvā naraḥ prathamaṁ: This phrase translates to "humans (naraḥ) first (prathamaṁ) seek you (tvā)." This highlights the human tendency to seek divine or higher guidance in times of uncertainty. For instance, people often turn to spirituality or meditation when facing challenges in life.
  • devayanto maho rāye: This can be interpreted as "those who are devoted to the divine (devayanto) seek great wealth (maho rāye)." In a practical sense, this suggests that those who are spiritually inclined often find fulfillment and abundance in their lives, not just materially but also in terms of happiness and peace.
  • citayanto anu gman: This means "those who contemplate (citayanto) and follow (anu gman)." This emphasizes the importance of reflection and following a righteous path. In real life, this can be seen in individuals who take time to reflect on their actions and strive to live ethically, leading to a more meaningful existence.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking divine guidance, the importance of dedication and hard work, and the value of reflection in our lives. It signifies that through devotion and ethical living, individuals can attain both spiritual and material wealth. In today's fast-paced world, this mantra serves as a reminder to stay grounded, seek help when needed, and strive for a life of purpose and integrity.
