नि काव्याः वेधसः शाश्वतस् कर हस्ते दधानो नार्याः पुरूणि |अग्निः भुवद् रयिपतिः रयीणां सत्रा चक्राणो अमृतानि विश्वा ||
ni kāvyā vedhasaḥ śaśvatas kar haste dadhāno naryā purūṇi | agnir bhuvad rayipatī rayīṇāṁ satrā cakrāṇo amṛtāni viśvā ||
  • ni kāvyā vedhasaḥ: This phrase can be interpreted as "the one who is skilled in the arts and knowledge." In real life, this can refer to individuals who excel in creative fields such as literature, music, or any form of artistic expression. For example, a poet or a musician who brings forth profound emotions through their work embodies this concept.
  • śaśvatas kar haste dadhāno: This translates to "holding eternal actions in their hands." This can be seen in leaders or visionaries who take decisive actions that have lasting impacts on society. For instance, a social activist working tirelessly for a cause demonstrates this eternal commitment to change.
  • naryā purūṇi: This means "the fullness of humanity." It signifies the importance of human values and the collective strength of people. In a real-life context, this can be observed in community gatherings where individuals come together to support one another, showcasing the power of unity.
  • agnir bhuvad rayipatī rayīṇāṁ: This phrase can be interpreted as "the fire that brings forth wealth and prosperity." Fire symbolizes transformation and energy, akin to how innovation and hard work can lead to success. For example, a startup that begins with a small idea and grows into a successful business reflects this principle.
  • satrā cakrāṇo amṛtāni viśvā: This translates to "the circle of life that encompasses all immortality." It emphasizes the interconnectedness of life and the eternal cycle of existence. In real life, this can be seen in nature, where ecosystems thrive on balance and harmony, reminding us of our role in preserving the environment.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of creativity, action, humanity, prosperity, and the cycle of life. Its significance lies in the reminder that our actions, rooted in knowledge and creativity, can lead to lasting impacts on society and the environment. In real-life scenarios, it encourages individuals to embrace their artistic talents, take meaningful actions, foster community, pursue prosperity through innovation, and recognize the interconnectedness of all life. By embodying these principles, we contribute to a more harmonious and prosperous world.
