वयं चिद्धि वां जरीतारः सत्यā विपण्यामहे वि पणिर हितावान् |अधā चिद्धि ष्माश्विनाव अनिन्द्यā पाठो हि ष्मा वृषणाव अन्तिदेवम् ||
vayaṁ cid dhi vāṁ jaritāraḥ satyā vipanyāmahe vi paṇir hitāvān | adhā cid dhi ṣmāśvināv anindyā pātho hi ṣmā vṛṣaṇāv antidevam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • vayaṁ: We
    • cid: indeed
    • hhi: certainly
    • vāṁ: you
    • jaritāraḥ: the ones who are strong or powerful
    • satyā: with truth
    • vipanyāmahe: we invoke or call upon
    • vi: in a special way
    • paṇir: the one who is skilled or adept
    • hitāvān: beneficial or helpful
    • adhā: below or beneath
    • cid: indeed
    • hhi: certainly
    • ṣmāśvināv: the Ashvins, the divine horsemen
    • anindyā: without blame or fault
    • pātho: the path or way
    • hi: indeed
    • ṣmā: the Ashvins
    • vṛṣaṇāv: the strong or powerful ones
    • antidevam: against the divine or in opposition to the divine
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra expresses a collective invocation to the divine, particularly to the Ashvins, who are revered as the twin gods of health and medicine in Vedic literature.
    • The phrase "we invoke you" signifies a communal effort to seek blessings and assistance from the divine powers.
    • The mention of "truth" emphasizes the importance of honesty and integrity in our endeavors.
    • The Ashvins are depicted as powerful and skilled, suggesting that we should aspire to embody these qualities in our own lives.
    • The term "anindyā" indicates that the path we seek should be free from blame, highlighting the need for righteousness in our actions.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, when a team collaborates on a project, invoking the qualities of the Ashvins can inspire teamwork, skillfulness, and a commitment to truthfulness in communication.
    • In personal relationships, striving for honesty and integrity can lead to stronger bonds and mutual respect, reflecting the essence of the mantra.
    • In health and wellness practices, seeking divine guidance for well-being can be seen in rituals or prayers that emphasize the importance of health, much like the blessings of the Ashvins.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the power of collective intention and the importance of seeking divine guidance in our lives.
    • It encourages us to embody qualities such as truthfulness, skillfulness, and righteousness, which are essential for personal and communal growth.
    • In real-life scenarios, whether in professional, personal, or spiritual contexts, the principles outlined in this mantra can guide us towards a more fulfilling and harmonious existence.
