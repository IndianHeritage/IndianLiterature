अग्निषोमा वृषणा वाजसातये पुरुप्रशस्तā वृषणा उप ब्रुवे |याव ईजिरे वृषणो देवयज्ञया ता नः शर्मा त्रिवरूथं वि यंषतः ||
agnīṣomā vṛṣaṇā vājasātaye purupraśastā vṛṣaṇā upa bruve | yāv ījire vṛṣaṇo devayajyayā tā naḥ śarma trivarūthaṁ vi yaṁsataḥ ||
  • Agnīṣomā: This term refers to the divine forces of fire (Agni) and the moon (Soma). In real life, fire symbolizes energy, transformation, and purification, while the moon represents calmness, nourishment, and the cyclical nature of life.
  • Vṛṣaṇā: This word can be interpreted as strength or power. It signifies the vigor and vitality that one needs to overcome challenges in life.
  • Vājasātaye: This term means for the purpose of achieving success or prosperity. It emphasizes the importance of striving for success in various endeavors, whether personal or professional.
  • Purupraśastā: This refers to the praise of the divine or the exalted. It highlights the significance of recognizing and honoring the higher powers that guide our lives.
  • Yāv ījire: This phrase can be translated as "as long as they are invoked." It suggests the importance of actively seeking the blessings and guidance of the divine forces.
  • Devayajyayā: This term refers to the act of worship or devotion to the divine. It signifies the importance of spiritual practices in achieving harmony and balance in life.
  • Tā naḥ śarma: This means "may they grant us protection." It emphasizes the need for safety and security in our lives, both physically and spiritually.
  • Trivarūthaṁ: This term can be interpreted as "threefold shelter." It signifies the various layers of protection that one seeks in life, such as emotional, physical, and spiritual support.
  • Vi yaṁsataḥ: This phrase means "may they lead us." It expresses the desire for guidance and direction in our life journey.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking divine guidance and strength in our lives. It emphasizes the importance of invoking the powers of fire and the moon for transformation and nourishment, respectively. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as during challenging times when one seeks strength and protection, or in moments of decision-making where guidance is essential. By recognizing and honoring the divine forces, individuals can cultivate a sense of security and purpose, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling life. The mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of spiritual practices and the pursuit of success and well-being.
