सद इद धि ते तुविजातस्य मन्ये सहः सहिष्ट turatas तुरस्य |उग्रं उग्रस्य तवसस तवीयो 'रध्रस्य रध्रतुरो बभूव ||
sad id dhi te tuvijātasya manye sahaḥ sahiṣṭha turatas turasya | ugram ugrasya tavasas tavīyo 'radhrasya radhraturo babhūva ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sad id dhi: "Indeed, this is true" - an affirmation of reality or existence.
    • te: "to you" - indicating direction or focus towards a deity or higher power.
    • tuvijātasya: "of the one who is born from you" - referring to creation or beings that originate from the divine.
    • manye: "I think" or "I believe" - expressing a personal conviction or thought.
    • sahaḥ: "together" - emphasizing unity or collective existence.
    • sahiṣṭha: "the strongest" - denoting power or strength.
    • turatas: "swift" - representing speed or agility.
    • turasya: "of the swift" - again referring to the quality of speed.
    • ugram: "fierce" - indicating intensity or ferocity.
    • ugrasya: "of the fierce" - reinforcing the idea of strength and intensity.
    • tavasas: "of your power" - referring to the divine strength or energy.
    • tavīyo: "the most powerful" - highlighting supreme strength.
    • ‘radhrasya: "of the one who is steady" - indicating stability or reliability.
    • radhraturo: "of the steady one" - again emphasizing steadiness.
    • babhūva: "has become" - indicating transformation or manifestation.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks to the essence of divine strength and the interconnectedness of all beings.
    • It emphasizes the importance of unity and collective strength, suggesting that true power lies in togetherness.
    • The reference to swiftness and fierceness can be seen as a metaphor for the challenges we face in life, which require both speed and strength to overcome.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a team setting, whether in sports or work, the mantra reflects how collaboration and unity can lead to greater achievements than individual efforts.
    • In personal challenges, the qualities of swiftness and fierceness can be applied when facing obstacles, encouraging individuals to act decisively and with strength.
    • In community efforts, this mantra can inspire collective action towards social causes, highlighting the power of coming together for a common goal.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its reminder of the power of unity and the strength derived from collective efforts.
    • It encourages individuals to recognize their interconnectedness with others and the divine, fostering a sense of community and shared purpose.
    • In real-life scenarios, applying the principles of this mantra can lead to more effective teamwork, resilience in the face of challenges, and a deeper understanding of one's role within a larger context.
