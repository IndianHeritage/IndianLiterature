मन्त्र: इहेह जाताऽसम अवावशीताम् अरेपसां तन्मा नामभिः स्वैः |जिष्णुर वाम अन्यः सुमखस्य सूरीर दिवो अन्यः सुभगः पुत्र ऊहे ||
iheha jātā sam avāvaśītām arepasā tanvā3 nāmabhiḥ svaiḥ | jiṣṇur vām anyaḥ sumakhasya sūrir divo anyaḥ subhagaḥ putra ūhe ||
  • iheha jātā sam avāvaśītām: This phrase can be interpreted as "those who are born here, may they be united." It emphasizes the importance of community and togetherness. In real life, this can be seen in families or communities coming together for celebrations, support during hardships, or collective efforts for a common cause.
  • arepasā tanvā3 nāmabhiḥ svaiḥ: This translates to "with their own names and forms." It suggests the significance of individuality within a collective. Each person has their unique identity, yet they contribute to the larger community. For example, in a workplace, each employee brings their skills and talents, which collectively drive the organization’s success.
  • jiṣṇur vām anyaḥ sumakhasya sūrir: This can be understood as "may the strong and capable ones be blessed." It highlights the value of strength and capability in achieving goals. In real life, this can relate to athletes who train hard to excel in their sports, or leaders who inspire and guide their teams towards success.
  • divo anyaḥ subhagaḥ putra ūhe: This phrase means "may the fortunate ones from the heavens be with us." It reflects the idea of divine blessings and support. In practical terms, this can be seen in moments of gratitude, where individuals acknowledge the role of luck or fate in their achievements, such as winning a competition or receiving unexpected help during difficult times.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of community, individuality, strength, and divine support. Its significance lies in the reminder that while we are unique individuals, our collective efforts and unity can lead to greater achievements. In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as family gatherings, team projects, or community service, where the blend of individual strengths and shared goals leads to success and fulfillment. It encourages us to recognize and appreciate both our personal identities and our interconnectedness with others.
