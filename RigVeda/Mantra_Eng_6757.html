विष्वाभिर धिभिर bhuvanena वाजिना दिवा पृथिव्याद्रिभिः सचाभुवाः |सजोषसां उषसा सूर्येण च सोमं पिबताम अश्विनाः ||
viśvābhir dhībhir bhuvanena vājinā divā pṛthivyādribhiḥ sacābhuvā | sajoṣasā uṣasā sūryeṇa ca somam pibatam aśvinā ||
  • viśvābhir dhībhir: This phrase can be translated as "with all kinds of wisdom." It emphasizes the importance of diverse knowledge and understanding in life. In real life, this can be seen in collaborative environments where individuals from different backgrounds come together to solve problems, such as in a team project at work or in community service initiatives.
  • bhuvanena vājinā: This means "with the strength of the world." It suggests harnessing the power and resources available in the world. For example, in business, leveraging market trends and consumer behavior can lead to successful strategies and innovations.
  • divā pṛthivyādribhiḥ sacābhuvā: This translates to "during the day, may the earth be filled with strength." It highlights the significance of vitality and energy in our daily lives. In practical terms, this can relate to maintaining a healthy lifestyle through exercise and nutrition, which empowers individuals to face daily challenges.
  • sajoṣasā uṣasā: This phrase means "with the dawn." It symbolizes new beginnings and opportunities that each day brings. In real life, this can be seen in the practice of setting daily goals or intentions each morning, which can lead to increased productivity and a positive mindset.
  • sūryeṇa ca somam: This translates to "with the sun and the moon." It represents the balance of energies in life, such as the interplay between work (sun) and rest (moon). In everyday life, this can be reflected in the importance of work-life balance, ensuring that one does not overexert themselves and takes time to recharge.
  • pibatam aśvinā: This means "may the Ashvins drink." The Ashvins are twin deities associated with health and medicine. This phrase can be interpreted as a prayer for health and well-being. In real life, this can be related to the importance of seeking medical care and maintaining mental health through practices like mindfulness and therapy.

  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of wisdom, strength, vitality, new beginnings, balance, and health. In our daily lives, we can apply these principles by fostering collaboration, leveraging resources, maintaining a healthy lifestyle, setting daily intentions, balancing work and rest, and prioritizing our well-being. By embodying these values, we can navigate life's challenges more effectively and lead a more fulfilling life.
