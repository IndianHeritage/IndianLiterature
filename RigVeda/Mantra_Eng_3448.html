सजोषा इन्द्र वरुणेन सोमं सजोषाः पाहि गिरवणो मरुद्भिः |अग्रेपाभिर ऋतुपाभिः सजोषा ज्ञास्पत्नीभिः रत्नधाभिः सजोषाः ||
sajoṣā indra varuṇena somaṁ sajoṣāḥ pāhi girvaṇo marudbhiḥ | agrepābhir ṛtupābhiḥ sajoṣā gnāspatnībhī ratnadhābhiḥ sajoṣāḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sajoṣā: Together with joy or harmony
    • indra: Indra, the king of gods, associated with rain and thunderstorms
    • varuṇena: With Varuna, the god of cosmic order and water
    • somaṁ: Soma, a sacred drink that symbolizes immortality and divine inspiration
    • pāhi: Protect or preserve
    • girvaṇo: Of the singers or poets
    • marudbhiḥ: With the Maruts, the storm deities
    • agre: In the forefront or at the beginning
    • pābhir: With the rays or light
    • ṛtupābhiḥ: With the forces of truth and order
    • gnāspatnībhī: With the goddesses of knowledge and wisdom
    • ratnadhābhiḥ: With the treasures or valuable things
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the divine forces of Indra and Varuna, seeking their protection and blessings.
    • The mention of Soma signifies the importance of spiritual nourishment and the quest for higher consciousness.
    • It emphasizes the need for harmony and collaboration among various divine entities, such as the Maruts and goddesses of knowledge.
    • The mantra can be seen as a call for unity and support in the pursuit of truth and wisdom.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community setting, this mantra can be applied to foster collaboration among individuals working towards a common goal, such as environmental conservation.
    • In educational institutions, invoking the spirit of this mantra can encourage students to work together harmoniously, sharing knowledge and resources.
    • In personal life, it can serve as a reminder to seek balance and support from friends and family during challenging times.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of unity, collaboration, and the pursuit of higher knowledge.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from community efforts to personal relationships, emphasizing the importance of working together for a greater purpose.
    • By invoking the blessings of divine forces, individuals and communities can strive for harmony and success in their endeavors.
