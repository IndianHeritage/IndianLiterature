तव त्ये सोम पवमान निन्ये विश्वे देवास त्रय एकादशासः |दश स्वधाभिर अधि सानो अव्ये मृजन्ति त्वा नद्यः सप्त यह्वीः ||
tava tye soma pavamāna niṇye viśve devās traya ekādaśāsaḥ | daśa svadhābhir adhi sāno avye mṛjanti tvā nadyaḥ sapta yahvīḥ ||
  • tava tye soma pavamāna: This phrase refers to the "Soma," a sacred drink in Vedic rituals, which is considered purifying and invigorating. "Pavamāna" means "the purifying one," indicating the transformative power of Soma.
  • niṇye viśve devās traya ekādaśāsaḥ: This part speaks of the "universal gods" (viśve devās) and mentions "three" (traya) and "eleven" (ekādaśāsaḥ), which can symbolize the various aspects of divinity and the interconnectedness of all beings.
  • daśa svadhābhir adhi sāno: "Daśa" means "ten," and "svadhā" refers to the offerings made to ancestors. This line emphasizes the importance of honoring our forebears and the continuity of life through generations.
  • avyemṛjanti tvā nadyaḥ sapta yahvīḥ: This phrase suggests that the rivers (nadyaḥ) cleanse and purify, symbolizing the flow of life and the importance of water in sustaining existence. "Sapta yahvīḥ" refers to the seven rivers, often associated with the sacred geography of the Vedic tradition.

  • Significance: This mantra encapsulates the essence of purification, reverence for the divine, and the importance of ancestral connections. It highlights the role of nature, particularly water, in sustaining life and the spiritual journey.
  • Real-life Application: In contemporary life, this mantra can inspire individuals to seek purification in their thoughts and actions, honor their heritage, and recognize the interconnectedness of all life. For example, engaging in environmental conservation can be seen as a way to honor the rivers and nature that sustain us.

  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a reminder of the sacredness of life, the importance of purification, and the need to honor both the divine and our ancestors. It encourages a holistic approach to living, where spirituality, nature, and heritage are intertwined, guiding individuals towards a more meaningful existence.
