आ नामभिर मरुतो वक्षि विश्वान आ रूपेभिर जातवेदो हुवानः | यज्ञं गिरो जारितुः सुष्टुतिं च विश्वे गन्त मरुतो विश्व ऊती ||
ā nāmabhir maruto vakṣi viśvān ā rūpebhir jātavedo huvānaḥ | yajñaṁ giro jarituḥ suṣṭutiṁ ca viśve ganta maruto viśva ūtī ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā: from
    • nāmabhir: by names
    • maruto: O Maruts (the storm deities)
    • vakṣi: speak
    • viśvān: all
    • ā: in
    • rūpebhir: forms
    • jātavedo: O Jātavedas (the fire god)
    • huvānaḥ: invoking
    • yajñaṁ: the sacrifice
    • giro: of the words
    • jarituḥ: of the one who is born
    • suṣṭutiṁ: good praise
    • ca: and
    • viśve: all
    • ganta: go
    • maruto: O Maruts
    • viśva: all
    • ūtī: prosperity
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a call to the Maruts, the storm deities, to come forth and speak their names.
    • It invokes the fire god Jātavedas, asking for the sacrifice to be accepted and for the words of praise to be heard.
    • The mantra emphasizes the importance of collective worship and the power of words in rituals.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern times, this mantra can be likened to the collective efforts in community gatherings, where people come together to celebrate and invoke blessings for prosperity.
    • For instance, during festivals, communities perform rituals that echo the essence of this mantra, seeking divine blessings through collective prayers and offerings.
    • In corporate settings, team-building exercises can reflect the spirit of this mantra, where individuals come together to achieve a common goal, invoking creativity and collaboration.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its emphasis on unity, collective effort, and the power of words in invoking divine blessings.
    • It teaches us that when we come together with a common purpose, we can achieve greater outcomes, whether in spiritual practices or in everyday life.
    • This mantra is applicable in various scenarios, from personal growth to community development, reminding us of the strength found in unity and shared intentions.
