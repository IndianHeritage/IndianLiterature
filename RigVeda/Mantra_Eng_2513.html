दिवश्चिदातेरुचयन्तरोका उषो विभातीरनुभाषिपूर्वीः |अपो यदाग्न उषधगवनेषु होतुरमन्द्रस्य पनयन्त देवाः ||
divaś cid ā te rucayanta rokā uṣo vibhātīr anu bhāsi pūrvīḥ | apo yad agna uśadhag vaneṣu hotur mandrasya panayanta devāḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • Divaś: Of the heavens
    • Cid: Indeed
    • Ā: To you
    • Te: Your
    • Rucayanta: Shining or illuminating
    • Rokā: The light or rays
    • Uṣo: Dawn
    • Vibhātīr: Shining or manifesting
    • Anu: Following
    • Bhāsi: You shine
    • Pūrvīḥ: The earlier ones or the past
    • Apo: From the waters
    • Yad: That which
    • Agna: O fire
    • Uśadhag: Of the herbs or plants
    • Vaneṣu: In the forests
    • Hotur: O priest or one who invokes
    • Mandrasya: Of the sweet or pleasant
    • Panayanta: Bestowing or giving
    • Devāḥ: The gods
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the divine light of dawn, which is a symbol of new beginnings and hope.
    • The phrase "Divaś cid ā te rucayanta" emphasizes the illuminating nature of the heavens, suggesting that divine guidance is always present.
    • The mention of "Uṣo vibhātīr" refers to the dawn that follows the night, symbolizing the cyclical nature of time and the inevitability of change.
    • "Apo yad agna uśadhag vaneṣu" connects the elements of nature, indicating that the divine presence is found in the waters, fire, and plants, which are essential for life.
    • The invocation of "devāḥ" highlights the role of the gods in bestowing blessings and prosperity upon humanity.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • Every morning, the rising sun brings light and warmth, reminding us of the opportunities that each new day holds.
    • In nature, the interdependence of fire, water, and plants illustrates the balance necessary for ecological harmony, which is crucial for sustainable living.
    • In many cultures, dawn is celebrated as a time for prayer and reflection, acknowledging the divine forces that guide our lives.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the divine light that guides us through darkness, encouraging us to seek knowledge and wisdom.
    • It emphasizes the importance of nature and the interconnectedness of all elements, urging us to respect and protect our environment.
    • In a broader sense, it teaches us to embrace change and new beginnings, fostering a mindset of hope and resilience in our daily lives.
