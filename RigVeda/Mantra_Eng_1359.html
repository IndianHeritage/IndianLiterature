अस्य मादे स्वार्यं दा ऋतायापीवृतम् उष्रियाणाम् अनीकम् |यद ह प्रसर्गे त्रिककुम निवर्तद अप द्रुहो मानवस्य दुरो वः ||
asya made svaryaṁ dā ṛtāyāpīvṛtam usriyāṇām anīkam | yad dha prasarge trikakum nivartad apa druho mānuṣasya duro vaḥ ||
  • Mantra: "asya made svaryaṁ dā ṛtāyāpīvṛtam usriyāṇām anīkam | yad dha prasarge trikakum nivartad apa druho mānuṣasya duro vaḥ ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • asya: of this
    • made: in my mind
    • svaryaṁ: heavenly or divine
    • dā: gift or offering
    • ṛtāyā: in accordance with truth
    • pīvṛtam: enveloped or surrounded
    • usriyāṇām: of the wise or enlightened
    • anīkam: a group or assembly
    • yad: that which
    • dha: indeed
    • prasarge: in the creation or manifestation
    • trikakum: threefold or triple
    • nivartad: may return or revert
    • apa: away from
    • druho: deceit or treachery
    • mānuṣasya: of humanity
    • duro: difficult or hard
    • vaḥ: to you
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks to the divine gifts that come from a pure and truthful mind.
    • It emphasizes the importance of wisdom and the collective strength of enlightened beings.
    • The phrase "may return" suggests a cyclical nature of existence, where actions and intentions come back to the individual.
    • It warns against deceit and treachery, highlighting the challenges faced by humanity.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace, a team that operates with transparency and honesty tends to be more successful and harmonious.
    • In personal relationships, being truthful fosters trust and strengthens bonds, while deceit can lead to conflict and separation.
    • In community settings, collective wisdom can lead to better decision-making and problem-solving, as seen in collaborative projects.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the significance of truth, wisdom, and community in our lives.
    • It encourages individuals to cultivate a truthful mindset and to be aware of the consequences of their actions.
    • In a world where deceit can be prevalent, this mantra inspires a return to integrity and the pursuit of collective enlightenment.
