मन्त्र: उता नो गोषणिं धियम अश्वसां वाजसाम उता | नृवत कृणुहि वीटये ||
uta no goṣaṇiṁ dhiyam aśvasāṁ vājasām uta | nṛvat kṛṇuhi vītaye ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • uta: and, also
    • no: our
    • goṣaṇiṁ: the thoughts or intellect
    • dhiyam: wisdom or understanding
    • aśvasāṁ: of the horses (symbolizing speed and strength)
    • vājasām: of the powerful or the strong
    • nṛvat: like a man or human
    • kṛṇuhi: make or create
    • vītaye: for victory or success
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer for the enhancement of our intellect and wisdom.
    • It emphasizes the importance of strength and speed, symbolized by horses, in achieving success.
    • The phrase "like a man" suggests a call for human-like qualities such as courage, determination, and resilience.
    • Overall, it is a request for divine assistance in attaining victory in our endeavors.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a competitive environment, such as a business setting, individuals often seek wisdom and strength to make informed decisions and to act swiftly.
    • A student preparing for exams may recite this mantra to invoke clarity of thought and the ability to grasp complex concepts quickly.
    • A sports team may use this mantra as a motivational tool to enhance their performance, focusing on teamwork and the strength of each member.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of wisdom, strength, and human qualities in achieving success.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, including education, business, and sports, where the combination of intellect and strength is crucial for victory.
    • By invoking this mantra, individuals can seek divine guidance to enhance their capabilities and achieve their goals.
