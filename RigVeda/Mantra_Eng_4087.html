आपथयो विपथयोऽन्तस्पथा अनुपथा: |एतेभिर मह्यं नामभिर यज्ञं विस्तारा ओहते ||
āpathayo vipathayo 'ntaspathā anupathāḥ | etebhir mahyaṁ nāmabhir yajñaṁ viṣṭāra ohate ||
  • Word Breakdown:
    • āpathayo: Refers to the paths or ways that are not obstructed.
    • vipathayo: Indicates the paths that are difficult or fraught with obstacles.
    • antaspathā: Represents the inner or true path.
    • anupathāḥ: Denotes the paths that are in accordance with righteousness or virtue.
    • etebhir: Means 'with these' or 'by these.'
    • mahyaṁ: Translates to 'to me' or 'for me.'
    • nāmabhir: Refers to names or identities.
    • yajñaṁ: Signifies a sacrifice or offering, often in a spiritual or ritualistic context.
    • viṣṭāra: Means 'expansion' or 'broadening.'
    • ohate: Indicates 'to be invoked' or 'to be called upon.'
  • Meaning:
    • This mantra speaks about the various paths one can take in life, highlighting both the easy and difficult routes.
    • It emphasizes the importance of choosing the right path, which aligns with inner truth and righteousness.
    • The invocation of names signifies the power of identity and intention in spiritual practices.
    • Ultimately, it calls for the expansion of one's spiritual offerings and the invocation of divine presence in those efforts.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a career context, one may face both straightforward and challenging paths. Choosing the right path that aligns with personal values can lead to fulfillment.
    • In relationships, understanding the importance of honesty and virtue can guide individuals toward healthier connections.
    • In spiritual practices, the act of chanting names or mantras can invoke a sense of peace and connection to the divine, expanding one's spiritual awareness.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder to navigate life's complexities with wisdom and integrity.
    • It encourages individuals to seek paths that resonate with their true selves and to expand their spiritual practices through intention and offerings.
    • In essence, it highlights the significance of conscious choices in shaping one's life journey and spiritual growth.
