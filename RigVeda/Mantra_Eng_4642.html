आभिः स्पृधो मिथतीर अरिषण्यन्न अमित्रस्य व्यथया मन्युम इन्द्र |आभिर विश्वा अभियुजो विषूचीर आर्याय विषो 'व तारीर दासीः ||
ābhiḥ spṛdho mithatīr ariṣaṇyann amitrasya vyathayā manyum indra | ābhir viśvā abhiyujo viṣūcīr āryāya viśo 'va tārīr dāsīḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ābhiḥ: With the help of
    • spṛdho: The one who is powerful
    • mithatīr: Together, in unity
    • ariṣaṇyann: The enemy or adversary
    • amitrasya: Of the one who is unfriendly
    • vyathayā: By distress or suffering
    • manyum: Anger or wrath
    • indra: O Indra, the king of gods
    • ābhir: With the help of
    • viśvā: All, universal
    • abhiyujo: United, connected
    • viṣūcīr: The one who is powerful
    • āryāya: For the noble or the worthy
    • viśo: The community or people
    • 'va: Or
    • tārīr: To protect or save
    • dāsīḥ: Servants or followers
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes Indra, the king of gods, asking for his assistance in overcoming adversities and enemies.
    • It emphasizes the importance of unity and collective strength in facing challenges.
    • The mention of "amitrasya vyathayā manyum" highlights the need to overcome anger and distress caused by unfriendly forces.
    • It calls for the protection of the community and the worthy individuals within it, suggesting a collective responsibility towards each other.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace setting, when a team faces competition or internal conflicts, this mantra can serve as a reminder to unite and support each other to overcome challenges.
    • In community service, when addressing social issues, the mantra encourages collective action and support for those in need, fostering a sense of responsibility towards one another.
    • In personal relationships, it can remind individuals to work together to resolve conflicts rather than allowing anger and distress to drive them apart.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for unity, strength, and collective action in the face of adversity.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal relationships to community efforts, emphasizing the importance of support and collaboration.
    • By invoking the divine assistance of Indra, it inspires individuals and communities to rise above challenges and protect one another, fostering a spirit of togetherness and resilience.
