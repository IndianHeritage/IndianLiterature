यम् ऐच्छाम मनसा सोऽयम् आगाद यज्ञस्य विद्वान् पुरुषश चिकीत्वान् |स नो यक्षद देवताता यजीयान न हि षट्सद अन्तरः पूर्वो अस्मत् ||
yam aicchāma manasā so3 'yam āgād yajñasya vidvān paruṣaś cikitvān | sa no yakṣad devatātā yajīyān ni hi ṣatsad antaraḥ pūrvo asmat ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yam: whom
    • aicchāma: we desire
    • manasā: with the mind
    • so: he
    • yam: whom
    • āgād: has come
    • yajñasya: of the sacrifice
    • vidvān: the wise one
    • paruṣaś: strong
    • cikitvān: having thought
    • sa: he
    • no: our
    • yakṣad: may grant
    • devatātā: the divine nature
    • yajīyān: worthy of worship
    • ni: indeed
    • hi: for
    • ṣatsad: six
    • antaraḥ: within
    • pūrvo: the first
    • asmat: us
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra expresses a deep desire for wisdom and guidance in the context of a sacrificial offering.
    • It emphasizes the importance of mental focus and intention in achieving one's goals.
    • The wise one, who has come to the sacrifice, is seen as a source of strength and insight.
    • The invocation of divine nature suggests a connection to higher powers that can aid in our endeavors.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, a team may gather to brainstorm ideas for a project, focusing their minds on a common goal, much like the mantra's emphasis on collective intention.
    • During personal challenges, individuals often seek guidance through meditation or prayer, aligning with the mantra's call for divine assistance.
    • In educational environments, students may invoke the wisdom of their teachers or mentors, paralleling the wise figure in the mantra who provides insight.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of intention, wisdom, and divine support in achieving our desires.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal growth to professional endeavors, reminding us to focus our minds and seek guidance.
    • Ultimately, it encourages a harmonious relationship between human effort and divine influence, fostering a sense of purpose and direction in our lives.
