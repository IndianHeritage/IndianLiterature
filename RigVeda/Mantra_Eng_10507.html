अपश्यं गोपाम अनिपद्यमानम् आ च परा च पथिभिः चरन्तम् |स सदृचिः स विषूचीर वसाना आ वरिवर्ति भुवनेष्व अन्तः ||
apaśyaṁ gopām anipadyamānam ā ca parā ca pathibhiś carantam | sa sadhrīcīḥ sa viṣūcīr vasāna ā varīvarti bhuvaneṣv antaḥ ||
  • apaśyaṁ: This word translates to "not seen" or "invisible." It signifies the unseen aspects of existence, which can relate to the deeper truths of life that are often overlooked.
  • gopām: This term refers to "the protector" or "the cowherd," symbolizing guardianship and nurturing. In real life, this can be likened to parents or mentors who guide and protect their wards.
  • anipadyamānam: This means "not having a place" or "not being established." It reflects the transient nature of life and the idea that many things in life are temporary and ever-changing.
  • ā ca parā ca: This phrase translates to "and also the other." It suggests a duality or the coexistence of different aspects of reality, emphasizing the importance of balance in life.
  • pathibhiś carantam: This means "moving along the paths." It signifies the journey of life and the various paths one can take, akin to the choices we make daily that shape our destinies.
  • sa sadhrīcīḥ: This translates to "he who is of the same nature." It indicates unity and harmony with the universe, suggesting that we are all interconnected.
  • sa viṣūcīr: This means "he who is the protector." It reinforces the idea of guardianship and the role of a protector in our lives, akin to leaders or deities who guide us.
  • vasāna: This term refers to "dwelling" or "residing." It can be interpreted as the place where one finds comfort and security, such as home or community.
  • ā varīvarti: This means "who moves in the vastness." It signifies the expansive nature of existence and the infinite possibilities that life offers.
  • bhuvaneṣv antaḥ: This translates to "within the worlds." It suggests that the essence of life and existence is found within the various realms we inhabit, both physical and spiritual.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates profound truths about existence, guardianship, and the journey of life. It emphasizes the importance of recognizing the unseen aspects of our lives, the transient nature of our experiences, and the interconnectedness of all beings. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire individuals to seek balance, embrace change, and appreciate the guidance of those who protect and nurture them. It serves as a reminder to be mindful of the paths we choose and to find comfort in the communities we build, ultimately leading to a deeper understanding of our place in the universe.
