प्रति त्वाद्य सुमनसो बुद्धन्तास्माकासो मगवाणो वयं च |तिलविलायध्वम् उषसो विभातीर यूयम् पात स्वस्तिभिः सदा नः ||
prati tvādya sumanaso budhantāsmākāso maghavāno vayaṁ ca | tilvilāyadhvam uṣaso vibhātīr yūyam pāta svastibhiḥ sadā naḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • prati: towards
    • tvādya: you (plural)
    • sumanaso: of good thoughts
    • budhantā: may we be enlightened
    • asmākāso: our (plural) existence
    • maghavāno: the powerful ones
    • vayaṁ: we
    • ca: and
    • tilvilāyadhvam: may you bestow (or grant) abundance
    • uṣaso: of the dawn
    • vibhātīr: shining
    • yūyam: you (plural)
    • pāta: protect
    • svastibhiḥ: with auspiciousness
    • sadā: always
    • naḥ: for us
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer for enlightenment and abundance.
    • It emphasizes the importance of good thoughts and the collective strength of individuals.
    • The invocation of dawn symbolizes new beginnings and the hope that comes with each day.
    • It calls upon powerful forces to protect and bless the community with auspiciousness.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace setting, this mantra can be applied during team meetings to foster a positive environment where everyone contributes good ideas.
    • In educational institutions, teachers can use this mantra to inspire students to think positively and work collaboratively towards common goals.
    • During community gatherings, this mantra can serve as a reminder of the collective responsibility to support and uplift one another.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for unity, positivity, and protection.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, such as workplaces, schools, and communities, where collective effort and good intentions are essential for success.
    • By invoking this mantra, individuals can cultivate an environment of support and auspiciousness, leading to overall well-being and prosperity.
