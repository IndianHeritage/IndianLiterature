आमूर अज प्रत्यावर्तयेमाः केतुमद दुण्डुभिर वावदीति |सम अश्वपर्णाश चरन्ति नो नरः अस्माकं इन्द्र रथिनो जयन्तु ||
āmūr aja pratyāvartayemāḥ ketumad dundubhir vāvadīti | sam aśvaparṇāś caranti no naro 'smākam indra rathino jayantu ||
  • Transliteration: āmūr aja pratyāvartayemāḥ ketumad dundubhir vāvadīti | sam aśvaparṇāś caranti no naro 'smākam indra rathino jayantu ||
  • Meaning:
    • āmūr: "We" or "us" - indicating a collective identity.
    • aja: "unborn" or "eternal" - often referring to the divine or the ultimate reality.
    • pratyāvartayemāḥ: "may we return" or "may we be restored" - a plea for rejuvenation or revival.
    • ketumad: "with banners" or "with flags" - symbolizing victory and triumph.
    • dundubhir: "with drums" - representing celebration and the announcement of victory.
    • vāvadīti: "may it sound" - a call for the proclamation of success.
    • sam: "together" - emphasizing unity.
    • aśvaparṇāḥ: "with horse wings" - symbolizing speed, power, and divine assistance.
    • caranti: "they move" or "they travel" - indicating action and progress.
    • no: "not" - a negation, often used to emphasize a positive outcome.
    • naro: "man" or "human" - referring to humanity.
    • 'smākam: "our" - indicating possession or belonging.
    • indra: "Indra" - the king of gods, representing strength and leadership.
    • rathino: "charioteers" - symbolizing those who guide and lead.
    • jayantu: "may they conquer" or "may they be victorious" - a wish for success.
  • Significance:
    • This mantra is a call for collective strength and unity, invoking the divine for guidance and support in achieving victory.
    • It emphasizes the importance of community and collaboration in overcoming challenges, much like a team working together to achieve a common goal.
    • The imagery of drums and banners signifies celebration and the acknowledgment of achievements, reminding us to celebrate our successes, both big and small.
  • Real-Life Application:
    • In a corporate setting, this mantra can be applied during team projects where collaboration and unity are essential for success. It encourages team members to work together harmoniously, much like the charioteers guiding their horses.
    • In personal life, it can serve as a reminder to seek support from friends and family during challenging times, reinforcing the idea that we are stronger together.
    • In community events, the call for celebration and acknowledgment of achievements can inspire individuals to come together and recognize collective efforts, fostering a sense of belonging and pride.
