आयामि घोष इन्द्र देवजामिर इराज्यन्त यच छुरुधो विवाचि |नहि स्वम् आयुश चिकीते जनेषु तानीद अंहांस्य अति पर्ष्य अस्मान ||
ayāmi ghoṣa indra devajāmir irajyanta yac churudho vivāci | nahi svam āyuś cikite janeṣu tānīd aṁhāṁsy ati parṣy asmān ||
  • ayāmi ghoṣa: This phrase can be translated as "the sound is coming" or "the call is approaching." It signifies the arrival of something significant, often associated with divine presence or important events.
  • indra devajāmir: "Indra" refers to the king of the gods in Vedic tradition, representing strength, power, and leadership. "Devajāmir" can be interpreted as "born of the gods," indicating a divine origin or influence.
  • irajyanta: This term suggests the idea of being energized or invigorated. It implies a sense of awakening or activation, often in a spiritual or communal context.
  • yac churudho vivāci: This phrase can be understood as "that which speaks sweetly" or "that which articulates beautifully." It emphasizes the importance of communication and expression, particularly in a harmonious manner.
  • nahi svam āyuś: Translated, this means "not my own life." It reflects the idea of selflessness or the understanding that one's existence is interconnected with others.
  • cikite janeṣu: This can be interpreted as "in the presence of people" or "among the beings." It highlights the social aspect of human life and the importance of community.
  • tānīd aṁhāṁsy: This phrase suggests "those who are strong" or "those who are powerful." It acknowledges the presence of strength and resilience in individuals or groups.
  • ati parṣy asmān: This can be translated as "beyond our reach" or "transcending us." It indicates aspirations or goals that may seem distant but are worth striving for.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates themes of divine presence, community, communication, and the interconnectedness of life. It serves as a reminder of the importance of collective strength and the pursuit of higher ideals. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as community gatherings, spiritual practices, or leadership roles, where the emphasis is on unity, selflessness, and the pursuit of common goals. It encourages individuals to recognize their place within a larger framework and to communicate effectively and harmoniously with others, fostering a sense of belonging and purpose.
