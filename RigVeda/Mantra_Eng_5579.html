यद अर्जुन सारमेय दतः पीशङ्ग यच्छसे |वीव भ्राजन्त ऋष्टय उप श्रक्वेषु बप्सतो नि षु स्वप ||
yad arjuna sārameya dataḥ piśaṅga yacchase | vīva bhrājanta ṛṣṭaya upa srakveṣu bapsato ni ṣu svapa ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yad: that which
    • arjuna: Arjuna, a character from the Mahabharata, symbolizes righteousness and valor
    • sārameya: related to the dog, often representing loyalty and protection
    • dataḥ: given or bestowed
    • piśaṅga: yellow or tawny, often associated with warmth and energy
    • yacchase: you grant or bestow
    • vīva: like or as if
    • bhrājanta: shining or radiating
    • ṛṣṭaya: the desires or aspirations
    • upa: near or close
    • srakveṣu: in the flowers or garlands
    • bapsato: of those who are seeking
    • ni: not
    • ṣu: in or among
    • svapa: sleep or rest
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks to the divine qualities of protection, loyalty, and the granting of desires.
    • It invokes the essence of Arjuna, who represents the ideal warrior, emphasizing the importance of righteousness in our pursuits.
    • The reference to the dog symbolizes loyalty and the protective nature of relationships, suggesting that true strength lies in our connections with others.
    • The imagery of shining aspirations and desires indicates that when we pursue our goals with integrity, they will flourish like flowers in a garland.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace setting, an employee who embodies the qualities of Arjuna may take on challenging projects with integrity, inspiring their colleagues to do the same.
    • In personal relationships, loyalty and protection can be seen when friends support each other during difficult times, showcasing the strength of their bond.
    • In community service, individuals who work together to fulfill the aspirations of their community reflect the essence of this mantra, as they shine together in their efforts.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of righteousness, loyalty, and the pursuit of aspirations in our lives.
    • It teaches us that by embodying these qualities, we can achieve our goals and support those around us, creating a harmonious environment.
    • In real-life scenarios, whether in personal relationships, professional settings, or community involvement, the principles outlined in this mantra can guide us towards a fulfilling and meaningful existence.
