एको बहूनाम् असि मन्यव īḻितो विषं-विषं युधये सं शिशाधि |अकृत्तरुक त्वया युजा वयं द्युमन्तं घोषं विजयाय कृण्महे ||
eko bahūnām asi manyav īḻito viśaṁ-viśaṁ yudhaye saṁ śiśādhi | akṛttaruk tvayā yujā vayaṁ dyumantaṁ ghoṣaṁ vijayāya kṛṇmahe ||
  • eko bahūnām asi: This phrase translates to "You are one among many." It emphasizes the idea of unity in diversity, suggesting that while there are many beings, there is a singular essence that connects them all. In real life, this can be seen in communities where individuals from different backgrounds come together for a common purpose, such as in a workplace or during community service.
  • manyav īḻito viśaṁ-viśaṁ: This part means "the many are united in thought." It highlights the importance of collective intention and shared goals. For example, in a team project, when all members align their thoughts and efforts towards a common objective, they are more likely to succeed.
  • yudhaye saṁ śiśādhi: This translates to "in the battle, may you be victorious." It signifies the pursuit of success and triumph over challenges. In everyday life, this can relate to personal struggles, such as overcoming obstacles in education or career, where determination and unity lead to victory.
  • akṛttaruk tvayā yujā: This means "may we be united with you." It expresses a desire for connection and collaboration. In real life, this can be seen in partnerships, whether in business or personal relationships, where working together leads to greater achievements.
  • vayaṁ dyumantaṁ ghoṣaṁ: This translates to "we create a radiant sound." It symbolizes the power of collective expression and creativity. For instance, in music bands or theatrical performances, the combined talents of individuals create something beautiful and impactful.
  • vijayāya kṛṇmahe: This means "we strive for victory." It encapsulates the essence of ambition and the pursuit of success. In real life, this can be applied to various fields, such as sports teams aiming for championships or students working hard to achieve academic excellence.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of unity, collaboration, and the pursuit of success. It teaches us that while we may be many, our collective thoughts and efforts can lead to victory in various aspects of life. This principle is applicable in numerous real-life scenarios, from teamwork in professional settings to community initiatives, emphasizing that together, we can achieve greatness. The significance of this mantra lies in its reminder that our individual strengths, when united, can create a powerful force for positive change and success.
