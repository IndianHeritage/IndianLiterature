सोमो देवो न सूर्यो 'द्रिभिः पवते सुतः |दधानाḥ कलशे रसम् ||
somo devo na sūryo 'dribhiḥ pavate sutaḥ | dadhānaḥ kalaśe rasam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • somo: Refers to 'Soma', a sacred drink in Vedic tradition, symbolizing nourishment and vitality.
    • devo: Means 'divine' or 'god', indicating a higher power or celestial being.
    • na: Translates to 'not', used for negation.
    • sūryo: Refers to 'Surya', the sun, representing light, energy, and life.
    • 'dribhiḥ: Means 'with drops' or 'with streams', indicating the essence or extract.
    • pavate: Means 'flows' or 'purifies', suggesting movement or the act of cleansing.
    • sutaḥ: Refers to 'son' or 'offspring', often used in the context of offerings or results of actions.
    • dadhānaḥ: Means 'holding' or 'possessing', indicating the act of carrying or maintaining.
    • kalaśe: Refers to 'pitcher' or 'vessel', symbolizing containment or storage.
    • rasam: Means 'essence' or 'juice', representing the vital fluid or energy.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the divine essence of Soma, which is not the sun but flows like a stream, purifying and nourishing.
    • The reference to 'Soma' highlights the importance of spiritual nourishment, akin to how we seek sustenance in our daily lives.
    • The act of 'holding' the essence in a 'pitcher' symbolizes the importance of preserving and cherishing our spiritual and emotional well-being.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern life, the concept of 'Soma' can be likened to the pursuit of mental and emotional health through practices like meditation, yoga, or mindfulness.
    • Just as the mantra emphasizes the flow of divine energy, individuals often seek to create environments that promote positivity and well-being, such as through community service or personal development.
    • The 'pitcher' can symbolize our minds and hearts, which must be filled with positive thoughts and experiences to thrive.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of spiritual nourishment and the flow of positive energy in our lives.
    • It encourages individuals to seek out and preserve the essence of life, much like how we care for our physical health.
    • In real-life scenarios, this can be applied through self-care practices, fostering relationships, and engaging in activities that uplift the spirit.
