आ नो याह्य उपश्रुत्य उक्तेषु रणया इह |दिवो अमुष्य शासतो दिवं यया दिवावसो ||
ā no yāhy upaśruty uktheṣu raṇayā iha | divo amuṣya śāsato divaṁ yaya divāvaso ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā no: "May we" or "Let us"
    • yāhy: "be led" or "be guided"
    • upaśruty: "by listening" or "by hearing"
    • uktheṣu: "in the utterances" or "in the words spoken"
    • raṇayā: "with strength" or "with vigor"
    • iha: "here" or "in this world"
    • divo: "of the heavens" or "of the divine"
    • amuṣya: "of that" or "of this"
    • śāsato: "commanding" or "governing"
    • divaṁ: "heaven" or "the celestial realm"
    • yaya: "by which" or "through which"
    • divāvaso: "the one who dwells in the day" or "the daytime being"
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra expresses a desire for guidance and strength in our actions and thoughts.
    • It emphasizes the importance of listening to wise words and teachings, which can lead us to a more fulfilling life.
    • The reference to "the heavens" suggests a connection to the divine or higher powers that govern our existence.
    • It encourages us to seek knowledge and wisdom that can help us navigate our lives effectively.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, this mantra can be applied when seeking guidance from mentors or leaders. Listening to their experiences can provide valuable insights that help in making informed decisions.
    • In personal relationships, being open to communication and understanding can strengthen bonds. Listening to each other’s perspectives can lead to more harmonious interactions.
    • In spiritual practices, this mantra can be a reminder to seek divine guidance through prayer or meditation, allowing one to connect with a higher purpose.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for guidance and strength through wisdom and divine connection.
    • It is applicable in various aspects of life, including personal growth, professional development, and spiritual exploration.
    • By embodying the principles of listening and seeking knowledge, individuals can navigate their lives with greater clarity and purpose.
