अस्माकं इन्द्र भूत्व ते स्तोमो वाहिष्ठो अन्तमः |अस्मान् राये महे हिनु ||
asmākam indra bhūtu te stomo vāhiṣṭho antamaḥ | asmān rāye mahe hinu ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • asmākam: our
    • indra: Indra, the king of gods, associated with strength and power
    • bhūtu: may become or manifest
    • te: your
    • stomo: praise or hymn
    • vāhiṣṭho: the best or most excellent
    • antamaḥ: the ultimate or the highest
    • asmān: us
    • rāye: wealth or prosperity
    • mahe: great
    • hinu: may we attain or achieve
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Indra, seeking his blessings for strength and prosperity.
    • It emphasizes the importance of collective effort and unity in achieving greatness.
    • The phrase "may we attain great wealth" reflects a desire for abundance, not just in material terms but also in spiritual and emotional well-being.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, a team may recite this mantra before a major project to invoke unity and strength, aiming for success and prosperity in their endeavors.
    • During community gatherings, this mantra can be used to inspire collective action towards common goals, such as charity work or environmental initiatives.
    • In personal life, individuals may reflect on this mantra when setting goals, reminding themselves of the importance of seeking support from others and working together for mutual benefit.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for unity, strength, and the pursuit of prosperity.
    • It serves as a reminder that individual success is often intertwined with the collective efforts of a community.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, from personal aspirations to professional teamwork, highlighting the importance of collaboration and shared goals.
