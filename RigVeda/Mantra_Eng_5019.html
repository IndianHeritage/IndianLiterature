तां नव्यसो जरामाणस्य मनमोपा भूषतो युयुजांसप्ती |शुभं पृक्षं इषम ऊर्जं वहन्ता होतā यक्षत प्रत्नो अध्रुग युवाना ||
tā navyaso jaramāṇasya manmopa bhūṣato yuyujānasaptī | śubham pṛkṣam iṣam ūrjaṁ vahantā hotā yakṣat pratno adhrug yuvānā ||
  • tā navyaso jaramāṇasya: This phrase can be interpreted as "those who are new and fresh, yet also aged." It suggests a duality of youth and experience, emphasizing the importance of both qualities in life.
  • manmopa bhūṣato: This translates to "may they adorn my mind." It indicates a desire for wisdom and knowledge to enhance one's mental state, akin to how jewelry adorns the body.
  • yuyujānasaptī: This can be understood as "the seven who are united." The number seven often symbolizes completeness and harmony in various cultures, representing the idea of collaboration and unity.
  • śubham pṛkṣam: This means "auspicious sight." It refers to the importance of perceiving positivity and good fortune in our surroundings, which can influence our mindset and actions.
  • iṣam ūrjaṁ: This translates to "the energy of the will." It emphasizes the power of intention and determination in achieving one's goals, similar to how a strong will can drive a person to success.
  • vahantā hotā: This phrase means "the one who carries the offering." It signifies the act of giving and sharing, which is essential in building relationships and communities.
  • yakṣat pratno adhrug yuvānā: This can be interpreted as "the young who are not hindered by obstacles." It highlights the importance of resilience and the ability to overcome challenges, much like young individuals who pursue their dreams despite difficulties.

Conclusion: This mantra from the RigVeda encapsulates the essence of balance between youth and experience, the importance of wisdom, unity, positivity, determination, generosity, and resilience. In real life, these principles can be applied in various scenarios, such as in teamwork, personal development, and community building. For instance, in a workplace, fostering a culture that values both fresh ideas from younger employees and the insights of seasoned professionals can lead to innovative solutions. Similarly, in personal relationships, embracing a mindset of positivity and resilience can help individuals navigate life's challenges more effectively. Overall, this mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of these qualities and their significance in leading a fulfilling life.
