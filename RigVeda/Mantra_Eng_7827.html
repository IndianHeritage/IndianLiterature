एष वसूनि पिब्दना परुषा ययिवाम् अति |अव शादेषु गच्छति ||
eṣa vasūni pibdanā paruṣā yayivām̐ ati | ava śādeṣu gacchati ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • eṣa: this
    • vasūni: wealth or riches
    • pibdanā: drinking or consuming
    • paruṣā: harsh or difficult
    • yayivām̐: one who moves or travels
    • ati: exceedingly or very
    • ava: down or away
    • śādeṣu: in the realms of peace or tranquility
    • gacchati: goes or moves
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the consumption of wealth and the journey through life's challenges.
    • The phrase "this wealth" suggests a focus on material and spiritual riches that one can attain.
    • "Drinking" symbolizes the act of fully engaging with these riches, whether they are experiences, knowledge, or material possessions.
    • The term "harsh" indicates that the path to achieving these riches may not always be easy; it may involve struggles and difficulties.
    • The phrase "one who moves exceedingly" implies that those who actively seek and strive for wealth will find their way, even through adversity.
    • Finally, "goes down to realms of peace" suggests that after navigating through challenges, one can find tranquility and fulfillment.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional context, an individual may face numerous challenges while climbing the corporate ladder. The mantra reflects the idea that through hard work and perseverance, they can achieve success and eventually find satisfaction in their career.
    • In personal development, someone may struggle with self-doubt and obstacles while pursuing their goals. The mantra encourages them to embrace these challenges as part of their journey toward personal growth and inner peace.
    • In financial matters, individuals may experience hardships while trying to build wealth. The mantra serves as a reminder that enduring these difficulties can lead to eventual prosperity and stability.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of resilience and determination in the pursuit of wealth and fulfillment.
    • It teaches that while the journey may be fraught with challenges, the rewards of perseverance are significant.
    • In real life, this mantra can be applied to various aspects, including career, personal growth, and financial stability, encouraging individuals to embrace their struggles as stepping stones to success.
