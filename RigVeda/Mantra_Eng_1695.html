मां त्वाग्निर्ध्वनयीद्धूमगन्धिर्मोखाभ्राजन्त्यभिविक्तजघ्रिः |इष्टंवीतमभिगूर्तंवषट्कृतं तं देवासः प्रतिगृभ्णन्त्यश्वम् ||
mā tvāgnir dhvanayīd dhūmagandhir mokhā bhrājanty abhi vikta jaghriḥ | iṣṭaṁ vītam abhigūrtaṁ vaṣaṭkṛtaṁ taṁ devāsaḥ prati gṛbhṇanty aśvam ||
  • mā tvāgnir dhvanayīd: This phrase can be translated as "May the fire (Agni) not call you." Here, Agni represents the divine fire, which is often associated with purification and sacrifice in Vedic rituals. In real life, this can symbolize the need to avoid negative influences or distractions that can lead one away from their true purpose.
  • dhūmagandhir mokhā bhrājanty abhi vikta jaghriḥ: This part translates to "The smoke-scented (fire) does not lead to liberation; it is the one that is clearly visible." This suggests that while some paths may seem appealing (like the smoke of a fire), they may not lead to true freedom or enlightenment. In everyday life, this can be likened to the allure of material possessions or superficial success, which often distracts individuals from their spiritual or personal growth.
  • iṣṭaṁ vītam abhigūrtaṁ vaṣaṭkṛtaṁ: This translates to "The desired (offering) is made, and the invocation is completed." This indicates the importance of intention and effort in achieving one's goals. In practical terms, this can be seen in various aspects of life, such as setting clear objectives in a career or personal life and working diligently towards them.
  • taṁ devāsaḥ prati gṛbhṇanty aśvam: This means "The gods accept the offering of the horse." In Vedic culture, the horse symbolizes strength, power, and the ability to overcome obstacles. This can be interpreted in modern life as the recognition of hard work and dedication, where one's efforts are acknowledged and rewarded, much like how the gods accept offerings.

  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of discernment in one's spiritual journey. It teaches that not all that glitters is gold; one must be cautious of distractions that may lead away from true liberation. The significance of intention and effort in achieving goals is highlighted, along with the idea that genuine dedication will be recognized and rewarded. In real life, this mantra can serve as a reminder to focus on what truly matters, to set clear intentions, and to work towards them with sincerity, ensuring that one's path leads to meaningful fulfillment rather than superficial success.
