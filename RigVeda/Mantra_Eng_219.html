अभि नो देवीर अवसा महः शर्मणा नृपत्नीः |अच्छिन्नपत्राः सचन्ताम् ||
abhi no devīr avasā mahaḥ śarmaṇā nṛpatnīḥ | acchinnapatrāḥ sacantām ||
  • Mantra: "abhi no devīr avasā mahaḥ śarmaṇā nṛpatnīḥ | acchinnapatrāḥ sacantām ||"
  • Translation: "May the goddesses grant us great protection and security, O noble ones; may we be free from obstacles and may we attain our goals."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • abhi: towards, for us
    • no: our
    • devīr: goddesses
    • avasā: grant, bestow
    • mahaḥ: great, immense
    • śarmaṇā: protection, security
    • nṛpatnīḥ: noble ones, leaders
    • acchinnapatrāḥ: free from obstacles
    • sacantām: may we attain, may we achieve
  • Context and Significance:
    • This mantra is a prayer for divine protection and guidance. It acknowledges the role of goddesses in providing security and overcoming challenges.
    • In real life, this can be related to seeking support from mentors, leaders, or spiritual guides who help navigate through difficult situations.
    • For example, a student preparing for exams may recite this mantra to invoke focus and clarity, hoping to overcome the obstacles of stress and distraction.
    • In a corporate setting, a team may use this mantra to foster unity and strength, aiming for collective success while facing market challenges.
  • Conclusion:
    • The mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking divine assistance in achieving goals and overcoming difficulties.
    • It serves as a reminder that we are not alone in our struggles; support can come from both the divine and the community around us.
    • In daily life, this mantra can be a source of motivation, encouraging individuals to remain resilient and focused on their aspirations.
