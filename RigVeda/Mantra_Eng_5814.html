कृतं नो यज्ञं विदथेṣu चारुं कृतम् ब्रह्माणि सूरिषु प्रशस्तā | उपो रयिर देवजूतो न एतु प्र णः स्पार्हाभिर ऊतिभिस तिरेतम ||
kṛtaṁ no yajñaṁ vidatheṣu cāruṁ kṛtam brahmāṇi sūriṣu praśastā | upo rayir devajūto na etu pra ṇaḥ spārhābhir ūtibhis tiretam ||
  • kṛtaṁ no yajñaṁ vidatheṣu cāruṁ: This phrase translates to "the sacrifice we have performed is beautiful in the gatherings." Here, "yajña" refers to a ritual or sacrifice, which is a central theme in Vedic literature. In real life, this can be likened to any communal effort or project that brings people together for a common purpose, such as community service or environmental clean-up drives.
  • kṛtam brahmāṇi sūriṣu praśastā: This means "the actions performed are praised among the wise." It emphasizes the importance of actions that are commendable and recognized by those who are knowledgeable. For example, a scientist's groundbreaking research may be celebrated in academic circles, highlighting the value of contributions that benefit society.
  • upo rayir devajūto na etu: This translates to "may the divine light not depart from us." It signifies a plea for divine guidance and support. In everyday life, this can be seen in the way people seek inspiration and strength from their beliefs or mentors during challenging times, such as students looking for guidance from teachers or individuals seeking support from spiritual leaders.
  • pra ṇaḥ spārhābhir ūtibhis tiretam: This means "may our aspirations be fulfilled through our efforts." It reflects the idea that hard work and dedication lead to the realization of one's goals. In practical terms, this can be observed in the journey of athletes who train rigorously to achieve their dreams, or entrepreneurs who invest time and resources to build successful businesses.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of collective effort, recognition of good deeds, the importance of divine guidance, and the fulfillment of aspirations through hard work. Its significance lies in its applicability to various aspects of life, from community initiatives to personal ambitions. By embodying these principles, individuals and communities can strive for a harmonious and prosperous existence, fostering a sense of unity and purpose in their endeavors.
