कदु द्युम्नं इन्द्र त्वावतो नृणं कया धियā करसे कन न आगन |मित्रो न सत्य उरुगाय भृत्या अन्ने समस्य यद आसन मनीषाः ||
kad u dyumnam indra tvāvato nṝn kayā dhiyā karase kan na āgan | mitro na satya urugāya bhṛtyā anne samasya yad asan manīṣāḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • kad: when
    • u: indeed
    • dyumnam: glory or fame
    • indra: O Indra (the king of gods)
    • tvāvato: you who are powerful
    • nṝn: for men
    • kayā: with what
    • dhiyā: intellect or wisdom
    • karase: do you perform
    • kan: what
    • na: not
    • āgan: come
    • mitro: O friend
    • na: not
    • satya: truth
    • urugāya: of the wide-ranging
    • bhṛtyā: servant or devotee
    • anne: in food
    • samasya: in harmony
    • yad: that
    • asan: is
    • manīṣāḥ: wisdom or thought
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra addresses Indra, the deity representing strength and power, and seeks to understand how he, with his wisdom, can help humanity.
    • The phrase "with what intellect do you perform" suggests a quest for knowledge and understanding in our actions.
    • The mention of "O friend" emphasizes the importance of companionship and truth in our endeavors.
    • The reference to "food" and "harmony" indicates that sustenance and unity are essential for wisdom and prosperity.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace, when teams collaborate effectively, they embody the essence of this mantra by combining their strengths and wisdom to achieve common goals.
    • In personal relationships, the importance of truth and support among friends reflects the mantra's call for harmony and mutual respect.
    • In community service, when individuals come together to help those in need, they demonstrate the principles of sharing resources (food) and working in unity (harmony).
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of seeking wisdom and truth in our actions, emphasizing the role of collaboration and harmony in achieving success.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal relationships to professional environments, highlighting that collective effort and integrity lead to greater outcomes.
    • Ultimately, it encourages individuals to reflect on their intentions and actions, fostering a sense of community and shared purpose.
