वृषा वि जजñe जनयन्न अमर्त्यः प्रतपञ्ज ज्योतिषा तमः |स सुष्टुतः कविभिर निर्णिजं दधे त्रिधात्व अस्य दंशसा ||
vṛṣā vi jajñe janayann amartyaḥ pratapañ jyotiṣā tamaḥ | sa suṣṭutaḥ kavibhir nirṇijaṁ dadhe tridhātv asya daṁsasā ||
  • vṛṣā vi jajñe: This phrase can be translated as "the powerful one was born." Here, "vṛṣā" refers to strength or power, and "jajñe" means "was born." This signifies the emergence of a force or entity that possesses great strength.
  • janayann amartyaḥ: This translates to "giving birth to the immortal." "Janayann" means "giving birth," and "amartyaḥ" refers to the immortal or divine beings. This suggests the creation of something eternal or beyond the mortal realm.
  • pratapañ jyotiṣā tamaḥ: This can be interpreted as "illuminating the darkness with brilliance." "Pratapañ" means "to shine" or "to illuminate," "jyotiṣā" refers to light or brilliance, and "tamaḥ" means darkness. This indicates the power of light to dispel ignorance or negativity.
  • sa suṣṭutaḥ: This means "he who is well praised." It implies that the entity being referred to is worthy of admiration and respect, often due to its qualities or achievements.
  • kavibhir nirṇijaṁ: This translates to "the seers have determined." "Kavibhir" refers to wise or enlightened beings, and "nirṇijaṁ" means "determined" or "decided." This suggests that the insights of wise individuals have shaped the understanding of this powerful entity.
  • dadhe tridhātv asya daṁsasā: This can be interpreted as "he established the three realms with his strength." "Dadhe" means "established," "tridhātv" refers to the three realms (often interpreted as physical, mental, and spiritual), and "daṁsasā" means "with strength." This indicates the foundational role of this entity in creating and maintaining the universe.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda encapsulates the essence of creation, strength, and illumination. It signifies the birth of a powerful, immortal force that brings light to darkness and is revered by wise beings. In real life, this can be applied to the pursuit of knowledge and enlightenment, where individuals strive to overcome ignorance and negativity through wisdom and strength. The mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of seeking truth and understanding, as well as the role of powerful forces in shaping our existence. It encourages us to recognize and honor the light within ourselves and others, fostering a sense of unity and purpose in our lives.
