तां जुषस्व गिरं मम वाजयन्तीम् अवा धियम् |वधूयुर इव योषणाम् ||
tāṁ juṣasva giram mama vājayantīm avā dhiyam | vadhūyur iva yoṣaṇām ||
  • tāṁ juṣasva: This phrase translates to "May you enjoy" or "May you take delight in." It signifies a call to appreciate or embrace something, often referring to the divine or a higher power.
  • giram mama: This means "my words" or "my speech." It emphasizes the importance of communication and expression, suggesting that the speaker's words hold significance and power.
  • vājayantīm: This term can be interpreted as "victorious" or "powerful." It often relates to strength, success, and the ability to overcome challenges.
  • avā dhiyam: This translates to "protect the intellect" or "guard the understanding." It highlights the need for wisdom and clarity in thought, urging the listener to safeguard their mental faculties.
  • vadhūyur iva yoṣaṇām: This phrase means "like the bride among women." It draws a comparison to the revered status of a bride, symbolizing beauty, grace, and the importance of nurturing relationships.

In essence, this mantra is a prayer or invocation that seeks to inspire appreciation for one's words, the pursuit of victory, and the protection of wisdom, all while drawing a parallel to the cherished qualities of a bride. It encourages individuals to value their communication, strive for success, and maintain clarity of thought.


Real-Life Application:

  • In personal relationships, the mantra can remind individuals to communicate openly and positively, fostering understanding and connection.
  • In professional settings, it encourages individuals to express their ideas confidently and strive for success while maintaining a clear and focused mindset.
  • In educational contexts, it emphasizes the importance of protecting one's intellect and nurturing a love for learning, akin to the care given to a bride.

Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its holistic approach to life, emphasizing the importance of communication, success, and wisdom. It serves as a reminder to appreciate the beauty in our words and relationships, protect our intellect, and strive for victory in our endeavors. By embodying these principles, individuals can lead more fulfilling and meaningful lives.
