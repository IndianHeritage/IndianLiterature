ये अग्नयो न शोशुचनिधाना द्विर्यत त्रिरमरुतो वावृद्धन्त। अरेणवो हिरण्ययास एषां साकं नृम्णैः पौंस्येभिः च भुवन॥
ye agnayo na śośucann idhānā dvir yat trir maruto vāvṛdhanta | areṇavo hiraṇyayāsa eṣāṁ sākaṁ nṛmṇaiḥ pauṁsyebhiś ca bhūvan ||
  • Translation: "Those who do not burn in the fire of Agni, who are not consumed, may they, together with the Maruts, bring forth wealth and prosperity for us."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • ye: Those who
    • agnayo: In the fire (Agni)
    • na: Not
    • śośucan: Burned or consumed
    • idhānā: Here, in this place
    • dvir: Twice
    • yat: That which
    • trir: Thrice
    • maruto: The Maruts (storm deities)
    • vāvṛdhanta: May they bring forth
    • areṇavo: With wealth
    • hiraṇyayāsa: Golden or prosperous
    • eṣāṁ: Of these
    • sākaṁ: Together with
    • nṛmṇaiḥ: With men
    • pauṁsyebhiś: With the strength of men
    • ca: And
    • bhūvan: The worlds or realms
  • Contextual Meaning:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of resilience and strength in the face of challenges, symbolized by the fire of Agni.
    • The Maruts represent the forces of nature and vitality, suggesting that collaboration with these forces can lead to prosperity.
    • The mantra calls for the blessings of wealth and abundance, highlighting the interconnectedness of human effort and divine support.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be interpreted as a call for teamwork and collaboration. Just as the Maruts work together to bring rain and fertility to the earth, individuals in a company can achieve greater success by working together harmoniously.
    • In personal life, it can serve as a reminder to remain steadfast and resilient during difficult times, much like how fire can purify and strengthen materials rather than merely destroy them.
    • In community settings, it encourages collective efforts towards prosperity, such as community service or cooperative projects that aim to uplift the entire community.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of resilience, collaboration, and the pursuit of prosperity through collective efforts.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal challenges to professional teamwork and community initiatives.
    • By invoking the blessings of Agni and the Maruts, individuals and communities can aspire to achieve wealth and success while remaining grounded and united.
