एषा स्या युजानां पराकात् पञ्च क्षितिः परि सद्यो जिगाति |अभिपश्यन्ती वयुनां जनानां दिवो दुहिता भुवनस्य पत्नी ||
eṣā syā yujānā parākāt pañca kṣitīḥ pari sadyo jigāti | abhipaśyantī vayunā janānāṁ divo duhitā bhuvanasya patnī ||
  • eṣā syā yujānā parākāt: This phrase can be translated as "This is the one who connects us from afar." It signifies the importance of unity and connection among people, even when they are physically distant. In real life, this can be seen in the way technology, such as social media and communication tools, allows individuals to maintain relationships across great distances.
  • pañca kṣitīḥ pari sadyo jigāti: This translates to "The five realms are immediately present." The five realms can be interpreted as the various aspects of existence—physical, emotional, mental, spiritual, and social. This line emphasizes the interconnectedness of these realms in our lives. For example, a person's mental health can significantly affect their physical health, illustrating the need for a holistic approach to well-being.
  • abhipaśyantī vayunā janānāṁ: This means "The one who sees the people with wisdom." It highlights the importance of wisdom and insight in understanding and guiding others. In a real-life context, leaders and mentors who possess wisdom can inspire and uplift those around them, fostering a supportive community.
  • divo duhitā bhuvanasya patnī: This translates to "The daughter of the sky, the mistress of the world." This line can be interpreted as a representation of divine feminine energy, symbolizing nurturing, creativity, and the power of nature. In everyday life, this can be seen in the role of women as caregivers, leaders, and innovators who shape the world around them.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of connection, unity, wisdom, and the divine feminine. Its significance lies in the reminder that we are all interconnected, and our actions in one realm can impact others. In real life, this mantra encourages us to foster relationships, seek wisdom, and honor the nurturing aspects of life. It is applicable in various scenarios, such as community building, leadership, and personal development, urging us to recognize our roles in the larger tapestry of existence.
