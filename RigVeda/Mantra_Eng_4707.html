मन्त्र: कर्हि स्वित तद इन्द्र यज जारित्रे विश्वाप्सु ब्रह्म कृत्नवः शविष्ठा |कदā धियः न नियुतो युवासे कदā गोमघा हवनानि गच्छाः ||
karhi svit tad indra yaj jaritre viśvapsu brahma kṛṇavaḥ śaviṣṭha | kadā dhiyo na niyuto yuvāse kadā gomaghā havanāni gacchāḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • karhi: When
    • svit: indeed
    • tad: that
    • indra: O Indra (the king of gods)
    • yaj: sacrifice
    • jaritre: for the one who protects
    • viśvapsu: in all waters
    • brahma: the ultimate reality or cosmic order
    • kṛṇavaḥ: we create
    • śaviṣṭha: the most powerful
    • kadā: when
    • dhiyo: intellects
    • na: not
    • niyuto: restrained
    • yuvāse: to you (O Indra)
    • kadā: when
    • gomaghā: the one who is a great benefactor
    • havanāni: offerings
    • gacchāḥ: may go
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Indra, the god of rain and thunderstorms, asking for his blessings and protection.
    • The speaker expresses a desire for the creation of a powerful and beneficial reality that supports all beings.
    • It reflects a yearning for the intellects of people to be free and unrestrained, allowing them to connect with the divine.
    • The mention of offerings signifies the importance of rituals and sacrifices in seeking divine favor.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In agriculture, farmers pray to Indra for rain, which is essential for their crops. This mantra can be recited during rituals to invoke his blessings.
    • In educational settings, students can relate to the desire for unrestrained intellects, seeking knowledge and wisdom without limitations.
    • During community gatherings, this mantra can be used to foster unity and collective efforts towards a common goal, emphasizing the importance of collaboration and support.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of divine connection, the importance of intellect, and the role of offerings in spiritual practices.
    • It serves as a reminder of the need for protection and guidance from higher powers in our endeavors.
    • In real life, it encourages individuals and communities to seek harmony, support one another, and strive for a greater understanding of the cosmic order.
