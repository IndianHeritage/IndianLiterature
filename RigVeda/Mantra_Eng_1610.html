आ वाम भूषण क्षितयो जन्म रोडस्योः प्रवाच्यं वृषणा दक्षसे महे |यद ईं ऋताय भरतो यद अर्वते प्र होत्र्याः शिम्या वीथो अध्वरम् ||
ā vām bhūṣan kṣitayo janma rodasyoḥ pravācyaṁ vṛṣaṇā dakṣase mahe | yad īm ṛtāya bharatho yad arvate pra hotrayā śimyā vītho adhvaram ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā vām: From us, to you
    • bhūṣan: Adornments or decorations
    • kṣitayo: Of the earth
    • janma: Birth or origin
    • rodasyoḥ: Of the two worlds (heaven and earth)
    • pravācyaṁ: That which is spoken or declared
    • vṛṣaṇā: By the strong or powerful
    • dakṣase: To the capable or skilled
    • mahe: Great or mighty
    • yad: That which
    • īm: We desire or seek
    • ṛtāya: For truth or righteousness
    • bharatho: The one who is strong or powerful
    • yad: That which
    • arvate: Is worthy or deserving
    • pra hotrayā: By the priest or the one who invokes
    • śimyā: By the one who is wise or knowledgeable
    • vītho: The path or way
    • adhvaram: The ritual or sacrifice
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the connection between the earthly and the divine, emphasizing the importance of skilled and capable individuals in performing rituals that uphold truth and righteousness.
    • It highlights the significance of adornments, both physical and spiritual, that enhance our existence and the world around us.
    • The invocation of strength and wisdom suggests that true power lies in the ability to act justly and with knowledge.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, a leader who embodies the qualities of strength and wisdom can inspire their team to achieve great results while maintaining ethical standards.
    • In personal relationships, individuals who communicate openly and truthfully create a strong foundation for trust and mutual respect.
    • During community rituals or gatherings, the involvement of knowledgeable individuals can enhance the experience, making it more meaningful and impactful.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for individuals to embody strength, wisdom, and righteousness in their actions.
    • It serves as a reminder that our words and deeds have the power to connect the earthly with the divine, creating a harmonious existence.
    • In real life, applying the principles of this mantra can lead to personal growth, stronger relationships, and a more just society.
