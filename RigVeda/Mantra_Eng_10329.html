मन्त्र: उता वा उ परि वृणक्षि बप्सद बहोर अग्न उलपस्य स्वधावः |उता खिल्या उर्वराणाम् भवन्ति मा ते हेतिं तविषीं चुकृधाम ||
uta vā u pari vṛṇakṣi bapsad bahor agna ulapasya svadhāvaḥ | uta khilyā urvarāṇām bhavanti mā te hetiṁ taviṣīṁ cukrudhāma ||
  • uta vā u pari vṛṇakṣi: This phrase can be translated as "whether you choose or not." It reflects the idea of choice and decision-making in life. In real life, we often face situations where we must make choices that can significantly impact our future, such as career paths, relationships, and personal beliefs.
  • bapsad bahor agna ulapasya svadhāvaḥ: This part refers to the "many forms of the divine fire" and the "self-existent." Fire is often seen as a symbol of transformation and purification. In our lives, we encounter challenges that can be likened to fire, which can either destroy or purify us, depending on how we respond to them. For example, a difficult experience can lead to personal growth and resilience.
  • uta khilyā urvarāṇām: This translates to "whether the fruits of the earth are plentiful." It emphasizes abundance and prosperity. In a practical sense, this can relate to agricultural success, economic stability, or even emotional fulfillment. For instance, a community that works together to cultivate its resources can experience shared prosperity.
  • bhavanti mā te hetiṁ taviṣīṁ cukrudhāma: This phrase can be interpreted as "may you not be hindered in your endeavors." It speaks to the importance of perseverance and support in achieving our goals. In real life, this can be seen in the context of teamwork and collaboration, where individuals support each other to overcome obstacles and achieve common objectives.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates themes of choice, transformation, abundance, and perseverance. Its significance lies in the reminder that we have the power to make choices that shape our lives, that challenges can lead to growth, and that support from others is crucial in overcoming difficulties. In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as personal development, community building, and professional endeavors, encouraging individuals to embrace their agency and work collaboratively towards a prosperous future.
