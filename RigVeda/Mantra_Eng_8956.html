आपो अस्मान मातरः शुन्धयन्तु घृतेन नो घृतप्वः पुनन्तु |विश्वं हि रिप्रम प्रवहन्ति देवीर उद इदाभ्यः शुचिरा पूता एमी ||
āpo asmān mātaraḥ śundhayantu ghṛtena no ghṛtapvaḥ punantu | viśvaṁ hi ripram pravahanti devīr ud id ābhyaḥ śucir ā pūta emi ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • āpo: Waters
    • asmān: Us
    • mātaraḥ: Mothers
    • śundhayantu: May they purify
    • ghṛtena: With ghee
    • no: For us
    • ghṛtapvaḥ: Those who are rich in ghee
    • punantu: May they purify
    • viśvaṁ: All
    • hi: Indeed
    • ripram: Enemies
    • pravahanti: Flow away
    • devīr: Goddesses
    • ud: From
    • id: Here
    • ābhyaḥ: From all sides
    • śucir: Pure
    • ā: To
    • pūta: Clean
    • emi: I come
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the purifying qualities of water, often regarded as a motherly figure in Vedic literature.
    • It emphasizes the importance of purity and cleanliness, both physically and spiritually.
    • The reference to ghee signifies nourishment and the sacredness associated with it in rituals.
    • The mantra seeks the blessings of divine forces (goddesses) to remove negativity and obstacles (enemies) from our lives.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, this mantra can be related to the practice of cleansing rituals before starting any significant task, such as a new job or a marriage.
    • It can also be applied in the context of mental and emotional purification, where individuals seek to let go of negative thoughts and feelings.
    • In environmental contexts, the emphasis on water can remind us of the importance of clean water and its role in sustaining life.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for purification and the removal of negativity, which is essential for personal growth and harmony.
    • It serves as a reminder to seek purity in our thoughts, actions, and surroundings, fostering a holistic approach to life.
    • Incorporating this mantra into daily practices can enhance mindfulness and promote a sense of well-being, making it applicable in various life scenarios.
