अस्मे वर्षिष्ठा कृणुहि ज्येष्ठा नृम्णानि सत्रा सहुरे सहांसि |अस्मभ्यं वृत्रा सुहनानि रन्धि जहि वधार वनुषो मार्त्यस्य ||
asme varṣiṣṭhā kṛṇuhi jyeṣṭhā nṛmṇāni satrā sahure sahāṁsi | asmabhyaṁ vṛtrā suhanāni randhi jahi vadhar vanuṣo martyasya ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • asme: for us
    • varṣiṣṭhā: the best or most excellent
    • kṛṇuhi: make or create
    • jyeṣṭhā: the elder or the most powerful
    • nṛmṇāni: the human beings or men
    • satrā: the sacred or the divine
    • sahure: to be strong or powerful
    • sahāṁsi: to be together or united
    • asmabhyaṁ: for us
    • vṛtrā: the obstacles or enemies
    • suhanāni: the good or favorable
    • randhi: to cut or remove
    • jahi: to defeat or conquer
    • vadhar: the evil or harmful
    • vanuṣo: of the mortals or humans
    • martyasya: of the mortal beings
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer for strength and protection. It calls upon the divine to create the best conditions for humanity.
    • The phrase "asme varṣiṣṭhā" emphasizes the desire for excellence and abundance in life.
    • By invoking "jyeṣṭhā nṛmṇāni," it acknowledges the power of the elder or more experienced beings, suggesting respect for wisdom and guidance.
    • The mantra seeks unity and strength among people, as indicated by "sahure sahāṁsi," highlighting the importance of community and collaboration.
    • It also addresses the removal of obstacles ("vṛtrā suhanāni randhi"), indicating a desire to overcome challenges and adversities in life.
    • The call to "jahi vadhar vanuṣo martyasya" signifies a plea to conquer evil and harmful influences that affect human existence.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace setting, this mantra can be applied when teams face challenges. By fostering unity and collaboration, they can overcome obstacles and achieve excellence.
    • In personal life, individuals can use this mantra as a reminder to seek guidance from elders and experienced individuals when making important decisions.
    • In community initiatives, this mantra can inspire collective efforts to address social issues, emphasizing the importance of working together for a common good.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for strength, unity, and the overcoming of obstacles. It serves as a reminder of the power of community and the importance of seeking wisdom from those who are more experienced.
    • In real-life scenarios, it encourages individuals and groups to work together, respect each other, and strive for excellence while facing challenges head-on.
    • Ultimately, this mantra embodies the essence of resilience and the human spirit's ability to rise above difficulties through collaboration and strength.
