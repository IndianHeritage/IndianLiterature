अमन्दान स्तोमान प्र भरे मनिषा सिन्धाव अधि क्षीयतो भाव्यस्य |यो मे सहस्रं अमिमीत सवान अतूर्तो राजा श्रव इच्छमानः ||
amandān stomān pra bhare manīṣā sindhāv adhi kṣiyato bhāvyasya | yo me sahasram amimīta savān atūrto rājā śrava icchamānaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • amandān: Unbounded, limitless
    • stomān: Praise, hymn
    • pra bhare: May I receive, may I be filled
    • manīṣā: Wisdom, intellect
    • sindhāv: Oceans, vastness
    • adhi: Above, over
    • kṣiyato: Diminishing, reducing
    • bhāvyasya: Of the future, what is to come
    • yo: Who
    • me: My
    • sahasram: Thousand
    • amimīta: Unmeasured, infinite
    • savān: Offerings, sacrifices
    • atūrto: Without delay, swiftly
    • rājā: King, ruler
    • śrava: Fame, glory
    • icchamānaḥ: Desiring, wishing
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra expresses a desire for limitless wisdom and the ability to receive praise and offerings.
    • The reference to "oceans" symbolizes vast knowledge and experiences that one can draw from.
    • The phrase "who my thousand unmeasured offerings" indicates a yearning for recognition and the fulfillment of one's potential.
    • It emphasizes the importance of seeking wisdom and understanding in the face of life's challenges.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • A student preparing for exams may recite this mantra to invoke clarity of thought and wisdom, seeking to absorb knowledge like the vastness of the ocean.
    • A leader in a community may reflect on this mantra to inspire themselves to seek wisdom and make decisions that benefit the future of their people.
    • In a corporate setting, an employee may use this mantra to remind themselves of the importance of innovation and creativity in their work, aiming for recognition and success.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the pursuit of wisdom and the importance of being open to receiving knowledge and praise.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from education to leadership, where the quest for understanding and recognition is vital.
    • By embodying the essence of this mantra, individuals can strive for greatness and contribute positively to their surroundings.
