प्रपतहे पथाम् अजनिष्ट पूषा प्रपतहे दिवः प्रपतहे पृथिव्याः |उभे अभि प्रियतम सधस्थे आ च परा च चरति प्रजानन् ||
prapathe pathām ajaniṣṭa pūṣā prapathe divaḥ prapathe pṛthivyāḥ | ubhe abhi priyatame sadhasthe ā ca parā ca carati prajānan ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • prapathe: To the path or way
    • pathām: Of the paths
    • ajaniṣṭa: Has not been obstructed or hindered
    • pūṣā: By the nourisher or sustainer
    • divaḥ: Of the heavens
    • pṛthivyāḥ: Of the earth
    • ubhe: Both
    • abhi: Towards
    • priyatame: The most beloved
    • sadhasthe: In the place of existence
    • ā: And
    • ca: Also
    • parā: The higher or beyond
    • carati: Moves or travels
    • prajānan: Knowing or understanding
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the paths that are not obstructed, guided by the nourishing forces of both the heavens and the earth.
    • It emphasizes the importance of following the right path in life, which is supported by divine and earthly energies.
    • The mention of "both" suggests a balance between spiritual and material aspects of existence.
    • The phrase "moves or travels" indicates the dynamic nature of life and the continuous journey of understanding and growth.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a career context, this mantra can be applied to the journey of professional growth. Just as one must navigate various paths to achieve success, the support of mentors (nourishers) and the environment (earth) plays a crucial role.
    • In personal relationships, the idea of following a path that is not obstructed can relate to maintaining healthy communication and understanding, which fosters deeper connections.
    • In spiritual practices, this mantra can inspire individuals to seek balance between their spiritual pursuits (heavens) and their daily responsibilities (earth).
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for individuals to recognize and follow the unobstructed paths in life, supported by both divine and earthly forces.
    • It encourages a holistic approach to living, where one seeks harmony between spiritual aspirations and material realities.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra serves as a reminder to remain aware of the paths we choose and the influences that guide us, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling and balanced existence.
