सृण्येव जर्भरी तुर्पहरीतू नितोशेव तुर्पहरी परफरीका |उदन्यजेव जेमना मदेरू ता मे जराyv अजराम मराजु ||
sṛṇyeva jarbharī turpharītū naitośeva turpharī parpharīkā | udanyajeva jemanā maderū tā me jarāyv ajaram marāyu ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sṛṇyeva: like a sound or echo
    • jarbharī: a term that can refer to a state of being filled or abundant
    • turpharītū: possibly referring to a state of fullness or richness
    • naitośeva: not to be taken lightly or disregarded
    • turpharī: again indicating richness or abundance
    • parpharīkā: a term that may denote a state of flourishing or thriving
    • udanyajeva: like the rising or awakening
    • jemanā: referring to the river or flow, often symbolizing life
    • maderū: possibly indicating intoxication or a state of bliss
    • tā me: that which is mine
    • jarāyv: related to aging or decay
    • ajaram: imperishable or eternal
    • marāyu: related to death or mortality
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks to the duality of life, emphasizing the contrast between abundance and decay.
    • The imagery of sound and echo suggests that our actions and words resonate through time, much like how our legacies are remembered.
    • The reference to rivers symbolizes the flow of life, indicating that while we may experience moments of richness, we must also confront the inevitability of aging and death.
    • Real-life example: Consider a successful entrepreneur who builds a thriving business. Their achievements echo in the community, but they must also prepare for the eventual transition of their legacy, ensuring it continues beyond their lifetime.
  • Significance:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder to embrace the fullness of life while acknowledging its transient nature.
    • It encourages individuals to live meaningfully, creating a legacy that resonates with others.
    • In practical terms, it can be applied in various life scenarios, such as in personal relationships, career pursuits, and community involvement, where one seeks to leave a lasting impact.
