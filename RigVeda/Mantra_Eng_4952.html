शृण्वन्तं पूषणं वयं इर्यम अनष्टवेदसम् |ईशानं राया इमहे ||
śṛṇvantam pūṣaṇaṁ vayam iryam anaṣṭavedasam | īśānaṁ rāya īmahe ||
  • śṛṇvantam: This word translates to "listening" or "hearing." It emphasizes the importance of being attentive and receptive to knowledge and wisdom.
  • pūṣaṇaṁ: Refers to "Pūṣan," a deity associated with nourishment and prosperity. Pūṣan is often seen as a guide who leads individuals towards growth and abundance.
  • vayam: This means "we" or "us," indicating a collective approach or community involvement in the pursuit of knowledge and prosperity.
  • iryam: Translates to "the path" or "the way," suggesting a journey or direction that one must follow.
  • anaṣṭavedasam: This term can be interpreted as "one who is not deprived of knowledge" or "possessing complete knowledge." It signifies the importance of wisdom in achieving success.
  • īśānaṁ: Refers to "Ishana," which means "lord" or "master." It denotes a higher power or authority that guides and protects.
  • rāya: This word means "wealth" or "prosperity," highlighting the goal of attaining abundance in life.
  • īmahe: Translates to "we invoke" or "we seek," indicating a call to action or a prayer for guidance and support.

The mantra can be understood as a collective invocation for guidance and prosperity. It emphasizes the importance of listening to wisdom, following the right path, and seeking the blessings of a higher power to achieve abundance in life.


In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts:

  • Education: Students can embody the essence of "śṛṇvantam" by being attentive in their studies, listening to their teachers, and seeking knowledge actively.
  • Community Support: The collective aspect of "vayam" encourages teamwork and collaboration in community projects, fostering a sense of unity in achieving common goals.
  • Career Growth: Professionals can reflect on "anaṣṭavedasam" by continuously seeking knowledge and skills that enhance their career prospects, ensuring they are not deprived of opportunities.
  • Spiritual Practices: The invocation of "īśānaṁ" can be seen in prayer or meditation practices where individuals seek guidance from a higher power for clarity and direction in their lives.

In conclusion, this mantra serves as a reminder of the significance of listening, seeking knowledge, and invoking divine guidance in the pursuit of prosperity. It highlights the interconnectedness of individuals in their journey towards success and the importance of community support in achieving shared goals.
