आ याह्य अग्ने पथ्या अनु स्वा मन्द्रो देवानां सख्यं जुषाणः |आ सानु शुष्मैर नदयन पृथिव्या जम्बेभिर विश्वम् उषधग वनानि ||
ā yāhy agne pathyā3 anu svā mandro devānāṁ sakhyaṁ juṣāṇaḥ | ā sānu śuṣmair nadayan pṛthivyā jambhebhir viśvam uśadhag vanāni ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā yāhy: "Come to us" or "May you approach us"
    • agne: "O Agni" (the fire god)
    • pathyā: "by the path" or "through the way"
    • anu: "following" or "after"
    • svā: "our" or "of us"
    • mandro: "sweet" or "pleasant"
    • devānāṁ: "of the gods"
    • sakhyaṁ: "friendship" or "companionship"
    • juṣāṇaḥ: "enjoying" or "delighting in"
    • ā sānu: "may we also" or "let us also"
    • śuṣmair: "with the best" or "with the finest"
    • nadayan: "flowing" or "moving"
    • pṛthivyā: "of the earth"
    • jambhebhir: "with the fruits" or "with the produce"
    • viśvam: "all" or "the whole"
    • uśadhag: "herbs" or "plants"
    • vanāni: "forests" or "woodlands"
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Agni, the fire god, asking for his presence and blessings.
    • It emphasizes the importance of friendship and companionship among the divine beings.
    • The invocation of Agni signifies the need for guidance and support in our endeavors.
    • The mention of "flowing" and "herbs" suggests a connection to nature and the sustenance it provides.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community gathering, people often invoke blessings for unity and cooperation, similar to how this mantra seeks divine companionship.
    • In agricultural practices, farmers may pray for a good harvest, reflecting the connection to the earth and its produce as mentioned in the mantra.
    • During rituals, the lighting of a fire symbolizes the presence of Agni, representing purification and the divine connection.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of divine guidance, friendship, and the interconnectedness of nature.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, such as community gatherings, agricultural practices, and spiritual rituals.
    • By invoking Agni and seeking companionship with the divine, individuals can foster a sense of unity and purpose in their lives.
