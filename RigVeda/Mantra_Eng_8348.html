आ तु न इन्दो शतदात्व अश्व्यं सहस्रदातु पशुमद धिरण्यवत् |उप मास्व बृहती रेवतीर इषो 'धि स्तोत्रस्य पवमान नो गहि ||
ā tū na indo śatadātv aśvyaṁ sahasradātu paśumad dhiraṇyavat | upa māsva bṛhatī revatīr iṣo 'dhi stotrasya pavamāna no gahi ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā: from
    • tū: you
    • na: not
    • indo: O Indra (the god of rain and thunder)
    • śatadātv: a hundredfold
    • aśvyaṁ: horses
    • sahasradātu: a thousandfold
    • paśumad: cattle
    • dhiraṇyavat: like gold
    • upa: near
    • māsva: come
    • bṛhatī: the great one
    • revatīr: the prosperous
    • iṣo: of the desires
    • 'dhi: indeed
    • stotrasya: of the hymn
    • pavamāna: the purifying
    • no: our
    • gahi: hold
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Indra, asking for abundance and prosperity.
    • The speaker requests Indra to grant them a hundredfold of horses and a thousandfold of cattle, symbolizing wealth and resources.
    • The mention of "like gold" signifies the value and importance of these blessings.
    • The invocation of "come near" suggests a desire for divine presence and support in achieving these goals.
    • The term "bṛhatī" refers to greatness, indicating the aspiration for a grand and prosperous life.
    • The phrase "the purifying hymn" emphasizes the importance of devotion and purity in seeking blessings.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In agriculture, farmers may recite this mantra during rituals to invoke blessings for a bountiful harvest, symbolizing the horses and cattle as representations of their crops and livestock.
    • In business, entrepreneurs might use this mantra to seek prosperity and success, hoping for growth and abundance in their ventures.
    • During personal challenges, individuals may recite this mantra to attract positive energy and support from the divine, reinforcing their faith in overcoming obstacles.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of seeking divine blessings for prosperity and abundance in various aspects of life.
    • It serves as a reminder of the importance of devotion, purity, and the pursuit of greatness.
    • In real-life scenarios, it can be applied in agriculture, business, and personal challenges, reinforcing the belief in divine support and the power of positive intentions.
