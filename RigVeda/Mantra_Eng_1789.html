प्र स्कम्भदेश्ना अनवभ्रराधसो 'लातृणासो विदथेṣu सुष्टुताः |अर्चन्त्य अर्कं मदिरस्य पीतये विदुर वीरस्य प्रथमानी पौंस्याः ||
pra skambhadeṣṇā anavabhrarādhaso 'lātṛṇāso vidatheṣu suṣṭutāḥ | arcanty arkam madirasya pītaye vidur vīrasya prathamāni pauṁsyā ||
  • pra skambhadeṣṇā: This phrase can be interpreted as "with the support of the firm foundation." In real life, this can relate to the importance of having a strong base in any endeavor, whether it be in personal relationships, business, or education. For example, a successful business often relies on a solid business plan and a strong team.
  • anavabhrarādhaso: This term suggests "without any hindrance or obstacles." In practical terms, this can be seen in the pursuit of goals where one strives to overcome challenges. For instance, an athlete training for a marathon must push through physical and mental barriers to achieve their goal.
  • 'lātṛṇāso: This can be understood as "those who are devoted or dedicated." In everyday life, dedication is crucial for success. A student who dedicates time to study and understand their subjects is more likely to excel in exams.
  • vidatheṣu suṣṭutāḥ: This phrase translates to "in the gatherings or assemblies, there is praise." This can be related to teamwork and collaboration in various settings, such as workplaces or community projects. For example, a team that works well together and supports each other often receives recognition for their collective efforts.
  • arcanty arkam: This means "they worship the sun." The sun is often seen as a source of life and energy. In real life, this can symbolize the importance of gratitude and appreciation for the resources and opportunities we have. For instance, practicing gratitude can lead to a more positive outlook on life.
  • madirasya pītaye: This phrase can be interpreted as "for the enjoyment of the intoxication." This can refer to the joy and fulfillment that comes from achieving one's desires or goals. For example, the satisfaction of completing a challenging project can be likened to a celebratory moment.
  • vidur vīrasya: This translates to "the wise ones of the brave." It emphasizes the value of wisdom and courage in overcoming challenges. In real life, this can be seen in leaders who make tough decisions for the greater good, such as a firefighter risking their life to save others.
  • prathamāni pauṁsyā: This means "the first among men." It signifies leadership and the role of pioneers in society. In practical terms, this can relate to innovators and trailblazers who pave the way for others, such as entrepreneurs who create new industries.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of a strong foundation, dedication, and the ability to overcome obstacles while working collaboratively and appreciating the resources we have. It highlights the significance of wisdom and courage in leadership and innovation. In real life, these principles can be applied in various scenarios, from personal growth to professional success, reminding us that with dedication and teamwork, we can achieve our goals and inspire others along the way.
