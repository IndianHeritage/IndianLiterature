ओषधिः प्रति मोदध्वम् पुष्पवतीः प्रसूवरिः |अश्वा इव सजित्वरीर वीरुधः पारयिष्णवः ||
oṣadhīḥ prati modadhvam puṣpavatīḥ prasūvarīḥ | aśvā iva sajitvarīr vīrudhaḥ pārayiṣṇvaḥ ||
  • Oṣadhīḥ: This term refers to plants or herbs, symbolizing growth, nourishment, and the healing properties of nature.
  • Prati modadhvam: This phrase can be interpreted as "may you rejoice" or "may you celebrate," indicating a call to happiness and joy.
  • Puṣpavatīḥ: Meaning "flowering" or "blossoming," this word emphasizes beauty, potential, and the fruition of efforts.
  • Prasūvarīḥ: This term suggests "those that produce" or "generators," referring to the life-giving aspects of nature and the abundance it provides.
  • Aśvā iva: Translated as "like horses," this phrase evokes strength, speed, and vitality, often associated with the power of nature.
  • Sajitvarīr: This word means "those who are swift" or "quick," highlighting the dynamic and energetic qualities of life.
  • Vīrudhaḥ: Referring to "the flourishing" or "the thriving," it signifies prosperity and growth in various aspects of life.
  • Pārayiṣṇvaḥ: This term can be understood as "may you achieve" or "may you attain," indicating a desire for success and fulfillment.

The mantra as a whole can be interpreted as a prayer or invocation for the flourishing of life, growth, and the celebration of nature's bounty. It emphasizes the interconnectedness of all living things and the importance of joy and vitality in our lives.


In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts:

  • Environmental Awareness: The reference to plants and nature encourages us to appreciate and protect our environment. For example, participating in tree-planting initiatives or supporting sustainable agriculture can embody the spirit of this mantra.
  • Personal Growth: The idea of blossoming and flourishing can inspire individuals to pursue their passions and develop their talents. Engaging in lifelong learning or creative endeavors can reflect the essence of this mantra.
  • Community Celebration: The call to rejoice can be seen in community gatherings, festivals, and celebrations that honor cultural heritage and foster unity. Organizing local events that bring people together can manifest the joy expressed in the mantra.

In conclusion, this RigVeda mantra serves as a reminder of the beauty and abundance of life, urging us to celebrate growth, vitality, and the interconnectedness of all beings. Its significance lies in its ability to inspire positive actions towards personal development, environmental stewardship, and community engagement.
