साध्व अपांसी सनता न उक्षिते उषासानक्ता वय्येव रण्विते |तन्तुं ततम् संव्यन्ती समीची यज्ञस्य पेषः सुदुघे पयस्वती ||
sādhv apāṁsi sanatā na ukṣite uṣāsānaktā vayyeva raṇvite | tantuṁ tataṁ saṁvayantī samīcī yajñasya peśaḥ sudughe payasvatī ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sādhv: well, rightly
    • apāṁsi: waters
    • sanatā: eternal, everlasting
    • na: not
    • ukṣite: stirred, agitated
    • uṣāsānaktā: the dawn, the light of day
    • vayyeva: like a cow
    • raṇvite: in the battle, in the struggle
    • tantuṁ: the thread, the connection
    • tataṁ: that which is spread
    • saṁvayantī: gathering, uniting
    • samīcī: properly, rightly
    • yajñasya: of the sacrifice, of the offering
    • peśaḥ: the essence, the core
    • sudughe: very nourishing
    • payasvatī: rich in milk, nourishing
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the eternal waters that are not disturbed, symbolizing purity and stability.
    • The dawn is compared to a cow, suggesting that it brings nourishment and sustenance, much like how a cow provides milk.
    • The mantra emphasizes the importance of unity and connection, particularly in the context of rituals and sacrifices.
    • The essence of the sacrifice is highlighted as being nourishing and beneficial, akin to the rich milk provided by a cow.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, the concept of "eternal waters" can be related to the importance of maintaining emotional stability and purity in our thoughts and actions.
    • The dawn symbolizes new beginnings; for instance, starting a new project or phase in life can be seen as a 'dawn' that brings new opportunities.
    • In community gatherings or family functions, the idea of unity and connection is crucial, as it fosters a sense of belonging and support.
    • The nourishing aspect of the mantra can be reflected in how we care for one another, much like how a mother nourishes her child.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of stability, nourishment, and unity in our lives.
    • It teaches us to seek purity in our actions and to embrace new beginnings with positivity.
    • In real-life scenarios, applying the principles of this mantra can lead to stronger relationships, emotional well-being, and a supportive community.
    • Ultimately, it encourages us to recognize the nourishing aspects of our lives and to cultivate them for personal and communal growth.
