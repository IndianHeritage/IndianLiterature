अधā यथा नः पितरः परासः प्रात्नासो अग्न ऋतम आशुषाणः |शुचीद अयन दीधितिम उक्थशासः क्षामā भिन्दन्तो अरुणीर अप व्रन ||
adhā yathā naḥ pitaraḥ parāsaḥ pratnāso agna ṛtam āśuṣāṇāḥ | śucīd ayan dīdhitim ukthaśāsaḥ kṣāmā bhindanto aruṇīr apa vran ||
  • adhā yathā naḥ pitaraḥ parāsaḥ: This phrase can be translated as "As our fathers have done." It emphasizes the importance of tradition and the wisdom passed down through generations. In real life, this can be seen in family practices, where children learn values and customs from their parents.
  • pratnāso agna ṛtam āśuṣāṇāḥ: This part means "O Agni, may we attain the truth swiftly." Agni, the fire god, symbolizes purity and transformation. In modern contexts, this can relate to the pursuit of knowledge and truth in various fields, such as science and philosophy, where individuals seek clarity and understanding.
  • śucīd ayan dīdhitim ukthaśāsaḥ: Translated, it means "May we be pure and may our words be powerful." This highlights the significance of purity in thought and speech. In everyday life, this can be applied to the importance of honesty and integrity in communication, whether in personal relationships or professional settings.
  • kṣāmā bhindanto aruṇīr apa vran: This phrase can be interpreted as "May we break through the darkness and reach the light." It signifies overcoming obstacles and striving for enlightenment. In real life, this can be seen in personal struggles, where individuals work to overcome challenges, such as addiction or mental health issues, to achieve a better state of being.

  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of tradition, the pursuit of truth, the power of pure speech, and the journey from darkness to light. In real life, these principles can guide individuals in their personal and professional lives, encouraging them to honor their heritage, seek knowledge, communicate with integrity, and strive for personal growth. By embodying these values, one can contribute positively to society and foster a sense of community and understanding.
