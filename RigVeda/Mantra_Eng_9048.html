अस्माकं ऊर्जां रथम पूषा अविष्टु माहिनः |भुवद वाजानां वृध इमं नः श्रिणवद धवम् ||
asmākam ūrjā ratham pūṣā aviṣṭu māhinaḥ | bhuvad vājānāṁ vṛdha imaṁ naḥ śṛṇavad dhavam ||
  • Translation: "May our strength (ūrjā) be like a chariot (ratham) driven by Pūṣa, the sun god. May we be endowed with wealth (vājānāṁ) and may we be heard (śṛṇavad) in our endeavors."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • asmākam: Our
    • ūrjā: Strength or energy
    • ratham: Chariot, symbolizing movement and progress
    • pūṣā: A deity associated with nourishment and prosperity, often identified with the sun
    • aviṣṭu: To be endowed or blessed
    • māhinaḥ: Wealth or abundance
    • bhuvad: May it be
    • vājānāṁ: Of wealth or riches
    • vṛdha: To grow or increase
    • imaṁ: This
    • naḥ: Our
    • śṛṇavad: To be heard or listened to
    • dhavam: In our endeavors or actions
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, a team may invoke this mantra to seek strength and unity in their projects, hoping to achieve success and recognition.
    • In personal life, individuals may recite this mantra during challenging times, seeking the strength to overcome obstacles and achieve their goals.
    • During community gatherings, this mantra can be used to inspire collective efforts towards prosperity and well-being.
  • Significance:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of strength and unity in achieving success.
    • It highlights the role of divine blessings in our endeavors, reminding us to seek support from higher powers.
    • The imagery of a chariot signifies movement and progress, encouraging individuals and communities to strive forward.
  • Conclusion:
    • The mantra serves as a reminder that with collective strength and divine support, we can overcome challenges and achieve our aspirations.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal growth to community development, reinforcing the idea that we are stronger together.
    • By invoking this mantra, individuals and groups can foster a sense of purpose and direction in their pursuits.
