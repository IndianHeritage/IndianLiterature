सोऽस्य विशे महि शर्म यच्छति योऽस्य धाम प्रथमं व्यानशे |पदं यदऽस्य परम व्योमनि अतो विश्वा अभि सं याती संयतः ||
so asya viśe mahi śarma yacchati yo asya dhāma prathamaṁ vyānaśe | padaṁ yad asya parame vyomany ato viśvā abhi saṁ yāti saṁyataḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • so: thus
    • asya: of this
    • viśe: in the community or society
    • mahi: great or vast
    • śarma: comfort, protection, or shelter
    • yacchati: gives or provides
    • yo: who
    • asya: of this
    • dhāma: abode or dwelling
    • prathamaṁ: first or foremost
    • vyānaśe: in the expanse or vastness
    • padaṁ: place or position
    • yad: which
    • asya: of this
    • parame: supreme or highest
    • vyomany: in the sky or space
    • ato: therefore
    • viśvā: all or universal
    • abhi: towards or in the direction of
    • saṁ: together or in unity
    • yāti: goes or moves
    • saṁyataḥ: with restraint or control
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the vastness and greatness of the community and the shelter it provides.
    • It emphasizes the importance of unity and collective strength in achieving a higher purpose.
    • The mention of "first abode" suggests the foundational role of community in individual lives.
    • The phrase "in the sky" can be interpreted as the ultimate goal or aspiration that is universal and transcendent.
    • It highlights the significance of moving together with restraint, indicating the need for discipline and cooperation.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace, teamwork and collaboration can lead to greater achievements than individual efforts.
    • In a community, collective actions during crises (like natural disasters) showcase the strength of unity.
    • In personal relationships, providing emotional support and shelter to one another fosters a sense of belonging and security.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of community, unity, and the shelter it provides in our lives.
    • It teaches us that by working together with discipline and control, we can achieve greater heights and navigate the challenges of life.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as family, workplace, and society, emphasizing the need for cooperation and mutual support.
