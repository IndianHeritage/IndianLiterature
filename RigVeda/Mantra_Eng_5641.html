मन्त्र: इमे मित्रो वरुणो दूलभासो 'चेतसां चिच्चितयन्ति दक्षैः |अपि क्रतुं सुचेतसां वतन्तस तिरश्चिद अंहः सुपथा नयन्ति ||
ime mitro varuṇo dūḻabhāso 'cetasaṁ cic citayanti dakṣaiḥ | api kratuṁ sucetasaṁ vatantas tiraś cid aṁhaḥ supathā nayanti ||
  • ime mitro varuṇo: This phrase refers to the deities Mitra and Varuna, who are often associated with the concepts of friendship, harmony, and cosmic order. In real life, this can be seen in the importance of maintaining good relationships and trust among individuals and communities.
  • dūḻabhāso 'cetasaṁ: This part suggests that those who are wise and perceptive can see beyond the superficial. In everyday life, this can be related to the ability to understand deeper truths in situations, such as recognizing the underlying motives in personal or professional relationships.
  • cic citayanti dakṣaiḥ: This means that the wise (dakṣaiḥ) are able to discern and understand the essence of things. In practical terms, this can be applied to decision-making processes where critical thinking and insight are required to navigate complex issues.
  • api kratuṁ sucetasaṁ: This phrase emphasizes the importance of intention (kratuṁ) and clarity of thought (sucetasaṁ). In real life, setting clear goals and having a focused mindset can lead to successful outcomes in various endeavors, such as career planning or personal development.
  • vatantas tiraś cid aṁhaḥ: This suggests that those who speak wisely (vatantas) can illuminate the path (tiraś cid aṁhaḥ) for others. This can be seen in leadership roles where effective communication and guidance can inspire and direct teams towards achieving common objectives.
  • supathā nayanti: This final part indicates that those who follow the right path (supathā) are guided towards success. In real life, this can be related to ethical decision-making and the importance of following moral principles in both personal and professional life.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the significance of wisdom, clarity of thought, and the importance of relationships in navigating life's challenges. It emphasizes that by fostering good relationships, maintaining a clear mind, and communicating effectively, individuals can lead themselves and others towards success. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various contexts such as teamwork, leadership, personal growth, and ethical decision-making, ultimately guiding individuals towards a harmonious and fulfilling life.
