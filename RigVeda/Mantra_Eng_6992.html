यद आविर यद अपीच्यं देवासो अस्ति दुश्कृतम् |त्रिते तद विश्वम् आप्त्य आरे अस्मद दधातनानेहसो व ऊतयः सुतयः व ऊतयः ||
yad āvir yad apīcya1ṁ devāso asti duṣkṛtam | trite tad viśvam āptya āre asmad dadhātanānehaso va ūtayaḥ suūtayo va ūtayaḥ ||
  • Mantra: "yad āvir yad apīcyaṁ devāso asti duṣkṛtam | trite tad viśvam āptya āre asmad dadhātanānehaso va ūtayaḥ suūtayo va ūtayaḥ ||"
  • Translation: "Whatever is manifest, whatever is hidden, may the gods remove our misdeeds. May that which is beneficial for all beings be established, and may we be endowed with strength and prosperity."

  • Word-by-Word Explanation:
    • yad āvir: Whatever is manifest or visible.
    • yad apīcyaṁ: Whatever is hidden or not immediately apparent.
    • devāso: May the gods.
    • asti: Exist or be present.
    • duṣkṛtam: Our misdeeds or wrongdoings.
    • trite: In the third instance or context.
    • tad viśvam: That which is universal or beneficial for all.
    • āptya: Establishment or realization.
    • āre: For us or for our benefit.
    • asmad: We or our.
    • dadhātanānehaso: Endowed with strength and prosperity.
    • va ūtayaḥ: May it be so or may it happen.
    • suūtayo: Good or beneficial.

  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a personal context, when facing challenges or setbacks, one might recite this mantra to seek divine assistance in overcoming obstacles and to cleanse oneself of past mistakes.
    • In a community setting, this mantra can be invoked during gatherings or rituals to promote unity and collective well-being, encouraging members to work together for the common good.
    • In a professional environment, leaders may use the essence of this mantra to inspire teams to focus on positive outcomes and to learn from past errors, fostering a culture of growth and resilience.

  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine intervention to cleanse misdeeds and to establish a path towards universal benefit. It emphasizes the importance of acknowledging both visible and hidden aspects of life, urging individuals and communities to strive for strength, prosperity, and collective well-being. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in personal growth, community harmony, and professional development, serving as a reminder to seek improvement and to act for the greater good.
