इष्कर्तारमध्वरस्य प्रचेतसं क्षयन्तं राधसो महः |रातिं वामस्य सुभगाम महीमिषं दधासि सानसिं रयिम ||
iṣkartāram adhvarasya pracetasaṁ kṣayantaṁ rādhaso mahaḥ | rātiṁ vāmasya subhagām mahīm iṣaṁ dadhāsi sānasiṁ rayim ||
  • iṣkartāram: This term refers to the "doer" or "creator," emphasizing the active role of a divine force in the universe.
  • adhvarasya: This word relates to "sacrifice" or "ritual," indicating the importance of rituals in connecting with the divine.
  • pracetasaṁ: This term means "of the wise" or "intelligent," suggesting that wisdom is essential in understanding and performing rituals.
  • kṣayantaṁ: This means "destroying" or "removing," indicating the power of the divine to eliminate obstacles or negativity.
  • rādhaso: This refers to "wealth" or "prosperity," highlighting the connection between divine favor and material abundance.
  • mahaḥ: This means "great" or "mighty," emphasizing the strength and significance of the divine presence.
  • rātiṁ: This term signifies "joy" or "delight," indicating the emotional and spiritual fulfillment that comes from divine connection.
  • vāmasya: This refers to "the left" or "the auspicious," suggesting a favorable direction or approach in life.
  • subhagām: This means "beautiful" or "auspicious," indicating the positive outcomes of divine blessings.
  • mahīm: This term signifies "earth" or "world," representing the physical realm and its connection to the divine.
  • iṣaṁ: This means "power" or "authority," emphasizing the divine's ability to bestow strength and influence.
  • dadhāsi: This term means "you give" or "you bestow," indicating the act of granting blessings or gifts.
  • sānasiṁ: This refers to "wealth" or "prosperity," reinforcing the theme of abundance in life.
  • rayim: This means "riches" or "prosperity," highlighting the material and spiritual wealth that can be attained through divine connection.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda emphasizes the importance of divine intervention in our lives, particularly through rituals and wisdom. It highlights the connection between spiritual practices and the attainment of prosperity, joy, and strength. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied by engaging in meaningful rituals, seeking wisdom in our actions, and recognizing the divine presence in our pursuit of happiness and abundance. By understanding and embodying these principles, individuals can cultivate a life filled with positivity and fulfillment, aligning themselves with the greater forces of the universe.
