अग्निम् īḻiṣvāवसे गाथाभिः शीरशोचिषम् |अग्निं राये पुरुमीḻha श्रुतं नरो 'ग्निं सुदीतये चर्धिः ||
agnim īḻiṣvāvase gāthābhiḥ śīraśociṣam | agniṁ rāye purumīḻha śrutaṁ naro 'gniṁ sudītaye chardiḥ ||
  • Agnim: This word refers to "Agni," the fire god in Vedic literature, symbolizing energy, light, and transformation.
  • īḻiṣvāvase: This term can be interpreted as "to invoke" or "to worship," indicating a call to the divine.
  • gāthābhiḥ: This means "with hymns" or "with verses," suggesting the importance of chanting and recitation in spiritual practices.
  • śīraśociṣam: This can be translated as "brilliant" or "radiant," emphasizing the illuminating nature of Agni.
  • rāye: This word means "wealth" or "prosperity," indicating the blessings that Agni can bestow.
  • purumīḻha: This term refers to "abundant" or "plentiful," suggesting a connection to abundance in life.
  • śrutaṁ: This means "heard" or "listened to," indicating the importance of knowledge and wisdom.
  • naro: This translates to "man" or "human," highlighting the human connection to the divine.
  • sudītaye: This means "for good fortune" or "for auspiciousness," indicating the positive outcomes of worshipping Agni.
  • chardiḥ: This term can be interpreted as "to be invoked" or "to be called upon," suggesting the active role of Agni in human affairs.

The mantra essentially invokes Agni, the fire god, asking for his blessings of prosperity, wisdom, and abundance. It emphasizes the importance of worship through hymns and the radiant nature of Agni, which symbolizes transformation and illumination in life.


In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts:

  • Rituals and Ceremonies: The mantra can be recited during rituals to invoke blessings for prosperity and success in endeavors.
  • Personal Growth: Individuals can reflect on the qualities of Agni—energy, transformation, and illumination—as they seek to improve themselves and their circumstances.
  • Community Gatherings: In communal settings, reciting this mantra can foster a sense of unity and shared purpose, invoking collective prosperity.

In conclusion, this mantra serves as a powerful invocation for blessings from Agni, symbolizing the light of knowledge and the warmth of prosperity. It encourages individuals to seek transformation and abundance in their lives, making it relevant in both personal and communal contexts.
