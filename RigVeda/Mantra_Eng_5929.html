किमित ते विष्णो परिचक्ष्यम् भूत्वा यद्ववक्षे शिपिविष्टोऽस्मि। मा वर्पो अस्मद अप गूहा एतद् यद् अन्यरूपः समिथे बभूथा॥
kim it te viṣṇo paricakṣyam bhūt pra yad vavakṣe śipiviṣṭo asmi | mā varpo asmad apa gūha etad yad anyarūpaḥ samithe babhūtha ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • kim: what
    • it: this
    • te: of you
    • viṣṇo: O Vishnu
    • paricakṣyam: should I perceive or understand
    • bhūt: being or existence
    • pra: indeed
    • yad: that which
    • vavakṣe: you have revealed
    • śipiviṣṭo: O one who is all-pervading
    • asmi: I am
    • mā: do not
    • varpo: hide or conceal
    • asmad: from us
    • apa: away
    • gūha: conceal
    • etad: this
    • yad: that which
    • anyarūpaḥ: of another form
    • samithe: in the assembly
    • babhūtha: has become
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Lord Vishnu, seeking clarity and understanding of existence.
    • The speaker is asking what they should understand about the divine presence of Vishnu and the nature of reality.
    • It emphasizes the importance of not hiding the truth from oneself and encourages openness to divine wisdom.
    • The phrase "do not conceal from us" suggests a desire for transparency in spiritual matters.
    • The reference to "another form" indicates the multifaceted nature of divinity and existence.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a personal context, this mantra can be applied when one is seeking guidance in difficult situations, encouraging individuals to seek clarity and understanding rather than being misled by illusions.
    • In a community setting, it can serve as a reminder for leaders to be transparent and honest with their followers, fostering trust and unity.
    • In a philosophical context, it invites contemplation on the nature of reality and the divine, prompting individuals to explore their beliefs and understanding of existence.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of seeking knowledge and understanding in the presence of the divine.
    • It encourages individuals to be open and honest in their spiritual journey, recognizing the complexity of existence.
    • In real life, it serves as a guiding principle for personal growth, community integrity, and philosophical exploration.
