मन्त्र: सिन्धोर इव प्राध्वने शूघनासो वातप्रमियः पतयन्ति यह्वाः | घृतस्य धाराऽरुषो न वाजी काष्ठा भिन्दन्न ऊर्मिभिः पिन्वमानः ||
sindhor iva prādhvane śūghanāso vātapramiyaḥ patayanti yahvāḥ | ghṛtasya dhārā aruṣo na vājī kāṣṭhā bhindann ūrmibhiḥ pinvamānaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sindhor - of the ocean
    • iva - like
    • prādhvane - in the flow
    • śūghanāso - with swift noses
    • vātapramiyaḥ - the wind that is powerful
    • patayanti - they fly
    • yahvāḥ - those who
    • ghṛtasya - of ghee
    • dhārā - the flow
    • aruṣo - red or bright
    • na - not
    • vājī - the swift one
    • kāṣṭhā - wood
    • bhindann - breaking
    • ūrmibhiḥ - with waves
    • pinvamānaḥ - being pressed
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra draws a vivid comparison between the powerful forces of nature and the divine. The "ocean" symbolizes vastness and depth, while "the wind" represents movement and change.
    • The phrase "with swift noses" can be interpreted as the quickness of thought or action, suggesting that those who are aware and alert can navigate through life's challenges effectively.
    • The "flow of ghee" signifies nourishment and abundance, indicating that the divine provides sustenance to those who seek it.
    • The imagery of "breaking wood with waves" illustrates the power of nature to overcome obstacles, reminding us that challenges can be surmounted with strength and determination.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, the mantra can be applied to the importance of adaptability. Just as the wind changes direction, businesses must be agile to respond to market demands.
    • In personal life, the idea of being "swift" in thought and action can be seen in how individuals make decisions. Quick and informed decisions often lead to success, much like the swift movement of the wind.
    • The concept of nourishment can be related to the importance of community support. Just as ghee nourishes the body, emotional and social support nourishes the spirit.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the interconnectedness of nature and the divine, highlighting the importance of being aware, adaptable, and resilient in the face of challenges.
    • Its significance lies in the reminder that we can draw strength from the natural world and the divine, allowing us to navigate life's complexities with grace and determination.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra encourages individuals to embrace change, seek nourishment from their surroundings, and face obstacles with courage, ultimately leading to personal growth and fulfillment.
