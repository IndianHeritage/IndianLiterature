दश क्षिपो युञ्जते बाहू अद्रिं सोमस्य या शमितारा सुहस्ता |मध्वो रसं सुगभस्तिर गिरिष्ठां चनिष्चदद दुदुहे शुक्रं अंशुः ||
daśa kṣipo yuñjate bāhū adriṁ somasya yā śamitārā suhastā | madhvo rasaṁ sugabhastir giriṣṭhāṁ caniścadad duduhe śukram aṁśuḥ ||
  • daśa kṣipo yuñjate bāhū: This phrase can be translated as "ten strong arms are engaged." Here, "daśa" means ten, "kṣipo" refers to strong or powerful, and "bāhū" means arms. This can symbolize teamwork and collaboration, where multiple individuals come together to achieve a common goal.
  • adriṁ somasya yā śamitārā suhastā: This translates to "the mountain of the moon, which is well-formed and auspicious." "Adriṁ" means mountain, "somasya" refers to the moon, and "śamitārā" indicates something that is well-formed or auspicious. This can represent stability and the nurturing aspects of nature, akin to how mountains provide a solid foundation for life.
  • madhvo rasaṁ sugabhastir: This means "the essence of sweetness is well-formed." "Madhvo" refers to sweetness, "rasaṁ" means essence or flavor, and "sugabhastir" indicates something that is well-formed or pleasing. This can be interpreted as the importance of seeking joy and positivity in life, akin to how sweet experiences enrich our lives.
  • giriṣṭhāṁ caniścadad duduhe: This translates to "from the highest mountain, they draw forth." "Giriṣṭhāṁ" means the highest mountain, "caniścadad" refers to drawing forth or extracting, and "duduhe" means to milk or obtain. This can symbolize the pursuit of knowledge and wisdom, where one seeks to extract valuable insights from the highest sources.
  • śukram aṁśuḥ: This means "the pure light." "Śukram" refers to purity or brightness, and "aṁśuḥ" means light or ray. This can represent enlightenment and clarity, emphasizing the importance of seeking truth and understanding in our lives.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of collaboration, stability, positivity, the pursuit of knowledge, and the quest for enlightenment. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various contexts such as teamwork in professional settings, the importance of a strong foundation in personal relationships, and the pursuit of knowledge through education. It encourages individuals to work together harmoniously, seek joy in their experiences, and strive for clarity and understanding in their lives. The mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of these elements in achieving a fulfilling and meaningful life.
