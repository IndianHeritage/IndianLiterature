समिद्धाग्निर वनवत स्तीर्णबर्हिर युक्तग्रावाऽ सुतसोमो जराते | ग्रावाणो यस्येषिरं वदन्त्य आयद अध्वर्युर हविषा व सिन्धुम ||
samiddhāgnir vanavat stīrṇabarhir yuktagrāvā sutasomo jarāte | grāvāṇo yasyeṣiraṁ vadanty ayad adhvaryur haviṣāva sindhum ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • samiddhāgnir: The fire that is kindled or ignited.
    • vanavat: Like a forest or in a forest-like manner.
    • stīrṇabarhir: With a well-prepared or spread-out covering.
    • yuktagrāvā: With a proper arrangement or organization.
    • sutasomo: The one who is rich in offerings or has abundant wealth.
    • jarāte: To age or to grow old.
    • grāvāṇo: The one who is knowledgeable or wise.
    • yasyeṣiraṁ: Whose mind or intellect.
    • vadanty: They speak or declare.
    • ayad: That which is not.
    • adhvaryur: The priest or the one who performs the rituals.
    • haviṣāva: Like an offering or sacrifice.
    • sindhum: The ocean or a large body of water.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the importance of a well-prepared and organized approach to life, akin to how a fire is kindled in a forest setting.
    • The reference to "sutasomo" suggests that abundance and wealth come to those who are diligent and wise in their actions.
    • The phrase "grāvāṇo yasyeṣiraṁ" emphasizes the significance of a wise mind, indicating that knowledge and intellect are crucial for success.
    • The mention of the "adhvaryur" highlights the role of rituals and structured practices in achieving one's goals.
    • Overall, the mantra encourages individuals to cultivate wisdom, organization, and a sense of purpose in their endeavors.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, a well-organized team that works together harmoniously can achieve greater success, much like a fire that is properly tended to burns brightly.
    • In personal life, individuals who plan their goals and manage their time effectively often find themselves more fulfilled and prosperous.
    • In education, students who approach their studies with discipline and a structured plan tend to perform better and gain more knowledge.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of preparation, wisdom, and organization in all aspects of life.
    • It is applicable in various scenarios, from personal development to professional success, reminding us that a thoughtful approach leads to abundance and fulfillment.
    • By embodying the principles outlined in this mantra, individuals can navigate life's challenges more effectively and achieve their desired outcomes.
