नमस्ते अग्न ओजसे गृणन्ति देव कृष्टयः |अमैर अमित्रम अर्दय ||
namas te agna ojase gṛṇanti deva kṛṣṭayaḥ | amair amitram ardaya ||
  • Translation: "Salutations to you, O Agni, the one who is powerful; the gods sing your praises. May you, O Agni, destroy our enemies and grant us protection."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • Namas: Salutations or reverence, indicating respect and acknowledgment.
    • Te: To you, referring directly to Agni.
    • Agna: Agni, the fire god, symbolizing energy, transformation, and purification.
    • Ojase: Powerful or strong, highlighting Agni's immense energy and strength.
    • Gṛṇanti: Sing or praise, indicating the act of worship and recognition by the gods.
    • Deva: Gods, representing divine beings who are revered in Vedic traditions.
    • Kṛṣṭayaḥ: Those who are devoted or dedicated, often referring to the worshippers or practitioners.
    • Amair: Without, indicating the absence of something, in this case, enemies.
    • Amitram: Enemies or adversaries, referring to those who oppose or threaten.
    • Ardaya: Destroy or harm, a plea for protection against negativity or harm.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a challenging situation, such as facing a difficult exam or job interview, individuals often seek strength and clarity, akin to invoking Agni's energy for support.
    • During community gatherings or rituals, people may chant this mantra to foster unity and protection against external threats, symbolizing collective strength.
    • In personal conflicts, individuals may reflect on this mantra to seek inner strength and the ability to overcome adversities, much like Agni's transformative power.
  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of reverence for divine energy and the need for protection against adversities. In real life, it serves as a reminder to seek strength and clarity in challenging situations, fostering a sense of community and resilience. By invoking Agni, individuals can cultivate inner strength and transform obstacles into opportunities for growth and protection.
