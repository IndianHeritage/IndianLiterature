अक्षीभ्यां ते नासिकाभ्यां कर्णाभ्यां चुबुकाद अधि |यक्ष्मं शीर्षण्यम मस्तिष्काज जिव्हायां वि वृहामि ते ||
akṣībhyāṁ te nāsikābhyāṁ karṇābhyāṁ chubukād adhi | yakṣmaṁ śīrṣaṇyam mastiṣkāj jihvāyā vi vṛhāmi te ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • akṣībhyāṁ: From the eyes
    • te: Your
    • nāsikābhyāṁ: From the nostrils
    • karṇābhyāṁ: From the ears
    • chubukād: From the chin
    • adhi: Above
    • yakṣmaṁ: Disease or ailment
    • śīrṣaṇyam: From the head
    • mastiṣkāj: From the brain
    • jihvāyā: From the tongue
    • vi: Indeed
    • vṛhāmi: I remove or I dispel
    • te: Your
  • Overall Meaning: This mantra is a prayer for the removal of ailments and diseases that may affect various parts of the body, including the eyes, nostrils, ears, chin, head, brain, and tongue. It emphasizes the holistic approach to health, recognizing that afflictions can arise from multiple sources and manifest in different ways.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • Eye Health: In modern life, many people suffer from eye strain due to prolonged screen time. This mantra can serve as a reminder to take breaks and care for our eyes.
    • Respiratory Issues: The mention of nostrils can relate to allergies or respiratory diseases. Practicing yoga or meditation can help in alleviating such issues.
    • Hearing Problems: The reference to ears highlights the importance of protecting our hearing, especially in noisy environments.
    • Speech and Communication: The tongue's mention signifies the importance of clear communication and the impact of words on our relationships.
  • Conclusion and Significance: This mantra underscores the interconnectedness of body parts and the importance of maintaining overall health. It serves as a reminder to be mindful of our physical and mental well-being. In real life, it can be applicable in various scenarios, such as during meditation, yoga practices, or even in daily affirmations for health. By reciting this mantra, individuals can cultivate a sense of awareness and responsibility towards their health, promoting a holistic approach to well-being.
