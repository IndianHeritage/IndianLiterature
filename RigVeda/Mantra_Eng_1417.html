दक्षिणावताम् इद इमानी चित्रा दक्षिणावतां दिवि सूर्याशः |दक्षिणावन्तो अमृतं भजन्ते दक्षिणावन्तः प्र तिरन्त आायुः ||
dakṣiṇāvatām id imāni citrā dakṣiṇāvatāṁ divi sūryāsaḥ | dakṣiṇāvanto amṛtam bhajante dakṣiṇāvantaḥ pra tiranta āyuḥ ||
  • Mantra: "dakṣiṇāvatām id imāni citrā dakṣiṇāvatāṁ divi sūryāsaḥ | dakṣiṇāvanto amṛtam bhajante dakṣiṇāvantaḥ pra tiranta āyuḥ ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • dakṣiṇāvatām: Of those who are endowed with auspiciousness or good fortune.
    • id: This.
    • imāni: These.
    • citrā: Colorful or diverse.
    • divi: In the heavens or celestial realm.
    • sūryāsaḥ: The rays of the sun.
    • amṛtam: Immortality or eternal bliss.
    • bhajante: They enjoy or partake.
    • pra: Forth or forward.
    • tiranta: They who cross over or transcend.
    • āyuḥ: Life or longevity.
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra speaks of those who are blessed with auspiciousness and good fortune.
    • The phrase "citrā dakṣiṇāvatāṁ" suggests a variety of blessings that come from the heavens, akin to the diverse rays of the sun that illuminate the world.
    • The mantra emphasizes the importance of enjoying the gifts of life, represented by "amṛtam," which signifies the pursuit of eternal happiness and fulfillment.
    • It also highlights the idea of transcending life's challenges and achieving longevity, as indicated by "tiranta āyuḥ."
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, individuals who embrace positivity and good fortune often find themselves surrounded by opportunities, much like the diverse rays of the sun that nurture growth.
    • In personal relationships, those who cultivate joy and share their blessings with others contribute to a supportive community, reflecting the essence of "amṛtam."
    • In health and wellness, practices such as meditation and gratitude can help individuals transcend stress and enhance their overall longevity, aligning with the mantra's message of crossing over challenges.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of embracing auspiciousness and sharing blessings in our lives.
    • It encourages individuals to seek joy and fulfillment while transcending obstacles, ultimately leading to a more enriched and longer life.
    • In real-life scenarios, applying the principles of this mantra can foster a positive mindset, enhance relationships, and promote overall well-being.
