अस्मे ā वहतं रयिं शतवन्तं सहस्रिणम् |पुरुक्षुं विश्वधायसम ||
asme ā vahataṁ rayiṁ śatavantaṁ sahasriṇam | purukṣuṁ viśvadhāyasam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • asme: for us
    • ā vahataṁ: may it bring
    • rayiṁ: wealth or prosperity
    • śatavantaṁ: abundant or plentiful
    • sahasriṇam: thousandfold
    • purukṣuṁ: the great being or person
    • viśvadhāyasam: the one who pervades all
  • Overall Meaning: This mantra expresses a prayer for abundant wealth and prosperity to come to us, invoking the great being who pervades all existence. It emphasizes the desire for a thousandfold increase in our resources and well-being.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be seen as a call for success and growth. Entrepreneurs often seek blessings for their ventures to flourish, hoping for a significant increase in profits and resources.
    • In personal life, individuals may recite this mantra during times of financial difficulty, seeking divine intervention for abundance and stability.
    • Communities may use this mantra during festivals or gatherings, wishing for collective prosperity and well-being for all members.
  • Significance and Application:
    • This mantra highlights the importance of seeking abundance not just for oneself but for the community and the world at large.
    • It serves as a reminder that prosperity is not merely material wealth but encompasses overall well-being, including health, happiness, and harmony.
    • In modern life, this mantra can be applied in various settings, such as during financial planning, community development projects, or personal goal-setting, encouraging a mindset of abundance and gratitude.
