तोषा वृत्रहणा हुवे सजित्वानापराजिता | इन्द्राग्नि वाजसातमा ||
tośā vṛtrahaṇā huve sajitvānāparājitā | indrāgnī vājasātamā ||
  • tośā: This word can be interpreted as "joy" or "delight." It signifies the happiness that comes from overcoming obstacles.
  • vṛtrahaṇā: This term means "the slayer of Vritra." Vritra is a serpent or dragon in Vedic mythology that represents chaos and obstacles. Indra, the king of the gods, is known for defeating Vritra, symbolizing the triumph of order over chaos.
  • huve: This translates to "I invoke" or "I call upon." It indicates a plea or a prayer directed towards the divine.
  • sajitvānāparājitā: This phrase can be broken down into "sajitvā" (having conquered) and "anaparājitā" (unconquered or invincible). Together, it suggests a state of being victorious and undefeated.
  • indrāgnī: This refers to Indra and Agni, two powerful deities in Vedic tradition. Indra represents strength and power, while Agni symbolizes fire and transformation.
  • vājasātamā: This term means "the best of strength" or "the most powerful." It emphasizes the idea of supreme power and capability.

The mantra can be understood as a call for divine assistance in overcoming challenges and achieving victory. It invokes the strength of Indra and Agni, seeking their blessings to conquer obstacles and attain success.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as:

  • Personal Challenges: When facing difficulties in life, such as health issues or personal conflicts, one can recite this mantra to seek strength and resilience.
  • Professional Obstacles: In the workplace, when encountering challenges in projects or competition, invoking this mantra can serve as a reminder to stay determined and focused on overcoming hurdles.
  • Spiritual Growth: The mantra can also be used in meditation or spiritual practices to cultivate inner strength and clarity, helping individuals to rise above their fears and doubts.

Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its powerful invocation of divine strength and the assurance of victory over challenges. By calling upon Indra and Agni, individuals can find the courage and determination needed to face life's obstacles, making it a valuable tool for personal empowerment and spiritual growth.
