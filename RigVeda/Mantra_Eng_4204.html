यद बंहिष्ठं नातिविधे सुदानु अचिद्रं शर्मा भुवनस्य गोपा |तेन नो मित्रावरुणाव अविष्टं सिषासन्तो जिगीवांसः स्याम ||
yad baṁhiṣṭhaṁ nātividhe sudānū acchidraṁ śarma bhuvanasya gopā | tena no mitrāvaruṇāv aviṣṭaṁ siṣāsanto jigīvāṁsaḥ syāma ||
  • yad baṁhiṣṭhaṁ: This phrase can be translated as "that which is most excellent" or "the best." It signifies the pursuit of the highest ideals and virtues in life.
  • nātividhe: This means "not to be transgressed" or "beyond the limits." It emphasizes the importance of adhering to moral and ethical boundaries.
  • Sudānū: Referring to "the well-formed" or "the one who is well-structured," this term highlights the significance of having a strong foundation in character and values.
  • acchidraṁ: This translates to "without holes" or "complete." It symbolizes wholeness and integrity, suggesting that one should strive to be undivided in purpose and action.
  • śarma bhuvanasya gopā: This phrase means "the protector of the world’s peace." It indicates the role of guardianship and responsibility towards the well-being of society and the universe.
  • tena no mitrāvaruṇāv: This translates to "may the friends Mitra and Varuna" invoke blessings upon us. Mitra and Varuna are deities representing friendship and cosmic order, respectively.
  • aviṣṭaṁ: Meaning "not to be disturbed" or "unshaken," this word emphasizes the importance of maintaining stability and peace in one’s life.
  • siṣāsanto: This can be interpreted as "those who are peaceful" or "who seek tranquility." It encourages individuals to cultivate peace within themselves and in their surroundings.
  • jigīvāṁsaḥ syāma: This phrase means "may we live long." It expresses a desire for longevity and vitality, not just in terms of lifespan but also in the quality of life.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a profound philosophy of life that emphasizes the pursuit of excellence, integrity, and peace. It encourages individuals to adhere to moral values, protect the well-being of the world, and seek harmony in their lives. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as in leadership roles where ethical decision-making is crucial, in personal relationships where integrity and trust are foundational, and in community service where the focus is on the welfare of others. By embodying the principles outlined in this mantra, individuals can contribute to a more harmonious and balanced society.
