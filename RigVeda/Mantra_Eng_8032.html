तं त्वा नृम्णानि बिभ्रतं सधस्थेषु महो दिवः |चारुं सुखृत्ययेमहे ||
taṁ tvā nṛmṇāni bibhrataṁ sadhastheṣu maho divaḥ | cāruṁ sukṛtyayemahe ||
  • taṁ tvā - This phrase translates to "to you" or "to that," indicating a direction of reverence or devotion towards a higher power or divine entity.
  • nṛmṇāni - This word refers to "human beings" or "mankind," emphasizing the connection between the divine and humanity.
  • bibhrataṁ - This means "holding" or "carrying," suggesting the idea of support or sustenance provided by the divine.
  • sadhastheṣu - This term can be interpreted as "in the good places" or "in the realms of goodness," indicating a focus on positive and virtuous aspects of life.
  • maho divaḥ - This translates to "of the great heavens," referring to the vastness and grandeur of the universe or divine realms.
  • cāruṁ - This word means "beautiful" or "pleasant," highlighting the aesthetic and positive qualities of existence.
  • sukṛtyayemahe - This phrase translates to "we perform good deeds" or "we engage in virtuous actions," emphasizing the importance of righteousness and ethical behavior.

In summary, this mantra expresses a deep reverence for the divine while acknowledging the connection between humanity and the higher realms. It emphasizes the importance of holding onto goodness and engaging in virtuous actions. The mantra serves as a reminder to seek beauty and positivity in our lives and to act in ways that contribute to the well-being of ourselves and others.


Significance and Real-Life Application:

  • This mantra can be applied in daily life by encouraging individuals to focus on positive actions and ethical behavior, fostering a sense of community and support among people.
  • In a practical sense, it can inspire people to engage in charitable acts, volunteer work, or any form of service that uplifts others, thereby creating a ripple effect of goodness in society.
  • Moreover, it serves as a reminder to appreciate the beauty in the world around us, whether through nature, art, or human connections, promoting a mindset of gratitude and positivity.
