अभि प्रयांसी सुदितानि हि ख्यो नि त्वा दधीत rodasī यजध्यै |अवā नो मघवन वाजसाताव अग्ने विश्वानि दुरिता तरेमा ता तरेमा तवावसā तरेमा ||
abhi prayāṁsi sudhitāni hi khyo ni tvā dadhīta rodasī yajadhyai | avā no maghavan vājasātāv agne viśvāni duritā tarema tā tarema tavāvasā tarema ||
  • Mantra: "abhi prayāṁsi sudhitāni hi khyo ni tvā dadhīta rodasī yajadhyai | avā no maghavan vājasātāv agne viśvāni duritā tarema tā tarema tavāvasā tarema ||"
  • Translation: This mantra can be translated as a prayer for guidance and strength. It invokes the divine to help us overcome obstacles and achieve our goals.
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • abhi: towards, in the direction of
    • prayāṁsi: intentions, aspirations
    • sudhitāni: well thought out, wise
    • hi: indeed, certainly
    • khyo: the sky, the heavens
    • ni: to, towards
    • tvā: you
    • dadhīta: may we establish, may we create
    • rodasī: the two worlds, the earth and the sky
    • yajadhyai: for the purpose of worship, for sacrifice
    • avā: may we be protected
    • no: our
    • maghavan: O bountiful one, a name for the divine
    • vājasātāv: the one who gives strength
    • agne: O Agni, the fire god
    • viśvāni: all, every
    • duritā: obstacles, difficulties
    • tarema: may we cross over, may we overcome
    • tavāvasā: your shelter, your protection
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a challenging situation, such as preparing for an important exam, students often seek guidance and support from their teachers and mentors, similar to invoking divine help.
    • During times of personal crisis, individuals may turn to spiritual practices or prayers to find strength and clarity, reflecting the essence of this mantra.
    • In community gatherings, people often come together to pray for collective well-being and to overcome societal challenges, embodying the spirit of unity and support expressed in the mantra.
  • Conclusion: This mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking divine guidance and strength in overcoming life's challenges. It serves as a reminder that we are not alone in our struggles and that with wisdom and support, we can navigate through difficulties. In real life, this mantra can be applied in various situations, such as personal challenges, academic pursuits, and community efforts, encouraging individuals to seek help and work together towards common goals.
