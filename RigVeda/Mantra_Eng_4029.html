विष्वे अस्यां व्युषि माहिनायाः सं यद गोभिर अंगिरसो नवन्त |उत्स आसां परम सदस्थ ऋतस्य पथा सरमा विदद गाः ||
viśve asyā vyuṣi māhināyāḥ saṁ yad gobhir aṅgiraso navanta | utsa āsām parame sadhastha ṛtasya pathā saramā vidad gāḥ ||
  • Translation: The mantra can be translated as: "All these beings, in the morning, as they are nourished by the Aṅgiras, may they come forth with strength. May they, on the path of truth, lead us to the ultimate assembly of the righteous."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • viśve: All beings or all creatures.
    • asyā: This or these.
    • vyuṣi: In the morning or at dawn.
    • māhināyāḥ: Nourished or supported.
    • saṁ: Together or with.
    • yad: As or that which.
    • gobhir: By the Aṅgiras (a group of sages or seers).
    • aṅgiraso: Of the Aṅgiras.
    • navanta: New or fresh.
    • utsa: May they rise or may they come forth.
    • āsām: Of these or of them.
    • parame: Ultimate or supreme.
    • sadhastha: In the assembly or gathering.
    • ṛtasya: Of truth or righteousness.
    • pathā: On the path.
    • saramā: A guiding force or a leader.
    • vidad: May lead or may guide.
    • gāḥ: Beings or creatures.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of community and collective strength. For instance, in a workplace, when team members support each other, they can achieve greater success together.
    • In a family setting, nurturing relationships and encouraging each other can lead to a harmonious and productive environment.
    • In educational institutions, collaboration among students can foster a deeper understanding of subjects and enhance learning experiences.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for unity, strength, and the pursuit of truth. It reminds us that by coming together and supporting one another, we can navigate the challenges of life and reach our highest potential. In real-life scenarios, whether in personal relationships, professional environments, or community engagements, the principles of collaboration, nurturing, and truth-seeking are essential for growth and success. This mantra serves as a guiding light, encouraging us to foster connections and strive for righteousness in our actions.
