नū चित स भ्रेṣते जनो न रेṣन मनो यो अस्य घोरम् आविवासात् |यज्ञैर य इन्द्रे दधते दुvāṁसि क्षयात स राया ऋतपाः ऋतेजाः ||
nū cit sa bhreṣate jano na reṣan mano yo asya ghoram āvivāsāt | yajñair ya indre dadhate duvāṁsi kṣayat sa rāya ṛtapā ṛtejāḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • nū: indeed
    • cit: aware, conscious
    • sa: he, that
    • bhreṣate: is protected, is sheltered
    • jano: the people, the community
    • na: not
    • reṣan: to be disturbed, to be troubled
    • mano: mind, thought
    • yo: who
    • asya: of this, of it
    • ghoram: terrible, fearsome
    • āvivāsāt: has been driven away, has been dispelled
    • yajñair: by sacrifices, by offerings
    • ya: who
    • indre: to Indra, the deity
    • dadhate: they establish, they create
    • duvāṁsi: the two paths, the dualities
    • kṣayat: for the benefit, for the welfare
    • sa: he, that
    • rāya: wealth, prosperity
    • ṛtapā: in accordance with truth, righteousness
    • ṛtejāḥ: with brilliance, with splendor
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the protection and well-being of the community.
    • It emphasizes the importance of being conscious and aware to avoid disturbances and fears.
    • The reference to Indra suggests that divine support and blessings are essential for establishing prosperity and righteousness.
    • The mention of "two paths" indicates the choices we face in life, highlighting the need for wise decision-making.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community setting, this mantra can be applied to promote unity and collective well-being, such as in community service projects.
    • In personal life, being aware of one's thoughts and actions can help in making better decisions, leading to a more fulfilling life.
    • In business, ethical practices aligned with truth and righteousness can lead to sustainable success and prosperity.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of awareness, community support, and ethical living.
    • It teaches us that through conscious efforts and divine guidance, we can dispel fears and achieve prosperity.
    • In real life, it encourages us to make choices that align with truth and righteousness, fostering a harmonious existence.
