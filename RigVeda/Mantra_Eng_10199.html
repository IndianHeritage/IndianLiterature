अदेवाद देवः प्रचता गुहा यन् प्रपश्यमानो अमृतत्वम् एमी |शिवं यत्सन्तम् अशिवो जहामि स्वात्सख्याद अरणीं नाभिम एमी ||
adevād devaḥ pracatā guhā yan prapaśyamāno amṛtatvam emi | śivaṁ yat santam aśivo jahāmi svāt sakhyād araṇīṁ nābhim emi ||
  • Translation: The mantra can be translated as: "From the non-existent, the divine emerges; when I perceive the hidden, I attain immortality. I abandon the unholy and embrace the auspicious; from the friendship of the sacred, I reach the source."
  • Word-by-Word Explanation:
    • Adevād: From the non-existent or the void.
    • Devaḥ: The divine or the godly essence.
    • Pracatā: Emerges or manifests.
    • Guhā: The hidden or the secret.
    • Yan: When or that which.
    • Prapaśyamāno: Perceiving or observing.
    • Amṛtatvam: Immortality or eternal life.
    • Emi: I attain or I reach.
    • Śivaṁ: The auspicious or the sacred.
    • Yat: That which.
    • Santam: The peaceful or the tranquil.
    • Aśivo: The unholy or the inauspicious.
    • Jahāmi: I abandon or I let go.
    • Svāt: From the friendship or the companionship.
    • Sakhyād: Of the sacred or the divine.
    • Araṇīṁ: The source or the origin.
    • Nābhim: The center or the core.
    • Emi: I reach or I attain.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In personal growth, one often has to let go of negative influences (Aśivo) to embrace positive ones (Śivaṁ) to achieve a fulfilling life.
    • In a professional setting, recognizing hidden talents (Guhā) within oneself or others can lead to greater success and innovation (Amṛtatvam).
    • In relationships, fostering friendships based on mutual respect and understanding (Svāt) can lead to deeper connections and a sense of belonging (Nābhim).
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its profound message about transformation and the journey from ignorance to enlightenment. It emphasizes the importance of recognizing the divine within the hidden aspects of life and the necessity of abandoning negativity to embrace positivity. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can guide individuals in their personal, professional, and spiritual journeys, encouraging them to seek the sacred in their experiences and relationships, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling and meaningful existence.
