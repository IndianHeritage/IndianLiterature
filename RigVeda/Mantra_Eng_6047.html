या ऋते चिद्गास्पदिभ्यो दात्सखानृभ्यः शचिवान् |ये अस्मिन्काममश्रियन् ||
ya ṛte cid gās padebhyo dāt sakhā nṛbhyaḥ śacīvān | ye asmin kāmam aśriyan ||
  • ya ṛte: This phrase translates to "without" or "except for." It indicates a condition or a limitation.
  • cid: This word means "if" or "whether," introducing a hypothetical situation.
  • gās: This refers to "songs" or "hymns," often associated with praise or devotion.
  • padebhyo: This means "from the feet" or "from the positions," suggesting a source or origin.
  • dāt: This translates to "give" or "bestow," indicating an act of generosity or sharing.
  • sakhā: This means "friend" or "companion," emphasizing the importance of relationships.
  • nṛbhyaḥ: This translates to "to men" or "to people," indicating the recipients of the action.
  • śacīvān: This word means "powerful" or "capable," suggesting strength or ability.
  • ye: This means "those who," referring to a group of people or entities.
  • asmin: This translates to "in this" or "in this context," indicating a specific situation or environment.
  • kāmam: This means "desire" or "wish," referring to aspirations or goals.
  • aśriyan: This translates to "have taken refuge" or "have relied upon," indicating a sense of trust or dependence.

The mantra can be interpreted as a reflection on the importance of friendship, support, and the fulfillment of desires in a communal context. It suggests that without the songs or praises that come from a place of unity and strength, the act of giving and sharing among friends becomes significant. It emphasizes that those who take refuge in their desires and aspirations, supported by their companions, are empowered to achieve their goals.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios:

  • Community Support: In a community setting, individuals often rely on each other for emotional and practical support. For example, during challenging times, friends and family come together to provide assistance, reflecting the essence of this mantra.
  • Teamwork: In professional environments, collaboration among team members can lead to greater success. When individuals work together, share their strengths, and support each other's goals, they create a powerful dynamic that enhances productivity.
  • Personal Relationships: The mantra highlights the significance of friendships in achieving personal desires. For instance, when pursuing a career goal, having friends who encourage and support you can make a significant difference in your journey.

In conclusion, this mantra underscores the significance of unity, friendship, and mutual support in achieving desires and aspirations. It serves as a reminder that our relationships with others play a crucial role in our personal and communal success. By fostering strong connections and supporting one another, we can navigate life's challenges more effectively and realize our dreams.
