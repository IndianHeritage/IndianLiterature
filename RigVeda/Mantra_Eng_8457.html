त्वं समुद्रोऽसि विश्ववित् कवेद्वेमाः पञ्च प्रदिशो विधर्मणि |त्वं dyāṁ च पृथिवीं चाति जभ्रिषे तव ज्योतिंषि पवमान सूर्यः ||
tvaṁ samudro asi viśvavit kave tavemāḥ pañca pradiśo vidharmaṇi | tvaṁ dyāṁ ca pṛthivīṁ cāti jabhriṣe tava jyotīṁṣi pavamāna sūryaḥ ||
  • tvaṁ samudro asi viśvavit: This phrase translates to "You are the ocean, the all-knowing." Here, the ocean symbolizes vastness and depth, representing the infinite knowledge and wisdom that one can attain. In real life, this can be likened to the pursuit of knowledge through education and experience, where individuals seek to understand the complexities of life, much like exploring the depths of the ocean.
  • kave tavemāḥ pañca pradiśo vidharmaṇi: This means "O poet, you hold the five directions in your grasp." The five directions (north, south, east, west, and the center) symbolize the comprehensive nature of existence. In practical terms, this can be seen in leadership roles where individuals must consider multiple perspectives and directions to make informed decisions, akin to a leader navigating through various challenges.
  • tvaṁ dyāṁ ca pṛthivīṁ cāti jabhriṣe: This translates to "You encompass both the sky and the earth." This phrase emphasizes the connection between the spiritual and the material worlds. In everyday life, this can be reflected in the balance one must maintain between aspirations (sky) and practical realities (earth), such as pursuing dreams while being grounded in reality.
  • tava jyotīṁṣi pavamāna sūryaḥ: This means "Your lights, O Sun, are purifying." The sun is often associated with enlightenment and clarity. In real life, this can be interpreted as the pursuit of truth and clarity in one's actions and thoughts, much like how sunlight dispels darkness. It encourages individuals to seek knowledge and understanding to purify their intentions and actions.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of knowledge, leadership, balance, and enlightenment. Its significance lies in the reminder that we are all interconnected with the universe, and our pursuit of wisdom and understanding can lead to personal and collective growth. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire individuals to seek knowledge, embrace diverse perspectives, maintain balance in their lives, and strive for clarity in their intentions, ultimately leading to a more harmonious existence.
