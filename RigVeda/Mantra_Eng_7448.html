युवं हि स्मा पुरुभुजेमम एधतुः विष्णापवे ददथुर वस्यैष्टये |तां वां विश्वको हवते तनूकृतhe मा नो वियौष्टं सख्या मुमोचतम् ||
yuvaṁ hi ṣmā purubhujemam edhatuṁ viṣṇāpve dadathur vasyaïṣṭaye | tā vāṁ viśvako havate tanūkṛthe mā no vi yauṣṭaṁ sakhyā mumocatam ||
  • yuvaṁ hi ṣmā purubhujemam edhatuṁ: This phrase can be translated as "O youthful one, may you be endowed with many forms." Here, "yuvaṁ" refers to youth or vitality, and "purubhujemam" suggests the idea of being multifaceted or versatile. In real life, this can be seen in individuals who possess diverse skills and talents, allowing them to adapt to various situations.
  • viṣṇāpve dadathur vasyaïṣṭaye: This part translates to "may the divine Vishnu grant you the ability to control your desires." Vishnu, in Hindu mythology, is often associated with preservation and protection. This line emphasizes the importance of self-control and discipline in achieving one's goals. For example, a student who practices self-discipline in their studies is more likely to succeed academically.
  • tā vāṁ viśvako havate tanūkṛthe: This can be interpreted as "those who are universal in nature, may they be beneficial to you." The term "viśvako" implies universality or inclusiveness. In a real-world context, this could refer to the importance of community and collaboration. For instance, a team that works together harmoniously can achieve greater success than individuals working in isolation.
  • mā no vi yauṣṭaṁ sakhyā mumocatam: This translates to "may we not be deprived of our strength and friendship." This line highlights the significance of camaraderie and support in overcoming challenges. In everyday life, this can be seen in friendships that provide emotional support during tough times, such as friends helping each other through personal struggles or professional setbacks.

  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of youthfulness, versatility, self-control, community, and friendship. In real life, these qualities are essential for personal growth and success. By embodying these principles, individuals can navigate life's challenges more effectively, fostering a supportive environment that encourages collaboration and mutual respect. This mantra serves as a reminder to cultivate these attributes in our daily lives, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling and harmonious existence.
