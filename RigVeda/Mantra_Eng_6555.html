न ते सव्यं न दक्षिणं हस्तं वरन्त आमुरः |न परिबाधो हरिवो गविष्टिषु ||
na te savyaṁ na dakṣiṇaṁ hastaṁ varanta āmuraḥ | na paribādho harivo gaviṣṭiṣu ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • na: not
    • te: your
    • savyam: left
    • na: not
    • dakṣiṇam: right
    • hastaṁ: hand
    • varanta: to be restrained
    • āmuraḥ: the one who is powerful
    • na: not
    • paribādho: obstruction
    • harivo: of the divine
    • gaviṣṭiṣu: in the realms of existence
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the divine hands that are neither left nor right, suggesting a balance and neutrality in divine action.
    • The phrase "not to be restrained" indicates that the divine power is free and unbound, capable of acting in any situation without obstruction.
    • The mention of "harivo" refers to the divine presence, emphasizing that this power operates in all realms of existence.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In decision-making, one often faces choices that seem to require a left or right approach. This mantra reminds us that sometimes the best path is a balanced one, where we do not lean too heavily on one side.
    • In conflict resolution, the idea of not being restrained can be applied. A mediator may need to act without bias, allowing for a free flow of dialogue and understanding between opposing parties.
    • In personal growth, the mantra encourages individuals to embrace their full potential without the fear of limitations, suggesting that divine support is always present in their endeavors.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of balance and the power of divine support in our lives. It teaches us that we can navigate through challenges without being confined to binary choices.
    • In real-life scenarios, whether in personal decisions, professional challenges, or interpersonal relationships, the essence of this mantra can guide us to seek harmony and embrace the freedom to act without obstruction.
    • Ultimately, it serves as a reminder that divine energy is always available to assist us in our journey, encouraging us to act with confidence and balance.
