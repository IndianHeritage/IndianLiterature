त्वं ह्य अग्ने दिव्यस्य राजसी त्वं पार्थिवस्य पशुपा इव तमनā |एनī त एते बृहती अभिश्रियā हिरण्यī वक्वरी बर्हिर आशाते ||
tvaṁ hy agne divyasya rājasi tvam pārthivasya paśupā iva tmanā | enī ta ete bṛhatī abhiśriyā hiraṇyayī vakvarī barhir āśāte ||
  • tvaṁ hy agne: This phrase translates to "You, O Agni." Agni is the Vedic fire god, symbolizing energy, transformation, and purification. In real life, fire is essential for cooking, warmth, and rituals, representing the transformative power of energy.
  • divyasya rājasi: This means "of the divine, you are the king." It signifies the supreme nature of Agni as a divine force. In our lives, we often look to leaders or guiding principles that embody divine qualities, such as wisdom and justice.
  • tvam pārthivasya paśupā iva tmanā: This translates to "you are like the lord of the earthly beings." Here, Agni is compared to a protector or guardian of all living beings. In a real-world context, this can be likened to how leaders or guardians protect their communities and ensure their well-being.
  • enī ta ete bṛhatī abhiśriyā: This phrase can be interpreted as "these great ones are drawn to you." It suggests that powerful forces or beings are attracted to Agni. In life, we often find that positive energy and leadership attract others, creating a community or movement.
  • hiraṇyayī vakvarī: This means "the golden, radiant one." It emphasizes the brilliance and purity of Agni. In our daily lives, we seek out and aspire to embody qualities that are radiant and uplifting, such as kindness and generosity.
  • barhir āśāte: This translates to "the one who is adorned with the sacred grass." This symbolizes the rituals and offerings made to Agni, representing the importance of respect and reverence in our lives. In modern contexts, this can be seen in how we honor traditions and values that are important to us.

Conclusion: The mantra highlights the significance of Agni as a divine force that transforms, protects, and attracts positive energies. In real life, this can be applied to our understanding of leadership, community, and the importance of embodying qualities that uplift ourselves and others. By recognizing the transformative power of energy in our lives, we can strive to be like Agni—radiant, protective, and a source of inspiration for those around us.
