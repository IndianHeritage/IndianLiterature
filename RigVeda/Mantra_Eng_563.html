यन् नासत्यā परावती यद vā स्थो अधि तुर्वशे |अतो रथेन सुवृतā न ā गतं साकं सूर्यस्य रश्मिभिः ||
yan nāsatyā parāvati yad vā stho adhi turvaśe | ato rathena suvṛtā na ā gataṁ sākaṁ sūryasya raśmibhiḥ ||
  • yan nāsatyā parāvati: This phrase can be translated to "what is not deceitful or false." It emphasizes the importance of truth and integrity in our actions and thoughts. In real life, this can be seen in the context of honesty in relationships, where trust is foundational. For example, a business that operates transparently and ethically builds a loyal customer base.
  • yad vā stho adhi turvaśe: This part means "what is established in the highest place." It suggests striving for excellence and aiming for the highest ideals. In practical terms, this can relate to personal goals, such as pursuing higher education or professional development. For instance, a student who aims for top grades and works diligently towards that goal embodies this principle.
  • ato rathena suvṛtā: Translated, this means "therefore, with a well-directed chariot." The chariot symbolizes the journey of life, and a well-directed chariot represents having a clear purpose and direction. In everyday life, this can be likened to setting clear goals and having a plan to achieve them. For example, an athlete training for a competition must have a structured training regimen to succeed.
  • na ā gataṁ sākaṁ sūryasya raśmibhiḥ: This phrase translates to "not having come together with the rays of the sun." It implies the idea of not being in harmony with the natural order or the universal truth. In real life, this can be interpreted as the consequences of living out of sync with one’s values or the environment. For instance, a company that neglects environmental sustainability may face backlash and loss of reputation.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of truth, integrity, and striving for excellence while maintaining a clear direction in life. It serves as a reminder to align our actions with our values and the natural order. In real-life scenarios, applying these principles can lead to personal growth, successful relationships, and a positive impact on society. By embodying these ideals, individuals can navigate their journeys with purpose and authenticity, ultimately contributing to a more harmonious world.
