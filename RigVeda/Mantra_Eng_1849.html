असाम यथा सुशखाय एन स्वभिष्टयो नरां न शंसैः |असद यथा न इन्द्रो वन्दनेष्ठास तुरो न कर्म नयमान उक्ताः ||
asāma yathā suṣakhāya ena svabhiṣṭayo narāṁ na śaṁsaiḥ | asad yathā na indro vandaneṣṭhās turo na karma nayamāna ukthā ||
  • asāma yathā suṣakhāya: This phrase can be interpreted as "like the unbroken, auspicious path." It suggests a journey or a way of life that is smooth and filled with good fortune.
  • ena svabhiṣṭayo narāṁ na śaṁsaiḥ: This translates to "by this, the desires of men are fulfilled without hindrance." It emphasizes the idea that following the auspicious path leads to the fulfillment of one's desires and aspirations.
  • asad yathā na indro: This part means "as the non-existent does not hinder Indra." Here, Indra represents strength and power, indicating that negativity or non-existence does not obstruct true strength.
  • vandaneṣṭhās turo na karma nayamāna ukthā: This can be understood as "the one who is worshipped does not lead to failure in action." It implies that sincere devotion and worship lead to successful outcomes in one's endeavors.

  • Real-Life Example: Consider a student preparing for an important exam. If they follow a disciplined study routine (the auspicious path), they are more likely to achieve their desired grades (fulfillment of desires). If they remain focused and avoid distractions (non-existent hindrances), their efforts will lead to success.
  • Significance: This mantra highlights the importance of following a righteous and auspicious path in life. It teaches that by aligning oneself with positive actions and intentions, one can achieve their goals without unnecessary obstacles.
  • Application: In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios such as career planning, personal relationships, and spiritual growth. By maintaining a positive mindset and taking constructive actions, individuals can navigate challenges effectively and reach their aspirations.

  • Conclusion: The essence of this mantra lies in the belief that a life led by positive intentions and actions will yield fruitful results. It encourages individuals to remain steadfast in their pursuits, worship the divine, and trust that their efforts will not go in vain. By embodying these principles, one can create a fulfilling and successful life.
