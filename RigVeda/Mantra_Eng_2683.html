यदी मन्थन्ति बाहुभिर वि रोचते 'श्वो न वाज्य अरुषो वनेष्व आ | चित्रो न याम्न्न अश्विनोर अनिवृतः परि वृणक्त्य अश्मनस तृणां दहन ||
yadī manthanti bāhubhir vi rocate 'śvo na vājy aruṣo vaneṣv ā | citro na yāmann aśvinor anivṛtaḥ pari vṛṇakty aśmanas tṛṇā dahan ||
  • Mantra: "yadī manthanti bāhubhir vi rocate 'śvo na vājy aruṣo vaneṣv ā | citro na yāmann aśvinor anivṛtaḥ pari vṛṇakty aśmanas tṛṇā dahan ||"
  • Translation: This mantra speaks of the power of collaboration and the importance of unity in achieving goals. It suggests that when individuals work together, they can create something beautiful and impactful, much like the Aśvins (the twin gods of medicine and health) who are known for their swift actions and healing abilities.
  • Word Breakdown:
    • yadī: if
    • manthanti: they churn
    • bāhubhir: with arms
    • vi rocate: it shines
    • śvo: the dawn
    • na vājy: not to be neglected
    • aruṣo: red, symbolizing energy
    • vaneṣv ā: in the forests
    • citro: colorful, beautiful
    • na yāmann: not to be forgotten
    • aśvinor: of the Aśvins
    • anivṛtaḥ: unrestrained
    • pari vṛṇakty: encompasses
    • aśmanas: of stones
    • tṛṇā: grass
    • dahan: burning
  • Real-Life Example: Consider a community project where individuals come together to build a playground. Each person contributes their unique skills—some may be carpenters, others may be painters, and some may provide food for the volunteers. When they work together, the project not only gets completed faster but also results in a vibrant and joyful space for children, symbolizing the beauty that arises from collaboration.
  • Significance: This mantra emphasizes the importance of teamwork and unity. In today's world, where individualism often prevails, it serves as a reminder that collective efforts can lead to greater achievements. Whether in a workplace, a community, or even in family settings, the mantra encourages individuals to come together, share their strengths, and create something meaningful.
  • Conclusion: The essence of this mantra lies in its call for collaboration and the celebration of diversity in efforts. It teaches us that when we unite our strengths, we can overcome challenges and create a lasting impact, much like the Aśvins who embody the spirit of healing and support. In real life, this principle can be applied in various scenarios, from professional environments to personal relationships, fostering a sense of community and shared purpose.
