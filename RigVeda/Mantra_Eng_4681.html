अधā मण्ये बृहद् असूर्यम् अस्य यानि दाधार नकीर ā मिनाति |दिवे-दिवे सूर्यो दर्शतो भूद् विसद्मान्य उर्वियā सुक्रतुर धात् ||
adhā manye bṛhad asuryam asya yāni dādhāra nakir ā mināti | dive-dive sūryo darśato bhūd vi sadmāny urviyā sukratur dhāt ||
  • adhā manye: This phrase can be translated as "I think deeply" or "I contemplate." It signifies the importance of reflection and understanding in our lives.
  • bṛhad asuryam: This means "the great sun" or "the vast light." The sun is often seen as a symbol of knowledge, enlightenment, and truth.
  • asya yāni dādhāra: This translates to "that which supports this." It implies the foundational elements or principles that uphold existence and reality.
  • nakir ā mināti: This can be interpreted as "not to be diminished" or "not to be reduced." It suggests the enduring nature of truth and knowledge.
  • dive-dive: This phrase means "day by day" or "each day." It emphasizes the continuous journey of life and learning.
  • sūryo darśato: This translates to "the sun that is seen" or "the visible sun." It represents clarity, perception, and the ability to see things as they are.
  • bhūd vi sadmāny: This means "may there be a dwelling" or "may there be a place." It signifies the importance of having a safe and nurturing environment.
  • urviyā sukratur dhāt: This can be interpreted as "may the one who acts rightly" or "the one who performs good deeds." It highlights the significance of righteous actions and their impact on the world.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda emphasizes the importance of contemplation, the pursuit of knowledge, and the enduring nature of truth. It encourages individuals to reflect on their actions and the environment they create around them. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios such as personal growth, ethical decision-making, and fostering a supportive community. By understanding and embodying these principles, individuals can contribute positively to society and enhance their own lives through continuous learning and righteous actions.
