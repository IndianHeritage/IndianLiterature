तेषां देवेष्व आयतिर अस्माकं तेषु नाभयः |तेषाम पदेन मह्य ā नाम गिरेन्द्राग्नī ā नाम गिरा ||
teṣāṁ deveṣv āyatir asmākaṁ teṣu nābhayaḥ | teṣām padena mahy ā name girendrāgnī ā name girā ||
  • teṣāṁ - This word translates to "of them" or "their," referring to the deities or divine beings.
  • deveṣv - This means "in the gods" or "among the deities," indicating a connection to the divine.
  • āyatir - This can be interpreted as "protection" or "shelter," suggesting a plea for safety and guidance.
  • asmākaṁ - This translates to "of us," indicating a personal or communal relationship with the divine.
  • teṣu - Meaning "in them," it emphasizes the presence of the divine in the lives of the devotees.
  • nābhayaḥ - This means "without fear," suggesting a state of security and confidence derived from divine protection.
  • teṣām - Again referring to "their," reinforcing the connection to the deities.
  • padena - This translates to "by the path" or "through the way," indicating a journey or method of approach.
  • mahy - Meaning "to me," it personalizes the request for divine guidance.
  • ā name - This phrase means "I bow" or "I offer my respects," indicating reverence.
  • girendrāgnī - This can be interpreted as "the great fire" or "the fire of the gods," symbolizing purity, transformation, and divine energy.
  • girā - Meaning "speech" or "word," it signifies the power of communication and expression.

The mantra essentially expresses a plea for divine protection and guidance. It acknowledges the presence of the deities in the lives of the devotees and seeks a path that leads to fearlessness and security. The invocation of "girendrāgnī" symbolizes the transformative power of divine energy, while "girā" emphasizes the importance of communication with the divine.


In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts:

  • Personal Challenges: When facing difficulties or fears, reciting this mantra can serve as a reminder of the divine support available, encouraging individuals to seek guidance and protection.
  • Community Support: In communal settings, this mantra can foster a sense of unity and collective strength, reminding members of the divine presence that protects and guides them.
  • Spiritual Practices: Incorporating this mantra into daily rituals or meditation can enhance one's spiritual connection, promoting a sense of peace and fearlessness.

In conclusion, this mantra holds significant importance as it encapsulates the essence of seeking divine protection and guidance. It serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness between the divine and human experience, encouraging individuals to embrace their spiritual journey with confidence and reverence.
