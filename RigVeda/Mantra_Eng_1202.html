आ मनीषाम अन्तरिक्षस्य नृभ्यः स्रुचेव घृतं जुहवाम विद्मना |तरणित्वा ये पितुर अस्य सच्चिर ऋभवो वाजम् अरुहन दिवो रजः ||
ā manīṣām antarikṣasya nṛbhyaḥ sruceva ghṛtaṁ juhavāma vidmanā | taraṇitvā ye pitur asya saścira ṛbhavo vājam aruhan divo rajaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā manīṣām: From the thoughts or intentions
    • antarikṣasya: Of the sky or the space
    • nṛbhyaḥ: To the men or humanity
    • sruceva: Like the flow or stream
    • ghṛtaṁ: Ghee or clarified butter
    • juhavāma: We offer or we invoke
    • vidmanā: With knowledge or wisdom
    • taraṇitvā: With the ability to traverse or cross
    • ye: Those who
    • pitur: Of the father or progenitor
    • asya: Of this
    • saścira: For a long time or enduring
    • ṛbhavo: The wise or enlightened ones
    • vājam: The strength or power
    • aruhan: Who have ascended or risen
    • dīvo: Of the heavens
    • rajaḥ: The realm or domain
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the connection between human intentions and the vastness of the universe.
    • It emphasizes the importance of offering our thoughts and actions with wisdom, akin to how ghee is offered in rituals.
    • The reference to traversing the sky suggests the potential for human beings to rise above their limitations and reach greater heights.
    • It acknowledges the role of wise individuals who have the strength to ascend to higher realms, both spiritually and intellectually.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern life, this mantra can be related to the pursuit of knowledge and wisdom in various fields, such as science, philosophy, and spirituality.
    • For instance, scientists who explore the universe embody the idea of "traversing the sky," pushing the boundaries of human understanding.
    • In personal development, individuals who strive to improve themselves and contribute positively to society reflect the essence of this mantra.
    • Community leaders who inspire others to rise and achieve their potential can be seen as the "wise ones" mentioned in the mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of aligning our thoughts and actions with a higher purpose, fostering wisdom and strength.
    • It encourages individuals to rise above challenges and contribute to the greater good, emphasizing the interconnectedness of humanity and the universe.
    • In practical terms, it serves as a reminder to approach life with intention, wisdom, and a desire to uplift oneself and others.
