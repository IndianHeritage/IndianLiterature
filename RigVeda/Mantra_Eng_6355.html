त्वं हि स्तोमवर्धन इन्द्रास्य उक्तवर्धनः | स्तोत्रीणामुत भद्रकृत ||
tvaṁ hi stomavardhana indrāsy ukthavardhanaḥ | stotṝṇām uta bhadrakṛt ||
  • tvaṁ hi stomavardhana: This phrase translates to "You are indeed the one who increases the praises." Here, "tvaṁ" means "you," "hi" emphasizes the statement, and "stomavardhana" refers to the act of enhancing or increasing praises or hymns. In real life, this can be likened to a leader or a mentor who inspires and elevates the efforts of those around them, much like a teacher who encourages students to excel.
  • indrāsy ukthavardhanaḥ: This part means "the one who increases the offerings to Indra." "Indra" is a prominent deity in the Rigveda, often associated with rain, thunder, and war. "Ukthavardhanaḥ" refers to the enhancement of offerings or hymns dedicated to Indra. In a modern context, this can be seen in how communities come together to celebrate and honor their traditions, enhancing their cultural practices through collective efforts.
  • stotṝṇām uta bhadrakṛt: This translates to "the one who brings good fortune to the praisers." "Stotṝṇām" refers to those who praise or offer hymns, while "uta" means "and," and "bhadrakṛt" signifies the act of creating or bringing about good fortune. This can be related to how positive reinforcement and recognition in workplaces or communities can lead to greater success and well-being for individuals and groups alike.

  • Significance: This mantra emphasizes the importance of praise, recognition, and the collective effort in enhancing spiritual and communal well-being. It highlights the role of a leader or deity in uplifting the community through encouragement and support.
  • Real-life Application: In everyday life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios such as in educational settings where teachers uplift students, in workplaces where managers recognize employee efforts, or in community gatherings where collective praise fosters unity and motivation. It serves as a reminder that acknowledgment and support can lead to greater achievements and harmony.
