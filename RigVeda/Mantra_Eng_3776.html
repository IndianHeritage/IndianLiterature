अस्य हि स्वयशस्तर आसां विधर्मन् मन्यसे |तं नाकं चित्रशोचिषम् मन्त्रं परो मनिषया ||
asya hi svayaśastara āsā vidharman manyase | taṁ nākaṁ citraśociṣam mandram paro manīṣayā ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • asya: of this
    • hi: indeed
    • svayaśastara: self-illuminated or self-glorifying
    • āsā: hope or aspiration
    • vidharman: the one who follows the path of righteousness
    • manyase: you think or you believe
    • taṁ: that
    • nākaṁ: the celestial realm or heaven
    • citraśociṣam: brightly shining or radiant
    • mandram: a hymn or mantra
    • paro: supreme or higher
    • manīṣayā: with wisdom or intellect
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the self-illuminated nature of hope and righteousness.
    • It emphasizes the importance of following a righteous path and the belief in a higher, radiant existence.
    • The phrase "you think" suggests a personal reflection on one's beliefs and aspirations.
    • The mention of "the celestial realm" indicates a connection to a higher state of being or consciousness.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, an individual who follows ethical practices and maintains integrity can be seen as embodying the essence of "vidharman." Their self-illuminated character inspires others to aspire to similar values.
    • In personal relationships, having hope and righteousness can lead to stronger bonds, as individuals support each other in their aspirations and moral choices.
    • In community service, those who work selflessly for the betterment of society reflect the qualities of "svayaśastara," shining brightly in their commitment to helping others.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the significance of hope, righteousness, and the pursuit of a higher purpose in life.
    • It encourages individuals to reflect on their beliefs and actions, promoting a life that is not only self-fulfilling but also beneficial to others.
    • In today's world, where ethical dilemmas are common, this mantra can guide individuals in making choices that align with their values and contribute positively to society.
