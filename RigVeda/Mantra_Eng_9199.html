युवं च्यवानं सनयं यथा रथम पुनर युवानं चरथाय तक्षथुः |निष टौग्रम ऊहथुर adbhyas परि विश्वेत ता वां सवनेषु प्रवाच्या ||
yuvaṁ cyavānaṁ sanayaṁ yathā ratham punar yuvānaṁ carathāya takṣathuḥ | niṣ ṭaugryam ūhathur adbhyas pari viśvet tā vāṁ savaneṣu pravācyā ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yuvaṁ: young
    • cyavānaṁ: the one who has fallen or strayed
    • sanayaṁ: to be united or brought together
    • yathā: as
    • ratham: chariot
    • punar: again
    • yuvānaṁ: the young ones
    • carathāya: for moving or progressing
    • takṣathuḥ: may they create or build
    • niṣ ṭaugryam: the strength or power
    • ūhathur: they have invoked or called upon
    • adbhyas: from the waters
    • pari: all around
    • viśvet: all beings
    • tā: those
    • vāṁ: you
    • savanṣu: in the offerings
    • pravācyā: may they be spoken or declared
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the rejuvenation and unity of youth, symbolized by the chariot, which represents progress and movement in life.
    • The phrase "yuvaṁ cyavānaṁ" suggests that even those who have strayed or fallen can be brought back together, emphasizing the importance of community and support.
    • The call to "takṣathuḥ" indicates a creative force, urging the young to build and innovate, which is essential in any society.
    • The invocation of strength from the waters ("adbhyas") symbolizes the nurturing and life-giving aspects of nature, reminding us of our connection to the environment.
    • Finally, the mantra encourages collective action and communication ("pravācyā"), highlighting the significance of sharing knowledge and experiences.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In educational settings, this mantra can be applied to foster collaboration among students, encouraging them to support each other in their learning journeys.
    • In community service, the idea of bringing together those who have strayed can be seen in programs aimed at rehabilitating individuals and reintegrating them into society.
    • In environmental initiatives, the reference to strength from water can inspire efforts to protect natural resources and promote sustainability.
    • In workplaces, the emphasis on creativity and innovation can drive teams to work together to develop new solutions and improve processes.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra encapsulates the essence of youth, unity, and creativity, urging individuals to come together for collective progress.
    • Its significance lies in its timeless message of support, innovation, and the importance of nurturing our environment.
    • In real life, it serves as a reminder that we can overcome challenges through collaboration and that every individual has the potential to contribute positively to society.
