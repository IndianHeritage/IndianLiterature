सहो षु णो वज्रहस्तैः कण्वासो अग्निम मरुद्भिः |स्तुषे हिरण्यवाशीभिः ||
saho ṣu ṇo vajrahastaiḥ kaṇvāso agnim marudbhiḥ | stuṣe hiraṇyavāśībhiḥ ||
  • Word Breakdown:
    • saho: Together, united.
    • ṣu: Good, auspicious.
    • ṇo: Our.
    • vajrahastaiḥ: With the hands of thunder, symbolizing strength and power.
    • kaṇvāso: The wise or learned ones, referring to the sages or seers.
    • agnim: Fire, representing energy, transformation, and the divine.
    • marudbhiḥ: With the winds, indicating movement and change.
    • stuṣe: Praise or worship.
    • hiraṇyavāśībhiḥ: With golden or prosperous words, suggesting richness in expression and thought.
  • Meaning:
    • This mantra calls for unity and strength among individuals, emphasizing the importance of collaboration and collective effort.
    • The reference to "vajrahastaiḥ" suggests that with strength and determination, one can overcome obstacles, much like a thunderbolt that breaks through barriers.
    • The invocation of "agnim" signifies the need for transformation and the harnessing of energy for positive outcomes.
    • By praising with "hiraṇyavāśībhiḥ," the mantra highlights the significance of using uplifting and enriching language in our interactions.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a team setting, when members work together harmoniously, they can achieve greater results, akin to the collective strength mentioned in the mantra.
    • In personal development, embracing change and transformation (symbolized by fire) can lead to significant growth and success.
    • Using positive and encouraging language in communication can foster a supportive environment, enhancing relationships and productivity.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the power of unity, strength, and positive communication in achieving goals.
    • Its significance lies in its applicability to various aspects of life, including personal growth, teamwork, and effective communication.
    • By embodying the principles of this mantra, individuals can create a more harmonious and productive environment in both personal and professional spheres.
