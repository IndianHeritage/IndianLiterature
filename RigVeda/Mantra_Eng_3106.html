मन्त्र: रक्षाणो अग्ने तव रक्षणेभिः रारक्षाणः सुमख प्रीणानः |प्रतिः स्पुर वि रुज वीद्व अंहो जहि रक्षो महि चिद वावृद्धानम् ||
rakṣā ṇo agne tava rakṣaṇebhī rārakṣāṇaḥ sumakha prīṇānaḥ | prati ṣphura vi ruja vīḍv aṁho jahi rakṣo mahi cid vāvṛdhānam ||
  • Translation: The mantra can be translated as: "O Agni, may your protective powers guard us; may those who are well-disposed towards us be pleased. Let the harmful forces be dispelled, and may we be protected from all dangers."
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • rakṣā: protection
    • ṇo: for us
    • agne: O Agni (the divine fire)
    • tava: your
    • rakṣaṇebhī: by your protective powers
    • rārakṣāṇaḥ: those who are well-disposed
    • sumakha: pleasing or auspicious
    • prīṇānaḥ: may they be pleased
    • prati: against
    • ṣphura: let it shine or manifest
    • vi: away
    • ruja: pain or harm
    • vīḍv: let it be dispelled
    • aṁho: from us
    • jahi: destroy
    • rakṣo: dangers or evil forces
    • mahi: great
    • cid: consciousness or awareness
    • vāvṛdhānam: may it be protected or increased
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, individuals often seek protection from various forms of harm, whether physical, emotional, or spiritual. This mantra serves as a reminder to invoke protective energies in times of distress.
    • During significant life events, such as weddings or new ventures, people may recite this mantra to ensure a safe and auspicious beginning, reflecting the desire for positive energies to surround them.
    • In a broader context, communities may come together to chant this mantra during festivals or rituals, emphasizing collective protection against societal challenges or natural calamities.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its invocation of divine protection and the dispelling of negative forces. It emphasizes the importance of seeking safety and well-being, both individually and collectively. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied during moments of vulnerability, transitions, or communal gatherings, reinforcing the belief in the power of positive energies and divine guardianship. By reciting this mantra, individuals and communities can cultivate a sense of security and harmony in their lives.
