प्रयुञ्जती दिव एति ब्रुवाणा मही माता दूहितुर बोधयन्ती |आविवासन्ती युवतिर मनिषा पितृभ्य आ सदने जोहुवाना ||
prayuñjatī diva eti bruvāṇā mahī mātā duhitur bodhayantī | āvivāsantī yuvatir manīṣā pitṛbhya ā sadane johuvānā ||
  • Translation: The mantra can be translated as: "The one who is wise and speaks, the great mother, awakens her daughter. The young woman, endowed with intelligence, calls upon her ancestors in the abode."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • prayuñjatī: Refers to someone who is wise or knowledgeable.
    • diva: Means 'heaven' or 'divine'.
    • eti: Translates to 'comes' or 'arrives'.
    • bruvāṇā: Means 'speaking' or 'expressing'.
    • mahī: Refers to 'great' or 'earth'.
    • mātā: Means 'mother'.
    • duhitur: Refers to 'daughter'.
    • bodhayantī: Means 'awakening' or 'enlightening'.
    • āvivāsantī: Refers to 'endowed' or 'possessing'.
    • yuvatir: Means 'young woman'.
    • manīṣā: Refers to 'intelligence' or 'wisdom'.
    • pitṛbhya: Means 'to the ancestors'.
    • ā: Means 'in' or 'to'.
    • sadane: Refers to 'abode' or 'home'.
    • johuvānā: Means 'calling upon' or 'invoking'.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • This mantra emphasizes the role of wisdom and communication in nurturing relationships, particularly between mothers and daughters. For instance, a mother guiding her daughter through life's challenges embodies the essence of "awakening" and "enlightening".
    • The invocation of ancestors signifies the importance of heritage and family values. In many cultures, rituals are performed to honor ancestors, reflecting the belief that their wisdom and guidance continue to influence the living.
    • The concept of a young woman endowed with intelligence can be seen in modern contexts where women are empowered to pursue education and careers, thus contributing to society while honoring their familial roots.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its celebration of wisdom, the nurturing role of mothers, and the connection to one's heritage. It serves as a reminder of the importance of communication and the passing down of knowledge through generations. In real life, this mantra can inspire individuals to seek wisdom from their elders, honor their family traditions, and empower the younger generation to embrace their intelligence and potential. It highlights the interconnectedness of family, wisdom, and the divine, encouraging a holistic approach to personal and communal growth.
