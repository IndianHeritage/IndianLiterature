यद इन्द्र चित्र मेहनास्ति त्वादातम अद्रिवः |राधस तन् नो विदद्वस उभयाहस्त्य आ भर ||
yad indra citra mehanāsti tvādātam adrivaḥ | rādhas tan no vidadvasa ubhayāhasty ā bhara ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yad: whatever
    • indra: Indra, the king of gods
    • citra: splendid or diverse
    • mehanā: in the great effort or endeavor
    • asti: exists
    • tvādātam: from you (Indra)
    • adrivaḥ: without obstacles
    • rādhas: prosperity or wealth
    • tan: that
    • no: for us
    • vidadvasa: may you bestow
    • ubhayāhasty: both (spiritual and material)
    • ā: to
    • bhara: bring or fill
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Indra, seeking his blessings for prosperity and success.
    • The phrase "yad indra citra mehanāsti" suggests that whatever splendid endeavors exist, they are attributed to Indra's grace.
    • The request for "rādhas" indicates a desire for both material wealth and spiritual fulfillment.
    • By invoking "ubhayāhasty," the mantra emphasizes the importance of achieving balance in life, encompassing both physical and spiritual aspects.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be recited by entrepreneurs seeking success in their ventures, acknowledging that their achievements are supported by divine grace.
    • Students can use this mantra before exams, recognizing that their hard work and efforts are complemented by blessings from a higher power.
    • During community gatherings or festivals, this mantra can be chanted to invoke prosperity and harmony among participants, fostering a sense of unity and shared purpose.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its holistic approach to life, encouraging individuals to seek both material success and spiritual growth.
    • It serves as a reminder that while personal efforts are essential, divine support plays a crucial role in achieving one's goals.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, from personal aspirations to communal endeavors, promoting a balanced and prosperous life.
