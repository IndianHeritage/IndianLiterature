ते हि श्रेष्टवरचस त उ नस् तिरो विश्वानि दुरिता नयन्ति | सुख्षत्रासो वरुणो मित्रो अग्निः ऋतधीतयो वकमराजसत्याः ||
te hi śreṣṭhavarcasas ta u nas tiro viśvāni duritā nayanti | sukṣatrāso varuṇo mitro agnir ṛtadhītayo vakmarājasatyāḥ ||
  • te hi śreṣṭhavarcasas: This phrase can be translated as "they are indeed the best of the radiant ones." Here, "śreṣṭha" means the best or superior, and "varcasas" refers to radiance or brilliance. In real life, this can be likened to individuals or entities that shine in their fields, such as leaders, innovators, or those who inspire others through their actions.
  • ta u nas tiro viśvāni duritā nayanti: This translates to "they lead us away from all difficulties." The word "duritā" means difficulties or obstacles, and "nayanti" means to lead or guide. This can be related to mentors or guiding figures in our lives who help us navigate challenges, such as teachers, parents, or friends who provide support during tough times.
  • sukṣatrāso varuṇo mitro agnir: This part can be interpreted as "the auspicious ones, Varuna, Mitra, and Agni." Varuna is the god of cosmic order, Mitra represents friendship and harmony, and Agni symbolizes fire and transformation. In a real-life context, this can refer to the importance of maintaining harmony in relationships (Mitra), upholding moral values (Varuna), and embracing change and transformation (Agni) in our personal and professional lives.
  • ṛtadhītayo vakmarājasatyāḥ: This translates to "those who are established in truth and righteousness." "Rta" refers to cosmic order or truth, and "satyāḥ" means truthfulness. This emphasizes the significance of living a life grounded in honesty and integrity. In everyday life, this can be seen in the actions of individuals who stand up for what is right, even in the face of adversity, such as whistleblowers or activists.

  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking guidance from those who embody brilliance and radiance, leading us away from difficulties, and upholding values of truth and righteousness. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied by surrounding ourselves with positive influences, seeking mentorship, and striving to live a life of integrity. The significance of this mantra lies in its call to action for individuals to aspire to be the best versions of themselves while supporting others in their journeys, ultimately contributing to a harmonious and just society.
