उपह्वरेषु यद अचिध्वं यइं वय इव मरुतः केन चित पथā |श्चोतन्ति कोशा उप वो रथेष्व ā घृतम् उक्षतā मधुवर्णम् आर्चते ||
upahvareṣu yad acidhvaṁ yayiṁ vaya iva marutaḥ kena cit pathā | ścotanti kośā upa vo ratheṣv ā ghṛtam ukṣatā madhuvarṇam arcate ||
  • Mantra: "upahvareṣu yad acidhvaṁ yayiṁ vaya iva marutaḥ kena cit pathā |
  • Translation: "In the offerings, what is it that you desire, like the Maruts (storm deities) who travel along the path?"
  • Explanation: This line speaks to the act of offering and the desires that accompany it. The Maruts, known for their strength and vitality, symbolize the dynamic forces of nature. Just as they traverse their path, we too seek fulfillment in our offerings, whether they be prayers, actions, or intentions.
  • Real-life Example: Consider a person who donates to a charity. Their desire may not just be to give but to make a meaningful impact, akin to the Maruts who bring rain and life. The act of giving is not just about the material but also about the intention behind it.
  • Mantra Continuation: "ścotanti kośā upa vo ratheṣv ā ghṛtam ukṣatā madhuvarṇam arcate ||"
  • Translation: "The treasures are illuminated, and they are offered to you, like ghee poured into the fire, which is sweet and golden."
  • Explanation: This part of the mantra emphasizes the illumination of treasures, which can be interpreted as knowledge, wealth, or spiritual insights. The act of offering ghee (clarified butter) into the fire symbolizes the transformation of these offerings into something greater, akin to how our efforts can lead to enlightenment or fulfillment.
  • Real-life Example: In a corporate setting, when a team works together on a project, the collective knowledge and effort can lead to innovative solutions. The 'ghee' here represents their hard work, which, when combined, creates a 'fire' of success and achievement.

  • Conclusion: This mantra encapsulates the essence of desire, offering, and transformation. It teaches us that our intentions behind actions are crucial, and that what we offer—be it in the form of time, resources, or love—can lead to greater outcomes. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios, from personal relationships to professional endeavors, reminding us to approach our actions with purpose and clarity.
