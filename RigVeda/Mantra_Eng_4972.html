उत पूषणं युवामहे 'भिषूम् इव सारथिः |मह्यां इन्द्रं स्वस्तये ||
ut pūṣaṇaṁ yuvāmahe 'bhīśūm̐r iva sārathiḥ | mahyā indraṁ svastaye ||
  • Mantra: "ut pūṣaṇaṁ yuvāmahe 'bhīśūm̐r iva sārathiḥ | mahyā indraṁ svastaye ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ut: O
    • pūṣaṇam: the nourisher or sustainer
    • yuvāmahe: we invoke or we call upon
    • 'bhīśūm̐r: like a powerful charioteer
    • iva: like
    • sārathiḥ: the charioteer
    • mahyā: for us
    • indraṁ: Indra, the king of gods
    • svastaye: for well-being or prosperity
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra is a prayer invoking the divine energy of Pūṣaṇa, who is considered a nourisher and sustainer of life.
    • The term "yuvāmahe" indicates a collective invocation, suggesting that the speaker is calling upon this divine energy together with others, emphasizing community and shared purpose.
    • By comparing Pūṣaṇa to a "sārathiḥ" (charioteer), the mantra highlights the role of guidance and direction in life, much like a charioteer guides a chariot to its destination.
    • Indra, mentioned in the mantra, is a symbol of strength and power, representing the ability to overcome obstacles and achieve success.
    • The phrase "svastaye" signifies a desire for well-being, prosperity, and auspiciousness, indicating that the invocation is not just for personal gain but for the collective good.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community setting, this mantra can be recited during gatherings or festivals to invoke blessings for collective prosperity and harmony.
    • In personal life, individuals may use this mantra during times of uncertainty or challenge, seeking guidance and nourishment from higher powers to navigate their paths.
    • In leadership contexts, invoking the qualities of a charioteer can serve as a reminder for leaders to guide their teams with wisdom and strength, ensuring that everyone moves towards a common goal.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of community, guidance, and the pursuit of well-being.
    • Its significance lies in the recognition of divine support in our endeavors, encouraging individuals and communities to seek nourishment and direction from higher powers.
    • In real-life scenarios, it serves as a reminder to invoke strength and guidance in both personal and collective journeys, fostering a sense of unity and purpose.
