आविष्ट्यो वर्धते चारुर आसु जिह्मानाम ऊर्ध्वः स्वयशा उपस्थे |उभे त्वष्टुर बिभ्यतुर जात्यमानात प्रतिची सिंहं प्रति जोषयते ||
āviṣṭyo vardhate cārur āsu jihmānām ūrdhvaḥ svayaśā upasthe | ubhe tvaṣṭur bibhyatur jāyamānāt pratīcī siṁham prati joṣayete ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • āviṣṭyo: Invoking or calling upon.
    • vardhate: Grows or increases.
    • cārur: Beautiful or attractive.
    • āsu: In the breath or life force.
    • jihmānām: Of those who are deceitful or cunning.
    • ūrdhvaḥ: Above or elevated.
    • svayaśā: Self-glorifying or renowned.
    • upasthe: In the presence or near.
    • ubhe: Both.
    • tvaṣṭur: Of the creator or divine craftsman.
    • bibhyatur: May fear or be afraid.
    • jāyamānāt: From the one who is being born or generated.
    • pratīcī: Towards the west or in the direction of.
    • siṁham: Lion.
    • prati: Against or towards.
    • joṣayete: Stimulates or encourages.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the divine presence, suggesting that beauty and strength grow in the life force.
    • It highlights the contrast between the deceitful and the elevated, emphasizing the importance of integrity and self-worth.
    • The mention of the creator indicates that both beauty and strength are divine gifts that should be recognized and nurtured.
    • The lion symbolizes courage and power, suggesting that one should face challenges boldly and with confidence.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In personal development, this mantra can inspire individuals to cultivate their inner beauty and strength, encouraging them to rise above deceit and negativity.
    • In leadership, it serves as a reminder for leaders to embody integrity and inspire their teams with courage and vision.
    • In creative endeavors, artists and creators can draw upon this mantra to find inspiration and confidence in their work, allowing their unique beauty to shine through.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of recognizing and nurturing one's inner beauty and strength while remaining vigilant against deceit.
    • It is applicable in various aspects of life, including personal growth, leadership, and creativity, encouraging individuals to face challenges with courage and integrity.
    • By embodying the qualities of the lion and the divine craftsman, one can inspire others and create a positive impact in their surroundings.
