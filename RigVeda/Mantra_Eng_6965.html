प्रभङ्गं दुरमतिनाम इन्द्र शविष्ठा भर |रयिम अस्मभ्यं युज्यं चोदयन्मते ज्येष्ठं चोदयन्मते ||
prabhaṅgaṁ durmatīnām indra śaviṣṭhā bhara | rayim asmabhyaṁ yujyaṁ codayanmate jyeṣṭhaṁ codayanmate ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • prabhaṅgaṁ: The one who brings light or illumination.
    • durmatīnām: Of those with bad or misguided thoughts.
    • indra: A powerful deity, often associated with strength and leadership.
    • śaviṣṭhā: The best or most excellent.
    • bhara: To bestow or provide.
    • rayim: Wealth or prosperity.
    • asmabhyaṁ: To us or for us.
    • yujyaṁ: To be connected or united.
    • codayanmate: Inspiring or motivating the mind.
    • jyeṣṭhaṁ: The elder or the most senior.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra calls upon Indra, the deity representing strength and leadership, to illuminate the minds of those who may have misguided thoughts.
    • It seeks the best blessings and prosperity for the community, emphasizing the importance of unity and collective well-being.
    • The invocation of Indra signifies a request for guidance and support from a higher power to overcome ignorance and achieve success.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, leaders can use this mantra to inspire their teams to work together harmoniously, fostering an environment where innovative ideas can flourish.
    • In educational institutions, teachers can invoke this mantra to encourage students to seek knowledge and wisdom, guiding them away from negative thoughts and towards enlightenment.
    • In community gatherings, this mantra can be recited to promote unity and collective prosperity, reminding individuals of the importance of working together for a common goal.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for illumination and guidance from a higher power, particularly in overcoming negative thoughts and fostering unity.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, such as leadership, education, and community building, where collective effort and positive thinking are essential for success.
    • By invoking this mantra, individuals and groups can seek the blessings of prosperity and wisdom, ultimately leading to a more harmonious and successful existence.
