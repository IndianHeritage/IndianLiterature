मन्त्र: शान्ति ह्य आर्य आशीष इन्द्र आयुर जनानाम् |अस्मान् नक्षस्व मगवन् उपावसे धुक्षस्व पिप्युषीम् इषम् ||
santi hy a1rya āśiṣa indra āyur janānām | asmān nakṣasva maghavann upāvase dhukṣasva pipyuṣīm iṣam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • santi: peace, tranquility
    • hy: indeed, certainly
    • a1rya: noble, honorable
    • āśiṣa: blessings, prosperity
    • indra: the king of gods, representing strength and power
    • āyur: life, longevity
    • janānām: of the people, of humanity
    • asmān: us, we
    • nakṣasva: may you bestow, grant
    • maghavann: O mighty one, referring to Indra
    • upāvase: may you dwell, reside
    • dhukṣasva: may you nourish, sustain
    • pipyuṣīm: with sweetness, richness
    • iṣam: strength, power
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes peace and blessings from Indra, the king of gods, emphasizing the importance of harmony and prosperity in life.
    • The phrase "santi hy a1rya āśiṣa" suggests that true nobility lies in the pursuit of peace and blessings for oneself and others.
    • By asking Indra to grant longevity and strength to the people, it highlights the collective well-being over individual desires.
    • The request for nourishment and richness ("dhukṣasva pipyuṣīm") signifies the need for sustenance that is not just physical but also spiritual and emotional.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community setting, this mantra can be recited during gatherings to promote unity and collective prosperity, reminding everyone of the importance of working together for the common good.
    • In personal life, individuals can reflect on this mantra during meditation, focusing on cultivating peace and strength within themselves and their relationships.
    • Organizations can adopt the essence of this mantra in their mission statements, emphasizing the importance of nurturing a harmonious work environment that supports the well-being of all employees.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of life and the importance of seeking peace and prosperity not just for oneself but for the entire community.
    • Its significance lies in promoting a holistic approach to life, where individual success is intertwined with the well-being of others.
    • In today's fast-paced world, the teachings of this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, from personal development to community building, fostering a sense of unity and shared purpose.
