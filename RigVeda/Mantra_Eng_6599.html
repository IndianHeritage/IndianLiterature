उदु ष्य शरणे दिवो ज्योतिर्मयंस्त सूर्यः |अग्निर्न शुक्रः समिधाना आहुतः ||
ud u ṣya śaraṇe divo jyotir ayaṁsta sūryaḥ | agnir na śukraḥ samidhāna āhutaḥ ||
  • ud uṣya: This phrase can be translated as "rising" or "emerging." It signifies the dawn or the beginning of a new day, symbolizing hope and renewal.
  • śaraṇe: This means "in the refuge" or "in the shelter." It indicates a place of safety and protection, often associated with divine guidance.
  • dīvo: This translates to "of the sky" or "heavenly." It refers to the celestial realm, emphasizing the connection between the earthly and the divine.
  • jyotir: Meaning "light" or "radiance," this word represents knowledge, enlightenment, and the dispelling of darkness.
  • ayaṁsta: This can be interpreted as "this" or "here," indicating the presence of the divine or the sacred in the immediate environment.
  • sūryaḥ: This refers to the "Sun," a symbol of life, energy, and vitality. The Sun is often seen as a source of illumination and sustenance.
  • agnir: Meaning "fire," it symbolizes transformation, purification, and the divine spark within all beings.
  • na: This translates to "not," indicating negation or absence.
  • śukraḥ: This word means "bright" or "pure," often associated with clarity and brilliance.
  • samidhāna: This refers to "offering" or "sacrificial fire," symbolizing devotion and the act of giving.
  • āhutaḥ: Meaning "invoked" or "called upon," it signifies the act of summoning divine presence through rituals or prayers.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking divine light and protection as one embarks on a new journey or phase in life. It emphasizes the importance of invoking the divine through rituals and offerings, symbolized by fire, to gain clarity and guidance. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied during significant transitions, such as starting a new job, moving to a new place, or embarking on a spiritual journey. By reciting this mantra, individuals can seek the blessings of the Sun and fire, inviting light and protection into their lives, and fostering a sense of hope and renewal.
