त्यां न्व अश्विनां हुवे सुदंशसा गृभे कृताः |ययोः अस्ति प्राणः सख्यं देवेष्वध्याप्यम् ||
tyā nv a1śvinā huve sudaṁsasā gṛbhe kṛtā | yayor asti pra ṇaḥ sakhyaṁ deveṣv adhy āpyam ||
  • Mantra: "tyā nv a1śvinā huve sudaṁsasā gṛbhe kṛtā | yayor asti pra ṇaḥ sakhyaṁ deveṣv adhy āpyam ||"
  • Translation: "I invoke you, O Ashvins, who are endowed with good fortune, to come to our home; may you grant us friendship and prosperity among the gods."

  • Explanation of Key Terms:
    • tyā: This word signifies an invocation or calling upon.
    • ashvinā: Refers to the Ashvins, the twin gods of medicine and health in Vedic literature, symbolizing duality and balance.
    • sudaṁsasā: Means "endowed with good fortune" or "blessed," indicating the auspicious nature of the Ashvins.
    • gṛbhe: Translates to "home," representing the domestic sphere and the importance of family and community.
    • pra ṇaḥ: Refers to "friendship" or "companionship," emphasizing the value of relationships.
    • deveṣv: Means "among the gods," indicating the divine context of the invocation.
    • adhy āpyam: Suggests "to be granted" or "to be bestowed," highlighting the act of receiving blessings.

  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a family setting, invoking the Ashvins can symbolize the desire for health and well-being among family members, akin to how families pray for the health of their loved ones during festivals or special occasions.
    • In a community gathering, this mantra can be recited to foster unity and friendship among participants, similar to how people come together during community service or local events to strengthen bonds.
    • In a healthcare context, the Ashvins represent the pursuit of medical knowledge and healing, reflecting modern practices where communities come together to support health initiatives, such as vaccination drives or health awareness campaigns.

  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its invocation of divine blessings for health, prosperity, and friendship. It emphasizes the importance of community and relationships in achieving a harmonious life. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applicable in family prayers, community gatherings, and health initiatives, reminding us of the interconnectedness of our lives and the divine support we seek in our endeavors. By invoking the Ashvins, we acknowledge the need for balance, health, and companionship in our daily lives, fostering a sense of unity and well-being.
