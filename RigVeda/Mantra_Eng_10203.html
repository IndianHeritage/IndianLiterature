इदं स्वर इदं इद आस वामम् अयम् प्रकाश उर्व अन्तरिक्षम् |हनावा वृतं निरहि सोम हविष त्वा संतं हविषा यजाम ||
idaṁ svar idam id āsa vāmam ayam prakāśa urv a1ntarikṣam | hanāva vṛtraṁ nirehi soma haviṣ ṭvā santaṁ haviṣā yajāma ||
  • idaṁ svar idam id āsa vāmam: This phrase can be translated to "This is the light, this is the essence of your being." It emphasizes the importance of light and consciousness in our lives. In real life, this can be related to the pursuit of knowledge and enlightenment, where individuals seek to understand their true nature and purpose.
  • ayam prakāśa urv a1ntarikṣam: This translates to "This is the illumination that pervades the universe." It signifies the omnipresence of light and knowledge. In practical terms, it can be seen in the way scientific discoveries illuminate our understanding of the universe, such as the discovery of gravity or the laws of motion, which have transformed our perception of reality.
  • hanāva vṛtraṁ nirehi soma: This part means "We drive away the obstacles, O Soma." Soma is often associated with the divine nectar or the essence of life. In everyday life, this can be interpreted as overcoming challenges and adversities, much like how individuals strive to overcome personal struggles, such as health issues or financial difficulties, to achieve their goals.
  • haviṣ ṭvā santaṁ haviṣā yajāma: This translates to "We offer our sacrifices to you, O Divine." It reflects the idea of devotion and the importance of offering one's efforts and intentions to a higher purpose. In real life, this can be seen in acts of service, charity, or dedication to a cause, where individuals contribute their time and resources for the betterment of society.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking knowledge, overcoming obstacles, and dedicating oneself to a higher purpose. Its significance lies in the reminder that light and consciousness are fundamental to our existence, and that through devotion and perseverance, we can navigate the challenges of life. In real-life scenarios, this mantra encourages individuals to pursue enlightenment, face their challenges head-on, and contribute positively to the world around them, fostering a sense of community and shared purpose.
