सप्त होत्राणि मनसा वृणाना इन्बन्तो विश्वं प्रति यन्न ऋतेना | नृपेऽशसो विदथेṣu प्र जाताऽभिमं यज्ञं विचरन्त पूर्वीः ||
sapta hotrāṇi manasā vṛṇānā invanto viśvam prati yann ṛtena | nṛpeśaso vidatheṣu pra jātā abhī3maṁ yajñaṁ vi caranta pūrvīḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • Sapta: Seven
    • Hotrāṇi: Priests or those who perform the sacrificial rites
    • Manasā: With the mind or through thought
    • Vṛṇānā: Choosing or selecting
    • Invanto: Invoking or calling upon
    • Viśvam: The universe or all
    • Prati: Towards or in relation to
    • Yann: That which
    • Ṛtena: With truth or in accordance with the cosmic order
    • Nṛpeśaso: Of the king or ruler
    • Vidatheṣu: In the assemblies or gatherings
    • Pra jātā: Born or arisen
    • Abhī3maṁ: The great or powerful
    • Yajñaṁ: Sacrifice or offering
    • Vi caranta: Moving or wandering
    • Pūrvīḥ: The ancient or previous ones
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the seven priests who, through their focused minds, invoke the universal energies.
    • It emphasizes the importance of truth and cosmic order in the rituals performed, particularly in the presence of a ruler or king.
    • The mantra suggests that the offerings made in these rituals are not just for the present but are connected to ancient traditions and wisdom.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern ceremonies, such as weddings or significant life events, the presence of a knowledgeable officiant (akin to the priests) is crucial for invoking blessings and ensuring the event aligns with cultural values.
    • Corporate gatherings often have rituals or practices that invoke the spirit of teamwork and collaboration, reflecting the idea of invoking universal energies for a common goal.
    • In leadership, a ruler or manager who leads with truth and integrity can inspire their team, much like the king in the mantra who is central to the rituals.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of collective effort, truth, and the invocation of higher energies in achieving goals.
    • It serves as a reminder that our actions, whether in personal or professional life, should be rooted in truth and guided by wisdom from the past.
    • In essence, it encourages us to engage in our endeavors with a mindful approach, respecting traditions while striving for progress.
