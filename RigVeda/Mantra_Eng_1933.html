अनेहो दात्रम अदितेर अनर्वं हुवे स्वर्वद अवधानं नमस्वत् |तद् रोदसी जनयतं जारित्रे dyāvā रक्षतं पृथिवी नो अभ्वात् ||
aneho dātram aditer anarvaṁ huve svarvad avadhaṁ namasvat | tad rodasī janayataṁ jaritre dyāvā rakṣatam pṛthivī no abhvāt ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • aneho: Without any hindrance or obstruction
    • dātram: Protector or guardian
    • aditer: Of Aditi, the mother of gods
    • anarvaṁ: Unbounded or limitless
    • huve: I invoke or call upon
    • svarvad: For all beings or universally
    • avadhaṁ: Protection or safeguarding
    • namasvat: Worthy of reverence or respect
  • Contextual Meaning:
    • This mantra is a prayer for protection and guidance from the divine, specifically invoking the limitless nature of Aditi, who represents the cosmic mother and the source of all creation.
    • The phrase "without any hindrance" emphasizes the desire for unobstructed blessings and support in life.
    • It reflects a universal appeal for protection, not just for oneself but for all beings, highlighting the interconnectedness of life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In times of crisis, such as natural disasters or personal challenges, individuals often seek divine protection and guidance, similar to the invocation in this mantra.
    • Communities may come together to chant this mantra during rituals or ceremonies, reinforcing their collective hope for safety and well-being.
    • Parents may recite this mantra for the protection of their children, invoking the nurturing aspect of Aditi to safeguard their future.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of seeking divine protection and guidance in our lives.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal challenges to communal gatherings, where the need for safety and support is paramount.
    • By invoking the limitless nature of Aditi, we remind ourselves of the universal connection we share and the protection that is available to all beings.
