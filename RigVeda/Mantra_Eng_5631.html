यो नो मरुतो अभि दुर्हृतनायुस तिरश्चित्तानि वसवो जिघांसति |द्रुहः पाशान प्रति स मुचिष्ट तपीष्ठेन हन्मना हन्तना तं ||
yo no maruto abhi durhṛṇāyus tiraś cittāni vasavo jighāṁsati | druhaḥ pāśān prati sa mucīṣṭa tapiṣṭhena hanmanā hantanā tam ||
  • Mantra: "yo no maruto abhi durhṛṇāyus tiraś cittāni vasavo jighāṁsati | druhaḥ pāśān prati sa mucīṣṭa tapiṣṭhena hanmanā hantanā tam ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yo: who
    • no: our
    • maruto: of the Maruts (storm deities)
    • abhi: towards
    • durhṛṇāyus: with a difficult life
    • tiraś: across
    • cittāni: thoughts
    • vasavo: of the Vasus (deities of abundance)
    • jighāṁsati: desires
    • druhaḥ: enmity
    • pāśān: bonds
    • prati: against
    • sa: he
    • mucīṣṭa: may be freed
    • tapiṣṭhena: with great effort
    • hanmanā: with strength
    • hantanā: may he be destroyed
    • tam: that
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra invokes the Maruts, the storm deities, who are known for their strength and power.
    • It speaks of overcoming difficulties in life, represented by "durhṛṇāyus," which can be related to real-life challenges such as health issues or financial struggles.
    • The phrase "tiraś cittāni vasavo jighāṁsati" suggests that the desires of abundance (Vasus) can sometimes lead to distractions or negative thoughts.
    • The mantra emphasizes the importance of overcoming enmity and bonds that hold us back, akin to personal conflicts or societal pressures that hinder our progress.
    • It calls for strength and effort ("tapiṣṭhena hanmanā") to break free from these constraints, which can be likened to the determination needed to achieve personal goals or to overcome adversities.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of invoking divine strength to overcome life's challenges and distractions.
    • It is applicable in real-life scenarios where individuals face obstacles, whether they are emotional, physical, or social.
    • By reciting this mantra, one can seek the strength to break free from negative influences and pursue a path of abundance and positivity.
    • Ultimately, it serves as a reminder that with determination and divine support, one can navigate through life's storms and emerge victorious.
