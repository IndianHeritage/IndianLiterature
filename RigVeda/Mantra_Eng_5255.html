स नो राधांस्याभरेशानः सहसो यहो |भागश्च दातु वार्यम् ||
sa no rādhāṁsy ā bhareśānaḥ sahaso yaho | bhagaś ca dātu vāryam ||
  • sa no rādhāṁsy: This phrase can be translated as "may He (the divine) bestow upon us prosperity." The term "rādhāṁsy" refers to wealth or abundance, indicating a desire for material and spiritual richness in life.
  • ā bhareśānaḥ: This means "may He who is the lord of strength and power." Here, "bhareśānaḥ" signifies a divine entity that possesses immense strength, suggesting that we seek blessings from a powerful source.
  • sahaso yaho: This translates to "with great speed and swiftness." It implies a request for the divine to grant us the ability to act quickly and effectively in our endeavors.
  • bhagaś ca dātu vāryam: This phrase means "and may He grant us the blessings." "Bhagaś" refers to divine fortune or blessings, while "dātu vāryam" indicates the act of giving or bestowing these blessings upon us.

  • Real-Life Example: In a business context, this mantra can be invoked by entrepreneurs seeking success and prosperity in their ventures. For instance, a startup founder may recite this mantra before a crucial pitch to investors, asking for divine support in achieving their goals swiftly and effectively.
  • Personal Application: Individuals facing challenges in their personal lives, such as health issues or financial difficulties, can use this mantra as a source of motivation and hope. By focusing on the idea of divine strength and blessings, they may find the courage to overcome obstacles.

  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine intervention in our pursuit of prosperity and strength. It emphasizes the importance of seeking support from a higher power in our endeavors, whether they are personal or professional. In real-life scenarios, this mantra serves as a reminder to remain hopeful and proactive, trusting that with divine blessings, we can achieve our goals with speed and effectiveness.
