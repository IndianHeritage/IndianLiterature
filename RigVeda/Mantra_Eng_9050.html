यदीद अहं युधये संनयायन अदेवयून तंवा3 शूशुजानान् |अमा ते तुम्रं वृषभं पचानि तीव्रं सुतं पञ्चदशं निःशिंचम् ||
yadīd ahaṁ yudhaye saṁnayāny adevayūn tanvā3 śūśujānān | amā te tumraṁ vṛṣabham pacāni tīvraṁ sutam pañcadaśaṁ ni ṣiñcam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yadīd: Whatever
    • ahaṁ: I
    • yudhaye: For battle
    • saṁnayāny: With determination
    • adevayūn: Divine energies
    • tanvā: With body
    • śūśujānān: Those who are strong
    • amā: Not
    • te: You
    • tumraṁ: Your strength
    • vṛṣabham: Bull (symbol of strength)
    • pacāni: I cook or prepare
    • tīvraṁ: Intense
    • sutam: Son or offspring
    • pañcadaśaṁ: Fifteen
    • ni ṣiñcam: I pour or bestow
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the determination and strength required in the face of challenges.
    • The phrase "yadīd ahaṁ yudhaye" emphasizes the readiness to engage in battle, which can be interpreted as facing life's challenges head-on.
    • "With determination" suggests that one should approach these challenges with a strong will and focus.
    • The mention of "divine energies" indicates that one can draw upon higher powers or inner strength to overcome obstacles.
    • The metaphor of the "bull" represents strength and resilience, qualities that are essential in both physical and mental battles.
    • The act of "cooking" or "preparing" signifies the need to nurture one's strengths and abilities to face challenges effectively.
    • The reference to "fifteen" could symbolize completeness or a full cycle, suggesting that one should be prepared for all aspects of a challenge.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, when facing a tough project, one must gather their resources and approach the task with determination, much like preparing a meal with the right ingredients.
    • In sports, athletes often draw upon their inner strength and determination to overcome their opponents, akin to the "battle" referenced in the mantra.
    • In personal life, individuals may face emotional challenges, and the mantra encourages them to harness their inner strength and resilience to navigate through tough times.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the importance of determination, strength, and preparation in overcoming life's challenges.
    • Its significance lies in the encouragement to tap into one's inner resources and divine energies, fostering resilience in the face of adversity.
    • In real-life scenarios, whether in personal struggles, professional challenges, or competitive environments, the principles outlined in this mantra can guide individuals toward success and fulfillment.
