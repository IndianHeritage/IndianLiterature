एव नः स्पृधा सम अज समत्स्व इन्द्र रारन्धि मिथतीर अदेविः |विद्याम वस्तोर अवस गृणन्तो भरद्वाज उता त इन्द्र नूनम् ||
evā naḥ spṛdhaḥ sam ajā samatsv indra rārandhi mithatīr adevīḥ | vidyāma vastor avasā gṛṇanto bharadvājā uta ta indra nūnam ||
  • evā naḥ spṛdhaḥ: This phrase can be translated as "May our desires be fulfilled." It expresses a longing for the realization of aspirations and goals.
  • sam ajā samatsv: This means "together, we are born, together we strive." It emphasizes the importance of unity and collective effort in achieving success.
  • indra rārandhi: Here, "Indra" refers to the king of gods, symbolizing strength and power. "Rārandhi" can be interpreted as "may you grant us." This invokes divine assistance in overcoming challenges.
  • mithatīr adevīḥ: This phrase translates to "with the goddesses, may we be united." It highlights the significance of collaboration and harmony with divine forces.
  • vidyāma vastor avasā gṛṇanto: This means "we seek knowledge and understanding." It underscores the value of wisdom and learning in our pursuits.
  • bharadvājā uta ta indra nūnam: "O Indra, we are the descendants of Bharadvaja, and we surely call upon you." This line connects the speaker to a lineage of wisdom and strength, invoking the support of the divine.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda encapsulates the essence of aspiration, unity, divine support, and the pursuit of knowledge. In real life, it serves as a reminder of the importance of working together towards common goals, seeking wisdom, and relying on higher powers for guidance. Whether in personal endeavors, community projects, or professional collaborations, the principles outlined in this mantra can inspire individuals to strive for success while fostering a sense of togetherness and respect for the divine. It encourages us to recognize that our achievements are often the result of collective effort and the support of greater forces at play.
