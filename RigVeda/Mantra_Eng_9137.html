प्रावेपा मा बृहतो मादयन्ति प्रवातेजा इरिणे वर्वृतानाः |सोमस्येव मोजवतस्य भक्षो विभीदको जागृविर मह्यम अच्छान ||
prāvepā mā bṛhato mādayanti pravātejā iriṇe varvṛtānāḥ | somasyeva maujavatasya bhakṣo vibhīdako jāgṛvir mahyam acchān ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • prāvepā: May they not disturb or agitate.
    • mā: Not.
    • bṛhato: Of the great or vast.
    • mādayanti: May they bring joy or intoxication.
    • pravātejā: With the force of the wind or energy.
    • iriṇe: In the path or direction.
    • varvṛtānāḥ: Those who are well-versed or knowledgeable.
    • somasyeva: Like the soma (a sacred drink).
    • maujavatasya: Of the one who is joyful or blissful.
    • bhakṣo: The one who consumes or partakes.
    • vibhīdako: The one who is a distributor or giver.
    • jāgṛvir: The one who is awake or vigilant.
    • mahyam: To me.
    • acchān: May they be favorable or auspicious.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra expresses a plea for protection from disturbances and negativity.
    • It emphasizes the importance of joy and knowledge, akin to the bliss derived from the sacred soma drink.
    • The invocation of vigilance suggests the need for awareness and mindfulness in our actions.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a stressful work environment, one might recite this mantra to seek peace and focus, ensuring that distractions do not hinder productivity.
    • During moments of personal turmoil, individuals can reflect on the joy and knowledge they possess, akin to the bliss of soma, to regain their composure.
    • In educational settings, students can use this mantra to cultivate a mindset of vigilance and awareness, enhancing their learning experience.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder to seek tranquility amidst chaos and to embrace joy and knowledge.
    • Its significance lies in its applicability to various aspects of life, from personal well-being to professional environments.
    • By invoking the essence of this mantra, individuals can foster a more harmonious and mindful existence.
