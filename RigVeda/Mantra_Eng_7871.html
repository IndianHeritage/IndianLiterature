आस्मिन पीषङ्गम् इन्दवो दधातां वेनम् आदिशे |यो अस्मभ्यम् अरावाः ||
āsmin piśaṅgam indavo dadhātā venam ādiśe | yo asmabhyam arāvā ||
  • Transliteration: āsmin piśaṅgam indavo dadhātā venam ādiśe | yo asmabhyam arāvā ||
  • Word-by-word Meaning:
    • āsmin: in this
    • piśaṅgam: yellow or golden
    • indavo: the divine beings or deities
    • dadhātā: who bestow or grant
    • venam: a boon or blessing
    • ādiśe: in the beginning or origin
    • yo: who
    • asmabhyam: to us
    • arāvā: a blessing or prosperity
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the divine beings who grant blessings and prosperity to those who seek it.
    • The term "piśaṅgam" can symbolize wealth and abundance, often associated with the color gold, which is revered in many cultures as a symbol of prosperity.
    • The invocation of "indavo" refers to the divine forces or deities that are believed to influence the material and spiritual realms.
    • The phrase "yo asmabhyam arāvā" emphasizes the personal connection and the request for blessings directed towards the individual or community.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be recited during the opening of a new venture, seeking divine blessings for success and prosperity.
    • During festivals, people often invoke blessings for wealth and happiness, reflecting the essence of this mantra in their prayers and rituals.
    • In personal life, individuals may recite this mantra during significant life events, such as weddings or housewarming ceremonies, to invite prosperity and divine favor.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of seeking divine blessings for prosperity and well-being in various aspects of life.
    • It serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness between the divine and human efforts, encouraging individuals to strive for success while remaining humble and grateful.
    • In contemporary life, the principles embodied in this mantra can be applied in personal aspirations, community endeavors, and spiritual practices, fostering a sense of hope and abundance.
