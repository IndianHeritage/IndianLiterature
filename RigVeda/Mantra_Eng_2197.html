सो अप्रतीनिं मनवे पुरूणिंद्रो दाशद दाशुषे हन्ति वृत्रम् |सद्यो यो नृभ्यो अतसाय्यो भूत्व पस्पृधानेभ्यः सूर्यस्य सातौ ||
so apratīni manave purūṇīndro dāśad dāśuṣe hanti vṛtram | sadyo yo nṛbhyo atasāyyo bhūt paspṛdhānebhyaḥ sūryasya sātau ||
  • Mantra: "so apratīni manave purūṇīndro dāśad dāśuṣe hanti vṛtram | sadyo yo nṛbhyo atasāyyo bhūt paspṛdhānebhyaḥ sūryasya sātau ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • so: he, that
    • apratīni: who is unyielding or unrestrained
    • manave: to man, or humanity
    • purūṇīndro: the great Indra, the king of gods
    • dāśad: who gives, or bestows
    • dāśuṣe: to the one who is in need
    • hanti: destroys or vanquishes
    • vṛtram: the demon or obstacle
    • sadyo: immediately
    • yo: who
    • nṛbhyo: to men or humanity
    • atasāyyo: who is powerful or mighty
    • bhūt: beings or creatures
    • paspṛdhānebhyaḥ: to those who are in distress
    • sūryasya: of the sun
    • sātau: in the presence or light
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra speaks of the divine qualities of Indra, who is often seen as a protector and a giver.
    • It emphasizes the idea that Indra, as a powerful deity, can vanquish obstacles (vṛtram) that hinder humanity.
    • The phrase "sadyo yo nṛbhyo" suggests that this divine intervention happens immediately for those in need.
    • In real life, this can be interpreted as the idea that when individuals face challenges or obstacles, they can seek help from a higher power or the universe, which can provide support and guidance.
    • For example, in times of personal crisis, many people turn to prayer or meditation, seeking strength and clarity to overcome their difficulties.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of divine assistance in overcoming life's challenges.
    • It teaches that there is a higher power that can help us navigate through obstacles, encouraging faith and resilience.
    • In practical terms, it can be applied in various situations, such as during personal struggles, community challenges, or even global issues, reminding us to seek support and remain hopeful.
