इमे त इन्द्र सोमास तीव्राः अस्मे सुतासः |शुक्रा आशिरं याचन्ते ||
ime ta indra somās tīvrā asme sutāsaḥ | śukrā āśiraṁ yācante ||
  • ime ta indra somās tīvrā asme sutāsaḥ
    • ime - This word translates to "these" or "this." It indicates a focus on the present moment or the offerings being made.
    • ta - Meaning "that" or "those," it connects the offerings to the divine.
    • indra - Refers to Indra, the king of the gods in Vedic tradition, symbolizing strength, power, and the ability to overcome obstacles.
    • somās - This refers to the soma, a sacred drink in Vedic rituals believed to confer immortality and divine inspiration.
    • tīvrā - Meaning "intense" or "powerful," it describes the potency of the offerings being made.
    • asme - Translates to "for us," indicating a personal appeal or request for blessings.
    • sutāsaḥ - Refers to "the offerings" or "the libations," emphasizing the act of giving and sharing with the divine.
  • śukrā āśiraṁ yācante
    • śukrā - Meaning "pure" or "bright," it signifies the quality of the blessings being sought.
    • āśiraṁ - This translates to "blessings" or "grace," indicating the desired outcome of the offerings.
    • yācante - Meaning "they request" or "they seek," it shows the active role of the devotees in seeking divine favor.

Conclusion:

This mantra from the Rigveda emphasizes the importance of making sincere offerings to the divine, particularly to Indra, who represents strength and protection. The invocation of intense and pure offerings signifies a deep desire for blessings and grace in one's life. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied to various situations where individuals seek strength and support, such as during challenging times, personal struggles, or when embarking on new ventures. The act of offering, whether through prayer, meditation, or charitable deeds, fosters a connection with the divine and invites positive energies into one's life. Ultimately, this mantra serves as a reminder of the power of intention and the importance of seeking blessings for personal growth and well-being.
