अधā चिन् नु यद् दिधिषामहे वाम् अभि प्रियं रेक्णः पत्यमानाः |दद्वाम् vā यत् पुष्यति रेक्णः सम् व आरन् नकि रस्य मग्हानि ||
adhā cin nu yad didhiṣāmahe vām abhi priyaṁ rekṇaḥ patyamānāḥ | dadvām̐ vā yat puṣyati rekṇaḥ sam v āran nakir asya maghāni ||
  • adhā cin nu yad didhiṣāmahe: This phrase can be translated as "Let us strive for that which we desire." It emphasizes the importance of intention and effort in achieving one's goals. In real life, this can be seen in the context of setting personal or professional objectives, where individuals must actively work towards their aspirations.
  • vām abhi priyaṁ rekṇaḥ patyamānāḥ: This part means "May we be united in our love and affection." It highlights the significance of relationships and community. For example, in a family or team setting, fostering love and support among members can lead to greater harmony and success.
  • dadvām̐ vā yat puṣyati rekṇaḥ: This translates to "We give what nourishes and sustains." It reflects the idea of sharing resources and support with others. In practical terms, this can be seen in charitable actions, where individuals or organizations provide help to those in need, thereby creating a more supportive society.
  • sam v āran nakir asya maghāni: This phrase means "Together, we shall attain great wealth." It signifies the collective effort towards prosperity and success. In a business context, collaboration among team members can lead to innovative solutions and increased productivity, ultimately resulting in shared success.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of intention, unity, nourishment, and collective effort in achieving goals and prosperity. Its significance lies in the reminder that personal and communal success is intertwined. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios such as teamwork in workplaces, nurturing relationships in families, and engaging in community service. By embodying these principles, individuals can contribute to a more harmonious and prosperous society.
