स वीरो दक्षसाधनो वि यस तस्तम्भ रोदसी |हरिः पवित्रे अव्यत वेधाऽ न योनिं आसदं ||
sa vīro dakṣasādhano vi yas tastambha rodasī | hariḥ pavitre avyata vedhā na yonim āsadam ||
  • sa vīro dakṣasādhano: This phrase can be translated as "that hero who is skillful and accomplished." In real life, this can refer to individuals who demonstrate exceptional skills in their fields, such as athletes, artists, or leaders who inspire others through their capabilities.
  • vi yas tastambha rodasī: This means "who has established the heavens." It signifies the power to create or influence the world around us. For example, innovators and thinkers like Albert Einstein or Mahatma Gandhi have shaped the course of history through their ideas and actions, establishing new paradigms in science and social justice.
  • hariḥ pavitre avyata vedhā: This translates to "the divine one, pure and untainted, who knows the sacred knowledge." This can be likened to spiritual leaders or philosophers who guide others towards enlightenment and understanding, such as the Dalai Lama or Socrates, who emphasize the importance of wisdom and purity in thought and action.
  • na yonim āsadam: This phrase means "who does not dwell in the womb." It suggests transcendence beyond the physical realm or material existence. In a modern context, this can relate to individuals who focus on spiritual growth and self-realization, such as practitioners of meditation or mindfulness, who seek to understand their true nature beyond physical limitations.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of heroism, skill, divine purity, and transcendence. It emphasizes the importance of being accomplished in one's endeavors while also striving for higher knowledge and spiritual growth. In real life, this mantra can inspire individuals to pursue excellence in their fields, contribute positively to society, and seek deeper understanding of themselves and the universe. It serves as a reminder that true success is not just about material achievements but also about personal and spiritual development.
