अस्माकं त्वा मतीनां आ स्तोमा इन्द्र यच्छतु |अर्वाग् आ वर्तय HARī ||
asmākaṁ tvā matīnām ā stoma indra yacchatu | arvāg ā vartayā harī ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • asmākaṁ: Our
    • tvā: You
    • matīnām: Of thoughts or minds
    • ā: To
    • stoma: Praise or hymn
    • indra: Indra, the king of gods
    • yacchatu: May grant or bestow
    • arvāg: All around or everywhere
    • ā: To
    • vartayā: Lead or guide
    • harī: The divine or auspicious
  • Overall Meaning: This mantra is a prayer to Indra, the king of gods, asking him to bestow his blessings upon our thoughts and minds. It seeks guidance and support in all directions, emphasizing the importance of divine assistance in our endeavors.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a challenging situation, such as preparing for an important exam, students often seek divine guidance to enhance their focus and clarity of thought. This mantra can be recited to invoke Indra's blessings for mental strength.
    • During a significant life decision, such as choosing a career path, individuals may reflect on this mantra to seek clarity and direction, trusting that divine wisdom will guide their choices.
    • In team settings, such as workplaces or sports, members can recite this mantra collectively to foster unity and shared purpose, asking for divine support in achieving their common goals.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine guidance and support in our thoughts and actions. It highlights the interconnectedness of our minds and the divine, reminding us that seeking higher wisdom can lead to better decision-making and clarity in our lives. In various real-life scenarios, whether in education, career choices, or teamwork, invoking this mantra can serve as a source of inspiration and strength, encouraging individuals to trust in a higher power while navigating their paths.
