चर्कृत्यम् मरुतः पृथ्सु दुष्टारं द्युमन्तं शुष्मम् मगवत्सु धत्तन | धनस्पृतम् उक्त्यम् विश्वचर्षणिं तोकम् पुष्येम तनयं शतम् हिमाः ||
carkṛtyam marutaḥ pṛtsu duṣṭaraṁ dyumantaṁ śuṣmam maghavatsu dhattana | dhanaspṛtam ukthyaṁ viśvacarṣaṇiṁ tokam puṣyema tanayaṁ śataṁ himāḥ ||
  • carkṛtyam marutaḥ: This phrase refers to the actions or deeds of the Maruts, the storm deities in Vedic literature. The Maruts are often associated with strength, power, and the forces of nature.
  • pṛtsu duṣṭaraṁ: This can be interpreted as "in the difficult or challenging situations." It highlights the idea that the Maruts can help overcome obstacles.
  • dyumantaṁ śuṣmam: "Shining and dry," this part emphasizes the contrast between brightness and dryness, possibly symbolizing clarity and purity in thought and action.
  • maghavatsu dhattana: This phrase can be translated as "may the powerful ones bestow." It suggests a plea for blessings from those who are strong and capable.
  • dhanaspṛtam ukthyaṁ: This refers to the "wealth that is desired." It indicates a longing for prosperity and abundance in life.
  • viśvacarṣaṇiṁ: This term can be understood as "the one who encompasses all." It signifies a universal aspect, perhaps referring to the interconnectedness of all beings.
  • tokam puṣyema: "May we nurture the offspring." This part emphasizes the importance of growth, both in terms of family and community.
  • tanayaṁ śataṁ himāḥ: This translates to "a hundred offspring." It symbolizes abundance and the continuation of lineage and prosperity.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a deep yearning for strength, prosperity, and the nurturing of future generations. It reflects the human desire to overcome challenges with the help of divine forces, represented by the Maruts. In real life, this can be applicable in various scenarios such as seeking guidance during difficult times, striving for personal and communal growth, and fostering a sense of abundance in one's life. The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine assistance in achieving a fulfilling and prosperous life, emphasizing the importance of community, strength, and continuity.
