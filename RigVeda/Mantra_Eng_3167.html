अस्मे रायो दिवे-दिवे सं चरन्तु पुरुष्प्र्हः |अस्मे वाजास इराताम् ||
asme rāyo dive-dive saṁ carantu puruspṛhaḥ | asme vājāsa īratām ||
  • Mantra: "asme rāyo dive-dive saṁ carantu puruspṛhaḥ | asme vājāsa īratām ||"
  • Translation: "May the riches (rāyo) come to us day by day (dive-dive), and may the desires of the people (puruspṛhaḥ) be fulfilled. May we have the strength (vājāsa) to achieve our goals."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • asme: "to us" - indicating a personal or collective appeal.
    • rāyo: "riches" - can refer to wealth, prosperity, or abundance.
    • dive-dive: "day by day" - signifies continuity and regularity in the arrival of prosperity.
    • saṁ carantu: "may they come" - a hopeful invocation for the arrival of wealth.
    • puruspṛhaḥ: "desires of the people" - refers to the aspirations and wishes of individuals or society.
    • vājāsa: "strength" - denotes power, capability, or resources needed to achieve goals.
    • īratām: "to achieve" - implies the action of attaining or accomplishing something.

  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be applied to the pursuit of success. For instance, a startup may invoke this mantra to attract investors and resources that will help them grow day by day.
    • In personal life, individuals may recite this mantra while setting goals for their career or personal development, seeking the strength and resources to fulfill their aspirations.
    • Communities may use this mantra during festivals or gatherings, expressing a collective wish for prosperity and fulfillment of shared desires.

  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of continuous effort and the collective aspirations of individuals. It serves as a reminder that prosperity and success are not just individual pursuits but are also tied to the desires of the community.
    • In real life, it encourages individuals and groups to work together towards common goals, fostering a sense of unity and shared purpose.
    • Ultimately, the mantra signifies that with strength, determination, and a focus on collective desires, one can achieve lasting success and fulfillment in various aspects of life.
