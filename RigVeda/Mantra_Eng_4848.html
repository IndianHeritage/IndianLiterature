आ क्रन्दय बलम् ओजो न आ धा निः ष्टनिहि दुरिता बाधमानः |अप प्रोथा दुण्डुभे दुष्चुनाऽ इता इन्द्रस्य मुष्टिर् असि वीळयस्व ||
ā krandaya balam ojo na ā dhā niḥ ṣṭanihi duritā bādhamānaḥ | apa protha dundubhe ducchunā ita indrasya muṣṭir asi vīḻayasva ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā krandaya: "cry out" or "invoke"
    • bala: "strength" or "power"
    • ojo: "vital energy" or "life force"
    • na ā dhā: "do not hold back"
    • niḥ ṣṭanihi: "from the obstacles" or "from the difficulties"
    • duritā: "evil" or "misfortune"
    • bādhamānaḥ: "afflicted" or "troubled"
    • apa protha: "remove" or "take away"
    • dundubhe: "the drum" (symbolizing victory or announcement)
    • ducchunā: "with great force" or "with intensity"
    • ita: "here" or "now"
    • indrasya: "of Indra" (the king of gods)
    • muṣṭir: "fist" or "power"
    • asi: "you are"
    • vīḻayasva: "be victorious" or "achieve success"
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a call to invoke strength and energy, urging the individual to not hold back in the face of challenges.
    • It emphasizes the importance of overcoming obstacles and misfortunes that may afflict us.
    • The reference to Indra, the god of rain and thunderstorms, symbolizes the power to overcome adversities and achieve victory.
    • The drum (dundubhe) signifies the announcement of success and the celebration of overcoming difficulties.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a competitive environment, such as a job interview, one might invoke this mantra to gather strength and confidence, reminding oneself to overcome self-doubt and present their best self.
    • During challenging times, such as illness or personal loss, individuals can reflect on this mantra to find the inner strength to persevere and seek healing.
    • In sports, athletes often call upon their inner strength and focus to overcome their opponents, akin to the invocation of Indra's power in this mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the strength and energy within us to face life's challenges.
    • It encourages individuals to actively seek to remove obstacles and misfortunes, fostering a mindset of resilience and determination.
    • In various real-life scenarios, whether in personal struggles, professional challenges, or competitive situations, this mantra can inspire individuals to harness their inner power and achieve success.
