प्रो आयासीद इन्दुर इन्द्रस्य निष्कृतं सखा सख्युर न प्र मिनाति संगिरम् |मर्य इव युवतिभिः सम अर्षति सोमः कलशे शतयाम्ना पथाः ||
pro ayāsīd indur indrasya niṣkṛtaṁ sakhā sakhyur na pra mināti saṁgiram | marya iva yuvatibhiḥ sam arṣati somaḥ kalaśe śatayāmnā pathā ||
  • Pro ayāsīd indur: This phrase can be translated as "May the moon (Indu) shine forth." The moon in Vedic literature often symbolizes nourishment, tranquility, and the mind's reflective qualities.
  • Indrasya niṣkṛtaṁ: This means "the release or liberation of Indra." Indra is the king of the gods and represents strength, power, and the forces of nature. The idea of liberation here can be interpreted as the release of energy or potential.
  • Sakhā sakhyur na pra mināti saṁgiram: This translates to "the friend of friends does not diminish in strength." It emphasizes the importance of camaraderie and support among individuals, suggesting that true friendship enhances one's strength rather than diminishes it.
  • Marya iva yuvatibhiḥ: This phrase means "like a young man among young women." It evokes the image of vitality, youth, and the exuberance of life, suggesting a sense of joy and celebration.
  • Sam arṣati somaḥ: This translates to "the soma (sacred drink) is stirred." Soma is often associated with immortality and divine inspiration, indicating a process of awakening or revitalization.
  • Kalaśe śatayāmnā pathā: This means "on the path of the hundred vessels." The 'hundred vessels' can symbolize abundance and the various paths one can take in life, suggesting that there are many opportunities for growth and fulfillment.

  • Conclusion: This mantra encapsulates themes of nourishment, friendship, vitality, and the pursuit of abundance. In real life, it can be applied to the importance of community and support systems in achieving personal and collective goals. Just as the moon provides light and guidance, individuals can shine in their own lives and help illuminate the paths of others. The mantra encourages us to embrace our friendships, celebrate our youth and vitality, and seek out the abundance that life has to offer. It serves as a reminder that through collaboration and support, we can achieve greater heights and navigate the complexities of life with strength and joy.
