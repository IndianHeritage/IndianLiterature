वियाजानाति जसुरिं विटृष्यन्तं विकामिनम् |देवतृ कृतुते मनः ||
vi yā jānāti jasuriṁ vi tṛṣyantaṁ vi kāminam | devatrā kṛṇute manaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • vi: a prefix indicating separation or distinction.
    • yā: who or that.
    • jānāti: knows or understands.
    • jasuriṁ: the one who is powerful or strong.
    • vi: again, indicating separation.
    • tṛṣyantaṁ: the one who is thirsty or desires.
    • vi: once more, indicating distinction.
    • kāminam: the one who desires or wishes.
    • devatrā: by the divine or gods.
    • kṛṇute: grants or bestows.
    • manaḥ: the mind or consciousness.
  • Overall Meaning: This mantra speaks about the divine ability to grant understanding and clarity to those who seek knowledge and have desires. It emphasizes the importance of recognizing one's own thirst for knowledge and desires, and how the divine can assist in fulfilling these through the right mindset.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • Seeking Knowledge: In a modern context, this mantra can be applied to students or professionals who are eager to learn and grow. For instance, a student preparing for exams may feel overwhelmed and thirsty for knowledge. By focusing their mind and seeking guidance, they can receive the clarity needed to succeed.
    • Desires and Aspirations: An individual pursuing a career change may feel a strong desire for a new path. This mantra suggests that by understanding their true aspirations and seeking divine guidance, they can find the right opportunities that align with their passions.
    • Mindfulness and Clarity: In daily life, people often face distractions and confusion. Practicing mindfulness, as suggested by this mantra, can help individuals clear their minds, recognize their true desires, and make informed decisions.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its reminder of the power of the mind and the divine's role in guiding us towards understanding and fulfillment. It encourages individuals to recognize their thirst for knowledge and desires, and to seek clarity through divine assistance. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in education, career choices, and personal growth, emphasizing the importance of a focused mind and the pursuit of wisdom.
