सुवृद् रथो वर्तते यन्न अभि क्षां यत् तिष्ठतः क्रतुमन्तानु पृक्षे |वपुर वपुष्याऽसचताम इयं गीर दिवो दुहित्रोषसाऽसचेत ||
suvṛd ratho vartate yann abhi kṣāṁ yat tiṣṭhathaḥ kratumantānu pṛkṣe | vapur vapuṣyā sacatām iyaṁ gīr divo duhitroṣasā sacethe ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • suvṛd: Well-formed or auspicious
    • ratho: Chariot
    • vartate: Moves or travels
    • yann: That which
    • abhi: Towards
    • kṣāṁ: The earth or the ground
    • yat: That which
    • tiṣṭhathaḥ: Stands or remains
    • kratumantā: Those who are wise or have understanding
    • anu: Following
    • pṛkṣe: The path or the way
    • vapur: Form or body
    • vapuṣyā: Of the body or form
    • sacatām: Of the good or auspicious
    • iyaṁ: This
    • gīr: Song or hymn
    • divo: Of the heavens
    • duhitroṣasā: Daughter of the dawn
    • sacethe: May it be auspicious
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of a well-formed chariot that moves towards the earth, symbolizing the journey of life.
    • The wise individuals who understand the path are encouraged to follow it, indicating the importance of wisdom and guidance in our lives.
    • The reference to the body and the auspiciousness of the hymn suggests that our physical existence is intertwined with spiritual and moral values.
    • The mention of the "daughter of the dawn" symbolizes new beginnings, hope, and the potential for growth and enlightenment.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, the mantra can be related to teamwork where a well-structured plan (the chariot) is essential for achieving goals (moving towards the earth).
    • In personal development, the wise individuals represent mentors or guides who help us navigate through life's challenges.
    • The idea of auspiciousness can be seen in rituals or practices that promote positivity and well-being, such as meditation or yoga.
    • The "daughter of the dawn" can be likened to the start of a new day, representing opportunities for growth and the importance of embracing change.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of wisdom, guidance, and auspiciousness in our journey through life.
    • It encourages us to seek knowledge and follow a righteous path, reminding us that our physical existence is connected to our spiritual and moral choices.
    • In real-life scenarios, applying the principles of this mantra can lead to personal growth, successful teamwork, and a positive outlook on new beginnings.
