एकस्य वस्तोर आवतं रणाय वशम अश्विनā सन्aye सहस्रā |निर्हतं दुच्छुनā इन्द्रवन्तā पृथुश्रवसो वृषणाव अरातिः ||
ekasyā vastor āvataṁ raṇāya vaśam aśvinā sanaye sahasrā | nir ahataṁ ducchunā indravantā pṛthuśravaso vṛṣaṇāv arātīḥ ||
  • ekasyā vastor āvataṁ: This phrase can be translated as "the essence of one thing." It emphasizes the importance of unity and the singular nature of existence. In real life, this can be seen in the concept of teamwork, where the collective effort of individuals leads to a greater outcome than individual efforts alone.
  • raṇāya vaśam aśvinā sanaye sahasrā: This part refers to "the power of the Ashvins (the twin deities) in battle." The Ashvins symbolize health, medicine, and the ability to overcome challenges. In a modern context, this can relate to the importance of resilience and support systems in overcoming life's battles, such as seeking help from friends or professionals during tough times.
  • nir ahataṁ ducchunā: This translates to "unharmed by the evil." It signifies protection from negativity and harm. In everyday life, this can be reflected in the practice of mindfulness and positive thinking, which helps individuals shield themselves from toxic influences and maintain mental well-being.
  • indravantā pṛthuśravaso vṛṣaṇāv arātīḥ: This phrase can be interpreted as "those who possess strength and glory, who are free from obstacles." It highlights the qualities of strength, honor, and the ability to navigate through challenges. In real life, this can be seen in leaders or individuals who inspire others through their courage and integrity, motivating them to overcome their own obstacles.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of unity, resilience, protection from negativity, and the pursuit of strength and honor. Its significance lies in its applicability to various aspects of life, such as teamwork, personal growth, mental well-being, and leadership. By embodying these principles, individuals can navigate life's challenges more effectively, fostering a sense of community and support while striving for personal and collective success.
