आद अस्यायुर ग्रभणवद वीलु शर्मा न सूनवे |भक्तम अभक्तम अवो व्यन्तो अजरा अग्नयो व्यन्तो अजरा: ||
ād asyāyur grabhaṇavad vīḻu śarma na sūnave | bhaktam abhaktam avo vyanto ajarā agnayo vyanto ajarāḥ ||
  • ād asyāyur: This phrase can be translated as "may this life be long." It emphasizes the importance of longevity and vitality in one's existence.
  • grabhaṇavad: This term refers to "like a womb" or "in the manner of a womb," symbolizing protection, nurturing, and the potential for growth.
  • vīḻu: This word signifies "wealth" or "prosperity," indicating the desire for abundance in life.
  • śarma: Translated as "comfort" or "shelter," it highlights the need for security and peace in one's life.
  • na sūnave: This phrase means "not for the unworthy," suggesting that the blessings of life, wealth, and comfort should be reserved for those who are deserving.
  • bhaktam abhaktam: This refers to "the devoted and the non-devoted," indicating a distinction between those who are committed to a righteous path and those who are not.
  • avo vyanto: This can be interpreted as "may they be free from obstacles," emphasizing the desire for a smooth journey in life.
  • ajarā: Meaning "immortal" or "everlasting," this term reflects the aspiration for eternal life or enduring legacy.
  • agnayo: This word relates to "the fires" or "the divine," often symbolizing purification and transformation.
  • vyanto ajarāḥ: This phrase reiterates the idea of being free from decay or death, reinforcing the theme of immortality.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a profound desire for a long, prosperous, and secure life, emphasizing the importance of deservingness and devotion. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied to various aspects such as personal growth, career aspirations, and relationships. For instance, individuals who work diligently and maintain integrity in their pursuits often find themselves rewarded with opportunities and success. The mantra serves as a reminder that a fulfilling life is not just about material wealth but also about nurturing relationships, personal development, and contributing positively to society. By embodying the principles of devotion and deservingness, one can navigate life's challenges with resilience and grace, ultimately leading to a legacy that transcends time.
