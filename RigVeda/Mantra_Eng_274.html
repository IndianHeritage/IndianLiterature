तद इति समानम् आशाते वेनन्ता न प्र युच्छतः |धृतव्रताय दाशुषे ||
tad it samānam āśāte venantā na pra yucchataḥ | dhṛtavratāya dāśuṣe ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • tad: that
    • it: it
    • samānam: equal, similar
    • āśāte: desires, wishes
    • venantā: those who are devoted
    • na: not
    • pra: forward, towards
    • yucchataḥ: to be separated, to be divided
    • dhṛtavratāya: to the one who is steadfast in vows
    • dāśuṣe: to the one who is devoted, to the servant
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the unity of desires and the importance of steadfastness in one's commitments.
    • The phrase "tad it samānam āśāte" suggests that the desires of those who are devoted are aligned and equal.
    • The line "na pra yucchataḥ" emphasizes that such devoted individuals should not be separated from their commitments or from each other.
    • Finally, "dhṛtavratāya dāśuṣe" highlights the significance of being steadfast in one's vows and serving with dedication.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a team setting, when all members share a common goal and are committed to achieving it, their collective efforts lead to success. For instance, a sports team that trains together and supports each other exemplifies the unity of desires.
    • In personal relationships, couples who maintain their vows and support each other through challenges demonstrate the essence of steadfastness and devotion.
    • In a workplace, employees who are dedicated to their company's mission and work collaboratively without division contribute to a positive and productive environment.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of unity in desires and the strength that comes from being steadfast in one's commitments.
    • It teaches us that when individuals are devoted and aligned in their goals, they can achieve greater outcomes without division.
    • In real life, this principle can be applied in various contexts, such as teamwork, relationships, and community service, where collective effort and dedication lead to success and harmony.
