उरुशंसां नमोवृद्धा मह्ना दक्षस्य राजथः | द्राघिष्ठाभिः शुचिव्रता ||
uruśaṁsā namovṛdhā mahnā dakṣasya rājathaḥ | drāghiṣṭhābhiḥ śucivratā ||
  • uruśaṁsā: This term can be broken down into two parts: "uru" meaning "great" or "vast," and "śaṁsā," which refers to "praise" or "glory." Thus, "uruśaṁsā" signifies the great glory or the vast praise that is due to the divine or the revered.
  • namovṛdhā: "Namo" translates to "salutation" or "obeisance," while "vṛdhā" means "to the one who is growing" or "to the one who is increasing." This phrase indicates a respectful acknowledgment of the divine presence that is ever-expanding and nurturing.
  • mahnā: This word means "by the great one" or "through the greatness." It emphasizes the importance of greatness in the context of the divine or the cosmic order.
  • dakṣasya: "Dakṣa" refers to "skillful" or "capable." In this context, it denotes the divine or cosmic force that is adept and proficient in its workings.
  • rājathaḥ: This term can be understood as "to the king" or "to the ruler." It signifies the authority and governance of the divine in the universe.
  • drāghiṣṭhābhiḥ: "Drāghiṣṭhā" means "with long-lasting" or "with enduring." This suggests a quality of permanence and stability, which is essential in the context of divine blessings.
  • śucivratā: "Śuci" means "pure" or "sacred," and "vratā" refers to "vows" or "commitments." Together, this phrase indicates a commitment to purity and sacredness, highlighting the importance of maintaining a righteous path.

Conclusion: The mantra "uruśaṁsā namovṛdhā mahnā dakṣasya rājathaḥ drāghiṣṭhābhiḥ śucivratā" encapsulates a profound reverence for the divine forces that govern the universe. It emphasizes the importance of acknowledging the vast glory of the divine, the nurturing aspect of growth, and the skillful governance that ensures stability and purity in life.
In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as during rituals, meditation, or moments of reflection. It serves as a reminder to honor the greatness around us, to strive for purity in our actions, and to recognize the enduring qualities of the divine that guide us through challenges. By embodying the principles of this mantra, individuals can cultivate a deeper connection with the universe and foster a sense of peace and purpose in their lives.
