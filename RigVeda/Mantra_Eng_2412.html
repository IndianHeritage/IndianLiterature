आयं वाम मित्रावरुणा सुतः सोम ऋतावृद्धा |ममेद इह श्रुतं हवम् ||
ayaṁ vām mitrāvaruṇā sutaḥ soma ṛtāvṛdhā | mamed iha śrutaṁ havam ||
  • ayaṁ: This word translates to "this" or "here." It signifies the presence of something immediate or relevant.
  • vām: Meaning "your," it indicates a connection or relationship with the divine or the audience being addressed.
  • mitrāvaruṇā: This compound word refers to the deities Mitra and Varuna. Mitra represents friendship, harmony, and the sun, while Varuna embodies the cosmic order, truth, and the waters. Together, they symbolize the balance of natural and social order.
  • sutaḥ: This means "son" or "offspring." In a broader sense, it can refer to the results or outcomes of one's actions or prayers.
  • soma: Soma is a sacred drink in Vedic tradition, often associated with immortality and divine inspiration. It represents the essence of life and spiritual awakening.
  • ṛtāvṛdhā: This term can be broken down into "ṛta," meaning "truth" or "cosmic order," and "vṛdhā," meaning "to increase" or "to grow." Thus, it signifies the growth or enhancement of truth and righteousness.
  • mamed: This translates to "my" or "mine," indicating a personal connection to the divine or the offerings being made.
  • iha: Meaning "here" or "in this place," it emphasizes the immediacy of the prayer or offering.
  • śrutaṁ: This word means "heard" or "listened to," suggesting that the offerings or prayers are acknowledged by the divine.
  • havam: This refers to "offering" or "sacrifice," which is a central theme in Vedic rituals, symbolizing devotion and the act of giving.

Conclusion: The mantra "ayaṁ vām mitrāvaruṇā sutaḥ soma ṛtāvṛdhā | mamed iha śrutaṁ havam ||" encapsulates a deep spiritual invocation that seeks the blessings of the deities Mitra and Varuna. It emphasizes the importance of truth, harmony, and the divine connection in our lives. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in moments of seeking guidance, establishing harmony in relationships, or during rituals that require a connection to the divine. It serves as a reminder of the balance between personal aspirations and the greater cosmic order, encouraging individuals to align their actions with truth and righteousness for a fulfilling life.
