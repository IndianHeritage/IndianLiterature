प्र सूत इन्द्र प्रवता हरिभ्यां प्र ते वज्रः प्रमृणन्न एतु शत्रून् | जहि प्रतीकः अनूचः पराचो विश्वं सत्यं कृतुहि विष्टम् अस्तु ||
pra sū ta indra pravatā haribhyām pra te vajraḥ pramṛṇann etu śatrūn | jahi pratīco anūcaḥ parāco viśvaṁ satyaṁ kṛṇuhi viṣṭam astu ||
  • pra sū ta indra pravatā haribhyām: This phrase can be interpreted as a call to Indra, the king of gods, to come forth and assist. The term "sūta" refers to the act of invoking or calling upon. In real life, this can be likened to seeking help from a higher power or authority in times of need, much like how individuals may pray or seek guidance during challenging situations.
  • pra te vajraḥ pramṛṇann etu śatrūn: Here, "vajraḥ" symbolizes strength and power, often associated with Indra's thunderbolt. The phrase suggests that this power should come forth to vanquish enemies ("śatrūn"). In a contemporary context, this can be seen as a metaphor for overcoming obstacles or adversaries in life, whether they be personal challenges, competition in the workplace, or societal issues.
  • jahi pratīco anūcaḥ parāco: This line emphasizes the need to defeat both visible and invisible foes. "Pratīco" refers to those who are directly in front of us, while "anūcaḥ" and "parāco" refer to those who may be hidden or indirect threats. In everyday life, this can relate to the idea of being aware of both overt and subtle challenges, such as dealing with direct conflicts as well as navigating office politics or social dynamics.
  • viśvaṁ satyaṁ kṛṇuhi viṣṭam astu: This concluding line calls for the establishment of universal truth and well-being. "Viśvaṁ" means all or universal, while "satyaṁ" refers to truth. The phrase can be interpreted as a plea for harmony and integrity in the world. In practical terms, this can be applied to efforts in promoting honesty, transparency, and ethical behavior in personal relationships, business practices, and governance.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a powerful invocation for strength, protection, and the establishment of truth. Its significance lies in the universal appeal for divine assistance in overcoming challenges and fostering a truthful and harmonious existence. In real life, this mantra can inspire individuals to seek help in difficult times, confront their adversaries, and strive for integrity in their actions, ultimately contributing to a more just and peaceful society.
