त्वं तं अग्नि अमृतत्व उत्तमे मर्तं दधासि श्रवसे दिवे-दिवे |यस् तातृषाण उभयाय जनमने मयः कृतोषि प्रय आ च सूरये ||
tvaṁ tam agne amṛtatva uttame martaṁ dadhāsi śravase dive-dive | yas tātṛṣāṇa ubhayāya janmane mayaḥ kṛṇoṣi praya ā ca sūraye ||
  • tvaṁ tam agne: This phrase translates to "You, O Agni." Agni is the Vedic fire god, symbolizing energy, transformation, and the divine spark within all beings.
  • amṛtatva uttame: This means "the highest immortality." It signifies the pursuit of eternal truth and the essence of life that transcends physical existence.
  • martaṁ dadhāsi: This translates to "You give life to the mortal." It reflects the idea that Agni, or divine energy, sustains life and provides vitality to all living beings.
  • śravase dive-dive: This means "to the one who is praised day by day." It emphasizes the continuous reverence and acknowledgment of the divine presence in our lives.
  • yas tātṛṣāṇa ubhayāya janmane: This translates to "You who fulfill the desires of both worlds." It indicates that Agni serves as a bridge between the material and spiritual realms, fulfilling the needs of both.
  • mayaḥ kṛṇoṣi: This means "You create for me." It expresses a personal connection and reliance on the divine for guidance and support in life.
  • praya ā ca sūraye: This translates to "Bestow upon me strength." It is a plea for empowerment and resilience in facing life's challenges.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda encapsulates the essence of seeking divine guidance and strength through Agni, the fire god. It highlights the importance of recognizing the divine in our daily lives and the pursuit of immortality through spiritual growth. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in moments of challenge or uncertainty, where individuals seek strength and clarity. By invoking the energy of Agni, one can find the motivation to overcome obstacles and strive for a higher purpose, fostering a deeper connection with both the material and spiritual aspects of existence.
