बृबदुक्थं हवामहे सृप्रकरसनम् ऊतये |साधु कृण्वन्तम् अवसे ||
bṛbadukthaṁ havāmahe sṛprakarasnam ūtaye | sādhu kṛṇvantam avase ||
  • Mantra: "bṛbadukthaṁ havāmahe sṛprakarasnam ūtaye | sādhu kṛṇvantam avase ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • bṛbaduktham: The great utterance or invocation.
    • havāmahe: We invoke or call upon.
    • sṛprakarasnam: The one who is well-prepared or well-equipped.
    • ūtaye: For the purpose of nourishment or sustenance.
    • sādhu: Good or virtuous.
    • kṛṇvantam: The one who creates or brings forth.
    • avase: To be present or to manifest.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a call to the divine or higher powers, invoking their presence and assistance.
    • The phrase "bṛbaduktham havāmahe" signifies a strong and earnest invocation, suggesting the importance of sincerity in our prayers.
    • "sṛprakarasnam ūtaye" emphasizes the need for being well-prepared and equipped in our endeavors, whether they are spiritual, personal, or professional.
    • The concluding part "sādhu kṛṇvantam avase" highlights the importance of virtuous actions and the manifestation of goodness in our lives.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, this mantra can be applied when a team comes together to launch a project. They invoke the spirit of collaboration and preparedness, ensuring that everyone is equipped with the necessary skills and resources.
    • In personal life, individuals may recite this mantra during times of decision-making, seeking guidance and clarity while emphasizing the importance of virtuous intentions.
    • In community service, this mantra can inspire groups to come together for a cause, invoking the presence of goodwill and the need for collective effort to bring about positive change.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the power of invocation and the importance of being well-prepared in all aspects of life.
    • It encourages individuals to act with virtue and to seek the presence of higher powers in their endeavors, fostering a sense of community and shared purpose.
    • Ultimately, the significance of this mantra lies in its ability to inspire individuals to strive for goodness and to be equipped for the challenges they face, leading to a more harmonious and fulfilling life.
