तवे इद अग्नि सुभगे यविष्ठ्य विश्वम् आ हूयते हविः |स तवं नो अद्य सुमना उतापरं यक्षि देवान सुवीर्याः ||
tve id agne subhage yaviṣṭhya viśvam ā hūyate haviḥ | sa tvaṁ no adya sumanā utāparaṁ yakṣi devān suvīryā ||
  • tve id agne: This phrase translates to "O Agni," referring to the Vedic fire god, Agni, who symbolizes energy, light, and the divine messenger between humans and gods.
  • subhage: This means "auspicious" or "blessed," indicating the positive qualities associated with Agni.
  • yaviṣṭhya: This term can be interpreted as "the one who is invoked" or "the one who is called upon," emphasizing the act of seeking Agni's presence and blessings.
  • viśvam ā hūyate haviḥ: This translates to "all offerings are called upon," suggesting that all forms of offerings and prayers are directed towards Agni, who is the recipient of these offerings.
  • sa tvaṁ no adya: This means "may you today," indicating a request for Agni's presence and blessings in the current moment.
  • sumanā: This word means "with a good mind" or "with a positive attitude," highlighting the importance of intention behind the offerings.
  • utāparaṁ: This can be interpreted as "and the other," suggesting a connection to other deities or aspects of life that are also being invoked.
  • yakṣi devān: This translates to "may you grant us the gods," indicating a plea for divine favor and blessings from the deities.
  • suvīryā: This means "with great strength," emphasizing the desire for empowerment and vitality in life.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda is a powerful invocation to Agni, the fire god, symbolizing the importance of divine energy and blessings in our lives. It emphasizes the significance of intention and positivity in our prayers and offerings. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied during rituals, ceremonies, or personal prayers where one seeks guidance, strength, and auspiciousness. By invoking Agni, individuals can foster a connection with the divine, seek blessings for themselves and their loved ones, and cultivate a mindset of positivity and strength in their daily lives.
