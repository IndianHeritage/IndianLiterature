मां सीम अवद्य आ भाग उर्वी काष्ठा हितं धनम् |अपावृक्ताऽरत्नयः ||
mā sīm avadya ā bhāg urvī kāṣṭhā hitaṁ dhanam | apāvṛktā aratnayaḥ ||
  • mā sīm avadya ā bhāg urvī kāṣṭhā hitaṁ dhanam
    • mā sīm avadya: This phrase can be interpreted as "do not let any obstacles or negativity come in the way." It emphasizes the importance of maintaining a positive outlook and avoiding distractions that can hinder progress.
    • ā bhāg urvī: This translates to "may we receive the blessings of the earth." It signifies a connection to nature and the abundance it provides, suggesting that we should be grateful for the resources available to us.
    • kāṣṭhā hitaṁ dhanam: This means "wealth that is beneficial and stable." It highlights the idea that true wealth is not just material but also encompasses stability and well-being.
  • apāvṛktā aratnayaḥ
    • apāvṛktā: This word can be understood as "unobstructed" or "free from barriers." It suggests a state of openness and receptivity to opportunities.
    • aratnayaḥ: This term refers to "those who are without jewels" or "those who lack material wealth." It implies that true richness comes from within and is not solely dependent on external possessions.

Conclusion:

The mantra emphasizes the importance of a positive mindset, gratitude for the earth's resources, and the pursuit of wealth that is both beneficial and stable. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios such as:

  • In personal development, maintaining a positive attitude can help individuals overcome challenges and achieve their goals.
  • In environmental awareness, recognizing the blessings of nature encourages sustainable practices and respect for the earth.
  • In financial planning, focusing on wealth that contributes to overall well-being rather than just material accumulation leads to a more fulfilling life.

Overall, this mantra serves as a reminder to seek balance, appreciate what we have, and strive for a wealth that enriches our lives and the lives of others.
