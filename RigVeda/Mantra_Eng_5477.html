शं नो अजा एकपाद देवो अस्तु शं नो 'हिर बुद्ध्यः शं समुद्रः |शं नो अपां नपत् पेुर अस्तु शं नः पृष्णिर्भवतु देवगोपाः ||
śaṁ no aja ekapād devo astu śaṁ no 'hir budhnya1ḥ śaṁ samudraḥ | śaṁ no apāṁ napāt perur astu śaṁ naḥ pṛśnir bhavatu devagopā ||
  • śaṁ no aja ekapād devo astu: This phrase translates to "May the one-footed deity bring us peace." Here, "aja" refers to a deity that is often associated with stability and singularity. The concept of a one-footed deity can symbolize balance and focus in life.
  • śaṁ no 'hir budhnyaḥ: This means "May the radiant one bring us peace." The term "hir" refers to brightness or radiance, often associated with knowledge and enlightenment. This suggests that wisdom and clarity can lead to a peaceful existence.
  • śaṁ samudraḥ: Translated as "May the ocean bring us peace." The ocean is a powerful symbol of vastness and depth, representing the emotional and spiritual aspects of life. It reminds us of the importance of being in tune with our inner selves and the world around us.
  • śaṁ no apāṁ napāt perur astu: This translates to "May the son of the waters bring us peace." Here, "apāṁ napāt" refers to the offspring of water, which can symbolize nourishment and sustenance. This line emphasizes the importance of water in our lives, both physically and spiritually.
  • śaṁ naḥ pṛśnir bhavatu devagopā: This means "May the protector of the gods bring us peace." "Devagopā" refers to a guardian or protector, suggesting that divine protection is essential for a peaceful life. It highlights the need for spiritual guidance and support in our daily lives.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of peace in various aspects of life, including stability, wisdom, emotional depth, nourishment, and divine protection. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in moments of stress or uncertainty, reminding individuals to seek balance, clarity, and support from both the natural world and spiritual entities. It encourages a holistic approach to well-being, integrating physical, emotional, and spiritual health. By invoking these qualities, one can cultivate a more peaceful and harmonious existence.
