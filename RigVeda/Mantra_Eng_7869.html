वृथा क्रीळन्त इन्दवः सदस्थम् अभ्य एकम् इति |सिन्धोर ऊर्मा व्य अक्षरन् ||
vṛthā krīḻanta indavaḥ sadhastham abhy ekam it | sindhor ūrmā vy akṣaran ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • vṛthā: in vain, futile
    • krīḻanta: playing, engaging
    • indavaḥ: the moon, celestial bodies
    • sadhastham: in the assembly, gathering
    • abhy: towards, in the direction of
    • ekam: one, singular
    • it: thus, in this manner
    • sindhor: of the ocean, sea
    • ūrmā: waves, ripples
    • vy: in, within
    • akṣaran: imperishable, eternal
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra reflects on the futility of actions that do not lead to a greater purpose or understanding.
    • The mention of "indavaḥ" (the moon) symbolizes illumination and guidance, suggesting that one should seek wisdom in their endeavors.
    • The term "sadhastham" indicates the importance of community and collective effort, emphasizing that individual actions should align with the greater good.
    • "Sindhor ūrmā" refers to the waves of the ocean, which can be seen as the challenges and fluctuations of life, while "akṣaran" signifies the eternal truths that remain constant amidst these changes.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace setting, employees may engage in tasks that seem productive but do not contribute to the overall goals of the organization. This mantra serves as a reminder to align individual efforts with the collective mission.
    • In personal relationships, one might invest time in activities that do not strengthen bonds. The mantra encourages individuals to focus on meaningful interactions that foster deeper connections.
    • In the context of environmental conservation, actions taken without a clear purpose can lead to wasted resources. This mantra highlights the need for intentional efforts that contribute to sustainability.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for purposeful action and the pursuit of wisdom.
    • It teaches that while individual efforts are important, they should be directed towards a common goal that benefits the community and aligns with eternal truths.
    • In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, encouraging individuals to reflect on their actions and ensure they contribute positively to their surroundings and relationships.
