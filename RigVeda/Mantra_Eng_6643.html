देवासो हि स्मा मनवे समन्यवो विश्वे साकं सरातयः |ते नो अद्य ते अपरं तुचे तु नो भवन्तु वरिवोविदः ||
devāso hi ṣmā manave samanyavo viśve sākaṁ sarātayaḥ | te no adya te aparaṁ tuce tu no bhavantu varivovidaḥ ||
  • Translation: "The gods are indeed united with Manu, all beings together, they are the ones who grant us abundance. May they, today and in the future, bestow upon us their bountiful blessings."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • devāso: Refers to the gods or divine beings.
    • hi: Indeed or truly, emphasizing the statement.
    • ṣmā: United or together.
    • manave: Referring to Manu, the progenitor of humanity in Hindu tradition.
    • samanyavo: All beings or creatures.
    • viśve: All or universal.
    • sākaṁ: Together or in unison.
    • sarātayaḥ: Those who grant abundance or prosperity.
    • te: They or those (referring to the gods).
    • no: Us or our.
    • adya: Today.
    • te: You (again referring to the gods).
    • aparaṁ: In the future or later.
    • tuce: Bestow or grant.
    • tu: Also or too.
    • no: Us or our (repeated for emphasis).
    • bhavantu: May they become or may they be.
    • varivovidaḥ: Those who are knowledgeable or wise in granting blessings.
  • Significance: This mantra emphasizes the unity between the divine and humanity, highlighting the importance of collaboration and harmony among all beings. It suggests that when humans and divine forces work together, they can achieve abundance and prosperity.
  • Real-Life Application:
    • In community service, when people come together to help others, they often experience a sense of fulfillment and collective achievement, reflecting the unity described in the mantra.
    • In business, collaboration among team members can lead to innovative solutions and greater success, mirroring the idea of divine abundance when working together.
    • In personal relationships, fostering harmony and understanding can lead to deeper connections and mutual support, embodying the essence of the mantra.
  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of all beings and the divine. It encourages individuals to seek unity and collaboration in their lives, whether in personal relationships, community efforts, or professional endeavors. By doing so, they can invite blessings and abundance into their lives, reflecting the wisdom of the divine.
