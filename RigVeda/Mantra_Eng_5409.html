गमद वाजं वाजयन्न इन्द्र मर्त्यो यस्य त्वं अविता भुवः |अस्माकं बोध्य अविता रथानाम अस्माकं शूर नृणाम ||
gamad vājaṁ vājayann indra martyo yasya tvam avitā bhuvaḥ | asmākam bodhy avitā rathānām asmākaṁ śūra nṛṇām ||
  • gamad vājaṁ vājayann: This phrase can be translated as "May we attain strength and vigor." The word "gamad" implies movement or progress, while "vājaṁ" refers to strength or power. In real life, this can be seen in the context of personal development, where individuals strive to gain strength in various aspects of life, such as physical fitness, mental resilience, or emotional stability.
  • indra martyo yasya tvam avitā bhuvaḥ: Here, "Indra" refers to the king of gods, symbolizing power and authority. "Martyo" means mortal, and "yasya tvam avitā bhuvaḥ" translates to "you who protect the mortals." This line emphasizes the need for divine protection and guidance in our lives. For example, in times of crisis or uncertainty, people often seek spiritual guidance or support from higher powers, reflecting the universal human desire for safety and assurance.
  • asmākam bodhy avitā rathānām: This translates to "May you, who are the protector of our chariots, enlighten us." The "rathānām" refers to chariots, which symbolize the journey of life. This line suggests that we seek wisdom and enlightenment to navigate our life's journey effectively. In practical terms, this can relate to seeking knowledge through education, mentorship, or life experiences that guide us in making informed decisions.
  • asmākaṁ śūra nṛṇām: This means "May we be courageous among men." The term "śūra" signifies bravery or valor, while "nṛṇām" refers to humans or men. This line encourages individuals to embody courage in their actions and decisions. In real life, this can be seen in various scenarios, such as standing up for one's beliefs, advocating for justice, or taking risks to achieve personal goals.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a profound message about the pursuit of strength, the need for divine protection, the quest for wisdom, and the importance of courage. Its significance lies in its applicability to everyday life, where individuals are constantly striving for personal growth, seeking guidance in challenging times, and embodying bravery in their endeavors. By reflecting on this mantra, one can find inspiration to navigate life's challenges with strength, wisdom, and courage, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling and purposeful existence.
