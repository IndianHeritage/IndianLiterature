अभि त्वा पाजो रक्षसो वि तस्थे महि जज्ञाणम् अभि तत् सु तिष्ठ || तव प्रात्नेन युज्येन सख्या वज्रेण धृष्णो अप ता नुदस्व ||
abhi tvā pājo rakṣaso vi tasthe mahi jajñānam abhi tat su tiṣṭha | tava pratnena yujyena sakhyā vajreṇa dhṛṣṇo apa tā nudasva ||
  • Mantra: "abhi tvā pājo rakṣaso vi tasthe mahi jajñānam abhi tat su tiṣṭha | tava pratnena yujyena sakhyā vajreṇa dhṛṣṇo apa tā nudasva ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • abhi: towards, for
    • tvā: you
    • pājo: protector, guardian
    • rakṣaso: from the demons or evil forces
    • vi: indeed, truly
    • tasthe: stand, remain
    • mahi: great, vast
    • jajñānam: knowledge, wisdom
    • abhi: towards, for
    • tat: that
    • su: good, auspicious
    • tiṣṭha: stand, remain
    • tava: your
    • pratnena: with devotion, prayer
    • yujyena: connected, united
    • sakhyā: friendship, alliance
    • vajreṇa: with strength, like a thunderbolt
    • dhṛṣṇo: courageous, bold
    • apa: away, from
    • tā: those
    • nudasva: drive away, remove
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra is a prayer for protection and strength. It invokes a guardian or protector to stand firm against evil forces.
    • The phrase "abhi tvā pājo rakṣaso" emphasizes the need for divine protection against negativity and harmful influences in life.
    • In real life, this can be likened to seeking support from friends or mentors when facing challenges, much like how one might rely on a strong network during difficult times.
    • The call for "mahi jajñānam" signifies the importance of wisdom and knowledge in overcoming obstacles, akin to how education and experience help individuals navigate life's complexities.
    • The mantra also highlights the significance of unity and friendship ("sakhyā") in achieving strength and resilience, similar to how teamwork in a workplace can lead to success.
    • Finally, the invocation to "drive away" negativity reflects the human desire to eliminate fear and doubt, which can be seen in practices like meditation or therapy that aim to foster mental well-being.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the power of protection, wisdom, and unity in overcoming life's challenges.
    • Its significance lies in the acknowledgment that we often need support from both divine and human sources to navigate difficulties.
    • In real-life scenarios, it encourages individuals to seek knowledge, build strong relationships, and maintain courage in the face of adversity.
    • Ultimately, it teaches that through collective strength and wisdom, one can effectively confront and dispel negativity from their life.
