मक्षू कनायाः सख्यं नवीयो राधो न रेत ऋतम इति तुरण्यन |शुचि यत ते रेक्ण आयजन्त सबर्दुघायाः पय उष्रियायाः ||
makṣū kanāyāḥ sakhyaṁ navīyo rādho na reta ṛtam it turaṇyan | śuci yat te rekṇa āyajanta sabardughāyāḥ paya usriyāyāḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • makṣū: a term often associated with the idea of being free or liberated.
    • kanāyāḥ: refers to the concept of connection or relationship.
    • sakhyaṁ: means friendship or companionship.
    • navīyo: signifies something new or fresh.
    • rādho: relates to the idea of nourishment or sustenance.
    • na: means 'not' or 'without.'
    • reta: refers to the essence or vital energy.
    • ṛtam: signifies truth or cosmic order.
    • it: means 'this' or 'here.'
    • turaṇyan: refers to swift or rapid movement.
    • śuci: means pure or clean.
    • yat: means 'that' or 'which.'
    • te: means 'your' or 'of you.'
    • rekṇa: refers to the act of offering or sacrifice.
    • āyajanta: means to invoke or call upon.
    • sabardughāyāḥ: signifies abundance or richness.
    • paya: refers to milk or nourishment.
    • usriyāyāḥ: relates to the idea of flowing or being abundant.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of friendship and connection in achieving a state of purity and truth.
    • The idea of 'newness' suggests that every relationship can bring fresh perspectives and nourishment to our lives.
    • It highlights the significance of invoking purity and abundance in our interactions with others.
    • In real life, this can be seen in how friendships can uplift us, providing emotional and spiritual nourishment.
    • For example, a supportive friend can help us navigate challenges, much like how pure milk nourishes the body.
  • Conclusion:
    • The mantra signifies the vital role of relationships in our lives, emphasizing that true nourishment comes from pure and meaningful connections.
    • It teaches us to value friendships and to seek purity in our intentions and actions.
    • In practical terms, this mantra can be applied in daily life by fostering genuine relationships, being supportive of friends, and striving for honesty and integrity in our interactions.
    • Ultimately, it reminds us that the essence of life is enriched through the bonds we create and the purity we maintain in our hearts.
