त्वं नः पश्चाद अधराद उत्तरत् पुर इन्द्र निपाहि विश्वतः |आरे अस्मत कृणुहि दैव्यम भयम् आरे हेतीर अदेविः ||
tvaṁ naḥ paścād adharād uttarāt pura indra ni pāhi viśvataḥ | āre asmat kṛṇuhi daivyam bhayam āre hetīr adevīḥ ||
  • tvaṁ naḥ - "You are ours": This phrase signifies a deep connection and reliance on a higher power, often interpreted as a plea to a divine entity for protection and guidance.
  • paścād adharād uttarāt - "from behind, from below, and from above": This part emphasizes the comprehensive nature of protection sought, covering all directions and aspects of life.
  • pura indra - "O Indra, the lord of the city": Indra is often regarded as the king of gods in Vedic literature, symbolizing strength, power, and protection. Here, he is invoked as a guardian of the community or individual.
  • ni pāhi viśvataḥ - "protect us from all sides": This is a direct request for safety and security, highlighting the vulnerability of human existence and the need for divine intervention.
  • āre asmat - "O divine one, from us": This phrase indicates a call to the divine to be aware of human struggles and to respond to them.
  • kṛṇuhi daivyam bhayam - "create divine fear": This can be interpreted as a request for the divine to instill a sense of reverence and caution, guiding individuals away from harm.
  • āre hetīr adevīḥ - "O divine, be our protector": This final plea reinforces the desire for divine guardianship, emphasizing the importance of faith and reliance on higher powers.

Conclusion: This mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking divine protection and guidance in all aspects of life. It reflects the human condition of vulnerability and the innate desire for safety and security. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applicable in various situations such as during times of uncertainty, fear, or danger. For instance, individuals may recite this mantra before embarking on a journey, facing a challenging situation, or during moments of personal crisis. Its significance lies in fostering a sense of connection with the divine, encouraging individuals to seek support beyond themselves, and instilling a sense of hope and courage in the face of adversity.
