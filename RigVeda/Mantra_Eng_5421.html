न त्वावाम् अन्यो दिव्यो न पार्थिवो न जातो न जनिष्यते |अश्वायन्तो मगवन्न इन्द्र वाजिनो गव्यन्तस त्वा हवामहे ||
na tvāvām̐ anyo divyo na pārthivo na jāto na janiṣyate | aśvāyanto maghavann indra vājino gavyantas tvā havāmahe ||
  • na tvāvām̐ anyo divyo: This phrase translates to "There is no other divine being like you." It emphasizes the uniqueness and supremacy of the deity being addressed, often Indra, the king of gods in Vedic literature.
  • na pārthivo: This means "nor is there any earthly being." It highlights that the subject of the mantra transcends both divine and earthly realms, suggesting an omnipresence and omnipotence.
  • na jāto na janiṣyate: This translates to "neither born nor will be born." It indicates the eternal nature of the divine, suggesting that the deity exists beyond the cycle of birth and death, which is a common theme in many spiritual philosophies.
  • aśvāyanto maghavann: This can be interpreted as "the powerful one who rides the horses." In Vedic culture, horses symbolize strength and speed, often associated with the sun and the divine chariot. This line signifies the might and valor of the deity.
  • indra vājino: This refers to "Indra, the one who bestows strength." Indra is often invoked for his power and ability to provide strength and victory in battles, both literal and metaphorical.
  • gavyantas tvā havāmahe: This means "we invoke you, who are the giver of wealth." The term "gavyantas" relates to abundance and prosperity, indicating that the deity is a source of material and spiritual wealth.

Conclusion: This mantra is a powerful invocation of Indra, emphasizing his unique divine status, eternal nature, and role as a provider of strength and prosperity. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as during rituals for success, strength in endeavors, or seeking blessings for abundance. It serves as a reminder of the divine support available to individuals in their pursuits, encouraging them to recognize and invoke higher powers in their lives for guidance and strength.
