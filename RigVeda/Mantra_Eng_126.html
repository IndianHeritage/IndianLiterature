अग्नि सुखतमे रथे देवां इळिता आ वह |असि होता मनुर्हितः ||
agne sukhatame rathe devām̐ īḻita ā vaha | asi hotā manurhitaḥ ||
  • Agne: This word refers to Agni, the fire god in Vedic literature, symbolizing energy, light, and purity.
  • Sukhatame: This translates to "the most auspicious" or "the best," indicating a state of well-being and positivity.
  • Rathe: This means "in the chariot," which can symbolize the journey of life or the vehicle of progress.
  • Devām̐: This refers to the divine beings or gods, representing higher powers and spiritual guidance.
  • Īḻita: This means "invoked" or "worshipped," indicating a call for divine presence and assistance.
  • Ā vaha: This translates to "bring forth" or "carry," suggesting a request for the divine to manifest in our lives.
  • Asi: This means "you are," affirming the presence and power of the divine.
  • Hotā: This refers to the priest or the one who offers sacrifices, symbolizing the act of devotion and connection to the divine.
  • Manurhitaḥ: This can be interpreted as "the one who is in accordance with the will of Manu," where Manu represents the archetypal human or the lawgiver, emphasizing righteousness and moral order.

The mantra can be understood as a prayer to Agni, asking for the divine to guide and support us on our life's journey, ensuring that we are aligned with higher principles and auspiciousness. It reflects a deep desire for spiritual connection and the invocation of divine energy to aid in our endeavors.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as:

  • Personal Growth: When facing challenges, one can recite this mantra to seek guidance and strength, invoking the energy of Agni to illuminate the path ahead.
  • Community and Relationships: In gatherings or ceremonies, this mantra can be used to foster a sense of unity and divine presence, encouraging positive interactions and collective well-being.
  • Decision Making: Before making significant life choices, reciting this mantra can help align one's intentions with higher values, ensuring that decisions are made with wisdom and righteousness.

In conclusion, this mantra emphasizes the importance of divine guidance and auspiciousness in our lives. By invoking Agni and the divine, we seek to align ourselves with higher principles, ensuring that our journey is filled with positivity, strength, and moral integrity. It serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of our actions with the divine and the significance of seeking spiritual support in our daily lives.
