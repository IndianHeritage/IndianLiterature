वयो न वृक्षं सुपलाशम् आसदन् सोमास इन्द्रं मन्दिनश चामूषदः |प्रैषाम अनीकं शवसाद दविद्युतद विदत स्वर्ण मनवे ज्योतिर आर्यम् ||
vayo na vṛkṣaṁ supalāśam āsadan somāsa indram mandinaś camūṣadaḥ | praiṣām anīkaṁ śavasā davidyutad vidat sva1r manave jyotir āryam ||
  • Vayo na vṛkṣaṁ supalāśam āsadan: This phrase can be translated as "May the winds not disturb the well-nourished tree." Here, the tree symbolizes stability and growth, while the winds represent challenges and adversities. In real life, this can be related to individuals or communities that strive to maintain their integrity and strength despite external pressures.
  • Somāsa indram mandinaś camūṣadaḥ: This part refers to "the Indra who is the lord of the celestial beings." Indra is often seen as a protector and a source of strength. In a practical sense, this can be likened to leaders or mentors in our lives who guide us through difficulties, providing support and wisdom.
  • Praiṣām anīkaṁ śavasā davidyutad: This translates to "May we be free from the fear of the unknown." It emphasizes the importance of courage and resilience in facing uncertainties. In everyday life, this can be applied to situations such as starting a new job, moving to a new city, or embarking on a new relationship, where fear of the unknown can be daunting.
  • Vidat sva1r manave jyotir āryam: This phrase means "May the light of knowledge shine upon us." It highlights the significance of wisdom and enlightenment in our lives. In practical terms, this can be seen in the pursuit of education, self-improvement, and the sharing of knowledge within communities.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of resilience, guidance, courage, and the pursuit of knowledge. Its significance lies in the reminder that, despite the challenges we face (represented by the winds), we can find strength in our roots (the tree), seek guidance from our mentors (Indra), confront our fears (the unknown), and strive for enlightenment (the light of knowledge). In real-life scenarios, this mantra encourages individuals to remain steadfast in their values, seek support from others, embrace change with courage, and continuously pursue knowledge for personal and communal growth.
