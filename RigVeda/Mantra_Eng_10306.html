अनाधृष्टानि धृष्टितो व्यास्यन् निधीमृदेवाम् अमृतन दयास्यः |मासेव सूर्यो वसु पुरीयम् आ दद गृणानः शत्रूम् अश्रिणाद् विरुक्मता ||
anādhṛṣṭāni dhṛṣito vy āsyan nidhīm̐r adevām̐ amṛṇad ayāsyaḥ | māseva sūryo vasu puryam ā dade gṛṇānaḥ śatrūm̐r aśṛṇād virukmatā ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • anādhṛṣṭāni: Those who are not discouraged or disheartened.
    • dhṛṣito: Those who are determined or resolute.
    • vy āsyan: They will attain or achieve.
    • nidhīm̐r: The treasures or wealth.
    • adevām̐: The divine or spiritual.
    • amṛṇad: Immortal or eternal.
    • ayāsyaḥ: Without fatigue or effort.
    • māseva: Like the month (or time).
    • sūryo: The sun.
    • vasu: Wealth or prosperity.
    • puryam: City or abode.
    • ā dade: They will give or bestow.
    • gṛṇānaḥ: Singing or praising.
    • śatrūm̐r: Enemies or obstacles.
    • aśṛṇād: Without hearing or being affected.
    • virukmatā: With brilliance or radiance.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of determination and resilience in the face of challenges.
    • It suggests that those who remain steadfast and undeterred will achieve great wealth and spiritual fulfillment effortlessly.
    • The comparison to the sun indicates that just as the sun shines consistently, so too should one’s efforts and resolve be unwavering.
    • Real-life examples include individuals who pursue their dreams despite setbacks, such as entrepreneurs who continue to innovate despite failures.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its encouragement to maintain a positive and determined mindset.
    • It teaches that perseverance leads to success and that one can achieve their goals without undue stress or fatigue.
    • This mantra can be applied in various real-life scenarios, such as in personal development, career pursuits, and overcoming obstacles in relationships.
    • By embodying the qualities of resilience and determination, individuals can navigate life's challenges and emerge victorious.
