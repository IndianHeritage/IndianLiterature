ते रुद्रासः सुमखाः अग्नयो यथा तुविद्युम्ना अवन्त्व एवयामरुत् |दीर्घं पृथु पप्रथे सद्म पार्थिवं येषाम् अज्मेष्वा महः शर्धांस्य अद्भुतैनेसाम् ||
te rudrāsaḥ sumakhā agnayo yathā tuvidyumnā avantv evayāmarut | dīrgham pṛthu paprathe sadma pārthivaṁ yeṣām ajmeṣv ā mahaḥ śardhāṁsy adbhutainasām ||
  • te rudrāsaḥ: This phrase refers to the "Rudras," who are often associated with storm and wind deities in Vedic literature. They symbolize the forces of nature that can be both destructive and protective.
  • sumakhā agnayo: "Sumakhā" means well-formed or auspicious, and "agnayo" refers to the "fires" or "energies." This can be interpreted as the positive and constructive aspects of energy in our lives.
  • yathā tuvidyumnā avantv evayāmarut: This part suggests that just as the Rudras and the auspicious fires work together, so too should we harness our energies and forces in a harmonious manner. "Avantv" implies a call for protection and guidance from these divine forces.
  • dīrgham pṛthu paprathe sadma pārthivaṁ: "Dīrgham" means long-lasting, "pṛthu" means vast, and "sadma" refers to a dwelling or home. This can be understood as a prayer for a long-lasting and expansive home or life filled with abundance.
  • yeṣām ajmeṣv ā mahaḥ śardhāṁsy adbhutainasām: This line speaks of those who possess great strength and wonder. "Ajmeṣv" refers to the powerful beings, and "mahaḥ" signifies greatness. It emphasizes the importance of strength and wonder in our lives.

  • Significance: This mantra encapsulates the essence of harnessing natural energies and divine forces for protection, abundance, and strength. It serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of nature and the divine, urging us to seek harmony in our lives.
  • Real-life Application: In practical terms, this mantra can be applied in various aspects of life, such as:
    • Environmental Awareness: Understanding the forces of nature and respecting them can lead to sustainable living practices.
    • Personal Growth: Harnessing one's inner energies and strengths can lead to personal development and resilience in the face of challenges.
    • Community Building: Working together harmoniously with others can create a supportive and abundant community.

  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the importance of aligning ourselves with the natural and divine forces around us. By recognizing and respecting these energies, we can cultivate a life that is not only abundant and strong but also harmonious and fulfilling.
