आ तु न इन्द्र मद्र्यग घुवानः सोमपीतये | हरिभ्यां याह्य अद्रिवः ||
ā tū na indra madryag ghuvānaḥ somapītaye | haribhyāṁ yāhy adrivaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā: O
    • tū: you
    • na: not
    • indra: Indra (the king of gods)
    • madryag: intoxicated or filled with joy
    • ghuvānaḥ: by the offerings or sacrifices
    • somapītaye: for the drinking of Soma (a sacred drink)
    • haribhyāṁ: by the horses or steeds
    • yāhy: may you come
    • adrivaḥ: to the non-stony or soft ground
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer directed towards Indra, the chief deity in Vedic tradition, who represents strength, rain, and the power of the heavens.
    • The speaker is invoking Indra to come and partake in the offerings made, particularly the Soma, which symbolizes divine inspiration and ecstasy.
    • The mention of "non-stony ground" suggests a desire for a welcoming and nurturing environment, free from obstacles, where divine blessings can be received.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a modern context, this mantra can be likened to inviting positive energies or influences into one’s life, akin to how people might seek guidance or blessings during significant life events such as weddings or new ventures.
    • For instance, during a wedding ceremony, couples often invoke blessings from deities or ancestors, similar to how this mantra seeks the presence of Indra for divine support.
    • In a corporate setting, leaders may invoke the spirit of teamwork and collaboration, akin to inviting Indra to partake in their efforts, ensuring a fruitful and harmonious work environment.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of divine connection and the role of offerings in establishing a relationship with higher powers.
    • Its significance lies in the acknowledgment of the need for support and guidance in various aspects of life, whether personal or communal.
    • In real-life scenarios, it serves as a reminder to seek positivity, collaboration, and divine blessings in our endeavors, fostering an environment conducive to growth and success.
