य इन्द्राग्नि चितृतमो रथो वाम अभि विश्वानि भुवनानि चष्टे |तेन यातं सरथं तस्थिवांसाथा सोमस्य पिबतं सुतस्य ||
ya indrāgnī citratamo ratho vām abhi viśvāni bhuvanāni caṣṭe | tenā yātaṁ sarathaṁ tasthivāṁsāthā somasya pibataṁ sutasya ||
  • Mantra: "ya indrāgnī citratamo ratho vām abhi viśvāni bhuvanāni caṣṭe | tenā yātaṁ sarathaṁ tasthivāṁsāthā somasya pibataṁ sutasya ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ya: who
    • indrāgnī: Indra and Agni (the deities representing strength and fire)
    • citratamo: the most colorful or splendid
    • ratho: chariot
    • vām: you (plural)
    • abhi: towards
    • viśvāni: all
    • bhuvanāni: worlds or realms
    • caṣṭe: moves or travels
    • tena: by that
    • yātaṁ: may it go
    • sarathaṁ: with the chariot
    • tasthivāṁsāthā: standing firm
    • somasya: of the soma (a sacred drink)
    • pibataṁ: drink
    • sutasya: of the son or offspring
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the combined powers of Indra and Agni, symbolizing strength and energy.
    • The "chariot" represents the journey of life, where one moves through various realms and experiences.
    • The phrase "may it go with the chariot" suggests a desire for guidance and support in navigating life's challenges.
    • The reference to "soma" indicates the importance of nourishment, both physical and spiritual, in sustaining life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, this mantra can be likened to a team working together (the chariot) under the leadership of strong figures (Indra and Agni) to achieve a common goal.
    • In personal life, it can represent an individual seeking guidance and strength from mentors or spiritual practices while navigating life's complexities.
    • During celebrations or rituals, the act of sharing food and drink (soma) symbolizes community and the importance of togetherness in achieving harmony.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of strength, guidance, and nourishment in our lives.
    • It teaches us to seek support from powerful forces, whether they be divine, communal, or personal, as we journey through various experiences.
    • In real-life scenarios, it encourages collaboration, the importance of community, and the need for spiritual and physical sustenance.
