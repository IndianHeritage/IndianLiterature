सुविवृतं सुनीरजम् इन्द्र त्वादातम् इद यशः |गवाम् अप व्रजं वृधि कृणुष्व राधो अद्रिवः ||
suvivṛtaṁ sunirajam indra tvādātam id yaśaḥ | gavām apa vrajaṁ vṛdhi kṛṇuṣva rādho adrivaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • suvivṛtaṁ: Well-distributed or well-spread.
    • sunirajam: Well-formed or well-structured.
    • indra: O Indra, the king of gods.
    • tvādātam: Bestowed by you.
    • id: This.
    • yaśaḥ: Fame or glory.
    • gavām: Of the cows (symbolizing wealth and nourishment).
    • apa: Away or towards.
    • vrajaṁ: The herd or group.
    • vṛdhi: Growth or increase.
    • kṛṇuṣva: Make or create.
    • rādho: Prosperity or abundance.
    • adrivaḥ: Without obstacles or hindrances.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Indra, the god of rain and thunder, asking for well-distributed wealth and fame.
    • The mention of "cows" symbolizes nourishment and prosperity, indicating a desire for abundance in life.
    • The phrase "make growth" emphasizes the importance of fostering development and prosperity in one's endeavors.
    • Overall, the mantra reflects a holistic approach to life, seeking not just material wealth but also fame and growth in a harmonious manner.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In agriculture, farmers may recite this mantra to invoke blessings for a bountiful harvest, ensuring that their crops grow well and are free from obstacles.
    • In business, entrepreneurs might use this mantra to seek success and recognition in their ventures, aiming for a well-structured and prosperous enterprise.
    • Individuals may chant this mantra during personal challenges, hoping for guidance and support from higher powers to overcome obstacles and achieve their goals.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine assistance in achieving a balanced and prosperous life.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal growth to professional success, emphasizing the interconnectedness of wealth, fame, and growth.
    • By invoking Indra's blessings, individuals can aspire to create a life that is abundant, fulfilling, and free from hindrances.
