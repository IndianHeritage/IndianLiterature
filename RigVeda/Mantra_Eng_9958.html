न वो गुहा चक्रिम भूरी दुष्कृतं नावीष्ट्यं वसवो देवहेळनम् |माकिर नो देवाः अनृतस्य वर्पस आ सर्वतातिम अदितिं वृणीमहे ||
na vo guhā cakṛma bhūri duṣkṛtaṁ nāviṣṭyaṁ vasavo devaheḻanam | mākir no devā anṛtasya varpasa ā sarvatātim aditiṁ vṛṇīmahe ||
  • na vo guhā cakṛma bhūri duṣkṛtaṁ: This phrase can be translated to "We do not create a hidden (guhā) burden (duṣkṛtaṁ) for you." It emphasizes the importance of transparency and honesty in our actions. In real life, this can relate to the idea of being open in relationships, whether personal or professional, to avoid misunderstandings and conflicts.
  • nāviṣṭyaṁ vasavo devaheḻanam: This part means "May the Vasus (deities of abundance) not be disturbed." The Vasus represent various aspects of nature and prosperity. This can be seen in our lives when we strive to maintain harmony with nature and seek abundance without causing harm. For example, sustainable living practices can be a way to honor this principle.
  • mākir no devā anṛtasya varpasa: Translated, this means "May the gods grant us the strength to overcome falsehood." This highlights the value of truthfulness and integrity. In everyday life, this can be applied in situations where one faces ethical dilemmas, encouraging individuals to choose honesty over deceit, such as in business dealings or personal relationships.
  • ā sarvatātim aditiṁ vṛṇīmahe: This phrase translates to "We choose the limitless (Aditi)." Aditi symbolizes infinity and the cosmos. In practical terms, this can be interpreted as striving for limitless potential and possibilities in our lives. It encourages individuals to pursue their dreams and aspirations without self-imposed limitations, such as pursuing higher education or starting a new venture.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of living a life of integrity, transparency, and harmony with nature. It serves as a reminder to seek truth, avoid deceit, and embrace the limitless possibilities that life offers. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various contexts, such as personal relationships, professional ethics, and environmental stewardship. By embodying these principles, individuals can contribute to a more harmonious and prosperous society.
