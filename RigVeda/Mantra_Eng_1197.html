ततम् मे अपस तदु तायते पुनः स्वादिष्टा धीतिरुचथाय शस्यते |अयं समुद्र इह विश्वदेव्यः स्वाहाकृतस्य समु तृप्णुत ऋभवः ||
tatam me apas tad u tāyate punaḥ svādiṣṭhā dhītir ucathāya śasyate | ayaṁ samudra iha viśvadevyaḥ svāhākṛtasya sam u tṛpṇuta ṛbhavaḥ ||
  • tatam me apas tad u tāyate: This phrase can be interpreted as a call for the waters (apas) to flow towards me. In a real-life context, this can symbolize the need for nourishment and support in our lives, much like how water is essential for survival. For example, when we seek help from friends or family during tough times, we are metaphorically asking for the waters of support to flow towards us.
  • punaḥ svādiṣṭhā dhītir ucathāya śasyate: This part speaks of the wisdom (dhītir) that is cultivated and nurtured (svādiṣṭhā) to bring forth prosperity (śasyate). In practical terms, this can relate to the importance of education and knowledge in achieving success. For instance, a student who diligently studies and applies their knowledge in real-world situations is likely to reap the benefits of their hard work.
  • ayaṁ samudra iha viśvadevyaḥ: Here, the mantra refers to the ocean (samudra) and the universal deities (viśvadevyaḥ). This can be seen as a reminder of the vastness of the universe and the interconnectedness of all beings. In everyday life, this can be reflected in our relationships with others and the environment, emphasizing the importance of harmony and respect for all forms of life.
  • svāhākṛtasya sam u tṛpṇuta ṛbhavaḥ: This concluding phrase suggests a fulfillment (tṛpṇuta) that comes from the offerings (svāhākṛtasya) made to the divine. In a real-life scenario, this can be interpreted as the rewards we receive from our actions and contributions to society. For example, volunteering for a cause can lead to a sense of fulfillment and community connection, illustrating the idea that giving back enriches our lives.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking support, nurturing wisdom, recognizing the vastness of existence, and understanding the importance of giving back. Its significance lies in the reminder that our actions, relationships, and knowledge shape our experiences and contribute to our overall well-being. In real life, this mantra encourages us to cultivate a sense of community, pursue knowledge, and remain connected to the world around us, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling and harmonious life.
