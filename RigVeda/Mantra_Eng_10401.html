अदो यद् दारु प्लवते सिन्धोः पारि अपूषम् |तद् आरभस्व दुर्हणो तेन गच्छ परस्तरम् ||
ado yad dāru plavate sindhoḥ pāre apūruṣam | tad ā rabhasva durhaṇo tena gaccha parastaram ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ado: here, in this place
    • yad: that which
    • dāru: wood, tree
    • plavate: floats, swims
    • sindhoḥ: of the ocean, sea
    • pāre: on the other side
    • apūruṣam: without a man, uninhabited
    • tad: that
    • ā rabhasva: may you strive, may you act
    • durhaṇo: difficult, hard to cross
    • tena: by that
    • gaccha: go, proceed
    • parastaram: beyond, further
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the journey across the ocean, symbolizing challenges and obstacles in life.
    • The "dāru" (wood) that floats represents resilience and the ability to navigate through difficulties.
    • The phrase "apūruṣam" suggests a state of being uninhabited or untouched, indicating the potential for new beginnings or unexplored territories.
    • "Ā rabhasva durhaṇo" encourages one to take action despite the challenges, emphasizing the importance of determination and effort.
    • The final part, "gaccha parastaram," signifies moving beyond current limitations and striving for greater achievements.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In personal development, individuals often face obstacles that seem insurmountable. This mantra can inspire them to take action and push through difficulties, much like a person learning to swim across a challenging body of water.
    • In business, entrepreneurs encounter numerous challenges when starting a new venture. The mantra serves as a reminder to remain resilient and to strive for success despite the odds.
    • In relationships, overcoming misunderstandings or conflicts can be difficult. This mantra encourages individuals to work through their issues and seek a deeper connection.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its encouragement to face challenges head-on and to strive for progress.
    • It teaches the value of resilience and determination, reminding us that even in the face of adversity, we can navigate through life's difficulties.
    • Applicable in various real-life scenarios, this mantra serves as a motivational tool for personal growth, professional endeavors, and interpersonal relationships.
