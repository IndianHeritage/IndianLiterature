अपघ्नन् पवसे मृधः क्रतुवित सोम मत्सरः |नुदस्वादेवयुं जनम् ||
apaghnan pavase mṛdhaḥ kratuvit soma matsaraḥ | nudasvādevayuṁ janam ||
  • Apaghnan: This term can be broken down into "apa" meaning "away" and "ghnan" meaning "to strike" or "to remove." Thus, it signifies the act of removing or dispelling negativity or obstacles.
  • Pavase: This word is derived from "pava," which means "to purify" or "to cleanse." It indicates a process of purification, both physically and spiritually.
  • Mṛdhaḥ: This term translates to "with strength" or "with power." It emphasizes the importance of strength in overcoming challenges and achieving goals.
  • Kratuvit: "Kratu" refers to "intellect" or "willpower," and "vit" means "one who knows." Therefore, "kratuvit" signifies someone who possesses wisdom and understanding, particularly in the context of making decisions.
  • Soma: In Vedic literature, "Soma" is often associated with a divine drink that grants immortality and enlightenment. It symbolizes the pursuit of higher consciousness and spiritual awakening.
  • Matsaraḥ: This term means "jealousy" or "envy." It highlights the negative emotions that can hinder personal growth and relationships.
  • Nudasvā: This word can be interpreted as "to remove" or "to dispel." It suggests the act of eliminating negative influences or thoughts.
  • Devayuṁ: This term refers to "divine beings" or "gods." It indicates a connection to the divine or higher powers that guide and support individuals.
  • Janam: This word means "people" or "humanity." It emphasizes the collective aspect of human experience and the importance of community.

Conclusion: The mantra "apaghnan pavase mṛdhaḥ kratuvit soma matsaraḥ | nudasvādevayuṁ janam ||" encapsulates the essence of overcoming negativity and obstacles through purification, strength, and wisdom. It encourages individuals to dispel jealousy and negative emotions while seeking divine guidance. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as personal development, conflict resolution, and community building. By focusing on purification and the pursuit of higher consciousness, individuals can foster a more harmonious and enlightened existence, both for themselves and for those around them.
