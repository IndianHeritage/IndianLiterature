त्वामिद्धित्वायवोऽनुनोनुवतश्चरान् |सखाय इन्द्र कारवः ||
tvām id dhi tvāyavo 'nunonuvataś carān | sakhāya indra kāravaḥ ||
  • tvām id dhi: This phrase translates to "Indeed, you are." It emphasizes the presence and importance of the addressed entity, often referring to a divine being or a significant force in life.
  • tvāyavo 'nunonuvataś carān: This can be interpreted as "We are devoted to you, following your path." It signifies a sense of loyalty and dedication to the divine or a guiding principle.
  • sakhāya indra kāravaḥ: This translates to "We, as friends, invoke Indra." Indra is a prominent deity in the Rigveda, often associated with strength, rain, and the warrior spirit. This line suggests a call for support and guidance from a powerful ally.

The mantra as a whole can be understood as a prayer or invocation, expressing devotion and seeking the blessings of a higher power, specifically Indra, who represents strength and protection. It reflects the human desire for guidance and support in life's endeavors.


In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts:

  • Personal Challenges: When facing difficulties, individuals can recite this mantra to seek strength and guidance, reminding themselves of their inner power and the support they can draw from their beliefs.
  • Community Support: In times of collective struggle, such as during natural disasters or social issues, communities can come together, invoking the spirit of unity and strength, much like the invocation of Indra in the mantra.
  • Leadership and Teamwork: In professional settings, leaders can embody the essence of this mantra by fostering a sense of loyalty and dedication among team members, encouraging them to work together towards a common goal.

Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for strength, unity, and devotion. It serves as a reminder of the importance of seeking guidance from higher powers and the strength found in community and friendship. In various aspects of life, whether personal, communal, or professional, this mantra encourages individuals to remain dedicated to their paths and to support one another in their journeys.
