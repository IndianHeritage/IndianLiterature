तं नो दाता मरुतो वाजिनं रथ आपानम् ब्रह्म चितयद दिवे-दिवे |इषं स्तोत्रिभ्यो वृजनेषु कारवे सनिम मेधाम् अरिष्टं दुष्टारं सहः ||
taṁ no dāta maruto vājinaṁ ratha āpānam brahma citayad dive-dive | iṣaṁ stotṛbhyo vṛjaneṣu kārave sanim medhām ariṣṭaṁ duṣṭaraṁ sahaḥ ||
  • taṁ no dāta maruto: This phrase can be translated as "May the Maruts, the storm deities, grant us." The Maruts are often associated with strength, power, and the forces of nature. In real life, this can be seen as a call for the support of powerful forces in our endeavors.
  • vājinaṁ ratha āpānam: This means "the one who brings forth the chariot of strength." Here, the chariot symbolizes progress and movement towards goals. In everyday life, this can relate to the tools and resources we utilize to achieve our ambitions, such as education, technology, or teamwork.
  • brahma citayad dive-dive: Translated, this means "may the supreme consciousness be awakened day by day." This emphasizes the importance of continuous growth and awareness. In practical terms, it suggests that we should strive for personal development and mindfulness in our daily lives.
  • iṣaṁ stotṛbhyo: This phrase translates to "the one who is worthy of praise." It highlights the importance of recognizing and honoring those who contribute positively to our lives. In real life, this can be applied by showing gratitude to mentors, friends, and family who support us.
  • vṛjaneṣu kārave: This means "may we be free from obstacles." It signifies the desire for a smooth path in our endeavors. In practical scenarios, this can relate to overcoming challenges in our careers, relationships, or personal goals.
  • sanim medhām ariṣṭaṁ duṣṭaraṁ sahaḥ: This translates to "may we have wisdom and strength to overcome difficulties." It emphasizes the need for resilience and intelligence in facing life's challenges. In real life, this can be seen in how individuals cope with adversity, using their knowledge and inner strength to navigate tough situations.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a profound message about seeking strength, wisdom, and support from the forces around us. It encourages continuous personal growth and the importance of gratitude and resilience. In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as pursuing education, overcoming personal challenges, and fostering positive relationships. By embodying the principles of this mantra, individuals can navigate their lives with purpose and strength, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling existence.
