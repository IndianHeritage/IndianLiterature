पृणीयाद इन नाधमानाय तव्यां द्राघीयांसम अनु पश्येत पन्थाम् |ओ हि वर्तन्ते रथ्येव चक्राण्यमन्यं उप तिष्ठन्त रायः ||
pṛṇīyād in nādhamānāya tavyān drāghīyāṁsam anu paśyeta panthām | o hi vartante rathyeva cakrānyam-anyam upa tiṣṭhanta rāyaḥ ||
  • pṛṇīyād: This word can be interpreted as "may he be filled" or "may he be satisfied." It suggests a desire for abundance and fulfillment in life.
  • in nādhamānāya: This phrase translates to "in the one who is not deprived." It emphasizes the importance of being in a state of abundance and not lacking in any aspect of life.
  • tavyān: This word means "those who are worthy" or "those who deserve." It indicates that fulfillment and abundance are reserved for those who have earned it through their actions and virtues.
  • drāghīyāṁsam: This term refers to "the long-lasting" or "the enduring." It highlights the significance of stability and permanence in the pursuit of fulfillment.
  • anu paśyeta: This means "may he see" or "may he perceive." It suggests the importance of awareness and insight in recognizing the path to fulfillment.
  • panthām: This translates to "the path." It signifies the journey one takes in life towards achieving their goals and desires.
  • o hi vartante: This phrase means "indeed they move" or "they indeed exist." It emphasizes the reality of the journey and the movement towards one's aspirations.
  • rathyeva: This word means "like a chariot." It symbolizes the vehicle of life that carries one towards their goals.
  • cakrānyam-anyam: This translates to "the wheels of another." It suggests that there are multiple paths and choices available in life.
  • upa tiṣṭhanta: This means "they stand up" or "they rise." It indicates the importance of resilience and determination in pursuing one's path.
  • rāyaḥ: This word refers to "the noble" or "the prosperous." It signifies the ultimate goal of achieving a noble and prosperous life.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking fulfillment and abundance in life while emphasizing the importance of awareness, resilience, and the pursuit of a noble path. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied to various aspects such as personal growth, career development, and relationships. For instance, in a professional setting, one may strive for success by being aware of opportunities, working hard, and remaining resilient in the face of challenges. Ultimately, the mantra serves as a reminder that the journey towards fulfillment is as important as the destination itself, and that one must remain committed to their path while being open to the various possibilities that life presents.
