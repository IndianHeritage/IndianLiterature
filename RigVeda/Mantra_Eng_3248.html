सम इन्द्रो गा अजायत सं हिरण्यā सम अश्वियā मगवā यो ह पूर्वीḥ |एभिर नृभिर नृतमो अस्य शाकै रयो विभक्तā संभरश च वस्वः ||
sam indro gā ajayat saṁ hiraṇyā sam aśviyā maghavā yo ha pūrvīḥ | ebhir nṛbhir nṛtamo asya śākai rāyo vibhaktā sambharaś ca vasvaḥ ||
  • Translation: The mantra can be translated as: "May Indra, the powerful one, conquer with his golden steeds, along with the Ashvins, the great ones who have come before. With these men, the most excellent of men, may we gather wealth and prosperity, and may all that is beneficial come to us."
  • Key Terms Explained:
    • Indra: In Vedic literature, Indra is the king of the gods and the god of rain and thunderstorms. He symbolizes strength and power.
    • Hiraṇyā: This term means "golden" and represents wealth, prosperity, and abundance.
    • Aśviyā: Refers to the Ashvins, twin gods associated with health, medicine, and the dawn. They symbolize duality and balance.
    • Nṛbhir: This means "men" or "people," indicating the collective strength and unity of individuals.
    • Rayo: Refers to wealth or resources, emphasizing the importance of material and spiritual abundance.
    • Sambharaś: This term means "to gather" or "to collect," indicating the act of bringing together resources or blessings.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be applied when a team comes together to achieve a common goal, pooling their resources and strengths to create a successful project.
    • In personal life, it can be seen in community efforts, such as organizing a charity event where individuals contribute their time and resources for a greater cause.
    • In health and wellness, the Ashvins' association with medicine can be reflected in modern practices where individuals seek holistic health through balanced lifestyles.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for unity, strength, and the gathering of resources for the betterment of individuals and communities. It emphasizes the importance of collaboration and the collective effort in achieving prosperity and well-being. In real-life scenarios, whether in business, community service, or personal health, the principles embodied in this mantra encourage individuals to work together harmoniously to create a prosperous and fulfilling life.
