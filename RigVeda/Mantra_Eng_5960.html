परः सो अस्तु तन्मा तना च तिस्रः पृथिवीर अधो अस्तु विश्वाः |प्रति शुष्यतु यशो अस्य देवā यो नो दिवा दिप्सति यश च नक्तम् ||
paraḥ so astu tanvā3 tanā ca tisraḥ pṛthivīr adho astu viśvāḥ | prati śuṣyatu yaśo asya devā yo no divā dipsati yaś ca naktam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • paraḥ: supreme, beyond
    • so: that
    • astu: may it be
    • tanvā: body, form
    • tanā: body, essence
    • ca: and
    • tisraḥ: three
    • pṛthivīr: of the earth
    • adho: below
    • astu: may it be
    • viśvāḥ: all, universal
    • prati: towards
    • śuṣyatu: may it dry up, may it be fulfilled
    • yaśo: fame, glory
    • asya: of this
    • devā: gods, divine beings
    • yo: who
    • no: our
    • divā: by day
    • dipsati: desires
    • yaś: who
    • ca: and
    • naktam: by night
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the supreme essence and seeks the blessings of the divine for the fulfillment of desires.
    • The mention of "three" can symbolize various triads in life, such as body, mind, and spirit, or the past, present, and future.
    • The phrase "may it dry up" suggests the removal of obstacles or negativity, allowing for the flourishing of positive attributes like fame and glory.
    • The reference to "gods" indicates a connection to higher powers that can aid in achieving one's aspirations.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, this mantra can be recited before a presentation or important meeting, invoking clarity and success.
    • Individuals facing challenges in personal relationships may use this mantra to seek harmony and understanding.
    • Students preparing for exams can chant this mantra to enhance focus and confidence, aiming for academic success.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking divine support in all aspects of life, whether it be personal, professional, or spiritual.
    • Its significance lies in the belief that by aligning oneself with higher powers, one can overcome challenges and achieve their goals.
    • In real-life scenarios, it serves as a reminder to remain connected to one's aspirations and to seek help from the universe or divine forces in times of need.
