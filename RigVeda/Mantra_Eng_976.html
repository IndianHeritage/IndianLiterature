मन्त्र: भद्रं कर्णैभिः श्रिणुयाम देवाः भद्रम पश्येमाक्षभिर यजत्राः |स्थिरैर अंगैः तुष्टुवांसस तनुभिः व्यशेम देवहितं यद आयुः ||
bhadraṁ karṇebhiḥ śṛṇuyāma devā bhadram paśyemākṣabhir yajatrāḥ | sthirair aṅgais tuṣṭuvāṁsas tanūbhir vy aśema devahitaṁ yad āyuḥ ||
  • Translation: "May we hear with our ears what is auspicious, O divine beings; may we see with our eyes what is auspicious, O worshippers. With firm bodies, may we praise you, O divine beings, and may we attain the good that is life."
  • Word-by-Word Explanation:
    • bhadraṁ: auspicious, good, beneficial
    • karṇebhiḥ: with our ears
    • śṛṇuyāma: may we hear
    • devā: O divine beings
    • bhadram: auspicious
    • paśyemākṣabhir: may we see with our eyes
    • yajatrāḥ: O worshippers
    • sthirair: with firm
    • aṅgais: bodies
    • tuṣṭuvāṁsas: may we praise
    • tanūbhir: with our bodies
    • vy aśema: may we attain
    • devahitaṁ: the good that is divine
    • yad: that which
    • āyuḥ: life, longevity
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community gathering, when people come together to listen to a speaker or a leader, they embody the essence of "hearing with our ears what is auspicious." This reflects the importance of being open to positive messages and guidance.
    • In educational settings, students are encouraged to "see with our eyes what is auspicious" by focusing on learning and understanding, which leads to personal and academic growth.
    • During rituals or ceremonies, participants often chant or recite mantras, embodying the idea of praising the divine with firm bodies and focused minds, which enhances their spiritual experience.
  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of being receptive to positive influences and experiences in life. It encourages individuals to engage actively with their surroundings, fostering a sense of community and spirituality. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts such as education, community gatherings, and spiritual practices, reminding us to seek and appreciate the auspicious aspects of life. By doing so, we cultivate a harmonious existence that aligns with our higher purpose and well-being.
