अस्य त्वेषा अजरा अस्य भानवः सुसंदृशः सुप्रतीकस्य सुद्युतः |भात्वकषसो अत्यक्तुर न सिंधवो 'ग्ने रेजन्ते asasanto अजरा: ||
asya tveṣā ajarā asya bhānavaḥ susaṁdṛśaḥ supratīkasya sudyutaḥ | bhātvakṣaso aty aktur na sindhavo 'gne rejante asasanto ajarāḥ ||
  • Mantra: "asya tveṣā ajarā asya bhānavaḥ susaṁdṛśaḥ supratīkasya sudyutaḥ | bhātvakṣaso aty aktur na sindhavo 'gne rejante asasanto ajarāḥ ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • asya: of this
    • tveṣā: essence or nature
    • ajarā: immortal or eternal
    • bhānavaḥ: radiant or shining
    • susaṁdṛśaḥ: well-seen or clearly visible
    • supratīkasya: of the well-formed or well-structured
    • sudyutaḥ: of good brilliance or light
    • bhātvakṣaso: of the one who is in the form of light
    • aty: beyond
    • aktur: the power or energy
    • na: not
    • sindhavo: of the rivers
    • 'gne: O fire
    • rejante: shine or radiate
    • asasanto: those who are not mortal
    • ajarāḥ: immortal or eternal beings
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra speaks of the eternal essence of existence, highlighting the nature of immortality and the brilliance of life.
    • The phrase "asya tveṣā ajarā" emphasizes the idea that the essence of this existence is eternal, suggesting that life transcends physical mortality.
    • In real life, this can be related to the concept of legacy. For example, the teachings of great leaders or thinkers like Mahatma Gandhi or Martin Luther King Jr. continue to inspire generations, showcasing how their essence remains immortal through their ideas and actions.
    • The mention of "bhānavaḥ susaṁdṛśaḥ" indicates that this eternal essence is radiant and clearly visible, akin to how love and compassion can illuminate the lives of those around us.
    • In practical terms, this can be seen in acts of kindness and community service, where individuals shine brightly in their contributions to society, creating a lasting impact.
    • The reference to "bhātvakṣaso aty aktur" suggests that this essence possesses a power that is beyond ordinary understanding, much like the profound influence of art, music, or literature that can evoke deep emotions and thoughts.
    • Furthermore, the line "na sindhavo 'gne rejante" implies that even the rivers, which are often seen as symbols of life and continuity, cannot shine as brightly as this eternal essence, reinforcing the idea that true brilliance comes from within.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the eternal nature of our essence and the impact we can have on the world around us.
    • It encourages individuals to seek and recognize the immortal aspects of life, such as love, wisdom, and compassion, which transcend physical existence.
    • In a real-life scenario, this can inspire people to focus on their contributions to society, fostering a sense of purpose and connection that lasts beyond their lifetime.
    • Ultimately, the significance of this mantra lies in its ability to motivate individuals to live meaningfully, embracing the eternal qualities that define our humanity.
