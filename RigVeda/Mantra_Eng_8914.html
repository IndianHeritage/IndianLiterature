उरूणसाव असुतृपाऽउदुम्बलौ यमस्य दूतौ चरतो जनाम् अनु |ताव अस्मभ्यं दृष्टये सूर्याय पुनर दाताम् असुम अद्येह भद्रम् ||
urūṇasāv asutṛpā udumbalau yamasya dūtau carato janām̐ anu | tāv asmabhyaṁ dṛśaye sūryāya punar dātām asum adyeha bhadram ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • urūṇasāv: The one who is vast or expansive.
    • asutṛpā: The one who is a protector or guardian.
    • udumbalau: Referring to the two divine beings or messengers.
    • yamasya: Of Yama, the god of death and the underworld.
    • dūtau: Messengers or envoys.
    • carato: Moving or wandering.
    • janām̐: Among the people or humanity.
    • anu: Following or in accordance with.
    • tāv: Those two.
    • asmabhyaṁ: To us.
    • dṛśaye: For the sight or vision.
    • sūryāya: To the Sun.
    • punar: Again or anew.
    • dātām: To give or bestow.
    • asum: Life or vitality.
    • adyeha: Here and now.
    • bhadram: Auspiciousness or well-being.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the expansive and protective qualities of divine beings, particularly in the context of Yama, the god associated with death.
    • The mention of "messengers" suggests a connection between the divine and human realms, emphasizing the importance of guidance and protection in our lives.
    • By calling upon these divine entities, the mantra seeks to enhance our perception and awareness, allowing us to see the auspiciousness in our lives.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In times of uncertainty or fear, individuals often seek guidance from spiritual practices or rituals, similar to how this mantra seeks divine protection and insight.
    • People may perform rituals to honor ancestors or deities, asking for blessings and clarity in their lives, reflecting the essence of this mantra.
    • In a broader sense, the mantra can be related to the importance of community and support systems, where individuals act as messengers of hope and guidance for one another.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine protection and insight, reminding us of the interconnectedness of life and the importance of seeking guidance.
    • It is applicable in real-life scenarios where individuals face challenges, encouraging them to look for support from both the divine and their community.
    • Ultimately, the mantra serves as a reminder to remain open to the auspiciousness that life offers, fostering a sense of well-being and clarity in our journey.
