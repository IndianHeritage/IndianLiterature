इन्द्र ऋभुमान वाजवान मत्स्वेह नोऽस्मिन सवने शच्यā पुरुष्टुत | इमानी तुभ्यं स्वसराणि येमिरे व्रता देवानाम मनुषश्च धर्मभिः ||
indra ṛbhumān vājavān matsveha no 'smin savane śacyā puruṣṭuta | imāni tubhyaṁ svasarāṇi yemire vratā devānām manuṣaś ca dharmabhiḥ ||
  • Indra: Refers to the king of the gods in Vedic tradition, symbolizing strength, power, and leadership.
  • ṛbhumān: Means one who is rich in hymns or praises, indicating the importance of devotion and reverence.
  • vājavān: Signifies one who possesses strength and vigor, often associated with the ability to overcome challenges.
  • matsveha: Translates to "in my mind" or "in my thoughts," emphasizing the importance of intention and focus.
  • no 'smin: Means "in this," referring to the present moment or situation.
  • savane: Relates to the act of invoking or calling upon, particularly in a ritualistic context.
  • śacyā: Means "with power" or "with strength," highlighting the need for divine support.
  • puruṣṭuta: Refers to the one who is praised or honored, indicating the significance of recognition and respect.
  • imāni: Means "these," referring to the offerings or prayers being made.
  • tubhyaṁ: Translates to "to you," indicating the direction of the offerings towards the divine.
  • svasarāṇi: Refers to "sisters," symbolizing unity and the importance of community.
  • yemire: Means "who have come," indicating the arrival of blessings or divine presence.
  • vratā: Refers to vows or commitments, emphasizing the importance of dedication and discipline.
  • devānām: Means "of the gods," indicating the divine nature of the entities being addressed.
  • manuṣaś: Refers to "of humans," highlighting the connection between the divine and human realms.
  • ca: Means "and," indicating the interconnectedness of all elements in the mantra.
  • dharmabhiḥ: Refers to "by righteous actions," emphasizing the importance of living a life of virtue and morality.

Conclusion: This mantra encapsulates the essence of invoking divine strength and support while emphasizing the importance of community, dedication, and righteous living. In real-life scenarios, it can be applied in moments of challenge or decision-making, where one seeks guidance and strength from a higher power. It serves as a reminder to maintain focus on virtuous actions and to honor the interconnectedness of all beings, fostering a sense of unity and purpose in our endeavors.
