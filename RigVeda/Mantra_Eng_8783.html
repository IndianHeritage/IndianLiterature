अश्वो वोळ्हा सुखं रथं हसनाम उपमन्त्रिणः |शेपो रोमन्वन्तौ भेदौ वार इन मण्डूका इच्छतीन्द्रायन्दो परि श्रव ||
aśvo voḻhā sukhaṁ rathaṁ hasanām upamantriṇaḥ | śepo romaṇvantau bhedau vār in maṇḍūka icchatīndrāyendo pari srava ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • aśvo: horse
    • voḻhā: may you be prosperous
    • sukhaṁ: happiness
    • rathaṁ: chariot
    • hasanām: of those who are joyful
    • upamantriṇaḥ: of the guiding or leading ones
    • śepo: auspiciousness
    • romaṇvantau: those who are adorned
    • bhedau: divisions or separations
    • vār: the best or the most excellent
    • in: in
    • maṇḍūka: frog
    • icchatī: desiring
    • ndrāyendo: to the lord of the heavens
    • pari: around
    • srava: flow or stream
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the imagery of a horse and chariot, symbolizing strength and the journey of life.
    • The mention of joy and guidance suggests the importance of having supportive relationships and a positive mindset.
    • The reference to auspiciousness and adornment indicates the value of beauty and positivity in our lives.
    • The frog, often associated with transformation and adaptability, signifies the ability to navigate through life's changes.
    • Overall, the mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking happiness, guidance, and auspiciousness in our endeavors.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, having a supportive team (upamantriṇaḥ) can lead to greater success and happiness (sukhaṁ) in achieving goals.
    • During challenging times, maintaining a positive outlook (hasanām) can help individuals navigate through difficulties, much like a chariot (rathaṁ) moving forward despite obstacles.
    • In personal relationships, fostering beauty and positivity (romaṇvantau) can enhance connections and create a harmonious environment.
    • Embracing change and adaptability (maṇḍūka) can lead to personal growth and new opportunities.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder to seek happiness, guidance, and auspiciousness in all aspects of life.
    • Its significance lies in the encouragement to cultivate positive relationships and a joyful mindset, which are essential for personal and communal growth.
    • In real-life scenarios, applying the principles of this mantra can lead to a more fulfilling and harmonious existence, both individually and collectively.
