मन्त्र: उता नो विष्णुर उता वातो अस्रिधो द्रविणोदā उता सोमो मयस् करत् |उता ऋभव उता राये नो अश्विनोता त्वष्टोता विभ्वानु मंसते ||
uta no viṣṇur uta vāto asridho draviṇodā uta somo mayas karat | uta ṛbhava uta rāye no aśvinota tvaṣṭota vibhvānu maṁsate ||
  • uta no viṣṇur: This phrase refers to Lord Vishnu, the preserver and protector in Hindu mythology. It signifies the importance of divine protection and guidance in our lives.
  • uta vāto asridho: This translates to "may the winds be favorable." It emphasizes the need for positive forces and energies in our environment, akin to how a sailor relies on favorable winds for a safe journey.
  • draviṇodā: This means "may we have wealth." It highlights the aspiration for prosperity and abundance, which is essential for a fulfilling life. For example, in modern society, financial stability is often linked to happiness and security.
  • uta somo mayas karat: This phrase can be interpreted as "may the soma (a sacred drink) grant us strength." Soma represents nourishment and vitality, akin to how we seek healthy food and lifestyle choices today to enhance our well-being.
  • uta ṛbhava: This means "may we attain success." It reflects the universal desire for achievement in various aspects of life, such as career, education, and personal goals.
  • uta rāye no: This translates to "may we have prosperity." It reinforces the idea of seeking abundance and success, similar to how individuals strive for career advancement and personal growth.
  • aśvinota: This refers to the Ashvins, the twin deities associated with health and medicine. It signifies the importance of well-being and healing in our lives, much like how we prioritize health in our daily routines.
  • tvaṣṭota: This term relates to Tvashtar, the divine craftsman. It symbolizes creativity and the ability to shape our destinies, akin to how artists and innovators influence the world through their creations.
  • vibhvānu maṁsate: This phrase can be interpreted as "may we be blessed with abundance." It encapsulates the essence of seeking blessings for a prosperous and fulfilling life.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a holistic approach to life, emphasizing the need for divine guidance, favorable circumstances, prosperity, health, creativity, and abundance. In real-life scenarios, it serves as a reminder to seek balance and harmony in our pursuits, whether in personal relationships, career aspirations, or health and wellness. By invoking these ideals, individuals can strive for a more fulfilling and enriched existence, aligning their actions with the greater cosmic order. This mantra can be recited during personal rituals, meditation, or even in moments of reflection to inspire positivity and abundance in daily life.
