एतच् चन तवो विघीतद एषां सत्यो मन्त्रः कविशस्त ऋघावान् |त्रिरश्रिं हन्ति चतुरश्रिर उग्रो देवनिदो ह प्रथमा अजूर्यन् ||
etac cana tvo vi ciketad eṣāṁ satyo mantraḥ kaviśasta ṛghāvān | triraśriṁ hanti caturaśrir ugro devanido ha prathamā ajūryan ||
  • etac cana tvo vi ciketad: This phrase can be translated as "This is what we seek from you." It expresses a desire for guidance or insight from a higher power or divine source.
  • eṣāṁ satyo mantraḥ: This means "This is the true mantra." It emphasizes the importance of truth and authenticity in spiritual practices and beliefs.
  • kaviśasta ṛghāvān: Translated as "the wise one, the poet," this refers to a figure of wisdom and creativity, suggesting that knowledge and inspiration come from those who are enlightened.
  • triraśriṁ hanti: This can be interpreted as "he who strikes thrice." It symbolizes strength and the ability to overcome challenges through repeated efforts.
  • caturaśrir ugro devanido: This phrase translates to "the four-faced, fierce divine being." It represents a powerful deity, embodying multiple aspects of divinity and strength.
  • ha prathamā ajūryan: This means "who first brings forth." It signifies the act of creation and the importance of being the initiator in various aspects of life.

Conclusion: This mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking wisdom and truth from divine sources while emphasizing the importance of strength, creativity, and initiation in our lives. In real-life scenarios, it can be applied in various contexts such as personal growth, leadership, and creative endeavors. For instance, when facing challenges, one can draw inspiration from the mantra to persistently strive for solutions, embodying the qualities of wisdom and strength. It serves as a reminder to seek authenticity in our pursuits and to embrace the divine guidance available to us.
