वरुणः प्रावित भुवन मित्रो विश्वाभिर ऊतिभिः |करतां नः सुराधसः ||
varuṇaḥ prāvitā bhuvan mitro viśvābhir ūtibhiḥ | karatāṁ naḥ surādhasaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • Varuṇaḥ: Refers to Varuna, the Vedic god of water, cosmic order, and the celestial ocean.
    • Prāvitā: Means 'having been made to flow' or 'having been set in motion.'
    • Bhuvan: Translates to 'world' or 'universe.'
    • Mitro: Refers to 'friend' or 'ally,' often associated with the sun and friendship.
    • Viśvābhir: Means 'with all' or 'by all,' indicating inclusiveness.
    • Ūtibhiḥ: Refers to 'strengths' or 'powers,' often in the context of blessings or support.
    • Karataṁ: Means 'may we do' or 'let us perform.'
    • Naḥ: Translates to 'our' or 'to us.'
    • Surādhasaḥ: Refers to 'the divine beings' or 'the gods,' often implying higher powers or deities.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes Varuna, asking for his blessings and support in the world.
    • It emphasizes the importance of friendship and unity among all beings, suggesting that we should work together for the common good.
    • The mention of 'strengths' indicates a desire for empowerment and divine assistance in our endeavors.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community project, individuals from diverse backgrounds come together, embodying the spirit of 'Mitro' and 'Viśvābhir,' working collaboratively to achieve a common goal.
    • In personal relationships, fostering friendships and support systems can lead to a more fulfilling life, reflecting the essence of unity and strength.
    • In environmental conservation efforts, invoking the blessings of nature (like Varuna) can inspire collective action towards sustainability.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of divine support, unity, and collective strength in our lives.
    • It serves as a reminder to seek blessings from higher powers while also emphasizing the importance of friendship and collaboration.
    • In real-life scenarios, it encourages individuals and communities to work together harmoniously, fostering a sense of belonging and shared purpose.
