आ ते रथस्य पूषन्न अज धुरं ववृत्युः |विश्वस्यार्थिनः सखा सनोजा अनपच्युतः ||
ā te rathasya pūṣann ajā dhuraṁ vavṛtyuḥ | viśvasyārthinaḥ sakhā sanojā anapacyutaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā: From
    • te: You
    • rathasya: Of the chariot
    • pūṣann: O nourisher
    • ajā: Unborn
    • dhuraṁ: The burden
    • vavṛtyuḥ: You have taken up
    • viśvasyārthinaḥ: Of all seekers of truth
    • sakhā: Friend
    • sanojā: With a companion
    • anapacyutaḥ: Never falling away
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra addresses the divine as a nourisher and protector, symbolizing the support and sustenance provided by the universe.
    • The "chariot" represents the journey of life, where the divine helps us carry our burdens and navigate through challenges.
    • The phrase "of all seekers of truth" emphasizes the universal nature of this support, indicating that it is available to everyone who seeks knowledge and understanding.
    • The idea of "never falling away" suggests a constant presence of divine support, reinforcing the notion that one is never truly alone in their journey.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In times of personal crisis, individuals often find solace in their faith or spirituality, feeling that a higher power is guiding them through their struggles.
    • Support systems, such as friends and family, can be seen as manifestations of this divine companionship, helping us carry our burdens and providing encouragement.
    • In professional settings, mentors can play a similar role, offering guidance and support to help individuals navigate their career paths.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of divine support in our lives, reminding us that we are not alone in our struggles.
    • It encourages us to seek truth and understanding, assuring us that there is a nurturing presence available to help us along our journey.
    • In practical terms, it serves as a reminder to cultivate relationships and support systems that can help us navigate life's challenges, reinforcing the idea that we are all interconnected in our pursuit of truth and fulfillment.
