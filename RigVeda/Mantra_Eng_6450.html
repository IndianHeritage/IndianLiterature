तव क्रत्वा सनेयं तव रातिभिर अग्ने तव प्रशस्तिभिः |त्वाम इद आहुः प्रमतिं वसो ममाग्ने हर्षस्व दातवे ||
tava kratvā saneyaṁ tava rātibhir agne tava praśastibhiḥ | tvām id āhuḥ pramatiṁ vaso mamāgne harṣasva dātave ||
  • tava kratvā saneyaṁ: This phrase can be translated as "by your power, O Agni." Here, "kratvā" refers to the strength or power, and "saneyaṁ" implies the ability to achieve or manifest. In real life, this can be likened to the strength we derive from our own capabilities and efforts in achieving our goals.
  • tava rātibhir agne: This means "by your radiance, O Agni." "Rātibhir" refers to the brilliance or light. This can be compared to the inspiration we receive from leaders or mentors in our lives who illuminate our paths and guide us towards success.
  • tava praśastibhiḥ: Translated as "by your praises," this phrase emphasizes the importance of recognition and appreciation. In everyday life, this can be seen in how acknowledgment of our efforts by peers or superiors can motivate us to perform better.
  • tvām id āhuḥ pramatiṁ: This translates to "they call you the wise one." "Pramatiṁ" refers to wisdom or intelligence. In a practical sense, this highlights the value of wisdom in decision-making, whether in personal life or in professional settings.
  • vaso mamāgne: This means "O Agni, grant me wealth." Here, "vaso" refers to wealth or prosperity. This can be related to the pursuit of financial stability and abundance in our lives, emphasizing the importance of seeking prosperity through hard work and dedication.
  • harṣasva dātave: This translates to "may you bestow joy." "Harṣasva" implies happiness or joy. In real life, this can be connected to the idea that true wealth is not just material but also includes emotional and spiritual fulfillment.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking strength, wisdom, and prosperity through the divine presence of Agni, the fire god. It signifies the importance of harnessing our inner power, seeking guidance, and recognizing the value of joy and wealth in our lives. In practical terms, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as during personal challenges, professional endeavors, or spiritual practices, reminding us to strive for excellence while remaining grounded in our values and aspirations.
