तरोभिर वो विदद्वासुम इन्द्रं सबाधा ऊतये | बृहद् गायन्तः सुतसोमे अध्वरे हुवे भरं न कारिणम् ||
tarobhir vo vidadvasum indraṁ sabādha ūtaye | bṛhad gāyantaḥ sutasome adhvare huve bharaṁ na kāriṇam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • tarobhir: By the stars or celestial bodies
    • vo: To you
    • vidadvasum: Bestow wealth or prosperity
    • indraṁ: Indra, the king of gods
    • sabādha: With great strength or power
    • ūtaye: For the purpose of elevation or upliftment
    • bṛhad: Great or vast
    • gāyantaḥ: Singing or chanting
    • sutasome: With the essence of Soma (a sacred drink)
    • adhvare: In the sacrificial rite
    • huve: I invoke or call upon
    • bharaṁ: Abundance or fullness
    • na: Not
    • kāriṇam: One who acts or performs
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Indra, the god of rain and thunderstorms, asking for prosperity and abundance.
    • The invocation emphasizes the connection between celestial bodies (stars) and earthly wealth, suggesting that divine forces influence material success.
    • The mention of "Soma" indicates a spiritual or ritualistic aspect, where the essence of life and vitality is sought through sacred practices.
    • It highlights the importance of collective effort ("gāyantaḥ") in achieving goals, suggesting that unity and harmony in community lead to greater outcomes.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern contexts, this mantra can be related to community gatherings where people come together to celebrate achievements or seek blessings for future endeavors.
    • In business, the idea of invoking prosperity can be seen in team-building exercises that aim to create a unified vision for success.
    • Rituals or ceremonies that involve collective prayers for abundance, such as harvest festivals, resonate with the essence of this mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of divine blessings in achieving prosperity and the role of community in invoking such blessings.
    • It teaches us that our efforts, when combined with spiritual practices and collective intentions, can lead to greater abundance in life.
    • In real-life scenarios, it encourages us to seek harmony with nature and the cosmos while working together towards common goals, reminding us of the interconnectedness of all beings.
