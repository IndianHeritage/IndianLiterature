स यो न मुहे न मिथू जनो भूत सुमन्तुनामाऽ चुमुरीं धुनिं च |वृणक पिप्रुं शम्बरं शुष्णं इन्द्रः पुरां च्यौत्नाय शयथा नू चित ||
sa yo na muhe na mithū jano bhūt sumantunāmā cumuriṁ dhuniṁ ca | vṛṇak pipruṁ śambaraṁ śuṣṇam indraḥ purāṁ cyautnāya śayathāya nū cit ||
  • sa yo na muhe na mithū jano bhūt: This phrase can be translated to "He who does not deceive or mislead others." It emphasizes the importance of honesty and integrity in human interactions. In real life, this can be seen in leaders who inspire trust among their followers by being transparent and truthful.
  • sumantunāmā cumuriṁ dhuniṁ ca: This part refers to "the one who is wise and possesses good judgment." It highlights the value of wisdom and discernment. For example, a wise mentor or teacher who guides students with sound advice embodies this quality, helping them navigate challenges effectively.
  • vṛṇak pipruṁ śambaraṁ śuṣṇam: This translates to "the one who chooses the right path and avoids the wrong." It signifies the importance of making conscious choices in life. In a practical scenario, this can relate to individuals who choose ethical business practices over shortcuts that may lead to success but compromise their values.
  • indraḥ purāṁ cyautnāya: This phrase can be interpreted as "Indra, the powerful, who protects and supports." Indra is often seen as a symbol of strength and protection. In real life, this can be likened to a supportive community or family that stands by individuals during difficult times, providing strength and encouragement.
  • shayathāya nū cit: This means "to bring forth clarity and understanding." It emphasizes the need for clear communication and understanding in relationships. For instance, in a workplace, open communication can lead to better teamwork and collaboration, fostering a positive environment.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of integrity, wisdom, ethical decision-making, support, and clarity in communication. Its significance lies in its applicability to everyday life, encouraging individuals to embody these virtues in their personal and professional interactions. By adhering to these principles, one can foster a harmonious and productive environment, ultimately leading to personal growth and societal well-being.
