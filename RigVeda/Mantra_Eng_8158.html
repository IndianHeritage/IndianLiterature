परीतो वायवे सुतं गिर इन्द्राय मत्सरम् |अव्यो वारेषु सिञ्चत ||
parīto vāyave sutaṁ gira indrāya matsaram | avyo vāreṣu siñcata ||
  • Mantra: "parīto vāyave sutaṁ gira indrāya matsaram | avyo vāreṣu siñcata ||"
  • Translation: "O Wind, pour forth the sonorous words for Indra, free from envy; let them flow in abundance."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • parīto: Surrounding or encompassing, indicating a sense of completeness or wholeness.
    • vāyave: Referring to the Wind, symbolizing movement, communication, and the vital breath of life.
    • sutaṁ: Son or offspring, often used in a broader sense to refer to the results or outcomes of actions.
    • gira: Words or speech, emphasizing the power of communication and expression.
    • indrāya: For Indra, the king of gods, representing strength, power, and leadership.
    • matsaram: Envy or jealousy, suggesting the importance of purity in intentions and actions.
    • avyō: Free from, indicating a desire for purity and clarity.
    • vāreṣu: In abundance or plentifully, suggesting a generous flow.
    • siñcata: To pour or to sprinkle, symbolizing the act of sharing or distributing.
  • Significance:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of clear and positive communication, free from negative emotions like envy.
    • It highlights the role of the Wind as a carrier of sound and messages, symbolizing the interconnectedness of all beings through communication.
    • By invoking Indra, the mantra seeks to channel strength and leadership in our words and actions, promoting unity and harmony.
  • Real-Life Application:
    • In personal relationships, this mantra can serve as a reminder to communicate openly and honestly, avoiding jealousy and misunderstandings.
    • In professional settings, it encourages teamwork and collaboration, where ideas can flow freely without the hindrance of envy or competition.
    • In community gatherings or discussions, it promotes a spirit of sharing and inclusivity, ensuring that everyone’s voice is heard and valued.
  • Conclusion:
    • The mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the significance of pure communication and the impact of our words on others.
    • By striving to communicate without envy and with a spirit of abundance, we can foster stronger relationships and create a more harmonious environment.
    • Ultimately, it encourages us to be mindful of our intentions and the energy we bring into our interactions, aligning with the greater good.
