वह कुत्सम् इन्द्र यस्मिञ्चाकन् स्युमन्युः ऋज्राऽवातस्याश्वा | प्र सूरश्चक्रं वृहताद अभीकऽभि स्पृधो यासिषद् वज्रबाहुः ||
vaha kutsam indra yasmiñ cākan syūmanyū ṛjrā vātasyāśvā | pra sūraś cakraṁ vṛhatād abhīke 'bhi spṛdho yāsiṣad vajrabāhuḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • vaha: to carry or bear
    • kutsam: the one who is worthy of respect
    • indra: the king of gods, representing strength and power
    • yasmiñ: in whom
    • cākan: who is capable
    • syūmanyū: with great energy or vigor
    • ṛjrā: the one who is swift
    • vātasyāśvā: the horses of the wind
    • pra: towards
    • sūraś: the brave or heroic
    • cakraṁ: the wheel or cycle, often symbolizing movement or action
    • vṛhatād: from the great
    • abhīke: to the one who is fierce
    • 'bhi: with
    • spṛdho: with desire or intention
    • yāsiṣad: you have gone
    • vajrabāhuḥ: the one with a thunderbolt arm, symbolizing strength and power
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes Indra, the king of gods, emphasizing his strength and capability.
    • The phrase "vaha kutsam" suggests the importance of carrying respect and dignity in our actions.
    • Indra is depicted as swift and powerful, akin to a leader who can navigate challenges with vigor.
    • The mention of "vātasyāśvā" symbolizes the dynamic forces of nature, reminding us of the energy that drives life.
    • The "sūraś cakraṁ" indicates the cyclical nature of life and the importance of taking action in a brave manner.
    • Overall, the mantra highlights the qualities of strength, respect, and the importance of action in the face of challenges.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In leadership, embodying the qualities of Indra can inspire teams to act with courage and respect.
    • In personal challenges, invoking the energy of this mantra can motivate individuals to overcome obstacles with vigor.
    • In community service, carrying respect and dignity can foster a sense of unity and strength among members.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of strength, respect, and action in our lives.
    • It encourages individuals to embody the qualities of a leader, facing challenges with courage and dignity.
    • In various real-life scenarios, such as leadership, personal growth, and community engagement, the principles derived from this mantra can guide us towards a more empowered and respectful existence.
