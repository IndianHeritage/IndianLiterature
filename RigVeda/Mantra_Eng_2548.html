त्रीणि शतां त्रि सहस्राण्य अग्निं त्रिंशच च देवā नव चासपर्यन् | aukṣan घृतैर अस्तृणन बर्हिर अस्मा आद इद धोतारं न्य असादयन्त ||
trīṇi śatā trī sahasrāṇy agniṁ triṁśac ca devā nava cāsaparyan | aukṣan ghṛtair astṛṇan barhir asmā ād id dhotāraṁ ny asādayanta ||
  • Mantra: "trīṇi śatā trī sahasrāṇy agniṁ triṁśac ca devā nava cāsaparyan | aukṣan ghṛtair astṛṇan barhir asmā ād id dhotāraṁ ny asādayanta ||"
  • Translation: This mantra speaks of the divine fire (Agni) and the offerings made to it, invoking the presence of deities and the importance of rituals.
  • Word Breakdown:
    • trīṇi: three
    • śatā: hundreds
    • trī: three
    • sahasrāṇy: thousands
    • agniṁ: fire
    • triṁśac: thirty
    • devā: deities
    • nava: nine
    • cāsaparyan: offerings
    • aukṣan: nourished
    • ghṛtair: with ghee
    • astṛṇan: spread
    • barhir: grass or sacred grass
    • asmā: here
    • ād: thus
    • id: this
    • dhotāraṁ: the one who is invoked
    • ny asādayanta: they have satisfied
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of fire (Agni) in Vedic rituals, symbolizing purity and the divine connection between humans and gods.
    • The mention of "trīṇi śatā" and "trī sahasrāṇy" indicates the vastness of offerings, suggesting that the rituals involve hundreds and thousands of offerings to please the deities.
    • The phrase "nava cāsaparyan" highlights the importance of specific offerings, which can be likened to how in modern life, we often make offerings or sacrifices for our goals, such as time, effort, or resources.
    • Using "ghṛtair" (with ghee) signifies the quality of offerings, akin to how we strive for excellence in our endeavors, ensuring that what we present is of the highest quality.
    • The act of spreading "barhir" (grass) can be compared to creating a conducive environment for our aspirations, much like how we prepare our surroundings for important events or gatherings.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of rituals and offerings in establishing a connection with the divine.
    • In real life, it encourages us to recognize the value of our efforts and the quality of what we offer to achieve our goals.
    • By understanding the significance of our actions and the environment we create, we can foster a sense of purpose and fulfillment in our lives.
    • Ultimately, it teaches us that through dedication and quality offerings, we can invoke blessings and support from higher powers in our personal and communal endeavors.
