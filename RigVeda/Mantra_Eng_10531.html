यस्मै पुत्रासो अदितेः प्र जीवसे मर्त्याय |ज्योतिर यच्छन्त्य अजस्रम ||
yasmai putrāso aditeḥ pra jīvase martyāya | jyotir yacchanty ajasram ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yasmai: To whom
    • putrāso: Sons or offspring
    • aditeḥ: Of Aditi (the mother of gods)
    • pra jīvase: You live or you are born
    • martyāya: For the mortal
    • jyotir: Light or radiance
    • yacchanty: They give or they bestow
    • ajasram: Everlasting or eternal
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks to the divine connection between Aditi, the mother of all gods, and her offspring.
    • It emphasizes the idea that those who are born from Aditi are granted a life that is illuminated by eternal light.
    • The phrase "for the mortal" suggests that this divine light is essential for human existence and understanding.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In many cultures, the concept of a divine mother figure is prevalent, symbolizing nurturing and protection. For instance, in Christianity, the Virgin Mary is often seen as a source of light and guidance for believers.
    • In modern life, the pursuit of knowledge and enlightenment can be seen as a reflection of the "light" mentioned in the mantra. Educational institutions often strive to illuminate the minds of their students, akin to the eternal light bestowed by Aditi.
    • In personal relationships, the support and love from family can be viewed as a source of light that helps individuals navigate the challenges of life, much like the divine light referenced in the mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of divine guidance and the nurturing aspect of life that comes from a higher power.
    • It reminds us that our existence is intertwined with the light of knowledge, love, and support, which can be found in our relationships and experiences.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra encourages individuals to seek enlightenment and to recognize the divine influences that shape their lives, fostering a sense of gratitude and connection to the greater universe.
