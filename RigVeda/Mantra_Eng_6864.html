तं त्वा वयं हवामहे श्रिण्वन्तं जातवेदसम् |अग्नि घ्नन्तमप द्विषः ||
taṁ tvā vayaṁ havāmahe śṛṇvantaṁ jātavedasam | agne ghnantam apa dviṣaḥ ||
  • Mantra: "taṁ tvā vayaṁ havāmahe śṛṇvantaṁ jātavedasam | agne ghnantam apa dviṣaḥ ||"
  • Translation: "We invoke you, O Jātavedas (the Knower of all births), listen to us. O Agni (Fire), drive away those who wish us harm."

  • Word-by-Word Explanation:
    • taṁ: that (referring to Agni)
    • tvā: you (addressing Agni)
    • vayaṁ: we (the worshippers)
    • havāmahe: we invoke or call upon
    • śṛṇvantaṁ: listening or paying attention
    • jātavedasam: the one who knows all births, a name for Agni
    • agne: O Agni
    • ghnantam: drive away or destroy
    • apa: away or from
    • dviṣaḥ: enemies or those who wish harm

  • Contextual Significance:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Agni, the fire god, who is considered a mediator between humans and the divine.
    • Agni is often associated with knowledge, transformation, and purification, making him a vital figure in Vedic rituals.
    • The invocation of Agni as "Jātavedas" emphasizes his role as the knower of all births, symbolizing the connection between the physical and spiritual realms.

  • Real-Life Application:
    • In modern life, this mantra can be seen as a call for protection and guidance in times of adversity.
    • For example, when facing challenges at work or in personal relationships, one might invoke the spirit of Agni to seek clarity and strength to overcome obstacles.
    • Additionally, the idea of driving away negativity can be applied in practices such as meditation or rituals that focus on cleansing one's environment and mind from harmful influences.

  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the importance of seeking divine assistance in overcoming challenges and protecting oneself from negativity.
    • It serves as a reminder of the power of intention and the need for spiritual guidance in our daily lives.
    • By invoking Agni, we acknowledge the transformative power of fire, both literally and metaphorically, in purifying our lives and driving away harmful energies.
