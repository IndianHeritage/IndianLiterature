तं ऋत्वियाऽ उप वाचः साचन्ते सर्गो न यो देवयताम् असर्जि | बृहस्पतिः स ह्य अञ्जो वरांसी विभ्वाभवत सम ऋते मातरिश्वा ||
tam ṛtviyā upa vācaḥ sacante sargo na yo devayatām asarji | bṛhaspatiḥ sa hy añjo varāṁsi vibhvābhavat sam ṛte mātariśvā ||
  • tam ṛtviyā upa vācaḥ: This phrase can be translated as "to that which is connected with the seasons or the cycles of nature." Here, "ṛtviyā" refers to the seasonal cycles, emphasizing the importance of understanding and aligning with the natural rhythms of life.
  • sacante sargo na yo devayatām asarji: This part means "the one who does not deviate from the divine order." It highlights the significance of adhering to the natural and divine laws that govern existence, suggesting that those who follow these principles will find harmony and prosperity.
  • bṛhaspatiḥ sa hy añjo varāṁsi vibhvābhavat: This translates to "Bṛhaspati, the lord of wisdom, who bestows blessings and abundance." Bṛhaspati is often associated with knowledge, wisdom, and the guiding principles of life. This line emphasizes the role of wisdom in achieving success and well-being.
  • sam ṛte mātariśvā: This phrase can be interpreted as "in harmony with the cosmic order." "Mātariśvā" refers to the wind or the cosmic force that connects all elements of the universe. It signifies the importance of being in sync with the universe to achieve balance and fulfillment.

Conclusion:

  • The mantra emphasizes the significance of aligning oneself with the natural and divine order of the universe. In real life, this can be applied by recognizing the importance of nature's cycles, such as seasons, and making decisions that respect these rhythms.
  • For example, in agriculture, farmers who plant and harvest according to seasonal changes often experience better yields. Similarly, in personal life, understanding one's own cycles of energy and productivity can lead to more effective time management and well-being.
  • Furthermore, the mantra highlights the value of wisdom and knowledge in navigating life's challenges. Seeking guidance from wise mentors or engaging in continuous learning can lead to better decision-making and success.
  • Ultimately, this mantra serves as a reminder to live in harmony with the universe, embracing both the natural order and the wisdom that comes from understanding it. By doing so, individuals can cultivate a life of abundance, peace, and fulfillment.
