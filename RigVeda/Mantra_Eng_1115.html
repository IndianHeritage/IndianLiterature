यद vā मरुत्वः परम सधस्थे यद वावमे वृजन मादयासे |अता ā याह्य अध्वरं नो अच्छा त्वायā हविष् चक्रिमā सत्यराधः ||
yad vā marutvaḥ parame sadhasthe yad vāvame vṛjane mādayāse | ata ā yāhy adhvaraṁ no acchā tvāyā haviś cakṛmā satyarādhaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yad: whatever
    • vā: or
    • marutvaḥ: of the Maruts (the storm deities)
    • parame: supreme
    • sadhasthe: in the place of truth
    • yad: whatever
    • vāvame: we invoke
    • vṛjane: in the act of sacrifice
    • mādayāse: may you be pleased
    • ata: therefore
    • ā: come
    • yāhy: we approach
    • adhvaraṁ: the sacred ritual
    • no: for us
    • acchā: may you be favorable
    • tvāyā: to you
    • haviś: the offerings
    • cakṛmā: we have made
    • satyarādhaḥ: with truth and righteousness
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to the Maruts, the deities associated with storms and winds, asking for their blessings and favor.
    • The phrase "in the place of truth" emphasizes the importance of sincerity and righteousness in rituals and offerings.
    • The invocation of the Maruts signifies a call for their presence and assistance in the sacred act of sacrifice, which is a central theme in Vedic rituals.
    • The mantra reflects the belief that divine beings are pleased by sincere offerings and that such offerings should be made with a pure heart and intention.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern life, this mantra can be related to the importance of integrity in our actions. For instance, when we engage in community service or charitable acts, doing so with genuine intent aligns with the essence of this mantra.
    • In professional settings, approaching tasks with honesty and dedication can lead to favorable outcomes, akin to invoking the blessings of the Maruts for success.
    • During personal challenges, seeking guidance through sincere prayer or meditation can be seen as a modern interpretation of invoking divine favor, similar to the ritualistic approach of the Vedic times.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its emphasis on truthfulness and the importance of sincere offerings in seeking divine blessings.
    • It teaches us that our intentions matter, and that approaching life’s challenges with integrity can lead to positive outcomes.
    • In a broader sense, it encourages us to cultivate a relationship with the divine through our actions, ensuring that we remain grounded in truth and righteousness.
