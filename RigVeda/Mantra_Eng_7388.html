सुशेवं नो मृळयाकुर अदृप्तक्रतुर अवातः |भव नः सोम शं हृदे ||
suśevo no mṛḻayākur adṛptakratur avātaḥ | bhavā naḥ soma śaṁ hṛde ||
  • suśevo: This word can be broken down into "su" meaning good or auspicious, and "śeva" which refers to service or action. Together, it implies a call for good actions or auspicious deeds.
  • no: This is a pronoun meaning "our," indicating a collective or communal aspect.
  • mṛḻayākur: This term can be interpreted as "may we be protected" or "may we be nurtured." It suggests a desire for care and safeguarding.
  • adṛptakratur: This compound word can be broken down into "adṛpta" meaning unyielding or steadfast, and "kratur" which refers to intention or will. It signifies a strong and unwavering intention.
  • avātaḥ: This word means "may it come" or "may it arrive," indicating a hopeful expectation for something beneficial.
  • bhavā: This term translates to "be" or "exist," often used in a context of existence or being.
  • naḥ: This is another pronoun meaning "our," reinforcing the communal aspect of the mantra.
  • soma: In Vedic literature, "soma" refers to a sacred drink that is believed to confer immortality and divine inspiration. It symbolizes nourishment and spiritual sustenance.
  • śaṁ: This word means "peace" or "well-being," indicating a desire for tranquility and harmony.
  • hṛde: This translates to "in the heart," suggesting that the essence of the mantra is deeply personal and internal.

Conclusion: The mantra "suśevo no mṛḻayākur adṛptakratur avātaḥ | bhavā naḥ soma śaṁ hṛde ||" emphasizes the importance of good actions, protection, steadfast intentions, and the pursuit of peace and nourishment in our lives. It calls for a collective aspiration towards auspiciousness and well-being, both individually and communally. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts such as community gatherings, personal reflections, or during rituals where individuals seek harmony and protection. It serves as a reminder to cultivate positive intentions and actions that contribute to the well-being of oneself and others, fostering a sense of unity and peace in society.
