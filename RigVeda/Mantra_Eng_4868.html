मन्त्र: परो हि मार्त्यैर असि समो देवैर उता श्रियाः |अभि ख्यः पूषण पृतनासु नस् त्वम् अवा नूनं यथा पुरा ||
paro hi martyair asi samo devair uta śriyā | abhi khyaḥ pūṣan pṛtanāsu nas tvam avā nūnaṁ yathā purā ||
  • Translation: "You are indeed superior to mortals, equal to the gods, and endowed with wealth. O Pūṣan, may you protect us in our endeavors as you did in the past."

  • Word-by-Word Explanation:
    • paro: superior, beyond
    • hi: indeed, certainly
    • martyair: to mortals, human beings
    • asi: you are
    • samo: equal, alike
    • devair: to the gods
    • uta: and, also
    • śriyā: endowed with wealth, prosperity
    • abhi: towards, for
    • khyaḥ: known, recognized
    • pūṣan: O Pūṣan, a deity associated with nourishment and prosperity
    • pṛtanāsu: in our endeavors, efforts
    • nas: us, our
    • tvam: you
    • avā: protect, guard
    • nūnaṁ: certainly, surely
    • yathā: as, like
    • purā: in the past, previously

  • Contextual Significance:
    • This mantra highlights the divine qualities of Pūṣan, who is revered as a deity that provides nourishment and prosperity.
    • It emphasizes the relationship between the divine and human realms, suggesting that while humans are mortal, they can aspire to divine qualities through their actions and intentions.
    • The invocation of Pūṣan for protection in endeavors reflects a common human desire for guidance and support in life's pursuits.

  • Real-Life Applications:
    • In daily life, individuals often seek blessings or guidance from higher powers when embarking on new ventures, such as starting a business or pursuing education.
    • The mantra can be recited during significant life events, such as weddings or new job opportunities, to invoke prosperity and protection.
    • It serves as a reminder to strive for excellence and to align one's actions with higher ideals, fostering a sense of purpose and direction.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the aspiration for divine support in human endeavors, highlighting the importance of seeking guidance and protection from higher powers. It serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of the mortal and divine, encouraging individuals to pursue their goals with integrity and purpose, while remaining open to the blessings of prosperity and nourishment in their lives.
