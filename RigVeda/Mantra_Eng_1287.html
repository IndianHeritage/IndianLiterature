चरित्रं हि वेरिवाच्छेदि पर्णम आज क्हेलस्य परितकम्यायाम् |सद्योजङ्घाम् आयसीं विश्पलायै धनहिते सर्तवे प्रत्यधत्तम् ||
caritraṁ hi ver ivācchedi parṇam ājā khelasya paritakmyāyām | sadyo jaṅghām āyasīṁ viśpalāyai dhane hite sartave praty adhattam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • caritraṁ: conduct or character
    • hi: indeed
    • ver: like a warrior or strong
    • ivācchedi: cuts through or overcomes
    • parṇam: leaves or foliage
    • ājā: in the game or play
    • khelasya: of the game
    • paritakmyāyām: in the surrounding or environment
    • sadyo: immediately
    • jaṅghām: the leg or limb
    • āyasīṁ: strong or powerful
    • viśpalāyai: for the one who is prosperous
    • dhane: wealth
    • hite: for the good
    • sartave: in the act of moving or striving
    • praty: towards
    • adhattam: has been placed or established
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of character and conduct in overcoming challenges.
    • It suggests that a strong character can cut through obstacles, much like a warrior in battle.
    • The reference to "leaves" symbolizes the various aspects of life that can be affected by one's actions and decisions.
    • The phrase "in the game" indicates that life is a play or a game where one's conduct is crucial for success.
    • It highlights the immediate impact of one's actions on their strength and prosperity.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • A person facing a difficult situation at work may rely on their strong character and ethical conduct to navigate challenges, leading to career advancement.
    • In sports, an athlete's discipline and commitment to training can be likened to the warrior's strength, enabling them to overcome competitors.
    • In personal relationships, maintaining integrity and good conduct can strengthen bonds and lead to a prosperous and fulfilling life.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the significance of character and conduct in achieving success and overcoming life's challenges.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, including professional settings, personal relationships, and competitive environments.
    • By embodying strong values and ethical behavior, individuals can create a positive impact on their surroundings and achieve their goals.
