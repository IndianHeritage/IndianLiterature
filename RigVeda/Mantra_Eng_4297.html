अत्रिर यद् वाम् अवरोहन्न ऋबीसम् अजोहवीन् नाधमानेव योषा |श्येनस्य चिज् जवासां नूतनेनागच्छताम् अश्विना शान्तमेना ||
atrir yad vām avarohann ṛbīsam ajohavīn nādhamāneva yoṣā | śyenasya cij javasā nūtanenāgacchatam aśvinā śaṁtamena ||
  • atrir yad vām avarohann: This phrase can be interpreted as "O Atri, who descends upon you." Atri is one of the ancient sages in Vedic literature, symbolizing wisdom and knowledge. In real life, this can relate to the idea of seeking guidance from wise individuals or mentors in our lives.
  • ṛbīsam ajohavīn nādhamāneva yoṣā: This translates to "the one who is not hindered by the obstacles, like a woman who is not restrained." This can be seen as a metaphor for resilience and determination. For example, a woman pursuing her career despite societal challenges embodies this spirit of overcoming obstacles.
  • śyenasya cij javasā: This means "with the swiftness of the falcon." The falcon is known for its speed and precision. In a real-life context, this can represent the importance of being quick and decisive in our actions, much like a business leader making swift decisions in a competitive market.
  • nūtanenāgacchatam: This translates to "may you come with newness." This phrase emphasizes the importance of innovation and renewal. In today's fast-paced world, businesses and individuals must adapt and innovate to stay relevant, akin to how technology evolves rapidly.
  • aśvinā śaṁtamena: This can be interpreted as "with the peace of the Ashvins." The Ashvins are twin deities associated with health and well-being. This signifies the importance of maintaining peace and harmony in our lives, which can be reflected in practices like meditation and mindfulness.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates themes of wisdom, resilience, swiftness, innovation, and peace. In real life, these qualities are essential for personal and professional growth. By seeking guidance from mentors, overcoming obstacles, making swift decisions, embracing innovation, and maintaining inner peace, individuals can navigate the complexities of modern life effectively. This mantra serves as a reminder of the virtues that can lead to a fulfilling and successful life.
