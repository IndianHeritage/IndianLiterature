त्वं नृचक्षां वृषभानु पूर्वीः कृष्णास्व अग्ने अरुषो वि भाहि |वसो नेषि च परषि चात्य अंहः कृधī नो राया उशिजो यविष्ठ ||
tvaṁ nṛcakṣā vṛṣabhānu pūrvīḥ kṛṣṇāsv agne aruṣo vi bhāhi | vaso neṣi ca parṣi cāty aṁhaḥ kṛdhī no rāya uśijo yaviṣṭha ||
  • tvaṁ nṛcakṣā: This phrase translates to "You, O Agni, with the eyes of a man." Here, Agni is personified and depicted as a divine being with human-like qualities. This suggests a connection between the divine and human realms, emphasizing the importance of recognizing the divine in our everyday lives.
  • vṛṣabhānu pūrvīḥ: "The one who shines like the sun." This metaphor highlights the brilliance and vitality of Agni, akin to the sun's life-giving energy. In real life, we can relate this to the importance of light and warmth in our lives, both literally and metaphorically, as sources of inspiration and motivation.
  • kṛṣṇāsv agne: "O Agni, who is black." This may refer to the transformative power of fire, which can consume and purify. In our lives, we often face challenges that may seem dark or difficult, but these experiences can lead to personal growth and transformation.
  • aruṣo vi bhāhi: "Shine forth with your red light." This line emphasizes the dynamic and vibrant nature of Agni. In a practical sense, it can be seen as a call to embrace our passions and let our inner light shine, inspiring others around us.
  • vaso neṣi ca parṣi cāty aṁhaḥ: "You who carry the wealth and the blessings." This suggests that Agni is a source of prosperity and abundance. In real life, this can be interpreted as the importance of nurturing our resources and relationships to cultivate a fulfilling life.
  • kṛdhī no rāya: "May you grant us strength." This is a plea for empowerment and support. In our daily lives, we often seek strength to overcome obstacles, whether in personal endeavors or professional challenges.
  • uśijo yaviṣṭha: "O Agni, the most excellent of the strong." This line acknowledges Agni's supreme power and excellence. It serves as a reminder to strive for excellence in our own lives, pushing ourselves to achieve our best potential.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of Agni as a divine force that embodies light, transformation, strength, and prosperity. In real life, it encourages us to recognize the divine in our experiences, embrace challenges as opportunities for growth, and strive for excellence in our pursuits. By invoking the qualities of Agni, we can cultivate a life filled with inspiration, strength, and abundance, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling existence.
