अस्येदुत्वेषसारन्त सिंधवः परि यद्वज्रेण सिमयच्छत् |ईशानकृत दाशुषे दशस्यन् तुर्वीतये गाधं तुर्वणिः कः ||
asyed u tveṣasā ranta sindhavaḥ pari yad vajreṇa sīm ayacchat | īśānakṛd dāśuṣe daśasyan turvītaye gādhaṁ turvaṇiḥ kaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • asyed: this (referring to the subject of the mantra)
    • u: indeed
    • tveṣasā: with your power
    • ranta: to the one who is pleased
    • sindhavaḥ: the rivers or the flowing waters
    • pari: around
    • yad: which
    • vajreṇa: with the thunderbolt
    • sīm: the boundary or protection
    • ayacchat: has given
    • īśānakṛd: the one who is the lord or ruler
    • dāśuṣe: for the servant or devotee
    • daśasyan: of the ten (often referring to the ten directions)
    • turvītaye: for the purpose of strength or power
    • gādhaṁ: deep or profound
    • turvaṇiḥ: the one who is powerful
    • kaḥ: who
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks to the divine power that governs the natural elements, particularly the rivers and waters.
    • It emphasizes the importance of divine protection and strength, suggesting that the ruler of the universe provides safety and support to those who are devoted.
    • The mention of "vajreṇa" (thunderbolt) symbolizes immense power and the ability to overcome obstacles.
    • In a real-life context, this can be related to the challenges we face, where we seek strength and guidance from a higher power or the universe.
  • Significance and Real-Life Application:
    • This mantra can be recited during times of uncertainty or when facing challenges, invoking the divine for strength and protection.
    • It serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of all elements in nature and the importance of respecting and honoring them.
    • In practical terms, it can be applied in situations such as seeking guidance in decision-making, overcoming personal struggles, or during rituals that honor nature and the divine.
    • Ultimately, it encourages individuals to recognize their own power and the support available to them from the universe.
