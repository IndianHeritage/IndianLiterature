अभि त्यं वीरं गिरवणसम आर्चेन्द्रं ब्रह्मणा जरीतर नवेन |श्रवद इद धवम् उप च स्तवानो रासद वाजाम् उप महो गृणानः ||
abhi tyaṁ vīraṁ girvaṇasam arcendram brahmaṇā jaritar navena | śravad id dhavam upa ca stavāno rāsad vājām̐ upa maho gṛṇānaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • abhi: towards, for
    • tyaṁ: that, this
    • vīraṁ: hero, brave one
    • girvaṇasam: of the one who is praised, the one who is celebrated
    • arcendram: the one who is worshipped, the divine
    • brahmaṇā: by the supreme, the ultimate reality
    • jaritar: old, ancient
    • navena: new, fresh
    • śravad: flowing, moving
    • id: this
    • dhavam: to the one who is swift
    • upa: near, towards
    • ca: and
    • stavāno: praising, invoking
    • rāsad: the one who is rich, prosperous
    • vājām̐: of the horses, strength
    • upa: towards
    • maho: great, mighty
    • gṛṇānaḥ: singing, chanting
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the essence of bravery and strength, calling upon a hero who is celebrated and worshipped.
    • It emphasizes the duality of the old and the new, suggesting a balance between tradition and innovation.
    • The mantra also highlights the importance of praise and invocation, recognizing the power of words and intentions in manifesting strength and prosperity.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, leaders often invoke the spirit of bravery and innovation to inspire their teams, much like this mantra calls upon a hero.
    • In personal development, individuals may reflect on their past experiences (the old) while embracing new opportunities (the new) to grow and succeed.
    • During ceremonies or rituals, the act of chanting and praising can be seen as a way to invoke positive energies and intentions, similar to the invocation in this mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of courage, the balance of tradition and modernity, and the power of praise in achieving greatness.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal growth to leadership and community building, reminding us that invoking strength and celebrating achievements can lead to prosperity and success.
