तस्य ते वाजिनो वयं विश्व धनानि जिग्युषः |सखित्वम् आ वृणीमहे ||
tasya te vājino vayaṁ viśvā dhanāni jigyuṣaḥ | sakhitvam ā vṛṇīmahe ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • tasya: of that
    • te: your
    • vājino: of the strong, powerful
    • vayaṁ: we
    • viśvā: all, universal
    • dhanāni: riches, wealth
    • jigyuṣaḥ: may we conquer, may we achieve
    • sakhitvam: friendship, companionship
    • ā: towards
    • vṛṇīmahe: we choose, we invoke
  • Overall Meaning: This mantra expresses a prayer for strength, wealth, and companionship. It emphasizes the desire to achieve universal riches and to foster friendship and unity among individuals.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • Strength and Power: In a corporate setting, a team may invoke this mantra to inspire strength and collaboration among its members, aiming to achieve collective goals.
    • Wealth and Prosperity: Individuals may reflect on this mantra when seeking financial success, reminding themselves to work together with others to create opportunities for wealth.
    • Friendship and Unity: In community gatherings, this mantra can serve as a reminder of the importance of building strong relationships and supporting one another for mutual growth.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for collective strength, prosperity, and friendship. It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, such as in workplaces, communities, and personal relationships. By invoking these ideals, individuals and groups can foster an environment of collaboration and success, ultimately leading to a more harmonious and prosperous existence.
