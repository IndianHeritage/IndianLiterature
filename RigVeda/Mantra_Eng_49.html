अक्षितोतिḥ सनेद इमं वाजं इन्द्रः सहस्रिणम् |यस्मिन विश्वानि पौंस्याः ||
akṣitotiḥ saned imaṁ vājam indraḥ sahasriṇam | yasmin viśvāni pauṁsyā ||
  • Mantra: "akṣitotiḥ saned imaṁ vājam indraḥ sahasriṇam | yasmin viśvāni pauṁsyā ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • akṣitotiḥ: The one who is unyielding or indomitable.
    • saned: To be strong or powerful.
    • imaṁ: This or these.
    • vājam: Wealth or strength, often referring to resources or energy.
    • indraḥ: Indra, the king of gods, symbolizing power and authority.
    • sahasriṇam: Thousandfold or abundant.
    • yasmin: In whom or in which.
    • viśvāni: All or universal.
    • pauṁsyā: Of the beings or creatures.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes Indra, the god of rain and thunderstorms, who is often associated with strength and abundance.
    • The phrase "akṣitotiḥ saned" suggests an unyielding strength, indicating that true power is resilient and enduring.
    • "imaṁ vājam indraḥ sahasriṇam" emphasizes the abundance of resources and strength that can be harnessed through divine blessings.
    • The concluding part "yasmin viśvāni pauṁsyā" implies that in this strength, all beings find their sustenance and support.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, a company that remains resilient in the face of challenges can be likened to the "akṣitotiḥ saned." For instance, during economic downturns, businesses that adapt and innovate often emerge stronger.
    • In personal life, individuals who face adversities with strength and determination embody the essence of this mantra. For example, someone overcoming health issues through perseverance reflects the unyielding spirit described.
    • Communities that come together in times of crisis, pooling their resources and strength, exemplify the idea of "sahasriṇam," where collective effort leads to abundance and support for all members.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of resilience, strength, and the abundance that comes from divine support.
    • It teaches us that true power lies not just in individual strength but in the collective strength of all beings.
    • In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios, such as teamwork in organizations, community support during crises, and personal growth through challenges.
    • Ultimately, it encourages us to seek strength in unity and to recognize the divine blessings that empower us in our endeavors.
