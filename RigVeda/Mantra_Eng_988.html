त्वं सोम प्र चिकीतो मनिषा त्वं रजिष्ठम् अनु नेषि पन्थाम् |तव प्रणीती पितरो न इन्दो देवेषु रत्नम् अभजन्त धीराः ||
tvaṁ soma pra cikito manīṣā tvaṁ rajiṣṭham anu neṣi panthām | tava praṇītī pitaro na indo deveṣu ratnam abhajanta dhīrāḥ ||
  • tvaṁ soma pra cikito manīṣā: This phrase can be translated as "You, O Soma, are the one who is sought after with wisdom." Soma is often associated with a divine drink that grants immortality and enlightenment. In real life, this can be likened to the pursuit of knowledge and wisdom that individuals seek to enhance their lives.
  • tvaṁ rajiṣṭham anu neṣi panthām: This means "You lead the path that is the most excellent." Here, the mantra acknowledges the guidance provided by the divine in navigating life's journey. In practical terms, this can be seen in how mentors or leaders guide individuals towards success, much like a teacher guiding students through their educational paths.
  • tava praṇītī pitaro na indo: This translates to "Your direction, O Soma, is not known to the fathers." This line suggests that the divine guidance is beyond human understanding. In everyday life, this can be related to the idea that sometimes, despite our best efforts, we may not fully comprehend the path we are on, yet we trust in a higher power or the universe to lead us.
  • deveṣu ratnam abhajanta dhīrāḥ: This means "The wise ones who worship the deities attain the jewel." This emphasizes the importance of devotion and wisdom in achieving the ultimate goals in life. In a real-world context, this can be seen in how individuals who practice mindfulness, gratitude, and spirituality often find deeper meaning and fulfillment in their lives.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking wisdom, guidance, and the importance of devotion in life. It signifies that while we may not always understand the path we are on, trusting in divine guidance and being wise in our pursuits can lead us to profound rewards. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various aspects such as education, career choices, and personal growth, where individuals seek knowledge, mentorship, and spiritual fulfillment to navigate their journeys successfully.
