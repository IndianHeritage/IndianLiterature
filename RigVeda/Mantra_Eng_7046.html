यस्य त्वं इन्द्र स्तोमेषु चाकनः वाजे वाजीं शतक्रतो |तं त्वा वयं सुदुघां इव गोदुहो जुहूमसि श्रवस्यवः ||
yasya tvam indra stomeṣu cākano vāje vājiñ chatakrato | taṁ tvā vayaṁ sudughām iva goduho juhūmasi śravasyavaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yasya: of whom
    • tvam: you
    • indra: O Indra (the king of gods)
    • stomeṣu: in the hymns
    • cākano: who is invoked
    • vāje: in the wealth
    • vājiñ: in the powerful
    • chatakrato: O wielder of a hundred powers
    • taṁ: him
    • tvā: we
    • vayaṁ: we
    • sudughām: with great effort
    • iva: like
    • goduho: a cow
    • juhūmasi: we offer
    • śravasyavaḥ: to the one who is worthy of praise
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Indra, the chief deity in Vedic tradition, who represents strength, power, and the forces of nature.
    • The invocation highlights the importance of hymns (stomas) in connecting with divine energies and seeking blessings.
    • The phrase "with great effort like a cow" symbolizes the dedication and hard work required to achieve one's goals, much like a cow that provides sustenance through its labor.
    • It emphasizes the need for collective effort ("we") in seeking divine assistance and blessings.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, teams often come together to achieve a common goal, invoking the spirit of collaboration and hard work, akin to the collective effort mentioned in the mantra.
    • In personal life, individuals may face challenges that require perseverance and dedication, similar to the effort of a cow providing milk, reminding us that success often comes from sustained effort.
    • During festivals or rituals, communities gather to chant hymns and prayers, seeking blessings from deities like Indra for prosperity and strength, reflecting the essence of this mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of invoking divine powers through collective effort and dedication.
    • It teaches us that success is not just an individual endeavor but often requires the support and collaboration of others.
    • In real life, whether in personal or professional spheres, the principles of hard work, teamwork, and seeking divine guidance can lead to greater achievements and fulfillment.
