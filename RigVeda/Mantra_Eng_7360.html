वाचम अष्टापदीं अहं नवस्रक्तिम ऋतस्पृशम् |इन्द्रात् परि तन्वम् ममे ||
vācam aṣṭāpadīm ahaṁ navasraktim ṛtaspṛśam | indrāt pari tanvam mame ||
  • vācam: This word translates to "speech" or "voice." In the context of the mantra, it signifies the power of communication and expression.
  • aṣṭāpadīm: This term means "eight-footed." It symbolizes stability and strength, often associated with the eight directions or the completeness of existence.
  • ahaṁ: This means "I" or "myself," indicating a personal connection and the speaker's identity.
  • navasraktim: This translates to "new energy" or "fresh vigor." It represents rejuvenation and the potential for new beginnings.
  • ṛtaspṛśam: This term means "touched by truth" or "connected to righteousness." It emphasizes the importance of truth and moral integrity in one's actions.
  • indrāt: This word refers to "Indra," the king of the gods in Vedic tradition, symbolizing power, strength, and leadership.
  • pari: This means "around" or "surrounding," indicating a sense of encompassing or protection.
  • tanvam: This translates to "body" or "form," referring to the physical aspect of existence.
  • mame: This means "my" or "of me," reinforcing the personal connection to the preceding elements.

The mantra can be interpreted as a plea for the speaker's voice to be empowered and stable, infused with new energy and connected to truth, while seeking the protection and strength of Indra. It reflects a desire for personal growth and integrity in communication, emphasizing the importance of being grounded and truthful in one's expressions.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios:

  • Public Speaking: When preparing for a speech or presentation, one can invoke the essence of this mantra to seek clarity, confidence, and the ability to communicate effectively.
  • Personal Development: The idea of rejuvenation and new energy can inspire individuals to embrace change and pursue new opportunities in their lives.
  • Conflict Resolution: In situations of disagreement, the emphasis on truth and integrity can guide individuals to communicate honestly and seek resolutions that honor all parties involved.

In conclusion, this mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the significance of clear and truthful communication, the strength found in stability, and the potential for personal growth. By embodying these principles, individuals can navigate their lives with greater purpose and integrity, fostering positive relationships and outcomes.
