जोषद यद ईम असुर्या सचध्यै विषितस्तुकā रोडसी नृमाणाः |आ सूर्येव विधतो रथं गात त्वेषप्रतिका नभसो नेत्याः ||
joṣad yad īm asuryā sacadhyai viṣitastukā rodasī nṛmaṇāḥ | ā sūryeva vidhato rathaṁ gāt tveṣapratīkā nabhaso netyā ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • joṣad: to be nourished or sustained
    • yad: that which
    • īm: we desire or seek
    • asuryā: of the sun or solar energy
    • sacadhyai: with truth or righteousness
    • viṣitastukā: those who are established or grounded
    • rodasī: the two worlds or realms
    • nṛmaṇāḥ: human beings or mortals
    • ā: come or arrive
    • sūryeva: like the sun
    • vidhato: of the creator or the one who arranges
    • rathaṁ: the chariot
    • gāt: to go or move
    • tveṣapratīkā: in accordance with your wishes
    • nabhaso: of the sky or heavens
    • netyā: leading or guiding
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the sustenance and nourishment that comes from the sun, which is a vital source of energy for all living beings.
    • It emphasizes the importance of seeking truth and righteousness in our lives, suggesting that these qualities are essential for a fulfilling existence.
    • The reference to "the two worlds" can be interpreted as the physical and spiritual realms, indicating that human beings navigate both in their lives.
    • The imagery of the sun and the chariot symbolizes guidance and movement towards a higher purpose or goal.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In agriculture, the sun is crucial for the growth of crops, representing how essential it is for sustenance and life.
    • In personal development, individuals often seek truth and righteousness in their actions, which can lead to a more meaningful and ethical life.
    • The concept of navigating between the physical and spiritual realms can be seen in practices like yoga and meditation, where individuals strive for balance and harmony.
    • The metaphor of a chariot can be likened to one's life journey, where individuals must steer their lives towards their goals with intention and purpose.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of the sun as a source of life and energy, while also emphasizing the importance of truth and righteousness in our lives.
    • It serves as a reminder that we are all on a journey, navigating through different realms of existence, and that our choices and intentions shape our path.
    • In real life, this mantra can inspire individuals to seek nourishment not just physically, but also spiritually and ethically, guiding them towards a more fulfilling and purposeful life.
