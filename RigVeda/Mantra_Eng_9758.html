मन्त्र: परि त्वाग्ने पुरं वयं विप्रं सहस्यम् धीमहि |धृषद्वर्णं दिवे-दिवे हन्तारं भङ्गुरावताम् ||
pari tvāgne puraṁ vayaṁ vipraṁ sahasya dhīmahi | dhṛṣadvarṇaṁ dive-dive hantāram bhaṅgurāvatām ||
  • Mantra: "pari tvāgne puraṁ vayaṁ vipraṁ sahasya dhīmahi | dhṛṣadvarṇaṁ dive-dive hantāram bhaṅgurāvatām ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • pari: around, in all directions
    • tvāgne: O Agni (the fire god)
    • puraṁ: the city, the abode
    • vayaṁ: we
    • vipraṁ: the wise, the learned
    • sahasya: with strength, with power
    • dhīmahi: we meditate, we contemplate
    • dhṛṣadvarṇaṁ: of the strong, of the powerful
    • dive-dive: day by day, every day
    • hantāram: the destroyer, the one who removes
    • bhaṅgurāvatām: of the fleeting, of the transient
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Agni, the fire god, who symbolizes energy, transformation, and purification.
    • It emphasizes the importance of seeking wisdom and strength in our lives.
    • The phrase "we meditate" indicates a collective effort to gain insight and understanding.
    • The reference to "day by day" suggests the continuous nature of this pursuit, highlighting that wisdom and strength are not one-time achievements but ongoing processes.
    • The mention of "the destroyer of the fleeting" reminds us of the impermanence of life and the need to focus on what truly matters.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, teams often gather to brainstorm and strategize, invoking the spirit of Agni to ignite creativity and collaboration.
    • In personal development, individuals may meditate daily to cultivate inner strength and wisdom, reflecting the mantra's call to "contemplate with power."
    • In environmental discussions, the transient nature of resources can lead to a focus on sustainable practices, aligning with the idea of recognizing the fleeting aspects of life.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of seeking wisdom and strength in our daily lives.
    • It encourages us to meditate on our goals and aspirations, recognizing the impermanence of life and the need to act with purpose.
    • By invoking Agni, we align ourselves with the transformative power of fire, which can purify and energize our endeavors.
    • Ultimately, this mantra is applicable in various aspects of life, from personal growth to community building, emphasizing the continuous journey towards enlightenment and strength.
