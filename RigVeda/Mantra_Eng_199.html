सं वो मदासो अग्मतेन्द्रेण च मरुत्वता |आदित्येभिः च राजभिः ||
saṁ vo madāso agmatendreṇa ca marutvatā | ādityebhiś ca rājabhiḥ ||
  • saṁ vo madāso: This phrase can be translated as "may we be filled with joy." The term "madāsa" refers to a state of bliss or ecstasy. In real life, this can be related to moments of happiness and fulfillment, such as celebrating achievements or enjoying time with loved ones.
  • agmatendreṇa: This word signifies "with the strength of the divine." It suggests that the joy we seek is not just personal but is also connected to a higher power or divine influence. For example, one might feel empowered and joyful when they feel a sense of purpose or connection to something greater, such as community service or spiritual practices.
  • ca marutvatā: This translates to "and with the energy of the winds." The Maruts are storm deities associated with strength and vitality. This can be likened to the invigorating feeling one gets from nature, such as a refreshing breeze during a hike or the energy felt during a lively gathering.
  • ādityebhiś ca rājabhiḥ: This phrase means "and with the radiance of the sun and the kings." The Adityas are solar deities, symbolizing light, power, and authority. In practical terms, this can represent the guidance and leadership we receive from influential figures in our lives, such as mentors or leaders who inspire us to shine in our own capacities.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a holistic approach to joy and fulfillment, emphasizing the importance of divine strength, natural energy, and the influence of leadership. In real life, it encourages individuals to seek joy not only through personal achievements but also by connecting with the divine, embracing the vitality of nature, and drawing inspiration from leaders. This mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as during celebrations, in moments of reflection, or when seeking motivation in challenging times. It serves as a reminder that true joy is multifaceted and can be cultivated through various sources of strength and inspiration.
