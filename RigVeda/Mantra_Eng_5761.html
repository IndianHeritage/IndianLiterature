प्र मे पन्था देवयानां अदृश्रन्न अमर्धन्तो वसुभिर इष्कृतासः | अभूद उ केतुर्व उषसः पुरस्तात प्रतिच्य आगाद अधि हार्म्येभ्यः ||
pra me panthā devayānā adṛśrann amardhanto vasubhir iṣkṛtāsaḥ | abhūd u ketur uṣasaḥ purastāt pratīcy āgād adhi harmyebhyaḥ ||
  • Translation: "May the path of the divine beings, which is unseen and unbroken, be revealed to me. It has been made by the gods, and it has come forth from the dawn, leading me towards the heights of the heavens."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • pra me: "for me" or "to me"
    • panthā: "path" or "way"
    • devayānā: "of the divine beings" or "gods"
    • adṛśrann: "unseen" or "invisible"
    • amardhanto: "unbroken" or "undivided"
    • vasubhir: "by the gods" or "with the divine"
    • iṣkṛtāsaḥ: "made" or "created"
    • abhūd: "has come" or "has arisen"
    • u ketur: "from the heights" or "from the top"
    • uṣasaḥ: "of the dawn" or "of the morning"
    • purastāt: "in front" or "before"
    • pratīcy: "towards the west" or "to the side"
    • āgād: "has gone" or "has arrived"
    • adhi: "above" or "over"
    • harmyebhyaḥ: "from the heights" or "from the abodes"
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • This mantra can be seen as a call for guidance in life. For instance, when someone is at a crossroads in their career, they may seek clarity and direction, much like the invocation of the divine path.
    • In moments of uncertainty, such as during a personal crisis, individuals often look for signs or guidance from a higher power, paralleling the plea for the divine path to be revealed.
    • In a broader sense, communities may come together in prayer or meditation, seeking a collective path forward, especially in challenging times, reflecting the essence of this mantra.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its invocation for divine guidance and clarity in one's life journey. It emphasizes the importance of seeking a path that is not only visible but also aligned with higher principles and values. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied during times of decision-making, personal growth, or community challenges, reminding individuals and groups to look beyond the immediate and seek a higher purpose or direction. By embracing this mantra, one can cultivate a sense of trust in the unseen forces that guide us, fostering resilience and hope in the face of life's uncertainties.
