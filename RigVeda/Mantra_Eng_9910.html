यत्रौषधिः समग्मता राजानः समिताव इव | विप्रः स उच्यते भीषग् रक्षोहमिवाचतनः ||
yatrauṣadhīḥ samagmata rājānaḥ samitāv iva | vipraḥ sa ucyate bhiṣag rakṣohāmīvacātanaḥ ||
  • yatrauṣadhīḥ: This term refers to the place where herbs or medicinal plants are found. In a broader sense, it symbolizes the source of healing and nourishment.
  • samagmata: This word means 'coming together' or 'united'. It emphasizes the importance of unity and collaboration among individuals or groups.
  • rājānaḥ: This translates to 'kings' or 'rulers'. It signifies authority, leadership, and the responsibility that comes with power.
  • samitāv iva: This phrase means 'like the gathered fuel'. It suggests the idea of collective strength and the potential that arises when individuals unite for a common purpose.
  • vipraḥ: This term refers to a learned or wise person, often a priest or sage. It highlights the value of knowledge and wisdom in guiding actions and decisions.
  • sa ucyate: This means 'he is called' or 'he is referred to'. It indicates recognition and acknowledgment of someone's qualities or contributions.
  • bhiṣag: This word means 'physician' or 'healer'. It represents the role of those who provide care and healing in society.
  • rakṣohāmīvacātanaḥ: This phrase can be interpreted as 'the protector against evil'. It signifies the importance of safeguarding oneself and others from negative influences.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of unity, leadership, and wisdom in the pursuit of healing and protection. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios such as community health initiatives, where leaders and knowledgeable individuals come together to promote well-being. For instance, during a public health crisis, collaboration among healthcare professionals, government officials, and community leaders is crucial to effectively address the situation. The mantra serves as a reminder that collective efforts, guided by wisdom and a sense of responsibility, can lead to healing and protection against adversities in society.
