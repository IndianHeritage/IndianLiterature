अवā नो वाजयुं रथं सुखरं ते किम इत् परि |अस्मान् सु जिग्युषस कृधि ||
avā no vājayuṁ rathaṁ sukaraṁ te kim it pari | asmān su jigyuṣas kṛdhi ||
  • Mantra: "avā no vājayuṁ rathaṁ sukaraṁ te kim it pari | asmān su jigyuṣas kṛdhi ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • avā: May we be protected
    • no: us
    • vājayuṁ: of strength or vigor
    • rathaṁ: chariot
    • sukaraṁ: easy or comfortable
    • te: your
    • kim: what
    • it: is this
    • pari: around or about
    • asmān: us
    • su: well or good
    • jigyuṣas: may we conquer
    • kṛdhi: do or make
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer for protection and strength. It expresses a desire for a comfortable journey (symbolized by the chariot) and seeks guidance on how to achieve success.
    • The chariot represents the journey of life, where we seek ease and comfort in our endeavors.
    • The phrase "may we conquer" emphasizes the importance of overcoming challenges and achieving our goals with the help of divine guidance.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, this mantra can be applied when embarking on a new project. One might seek the support of colleagues and mentors to ensure a smooth and successful execution.
    • In personal life, it can be relevant when facing challenges such as health issues or family problems, where one seeks strength and guidance to navigate through difficulties.
    • Students preparing for exams can recite this mantra to invoke focus and clarity, hoping for a successful outcome in their academic pursuits.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its universal appeal for strength, protection, and guidance in life's journey.
    • It serves as a reminder that while we strive for success, we should also seek support from others and the divine.
    • In various aspects of life, whether personal, professional, or academic, this mantra encourages individuals to approach challenges with confidence and a sense of community.
