आशुं दूतं विवस्वतो विश्वा यश चर्षणीर अभि |आ जभ्रुः केतुम आयवो भृगवाणं विशे-विशे ||
āśuṁ dūtaṁ vivasvato viśvā yaś carṣaṇīr abhi | ā jabhruḥ ketum āyavo bhṛgavāṇaṁ viśe-viśe ||
  • Word Breakdown:
    • āśuṁ: Swift or quick.
    • dūtaṁ: Messenger or envoy.
    • vivasvato: Of the sun or solar.
    • viśvā: All or universal.
    • yaś: Fame or glory.
    • carṣaṇīr: With the support or foundation.
    • abhi: Towards or in the direction of.
    • ā jabhruḥ: They have come forth or emerged.
    • ketum: A banner or sign.
    • āyavo: Those who are strong or powerful.
    • bhṛgavāṇaṁ: Of the Bhrigus, a family of sages.
    • viśe-viśe: In various groups or communities.
  • Meaning and Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of a swift messenger of the sun, representing the universal fame and glory that emerges with strength and support.
    • The mention of "dūtaṁ" (messenger) signifies the importance of communication and the role of messengers in conveying vital information.
    • The phrase "viśvā yaś" emphasizes the idea of universal recognition and the interconnectedness of all beings.
    • The reference to "bhṛgavāṇaṁ" connects to the lineage of sages, suggesting that wisdom and guidance are essential in navigating life's challenges.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern communication, the role of a messenger can be likened to the importance of technology in connecting people across the globe, such as through social media or instant messaging.
    • The idea of universal fame can be seen in the way certain individuals or movements gain global recognition, impacting society positively, like environmental activists advocating for climate change.
    • The strength and support mentioned can be related to teamwork in professional settings, where collaboration leads to greater achievements.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of swift communication, the power of collective strength, and the wisdom of sages in guiding humanity.
    • In real life, it serves as a reminder of the importance of effective communication, collaboration, and the pursuit of knowledge.
    • By embodying these principles, individuals and communities can navigate challenges and achieve greater success, fostering a sense of unity and purpose.
