उत ते शतान् मघवन् उच् च भूयसा उत सहस्राद् रिरिचे कृष्ष्टिषु श्रवः |अमात्रं त्वा धिषणा तित्विषे मह्य अधा वृत्राणि जिघ्नसे पुरंदर ||
ut te śatān maghavann uc ca bhūyasa ut sahasrād ririce kṛṣṭiṣu śravaḥ | amātraṁ tvā dhiṣaṇā titviṣe mahy adhā vṛtrāṇi jighnase puraṁdara ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ut: O
    • te: your
    • śatān: of hundreds
    • maghavann: O powerful one
    • uc ca: and also
    • bhūyasa: more abundant
    • ut: O
    • sahasrād: from thousands
    • ririce: may you attain
    • kṛṣṭiṣu: in the fields
    • śravaḥ: of fame
    • amātraṁ: without measure
    • tvā: you
    • dhiṣaṇā: by the power
    • titviṣe: for your greatness
    • mahy: to me
    • adhā: grant
    • vṛtrāṇi: the obstacles
    • jighnase: to be destroyed
    • puraṁdara: O destroyer of cities
  • Overall Meaning: This mantra is a prayer to the deity Indra, who is often referred to as "Puraṁdara," the destroyer of cities and obstacles. The devotee seeks the blessings of Indra for abundance, fame, and the removal of challenges. The mantra emphasizes the desire for both material and spiritual prosperity, invoking Indra's power to overcome difficulties and achieve greatness.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, an entrepreneur may recite this mantra to seek success and recognition in their field, hoping to overcome competition and obstacles.
    • Students preparing for exams might use this mantra to invoke focus and clarity, aiming for academic excellence and fame in their educational pursuits.
    • Individuals facing personal challenges may chant this mantra to seek strength and support in overcoming life's hurdles, fostering resilience and determination.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine assistance in achieving success and overcoming obstacles. It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal struggles to professional endeavors. By invoking the power of Indra, individuals can find motivation and strength to pursue their goals, reminding them that with faith and effort, they can conquer challenges and attain greatness.
