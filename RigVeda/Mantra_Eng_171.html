युवाकु हि शचीनां युवाकु सुमतीनाम् |भूयाम वाजदाव्नाम् ||
yuvāku hi śacīnāṁ yuvāku sumatīnām | bhūyāma vājadāvnām ||
  • yuvāku hi śacīnāṁ: This phrase can be translated to "May we be youthful in wisdom." The term "yuvāku" refers to youthfulness or vigor, while "śacīnāṁ" relates to wisdom or intelligence. This suggests a desire for a fresh perspective and the ability to think clearly and wisely.
  • yuvāku sumatīnām: Here, "sumatīnām" means "of those with good thoughts." This part emphasizes the importance of having positive and constructive thoughts. It implies that youthfulness in wisdom should be accompanied by good intentions and thoughts.
  • bhūyāma vājadāvnām: This translates to "May we become strong like the thunder." The word "bhūyāma" indicates a wish to grow or become, while "vājadāvnām" refers to the strength and power associated with thunder. This metaphor suggests a desire for strength and resilience in our endeavors.

  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, a young leader may embody the qualities of "yuvāku hi śacīnāṁ" by approaching challenges with innovative ideas and a fresh perspective, while also valuing the wisdom of experienced colleagues.
    • In personal relationships, embodying "yuvāku sumatīnām" can mean fostering positive communication and understanding, ensuring that interactions are rooted in kindness and constructive thoughts.
    • The phrase "bhūyāma vājadāvnām" can be seen in athletes who train rigorously to build their strength and resilience, aiming to perform at their peak, much like the powerful force of thunder.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a profound aspiration for wisdom, positivity, and strength. In real life, it serves as a reminder to cultivate a youthful mindset that embraces learning and growth while maintaining good intentions. Whether in personal development, professional growth, or interpersonal relationships, the principles of this mantra encourage individuals to strive for wisdom, positivity, and resilience, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling and impactful life.
