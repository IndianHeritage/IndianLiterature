मित्रस्य चरषणीधृतो 'वो देवस्य सानसि |द्यम्नं चित्रश्रवस्तमम् ||
mitrasya carṣaṇīdhṛto 'vo devasya sānasi | dyumnaṁ citraśravastamam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • mitrasya: of Mitra, the deity associated with friendship and harmony.
    • carṣaṇīdhṛto: holding or maintaining the sacredness or the essence.
    • 'vo: for you, addressing the divine or the audience.
    • devasya: of the divine, referring to the supreme being or god.
    • sānasi: in the assembly or gathering, indicating a communal aspect.
    • dyumnaṁ: glory or fame, representing the positive attributes of the divine.
    • citraśravastamam: most renowned or having diverse and colorful fame.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the essence of Mitra, emphasizing the importance of friendship and unity among people.
    • It highlights the significance of maintaining sacredness in relationships and gatherings, suggesting that divine presence is felt in communal harmony.
    • The mention of "dyumnaṁ" and "citraśravastamam" signifies the aspiration for glory and recognition that comes from living in accordance with divine principles.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace, fostering a culture of collaboration and support among colleagues can lead to greater productivity and job satisfaction, reflecting the essence of Mitra.
    • Community gatherings, such as festivals or local events, embody the spirit of "sānasi," where people come together to celebrate and strengthen their bonds.
    • Acts of kindness and support in personal relationships can enhance one's reputation and create a legacy of goodwill, aligning with the concepts of "dyumnaṁ" and "citraśravastamam."
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of friendship, unity, and the divine presence in our lives.
    • It encourages individuals to cultivate harmonious relationships and to seek glory through positive actions and communal support.
    • In today's world, where divisions often arise, the teachings of this mantra are applicable in promoting peace, understanding, and collaboration in various aspects of life.
