वयं इन्द्र त्वे सचा वयं त्वाभि नोनुमः |अस्मां-अस्मां इद उद अव ||
vayam indra tve sacā vayaṁ tvābhi nonumaḥ | asmām̐-asmām̐ id ud ava ||
  • Mantra: "vayam indra tve sacā vayaṁ tvābhi nonumaḥ | asmām̐-asmām̐ id ud ava ||"
  • Translation: "We, O Indra, with our strength, do not forsake you; may we be united with you in our endeavors."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • vayam: We
    • indra: O Indra (the king of gods, representing strength and power)
    • tve: You
    • sacā: With strength
    • tvābhi: With you
    • nonumaḥ: Do not forsake
    • asmām̐-asmām̐: In our endeavors
    • id: This
    • ud: Upward
    • ava: To attain
  • Context and Significance:
    • This mantra is a call for unity and strength, invoking the divine presence of Indra, who symbolizes power and leadership.
    • It emphasizes the importance of collaboration and support in achieving goals, suggesting that strength is amplified when shared.
    • In real life, this can be seen in team sports, where players rely on each other to succeed, or in workplaces where collaboration leads to innovation and success.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • Team Sports: In a football team, each player must work together, supporting one another to win the game. The mantra reflects the idea that individual strengths contribute to a collective goal.
    • Workplace Collaboration: In a project team, members bring their unique skills to the table. The mantra serves as a reminder that success is often a result of teamwork and mutual support.
    • Community Initiatives: In community service projects, individuals come together to address social issues. The mantra highlights the power of collective action in making a difference.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra teaches us the value of unity and collaboration in our endeavors.
    • It encourages us to seek strength in our connections with others, reminding us that we are stronger together.
    • In various aspects of life, from personal relationships to professional settings, the principles embodied in this mantra can guide us toward achieving our goals and overcoming challenges.
