शुभ्रं नु ते शुष्मं वर्धयन्तः शुभ्रं वज्रम बाह्वोः दधानाः |शुभ्रस्त्वं इन्द्र वावृधानो अस्मे दासीर विषः सूर्येण सह्याः ||
śubhraṁ nu te śuṣmaṁ vardhayantaḥ śubhraṁ vajram bāhvor dadhānāḥ | śubhras tvam indra vāvṛdhāno asme dāsīr viśaḥ sūryeṇa sahyāḥ ||
  • śubhraṁ nu te śuṣmaṁ vardhayantaḥ: This phrase can be translated as "May your brilliance, O Indra, be enhanced." The term "śubhraṁ" signifies purity and brightness, while "vardhayantaḥ" implies growth or enhancement. In real life, this can be related to the pursuit of knowledge and wisdom, where individuals strive to enhance their understanding and capabilities.
  • śubhraṁ vajram bāhvor dadhānāḥ: Here, "vajram" refers to the thunderbolt or a powerful weapon, and "bāhvor" means "of the arms." This line suggests that Indra wields a powerful force. In a contemporary context, this can symbolize the strength and determination one needs to overcome challenges, akin to facing life's adversities with resilience.
  • śubhras tvam indra vāvṛdhāno: This translates to "You, Indra, who are pure, are increasing." It emphasizes the idea of purity and growth. In everyday life, this can be seen in the context of personal development, where individuals strive to cultivate their character and virtues, leading to a more fulfilling life.
  • asme dāsīr viśaḥ sūryeṇa sahyāḥ: This line can be interpreted as "May we, with the help of the sun, be strong and protected." The "sūryeṇa" refers to the sun, which is often associated with energy, vitality, and life. This can be related to the importance of community and support systems in our lives, where collective strength helps individuals thrive.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the themes of purity, strength, and growth. It signifies the importance of enhancing one's brilliance and character while relying on the support of the community and natural forces, like the sun, for strength and protection. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire individuals to pursue personal development, face challenges with resilience, and value the support of their community, ultimately leading to a more harmonious and fulfilling existence.
