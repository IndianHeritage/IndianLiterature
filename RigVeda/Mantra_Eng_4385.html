ऋधद यस् ते सुदानवे धियā मर्तः शशमते |ऊती ष बृहतो दिवो द्विषो अंहो न तरति ||
ṛdhad yas te sudānave dhiyā martaḥ śaśamate | ūtī ṣa bṛhato divo dviṣo aṁho na tarati ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ṛdhad: Strengthened or empowered
    • yas: Who
    • te: Your
    • Sudānave: To the one who is well-versed or knowledgeable
    • dhiyā: With intellect or understanding
    • martaḥ: Mortal or human
    • śaśamate: May prosper or thrive
    • ūtī: Abundance or wealth
    • ṣa: Six
    • bṛhato: Of the great or vast
    • divo: Of the heavens
    • dviṣo: Of the enemies
    • aṁho: Of the light or brilliance
    • na: Not
    • tarati: Crosses or overcomes
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of wisdom and understanding in achieving prosperity and overcoming challenges.
    • The phrase "strengthened by your intellect" suggests that knowledge and insight are crucial for success in life.
    • The reference to "abundance of the great" indicates that true wealth comes from a higher understanding and connection to the universe.
    • The mention of "not crossing the enemies" implies that with wisdom, one can navigate through life's adversities without falling prey to negativity or harmful influences.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, a leader who makes decisions based on thorough research and understanding of market dynamics is more likely to succeed than one who acts impulsively.
    • In personal relationships, individuals who communicate openly and seek to understand each other's perspectives often build stronger, more resilient connections.
    • In education, students who engage deeply with their subjects and seek to understand the underlying principles tend to perform better than those who memorize facts without comprehension.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the power of knowledge and understanding in achieving success and overcoming obstacles.
    • It highlights the significance of intellectual growth and the pursuit of wisdom in various aspects of life, including personal, professional, and spiritual domains.
    • By embodying the principles of this mantra, individuals can navigate their lives with greater clarity, purpose, and resilience, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling existence.
