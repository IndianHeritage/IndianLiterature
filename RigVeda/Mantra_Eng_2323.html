यास ते राके सुमतयः सुपेशसो याभिर ददासि दाशुषे वसूनि |ताभिर नो अद्य सुमना उपागहि सहस्रपोषं सुभगे रराणा ||
yās te rāke sumatayaḥ supeśaso yābhir dadāsi dāśuṣe vasūni | tābhir no adya sumanā upāgahi sahasrapoṣaṁ subhage rarāṇā ||
  • yās te rāke sumatayaḥ supeśaso: This phrase can be translated to "Those who are endowed with good thoughts and auspicious qualities." Here, "rāke" refers to protection or guardianship, while "sumatayaḥ" signifies good intentions or wisdom. In real life, this can be related to individuals who possess a positive mindset and are protective of their loved ones, such as parents or mentors who guide and support their children or mentees.
  • yābhir dadāsi dāśuṣe vasūni: This translates to "With whom you bestow wealth and prosperity." The term "dāśuṣe" refers to the one who is deserving or in need, and "vasūni" signifies wealth or resources. This can be exemplified by charitable acts, where individuals or organizations provide support to those in need, such as donating to food banks or funding education for underprivileged children.
  • tābhir no adya sumanā upāgahi: This means "With those, may we today attain good thoughts." The word "upāgahi" implies the act of reaching or achieving. In a practical sense, this can be seen in collaborative efforts where groups come together to brainstorm and generate innovative ideas, such as in team meetings or community projects aimed at improving local conditions.
  • sahasrapoṣaṁ subhage rarāṇā: This translates to "May we, with abundant nourishment, thrive in auspiciousness." "Sahasrapoṣaṁ" refers to abundant sustenance, while "subhage" indicates good fortune or auspiciousness. This can be related to sustainable practices in agriculture or community gardening, where individuals work together to cultivate food, ensuring that everyone has access to nutritious meals and fostering a sense of community.

  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of good thoughts, generosity, collaboration, and abundance. It signifies that by fostering positive intentions and supporting one another, individuals can create a prosperous and harmonious community. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios, such as community service, teamwork in professional settings, and family support systems. The essence of the mantra encourages us to be mindful of our actions and their impact on others, promoting a cycle of giving and receiving that leads to collective well-being.
