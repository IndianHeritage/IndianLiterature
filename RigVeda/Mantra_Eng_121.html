स नः स्तवाना आ भर गायत्रेण नवीयसा |रयिं वीरवतिम् इषम् ||
sa naḥ stavāna ā bhara gāyatreṇa navīyasā | rayiṁ vīravatīm iṣam ||
  • sa naḥ: This phrase translates to "may he" or "may that" and indicates a prayer or invocation directed towards a divine entity.
  • stavāna: This word means "praise" or "glory." It signifies the act of honoring or venerating a higher power.
  • ā bhara: This translates to "bring" or "bestow." It is a request for the divine to provide something beneficial.
  • gāyatreṇa: This refers to the "Gayatri," a sacred mantra that embodies the essence of the Vedas. It symbolizes enlightenment and spiritual awakening.
  • navīyasā: This means "new" or "fresh." It suggests a desire for renewal or rejuvenation in life.
  • rayiṁ: This word translates to "wealth" or "prosperity." It encompasses not just material wealth but also spiritual and emotional abundance.
  • vīravatīm: This means "brave" or "heroic." It signifies strength, valor, and the qualities of a true warrior.
  • iṣam: This translates to "power" or "might." It indicates the ability to achieve goals and overcome challenges.

The mantra as a whole can be interpreted as a prayer for the divine to bestow upon us the strength, wealth, and valor necessary to lead a prosperous and fulfilling life. It emphasizes the importance of seeking divine guidance and support in our endeavors.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios:

  • Personal Growth: Individuals can recite this mantra during meditation or prayer to seek inner strength and clarity in their life choices.
  • Professional Success: In a work environment, invoking this mantra can serve as a reminder to pursue goals with courage and integrity, fostering a sense of teamwork and collaboration.
  • Community Well-being: Communities can come together to chant this mantra during gatherings or festivals, promoting unity and collective strength in overcoming challenges.

In conclusion, this RigVeda mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the importance of divine support in our lives. It encourages us to seek renewal, strength, and prosperity, not just for ourselves but for our communities as well. By embodying the qualities of bravery and valor, we can navigate life's challenges with confidence and purpose.
