वीतिहोत्रं त्वा कवेद्युमन्तं सम इधीमहि |अग्नि बृहन्तमध्वरे ||
vītihotraṁ tvā kave dyumantaṁ sam idhīmahi | agne bṛhantam adhvare ||
  • Mantra: "vītihotraṁ tvā kave dyumantaṁ sam idhīmahi | agne bṛhantam adhvare ||"
  • Translation: "We meditate upon you, O Agni, the divine priest, the radiant one, who is the great performer of the sacrifice."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • vītihotraṁ: Refers to Agni as the priest or the one who performs the rituals.
    • tvā: Means "you," addressing Agni directly.
    • kave: Translates to "the wise" or "the poet," indicating Agni's wisdom.
    • dyumantaṁ: Means "radiant" or "brilliant," describing Agni's luminous nature.
    • sam idhīmahi: Means "we meditate upon" or "we invoke," expressing the act of worship.
    • agne: Directly addresses Agni, the fire god.
    • bṛhantam: Means "the great" or "the vast," emphasizing Agni's significance.
    • adhvare: Refers to "sacrifice" or "ritual," highlighting the context of worship.
  • Significance:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of Agni as a central figure in Vedic rituals, symbolizing the connection between the divine and the earthly realms.
    • Agni is seen as a mediator who carries offerings to the gods, making him essential in the performance of sacrifices.
    • The invocation of Agni reflects the human desire for divine guidance, wisdom, and the fulfillment of spiritual needs.
  • Real-Life Application:
    • In modern life, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as during significant life events like weddings, housewarming ceremonies, or other rituals where blessings are sought.
    • It serves as a reminder of the importance of intention and mindfulness in our actions, encouraging individuals to approach their tasks with dedication and reverence.
    • The qualities of Agni—wisdom, radiance, and the ability to connect different realms—can inspire individuals to seek knowledge and enlightenment in their personal and professional lives.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra encapsulates the essence of devotion and the pursuit of higher understanding through the worship of Agni.
    • It highlights the significance of rituals in fostering a connection with the divine and the importance of approaching life with a sense of purpose and reverence.
    • By invoking Agni, individuals can cultivate a deeper awareness of their actions and their impact on the world around them.
