स तेजीयसā मनसā त्वोता उता शिक्ष स्वपत्यम् शिक्षोः |अग्ने रायो नृतमस्य प्रभूतौ भूयाम ते सुश्रुतयश्च वस्वः ||
sa tejīyasā manasā tvota uta śikṣa svapatyasya śikṣoḥ | agne rāyo nṛtamasya prabhūtau bhūyāma te suṣṭutayaś ca vasvaḥ ||
  • sa tejīyasā manasā: This phrase can be translated as "with the brilliance of the mind." It emphasizes the importance of mental clarity and intelligence in achieving one's goals. In real life, this can be seen in situations where individuals use their intellect to solve complex problems, such as scientists conducting research or students studying for exams.
  • tvota uta śikṣa svapatyasya śikṣoḥ: This part means "may you be inspired and learn from the teachings." It highlights the value of learning and gaining wisdom from various sources. For example, in a corporate setting, employees often attend workshops and training sessions to enhance their skills and knowledge, which aligns with the idea of continuous learning.
  • agne rāyo nṛtamasya prabhūtau: Here, "Agni" refers to the divine fire or energy, symbolizing transformation and purification. "Rāyo" means wealth or prosperity, and "nṛtamasya prabhūtau" suggests the abundance of human endeavors. This can be related to entrepreneurs who harness their passion (Agni) to create successful businesses, leading to prosperity for themselves and their communities.
  • bhūyāma te suṣṭutayaś ca vasvaḥ: This translates to "may we attain your praise and glory." It signifies the aspiration for recognition and success in one's endeavors. In everyday life, this can be seen in individuals striving for excellence in their careers, seeking acknowledgment for their hard work and contributions, such as receiving awards or promotions.

  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of mental brilliance, continuous learning, the transformative power of passion, and the pursuit of recognition. In real life, these elements are applicable in various scenarios, such as education, professional development, and entrepreneurship. By embodying these principles, individuals can achieve personal and collective success, leading to a more prosperous and enlightened society.
