तं त्वा यज्ञेभिर इमहे तं गीरभिर गिरवणस्तम | इन्द्र यथा चिद आविथ वाजेषु पुरुमाय्यम ||
taṁ tvā yajñebhir īmahe taṁ gīrbhir girvaṇastama | indra yathā cid āvitha vājeṣu purumāyyam ||
  • taṁ tvā yajñebhir īmahe: This phrase translates to "We invoke you with our sacrifices." Here, "yajñebhir" refers to the offerings or sacrifices made in rituals. In real life, this can be likened to the way people express gratitude or seek blessings through various forms of offerings, whether in religious ceremonies or personal acts of kindness.
  • taṁ gīrbhir girvaṇastama: This means "We praise you with our hymns." "Gīrbhir" refers to the hymns or praises sung in honor of a deity. In contemporary life, this can be seen in how people celebrate achievements or express admiration for others through songs, poetry, or speeches, reinforcing the importance of recognition and appreciation.
  • indra yathā cid āvitha: This translates to "As Indra, you are invoked." Indra is a prominent deity in the Rigveda, often associated with strength and power. In modern contexts, this can represent the invocation of strength and courage in challenging situations, such as facing personal struggles or societal issues.
  • vājeṣu purumāyyam: This means "In the battles, you are the foremost." This phrase emphasizes the idea of being a leader or a guiding force in times of conflict or challenge. In real life, this can be related to leadership roles in various fields, such as politics, business, or community service, where individuals take charge to navigate through difficulties.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of invoking divine support through sacrifices and praises, emphasizing the importance of strength and leadership in overcoming challenges. Its significance lies in the reminder that in our daily lives, we can seek guidance and strength through our actions, whether they be in the form of gratitude, recognition, or leadership. This mantra encourages individuals to actively engage in their communities and personal lives, fostering a sense of responsibility and connection to the greater good.
