एविवापाग अपरे सन्तु दूद्योऽश्वा येषां दुर्युज आयुयुज्रे |इत्तथा ये प्राग उपरे सन्ति दावने पुरूणि यत्र वयुनानि भोजना ||
evaivāpāg apare santu dūḍhyo 'śvā yeṣāṁ duryuja āyuyujre | itthā ye prāg upare santi dāvane purūṇi yatra vayunāni bhojanā ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • evaivāpāg: Thus, may they be free from obstacles.
    • apare: Others.
    • santu: May they be.
    • dūḍhyo: Free from distress or difficulties.
    • 'śvā: O horses (symbolizing strength and speed).
    • yeṣāṁ: Whose.
    • duryuja: Difficult to conquer or strong.
    • āyuyujre: May they be united or connected.
    • itthā: Thus.
    • ye: Those who.
    • prāg: Previously or before.
    • upare: Above or higher.
    • santi: Are.
    • dāvane: In the fire or in the struggle.
    • purūṇi: Many or numerous.
    • yatra: Where.
    • vayunāni: The offerings or sacrifices.
    • bhojanā: Food or nourishment.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra expresses a desire for freedom from obstacles and difficulties for all beings.
    • The mention of "horses" symbolizes strength and the ability to overcome challenges, suggesting that those who are strong and united can face adversities together.
    • The reference to "fire" indicates the trials and tribulations that one may face in life, emphasizing the importance of resilience and unity in overcoming them.
    • The mantra also highlights the significance of nourishment, both physical and spiritual, as essential for growth and sustenance in life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a team setting, when individuals come together with a common goal, they can overcome challenges more effectively, much like the horses in the mantra.
    • In personal life, facing difficulties such as health issues or financial struggles can be made easier with the support of family and friends, reflecting the unity and strength mentioned.
    • In community service, when people unite to help those in need, they embody the essence of this mantra by providing nourishment and support to others.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of unity, strength, and support in overcoming life's challenges.
    • It encourages individuals to seek nourishment, both materially and spiritually, and to help others in their struggles.
    • In essence, it teaches that together, we can face adversities and emerge stronger, making it applicable in various aspects of life, from personal relationships to community efforts.
