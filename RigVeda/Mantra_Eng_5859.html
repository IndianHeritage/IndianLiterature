यावत तरस तन्भो यावद ओजो यावन नरश चक्षसा दीध्यानः |शुचिं सोमं शुचिपा पातम अस्मे इन्द्रवायू सदतम बरहिर एदं ||
yāvat taras tanvo3 yāvad ojo yāvan naraś cakṣasā dīdhyānāḥ | śuciṁ somaṁ śucipā pātam asme indravāyū sadatam barhir edam ||
  • yāvat taras tanvo3: This phrase can be translated to "as long as there is energy in the body." It emphasizes the importance of vitality and strength in life. For example, athletes often train rigorously to maintain their physical energy, which allows them to perform at their best during competitions.
  • yāvad ojo: This means "as long as there is vigor." It highlights the necessity of mental and emotional strength. In real life, individuals facing challenges, such as students preparing for exams or professionals meeting deadlines, must harness their inner vigor to succeed.
  • yāvan naraś cakṣasā dīdhyānāḥ: This translates to "as long as a person has the ability to see." It signifies awareness and perception. For instance, a person who is observant and aware of their surroundings can make better decisions, whether in personal relationships or in business.
  • śuciṁ somaṁ: This phrase refers to "pure soma," where 'soma' symbolizes nourishment and vitality. In modern terms, this can relate to the importance of a healthy diet. Consuming pure and nutritious food can enhance one's physical and mental well-being.
  • śucipā pātam: This means "the drink of purity." It suggests the significance of consuming clean and wholesome substances. For example, drinking clean water and avoiding processed foods can lead to better health outcomes.
  • asme indravāyū: This translates to "may we have the strength of Indra." Indra is a deity associated with power and strength. In everyday life, this can be interpreted as seeking the courage and strength to overcome obstacles, much like how leaders inspire their teams to achieve goals.
  • sadatam barhir edam: This means "may this be our constant support." It emphasizes the need for a stable foundation in life. For instance, having a supportive family or community can provide the necessary encouragement during tough times.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of vitality, strength, awareness, purity, and support. Its significance lies in the reminder to maintain our physical and mental energy, to be aware of our surroundings, and to seek nourishment that is pure and wholesome. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various aspects, such as health and wellness, personal development, and community support. By embodying these principles, individuals can lead more fulfilling and empowered lives.
