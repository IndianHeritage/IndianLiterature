तं त्वा नरो दमा आ नित्यं इद्धम अग्ने सचन्त क्षितिषु ध्रुवासु |अधि द्युम्नं नि दधुर भूय अस्मिन भवā विश्वायुर धरणो रयीणाम् ||
taṁ tvā naro dama ā nityam iddham agne sacanta kṣitiṣu dhruvāsu | adhi dyumnaṁ ni dadhur bhūry asmin bhavā viśvāyur dharuṇo rayīṇām ||
  • taṁ tvā naro dama ā nityam - This phrase can be translated to "We seek you, O Agni, with our minds and hearts, always." Here, "taṁ" refers to the divine, "tvā" means "you," "naro" signifies "humans," and "dama" implies "control" or "discipline." This suggests a continuous pursuit of divine guidance and control in our lives.
  • iddham agne sacanta kṣitiṣu dhruvāsu - This part translates to "O Agni, you who are established in the earth and the heavens." "Agni" represents the fire or divine energy, "iddham" means "established," "sacanta" refers to "sacred," "kṣitiṣu" means "on the earth," and "dhruvāsu" implies "in the heavens." This highlights the omnipresence of divine energy in both the earthly and celestial realms.
  • adhi dyumnaṁ ni dadhur bhūry asmin - This translates to "They who are endowed with glory and wealth." "Adhi" means "upon," "dyumnaṁ" refers to "glory," "ni dadhur" means "they possess," and "bhūry asmin" implies "in this world." This emphasizes the importance of striving for glory and abundance in our lives.
  • bhavā viśvāyur dharuṇo rayīṇām - This can be interpreted as "May you grant us longevity and prosperity." "Bhavā" means "may you become," "viśvāyur" refers to "long life," "dharuṇo" means "strong," and "rayīṇām" signifies "wealth." This expresses a prayer for health, strength, and material well-being.

  • Significance: This mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking divine guidance and control in our lives. It acknowledges the omnipresence of divine energy and the need for glory, longevity, and prosperity.
  • Real-life Application: In daily life, this mantra can inspire individuals to pursue their goals with discipline and dedication. For example, a student may recite this mantra before exams to seek focus and clarity, while a businessperson may use it to attract success and abundance in their ventures.
  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of the divine and human experience. By seeking divine guidance and striving for glory and prosperity, individuals can lead fulfilling lives enriched with purpose and abundance.
