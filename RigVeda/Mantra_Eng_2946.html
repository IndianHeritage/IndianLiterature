महि महे दिवे आर्चा पृथिव्यै कामो मा इच्छन् चरति प्रजानन |ययोः ह स्तोमे विदथेṣu देवाः सपर्व्यो मादयन्ते सचायोः ||
mahi mahe dive arcā pṛthivyai kāmo ma icchañ carati prajānan | yayor ha stome vidatheṣu devāḥ saparyavo mādayante sacāyoḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • mahi: great, vast
    • mahe: to the great one
    • dive: in the sky, heavenly
    • arcā: worship, offering
    • pṛthivyai: to the earth
    • kāmo: desire, wish
    • ma: not, do not
    • icchañ: wishing, desiring
    • carati: moves, acts
    • prajānan: knowing, understanding
    • yayor: by whom
    • ha: indeed
    • stome: in praise, hymn
    • vidatheṣu: in the gatherings, assemblies
    • devāḥ: the gods
    • saparyavo: with reverence, worship
    • mādayante: delight, rejoice
    • sacāyoḥ: with the two, together
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the vastness of the universe and the importance of worshipping both the heavens and the earth.
    • It emphasizes the significance of desires and intentions, suggesting that one should act with knowledge and understanding.
    • The reference to the gods rejoicing in hymns indicates the power of collective worship and the impact of sincere devotion.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community gathering, when people come together to celebrate a festival, they often perform rituals that honor both nature (earth) and the divine (heavens). This reflects the essence of the mantra.
    • In personal life, when individuals set intentions for their goals, they should do so with clarity and understanding, ensuring their desires align with a greater purpose.
    • In workplaces, team-building activities that promote collaboration and shared goals can be seen as a modern-day interpretation of the collective worship mentioned in the mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the interconnectedness of the universe, the importance of intention in our actions, and the joy that comes from collective worship.
    • Its significance lies in reminding us to honor both the material and spiritual aspects of life, fostering a sense of community and shared purpose.
    • In real-life scenarios, it encourages us to act with awareness and to celebrate our connections with others and the world around us.
