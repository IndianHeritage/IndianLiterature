अन्यं अस्मद् भीया इयं अग्नि सिषक्तु दुष्चुनः |वर्धा नो अमवच् शवः ||
anyam asmad bhiyā iyam agne siṣaktu ducchunā | vardhā no amavac chavaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • anyam: another, different
    • asmad: from us, from our side
    • bhiyā: fear, anxiety
    • iyam: this, here
    • agne: O Agni (the fire god)
    • siṣaktu: may you empower, may you bless
    • ducchunā: with great strength, with vigor
    • vardhā: may you increase, may you grow
    • no: for us, to us
    • amavac: immortality, eternal
    • chavaḥ: vitality, life force
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Agni, the fire god, asking for protection from external fears and anxieties.
    • It emphasizes the need for empowerment and strength to overcome challenges.
    • The invocation of Agni signifies the importance of fire as a purifying and transformative force in life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In times of uncertainty, such as during a job loss or health crisis, individuals often seek strength and guidance, similar to invoking Agni for empowerment.
    • In a community setting, when facing natural disasters, people come together to support each other, reflecting the collective strength and protection sought in this mantra.
    • In personal development, individuals may use this mantra as a motivational tool to overcome fears and pursue their goals with vigor.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for strength and protection from fears, highlighting the importance of resilience in the face of adversity.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal challenges to communal support during crises.
    • By invoking Agni, individuals can find the courage to confront their fears and strive for growth and vitality in their lives.
