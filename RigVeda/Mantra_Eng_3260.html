नाहम ato निर आयā दुर्गहेतत तिरश्चतā पार्श्वान निर गमाणि |बहूनी मे अकृतā कर्त्वानि युध्यै त्वेन सं त्वेन पृच्छै ||
nāham ato nir ayā durgahaitat tiraścatā pārśvān nir gamāṇi | bahūni me akṛtā kartvāni yudhyai tvena saṁ tvena pṛcchai ||
  • nāham ato nir ayā durgahaitat tiraścatā pārśvān nir gamāṇi
    • nāham - "I am not"
    • ato - "from this"
    • nir - "without"
    • ayā - "this path"
    • durgahaitat - "difficult to cross"
    • tiraścatā - "obstacles"
    • pārśvān - "on the sides"
    • nir gamāṇi - "without any way to go"
  • bahūni me akṛtā kartvāni yudhyai tvena saṁ tvena pṛcchai
    • bahūni - "many"
    • me - "of me"
    • akṛtā - "not done"
    • kartvāni - "actions"
    • yudhyai - "for battle"
    • tvena - "by that"
    • saṁ - "together"
    • tvena - "by that"
    • pṛcchai - "I ask"

This mantra reflects a profound understanding of the challenges and obstacles one faces in life. The speaker acknowledges that they are not without difficulties and that the path ahead is fraught with challenges. The phrase "durgahaitat tiraścatā" signifies the various hurdles that can appear on the journey, suggesting that life is not a straight path but rather one filled with twists and turns.


In real life, this can be likened to the struggles one faces in personal or professional endeavors. For instance, a student preparing for competitive exams may encounter numerous obstacles such as time management, stress, and distractions. Similarly, an entrepreneur may face challenges like market competition, financial constraints, and operational hurdles. The mantra serves as a reminder that these difficulties are a part of the journey and that one must confront them with courage and determination.


The latter part of the mantra, "bahūni me akṛtā kartvāni yudhyai," emphasizes the importance of taking action despite the challenges. It suggests that one must engage in the battle of life, taking responsibility for their actions and decisions. This can be seen in the context of a person striving to achieve their goals, whether it be in their career, relationships, or personal growth. The mantra encourages individuals to ask themselves what actions they have not yet taken and to reflect on their journey.


Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call to acknowledge the difficulties of life while also emphasizing the importance of action and perseverance. It teaches us that while obstacles may seem insurmountable, they are part of the human experience. By recognizing these challenges and actively engaging in our pursuits, we can navigate through life's complexities and emerge stronger. This mantra is applicable in various real-life scenarios, reminding us to face our battles with resilience and to continually strive for our goals, regardless of the hurdles we encounter.
