उत म ऋज्रे पुरायस्य राघवी सुमीळहे शतम् पेरुके च पक्वा |शाण्डो दाद धिरणिनः स्मद्दिष्टीन दश वशासो अभिषाच ऋष्वान् ||
uta ma ṛjre purayasya raghvī sumīḻhe śatam peruke ca pakvā | śāṇḍo dād dhiraṇinaḥ smaddiṣṭīn daśa vaśāso abhiṣāca ṛṣvān ||
  • uta ma ṛjre purayasya raghvī: This phrase can be interpreted as a call for strength and support in the face of challenges. The word "uta" signifies a plea or invocation, while "ma ṛjre" suggests a desire for protection or assistance. "Purayasya" refers to the city or community, and "raghvī" can be associated with the lineage of the Raghu dynasty, known for its valor and righteousness.
  • sumīḻhe śatam peruke ca pakvā: Here, "sumīḻhe" indicates a state of well-being or prosperity. "Śatam" means a hundred, symbolizing abundance, while "peruke" refers to the various aspects of life. "Pakvā" suggests maturity or ripeness, indicating that the blessings sought are not just superficial but deeply rooted and fully developed.
  • śāṇḍo dād dhiraṇinaḥ smaddiṣṭīn: "Śāṇḍo" can be interpreted as a reference to stability or strength. "Dād" means to give, and "dhiraṇinaḥ" refers to those who are wise or knowledgeable. "Smaddiṣṭīn" implies the attainment of goals or objectives, suggesting that wisdom leads to the fulfillment of desires.
  • daśa vaśāso abhiṣāca ṛṣvān: This line emphasizes the importance of tenacity and control. "Daśa" means ten, which can symbolize completeness or wholeness. "Vaśāso" refers to those who are under control or influence, while "abhiṣāca" suggests a blessing or anointing. "Ṛṣvān" refers to the wise or enlightened ones, indicating that the blessings are bestowed upon those who are deserving.

  • Significance: This mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking strength, wisdom, and prosperity in life. It emphasizes the importance of community support, personal growth, and the pursuit of knowledge. The invocation of blessings for stability and control reflects a holistic approach to achieving success.
  • Real-life Application: In practical terms, this mantra can be applied in various aspects of life, such as:
    • Community Building: Just as the mantra calls for support from the community, individuals can engage in community service or collaborative projects to foster unity and strength.
    • Personal Development: The emphasis on wisdom and maturity encourages individuals to pursue education and self-improvement, leading to a more fulfilling life.
    • Goal Setting: The mantra serves as a reminder to set clear objectives and work diligently towards them, seeking guidance and support from knowledgeable individuals.

  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the interconnectedness of strength, wisdom, and community. By invoking these qualities, individuals can navigate life's challenges more effectively, leading to a prosperous and fulfilling existence. Its teachings are applicable in everyday life, encouraging a balanced approach to personal and communal growth.
