यो नः कश्चिद् रिरिक्षति रक्षस्त्वेन मार्त्यः |स्वैः ष एवै रिरिषीष्ट युर जनः ||
yo naḥ kaś cid ririkṣati rakṣastvena martyaḥ | svaiḥ ṣa evai ririṣīṣṭa yur janaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yo: who
    • naḥ: our
    • kaś cid: anyone
    • ririkṣati: desires or wishes
    • rakṣastvena: with protection
    • martyaḥ: mortal or human
    • svaiḥ: with their own
    • ṣa: six
    • evai: indeed
    • ririṣīṣṭa: desires or wishes
    • yur: strength or power
    • janaḥ: people or beings
  • Overall Meaning:
    • This mantra speaks about the protection and desires of individuals.
    • It emphasizes that no one should wish harm upon us, and we should be safeguarded by our own strength and desires.
    • The mantra reflects a collective aspiration for safety and well-being among people.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community setting, this mantra can be applied to foster a sense of unity and protection among its members. For instance, during a neighborhood watch program, residents come together to ensure the safety of their homes and families.
    • In personal relationships, individuals can use this mantra to remind themselves and others to wish well for each other, creating a supportive environment where everyone feels secure and valued.
    • In workplaces, this mantra can inspire a culture of collaboration and mutual respect, where employees protect each other's interests and work towards common goals without harboring ill will.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for collective protection and positive intentions among individuals.
    • It serves as a reminder that our desires and actions should align with the well-being of others, fostering a harmonious existence.
