यदाद्य सूर्य ब्रह्मो 'नागा उद्यान मित्राय वरुणाय सत्यं |वयं देवत्रादिते स्याम तव प्रियासो आर्यमन् गृणन्तः ||
yad adya sūrya bravo 'nāgā udyan mitrāya varuṇāya satyam | vayaṁ devatrādite syāma tava priyāso aryaman gṛṇantaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yad: whatever
    • adya: today
    • sūrya: O Sun
    • bravo: you speak
    • nāgā: O great one
    • udyān: rising
    • mitrāya: to Mitra (the friend)
    • varuṇāya: to Varuna (the cosmic order)
    • satyam: truth
    • vayaṁ: we
    • devatrādite: O divine Aditi
    • syāma: may we be
    • tava: your
    • priyāso: beloved
    • aryaman: O Aryaman (the noble one)
    • gṛṇantaḥ: singing
  • Overall Meaning:
    • This mantra is a prayer to the Sun, acknowledging its power and the cosmic order it represents.
    • It emphasizes the importance of truth and friendship, invoking the deities Mitra and Varuna, who symbolize these qualities.
    • The mantra expresses a desire to be aligned with divine principles and to be in harmony with the universe.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, this mantra can inspire individuals to seek truth in their actions and relationships, promoting honesty and integrity.
    • In a community setting, it encourages collaboration and friendship, reminding people to work together for a common good.
    • In personal development, it serves as a reminder to align one's goals with higher values and principles, fostering a sense of purpose.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for truth, friendship, and alignment with divine principles.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal relationships to community interactions, emphasizing the importance of living in harmony with oneself and others.
    • By reciting this mantra, individuals can cultivate a mindset that values truth and cooperation, leading to a more fulfilling and harmonious life.
