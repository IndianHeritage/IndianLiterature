मन्त्र: इमो अग्नि वीततमानि हव्याजस्रो वक्षि देवतातिमच्छ || प्रति न ईं सुरभिणि व्यन्तु ||
imo agne vītatamāni havyājasro vakṣi devatātim accha | prati na īṁ surabhīṇi vyantu ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • imo: this
    • agne: O Agni (the fire god)
    • vītatamāni: the most expansive or the most widespread
    • havyājasro: the one who is worthy of offerings
    • vakṣi: may you speak or may you express
    • devatātim: the divine essence or the divine quality
    • accha: may it be good or auspicious
    • prati: towards or in accordance with
    • na: not
    • īṁ: the sacred sound or the essence
    • surabhīṇi: the fragrant or the pleasing
    • vyantu: may they come or may they arrive
  • Overall Meaning: This mantra is a prayer to Agni, the fire god, asking for his blessings and guidance. It emphasizes the importance of divine qualities and the desire for auspiciousness in life. The speaker seeks the expansive and worthy nature of Agni to manifest in their life, while also wishing for the arrival of pleasant and fragrant experiences.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a wedding ceremony, the fire is often invoked as a witness and a purifying element. This mantra can be recited to seek blessings for a harmonious and prosperous married life.
    • During a new venture or business opening, entrepreneurs may chant this mantra to invoke the divine qualities of Agni, hoping for success and positive outcomes.
    • In daily life, individuals can use this mantra as a reminder to seek positivity and divine guidance in their actions and decisions, fostering a mindset of growth and expansion.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its invocation of divine qualities and the pursuit of auspiciousness in life. It serves as a reminder to align oneself with positive energies and to seek blessings from higher powers. In real-life scenarios, it can be applied in various rituals, personal reflections, and moments of decision-making, encouraging individuals to embrace the expansive and worthy aspects of existence while inviting pleasant experiences into their lives.
