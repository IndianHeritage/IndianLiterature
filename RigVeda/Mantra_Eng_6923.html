यच चिद्धि ते अपि व्यथिर जगन्वांसो अमनमहि |गोदा इद इन्द्र बोधि नः ||
yac cid dhi te api vyathir jaganvāṁso amanmahi | godā id indra bodhi naḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yac - whatever
    • cid - consciousness or awareness
    • dhi - intellect or understanding
    • te - your
    • api - also
    • vyathir - disturbance or affliction
    • jaganvāṁso - of the universe or the world
    • amanmahi - we do not desire or we do not wish
    • godā - the gift or boon
    • id - this
    • indra - Indra, the king of gods
    • bodhi - wisdom or enlightenment
    • naḥ - for us
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra expresses a plea for understanding and wisdom from a higher power, specifically addressing Indra, who is often associated with strength and enlightenment.
    • The phrase "whatever your consciousness may be" suggests an acknowledgment of the vastness of existence and the complexities of life.
    • It reflects a desire to be free from disturbances and afflictions that may arise from worldly experiences.
    • The invocation of Indra signifies a request for divine guidance and clarity in navigating life's challenges.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In moments of stress or confusion, individuals often seek guidance through meditation or prayer, similar to the invocation of Indra for wisdom.
    • In a workplace setting, when faced with challenges, teams may come together to brainstorm solutions, reflecting the collective desire for clarity and understanding.
    • Students preparing for exams may recite this mantra to seek focus and alleviate anxiety, embodying the essence of seeking wisdom and relief from disturbances.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking wisdom and understanding in the face of life's complexities.
    • It serves as a reminder that while we may encounter disturbances, there is a higher power we can turn to for guidance.
    • In real life, this mantra can be applied in various situations where clarity and peace of mind are needed, reinforcing the idea that seeking knowledge and support is a vital part of the human experience.
