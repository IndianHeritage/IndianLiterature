नि पर्वतः साद्य अप्रयुच्छन् सम मातृभिर वावशानो अक्रान् |दूरे पारि वाणीं वर्धयन्त इन्द्रेषितां धमनिम् पप्रथन् नि ||
ni parvataḥ sādy aprayucchan sam mātṛbhir vāvaśāno akrān | dūre pāre vāṇīṁ vardhayanta indreṣitāṁ dhamanim paprathan ni ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ni: not
    • parvataḥ: mountain
    • sādy: immediately
    • aprayucchan: not abandoning
    • sam: together
    • mātṛbhir: by the mothers
    • vāvaśāno: being nourished
    • akrān: having been created
    • dūre: far away
    • pāre: across
    • vāṇīṁ: speech or voice
    • vardhayanta: increasing
    • indreṣitāṁ: directed by Indra
    • dhamanim: the power or strength
    • paprathan: having been spread
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the strength and nurturing qualities of the divine, represented by the mothers (mātṛbhir) and the god Indra.
    • It emphasizes the idea of not abandoning one's roots or foundational values (aprayucchan) even when faced with challenges (symbolized by mountains).
    • The mantra suggests that through collective strength and support (sam), one can overcome obstacles and enhance their voice or expression (vāṇīṁ vardhayanta).
    • It highlights the importance of community and the nurturing aspect of relationships, where individuals support each other to grow and thrive.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a family setting, parents (mātṛbhir) provide support and guidance to their children, ensuring they do not stray from their values even when faced with societal pressures.
    • In a workplace, teamwork and collaboration can be seen as a manifestation of this mantra, where colleagues support each other to achieve common goals, thereby increasing their collective voice and impact.
    • In community service, volunteers come together to help those in need, embodying the spirit of not abandoning those who require assistance, thus spreading strength and compassion.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of community, support, and resilience in the face of challenges.
    • It encourages individuals to stay connected to their roots and values while also uplifting others around them.
    • In real life, it is applicable in various scenarios such as family dynamics, workplace environments, and community interactions, promoting a culture of mutual support and growth.
