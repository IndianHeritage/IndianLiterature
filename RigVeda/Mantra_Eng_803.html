अराधि होताऽस्वर्ण निषत्तः कृत्वान विश्वानि अपांसी सत्याः ||
arādhi hotā sva1r niṣattaḥ kṛṇvan viśvāny apāṁsi satyā ||
  • Mantra: "arādhi hotā sva1r niṣattaḥ kṛṇvan viśvāny apāṁsi satyā"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • arādhi: To praise or to invoke.
    • hotā: The priest or the one who offers the sacrifice.
    • sva1r: The celestial or divine realm.
    • niṣattaḥ: With determination or focus.
    • kṛṇvan: Creating or manifesting.
    • viśvāny: All or universal.
    • apāṁsi: Waters or the essence of life.
    • satyā: Truth or reality.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of invoking the divine with sincerity and focus.
    • The role of the priest (hotā) signifies the need for guidance and support in our spiritual endeavors.
    • By creating a connection with the celestial realm (sva1r), we can manifest our desires and intentions.
    • The mention of "viśvāny apāṁsi" highlights the interconnectedness of all life and the importance of nurturing our environment.
    • Finally, the reference to "satyā" underscores the significance of truth in our actions and thoughts.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, a leader who invokes the collective spirit of the team (arādhi) can inspire and motivate others to work towards a common goal (kṛṇvan viśvāny).
    • In personal relationships, focusing on truth and sincerity (satyā) can strengthen bonds and foster trust.
    • Environmental initiatives that emphasize the importance of water (apāṁsi) and its preservation reflect the interconnectedness of all living beings.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the power of intention and the importance of invoking the divine in our pursuits.
    • It encourages us to act with truth and integrity while recognizing our connection to the greater universe.
    • In real life, applying the principles of this mantra can lead to personal growth, harmonious relationships, and a sustainable environment.
