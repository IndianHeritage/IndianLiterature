सूत्रामाणं पृथिवीं द्यां अनहसं सुशर्माणं अदितिं सुप्रणीतिम् |दैवीं नावं स्वरित्राम अनागसम अस्रवंतिम् आ रुहेमा स्वस्तये ||
sutrāmāṇam pṛthivīṁ dyām anehasaṁ suśarmāṇam aditiṁ supraṇītim | daivīṁ nāvaṁ svaritrām anāgasam asravantīm ā ruhemā svastaye ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sutrāmāṇam: The one who is connected or tied together.
    • pṛthivīṁ: The Earth.
    • dyām: The sky or heavens.
    • anehasaṁ: Without darkness or obstacles.
    • suśarmāṇam: The one who brings good fortune or auspiciousness.
    • aditiṁ: The infinite or boundless.
    • supraṇītim: The one who is well-guided or directed.
    • daivīṁ: Divine or heavenly.
    • nāvaṁ: A boat or vessel.
    • svaritrām: The one that leads to the heavens.
    • anāgasam: Without sin or fault.
    • asravantīm: Flowing or abundant.
    • ā: To or towards.
    • ruhemā: May we rise or elevate.
    • svastaye: For well-being or prosperity.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the connection between the Earth and the heavens, symbolizing the unity of the physical and spiritual realms.
    • The phrase "anehasaṁ suśarmāṇam" suggests a journey towards auspiciousness, free from obstacles, which can be likened to pursuing a goal in life with clarity and positivity.
    • "Aditiṁ supraṇītim" emphasizes the importance of guidance and direction, akin to seeking mentorship or wisdom in our personal and professional lives.
    • The metaphor of a "daivīṁ nāvaṁ" (divine boat) signifies the support we receive from the universe or divine forces as we navigate through life's challenges.
    • "Anāgasam asravantīm" reflects the idea of purity and abundance, reminding us to strive for a life free from negativity and filled with positive energy.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, this mantra can inspire individuals to work collaboratively, ensuring that their efforts are aligned with a common goal, much like a team rowing together in a boat.
    • In personal development, it encourages individuals to seek guidance from mentors or wise figures, helping them navigate their paths with clarity and purpose.
    • In community building, it highlights the importance of creating a supportive environment where everyone can thrive, akin to a nurturing ecosystem.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of all aspects of life, urging us to seek auspiciousness and guidance while navigating our journeys.
    • Its significance lies in promoting well-being, unity, and the pursuit of a life filled with positivity and purpose.
    • In real-life scenarios, it can be applied in personal growth, professional endeavors, and community interactions, fostering a holistic approach to living.
