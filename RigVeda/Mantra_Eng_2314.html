उत स्य न इन्द्रो विश्वचर्षणिर दिवः शर्धेन मारुतेन सुक्रतुः |अनु नु स्थात्य अवृकाभिर ऊतिभी रथम महे सनये वाजसातये ||
uta sya na indro viśvacarṣaṇir divaḥ śardhena mārutena sukratuḥ | anu nu sthāty avṛkābhir ūtibhī ratham mahe sanaye vājasātaye ||
  • uta sya na indro viśvacarṣaṇir: This phrase can be translated as "May Indra, the all-pervading one, be with us." Indra is often regarded as the king of the gods in Vedic literature, symbolizing strength and power. In real life, this can be seen as a call for divine support in our endeavors, much like seeking guidance from a mentor or leader.
  • divaḥ śardhena mārutena sukratuḥ: This translates to "with the help of the Maruts, may we achieve our goals." The Maruts are storm deities associated with strength and vitality. In a modern context, this can be likened to the support we receive from our peers or colleagues when working towards a common goal, emphasizing the importance of teamwork and collaboration.
  • anu nu sthāty avṛkābhir ūtibhī: This means "may we be steadfast and supported by the forces of nature." This can be interpreted as a reminder to remain grounded and resilient in the face of challenges. In everyday life, this could relate to how we draw strength from our environment, such as nature or community, to overcome obstacles.
  • ratham mahe sanaye vājasātaye: This translates to "may we be blessed with a great chariot and strength." The chariot symbolizes progress and the journey of life. In practical terms, this can represent the resources and opportunities we have at our disposal to achieve our aspirations, akin to having the right tools and support to succeed in our personal and professional lives.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of divine support, teamwork, resilience, and the resources necessary for success. In real life, it serves as a reminder to seek guidance, collaborate with others, remain steadfast in our pursuits, and utilize the tools available to us. By embodying these principles, we can navigate the challenges of life more effectively and achieve our goals with greater ease.
