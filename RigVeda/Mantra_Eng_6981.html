विदा देवाः अघानां आदित्यासो अपाकृतिम् |पक्षाः वयो यथोपरी व्यास्मे शर्म यच्छतानेहसो व ऊतयः सुतयः व ऊतयः ||
vidā devā aghānām ādityāso apākṛtim | pakṣā vayo yathopari vy a1sme śarma yacchatānehaso va ūtayaḥ suūtayo va ūtayaḥ ||
  • Vidā devā aghānām: This phrase can be translated as "the gods who know the unknowing." It emphasizes the divine wisdom that transcends human understanding. In real life, this can be related to the pursuit of knowledge and the importance of seeking guidance from those who possess greater wisdom, such as mentors or spiritual leaders.
  • Ādityāso apākṛtim: This means "the sons of Aditi, who remove obstacles." Aditi is often associated with the infinite and the cosmic. In practical terms, this can be seen in how individuals or communities work together to overcome challenges, drawing on collective strength and support.
  • Pakṣā vayo yathopari: This translates to "like the wings of a bird." It symbolizes freedom and the ability to rise above difficulties. In everyday life, this can be likened to the aspirations of individuals who strive to achieve their dreams, much like a bird soaring high in the sky.
  • Vy a1sme śarma yacchatānehaso: This phrase can be interpreted as "may they grant us protection and comfort." It highlights the human need for safety and security. In real life, this can be reflected in the importance of community support, family bonds, and the role of social systems in providing a sense of belonging and protection.
  • Va ūtayaḥ suūtayo va ūtayaḥ: This can be understood as "may they bring forth auspiciousness." It speaks to the desire for positive outcomes and blessings in life. In practical terms, this can be seen in rituals, prayers, or positive affirmations that individuals or communities engage in to attract good fortune and success.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking divine wisdom, overcoming obstacles, aspiring for freedom, and the importance of protection and auspiciousness in life. Its significance lies in the reminder that while we navigate through challenges, we can draw on the support of the divine and our community. In real-life scenarios, this mantra encourages individuals to seek knowledge, work collaboratively to overcome difficulties, and maintain a positive outlook towards achieving their goals. It serves as a guiding principle for personal growth and communal harmony.
