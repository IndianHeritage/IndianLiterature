प्राग्नये तवसे भरध्वं गिरं दिवो अरतये पृथिव्याः |यो विश्वेषाम अमृतानाम उपस्थे वैश्वानरो वावृधे जागृवद्भिः ||
prāgnaye tavase bharadhvaṁ giraṁ divo arataye pṛthivyāḥ | yo viśveṣām amṛtānām upasthe vaiśvānaro vāvṛdhe jāgṛvadbhiḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • prāgnaye: To the wise or knowledgeable.
    • tavase: To the strength or power.
    • bharadhvaṁ: May you carry or bear.
    • giraṁ: The words or speech.
    • divo: Of the heavens or sky.
    • arataye: For the purpose of prosperity or well-being.
    • pṛthivyāḥ: Of the earth.
    • yo: Who.
    • viśveṣām: Of all beings or creatures.
    • amṛtānām: Of the immortal or eternal.
    • upasthe: In the presence or vicinity.
    • vaiśvānaro: The universal or cosmic being.
    • vāvṛdhe: Has grown or expanded.
    • jāgṛvadbhiḥ: By the awakened or alert ones.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the essence of wisdom and strength, urging individuals to carry forth the power of speech and knowledge for the prosperity of both the heavens and the earth.
    • It emphasizes the importance of being aware and alert, suggesting that true growth and understanding come from being conscious and engaged with the world around us.
    • The reference to "amṛtānām" highlights the eternal nature of knowledge and wisdom, suggesting that these qualities transcend the physical realm and connect us to a greater cosmic existence.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, leaders who embody the qualities of wisdom and strength can inspire their teams to achieve greater heights, fostering an environment where knowledge is shared and valued.
    • In education, teachers who are aware and engaged with their students can create a more enriching learning experience, helping students to grow intellectually and emotionally.
    • In personal relationships, being alert and present can lead to deeper connections, as individuals communicate openly and support each other's growth.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the significance of wisdom, strength, and awareness in our lives.
    • It encourages us to harness our knowledge and power for the betterment of ourselves and the world around us.
    • By embodying these principles, we can contribute to a more harmonious and prosperous existence, both individually and collectively.
