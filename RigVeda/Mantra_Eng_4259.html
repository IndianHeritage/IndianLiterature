आ यद वां सूर्य रथं तिष्ठद रघुश्यदं सदा |परि वां अरुषा वयो घृणा वरन्त आतपः ||
ā yad vāṁ sūryā rathaṁ tiṣṭhad raghuṣyadaṁ sadā | pari vām aruṣā vayo ghṛṇā varanta ātapaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā - from
    • yad - whatever
    • vāṁ - your
    • sūryā - sun
    • rathaṁ - chariot
    • tiṣṭhad - stands
    • raghuṣyadaṁ - of the noble
    • sadā - always
    • pari - around
    • vām - you
    • aruṣā - red, fiery
    • vayo - age
    • ghṛṇā - compassion
    • varanta - bestowing
    • ātapaḥ - heat, warmth
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the divine presence and the blessings that come from the sun, symbolizing vitality and energy.
    • The "chariot of the sun" represents the journey of life, where the sun is a guiding force.
    • The phrase "of the noble" suggests that this energy is associated with those who are virtuous and righteous.
    • The mantra emphasizes the importance of compassion and warmth in our lives, suggesting that these qualities are essential for a fulfilling existence.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, we often seek guidance and inspiration from nature, much like how the sun provides light and warmth. For instance, people often feel rejuvenated after spending time outdoors in the sunlight.
    • Compassion can be seen in acts of kindness, such as helping a neighbor or volunteering in the community, reflecting the "ghṛṇā varanta" aspect of the mantra.
    • The journey of life can be likened to a road trip where the sun serves as a metaphorical compass, guiding us through challenges and illuminating our path.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of divine guidance, compassion, and the energy that sustains life.
    • It is applicable in real-life scenarios where individuals seek strength and direction, reminding us to embody warmth and kindness in our interactions.
    • By reflecting on this mantra, we can cultivate a deeper connection with nature and the virtues that enhance our lives, ultimately leading to a more harmonious existence.
