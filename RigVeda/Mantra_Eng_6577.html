य ऋक्षाद अम्हसो मुचद यो वार्यāt सप्त सिन्धुषु |वधार दासस्य तुविनृम्ण नीनमः ||
ya ṛkṣād aṁhaso mucad yo vāryāt sapta sindhuṣu | vadhar dāsasya tuvinṛmṇa nīnamaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ya: who
    • ṛkṣād: from the bear
    • aṁhaso: of the strength
    • mucad: release
    • yo: who
    • vāryāt: from the waters
    • sapta: seven
    • sindhuṣu: rivers
    • vadhar: to the one who is bound
    • dāsasya: of the servant
    • tuvinṛmṇa: to the one who is in distress
    • nīnamaḥ: we bow down
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of liberation and the release from bondage.
    • The reference to "the bear" and "the strength" suggests overcoming obstacles and challenges.
    • The mention of "seven rivers" symbolizes abundance and the flow of life, representing various paths and choices.
    • It emphasizes the importance of seeking help and guidance when in distress, particularly from divine or higher powers.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a personal context, when someone faces challenges in life, such as job loss or health issues, they may seek support from friends, family, or spiritual practices to find strength and overcome these difficulties.
    • In a community setting, this mantra can be applied when groups come together to support those in need, such as during natural disasters where collective efforts help in recovery and rebuilding.
    • In a corporate environment, leaders may refer to this mantra when encouraging teamwork and collaboration to overcome business challenges, emphasizing the importance of unity and support.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of seeking liberation from struggles and the strength found in unity and support.
    • It teaches us that in times of distress, we should not hesitate to seek help and that there is power in collective action.
    • In real life, this mantra can inspire individuals and communities to work together, support one another, and strive for freedom from their challenges, ultimately leading to personal and collective growth.
