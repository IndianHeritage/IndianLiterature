वित्वक्षणः समृतौ चक्रमासजो 'सुन्वतो विषुणः सुन्वतो वृद्धः |इन्द्रो विश्वस्य दामितā विभीषणो यथावशं नयति दासम आर्यः ||
vitvakṣaṇaḥ samṛtau cakramāsajo 'sunvato viṣuṇaḥ sunvato vṛdhaḥ | indro viśvasya damitā vibhīṣaṇo yathāvaśaṁ nayati dāsam āryaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • vitvakṣaṇaḥ: One who is wise or knowledgeable.
    • samṛtau: In prosperity or abundance.
    • cakramāsajo: One who is seated on the wheel or cycle of life.
    • 'sunvato: Of those who are virtuous or righteous.
    • viṣuṇaḥ: Of those who are wise or learned.
    • vṛdhaḥ: The elder or the wise one.
    • indro: Indra, the king of gods, representing power and authority.
    • viśvasya: Of the universe or all beings.
    • damitā: One who has control or mastery.
    • vibhīṣaṇo: One who is formidable or powerful.
    • yathāvaśaṁ: As per one's will or desire.
    • nayati: Leads or guides.
    • dāsam: The servant or follower.
    • āryaḥ: The noble or honorable one.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the qualities of wisdom, prosperity, and leadership.
    • It emphasizes the importance of being knowledgeable and virtuous in order to achieve success and abundance in life.
    • Indra, as a symbol of power, represents the ability to control and guide others, highlighting the role of a leader in society.
    • The mantra suggests that true leadership comes from wisdom and the ability to act in accordance with righteousness.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • A successful business leader who makes informed decisions based on knowledge and ethical practices embodies the essence of this mantra.
    • A teacher who guides students with wisdom and compassion reflects the qualities of the elder or wise one mentioned in the mantra.
    • In community leadership, individuals who promote prosperity and well-being through virtuous actions exemplify the teachings of this mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of wisdom, virtue, and leadership in achieving success and harmony in life.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, such as in business, education, and community service, where knowledgeable and ethical leadership is essential.
    • By embodying the qualities described in this mantra, individuals can contribute positively to society and inspire others to follow a righteous path.
