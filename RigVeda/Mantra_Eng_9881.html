सच यद आसु जहतीष्व अतकम अमानुषीषु मानवो निषेवे |अप स्म मत तरसन्ती न भुज्युस ता अत्रसन रथस्पृशो नाश्वाः ||
sacā yad āsu jahatīṣv atkam amānuṣīṣu mānuṣo niṣeve | apa sma mat tarasantī na bhujyus tā atrasan rathaspṛśo nāśvāḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sacā: and indeed
    • yad: whatever
    • āsu: is in existence
    • jahatīṣv: in the realms of the non-human
    • atkam: the essence or the core
    • amānuṣīṣu: in the non-human beings
    • mānuṣo: the human
    • niṣeve: should serve or worship
    • apa: away from
    • sma: indeed
    • mat: my thought or understanding
    • tarasantī: those who are crossing over
    • na: not
    • bhujyus: may consume or partake
    • tā: those
    • atrasan: who are in this place
    • rathaspṛśo: touched by the chariot
    • nāśvāḥ: not the horses
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks to the relationship between humans and non-human entities.
    • It suggests that whatever exists in the non-human realms should be acknowledged and respected by humans.
    • The phrase "my thought" indicates a personal reflection on the interconnectedness of all beings.
    • The mantra warns against the consumption or exploitation of those who are present in this realm, particularly those touched by the chariot, symbolizing progress or movement.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In environmental conservation, this mantra can be seen as a call to respect nature and all living beings, urging humans to serve and protect rather than exploit.
    • In the context of animal rights, it emphasizes the importance of treating animals with dignity and not consuming them thoughtlessly.
    • In social justice, it can be interpreted as a reminder to consider the marginalized and non-human entities in our society, advocating for their rights and well-being.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of respect and reverence for all forms of life, urging humans to serve rather than dominate.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, including environmentalism, animal rights, and social justice, promoting a holistic view of existence.
    • Ultimately, it calls for a harmonious coexistence with all beings, fostering a sense of responsibility and stewardship towards the world around us.
