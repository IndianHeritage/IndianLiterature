वावृद्धानस्य ते वयं विश्वा धनानि जिग्युषः |ऊतिम इन्द्रा वृणीमहे ||
vāvṛdhānasya te vayaṁ viśvā dhanāni jigyuṣaḥ | ūtim indrā vṛṇīmahe ||
  • vāvṛdhānasya: This term can be broken down into "vā" meaning "to grow" or "to increase" and "vṛdhāna" which refers to "prosperity" or "wealth." Thus, it signifies the growth of prosperity.
  • te: This word translates to "your," indicating possession or belonging.
  • vayaṁ: This means "we" or "us," referring to the collective group or community.
  • viśvā: This term means "all" or "universal," suggesting inclusivity and the idea of encompassing everything.
  • dhanāni: This translates to "wealth" or "riches," referring to material and spiritual abundance.
  • jigyuṣaḥ: This word means "to achieve" or "to attain," indicating the act of reaching or acquiring something.
  • ūtim: This term refers to "strength" or "power," often associated with vitality and energy.
  • indrā: This is a reference to Indra, the king of the gods in Vedic tradition, symbolizing authority, power, and leadership.
  • vṛṇīmahe: This means "we choose" or "we invoke," indicating a call to action or a desire to select something beneficial.

The mantra can be interpreted as a collective invocation for the growth of prosperity and wealth, where the community seeks the strength and blessings of Indra to achieve universal abundance. It emphasizes the importance of unity and collective effort in attaining prosperity.


In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts such as:

  • Community Development: When a community comes together to work on a project, such as building infrastructure or starting a cooperative business, they can invoke this mantra to seek collective prosperity and strength.
  • Teamwork in Organizations: In a corporate setting, teams can use the essence of this mantra to foster collaboration and unity, aiming for shared goals and achievements.
  • Spiritual Gatherings: During rituals or gatherings, this mantra can be recited to invoke blessings for the community's growth and well-being.

Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for collective prosperity and strength. It highlights the importance of unity in achieving common goals and the role of divine blessings in facilitating growth. By invoking the essence of this mantra, individuals and communities can strive for a harmonious and prosperous existence, fostering an environment where everyone can thrive together.
