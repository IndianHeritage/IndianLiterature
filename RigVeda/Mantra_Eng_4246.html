तां वां सम्यग अद्रुह्वाणेशम् अश्यामा धायसे |वयं ते रुद्राः स्याम ||
tā vāṁ samyag adruhvāṇeṣam aśyāma dhāyase | vayaṁ te rudrā syāma ||
  • tā vāṁ: This phrase can be translated as "that we" or "we are." It signifies a collective identity or unity among individuals.
  • samyag: This word means "properly" or "correctly." It emphasizes the importance of doing things in the right manner.
  • adruhvāṇeṣam: This term can be broken down into "adruhvā" (not deviating) and "eṣam" (that which is). Together, it suggests a path or way that is steadfast and unwavering.
  • aśyāma: This word translates to "dark" or "black," often symbolizing the unknown or the challenges in life.
  • dhāyase: This means "you support" or "you uphold." It indicates a source of strength or stability.
  • vayaṁ te: This translates to "we are yours," expressing a sense of belonging or devotion to a higher power.
  • rudrā: Referring to Rudra, a deity associated with storms, wind, and the hunt, symbolizing both destruction and regeneration.
  • syāma: This means "may we be" or "let us be," indicating a desire for transformation or a state of being.

The mantra can be interpreted as a prayer for guidance and support from a higher power, specifically invoking Rudra. It emphasizes the importance of unity, proper conduct, and the strength to face challenges. The phrase "we are yours" signifies a deep connection and surrender to divine will.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios:

  • Community Support: Just as the mantra emphasizes unity, it can inspire communities to come together in times of crisis, supporting one another through challenges.
  • Personal Growth: The idea of not deviating from a steadfast path can encourage individuals to remain focused on their goals, even when faced with difficulties.
  • Spiritual Connection: The invocation of Rudra can serve as a reminder to seek guidance from a higher power, fostering a sense of peace and purpose in one's life.

In conclusion, this mantra holds significant meaning in promoting unity, resilience, and spiritual connection. It encourages individuals and communities to support one another and remain steadfast in their pursuits, ultimately leading to personal and collective growth.
