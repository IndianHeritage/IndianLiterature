स नो वस्व उप मास्य ऊर्जो नपान माहिनस्य |सखे वसो जरीतृभ्यः ||
sa no vasva upa māsy ūrjo napān māhinasya | sakhe vaso jaritṛbhyaḥ ||
  • sa no vasva upa māsy: This phrase can be translated to "May all our energies come together." The word "sa" means "together," "no" refers to "our," "vasva" signifies "all," and "upa māsy" implies "may come." This suggests a collective effort or unity in purpose.
  • ūrjo napān māhinasya: Here, "ūrjo" means "energy" or "vitality," "napān" refers to "nourishment" or "sustenance," and "māhinasya" translates to "of the great one." This part emphasizes the importance of energy and nourishment that comes from a higher source or the divine.
  • sakhe vaso jaritṛbhyaḥ: "sakhe" means "friend" or "companion," "vaso" translates to "may you dwell," and "jaritṛbhyaḥ" refers to "those who are strong or powerful." This line conveys a message of friendship and support among those who are strong and capable.

  • Significance: This mantra emphasizes the importance of unity, energy, and friendship. It calls for collective strength and the nurturing of vital energies that come from a higher source. In a world where individualism often prevails, this mantra serves as a reminder of the power of collaboration and mutual support.
  • Real-life Application: In various aspects of life, such as teamwork in a corporate environment, community service, or even family dynamics, the essence of this mantra can be applied. For instance, in a workplace, when team members come together with a shared goal, they can achieve greater results than when working in isolation. Similarly, in community projects, the collective effort of individuals can lead to significant positive changes.
  • Conclusion: The mantra "sa no vasva upa māsy ūrjo napān māhinasya | sakhe vaso jaritṛbhyaḥ" serves as a powerful reminder of the strength found in unity and the importance of nurturing our energies through collaboration and friendship. It encourages individuals to come together, support one another, and draw upon the divine energies that sustain us, ultimately leading to a more harmonious and productive existence.
