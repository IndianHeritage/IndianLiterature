मन्त्र: सिन्धूम् iva प्रवण āशुयā यतो यदि क्लोशम् अनु ष्वणि |आ ये वयो न वर्वृतत्य आमिषि गृभीता बाह्वोर गवि ||
sindhūm̐r iva pravaṇa āśuyā yato yadi klośam anu ṣvaṇi | ā ye vayo na varvṛtaty āmiṣi gṛbhītā bāhvor gavi ||
  • Mantra: "sindhūm̐r iva pravaṇa āśuyā yato yadi klośam anu ṣvaṇi | ā ye vayo na varvṛtaty āmiṣi gṛbhītā bāhvor gavi ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sindhūm̐r: like a river
    • iva: as if
    • pravaṇa: flowing
    • āśuyā: with great speed or energy
    • yato: from where
    • yadi: if
    • klośam: a covering or veil
    • anu: after
    • ṣvaṇi: sound or voice
    • ā: those
    • ye: who
    • vayo: of the ages or time
    • na: not
    • varvṛtaty: are turned away
    • āmiṣi: in the midst
    • gṛbhītā: having been taken or grasped
    • bāhvor: of the arms
    • gavi: in the cow or in the womb
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra draws a parallel between the flow of a river and the energy or vitality of life.
    • The imagery of a river suggests a continuous and dynamic movement, symbolizing the flow of time and experiences.
    • The phrase "if a covering or veil" indicates that there may be obstacles or distractions that obscure our true path or purpose.
    • The mention of "those who are not turned away" emphasizes the importance of perseverance and focus in the face of challenges.
    • The reference to "arms" and "womb" can symbolize strength and nurturing, suggesting that both action and care are essential in our journey.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, this mantra can be applied to the pursuit of goals. Just as a river flows steadily, individuals must remain focused and determined, overcoming obstacles that may arise.
    • In personal relationships, the mantra encourages open communication and understanding, akin to the flowing nature of a river that nurtures the land around it.
    • In health and wellness, the idea of energy and vitality can inspire individuals to maintain a balanced lifestyle, flowing through life with purpose and intention.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of resilience and clarity in our lives.
    • It encourages us to embrace the flow of life, to remain focused on our goals, and to nurture our relationships and well-being.
    • By understanding and applying the teachings of this mantra, we can navigate the complexities of life with grace and strength.
