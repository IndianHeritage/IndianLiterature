अभ्य अवस्थाः प्र जायत प्र वव्रे ववृष चिकेट |उपस्थे मातुर विच चष्टे ||
abhy avasthāḥ pra jāyante pra vavrer vavriś ciketa | upasthe mātur vi caṣṭe ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • abhy: towards, in the direction of
    • avasthāḥ: states, conditions, or situations
    • pra: forth, forward
    • jāyante: are born, arise
    • pra: forth, forward
    • vavrer: to be strong, to be powerful
    • vavriś: to be wise, to understand
    • ciketa: to perceive, to know
    • upasthe: in the womb, in the place of birth
    • mātur: of the mother
    • vi: indeed, certainly
    • caṣṭe: moves, acts
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the various states of existence that arise and the strength and wisdom that come from them.
    • It highlights the role of the mother, particularly in the context of birth and nurturing, suggesting that the womb is a place of significant transformation and potential.
    • The repetition of "pra" emphasizes the forward movement and emergence of life and consciousness.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In the context of childbirth, this mantra can be seen as a celebration of the journey from conception to birth, where the mother plays a crucial role in nurturing life.
    • In personal development, the mantra can be interpreted as a reminder that various states of being (emotional, mental, spiritual) arise as we grow and learn, and that wisdom and strength are gained through these experiences.
    • In a broader societal context, it can reflect the importance of nurturing environments—be it familial, educational, or communal—that foster growth and understanding.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its acknowledgment of the transformative power of life and the essential role of nurturing, particularly from mothers.
    • It serves as a reminder that wisdom and strength are cultivated through experiences and relationships, urging us to appreciate the various states of existence that shape our lives.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire individuals to value their journeys, embrace their growth, and recognize the importance of supportive relationships in fostering strength and understanding.
