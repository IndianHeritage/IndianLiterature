आयम् अस्मि जरीतः पश्य मेह विश्वा जातान्य अभ्य अस्मि मह्ना |ऋतस्य मा प्रदिशो वर्धयन्त्य आदर्दिरो भुवना दर्दरीमि ||
ayam asmi jaritaḥ paśya meha viśvā jātāny abhy asmi mahnā | ṛtasya mā pradiśo vardhayanty ādardiro bhuvanā dardarīmi ||
  • ayam asmi jaritaḥ: This phrase translates to "I am the one who is born." It signifies the essence of existence and the acknowledgment of one's own being. In real life, this can be related to self-awareness and understanding one's identity, much like how individuals often seek to understand their purpose in life.
  • paśya meha viśvā: This means "see me, O universe." It reflects a call for recognition and connection with the cosmos. In practical terms, this can be likened to the way people seek validation and understanding from the world around them, whether through relationships, achievements, or contributions to society.
  • jātāny abhy asmi mahnā: This translates to "I am born from the great." It emphasizes the idea of being part of something larger than oneself. This can be seen in real life through community involvement, where individuals contribute to a greater cause, such as environmental conservation or social justice, recognizing their role in the larger tapestry of life.
  • ṛtasya mā pradiśo vardhayanty: This means "may they increase the paths of truth." It highlights the importance of truth and righteousness in life. In everyday scenarios, this can be observed in the pursuit of honesty in personal and professional relationships, where integrity is valued and upheld.
  • ādardiro bhuvanā dardarīmi: This translates to "I am the one who supports the worlds." It signifies a sense of responsibility and support for the universe and its inhabitants. In real life, this can be reflected in acts of kindness, charity, and support for others, where individuals take on the role of nurturers and protectors within their communities.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of existence, self-awareness, and the interconnectedness of all beings. It emphasizes the importance of truth, responsibility, and the recognition of one's role in the universe. In real-life scenarios, these themes are applicable in personal growth, community involvement, and the pursuit of integrity. By embodying the principles of this mantra, individuals can contribute positively to their surroundings and foster a sense of unity and purpose in their lives.
