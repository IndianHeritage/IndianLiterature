यदा वीरस्य रेवतो दुरोणे स्योनशीर अतिथिर आचिकेतत् |सुप्रीतो अग्निः सुधितो दमा आ स विशे दाति वार्यम इयत्यै ||
yadā vīrasya revato duroṇe syonaśīr atithir āciketat | suprīto agniḥ sudhito dama ā sa viśe dāti vāryam iyatyai ||
  • yadā vīrasya revato: This phrase can be translated as "when the hero (vīra) is in the midst of his strength (revata)." It signifies a moment of valor and courage, where an individual is at their peak performance.
  • duroṇe syonaśīr: This translates to "in the difficult situations (duroṇe) where one is not easily defeated (syonaśīr)." It emphasizes the importance of resilience and determination in challenging circumstances.
  • atithir āciketat: This means "the guest (atithi) should be treated with respect (āciketat)." In many cultures, hospitality is a virtue, and this line highlights the significance of honoring guests and strangers.
  • suprīto agniḥ: This translates to "the pleased fire (agniḥ)." Fire is often a symbol of purity and transformation, and when it is pleased, it signifies a harmonious environment.
  • sudhito dama: This means "the well-prepared (sudhito) restraint (dama)." It suggests that self-control and discipline are essential for achieving success and maintaining balance in life.
  • ā sa viśe dāti vāryam: This translates to "that (ā) which gives (dāti) the strength (vāryam) to the community (viśe)." It indicates the importance of collective strength and support within a community.
  • iyatyai: This word can be interpreted as "to those who strive (iyatyai)." It emphasizes the value of effort and perseverance in achieving one's goals.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of courage, resilience, hospitality, self-discipline, and community strength. In real-life scenarios, it can be applied in various contexts such as leadership, teamwork, and personal development. For instance, in a workplace, a leader who embodies these qualities can inspire their team to overcome challenges and work harmoniously. Similarly, in personal relationships, treating guests with respect and maintaining self-control can lead to stronger bonds and a supportive environment. Overall, this mantra serves as a guiding principle for living a balanced and fulfilling life, emphasizing the interconnectedness of individual strength and community support.
