अभि सूयवसंस नय न नवज्वारो अध्वने |पुषन्न इह क्रतुम् विदः ||
abhi sūyavasaṁ naya na navajvāro adhvane | pūṣann iha kratuṁ vidaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • abhi: towards, in the direction of
    • sūyavasaṁ: one who is full of wisdom or knowledge
    • naya: lead, guide
    • na: not
    • navajvāro: new or inexperienced
    • adhvane: on the path, in the journey
    • pūṣann: O nourisher, or one who provides
    • iha: here, in this world
    • kratuṁ: intellect, understanding, or will
    • vidaḥ: know, understand
  • Overall Meaning:
    • This mantra is a prayer for guidance and wisdom.
    • The speaker is asking for direction from a knowledgeable source, avoiding the paths of the inexperienced.
    • It emphasizes the importance of understanding and intellect in navigating life's journey.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, when starting a new project, one might seek guidance from a mentor who has experience in that area, rather than relying solely on their own limited knowledge.
    • Students often look for guidance from teachers or experienced peers when tackling complex subjects, ensuring they are on the right path to understanding.
    • In personal relationships, individuals may seek advice from trusted friends or family members to navigate difficult situations, ensuring they make informed decisions.
  • Conclusion and Significance:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of seeking wisdom and guidance in all aspects of life.
    • It serves as a reminder that while one may have their own ideas and plans, the input of knowledgeable individuals can lead to better outcomes.
    • In today's fast-paced world, where information is abundant but wisdom can be scarce, this mantra encourages individuals to be discerning in their choices and to seek out those who can provide valuable insights.
