अग्नि त्वं अस्मद युयोध्य अमीवाऽ अनाग्नित्रा अभ्य अमन्त कृष्टिः |पुनर अस्मभ्यं सुविताया देव क्षां विश्वेभिर अमृतैः यजत्र ||
agne tvam asmad yuyodhy amīvā anagnitrā abhy amanta kṛṣṭīḥ | punar asmabhyaṁ suvitāya deva kṣāṁ viśvebhir amṛtebhir yajatra ||
  • Agne: This word refers to Agni, the fire god in Vedic literature, symbolizing energy, light, and purity.
  • Tvam: This means "you," addressing Agni directly, indicating a personal relationship with the divine.
  • Asmad: This translates to "us," representing the collective group or community seeking guidance.
  • Yuyodhy: This means "to fight" or "to strive," suggesting a call to action or effort in overcoming challenges.
  • Amīvā: This can be interpreted as "with strength" or "with vigor," emphasizing the need for resilience.
  • Anagnitrā: This term means "without fire," indicating a state of being unlit or uninspired, which is to be avoided.
  • Abhy: This means "towards" or "in the direction of," suggesting a movement or intention towards something positive.
  • Amanta: This translates to "to the end" or "to completion," indicating a goal or purpose.
  • Kṛṣṭīḥ: This refers to "cultivation" or "nurturing," symbolizing growth and development.
  • Punar: This means "again," suggesting renewal or a second chance.
  • Asmabhyaṁ: This translates to "for us," reinforcing the communal aspect of the mantra.
  • Suvitāya: This means "well-nourished" or "well-cultivated," indicating prosperity and abundance.
  • Deva: This refers to "god" or "divine being," emphasizing the spiritual aspect of the plea.
  • Kṣāṁ: This can be interpreted as "to grant" or "to bestow," indicating a request for blessings.
  • Viśvebhir: This means "by all" or "with everyone," suggesting inclusivity and unity.
  • Amṛtebhir: This translates to "immortal" or "eternal," indicating a desire for lasting impact or legacy.
  • Yajatra: This means "in the sacrifice" or "in the offering," highlighting the importance of devotion and service.

Conclusion: This mantra is a powerful invocation to Agni, seeking strength, guidance, and prosperity for the community. It emphasizes the importance of striving together, nurturing growth, and renewing efforts towards a common goal. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios such as teamwork in professional settings, community service, or personal development. By invoking the spirit of Agni, individuals and groups can find the motivation to overcome challenges, cultivate their potential, and work towards a brighter future collectively.
