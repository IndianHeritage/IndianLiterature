मां नो अग्ने दुर्भृतये सच्चैषु देवेद्धेष्व अग्निषु प्र वोचः |मां ते अस्मान दुर्मतयो भ्रिमाच चिद देवस्य सूनो सहसो नशन्त ||
mā no agne durbhṛtaye sacaiṣu deveddheṣv agniṣu pra vocaḥ | mā te asmān durmatayo bhṛmāc cid devasya sūno sahaso naśanta ||
  • mā no agne durbhṛtaye sacaiṣu deveddheṣv agniṣu pra vocaḥ
    • mā no agne: "O Agni, do not let us be led astray." Here, Agni represents the divine fire, symbolizing knowledge, purity, and the guiding force in life.
    • durbhṛtaye: "to the path of misfortune or adversity." This indicates a plea for protection from negative influences or situations.
    • sacaiṣu: "in the company of the righteous." This emphasizes the importance of surrounding oneself with virtuous and wise individuals.
    • deveddheṣv agniṣu pra vocaḥ: "may we be guided by the divine in our actions." This suggests a desire for divine guidance in our endeavors.
  • mā te asmān durmatayo bhṛmāc cid devasya sūno sahaso naśanta
    • mā te asmān: "Do not let us be." This is a direct appeal to the divine for protection.
    • durbhṛtayo: "of ill thoughts or intentions." This refers to the negative mindset that can lead to harmful actions.
    • bhṛmāc: "from the burdens or troubles." This indicates a request to be freed from the weight of negativity.
    • cid devasya sūno: "O son of the divine." This acknowledges the divine lineage and the power of the divine to assist.
    • sahaso naśanta: "may we not perish." This is a plea for survival and protection from harm.

Conclusion:

This mantra from the Rigveda emphasizes the importance of seeking divine guidance and protection from negative influences and thoughts. In real life, it can be applied in various scenarios such as:

  • In personal relationships, it encourages individuals to surround themselves with positive and virtuous people to foster a healthy environment.
  • In professional settings, it serves as a reminder to seek guidance and clarity in decision-making, ensuring that actions are aligned with ethical and moral values.
  • In times of adversity, it acts as a source of strength, urging individuals to seek divine support and maintain a positive mindset.

Overall, this mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the need for divine guidance and the importance of maintaining a positive and virtuous path in life.
