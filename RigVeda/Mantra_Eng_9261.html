मन्त्र: विद्मा ते अग्ने त्रेधा त्रयाणि विद्मा ते धाम विभृतā पुरुत्रा |विद्मा ते नाम परमं गुहा यद विद्मा तं उत्सं यत आगजन्त ||
vidmā te agne tredhā trayāṇi vidmā te dhāma vibhṛtā purutrā | vidmā te nāma paramaṁ guhā yad vidmā tam utsaṁ yata ājagantha ||
  • Translation: The mantra can be translated as: "We know you, O Agni, in three ways; we know your great abode, which is spread everywhere. We know your supreme name, the ultimate secret, which we know as the source from which everything emerges."
  • Explanation of Key Terms:
    • Agni: In Vedic literature, Agni represents fire and is considered a divine force. Fire symbolizes transformation, energy, and purification. In real life, we can relate this to the fire of knowledge or passion that drives individuals to achieve their goals.
    • Tredhā: This term refers to the three aspects or forms of Agni. In a broader sense, it can symbolize the three stages of life: creation, preservation, and destruction. For example, in nature, we see this cycle in the changing seasons.
    • Dhāma: This word means 'abode' or 'place.' It signifies the vastness and omnipresence of Agni. In our lives, this can be seen as the universal energy that connects all living beings.
    • Paramaṁ Guhā: This phrase translates to 'the ultimate secret.' It represents the hidden knowledge or truth that is often sought after in spiritual practices. In real life, this can be likened to the pursuit of wisdom and understanding in various fields, such as science or philosophy.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a personal context, when someone is passionate about their career, they embody the essence of Agni, using their energy to transform their dreams into reality.
    • In nature, the cycle of life and death can be seen as a manifestation of the three forms of Agni, where every ending leads to a new beginning.
    • In education, the quest for knowledge can be viewed as seeking the 'ultimate secret,' where students strive to uncover deeper truths about the world around them.
  • Conclusion: This mantra emphasizes the importance of understanding the divine energy represented by Agni in various forms. It teaches us to recognize the interconnectedness of all things and the transformative power of knowledge and passion. In real life, this can inspire individuals to pursue their goals with fervor, embrace change, and seek deeper truths in their personal and professional lives. The significance of this mantra lies in its ability to guide us towards a more profound understanding of ourselves and the universe.
