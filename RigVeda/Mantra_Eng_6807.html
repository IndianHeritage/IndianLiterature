तत्-तद् अग्निर वयो दधे यथा-यथा कृपण्यति |ऊर्जाहुतिर वसूनां शं च योश च मयो दधे विश्वस्यै देवहूत्यै नभन्ताम् अन्यके समे ||
tat-tad agnir vayo dadhe yathā-yathā kṛpaṇyati | ūrjāhutir vasūnāṁ śaṁ ca yoś ca mayo dadhe viśvasyai devahūtyai nabhantām anyake same ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • tat-tad: that, it
    • agnir: fire
    • vayo: of the wind
    • dadhe: establishes
    • yathā-yathā: as, wherever
    • kṛpaṇyati: it is generous
    • ūrjāhutir: the offering of energy
    • vasūnāṁ: of the wealth
    • śaṁ: peace
    • ca: and
    • yoś: of the beings
    • ca: and
    • mayo: of the divine
    • dadhe: establishes
    • viśvasyai: for the universe
    • devahūtyai: for the divine offering
    • nabhantām: in the sky
    • anyake: other
    • same: in harmony
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the interconnectedness of fire (agnir) and wind (vayo), suggesting that they work together in the universe.
    • The phrase "yathā-yathā kṛpaṇyati" implies that wherever there is generosity or abundance, it is established by these natural elements.
    • The mention of "ūrjāhutir" refers to the offering of energy, indicating that energy is a vital force that sustains life and prosperity.
    • It emphasizes the importance of peace ("śaṁ") and harmony ("same") in the universe, suggesting that these qualities are essential for the well-being of all beings.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In nature, the relationship between fire and wind can be observed in how wildfires spread. The wind can either help a fire grow or help extinguish it, symbolizing the duality of forces in life.
    • In human relationships, generosity and kindness can create a harmonious environment, much like how energy flows in nature. Acts of kindness can lead to a peaceful community.
    • In environmental sustainability, the balance of natural elements is crucial. For instance, renewable energy sources like wind and solar power are essential for a sustainable future, reflecting the mantra's emphasis on energy and harmony.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of natural elements and their roles in establishing harmony and abundance in the universe.
    • It serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of all things and the importance of maintaining balance and peace in our lives.
    • In real-life scenarios, it encourages us to be generous, to seek harmony in our relationships, and to respect the natural world, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling and peaceful existence.
