न तं अंहो न दुरितानि मार्त्यम इन्द्रावरुणा न तपः कुतश्चन |यस्य देवाः गच्छथो वीथो अध्वरं न तं मार्तस्य नशते परिहृतिः ||
na tam aṁho na duritāni martyam indrāvaruṇā na tapaḥ kutaś cana | yasya devā gacchatho vītho adhvaraṁ na tam martasya naśate parihvṛtiḥ ||
  • na tam aṁho - This phrase can be interpreted as "not that which is far away" or "not that which is unattainable." It suggests a connection to the divine or the ultimate truth that is accessible to all.
  • na duritāni martyam - This translates to "not the misfortunes of mortals." It emphasizes the idea that the divine can help transcend human suffering and difficulties.
  • indrāvaruṇā - Referring to the deities Indra and Varuna, who symbolize strength and cosmic order, respectively. They represent the forces that govern the universe and human life.
  • na tapaḥ kutaś cana - This means "not from austerity or penance." It suggests that divine blessings are not solely the result of rigorous spiritual practices but can be attained through sincere devotion.
  • yasya devā gacchatho - This phrase means "to whom the gods go." It indicates that the divine presence is drawn to those who are righteous and virtuous.
  • vītho adhvaraṁ - This can be interpreted as "the path of the sacred ritual." It signifies the importance of following a righteous path and engaging in spiritual practices that align with cosmic order.
  • na tam martasya - This translates to "not for the mortal." It implies that certain divine truths or blessings are beyond the reach of ordinary human existence.
  • naśate parihvṛtiḥ - This means "the protection that does not perish." It suggests that divine protection is eternal and can shield individuals from the trials of life.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking divine guidance and protection in the face of life's challenges. It emphasizes that true connection with the divine is not limited to rigorous austerities but is accessible to all who seek righteousness and virtue. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied during times of distress or uncertainty, reminding individuals to seek divine support and to follow a path of integrity and devotion. It serves as a reminder that while human life is fraught with difficulties, there exists a higher power that offers protection and guidance, encouraging individuals to remain steadfast in their spiritual journey.
