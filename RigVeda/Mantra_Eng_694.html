अस्मा इद उ प्रय इव प्र यंसी भराम्य आङ्गूषम् बाधे सुवृक्ति |इन्द्राय हृदा मनसा मनिषा प्रत्नाय पत्ये धियो मरजयन्त ||
asmā id u praya iva pra yaṁsi bharāmy āṅgūṣam bādhe suvṛkti | indrāya hṛdā manasā manīṣā pratnāya patye dhiyo marjayanta ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • asmā: from us
    • id: this
    • u: indeed
    • praya: to the goal
    • iva: like
    • pra: forward
    • yaṁsi: you (plural) will bring
    • bharāmy: I bear or carry
    • āṅgūṣam: the finger (symbolizing action)
    • bādhe: to bind or connect
    • suvṛkti: good thought or intention
    • indrāya: to Indra (the deity representing strength and power)
    • hṛdā: with the heart
    • manasā: with the mind
    • manīṣā: with wisdom or intellect
    • pratnāya: for the purpose of offering
    • patye: to the lord or master
    • dhiyo: the intellects or thoughts
    • marjayanta: they purify or cleanse
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of collective effort and intention.
    • It suggests that through our actions and thoughts, we can connect with higher powers (like Indra) and achieve our goals.
    • The reference to "binding with good thought" indicates that our intentions should be pure and directed towards positive outcomes.
    • It highlights the role of the heart and mind in aligning our actions with our higher purpose.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a team project, when all members work together with a shared vision and positive intentions, they are more likely to succeed.
    • In personal development, setting clear goals and aligning them with one's values can lead to a more fulfilling life.
    • In community service, when individuals come together with a common purpose and good intentions, they can create significant positive change.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the power of collective intention and the importance of aligning our thoughts and actions with our higher goals.
    • It is applicable in various aspects of life, including personal growth, teamwork, and community engagement.
    • By focusing on good intentions and connecting with our inner wisdom, we can navigate challenges and achieve our aspirations.
