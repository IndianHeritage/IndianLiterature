दीर्घतन्तुर बृहदुक्षायम अग्निः सहस्रस्तरीः शतनीतः ऋभ्वा |द्यमान द्यमत्सु नृभिर मृज्यमानः सुमित्रेषु दीदयो देवयत्सु ||
dīrghatantur bṛhadukṣāyam agniḥ sahasrastarīḥ śatanītha ṛbhvā | dyumān dyumatsu nṛbhir mṛjyamānaḥ sumitreṣu dīdayo devayatsu ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • dīrghatantur: Long-lasting or enduring
    • bṛhadukṣāyam: Great and powerful
    • agniḥ: Fire
    • sahasrastarīḥ: With a thousand rays
    • śatanītha: With a hundred paths
    • ṛbhvā: With strength or might
    • dyumān: Shining or radiant
    • dyumatsu: Among the shining ones
    • nṛbhir: By men or humans
    • mṛjyamānaḥ: Being worshipped or revered
    • sumitreṣu: In the company of good friends
    • dīdayo: Bestowing or granting
    • devayatsu: Among the divine beings
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra invokes Agni, the fire god, symbolizing purity, transformation, and the divine presence in rituals.
    • The phrase "dīrghatantur bṛhadukṣāyam" emphasizes the enduring and powerful nature of Agni, suggesting that the divine energy is everlasting and impactful.
    • "Sahasrastarīḥ" refers to Agni's thousand rays, symbolizing the various forms of energy and light that fire represents in our lives.
    • The mention of "śatanītha" indicates the many paths that one can take in life, guided by the light of knowledge and wisdom that Agni provides.
    • "Dyumān dyumatsu" highlights the radiance of Agni, which can be seen as a metaphor for enlightenment and clarity in human endeavors.
    • The phrase "mṛjyamānaḥ sumitreṣu" suggests that Agni is revered by humans, especially in the company of good friends, indicating the importance of community and support in spiritual practices.
    • Finally, "dīdayo devayatsu" signifies that Agni bestows blessings among the divine, reinforcing the idea that fire is a bridge between the earthly and the divine realms.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of Agni as a source of light, guidance, and transformation in our lives.
    • In real-life scenarios, it can be applied to moments of change, where one seeks clarity and strength to navigate through challenges.
    • The mantra encourages individuals to surround themselves with supportive communities, emphasizing the role of friendship and collaboration in achieving personal and spiritual growth.
    • Overall, it serves as a reminder of the divine presence in our lives and the transformative power of fire, both literally and metaphorically.
