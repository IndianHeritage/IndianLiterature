अक्षाहनो नहत्यानोत सोम्या इष्कृतुध्वं रशनाऽ ओत पिंशत |अष्टावन्धुरं वहताभितो रथं येन देवासो अनयन्न अभि प्रियं ||
akṣānaho nahyatanota somyā iṣkṛṇudhvaṁ raśanā ota piṁśata | aṣṭāvandhuraṁ vahatābhito rathaṁ yena devāso anayann abhi priyam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • akṣānaho: O, the one who is unbreakable or eternal.
    • nahyatanota: We do not let go or abandon.
    • somyā: O, the one who is soothing or pleasant.
    • iṣkṛṇudhvaṁ: You should perform or create.
    • raśanā: The rope or bond.
    • ota: To be tied or bound.
    • piṁśata: To be made firm or strong.
    • aṣṭāvandhuraṁ: The eightfold path or direction.
    • vahatābhito: Carrying or transporting from all sides.
    • rathaṁ: The chariot.
    • yena: By which.
    • devāso: The gods or divine beings.
    • anayann: Leading or guiding.
    • abhi: Towards or in favor of.
    • priyam: The beloved or dear.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks to the eternal nature of existence and the importance of not abandoning our connections and responsibilities.
    • The soothing aspect of life is emphasized, suggesting that we should create bonds that are strong and supportive.
    • The mention of the "eightfold path" can be interpreted as a guide to living a balanced and harmonious life.
    • The chariot symbolizes the journey of life, which is guided by divine forces, leading us towards what is dear and valuable.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In personal relationships, this mantra can remind us to nurture our bonds with family and friends, ensuring they remain strong and supportive.
    • In a professional setting, it can be applied to teamwork, where collaboration and mutual support lead to success.
    • In spiritual practices, the "eightfold path" can be related to various philosophies that promote ethical living and mindfulness.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of eternal bonds and the importance of nurturing them in our lives.
    • It serves as a reminder that our journey is guided by higher forces, and we should strive to create strong, supportive connections.
    • In real life, applying the principles of this mantra can lead to more fulfilling relationships, successful teamwork, and a balanced approach to life.
