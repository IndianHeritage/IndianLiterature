आध्रेण चित तद्वेकं चकार सिंह्यं चित पेट्वेन जग्हान | अव श्रक्तीर वेश्यावृश्चद इन्द्रः प्रायच्छद विश्वा भोजना सुदासे ||
ādhreṇa cit tad v ekaṁ cakāra siṁhyaṁ cit petvenā jaghāna | ava sraktīr veśyāvṛścad indraḥ prāyacchad viśvā bhojanā sudāse ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ādhreṇa: By the support or foundation
    • cit: Consciousness or awareness
    • tad: That
    • v: Indeed
    • ekaṁ: One
    • cakāra: Created or manifested
    • siṁhyaṁ: Lion-like or powerful
    • cit: Consciousness
    • petvenā: By falling or descending
    • jaghāna: Struck or defeated
    • ava: Down or away
    • sraktīr: Flowing or streaming
    • veśyāvṛścad: From the courtesan's abode
    • indraḥ: Indra, the king of gods
    • prāyacchad: Gave or bestowed
    • viśvā: All or universal
    • bhojanā: Food or nourishment
    • sudāse: To the well-fed or nourished
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the creation and manifestation of a powerful consciousness that can overcome challenges.
    • The reference to "lion-like" suggests strength and courage, qualities necessary to face adversities.
    • The act of "striking down" indicates the removal of obstacles or negativity that may hinder progress.
    • Indra, as the king of gods, symbolizes authority and the ability to provide sustenance and support to all beings.
    • The mention of "courtesan's abode" may imply the complexities of life and the need to navigate through them wisely.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, one may face challenges that require a strong and courageous approach, akin to the "lion-like" strength mentioned in the mantra.
    • When dealing with personal struggles, the idea of overcoming obstacles resonates with individuals who strive to rise above their circumstances.
    • In community service, providing nourishment and support to those in need reflects the essence of Indra's role in bestowing sustenance.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of strength, awareness, and the ability to overcome challenges in life.
    • It serves as a reminder that with the right mindset and support, one can navigate through life's complexities and emerge victorious.
    • In practical terms, it encourages individuals to cultivate courage and resilience, ensuring they are well-equipped to face adversities and support others in their journey.
