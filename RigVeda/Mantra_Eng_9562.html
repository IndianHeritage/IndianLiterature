हृदā तषṭेṣu मनसो जवेṣu यद् ब्राह्मणाः संयजन्ते सखायः |अत्राह त्वं विह जहर वेद्याभिर ओहब्रह्माणो विह चरन्त्यु त्वे ||
hṛdā taṣṭeṣu manaso javeṣu yad brāhmaṇāḥ saṁyajante sakhāyaḥ | atrāha tvaṁ vi jahur vedyābhir ohabrahmāṇo vi caranty u tve ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • hṛdā: with the heart
    • taṣṭeṣu: in the steadfast
    • manaso: of the mind
    • javeṣu: in the swift
    • yad: that which
    • brāhmaṇāḥ: the wise or learned ones
    • saṁyajante: they unite or come together
    • sakhāyaḥ: as friends
    • atrāha: here indeed
    • tvaṁ: you
    • vi: indeed
    • jahur: they have offered
    • vedyābhir: with the knowledge of the Vedas
    • ohabrahmāṇo: the great seers or sages
    • vi: indeed
    • caranty: they move or wander
    • u: and
    • tve: you
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of unity and collaboration among wise individuals.
    • It suggests that true understanding and wisdom come from the heart and mind working together.
    • The phrase "brāhmaṇāḥ saṁyajante sakhāyaḥ" indicates that learned individuals come together as friends, highlighting the value of community and support.
    • The reference to "vedyābhir ohabrahmāṇo" points to the role of ancient wisdom and knowledge in guiding actions and decisions.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace, collaboration among team members can lead to innovative solutions, much like the unity described in the mantra.
    • In educational settings, students working together on projects can enhance their learning experience, reflecting the idea of coming together as friends for a common goal.
    • Community service initiatives often bring together individuals with diverse skills and knowledge, demonstrating the power of collective effort.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of unity, collaboration, and the pursuit of knowledge.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, such as teamwork in professional environments, collaborative learning in education, and community engagement.
    • By fostering relationships based on mutual respect and shared goals, individuals can achieve greater wisdom and understanding, ultimately leading to a more harmonious society.
