ऋतेनाद्रिं व्यसन भिदन्तः समाङ्गिरसो नवन्त गोभिः |शुनं नरः परि षदन्न उषासम आविः स्वर अभवज जाते अग्नौ ||
ṛtenādriṁ vy asan bhidantaḥ sam aṅgiraso navanta gobhiḥ | śunaṁ naraḥ pari ṣadann uṣāsam āviḥ svar abhavaj jāte agnau ||
  • ṛtenādriṁ vy asan bhidantaḥ: This phrase can be translated as "They who break through the mountain of truth." Here, "ṛta" refers to the cosmic order or truth, and "adri" means mountain. This signifies the pursuit of truth and the breaking of barriers that prevent understanding.
  • sam aṅgiraso navanta gobhiḥ: This translates to "The Angirasas, with their new thoughts, are coming with the cows." The Angirasas are a group of sages known for their wisdom. "Navanta" implies newness or innovation, while "gobhiḥ" refers to cows, which symbolize wealth and nourishment in Vedic culture. This suggests that wisdom and new ideas bring prosperity.
  • śunaṁ naraḥ pari ṣadann uṣāsam: This means "The man who is devoted to the dawn." "Uṣās" refers to the dawn, symbolizing new beginnings and hope. This line emphasizes the importance of being dedicated to new opportunities and the potential that each day brings.
  • āviḥ svar abhavaj jāte agnau: This translates to "The one who is born in the fire of the divine." "Agnau" refers to fire, which is often associated with purification and transformation. This line suggests that through challenges and trials, one can achieve a higher state of being or enlightenment.

  • Significance: This mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking truth, embracing new ideas, and recognizing the potential of each new day. It highlights the journey of self-discovery and the importance of wisdom in achieving prosperity.
  • Real-life Application: In today's world, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as:
    • In education, where the pursuit of knowledge and truth is paramount.
    • In personal development, where embracing new ideas and opportunities can lead to growth.
    • In business, where innovation and adaptability are crucial for success.
  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a reminder to break through barriers, seek wisdom, and embrace new beginnings. It encourages individuals to transform challenges into opportunities for growth and enlightenment, making it relevant in various aspects of life.
