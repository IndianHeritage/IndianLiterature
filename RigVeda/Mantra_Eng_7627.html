आयं त एमी तंवा पुरस्ताद विश्वे देवाः अभि मां यान्ति पश्चात् |यदा मह्यम् दीधरो भागम् इन्द्राद् इन् मया कृणवो वीर्याणि ||
ayaṁ ta emi tanvā purastād viśve devā abhi mā yanti paścāt | yadā mahyaṁ dīdharo bhāgam indrād in mayā kṛṇavo vīryāṇi ||
  • ayaṁ ta emi tanvā purastād: This phrase can be interpreted as "I approach you with my body in front." It signifies the act of seeking connection or guidance from the divine. In real life, this can be likened to how individuals often seek help or inspiration from mentors, leaders, or spiritual figures when facing challenges.
  • viśve devā abhi mā yanti paścāt: This translates to "All the gods are coming to me from behind." It suggests that divine support and blessings are always present, even if not immediately visible. In everyday life, this can be seen in moments of unexpected help or support from friends, family, or even strangers during difficult times.
  • yadā mahyaṁ dīdharo bhāgam: This means "When the divine grants me strength." It emphasizes the importance of receiving strength and courage from a higher power. For instance, athletes often speak about feeling a surge of energy or motivation during competitions, which can be interpreted as a divine influence pushing them to perform better.
  • indrād in mayā kṛṇavo vīryāṇi: This translates to "I create strength from Indra." Indra, in Vedic literature, is often associated with power and strength. This line suggests that one can harness divine energy to cultivate personal strength. In practical terms, this can be seen in how individuals draw upon their faith or spiritual beliefs to overcome obstacles in their lives.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking divine guidance and strength in our lives. It highlights the importance of recognizing the support that surrounds us, even when it is not immediately apparent. In real-life scenarios, this can be applicable in various situations such as during personal struggles, professional challenges, or moments of self-doubt. By acknowledging the presence of divine support and drawing upon it, individuals can find the courage and strength to navigate their paths effectively. This mantra serves as a reminder that we are never alone in our endeavors, and that divine energy can empower us to achieve our goals.
