न तस्य विद्म तदु षु प्र वोचत युवां ह यद युवत्याः क्षेति योनिषु |प्रियोश्रियस्य वृषभस्य रेतिनो गृहम् गमेमा अश्विनां तदुश्मसि ||
na tasya vidma tad u ṣu pra vocata yuvā ha yad yuvatyāḥ kṣeti yoniṣu | priyosriyasya vṛṣabhasya retino gṛhaṁ gamemāśvinā tad uśmasi ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • na: not
    • tasya: of that
    • vidma: we know
    • tad: that
    • u: indeed
    • ṣu: in whom
    • pra: speak
    • vocata: let us speak
    • yuvā: young
    • ha: indeed
    • yad: what
    • yuvatyāḥ: of the young woman
    • kṣeti: dwells
    • yoniṣu: in the wombs
    • priyosriyasya: of the beloved and prosperous
    • vṛṣabhasya: of the bull (symbolizing strength)
    • retino: of the seed
    • gṛham: house
    • gamemā: may we go
    • āśvinā: O Ashvins (the divine twin horsemen)
    • tad: that
    • uśmasi: may we attain
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra expresses a quest for knowledge and understanding about the divine and the natural world.
    • The speaker acknowledges a lack of knowledge about certain aspects of existence, particularly concerning the young woman and the nature of creation.
    • The invocation of the Ashvins, who are known for their healing and benevolent qualities, suggests a desire for guidance and blessings in matters of fertility and prosperity.
    • The reference to the "house" of the beloved and prosperous indicates a longing for a nurturing and fruitful environment, whether in personal relationships or in broader societal contexts.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern life, this mantra can be related to the journey of understanding relationships, especially in the context of marriage and family. Couples often seek guidance and blessings for a harmonious and prosperous life together.
    • In the context of fertility treatments, individuals may invoke divine blessings for success in conceiving, paralleling the mantra's plea for understanding and support in matters of creation.
    • In a broader societal sense, communities may come together to seek blessings for prosperity and well-being, reflecting the collective desire for a nurturing environment.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the importance of seeking knowledge and understanding in our lives, particularly in relationships and creation.
    • It emphasizes the role of divine guidance in achieving prosperity and harmony, reminding us to remain humble in our quest for understanding.
    • In real-life scenarios, the principles embodied in this mantra can inspire individuals and communities to seek blessings for love, fertility, and overall well-being, fostering a sense of connection with the divine and with each other.
