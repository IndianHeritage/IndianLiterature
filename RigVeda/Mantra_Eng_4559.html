त्वं ह नु त्यद अदमायो दस्यूम् एकः कृष्णीर अवनोः आर्याय |अस्ति स्विन नु वीर्यं तत त इन्द्र न स्विद अस्ति तद ऋतुथा वि वोचः ||
tvaṁ ha nu tyad adamāyo dasyūm̐r ekaḥ kṛṣṭīr avanor āryāya | asti svin nu vīrya1ṁ tat ta indra na svid asti tad ṛtuthā vi vocaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • tvaṁ: You
    • ha: indeed
    • nu: now
    • tyad: that
    • adamāyo: invincible
    • dasyūm̐r: the enemies or obstacles
    • ekaḥ: alone
    • kṛṣṭīr: the strength or power
    • avanor: of the earth
    • āryāya: for the noble or the worthy
    • asti: there is
    • svin: indeed
    • nu: now
    • vīryaṁ: valor or strength
    • tat: that
    • ta: that
    • indra: O Indra (the deity)
    • na: not
    • svid: where
    • asti: is
    • tad: that
    • ṛtuthā: in truth
    • vi: indeed
    • vocaḥ: we speak
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the divine strength and valor of Indra, asking for protection against enemies and obstacles.
    • The phrase "invincible" suggests a plea for resilience and the ability to overcome challenges.
    • It emphasizes the importance of strength and nobility in facing adversities, highlighting that true power lies in righteousness.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a competitive work environment, individuals often face challenges that can be likened to "enemies." This mantra can inspire one to seek inner strength and resilience to overcome these challenges.
    • In sports, athletes often face formidable opponents. The invocation of strength and valor can motivate them to perform at their best, embodying the spirit of the mantra.
    • In personal life, when facing difficulties such as health issues or financial troubles, this mantra serves as a reminder to remain strong and noble in one’s actions and decisions.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine support in overcoming obstacles and embodying strength and nobility.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal struggles to professional challenges, reminding individuals to seek inner strength and maintain integrity.
    • Ultimately, it encourages a mindset of resilience, valor, and righteousness, essential for navigating the complexities of life.
