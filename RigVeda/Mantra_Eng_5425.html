मां नो अजñातā वृजनā दुराध्यो3 मांशिवासो अव क्रमुः |त्वया वयम् प्रवतः शाश्वतीर अपो 'ति शूर तरामसि ||
mā no ajñātā vṛjanā durādhyo3 māśivāso ava kramuḥ | tvayā vayam pravataḥ śaśvatīr apo 'ti śūra tarāmasi ||
  • mā no ajñātā vṛjanā durādhyo: This phrase can be translated as "May we not be led astray by the unknown or the difficult." Here, "mā" means "not," "ajñātā" refers to "unknown," "vṛjanā" means "to lead astray," and "durādhyo" signifies "difficult" or "hard to understand." This part of the mantra emphasizes the importance of clarity and guidance in our lives, urging us to seek knowledge and understanding rather than being misled by confusion or complexity.
  • māśivāso ava kramuḥ: This translates to "May we not be hindered in our progress." "Māśivāso" can be interpreted as "not hindered," and "ava kramuḥ" means "in our progress" or "in our journey." This line encourages individuals to pursue their goals and aspirations without obstacles, highlighting the significance of perseverance and determination in achieving success.
  • tvayā vayam pravataḥ: This means "We are devoted to you." "Tvayā" refers to "you," and "vayam" means "we," while "pravataḥ" signifies "devoted" or "surrendered." This part of the mantra expresses a sense of devotion and surrender to a higher power or divine force, suggesting that true strength and guidance come from this connection.
  • śaśvatīr apo 'ti śūra tarāmasi: This can be translated as "May we attain the eternal waters of strength." "Śaśvatīr" means "eternal," "apo" refers to "waters," "ati" means "beyond," "śūra" signifies "strength," and "tarāmasi" means "to attain." This line symbolizes the quest for everlasting wisdom and strength, akin to seeking a source of inspiration and resilience that transcends the challenges of life.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a profound message about the importance of seeking knowledge, remaining steadfast in our pursuits, and fostering a connection with a higher power. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various contexts, such as in education, where students are encouraged to seek clarity in their studies and not be misled by distractions. In professional settings, it serves as a reminder to stay focused on goals and overcome obstacles with determination. Ultimately, the mantra emphasizes the significance of spiritual devotion and the pursuit of eternal wisdom, guiding individuals toward a fulfilling and purposeful life.
