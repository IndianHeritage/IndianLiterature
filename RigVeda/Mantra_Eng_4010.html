प्र व एते सुउजो याम्न्न इष्टये नीचीर अमुष्मै यम्य ऋतावृद्धः |सुउयन्तुभिः सर्वशासैर अभिषुभिः कृविर नामानि प्रवणेमुशायति ||
pra va ete suyujo yāmann iṣṭaye nīcīr amuṣmai yamya ṛtāvṛdhaḥ | suyantubhiḥ sarvaśāsair abhīśubhiḥ krivir nāmāni pravaṇe muṣāyati ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • pra va: to proclaim or announce
    • ete: these
    • suyujo: well-connected or well-joined
    • yāmann: to whom
    • iṣṭaye: for the purpose of worship or offering
    • nīcīr: low or humble
    • amuṣmai: to that one
    • yamya: who is to be invoked
    • ṛtāvṛdhaḥ: who is the embodiment of truth
    • suyantubhiḥ: with good intentions
    • sarvaśāsair: with all sciences or knowledge
    • abhīśubhiḥ: with auspiciousness
    • krivir: the actions or deeds
    • nāmāni: names
    • pravaṇe: in the act of invoking
    • muṣāyati: to bring forth or manifest
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of invoking the divine with sincerity and good intentions.
    • It suggests that when we connect with the divine or higher powers, we should do so with humility and truthfulness.
    • The phrase "well-connected" indicates the significance of relationships and community in spiritual practices.
    • It also highlights the role of knowledge and auspiciousness in our actions, suggesting that our deeds should be informed by wisdom and positivity.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community gathering, when people come together to celebrate a festival, they often invoke blessings with sincerity, reflecting the essence of this mantra.
    • In personal life, when making important decisions, individuals may seek guidance through prayer or meditation, embodying the humility and truthfulness emphasized in the mantra.
    • In educational settings, teachers and students can apply the principles of knowledge and auspiciousness by fostering a supportive and positive learning environment.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of intention, humility, and truth in our spiritual and everyday actions.
    • It encourages individuals to connect with the divine and each other in meaningful ways, fostering a sense of community and shared purpose.
    • By applying the teachings of this mantra in real life, we can cultivate a more harmonious and fulfilling existence, both personally and collectively.
