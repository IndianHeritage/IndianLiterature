स मन्दस्वा ह्यनु जोषम् उग्र प्र त्वा यज्ञास इमे अशनुवन्तु |प्रेमे हवासाḥ पुरुहूतम अस्मे आ त्वेयं धीर अवसा इन्द्र यम्याः ||
sa mandasvā hy anu joṣam ugra pra tvā yajñāsa ime aśnuvantu | preme havāsaḥ puruhūtam asme ā tveyaṁ dhīr avasa indra yamyāḥ ||
  • sa mandasvā hy anu joṣam: This phrase can be translated as "May we be filled with the essence of the divine." The term "mandasvā" refers to a state of being filled or imbued with something positive, while "anu joṣam" suggests a following or a pursuit of that essence.
  • ugra pra tvā yajñāsa ime aśnuvantu: Here, "ugra" means powerful or fierce, and "pra tvā" refers to the invocation of a deity or divine force. "Yajñāsa" relates to sacrifices or offerings, and "ime aśnuvantu" implies that we may partake in these offerings. This line emphasizes the importance of engaging with the divine through rituals and offerings.
  • preme havāsaḥ puruhūtam: "Preme" translates to love or devotion, while "havāsaḥ" refers to offerings or sacrifices. "Puruhūtam" is a name for the divine, often associated with the god Indra. This line suggests that our offerings should be filled with love and devotion towards the divine.
  • asme ā tveyaṁ dhīr avasa indra yamyāḥ: "Asme" means "to us," and "ā tveyaṁ" translates to "may you come to us." "Dhīr" refers to wisdom or understanding, while "avasa" means to dwell or reside. "Indra yamyāḥ" invokes Indra, the king of gods, suggesting that we seek his presence and wisdom in our lives.

  • Significance: This mantra encapsulates the essence of devotion, the importance of rituals, and the pursuit of divine wisdom. It emphasizes that through love and offerings, one can connect with the divine and seek guidance.
  • Real-life Application: In daily life, this mantra can inspire individuals to engage in acts of kindness and service, viewing them as offerings to the divine. For example, volunteering for community service can be seen as a way to express love and devotion, akin to a modern-day yajña (sacrifice). Additionally, it encourages individuals to seek wisdom and guidance in their decisions, fostering a deeper connection with their spiritual beliefs.
