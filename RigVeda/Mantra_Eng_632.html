शचीव इन्द्र पुरुकृत द्युमत्तम तवेद इदं अभितश चेकिते वसु |अतः संगृभ्याभिभूत आ भर मा त्वायतो जरितुः कामम् ऊनयीः ||
śacīva indra purukṛd dyumattama taved idam abhitaś cekite vasu | ataḥ saṁgṛbhyābhibhūta ā bhara mā tvāyato jarituḥ kāmam ūnayīḥ ||
  • śacīva indra: This phrase refers to Indra, the king of the gods in Vedic literature, who is often associated with strength, power, and the ability to overcome obstacles. The term "śacīva" can be interpreted as "the one who possesses strength" or "the powerful one."
  • purukṛd dyumattama: "Purukṛd" means "the one who performs great deeds," while "dyumattama" translates to "the most radiant" or "the one who shines brightly." Together, this suggests that Indra is not only powerful but also a source of light and inspiration.
  • taved idam abhitaś cekite vasu: This line can be understood as a call to Indra to bestow his blessings and wealth ("vasu") upon the devotees. "Taved" implies a request for action, while "abhitaś" means "from all sides," indicating a desire for comprehensive support and protection.
  • ataḥ saṁgṛbhyābhibhūta: "Ataḥ" means "therefore," and "saṁgṛbhyābhibhūta" suggests gathering or accumulating strength. This indicates that the speaker is seeking to harness the power of Indra to overcome challenges.
  • ā bhara mā tvāyato: This phrase translates to "bring forth" or "fill me with." It is a plea for Indra to provide strength and support in times of need.
  • jarituḥ kāmam ūnayīḥ: "Jarituḥ" refers to aging or decline, while "kāmam" means "desire" or "wish." "Ūnayīḥ" can be interpreted as "to remove" or "to alleviate." This line expresses a desire to overcome the limitations of aging and to fulfill one's aspirations.

  • Significance: This mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking divine support and strength in overcoming life's challenges. It reflects the belief that with the help of a higher power, individuals can achieve their goals and navigate difficulties.
  • Real-life Application: In contemporary life, this mantra can be applied in various situations, such as during personal struggles, professional challenges, or health issues. For instance, someone facing a career setback might recite this mantra to invoke strength and guidance from a higher power, encouraging them to persevere and find new opportunities.
  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness between human effort and divine assistance. It encourages individuals to seek strength from the universe while actively working towards their goals. By acknowledging the need for support, one can cultivate resilience and determination in the face of adversity.
