इमं स्तोमं अरहते जातवेदसे रथम इव सम महेमाऽ मनिषया |भद्रā हि नः प्रमतिर अस्य संसद्य अग्ने सख्ये मा ऋषामā वयं तव ||
imaṁ stomam arhate jātavedase ratham iva sam mahemā manīṣayā | bhadrā hi naḥ pramatir asya saṁsady agne sakhye mā riṣāmā vayaṁ tava ||
  • imaṁ stomam arhate: This phrase can be translated as "this hymn is worthy." It emphasizes the importance of the hymn being offered to the divine, suggesting that it holds significant value and should be respected.
  • jātavedase: Referring to Agni, the fire god, this term means "born of the sacrifice." Agni is often seen as a mediator between humans and the divine, representing the transformative power of fire.
  • ratham iva sam mahemā manīṣayā: This can be interpreted as "like a chariot, may we be united in thought." The chariot symbolizes strength and movement, indicating a desire for unity and progress in our endeavors.
  • bhadrā hi naḥ pramatir asya saṁsady: This translates to "may our understanding be auspicious." It highlights the importance of having a clear and positive mindset, which is essential for achieving success in any venture.
  • agne sakhye mā riṣāmā vayaṁ tava: This means "O Agni, may we not be angry with you." It reflects a plea for harmony and goodwill, acknowledging the need for a positive relationship with the divine.

In conclusion, this mantra from the Rigveda emphasizes the significance of respect, unity, and positive intention in our lives. It serves as a reminder that our thoughts and actions should be aligned with our higher purpose, fostering a harmonious relationship with the divine and with each other. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as:


  • In personal relationships, promoting understanding and goodwill among friends and family.
  • In professional settings, encouraging teamwork and collaboration to achieve common goals.
  • In spiritual practices, fostering a connection with the divine through respectful offerings and intentions.

Ultimately, the mantra teaches us that by cultivating a positive mindset and harmonious relationships, we can navigate life's challenges more effectively and achieve our aspirations.
