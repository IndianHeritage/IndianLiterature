प्र सोमासो विपश्चितोऽपां न यन्त्यूर्मयः |वनानि महिषा इव ||
pra somāso vipaścito 'pāṁ na yanty ūrmayaḥ | vanāni mahiṣā iva ||
  • Mantra: "pra somāso vipaścito 'pāṁ na yanty ūrmayaḥ | vanāni mahiṣā iva ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • pra: before, in front of
    • somāso: those who are endowed with the essence of Soma (a sacred drink)
    • vipaścito: wise, knowledgeable
    • 'pāṁ: the waves, the currents
    • na: not
    • yanty: go, move
    • ūrmayaḥ: the waves, the billows
    • vanāni: forests, woods
    • mahiṣā: like the buffaloes
    • iva: like, as
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the wise individuals who are not swayed by the tumultuous waves of life, much like how buffaloes move through the forests with strength and purpose.
    • The essence of Soma symbolizes the divine knowledge and enlightenment that guides individuals through challenges.
    • The phrase "na yanty ūrmayaḥ" suggests that those who possess wisdom and understanding remain unaffected by external disturbances, akin to how buffaloes navigate through dense forests without losing their way.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate environment, a wise leader remains calm and composed during a crisis, guiding their team through challenges without being overwhelmed by stress or chaos.
    • In personal life, an individual facing difficulties, such as financial troubles or health issues, can draw upon their inner strength and wisdom to navigate through these challenges without succumbing to despair.
    • Students preparing for exams can relate to this mantra by focusing on their studies and not getting distracted by the pressures and anxieties that come with competition.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of wisdom and inner strength in overcoming life's challenges.
    • It teaches us that by cultivating knowledge and understanding, we can remain steadfast and unaffected by the external turmoil around us.
    • In real life, applying the principles of this mantra can lead to better decision-making, resilience in the face of adversity, and a more purposeful approach to navigating through life's complexities.
