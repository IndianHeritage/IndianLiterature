यस्त्वा हृदाऽकीरिणा मन्यमानोऽमर्त्यम् मर्त्यो जोहवीमि |जातवेदो यशोऽस्मासु धेहि प्रजाभिर अग्ने अमृतत्वम् अश्यां ||
yas tvā hṛdā kīriṇā manyamāno 'martyam martyo johavīmi | jātavedo yaśo asmāsu dhehi prajābhir agne amṛtatvam aśyām ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yas: who
    • tvā: you
    • hṛdā: with the heart
    • kīriṇā: by the one who is praised
    • manyamāno: considering
    • 'martyam: the mortal
    • martyo: the mortal
    • johavīmi: I invoke
    • jātavedo: O Agni, the one who knows all births
    • yaśo: fame
    • asmāsu: in us
    • dhehi: bestow
    • prajābhir: with progeny
    • agne: O Fire
    • amṛtatvam: immortality
    • aśyām: grant
  • Overall Meaning: This mantra is a prayer to Agni, the divine fire, asking for blessings of immortality and fame. The speaker acknowledges the mortal nature of human beings and seeks the grace of Agni to bestow upon them the qualities of eternal life and renown through their progeny.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In many cultures, fire symbolizes transformation and purification. For instance, in Hindu rituals, Agni is invoked during ceremonies to purify the surroundings and the participants.
    • In modern life, the pursuit of fame and legacy can be seen in the efforts of individuals who strive to make a significant impact in their fields, whether through art, science, or social work, thereby seeking a form of immortality through their contributions.
  • Significance and Application: The mantra emphasizes the importance of recognizing our mortal nature while aspiring for greatness and lasting impact. It teaches us to seek wisdom and blessings from higher powers (like Agni) to achieve our goals. In practical terms, this can be applied in various aspects of life, such as:
    • Setting personal goals that align with one's values and aspirations.
    • Engaging in community service or initiatives that leave a positive legacy.
    • Fostering relationships and family ties that contribute to a sense of continuity and immortality through future generations.
