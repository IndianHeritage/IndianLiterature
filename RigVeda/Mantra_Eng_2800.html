शुनं हुवेम मगवाणं इन्द्रं अस्मिन भरे नृतमं वाजसातौ | श्रिण्वन्तं उग्रं ऊतये समत्सु घ्नन्तं वृत्राणि संजितं धनानाम् ||
śunaṁ huvema maghavānam indram asmin bhare nṛtamaṁ vājasātau | śṛṇvantam ugram ūtaye samatsu ghnantaṁ vṛtrāṇi saṁjitaṁ dhanānām ||
  • śunaṁ huvema: This phrase can be translated as "We invoke the powerful." The term "śunaṁ" refers to something that is auspicious or beneficial, while "huvema" indicates the act of invoking or calling upon.
  • maghavānam indram: Here, "maghavānam" refers to the mighty or powerful, and "indram" is a reference to Indra, the king of the gods in Vedic literature, known for his strength and ability to overcome obstacles.
  • asmin bhare nṛtamaṁ vājasātau: This part can be interpreted as "in this place, we offer our best." "Nṛtamaṁ" signifies the best or the most excellent, while "vājasātau" refers to the essence of strength or vigor.
  • śṛṇvantam ugram: This translates to "listening to the fierce." "Śṛṇvantam" means listening or hearing, and "ugram" denotes something fierce or powerful.
  • ūtaye samatsu: This phrase means "for the purpose of prosperity." "Ūtaye" refers to the act of achieving or obtaining, while "samatsu" indicates harmony or balance.
  • ghnantaṁ vṛtrāṇi: This can be understood as "striking down the obstacles." "Ghnantaṁ" means to strike or defeat, and "vṛtrāṇi" refers to the obstacles or enemies that hinder progress.
  • saṁjitaṁ dhanānām: This translates to "the gathered wealth." "Saṁjitaṁ" means collected or gathered, and "dhanānām" refers to wealth or resources.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of invoking divine strength and guidance to overcome obstacles and achieve prosperity. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various contexts such as personal challenges, business endeavors, or community efforts. By calling upon the strength of the divine, individuals and groups can find the courage and resources needed to face difficulties and strive for success. The significance of this mantra lies in its reminder that with the right intentions and support, one can navigate through life's challenges and attain their goals.
