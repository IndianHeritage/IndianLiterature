त्वम एतद अधारयः कृष्णासु रोहिणीषु च |परुष्णीषु रुशत पयः ||
tvam etad adhārayaḥ kṛṣṇāsu rohiṇīṣu ca | paruṣṇīṣu ruśat payaḥ ||
  • tvam etad adhārayaḥ: This phrase translates to "You are the support of this." Here, "tvam" refers to the divine or the ultimate reality, while "adhārayaḥ" signifies the act of supporting or sustaining. In real life, this can be understood as the foundational principles or values that support our existence and well-being.
  • kṛṣṇāsu rohiṇīṣu ca: "Kṛṣṇāsu" refers to the dark or black (often associated with Lord Krishna), and "rohiṇīṣu" refers to the Rohini star, which is significant in Hindu astrology. This part of the mantra emphasizes the connection between the divine and the celestial bodies, suggesting that the divine presence is reflected in the stars and the cosmos. In practical terms, this can be seen in how people often look to the stars for guidance and inspiration, much like how they seek divine support in their lives.
  • paruṣṇīṣu ruśat payaḥ: "Paruṣṇīṣu" refers to the rivers, and "ruśat payaḥ" translates to "the flowing waters." This part of the mantra highlights the importance of nature and its elements, particularly water, which is essential for life. In real life, this can be related to the significance of rivers in sustaining agriculture, providing drinking water, and supporting ecosystems. The flowing waters symbolize the continuous flow of life and the nourishment that nature provides.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of divine support, the connection between the cosmos and our lives, and the vital role of nature in sustaining existence. Its significance lies in reminding us to recognize the foundational values that support us, seek guidance from the universe, and appreciate the natural resources that nourish our lives. In practical scenarios, this mantra can inspire individuals to cultivate gratitude for the support they receive from both the divine and the environment, encouraging a harmonious relationship with nature and a deeper understanding of their place in the universe.
