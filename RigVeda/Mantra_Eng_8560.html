सम उ प्रियो मृज्यते सानो अव्ये यशस्तरो यशसां क्षैतो अस्मे |अभि स्वर धन्वा पूयमानो यूम पात स्वस्तिभिः सदा नः ||
sam u priyo mṛjyate sāno avye yaśastaro yaśasāṁ kṣaito asme | abhi svara dhanvā pūyamāno yūyam pāta svastibhiḥ sadā naḥ ||
  • Translation: The mantra can be translated as: "May we be dear to the gods, may we be blessed with fame and prosperity, may we be protected by your grace, and may we always be in a state of well-being."
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sam: together, in harmony
    • u: indeed, certainly
    • priyo: dear, beloved
    • mṛjyate: may be blessed, may be purified
    • sāno: may we be, may we attain
    • avye: without obstacles, unhindered
    • yaśastaro: of fame, of glory
    • yaśasāṁ: of prosperity, of success
    • kṣaito: may we be protected, may we be safeguarded
    • asme: for us, to us
    • abhi: towards, in the direction of
    • svara: sound, voice, divine call
    • dhanvā: bow, weapon, strength
    • pūyamāno: being filled, being blessed
    • yūyam: you (plural), the divine beings
    • pāta: protect, guard
    • svastibhiḥ: with well-being, auspiciousness
    • sadā: always, forever
    • naḥ: us, our
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community gathering, when people come together to celebrate a festival, they often invoke blessings for harmony and prosperity, similar to the essence of this mantra.
    • In personal life, individuals may recite this mantra during significant events like weddings or new ventures, seeking divine blessings for success and protection.
    • In workplaces, teams may use this mantra to foster a spirit of collaboration and mutual respect, aiming for collective success and well-being.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for unity, divine blessings, and protection. It emphasizes the importance of being in harmony with oneself and the universe, seeking fame and prosperity not just for oneself but for the collective. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as community events, personal milestones, and professional environments, where the focus is on collaboration, well-being, and mutual support. By invoking these blessings, individuals and communities can strive for a more harmonious and prosperous existence.
