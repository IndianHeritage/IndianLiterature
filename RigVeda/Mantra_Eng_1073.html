रायो बुध्नः संगमनो वसूनां यज्ञस्य केतुर्मन्मसाधनो वेः |अमृतत्वं रक्षमाणासा एनं देवाः अग्निं धारयन द्रविणोदाम ||
rāyo budhnaḥ saṁgamano vasūnāṁ yajñasya ketur manmasādhano veḥ | amṛtatvaṁ rakṣamāṇāsa enaṁ devā agniṁ dhārayan draviṇodām ||
  • Translation: The mantra can be translated as: "May we attain the wealth of the wise, the gathering of riches, the banner of the sacrifice, and the fulfillment of our desires. Protect us from mortality, O Gods, as we hold onto Agni, the divine fire, and the wealth of resources."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • rāyo: Wealth or riches.
    • budhnaḥ: Of the wise or enlightened.
    • saṁgamano: Gathering or coming together.
    • vasūnāṁ: Of the riches or resources.
    • yajñasya: Of the sacrifice or offering.
    • ketur: Banner or standard.
    • manmasādhano: Fulfillment of desires or intentions.
    • veḥ: Protection or safeguarding.
    • amṛtatvaṁ: Immortality or eternal life.
    • rakṣamāṇāsa: Protecting or safeguarding.
    • enaṁ: This or that (referring to Agni).
    • devā: Gods or divine beings.
    • agniṁ: Fire, often symbolizing energy and transformation.
    • dhārayan: Holding or sustaining.
    • draviṇodām: Wealth or resources.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, the mantra can be interpreted as a call for collaboration and unity among team members to achieve common goals, akin to gathering resources for a successful project.
    • In personal life, it can signify the importance of wise financial planning and the pursuit of knowledge to attain wealth and prosperity.
    • In spiritual practices, invoking Agni represents the transformative power of fire, which can be seen in rituals where fire is used to purify and bring about change.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its holistic approach to wealth, wisdom, and protection. It emphasizes the importance of community and collaboration in achieving prosperity while also seeking divine protection from mortality. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire individuals and groups to work together towards common goals, safeguard their resources, and pursue knowledge and wisdom as a means to attain lasting success and fulfillment. It serves as a reminder that true wealth encompasses not just material riches but also the richness of relationships, knowledge, and spiritual well-being.
