मां नो गुह्याऽरिप आयोर अहन दभन मां नाभ्यो रीरधो दुच्छुनाभ्यः |मां नो वि यौः सख्या विद्धि तस्य नः सुम्नायतां मनसा तद्वेमहे ||
mā no guhyā ripa āyor ahan dabhan mā na ābhyo rīradho ducchunābhyaḥ | mā no vi yauḥ sakhyā viddhi tasya naḥ sumnāyatā manasā tat tvemahe ||
  • Translation: The mantra can be translated as: "May we not be harmed by the secretive enemy; may we not be afflicted by those who wish us ill. May we be united in friendship; may our minds be directed towards auspiciousness."
  • Word-by-Word Explanation:
    • mā: May not
    • no: us
    • guhyā: secretive, hidden
    • ripa: enemy
    • āyor: of life
    • ahan: day
    • dabhan: afflict
    • mā: may not
    • na: not
    • ābhyo: from those
    • rīradho: who wish ill
    • ducchunābhyaḥ: from those who cause distress
    • mā: may not
    • no: us
    • vi: in friendship
    • yauḥ: united
    • sakhyā: in companionship
    • viddhi: know
    • tasya: of that
    • naḥ: our
    • sumnāyatā: auspiciousness
    • manasā: with the mind
    • tat: that
    • tvemahe: we aspire to
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace setting, this mantra can be invoked to foster a harmonious environment where colleagues support each other rather than compete secretly.
    • In personal relationships, it serves as a reminder to maintain transparency and trust, avoiding hidden agendas that can lead to misunderstandings.
    • In community settings, it encourages unity and collective well-being, urging individuals to work together for common goals rather than allowing divisive forces to create discord.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for protection against hidden adversities and the promotion of unity and auspiciousness. In real life, it is applicable in various scenarios, such as fostering healthy relationships, creating supportive work environments, and building cohesive communities. By reciting this mantra, individuals can cultivate a mindset focused on positivity, collaboration, and resilience against negativity, ultimately leading to a more harmonious existence.
