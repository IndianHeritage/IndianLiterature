आ विश्वतः प्रत्यञ्चं जिघर्म्य अरक्षसा मनसा तज जुषेत |मार्यश्रीः स्पृहयद्वर्णो अग्निर नाभिमृशे तंवा जर्भुराणः ||
ā viśvataḥ pratyañcaṁ jigharmy arakṣasā manasā taj juṣeta | maryaśrīḥ spṛhayadvarṇo agnir nābhimṛśe tanvā3 jarbhurāṇaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā viśvataḥ: From all directions
    • pratyañcaṁ: And from the opposite side
    • jigharmy: I desire
    • arakṣasā: Without any protection
    • manasā: With the mind
    • taj: That
    • juṣeta: May it be enjoyed
    • maryaśrīḥ: The noble one
    • spṛhayad: Desiring
    • varṇo: Color or form
    • agnir: Fire
    • nābhimṛśe: May not touch the navel
    • tanvā: With the body
    • jarbhurāṇaḥ: May it be nourished
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of a universal desire for protection and nourishment.
    • The phrase "from all directions" suggests a holistic approach to life, where one seeks blessings and support from every aspect of existence.
    • The mention of "without any protection" indicates a vulnerability that is inherent in the human experience, emphasizing the need for mental strength and resilience.
    • The desire for "that" which is noble and beautiful reflects the aspiration for higher values and virtues in life.
    • The reference to "fire" symbolizes transformation and energy, suggesting that one should harness their inner strength while being cautious of its potential dangers.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a challenging situation, such as facing a difficult exam or job interview, one might invoke this mantra to seek mental clarity and strength from all aspects of their life.
    • In personal relationships, the desire for protection and nourishment can be seen when individuals strive to create a supportive environment for their loved ones.
    • In the context of personal growth, the mantra encourages individuals to embrace their vulnerabilities while seeking transformation through their experiences.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking holistic support and nourishment in life.
    • It serves as a reminder to embrace vulnerability while striving for personal growth and transformation.
    • In real-life scenarios, it can be applied in various contexts, such as education, relationships, and self-improvement, encouraging individuals to seek balance and strength from all directions.
