मन्त्र: उता न एषु नृषु श्रवो धुः प्र राये यन्तु शर्धन्तो आर्यः ||
uta na eṣu nṛṣu śravo dhuḥ pra rāye yantu śardhanto aryaḥ ||
  • Word Breakdown:
    • uta: and, also
    • na: not
    • eṣu: in these, among these
    • nṛṣu: among men, in humanity
    • śravo: fame, glory
    • dhuḥ: to be troubled, to suffer
    • pra: for, towards
    • rāye: wealth, prosperity
    • yantu: may they go, may they proceed
    • śardhanto: united, together
    • aryaḥ: noble ones, the righteous
  • Meaning:
    • This mantra expresses a desire for unity and collective prosperity among people.
    • It emphasizes that among humanity, one should not suffer or be troubled, but rather strive towards wealth and prosperity.
    • The term "aryaḥ" refers to noble individuals who act with righteousness and integrity.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace, this mantra can be applied to foster teamwork and collaboration, ensuring that no employee feels isolated or troubled.
    • In community settings, it encourages collective efforts towards common goals, such as charity events or local development projects, where everyone works together for mutual benefit.
    • In personal relationships, it serves as a reminder to support one another, ensuring that friends and family do not face hardships alone.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for unity and shared prosperity, highlighting the importance of community and support among individuals.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from workplaces to personal relationships, promoting a culture of collaboration and mutual aid.
    • By embodying the principles of this mantra, individuals can contribute to a more harmonious and prosperous society, where everyone thrives together.
