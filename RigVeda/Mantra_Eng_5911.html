यद दधिषे प्रदिवि चार्व अन्नं दिवे-दिवे पीतिम इद अस्य वक्षि |उता हृदोत मनसा जुषाण उषन्न इन्द्र प्रस्थितान पाहि सोमान ||
yad dadhiṣe pradivi cārv annaṁ dive-dive pītim id asya vakṣi | uta hṛdota manasā juṣāṇa uśann indra prasthitān pāhi somān ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yad: whatever
    • dadhiṣe: you provide
    • pradivi: in the sky
    • cārv: nourishing
    • annaṁ: food
    • dive-dive: day by day
    • pītim: satisfaction
    • id: this
    • asya: of this
    • vakṣi: you will speak
    • uta: and
    • hṛdota: with a loving heart
    • manasā: with the mind
    • juṣāṇa: enjoying
    • uśann: shining
    • indra: O Indra (the deity)
    • prasthitān: those who have set forth
    • pāhi: protect
    • somān: the soma (sacred drink)
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra expresses a prayer for sustenance and nourishment provided by the divine.
    • The reference to "day by day" emphasizes the continuous need for sustenance in our lives.
    • The invocation of Indra signifies a request for protection and blessings from a higher power.
    • The mention of "soma" symbolizes the sacred and divine aspects of nourishment, both physical and spiritual.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, this mantra can be related to the importance of gratitude for the food we receive, whether it is from nature or through the efforts of farmers and producers.
    • It can also be applied in moments of communal gatherings, such as family meals or festivals, where the act of sharing food becomes a sacred ritual.
    • In a broader sense, it reflects the need for mental and emotional nourishment, highlighting the importance of community support and love.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of life, where physical sustenance is intertwined with spiritual and emotional well-being.
    • It encourages us to appreciate the daily blessings we receive and to seek protection and guidance from higher powers in our endeavors.
    • In essence, it teaches us to live with gratitude, mindfulness, and a sense of community, making it applicable in various aspects of our lives.
