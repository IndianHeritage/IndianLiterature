त ū षु णो महो यजत्रा भूत देवास ūtaye सजोषाः |ये वाजाम् anayatā वियन्तो ये स्था निचेतारो अमूराः ||
ta ū ṣu ṇo maho yajatrā bhūta devāsa ūtaye sajoṣāḥ | ye vājām̐ anayatā viyanto ye sthā nicetāro amūrāḥ ||
  • ta ū ṣu ṇo: This phrase can be interpreted as "may we be endowed with strength." It emphasizes the importance of strength and vitality in our lives.
  • maho yajatrā: Translated as "great is the one who performs the sacrifice," this part highlights the significance of selfless actions and contributions to society.
  • bhūta devāsa: This means "to the beings of the past," suggesting a connection to ancestors and the importance of honoring those who came before us.
  • ūtaye sajoṣāḥ: This can be understood as "for the purpose of prosperity and joy," indicating that our actions should lead to happiness and abundance.
  • ye vājām̐ anayatā: This translates to "those who bring forth wealth," emphasizing the role of individuals who contribute to the prosperity of the community.
  • viyanto: Meaning "those who are active," this word signifies the importance of being proactive and engaged in our endeavors.
  • ye sthā nicetāro amūrāḥ: This phrase can be interpreted as "those who are steadfast and unyielding," highlighting the value of resilience and determination in achieving our goals.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of strength, selflessness, and the pursuit of prosperity. It serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of individuals within a community and the importance of honoring our ancestors. In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as in community service, where individuals come together to support one another, or in personal endeavors where resilience and proactive efforts lead to success. By embodying the principles outlined in this mantra, we can contribute to a more harmonious and prosperous society.
