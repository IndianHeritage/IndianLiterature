इमं स्तोमं रोदसी प्र ब्रवीम्य ऋदूदराः श्रिणवन्न अग्निजिह्वाः | मित्रः सम्राजो वरुणो युवान आदित्यासः कवयः पप्रथानाः ||
imaṁ stomaṁ rodasī pra bravīmy ṛdūdarāḥ śṛṇavann agnijihvāḥ | mitraḥ samrājo varuṇo yuvāna ādityāsaḥ kavayaḥ paprathānāḥ ||
  • imaṁ stomaṁ rodasī pra bravīmy: This phrase translates to "I speak this hymn in the presence of the heavens." Here, "stomaṁ" refers to a hymn or praise, and "rodasī" signifies the sky or heavens. This indicates the importance of expressing reverence and gratitude towards the cosmic forces.
  • ṛdūdarāḥ śṛṇavann agnijihvāḥ: This can be interpreted as "the wise ones, who are like the flames of fire, listen." "ṛdūdarāḥ" refers to the wise or knowledgeable, while "agnijihvāḥ" symbolizes the flames, representing knowledge and enlightenment. This suggests that wisdom is akin to fire, illuminating the path of understanding.
  • mitraḥ samrājo varuṇo yuvāna: This translates to "Mitra, the king, and Varuna, the youthful one." "Mitra" represents friendship and harmony, while "Varuna" symbolizes cosmic order and truth. Together, they embody the balance of social and universal laws, emphasizing the importance of cooperation and righteousness in society.
  • ādityāsaḥ kavayaḥ paprathānāḥ: This means "the poets of the Adityas proclaim." "Adityas" refers to the solar deities, and "kavayaḥ" signifies poets or seers. This highlights the role of creativity and expression in understanding and conveying the divine truths of existence.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda emphasizes the interconnectedness of wisdom, cosmic order, and the power of expression. In real life, it signifies the importance of seeking knowledge, fostering harmony, and using creativity to articulate truths. It encourages individuals to recognize the divine presence in their lives and to act with integrity and respect towards the universe and each other. This mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as in educational settings where knowledge is shared, in community gatherings that promote harmony, and in artistic expressions that seek to convey deeper truths about existence.
