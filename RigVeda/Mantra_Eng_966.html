अहानि गृध्राः पर्य आव आगुर इमां धियं वार्कार्यां च देवीम् |ब्रह्म कृत्वन्तो गोतमासो अर्कैर ऊर्ध्वं नुनुद्र उत्सधिम पिबध्यै ||
ahāni gṛdhrāḥ pary ā va āgur imāṁ dhiyaṁ vārkāryāṁ ca devīm | brahma kṛṇvanto gotamāso arkair ūrdhvaṁ nunudra utsadhim pibadhyai ||
  • ahāni gṛdhrāḥ: This phrase translates to "the vultures of the day." Vultures are often seen as scavengers, and in this context, they symbolize the forces that consume or take away what is no longer needed.
  • pary ā va āgur imāṁ dhiyaṁ: This means "may they surround this thought." It suggests a protective or nurturing aspect, where thoughts or ideas are enveloped by positive influences.
  • vārkāryāṁ ca devīm: This translates to "and the goddess of the work." Here, the goddess represents the divine energy that empowers actions and endeavors, emphasizing the importance of divine support in our efforts.
  • brahma kṛṇvanto: This means "those who create the ultimate reality." It refers to individuals who are engaged in the pursuit of truth and higher knowledge, striving to manifest their highest potential.
  • gotamāso: This term can be interpreted as "the wise ones" or "those who are enlightened." It highlights the importance of wisdom in guiding one's actions and thoughts.
  • arkair ūrdhvaṁ nunudra: This translates to "with rays of light, they elevate." It symbolizes the uplifting power of knowledge and enlightenment, suggesting that wisdom can elevate one's existence.
  • utsadhim pibadhyai: This means "may they drink the essence." It implies the importance of absorbing the core truths and wisdom that life offers, nurturing one's spirit and intellect.

Conclusion: This mantra emphasizes the significance of wisdom, divine support, and the nurturing of positive thoughts in our lives. It teaches us to surround ourselves with uplifting influences and to seek higher knowledge. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various contexts such as personal development, education, and spiritual growth. By embodying the principles of this mantra, individuals can strive to create a meaningful existence, guided by wisdom and supported by divine energies. It serves as a reminder to focus on the essence of life, absorb knowledge, and elevate our thoughts and actions towards a higher purpose.
