सध्रीचिः सिन्धुम उषतीर इवायन सना जारा आरितः पूर्ब्हिद आसाम् |अस्तम आ ते पार्थिवा वसून्य अस्मे जग्मुः सूनृताः इन्द्र पूर्वीः ||
sadhrīcīḥ sindhum uśatīr ivāyan sanāj jāra āritaḥ pūrbhid āsām | astam ā te pārthivā vasūny asme jagmuḥ sūnṛtā indra pūrvīḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sadhrīcīḥ: Those who are united or harmonious.
    • sindhum: River or flow, often symbolizing abundance.
    • uśatīr: Good or auspicious.
    • ivāyan: Like or as if.
    • sanāj: A gathering or assembly.
    • j jāra: Those who are strong or powerful.
    • āritaḥ: Invoked or called upon.
    • pūrbhid: Those who break or overcome obstacles.
    • āsām: Of these or among them.
    • astam: To you or towards you.
    • ā te: To you or for you.
    • pārthivā: Earthly or worldly.
    • vasūny: Wealth or prosperity.
    • asme: To us or for us.
    • jagmuḥ: They have gone or moved.
    • sūnṛtā: With truth or righteousness.
    • indra: The king of gods, representing power and strength.
    • pūrvīḥ: Of the past or ancient.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of unity and harmony among people, akin to a river that flows abundantly.
    • It emphasizes the importance of coming together as a community, invoking strength and overcoming obstacles.
    • The reference to Indra signifies the need for divine support in achieving prosperity and truthfulness in our endeavors.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace, teamwork and collaboration can be seen as a manifestation of "sadhrīcīḥ," where individuals unite to achieve common goals.
    • Community service projects often embody the spirit of "uśatīr," where people come together to help those in need, creating a flow of goodwill.
    • In personal relationships, overcoming challenges together reflects the essence of "pūrbhid," where partners support each other in difficult times.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of unity, strength, and truth in our lives.
    • It is applicable in various scenarios, from personal relationships to community efforts and professional environments.
    • By embodying the principles of harmony and collective strength, we can navigate challenges and achieve prosperity, invoking the support of higher powers like Indra.
