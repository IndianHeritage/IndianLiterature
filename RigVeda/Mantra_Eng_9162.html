तन् नो देवाः यच्छत सुप्रवाचनं छर्दिर आदित्याः सुभरं नृपाय्यम् |पश्वे तोकाय तनयाय जीवसे स्वस्त्य अग्निं समिधानम् ईमहे ||
tan no devā yacchata supravācanaṁ chardir ādityāḥ subharaṁ nṛpāyyam | paśve tokāya tanayāya jīvase svasty a1gniṁ samidhānam īmahe ||
  • tan no devā yacchata: This phrase translates to "May the gods grant us." It signifies a plea for divine blessings and support. In real life, this can be seen in various cultures where people seek blessings from higher powers before starting important tasks, such as a wedding or a new business venture.
  • supravācanaṁ chardir ādityāḥ: This means "the excellent speech of the radiant ones." Here, "Ādityāḥ" refers to the sun gods, symbolizing enlightenment and knowledge. In practical terms, this can relate to the importance of clear communication and wisdom in our daily interactions, such as in leadership or education.
  • subharaṁ nṛpāyyam: This translates to "the auspicious wealth of the rulers." It emphasizes the significance of prosperity and good governance. In modern contexts, this can be related to the role of leaders in ensuring the welfare of their people, such as through fair policies and economic development.
  • paśve tokāya tanayāya: This phrase means "for the cattle, for the offspring." It highlights the importance of family and livelihood. In today's world, this can be seen in the emphasis on sustainable agriculture and the care for future generations, ensuring that resources are available for them.
  • jīvase svasty a1gniṁ samidhānam īmahe: This translates to "we invoke the auspicious fire for life." Fire here symbolizes energy, transformation, and sustenance. In real life, this can be related to the importance of maintaining a healthy lifestyle, as well as the role of innovation and technology in transforming our lives for the better.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a holistic approach to life, emphasizing the need for divine blessings, clear communication, prosperity, family welfare, and the transformative power of energy. Its significance lies in its applicability to various aspects of modern life, such as leadership, sustainability, and personal well-being. By invoking these principles, individuals and communities can strive for a balanced and prosperous existence.
