शतम् मे गद्भानां शतम् ऊर्णावतिनाम् |शतम् दासाम् अतिस्रजः ||
śatam me gardabhānāṁ śatam ūrṇāvatīnām | śataṁ dāsām̐ ati srajaḥ ||
  • śatam me gardabhānāṁ: This phrase translates to "a hundred of my donkeys." In ancient times, donkeys were valuable assets for transportation and labor. They symbolize wealth and prosperity.
  • śatam ūrṇāvatīnām: This means "a hundred of those who are adorned with ornaments." Here, "ūrṇāvatīnām" refers to individuals who are decorated or adorned, indicating beauty, status, and the importance of aesthetics in society.
  • śataṁ dāsām̐ ati srajaḥ: This translates to "a hundred servants with garlands." The term "dāsām̐" refers to servants or attendants, while "ati srajaḥ" implies they are adorned with garlands, symbolizing honor and respect.

  • Wealth and Prosperity: The mantra emphasizes the importance of material wealth, represented by the donkeys. In modern life, this can be related to the value of resources and assets that contribute to one's livelihood.
  • Beauty and Status: The mention of adorned individuals highlights the significance of beauty and social status. In contemporary society, this can be seen in the way people present themselves, the importance of fashion, and how social status can influence relationships and opportunities.
  • Service and Respect: The reference to servants adorned with garlands signifies the value of service and respect in relationships. In today's context, this can be related to the importance of teamwork, respect for colleagues, and the acknowledgment of those who support us in our endeavors.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda encapsulates themes of wealth, beauty, and respect for service. Its significance lies in the understanding that material wealth, social status, and the acknowledgment of those who serve us are essential aspects of life. In real-life scenarios, it encourages individuals to appreciate their resources, value aesthetics, and foster respectful relationships with those who support them, ultimately leading to a harmonious and prosperous life.
