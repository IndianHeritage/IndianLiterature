शुकेषु मे हरिमाणं रोपणाकासु दध्मसि |अथो हारिद्रवेषु मे हरिमाणं न दध्मसि ||
śukeṣu me harimāṇaṁ ropaṇākāsu dadhmasi | atho hāridraveṣu me harimāṇaṁ ni dadhmasi ||
  • śukeṣu me harimāṇaṁ ropaṇākāsu dadhmasi
    • śukeṣu: This term refers to "in the auspicious" or "in the good." It signifies a state of positivity and purity.
    • me: This means "to me," indicating a personal connection or request.
    • harimāṇaṁ: This translates to "the divine or auspicious one," often referring to Lord Vishnu or a similar deity associated with goodness and protection.
    • ropaṇākāsu: This can be interpreted as "in the actions or deeds." It suggests that the mantra is related to the actions one takes in life.
    • dadhmasi: This means "we perform" or "we do," indicating an active engagement in the process of life.
  • atho hāridraveṣu me harimāṇaṁ ni dadhmasi
    • atho: This means "and then" or "therefore," indicating a continuation or consequence of the previous statement.
    • hāridraveṣu: This term refers to "in the golden" or "in the auspiciousness," symbolizing wealth, prosperity, and success.
    • me: Again, this indicates a personal connection.
    • harimāṇaṁ: Reiterating the divine or auspicious one, emphasizing the importance of divine presence in one's life.
    • ni dadhmasi: This means "we do not perform," suggesting a caution against actions that may not align with divine will or auspiciousness.

Conclusion:

The mantra emphasizes the importance of aligning one's actions with auspiciousness and divine guidance. It serves as a reminder to engage in positive deeds and to seek the blessings of the divine in all endeavors. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios such as:

  • In personal relationships, one should strive to act with kindness and positivity, ensuring that their actions contribute to a harmonious environment.
  • In professional settings, this mantra encourages individuals to pursue ethical practices and decisions that lead to collective success and prosperity.
  • In spiritual practices, it serves as a reminder to seek divine guidance and blessings before undertaking significant tasks or decisions.

Overall, the mantra highlights the significance of conscious living, where one's actions are in harmony with divine principles, leading to a fulfilling and prosperous life.
