ऋतज्येन क्षिप्रेण ब्रह्मणस् पतिर् यत्र वष्टि प्र तद् अश्नोति धन्वना |तस्य साध्वीः इषवो याभिर् अस्यति नृचक्षसो दृष्टये कर्णयोनयः ||
ṛtajyena kṣipreṇa brahmaṇas patir yatra vaṣṭi pra tad aśnoti dhanvanā | tasya sādhvīr iṣavo yābhir asyati nṛcakṣaso dṛśaye karṇayonayaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ṛtajyena: By truth or righteousness
    • kṣipreṇa: Swiftly or quickly
    • brahmaṇas: Of the divine or the ultimate reality
    • patir: Lord or master
    • yatra: Where
    • vaṣṭi: Dwells or resides
    • pra: Indeed or truly
    • tad: That
    • aśnoti: Attains or achieves
    • dhanvanā: By the one who possesses wealth or strength
    • tasya: Of that
    • sādhvīr: With good or virtuous
    • iṣavo: Arrows or intentions
    • yābhir: By which
    • asyati: Is directed or aimed
    • nṛcakṣaso: Of the human eye or perception
    • dṛśaye: For vision or sight
    • karṇayonayaḥ: Of the ear or hearing
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of truth and righteousness as a means to swiftly attain the divine or ultimate reality.
    • It suggests that where the divine resides, one can achieve great things through virtuous actions and intentions.
    • The reference to "arrows" symbolizes focused intentions or efforts that are directed towards achieving one's goals.
    • The mention of "human eye" and "ear" indicates the importance of perception and understanding in the pursuit of truth and righteousness.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, a person who acts with integrity and honesty is often rewarded with trust and opportunities, reflecting the idea of achieving success through righteousness.
    • In personal relationships, being truthful and virtuous can lead to stronger bonds and deeper connections, illustrating the mantra's emphasis on good intentions.
    • In education, students who focus on learning with sincerity and dedication often achieve better results, showcasing the swift attainment of knowledge through righteous efforts.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the significance of truth and righteousness in our lives.
    • It highlights that by aligning our actions with virtuous intentions, we can achieve our goals and connect with the divine.
    • In a world where distractions and dishonesty are prevalent, this mantra encourages individuals to remain steadfast in their pursuit of truth, ultimately leading to personal and collective growth.
