आ विंशत्यां त्रिंशतां याह्य अर्वाङ् आ चत्वारिंशतां हरिभिर युजाना: |आ पञ्चाशतां सुरथेभिर इन्द्रा षष्ट्यां सप्तत्यां सोमपेयं ||
ā viṁśatyā triṁśatā yāhy arvāṅ ā catvāriṁśatā haribhir yujānaḥ | ā pañcāśatā surathebhir indrā ṣaṣṭyā saptatyā somapeyam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā: from
    • viṁśatyā: twenty
    • triṁśatā: thirty
    • yāhy: who
    • arvāṅ: with strength
    • ā: from
    • catvāriṁśatā: forty
    • haribhir: with the gods
    • yujānaḥ: united
    • ā: from
    • pañcāśatā: fifty
    • surathebhir: with the well-equipped
    • indrā: Indra (the king of gods)
    • ṣaṣṭyā: sixty
    • saptatyā: seventy
    • somapeyam: the drinker of Soma
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of a divine assembly or gathering, emphasizing the importance of unity and strength.
    • The numbers mentioned (20, 30, 40, 50, 60, 70) can symbolize various stages of life or levels of achievement.
    • Indra, as the king of gods, represents leadership and the power of collaboration among divine forces.
    • The term "Soma" refers to a sacred drink that is believed to confer immortality and divine insight.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, this mantra can be likened to a team working together towards a common goal, where each member contributes their unique strengths.
    • In community service, individuals from diverse backgrounds unite to achieve a shared purpose, reflecting the essence of collaboration and strength.
    • In personal development, the stages of life can be seen as milestones that one must navigate, drawing on inner strength and support from others.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of unity, strength, and collaboration in achieving greater goals.
    • It serves as a reminder that individual efforts, when combined with the support of others, can lead to profound outcomes.
    • In real life, whether in personal relationships, professional environments, or community initiatives, the principles embodied in this mantra can guide individuals towards success and fulfillment.
