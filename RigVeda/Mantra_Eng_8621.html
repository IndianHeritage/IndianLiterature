द्विर्यं पञ्च स्वयशसं स्वसारो अद्रिसंहतम् |प्रियं इन्द्रस्य काम्यम् प्रश्नापयन्त्यूर्मिणम् ||
dvir yam pañca svayaśasaṁ svasāro adrisaṁhatam | priyam indrasya kāmyam prasnāpayanty ūrmiṇam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • dvir: Twice
    • yam: Who
    • pañca: Five
    • svayaśasaṁ: Of one's own glory
    • svasāro: Of one's own kin
    • adrisaṁhatam: United like mountains
    • priyam: Dear
    • indrasya: Of Indra (the king of gods)
    • kāmyam: Desired
    • prasnāpayanty: They who ask
    • ūrmiṇam: Of the waves
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of a collective strength and unity, symbolized by the phrase "united like mountains." It emphasizes the importance of community and kinship.
    • The mention of "five" can be interpreted as the five elements or the five senses, suggesting a holistic approach to life.
    • Indra, as the king of gods, represents power and authority. The mantra implies that when one seeks the blessings of the divine, they should do so with a sense of unity and purpose.
    • The "waves" symbolize the flow of life and the challenges one faces. Asking questions or seeking guidance is essential in navigating these challenges.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, teamwork is crucial. Just as the mantra emphasizes unity, successful projects often rely on the collaboration of diverse individuals working towards a common goal.
    • In family dynamics, the strength of kinship is vital. Families that support each other, much like the "united mountains," can overcome challenges more effectively.
    • In spiritual practices, seeking guidance from a higher power or community can provide clarity and direction, akin to asking the waves for their wisdom.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of unity, kinship, and the pursuit of divine guidance in our lives.
    • Its teachings are applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal relationships to professional environments, emphasizing that collective strength and seeking wisdom are essential for overcoming life's challenges.
    • Ultimately, it encourages individuals to foster connections and seek support, reinforcing the idea that together, we can achieve greater heights.
