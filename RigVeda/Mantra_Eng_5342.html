नū चिन् नु ते मन्यमानस्य दसमोद अशनुवन्ति महिमानम् उग्र |न वीर्यम इन्द्र ते न राधः ||
nū cin nu te manyamānasya dasmod aśnuvanti mahimānam ugra | na vīryam indra te na rādhaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • nū: indeed
    • cin: to think or to consider
    • nu: indeed
    • te: your
    • manyamānasya: of one who is contemplating or thinking
    • dasmod: from the ten (or the multitude)
    • aśnuvanti: they attain or receive
    • mahimānam: greatness or glory
    • ugra: fierce or powerful
    • na: not
    • vīryam: strength or valor
    • indra: O Indra (the king of gods)
    • te: your
    • na: not
    • rādhaḥ: prosperity or wealth
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra reflects a deep contemplation on the nature of strength and glory.
    • It suggests that those who are thoughtful and reflective can attain greatness, even from a multitude of challenges.
    • The mention of Indra signifies the importance of divine support in achieving one's goals.
    • It emphasizes that true strength and prosperity come from inner contemplation and connection with the divine.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, a leader who reflects on their decisions and seeks guidance often leads their team to success, much like how the mantra suggests that contemplation leads to greatness.
    • In personal life, individuals who meditate or engage in self-reflection often find clarity and strength to overcome challenges, embodying the essence of this mantra.
    • Sports figures often speak about the mental aspect of their game, where contemplation and focus lead to victories, paralleling the idea of attaining glory through thoughtful consideration.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of introspection and the pursuit of inner strength.
    • It teaches that greatness is not merely a result of external actions but is deeply rooted in one's thoughts and connection to the divine.
    • In real life, applying the principles of this mantra can lead to personal and professional success, as it encourages individuals to reflect, seek guidance, and cultivate their inner strength.
