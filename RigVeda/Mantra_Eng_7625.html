इता ऊती वो अजमर प्रहेतारम अप्रहितम् |आशुं जेतारं हेतारं रथीतमम् अतूर्तं तुग्र्यावृधम् ||
ita ūtī vo ajaram prahetāram aprahitam | āśuṁ jetāraṁ hetāraṁ rathītamam atūrtaṁ tugryāvṛdham ||
  • ita ūtī: This phrase can be interpreted as "here is the path" or "this is the way." It signifies the importance of direction and guidance in life.
  • vo ajaram: "for you, the imperishable." This suggests that the essence of life or the ultimate truth is eternal and unchanging, despite the transient nature of the physical world.
  • prahetāram aprahitam: "the one who removes obstacles." This part emphasizes the need for a guiding force or divine intervention that helps in overcoming challenges and difficulties.
  • āśuṁ jetāraṁ: "swift conqueror." This indicates the importance of speed and decisiveness in achieving goals, suggesting that timely action is crucial for success.
  • hetāraṁ: "the cause." This refers to the underlying reasons or motivations behind actions, highlighting the significance of understanding one's purpose.
  • rathītamam: "the best charioteer." This metaphor suggests that having a skilled guide or leader is essential for navigating through life's journey effectively.
  • atūrtaṁ: "the one who is not hindered." This implies resilience and the ability to move forward without being bogged down by obstacles.
  • tugryāvṛdham: "the one who is powerful and strong." This emphasizes the importance of strength, both physical and mental, in overcoming life's challenges.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of guidance, resilience, and the pursuit of truth in life. It signifies the importance of having a clear direction, understanding one's purpose, and the need for a capable leader or guide to navigate through challenges. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various contexts such as leadership in organizations, personal development, and spiritual journeys. By embodying the qualities of decisiveness, strength, and the ability to overcome obstacles, individuals can achieve their goals and lead fulfilling lives. This mantra serves as a reminder to seek guidance and remain steadfast in the pursuit of one's aspirations.
