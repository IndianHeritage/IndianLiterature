असादि वृतो वह्निर आजगन्बान अग्निर ब्रह्मा नृषदने विधर्ता |द्युश्च यं पृथिवी वावृद्धाते आ यं होता यजति विश्ववारम् ||
asādi vṛto vahnir ājaganvān agnir brahmā nṛṣadane vidhartā | dyauś ca yam pṛthivī vāvṛdhāte ā yaṁ hotā yajati viśvavāram ||
  • asādi vṛto vahnir: This phrase can be translated as "the fire that is surrounded by truth." Here, "vahnir" refers to fire, which symbolizes purity and transformation, while "asādi vṛto" implies being enveloped in truth. In real life, this can be likened to the idea that true knowledge and integrity can illuminate our paths, much like fire dispels darkness.
  • ājaganvān agnir: This means "the fire that has come forth." It signifies the emergence of energy and creativity. In practical terms, this can be seen in the way new ideas and innovations arise in society, igniting progress and change.
  • brahmā nṛṣadane vidhartā: This translates to "the creator who sustains the world." "Brahmā" represents the ultimate reality or cosmic spirit, while "vidhartā" means sustainer. This can be related to the role of leaders and caretakers in our communities who nurture and support growth, much like a gardener tending to plants.
  • dyauś ca yam pṛthivī vāvṛdhāte: This phrase means "the sky and the earth that support each other." It emphasizes the interconnectedness of all elements of nature. In real life, this can be observed in environmental conservation efforts where the balance between land and sky (or air) is crucial for sustaining life.
  • ā yaṁ hotā yajati viśvavāram: This translates to "the one who is invoked in all sacrifices." It highlights the importance of rituals and offerings in various cultures. In modern contexts, this can be seen in how communities come together for celebrations, honoring traditions that foster unity and shared values.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of creation, sustenance, and the interconnectedness of all life. Its significance lies in the reminder that truth, creativity, and community are vital for a harmonious existence. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios such as environmental stewardship, community building, and the pursuit of knowledge. By embracing these principles, individuals and societies can foster a more balanced and fulfilling life.
