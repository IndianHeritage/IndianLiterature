तां ते गृणन्ति वेधसो यानि चकर्थ पौंस्याः |सुतेष्व इन्द्र गिर्वणः ||
tā te gṛṇanti vedhaso yāni cakartha pauṁsyā | suteṣv indra girvaṇaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • tā: those
    • te: your
    • gṛṇanti: sing or praise
    • vedhaso: of the wise or learned
    • yāni: which
    • cakartha: have accomplished or achieved
    • pauṁsyā: in the realm of manhood or strength
    • suteṣv: in the offerings or sacrifices
    • indra: Indra, the king of gods
    • girvaṇaḥ: of the singers or bards
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks to the praises sung by the wise regarding the achievements in the realm of strength and manhood.
    • It emphasizes the importance of recognizing and celebrating accomplishments, particularly in the context of offerings made to Indra, who represents power and success.
    • The mention of "singers" suggests that these praises are not just personal but are shared within a community, highlighting the collective acknowledgment of strength and success.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, when a team achieves a significant milestone, it is common for the organization to celebrate this success through awards or recognition ceremonies. This mirrors the essence of the mantra, where achievements are acknowledged and praised.
    • In sports, when a team wins a championship, the players are celebrated not just individually but as a collective unit, similar to how the mantra emphasizes communal recognition of strength and success.
    • In cultural festivals, communities often come together to celebrate their heritage and achievements, akin to the praises sung by the wise in the mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call to recognize and celebrate achievements, particularly those that contribute to strength and success.
    • It serves as a reminder of the importance of community and collective acknowledgment in fostering a sense of belonging and motivation.
    • In real life, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as workplaces, sports, and cultural gatherings, where celebrating achievements can inspire further success and unity.
