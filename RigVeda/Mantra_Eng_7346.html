यस्याजुषन् नमस्विनः शमीं अदुर्मखस्य वा |तं घेद अग्निर वृधावति ||
yasyājuṣan namasvinaḥ śamīm adurmakhasya vā | taṁ ghed agnir vṛdhāvati ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yasyā: of whom
    • ūṣan: the one who is praised
    • namasvinaḥ: of the respectful or venerable
    • śamīm: the peace or tranquility
    • adurmakhasya: of the one who is not obstructed or hindered
    • vā: or
    • taṁ: him
    • ghed: may he be invoked
    • agnir: fire
    • vṛdhāvati: may he grow or increase
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of invoking the divine presence of fire, which symbolizes energy, transformation, and purification.
    • The phrase "yasyāūṣan namasvinaḥ" refers to the one who is worthy of respect and praise, indicating a connection to a higher power or divine entity.
    • "Śamīm adurmakhasya vā" suggests the idea of peace and tranquility that is unobstructed, highlighting the importance of harmony in life.
    • The invocation of fire ("taṁ ghed agnir") signifies a call for the divine energy to manifest and grow in our lives, promoting transformation and enlightenment.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, this mantra can be related to the pursuit of knowledge and wisdom. Just as fire transforms materials, knowledge transforms our understanding and perspective.
    • In moments of conflict or stress, invoking peace and tranquility can help restore harmony, much like the calming effect of a warm fire in a gathering.
    • In rituals and ceremonies, fire is often used as a symbol of purification, representing the burning away of negativity and the welcoming of positive energy.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of respect, peace, and the transformative power of fire in our lives.
    • It serves as a reminder to seek harmony and invoke positive energies, whether in personal growth, relationships, or spiritual practices.
    • By understanding and applying the essence of this mantra, individuals can cultivate a more peaceful and enlightened existence, fostering growth and transformation in various aspects of life.
