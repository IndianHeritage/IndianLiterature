यस्यā रुशन्तो अर्चयः प्रति भद्रā अदृक्षत | सा नो रयिं विश्ववारं सुपेशसम उषा ददातु सुग्म्यम् ||
yasyā ruśanto arcayaḥ prati bhadrā adṛkṣata | sā no rayiṁ viśvavāraṁ supeśasam uṣā dadātu sugmyam ||
  • yasyā ruśanto arcayaḥ: This phrase can be translated to "those who are angry or fierce, we worship." It signifies the importance of acknowledging and respecting the forces of nature or deities that may seem fierce or intimidating. In real life, this can be related to respecting powerful natural phenomena, such as storms or earthquakes, which demand our reverence and caution.
  • prati bhadrā adṛkṣata: This translates to "may they (the fierce ones) look upon us with favor." It emphasizes the desire for positive attention from these powerful forces. In everyday life, this can be seen in how people seek blessings or goodwill from influential figures or forces, whether in personal relationships or in the workplace.
  • sā no rayiṁ viśvavāraṁ: This means "may she (the goddess or force) grant us all kinds of wealth." Here, 'wealth' is not limited to material possessions but can also refer to spiritual wealth, knowledge, and well-being. In a practical sense, this can be related to the pursuit of holistic success in life, encompassing health, happiness, and prosperity.
  • supeśasam uṣā dadātu: This translates to "may the well-formed dawn grant us." The dawn symbolizes new beginnings and opportunities. In real life, this can be likened to the start of a new day, where individuals have the chance to set new goals and pursue fresh opportunities.
  • sugmyam: This word means "pleasant" or "auspicious." It suggests that the outcomes we seek should be favorable and beneficial. In practical terms, this can be related to the importance of creating a positive environment in our lives, whether at home, work, or in our communities.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a deep respect for the powerful forces of nature and the divine, while also expressing a desire for their blessings in our lives. It highlights the significance of seeking favor from these forces to attain wealth and prosperity, both materially and spiritually. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various contexts, such as seeking guidance in challenging situations, striving for personal growth, and fostering a positive outlook towards new beginnings. By acknowledging the fierce aspects of life and seeking their blessings, individuals can navigate their paths with greater wisdom and purpose.
