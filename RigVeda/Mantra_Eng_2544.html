मन्त्र: ससृवांसम iva तमनाग्निम इत्त्था तिरोहितम् |ऐनं नयन मातरिश्वा परावतो देवभ्यः मथितं परी ||
sasṛvāṁsam iva tmanāgnim itthā tirohitam | ainaṁ nayan mātariśvā parāvato devebhyo mathitam pari ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sasṛvāṁsam: resembling or similar to the one who has been born or created.
    • iva: like or as.
    • tmanā: with the self or by oneself.
    • agnim: the fire or the divine energy.
    • itthā: thus or in this manner.
    • tirohitam: hidden or concealed.
    • ainaṁ: this or that.
    • nayan: the eye or vision.
    • mātariśvā: the wind or the atmospheric deity.
    • parāvato: from the heights or the upper realms.
    • devebhyo: from the gods or divine beings.
    • mathitam: churned or agitated.
    • pari: all around or completely.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the divine fire (Agni) that is both hidden and revealed, suggesting a duality in existence.
    • The reference to "mātariśvā" indicates the connection between the divine and the natural elements, such as wind, which can be seen as a metaphor for the unseen forces in our lives.
    • The act of churning (mathitam) symbolizes the process of transformation and creation, akin to how ideas and thoughts are refined through contemplation and experience.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In personal development, individuals often go through a process of self-discovery that resembles the churning of thoughts and emotions, leading to a clearer understanding of their purpose.
    • In nature, the interplay of fire and wind can be seen in how wildfires can be both destructive and regenerative, clearing old growth to make way for new life.
    • In relationships, the hidden aspects of our personalities can be revealed through communication and understanding, much like the hidden fire that is brought to light.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of recognizing the hidden forces in our lives and the transformative power of self-awareness.
    • It teaches us that through introspection and interaction with the world around us, we can uncover deeper truths and foster growth.
    • In a broader context, it serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of all elements in life, urging us to seek balance and harmony.
