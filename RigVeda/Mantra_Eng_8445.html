प्र वो धियो मन्द्रयुवो विपण्युवः पनस्युवः संवासनेष्व अक्रमुः |सोमं मनीषा अभ्य अनूषत स्तुभो 'भि धेनवः पयसेम अशिश्रयुः ||
pra vo dhiyo mandrayuvo vipanyuvaḥ panasyuvaḥ saṁvasaneṣv akramuḥ | somam manīṣā abhy anūṣata stubho 'bhi dhenavaḥ payasem aśiśrayuḥ ||
  • pra vo dhiyo mandrayuvo vipanyuvaḥ panasyuvaḥ saṁvasaneṣv akramuḥ
    • pra vo: This phrase can be interpreted as "to you all" or "for you all," indicating a collective address to a group.
    • dhiyo: Refers to "intellects" or "understandings," emphasizing the importance of wisdom and knowledge.
    • mandrayuvo: This term suggests "those who are united" or "those who come together," highlighting the significance of collaboration and unity.
    • vipanyuvaḥ: This can be understood as "those who are prosperous" or "those who bring wealth," indicating a focus on abundance and success.
    • panasyuvaḥ: Refers to "those who are nourished" or "those who provide sustenance," emphasizing the importance of support and care within a community.
    • saṁvasaneṣv akramuḥ: This phrase means "in gatherings, they are unbroken," suggesting the strength and resilience found in community and togetherness.
  • somam manīṣā abhy anūṣata stubho 'bhi dhenavaḥ payasem aśiśrayuḥ
    • somam: Refers to "the moon" or "soma," which symbolizes nourishment, vitality, and the essence of life.
    • manīṣā: This term means "intellect" or "thought," indicating the importance of deep contemplation and wisdom.
    • abhy anūṣata: This can be interpreted as "they have approached" or "they have come near," suggesting a call to action or engagement.
    • stubho: Refers to "praise" or "hymn," indicating the significance of expressing gratitude and reverence.
    • 'bhi dhenavaḥ: This phrase means "like cows" or "nourishing," symbolizing abundance and the nurturing aspect of life.
    • payasem aśiśrayuḥ: This can be understood as "they have taken refuge in nourishment," emphasizing the importance of sustenance and support.

Conclusion:

The mantra emphasizes the significance of unity, intellect, and nourishment within a community. It highlights the importance of coming together, sharing knowledge, and supporting one another to achieve prosperity and resilience. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various contexts such as:

  • In workplaces, fostering teamwork and collaboration can lead to greater success and innovation.
  • In families, supporting each other emotionally and physically creates a nurturing environment.
  • In communities, collective efforts in social initiatives can lead to improved living conditions and shared prosperity.

Overall, the mantra serves as a reminder of the power of collective wisdom and the importance of nurturing relationships for a prosperous and harmonious life.
