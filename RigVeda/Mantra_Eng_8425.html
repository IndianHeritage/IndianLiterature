अधि द्यां अस्ताद् वृषभो विचक्षणो 'रूरुचद् वि दिवो रोचनाः कविः | राजा पवित्रम् अत्येति रोरुवद् दिवः पीयूषं दूहते नृचक्षसः ||
adhi dyām asthād vṛṣabho vicakṣaṇo 'rūrucad vi divo rocanā kaviḥ | rājā pavitram aty eti roruvad divaḥ pīyūṣaṁ duhate nṛcakṣasaḥ ||
  • adhi dyām asthād: This phrase can be translated as "He who has established the heavens." It signifies the divine power that creates and sustains the universe.
  • vṛṣabho vicakṣaṇo: This means "the wise bull." In Vedic literature, the bull often symbolizes strength and wisdom, representing a powerful force that guides and protects.
  • 'rūrucad vi divo rocanā kaviḥ: This translates to "the poet who shines in the heavens." It emphasizes the role of creativity and inspiration in the divine realm, suggesting that the divine is also a source of artistic expression.
  • rājā pavitram aty eti: This means "the king who transcends purity." It indicates that true leadership involves a commitment to purity and righteousness, elevating oneself above worldly concerns.
  • roruvad divaḥ pīyūṣaṁ: This translates to "the nectar of the heavens." It symbolizes the ultimate bliss and nourishment that comes from divine connection and spiritual fulfillment.
  • duhate nṛcakṣasaḥ: This means "he who nourishes the eyes of men." It suggests that the divine provides insight and wisdom to humanity, guiding them towards enlightenment.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda encapsulates the essence of divine wisdom, strength, and purity. It highlights the importance of creativity and leadership in the spiritual journey. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios such as:

  • In leadership roles, individuals can strive to embody the qualities of purity and wisdom, guiding their teams with integrity.
  • Artists and creators can draw inspiration from the divine, using their talents to uplift and enlighten society.
  • Individuals seeking personal growth can reflect on the importance of connecting with the divine to gain insight and clarity in their lives.

Ultimately, this mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of the divine and human experience, encouraging us to seek wisdom, purity, and inspiration in our daily lives.
