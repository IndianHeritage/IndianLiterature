स दूतो विश्वेद अभि वष्टि सद्मा होतā हिरण्यरथो रंसुजिह्वः |रोहिदश्वो वपुष्यो विभावā सदा रण्वः पितुमतीव संसत ||
sa dūto viśved abhi vaṣṭi sadmā hotā hiraṇyaratho raṁsujihvaḥ | rohidaśvo vapuṣyo vibhāvā sadā raṇvaḥ pitumatīva saṁsat ||
  • sa dūto viśved abhi vaṣṭi sadmā: This phrase can be translated as "the messenger (dūto) of all (viśved) approaches (vaṣṭi) the abode (sadmā)." Here, the concept of a messenger signifies communication and connection among people or divine entities. In real life, this can be likened to how we rely on technology and social networks to connect with others, bridging distances and fostering relationships.
  • hotā hiraṇyaratho raṁsujihvaḥ: This part refers to "the priest (hotā) with a golden chariot (hiraṇyaratho) and radiant tongue (raṁsujihvaḥ)." The priest symbolizes guidance and wisdom, while the golden chariot represents prosperity and success. In contemporary terms, this can be seen in leaders or mentors who guide us towards our goals, embodying wisdom and the ability to inspire others.
  • rohidaśvo vapuṣyo vibhāvā: This translates to "the red horse (rohidaśvo) with a beautiful form (vapuṣyo) and splendor (vibhāvā)." The imagery of a horse often symbolizes strength, speed, and vitality. In modern life, this can be related to the pursuit of health and fitness, where individuals strive to maintain their physical well-being and vigor.
  • sadā raṇvaḥ pitumatīva saṁsat: This phrase means "always (sadā) engaged in (raṇvaḥ) the gathering (saṁsat) like a father (pitumatīva)." This highlights the importance of community and familial bonds. In today's world, this can be reflected in how families and communities come together to support one another, especially during challenging times, emphasizing the value of unity and cooperation.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates themes of communication, guidance, strength, and community. Its significance lies in the reminder of the interconnectedness of individuals and the importance of support systems in our lives. In real-life scenarios, it encourages us to be messengers of goodwill, to seek wisdom from leaders, to maintain our health, and to foster strong relationships within our communities. By embodying these principles, we can create a harmonious and prosperous environment for ourselves and those around us.
