अव स्वयुक्ता दिव आ वृथा ययुर अमर्त्याः कशयाः चोदता तमनाऽ। अरेणवस् तुविजाता अचुच्यवुर दृढानि चिन् मरुतो भ्राजदृष्टयः ॥
ava svayuktā diva ā vṛthā yayur amartyāḥ kaśayā codata tmanā | areṇavas tuvijātā acucyavur dṛḻhāni cin maruto bhrājadṛṣṭayaḥ ||
  • ava svayuktā: This phrase can be interpreted as "having been united" or "coming together." It signifies the importance of unity and collaboration among individuals or forces.
  • diva ā vṛthā: Translated as "in the heavens, not in vain," this suggests that actions taken with purpose and intention are recognized in the higher realms. It emphasizes the significance of meaningful endeavors.
  • yayur amartyāḥ: This means "the immortal ones." It refers to beings or forces that transcend mortality, symbolizing eternal truths or principles that guide human existence.
  • kaśayā codata tmanā: This can be understood as "with determination, they inspire the self." It highlights the role of inner strength and motivation in achieving one's goals.
  • areṇavas tuvijātā: This phrase translates to "born from the sand." It can symbolize resilience and adaptability, as sand can be shaped and molded, representing the ability to overcome challenges.
  • acucyavur dṛḻhāni: Meaning "unwavering strength," this emphasizes the importance of steadfastness in the face of adversity. It suggests that true strength lies in persistence and determination.
  • cin maruto bhrājadṛṣṭayaḥ: This translates to "the winds that shine with vision." It symbolizes the power of insight and clarity, suggesting that those who possess vision can navigate through life's challenges effectively.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates themes of unity, purpose, resilience, and vision. In real-life scenarios, these principles can be applied in various contexts, such as teamwork in professional settings, personal growth through self-motivation, and the importance of having a clear vision in achieving one's goals. By embodying these qualities, individuals can navigate their lives with greater clarity and strength, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling existence. The mantra serves as a reminder that our actions, when taken with intention and unity, resonate beyond the immediate and contribute to a greater cosmic order.
