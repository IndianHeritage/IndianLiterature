इन्दुर देवानामुप सख्यमायन सहस्रधारः पवते मदाय |नृभिः स्तवानो अनु धाम पूर्वम् अगन्न इन्द्रं महते सौभगाय ||
indur devānām upa sakhyam āyan sahasradhāraḥ pavate madāya | nṛbhiḥ stavāno anu dhāma pūrvam agann indram mahate saubhagāya ||
  • Indur devānām: The term "Indur" refers to the moon, which is often associated with nourishment and the divine. In this context, it signifies the celestial bodies that provide sustenance and guidance.
  • upa sakhyam āyan: This phrase translates to "coming together as friends." It emphasizes the importance of unity and collaboration among beings, suggesting that harmony leads to greater strength and prosperity.
  • sahasradhāraḥ: This means "with a thousand streams." It symbolizes abundance and the flow of resources, indicating that when there is unity, the benefits multiply and flow freely.
  • pavate madāya: "Pavate" means "to blow" or "to move," and "madāya" refers to joy or intoxication. This can be interpreted as the joyous energy that propels life forward, akin to the wind that carries seeds for growth.
  • nṛbhiḥ stavāno: This translates to "sung by men." It highlights the role of human beings in expressing gratitude and reverence, suggesting that our voices and actions can invoke divine blessings.
  • anu dhāma pūrvam: "Anu" means "following" and "dhāma" refers to "the abode" or "place." This indicates a journey or path that leads to a higher state of being, suggesting that we should follow the righteous path laid out by our predecessors.
  • agann indram mahate saubhagāya: This phrase means "may we attain the great Indra for prosperity." Indra is the king of the gods and symbolizes strength, power, and abundance. The mantra concludes with a prayer for blessings and prosperity in life.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda emphasizes the significance of unity, collaboration, and gratitude in achieving prosperity and abundance. In real-life scenarios, it can be applied in various contexts such as teamwork in professional settings, community building, and fostering relationships. By coming together as friends and working towards common goals, individuals can create a flow of resources and opportunities that benefit all. The invocation of divine blessings through collective efforts highlights the importance of recognizing and appreciating the interconnectedness of life. Ultimately, this mantra serves as a reminder that through unity and reverence, we can attain greater heights and prosperity in our lives.
