मन्त्र: असपत्नः सपत्नहाभिराष्ट्रो विषासहिः |यथाहमेषां भूतानां विराजानि जनस्य च ||
asapatnaḥ sapatnahābhirāṣṭro viṣāsahiḥ | yathāham eṣām bhūtānāṁ virājāni janasya ca ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • asapatnaḥ: Without enemies or adversaries
    • sapatnahā: With friends or allies
    • bhirāṣṭro: In the realm of the state or community
    • viṣāsahiḥ: With strength and confidence
    • yathāham: As I
    • eṣām: Of these
    • bhūtānāṁ: Beings or creatures
    • virājāni: Radiant or glorious
    • janasya: Of the people or community
    • ca: And
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks to the idea of living in harmony and peace, devoid of enmity.
    • It emphasizes the importance of having allies and friends in the community, which fosters a supportive environment.
    • The phrase "asapatnaḥ" suggests a state of being where one is free from conflict, which is essential for personal and communal growth.
    • “viṣāsahiḥ” indicates that strength and confidence come from unity and collaboration with others.
    • The mantra reflects a vision of a society where individuals thrive together, contributing to the glory and radiance of the community.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace, fostering a collaborative environment where colleagues support each other leads to greater productivity and job satisfaction.
    • In communities, initiatives that promote teamwork and mutual aid, such as neighborhood watch programs or community gardens, exemplify the principles of this mantra.
    • In personal relationships, surrounding oneself with supportive friends and family can create a nurturing atmosphere that encourages personal growth and resilience.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of unity, friendship, and strength in overcoming challenges.
    • It serves as a reminder that a harmonious society is built on the foundation of mutual respect and collaboration.
    • In real life, applying the principles of this mantra can lead to more fulfilling relationships, stronger communities, and a more peaceful existence.
