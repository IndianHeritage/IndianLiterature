सहदानुम पुरुहूत क्षियन्तम अहस्तम इन्द्र सम पिणक कुणारुम् |अभि वृत्रं वर्धमानम् पियारुम अपादम् इन्द्र तवसा जगन्थ ||
sahadānum puruhūta kṣiyantam ahastam indra sam piṇak kuṇārum | abhi vṛtraṁ vardhamānam piyārum apādam indra tavasā jaghantha ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sahadānum: Together with, in unity
    • puruhūta: Many offerings, often referring to the various forms of worship or prayers
    • kṣiyantam: Diminishing, reducing
    • ahastam: Without hands, symbolizing helplessness or lack of power
    • indra: The king of gods, representing strength and power
    • sam piṇak kuṇārum: To crush or destroy the enemy or obstacles
    • abhi: Towards, in the direction of
    • vṛtraṁ: The obstacle or demon, often symbolizing challenges in life
    • vardhamānam: Growing, increasing
    • piyārum: Beloved, dear
    • apādam: Without feet, indicating a lack of mobility or power
    • indra tavasā: By the strength of Indra
    • jaghantha: To strike down or defeat
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the strength of Indra, the king of gods, to help overcome obstacles and challenges.
    • The phrase "sahadānum puruhūta" emphasizes the importance of unity and collective effort in facing difficulties.
    • By referring to "vṛtraṁ," it symbolizes the various challenges we encounter in life, which may seem overwhelming.
    • The mantra suggests that with the support of divine strength, one can overcome these challenges, even when feeling powerless or without resources.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a team project at work, when faced with a tight deadline, the team can come together (sahadānum) to brainstorm and support each other, invoking the spirit of collaboration to overcome the challenge (vṛtraṁ).
    • In personal life, during times of crisis, such as illness or loss, individuals often seek support from family and friends, embodying the idea of collective strength to face hardships.
    • In community service, when a community faces a natural disaster, the collective effort to rebuild and support each other reflects the essence of this mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of unity and divine strength in overcoming life's challenges.
    • It serves as a reminder that even in moments of feeling powerless, collective effort and faith can lead to triumph over obstacles.
    • In real-life scenarios, whether in personal struggles, professional challenges, or community efforts, the principles embodied in this mantra can inspire resilience and cooperation.
