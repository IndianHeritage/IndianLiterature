यद अग्न एषा समितिः भवाति देवी देवेषु यजतā यजत्रा |रत्ना च यद विभजासि स्वधावो भागं नो अत्र वसुमन्तं वीतात ||
yad agna eṣā samitir bhavāti devī deveṣu yajatā yajatra | ratnā ca yad vibhajāsi svadhāvo bhāgaṁ no atra vasumantaṁ vītāt ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yad: whatever
    • agna: O Agni (the fire god)
    • eṣā: this
    • samitir: harmony or peace
    • bhavāti: becomes
    • devī: divine or goddess
    • deveṣu: among the gods
    • yajatā: worshipped
    • yajatra: by the worshippers
    • ratnā: jewels or treasures
    • ca: and
    • yad: whatever
    • vibhajāsi: you distribute
    • svadhāvo: by your own will
    • bhāgaṁ: share or portion
    • no: for us
    • atra: here
    • vasumantaṁ: the prosperous
    • vītāt: may it be extended
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes Agni, the fire god, emphasizing the importance of harmony and divine blessings in rituals.
    • It suggests that whatever is offered in worship becomes a source of peace and prosperity.
    • The mention of distributing treasures signifies the sharing of wealth and blessings among the community.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community gathering, when people come together to celebrate a festival, they often share food and resources, creating a sense of unity and harmony.
    • In corporate settings, when leaders recognize and reward employees' contributions, it fosters a culture of appreciation and motivates others to perform better.
    • During charitable events, the act of giving not only helps those in need but also strengthens community bonds and promotes collective well-being.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for harmony, divine blessings, and the importance of sharing wealth and resources.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, such as community gatherings, corporate environments, and charitable activities, where the focus is on unity, appreciation, and collective prosperity.
    • By embodying the principles of this mantra, individuals and communities can cultivate a more harmonious and prosperous existence.
