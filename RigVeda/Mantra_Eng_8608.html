अभी नो अर्ष दिव्या वसूनी अभि विश्वा पार्थिवा पूयमानः |अभि येन द्रविणम् अश्नवामाभ्य आर्षेयम् जमदग्निवन नः ||
abhī no arṣa divyā vasūny abhi viśvā pārthivā pūyamānaḥ | abhi yena draviṇam aśnavāmābhy ārṣeyaṁ jamadagnivan naḥ ||
  • abhī no arṣa divyā vasūny: This phrase can be translated as "May the divine wealth come to us." Here, "abhī" signifies a call or invocation, "no" means "to us," "arṣa" refers to the divine or celestial, and "vasūny" indicates wealth or abundance. In real life, this can be related to the pursuit of prosperity and success in various endeavors, whether in business, education, or personal growth.
  • abhi viśvā pārthivā pūyamānaḥ: This translates to "May all earthly treasures be filled." "Viśvā" means "all," "pārthivā" refers to earthly or material, and "pūyamānaḥ" indicates being filled or enriched. This can be seen in the context of community efforts to enhance local resources, such as community gardens or cooperative businesses that aim to enrich the lives of all members.
  • abhi yena draviṇam aśnavāmā: This means "By which we may attain wealth." "Yena" translates to "by which," "draviṇam" means "wealth," and "aśnavāmā" indicates the act of attaining or achieving. This can be applied in real life through hard work, education, and strategic planning to achieve financial stability and success.
  • abhy ārṣeyaṁ jamadagnivan naḥ: This translates to "May we be blessed like the sage Jamadagni." "Abhy" signifies a blessing or invocation, "ārṣeyaṁ" refers to the sage or wise one, "jamadagnivan" is a reference to the sage Jamadagni, known for his wisdom and wealth, and "naḥ" means "to us." This can be related to seeking guidance from wise mentors or role models in our lives who can help us navigate challenges and achieve our goals.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of invoking divine wealth and earthly treasures while seeking guidance from wise figures. It highlights the interconnectedness of spiritual and material prosperity. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios, such as in business ventures, community development, and personal growth. By striving for abundance and seeking wisdom, individuals and communities can create a more prosperous and fulfilling life. The mantra serves as a reminder to balance spiritual aspirations with practical efforts to achieve success and well-being.
