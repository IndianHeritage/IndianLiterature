ज्योतिर वृणीत तमसो विजानन्नारे स्याम दुृताद अभीकें |इमा गिरः सोमपाः सोमवृद्ध जुषस्वेन्द्र पुरुतमस्य कारोः ||
jyotir vṛṇīta tamaso vijānann āre syāma duritād abhīke | imā giraḥ somapāḥ somavṛddha juṣasvendra purutamasya kāroḥ ||
  • jyotir vṛṇīta: This phrase translates to "choose the light." It signifies the importance of seeking knowledge, wisdom, and enlightenment in life. In real life, this can be compared to the pursuit of education and personal growth, where individuals strive to illuminate their minds and surroundings.
  • tamaso vijānann: This means "understanding the darkness." It suggests that one must recognize and comprehend ignorance or negativity in order to overcome it. For example, in a workplace, acknowledging challenges and obstacles is crucial for finding effective solutions.
  • āre syāma duritād abhīke: This can be interpreted as "may we be protected from misfortunes." It emphasizes the desire for safety and well-being. In everyday life, this reflects the human instinct to seek protection from harm, whether it be physical, emotional, or financial.
  • imā giraḥ somapāḥ: This translates to "these words are like the nectar of the moon." It symbolizes the sweetness and nourishment that comes from wise speech and communication. In real life, this can be seen in the importance of positive dialogue and constructive conversations that foster relationships and understanding.
  • somavṛddha juṣasvendra: This means "may the lord of the moon, who is nourished by the soma, be pleased." It highlights the connection between divine blessings and the sustenance of life. In practical terms, this can relate to the idea of gratitude and respect towards nature and the universe, recognizing that our well-being is intertwined with the environment.
  • purutamasya kāroḥ: This phrase translates to "the creator of the most excellent." It refers to the supreme being or force that brings forth the best in life. In real life, this can be associated with the pursuit of excellence in various fields, whether in personal endeavors, professional achievements, or artistic expressions.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking light and knowledge while acknowledging the darkness of ignorance. It emphasizes the importance of protection from misfortunes and the value of wise communication. In real life, this mantra serves as a guiding principle for personal development, resilience in the face of challenges, and the pursuit of excellence. By embodying these teachings, individuals can navigate their lives with purpose and positivity, fostering a harmonious existence with themselves and the world around them.
