पराद्य देवाः वृजिनं श्रिणन्तु प्रत्यग एनं शपथाः यान्तु तृष्टाः |वाचस्तेनं शरव ऋच्छन्तु मर्मन् विश्वस्यैतु प्रसितिं यातुधानः ||
parādya devā vṛjinaṁ śṛṇantu pratyag enaṁ śapathā yantu tṛṣṭāḥ | vācāstenaṁ śarava ṛcchantu marman viśvasyaitu prasitiṁ yātudhānaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • parādya: O divine beings
    • devā: gods
    • vṛjinaṁ: the obstacles or afflictions
    • śṛṇantu: may they hear
    • pratyag: within oneself
    • enaṁ: this
    • śapathā: by the oath
    • yantu: may they go
    • tṛṣṭāḥ: those who are thirsty or eager
    • vācā: with words
    • tenaṁ: this
    • śarava: may they be freed
    • ṛcchantu: may they be released
    • marman: the vital points or essence
    • viśvasy: of all
    • aitu: may it come
    • prasitiṁ: the protection or shelter
    • yātudhānaḥ: the evil spirits or malevolent forces
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to the divine beings, asking them to listen and help remove obstacles and afflictions from our lives.
    • The invocation of gods signifies a call for divine intervention, emphasizing the importance of seeking higher powers in times of need.
    • The phrase "by the oath" suggests a commitment to truth and righteousness, indicating that sincerity is essential in our prayers.
    • The mention of "those who are thirsty" symbolizes individuals who are eager for spiritual fulfillment or relief from suffering.
    • By asking for protection from "evil spirits," the mantra acknowledges the presence of negative forces in life and the need for divine safeguarding.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In times of personal crisis, such as illness or emotional distress, individuals often turn to prayer or meditation, similar to the invocation in this mantra, seeking divine assistance.
    • Communities facing natural disasters may recite such mantras collectively, hoping for divine protection and relief from calamities.
    • In a corporate setting, teams may use this mantra to foster unity and seek guidance in overcoming challenges, emphasizing the importance of collective effort and divine support.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of divine intervention in overcoming life's challenges and afflictions.
    • It serves as a reminder of the power of faith, community, and the importance of seeking higher guidance in difficult times.
    • In real life, it encourages individuals to remain hopeful and proactive in their spiritual practices, fostering resilience against negativity and adversity.
