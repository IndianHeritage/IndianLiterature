मन्त्र: चक्रवाँस ऋभवस् तद अपृच्छत क्वेद अभूद यः स्य दूतो न आजगन् |यदावाख्यच चमसां चतुरः कृतान आद इति त्वष्टा ज्ञास्व अन्तर न्य आनजे ||
cakṛvāṁsa ṛbhavas tad apṛcchata kved abhūd yaḥ sya dūto na ājagan | yadāvākhyac camasāñ caturaḥ kṛtān ād it tvaṣṭā gnāsv antar ny ānaje ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • cakṛvāṁsa: One who is endowed with the power of action or creation.
    • ṛbhavas: The wise or the knowledgeable.
    • tad: That.
    • apṛcchata: Asked or inquired.
    • kved: Where.
    • abhūd: Has come to be or has existed.
    • yaḥ: Who.
    • sya: Is or exists.
    • dūto: Messenger or envoy.
    • na: Not.
    • ājagan: Came or arrived.
    • yad: What.
    • āvākhyac: Has been declared or stated.
    • camasāñ: With the vessels or tools.
    • caturaḥ: Clever or skillful.
    • kṛtān: Created or made.
    • ād: From the beginning.
    • it: Thus.
    • tvaṣṭā: The divine craftsman or creator.
    • gnāsv: Know or understand.
    • antar: Within.
    • ny: Not.
    • ānaje: To be born or to come into existence.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the inquiry into the nature of existence and creation.
    • It emphasizes the role of the wise and knowledgeable in understanding the universe.
    • The mention of a messenger suggests the importance of communication and the sharing of knowledge.
    • The reference to the divine craftsman (tvaṣṭā) highlights the creative aspect of the divine in shaping reality.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In scientific inquiry, researchers often ask fundamental questions about the nature of the universe, similar to the inquiry in this mantra.
    • In everyday life, effective communication (like that of a messenger) is crucial in relationships and teamwork, where sharing knowledge leads to better understanding.
    • Artists and creators embody the spirit of tvaṣṭā by bringing new ideas and forms into existence, reflecting the creative process described in the mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of inquiry, knowledge, and creativity in understanding existence.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from scientific research to personal relationships and creative endeavors.
    • By embracing the qualities of the wise and the creative, individuals can contribute to a deeper understanding of the world and foster meaningful connections.
