ते ते देव नेतर ये चेमां अनुशसे |ते राया ते ह्य आपृचे सचेमहि सच्चथ्यैः ||
te te deva netar ye cemām̐ anuśase | te rāyā te hy ā3pṛce sacemahi sacathyaiḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • te te: "You, you" - a call to the divine beings.
    • deva: "gods" - refers to the celestial beings or deities.
    • netar: "guides" - those who lead or direct.
    • ye: "who" - referring to the deities mentioned.
    • cemām̐: "this" - indicating the present situation or context.
    • anuśase: "to govern" - to rule or control.
    • te: "you" - again addressing the deities.
    • rāyā: "wealth" - signifies prosperity or abundance.
    • te: "you" - repetition for emphasis.
    • hy: "indeed" - an affirmation of the statement.
    • ā3pṛce: "we invoke" - a call for attention or response.
    • sacemahi: "we meditate" - to reflect or contemplate.
    • sacathyaiḥ: "with truth" - signifies sincerity and honesty.
  • Overall Meaning:
    • This mantra is a prayer to the divine beings, asking them to guide and govern our lives.
    • It emphasizes the importance of invoking the deities for wealth and prosperity.
    • The repetition of "you" signifies a deep connection and respect towards the divine.
    • It highlights the significance of truth and sincerity in our thoughts and actions.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be seen as a reminder to seek guidance from higher principles or ethics when making decisions.
    • In personal life, it encourages individuals to reflect on their actions and ensure they are aligned with truth and integrity.
    • During times of uncertainty, invoking divine guidance can provide clarity and direction, much like seeking advice from a mentor.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine guidance and the pursuit of truth.
    • It is applicable in various aspects of life, including personal growth, ethical decision-making, and seeking prosperity.
    • By meditating on this mantra, individuals can cultivate a deeper connection with the divine and align their actions with higher values.
