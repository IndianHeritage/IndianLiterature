उता स्यां नो दिवा मतिर्दितिर ऊत्यागमत् |सा शान्ताति मयस् करद अप श्रिधः ||
uta syā no divā matir aditir ūtyā gamat | sā śaṁtāti mayas karad apa sridhaḥ ||
  • uta syā no divā matir: This phrase can be translated to "May our intellect be directed towards the divine during the day." Here, "uta" means "may," "syā" refers to "be," "no" means "our," "divā" translates to "during the day," and "matir" signifies "intellect" or "thought." This suggests a call for divine guidance in our thoughts and actions throughout the day.
  • aditir ūtyā gamat: This part translates to "May the unbounded (Aditi) lead us." "Aditi" is often associated with the concept of infinity or the boundless universe, representing a source of all creation. "ūtyā" means "lead" or "guide," and "gamat" means "to go" or "to move." This implies a desire for guidance from the infinite source, encouraging us to seek wisdom and direction from a higher power.
  • sā śaṁtāti mayas karad: This translates to "That (Aditi) brings peace to our actions." "sā" means "that," "śaṁtāti" refers to "brings peace," "mayas" means "our actions," and "karad" signifies "to do" or "to act." This indicates that when we align our actions with the divine, we can achieve peace and harmony in our lives.
  • apa sridhaḥ: This phrase translates to "May we attain prosperity." "apa" means "may we attain," and "sridhaḥ" refers to "prosperity" or "wealth." This suggests a prayer for abundance and success in our endeavors, emphasizing the importance of seeking divine blessings for material and spiritual wealth.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a profound spiritual message that emphasizes the importance of seeking divine guidance in our thoughts and actions. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various ways:

  • In daily life, individuals can recite this mantra during meditation or prayer to cultivate a mindset focused on peace, wisdom, and prosperity.
  • In professional settings, it can serve as a reminder to seek ethical and thoughtful decision-making, ensuring that one's actions align with higher values.
  • In personal relationships, the mantra encourages individuals to foster harmony and understanding, leading to peaceful interactions.

Overall, this mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of our thoughts, actions, and the divine, guiding us towards a life of peace and prosperity.
