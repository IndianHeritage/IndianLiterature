को देवानाम् अवो अद्य वृष्णीते क आदित्यां अदितिं ज्योतिर ईट्टे |कस्याश्विनाव् इन्द्रो अग्निः सुतस्यांषोः पिबन्ति मनसाविवेनेम् ||
ko devānām avo adyā vṛṇīte ka ādityām̐ aditiṁ jyotir īṭṭe | kasyāśvināv indro agniḥ sutasyāṁśoḥ pibanti manasāvivenam ||
  • Translation: The mantra can be translated as: "Who among the gods will today choose us? Which of the Adityas, Aditi, and the light will we invoke? Whose Ashvins, Indra, and Agni will drink the essence of our thoughts?"
  • Word-by-Word Explanation:
    • ko: Who
    • devānām: of the gods
    • avo: will choose
    • adyā: today
    • vṛṇīte: will select
    • ka: which
    • ādityām̐: of the Adityas (solar deities)
    • aditiṁ: Aditi (the mother of the gods)
    • jyotir: light
    • īṭṭe: we invoke
    • kasyāśvināv: whose Ashvins (twin gods of medicine)
    • indro: Indra (the king of gods)
    • agniḥ: Agni (the fire god)
    • sutasyāṁśoḥ: of the essence of our thoughts
    • pibanti: drink
    • manasāvivenam: of our minds
  • Contextual Significance:
    • This mantra reflects a deep yearning for divine connection and guidance. It emphasizes the importance of choosing the right deities to invoke for blessings and support in our lives.
    • In real life, this can be seen in various rituals and prayers where individuals seek the favor of specific deities for health, prosperity, and wisdom. For example, before starting a new venture, people may pray to Ganesha for success and the removal of obstacles.
    • The mention of Aditi and the Adityas signifies the importance of nurturing and protective energies, akin to how parents care for their children. In modern life, this can relate to seeking guidance from mentors or elders who provide wisdom and support.
    • The invocation of Indra and Agni symbolizes the need for strength and purification. In practical terms, this can be likened to seeking motivation and clarity in challenging situations, such as during exams or important life decisions.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness between humans and the divine. It highlights the importance of intention in our prayers and actions.
    • In everyday life, it encourages individuals to reflect on their choices and the energies they wish to attract. By consciously invoking positive influences, one can navigate life's challenges more effectively.
    • Ultimately, the mantra teaches us to seek guidance and support from higher powers, fostering a sense of community and connection with the universe.
