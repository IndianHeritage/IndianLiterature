मन्त्र: उता स्म राशिम् परि यासि गोनाम् इन्द्रेण सोम सरथम पुनानः | पूर्वीर इषो बृहतीर जीरदानो शिक्षां शचीवास तव ता उपष्टुत ||
uta sma rāśim pari yāsi gonām indreṇa soma saratham punānaḥ | pūrvīr iṣo bṛhatīr jīradāno śikṣā śacīvas tava tā upaṣṭut ||
  • uta sma rāśim pari yāsi gonām: This phrase can be interpreted as a call to the divine, acknowledging the vastness of the universe and the interconnectedness of all beings. The word "rāśim" refers to a collection or a multitude, suggesting unity in diversity.
  • indreṇa soma saratham punānaḥ: Here, "Indra" represents the king of gods, symbolizing strength and power. "Soma" refers to a sacred drink that is believed to bestow immortality and divine insight. "Saratham" implies the act of purification or renewal, indicating a desire for spiritual rejuvenation.
  • pūrvīr iṣo bṛhatīr jīradāno: This line speaks to the ancient wisdom and teachings passed down through generations. "Pūrvīr" refers to the ancestors or those who came before us, while "bṛhatīr" signifies greatness or vastness, emphasizing the importance of learning from the past.
  • śikṣā śacīvas tava tā upaṣṭut: "Śikṣā" means instruction or teaching, and "śacīvas" refers to the divine qualities or virtues. This line suggests that through learning and embodying these virtues, one can attain a higher state of being. "Tā upaṣṭut" indicates a call for divine presence and support in this journey.

Conclusion: This mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking divine guidance and wisdom while acknowledging the interconnectedness of all life. It emphasizes the importance of learning from our ancestors and striving for spiritual renewal through divine virtues. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various contexts such as personal growth, community building, and environmental stewardship. By recognizing our place in the universe and the importance of collective well-being, we can foster a more harmonious existence. The mantra serves as a reminder to seek knowledge, embrace our heritage, and cultivate virtues that uplift ourselves and those around us.
