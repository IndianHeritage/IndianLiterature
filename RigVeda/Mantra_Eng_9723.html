सँहोत्रं स्म पुरा नारी समनं वाव गच्छति |वेधा ऋतस्य वीरिणीन्द्रपत्नी महीयते विश्वस्माद इन्द्र उत्तरः ||
saṁhotraṁ sma purā nārī samanaṁ vāva gacchati | vedhā ṛtasya vīriṇīndrapatnī mahīyate viśvasmād indra uttaraḥ ||
  • saṁhotraṁ sma purā nārī samanaṁ vāva gacchati: This phrase can be translated to mean "In the past, the woman who is united in harmony moves together." Here, the term "saṁhotraṁ" refers to unity or harmony, while "nāri" signifies a woman. This suggests the importance of women in society and their role in maintaining harmony.
  • vedhā ṛtasya vīriṇīndrapatnī: This part translates to "the wife of the powerful Indra, who is the guardian of truth." "Vedhā" refers to the one who protects or guards, "ṛtasya" means truth or order, and "vīriṇīndrapatnī" refers to the wife of Indra, symbolizing strength and valor. This highlights the significance of women as protectors of truth and order in society.
  • mahīyate viśvasmād indra uttaraḥ: This translates to "Indra, the supreme being, is exalted above all." "Mahīyate" means to be exalted or honored, "viśvasmād" refers to being above all, and "indra uttaraḥ" signifies the supreme Indra. This emphasizes the idea of a higher power that oversees and protects the order of the universe.

  • Significance: This mantra underscores the importance of unity, the role of women in society, and the reverence for higher powers that maintain cosmic order. It reflects the belief that harmony and truth are essential for a prosperous society.
  • Real-life Application: In contemporary society, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as promoting gender equality, recognizing the contributions of women in leadership roles, and fostering community harmony. For instance, in family settings, encouraging collaboration and mutual respect among family members can lead to a more harmonious environment.
  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a reminder of the vital role that women play in upholding societal values and the importance of unity and truth. By embracing these principles, individuals and communities can work towards a more balanced and harmonious existence.
