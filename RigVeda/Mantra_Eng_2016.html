त्वं अग्न ऋभुर आके नमस्यं तवां वाजस्य क्षुमतो राया ईशिषे |त्वं विभास्य अनु दक्षिण दावने त्वं विशिक्षुर असि यज्ञं आतनिः ||
tvam agna ṛbhur āke namasya1s tvaṁ vājasya kṣumato rāya īśiṣe | tvaṁ vi bhāsy anu dakṣi dāvane tvaṁ viśikṣur asi yajñam ātaniḥ ||
  • tvam agna: This phrase translates to "You, O Agni (Fire)." Agni is a significant deity in Vedic literature, representing fire, energy, and transformation. In real life, fire symbolizes purification and the energy that fuels our daily activities.
  • ṛbhur āke namasya1s: This means "We bow to you, O Agni, who is rich in offerings." It emphasizes the importance of respect and gratitude towards the divine forces that provide us with resources. In everyday life, this can be seen in how we honor our sources of sustenance, whether they be nature, family, or community.
  • tvaṁ vājasya kṣumato rāya īśiṣe: This translates to "You are the source of strength and prosperity." Here, Agni is recognized as a provider of vigor and wealth. In practical terms, this can relate to how we seek strength in our endeavors, whether in our careers or personal lives, and how we strive for prosperity through hard work and dedication.
  • tvaṁ vi bhāsy anu dakṣi dāvane: This means "You shine forth, O Agni, in the right direction." It suggests that Agni guides us towards the right path. In real life, this can be likened to the guidance we receive from mentors or our inner moral compass that helps us make decisions aligned with our values.
  • tvaṁ viśikṣur asi yajñam ātaniḥ: This translates to "You are the one who establishes the sacrifice." This line highlights the importance of rituals and offerings in connecting with the divine. In modern contexts, this can be seen in how we celebrate milestones and achievements, recognizing the effort and sacrifices made along the way.

  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of Agni as a divine force that provides strength, prosperity, and guidance. In our daily lives, we can apply the teachings of this mantra by recognizing the sources of our energy and resources, showing gratitude, and seeking guidance in our decisions. It reminds us of the importance of rituals and the sacrifices we make to achieve our goals, encouraging us to honor the divine presence in our lives and the interconnectedness of all things.
