इन्द्र त्वोतास आ वयं वज्रं घना ददिमहि |जयेम सं युधि स्पृधः ||
indra tvotāsa ā vayaṁ vajraṁ ghanā dadīmahi | jayema saṁ yudhi spṛdhaḥ ||
  • Indra: Refers to the king of the gods in Vedic mythology, symbolizing strength, power, and leadership.
  • Tvotāsa: This term can be interpreted as "we invoke" or "we praise," indicating a call to the divine for assistance or blessings.
  • Ā Vayaṁ: Translates to "we," emphasizing the collective nature of the invocation, suggesting unity among the people.
  • Vajraṁ: Means "thunderbolt" or "diamond," symbolizing indestructibility and immense power. It represents the strength and resilience needed in challenges.
  • Ghanā: This word can mean "dense" or "thick," often referring to the strength of the thunderbolt or the power of the divine.
  • Dadīmahi: Translates to "we create" or "we produce," indicating an active role in manifesting strength and power.
  • Jayema: Means "may we conquer" or "may we triumph," expressing a desire for victory and success.
  • Saṁ Yudhi: Refers to "in battle" or "in conflict," highlighting the context of struggle or competition.
  • Spṛdhaḥ: This term means "with desire" or "with intent," suggesting that the pursuit of victory should be driven by a strong will and determination.

The mantra can be understood as a collective invocation to Indra, seeking his blessings for strength and victory in the face of challenges. It emphasizes the importance of unity, resilience, and the active pursuit of success.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as:

  • Teamwork: In a corporate setting, teams can invoke the spirit of this mantra to foster collaboration and collective strength when facing challenges or competition.
  • Sports: Athletes and teams can draw inspiration from this mantra to cultivate a mindset of resilience and determination, especially during crucial matches or competitions.
  • Personal Challenges: Individuals facing personal struggles can use the essence of this mantra to motivate themselves to overcome obstacles and strive for success.

In conclusion, this mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the strength that comes from unity, the importance of resilience, and the active pursuit of victory. It encourages individuals and groups to seek divine support while embodying the qualities of determination and collaboration in their endeavors.
