यो वां रथो नृपती अस्ति वोळ्हा त्रिवन्धुरो वसुमाम् उष्रयामाः |आ न एनā नासत्योप यातम अभि यद वां विश्वाप्स्न्यो जिगाति ||
yo vāṁ ratho nṛpatī asti voḻhā trivandhuro vasumām̐ usrayāmā | ā na enā nāsatyopa yātam abhi yad vāṁ viśvapsnyo jigāti ||
  • Mantra: "yo vāṁ ratho nṛpatī asti voḻhā trivandhuro vasumām̐ usrayāmā | ā na enā nāsatyopa yātam abhi yad vāṁ viśvapsnyo jigāti ||"
  • Translation: This mantra can be interpreted as a prayer for guidance and support. It speaks of a chariot (ratha) that is driven by a noble leader (nṛpatī) and seeks the blessings of the divine for prosperity and success.
  • Word Breakdown:
    • yo: who
    • vāṁ: you
    • ratho: chariot
    • nṛpatī: noble leader or king
    • asti: is
    • voḻhā: blessings or support
    • trivandhuro: threefold or triple
    • vasumām̐: wealth or prosperity
    • usrayāmā: to be uplifted or elevated
    • ā: towards
    • na: not
    • enā: this
    • nāsatyopa: to the truthful or honest
    • yātam: may go
    • abhi: towards
    • yad: that
    • vāṁ: you
    • viśvapsnyo: all-encompassing or universal
    • jigāti: may thrive or prosper
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In leadership roles, this mantra can be applied to seek guidance and support from mentors or advisors, ensuring that decisions made are for the greater good.
    • In business, the mantra can be a reminder to seek prosperity and success through ethical practices and honest dealings, fostering a culture of integrity.
    • In personal life, it can serve as a motivation to uplift oneself and others, promoting a sense of community and shared success.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine guidance and support in the pursuit of prosperity and ethical leadership. It emphasizes the importance of integrity and community in achieving success. In real-life scenarios, it can be applied in various contexts such as leadership, business ethics, and personal development, reminding individuals to seek blessings and upliftment in their endeavors.
