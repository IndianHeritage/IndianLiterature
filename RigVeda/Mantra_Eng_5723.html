आ विश्ववाराश्विना गतं नः प्र तत् स्थानम् अवाचि वाम पृथिव्याम् | अश्वो न वाजी शुनपृष्ठो अस्ताद् आ यत् सेदथुर ध्रुवसे न योनीम् ||
ā viśvavārāśvinā gataṁ naḥ pra tat sthānam avāci vām pṛthivyām | aśvo na vājī śunapṛṣṭho asthād ā yat sedathur dhruvase na yonim ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā: from
    • viśvavārāśvinā: O Ashvins, the universal protectors
    • gataṁ: gone
    • naḥ: our
    • pra: forward
    • tat: that
    • sthānam: place or position
    • avāci: may speak
    • vām: your
    • pṛthivyām: on the earth
    • aśvo: horse
    • na: not
    • vājī: swift or strong
    • śunapṛṣṭho: with a well-formed back
    • asthād: has stood
    • ā: from
    • yat: which
    • sedathur: they have established
    • dhruvase: for the steadfast
    • na: not
    • yonim: womb or origin
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to the Ashvins, the divine twin horsemen in Vedic mythology, who symbolize health, prosperity, and the swift movement of time.
    • The invocation seeks guidance and support from the Ashvins to help us find our rightful place in the world.
    • It emphasizes the importance of being grounded and connected to the earth while also aspiring for higher goals.
    • The mention of horses signifies strength, speed, and the ability to overcome obstacles, which can be related to personal growth and achievement in life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, this mantra can be applied when seeking clarity in one's career path. Just as the Ashvins guide individuals, one can seek mentorship or guidance to navigate challenges.
    • In personal relationships, the mantra can remind us to communicate openly and seek understanding, much like the Ashvins who are known for their benevolence and support.
    • In times of uncertainty, invoking the qualities of the Ashvins can inspire individuals to remain steadfast and resilient, akin to a horse that stands strong despite the storms.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine assistance in finding one's place in the world while remaining grounded.
    • It teaches us the value of strength, speed, and resilience in our pursuits, encouraging us to seek help when needed.
    • In real life, this mantra can be a source of inspiration for personal and professional growth, reminding us to stay connected to our roots while striving for our aspirations.
