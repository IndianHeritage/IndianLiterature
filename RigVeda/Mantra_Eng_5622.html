तां ā रुद्रस्य मीळ्हुषो विवासे कुर्विन नंसन्ते मरुतः पुनर नः |यत सस्वर्ता जिहीळिरे यद आविर अव तद एन ईमहे तुराणाम् ||
tām̐ ā rudrasya mīḻhuṣo vivāse kuvin naṁsante marutaḥ punar naḥ | yat sasvartā jihīḻire yad āvir ava tad ena īmahe turāṇām ||
  • tām̐ ā rudrasya: This phrase refers to the essence or the qualities of Rudra, a significant deity in the Vedic tradition, often associated with storms, wind, and the hunt. Rudra embodies both destructive and protective aspects, symbolizing the duality of nature.
  • mīḻhuṣo vivāse: This can be interpreted as the harmonious existence or the balance that is sought in life. It emphasizes the importance of living in accordance with natural laws and the universe.
  • kuvin naṁsante marutaḥ: Here, "kuvin" refers to the wise or knowledgeable, while "marutaḥ" denotes the storm gods or winds. This line suggests that the wise ones are not disturbed by the chaotic forces of nature, indicating a state of inner peace and resilience.
  • punar naḥ: This phrase translates to "again for us," implying a request for renewal or restoration. It signifies the cyclical nature of life and the need for rejuvenation.
  • yat sasvartā jihīḻire: This part can be understood as a call to the forces that bring forth life and vitality. It acknowledges the importance of these forces in sustaining existence.
  • yad āvir ava: This translates to "that which has manifested," indicating the importance of recognizing and embracing the realities of life as they present themselves.
  • tad ena īmahe turāṇām: This line expresses a desire to connect with the divine or higher powers, seeking guidance and support from the forces that govern the universe.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking balance and harmony in life amidst the chaos represented by nature's forces. It emphasizes the importance of wisdom, resilience, and the cyclical nature of existence. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as during times of personal turmoil or societal upheaval, where individuals can draw strength from the understanding that challenges are part of a larger cycle. It encourages individuals to seek inner peace and connect with the greater forces of the universe, fostering a sense of unity and purpose in their lives.
