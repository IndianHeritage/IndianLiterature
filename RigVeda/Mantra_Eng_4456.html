दशस्य नः पूर्वणीका होतर देवैभिर अग्ने अग्निभिर इधानः |रायः सूनो सहसो वावसानाऽति स्रसेमा वृजनं नांहः ||
daśasyā naḥ purvaṇīka hotar devebhir agne agnibhir idhānaḥ | rāyaḥ sūno sahaso vāvasānā ati srasema vṛjanaṁ nāṁhaḥ ||
  • daśasyā naḥ purvaṇīka hotar: This phrase can be translated to "O Agni, the hotar (priest) of the ten (sacred)". Here, "daśa" refers to the number ten, which can symbolize completeness or wholeness in various contexts, such as the ten directions or ten senses in human experience.
  • devebhir agne agnibhir idhānaḥ: This means "with the gods, O Agni, we invoke you". Agni, the fire god, represents energy, transformation, and purification. The invocation of Agni signifies the importance of divine energy in our lives, akin to how we seek guidance and support from higher powers in our daily endeavors.
  • rāyaḥ sūno sahaso vāvasānā: This translates to "may we attain wealth and prosperity". "Rāyaḥ" refers to wealth, and "sūno" implies offspring or progeny. This line emphasizes the desire for abundance not just for oneself but for future generations, reflecting the universal human aspiration for prosperity and security.
  • ati srasema vṛjanaṁ nāṁhaḥ: This can be interpreted as "may we be free from obstacles". The term "vṛjana" refers to obstacles or hindrances, and "srasema" suggests liberation or freedom. This line highlights the importance of overcoming challenges in life, which is a common theme in personal growth and development.

  • Significance: This mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking divine guidance, prosperity, and the removal of obstacles in life. It reflects the interconnectedness of human aspirations with spiritual energy, emphasizing the role of Agni as a mediator between the material and spiritual realms.
  • Real-life Application: In practical terms, this mantra can be applied in various life situations, such as during rituals for new beginnings, business ventures, or family gatherings. For instance, when starting a new job or project, one might recite this mantra to invoke positive energy and seek divine blessings for success and prosperity.
  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of spiritual connection in our pursuit of material success. It encourages individuals to seek divine support in overcoming life's challenges while striving for prosperity, not just for themselves but for future generations. By integrating these principles into daily life, one can cultivate a balanced approach to personal and spiritual growth.
