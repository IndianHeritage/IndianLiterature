तवे राया इन्द्र तोषतमाः प्रणेतारः कस्य चिद ऋतायोः |ते षु णो मरुतो मृळयन्तु ये स्मा पुरा गातूयन्तीव देवाः ||
tve rāya indra tośatamāḥ praṇetāraḥ kasya cid ṛtāyoḥ | te ṣu ṇo maruto mṛḻayantu ye smā purā gātūyantīva devāḥ ||
  • tve rāya indra tośatamāḥ: This phrase can be translated as "O Indra, the one who brings prosperity." Indra is often regarded as the king of the gods in Vedic literature, symbolizing strength and power. In real life, this can be related to leaders or figures who bring prosperity and success to their communities or organizations.
  • praṇetāraḥ kasya cid ṛtāyoḥ: This translates to "the guides of truth." It emphasizes the importance of guidance in the pursuit of truth and righteousness. In everyday life, this can be seen in mentors, teachers, or leaders who help individuals navigate their paths with integrity.
  • te ṣu ṇo maruto mṛḻayantu: This means "may the Maruts (storm gods) bring us peace." The Maruts are associated with storms and winds, symbolizing change and dynamism. In a real-world context, this can be interpreted as a call for positive change and harmony in our lives, especially during turbulent times.
  • ye smā purā gātūyantīva devāḥ: This translates to "those gods who have come to us in the past." It reflects on the blessings and support received from divine forces throughout history. In practical terms, this can be related to the gratitude we feel for the support of our ancestors, culture, and community that shapes our present.

  • Conclusion: This mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking prosperity, guidance, peace, and gratitude. Its significance lies in the recognition of the divine forces that influence our lives and the importance of righteous leadership and mentorship. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as in leadership roles, educational settings, and during times of change or uncertainty. By invoking these principles, individuals and communities can strive for a harmonious and prosperous existence, guided by truth and supported by the wisdom of the past.
