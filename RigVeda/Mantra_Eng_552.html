अभूदु पाम etave पन्था ऋतस्य साधुया | अदर्शि वि श्रुतिर्दिवः ||
abhūd u pāram etave panthā ṛtasya sādhuyā | adarśi vi srutir divaḥ ||
  • Mantra: "abhūd u pāram etave panthā ṛtasya sādhuyā | adarśi vi srutir divaḥ ||"
  • Translation: "The path of righteousness has been revealed; it is the way of truth and virtue. The divine wisdom has been perceived from the heavens."

  • Word-by-Word Explanation:
    • abhūd: has come into being, has emerged
    • u: indeed, certainly
    • pāram: beyond, transcendent
    • etave: this, of this kind
    • panthā: path, way
    • ṛtasya: of truth, of righteousness
    • sādhuyā: by the virtuous, by the righteous
    • adarśi: has been perceived, has been seen
    • vi: indeed, truly
    • srutir: wisdom, knowledge
    • divaḥ: of the heavens, divine

  • Real-Life Examples:
    • Path of Righteousness: In everyday life, individuals often face moral dilemmas. For instance, a businessperson may encounter a situation where they can either cut corners to maximize profit or adhere to ethical practices. Choosing the latter aligns with the "path of righteousness" mentioned in the mantra.
    • Divine Wisdom: Many people seek guidance through meditation or prayer, hoping to gain clarity on their life choices. This pursuit of wisdom reflects the idea of "divine wisdom" being perceived from the heavens, as individuals strive to connect with a higher power for insight.

  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its emphasis on the importance of following a righteous path and seeking wisdom. In a world filled with challenges and ethical dilemmas, this mantra serves as a reminder to adhere to truth and virtue. It encourages individuals to look beyond immediate gains and to seek a deeper understanding of their actions and their consequences. By embodying these principles, one can contribute positively to society and foster a sense of harmony and integrity in their personal and professional lives.
