या उद्नः फालिगं भिनन् न्याक् सिंधूम् आवसृजत् |यो गोषु पक्वं धारयत् ||
ya udnaḥ phaligam bhinan nya1k sindhūm̐r avāsṛjat | yo goṣu pakvaṁ dhārayat ||
  • Mantra: "ya udnaḥ phaligam bhinan nya1k sindhūm̐r avāsṛjat | yo goṣu pakvaṁ dhārayat ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ya: who
    • udnaḥ: of the one who rises or ascends
    • phaligam: the fruit or result
    • bhinan: breaking or splitting
    • nya1k: into many
    • sindhūm̐r: the ocean or vastness
    • avāsṛjat: has released or brought forth
    • yo: who
    • goṣu: in the cows or in the beings
    • pakvaṁ: ripened or matured
    • dhārayat: sustains or holds
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of a divine force or entity that rises and brings forth the fruits of creation.
    • The phrase "breaking into many" suggests the idea of diversity and multiplicity in creation, akin to how a single seed can grow into a vast forest.
    • The reference to the ocean symbolizes the vastness of existence and the interconnectedness of all life.
    • The second part of the mantra emphasizes the nurturing aspect, where the divine sustains the matured beings, similar to how a mother nurtures her children.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In nature, we see how a single tree can produce countless fruits, each containing seeds that can grow into new trees, illustrating the concept of "breaking into many."
    • In human society, a single idea can inspire a movement, leading to significant changes and innovations, reflecting the divine force that brings forth results.
    • The nurturing aspect can be seen in parenting, where caregivers provide support and sustenance to help individuals reach their potential.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the themes of creation, diversity, and sustenance, reminding us of the interconnectedness of all beings.
    • Its significance lies in recognizing the divine forces at play in our lives, encouraging us to appreciate the fruits of our actions and the nurturing relationships we cultivate.
    • In real life, this mantra can inspire us to embrace diversity, support one another, and acknowledge the larger forces that shape our existence.
