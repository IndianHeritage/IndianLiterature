अश्याम ते सुमतिं देवयज्ञया क्षयद्वीरस्य तव रुद्र मीढ्वः |सुम्नायन्निद विषो अस्माकं आ चरारीष्टवीराः जुहवाम ते हविः ||
aśyāma te sumatiṁ devayajyayā kṣayadvīrasya tava rudra mīḍhvaḥ | sumnāyann id viśo asmākam ā carāriṣṭavīrā juhavāma te haviḥ ||
  • Mantra: aśyāma te sumatiṁ devayajyayā kṣayadvīrasya tava rudra mīḍhvaḥ | sumnāyann id viśo asmākam ā carāriṣṭavīrā juhavāma te haviḥ ||
  • Translation: "O Rudra, may we attain auspicious intelligence through your divine worship. May we be free from the destruction of our enemies, and may we offer you our oblations with the strength of our valor."

  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • aśyāma: auspicious, beneficial
    • te: your
    • sumatiṁ: intelligence, wisdom
    • devayajyayā: through divine worship
    • kṣayadvīrasya: of the one who destroys enemies
    • tava: your
    • rudra: a form of the divine, often associated with Shiva
    • mīḍhvaḥ: may we attain
    • sumnāyann: auspiciously
    • id: this
    • viśo: of the community
    • asmākam: our
    • ā: towards
    • carāriṣṭavīrā: those who are valorous and strong
    • juhavāma: we offer
    • te: to you
    • haviḥ: our offerings

  • Significance:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking divine guidance and wisdom in our lives.
    • It reflects a desire for protection from adversities and the strength to overcome challenges.
    • The invocation of Rudra signifies a call for transformation and the removal of obstacles.
  • Real-Life Application:
    • In personal life, individuals can recite this mantra during times of difficulty, seeking clarity and strength to face challenges.
    • In community settings, it can be used to foster unity and collective strength against common adversities.
    • In professional environments, it serves as a reminder to approach tasks with wisdom and valor, ensuring that decisions are made with a clear mind and strong intent.

  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the need for divine support in our endeavors.
    • It encourages individuals and communities to cultivate wisdom and strength, ensuring that they can navigate life's challenges effectively.
    • By invoking the qualities of Rudra, we align ourselves with the forces of transformation and protection, making this mantra relevant in various aspects of life.
