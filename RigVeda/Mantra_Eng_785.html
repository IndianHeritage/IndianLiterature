विराय और्णोद दुरः पुरुषः पिपेश नाकं स्त्रिभिर दमूनाः ||
vi rāya aurṇod duraḥ purukṣuḥ pipeśa nākaṁ stṛbhir damūnāḥ ||
  • vi rāya: This phrase can be interpreted as "to the wealth" or "to the prosperity." It signifies the pursuit of abundance and success in life.
  • aurṇod: This term refers to "the golden" or "the radiant." It symbolizes something precious and valuable, often associated with enlightenment or divine blessings.
  • duraḥ: Meaning "far" or "difficult," this word suggests challenges or obstacles that one may face in the pursuit of their goals.
  • purukṣuḥ: This can be translated as "the great man" or "the strong individual." It represents a person of strength, character, and determination.
  • pipeśa: This term means "to flow" or "to spread." It indicates the idea of abundance and the natural flow of resources or blessings.
  • nākaṁ: Referring to "the sky" or "heaven," this word symbolizes higher aspirations, spiritual goals, or the ultimate destination of one's efforts.
  • stṛbhir: This word means "by women" or "through the feminine." It highlights the role of women in society and their contributions to prosperity and well-being.
  • damūnāḥ: This term can be interpreted as "with restraint" or "with control." It emphasizes the importance of discipline and moderation in achieving success.

In summary, this mantra encapsulates the journey towards prosperity and success, acknowledging the challenges one may face along the way. It emphasizes the importance of strength, the flow of resources, and the contributions of women, while also highlighting the need for discipline and control. In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as in personal development, business endeavors, and community building. It serves as a reminder that while the path to success may be fraught with difficulties, with determination, collaboration, and self-discipline, one can achieve their goals and reach new heights.
