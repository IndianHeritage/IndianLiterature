यद धा यान्ति मरुतः सं ह ब्रुवते 'ध्वन्न आ |शृणोति कश्चिद एषाम् ||
yad dha yānti marutaḥ saṁ ha bruvate 'dhvann ā | śṛṇoti kaś cid eṣām ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yad: whatever
    • dha: they hold or possess
    • yānti: they go or move
    • marutaḥ: of the winds or storm gods
    • saṁ: together or in unison
    • ha: indeed or certainly
    • bruvate: they speak or declare
    • adhvann: on the path or journey
    • ā: towards or in the direction of
    • śṛṇoti: hears or listens
    • kaś cid: anyone or someone
    • eṣām: of these
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the collective movement and communication of the Maruts, the storm gods or winds, who are often associated with strength and power.
    • The phrase "yad dha yānti marutaḥ" suggests that whatever the winds carry or possess, they move together in harmony.
    • The line "saṁ ha bruvate 'dhvann ā" indicates that they speak or declare something significant on their journey.
    • Finally, "śṛṇoti kaś cid eṣām" implies that someone, anyone, is able to hear or listen to what they convey.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In nature, the winds can symbolize change and movement. For instance, when a storm approaches, it brings with it a sense of urgency and transformation, much like the Maruts in the mantra.
    • In human life, this can be likened to the collective efforts of a community working together towards a common goal, where each individual's contribution is vital for the success of the whole.
    • In communication, the idea that "someone hears" can relate to the importance of listening in relationships, where understanding and empathy are crucial for harmony.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its emphasis on unity, communication, and the power of collective action.
    • It teaches us that just as the winds move together and communicate, we too should strive for harmony in our interactions and endeavors.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire teamwork, collaboration, and the importance of listening to one another, fostering a sense of community and shared purpose.
