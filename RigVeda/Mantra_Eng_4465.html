स सत्पतिः शवसां हन्ति वृत्रम अग्ने विप्रो वि पाणेर भारति वाजम् |यं त्वं प्रचेत ऋतजात राया सजोषा नप्त्रापां हिनोषि ||
sa satpatiḥ śavasā hanti vṛtram agne vipro vi paṇer bharti vājam | yaṁ tvam praceta ṛtajāta rāyā sajoṣā naptrāpāṁ hinoṣi ||
  • sa satpatiḥ: This phrase can be translated as "the one who is the lord of the seven." In the context of the Rigveda, it often refers to a divine being or a powerful entity that governs various aspects of existence.
  • śavasā hanti vṛtram: This means "with strength, he kills the Vritra." Vritra is a serpent or dragon-like figure in Vedic mythology, representing chaos and obstruction. The act of killing Vritra symbolizes the triumph of order over chaos.
  • agne vipro vi paṇer bharti vājam: Here, "Agne" refers to Agni, the fire god, who is often seen as a mediator between humans and the divine. "Vipra" means wise or learned, and "vājam" refers to wealth or resources. This line suggests that Agni, through the wisdom of the learned, bestows wealth and prosperity.
  • yaṁ tvam praceta ṛtajāta rāyā: This translates to "that which you, O Agni, have produced with your intelligence and truth." It emphasizes the importance of wisdom and truth in the creation and sustenance of life.
  • sajoṣā naptrāpāṁ hinoṣi: This means "you remove the obstacles of those who are devoted." It highlights the idea that devotion and sincerity can lead to the removal of difficulties and the attainment of goals.

Conclusion: This mantra encapsulates the essence of overcoming chaos through wisdom and devotion. It signifies the importance of divine guidance in achieving prosperity and success in life. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied to various situations where individuals face challenges or obstacles. By seeking knowledge, remaining devoted to one's goals, and relying on inner strength, one can navigate through difficulties and emerge victorious. The mantra serves as a reminder of the power of faith and the importance of aligning oneself with higher principles to achieve a harmonious and prosperous life.
