प्र हिन्वानो जनिता रोडस्य रथो न वाजं सनीष्यन्न अयासीत् | इन्द्रं गच्छन्न आयुधा संशिशानो विश्व वसु हस्तयोः आदधानाḥ ||
pra hinvāno janitā rodasyo ratho na vājaṁ saniṣyann ayāsīt | indraṁ gacchann āyudhā saṁśiśāno viśvā vasu hastayor ādadhānaḥ ||
  • pra hinvāno: This phrase can be interpreted as "removing obstacles" or "overcoming challenges." In real life, this can relate to situations where individuals face difficulties, such as pursuing a career or overcoming personal struggles. For example, a student preparing for competitive exams may need to overcome distractions and self-doubt to succeed.
  • janitā rodasyo: This translates to "the creator of the world" or "the one who brings forth." It signifies the power of creation and innovation. In a modern context, this can be seen in entrepreneurs who create new businesses or technologies that change the landscape of society, like Steve Jobs with Apple.
  • ratho na vājaṁ saniṣyann ayāsīt: This can be understood as "the chariot that does not tire." It symbolizes perseverance and resilience. In everyday life, this can be likened to athletes who train tirelessly for competitions, demonstrating that consistent effort leads to success.
  • indraṁ gacchann: This means "going towards Indra," who is often seen as a deity of strength and power. This can represent the pursuit of strength and leadership in one’s life. For instance, a leader in a community who works towards uplifting others embodies this pursuit of strength.
  • āyudhā saṁśiśāno: This phrase translates to "armed with weapons." In a metaphorical sense, it can refer to being equipped with knowledge and skills. For example, a student who prepares thoroughly for exams is "armed" with the knowledge needed to succeed.
  • viśvā vasu hastayor ādadhānaḥ: This can be interpreted as "holding all the treasures in both hands." It signifies abundance and the ability to harness resources effectively. In real life, this can be seen in individuals who manage their finances wisely, investing in their future while enjoying the present.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of overcoming obstacles, creativity, perseverance, strength, preparation, and resourcefulness. Its significance lies in the holistic approach to life, encouraging individuals to strive for success while being equipped with the right tools and mindset. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire people to face challenges head-on, innovate in their fields, and lead with strength and wisdom, ultimately contributing to personal and communal growth.
