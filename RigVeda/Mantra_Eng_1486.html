ऊर्ध्वा ते अनु सूनृतā मनस तिष्ठतु जानती |नियुत्वता रथेनā याहि दावने वायो मखस्य दावने ||
ūrdhvā te anu sūnṛtā manas tiṣṭhatu jānatī | niyutvatā rathenā yāhi dāvane vāyo makhasya dāvane ||
  • ūrdhvā te anu sūnṛtā manas tiṣṭhatu jānatī
    • ūrdhvā: This word means "upward" or "higher." It signifies the aspiration to rise above challenges and limitations.
    • te: This translates to "your," indicating a personal connection or direction towards the divine or higher power.
    • anu: Meaning "following" or "in accordance with," it suggests alignment with a higher purpose or truth.
    • sūnṛtā: This term refers to "truth" or "righteousness." It emphasizes the importance of living a life based on truth and integrity.
    • manas: This means "mind" or "intellect." It highlights the role of mental clarity and focus in achieving one's goals.
    • tiṣṭhatu: This translates to "may it stand" or "may it remain," indicating a desire for stability and permanence in one's pursuits.
    • jānatī: Meaning "knowing" or "understanding," it emphasizes the importance of knowledge and awareness in guiding one's actions.
  • niyutvatā rathenā yāhi dāvane vāyo makhasya dāvane
    • niyutvatā: This word means "with speed" or "swiftly," suggesting the need for prompt action and decisiveness.
    • rathenā: This translates to "by the chariot," symbolizing the journey of life and the means by which we navigate our paths.
    • yāhi: Meaning "go" or "move," it encourages taking action and progressing towards one's goals.
    • dāvane: This term refers to "the fire" or "sacrifice," indicating the transformative power of dedication and effort.
    • vāyo: Meaning "of the wind," it symbolizes the dynamic and ever-changing nature of life.
    • makhasya: This translates to "of the sacrifice," emphasizing the importance of commitment and the willingness to give for a greater cause.

Conclusion:

The mantra encapsulates the essence of striving for higher truths and living a life of integrity and knowledge. It encourages individuals to rise above challenges, act swiftly, and navigate their life's journey with purpose and dedication. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as:

  • In personal development, it inspires individuals to pursue their goals with clarity and commitment.
  • In leadership, it emphasizes the importance of integrity and truthfulness in guiding others.
  • In community service, it highlights the significance of sacrifice and dedication to the welfare of others.

Ultimately, this mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of aligning one's actions with higher principles and the transformative power of dedication and truth in achieving success and fulfillment in life.
