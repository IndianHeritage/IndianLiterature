आ यद वां योषणा रथम अतीष्ठद वाजिनीवासू |विश्वान्य अश्विनां युवं प्र धीतान्य अगच्छतम् ||
ā yad vāṁ yoṣaṇā ratham atiṣṭhad vājinīvasū | viśvāny aśvinā yuvam pra dhītāny agacchatam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā yad: Whatever
    • vāṁ: Your
    • yoṣaṇā: With the women
    • ratham: Chariot
    • atiṣṭhad: Stood
    • vājinīvasū: Like a horse
    • viśvāny: All
    • aśvinā: O Ashvins (the twin deities)
    • yuvam: You two
    • pra: Forward
    • dhītāny: The thoughts
    • agacchatam: May go
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the Ashvins, the twin deities associated with health, medicine, and the dawn.
    • The phrase "ā yad vāṁ yoṣaṇā ratham atiṣṭhad vājinīvasū" suggests a scenario where the chariot, symbolizing progress and movement, is supported by the strength and grace of women.
    • The invocation of "viśvāny aśvinā yuvam" emphasizes the collective power and unity of the Ashvins, urging them to guide and bless the thoughts and actions of the speaker.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern society, the empowerment of women can be likened to the strength of the chariot, where their contributions drive progress in various fields such as business, science, and politics.
    • The Ashvins can be seen as a metaphor for teamwork and collaboration, where individuals come together to achieve common goals, much like how healthcare professionals work together to provide holistic care.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of unity, strength, and collaboration in achieving success and well-being.
    • It is applicable in real-life scenarios such as community building, teamwork in workplaces, and promoting gender equality.
    • By invoking the blessings of the Ashvins, individuals and communities can aspire to move forward with clarity and purpose, ensuring that all voices are heard and valued.
