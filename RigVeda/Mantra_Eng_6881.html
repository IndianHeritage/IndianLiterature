प्रातनं होतारं ईड्यं जुष्टम अग्निं कविक्रतुम् |अध्वराणाम् अभिश्रियम् ||
pratnaṁ hotāram īḍyaṁ juṣṭam agniṁ kavikratum | adhvarāṇām abhiśriyam ||
  • pratnaṁ: This word translates to "prayer" or "invocation." It signifies the act of reaching out to the divine or higher powers through sincere requests or offerings.
  • hotāram: This term refers to the "one who is invoked" or "the priest." In the context of the mantra, it indicates the role of the priest or the divine being who is called upon to accept the offerings.
  • īḍyaṁ: This means "worthy of worship" or "adorable." It highlights the qualities of the divine that make them deserving of reverence and devotion.
  • juṣṭam: This word translates to "pleasing" or "enjoyable." It suggests that the offerings made are not only accepted but also bring joy to the divine.
  • agniṁ: This refers to "Agni," the fire god in Vedic tradition. Agni is considered a mediator between humans and the divine, representing transformation and purification.
  • kavikratum: This term means "the will of the wise" or "the intention of the seer." It emphasizes the importance of wisdom and intention behind the offerings made to the divine.
  • adhvarāṇām: This translates to "of the sacrifices" or "of the rituals." It indicates the context of the mantra within the framework of Vedic rituals and sacrifices.
  • abhiśriyam: This means "the glory" or "the prosperity." It signifies the blessings and abundance that come from performing the rituals correctly and with devotion.

In summary, this mantra is a prayer invoking Agni, the fire god, as a worthy recipient of offerings. It emphasizes the importance of intention, wisdom, and the joy that comes from performing rituals. The mantra serves as a reminder of the connection between the material and spiritual worlds, highlighting how sincere offerings can lead to divine blessings.


Significance and Real-Life Application:

  • This mantra can be applied in daily life by encouraging individuals to approach their goals and aspirations with intention and sincerity, much like a prayer.
  • In a modern context, it can be seen as a call to engage in practices that promote personal growth, community service, and environmental stewardship, reflecting the qualities of Agni as a transformative force.
  • By recognizing the importance of wisdom and intention in our actions, we can create a more harmonious and prosperous life, both for ourselves and for those around us.
