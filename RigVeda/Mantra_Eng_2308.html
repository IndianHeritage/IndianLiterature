सरस्वति त्वम् अस्मांं अविद्धि मरुत्वती धृतशती जेषि शत्रून् |त्यं चिच्छर्धन्तं तविषीयमानं इन्द्रो हन्ति वृषभं शण्डिकानाम् ||
sarasvati tvam asmām̐ aviḍḍhi marutvatī dhṛṣatī jeṣi śatrūn | tyaṁ cic chardhantaṁ taviṣīyamāṇam indro hanti vṛṣabhaṁ śaṇḍikānām ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • Sarasvati: Refers to the goddess of knowledge, wisdom, and learning.
    • Tvam: You (addressing Sarasvati).
    • Asmām̐: In us or among us.
    • Aviḍḍhi: Do not abandon or forsake.
    • Marutvatī: Like the Maruts, the storm deities, representing strength and power.
    • Dhṛṣatī: The one who is courageous or bold.
    • Jeṣi: The victorious one.
    • Śatrūn: Enemies or adversaries.
    • Tyaṁ: That (referring to the strength or power).
    • Cic: Indeed or truly.
    • Shardhantaṁ: The one who is strong or powerful.
    • Taviṣīyamāṇam: Being empowered or endowed with strength.
    • Indro: Indra, the king of gods, representing authority and power.
    • Hanti: Kills or destroys.
    • Vṛṣabhaṁ: The bull, symbolizing strength and virility.
    • Śaṇḍikānām: Of the weak or those who are feeble.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes Sarasvati, asking her not to forsake us and to bestow her wisdom and strength.
    • It emphasizes the importance of courage and victory over adversaries, suggesting that with knowledge and strength, one can overcome challenges.
    • The reference to Indra signifies the need for divine support in achieving success and overcoming obstacles.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In academic pursuits, students often seek the blessings of Sarasvati for knowledge and wisdom to excel in their studies.
    • In competitive environments, such as sports or business, individuals may draw upon the qualities of courage and strength to face their opponents.
    • In times of personal challenges, people may recite this mantra to invoke inner strength and resilience to overcome difficulties.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of knowledge, courage, and divine support in overcoming life's challenges.
    • It serves as a reminder that with wisdom and strength, one can achieve victory over adversities.
    • In real life, it encourages individuals to seek knowledge and inner strength, fostering a mindset of resilience and determination.
