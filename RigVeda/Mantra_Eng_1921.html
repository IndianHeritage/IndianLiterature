आ तिष्ठतं सुवृतं यो रथो वाम अनु व्रतानि वर्तते हविष्मान् |येन नराः नासत्येषयध्यै वर्तिर याथस तनयाय त्मने च ||
ā tiṣṭhataṁ suvṛtaṁ yo ratho vām anu vratāni vartate haviṣmān | yena narā nāsatyeṣayadhyai vartir yāthas tanayāya tmane ca ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā tiṣṭhataṁ: "May you stand firm" - a call for stability and strength.
    • suvṛtaṁ: "Well-directed" - signifies the importance of having a righteous path.
    • yo: "Who" - refers to the individual or entity being addressed.
    • ratho: "Chariot" - symbolizes the journey of life and the means of progress.
    • vām: "Your" - personalizes the mantra, making it relevant to the individual.
    • anu: "Following" - indicates the importance of following a path or guidance.
    • vratāni: "Vows" - represents commitments or promises made.
    • vartate: "Is engaged" - suggests active participation in one's duties.
    • haviṣmān: "With offerings" - implies the act of giving or contributing.
    • yena: "By whom" - refers to the source or the one who enables.
    • narā: "Men" - signifies humanity or individuals.
    • nāsatyeṣayadhyai: "In the realm of truth" - emphasizes the importance of truthfulness.
    • vartir: "Conduct" - refers to behavior or way of living.
    • yāthas: "As per" - indicates alignment with certain principles.
    • tanayāya: "For the offspring" - highlights the significance of future generations.
    • tmane: "For oneself" - emphasizes self-care and personal responsibility.
  • Significance:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of standing firm in one's values and commitments.
    • It encourages individuals to follow a righteous path while actively engaging in their duties.
    • The reference to offerings suggests that contributing to the community and society is vital.
    • It highlights the importance of truthfulness in one's conduct and the impact it has on future generations.
    • Ultimately, it calls for a balance between personal responsibility and the well-being of others.
  • Real-Life Application:
    • In a professional setting, this mantra can inspire individuals to remain committed to their work ethics and responsibilities.
    • In personal relationships, it encourages honesty and integrity, fostering trust and respect.
    • In community service, it highlights the importance of giving back and contributing to the welfare of society.
    • In parenting, it serves as a reminder to instill values of truthfulness and responsibility in children.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a guiding principle for living a life of integrity, responsibility, and service to others.
    • By embodying these values, individuals can create a positive impact on their lives and the lives of those around them.
    • Ultimately, it reinforces the idea that personal growth and societal well-being are interconnected.
