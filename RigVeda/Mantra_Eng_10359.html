श्रत ते दधामि प्रथमाया मन्यवे 'हन यद वृतं नर्यं विवे र अपः |उभे यत त्वा भवतो रोदसी अनु रेजते शुष्मात पृथिवी चिद अद्रिवः ||
śrat te dadhāmi prathamāya manyave 'han yad vṛtraṁ naryaṁ viver apaḥ | ubhe yat tvā bhavato rodasī anu rejate śuṣmāt pṛthivī cid adrivaḥ ||
  • śrat te dadhāmi prathamāya manyave: This phrase can be translated as "I offer you my first thought or intention." Here, the speaker is expressing a deep respect and reverence, indicating that the intention behind the offering is of utmost importance. In real life, this can be likened to the way we approach our goals or relationships, emphasizing the significance of our initial intentions.
  • 'han yad vṛtraṁ naryaṁ viver apaḥ: This part translates to "to slay the Vritra, the one who obstructs the waters." Vritra is often seen as a demon or a force that blocks the flow of life-giving water. In a modern context, this can symbolize overcoming obstacles in our lives that prevent us from achieving our goals or accessing our true potential, much like how individuals face challenges in their careers or personal lives.
  • ubhe yat tvā bhavato rodasī anu rejate: This translates to "both the heavens and the earth are illuminated by you." This line signifies the omnipresence and the illuminating power of the divine. In everyday life, this can be related to the idea that our actions and thoughts can have a profound impact on our surroundings, much like how a positive attitude can brighten the atmosphere in a workplace or home.
  • śuṣmāt pṛthivī cid adrivaḥ: This can be interpreted as "the earth is filled with your essence." It suggests that the divine presence permeates all aspects of life. In practical terms, this can be seen in how nature and the environment reflect the interconnectedness of all living beings, reminding us to respect and nurture our planet.

  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of intention, the need to overcome obstacles, and the recognition of the divine presence in all aspects of life. Its significance lies in encouraging individuals to set positive intentions, confront challenges with courage, and acknowledge the interconnectedness of all existence. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in personal development, environmental consciousness, and fostering harmonious relationships, ultimately guiding us towards a more fulfilling and purposeful life.
