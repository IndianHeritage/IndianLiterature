ऐभ्यः समान्याः दिशास्मभ्यं जेषि योत्सि च |सुन्वद्भ्यो रन्धया कं चिद अव्रतं हृणायन्तं चिद अव्रतम् ||
aibhyaḥ samānyā diśāsmabhyaṁ jeṣi yotsi ca | sunvadbhyo randhayā kaṁ cid avrataṁ hṛṇāyantaṁ cid avratam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • aibhyaḥ: From fear or danger
    • samānyā: Common or shared
    • diśāsmabhyaṁ: To all directions or paths
    • jeṣi: Victorious or conquer
    • yotsi: Engage in battle or conflict
    • ca: And
    • sunvadbhyo: To those who speak well or positively
    • randhayā: To those who are united or connected
    • kaṁ cid: Anyone or anything
    • avrataṁ: Without a vow or commitment
    • hṛṇāyantaṁ: To those who are in need or seeking
    • cid: Indeed
    • avratam: Without a vow or commitment
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks to the idea of overcoming fear and engaging in a collective effort towards victory.
    • It emphasizes the importance of unity and positive communication among individuals.
    • The phrase "to all directions" suggests that the message is universal and applicable in various contexts.
    • It encourages individuals to support those who are in need, regardless of their commitments or vows.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace setting, this mantra can be applied when teams face challenges. By fostering open communication and collaboration, team members can overcome obstacles together.
    • In community service, individuals can come together to help those in need, regardless of their personal commitments, embodying the spirit of unity and support.
    • In personal relationships, this mantra encourages friends and family to communicate positively and support each other through difficult times.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for unity, support, and positive communication in the face of challenges.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal relationships to community efforts and workplace dynamics.
    • By embodying the principles of this mantra, individuals can create a more supportive and victorious environment for themselves and others.
