उत तिष्ठसि स्वाहुतो घृतानि प्रति मोदसे |यत त्वा श्रुचः समस्थिरन् ||
ut tiṣṭhasi svāhuto ghṛtāni prati modase | yat tvā srucaḥ samasthiran ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ut: indeed, or certainly
    • tiṣṭhasi: you stand or you are established
    • svāhuto: invoked or called upon
    • ghṛtāni: clarified butter or ghee, often used in rituals
    • prati: towards or in relation to
    • modase: you delight or you are pleased
    • yat: that which
    • tvā: you
    • srucaḥ: offerings or oblations
    • samasthiran: all together or in harmony
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks to the divine presence that stands established and is invoked through offerings.
    • The use of "ghṛtāni" signifies the importance of purity and nourishment in spiritual practices, as ghee is often associated with auspiciousness and prosperity.
    • The phrase "prati modase" indicates that the divine is pleased by these offerings, suggesting a reciprocal relationship between humans and the divine.
    • The concluding part, "yat tvā srucaḥ samasthiran," emphasizes the unity and harmony in the offerings made to the divine, highlighting the collective nature of worship.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In Hindu rituals, offerings of ghee are made to deities during ceremonies, symbolizing the nourishment of the soul and the community.
    • In modern contexts, this mantra can be reflected in community gatherings where people come together to celebrate and offer their collective gratitude, such as during festivals or communal prayers.
    • The idea of standing together in harmony can be seen in social movements where individuals unite for a common cause, invoking a sense of collective strength and purpose.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of establishing a connection with the divine through pure offerings and collective harmony.
    • It teaches us that our actions, when performed with sincerity and unity, can invoke divine blessings and foster a sense of community.
    • In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios, from personal spiritual practices to community service, emphasizing the significance of intention and togetherness in our endeavors.
