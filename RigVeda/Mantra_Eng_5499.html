मन्त्र: अपि ष्टुतः सविता देवो अस्तु यं आ चिद् विश्वे वसवो गृणन्ति | स नः स्तोमान नमस्य1श चनो धाद् विश्वेभिः पातु पायुभिर न सूरीन् ||
api ṣṭutaḥ savitā devo astu yam ā cid viśve vasavo gṛṇanti | sa naḥ stomān namasya1ś cano dhād viśvebhiḥ pātu pāyubhir ni sūrīn ||
  • api ṣṭutaḥ savitā devo astu: This phrase translates to "May the praised Savitar, the divine, be present." Savitar is a solar deity in the Rigveda, representing the sun's life-giving energy. In real life, this can be likened to the importance of the sun in agriculture, where sunlight is essential for crops to grow and thrive.
  • yam ā cid viśve vasavo gṛṇanti: This means "Whom all the Vasus (elements) praise." The Vasus are a group of deities representing various natural elements. This line emphasizes the interconnectedness of nature and the divine, similar to how different elements of nature work together to sustain life, such as water, air, and earth collaborating to create a balanced ecosystem.
  • sa naḥ stomān namasya1ś cano dhād: This translates to "May He grant us the hymns of praise." This line suggests a request for divine inspiration and guidance in our endeavors. In a real-life context, this can be seen in the way artists, writers, and musicians seek inspiration from nature or spirituality to create their works.
  • viśvebhiḥ pātu pāyubhir ni sūrīn: This means "May He protect us with all His powers." This is a plea for protection and support from the divine. In everyday life, this can be related to the need for safety and security, whether it be physical, emotional, or spiritual. People often seek protection through rituals, prayers, or community support.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a deep reverence for the divine and the natural world, highlighting the importance of harmony between humanity and nature. It serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of all elements of life and the need for divine guidance and protection. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire individuals to seek balance in their lives, appreciate the beauty of nature, and foster a sense of community and support among one another. It encourages us to recognize the divine presence in our daily lives and to express gratitude for the blessings we receive.
