एवेद यूने युवतयो नमन्त यद इम उषन्न उषतीर एत्य अच्छ |सं जानते मनसा सं चिकीत्रे 'ध्वर्यवो धिषणापश च देविः ||
eved yūne yuvatayo namanta yad īm uśann uśatīr ety accha | saṁ jānate manasā saṁ cikitre 'dhvaryavo dhiṣaṇāpaś ca devīḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • eved: Indeed, this
    • yūne: in the union
    • yuvatayo: of the youthful ones
    • namanta: who bow down
    • yad: that
    • īm: which
    • uśann: is shining
    • uśatīr: the auspicious
    • ety: comes
    • accha: good
    • saṁ: together
    • jānate: knows
    • manasā: with the mind
    • saṁ: together
    • cikitre: creates
    • 'dhvaryavo: the priests
    • dhiṣaṇā: the divine order
    • paś: and the seers
    • ca: and
    • devīḥ: the goddesses
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the unity and collaboration of youthful energies and divine forces.
    • It emphasizes the importance of humility and reverence, as those who bow down are acknowledged.
    • The shining and auspicious qualities represent positivity and good fortune that come from this unity.
    • It highlights the role of the mind in understanding and creating harmony in the world.
    • The mention of priests and seers indicates the importance of spiritual guidance and wisdom in achieving this unity.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a team setting, when individuals come together with respect and humility, they can achieve greater success, much like the youthful energies in the mantra.
    • In community service, when people unite for a common cause, they create positive change, reflecting the auspicious qualities mentioned.
    • In personal relationships, understanding and collaboration lead to stronger bonds, akin to the divine order and guidance from the mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the power of unity, respect, and collaboration in achieving positive outcomes.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, such as teamwork, community efforts, and personal relationships.
    • By embodying the principles of humility and cooperation, individuals can create a harmonious and prosperous environment.
