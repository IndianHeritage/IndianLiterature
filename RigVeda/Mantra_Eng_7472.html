ब्रह्मा त इन्द्र गिरवणः क्रियन्ते अनातिद्भुताः |इमा जुषस्व हर्यश्व योजनेंद्र या ते अमन्महि ||
brahmā ta indra girvaṇaḥ kriyante anatidbhutā | imā juṣasva haryaśva yojanendra yā te amanmahi ||
  • Translation: The mantra can be translated as: "O Brahman, may the powerful Indra, who is the master of the world, be pleased with our offerings. May we enjoy the benefits of your grace, and may we be united with your divine energy."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • Brahmā: Refers to the ultimate reality or cosmic spirit in Hindu philosophy.
    • Indra: The king of the gods, often associated with rain, storms, and strength.
    • Girvaṇaḥ: Pertaining to the world or the universe.
    • Kriyante: To perform or to act.
    • Anatidbhutā: Not extraordinary or beyond comprehension; signifies the natural order of things.
    • Imā: These or this.
    • Juṣasva: May you enjoy or may you be pleased.
    • Haryaśva: A term that can refer to the divine energy or strength.
    • Yojanendra: The lord of the yojanas, which can mean the paths or journeys.
    • Amanmahi: We are united or we are connected.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community gathering, when people come together to celebrate a festival, they often invoke divine blessings for prosperity and unity, similar to the invocation in this mantra.
    • In a corporate setting, when teams collaborate on a project, they seek the collective strength and wisdom of all members, akin to the unity and divine energy mentioned in the mantra.
  • Significance: The mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking divine blessings and unity in our actions. It reflects the belief that by aligning ourselves with higher energies, we can achieve our goals and enhance our collective well-being.
  • Application in Real Life: This mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as:
    • During rituals or prayers, where individuals seek blessings for success and harmony.
    • In teamwork and collaboration, where the focus is on collective effort and shared goals.
    • In personal development, where one seeks to connect with their inner strength and purpose.
