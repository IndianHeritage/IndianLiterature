ये के च ज्मा महिनो अहिमायाऽ दिवो जज्निरे अपां सधस्थे |ते अस्मभ्यम् इषये विश्वम् आयुः क्षप usrā वरिवस्यन्तु देवाः ||
ye ke ca jmā mahino ahimāyā divo jajñire apāṁ sadhasthe | te asmabhyam iṣaye viśvam āyuḥ kṣapa usrā varivasyantu devāḥ ||
  • Mantra: "ye ke ca jmā mahino ahimāyā divo jajñire apāṁ sadhasthe | te asmabhyam iṣaye viśvam āyuḥ kṣapa usrā varivasyantu devāḥ ||"
  • Translation: "Those who are born from the great and powerful (divine) source, who dwell in the heavens and the waters, may all the divine beings grant us a long life, strength, and prosperity."
  • Word-by-Word Explanation:
    • ye ke ca: "those who" - refers to the divine beings or entities.
    • jmā: "born" - signifies the origin or creation of these beings.
    • mahino: "great" - indicates the magnitude and power of these divine entities.
    • ahimāyā: "powerful" - emphasizes their strength and influence.
    • divo: "of the heavens" - relates to the celestial realm.
    • jajñire: "who have come into being" - denotes the existence of these beings.
    • apāṁ: "of the waters" - connects to the life-giving properties of water.
    • sadhasthe: "who dwell" - indicates their presence in specific realms.
    • te: "may they" - a call for blessings from these beings.
    • asmabhyam: "to us" - signifies the recipients of these blessings.
    • iṣaye: "for strength" - refers to the desire for power and vitality.
    • viśvam: "all" - encompasses everything in existence.
    • āyuḥ: "life" - signifies longevity and health.
    • kṣapa: "prosperity" - relates to wealth and abundance.
    • usrā: "strength" - indicates physical and mental fortitude.
    • varivasyantu: "may they bestow" - a request for blessings.
    • devāḥ: "the divine beings" - refers to gods or celestial entities.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community gathering, people often invoke blessings for health and prosperity, similar to the essence of this mantra.
    • During festivals, prayers are offered to deities for strength and longevity, reflecting the desire for divine support in daily life.
    • In personal rituals, individuals may seek blessings for success in their endeavors, resonating with the mantra's call for divine assistance.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking blessings from divine entities for a fulfilling life.
    • It highlights the interconnectedness of life, strength, and prosperity, reminding us to acknowledge and honor the forces that support our existence.
    • In real life, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as personal prayers, community rituals, and during significant life events, reinforcing the belief in divine guidance and support.
