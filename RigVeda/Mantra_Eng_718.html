सनात् सनीळा अवनीर अवाताऽ व्रताऽ रक्षन्ते अमृताः सहोभिः |पुरू सहस्राः जनयो न पत्नीर दुवास्यन्ति स्वसारो अह्रयाणम् ||
sanāt sanīḻā avanīr avātā vratā rakṣante amṛtāḥ sahobhiḥ | purū sahasrā janayo na patnīr duvasyanti svasāro ahrayāṇam ||
  • Sanāt sanīḻā: This phrase can be interpreted as "eternal and unchanging." It signifies the timeless nature of existence and the universe.
  • Avanīr avātā: This translates to "the protectors of the earth." It refers to the forces or beings that safeguard the planet and its inhabitants.
  • Vratā rakṣante: This means "those who uphold vows protect." It emphasizes the importance of commitment and dedication in ensuring safety and well-being.
  • Amṛtāḥ sahobhiḥ: This can be understood as "immortal beings together." It suggests a collective of eternal entities that work in harmony.
  • Purū sahasrā: This translates to "many thousands." It indicates a vast number of beings or entities involved in a particular action or state.
  • Janayo na patnīr: This means "they do not have wives." It could imply a state of being beyond material attachments or relationships.
  • Duvasyanti svasāro: This translates to "they are not disturbed by their own kin." It suggests a level of detachment or independence from familial ties.
  • Ahrayāṇam: This can be interpreted as "those who are engaged in the pursuit of truth." It refers to individuals or entities dedicated to seeking deeper understanding and wisdom.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of eternal existence, protection, and the pursuit of truth. It highlights the significance of commitment and the collective effort of beings in safeguarding the earth and its inhabitants. In real life, this can be applied to various scenarios, such as environmental conservation efforts, where individuals and communities come together to protect nature. It also resonates with the idea of personal dedication to a cause, whether it be in social justice, education, or spiritual growth. By embodying the principles of this mantra, individuals can contribute to a harmonious and sustainable world.
