यं क्रन्दसी अवसात तस्तभाने अभ्य ऐक्षेताम् मनसा रेजमानै | यत्राधि सूर उदितो विभाति कसमै देवाय हविषा विद्हेम ||
yaṁ krandasī avasā tastabhāne abhy aikṣetām manasā rejamāne | yatrādhi sūra udito vibhāti kasmai devāya haviṣā vidhema ||
  • yaṁ krandasī avasā tastabhāne: This phrase can be interpreted as a call to the divine, invoking a presence that is both powerful and protective. The term "krandasī" suggests a form of strength or might, while "avasā" indicates a state of being or existence. In real life, this can be likened to seeking guidance or support from a mentor or a leader during challenging times.
  • abhy aikṣetām manasā rejamāne: Here, "abhy aikṣetām" refers to the act of looking or observing with intent, while "manasā rejamāne" implies a state of mental clarity or focus. This can be compared to a student preparing for an important exam, where they must concentrate their thoughts and energies to achieve success.
  • yatrādhi sūra udito vibhāti: This line speaks of a place where the "sūra" (hero or divine being) shines brightly. The word "udito" suggests rising or emerging, indicating a moment of enlightenment or realization. In everyday life, this can be seen in moments of inspiration, such as an artist finding their muse or a scientist making a groundbreaking discovery.
  • kasmai devāya haviṣā vidhema: This final part translates to a question of whom we should offer our devotion or offerings ("haviṣā"). It reflects the idea of seeking connection with the divine or higher powers. In practical terms, this can be related to the various ways people express their spirituality, whether through prayer, meditation, or acts of kindness towards others.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking divine guidance, clarity of mind, and the pursuit of inspiration. Its significance lies in the reminder that in times of uncertainty or challenge, one can turn to higher powers for support and clarity. In real-life scenarios, this can be applicable in various contexts, such as during personal struggles, professional challenges, or moments of creative block. By invoking this mantra, individuals can cultivate a sense of purpose and direction, ultimately leading to personal growth and fulfillment.
