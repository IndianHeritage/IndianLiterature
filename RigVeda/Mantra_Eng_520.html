आद्यां दूतं वृणीमहे वसुम अग्निम पुरुप्रियम् |धूमकेतुं भार्जीकं व्युष्टिषु यज्ञानाम् अध्वरश्रियम् ||
adyā dūtaṁ vṛṇīmahe vasum agnim purupriyam | dhūmaketum bhāṛjīkaṁ vyuṣṭiṣu yajñānām adhvaraśriyam ||
  • adyā: This word translates to "today" or "now," indicating the immediacy and relevance of the invocation.
  • dūtaṁ: Meaning "messenger," this term signifies the importance of communication and the role of intermediaries in conveying messages between the divine and human realms.
  • vṛṇīmahe: This translates to "we choose" or "we invoke," suggesting an active participation in the ritual or prayer, emphasizing the power of intention.
  • vasum: This word means "wealth" or "prosperity," representing the abundance that one seeks in life.
  • agnim: Referring to "fire," this symbolizes transformation, purification, and the divine presence in rituals.
  • purupriyam: This means "beloved of many," indicating the universal appeal and acceptance of the divine or the offerings made.
  • dhūmaketum: This translates to "smoke-colored," often associated with the offerings made to the fire, representing the connection between the material and spiritual worlds.
  • bhāṛjīkaṁ: This word means "radiant" or "brilliant," symbolizing the illumination that comes from knowledge and divine blessings.
  • vyuṣṭiṣu: This translates to "in the offerings," indicating the context of rituals and the importance of proper conduct in them.
  • yajñānām: Meaning "of the sacrifices," this term emphasizes the significance of rituals in connecting with the divine.
  • adhvaraśriyam: This translates to "the glory of the sacrifice," highlighting the honor and reverence associated with performing rituals correctly.

In conclusion, this mantra encapsulates the essence of invoking divine blessings through rituals and sacrifices. It emphasizes the importance of intention, communication, and the pursuit of prosperity and knowledge. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various contexts such as:

  • Personal growth: Actively choosing to seek knowledge and prosperity in one's life.
  • Community rituals: Engaging in communal activities that foster unity and shared purpose.
  • Environmental consciousness: Recognizing the transformative power of fire and its role in nature, leading to sustainable practices.

The significance of this mantra lies in its call to action, urging individuals to actively participate in their spiritual and material pursuits while honoring the interconnectedness of all beings through rituals and offerings.
