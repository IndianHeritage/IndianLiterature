एह गमन ऋषयः सोमशिताऽयास्योऽङ्गिरसो नवग्वाः |त एतम ऊर्वं वि भजन्त गोनाम् अथैतद् वचः पाणयो वमनित् ||
eha gamann ṛṣayaḥ somaśitā ayāsyo aṅgiraso navagvāḥ | ta etam ūrvaṁ vi bhajanta gonām athaitad vacaḥ paṇayo vamann it ||
  • eha gamann ṛṣayaḥ: This phrase can be translated as "the sages who are moving forward." Here, "eha" means "here" or "in this world," "gamann" refers to "moving" or "going," and "ṛṣayaḥ" means "sages" or "wise ones." This suggests the idea of enlightened individuals progressing in their spiritual journey.
  • somaśitā ayāsyo aṅgiraso navagvāḥ: This part refers to "the ones who consume Soma, the unwearied Angirasas, the new cows." "Soma" is a sacred drink in Vedic tradition, symbolizing immortality and divine inspiration. "Ayāsyo" means "unwearied" or "tireless," indicating the relentless pursuit of knowledge. "Aṅgiraso" refers to a group of sages known for their wisdom, and "navagvāḥ" means "new cows," which can symbolize new ideas or resources.
  • ta etam ūrvaṁ vi bhajanta: This translates to "they who partake of this wealth." "Ta" means "they," "etam" refers to "this," "ūrvaṁ" means "wealth" or "prosperity," and "vi bhajanta" means "partake" or "share." This emphasizes the importance of sharing knowledge and resources among the wise.
  • gonām athaitad vacaḥ paṇayo vamann it: This can be interpreted as "the speech of the cows, and this is the utterance of the wise." "Gonām" refers to "of the cows," which can symbolize abundance or nourishment, while "vacaḥ" means "speech" or "words." "Paṇayo" refers to "utterance" or "expression," and "vamann" means "to speak." This highlights the significance of communication and the sharing of wisdom.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the journey of sages who are in pursuit of knowledge and wisdom, symbolized by the consumption of Soma. It highlights the importance of sharing wealth, knowledge, and resources among enlightened individuals. In real life, this can be applied in various contexts such as education, mentorship, and community building, where the sharing of knowledge leads to collective growth and prosperity. The significance lies in fostering a culture of collaboration and continuous learning, which is essential for personal and societal development.
