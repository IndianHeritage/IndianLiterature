या ओहते रक्षसो देववीताव अचक्रेभिस तं मरुतो नि यात |यो वः शमीं शशमानस्य निन्दात तुच्यां कामान कराते सिष्विदानः ||
ya ohate rakṣaso devavītāv acakrebhis tam maruto ni yāta | yo vaḥ śamīṁ śaśamānasya nindāt tucchyān kāmān karate siṣvidānaḥ ||
  • Mantra: "ya ohate rakṣaso devavītāv acakrebhis tam maruto ni yāta | yo vaḥ śamīṁ śaśamānasya nindāt tucchyān kāmān karate siṣvidānaḥ ||"
  • Translation: This mantra invokes the Maruts, the storm deities, to protect against evil forces and to grant peace and fulfillment of desires.
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ya: who
    • ohate: calls upon
    • rakṣaso: evil spirits or forces
    • devavītāv: in the presence of the divine
    • acakrebhis: with their powerful voices
    • tam: that
    • maruto: O Maruts (storm deities)
    • ni yāta: may you come
    • yo: who
    • vaḥ: of you
    • śamīṁ: peace
    • śaśamānasya: of the one who is in harmony
    • nindāt: from criticism or negativity
    • tucchyān: empty or unfulfilled
    • kāmān: desires
    • karate: fulfills
    • siṣvidānaḥ: with great care
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of invoking divine protection against negative influences in our lives.
    • The Maruts, representing the forces of nature, are called upon to assist in overcoming obstacles and ensuring peace.
    • In real life, this can be likened to seeking support from friends, family, or mentors when facing challenges, much like how one might call upon a trusted ally in times of need.
    • The reference to "peace" and "fulfillment of desires" highlights the human pursuit of happiness and contentment, which can be achieved through positive actions and intentions.
    • For example, in a workplace setting, fostering a supportive environment can lead to greater productivity and satisfaction among employees, akin to the harmony sought in this mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the power of divine support and the importance of maintaining a positive mindset.
    • It encourages individuals to seek harmony in their lives and to be proactive in overcoming negativity.
    • In practical terms, it can be applied in daily life through mindfulness practices, community support, and personal development efforts.
    • Ultimately, the mantra signifies the interconnectedness of the divine, nature, and human experience, urging us to align ourselves with positive energies for a fulfilling life.
