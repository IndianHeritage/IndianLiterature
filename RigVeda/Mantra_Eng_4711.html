अनु प्र येज जन ओजो अस्य सत्रा दधिरे अनु वीर्याय |स्यूमगृह्बे दुधये 'र्वते च क्रतुं वृञ्जन्त्य अपि वृत्रहत्ये ||
anu pra yeje jana ojo asya satrā dadhire anu vīryāya | syūmagṛbhe dudhaye 'rvate ca kratuṁ vṛñjanty api vṛtrahatye ||
  • anu pra yeje jana ojo asya satrā dadhire anu vīryāya
    • anu pra yeje: This phrase can be interpreted as "to those who strive" or "to those who seek." It emphasizes the importance of effort and intention in achieving one's goals.
    • jana ojo asya: "The strength of the people" refers to the collective power and energy of a community or society. It highlights the significance of unity and collaboration.
    • satrā dadhire: This can be understood as "they hold the sacred knowledge" or "they maintain the rituals." It points to the importance of tradition and the wisdom passed down through generations.
    • anu vīryāya: "Towards strength" signifies the pursuit of power, courage, and resilience. It encourages individuals to seek inner strength and fortitude.
  • syūmagṛbhe dudhaye 'rvate ca kratuṁ vṛñjanty api vṛtrahatye
    • syūmagṛbhe: This term can be interpreted as "in the abode of the wise" or "in the presence of the enlightened." It suggests that wisdom and knowledge are essential for progress.
    • dudhaye 'rvate: "They churn the milk" symbolizes the process of extracting the essence or the best from something. It can be likened to the idea of hard work leading to fruitful results.
    • kratuṁ vṛñjanty: This phrase means "they choose the right path" or "they select the appropriate actions." It emphasizes the importance of making wise decisions.
    • api vṛtrahatye: "Even in the slaying of Vritra" refers to overcoming obstacles and challenges. Vritra is often seen as a dragon or demon representing chaos and ignorance.

Conclusion:

The mantra encapsulates the essence of striving for strength and wisdom within a community. It emphasizes the importance of collective effort, the pursuit of knowledge, and the courage to overcome challenges. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios such as:

  • In a workplace, teams can achieve greater success by collaborating and leveraging each member's strengths.
  • In personal development, individuals are encouraged to seek knowledge and wisdom to make informed decisions.
  • In community initiatives, collective action can lead to significant changes and improvements in society.

Overall, this mantra serves as a reminder of the power of unity, knowledge, and resilience in overcoming life's challenges and achieving one's goals.
