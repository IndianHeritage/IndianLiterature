तां तु ते सत्यā तुविनृम्णा विश्वा प्र दhenवः सिस्रते वृष्ण ūध्नः |अधā ह तवद वृषमणो भियानाः प्र सिंधवो जवसā चक्रमन्त ||
tā tū te satyā tuvinṛmṇa viśvā pra dhenavaḥ sisrate vṛṣṇa ūdhnaḥ | adhā ha tvad vṛṣamaṇo bhiyānāḥ pra sindhavo javasā cakramanta ||
  • tā tū te satyā: This phrase can be translated as "That truth is yours." It emphasizes the importance of truth in our lives and suggests that truth is a fundamental aspect of existence.
  • tuvinṛmṇa viśvā: This means "You are the one who sustains all." It highlights the idea that a higher power or universal truth supports and sustains everything in the universe.
  • pra dhenavaḥ sisrate vṛṣṇa ūdhnaḥ: This can be interpreted as "The forces of nature and the divine energies flow towards you." It signifies the interconnectedness of all beings and the divine energy that drives the universe.
  • adhā ha tvad vṛṣamaṇo bhiyānāḥ: This translates to "We seek your strength and protection." It reflects the human desire for guidance and support from a higher power in times of need.
  • pra sindhavo javasā cakramanta: This means "The rivers of energy flow swiftly." It symbolizes the dynamic nature of life and the constant movement of energy in the universe.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda encapsulates the essence of truth, sustenance, divine energy, and the human quest for strength and protection. In real life, it can be applied in various scenarios such as:

  • In personal development, embracing truth can lead to a more authentic life.
  • In community building, recognizing the interconnectedness of all individuals fosters unity and cooperation.
  • In times of crisis, seeking strength from a higher power can provide comfort and resilience.

Overall, this mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of truth, the support of the universe, and the need for divine guidance in our lives.
