तां नो रासन् रातिषाचो वसून्य आ रोडसी वरुणानी श्रिणोतु |वरूत्रीभिः सुशरणो नो अस्तु त्वष्टा सुदात्रो विदधातु रायः ||
tā no rāsan rātiṣāco vasūny ā rodasī varuṇānī śṛṇotu | varūtrībhiḥ suśaraṇo no astu tvaṣṭā sudatro vi dadhātu rāyaḥ ||
  • tā no rāsan: This phrase can be interpreted as a call for the divine to bestow upon us the essence of life and nourishment. In real life, this can be likened to seeking guidance and support in our daily endeavors, much like how a farmer prays for rain to nourish his crops.
  • rātiṣāco: This term refers to the divine energy or force that drives prosperity and success. In a modern context, it can be associated with the motivation and inspiration that individuals seek in their careers or personal lives, akin to an athlete striving for excellence in their sport.
  • vasūny ā rodasī: This phrase signifies the abundance of resources and wealth that the universe can provide. It reflects the idea of being open to receiving blessings, similar to how a businessperson might seek new opportunities for growth and expansion.
  • varuṇānī śṛṇotu: Here, Varuna is invoked as a deity of cosmic order and truth. This can be related to the importance of integrity and ethical behavior in our lives, much like how a leader must uphold honesty to gain the trust of their followers.
  • varūtrībhiḥ suśaraṇo: This phrase suggests the removal of obstacles and challenges. In real life, this can be compared to overcoming difficulties in personal or professional life, such as a student facing academic challenges and seeking help to succeed.
  • no astu tvaṣṭā: Tvaṣṭā is the divine craftsman, symbolizing creativity and innovation. This can be seen in the context of artists or inventors who strive to create something new and impactful, much like a tech entrepreneur developing a groundbreaking app.
  • sudatro vi dadhātu rāyaḥ: This line calls for the divine to grant us prosperity and well-being. In everyday life, this can be related to the pursuit of happiness and fulfillment, such as a family working together to create a loving and supportive home environment.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a prayer for divine guidance, prosperity, and the removal of obstacles in our lives. Its significance lies in the universal quest for support and abundance, applicable in various real-life scenarios such as personal growth, professional success, and the nurturing of relationships. By invoking these divine qualities, individuals can strive to align their actions with higher principles, fostering a life of integrity, creativity, and fulfillment.
