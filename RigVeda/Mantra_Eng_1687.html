उप प्रागात् सुमन मे 'धायि मनमा देवानाम् आशा उप वीतपृष्ठः |अन्व एनं विप्राः ऋषयो मदन्ति देवानाम् पुष्टे चक्रिमा सुभन्धुम् ||
upa prāgāt suman me 'dhāyi manma devānām āśā upa vītapṛṣṭhaḥ | anv enaṁ viprā ṛṣayo madanti devānām puṣṭe cakṛmā subandhum ||
  • upa prāgāt: This phrase can be translated as "may I go forward" or "may I advance." It signifies a desire for progress and movement towards a higher state of being.
  • suman me 'dhāyi: This means "may good thoughts be placed in my mind." It emphasizes the importance of positive thinking and mental clarity in achieving one's goals.
  • manma devānām āśā: This translates to "the hope of the gods in my mind." It suggests that one's aspirations should align with divine will or higher principles.
  • upa vītapṛṣṭhaḥ: This can be interpreted as "may I be supported by the divine." It indicates a plea for guidance and support from higher powers.
  • anv enaṁ viprā ṛṣayo: This means "the wise seers follow this path." It highlights the importance of wisdom and guidance from enlightened individuals in one's journey.
  • madanti devānām puṣṭe: This translates to "the gods rejoice in abundance." It signifies that when one is aligned with divine principles, prosperity and abundance follow.
  • cakṛmā subandhum: This means "may I create good connections." It emphasizes the significance of building positive relationships and networks in life.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a profound message about the journey of life, emphasizing the importance of positive thoughts, divine alignment, wisdom, and the creation of beneficial relationships. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts such as personal development, professional growth, and spiritual practices. For instance, when embarking on a new project or career path, one can recite this mantra to invoke clarity of thought, seek divine support, and foster connections with mentors or peers. Ultimately, it serves as a reminder that aligning one's aspirations with higher principles and nurturing positive relationships can lead to a fulfilling and prosperous life.
