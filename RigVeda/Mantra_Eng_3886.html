त्यं चिदेषां स्वधया मदन्तं मिहो नपातं सुवृद्धं तमोगाम् |वृषप्रभर्मा दानवस्य भामं वज्रेण वज्री न जग्हान शुष्णम् ||
tyaṁ cid eṣāṁ svadhayā madantam miho napātaṁ suvṛdhaṁ tamogām | vṛṣaprabharmā dānavasya bhāmaṁ vajreṇa vajrī ni jaghāna śuṣṇam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • tyaṁ: that
    • cid: indeed
    • eṣāṁ: of these
    • svadhayā: by one's own nature
    • madantam: intoxicated
    • miho: of the great
    • napātaṁ: offspring
    • suvṛdhaṁ: well-nourished
    • tamogām: of darkness
    • vṛṣaprabharmā: the one who is powerful like a bull
    • dānavasya: of the demon
    • bhāmaṁ: the glorious
    • vajreṇa: with the thunderbolt
    • vajrī: the one who wields the thunderbolt
    • ni: down
    • jaghāna: struck
    • šuṣṇam: the dry or barren
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of a powerful force that can overcome darkness and ignorance.
    • The phrase "by one's own nature" suggests that true strength comes from within.
    • The imagery of a thunderbolt symbolizes divine power and the ability to dispel negativity.
    • The reference to "the glorious" indicates that this force is not only powerful but also noble and virtuous.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In personal development, individuals often face internal struggles (darkness) that can be overcome by tapping into their inner strength (svadhayā).
    • In leadership, a strong leader (vṛṣaprabharmā) can inspire and uplift their team, driving them towards success despite challenges.
    • In social movements, activists often embody the qualities of the "vajrī," using their voice and actions to challenge oppressive systems (dānavasya) and bring about change.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of inner strength and the ability to confront and overcome darkness in various forms.
    • Its significance lies in the reminder that true power comes from within and can be harnessed to create positive change in oneself and the world.
    • In real-life scenarios, whether in personal growth, leadership, or social justice, the principles embodied in this mantra can guide individuals to act with courage and integrity.
