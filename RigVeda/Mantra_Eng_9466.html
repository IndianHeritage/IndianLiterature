ते नो अर्वन्तो हवनश्रुतो हवं विश्वे श्रिण्वन्तु वाजिनो मित्रद्रवः |सहस्रसां मेधसाताव इव त्मना महो ये धनं समिथेषु जब्रिरे ||
te no arvanto havanaśruto havaṁ viśve śṛṇvantu vājino mitadravaḥ | sahasrasā medhasātāv iva tmanā maho ye dhanaṁ samitheṣu jabhrire ||
  • te no arvanto: This phrase can be interpreted as "may those who are worthy" or "may those who are capable." It suggests a call for the attention of those who possess the qualities needed to fulfill a purpose.
  • havanaśruto: This term refers to "the one who is invoked" or "the one who is called upon." It emphasizes the importance of invoking the right energies or deities in our endeavors.
  • havaṁ viśve: This translates to "may all beings" or "may everyone." It signifies inclusivity and the desire for collective participation in the invocation.
  • śṛṇvantu vājino mitadravaḥ: This phrase means "may the swift ones hear us." Here, "vājino" refers to those who are powerful or strong, and "mitadravaḥ" implies a benevolent or kind nature. It calls for the attention of those who can act swiftly and positively.
  • sahasrasā medhasātāv iva tmanā: This can be interpreted as "with a thousandfold intellect." It suggests a desire for enhanced wisdom and understanding, akin to having the insight of many minds combined.
  • maho ye dhanaṁ samitheṣu jabhrire: This translates to "those who have gathered great wealth." It refers to individuals or entities that have accumulated resources or knowledge, emphasizing the importance of collaboration and sharing of wealth for the greater good.

  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of invoking the right energies and collective participation in our endeavors. It highlights the need for wisdom, strength, and benevolence in achieving our goals. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios such as teamwork in a corporate environment, community service projects, or even in personal relationships where collaboration and mutual support are essential. By recognizing the strengths and contributions of others, we can create a more harmonious and productive environment, ultimately leading to shared success and well-being.
