एष श्रिङ्गाणि डोधुवच छिशीते यूथ्यो वृषा |नृम्णा दधाना ओजसा ||
eṣa śṛṅgāṇi dodhuvac chiśīte yūthyo3 vṛṣā | nṛmṇā dadhāna ojasā ||
  • Word Breakdown:
    • eṣa: This means "this" or "he" in Sanskrit, indicating a specific subject or entity.
    • śṛṅgāṇi: Refers to "horns" or "antlers," symbolizing strength and power.
    • dodhuvac: This translates to "has spoken" or "has declared," suggesting communication or expression.
    • chiśīte: Means "in the midst" or "among," indicating a presence or situation.
    • yūthyo: Refers to "herds" or "groups," often used in the context of collective strength.
    • vṛṣā: This means "bull" or "strong one," symbolizing vigor and masculinity.
    • nṛmṇā: Translates to "by men" or "of humans," indicating human involvement or action.
    • dadhāna: Means "holding" or "possessing," suggesting control or ownership.
    • ojasā: Refers to "energy" or "vitality," symbolizing strength and life force.
  • Meaning:
    • This mantra speaks of a powerful entity (possibly a deity or a strong leader) that has declared its presence among the herds, symbolizing strength and unity.
    • The imagery of horns and bulls suggests a connection to nature and the importance of strength in both physical and social contexts.
    • It emphasizes the role of humans in harnessing this strength and energy, indicating that collective effort and vitality are essential for success.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, a strong leader (the "bull") can inspire and unite a team (the "herd") to achieve common goals, demonstrating the importance of leadership and collective effort.
    • In community initiatives, individuals coming together to support a cause can be seen as harnessing their collective energy ("ojasā") to create positive change.
    • In sports, a team that works together, drawing on the strengths of each member, exemplifies the idea of unity and shared strength.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of strength, unity, and the vital role of human effort in achieving success.
    • It serves as a reminder that collective action, inspired leadership, and harnessing one's energy are crucial in various aspects of life, from personal endeavors to community and organizational goals.
    • In essence, it encourages individuals to recognize their potential and the power of collaboration in overcoming challenges and achieving greatness.
