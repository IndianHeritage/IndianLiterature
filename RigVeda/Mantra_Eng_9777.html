द्वे समीची बिभृतश्चरण्तं शीरषतो जातं मनसा विमृष्टम् |स प्रत्यान् विश्वा भुवनानि तस्थाव अप्रयुच्छन् तरणिर भ्राजमानः ||
dve samīcī bibhṛtaś carantaṁ śīrṣato jātam manasā vimṛṣṭam | sa pratyaṅ viśvā bhuvanāni tasthāv aprayucchan taraṇir bhrājamānaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • dve: two
    • samīcī: well-matched or harmonious
    • bibhṛtaś: carrying or bearing
    • carantaṁ: moving or wandering
    • śīrṣato: from the head or top
    • jātam: born or created
    • manasā: by the mind
    • vimṛṣṭam: contemplated or thought upon
    • sa: he or that
    • pratyaṅ: in the direction of
    • viśvā: all or universal
    • bhuvanāni: worlds or realms
    • tasthāv: stood or remained
    • aprayucchan: not abandoning or not letting go
    • taraṇir: the one who traverses or moves
    • bhrājamānaḥ: shining or radiating
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of two harmonious entities that are moving and carrying something from the top, which has been contemplated by the mind.
    • It suggests a universal presence that stands firm in all realms, not abandoning its essence, while shining brightly as it traverses through existence.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • The concept of duality in relationships, such as partnerships or friendships, where two individuals complement each other and move forward together.
    • In nature, the balance between the sun and the moon can be seen as two entities that harmoniously coexist, each illuminating the world in their own way.
    • In a workplace, teamwork can be illustrated by two colleagues who support each other, sharing ideas and responsibilities, leading to a successful project outcome.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of harmony and collaboration in various aspects of life.
    • It signifies that through contemplation and mutual support, individuals can navigate the complexities of existence while shining in their unique ways.
    • In real-life scenarios, it encourages us to recognize the value of partnerships, whether in personal relationships, professional settings, or in the natural world.
