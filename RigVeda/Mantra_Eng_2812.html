अभि तषṭेवा दीधया मनिषाम् अत्यो न वाजी सुदुरो जिहानः |अभि प्रियाणि मर्मृषत पराणि कविम् इच्छामि संदृशे सुमेधाḥ ||
abhi taṣṭeva dīdhayā manīṣām atyo na vājī sudhuro jihānaḥ | abhi priyāṇi marmṛśat parāṇi kavīm̐r icchāmi saṁdṛśe sumedhāḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • abhi: towards, for
    • taṣṭeva: may I attain, may I achieve
    • dīdhayā: with wisdom, with intelligence
    • manīṣām: of thought, of understanding
    • atyō: beyond, surpassing
    • na: not
    • vājī: the one who is strong, powerful
    • sudhuro: difficult, hard
    • jihānaḥ: to overcome, to conquer
    • abhi: towards, for
    • priyāṇi: dear, beloved
    • marmṛśat: to touch, to feel
    • parāṇi: the higher, the supreme
    • kavīm̐r: the wise, the poets
    • icchāmi: I desire, I wish
    • saṁdṛśe: to see, to perceive
    • sumedhāḥ: with good understanding, with wisdom
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra expresses a deep yearning for wisdom and understanding.
    • The speaker seeks to achieve a state of heightened awareness and intelligence.
    • It emphasizes the importance of overcoming challenges and difficulties in the pursuit of knowledge.
    • The reference to "dear" and "beloved" suggests that wisdom and understanding are cherished goals.
    • The invocation of "kavīm̐r" highlights the role of poets and wise individuals in guiding others towards enlightenment.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, this mantra can inspire individuals to strive for excellence and overcome obstacles in their careers.
    • Students can relate to this mantra as they seek to gain knowledge and wisdom through education, facing challenges along the way.
    • In personal development, individuals may use this mantra as a reminder to pursue self-improvement and to seek guidance from mentors.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of striving for wisdom and understanding in all aspects of life.
    • It encourages individuals to face challenges with courage and to seek the guidance of those who possess knowledge.
    • In a world filled with distractions and difficulties, this mantra serves as a reminder to focus on personal growth and the pursuit of higher understanding.
