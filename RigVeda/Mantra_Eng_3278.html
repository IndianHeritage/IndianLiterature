प्राग्रुवो नभन्वो न वक्वा ध्वस्राऽ अपिन्वद युवतीः ऋतज्ञाः |धन्वान्य अज्रांऽ अपृणक तृषाणां अधोग इन्द्रः स्तर्योऽ दंषुपत्नीः ||
prāgruvo nabhanvo3 na vakvā dhvasrā apinvad yuvatīr ṛtajñāḥ | dhanvāny ajrām̐ apṛṇak tṛṣāṇām̐ adhog indraḥ staryo3 daṁsupatnīḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • prāgruvo: The one who is powerful or strong.
    • nabhanvo: Of the sky or celestial.
    • na: Not.
    • vakvā: Speaking or uttering.
    • dhvasrā: Those who are radiant or bright.
    • apinvad: They who are not to be denied.
    • yuvatīr: Young women or youthful beings.
    • ṛtajñāḥ: Those who know the truth or righteousness.
    • dhanvāny: Wealth or abundance.
    • ajrām̐: Not old or eternal.
    • apṛṇak: They who do not lack or are not deprived.
    • tṛṣāṇām: Of thirst or desire.
    • adhog: Below or downward.
    • indraḥ: Indra, the king of gods.
    • staryo: Stars or celestial bodies.
    • daṁsupatnīḥ: The one who is the master of the light or brilliance.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the powerful forces of nature and the divine, emphasizing the strength and brilliance of the celestial realm.
    • The mention of "young women" and "those who know the truth" suggests a connection between youth, knowledge, and the pursuit of righteousness.
    • Indra, as the king of gods, represents authority and the ability to fulfill desires, indicating that divine intervention can help in overcoming challenges.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a modern context, this mantra can be related to the empowerment of youth and women in society, highlighting their role in shaping the future.
    • It can also be seen in the context of environmental awareness, where the "powerful forces of nature" remind us of the importance of respecting and preserving our natural resources.
    • The pursuit of knowledge and truth can be exemplified by individuals or organizations that strive for transparency and integrity in their actions.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the interconnectedness of strength, knowledge, and the divine in our lives.
    • It encourages individuals to seek truth and righteousness while recognizing the power of youth and the importance of nature.
    • In real-life scenarios, it serves as a reminder to harness our inner strength and work towards a harmonious existence with the world around us.
