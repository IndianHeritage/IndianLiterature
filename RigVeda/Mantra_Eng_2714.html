आ नो गोत्रा दर्धृहि गोपते गाः सम अस्मभ्यं सानयो यन्तु वाजाः |दिवक्षाऽसि वृषभ सत्यशुष्मोऽस्मभ्यं सु मगवन् बोधि गोदाः ||
ā no gotrā dardṛhi gopate gāḥ sam asmabhyaṁ sanayo yantu vājāḥ | divakṣā asi vṛṣabha satyaśuṣmo 'smabhyaṁ su maghavan bodhi godāḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā no: "May we" or "Let us"
    • gotrā: "from the lineage" or "from the family"
    • dardṛhi: "become strong" or "become powerful"
    • gopate: "O Lord of the cows" or "O protector"
    • gāḥ: "the songs" or "the praises"
    • sam: "together" or "united"
    • asmabhyaṁ: "to us" or "for us"
    • sanayo: "may they come" or "may they arrive"
    • yantu: "may they go" or "may they lead"
    • vājāḥ: "the steeds" or "the powerful ones"
    • divakṣā: "O divine one" or "O heavenly being"
    • asi: "you are"
    • vṛṣabha: "the bull" or "the strong one"
    • satyaśuṣmo: "truthful" or "one who speaks the truth"
    • 'smabhyaṁ: "to us" or "for us"
    • su: "good" or "well"
    • maghavan: "O bountiful one" or "O generous one"
    • bodhi: "knowledge" or "wisdom"
    • godāḥ: "the cows" or "the wealth"
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer for strength and prosperity, invoking the divine for blessings.
    • It emphasizes unity and collective strength, suggesting that together we can achieve greatness.
    • The reference to "gopate" signifies a connection to nature and the importance of nurturing and protecting our resources.
    • The mention of "vājāḥ" symbolizes power and energy, which can be related to the vigor needed in our daily lives to overcome challenges.
    • By calling upon "maghavan," the mantra seeks abundance and generosity, which can be seen in the context of community support and sharing resources.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community setting, this mantra can be applied during gatherings where collective efforts are made to support local initiatives, such as food drives or environmental clean-ups.
    • In personal life, individuals can recite this mantra when embarking on new ventures, seeking strength and guidance to navigate challenges.
    • In corporate environments, teams can use the essence of this mantra to foster collaboration and unity, ensuring that everyone works towards a common goal.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for unity, strength, and abundance.
    • It serves as a reminder that we are stronger together and that seeking divine guidance can lead to prosperity and wisdom.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra encourages individuals and communities to work collaboratively, support one another, and strive for collective success.
