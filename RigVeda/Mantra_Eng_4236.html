प्र वो मित्राय गायत वरुणाय विपा गिरा |महिक्षत्राव ऋतम् बृहत् ||
pra vo mitrāya gāyata varuṇāya vipā girā | mahikṣatrāv ṛtam bṛhat ||
  • pra vo mitrāya: This phrase can be translated as "for you, O Mitra." Mitra is a deity associated with friendship, harmony, and the upholding of cosmic order. In real life, this can be seen in the importance of building and maintaining friendships and alliances, whether in personal relationships or in business contexts.
  • gāyata varuṇāya: This translates to "sing to Varuna." Varuna is the god of water and the celestial ocean, representing the moral and cosmic order (Rta). Singing to Varuna can symbolize the act of invoking divine support and guidance in our endeavors. In everyday life, this can be likened to seeking inspiration or guidance from nature or higher powers when faced with challenges.
  • vipā girā: This means "with the voice of the wise." It emphasizes the importance of wisdom and knowledge in our speech and actions. In practical terms, this can relate to the value of communication and the impact of wise counsel in decision-making processes, whether in personal life or in leadership roles.
  • mahikṣatrāv: This translates to "in the great expanse." It refers to the vastness of the universe and the interconnectedness of all beings. In real life, this can be understood as recognizing our place within the larger context of society and the environment, promoting a sense of responsibility towards the world around us.
  • ṛtam bṛhat: This means "the great truth." Rta represents the principle of natural order and truth in the universe. In practical terms, this can be seen in the pursuit of truth and justice in our lives, whether in personal integrity or in societal structures.

Conclusion: The mantra "pra vo mitrāya gāyata varuṇāya vipā girā | mahikṣatrāv ṛtam bṛhat ||" emphasizes the significance of friendship, wisdom, and the pursuit of truth within the vastness of existence. It encourages individuals to seek harmony in relationships, to communicate wisely, and to recognize their role in the greater cosmic order. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire us to foster meaningful connections, make informed decisions, and strive for justice and truth in our actions, ultimately contributing to a more harmonious and balanced society.
