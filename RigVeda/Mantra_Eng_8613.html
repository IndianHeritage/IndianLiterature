एष विश्ववित् पवते मनिषी सोमो विश्वस्य भुवनस्य राजा |द्रप्साम् ईरयन विदथेष्व इन्दुर व वारण अव्यं समयाति याति ||
eṣa viśvavit pavate manīṣī somo viśvasya bhuvanasya rājā | drapsām̐ īrayan vidatheṣv indur vi vāram avyaṁ samayāti yāti ||
  • eṣa viśvavit pavate manīṣī: This phrase can be translated as "This one, who knows everything, moves with wisdom." Here, "eṣa" refers to "this one," "viśvavit" means "all-knowing," and "manīṣī" signifies "wise" or "intelligent." In real life, this can be likened to a leader or a mentor who possesses deep knowledge and guides others with wisdom.
  • somo viśvasya bhuvanasya rājā: This translates to "the moon is the king of the universe." The moon, or "soma," is often associated with nourishment and tranquility. In a practical sense, this can be seen in how the moon influences tides and natural rhythms, symbolizing the importance of balance and harmony in our lives.
  • drapsām̐ īrayan vidatheṣv indur: This means "the moon, who brings forth abundance in gatherings." "Drapsām̐" refers to abundance or nourishment, and "vidatheṣv" indicates gatherings or assemblies. This can be related to community events or family gatherings where sharing and abundance are celebrated, emphasizing the importance of togetherness.
  • vi vāram avyaṁ samayāti yāti: This translates to "he who does not hold back, moves forward." "Vāram" means to hold back or restrain, while "avyam" signifies freedom or openness. This can be applied to personal growth, where individuals are encouraged to pursue their goals without fear or hesitation, embodying the spirit of perseverance.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of wisdom, leadership, abundance, and the courage to move forward without restraint. In real life, these principles can be applied in various scenarios, such as in leadership roles, community building, and personal development. By embodying these qualities, individuals can create a harmonious and prosperous environment for themselves and those around them. The mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of knowledge, community, and personal growth, encouraging us to strive for balance and wisdom in our lives.
