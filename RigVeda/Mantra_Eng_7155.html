उत त्वा मन्दन्तु स्तोमाḥ कृत्स्व राधो अद्रिवः |अव ब्रह्मद्विषो जहि ||
ut tvā mandantu stomāḥ kṛṇuṣva rādho adrivaḥ | ava brahmadviṣo jahi ||
  • ut tvā mandantu stomāḥ: This phrase can be translated as "May the hymns (stomas) approach you." Here, "ut" signifies a call or invocation, while "tvā" refers to the divine or the deity being addressed. "Mandantu" implies that the hymns are coming together or uniting in their praise.
  • kṛṇuṣva rādho adrivaḥ: This part translates to "Please grant us the wealth (rādho) that is free from obstacles (adrivaḥ)." "Kṛṇuṣva" is a request for action, asking the deity to bestow blessings or wealth. "Rādho" signifies prosperity or abundance, while "adrivaḥ" indicates the absence of hindrances or barriers.
  • ava brahmadviṣo jahi: This can be interpreted as "Remove (jahi) the enmity (brahmadviṣo) towards the divine." "Ava" is a term that conveys a sense of removal or negation, while "brahmadviṣo" refers to those who harbor animosity towards the divine or spiritual principles.

  • Significance: This mantra emphasizes the importance of unity in praise and the pursuit of prosperity that is unobstructed by negativity or enmity. It reflects a deep yearning for divine blessings and the removal of obstacles that hinder spiritual and material growth.
  • Real-life Application: In practical terms, this mantra can be applied in various aspects of life. For instance, in a business setting, individuals can recite this mantra to invoke success and prosperity while fostering a collaborative environment free from jealousy or competition. In personal life, it can serve as a reminder to seek abundance while maintaining positive relationships with others, thus promoting harmony and goodwill.
  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a holistic approach to life, where the pursuit of wealth and success is intertwined with spiritual growth and the fostering of positive relationships. By invoking divine blessings and seeking the removal of negativity, individuals can create a more prosperous and harmonious existence.
