तत् ते भद्रं यत् समिद्धः स्वे दामे सोमाहुतो जरसे मृळयत्तमः | दधासि रत्नं द्रविणं च दाशुषे 'अग्ने सख्ये मा रिषामाऽ वयं तव ||
tat te bhadraṁ yat samiddhaḥ sve dame somāhuto jarase mṛḻayattamaḥ | dadhāsi ratnaṁ draviṇaṁ ca dāśuṣe 'gne sakhye mā riṣāmā vayaṁ tava ||
  • tat te bhadraṁ: This phrase translates to "that which is auspicious for you." It signifies a wish for well-being and prosperity.
  • yat samiddhaḥ: Meaning "that which is kindled," this refers to the fire or energy that is activated or brought to life, symbolizing the importance of action and effort in achieving goals.
  • sve dame: This translates to "in your own home," indicating a focus on personal space and the importance of nurturing one's environment for growth and prosperity.
  • somāhuto: This means "invoked by the Soma," referring to the sacred drink in Vedic rituals that symbolizes immortality and divine inspiration.
  • jarase mṛḻayattamaḥ: This phrase can be interpreted as "the best among those who are aged," suggesting wisdom and experience that come with age, and the respect that should be given to elders.
  • dadhāsi ratnaṁ draviṇaṁ: This translates to "you bestow jewels and wealth," indicating the blessings of abundance and prosperity that come from divine sources.
  • dāśuṣe 'gne: Meaning "to the giver, O Fire," this phrase acknowledges the role of fire as a purifying and transformative force in rituals and life.
  • sakhye mā riṣāmā: This translates to "may we not be angry with you," expressing a desire for harmony and understanding in relationships.
  • vayaṁ tava: Meaning "we are yours," this signifies a sense of belonging and devotion to the divine or to a higher purpose.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda encapsulates themes of auspiciousness, prosperity, and the importance of nurturing one's environment and relationships. In real life, it can be applied in various scenarios such as family gatherings, community events, or personal rituals where blessings for well-being and abundance are sought. It emphasizes the significance of respect for elders, the transformative power of fire, and the importance of maintaining harmonious relationships. By invoking these principles, individuals can create a positive atmosphere in their lives, fostering growth, understanding, and prosperity.
