आ यूथेव क्षुमति पश्वो अख्यद देवानां यज जनिमान्त्य उग्र |मार्तानां चिद उर्वशीः अकृप्रण वृद्धे चिद आर्य उपरस्यायोः ||
ā yūtheva kṣumati paśvo akhyad devānāṁ yaj janimānty ugra | martānāṁ cid urvaśīr akṛpran vṛdhe cid arya uparasyāyoḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā yūtheva: Like a herd or group
    • kṣumati: To be nourished or fed
    • paśvo: Of the animals or cattle
    • akhyad: He has declared or announced
    • devānāṁ: Of the gods
    • yaj: Sacrifice or worship
    • janimānty: That which is born or created
    • ugra: Fierce or powerful
    • martānāṁ: Of the mortals or humans
    • cid: Indeed or certainly
    • urvaśīr: Abundant or rich
    • akṛpran: Not to be neglected or ignored
    • vṛdhe: In growth or increase
    • cid arya: Indeed, noble or honorable
    • uparasyāyoḥ: Of the higher realms or above
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the interconnectedness of life, emphasizing the importance of community and nourishment.
    • It suggests that just as cattle thrive in a herd, humans too flourish in a supportive environment.
    • The reference to the gods indicates that divine blessings are essential for growth and prosperity.
    • The term "ugra" highlights the power and intensity of life, suggesting that challenges can lead to strength.
    • It also points to the significance of honoring the divine and the natural order, which can lead to abundance and fulfillment.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace, teamwork and collaboration can lead to greater success, much like how animals thrive in a herd.
    • Communities that support each other, such as through mutual aid groups, demonstrate the principle of nourishment and growth.
    • In personal development, facing challenges (ugra) can lead to resilience and strength, akin to the growth mentioned in the mantra.
    • Spiritual practices, such as meditation or prayer, can connect individuals to a higher purpose, reflecting the divine aspect of the mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of community, divine connection, and the strength found in challenges.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal growth to community support, highlighting the importance of nurturing relationships and honoring the divine.
    • Ultimately, it serves as a reminder that we are all part of a larger whole, and our growth is intertwined with the support of others and the blessings of the divine.
