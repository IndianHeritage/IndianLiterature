सनेमि कृध्य अस्मद ā रक्षसं कं चिद अत्रिणम् |अपादेवं द्वयुम अंहो युयोधि नः ||
sanemi kṛdhy a1smad ā rakṣasaṁ kaṁ cid atriṇam | apādevaṁ dvayum aṁho yuyodhi naḥ ||
  • Transliteration: sanemi kṛdhy a1smad ā rakṣasaṁ kaṁ cid atriṇam | apādevaṁ dvayum aṁho yuyodhi naḥ ||
  • Meaning:
    • Sanemi: We invoke or we call upon.
    • Kṛdhy: To be pleased or to be satisfied.
    • A1smad: Our or of us.
    • Ā: To or towards.
    • Rakṣasaṁ: The demons or evil forces.
    • Kaṁ cid: Any or whoever.
    • Atriṇam: Of the one who is a protector or a guardian.
    • Apādevaṁ: Without any hindrance or obstacle.
    • Dvayum: The two or duality.
    • Aṁho: O, you or hey.
    • Yuyodhi: Fight or battle.
    • Naḥ: For us or our.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a call for divine protection against negative forces or obstacles in life.
    • It emphasizes the importance of seeking help from a higher power to overcome challenges.
    • In real life, this can be likened to seeking guidance from mentors or spiritual leaders when facing difficulties.
    • The mention of "demons" can be interpreted as personal struggles, fears, or societal issues that hinder progress.
  • Significance:
    • This mantra highlights the need for unity and collective strength in overcoming adversities.
    • It serves as a reminder that we are not alone in our struggles and that seeking support is vital.
    • In practical terms, this can be applied in community efforts, teamwork in workplaces, or family support systems.
    • By invoking protection and strength, individuals can foster resilience and courage in the face of challenges.
