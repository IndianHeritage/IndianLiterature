शुनम अष्ट्राव्य अचरत कपर्दी वरत्रायां दार्व आन्ह्यमानः | नृम्णानि कृत्वान बहवे जनाय गाः पस्पशानस् तविषीर अधत्त ||
śunam aṣṭrāvy acarat kapardī varatrāyāṁ dārv ānahyamānaḥ | nṛmṇāni kṛṇvan bahave janāya gāḥ paspaśānas taviṣīr adhatta ||
  • śunam: This word can be interpreted as "good" or "auspicious." It signifies positivity and the beneficial aspects of life.
  • aṣṭrāvy: This term refers to "weapons" or "instruments." In a broader sense, it can symbolize the tools or means we use to achieve our goals.
  • acarat: Meaning "to move" or "to act," this word emphasizes the importance of action in life.
  • kapardī: This can refer to a "matted hair" or "ascetic." It symbolizes a person who has renounced worldly pleasures for spiritual pursuits.
  • varatrāyāṁ: This term can be understood as "in the path of victory." It signifies the journey towards success and triumph.
  • dārv: This word means "to give" or "to offer." It highlights the importance of generosity and sharing.
  • ānahyamānaḥ: This can be interpreted as "being unrestrained" or "free." It signifies the liberation of the spirit and mind.
  • nṛmṇāni: This term refers to "human beings" or "people." It emphasizes the collective aspect of humanity.
  • kṛṇvan: Meaning "to create" or "to make," this word underscores the act of bringing something into existence.
  • bahave: This means "many" or "numerous." It signifies abundance and the multitude of experiences in life.
  • janāya: This term translates to "for the people" or "for humanity." It emphasizes the importance of community and social responsibility.
  • gāḥ: This word means "songs" or "hymns." It symbolizes expression and the sharing of experiences through art.
  • paspaśānas: This can be interpreted as "those who are strong" or "powerful." It signifies resilience and strength in the face of challenges.
  • taviṣīr: Meaning "to be strong" or "to be powerful," this word emphasizes the importance of inner strength.
  • adhatta: This term means "to establish" or "to set." It signifies the act of creating a foundation for future endeavors.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of action, community, and the pursuit of victory through strength and generosity. It encourages individuals to act positively, create for the benefit of humanity, and establish a strong foundation for future generations. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as community service, leadership, and personal development. By embodying the principles of this mantra, individuals can contribute to a more harmonious and prosperous society.
