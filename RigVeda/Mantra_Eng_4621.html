सुत इति त्वं निमिष्ल इन्द्र सोमे स्तोमे ब्रह्मणि शस्यमान उक्ते |यद वा युक्ताभ्यां मघवन हरिभ्यां बिभ्रद वज्रम बाह्वोर इन्द्र यासि ||
suta it tvaṁ nimiśla indra some stome brahmaṇi śasyamāna ukthe | yad vā yuktābhyām maghavan haribhyām bibhrad vajram bāhvor indra yāsi ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • suta: son or offspring
    • it: thus or so
    • tvaṁ: you
    • nimiśla: the one who is alert or watchful
    • indra: Indra, the king of gods
    • some: in the context of soma, a sacred drink
    • stome: praise or hymn
    • brahmaṇi: in the sacred or divine context
    • śasyamāna: being praised or celebrated
    • ukthe: in the utterance or speech
    • yad: that
    • vā: or
    • yuktābhyām: by the wise or learned
    • maghavan: the generous one, another name for Indra
    • haribhyām: by the swift or powerful
    • bibhrad: holding or bearing
    • vajram: thunderbolt or weapon
    • bāhvor: of the arms
    • yāsi: you go or you move
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes Indra, the king of gods, emphasizing his qualities of vigilance and strength.
    • The reference to "soma" indicates the importance of divine nourishment and the sacred drink that symbolizes immortality and enlightenment.
    • The phrase "holding the thunderbolt" signifies power and the ability to overcome obstacles, suggesting that one should embody strength and resilience in life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, a leader who embodies the qualities of Indra—alertness, strength, and wisdom—can inspire their team to overcome challenges and achieve success.
    • In personal life, individuals facing difficulties can draw inspiration from this mantra to cultivate resilience and seek divine guidance through meditation or prayer.
    • During community gatherings or rituals, invoking Indra can serve as a reminder of the collective strength and support available to overcome societal challenges.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of divine strength, wisdom, and the importance of community support.
    • It encourages individuals to remain vigilant and strong in the face of adversity, drawing upon both inner strength and divine guidance.
    • In real-life scenarios, the teachings of this mantra can be applied in leadership, personal development, and community resilience, making it a timeless source of inspiration.
