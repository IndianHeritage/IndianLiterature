ऋधक सोम स्वस्तये संजग्मानो दिवः कविः |पवस्व सूर्यो दृष्टे ||
ṛdhak soma svastaye saṁjagmāno divaḥ kaviḥ | pavasva sūryo dṛśe ||
  • ṛdhak: This word can be interpreted as "to increase" or "to enhance." It signifies growth and prosperity.
  • soma: Soma refers to a sacred drink in Vedic tradition, often associated with immortality and divine inspiration. It symbolizes the essence of life and vitality.
  • svastaye: This term translates to "for well-being" or "for auspiciousness." It emphasizes the importance of health and prosperity in life.
  • saṁjagmāno: This word means "having come together" or "united." It reflects the idea of harmony and collective effort.
  • divaḥ: This translates to "of the heavens" or "divine." It indicates a connection to the celestial or spiritual realm.
  • kaviḥ: Kavi means "poet" or "sage." It represents wisdom and the ability to perceive deeper truths.
  • pavasva: This word means "to purify" or "to cleanse." It signifies the importance of purification in both physical and spiritual contexts.
  • sūryo: Sūrya refers to the sun, symbolizing light, energy, and life. It represents the source of vitality and knowledge.
  • dṛśe: This translates to "to the sight" or "to be seen." It emphasizes the importance of perception and awareness.

The mantra can be interpreted as a prayer for the enhancement of well-being through the divine essence of life, invoking the sun's energy for purification and clarity. It calls for unity and collective effort towards achieving prosperity and wisdom.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios:

  • Personal Growth: Individuals can use this mantra as a reminder to seek personal development and well-being through positive actions and thoughts.
  • Community Harmony: It can serve as a call for communities to come together for common goals, promoting unity and collective prosperity.
  • Health and Wellness: The emphasis on purification can inspire practices such as meditation, yoga, and healthy living, contributing to overall well-being.
  • Environmental Awareness: The connection to the sun can remind us of the importance of nature and the need to protect our environment for future generations.

In conclusion, this mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking well-being, unity, and wisdom through divine inspiration. Its significance lies in its applicability to personal and communal life, encouraging individuals to strive for growth, harmony, and a deeper understanding of their existence.
