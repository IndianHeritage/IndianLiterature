उत ते शुष्मासो अस्तु रक्षो भिन्दन्तो अद्रिवः |नुदस्व याः परिस्पृधः ||
ut te śuṣmāso asthū rakṣo bhindanto adrivaḥ | nudasva yāḥ parispṛdhaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ut: "thus" or "therefore"
    • te: "your"
    • śuṣmāso: "those who are swift" or "those who are powerful"
    • asthū: "the strong" or "the mighty"
    • rakṣo: "the demons" or "the evil forces"
    • bhindanto: "breaking" or "destroying"
    • adrivaḥ: "the obstacles" or "the barriers"
    • nudasva: "drive away" or "remove"
    • yāḥ: "those who"
    • parispṛdhaḥ: "are obstructing" or "are hindering"
  • Overall Meaning:
    • This mantra calls upon powerful forces to break and remove the obstacles and evil influences that hinder progress.
    • It emphasizes the need for strength and swiftness in overcoming challenges.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, this mantra can be applied when facing challenges in a project. For instance, if a team is struggling with a tight deadline, invoking the spirit of this mantra can inspire them to work swiftly and effectively to overcome the hurdles.
    • In personal life, when dealing with negative influences or toxic relationships, one can use the essence of this mantra to seek strength and clarity to remove such obstacles from their life.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for empowerment and the removal of negativity. It serves as a reminder that with strength and determination, one can overcome any barriers in life.
    • In practical scenarios, whether in personal growth, professional challenges, or spiritual journeys, this mantra encourages individuals to harness their inner strength and act decisively against any form of obstruction.
