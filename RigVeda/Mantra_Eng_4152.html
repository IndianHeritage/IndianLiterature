हये नरो मरुतो मृळतां नस् तुवीमघासो अमृताः ऋतज्ञाः |सत्यश्रुतः कवयो युवानो बृहद्गिरयो बृहद् उक्षमानाः ||
haye naro maruto mṛḻatā nas tuvīmaghāso amṛtā ṛtajñāḥ | satyaśrutaḥ kavayo yuvāno bṛhadgirayo bṛhad ukṣamāṇāḥ ||
  • haye naro maruto mṛḻatā: This phrase can be interpreted as a call to the Maruts, the storm deities, to come forth. The term "haye" signifies a call or invocation, while "naro" refers to men or humans. "Maruto" indicates the Maruts, and "mṛḻatā" suggests the act of moving or coming forth. In real life, this can be seen as a metaphor for invoking strength and energy in challenging situations.
  • nas tuvīmaghāso amṛtā ṛtajñāḥ: Here, "nas" means 'to us', "tuvīmaghāso" can be interpreted as 'those who are powerful', and "amṛtā ṛtajñāḥ" refers to 'immortal beings who know the truth'. This part of the mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking guidance and support from those who possess wisdom and strength, akin to seeking mentorship or guidance from experienced individuals in our lives.
  • satyaśrutaḥ kavayo yuvāno: "Satyaśrutaḥ" means 'those who have heard the truth', "kavayo" refers to 'poets or seers', and "yuvāno" means 'young or youthful'. This suggests that wisdom and truth are often found in the insights of the young and the creative. In a real-life context, this can be related to valuing the perspectives of younger generations and their innovative ideas.
  • bṛhadgirayo bṛhad ukṣamāṇāḥ: "Bṛhadgirayo" translates to 'those who sing great songs', and "bṛhad ukṣamāṇāḥ" means 'those who are great and powerful'. This part of the mantra highlights the significance of creativity and expression in achieving greatness. In everyday life, this can be seen in how art, music, and literature can inspire and elevate society.

  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of invoking strength, seeking wisdom from powerful and knowledgeable beings, valuing youthful perspectives, and recognizing the role of creativity in achieving greatness. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios such as leadership, mentorship, and community building, where collaboration, guidance, and creative expression are essential for progress and success.
