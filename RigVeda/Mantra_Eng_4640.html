सचस्व नायम अवसे अभीक इतो वा तम इन्द्र पाहि ऋषः |अमा चैनम अरण्ये पाहि ऋषो मदेम शतहिमाः सुवीराः ||
sacasva nāyam avase abhīka ito vā tam indra pāhi riṣaḥ | amā cainam araṇye pāhi riṣo madema śatahimāḥ suvīrāḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sacasva: Be strong or powerful
    • nāyam: This path or way
    • avase: Protect or guard
    • abhīka: From fear or danger
    • ito vā: From here or from this place
    • tam: That
    • indra: O Indra (the king of gods)
    • pāhi: Protect or save
    • riṣaḥ: The seers or sages
    • amā: We are
    • cainam: This
    • araṇye: In the wilderness or forest
    • pāhi: Protect or save
    • riṣo: The seers or sages
    • madema: May we be strong or powerful
    • śatahimāḥ: With a hundred powers or strengths
    • suvīrāḥ: With great heroes or warriors
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer for strength and protection. It calls upon Indra, the king of gods, to safeguard the seers and sages from fear and danger.
    • The phrase "sacasva nāyam avase" emphasizes the need for strength and guidance in our lives, suggesting that we should seek a powerful path that leads to safety and wisdom.
    • In real life, this can be related to seeking guidance from mentors or leaders who can help us navigate challenges and protect us from harm.
    • The mention of "araṇye" (in the wilderness) symbolizes the uncertainties and challenges we face in life, akin to navigating through a dense forest where dangers may lurk.
    • The call for "madema śatahimāḥ suvīrāḥ" reflects the desire for collective strength and courage, highlighting the importance of community and teamwork in overcoming obstacles.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of seeking divine protection and strength in our endeavors. It teaches us to rely on higher powers and the wisdom of those who have walked the path before us.
    • In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various contexts such as personal challenges, professional endeavors, or community efforts where collective strength and guidance are essential for success.
    • Ultimately, it reminds us that we are not alone in our struggles and that with the right support and strength, we can overcome any adversity.
