मां न इन्द्र परा वृणग भव नः सदहमाद्यः |त्वं न ऊती त्वम इन न आप्यम मां न इन्द्र परा वृणक ||
mā na indra parā vṛṇag bhavā naḥ sadhamādyaḥ | tvaṁ na ūtī tvam in na āpyam mā na indra parā vṛṇak ||
  • mā na indra parā vṛṇag: This phrase can be translated as "May we not be deprived of the blessings of Indra." Indra is often regarded as the king of the gods in Vedic literature, representing strength, power, and the forces of nature. In real life, this can be understood as a plea for divine support and protection in our endeavors.
  • bhavā naḥ sadhamādyaḥ: This translates to "May you, Indra, grant us prosperity and well-being." Here, the focus is on seeking abundance and a harmonious life. In practical terms, this can relate to the pursuit of success in various aspects of life, such as career, relationships, and health.
  • tvaṁ na ūtī: This means "You are the source of our strength." It emphasizes the belief that divine energy and support are essential for overcoming challenges. In everyday life, this can be seen in how people draw strength from their faith, community, or personal beliefs during difficult times.
  • tvam in na āpyam: This can be interpreted as "You are the one who provides us with sustenance." It highlights the importance of divine grace in providing for our needs. In a real-world context, this can relate to the gratitude we express for the resources and opportunities that come our way, whether through nature, relationships, or personal efforts.
  • mā na indra parā vṛṇak: This reiterates the initial plea, emphasizing the desire to remain connected to Indra's blessings. It serves as a reminder of the importance of maintaining a relationship with the divine or higher powers in our lives.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a deep yearning for divine support, prosperity, and strength. Its significance lies in the recognition of the interconnectedness between human efforts and divine grace. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applicable in various situations, such as during personal challenges, communal gatherings, or rituals where individuals seek blessings for success and well-being. It serves as a reminder to remain humble and grateful for the forces that aid us in our journey through life.
