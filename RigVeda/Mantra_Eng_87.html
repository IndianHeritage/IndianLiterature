सं गोमद इन्द्र वाजवद अस्मे पृथु श्रवो बृहत् |विश्वायुर धेहि अक्षितम् ||
saṁ gomad indra vājavad asme pṛthu śravo bṛhat | viśvāyur dhehy akṣitam ||
  • saṁ gomad: This phrase can be translated as "together with the wealth of cows." In Vedic culture, cows were considered a symbol of prosperity and abundance. The mention of cows signifies the importance of wealth and sustenance in life.
  • indra: Indra is the king of the gods in Vedic mythology, often associated with rain, storms, and warfare. He represents strength, power, and the ability to overcome obstacles.
  • vājavad: This term means "like a chariot" or "swift." It implies speed, efficiency, and the ability to move forward in life with purpose and determination.
  • asme: This word translates to "to us" or "for us," indicating a personal appeal or invocation for blessings and support.
  • pṛthu: Meaning "broad" or "vast," this term signifies expansiveness and the ability to encompass a wide range of experiences and opportunities.
  • śravo: This word means "fame" or "glory." It emphasizes the importance of reputation and the desire to achieve recognition in society.
  • bṛhat: Translated as "great" or "large," this term signifies the vastness of existence and the importance of aiming for greatness in one's endeavors.
  • viśvāyur: This term means "universal life" or "all-encompassing vitality." It reflects the interconnectedness of all living beings and the importance of harmony in life.
  • dhehy: This word translates to "may we be endowed" or "may we receive." It is a plea for blessings and support from the divine.
  • akṣitam: Meaning "unfailing" or "imperishable," this term signifies the eternal nature of certain values and blessings that one seeks in life.

Conclusion: The mantra "saṁ gomad indra vājavad asme pṛthu śravo bṛhat | viśvāyur dhehy akṣitam ||" encapsulates a deep yearning for prosperity, strength, and recognition in life. It emphasizes the importance of seeking divine blessings for abundance and success. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as during rituals for prosperity, in personal affirmations for success, or in moments of seeking strength to overcome challenges. By invoking these qualities, individuals can strive for a life filled with abundance, recognition, and a sense of universal connection, ultimately leading to a fulfilling existence.
