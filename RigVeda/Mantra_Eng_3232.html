तं इद व इन्द्रं सुहवं हुवेम यस् ता चकार नर्याः पुरूणि |यो मावत जरीत्रे गध्यं चिन् मक्षू वाजं भरति स्पार्हराधाः ||
tam id va indraṁ suhavaṁ huvema yas tā cakāra naryā purūṇi | yo māvate jaritre gadhyaṁ cin makṣū vājam bharati spārharādhāḥ ||
  • tam id va indraṁ suhavaṁ huvema: This phrase can be translated to "We invoke that Indra, the auspicious one." Here, Indra is recognized as a powerful deity associated with rain, thunder, and strength. In real life, invoking Indra can symbolize seeking strength and support in challenging situations, much like how people pray for rain during droughts or for strength during difficult times.
  • yas tā cakāra naryā purūṇi: This translates to "Who has created the many forms of life." This line emphasizes the diversity of life and the creative power of the divine. In our daily lives, we can see this in the variety of cultures, languages, and ecosystems that exist around us, reminding us of the beauty of diversity and the importance of respecting all forms of life.
  • yo māvate jaritre gadhyaṁ: This means "Who brings forth the strength in the battle." This can be interpreted as a call for courage and resilience in the face of adversity. In real life, this can relate to personal struggles, such as overcoming illness or facing challenges at work, where one must summon inner strength to persevere.
  • cin makṣū vājam bharati spārharādhāḥ: This translates to "Who bestows the power of victory." This line signifies the importance of divine support in achieving success. In practical terms, this can be seen in competitive scenarios, such as sports or business, where individuals or teams seek blessings for success and victory, often through rituals or prayers.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking divine support and strength in various aspects of life. It highlights the importance of resilience, the beauty of diversity, and the pursuit of victory. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applicable in moments of challenge, whether personal or communal, reminding individuals to seek strength from a higher power and to appreciate the diversity of life around them. It serves as a spiritual guide, encouraging people to invoke strength and support in their endeavors, fostering a sense of unity and purpose in the face of life's battles.
