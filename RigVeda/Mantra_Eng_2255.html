मन्त्र: ब्रह्मणस पते सुघमस्य विश्वहा रायः स्याम रथ्योऽ वयस्वतः |वीरेषु वीराम् उप पृङ्धि नस् त्वं यद ईशानो ब्रह्मणा वेषि मे हवम् ||
brahmaṇas pate suyamasya viśvahā rāyaḥ syāma rathyo3 vayasvataḥ | vīreṣu vīrām̐ upa pṛṅdhi nas tvaṁ yad īśāno brahmaṇā veṣi me havam ||
  • Mantra: "brahmaṇas pate suyamasya viśvahā rāyaḥ syāma rathyo3 vayasvataḥ | vīreṣu vīrām̐ upa pṛṅdhi nas tvaṁ yad īśāno brahmaṇā veṣi me havam ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • brahmaṇas: of the Brahman, the ultimate reality or cosmic spirit
    • pate: O Lord or Master
    • suyamasya: of the well-controlled or well-governed
    • viśvahā: who carries all, universal
    • rāyaḥ: wealth or prosperity
    • syāma: may we be
    • rathyoḥ: of the chariots or vehicles
    • vayasvataḥ: of the strong or powerful
    • vīreṣu: among the heroes
    • vīrām: the brave or valiant
    • upa: near or close
    • pṛṅdhi: may you inspire or empower
    • nas: us
    • tvaṁ: you
    • yad: that
    • īśāno: O Lord or Master
    • brahmaṇā: by the Brahman
    • veṣi: may you protect or guard
    • me: my
    • havam: offering or invocation
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to the divine, seeking guidance and support from the ultimate reality (Brahman).
    • It emphasizes the importance of being well-governed and controlled in one's actions, which can be related to personal discipline in real life.
    • The invocation of prosperity and strength reflects the human desire for success and resilience in the face of challenges.
    • For example, in a competitive work environment, individuals may seek to embody the qualities of strength and bravery to overcome obstacles and achieve their goals.
    • The mantra also highlights the significance of community and support, as it calls upon the divine to empower not just the individual but also those around them.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of individuals and the divine, encouraging us to seek strength and guidance in our endeavors.
    • Its significance lies in the universal quest for prosperity, strength, and support, applicable in various aspects of life, including personal growth, professional challenges, and community building.
    • By invoking these qualities, individuals can strive to create a more harmonious and prosperous existence, both for themselves and for those around them.
