ये मे पञ्चाशतं ददुर अश्वानां सधस्तुति |द्यमद अग्नि महि श्रवो बृहत् कृधि मघोनां नृवद अमृत नृणाम् ||
ye me pañcāśataṁ dadur aśvānāṁ sadhastuti | dyumad agne mahi śravo bṛhat kṛdhi maghonāṁ nṛvad amṛta nṛṇām ||
  • Translation: "Those who have given me fifty steeds, O Agni, with praise, grant me great glory and wealth, and make me immortal among men."

  • Word Breakdown:
    • ye: Those who
    • me: to me
    • pañcāśataṁ: fifty
    • dadur: have given
    • aśvānāṁ: of steeds
    • sadhastuti: with praise
    • dyumad: grant me glory
    • agne: O Agni (the fire god)
    • mahi: great
    • śravo: fame or wealth
    • bṛhat: vast or immense
    • kṛdhi: make
    • maghonāṁ: of wealth
    • nṛvad: among men
    • amṛta: immortal
    • nṛṇām: of men

  • Contextual Explanation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Agni, the fire god, who is often seen as a mediator between humans and the divine.
    • The mention of "fifty steeds" symbolizes abundance and prosperity. In ancient times, horses were a sign of wealth and power.
    • The phrase "with praise" indicates the importance of gratitude and recognition of divine blessings in achieving success.
    • The request for "great glory and wealth" reflects a desire for recognition and material success, which are often pursued in various aspects of life, such as career and personal achievements.
    • The aspiration for immortality among men signifies a longing for a lasting legacy, which can be achieved through impactful contributions to society.

  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern business, leaders often seek to create a legacy through their companies, aiming for both financial success and a positive impact on society.
    • In personal life, individuals may strive for recognition in their fields, whether through awards, community service, or innovative contributions.
    • The importance of gratitude can be seen in practices like giving thanks in personal relationships or acknowledging mentors in professional settings.

  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of gratitude, the pursuit of wealth and glory, and the desire for a lasting impact.
    • It serves as a reminder that success is not just about material gain but also about the legacy one leaves behind.
    • In real life, applying the principles of this mantra can lead to a more fulfilling and impactful existence, encouraging individuals to recognize their blessings and strive for greatness.
