मां वो दातृान मरुतो निर अराम मां पश्चाद दघ्म रथ्यो विभागे |आ नः स्पार्हे भजतनाऽ वसव्ये यद ईं सुजातं वृषणो वो अस्ति ||
mā vo dātrān maruto nir arāma mā paścād daghma rathyo vibhāge | ā naḥ spārhe bhajatanā vasavye3 yad īṁ sujātaṁ vṛṣaṇo vo asti ||
  • mā vo dātrān maruto nir arāma: This phrase can be interpreted as a plea for protection and support from the Maruts, the storm deities associated with strength and vitality. The term "mā" signifies a request or a negation, suggesting that the speaker does not wish to be abandoned or left without assistance.
  • mā paścād daghma rathyo vibhāge: Here, the speaker asks not to be harmed or obstructed in their endeavors. "Dagham" refers to destruction or harm, while "rathyo vibhāge" can be understood as the division of paths or journeys. This line emphasizes the importance of safe passage and the avoidance of obstacles in life.
  • ā naḥ spārhe bhajatanā vasavye: This line invokes the Vasus, another group of deities associated with abundance and prosperity. "Spārhe" suggests a desire for connection or favor, indicating that the speaker seeks the blessings of these deities for a fruitful life.
  • yad īṁ sujātaṁ vṛṣaṇo vo asti: The final part of the mantra speaks of a "su-jātaṁ," or well-born, which can refer to auspiciousness or good fortune. "Vṛṣaṇo" implies strength or power, suggesting that the speaker acknowledges the presence of divine strength in their life.

  • Significance: This mantra encapsulates a deep yearning for divine support and protection in the face of life's challenges. It reflects the human condition of seeking guidance and strength from higher powers.
  • Real-life Application: In contemporary life, this mantra can be recited during times of uncertainty or when embarking on new ventures, such as starting a new job, moving to a new place, or facing personal challenges. It serves as a reminder to seek support from both the divine and the community around us.
  • Conclusion: The essence of this mantra lies in its call for divine assistance and the importance of community support. It teaches us that while we may strive for success and strength, it is equally vital to acknowledge and seek help from the forces greater than ourselves, whether they be spiritual or social.
