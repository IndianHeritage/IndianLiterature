rathānāṁ na ye1 'rāḥ sanābhayo jigīvāṁso na śūrā abhidyavaḥ | vareyavo na maryā ghṛtapruṣo 'bhisvartāro arkaṁ na suṣṭubhaḥ ||
  • Mantra: "rathānāṁ na ye1 'rāḥ sanābhayo jigīvāṁso na śūrā abhidyavaḥ | vareyavo na maryā ghṛtapruṣo 'bhisvartāro arkaṁ na suṣṭubhaḥ ||"
  • Translation: This mantra speaks of the qualities of those who are worthy of praise and honor. It emphasizes the importance of strength, valor, and the ability to overcome challenges.
  • Word Breakdown:
    • rathānāṁ: Of the chariots, symbolizing strength and mobility.
    • na ye 'rāḥ: Not those who are weak or cowardly.
    • sanābhayo: Those who are united and strong.
    • jigīvāṁso: Those who strive for life and success.
    • na śūrā: Not the brave ones who do not act.
    • abhidyavaḥ: Those who are aware and knowledgeable.
    • vareyavo: The worthy ones, deserving of respect.
    • na maryā: Not those who are limited or constrained.
    • ghṛtapruṣo: Those who are rich in resources and wisdom.
    • abhisvartāro: Those who are proactive and take initiative.
    • arkaṁ: The sun, representing enlightenment and energy.
    • na suṣṭubhaḥ: Not those who are complacent or stagnant.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, a team that works together harmoniously and strives for success embodies the qualities described in this mantra. They are proactive, knowledgeable, and united in their goals.
    • In sports, a team that shows strength, courage, and the ability to overcome challenges exemplifies the essence of this mantra. They do not shy away from competition but rather embrace it with determination.
    • In personal life, individuals who seek knowledge, act with purpose, and support one another reflect the values of this mantra. They are not limited by fear or complacency but strive for growth and enlightenment.
  • Conclusion: This mantra serves as a reminder of the qualities that lead to success and honor in life. It encourages individuals and groups to embody strength, unity, and proactivity. In real-life scenarios, whether in personal endeavors, professional settings, or community efforts, the principles outlined in this mantra can guide actions and decisions, fostering an environment of growth, respect, and achievement.
