इदा हि त उषो अद्रिसानो गोत्रा गवाम् अङ्गिरसो गृणन्ति |व्य आ1र्केण बिभिदुर ब्रह्मणा च सत्याऽ नृणाम् अभवद् देवहूतिḥ ||
idā hi ta uṣo adrisāno gotrā gavām aṅgiraso gṛṇanti | vy a1rkeṇa bibhidur brahmaṇā ca satyā nṛṇām abhavad devahūtiḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • idā: Here, in this context
    • hi: Indeed, certainly
    • ta: Those
    • uṣo: Dawn, light
    • adrisāno: Of the mountains, hills
    • gotrā: Of the herds, cattle
    • gavām: Of the cows
    • aṅgiraso: Of the Angiras, a group of sages
    • gṛṇanti: Sing, chant
    • vy: With, by
    • arḳeṇa: With the light, radiance
    • bibhidur: They are filled, they possess
    • brahmaṇā: By the divine, the ultimate reality
    • ca: And
    • satyā: Truth, reality
    • nṛṇām: Of men, of humans
    • abhavad: Has become, has arisen
    • devahūtiḥ: Divine inspiration, divine essence
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the dawn and its significance in the natural and spiritual realms.
    • The phrase "idā hi ta uṣo" emphasizes the importance of light and enlightenment that dawn brings.
    • The reference to "adrisāno gotrā" suggests the connection between nature (mountains) and the sustenance provided by cattle, symbolizing abundance and nourishment.
    • The "aṅgiraso gṛṇanti" indicates the sages or wise individuals who recognize and celebrate this divine light.
    • The mantra concludes with the idea that through this light, truth and divine essence manifest in human life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, the dawn symbolizes new beginnings. For instance, starting a new project or a fresh chapter in life can be likened to the arrival of dawn.
    • The importance of cattle in agriculture reflects the reliance on nature for sustenance, similar to how modern society depends on sustainable practices for food production.
    • Wise individuals in society, like teachers and mentors, often guide others towards enlightenment and truth, akin to the sages mentioned in the mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of light, truth, and divine inspiration in our lives.
    • It serves as a reminder to seek enlightenment and to appreciate the natural world around us.
    • In real-life scenarios, it encourages individuals to embrace new beginnings, recognize the importance of wisdom, and strive for truth in their actions.
