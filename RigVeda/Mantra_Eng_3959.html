अभि वो अर्चे पोष्यावतो नृन् वास्तोष पतिं तवष्टारं रराणः |धन्या सजोषा धिषणा नमोभिर वनस्पतीं ओषधी राया एषे ||
abhi vo arce poṣyāvato nṝn vāstoṣ patiṁ tvaṣṭāraṁ rarāṇaḥ | dhanyā sajoṣā dhiṣaṇā namobhir vanaspatīm̐r oṣadhī rāya eṣe ||
  • Mantra: "abhi vo arce poṣyāvato nṝn vāstoṣ patiṁ tvaṣṭāraṁ rarāṇaḥ | dhanyā sajoṣā dhiṣaṇā namobhir vanaspatīm̐r oṣadhī rāya eṣe ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • abhi: towards, for
    • vo: you (plural)
    • arce: in the worship, in the praise
    • poṣyāvato: of the nourisher, the one who provides sustenance
    • nṝn: men, people
    • vāstoṣ: of the dwelling, the home
    • patiṁ: lord, master
    • tvaṣṭāraṁ: the creator, the one who shapes
    • rarāṇaḥ: who is to be praised, who is worthy of worship
    • dhanyā: blessed, fortunate
    • sajoṣā: with joy, with happiness
    • dhiṣaṇā: with wisdom, with understanding
    • namobhir: with salutations, with reverence
    • vanaspatīm̐r: the trees, the plants
    • oṣadhī: the herbs, the medicinal plants
    • rāya: wealth, prosperity
    • eṣe: this, here
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra is a prayer that acknowledges the divine forces that provide sustenance and nourishment to humanity.
    • It emphasizes the importance of nature, particularly plants and herbs, in sustaining life and promoting well-being.
    • The invocation of "tvaṣṭāraṁ," the creator, signifies the recognition of a higher power that shapes and nurtures the world.
    • In real life, this can be related to the way we depend on agriculture and natural resources for our survival.
    • For example, the food we eat, the medicines we use, and the environment we live in are all products of nature's bounty.
    • The mantra also highlights the importance of gratitude and reverence towards these natural elements, encouraging a harmonious relationship with the environment.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for respect and gratitude towards nature and its resources.
    • It serves as a reminder of our interconnectedness with the environment and the importance of sustainable living.
    • In modern times, this mantra can inspire practices such as organic farming, conservation efforts, and holistic health approaches that honor the gifts of nature.
    • By acknowledging the divine in nature, we cultivate a sense of responsibility towards preserving our planet for future generations.
