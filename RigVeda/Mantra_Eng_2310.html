अस्माकेबहि: सत्वभि: शूर शूरैर वीर्याः कृत्य यानि ते कर्त्वानि |ज्योग अभूवन् अनुधूपितासो हत्वी तेषाम आ भरā नो वसूनि ||
asmākebhiḥ satvabhiḥ śūra śūrair vīryā kṛdhi yāni te kartvāni | jyog abhūvann anudhūpitāso hatvī teṣām ā bharā no vasūni ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • asmākebhiḥ: By us
    • satvabhiḥ: With strength
    • śūra: Brave
    • śūrair: By the brave
    • vīryā: Valor
    • kṛdhi: Create or perform
    • yāni: Whatever
    • te: Those
    • kartvāni: Actions
    • jyog: May be united
    • abhūvann: May become
    • anudhūpitāso: With great effort
    • hatvī: Having conquered
    • teṣām: Of them
    • ā: To
    • bharā: Bring forth
    • no: Our
    • vasūni: Wealth or riches
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the collective strength and bravery of a group.
    • It calls for the creation of valorous actions that lead to success.
    • The phrase "by us with strength" signifies unity and collaboration in achieving goals.
    • It suggests that through effort and bravery, one can conquer challenges and attain wealth or prosperity.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, a team working together with courage and determination can overcome obstacles and achieve business goals.
    • In sports, a team that collaborates and supports each other can win championships, demonstrating the power of unity and bravery.
    • In community service, individuals coming together to address social issues can create significant positive change, showcasing collective strength.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the importance of teamwork, bravery, and effort in achieving success.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, including workplaces, sports, and community initiatives.
    • By embodying the principles of unity and valor, individuals and groups can conquer challenges and attain prosperity.
