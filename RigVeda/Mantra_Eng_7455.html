आ नूनं यातम अश्विनाश्वेभिः पृथितप्सुभिः |दस्रां हिरण्यवर्तनी शुभस पती पातं सोमं ऋतावृद्धा ||
ā nūnaṁ yātam aśvināśvebhiḥ pruṣitapsubhiḥ | dasrā hiraṇyavartanī śubhas patī pātaṁ somam ṛtāvṛdhā ||
  • Mantra: "ā nūnaṁ yātam aśvināśvebhiḥ pruṣitapsubhiḥ | dasrā hiraṇyavartanī śubhas patī pātaṁ somam ṛtāvṛdhā ||"
  • Translation: This mantra invokes the Aśvins, the divine twin horsemen, asking them to come with their beneficial powers and bring forth the golden, auspicious drink (soma) that enhances our well-being and prosperity.
  • Word Breakdown:
    • ā nūnaṁ: "Indeed, surely" - an expression of certainty and invocation.
    • yātam: "come" - a call for the divine presence.
    • aśvināśvebhiḥ: "with the Aśvins" - referring to the twin deities associated with health and medicine.
    • pruṣitapsubhiḥ: "with beneficial powers" - highlighting the positive attributes of the Aśvins.
    • dasrā: "golden" - symbolizing wealth and prosperity.
    • hiraṇyavartanī: "that which brings forth gold" - a metaphor for abundance.
    • śubhas: "auspicious" - indicating good fortune and positivity.
    • patī: "lord" - a term of reverence for the divine.
    • pātaṁ: "bring" - a request for the divine to bestow blessings.
    • somam: "soma" - a sacred drink believed to confer immortality and divine insight.
    • ṛtāvṛdhā: "that enhances our well-being" - a call for health and prosperity.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern times, people often seek blessings for health and prosperity through rituals and prayers, similar to how this mantra invokes the Aśvins for well-being.
    • In the context of health, individuals may turn to holistic practices or seek medical assistance, paralleling the Aśvins' role as divine healers.
    • During festivals, offerings of soma-like drinks (like herbal teas) are made to symbolize abundance and health, reflecting the essence of this mantra.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its invocation of divine forces for health, prosperity, and well-being. It emphasizes the importance of seeking higher powers for guidance and blessings in our lives. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire individuals to pursue holistic health, engage in community rituals for prosperity, and foster a mindset of abundance and positivity. By recognizing the divine in our daily lives, we can cultivate a sense of gratitude and connection to the greater universe.
