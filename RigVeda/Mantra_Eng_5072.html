मन्त्र: श्रुष्टि वां यज्ञ उद्यतः सजोषा मनुष्वद वृक्तबरिषो यजध्यै |आ य इन्द्रावरुणाव इषे अद्य महे सुम्नाय मह आववर्तत ||
śruṣṭī vāṁ yajña udyataḥ sajoṣā manuṣvad vṛktabarhiṣo yajadhyai | ā ya indrāvaruṇāv iṣe adya mahe sumnāya maha āvavartat ||
  • śruṣṭī vāṁ yajña udyataḥ: This phrase can be translated as "Creation, indeed, is the result of the sacrificial act." It emphasizes the idea that the universe and all its elements are born out of a cosmic sacrifice. In real life, this can be likened to the efforts and sacrifices individuals make for the greater good, such as parents working hard to provide for their children or communities coming together to support a cause.
  • sajoṣā manuṣvad vṛktabarhiṣo yajadhyai: This part means "With the support of the divine, we perform our sacrifices." It highlights the importance of collaboration and divine assistance in our endeavors. For example, in a workplace, teamwork and collaboration often lead to successful projects, much like how divine forces are believed to aid human efforts in achieving their goals.
  • ā ya indrāvaruṇāv iṣe: This translates to "May Indra and Varuna, the deities of rain and water, be invoked." Indra and Varuna represent natural forces that are essential for life. In a practical sense, this can be related to the importance of respecting and nurturing our environment, as water and rain are vital for agriculture and sustaining life on Earth.
  • adya mahe sumnāya maha āvavartat: This phrase means "Today, may great blessings come forth." It signifies the hope for prosperity and well-being. In everyday life, this can be seen in rituals or prayers for success in new ventures, such as starting a business or embarking on a new journey, where individuals seek blessings for favorable outcomes.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of creation through sacrifice, the importance of divine support, and the invocation of natural forces for prosperity. Its significance lies in the understanding that our actions, when aligned with a higher purpose and supported by collaboration and respect for nature, can lead to fruitful outcomes. In real life, this mantra can inspire individuals to engage in selfless acts, work together towards common goals, and appreciate the natural world, ultimately fostering a sense of community and responsibility towards the environment.
