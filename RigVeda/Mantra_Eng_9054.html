दर्शन nv अत्र श्रितपाम् अनिन्द्रान बाहुक्षदः शरवे पत्यमानान् |घृषुं वा ये निनिदुः सखायम् अध्य ū nv एषु पवयो ववृत्युः ||
darśan nv atra śṛtapām̐ anindrān bāhukṣadaḥ śarave patyamānān | ghṛṣuṁ vā ye niniduḥ sakhāyam adhy ū nv eṣu pavayo vavṛtyuḥ ||
  • darśan nv atra śṛtapām̐: This phrase can be interpreted as "the vision that is here, which has been heard." It emphasizes the importance of perception and understanding in our lives. For example, in a real-life scenario, when we witness an event, our interpretation of it shapes our understanding of reality.
  • anindrān bāhukṣadaḥ: This translates to "the ones who are not obstructed by the powerful." It suggests that there are forces or individuals in life that may try to hinder our progress. For instance, in a workplace, a person may face challenges from colleagues but can overcome them through perseverance and skill.
  • śarave patyamānān: This means "those who are moving swiftly." It signifies the importance of being proactive and agile in our actions. In everyday life, this can relate to how quickly we adapt to changes, such as learning new technologies or adjusting to new environments.
  • ghṛṣuṁ vā ye niniduḥ: This can be interpreted as "those who have gathered together." It highlights the significance of community and collaboration. For example, in a community project, individuals come together to achieve a common goal, showcasing the power of teamwork.
  • sakhāyam adhy ū nv eṣu pavayo vavṛtyuḥ: This phrase translates to "friends who are present here, may they flourish." It emphasizes the value of friendship and support in our lives. In real life, having a strong support system can lead to personal and professional growth, as friends encourage and uplift each other.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of perception, resilience, agility, community, and friendship. Its significance lies in the reminder that our understanding of the world is shaped by our experiences and interactions. In real-life scenarios, applying these principles can lead to personal growth, successful collaborations, and a supportive network that fosters well-being and achievement. By embracing these values, individuals can navigate challenges more effectively and contribute positively to their communities.
