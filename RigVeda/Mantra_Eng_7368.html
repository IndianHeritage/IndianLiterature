तेन स्तोत्रिभ्य आ भर नृभ्यो नारिभ्यो अत्तवे |सद्योजाता ऋभुष्ठिर ||
tena stotṛbhya ā bhara nṛbhyo nāribhyo attave | sadyo jāta ṛbhuṣṭhira ||
  • Mantra: "tena stotṛbhya ā bhara nṛbhyo nāribhyo atende | sadyo jāta ṛbhuṣṭhira ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • tena: by that
    • stotṛbhya: to the worshippers
    • ā bhara: may you bring forth
    • nṛbhyo: to men
    • nāribhyo: to women
    • attave: may you grant
    • sadyo: immediately
    • jāta: born
    • ṛbhuṣṭhira: of the seers or sages
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a call for blessings and abundance from the divine.
    • It emphasizes the importance of both men and women in the community, suggesting that blessings should be equally distributed.
    • The phrase "may you bring forth" indicates a desire for prosperity and success in endeavors.
    • The term "immediately born" signifies the urgency and immediacy of these blessings, reflecting a deep yearning for divine support.
    • The reference to "seers" or "sages" connects the mantra to wisdom and knowledge, suggesting that true prosperity comes from understanding and enlightenment.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community gathering, this mantra can be recited to invoke blessings for collective prosperity, ensuring that both men and women are recognized for their contributions.
    • In a business setting, leaders might use this mantra to inspire teamwork and collaboration, emphasizing that success is a shared journey.
    • During family rituals, this mantra can be invoked to seek blessings for the well-being of all family members, reinforcing the idea of unity and support.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its inclusive approach to blessings, recognizing the roles of both genders in society.
    • It serves as a reminder that prosperity and success are not just individual pursuits but collective efforts that require the support of the community.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, from personal relationships to professional environments, promoting harmony and shared success.
