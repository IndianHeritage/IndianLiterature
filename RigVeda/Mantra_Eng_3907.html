यो अस्मै घ्रंसा उता वा य ऊधनि सोमं सुनोति भवति द्युमाम् अह |अपाप शक्रस ततनुष्टिम ऊहति तनूशुभ्रम मगवाऽ यः कवासखः ||
yo asmai ghraṁsa uta vā ya ūdhani somaṁ sunoti bhavati dyumām̐ aha | apāpa śakras tatanuṣṭim ūhati tanūśubhram maghavā yaḥ kavāsakhaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yo: who
    • asmai: to him
    • ghraṁsa: the one who is strong
    • uta: or
    • vā: or
    • ya: who
    • ūdhani: in the waters
    • somaṁ: the soma (a sacred drink)
    • sunoti: hears
    • bhavati: becomes
    • dyumām̐: radiant
    • aha: day
    • apāpa: free from sin
    • śakras: of the powerful
    • tatanuṣṭim: the one who is steadfast
    • ūhati: brings forth
    • tanūśubhram: of pure body
    • maghavā: the mighty
    • yaḥ: who
    • kavāsakhaḥ: the one who is a friend of the poets
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of a divine being who is strong and radiant, capable of hearing and understanding the sacred offerings made to him.
    • The reference to "soma" indicates the importance of this sacred drink in rituals, symbolizing divine inspiration and connection.
    • The mantra emphasizes the qualities of purity, strength, and steadfastness, suggesting that these attributes are essential for achieving greatness and divine favor.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, a leader who embodies strength and integrity can inspire their team, much like the divine being in the mantra.
    • In personal development, individuals who strive for purity of thought and action often find themselves more aligned with their goals and aspirations.
    • During rituals or significant life events, invoking the qualities of the divine can help individuals feel more connected to their purpose and the greater universe.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of strength, purity, and steadfastness in our lives.
    • It encourages individuals to seek divine inspiration and to embody the qualities that lead to success and fulfillment.
    • In practical terms, it can be applied in various aspects of life, from personal growth to leadership, emphasizing the need for integrity and connection to the divine.
