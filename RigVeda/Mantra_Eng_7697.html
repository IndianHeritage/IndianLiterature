तं ईम अण्वीः समर्य आ गृभ्णन्ति योषणो दश |स्वसारः पार्ये दिवि ||
tam īm aṇvīḥ samarya ā gṛbhṇanti yoṣaṇo daśa | svasāraḥ pārye divi ||
  • tam - This word translates to "that" or "him," indicating a focus on a specific entity or concept.
  • īm - This can mean "these" or "this," suggesting a connection to the present or immediate context.
  • aṇvīḥ - Refers to "the desires" or "the aspirations," highlighting the importance of intentions and goals.
  • samarya - This word means "together" or "in harmony," emphasizing unity and collective effort.
  • ā gṛbhṇanti - Translates to "they grasp" or "they attain," indicating the act of achieving or acquiring something.
  • yoṣaṇo - Refers to "the women" or "the feminine," which can symbolize nurturing, creativity, and life-giving forces.
  • daśa - This means "ten," which can represent completeness or a full cycle.
  • svasāraḥ - Translates to "the sisters" or "the kin," indicating familial bonds and relationships.
  • pārye - This word means "in the sky" or "in the heavens," suggesting a higher realm or spiritual aspect.
  • divi - Refers to "in the divine" or "in the celestial," reinforcing the connection to the spiritual or divine order.

The mantra speaks to the interconnectedness of desires, aspirations, and relationships, particularly emphasizing the role of women and familial bonds in achieving harmony and unity. It suggests that when individuals come together with shared intentions, they can attain their goals and reach a higher state of existence.


In real life, this can be seen in various scenarios, such as:

  • Community Projects: When a group of people, regardless of gender, collaborates on a community project, their combined aspirations can lead to significant achievements, such as building a park or organizing a festival.
  • Family Support: In families, the support and nurturing provided by women (mothers, sisters) can help individuals pursue their dreams, showcasing the importance of these relationships in personal growth.
  • Workplace Collaboration: In a professional setting, diverse teams that work together harmoniously can innovate and solve problems more effectively, demonstrating the power of collective effort.

In conclusion, this mantra highlights the significance of unity, shared aspirations, and the vital role of relationships in achieving goals. It serves as a reminder that when we come together with a common purpose, we can reach greater heights, both in our personal lives and within our communities.
