श्रातं मन्य उधनि श्रातं अग्नौ सुश्रातं मन्ये तद ऋतं नवीयः |माध्यंदिनस्य सवनस्य दध्नः पिबेन्द्र वज्रिन पुरुक्रिज्जुषाणः ||
śrātam manya ūdhani śrātam agnau suśrātam manye tad ṛtaṁ navīyaḥ | mādhyaṁdinasya savanasya dadhnaḥ pibendra vajrin purukṛj juṣāṇaḥ ||
  • śrātam manya ūdhani: This phrase can be interpreted as "the one who is worthy of being praised." In real life, this can relate to individuals who demonstrate qualities of leadership, integrity, and wisdom, making them deserving of respect and admiration.
  • śrātam agnau: Here, "agnau" refers to fire, which symbolizes purity and transformation. In many cultures, fire is seen as a sacred element. For example, in Hindu rituals, fire is used in ceremonies to purify offerings and invoke divine blessings.
  • suśrātam manye tad ṛtaṁ navīyaḥ: This can be understood as "I consider that truth to be new and fresh." This emphasizes the importance of seeking and valuing truth in our lives. In a practical sense, this can relate to the pursuit of knowledge and understanding, encouraging individuals to remain open-minded and adaptable to new ideas.
  • mādhyaṁdinasya savanasya dadhnaḥ: This phrase refers to the "midday offering." In daily life, this can symbolize the importance of taking time to reflect and give thanks during the day, much like a midday break where one can recharge and appreciate the present moment.
  • pibendra vajrin purukṛj juṣāṇaḥ: "Pibendra" refers to the "lord of the drink," which can symbolize nourishment and sustenance. This can be related to the idea of consuming knowledge and experiences that enrich our lives. For instance, engaging in meaningful conversations or reading can be seen as a way to nourish the mind and spirit.

  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of truth, respect, and the transformative power of knowledge and rituals in our lives. It encourages individuals to recognize and honor those who embody these qualities, to seek truth actively, and to take moments of reflection and gratitude throughout the day. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in personal development, leadership, and community building, fostering an environment where wisdom and integrity are valued and celebrated.
