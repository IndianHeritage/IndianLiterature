प्र वाम् अन्धांसी मद्यान्य अस्तुर अरं गन्तं हविषो वीतये मे |तिरो आर्यो हवनानि श्रुतं नः ||
pra vām andhāṁsi madyāny asthur araṁ gantaṁ haviṣo vītaye me | tiro aryo havanāni śrutaṁ naḥ ||
  • pra vām: This phrase can be interpreted as "to you" or "for you," indicating a direction of devotion or offering.
  • andhāṁsi: This word means "blindness" or "ignorance," suggesting a state of unawareness or lack of insight.
  • madyāny: Refers to "intoxicating substances" or "soma," which in Vedic rituals symbolizes divine inspiration and ecstasy.
  • asthur: This term can be understood as "to be strong" or "to be powerful," indicating a plea for strength or support.
  • araṁ: This word means "to go" or "to move," suggesting a journey or progression towards a goal.
  • gantaṁ: This translates to "to reach" or "to attain," emphasizing the importance of achieving one's objectives.
  • haviṣo: Refers to "offerings" or "sacrifices," which are central to Vedic rituals, symbolizing devotion and gratitude.
  • vītaye: This means "for liberation" or "for freedom," indicating a desire to transcend limitations.
  • me: This translates to "my," personalizing the plea or offering.
  • tiro: This word means "to remove" or "to dispel," suggesting a desire to eliminate obstacles or negativity.
  • aryo: This term can be interpreted as "noble" or "worthy," indicating a call for high ideals or virtues.
  • havanāni: Refers to "sacrificial offerings," emphasizing the importance of rituals in connecting with the divine.
  • śrutaṁ: This means "heard" or "listened to," indicating the importance of communication and understanding in spiritual practices.
  • naḥ: This translates to "our," indicating a collective plea or offering.

Conclusion: This mantra encapsulates a profound spiritual plea for guidance, strength, and liberation from ignorance. It emphasizes the importance of offerings and rituals in seeking divine support and clarity. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in moments of confusion or difficulty, where individuals seek to dispel ignorance and attain higher understanding. It serves as a reminder of the power of devotion and the significance of striving for noble ideals in our lives.
