युṣ्मादत्तस्य मरुतो विचेतसो रायः स्याम रथ्यो वयस्वतः |न यो युच्छति तिष्यो यथा दिवोऽस्मे रारन्त मरुतः सहस्रिणम् ||
yuṣmādattasya maruto vicetaso rāyaḥ syāma rathyo3 vayasvataḥ | na yo yucchati tiṣyo3 yathā divo3 'sme rāranta marutaḥ sahasriṇam ||
  • yuṣmādattasya: This word translates to "from you" or "from your gifts." It signifies the importance of receiving blessings or resources from a higher power or divine source.
  • maruto: Refers to the Maruts, the storm deities in Vedic literature, symbolizing strength, power, and the forces of nature.
  • vicetaso: This means "with intelligence" or "with awareness," indicating the need for wisdom in utilizing the gifts received.
  • rāyaḥ syāma: Translates to "may we attain prosperity" or "may we be rich," emphasizing the desire for abundance and well-being.
  • rathyo3 vayasvataḥ: This phrase can be interpreted as "in the chariot of youth," suggesting the importance of vitality and energy in achieving one's goals.
  • na yo yucchati: Means "he who does not strive" or "he who does not exert effort," highlighting the necessity of action and effort in life.
  • tiṣyo3: This word signifies "to be steadfast" or "to endure," which is crucial for achieving success.
  • yathā divo3: Translates to "as in the heavens," indicating a connection between earthly endeavors and divine or cosmic order.
  • 'sme rāranta: This means "may we be united" or "may we come together," emphasizing the importance of community and collaboration.
  • marutaḥ sahasriṇam: Refers to "the countless Maruts," symbolizing the abundance of blessings and the multitude of forces that can aid in our endeavors.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda encapsulates the essence of seeking divine blessings for prosperity and success while emphasizing the importance of effort, wisdom, and community. In real-life scenarios, it can be applied in various contexts such as personal development, teamwork, and spiritual practices. For instance, in a business setting, this mantra can inspire a team to work collaboratively towards a common goal, recognizing that their combined efforts, guided by wisdom and divine support, can lead to greater success. It serves as a reminder that while we seek abundance, we must also be willing to put in the necessary work and remain steadfast in our pursuits, fostering a sense of unity and purpose in our endeavors.
