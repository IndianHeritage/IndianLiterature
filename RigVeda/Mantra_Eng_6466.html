गोभिर वाणो अज्यते सोभरिणां रथे कोशे हिरण्यये |गोबन्धवः सुजातास इषे भुजे महान्तो नः स्परसे नु ||
gobhir vāṇo ajyate sobharīṇāṁ rathe kośe hiraṇyaye | gobandhavaḥ sujātāsa iṣe bhuje mahānto naḥ sparase nu ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • gobhir: by the cows or through the wealth
    • vāṇo: of speech or words
    • ajyate: is invoked or is called upon
    • sobharīṇāṁ: of those who are prosperous or wealthy
    • rathe: in the chariot
    • kośe: in the treasure or wealth
    • hiraṇyaye: golden or of gold
    • gobandhavaḥ: the one who is the lord of cows
    • sujātāsa: of good birth or noble lineage
    • iṣe: in the command or authority
    • bhuje: in the arms or strength
    • mahānto: of the great or mighty
    • naḥ: for us
    • sparase: may touch or influence
    • nu: indeed or surely
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about invoking the divine presence through the wealth and prosperity represented by cows, which are considered sacred in Vedic culture.
    • The reference to "speech" suggests the importance of communication and the power of words in manifesting desires and intentions.
    • The "chariot" symbolizes the journey of life, where wealth and prosperity are essential for progress.
    • The mention of "gold" signifies not just material wealth but also the richness of life experiences and spiritual growth.
    • The invocation of the "lord of cows" emphasizes the connection between material wealth and spiritual well-being.
    • The phrase "may touch us" indicates a desire for divine blessings to influence our lives positively.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In agriculture, the prosperity of farmers is often linked to the health of their livestock, particularly cows, which provide milk and other resources.
    • In business, effective communication (the power of words) can lead to successful negotiations and partnerships, much like invoking the divine for guidance.
    • In personal development, the journey of life can be seen as a chariot ride where one seeks wealth not just in material terms but also in knowledge and experiences.
    • Spiritual practices often emphasize the importance of noble lineage and good character, which can be seen as the foundation for a prosperous life.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the interconnectedness of material wealth, communication, and spiritual growth.
    • It serves as a reminder to seek balance in life, where prosperity is not just about financial success but also about nurturing relationships and personal development.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire individuals to invoke positive energies in their endeavors, ensuring that their journey through life is enriched with both material and spiritual wealth.
