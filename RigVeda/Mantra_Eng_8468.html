उन्मध्वा ऊर्मिर वनना अतिष्ठिपद अपो वसानो महिषो वि गाहते |राजा पवित्ररथो वाजम् आरुहत सहस्रभृष्टिर जयति श्रवो बृहत् ||
un madhva ūrmir vananā atiṣṭhipad apo vasāno mahiṣo vi gāhate | rājā pavitraratho vājam āruhat sahasrabhṛṣṭir jayati śravo bṛhat ||
  • Un Madhva Ūrmir Vananā Atiṣṭhipad: This phrase can be interpreted as "the sweet waves of the ocean rise up." Here, "Madhva" refers to sweetness, "Ūrmir" to waves, and "Vananā" to the act of rising. In real life, this can symbolize the uplifting nature of positive experiences or emotions that elevate our spirits.
  • Apo Vasāno Mahiṣo Vi Gāhate: This translates to "the great one who dwells in the waters." "Apo" means water, "Vasāno" refers to dwelling, and "Mahiṣo" signifies greatness. This can be related to the idea of finding strength and wisdom in the depths of our emotions or the subconscious, much like how water can be both calming and powerful.
  • Rājā Pavitraratho Vājam Āruhat: This means "the king who rides the pure chariot." "Rājā" stands for king, "Pavitraratho" refers to purity, and "Vājam" means chariot. This can be seen as a metaphor for leadership that is guided by integrity and righteousness, akin to a leader who makes decisions based on ethical principles.
  • Sahasrabhṛṣṭir Jayati Śravo Bṛhat: This translates to "the one with a thousand strengths who achieves great fame." "Sahasrabhṛṣṭir" refers to having many strengths, "Jayati" means to achieve or conquer, and "Śravo Bṛhat" signifies great fame or renown. This can be related to individuals in society who, through their diverse talents and hard work, achieve recognition and success.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates themes of positivity, emotional depth, ethical leadership, and the pursuit of greatness. In real life, it serves as a reminder to embrace uplifting experiences, seek wisdom within ourselves, lead with integrity, and recognize the diverse strengths we possess. This mantra can be applicable in various scenarios, such as personal development, leadership roles, and community building, encouraging individuals to strive for a harmonious and successful life.
