अच्छा वो देवीं उषसं विभातिम् प्र वो भरध्वं नमसां सुवृक्तिम् |ऊर्ध्वं मधुधा दिवि पाजो अश्रेत प्र रोचनां रुरुचे रण्वसंदृक् ||
acchā vo devīm uṣasaṁ vibhātīm pra vo bharadhvaṁ namasā suvṛktim | ūrdhvam madhudhā divi pājo aśret pra rocanā ruruce raṇvasaṁdṛk ||
  • acchā vo devīm uṣasaṁ vibhātīm: This phrase can be translated as "May we see the radiant goddess of dawn." Here, "uṣasa" refers to the goddess of dawn, symbolizing new beginnings and enlightenment. In real life, dawn represents the start of a new day, a time for fresh opportunities and hope.
  • pra vo bharadhvaṁ namasā suvṛktim: This means "May we invoke her with reverence and receive her blessings." The act of invoking signifies a deep respect and acknowledgment of the divine. In practical terms, this can be seen in rituals or moments of gratitude where individuals seek guidance or blessings for their endeavors.
  • ūrdhvam madhudhā divi pājo aśret: This translates to "May she elevate us to the heights of sweetness in the heavens." The word "madhudhā" (sweetness) symbolizes joy and fulfillment. In everyday life, this can relate to aspirations and achievements that bring happiness, such as personal success, fulfilling relationships, or spiritual growth.
  • pra rocanā ruruce raṇvasaṁdṛk: This phrase means "May her brilliance shine forth and illuminate our path." The concept of light is often associated with knowledge and clarity. In real life, this can be likened to moments of insight or understanding that guide decisions and actions, much like a mentor or a guiding principle.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking divine guidance and blessings as we navigate through life. It highlights the significance of new beginnings, respect for the divine, the pursuit of joy, and the illumination of our paths. In practical scenarios, this mantra can be applied during morning rituals, personal reflections, or any moment when one seeks clarity and inspiration. By invoking the goddess of dawn, individuals can cultivate a mindset of positivity and openness to new opportunities, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling life.
