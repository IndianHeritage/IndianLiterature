मां ते अस्यां सहसावन परिष्टाव अघाय भूम हरिवः परादै |त्रायस्व नो 'वृकेभिर वरूथैस तव प्रियासः सूरिषु स्याम ||
mā te asyāṁ sahasāvan pariṣṭāv aghāya bhūma harivaḥ parādai | trāyasva no 'vṛkebhir varūthais tava priyāsaḥ sūriṣu syāma ||
  • mā te asyāṁ sahasāvan: This phrase can be interpreted as "May we not be overwhelmed by your might." It reflects a plea for protection against overwhelming forces, whether they be natural disasters, personal challenges, or societal pressures.
  • pariṣṭāv aghāya bhūma: This translates to "In the midst of dangers, may we be safe." It emphasizes the importance of safety and security in our lives, especially when faced with adversities. For example, during a natural calamity like an earthquake, communities come together to ensure each other's safety.
  • harivaḥ parādai: This can be understood as "May we be free from the clutches of enemies." It signifies the desire for freedom from those who may wish to harm us, whether they are personal adversaries or larger societal threats. In real life, this could relate to standing up against bullying or injustice.
  • trāyasva no 'vṛkebhir: This phrase means "Protect us from the wolves." Here, 'wolves' symbolize threats or dangers that lurk in society. This could refer to various forms of danger, such as crime or deceit. For instance, community watch programs aim to protect neighborhoods from crime, embodying this protective spirit.
  • varūthais tava priyāsaḥ: This translates to "With your beloved ones." It highlights the importance of companionship and support from loved ones during difficult times. In real life, having a strong support system of family and friends can significantly impact our ability to cope with challenges.
  • sūriṣu syāma: This means "May we shine among the radiant ones." It expresses a desire to thrive and succeed in life, to be recognized for our efforts and contributions. For example, in a workplace, employees strive to excel and be acknowledged for their hard work, reflecting this aspiration.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a profound plea for protection, safety, and the desire to thrive amidst challenges. Its significance lies in the universal human experience of facing adversities and seeking support from loved ones. In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as during personal struggles, community challenges, or even in the workplace. It serves as a reminder of the importance of resilience, community support, and the pursuit of excellence in our endeavors.
