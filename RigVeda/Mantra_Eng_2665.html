धियाऽचक्रे वरेण्यो भूतानां गर्भमादधे |दक्षस्य पितरं तना ||
dhiyā cakre vareṇyo bhūtānāṁ garbham ā dadhe | dakṣasya pitaraṁ tanā ||
  • dhiyā: This word translates to "with intellect" or "with understanding." It emphasizes the importance of wisdom and knowledge in our actions and decisions.
  • cakre: This means "to create" or "to establish." It signifies the act of bringing something into existence or forming a foundation.
  • vareṇyo: This term can be interpreted as "the most excellent" or "the most desirable." It refers to something that is highly valued or esteemed.
  • bhūtānāṁ: This word means "of beings" or "of creatures." It encompasses all forms of life and existence.
  • garbham: This translates to "the womb" or "the seed." It symbolizes potential, creation, and the nurturing aspect of life.
  • ā dadhe: This means "he/she/it establishes" or "he/she/it places." It indicates the act of setting something in motion or giving it a place in the world.
  • dakṣasya: This word means "of the skilled" or "of the capable." It refers to those who possess the ability and expertise to accomplish tasks effectively.
  • pitaraṁ: This translates to "father" or "parent." It signifies the role of guidance, protection, and nurturing in the process of creation.
  • tanā: This means "body" or "form." It represents the physical manifestation of life and existence.

The mantra can be interpreted as a call to harness intellect and wisdom to create and nurture the potential within all beings. It emphasizes the importance of skilled guidance and the nurturing aspect of creation, akin to a parent caring for a child.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios:

  • Education: Teachers and mentors can embody the principles of this mantra by using their knowledge and skills to nurture the potential of their students.
  • Parenting: Parents can reflect the essence of this mantra by providing a supportive environment that fosters the growth and development of their children.
  • Leadership: Leaders in any field can apply this wisdom by guiding their teams with understanding and creating a culture that values growth and excellence.

In conclusion, this mantra signifies the profound connection between intellect, creation, and nurturing. It serves as a reminder of the responsibilities we hold in guiding and supporting the potential within ourselves and others. By embodying these principles, we can contribute to a more harmonious and flourishing existence for all beings.
