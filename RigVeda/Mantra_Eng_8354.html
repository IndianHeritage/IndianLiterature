प्रत्नान मानाद अध्य ये समस्वरञ्ज च्लोकयन्त्रासो रभसस्य मन्तवः |अपानक्षासो बधिरा अहासत ऋतस्य पन्थां न तरन्ति दुष्कृतः ||
pratnān mānād adhy ā ye samasvarañ chlokayantrāso rabhasasya mantavaḥ | apānakṣāso badhirā ahāsata ṛtasya panthāṁ na taranti duṣkṛtaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • pratnān: from the knowledge or understanding
    • mānād: from respect or honor
    • adhy: in the context of or regarding
    • ye: those who
    • samasvarañ: with a harmonious sound or voice
    • chlokayantrāso: those who are engaged in the art of recitation or chanting
    • rabhasasya: of the tumultuous or chaotic
    • mantavaḥ: those who are wise or knowledgeable
    • apānakṣāso: those who are free from distractions
    • badhirā: deaf or unhearing
    • ahāsata: they do not laugh or mock
    • ṛtasya: of truth or righteousness
    • panthāṁ: the path
    • na: not
    • taranti: they cross or overcome
    • duṣkṛtaḥ: those who commit wrongdoings
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of knowledge and respect in achieving harmony and wisdom.
    • It suggests that those who engage in the recitation of sacred texts with a pure heart and mind can navigate through the chaos of life.
    • It warns that those who are deaf to the truth and engage in wrongful actions will not find the righteous path.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, employees who respect their colleagues and work harmoniously are more likely to succeed and overcome challenges.
    • In personal relationships, open communication and understanding can help navigate conflicts and lead to stronger bonds.
    • In education, students who engage with their studies earnestly and respect their teachers are more likely to excel.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the significance of knowledge, respect, and righteousness in our lives.
    • It highlights that true wisdom comes from understanding and engaging with the world in a meaningful way.
    • In real-life scenarios, applying these principles can lead to personal growth, harmonious relationships, and a successful path in life.
