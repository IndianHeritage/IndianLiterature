वामं नो अस्त्व आर्यमन् वामं वरुण शंस्यम् |वामं ह्य आवृणीमहे ||
vāmaṁ no astv aryaman vāmaṁ varuṇa śaṁsyam | vāmaṁ hy āvṛṇīmahe ||
  • vāmaṁ no astv aryaman: This phrase can be translated to "May the pleasant (vāma) be with us, O Aryaman." Here, "vāma" signifies something that is agreeable or favorable, while "Aryaman" is a deity associated with friendship and harmony.
  • vāmaṁ varuṇa śaṁsyam: This translates to "May the pleasant (vāma) be with us, O Varuna." Varuna is a significant deity in the Rigveda, representing the cosmic order and moral authority. The invocation of Varuna emphasizes the importance of righteousness and ethical conduct.
  • vāmaṁ hy āvṛṇīmahe: This means "We indeed seek the pleasant (vāma)." This line expresses a collective desire for positivity and well-being, indicating a communal aspiration for harmony and good fortune.

  • Real-Life Example: In a modern context, this mantra can be related to the importance of fostering positive relationships and ethical behavior in our daily lives. For instance, in a workplace setting, promoting a culture of collaboration and respect among colleagues can lead to a more productive and harmonious environment.
  • Significance: The mantra emphasizes the value of seeking positivity and ethical conduct in our lives. It serves as a reminder to cultivate good relationships and uphold moral values, which are essential for personal and communal well-being.
  • Application: This mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as in family dynamics, community interactions, and professional environments. By invoking the principles of harmony and righteousness, individuals can contribute to a more peaceful and supportive society.

  • Conclusion: The mantra "vāmaṁ no astv aryaman vāmaṁ varuṇa śaṁsyam | vāmaṁ hy āvṛṇīmahe" serves as a powerful invocation for positivity and ethical living. Its significance lies in the universal aspiration for harmony and moral integrity, which is applicable in all aspects of life, encouraging individuals to strive for a better, more connected world.
