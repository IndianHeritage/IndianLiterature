आन्यं दिवो मातरिश्वा जभारामथ्नाद अन्यं परि श्येनो अद्रेः | अग्निषोमा ब्रह्मणा वावृद्धानोरुं यज्ञाय चक्रथुर उ लोकम् ||
ānyaṁ divo mātariśvā jabhārāmathnād anyam pari śyeno adreḥ | agnīṣomā brahmaṇā vāvṛdhānoruṁ yajñāya cakrathur u lokam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ānyaṁ: another
    • divo: of the sky
    • mātariśvā: the wind or the one who moves in the sky
    • jabhārā: has brought forth
    • amathnād: without effort
    • anyam: another
    • pari: around
    • śyeno: the eagle or the bird
    • adreḥ: of the mountain
    • agnīṣomā: the fire and the moon
    • brahmaṇā: by the divine power
    • vāvṛdhān: have increased
    • oruṁ: the strength
    • yajñāya: for the sacrifice
    • cakrathur: they have created
    • u: and
    • lokam: the world
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the divine forces that govern the universe, emphasizing the interconnectedness of elements like the sky, wind, fire, and moon.
    • The mention of "mātariśvā" symbolizes the vital energy that flows through the cosmos, akin to how we perceive the wind as a life-giving force.
    • The reference to "śyeno" (the eagle) signifies strength and vision, representing the ability to rise above challenges and gain a broader perspective in life.
    • The phrase "agnīṣomā" highlights the duality of fire and moon, symbolizing the balance of energy and calmness, which is essential in our daily lives.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In nature, the wind (mātariśvā) can be seen as a force that shapes landscapes, much like how challenges in life shape our character.
    • The eagle (śyeno) is often used as a metaphor for leadership and vision in business, where leaders must rise above the fray to see the bigger picture.
    • In personal development, the balance of fire (passion) and moon (calmness) is crucial for maintaining mental health and well-being.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of recognizing the divine forces at play in our lives and the world around us.
    • It teaches us to harness our inner strength and maintain balance, which is applicable in various aspects of life, including personal growth, leadership, and relationships.
    • By understanding and embodying these principles, we can navigate life's challenges with grace and purpose, ultimately contributing to a harmonious existence.
