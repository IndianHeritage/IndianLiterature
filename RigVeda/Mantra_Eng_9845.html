मन्त्र: महि द्यावापृथिवी भूतम् उर्वी नारी यह्वी न रोडसी सदं नः |तेभिर नः पातं सह्यसा एभिर नः पातं शूषणि ||
mahi dyāvāpṛthivī bhūtam urvī nārī yahvī na rodasī sadaṁ naḥ | tebhir naḥ pātaṁ sahyasa ebhir naḥ pātaṁ śūṣaṇi ||
  • mahi dyāvāpṛthivī: This phrase translates to "great is the sky and the earth." It signifies the vastness and interconnectedness of the universe. In real life, this can be seen in the way we rely on both the sky (for weather, sunlight) and the earth (for resources, sustenance).
  • bhūtam urvī: "The earth is the source of all beings." This emphasizes the importance of nature and the environment in sustaining life. For example, agriculture relies on the earth to produce food, highlighting our dependence on it.
  • nārī yahvī: "The woman who is the embodiment of strength." This acknowledges the role of women in society as nurturers and leaders. In contemporary life, women are increasingly recognized for their contributions in various fields, from science to politics.
  • na rodasī sadaṁ naḥ: "May the heavens and the earth be ever favorable to us." This is a prayer for harmony and balance in our lives. In practice, this can be seen in community efforts to promote environmental sustainability and social justice.
  • tebhir naḥ pātaṁ: "May they protect us." This is a call for divine protection and guidance. In real life, this can be related to seeking support from family, friends, and community during challenging times.
  • sahyasa ebhir naḥ pātaṁ: "May we be protected by the forces of nature." This highlights the importance of living in harmony with nature. For instance, conservation efforts aim to protect ecosystems that are vital for our survival.
  • śūṣaṇi: "In times of drought or hardship." This acknowledges the challenges we face and the need for resilience. In real life, communities often come together to support each other during crises, such as natural disasters or economic downturns.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda emphasizes the interconnectedness of the universe, the importance of nature, and the strength of community and women. Its significance lies in the call for harmony, protection, and resilience in the face of challenges. In today's world, these themes are applicable in various scenarios, such as environmental conservation, gender equality, and community support systems. By embodying these principles, we can work towards a more balanced and sustainable future.
