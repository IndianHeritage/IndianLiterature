परी विश्वानि चेतसा मृषसे पवसे मती |स नः सोम श्रवो विदः ||
pari viśvāni cetasā mṛśase pavase matī | sa naḥ soma śravo vidaḥ ||
  • Mantra: "pari viśvāni cetasā mṛśase pavase matī | sa naḥ soma śravo vidaḥ ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • pari: all around, everywhere
    • viśvāni: all, everything
    • cetasā: with the mind, with consciousness
    • mṛśase: you who are the source of truth
    • pavase: purify, cleanse
    • matī: intellect, understanding
    • sa: he, that
    • naḥ: our
    • soma: the divine nectar, often associated with immortality
    • śravo: fame, glory
    • vidaḥ: know, understand
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes a sense of universal consciousness and understanding.
    • It emphasizes the importance of purifying the mind and intellect to grasp the truth.
    • The reference to "soma" symbolizes the divine essence that nourishes and grants wisdom.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, this mantra can be applied to foster teamwork and collaboration, encouraging individuals to share their ideas and insights for a common goal.
    • In personal development, it can inspire individuals to seek clarity and understanding in their thoughts, leading to better decision-making.
    • In spiritual practices, it can be a reminder to purify one's thoughts and intentions, aligning them with higher truths.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the quest for knowledge and the importance of a pure mind in achieving understanding.
    • It is applicable in various aspects of life, from personal growth to professional environments, highlighting the need for clarity and collective wisdom.
    • By embracing the essence of this mantra, individuals can cultivate a deeper connection with themselves and the world around them, leading to a more fulfilling life.
