हस्काराद विद्युतस् पर्यतो जाताऽवंतु नः |मरुतो मृळयन्तु नः ||
haskārād vidyutas pary ato jātā avantu naḥ | maruto mṛḻayantu naḥ ||
  • Mantra: "haskārād vidyutas pary ato jātā avantu naḥ | maruto mṛḻayantu naḥ ||"
  • Translation: "From the brilliance of the lightning, may the forces that have arisen come to us; may the Maruts (storm deities) bring us prosperity."

  • Word Breakdown:
    • Haskārād: From the brilliance or radiance.
    • Vidyutas: Lightning or electrical energy.
    • Pary: From all around or surrounding.
    • Jātā: Arisen or born.
    • Avantu: May they come or may they arrive.
    • Naḥ: To us or for us.
    • Maruto: Referring to the Maruts, the storm deities associated with wind and storms.
    • Mṛḻayantu: May they bring or may they bestow.

  • Contextual Explanation:
    • This mantra invokes the power of natural forces, particularly the lightning and the Maruts, to bring blessings and prosperity.
    • Lightning is often seen as a symbol of divine energy and illumination, representing knowledge and enlightenment.
    • The Maruts are considered to be benevolent deities who control storms and winds, and they are often associated with strength and vitality.

  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In agriculture, farmers often pray for rain and favorable weather conditions. This mantra can be seen as a call for the natural elements to support their crops.
    • In personal life, individuals may seek guidance and clarity during challenging times, invoking the energy of lightning as a metaphor for sudden insights or revelations.
    • During community gatherings or festivals, this mantra can be recited to invoke the blessings of nature, promoting unity and prosperity among the participants.

  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the interconnectedness of nature and human life, highlighting the importance of seeking blessings from natural forces.
    • Its significance lies in the recognition of the divine in the elements around us, encouraging respect and gratitude towards nature.
    • In modern life, this mantra can inspire individuals to harness the power of nature for personal growth, community well-being, and environmental consciousness.
