तं व इन्द्रं चतिनम् अस्य शाकैर इह नूनं वाजयन्तो हुवेम |यथा चित पूर्वे जरितार आसुर अनद्यां अनवद्यां अरिष्टाः ||
taṁ va indraṁ catinam asya śākair iha nūnaṁ vājayanto huvema | yathā cit pūrve jaritāra āsur anedyā anavadyā ariṣṭāḥ ||
  • taṁ va indraṁ: This phrase refers to invoking Indra, the king of the gods in Vedic tradition, who represents strength, power, and the ability to overcome obstacles.
  • catinam asya: This can be interpreted as "with all of this," suggesting a collective effort or unity in purpose.
  • śākair: This word means "with branches" or "with strength," indicating the support and resources available to achieve a goal.
  • iha nūnaṁ: Translated as "here indeed," it emphasizes the present moment and the importance of the current situation.
  • vājayanto huvema: This phrase means "we invoke for victory," highlighting the desire for success and triumph in endeavors.
  • yathā cit: This means "as indeed," suggesting a connection to past experiences or traditions that guide current actions.
  • pūrve jaritāra: This refers to "the ancient ones" or "the ancestors," acknowledging the wisdom and achievements of those who came before us.
  • āsur: This term can mean "demons" or "obstacles," representing challenges that one must face.
  • anedyā anavadyā: These words mean "without fault" or "pure," indicating the importance of integrity and righteousness in one's actions.
  • ariṣṭāḥ: This translates to "those who are free from harm," suggesting a state of safety and protection that one seeks through divine intervention.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of invoking divine support, particularly from Indra, in the pursuit of success and overcoming challenges. It highlights the significance of unity, strength, and the wisdom of ancestors in navigating life's obstacles. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applicable in various situations such as starting a new venture, facing personal challenges, or seeking guidance in difficult times. By recognizing the collective strength and the purity of intentions, individuals can find the courage to overcome adversities and achieve their goals with integrity and support from the divine.
