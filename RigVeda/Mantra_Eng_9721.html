किं सुभाहो स्वङ्गुरे पृथुष्टो पृथुजाघने |किं शूरपत्नी नस् त्वम् अभ्य अमीषि वृषाकपिं विश्वस्माद इन्द्र उत्तरः ||
kiṁ subāho svaṅgure pṛthuṣṭo pṛthujāghane | kiṁ śūrapatni nas tvam abhy amīṣi vṛṣākapiṁ viśvasmād indra uttaraḥ ||
  • kiṁ subāho: This phrase can be interpreted as "What is the purpose of the strong?" Here, "subāho" refers to strength or valor. In real life, this can relate to individuals or communities that exhibit strength in overcoming challenges, such as athletes or leaders who inspire others through their resilience.
  • svaṅgure pṛthuṣṭo pṛthujāghane: This part can be translated to "What is the benefit of the one who is broad and mighty?" The term "pṛthuṣṭo" signifies someone who is expansive or influential. This can be likened to influential figures in society, such as philanthropists or activists who work towards the betterment of humanity.
  • kiṁ śūrapatni: This translates to "What is the role of the brave wife?" The term "śūrapatni" refers to a courageous partner. In modern contexts, this can be seen in the support systems provided by spouses or partners who stand by each other during difficult times, showcasing the importance of partnership in overcoming life's challenges.
  • nas tvam abhy amīṣi: This phrase means "You do not abandon us." It emphasizes the idea of support and loyalty. In real life, this can be reflected in friendships or family bonds where individuals stand by each other through thick and thin, reinforcing the value of trust and reliability in relationships.
  • vṛṣākapiṁ viśvasmād indra uttaraḥ: This can be interpreted as "O Indra, the one who is the best among all." Indra is often seen as a deity representing strength and leadership. In contemporary life, this can symbolize the pursuit of excellence and the aspiration to be a leader in one's field, whether in business, education, or community service.

Conclusion: The mantra reflects themes of strength, support, and leadership. It emphasizes the importance of resilience in the face of challenges, the value of partnerships, and the pursuit of excellence. In real-life scenarios, these principles can be applied in various contexts, such as personal relationships, community involvement, and professional endeavors. By embodying these qualities, individuals can contribute positively to their surroundings and inspire others to do the same.
