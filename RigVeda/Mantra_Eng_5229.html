इन्द्रं नो अग्नि वसुभिः सजोषा रुद्रं रुद्रैभिर आवह बृहन्तम् |आदित्यैभिर अदितिं विश्वजन्यां बृहस्पतिं ऋक्वभिर विश्ववारम् ||
indraṁ no agne vasubhiḥ sajoṣā rudraṁ rudrebhir ā vahā bṛhantam | ādityebhir aditiṁ viśvajanyām bṛhaspatim ṛkvabhir viśvavāram ||
  • Indraṁ no agne vasubhiḥ sajoṣā: This phrase invokes Indra, the king of gods, alongside Agni, the fire god. Indra represents strength and power, while Agni symbolizes energy and transformation. In real life, this can be seen as a call for strength and energy in our endeavors, whether in personal challenges or communal efforts.
  • Rudraṁ rudrebhir ā vahā bṛhantam: Here, Rudra, often associated with storms and the wild, is invoked. The term "bṛhantam" suggests greatness or vastness. This can be interpreted as a plea for the protection and guidance of powerful forces in our lives, akin to seeking support from mentors or leaders during turbulent times.
  • Ādityebhir aditiṁ viśvajanyām: Aditi is the mother of the gods and represents boundless potential. The mention of "Ādityebhir" refers to the solar deities, symbolizing light and knowledge. This line emphasizes the importance of nurturing potential and seeking enlightenment, much like how education and wisdom can illuminate our paths in life.
  • Bṛhaspatim ṛkvabhir viśvavāram: Bṛhaspati is the guru of the gods, representing wisdom and knowledge. The term "viśvavāram" suggests universal protection. This can be related to the importance of guidance and wisdom in our lives, akin to seeking advice from experienced individuals when making significant decisions.

  • Conclusion: This mantra encapsulates a plea for strength, guidance, and enlightenment from divine forces. In real life, it signifies the importance of seeking support from powerful and wise entities, whether they be spiritual, educational, or communal. It encourages individuals to harness their inner strength and seek knowledge to navigate life's challenges effectively. The mantra serves as a reminder that we are not alone in our struggles and that there are greater forces at play that can aid us in our journey.
