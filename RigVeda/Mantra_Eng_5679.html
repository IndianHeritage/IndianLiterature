तां नः स्तिपा तनूपा वरुण जरीतृणाम् |मित्र साधयतं धियः ||
tā naḥ stipā tanūpā varuṇa jaritṝṇām | mitra sādhayataṁ dhiyaḥ ||
  • tā naḥ - "That (which) is ours" or "That (which) belongs to us". This phrase indicates a sense of possession or connection to a higher power.
  • stipā - "firm" or "stable". This word suggests strength and reliability, emphasizing the need for a solid foundation in life.
  • tanūpā - "bodies" or "forms". This can refer to the physical existence or the various manifestations of life.
  • varuṇa - "Varuna\ the deity associated with cosmic order and water. Varuna represents the moral and natural laws that govern the universe.
  • jaritṝṇām - "of the seers" or "of the wise". This term refers to those who possess knowledge and insight, often seen as guides or mentors.
  • mitra - "friend" or "ally". This word signifies companionship and support, highlighting the importance of relationships in our lives.
  • sādhayataṁ - "may you accomplish" or "may you achieve". This is a call for success and fulfillment in endeavors.
  • dhiyaḥ - "intellect" or "thoughts". This refers to the mental faculties that guide our actions and decisions.

The mantra can be interpreted as a prayer or invocation for stability and support from the divine, particularly from Varuna, the deity of cosmic order. It emphasizes the importance of wisdom and friendship in achieving our goals and maintaining a stable existence.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios:

  • In personal relationships, it reminds us to seek supportive friendships that help us navigate challenges.
  • In professional settings, it encourages collaboration and teamwork, where the collective wisdom of a group can lead to success.
  • In spiritual practices, it serves as a reminder to connect with higher principles and seek guidance from wise mentors.

Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for stability, wisdom, and supportive relationships. It teaches us that by aligning ourselves with higher principles and fostering meaningful connections, we can achieve our goals and navigate the complexities of life with grace and strength.
