आ नो याहि महेमते सहस्रोते शतामघः |दिवो अमुष्य शासतो दिवं यय दिवावसो ||
ā no yāhi mahemate sahasrote śatāmagha | divo amuṣya śāsato divaṁ yaya divāvaso ||
  • Mantra: "ā no yāhi mahemate sahasrote śatāmagha | divo amuṣya śāsato divaṁ yaya divāvaso ||"
  • Translation: "Come to us, O mighty one, with your hundred streams of energy; may we attain the divine light that is beyond the heavens, where the day resides."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • ā no yāhi: "Come to us" - a call for divine presence and assistance.
    • mahemate: "O mighty one" - refers to a powerful deity or force.
    • sahasrote: "with your hundred streams" - symbolizes abundance and the flow of energy or blessings.
    • śatāmagha: "of energy" - indicates the strength and potency of the divine force.
    • dīvo: "of the heavens" - refers to the celestial realm or divine space.
    • amuṣya: "beyond" - signifies something that transcends ordinary experience.
    • śāsato: "eternal" - implies timelessness and permanence.
    • divaṁ: "light" - represents knowledge, enlightenment, and divine presence.
    • yaya: "where" - indicates a location or state of being.
    • divāvaso: "where the day resides" - symbolizes clarity, awareness, and the presence of truth.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In times of difficulty, individuals often seek guidance and support from a higher power, much like the invocation in this mantra. For instance, during a personal crisis, one might pray for strength and clarity, hoping to receive divine assistance.
    • In a corporate setting, leaders may invoke the spirit of collaboration and abundance when launching a new project, aiming to harness the collective energy of their team to achieve success.
    • During community gatherings or rituals, people often call upon divine forces to bless their endeavors, similar to how this mantra seeks the presence of a mighty force to illuminate their path.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine energy and guidance. It emphasizes the importance of seeking higher wisdom and support in our lives. In real-life scenarios, whether in personal challenges, professional endeavors, or communal activities, invoking such energies can lead to greater clarity, strength, and success. This mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of our efforts with the divine, encouraging us to remain open to receiving guidance and blessings from higher realms.
