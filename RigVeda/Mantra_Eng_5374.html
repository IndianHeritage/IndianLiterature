तव प्रणीतीन्द्र जोहुवानान सं यन् नृन् न रोदसी निन्थे |महें क्षत्राय शवसे हि जजñe 'तूतुजिं चित तूतुजिर अषिष्नत ||
tava praṇītīndra johuvānān saṁ yan nṝn na rodasī ninetha | mahe kṣatrāya śavase hi jajñe 'tūtujiṁ cit tūtujir aśiśnat ||
  • tava praṇītīndra: This phrase can be translated as "your guidance, O Indra." Here, Indra is a significant deity in Vedic literature, often associated with strength, rain, and the heavens. In real life, this can be seen as a call for divine guidance in our endeavors, much like seeking mentorship or leadership in our personal and professional lives.
  • johuvānān: This term means "those who invoke." It emphasizes the importance of actively seeking the divine or higher powers in our lives. For example, in a corporate setting, employees might invoke the spirit of teamwork and collaboration to achieve common goals.
  • saṁ yan nṝn na rodasī ninetha: This translates to "as you lead the men who do not stray from the path." It highlights the significance of staying true to one's values and principles. In everyday life, this can be likened to individuals who adhere to ethical practices in business, ensuring they do not compromise their integrity for short-term gains.
  • mahe kṣatrāya: This phrase means "for great power." It signifies the pursuit of strength and authority, not just in a physical sense but also in terms of influence and leadership. In real life, this can be seen in leaders who inspire and empower others, creating a strong and cohesive team.
  • śavase hi jajñe: This translates to "born from the great strength." It suggests that true power comes from a solid foundation and effort. For instance, athletes often train rigorously to build their strength, which leads to success in competitions.
  • 'tūtujiṁ cit tūtujir aśiśnat: This can be interpreted as "may we be blessed with prosperity and abundance." It reflects a desire for well-being and success. In practical terms, this can relate to individuals or communities striving for economic growth and stability, working together to create a prosperous environment.

  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of divine guidance, ethical leadership, and the pursuit of strength and prosperity. In real life, it serves as a reminder to seek mentorship, adhere to one's values, and work collaboratively towards common goals. Whether in personal relationships, professional settings, or community initiatives, the principles outlined in this mantra can inspire individuals to strive for greatness while remaining grounded in their ethical beliefs.
