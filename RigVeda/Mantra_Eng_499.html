सम पूषन्न अध्वनस तिर व्यंहो विमुचो नपात् |सक्ष्वा देव प्रणस पुरः ||
sam pūṣann adhvanas tira vy aṁho vimuco napāt | sakṣvā deva pra ṇas puraḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sam: together, in harmony
    • pūṣann: O nourisher, O provider
    • adhvanas: of the paths, of the ways
    • tira: across, beyond
    • vy aṁho: of the heavens, of the sky
    • vimuco: release, free
    • napāt: from the offspring, from the progeny
    • sakṣvā: with strength, with power
    • deva: O divine one, O god
    • pra ṇas: of the life force, of the vitality
    • puraḥ: in front, ahead
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to the divine for nourishment and guidance.
    • It emphasizes the importance of harmony and unity in our paths and endeavors.
    • The invocation of the divine suggests seeking strength and support in our lives.
    • It also highlights the need to free ourselves from the burdens of the past and to move forward with vitality.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, this mantra can be applied when a team is working together on a project. The emphasis on harmony and nourishment can lead to better collaboration and innovation.
    • In personal life, individuals may use this mantra during times of transition, such as starting a new job or moving to a new city, to seek divine guidance and strength.
    • In community service, the mantra can inspire collective efforts to uplift and support those in need, fostering a sense of unity and purpose.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for unity, strength, and divine support in our journeys.
    • It serves as a reminder to seek nourishment not just for ourselves but for those around us, promoting a sense of community and shared purpose.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be a source of inspiration during challenges, encouraging individuals and groups to move forward with confidence and vitality.
