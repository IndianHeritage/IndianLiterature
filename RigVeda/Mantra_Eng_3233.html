तिग्मा यदन्तर अशनिः पताति कस्मिञ्चिच्छूर मुहुके जनानाम् |घोरा यद आर्य समृतिर भवात्य अध स्मा नस् तन्भो बोधि गोपाः ||
tigmā yad antar aśaniḥ patāti kasmiñ cic chūra muhuke janānām | ghorā yad arya samṛtir bhavāty adha smā nas tanvo bodhi gopāḥ ||
  • tigmā yad antar aśaniḥ patāti: This phrase can be translated as "the sharp lightning that strikes within." Here, "tigmā" means sharp or intense, "antar" means within, and "aśaniḥ" refers to lightning. This can symbolize sudden insights or realizations that can occur in our lives, akin to a moment of clarity that can change our perspective.
  • kasmiñ cic chūra muhuke janānām: This translates to "in which place does the fierce one strike among people?" The term "chūra" refers to something fierce or powerful, and "muhuke" can mean among people. This can be interpreted as questioning where such powerful moments of realization or change occur in society, perhaps in times of crisis or transformation.
  • ghorā yad arya samṛtir bhavāty: This means "when the terrible (ghorā) noble (arya) strength (samṛtir) arises." Here, "ghorā" indicates something fearsome or intense, while "arya" refers to nobility or virtue. This can reflect the idea that from intense challenges, noble qualities can emerge, such as courage and resilience.
  • adha smā nas tanvo bodhi gopāḥ: This translates to "therefore, may the protectors (gopāḥ) of our bodies (tanvo) grant us wisdom (bodhi)." "Gopāḥ" refers to protectors or guardians, and "bodhi" signifies enlightenment or wisdom. This suggests a plea for guidance and protection in our journey towards understanding and enlightenment.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the transformative power of intense experiences and realizations in our lives. It highlights how moments of clarity can arise from challenges, leading to noble qualities and wisdom. In real-life scenarios, this can be applicable in various contexts, such as personal growth during difficult times, societal changes during crises, or even in the pursuit of knowledge and understanding. The mantra serves as a reminder to seek wisdom and protection as we navigate through life's challenges, encouraging us to embrace the fierce moments that can lead to profound insights and growth.
