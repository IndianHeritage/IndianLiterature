अव स्म दुर्हणायतो मार्तस्य तनुहि स्थिरम् |अधस्पदं तं ईं कृत्‍हि यो अस्मां ādideśati देवī जनित्री अजīजनद भद्रā जनित्री अजīजनत ||
ava sma durhaṇāyato martasya tanuhi sthiram | adhaspadaṁ tam īṁ kṛdhi yo asmām̐ ādideśati devī janitry ajījanad bhadrā janitry ajījanat ||
  • ava sma durhaṇāyato martasya tanuhi sthiram: This phrase can be translated to mean "May we be protected from the harmful forces that threaten our physical bodies." Here, "ava" signifies a plea for protection, while "durhaṇāyato" refers to harmful or negative influences. "Martasya tanuhi" indicates the physical body of a mortal, and "sthiram" means stable or firm.
  • adhaspadaṁ tam īṁ kṛdhi: This part translates to "Establish that which is below." "Adhaspadaṁ" refers to the foundation or the base, while "tam īṁ kṛdhi" means to create or establish that which is necessary for stability and support.
  • yo asmām̐ ādideśati devī janitry ajījanad: This translates to "The one who commands us, the divine mother, has given birth." "Yo asmām̐ ādideśati" refers to the divine force or deity that guides us, while "devī janitry ajījanad" emphasizes the nurturing aspect of the divine feminine, suggesting that she is the source of creation and sustenance.
  • bhadrā janitry ajījanat: This can be interpreted as "The auspicious mother has given birth." "Bhadrā" signifies auspiciousness or goodness, reinforcing the idea that the divine feminine brings forth positive and beneficial outcomes.

  • Significance: This mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking protection from negative influences and establishing a strong foundation in life. It highlights the nurturing aspect of the divine feminine, suggesting that we are guided and supported by a higher power.
  • Real-life application: In daily life, this mantra can be applied in various situations where one seeks stability and protection. For instance, during challenging times such as illness or personal crises, individuals can recite this mantra to invoke a sense of security and divine support. It can also be used in rituals or meditative practices aimed at grounding oneself and fostering a connection with the nurturing aspects of the universe.

  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a reminder of the protective and nurturing forces in our lives. By acknowledging the divine feminine and seeking her guidance, we can cultivate a sense of stability and well-being. This mantra encourages us to remain grounded and to trust in the support that surrounds us, ultimately leading to a more harmonious existence.
