सम अध्वरायोषसो नमन्त दधिक्रावेव शुचये पदाय |अर्वाचीनं वसुविदं भगं नो रथम इवाश्वा वाजिन आ वहन्तु ||
sam adhvarāyoṣaso namanta dadhikrāveva śucaye padāya | arvācīnaṁ vasuvidam bhagaṁ no ratham ivāśvā vājina ā vahantu ||
  • Translation: "As the priests perform the sacrifice, we bow down to the one who is pure like the milk of the cow. May the wealth of the ancient ones come to us, just as the horses draw the chariot."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • sam adhvarāyoṣaso: Refers to the priests performing the sacred rituals or sacrifices.
    • namanta: To bow down or show reverence.
    • dadhikrāveva: Like the pure milk, symbolizing purity and nourishment.
    • śucaye: To the pure one, indicating a divine or sacred entity.
    • padāya: Referring to the path or way, often associated with guidance.
    • arvācīnaṁ: The ancient or traditional, representing wisdom and heritage.
    • vasuvidam: Wealth or prosperity, often linked to material and spiritual abundance.
    • bhagaṁ: Blessing or fortune, indicating divine favor.
    • no: Our, indicating possession or belonging.
    • ratham: Chariot, symbolizing movement and progress.
    • ivāśvā: Like horses, representing strength and speed.
    • vājina: The swift or powerful, often associated with energy and vitality.
    • ā vahantu: May they bring, a prayer for the arrival of blessings.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a modern context, this mantra can be recited during significant life events such as weddings or housewarming ceremonies, where individuals seek blessings for purity, prosperity, and guidance.
    • In corporate settings, leaders may invoke similar sentiments during meetings or launches, emphasizing teamwork (like the chariot and horses) and the importance of a pure intention behind their endeavors.
    • In personal development, individuals may reflect on the mantra's themes of reverence and purity as they strive for personal growth and ethical living, akin to the nourishing qualities of milk.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for reverence, purity, and the pursuit of wealth and blessings through collective effort and divine guidance. It emphasizes the importance of tradition and the wisdom of the ancients in navigating life's journey. In real-life scenarios, it serves as a reminder to approach endeavors with respect and integrity, fostering a sense of community and shared purpose, much like the harmonious functioning of a chariot drawn by strong horses.
