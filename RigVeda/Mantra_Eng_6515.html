ताव इदाचिद अहानां ताव अश्विनां वन्दमान उप ब्रुवे |तौ उ नमोभिर इमहे ||
tāv idā cid ahānāṁ tāv aśvinā vandamāna upa bruve | tā u namobhir īmahe ||
  • tāv idā cid ahānāṁ: This phrase can be translated as "You two, indeed, are the ones who are here." It acknowledges the presence of the Ashvins, the twin deities in Vedic mythology known for their healing and benevolent qualities.
  • tāv aśvinā vandamāna upa bruve: This translates to "I speak to you, O Ashvins, with reverence." It signifies a respectful invocation, recognizing the divine qualities of the Ashvins and seeking their blessings.
  • tā u namobhir īmahe: This means "We invoke you with our salutations." It emphasizes the act of worship and the importance of humility in seeking divine assistance.

The mantra as a whole is a prayer to the Ashvins, expressing reverence and a desire for their presence and blessings. The Ashvins are often associated with health, vitality, and the dispelling of darkness, both literally and metaphorically.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios:

  • Health and Healing: Just as the Ashvins are known for their healing powers, individuals can recite this mantra during times of illness or when seeking good health. It serves as a reminder to seek help from higher powers and to maintain a positive outlook on recovery.
  • Guidance and Support: The invocation of the Ashvins can be relevant in situations where one seeks guidance, such as during important life decisions or transitions. It encourages individuals to remain open to divine support and wisdom.
  • Community and Togetherness: The mantra emphasizes the importance of collective invocation. In community gatherings or rituals, reciting this mantra can foster a sense of unity and shared purpose, invoking blessings for the entire group.

In conclusion, this mantra highlights the significance of recognizing divine presence and seeking blessings with humility and respect. It serves as a powerful reminder of the interconnectedness of health, guidance, and community support in our lives. By invoking the Ashvins, individuals can cultivate a sense of hope and resilience, drawing upon the divine for strength and healing in their everyday experiences.
