इमं यम प्रस्तरम् आ हि सीदाङ्गिरोभिः पितृभिः संविदानः |आ त्वा मन्त्राः कविशस्तां वहन्त्व एनां राजन हविषा मादयस्व ||
imaṁ yama prastaram ā hi sīdāṅgirobhiḥ pitṛbhiḥ saṁvidānaḥ | ā tvā mantrāḥ kaviśastā vahantv enā rājan haviṣā mādayasva ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • imaṁ: this
    • yama: the path or way
    • prastaram: spread out or laid out
    • ā hi: indeed
    • sīdāṅgirobhiḥ: with the words of the ancestors
    • pitṛbhiḥ: by the forefathers
    • saṁvidānaḥ: having been established or arranged
    • ā: to
    • tvā: you
    • mantrāḥ: the mantras or hymns
    • kaviśastā: the wise or the poets
    • vahantv: may carry or bring
    • enā: this
    • rājan: O king
    • haviṣā: with offerings
    • mādayasva: may you be pleased
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the path laid out by the ancestors, emphasizing the importance of tradition and wisdom passed down through generations.
    • The phrase "with the words of the ancestors" highlights the significance of honoring those who came before us, suggesting that their guidance is essential for our journey.
    • It calls upon the wise poets to carry forth the mantras, indicating the role of creativity and expression in connecting with the divine and the past.
    • By addressing the king, it signifies the importance of leadership and the responsibility of rulers to uphold the values and teachings of their forefathers.
    • The offering aspect suggests that gratitude and respect towards the divine and ancestors are crucial for receiving blessings and guidance.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In many cultures, rituals are performed to honor ancestors, such as the Day of the Dead in Mexico, where families remember and celebrate their deceased loved ones.
    • In modern leadership, successful leaders often refer to the wisdom of past leaders and mentors, using their teachings to guide their decisions and actions.
    • Creative expressions, such as poetry and music, often draw inspiration from historical events and figures, helping to keep the wisdom of the past alive in contemporary society.
    • In personal development, individuals may seek guidance from their family history, learning from the experiences of their ancestors to navigate their own lives.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of honoring our roots and the wisdom of our ancestors in shaping our present and future.
    • It serves as a reminder that our journey is interconnected with those who came before us, and their teachings can guide us in our decisions.
    • In real life, applying the principles of respect, gratitude, and wisdom can lead to personal growth, stronger communities, and effective leadership.
    • Ultimately, this mantra encourages us to carry forward the legacy of our ancestors while embracing the creative expressions that connect us to our heritage.
