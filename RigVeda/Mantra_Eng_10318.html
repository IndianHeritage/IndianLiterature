इराज्यन्न अग्ने प्रथयस्व जन्तुभिर अस्मे रायो अमर्त्य |स दर्षतस्य वपुषो वि राजसि पृणक्षि सानसिं क्रतुम् ||
irajyann agne prathayasva jantubhir asme rāyo amartya | sa darśatasya vapuṣo vi rājasi pṛṇakṣi sānasiṁ kratum ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • irajyann: O Agni, the one who is invoked.
    • agne: O Fire, representing the divine energy.
    • prathayasva: Shine forth or manifest.
    • jantubhir: With all beings or creatures.
    • asme: For us or to us.
    • rāyo: Wealth or prosperity.
    • amartya: Immortal or divine.
    • sa: He or that.
    • darśatasya: Of the one who is seen or perceived.
    • vapuṣo: Of the body or form.
    • vi: In or among.
    • rājasi: You reign or rule.
    • pṛṇakṣi: You fill or satisfy.
    • sānasiṁ: With the essence or the core.
    • kratum: Intention or resolve.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Agni, the fire god, who symbolizes energy, transformation, and the divine presence in the universe.
    • It calls upon Agni to manifest his brilliance and to bless all beings with prosperity and immortality.
    • The invocation emphasizes the importance of divine energy in achieving wealth and fulfilling one's intentions.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, invoking the spirit of Agni can be likened to fostering a culture of innovation and transformation, where employees are encouraged to shine and contribute to the company's success.
    • In personal life, this mantra can inspire individuals to pursue their goals with determination, seeking divine guidance to manifest their dreams and aspirations.
    • During rituals or ceremonies, lighting a fire symbolizes the presence of Agni, representing the connection between the material and spiritual worlds, and inviting prosperity into one's life.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine energy to manifest in our lives, promoting prosperity and fulfilling our intentions.
    • It teaches us the importance of seeking higher guidance and the transformative power of intention in achieving our goals.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, from personal development to community building, emphasizing the role of divine energy in our endeavors.
