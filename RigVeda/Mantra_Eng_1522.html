यद वश चित्रं युगे युगे नव्यं घोषाद अमर्त्यम् |अस्मासु तन् मरुतो यच च दुष्टरं दिधृतā यच च दुष्टарам ||
yad vaś citraṁ yuge-yuge navyaṁ ghoṣād amartyam | asmāsu tan maruto yac ca duṣṭaraṁ didhṛtā yac ca duṣṭaram ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yad: whatever
    • vaś: is under control
    • citraṁ: colorful or diverse
    • yuge-yuge: in every age or era
    • navyaṁ: new or fresh
    • ghoṣād: from the sound or call
    • amartyam: immortal or eternal
    • asmāsu: in us
    • tan: that
    • maruto: of the winds or the powerful
    • yac: which
    • ca: and
    • duṣṭaraṁ: difficult or challenging
    • didhṛtā: held or maintained
    • yac: which
    • ca: and
    • duṣṭaram: more difficult or worse
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the eternal and diverse aspects of existence that are present in every age.
    • It emphasizes the idea that whatever is under control, whether it is colorful or new, is a part of the immortal essence.
    • The reference to "maruto" suggests the powerful forces of nature, indicating that these forces can be both beneficial and challenging.
    • The mantra acknowledges the difficulties and challenges ("duṣṭaraṁ") that we face, yet it also highlights the importance of holding onto the essence of life amidst these challenges.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In the context of personal growth, individuals often face challenges that seem insurmountable. This mantra reminds us that these challenges are part of the journey and that we can find strength in our essence.
    • In a professional setting, teams may encounter difficult projects. The mantra encourages collaboration and resilience, suggesting that by working together, they can overcome obstacles.
    • In nature, the changing seasons represent the cycles of life. The mantra reflects the idea that while seasons change and challenges arise, the underlying essence of nature remains eternal.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of recognizing the eternal aspects of life amidst the challenges we face.
    • It teaches us to embrace change and diversity while remaining grounded in our core values and essence.
    • In real-life scenarios, this understanding can lead to resilience, collaboration, and a deeper appreciation for the journey of life.
