प्रति त्वा दूहितर दिव उषो जीराऽभुत्स्महि |या वहसि पुरु स्पार्हं वनन्वति रत्नं न दाशुषे मयः ||
prati tvā duhitar diva uṣo jīrā abhutsmahi | yā vahasi puru spārhaṁ vananvati ratnaṁ na dāśuṣe mayaḥ ||
  • Mantra: "prati tvā duhitar diva uṣo jīrā abhutsmahi | yā vahasi puru spārhaṁ vananvati ratnaṁ na dāśuṣe mayaḥ ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • prati: towards
    • tvā: you
    • duhitar: daughter
    • diva: of the sky
    • uṣo: dawn
    • jīrā: we invoke
    • abhutsmahi: we have come
    • yā: who
    • vahasi: carries
    • puru: many
    • spārhaṁ: desires
    • vananvati: flourishing
    • ratnaṁ: jewels
    • na: not
    • dāśuṣe: to the unworthy
    • mayaḥ: by us
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer directed towards the divine feminine energy represented by the dawn (Uṣa). It acknowledges the dawn as a daughter of the sky, symbolizing new beginnings and enlightenment.
    • The invocation "jīrā abhutsmahi" signifies a call to the dawn to come forth and bless us with its presence, illuminating our path and fulfilling our desires.
    • The phrase "yā vahasi puru spārhaṁ" indicates that the dawn carries many desires, suggesting that it is a time for aspirations and hopes to be realized.
    • Furthermore, "vananvati ratnaṁ" refers to the flourishing jewels, which can be interpreted as the blessings and abundance that come with the dawn, while "na dāśuṣe mayaḥ" emphasizes that these blessings should not be given to the unworthy, highlighting the importance of deservingness in receiving divine grace.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, the dawn represents a fresh start. For instance, every morning, people often set intentions or goals for the day, much like invoking the dawn for guidance and support.
    • In a broader context, this mantra can be applied during significant life transitions, such as starting a new job or moving to a new place, where one seeks clarity and direction.
    • In personal development, the idea of deservingness can be reflected in how individuals work towards their goals, ensuring they are prepared and worthy of the success they seek.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of new beginnings, the pursuit of desires, and the importance of being worthy of the blessings we seek. It serves as a reminder to approach each day with intention and gratitude, recognizing the potential for growth and abundance.
    • In real life, it encourages individuals to reflect on their aspirations and to align their actions with their values, ensuring that they are deserving of the blessings that come their way.
