गृह्णामि ते सौभगत्वाय हस्तं मया पत्या जरदष्टिर यथासः |भागो आर्यमा सविता पुरंधिर मह्यं त्वादुर गार्हपत्या देवाः ||
gṛbhṇāmi te saubhagatvāya hastam mayā patyā jaradaṣṭir yathāsaḥ | bhago aryamā savitā puraṁdhir mahyaṁ tvādur gārhapatyāya devāḥ ||
  • gṛbhṇāmi te saubhagatvāya: This phrase translates to "I grasp you for auspiciousness." Here, the speaker expresses a desire to hold onto or embrace something that brings good fortune. In real life, this can be likened to seeking positive opportunities or relationships that enhance one's life.
  • hastam mayā patyā: This means "with my husband’s hand." It signifies partnership and collaboration in achieving goals. For example, in a marriage, both partners work together to create a harmonious and prosperous life.
  • jaradaṣṭir yathāsaḥ: This can be interpreted as "like the one who is wise." Wisdom is a crucial aspect of making informed decisions in life. For instance, a wise person may choose to invest in education or health, leading to long-term benefits.
  • bhago aryamā savitā: This phrase refers to "the divine light of Savitā." Savitā is often associated with the sun, symbolizing enlightenment and clarity. In practical terms, this can relate to seeking knowledge and understanding in various aspects of life, such as pursuing a career or personal growth.
  • puraṁdhir mahyaṁ: This translates to "the one who is the creator of the universe." It emphasizes the importance of creation and innovation. In real life, this can be seen in entrepreneurs who create new businesses or artists who bring new ideas to life.
  • tvādur gārhapatyāya devāḥ: This means "the deities who are far from the household." It suggests a connection to the divine and the importance of spirituality in everyday life. For example, many people find solace and guidance through prayer or meditation, which can help them navigate challenges.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda emphasizes the importance of auspiciousness, partnership, wisdom, enlightenment, creativity, and spirituality in our lives. It serves as a reminder to seek positive influences, work collaboratively with loved ones, make wise choices, pursue knowledge, innovate, and maintain a connection with the divine. In real-life scenarios, these principles can guide individuals in their personal and professional journeys, leading to a fulfilling and prosperous life.
