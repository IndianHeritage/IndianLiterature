उद īरतां सूनृतā उत पुरंधीऱ उद अग्नयः शुशुचānासो अस्तुḥ |स्पार्हा वसूनि तमसापगूḻ्हाविष कृत्वन्त्य उषसो विभातिः ||
ud īratāṁ sūnṛtā ut puraṁdhīr ud agnayaḥ śuśucānāso asthuḥ | spārhā vasūni tamasāpagūḻhāviṣ kṛṇvanty uṣaso vibhātīḥ ||
  • ud īratāṁ: This phrase can be interpreted as "may we rise" or "may we elevate ourselves." It signifies the aspiration for growth and enlightenment.
  • sūnṛtā: This term means "truthful" or "righteous." It emphasizes the importance of honesty and integrity in our actions and thoughts.
  • ut puraṁdhīr: This can be translated as "the one who is wise" or "the enlightened one." It refers to the pursuit of wisdom and understanding in life.
  • ud agnayaḥ: This phrase means "may the fires rise." Fire is often a symbol of energy, transformation, and purification. It suggests the idea of igniting passion and purpose.
  • śuśucānāso asthuḥ: This can be interpreted as "those who are aware" or "those who are conscious." It highlights the importance of awareness and mindfulness in our lives.
  • spārhā vasūni: This phrase means "the desires for wealth" or "the aspirations for prosperity." It reflects the human pursuit of material and spiritual wealth.
  • tamasāpagūḻhāviṣ: This can be translated as "overcoming darkness" or "dispelling ignorance." It signifies the quest for knowledge and enlightenment, moving away from ignorance.
  • kṛṇvanty: This term means "they create" or "they bring forth." It emphasizes the active role individuals play in shaping their destinies and environments.
  • uṣaso vibhātīḥ: This phrase translates to "the dawn shines." It symbolizes new beginnings, hope, and the arrival of knowledge and clarity.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a profound message about the journey of life, emphasizing the importance of rising above challenges, seeking truth, and pursuing wisdom. It encourages individuals to ignite their inner fire, be aware of their surroundings, and strive for prosperity while overcoming ignorance. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in personal development, leadership, and community building, where individuals are motivated to uplift themselves and others, fostering a culture of integrity, awareness, and enlightenment. It serves as a reminder that every dawn brings new opportunities for growth and understanding, urging us to embrace them with open hearts and minds.
