धन्वा च यत्कृतान्त्रम् च कति स्वित ता वि योजनाः |नेदीयसो वृषाकपेऽस्तम् एहि गृहां उप विश्वस्माद इन्द्र उत्तरः ||
dhanva ca yat kṛntatraṁ ca kati svit tā vi yojanā | nedīyaso vṛṣākape 'stam ehi gṛhām̐ upa viśvasmād indra uttaraḥ ||
  • Mantra: "dhanva ca yat kṛntatraṁ ca kati svit tā vi yojanā | nedīyaso vṛṣākape 'stam ehi gṛhām̐ upa viśvasmād indra uttaraḥ ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • dhanva: bow or weapon
    • ca: and
    • yat: that which
    • kṛntatraṁ: cuts or destroys
    • ca: and
    • kati: how many
    • svit: indeed
    • tā: those
    • vi: in
    • yojanā: measurement or distance
    • nedīyaso: of the non-possessors
    • vṛṣākape: O Indra, the strong one
    • 'stam: go away or depart
    • ehi: come
    • gṛhām̐: to the house
    • upa: near
    • viśvasmād: from all
    • indra: O Indra, the deity
    • uttaraḥ: the higher or supreme
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the deity Indra, often associated with strength, power, and protection.
    • The reference to "dhanva" and "kṛntatraṁ" suggests a focus on overcoming obstacles or enemies, akin to how a warrior uses a bow to defeat foes.
    • The phrase "kati svit tā vi yojanā" can be interpreted as questioning the extent or distance of challenges faced, emphasizing the need for awareness of one's surroundings.
    • By calling upon Indra, the mantra seeks divine assistance in navigating life's challenges, much like a person might seek guidance or support from a mentor or leader in difficult times.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a competitive work environment, one might feel overwhelmed by challenges. This mantra serves as a reminder to seek strength and guidance, just as a team might rally around a leader for support.
    • In personal relationships, when conflicts arise, invoking the spirit of Indra can symbolize the need for resolution and strength to overcome misunderstandings.
    • During times of crisis, such as natural disasters, communities often come together, invoking higher powers for protection and guidance, similar to the essence of this mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the importance of seeking strength and guidance in the face of challenges.
    • It serves as a reminder that, like the warrior with a bow, we must be prepared to confront obstacles with courage and support from higher powers or leaders.
    • In real life, it encourages individuals to recognize their challenges, seek help, and strive for resolution, embodying the spirit of resilience and community.
