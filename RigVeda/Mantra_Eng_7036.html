यस्मा अर्कं सप्तशीर्षाणम आनृचुस त्रिधातुम उत्तमे पदे |स तव इमां विश्वा भुवनानि चक्रददाद इज जनिष्ट पौंस्यम् ||
yasmā arkaṁ saptaśīrṣāṇam ānṛcus tridhātum uttame pade | sa tv i1mā viśvā bhuvanāni cikradad ād ij janiṣṭa pauṁsyam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yasmā: From whom
    • arkaṁ: The sun or light
    • saptaśīrṣāṇam: Seven heads or aspects
    • ānṛcus: The hymns or praises
    • tridhātum: In three forms or realms
    • uttame: The highest or supreme
    • pade: Place or position
    • sa: He or that
    • tv: You
    • imā: These
    • viśvā: All or universal
    • bhuvanāni: Worlds or realms
    • cikradad: Created or manifested
    • ād: Indeed
    • ij: To be born or generated
    • janiṣṭa: Has been born
    • pauṁsyam: Of the male or masculine
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of a supreme source from which the light (the sun) and its seven aspects emerge.
    • The "seven heads" can symbolize various dimensions of existence or consciousness, much like how different cultures recognize multiple facets of reality.
    • The phrase "in three forms" may refer to the physical, mental, and spiritual realms, indicating a holistic view of existence.
    • The mantra emphasizes the creative power of this supreme source, which manifests all worlds and realities.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern science, the concept of light is fundamental to understanding the universe, much like the sun's role in sustaining life on Earth.
    • The idea of multiple dimensions can be seen in various fields, such as psychology, where different aspects of the self are explored.
    • In spirituality, the recognition of a supreme source that creates and sustains all existence is a common theme across many religions.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the interconnectedness of all existence and the supreme source from which everything arises.
    • It encourages individuals to recognize the light within themselves and the world around them, promoting a sense of unity and purpose.
    • In practical terms, this can inspire people to seek knowledge, embrace creativity, and foster a deeper understanding of their place in the universe.
