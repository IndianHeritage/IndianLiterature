एवाग्निर गोतमैभिर ऋतावाऽ विप्रैभिर अस्तोष्ठ जातवेदाः |स एषु द्युम्नं पीपयत स वाजं स पुष्टिं याति जोषं आ चिकीत्वान् ||
evāgnir gotamebhir ṛtāvā viprebhir astoṣṭa jātavedāḥ | sa eṣu dyumnam pīpayat sa vājaṁ sa puṣṭiṁ yāti joṣam ā cikitvān ||
  • evāgnir: This term refers to the divine fire or Agni, which is considered a vital force in Vedic literature. Agni symbolizes energy, transformation, and the connection between the physical and spiritual realms.
  • gotamebhir: This word indicates the connection to the Gotama lineage, which is significant in the context of wisdom and knowledge. It suggests that the wisdom of the sages is invoked.
  • ṛtāvā: This term means "truthful" or "righteous." It emphasizes the importance of living in accordance with cosmic order and truth.
  • viprebhir: This refers to the learned or wise individuals, often the priests or sages who possess knowledge and understanding of the Vedic texts.
  • astoṣṭa: This word means "praise" or "worship." It signifies the act of honoring the divine through rituals and offerings.
  • jātavedāḥ: This term refers to Agni as the "knower of births," highlighting the role of fire in creation and transformation.
  • sa eṣu dyumnam pīpayat: This phrase suggests that Agni nourishes or enhances glory and brilliance. It indicates the transformative power of fire in elevating one's status or achievements.
  • sa vājaṁ: This means "he who brings strength." It signifies the ability of Agni to provide vigor and vitality.
  • sa puṣṭiṁ: This term refers to nourishment or abundance, indicating that Agni is a source of sustenance and prosperity.
  • yāti joṣam ā cikitvān: This phrase means "he who knows how to bring joy." It emphasizes the importance of understanding and utilizing the divine forces for happiness and fulfillment.

  • Significance: This mantra highlights the multifaceted nature of Agni as a divine force that embodies truth, wisdom, nourishment, and strength. It emphasizes the importance of honoring and invoking this energy in our lives.
  • Real-life Application: In modern life, this mantra can be applied in various ways. For instance, in personal development, one can draw inspiration from Agni's transformative power to overcome challenges and achieve goals. In community settings, rituals that honor Agni can foster unity and collective strength.
  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of energy, wisdom, and truth in our lives. By acknowledging and invoking these qualities, we can enhance our personal and communal well-being, leading to a more harmonious existence.
