सिञ्चन्ति नमसावतम् उच्चाचक्रं परिज्मानम् |नीचीनबारणम् अक्षितम् ||
siñcanti namasāvatam uccācakram parijmānam | nīcīnabāram akṣitam ||
  • siñcanti: This word translates to "they pour" or "they sprinkle." It signifies the act of offering or bestowing something valuable, often in a ritualistic context.
  • namasāvatam: This can be interpreted as "with reverence" or "with respect." It emphasizes the importance of humility and devotion in the act of offering.
  • uccācakram: This term means "the high wheel" or "the elevated circle." It symbolizes the cycle of life, the cosmos, or the divine order that governs existence.
  • parijmānam: This word refers to "encompassing" or "surrounding." It suggests a holistic approach, where one acknowledges the interconnectedness of all beings and elements in the universe.
  • nīcīnabāram: This can be translated as "the low burden" or "the weight of the low." It indicates the challenges or burdens that one may face in life, often representing the struggles of the material world.
  • akṣitam: This means "unbroken" or "imperishable." It signifies resilience, the enduring nature of the spirit, and the eternal truths that persist despite worldly challenges.

The mantra as a whole can be understood as a call to offer reverence and humility while acknowledging the higher cosmic order. It encourages individuals to recognize the burdens of life but also to understand that the spirit remains unbroken and eternal.


In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts:

  • Spiritual Practices: Individuals can recite this mantra during meditation or prayer, fostering a sense of connection to the universe and a reminder of the importance of humility.
  • Community Service: The act of "pouring" or "sprinkling" can be seen in acts of kindness and charity, where one offers help to those in need with reverence and respect.
  • Personal Challenges: When facing difficulties, this mantra serves as a reminder that while burdens may be low, the spirit is unbroken, encouraging resilience and perseverance.

In conclusion, this mantra encapsulates the essence of humility, reverence, and the recognition of the eternal spirit amidst life's challenges. It serves as a guiding principle for individuals seeking to navigate the complexities of existence with grace and strength.
