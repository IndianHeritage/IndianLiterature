जयेम कारे पुरुहूत कारीणोऽभि तिष्ठेम दूद्यः |नृभिर वृतं हन्यामा शूशुयाम चावर इन्द्र प्र णो धियः ||
jayema kāre puruhūta kāriṇo 'bhi tiṣṭhema dūḍhyaḥ | nṛbhir vṛtraṁ hanyāma śūśuyāma cāver indra pra ṇo dhiyaḥ ||
  • Mantra: "jayema kāre puruhūta kāriṇo 'bhi tiṣṭhema dūḍhyaḥ | nṛbhir vṛtraṁ hanyāma śūśuyāma cāver indra pra ṇo dhiyaḥ ||"
  • Translation: "May we be victorious in our endeavors, O Puruhūta (Indra), may we stand firm and strong. With the strength of men, we shall slay the Vritra (obstacles) and may we attain the highest wisdom."

  • Word Breakdown:
    • Jayema: We may be victorious.
    • Kāre: In our endeavors or actions.
    • Puruhūta: A name for Indra, the king of gods, often associated with strength and power.
    • Kāriṇo: Those who act or perform.
    • Abhi: Towards or in the direction of.
    • Tiṣṭhema: May we stand firm or strong.
    • Dūḍhyaḥ: Strong or powerful.
    • Nṛbhir: With the strength of men.
    • Vṛtraṁ: Vritra, a demon representing obstacles or challenges.
    • Hanyāma: We shall slay or overcome.
    • Śūśuyāma: May we attain or achieve.
    • Cāver: The highest or supreme.
    • Indra: The deity invoked for strength and victory.
    • Pra: Towards or in the direction of.
    • ṇo: Our.
    • Dhiyaḥ: Wisdom or intellect.

  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a competitive environment, such as a business setting, this mantra can inspire teams to work together towards a common goal, overcoming challenges (Vritra) with collective strength (Nṛbhir).
    • In personal development, individuals can use the essence of this mantra to motivate themselves to face their fears and obstacles, standing firm in their resolve to achieve their goals.
    • In sports, athletes often invoke a sense of unity and strength among teammates, akin to the invocation of Indra, to overcome their opponents and achieve victory.

  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for strength, unity, and the overcoming of obstacles. It emphasizes the importance of collective effort and the pursuit of wisdom in achieving success. In real-life scenarios, whether in personal challenges, professional endeavors, or communal efforts, this mantra serves as a reminder that with determination and collaboration, one can conquer difficulties and attain their aspirations. It encourages individuals and groups to harness their inner strength and work together towards a brighter future.
