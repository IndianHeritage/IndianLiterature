त्वं आविथ सुश्रवसं तवोतिभिस् तव त्रामभिर इन्द्र तूर्वयाणम् |त्वं अस्मै कुत्सम् अतिथिग्वम् आयुम् महे राज्ञे यूने अरन्धनायः ||
tvam āvitha suśravasaṁ tavotibhis tava trāmabhir indra tūrvayāṇam | tvam asmai kutsam atithigvam āyum mahe rājñe yūne arandhanāyaḥ ||
  • tvam āvitha suśravasaṁ: This phrase can be translated as "You, who are adorned with great fame." Here, "tvam" refers to the divine or a deity, and "suśravasaṁ" signifies someone who is renowned or has a good reputation. In real life, this can relate to individuals who are respected in their fields, such as leaders, scholars, or artists.
  • tavotibhis tava trāmabhir indra tūrvayāṇam: This part means "With your great powers and protections, O Indra, grant us strength." "Indra" is a prominent deity in the Rigveda, often associated with strength and protection. This can be likened to seeking support from a mentor or a leader in challenging times, where their guidance and strength can help one overcome obstacles.
  • tvam asmai kutsam atithigvam āyum: This translates to "You bestow upon him a long life and prosperity." The term "kutsam" refers to a long life, while "atithigvam" signifies prosperity or wealth. In practical terms, this can be seen in the context of wishing well for someone, such as a friend or family member, hoping they achieve success and longevity in their endeavors.
  • mahe rājñe yūne arandhanāyaḥ: This means "For the great king, may he be blessed with abundance." "Rājñe" refers to a king or leader, and "arandhanāyaḥ" implies a state of being free from want or need. This can be related to the idea of leadership in society, where a good leader is expected to provide for their people and ensure their well-being.

  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of divine support, strength, and prosperity in leadership and life. It reflects the human desire for protection and guidance from higher powers, as well as the aspiration for longevity and success. In real-life scenarios, this can be applicable in various contexts, such as seeking blessings for new ventures, wishing well for leaders, or invoking strength during challenging times. The significance lies in recognizing the interconnectedness of individual efforts and divine grace in achieving one's goals.
