स नो महाम् अनिमानो धूमकेतुः पुरुषचन्द्रः |धिये वाजाय हिन्वतु ||
sa no mahām̐ animāno dhūmaketuḥ puruścandraḥ | dhiye vājāya hinvatu ||
  • sa no: This phrase translates to "may he" or "let him." It indicates a prayer or a wish directed towards a divine entity.
  • mahām̐: This word means "great" or "mighty." It signifies the power and strength of the divine being being addressed.
  • animāno: This term can be interpreted as "the one who is unbounded" or "the one who is limitless." It reflects the infinite nature of the divine.
  • dhūmaketuḥ: This word means "the one with a smoky banner." In a metaphorical sense, it can represent the challenges and obstacles in life, akin to smoke that obscures vision.
  • puruścandraḥ: This translates to "the man who is like the moon." It symbolizes beauty, calmness, and the ability to illuminate darkness, representing wisdom and guidance.
  • dhiye: This word means "intellect" or "understanding." It emphasizes the importance of wisdom in navigating life's challenges.
  • vājāya: This term translates to "for the purpose of strength" or "for the purpose of victory." It indicates a desire for empowerment and success.
  • hinvatu: This means "may it inspire" or "may it lead." It is a call for guidance and support from the divine.

The mantra as a whole can be interpreted as a prayer for divine guidance and strength in overcoming obstacles and achieving success. It acknowledges the greatness and limitless nature of the divine while seeking wisdom and empowerment to navigate life's challenges.


In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts:

  • Personal Growth: Individuals can recite this mantra when facing personal challenges, seeking clarity and strength to overcome difficulties.
  • Professional Success: In a work environment, this mantra can be used to invoke guidance and wisdom when making important decisions or pursuing career goals.
  • Emotional Resilience: During times of emotional turmoil, reciting this mantra can provide comfort and a sense of direction, helping individuals to find their inner strength.

Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its ability to inspire individuals to seek divine guidance and strength in their lives. By acknowledging the challenges represented by "dhūmaketuḥ" and aspiring for the wisdom of "puruścandraḥ," individuals can cultivate resilience and clarity. This mantra serves as a reminder that, with the right mindset and divine support, one can navigate through life's complexities and emerge victorious.
