पुनर ये चक्रुः पितराः युवानाः सनाः यूपेव जरणा शयानाः |ते वाजो विभ्वाम् ऋभुर इन्द्रवन्तो मधुप्सरसः नोऽवन्तु यज्ञम् ||
punar ye cakruḥ pitarā yuvānā sanā yūpeva jaraṇā śayānā | te vājo vibhvām̐ ṛbhur indravanto madhupsaraso no 'vantu yajñam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • punar: again, anew
    • ye: those who
    • cakruḥ: have made, created
    • pitarā: by the ancestors, forefathers
    • yuvānā: youthful, vibrant
    • sanā: eternal, everlasting
    • yūpeva: like a sacrificial post
    • jaraṇā: old age, decay
    • śayānā: lying down, resting
    • te: they
    • vājo: strength, energy
    • vibhvām̐: may they be abundant
    • ṛbhur: the wise, the learned
    • indravanto: those who are like Indra, powerful
    • madhupsaraso: of the sweet waters, nectar
    • no: for us
    • 'vantu: may they come
    • yajñam: to the sacrifice, offering
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the renewal and creation by the ancestors, emphasizing the importance of their wisdom and vitality.
    • It draws a parallel between the ancestors and a sacrificial post, suggesting that their legacy supports the community.
    • The invocation of strength and abundance indicates a desire for prosperity and success in endeavors, particularly in spiritual or communal sacrifices.
    • The reference to "madhupsaraso" symbolizes the sweetness of life and the nourishment that comes from wisdom and strength.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In many cultures, honoring ancestors through rituals or festivals reflects the idea of drawing strength from their legacy, similar to how this mantra invokes the ancestors' vitality.
    • In modern contexts, community gatherings or family reunions can be seen as a way to celebrate the wisdom and contributions of previous generations, fostering a sense of unity and purpose.
    • In personal development, individuals often seek guidance from the teachings of their forebears, using their experiences to navigate challenges, akin to the wisdom invoked in this mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of ancestral wisdom and the continuous cycle of renewal and strength in life.
    • It serves as a reminder of the importance of honoring our roots while striving for growth and prosperity.
    • In real-life scenarios, it encourages individuals and communities to draw upon their heritage to foster resilience and success in their endeavors.
