सखायः सं वः सम्यञ्चम् इषं स्तोमं चाग्नये | वर्षिष्ठाय क्षितीनाम ऊर्जो नप्त्रे सहस्वते ||
sakhāyaḥ saṁ vaḥ samyañcam iṣaṁ stomaṁ cāgnaye | varṣiṣṭhāya kṣitīnām ūrjo naptre sahasvate ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sakhāyaḥ: Friends or companions
    • saṁ: Together
    • vaḥ: Your
    • samyañcam: Harmonious or well-ordered
    • iṣaṁ: Power or strength
    • stomaṁ: Praise or hymn
    • cāgnaye: To Agni (the fire god)
    • varṣiṣṭhāya: For the most abundant
    • kṣitīnām: Of the earth or land
    • ūrjo: Energy or vigor
    • naptre: To the descendant or progeny
    • sahasvate: Powerful or strong
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of friendship and unity among individuals.
    • It calls upon Agni, the fire god, to bless the community with strength and harmony.
    • The reference to "kṣitīnām" signifies the connection to the earth and the importance of nurturing it for future generations.
    • The term "ūrjo" highlights the need for energy and vigor in our endeavors, suggesting that we should approach life with enthusiasm and strength.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace, fostering a sense of camaraderie among colleagues can lead to a more productive and harmonious environment.
    • Community initiatives that focus on environmental sustainability reflect the essence of nurturing the earth for future generations.
    • Sports teams exemplify the power of unity and collective strength, where each member contributes to the overall success.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the significance of friendship, unity, and collective strength in achieving common goals.
    • It encourages individuals to work together harmoniously while recognizing the importance of nurturing the earth and its resources.
    • In real life, applying the principles of this mantra can lead to stronger communities, successful collaborations, and a more sustainable future.
