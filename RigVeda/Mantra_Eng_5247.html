स नो वेदो अमात्यम अग्नि रक्षतु विश्वतः |उतास्मान पातु अंहसः ||
sa no vedo amātyam agnī rakṣatu viśvataḥ | utāsmān pātv aṁhasaḥ ||
  • sa no vedo amātyam agnī rakṣatu viśvataḥ
    • sa: This word translates to "may he" or "let him." It indicates a wish or a prayer directed towards a divine entity.
    • no: This means "us" or "our," indicating a collective desire or need.
    • vedo: Refers to "knowledge" or "wisdom," particularly the sacred knowledge contained in the Vedas.
    • amātyam: This term means "counsel" or "advisors," referring to wise individuals or guides who provide support and direction.
    • agnī: This is the word for "fire," often symbolizing purity, transformation, and divine presence.
    • rakṣatu: This means "may protect" or "may safeguard," indicating a plea for protection.
    • viśvataḥ: This translates to "from all sides" or "in all directions," emphasizing the comprehensive nature of the protection sought.
  • utāsmān pātv aṁhasaḥ
    • utāsmān: This means "and may he" or "and let him," continuing the prayerful tone.
    • pātv: This translates to "may he grant" or "may he bestow," indicating a request for blessings.
    • aṁhasaḥ: This word means "light" or "radiance," often associated with knowledge, enlightenment, and positivity.

Conclusion:

This mantra is a prayer for divine protection and guidance. It seeks the safeguarding of knowledge and wisdom, as well as the illumination of one's path through the blessings of the divine. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as:

  • Education: Students can recite this mantra before exams or important learning sessions to seek clarity and guidance in their studies.
  • Decision Making: Individuals facing significant life choices can use this mantra to invoke wisdom and protection from negative influences.
  • Community Gatherings: During meetings or discussions, this mantra can be recited to foster a spirit of cooperation and collective wisdom among participants.

Overall, the significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine support in the pursuit of knowledge and the protection of one's endeavors, making it relevant in various aspects of life.
