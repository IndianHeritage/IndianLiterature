गृहमेधासा आगत मरुतो माप भूतन |युष्माकोटी सुदानवः ||
gṛhamedhāsa ā gata maruto māpa bhūtana | yuṣmākotī sudānavaḥ ||
  • gṛhamedhāsa: This term refers to the householders or those who live in a home. It signifies the importance of domestic life and the responsibilities that come with it.
  • ā gata: This phrase means "having come" or "arrived." It indicates a sense of presence or arrival, suggesting that something significant has been achieved or attained.
  • maruto: This word refers to the Maruts, the storm deities in Vedic literature. They symbolize strength, power, and the forces of nature, often associated with wind and storms.
  • māpa bhūtana: This can be interpreted as "do not let them be disturbed" or "do not let them be harmed." It conveys a sense of protection and care for the household and its inhabitants.
  • yuṣmākotī: This term means "your multitude" or "your group." It emphasizes community and the collective strength of people coming together.
  • sudānavaḥ: This translates to "good and prosperous." It signifies well-being, abundance, and the positive aspects of life that one wishes to attain.

  • Meaning: The mantra speaks to the importance of a harmonious household and the protection of its members. It acknowledges the arrival of the Maruts, who bring strength and vitality, while also emphasizing the need for safeguarding the well-being of the household and its community.
  • Real-life Example: In modern life, this mantra can be applied to the concept of family and community support. For instance, during challenging times, families often come together to provide emotional and financial support, ensuring that no member feels disturbed or harmed. Community gatherings, such as festivals or support groups, reflect the essence of "yuṣmākotī sudānavaḥ," where collective strength leads to prosperity and well-being.

  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for unity, protection, and the nurturing of domestic life. It serves as a reminder that a strong community and a harmonious household are essential for individual and collective prosperity. In real-life scenarios, it encourages individuals to foster supportive relationships, prioritize family well-being, and work together for the common good, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling and prosperous life.
