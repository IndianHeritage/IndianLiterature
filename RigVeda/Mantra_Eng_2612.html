कृधि रत्नं सुसंहित धनानां स गहेद अग्ने भवसि यत समिद्धः |स्तोतुर दुरोणे सुभगस्य रेवत सृप्रा करस्ना दधिषे वपूंषि ||
kṛdhi ratnaṁ susanitar dhanānāṁ sa ghed agne bhavasi yat samiddhaḥ | stotur duroṇe subhagasya revat sṛprā karasnā dadhiṣe vapūṁṣi ||
  • kṛdhi ratnaṁ: This phrase translates to "create wealth" or "bring forth riches." It emphasizes the importance of generating prosperity in life.
  • susanitar dhanānāṁ: This means "of those who are well-off" or "of the prosperous." It highlights the idea that wealth should be cultivated in a manner that is beneficial and ethical.
  • sa ghed agne: This translates to "may you, O Agni," referring to the fire god Agni, who symbolizes energy, transformation, and the divine spark in all endeavors.
  • bhavasi yat samiddhaḥ: This means "may you become that which is kindled." It suggests that through the act of creation and transformation, one can achieve greatness.
  • stotur duroṇe: This translates to "to be praised in the difficult times." It indicates that true wealth and prosperity are recognized and celebrated even in challenging circumstances.
  • subhagasya revat: This means "of the fortunate and prosperous." It emphasizes the idea that those who are blessed with good fortune should share their wealth and blessings with others.
  • sṛprā karasnā: This translates to "may you grant us strength." It signifies the need for divine support and strength in our endeavors to achieve prosperity.
  • dadhiṣe vapūṁṣi: This means "may you bestow upon us bodies." It suggests a request for vitality and health, which are essential for enjoying the fruits of one's labor.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda encapsulates the essence of prosperity, ethical wealth creation, and the importance of divine support in our endeavors. It teaches us that true wealth is not just material but also encompasses health, strength, and the ability to uplift others. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as in business practices that prioritize ethical standards, community support initiatives, and personal development efforts that seek to enhance one's capabilities while contributing positively to society. By invoking the blessings of Agni, we are reminded to pursue our goals with integrity and to share our successes with those around us, fostering a cycle of prosperity and well-being.
