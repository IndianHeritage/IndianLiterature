को वः स्तोमं राधति यं जुजोषथ विश्वे देवासो मनुषो यति ष्ठना |को वो 'ध्वरं तुविजाताऽरं करद यो नः पार्षद अत्य अंहः स्वस्तये ||
ko vaḥ stomaṁ rādhati yaṁ jujoṣatha viśve devāso manuṣo yati ṣṭhana | ko vo 'dhvaraṁ tuvijātā araṁ karad yo naḥ parṣad aty aṁhaḥ svastaye ||
  • Translation: The mantra can be translated as: "Who among you praises the hymn that you all sing? All the gods and men are present here. Who among you has the power to perform the sacrifice? May he who is our friend grant us prosperity."
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ko vaḥ: Who among you
    • stomaṁ: the hymn or praise
    • rādhati: praises or sings
    • yaṁ: that which
    • jujoṣatha: you all sing or chant
    • viśve: all
    • devāso: gods
    • manuṣo: men or humans
    • yati: who has the power
    • ṣṭhana: to perform or to act
    • ko vo: who among you
    • 'dhvaraṁ: the sacrifice or offering
    • tuvijātā: has the ability to perform
    • araṁ: to grant or bestow
    • karad: who is our friend
    • yo: who
    • naḥ: for us
    • parṣad: assembly or group
    • aty: very or exceedingly
    • aṁhaḥ: powerful or strong
    • svastaye: for prosperity or well-being
  • Contextual Significance:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of collective participation in rituals and the acknowledgment of divine presence.
    • It reflects the idea that both gods and humans are interconnected in the act of worship and sacrifice.
    • The call for someone to perform the sacrifice highlights the need for leadership and responsibility within a community.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern society, this mantra can be seen in community gatherings where collective efforts are made for a common cause, such as charity events or cultural festivals.
    • In workplaces, team projects require individuals to come together, each contributing their strengths to achieve a shared goal, similar to the collective worship in the mantra.
    • In family settings, the importance of unity during celebrations or rituals, where each member plays a role, mirrors the essence of this mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • The mantra serves as a reminder of the significance of community, collaboration, and the divine in our lives.
    • It encourages individuals to recognize their roles within a larger framework, whether in spiritual practices or everyday interactions.
    • Ultimately, it highlights the pursuit of prosperity and well-being through collective effort and divine connection.
