सोमो ददद गन्धर्वाय गन्धर्वो ददद अग्नये |रयिं च पुत्रांश चादाद अग्निर मह्यम अथो इमाम ||
somo dadad gandharvāya gandharvo dadad agnaye | rayiṁ ca putrām̐ś cādād agnir mahyam atho imām ||
  • Mantra: "somo dadad gandharvāya gandharvo dadad agnaye | rayiṁ ca putrām̐ś cādād agnir mahyam atho imām ||"
  • Translation: "May the Soma (the divine nectar) be given to the Gandharva; may the Gandharva be given to Agni (the fire god); may he bestow wealth and sons upon me; may Agni grant me these."

  • Word Breakdown:
    • Soma: Refers to a sacred drink in Vedic rituals, symbolizing immortality and divine inspiration.
    • Gandharva: Celestial beings associated with music and joy, often seen as guardians of the divine and the arts.
    • Agni: The fire god, representing energy, transformation, and the divine messenger between humans and gods.
    • Rayiṁ: Wealth or prosperity, often associated with material and spiritual abundance.
    • Putrām̐ś: Sons or progeny, symbolizing continuity, legacy, and family.

  • Significance:
    • This mantra emphasizes the interconnectedness of divine forces and their roles in human life.
    • It highlights the importance of seeking blessings from various deities for prosperity and well-being.
    • The invocation of Soma and Gandharva signifies the pursuit of joy and inspiration, while Agni represents the transformative power of fire in our lives.

  • Real-Life Application:
    • In modern life, this mantra can be seen as a reminder to seek inspiration and joy in our daily activities, much like how artists and musicians draw from divine sources.
    • It encourages individuals to cultivate relationships that bring prosperity and happiness, akin to how families and communities support one another.
    • The invocation of Agni can be related to the transformative processes in our lives, such as personal growth, education, and career development.

  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the need for divine blessings in our pursuit of wealth, joy, and legacy.
    • It encourages us to recognize the importance of spiritual and material abundance in our lives.
    • By invoking these divine energies, we align ourselves with the greater forces of the universe, fostering a life of fulfillment and purpose.
