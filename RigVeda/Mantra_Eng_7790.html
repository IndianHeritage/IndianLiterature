बभ्रवे नु स्वतवसे 'रुणाय दिविष्पृशे |सोमाय गाथम् अर्चत ||
babhrave nu svatavase 'ruṇāya divispṛśe | somāya gātham arcata ||
  • babhrave: This word can be interpreted as "to the one who is powerful" or "to the one who is strong." It signifies strength and vitality.
  • nu: This is an expression of emphasis, often translated as "indeed" or "certainly." It adds a sense of affirmation to the mantra.
  • svatavase: This term refers to "in one's own power" or "by one's own strength." It emphasizes self-reliance and personal capability.
  • 'ruṇāya: This word can be understood as "for the one who is in need" or "for the one who is seeking." It indicates a call for assistance or support.
  • divispṛśe: This translates to "to the one who touches the heavens" or "to the celestial." It refers to a divine or higher power, often associated with the gods.
  • somāya: This word relates to "Soma," which is a sacred drink in Vedic tradition, symbolizing immortality and divine inspiration.
  • gātham: This means "song" or "hymn," indicating a form of praise or worship.
  • arcata: This translates to "worshipped" or "offered," suggesting an act of devotion or reverence.

The mantra can be interpreted as a call to the powerful divine forces, emphasizing the importance of self-reliance while also acknowledging the need for support from higher powers. It reflects a balance between personal strength and the recognition of divine assistance.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios:

  • In personal development, individuals can draw strength from within while also seeking guidance from mentors or spiritual leaders.
  • In challenging situations, such as during a crisis or a difficult decision, one can rely on their own capabilities while praying or seeking blessings from a higher power.
  • In community settings, this mantra can inspire collective strength, where individuals support each other while also invoking divine blessings for their endeavors.

Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its dual emphasis on personal strength and the need for divine support. It teaches us that while we must strive to harness our own abilities, it is equally important to seek help from the universe or higher powers when needed. This balance is crucial in navigating life's challenges and achieving our goals.
