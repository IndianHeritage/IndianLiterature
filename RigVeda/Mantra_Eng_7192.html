त्वं नो अस्यां अमतेर उता क्षुधोऽभिषक्तेर अव स्पृधि |त्वं न ऊती तव चित्रया धियā शिक्षā शचिष्ठ गातुवित ||
tvaṁ no asyā amater uta kṣudho3 'bhiśaster ava spṛdhi | tvaṁ na ūtī tava citrayā dhiyā śikṣā śaciṣṭha gātuvit ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • tvaṁ: You
    • no: for us
    • asyā: of this
    • amater: of the mother
    • uta: and
    • kṣudho: hunger
    • ‘bhiśaster: of the one who is powerful
    • ava: down
    • spṛdhi: touch
    • tvaṁ: You
    • na: not
    • ūtī: the strength
    • tava: your
    • citrayā: with variety
    • dhiyā: with intellect
    • śikṣā: instruction
    • śaciṣṭha: the most powerful
    • gātuvit: the one who knows how to sing
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer seeking the blessings of a higher power, often interpreted as a divine force or deity.
    • The phrase "tvaṁ no asyā amater" indicates a request for guidance and support from the divine mother, symbolizing nurturing and protection.
    • "Kṣudho 'bhiśaster ava spṛdhi" reflects a plea for relief from hunger and suffering, emphasizing the need for sustenance and well-being.
    • The latter part, "tvaṁ na ūtī tava citrayā dhiyā," suggests a desire for diverse knowledge and wisdom, highlighting the importance of learning and understanding in life.
    • Finally, "śikṣā śaciṣṭha gātuvit" points to the significance of instruction and the power of knowledge, indicating that true strength lies in wisdom and the ability to communicate effectively.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In times of personal crisis, individuals often seek guidance from mentors or spiritual leaders, akin to invoking the divine mother for support.
    • Communities facing hunger and poverty can relate to the plea for sustenance, prompting collective efforts to provide food and resources to those in need.
    • The emphasis on diverse knowledge can be seen in educational institutions that promote interdisciplinary learning, preparing students to tackle complex global issues.
    • In professional settings, effective communication and the sharing of knowledge are crucial for teamwork and innovation, reflecting the mantra's call for wisdom and instruction.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking divine guidance, nourishment, and wisdom in our lives.
    • Its significance lies in the recognition of the interconnectedness of spiritual, physical, and intellectual sustenance.
    • In real-life scenarios, it serves as a reminder to seek support from higher powers, to care for the needy, and to value education and communication as pillars of strength.
    • Ultimately, it encourages individuals and communities to strive for holistic well-being through nurturing relationships, knowledge, and compassion.
