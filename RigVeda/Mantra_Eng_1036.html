यो अग्निषोमा हविषा सपर्ण्याद देवदृचां मनसा यो घृतेन |तस्य व्रतं रक्षतं पातं अम्हसो विशे जनाय महि शर्मा यच्छतं ||
yo agnīṣomā haviṣā saparyād devadrīcā manasā yo ghṛtena | tasya vrataṁ rakṣatam pātam aṁhaso viśe janāya mahi śarma yacchatam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yo: who
    • agnīṣomā: of Agni and Soma (representing fire and the moon)
    • haviṣā: with offerings
    • saparyād: with worship
    • devadrīcā: with divine insight
    • manasā: with the mind
    • yo: who
    • ghṛtena: with ghee (clarified butter)
    • tasya: of that (deity)
    • vrataṁ: vow or promise
    • rakṣatam: may it protect
    • pātam: may it grant
    • aṁhaso: of strength
    • viśe: to the community
    • janāya: for the people
    • mahi: great
    • śarma: peace or happiness
    • yacchatam: may it bestow
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the divine energies of Agni (fire) and Soma (moon), symbolizing the balance of energy and nourishment.
    • It emphasizes the importance of offerings and worship, suggesting that a sincere mind and heart are essential in spiritual practices.
    • The mantra seeks protection and blessings from the divine, highlighting the need for strength and peace in the community.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community gathering, people often come together to share food and resources, symbolizing the offerings made to Agni and Soma, fostering unity and strength.
    • During festivals, rituals are performed with ghee and other offerings, reflecting the essence of this mantra in seeking divine blessings for peace and prosperity.
    • In personal life, individuals may set vows or intentions (vrata) for personal growth, seeking protection and strength through meditation and mindfulness.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of divine connection through offerings and worship, emphasizing community strength and individual peace.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from communal rituals to personal intentions, reminding us of the balance between spiritual and material aspects of life.
    • By invoking these energies, individuals and communities can seek protection, strength, and happiness, fostering a harmonious existence.
