अभि यं देव्य अदितिः गृणाति सवं देवस्य सवितुः जुषाणाः |अभि सम्राजो वरुणो गृणन्त्य अभि मित्रासो आर्यमाः सजोषाः ||
abhi yaṁ devy aditir gṛṇāti savaṁ devasya savitur juṣāṇā | abhi samrājo varuṇo gṛṇanty abhi mitrāso aryamā sajoṣāḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • abhi: towards
    • yaṁ: whom
    • devy: the divine
    • aditir: Aditi, the mother of gods
    • gṛṇāti: sings or praises
    • savaṁ: all or entire
    • devasya: of the divine
    • savitur: of the sun
    • juṣāṇā: who is enjoyed or revered
  • Second Line Breakdown:
    • abhi: towards
    • samrājo: the great ruler
    • varuṇo: Varuna, the god of cosmic order
    • gṛṇanty: sings or praises
    • abhi: towards
    • mitrāso: of the friends
    • aryamā: the god of friendship and harmony
    • sajoṣāḥ: who is joyful or blissful
  • Overall Meaning:
    • This mantra invokes the divine presence of Aditi, the mother of all gods, and praises the sun as a source of life and energy.
    • It emphasizes the importance of cosmic order and harmony, represented by Varuna and Aryama, who are seen as protectors and friends.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, this mantra can be a reminder to seek harmony and balance in our relationships, much like the friendship represented by Aryama.
    • It encourages us to appreciate the natural world, particularly the sun, which provides light and sustenance, similar to how we rely on the sun for energy in our daily activities.
    • In times of conflict or disorder, invoking the principles of Varuna can guide us towards maintaining ethical conduct and cosmic order in our actions.
  • Conclusion and Significance:
    • This mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the interconnectedness of all beings and the divine forces that govern our lives.
    • It highlights the importance of reverence for nature and the divine, encouraging us to live in harmony with ourselves and others.
    • In practical terms, it can be used in meditation or prayer to foster a sense of peace, unity, and respect for the cosmic order, applicable in both personal and communal settings.
