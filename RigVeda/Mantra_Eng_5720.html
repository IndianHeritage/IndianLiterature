नरā गौरेव विद्युतं तृषाणास्माकम अद्य सवनोप यातम् |पुरुत्रा हि वाम मतिभिर हवन्ते मा वाम अन्ये नि यमन देवयन्तः ||
narā gaureva vidyutaṁ tṛṣāṇāsmākam adya savanopa yātam | purutrā hi vām matibhir havante mā vām anye ni yaman devayantaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • narā: men or humans
    • gaureva: like the white (or bright) one
    • vidyutaṁ: lightning or brilliance
    • tṛṣāṇāsmākam: our thirst or desire
    • adya: today
    • savanopa: to the offering or sacrifice
    • yātam: may it go or be directed
    • purutrā: from all directions
    • hi: indeed
    • vām: your
    • matibhir: with our thoughts or intentions
    • havante: may they offer or invoke
    • mā: not
    • vām: your
    • anye: others
    • ni yaman: may they not control or dominate
    • devayantaḥ: the divine beings or gods
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra expresses a plea for divine guidance and support.
    • The reference to "lightning" symbolizes clarity, enlightenment, and the power of knowledge.
    • The phrase "our thirst" indicates a deep yearning for spiritual fulfillment and understanding.
    • It emphasizes the importance of collective intention and thought in invoking divine presence.
    • The caution against being controlled by others highlights the need for personal agency and autonomy in spiritual matters.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, teams often gather to brainstorm and share ideas, invoking a collective spirit to achieve a common goal, much like the invocation in the mantra.
    • During personal challenges, individuals may seek guidance through meditation or prayer, reflecting the desire for clarity and support from a higher power.
    • In community gatherings, people come together to celebrate and support one another, emphasizing the importance of unity and shared intentions.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the power of collective thought and intention in seeking divine guidance.
    • It encourages individuals to pursue their spiritual desires while maintaining their autonomy and not being swayed by external influences.
    • In real life, it can be applied in various contexts, such as teamwork, personal growth, and community support, highlighting the significance of unity and clarity in our pursuits.
