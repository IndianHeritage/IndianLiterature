उद īर्ष्व नारी अभि जीवलोकं गतासुं एतम उप शेष एहि |हस्तग्राभस्य दिधिषोस तवेदम पत्युर जनित्वं अभि सम बभूथा ||
ud īrṣva nāry abhi jīvalokaṁ gatāsum etam upa śeṣa ehi | hastagrābhasya didhiṣos tavedam patyur janitvam abhi sam babhūtha ||
  • ud īrṣva: This phrase can be translated as "rise up" or "elevate." It signifies the importance of awakening or uplifting oneself, both spiritually and materially.
  • nāry: This word refers to "women" or "goddesses." In the context of the mantra, it emphasizes the role of women in society and their significance in the spiritual and familial realms.
  • abhi jīvalokaṁ: This translates to "towards the world of life." It suggests a focus on living a life that is vibrant and full of purpose, engaging with the world around us.
  • gatāsum: This means "having gone" or "having reached." It indicates a journey or a transition, possibly referring to the journey of life or spiritual evolution.
  • etam upa śeṣa ehi: This can be interpreted as "come here to this remaining part." It suggests a call to gather or unite, emphasizing community and togetherness.
  • hastagrābhasya: This term refers to "the one who holds in hand." It symbolizes control, power, and the ability to manifest one's desires through action.
  • didhiṣos: This word means "to desire" or "to wish for." It reflects the aspirations and intentions that drive individuals in their pursuits.
  • tavedam: This translates to "this is your." It personalizes the message, indicating ownership and responsibility for one's actions and desires.
  • patyur janitvam: This phrase means "the birth of a husband." It highlights the importance of relationships and the roles individuals play within them.
  • abhi sam babhūtha: This can be interpreted as "has become." It signifies transformation and the realization of potential.

Conclusion: This mantra emphasizes the importance of rising to one's potential, recognizing the roles of women and community, and taking responsibility for one's actions and desires. In real life, it can be applied in various scenarios such as personal development, fostering relationships, and engaging with the community. By uplifting ourselves and those around us, we can create a more harmonious and purposeful existence. The mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of life and the significance of our choices in shaping our destinies.
