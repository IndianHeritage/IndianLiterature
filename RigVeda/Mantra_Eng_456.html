अस्ति हि ष्मा मदाया वः स्मसि ष्मा वयम् एषाम् |विश्वं चिद् आयुर जीवसे ||
asti hi ṣmā madāya vaḥ smasi ṣmā vayam eṣām | viśvaṁ cid āyur jīvase ||
  • Translation: "Indeed, we are here for the sake of joy; we are here for the sake of life. May we all live together in harmony."

  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • asti hi: Indeed, there is
    • ṣmā: joy or happiness
    • madāya: for the sake of
    • vaḥ: you (plural)
    • smasi: we are here
    • vayam: we
    • eṣām: of these (people)
    • viśvaṁ: all or entire
    • cid: consciousness or awareness
    • āyur: life or longevity
    • jīvase: may you live

  • Explanation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of joy and life in our existence. It suggests that our purpose is to seek happiness and to live harmoniously with others.
    • The phrase "for the sake of joy" indicates that joy is a fundamental aspect of life, and it is something we should strive for collectively.
    • The call for living together in harmony reflects the idea of community and interconnectedness, which is essential in various aspects of life, such as family, friendships, and society.

  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace, fostering a joyful environment can lead to increased productivity and employee satisfaction. Team-building activities and open communication can enhance this joy.
    • In communities, organizing events that celebrate cultural diversity can promote harmony and understanding among different groups, leading to a more peaceful coexistence.
    • In personal relationships, prioritizing joy through shared experiences, such as family outings or celebrations, strengthens bonds and creates lasting memories.

  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the significance of joy and harmony in our lives. It encourages us to seek happiness not just for ourselves but for the collective well-being of those around us.
    • In real-life scenarios, applying the principles of this mantra can lead to healthier relationships, more cohesive communities, and a fulfilling life overall.
    • Ultimately, it highlights the interconnectedness of all beings and the shared journey towards a joyful existence.
