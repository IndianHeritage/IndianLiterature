आ ये विश्वा स्वपत्यानि तस्थुः कृत्वानासो अमृतत्वाय गातुम् |महना महद्भिः पृथिवी वि तस्थे माता पुत्रैर अदितिः धायसे वेः ||
ā ye viśvā svapatyāni tasthuḥ kṛṇvānāso amṛtatvāya gātum | mahnā mahadbhiḥ pṛthivī vi tasthe mātā putrair aditir dhāyase veḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā ye: Those who
    • viśvā: all, universal
    • svapatyāni: who are in harmony with their own nature
    • tasthuḥ: may they stand firm
    • kṛṇvānāso: creating, making
    • amṛtatvāya: for immortality
    • gātum: to attain
    • mahnā: by great
    • mahadbhiḥ: by the mighty
    • pṛthivī: earth
    • vi: in, with
    • tasthe: may it stand
    • mātā: mother
    • putrair: by sons
    • aditir: the infinite, boundless
    • dhāyase: you support
    • veḥ: strength, power
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks to the universal harmony and the strength that comes from being in alignment with one's true nature.
    • It emphasizes the importance of creating a firm foundation for life, aiming for immortality, not just in a physical sense but in the legacy we leave behind.
    • The mention of "mother" and "sons" signifies the nurturing aspect of life and the responsibilities that come with familial bonds.
    • The term "Aditi" represents the infinite and the boundless, suggesting that true strength and support come from a connection to the greater universe.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a family setting, this mantra can be applied to the relationships between parents and children, where nurturing and support are essential for growth and development.
    • In a professional environment, it can be seen in teamwork, where individuals work together harmoniously to achieve a common goal, creating a strong foundation for success.
    • In personal development, striving for immortality can be interpreted as leaving a lasting impact through one's actions, values, and contributions to society.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of harmony, strength, and the nurturing aspects of life.
    • It encourages individuals to create a solid foundation in their lives, aiming for a legacy that transcends time.
    • In real-life scenarios, it serves as a reminder of the importance of relationships, teamwork, and the pursuit of a meaningful existence.
