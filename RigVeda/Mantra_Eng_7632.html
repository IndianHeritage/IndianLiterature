विष्वेत ता ते सवनेषु प्रावाच्य या चकर्थ मगवन्न इन्द्र सुन्वते |पारावतं यत पुरुषम्भृतं वस्व अपावृणोः शरभाय ऋषिबन्धवे ||
viśvet tā te savaneṣu pravācyā yā cakartha maghavann indra sunvate | pārāvataṁ yat purusambhṛtaṁ vasv apāvṛṇoḥ śarabhāya ṛṣibandhave ||
  • viśvet tā te savaneṣu pravācyā: This phrase can be translated as "All those who are to be invoked in your sacrifices." It emphasizes the importance of inclusivity in spiritual practices, suggesting that all deities and forces should be acknowledged and invited during rituals.
  • yā cakartha maghavann indra sunvate: This part means "Those who are invoked by the powerful Indra." Indra, the king of gods in Vedic tradition, represents strength and power. This line highlights the role of divine intervention and the importance of seeking blessings from higher powers in our endeavors.
  • pārāvataṁ yat purusambhṛtaṁ: This translates to "The strength that is held by the great beings." It signifies the collective strength and wisdom of humanity and the universe, suggesting that we are all interconnected and can draw upon this shared power.
  • vasv apāvṛṇoḥ śarabhāya ṛṣibandhave: This can be interpreted as "May the blessings of the sages and the divine protect us." It emphasizes the need for guidance and protection from wise beings, both human and divine, in our lives.

  • Significance: This mantra encapsulates the essence of community, divine support, and the interconnectedness of all beings. It serves as a reminder that in our spiritual and daily lives, we should seek the blessings of all, acknowledge the power of the divine, and rely on the wisdom of those who have come before us.
  • Real-life Application: In practical terms, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios such as:
    • Community Gatherings: When organizing events or rituals, it is essential to include everyone and acknowledge their contributions, fostering a sense of unity.
    • Seeking Guidance: In times of uncertainty, individuals can reflect on the wisdom of mentors, elders, or spiritual leaders, drawing strength from their experiences.
    • Rituals and Prayers: Incorporating this mantra into personal prayers or group rituals can enhance the sense of connection to the divine and the community.

  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the importance of inclusivity, divine support, and the wisdom of the collective. By embracing these principles, individuals can navigate life's challenges with greater strength and purpose, fostering a harmonious existence within their communities and with the divine.
