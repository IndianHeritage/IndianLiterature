अग्निमच्छा देवयताम् मनांसि चक्षूषीव सूर्ये सं चरन्ति |यद ईं सुवाते उषसा विरूपे श्वेतो वाजी जायते अग्रे अह्नाम् ||
agnim acchā devayatām manāṁsi cakṣūṁṣīva sūrye saṁ caranti | yad īṁ suvāte uṣasā virūpe śveto vājī jāyate agre ahnām ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • agnim: Fire
    • acchā: Good or auspicious
    • devayatām: To the divine
    • manāṁsi: Minds or thoughts
    • cakṣūṁṣīva: Like the eyes
    • sūrye: In the sun
    • saṁ caranti: Move or travel
    • yad: That which
    • īṁ: Good or auspicious
    • suvāte: With good winds
    • uṣasā: Dawn
    • virūpe: Beautiful or well-formed
    • śveto: White
    • vājī: Strong or powerful
    • jāyate: Is born or arises
    • agre: In front or ahead
    • ahnām: Of the days
  • Detailed Explanation:
    • This mantra invokes the essence of fire, symbolizing purity, transformation, and energy.
    • The phrase "agnim acchā" suggests that the fire is auspicious and beneficial, representing the divine presence in our lives.
    • “Devayatām manāṁsi” indicates that our thoughts should be directed towards the divine, emphasizing the importance of spiritual focus.
    • “Cakṣūṁṣīva sūrye saṁ caranti” compares our thoughts to the eyes that move in the light of the sun, highlighting clarity and enlightenment.
    • The latter part of the mantra speaks of the dawn (“uṣasā”) and the beauty of new beginnings, symbolized by the “virūpe” and “śveto” (beautiful and white).
    • “Vājī jāyate” signifies the emergence of strength and power, suggesting that with each new day, we are given the opportunity to harness our inner strength.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, this mantra can be reflected in the practice of meditation, where one focuses their thoughts towards positivity and divinity, akin to how fire purifies.
    • During challenging times, invoking the essence of this mantra can inspire individuals to seek clarity and strength, much like the dawn brings light after darkness.
    • In a professional setting, the mantra encourages teamwork and collaboration, where collective thoughts and efforts can lead to successful outcomes, similar to how the sun nurtures life.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of directing our thoughts towards the divine and embracing the transformative power of fire.
    • It serves as a reminder that each day is a new opportunity to cultivate strength, clarity, and positivity in our lives.
    • By applying the teachings of this mantra, individuals can enhance their spiritual journey and foster a deeper connection with themselves and the universe.
