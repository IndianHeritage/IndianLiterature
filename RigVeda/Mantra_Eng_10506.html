पतङ्गो वाचं मनसा बिभर्ति तां गन्धर्वोऽवदद गर्भे अन्तः |तां द्योतमानां स्वर्यम मनिषां ऋतस्य पदे कवयो न पान्ति ||
pataṁgo vācam manasā bibharti tāṁ gandharvo 'vadad garbhe antaḥ | tāṁ dyotamānāṁ svaryam manīṣām ṛtasya pade kavayo ni pānti ||
  • pataṁgo vācam manasā bibharti: This phrase can be translated as "the bird carries the speech in its mind." Here, the "bird" symbolizes freedom and the ability to express thoughts and ideas. In real life, this can be likened to how individuals often have thoughts and ideas that they wish to communicate, but may struggle to find the right words or the courage to express them.
  • tāṁ gandharvo 'vadad garbhe antaḥ: This translates to "the celestial being speaks of her from within." The "gandharva" represents a divine musician or a celestial being, suggesting that there is a deeper, often unexpressed wisdom or creativity within us. In practical terms, this can relate to moments of inspiration that come from within, such as when an artist suddenly finds the perfect way to express their feelings through their work.
  • tāṁ dyotamānāṁ svaryam manīṣām: This means "that which shines forth, the sound of thought." It emphasizes the clarity and brilliance of ideas when they are fully formed and expressed. In everyday life, this can be seen in situations where a person articulates a complex idea clearly, leading to a breakthrough in understanding or collaboration.
  • ṛtasya pade kavayo ni pānti: This translates to "the wise protect the path of truth." It highlights the importance of wisdom and integrity in guiding one's actions and decisions. In real life, this can be observed in leaders or mentors who uphold ethical standards and inspire others to follow a righteous path, ensuring that truth prevails in their communities.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of inner wisdom, the power of expression, and the pursuit of truth. It teaches us that our thoughts and ideas, when nurtured and expressed, can lead to profound insights and creativity. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various fields such as art, leadership, and personal development, encouraging individuals to seek their inner voice, communicate effectively, and uphold truth in their actions. By embodying these principles, we can contribute positively to our surroundings and inspire others to do the same.
