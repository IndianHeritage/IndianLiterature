सखायस त्वा ववृमहे देवम् मर्तास ऊतये |अपां नपातं शुभगं सुदीदितिं सुप्रतूर्तिम अनहसम् ||
sakhāyas tvā vavṛmahe devam martāsa ūtaye | apāṁ napātaṁ subhagaṁ sudīditiṁ supratūrtim anehasam ||
  • Mantra: "sakhāyas tvā vavṛmahe devam martāsa ūtaye | apāṁ napātaṁ subhagaṁ sudīditiṁ supratūrtim anehasam ||"
  • Translation: "We invoke you, O divine one, as friends, for the benefit of mortals. We seek the auspicious flow of water, the radiant light, and the abundant blessings that come from the heavens."

  • Word Breakdown:
    • sakhāyas: Friends or companions, indicating a sense of community and support.
    • tvā: You, referring to the divine or a higher power.
    • vavṛmahe: We invoke or we call upon, suggesting a plea or request for assistance.
    • devam: Divine one, representing a god or celestial being.
    • martāsa: Mortals, highlighting the human condition and our reliance on the divine.
    • ūtaye: For benefit or welfare, indicating the purpose of the invocation.
    • apāṁ: Of water, emphasizing the importance of water in life.
    • napātaṁ: Flow or stream, referring to the movement of water.
    • subhagaṁ: Auspicious or fortunate, suggesting positive outcomes.
    • sudīditiṁ: Radiant light, symbolizing knowledge and enlightenment.
    • supratūrtim: Abundant blessings, indicating prosperity and well-being.
    • anehasam: From the heavens, connecting the divine with earthly existence.

  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community setting, this mantra can be recited during gatherings to invoke unity and support among members, fostering a sense of friendship and collaboration.
    • In environmental contexts, the emphasis on water can be related to initiatives aimed at conserving water resources, highlighting the importance of clean water for all living beings.
    • During personal challenges, individuals may recite this mantra as a form of meditation or prayer, seeking divine guidance and blessings for clarity and abundance in their lives.

  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine assistance and the recognition of the interconnectedness of all beings. It emphasizes the importance of community, the vital role of water, and the pursuit of enlightenment and prosperity. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as community building, environmental conservation, and personal growth, reminding us of our reliance on the divine and the blessings that flow from it.
