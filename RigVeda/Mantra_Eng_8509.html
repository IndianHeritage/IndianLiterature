वृषा वृष्णे रोर्वद अंशुर अस्मै पवमानो रुशद ईर्ते पयो गोः | सहस्रं ऋक्वा पथिभिर वचोविद अध्वस्मभिः सूरो अण्वं वि याति ||
vṛṣā vṛṣṇe roruvad aṁśur asmai pavamāno ruśad īrte payo goḥ | sahasram ṛkvā pathibhir vacovid adhvasmabhiḥ sūro aṇvaṁ vi yāti ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • vṛṣā: Strong, powerful
    • vṛṣṇe: To Vishnu or the divine
    • roruvad: Roaring, making a sound
    • aṁśur: Ray, light
    • asmai: To him, to that being
    • pavamāno: Purifying, sacred
    • ruśad: Shining, bright
    • īrte: In the waters, in the flow
    • payo: Milk, nourishment
    • goḥ: Of the cow, symbolizing abundance
    • sahasram: Thousand, multitude
    • ṛkvā: With hymns, praises
    • pathibhir: By the paths, ways
    • vacovid: With speech, words
    • adhvasmabhiḥ: By our journey, our path
    • sūro: Hero, strong one
    • aṇvaṁ: To the small, the humble
    • vi yāti: Goes forth, moves ahead
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the divine power, represented by Vishnu, to bestow strength and purity.
    • The imagery of roaring and shining suggests a powerful force that is both protective and nurturing.
    • The mention of milk and cows symbolizes abundance and nourishment, essential for life.
    • The reference to a thousand hymns indicates the collective strength of community and shared beliefs.
    • It emphasizes the importance of words and communication in guiding our paths and journeys.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community setting, this mantra can be seen as a call for unity and strength among its members, much like how a team works together to achieve a common goal.
    • In personal development, the idea of purifying oneself and seeking nourishment can relate to the importance of mental and physical health.
    • In leadership, the emphasis on communication and guidance reflects how effective leaders inspire and direct their teams.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of divine strength, community, and communication in our lives.
    • It teaches us to seek purity and nourishment, both physically and spiritually, while also emphasizing the power of collective effort.
    • In real-life scenarios, it can be applied in personal growth, community building, and effective leadership, reminding us of the interconnectedness of our journeys.
