ब्रह्म प्रजावद आ भर जातवेदो विचर्षणे |अग्नि यद दीदायद दिवि ||
brahma prajāvad ā bhara jātavedo vicarṣaṇe | agne yad dīdayad divi ||
  • Mantra: "brahma prajāvad ā bhara jātavedo vicarṣaṇe | agne yad dīdayad divi ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • Brahma: The ultimate reality or cosmic spirit.
    • Prajāvad: One who brings forth or creates beings.
    • Ā bhara: May you bring forth or manifest.
    • Jātavedo: The one who knows all births; often refers to Agni, the fire god.
    • Vicarṣaṇe: In the act of moving or wandering.
    • Agne: O Agni, the divine fire.
    • Yad: Whatever or that which.
    • Dīdayad: Shines or illuminates.
    • Divi: In the heavens or celestial realm.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes Agni, the fire god, who is seen as a mediator between the divine and human realms.
    • It emphasizes the creation and manifestation of life, suggesting that through the divine fire, new beings and ideas can emerge.
    • The phrase "may you bring forth" indicates a desire for abundance and prosperity, both materially and spiritually.
    • The reference to illumination in the heavens signifies the pursuit of knowledge, enlightenment, and higher understanding.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a personal context, this mantra can be applied when starting a new venture or project, invoking the energy of creation and illumination to guide the process.
    • In community settings, it can be used during rituals or gatherings to seek blessings for collective growth and prosperity.
    • In educational pursuits, it serves as a reminder to seek knowledge and enlightenment, encouraging individuals to strive for wisdom and understanding.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of Agni as a source of creation and illumination, symbolizing the transformative power of fire in both physical and spiritual realms.
    • Its application in real life encourages individuals and communities to embrace creativity, seek knowledge, and foster growth.
    • By invoking this mantra, one can align themselves with the cosmic forces of creation, leading to a more fulfilled and enlightened existence.
