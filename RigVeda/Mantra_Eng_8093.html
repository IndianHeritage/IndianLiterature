ये ते पवित्रं ऊर्मयोऽभिक्षरन्ति धारया |तेभिर नः सोम मृळय ||
ye te pavitram ūrmayo 'bhikṣaranti dhārayā | tebhir naḥ soma mṛḻaya ||
  • Translation: "Those who, with pure waves, offer us their support; may they, through their essence, grant us the nectar of Soma."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • ye: Those who
    • te: You (referring to the divine or celestial beings)
    • pavitram: Pure, sacred
    • ūrmayo: Waves, often symbolizing the flow of life or energy
    • 'bhikṣaranti: Offer, provide
    • dhārayā: Support, sustain
    • tebhir: By them
    • naḥ: Us
    • soma: Nectar, often associated with immortality and divine bliss
    • mṛḻaya: Grant, bestow
  • Contextual Meaning:
    • This mantra invokes the divine forces that provide purity and sustenance in our lives.
    • The "pure waves" can be interpreted as the positive energies or influences that surround us, akin to the support we receive from nature, family, and community.
    • The reference to "Soma" symbolizes the quest for spiritual nourishment and enlightenment, much like how we seek knowledge and experiences that enrich our lives.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community setting, the support from friends and family during challenging times can be seen as the "pure waves" that help us navigate through difficulties.
    • In a professional environment, mentorship can be viewed as a form of sustenance that guides individuals towards achieving their goals, similar to how the mantra seeks divine support.
    • Practices such as meditation and yoga can be seen as ways to connect with the "pure waves" of energy, promoting mental clarity and spiritual growth, akin to receiving the nectar of Soma.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking and acknowledging the pure influences in our lives that support our growth and well-being.
    • It serves as a reminder to cultivate positive relationships and environments that nourish our spirit and intellect.
    • In real life, applying the essence of this mantra can lead to a more fulfilling existence, where we actively seek out and appreciate the sources of purity and support around us.
