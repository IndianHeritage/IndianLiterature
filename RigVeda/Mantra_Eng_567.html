मन्त्र: सह वामेन न उषो व्य उच्छा दुतितर दिवः | सह द्युम्नेन बृहता विभावरि राया देवी दास्वती ||
saha vāmena na uṣo vy ucchā duhitar divaḥ | saha dyumnena bṛhatā vibhāvari rāyā devi dāsvatī ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • saha: together
    • vāmena: with the auspicious
    • na: not
    • uṣo: of the dawn
    • vy: you (plural)
    • ucchā: raised or elevated
    • duhitar: daughter
    • divaḥ: of the sky
    • saha: together
    • dyumnena: with glory
    • bṛhatā: with greatness
    • vibhāvari: illuminating
    • rāyā: wealth
    • devi: goddess
    • dāsvatī: bestowing
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the divine presence, asking for the blessings of the goddess who brings light and prosperity.
    • The phrase "saha vāmena" emphasizes the importance of unity and auspiciousness in our endeavors.
    • “uṣo vy ucchā” suggests the elevation of our spirits and aspirations, akin to the rising sun that dispels darkness.
    • “duhitar divaḥ” refers to the daughter of the sky, symbolizing the nurturing aspect of nature and the divine.
    • “saha dyumnena bṛhatā” highlights the need for collective glory and greatness, which can be seen in teamwork and collaboration in various fields.
    • “vibhāvari rāyā devi dāsvatī” calls upon the goddess to bestow wealth and illumination, akin to seeking guidance and support in our personal and professional lives.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, this mantra can be applied to foster teamwork, where individuals come together to achieve a common goal, much like the collective effort needed to launch a successful project.
    • In personal relationships, it emphasizes the importance of mutual support and understanding, akin to family members working together to overcome challenges.
    • In community service, it reflects the spirit of collaboration among volunteers who unite to bring about positive change in society.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the power of unity, collaboration, and divine blessings in achieving greatness and prosperity.
    • It serves as a reminder that by working together with a shared purpose and seeking divine guidance, we can illuminate our paths and attain our goals.
    • In essence, it encourages us to embrace collective efforts and the nurturing aspects of life, leading to a harmonious and prosperous existence.
