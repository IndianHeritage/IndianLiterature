अभुत्स्यु प्र देव्या साकं वाचाहम अश्विनोः |व्य आवर देव्या मतीं विरातिम मार्त्येभ्यः ||
abhutsy u pra devyā sākaṁ vācāham aśvinoḥ | vy āvar devy ā matiṁ vi rātim martyebhyaḥ ||
  • Mantra: "abhutsy u pra devyā sākaṁ vācāham aśvinoḥ | vy āvar devy ā matiṁ vi rātim martyebhyaḥ ||"
  • Translation: "I speak with the divine words of the Aśvins; may they grant us wisdom and understanding, and may they protect us from the mortal dangers."

  • Word Breakdown:
    • abhutsy: Refers to the act of speaking or expressing.
    • u: A particle often used for emphasis, similar to "indeed" or "certainly."
    • pra: Means "forth" or "out," indicating a direction or intention.
    • devyā: Relates to the divine or celestial beings, often referring to goddesses or divine forces.
    • sākaṁ: Means "together" or "with," indicating a collective or unified action.
    • vācāham: Refers to "words" or "speech," emphasizing the power of communication.
    • aśvinoḥ: Refers to the Aśvins, twin deities associated with health, medicine, and the dawn.
    • vy: A prefix that can mean "away" or "from," indicating a protective action.
    • āvar: Means "to protect" or "to cover," suggesting a safeguarding action.
    • devy: Again refers to the divine or goddess-like qualities.
    • ā matiṁ: Refers to "wisdom" or "intellect," indicating a desire for understanding.
    • vi: A prefix meaning "apart" or "separate," often used to indicate distinction.
    • rātim: Refers to "delight" or "joy," suggesting a positive emotional state.
    • martyebhyaḥ: Refers to "mortals" or "human beings," indicating the human condition.

  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a challenging situation, such as a medical emergency, invoking the Aśvins for their divine assistance can be seen as a metaphor for seeking help from healthcare professionals who embody wisdom and skill.
    • In moments of decision-making, individuals often seek guidance from mentors or wise figures, paralleling the request for divine wisdom in the mantra.
    • During community gatherings or rituals, the collective invocation of divine protection can foster a sense of unity and shared purpose among participants.

  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of divine communication and the pursuit of wisdom. It highlights the need for protection from the challenges of mortal life while seeking guidance from higher powers. In real life, this can be applicable in various scenarios, such as seeking help in times of crisis, making important decisions, or fostering community spirit. The invocation of divine assistance serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of human experience and the support we can seek from both the divine and our community.
