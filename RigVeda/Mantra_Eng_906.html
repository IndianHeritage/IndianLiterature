स घातं वृषणं रथम अधि तिष्ठाति गोविदम् |यः पात्रं हारियोजनं पूर्णं इन्द्र चिकेटति योजा nv इन्द्र ते हरि ||
sa ghā taṁ vṛṣaṇaṁ ratham adhi tiṣṭhāti govidam | yaḥ pātraṁ hāriyojanam pūrṇam indra ciketati yojā nv indra te harī ||
  • sa ghā taṁ vṛṣaṇaṁ ratham adhi tiṣṭhāti govidam: This line can be interpreted as a call to the divine forces that govern strength and power. The term "vṛṣaṇaṁ" refers to strength or might, while "ratham" signifies a chariot, symbolizing the journey of life. "Govidam" refers to the divine or the protector of cows, often associated with Lord Krishna, who embodies wisdom and guidance.
  • yaḥ pātraṁ hāriyojanam pūrṇam indra ciketati: Here, "pātraṁ" means vessel or container, and "hāriyojanam" can be understood as a means of achieving or attaining something. "Pūrṇam" signifies fullness or completeness, while "indra" refers to the king of gods, representing power and authority. This line suggests that the vessel of one's life should be filled with the blessings and wisdom of the divine.
  • yojā nv indra te harī: This final line emphasizes the connection between the individual and the divine. "Yojā" refers to the act of joining or connecting, while "harī" is another name for the divine, often associated with Lord Vishnu. This line signifies the importance of aligning oneself with divine will and purpose.

  • Real-Life Example: In a practical sense, this mantra can be applied to personal development and leadership. Just as a chariot needs a strong foundation and guidance to move forward, individuals must cultivate their inner strength and seek wisdom to navigate life's challenges. For instance, a leader in a corporate setting can embody the principles of this mantra by fostering a supportive environment, encouraging teamwork, and ensuring that their team feels empowered and valued.
  • Significance: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for strength, wisdom, and divine connection. It serves as a reminder that in our journey through life, we must seek to fill our 'vessel' with positive attributes and align ourselves with higher principles. This alignment can lead to personal fulfillment and success in various endeavors.
  • Application: This mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as in personal growth, leadership, and community building. By embodying the qualities of strength, wisdom, and connection to the divine, individuals can inspire others and create a positive impact in their surroundings.
