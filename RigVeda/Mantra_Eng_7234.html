प्र-प्र वस त्रिष्टुभम् इषम् मन्दद्वीरायन्दवे |धियā वो मेधसातये पुरंढ्या विवासति ||
pra-pra vas triṣṭubham iṣam mandadvīrāyendave | dhiyā vo medhasātaye puraṁdhyā vivāsati ||
  • pra-pra: This term can be understood as an invocation or a call to action, suggesting a plea for assistance or guidance.
  • vas: This word means to dwell or reside, indicating a sense of stability or presence.
  • triṣṭubham: Refers to a specific meter in Vedic poetry, often associated with the rhythm and structure of the verses, symbolizing harmony and balance.
  • iṣam: This translates to 'desire' or 'wish', representing the aspirations and goals one seeks to achieve.
  • mandadvīrāyendave: This compound word can be broken down into 'mandad' (gentle or kind), 'vīrā' (heroes or warriors), and 'indave' (to the divine or god), suggesting a call to the benevolent forces or deities that support the brave and virtuous.
  • dhiyā: This means 'with intellect' or 'with understanding', emphasizing the importance of wisdom in achieving one's goals.
  • vo: This is a pronoun meaning 'your', indicating a connection to the audience or the divine beings being addressed.
  • medhasātaye: This term refers to 'for the sake of intelligence' or 'for the enhancement of wisdom', highlighting the pursuit of knowledge and understanding.
  • puraṁdhyā: This can be interpreted as 'the one who is the creator of the city' or 'the architect of the universe', symbolizing the divine force that shapes reality.
  • vivāsati: This means 'to reside' or 'to dwell', suggesting a sense of permanence and stability in the presence of the divine.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking divine guidance and wisdom in our pursuits. It highlights the need for a harmonious balance in our desires and actions, encouraging us to call upon benevolent forces to support our endeavors. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios such as decision-making processes, personal growth, and community building. By invoking this mantra, individuals can cultivate a mindset that values intelligence, kindness, and the pursuit of noble goals, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling and purposeful life.
