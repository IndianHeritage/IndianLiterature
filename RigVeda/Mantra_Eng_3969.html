तां वो देवाः सुमतिम ऊर्जयन्तीषम अश्यामा वसवः शसागोः |सा नः सुदानुर मृळयन्ती देवी प्रति द्रवन्ती सुविताया गम्याः ||
tāṁ vo devāḥ sumatim ūrjayantīm iṣam aśyāma vasavaḥ śasā goḥ | sā naḥ sudānur mṛḻayantī devī prati dravantī suvitāya gamyāḥ ||
  • tāṁ vo devāḥ: This phrase translates to "May the gods grant you." It signifies a request for divine blessings and guidance.
  • sumatim ūrjayantīm: This means "with good understanding and strength." It emphasizes the importance of wisdom and vitality in one's life.
  • iṣam aśyāma: This translates to "may we attain our desires." It reflects the human aspiration to achieve goals and fulfill wishes.
  • vasavaḥ śasā goḥ: This phrase refers to the "wealth of the earth" or "prosperity." It highlights the significance of material and spiritual abundance.
  • sā naḥ sudānur: This means "may she, the goddess, be favorable to us." It invokes the blessings of a divine feminine presence, often associated with nurturing and protection.
  • mṛḻayantī devī: This translates to "the goddess who brings joy." It signifies the importance of happiness and contentment in life.
  • prati dravantī: This means "who flows towards us." It symbolizes the idea of divine grace and abundance coming into our lives.
  • suvitāya gamyāḥ: This translates to "leading us to good fortune." It emphasizes the guidance towards positive outcomes and success.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda encapsulates a profound prayer for wisdom, prosperity, and divine guidance. In real-life scenarios, it can be applied in various contexts such as during important decisions, starting new ventures, or seeking clarity in challenging situations. By invoking the blessings of the divine, individuals can cultivate a mindset of positivity and resilience, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling and prosperous life. The mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of spiritual and material well-being, encouraging individuals to seek balance and harmony in their pursuits.
