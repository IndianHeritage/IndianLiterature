अभि ये त्वा विभावरि स्तोमैर गृणन्ति वह्नयः |मघैर मघोनि सुश्रियो दामन्वन्तः सुरातयः सुजाते अश्वसूत्रते ||
abhi ye tvā vibhāvari stomair gṛṇanti vahnayaḥ | maghair maghoni suśriyo dāmanvantaḥ surātayaḥ sujāte aśvasūnṛte ||
  • Mantra: "abhi ye tvā vibhāvari stomair gṛṇanti vahnayaḥ | maghair maghoni suśriyo dāmanvantaḥ surātayaḥ sujāte aśvasūnṛte ||"
  • Translation: "Those who sing your praises with their hymns, O Vibhāvari, are the ones who are blessed by the divine fire. They are endowed with great wealth, and they are the ones who bring forth the best of offerings, who are worthy of respect and who are born of the divine essence."

  • Word Breakdown:
    • abhi: towards, in the direction of
    • ye: those who
    • tvā: you
    • vibhāvari: the radiant one, often associated with light and illumination
    • stomair: with hymns or praises
    • gṛṇanti: sing or chant
    • vahnayaḥ: of the divine fire
    • maghair: with great wealth or abundance
    • maghoni: endowed with wealth
    • suśriyo: those who are well-respected or renowned
    • dāmanvantaḥ: those who bring forth offerings
    • surātayaḥ: born of the divine essence
    • sujāte: well-born or noble
    • aśvasūnṛte: in the context of horses, often symbolizing strength and vitality

  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community gathering, when people come together to celebrate a festival, they often sing hymns and praises to deities, similar to how the mantra describes the act of singing praises to the divine.
    • In modern corporate settings, companies often recognize and reward employees who contribute significantly to the organization, akin to the respect and recognition mentioned in the mantra.
    • In personal life, individuals who practice gratitude and acknowledge the contributions of others often find themselves surrounded by positivity and abundance, reflecting the blessings of the divine fire.

  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its emphasis on the power of praise and recognition. It teaches us that by acknowledging the divine and the contributions of others, we can attract blessings and abundance into our lives. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various contexts, such as community celebrations, workplace recognition, and personal relationships. By embodying the principles of respect, gratitude, and acknowledgment, we can create a harmonious environment that fosters growth and prosperity.
