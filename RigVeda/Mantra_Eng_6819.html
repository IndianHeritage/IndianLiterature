मन्त्र: अपि वृश्च पुराणवत व्रततेर इव गुष्पितं ओजो दासस्य दम्भय | वयं तद अस्य संभृतं वस्व इन्द्रेण वि भजेमहि नभन्ताम् अन्यके समे ||
api vṛśca purāṇavad vratater iva guṣpitam ojo dāsasya dambhaya | vayaṁ tad asya sambhṛtaṁ vasv indreṇa vi bhajemahi nabhantām anyake same ||
  • api vṛśca purāṇavad: This phrase can be interpreted as "even like the ancient ones." It suggests a connection to the wisdom and practices of the past, emphasizing the importance of tradition and heritage in our lives.
  • vratater iva guṣpitam: This translates to "like the vow that is well-nourished." It signifies the importance of commitment and dedication in achieving one's goals. Just as a vow requires nurturing, so do our aspirations and responsibilities.
  • ojo dāsasya dambhaya: Here, "ojo" refers to strength or energy, while "dāsasya dambhaya" can be understood as "the strength of the servant." This highlights the idea that true strength comes from humility and service to others, rather than from power or dominance.
  • vayaṁ tad asya sambhṛtaṁ: This means "we are that which is gathered." It reflects the idea of community and collective effort. In real life, this can be seen in teamwork, where individuals come together to achieve a common goal.
  • vasv indreṇa vi bhajemahi: This translates to "we invoke the universal power." It signifies the importance of seeking guidance and support from a higher power or the universe in our endeavors.
  • nabhantām anyake same: This phrase can be interpreted as "may we be united in the same space." It emphasizes unity and harmony among individuals, which is essential for a peaceful coexistence.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of tradition, commitment, humility, community, divine guidance, and unity. In real life, these principles can be applied in various scenarios, such as in family dynamics, workplace environments, and community service. By embracing these values, individuals can foster a sense of belonging and purpose, leading to a more harmonious and fulfilling life. The significance of this mantra lies in its timeless wisdom, encouraging us to draw strength from our roots while working together towards a common goal, invoking the support of the universe along the way.
