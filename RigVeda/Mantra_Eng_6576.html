तं उ त्वा नूनम इमहे नव्यं दंसीष्ठ सञ्जसे |स तवं नो विश्वा अभिमातिः सक्षणिः ||
tam u tvā nūnam īmahe navyaṁ daṁsiṣṭha sanyase | sa tvaṁ no viśvā abhimātīḥ sakṣaṇiḥ ||
  • tam u tvā nūnam īmahe: This phrase can be translated as "Indeed, we invoke you." Here, the speaker expresses a deep reverence and intention to connect with a divine presence or energy.
  • navyaṁ daṁsiṣṭha sanyase: This part translates to "the new and most powerful." It signifies the freshness and strength of the divine or the energy being invoked, suggesting that it is not only new but also potent and transformative.
  • sa tvaṁ no viśvā abhimātīḥ: This means "may you be our all-encompassing protector." It emphasizes the desire for the divine to provide comprehensive support and guidance in all aspects of life.
  • sakṣaṇiḥ: This term can be interpreted as "in every moment" or "in every instance." It highlights the importance of divine presence in the continuous flow of life, suggesting that the divine is always with us, guiding our actions and decisions.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a profound invocation for divine support and guidance. It emphasizes the importance of connecting with a powerful, new energy that can transform our lives. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applicable in various situations such as during challenging times when one seeks strength and protection, or when embarking on new ventures that require courage and clarity. By reciting this mantra, individuals can cultivate a sense of trust in the divine presence, encouraging them to move forward with confidence and assurance that they are supported in their journey. The significance of this mantra lies in its ability to remind us of the ever-present divine energy that guides and protects us, urging us to remain open to new possibilities and transformations in our lives.
