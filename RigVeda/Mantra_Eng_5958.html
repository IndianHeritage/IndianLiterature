ये पाकशंसं विहरन्त एवैर ये वा भद्रं दूषयन्ति स्वधाभिः |अहये वा तान प्रददातु सोम आ वा दधातु निरृतेर उपस्थे ||
ye pākaśaṁsaṁ viharanta evair ye vā bhadraṁ dūṣayanti svadhābhiḥ | ahaye vā tān pradadātu soma ā vā dadhātu nirṛter upasthe ||
  • Translation: "Those who enjoy the offerings, who do not harm the good, may the Soma grant them strength, and may they be free from misfortune."

  • Word Breakdown:
    • ye: Those who
    • pākaśaṁsaṁ: enjoy the offerings
    • viharanta: who dwell or reside
    • evair: indeed
    • ye vā: those who also
    • bhadraṁ: the good or auspicious
    • dūṣayanti: harm or spoil
    • svadhābhiḥ: with their own actions
    • ahaye: may they
    • vā: or
    • tān: them
    • pradadātu: grant or bestow
    • soma: the divine nectar or strength
    • ā vā: and may they
    • dadhātu: be free or protected
    • nirṛter: from misfortune
    • upasthe: in their presence or vicinity

  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community setting, individuals who contribute positively and refrain from harming others can be seen as embodying the essence of this mantra. For instance, a volunteer who helps organize community events without seeking personal gain reflects the spirit of "enjoying the offerings."
    • In a workplace, employees who support their colleagues and foster a positive environment contribute to the overall well-being of the organization. Their actions align with the idea of not harming the good.
    • In personal relationships, individuals who practice kindness and understanding, avoiding actions that could hurt others, exemplify the teachings of this mantra.

  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its emphasis on positive actions and the importance of community well-being. It encourages individuals to engage in behaviors that uplift others and contribute to a harmonious environment. In real life, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as community service, workplace dynamics, and personal relationships, promoting a culture of support and positivity. By embodying these principles, individuals can foster a sense of unity and resilience, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling and prosperous life for themselves and those around them.
