असुंवाम इन्द्र संसदं विषूचिं व्य अनाशयः |सोमपा उत्तरो भवन ||
asunvām indra saṁsadaṁ viṣūcīṁ vy anāśayaḥ | somapā uttaro bhavan ||
  • asunvām: This term can be interpreted as "we are seeking" or "we are invoking." It signifies a call to action or a desire for connection.
  • indra: Indra is the king of the gods in Vedic mythology, often associated with rain, thunderstorms, and strength. He represents power and authority.
  • saṁsadaṁ: This word refers to a gathering or assembly. It implies the importance of community and collective effort.
  • viṣūcīṁ: This term can be understood as "the one who dispels darkness" or "the one who brings clarity." It symbolizes enlightenment and the removal of ignorance.
  • vy anāśayaḥ: This phrase means "to remove obstacles" or "to eliminate difficulties." It emphasizes the need for overcoming challenges in life.
  • somapā: This term refers to "those who drink soma," a sacred drink in Vedic rituals believed to confer immortality and divine insight.
  • uttaro: This word means "higher" or "superior," indicating a pursuit of elevated states of being or consciousness.
  • bhavan: This term translates to "becoming" or "manifesting," suggesting the realization of potential or aspirations.

The mantra can be interpreted as a prayer or invocation to Indra, seeking his blessings for clarity, community support, and the removal of obstacles in the pursuit of higher consciousness and fulfillment. It emphasizes the importance of collective effort in overcoming challenges and achieving one's goals.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as:

  • In a team setting, invoking the spirit of collaboration and unity to achieve a common goal.
  • In personal development, seeking guidance and clarity in times of confusion or difficulty.
  • In community gatherings, fostering a sense of togetherness and support to overcome societal challenges.

In conclusion, this mantra highlights the significance of seeking divine guidance, the power of community, and the importance of overcoming obstacles in the journey towards higher aspirations. It serves as a reminder that through collective effort and divine support, individuals can achieve clarity and fulfillment in their lives.
