शर्धो मारुतम् उच छंसा सत्यशवसम ऋभवसम |उता स्म ते शुभे नरः प्र स्यन्द्रा युजता तमनाः ||
śardho mārutam uc chaṁsa satyaśavasam ṛbhvasam | uta sma te śubhe naraḥ pra syandrā yujata tmanā ||
  • śardho: This word can be interpreted as "with strength" or "with vigor." It signifies the importance of energy and vitality in our actions.
  • mārutam: Referring to the wind or the forces of nature, this term symbolizes the dynamic and ever-changing aspects of life.
  • uc chaṁsa: This phrase can be translated as "to the highest glory" or "to the exalted state," indicating a pursuit of excellence and high ideals.
  • satyaśavasam: This means "truthful" or "possessing truth," emphasizing the value of honesty and integrity in our endeavors.
  • ṛbhvasam: This term suggests "noble" or "virtuous," highlighting the importance of moral character and ethical behavior.
  • uta: This word can be understood as "and" or "also," connecting the various elements of the mantra.
  • sma: This can mean "indeed" or "certainly," reinforcing the certainty of the statements being made.
  • te: This translates to "your," indicating a personal connection or appeal to a higher power or the divine.
  • śubhe: Meaning "auspicious" or "good," this word reflects the desire for positive outcomes and blessings.
  • naraḥ: This refers to "man" or "human," emphasizing the focus on human experience and existence.
  • pra: This prefix can mean "forth" or "forward," suggesting progress and movement towards a goal.
  • syandrā: This term can be interpreted as "to be united" or "to come together," highlighting the importance of collaboration and unity.
  • yujata: Meaning "to join" or "to connect," this word emphasizes the act of bringing together different elements or people.
  • tmanā: This translates to "with the mind" or "with intention," indicating the significance of mental focus and clarity in achieving one's goals.

In conclusion, this mantra encapsulates a profound message about the importance of strength, truth, nobility, and unity in human endeavors. It encourages individuals to pursue high ideals with integrity and to work collaboratively towards auspicious outcomes. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios such as teamwork in professional settings, community service, or personal development. By embodying these principles, individuals can contribute positively to society and foster a sense of harmony and purpose in their lives.
