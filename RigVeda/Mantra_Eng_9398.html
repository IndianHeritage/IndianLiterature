इन्द्र क्षत्रासमातिषु रथप्रोष्ठेषु धारय |दिवीव सूर्यं दृष्टे ||
indra kṣatrāsamātiṣu rathaproṣṭheṣu dhāraya | divīva sūryaṁ dṛśe ||
  • Indra: Indra is the king of the gods in Vedic mythology, often associated with thunder, rain, and war. He represents strength and power.
  • Kṣatrāsamātiṣu: This term refers to the assembly or gathering of warriors or rulers. It signifies unity and collaboration among those in power.
  • Rathaproṣṭheṣu: This word translates to "on the chariot." Chariots symbolize speed, progress, and the journey of life. They are often associated with battles and victories.
  • Dhāraya: This means "to hold" or "to sustain." It implies the responsibility of leadership and the need to support and protect the community.
  • Divīva: This translates to "like the heavens" or "divine." It suggests a connection to higher powers or ideals.
  • Sūryaṁ: Sūrya refers to the sun, symbolizing light, knowledge, and life. The sun is a source of energy and vitality.
  • Dṛśe: This means "to the sight" or "to be seen." It emphasizes visibility, clarity, and the importance of perception.

  • Meaning: The mantra can be interpreted as a call for Indra to support and sustain the assembly of warriors on their chariots, just as the sun illuminates the heavens. It highlights the importance of leadership, unity, and the divine guidance that empowers individuals in their pursuits.
  • Real-life Example: In a corporate setting, a leader (akin to Indra) must unite their team (the kṣatrāsamāti) to work together towards a common goal (the chariot). Just as the sun provides light and energy, a good leader inspires and motivates their team, ensuring clarity and direction in their tasks.

  • Significance: This mantra emphasizes the importance of leadership, unity, and divine guidance in achieving success. It serves as a reminder that effective leadership requires the ability to hold and sustain a group, much like Indra supports the warriors.
  • Application in Real Life: The principles derived from this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as team management, community leadership, and even personal development. By fostering unity and drawing upon higher ideals, individuals can navigate challenges and achieve their goals more effectively.
