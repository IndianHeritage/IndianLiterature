नृचक्षसो अनिमिषन्तो अर्हणा बृहद् देवासो अमृतत्वम् आनशुः |ज्योतीरथाः अहिमायाः अनागसो दिवो वर्ष्माणं वसते स्वस्तये ||
nṛcakṣaso animiṣanto arhaṇā bṛhad devāso amṛtatvam ānaśuḥ | jyotīrathā ahimāyā anāgaso divo varṣmāṇaṁ vasate svastaye ||
  • nṛcakṣaso animiṣanto: This phrase can be translated as "the beings with human-like eyes who are unblinking." It signifies the divine beings or deities who are ever-watchful and aware. In real life, this can be likened to the concept of guardians or protectors who are vigilant and attentive to the needs of those they watch over.
  • arhaṇā bṛhad devāso: This means "the great deities who are worthy of worship." It emphasizes the importance of reverence and respect towards the divine. In everyday life, this can be seen in the way people honor their mentors, leaders, or anyone who has significantly contributed to their lives.
  • amṛtatvam ānaśuḥ: This translates to "they attain immortality." It suggests that those who are devoted and righteous can achieve a state beyond physical existence. In a practical sense, this can be related to the legacy one leaves behind through their actions, values, and contributions to society.
  • jyotīrathā ahimāyā: This phrase means "the radiant chariot of the non-violent." It symbolizes the journey of life that is guided by light and peace. In real life, this can be compared to the pursuit of knowledge and wisdom, where individuals strive to lead a life free from conflict and negativity.
  • anāgaso divo varṣmāṇaṁ: This translates to "the one who is free from sin, dwelling in the heavens." It highlights the idea of purity and the aspiration to rise above worldly troubles. In practical terms, this can be seen in the efforts of individuals who strive for ethical living and moral integrity.
  • vasate svastaye: This means "may they dwell in well-being." It is a prayer for peace and prosperity. In real life, this can be reflected in community efforts to create a harmonious environment, where everyone works together for the common good.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of divine vigilance, reverence, immortality, peaceful existence, purity, and well-being. Its significance lies in the reminder to aspire towards higher virtues and to live a life that is mindful of the greater good. In real-life scenarios, it encourages individuals to be watchful and respectful, to seek knowledge and peace, and to contribute positively to their communities, ultimately leading to a harmonious and fulfilling existence.
