अजिरासो हरयो ये त आषवो वाताऽ इव प्रसक्षिणः |येभिर अपत्यं मनुष्यः परीयसे येभिर विश्वं स्वर दृष्टे ||
ajirāso harayo ye ta āśavo vātā iva prasakṣiṇaḥ | yebhir apatyam manuṣaḥ parīyase yebhir viśvaṁ svar dṛśe ||
  • Mantra: "ajirāso harayo ye ta āśavo vātā iva prasakṣiṇaḥ | yebhir apatyam manuṣaḥ parīyase yebhir viśvaṁ svar dṛśe ||"
  • Translation: "Those who are swift like the wind, who are the harbingers of life, they who are the breath of the universe, through whom humanity flourishes and the world is perceived."

  • Word Breakdown:
    • ajirāso: Refers to the swift or quick, symbolizing speed and agility.
    • harayo: Represents the life-giving forces, often associated with vitality and energy.
    • āśavo: Indicates those who are energetic or full of life.
    • vātā: Means wind, symbolizing movement and the unseen forces that drive life.
    • prasakṣiṇaḥ: Refers to those who are perceptive or aware, highlighting the importance of awareness in life.
    • yebhir: Means "through whom," indicating a connection or medium.
    • apatyam: Refers to offspring or progeny, emphasizing the continuity of life.
    • manuṣaḥ: Means humanity or human beings, focusing on the human experience.
    • parīyase: Indicates flourishing or thriving, suggesting growth and prosperity.
    • viśvaṁ: Refers to the universe or the entirety of existence.
    • svar: Means light or the celestial realm, often associated with higher consciousness.
    • dṛśe: Refers to perception or sight, emphasizing the importance of awareness in understanding the universe.

  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In nature, the wind can be seen as a force that carries seeds and pollen, facilitating growth and reproduction in plants. This mirrors the idea of life-giving forces in the mantra.
    • In human society, individuals who are energetic and perceptive often lead initiatives that promote community welfare, akin to the "harayo" who help humanity flourish.
    • In personal development, being aware and perceptive of one's surroundings can lead to better decision-making and a more fulfilling life, reflecting the mantra's emphasis on awareness.

  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the interconnectedness of life, the importance of awareness, and the role of energetic forces in fostering growth and prosperity. In real life, it serves as a reminder to be perceptive and to harness the life-giving energies around us, whether in nature, community, or personal growth. By embodying these principles, individuals can contribute to a thriving existence for themselves and others, ultimately leading to a more harmonious world.
