वेदीषदे प्रियधामाया सुद्युते धासिम इव प्र भरā योनिम अग्नये |वस्त्रेणेव वासयā मनमनā शुचिं ज्योतीरथं शुक्रवर्णं तमोहनम् ||
vediṣade priyadhāmāya sudyute dhāsim iva pra bharā yonim agnaye | vastreṇeva vāsayā manmanā śuciṁ jyotīrathaṁ śukravarṇaṁ tamohanam ||
  • vediṣade: This term can be interpreted as "to the one who is knowledgeable" or "to the one who is wise." It emphasizes the importance of wisdom and knowledge in our lives.
  • priyadhāmāya: This means "to the beloved abode" or "to the place of affection." It signifies the importance of love and compassion in our relationships and surroundings.
  • sudyute: Translated as "with bright radiance," this word highlights the significance of positivity and enlightenment in our lives.
  • dhāsim iva pra bharā: This phrase can be understood as "like a servant, I offer." It reflects the idea of humility and service to others, which is a vital aspect of human interaction.
  • yonim agnaye: This translates to "to the source of fire." Fire symbolizes energy, transformation, and purification, representing the inner strength we possess.
  • vastreṇeva vāsayā: This means "like a garment, I cover." It suggests the idea of protection and nurturing, akin to how a garment protects the body.
  • manmanā: This term translates to "with a focused mind." It emphasizes the importance of concentration and mindfulness in achieving our goals.
  • śuciṁ: Meaning "pure," this word signifies the value of purity in thoughts, actions, and intentions.
  • jyotīrathaṁ: This can be interpreted as "the chariot of light," symbolizing guidance and direction in our lives.
  • śukravarṇaṁ: Translated as "of the color of brightness," it represents positivity and the pursuit of knowledge and wisdom.
  • tamohanam: This means "the dispeller of darkness," symbolizing the removal of ignorance and negativity from our lives.

Conclusion: This mantra encapsulates the essence of wisdom, love, service, and purity. It serves as a reminder to seek knowledge, maintain a positive outlook, and serve others with humility. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as in personal relationships, professional environments, and spiritual practices. By embodying the principles outlined in this mantra, individuals can foster a more harmonious and enlightened existence, contributing positively to their communities and the world at large.
