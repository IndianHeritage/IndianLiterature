प्र ताऱ्य आयुः प्रतरं नवीयः स्थातारेव क्रतुमता रथस्य |अध च्यवान उत तवीत्य अर्थं परातरं सु निरृतिर जिहीताम् ||
pra tāry āyuḥ prataraṁ navīyaḥ sthātāreva kratumatā rathasya | adha cyavāna ut tavīty artham parātaraṁ su nirṛtir jihītām ||
  • pra tāry āyuḥ: This phrase can be translated as "may we attain longevity." It emphasizes the importance of a long and healthy life, which is a common aspiration in many cultures. For example, people often seek out healthy lifestyles, diets, and exercise routines to prolong their lives.
  • prataraṁ navīyaḥ: This means "may we cross over to newness." It suggests the idea of renewal and transformation. In real life, this can be seen in personal growth, such as changing careers, adopting new habits, or embracing new technologies that enhance our lives.
  • sthātāreva kratumatā rathasya: This translates to "like the steadfastness of a charioteer." It highlights the qualities of determination and control. In practical terms, this can relate to leadership in various fields, where a strong leader guides their team with confidence and stability, much like a charioteer guiding their horses.
  • adha cyavāna ut tavīty artham: This phrase means "may we not fall away from our purpose." It speaks to the importance of staying true to one's goals and intentions. In everyday life, this can be seen in individuals who remain committed to their dreams despite challenges, such as students pursuing their education against all odds.
  • parātaraṁ su nirṛtir jihītām: This translates to "may the highest good be our guiding principle." It emphasizes the pursuit of righteousness and ethical living. In real life, this can manifest in community service, environmental stewardship, or any actions that contribute positively to society.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a holistic approach to life, emphasizing longevity, renewal, steadfastness, purpose, and ethical living. Its significance lies in its guidance for individuals to strive for a balanced and meaningful existence. In practical scenarios, this mantra can inspire people to pursue personal growth, maintain focus on their goals, and contribute positively to their communities, ultimately leading to a fulfilling life. By embodying these principles, individuals can navigate the complexities of life with resilience and purpose.
