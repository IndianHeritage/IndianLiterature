पद-पद मे जरीमा न धायि वरूत्री वा शक्रा या पायुभिश्च |सिषक्तु माता मही रसाऽनः स्मत सूरिभिर ऋजुहस्त ऋजुवनिः ||
pade-pade me jarimā ni dhāyi varūtrī vā śakrā yā pāyubhiś ca | siṣaktu mātā mahī rasā naḥ smat sūribhir ṛjuhasta ṛjuvaniḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • pade-pade: at every step
    • me: my
    • jarimā: old age
    • ni dhāyi: may not come
    • varūtrī: the one who removes obstacles
    • vā: or
    • śakrā: the powerful one
    • yā: who
    • pāyubhiś: with the thighs
    • ca: and
    • siṣaktu: may bless
    • mātā: mother
    • mahī: the earth
    • rasā: essence
    • naḥ: our
    • smat: from us
    • sūribhir: by the wise
    • ṛjuhasta: with straight hands
    • ṛjuvaniḥ: with straight speech
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra expresses a prayer for protection and blessings at every step of life.
    • It seeks the removal of old age and obstacles, invoking the powerful forces of the universe.
    • The mention of "mother earth" signifies a connection to nature and the nurturing aspect of life.
    • The phrase "by the wise" emphasizes the importance of wisdom and righteousness in our actions.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, this mantra can be recited by individuals facing challenges, such as health issues or career obstacles, as a form of mental and spiritual support.
    • For students, it can serve as a reminder to seek wisdom and clarity in their studies, ensuring they approach their education with integrity.
    • In community gatherings, this mantra can be invoked to foster unity and strength among members, encouraging them to support one another in overcoming difficulties.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its holistic approach to life, emphasizing the need for protection, wisdom, and the nurturing aspects of nature.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal challenges to communal support, reminding us of the interconnectedness of our actions and the environment.
    • By reciting this mantra, individuals can cultivate a mindset of resilience and positivity, fostering a deeper connection with themselves and the world around them.
