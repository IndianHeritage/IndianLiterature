स इत् स्वपā भुवनेष्व् आस य इमे dyāvāpṛthivī jajāna |उर्वी गभीर रजसी सुमेके अवंशे धीरः शच्यā सम ऐरत् ||
sa it svapā bhuvaneṣv āsa ya ime dyāvāpṛthivī jajāna | urvī gabhīre rajasī sumeke avaṁśe dhīraḥ śacyā sam airat ||
  • sa it svapā bhuvaneṣv āsa: This phrase can be translated to "He who is the essence of all that exists in the universe." It signifies the omnipresence of the divine, suggesting that the divine is inherent in all aspects of life and nature.
  • ya ime dyāvāpṛthivī jajāna: This translates to "who has created the heavens and the earth." It emphasizes the idea of creation and the interconnectedness of all elements in the universe, highlighting the divine role in the formation of the cosmos.
  • urvī gabhīre rajasī sumeke: This can be interpreted as "the vast and deep earth, rich in resources." It points to the abundance of nature and the importance of the earth as a nurturing force for all living beings.
  • avaṁśe dhīraḥ śacyā sam airat: This means "the wise and courageous one who is endowed with strength." It speaks to the qualities of wisdom, courage, and strength that are essential for navigating life's challenges.

  • Significance: This mantra encapsulates the essence of creation, the divine presence in the universe, and the qualities necessary for human existence. It serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of all beings and the importance of recognizing the divine in everyday life.
  • Real-life Application: In practical terms, this mantra can inspire individuals to cultivate qualities such as wisdom and courage in their personal and professional lives. For example, a leader in a community can embody these traits by making decisions that benefit the collective, drawing on the strength and resources of the community, much like the earth provides for all living beings.
  • Conclusion: The mantra encourages us to acknowledge the divine presence in our lives and to strive for wisdom and courage. By recognizing our connection to the universe and the resources it offers, we can lead more fulfilling and purposeful lives, contributing positively to our communities and the world at large.
