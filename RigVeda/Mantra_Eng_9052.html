यद अज्ञातेषु वृजनेष्व आसं विश्वे सतो मगवाणो मा आसन |जिनामि वेत क्षेम आ संतम आब्हुम प्र तं क्षिणाम पर्वते पादगृह्य ||
yad ajñāteṣu vṛjaneṣv āsaṁ viśve sato maghavāno ma āsan | jināmi vet kṣema ā santam ābhum pra taṁ kṣiṇām parvate pādagṛhya ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yad: that which
    • ajñāteṣu: in the unknown
    • vṛjaneṣv: in the realms of freedom
    • āsaṁ: is present
    • viśve: all
    • sato: of the truthful
    • maghavāno: the powerful
    • ma āsan: do not sit
    • jināmi: I know
    • vet: the one who knows
    • kṣema: safety or well-being
    • ā: indeed
    • santam: the peaceful
    • ābhum: the abode
    • pra: towards
    • taṁ: that
    • kṣiṇām: of the diminishing
    • parvate: in the mountains
    • pādagṛhya: grasping the feet
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the presence of the divine in the unknown realms and emphasizes the importance of truthfulness and power.
    • It suggests that one should not remain idle or complacent in the face of challenges but should actively seek knowledge and safety.
    • The reference to "grasping the feet" can symbolize seeking guidance and support from a higher power or wisdom.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, when faced with uncertainty, individuals are encouraged to seek knowledge and guidance from mentors or experienced colleagues, rather than remaining passive.
    • In personal life, during times of crisis, turning to spiritual practices or community support can provide a sense of safety and well-being.
    • In environmental contexts, acknowledging the unknown aspects of nature and actively working towards sustainability reflects the essence of this mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of seeking knowledge and truth in the face of uncertainty.
    • It encourages individuals to take proactive steps towards achieving safety and well-being, both personally and collectively.
    • In essence, it serves as a reminder to remain vigilant, seek guidance, and embrace the unknown with courage and wisdom.
