एकं विचक्र चामसं चतुर्वयं निश्चर्मणो गाम अरिणीत धीतिभिः |अथा देवेष्व अमृतत्वम् आनश श्रुष्टि वाजाः ऋभवस्तद व उक्त्यम् ||
ekaṁ vi cakra camasaṁ caturvayaṁ niś carmaṇo gām ariṇīta dhītibhiḥ | athā deveṣv amṛtatvam ānaśa śruṣṭī vājā ṛbhavas tad va ukthyam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ekaṁ: one
    • vi: indeed
    • cakra: wheel or cycle
    • camasaṁ: vessel or container
    • caturvayaṁ: fourfold or having four aspects
    • niś: night or darkness
    • carmaṇo: of the skin or covering
    • gām: the world or the earth
    • ariṇīta: to be led or guided
    • dhītibhiḥ: with wisdom or understanding
    • athā: then or thereafter
    • deveṣv: among the gods
    • amṛtatvam: immortality or eternal life
    • ānaśa: to attain or achieve
    • śruṣṭī: creation or manifestation
    • vājā: strength or power
    • ṛbhavas: the noble or the exalted
    • tad: that
    • va: indeed
    • ukthyam: utterance or hymn
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the unity and interconnectedness of all things, represented by the "one wheel" and the "fourfold" aspects of existence.
    • The "night" symbolizes the unknown or the unconscious, while "wisdom" suggests the importance of understanding and enlightenment in navigating through life.
    • The reference to "gods" and "immortality" indicates a spiritual aspiration, suggesting that through wisdom and understanding, one can attain a higher state of existence.
    • The "strength" and "nobility" mentioned in the mantra highlight the virtues that one should aspire to in life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, the idea of "one wheel" can be likened to teamwork, where every member plays a crucial role in achieving a common goal.
    • In personal development, the journey through "night" can represent the challenges and struggles one faces, while "wisdom" signifies the lessons learned that lead to personal growth.
    • In spiritual practices, the pursuit of "immortality" can be interpreted as striving for a legacy or impact that transcends one's physical existence.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of unity, wisdom, and the pursuit of higher ideals in life.
    • It encourages individuals to seek understanding and enlightenment, which can lead to a more fulfilling and meaningful existence.
    • In various aspects of life, whether personal, professional, or spiritual, the principles outlined in this mantra can guide individuals towards achieving their goals and aspirations.
