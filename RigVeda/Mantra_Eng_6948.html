त्वां हि सत्यं अद्रिवो विद्म दातारं इषाम् |विद्म दातारं रयीणाम् ||
tvāṁ hi satyam adrivo vidma dātāram iṣām | vidma dātāraṁ rayīṇām ||
  • tvāṁ hi satyam: This phrase translates to "You are indeed the truth." Here, "tvāṁ" refers to the divine or the ultimate reality, while "satyam" signifies truth. In our daily lives, this can be understood as the pursuit of honesty and integrity in our actions and thoughts. For example, in a workplace, being truthful in communication fosters trust and collaboration among colleagues.
  • adrivo vidma: This means "we know the one who is beyond the mountains." "Adrivo" refers to the mountains, symbolizing obstacles or challenges in life. This part of the mantra suggests that there is a higher power or truth that transcends these challenges. In real life, this can be seen in situations where individuals face significant hurdles, such as health issues or financial difficulties, yet find strength and resilience through faith or a supportive community.
  • dātāram iṣām: This translates to "the giver of wealth." "Dātāram" refers to the giver or provider, while "iṣām" relates to wealth or prosperity. This part emphasizes the importance of recognizing and appreciating the sources of our abundance, whether they be material, emotional, or spiritual. For instance, a person may acknowledge their family, education, or mentors as the true sources of their success and prosperity.
  • vidma dātāraṁ rayīṇām: This means "we know the giver of riches." It reinforces the idea that understanding the source of our wealth and blessings is crucial. In practical terms, this can be applied in our lives by practicing gratitude and giving back to the community, recognizing that our successes are often built on the support and resources provided by others.

Conclusion: The mantra "tvāṁ hi satyam adrivo vidma dātāram iṣām | vidma dātāraṁ rayīṇām" encapsulates the essence of truth, the recognition of higher powers beyond our challenges, and the appreciation of the sources of our wealth. Its significance lies in encouraging individuals to seek truth in their lives, acknowledge the support they receive, and cultivate gratitude. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire ethical behavior, resilience in the face of adversity, and a commitment to giving back to society, ultimately leading to a more harmonious and fulfilling existence.
