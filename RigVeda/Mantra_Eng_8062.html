आयं सूर्य इवोपदृग आयं सरांसि धावति |सप्त प्रवत आ दिवम् ||
ayaṁ sūrya ivopadṛg ayaṁ sarāṁsi dhāvati | sapta pravata ā divam ||
  • ayaṁ sūrya ivopadṛg: This phrase translates to "this (is) like the sun." Here, the sun symbolizes brightness, energy, and life. In real life, we can relate this to the sun's role in providing warmth and sustenance to all living beings. For example, plants rely on sunlight for photosynthesis, which is essential for their growth and for producing oxygen that we breathe.
  • ayaṁ sarāṁsi dhāvati: This means "this (is) running towards the waters." The waters can represent the source of life, nourishment, and purity. In a practical sense, this can be seen in rivers and lakes that provide water for drinking, agriculture, and recreation. For instance, communities often gather around water bodies for sustenance and social activities.
  • sapta pravata ā divam: This translates to "the seven winds (or directions) blow towards the sky." The number seven often symbolizes completeness or totality in various cultures. The winds can be seen as the forces of nature that connect the earth to the heavens. In real life, we can observe how winds influence weather patterns, agriculture, and even the migration of birds and other animals.

  • Significance of the Mantra: This mantra emphasizes the interconnectedness of nature and the divine. It highlights the importance of the sun, water, and the winds in sustaining life on earth. Each element plays a crucial role in maintaining the balance of the ecosystem.
  • Real-Life Application: Understanding this mantra can inspire us to respect and protect our natural resources. For example, we can take action to conserve water, promote renewable energy sources like solar power, and support environmental initiatives that aim to preserve our ecosystems.
  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a reminder of the vital forces that sustain life and the need for harmony between humanity and nature. By recognizing the significance of these elements, we can foster a deeper appreciation for the environment and work towards a sustainable future.
