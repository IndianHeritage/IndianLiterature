ब्रह्मणाग्निः संविदानो रक्षोहा बादताम इतः |अमीवा यस ते गर्भं दुर्णामा योनिम आशये ||
brahmaṇāgniḥ saṁvidāno rakṣohā bādhatām itaḥ | amīvā yas te garbhaṁ durṇāmā yonim āśaye ||
  • Brahmaṇāgniḥ: This term refers to the divine fire or Agni, which is often associated with the creative force of the universe. In many cultures, fire symbolizes transformation and purification.
  • Saṁvidāno: This word means 'to know' or 'to understand.' It implies a deep awareness or consciousness that is essential for spiritual growth.
  • Rakṣohā: This term translates to 'protection from evil' or 'removal of obstacles.' It signifies the need for safeguarding oneself from negative influences.
  • Bādhatām: This means 'to be hindered' or 'to be obstructed.' It reflects the challenges and difficulties one may face in life.
  • Itaḥ: This word means 'from here' or 'from this place,' indicating a starting point or origin.
  • Amīvā: This term can be interpreted as 'the one who is not like others' or 'unique.' It emphasizes individuality and the importance of one's own path.
  • Yas te: This phrase means 'your' or 'of you,' indicating a personal connection or relationship.
  • Garbhaṁ: This translates to 'womb' or 'embryo,' symbolizing potential and the beginning of life.
  • Durṇāmā: This term means 'difficult to name' or 'hard to define,' suggesting complexities in understanding certain aspects of life.
  • Yonim: This word refers to 'source' or 'origin,' often associated with the feminine principle and creation.
  • Āśaye: This means 'in the intention' or 'in the desire,' indicating the importance of one's aspirations and goals.

In conclusion, this mantra emphasizes the significance of understanding the divine forces at play in our lives, the need for protection from negativity, and the importance of recognizing our unique paths and potential. It serves as a reminder that while we may face obstacles, our intentions and aspirations can guide us toward transformation and growth. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in moments of challenge, encouraging individuals to seek clarity, protection, and a deeper understanding of their purpose and potential in the world.
