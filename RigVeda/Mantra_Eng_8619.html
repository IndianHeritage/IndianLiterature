स हि त्वं देव शाश्वते वसु मर्ताय दाशुषे |इन्दो सहस्रिणं रयिं शतात्मानं विवाससि ||
sa hi tvaṁ deva śaśvate vasu martāya dāśuṣe | indo sahasriṇaṁ rayiṁ śatātmānaṁ vivāsasi ||
  • sa hi tvaṁ: This phrase translates to "Indeed, you are." It emphasizes the presence and importance of the divine or the deity being addressed.
  • deva: This word means "god" or "deity." It signifies the higher power or divine force that governs the universe.
  • śaśvate: This term means "eternal" or "everlasting." It highlights the timeless nature of the divine and its unchanging essence.
  • vasu: This word can be interpreted as "wealth" or "prosperity." It represents the abundance and resources that the divine bestows upon humanity.
  • martāya: This translates to "for mortals" or "for human beings." It indicates that the blessings and wealth are meant for the living beings on Earth.
  • dāśuṣe: This term means "to the one who serves" or "to the devotee." It signifies the importance of devotion and service to the divine.
  • indo: This word refers to "Indra," the king of gods in Vedic literature, symbolizing strength and power.
  • sahasriṇaṁ: This translates to "thousandfold" or "in abundance." It emphasizes the vastness of the blessings being sought.
  • rayiṁ: This word means "wealth" or "prosperity." It reiterates the desire for material and spiritual abundance.
  • śatātmānaṁ: This term means "with a hundred souls" or "with multiple aspects." It signifies the multifaceted nature of existence and the divine.
  • vivāsasi: This translates to "you bestow" or "you grant." It indicates the act of the divine in providing blessings and wealth.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking divine blessings for prosperity and abundance in life. It acknowledges the eternal nature of the divine and emphasizes the importance of devotion and service. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as during rituals for wealth creation, in prayers for success in endeavors, or in moments of gratitude for the abundance one receives. It serves as a reminder that the divine is always present, ready to bestow blessings upon those who serve and honor it. By recognizing the divine's role in our lives, we can cultivate a sense of gratitude and purpose, leading to a more fulfilling existence.
