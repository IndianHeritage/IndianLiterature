युवं ह स्थो भिषजाः भेषजैः अथो ह स्थो रथ्या राथ्यैभिः |अथो ह क्षत्रं अधि धत्त उग्रा यो वां हविष्मान मनसा ददाश ||
yuvaṁ ha stho bhiṣajā bheṣajebhir atho ha stho rathyā3 rāthyebhiḥ | atho ha kṣatram adhi dhattha ugrā yo vāṁ haviṣmān manasā dadāśa ||
  • yuvaṁ ha stho: This phrase can be translated as "young and vibrant." It signifies the importance of youth and vitality in life.
  • bhiṣajā bheṣajebhir: This refers to "healers and remedies." It emphasizes the role of healing and the importance of health in our lives.
  • atho ha stho rathyā3 rāthyebhiḥ: This means "and thus, may you be established in the paths." It suggests the significance of following the right path or direction in life.
  • atho ha kṣatram adhi dhattha: This translates to "and thus, may you hold the power." It indicates the importance of strength and authority in one's endeavors.
  • ugrā yo vāṁ haviṣmān manasā dadāśa: This can be interpreted as "the fierce one who grants you offerings with the mind." It highlights the connection between intention and the outcomes we seek.

In conclusion, this mantra from the RigVeda encapsulates the essence of youth, health, direction, strength, and intention. It serves as a reminder of the importance of maintaining vitality and health while navigating through life. The mantra encourages individuals to be mindful of their paths and to harness their inner strength to achieve their goals. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various contexts such as personal development, health and wellness practices, and leadership roles. By embodying the principles outlined in this mantra, one can strive for a balanced and purposeful life, ultimately leading to success and fulfillment.
