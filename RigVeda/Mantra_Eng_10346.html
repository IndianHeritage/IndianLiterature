एव तद इन्द्र इन्दुनā देवेषु चिद धारणायते महि त्यजः |क्रत्वा वयो वि तारण आयुः सुक्रतो क्रत्वायम अस्मद ā सुतः ||
evā tad indra indunā deveṣu cid dhārayāte mahi tyajaḥ | kratvā vayo vi tāry āyuḥ sukrato kratvāyam asmad ā sutaḥ ||
  • evā tad indra indunā: This phrase can be interpreted as "Thus, O Indra, with the moon." Here, Indra is a significant deity in Vedic literature, often associated with strength, rain, and the heavens. The moon symbolizes calmness and serenity, suggesting a balance between power and tranquility.
  • deveṣu cid dhārayāte: This translates to "He who holds the divine essence." It emphasizes the importance of maintaining a connection with the divine and the spiritual essence that sustains life and the universe.
  • mahi tyajaḥ: This means "Let go of the great." It suggests the idea of relinquishing attachments to material or worldly concerns, encouraging individuals to focus on higher spiritual goals.
  • kratvā vayo vi tāry āyuḥ: This can be understood as "By our actions, may we attain longevity." It highlights the significance of righteous actions and their impact on one's life span and overall well-being.
  • sukrato kratvāyam asmad ā sutaḥ: This translates to "By good deeds, may we be blessed with offspring." It underscores the importance of virtuous living and how it can lead to prosperity and the continuation of one's lineage.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda encapsulates the essence of living a life aligned with divine principles, emphasizing the balance between strength and serenity, the importance of righteous actions, and the pursuit of spiritual goals over material attachments. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various ways, such as in personal development, community service, and family life. By embodying these principles, individuals can foster a harmonious existence that not only benefits themselves but also contributes positively to society. The mantra serves as a reminder to prioritize spiritual growth and ethical living, ultimately leading to a fulfilling and meaningful life.
