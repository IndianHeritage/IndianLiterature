श्रावयेद अस्य कर्णा वाजयध्यै जुष्टाम् अनु प्र दिशम् मन्दयध्यै |उद्वावृषाणो राधसे तुविष्मान् करण न इन्द्रः सुतीरथाभयं च ||
śrāvayed asya karṇā vājayadhyai juṣṭām anu pra diśam mandayadhyai | udvāvṛṣāṇo rādhase tuviṣmān karan na indraḥ sutīrthābhayaṁ ca ||
  • śrāvayed asya karṇā: This phrase translates to "Let the ears be attentive." It emphasizes the importance of listening and being receptive to knowledge and wisdom. In real life, this can be seen in educational settings where active listening enhances learning outcomes.
  • vājayadhyai juṣṭām anu pra diśam mandayadhyai: This part means "Let us be endowed with strength and direct our thoughts towards the right path." It signifies the need for strength and guidance in our endeavors. For example, athletes often visualize their goals and focus their energy on achieving them, which aligns with this mantra's call for directed strength.
  • udvāvṛṣāṇo rādhase tuviṣmān: This translates to "May the powerful ones inspire us." It suggests seeking inspiration from those who are strong and capable. In a corporate environment, leaders often look up to successful figures in their industry for motivation and guidance, embodying this principle.
  • karan na indraḥ sutīrthābhayaṁ ca: This means "May Indra, the king of gods, grant us safety and prosperity." Indra represents strength and protection. In everyday life, people often seek blessings for safety and success in their ventures, whether it be starting a new business or embarking on a journey.

  • Conclusion: This mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking knowledge, strength, guidance, and protection. Its significance lies in its applicability to various aspects of life, from education to personal growth and professional success. By embodying the principles of attentive listening, directed strength, seeking inspiration, and requesting blessings for safety, individuals can navigate their lives more effectively and purposefully. In essence, it serves as a reminder to remain open to learning, focused on goals, inspired by others, and grounded in the pursuit of safety and prosperity.
