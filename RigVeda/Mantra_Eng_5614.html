ऋधक साऽ वो मरुतो दिद्युद अस्तु यद वा आगः पुरुषता कराम |मा वस तस्याम् अपि भूमाऽ यजत्रा अस्मे वो अस्तु सुमतिश्च निष्ठा ||
ṛdhak sā vo maruto didyud astu yad va āgaḥ puruṣatā karāma | mā vas tasyām api bhūmā yajatrā asme vo astu sumatiś caniṣṭhā ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ṛdhak: May it be fulfilled or accomplished.
    • sā: That.
    • vo: For you (plural).
    • maruto: O Maruts (the storm deities).
    • didyud: May it shine or illuminate.
    • astu: Let it be.
    • yad: Whatever.
    • va: Or.
    • āgaḥ: Coming or arriving.
    • puruṣatā: The essence of humanity or human qualities.
    • karāma: We perform or we do.
    • mā: Do not.
    • vas: Dwell or reside.
    • tasyām: In that.
    • api: Also.
    • bhūmā: Earth or the material world.
    • yajatrā: In the act of worship or sacrifice.
    • asme: We are.
    • vo: For you (plural).
    • astu: Let it be.
    • sumatiś: Good understanding or wisdom.
    • caniṣṭhā: The best or the highest.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to the Maruts, the storm deities, asking for their blessings and illumination in our lives.
    • It emphasizes the importance of human qualities and actions, suggesting that our endeavors should be aligned with wisdom and understanding.
    • The phrase "mā vas tasyām api bhūmā" indicates a desire to not be confined to the material world, urging a higher purpose in our actions.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, this mantra can be applied when teams seek clarity and guidance in their projects, aiming for successful outcomes that benefit all stakeholders.
    • In personal life, individuals may recite this mantra during times of decision-making, seeking wisdom and illumination to guide their choices.
    • In community gatherings, it can serve as a reminder to work together harmoniously, focusing on collective well-being rather than individual gain.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine guidance and the pursuit of wisdom in our actions.
    • It encourages individuals and communities to strive for higher ideals, fostering a sense of unity and purpose.
    • In real-life scenarios, it serves as a reminder to seek clarity, act with integrity, and prioritize collective well-being over material pursuits.
