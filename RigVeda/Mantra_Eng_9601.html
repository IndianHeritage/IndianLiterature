आ व ऋञ्जस उर्जां व्युष्टिष्व इन्द्रं मरुतो रोडसी अनक्तन |उभे यथा नो अहनी सचाभुवां सदा-सदो वरिवस्यात उद्भिदा ||
ā va ṛñjasa ūrjāṁ vyuṣṭiṣv indram maruto rodasī anaktana | ubhe yathā no ahanī sacābhuvā sadaḥ-sado varivasyāta udbhidā ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā: from
    • va: and
    • ṛñjasa: of the shining, radiant
    • ūrjāṁ: energy, strength
    • vyuṣṭiṣv: in the realms of the dawn
    • indram: Indra, the king of gods
    • maruto: the Maruts, storm deities
    • rodasī: in the two worlds (heavens and earth)
    • anaktana: not yet having been born
    • ubhe: both
    • yathā: as
    • no: for us
    • ahanī: the two days (or cycles)
    • sacābhuvā: having been born with truth
    • sadaḥ-sado: always, ever
    • varivasyāta: may it be favorable
    • udbhidā: may it arise
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the radiant energy and strength from the divine realms, particularly from Indra and the Maruts, who are associated with storms and vitality.
    • The phrase "in the realms of the dawn" signifies new beginnings and the potential for growth and enlightenment.
    • The mention of "both worlds" refers to the interconnectedness of the physical and spiritual realms, emphasizing the importance of balance in life.
    • The mantra seeks blessings for favorable outcomes and the emergence of truth and strength in our lives.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a challenging situation, such as starting a new business, one might recite this mantra to invoke the energy and support of divine forces, seeking guidance and strength.
    • During times of personal transformation, like moving to a new city or beginning a new job, this mantra can serve as a reminder to embrace new beginnings with positivity and strength.
    • In a community setting, such as during a festival or gathering, this mantra can be chanted to foster unity and collective strength among participants, invoking blessings for harmony and success.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine energy and strength, encouraging individuals to seek balance and truth in their lives.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal challenges to communal gatherings, serving as a source of inspiration and support.
    • By invoking the blessings of Indra and the Maruts, one can cultivate resilience and positivity, essential for navigating the complexities of life.
