इमा उ त्वा पुरुषाक प्रयज्यो जरतारो अभ्यर्चन्त्यर्कैः |श्रुधी हवम आ हुवतो हुवानो न त्वावाम् अन्यो अमृत त्वद अस्ति ||
ima u tvā puruśāka prayajyo jaritāro abhy arcanty arkaiḥ | śrudhī havam ā huvato huvāno na tvāvām̐ anyo amṛta tvad asti ||
  • Translation: "These offerings, O Purusha, are made to you; the singers, the seers, invoke you with their hymns. Listen to our call, O Immortal One, for there is none other like you."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • ima: These
    • u: O
    • tvā: you
    • puruśāka: O Purusha (the cosmic being)
    • prayajyo: offerings
    • jaritāro: singers or seers
    • abhy: towards
    • arcanty: invoke or worship
    • arkaiḥ: hymns
    • śrudhī: listen
    • havam: our call
    • ā huvato: we invoke
    • huvāno: O Immortal One
    • na: not
    • tvāvām̐: like you
    • anyo: another
    • amṛta: immortal
    • tvad: you
    • asti: there is
  • Contextual Significance:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of divine connection and the act of worship. It reflects the belief that offerings and hymns are a means to connect with the divine essence of the universe.
    • The term "Purusha" represents the cosmic spirit or the universal consciousness, which is a central theme in many spiritual traditions, symbolizing the interconnectedness of all beings.
    • The invocation of the "Immortal One" signifies the quest for eternal truth and wisdom, which is a common pursuit in various philosophical and spiritual practices.
  • Real-Life Application:
    • In daily life, this mantra can inspire individuals to seek a deeper connection with their own purpose and the universe. For example, during meditation or prayer, one might reflect on their intentions and the greater cosmic order.
    • In community gatherings or rituals, this mantra can serve as a reminder of the collective effort to honor the divine, fostering unity and shared purpose among participants.
    • In moments of decision-making or seeking guidance, individuals can invoke the essence of this mantra to remind themselves of the importance of listening to their inner voice and the wisdom of the universe.
  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of devotion, the pursuit of immortality through spiritual connection, and the recognition of the divine presence in all aspects of life. Its significance lies in its ability to inspire individuals to seek harmony with the universe, fostering a sense of belonging and purpose in the grand tapestry of existence.
