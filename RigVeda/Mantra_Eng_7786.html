अभि प्रियाऽदिवस पदम् अध्वर्युभिर् गुहा हितम् |सूरः पश्यति चक्षसा ||
abhi priyā divas padam adhvaryubhir guhā hitam | sūraḥ paśyati cakṣasā ||
  • Mantra: "abhi priyā divas padam adhvaryubhir guhā hitam | sūraḥ paśyati cakṣasā ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • abhi: towards, in the direction of
    • priyā: beloved, dear
    • divas: day, sky, or heaven
    • padam: place, position, or goal
    • adhvaryubhir: by the priests (adhvaryus) who perform rituals
    • guhā: cave, secret place, or hidden
    • hitam: beneficial, good, or auspicious
    • sūraḥ: the brave, the strong, or the hero
    • paśyati: sees or perceives
    • cakṣasā: with the eyes or vision
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of a beloved place in the sky or heaven that is reached through the rituals performed by priests.
    • The "guhā hitam" suggests a hidden or secret place that is beneficial, indicating that there are deeper truths or insights to be discovered.
    • The "sūraḥ" refers to those who are brave or heroic, implying that it is the courageous who can perceive these hidden truths.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a personal context, this mantra can be related to the pursuit of one's dreams or goals. Just as the priests perform rituals to reach a divine state, individuals must work diligently and courageously to achieve their aspirations.
    • In a broader societal context, it can be seen in the efforts of leaders or activists who strive to uncover hidden injustices and work towards a better society, embodying the qualities of the "sūraḥ."
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its encouragement to seek out and recognize the hidden truths in life, which often require courage and effort to uncover.
    • It teaches us that through dedication and bravery, we can reach our goals and discover beneficial insights that may not be immediately visible.
    • This mantra is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal growth to social change, reminding us of the importance of perseverance and vision in our journeys.
