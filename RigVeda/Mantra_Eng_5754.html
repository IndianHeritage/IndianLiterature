मन्त्र: एते त्ये भानवो दर्शतायाश्चित्रा उषसो अमृतासा आगुः |जनयन्तो दैव्याणि व्रतान्यापृणन्तो अन्तरिक्षा व्य अस्तुः ||
ete tye bhānavo darśatāyāś citrā uṣaso amṛtāsa āguḥ | janayanto daivyāni vratāny āpṛṇanto antarikṣā vy asthuḥ ||
  • Translation: "These radiant ones, the beautiful dawns, have come forth, bringing immortality. They, who generate divine vows, have filled the atmosphere with their presence."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • ete tye bhānavo: "These radiant ones" - Refers to the celestial beings or deities that illuminate the world.
    • darśatāyāś: "have come forth" - Indicates the arrival or manifestation of these divine entities.
    • citrā uṣaso: "beautiful dawns" - Symbolizes new beginnings, hope, and the beauty of nature.
    • amṛtāsa āguḥ: "bringing immortality" - Suggests the eternal nature of the divine and the blessings they bestow.
    • janayanto: "who generate" - Refers to the creative power of these beings.
    • daivyāni vratāny: "divine vows" - Represents sacred commitments or principles that guide human behavior.
    • āpṛṇanto: "have filled" - Indicates the act of enriching or saturating the environment.
    • antarikṣā vy asthuḥ: "the atmosphere with their presence" - Emphasizes the omnipresence of these divine forces in the universe.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • The concept of dawn as a new beginning can be seen in daily life when people start fresh each day, setting new goals and intentions.
    • In various cultures, the arrival of dawn is celebrated as a time of renewal, such as in festivals that honor the sun or light.
    • The idea of divine vows can be related to personal commitments, such as marriage vows or promises made to oneself to live a better life.
    • Environmental awareness can be linked to the idea of filling the atmosphere with positive energy, encouraging actions that promote sustainability and harmony with nature.
  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of divine presence in our lives, symbolizing hope, renewal, and the importance of sacred commitments. In real life, it encourages individuals to embrace new beginnings, uphold their promises, and recognize the beauty and divinity in the world around them. By acknowledging these principles, one can cultivate a more meaningful and harmonious existence, fostering a connection with the divine and the universe.
