यो राजभ्य ऋतनिभ्यो ददाश यं वर्धयन्ति पुष्टयश च नित्याः |स रेवान याति प्रथमो रथेन वसुदावा विदथेṣu प्रशस्तः ||
yo rājabhya ṛtanibhyo dadāśa yaṁ vardhayanti puṣṭayaś ca nityāḥ | sa revān yāti prathamo rathena vasudāvā vidatheṣu praśastaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yo: who
    • rājabhya: to the kings
    • ṛtanibhyo: to those who uphold truth
    • dadāśa: gives
    • yaṁ: whom
    • vardhayanti: nourish or increase
    • puṣṭayaś: with prosperity
    • ca: and
    • nityāḥ: eternal or everlasting
    • sa: he
    • revān: is the one who shines
    • yāti: goes
    • prathamo: first
    • rathena: by chariot
    • vasudāvā: to the land of wealth
    • vidatheṣu: in the assemblies
    • praśastaḥ: praised or honored
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about a person who is revered by kings and those who uphold truth.
    • It emphasizes the importance of nourishing and increasing prosperity, suggesting that such actions are eternal.
    • The individual described is portrayed as shining and is the first to travel by chariot to a land of wealth, indicating leadership and success.
    • The mention of assemblies suggests that this person is honored in social and communal gatherings.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern society, leaders who promote truth and integrity, such as activists or ethical business leaders, can be seen as embodying the qualities described in this mantra.
    • Successful entrepreneurs who create wealth not just for themselves but also for their communities reflect the essence of nourishing prosperity.
    • In political contexts, leaders who prioritize the welfare of their citizens and uphold justice can be likened to the revered figure in this mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the value of truth, prosperity, and leadership in society.
    • It serves as a reminder that those who uphold these values are honored and successful, both in their personal lives and in the broader community.
    • In real life, it encourages individuals to strive for integrity and to contribute positively to society, thereby creating a legacy that is respected and celebrated.
