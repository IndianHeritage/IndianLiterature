आ तु सुशिप्र दम्पते रथं तिष्ठा हिरण्ययम् |अध द्युक्षं सचेवहि सहस्रपादम अरुषं स्वस्तिगाम अनहसम् ||
ā tū suśipra dampate rathaṁ tiṣṭhā hiraṇyayam | adha dyukṣaṁ sacevahi sahasrapādam aruṣaṁ svastigām anehasam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā: From
    • tū: You
    • suśipra: Well-prepared or auspicious
    • dampate: O Lord or Master
    • rathaṁ: Chariot
    • tiṣṭhā: Stand or remain
    • hiraṇyayam: Golden or prosperous
    • adha: Below or beneath
    • dyukṣaṁ: Bright or shining
    • sacevahi: May you be pleased
    • sahasrapādam: With a thousand feet
    • aruṣaṁ: Reddish or radiant
    • svastigām: Leading to well-being
    • anehasam: Without darkness or obstacles
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to a divine entity, asking for a well-prepared and auspicious chariot to stand firm and lead towards prosperity.
    • The mention of "hiraṇyayam" signifies a desire for wealth and abundance, while "sahasrapādam" suggests a journey that is vast and full of opportunities.
    • The phrase "svastigām anehasam" emphasizes the importance of a path that is free from obstacles, leading to well-being and success.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be applied when starting a new venture. Entrepreneurs often seek a solid foundation (the chariot) that is well-prepared to navigate the challenges of the market.
    • In personal life, individuals may recite this mantra when embarking on a new journey, such as marriage or parenthood, wishing for a prosperous and obstacle-free path.
    • In education, students may invoke this mantra before exams or important presentations, hoping for clarity and success in their endeavors.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its invocation for strength, prosperity, and a clear path in life.
    • It serves as a reminder to prepare well for any journey, whether it be personal, professional, or spiritual.
    • By focusing on the qualities of auspiciousness and abundance, individuals can align their intentions with their actions, leading to a fulfilling and successful life.
