अग्निम् ईळेन्यं कविं घृतपृष्ठं सपर्यत | वेतु मे श्रिणवद् धवम् ||
agnim īḻenyaṁ kaviṁ ghṛtapṛṣṭhaṁ saparyata | vetu me śṛṇavad dhavam ||
  • Agnim: This word refers to "Agni," the fire god in Vedic literature. Fire symbolizes purity, transformation, and energy.
  • īḻenyaṁ: This term can be interpreted as "the one who is invoked" or "the one who is worshipped." It signifies the importance of reverence in spiritual practices.
  • Kaviṁ: This means "the seer" or "the wise one." It represents knowledge, insight, and the ability to perceive deeper truths.
  • Ghṛtapṛṣṭhaṁ: This translates to "having a ghee-covered back." Ghee (clarified butter) is a symbol of nourishment and purity, indicating that the offerings made to Agni are pure and sacred.
  • Saparyata: This means "to be worshipped" or "to be honored." It emphasizes the act of devotion and the importance of rituals in connecting with the divine.
  • Vetu: This word means "to know" or "to understand." It signifies the quest for knowledge and wisdom.
  • Me: This translates to "my" or "to me," indicating a personal connection and the desire for divine guidance.
  • Śṛṇavad: This means "hearing" or "listening." It emphasizes the importance of being receptive to divine messages and wisdom.
  • Dhavam: This can be interpreted as "the one who is swift" or "the one who moves quickly." It signifies the dynamic nature of knowledge and the rapid changes in life.

Conclusion: The mantra "agnim īḻenyaṁ kaviṁ ghṛtapṛṣṭhaṁ saparyata | vetu me śṛṇavad dhavam ||" encapsulates the essence of seeking divine knowledge and guidance through the worship of Agni. In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as during rituals, meditation, or moments of reflection when one seeks clarity and insight. It reminds us of the importance of reverence, the pursuit of wisdom, and the transformative power of fire, both literally and metaphorically. By invoking Agni, we acknowledge the need for purity in our intentions and actions, fostering a deeper connection with the divine and the universe around us.
