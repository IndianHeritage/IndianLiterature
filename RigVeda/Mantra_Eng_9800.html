पुरुष एवेदं सर्वं यद्भूतं यच्च भव्यम् |उतामृतत्वस्येशानो यदन्नेनातिरोहति ||
puruṣa evedaṁ sarvaṁ yad bhūtaṁ yac ca bhavyam | utāmṛtatvasyeśāno yad annenātirohati ||
  • Puruṣa: This term refers to the cosmic being or the universal spirit. In a real-life context, it can be understood as the essence of humanity or the collective consciousness that connects all individuals.
  • evedaṁ sarvaṁ: This phrase translates to "this is all that exists." It emphasizes the idea that everything in the universe is interconnected and part of a greater whole. For example, in environmental conservation, recognizing that all living beings and ecosystems are interdependent reflects this concept.
  • yad bhūtaṁ yac ca bhavyam: This means "whatever has been and whatever is to be." It highlights the continuity of existence, encompassing the past, present, and future. In personal growth, understanding our past experiences shapes our present actions and future aspirations.
  • utāmṛtatvasyeśāno: This phrase can be interpreted as "the lord of immortality." It signifies the eternal aspect of existence, suggesting that while physical forms may perish, the essence or spirit remains. In spiritual practices, this can be seen in the belief of life after death or the continuity of the soul.
  • yad annenātirohati: This translates to "that which is nourished by food." It points to the idea that physical sustenance is essential for life. In a practical sense, this can relate to the importance of nutrition and how it affects our health and well-being.
  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates profound philosophical concepts about existence, interconnectedness, and the eternal nature of the spirit. Its significance lies in its application to various aspects of life, such as personal development, environmental awareness, and spiritual understanding. By recognizing our connection to the universe and the importance of nourishment—both physical and spiritual—we can lead more fulfilling and conscious lives. This mantra encourages us to reflect on our place in the cosmos and the impact of our actions on the world around us.
