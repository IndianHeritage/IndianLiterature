अच्छा नो याह्य आ वहाभि प्रयांसी वीतये |आ देवान सोमपीतये ||
acchā no yāhy ā vahābhi prayāṁsi vītaye | ā devān somapītaye ||
  • Mantra: "acchā no yāhy ā vahābhi prayāṁsi vītaye | ā devān somapītaye ||"
  • Translation: "May we be led by auspicious paths, and may we attain the blessings of the gods through the offering of Soma."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • acchā: auspicious, good, or favorable.
    • no: our, us.
    • yāhy: may we be led or guided.
    • ā vahābhi: by auspicious paths or ways.
    • prayāṁsi: paths or journeys.
    • vītaye: for the attainment or realization.
    • ā: to, towards.
    • devān: gods or divine beings.
    • somapītaye: through the offering of Soma, a sacred drink in Vedic rituals.
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of being guided on a favorable path in life. Just as a traveler seeks the best route to reach their destination, individuals are encouraged to seek auspicious guidance in their endeavors.
    • The reference to "Soma" highlights the significance of rituals and offerings in connecting with the divine. In real life, this can be likened to the practice of gratitude and seeking blessings through various means, such as prayer, meditation, or acts of kindness.
    • In a practical sense, this mantra can be applied when making important decisions, such as choosing a career path, entering a new relationship, or embarking on a significant project. Seeking guidance from mentors, spiritual leaders, or even through self-reflection can be seen as a modern interpretation of "being led by auspicious paths."
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine guidance and the pursuit of favorable outcomes in life. It serves as a reminder to seek positivity and blessings in our actions.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra encourages individuals to reflect on their choices, seek wisdom, and remain open to the guidance of higher powers or the universe.
    • Ultimately, it teaches that by aligning ourselves with auspicious paths and honoring the divine through our actions, we can achieve our goals and live a fulfilling life.
