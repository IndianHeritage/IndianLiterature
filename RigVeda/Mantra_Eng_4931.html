विष्वे देवाः मम श्रिण्वन्तु यज्ञियाः उभे रोधसि अपां नपाच च मन्मा |मा वो वचांसि परिचक्ष्याणि वोचं सुम्नेष्व इद वो अन्तमा मदेम ||
viśve devā mama śṛṇvantu yajñiyā ubhe rodasī apāṁ napāc ca manma | mā vo vacāṁsi paricakṣyāṇi vocaṁ sumneṣv id vo antamā madema ||
  • Translation: "May all the gods listen to my prayers; may both the heavens and the earth, and the waters, hear me. I do not wish to be deprived of your words; may I speak to you in a way that is pleasing, and may I attain the best of what you have to offer."
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • viśve devā: All gods
    • mama: my
    • śṛṇvantu: listen
    • yajñiyā: in the context of sacrifice or worship
    • ubhe: both
    • rodasī: heavens and earth
    • apāṁ: of the waters
    • napāc: hear me
    • ca: and
    • manma: my thoughts
    • mā: do not
    • vo: your
    • vacāṁsi: words
    • paricakṣyāṇi: be deprived
    • vocaṁ: I speak
    • sumneṣv: in a pleasing manner
    • id: this
    • vo: your
    • antamā: best
    • madema: may I attain
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community gathering, when people come together to celebrate a festival, they often invoke the blessings of deities, similar to how the mantra calls upon the gods to listen to prayers.
    • During a wedding ceremony, the couple may seek the blessings of both families and the divine, echoing the sentiment of wanting to be heard and understood by all present.
    • In a corporate setting, when a team presents a project, they hope for the support and understanding of their superiors, akin to asking for the gods' attention and favor.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its universal appeal for divine attention and support. It emphasizes the importance of communication, both with the divine and within our communities. In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios where we seek understanding, support, and blessings from others, whether in personal relationships, community events, or professional endeavors. It serves as a reminder that our words and intentions should be directed towards fostering harmony and connection with the world around us.
