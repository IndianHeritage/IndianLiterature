अस्माकं अग्नि मगवत्सु दीदिह्य अध श्वसीवान वृषभो दामूनाः |अवाश्य शिशुमतीर अदीदेर वर्मेव युत्सु परिजर्भुराणः ||
asmākam agne maghavatsu dīdihy adha śvasīvān vṛṣabho damūnāḥ | avāsyā śiśumatīr adīder varmeva yutsu parijarbhurāṇaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • asmākam: our
    • agne: O Agni (the fire god)
    • maghavatsu: the powerful one
    • dīdihy: may you shine
    • adha: downwards
    • śvasīvān: the one who breathes
    • vṛṣabho: the bull, symbolizing strength
    • damūnāḥ: with restraint
    • avāsyā: may you protect
    • śiśumatīr: the one who is auspicious
    • adīder: may you grant
    • varmeva: the best of blessings
    • yutsu: in the battles
    • parijarbhurāṇaḥ: may you be victorious
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Agni, the fire god, who is often seen as a mediator between humans and the divine.
    • It emphasizes the qualities of strength, power, and protection, invoking Agni to shine brightly and provide blessings.
    • The reference to "the bull" symbolizes not just physical strength but also the idea of resilience and stability in life.
    • The mantra calls for Agni to protect and bless us, especially in challenging situations, akin to how fire provides warmth and light in darkness.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In times of personal struggle, such as facing a difficult decision or a challenging situation at work, one might recite this mantra to seek strength and clarity.
    • During significant life events, like weddings or new beginnings, invoking Agni can symbolize the purification of intentions and the strength of the union.
    • In a community setting, such as during festivals or rituals, this mantra can be recited to foster unity and collective strength among participants.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its invocation of divine strength and protection, reminding us of the importance of resilience and support in our lives.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal challenges to communal gatherings, serving as a source of inspiration and empowerment.
    • Ultimately, it teaches us to seek the light of knowledge and strength in our endeavors, encouraging us to face life's battles with courage and grace.
