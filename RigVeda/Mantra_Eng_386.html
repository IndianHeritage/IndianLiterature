मन्त्र: निसर्वसेन इषुधीर्म् असक्त सम आर्यो गा अजति यस्य वष्टि |चोष्कूयमान इन्द्र भूरी वामम् मा पणिर भूः अस्मद अधि प्रवृद्ध ||
ni sarvasena iṣudhīm̐r asakta sam aryo gā ajati yasya vaṣṭi | coṣkūyamāṇa indra bhūri vāmam mā paṇir bhūr asmad adhi pravṛddha ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ni: down, away
    • sarvasena: with all forces
    • iṣudhīm̐r: the one who is skilled in archery or warfare
    • asakta: unattached, free from desire
    • sam: together, in harmony
    • aryo: noble, worthy
    • gā: to sing, to praise
    • ajati: moves, goes
    • yasya: whose
    • vaṣṭi: is present, is there
    • coṣkūyamāṇa: being stirred, agitated
    • indra: the king of gods, a powerful deity
    • bhūri: abundant, plentiful
    • vāmam: your, of you
    • mā: do not
    • paṇir: hand, power
    • bhūr: great, vast
    • asmad: our
    • adhi: upon, over
    • pravṛddha: increased, grown
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of a powerful force that is skilled and unattached, moving in harmony with noble intentions.
    • It emphasizes the presence of a divine force (Indra) that is abundant and protective, urging not to let our own power diminish.
    • The mantra can be seen as a call for strength and unity in the face of challenges, encouraging individuals to remain focused and not be swayed by distractions.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a team setting, this mantra can be applied when working on a project. Each member should contribute their skills (like the skilled archer) while remaining unattached to personal accolades, focusing instead on the collective goal.
    • In personal development, one can interpret this as a reminder to pursue goals with dedication and skill, while also maintaining a sense of detachment from the outcome, thus reducing stress and anxiety.
    • In leadership, a leader can embody the qualities of Indra by being abundant in support and guidance, ensuring that their team feels empowered and valued.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of skill, unity, and divine support in overcoming challenges.
    • It teaches us to harness our abilities while remaining humble and focused on the greater good.
    • In real life, applying the principles of this mantra can lead to personal growth, effective teamwork, and strong leadership, ultimately fostering a harmonious environment.
