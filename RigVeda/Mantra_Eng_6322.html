तूतुजानो महेमते 'श्वेभिः प्रुषिताप्सुभिः |आ याहि यज्ञम आशुभिः शम इद धि ते ||
tūtujāno mahemate 'śvebhiḥ pruṣitapsubhiḥ | ā yāhi yajñam āśubhiḥ śam id dhi te ||
  • tūtujāno: This word can be interpreted as "one who is swift" or "quick." In real life, this can refer to someone who is agile in thought and action, able to respond to situations promptly.
  • mahemate: This translates to "with great intellect" or "with wisdom." It emphasizes the importance of using one's intellect to navigate through life’s challenges.
  • 'śvebhiḥ: This term refers to "the white ones" or "the pure." In a broader sense, it can symbolize purity of thought and action, akin to having a clear conscience.
  • pruṣitapsubhiḥ: This can be understood as "with those who are endowed with strength." It suggests the importance of surrounding oneself with strong, capable individuals who can provide support and guidance.
  • ā yāhi: This phrase means "come here" or "approach." It signifies an invitation or a call to action, urging individuals to come together for a common purpose.
  • yajñam: This word translates to "sacrifice" or "offering." In many cultures, including Hinduism, sacrifice is seen as a way to connect with the divine and to contribute to the greater good.
  • āśubhiḥ: This means "with auspiciousness" or "with good intentions." It highlights the importance of performing actions with a positive mindset and for beneficial outcomes.
  • śam id dhi te: This phrase translates to "peace is indeed yours." It conveys a message of assurance that peace and tranquility will be granted to those who follow the path of righteousness and wisdom.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of wisdom, purity, strength, and collective action in achieving peace and fulfillment in life. It encourages individuals to act swiftly and wisely, surround themselves with capable allies, and engage in selfless acts for the greater good. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various contexts such as teamwork in professional settings, community service, and personal relationships, where collaboration and positive intentions lead to harmonious outcomes. By embodying these principles, individuals can foster a peaceful and prosperous environment for themselves and those around them.
