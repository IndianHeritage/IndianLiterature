अधि वां स्थाम वन्धुरे रथे दश्रां हिरण्यये |पथेव यन्ताव अनुशासता रजोऽञ्जसा शासता राजाḥ ||
adhi vāṁ sthāma vandhure rathe dasrā hiraṇyaye | patheva yantāv anuśāsatā rajo 'ñjasā śāsatā rajaḥ ||
  • adhi vāṁ sthāma: This phrase can be translated as "we shall place ourselves" or "we shall establish ourselves." It signifies the intention to position oneself in a place of strength or stability.
  • vandhure rathe: "In the chariot of the strong" suggests a journey or a path taken with strength and support. The chariot symbolizes progress and movement in life.
  • dasrā hiraṇyaye: "For the golden one" refers to a goal or aspiration that is precious and valuable, akin to gold. It represents the pursuit of something significant in life.
  • patheva yantāv: This can be interpreted as "like the one who guides on the path." It emphasizes the importance of guidance and direction in our endeavors.
  • anuśāsatā: This word means "by the one who commands" or "by the one who instructs." It highlights the role of authority and wisdom in leading us towards our goals.
  • rajo 'ñjasā: "With ease and swiftness" suggests that the journey or the process should be smooth and efficient, indicating the importance of being adaptable and responsive.
  • śāsatā rajaḥ: This phrase can be understood as "the command of the mind" or "the control of the senses." It points to the necessity of self-discipline and mental clarity in achieving our objectives.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of establishing oneself firmly on a path of strength and purpose, guided by wisdom and authority. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios such as career development, personal growth, and leadership. For instance, when pursuing a career, one must position themselves strategically, seek guidance from mentors, and maintain discipline to achieve their goals. The mantra serves as a reminder that with the right mindset and support, one can navigate the challenges of life effectively and reach their aspirations.
