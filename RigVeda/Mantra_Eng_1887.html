ये चिद्धि पूर्व ऋतसाप आसन साकं देवैभिर अवदन्न ऋतानि |ते चिद अवासुर नह्य अन्तम आपुः सम ऊ नु पत्नीर वृषभिर जगम्युः ||
ye cid dhi pūrva ṛtasāpa āsan sākaṁ devebhir avadann ṛtāni | te cid avāsur nahy antam āpuḥ sam ū nu patnīr vṛṣabhir jagamyuḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ye: those who
    • cid: indeed
    • dhi: intellect or understanding
    • pūrva: ancient or prior
    • ṛtasāpa: the truth or the order of the universe
    • āsan: have established
    • sākaṁ: together with
    • devebhir: the gods
    • avadann: have spoken or declared
    • ṛtāni: truths or realities
    • te: they
    • cid: indeed
    • avāsur: have attained
    • nahy: not
    • antam: the end
    • āpuḥ: they have reached
    • sam: together
    • ū: indeed
    • nu: now
    • patnīr: the wives or partners
    • vṛṣabhir: with strength or power
    • jagamyuḥ: they have moved or traveled
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of those who have established the ancient truths of the universe in collaboration with the divine beings (gods).
    • It emphasizes the importance of understanding and adhering to these truths, which are timeless and universal.
    • The phrase "they have reached not the end" suggests that the pursuit of truth and understanding is an ongoing journey, not a destination.
    • The mention of "wives" and "strength" indicates the importance of relationships and support systems in this journey of truth-seeking.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern life, this mantra can be related to the pursuit of knowledge and wisdom in various fields such as science, philosophy, and spirituality.
    • For instance, scientists working together to uncover the mysteries of the universe embody the spirit of collaboration with the divine order of nature.
    • In personal relationships, the support of partners and family can be crucial in one’s journey towards self-discovery and understanding of deeper truths.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its reminder that the quest for truth is a collective and continuous effort.
    • It encourages individuals to seek knowledge, collaborate with others, and remain steadfast in their pursuit of understanding.
    • In real life, this can be applied in various contexts, from academic pursuits to personal growth, highlighting the interconnectedness of all beings in the journey of life.
