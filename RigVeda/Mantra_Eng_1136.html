तद इन्द्र प्रेवा वीर्यं चकर्थ यत ससन्तं वज्रेणाबोधयोऽहिम् |अनु त्वा पत्नीर हृषितं वयश्च विश्वे देवासो अमदन्न अनु त्वा ||
tad indra preva vīryaṁ cakartha yat sasantaṁ vajreṇābodhayo 'him | anu tvā patnīr hṛṣitaṁ vayaś ca viśve devāso amadann anu tvā ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • tad: that
    • indra: Indra, the king of gods
    • preva: who is powerful
    • vīryaṁ: strength or valor
    • cakartha: has accomplished
    • yat: which
    • sasantaṁ: with great effort
    • vajreṇā: with the thunderbolt
    • abodhayo: awaken
    • 'him: the cold or the frost
    • anu: after
    • tvā: you
    • patnīr: wives or partners
    • hṛṣitaṁ: joyful
    • vayaś: our age or life
    • ca: and
    • viśve: all
    • devāso: the gods
    • amadann: have given nectar
    • anu: after
    • tvā: you
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes Indra, the god of rain and thunderstorms, emphasizing his strength and valor.
    • It speaks of the accomplishments achieved through great effort, symbolized by the thunderbolt, which represents power and the ability to overcome obstacles.
    • The mention of 'him' refers to the cold or frost, indicating the challenges one faces in life.
    • It highlights the importance of joy and support from partners and the community, as the gods provide blessings and nourishment (nectar) to all.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, this mantra can be related to teamwork and collaboration, where the strength of the group (like Indra) helps overcome challenges.
    • In personal life, it can signify the importance of family and friends who support us during tough times, bringing joy and strength.
    • In environmental contexts, it can be seen as a call to respect nature's power, like storms, and to work together to mitigate climate challenges.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of strength, community, and the divine support we receive in our endeavors.
    • It teaches us to recognize the power within ourselves and the collective, encouraging us to face challenges with valor and joy.
    • In real life, it serves as a reminder to appreciate the support of others and to strive for accomplishments through unity and effort.
