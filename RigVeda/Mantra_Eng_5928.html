प्र तत्ते अद्य शिपिविष्ट नामार्यः शंसामि वयुनानि विद्वान् | तं त्वा गृणामि तवसम अतव्यां क्षयन्तम अस्य रजसः पराके ||
pra tat te adya śipiviṣṭa nāmāryaḥ śaṁsāmi vayunāni vidvān | taṁ tvā gṛṇāmi tavasam atavyān kṣayantam asya rajasaḥ parāke ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • pra: forward, towards
    • tat: that
    • te: to you
    • adya: today, now
    • śipiviṣṭa: one who is seated, established
    • nāmāryaḥ: the noble name
    • śaṁsāmi: I praise, I extol
    • vayunāni: the powers, the strengths
    • vidvān: the wise, the knowledgeable
    • taṁ: him, that
    • tvā: you
    • gṛṇāmi: I sing, I chant
    • tavasam: your essence, your nature
    • atavyān: the one who is beyond, transcendent
    • kṣayantam: the one who diminishes, reduces
    • asya: of this
    • rajasaḥ: of the material, of the worldly
    • parāke: in the higher realms, in the supreme
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer that acknowledges the divine presence and seeks blessings for wisdom and strength.
    • The phrase "pra tat te adya" signifies a call to the divine to be present in the current moment, emphasizing the importance of the now.
    • By referring to the "nāmāryaḥ," it highlights the nobility and purity of the divine name, suggesting that invoking this name brings forth positive energies.
    • The term "vidvān" indicates the pursuit of knowledge and wisdom, which is essential in navigating life's challenges.
    • In the context of "kṣayantam asya rajasaḥ," it suggests a desire to transcend the material distractions of life, seeking a higher spiritual understanding.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a stressful work environment, one might recite this mantra to invoke calmness and clarity, focusing on the present moment to make better decisions.
    • During a significant life transition, such as a career change or moving to a new city, this mantra can serve as a reminder to seek wisdom and strength from the divine.
    • In moments of doubt or confusion, individuals can chant this mantra to connect with their inner wisdom and find guidance.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the importance of being present, seeking wisdom, and transcending material distractions.
    • Its significance lies in its ability to connect individuals with the divine, fostering a sense of peace and clarity in their lives.
    • In real-life scenarios, it can be applied in moments of stress, decision-making, and personal growth, making it a valuable tool for spiritual and emotional well-being.
