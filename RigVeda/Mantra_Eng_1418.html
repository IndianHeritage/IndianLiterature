मां पृणन्तो दुरितं एन आरन मां जारिषुः सूरयः सुव्रतासः |अन्यस् तेषां परिधिर अस्तु कश्चिद अपृणन्तम अभि सं यन्तु शोकाः ||
mā pṛṇanto duritam ena āran mā jāriṣuḥ sūrayaḥ suvratāsaḥ | anyas teṣām paridhir astu kaś cid apṛṇantam abhi saṁ yantu śokāḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • mā: do not
    • pṛṇanto: fill or burden
    • duritam: difficulties or misfortunes
    • ena: with this
    • āran: to be removed or taken away
    • mā: do not
    • jāriṣuḥ: in the sorrows or troubles
    • sūrayaḥ: the brave or noble ones
    • suvratāsaḥ: those with good vows or intentions
    • anyas: let there be another
    • teṣām: of them
    • paridhir: a covering or protection
    • astu: may it be
    • kaś cid: anyone
    • apṛṇantam: who does not fill or burden
    • abhi: towards
    • saṁ: together
    • yantu: may they go
    • śokāḥ: sorrows or griefs
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra expresses a plea for the removal of burdens and difficulties from the lives of noble individuals.
    • It emphasizes the importance of not allowing sorrows to overwhelm those who are brave and have good intentions.
    • The mantra suggests that there should be a protective covering for those who do not contribute to the burdens of others.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace, this mantra can be applied to create a supportive environment where colleagues help each other overcome challenges rather than adding to each other's stress.
    • In a family setting, it encourages members to share responsibilities and not let one person bear the weight of difficulties alone.
    • In community service, it highlights the importance of uplifting those who are struggling, ensuring that their burdens are lightened by collective support.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for compassion and support among individuals.
    • It serves as a reminder to be mindful of the burdens we place on others and to actively work towards alleviating their sorrows.
    • In real life, applying the principles of this mantra can lead to stronger relationships, healthier communities, and a more supportive society.
