ये वावृधन्त पार्थिवा य उराव अन्तरिक्ष आ |वृजन वाऽ नदीनां सधस्थे वाऽ महो दिवः ||
ye vāvṛdhanta pārthivā ya urāv antarikṣa ā | vṛjane vā nadīnāṁ sadhasthe vā maho divaḥ ||
  • Translation: "Those who are expanding the earthly realms, those who are in the atmosphere, and those who are in the vastness of the sky, may they protect us from all that is harmful."

  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ye: Those who
    • vāvṛdhanta: are expanding or growing
    • pārthivā: earthly or terrestrial
    • ya: who
    • urāv: in the atmosphere
    • antarikṣa: space or sky
    • ā: may
    • vṛjane: protect or shield
    • vā: or
    • nadīnāṁ: of the rivers
    • sadhasthe: in the vastness
    • vā: or
    • maho: great
    • dvaḥ: of the heavens

  • Explanation:
    • This mantra speaks to the interconnectedness of various realms: the earth, the atmosphere, and the sky. It emphasizes the importance of growth and protection across these domains.
    • In real life, this can be seen in environmental conservation efforts. For example, initiatives aimed at protecting forests (earthly realms) also contribute to the health of the atmosphere and the overall ecosystem.
    • The mention of rivers signifies the importance of water bodies in sustaining life. Protecting rivers ensures the well-being of both terrestrial and atmospheric environments.
    • In a broader sense, this mantra can be applied to the idea of holistic development, where growth in one area (like technology or economy) should not come at the expense of another (like nature or community well-being).

  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for protection and growth across all realms of existence. It serves as a reminder that our actions should be mindful of their impact on the environment and society.
    • In today's world, where climate change and environmental degradation are pressing issues, this mantra encourages us to seek balance and harmony in our pursuits.
    • By invoking the essence of this mantra, individuals and communities can strive for sustainable practices that honor the interconnectedness of life.
