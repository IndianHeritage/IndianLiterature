एतत् त इन्द्र वीर्यं गीरभिर् गृणन्ति कारवः |ते स्तोभन्त ūrजम् आवन घृतश्चुतम् पौरासो नक्षन् धीतिभिः ||
etat ta indra vīryaṁ gīrbhir gṛṇanti kāravaḥ | te stobhanta ūrjam āvan ghṛtaścutam paurāso nakṣan dhītibhiḥ ||
  • Translation: "This strength of Indra is sung by the singers; they invoke the energy that is present in the ghee, which is poured forth by the people with wisdom."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • etat: This
    • ta: of that
    • indra: Indra, the king of gods, symbolizing strength and power
    • vīryaṁ: strength or valor
    • gīrbhir: by the songs or hymns
    • gṛṇanti: they sing or chant
    • kāravaḥ: those who invoke or call upon
    • te: they
    • stobhanta: they create a sound or vibration
    • ūrjam: energy or power
    • āvan: that is present or available
    • ghṛtaścutam: ghee that is poured forth
    • paurāso: by the people or community
    • nakṣan: with wisdom or intelligence
    • dhītibhiḥ: with understanding or insight
  • Contextual Explanation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of collective effort and the invocation of divine strength through song and wisdom.
    • Indra represents not just a deity but the embodiment of strength and valor that can be invoked through communal harmony and shared purpose.
    • The reference to ghee symbolizes nourishment and the essence of life, suggesting that the energy we invoke must be pure and beneficial.
    • Real-life example: In a community gathering, when people come together to celebrate or support a cause, they often sing songs or share stories that invoke a sense of unity and strength, much like the singers in the mantra.
  • Significance:
    • This mantra teaches us the power of collective action and the importance of invoking higher energies through positive expressions like music and wisdom.
    • It highlights that strength is not just an individual trait but a communal attribute that can be harnessed through shared beliefs and practices.
    • In modern life, this can be applied in various scenarios such as team-building exercises, community service, or any collaborative effort where the goal is to achieve something greater than individual capabilities.
