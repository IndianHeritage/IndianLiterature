यं त्वा जनासो अभि संचरन्ति गावा उष्णम इव व्रजं यविष्ठ |दूतो देवानाम् असि मार्त्यानाम् अन्तर महांश् चरसि रोचनena ||
yaṁ tvā janāso abhi saṁcaranti gāva uṣṇam iva vrajaṁ yaviṣṭha | dūto devānām asi martyānām antar mahām̐ś carasi rocanena ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yaṁ: whom
    • tvā: you
    • janāso: people
    • abhi: towards
    • saṁcaranti: move or travel
    • gāva: cows
    • uṣṇam: warm or hot
    • iva: like
    • vrajaṁ: the herd
    • yaviṣṭha: the best or most excellent
    • dūto: messenger
    • devānām: of the gods
    • asi: you are
    • martyānām: of mortals
    • antar: within
    • mahām̐ś: great or vast
    • carasi: you move
    • rocanena: with brilliance or light
  • Overall Meaning:
    • This mantra speaks of a divine presence that is revered by people, akin to how they would regard a warm and nurturing herd of cows.
    • The divine is described as a messenger of the gods, bridging the gap between the divine and the mortal realms.
    • It emphasizes the brilliance and light that this divine presence brings into the lives of mortals.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In many cultures, cows are seen as symbols of nourishment and prosperity. This mantra draws a parallel between the divine and the nurturing qualities of cows, suggesting that the divine provides warmth and sustenance to humanity.
    • In modern life, we often seek guidance and support from mentors or leaders who act as messengers of wisdom, much like the divine messenger described in the mantra.
    • The concept of light and brilliance can be related to moments of inspiration or enlightenment that individuals experience, guiding them through challenges.
  • Conclusion and Significance:
    • This mantra highlights the importance of recognizing and honoring the divine presence in our lives, which serves as a source of guidance and support.
    • It encourages individuals to seek out the nurturing aspects of life, whether through relationships, mentorship, or spiritual practices.
    • In a broader context, it reminds us of the interconnectedness of all beings and the importance of fostering a sense of community and support, much like a herd of cows that provides warmth and sustenance to one another.
