प्र देवं देववीतये भरता वसुवित्तमम् |आ स्वे योनौ नि शीदतु ||
pra devaṁ devavītaye bharatā vasuvittamam | ā sve yonau ni ṣīdatu ||
  • pra devaṁ devavītaye: This phrase can be translated as "to the divine, for the sake of the divine." Here, "pra" signifies a direction towards or an offering to the divine, while "devam" refers to the divine or gods. "Devavītaye" implies a purpose or intention related to the divine.
  • bharatā vasuvittamam: "Bharatā" refers to the people of Bharat (India), and "vasuvittamam" translates to "the best of wealth" or "the most prosperous." This suggests a connection between the divine and the prosperity of the people.
  • ā sve yonau ni ṣīdatu: This can be interpreted as "may we sit in our own place." "Ā sve" means "in our own," "yonau" refers to "place" or "source," and "ni ṣīdatu" means "may we sit" or "may we be established." This indicates a desire for stability and comfort in one's rightful place.

  • Significance: This mantra emphasizes the relationship between the divine and human prosperity. It calls for divine blessings to ensure that the people of Bharat are prosperous and secure in their rightful places.
  • Real-life Application: In contemporary life, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as:
    • Community Welfare: It can inspire community leaders to seek divine guidance in their efforts to improve the welfare of their communities, ensuring that everyone has access to resources and opportunities.
    • Personal Growth: Individuals can use this mantra as a reminder to seek divine support in their personal and professional endeavors, fostering a sense of purpose and direction.
    • Spiritual Practices: It can be incorporated into daily prayers or meditations, encouraging individuals to connect with the divine and seek blessings for prosperity and stability.

  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the interconnectedness between the divine and human existence. By invoking divine blessings for prosperity and stability, individuals and communities can strive for a harmonious and fulfilling life. It encourages a sense of responsibility towards oneself and others, fostering a collective effort towards achieving a prosperous society.
