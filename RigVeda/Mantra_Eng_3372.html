यत्र देवां ऋघायतो विश्वां आयुध्य एक इति |त्वं इन्द्र वनूम्र अहन ||
yatra devām̐ ṛghāyato viśvām̐ ayudhya eka it | tvam indra vanūm̐r ahan ||
  • Mantra: "yatra devām̐ ṛghāyato viśvām̐ ayudhya eka it | tvam indra vanūm̐r ahan ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yatra: where
    • devām̐: the gods
    • ṛghāyato: with great strength
    • viśvām̐: all beings
    • ayudhya: united
    • eka: one
    • it: thus
    • tvam: you
    • indra: Indra, the king of gods
    • vanūm̐r: the one who brings forth
    • ahan: day or light
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of a place where the gods, endowed with great strength, unite all beings as one.
    • It invokes Indra, the king of gods, who is seen as a powerful force that brings forth light and unity.
    • The essence of the mantra emphasizes the importance of unity and strength in the divine realm, which can be reflected in our daily lives.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community, when people come together for a common cause, they embody the spirit of this mantra. For instance, during natural disasters, communities unite to help those affected, showcasing strength and unity.
    • In workplaces, when teams collaborate effectively, they reflect the idea of being united as one, leading to greater productivity and success.
    • In personal relationships, fostering understanding and support among family and friends can create a harmonious environment, akin to the unity described in the mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for unity and strength among all beings, guided by divine forces.
    • It serves as a reminder that when we come together, we can achieve greater things, whether in our personal lives, communities, or workplaces.
    • By embodying the principles of this mantra, we can create a more harmonious and supportive environment in our daily interactions.
