नहि वा ऊतिः पृथनासु मर्धति यस्मा अराध्वं नरः |अभि वा आवर्त सुमतिर नवीयसी तुयं यात पिपीषवः ||
nahi va ūtiḥ pṛtanāsu mardhati yasmā arādhvaṁ naraḥ | abhi va āvart sumatir navīyasī tūyaṁ yāta pipīṣavaḥ ||
  • Translation: "Indeed, the one who does not strive in the battles of life, does not achieve success; for the one who is not diligent, the path remains untraveled. Therefore, let your intellect be directed towards new and fresh ideas, as the wise ones do."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • nahi: indeed, not
    • va: or
    • ūtiḥ: effort, striving
    • pṛtanāsu: in the battles, struggles
    • mardhati: achieves, succeeds
    • yasmā: because, for the one who
    • arādhvaṁ: not diligent, not striving
    • naraḥ: person, human
    • abhi: therefore, thus
    • va: or
    • āvart: direct, turn towards
    • sumatir: intellect, wisdom
    • navīyasī: new, fresh
    • tūyaṁ: ideas, thoughts
    • yāta: go, travel
    • pipīṣavaḥ: wise, knowledgeable
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • Education: A student who does not put in the effort to study and prepare for exams will likely not succeed. Conversely, those who work hard and seek new knowledge will excel.
    • Career: In the workplace, an employee who does not strive to improve their skills or take on challenges may find themselves stagnant, while those who embrace new ideas and work diligently will advance in their careers.
    • Personal Growth: Individuals who do not put effort into self-improvement or personal development may find their lives unfulfilling. In contrast, those who actively seek new experiences and knowledge often lead richer, more satisfying lives.
  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of effort and diligence in achieving success in various aspects of life. It serves as a reminder that without striving and a willingness to embrace new ideas, one may remain stagnant. This principle is applicable in education, career, and personal growth, encouraging individuals to actively engage in their pursuits and seek continuous improvement. By fostering a mindset of diligence and openness to new experiences, one can navigate the challenges of life more effectively and achieve greater fulfillment.
