वृषा ह्यसि भानुना द्युमन्तं त्वा हवामहे |पवमान स्वाध्यः ||
vṛṣā hy asi bhānunā dyumantaṁ tvā havāmahe | pavamāna svādhyaḥ ||
  • Mantra: "vṛṣā hy asi bhānunā dyumantaṁ tvā havāmahe | pavamāna svādhyaḥ ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • vṛṣā: Strong, powerful, or mighty.
    • hy: Indeed or certainly.
    • asi: You are.
    • bhānunā: With the light or radiance.
    • dyumantaṁ: Shining or brilliant.
    • tvā: You.
    • havāmahe: We invoke or we call upon.
    • pavamāna: The purifying or the sacred.
    • svādhyaḥ: Self-controlled or self-possessed.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a call to a powerful entity, acknowledging its strength and brilliance.
    • The phrase "vṛṣā hy asi" emphasizes the might and importance of the entity being invoked.
    • By referring to the entity as "bhānunā dyumantaṁ," it highlights the radiance and purity associated with it.
    • The invocation "tvā havāmahe" signifies a deep respect and a plea for guidance or assistance.
    • The concluding part "pavamāna svādhyaḥ" suggests a focus on purity and self-control, indicating that the invocation is not just for external help but also for internal strength and discipline.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In moments of personal struggle, individuals often seek strength and guidance from a higher power, similar to how this mantra invokes a powerful entity.
    • In leadership, the qualities of strength and radiance are essential. Leaders who embody these traits can inspire and guide their teams effectively.
    • Practicing self-control and discipline in daily life, such as in diet or exercise, reflects the essence of "svādhyaḥ," promoting personal growth and well-being.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of invoking strength and purity in our lives.
    • It encourages individuals to seek guidance from powerful forces while also emphasizing the need for self-discipline and inner strength.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in personal development, leadership, and spiritual practices, fostering a balanced approach to life’s challenges.
