ऋषिः हि पूर्वजाः अस्य एक ईशान ओजसा | इन्द्र चोष्कूयसे वसु ||
ṛṣir hi pūrvajā asy eka īśāna ojasā | indra coṣkūyase vasu ||
  • ṛṣir hi pūrvajā asy eka īśāna ojasā
    • ṛṣir: This word refers to a sage or seer, someone who has profound knowledge and insight.
    • hi: This is an emphatic particle, often translated as "indeed" or "certainly," emphasizing the truth of the statement.
    • pūrvajā: Meaning "born before" or "ancient," it signifies the timeless wisdom of the sages.
    • asy: This means "of this" or "of this mantra," indicating the context of the wisdom being discussed.
    • eka: Translated as "one," it suggests unity or singularity in purpose or essence.
    • īśāna: This term means "lord" or "ruler," often referring to a divine power or authority.
    • ojasā: This word signifies "energy" or "vitality," indicating the strength and power that comes from divine wisdom.
  • indra coṣkūyase vasu
    • indra: Referring to the king of gods in Vedic tradition, Indra symbolizes strength, power, and the ability to overcome obstacles.
    • coṣkūyase: This term can be interpreted as "you who are powerful" or "you who are capable," addressing Indra's might.
    • vasu: Meaning "wealth" or "prosperity," it signifies the abundance that comes from divine blessings.

Conclusion:

This mantra emphasizes the importance of ancient wisdom and the divine energy that comes from it. It highlights the role of sages in imparting knowledge and the power of divine forces like Indra in bestowing prosperity and strength. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios such as:

  • Seeking guidance from wise mentors or elders who possess knowledge and experience.
  • Recognizing the importance of unity and strength in overcoming challenges.
  • Understanding that true wealth comes not just from material possessions but from spiritual and intellectual growth.

Overall, the mantra serves as a reminder to seek wisdom, embrace unity, and acknowledge the divine forces that guide us towards prosperity and strength in our lives.
