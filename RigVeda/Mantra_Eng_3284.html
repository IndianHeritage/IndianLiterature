आ न इन्द्रो हरिभिर यात्व अच्छार्वाचीनो 'वसे राधसे च |तिष्ठाति वज्री मगवाऽ विरप्शीमं यज्ञं अनु नो वाजसातौ ||
ā na indro haribhir yātv acchārvācīno 'vase rādhase ca | tiṣṭhāti vajrī maghavā virapśīmaṁ yajñam anu no vājasātau ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā - towards
    • na - not
    • indro - Indra, the king of gods
    • haribhir - with the swift ones (horses)
    • yātv - may he go
    • acchārvācīno - with auspicious speech
    • 'vase - may he dwell
    • rādhase - in abundance
    • ca - and
    • tiṣṭhāti - may he stand
    • vajrī - the one with the thunderbolt
    • maghavā - the powerful one
    • virapśīmaṁ - may he protect
    • yajñam - the sacrifice
    • anu - after
    • no - our
    • vājasātau - in the pursuit of strength
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes Indra, the king of gods, asking him to come towards us with the swift horses, symbolizing speed and power.
    • The phrase "with auspicious speech" suggests the importance of positive communication and intentions in our endeavors.
    • Indra is asked to dwell in abundance, indicating a desire for prosperity and success in our actions.
    • The mention of "the one with the thunderbolt" refers to Indra's strength and ability to protect, emphasizing the need for divine support in our efforts.
    • Finally, the mantra highlights the significance of sacrifice (yajña) and the pursuit of strength, suggesting that true success comes from dedication and effort.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be applied when launching a new project. Invoking the qualities of speed, communication, and strength can lead to successful outcomes.
    • In personal relationships, the emphasis on auspicious speech can remind individuals to communicate positively, fostering better connections.
    • In community service, the idea of sacrifice can inspire individuals to contribute selflessly for the greater good, leading to collective strength and prosperity.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of divine support, positive communication, and the pursuit of strength through dedication and sacrifice.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, including personal growth, professional endeavors, and community service, reminding us that success is often a collective effort supported by divine grace.
