परिहृतद अनां जनो युष्मादत्तस्य वायति |देवा अदभ्रम आशा वो यं आदित्याऽहेतनानेहसो व ऊतयः सुतयः व ऊतयः ||
parihvṛted anā jano yuṣmādattasya vāyati | devā adabhram āśa vo yam ādityā ahetanānehaso va ūtayaḥ suūtayo va ūtayaḥ ||
  • Mantra: "parihvṛted anā jano yuṣmādattasya vāyati | devā adabhram āśa vo yam ādityā ahetanānehaso va ūtayaḥ suūtayo va ūtayaḥ ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • parihvṛted: Surrounded or enveloped
    • anā: Without
    • jano: People or beings
    • yuṣmād: From you
    • attasya: Of the one who is giving
    • vāyati: Flows or moves
    • devā: Gods or divine beings
    • adabhram: Without blemish or pure
    • āśa: Hope or desire
    • vo: For you
    • yam: Whom
    • ādityā: The sun or solar deities
    • ahetanānehaso: Without cause or reason
    • va: Or
    • ūtayaḥ: Rising or uplifting
    • suūtayo: Well-risen or auspicious
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the divine flow of energy and blessings that surround us.
    • The phrase "parihvṛted anā jano" suggests that even in the absence of people, divine energies are present and active.
    • The mention of "devā adabhram" emphasizes the purity and benevolence of divine forces, which are always available to guide and support us.
    • In real life, this can be likened to moments when we feel a sense of peace or inspiration, even when we are alone. For example, a person may feel a surge of creativity while working on a project in solitude, indicating the presence of divine inspiration.
    • The reference to "ādityā" connects to the sun, symbolizing enlightenment and clarity. Just as the sun rises each day, bringing light and warmth, so too do divine blessings rise to uplift us.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the omnipresence of divine energy and the importance of remaining open to receiving it, regardless of our circumstances.
    • It teaches us that even in solitude, we are never truly alone; divine forces are always at work, guiding us towards our highest potential.
    • In practical terms, this mantra can be applied in daily life by fostering a sense of mindfulness and gratitude, recognizing the blessings that surround us, and seeking inspiration in moments of quiet reflection.
    • Ultimately, it encourages us to trust in the divine flow of life and to remain receptive to the uplifting energies that can guide us through challenges and inspire our journeys.
