मन्त्र: उक्तेभिरर्वागवसे पुरूवासू अर्कैश्च निह्वयामहे |शाश्वत कण्वानां सदसि प्रिये हि कं सोमं पपतुर अश्विनाः ||
ukthebhir arvāg avase purūvasū arkaiś ca ni hvayāmahe | śaśvat kaṇvānāṁ sadasi priye hi kaṁ somam papathur aśvinā ||
  • ukthebhir arvāg avase purūvasū arkaiś ca ni hvayāmahe: This line can be translated as "With our hymns, we invoke the Purus, the great beings, with our prayers." Here, "ukthebhir" refers to hymns or praises, "arvāg" signifies the great beings or divine entities, and "purūvasū" indicates the essence of the universe or the cosmic order. This invocation emphasizes the importance of prayer and the collective effort to connect with the divine.
  • śaśvat kaṇvānāṁ sadasi priye hi kaṁ somam papathur aśvinā: This line translates to "In the eternal assembly, who among the beloved ones has brought forth the Soma, the divine nectar, by the Ashvins?" "śaśvat" means eternal, "kaṇvānāṁ" refers to the wise or learned, "sadasi" indicates the assembly or gathering, and "Soma" symbolizes the divine elixir that grants immortality and enlightenment. The Ashvins are twin deities associated with health and medicine, representing the healing aspects of life.

  • Significance: This mantra highlights the importance of collective worship and the pursuit of divine knowledge. It emphasizes the role of hymns and prayers in connecting with higher powers and the quest for enlightenment through divine blessings.
  • Real-life Application: In modern life, this mantra can be applied in various contexts such as:
    • Community Gatherings: Just as the mantra calls for collective invocation, community gatherings for spiritual or social causes can foster unity and shared purpose.
    • Health and Well-being: The reference to the Ashvins and Soma can inspire practices focused on health, wellness, and holistic healing, such as yoga and meditation.
    • Seeking Knowledge: The pursuit of wisdom and enlightenment can be reflected in educational endeavors, where individuals come together to learn and grow.

  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a reminder of the power of collective intention and the importance of seeking divine guidance in our lives. It encourages individuals to come together in prayer and action, fostering a sense of community and shared purpose in the pursuit of knowledge, health, and spiritual growth.
