तं इद वर्धन्तु नो गीरो वत्सं संशिश्वरीर इव |य इन्द्रस्य हृदंसनिः ||
tam id vardhantu no giro vatsaṁ saṁśiśvarīr iva | ya indrasya hṛdaṁsaniḥ ||
  • tam id vardhantu: This phrase can be translated as "may this (knowledge) grow" or "may this be nourished." It emphasizes the importance of growth and development, particularly in the context of wisdom and understanding.
  • no giro: "our words" or "our speech." This indicates a collective aspect, suggesting that the words or expressions of the community are significant and should be nurtured.
  • vatsaṁ: This term means "calf" or "young one," symbolizing innocence, potential, and the future. It represents the idea of nurturing something that is young and has the potential to grow into something great.
  • saṁśiśvarīr iva: This can be interpreted as "like the powerful" or "like the strong." It suggests that the growth and nurturing of knowledge and speech should be as strong and impactful as the forces of nature or divine powers.
  • ya indrasya hṛdaṁsaniḥ: This phrase translates to "that which is in the heart of Indra." Indra is a significant deity in Vedic literature, often associated with strength, rain, and the heavens. This implies that the essence of wisdom and strength is deeply rooted in the divine.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of nurturing knowledge and speech within a community, likening it to the growth of a young calf that has the potential to become strong and impactful. In real life, this can be applied in educational settings, where fostering a supportive environment for learning and expression is crucial. It also highlights the significance of collective wisdom and the need for strong, impactful communication in society. By nurturing our words and knowledge, we can create a powerful foundation for future generations, much like the divine strength represented by Indra. This mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of growth, community, and the divine, encouraging us to cultivate our inner strengths and share them with the world.
