यो वाचां विवाचो मृध्रवाचः पुरू सहस्राशिवा जग्हान | तत्-तदिद अस्य पौंस्यं गृणीमसि पितेव यस तविषीं वावृधे शवः ||
yo vācā vivāco mṛdhravācaḥ purū sahasrāśivā jaghāna | tat-tad id asya pauṁsyaṁ gṛṇīmasi piteva yas taviṣīṁ vāvṛdhe śavaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yo: who
    • vācā: by speech
    • vivāco: of the many kinds
    • mṛdhravācaḥ: of gentle speech
    • purū: many
    • sahasrāśivā: thousands of auspicious things
    • jaghāna: has achieved
    • tat-tad: that which
    • id: this
    • asya: of this
    • pauṁsyaṁ: manliness or strength
    • gṛṇīmasi: we sing
    • piteva: like a father
    • yas: who
    • taviṣīṁ: of strength
    • vāvṛdhe: has increased
    • śavaḥ: the dead or lifeless
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the power of speech and communication. It emphasizes the importance of gentle and effective communication that can lead to great achievements.
    • The phrase "who by speech has achieved many auspicious things" suggests that effective communication can lead to positive outcomes in life.
    • The reference to "like a father" indicates a nurturing aspect of communication, where one guides and supports others through their words.
    • The mantra also touches upon the idea of increasing strength and vitality, suggesting that good communication can revive and energize individuals or communities.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace setting, a manager who communicates effectively can inspire their team, leading to increased productivity and morale.
    • In personal relationships, gentle and thoughtful communication can resolve conflicts and strengthen bonds between individuals.
    • In community settings, leaders who articulate their vision clearly can mobilize people towards a common goal, fostering unity and cooperation.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its emphasis on the transformative power of speech. It teaches us that our words can create, inspire, and heal.
    • In real life, this mantra is applicable in various scenarios, from personal relationships to professional environments, highlighting the need for effective communication.
    • By practicing gentle and constructive communication, we can achieve greater harmony and success in our interactions with others.
