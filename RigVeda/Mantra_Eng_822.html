त्रिः सप्त यद गुह्याणि त्वे इति पदाविदान निहिताऽ यज्ञीयासः |तेभिः रक्षन्ते अमृतं सजोषाः पशूः च स्थातृः चरतं च पाही ||
triḥ sapta yad guhyāni tve it padāvidan nihitā yajñiyāsaḥ | tebhī rakṣante amṛtaṁ sajoṣāḥ paśūñ ca sthātṝñ carathaṁ ca pāhi ||
  • Mantra: "triḥ sapta yad guhyāni tve it padāvidan nihitā yajñiyāsaḥ | tebhī rakṣante amṛtaṁ sajoṣāḥ paśūñ ca sthātṝñ carathaṁ ca pāhi ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • triḥ: three
    • sapta: seven
    • yad: whatever
    • guhyāni: secret or hidden
    • tve: you
    • it: here
    • padāvidan: those who know the paths
    • nihitā: placed or established
    • yajñiyāsaḥ: related to sacrifice or offerings
    • tebhī: by them
    • rakṣante: protect
    • amṛtaṁ: immortality or eternal life
    • sajoṣāḥ: with joy
    • paśūñ: animals or beings
    • ca: and
    • sthātṝñ: those who stand
    • carathaṁ: those who move
    • pāhi: protect or guard
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra speaks of the protection and guidance provided by the hidden knowledge and the paths known to the wise.
    • The mention of "three" and "seven" can symbolize completeness and the various aspects of life, suggesting that there are multiple dimensions to understanding and existence.
    • The term "guhyāni" refers to the secrets of life, which can be interpreted as the deeper truths that are often overlooked in daily life.
    • "Yajñiyāsaḥ" connects the mantra to the concept of sacrifice, indicating that through selfless actions and offerings, one can attain higher states of being.
    • The phrase "rakṣante amṛtaṁ" emphasizes the idea that through this knowledge and sacrifice, one can achieve immortality, not just in a physical sense but also in terms of legacy and impact on the world.
    • The inclusion of "paśūñ" and "sthātṝñ carathaṁ" suggests a holistic approach to life, where both stationary and moving beings are protected and nurtured.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community, when individuals come together to support each other (akin to "yajñiyāsaḥ"), they create a nurturing environment that fosters growth and resilience.
    • In personal development, seeking hidden knowledge through education and self-reflection can lead to profound insights that guide one's life path.
    • Practices such as meditation or mindfulness can be seen as modern interpretations of the "paths" that lead to deeper understanding and protection of one's mental and emotional well-being.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of seeking hidden knowledge and the importance of community and sacrifice in achieving a fulfilling life.
    • It serves as a reminder that through collective efforts and understanding, we can protect not only ourselves but also the beings around us, leading to a harmonious existence.
    • In real life, applying the principles of this mantra can lead to personal growth, stronger relationships, and a deeper connection to the world, ultimately guiding us towards a more meaningful and impactful life.
