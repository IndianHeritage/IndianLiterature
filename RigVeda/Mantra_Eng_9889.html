यद विरूपाचरं मर्त्येष्व अवसां रात्रिः शरदश चतस्रः |घृतस्य स्तोकं सकृत् अह्न आशनां ताद एवेदं तातृपाणां चरामि ||
yad virūpācaram martyeṣv avasaṁ rātrīḥ śaradaś catasraḥ | ghṛtasya stokaṁ sakṛd ahna āśnāṁ tād evedaṁ tātṛpāṇā carāmi ||
  • yad virūpācaram: This phrase can be translated as "whatever is diverse in form." It suggests the existence of various forms and manifestations in the universe. In real life, this can be seen in the diversity of cultures, languages, and lifestyles that exist around the world.
  • martyeṣv avasaṁ: This means "among mortals." It emphasizes the human experience and the transient nature of life. For example, in our daily lives, we encounter the impermanence of relationships, careers, and even our own existence.
  • rātrīḥ śaradaś catasraḥ: This translates to "the four autumn nights." Autumn is often associated with change and transition. In a real-life context, this can symbolize the phases of life, where we experience different seasons of growth, maturity, and reflection.
  • ghṛtasya stokaṁ: This means "a little of ghee." Ghee, or clarified butter, is often used in rituals and cooking in Indian culture. It represents nourishment and sustenance. In our lives, this can be likened to the small joys and comforts that sustain us through difficult times.
  • sakṛd ahna āśnāṁ: This translates to "once in the day." It signifies the importance of regularity and routine in our lives. For instance, having a daily practice of meditation or exercise can greatly enhance our well-being.
  • tād evedaṁ: This means "this is indeed." It emphasizes certainty and affirmation. In real life, this can relate to the importance of conviction in our beliefs and actions, leading to a more fulfilling life.
  • tātṛpāṇā carāmi: This translates to "I move with the hand of the father." This phrase can symbolize guidance and support from parental figures or mentors in our lives. It reflects the importance of community and familial bonds in navigating life's challenges.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of life’s diversity, the transient nature of human existence, and the importance of nourishment, routine, and guidance. It serves as a reminder to appreciate the small joys, embrace change, and seek support from our community. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire individuals to cultivate gratitude, maintain balance, and foster connections with others, ultimately leading to a more harmonious and fulfilling life.
