विष्वा सोम पवमान द्युम्नानिंदव आ भर |विदाः सहस्रिणीर इषः ||
viśvā soma pavamāna dyumnānīndav ā bhara | vidāḥ sahasriṇīr iṣaḥ ||
  • viśvā: This word translates to "all" or "universal." It signifies inclusiveness and the interconnectedness of all beings and elements in the universe.
  • soma: Soma refers to a sacred drink in Vedic tradition, often associated with immortality and divine inspiration. It symbolizes the essence of life and vitality.
  • pavamāna: This term means "purifying" or "flowing." It indicates a process of cleansing and rejuvenation, both physically and spiritually.
  • dyumnānīndav: This can be interpreted as "bestowing glory" or "radiance." It emphasizes the importance of light, knowledge, and enlightenment in life.
  • ā bhara: This phrase means "may you bring" or "may you fill." It is a call for abundance and fulfillment in various aspects of life.
  • vidāḥ: This word translates to "knowledge" or "wisdom." It highlights the significance of understanding and insight in navigating life.
  • sahasriṇīr: This term means "thousands" or "multitudes." It suggests the vastness of knowledge and the multitude of experiences one can encounter.
  • iṣaḥ: This word means "desire" or "will." It reflects the human capacity to aspire and strive for goals and aspirations.

The mantra as a whole can be interpreted as a prayer or invocation for universal abundance, purity, and enlightenment. It calls upon the divine to bestow glory and wisdom upon individuals, encouraging them to seek knowledge and fulfillment in their lives.


In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts:

  • Personal Growth: Individuals can use this mantra as a daily affirmation to inspire themselves to seek knowledge and strive for personal development.
  • Community Well-being: In a community setting, this mantra can serve as a reminder of the importance of inclusivity and collective growth, encouraging members to support one another.
  • Environmental Awareness: The emphasis on purity and interconnectedness can inspire actions towards environmental conservation, recognizing the importance of maintaining a clean and healthy planet for all beings.

In conclusion, this mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking universal knowledge, purity, and abundance. It serves as a guiding principle for individuals and communities alike, encouraging a holistic approach to life that values wisdom, interconnectedness, and the pursuit of higher ideals.
