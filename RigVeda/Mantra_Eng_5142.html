विये ते अग्नि भेजिरे अनीकम् मर्ता नरः पितृयासः पुरुत्रा |उतो न एभिः सुमना इह स्याः ||
vi ye te agne bhejire anīkam martā naraḥ pitryāsaḥ purutrā | uto na ebhiḥ sumanā iha syāḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • vi: apart, separately
    • ye: those who
    • te: your
    • agne: O Agni (the fire god)
    • bhejire: have sent forth
    • anīkam: the multitude, the assembly
    • martā: mortal beings
    • naraḥ: men, humans
    • pitryāsaḥ: of the ancestors
    • purutrā: in many places
    • uto: and
    • na: not
    • ebhiḥ: with these
    • sumanā: with good thoughts
    • iha: here, in this world
    • syāḥ: may be
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes Agni, the fire god, who is seen as a mediator between humans and the divine.
    • It emphasizes the importance of community and collective action, as it refers to the assembly of mortals.
    • The mention of ancestors highlights the connection between the past and present, suggesting that the blessings of those who came before us are vital.
    • The phrase "with good thoughts" indicates that the intentions behind our actions are crucial for their success.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community gathering, such as a wedding or a festival, people come together to celebrate and honor their ancestors, reflecting the idea of unity and collective blessings.
    • In modern workplaces, teamwork and collaboration are essential for success, echoing the mantra's emphasis on the assembly of individuals working towards a common goal.
    • Practicing mindfulness and positive thinking in daily life can lead to better outcomes, aligning with the mantra's call for good thoughts.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of community, the blessings of ancestors, and the power of positive intentions.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal relationships to professional environments, reminding us that our actions and thoughts can have a profound impact on our lives and the lives of others.
    • By invoking Agni, we are encouraged to seek guidance and support from the divine while fostering unity and goodwill among ourselves.
