कयā नश् चित्र ā भुवद ūtī सदावृद्धः सखा | कयā शचिष्टयā वृता ||
kayā naś citra ā bhuvad ūtī sadāvṛdhaḥ sakhā | kayā śaciṣṭhayā vṛtā ||
  • Mantra: "kayā naś citra ā bhuvad ūtī sadāvṛdhaḥ sakhā | kayā śaciṣṭhayā vṛtā ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • kayā: By what (means or method)
    • naś: of us
    • citra: wonderful or diverse
    • ā: towards
    • bhuvad: becoming or manifesting
    • ūtī: strength or power
    • sadāvṛdhaḥ: always growing or ever-increasing
    • sakhā: friend or companion
    • śaciṣṭhayā: with wisdom or intelligence
    • vṛtā: surrounded or enveloped
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra reflects a quest for understanding the means through which we can achieve a wonderful and powerful existence.
    • It emphasizes the importance of companionship and friendship in our journey of growth and wisdom.
    • The phrase "always growing" suggests a continuous process of development, both personally and collectively.
    • It highlights the significance of wisdom in navigating life's challenges and forming meaningful relationships.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, teamwork exemplifies the idea of "sakhā" (friendship). Colleagues who support each other can lead to greater success and innovation.
    • In personal relationships, the mantra encourages individuals to seek wisdom and understanding, fostering deeper connections with friends and family.
    • In community building, the concept of "always growing" can be seen in initiatives that promote collective learning and support, such as mentorship programs.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of our lives and the importance of nurturing relationships that contribute to our growth.
    • It encourages us to seek wisdom and strength through collaboration and companionship, which are essential for personal and communal development.
    • In essence, the mantra is applicable in various aspects of life, from personal relationships to professional environments, emphasizing the value of friendship and wisdom in achieving a fulfilling existence.
