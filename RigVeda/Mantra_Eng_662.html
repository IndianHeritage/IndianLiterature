स तुर्वणिर महां अरेणु पौंस्ये गिरेर भ्रिष्टिर न भ्राजते तुजा शवः |येना शुष्णं मायिनं आयसो मदे दुध्र आभूषु रामयन न दामनि ||
sa turvaṇir mahām̐ areṇu pauṁsye girer bhṛṣṭir na bhrājate tujā śavaḥ | yena śuṣṇam māyinam āyaso made dudhra ābhūṣu rāmayan ni dāmani ||
  • sa turvaṇir mahām̐: This phrase can be interpreted as "that great one who is powerful." It signifies a divine or cosmic force that holds immense strength and authority.
  • areṇu pauṁsye: Translated, this means "in the dust of the earth." It suggests a connection to the physical world, emphasizing the importance of nature and the earth in our existence.
  • girer bhṛṣṭir: This translates to "the dust of the mountains." It symbolizes the heights of spiritual and physical aspirations, representing the challenges and achievements that come from striving for greatness.
  • na bhrājate tujā śavaḥ: This means "does not shine with your body." It implies that the true essence or spirit of a being is not merely in its physical form but in its deeper, spiritual nature.
  • yena śuṣṇam māyinam: This can be interpreted as "by which the illusion of the dry is overcome." It suggests the power of divine energy to transform and revitalize, overcoming lifelessness and stagnation.
  • āyaso made: This translates to "in the intoxication of iron." It symbolizes strength and resilience, indicating that true power comes from within and can be harnessed to overcome obstacles.
  • dudhra ābhūṣu: This means "in the shining brilliance." It signifies enlightenment and clarity, suggesting that through understanding and wisdom, one can achieve a higher state of being.
  • rāmayan ni dāmani: This can be interpreted as "to those who are in the realm of righteousness." It emphasizes the importance of living a life of virtue and integrity, aligning oneself with higher moral principles.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda encapsulates the essence of strength, transformation, and spiritual enlightenment. It teaches us that true power lies not in physical form but in our spiritual essence and moral integrity. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios, such as overcoming personal challenges, striving for excellence in our endeavors, and maintaining ethical standards in our actions. By embodying these principles, we can navigate the complexities of life with resilience and purpose, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling existence.
