ऋभुम ऋभुक्षणो रयिं वाजे वाजिन्तमं युजम् |इन्द्रस्वन्तं हवामहे सदासाततमं अश्विनम् ||
ṛbhum ṛbhukṣaṇo rayiṁ vāje vājintamaṁ yujam | indrasvantaṁ havāmahe sadāsātamam aśvinam ||
  • ṛbhum: This word can be interpreted as "the one who is righteous" or "the one who is true." It signifies the importance of truth and righteousness in our actions and thoughts.
  • ṛbhukṣaṇo: This term refers to "the one who provides sustenance" or "the nourisher." It emphasizes the role of those who support and nurture others, akin to caregivers or providers in society.
  • rayiṁ: This word means "wealth" or "prosperity." It represents not just material wealth but also spiritual and emotional richness in life.
  • vāje: This translates to "strength" or "power." It signifies the importance of having strength in various aspects of life, whether physical, mental, or emotional.
  • vājintamaṁ: This term means "the best among the strong" or "the most powerful." It highlights the aspiration to achieve the highest form of strength and excellence.
  • yujam: This word means "to unite" or "to join." It signifies the importance of collaboration and unity in achieving common goals.
  • indrasvantaṁ: This term refers to "the one who is like Indra," the king of gods in Vedic literature, symbolizing leadership and authority.
  • havāmahe: This means "we invoke" or "we call upon." It signifies the act of seeking blessings or guidance from higher powers.
  • sadāsātamam: This term translates to "the ever-present best" or "the supreme." It emphasizes the idea of seeking the highest good in all endeavors.
  • aśvinam: This refers to the twin gods, the Aśvins, who are associated with health, medicine, and well-being. They symbolize the importance of health and vitality in life.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking truth, nourishment, strength, and unity while invoking divine blessings for prosperity and well-being. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios such as:

  • In personal relationships, embodying righteousness and support can lead to stronger bonds and mutual growth.
  • In professional settings, collaboration and unity among team members can enhance productivity and success.
  • In community service, invoking the spirit of the mantra can inspire individuals to contribute positively to society, promoting health and well-being.

Overall, this mantra serves as a reminder of the values that lead to a fulfilling and prosperous life, encouraging individuals to strive for excellence while supporting one another in their journeys.
