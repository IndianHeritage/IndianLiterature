प्रत्नं रयीणां युजं सखायं कीरिकोदनम् |ब्रह्मवाहस्तमं हुवे ||
pratnaṁ rayīṇāṁ yujaṁ sakhāyaṁ kīricodanam | brahmavāhastamaṁ huve ||
  • Mantra: "pratnaṁ rayīṇāṁ yujaṁ sakhāyaṁ kīricodanam | brahmavāhastamaṁ huve ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • pratnaṁ: prayer or invocation
    • rayīṇāṁ: of wealth or prosperity
    • yujaṁ: to unite or connect
    • sakhāyaṁ: as friends or companions
    • kīricodanam: the inspiration or guidance
    • brahmavāhastamaṁ: the supreme or ultimate truth
    • huve: I invoke or call upon
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer for the unification of wealth and prosperity among friends and companions.
    • It emphasizes the importance of collective growth and support in achieving success.
    • The invocation of "brahmavāhastamaṁ" suggests a connection to the ultimate truth or divine guidance in this pursuit.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business setting, this mantra can be applied when a team works together to achieve a common goal, emphasizing collaboration and shared success.
    • In personal relationships, it highlights the importance of supporting each other in times of need, fostering a sense of community and friendship.
    • In community service, it can inspire groups to unite for a cause, pooling resources and efforts to create a positive impact.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for unity and collective effort in the pursuit of prosperity and truth.
    • It serves as a reminder that individual success is often intertwined with the support and collaboration of others.
    • In real-life scenarios, applying the principles of this mantra can lead to stronger relationships, successful teamwork, and a more harmonious community.
