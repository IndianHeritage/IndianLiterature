अग्निमुषसमश्विनादधिक्रां व्युष्टिषु हवते वह्निरुक्तैः |सुज्योतिषो नः श्रिण्वन्तु देवाः सजोषसो अध्वरं वावशानाः ||
agnim uṣasam aśvinā dadhikrāṁ vyuṣṭiṣu havate vahnir ukthaiḥ | sujyotiṣo naḥ śṛṇvantu devāḥ sajoṣaso adhvaraṁ vāvaśānāḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • agnim: Fire
    • uṣasam: Dawn
    • aśvinā: O Ashvins (the twin deities)
    • dadhikrāṁ: Bestowing
    • vyuṣṭiṣu: In the offerings
    • havate: Invoked
    • vahnir: The fire
    • ukthaiḥ: With hymns
    • sujyotiṣo: Of good light
    • naḥ: Our
    • śṛṇvantu: May they listen
    • devāḥ: The gods
    • sajoṣaso: With joy
    • adhvaraṁ: The sacrifice
    • vāvaśānāḥ: May they accept
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra invokes the divine fire (Agni) and the dawn (Uṣas), symbolizing the beginning of a new day and the light that dispels darkness.
    • The mention of the Ashvins, who are known as the divine physicians, emphasizes the healing and rejuvenating aspects of fire and light.
    • The phrase "dadhikrāṁ vyuṣṭiṣu" suggests the act of offering, indicating that the fire is being invoked in the context of a ritual or sacrifice.
    • By calling upon the gods to listen and accept the offerings, the mantra highlights the importance of divine presence and approval in human endeavors.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, this mantra can be related to the practice of starting each day with gratitude and intention, much like a morning ritual that sets a positive tone for the day.
    • In various cultures, lighting a fire or a candle in the morning symbolizes the welcoming of light and warmth, akin to the invocation of Agni and Uṣas.
    • During significant life events, such as weddings or festivals, rituals involving fire are performed to seek blessings from the divine, reflecting the essence of this mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of fire and light as purifiers and sources of energy in our lives.
    • It teaches us to acknowledge the divine forces that support our actions and to seek their blessings in our endeavors.
    • In a broader sense, it encourages us to embrace new beginnings with positivity and to cultivate a sense of gratitude for the light in our lives.
