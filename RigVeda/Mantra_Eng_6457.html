अदान मे पौरुकुत्स्यः पञ्चाशतं त्रसदस्युर वधूनाम् |मंहिष्ठो आर्यः सत्पतिः ||
adān me paurukutsyaḥ pañcāśataṁ trasadasyur vadhūnām | maṁhiṣṭho aryaḥ satpatiḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • adān: giving, offering
    • me: to me
    • paurukutsyaḥ: of the people, community
    • pañcāśataṁ: fifty
    • trasadasyur: of the one who is strong, powerful
    • vadhūnām: of the brides, women
    • maṁhiṣṭho: the best, the most excellent
    • aryaḥ: noble, honorable
    • satpatiḥ: the lord of truth, the righteous ruler
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the act of giving and the importance of community.
    • The mention of "fifty" could symbolize abundance or a significant number of offerings or blessings.
    • The term "trasadasyur" refers to strength and power, indicating that the act of giving is not just a simple gesture but a powerful act that can uplift the community.
    • The reference to "vadhūnām" highlights the role of women in society, suggesting that their well-being is crucial for the prosperity of the community.
    • "Maṁhiṣṭho aryaḥ satpatiḥ" emphasizes the qualities of a noble leader who embodies truth and righteousness, suggesting that such leaders are essential for a thriving society.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern society, community service and charitable acts can be seen as a reflection of the principles in this mantra. For instance, organizations that provide food and shelter to the needy embody the spirit of giving.
    • The empowerment of women through education and leadership roles can be linked to the mention of "vadhūnām," as their contributions are vital for societal progress.
    • Leaders who prioritize ethical governance and community welfare resonate with the qualities of "satpatiḥ," showcasing the importance of integrity in leadership.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of community, the power of giving, and the role of noble leadership in fostering a harmonious society.
    • It is applicable in real-life scenarios where individuals and leaders are encouraged to act selflessly for the greater good, ensuring that the needs of all, especially women, are met.
    • Ultimately, it serves as a reminder that true strength lies in compassion, ethical leadership, and the collective well-being of the community.
