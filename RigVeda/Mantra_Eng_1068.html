स प्रत्नथाः सहसाः जायमानः सद्यः काव्यानि बल अधत्त विश्वा | आपश्च मित्रं धिषणा च साधन देवाः अग्निं धारयन द्रविणोदाम् ||
sa pratnathā sahasā jāyamānaḥ sadyaḥ kāvyāni baḻ adhatta viśvā | āpaś ca mitraṁ dhiṣaṇā ca sādhan devā agniṁ dhārayan draviṇodām ||
  • sa pratnathā sahasā jāyamānaḥ: This phrase can be translated as "He who is born with great strength." It signifies the power and potential inherent in every individual at the time of birth. In real life, this can be seen in the way children exhibit innate talents or abilities, such as a natural inclination towards music, sports, or academics.
  • sadyaḥ kāvyāni baḻ adhatta viśvā: This translates to "Immediately, he brings forth the poetic arts." It emphasizes the importance of creativity and expression in human life. For example, a person who writes poetry or engages in artistic endeavors often finds a deeper connection to their emotions and the world around them, showcasing the universal human experience.
  • āpaś ca mitraṁ dhiṣaṇā ca: This means "The waters and the friend, and the divine inspiration." Here, 'waters' can symbolize the nurturing aspects of life, while 'friend' represents companionship and support. In practical terms, this can relate to how friendships and community support can help individuals thrive, much like how water sustains life.
  • sādhan devā agniṁ dhārayan draviṇodām: This translates to "The gods, holding the fire, bestow wealth." Fire here can symbolize knowledge, transformation, and energy, while 'wealth' can refer to both material and spiritual abundance. In everyday life, this can be seen in how education and knowledge empower individuals to achieve their goals and improve their circumstances.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of human potential, creativity, support, and the transformative power of knowledge. It signifies that every individual is born with strength and the ability to create, and that nurturing relationships and divine inspiration can lead to prosperity. In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as in education, where fostering creativity and providing support can help individuals realize their potential, or in community building, where collaboration and shared knowledge can lead to collective growth and success.
