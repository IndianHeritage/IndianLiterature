अजीरासस तदप इयमानाऽआतस्थिवांसो अमृतस्य नाभिम् |अनन्तास उरवो विश्वतः सीम पर्य द्यावापृथिवी यन्ति पन्थाः ||
ajirāsas tadapa īyamānā ātasthivāṁso amṛtasya nābhim | anantāsa uravo viśvataḥ sīm pari dyāvāpṛthivī yanti panthāḥ ||
  • ajirāsas: This term can be interpreted as "those who are not tired" or "those who are ever-active." It signifies a state of perpetual energy and vitality.
  • tadapa: This means "that which is beyond" or "that which transcends." It suggests a connection to something greater than oneself.
  • īyamānā: This word translates to "moving" or "progressing." It indicates a sense of motion and evolution in life.
  • ātasthivāṁso: This can be understood as "those who are established" or "those who have a firm foundation." It emphasizes stability and strength.
  • amṛtasya: This term means "of immortality" or "of the eternal." It refers to the concept of everlasting life or the essence of the divine.
  • nābhim: This translates to "the navel" or "the center." In many cultures, the navel is seen as a source of life and nourishment.
  • anantāsa: This means "of the infinite" or "endless." It signifies boundlessness and the limitless nature of existence.
  • uravo: This term can be interpreted as "paths" or "ways." It suggests the various routes one can take in life.
  • viśvataḥ: This means "from all directions" or "universally." It indicates inclusivity and the interconnectedness of all things.
  • sīm: This translates to "boundaries" or "limits." It refers to the idea of defining spaces and realms.
  • pari: This means "around" or "encompassing." It suggests a holistic view of existence.
  • dyāvāpṛthivī: This term refers to "heaven and earth." It symbolizes the duality of existence and the balance between the spiritual and the material.
  • yanti: This means "they go" or "they proceed." It indicates movement towards a goal or destination.
  • panthāḥ: This translates to "paths" or "ways." It emphasizes the journey one undertakes in life.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of life as a journey filled with energy, stability, and infinite possibilities. It encourages individuals to seek a path that transcends the mundane, embracing both the spiritual and material aspects of existence. In real life, this can be applied by recognizing the importance of maintaining vitality and purpose, establishing a strong foundation in our endeavors, and being open to the myriad paths that life offers. It serves as a reminder that while we navigate through life, we are part of a larger, interconnected universe, and our choices shape our journey towards immortality and fulfillment.
