आ प्यायस्व सम एतु ते विश्वतः सोम वृष्ण्यम् |भवा वाजस्य संगथे ||
ā pyāyasva sam etu te viśvataḥ soma vṛṣṇyam | bhavā vājasya saṁgathe ||
  • Word Breakdown:
    • ā: A prefix indicating a sense of direction or movement towards.
    • pyāyasva: Derived from the root "pyāy," meaning to protect or to nourish.
    • sam: Meaning together or in harmony.
    • etu: A form of the verb "to come" or "to arrive."
    • te: Meaning "your" or "of you."
    • viśvataḥ: Meaning "from all sides" or "universally."
    • soma: Referring to the sacred drink or plant, often associated with immortality and divine inspiration.
    • vṛṣṇyam: Meaning "strength" or "power," often associated with the divine qualities of the gods.
    • bhavā: Meaning "become" or "may you become."
    • vājasya: Meaning "of strength" or "of vigor."
    • saṁgathe: Meaning "in union" or "together."
  • Meaning:
    • This mantra invokes a sense of protection and nourishment from all directions.
    • It emphasizes the importance of unity and strength, suggesting that when we come together, we can achieve greater power and resilience.
    • The reference to "soma" highlights the divine inspiration and the essence of life that nourishes us.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community setting, this mantra can be applied to emphasize the importance of collaboration and support among members. For instance, during a natural disaster, communities that come together to help each other often recover more quickly.
    • In a workplace, fostering a culture of teamwork and mutual support can lead to increased productivity and job satisfaction, reflecting the essence of "sam etu te" (come together).
    • In personal relationships, nurturing bonds with family and friends can provide emotional strength and resilience, akin to the protective and nourishing qualities mentioned in the mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for unity, strength, and divine nourishment.
    • It serves as a reminder that we are stronger together and that seeking protection and support from the universe can lead to personal and communal growth.
    • In real-life scenarios, whether in communities, workplaces, or personal relationships, the principles of collaboration, support, and shared strength can lead to a more fulfilling and resilient existence.
