स्यूमानā वाच उद इयर्ति वह्निः स्तवानो रेब्ह उषसो विभातिः |अद्यā तद उच्छ गृणते मघोन्य अस्मे आयुर नि दिदीहि प्रजावत ||
syūmanā vāca ud iyarti vahniḥ stavāno rebha uṣaso vibhātīḥ | adyā tad uccha gṛṇate maghony asme āyur ni didīhi prajāvat ||
  • syūmanā vāca ud iyarti vahniḥ: This phrase can be translated to "the well-spoken words rise up like fire." Here, "syūmanā" refers to well-spoken or auspicious words, "vāca" means speech or words, "ud iyarti" means to rise up, and "vahniḥ" refers to fire. This suggests that positive and constructive speech has the power to elevate and inspire, much like fire that brings warmth and light.
  • stavāno rebha uṣaso vibhātīḥ: This part translates to "the praises of the dawn shine forth." "Stavāno" means praising, "rebha" refers to the dawn, and "uṣaso" means of the dawn. This indicates that the act of praising or acknowledging the beauty of new beginnings (dawn) brings clarity and illumination to our lives.
  • adyā tad uccha gṛṇate: This translates to "today, that which is high is sung." "Adyā" means today, "tad" refers to that, "uccha" means high or elevated, and "gṛṇate" means to sing or chant. This emphasizes the importance of celebrating and recognizing the elevated aspects of life in the present moment.
  • maghony asme āyur ni didīhi prajāvat: This can be interpreted as "may the bountiful grant us longevity and progeny." "Maghony" refers to the bountiful or generous, "asme" means to us, "āyur" means longevity, "ni didīhi" means grant, and "prajāvat" refers to progeny or offspring. This highlights the desire for abundance in life, not just in terms of material wealth but also in health and family.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of positive speech, the beauty of new beginnings, the importance of celebrating the present, and the desire for abundance in life. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various ways:

  • In personal relationships, using well-spoken words can foster understanding and strengthen bonds.
  • Recognizing and appreciating new opportunities can lead to personal growth and success.
  • Celebrating achievements and milestones in the present can enhance motivation and happiness.
  • Wishing for health and prosperity not only for oneself but also for family and community can create a supportive environment.

This mantra serves as a reminder of the power of words, the significance of new beginnings, and the importance of gratitude and abundance in our lives.
