तं अस्य द्यावापृथिवी सचेता सा विश्वेभिर देवैर अनु शुष्मम् आवाताम् |यद ऐत कृत्वानो महिमानम् इन्द्रियम् पीत्वी सोमस्य क्रतुमाम् अवर्धत ||
tam asya dyāvāpṛthivī sacetasā viśvebhir devair anu śuṣmam āvatām | yad ait kṛṇvāno mahimānam indriyam pītvī somasya kratumām̐ avardhata ||
  • tam asya dyāvāpṛthivī: This phrase translates to "that which is of the sky and the earth." It signifies the unity of the cosmos, where the heavens (dyau) and the earth (pṛthivī) are interconnected. In real life, this can be seen in the balance of nature, where the sky provides rain for the earth, allowing life to flourish.
  • sacetasā: This means "with a conscious mind." It emphasizes the importance of awareness and intention in our actions. For example, when making decisions in life, being mindful and conscious of the consequences can lead to better outcomes.
  • viśvebhir devair anu śuṣmam āvatām: This translates to "may all the gods protect us from drought." It highlights the need for divine protection and blessings for prosperity. In a real-world context, this can relate to the importance of community and support systems in overcoming challenges, such as droughts affecting agriculture.
  • yad ait kṛṇvāno mahimānam indriyam: This means "whatever we do, may it be with great strength." It encourages individuals to act with vigor and purpose. For instance, in a professional setting, approaching tasks with enthusiasm and determination can lead to success and recognition.
  • pītvī somasya kratumām̐ avardhata: This translates to "may the essence of the soma enhance our intellect." Soma is often associated with nourishment and vitality. In everyday life, this can be interpreted as the importance of education and knowledge in enhancing our capabilities and decision-making.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the interconnectedness of the universe, the importance of conscious action, and the need for divine support in overcoming challenges. It serves as a reminder to approach life with awareness, strength, and a commitment to growth. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various aspects such as environmental conservation, personal development, and community support, highlighting the significance of unity and intention in achieving prosperity and well-being.
