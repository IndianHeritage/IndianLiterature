स पवस्व सहमानः पृथन्यून सेधन रक्षांस्य अप दुर्गहानि |स्वायुधः सासह्वान सोम शत्रून ||
sa pavasva sahamānaḥ pṛtanyūn sedhan rakṣāṁsy apa durgahāṇi | svāyudhaḥ sāsahvān soma śatrūn ||
  • sa pavasva: This phrase can be interpreted as "you who are swift" or "you who are powerful." It signifies the importance of speed and strength in overcoming obstacles.
  • sahamānaḥ: This term means "together with strength" or "united in power." It emphasizes the value of collaboration and unity in achieving goals.
  • pṛtanyūn: This word refers to "the one who is firm" or "the steadfast." It highlights the necessity of resilience and determination in the face of challenges.
  • sedhan: This can be understood as "to protect" or "to guard." It indicates the need for safeguarding oneself and one's community from harm.
  • rakṣāṁsy: This term means "protection" or "defense." It underscores the importance of security and safety in life.
  • apa durgahāṇi: This phrase translates to "from the difficult paths" or "from the hard obstacles." It suggests the idea of overcoming challenges that may seem insurmountable.
  • svāyudhaḥ: This means "with one's own weapons" or "armed with one's own strength." It signifies self-reliance and the use of one's own resources to face adversities.
  • sāsahvān: This term can be interpreted as "with strength" or "with valor." It emphasizes the need for courage and bravery in confronting difficulties.
  • soma: In this context, "soma" can refer to a divine drink or essence that provides strength and vitality. It symbolizes the nourishment of the spirit and mind.
  • śatrūn: This word means "enemies" or "adversaries." It represents the challenges and obstacles one faces in life.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of strength, unity, and resilience in overcoming life's challenges. It serves as a reminder that by harnessing our inner power and working together, we can protect ourselves and navigate through difficult situations. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as teamwork in professional settings, community support during crises, or personal struggles where one must rely on their own strength and resources. Ultimately, it encourages individuals to face adversities with courage and determination, fostering a sense of empowerment and collective strength.
