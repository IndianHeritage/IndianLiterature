मन्त्र: सेद उग्रो अस्तु मरुतः स शुष्मी यं मार्त्यम पृषदश्वा अवाथ | उतेम अग्निः सरस्वती जुनेन्ति न तस्य रायः पर्येतास्ति ||
sed ugro astu marutaḥ sa śuṣmī yam martyam pṛṣadaśvā avātha | utem agniḥ sarasvatī junanti na tasya rāyaḥ paryetāsti ||
  • Translation: "May the powerful Maruts, the fierce ones, protect us; may they bring us the wealth of the earth, like the swift horses. May Agni and Sarasvatī unite us; may we not be deprived of prosperity."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • Marutaḥ: Refers to the Maruts, the storm deities associated with wind and storms, symbolizing power and strength.
    • Ugro: Means fierce or powerful, indicating the strength of the Maruts.
    • Martyam: Refers to mortals or human beings, highlighting the connection between the divine and humanity.
    • Pṛṣadaśvā: Literally translates to "swift horses," symbolizing speed, agility, and the ability to overcome obstacles.
    • Agni: The fire god, representing energy, transformation, and purification.
    • Sarasvatī: The goddess of knowledge, music, and wisdom, symbolizing creativity and intellect.
    • Rāyaḥ: Refers to wealth or prosperity, indicating the desire for abundance in life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, invoking the Maruts can symbolize the need for strength and resilience in facing challenges, much like a company that adapts to market changes with agility.
    • In personal life, the mention of Agni and Sarasvatī can be seen in the pursuit of knowledge and self-improvement, akin to a student striving for academic excellence.
    • In community settings, the mantra can inspire collective efforts towards prosperity, similar to a group project where collaboration leads to success.
  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of divine support in achieving strength, knowledge, and prosperity. It serves as a reminder that in our endeavors, whether personal or communal, we should seek the blessings of powerful forces that guide and protect us. In real life, this can be applicable in various scenarios such as business ventures, educational pursuits, and community development, where the combination of strength, wisdom, and collaboration leads to success and abundance.
