नप्तीभिर्यो विवस्वतः शुभ्रो न मामृजे युवाः |गाः कृत्वानो न निर्णिजम् ||
naptībhir yo vivasvataḥ śubhro na māmṛje yuvā | gāḥ kṛṇvāno na nirṇijam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • naptībhir: by the descendants or grandsons
    • yo: who
    • vivasvataḥ: of the radiant one (often referring to the Sun)
    • śubhro: auspicious or pure
    • na: not
    • māmṛje: wipe away or remove
    • yuvā: young or youthful
    • gāḥ: the cows (often symbolizing wealth and nourishment)
    • kṛṇvāno: creating or making
    • na: not
    • nirṇijam: to be destroyed or to perish
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the relationship between the descendants of the radiant one (the Sun) and the auspicious qualities they embody.
    • It emphasizes the idea that the youth or the new generation should not be tainted or diminished by negativity.
    • The mention of cows symbolizes prosperity and sustenance, suggesting that the efforts of the youth should lead to the creation of wealth and nourishment without leading to destruction.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a family business, the younger generation often inherits the responsibilities and values of the previous generation. This mantra can be seen as a reminder for them to uphold the purity and auspiciousness of their lineage while innovating and creating new opportunities.
    • In educational settings, students are encouraged to learn from their predecessors while also being reminded to maintain integrity and positivity in their pursuits, ensuring that their actions do not lead to negative consequences.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of maintaining purity and auspiciousness in the actions of the younger generation.
    • It serves as a reminder that while striving for success and prosperity, one should not lose sight of ethical values and the well-being of the community.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in family dynamics, educational institutions, and any setting where the youth are stepping into roles of responsibility, encouraging them to create a positive impact while honoring their heritage.
