मन्त्र: द्वे विरूपे चरतः स्वार्थे अन्यान्यां वत्सम् उप धापयते |हरिर अन्यस्यां भवति स्वधावाञ्च चुक्रो अन्यस्यां ददृशे सुवर्चाः ||
dve virūpe carataḥ svarthe anyānyā vatsam upa dhāpayete | harir anyasyām bhavati svadhāvāñ chukro anyasyāṁ dadṛśe suvarcāḥ ||
  • Mantra: "dve virūpe carataḥ svarthe anyānyā vatsam upa dhāpayete | harir anyasyām bhavati svadhāvāñ chukro anyasyāṁ dadṛśe suvarcāḥ ||"
  • Translation: "Two beings, moving in different forms, nurture each other in their own ways. The divine one becomes manifest in another's nature, shining brightly in the other."
  • Word-by-Word Explanation:
    • dve: Two
    • virūpe: In different forms or appearances
    • carataḥ: Moving or acting
    • svarthe: For their own benefit
    • anyānyā: Each other
    • vatsam: Nurturing or caring
    • upa dhāpayete: They support or uphold
    • harir: The divine or the bright one
    • anyasyām: In another's
    • bhavati: Becomes
    • svadhāvāñ: In its own nature
    • chukro: Shining or bright
    • anyasyāṁ: In another
    • dadṛśe: Is seen or manifests
    • suvarcāḥ: With brilliance or radiance
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • Interpersonal Relationships: In friendships or partnerships, individuals often support each other in their personal growth. For instance, a mentor may guide a mentee, nurturing their potential while also gaining insights from the mentee's fresh perspective.
    • Teamwork in Organizations: In a workplace, diverse team members bring different skills and perspectives. They nurture each other's strengths, leading to innovative solutions. For example, a marketing team may rely on the analytical skills of a data analyst while the analyst learns about creative strategies from the marketers.
    • Community Support: In communities, people often come together to support one another. For instance, during a crisis, neighbors may help each other, showcasing how individuals can shine in their unique ways while uplifting others.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the interconnectedness of beings and the importance of mutual support. It signifies that while individuals may have different forms and paths, their nurturing of one another leads to collective growth and brilliance.
    • In real life, this principle can be applied in various scenarios, such as in personal relationships, professional environments, and community interactions. Recognizing the value of collaboration and support can lead to a more harmonious and productive existence.
