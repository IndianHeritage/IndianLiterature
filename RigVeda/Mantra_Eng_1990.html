एवामहस्तुविजातस्तुविष्मान्बृहस्पतिः वृषभो धायि देवः |स नः स्तुतो वीरवत् धातु गोमद् विद्यामेषं वृजनं जीरदानुम् ||
evā mahas tuvijātas tuviṣmān bṛhaspatir vṛṣabho dhāyi devaḥ | sa naḥ stuto vīravad dhātu gomad vidyāmeṣaṁ vṛjanaṁ jīradānum ||
  • evā mahas tuvijātas: This phrase can be translated as "Indeed, you are great, O one who is born of greatness." It acknowledges the divine nature and the immense power of the deity being addressed.
  • tuviṣmān bṛhaspatir: Here, "tuviṣmān" refers to the one who is wise or knowledgeable, and "bṛhaspatir" is a reference to Brihaspati, the deity of wisdom and eloquence. This highlights the importance of wisdom in guiding one's actions.
  • vṛṣabho dhāyi devaḥ: This translates to "the divine one who is the protector and sustainer." It emphasizes the role of the divine in providing support and protection to humanity.
  • sa naḥ stuto vīravad: This means "may he be praised by us like a hero." It suggests that the divine should be honored and revered for their strength and valor.
  • dhātu gomad vidyāmeṣaṁ: "dhātu" refers to the essence or substance, "gomad" can be interpreted as "the one who brings prosperity," and "vidyāmeṣaṁ" relates to knowledge or wisdom. This part of the mantra emphasizes the connection between divine essence, prosperity, and knowledge.
  • vṛjanaṁ jīradānum: This can be understood as "may we be granted the strength to overcome obstacles." It reflects the desire for divine assistance in overcoming challenges in life.

  • Significance: This mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking divine wisdom, protection, and strength. It acknowledges the greatness of the divine and the importance of wisdom in navigating life's challenges.
  • Real-life Application: In everyday life, individuals can recite this mantra during times of difficulty or when facing important decisions. It serves as a reminder to seek wisdom and guidance from higher powers, fostering a sense of connection to the divine.
  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of divine wisdom and support in achieving success and overcoming obstacles. By invoking the qualities of the divine, individuals can cultivate a mindset of strength, resilience, and reverence, which can lead to a more fulfilling and prosperous life.
