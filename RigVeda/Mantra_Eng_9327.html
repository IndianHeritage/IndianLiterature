तव प्रयाजाऽनुयाजाश्च केवल ऊर्जस्वन्तो हविषः सन्तु भागाः |तवाग्ने यज्ञोऽयं अस्तु सर्वस तुभ्यं नमन्ताम् प्रदिशश चतस्रः ||
tava prayājā anuyājāś ca kevala ūrjasvanto haviṣaḥ santu bhāgāḥ | tavāgne yajño3 'yam astu sarvas tubhyaṁ namantām pradiśaś catasraḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • Tava: Your
    • Prayājā: Offerings
    • Anuyājāś: Those who follow the rituals
    • Kevala: Only
    • Ūrjasvanto: Energetic or powerful
    • Haviṣaḥ: With offerings
    • Santu: May they be
    • Bhāgāḥ: Beneficial or prosperous
    • Tavāgne: O Agni (Fire)
    • Yajño: Sacrifice or worship
    • ‘Yam: This
    • Asthu: May it be
    • Sarvas: All
    • Tubhyaṁ: To you
    • Namantām: May they bow
    • Pradiśaś: In all directions
    • Catasraḥ: Four
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Agni, the fire god, who is considered a mediator between humans and the divine.
    • The mantra emphasizes the importance of offerings and rituals, suggesting that those who perform them with energy and devotion will receive blessings.
    • The phrase "may they be beneficial" indicates a desire for prosperity and well-being for all involved in the rituals.
    • The mention of "four directions" signifies the universality of the blessings being sought, extending to all corners of the world.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern times, this mantra can be related to community gatherings where people come together to celebrate and perform rituals, such as festivals or religious ceremonies.
    • For instance, during Diwali, the festival of lights, families perform rituals to honor Agni and seek blessings for prosperity and happiness.
    • In a corporate setting, team-building activities can be seen as a form of ritual where members come together with energy and enthusiasm to foster collaboration and success.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of rituals and offerings in connecting with the divine and seeking blessings for prosperity.
    • It teaches us that when we come together with intention and energy, we can create positive outcomes not just for ourselves but for our communities as well.
    • In essence, it serves as a reminder of the power of collective effort and the importance of honoring the forces that sustain us.
