मन्त्र: परि सोम ऋतम् बृहद् आशुः पवित्रे अर्षति | विघ्नान् रक्षांसी देवयुः ||
pari soma ṛtam bṛhad āśuḥ pavitre arṣati | vighnan rakṣāṁsi devayuḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • pari: around, in all directions
    • soma: a sacred drink, often associated with immortality and divine inspiration
    • ṛtam: truth, order, or cosmic law
    • bṛhad: great, vast
    • āśuḥ: quickly, swiftly
    • pavitre: pure, sacred
    • arṣati: shines, radiates
    • vighnan: obstacles, hindrances
    • rakṣāṁsi: protection, guardians
    • devayuḥ: divine beings, gods
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the essence of the sacred drink 'soma' which symbolizes divine inspiration and the pursuit of truth.
    • The phrase "pari soma ṛtam" suggests that the truth is enveloped in the divine essence, indicating that understanding and enlightenment come from a higher source.
    • "bṛhad āśuḥ pavitre arṣati" emphasizes the swift and pure radiance of this truth, suggesting that when one aligns with this divine essence, clarity and purity of thought and action are achieved.
    • The latter part, "vighnan rakṣāṁsi devayuḥ," calls upon divine beings to protect against obstacles, highlighting the importance of seeking divine assistance in overcoming life's challenges.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, when faced with challenges, individuals can seek inspiration and clarity through meditation or reflection, akin to consuming 'soma' for divine guidance.
    • In personal relationships, understanding and truth can be sought through open communication, which can be seen as a way to invoke the purity and radiance mentioned in the mantra.
    • During difficult times, people often turn to spiritual practices or prayers, invoking divine protection against obstacles, similar to the plea for protection in the mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the pursuit of truth and the importance of divine guidance in overcoming obstacles.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal growth to professional challenges, reminding us to seek clarity and protection through higher wisdom.
    • Ultimately, it encourages individuals to connect with the divine for inspiration and support, fostering a deeper understanding of their path in life.
