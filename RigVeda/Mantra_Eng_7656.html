स न īḻānयā सह देवाम् अग्ने दुवस्युवā |चिकिद् विभानव ā वह ||
sa na īḻānayā saha devām̐ agne duvasyuvā | cikid vibhānav ā vaha ||
  • sa na īḻānayā: This phrase can be interpreted as "may we be united" or "together with us." It emphasizes the importance of unity and collaboration among individuals.
  • saha devām̐: This translates to "with the gods" or "alongside the divine." It suggests seeking guidance and support from higher powers or divine entities in our endeavors.
  • agne: Referring to Agni, the fire god, who symbolizes energy, transformation, and the divine spark within us. Agni is often associated with purification and the power of sacrifice.
  • duvasyuvā: This term can be understood as "may we be blessed" or "may we receive abundance." It conveys a desire for prosperity and well-being.
  • cikid: This word means "to know" or "to understand." It highlights the importance of knowledge and wisdom in our lives.
  • vibhānav: This can be interpreted as "manifestation" or "illumination." It signifies the process of bringing forth ideas, creativity, and enlightenment.
  • ā vaha: This phrase means "may we carry" or "may we bring forth." It suggests the act of taking responsibility and actively participating in the creation of our reality.

In conclusion, this mantra emphasizes the significance of unity, divine guidance, and the pursuit of knowledge and prosperity. It encourages individuals to work together harmoniously, seek blessings from the divine, and actively engage in the process of manifestation. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as:


  • Teamwork: In professional settings, fostering collaboration among team members can lead to greater success and innovation.
  • Spiritual Practices: Individuals can recite this mantra during meditation or prayer to seek guidance and blessings from higher powers.
  • Community Building: The mantra can inspire collective efforts in community service, promoting unity and shared goals.
  • Personal Growth: By focusing on knowledge and understanding, individuals can enhance their personal development and decision-making skills.

Overall, the mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of all beings and the importance of seeking divine support in our journey towards prosperity and enlightenment.
