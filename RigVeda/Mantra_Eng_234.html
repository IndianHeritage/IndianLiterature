ऋतेन याव ऋतावृद्धाव ऋतस्य ज्योतिषस पति |तां मित्रावरुणा हुवे ||
ṛtena yāv ṛtāvṛdhāv ṛtasya jyotiṣas patī | tā mitrāvaruṇā huve ||
  • ṛtena: This word translates to "by truth" or "through righteousness." It signifies the importance of living a life based on truth and moral values.
  • yāv: This means "as long as" or "until." It indicates a duration or a condition that must be met.
  • ṛtāvṛdhāv: This can be interpreted as "in the growth of truth" or "in the flourishing of righteousness." It emphasizes the idea that truth and righteousness should be nurtured and allowed to grow.
  • ṛtasya: This refers to "of truth" or "of righteousness." It denotes possession or belonging to the realm of truth.
  • jyotiṣas: This translates to "of the luminaries" or "of the celestial bodies." It symbolizes knowledge, enlightenment, and guidance.
  • patī: This means "lord" or "master." It signifies authority and control, particularly in the context of guiding forces.
  • tā: This word means "those" or "that." It refers to the entities being addressed in the mantra.
  • mitrāvaruṇā: This is a compound word referring to "Mitra" and "Varuna," two deities associated with friendship, harmony, and cosmic order. They represent the balance of natural laws and social ethics.
  • huve: This means "I invoke" or "I call upon." It indicates a plea or a prayer directed towards the deities.

The mantra can be understood as a prayer for the flourishing of truth and righteousness, invoking the divine forces of Mitra and Varuna to guide and protect the seeker. It emphasizes the significance of living a life aligned with truth, as this alignment brings about growth and enlightenment.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios:

  • Personal Integrity: In our daily lives, adhering to truth and righteousness can lead to personal growth and fulfillment. For example, a student who studies honestly and upholds academic integrity is likely to achieve genuine success.
  • Social Harmony: In communities, promoting truth and ethical behavior fosters trust and cooperation among individuals. For instance, a community that values transparency in governance will likely experience less corruption and more civic engagement.
  • Conflict Resolution: In situations of conflict, invoking the principles of truth and righteousness can help mediate disputes. For example, in a workplace disagreement, focusing on honest communication and ethical considerations can lead to a fair resolution.

In conclusion, this mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of truth and righteousness in our lives. By invoking the divine forces of Mitra and Varuna, we seek guidance and support in our endeavors to live ethically and harmoniously. Its significance lies in its universal applicability, encouraging individuals and communities to strive for integrity and enlightenment in all aspects of life.
