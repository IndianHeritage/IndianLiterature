प्र बोधयोषः पृणतो मघोन्य अबुध्यमानाः पणयः ससन्तु |रेवद उच्छ मघवत्भ्यो मघोनि रेवत स्तोत्रे सूनृते जारयन्ती ||
pra bodhayoṣaḥ pṛṇato maghony abudhyamānāḥ paṇayaḥ sasantu | revad uccha maghavadbhyo maghoni revat stotre sūnṛte jārayantī ||
  • pra bodhayoṣaḥ: This phrase can be translated as "may we awaken to the dawn." It signifies the importance of enlightenment and awareness as we begin a new day.
  • pṛṇato maghony: This means "may the rich and prosperous ones bless us." It emphasizes the value of abundance and the blessings that come from those who are prosperous.
  • abudhyamānāḥ: This translates to "those who are awakening." It refers to individuals who are becoming aware and enlightened, suggesting a collective journey towards knowledge.
  • paṇayaḥ sasantu: This can be interpreted as "may the wealth and prosperity be ours." It expresses a desire for material and spiritual wealth, highlighting the importance of both in life.
  • revad uccha: This means "may we rise high." It signifies aspirations and the pursuit of higher goals, both in personal and communal contexts.
  • maghavadbhyo: This translates to "from the prosperous ones." It indicates a connection to those who have achieved success and abundance, suggesting that we can learn from them.
  • maghoni revat: This phrase means "the wealth of the dawn." It symbolizes new beginnings and the potential that each day holds for growth and prosperity.
  • stotre sūnṛte: This translates to "in the praise of the good." It emphasizes the importance of recognizing and celebrating goodness in our lives and the world around us.
  • jārayantī: This means "may they nourish." It signifies the nurturing aspect of wealth and prosperity, suggesting that true abundance is one that supports and uplifts others.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a profound message about awakening to the possibilities of life, seeking prosperity, and recognizing the importance of community and support. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various ways:

  • In personal development, it encourages individuals to seek knowledge and enlightenment, fostering a mindset of growth.
  • In business and entrepreneurship, it highlights the importance of learning from successful individuals and striving for prosperity while also uplifting others.
  • In community building, it emphasizes the need for collective support and nurturing, ensuring that wealth and resources are shared for the greater good.

This mantra serves as a reminder that true wealth is not just material but also encompasses knowledge, community, and the ability to uplift one another.
