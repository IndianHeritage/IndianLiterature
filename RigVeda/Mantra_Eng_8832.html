सप्त मर्यादाः कवयस् तातक्षुस तासां एकामिद अभ्य अंहुरो गात् |आयोर ह स्कम्भ उपमस्य नीलि पथां विसर्जे धरणेषु तस्थौ ||
sapta maryādāḥ kavayas tatakṣus tāsām ekām id abhy aṁhuro gāt | āyor ha skambha upamasya nīḻe pathāṁ visarge dharuṇeṣu tasthau ||
  • Mantra: "sapta maryādāḥ kavayas tatakṣus tāsām ekām id abhy aṁhuro gāt | āyor ha skambha upamasya nīḻe pathāṁ visarge dharuṇeṣu tasthau ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • Sapta: Seven
    • Maryādāḥ: Boundaries or limits
    • Kavayas: Poets or wise ones
    • Tatakṣus: With vision or insight
    • Tāsām: Of them
    • Ekām: One
    • Id: This
    • Abhy: Towards
    • Aṁhuro: The one who is powerful
    • Gāt: Song or hymn
    • Āyor: Of life
    • Ha: Indeed
    • Skambha: Pillar or support
    • Upamasya: Of the metaphor
    • Nīḻe: In the blue or deep
    • Pathāṁ: Of the paths
    • Visarge: In the creation or release
    • Dharuṇeṣu: In the strong or firm
    • Tasthau: Stood or remained
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra speaks of the "seven boundaries" established by wise poets, indicating the importance of limits and structure in life.
    • The "one powerful hymn" suggests that amidst various paths and choices, there is a singular truth or essence that guides us.
    • The metaphor of a "pillar of life" emphasizes stability and support, which can be likened to foundational values or principles that uphold our existence.
    • The imagery of "paths" and "creation" reflects the journey of life, where we navigate through various experiences and challenges.
    • In real life, this can be seen in the way individuals set personal boundaries in relationships, careers, and personal growth, ensuring they remain grounded and focused.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its reminder of the importance of boundaries and the pursuit of a singular truth in a complex world.
    • It encourages individuals to establish their own limits while seeking deeper understanding and stability in their lives.
    • In practical terms, this can be applied in various scenarios such as setting healthy boundaries in personal relationships, maintaining work-life balance, and adhering to ethical principles in decision-making.
    • Ultimately, it serves as a guide for navigating life's complexities with wisdom and strength.
