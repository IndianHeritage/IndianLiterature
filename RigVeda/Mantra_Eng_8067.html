उत नो गोविद अश्ववित पवस्व सोमान्धसाः |मक्षूतमेभिर अहभिः ||
uta no govid aśvavit pavasva somāndhasā | makṣūtamebhir ahabhiḥ ||
  • Mantra: "uta no govid aśvavit pavasva somāndhasā | makṣūtamebhir ahabhiḥ ||"
  • Translation: This mantra can be translated as: "O Soma, may you, the giver of strength, invigorate us with your essence, and may we be protected from all harm."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • Uta: A term often used to invoke or call upon a deity or force.
    • No: Means "our," indicating a personal connection or request.
    • Govid: Refers to the divine or sacred essence, often associated with strength and vitality.
    • Aśvavit: Derived from "aśva," meaning horse, symbolizing speed, power, and energy.
    • Pavasva: A request for purification or invigorating energy.
    • Somāndhasā: Refers to the essence of Soma, a sacred drink believed to confer immortality and strength.
    • Makṣūtamebhir: A term that implies protection or safeguarding from harm.
    • Ahabhiḥ: Refers to the day or light, symbolizing clarity and enlightenment.

  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, individuals often seek strength and vitality through various means such as exercise, nutrition, and meditation. This mantra can be a reminder to seek inner strength and clarity in challenging situations.
    • In a corporate setting, teams may invoke this mantra metaphorically when embarking on a new project, seeking the strength and protection needed to succeed and overcome obstacles.
    • During personal challenges, such as health issues or emotional struggles, individuals may recite this mantra to invoke a sense of empowerment and resilience.

  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for strength, protection, and vitality. It serves as a reminder that we can seek divine assistance in our endeavors, whether they are personal, professional, or spiritual. By invoking the essence of strength and clarity, we can navigate life's challenges with greater resilience and purpose. This mantra is applicable in various real-life scenarios, encouraging individuals to connect with their inner strength and seek support from higher powers in times of need.
