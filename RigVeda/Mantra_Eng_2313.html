अध स्मा न उद अवतात सजोषसो रथम देवासो अभि विक्षु वाजयुम् |यद आशवः पद्याभिस तित्रतो रजः पृथिव्याः सानौ जङ्घनन्त पाणिभिः ||
adha smā na ud avatā sajoṣaso rathaṁ devāso abhi vikṣu vājayum | yad āśavaḥ padyābhis titrato rajaḥ pṛthivyāḥ sānau jaṅghananta pāṇibhiḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • adha: down, below
    • smā: indeed, certainly
    • na: not
    • ud: up, above
    • avatā: descended, come down
    • sajoṣaso: with joy, with enthusiasm
    • rathaṁ: chariot, vehicle
    • devāso: the gods, divine beings
    • abhi: towards, in the direction of
    • vikṣu: to be invoked, to be called upon
    • vājayum: to be made powerful, to be energized
    • yad: when, that which
    • āśavaḥ: swift, quick
    • padyābhis: with steps, with feet
    • titrato: they move, they travel
    • rajaḥ: dust, earth
    • pṛthivyāḥ: of the earth
    • sānau: together, in unison
    • jaṅghananta: with extended limbs, with outstretched arms
    • pāṇibhiḥ: with hands
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the divine forces that descend from above, bringing joy and energy to the earthly realm.
    • The reference to the "chariot" symbolizes the journey of life, where the gods are invoked to empower and guide us.
    • The phrase "swift with steps" suggests the importance of taking action and moving forward in life, while "dust of the earth" signifies our connection to the material world.
    • The imagery of "outstretched arms" indicates readiness to receive blessings and opportunities.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a challenging situation, such as starting a new job or project, one might invoke this mantra to seek divine guidance and strength.
    • During a significant life event, like a wedding or a graduation, individuals may reflect on this mantra to embrace the joy and energy of the occasion.
    • In moments of uncertainty, such as facing a personal crisis, the mantra serves as a reminder to take action and move forward with confidence.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the connection between the divine and the earthly, highlighting the importance of invoking higher powers for guidance and strength.
    • Its significance lies in the encouragement to take action, embrace joy, and remain connected to the material world while seeking spiritual support.
    • In real-life scenarios, it serves as a powerful reminder to remain proactive and open to the blessings that come from both divine and earthly realms.
