मन्त्र: स्वर्जितं महि मन्दानम् अन्धसो हवामहे परि शक्रं सुतां उप || इमं नो यज्ञं इह बोध्य आगहि स्पृधो जयन्तं मगवानम् ईमहे ||
svarjitam mahi mandānam andhaso havāmahe pari śakraṁ sutām̐ upa | imaṁ no yajñam iha bodhy ā gahi spṛdho jayantam maghavānam īmahe ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • svarjitam: that which is created or produced
    • mahi: great or vast
    • mandānam: the one who is slow or sluggish
    • andhaso: from darkness or ignorance
    • havāmahe: we invoke or call upon
    • pari: around or in all directions
    • śakraṁ: the powerful or mighty
    • sutām̐: the one who is well-born or noble
    • upa: near or close
    • imaṁ: this
    • no: our
    • yajñam: sacrifice or offering
    • iha: here or in this world
    • bodhy: to be understood or realized
    • ā: from or out of
    • gahi: to grasp or hold
    • spṛdho: with desire or intention
    • jayantam: victorious or triumphant
    • maghavānam: the one who is powerful or wealthy
    • īmahe: we meditate or contemplate
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of invoking the great and powerful forces of the universe.
    • It emphasizes the importance of moving from darkness (ignorance) to light (knowledge).
    • The call to the mighty (śakraṁ) suggests seeking strength and support in our endeavors.
    • By focusing on our offerings (yajñam), we align ourselves with the greater cosmic order.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In personal development, this mantra can be seen as a call to overcome challenges and ignorance in our lives.
    • In a community setting, it can represent collective efforts to bring about positive change and enlightenment.
    • In professional environments, invoking the strength of collaboration and teamwork can lead to successful outcomes.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for enlightenment and strength in the face of challenges.
    • It encourages individuals and communities to seek knowledge, support, and collaboration.
    • In real-life scenarios, it can be applied in personal growth, community initiatives, and professional teamwork.
    • Ultimately, it serves as a reminder of the power of collective effort and the pursuit of knowledge in overcoming ignorance.
