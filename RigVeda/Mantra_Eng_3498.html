मन्त्र: उता स्य वाजी क्षिपणिं तुरण्यति ग्रीवायाम् बद्धो अपिकक्ष आसनिः | क्रतुं दधिक्राऽनु संतवीत्वत पथाम् अङ्कांस्य अन्व आपनीफणत ||
uta sya vājī kṣipaṇiṁ turaṇyati grīvāyām baddho apikakṣa āsani | kratuṁ dadhikrā anu saṁtavītvat pathām aṅkāṁsy anv āpanīphaṇat ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • uta: and
    • sya: of this
    • vājī: the one who is strong or powerful
    • kṣipaṇiṁ: the one who is swift or quick
    • turaṇyati: to be directed or guided
    • grīvāyām: in the neck or throat
    • baddho: bound or tied
    • apikakṣa: with a strong desire or intention
    • āsani: to sit or reside
    • kratuṁ: the will or intention
    • dadhikrā: to be established or made firm
    • anu: after or following
    • saṁtavītvat: to be in harmony or agreement
    • pathām: on the path
    • aṅkāṁsy: the limbs or parts
    • anv: following or pursuing
    • āpanīphaṇat: to bring forth or manifest
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the strength and swiftness of an individual, emphasizing the importance of being guided and directed in life.
    • The imagery of being bound in the neck suggests a connection to one's purpose or mission, indicating that one should be firmly anchored in their intentions.
    • The mention of harmony and agreement highlights the significance of aligning one's actions with their goals and the paths they choose to follow.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, a leader who is both strong and swift in decision-making can guide their team effectively, ensuring that everyone is aligned with the organization's goals.
    • In personal development, individuals who are clear about their intentions and remain committed to their paths often achieve greater success and fulfillment.
    • In relationships, being in harmony with one’s partner and aligning intentions can lead to a more profound and meaningful connection.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of strength, clarity of purpose, and alignment in our lives.
    • It encourages individuals to be swift in their actions while remaining grounded in their intentions, fostering harmony in their pursuits.
    • In essence, it is applicable in various aspects of life, including personal growth, professional endeavors, and interpersonal relationships, guiding us towards a more fulfilled existence.
