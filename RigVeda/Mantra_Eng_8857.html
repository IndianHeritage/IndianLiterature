आपो हि ष्ठा मयोभुवस् ता न ऊर्जे दधातना |महेर रणाय चक्षसे ||
āpo hi ṣṭhā mayobhuvas tā na ūrje dadhātana | mahe raṇāya cakṣase ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • āpo: Waters
    • hi: indeed
    • ṣṭhā: stand or be stable
    • mayobhuvas: may we be endowed with strength
    • tā: that
    • na: not
    • ūrje: energy or power
    • dadhātana: may it bestow
    • mahe: great
    • raṇāya: for battle or conflict
    • cakṣase: for the eye or vision
  • Overall Meaning: This mantra invokes the waters, asking them to be stable and to bestow strength and energy upon us, particularly for the purpose of great endeavors or battles. It emphasizes the importance of stability and the nurturing aspect of water in providing the necessary energy for success in challenging situations.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • Stability in Life: Just as water is essential for life and must be stable to support ecosystems, individuals need stability in their lives—be it emotional, financial, or social—to thrive. For instance, a stable job can provide the foundation for personal growth and family well-being.
    • Strength in Adversity: In times of conflict or challenge, such as during a competitive exam or a sports event, individuals often seek inner strength. This mantra serves as a reminder to draw upon one's inner resources and the support of the community, much like how water nourishes plants to grow strong and resilient.
    • Vision and Clarity: The reference to "cakṣase" (for vision) can be related to the clarity of thought needed in decision-making. For example, in business, having a clear vision and strategy is crucial for success, akin to how water clears the path for growth.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its holistic approach to life, emphasizing the interconnectedness of stability, strength, and clarity. In real-life scenarios, it can be applied in various contexts—personal development, professional challenges, and even in community building. By invoking the essence of water, we are reminded of the importance of nurturing our inner strength and maintaining stability to face life's battles effectively.
