यः सत्राहा विचर्षणिर इन्द्रं तं हूमहे वयम् | सहस्रमुष्क तुविनृम्ण सत्पते भवाऽ समत्सु नो वृधे ||
yaḥ satrāhā vicarṣaṇir indraṁ taṁ hūmahe vayam | sahasramuṣka tuvinṛmṇa satpate bhavā samatsu no vṛdhe ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yaḥ: who
    • satrāhā: the one who is the protector or the one who brings together
    • vicarṣaṇir: the one who moves or travels
    • indraṁ: Indra, the king of gods and god of rain and thunderstorms
    • taṁ: him
    • hūmahe: we invoke or we call upon
    • vayam: we
    • sahasramuṣka: thousandfold or abundant
    • tuvinṛmṇa: to grant or to bestow
    • satpate: the lord of truth or the one who is righteous
    • bhavā: may you become
    • samatsu: in harmony or in good company
    • no: for us
    • vṛdhe: may you increase or may you prosper
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a call to Indra, the powerful deity who represents strength, rain, and prosperity.
    • It emphasizes the importance of invoking divine support in our endeavors, particularly in times of need.
    • The phrase "sahasramuṣka" suggests a desire for abundance and prosperity, indicating that we seek not just enough, but a thousandfold increase in our efforts.
    • By referring to "satpate," the mantra highlights the value of truth and righteousness, suggesting that our pursuits should be aligned with moral integrity.
    • The invocation of harmony ("samatsu") indicates the importance of community and collaboration in achieving our goals.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be applied when a team seeks to achieve a common goal, invoking the spirit of collaboration and integrity to ensure success.
    • In personal life, individuals may recite this mantra during challenging times, seeking divine guidance and support to overcome obstacles and achieve prosperity.
    • In community gatherings, this mantra can serve as a reminder of the importance of working together harmoniously for the greater good.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the importance of invoking divine support in our lives.
    • It emphasizes the need for truth, collaboration, and the pursuit of abundance, making it applicable in various real-life scenarios.
    • By aligning our actions with righteousness and seeking harmony, we can enhance our chances of success and prosperity in our endeavors.
