इमा ब्रह्मेन्द्र तुभ्यं शंसी दा नृभ्यो नृणां शूर शवः |तेभिर भव सक्रतुर येषु चाकन्न उता त्रायस्व गृणत उता स्तीन ||
imā brahmendra tubhyaṁ śaṁsi dā nṛbhyo nṛṇāṁ śūra śavaḥ | tebhir bhava sakratur yeṣu cākann uta trāyasva gṛṇata uta stīn ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • imā: these
    • brahmendra: O Lord of Creation
    • tubhyaṁ: to you
    • śaṁsi: may there be peace
    • dā: grant
    • nṛbhyo: to humans
    • nṛṇāṁ: of men
    • śūra: the brave
    • śavaḥ: the dead
    • tebhir: by them
    • bhava: may you become
    • sakratur: the one who is powerful
    • yeṣu: in whom
    • cākann: who are gathered
    • uta: or
    • trāyasva: protect
    • gṛṇata: who sing
    • uta: or
    • stīn: the offerings
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer addressed to the divine, specifically to the Lord of Creation, seeking peace and blessings for humanity.
    • The invocation of "O Lord of Creation" signifies a recognition of a higher power that governs the universe.
    • The phrase "may there be peace" emphasizes the importance of harmony and tranquility in human life.
    • By asking for blessings to be granted to the brave and the deceased, it acknowledges the valor of the living and honors the memory of those who have passed away.
    • The call for protection and support for those who gather in unity highlights the strength found in community and collective effort.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In times of crisis, such as natural disasters or pandemics, communities often come together to support one another, reflecting the essence of this mantra.
    • In military or emergency services, the bravery of individuals is celebrated, and prayers for their safety and peace for the fallen are common.
    • During cultural or religious gatherings, the collective singing and chanting of prayers can be seen as a modern-day application of the mantra's call for unity and protection.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of life, the importance of community, and the need for divine blessings in our endeavors.
    • Its significance lies in the acknowledgment of both the living and the deceased, urging us to honor our past while striving for a peaceful future.
    • In real-life scenarios, it encourages individuals to come together in times of need, fostering a sense of solidarity and shared purpose.
