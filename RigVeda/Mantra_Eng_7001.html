शं नो भव हृदा आपीत इन्दो पितेव सोम सूनवे सुशेवः |सखेव सख्य उरुशंसा धीरा प्र ण आयुर जीवसे सोम तारिः ||
śaṁ no bhava hṛda ā pīta indo piteva soma sūnave suśevaḥ | sakheva sakhya uruśaṁsa dhīraḥ pra ṇa āyur jīvase soma tārīḥ ||
  • śaṁ no bhava hṛda ā pīta indo piteva soma sūnave suśevaḥ
    • śaṁ no bhava: This phrase translates to "May peace be with us." It emphasizes the importance of tranquility and harmony in our lives.
    • hṛda ā pīta: This means "may our hearts be filled." It suggests a desire for emotional fulfillment and connection.
    • indo piteva: "Like a father to the moon." This metaphor highlights the nurturing and protective qualities of a father figure, suggesting that we seek guidance and support.
    • soma sūnave suśevaḥ: "May we be blessed with the essence of Soma." Soma is often associated with vitality and immortality, indicating a wish for health and longevity.
  • sakheva sakhya uruśaṁsa dhīraḥ
    • sakheva sakhya: "Like a friend to a friend." This phrase emphasizes the importance of friendship and camaraderie in our lives.
    • uruśaṁsa: "May we be blessed with great fortune." This indicates a desire for prosperity and success in our endeavors.
    • dhīraḥ: "May we be wise." Wisdom is a crucial aspect of making sound decisions and navigating life's challenges.
  • pra ṇa āyur jīvase soma tārīḥ
    • pra ṇa āyur: "May we have a long life." This is a direct wish for longevity and vitality.
    • jīvase: "For living." This emphasizes the importance of life itself and the experiences that come with it.
    • soma tārīḥ: "May we attain the essence of Soma." Again, this refers to the desire for health and well-being.

Conclusion:

The mantra encapsulates a profound wish for peace, emotional fulfillment, guidance, friendship, wisdom, and longevity. In real-life scenarios, it can be applied in various contexts such as:

  • In personal relationships, it encourages nurturing bonds and emotional support.
  • In professional settings, it highlights the importance of teamwork and collaboration.
  • In health and wellness, it serves as a reminder to seek vitality and well-being.

Overall, this mantra serves as a guiding principle for living a balanced and fulfilling life, fostering connections with others, and striving for personal growth and health.
