स चक्रमे महतो निर उरुक्रमः समानस्मात सदस एवयामरुत् |यदायुक्त तमनā स्वाद अधि ष्णुभिर विष्पर्धसो विमहसो जिगाति शेवृधो नृभिः ||
sa cakrame mahato nir urukramaḥ samānasmāt sadasa evayāmarut | yadāyukta tmanā svād adhi ṣṇubhir viṣpardhaso vimahaso jigāti śevṛdho nṛbhiḥ ||
  • sa cakrame mahato nir urukramaḥ: This phrase can be translated as "He who moves in the great wheel, the one who is not limited by space." Here, "cakra" refers to a wheel, symbolizing the cyclical nature of life and the universe. In real life, this can be related to the cycles of nature, such as the changing seasons or the life cycle of living beings.
  • samānasmāt sadasa evayāmarut: This means "from the same source, the eternal ones." It emphasizes the idea of unity and interconnectedness among all beings. In practical terms, this can be seen in how communities come together to support each other, reflecting the idea that we all originate from the same source and are part of a larger whole.
  • yadāyukta tmanā svād adhi: This translates to "when one is united in mind, they experience sweetness." This highlights the importance of mental unity and harmony. In everyday life, this can be observed in teamwork, where a group of individuals working together towards a common goal can achieve greater success and satisfaction than working in isolation.
  • ṣṇubhir viṣpardhaso vimahaso jigāti: This phrase can be interpreted as "with the strength of the bonds, the powerful ones thrive." It suggests that strong relationships and connections empower individuals. In real life, this can be seen in the support systems we build, such as friendships and family ties, which provide strength during challenging times.
  • śevṛdho nṛbhiḥ: This means "the one who is revered by men." It signifies the respect and honor given to those who embody these qualities. In a practical context, this can relate to leaders or mentors who inspire others through their actions and wisdom.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates profound philosophical ideas about unity, interconnectedness, and the cyclical nature of existence. Its significance lies in the reminder that we are all part of a larger whole, and that our strength comes from our relationships and collective efforts. In real life, these principles can be applied in various scenarios, such as community building, teamwork, and personal development, encouraging individuals to work together harmoniously for a common purpose. By embodying these teachings, we can foster a more compassionate and supportive society.
