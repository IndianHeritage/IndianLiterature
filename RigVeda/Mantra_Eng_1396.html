सुसंकाशा मातृमृष्टेव योषाविस तंवं कृतुषे दृष्टे कम् |भद्रा त्वम उषो वितरं व्य उच्छ न तत्ते अन्यā उषसो नशन्त ||
susaṁkāśā mātṛmṛṣṭeva yoṣāvis tanvaṁ kṛṇuṣe dṛśe kam | bhadrā tvam uṣo vitaraṁ vy uccha na tat te anyā uṣaso naśanta ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • susaṁkāśā: Well-formed or beautiful
    • mātṛmṛṣṭeva: Like a mother’s touch
    • yoṣā: Woman or feminine energy
    • vis tanvaṁ: Your body or form
    • kṛṇuṣe: You create or manifest
    • dṛśe: For the sight or vision
    • kam: Whom or what
    • bhadrā: Auspicious or good
    • tvam: You
    • uṣo: Dawn or light
    • vitaraṁ: Spread or distribute
    • vy: Indeed
    • uccha: High or elevated
    • na: Not
    • tat: That
    • te: Your
    • anyā: Other
    • uṣaso: Of the dawn
    • naśanta: Fading or diminishing
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the essence of dawn, personifying it as a beautiful woman who brings light and auspiciousness.
    • The phrase "susaṁkāśā mātṛmṛṣṭeva" suggests that the dawn is as nurturing and comforting as a mother's touch, emphasizing the importance of care and warmth in our lives.
    • By asking the dawn to "create" or "manifest" beauty for our sight, it highlights the desire for clarity, positivity, and inspiration in our daily experiences.
    • The mantra also expresses a wish for the dawn to spread its auspiciousness and elevate our lives, indicating a longing for growth and enlightenment.
    • The closing lines remind us that the dawn should not fade away, symbolizing the need for continuous positivity and light in our lives.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, this mantra can be related to the start of a new day, where each morning represents a fresh opportunity to embrace positivity and growth.
    • In personal development, one might meditate on this mantra to cultivate a mindset that welcomes beauty and auspiciousness, akin to how the dawn brings light after darkness.
    • In relationships, the nurturing aspect of the mantra can inspire individuals to approach their loved ones with care and warmth, much like a mother would.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the beauty and positivity that each new day brings, encouraging us to embrace the light and opportunities that come our way.
    • Its significance lies in the nurturing qualities of dawn, urging us to cultivate a mindset that seeks beauty, clarity, and growth in our lives.
    • By incorporating the essence of this mantra into our daily routines, we can foster a more positive and enriching environment for ourselves and those around us.
