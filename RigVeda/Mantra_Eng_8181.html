अश्वो न चक्रदो वृषा सं गा इन्दो सम अर्वतः |वि नो राय दुरो वृद्धि ||
aśvo na cakrado vṛṣā saṁ gā indo sam arvataḥ | vi no rāye duro vṛdhi ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • aśvo: horse
    • na: not
    • cakrado: with a wheel (symbolizing movement or progress)
    • vṛṣā: strong or powerful
    • saṁ: together
    • gā: song or hymn
    • indo: of the moon or the divine
    • sam: together or in harmony
    • arvataḥ: of the strong or powerful
    • vi: for us
    • no: our
    • rāye: wealth or prosperity
    • duro: far or distant
    • vṛdhi: increase or growth
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the strength and power of the horse, symbolizing speed, agility, and progress.
    • The mention of "cakrado" suggests the importance of movement and the cyclical nature of life, akin to the wheels of a chariot.
    • The phrase "saṁ gā indo" emphasizes harmony and unity, indicating that prosperity is achieved when we work together.
    • It calls for divine blessings for wealth and growth, highlighting the need for collaboration and support in achieving success.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, this mantra can be applied to teamwork. Just as a horse pulls a chariot forward, a team must work together to achieve common goals.
    • In community projects, the mantra reflects the importance of collective effort. When individuals unite for a cause, they can achieve greater prosperity and growth.
    • In personal development, the mantra serves as a reminder that progress often requires collaboration and support from others, much like a horse needs a rider to guide it.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of strength, unity, and divine support in achieving prosperity and growth.
    • It teaches us that success is not just an individual endeavor but a collective journey where collaboration and harmony play crucial roles.
    • In real life, whether in personal relationships, professional environments, or community initiatives, the principles embodied in this mantra can guide us toward achieving our goals and enhancing our lives.
