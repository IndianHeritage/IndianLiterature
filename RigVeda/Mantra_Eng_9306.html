अहं स यो नववास्त्वम् बृहद्रथं सं वृत्रेवं दासं वृत्रहारुजम् |यद वर्धयन्तं प्रथयन्तं आनुशग दूरें पारें रजसो रोचनाकरम् ||
ahaṁ sa yo navavāstvam bṛhadrathaṁ saṁ vṛtreva dāsaṁ vṛtrahārujam | yad vardhayantam prathayantam ānuṣag dūre pāre rajaso rocanākaram ||
  • ahaṁ sa yo navavāstvam: This phrase can be translated to "I am he who is new and fresh." It signifies the importance of renewal and the potential for new beginnings in life. For example, in personal development, individuals often seek to reinvent themselves, shedding old habits and embracing new ones.
  • bṛhadrathaṁ saṁ vṛtreva dāsaṁ: This part translates to "the great chariot, like a servant of Vritra." Here, Vritra symbolizes obstacles or challenges. The "great chariot" can represent the strength and support we have in overcoming these challenges. In real life, this could relate to the support systems we build, such as friends, family, or mentors, who help us navigate difficulties.
  • vṛtrahārujam: This term means "the one who destroys Vritra." It emphasizes the power of overcoming obstacles. In a practical sense, this can be seen in how individuals confront and overcome fears or societal barriers, such as a student overcoming academic challenges to achieve their goals.
  • yad vardhayantam prathayantam: This translates to "that which promotes and brings forth." It highlights the importance of growth and development. In everyday life, this can be related to nurturing talents and skills, such as a coach encouraging an athlete to reach their full potential.
  • ānuṣag dūre pāre rajaso rocanākaram: This phrase means "far and wide, illuminating the realms of desire." It suggests the idea of spreading knowledge and positivity across various aspects of life. For instance, a teacher who inspires students to pursue their dreams is illuminating their paths and helping them navigate their desires.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the themes of renewal, overcoming obstacles, growth, and the dissemination of knowledge. Its significance lies in its applicability to various life scenarios, such as personal development, mentorship, and the pursuit of goals. By embodying the principles expressed in this mantra, individuals can strive for continuous improvement, support one another in overcoming challenges, and illuminate the paths of those around them, ultimately contributing to a more enlightened and supportive community.
