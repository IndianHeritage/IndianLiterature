पतङ्गम् अक्तम् असुरस्य मायया हृदा पश्यन्ति मनसा विपश्चितः |समुद्रे अन्तः कवयो वि चक्षते मरीचीनाम् पदम् इच्छन्ति वेधसः ||
pataṁgam aktam asurasya māyayā hṛdā paśyanti manasā vipaścitaḥ | samudre antaḥ kavayo vi cakṣate marīcīnām padam icchanti vedhasaḥ ||
  • pataṁgam aktam asurasya māyayā: This phrase can be translated as "the bird (or the winged one) is bound by the illusion of the asuras (demons)." Here, the "bird" symbolizes the soul or consciousness, while "asuras" represent the forces of ignorance and illusion that bind us.
  • hṛdā paśyanti manasā vipaścitaḥ: This means "the wise see with their hearts and minds." It emphasizes the importance of inner vision and understanding, suggesting that true wisdom comes from a deeper perception beyond mere intellect.
  • samudre antaḥ kavayo vi cakṣate: Translated, this means "the seers perceive the depths of the ocean." The "ocean" symbolizes the vastness of knowledge and existence, and the "seers" are those who have the insight to explore and understand these depths.
  • marīcīnām padam icchanti vedhasaḥ: This translates to "the wise desire the path of the rays of light." Here, "marīcī" refers to rays of light, symbolizing knowledge, enlightenment, and the pursuit of truth.

  • Significance: This mantra highlights the journey of the soul from ignorance to enlightenment. It emphasizes the need for inner wisdom and the ability to see beyond the illusions of the material world.
  • Real-life Application: In our daily lives, we often encounter distractions and illusions that can cloud our judgment. This mantra serves as a reminder to seek deeper understanding and clarity. For example, in decision-making, rather than relying solely on surface-level information, we should strive to understand the underlying truths and motivations.
  • Conclusion: The mantra encourages us to cultivate inner wisdom and to seek the light of knowledge amidst the darkness of ignorance. It is applicable in various aspects of life, including personal growth, relationships, and professional endeavors, guiding us to make informed and enlightened choices.
