इमां खानाम्योषधिं वीरुधं बलवत्तमाम् |यया sapatnīm बाधते यया संविन्दते पतिम् ||
imāṁ khanāmy oṣadhiṁ vīrudham balavattamām | yayā sapatnīm bādhate yayā saṁvindate patim ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • imāṁ: this
    • khanāmy: I invoke or I call upon
    • oṣadhiṁ: the medicinal herbs or plants
    • vīrudham: that which is flourishing or strong
    • balavattamām: the most powerful or potent
    • yayā: by which
    • sapatnīm: the rival or enemy
    • bādhate: is harmed or obstructed
    • yayā: by which
    • saṁvindate: one attains or realizes
    • patim: the lord or master
  • Overall Meaning: This mantra expresses a prayer for the invocation of powerful medicinal herbs that can protect and empower the individual. It emphasizes the ability of these herbs to overcome adversaries and to help one realize their true potential or lordship over their circumstances.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • Herbal Medicine: In modern times, many people turn to herbal remedies for healing and wellness. For instance, turmeric is known for its anti-inflammatory properties and is often used to combat various ailments.
    • Personal Empowerment: The mantra can be related to personal development practices where individuals seek to harness their inner strength to overcome challenges, similar to how one might use affirmations or meditation to build confidence.
    • Environmental Awareness: The invocation of flourishing plants can also be seen in the context of environmental conservation, where protecting and nurturing medicinal plants is crucial for biodiversity and health.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its holistic approach to health and empowerment. It highlights the importance of nature in providing remedies and the strength that can be derived from it. In real life, this mantra can inspire individuals to seek natural solutions for their health, to cultivate resilience against adversities, and to recognize the power of nature in their lives. It serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of life, health, and personal strength.
