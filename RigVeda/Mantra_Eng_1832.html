प्रति वा एनā नमसाहम् एमी सूक्तेन भिक्षे सुमतिं तुराणाम् |रराणता मरुतो वेद्याभिर नि हेळो धत्त विं मुचध्वम् अश्वान् ||
prati va enā namasāham emi sūktena bhikṣe sumatiṁ turāṇām | rarāṇatā maruto vedyābhir ni heḻo dhatta vi mucadhvam aśvān ||
  • Mantra: "prati va enā namasāham emi sūktena bhikṣe sumatiṁ turāṇām | rarāṇatā maruto vedyābhir ni heḻo dhatta vi mucadhvam aśvān ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • prati: towards
    • va: indeed
    • enā: this (feminine form)
    • namasāham: I bow down
    • emi: I come
    • sūktena: with the hymn
    • bhikṣe: in the act of seeking
    • sumatiṁ: good understanding or wisdom
    • turāṇām: of the swift ones
    • rarāṇatā: the power of the winds
    • maruto: O Maruts (the storm deities)
    • vedyābhir: with knowledge
    • ni: not
    • heḻo: to be restrained
    • dhatta: hold
    • vi: indeed
    • mucadhvam: release
    • aśvān: the horses (symbolizing energy or power)
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra expresses a deep reverence and a call for wisdom and understanding.
    • The speaker bows down and seeks to come closer to the divine through the power of hymns.
    • It emphasizes the importance of good understanding and wisdom, especially in the context of swift actions and decisions.
    • The reference to the Maruts, the storm deities, signifies the dynamic and powerful forces of nature, which can be harnessed for positive outcomes.
    • The mantra encourages the release of energy (symbolized by horses) to achieve one's goals and aspirations.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, this mantra can be applied when teams seek to innovate and make swift decisions. By fostering a culture of wisdom and understanding, organizations can harness the collective energy of their members to achieve success.
    • In personal life, individuals can reflect on this mantra when facing challenges. By seeking wisdom and understanding, one can navigate through difficulties with clarity and purpose.
    • In community initiatives, this mantra can inspire collective action towards a common goal, encouraging members to release their energies for the greater good.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of seeking wisdom and understanding in all aspects of life.
    • It serves as a reminder to harness the dynamic forces around us, whether in personal endeavors or collective actions.
    • By bowing down to the divine and seeking guidance, we can navigate life's challenges with greater clarity and purpose, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling existence.
