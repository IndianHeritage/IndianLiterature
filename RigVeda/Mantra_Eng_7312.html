यो वां राजांस्य अश्विनाऽ रथो वियाति रोडसी |अन्ति षड् भूत्वां अवः ||
yo vāṁ rajāṁsy aśvinā ratho viyāti rodasī | anti ṣad bhūtu vām avaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yo: who
    • vāṁ: your
    • rajāṁsy: kingship or royal power
    • aśvinā: O Ashvins (the twin deities)
    • ratho: chariot
    • viyāti: moves or travels
    • rodasī: in the sky or the heavens
    • anti: may they
    • ṣad: six
    • bhūtu: beings or entities
    • vām: your
    • avaḥ: bring or lead
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the Ashvins, who are considered divine physicians and bringers of health and prosperity.
    • The phrase "who your kingship" suggests a connection to leadership and authority, indicating that the Ashvins are being called upon to bestow their blessings on those in power.
    • The mention of "chariot" symbolizes movement and progress, indicating that the blessings of the Ashvins can help in the journey of life.
    • "In the sky" signifies the divine realm, suggesting that the blessings are not just earthly but also celestial.
    • The phrase "may they bring six beings" can be interpreted as a call for the Ashvins to bring forth various forms of life or prosperity, emphasizing abundance and well-being.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a leadership context, this mantra can be recited by leaders or individuals in positions of authority to seek guidance and blessings for wise governance.
    • In health and wellness practices, individuals may chant this mantra to invoke the healing powers of the Ashvins, especially during times of illness or distress.
    • During significant life events, such as weddings or new ventures, this mantra can be used to seek blessings for prosperity and success.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of divine guidance in leadership and the journey of life.
    • It highlights the interconnectedness of health, prosperity, and divine blessings, making it relevant in various aspects of life.
    • By invoking the Ashvins, individuals can seek support in their endeavors, ensuring that they are aligned with higher powers for success and well-being.
