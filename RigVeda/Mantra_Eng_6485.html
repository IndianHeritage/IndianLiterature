वयं उ त्वां अपूर्व्य स्थूरं न कच चिद् भरन्तो 'वस्यवः |वाजे चित्रं हवामहे ||
vayam u tvām apūrvya sthūraṁ na kac cid bharanto 'vasyavaḥ | vāje citraṁ havāmahe ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • vayam: We
    • u: indeed
    • tvām: you
    • apūrvya: unique or unprecedented
    • sthūraṁ: strong or powerful
    • na: not
    • kac cid: anything at all
    • bharanto: bearing or carrying
    • 'vasyavaḥ: those who are under control or influence
    • vāje: in the context of strength or power
    • citraṁ: splendid or remarkable
    • havāmahe: we invoke or we call upon
  • Overall Meaning:
    • This mantra expresses a collective invocation to a powerful and unique entity, emphasizing the strength and control that this entity possesses.
    • It suggests that the speaker and their community are not burdened by anything trivial, as they seek the support and influence of this powerful force.
    • The use of "citraṁ" indicates that the qualities or blessings being sought are not only strong but also remarkable and extraordinary.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, a team may invoke the spirit of collaboration and innovation when embarking on a new project, seeking to harness the unique strengths of each member to achieve remarkable results.
    • In personal life, individuals may call upon their inner strength and resilience during challenging times, reminding themselves that they are not alone and that they possess the power to overcome obstacles.
    • In community gatherings, people may come together to invoke a sense of unity and strength, recognizing that their collective efforts can lead to extraordinary outcomes, such as organizing a successful event or supporting a charitable cause.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for strength, unity, and the pursuit of extraordinary achievements.
    • It serves as a reminder that by coming together and invoking powerful influences, individuals and communities can overcome challenges and create remarkable outcomes.
    • This mantra can be applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal struggles to collective endeavors, emphasizing the importance of strength and collaboration in achieving greatness.
