अग्निषोमा सवेदसā सहूतī वनतं गिरः |सं देवत्रा बभूवथुः ||
agnīṣomā savedasā sahūtī vanataṁ giraḥ | saṁ devatrā babhūvathuḥ ||
  • Agnīṣomā: This term refers to the duality of Agni (fire) and Soma (the moon). Agni symbolizes energy, transformation, and purification, while Soma represents nourishment, tranquility, and the mind. Together, they embody the balance of opposites in nature.
  • Savedasā: This word can be interpreted as "with knowledge" or "with wisdom." It emphasizes the importance of understanding and insight in our actions and decisions.
  • Sahūtī: This term signifies "together" or "in unity." It highlights the significance of collaboration and collective effort in achieving goals.
  • Vanataṁ: This word means "to be directed" or "to be guided." It suggests the need for direction and purpose in our endeavors.
  • Giraḥ: This term translates to "words" or "speech." It underscores the power of communication and expression in human interactions.
  • Saṁ: This prefix means "together" or "with." It reinforces the theme of unity and collective action.
  • Devatrā: This word refers to "the divine" or "gods." It indicates the presence of higher powers or spiritual forces in our lives.
  • Babhūvathuḥ: This term means "may they become" or "may they manifest." It expresses a desire for transformation and realization of potential.

Conclusion: The mantra "agnīṣomā savedasā sahūtī vanataṁ giraḥ | saṁ devatrā babhūvathuḥ ||" encapsulates the essence of balance, wisdom, unity, and divine guidance. In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as teamwork in a workplace, where collaboration and shared knowledge lead to success. It also serves as a reminder to seek balance in our lives, integrating both energy (Agni) and tranquility (Soma) to achieve holistic well-being. By recognizing the power of our words and the importance of divine guidance, we can navigate challenges more effectively and work towards our goals with purpose and clarity.
