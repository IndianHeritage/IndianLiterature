स मर्मृजान आयुभिर इभो राजेव सुव्रतः |श्येनो न वंसु शीदति ||
sa marmṛjāna āyubhir ibho rājeva suvrataḥ | śyeno na vaṁsu ṣīdati ||
  • sa marmṛjāna: This phrase can be interpreted as "those who are engaged in the pursuit of life" or "those who are striving for longevity." It emphasizes the importance of actively seeking a fulfilling life.
  • āyubhir: This word translates to "with life" or "by means of life." It suggests that life itself is a valuable resource that should be cherished and utilized wisely.
  • ibho: This term means "like an elephant." Elephants are often seen as symbols of strength, wisdom, and longevity in various cultures. This comparison implies that one should embody these qualities in their life journey.
  • rājeva: This translates to "like a king." It signifies the importance of ruling one's own life with authority and responsibility, making decisions that lead to a prosperous existence.
  • suvrataḥ: This word means "with good vows" or "with righteous intentions." It highlights the significance of having a moral compass and making commitments that align with ethical values.
  • śyeno: This term refers to "the falcon" or "the bird of prey." Falcons are known for their keen vision and ability to soar high, symbolizing clarity of purpose and the pursuit of higher goals.
  • na vaṁsu: This phrase means "not falling" or "not failing." It emphasizes resilience and the importance of maintaining one's course despite challenges.
  • ṣīdati: This word translates to "to sink" or "to decline." It serves as a warning against complacency and the consequences of not striving for excellence.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of living a purposeful and fulfilling life. It encourages individuals to actively engage in their life journey, embody strength and wisdom, rule their lives with responsibility, and uphold ethical values. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various contexts such as personal development, leadership, and ethical decision-making. For instance, a leader in a corporate setting can draw inspiration from this mantra to lead with integrity and vision, ensuring that their actions contribute positively to their team and organization. Ultimately, the mantra serves as a reminder to strive for greatness while remaining grounded in moral principles, ensuring that one does not falter in their pursuits.
