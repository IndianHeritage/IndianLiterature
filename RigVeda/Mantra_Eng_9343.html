सतो नूनं कवयः सं शिशीत वाशीभिर याभिर अमृताय तक्षथा |विद्वांसः पदा गुह्यानी कर्तन येन देवासो अमृतत्वम आनशुः ||
sato nūnaṁ kavayaḥ saṁ śiśīta vāśībhir yābhir amṛtāya takṣatha | vidvāṁsaḥ padā guhyāni kartana yena devāso amṛtatvam ānaśuḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sato: of the good or the true
    • nūnaṁ: indeed
    • kavayaḥ: the wise or the seers
    • saṁ: together
    • śiśīta: have thought or contemplated
    • vāśībhir: by those who are endowed with wisdom
    • yābhir: by whom
    • amṛtāya: for immortality
    • takṣatha: you create or fashion
    • vidvāṁsaḥ: the knowledgeable or the learned
    • padā: by the path
    • guhyāni: the secret or hidden
    • kartana: by the act of doing
    • yena: by which
    • devāso: the divine beings or gods
    • amṛtatvam: immortality
    • ānaśuḥ: they attain or achieve
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the collective wisdom of seers and sages who contemplate the essence of truth.
    • It emphasizes the importance of collaboration and shared knowledge in the pursuit of immortality.
    • The wise individuals, through their actions and understanding, can unlock the secrets of existence and attain a divine state.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern science, collaboration among researchers leads to breakthroughs in medicine, which can be seen as a pursuit of 'immortality' through health.
    • In community initiatives, when people come together to share knowledge and resources, they can create sustainable solutions that benefit all, reflecting the essence of this mantra.
    • In personal development, seeking wisdom from mentors and sharing experiences with peers can lead to profound insights and growth, akin to achieving a higher state of being.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of wisdom, collaboration, and the pursuit of truth in achieving a higher state of existence.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from scientific research to community building and personal growth.
    • Ultimately, it teaches us that through collective effort and understanding, we can unlock the secrets of life and strive for a form of immortality, whether it be through knowledge, legacy, or spiritual enlightenment.
