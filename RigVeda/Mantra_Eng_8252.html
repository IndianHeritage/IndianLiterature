अस्य ते सख्ये वयं इयक्षन्तस तवोतयः |इन्दो सखित्वम् उष्मसि ||
asya te sakhye vayam iyakṣantas tvotayaḥ | indo sakhitvam uśmasi ||
  • Mantra: "asya te sakhye vayam iyakṣantas tvotayaḥ | indo sakhitvam uśmasi ||"
  • Translation: "In friendship with you, we are united; may we be strong together. O Indra, may we share in the warmth of friendship."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • asya: of this
    • te: your
    • sakhye: in friendship
    • vayam: we
    • iyakṣantas: united or connected
    • tvotayaḥ: may we be strong
    • indo: O Indra (a deity representing strength and power)
    • sakhitvam: the quality of friendship
    • uśmasi: may we share warmth or affection
  • Meaning and Explanation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of friendship and unity among individuals.
    • It invokes the deity Indra, symbolizing strength and support, to bless the bond of friendship.
    • The phrase "may we be strong together" highlights the idea that collective strength is essential for overcoming challenges.
    • In real life, this can be seen in various contexts, such as teamwork in a workplace, where collaboration leads to greater success.
    • Another example is in personal relationships, where friends support each other through difficult times, enhancing emotional resilience.
  • Significance:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the value of camaraderie and mutual support.
    • It encourages individuals to foster strong relationships, whether in personal or professional settings.
    • By invoking the spirit of Indra, it reinforces the idea that divine blessings can enhance human connections.
    • In contemporary society, this mantra can inspire community building, where people come together to support one another in various endeavors.
