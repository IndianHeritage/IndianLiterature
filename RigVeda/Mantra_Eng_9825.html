यस्तुभ्यं अग्ने अमृताय मर्त्यः समिधा दाशद उता व हविष्कृतिः |तस्य होताभवसि यासि दूत्यम उप ब्रूषे यजस्य अध्वरियसि ||
yas tubhyam agne amṛtāya martyaḥ samidhā dāśad uta vā haviṣkṛti | tasya hotā bhavasi yāsi dūtya1m upa brūṣe yajasy adhvarīyasi ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yas: who
    • tubhyam: to you
    • agne: O Agni (the fire god)
    • amṛtāya: for immortality
    • martyaḥ: mortal beings
    • samidhā: offering (sacrificial)
    • dāśad: tenfold
    • uta: or
    • vā: or
    • haviṣkṛti: the offerings made
  • Meaning of the Mantra:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Agni, the fire god, who is considered a mediator between humans and the divine.
    • The phrase "O Agni, for the sake of immortality, we offer our sacrifices" signifies the importance of seeking divine blessings for a long and fulfilling life.
    • The term "dāśad" implies that the offerings made to Agni are not just for personal gain but are multiplied, suggesting that selfless acts yield greater rewards.
    • The mantra emphasizes the role of Agni as a hota (priest) who facilitates the connection between the worshippers and the divine, urging him to communicate our intentions and desires.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern life, this mantra can be related to the practice of making offerings or sacrifices in various forms, such as charitable acts, community service, or environmental conservation efforts.
    • For instance, when individuals contribute to a cause, they are not only seeking personal satisfaction but also aiming to create a positive impact on society, which can be seen as a tenfold return of goodwill.
    • In a corporate setting, businesses that engage in corporate social responsibility (CSR) initiatives embody the essence of this mantra by investing in the community and environment, thus fostering a sense of immortality through legacy.
  • Conclusion and Significance:
    • This mantra highlights the importance of selfless offerings and the pursuit of immortality through good deeds.
    • It serves as a reminder that our actions, when aligned with higher principles, can lead to greater rewards not just for ourselves but for the community at large.
    • In essence, it encourages individuals to act with intention and purpose, fostering a connection with the divine and ensuring that their contributions resonate beyond their lifetime.
