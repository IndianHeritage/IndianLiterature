सद्यश्चिद्यः शवसापञ्च कृष्टिः सूर्य इव ज्योतीषापस ततान |सहस्रसाः शतसाऽस्य रंहिर न स्मा वरन्ते युवतिं न शर्याम् ||
sadyaś cid yaḥ śavasā pañca kṛṣṭīḥ sūrya iva jyotiṣāpas tatāna | sahasrasāḥ śatasā asya raṁhir na smā varante yuvatiṁ na śaryām ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sadyaḥ: immediately, at once
    • cid: indeed, certainly
    • yaḥ: who
    • śavasā: with the body, with the form
    • pañca: five
    • kṛṣṭīḥ: the fields, the realms
    • sūrya: sun
    • iva: like, as
    • jyotiṣā: light, illumination
    • apas: from, away from
    • tatāna: he spread, he extended
    • sahasrasāḥ: thousands
    • śatasā: hundreds
    • asya: of this
    • raṁhir: delight, joy
    • na: not
    • smā: indeed
    • varante: they choose, they desire
    • yuvatiṁ: young woman, maiden
    • na: not
    • śaryām: of the beautiful, of the lovely
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the divine presence that spreads light and joy across various realms.
    • The reference to "five fields" can symbolize the five elements (earth, water, fire, air, and space) that constitute our universe.
    • The sun is a powerful metaphor for knowledge and enlightenment, illuminating the path for all beings.
    • The phrase "thousands and hundreds" signifies abundance and the vastness of joy and delight that can be experienced.
    • It suggests that true joy is not found in transient beauty or youth, but in the deeper, eternal truths of existence.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In our daily lives, we often seek happiness in material possessions or fleeting experiences. This mantra reminds us that true joy comes from understanding and connecting with the deeper aspects of life.
    • For instance, a person may find joy in nature, appreciating the beauty of the sun rising over a landscape, rather than in superficial pursuits.
    • In relationships, the mantra encourages us to value deeper connections over mere physical attraction, fostering lasting bonds based on mutual understanding and respect.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking deeper truths and joy in life, rather than being distracted by superficial appearances.
    • Its significance lies in guiding individuals towards enlightenment and a more profound understanding of existence.
    • In practical terms, it encourages mindfulness and appreciation of the present moment, leading to a more fulfilling and joyful life.
