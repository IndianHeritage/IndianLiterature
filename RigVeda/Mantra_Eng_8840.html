अधā ह्य अग्ने मह्ना निषद्यā सद्यो जजñāनो हव्यो बभूथा |तं ते देवासो अनु केतम आयन्न अधावर्धन्त प्रथमास ऊमाः ||
adhā hy agne mahnā niṣadyā sadyo jajñāno havyo babhūtha | taṁ te devāso anu ketam āyann adhāvardhanta prathamāsa ūmāḥ ||
  • adhā hy agne: This phrase translates to "Indeed, O Agni (the Fire God)". Agni is a significant deity in Vedic literature, representing fire, energy, and transformation. In real life, fire symbolizes purification and the transformative power of energy.
  • mahnā niṣadyā: This means "by the great power". It emphasizes the strength and potency of Agni. In our daily lives, we often encounter situations where we need to harness our inner strength to overcome challenges, much like how fire can be both a tool and a force of nature.
  • sadyo jajñāno: This translates to "born immediately". It signifies the immediacy and urgency of creation and manifestation. In real life, this can be related to moments of inspiration or creativity that arise suddenly, leading to new ideas or projects.
  • havyo babhūtha: This means "worthy of offerings". It indicates that Agni is deserving of respect and offerings, symbolizing the importance of gratitude and acknowledgment in our lives. For example, in various cultures, people express gratitude through rituals, ceremonies, or simple acts of kindness.
  • taṁ te devāso anu ketam āyann: This translates to "the gods have approached him". It suggests that divine forces are drawn to the energy and offerings made to Agni. In a real-life context, this can be seen in how positive actions and intentions attract good fortune and support from others.
  • adhāvardhanta prathamāsa ūmāḥ: This means "they have increased the first offerings". It highlights the idea of growth and abundance that comes from honoring the divine. In practical terms, this can relate to how nurturing relationships and being generous can lead to greater rewards and fulfillment in life.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of Agni as a transformative force and the importance of honoring divine energies through our actions and intentions. In real life, it teaches us about the power of inner strength, the immediacy of creativity, the value of gratitude, and the benefits of nurturing relationships. By applying these principles, we can create a more fulfilling and abundant life, drawing positive energies and support from the universe around us.
