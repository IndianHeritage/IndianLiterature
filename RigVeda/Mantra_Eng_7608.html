त्वं इन्द्राभिभूर् असि त्वं सूर्यं अरोचयः |विश्वकर्मा विश्वदेवो महाम् असि ||
tvam indrābhibhūr asi tvaṁ sūryam arocayaḥ | viśvakarmā viśvadevo mahām̐ asi ||
  • tvam indrābhibhūr asi: This phrase translates to "You are the one who is endowed with the power of Indra." Indra is the king of the gods in Vedic mythology, symbolizing strength, power, and leadership. In real life, this can be interpreted as recognizing the inherent strength and leadership qualities within oneself or in others.
  • tvaṁ sūryam arocayaḥ: This means "You are the one who shines like the sun." The sun is often associated with vitality, energy, and enlightenment. In practical terms, this can refer to individuals who inspire others through their actions and positivity, much like a leader who brings light and guidance to their community.
  • viśvakarmā: This term refers to "the all-creating" or "the universal architect." It signifies creativity and the ability to manifest ideas into reality. In everyday life, this can be seen in innovators and creators who bring new concepts to life, such as entrepreneurs or artists who shape culture and society.
  • viśvadevo: This translates to "the universal god" or "the deity of all." It emphasizes the interconnectedness of all beings and the divine presence in every aspect of life. This can be reflected in the idea of community and the importance of recognizing the divine in everyone, promoting unity and harmony.
  • mahām̐ asi: This means "You are the great one." It acknowledges the greatness and potential within every individual. In real life, this can serve as a reminder to recognize and cultivate our own greatness and that of others, fostering an environment of encouragement and support.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of strength, creativity, and divinity present in every individual. It serves as a reminder to recognize our own potential and the potential in others, encouraging leadership, creativity, and unity. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in leadership roles, community building, and personal development, inspiring individuals to shine brightly and contribute positively to society. By embodying these qualities, we can create a more harmonious and empowered world.
