मन्त्र: असि हि वीर सेन्योऽसि भूरी पराददिः |असि दभ्रस्य चिद्वृधो यजमानाय शिक्षसि सुन्वते भूरी ते वसु ||
asi hi vīra senyo 'si bhūri parādadiḥ | asi dabhrasya cid vṛdho yajamānāya śikṣasi sunvate bhūri te vasu ||
  • Translation: "Indeed, you are the hero of the army, the one who bestows abundant wealth. You are the one who, through your wisdom, teaches the worshipper to attain great prosperity."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • asi: "you are" - a declaration of identity and presence.
    • hi: "indeed" - emphasizes the truth of the statement.
    • vīra: "hero" - signifies bravery and valor.
    • senyo: "of the army" - relates to strength in numbers and collective effort.
    • bhūri: "abundant" - indicates plenty or richness.
    • parādadiḥ: "bestower" - one who gives or grants.
    • dabhrasya: "of the wise" - refers to intelligence and insight.
    • cid: "through wisdom" - signifies the importance of knowledge.
    • vṛdho: "growing" - implies development and progress.
    • yajamānāya: "to the worshipper" - indicates the recipient of blessings.
    • śikṣasi: "you teach" - denotes guidance and instruction.
    • sunvate: "to attain" - signifies achievement and success.
    • bhūri te: "great for you" - emphasizes the magnitude of the blessings.
    • vasu: "wealth" - refers to material and spiritual abundance.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, a leader who embodies the qualities of a "vīra" inspires their team to work together towards a common goal, fostering a sense of unity and strength.
    • A mentor who imparts wisdom to their mentees exemplifies the role of "dabhrasya," guiding them to achieve their personal and professional aspirations.
    • In community service, individuals who dedicate their time and resources to help others reflect the essence of "bhūri parādadiḥ," as they bestow wealth in the form of support and care.
  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of leadership, wisdom, and the collective effort of individuals working towards prosperity. It signifies that true wealth is not just material but also encompasses knowledge, guidance, and the ability to uplift others. In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as in leadership roles, mentorship, and community service, where individuals strive to inspire and support others, ultimately leading to a more prosperous and harmonious society.
