उपेमा सृष्क्षि वाजयुर वचस्यां चनो दधीत नाद्यो गिरो मे |अपां नपाद आशुहेमा कुवित स सुपेशसस करति जोषिषद हि ||
upem asṛkṣi vājayur vacasyāṁ cano dadhīta nādyo giro me | apāṁ napād āśuhemā kuvit sa supeśasas karati joṣiṣad dhi ||
  • upem asṛkṣi: This phrase can be interpreted as "may we be endowed with strength and vigor." The term "upem" signifies a desire for empowerment, while "asṛkṣi" relates to the idea of being filled with energy or vitality.
  • vājayur: This word translates to "the one who brings victory" or "the one who is strong." It emphasizes the importance of strength and success in endeavors, akin to a warrior who triumphs in battle.
  • vacasyāṁ cano dadhīta: This part means "may my words be powerful and effective." It highlights the significance of communication and the impact of spoken words in influencing others and achieving goals.
  • nādyo giro me: This translates to "may my words resonate and reach their destination." It suggests the importance of clarity and purpose in communication, ensuring that messages are understood and appreciated.
  • apāṁ napād: This phrase can be interpreted as "may I be free from obstacles." It reflects the desire to overcome challenges and barriers in life, similar to how a river flows smoothly without hindrance.
  • āśuhemā: This term signifies "swift and agile." It emphasizes the need for quick thinking and adaptability in various situations, much like a successful athlete who responds rapidly to changing circumstances.
  • kuvit sa supeśasas: This can be understood as "may I be surrounded by good company." It stresses the importance of positive relationships and support systems in achieving success, akin to a team working together towards a common goal.
  • karati joṣiṣad dhi: This phrase means "may I act with enthusiasm and energy." It encourages taking action with passion and commitment, similar to an artist pouring their heart into a masterpiece.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of strength, effective communication, overcoming obstacles, and the importance of positive relationships. In real life, it serves as a reminder to approach challenges with vigor, to communicate clearly and purposefully, and to surround oneself with supportive individuals. Whether in personal endeavors, professional settings, or community engagements, the principles outlined in this mantra can guide individuals towards success and fulfillment. By embodying these qualities, one can navigate life's complexities with confidence and resilience.
