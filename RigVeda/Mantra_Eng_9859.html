अधीन nv अत्र सप्ततिं च सप्त च |सद्यो दिदिष्ट तान्वः सद्यो दिदिष्ट पार्थ्यः सद्यो दिदिष्ट मायवः ||
adhīn nv atra saptatiṁ ca sapta ca | sadyo didiṣṭa tānvaḥ sadyo didiṣṭa pārthyaḥ sadyo didiṣṭa māyavaḥ ||
  • adhīn nv atra saptatiṁ ca sapta ca: This phrase can be translated to "Here, we have the seven and the seventy." The number seven often symbolizes completeness and perfection in various cultures, while seventy can represent a larger, more expansive completeness. In real life, this can be seen in the context of teamwork, where a small group (the seven) works together to achieve a larger goal (the seventy).
  • sadyo didiṣṭa tānvaḥ: This translates to "immediately, we desire the bodies." This can refer to the immediate needs and desires of individuals or communities. For example, in a business setting, the immediate needs of employees (such as fair wages and good working conditions) are crucial for maintaining morale and productivity.
  • sadyo didiṣṭa pārthyaḥ: This means "immediately, we desire the earthly." This can be interpreted as a longing for material or earthly possessions. In modern life, this could relate to the pursuit of wealth, property, or resources that provide comfort and security.
  • sadyo didiṣṭa māyavaḥ: This translates to "immediately, we desire the illusory." This phrase can refer to the transient nature of desires and material possessions. In real life, this can be seen in consumer culture, where people often chase after the latest trends or gadgets, only to find that these do not bring lasting happiness.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of recognizing our immediate desires and the various aspects of life they pertain to—be it physical, material, or illusory. In a world where instant gratification is prevalent, this mantra serves as a reminder to reflect on what we truly need versus what we merely desire. It encourages individuals to seek balance in their pursuits, understanding that while immediate needs are important, they should not overshadow deeper, more meaningful aspirations. This can be applied in various real-life scenarios, such as in personal relationships, career choices, and financial decisions, guiding individuals to make choices that align with their true values and long-term goals.
