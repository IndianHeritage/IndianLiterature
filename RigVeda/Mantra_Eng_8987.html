जुषद धव्यā मानवस्योर्ध्वस तस्थाव ऋभ्वा यज्ञे |मिन्वन् सद्म पुर एति ||
juṣad dhavyā mānuṣasyordhvas tasthāv ṛbhvā yajñe | minvan sadma pura eti ||
  • juṣad: This word translates to "may you enjoy" or "may you be pleased." It signifies the importance of joy and satisfaction in life.
  • dhavyā: Refers to "sacrificial offerings" or "that which is offered." This highlights the significance of giving and sharing in various forms, whether material or spiritual.
  • mānuṣasy: This means "of humans" or "of mankind." It emphasizes the human experience and the collective journey of humanity.
  • ordhvas: Translates to "upward" or "higher." It suggests striving for higher ideals, aspirations, and spiritual growth.
  • tasthāv: This means "stood" or "remained." It indicates stability and the importance of standing firm in one's beliefs and values.
  • ṛbhvā: Refers to "the wise" or "the enlightened." This points to the value of wisdom and knowledge in guiding one's actions and decisions.
  • yajñe: This translates to "in the sacrifice" or "in the act of worship." It signifies the importance of rituals and the connection to the divine through acts of devotion.
  • minvan: Means "by means of" or "through." It suggests that actions and intentions are the means to achieve higher goals.
  • sadma: Refers to "home" or "abode." It symbolizes a place of safety, comfort, and belonging.
  • pura: This means "city" or "place." It represents the community and the social structures that support human life.
  • eti: Translates to "goes" or "approaches." It indicates movement towards a goal or destination.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of human existence, emphasizing joy, sacrifice, wisdom, and the pursuit of higher ideals. It serves as a reminder that in our journey through life, we should strive for personal and collective growth while remaining grounded in our values. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as community service, personal development, and spiritual practices. By embracing the principles of joy, giving, and wisdom, individuals can create a harmonious and fulfilling life, contributing positively to society and fostering a sense of belonging and purpose.
