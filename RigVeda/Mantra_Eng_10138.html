कृषन्नित्फालाशितं कृत्य यन्नध्वानमपवृङ्क्ते चरित्रैः |वदन ब्रह्मावदतो वनीयान पृणन्नापीर अपृणन्तमभिष्यात् ||
kṛṣann it phāla āśitaṁ kṛṇoti yann adhvānam apa vṛṅkte caritraiḥ | vadan brahmāvadato vanīyān pṛṇann āpir apṛṇantam abhi ṣyāt ||
  • kṛṣann it phāla āśitaṁ: This phrase can be translated as "the one who cultivates and enjoys the fruits." It signifies the importance of hard work and the rewards that come from it. In real life, this can be seen in the context of farmers who toil in their fields to produce crops, ultimately reaping the benefits of their labor.
  • kṛṇoti yann adhvānam: This means "he who creates the path." It emphasizes the idea of forging one's own way in life. For example, entrepreneurs often create new business models or products, paving the way for innovation and progress in society.
  • apa vṛṅkte caritraiḥ: This translates to "he who removes obstacles through good deeds." It highlights the significance of ethical actions and integrity. In everyday life, individuals who engage in community service or help others often find that they can overcome personal challenges and inspire others to do the same.
  • vadan brahmāvadato vanīyān: This phrase means "speaking like Brahma, the creator." It suggests the power of words and communication. In real life, effective communication can lead to successful negotiations, conflict resolution, and the building of strong relationships.
  • pṛṇann āpir apṛṇantam abhi ṣyāt: This can be interpreted as "may he who is generous and gives abundantly be blessed." It underscores the value of generosity and sharing. For instance, charitable acts, such as donating to those in need, not only help the recipients but also enrich the giver's life through a sense of fulfillment and community connection.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of hard work, ethical living, effective communication, and generosity. Its significance lies in the reminder that our actions, words, and intentions shape our lives and the lives of those around us. In real-life scenarios, applying these principles can lead to personal growth, community development, and a more harmonious society. By cultivating our skills, creating paths for ourselves and others, acting with integrity, communicating wisely, and sharing generously, we contribute to a better world.
