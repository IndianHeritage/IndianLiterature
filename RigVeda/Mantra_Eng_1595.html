मथीद यद ईं विष्टो मातरिश्वा होतारं विश्वाप्सुं विश्वदेव्यं |न यं दधुर मनुष्यासु विक्षु स्वर्ण न चित्रम वपुषे विभावम् ||
mathīd yad īṁ viṣṭo mātariśvā hotāraṁ viśvāpsuṁ viśvadevyam | ni yaṁ dadhur manuṣyāsu vikṣu sva1r ṇa citraṁ vapuṣe vibhāvam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • mathīd: May we think or contemplate
    • yad: that which
    • īṁ: the divine or the sacred
    • viṣṭo: pervading or residing
    • mātariśvā: the wind or the cosmic force
    • hotāraṁ: the one who invokes or calls upon
    • viśvāpsuṁ: the all-pervading essence
    • viśvadevyam: the universal divine or goddess
    • ni: not
    • yaṁ: whom
    • dadhur: they have held or grasped
    • manuṣyāsu: among humans
    • vikṣu: in the various forms
    • sva1r: the self or the essence
    • ṇa: not
    • citraṁ: diverse or varied
    • vapuṣe: in the form or body
    • vibhāvam: the manifestation or appearance
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra encourages contemplation of the divine essence that pervades all existence.
    • It highlights the importance of recognizing the universal divine force, represented by the wind (mātariśvā), which connects all beings.
    • The mantra suggests that humans often fail to grasp the true essence of this divine force, which manifests in various forms.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, we often overlook the interconnectedness of all beings. For instance, in nature, the wind carries seeds, enabling plants to grow in new areas, symbolizing the divine force at work.
    • In relationships, recognizing the divine essence in others can lead to greater empathy and understanding, fostering harmony and connection.
    • In moments of stress or confusion, contemplating the universal divine can provide clarity and peace, reminding us of the larger picture beyond our immediate concerns.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder to seek the divine essence in ourselves and others, promoting unity and understanding.
    • Its significance lies in encouraging mindfulness and awareness of the interconnectedness of all life, which is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal relationships to environmental consciousness.
    • By embracing this perspective, we can cultivate a more compassionate and harmonious existence, recognizing the divine in every aspect of life.
