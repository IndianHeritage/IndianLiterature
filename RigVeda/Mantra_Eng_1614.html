यो वां यज्ञैः शशमानो ह दाशति कविर होताऽ यजति मनमसाधनः |उपाह तं गच्छथो वीथो अध्वरम् अच्छा गिरः सुमतिं गन्तम अस्मयु ||
yo vāṁ yajñaiḥ śaśamāno ha dāśati kavir hotā yajati manmasādhanaḥ | upāha taṁ gacchatho vītho adhvaram acchā giraḥ sumatiṁ gantam asmayū ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yo: who
    • vāṁ: you
    • yajñaiḥ: with sacrifices
    • śaśamāno: who is satisfied
    • ha: indeed
    • dāśati: gives
    • kavir: the wise one
    • hotā: the priest
    • yajati: performs the sacrifice
    • manmasādhanaḥ: with a focused mind
    • upāha: approach
    • taṁ: him
    • gacchatho: you go
    • vītho: the path
    • adhvaram: of the sacrifice
    • acchā: good
    • giraḥ: words
    • sumatiṁ: good understanding
    • gantam: to reach
    • asmayū: with strength
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of performing sacrifices (yajñas) with a focused and pure mind.
    • The wise one (kavir) who is satisfied (śaśamāno) gives (dāśati) blessings to those who perform these rituals.
    • It suggests that one should approach (upāha) the wise and knowledgeable (kavir) to gain insights and guidance.
    • The mantra encourages individuals to follow the righteous path (vītho adhvaram) and to speak (giraḥ) words of wisdom and understanding (sumatiṁ).
    • It highlights the significance of mental focus (manmasādhanaḥ) in achieving success in one's endeavors.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, a team that collaborates with a focused mindset and clear communication often achieves better results than one that does not.
    • In personal relationships, approaching conflicts with a calm and understanding attitude can lead to resolutions that benefit all parties involved.
    • In education, students who engage deeply with their studies and seek guidance from knowledgeable mentors tend to perform better academically.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of intention and focus in all actions, particularly in spiritual and communal practices.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal growth to professional success, emphasizing that a clear mind and good intentions lead to positive outcomes.
    • By embodying the principles of this mantra, individuals can cultivate a life of purpose, wisdom, and fulfillment.
