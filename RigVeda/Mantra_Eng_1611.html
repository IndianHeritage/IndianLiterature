प्र साक्षितिर असुर या महि प्रिय ऋतावानाव ऋतम आगोषथो बृहत् |युवं दिवो बृहतो दक्षम आबुवं गां न धुर्य उप युञ्जाथे अपः ||
pra sā kṣitir asura yā mahi priya ṛtāvānāv ṛtam ā ghoṣatho bṛhat | yuvaṁ divo bṛhato dakṣam ābhuvaṁ gāṁ na dhury upa yuñjāthe apaḥ ||
  • pra sā kṣitir asura: This phrase can be interpreted as "the earth is the foundation of all beings." Here, "kṣitir" refers to the earth, and "asura" signifies the powerful or divine nature of existence. In real life, this can be seen in how we rely on the earth for sustenance, shelter, and resources.
  • yā mahi priya: This translates to "which is dear to the great." "Mahi" refers to the great or the vast, indicating the importance of the earth in the grand scheme of life. For example, environmental conservation efforts highlight how the earth is cherished and must be protected for future generations.
  • ṛtāvānāv ṛtam ā ghoṣatho bṛhat: This can be understood as "the truth that is vast and resounding." "ṛtam" signifies truth or order, while "bṛhat" means vast. This reflects the idea that truth is expansive and resonates throughout the universe. In daily life, this can be seen in the pursuit of knowledge and understanding, where truth leads to harmony and balance in society.
  • yuvaṁ divo bṛhato dakṣam ābhuvaṁ: This phrase means "may we be endowed with the strength of the heavens." "Divo" refers to the heavens, and "dakṣam" signifies skill or capability. This can be related to the aspirations of individuals to achieve their goals and dreams, drawing strength from the universe and their surroundings.
  • gāṁ na dhury upa yuñjāthe apaḥ: This translates to "may we not be separated from the waters." "Gāṁ" refers to the earth, and "apaḥ" signifies water. This highlights the interconnectedness of earth and water, essential for life. In real life, this can be seen in the importance of water conservation and the impact of climate change on our water resources.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the interconnectedness of earth, truth, and the divine. It signifies the importance of respecting and nurturing our environment, seeking truth, and drawing strength from the universe. In real life, this can be applied through environmental stewardship, the pursuit of knowledge, and the recognition of our place within the larger cosmos. By embodying these principles, we can contribute to a more harmonious and sustainable world.
