पर्षि तोकं तनयम् पर्तृभिष त्वम् अदब्धैर अप्रयुत्वभिः |अग्नि हेळांसी दैव्याः युयोधि नो 'देवानी हवरांसी च ||
parṣi tokaṁ tanayam partṛbhiṣ ṭvam adabdhair aprayutvabhiḥ | agne heḻāṁsi daivyā yuyodhi no 'devāni hvarāṁsi ca ||
  • Mantra: "parṣi tokaṁ tanayam partṛbhiṣ ṭvam adabdhair aprayutvabhiḥ | agne heḻāṁsi daivyā yuyodhi no 'devāni hvarāṁsi ca ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • parṣi: to protect or guard
    • tokaṁ: offspring or progeny
    • tanayam: son or child
    • partṛbhiṣ: by the fathers or ancestors
    • ṭvam: you
    • adabdhair: unblemished or pure
    • aprayutvabhiḥ: without any hindrance or obstacles
    • agne: O Agni (the fire god)
    • heḻāṁsi: divine or celestial
    • daivyā: divine or heavenly
    • yuyodhi: may you fight or strive
    • no: for us
    • devāni: the gods
    • hvarāṁsi: offerings or sacrifices
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Agni, the fire god, who is considered a mediator between humans and the divine.
    • The invocation of Agni signifies the importance of fire in rituals and as a source of energy and transformation.
    • The phrase "parṣi tokaṁ tanayam" emphasizes the desire for protection and blessings for one's progeny, highlighting the significance of family and lineage.
    • By calling upon Agni to fight for us, it reflects the human need for divine assistance in overcoming obstacles and challenges in life.
    • The mention of "adabdhair aprayutvabhiḥ" suggests a plea for purity and freedom from hindrances, which can be interpreted as a desire for a life free from negativity and obstacles.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern life, this mantra can be related to the importance of family values and the protection of future generations, as seen in various cultural practices that emphasize family gatherings and blessings for children.
    • The invocation of divine support in facing life's challenges can be likened to seeking guidance through prayer or meditation in times of difficulty.
    • In a professional context, the mantra can inspire individuals to strive for success while seeking ethical and moral guidance, akin to invoking a higher power for support in their endeavors.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking divine protection and guidance for oneself and one's family.
    • Its significance lies in the acknowledgment of the interconnectedness of human efforts and divine support, reminding us to strive for purity and overcome obstacles.
    • In real life, it serves as a reminder to maintain strong family ties, seek divine assistance in challenges, and uphold ethical values in our pursuits.
