यद् वो वयम् प्रामिणाम व्रतानि विदुषां देवाऽविदुष्टारासः |अग्निष्टद् विश्वम् आपृणाति विद्वान् येभिर् देवां ऋतुभिः कल्पयाति ||
yad vo vayam pramināma vratāni viduṣāṁ devā aviduṣṭarāsaḥ | agniṣ ṭad viśvam ā pṛṇāti vidvān yebhir devām̐ ṛtubhiḥ kalpayāti ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yad: that which
    • vo: for you
    • vayam: we
    • pramināma: we praise
    • vratāni: vows or commitments
    • viduṣāṁ: of the wise
    • devā: the gods
    • aviduṣṭarāsaḥ: those who are ignorant
    • agniṣ: to the fire
    • ṭad: that
    • viśvam: the whole universe
    • ā: towards
    • pṛṇāti: fills or nourishes
    • vidvān: the knowledgeable
    • yebhir: by whom
    • devām̐: the gods
    • ṛtubhiḥ: with the seasons
    • kalpayāti: creates or establishes
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the relationship between humans and the divine, emphasizing the importance of wisdom and knowledge.
    • It suggests that through our commitments and praises, we connect with the divine forces that govern the universe.
    • The reference to "fire" symbolizes transformation and energy, which is essential for life and sustenance.
    • The wise (vidvān) are seen as those who understand the deeper truths of existence and can create harmony with the divine through their actions.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, individuals often make commitments (vratāni) to personal goals, such as pursuing education or maintaining health, which can be seen as a form of praise to the divine for guidance.
    • In a community setting, collective efforts to support each other can be viewed as a way to connect with the divine, fostering a sense of unity and purpose.
    • Environmental conservation efforts can be likened to the act of nourishing the universe (viśvam ā pṛṇāti), as they reflect a commitment to sustaining the earth for future generations.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of wisdom, commitment, and the interconnectedness of all beings with the divine.
    • It encourages individuals to reflect on their actions and commitments, understanding that they play a role in the larger cosmic order.
    • In real life, applying the principles of this mantra can lead to a more harmonious existence, fostering personal growth and community well-being.
