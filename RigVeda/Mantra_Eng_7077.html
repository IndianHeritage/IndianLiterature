तत्रोऽपि प्राणीयत पूतक्रतायै व्यक्ता |अश्वानाम् इन् न यूथ्याम् ||
tatro api prāṇīyata pūtakratāyai vyaktā | aśvānām in na yūthyām ||
  • Mantra: "tatro api prāṇīyata pūtakratāyai vyaktā | aśvānām in na yūthyām ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • tatro: there, in that place
    • api: also, even
    • prāṇīyata: may it be alive, may it thrive
    • pūtakratāyai: for the one who is pure in action
    • vyaktā: manifest, evident
    • aśvānām: of the horses
    • in: not
    • na: not
    • yūthyām: in the group, in the herd
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks to the idea of vitality and purity in action.
    • The phrase "may it be alive" suggests a desire for life and energy to flourish in a particular context.
    • The term "pure in action" emphasizes the importance of integrity and righteousness in one's deeds.
    • The reference to "horses" can symbolize strength, speed, and the ability to overcome obstacles.
    • Overall, the mantra conveys a message of thriving through purity and strength, while also hinting at the importance of individuality within a collective.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace setting, an employee who embodies integrity and works diligently can inspire others, leading to a thriving team environment.
    • In sports, a team that values fair play and teamwork can achieve greater success, as each member contributes their unique strengths while maintaining a collective spirit.
    • In personal relationships, individuals who act with honesty and purity of heart foster deeper connections, allowing their relationships to flourish.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its emphasis on the importance of purity in actions and the vitality that comes from it.
    • It teaches us that while we may be part of a larger group, our individual integrity and strength are crucial for the overall success and harmony of that group.
    • This mantra can be applied in various aspects of life, including personal development, professional ethics, and community building, reminding us to strive for purity in our actions and to support one another in thriving together.
