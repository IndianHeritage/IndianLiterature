प्र सप्तगुम ऋतधीतिं सुमेधाम बृहस्पतिम मतिर अच् छा जिगाति |य आङ्गिरसो नमसोपसद्यः स्मभ्यं चित्रं वृषणं रयिं दाह ||
pra saptagum ṛtadhītiṁ sumedhām bṛhaspatim matir acchā jigāti | ya āṅgiraso namasopasadyo 'smabhyaṁ citraṁ vṛṣaṇaṁ rayiṁ dāḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • pra: forward, towards
    • saptagum: sevenfold, representing completeness or fullness
    • ṛtadhītiṁ: the understanding of truth or righteousness
    • sumedhām: of good intellect or wisdom
    • bṛhaspatim: referring to Brihaspati, the deity of wisdom and knowledge
    • matir: thought, intention, or understanding
    • acchā: good, excellent
    • jigāti: to thrive or prosper
    • ya: who
    • āṅgiraso: referring to the Angirasas, a group of sages
    • namasopasadyo: those who are worthy of reverence
    • ‘smabhyaṁ: to us
    • citraṁ: diverse, colorful, or unique
    • vṛṣaṇaṁ: strength or power
    • rayiṁ: wealth or prosperity
    • dāḥ: grant or bestow
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the blessings of Brihaspati, the deity associated with wisdom and intellect.
    • The phrase "saptagum ṛtadhītiṁ" suggests a holistic understanding of truth, emphasizing the importance of wisdom in navigating life's complexities.
    • By seeking "sumedhām," the mantra encourages individuals to cultivate good intellect and wisdom in their thoughts and actions.
    • The reference to "āṅgiraso" highlights the importance of reverence towards those who possess knowledge and wisdom.
    • Ultimately, the mantra seeks the bestowal of diverse strengths and prosperity upon the individual or community.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, this mantra can be applied when making important decisions. Seeking wisdom and understanding can lead to better outcomes.
    • In education, students can embody the essence of this mantra by striving for knowledge and wisdom, thus enhancing their learning experience.
    • In personal relationships, fostering good intentions and understanding can lead to stronger bonds and harmony.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of wisdom, truth, and reverence in achieving prosperity and strength in life.
    • It serves as a reminder to seek knowledge and understanding in all aspects of life, whether personal, professional, or spiritual.
    • By embodying the principles of this mantra, individuals can navigate challenges more effectively and contribute positively to their communities.
