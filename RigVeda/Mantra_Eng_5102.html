वामम् अद्य सवितर वामम् उ श्वो दिदेव-दिवे वामम् अस्मभ्यं सावीः |वामस्य हि क्षयस्य देव भूरेर अया धियā वामभाजः स्याम ||
vāmam adya savitar vāmam u śvo dive-dive vāmam asmabhyaṁ sāvīḥ | vāmasya hi kṣayasya deva bhūrer ayā dhiyā vāmabhājaḥ syāma ||
  • Mantra: "vāmam adya savitar vāmam u śvo dive-dive vāmam asmabhyaṁ sāvīḥ | vāmasya hi kṣayasya deva bhūrer ayā dhiyā vāmabhājaḥ syāma ||"
  • Translation: "O Savitar, grant us your auspiciousness today and every day. May we be blessed with your divine light, and may we be protected from all harm. Let us be the recipients of your grace and wisdom."
  • Word-by-Word Explanation:
    • vāmam: auspiciousness or goodness
    • adya: today
    • savitā: Savitar, the Sun God, representing light and energy
    • u: and
    • śvo: tomorrow
    • dive-dive: day by day
    • asmabhyaṁ: for us
    • sāvīḥ: may we be blessed
    • vāmasya: of the auspicious
    • hi: indeed
    • kṣayasya: from harm or destruction
    • deva: divine or god
    • bhūrer: of the earth
    • ayā: with this
    • dhiyā: wisdom or intellect
    • vāma-bhājaḥ: recipients of auspiciousness
    • syāma: may we become
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, individuals often seek blessings for success and protection. For instance, before starting a new venture or project, people may recite this mantra to invoke positive energy and divine guidance.
    • In a family setting, parents may chant this mantra for the well-being and safety of their children, hoping to shield them from harm and guide them towards a prosperous future.
    • During community gatherings or festivals, this mantra can be recited to foster unity and collective well-being, emphasizing the importance of auspiciousness in communal life.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its invocation of divine blessings and protection. It emphasizes the importance of seeking auspiciousness in our daily lives, both for ourselves and for those around us. By reciting this mantra, individuals can cultivate a mindset of positivity and resilience, making it applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal endeavors to communal activities. Ultimately, it serves as a reminder of the divine support available to us as we navigate the challenges of life.
