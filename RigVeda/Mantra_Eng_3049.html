अस्माकम् आयुर् वर्धयन्न् अभिमातिः सहमानः |सोमः सदस्स्थम् आसदत् ||
asmākam āyur vardhayann abhimātīḥ sahamānaḥ | somaḥ sadhastham āsadat ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • asmākam: our
    • āyur: life or longevity
    • vardhayann: increasing or enhancing
    • abhimātīḥ: desires or aspirations
    • sahamānaḥ: together or in unity
    • somaḥ: the divine nectar or soma, often associated with immortality
    • sadhastham: in the assembly or gathering
    • āsadat: may sit or reside
  • Overall Meaning:
    • This mantra expresses a prayer for the enhancement of our life and aspirations.
    • It emphasizes the importance of unity and collective strength in achieving our goals.
    • The reference to "soma" signifies the divine essence that nourishes and sustains life, suggesting that spiritual and communal support is vital for well-being.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace setting, when team members collaborate effectively, they can enhance their productivity and achieve common goals, reflecting the essence of "sahamānaḥ."
    • In a family, when members support each other's aspirations, it leads to a harmonious environment that fosters growth and happiness, akin to the prayer for increased "āyur" and "abhimātīḥ."
    • Communities that come together for a common cause, such as environmental conservation, embody the spirit of unity and shared purpose, resonating with the mantra's call for collective enhancement.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of collective effort and spiritual nourishment in enhancing life and fulfilling aspirations.
    • It serves as a reminder that our individual well-being is interconnected with the well-being of others, and that unity can lead to greater achievements.
    • In real-life scenarios, whether in personal relationships, workplaces, or communities, the principles encapsulated in this mantra can guide us towards a more fulfilling and harmonious existence.
