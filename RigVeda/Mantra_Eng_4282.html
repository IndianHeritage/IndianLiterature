अस्मिन यज्ञे अदाभ्यां जरीतारं शुभस पतिः |अवश्युम अश्विनौ युवं गृणन्तं उप भूषथो माध्वी मम श्रुतं हवम् ||
asmin yajñe adābhyā jaritāraṁ śubhas patī | avasyum aśvinā yuvaṁ gṛṇantam upa bhūṣatho mādhvī mama śrutaṁ havam ||
  • Mantra: "asmin yajñe adābhyā jaritāraṁ śubhas patī | avasyum aśvinā yuvaṁ gṛṇantam upa bhūṣatho mādhvī mama śrutaṁ havam ||"
  • Translation: "In this sacrifice, O divine beings, may the auspicious lord, the one who brings forth prosperity, be invoked. May the youthful Aśvins, who are always ready to help, come forth and bless my offerings with sweetness."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • asmin yajñe: In this sacrifice
    • adābhyā: O divine beings
    • jaritāraṁ: The one who brings forth prosperity
    • śubhas patī: Auspicious lord
    • avasyum: May come forth
    • aśvinā: Aśvins, the twin gods of health and medicine
    • yuvaṁ: Youthful
    • gṛṇantam: Who are always ready to help
    • upa bhūṣatho: Bless my offerings
    • mādhvī: With sweetness
    • mama: My
    • śrutaṁ: Offerings
    • havam: Sacrificial offerings
  • Significance:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of invoking divine blessings during rituals and sacrifices.
    • The Aśvins represent health, vitality, and the duality of life, symbolizing the balance between physical and spiritual well-being.
    • It highlights the need for auspiciousness and prosperity in our endeavors, reminding us to seek divine support in our actions.
  • Real-Life Application:
    • In modern life, this mantra can be applied during significant events such as weddings, new business ventures, or any new beginnings where blessings are sought.
    • It serves as a reminder to maintain a positive mindset and seek support from the community and divine forces when facing challenges.
    • Practicing gratitude and acknowledging the contributions of others can lead to a more fulfilling and prosperous life.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking divine intervention and support in our lives.
    • By invoking the Aśvins and the auspicious lord, we align ourselves with positive energies that can guide us towards success and well-being.
    • Ultimately, it teaches us the value of community, support, and the importance of maintaining a harmonious relationship with the divine.
