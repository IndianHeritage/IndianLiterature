मन्त्र: शं नो मित्रः शं वरुणः शं नो भवतु आर्यमाः |शं न इन्द्रो बृहस्पतिः शं नो विष्णुर उरुक्रमः ||
śaṁ no mitraḥ śaṁ varuṇaḥ śaṁ no bhavatv aryamā | śaṁ na indro bṛhaspatiḥ śaṁ no viṣṇur urukramaḥ ||
  • śaṁ no mitraḥ: This phrase translates to "May Mitra be auspicious to us." Mitra is the deity associated with friendship, harmony, and the sun. In real life, this can be understood as a prayer for positive relationships and social harmony.
  • śaṁ varuṇaḥ: This means "May Varuna be auspicious to us." Varuna is the god of cosmic order and water. This can be related to the importance of maintaining balance and order in our lives, akin to how water sustains life. For example, in environmental conservation, we seek to maintain the balance of nature.
  • śaṁ no bhavatv aryamā: This translates to "May Aryama be auspicious to us." Aryama is associated with the afterlife and the moral order. This can be seen in the context of ethical living and the importance of making choices that lead to a good reputation and legacy.
  • śaṁ na indro bṛhaspatiḥ: This means "May Indra and Brihaspati be auspicious to us." Indra is the king of gods and represents strength and power, while Brihaspati is the god of wisdom and knowledge. This can be applied in our lives by seeking strength in our endeavors and wisdom in our decisions, such as in leadership roles or educational pursuits.
  • śaṁ no viṣṇur urukramaḥ: This translates to "May Vishnu, the one of great strides, be auspicious to us." Vishnu is the preserver in the Hindu trinity and represents protection and sustenance. In practical terms, this can be related to the importance of resilience and adaptability in facing life's challenges, much like how Vishnu preserves the universe.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a prayer for auspiciousness from various deities, each representing different aspects of life such as friendship, order, ethics, strength, wisdom, and preservation. In real life, this mantra serves as a reminder to seek harmony in relationships, maintain balance in our actions, live ethically, pursue knowledge, and adapt to challenges. It emphasizes the interconnectedness of these qualities in leading a fulfilling and meaningful life.
