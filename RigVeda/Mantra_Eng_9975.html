मन्त्र: कप्रं नरः कप्रथमुद्दधातन चोदयत खुदत वाजसातये | निष्टिग्र्यः पुत्रम् आ च्यावयोतय इन्द्रं सबाध इह सोमपीतये ||
kapṛn naraḥ kapṛtham ud dadhātana codayata khudata vājasātaye | niṣṭigryaḥ putram ā cyāvayotaya indraṁ sabādha iha somapītaye ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • kapṛn: One who is strong or powerful.
    • naraḥ: Man or human being.
    • kapṛtham: The strength or power of the strong.
    • ud dadhātana: Who brings forth or creates.
    • codayata: Inspire or motivate.
    • khudata: To be aware or conscious.
    • vājasātaye: For the purpose of strength or victory.
    • niṣṭigryaḥ: One who is steadfast or firm.
    • putram: Son or offspring.
    • ā cyāvayotaya: To be protected or guided.
    • indraṁ: Indra, the king of gods.
    • sabādha: With all strength or power.
    • iha: Here or in this world.
    • somapītaye: For the purpose of enjoying the nectar of immortality.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks to the strength and power inherent in humanity, urging individuals to harness their inner strength.
    • It emphasizes the importance of motivation and awareness in achieving one's goals, akin to how a coach inspires athletes to perform at their best.
    • The reference to Indra signifies the aspiration for divine support and guidance in our endeavors, much like seeking mentorship in professional life.
    • The idea of protecting and guiding one's offspring reflects the universal parental instinct to nurture and safeguard the next generation.
    • The mention of enjoying the nectar of immortality can be interpreted as striving for lasting impact and legacy in one's life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • A teacher motivating students to excel in their studies embodies the essence of "codayata," inspiring them to reach their full potential.
    • In a corporate setting, a leader who supports their team and fosters a strong work environment exemplifies the qualities of "niṣṭigryaḥ," ensuring stability and growth.
    • Parents guiding their children through life's challenges reflect the protective aspect of "ā cyāvayotaya," ensuring they are well-prepared for the future.
    • Individuals striving for personal growth and leaving a positive legacy in their communities resonate with the pursuit of "somapītaye."
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the strength and potential within each individual, encouraging self-awareness and motivation.
    • It highlights the importance of guidance, both divine and parental, in navigating life's challenges.
    • Ultimately, it calls for a pursuit of excellence and a lasting impact, applicable in various aspects of life, from personal development to community engagement.
