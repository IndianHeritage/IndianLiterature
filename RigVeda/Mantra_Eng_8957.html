द्रप्सश्च कस्कन्द प्रथमाṁ अनु dyūn इमं च योनिम अनु यश्च पूर्वः |समानं योनिम अनु संचरन्तं द्रप्सं जुहोम्य अनु सप्त होत्राः ||
drapsaś caskanda prathamām̐ anu dyūn imaṁ ca yonim anu yaś ca pūrvaḥ | samānaṁ yonim anu saṁcarantaṁ drapsaṁ juhomy anu sapta hotrāḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • drapsaś: Refers to the essence or the vital energy.
    • caskanda: Indicates the act of shining or illuminating.
    • prathamām̐: Means the first or foremost.
    • anu: Signifies following or in accordance with.
    • dyūn: Refers to the heavens or the sky.
    • imaṁ: Means this or here.
    • ca: And.
    • yonim: Refers to the source or womb.
    • yaś: Means who or that which.
    • pūrvaḥ: Refers to the previous or ancient.
    • samānaṁ: Means similar or equal.
    • saṁcarantaṁ: Refers to moving or traveling.
    • drapsaṁ: Again refers to the essence or vital energy.
    • juhomy: Means I offer or I invoke.
    • anu: Again signifies following.
    • sapta: Refers to seven.
    • hotrāḥ: Refers to the priests or those who perform rituals.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of invoking the vital energies and illuminating forces that are present in the universe.
    • It emphasizes the importance of the first or foremost energies that guide and influence our existence.
    • The reference to the heavens suggests a connection between the earthly and the divine, indicating that our actions and energies are aligned with cosmic principles.
    • The mention of "seven priests" signifies the importance of community and collective effort in spiritual practices.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, this mantra can be related to the importance of seeking guidance from mentors or elders who have wisdom and experience.
    • In a team setting, the idea of collective effort can be seen in how teams work together to achieve a common goal, invoking the energies of collaboration and unity.
    • In personal development, one might reflect on the "first energies" that inspire them, such as passion or purpose, and how these can illuminate their path forward.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call to recognize and honor the vital energies that shape our lives.
    • It encourages individuals to align their actions with higher principles and to work together in harmony with others.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra can serve as a reminder to seek illumination in our choices and to appreciate the interconnectedness of all beings.
