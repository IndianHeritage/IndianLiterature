मन्त्र: नि षसाद धृतव्रतो वरुणः पस्त्याःस्व आ | साम्राज्याय सुक्रतुः ||
ni ṣasāda dhṛtavrato varuṇaḥ pastyā3sv ā | sāmrājyāya sukratuḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ni ṣasāda: "He has sat down" or "He has established himself."
    • dhṛtavrato: "With firm resolve" or "With steadfast determination."
    • varuṇaḥ: "Varuna," the god of cosmic order and water.
    • pastyā3sv: "In the midst of the waters" or "In the ocean of existence."
    • sāmrājyāya: "For the kingdom" or "For the realm."
    • sukratuḥ: "Of good intention" or "With noble thoughts."
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of Varuna, who is depicted as a deity embodying the principles of order and truth.
    • The phrase "He has established himself" suggests a sense of permanence and authority, indicating that Varuna is a stabilizing force in the universe.
    • "With firm resolve" emphasizes the importance of determination and commitment in achieving one's goals.
    • The reference to "the waters" symbolizes the vastness of existence and the interconnectedness of all life.
    • The mantra concludes with a focus on noble intentions, suggesting that actions taken with good thoughts lead to a prosperous and harmonious existence.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In leadership, a leader who embodies the qualities of Varuna—steadfastness, commitment, and good intentions—can create a positive and productive environment for their team.
    • In personal development, individuals who set firm resolutions and pursue them with determination often achieve their goals, reflecting the essence of "dhṛtavrato."
    • In environmental conservation, recognizing the interconnectedness of all life (symbolized by "the waters") can inspire collective action towards sustainability.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of steadfastness, noble intentions, and the cosmic order represented by Varuna.
    • It serves as a reminder that our actions, when rooted in good intentions and firm resolve, can lead to a harmonious and prosperous life.
    • In real-life scenarios, whether in leadership, personal growth, or environmental stewardship, embodying these principles can lead to positive outcomes and a sense of fulfillment.
