न वा उ सोमो वृजिनं हिनोति न क्षत्रियम मिथुया धारयन्तम् |हन्ति रक्षो हन्त्य आसद वदन्तम् उभाव इन्द्रस्य प्रसितौ शयाते ||
na vā u somo vṛjinaṁ hinoti na kṣatriyam mithuyā dhārayantam | hanti rakṣo hanty āsad vadantam ubhāv indrasya prasitau śayāte ||
  • na vā u somo vṛjinaṁ hinoti: This phrase can be translated to "the soma does not destroy the sin." Here, "soma" refers to a sacred drink in Vedic rituals, symbolizing divine inspiration and enlightenment. In real life, this can be likened to the pursuit of knowledge and wisdom that helps individuals overcome their shortcomings and moral failings.
  • na kṣatriyam mithuyā dhārayantam: This translates to "nor does it support the warrior who is deceitful." The term "kṣatriya" refers to the warrior class in ancient Indian society. This line emphasizes the importance of integrity and honesty in leadership and governance. In modern contexts, it can be related to ethical leadership in politics and business, where deceit can lead to downfall.
  • hanti rakṣo hanty āsad vadantam: This means "it destroys the evil, it destroys the one who speaks falsely." This highlights the power of truth and righteousness in overcoming malevolence. In everyday life, this can be seen in the triumph of justice over corruption, where truth ultimately prevails.
  • ubhāv indrasya prasitau śayāte: This translates to "both of them lie in the power of Indra." Indra is the king of the gods in Vedic mythology, representing strength and authority. This line suggests that both good and evil are under the influence of divine power. In a contemporary sense, it can be interpreted as the idea that higher forces or universal laws govern the outcomes of human actions.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of truth, integrity, and the pursuit of knowledge in overcoming evil and moral failings. It serves as a reminder that while divine forces may influence our lives, it is our actions—rooted in honesty and righteousness—that ultimately determine our fate. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire individuals to strive for ethical behavior in their personal and professional lives, reinforcing the idea that integrity leads to success and harmony, while deceit leads to destruction and chaos.
