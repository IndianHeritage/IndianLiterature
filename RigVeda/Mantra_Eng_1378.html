अस्य स्तुषे महिमघस्य राधः सचा सनेमा नहुषः सुवीराः |जनो यः पज्रेभ्यो वाजिनीवान अश्वावतो रथिनो मह्यं सूरिः ||
asya stuṣe mahimaghasya rādhaḥ sacā sanema nahuṣaḥ suvīrāḥ | jano yaḥ pajrebhyo vājinīvān aśvāvato rathino mahyaṁ sūriḥ ||
  • Asya stuṣe mahimaghasya rādhaḥ: This phrase can be interpreted as a call to the divine for the bestowal of glory and strength. The term "stuṣe" refers to praise or worship, while "mahimaghasya" signifies the greatness or magnificence of the divine. "Rādhaḥ" can be understood as a reference to the divine energy or essence.
  • Sacā sanema nahuṣaḥ suvīrāḥ: Here, "sacā" means together or in unity, while "sanema" suggests a desire for well-being or prosperity. "Nahuṣaḥ" refers to a powerful or heroic figure, and "suvīrāḥ" translates to those who are valiant or courageous. This line emphasizes the importance of collective strength and valor in achieving success.
  • Jano yaḥ pajrebhyo vājinīvān: "Jano" refers to people or humanity, while "yaḥ" means who. "Pajrebhyo" can be interpreted as those who are devoted or dedicated. "Vājinīvān" signifies a charioteer or one who leads, suggesting guidance and direction.
  • Aśvāvato rathino mahyaṁ sūriḥ: "Aśvāvato" refers to those who are swift like horses, and "rathino" means charioteers. "Mahyaṁ" translates to 'to me' or 'for me', while "sūriḥ" signifies a hero or a champion. This line highlights the importance of speed, agility, and leadership in overcoming challenges.

  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of collective strength, valor, and guidance in achieving greatness. It calls upon the divine for blessings and support in the pursuit of success. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various contexts such as teamwork in professional settings, community efforts in social causes, or even personal endeavors where collaboration and courage are essential. The mantra serves as a reminder that unity and shared purpose can lead to remarkable achievements, much like a well-coordinated team working together to reach a common goal.
