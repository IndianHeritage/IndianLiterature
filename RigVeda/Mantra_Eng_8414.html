आ यो गोभिः सृज्यत ओषधिष्व आ देवानां सुम्न इषयन्न उपावसुः |आ विद्युतā पवते धारयā सुत इन्द्रं सोमो मादयन दैव्यम् जनम् ||
ā yo gobhiḥ sṛjyata oṣadhīṣv ā devānāṁ sumna iṣayann upāvasuḥ | ā vidyutā pavate dhārayā suta indraṁ somo mādayan daivyaṁ janam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā yo: He who
    • gobhiḥ: with the cows (or wealth)
    • sṛjyata: is created
    • oṣadhīṣv: in the herbs (or plants)
    • ā: He
    • devānāṁ: of the gods
    • sumna: auspicious
    • iṣayann: ruling
    • upāvasuḥ: dwelling (or residing)
    • ā: He
    • vidyutā: with lightning
    • pavate: blows (or moves)
    • dhārayā: holding
    • suta: the son
    • indraṁ: Indra (the king of gods)
    • somo: the moon (or soma, a sacred drink)
    • mādayan: making joyful
    • daivyaṁ: divine
    • janam: people (or beings)
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the divine connection between nature, wealth, and the gods.
    • The reference to "gobhiḥ" (cows) symbolizes wealth and prosperity, which is essential for a thriving community.
    • "Oṣadhīṣv" (herbs) indicates the importance of nature and its resources in sustaining life.
    • The mantra emphasizes the auspiciousness of divine presence ("devānāṁ sumna") and the blessings that come from it.
    • It highlights the role of Indra, the king of gods, who is associated with rain, storms, and fertility, thus linking the divine with agricultural prosperity.
    • The mention of "somo" (moon) and "mādayan" (making joyful) suggests the importance of rituals and offerings that bring joy and abundance to the community.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In agriculture, farmers often pray for rain and good harvests, reflecting the belief in divine intervention for prosperity.
    • Communities celebrate festivals that honor nature and the gods, such as harvest festivals, which embody the essence of this mantra.
    • In modern times, the mantra can inspire sustainable practices that respect nature, emphasizing the interconnectedness of life.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of harmony between nature, wealth, and divine blessings.
    • It teaches us to appreciate the resources provided by nature and the role of spirituality in our lives.
    • In real-life scenarios, it encourages us to seek balance and prosperity through respect for the environment and gratitude towards the divine.
