तं उ नूनं तविषीमन्तमेषां स्तुषे गणं मारुतं नव्यसीनाम् |य आष्वश्वा अमवद् वहन्त उतेशिरे अमृतस्य स्वराजः ||
tam u nūnaṁ taviṣīmantam eṣāṁ stuṣe gaṇam mārutaṁ navyasīnām | ya āśvaśvā amavad vahanta uteśire amṛtasya svarājaḥ ||
  • tam u nūnaṁ taviṣīmantam: This phrase can be translated to "Indeed, that powerful one." Here, "taviṣīmantam" refers to a being or force that possesses great strength and capability. In real life, this can be likened to individuals or entities that exhibit exceptional leadership qualities, such as a successful entrepreneur or a visionary leader who inspires others.
  • eṣāṁ stuṣe gaṇam mārutaṁ: This translates to "We praise the assembly of the Maruts." The Maruts are storm deities in Vedic literature, representing the forces of nature. In a contemporary context, this can symbolize the importance of teamwork and collaboration in achieving common goals, much like how a group of individuals come together to create a powerful impact in a community or organization.
  • navyasīnām: This word means "newly arisen" or "fresh." It signifies innovation and the emergence of new ideas or movements. In today's world, this can be related to startups or new social movements that bring about change and progress, emphasizing the need for adaptability and openness to new concepts.
  • ya āśvaśvā amavad vahanta: This can be interpreted as "who carry the swift steeds." Here, "āśvaśvā" refers to horses, which symbolize speed, power, and nobility. In modern terms, this could represent individuals or organizations that drive progress and innovation, akin to tech companies that lead the charge in advancements and efficiency.
  • uteśire amṛtasya svarājaḥ: This translates to "they have attained the immortality of the realm." "Amṛtasya" refers to immortality or eternal life, while "svarājaḥ" signifies a state of sovereignty or self-rule. This can be related to the pursuit of knowledge and wisdom, where individuals strive for personal growth and enlightenment, leading to a lasting impact on society.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of strength, collaboration, innovation, and the pursuit of knowledge. In real life, it serves as a reminder of the power of unity and the importance of embracing new ideas and leadership. Whether in personal endeavors, community projects, or professional settings, the principles outlined in this mantra can guide individuals and groups toward achieving greatness and making a meaningful contribution to the world.
