अग्निं दूतं प्रति यद अब्रवीतानाश्वः कर्त्वो रथ उतेह कर्त्वः |धेनुः कर्त्वा युवशा कर्त्वा द्वा तानि भ्रातर अनु वः कृत्व्य एमसि ||
agniṁ dūtam prati yad abravītanāśvaḥ kartvo ratha uteha kartvaḥ | dhenuḥ kartvā yuvaśā kartvā dvā tāni bhrātar anu vaḥ kṛtvy emasi ||
  • Agniṁ dūtam prati: This phrase refers to Agni, the fire god, who is often seen as a messenger or intermediary between humans and the divine. In real life, this can be likened to the role of communication in our lives, where we seek to convey our thoughts and intentions to others.
  • yad abravītanāśvaḥ: This translates to "what the horse has spoken." Horses are symbols of strength and speed. In a modern context, this can represent the importance of swift action and decision-making in our lives, akin to how businesses must adapt quickly to market changes.
  • kartvo ratha uteha kartvaḥ: This means "the chariot is prepared." The chariot symbolizes the journey of life and the preparations we must make to achieve our goals. In everyday life, this can be seen in how we plan and organize our tasks to reach our objectives, whether in personal projects or professional endeavors.
  • dhenuḥ kartvā yuvaśā kartvā: This translates to "the cow is prepared, the young are prepared." Cows represent nourishment and sustenance, while youth symbolizes potential and future. This can be related to how we nurture our resources and the younger generation to ensure a prosperous future, much like how parents invest in their children's education.
  • dvā tāni bhrātar anu vaḥ kṛtvy emasi: This means "we have performed these duties, brothers." It emphasizes the importance of community and collaboration. In real life, this can be seen in teamwork, where collective efforts lead to success, such as in group projects or community service initiatives.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of preparation, communication, and collaboration in our lives. It signifies the importance of being ready for the journey ahead, nurturing our resources, and working together as a community. In today's fast-paced world, these principles are applicable in various scenarios, from personal development to professional teamwork, reminding us that success often comes from a combination of individual effort and collective support.
