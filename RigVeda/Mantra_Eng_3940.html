यत् ते दित्सु प्रराध्यम् मनो अस्ति श्रुतम् बृहत् |तेन दृढा चिद अद्रिवा आ वाजं दर्शि सातये ||
yat te ditsu prarādhyam mano asti śrutam bṛhat | tena dṛḻhā cid adriva ā vājaṁ darṣi sātaye ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yat: that which
    • te: of you
    • ditsu: in the desire
    • prarādhyam: that which is sought
    • mano: mind
    • asti: exists
    • śrutam: heard or known
    • bṛhat: great or vast
    • tena: by that
    • dṛḻhā: firmly
    • cid: consciousness
    • adriva: without obstacles
    • ā: towards
    • vājaṁ: strength or power
    • darṣi: to see or perceive
    • sātaye: for the purpose of truth or reality
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the connection between desire, the mind, and the vastness of knowledge.
    • It emphasizes that what we seek in our desires is already present in our consciousness and can be realized through firm determination.
    • The phrase "by that" suggests that the realization of our desires is facilitated by a deeper understanding or knowledge.
    • In practical terms, this can be related to the idea that when we focus our minds on our goals and desires, we can overcome obstacles and achieve our aspirations.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • A student who desires to excel in their studies can achieve this by focusing their mind on learning and understanding the material, thus overcoming challenges.
    • An athlete aiming for a championship must visualize their success and train diligently, using their mental strength to push through physical barriers.
    • In a business context, an entrepreneur who has a clear vision and determination can navigate through market challenges to achieve their business goals.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of aligning our desires with our consciousness and the power of focused intention.
    • It teaches us that by understanding our true desires and committing to them, we can manifest our goals in reality.
    • In everyday life, this principle can be applied in various fields such as education, sports, and business, encouraging individuals to harness their mental strength to achieve success.
