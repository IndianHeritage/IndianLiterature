वृष्टिद्यावा रीत्यापेषस पति दानुमत्याः | बृहन्तं गर्तम आशाते ||
vṛṣṭidyāvā rītyāpeṣas patī dānumatyāḥ | bṛhantaṁ gartam āśāte ||
  • vṛṣṭi: This word translates to "rain" or "precipitation." In a real-life context, rain is essential for agriculture, replenishing water sources, and maintaining ecological balance.
  • dyāvā: This means "heaven" or "sky." The sky is often associated with the divine and is crucial for the sustenance of life on Earth.
  • rītyā: This can be interpreted as "through the path" or "by the way." It signifies the journey or process through which one achieves their goals.
  • apeṣas: This term refers to "the one who is the master" or "lord." It denotes authority and control, often associated with deities or powerful figures in society.
  • patī: This translates to "lord" or "master," emphasizing the role of a guiding force or protector in life.
  • dānumatyāḥ: This word can be understood as "with generosity" or "with abundance." It highlights the importance of giving and sharing in human relationships.
  • bṛhantaṁ: This means "great" or "vast," often referring to the universe or the expansive nature of existence.
  • gartam: This translates to "the pit" or "the depth," which can symbolize the challenges and struggles one faces in life.
  • āśāte: This means "to seek" or "to aspire." It reflects the human desire for growth, understanding, and fulfillment.

The mantra can be interpreted as a prayer or invocation for rain and abundance, recognizing the divine forces that govern nature and life. It emphasizes the importance of seeking guidance and support from higher powers while acknowledging the challenges that come with existence.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as:

  • Agriculture: Farmers may recite this mantra to invoke rain for their crops, understanding the significance of nature's cycles in their livelihood.
  • Personal Growth: Individuals may reflect on the journey of life, seeking guidance and support from mentors or spiritual leaders as they navigate challenges.
  • Community Support: The emphasis on generosity can inspire people to engage in charitable acts, fostering a sense of community and shared responsibility.

In conclusion, this mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of life, the importance of seeking guidance, and the value of generosity. It encourages individuals to recognize the divine forces at play in their lives and to aspire for growth and abundance while navigating the challenges they face.
