महान्तं कोशमुदचानि षिञ्चस्यन्दन्तां कुल्याविषिताः पुरस्तात् |घृतेन dyāvāpṛthivī व्युण्धि सुप्रपाणं भवत्वघ्न्याभ्यः ||
mahāntaṁ kośam ud acā ni ṣiñca syandantāṁ kulyā viṣitāḥ purastāt | ghṛtena dyāvāpṛthivī vy undhi suprapāṇam bhavatv aghnyābhyaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • mahāntaṁ: Great, vast, or significant.
    • kośam: Treasure, wealth, or storehouse.
    • ud acā: Rising or emerging.
    • ni ṣiñca: Pouring or bestowing.
    • syandantāṁ: Flowing or streaming.
    • kulyā: Stream or channel.
    • viṣitāḥ: Having reached or arrived.
    • purastāt: In front or ahead.
    • ghṛtena: With ghee (clarified butter).
    • dyāvāpṛthivī: Heaven and Earth.
    • vy undhi: May you be filled or nourished.
    • suprapāṇam: Well-drinking or well-sustained.
    • bhavatv: May it be or may it become.
    • aghnyābhyaḥ: From those who are not to be harmed or from the non-violent.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the vast treasures that are to be poured forth, symbolizing the abundance of resources and blessings in life.
    • The imagery of streams flowing suggests a continuous and generous supply of nourishment and support, akin to how rivers provide water to the land.
    • The mention of ghee signifies purity and auspiciousness, often used in rituals to invoke divine blessings.
    • Heaven and Earth represent the duality of existence, emphasizing the interconnectedness of the spiritual and material worlds.
    • The call for nourishment from the non-violent indicates a desire for peace and harmony in the pursuit of abundance.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In agriculture, the concept of nurturing the land with water and nutrients can be likened to the flowing streams mentioned in the mantra, which ensure a bountiful harvest.
    • In personal development, the idea of pouring forth one's talents and resources can lead to success and fulfillment, much like the treasures that are bestowed upon those who seek them.
    • In community building, fostering a non-violent and supportive environment can create a space where everyone thrives, reflecting the essence of the mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of abundance, nourishment, and the interconnectedness of all beings.
    • It serves as a reminder to seek and share resources generously while maintaining a commitment to peace and non-violence.
    • In real life, applying the principles of this mantra can lead to personal growth, community well-being, and a harmonious existence with nature.
