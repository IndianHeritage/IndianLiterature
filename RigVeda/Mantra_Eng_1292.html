परिविष्टं जाहुषं विश्वतः सीं सुगेभिर नक्तम ऊहथू रजोभिः |विभिन्दुना नासत्याऽ रथेन विपर्वताम् अजयर्यू आयातम् ||
pariviṣṭaṁ jāhuṣaṁ viśvataḥ sīṁ sugebhir naktam ūhathū rajobhiḥ | vibhindunā nāsatyā rathena vi parvatām̐ ajarayū ayātam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • pariviṣṭaṁ: Surrounded or enveloped
    • jāhuṣaṁ: The one who is to be invoked or called upon
    • viśvataḥ: From all directions or universally
    • sīṁ: With strength or power
    • sugebhir: With auspicious or good qualities
    • naktam: Night
    • ūhathū: May you be protected or may you be guided
    • rajobhiḥ: By the dust or particles
    • vibhindunā: By the one who separates or divides
    • nāsatyā: The truthful or the honest
    • rathena: By the chariot
    • vi: With or through
    • parvatām: The mountains
    • ajarayū: The one who is immortal or ageless
    • ayātam: May you come or may you arrive
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes a powerful presence that is surrounded by auspicious qualities and is called upon from all directions.
    • The reference to "night" suggests a time of introspection and reflection, where one seeks guidance and protection.
    • The mention of "dust" can symbolize the challenges and obstacles in life, while "the one who separates" indicates the ability to discern and navigate through these challenges.
    • The "chariot" symbolizes movement and progress, suggesting that one should move forward with truthfulness and integrity.
    • The "mountains" represent stability and strength, indicating that one should remain grounded and resilient.
    • The concept of "immortality" speaks to the eternal nature of the soul and the importance of spiritual growth.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a challenging situation at work, invoking this mantra can serve as a reminder to stay true to one's values and seek guidance from a higher power.
    • During times of personal crisis, reflecting on the qualities of strength and resilience can help individuals navigate through their difficulties.
    • In moments of doubt, remembering the importance of truthfulness can guide one in making ethical decisions.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of seeking guidance and protection while navigating life's challenges.
    • It encourages individuals to remain grounded, truthful, and resilient, drawing upon their inner strength and the support of the universe.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be a source of inspiration and a reminder to uphold one's values amidst adversity.
