या ईं चकार न सो अस्य वेद या ईं ददर्श हिरुग इन नु तस्मात् |स मातुर योनā परिवीतो अन्तर बहुप्रजा निरृतिम ā विवेश ||
ya īṁ cakāra na so asya veda ya īṁ dadarśa hirug in nu tasmāt | sa mātur yonā parivīto antar bahuprajā nirṛtim ā viveśa ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ya: who
    • īṁ: the sound or vibration (often associated with creation)
    • cakāra: created or manifested
    • na: not
    • so: he
    • asya: of this
    • veda: knowledge or wisdom
    • ya: who
    • īṁ: the sound or vibration
    • dadarśa: saw or perceived
    • hirug: the divine or the sacred
    • in: in
    • nu: indeed
    • tasmāt: therefore
    • sa: he
    • mātur: of the mother
    • yonā: womb or source
    • parivīto: surrounded or enveloped
    • antar: within
    • bahuprajā: many beings or creatures
    • nirṛtim: destruction or chaos
    • ā: to
    • viveśa: entered or penetrated
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the creative force that manifests knowledge and wisdom.
    • It emphasizes the importance of perception and understanding in the process of creation.
    • The reference to the "mother's womb" symbolizes the source of all creation, suggesting that all beings emerge from a common origin.
    • The mention of "chaos" indicates the challenges and destructive forces that can arise in life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In the context of personal growth, this mantra can be seen as a reminder that knowledge and wisdom are essential for navigating life's challenges.
    • In a community setting, it highlights the importance of unity and understanding among diverse individuals, as they all share a common source.
    • In environmental discussions, it can be interpreted as a call to recognize the interconnectedness of all life and the need to protect our shared home.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the profound connection between creation, knowledge, and the challenges of existence.
    • It serves as a reminder to seek wisdom and understanding in our lives, to recognize our shared origins, and to navigate the chaos with awareness.
    • In practical terms, it encourages individuals and communities to foster unity, embrace knowledge, and work towards harmony in the face of adversity.
