मन्त्र: एते स्तोमा नरां नृतम तुभ्यम अस्मद्र्यञ्चो ददतो मग्हानि |तेषां इन्द्र वृत्रहत्ये शिवो भूः सखा च शूरो 'विता च नृणाम ||
ete stomā narāṁ nṛtama tubhyam asmadryañco dadato maghāni | teṣām indra vṛtrahatye śivo bhūḥ sakhā ca śūro 'vitā ca nṛṇām ||
  • Translation: "These hymns (stomas) are for you, O Indra, the best among men (nṛtama), who grants us wealth (maghāni) and is our friend (sakhā). May you, who are the slayer of Vritra, be auspicious (śivo) and protect us."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • ete stomā: These hymns or praises.
    • narāṁ: Of men or among men.
    • nṛtama: The best or the greatest.
    • tubhyam: For you.
    • asmadryañco: Our offerings or gifts.
    • dadato: Who grants or gives.
    • maghāni: Wealth or abundance.
    • teṣām: Of them or for them.
    • indra: The deity Indra, often associated with strength and rain.
    • vṛtrahatye: In the slaying of Vritra, a demon representing drought and obstacles.
    • śivo bhūḥ: May you be auspicious or benevolent.
    • sakhā: Friend or ally.
    • ca: And.
    • śūro: The hero or the brave one.
    • 'vitā: Unharmed or invincible.
    • nṛṇām: Of men or among men.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community, when people come together to support each other during difficult times, they embody the spirit of Indra as a protector and friend.
    • In business, a leader who provides resources and support to their team can be seen as a modern-day Indra, granting wealth and success.
    • During natural disasters, the collective efforts of individuals to help those in need reflect the essence of the mantra, where the community acts as a protective force.
  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of community, support, and the role of a protector. In real life, it serves as a reminder that we should strive to be like Indra—offering help, sharing resources, and standing as a friend to those in need. It highlights the significance of collaboration and the strength found in unity, encouraging individuals to act with benevolence and courage in their daily lives.
