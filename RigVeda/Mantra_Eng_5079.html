नू न इन्द्रावरुणा गृणाना पृङ्क्तं रयिं साउश्रवसाया देवाः |इत्त्था गृणन्तो महिनस्य शर्धोऽपो न नाव दुरीता तरेमा ||
nū na indrāvaruṇā gṛṇānā pṛṅktaṁ rayiṁ sauśravasāya devā | itthā gṛṇanto mahinasya śardho 'po na nāvā duritā tarema ||
  • Mantra: "nū na indrāvaruṇā gṛṇānā pṛṅktaṁ rayiṁ sauśravasāya devā | itthā gṛṇanto mahinasya śardho 'po na nāvā duritā tarema ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • nū: indeed
    • na: not
    • indrāvaruṇā: Indra and Varuna (deities representing power and cosmic order)
    • gṛṇānā: singing or praising
    • pṛṅktaṁ: the wealth or prosperity
    • rayiṁ: riches or abundance
    • sauśravasāya: for the one who is renowned or famous
    • devā: the gods
    • itthā: thus
    • gṛṇanto: while singing or praising
    • mahinasya: of the great or powerful
    • śardho: in the season or time
    • 'po: waters
    • na: not
    • nāvā: the boat or vessel
    • duritā: misfortunes or difficulties
    • tarema: may we cross or overcome
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra invokes the blessings of the deities Indra and Varuna, who symbolize strength and cosmic order, respectively.
    • It emphasizes the importance of praising these deities to attain wealth and prosperity.
    • The phrase "may we cross over misfortunes" suggests a plea for protection and guidance through life's challenges.
    • In real life, this can be likened to seeking support from mentors or leaders during difficult times, much like how individuals turn to community leaders or spiritual guides for wisdom and strength.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine assistance in overcoming obstacles and achieving prosperity.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, such as during times of personal crisis, financial difficulties, or when embarking on new ventures.
    • By invoking the blessings of higher powers, individuals can find the courage and resources needed to navigate through challenges, much like how people often seek inspiration from religious texts or spiritual practices to find hope and resilience.
