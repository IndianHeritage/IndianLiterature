उप नो वाजिनीवासू यातं ऋतस्य पथिभिः |येभिः तृक्षिं वृषणाः त्रासदस्यवम् महे क्षत्राय जिवथः ||
upa no vājinīvasū yātam ṛtasya pathibhiḥ | yebhis tṛkṣiṁ vṛṣaṇā trāsadasyavam mahe kṣatrāya jinvathaḥ ||
  • Mantra: "upa no vājinīvasū yātam ṛtasya pathibhiḥ | yebhis tṛkṣiṁ vṛṣaṇā trāsadasyavam mahe kṣatrāya jinvathaḥ ||"
  • Translation: "May we, with the help of the swift steeds, follow the path of truth; may we, with the strength of the mighty, overcome obstacles and achieve greatness."
  • Word-by-Word Explanation:
    • upa: towards, in the direction of
    • no: our
    • vājinīvasū: swift steeds, representing strength and speed
    • yātam: may we go, may we proceed
    • ṛtasya: of truth, righteousness
    • pathibhiḥ: by the paths, ways
    • yebhis: with which
    • tṛkṣiṁ: obstacles, challenges
    • vṛṣaṇā: with strength, power
    • trāsadasyavam: may we overcome, may we conquer
    • mahe: great, mighty
    • kṣatrāya: for the warrior, for strength
    • jinvathaḥ: may we achieve, may we succeed
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a competitive environment, such as a corporate setting, individuals often face challenges that require them to harness their strengths and work collaboratively to achieve success.
    • In sports, athletes train rigorously to overcome obstacles and strive for greatness, embodying the essence of this mantra.
    • In personal development, individuals may face various life challenges, and by following a path of truth and righteousness, they can navigate through difficulties and emerge victorious.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call to action for individuals to pursue truth and righteousness while harnessing their strengths to overcome challenges. It emphasizes the importance of unity, strength, and determination in achieving greatness. In real-life scenarios, whether in personal endeavors, professional settings, or communal efforts, this mantra serves as a reminder to stay true to one's values and work collaboratively towards common goals, ultimately leading to success and fulfillment.
