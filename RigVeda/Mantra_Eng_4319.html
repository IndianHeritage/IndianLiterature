युञ्जते मन उता युञ्जते धियो विप्रā विप्रस्य बृहतो विपश्चितः |वि होत्रा दधे वयुनाविद एक इन मही देवस्य सवितुः परिष्टुतिः ||
yuñjate mana uta yuñjate dhiyo viprā viprasya bṛhato vipaścitaḥ | vi hotrā dadhe vayunāvid eka in mahī devasya savituḥ pariṣṭutiḥ ||
  • yuñjate mana uta: This phrase can be translated as "Let the minds be united." It emphasizes the importance of collective thought and harmony among individuals. In real life, this can be seen in team settings, such as in workplaces or community projects, where collaboration leads to better outcomes.
  • yuñjate dhiyo viprā: This means "Let the wise ones unite their intellects." It suggests that those with knowledge and wisdom should come together to share insights and ideas. For example, in academic conferences, scholars from various fields gather to exchange knowledge, fostering innovation and progress.
  • viprasya bṛhato vipaścitaḥ: This translates to "The wise one who is great and insightful." It highlights the value of wisdom and understanding in leadership. In a corporate environment, a leader who possesses deep knowledge and insight can guide their team effectively, making informed decisions that benefit the organization.
  • vi hotrā dadhe: This phrase means "Let the priest establish the offerings." It signifies the role of rituals and offerings in connecting with the divine. In modern life, this can be likened to the importance of gratitude and acknowledgment in various cultures, where people express thanks through rituals or ceremonies.
  • vayunāvid eka: This translates to "With a single purpose." It emphasizes the significance of unity in intention and action. In real life, this can be observed in social movements where individuals come together for a common cause, such as environmental conservation or social justice.
  • in mahī devasya savituḥ pariṣṭutiḥ: This means "In praise of the great deity Savitar." Savitar is often associated with the sun and the life-giving force. This can be related to the appreciation of nature and the environment, reminding us of the importance of sustainability and respect for the earth in our daily lives.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of unity, wisdom, and collective purpose. In today's world, where individualism often prevails, this mantra serves as a reminder of the power of collaboration and shared goals. Whether in personal relationships, professional environments, or community initiatives, the principles outlined in this mantra can guide us towards more harmonious and productive interactions. By fostering unity of thought and purpose, we can create a more connected and supportive society.
