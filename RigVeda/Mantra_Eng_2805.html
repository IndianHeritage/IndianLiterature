इन्द्रं वृत्राय हन्तवे पुरुहूतम उप ब्रुवे |भरिषु वाजसातये ||
indraṁ vṛtrāya hantave puruhūtam upa bruve | bhareṣu vājasātaye ||
  • Indraṁ: Refers to Indra, the king of the gods in Vedic mythology, symbolizing strength, power, and leadership.
  • Vṛtrāya: Refers to Vritra, a serpent or dragon-like demon representing obstacles, chaos, or ignorance that hinders progress.
  • Hantave: Means "to slay" or "to overcome," indicating the act of defeating challenges or adversities.
  • Puruhūtam: A name of Indra, meaning "the one who is invoked by many," highlighting the importance of collective worship and support.
  • Upa bruve: Translates to "I speak to" or "I call upon," indicating a plea or invocation for assistance.
  • Bhareṣu: Means "in the burdens" or "in the challenges," referring to the difficulties faced in life.
  • Vājasātaye: Refers to "for the sake of strength" or "for the purpose of victory," emphasizing the desire for empowerment and success.

The mantra can be understood as a prayer or invocation to Indra, asking for his strength and support in overcoming the obstacles represented by Vritra. It emphasizes the importance of collective effort in seeking divine assistance to conquer challenges and achieve success.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios such as:

  • Personal Challenges: When facing difficulties in personal life, such as health issues or emotional struggles, one can invoke the strength of Indra to overcome these challenges.
  • Professional Obstacles: In a work environment, when encountering barriers to success, this mantra serves as a reminder to seek support from colleagues and work together to achieve common goals.
  • Community Support: In times of social or communal challenges, this mantra encourages collective action and unity in overcoming adversities.

Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for strength and unity in the face of challenges. It teaches us that invoking divine support, along with collective effort, can lead to overcoming obstacles and achieving success in various aspects of life. By recognizing the power of community and the divine, individuals can find the courage to face their struggles and emerge victorious.
