युक्तस्ते अस्तु दक्षिण उता सव्यः शतक्रतो |तेन जायाम उप प्रियाम मंदानो याह्य अन्धसो योजा nv इन्द्र ते हरि ||
yuktas te astu dakṣiṇa uta savyaḥ śatakrato | tena jāyām upa priyām mandāno yāhy andhaso yojā nv indra te harī ||
  • yuktas te astu dakṣiṇa uta savyaḥ śatakrato: This phrase can be translated to "May you be endowed with wisdom, whether you are on the right or the left, O hundred-fold powerful one (Indra)." Here, "yuktas" refers to being wise or endowed with intelligence, while "dakṣiṇa" and "savyaḥ" signify the right and left directions, respectively. "Śatakrato" is a name for Indra, indicating his immense power and capability.
  • tena jāyām upa priyām: This translates to "By that wisdom, may we attain the beloved." This line emphasizes the importance of wisdom in achieving what is dear to us, suggesting that intelligence and understanding lead to fulfilling our desires and goals.
  • mandāno yāhy andhaso yojā nv indra te harī: This can be interpreted as "May the one who is slow (or gentle) lead us away from darkness, O Indra, your grace." The term "mandāno" suggests a gentle or slow approach, while "andhaso" refers to darkness or ignorance. This line highlights the role of divine guidance in helping individuals navigate through life's challenges and ignorance.

  • Real-Life Example: In a practical context, this mantra can be applied in various situations where wisdom and guidance are needed. For instance, in decision-making processes, whether in personal life or business, individuals often face choices that require careful consideration. By invoking the essence of this mantra, one can seek clarity and insight, ensuring that decisions are made with wisdom, regardless of the direction they may take.
  • Significance: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for wisdom and guidance from a higher power, represented by Indra. It serves as a reminder that intelligence and understanding are crucial in navigating life's complexities. The mantra encourages individuals to seek knowledge and clarity, which can lead to achieving their goals and desires.
  • Application: This mantra can be applicable in various real-life scenarios, such as during times of uncertainty, when facing moral dilemmas, or when pursuing personal or professional aspirations. By reflecting on the wisdom it embodies, individuals can cultivate a mindset that values thoughtful decision-making and seeks divine guidance in their endeavors.
