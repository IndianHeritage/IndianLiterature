किमु ष्विद अस्मै निविदो भणन्तेन्द्रस्यावद्यं दिधिषन्त आपः |ममैतान पुत्रो महता वधेन वृतं जगहन्वां असृजद वि सिन्धून ||
kim u ṣvid asmai nivido bhanantendrasyāvadyaṁ didhiṣanta āpaḥ | mamaitān putro mahatā vadhena vṛtraṁ jaghanvām̐ asṛjad vi sindhūn ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • kim u: What indeed
    • ṣvid: is the cause
    • asmai: for him
    • nivido: who is aware
    • bhanantendrasyāvadyaṁ: of the divine light
    • didhiṣanta: who desires
    • āpaḥ: the waters
    • mamaitān: these (my)
    • putro: sons
    • mahatā: with great
    • vadhena: strength
    • vṛtraṁ: the demon
    • jaghanvām̐: we shall strike down
    • asṛjad: have created
    • vi: in the
    • sindhūn: rivers
  • Contextual Meaning:
    • This mantra reflects a dialogue with the divine, questioning the nature of existence and the forces that govern it.
    • The speaker seeks to understand the divine light and its implications for life, emphasizing the importance of awareness and knowledge.
    • The mention of "sons" symbolizes the continuation of life and legacy, while "strength" refers to the power needed to overcome obstacles, represented by the demon Vṛtra.
    • The "waters" signify the source of life and nourishment, suggesting that understanding and harnessing these forces is crucial for growth and prosperity.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a personal context, this mantra can be applied to the journey of self-discovery, where one seeks knowledge and understanding to overcome personal challenges.
    • In a community setting, it can represent collective efforts to address societal issues, where awareness and strength are necessary to combat adversity.
    • In environmental discussions, the reference to "waters" can relate to the importance of preserving natural resources and understanding their role in sustaining life.
  • Conclusion and Significance:
    • This mantra emphasizes the quest for knowledge and the importance of strength in overcoming challenges.
    • It serves as a reminder that awareness and understanding are essential in navigating life's complexities.
    • In real life, it encourages individuals and communities to seek wisdom, harness their strengths, and work together to create a better future.
