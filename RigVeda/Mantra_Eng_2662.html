तं सबाधो यतश्रुच इथ्था धियाऽ यज्ञवन्तः |आ चक्रुर अग्निम ऊतये ||
taṁ sabādho yatasruca itthā dhiyā yajñavantaḥ | ā cakrur agnim ūtaye ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • taṁ: that
    • sabādho: with all the sounds or voices
    • yatasruca: from where the offerings are made
    • itthā: thus or in this manner
    • dhiyā: with intellect or understanding
    • yajñavantaḥ: those who perform sacrifices or rituals
    • ā: towards
    • cakrur: they have created or established
    • agnim: the fire (often symbolizing the divine or the energy)
    • ūtaye: for the purpose of nourishment or sustenance
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the collective effort of individuals who, with their intellect and understanding, come together to perform rituals or sacrifices.
    • The phrase "with all the sounds" suggests a harmonious collaboration, where different voices and contributions are valued.
    • The establishment of fire symbolizes the divine presence and the energy that is invoked through these rituals, which is essential for nourishment and sustenance.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community gathering, when people come together to celebrate a festival, they often perform rituals that involve music, dance, and offerings. This reflects the essence of "sabādho" where various voices and talents unite for a common purpose.
    • In a corporate setting, team-building exercises can be seen as a modern-day ritual where individuals contribute their skills and ideas (yajñavantaḥ) to create a successful project (agnim) that benefits the organization (ūtaye).
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its emphasis on collaboration, understanding, and the collective pursuit of a higher purpose.
    • It teaches us that when individuals come together with a shared intention and respect for each other's contributions, they can create something powerful and nourishing for the community.
    • This principle is applicable in various aspects of life, from personal relationships to professional environments, highlighting the importance of unity and shared goals.
