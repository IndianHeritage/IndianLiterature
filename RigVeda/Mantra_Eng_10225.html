निर्उ स्वसारम् अस्कृतोषसँ देवी आयती |अपेदु हासते तमः ||
nir u svasāram askṛtoṣasaṁ devy āyatī | aped u hāsate tamaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • nir: without
    • u: or
    • svasāram: the essence of breath or life
    • askṛtoṣasaṁ: created or formed
    • devy: divine or goddess-like
    • āyatī: is present or exists
    • aped: even
    • u: or
    • hāsate: laughs or rejoices
    • tamaḥ: darkness or ignorance
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the essence of life and the divine presence that exists even in the absence of breath or life.
    • The phrase "without the essence of breath" suggests a state of being that transcends physical existence, indicating a spiritual or metaphysical reality.
    • The term "devy āyatī" emphasizes the idea that divinity is always present, regardless of the material conditions.
    • The latter part of the mantra, "aped u hāsate tamaḥ," suggests that even in the face of darkness or ignorance, there is a possibility of joy and laughter, hinting at the resilience of the spirit.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In moments of grief or loss, individuals often find solace in the belief that the essence of their loved ones continues to exist in a spiritual form, reflecting the idea of "devy āyatī."
    • People facing challenges or hardships may experience moments of joy and laughter, even when surrounded by difficulties, illustrating the concept of finding light in darkness.
    • In meditation practices, individuals often seek to transcend their physical existence, focusing on the breath and the divine presence within, resonating with the mantra's themes.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of recognizing the divine presence in all aspects of life, even in challenging times.
    • It encourages individuals to seek joy and laughter, despite the darkness they may encounter, promoting resilience and hope.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra serves as a reminder to connect with the spiritual essence within and to find light amidst adversity, fostering a deeper understanding of existence beyond the physical realm.
