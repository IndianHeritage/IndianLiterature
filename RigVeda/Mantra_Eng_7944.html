अभि द्रोणानि बभ्रवः शुक्रा ऋतस्य धारया |वाजं गोमन्तम अक्षरन् ||
abhi droṇāni babhravaḥ śukrā ṛtasya dhārayā | vājaṁ gomantam akṣaran ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • abhi: towards
    • droṇāni: the containers or vessels
    • babhravaḥ: filled or abundant
    • śukrā: pure or auspicious
    • ṛtasya: of truth or righteousness
    • dhārayā: by holding or sustaining
    • vājaṁ: the best or excellent
    • gomantam: possessing cows (wealth)
    • akṣaran: imperishable or eternal
  • Overall Meaning: This mantra speaks of the abundance of pure and righteous qualities that sustain and support the best of wealth, symbolized by cows, which were considered a sign of prosperity in ancient times. It emphasizes the importance of truth and righteousness in achieving lasting success and abundance.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, a company that operates with integrity and transparency is more likely to build lasting relationships with clients and partners, leading to sustainable growth.
    • In personal life, individuals who uphold their values and act with honesty tend to attract positive relationships and opportunities, reflecting the idea of "abhi droṇāni babhravaḥ" where righteousness leads to abundance.
    • In community service, organizations that focus on truth and ethical practices often gain the trust of the community, resulting in greater support and resources for their initiatives.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its reminder that true wealth and abundance come from living a life of truth and righteousness. It teaches us that by holding onto these values, we can create a fulfilling and prosperous life. In real-life scenarios, whether in personal relationships, business dealings, or community engagements, the principles of integrity and ethical conduct are essential for achieving lasting success and harmony.
