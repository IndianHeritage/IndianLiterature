मां नो हिंसीज जनिता यः पृथिव्या यो वा दिवं सत्यधर्मा जजाना |यश्चापश चन्द्रा बृहतीर जजाना कसमै देवाय हविषा विद्हेम ||
mā no hiṁsīj janitā yaḥ pṛthivyā yo vā divaṁ satyadharmā jajāna | yaś cāpaś candrā bṛhatīr jajāna kasmai devāya haviṣā vidhema ||
  • mā no hiṁsīj janitā: This phrase translates to "May he not harm us, the creator." It emphasizes the importance of non-violence and protection from harm. In real life, this can be related to the concept of peace and safety in our communities, where we seek to create an environment free from violence.
  • yaḥ pṛthivyā: This means "who is on the earth." It signifies the connection between the divine and the earthly realm. In practical terms, it reminds us of our responsibilities towards the planet and the importance of environmental stewardship.
  • yo vā divaṁ: This translates to "who is in the heavens." It highlights the divine presence in the cosmos. This can be related to our quest for knowledge and understanding of the universe, inspiring scientific exploration and spiritual reflection.
  • satyadharmā jajāna: This means "who has established truth and righteousness." It underscores the significance of living a life based on truth and moral values. In everyday life, this can be seen in the importance of integrity in personal and professional relationships.
  • yaś cāpaś candrā bṛhatīr jajāna: This translates to "who has created the vastness of the moon." It symbolizes the vastness and beauty of creation. In real life, this can inspire us to appreciate the wonders of nature and the universe, encouraging a sense of awe and gratitude.
  • kasmai devāya haviṣā vidhema: This means "to which deity shall we offer our oblation?" It reflects the act of devotion and seeking blessings from the divine. In practical terms, this can be related to the importance of gratitude and seeking guidance in our lives, whether through prayer, meditation, or acts of kindness.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking protection, truth, and connection with the divine. Its significance lies in promoting values such as non-violence, environmental responsibility, integrity, and gratitude. In real life, these principles can guide our actions and decisions, fostering a harmonious existence with ourselves, others, and the world around us. By embodying these teachings, we contribute to a more peaceful and just society.
