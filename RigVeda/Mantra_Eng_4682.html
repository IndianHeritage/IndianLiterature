आद्यां चिन नू चित तद अपो नदीनां यदाभ्यो अरदो गातुम इन्द्र |नि पर्वता अद्मसदो न सेदुस त्वया दृढानि सुक्रतो राजांसि ||
adyā cin nū cit tad apo nadīnāṁ yad ābhyo arado gātum indra | ni parvatā admasado na sedus tvayā dṛḻhāni sukrato rajāṁsi ||
  • adyā - Today
  • cin - To know or to perceive
  • nū - Indeed or certainly
  • cit - Consciousness or awareness
  • tad - That
  • apo - Water
  • nadīnāṁ - Of the rivers
  • yad - Which
  • ābhyo - From the sources
  • arado - To flow or to move
  • gātum - To go or to reach
  • indra - The deity Indra, often associated with strength and power
  • ni - Not
  • parvatā - Mountains
  • admasado - To be seated or established
  • na - Not
  • sedus - To be firm or stable
  • tvayā - By you
  • dṛḻhāni - Strong or steadfast
  • sukrato - Good deeds or virtuous actions
  • rajāṁsi - Kingdoms or realms

This mantra can be interpreted as a call for awareness and understanding of the divine forces that govern the natural world, particularly the flow of water and the strength of the mountains. It emphasizes the importance of recognizing the interconnectedness of all elements in nature and the role of divine power in maintaining balance.


In real life, this can be seen in the way we interact with our environment. For example:

  • Water is essential for life, and understanding its flow in rivers can teach us about sustainability and conservation.
  • The mountains symbolize stability and strength, reminding us to remain grounded in our values and principles.
  • Indra, as a symbol of power, encourages us to harness our inner strength to overcome challenges.

Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for awareness and respect for the natural world and the divine forces that shape it. It encourages us to engage in virtuous actions and to recognize our place within the larger ecosystem. In practical terms, this can be applied in our daily lives by promoting environmental stewardship, making conscious choices that reflect our values, and striving for personal growth and strength in the face of adversity.
