समिधा यो निशिती दाशद अदितिं धामभिर अस्य mortalः |विश्वेत स धिभिः शुभगो जनाम् अति द्युम्नैर उद्न इव तारिषत ||
samidhā yo niśitī dāśad aditiṁ dhāmabhir asya martyaḥ | viśvet sa dhībhiḥ subhago janām̐ ati dyumnair udna iva tāriṣat ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • samidhā: kindled, ignited
    • yo: who
    • niśitī: sharp, keen
    • dāśad: to give, to bestow
    • aditiṁ: the infinite, the boundless
    • dhāmabhir: with the realms, with the worlds
    • asya: of this, of these
    • martyaḥ: mortal, human
    • viśvet: all, universal
    • sa: he, that
    • dhībhiḥ: with wisdom, with intellect
    • subhago: auspicious, fortunate
    • janām̐: people, beings
    • ati: exceedingly, very
    • dyumnair: with glory, with brilliance
    • udna: like, as
    • iva: like, as if
    • tāriṣat: to shine, to illuminate
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the importance of wisdom and enlightenment in human life.
    • The phrase "samidhā yo niśitī" suggests that those who are ignited with knowledge and sharp intellect are capable of bestowing the infinite blessings of the universe.
    • "Aditiṁ dhāmabhir asya martyaḥ" indicates that through wisdom, mortals can connect with the boundless realms of existence.
    • The mantra emphasizes that all beings ("viśvet sa dhībhiḥ") can achieve auspiciousness and fortune through the pursuit of knowledge and understanding.
    • The concluding part, "ati dyumnair udna iva tāriṣat," implies that wisdom and knowledge illuminate life, much like the sun brightens the day.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In education, students who seek knowledge with curiosity and dedication often find themselves achieving great success, akin to the illumination described in the mantra.
    • In professional settings, individuals who apply wisdom and intellect to their work tend to create positive impacts, leading to prosperous outcomes for themselves and their teams.
    • In personal relationships, those who communicate with understanding and empathy foster deeper connections, reflecting the auspiciousness mentioned in the mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of wisdom and knowledge in human life, suggesting that they are essential for achieving a fulfilling and prosperous existence.
    • It encourages individuals to seek enlightenment and understanding, which can lead to personal growth and a positive impact on society.
    • In essence, the mantra serves as a reminder that through the pursuit of knowledge, we can illuminate our lives and the lives of others, creating a more harmonious and prosperous world.
