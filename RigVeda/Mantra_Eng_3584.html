रयिं दिवो दुतितरो विभातिः प्रजावन्तं यच्छतास्मासु देविः |स्योनाद आ वः प्रतिबुध्यमानाḥ सुवीर्यस्य पतयः स्याम ||
rayiṁ divo duhitaro vibhātīḥ prajāvantaṁ yacchatāsmāsu devīḥ | syonād ā vaḥ pratibudhyamānāḥ suvīryasya patayaḥ syāma ||
  • Translation: The mantra can be translated as: "The daughters of the sky shine forth, bringing forth progeny; may the divine ones who are aware of us, grant us strength and prosperity."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • rayiṁ: wealth, prosperity, or abundance.
    • divo: of the sky or heaven.
    • duhitaro: daughters, often symbolizing nurturing and growth.
    • vibhātīḥ: shine forth or illuminate.
    • prajāvantaṁ: one who brings forth progeny or creation.
    • yacchatāsmāsu: may grant us or bestow upon us.
    • devīḥ: divine beings or goddesses.
    • syonād: may we be strong or powerful.
    • ā vaḥ: to us or for us.
    • pratibudhyamānāḥ: being aware or conscious.
    • suvīryasya: of great strength or valor.
    • patayaḥ: may we be leaders or rulers.
    • syāma: may we become or may we be.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of nurturing and growth, akin to how parents nurture their children to become strong and capable individuals.
    • In a corporate setting, this can be related to how leaders foster talent within their teams, ensuring that the next generation of leaders is well-prepared and empowered.
    • In community development, the mantra reflects the collective effort of individuals working together to create a prosperous environment for future generations.
  • Significance:
    • This mantra highlights the connection between the divine and human efforts in achieving prosperity and strength.
    • It serves as a reminder of the importance of community, collaboration, and the nurturing of future generations.
    • In spiritual practices, reciting this mantra can invoke blessings for strength, prosperity, and awareness from the divine, encouraging individuals to strive for personal and communal growth.
  • Conclusion:
    • The mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking divine support in our endeavors for prosperity and strength.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal growth to community development, emphasizing the importance of nurturing and collaboration.
    • By embodying the principles of this mantra, individuals and communities can work towards a brighter, more prosperous future.
