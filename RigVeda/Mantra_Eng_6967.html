आ स एतु य ईवद आमं अदेवः पूर्तम् आददे |यथा चिद वशो अश्व्यः पृथुश्रवसि कानीत3 'स्य व्युष्य आददे ||
ā sa etu ya īvad ām̐ adevaḥ pūrtam ādade | yathā cid vaśo aśvyaḥ pṛthuśravasi kānīte3 'syā vyuṣy ādade ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā: from, out of
    • sa: he, that
    • etu: may come, may arrive
    • ya: who, which
    • īvad: like, as if
    • ām̐: indeed, truly
    • adevaḥ: the divine, the godly
    • pūrtam: fullness, completeness
    • ādade: he takes, he receives
    • yathā: as, just as
    • cid: indeed, certainly
    • vaśo: under control, in command
    • aśvyaḥ: horses, steeds
    • pṛthuśravasi: of great fame, renowned
    • kānīte: may be drawn, may be attracted
    • syā: may be, may exist
    • vyuṣy: shining, illuminating
    • ādade: he takes, he receives
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the divine presence and the fullness that it brings into our lives.
    • It suggests that when one is aligned with the divine, they can attract abundance and greatness.
    • The reference to "horses" symbolizes strength, power, and the ability to move forward in life.
    • The phrase "of great fame" indicates that those who are in harmony with the divine will achieve recognition and success.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, a leader who embodies integrity and aligns their actions with ethical principles often attracts loyal customers and employees, leading to a successful enterprise.
    • In personal relationships, individuals who practice kindness and empathy tend to build strong, supportive networks, enhancing their social and emotional well-being.
    • In the realm of personal development, those who seek knowledge and self-improvement often find opportunities that lead to personal and professional growth.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of aligning oneself with divine principles to attract abundance and success.
    • It serves as a reminder that our actions and intentions can lead to greater fulfillment and recognition in life.
    • In practical terms, it encourages individuals to cultivate qualities such as integrity, kindness, and a commitment to personal growth, which can lead to a more prosperous and meaningful life.
