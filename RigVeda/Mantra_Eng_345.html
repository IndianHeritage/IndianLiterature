आश्विनाव अश्वावत्येषा यातं शवीरया |गोमद दसराऽ हिरण्यवत ||
āśvināv aśvāvatyeṣā yātaṁ śavīrayā | gomad dasrā hiraṇyavat ||
  • Word Breakdown:
    • āśvināv: Refers to the Ashvins, the twin deities in Vedic mythology associated with health, medicine, and the dawn.
    • aśvāvatyeṣā: This can be interpreted as "this is like horses," indicating strength, speed, and vitality.
    • yātaṁ: Means "having gone" or "having traveled," suggesting movement or progress.
    • śavīrayā: This term can be understood as "with strength" or "with vigor," emphasizing the power and energy involved.
    • gomad: Refers to "the one who has cows," symbolizing wealth, prosperity, and nourishment.
    • dasrā: This can mean "swift" or "quick," indicating agility and readiness.
    • hiraṇyavat: Translates to "golden" or "wealthy," often associated with abundance and prosperity.
  • Meaning and Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the Ashvins, seeking their blessings for health and vitality.
    • The imagery of horses suggests a desire for strength and speed in one's endeavors.
    • The mention of cows symbolizes wealth and sustenance, indicating a holistic approach to prosperity.
    • The overall tone of the mantra is one of seeking divine assistance in achieving success and well-being.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be applied when launching a new venture, invoking the Ashvins for success and prosperity.
    • Athletes may recite this mantra before competitions to seek strength and agility, akin to the qualities of horses.
    • Farmers may use this mantra during rituals for a bountiful harvest, emphasizing the importance of cows as a symbol of wealth and nourishment.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its holistic approach to health, wealth, and success.
    • It serves as a reminder to seek divine guidance in all aspects of life, from physical well-being to material prosperity.
    • In contemporary life, this mantra can be a source of inspiration and motivation, encouraging individuals to strive for their goals with vigor and determination.
