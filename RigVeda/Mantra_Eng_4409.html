हुवे वः सूनुं सहसो युवानम् अद्रोघवाचम् मतीभिर यविष्टम् |य इन्बति द्रविणानि प्रचेताः विश्ववाराणि पुरुवारो अध्रुक् ||
huve vaḥ sūnuṁ sahaso yuvānam adroghavācam matibhir yaviṣṭham | ya invati draviṇāni pracetā viśvavārāṇi puruvāro adhruk ||
  • huve vaḥ: This phrase can be interpreted as "I invoke you" or "I call upon you." It signifies the act of seeking divine assistance or guidance.
  • sūnuṁ: This word means "son" or "offspring." In a broader sense, it can represent the next generation or the continuation of lineage.
  • sahaso: This term translates to "with strength" or "powerful." It emphasizes the importance of strength in achieving goals.
  • yuvānam: Meaning "young" or "youthful," this word highlights the vitality and potential of youth.
  • adroghavācam: This can be understood as "truthful speech" or "words that do not deceive." It underscores the value of honesty and integrity in communication.
  • matibhir: This word refers to "thoughts" or "intellect." It signifies the importance of wisdom and discernment in decision-making.
  • yaviṣṭham: Meaning "the best" or "the most excellent," this term emphasizes the pursuit of excellence in all endeavors.
  • ya invati: This phrase translates to "who brings" or "who leads." It indicates a guiding force or influence.
  • draviṇāni: This word means "wealth" or "resources." It highlights the significance of material and spiritual abundance.
  • pracetā: This term refers to "intelligence" or "awareness." It emphasizes the need for insight and understanding in navigating life.
  • viśvavārāṇi: This can be interpreted as "all-encompassing" or "universal." It signifies the interconnectedness of all beings and the universe.
  • puruvāro: Meaning "the one who is ahead" or "the leader," this word highlights the importance of leadership and guidance.
  • adhruk: This term can be understood as "to protect" or "to safeguard." It emphasizes the need for protection and support in life.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a profound message about invoking strength, wisdom, and integrity in our lives. It encourages us to seek guidance and support while emphasizing the importance of truthful communication and the pursuit of excellence. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as leadership roles, parenting, and personal development. By embodying the principles outlined in this mantra, individuals can foster a harmonious and prosperous environment for themselves and those around them, ultimately contributing to the well-being of society as a whole.
