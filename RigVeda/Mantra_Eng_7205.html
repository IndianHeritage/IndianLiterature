अनेहो न उर्व्रज उरूचि वि प्रसर्तवे |कृधि तोकाय जीवसे ||
aneho na uruvraja urūci vi prasartave | kṛdhi tokāya jīvase ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • aneho: Without delay, without hindrance
    • na: Not
    • uruvraja: Abundant wealth or prosperity
    • urūci: Great radiance or brilliance
    • vi: In various ways
    • prasartave: To be spread or expanded
    • kṛdhi: Make or create
    • tokāya: For the offspring or progeny
    • jīvase: For living or existence
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks to the idea of creating a life filled with abundance and prosperity.
    • The phrase "without delay" suggests that one should not hesitate in pursuing wealth and success.
    • The mention of "great radiance" indicates that this wealth should not only be material but also bring joy and enlightenment.
    • It emphasizes the importance of spreading this prosperity in various ways, suggesting a communal or shared approach to wealth.
    • Finally, the mantra highlights the significance of creating a legacy for future generations, ensuring that prosperity continues.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • A business owner who invests in sustainable practices not only creates wealth for themselves but also contributes to the community and environment.
    • A philanthropist who shares their wealth with educational institutions ensures that future generations have access to knowledge and opportunities.
    • Individuals who mentor others in their field help to spread their knowledge and success, creating a ripple effect of prosperity.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of pursuing wealth and prosperity without hesitation.
    • It encourages individuals to not only seek personal gain but also to share their success with others, fostering a sense of community.
    • Ultimately, it highlights the responsibility of creating a legacy that benefits future generations, ensuring that prosperity is not just a fleeting moment but a lasting impact.
