एष स्य कारुर जरेते सूक्तैर अग्रे बुधान उषसां सुमन्मा |इषा तं वर्धद अग्न्याः पयोभिर यूयम् पात स्वस्तिभिः सदा नः ||
eṣa sya kārur jarate sūktair agre budhāna uṣasāṁ sumanmā | iṣā taṁ vardhad aghnyā payobhir yūyam pāta svastibhiḥ sadā naḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • eṣa: this
    • sya: of this
    • kārur: fierce or powerful
    • jarate: brings forth or generates
    • sūktair: through hymns or praises
    • agre: in the forefront or at the beginning
    • budhāna: wise or enlightened beings
    • uṣasāṁ: of the dawn
    • sumanmā: with good thoughts or auspiciousness
    • iṣā: may it be
    • taṁ: that
    • vardhad: may it grow or increase
    • aghnyā: without harm or injury
    • payobhir: with nourishing substances or waters
    • yūyam: you all
    • pāta: protect or preserve
    • svastibhiḥ: with auspiciousness or well-being
    • sadā: always
    • naḥ: for us
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the powerful forces of nature and the divine, emphasizing the importance of wisdom and auspiciousness.
    • It suggests that through the power of hymns and praises, one can generate positive energy and enlightenment, especially at the dawn of a new day.
    • The mantra calls for protection and nourishment, highlighting the interconnectedness of all beings and the need for collective well-being.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, this mantra can be applied during morning rituals or meditations, where individuals seek to start their day with positive intentions and clarity.
    • In community gatherings, such as festivals or ceremonies, invoking this mantra can foster a sense of unity and collective growth, encouraging participants to share good thoughts and actions.
    • In personal development, individuals can reflect on the qualities of wisdom and auspiciousness, striving to cultivate these traits in their interactions and decisions.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for harmony, wisdom, and protection, reminding us of the power of collective intention and the importance of nurturing positive energies.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal growth to community well-being, encouraging individuals to seek enlightenment and auspiciousness in their lives.
    • Ultimately, this mantra serves as a reminder of our interconnectedness and the potential for growth and positivity when we come together with good intentions.
