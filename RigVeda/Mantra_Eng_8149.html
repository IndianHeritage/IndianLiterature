आ पवस्व सहस्रिणं रयिं सोम सुवीर्यम् |अस्मे श्रवांसी धारय ||
ā pavasva sahasriṇaṁ rayiṁ soma suvīryam | asme śravāṁsi dhāraya ||
  • Mantra: "ā pavasva sahasriṇaṁ rayiṁ soma suvīryam | asme śravāṁsi dhāraya ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā: O (a call or invocation)
    • pavasva: may you purify or cleanse
    • sahasriṇaṁ: of the thousand (often refers to abundance)
    • rayiṁ: wealth or prosperity
    • soma: the divine nectar or drink, often associated with immortality and strength
    • suvīryam: of great strength or valor
    • asme: for us
    • śravāṁsi: the glories or fame
    • dhāraya: may you hold or sustain
  • Detailed Explanation:
    • This mantra is a prayer for the purification and enhancement of wealth and strength.
    • The invocation of "ā pavasva" suggests a desire for divine intervention to cleanse and purify one's life.
    • "Sahasriṇaṁ rayiṁ" emphasizes the abundance of wealth, indicating that the speaker seeks not just material wealth but also spiritual richness.
    • "Soma" represents the divine essence that nourishes and strengthens, akin to how we seek nourishment in our daily lives through food, relationships, and experiences.
    • "Suvīryam" highlights the importance of strength, which can be interpreted as physical, mental, or emotional resilience.
    • The phrase "asme śravāṁsi dhāraya" is a request for sustaining glory and fame, which can be related to personal achievements, reputation, and the impact one has on society.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be applied when seeking to purify one's intentions and actions to attract prosperity and success.
    • In personal development, individuals may meditate on this mantra to cultivate inner strength and resilience during challenging times.
    • In community settings, it can inspire collective efforts to achieve common goals, ensuring that the group's achievements are recognized and celebrated.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the interconnectedness of wealth, strength, and reputation.
    • Its significance lies in the holistic approach to prosperity, emphasizing that true wealth encompasses not just material gains but also spiritual and emotional well-being.
    • In real life, it encourages individuals and communities to strive for purity in their actions, seek strength in adversity, and aim for lasting recognition through their contributions.
