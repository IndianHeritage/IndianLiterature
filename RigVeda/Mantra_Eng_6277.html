समत्स्व अग्निम अवसे वाजयन्तो हवामहे |वाजेषु चित्तराद्हसम् ||
samatsv agnim avase vājayanto havāmahe | vājeṣu citrarādhasam ||
  • Mantra: "samatsv agnim avase vājayanto havāmahe | vājeṣu citrarādhasam ||"
  • Translation: "We invoke the fire (Agni) with a harmonious mind, seeking the strength of the powerful, who is the bestower of wealth."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • samatsv: This word signifies harmony or balance, indicating a collective and unified approach.
    • agnim: Refers to Agni, the sacred fire, which symbolizes purity, transformation, and the divine presence.
    • avase: Means to invoke or call upon, suggesting a deliberate action of seeking connection.
    • vājayanto: Translates to those who bestow strength or power, often associated with wealth and prosperity.
    • havāmahe: This is a call to action, meaning "we offer" or "we invoke," emphasizing the communal aspect of the prayer.
    • vājeṣu: Refers to wealth or resources, indicating the material and spiritual abundance we seek.
    • citrarādhasam: This term denotes the one who is diverse in riches or the one who provides various forms of wealth.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, a team may come together to invoke the spirit of collaboration (samatsv) to achieve a common goal, much like invoking Agni for guidance and strength.
    • During a community gathering, people may light a fire (Agni) to symbolize unity and the sharing of resources, reflecting the essence of vājayanto and vājeṣu.
    • In personal development, individuals may meditate on their goals, seeking the strength and clarity (citrarādhasam) to manifest their desires, akin to invoking Agni for support.
  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of unity, intention, and the invocation of divine energy (Agni) to achieve prosperity and strength in various aspects of life. It serves as a reminder that when individuals come together with a harmonious mindset, they can attract abundance and support in their endeavors. This principle is applicable in personal, professional, and communal contexts, encouraging collaboration and shared purpose to manifest desired outcomes.
