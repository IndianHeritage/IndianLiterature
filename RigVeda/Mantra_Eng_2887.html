यज जायतास तद अहर अस्य कामे ऽंशोः पीयूषम अपिबो गिरिष्ठाम् |तं ते माता परि योषा जनित्री महः पितुर दमा आसिन्चद अग्रे ||
yaj jāyathās tad ahar asya kāme 'ṁśoḥ pīyūṣam apibo giriṣṭhām | taṁ te mātā pari yoṣā janitrī mahaḥ pitur dama āsiñcad agre ||
  • yaj jāyathās: This phrase can be interpreted as "whatever is born" or "whatever comes into existence." It signifies the importance of creation and the natural processes that lead to the birth of life.
  • tad ahar asya kāme 'ṁśoḥ: This translates to "that day, for the sake of desires." It emphasizes the connection between time and human desires, suggesting that our aspirations and needs are tied to specific moments in our lives.
  • pīyūṣam apibo giriṣṭhām: Here, "pīyūṣam" refers to the essence or nectar, while "giriṣṭhām" means "the highest" or "the best." This part of the mantra highlights the pursuit of the highest form of nourishment or fulfillment, both physically and spiritually.
  • taṁ te mātā pari yoṣā janitrī: This can be understood as "to you, the mother, the nurturing woman." It acknowledges the role of the feminine divine, representing nurturing, care, and the source of life.
  • mahaḥ pitur dama āsiñcad agre: This translates to "the great father’s abode, may it be poured forth." It signifies the blessings and abundance that come from the paternal figure, suggesting a balance between maternal and paternal influences in life.

  • Significance: This mantra encapsulates the essence of creation, desire, nourishment, and the duality of maternal and paternal influences in life. It reflects the interconnectedness of all beings and the importance of nurturing relationships.
  • Real-life Application: In our daily lives, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as family dynamics, where the nurturing role of mothers and the guiding role of fathers are essential for a balanced upbringing. It also resonates in the pursuit of personal goals, reminding us to seek fulfillment and nourishment in our endeavors.

  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a reminder of the cyclical nature of life, the importance of nurturing relationships, and the pursuit of higher aspirations. It encourages us to honor both our desires and the sources of our nourishment, fostering a holistic approach to living.
