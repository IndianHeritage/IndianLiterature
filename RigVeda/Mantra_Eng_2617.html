यत् त्वा होतारं अनजन मियेदे निषादयन्तो यजथाय देवाः |स त्वं नो अग्नि 'वितेह बोध्य अधि श्रवांसि धेहि नस् तनूषु ||
yat tvā hotāram anajan miyedhe niṣādayanto yajathāya devāḥ | sa tvaṁ no agne 'viteha bodhy adhi śravāṁsi dhehi nas tanūṣu ||
  • Mantra: "yat tvā hotāram anajan miyedhe niṣādayanto yajathāya devāḥ | sa tvaṁ no agne 'viteha bodhy adhi śravāṁsi dhehi nas tanūṣu ||"
  • Translation: "O Agni, the one who is invoked, may the gods who are engaged in sacrifice, inspire us. May you, O Agni, enlighten us and grant us strength in our bodies."

  • Word-by-Word Explanation:
    • yat: that which
    • tvā: you
    • hotāram: the one who is invoked or called upon
    • anajan: unblemished or pure
    • miyedhe: may inspire or enlighten
    • niṣādayanto: those who are engaged in sacrifice
    • yajathāya: for the purpose of worship or sacrifice
    • devāḥ: the gods
    • sa: he or that
    • tvaṁ: you
    • no: for us
    • agne: O Agni (the fire god)
    • viteha: may you enlighten
    • bodhy: may you awaken or inspire
    • adhi: upon or in
    • śravāṁsi: the glories or strengths
    • dhehi: grant or bestow
    • nas: to us
    • tanūṣu: in our bodies

  • Significance:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Agni, the fire god, who symbolizes purity, transformation, and the divine connection between humans and the gods.
    • It emphasizes the importance of invoking divine energies to inspire and enlighten individuals, particularly in the context of rituals and sacrifices.
    • The mantra reflects a desire for strength and clarity in one's physical and spiritual existence, highlighting the interconnectedness of body and spirit.

  • Real-Life Application:
    • In modern life, this mantra can be applied in various contexts such as meditation, yoga, and personal development practices where individuals seek clarity and strength.
    • For example, during a challenging situation, one might recite this mantra to invoke inner strength and guidance, similar to how athletes visualize success before a competition.
    • In community gatherings or rituals, invoking Agni can symbolize the collective aspiration for enlightenment and unity, fostering a sense of purpose and connection among participants.

  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the power of divine invocation and the importance of seeking inspiration and strength from higher energies.
    • It encourages individuals to cultivate a deeper connection with their inner selves and the universe, promoting personal growth and communal harmony.
    • Ultimately, the mantra highlights the significance of spiritual practices in enhancing one's life and achieving a balanced existence.
