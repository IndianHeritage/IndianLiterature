तīvro vo मधुमाम् ayaṁ शुनहोत्रेषु मत्सरः |etam pibata kāmyam ||
tīvro vo madhumām̐ ayaṁ śunahotreṣu matsaraḥ | etam pibata kāmyam ||
  • tīvro: This word translates to "intense" or "powerful." It signifies a strong desire or a fervent wish.
  • vo: This means "your" or "to you," indicating a sense of belonging or possession.
  • madhumām̐: This term can be interpreted as "sweet" or "pleasant," often referring to something desirable or enjoyable.
  • ayam: This means "this," pointing to something specific that is being referred to.
  • śunahotreṣu: This word relates to "sacrifices" or "offerings," particularly in the context of rituals performed by priests (hotri) in Vedic traditions.
  • matsaraḥ: This translates to "jealousy" or "envy," indicating a negative emotion that can arise in competitive situations.
  • etam: This means "this" or "this one," again referring to something specific.
  • pibata: This translates to "drink" or "consume," suggesting an act of taking in or absorbing something.
  • kāmyam: This means "desired" or "wishful," indicating something that is sought after or longed for.

The mantra can be interpreted as a call to embrace intense desires and to consume the sweetness of life while being aware of the negative emotions like jealousy that can arise in competitive environments. It encourages individuals to focus on their aspirations and to partake in the offerings of life, while also recognizing the potential pitfalls of envy.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios:

  • Personal Growth: Individuals can use this mantra as a reminder to pursue their goals passionately and to enjoy the journey, rather than getting caught up in comparisons with others.
  • Workplace Dynamics: In a professional setting, it serves as a reminder to focus on one's own contributions and achievements, rather than succumbing to jealousy of colleagues' successes.
  • Relationships: In personal relationships, it encourages individuals to appreciate the sweetness of their connections and to avoid feelings of envy that can harm bonds.

In conclusion, this mantra emphasizes the importance of channeling intense desires positively while being mindful of the negative emotions that can arise in competitive situations. It serves as a guide to living a fulfilling life, encouraging individuals to savor their experiences and aspirations without letting jealousy cloud their judgment.
