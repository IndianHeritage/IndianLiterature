मन्त्र: विद्मा हि त्वा तुविकूर्मिं तुविदेष्णं तुवीमघम् |तुविमात्रं अवोभिः ||
vidmā hi tvā tuvikūrmiṁ tuvideṣṇaṁ tuvīmagham | tuvimātram avobhiḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • vidmā: We know
    • hi: indeed
    • tvā: you
    • tuvikūrmiṁ: the one who creates
    • tuvideṣṇaṁ: the one who is to be desired
    • tuvīmagham: the one who removes obstacles
    • tuvimātram: the one who is the essence
    • avobhiḥ: we invoke
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra expresses a deep reverence for a divine entity, acknowledging its role as the creator and sustainer of the universe.
    • It emphasizes the importance of knowledge and understanding in recognizing the divine presence in our lives.
    • The mantra also highlights the divine's ability to remove obstacles and challenges, making it a source of hope and strength for individuals facing difficulties.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In times of personal crisis, such as losing a job or facing health issues, individuals often turn to spiritual practices, including chanting mantras, to seek guidance and strength.
    • In a corporate setting, leaders may invoke this mantra to inspire their teams, fostering a culture of resilience and creativity in overcoming challenges.
    • Students preparing for exams may recite this mantra to enhance their focus and clarity, believing that it will help them overcome academic obstacles.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of knowledge, creation, and the removal of obstacles in our lives.
    • Its significance lies in its ability to inspire individuals to seek understanding and strength from the divine, especially during challenging times.
    • In various real-life scenarios, whether personal or professional, invoking this mantra can provide comfort, clarity, and a sense of purpose.
