साहा ये सन्ति मुष्ठिहेव हव्यो विश्वासु पृत्सु होत्रिषु |वृष्णश चन्द्रान न सुश्रवस्तमान गिरा वन्दस्व मरुतो अह ||
sāhā ye santi muṣṭiheva havyo viśvāsu pṛtsu hotṛṣu | vṛṣṇaś candrān na suśravastamān girā vandasva maruto aha ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sāhā: Together, in unity
    • ye: Those who
    • santi: Are, exist
    • muṣṭiheva: Like a fist
    • havyo: Worthy of offerings
    • viśvāsu: In all, everywhere
    • pṛtsu: In the realms, in the world
    • hotṛṣu: Among the priests, in the rituals
    • vṛṣṇaś: O Vṛṣṇa, the strong one
    • candrān: The moon, the shining one
    • na: Not
    • suśravastamān: The one of good fame
    • girā: With words, through speech
    • vandasva: Praise, honor
    • maruto: O Maruts, the storm gods
    • aha: I, me
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of unity and collective strength.
    • The phrase "sāhā ye santi" suggests that those who come together in harmony are like a fist, symbolizing strength and power.
    • The reference to "havyo viśvāsu pṛtsu hotṛṣu" indicates that this unity is essential in all aspects of life, especially in rituals and offerings.
    • By invoking "vṛṣṇaś candrān," the mantra calls upon the divine strength and brilliance, akin to the moon's light guiding us.
    • The plea to "vandasva maruto" signifies the importance of honoring and praising the forces of nature and divinity that support us.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace, teamwork and collaboration can lead to greater success, much like the unity described in the mantra.
    • In community service, when individuals come together for a common cause, they can achieve significant impact, reflecting the strength of unity.
    • During festivals or rituals, collective participation enhances the spiritual experience, embodying the essence of the mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of unity, collective strength, and the importance of honoring divine forces.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, such as teamwork, community efforts, and spiritual practices.
    • By embracing the principles of this mantra, individuals and communities can foster harmony, strength, and a deeper connection to the divine.
