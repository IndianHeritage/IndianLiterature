सृजो महीर इन्द्र या अपिन्वः परिष्टिता अहिना शूर पूर्वीः |अमर्त्यं चिद दासं मन्यमानम अवाभिनद उक्थैर वावृद्धानः ||
sṛjo mahīr indra yā apinvaḥ pariṣṭhitā ahinā śūra pūrvīḥ | amartyaṁ cid dāsam manyamānam avābhinad ukthair vāvṛdhānaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sṛjo: Create or bring forth
    • mahīr: Great or vast
    • indra: Indra, the king of gods, representing strength and power
    • yā: Who or that
    • apinvaḥ: Those who are not obstructed or hindered
    • pariṣṭhitā: Established or situated
    • ahinā: By the serpent or the powerful
    • śūra: Brave or valiant
    • pūrvīḥ: The ancient or the previous
    • amartyaṁ: Immortal or eternal
    • cid: Consciousness or awareness
    • dāsam: Servant or devotee
    • manyamānam: Considering or thinking
    • avābhinad: To be united or connected
    • ukthair: By the words or hymns
    • vāvṛdhānaḥ: Increasing or enhancing
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the creation and establishment of a great power, represented by Indra, who is unimpeded and connected to the ancient brave ones.
    • It emphasizes the importance of consciousness and the eternal nature of existence, suggesting that through devotion and connection, one can achieve a higher state of being.
    • The reference to "ahinā" (serpent) can symbolize the transformative power of energy, akin to the Kundalini in yoga, which when awakened leads to spiritual enlightenment.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In leadership, one can draw parallels to Indra's qualities of strength and bravery, inspiring teams to overcome obstacles and achieve greatness.
    • In personal development, the idea of being "unhindered" can relate to overcoming fears and limitations, much like individuals who pursue their passions despite societal expectations.
    • The concept of "amartyaṁ" can be reflected in the pursuit of knowledge and wisdom, which leads to a more profound understanding of life and existence.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of strength, consciousness, and connection in achieving greatness and overcoming challenges.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal growth to leadership, encouraging individuals to embrace their inner power and strive for a higher purpose.
    • Ultimately, it serves as a reminder that through awareness and connection, one can enhance their life and the lives of others, fostering a sense of community and shared strength.
