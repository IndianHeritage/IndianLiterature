आ मन्द्रस्य सनिṣ्यन्तो वरेण्यं वृणीमहे अह्रयं वाजम् ऋग्मियम् | रातिम भृगूणाम् उशिजं कविक्रतुम् अग्निं राजन्तं दिव्येन शोचिषा ||
ā mandrasya saniṣyanto vareṇyaṁ vṛṇīmahe ahrayaṁ vājam ṛgmiyam | rātim bhṛgūṇām uśijaṁ kavikratum agniṁ rājantaṁ divyena śociṣā ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā mandrasya: From the essence of the mantra or the sacred sound.
    • saniṣyanto: Those who are focused or concentrated.
    • vareṇyaṁ: The most excellent or worthy.
    • vṛṇīmahe: We choose or we invoke.
    • ahrayaṁ: The one who brings forth or manifests.
    • vājam: The strength or power.
    • ṛgmiyam: Related to the hymns or verses.
    • rātim: Delight or joy.
    • bhṛgūṇām: Of the Bhṛgus, a group of sages.
    • uśijaṁ: The one who is born or created.
    • kavikratum: The creative power of the poet or seer.
    • agniṁ: Fire, often symbolizing energy or divine presence.
    • rājantaṁ: Shining or radiant.
    • divyena: Divine or heavenly.
    • śociṣā: With brilliance or light.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the essence of divine energy and creativity.
    • It emphasizes the importance of focusing on the worthy and excellent aspects of life.
    • The reference to Agni symbolizes the transformative power of fire, which can be seen as a metaphor for knowledge and enlightenment.
    • The mention of the Bhṛgus highlights the connection to ancient wisdom and the pursuit of truth.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, this mantra can be applied when teams focus on innovative ideas (Agni) to drive projects forward, emphasizing collaboration and creativity.
    • In personal development, individuals can reflect on their goals and aspirations, choosing to focus on what is truly valuable and worthy in their lives.
    • In education, teachers can inspire students to seek knowledge (the divine light) and cultivate their creative potential.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder to seek excellence and focus on the divine aspects of life.
    • It encourages individuals to harness their inner strength and creativity to manifest their desires and aspirations.
    • In a broader context, it highlights the significance of wisdom, collaboration, and the pursuit of truth in all aspects of life.
