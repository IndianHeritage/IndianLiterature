ग्राव्णा तुन्नो अभिष्टुतः पवित्रं सोम गच्छसि |दधत स्तोत्रे सुवीर्यम् ||
grāvṇā tunno abhiṣṭutaḥ pavitraṁ soma gacchasi | dadhat stotre suvīryam ||
  • Translation: The mantra can be translated as: "O Soma, you who are praised, pure and powerful, may you come to us, bringing strength and vigor."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • grāvṇā: This term refers to the act of being praised or celebrated.
    • tunno: It signifies something that is pure or sacred.
    • abhiṣṭutaḥ: This means "who is praised" or "who is extolled."
    • pavitraṁ: This word translates to "pure" or "clean," often associated with spiritual purity.
    • soma: Soma is a significant deity in Vedic literature, often associated with a sacred drink that grants immortality and strength.
    • gacchasi: This means "may you come" or "may you arrive."
    • dadhat: This term means "bringing" or "carrying."
    • stotre: This refers to "praise" or "hymn," indicating a form of worship or acknowledgment.
    • suvīryam: This translates to "great strength" or "vigor," emphasizing the power that is being sought.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, a team may invoke this mantra metaphorically when seeking inspiration and strength to overcome challenges, emphasizing the importance of unity and collective effort.
    • During a sports event, athletes may embody the essence of this mantra by focusing on purity of purpose and strength, channeling their energy to achieve victory.
    • In personal development, individuals may reflect on the qualities of purity and strength in their daily lives, striving to cultivate these attributes in their actions and decisions.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its invocation of purity and strength, qualities that are essential in various aspects of life. It serves as a reminder to seek inner strength and maintain purity of intention, whether in personal endeavors, professional pursuits, or spiritual practices. By embodying these principles, individuals can navigate challenges with resilience and integrity, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling and impactful life.
