येषां इळा घृतहस्ता दुरोण आमं अपि प्रातः निषीदति |तांस्त्रायस्व सहस्य द्रुहो निदो यच्छा नः शर्मा दीर्घश्रुत ||
yeṣām iḻā ghṛtahastā duroṇa ām̐ api prātā niṣīdati | tām̐s trāyasva sahasya druho nido yacchā naḥ śarma dīrghaśrut ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yeṣām: of whom
    • iḻā: the goddess or divine power
    • ghṛta-hastā: having clarified butter in hand (symbolizing nourishment and prosperity)
    • duroṇa: the strong or powerful
    • ām̐: indeed
    • api: even
    • prātā: in the morning
    • niṣīdati: sits or resides
    • tām̐s: those
    • trāyasva: protect or save
    • sahasya: with strength or courage
    • druho: from enmity or harm
    • nido: from the evil or negative
    • yacchā: grant or give
    • naḥ: to us
    • śarma: happiness, peace, or shelter
    • dīrghaśrut: long-lasting or enduring
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the divine power (iḻā) that brings nourishment and prosperity (ghṛta-hastā) to those who are strong and powerful (duroṇa).
    • It emphasizes the importance of seeking protection (trāyasva) from harm (druho) and negativity (nido) in our lives.
    • The mantra suggests that even in the morning (prātā), when new beginnings occur, one should be mindful of the divine presence that offers shelter and happiness (śarma).
    • It calls for the granting of enduring peace (dīrghaśrut) to individuals, highlighting the need for long-term stability and security in life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, individuals often seek blessings for prosperity and protection, especially during significant events like weddings or new ventures. This mantra can be recited to invoke divine support.
    • In times of crisis or conflict, people may turn to spiritual practices or prayers for strength and protection, reflecting the essence of this mantra.
    • Communities often come together to seek collective peace and harmony, especially in challenging times, resonating with the call for enduring happiness and shelter.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of divine support in our lives, emphasizing the need for protection from negativity and the pursuit of long-lasting happiness.
    • Its significance lies in its applicability to various aspects of life, from personal challenges to communal harmony, making it relevant in both individual and collective contexts.
    • By invoking this mantra, individuals can cultivate a sense of security and peace, fostering resilience in the face of adversity.
