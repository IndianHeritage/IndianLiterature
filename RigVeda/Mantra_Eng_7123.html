न पापासो मनामहे नारायासो न जल्हवः |यद इन्व इन्द्रं वृषणं सचा सुतें सखायं कृणवामहै ||
na pāpāso manāmahe nārāyāso na jaḻhavaḥ | yad in nv indraṁ vṛṣaṇaṁ sacā sute sakhāyaṁ kṛṇavāmahai ||
  • na pāpāso manāmahe: This phrase translates to "We do not meditate on the sinful." It emphasizes the importance of focusing on purity of thought and intention. In real life, this can be related to the idea of avoiding negative influences and surrounding oneself with positivity. For example, in a workplace, maintaining a positive environment can lead to better productivity and morale.
  • nārāyāso na jaḻhavaḥ: This part means "We do not seek the unworthy." It suggests that one should strive for higher ideals and not get distracted by trivial or unworthy pursuits. In everyday life, this can be seen in the pursuit of meaningful goals, such as education or personal development, rather than engaging in activities that do not contribute to one's growth.
  • yad in nv indraṁ vṛṣaṇaṁ: This translates to "Whatever we may offer to Indra, the powerful." Indra is often regarded as a deity of strength and power. This line signifies the importance of offering our best efforts and intentions to higher powers or ideals. In practical terms, this can be related to the dedication one shows in their work or personal projects, striving to give their best in all endeavors.
  • sacā sute sakhāyaṁ kṛṇavāmahai: This means "We create a bond with the truth and the friend." It highlights the significance of truthfulness and friendship in our lives. In real life, this can be exemplified by the importance of honesty in relationships and the value of having supportive friends who encourage us to be our best selves.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of purity in thought, the pursuit of worthy goals, dedication to higher ideals, and the importance of truth and friendship. In a world filled with distractions and negativity, this mantra serves as a guiding principle to focus on what truly matters. By applying these teachings in our daily lives, we can cultivate a more meaningful existence, foster positive relationships, and contribute to a harmonious society.
