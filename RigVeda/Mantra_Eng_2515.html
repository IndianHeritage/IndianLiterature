मन्त्र: ऐभिर अग्नि सरथं याह्य अर्वाङ नानारथं वा विभवो ह्य अश्वाः |पत्नीवतस त्रिंशतं त्रिम्श च देवान अनुष्वधम आ वह मादयस्व ||
aibhir agne sarathaṁ yāhy arvāṅ nānārathaṁ vā vibhavo hy aśvāḥ | patnīvatas triṁśataṁ trīm̐ś ca devān anuṣvadham ā vaha mādayasva ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • aibhir: With the help of
    • agne: O Agni (the fire god)
    • sarathaṁ: Charioteer
    • yāhy: May you come
    • arvāṅ: With the horses
    • nānārathaṁ: Various chariots
    • vā: Or
    • vibhavo: Abundant
    • hy: Indeed
    • aśvāḥ: Horses
    • patnīvatas: Of the married ones
    • triṁśataṁ: Thirty
    • trīm̐ś: Three
    • ca: And
    • devān: The gods
    • anuṣvadham: Following the path
    • ā: Come
    • vaha: Carry
    • mādayasva: May you be pleased
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Agni, the fire god, who is often seen as a mediator between humans and the divine.
    • The invocation of Agni as a charioteer suggests guidance and direction in life, akin to how a charioteer steers a chariot.
    • The mention of "various chariots" and "abundant horses" symbolizes the diversity of paths and opportunities available to individuals.
    • The phrase "of the married ones" indicates a focus on family and community, emphasizing the importance of relationships in achieving one's goals.
    • The call for the gods to "come" and "carry" signifies a request for divine support and blessings in one's endeavors.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, this mantra can be applied when seeking guidance in career choices. Just as a charioteer navigates through challenges, individuals can look for mentors or advisors to help steer their careers.
    • In personal relationships, the emphasis on family and community can remind individuals to nurture their connections, as these relationships provide support and strength in times of need.
    • During significant life events, such as weddings or new ventures, invoking divine blessings can serve as a reminder to seek guidance and support from a higher power.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the importance of seeking guidance, recognizing the diversity of paths in life, and valuing relationships.
    • Its significance lies in the reminder that, like a charioteer, we must navigate our lives with intention and seek support from both the divine and our community.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra encourages individuals to embrace opportunities, foster relationships, and remain open to divine guidance in their journeys.
