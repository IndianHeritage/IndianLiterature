मन्त्र: असृग्रम् इन्दवः पथā धर्मन् ऋतस्य सुश्रियः | विदाना अस्य योजनम् ||
asṛgram indavaḥ pathā dharmann ṛtasya suśriyaḥ | vidānā asya yojanam ||
  • asṛgram: This term can be interpreted as "the essence" or "the core." It signifies the fundamental nature of something.
  • indavaḥ: This word refers to "the moon" or "lunar." In Vedic literature, the moon is often associated with nourishment, fertility, and the passage of time.
  • pathā: Meaning "path" or "way," this term suggests a journey or a method of achieving something.
  • dharmann: This word translates to "righteousness" or "duty." It embodies the concept of living in accordance with moral and ethical principles.
  • ṛtasya: This term means "truth" or "cosmic order." It represents the universal laws that govern existence and the importance of aligning with them.
  • suśriyaḥ: This can be understood as "well-known" or "renowned." It implies that the path of righteousness and truth is recognized and respected.
  • vidānā: This word means "knowledge" or "understanding." It emphasizes the importance of wisdom in navigating life's challenges.
  • asya: This translates to "of this" or "of it," referring to the subject matter at hand.
  • yojanam: Meaning "connection" or "joining," this term suggests the act of bringing together different elements or ideas.

  • Meaning of the Mantra: The mantra speaks to the essence of the moon as a guiding light on the path of righteousness and truth. It emphasizes the importance of knowledge and understanding in connecting with the cosmic order.
  • Real-Life Example: In our daily lives, we often face moral dilemmas where we must choose between right and wrong. For instance, in a workplace scenario, an employee may witness unethical behavior. The mantra encourages individuals to seek knowledge and act in accordance with dharma (righteousness) to address such situations.

  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call to align oneself with the principles of truth and righteousness. It serves as a reminder that knowledge and understanding are essential in navigating life's complexities. By following this path, individuals can contribute to a harmonious society and foster personal growth.
  • Application in Real Life: This mantra can be applied in various aspects of life, such as decision-making, ethical leadership, and personal development. It encourages individuals to reflect on their actions and strive for a deeper understanding of their responsibilities within the larger cosmic order.
