संविव्याना ओजसा शवोभिर इन्द्र मजनना |तष्टेवा वृक्षं वनिनो नि वृक्षसि परश्वेवा नि वृक्षसि ||
saṁvivyāna ojasā śavobhir indra majmanā | taṣṭeva vṛkṣaṁ vanino ni vṛścasi paraśveva ni vṛścasi ||
  • saṁvivyāna ojasā: This phrase can be translated as "gathering strength" or "unifying energy." In real life, this can be seen in teamwork or community efforts where individuals come together to achieve a common goal, such as organizing a charity event or a community clean-up.
  • śavobhir indra majmanā: Here, "Indra" refers to the king of gods, symbolizing power and authority. "Majmanā" can be interpreted as "in the assembly" or "in the gathering." This suggests that when individuals unite with strength, they can invoke the support of higher powers or authorities. For example, in a corporate setting, when employees present a united front to management, they can influence decisions more effectively.
  • taṣṭeva vṛkṣaṁ vanino: This part translates to "like a tree in the forest." Trees symbolize stability, growth, and nourishment. In a real-life context, this can represent the importance of having strong roots in one’s community or family, much like a tree that provides shelter and sustenance to various forms of life around it.
  • ni vṛścasi paraśveva: This phrase can be interpreted as "you do not fall like a tree." It emphasizes resilience and strength in the face of challenges. In everyday life, this can be seen in individuals who overcome adversity, such as a student who perseveres through academic challenges or an athlete who trains hard despite setbacks.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of unity, strength, and resilience. It teaches us that when individuals come together with a common purpose, they can invoke greater power and support, much like a tree that stands tall and provides for its surroundings. This principle is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from community initiatives to personal challenges, reminding us of the strength found in collaboration and the importance of staying grounded and resilient in our endeavors.
