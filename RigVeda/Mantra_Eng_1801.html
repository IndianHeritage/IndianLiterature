परā शुभ्रā अयासो यव्यā साधारण्येव मरुतो मिमिक्षुः |न रोदसी अप नुदन्त घोरा जुषन्त वृद्धं सख्याय देवाः ||
parā śubhrā ayāso yavyā sādhāraṇyeva maruto mimikṣuḥ | na rodasī apa nudanta ghorā juṣanta vṛdhaṁ sakhyāya devāḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • parā: supreme, beyond
    • śubhrā: pure, bright
    • ayāso: without effort, effortless
    • yavyā: those who are capable
    • sādhāraṇyeva: like the common, universal
    • maruto: the Maruts, storm deities
    • mimikṣuḥ: who are seeking
    • na: not
    • rodasī: the two worlds, the heavens and the earth
    • apa: away, apart
    • nudanta: driving away, dispelling
    • ghorā: terrible, fierce
    • juṣanta: enjoying, delighting
    • vṛdhaṁ: the aged, the wise
    • sakhyāya: for friendship, for companionship
    • devāḥ: the gods
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the supreme and pure forces that operate effortlessly in the universe.
    • The Maruts, representing the storm and winds, are depicted as seeking something beyond the ordinary.
    • It emphasizes the idea that the divine forces do not shy away from the challenges of the world.
    • The phrase "na rodasī apa nudanta ghorā" suggests that these divine beings dispel the fierce and terrible aspects of existence.
    • Ultimately, it highlights the importance of companionship and friendship among the divine, which can be mirrored in human relationships.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In times of personal crisis, individuals often seek the support of friends and family, reflecting the divine companionship mentioned in the mantra.
    • The Maruts can be likened to the forces of nature that, while fierce, also bring renewal and change, similar to how challenges in life can lead to personal growth.
    • Effortlessness in achieving goals can be seen in moments of inspiration where ideas flow naturally, akin to the divine influence described.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the interplay between divine forces and human experiences, emphasizing the importance of friendship and support in overcoming life's challenges.
    • It serves as a reminder that even in the face of adversity, there are greater forces at work that can help dispel negativity and foster growth.
    • In real life, it encourages individuals to seek companionship and support, recognizing that we are not alone in our struggles.
