व्यास्मे अधि शर्म तत् पक्षाः वयो न यन्तना। विश्वानि विश्ववेदसो वरूथ्या मनामहे 'नेहसो व ऊतयः सूतयः व ऊतयः॥
vy a1sme adhi śarma tat pakṣā vayo na yantana | viśvāni viśvavedaso varūthyā manāmahe 'nehaso va ūtayaḥ suūtayo va ūtayaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • vy a1sme: "We are" or "We are indeed."
    • adhi: "Above" or "In addition to."
    • śarma: "Comfort," "shelter," or "protection."
    • tat: "That."
    • pakṣā: "Wings" or "aspects."
    • vayo: "Of the ages" or "of the life."
    • na: "Not."
    • yantana: "Moving" or "going."
    • viśvāni: "All" or "universal."
    • viśvavedaso: "Of all knowledge" or "of universal wisdom."
    • varūthyā: "With protection" or "with care."
    • manāmahe: "We meditate" or "we contemplate."
    • 'nehaso: "Of the light" or "of the brilliance."
    • va: "And."
    • ūtayaḥ: "Those who rise" or "those who elevate."
    • suūtayo: "Well-formed" or "well-structured."
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra expresses a collective aspiration for protection and enlightenment.
    • It emphasizes the importance of seeking shelter and comfort in a universal sense, suggesting that we are all interconnected.
    • The reference to "wings" can symbolize freedom and the ability to rise above challenges.
    • By invoking "universal wisdom," the mantra encourages individuals to seek knowledge and understanding in their lives.
    • The phrase "we meditate" indicates a communal effort towards achieving a higher state of consciousness.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In times of crisis, communities often come together to provide support and protection, reflecting the mantra's call for shelter and comfort.
    • Educational institutions promote the pursuit of knowledge, embodying the idea of "universal wisdom" and encouraging students to rise above challenges.
    • Environmental movements advocate for the protection of our planet, aligning with the mantra's emphasis on care and protection.
    • Mindfulness practices, such as meditation, foster a sense of community and collective well-being, resonating with the mantra's call for contemplation.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of community, knowledge, and protection in our lives.
    • It encourages individuals to seek shelter in each other and to rise together towards enlightenment and understanding.
    • In a world filled with challenges, the teachings of this mantra can guide us towards unity, wisdom, and resilience.
