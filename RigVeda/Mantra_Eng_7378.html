मन्त्र: क्रत्वा इति पूर्णम् उदरं तुरस्यास्ति विधतः |वृत्रघ्नः सोमपावनः ||
kratva it pūrṇam udaraṁ turasyāsti vidhataḥ | vṛtraghnaḥ somapāvnaḥ ||
  • kratva: This term refers to "action" or "deed." It emphasizes the importance of purposeful actions in life.
  • it: This word translates to "thus" or "therefore," indicating a conclusion or a result of the preceding thought.
  • pūrṇam: Meaning "full" or "complete," it signifies wholeness and fulfillment in various aspects of life.
  • udaraṁ: This translates to "belly" or "stomach," symbolizing nourishment and sustenance, both physically and spiritually.
  • turasyāsti: This phrase can be interpreted as "there exists a path" or "there is a way," suggesting the presence of guidance or direction in life.
  • vidhataḥ: Meaning "of the creator" or "of the divine," it refers to the higher power that governs the universe and our lives.
  • vṛtraghnaḥ: This term means "the slayer of Vritra," where Vritra symbolizes obstacles or challenges. It represents the overcoming of difficulties.
  • somapāvnaḥ: This translates to "purified by the soma," where soma is a sacred drink in Vedic tradition, symbolizing spiritual enlightenment and rejuvenation.

The mantra can be interpreted as a call to engage in meaningful actions that lead to a sense of completeness and fulfillment. It suggests that there is a divine path available to us, guided by a higher power, which helps us overcome obstacles in our lives. The reference to being "purified by the soma" indicates that through spiritual practices and rituals, one can attain clarity and strength to face life's challenges.


In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts:

  • Personal Growth: Engaging in purposeful actions, such as setting goals and working towards them, can lead to a sense of fulfillment.
  • Overcoming Challenges: When faced with difficulties, remembering that there is a divine path can provide comfort and motivation to persevere.
  • Spiritual Practices: Incorporating rituals or meditative practices can help in achieving mental clarity and emotional balance.

In conclusion, this mantra emphasizes the significance of purposeful action, the guidance of a higher power, and the importance of spiritual practices in overcoming life's challenges. It serves as a reminder that through our deeds, we can achieve completeness and navigate the complexities of existence with grace and strength.
