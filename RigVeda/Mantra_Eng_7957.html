त्वया वीरैण वीरवोऽभि ष्यामा पृथन्यतः |क्षराणोऽभि वार्यम् ||
tvayā vīreṇa vīravo 'bhi ṣyāma pṛtanyataḥ | kṣarā ṇo abhi vāryam ||
  • tvayā: This word translates to "by you" or "through you." It signifies a connection or reliance on a higher power or force.
  • vīreṇa: This means "with valor" or "with strength." It emphasizes the importance of courage and bravery in facing challenges.
  • vīravo: This term refers to "heroes" or "valiant ones." It highlights the qualities of those who are courageous and noble.
  • 'bhiṣyāma: This translates to "we shall be protected" or "we shall be safeguarded." It conveys a sense of seeking protection and security.
  • pṛtanyataḥ: This means "from all directions" or "from every side." It indicates a comprehensive approach to safety and protection.
  • kṣarā: This word means "perishable" or "decaying." It reflects the transient nature of life and the material world.
  • ṇo: This translates to "our" or "of us," indicating a collective identity or community.
  • abhi: This means "towards" or "against," suggesting a proactive stance in facing adversities.
  • vāryam: This translates to "to be warded off" or "to be repelled." It emphasizes the need to defend against negative forces or influences.

The mantra can be interpreted as a plea for strength and protection from a higher power, invoking the qualities of valor and heroism. It emphasizes the importance of community and collective strength in facing challenges and adversities. The acknowledgment of the transient nature of life serves as a reminder to focus on what truly matters and to seek protection from negative influences.


In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as:

  • Personal Challenges: When facing difficulties in life, individuals can invoke this mantra to seek inner strength and courage to overcome obstacles.
  • Community Support: In times of crisis, communities can come together, embodying the spirit of the mantra to protect and support one another.
  • Leadership: Leaders can draw inspiration from the qualities of valor and heroism, guiding their teams with strength and resilience.

In conclusion, this mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the importance of courage, community, and the need for protection against life's challenges. It encourages individuals and groups to seek strength from a higher power while fostering a sense of unity and collective resilience.
