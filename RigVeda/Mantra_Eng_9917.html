साकं यक्ष्म प्र पत चाशेण किकिदीविना |साकं वातस्य ध्राज्या साकं नश्य निहाकया ||
sākaṁ yakṣma pra pata cāṣeṇa kikidīvinā | sākaṁ vātasya dhrājyā sākaṁ naśya nihākayā ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sākaṁ: Together, in unity
    • yakṣma: Illness, disease
    • pra pata: To fall away, to be removed
    • cāṣeṇa: By the power, by the strength
    • kikidīvinā: With the divine, celestial
    • vātasya: Of the wind, of the air
    • dhrājyā: With strength, with support
    • naśya: Destruction, loss
    • nihākayā: By the power of the divine
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of unity and collective strength in overcoming challenges, particularly illness and adversity.
    • The phrase "sākaṁ yakṣma" suggests that together, we can combat diseases and challenges that life presents.
    • The invocation of divine power ("kikidīvinā") indicates that spiritual support is essential in our struggles.
    • The reference to "vātasya dhrājyā" highlights the role of natural forces, like the wind, which can symbolize change and the need for adaptability in facing difficulties.
    • Overall, the mantra calls for a collective effort, supported by divine strength, to overcome obstacles and ensure well-being.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community facing a health crisis, such as an outbreak of disease, the mantra's message of unity can inspire collective action, where individuals come together to support one another, share resources, and promote health.
    • In a workplace setting, when teams face project challenges, the idea of "sākaṁ" can encourage collaboration and teamwork, leading to innovative solutions and shared success.
    • During personal hardships, such as loss or grief, seeking support from friends and family aligns with the mantra's emphasis on collective strength and divine guidance.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its reminder of the power of unity and the importance of seeking divine support in overcoming life's challenges.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from health crises to personal struggles, emphasizing that together, with spiritual backing, we can navigate difficulties more effectively.
    • Ultimately, this mantra serves as a call to action for individuals and communities to come together, fostering resilience and strength in the face of adversity.
