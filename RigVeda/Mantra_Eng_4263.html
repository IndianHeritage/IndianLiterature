सत्यं इद् वा उ अश्विनौ युवाम् आहुर मयोभुवा |तā यामन् यामहूतमा यामन् न मृळयत्तमा ||
satyam id vā u aśvinā yuvām āhur mayobhuvā | tā yāman yāmahūtamā yāmann ā mṛḻayattamā ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • satyam: Truth
    • id: This
    • vā: Or
    • u: And
    • aśvinā: O Ashvins (the twin deities)
    • yuvām: You two
    • āhur: They have said
    • mayobhuvā: With our minds
    • tā: Those
    • yāman: Who are the best
    • yāmahūtamā: The most excellent
    • yāmann: Who are not
    • ā: To
    • mṛḻayattamā: The most soothing
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks to the essence of truth and the divine qualities of the Ashvins, who are often associated with health, medicine, and the dawn.
    • The phrase "satyam id vā" emphasizes the importance of truth in our lives, suggesting that truth is a guiding principle.
    • The invocation of the Ashvins indicates a call for their blessings, particularly in the pursuit of knowledge and well-being.
    • The mantra suggests that those who embody truth and excellence are to be revered and sought after.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, adhering to the principle of truth can lead to trust and integrity within a team, fostering a positive work environment.
    • In healthcare, the Ashvins symbolize the importance of seeking the best medical practices and treatments, highlighting the need for truth in diagnosis and care.
    • In personal relationships, valuing truth can strengthen bonds and create a foundation of trust and respect.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the significance of truth in all aspects of life.
    • It encourages individuals to seek excellence and soothing qualities in their pursuits, whether in health, relationships, or professional endeavors.
    • By invoking the Ashvins, it highlights the importance of divine guidance in achieving these ideals.
    • Ultimately, the mantra teaches that living in truth leads to a harmonious and fulfilling life.
