विष्पर्धसो नरां न शंसैर अस्माकासद इन्द्रो वज्रहस्तः |मित्रायुवो न पूर्पतिं सुशिष्टौ मध्यायुव उप शिक्षन्ति यज्ञैः ||
viṣpardhaso narāṁ na śaṁsair asmākāsad indro vajrahastaḥ | mitrāyuvo na pūrpatiṁ suśiṣṭau madhyāyuva upa śikṣanti yajñaiḥ ||
  • viṣpardhaso narāṁ: This phrase can be translated as "to the men who are full of vigor." It emphasizes the importance of strength and vitality in human beings.
  • na śaṁsair: This means "without any blame." It suggests that those who are vigorous should not be criticized or blamed for their actions.
  • asmākāsad: This translates to "we have." It indicates a sense of possession or belonging to a community or group.
  • indro vajrahastaḥ: "Indra with the thunderbolt in his hand." Indra is a significant deity in Vedic literature, representing power, strength, and leadership.
  • mitrāyuvo: This means "friends who are youthful." It highlights the importance of companionship and youthful energy in achieving goals.
  • na pūrpatiṁ: This translates to "without any obstacles." It signifies the need for a clear path to success.
  • suśiṣṭau: This means "well-instructed." It emphasizes the value of knowledge and guidance in one's endeavors.
  • madhyāyuva: This translates to "those who are in the middle of their lives." It refers to individuals who are at a crucial stage in their life journey.
  • upa śikṣanti yajñaiḥ: This means "they are taught through sacrifices." It indicates that learning and growth come through dedication and effort.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda emphasizes the significance of strength, community, and guidance in achieving success. It highlights that individuals who are vigorous and well-instructed, along with their friends, can overcome obstacles and learn through their efforts. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios such as teamwork in professional settings, where collaboration and mutual support lead to success. It also underscores the importance of mentorship and continuous learning, suggesting that individuals should seek knowledge and guidance to navigate challenges effectively. Overall, the mantra serves as a reminder of the power of unity, strength, and wisdom in the pursuit of goals.
