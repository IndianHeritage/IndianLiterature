स मृज्यते सुखर्मभिर देवो देवभ्यः सुतः |विदे यद आसु संददिर महीर अपो विगाहते ||
sa mṛjyate sukarmabhir devo devebhyaḥ sutaḥ | vide yad āsu saṁdadir mahīr apo vi gāhate ||
  • sa mṛjyate: This phrase can be translated as "he is purified" or "he is cleansed." In a real-life context, this can refer to the process of self-improvement and personal growth, where individuals strive to cleanse themselves of negative thoughts and actions.
  • sukarmabhir: This means "by good deeds" or "through virtuous actions." It emphasizes the importance of performing righteous actions in our daily lives. For example, volunteering for community service or helping those in need can be seen as sukarmabhir.
  • devo devebhyaḥ sutaḥ: This translates to "the divine is the offspring of the gods." It signifies the connection between the divine and humanity, suggesting that we are all part of a greater cosmic order. In real life, this can be reflected in the way we treat each other with respect and kindness, recognizing the divine spark in everyone.
  • vide yad āsu: This phrase means "wherever it is." It implies a sense of universality and inclusiveness. In practical terms, it can refer to the idea that good deeds and divine qualities should be spread across all areas of life, transcending boundaries and differences.
  • saṁdadir: This can be interpreted as "he who is established" or "he who is firm." This suggests the importance of stability and strength in one's character. For instance, a leader who stands firm in their values and principles can inspire others to do the same.
  • mahīr apo vi gāhate: This translates to "the great waters flow." This can symbolize the flow of life and the interconnectedness of all beings. In real life, it can be seen in the way communities come together to support each other, much like rivers merging to form a larger body of water.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of purification through good deeds, the divine connection among all beings, and the importance of stability and interconnectedness in life. It encourages individuals to engage in virtuous actions, recognize the divine in others, and contribute to the greater good of society. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied through community service, fostering respect and kindness, and standing firm in one's values to create a harmonious and supportive environment for all.
