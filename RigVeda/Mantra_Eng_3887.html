त्यं चिद अस्य क्रतुभिर निःशत्तम अमर्मणो विदद इद अस्य मरम |यद ईं सुख्षत्र प्रभृतā मदस्य युयुत्सन्तं तमसि हार्म्ये धाह् ||
tyaṁ cid asya kratubhir niṣattam amarmaṇo vidad id asya marma | yad īṁ sukṣatra prabhṛtā madasya yuyutsantaṁ tamasi harmye dhāḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • tyaṁ: that
    • cid: indeed
    • asya: of this
    • kratubhir: by the rituals or intentions
    • niṣattam: most powerful
    • amarmaṇo: of the secret or hidden
    • vidad: may know
    • id: this
    • asya: of this
    • marma: essence or core
    • yad: which
    • īṁ: the one who is
    • sukṣatra: well-placed or auspicious
    • prabhṛtā: endowed with
    • madasya: of the intoxication or bliss
    • yuyutsantaṁ: striving or fighting
    • tamasi: in darkness
    • harmye: in the abode or home
    • dhāḥ: may dwell or reside
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the power of intention and the hidden essence of existence.
    • It suggests that through rituals and focused intentions, one can uncover deeper truths and knowledge.
    • The phrase "yuyutsantaṁ tamasi" indicates the struggle against ignorance or darkness, emphasizing the importance of striving for enlightenment.
    • The mention of "sukṣatra" implies that when one is aligned with auspicious energies, they can achieve their goals more effectively.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In personal development, setting clear intentions and goals can lead to greater clarity and success, much like the rituals mentioned in the mantra.
    • In a corporate environment, teams that focus on a shared vision and work collaboratively can overcome challenges, akin to "yuyutsantaṁ" in the face of adversity.
    • In spiritual practices, meditation and mindfulness can help individuals uncover their inner truths, resonating with the idea of discovering the "marma" or essence of life.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of intention, knowledge, and the pursuit of enlightenment.
    • It is applicable in various aspects of life, including personal growth, teamwork, and spiritual practices.
    • By understanding and applying the principles within this mantra, individuals can navigate through challenges and strive for a more meaningful existence.
