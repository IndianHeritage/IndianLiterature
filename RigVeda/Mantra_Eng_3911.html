सम ईं पाणेर अजति भोजने मुषे विदाशुशे भजति सूनरं वसु |दुर्गे चन ध्रियते विश्वा आपुरु जनो यो अस्य तविषीम अचुक्रुधत ||
sam īm paṇer ajati bhojanam muṣe vi dāśuṣe bhajati sūnaraṁ vasu | durge cana dhriyate viśva ā puru jano yo asya taviṣīm acukrudhat ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sam: together, in harmony
    • īm: to the goal, towards the aim
    • paṇer: of the wealth, of the resources
    • ajati: does not move, does not waver
    • bhojanam: food, sustenance
    • muṣe: for the one who is wise
    • vi: in, within
    • dāśuṣe: for the one who gives
    • bhajati: worships, honors
    • sūnaraṁ: beautiful, auspicious
    • vasu: wealth, prosperity
    • durge: in difficult times, in challenges
    • cana: indeed, certainly
    • dhriyate: is held, is maintained
    • viśva: all, universal
    • ā: towards, in the direction of
    • puru: many, numerous
    • jano: people, beings
    • yo: who
    • asya: of this, of that
    • taviṣīm: strength, power
    • acukrudhat: did not become angry, did not lose control
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of unity and harmony in achieving goals.
    • It suggests that true wealth and sustenance come from wise actions and generosity.
    • In challenging times, maintaining composure and strength is crucial for the collective well-being of society.
    • It highlights the significance of not losing control or becoming angry, which can disrupt harmony.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace, when teams collaborate harmoniously towards a common goal, they achieve greater success.
    • During community service, individuals who contribute selflessly create a positive impact, reflecting the essence of giving.
    • In personal relationships, maintaining calm during conflicts leads to stronger bonds and understanding.
    • In times of crisis, such as natural disasters, communities that come together and support each other demonstrate the power of unity.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the significance of unity, wisdom, and composure in our lives.
    • It is applicable in various scenarios, from personal relationships to professional environments and community interactions.
    • By embodying these principles, individuals can contribute to a more harmonious and prosperous society.
