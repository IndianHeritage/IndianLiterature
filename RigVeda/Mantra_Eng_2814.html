निषीमिदात्रगुह्यादधाना उता क्षत्राय रोडसी समञ्जन |सम्मात्राभिर्ममिरे येमुरुर्वी अन्तरमहि समृतिधायसे धुः ||
ni ṣīm id atra guhyā dadhānā uta kṣatrāya rodasī sam añjan | sam mātrābhir mamire yemur urvī antar mahī samṛte dhāyase dhuḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ni ṣīm: to be free from fear or danger
    • id: here
    • atra: in this place
    • guhyā: secret or hidden
    • dadhānā: holding or possessing
    • uta: and
    • kṣatrāya: for the warrior or protector
    • rodasī: the two worlds or realms
    • sam añjan: equally or together
    • sam mātrābhir: with equal measures
    • mamire: they have attained
    • yemur: those who have come
    • urvī: the earth
    • antar: within
    • mahī: the great or vast
    • samṛte: remembered or invoked
    • dhāyase: you are running or moving
    • dhuḥ: to be free or liberated
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of a state of fearlessness and protection.
    • It emphasizes the importance of holding onto hidden truths and wisdom.
    • The reference to the two worlds suggests a connection between the physical and spiritual realms.
    • It highlights the idea of equality and balance in life, urging individuals to seek harmony.
    • The invocation of the earth and the great vastness signifies grounding oneself in reality while pursuing higher truths.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In times of crisis, such as natural disasters, communities often come together, embodying the mantra's call for fearlessness and unity.
    • In personal development, individuals may seek hidden knowledge or wisdom through meditation or study, reflecting the mantra's emphasis on understanding deeper truths.
    • In leadership, the idea of protecting and serving others resonates with the role of a leader as a guardian of their community.
    • Environmental movements often invoke the earth's vastness, reminding us of our responsibility to protect it while seeking balance in our actions.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of fearlessness, wisdom, and balance in our lives.
    • It encourages individuals to seek hidden truths and to protect and serve their communities.
    • In a world filled with challenges, the teachings of this mantra can guide us towards a more harmonious and grounded existence.
    • Ultimately, it signifies the interconnectedness of all beings and the importance of striving for unity and understanding.
