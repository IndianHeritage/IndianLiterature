मन्त्र: अनेनो वो मरुतो यामो अस्त्व अनश्वश् चिद यम् अजत्य अरथीः | अनवसो अनभिषू रजस्तूर् वि रोडसी पथ्या याती साधन ||
aneno vo maruto yāmo astv anaśvaś cid yam ajaty arathīḥ | anavaso anabhīśū rajastūr vi rodasī pathyā yāti sādhan ||
  • Mantra: "aneno vo maruto yāmo astv anaśvaś cid yam ajaty arathīḥ | anavaso anabhīśū rajastūr vi rodasī pathyā yāti sādhan ||"
  • Translation: This mantra can be translated as: "May we, with the help of the Maruts (the storm deities), move forward without obstacles, and may we be free from fear and confusion as we traverse the paths of life."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • Maruto: Refers to the Maruts, the storm gods associated with strength and vitality.
    • Yāmo: Means to move or progress.
    • Anaśvaś: Indicates freedom from obstacles or hindrances.
    • Cid: Refers to consciousness or awareness.
    • Ajaty: Means to be free from fear or confusion.
    • Arathīḥ: Refers to the paths or journeys we undertake in life.
    • Anavaso: Signifies being untroubled or free from worries.
    • Anabhīśū: Means to be fearless or without anxiety.
    • Rajastūr: Refers to the energies or forces that guide us.
    • Rodasī: Indicates the vastness of the universe or the paths we travel.
    • Pathyā: Means the way or the path.
    • Sādhan: Refers to the means or methods we use to achieve our goals.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, when facing challenges at work, invoking the spirit of the Maruts can symbolize seeking strength and support from colleagues to overcome obstacles.
    • During personal struggles, such as dealing with anxiety or fear, this mantra can serve as a reminder to seek clarity and courage, allowing one to navigate through life's uncertainties.
    • In community gatherings, this mantra can be recited to foster a sense of unity and collective strength, encouraging individuals to support each other in their journeys.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for strength, clarity, and fearlessness in the face of life's challenges. It emphasizes the importance of community support and the need to remain aware and conscious as we navigate our paths. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as personal growth, professional challenges, and community building, reminding us that with the right mindset and support, we can overcome obstacles and achieve our goals.
