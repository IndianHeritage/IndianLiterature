साह्वान विश्वा अभियुजः क्रतुर देवानाम अमृतः |अग्निस तुविश्रवस्तमः ||
sāhvān viśvā abhiyujaḥ kratur devānām amṛktaḥ | agnis tuviśravastamaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sāhvān: Together, united
    • viśvā: All, universal
    • abhiyujaḥ: Striving, aspiring
    • kratur: Intention, will
    • devānām: Of the gods
    • amṛktaḥ: Immortal, eternal
    • agnis: Fire
    • tuviśravastamaḥ: Most renowned, glorious
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of unity and collective aspiration.
    • The phrase "sāhvān viśvā" suggests that when individuals come together with a common purpose, they can achieve great things.
    • "Kratur devānām amṛktaḥ" indicates that the intentions and will of the divine are eternal and should guide human actions.
    • The mention of "agnis tuviśravastamaḥ" highlights the significance of fire, which symbolizes transformation, purity, and the divine presence in various cultures.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace, when team members unite with a shared goal, they can innovate and overcome challenges more effectively.
    • In community service, collective efforts can lead to significant improvements in society, such as organizing food drives or environmental clean-ups.
    • In spiritual practices, the concept of fire is often used in rituals to symbolize the burning away of negativity and the welcoming of positive energy.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the power of unity and shared intentions in achieving greatness.
    • It encourages individuals to align their personal goals with a higher purpose, fostering a sense of community and collaboration.
    • In real life, applying the principles of this mantra can lead to transformative experiences, both personally and collectively, as we strive for a better world.
