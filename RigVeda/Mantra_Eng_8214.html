यदद्भिः परिषिच्यसे मृज्यमानो गभस्त्योः |द्रुणा सदस्थम् अश्नुषे ||
yad adbhiḥ pariṣicyase mṛjyamāno gabhastyoḥ | druṇā sadhastham aśnuṣe ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yad: whatever
    • adbhiḥ: with waters
    • pariṣicyase: you are being sprinkled
    • mṛjyamāno: being purified
    • gabhastyoḥ: of the luminous ones
    • druṇā: with the support
    • sadhastham: in the assembly
    • aśnuṣe: you may eat
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the act of purification and the blessings that come from the divine or luminous beings.
    • The phrase "yad adbhiḥ pariṣicyase" suggests a ritualistic sprinkling of water, which is often associated with cleansing and sanctification in various cultures.
    • The term "mṛjyamāno" indicates a process of purification, which can be seen in real-life practices such as bathing in holy rivers or performing rituals to cleanse oneself of negativity.
    • "Gabhastyoḥ" refers to the luminous beings, which can be interpreted as divine entities or enlightened individuals who guide and support us.
    • The phrase "druṇā sadhastham aśnuṣe" implies that with the support of these divine beings, one can partake in the abundance of life, symbolizing nourishment and sustenance.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In many cultures, water is considered a purifying element. For instance, in Hinduism, taking a dip in the Ganges River is believed to wash away sins and purify the soul.
    • In modern practices, meditation and mindfulness can be seen as forms of purification, where individuals seek to cleanse their minds and spirits from negativity.
    • Communal gatherings, such as family meals or community feasts, can be seen as a way of sharing abundance and support, reflecting the idea of "aśnuṣe" where one partakes in the blessings of life.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of purification and the role of divine support in our lives.
    • It teaches us that through rituals, whether they be physical or mental, we can cleanse ourselves and connect with higher energies.
    • In real life, this can be applied through practices that promote mental clarity, emotional healing, and community bonding, reminding us of the interconnectedness of all beings.
