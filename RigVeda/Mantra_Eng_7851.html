मन्त्र: परि यो रोधसी उभे सद्यो वाजेभिर अर्षति |मदेषु सर्वधाऽसि ||
pari yo rodasī ubhe sadyo vājebhir arṣati | madeṣu sarvadhā asi ||
  • Mantra: "pari yo rodasī ubhe sadyo vājebhir arṣati | madeṣu sarvadhā asi ||"
  • Translation: This mantra can be translated as "He who, in the two worlds, is swiftly stirred by the steeds; you are everywhere in the intoxicating drinks."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • pari: around, in all directions
    • yo: who
    • rodasī: the two worlds (often interpreted as the physical and spiritual realms)
    • ubhe: both
    • sadyo: swiftly, immediately
    • vājebhir: by the steeds (often symbolizing energy or power)
    • arṣati: is stirred, is moved
    • madeṣu: in the intoxicating drinks (symbolizing joy or ecstasy)
    • sarvadhā: everywhere, in all ways
    • asi: you are
  • Contextual Explanation:
    • This mantra reflects the interconnectedness of the physical and spiritual realms, suggesting that divine energy is present in all aspects of life.
    • The "two worlds" can be seen as the material world we live in and the spiritual world that influences our existence.
    • The "steeds" symbolize the forces that drive us, such as ambition, passion, and creativity, which can be harnessed to achieve our goals.
    • The mention of "intoxicating drinks" can be interpreted as the joys and pleasures of life, which can elevate our spirits and connect us to the divine.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, when a team works together harmoniously, they can achieve great results, akin to the "steeds" working in unison.
    • During celebrations, people often indulge in drinks that bring joy and foster connections, reflecting the essence of "madeṣu."
    • In moments of meditation or reflection, individuals can tap into the spiritual realm, finding peace and clarity, which aligns with the idea of being present in both worlds.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of recognizing the divine presence in all aspects of life, encouraging individuals to find balance between the material and spiritual realms.
    • It serves as a reminder to harness our inner energies and passions to create joy and fulfillment in our lives.
    • In practical terms, it can inspire us to seek harmony in our actions, relationships, and pursuits, ultimately leading to a more enriched and meaningful existence.
