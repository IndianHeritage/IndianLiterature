आ विबाध्याऽपरिरापस तमांसि च ज्योतिष्मन्तं रथम ऋतस्य तिष्ठसि | बृहस्पते भीमम् अमित्रदम्भनं रक्षोहनं गोत्रभिदं स्वरविदम् ||
ā vibādhyā parirāpas tamāṁsi ca jyotiṣmantaṁ ratham ṛtasya tiṣṭhasi | bṛhaspate bhīmam amitradambhanaṁ rakṣohaṇaṁ gotrabhidaṁ svarvidam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā vibādhyā: "May we not be harmed" - a plea for protection and safety.
    • parirāpas: "Surrounding" - signifies the need for a protective shield.
    • tamāṁsi: "Darkness" - represents ignorance or negativity.
    • ca: "And" - a conjunction linking ideas.
    • jyotiṣmantaṁ: "Illuminated" - symbolizes knowledge and enlightenment.
    • ratham: "Chariot" - a metaphor for the journey of life.
    • ṛtasya: "Of truth" - signifies the importance of living in accordance with truth.
    • tiṣṭhasi: "You stand" - indicates stability and strength.
    • bṛhaspate: "O Bṛhaspati" - a reference to the deity of wisdom and eloquence.
    • bhīmam: "Fearsome" - denotes the power and might of the divine.
    • amitradambhanaṁ: "Destroyer of enemies" - signifies protection from adversaries.
    • rakṣohaṇaṁ: "Protector" - emphasizes the role of divine guardianship.
    • gotrabhidaṁ: "Divider of clans" - refers to the ability to discern and protect one's lineage.
    • svarvidam: "Knowledge of the heavens" - symbolizes spiritual wisdom and enlightenment.
  • Significance:
    • This mantra is a prayer for protection and guidance from negative forces and ignorance.
    • It emphasizes the importance of truth and wisdom in navigating life's challenges.
    • The invocation of Bṛhaspati highlights the need for divine assistance in overcoming obstacles.
  • Real-Life Application:
    • In daily life, individuals can recite this mantra during times of uncertainty or fear, seeking protection and clarity.
    • It can be used in rituals or meditative practices to foster a sense of security and enlightenment.
    • In a broader context, communities can adopt the principles of this mantra to promote unity, wisdom, and resilience against adversities.
  • Conclusion:
    • The mantra serves as a reminder of the power of divine protection and the importance of seeking knowledge and truth.
    • By embodying the principles of this mantra, individuals can cultivate a life of strength, wisdom, and harmony.
    • Ultimately, it encourages a proactive approach to overcoming challenges and fostering a supportive community.
