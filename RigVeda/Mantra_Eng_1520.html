ते त्वा मन्दन्तु दावने महे चित्राय राधसे |गीर्भिर गिर्वाहः स्तवमान आ गहि सुमृळीको न आ गहि ||
te tvā mandantu dāvane mahe citrāya rādhase | gīrbhir girvāhaḥ stavamāna ā gahi sumṛḻīko na ā gahi ||
  • te tvā mandantu dāvane mahe citrāya rādhase
    • te - "you" or "to you"
    • tvā - "you" (accusative case)
    • mandantu - "may they praise" or "may they honor"
    • dāvane - "in the act of giving" or "in the act of sacrifice"
    • mahe - "great" or "mighty"
    • citrāya - "to the one who is colorful" or "to the one who is diverse"
    • rādhase - "to the one who is worthy of praise" or "to the one who is revered"
  • gīrbhir girvāhaḥ stavamāna ā gahi sumṛḻīko na ā gahi
    • gīrbhir - "with hymns" or "with songs"
    • girvāhaḥ - "the one who is praised" or "the one who is celebrated"
    • stavamāna - "while being praised" or "while being honored"
    • ā gahi - "come to us" or "approach us"
    • sumṛḻīko - "the one who is benevolent" or "the one who is kind"
    • na ā gahi - "do not come" or "do not approach"

This mantra can be interpreted as a call for divine presence and blessings. The speaker is invoking a higher power, asking for praise and recognition in the context of sacrifice and offerings. The use of the word "citrāya" suggests a multifaceted nature of the divine, indicating that the divine can manifest in various forms and colors, much like the diversity found in nature.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as:

  • Rituals and Ceremonies: During religious ceremonies, individuals often seek blessings from the divine. This mantra serves as a reminder to honor and praise the divine presence in our lives.
  • Community Gatherings: In community events, invoking collective praise can foster unity and a sense of belonging, much like the mantra calls for collective recognition of the divine.
  • Personal Reflection: Individuals can use this mantra during meditation or personal reflection to connect with their inner selves and seek guidance from a higher power.

Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its ability to connect individuals with the divine through praise and recognition. It emphasizes the importance of honoring the multifaceted nature of the divine and encourages a sense of community and personal reflection. In a world where individuals often seek meaning and connection, this mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the divine presence in our lives and the importance of gratitude and reverence.
