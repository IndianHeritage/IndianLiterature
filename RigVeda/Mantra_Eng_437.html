घनेव विष्वग् वि जह्य अराव्णस् तपुर्जम्भ यो अस्मध्रुक् |यो मर्त्यः शिशीते अत्यक्तुभिर् मा नः स रिपुर् ईशत ||
ghaneva viṣvag vi jahy arāvṇas tapurjambha yo asmadhruk | yo martyaḥ śiśīte aty aktubhir mā naḥ sa ripur īśata ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ghaneva: like a dense cloud
    • viṣvag: all-pervading
    • vi: may
    • jahy: may we overcome
    • arāvṇas: the enemies
    • tapurjambha: the one who is powerful
    • yo: who
    • asmadhruk: our protector
    • yo: who
    • martyaḥ: mortal
    • śiśīte: may he shine
    • aty: exceedingly
    • aktubhir: with strength
    • mā: may not
    • naḥ: us
    • sa: he
    • ripur: the enemy
    • īśata: may he rule
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes a powerful force, likening it to a dense cloud that encompasses everything.
    • It expresses a desire to overcome all enemies and obstacles in life.
    • The mantra emphasizes the importance of a protector who can shield us from adversities.
    • It acknowledges the mortal condition and seeks strength to shine brightly despite challenges.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a competitive work environment, this mantra can be a source of motivation to overcome challenges and rivalries.
    • During personal struggles, such as health issues or emotional turmoil, one can recite this mantra to seek strength and protection.
    • In community settings, it can be used to foster unity and resilience against external threats or conflicts.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the strength that lies within us and the importance of seeking protection from higher powers.
    • It is applicable in various life scenarios, encouraging individuals to face adversities with courage and resilience.
    • By invoking this mantra, one can cultivate a mindset of overcoming obstacles and shining brightly in the face of challenges.
