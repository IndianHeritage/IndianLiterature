मन्त्र: कद व ऋतस्य धार्णसि कद वरुणस्य चक्षणम् | कद आर्यम्णो महस पथाति क्रामेम दूद्यो वित्तम् मे अस्य रोडसी ||
kad va ṛtasya dharṇasi kad varuṇasya cakṣaṇam | kad aryamṇo mahas pathāti krāmema dūḍhyo vittam me asya rodasī ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • kad: when
    • va: or
    • ṛtasya: of truth or order
    • dharṇasi: you uphold
    • varuṇasya: of Varuna (the god of cosmic order)
    • cakṣaṇam: vision or sight
    • aryamṇo: of the noble or the worthy
    • mahas: great or vast
    • pathāti: leads or guides
    • krāmema: may we walk or proceed
    • dūḍhyo: with strength or vigor
    • vittam: wealth or resources
    • me: my
    • asya: of this
    • rodasī: the two worlds or realms
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer seeking guidance and strength from divine forces.
    • It emphasizes the importance of truth and cosmic order, represented by Varuna.
    • The speaker expresses a desire to walk the path of righteousness and prosperity.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be applied when making ethical decisions, ensuring that actions align with truth and integrity.
    • In personal life, it can serve as a reminder to seek clarity and guidance when faced with moral dilemmas.
    • In community leadership, it encourages leaders to uphold justice and fairness, fostering a harmonious environment.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for alignment with universal truths and ethical principles.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal growth to professional integrity.
    • By invoking this mantra, individuals can cultivate a sense of purpose and direction, ensuring that their actions contribute positively to the world around them.
