अप्रतीतो जयति सं धनानि प्रतिजन्याण्य उता याऽसजन्याः |अवश्यवे यो वरिवः कृत्नोति ब्रह्मणे राजा तं अवन्ति देवाः ||
apratīto jayati saṁ dhanāni pratijanyāny uta yā sajanyā | avasyave yo varivaḥ kṛṇoti brahmaṇe rājā tam avanti devāḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • apratīto: Unseen or unknown
    • jayati: Triumphs or prevails
    • saṁ: Together or with
    • dhanāni: Wealth or riches
    • pratijanyāny: Those that are produced or generated
    • uta: And or also
    • yā: Which or that
    • sajanyā: Well-produced or well-generated
    • avasyave: To the one who is destined or inevitable
    • yo: Who
    • varivaḥ: The best or the most excellent
    • kṛṇoti: Creates or makes
    • brahmaṇe: For the divine or the ultimate reality
    • rājā: King or ruler
    • tam: That
    • avanti: Protect or support
    • devāḥ: The gods or divine beings
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the unseen forces that govern success and wealth.
    • It emphasizes that true wealth is not just material but also spiritual and ethical.
    • The phrase "apratīto jayati" suggests that even what is not visible can lead to triumph.
    • It highlights the importance of intention and effort in generating wealth and prosperity.
    • The reference to "brahmaṇe rājā" indicates that the ultimate ruler or king is the divine, and those who align with this divine will be supported by the gods.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • A business that operates ethically and with integrity may not always see immediate profits, but over time, it builds a reputation that leads to sustained success.
    • Individuals who invest in their personal development and education may not see immediate results, but their unseen efforts often lead to greater opportunities in the future.
    • Communities that come together to support one another, even in difficult times, often find that their collective strength leads to resilience and prosperity.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder that success and wealth are not solely dependent on visible actions but also on unseen efforts and intentions.
    • It encourages individuals to align their actions with higher principles and values, trusting that divine support will follow.
    • In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as in business ethics, personal growth, and community support, emphasizing the importance of integrity and intention in achieving true success.
