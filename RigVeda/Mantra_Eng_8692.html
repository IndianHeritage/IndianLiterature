अस्मभ्यं गातुवित्तमो देवभ्यो मधुमत्तमः |सहस्रं याहि पथिभिः कनिक्रदत ||
asmabhyaṁ gātuvittamo devebhyo madhumattamaḥ | sahasraṁ yāhi pathibhiḥ kanikradat ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • asmabhyaṁ: for us
    • gātuvittamo: the best of singers or poets
    • devebhyo: from the gods
    • madhumattamaḥ: the sweetest or most delightful
    • sahasraṁ: a thousand
    • yāhi: come
    • pathibhiḥ: by the paths
    • kanikradat: who is full of joy or happiness
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra expresses a prayer or invocation for the best of singers or poets to come to us, bringing the sweetest offerings from the gods.
    • The mention of "sahasraṁ" (a thousand) signifies abundance, suggesting that we seek not just a single blessing but a multitude of divine gifts.
    • The term "pathibhiḥ" indicates the various paths or ways through which these blessings may arrive, emphasizing the importance of being open to different opportunities and experiences.
    • "Kanikradat" reflects a state of joy, suggesting that the blessings we seek should not only be material but also bring happiness and fulfillment.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a personal context, this mantra can be applied when one is seeking inspiration or creativity, such as a writer looking for new ideas or a musician seeking to compose a beautiful piece.
    • In a community setting, it can represent the collective desire for prosperity and joy, where a group comes together to celebrate achievements and share resources.
    • In a spiritual context, it can be a reminder to remain open to the blessings that come from various sources, whether they be divine, natural, or through human connections.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of seeking divine inspiration and joy in our lives. It encourages us to be receptive to the abundance of blessings that can come from various paths.
    • In real life, it serves as a reminder to cultivate creativity, foster community, and embrace happiness, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling existence.
    • By invoking this mantra, individuals can align themselves with positive energies and open their hearts to the myriad of possibilities that life has to offer.
