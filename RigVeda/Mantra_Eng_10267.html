युवं ह्य अप्नराजाव असीदतं तिष्ठद रथं न धूर्षदं वनर्षदं |तां नः कणूकयन्तीर नृमेधस तत्र एंहसः सुमेधस तत्र एंहसः ||
yuvaṁ hy apnarājāv asīdataṁ tiṣṭhad rathaṁ na dhūrṣadaṁ vanarṣadam | tā naḥ kaṇūkayantīr nṛmedhas tatre aṁhasaḥ sumedhas tatre aṁhasaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yuvaṁ: young, youthful
    • hy: indeed
    • apnarājāv: of the rulers or kings
    • asīdataṁ: may they be protected
    • tiṣṭhad: standing, remaining
    • rathaṁ: chariot
    • na: not
    • dhūrṣadaṁ: to be disturbed
    • vanarṣadam: the forest-dwelling
    • tā: those
    • naḥ: for us
    • kaṇūkayantīr: may they bring prosperity
    • nṛmedhas: of the human mind
    • tatre: there
    • aṁhasaḥ: of the light, brilliance
    • sumedhas: of good intellect
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks to the youth and their potential as leaders.
    • It emphasizes the importance of protection and stability for those in power.
    • The chariot symbolizes the journey of life and the challenges faced along the way.
    • The mention of not being disturbed suggests the need for focus and clarity in decision-making.
    • It calls for prosperity and wisdom, highlighting the role of intellect in achieving success.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern leadership, young leaders often face challenges that require them to remain steadfast and focused, much like the chariot that must not be disturbed.
    • Organizations that prioritize the development of young talent can create a stable environment that fosters innovation and growth.
    • In personal life, individuals can relate to this mantra by seeking wisdom and clarity in their decisions, ensuring they remain on the right path.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the potential of youth and the importance of nurturing their abilities.
    • It highlights the significance of stability, wisdom, and prosperity in leadership and personal growth.
    • In real life, applying the principles of this mantra can lead to better decision-making, enhanced leadership qualities, and a more prosperous life.
