त्वं अग्नि प्रयतदक्षिणं नरं वर्मेव स्युतं परि पाशि विश्वतः |स्वादुक्षद्मा यो वसतौ स्योनकृत् जीवयाजं यजते सोपमा दिवः ||
tvam agne prayatadakṣiṇaṁ naraṁ varmeva syūtam pari pāsi viśvataḥ | svādukṣadmā yo vasatau syonakṛj jīvayājaṁ yajate sopamā divaḥ ||
  • tvam agne: This phrase translates to "You, O Agni." Agni is the Vedic fire god, symbolizing energy, transformation, and the divine messenger between humans and the gods.
  • prayatadakṣiṇaṁ naraṁ: This means "the one who is devoted and skilled." It refers to a person who is dedicated and capable, embodying qualities of diligence and expertise.
  • varmeva syūtam: This translates to "may you be clothed in warmth." It signifies the importance of protection and comfort, akin to being wrapped in a warm embrace.
  • pari pāsi viśvataḥ: This means "protect from all sides." It emphasizes the need for comprehensive protection and security in life, akin to a guardian watching over someone.
  • svādukṣadmā: This translates to "the one who is sweet and pleasant." It refers to the qualities of kindness and gentleness, suggesting that one should embody these traits.
  • yo vasatau syonakṛj: This means "who dwells in the abode of the divine." It signifies a connection to the spiritual realm, suggesting that one should strive for a higher purpose.
  • jīvayājaṁ yajate: This translates to "who performs the sacrifice for life." It highlights the importance of selfless actions and contributions to the well-being of others.
  • sopamā divaḥ: This means "like the divine." It suggests that one's actions and qualities should aspire to the divine, aiming for a higher moral and ethical standard.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda emphasizes the importance of devotion, protection, and selfless service. In real life, it can be applied in various scenarios such as leadership, where a leader must protect and guide their team (pari pāsi viśvataḥ), or in personal relationships, where kindness and warmth (svādukṣadmā) are essential for nurturing bonds. The mantra serves as a reminder to embody qualities that uplift oneself and others, striving for a life of purpose and connection to the divine. It encourages individuals to act with integrity and compassion, ultimately contributing to a harmonious society.
