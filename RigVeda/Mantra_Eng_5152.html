मां नो अग्ने 'वीरते परा दा दुर्वाससे 'मतये मां नो अस्यै |मां नः क्षुधे मां रक्षसा ऋतावो मां नो दमे मां वन आ जुहूर्तः ||
mā no agne 'vīrate parā dā durvāsase 'mataye mā no asyai | mā naḥ kṣudhe mā rakṣasa ṛtāvo mā no dame mā vana ā juhūrthāḥ ||
  • mā no agne 'vīrate parā dā durvāsase 'mataye mā no asyai
  • mā naḥ kṣudhe mā rakṣasa ṛtāvo mā no dame mā vana ā juhūrthāḥ

  • Translation: This mantra is a prayer to Agni, the fire god, asking for protection and guidance. It expresses a desire for the removal of obstacles and negative influences, while seeking blessings for prosperity and well-being.
  • Word-by-word Meaning:
    • mā no: May it not be for us
    • agne: O Agni (the fire god)
    • 'vīrate: to be restrained or held back
    • parā: supreme or beyond
    • dā: give or grant
    • durvāsase: to the one who is difficult to approach or harmful
    • 'mataye: for our understanding or wisdom
    • mā no asyai: may it not be for us (in terms of) misfortune
    • mā naḥ: may it not be for us
    • kṣudhe: in hunger or need
    • mā rakṣasa: may it not be from demons or evil forces
    • ṛtāvo: in accordance with truth or righteousness
    • mā no dame: may it not be in our home or dwelling
    • mā vana: may it not be in the forest or wilderness
    • ā juhūrthāḥ: may we not be consumed or harmed

  • Real-life Examples:
    • In times of personal crisis, individuals often seek spiritual guidance or protection, similar to how this mantra seeks the blessings of Agni to ward off negativity.
    • In a community setting, people may come together to perform rituals or prayers to invoke divine protection against calamities, reflecting the collective desire for safety and prosperity.

  • Conclusion: This mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking divine protection and wisdom in our lives. It serves as a reminder to be aware of the negative influences around us and to strive for a life aligned with truth and righteousness. In practical terms, it can be applied in daily life through mindfulness practices, community support, and spiritual rituals that foster a sense of safety and well-being.
