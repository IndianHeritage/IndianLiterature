आ नो गन्तं ऋषादसेमं स्तोमं पुरुभुजा |कृतं नः सुश्रियो नरेमा दातम अभिष्टये ||
ā no gantaṁ riśādasemaṁ stomam purubhujā | kṛtaṁ naḥ suśriyo naremā dātam abhiṣṭaye ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā no: "May we" - a call for collective well-being.
    • gantaṁ: "to the goal" - signifies a destination or purpose.
    • riśādasemaṁ: "to the one who is strong" - refers to a powerful entity or force.
    • stomam: "praise" - an expression of reverence or admiration.
    • purubhujā: "who enjoys many" - indicates abundance or richness.
    • kṛtaṁ: "done" - signifies actions taken or efforts made.
    • naḥ: "for us" - a plea for personal or communal benefit.
    • suśriyo: "good fame" - refers to a positive reputation or honor.
    • naremā: "to the man" - signifies humanity or the human condition.
    • dātam: "may be given" - a request for bestowal or granting.
    • abhiṣṭaye: "for our desires" - indicates aspirations or wishes.
  • Overall Meaning:
    • This mantra is a prayer for guidance and support towards achieving our goals.
    • It emphasizes the importance of collective effort and the strength derived from unity.
    • The invocation of a powerful entity suggests seeking divine assistance in our endeavors.
    • It highlights the significance of good reputation and honor in our pursuits.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, teams often come together to achieve a common goal, invoking the spirit of collaboration and support.
    • In personal life, individuals may seek blessings or guidance from a higher power when embarking on significant life changes, such as marriage or starting a new job.
    • Communities often unite for social causes, aiming for a better reputation and positive impact, reflecting the essence of this mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of unity, strength, and the pursuit of honorable goals.
    • It encourages individuals and communities to seek divine guidance in their endeavors, fostering a sense of purpose and direction.
    • In real life, it can be applied in various contexts, from personal aspirations to collective efforts in society, reinforcing the idea that together we can achieve greater heights.
