प्रजापते न त्वद एतान्य अन्यो विश्वा जातानि परि ता बभूव |यत्कामास ते जुहुमुस तन् नो अस्तु वयं स्याम पतयो रयीणाम ||
prajāpate na tvad etāny anyo viśvā jātāni pari tā babhūva | yatkāmās te juhumas tan no astu vayaṁ syāma patayo rayīṇām ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • prajāpate: O Lord of Creatures
    • na: not
    • tvad: your
    • etāny: these
    • anyo: other
    • viśvā: all
    • jātāni: born
    • pari: around
    • tā: that
    • babhūva: have become
    • yat: whatever
    • kāmās: desires
    • te: your
    • juhumas: we offer
    • tan: that
    • no: to us
    • astu: may it be
    • vayaṁ: we
    • syāma: may become
    • patayo: lords
    • rayīṇām: of wealth
  • Overall Meaning:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Prajāpati, the Lord of Creatures, acknowledging that all beings have originated from Him.
    • It emphasizes the unity of all creation and the importance of seeking blessings for prosperity and fulfillment of desires.
    • The mantra expresses a desire for divine grace, asking that whatever we offer in devotion may lead us to become lords of wealth and abundance.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, this mantra can be invoked during meetings or decision-making processes to foster unity and collaboration among team members, recognizing that everyone contributes to the collective goal.
    • In personal life, individuals may recite this mantra during times of need or when pursuing goals, seeking divine support to achieve their aspirations and enhance their well-being.
    • During community gatherings or rituals, this mantra can be chanted to promote harmony and shared prosperity, reminding participants of their interconnectedness.
  • Conclusion and Significance:
    • The mantra highlights the significance of recognizing a higher power in the creation and sustenance of life.
    • It serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of all beings and the importance of collective well-being.
    • In practical terms, it encourages individuals and communities to seek blessings for prosperity while fostering unity and cooperation in their endeavors.
    • Ultimately, this mantra can be a source of inspiration and motivation, guiding individuals towards a path of abundance and fulfillment in their lives.
