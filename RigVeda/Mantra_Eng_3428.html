प्र ते बभ्रू विचक्षण शंसामि गोषाणो नपात् |माभ्यां गा अनु शिश्रथः ||
pra te babhrū vicakṣaṇa śaṁsāmi goṣaṇo napāt | mābhyāṁ gā anu śiśrathaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • pra: for you
    • te: your
    • babhrū: I speak
    • vicakṣaṇa: wise or knowledgeable
    • śaṁsāmi: I praise or commend
    • goṣaṇo: of the cattle or herds
    • napāt: offspring or progeny
    • mābhyāṁ: from us
    • gā: the song or hymn
    • anu: following
    • śiśrathaḥ: may you prosper or thrive
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer or invocation directed towards a divine entity, expressing gratitude and seeking blessings.
    • The speaker acknowledges the wisdom of the divine and praises the abundance of cattle, which symbolizes wealth and prosperity in ancient agrarian societies.
    • The phrase "may you prosper" indicates a wish for growth and success, not just for oneself but for the community as a whole.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In agricultural communities, cattle are often seen as a sign of wealth. This mantra can be related to farmers who pray for a good harvest and healthy livestock, ensuring their livelihood.
    • In modern contexts, this mantra can be applied to businesses seeking growth and prosperity. Just as the speaker wishes for the flourishing of cattle, entrepreneurs may seek blessings for their ventures to thrive.
    • In personal life, individuals may use this mantra to invoke wisdom and guidance in their decisions, hoping to make choices that lead to prosperity and well-being.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its emphasis on wisdom, prosperity, and community well-being.
    • It serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of individuals and their environment, highlighting the importance of seeking knowledge and blessings for collective growth.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire individuals and communities to strive for success while remaining grounded in wisdom and gratitude.
