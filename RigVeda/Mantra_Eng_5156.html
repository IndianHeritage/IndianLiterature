स मार्तो अग्नि स्वनीक रेवान अमर्त्ये य आजुहोति हव्यं |स देवता वसुवनिं दधाति यं सूरीर अर्थी पृच्छमाना एति ||
sa marto agne svanīka revān amartye ya ājuhoti havyam | sa devatā vasuvaniṁ dadhāti yaṁ sūrir arthī pṛcchamāna eti ||
  • sa marto agne: This phrase can be translated as "that mortal, O Agni." Here, "Agni" refers to the fire god, a significant deity in Vedic literature, symbolizing energy, transformation, and the divine presence in rituals.
  • svanīka revān: This means "who is the one who calls." It suggests a connection to the divine through invocation, emphasizing the importance of communication with the divine forces.
  • amartye ya ājuhoti havyam: This translates to "the immortal one who offers the oblation." It highlights the act of offering, which is central to Vedic rituals, where offerings are made to please the gods and seek their blessings.
  • sa devatā vasuvaniṁ dadhāti: This means "that deity bestows wealth." It signifies the blessings of prosperity and abundance that come from the divine, reinforcing the idea that spiritual practices can lead to material and spiritual wealth.
  • yaṁ sūrir arthī pṛcchamāna eti: This translates to "the wise one who seeks knowledge." It emphasizes the pursuit of wisdom and understanding, suggesting that true wealth lies in knowledge and enlightenment.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda encapsulates the essence of seeking divine blessings through offerings and the importance of wisdom in achieving prosperity. In real life, it can be applied in various scenarios, such as:

  • In personal rituals or prayers, individuals can invoke divine energy for guidance and support in their endeavors.
  • In community gatherings, the mantra can serve as a reminder of the collective pursuit of knowledge and prosperity, encouraging collaboration and sharing of resources.
  • In educational settings, it can inspire students to seek knowledge and wisdom as the true form of wealth, rather than material possessions.

Overall, this mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the interconnectedness of the spiritual and material worlds, urging individuals to seek divine guidance while pursuing knowledge and prosperity in their lives.
