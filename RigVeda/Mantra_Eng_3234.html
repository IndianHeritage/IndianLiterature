भुवोऽवितावामदेवस्य धीनाṁ भुवः सखावृको वाजसातौ |त्वामनु प्रमतिमाऽजगन्मोरुशंसो जरीत्रे विश्वध स्याः ||
bhuvo 'vitā vāmadevasya dhīnām bhuvaḥ sakhāvṛko vājasātau | tvām anu pramatim ā jaganmoruśaṁso jaritre viśvadha syāḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • bhuvo: of the world
    • vitā: to be free or liberated
    • vāmadevasya: of the Vāmadeva (a name associated with the divine)
    • dhīnām: of those who are wise or knowledgeable
    • bhuvaḥ: of the earth or existence
    • sakhāvṛko: the friend or companion
    • vājasātau: in the context of strength or power
    • tvām: you
    • anu: following or in accordance with
    • pramatim: wisdom or understanding
    • ā: towards
    • jagan: the universe or the world
    • moruśaṁso: of the one who is powerful or mighty
    • jaritre: in the context of creation or birth
    • viśvadha: all-encompassing or universal
    • syāḥ: may be or may exist
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks to the interconnectedness of wisdom, strength, and the divine.
    • It emphasizes the importance of being liberated and knowledgeable in navigating the world.
    • The reference to Vāmadeva suggests a connection to a higher power or divine wisdom that guides individuals.
    • The idea of companionship and strength indicates that we are not alone in our journey; we have allies and support.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, this mantra can be applied to teamwork. Just as the mantra speaks of companionship, successful projects often rely on collaboration and shared wisdom.
    • In personal development, seeking knowledge and understanding can lead to liberation from ignorance, much like the mantra suggests.
    • In times of crisis, turning to a higher power or seeking guidance from wise mentors can provide strength and clarity, reflecting the essence of this mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for wisdom, strength, and divine guidance in our lives.
    • It encourages individuals to seek knowledge and support from others, fostering a sense of community and shared purpose.
    • In real-life scenarios, applying the principles of this mantra can lead to personal growth, successful collaborations, and a deeper understanding of our place in the universe.
