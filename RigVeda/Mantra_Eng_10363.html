त्वं शर्धाय महिना गृणाना उरु कृधि मगवञ्च चग्धि रायः |त्वं नो मित्रो वरुणो न मायī पित्वो न दसम दयसे विभक्ताः ||
tvaṁ śardhāya mahinā gṛṇāna uru kṛdhi maghavañ chagdhi rāyaḥ | tvaṁ no mitro varuṇo na māyī pitvo na dasma dayase vibhaktā ||
  • tvaṁ - You
  • śardhāya - With devotion
  • mahinā - With greatness
  • gṛṇāna - Singing
  • uru - Vast
  • kṛdhi - Create
  • maghavañ - O Mighty One
  • chagdhi - Bestow
  • rāyaḥ - Wealth
  • no - Our
  • mitro - Friend
  • varuṇo - Varuna (the god of cosmic order)
  • na - Not
  • māyī - Deceptive
  • pitvo - O Lord
  • na - Not
  • dasma - From us
  • dayase - Show mercy
  • vibhaktā - Divided

This mantra is a prayer to the divine, invoking the qualities of greatness, friendship, and mercy. The speaker addresses a powerful deity, asking for the creation of abundance and wealth while emphasizing the importance of devotion and sincerity.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios:

  • Devotion in Daily Life: Just as the mantra begins with a call for devotion, individuals can incorporate this into their daily routines by practicing gratitude and mindfulness, recognizing the greatness in their lives.
  • Seeking Abundance: The request for wealth and prosperity can be seen in the context of personal and professional growth. For instance, entrepreneurs may recite this mantra to attract success and opportunities.
  • Friendship and Support: The invocation of Mitra (friend) and Varuna (cosmic order) highlights the importance of relationships. In real life, this can translate to fostering supportive networks and communities that uplift one another.
  • Overcoming Deception: The mention of "not being deceptive" serves as a reminder to maintain integrity in all dealings, whether personal or professional. This can be particularly relevant in business ethics and personal relationships.

Conclusion: This mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking divine support in achieving greatness, fostering relationships, and maintaining integrity. Its significance lies in its universal applicability, encouraging individuals to cultivate devotion, seek abundance, and build meaningful connections while navigating the complexities of life. By embodying these principles, one can create a harmonious and prosperous existence.
