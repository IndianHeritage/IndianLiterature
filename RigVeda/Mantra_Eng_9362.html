महिम्ना एषां पितरश्चनेशिरे देवाः देवेष्व् अदधुर अपि क्रतुम् |सम अविव्यचुर उता यानि अत्विषुर ऐषां तनूषु न विविशुः पुनः ||
mahimna eṣām pitaraś caneśire devā deveṣv adadhur api kratum | sam avivyacur uta yāny atviṣur aiṣāṁ tanūṣu ni viviśuḥ punaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • mahimna: of greatness
    • eṣām: of these
    • pitaraś: fathers
    • caneśire: in the divine realm
    • devā: the gods
    • deveṣv: among the deities
    • adadhur: established
    • api: even
    • kratum: intention or will
    • sam: together
    • avivyacur: did not separate
    • uta: or
    • yāny: whatever
    • atviṣur: those who are wise
    • aiṣāṁ: of these
    • tanūṣu: in the bodies
    • ni: into
    • viviśuḥ: entered
    • punaḥ: again
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the greatness of the divine fathers (ancestors) and how the gods have established their intentions among the deities.
    • It emphasizes the unity and collective will of the divine beings, suggesting that they do not separate their intentions.
    • The wise individuals are acknowledged for their understanding and connection to the divine, indicating that they embody the essence of the divine will within their own bodies.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a family, the values and teachings passed down from ancestors can be seen as a guiding force, similar to how the divine fathers influence the gods.
    • In teamwork, the collective intention of a group can lead to successful outcomes, reflecting the unity mentioned in the mantra.
    • Individuals who seek wisdom and understanding often find themselves embodying the qualities of their ancestors, much like the wise individuals in the mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of ancestral wisdom and the collective will of the divine.
    • It teaches us the importance of unity, intention, and the connection between the divine and the wise in our lives.
    • In real-life scenarios, it encourages us to honor our roots, work together towards common goals, and strive for wisdom in our actions.
