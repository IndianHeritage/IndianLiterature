ऋषभं मा समानानां sapatnānāṁ विषासहिम् |हन्तारं शत्रूणां कृधि विराजं गोपतिं गवाम् ||
ṛṣabham mā samānānāṁ sapatnānāṁ viṣāsahim | hantāraṁ śatrūṇāṁ kṛdhi virājaṁ gopatiṁ gavām ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ṛṣabham: The best or the strongest.
    • mā: Not or do not.
    • samānānāṁ: Of those who are equal or similar.
    • sapatnānāṁ: Of the rivals or enemies.
    • viṣāsahim: The one who is trustworthy or reliable.
    • hantāraṁ: The destroyer or the one who eliminates.
    • śatrūṇāṁ: Of the enemies.
    • kṛdhi: Make or create.
    • virājaṁ: The one who shines or is glorious.
    • gopatiṁ: The lord of the cows or protector.
    • gavām: Of the cows.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the strongest and most reliable protector, asking for the elimination of rivals and enemies.
    • The term "ṛṣabham" signifies the highest form of strength and excellence, suggesting that one should strive for the best qualities in life.
    • By addressing the "gopatiṁ," it emphasizes the importance of protection and guardianship, akin to how a shepherd cares for their flock.
    • The mantra can be seen as a plea for divine assistance in overcoming obstacles and adversaries, much like a student seeking guidance during challenging exams.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a competitive work environment, this mantra can be a reminder to seek the support of mentors who can help navigate challenges and rivalries.
    • In personal relationships, it can serve as a call to eliminate negativity and foster trust and reliability among friends and family.
    • During times of conflict, such as in community disputes, invoking this mantra can symbolize the desire for peace and resolution, much like mediators work to resolve issues.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of seeking strength and protection in the face of challenges.
    • It encourages individuals to strive for excellence and to surround themselves with trustworthy allies.
    • In real-life scenarios, it serves as a reminder of the importance of support systems, whether in personal, professional, or communal contexts.
    • Ultimately, it emphasizes the need for resilience and the pursuit of harmony in overcoming adversities.
