सिषक्ति सा वां सुमतिश्च चनिष्ठातापि घर्मो मनुषो दुरोणे |यो वां समुद्रान सरितः पिपर्त्य एतग्वा चिन न सुयुजा युजानाḥ ||
siṣakti sā vāṁ sumatiś caniṣṭhātāpi gharmo manuṣo duroṇe | yo vāṁ samudrān saritaḥ piparty etagvā cin na suyujā yujānaḥ ||
  • siṣakti sā vāṁ: This phrase can be interpreted as "the power that is within you." It emphasizes the inherent strength and potential that every individual possesses. For example, consider a student preparing for an important exam. The student may feel overwhelmed, but by recognizing their inner strength and capabilities, they can approach their studies with confidence and determination.
  • sumatiś caniṣṭhātāpi: This translates to "good thoughts and the ability to act." It highlights the importance of having a positive mindset and the capacity to take action. In real life, a person who maintains a positive attitude in the face of challenges, such as a job seeker facing multiple rejections, can eventually find success by continuing to apply and improve their skills.
  • gharmo manuṣo duroṇe: This can be understood as "the warmth of humanity in difficult times." It suggests that human connection and compassion are vital, especially during hardships. For instance, during a natural disaster, communities often come together to support one another, showcasing the warmth and resilience of humanity.
  • yo vāṁ samudrān saritaḥ piparty: This phrase means "who nourishes you like rivers and oceans." It symbolizes the abundance and sustenance provided by nature. In practical terms, this can relate to how clean water sources and fertile land are essential for agriculture, which in turn supports communities and economies.
  • etagvā cin na suyujā yujānaḥ: This translates to "those who are not united in purpose will not thrive." It emphasizes the importance of unity and collaboration. In a workplace setting, teams that work together harmoniously and share common goals are more likely to succeed than those that operate in silos.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of inner strength, positive thinking, human compassion, the nurturing aspect of nature, and the significance of unity. In real life, these principles can guide individuals in personal development, community building, and collaborative efforts. By recognizing our inner power, maintaining a positive mindset, supporting one another, valuing nature's resources, and working together, we can create a more harmonious and prosperous society.
