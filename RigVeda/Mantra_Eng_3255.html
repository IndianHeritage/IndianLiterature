सखीयताम् अविता बोधि सखा गृणान इन्द्र स्तुवते वयो धाः |वयं ह्य ā ते चक्रमा सबाधा आबिः शमीभिर महयन्त इन्द्र ||
sakhīyatām avitā bodhi sakhā gṛṇāna indra stuvate vayo dhāḥ | vayaṁ hy ā te cakṛmā sabādha ābhiḥ śamībhir mahayanta indra ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sakhīyatām: May we be friends or companions.
    • avitā: Protect or safeguard.
    • bodhi: Wisdom or enlightenment.
    • sakhā: Friend or ally.
    • gṛṇāna: Singing or praising.
    • indra: A deity, often associated with strength and power.
    • stuvate: To praise or worship.
    • vayo: Our age or time.
    • dhāḥ: To hold or maintain.
    • vayaṁ: We or us.
    • hy: Indeed or certainly.
    • ā te: To you (Indra).
    • cakṛmā: We have made or created.
    • sabādha: With all our efforts or resources.
    • ābhiḥ: With or through.
    • śamībhir: With peace or tranquility.
    • mahayanta: Great or powerful.
    • indra: Again referring to the deity Indra.
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra expresses a desire for companionship and protection through wisdom.
    • It emphasizes the importance of friendship and collaboration in achieving strength and success.
    • Indra, as a powerful deity, symbolizes the strength that comes from unity and collective effort.
    • The invocation of Indra suggests seeking divine support in our endeavors.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace, teamwork and collaboration can lead to greater achievements, much like the mantra's call for companionship.
    • In personal relationships, fostering friendships based on mutual respect and support can lead to a more fulfilling life.
    • In community service, working together for a common cause can amplify the impact of individual efforts.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of friendship, wisdom, and collective strength.
    • It is applicable in various aspects of life, including personal relationships, professional environments, and community engagements.
    • By invoking the spirit of unity and seeking divine guidance, individuals can navigate challenges more effectively and achieve greater success.
