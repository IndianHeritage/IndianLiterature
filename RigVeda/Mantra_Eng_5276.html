वंस्वा विश्वा वार्याणि प्रचेतः सत्याः भवन्तु आशिषो नो अद्य ||
vaṁsva viśvā vāryāṇi pracetaḥ satyā bhavantv āśiṣo no adya ||
  • vaṁsva: This term can be interpreted as "belonging to the lineage" or "of the family." It signifies the importance of heritage and the values passed down through generations.
  • viśvā: Meaning "all" or "universal," this word emphasizes inclusivity and the interconnectedness of all beings and elements in the universe.
  • vāryāṇi: This translates to "the best" or "the most excellent." It suggests striving for the highest quality in thoughts, actions, and relationships.
  • pracetaḥ: This word means "wise" or "intelligent." It highlights the importance of wisdom and discernment in decision-making and understanding the world around us.
  • satyā: Meaning "truth," this term underscores the significance of honesty and integrity in all aspects of life.
  • bhavantv: This is a form of blessing, meaning "may you become" or "may you be." It expresses a wish for positive outcomes and growth.
  • āśiṣo: This word refers to "blessings" or "auspiciousness." It indicates the desire for favorable conditions and support in one's endeavors.
  • no: This means "our," indicating a collective aspect, emphasizing community and shared experiences.
  • adya: This translates to "today," signifying the present moment and the importance of acting in the now.

In summary, the mantra "vaṁsva viśvā vāryāṇi pracetaḥ satyā bhavantv āśiṣo no adya" encapsulates a profound message about the importance of lineage, universal connection, excellence, wisdom, truth, and blessings in our lives. It encourages individuals to recognize their heritage while striving for the best in their actions and thoughts, guided by wisdom and truth.


In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts:

  • Family Values: Emphasizing the importance of family heritage and values can guide individuals in making ethical decisions and maintaining strong relationships.
  • Community Engagement: Recognizing the interconnectedness of all people can foster a sense of community and encourage collaborative efforts for the greater good.
  • Personal Development: Striving for excellence and wisdom in personal and professional endeavors can lead to success and fulfillment.
  • Truthfulness: Upholding truth in communication and actions can build trust and integrity in relationships.

Ultimately, this mantra serves as a reminder to live with intention, honor our roots, and seek wisdom and truth in our daily lives, fostering a harmonious existence for ourselves and those around us.
