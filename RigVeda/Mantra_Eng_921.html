मन्त्र: य एक इद विदयते वसु मार्ताय दाशुषे |ईशानो अप्रतिष्कुत इन्द्रो अङ्ग ||
ya eka id vidayate vasu martāya dāśuṣe | īśāno apratiṣkuta indro aṅga ||
  • Mantra: "ya eka id vidayate vasu martāya dāśuṣe | īśāno apratiṣkuta indro aṅga ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ya: who
    • eka: one
    • id: here
    • vidayate: is known or revealed
    • vasu: wealth or good things
    • martāya: for the mortal or human
    • dāśuṣe: for the one who gives or bestows
    • īśāno: the lord or ruler
    • apratiṣkuta: unrestrained or unbounded
    • indro: Indra, the king of gods
    • aṅga: body or essence
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the singular essence of wealth and prosperity that is revealed to those who are mortal and generous.
    • It emphasizes the importance of being open-handed and giving, suggesting that true wealth is not just material but also in the act of sharing.
    • Indra, as the king of gods, represents power and authority, and the mantra suggests that he is unrestrained in his blessings to those who embody these qualities.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community, a person who shares their resources, whether it be time, money, or knowledge, often becomes a respected figure, much like Indra, who is revered for his generosity.
    • Philanthropy is a modern-day example where individuals or organizations give back to society, embodying the spirit of the mantra by bestowing wealth and resources to those in need.
    • In personal relationships, being generous with love and support can strengthen bonds and create a sense of community, reflecting the essence of the mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for generosity and the recognition that true wealth is found in giving.
    • It teaches that by being unrestrained in our kindness and support, we can embody the qualities of Indra and attract blessings into our lives.
    • This mantra is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal relationships to community involvement, reminding us that our actions can create a ripple effect of positivity and abundance.
