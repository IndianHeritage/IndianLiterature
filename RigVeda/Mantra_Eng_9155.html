प्र याः सिस्रते सूर्यस्य रश्मिभिर ज्योतिर्भरन्तीर उषसो व्युष्टिषु | भद्रā नो अद्य श्रवसे व्य उच्छत स्वस्त्य अग्निं समिधानम् ईमहे ||
pra yāḥ sisrate sūryasya raśmibhir jyotir bharantīr uṣaso vyuṣṭiṣu | bhadrā no adya śravase vy ucchata svasty a1gniṁ samidhānam īmahe ||
  • Translation: "Those who rise with the rays of the sun, bringing light, may they elevate our glory today. We invoke the auspicious Agni, the fire, to be kindled."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • pra yāḥ: Those who rise or come forth.
    • sisrate: To shine or illuminate.
    • sūryasya: Of the sun.
    • raśmibhir: With rays.
    • jyotir: Light or brightness.
    • bharantīr: Bringing or carrying.
    • uṣaso: Of the dawn.
    • vyuṣṭiṣu: In the mornings or at dawn.
    • bhadrā: Auspicious or beneficial.
    • no: Our.
    • adya: Today.
    • śravase: Glory or fame.
    • vy ucchata: May it rise or elevate.
    • svasty: Well-being or auspiciousness.
    • agniṁ: Fire.
    • samidhānam: To be kindled or ignited.
    • īmahe: We invoke or we call upon.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, the rising sun symbolizes new beginnings and opportunities. Just as the sun rises each day, individuals can embrace new challenges and strive for personal growth.
    • The invocation of Agni, or fire, can be seen in various cultures where fire is used in rituals to symbolize purification and transformation. For instance, in many Indian ceremonies, fire is central to the rituals, representing the divine and the energy that sustains life.
    • The idea of elevating one's glory can be related to personal achievements, such as pursuing education or career goals. Just as the mantra calls for elevation, individuals can work towards enhancing their skills and reputation in their respective fields.
  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of light, auspiciousness, and the invocation of divine energy for personal and communal well-being. It serves as a reminder to seek enlightenment and strive for growth in our lives. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied by embracing new opportunities, engaging in rituals that promote positivity, and working towards personal and collective goals. The significance of this mantra lies in its call for a harmonious and prosperous life, guided by the light of knowledge and the warmth of community.
