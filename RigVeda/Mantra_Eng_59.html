अतः परिजन्म्ना गहि दिवो वा रोचनाद अधि |सम अस्मिन्न ऋञ्जते गिरः ||
ataḥ parijmann ā gahi divo vā rocanād adhi | sam asminn ṛñjate giraḥ ||
  • ataḥ - This word translates to "therefore" or "hence," indicating a conclusion or a result derived from previous statements or observations.
  • parijmann - This term can be understood as "to be surrounded" or "to be enveloped," suggesting a sense of encompassing or being within a certain space or condition.
  • ā gahi - This phrase means "take hold" or "grasp," implying an action of seizing or understanding something deeply.
  • dīvo - This word refers to "heaven" or "sky," often symbolizing higher realms of existence or consciousness.
  • vā - This is a conjunction meaning "or," presenting an alternative or choice.
  • rocanād - This term translates to "from the light" or "from brightness," indicating a source of illumination or clarity.
  • adhi - This word means "above" or "over," suggesting a position of superiority or transcendence.
  • sam - This term means "together" or "in harmony," indicating unity or collective existence.
  • asminn - This word translates to "in this," referring to the present context or situation.
  • ṛñjate - This term means "to shine" or "to be radiant," suggesting a state of brilliance or clarity.
  • giraḥ - This word refers to "words" or "speech," indicating communication or expression.

The mantra can be interpreted as a call to grasp the essence of existence that is enveloped in the light of higher consciousness. It emphasizes the importance of understanding and articulating our experiences and insights in a harmonious manner.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios:

  • Personal Growth: Just as one must take hold of their experiences to grow, individuals can reflect on their life lessons and articulate them to gain clarity and wisdom.
  • Communication: The emphasis on words suggests the importance of effective communication in relationships. By expressing thoughts clearly and harmoniously, one can foster better understanding and connection with others.
  • Spiritual Awareness: The reference to light and higher realms encourages individuals to seek spiritual enlightenment and understanding, guiding them to live a more meaningful life.

Conclusion: This mantra signifies the importance of grasping the deeper truths of existence and expressing them in a way that promotes harmony and understanding. It serves as a reminder to seek clarity in our thoughts and communications, ultimately leading to personal and collective growth.
