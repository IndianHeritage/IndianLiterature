मन्त्र: सूर्यस्येव रश्मयो द्रावयित्नवो मत्सरासः प्रसुपः साकं ईरते |तन्तुं ततम् परि सर्गास आाशवो नेंद्राद ऋते पवते धाम किं चन ||
sūryasyeva raśmayo drāvayitnavo matsarāsaḥ prasupaḥ sākam īrate | tantuṁ tatam pari sargāsa āśavo nendrād ṛte pavate dhāma kiṁ cana ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sūryasya: of the sun
    • eva: indeed
    • raśmayo: rays
    • drāvayitnavo: those that bring forth or flow
    • matsarāsaḥ: free from envy
    • prasupaḥ: those who are awake or alert
    • sākam: together
    • īrate: they shine or radiate
    • tantuṁ: the web or fabric
    • tatam: spread out
    • pari: all around
    • sargāsa: of creation or beings
    • āśavo: swift or quick
    • nendrād: without the lord or ruler
    • ṛte: except
    • pavate: the wind or air
    • dhāma: abode or place
    • kiṁ cana: anything at all
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the sun's rays, which are essential for life, symbolizing enlightenment and knowledge.
    • The phrase "free from envy" suggests a state of purity and harmony, which is crucial for personal and communal well-being.
    • The idea of "together" emphasizes unity and collaboration, which are vital in any community or organization.
    • The mention of "swift" and "creation" indicates the dynamic nature of life and the importance of being proactive in our endeavors.
    • Finally, the reference to the wind and the abode signifies the omnipresence of nature and the interconnectedness of all beings.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace, fostering an environment free from envy can lead to better teamwork and productivity.
    • In personal relationships, being alert and aware of each other's needs can strengthen bonds and promote harmony.
    • In community service, working together towards a common goal can create a sense of belonging and purpose.
    • In environmental conservation, recognizing the interconnectedness of all life can inspire collective action to protect our planet.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of unity, awareness, and the importance of a harmonious existence.
    • It serves as a reminder to cultivate positive relationships, both personally and within the community.
    • In real life, applying these principles can lead to a more fulfilling and enriched life, fostering a sense of peace and collaboration.
