उप प्रागात् परमं यत् सदस्थम् अर्वाम् अच््छा पितरम् मातरं च |अद्य देवाञ्जुष्टतमो हि गम्याऽथ शास्ते दाशुषे वार्याणि ||
upa prāgāt paramaṁ yat sadhastham arvām̐ acchā pitaram mātaraṁ ca | adyā devāñ juṣṭatamo hi gamyā athā śāste dāśuṣe vāryāṇi ||
  • upa prāgāt paramaṁ: This phrase can be translated as "may we approach the highest." It signifies the aspiration to reach a higher state of being or consciousness.
  • yat sadhastham: This means "that which is established in the assembly." It refers to the collective wisdom or knowledge that is shared among people.
  • arvām̐ acchā: This translates to "the one who is all-encompassing." It suggests a universal presence or force that is nurturing and protective.
  • pitaram mātaraṁ ca: This means "both the father and the mother." It symbolizes the duality of parental figures, representing guidance, support, and love.
  • adyā devāñ juṣṭatamo hi gamyā: This can be interpreted as "today, the deities are most pleased." It indicates a time of divine favor and blessings.
  • athā śāste dāśuṣe vāryāṇi: This translates to "therefore, let us follow the commands of the divine." It emphasizes the importance of adhering to spiritual guidance and moral principles.

  • Significance: This mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking higher knowledge and understanding through collective wisdom, while also acknowledging the nurturing aspects of parental figures. It highlights the importance of divine favor and the necessity of following spiritual guidance.
  • Real-life Application: In everyday life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios such as:
    • In educational settings, where students strive to reach their highest potential by learning from teachers and peers.
    • In family dynamics, where the roles of parents are crucial in providing support and guidance to their children.
    • In community gatherings, where collective wisdom is shared to solve problems and foster unity.
    • In moments of decision-making, where individuals seek divine guidance to ensure their choices align with moral and ethical standards.

  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of knowledge, parental guidance, and divine influence in our lives. By aspiring to reach higher states of consciousness and adhering to spiritual principles, individuals can navigate their lives with purpose and integrity.
