उभयं ते न क्षीयते वसव्यं दिवे-दिवे जायमानस्य दसम | कृधि क्षुमन्तं जरीतारम अग्ने कृधि पतिं स्वपत्यस्य रायः ||
ubhayaṁ te na kṣīyate vasavyaṁ dive-dive jāyamānasya dasma | kṛdhi kṣumantaṁ jaritāram agne kṛdhi patiṁ svapatyasya rāyaḥ ||
  • Ubhayaṁ te na kṣīyate: This phrase translates to "May both (the material and spiritual) not diminish." It emphasizes the importance of balance in life, where both physical and spiritual well-being are essential for a fulfilling existence.
  • Vasavyaṁ dive-dive jāyamānasya dasma: This part means "May the wealth that is born every day be ours." It signifies the continuous flow of prosperity and abundance in our lives, suggesting that we should be open to receiving blessings daily.
  • Kṛdhi kṣumantaṁ jaritāram agne: Here, "Agne" refers to the divine fire or energy, and "kṣumantaṁ jaritāram" can be interpreted as "the one who brings forth nourishment." This line calls upon the divine to provide sustenance and support in our endeavors.
  • Kṛdhi patiṁ svapatyasya rāyaḥ: This translates to "May you, O Lord, grant us the lordship of our offspring." It highlights the importance of legacy and the desire for prosperity to continue through future generations.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking balance in life, the importance of daily abundance, and the divine support in our pursuits. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various ways, such as:

  • Striving for a balance between work and personal life to ensure both material success and spiritual fulfillment.
  • Practicing gratitude daily to recognize and attract abundance in our lives.
  • Seeking guidance and support from higher powers or mentors in our professional and personal journeys.
  • Focusing on building a legacy that benefits future generations, whether through family, community service, or sustainable practices.

This mantra serves as a reminder to cultivate a holistic approach to life, where both material and spiritual aspects are nurtured for overall well-being and prosperity.
