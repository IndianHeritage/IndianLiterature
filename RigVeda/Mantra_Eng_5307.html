त्वं नृभिर नृमणो देववीतौ भूरीणि वृत्राः हर्यश्व हंसि |त्वं नि दस्युं चुमुरीं धुनिं चास्वापयो दभीतये सुहन्तु ||
tvaṁ nṛbhir nṛmaṇo devavītau bhūrīṇi vṛtrā haryaśva haṁsi | tvaṁ ni dasyuṁ cumuriṁ dhuniṁ cāsvāpayo dabhītaye suhantu ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • tvaṁ: You
    • nṛbhir: By men
    • nṛmaṇo: Of mankind
    • devavītau: In the presence of the divine
    • bhūrīṇi: Abundant
    • vṛtrā: The obstacles or enemies
    • haryaśva: The swift horses
    • haṁsi: You slay
    • ni: Down
    • dasyuṁ: The wicked or the evil
    • cumuriṁ: The powerful
    • dhuniṁ: The one who creates disturbance
    • cāsvāpayo: You drive away
    • dabhītaye: For the purpose of protection
    • suhantu: May they be well
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes a divine presence to assist humanity in overcoming obstacles and adversities.
    • The phrase "by men" suggests a collective effort, emphasizing the importance of unity in facing challenges.
    • It acknowledges the existence of enemies or obstacles ("vṛtrā") that hinder progress and well-being.
    • The mention of "swift horses" symbolizes strength and speed, indicating the need for quick action against adversities.
    • By addressing the "wicked" and "powerful," the mantra highlights the struggle against negative forces in life.
    • The concluding wish for well-being ("suhantu") reflects a desire for peace and harmony in society.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community facing natural disasters, this mantra can be a source of strength, encouraging collective action to rebuild and protect one another.
    • In personal struggles, such as overcoming addiction or mental health issues, individuals can find empowerment in the idea of driving away negative influences.
    • In a workplace setting, this mantra can inspire teamwork to tackle challenges, fostering a supportive environment where everyone contributes to overcoming obstacles.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine assistance in overcoming life's challenges.
    • It emphasizes the importance of unity, strength, and the collective effort of humanity in facing adversities.
    • In real-life scenarios, it serves as a reminder that with faith, support, and swift action, individuals and communities can triumph over difficulties and strive for peace and well-being.
