शुनं वाहाः शुनं नरः शुनं कृषतु लाङ्गलम् |शुनं वरत्रा बद्ध्यन्तां शुनं अस्त्रामुद इङ्गय ||
śunaṁ vāhāḥ śunaṁ naraḥ śunaṁ kṛṣatu lāṅgalam | śunaṁ varatrā badhyantāṁ śunam aṣṭrām ud iṅgaya ||
  • śunaṁ vāhāḥ: This phrase can be translated to "Let the vehicles be auspicious." In a real-life context, this can refer to the importance of having good means of transportation, whether it be physical vehicles or metaphorical ones, such as the tools and resources we use to achieve our goals.
  • śunaṁ naraḥ: This means "Let the person be auspicious." It emphasizes the significance of individuals being in a positive state, both mentally and physically. For example, a healthy and motivated individual can contribute positively to society.
  • śunaṁ kṛṣatu lāṅgalam: Translated as "Let the plow be auspicious," this line highlights the importance of agriculture and the tools used in farming. In modern times, this can relate to sustainable farming practices that ensure a good harvest and environmental health.
  • śunaṁ varatrā badhyantāṁ: This phrase means "Let the weapons be auspicious." It can be interpreted as a call for peace and the responsible use of power. In today's world, this can relate to the ethical use of technology and military power, promoting peace rather than conflict.
  • śunam aṣṭrām ud iṅgaya: This translates to "Let the instruments be auspicious." This can refer to the tools and methods we use in various fields, such as education, technology, and healthcare. For instance, using advanced medical instruments can lead to better health outcomes.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of auspiciousness in various aspects of life, including transportation, individual well-being, agriculture, responsible use of power, and the tools we employ. Its significance lies in the holistic approach to life, advocating for positivity and prosperity in all endeavors. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire individuals and communities to strive for excellence and ethical practices in their respective fields, ultimately contributing to a harmonious and prosperous society.
