तव त्ये अग्ने आर्चयो भ्राजन्तो यन्ति धृष्णुया |परिज्मानो न विद्युतः स्वानो रथो न वाजयुः ||
tava tye agne arcayo bhrājanto yanti dhṛṣṇuyā | parijmāno na vidyutaḥ svāno ratho na vājayuḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • tava: Your
    • tye: To you
    • agne: O Fire (Agni)
    • arcayo: We offer our praises
    • bhrājanto: Shining
    • yanti: They go
    • dhṛṣṇuyā: With strength
    • parijmāno: Surrounded
    • na: Not
    • vidyutaḥ: Lightning
    • svāno: The one who is auspicious
    • ratho: Chariot
    • na: Not
    • vājayuḥ: The one who is swift
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Agni, the Vedic fire god, symbolizing purity, energy, and the divine messenger.
    • The phrase "tava tye agne arcayo" indicates a deep reverence and devotion towards Agni, suggesting that the speaker is offering praises and seeking blessings.
    • "Bhrājanto yanti dhṛṣṇuyā" implies that those who are devoted and strong in their faith will be guided and protected by Agni.
    • The latter part, "parijmāno na vidyutaḥ svāno ratho na vājayuḥ," suggests that the divine presence of Agni is not just about physical strength or speed, but rather about the inner strength and auspiciousness that guides one through life's challenges.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern life, this mantra can be related to the importance of inner strength and resilience. For instance, during challenging times, individuals often turn to their spiritual beliefs or practices to find guidance and support.
    • In a corporate setting, leaders who embody the qualities of Agni—such as integrity, energy, and the ability to inspire—can lead their teams effectively, fostering a culture of strength and collaboration.
    • In personal relationships, the mantra emphasizes the importance of nurturing connections with loved ones, where mutual respect and support can help overcome obstacles.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of devotion, inner strength, and the divine guidance that comes from a higher power.
    • It teaches us that true power lies not in external attributes like speed or strength, but in the inner qualities that help us navigate life's challenges.
    • In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios, from personal growth to leadership, reminding us to seek strength from within and to honor the divine in our lives.
