अस्मे प्र यन्धि मगवन्न ऋजिषिन इन्द्र रायो विश्ववारस्य भूरेः |अस्मे शतं शरदो जीवसे धा अस्मे वीरां छश्वत इन्द्र शिप्रिन ||
asme pra yandhi maghavann ṛjīṣinn indra rāyo viśvavārasya bhūreḥ | asme śataṁ śarado jīvase dhā asme vīrāñ chaśvata indra śiprin ||
  • Mantra: "asme pra yandhi maghavann ṛjīṣinn indra rāyo viśvavārasya bhūreḥ | asme śataṁ śarado jīvase dhā asme vīrāñ chaśvata indra śiprin ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • asme: for us
    • pra yandhi: may you lead
    • maghavann: O mighty one
    • ṛjīṣinn: who is righteous
    • indra: O Indra (the king of gods)
    • rāyo: of wealth
    • viśvavārasya: of the all-encompassing
    • bhūreḥ: of abundance
    • śataṁ: a hundred
    • śarado: autumns (or years)
    • jīvase: may you live
    • dhā: may you hold
    • vīrāñ: heroes
    • chaśvata: eternal
    • indra: O Indra
    • śiprin: who is a protector
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Indra, the king of gods, asking for guidance and support.
    • The phrase "asme pra yandhi" signifies a request for leadership and direction in life.
    • By addressing Indra as "maghavann ṛjīṣinn," the mantra acknowledges his might and righteousness, suggesting that true strength comes from moral integrity.
    • The mention of "rāyo viśvavārasya bhūreḥ" emphasizes the desire for abundant wealth and resources, not just materially but also in wisdom and relationships.
    • Wishing for "śataṁ śarado jīvase" reflects a longing for longevity and prosperity over many years, indicating the importance of a fulfilling life.
    • Finally, invoking "vīrāñ chaśvata indra śiprin" highlights the aspiration to be surrounded by eternal heroes and protectors, symbolizing the value of community and support in achieving one's goals.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, leaders often seek guidance from mentors (akin to Indra) to navigate challenges and make ethical decisions.
    • Individuals may pray for abundance in their personal lives, whether through financial stability or strong relationships, reflecting the mantra's call for wealth and support.
    • Communities often come together to celebrate milestones, wishing for longevity and prosperity, similar to the blessings sought in this mantra.
    • In times of crisis, people look for heroes—be it first responders or community leaders—who embody the qualities of strength and protection, resonating with the mantra's final invocation.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of seeking guidance, valuing integrity, and fostering community support in our lives.
    • Its significance lies in the holistic approach to life, encompassing physical, emotional, and spiritual well-being.
    • In real-life scenarios, the principles embedded in this mantra can inspire individuals to strive for a balanced and prosperous existence, while also encouraging them to uplift those around them.
