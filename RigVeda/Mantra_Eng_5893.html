अयम् उ ते सरस्वती वसिष्ठो द्वाराव ऋतस्य सुभगे व्य आवः |वर्ध शुभ्रे स्तुवते राशि वाजान यूयम् पात स्वस्तिभिः सदा नः ||
ayam u te sarasvati vasiṣṭho dvārāv ṛtasya subhage vy āvaḥ | vardha śubhre stuvate rāsi vājān yūyam pāta svastibhiḥ sadā naḥ ||
  • Mantra: "ayam u te sarasvati vasiṣṭho dvārāv ṛtasya subhage vy āvaḥ | vardha śubhre stuvate rāsi vājān yūyam pāta svastibhiḥ sadā naḥ ||"
  • Translation: This mantra invokes the goddess Sarasvati, asking her to bless us with wisdom and prosperity. It emphasizes the importance of righteousness (ṛta) and the auspiciousness (subhage) of the path we tread.
  • Word Breakdown:
    • ayam: this
    • u: and
    • te: your
    • sarasvati: Sarasvati, the goddess of knowledge and wisdom
    • vasiṣṭho: the best or the most excellent
    • dvārāv: doors or gateways
    • ṛtasya: of righteousness or truth
    • subhage: auspicious or fortunate
    • vy āvaḥ: may you come to us
    • vardha: increase or enhance
    • śubhre: auspicious or good
    • stuvate: praising or worshipping
    • rāsi: abundance or wealth
    • vājān: of the horses, symbolizing strength and speed
    • yūyam: you all
    • pāta: protect or safeguard
    • svastibhiḥ: with auspiciousness
    • sadā: always
    • naḥ: us
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In educational settings, students often recite this mantra before exams to seek the blessings of Sarasvati for wisdom and clarity of thought.
    • During significant life events, such as weddings or new ventures, individuals may invoke this mantra to ensure a prosperous and righteous path ahead.
    • In corporate environments, teams may use this mantra to foster a culture of collaboration and ethical practices, ensuring that their endeavors are both successful and virtuous.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for wisdom, prosperity, and righteousness. It serves as a reminder to seek knowledge and act ethically in all aspects of life. By invoking Sarasvati, individuals and communities can aspire to enhance their understanding and navigate their paths with auspiciousness. This mantra is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from education to personal milestones, emphasizing the importance of wisdom and ethical conduct in achieving success and fulfillment.
