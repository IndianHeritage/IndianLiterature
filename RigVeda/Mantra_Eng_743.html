प्र नू स मार्तः शवसां जनाम् अति तस्थौ व ऊती मरुतो यम् आवत |अर्वद्भिर वाजम् भरते धनानृभिर आपृच्छ्यं क्रतुम् आ क्षेति पुष्यति ||
pra nū sa martaḥ śavasā janām̐ ati tasthau va ūtī maruto yam āvata | arvadbhir vājam bharate dhanā nṛbhir āpṛcchyaṁ kratum ā kṣeti puṣyati ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • pra nū: Indeed, let us invoke
    • sa martaḥ: that mortal
    • śavasā: with strength
    • janām̐: the people
    • ati tasthau: who have transcended
    • va ūtī: and the energy
    • maruto: of the storm gods
    • yam āvata: whom we invoke
    • arvadbhir: with great abundance
    • vājam: the wealth
    • bharate: that is brought forth
    • dhanā: by riches
    • nṛbhir: by men
    • āpṛcchyaṁ: we inquire
    • kratum: the intention
    • ā kṣeti: may it be fulfilled
    • puṣyati: may it prosper
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the strength and energy of the divine, particularly focusing on the mortal aspect of humanity.
    • It emphasizes the importance of community and collective strength, suggesting that when people come together with a common purpose, they can transcend their limitations.
    • The reference to the storm gods (Maruts) symbolizes the dynamic and transformative forces of nature that can bring about change and prosperity.
    • The mantra also highlights the significance of wealth and resources, indicating that they should be sought after and utilized wisely for the benefit of all.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community project, when individuals unite their strengths and resources, they can achieve remarkable outcomes, such as building a school or a community center.
    • In business, collaboration among team members can lead to innovative solutions and increased productivity, reflecting the collective strength mentioned in the mantra.
    • Environmental initiatives often require collective action, where communities come together to address issues like pollution or deforestation, embodying the spirit of the mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the power of unity and collective effort in achieving goals.
    • It encourages individuals to seek abundance and prosperity not just for themselves, but for the community as a whole.
    • In real life, the principles outlined in this mantra can be applied in various contexts, from personal relationships to professional collaborations, emphasizing the importance of working together for a common purpose.
