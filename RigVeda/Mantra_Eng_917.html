आ तिष्ठ वृत्रहन् रथं युक्ता ते ब्रह्मणा हरि |अर्वाचीनं सु ते मनो ग्रावा कृतोतु वाग्नुना ||
ā tiṣṭha vṛtrahan rathaṁ yuktā te brahmaṇā harī | arvācīnaṁ su te mano grāvā kṛṇotu vagnunā ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā tiṣṭha: "Stand firm" or "be steadfast."
    • vṛtrahan: "Slayer of Vritra," referring to the divine force that overcomes obstacles.
    • rathaṁ: "Chariot," symbolizing movement, progress, or the journey of life.
    • yuktā: "Equipped" or "joined," indicating readiness and alignment.
    • te: "Your," referring to the divine or higher power.
    • brahmaṇā: "By the divine knowledge" or "through the ultimate reality."
    • harī: "The divine" or "the one who removes obstacles."
    • arvācīnaṁ: "From the past" or "ancient," suggesting wisdom from history.
    • su: "Well" or "good," indicating positivity.
    • te mano: "Your mind," referring to the mental state or consciousness.
    • grāvā: "May it be directed" or "may it be guided."
    • kṛṇotu: "May it create" or "may it bring forth."
    • vagnunā: "With fire" or "with energy," symbolizing transformation and vitality.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of being steadfast and equipped to face challenges in life.
    • The reference to "Vritra" symbolizes overcoming obstacles, akin to facing personal or societal challenges.
    • The "chariot" represents our journey through life, where we must be prepared and aligned with our goals.
    • The invocation of divine knowledge suggests that wisdom from the past can guide our present actions.
    • By focusing our minds positively, we can create a transformative energy that propels us forward.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, when facing a challenging project, one must "stand firm" and utilize past experiences to navigate obstacles.
    • In personal development, individuals often draw on historical wisdom or mentors to guide their decisions and actions.
    • During times of crisis, such as health issues or financial difficulties, maintaining a positive mindset and focusing on solutions can lead to recovery and growth.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the strength and resilience within us to overcome life's challenges.
    • It highlights the significance of aligning our thoughts and actions with divine wisdom and positivity.
    • In real-life scenarios, applying the principles of steadfastness, guidance from the past, and a positive mindset can lead to personal and collective transformation.
