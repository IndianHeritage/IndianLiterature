स हि रत्नानि दाशुषे सुवाति सविता भगः |तम भागं चित्रं ईमहे ||
sa hi ratnāni dāśuṣe suvāti savitā bhagaḥ | tam bhāgaṁ citram īmahe ||
  • Translation: "Indeed, the treasures (ratnāni) are for the one who is deserving (dāśuṣe), may Savita (the Sun) bestow (suvāti) his blessings (bhagaḥ) upon us. We meditate (īmahe) upon that wondrous (citram) fortune (bhāgaṁ)."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • sa hi: Indeed, this emphasizes certainty and truth.
    • ratnāni: Treasures or valuable things, which can be interpreted as both material wealth and spiritual wisdom.
    • dāśuṣe: For the deserving or the one who is worthy, indicating that blessings come to those who are righteous.
    • suvāti: Bestow or grant, often associated with the Sun's life-giving energy.
    • savitā: Refers to the Sun, a symbol of illumination, energy, and life.
    • bhagaḥ: Blessings or fortune, often linked to prosperity and well-being.
    • tam: That, referring to the object of meditation or focus.
    • bhāgaṁ: Fortune or share, indicating a portion of blessings one seeks.
    • citram: Wondrous or extraordinary, suggesting something remarkable or unique.
    • īmahe: We meditate or contemplate, indicating a deep focus on the subject.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, individuals who work hard and demonstrate integrity often receive recognition and rewards, akin to the "treasures" mentioned in the mantra.
    • In personal relationships, those who are deserving of love and respect often find themselves surrounded by supportive friends and family, reflecting the idea of blessings bestowed upon the worthy.
    • In spiritual practices, meditation on the Sun (Savitā) can symbolize seeking enlightenment and clarity in life, as the Sun is a source of light and energy.
  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of deservingness and righteousness in receiving blessings and treasures in life. It teaches that true fortune comes to those who are worthy and that one should meditate on the extraordinary aspects of life, seeking guidance and illumination from higher powers, such as the Sun. In real life, this can be applied in various contexts, including personal growth, professional success, and spiritual enlightenment, reminding us to strive for integrity and to appreciate the blessings we receive.
