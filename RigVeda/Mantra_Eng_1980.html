विघात्वावां ऋतजात यंसद गृणानो अग्ने तन्वे3 वरूथम् |विश्वाद रिरिक्षोर उता व निनित्सोर अभिहृताम् असि हि देव विष्पट् ||
vi gha tvāvām̐ ṛtajāta yaṁsad gṛṇāno agne tanve3 varūtham | viśvād ririkṣor uta vā ninitsor abhihrutām asi hi deva viṣpaṭ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • vi gha: "May we be blessed" or "May we attain."
    • tvāvām̐: "You" (referring to the divine or Agni).
    • ṛtajāta: "Born of truth" or "truthful."
    • yaṁsad: "Who is praised" or "who is worshipped."
    • gṛṇāno: "Singing" or "chanting."
    • agne: "O Agni" (the fire god).
    • tanve3: "In your body" or "in your form."
    • varūtham: "Your protection" or "your shelter."
    • viśvād: "From all sides" or "universally."
    • ririkṣor: "Of the one who desires" or "of the one who seeks."
    • uta: "Or" (indicating an alternative).
    • vā: "Or" (again indicating an alternative).
    • ninitsor: "Of the one who is vigilant" or "of the one who watches."
    • abhihrutām: "You are invoked" or "you are called upon."
    • asi: "You are."
    • hi: "Indeed."
    • deva: "O divine being" or "O god."
    • viṣpaṭ: "All" or "everywhere."
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Agni, the fire god, who symbolizes purity, transformation, and the divine presence in the world.
    • The invocation seeks blessings and protection from Agni, emphasizing the importance of truth and vigilance in life.
    • It highlights the need for divine guidance in our pursuits and the significance of being aware and watchful in our actions.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, this mantra can be applied when seeking clarity and truth in decision-making, such as in ethical dilemmas at work.
    • It can also be relevant in personal relationships, where honesty and vigilance are crucial for maintaining trust and understanding.
    • During challenging times, invoking Agni's protection can serve as a reminder to stay grounded and focused on one's goals.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of seeking divine guidance and protection in all aspects of life.
    • It encourages individuals to remain truthful and vigilant, fostering a sense of awareness and responsibility.
    • By incorporating this mantra into daily practices, one can cultivate a deeper connection with the divine and navigate life's challenges with wisdom and grace.
