यन् नूनं धीभिर अश्विनां पितुः योना निषीदथः | यद वा सुम्नेभिर उक्थ्या ||
yan nūnaṁ dhībhir aśvinā pitur yonā niṣīdathaḥ | yad vā sumnebhir ukthyā ||
  • Mantra: "yan nūnaṁ dhībhir aśvinā pitur yonā niṣīdathaḥ | yad vā sumnebhir ukthyā ||"
  • Translation: "O Ashvins, may you, who are endowed with wisdom, sit in the womb of our father; or may you be invoked with auspicious hymns."
  • Word-by-Word Explanation:
    • yan: "that" or "which"
    • nūnaṁ: "indeed" or "certainly"
    • dhībhir: "with wisdom" or "with intellect"
    • aśvinā: "O Ashvins" (the twin deities associated with health and medicine)
    • pitur: "of the father"
    • yonā: "in the womb" or "in the source"
    • niṣīdathaḥ: "may you sit" or "may you dwell"
    • yad: "that" or "which"
    • vā: "or"
    • sumnebhir: "with auspicious" or "with good"
    • ukthyā: "hymns" or "invocations"
  • Contextual Significance:
    • The Ashvins are revered as divine physicians and bringers of health and well-being. This mantra invokes their presence and assistance.
    • The reference to "sitting in the womb of our father" symbolizes the importance of nurturing and the source of life, emphasizing the connection between divine blessings and human existence.
    • Invoking the Ashvins with "auspicious hymns" highlights the significance of prayer and positive intentions in seeking divine intervention.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern healthcare, the invocation of the Ashvins can be likened to seeking the best medical care and wisdom from healthcare professionals when facing health challenges.
    • During significant life events, such as childbirth or marriage, individuals often seek blessings through prayers and rituals, similar to invoking the Ashvins for auspiciousness and well-being.
    • In personal development, the mantra can inspire individuals to seek wisdom and guidance in their decisions, emphasizing the importance of thoughtful consideration and positive intentions.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of divine blessings, human existence, and the pursuit of wisdom.
    • It encourages individuals to seek guidance and support from higher powers in their lives, particularly in times of need.
    • Ultimately, the mantra emphasizes the importance of nurturing relationships, both with the divine and within the community, to foster health, well-being, and prosperity.
