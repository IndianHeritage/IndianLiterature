निर्मायाऽउ त्ये असुराऽभूवन त्वं च मा वरुण कामयासे |ऋतेन राजन् अनृतं विविञ्चन् मम राष्ट्रस्याधिपत्यं एहि ||
nirmāyā u tye asurā abhūvan tvaṁ ca mā varuṇa kāmayāse | ṛtena rājann anṛtaṁ viviñcan mama rāṣṭrasyādhipatyam ehi ||
  • nirmāyā u tye asurā abhūvan: This phrase can be translated as "the Asuras (demons) have been created by you." In this context, it refers to the duality of creation, where both divine and demonic forces exist. In real life, this can be seen in the balance of good and evil in society, where individuals may choose to act in ways that align with either moral or immoral principles.
  • tvaṁ ca mā varuṇa kāmayāse: This translates to "and you, Varuna, I desire." Varuna is a deity associated with cosmic order and truth. This line expresses a longing for divine guidance and support. In everyday life, people often seek guidance from higher powers or principles when faced with moral dilemmas or challenges, reflecting a desire for clarity and righteousness.
  • ṛtena rājann anṛtaṁ viviñcan: This means "O King, separating truth from falsehood." Here, the mantra emphasizes the importance of discernment and the pursuit of truth. In practical terms, this can relate to the need for integrity in leadership and decision-making, where leaders must navigate complex situations and distinguish between what is true and what is misleading.
  • mama rāṣṭrasyādhipatyam ehi: This translates to "come to the sovereignty of my realm." This line is a call for divine presence and support in governance and leadership. In a real-world context, this can be seen in the way leaders invoke higher principles or divine favor to guide their actions and decisions for the benefit of their communities or nations.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the themes of creation, the quest for truth, and the invocation of divine support in leadership. Its significance lies in the recognition of the dual forces of good and evil, the importance of seeking truth, and the need for divine guidance in governance. In contemporary life, this mantra can inspire individuals and leaders alike to strive for integrity, balance, and moral clarity in their actions, fostering a society that values truth and righteousness.
