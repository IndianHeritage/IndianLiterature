यच छल्मलौ भवति यन् नदिषु यद ओषधिभ्यः परि जायते विषम् |विश्वे देवाः निर इतस तात सुवन्तु मा माम पद्येन रपसां विदत्सरुः ||
yac chalmalau bhavati yan nadīṣu yad oṣadhībhyaḥ pari jāyate viṣam | viśve devā nir itas tat suvantu mā mām padyena rapasā vidat tsaruḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yac: whatever
    • chalmalau: moves or flows
    • bhavati: becomes or is
    • yan: whatever
    • nadīṣu: in rivers
    • yad: whatever
    • oṣadhībhyaḥ: from plants or herbs
    • pari: all around
    • jāyate: is born or arises
    • viṣam: poison or harmful substances
    • viśve: all
    • devā: gods or divine beings
    • nir: free from
    • itas: from here
    • tat: that
    • suvantu: may they be pleased
    • mā: not
    • mām: me
    • padyena: by foot or by path
    • rapasā: by the power
    • vidat: knowing
    • tsaruḥ: the tree or plant
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the interconnectedness of all elements in nature.
    • It emphasizes that everything that flows or moves, whether in rivers or from plants, has a purpose and significance.
    • The mention of "viṣam" (poison) suggests that even harmful substances can have a role in the cycle of life.
    • The invocation to the gods ("viśve devā") indicates a plea for divine protection and guidance.
    • The phrase "suvantu mā mām" expresses a desire for the divine to not let harm come to the speaker.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In environmental science, the balance of ecosystems shows how every element, even those considered harmful, plays a role in maintaining ecological balance.
    • In medicine, certain plants that are toxic in high doses can have healing properties when used correctly, illustrating the duality of nature.
    • In daily life, we often encounter challenges (viṣam) that can lead to personal growth and resilience, highlighting the importance of perspective.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the harmony in nature and the importance of respecting all forms of life.
    • It encourages us to seek divine guidance in navigating life's challenges and to appreciate the interconnectedness of all beings.
    • In practical terms, it can inspire individuals to adopt a holistic approach to life, recognizing that every experience, good or bad, contributes to personal and collective growth.
