सखाऽ ह यत्र सकिभिर नवग्वैर अभिज्ञ्वा सत्वभिर गा अनुग्मन |सत्यं तद इन्द्रो दशभिर दशग्वैः सूर्यं विवेद तमसि क्षीयन्तम् ||
sakhā ha yatra sakhibhir navagvair abhijñv ā satvabhir gā anugman | satyaṁ tad indro daśabhir daśagvaiḥ sūryaṁ viveda tamasi kṣiyantam ||
  • Mantra: "sakhā ha yatra sakhibhir navagvair abhijñv ā satvabhir gā anugman | satyaṁ tad indro daśabhir daśagvaiḥ sūryaṁ viveda tamasi kṣiyantam ||"
  • Translation: "Where friends gather together, with new and knowledgeable companions, they move forward with strength. That truth, Indra, with tenfold power, knows the sun that dispels darkness."
  • Word-by-Word Explanation:
    • sakhā: friend or companion
    • yatra: where
    • sakhibhir: with friends
    • navagvair: new and knowledgeable
    • abhijñv: knowledgeable or wise
    • satvabhir: with strength or power
    • gā: to move or progress
    • anugman: to follow or pursue
    • satyaṁ: truth
    • tad: that
    • indro: Indra, the king of gods
    • daśabhir: with tenfold
    • daśagvaiḥ: tenfold powers
    • sūryaṁ: the sun
    • viveda: knows or perceives
    • tamasi: darkness
    • kṣiyantam: that which is dispelled or removed
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace, collaboration among colleagues (friends) can lead to innovative solutions, much like the mantra suggests that gathering with knowledgeable companions leads to progress.
    • In educational settings, students working together on projects can enhance their understanding and knowledge, reflecting the idea of moving forward with strength.
    • In personal relationships, having supportive friends can help individuals overcome challenges, akin to the truth that dispels darkness.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of friendship, collaboration, and the pursuit of truth. It signifies that when individuals come together with knowledge and strength, they can overcome obstacles and dispel ignorance.
    • In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios such as teamwork in professional environments, study groups in education, and supportive social networks in personal life.
    • Ultimately, the mantra serves as a reminder of the power of unity and the pursuit of truth in achieving success and enlightenment.
