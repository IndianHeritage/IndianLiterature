आ त्वा गीरो रथीर इवास्थुः सुतेषु गिरवणः | अभि त्वा सम अनूषतेंद्र वत्सं न मातरः ||
ā tvā giro rathīr ivāsthuḥ suteṣu girvaṇaḥ | abhi tvā sam anūṣatendra vatsaṁ na mātaraḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā: to you
    • tvā: you
    • giro: of the songs or hymns
    • rathīr: like a chariot
    • ivāsthuḥ: may it be established
    • suteṣu: in the offerings
    • girvaṇaḥ: of the singers or poets
    • abhi: towards
    • tvā: you
    • sam: together
    • anūṣat: may we invoke
    • indra: O Indra (the king of gods)
    • vatsaṁ: the calf or offspring
    • na: not
    • mātaraḥ: the mothers
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer directed towards Indra, the king of the gods, asking for blessings and support.
    • The phrase "ā tvā giro rathīr ivāsthuḥ" suggests that the hymns and praises are directed towards Indra, likening them to a chariot that carries the devotee's intentions and desires.
    • The invocation "abhi tvā sam anūṣatendra" emphasizes the collective effort of the community in seeking Indra's favor, highlighting the importance of unity in spiritual pursuits.
    • The mention of "vatsaṁ na mātaraḥ" can be interpreted as a plea for protection and nurturing, akin to how a mother cares for her child, indicating the need for divine guidance in times of need.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community setting, this mantra can be recited during festivals or gatherings to invoke blessings for prosperity and harmony among the members.
    • In personal life, individuals may use this mantra during times of uncertainty or challenge, seeking divine support and guidance, much like how one would seek advice from a trusted mentor.
    • In educational or professional environments, the essence of this mantra can be applied by fostering teamwork and collaboration, invoking a collective spirit to achieve common goals.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its emphasis on community, divine support, and the nurturing aspect of spirituality.
    • It serves as a reminder that in our pursuits, whether personal or communal, invoking higher powers and working together can lead to greater success and fulfillment.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra encourages individuals and communities to seek unity and divine guidance, reinforcing the idea that we are not alone in our journeys.
