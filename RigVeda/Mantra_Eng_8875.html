आ घात ता गच्छान उत्तरā युगानि यत्र जामयः कृणवन्न अजामि |उप बर्बृहि वृषभाय बाहुम अन्यं इच्छस्व सुभगे पतिम मत ||
ā ghā tā gacchān uttarā yugāni yatra jāmayaḥ kṛṇavann ajāmi | upa barbṛhi vṛṣabhāya bāhum anyam icchasva subhage patim mat ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā ghā: O, come forth
    • tā: that
    • gacchān: going
    • uttarā: to the higher
    • yugāni: ages or epochs
    • yatra: where
    • jāmayaḥ: the offspring or progeny
    • kṛṇavann: creating or establishing
    • ajāmi: I have been born
    • upa: near or close
    • barbṛhi: to the strong or powerful
    • vṛṣabhāya: to the bull or the leader
    • bāhum: arm
    • anyam: another
    • icchasva: desire or wish
    • subhage: auspicious or fortunate
    • patim: lord or husband
    • mat: my
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes a sense of journey and aspiration towards higher realms.
    • It emphasizes the importance of creating a legacy and nurturing future generations.
    • The reference to "the strong" and "the bull" symbolizes leadership and strength, suggesting that one should seek a partner who embodies these qualities.
    • The phrase "subhage patim mat" indicates a desire for a fortunate and auspicious partner, highlighting the importance of compatibility and shared values in relationships.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, this mantra can be applied to the idea of mentorship, where experienced individuals guide the younger generation towards success.
    • In personal relationships, it encourages individuals to seek partners who are not only strong and capable but also share similar aspirations and values.
    • In family dynamics, it highlights the importance of nurturing children and preparing them for the future, ensuring they inherit wisdom and strength.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of generations and the importance of striving for higher ideals.
    • It encourages individuals to take active roles in shaping their futures and the futures of those around them.
    • Ultimately, it emphasizes the significance of choosing partners and mentors wisely, as they play a crucial role in our journey through life.
