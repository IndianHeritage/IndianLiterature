आ वो मक्षू तनाया कं रुद्राऽवो वृष्णीमहे |गन्ता नूनं नोऽवसा यथा पुरेत्था कण्वाय बिभ्युषे ||
ā vo makṣū tanāya kaṁ rudrā avo vṛṇīmahe | gantā nūnaṁ no 'vasā yathā puretthā kaṇvāya bibhyuṣe ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā vo: To you all
    • makṣū: We offer
    • tanāya: To the body or form
    • kaṁ: Who
    • rudrā: O Rudra (a form of Shiva)
    • avo: May we have
    • vṛṇīmahe: We choose or we invoke
    • gantā: The one who goes
    • nūnaṁ: Certainly
    • no: Our
    • 'vasā: Protection or shelter
    • yathā: As
    • puretthā: In the past
    • kaṇvāya: To the wise or learned
    • bibhyuṣe: For fear or protection
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Rudra, a deity associated with storms, wind, and the hunt, often considered a protector and a fierce force of nature.
    • The invocation "ā vo makṣū tanāya" suggests a collective offering or prayer, emphasizing community and shared intentions.
    • By addressing Rudra, the speaker seeks guidance and protection, indicating a reliance on divine forces in times of uncertainty.
    • The phrase "gantā nūnaṁ no 'vasā" reflects a desire for safety and shelter, highlighting the human need for security in life.
    • The reference to "yathā puretthā kaṇvāya bibhyuṣe" connects the present plea for protection to past experiences, suggesting that wisdom and learning from history are crucial for navigating current challenges.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In times of natural disasters, communities often come together to pray for safety and protection, similar to the invocation of Rudra for shelter.
    • In personal crises, individuals may seek guidance from spiritual leaders or texts, reflecting the need for wisdom and support in difficult times.
    • Organizations often invoke collective prayers or meditations to foster unity and strength among members, paralleling the communal aspect of this mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking divine protection and guidance, particularly in challenging times.
    • It highlights the value of community, wisdom, and learning from the past, which are applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal struggles to collective challenges.
    • Ultimately, it serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of individuals and the divine, encouraging a proactive approach to seeking safety and wisdom in life.
