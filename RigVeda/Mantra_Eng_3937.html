नू त आभिर अभिष्टिभिस तव शर्मण चतक्रतो |इन्द्र स्याम सुगोपाः शूर स्याम सुगोपाः ||
nū ta ābhir abhiṣṭibhis tava śarmañ chatakrato | indra syāma sugopāḥ śūra syāma sugopāḥ ||
  • nū ta ābhir abhiṣṭibhis tava śarmañ chatakrato
    • nū - This word can be interpreted as "indeed" or "truly," emphasizing the sincerity of the invocation.
    • ta - This means "you," referring to the deity being addressed, in this case, Indra.
    • ābhir - This translates to "with blessings" or "with auspiciousness," indicating a request for divine favor.
    • abhiṣṭibhis - This word means "with prayers" or "with hymns," suggesting that the speaker is invoking Indra through sincere worship.
    • tava - Meaning "your," it signifies possession, indicating that the blessings or qualities being sought belong to Indra.
    • śarmañ - This translates to "protection" or "shelter," indicating a desire for safety and security.
    • chatakrato - This is an epithet of Indra, meaning "one who has a hundred powers," highlighting his omnipotence and capability.
  • indra syāma sugopāḥ śūra syāma sugopāḥ
    • indra - Referring to the king of gods, symbolizing strength, power, and leadership.
    • syāma - This word means "may you be" or "let there be," indicating a wish or prayer for the qualities being invoked.
    • sugopāḥ - This translates to "good protectors," suggesting a plea for guardianship and safety.
    • śūra - Meaning "brave" or "heroic," it emphasizes the qualities of valor and courage.
    • syāma - Repeated for emphasis, reinforcing the desire for these qualities to manifest.

Conclusion:

This mantra is a heartfelt invocation to Indra, seeking his blessings for protection, strength, and courage. In real-life scenarios, it can be applicable in various situations where individuals seek divine support, such as during challenging times, battles, or when facing fears. For instance, a student preparing for an important exam might recite this mantra to invoke focus and protection from distractions. Similarly, a leader facing a critical decision may use this mantra to seek wisdom and bravery in their choices. The significance of this mantra lies in its ability to connect the devotee with divine energies, fostering a sense of security and empowerment in their endeavors.
