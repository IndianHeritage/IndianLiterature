मन्त्र: सुदासे दास्रā वसु बिभ्रता रथे पृक्षो वहतम अश्विनā | रयिं समुद्राद उता वा दिवस पर्य अस्मे धत्तम पुरुष्पृहम ||
sudāse dasrā vasu bibhratā rathe pṛkṣo vahatam aśvinā | rayiṁ samudrād uta vā divas pary asme dhattam puruspṛham ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • Sudāse: One who is well-versed or knowledgeable.
    • Dasrā: The one who is swift or quick.
    • Vasu: Wealth or prosperity.
    • Bibhratā: Bearing or carrying.
    • Rathe: In the chariot.
    • Pṛkṣo: Of the two Ashvins (the divine twin horsemen).
    • Vahatam: Driving or guiding.
    • Aśvinā: Referring to the Ashvins, the deities of health and medicine.
    • Rayiṁ: Wealth or abundance.
    • Samudrād: From the ocean.
    • Uta: Or.
    • Vā: Or.
    • Divas: Day or light.
    • Pary: Around or about.
    • Asme: To us.
    • Dhattam: May grant or bestow.
    • Puruspṛham: The one who is full of vigor or strength.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the Ashvins, the divine twin horsemen, who are known for their swiftness and ability to bring health and prosperity.
    • The phrase "sudāse dasrā" emphasizes the importance of knowledge and speed in achieving success.
    • By referring to "rathe pṛkṣo," it highlights the journey of life as a chariot ride, where the Ashvins guide us through challenges.
    • The mention of "rayiṁ samudrād" suggests that wealth and abundance can be drawn from vast sources, much like the ocean.
    • Overall, the mantra is a prayer for guidance, prosperity, and strength in our endeavors.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be applied to the pursuit of success where knowledge and quick decision-making are crucial.
    • In personal life, it can serve as a reminder to seek guidance and support from mentors (the Ashvins) when facing challenges.
    • In health, it emphasizes the importance of seeking medical help promptly, as the Ashvins are deities of health.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine assistance in achieving prosperity and health.
    • It teaches us the value of knowledge, speed, and guidance in our pursuits.
    • In real life, it encourages us to seek help and wisdom from others, reminding us that we are not alone in our journeys.
