यज्ञेभिर यज्ञवाहसं सोमैभिः सोमपातमम् |होत्राभिर इन्द्रं वावृधुर व्यानशुः ||
yajñebhir yajñavāhasaṁ somebhiḥ somapātamam | hotrābhir indraṁ vāvṛdhur vy ānaśuḥ ||
  • yajñebhir: This word refers to the offerings or sacrifices made during rituals. In real life, this can be likened to acts of charity or community service, where individuals contribute to the welfare of others.
  • yajñavāhasaṁ: This term signifies the essence or the spirit of sacrifice. It emphasizes the importance of selflessness and the collective effort in achieving a common goal, similar to teamwork in a workplace or community project.
  • somebhiḥ: This word relates to the moon or soma, which is often associated with nourishment and vitality. In everyday life, this can represent the importance of mental and emotional well-being, akin to how we seek balance in our lives through relaxation and self-care.
  • somapātamam: This term can be interpreted as the ultimate source of nourishment or the best of the soma. It highlights the pursuit of the highest ideals in life, such as striving for excellence in personal and professional endeavors.
  • hotrābhir: This refers to the priests or those who perform the rituals. In a modern context, this can be seen as leaders or mentors who guide and inspire others in their journeys.
  • indraṁ: Indra is the king of the gods and represents strength, power, and leadership. In real life, this can symbolize the qualities of a strong leader or a person who takes charge in difficult situations.
  • vāvṛdhur: This word means to increase or to enhance. It signifies growth and development, which can be related to personal growth or the progress of a community through collective efforts.
  • vy ānaśuḥ: This term can be interpreted as the act of invoking or calling upon. It reflects the idea of seeking support or guidance from higher powers or from the community, similar to how individuals seek help from friends or family during challenging times.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of collective effort, selflessness, and the pursuit of higher ideals in life. It teaches us that through acts of sacrifice and community service, we can invoke strength and support from others, leading to personal and communal growth. In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as teamwork in professional settings, community service initiatives, and personal development efforts, reminding us of the importance of collaboration and the pursuit of excellence for the greater good.
