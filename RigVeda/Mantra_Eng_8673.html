परी दैवीर अनु स्वधा इन्द्रेण याहि सारथम् |पुनानो वाघद वाघद्भिर अमर्त्यः ||
pari daivīr anu svadhā indreṇa yāhi saratham | punāno vāghad vāghadbhir amartyaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • pari: around, in all directions
    • daivīr: divine, celestial
    • anu: following, in accordance with
    • svadhā: self, one's own, or the essence of oneself
    • indreṇa: by Indra, the king of gods
    • yāhi: come, go, or proceed
    • saratham: with the chariot, or in a well-equipped manner
    • punāno: renewed, refreshed
    • vāghad: speech, or the power of expression
    • vāghadbhir: with the powers of speech
    • amartyaḥ: immortal, divine beings
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the divine forces to come together in harmony and support.
    • The phrase "pari daivīr anu svadhā" suggests that one should align with divine energies and follow their guidance.
    • By calling upon Indra, the mantra emphasizes the importance of divine intervention and strength in overcoming challenges.
    • The mention of "saratham" indicates the need for readiness and preparation, akin to being equipped for a journey.
    • The renewal aspect in "punāno" signifies the importance of rejuvenation and revitalization in life.
    • Finally, "amartyaḥ" highlights the aspiration towards immortality or achieving a higher state of existence through divine support.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a challenging situation, such as a major life decision or career change, one might meditate on this mantra to seek clarity and divine guidance.
    • During a team project, invoking the spirit of collaboration and divine support can lead to a more harmonious and productive environment.
    • In personal development, the idea of renewal can be applied by setting new goals and seeking inspiration from higher ideals.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of divine forces and human endeavors.
    • It encourages individuals to seek divine guidance, prepare themselves for challenges, and strive for renewal in their lives.
    • In practical terms, it can be applied in various aspects of life, including personal growth, teamwork, and decision-making, fostering a sense of purpose and direction.
