स नो धीति वरिष्टया श्रेष्टया च सुमत्या |अग्ने रायो दिदीहि नः सुवृक्तिभिर वरेण्या ||
sa no dhītī variṣṭhayā śreṣṭhayā ca sumatyā | agne rāyo didīhi naḥ suvṛktibhir vareṇya ||
  • sa no dhītī: This phrase translates to "may our intellect" or "may our understanding." It emphasizes the importance of wisdom and knowledge in our lives.
  • variṣṭhayā: This word means "the best" or "the most excellent." It signifies the pursuit of the highest form of understanding and intellect.
  • śreṣṭhayā: Similar to variṣṭhayā, this term also means "the best" or "the most superior." It reinforces the idea of striving for excellence in our thoughts and actions.
  • ca: This is a conjunction meaning "and," linking the concepts of intellect and excellence together.
  • sumatyā: This word translates to "with good thoughts" or "with auspicious intentions." It highlights the significance of positive thinking and intentions in our pursuits.
  • agne: This refers to "Agni," the fire god, symbolizing energy, transformation, and the divine spark within us.
  • rāyo: This means "wealth" or "prosperity." It indicates the desire for abundance and success in life.
  • didīhi: This translates to "bestow" or "grant." It expresses a plea for divine blessings and support.
  • naḥ: This means "to us," indicating a collective request for blessings.
  • suvṛktibhir: This term means "with good speech" or "with excellent words." It emphasizes the importance of communication and expression in achieving our goals.
  • vareṇya: This translates to "worthy of worship" or "most excellent." It signifies the divine nature of the blessings being sought.

Conclusion: The mantra "sa no dhītī variṣṭhayā śreṣṭhayā ca sumatyā | agne rāyo didīhi naḥ suvṛktibhir vareṇya ||" is a profound invocation for wisdom, excellence, and prosperity. It encourages individuals to seek the highest form of understanding and to cultivate positive thoughts and intentions. In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as in educational pursuits, professional endeavors, and personal development. By focusing on acquiring knowledge, fostering good intentions, and communicating effectively, individuals can attract success and abundance into their lives. The significance of this mantra lies in its holistic approach to achieving a fulfilling and prosperous life, reminding us that wisdom, positivity, and divine support are essential components of our journey.
