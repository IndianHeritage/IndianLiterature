तं सखायः पुरोर्चं यूयं वयं च सूरयः |अश्याम वाजगन्ध्यं सनेमा वाजपस्त्यम् ||
taṁ sakhāyaḥ purorucaṁ yūyaṁ vayaṁ ca sūrayaḥ | aśyāma vājagandhyaṁ sanema vājapastyam ||
  • taṁ sakhāyaḥ: This phrase translates to "that friend" or "those companions." It emphasizes the importance of friendship and companionship in life.
  • purorucaṁ: This word means "of the past" or "from ancient times." It suggests a connection to tradition and the wisdom of ancestors.
  • yūyaṁ vayaṁ ca: This translates to "you and we." It signifies unity and collective effort among individuals.
  • sūrayaḥ: This term means "the strong" or "the powerful." It refers to those who possess strength, courage, and valor.
  • aśyāma: This word can be interpreted as "to be auspicious" or "to bring good fortune." It highlights the desire for positive outcomes.
  • vājagandhyaṁ: This term refers to "the essence of strength" or "the fragrance of power." It symbolizes the qualities that make one strong and successful.
  • sanema: This means "we may attain" or "let us achieve." It expresses a wish or intention to reach a goal.
  • vājapastyam: This word translates to "the essence of victory" or "the strength of success." It signifies the ultimate achievement and triumph.

The mantra as a whole can be interpreted as a call for unity among friends and companions, drawing strength from tradition and the wisdom of the past. It emphasizes the importance of collective effort in achieving success and auspicious outcomes in life.


In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts:

  • Teamwork: In a workplace setting, this mantra encourages collaboration among colleagues, highlighting that success is often a collective effort.
  • Community Building: In social or community initiatives, it underscores the importance of coming together for a common cause, drawing on the strengths of each individual.
  • Friendship: In personal relationships, it serves as a reminder of the value of supportive friendships that help individuals navigate challenges and celebrate successes together.

Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its emphasis on unity, strength, and the pursuit of success through collective effort. It teaches us that by coming together with friends and companions, we can draw on shared wisdom and strength to achieve our goals and create a positive impact in our lives and communities.
