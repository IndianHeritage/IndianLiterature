अवर्त्या शुन आन्त्राणि पेचे न देवेषु विविदे मार्डितारम् |अपश्यं जायााम अमहीयमानाम् अधा मे श्येनो मध्व आ जभारा ||
avartyā śuna āntrāṇi pece na deveṣu vivide marḍitāram | apaśyaṁ jāyām amahīyamānām adhā me śyeno madhv ā jabhāra ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • avartyā: turning back or returning
    • śuna: to the sound or noise
    • āntrāṇi: the inner parts or the essence
    • pece: to speak or to express
    • na: not
    • deveṣu: among the gods
    • vivide: diverse or various
    • marḍitāram: the one who is praised or celebrated
    • apaśyaṁ: I did not see
    • jāyām: the wife or the female
    • amahīyamānām: not being held or restrained
    • adhā: below or beneath
    • me: my
    • śyeno: the falcon or the bird of prey
    • madhv: sweet or pleasant
    • ā: to
    • jabhāra: has brought or carried
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the essence of returning to one's roots and the importance of expressing oneself without fear.
    • The phrase "avartyā śuna āntrāṇi" suggests a return to the core of one's being, emphasizing the need to listen to the inner voice.
    • It highlights the idea that one should not be afraid to express their thoughts and feelings, even in a diverse world filled with various beliefs and opinions.
    • The mention of "deveṣu vivide" indicates the multitude of perspectives that exist, urging individuals to find their unique voice amidst the noise.
    • The line "apaśyaṁ jāyām amahīyamānām" reflects the struggle of not being able to see or recognize one's true self or potential, which can often be overshadowed by external influences.
    • Finally, "adhā me śyeno madhv ā jabhāra" suggests that the sweet rewards of life come from embracing one's true nature and expressing it freely.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate environment, employees may feel pressured to conform to a certain culture. This mantra encourages them to express their individuality and contribute their unique perspectives.
    • In personal relationships, individuals often struggle to communicate their feelings. This mantra serves as a reminder to speak openly and honestly, fostering deeper connections.
    • Artists and creators may face criticism or doubt. The mantra inspires them to return to their essence and create authentically, regardless of external opinions.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of self-awareness and the courage to express one's true self in a diverse world.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal relationships to professional settings, encouraging individuals to embrace their uniqueness.
    • Ultimately, it teaches that true fulfillment and sweetness in life come from being authentic and true to oneself.
