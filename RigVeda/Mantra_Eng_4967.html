इन्द्रा नु पूषणा वयं सख्याय स्वस्तये |हुवेमा वाजसातये ||
indrā nu pūṣaṇā vayaṁ sakhyāya svastaye | huvema vājasātaye ||
  • Indrā: Refers to Indra, the king of the gods in Vedic mythology, symbolizing strength, power, and leadership.
  • Nu: A particle that can be translated as "now" or "indeed," emphasizing immediacy or affirmation.
  • Pūṣaṇā: Refers to Pūṣan, the deity associated with nourishment and prosperity, representing growth and sustenance.
  • Vayaṁ: Means "we," indicating a collective or community aspect.
  • Sakhyāya: Translates to "for friendship" or "for companionship," highlighting the importance of relationships and unity.
  • Svastaye: Means "for well-being" or "for auspiciousness," indicating a desire for health and prosperity.
  • Huvema: A form of the verb "to invoke" or "to call upon," suggesting a plea or prayer.
  • Vājasātaye: Refers to "for strength" or "for power," often associated with the idea of victory and success.

The mantra can be translated as: "Indeed, we invoke Indra and Pūṣan for friendship and well-being; may we be blessed with strength and prosperity." This invocation emphasizes the importance of community, relationships, and the desire for collective well-being.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios:

  • Community Building: In a society where collaboration and friendship are essential, this mantra serves as a reminder to foster strong relationships for mutual growth.
  • Teamwork: In workplaces, invoking the spirit of this mantra can encourage teamwork and camaraderie, leading to a more productive and harmonious environment.
  • Health and Well-being: The focus on well-being can inspire individuals to prioritize health, both physically and mentally, promoting a balanced lifestyle.
  • Celebrations and Gatherings: During festivals or communal gatherings, reciting this mantra can enhance the spirit of unity and shared joy among participants.

In conclusion, this mantra encapsulates the essence of invoking divine support for friendship, well-being, and strength. Its significance lies in its ability to inspire individuals and communities to work together harmoniously, fostering an environment of mutual respect and prosperity. By applying its teachings in daily life, one can cultivate a sense of belonging and collective success.
