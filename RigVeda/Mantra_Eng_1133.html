तद ūचुṣe mānuṣemā युगानि कीर्तेन्यं मगवā नाम बिभ्रत् |उपप्रयाण दस्युहत्याय वज्री यद ध सूनुः श्रवसे नाम दधे ||
tad ūcuṣe mānuṣemā yugāni kīrtenyam maghavā nāma bibhrat | upaprayan dasyuhatyāya vajrī yad dha sūnuḥ śravase nāma dadhe ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • tad: that
    • ūcuṣe: speaks or expresses
    • mānuṣemā: human or humanity
    • yugāni: ages or epochs
    • kīrtenyam: to be praised or celebrated
    • maghavā: the powerful or mighty one
    • nāma: by name
    • bibhrat: bearing or holding
    • upaprayan: to lead or guide
    • dasyuhatyāya: for the destruction of enemies
    • vajrī: the one who wields the thunderbolt
    • yad: that which
    • dha: to place or establish
    • sūnuḥ: son or offspring
    • śravase: for fame or glory
    • nāma: by name
    • dadhe: has established
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of a powerful entity that expresses itself through humanity across different ages.
    • The term "maghavā" refers to a mighty force, often associated with divine power or strength.
    • The mantra emphasizes the importance of guidance and leadership in overcoming adversities, particularly in the context of defeating enemies or challenges.
    • The reference to "vajrī" symbolizes strength and resilience, akin to the thunderbolt, which is indestructible and powerful.
    • It highlights the significance of establishing a legacy of fame and glory through righteous actions and leadership.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern leadership, this mantra can be reflected in leaders who inspire and guide their teams through challenges, much like a coach leading a sports team to victory.
    • In personal life, individuals who stand up against injustice or adversity embody the essence of this mantra, striving to create a better environment for future generations.
    • In community service, those who dedicate their efforts to uplift others and fight against societal issues resonate with the themes of strength and guidance found in this mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the power of human agency and the importance of leadership in navigating life's challenges.
    • It encourages individuals to embrace their strength and take on the responsibility of guiding others, fostering a legacy of courage and resilience.
    • In essence, it is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal growth to community leadership, emphasizing the timeless relevance of strength, guidance, and the pursuit of glory through righteous actions.
