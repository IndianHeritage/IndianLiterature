यस्त्वा स्वश्वः सुहिरण्यो अग्न उपयाति वसुमता रथेन |तस्य त्राताऽ भवसि तस्य सखा यस्त तातिथ्यम अनुशग जुजोषत ||
yas tvā svaśvaḥ suhiraṇyo agna upayāti vasumatā rathena | tasya trātā bhavasi tasya sakhā yas ta ātithyam ānuṣag jujoṣat ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yas: who
    • tvā: you
    • svaśvaḥ: of your own
    • suhiraṇyo: good and prosperous
    • agna: O Agni (the fire god)
    • upayāti: approaches
    • vasumatā: of the prosperous
    • rathena: with the chariot
    • tasya: of him
    • trātā: protector
    • bhavasi: you become
    • tasya: of him
    • sakhā: friend
    • yas: who
    • ta: that
    • ātithyam: hospitality
    • ānuṣag: following
    • jujoṣat: enjoys
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes Agni, the fire god, emphasizing the importance of approaching him with a sense of prosperity and goodwill.
    • The phrase "who approaches you with his own good and prosperous chariot" suggests that one should come to Agni with their own resources and intentions, symbolizing self-reliance and initiative.
    • The mantra highlights the dual roles of Agni as a protector and a friend, indicating that divine support is available to those who seek it with sincerity.
    • The mention of hospitality ("ātithyam") signifies the importance of welcoming others and sharing one's blessings, which is a core value in many cultures.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, approaching a new venture with a "prosperous chariot" can mean coming prepared with resources, knowledge, and a positive mindset, which can lead to success.
    • In personal relationships, being a protector and friend to others can foster deeper connections and a supportive community, reflecting the essence of the mantra.
    • Practicing hospitality, such as inviting friends and family over for meals, embodies the spirit of sharing and caring, which can strengthen bonds and create a sense of belonging.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of self-reliance, the value of friendship, and the significance of hospitality in our lives.
    • Its teachings can be applied in various aspects of life, from personal relationships to professional endeavors, encouraging individuals to approach challenges with a positive attitude and a spirit of generosity.
    • Ultimately, it emphasizes that divine support is available to those who act with integrity and goodwill, making it a timeless guide for living harmoniously with oneself and others.
