नू ते पूर्वस्यावसो अधीतौ तृतीये विदथे मनमा शंसि |अस्मे अग्ने संयद्वीरम् बृहन्तं क्षुमन्तं वाजं स्वपत्यम् रयिं दाह ||
nū te pūrvasyāvaso adhītau tṛtīye vidathe manma śaṁsi | asme agne saṁyadvīram bṛhantaṁ kṣumantaṁ vājaṁ svapatyaṁ rayiṁ dāḥ ||
  • nū te pūrvasyāvaso adhītau: This phrase can be translated as "We have understood your previous teachings." It signifies the importance of learning from past wisdom and experiences. In real life, this can be seen in educational settings where students build upon foundational knowledge to grasp more complex concepts.
  • tṛtīye vidathe manma śaṁsi: This translates to "In the third gathering, may my thoughts be auspicious." The idea of gatherings or assemblies is crucial in many cultures, where collective decision-making and sharing of ideas lead to positive outcomes. For example, in a corporate environment, team meetings can foster collaboration and innovation.
  • asme agne saṁyadvīram: This means "O Agni, unite us with strength." Agni, the fire god, symbolizes energy and transformation. In everyday life, this can relate to the need for unity and strength in communities or teams, where collective effort leads to achieving common goals, such as in social movements or community service projects.
  • bṛhantaṁ kṣumantaṁ vājaṁ svapatyaṁ rayiṁ dāḥ: This can be interpreted as "Grant us the great, the prosperous, the wealth that comes from our efforts." It emphasizes the pursuit of prosperity and success through hard work and dedication. In practical terms, this can be seen in entrepreneurship, where individuals strive to create value and wealth through their innovations and efforts.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of learning from the past, the importance of collective thought, the need for unity and strength, and the pursuit of prosperity through hard work. Its significance lies in its applicability to various aspects of life, including education, teamwork, community building, and personal growth. By embodying these principles, individuals and societies can strive for a harmonious and prosperous existence.
