त्रिः स्म माह्नः शनथयो वैतसेनोत स्म मे 'व्यत्यै पृणासि |पुरूरवो 'नु ते केतम आयं राजा मे वीर तन्वस तद आसिः ||
triḥ sma māhnaḥ śnathayo vaitasenota sma me 'vyatyai pṛṇāsi | purūravo 'nu te ketam āyaṁ rājā me vīra tanva1s tad āsīḥ ||
  • Mantra: "triḥ sma māhnaḥ śnathayo vaitasenota sma me 'vyatyai pṛṇāsi | purūravo 'nu te ketam āyaṁ rājā me vīra tanva1s tad āsīḥ ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • triḥ: three
    • sma: indeed
    • māhnaḥ: of the great
    • śnathayo: of the strong
    • vaitasenota: may you be pleased
    • sma: indeed
    • me: my
    • 'vyatyai: for the purpose of overcoming
    • pṛṇāsi: you fulfill
    • purūravo: of the many
    • 'nu: indeed
    • te: your
    • ketam: abode
    • āyaṁ: may it come
    • rājā: king
    • me: my
    • vīra: hero
    • tanva1s: body
    • tad: that
    • āsīḥ: may it be
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the strength and greatness of the divine, asking for blessings and fulfillment of desires.
    • The repetition of "triḥ" emphasizes the importance of the number three, which can symbolize completeness or stability in various cultures.
    • The phrase "may you be pleased" indicates a desire for divine favor, which is a common theme in many spiritual practices.
    • The request for overcoming obstacles ("for the purpose of overcoming") reflects the human experience of facing challenges and seeking support.
    • The mention of "king" and "hero" signifies the qualities of leadership and bravery, which are essential in both personal and communal contexts.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, a team may recite this mantra before a major presentation, seeking strength and success in their endeavors.
    • During personal challenges, individuals might reflect on the qualities of a "hero" to inspire courage and resilience in their lives.
    • In community gatherings, invoking the divine for blessings can foster a sense of unity and shared purpose among participants.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the strength and support available to us in our pursuits.
    • Its significance lies in the acknowledgment of divine presence in overcoming life's challenges and achieving greatness.
    • In real-life scenarios, it can be applied in various contexts, from personal growth to collective efforts, reinforcing the idea that we are not alone in our journeys.
