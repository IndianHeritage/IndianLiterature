अस्माकं इति सु श्रिणुहि त्वं इन्द्रास्मभ्यं चित्राम् उप माही वाजान् |अस्मभ्यं विश्वा इषणः पुरन्धीर अस्माकं सु मगवन् बोधि गोदाः ||
asmākam it su śṛṇuhi tvam indrāsmabhyaṁ citrām̐ upa māhi vājān | asmabhyaṁ viśvā iṣaṇaḥ puraṁdhīr asmākaṁ su maghavan bodhi godāḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • asmākam: our
    • it: this
    • su: good
    • śṛṇuhi: listen
    • tvam: you
    • indra: O Indra (the king of gods)
    • asmabhyaṁ: for us
    • citrām̐: wonderful
    • upa: near
    • māhi: may we attain
    • vājān: the riches
    • viśvā: all
    • iṣaṇaḥ: powerful
    • puraṁdhīr: the wise one
    • asmākaṁ: our
    • su: good
    • maghavan: O bounteous one
    • bodhi: knowledge
    • godāḥ: cows (symbolizing wealth)
  • Overall Meaning: This mantra is a prayer to Indra, the king of gods, asking him to listen to our pleas and grant us wonderful riches and knowledge. It emphasizes the importance of divine blessings in achieving prosperity and wisdom.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be seen as a call for guidance and support from higher powers when making important decisions. For instance, entrepreneurs often seek blessings before launching a new venture, hoping for success and prosperity.
    • In personal life, individuals may recite this mantra during times of uncertainty, such as during exams or job interviews, to invoke confidence and clarity of thought.
    • Communities may come together to chant this mantra during festivals or rituals, seeking collective blessings for abundance and harmony.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its invocation of divine support for achieving prosperity and wisdom. It teaches us the value of seeking guidance from higher powers in our endeavors, whether in personal or communal aspects of life. By recognizing the interconnectedness of our efforts and the blessings we seek, we can cultivate a mindset of gratitude and openness to the abundance that life offers. This mantra serves as a reminder that with the right intentions and divine support, we can attain our goals and enrich our lives.
