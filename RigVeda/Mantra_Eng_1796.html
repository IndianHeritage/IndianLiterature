येन दीर्घं मरुतः शूषवाम युष्माकेन परीनसā तुरासः |आ यत ततनन वृजनें जनासा एभिर यज्ञेभिस तद अभिष्टिम अश्यां ||
yena dīrgham marutaḥ śūśavāma yuṣmākena parīṇasā turāsaḥ | ā yat tatanan vṛjane janāsa ebhir yajñebhis tad abhīṣṭim aśyām ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yena: by whom
    • dīrgham: long, enduring
    • marutaḥ: of the winds
    • śūśavāma: we have become strong
    • yuṣmākena: by you
    • parīṇasā: with full strength
    • turāsaḥ: swift, powerful
    • ā: that
    • yat: which
    • tatanan: we have attained
    • vṛjane: in the act of sacrifice
    • janāsa: of the people
    • ebhir: with these
    • yajñebhis: sacrifices
    • tad: that
    • abhīṣṭim: desire, wish
    • aśyām: auspicious, beneficial
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the power of the winds (marutaḥ) and their ability to bring strength and endurance (dīrgham) to those who invoke them.
    • It emphasizes the importance of collective effort (by you - yuṣmākena) in achieving strength and success (parīṇasā turāsaḥ).
    • The mantra also highlights the significance of sacrifices (yajñebhis) made by the community (janāsa) to attain their desires (abhīṣṭim).
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, teamwork and collaboration can be likened to the invocation of the winds, where collective efforts lead to successful project outcomes.
    • In community service, when people come together to support a cause, their combined strength can create significant positive change, reflecting the essence of this mantra.
    • In personal development, individuals often rely on the support of friends and family (the 'winds') to achieve their long-term goals, demonstrating the mantra's relevance in everyday life.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of unity, strength, and the power of collective action in achieving desired outcomes.
    • It serves as a reminder that through collaboration and shared sacrifices, individuals and communities can attain their aspirations and create a more auspicious and beneficial environment.
    • In essence, it encourages us to harness the power of collective strength in our personal and communal endeavors, making it applicable in various aspects of life, from personal relationships to professional collaborations.
