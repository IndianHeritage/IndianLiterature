मन्त्र: पुरोळाशं च नो घसो जोषयासे गिरश्च नः | वधूयुर इव योषणाम् ||
puroḻāśaṁ ca no ghaso joṣayāse giraś ca naḥ | vadhūyur iva yoṣaṇām ||
  • puroḻāśaṁ: This term can be broken down into two parts: "puro" meaning "in front" or "before," and "ḻāśaṁ" which refers to "prosperity" or "wealth." Together, it signifies the prosperity that lies ahead or the blessings that are to come.
  • ca: This is a conjunction meaning "and," indicating a connection between the ideas presented in the mantra.
  • no: This word translates to "our," indicating possession or a collective aspect of the blessings being sought.
  • ghaso: This term refers to "food" or "nourishment." It symbolizes the sustenance that is essential for life and well-being.
  • joṣayāse: This word can be understood as "to invigorate" or "to energize." It implies a desire for vitality and strength in life.
  • giraś: This term means "speech" or "words." It signifies the importance of communication and expression in human life.
  • ca: Again, this conjunction connects the previous ideas with what follows.
  • naḥ: This translates to "us" or "our," reinforcing the collective aspect of the mantra.
  • vadhūyur: This term refers to "the bride" or "the woman," symbolizing femininity, nurturing, and the essence of life.
  • iva: This means "like" or "as," indicating a comparison or similarity.
  • yoṣaṇām: This word means "of women" or "of the feminine," emphasizing the role of women in society and their contributions to life and culture.

Conclusion: The mantra "puroḻāśaṁ ca no ghaso joṣayāse giraś ca naḥ | vadhūyur iva yoṣaṇām ||" encapsulates a prayer for prosperity, nourishment, and the vitality of communication, particularly in the context of relationships and the feminine aspect of life. It highlights the importance of collective well-being and the role of women in nurturing and sustaining life. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts such as family gatherings, weddings, or community events where blessings for prosperity and harmony are sought. It serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of life, the significance of nourishment—both physical and emotional—and the power of words in fostering relationships and community bonds.
