स व्राधतः शवसानेभिर अस्य कुत्साय शुष्णं कृपणे परादात् |अयं कविम अनयच छस्यमानम् अत्कं यो अस्य सनितोत नृणाम् ||
sa vrādhataḥ śavasānebhir asya kutsāya śuṣṇaṁ kṛpaṇe parādāt | ayaṁ kavim anayac chasyamānam atkaṁ yo asya sanitota nṛṇām ||
  • sa vrādhataḥ: This phrase can be interpreted as "from the one who is nourished" or "from the one who is supported." It signifies the importance of sustenance and support in life.
  • śavasānebhir: This term refers to "the dead" or "those who have passed away." It highlights the connection between life and death, and the respect owed to those who have departed.
  • asya kutsāya: This means "to this one who is in distress" or "to this one who is suffering." It emphasizes compassion and the need to help those in need.
  • śuṣṇaṁ kṛpaṇe parādāt: This can be translated as "to the one who is poor and in need." It calls for generosity and support for the less fortunate in society.
  • ayaṁ kavim: This phrase means "this wise one" or "this poet." It suggests the value of wisdom and creativity in understanding and interpreting life.
  • anayac: This translates to "he who leads" or "he who guides." It indicates the role of leadership and guidance in navigating life's challenges.
  • shasyamānam atkaṁ: This means "the one who is being cultivated" or "the one who is growing." It symbolizes growth, development, and the nurturing of potential.
  • yo asya sanitota nṛṇām: This can be interpreted as "whoever is in harmony with humanity." It emphasizes the importance of community and the interconnectedness of all individuals.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda encapsulates themes of support, compassion, wisdom, and community. It serves as a reminder of the importance of nurturing both ourselves and those around us. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios such as community service, where individuals come together to support the less fortunate, or in personal relationships, where we offer help and guidance to friends and family in distress. The mantra encourages us to cultivate a sense of responsibility towards others, fostering a harmonious society where everyone can thrive. Its significance lies in the timeless values it promotes, which are essential for a balanced and compassionate life.
