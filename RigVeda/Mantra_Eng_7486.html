अपादुशिप्रियन्दसः सुदक्षस्य प्रहोषिणः |इन्दोः इन्द्रो यवाशिरः ||
apād u śipry andhasaḥ sudakṣasya prahoṣiṇaḥ | indor indro yavāśiraḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • apād: without feet or support
    • u: and
    • śipry: of the swift or the quick
    • andhasaḥ: of the blind or darkness
    • sudakṣasya: of the well-skilled or the proficient
    • prahoṣiṇaḥ: of the one who is powerful or strong
    • indor: of Indra, the king of gods
    • indro: Indra, the powerful
    • yavāśiraḥ: of the one who is youthful or vigorous
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks to the qualities of strength, skill, and vitality.
    • The phrase "apād u śipry andhasaḥ" suggests a state of being that is unencumbered and swift, indicating the importance of agility and adaptability in life.
    • "Sudakṣasya prahoṣiṇaḥ" emphasizes the value of skill and power, suggesting that those who are proficient and strong can overcome obstacles.
    • The invocation of Indra, a symbol of power and leadership, highlights the need for divine support in achieving one's goals.
    • "Yavāśiraḥ" reflects the importance of maintaining youthfulness and vigor, which can be interpreted as a call to embrace life with enthusiasm and energy.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, an employee who is adaptable and quick to learn new skills can navigate challenges more effectively, much like the "swift" mentioned in the mantra.
    • A sports team that trains rigorously and develops strong teamwork exemplifies "sudakṣasya prahoṣiṇaḥ," as their skill and strength lead them to victory.
    • In personal development, maintaining a youthful spirit and enthusiasm for life can lead to greater happiness and fulfillment, resonating with "yavāśiraḥ."
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of agility, skill, and vitality in our lives.
    • It encourages individuals to seek strength and proficiency in their endeavors while remaining adaptable to change.
    • By invoking the qualities of Indra, it suggests that divine support and guidance can aid us in overcoming challenges.
    • Ultimately, the mantra inspires us to embrace life with vigor and enthusiasm, making it applicable in various aspects of personal and professional growth.
