आ वो वहन्तु सप्तयो रघुष्यदो रघुपत्वानः प्र जिगात बाहुभिः |सीदतां बर्हिर उरु वह सदस कृतम मादयध्वं मरुतो मध्वो अन्धसः ||
ā vo vahantu saptayo raghuṣyado raghupatvānaḥ pra jigāta bāhubhiḥ | sīdatā barhir uru vaḥ sadas kṛtam mādayadhvam maruto madhvo andhasaḥ ||
  • Mantra: ā vo vahantu saptayo raghuṣyado raghupatvānaḥ pra jigāta bāhubhiḥ | sīdatā barhir uru vaḥ sadas kṛtam mādayadhvam maruto madhvo andhasaḥ ||
  • Translation: "May the seven (sages) who are endowed with strength and power, come to you. May they, with their mighty arms, bring forth prosperity. May the vast and expansive blessings be upon you, and may you rejoice in the presence of the Maruts, the divine winds, who dispel darkness."

  • Word-by-Word Explanation:
    • ā vo vahantu: "May they come to you" - a call for divine presence and blessings.
    • saptayo: "the seven" - often refers to the seven sages or seers, symbolizing wisdom and guidance.
    • raghuṣyado: "endowed with strength" - signifies the power and capability of these sages.
    • raghupatvānaḥ: "with their might" - emphasizes the strength and authority of the divine beings.
    • pra jigāta bāhubhiḥ: "bring forth prosperity" - a wish for abundance and success through their intervention.
    • sīdatā: "may it be vast" - a call for expansive blessings.
    • barhir uru: "the vast and expansive" - refers to the wide-reaching nature of these blessings.
    • vaḥ sadas: "be upon you" - a direct blessing to the recipient.
    • kṛtam: "what is done" - refers to actions taken in the pursuit of prosperity.
    • mādayadhvam: "may you rejoice" - a call for joy and celebration.
    • maruto: "the Maruts" - divine winds, representing strength and vitality.
    • madhvo andhasaḥ: "who dispel darkness" - signifies the removal of ignorance and obstacles.

  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, this mantra can be invoked during a team meeting to inspire collaboration and strength among team members, encouraging them to work together towards a common goal.
    • During a wedding ceremony, the couple may recite this mantra to seek blessings for a prosperous and joyful life together, invoking the support of their families and friends.
    • In personal development, individuals may use this mantra as a daily affirmation to attract positive energy and dispel negativity, fostering a mindset of abundance and joy.

  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its invocation of divine strength and blessings. It emphasizes the importance of community, collaboration, and the pursuit of prosperity. In real-life scenarios, it can be applied in various contexts such as personal growth, teamwork, and celebrations, reminding us of the power of collective effort and the joy that comes from shared success. By embracing the essence of this mantra, individuals and groups can cultivate a positive environment that fosters growth, happiness, and enlightenment.
