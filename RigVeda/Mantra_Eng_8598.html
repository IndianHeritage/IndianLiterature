महत् तत् सोमो महिषश्चकारापां यद्गर्भो 'वृणीत देवान् |अदधाद इन्द्रे पवमान ओजो 'जनयत सूर्ये ज्योतिर इन्दुः ||
mahat tat somo mahiṣaś cakārāpāṁ yad garbho 'vṛṇīta devān | adadhād indre pavamāna ojo 'janayat sūrye jyotir induḥ ||
  • mahat tat somo mahiṣaś cakārāpāṁ: This phrase can be translated to "That great Soma, the powerful one, has created." Here, "Soma" refers to a sacred drink in Vedic tradition, symbolizing nourishment and immortality. In real life, this can be likened to the importance of sustenance and the vital resources we rely on for our well-being.
  • yad garbho 'vṛṇīta devān: This translates to "which the embryo chooses the gods." This line emphasizes the idea of choice and selection, suggesting that even in the womb, there is a connection to the divine. In a modern context, this can relate to the choices we make in life and how they shape our destinies, akin to how parents choose values and beliefs to instill in their children.
  • adadhād indre pavamāna ojo 'janayat: This means "He established strength in Indra, the wind." Indra is a significant deity associated with strength and power. This line can be interpreted as the importance of harnessing one's inner strength and the power of nature. In everyday life, this can be seen in how individuals draw upon their inner resources to overcome challenges, much like how communities come together to face natural disasters.
  • sūrye jyotir induḥ: This translates to "the light of the sun is the moon." This line symbolizes the relationship between different celestial bodies and their roles in providing light and guidance. In a practical sense, this can be related to how we seek guidance and inspiration from various sources in our lives, such as mentors, books, or spiritual teachings.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates themes of creation, choice, strength, and guidance. Its significance lies in the interconnectedness of life and the divine, reminding us that our choices and inner strength shape our paths. In real-life scenarios, this mantra encourages individuals to seek nourishment, make conscious choices, harness their inner power, and look for guidance in their journeys. It serves as a reminder of the sacredness of life and the importance of aligning with higher principles for a fulfilling existence.
