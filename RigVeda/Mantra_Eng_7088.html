सत्यं तद इन्द्रावरुणा कृशस्य वाम मध्वा ऊर्मिं दूहते सप्त वाणिः | ताभिर दाश्वांसम अवतं शुभस पती यो वाम अदब्धो अभि पाति चित्तिभिः ||
satyaṁ tad indrāvaruṇā kṛśasya vām madhva ūrmiṁ duhate sapta vāṇīḥ | tābhir dāśvāṁsam avataṁ śubhas patī yo vām adabdho abhi pāti cittibhiḥ ||
  • Meaning of the Mantra:
    • Satyaṁ: Truth or reality.
    • Tad: That.
    • Indrāvaruṇā: Referring to the divine powers of Indra and Varuna, representing strength and cosmic order.
    • Kṛśasya: Of the weak or the humble.
    • Vām: Your.
    • Madhva: Sweetness or nectar.
    • Ūrmiṁ: Wave or energy.
    • Duhate: To extract or draw out.
    • Sapta: Seven.
    • Vāṇīḥ: Voices or sounds.
    • Tābhir: By them.
    • Dāśvāṁsam: The one who is worthy of worship.
    • Avataṁ: To protect or to shelter.
    • Śubhas: Auspicious or good.
    • Patī: Lord or master.
    • Yo: Who.
    • Vām: Your.
    • Adabdho: Unharmed or untroubled.
    • Abhi: Towards.
    • Pāti: To protect or to guard.
    • Cittibhiḥ: With the mind or consciousness.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of truth and the divine support that comes from the cosmic powers of Indra and Varuna.
    • It suggests that even those who are weak or humble can draw out sweetness and energy from the universe.
    • The mention of "seven voices" can symbolize the various aspects of life or the different paths one can take.
    • It highlights the role of divine protection and guidance in achieving one's goals and maintaining auspiciousness in life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a challenging situation, such as a job interview, one can invoke the essence of this mantra by staying truthful and humble, trusting that the divine will guide them towards success.
    • In personal relationships, being honest and nurturing can help in building strong bonds, drawing out the sweetness of companionship.
    • In community service, helping the weak and vulnerable can be seen as embodying the spirit of this mantra, where one acts as a protector and guide.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the power of truth and the importance of divine support in our lives.
    • It encourages individuals to remain humble and seek guidance from higher powers while striving for their goals.
    • In essence, it teaches that through truthfulness and divine connection, one can navigate life's challenges and emerge victorious.
