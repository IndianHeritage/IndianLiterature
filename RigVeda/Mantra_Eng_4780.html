विदृढानि चिदद्रिवो जनानां शचीपते |वृह माया अनानत ||
vi dṛḻhāni cid adrivo janānāṁ śacīpate | vṛha māyā anānata ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • vi: indeed, truly
    • dṛḻhāni: strong, firm, steadfast
    • cid: consciousness, awareness
    • adrivo: without obstacles, unhindered
    • janānāṁ: of the people, of the beings
    • śacīpate: O Lord of strength, O powerful one
    • vṛha: great, vast
    • māyā: illusion, magic, creative power
    • anānata: without end, infinite
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks to the strength and consciousness that exists within individuals and the collective.
    • It emphasizes the idea that true strength is unhindered by obstacles, suggesting that awareness and consciousness can lead to overcoming challenges.
    • The invocation of "O Lord of strength" signifies a call for divine support in harnessing this strength.
    • The mention of "great illusion" reflects the complexities of life and the challenges that may seem overwhelming but can be navigated with awareness.
    • Finally, the concept of "infinite" suggests that the potential for strength and awareness is boundless.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In personal development, individuals often face obstacles that can be overcome through self-awareness and mental strength. For instance, someone struggling with anxiety may find that understanding their thoughts and feelings allows them to navigate their challenges more effectively.
    • In a team setting, a group facing a project deadline may draw on their collective strengths and awareness of each member's capabilities to overcome hurdles and achieve their goals.
    • In a broader societal context, communities facing adversity can unite, drawing on their shared strength and awareness to create positive change, such as in social movements advocating for justice and equality.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the inherent strength and consciousness within each individual and community.
    • It encourages us to recognize and harness our inner power to overcome obstacles and navigate the complexities of life.
    • In real-life scenarios, whether personal, professional, or societal, the principles of awareness, strength, and unity can lead to transformative outcomes.
    • Ultimately, the mantra inspires us to seek the infinite potential within ourselves and others, fostering resilience and growth.
