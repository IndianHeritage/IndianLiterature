त्वं अग्नि वानुष्यतो न पाही त्वं उ नः सहसावन्न अवद्यāt |सं त्वा ध्वसमन्वद अभ्य एतु पाठः सं रयिः स्पृहयाय्यः सहस्री ||
tvam agne vanuṣyato ni pāhi tvam u naḥ sahasāvann avadyāt | saṁ tvā dhvasmanvad abhy etu pāthaḥ saṁ rayiḥ spṛhayāyyaḥ sahasrī ||
  • tvam agne: This phrase translates to "You, O Agni." Agni is the Vedic fire god, symbolizing energy, light, and transformation. In real life, this can be likened to the role of fire in our daily lives, such as cooking food, providing warmth, and serving as a source of light.
  • vanuṣyato ni pāhi: This means "protect us from all that is harmful." It emphasizes the need for safety and protection in our lives. For example, in modern times, we seek protection from various dangers, whether they be physical, emotional, or spiritual, such as through community support or personal safety measures.
  • tvam u naḥ sahasāvann avadyāt: This translates to "may you not abandon us, O Agni, with your countless blessings." It reflects the human desire for abundance and support. In real life, this can be seen in our pursuit of prosperity, whether through career success, healthy relationships, or personal growth.
  • saṁ tvā dhvasmanvad abhy etu pāthaḥ: This means "may our paths be illuminated by you." It signifies the importance of guidance and clarity in our lives. For instance, in decision-making processes, we often seek advice from mentors or trusted individuals to help us navigate challenges.
  • saṁ rayiḥ spṛhayāyyaḥ sahasrī: This translates to "may we be blessed with abundant wealth and prosperity." This reflects the universal aspiration for a fulfilling and prosperous life. In contemporary society, this can be related to the pursuit of financial stability, career advancement, and overall well-being.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking protection, guidance, and abundance in life. It highlights the importance of divine support in our endeavors and the human quest for safety and prosperity. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as during personal challenges, career pursuits, or when seeking clarity in decision-making. By invoking the blessings of Agni, individuals can aspire to lead a life filled with light, protection, and abundance.
