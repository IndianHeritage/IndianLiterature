मन्त्र: अदितिः ह्य् अजनिष्ट दक्ष यā दुतिता तव | तां देवाः अन्व् अजायन्त भद्रā अमृतबन्धवः ||
aditir hy ajaniṣṭa dakṣa yā duhitā tava | tāṁ devā anv ajāyanta bhadrā amṛtabandhavaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • Aditi: A primordial goddess, representing the infinite and the boundless.
    • hi: Indeed, or for sure.
    • ajaniṣṭa: Gave birth or manifested.
    • dakṣa: Skilled or capable.
    • yā: Who.
    • duhitā: Daughter.
    • tava: Your.
    • tām: Her.
    • devā: The gods.
    • anv: Followed or pursued.
    • ajāyanta: Were born or came into being.
    • bhadrā: Auspicious or beneficial.
    • amṛtabandhavaḥ: Immortal relatives or kin.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of Aditi, the mother of the gods, who is portrayed as a skilled and capable figure.
    • It emphasizes the idea that from Aditi, the gods were born, highlighting her significance in the cosmic order.
    • The term "bhadrā" suggests that the offspring of Aditi are auspicious, indicating that they bring goodness and prosperity.
    • The phrase "amṛtabandhavaḥ" implies a connection to immortality, suggesting that the divine lineage is eternal and transcends the mortal realm.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In many cultures, the concept of a nurturing mother figure is central. For instance, in Christianity, the Virgin Mary is revered as a mother who brings forth divine grace.
    • In modern society, the idea of skilled and capable women in leadership roles can be seen as a reflection of Aditi's qualities, inspiring future generations.
    • The importance of family and kinship, as highlighted by "amṛtabandhavaḥ," can be observed in various cultures where ancestral lineage and heritage are celebrated.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of divine motherhood and the nurturing aspect of creation.
    • It serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of life and the auspicious nature of divine blessings.
    • In real life, it encourages us to recognize and honor the contributions of maternal figures and the importance of family ties in fostering a supportive community.
