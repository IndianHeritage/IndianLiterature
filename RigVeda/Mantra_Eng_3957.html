प्र वो वायुं रथयुजं कृतुध्वं प्र देवं विप्रम पनितारं अर्कैः |इषुध्यव ऋतसापः पुरंधीर्वस्वीः नो अत्र पत्नीर आ धिये धुः ||
pra vo vāyuṁ rathayujaṁ kṛṇudhvam pra devaṁ vipram panitāram arkaiḥ | iṣudhyava ṛtasāpaḥ puraṁdhīr vasvīr no atra patnīr ā dhiye dhuḥ ||
  • pra vo vāyuṁ rathayujaṁ kṛṇudhvam: This phrase can be translated as "O Wind, harness the chariot." Here, "vāyu" refers to the wind or the vital force, and "rathayujaṁ" signifies a chariot, symbolizing movement and progress. In real life, this can be likened to the idea of harnessing one's energy and resources to achieve goals, much like how athletes prepare for a race.
  • pra devaṁ vipram panitāram arkaiḥ: This translates to "We invoke the divine, the wise one, with our hymns." "Devaṁ" refers to the divine or gods, while "vipram" signifies wisdom. This line emphasizes the importance of seeking guidance from higher powers or wisdom figures in our lives, akin to consulting mentors or spiritual leaders when faced with challenges.
  • iṣudhyava ṛtasāpaḥ puraṁdhīr: This can be interpreted as "May the arrows of truth strike true." "Iṣudhyava" refers to arrows, and "ṛtasāpaḥ" signifies the essence of truth. This line highlights the importance of truthfulness and integrity in our actions, similar to how a person must be honest in their dealings to build trust in relationships.
  • vasvīr no atra patnīr ā dhiye dhuḥ: This translates to "May we be blessed with prosperity and wisdom." "Vasvīr" refers to abundance, and "patnīr" signifies the nurturing aspect, often associated with the feminine. This line underscores the significance of nurturing relationships and environments that foster growth and prosperity, much like how a supportive family or community can lead to individual success.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of harnessing one's energy, seeking wisdom, adhering to truth, and nurturing relationships for prosperity. In real life, these principles can guide individuals in their personal and professional lives, encouraging them to strive for their goals while remaining grounded in integrity and community support. By applying these teachings, one can cultivate a balanced and fulfilling life, much like the harmonious interplay of elements in nature.
