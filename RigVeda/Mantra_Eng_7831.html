मन्त्र: क्रत्वा दाक्षस्य रथ्यम् अपो वसानम् अन्धसा | गोषाम् अण्वेषु सश्चिम ||
kratvā dakṣasya rathyam apo vasānam andhasā | goṣām aṇveṣu saścima ||
  • kratvā: This word can be understood as "by the action" or "through the deed." It emphasizes the importance of actions in achieving results.
  • dakṣasya: This refers to "the skilled" or "the capable." It highlights the value of skill and competence in any endeavor.
  • rathyam: This term means "the path" or "the way." It signifies the journey one takes in life, which can be both literal and metaphorical.
  • apo: This word translates to "from the waters" or "from the source." It can symbolize nourishment, knowledge, or inspiration that comes from a deeper source.
  • vasānam: This means "dwelling" or "residing." It suggests a place of comfort or stability, where one can find peace and security.
  • andhasā: This term means "in darkness" or "blindness." It represents ignorance or lack of awareness, which can hinder progress.
  • goṣām: This refers to "of the cows" or "of the herd." In Vedic culture, cows symbolize wealth, nourishment, and prosperity.
  • aṇveṣu: This word means "in the small" or "in the lesser." It can indicate the importance of small things or details in the larger context of life.
  • saścima: This translates to "may it be so" or "let it be." It expresses a wish or intention for the desired outcome to manifest.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of skilled actions taken on the right path, drawing from deeper sources of knowledge and inspiration, while being aware of the potential ignorance that can cloud judgment. It highlights the importance of nurturing prosperity and recognizing the value in small details. In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as in personal development, professional endeavors, and community building. By focusing on our actions, honing our skills, and remaining aware of our surroundings, we can navigate through life's challenges and achieve our goals effectively.
