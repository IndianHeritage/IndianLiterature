आ वां रथं दुहितā सूर्यस्य कार्ष्मेवातिष्ठदर्वतā जयन्ती |विश्वे देवā अन्व अमन्यन्त हृद्भिः समु श्रियā नासत्यā सचेथे ||
ā vāṁ rathaṁ duhitā sūryasya kārṣmevātiṣṭhad arvatā jayantī | viśve devā anv amanyanta hṛdbhiḥ sam u śriyā nāsatyā sacethe ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā vāṁ: O you (plural)
    • rathaṁ: chariot
    • duhitā: daughter
    • sūryasya: of the Sun
    • kārṣmev: like the black (or dark) one
    • ātiṣṭhad: has risen
    • arvatā: from the strong
    • jayantī: victorious
    • viśve: all
    • devā: gods
    • anv: after
    • amanyanta: thought
    • hṛdbhiḥ: with hearts
    • sam: together
    • u: and
    • śriyā: with prosperity
    • nāsatyā: O Nāsatyas (the twin gods)
    • sacethe: may you be present
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the divine presence of the Sun and the gods, emphasizing the importance of unity and collective thought.
    • The "chariot of the Sun" symbolizes the journey of life, where the "daughter of the Sun" represents enlightenment and knowledge.
    • The phrase "like the black one" may refer to the duality of existence, where light and darkness coexist, reminding us of the balance in life.
    • The victorious aspect signifies overcoming challenges and achieving success through divine guidance and support.
    • The mention of "Nāsatyas" refers to the twin gods who embody healing and truth, suggesting that honesty and integrity are essential for prosperity.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a team setting, this mantra can be applied to emphasize the importance of collaboration and shared goals, where each member contributes to the collective success.
    • In personal development, the journey of self-discovery can be likened to the "chariot of the Sun," where one seeks knowledge and enlightenment to navigate life's challenges.
    • In community initiatives, invoking the spirit of unity and collective thought can lead to successful outcomes, as seen in grassroots movements that bring people together for a common cause.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of divine guidance, unity, and the pursuit of knowledge in achieving success and prosperity.
    • It serves as a reminder that by working together with integrity and a shared vision, individuals and communities can overcome obstacles and thrive.
    • In essence, it encourages us to seek enlightenment and support from the divine while fostering collaboration in our endeavors.
