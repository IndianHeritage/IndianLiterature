अस्मā एतन मह्य āङ्गूषम् अस्मā इन्द्राय स्तोत्रम् मतिभिर अवाचि | असद यथा महति वृततूर्य इन्द्रो विश्वायुर अवितā वृद्धश च ||
asmā etan mahy āṅgūṣam asmā indrāya stotram matibhir avāci | asad yathā mahati vṛtratūrya indro viśvāyur avitā vṛdhaś ca ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • asmā: for us
    • etan: this
    • mahy: for me
    • āṅgūṣam: the finger (symbolizing action or effort)
    • asmā: for us
    • indrāya: to Indra (the king of gods)
    • stotram: praise or hymn
    • matibhir: with thoughts or intentions
    • avāci: we speak or express
    • asad: as if not (negation)
    • yathā: as
    • mahati: great
    • vṛtratūrya: the slayer of Vritra (a demon representing obstacles)
    • indro: Indra
    • viśvāyur: all life or universal life
    • avitā: protector or guardian
    • vṛdhaś: great or powerful
    • ca: and
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer directed towards Indra, the god of rain and thunder, who is often invoked for strength and protection.
    • The phrase "asmā etan mahy āṅgūṣam" suggests a call for action and effort on behalf of the speaker and the community.
    • By praising Indra with "stotram matibhir avāci," the speaker emphasizes the importance of intention and thought in their prayers.
    • The reference to "vṛtratūrya" highlights Indra's role as a slayer of obstacles, symbolizing the removal of challenges in life.
    • Overall, the mantra conveys a deep sense of reliance on divine support for overcoming difficulties and achieving success.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a challenging work environment, individuals may invoke this mantra to seek strength and clarity in their efforts to overcome obstacles.
    • During times of personal struggle, such as health issues or emotional distress, one might reflect on this mantra to find courage and resilience.
    • In community gatherings, this mantra can serve as a reminder of collective strength and the importance of supporting one another in facing challenges.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for action, intention, and divine support in overcoming life's challenges.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal struggles to communal efforts, emphasizing the importance of unity and strength.
    • By invoking Indra's protection and guidance, individuals can find the courage to face obstacles and strive for success in their endeavors.
