तद इन नक्तं तद दिवा मह्यम् आहुस तद अयं केतो हृदा आ विचष्टे |शुनःशेपो यं अह्वद गृभीतः सो अस्मान राजा वरुणो मुमोकतु ||
tad in naktaṁ tad divā mahyam āhus tad ayaṁ keto hṛda ā vi caṣṭe | śunaḥśepo yam ahvad gṛbhītaḥ so asmān rājā varuṇo mumoktu ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • tad: that
    • in: in
    • naktaṁ: night
    • tad: that
    • divā: day
    • mahyam: to me
    • āhus: they say
    • tad: that
    • ayam: this
    • keto: of the mind
    • hṛda: heart
    • ā: towards
    • vi: indeed
    • caṣṭe: moves
    • śunaḥśepo: the one who is called Shunahshepa
    • yam: whom
    • ahvad: I invoke
    • gṛbhītaḥ: having been grasped
    • so: he
    • asmān: us
    • rājā: king
    • varuṇo: Varuna
    • mumoktu: may liberate
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the divine presence that exists both in the night and the day, suggesting a continuous connection with the divine.
    • The phrase "they say" indicates a shared wisdom or knowledge that is passed down through generations.
    • The reference to the mind and heart signifies the importance of inner awareness and emotional intelligence in understanding the divine.
    • Shunahshepa is a character from the Vedic texts who represents sacrifice and devotion, highlighting the theme of surrender to a higher power.
    • The invocation of Varuna, the king of cosmic order, emphasizes the need for guidance and liberation from worldly troubles.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, one can relate to the idea of seeking guidance during challenging times, much like invoking Varuna for support.
    • The mantra encourages mindfulness, reminding individuals to stay connected with their inner selves, akin to practices like meditation or yoga.
    • In moments of decision-making, reflecting on the wisdom of ancestors or teachings can provide clarity, similar to the shared knowledge mentioned in the mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the omnipresence of the divine in all aspects of life, urging individuals to seek connection and guidance.
    • It highlights the importance of inner awareness and emotional intelligence, which are crucial in navigating life's challenges.
    • In a broader context, it serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of all beings and the importance of surrendering to a higher power for liberation and peace.
