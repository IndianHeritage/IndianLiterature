इन्द्रा याहि तूतुजाना उप ब्रह्माणि हरिवः |सुते दधिष्व नश चनः ||
indrā yāhi tūtujāna upa brahmāṇi harivaḥ | sute dadhiṣva naś canaḥ ||
  • Indrā: Refers to Indra, the king of the gods in Vedic mythology, symbolizing strength, power, and leadership.
  • yāhi: A call to come or to approach, indicating a request or invocation.
  • tūtujāna: This term can be interpreted as one who is capable of overcoming obstacles or challenges.
  • upa: Means 'near' or 'towards', suggesting a closeness or a desire for connection.
  • brahmāṇi: Refers to the ultimate reality or the cosmic spirit, often associated with the source of creation.
  • harivaḥ: This can be understood as a call to the divine or auspicious forces, invoking their presence.
  • sute: Means 'to grant' or 'to bestow', indicating a desire for blessings or gifts.
  • dadhiṣva: This term suggests the act of nourishing or providing sustenance.
  • naś: Refers to 'us' or 'our', indicating a collective identity or community.
  • canaḥ: This can be interpreted as 'in any way' or 'in any manner', emphasizing the inclusivity of the request.

The mantra can be translated as: "O Indra, come near to us, you who are capable of overcoming challenges. Bestow upon us the blessings of the ultimate reality, and nourish us in every way."


Significance: This mantra emphasizes the importance of divine intervention and support in overcoming life's challenges. It reflects a deep yearning for connection with the divine and the ultimate reality, seeking blessings for sustenance and strength.


Real-Life Application: In everyday life, this mantra can be applied in various situations where individuals or communities face difficulties. For instance, during times of crisis, such as natural disasters or personal hardships, invoking the strength of a higher power can provide comfort and resilience. It can also be relevant in leadership contexts, where leaders seek guidance and support to navigate challenges effectively.


In conclusion, this mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness between the divine and human experience, encouraging individuals to seek strength and nourishment from higher powers in their journey through life.
