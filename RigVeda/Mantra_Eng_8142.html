मन्त्र: उता नो गोमतीर इषो विश्वा अर्ष परिष्टुभः |गृणानो जमदग्निना ||
uta no gomatīr iṣo viśvā arṣa pariṣṭubhaḥ | gṛṇāno jamadagninā ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • uta: and, also
    • no: our
    • gomatīr: possessing wealth or prosperity
    • iṣo: of the divine or the powerful
    • viśvā: all, universal
    • arṣa: sacred, revered
    • pariṣṭubhaḥ: surrounded, enveloped
    • gṛṇāno: singing, praising
    • jamadagninā: by the one who is like fire, possibly referring to a sage or a divine being
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of invoking the divine presence that brings prosperity and wealth.
    • The term "gomatīr" suggests a connection to abundance, which can be interpreted as both material wealth and spiritual richness.
    • The phrase "iṣo viśvā" indicates a universal power that encompasses all aspects of life, suggesting that the divine is present in every facet of existence.
    • "arṣa pariṣṭubhaḥ" implies a sacred environment that is enveloped by divine energy, highlighting the importance of creating a space that is conducive to spiritual growth.
    • The act of "gṛṇāno" signifies the importance of praise and gratitude, which are essential in acknowledging the divine forces at work in our lives.
    • "jamadagninā" can be seen as a reference to a sage or a divine being who embodies the qualities of fire—purity, transformation, and illumination.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be applied to the pursuit of success and prosperity. Just as one seeks to create a thriving enterprise, invoking the divine presence can be seen as a way to align one's efforts with universal energies that promote abundance.
    • In personal relationships, the idea of creating a sacred space can be reflected in nurturing environments that foster love, respect, and understanding, allowing individuals to grow together.
    • In spiritual practices, chanting this mantra can serve as a reminder to express gratitude for the blessings in life, reinforcing the connection to the divine and the importance of recognizing the abundance that surrounds us.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of invoking divine energies to attract prosperity and create a sacred environment.
    • It teaches us the value of gratitude and praise, encouraging us to recognize the abundance in our lives.
    • In real-life scenarios, whether in business, relationships, or spiritual practices, the principles embodied in this mantra can guide us toward a more fulfilling and prosperous existence.
