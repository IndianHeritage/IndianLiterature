देवेना नो मनसा देव सोम रायो भागं सहसावन्न अभि युध्य |मा त्वा तानद ईशिषे वीर्यस्योभयभ्यः प्र चिकीर्त्सा गविष्टौ ||
devena no manasā deva soma rāyo bhāgaṁ sahasāvann abhi yudhya | mā tvā tanad īśiṣe vīryasyobhayebhyaḥ pra cikitsā gaviṣṭau ||
  • Translation: The mantra can be translated as: "O Divine One, may we be endowed with the strength of the divine Soma, may we be victorious in our endeavors. Do not let us be deprived of the vigor of our actions, and may we find healing in our pursuits."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • devena: By the divine or through the divine.
    • manasā: With the mind or intellect.
    • deva: God or divine being.
    • soma: A sacred drink, often associated with immortality and divine inspiration.
    • rāyo: Wealth or prosperity.
    • bhāgaṁ: Share or portion.
    • sahasāvann: With great strength or power.
    • abhi yudhya: Engaging in battle or striving.
    • mā: Not or do not.
    • tvā: You.
    • tanad: Deprived or taken away.
    • īśiṣe: Of strength or power.
    • vīryasyobhayebhyaḥ: From both sides of strength or vigor.
    • pra cikitsā: Healing or remedy.
    • gaviṣṭau: In the pursuit or endeavor.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a competitive work environment, individuals often seek divine guidance or inspiration to achieve their goals. This mantra can be recited to invoke strength and clarity of mind.
    • During challenging times, such as illness or personal loss, people may turn to spiritual practices for healing and resilience, reflecting the healing aspect of the mantra.
    • In sports, athletes often seek mental fortitude and physical strength to overcome their opponents, paralleling the call for divine strength in the mantra.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its invocation of divine strength and healing. It emphasizes the importance of mental clarity, physical vigor, and the pursuit of prosperity. In real-life scenarios, whether in personal challenges, professional endeavors, or competitive situations, this mantra serves as a reminder to seek divine support and maintain resilience. By aligning oneself with these divine qualities, individuals can navigate life's battles with greater confidence and strength.
