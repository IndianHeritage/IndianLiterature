युञ्जन्ति ब्रह्मन् अरुषं चरन्तं परि तस्थुषः |रोचन्ते रोचना दिवि ||
yuñjanti bradhnam aruṣaṁ carantam pari tasthuṣaḥ | rocante rocanā divi ||
  • Mantra: "yuñjanti bradhnam aruṣaṁ carantam pari tasthuṣaḥ | rocante rocanā divi ||"
  • Translation: "They unite the great, the red one that moves around; the shining ones shine in the sky."
  • Word-by-Word Explanation:
    • yuñjanti: They unite or connect.
    • bradhnam: The great or the powerful.
    • aruṣaṁ: The red one, often symbolizing energy or vitality.
    • carantam: Moving or wandering.
    • pari: Around or in all directions.
    • tasthuṣaḥ: Of the shining or luminous ones.
    • rocante: They shine or radiate.
    • rocanā: The shining or bright ones.
    • divi: In the sky or heaven.
  • Detailed Explanation:
    • This mantra speaks to the concept of unity and the interconnectedness of all things. The "great" and "red one" can be interpreted as a metaphor for the sun or a powerful force in nature that brings life and energy.
    • The phrase "moving around" suggests the dynamic nature of life and the universe, where everything is in constant motion and change.
    • The "shining ones" in the sky can refer to celestial bodies, such as stars and planets, which have been a source of inspiration and guidance for humanity throughout history.
    • In real life, this mantra can be related to the idea of collaboration and teamwork. Just as the elements of nature work together harmoniously, individuals can achieve greater success when they unite their strengths and efforts.
    • For example, in a workplace setting, when team members collaborate effectively, they can create innovative solutions and achieve common goals, much like the "shining ones" illuminating the sky.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of unity, collaboration, and the dynamic nature of existence. It serves as a reminder that we are all interconnected and that our collective efforts can lead to greater achievements.
    • In real-life scenarios, whether in personal relationships, community projects, or professional environments, embracing the spirit of cooperation and recognizing the value of each individual's contribution can lead to success and fulfillment.
    • Ultimately, the mantra encourages us to shine together, just as the stars do in the night sky, creating a brighter and more harmonious world.
