उपस्तुतिर औचत्यं उरुष्येन मां मां इमे पतत्रिणी वि दुग्धाम् |मा मां एधो दशतयश चितो धाक प्र यद वाम बद्धस त्मनि खादति क्षाम ||
upastutir aucathyam uruṣyen mā mām ime patatriṇī vi dugdhām | mā mām edho daśatayaś cito dhāk pra yad vām baddhas tmani khādati kṣām ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • upastutir: Praise or worship
    • aucathyam: Excellence or virtue
    • uruṣyen: By the great or powerful
    • mā mām: Do not harm me
    • ime: These
    • patatriṇī: Birds or winged beings
    • vi dugdhām: Who are nourished or fed
    • mā mām edho: Do not let me be harmed
    • daśatayaś: By the tenfold or in many ways
    • cito: With thought or intention
    • dhāk: Cover or protect
    • pra: Indeed or truly
    • yad: When
    • vām: You
    • baddhas: Bound or tied
    • tmani: In the self or within
    • khādati: Eats or consumes
    • kṣām: Patience or endurance
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra expresses a plea for protection and nourishment from the divine or powerful beings.
    • The speaker seeks to be free from harm, particularly from those who may wish to cause injury or distress.
    • The mention of "birds" can symbolize freedom, spiritual elevation, or the soul's journey, indicating a desire for spiritual upliftment.
    • The phrase "do not harm me" emphasizes the importance of safety and well-being in one's life.
    • The reference to being "bound" suggests the constraints or challenges one faces, and the need for inner strength to overcome them.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate environment, employees often seek protection from workplace politics and strive for a nurturing atmosphere where they can thrive.
    • Parents may recite this mantra for their children, wishing for their safety and success in life, free from negative influences.
    • Individuals facing personal challenges, such as health issues or emotional distress, may find solace in this mantra, seeking divine support and resilience.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the need for protection and nourishment in various aspects of life.
    • It highlights the significance of seeking divine support in overcoming challenges and maintaining inner strength.
    • In real-life scenarios, it can be applied in personal prayers, meditation practices, or during moments of uncertainty, reinforcing the belief in a higher power that provides guidance and safety.
