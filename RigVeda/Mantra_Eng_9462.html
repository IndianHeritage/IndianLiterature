मन्त्र: क्रतूयन्ति क्रतवो हृत्सु धीतयः वेनन्ति वेनाः पतयन्त्यादिशः |न मर्दिता विद्यते अन्य एभ्यो देवेषु मे अधि कामा अयंषत ||
kratūyanti kratavo hṛtsu dhītayo venanti venāḥ patayanty ā diśaḥ | na marḍitā vidyate anya ebhyo deveṣu me adhi kāmā ayaṁsata ||
  • Mantra: "kratūyanti kratavo hṛtsu dhītayo venanti venāḥ patayanty ā diśaḥ | na marḍitā vidyate anya ebhyo deveṣu me adhi kāmā ayaṁsata ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • kratūyanti: They (the powers or energies) act or perform.
    • kratavo: The energies or powers.
    • hṛtsu: In the hearts or minds.
    • dhītayo: The thoughts or intellects.
    • venanti: They lead or guide.
    • venāḥ: The guiding forces.
    • patayanty: They spread or extend.
    • ā diśaḥ: In all directions.
    • na marḍitā: Not to be disturbed or agitated.
    • vidyate: Is found or exists.
    • anya: Other or different.
    • ebhyo: From these (deities or forces).
    • deveṣu: Among the gods or divine beings.
    • me: To me.
    • adhi: Over or above.
    • kāmā: Desires or wishes.
    • ayaṁsata: May they be fulfilled or granted.
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra speaks about the powers and energies that act within our hearts and minds, guiding our thoughts and extending their influence in all directions.
    • It emphasizes the importance of inner strength and clarity of thought, suggesting that when our minds are clear and focused, we can effectively channel our desires and intentions.
    • The phrase "na marḍitā vidyate" indicates a state of tranquility and stability, suggesting that true fulfillment of desires comes from a place of calmness and not from external disturbances.
    • In real life, this can be related to situations where individuals find clarity in their goals and aspirations, leading to successful outcomes. For example, a student preparing for exams may find that a calm and focused mind helps them absorb information better and perform well.
    • Similarly, in professional settings, leaders who maintain a clear vision and calm demeanor can inspire their teams to achieve collective goals, demonstrating the power of focused intention.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of inner strength, clarity of thought, and the power of focused intention in achieving our desires.
    • It teaches us that by cultivating a calm and stable mind, we can effectively navigate the challenges of life and manifest our aspirations.
    • In practical terms, this mantra can be applied in various aspects of life, including education, career, and personal relationships, where clarity and focus are essential for success.
    • Ultimately, it serves as a reminder that our inner state greatly influences our external reality, and by nurturing our thoughts and intentions, we can create a fulfilling life.
