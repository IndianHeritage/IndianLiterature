क्षेत्रम् इव विममुस तेजनेनम् एकं पात्रम् ऋभवो जेहमानम् | उपस्तुताः उपमं नाधमानाः अमर्त्येषु श्रव इच्छमानाः ||
kṣetram iva vi mamus tejanenam̐ ekam pātram ṛbhavo jehamānam | upastutā upamaṁ nādhamānā amartyeṣu śrava icchamānāḥ ||
  • kṣetram iva vi mamus tejanenam̐: This phrase can be translated as "like a field, we are filled with energy." Here, "kṣetram" refers to a field, symbolizing a space of potential and growth. In real life, this can be likened to a fertile land where crops grow, representing the potential within individuals to cultivate their talents and abilities.
  • ekam pātram ṛbhavo jehamānam: This translates to "one vessel, filled with the essence of the wise." The "vessel" symbolizes the individual or community that holds knowledge and wisdom. For example, a classroom or a community center can be seen as a vessel where knowledge is shared and wisdom is cultivated.
  • upastutā upamaṁ nādhamānā: This means "praising the analogy, not diminishing it." It emphasizes the importance of recognizing and valuing the comparisons we make in life. For instance, when we compare our struggles to those of others, we can find strength and inspiration, rather than feeling diminished by our challenges.
  • amartyeṣu śrava icchamānāḥ: This translates to "desiring the fame among the immortal." Here, "amartyeṣu" refers to the immortal beings, which can symbolize ideals or values that transcend time. In a real-life context, this can relate to individuals striving for greatness in their fields, seeking to leave a lasting legacy through their contributions.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of growth, knowledge, and the pursuit of excellence. It signifies the importance of nurturing our potential, sharing wisdom, and striving for recognition in a way that uplifts rather than diminishes others. In real life, this can be applied in educational settings, workplaces, and personal development journeys, where individuals are encouraged to cultivate their skills, share knowledge, and aspire to make a meaningful impact in their communities and beyond.
