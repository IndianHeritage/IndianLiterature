स्वस्ति नः पथ्यासु धन्वसु स्वस्त्य अप्सु वृजनें स्वरवति |स्वस्ति नः पुत्रकृतहेषु योनिषु स्वस्ति राये मरुतो दधातन ||
svasti naḥ pathyāsu dhanvasu svasty a1psu vṛjane svarvati | svasti naḥ putrakṛtheṣu yoniṣu svasti rāye maruto dadhātana ||
  • svasti naḥ: This phrase translates to "may there be well-being for us." It expresses a universal wish for health and prosperity.
  • pathyāsu: This word means "on the paths" or "in our journeys." It signifies the importance of safe and auspicious travels in life.
  • dhanvasu: This refers to "in wealth" or "in resources." It highlights the need for abundance and prosperity in our lives.
  • svasty a1psu: This can be interpreted as "in the waters" or "in the sources of nourishment." It emphasizes the significance of water and sustenance for life.
  • vṛjane: This means "in the act of overcoming" or "in the process of removal of obstacles." It suggests the importance of overcoming challenges in life.
  • svarvati: This translates to "may it shine" or "may it be bright." It indicates a desire for clarity and enlightenment in our endeavors.
  • putrakṛtheṣu: This phrase means "in the actions of our children" or "in the endeavors of progeny." It reflects the hope for the success and well-being of future generations.
  • yoniṣu: This refers to "in the wombs" or "in the sources of life." It signifies the importance of family and lineage.
  • svasti rāye: This means "may there be well-being in wealth." It emphasizes the connection between prosperity and well-being.
  • maruto dadhātana: This translates to "may the Maruts (storm deities) bestow." The Maruts are associated with strength and vitality, suggesting a blessing of energy and power.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda encapsulates a holistic vision of well-being that encompasses personal health, prosperity, safe journeys, and the success of future generations. It is applicable in real-life scenarios such as family gatherings, rituals for prosperity, or even during significant life events like weddings and childbirth. By invoking these blessings, individuals and communities can foster an environment of positivity, resilience, and abundance, ultimately leading to a harmonious existence. The mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of our lives and the importance of seeking blessings for ourselves and our loved ones.
