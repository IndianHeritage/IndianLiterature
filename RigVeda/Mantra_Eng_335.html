योगे-योगे तवस्तरं वाजे-वाजे हवामहे |सखाय इन्द्रं ऊतये ||
yoge-yoge tavastaraṁ vāje-vāje havāmahe | sakhāya indram ūtaye ||
  • Mantra: "yoge-yoge tavastaraṁ vāje-vāje havāmahe | sakhāya indram ūtaye ||"
  • Translation: "In every yoga, we invoke your strength; in every effort, we call upon you, O Indra, our friend."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • yoge-yoge: Refers to "in every yoga" or "in every practice." This emphasizes the importance of consistent effort and dedication in various aspects of life.
    • tavastaraṁ: Means "your strength" or "your power." It signifies the divine energy or support that one seeks in their endeavors.
    • vāje-vāje: Translates to "in every effort" or "in every action." This highlights the need for divine assistance in all our pursuits.
    • havāmahe: Means "we invoke" or "we call upon." It indicates a plea or request for help and guidance.
    • sakhāya: Means "friend" or "companion." It denotes a close relationship with the divine, suggesting that one can rely on this friendship for support.
    • indram: Refers to Indra, the king of gods in Vedic tradition, symbolizing strength, power, and leadership.
    • ūtaye: Means "to bestow" or "to grant." It signifies the act of receiving blessings or assistance from the divine.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, when embarking on a new project, individuals often seek guidance and support from mentors or colleagues, akin to invoking Indra's strength in their efforts.
    • During challenging times, such as personal loss or health issues, people may turn to spiritual practices like yoga or meditation, calling upon a higher power for strength and resilience.
    • In sports, athletes often rely on their training (yoga) and seek motivation (strength) from their coaches or teammates (friends) to perform at their best.
  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of seeking divine support in all aspects of life, whether in personal growth, professional endeavors, or spiritual practices. It teaches us that through consistent effort and a strong connection with the divine, we can overcome challenges and achieve our goals. This invocation of strength and friendship with the divine can be applied in various real-life scenarios, reminding us that we are not alone in our journeys and that support is always available if we seek it earnestly.
