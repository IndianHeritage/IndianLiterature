मन्त्र: विश्वा रूपाणि प्रति मुञ्चते कविः प्रासवीद भद्रं द्विपदे चतुष्पदे | विनाकम् अख्यात सविता वरेण्योऽनु प्रयाणम् उषसो वि राजति ||
viśvā rūpāṇi prati muñcate kaviḥ prāsāvīd bhadraṁ dvipade catuṣpade | vi nākam akhyat savitā vareṇyo 'nu prayāṇam uṣaso vi rājati ||
  • viśvā rūpāṇi prati muñcate: This phrase translates to "the poet (kavi) reveals the universal forms." Here, "viśvā" means universal or all-encompassing, and "rūpāṇi" refers to forms or manifestations. The poet, through his creativity, brings forth various aspects of existence.
  • kaviḥ prāsāvīd bhadraṁ dvipade catuṣpade: This means "the poet has given birth to auspiciousness for both bipeds and quadrupeds." The term "kaviḥ" refers to the poet or seer, while "bhadraṁ" signifies auspiciousness or goodness. This line emphasizes the role of creativity and expression in fostering well-being for all living beings, whether they walk on two legs (humans) or four (animals).
  • vi nākam akhyat savitā vareṇyo: This translates to "the sun (Savitā) is the one who reveals the sky." "Nākam" refers to the sky or heaven, and "Savitā" is a name for the sun, symbolizing enlightenment and knowledge. This line highlights the sun's role in illuminating the world, both literally and metaphorically, as a source of wisdom and clarity.
  • 'anu prayāṇam uṣaso vi rājati: This means "the dawn (uṣasa) shines forth in its journey." "Uṣasa" represents the dawn, symbolizing new beginnings and hope. The phrase suggests that with each new day, there is a journey towards enlightenment and progress.

Conclusion:

  • The mantra emphasizes the power of creativity and expression in shaping the world around us. Just as a poet brings forth various forms of existence, individuals can contribute positively to society through their unique talents and perspectives.
  • It highlights the interconnectedness of all living beings, reminding us that our actions can foster well-being for both humans and animals.
  • The sun symbolizes knowledge and enlightenment, encouraging us to seek wisdom and clarity in our lives.
  • The dawn represents new beginnings, urging us to embrace each day as an opportunity for growth and progress.

This mantra is applicable in real-life scenarios such as creative endeavors, community service, and personal development. It serves as a reminder to harness our creativity for the greater good, seek knowledge, and embrace new opportunities with optimism.
