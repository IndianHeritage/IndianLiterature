तां जिघ्वया सदम एदं सुमेधा आ यद वां सत्यो अरतिर ऋते भूत |तद वां महित्वं घृतान्नाव अस्तु युवं दाशुषे वि चयिष्टम अंहः ||
tā jihvayā sadam edaṁ sumedhā ā yad vāṁ satyo aratir ṛte bhūt | tad vām mahitvaṁ ghṛtānnāv astu yuvaṁ dāśuṣe vi cayiṣṭam aṁhaḥ ||
  • tā jihvayā: This phrase can be translated as "that which is spoken" or "through the tongue." It emphasizes the power of speech and communication.
  • sadam edaṁ: This means "this is the truth" or "this is the essence." It signifies the importance of truthfulness in our expressions.
  • sumedhā: Translated as "with good intellect" or "wise," it highlights the value of wisdom in our thoughts and actions.
  • ā yad vāṁ: This phrase means "whatever you desire" or "whatever you wish for." It reflects the aspirations and goals we hold.
  • satyo aratir: This translates to "the true path" or "the righteous way." It emphasizes the importance of following a path of truth and righteousness.
  • ṛte bhūt: Meaning "without falsehood" or "beyond illusion," it stresses the need for authenticity in our lives.
  • tad vām mahitvaṁ: This means "that greatness of yours" or "your esteemed nature." It acknowledges the inherent greatness within individuals.
  • ghṛtānnāv astu: Translated as "may there be nourishment" or "may there be sustenance," it signifies the need for physical and spiritual nourishment.
  • yuvaṁ dāśuṣe: This phrase means "for the young" or "for the youth," indicating a focus on the younger generation and their potential.
  • vi cayiṣṭam aṁhaḥ: This translates to "may there be strength" or "may there be vigor," emphasizing the importance of vitality and energy in life.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda encapsulates the essence of communication, truth, wisdom, and the pursuit of righteousness. It serves as a reminder of the power of our words and thoughts, urging us to speak truthfully and wisely. In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as in education, where teachers inspire students to seek knowledge and truth, or in personal relationships, where honest communication fosters trust and understanding. Ultimately, it encourages individuals to recognize their inherent greatness and strive for a life filled with purpose, nourishment, and vitality.
