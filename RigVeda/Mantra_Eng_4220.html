तā हि श्रेष्टवरचसā राजानā दीर्घश्रुत्तमā | तā सत्पतī ऋतावृद्ध ऋतावानā जन-जनē ||
tā hi śreṣṭhavarcasā rājānā dīrghaśruttamā | tā satpatī ṛtāvṛdha ṛtāvānā jane-jane ||
  • tā hi: This phrase translates to "they indeed" or "those indeed," indicating a reference to a group or concept that is being emphasized.
  • śreṣṭhavarcasā: This term means "of the highest brilliance" or "of the best radiance." It signifies excellence and superiority, often associated with qualities that are admirable and worthy of respect.
  • rājānā: This word translates to "kings" or "rulers." It refers to those in positions of authority and leadership, highlighting the importance of wise and just governance.
  • dīrghaśruttamā: This means "long-lasting fame" or "renowned for a long time." It suggests that the actions or qualities of the subject will be remembered and celebrated over time.
  • tā: Again, this refers to "they" or "those," reinforcing the focus on the previously mentioned subjects.
  • satpatī: This term means "the good path" or "the righteous way." It emphasizes the importance of following a moral and ethical path in life.
  • ṛtāvṛdha: This translates to "increasing righteousness" or "growing truth." It signifies the idea of nurturing and promoting truth and justice in society.
  • ṛtāvānā: This means "those who possess truth" or "truthful ones." It highlights the value of integrity and honesty among individuals.
  • jane-jane: This phrase translates to "in every person" or "among all people." It emphasizes the universality of the qualities being discussed, suggesting that they should be present in everyone.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of excellence, righteousness, and truth in leadership and among individuals. It calls for rulers to embody these qualities, ensuring that their legacy is one of long-lasting fame and respect. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied to the importance of ethical leadership in politics, business, and community service. Leaders who prioritize integrity and righteousness inspire others to follow suit, creating a society where truth and justice thrive. This mantra serves as a reminder that the qualities of good governance and personal integrity are essential for the well-being of all individuals in society.
