वि श्रयन्ताम ऋतावृधो द्वारो देवीर असश्चतः |अद्यā नूनं च यष्टवे ||
vi śrayantām ṛtāvṛdho dvāro devīr asaścataḥ | adyā nūnaṁ ca yaṣṭave ||
  • vi śrayantām: This phrase can be translated as "to those who are dependent" or "to those who seek refuge." It signifies the importance of seeking support and guidance from higher powers or divine entities.
  • ṛtāvṛdho: This term refers to "the one who is established in truth" or "the one who upholds righteousness." It emphasizes the value of truth and moral integrity in our lives.
  • dvāro: This word means "the door" or "the gateway." It symbolizes opportunities and transitions in life, suggesting that we must be aware of the paths we choose.
  • devīr: This translates to "goddesses" or "divine beings." It indicates the presence of divine forces that guide and protect us in our endeavors.
  • asaścataḥ: This phrase can be interpreted as "may they be favorable" or "may they grant us blessings." It reflects the desire for positive outcomes and support from the divine.
  • adyā: This word means "today" or "in the present." It emphasizes the importance of the current moment and the actions we take now.
  • nūnaṁ: This term translates to "indeed" or "certainly," reinforcing the certainty of the statement that follows.
  • ca: This is a conjunction meaning "and," linking the ideas together.
  • yaṣṭave: This word means "to the one who is being worshipped" or "to the one who is being offered sacrifices." It signifies devotion and the act of seeking blessings through worship.

In summary, this mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking refuge in divine forces while upholding truth and righteousness. It highlights the significance of being aware of the opportunities that life presents and the need for divine blessings in our endeavors. The mantra encourages individuals to act in the present moment with devotion and integrity.


Significance and Real-Life Application:

  • This mantra can be applied in daily life by encouraging individuals to seek guidance from their spiritual beliefs or practices when faced with challenges.
  • It serves as a reminder to act with integrity and truthfulness in all aspects of life, whether in personal relationships, professional settings, or community interactions.
  • The concept of recognizing opportunities (the "door") can be related to career choices, personal growth, and decision-making processes.
  • In moments of uncertainty, individuals can invoke this mantra to seek blessings and support from the divine, fostering a sense of peace and assurance.
