य ऋष्वा ऋष्टविद्युतः कवयः santi वेधसः |तम ऋषे मारुतं गणं नमस्यā रमया गिरा ||
ya ṛṣvā ṛṣṭividyutaḥ kavayaḥ santi vedhasaḥ | tam ṛṣe mārutaṁ gaṇaṁ namasyā ramayā girā ||
  • ya ṛṣvā ṛṣṭividyutaḥ kavayaḥ santi vedhasaḥ
    • ya - "who" or "that" refers to a subject or entity.
    • ṛṣvā - "the seers" or "the sages," indicating wise individuals with deep knowledge.
    • ṛṣṭividyutaḥ - "possessing knowledge of the truth," suggesting enlightenment and understanding.
    • kavayaḥ - "the poets" or "the wise ones," referring to those who express profound truths through creativity.
    • santi - "are" or "exist," indicating presence or existence.
    • vedhasaḥ - "of the Vedas," connecting the knowledge to the sacred texts of Hinduism.
  • tam ṛṣe mārutaṁ gaṇaṁ namasyā ramayā girā
    • tam - "that" or "him," referring to a specific entity or divine presence.
    • ṛṣe - "of the sage," indicating reverence towards a wise figure.
    • mārutaṁ - "the wind" or "the storm," symbolizing power and movement.
    • gaṇaṁ - "the group" or "the assembly," suggesting a collective of beings or forces.
    • namasyā - "we bow" or "we salute," indicating respect and reverence.
    • ramayā - "with joy" or "with delight," suggesting a positive and uplifting attitude.
    • girā - "with words" or "through speech," emphasizing the importance of communication and expression.

Conclusion:

This mantra emphasizes the reverence for the sages and poets who possess profound knowledge and understanding of the Vedas. It highlights the importance of wisdom, creativity, and the collective power of nature, represented by the wind. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios such as:

  • Recognizing and respecting the contributions of thinkers, artists, and leaders in society.
  • Valuing the pursuit of knowledge and truth in personal and professional life.
  • Understanding the significance of nature and its forces, and how they influence our lives.

Ultimately, this mantra serves as a reminder to honor wisdom and creativity, and to approach life with respect and joy in our expressions and interactions.
