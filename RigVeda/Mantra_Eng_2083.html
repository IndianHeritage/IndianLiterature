अग्नेर इन्द्रस्य सोमस्य देवानाम् ऊतिभिर वयम् |अरीष्यन्तः सचेमह्य अभि ष्यामा पृथन्यतः ||
agner indrasya somasya devānām ūtibhir vayam | ariṣyantaḥ sacemahy abhi ṣyāma pṛtanyataḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • agner: of fire
    • indrasya: of Indra (the king of gods)
    • somasya: of Soma (a sacred drink)
    • devānām: of the gods
    • ūtibhir: with strength or vigor
    • vayam: we
    • ariṣyantaḥ: who are free from enemies
    • sacemahy: may we be strong
    • abhi: towards
    • ṣyāma: may we be protected
    • pṛtanyataḥ: from all directions
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the divine forces of fire, Indra, and Soma, seeking their blessings for strength and protection.
    • It emphasizes the importance of unity and collective strength among individuals, as indicated by the use of "we" (vayam).
    • The phrase "ariṣyantaḥ" suggests a state of being free from adversities or enemies, which can be interpreted as a desire for peace and harmony.
    • The mantra calls for divine protection from all directions, highlighting the need for comprehensive safety and security in life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community setting, this mantra can be recited during gatherings or festivals to invoke blessings for collective strength and harmony among the members.
    • In personal life, individuals may chant this mantra during challenging times to seek protection and strength from adversities.
    • In corporate environments, teams can use this mantra to foster a sense of unity and collective purpose, especially during project launches or critical meetings.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of divine support in achieving strength and protection in life.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal challenges to community gatherings, emphasizing the need for unity and divine blessings.
    • By invoking these divine energies, individuals and communities can aspire to overcome obstacles and foster a harmonious existence.
