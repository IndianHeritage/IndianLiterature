सम एनम् अहृताः इमा गिरो अर्षन्ति ससृतः |धेनूर वाश्रो अवीवशत् ||
sam enam ahrutā imā giro arṣanti sasrutaḥ | dhenūr vāśro avīvaśat ||
  • Mantra: "sam enam ahrutā imā giro arṣanti sasrutaḥ | dhenūr vāśro avīvaśat ||"
  • Translation: This mantra can be translated as: "May these words, which are well-formed and harmonious, reach us. The cow, the source of nourishment, is ever-present and beneficial."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • sam: Together, in harmony.
    • enam: This (referring to the words or sounds).
    • ahrutā: Well-formed, well-structured.
    • imā: These (referring to the words).
    • giro: Words, sounds, or speech.
    • arṣanti: Reach, arrive, or come to us.
    • sasrutaḥ: Well-heard, understood.
    • dhenūr: Cow, symbolizing nourishment and sustenance.
    • vāśro: Ever-present, beneficial.
    • avīvaśat: To be present, to be beneficial.
  • Contextual Explanation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of harmonious communication and the power of words. In our daily lives, effective communication is crucial in various settings, such as in personal relationships, workplaces, and community interactions.
    • The cow, a symbol of nourishment in many cultures, represents the sustenance we receive from nature and the importance of gratitude towards it. For example, in agricultural societies, cows provide milk, which is a vital source of nutrition.
    • The mantra suggests that when words are well-formed and harmonious, they can lead to understanding and connection among individuals. This can be seen in practices like conflict resolution, where clear and respectful communication can help resolve disputes.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for harmonious communication and the recognition of the nourishing aspects of life, represented by the cow.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as fostering better relationships through effective communication, promoting gratitude for the resources we have, and encouraging a sense of community and support.
    • Ultimately, it serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of words, nourishment, and the importance of harmony in our lives.
