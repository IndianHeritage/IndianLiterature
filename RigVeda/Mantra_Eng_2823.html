दिवश्चिदापूर्व्याज्जायमानाविजागृविरविदथे शस्यमानाः |भद्रावस्त्राण्यर्जुनावसाना सेयमस्मे सञ्जातपितृयाः धीः ||
divaś cid ā pūrvyā jāyamānā vi jāgṛvir vidathe śasyamānā | bhadrā vastrāṇy arjunā vasānā seyam asme sanajā pitryā dhīḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • divaś: of the heavens
    • cid: indeed
    • ā: from
    • pūrvyā: the east
    • jāyamānā: being born
    • vi: in various ways
    • jāgṛvir: those who are awake
    • vidathe: in the assembly
    • śasyamānā: being nourished
    • bhadrā: auspicious
    • vastrāṇy: garments
    • arjunā: bright or pure
    • vasānā: wearing
    • seyam: this
    • asme: for us
    • sanajā: born from
    • pitryā: the ancestors
    • dhīḥ: wisdom or intellect
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the auspiciousness that comes from the heavens, particularly from the east, symbolizing new beginnings and enlightenment.
    • The phrase "being born in various ways" suggests the diversity of experiences and perspectives that contribute to wisdom.
    • The reference to "those who are awake" emphasizes the importance of awareness and consciousness in our lives.
    • "In the assembly" indicates the significance of community and collaboration in achieving common goals.
    • The mantra also highlights the nourishment that comes from auspicious garments, symbolizing the blessings and protection we receive from our ancestors.
    • The mention of "wisdom" signifies the importance of learning from the past and applying that knowledge to our present and future.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, this mantra can be applied to the importance of teamwork and collaboration, where diverse perspectives lead to innovative solutions.
    • In personal life, it can remind individuals to stay aware and conscious of their surroundings, making informed decisions that honor their heritage and values.
    • In educational contexts, it emphasizes the importance of learning from history and the wisdom of previous generations to guide future actions.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of life, the importance of community, and the wisdom that comes from our ancestors.
    • Its significance lies in encouraging individuals to embrace diversity, remain aware, and seek nourishment from both the past and the present.
    • In real-life scenarios, it can inspire us to foster collaboration, respect our heritage, and strive for a brighter future through collective wisdom.
