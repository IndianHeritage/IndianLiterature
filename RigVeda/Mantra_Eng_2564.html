अभि प्रयांसी वाहसां दाश्वां अशनोति mortalः |क्षयम् पावकशोचिषः ||
abhi prayāṁsi vāhasā dāśvām̐ aśnoti martyaḥ | kṣayam pāvakaśociṣaḥ ||
  • Mantra: "abhi prayāṁsi vāhasā dāśvām̐ aśnoti martyaḥ | kṣayam pāvakaśociṣaḥ ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • abhi: towards, in the direction of
    • prayāṁsi: the goals or objectives
    • vāhasā: with the help of vehicles or means
    • dāśvām̐: the ten (often refers to the ten directions or aspects)
    • aśnoti: attains or achieves
    • martyaḥ: the mortal or human being
    • kṣayam: destruction or decline
    • pāvaka: fire or purification
    • śociṣaḥ: shining or illuminating
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the journey of a human being towards achieving their goals.
    • The phrase "abhi prayāṁsi vāhasā" suggests that with the right means or vehicles, one can reach their objectives.
    • The term "dāśvām̐" can be interpreted as encompassing all directions, indicating that the pursuit of goals is holistic and requires attention to various aspects of life.
    • "aśnoti martyaḥ" emphasizes that it is the human who can attain these goals, highlighting human agency and effort.
    • The latter part, "kṣayam pāvakaśociṣaḥ," warns of the destructive forces that can arise if one does not remain vigilant and focused on their path.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional context, an individual may set career goals (prayāṁsi) and utilize various resources (vāhasā) such as education, mentorship, and networking to achieve success (aśnoti martyaḥ).
    • In personal development, one might aim for holistic growth by addressing mental, physical, and emotional well-being (dāśvām̐) while being aware of distractions or negative influences (kṣayam).
    • In environmental contexts, the mantra can be seen as a reminder to pursue sustainable practices (pāvaka) that illuminate our path towards a healthier planet (śociṣaḥ).
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the importance of setting clear goals and utilizing the right means to achieve them.
    • It emphasizes the need for awareness of potential pitfalls and the importance of purification and illumination in our pursuits.
    • In real life, it encourages individuals to take charge of their destinies while remaining mindful of the broader impact of their actions.
