यत् ते दिवं यत् पृथिवीं मनो जगाम दूरकम् |तत् ता आ वर्तयामसिह क्षयाय जीवसे ||
yat te divaṁ yat pṛthivīm mano jagāma dūrakam | tat ta ā vartayāmasīha kṣayāya jīvase ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yat: that which
    • te: of you
    • dvaṁ: heaven
    • yat: that which
    • pṛthivīm: earth
    • mano: mind
    • jagāma: has gone
    • dūrakam: far away
    • tat: that
    • ta: that
    • ā: indeed
    • vartayāmasi: we are moving
    • ha: indeed
    • kṣayāya: for destruction
    • jīvase: for life
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks to the connection between the heavens and the earth, emphasizing the role of the mind in bridging the two.
    • The phrase "yat te divaṁ yat pṛthivīm" suggests a unity between the celestial and terrestrial realms, indicating that both are essential for existence.
    • "mano jagāma dūrakam" implies that the mind can transcend physical boundaries, exploring realms beyond immediate perception.
    • The latter part, "tat ta ā vartayāmasīha kṣayāya jīvase," indicates that our actions and thoughts should align with the purpose of sustaining life rather than leading to destruction.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern life, this mantra can be reflected in the pursuit of knowledge and wisdom. For instance, scientists and philosophers often seek to understand the universe (heavens) and our place within it (earth).
    • In environmental conservation, the mantra serves as a reminder that our actions should protect the earth and promote life, rather than contribute to its destruction.
    • In personal development, the idea of the mind transcending boundaries can be seen in practices like meditation, where individuals seek to connect with a higher consciousness or purpose.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of harmony between the spiritual and material worlds, urging individuals to use their minds to foster life and avoid destruction.
    • Its significance lies in its call for mindfulness in our actions and thoughts, reminding us that we are part of a larger cosmic order.
    • In real-life scenarios, it encourages us to seek balance in our pursuits, whether in personal growth, environmental stewardship, or the quest for knowledge.
