प्र मे नमī साप्य इषे भुजे भूद गवाम एषे सख्यā कृतु द्वितā |दिद्युं यद अस्य समिथेषु मंहयम् आद इद एनं शंस्यम उक्त्यम करम् ||
pra me namī sāpya iṣe bhuje bhūd gavām eṣe sakhyā kṛṇuta dvitā | didyuṁ yad asya samitheṣu maṁhayam ād id enaṁ śaṁsyam ukthyaṁ karam ||
  • pra me namī: This phrase can be interpreted as "may I be honored" or "may I be revered." It signifies a request for respect and acknowledgment from the divine or the community.
  • sāpya iṣe bhuje: This translates to "in the presence of the one who is worthy." It emphasizes the importance of recognizing and honoring those who are deserving of respect, such as elders, teachers, or leaders.
  • bhūd gavām eṣe: This means "may there be abundance in the herds." It reflects a desire for prosperity and well-being, particularly in terms of livestock, which was a significant aspect of wealth in ancient societies.
  • sakhyā kṛṇuta dvitā: This can be understood as "may friendship be established." It highlights the value of relationships and alliances, suggesting that cooperation and camaraderie are essential for a thriving community.
  • didyuṁ yad asya samitheṣu: This phrase translates to "may there be brilliance in gatherings." It signifies the importance of collective wisdom and the illumination that comes from shared knowledge and experiences.
  • maṁhayam ād id enaṁ: This means "may this be a source of strength." It emphasizes the need for support and empowerment within the community, encouraging individuals to uplift one another.
  • śaṁsyam ukthyaṁ karam: This translates to "may the actions be praiseworthy." It calls for integrity and virtue in one's deeds, urging individuals to act in ways that are commendable and beneficial to society.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a profound message about respect, abundance, friendship, collective wisdom, strength, and virtuous actions. In real-life scenarios, it can be applied in various contexts, such as community gatherings, family functions, or even in professional settings where teamwork and collaboration are essential. By embodying the principles outlined in this mantra, individuals can contribute to a harmonious and prosperous society, fostering relationships built on respect and mutual support. The significance of this mantra lies in its timeless relevance, reminding us of the values that sustain and enrich our lives and communities.
