समानो अध्वा स्वस्रोर अनन्तस तं अन्यान्याञ्चरतो देवशिष्टे |न मेथेते न तस्थतुः सुमेके नक्तोषासां समनसा विरूपे ||
samāno adhvā svasror anantas tam anyānyā carato devaśiṣṭe | na methete na tasthatuḥ sumeke naktoṣāsā samanasā virūpe ||
  • Translation: The mantra can be translated as: "May we be united in our paths, O divine beings, and may we not be divided in our thoughts. Let us not be separated, but rather be in harmony, as the night and day are in balance."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • samāno: united or similar
    • adhvā: paths or journeys
    • svasror: of our own (referring to individuals)
    • anantas: endless or infinite
    • tam: that
    • anyānyā: different or separate
    • carato: moving or wandering
    • devaśiṣṭe: divine beings or gods
    • na: not
    • methete: to be divided
    • na: not
    • tasthatuḥ: to stand or remain
    • sumeke: in harmony
    • naktoṣāsā: night and day
    • samanasā: in balance or equality
    • virūpe: in different forms
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace, teamwork is essential for success. When team members work together harmoniously, they can achieve common goals more effectively, just as the mantra suggests unity in paths.
    • In a family, maintaining open communication and understanding among members can prevent conflicts and foster a supportive environment, reflecting the idea of not being divided in thoughts.
    • In community settings, diverse groups can come together for a common cause, such as environmental conservation, demonstrating how different individuals can unite for a shared purpose while respecting their unique perspectives.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for unity and harmony among individuals, regardless of their differences. It emphasizes the importance of collaboration and understanding in various aspects of life, including personal relationships, professional environments, and community interactions. By embodying the principles of this mantra, we can create a more cohesive and supportive society where diverse paths converge towards common goals, ultimately leading to a more peaceful and prosperous existence for all.
