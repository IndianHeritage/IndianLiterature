सुगव्यं नो वाजी स्वश्व्यम् पुंसः पुत्राम् उता विश्वापुषं रयिम् |अनागास्त्वं नो अदितिः कृणोतु क्षत्रं नो अश्वो वनतां हविष्मान् ||
sugavyaṁ no vājī svaśvyam puṁsaḥ putrām̐ uta viśvāpuṣaṁ rayim | anāgāstvaṁ no aditiḥ kṛṇotu kṣatraṁ no aśvo vanatāṁ haviṣmān ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sugavyaṁ: auspicious, good fortune
    • no: our
    • vājī: the one who is strong, powerful
    • svaśvyam: our own, self
    • puṁsaḥ: of man, of the person
    • putrām̐: sons, offspring
    • uta: and, also
    • viśvāpuṣaṁ: all-encompassing wealth
    • rayim: prosperity, riches
    • anāgāstvaṁ: may you be free from sin
    • no: our
    • aditiḥ: the infinite, the boundless
    • kṛṇotu: may grant, may bestow
    • kṣatraṁ: power, dominion
    • no: our
    • aśvo: horse, symbol of strength
    • vanatāṁ: may it flourish
    • haviṣmān: the one who offers, the one who sacrifices
  • Overall Meaning: This mantra is a prayer for blessings and prosperity. It seeks auspiciousness and strength for oneself and one's offspring, while also asking for the bestowal of power and flourishing wealth. The invocation of Aditi, the infinite, signifies a desire for boundless blessings and protection from sin.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a family setting, parents often pray for the well-being and success of their children, reflecting the desire for "putrām̐" (offspring) to thrive and prosper.
    • In a business context, entrepreneurs may seek blessings for "rayim" (prosperity) and "kṣatraṁ" (power) to ensure their ventures flourish and succeed in a competitive market.
    • During significant life events, such as weddings or housewarming ceremonies, individuals often recite prayers similar to this mantra to invoke blessings for a prosperous and harmonious life.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its holistic approach to seeking blessings for oneself and one's family. It emphasizes the importance of strength, prosperity, and moral integrity. In real life, this mantra can be applicable in various scenarios, such as family prayers, business ventures, and significant life events, where individuals seek divine support for success and well-being. By invoking these blessings, one can cultivate a life filled with abundance and positive energy.
