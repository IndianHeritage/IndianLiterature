आ वां श्येनासो अश्विनā वहन्तु रथे युक्तास आषवः पतङ्गाः |ये अप्तुरो दिव्यासो न गृध्रā अभि प्रयो नासत्यā वहन्ति ||
ā vāṁ śyenāso aśvinā vahantu rathe yuktāsa āśavaḥ pataṁgāḥ | ye apturo divyāso na gṛdhrā abhi prayo nāsatyā vahanti ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā vāṁ: May they (the divine beings) come to us.
    • śyenāso: With the strength of the hawk.
    • aśvinā: O Ashvins (the twin deities of health and medicine).
    • vahantu: May they carry or bring.
    • rathe: In the chariot.
    • yuktāsa: Well-equipped or well-prepared.
    • āśavaḥ: With swiftness or speed.
    • pataṁgāḥ: Like the swift-winged ones (birds).
    • ye: Those who.
    • apturo: Who are capable or skilled.
    • divyāso: Divine or celestial beings.
    • na gṛdhrā: Not the vultures.
    • abhi: Towards or in the direction of.
    • prayo: The approach or the way.
    • nāsatyā: O Nāsatyas (another name for the Ashvins).
    • vahanti: They carry or bring forth.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the Ashvins, the divine twin horsemen known for their healing powers and swiftness.
    • It emphasizes the importance of being well-prepared and equipped, akin to a chariot ready for a journey.
    • The reference to "swift-winged ones" suggests the need for agility and speed in our endeavors.
    • By contrasting the divine beings with vultures, it highlights the pursuit of higher, noble goals rather than scavenging for the leftovers of life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a competitive environment, such as a corporate setting, being "well-equipped" can mean having the right skills and knowledge to excel.
    • In sports, athletes often prepare rigorously, akin to a chariot being readied for a race, emphasizing the importance of preparation and teamwork.
    • In personal development, seeking guidance from mentors (the divine beings) can lead to swift progress, much like the Ashvins guiding individuals towards success.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of divine guidance, preparation, and the pursuit of noble goals in life.
    • It encourages individuals to seek higher aspirations and to be swift and agile in their pursuits, avoiding the pitfalls of mediocrity.
    • In real life, this can be applied in various fields such as education, career, and personal growth, reminding us to strive for excellence and seek the support of those who uplift us.
