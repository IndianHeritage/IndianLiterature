त्वं आविथ नर्यं तुर्वशं यदुं त्वं तुर्वीतिं वय्यं शतक्रतो |त्वं रथम एतशं कृत्व्ये धनं त्वं पुरो नवतिं दम्भयो नवा ||
tvam āvitha naryaṁ turvaśaṁ yaduṁ tvaṁ turvītiṁ vayyaṁ śatakrato | tvaṁ ratham etaśaṁ kṛtvye dhane tvam puro navatiṁ dambhayo nava ||
  • tvam āvitha naryaṁ turvaśaṁ: This phrase can be interpreted as a call to the divine to envelop or protect the speaker. The term "naryaṁ" refers to the essence of life or vitality, while "turvaśaṁ" signifies strength or power. In real life, this can be likened to seeking protection and guidance during challenging times, much like how individuals turn to their faith or community for support.
  • yaduṁ tvaṁ turvītiṁ vayyaṁ śatakrato: Here, "yaduṁ" refers to the divine presence, and "turvītiṁ" suggests the act of bestowing strength or blessings. "Śatakrato" is often associated with Indra, the king of gods, symbolizing authority and control. This can be related to how leaders or mentors in our lives provide us with the strength to overcome obstacles, akin to how a teacher empowers students to achieve their goals.
  • tvaṁ ratham etaśaṁ kṛtvye dhane: This line speaks to the creation of a chariot ("ratham") that symbolizes a journey or path in life. "Kṛtvye" implies the act of making or creating, while "dhane" refers to wealth or resources. This can be seen in the context of setting goals and working towards them, much like how individuals plan their careers or personal projects, building their own 'chariots' to success.
  • tvam puro navatiṁ dambhayo nava: The final part emphasizes the idea of new beginnings and the importance of authenticity ("dambhayo" suggests falsehood). "Navatiṁ" indicates freshness or renewal. In real life, this can be interpreted as the importance of starting anew, shedding old habits, and embracing honesty in our endeavors, similar to how individuals often seek personal growth and transformation.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking divine protection, empowerment, and the importance of authenticity in our journeys. It serves as a reminder that in our pursuit of goals, we should remain true to ourselves and seek guidance from higher powers or mentors. In real life, this can be applicable in various scenarios such as personal development, leadership, and community support, encouraging individuals to embrace their paths with strength and integrity.
