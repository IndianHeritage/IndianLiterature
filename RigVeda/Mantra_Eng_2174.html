पुरा सम्बाधाद अभ्य आववृत्स्व नो धेनुर न वत्सं यवसस्य पिप्युषी |सकृत सु ते सुमतिभिः शतक्रतो सम पत्नीभिर न वृषणो नसीमहि ||
purā sambādhād abhy ā vavṛtsva no dhenur na vatsaṁ yavasasya pipyuṣī | sakṛt su te sumatibhiḥ śatakrato sam patnībhir na vṛṣaṇo nasīmahi ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • purā: formerly, in the past
    • sambādhād: from the connection, from the bond
    • abhy: towards, in the direction of
    • āvavṛtsva: may you come forth, may you manifest
    • no: for us
    • dhenur: the cow, symbolizing nourishment and wealth
    • na: not
    • vatsaṁ: the calf, representing offspring and prosperity
    • yavasasya: of the grains, symbolizing sustenance
    • pipyuṣī: may you be full, may you be abundant
  • Contextual Meaning:
    • This mantra invokes the blessings of abundance and prosperity.
    • The cow (dhenur) is a symbol of nourishment, and the mantra seeks the manifestation of wealth and sustenance.
    • The reference to the calf (vatsaṁ) emphasizes the importance of offspring and future generations in the context of prosperity.
    • The mention of grains (yavasasya) highlights the significance of agriculture and food security in sustaining life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In agricultural communities, this mantra can be recited during harvest festivals to invoke blessings for a bountiful crop.
    • Families may chant this mantra during auspicious occasions, such as weddings or childbirth, to seek prosperity and well-being for future generations.
    • In modern contexts, businesses may use this mantra to invoke success and abundance in their ventures, reflecting the desire for growth and prosperity.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the interconnectedness of nourishment, prosperity, and future generations.
    • Its significance lies in the universal desire for abundance and well-being, applicable in various aspects of life, from agriculture to family and business.
    • By invoking these blessings, individuals and communities can foster a sense of gratitude and responsibility towards sustaining life and prosperity.
