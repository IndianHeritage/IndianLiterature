इमं नरो मरुतः सश्चतां वृधं यस्मिन रायः शेवृधासः |अभि ये सन्ति पृतनासु दूद्यो विश्वाहाः शत्रुम आदभुः ||
imaṁ naro marutaḥ saścatā vṛdhaṁ yasmin rāyaḥ śevṛdhāsaḥ | abhi ye santi pṛtanāsu dūḍhyo viśvāhā śatrum ādabhuḥ ||
  • imaṁ naro marutaḥ saścatā vṛdhaṁ: This phrase can be translated to "these men, the Maruts, are strong and powerful." The Maruts are a group of storm deities in the Rigveda, often associated with strength, vitality, and the forces of nature.
  • yasmin rāyaḥ śevṛdhāsaḥ: This translates to "in whom the riches are well established." Here, "rāyaḥ" refers to wealth or prosperity, indicating a connection between strength and the establishment of wealth.
  • abhi ye santi pṛtanāsu dūḍhyo: This means "those who are present in the gatherings." It emphasizes the importance of community and collective strength in achieving goals.
  • viśvāhā śatrum ādabhuḥ: This translates to "may they overcome all enemies." It signifies the aspiration for victory over adversities and challenges.

In summary, this mantra highlights the themes of strength, community, and prosperity. It emphasizes the importance of collective effort and the divine support of the Maruts in overcoming challenges and achieving success. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios such as:


  • Teamwork in Organizations: Just as the Maruts symbolize collective strength, successful organizations thrive on teamwork. When individuals come together, they can achieve greater results than they could alone.
  • Community Support: In times of crisis, communities that unite can provide support and resources to overcome challenges, reflecting the mantra's emphasis on gathering strength.
  • Personal Growth: Individuals can draw inspiration from the mantra to seek strength within themselves and their communities, fostering resilience in the face of adversity.

In conclusion, this mantra serves as a reminder of the power of unity, the importance of strength in overcoming challenges, and the role of divine support in achieving prosperity. It encourages individuals and communities to work together towards common goals, ensuring that they can face and conquer any adversities that come their way.
