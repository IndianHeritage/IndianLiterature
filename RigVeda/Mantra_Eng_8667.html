समीचीन अभि तमनā यह्वी ऋतस्य मातरā |तन्वानā यज्ञं अनुषग यद अञ्जते ||
samīcīne abhi tmanā yahvī ṛtasya mātarā | tanvānā yajñam ānuṣag yad añjate ||
  • Word Breakdown:
    • samīcīne: In harmony or in alignment.
    • abhi: Towards or in the direction of.
    • tmanā: With the self or through one's own essence.
    • yahvī: The one who is strong or powerful.
    • ṛtasya: Of truth or cosmic order.
    • mātarā: As a mother or nurturing force.
    • tanvānā: With a body or in a physical form.
    • yajñam: Sacrifice or offering.
    • ānuṣag: Following or in accordance with.
    • yad: That which.
    • añjate: Is invoked or is called upon.
  • Meaning:
    • This mantra speaks about the importance of harmony and alignment with one's true self.
    • It emphasizes the nurturing aspect of truth, akin to a mother guiding her child.
    • The mantra suggests that when one aligns with their true essence, they can effectively perform sacrifices or offerings that resonate with the cosmic order.
    • It highlights the strength that comes from being in tune with one's inner self and the greater truth of the universe.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace, when team members work in harmony and align their individual goals with the organization's mission, they can achieve greater success, much like the mantra suggests.
    • In personal relationships, nurturing and understanding each other's true selves can lead to stronger bonds, reflecting the motherly aspect of the mantra.
    • Practices like meditation and self-reflection can help individuals connect with their inner essence, allowing them to make offerings in their lives that are meaningful and impactful.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for individuals to seek harmony within themselves and with the universe.
    • It teaches that true strength and power come from understanding and nurturing one's essence, leading to actions that are in accordance with the greater cosmic order.
    • In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as personal growth, teamwork, and nurturing relationships, ultimately guiding individuals towards a more fulfilling and harmonious existence.
