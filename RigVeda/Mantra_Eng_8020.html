तया पवस्व धारया यया पीतो विचक्षसे |इन्दो स्तोत्रे सुवीर्यम् ||
tayā pavasva dhārayā yayā pīto vicakṣase | indo stotre suvīryam ||
  • Mantra: "tayā pavasva dhārayā yayā pīto vicakṣase | indo stotre suvīryam ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • tayā: by that
    • pavasva: may you purify
    • dhārayā: by holding or sustaining
    • yayā: by which
    • pīto: having been drunk or consumed
    • vicakṣase: may you become wise or discerning
    • indo: O Indra (the deity)
    • stotre: in praise or hymn
    • suvīryam: of great strength or valor
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer directed towards Indra, the king of the gods in Vedic tradition, who represents strength, power, and wisdom.
    • The phrase "tayā pavasva" suggests a request for purification, indicating the importance of mental and spiritual clarity.
    • "dhārayā yayā pīto" implies that through the sustenance provided by divine grace, one can gain wisdom and discernment.
    • The invocation of "Indo stotre suvīryam" emphasizes the strength and valor that comes from divine blessings, encouraging individuals to seek and embody these qualities in their lives.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a challenging situation, such as a difficult project at work, one might invoke the qualities of Indra by seeking clarity and strength to overcome obstacles.
    • In personal relationships, the mantra can serve as a reminder to maintain wisdom and discernment, ensuring that decisions are made with a clear mind and pure intentions.
    • During times of stress or confusion, individuals can meditate on this mantra to cultivate inner strength and clarity, much like athletes who visualize success to enhance their performance.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of divine support in achieving clarity, strength, and wisdom in our lives.
    • It serves as a reminder that through spiritual practices and seeking divine guidance, one can navigate life's challenges with greater discernment and power.
    • Incorporating this mantra into daily life can inspire individuals to pursue their goals with confidence and integrity, fostering a deeper connection with the divine and oneself.
