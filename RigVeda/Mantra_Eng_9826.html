इमा अस्मै मतयो वाचो अस्मद आमं ऋचो गिरः सुश्रुतयः सम अग्मत |वसूयवो वसवे जातवेदसे वृद्धासु चिद् वर्धनो यासु चाकनत ||
imā asmai matayo vāco asmad ām̐ ṛco giraḥ suṣṭutayaḥ sam agmata | vasūyavo vasave jātavedase vṛddhāsu cid vardhano yāsu cākanat ||
  • imā asmai matayo vāco: This phrase can be translated to "These thoughts and words are directed towards him." It signifies the importance of communication and intention in our thoughts and expressions. In real life, this can be seen in how we express our feelings and ideas to others, emphasizing the need for clarity and purpose in our communication.
  • asmad ām̐ ṛco giraḥ suṣṭutayaḥ sam agmata: This part means "Our hymns and praises have come together." It highlights the collective effort in worship and the power of unity in expressing gratitude or reverence. For example, in a community gathering or a family celebration, the collective voices of individuals create a harmonious atmosphere, enhancing the experience of togetherness.
  • vasūyavo vasave jātavedase: This translates to "To the one who is the source of all wealth and prosperity." It refers to the divine or universal energy that provides abundance. In practical terms, this can relate to how individuals seek prosperity in their lives, whether through hard work, education, or spiritual practices that align them with abundance.
  • vṛddhāsu cid vardhano yāsu cākanat: This means "In the wise, may the one who increases our understanding be present." It emphasizes the value of wisdom and the pursuit of knowledge. In everyday life, this can be seen in the importance of education, mentorship, and learning from experiences, which contribute to personal and communal growth.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of communication, unity, abundance, and wisdom. Its significance lies in the reminder that our thoughts and words have power, that collective efforts can lead to greater outcomes, and that seeking wisdom is essential for growth. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts such as family gatherings, community events, and personal development practices, encouraging individuals to express their intentions clearly, work together harmoniously, seek prosperity, and value wisdom in their lives.
