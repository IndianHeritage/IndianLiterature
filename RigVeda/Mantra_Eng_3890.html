त्यं चिदर्णं मधुपं शयानं असिन्वं वव्रम मह्य आदद उग्रः |अपादमात्रं महता वधेन न दुर्योन आवृणं मृध्रवाचम् ||
tyaṁ cid arṇam madhupaṁ śayānam asinvaṁ vavram mahy ādad ugraḥ | apādam atram mahatā vadhena ni duryoṇa āvṛṇaṅ mṛdhravācam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • tyaṁ: that
    • cid: indeed
    • arṇam: the ocean or vastness
    • madhupaṁ: the bee or sweetness
    • śayānam: lying down or resting
    • asinvaṁ: the one who is powerful or strong
    • vavram: I have taken or I have grasped
    • mahy: for me
    • ādad: to take or to receive
    • ugraḥ: fierce or intense
    • apādam: without obstacles
    • atram: here or in this place
    • mahatā: by the great
    • vadhena: by the act of killing or destruction
    • ni: not
    • duryoṇa: difficult to overcome
    • āvṛṇaṅ: to cover or to envelop
    • mṛdhravācam: with gentle speech or kind words
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the vastness of existence and the sweetness of life, represented by the ocean and the bee.
    • The phrase "lying down" suggests a state of rest or contemplation, indicating the importance of reflection in our lives.
    • The mention of "fierce" and "without obstacles" implies a call to embrace challenges with strength and determination.
    • The mantra also emphasizes the power of gentle speech, suggesting that kindness and compassion can overcome difficulties.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, when faced with a challenging project, one can reflect on the vastness of possibilities (arṇam) and approach the task with a calm and determined mindset (ugraḥ).
    • In personal relationships, using gentle words (mṛdhravācam) can help resolve conflicts and foster understanding, demonstrating the power of kindness.
    • During times of stress, taking a moment to rest and reflect (śayānam) can provide clarity and strength to tackle obstacles (apādam).
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the balance between strength and gentleness in our lives.
    • It encourages us to embrace challenges while maintaining a compassionate approach towards ourselves and others.
    • In essence, it highlights the significance of reflection, kindness, and resilience in navigating the complexities of life.
