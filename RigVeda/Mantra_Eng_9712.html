सम्राज्ञी श्वशुरे भव सम्राज्ञी श्वश्र्वाम् भव |ननांदरी सम्राज्ञी भव सम्राज्ञी अधि देवृषु ||
samrājñī śvaśure bhava samrājñī śvaśrvām bhava | nanāndari samrājñī bhava samrājñī adhi devṛṣu ||
  • Mantra: "samrājñī śvaśure bhava samrājñī śvaśrvām bhava | nanāndari samrājñī bhava samrājñī adhi devṛṣu ||"
  • Translation: This mantra can be translated as a prayer for the well-being and prosperity of the family and the community. It invokes the divine presence to bless the relationships within the family, particularly focusing on the mother-in-law and daughter-in-law dynamics.
  • Word Breakdown:
    • samrājñī: Refers to a queen or a ruler, symbolizing authority and respect.
    • śvaśure: Means 'father-in-law', indicating the importance of paternal relationships in a family.
    • śvaśrvām: Refers to 'mother-in-law', highlighting the role of maternal figures in family dynamics.
    • nanāndari: Means 'sister-in-law', representing the bond between sisters-in-law.
    • adhi devṛṣu: Implies 'among the divine', suggesting that these relationships are sacred and should be nurtured with divine blessings.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In many cultures, the relationship between a mother-in-law and daughter-in-law is pivotal. This mantra emphasizes the need for harmony and respect in these relationships, which can be seen in family gatherings where both parties come together to celebrate festivals.
    • In a workplace setting, the mantra can be applied to foster teamwork and collaboration among colleagues, akin to the familial bonds it describes. For instance, a project team that respects and values each member's contributions can achieve greater success.
  • Significance:
    • This mantra highlights the importance of familial relationships and the need for mutual respect and understanding. It serves as a reminder that strong family ties contribute to a harmonious society.
    • In a broader context, it can be applied to any community or organization, emphasizing the need for cooperation and support among its members.
  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the significance of nurturing relationships within families and communities. By invoking divine blessings, it encourages individuals to foster love, respect, and harmony, which are essential for a prosperous and peaceful life.
