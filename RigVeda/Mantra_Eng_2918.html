त्रितीये धानाः सवने पुरुष्टुत पुरोळाशम् आहुतम् मामहस्व नः | ऋभुमन्तं वाजवन्तं त्वा कवे प्रयस्वन्त उप शिक्षेम धीतिभिः ||
tṛtīye dhānāḥ savane puruṣṭuta puroḻāśam āhutam māmahasva naḥ | ṛbhumantaṁ vājavantaṁ tvā kave prayasvanta upa śikṣema dhītibhiḥ ||
  • tṛtīye dhānāḥ: This phrase can be translated as "in the third (sacrifice)". The number three often symbolizes completeness and balance in various cultures. In real life, this can be seen in the concept of the "three pillars" of sustainability: social, economic, and environmental.
  • savane puruṣṭuta: This means "in the offering, praised by men". It highlights the importance of community and recognition in our endeavors. For example, in a workplace, when team members acknowledge each other's contributions, it fosters a positive environment and encourages collaboration.
  • puroḻāśam āhutam: This translates to "the one who is invoked". It signifies the act of calling upon a higher power or divine presence. In daily life, people often seek guidance through prayer or meditation, invoking a sense of peace and clarity in decision-making.
  • māmahasva naḥ: This means "may you be great for us". It expresses a desire for blessings and greatness to be bestowed upon the community. In a practical sense, this can relate to leaders or mentors who inspire and uplift those around them, contributing to collective success.
  • ṛbhumantaṁ vājavantaṁ: This phrase translates to "the one who is rich in offerings and strength". It emphasizes the value of generosity and strength in character. In real life, individuals who contribute to charitable causes or support their communities exemplify this principle, enriching the lives of others.
  • tvā kave prayasvanta: This means "to you, O poet, striving". It acknowledges the role of creativity and effort in achieving goals. For instance, artists, writers, and innovators who dedicate themselves to their craft often inspire others and drive cultural progress.
  • upa śikṣema dhītibhiḥ: This translates to "may we attain wisdom through knowledge". It highlights the importance of learning and wisdom in personal and communal growth. In real life, education and continuous learning are vital for adapting to change and making informed decisions.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of community, creativity, and the pursuit of wisdom. Its significance lies in the interconnectedness of individuals striving for greatness together. In real-life scenarios, it encourages collaboration, recognition of contributions, and the importance of learning. By embodying these principles, individuals and communities can foster an environment of support, growth, and shared success.
