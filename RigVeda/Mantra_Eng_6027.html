ओ षु प्र याहि वाजेभिर मा हृणीथा अभ्य अस्मान् |महाम् इव युवजानिः ||
o ṣu pra yāhi vājebhir mā hṛṇīthā abhy a1smān | mahām̐ iva yuvajāniḥ ||
  • Mantra: "o ṣu pra yāhi vājebhir mā hṛṇīthā abhy a1smān | mahām̐ iva yuvajāniḥ ||"
  • Translation: "O, come here with your powerful forces; do not hold us back. Like the great ones, may we be youthful."

  • Word-by-Word Explanation:
    • o: A call or invocation, often used to address deities or forces.
    • ṣu: Good, auspicious, or beneficial.
    • pra: Forward, towards, or in favor of.
    • yāhi: Come or arrive.
    • vājebhir: With strength or power; often associated with the concept of 'vāj' which means strength or vigor.
    • mā: Not or do not.
    • hṛṇīthā: Hold back or restrain.
    • abhy: Towards or in the direction of.
    • a1smān: Us or we.
    • mahām̐: Great or mighty.
    • iva: Like or as if.
    • yuvajāniḥ: Youthful or vigorous beings.

  • Significance:
    • This mantra is a call for strength and vitality, urging powerful forces to come forth and support us.
    • It emphasizes the importance of not being held back by obstacles, whether they are internal doubts or external challenges.
    • The reference to being youthful suggests a desire for energy, enthusiasm, and a fresh perspective in life.

  • Real-Life Application:
    • In personal development, this mantra can be a reminder to seek out positive influences and to embrace change without fear.
    • In a professional context, it can inspire teams to work together with vigor and not be deterred by setbacks.
    • In health and wellness, it encourages individuals to pursue vitality and a youthful spirit, regardless of age.

  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the strength that comes from unity and the importance of maintaining a youthful spirit in all endeavors.
    • By invoking this energy, individuals can overcome challenges and embrace opportunities for growth and renewal.
