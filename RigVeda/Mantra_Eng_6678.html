पुत्रिणा ता कुमारिणा विश्वम् आयुर व्य अश्नुता: |उभा हिरण्यपेक्षसा ||
putriṇā tā kumāriṇā viśvam āyur vy aśnutaḥ | ubhā hiraṇyapeśasā ||
  • Mantra: "putriṇā tā kumāriṇā viśvam āyur vy aśnutaḥ | ubhā hiraṇyapeśasā ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • putriṇā: by the daughters
    • tā: those
    • kumāriṇā: by the young maidens
    • viśvam: all, the universe
    • āyur: life, longevity
    • vy aśnutaḥ: may they attain
    • ubhā: both
    • hiraṇyapeśasā: with golden adornments
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of young women and daughters in society.
    • It suggests that through their presence and contributions, they can bring longevity and prosperity to the community.
    • The phrase "hiraṇyapeśasā" symbolizes wealth and beauty, indicating that these qualities are enhanced by the participation of women.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In many cultures, daughters are seen as bearers of tradition and values, contributing to the family and society's well-being.
    • Women in leadership roles often bring new perspectives that can lead to sustainable development and community growth.
    • Initiatives that empower young women, such as education and entrepreneurship programs, can lead to improved health and economic outcomes for entire communities.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of women in fostering life and prosperity.
    • It serves as a reminder of the vital role that young women play in shaping a healthy and thriving society.
    • In contemporary contexts, it encourages the support and empowerment of women, recognizing their contributions as essential for the overall well-being of humanity.
