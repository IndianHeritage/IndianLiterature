अध स्वनाद उता बिभ्युः पतत्रिणो द्रप्सा यत ते यवसादो व्य अस्थिरन् |सुगं तात ते तावकेभ्यो रथेभ्यो 'ग्ने सख्ये मा रिषामा वयं तव ||
adha svanād uta bibhyuḥ patatriṇo drapsā yat te yavasādo vy asthiran | sugaṁ tat te tāvakebhyo rathebhyo 'gne sakhye mā riṣāmā vayaṁ tava ||
  • Adha svanād uta bibhyuḥ: This phrase can be translated as "From the sound of the horn, we are not afraid." It signifies the importance of courage and readiness in the face of challenges. In real life, this can be likened to a soldier preparing for battle, where the sound of a horn signifies the start of action, and fear must be overcome.
  • Patatriṇo drapsā yat te yavasādo vy asthiran: This part refers to the swift movement of birds and the stability of the earth. It suggests a balance between agility and steadfastness. For example, in business, a company must be agile to adapt to market changes while also maintaining a strong foundation in its core values and mission.
  • Sugaṁ tat te tāvakebhyo rathebhyo: This translates to "That which is good for you, from the chariots." It implies seeking what is beneficial and advantageous. In everyday life, this can be seen in the choices we make, such as selecting a career path that aligns with our strengths and passions, leading to personal fulfillment.
  • Agne sakhye mā riṣāmā vayaṁ tava: This line invokes Agni, the fire god, asking for friendship and protection. It emphasizes the importance of seeking divine guidance and support in our endeavors. In a real-life context, this can be related to the practice of prayer or meditation, where individuals seek strength and clarity in their lives.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates themes of courage, balance, seeking goodness, and divine support. Its significance lies in the reminder to face challenges without fear, to adapt while remaining grounded, to make beneficial choices, and to seek guidance in our journeys. In real life, these principles can be applied in various scenarios, such as personal development, professional growth, and spiritual practices, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling and purposeful life.
