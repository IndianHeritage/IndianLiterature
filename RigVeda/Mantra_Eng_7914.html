एष पवित्रे अक्षरात सोमो देवभ्यः सुतः |विश्वा धामान्य आविशन् ||
eṣa pavitre akṣarat somo devebhyaḥ sutaḥ | viśvā dhāmāny āviśan ||
  • eṣa pavitre akṣarat somo devebhyaḥ sutaḥ
    • eṣa - This means "this" or "here." It indicates something that is present or being referred to.
    • pavitre - This translates to "pure" or "sacred." It signifies the importance of purity in spiritual and physical realms.
    • akṣarat - Meaning "imperishable" or "eternal," this word emphasizes the everlasting nature of certain truths or entities.
    • somo - Refers to "Soma," a sacred drink in Vedic tradition, symbolizing nourishment and immortality.
    • devebhyaḥ - This means "to the gods" or "from the divine." It indicates a connection to the divine or celestial beings.
    • sutaḥ - Translates to "son" or "offspring," suggesting a lineage or a product of a divine source.
  • viśvā dhāmāny āviśan
    • viśvā - This means "all" or "universal," indicating inclusiveness and the totality of existence.
    • dhāmāny - Refers to "abodes" or "places," suggesting realms or states of being.
    • āviśan - This translates to "entered" or "pervaded," indicating the act of encompassing or filling.

The mantra can be interpreted as follows: "This pure and eternal Soma, the offspring of the gods, has entered all abodes." It highlights the significance of Soma as a divine essence that is both pure and eternal, connecting the material and spiritual worlds.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various contexts:

  • Spiritual Practices: The emphasis on purity and the divine connection can inspire individuals to seek purity in their thoughts and actions, fostering a deeper spiritual practice.
  • Community and Inclusiveness: The idea of "all abodes" encourages inclusivity and unity among diverse groups, promoting harmony in communities.
  • Health and Well-being: The concept of Soma as a nourishing essence can be related to the importance of maintaining physical and mental health through pure and wholesome practices.

Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for purity, divine connection, and inclusiveness. It serves as a reminder to seek the eternal truths in our lives, to nourish our spirits, and to foster unity among all beings. By embodying these principles, individuals can create a more harmonious and fulfilling existence.
