आ यस ते सर्पिरासुते 'ग्ने शम् अस्ति धायसे |ऐषु द्युम्नम उता श्रव आ चित्तं मर्त्येषु धाः ||
ā yas te sarpirāsute 'gne śam asti dhāyase | aiṣu dyumnam uta śrava ā cittam martyeṣu dhāḥ ||
  • Word Breakdown:
    • ā - a prefix indicating direction or movement towards.
    • yas - who or which.
    • te - your.
    • sarpirāsute - in the essence of ghee or clarified butter, symbolizing nourishment and purity.
    • 'gne - O Agni, the fire god, representing energy and transformation.
    • śam - peace or tranquility.
    • asti - there is or exists.
    • dhāyase - you bestow or grant.
    • aiṣu - in this or in these.
    • dyumnam - glory or brilliance.
    • uta - and or also.
    • śrava - fame or renown.
    • ā - again, indicating direction.
    • cittam - mind or consciousness.
    • martyeṣu - among mortals or human beings.
    • dhāḥ - you grant or bestow.
  • Meaning:
    • This mantra invokes Agni, the fire god, asking for the essence of ghee, which symbolizes nourishment and purity.
    • It emphasizes the importance of peace and tranquility that Agni bestows upon us.
    • The mantra seeks glory and fame in the mortal realm, highlighting the desire for recognition and respect in society.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a traditional Indian household, ghee is often used in rituals and cooking, symbolizing the nourishment of both body and spirit.
    • In modern life, the pursuit of peace can be seen in practices like meditation and yoga, which help individuals find tranquility amidst chaos.
    • Seeking recognition in one's career or community can be paralleled with the desire for 'dyumnam' or glory, motivating individuals to excel in their fields.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of seeking divine blessings for nourishment, peace, and recognition in life.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal growth to professional achievements, reminding us to seek balance and harmony.
    • By invoking Agni, we are encouraged to transform our energies positively, fostering a sense of community and shared glory among mortals.
