यā ते धामानि दिवि यā पृथिव्यां यā पर्वतेष्वोषधिष्वप्सु |तेभिर नो विश्वैः सुमना अहेलन राजन सोम प्रति हव्यā गृभाय ||
yā te dhāmāni divi yā pṛthivyāṁ yā parvateṣv oṣadhīṣv apsu | tebhir no viśvaiḥ sumanā aheḻan rājan soma prati havyā gṛbhāya ||
  • yā te dhāmāni divi: This phrase refers to the divine abodes or realms that belong to the deity. In a real-life context, this can be understood as the aspirations or goals we seek in our lives, such as peace, happiness, and fulfillment.
  • yā pṛthivyāṁ: This translates to "that which is on Earth." It signifies the material world and our earthly existence. For example, our relationships, careers, and daily experiences are part of this earthly realm.
  • yā parvateṣv oṣadhīṣv apsu: This part speaks of the mountains, herbs, and waters. Mountains can symbolize challenges or obstacles in life, while herbs represent healing and nourishment. Water signifies the flow of life and emotions. In real life, we encounter challenges (mountains), seek healing (herbs), and navigate our emotional landscapes (waters).
  • tebhir no viśvaiḥ: This means "with these, may we be blessed." It emphasizes the importance of seeking blessings from the divine and the natural world. In our lives, we often seek guidance and support from higher powers or the universe.
  • sumanā aheḻan: This phrase translates to "may we be of good mind." It highlights the significance of maintaining a positive and constructive mindset. In real life, a positive attitude can lead to better relationships and success in various endeavors.
  • rājan soma prati havyā gṛbhāya: This part refers to the offerings made to the divine, particularly to the deity Soma. It signifies the act of giving and sharing. In our lives, this can be seen in acts of kindness, charity, and community service, where we contribute to the well-being of others.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the interconnectedness of the divine, the natural world, and human existence. It teaches us to seek blessings from the universe, maintain a positive mindset, and contribute to the well-being of others. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire us to set meaningful goals, overcome challenges, and engage in acts of kindness, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling and harmonious life.
