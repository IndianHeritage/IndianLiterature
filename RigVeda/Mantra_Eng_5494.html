वासयसीव वेधसस् त्वं नः कदा न इन्द्र वचसो बुबोधः |अस्तं तात्याऽ धियā रयिं सुवीरम् पृक्षो नो अर्वा न्युहित वाजी ||
vāsayasīva vedhasas tvaṁ naḥ kadā na indra vacaso bubodhaḥ | astaṁ tātyā dhiyā rayiṁ suvīram pṛkṣo no arvā ny uhīta vājī ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • vāsayasīva: O Indra, you who are the protector or the one who provides shelter.
    • vedhasas: You who are wise or knowledgeable.
    • tvaṁ: You.
    • naḥ: Our.
    • kadā: When.
    • na: Not.
    • indra: O Indra, the king of gods.
    • vacaso: Of the words or speech.
    • bubodhaḥ: May you understand or may you be aware.
    • astaṁ: To the end or to the conclusion.
    • tātyā: With our intellect or understanding.
    • dhiyā: With wisdom.
    • rayiṁ: Wealth or prosperity.
    • suvīram: Good or noble heroes.
    • pṛkṣo: We seek or we desire.
    • no: Our.
    • arvā: The best or the most excellent.
    • ny: Not.
    • uhīta: To be hidden or concealed.
    • vājī: The one who brings victory or success.
  • Overall Meaning:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Indra, the king of gods, asking for his wisdom and guidance.
    • It emphasizes the importance of understanding and awareness in our speech and actions.
    • The mantra seeks prosperity and noble qualities, urging the divine to help us achieve our goals without obstacles.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, this mantra can be applied when seeking guidance for important decisions, such as during a business meeting where clarity and wisdom are crucial.
    • In personal relationships, invoking this mantra can help individuals communicate better, ensuring that their words are understood and appreciated.
    • During challenging times, such as facing obstacles in life, this mantra serves as a reminder to seek wisdom and support from higher powers or mentors.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine assistance in achieving clarity, wisdom, and prosperity.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal growth to professional success, highlighting the need for guidance and understanding in our endeavors.
    • By reciting this mantra, individuals can cultivate a mindset of seeking knowledge and support, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling and successful life.
