समानं वां सजात्यं समानो बन्धुर अश्विनाः |अन्ति षद्भूत वाम अवः ||
samānaṁ vāṁ sajātyaṁ samāno bandhur aśvinā | anti ṣad bhūtu vām avaḥ ||
  • Translation: The mantra "samānaṁ vāṁ sajātyaṁ samāno bandhur aśvinā | anti ṣad bhūtu vām avaḥ ||" can be translated as: "May we be united in our thoughts and actions, may we be like friends, O Ashvins, and may we be free from all obstacles."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • samānaṁ: Similar or equal; signifies unity and harmony.
    • vāṁ: You; refers to the collective 'you' or 'us'.
    • sajātyaṁ: Of the same kind; emphasizes the importance of shared values and goals.
    • samāno: Together; indicates collaboration and togetherness.
    • bandhur: Friend; highlights the importance of friendship and support.
    • aśvinā: Refers to the Ashvins, the twin deities associated with health and well-being.
    • anti: May; a wish or prayer for something to happen.
    • ṣad: Six; often symbolizes completeness or wholeness.
    • bhūtu: Beings or existence; refers to all living entities.
    • vām: You; again referring to the collective.
    • avaḥ: Bring; a call to action for the desired outcome.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace setting, this mantra can be applied to foster teamwork. When colleagues work together harmoniously, sharing the same goals and supporting each other, they can achieve greater success.
    • In a community, this mantra encourages unity among diverse groups. For instance, during community service projects, individuals from different backgrounds come together, emphasizing shared values and mutual support.
    • In personal relationships, this mantra serves as a reminder to maintain harmony and understanding. Friends and family members who communicate openly and support each other can navigate challenges more effectively.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for unity, friendship, and the removal of obstacles. It emphasizes the importance of working together towards common goals, whether in personal relationships, workplaces, or communities. By embodying the principles of this mantra, individuals can create a more harmonious and supportive environment, leading to collective growth and well-being. In essence, it serves as a timeless reminder of the power of collaboration and mutual respect in all aspects of life.
