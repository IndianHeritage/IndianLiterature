सांतपना इदं हविः मरुतस् तज जुञ्जुष्टन |युश्माकोटी ऋशादसः ||
sāṁtapanā idaṁ havir marutas taj jujuṣṭana | yuṣmākotī riśādasaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sāṁtapanā: This can be interpreted as "the one who brings peace" or "the one who calms." It signifies a state of tranquility and harmony.
    • idaṁ: This means "this" or "here," indicating the present moment or the offering being made.
    • havir: Refers to "offering" or "sacrifice," often associated with rituals and the act of giving.
    • marutas: This term refers to the "Maruts," the storm deities in Vedic literature, symbolizing strength, power, and the forces of nature.
    • taj: Meaning "that," it points to the divine or the higher power being addressed.
    • jujuṣṭana: This can be understood as "to be desired" or "to be sought after," indicating the longing for divine blessings.
    • yuṣmākotī: This translates to "your multitude" or "your many," referring to the collective presence of the deities or the community.
    • riśādasaḥ: This term can be interpreted as "those who are strong" or "the powerful ones," emphasizing the strength and might of the Maruts.
  • Contextual Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the Maruts, seeking their blessings for peace and strength. It highlights the importance of harmony in the face of challenges.
    • In real life, this can be related to situations where individuals or communities seek to overcome obstacles, whether they be personal struggles, societal issues, or natural calamities.
    • For example, during a community gathering aimed at resolving conflicts, invoking the essence of this mantra can serve as a reminder to seek peace and collective strength.
  • Significance and Application:
    • The mantra emphasizes the need for peace and the power of collective strength in overcoming challenges.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, such as conflict resolution, community building, and personal growth.
    • In a corporate setting, this mantra can inspire teamwork and collaboration, reminding individuals to work together harmoniously towards common goals.
    • In personal life, it encourages individuals to seek inner peace and strength during difficult times, fostering resilience and calmness.
