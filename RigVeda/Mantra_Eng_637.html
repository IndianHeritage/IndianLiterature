त्वं करञ्जम उता पर्णयं वधीस तेजिष्ठयातिथिग्वस्य वर्तनी |त्वं शतां वङ्गृदस्याभिनत पुरो 'नानुदः परिषूतā ऋजिष्वना ||
tvaṁ karañjam uta parṇayaṁ vadhīs tejiṣṭhayātithigvasya vartanī | tvaṁ śatā vaṅgṛdasyābhinat puro 'nānudaḥ pariṣūtā ṛjiśvanā ||
  • tvaṁ karañjam uta parṇayaṁ: This phrase can be interpreted as a call to the divine, acknowledging the presence of a higher power. The word "tvaṁ" means "you," referring to the divine or a deity. "Karañjam" suggests action or creation, while "parṇayaṁ" relates to leaves or foliage, symbolizing growth and sustenance.
  • vadhīs tejiṣṭhayātithigvasya vartanī: Here, "vadhīs" can be understood as a form of protection or support. "Tejiṣṭha" refers to brilliance or radiance, indicating the divine's powerful presence. "Atithigvasya" suggests the idea of hospitality or welcoming, while "vartanī" means existence or being. This line emphasizes the nurturing aspect of the divine, providing strength and support to those who seek it.
  • tvaṁ śatā vaṅgṛdasyābhinat: In this line, "śatā" means "hundreds" or "many," indicating abundance. "Vaṅgṛdasyā" can be interpreted as a reference to the various forms of life or creation. "Abhinat" suggests a sense of renewal or rebirth, highlighting the continuous cycle of life.
  • puro 'nānudaḥ pariṣūtā ṛjiśvanā: "Puro" means "in front" or "before," indicating a presence that guides. "Anānudaḥ" can be understood as "without delay," suggesting the immediacy of divine assistance. "Pariṣūtā" refers to purity or sanctity, while "ṛjiśvanā" implies a straight path or righteousness. This line reinforces the idea of divine guidance leading one towards a pure and righteous life.

  • Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda encapsulates the essence of divine support, growth, and guidance. It signifies the importance of recognizing a higher power that nurtures and protects us in our journey through life. In real-life scenarios, this can be applicable in moments of uncertainty or challenge, where individuals seek strength and clarity. For instance, during difficult times such as illness or personal loss, invoking this mantra can serve as a reminder of the divine presence that offers comfort and direction. It encourages individuals to embrace the abundance of life and to remain steadfast on their path, trusting in the support of the universe.
