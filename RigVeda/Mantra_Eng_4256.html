इह त्या पुरुभूतमा पुरू दंसांसी बिभ्रता |वरस्यां याम्य अध्रिगू हुवे तुविष्टमा भुजे ||
iha tyā purubhūtamā purū daṁsāṁsi bibhratā | varasyā yāmy adhrigū huve tuviṣṭamā bhuje ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • iha: here, in this world
    • tyā: that, which
    • purubhūtamā: of many forms, diverse
    • purū: many, numerous
    • daṁsāṁsi: the powers, energies
    • bibhratā: holding, possessing
    • varasyā: of the divine, of the gods
    • yāmy: of the night, of the dark
    • adhri: the one who is not bound
    • gū: the one who is hidden
    • huve: I invoke, I call upon
    • tuviṣṭamā: the most auspicious, the best
    • bhuje: to the one who is in the form of, to the one who possesses
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the divine presence that manifests in various forms in our world.
    • It emphasizes the idea that the divine holds numerous powers and energies, which can be invoked for guidance and support.
    • The reference to "the night" and "the hidden" suggests that there are aspects of life and the universe that are not immediately visible or understandable, yet they are significant.
    • By calling upon the divine, one seeks to connect with the auspicious energies that can aid in overcoming challenges and achieving goals.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In times of uncertainty or difficulty, individuals often turn to prayer or meditation, invoking a higher power for strength and clarity.
    • In various cultures, rituals are performed to honor the divine, seeking blessings for health, prosperity, and protection.
    • People may find comfort in the idea that there are unseen forces at work in their lives, guiding them through challenges.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the divine presence in our lives and the multitude of forms it can take.
    • It encourages individuals to recognize and invoke the powers that exist beyond the physical realm, fostering a sense of connection and support.
    • In practical terms, it highlights the importance of faith, intention, and the acknowledgment of unseen energies that influence our experiences.
