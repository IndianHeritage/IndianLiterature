विष्वे अद्य मरुतो विष्व ऊती विष्वे भवन्तु अग्नयः समिद्धाः |विष्वे नो देवाः अवसागमन्तु विष्वम अस्तु द्रविणं वाजो अस्मे ||
viśve adya maruto viśva ūtī viśve bhavantv agnayaḥ samiddhāḥ | viśve no devā avasā gamantu viśvam astu draviṇaṁ vājo asme ||
  • Translation: "O all-encompassing Maruts, may your universal energy be present today. May all the Agnis (fires) be kindled and may all the deities come to us. Let there be abundance and prosperity for us."
  • Word-by-Word Explanation:
    • viśve: All-encompassing, universal.
    • adya: Today, in the present moment.
    • maruto: Referring to the Maruts, the storm deities associated with wind and storms.
    • viśva ūtī: Universal energy or strength.
    • bhavantv: May you become or may you be.
    • agnayaḥ: Referring to Agni, the fire god, symbolizing energy and transformation.
    • samiddhāḥ: Kindled, ignited, or activated.
    • devā: Deities or divine beings.
    • avasā gamantu: May they come to us or may they approach us.
    • draviṇaṁ: Abundance, wealth, or resources.
    • vājo: Strength, vigor, or energy.
    • asme: For us, to us.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • This mantra can be recited during rituals or ceremonies to invoke the presence of divine energies and blessings. For instance, during a housewarming ceremony, families may chant this mantra to invite prosperity and protection into their new home.
    • In a corporate setting, leaders may use the essence of this mantra to inspire teamwork and collective effort, emphasizing the importance of unity and shared goals to achieve success.
    • During festivals, communities may gather to chant this mantra, reinforcing the idea of collective abundance and the importance of coming together to celebrate and share resources.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for unity, divine presence, and abundance. It emphasizes the importance of collective energy and the need for divine blessings in our endeavors. In real-life scenarios, whether in personal, communal, or professional settings, invoking this mantra can serve as a reminder of the interconnectedness of all beings and the shared pursuit of prosperity and well-being. By recognizing the universal energies at play, individuals and communities can foster a sense of harmony and abundance in their lives.
