त्वं सूकरस्य दर्धि तव दर्धर्तु सूकरः |स्तोत्रिन् इन्द्रस्य रायसि किम अस्मान दुच्चुनायसे नि षु स्वप ||
tvaṁ sūkarasya dardṛhi tava dardartu sūkaraḥ | stotṝn indrasya rāyasi kim asmān ducchunāyase ni ṣu svapa ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • tvaṁ: You
    • sūkarasya: of the boar
    • dardṛhi: be strong
    • tava: your
    • dardartu: the one who is strong
    • sūkaraḥ: boar
    • stotṝn: praises
    • indrasya: of Indra
    • rāyasi: wealth
    • kim: what
    • asmān: us
    • ducchunāyase: you will not abandon
    • ni: not
    • ṣu: good
    • svapa: sleep
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the strength and power of the divine, represented by the boar, which is a symbol of fertility and abundance.
    • The phrase "be strong" emphasizes the need for resilience and courage in facing life's challenges.
    • The reference to Indra, the king of gods, highlights the importance of divine support in achieving wealth and prosperity.
    • The plea for not being abandoned suggests a deep yearning for divine presence and guidance in times of need.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In times of personal struggle, such as during a financial crisis, individuals often seek strength and support from their faith or community, similar to the invocation in this mantra.
    • In a corporate setting, leaders may draw upon the strength of their teams, akin to the strength of the boar, to overcome challenges and achieve success.
    • During significant life events, such as weddings or births, people often call upon divine blessings for prosperity and happiness, reflecting the essence of this mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of divine strength and support in our lives, encouraging us to seek resilience and courage.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal struggles to communal celebrations, reminding us of the interconnectedness of our lives with the divine.
    • Ultimately, it teaches us to trust in the strength that comes from faith and the support of the divine in our journey through life.
