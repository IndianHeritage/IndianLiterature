आ वाम मित्रावरुणा हव्यजुष्टिं नमसā देवाव अवसā ववृत्यम् |अस्माकं ब्रह्म पृतनासु सह्या अस्माकं वृष्टिर दिव्या सुपारा ||
ā vām mitrāvaruṇā havyajuṣṭiṁ namasā devāv avasā vavṛtyām | asmākam brahma pṛtanāsu sahyā asmākaṁ vṛṣṭir divyā supārā ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā vām: To you both
    • mitrāvaruṇā: O Mitra and Varuna (the deities representing friendship and cosmic order)
    • havyajuṣṭiṁ: Offering that is worthy of worship
    • namasā: With reverence
    • devāv: O divine beings
    • avasā: In your presence
    • vavṛtyām: In the act of gathering
    • asmākam: Our
    • brahma: Divine essence or spiritual power
    • pṛtanāsu: In our endeavors
    • sahyā: Together
    • asmākaṁ: Our
    • vṛṣṭir: Rain or abundance
    • divyā: Divine
    • supārā: Well-formed or auspicious
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to the deities Mitra and Varuna, who symbolize friendship, harmony, and the cosmic order.
    • The invocation of "havyajuṣṭiṁ" signifies the offering of something sacred and valuable, indicating the importance of reverence in our actions.
    • By addressing the divine beings with "namasā," the mantra emphasizes humility and respect, which are essential in any relationship, whether with the divine or with fellow humans.
    • The phrase "asmākam brahma pṛtanāsu sahyā" highlights the collective effort in pursuing spiritual and material goals, suggesting that unity and collaboration are vital for success.
    • The concluding part of the mantra, "asmākaṁ vṛṣṭir divyā supārā," expresses a desire for divine blessings in the form of abundance and prosperity, which can be interpreted as a wish for both spiritual and material well-being.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community setting, this mantra can be applied when people come together for a common cause, such as environmental conservation. The collective effort symbolizes the unity and harmony that Mitra and Varuna represent.
    • In personal relationships, the principles of respect and collaboration can be seen in partnerships where both individuals work together towards shared goals, fostering a sense of mutual support and understanding.
    • In a corporate environment, this mantra can inspire teamwork and collaboration, where employees are encouraged to work together harmoniously to achieve the company's objectives, leading to a prosperous and productive workplace.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its emphasis on unity, respect, and the pursuit of collective goals. It serves as a reminder that spiritual and material success is best achieved through collaboration and reverence.
    • In real life, the principles encapsulated in this mantra can guide individuals and communities in fostering harmonious relationships, whether in personal, social, or professional contexts.
    • Ultimately, the mantra encourages us to seek divine blessings for abundance while recognizing the importance of working together in harmony.
