अप नः शोशुचद अगम अग्ने शुशुग्ध्य ā रयिम् |अप नः शोशुचद अगम ||
apa naḥ śośucad agham agne śuśugdhy ā rayim | apa naḥ śośucad agham ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • apa: away from
    • naḥ: our
    • śośucad: may cleanse
    • agham: sin or impurity
    • agne: O Agni (Fire)
    • śuśugdhy: may you bestow
    • ā: towards
    • rayim: wealth or prosperity
  • Overall Meaning:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Agni, the fire god, asking for the removal of sins and impurities from our lives.
    • It seeks blessings for prosperity and well-being, emphasizing the importance of purity in thoughts and actions.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, this mantra can be related to the practice of self-reflection and seeking forgiveness for wrongdoings, which is essential for personal growth.
    • In a community setting, it can symbolize the collective effort to cleanse societal issues, such as corruption or injustice, and work towards a prosperous future.
  • Significance and Application:
    • The mantra highlights the importance of spiritual and moral cleanliness, which is crucial in various aspects of life, including relationships, work, and community.
    • It can be applied in rituals, meditation, or personal affirmations to foster a mindset of purity and abundance.
    • In a broader context, it serves as a reminder that seeking forgiveness and striving for improvement can lead to a more harmonious and prosperous life.
