अनप्तम अप्सु दुष्टरं सोमं पवित्र आ सृजा |पुनिहीन्द्राय पातवे ||
anaptam apsu duṣṭaraṁ somam pavitra ā sṛja | punīhīndrāya pātave ||
  • Mantra: "anaptam apsu duṣṭaraṁ somam pavitra ā sṛja | punīhīndrāya pātave ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • anaptam: unblemished, pure
    • apsu: in the waters
    • duṣṭaraṁ: difficult to attain
    • somam: the soma, a sacred drink
    • pavitra: pure, sacred
    • ā sṛja: let it be produced or created
    • punīhīndrāya: for the purifying Indra
    • pātave: for the one who is powerful or strong
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra invokes the essence of purity and the sacredness of the soma, which is often associated with divine consciousness and enlightenment.
    • The phrase "anaptam apsu" suggests a state of purity that is sought in the waters, symbolizing the quest for clarity and truth in life.
    • "Duṣṭaraṁ somam pavitra ā sṛja" indicates the desire to create or manifest this pure essence of soma, which can be interpreted as the pursuit of spiritual knowledge or enlightenment.
    • The invocation to "punīhīndrāya pātave" emphasizes the importance of purification and strength, calling upon Indra, the king of gods, who represents power and the ability to overcome obstacles.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, this mantra can be related to the pursuit of personal growth and self-improvement. Just as one seeks purity in thoughts and actions, individuals strive to overcome challenges and attain their goals.
    • In a community context, the mantra can inspire collective efforts towards creating a harmonious and pure environment, free from negativity and conflict.
    • In spiritual practices, this mantra can be chanted during meditation or rituals to invoke clarity of mind and purity of heart, aiding in the journey towards enlightenment.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for purity and strength in the pursuit of spiritual and personal goals.
    • It serves as a reminder that while the path to enlightenment may be difficult, the quest for purity and clarity is essential.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra encourages individuals and communities to strive for a higher state of being, fostering an environment of growth, understanding, and harmony.
