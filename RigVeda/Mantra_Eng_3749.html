कया नो अग्न ऋतयन्न ऋतेन भुवो नवेदाऽउचथस्य नव्यः |वेदाऽमे देव ऋतुपा ऋतूनां नाहं पतिं सन्तुर अस्य रायाḥ ||
kayā no agna ṛtayann ṛtena bhuvo navedā ucathasya navyaḥ | vedā me deva ṛtupā ṛtūnāṁ nāham patiṁ sanitur asya rāyaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • kayā: By what (means)
    • no: our
    • agna: O Fire (Agni, the divine energy)
    • ṛtayann: who establishes (or maintains) the order
    • ṛtena: by the truth/order
    • bhuvo: of the worlds
    • navedā: new knowledge
    • ucathasya: of the one who is exalted
    • navyaḥ: new
    • vedā: knowledge
    • me: my
    • deva: O God
    • ṛtupā: the protector of truth/order
    • ṛtūnāṁ: of the seasons
    • nāham: I am not
    • patiṁ: the lord
    • sanitur: of the one who is established
    • asya: of this
    • rāyaḥ: wealth or prosperity
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes Agni, the divine fire, as a symbol of energy and transformation.
    • It emphasizes the importance of truth and order (ṛta) in the universe and how they are essential for the functioning of the world.
    • The speaker seeks guidance on how to attain new knowledge and prosperity through the divine order.
    • It reflects a humble acknowledgment of one's position in the grand scheme of things, recognizing that one is not the ultimate authority but rather a seeker of truth and prosperity.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, this mantra can be applied to the pursuit of ethical business practices. Just as Agni represents the transformative power of fire, businesses can transform their operations by adhering to ethical standards and seeking new knowledge to innovate.
    • In personal life, individuals can reflect on their own journeys of self-improvement. By acknowledging the importance of truth and order, one can strive to make better decisions that lead to personal growth and prosperity.
    • In environmental contexts, the mantra can inspire actions that respect the natural order of the world, promoting sustainability and harmony with nature.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the significance of truth and order in our lives. It encourages individuals to seek knowledge and prosperity while recognizing their place within the larger universe.
    • By invoking Agni, we are reminded of the transformative power of divine energy and the importance of aligning our actions with the principles of truth and righteousness.
    • In essence, this mantra is applicable in various aspects of life, guiding us towards ethical living, personal growth, and a harmonious relationship with the world around us.
