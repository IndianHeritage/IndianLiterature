सो अङ्गिरोभिर अङ्गिरस्तमो भूद वृषा वृषभिः सकिभिः सखा सन |ऋग्मिभिर ऋग्मी गातुभिर ज्येष्ठो मरुत्वान नो भवत्व इन्द्र ऊती ||
so aṅgirobhir aṅgirastamo bhūd vṛṣā vṛṣabhiḥ sakhibhiḥ sakhā san | ṛgmibhir ṛgmī gātubhir jyeṣṭho marutvān no bhavatv indra ūtī ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • so: thus
    • aṅgirobhir: by the Angiras (a group of sages)
    • aṅgirastamo: the best among the Angiras
    • bhūd: may he become
    • vṛṣā: the strong
    • vṛṣabhiḥ: with the powerful
    • sakhibhiḥ: with friends
    • sakhā: a companion
    • san: being
    • ṛgmibhir: by the hymns
    • ṛgmī: the one who sings the hymns
    • gātubhir: by the singers
    • jyeṣṭho: the eldest
    • marutvān: the one who is powerful like the Maruts (storm deities)
    • no: for us
    • bhavatv: may he become
    • indra: Indra (the king of gods)
    • ūtī: the strength or power
  • Overall Meaning: This mantra is a prayer invoking the blessings of Indra, the king of gods, asking for strength, companionship, and the power to overcome challenges. It emphasizes the importance of community and support from friends and powerful allies in achieving success.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, this mantra can be related to teamwork. Just as the mantra calls for the support of friends and powerful allies, successful projects often rely on collaboration and the combined strengths of team members.
    • In personal life, it can be seen in the context of friendships and relationships. The mantra highlights the importance of having strong, supportive friends who can help one navigate through life's challenges.
    • In sports, athletes often rely on their teammates for support and motivation. This mantra reflects the spirit of camaraderie and the collective effort needed to achieve victory.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its emphasis on the power of unity, strength, and support from others. In real life, it serves as a reminder that we are not alone in our endeavors; we have friends, family, and allies who can help us achieve our goals. Whether in personal relationships, professional settings, or community efforts, the essence of this mantra encourages us to seek and offer support, fostering a sense of togetherness and shared strength.
