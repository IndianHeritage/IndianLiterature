सुप्रतीके वयोवृद्धा यह्वी ऋतस्य मातरा |दोषाम उषासम ईमहे ||
supratīke vayovṛdhā yahvī ṛtasya mātarā | doṣām uṣāsam īmahe ||
  • supratīke: This word can be broken down into "su" meaning good or well, and "pratīke" which refers to a sign or symbol. Together, it suggests a favorable or auspicious sign.
  • vayovṛdhā: This term refers to the growth or increase of vitality or life force. "Vayo" means age or life, and "vṛdhā" means to grow or increase.
  • yahvī: This word can be interpreted as a reference to the divine or celestial beings, often associated with the forces of nature.
  • ṛtasya: "ṛta" signifies truth, order, or cosmic law. It represents the underlying principle that governs the universe.
  • mātarā: This term means "mothers" or "nurturers," indicating a source of nourishment and care.
  • doṣām: This word translates to "faults" or "defects," suggesting the imperfections or challenges that may arise.
  • uṣāsam: "Uṣā" refers to dawn or the early morning light, symbolizing new beginnings and hope.
  • īmahe: This term means "we invoke" or "we seek," indicating a call to action or a desire to connect with the divine.

The mantra can be interpreted as a prayer or invocation for the growth of life and vitality, seeking auspicious signs and nurturing forces to overcome faults and challenges, while embracing the new beginnings represented by dawn.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios:

  • Personal Growth: Just as the mantra invokes growth and vitality, individuals can use it as a reminder to focus on their personal development, seeking positive signs and nurturing relationships that help them overcome challenges.
  • Community Support: In a community setting, this mantra can inspire collective efforts to support one another, fostering an environment where everyone can thrive and grow, much like the nurturing aspect of "mātarā."
  • New Beginnings: The reference to dawn encourages individuals to embrace new opportunities and fresh starts, whether in career, relationships, or personal endeavors.

In conclusion, this mantra emphasizes the importance of nurturing growth, seeking auspicious signs, and overcoming challenges with the support of the divine and the community. It serves as a powerful reminder of the interconnectedness of life and the potential for renewal and transformation in our daily lives.
