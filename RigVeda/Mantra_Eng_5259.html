त्वं नः पाहि अंहसो दोषावस्तर अग्हायतः |दिवा नक्तम अदाभ्य ||
tvaṁ naḥ pāhy aṁhaso doṣāvastar aghāyataḥ | divā naktam adābhya ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • tvaṁ: You
    • naḥ: our
    • pāhy: protect
    • aṁhaso: from our sins
    • doṣā: faults or defects
    • avastar: remove
    • aghāyataḥ: from the evil or harm
    • divā: during the day
    • naktam: during the night
    • adābhya: from all troubles or adversities
  • Overall Meaning: This mantra is a plea for divine protection. It calls upon a higher power to safeguard us from our own mistakes and the negative consequences that arise from them, both during the day and at night. It emphasizes the need for spiritual guidance and support in overcoming our flaws and the evils that may surround us.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • Personal Growth: In our daily lives, we often make mistakes or face challenges that can lead to negative outcomes. For instance, a student may struggle with procrastination, leading to poor academic performance. By invoking this mantra, the student seeks protection and guidance to overcome these personal flaws and improve their situation.
    • Conflict Resolution: In relationships, misunderstandings and conflicts can arise due to our faults. By reciting this mantra, individuals can seek divine intervention to help them navigate these issues, fostering forgiveness and understanding.
    • Health and Well-being: People often face health challenges that may stem from lifestyle choices. This mantra serves as a reminder to seek protection from the consequences of unhealthy habits and encourages individuals to make better choices for their well-being.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its universal appeal for protection and guidance. It serves as a reminder that we are not alone in our struggles and that seeking help from a higher power can lead to personal transformation and healing. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as personal development, conflict resolution, and health improvement, encouraging individuals to strive for betterment while acknowledging their imperfections.
