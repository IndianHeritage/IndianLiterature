अस्मा इदु सप्तिम इव श्रवस्येन्द्रायार्कं जुह्वा सम अञ्जे |वीरं दानौकसं वन्दध्यै पुरां गूर्तश्रवसं धर्माणम् ||
asmā id u saptim iva śravasyendrāyārkaṁ juhvā3 sam añje | vīraṁ dānaukasaṁ vandadhyai purāṁ gūrtaśravasaṁ darmāṇam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • asmā: from us
    • id: this
    • u: indeed
    • saptim: seven
    • iva: like
    • śravasyendrāyārkaṁ: to the powerful sun (Indra)
    • juhvā: we offer
    • sam: together
    • añje: in the right way
    • vīraṁ: the hero
    • dānaukasaṁ: endowed with wealth
    • vandadhyai: we worship
    • purāṁ: the ancient
    • gūrtaśravasaṁ: of great fame
    • darmāṇam: of righteousness
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra expresses a collective offering to the divine, particularly to the sun god, Indra, who is often associated with strength and power.
    • The mention of "seven" can symbolize completeness or perfection, as in the seven days of the week or the seven colors of the rainbow.
    • By invoking the qualities of a hero endowed with wealth and righteousness, the mantra emphasizes the importance of virtues such as bravery, generosity, and moral integrity.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community setting, this mantra can be applied during a festival where people come together to celebrate and offer their gratitude to the sun for its life-giving energy.
    • In personal life, individuals can reflect on the qualities of a hero and strive to embody them in their daily actions, such as helping others in need or standing up for what is right.
    • Organizations can use the essence of this mantra to promote teamwork and collective efforts towards a common goal, fostering a sense of unity and purpose.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for unity, strength, and righteousness. It encourages individuals and communities to come together in worship and gratitude, recognizing the power of collective action.
    • In real-life scenarios, the principles embodied in this mantra can guide personal development, community engagement, and ethical leadership, making it relevant in various aspects of life.
    • Ultimately, it serves as a reminder of the importance of virtues and the strength found in unity, inspiring individuals to strive for greatness while upholding moral values.
