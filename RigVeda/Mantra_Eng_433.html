रायस पूर्धि स्वधावोऽस्ति हि तेऽग्ने देवेष्वाप्यम् |त्वं वाजस्य श्रुत्यस्य राजसि स नो मृळ महाम्ऽऽसि ||
rāyas pūrdhi svadhāvo 'sti hi te 'gne deveṣv āpyam | tvaṁ vājasya śrutyasya rājasi sa no mṛḻa mahām̐ asi ||
  • Translation: The mantra can be translated as: "O Agni, you are the embodiment of wealth and abundance; you are the one who fulfills our desires. You are the essence of strength and power; may you grant us prosperity and protect us."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • rāyas: Wealth or prosperity.
    • pūrdhi: Fulfillment or abundance.
    • svadhāvo: Referring to the essence or nature of something.
    • 'sti: Is or exists.
    • hi: Indeed or certainly.
    • te: Your.
    • 'gne: O Agni (the fire god).
    • deveṣv: Among the gods.
    • āpyam: To be attained or achieved.
    • tvaṁ: You.
    • vājasya: Of strength or power.
    • śrutyasya: Of knowledge or wisdom.
    • rājasi: In the realm of kings or authority.
    • sa: May he.
    • no: Us.
    • mṛḻa: Grant or bestow.
    • mahām̐: Great or significant.
    • asi: You are.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, invoking this mantra can be seen as a way to seek prosperity and success in ventures. For instance, entrepreneurs may recite this mantra before launching a new product to attract wealth and positive outcomes.
    • In personal life, individuals may use this mantra during rituals or prayers to seek abundance in their lives, such as good health, happiness, and financial stability.
    • In community gatherings, this mantra can be recited to invoke blessings for collective prosperity, ensuring that the community thrives together.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its invocation of Agni, the fire god, as a source of wealth and abundance. It emphasizes the importance of seeking not just material wealth but also strength, wisdom, and protection. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as business, personal growth, and community well-being, serving as a reminder to align one's actions with the pursuit of prosperity and fulfillment. By acknowledging the divine source of abundance, individuals and communities can foster a mindset of gratitude and openness to receiving blessings in their lives.
