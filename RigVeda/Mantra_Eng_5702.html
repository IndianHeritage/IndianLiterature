एष स्य वाम पूर्वगत्वेवा सख्ये निधिर हितो माध्वी रातो अस्मे |अहेळतां मनसा यातम अर्वाग अशnantां हव्यं मानविषु विक्षु ||
eṣa sya vām pūrvagatveva sakhye nidhir hito mādhvī rāto asme | aheḻatā manasā yātam arvāg aśnantā havyam mānuṣīṣu vikṣu ||
  • eṣa sya vām pūrvagatveva sakhye: This phrase can be interpreted as "this is your friend from the past." It emphasizes the importance of friendship and connections that transcend time. In real life, this can relate to long-lasting friendships that provide support and comfort throughout life’s challenges.
  • nidhir hito mādhvī rāto asme: This translates to "the treasure is beneficial, sweet, and brings joy to us." Here, "nidhir" refers to a treasure or wealth, which can be understood not just in material terms but also as emotional and spiritual wealth. For example, the joy derived from family, love, and community can be seen as a treasure that enriches our lives.
  • aheḻatā manasā yātam arvāg: This can be interpreted as "let the mind be directed towards the good." It suggests the importance of positive thinking and intention. In practical terms, this can be applied in daily life by focusing on constructive thoughts and actions, which can lead to better mental health and well-being.
  • aśnantā havyam mānuṣīṣu vikṣu: This phrase means "the offerings made by humans are to be enjoyed." It highlights the significance of sharing and giving. In real life, this can be seen in acts of charity, community service, or simply sharing resources with those in need, which fosters a sense of community and belonging.

  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the value of friendship, the importance of positive intentions, and the joy of sharing. In a world where individualism often prevails, this mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of human relationships and the treasures that come from love, support, and community. By applying these principles in our daily lives, we can cultivate a more harmonious and fulfilling existence, enriching not only our own lives but also the lives of those around us.
