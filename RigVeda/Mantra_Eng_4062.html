एष ते देव नेताऽरथस्पतिः शं रयिः |शं राये शं स्वस्तय इषःस्तुतो मनामहे देवस्तुतो मनामहे ||
eṣa te deva netā rathaspatiḥ śaṁ rayiḥ | śaṁ rāye śaṁ svastaya iṣaḥstuto manāmahe devastuto manāmahe ||
  • eṣa te deva netā rathaspatiḥ: This phrase translates to "This is your divine leader, O Lord of the chariot." Here, "deva" refers to the divine or godly aspect, while "netā" means leader or guide. "Rathaspatiḥ" signifies the lord of the chariot, symbolizing guidance and direction in life.
  • śaṁ rayiḥ: This means "may there be prosperity." The term "śaṁ" denotes peace or auspiciousness, and "rayiḥ" refers to wealth or abundance. This part of the mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking prosperity in all aspects of life.
  • śaṁ rāye: This phrase reiterates the desire for prosperity, reinforcing the previous sentiment. It highlights the significance of abundance and well-being in one's life.
  • śaṁ svastaya: Translated as "may there be well-being," this phrase emphasizes health and harmony. "Svastaya" is derived from "svasti," which means welfare or good fortune, indicating a holistic approach to prosperity that includes physical and mental well-being.
  • iṣaḥstuto manāmahe: This translates to "we meditate upon the one who is worthy of praise." "Iṣaḥ" refers to the one who is desired or sought after, while "stuto" means praised. This part of the mantra encourages individuals to focus their thoughts and intentions on the divine or higher power that deserves reverence.
  • devastuto manāmahe: This means "we meditate upon the divine." It emphasizes the importance of connecting with the divine presence in our lives, recognizing its role in guiding us towards prosperity and well-being.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a profound message about seeking divine guidance and prosperity in life. It emphasizes the importance of recognizing a higher power as a source of direction, wealth, and well-being. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as during personal challenges, business endeavors, or health issues. By meditating on the divine and seeking blessings for prosperity and peace, individuals can cultivate a mindset that attracts abundance and harmony into their lives. This mantra serves as a reminder to align one's intentions with higher values and to seek guidance in all endeavors, fostering a sense of purpose and fulfillment.
