इन्द्रश्च सोमं पिबतं बृहस्पतेऽस्मिन्यज्ञे मन्दसाना वृषण्वसू। आ वां विशन्तु इन्दवः स्वाभुवोऽस्मे रयिं सर्ववीरं न यच्छताम्॥
indraś ca somam pibatam bṛhaspate 'smin yajñe mandasānā vṛṣaṇvasū | ā vāṁ viśantv indavaḥ svābhuvo 'sme rayiṁ sarvavīraṁ ni yacchatam ||
  • Indraś ca somam pibatam: This phrase refers to Indra, the king of the gods in Vedic mythology, drinking soma, a sacred ritual drink believed to confer immortality and divine inspiration. In real life, this can be likened to the pursuit of knowledge and enlightenment, where individuals seek experiences that elevate their understanding and consciousness.
  • Bṛhaspate 'smin yajñe mandasānā vṛṣaṇvasū: Here, Bṛhaspati, the deity of wisdom and eloquence, is invoked in the context of a yajña (sacred ritual). The term "mandasānā" suggests a state of calmness and receptivity. This can be related to moments in life where we gather for collective learning or spiritual practices, such as meditation retreats or community gatherings aimed at personal growth.
  • Ā vāṁ viśantv indavaḥ svābhuvo: This line calls for the divine blessings to enter the lives of the participants. "Indavaḥ" refers to the divine beings or blessings. In practical terms, this can be seen in how people seek guidance and support from mentors or spiritual leaders in their journey, inviting positive influences into their lives.
  • Rayiṁ sarvavīraṁ ni yacchatam: This part emphasizes the desire for prosperity and strength ("rayiṁ" meaning wealth and "sarvavīraṁ" meaning all-heroic). In everyday life, this can be interpreted as the aspiration for success and abundance in various endeavors, whether in career, relationships, or personal achievements.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a profound invocation for divine blessings, wisdom, and prosperity. Its significance lies in the collective aspiration for enlightenment and abundance, reminding us of the importance of community, shared experiences, and the pursuit of higher knowledge. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applicable during rituals, gatherings, or personal reflections where individuals seek to align themselves with higher ideals and invite positive energies into their lives.
