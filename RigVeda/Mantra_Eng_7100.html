मां नो मर्ताय रिपवे रक्षस्विनं मागशंसाय रीरधः |अस्रेदधभिस तरणिभिर यविष्ट्य शिवेभिः पाहि पायुभिः ||
mā no martāya ripave rakṣasvine māghaśaṁsāya rīradhaḥ | asredhadbhis taraṇibhir yaviṣṭhya śivebhiḥ pāhi pāyubhiḥ ||
  • Translation: The mantra can be translated as: "May we not be harmed by the enemy, protect us from the evil forces, and grant us the blessings of prosperity and well-being." This invocation seeks protection and blessings from malevolent forces.
  • Word Breakdown:
    • mā: May not
    • no: us
    • martāya: to the mortal (or death)
    • ripave: from the enemy
    • rakṣasvine: from the evil forces
    • māghaśaṁsāya: for the blessings of prosperity
    • rīradhaḥ: for well-being
    • asredhadbhis: with the blessings
    • taraṇibhir: with the help
    • yaviṣṭhya: of the auspicious
    • śivebhiḥ: with the benevolent
    • pāhi: protect
    • pāyubhiḥ: with strength
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, this mantra can be invoked during meetings or strategic planning sessions to seek protection from competition and to ensure the prosperity of the business.
    • During personal challenges, such as health issues or conflicts, individuals may recite this mantra to seek divine protection and blessings for recovery and well-being.
    • In community gatherings or festivals, this mantra can be chanted to foster a sense of unity and protection against negative influences, promoting a harmonious environment.
  • Significance:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking protection from negative forces, whether they be external enemies or internal fears and doubts.
    • It highlights the human desire for safety, prosperity, and well-being, which are universal aspirations across cultures and societies.
    • By invoking this mantra, individuals and communities can cultivate a mindset of resilience and positivity, reinforcing their commitment to overcoming challenges.
  • Conclusion:
    • The mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the need for protection and blessings in our lives.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal struggles to collective challenges faced by communities.
    • Ultimately, it encourages individuals to seek strength and support from higher powers while fostering a sense of unity and purpose in their endeavors.
