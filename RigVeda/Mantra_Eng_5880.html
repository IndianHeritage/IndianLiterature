तā हि शश्वन्त īḻत इत्त्था विप्रास ūtये |सबाधो वाजसातये ||
tā hi śaśvanta īḻata itthā viprāsa ūtaye | sabādho vājasātaye ||
  • tā hi: This phrase can be translated as "indeed they" or "truly they," indicating a sense of affirmation or emphasis on the subject being discussed.
  • śaśvanta: This word refers to "everlasting" or "eternal," suggesting a quality of permanence and stability.
  • īḻata: This term can be interpreted as "to be invoked" or "to be called upon," indicating a request for attention or recognition.
  • itthā: This word means "thus" or "in this manner," providing a context or manner in which something is to be understood or acted upon.
  • viprāsa: This term refers to "the wise" or "the learned," often used to denote those who possess knowledge and insight.
  • ūtaye: This word translates to "for the purpose of" or "for the attainment of," indicating a goal or objective.
  • sabādho: This term can be understood as "with all sounds" or "with all voices," suggesting a collective or communal aspect.
  • vājasātaye: This phrase refers to "for the purpose of strength" or "for the attainment of power," emphasizing the pursuit of vigor and capability.

The mantra can be interpreted as a call to the wise and learned individuals to invoke the everlasting strength and power that is available to them. It emphasizes the importance of collective effort and wisdom in achieving goals and attaining strength.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as:

  • Community Building: When a community comes together to achieve a common goal, invoking the wisdom of its members can lead to lasting success.
  • Leadership: Leaders can draw upon the collective strength and knowledge of their teams to make informed decisions and drive progress.
  • Personal Development: Individuals seeking personal growth can benefit from the wisdom of mentors and the support of peers, fostering an environment of learning and strength.

Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its emphasis on the power of collective wisdom and the pursuit of everlasting strength. It serves as a reminder that by coming together and invoking our shared knowledge, we can achieve greater heights and create a lasting impact in our lives and communities.
