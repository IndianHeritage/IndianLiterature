एतान्य अग्ने नवतिं सहस्रā सम प्र यच्छ वृष्ण इन्द्राय भागम् | विद्वान् पथ ऋतुषो देवयानान् अप्य औलानं दिवि देवेषु धेहि ||
etāny agne navatiṁ sahasrā sam pra yaccha vṛṣṇa indrāya bhāgam | vidvān patha ṛtuśo devayānān apy aulānaṁ divi deveṣu dhehi ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • etāny: these
    • agne: O Agni (the fire god)
    • navatiṁ: ninety
    • sahasrā: thousand
    • sam: together
    • pra: for
    • yaccha: grant
    • vṛṣṇa: strength or wealth
    • indrāya: to Indra (the king of gods)
    • bhāgam: share or portion
    • vidvān: the wise
    • patha: path
    • ṛtuśo: of the seasons
    • devayānān: of the divine beings
    • apy: also
    • aulānaṁ: the best
    • divi: in the heavens
    • deveṣu: among the gods
    • dhehi: place or establish
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Agni, the fire god, asking for a bountiful share of strength and wealth.
    • The mention of "ninety thousand" signifies a vast quantity, indicating the desire for abundance.
    • It invokes Indra, the king of gods, suggesting a request for divine support and blessings.
    • The wise are encouraged to seek the right path, especially in the context of the changing seasons, which can symbolize the cycles of life.
    • The mantra emphasizes the importance of divine beings and their role in bestowing blessings upon humanity.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a modern context, this mantra can be recited during significant life events such as weddings or housewarming ceremonies, where blessings for prosperity and strength are sought.
    • In business, entrepreneurs may invoke this mantra to attract success and abundance in their ventures, aligning with the idea of seeking divine favor.
    • During seasonal festivals, communities may chant this mantra to honor the changing seasons and seek blessings for a fruitful harvest.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine blessings and abundance in various aspects of life.
    • It serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of human efforts and divine support, encouraging individuals to seek wisdom and guidance.
    • In real-life scenarios, it can be applied in rituals, personal aspirations, and community gatherings, reinforcing the belief in the power of prayer and the importance of aligning with higher forces for success and prosperity.
