तव प्रात्नैभिर अध्वभिर अव्यो वारे प्रियः |सहस्रधारो यात तना ||
tava pratnebhir adhvabhir avyo vāre pari priyaḥ | sahasradhāro yāt tanā ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • Tava: Your
    • Pratnebhir: By the paths
    • Adhvabhir: By the journeys
    • Avyo: Unhindered
    • Vāre: In the protection
    • Pari: All around
    • Priyaḥ: Dear or beloved
    • Sahasradhāro: One who has a thousand streams or flows
    • Yāt: May go
    • Tanā: With the body or form
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the divine guidance and protection that one receives on their journey through life.
    • The phrase "tava pratnebhir adhvabhir avyo vāre" suggests that with your paths and journeys, one is unhindered and protected.
    • The mention of "sahasradhāro" indicates a source of abundant energy or blessings that flows continuously, symbolizing the divine support that surrounds us.
    • Overall, it emphasizes the importance of divine presence in our endeavors, ensuring that we are safeguarded as we navigate through life's challenges.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • When embarking on a new career path, individuals often seek guidance and support from mentors or spiritual practices, reflecting the essence of this mantra.
    • In times of uncertainty, such as during a health crisis or personal loss, people may turn to prayer or meditation, invoking a sense of protection and clarity.
    • Traveling to new places, many people perform rituals or seek blessings to ensure a safe journey, resonating with the idea of being unhindered and protected.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the divine support available to us in our journeys, encouraging us to seek guidance and protection.
    • Its significance lies in fostering a sense of security and assurance that we are not alone in our endeavors, whether they are personal, professional, or spiritual.
    • In real-life scenarios, invoking this mantra can help individuals feel more grounded and confident as they navigate through various life paths, reinforcing the belief in a higher power that guides and protects.
