यत ते मनुर यद अनीकं सुमित्रः समिधे अग्ने तद इदं नवीयः |स रेवच्छोच स गीरु जुषस्व स वाजं दर्षि स इह श्रवो धाह् ||
yat te manur yad anīkaṁ sumitraḥ samīdhe agne tad idaṁ navīyaḥ | sa revac choca sa giro juṣasva sa vājaṁ darṣi sa iha śravo dhāḥ ||
  • yat te manur - "What is your intention" or "What is your thought". This phrase invites reflection on the purpose behind actions and thoughts.
  • yad anīkaṁ - "What is not divided" or "What is whole". This suggests the importance of unity and integrity in our endeavors.
  • sumitraḥ - "Good friend" or "Well-wisher". This emphasizes the value of companionship and support in our lives.
  • samīdhe - "I invoke" or "I call upon". This indicates a plea for divine assistance or guidance.
  • agne - "O Fire" or "O Divine Light". Fire symbolizes knowledge, purity, and transformation, representing the divine presence in our lives.
  • tad idaṁ navīyaḥ - "This is new" or "This is fresh". This phrase highlights the importance of renewal and innovation in our thoughts and actions.
  • sa revac choca - "Let that be radiant" or "Let that shine forth". This encourages the pursuit of brilliance and excellence in our endeavors.
  • sa giro juṣasva - "Let that speech be pleasing" or "Let that voice be delightful". This emphasizes the significance of communication and the impact of our words on others.
  • sa vājaṁ darṣi - "Let that strength be visible" or "Let that power be evident". This speaks to the importance of demonstrating our capabilities and strengths.
  • sa iha śravo dhāḥ - "Let that fame be established here" or "Let that glory reside here". This suggests the aspiration for recognition and respect in our community.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda encapsulates the essence of intention, unity, support, and the pursuit of excellence. It serves as a reminder to reflect on our thoughts and actions, to seek divine guidance, and to foster positive relationships. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios such as teamwork in a professional setting, where collaboration and mutual support lead to success. It also encourages individuals to communicate effectively and to strive for personal growth and recognition in their respective fields. Ultimately, this mantra inspires us to embrace renewal and to shine brightly in our endeavors, fostering a harmonious and supportive environment around us.
