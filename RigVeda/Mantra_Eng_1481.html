अवासाम् मगवञ्ज जहि शर्धो यातुमतिनाम् |वैलस्थानेके आर्मके महावैलस्थे आर्मके ||
avāsām maghavañ jahi śardho yātumatīnām | vailasthānake armake mahāvailasthe armake ||
  • avāsām: This word translates to "our dwelling" or "our abode." It signifies a place of safety and comfort, akin to a home where one feels secure.
  • maghavañ: This term refers to "the powerful" or "the mighty one." It often denotes a deity or a force that possesses great strength and influence.
  • jahi: This means "to conquer" or "to overcome." It implies a sense of victory over challenges or adversities.
  • śardho: This word can be interpreted as "with strength" or "with vigor." It emphasizes the importance of resilience and determination.
  • yātumatīnām: This translates to "of those who are wise" or "of the intelligent." It highlights the value of wisdom and intellect in navigating life's challenges.
  • vailasthānake: This term refers to "in the place of strength" or "in the abode of power." It suggests a location or state where one can harness strength.
  • armake: This word means "in the shelter" or "in the protection." It conveys a sense of safety and security.
  • mahāvailasthe: This translates to "in the great power" or "in the supreme strength." It signifies a higher level of strength or authority.
  • armake: Repeated for emphasis, it reinforces the idea of being in a protective and secure environment.

The mantra speaks to the themes of strength, wisdom, and the importance of a secure dwelling. It emphasizes the need to overcome challenges with the help of powerful forces and wise guidance. In real life, this can be seen in various scenarios:


  • In personal development, individuals often seek a "safe space" where they can grow and learn, much like a home that nurtures their ambitions.
  • In the workplace, teams that operate in a supportive environment tend to perform better, as they can draw on collective strength and wisdom to tackle challenges.
  • In community settings, neighborhoods that foster a sense of security and empowerment enable residents to thrive and overcome adversities together.

In conclusion, this mantra serves as a reminder of the significance of strength, wisdom, and a secure environment in achieving success and overcoming life's challenges. It encourages individuals to seek out supportive spaces and to harness their inner power and intelligence to navigate through difficulties. The application of these principles can lead to personal growth, effective teamwork, and resilient communities.
