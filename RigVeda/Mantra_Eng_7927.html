आ नः शुष्मं नृषाह्यं वीरवंतं पुरुष्पृहं |पवस्व सोम धारया ||
ā naḥ śuṣmaṁ nṛṣāhyaṁ vīravantam puruspṛham | pavasva soma dhārayā ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā: "to us" or "for us"
    • naḥ: "our"
    • śuṣmaṁ: "the best" or "the most excellent"
    • nṛṣāhyaṁ: "the one who is heroic" or "the brave one"
    • vīravantam: "the one who possesses valor" or "the valiant"
    • puruspṛham: "the one who is full of strength" or "the powerful"
    • pavasva: "may you purify" or "may you cleanse"
    • soma: "Soma," a sacred drink or deity associated with vitality and immortality
    • dhārayā: "by holding" or "by sustaining"
  • Overall Meaning: This mantra is a prayer for the best and most heroic qualities to be bestowed upon us. It invokes the divine essence of Soma, asking for purification and strength. The mantra emphasizes the importance of valor, excellence, and the sustaining power of divine energy in our lives.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a challenging situation, such as a competitive exam or a job interview, individuals often seek inner strength and courage. This mantra can serve as a reminder to invoke their best qualities and face challenges with valor.
    • In team sports, players often rely on each other’s strengths. This mantra can symbolize the collective strength and bravery of a team, encouraging them to support one another and strive for excellence.
    • During times of personal crisis, such as illness or loss, individuals may seek spiritual guidance and strength. Reciting this mantra can provide comfort and a sense of connection to a higher power, promoting resilience and purification of the mind.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine support in cultivating bravery, excellence, and strength. It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal challenges to collective endeavors. By invoking the qualities represented in this mantra, individuals can find the courage to face difficulties, enhance their performance, and foster a sense of unity and purpose in their lives. The mantra serves as a reminder that with divine support, one can achieve greatness and overcome obstacles.
