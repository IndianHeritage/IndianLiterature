सम अञ्जन्तु विश्वे देवाः सम आपो हृदयानि नौ |सम मातरिश्वा सं धाता सम उ देष्ट्री दधातु नौ ||
sam añjantu viśve devāḥ sam āpo hṛdayāni nau | sam mātariśvā saṁ dhātā sam u deṣṭrī dadhātu nau ||
  • Translation: "May all the gods unite for our welfare; may the waters unite our hearts. May the all-pervading wind, the creator, and the giver of strength, bless us."
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sam añjantu: May they unite or come together.
    • viśve devāḥ: All the gods or divine beings.
    • sam āpo: May the waters unite.
    • hṛdayāni: Hearts or minds.
    • nau: For us or our.
    • mātariśvā: The all-pervading wind or the cosmic force.
    • saṁ dhātā: The creator or the one who establishes.
    • sam u deṣṭrī: And the giver of strength.
    • dadhātu: May bless or grant.
  • Significance:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of unity and collaboration among divine forces for the well-being of individuals and communities.
    • It highlights the interconnectedness of all elements—gods, nature (represented by waters), and the cosmic wind—suggesting that harmony in these aspects leads to a prosperous life.
  • Real-Life Application:
    • In a community setting, this mantra can be a reminder for people to work together towards common goals, fostering a sense of belonging and cooperation.
    • In personal relationships, it encourages individuals to unite their hearts and minds, promoting understanding and empathy.
    • In environmental contexts, it serves as a call to respect and protect natural resources, recognizing their role in our collective well-being.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the significance of unity, cooperation, and respect for nature in achieving personal and communal well-being.
    • Its teachings are applicable in various aspects of life, from personal relationships to community dynamics and environmental stewardship, making it a timeless guide for harmonious living.
