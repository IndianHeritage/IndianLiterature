न भोजा मम्रुर न न्यार्थम ईयुर न ऋष्यन्ति न व्यथन्ते ह भोजाः |इदं यद विश्वं bhuvanaṁ स्वश्चैतत सर्वं दक्षिणैभ्यो ददाति ||
na bhojā mamrur na nyartham īyur na riṣyanti na vyathante ha bhojāḥ | idaṁ yad viśvam bhuvanaṁ svaś caitat sarvaṁ dakṣiṇaibhyo dadāti ||
  • na bhojā mamrur: This phrase can be interpreted as "those who do not partake in the offerings." It suggests a sense of detachment from material possessions and the importance of selflessness.
  • na nyartham īyur: This translates to "they do not seek for themselves." It emphasizes the idea of altruism and the act of giving without expecting anything in return.
  • na riṣyanti: This means "they do not suffer." It indicates that those who live selflessly and without attachment are free from suffering and pain.
  • na vyathante ha bhojāḥ: This can be understood as "they are not troubled, indeed, those who partake." It reinforces the notion that true contentment comes from generosity and selflessness.
  • idaṁ yad viśvam bhuvanaṁ: This phrase translates to "this entire universe." It signifies the interconnectedness of all beings and the importance of harmony within the cosmos.
  • svaś caitat sarvaṁ: This means "this is all one's own." It suggests that everything in the universe is part of a greater whole, and we are all interconnected.
  • dakṣiṇaibhyo dadāti: This translates to "gives to the south." In many cultures, the south is associated with prosperity and abundance, indicating that generosity leads to blessings and fulfillment.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the virtues of selflessness, generosity, and the interconnectedness of all beings. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios, such as community service, charitable acts, and fostering relationships based on mutual respect and support. By embodying these principles, individuals can experience a sense of peace and fulfillment, free from the burdens of attachment and selfish desires. The significance of this mantra lies in its call to cultivate a spirit of giving and to recognize our shared existence within the universe, ultimately leading to a harmonious and prosperous life.
