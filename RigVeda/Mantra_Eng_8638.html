त्वं धियम् मनोयुजं सृजा वृष्टिं न तन्यतुः |त्वं वसूनि पार्थिवा दिव्या च सोम पुष्यसि ||
tvaṁ dhiyam manoyujaṁ sṛjā vṛṣṭiṁ na tanyatuḥ | tvaṁ vasūni pārthivā divyā ca soma puṣyasi ||
  • tvaṁ dhiyam manoyujaṁ: This phrase can be translated as "You, the intellect that is born of the mind." Here, "tvaṁ" refers to the divine or the higher consciousness, while "dhiyam" signifies intellect or wisdom. "Manoyujaṁ" indicates that this intellect is a product of the mind's creative power.
  • sṛjā vṛṣṭiṁ na tanyatuḥ: This translates to "Create the rain, do not withhold it." "Sṛjā" means to create or manifest, and "vṛṣṭiṁ" refers to rain or abundance. The phrase emphasizes the importance of abundance and prosperity in life, urging the divine to bestow blessings without restraint.
  • tvaṁ vasūni pārthivā: This means "You, the earthly treasures." "Vasūni" refers to wealth or resources, and "pārthivā" indicates that these are of the earth. This line acknowledges the material aspects of life and the need for earthly resources to sustain life.
  • divyā ca soma puṣyasi: This translates to "And you, the divine, nourish us with soma." "Divyā" means divine or celestial, while "soma" refers to a sacred drink that symbolizes immortality and nourishment. "Puṣyasi" means to nourish or sustain, indicating the need for spiritual sustenance alongside material wealth.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda encapsulates the relationship between the divine, intellect, and the material world. It highlights the importance of seeking both spiritual and material abundance. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios, such as in personal development, where one seeks to cultivate wisdom and creativity (intellect) while also striving for prosperity and well-being (earthly treasures). It encourages a balanced approach to life, where one does not neglect spiritual nourishment in the pursuit of material success. This mantra serves as a reminder to seek harmony between the material and spiritual aspects of existence, fostering a holistic approach to life and well-being.
