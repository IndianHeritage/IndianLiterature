ते नो वसूनि काम्या पुरुषचन्द्रा ऋशादसः |आ यज्ञियासो ववृत्तन ||
te no vasūni kāmyā puruścandrā riśādasaḥ | ā yajñiyāso vavṛttana ||
  • Translation: "May the desirable wealth (vasūni) come to us, like the moon (puruścandrā) that shines brightly. May we be blessed with the fruits of our sacrifices (yajñiyāso) and may our endeavors flourish."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • te: to you, for you
    • no: our
    • vasūni: wealth, riches, or desirable things
    • kāmyā: that which is desired or wished for
    • puruścandrā: the moon, symbolizing brightness and beauty
    • riśādasaḥ: shining, radiant
    • ā: may, let it be
    • yajñiyāso: fruits of sacrifices, results of our efforts
    • vavṛttana: may they flourish, may they grow
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be interpreted as a prayer for prosperity and success. For instance, a startup may recite this mantra during their launch event, seeking blessings for growth and favorable outcomes.
    • In personal life, individuals may use this mantra during significant life events, such as weddings or housewarming ceremonies, to invoke blessings for happiness and abundance in their new journey.
    • In educational settings, students may chant this mantra before exams or important presentations, wishing for clarity of thought and successful results from their hard work.
  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking blessings for wealth and success through our efforts and sacrifices. It highlights the belief that with dedication and the right intentions, one can attract prosperity and positive outcomes in various aspects of life. This mantra is applicable in numerous real-life scenarios, from personal aspirations to professional endeavors, serving as a reminder of the connection between our actions and the blessings we seek.
