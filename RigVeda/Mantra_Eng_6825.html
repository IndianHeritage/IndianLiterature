एवेन्द्राग्निभ्यां पितृवन नवीयो मन्धातृवद अंगिरस्वद अवाचि |त्रिधातुना शर्मणा पातम अस्मान वयं स्याम पतयो रयीणाम् ||
evendrāgnibhyām pitṛvan navīyo mandhātṛvad aṅgirasvad avāci | tridhātunā śarmaṇā pātam asmān vayaṁ syāma patayo rayīṇām ||
  • Mantra: "evendrāgnibhyām pitṛvan navīyo mandhātṛvad aṅgirasvad avāci | tridhātunā śarmaṇā pātam asmān vayaṁ syāma patayo rayīṇām ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • evendrāgnibhyām: "by the power of Indra and Agni"
    • pitṛvan: "like the ancestors"
    • navīyo: "new, fresh"
    • mandhātṛvad: "like Mandhātṛ, a legendary king"
    • aṅgirasvad: "like the Aṅgiras, a group of sages"
    • avāci: "may we speak"
    • tridhātunā: "with threefold support"
    • śarmaṇā: "for our well-being"
    • pātam: "may we attain"
    • asmān: "us"
    • vayaṁ: "we"
    • syāma: "may we be"
    • patayo: "like the lords"
    • rayīṇām: "of wealth"
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra invokes the divine powers of Indra and Agni, symbolizing strength and purification.
    • It emphasizes the importance of lineage and wisdom, drawing parallels to ancestors and revered sages.
    • The reference to Mandhātṛ suggests the aspiration for leadership and righteousness.
    • The phrase "may we speak" indicates a desire for clear communication and expression.
    • The concept of "threefold support" can be interpreted as the balance of body, mind, and spirit, essential for holistic well-being.
    • Ultimately, the mantra seeks prosperity and abundance, aligning oneself with the qualities of noble leaders and wise sages.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, invoking the qualities of Indra and Agni can inspire teamwork and innovation, leading to successful projects.
    • In family gatherings, honoring ancestors can strengthen bonds and instill values in younger generations.
    • In personal development, striving for the qualities of Mandhātṛ can motivate individuals to take on leadership roles in their communities.
    • Practicing clear communication in relationships can enhance understanding and reduce conflicts.
    • Maintaining a balance of physical health, mental clarity, and spiritual growth can lead to a fulfilling life.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking divine support, wisdom from ancestors, and the aspiration for prosperity.
    • Its significance lies in its application to various aspects of life, from personal growth to community leadership.
    • By embodying the qualities represented in the mantra, individuals can navigate life's challenges with strength and clarity.
    • Ultimately, it serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of our actions, heritage, and aspirations.
