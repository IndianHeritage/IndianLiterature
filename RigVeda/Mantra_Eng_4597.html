तव ह त्यद इन्द्र विश्वम् आजौ सस्तो धुनिचुमुरी या ह सिष्वप् |दीदायद इत तुभ्यं सोमैभिः सुन्वन् दभीतिर इध्मभृतिः पक्थ्य अ1र्कैः ||
tava ha tyad indra viśvam ājau sasto dhunīcumurī yā ha siṣvap | dīdayad it tubhyaṁ somebhiḥ sunvan dabhītir idhmabhṛtiḥ pakthy a1rkaiḥ ||
  • tava ha tyad indra viśvam ājau: This phrase can be translated to "O Indra, you who are the master of the universe." Here, Indra is recognized as a powerful deity, often associated with strength and leadership. In real life, this can be likened to a leader or a guiding figure in a community or organization who brings people together for a common purpose.
  • sasto dhunīcumurī yā ha siṣvap: This part means "you who are the source of all prosperity and well-being." It emphasizes the importance of abundance and health in life. For example, a community that works together to ensure the well-being of its members reflects this idea, as they create a supportive environment that fosters growth and prosperity.
  • dīdayad it tubhyaṁ somebhiḥ: This translates to "may we offer you our praises and offerings." It signifies the act of gratitude and respect towards a higher power or authority. In everyday life, this can be seen in practices such as giving thanks before meals or expressing appreciation to mentors and leaders who guide us.
  • sunvan dabhītir idhmabhṛtiḥ pakthy a1rkaiḥ: This phrase can be interpreted as "may we be blessed with strength and courage." It highlights the need for resilience in facing challenges. For instance, individuals who overcome personal struggles, such as health issues or financial difficulties, embody this strength and courage in their lives.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of leadership, prosperity, gratitude, and resilience. Its significance lies in the recognition of a higher power that guides and supports us in our endeavors. In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as community building, personal development, and leadership roles. By embodying the qualities of strength, gratitude, and a commitment to the well-being of others, individuals can create a positive impact in their surroundings, fostering a sense of unity and purpose.
