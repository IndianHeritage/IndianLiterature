ते सिषपन्त जोषम् आ यजत्रा ऋतस्य धाराः सुदुघा दूहानाः |ज्येष्ठं वो अद्य मह आ वसूनाम आ गन्तना समनसो यति ष्ठ ||
te sīṣapanta joṣam ā yajatrā ṛtasya dhārāḥ sudughā duhānāḥ | jyeṣṭhaṁ vo adya maha ā vasūnām ā gantana samanaso yati ṣṭha ||
  • te sīṣapanta: This phrase can be interpreted as "those who are engaged in the act of worship" or "those who are devoted." It emphasizes the importance of dedication and commitment in spiritual practices.
  • joṣam ā yajatrā: This refers to the "joyful offerings" made by the worshippers. It highlights the significance of joy and positivity in rituals, suggesting that the act of worship should be filled with happiness and sincerity.
  • ṛtasya dhārāḥ: This translates to "the flow of truth" or "the stream of righteousness." It signifies the importance of adhering to truth and moral values in life, suggesting that one should always strive to live in accordance with dharma (righteousness).
  • sudughā duhānāḥ: This phrase can be understood as "the one who produces abundance" or "the one who brings forth prosperity." It emphasizes the idea that through sincere efforts and devotion, one can achieve prosperity and success.
  • jyeṣṭhaṁ vo adya: This means "the elder or the most revered among you today." It suggests the importance of respecting elders and those who have more experience, as they can provide guidance and wisdom.
  • maha ā vasūnām: This translates to "greatness among the wealth." It signifies the idea that true wealth is not just material but also includes spiritual and moral richness.
  • ā gantana samanaso: This phrase can be interpreted as "to reach a state of harmony." It emphasizes the importance of unity and balance in life, suggesting that one should strive for harmony in relationships and within oneself.
  • yati ṣṭha: This means "may you remain steadfast." It is a call to maintain one's commitment and dedication, regardless of the challenges faced.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of devotion, truth, prosperity, respect for elders, and the pursuit of harmony. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios such as:

  • In personal relationships, fostering joy and sincerity in interactions can lead to stronger bonds.
  • In professional settings, adhering to ethical practices and respecting experienced colleagues can create a positive work environment.
  • In community life, promoting unity and harmony can lead to collective growth and prosperity.

Overall, this mantra serves as a reminder to live a life filled with devotion, truth, and respect, ultimately leading to personal and communal well-being.
