गावो भागो गावा इन्द्रो मे अच्छान गावः सोमस्य प्रथमस्य भक्षः |इमा या गावः स जनासा इन्द्र इच्छामीद धृदा मनसा चिद इन्द्रम् ||
gāvo bhago gāva indro me acchān gāvaḥ somasya prathamasya bhakṣaḥ | imā yā gāvaḥ sa janāsa indra icchāmīd dhṛdā manasā cid indram ||
  • gāvo bhago gāva indro me acchān gāvaḥ somasya prathamasya bhakṣaḥ
    • gāvo - Refers to "cows," which are often seen as symbols of wealth and nourishment in Vedic literature.
    • bhago - Means "prosperity" or "wealth," indicating the abundance that comes from nature.
    • gāva indro - "Indra, the lord of the cows," signifies the divine protector and provider of resources.
    • me acchān - Translates to "may they be good for me," expressing a desire for blessings and positive outcomes.
    • gāvaḥ somasya prathamasya bhakṣaḥ - Refers to the "first offering of the soma," which is a sacred drink in Vedic rituals, symbolizing spiritual nourishment.
  • imā yā gāvaḥ sa janāsa indra icchāmīd dhṛdā manasā cid indram
    • imā yā gāvaḥ - "These cows," indicating a direct connection to the resources and blessings being referred to.
    • sa janāsa - "For the people," highlighting the communal aspect of prosperity and well-being.
    • indra icchāmīd - "I desire Indra," expressing a longing for divine intervention and support.
    • dhṛdā manasā - "With a steadfast mind," indicating determination and focus in seeking blessings.
    • cid indram - "The essence of Indra," referring to the ultimate realization of divine presence and support in life.

Conclusion:

This mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking divine blessings for prosperity and well-being, particularly through the symbolism of cows and the sacred soma. In real life, this can be applied in various contexts such as:

  • In agriculture, where the health of livestock and crops is crucial for sustenance and economic stability.
  • In community gatherings, where collective prayers and rituals are performed to invoke blessings for the entire community.
  • In personal endeavors, where individuals seek clarity and strength in their pursuits, aligning their intentions with divine support.

Overall, the mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of nature, community, and the divine, encouraging individuals to cultivate a mindset of gratitude and determination in their lives.
