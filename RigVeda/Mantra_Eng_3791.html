मन्त्र: ये अग्ने नेरयन्ति ते वृद्धा उग्रस्य शवसः |अप द्वेषो अप ह्वरो अन्यव्रतस्य सश्चिरे ||
ye agne nerayanti te vṛddhā ugrasya śavasaḥ | apa dveṣo apa hvaro 'nyavratasya saścire ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ye: those who
    • agne: O Agni (the fire god)
    • nerayanti: who lead or guide
    • te: you
    • vṛddhā: the wise or the elders
    • ugrasya: of the powerful or fierce
    • śavasaḥ: of the dead or the departed
    • apa: away from
    • dveṣo: hatred
    • apa: away from
    • hvaro: from the harmful
    • anyavratasya: of one who has different vows
    • saścire: may you be long-lasting
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra addresses Agni, the fire god, who is often seen as a mediator between humans and the divine.
    • The phrase "ye agne nerayanti" suggests that those who are wise and powerful guide us towards the right path.
    • The mantra emphasizes the importance of steering clear of negative influences, such as hatred and harm, which can lead one astray.
    • It also highlights the significance of adhering to one's vows and commitments, suggesting that true strength lies in maintaining integrity.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace setting, a leader who embodies the qualities of Agni can guide their team towards success by fostering a positive environment and discouraging negativity.
    • In personal relationships, individuals can strive to avoid hatred and harmful behaviors, promoting understanding and compassion instead.
    • In community service, volunteers can commit to their vows of service, ensuring that their actions contribute positively to society.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of guidance from wise individuals and the need to avoid negative influences in our lives.
    • It encourages us to uphold our commitments and act with integrity, which is essential for personal growth and societal harmony.
    • In essence, the mantra is applicable in various aspects of life, including personal development, professional conduct, and community engagement, promoting a life of purpose and positivity.
