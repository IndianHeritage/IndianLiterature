उद उ तिष्ठ सवितः श्रुध्य अस्य हिरण्यपाणे प्रभरिताव ऋतस्य |व्य उर्वीम् पृथ्वीम् अमतिं सृजान आ नृभ्यो मर्तभोजनं सुवानः ||
ud u tiṣṭha savitaḥ śrudhy a1sya hiraṇyapāṇe prabhṛtāv ṛtasya | vy u1rvīm pṛthvīm amatiṁ sṛjāna ā nṛbhyo martabhojanaṁ suvānaḥ ||
  • ud u tiṣṭha savitaḥ: This phrase translates to "Arise, O Savita (the Sun)". Here, "ud" means to rise or awaken, and "tiṣṭha" means to stand or be present. Savita refers to the Sun, symbolizing light, energy, and life.
  • śrudhy a1sya hiraṇyapāṇe: This part means "Listen to this, O one with golden hands". "Hiraṇyapāṇe" refers to the Sun, often depicted as having golden hands, representing the life-giving energy it provides. The call to "listen" signifies the importance of paying attention to the divine guidance and wisdom.
  • prabhṛtāv ṛtasya: This translates to "in the realm of truth". "Prabhṛtāv" refers to the domain or realm, while "ṛtasya" means truth or cosmic order. This emphasizes the connection between the Sun and the fundamental truths of the universe.
  • vy u1rvīm pṛthvīm amatiṁ sṛjāna: This means "You create the vastness of the earth". "Urvīm" refers to the vastness or expanse, "pṛthvīm" means earth, and "sṛjāna" means to create. This highlights the Sun's role in sustaining life on Earth through its energy.
  • ā nṛbhyo martabhojanaṁ suvānaḥ: This translates to "Bestow upon us the nourishment of life". "Nṛbhyo" refers to humans, "martabhojanaṁ" means the sustenance of life, and "suvānaḥ" means to bestow or grant. This signifies the Sun's role in providing the essential energy for life, urging us to seek its blessings for sustenance.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda emphasizes the significance of the Sun as a source of life, energy, and truth. It calls upon the Sun to rise and provide guidance, nourishment, and sustenance to humanity. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in daily rituals, meditation, or as a reminder to appreciate the natural world and the energy that sustains us. It encourages us to seek truth and harmony in our lives, recognizing the interconnectedness of all beings and the vital role of the Sun in our existence.
