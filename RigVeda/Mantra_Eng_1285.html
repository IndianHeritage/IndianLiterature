अजोहवीं नासत्यā करā वाम महे यामन पुरुभुजā पुरंढिः |श्रुतं तच छासुर इव वध्रिमत्यā हिरण्यहस्तम अश्विनाव अदत्तम् ||
ajohavīn nāsatyā karā vām mahe yāman purubhujā puraṁdhiḥ | śrutaṁ tac chāsur iva vadhrimatyā hiraṇyahastam aśvināv adattam ||
  • ajohavīn nāsatyā: This phrase can be interpreted as "the one who is not to be deceived" or "the one who is truthful." In real life, this can relate to the importance of honesty and integrity in our actions and relationships. For example, a leader who is transparent and truthful fosters trust within their team.
  • karā vām mahe: This translates to "may your hands be powerful." This can signify the strength and capability of individuals to take action. In a practical sense, this can be seen in the context of teamwork, where each member's contributions are vital for achieving a common goal, such as in a community service project.
  • yāman purubhujā puraṁdhiḥ: This can be understood as "the one who enjoys the fruits of labor." It emphasizes the rewards of hard work and dedication. For instance, a student who diligently studies and prepares for exams ultimately reaps the benefits through good grades and knowledge.
  • śrutaṁ tac chāsur iva vadhrimatyā: This phrase suggests "that which is heard is like a powerful weapon." It highlights the significance of knowledge and wisdom. In real life, this can be related to the idea that education and learning empower individuals to make informed decisions, similar to how a skilled negotiator uses their knowledge to achieve favorable outcomes.
  • hiraṇyahastam aśvināv adattam: This translates to "the golden hands of the Ashvins." The Ashvins are twin deities associated with health and medicine. This can symbolize the healing and nurturing aspects of life. In a modern context, this can be seen in the work of healthcare professionals who use their skills to heal and support others, reflecting the divine qualities of the Ashvins.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the values of truthfulness, strength in action, the rewards of hard work, the power of knowledge, and the healing aspects of life. Its significance lies in its application to everyday scenarios, encouraging individuals to embody these qualities in their personal and professional lives. By embracing these principles, one can contribute positively to society, foster trust in relationships, and promote well-being in their community.
