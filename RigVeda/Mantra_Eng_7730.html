रयिं नश् चित्रम् अश्विनम् इन्दो विश्वायुम् आ भर |अथा नो वस्यसस् कृधि ||
rayiṁ naś citram aśvinam indo viśvāyum ā bhara | athā no vasyasas kṛdhi ||
  • Translation: The mantra translates to "O Ashvins, bring us the diverse wealth of the world; may you grant us the best of all that is desirable."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • rayiṁ: wealth or prosperity, often associated with material and spiritual abundance.
    • naś: our, indicating possession or belonging.
    • citram: diverse or colorful, suggesting variety and richness in life.
    • aśvinam: referring to the Ashvins, the twin deities in Vedic mythology known for their healing and benevolent qualities.
    • indo: O Indra, a reference to the divine, often associated with strength and power.
    • viśvāyum: all that is desirable or beneficial, encompassing all forms of good.
    • ā bhara: may you bring or bestow, a call for divine intervention.
    • athā: then or thus, indicating a transition or a request.
    • no: our, again emphasizing possession.
    • vasyasas: the best or most favorable, indicating a desire for the highest quality.
    • kṛdhi: may you create or make, a plea for action from the divine.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be invoked during meetings or negotiations to seek diverse opportunities and prosperity for the organization.
    • In personal life, individuals may recite this mantra during times of financial difficulty, hoping to attract wealth and abundance into their lives.
    • During community gatherings or festivals, this mantra can be chanted to invoke blessings for collective prosperity and well-being.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine assistance in attracting wealth and prosperity. It emphasizes the importance of seeking not just material wealth but also the richness of experiences and relationships. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as personal aspirations, business endeavors, and community welfare. By invoking the blessings of the Ashvins, individuals and communities can aspire to achieve their highest potential and create a life filled with abundance and joy.
