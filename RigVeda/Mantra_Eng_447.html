को वो वर्षिष्ठ आ नरो दिवश्च गमश्च धूतयः |यत सीम अन्तं न धूनुथ ||
ko vo varṣiṣṭha ā naro divaś ca gmaś ca dhūtayaḥ | yat sīm antaṁ na dhūnutha ||
  • Mantra: "ko vo varṣiṣṭha ā naro divaś ca gmaś ca dhūtayaḥ | yat sīm antaṁ na dhūnutha ||"
  • Translation: "Who among you is the best among men, both in the heavens and on earth? What is the limit that you do not cross?"
  • Word Breakdown:
    • ko: Who
    • vo: among you
    • varṣiṣṭha: the best or the most excellent
    • ā: in
    • naro: men
    • divaś: of the heavens
    • ca: and
    • gmaś: on earth
    • ca: and
    • dhūtayaḥ: those who are pure or enlightened
    • yat: what
    • sīm: limit or boundary
    • antaṁ: end or conclusion
    • na: not
    • dhūnutha: to cross or to transgress
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra poses a question about excellence and virtue among individuals.
    • It invites reflection on who stands out as the best among people, both in spiritual and worldly contexts.
    • The mention of "heavens" and "earth" signifies the duality of existence, urging individuals to consider their actions in both realms.
    • The phrase "what is the limit that you do not cross" challenges individuals to think about their moral and ethical boundaries.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace, a leader may embody the qualities of the "best among men" by demonstrating integrity, compassion, and excellence in their work.
    • In community service, individuals who go above and beyond to help others exemplify the virtues highlighted in this mantra.
    • In personal relationships, being the best version of oneself can mean being supportive, honest, and respectful towards others.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra encourages self-reflection and the pursuit of excellence in all aspects of life.
    • It serves as a reminder to uphold moral values and to strive for personal growth while being mindful of the impact of our actions on others.
    • In a broader context, it can be applied in various fields such as leadership, education, and community engagement, promoting a culture of integrity and responsibility.
