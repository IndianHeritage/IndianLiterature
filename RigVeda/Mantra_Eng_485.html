यो वाघते ददाति सूनरं वसु स धत्ते अक्षिति श्रवः |तस्मा इळां सुवीराम आ यजामहे सुप्रतूर्तिम अनहसम् ||
yo vāghate dadāti sūnaraṁ vasu sa dhatte akṣiti śravaḥ | tasmā iḻāṁ suvīrām ā yajāmahe supratūrtim anehasam ||
  • yo vāghate: This phrase can be translated as "who gives speech" or "who bestows words." In a real-life context, this can refer to the power of communication and expression, highlighting the importance of effective dialogue in personal and professional relationships.
  • dadāti sūnaraṁ vasu: This means "gives beautiful wealth." Here, "wealth" can be interpreted not just in material terms but also as knowledge, wisdom, and virtues. For example, a teacher imparts knowledge to students, enriching their lives and future prospects.
  • sa dhatte akṣiti śravaḥ: This translates to "he who holds the strength of fame." This emphasizes the idea that true strength lies in one's reputation and the impact one has on others. In modern life, this can be seen in leaders who inspire and motivate their teams, thereby building a strong legacy.
  • tasmā iḻāṁ suvīrām: This phrase means "therefore, we invoke the strong and excellent." It suggests a call to action, encouraging individuals to seek out and honor those who embody strength and excellence. In practice, this could be seen in community initiatives that recognize and celebrate local heroes or role models.
  • ā yajāmahe: This translates to "we worship" or "we offer." This indicates a sense of reverence and gratitude towards the forces that provide us with strength and wisdom. In everyday life, this can manifest in practices of gratitude, such as acknowledging the contributions of others in our lives.
  • supratūrtim anehasam: This means "the excellent and radiant." It signifies the pursuit of excellence and brilliance in all endeavors. In a real-world scenario, this can be applied to striving for excellence in one's career, education, or personal development, aiming to shine brightly in one's chosen path.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of communication, the value of knowledge, the strength of reputation, and the pursuit of excellence. It encourages individuals to recognize and honor those who contribute positively to society while also inspiring them to strive for their own greatness. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as in education, leadership, community service, and personal growth, reminding us of the interconnectedness of our actions and the impact they have on the world around us.
