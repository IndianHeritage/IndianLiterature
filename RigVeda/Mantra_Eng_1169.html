मन्तर: नराशंसं वाजिनं वाजयन्न इह क्षयद्वीरम् पूषणं सुम्नैर इमहे | रथं न दुर्गाद वसवः सुदानवो विश्वस्मान नो अंहसो निष पिपर्तन ||
narāśaṁsaṁ vājinaṁ vājayann iha kṣayadvīram pūṣaṇaṁ sumnair īmahe | rathaṁ na durgād vasavaḥ sudānavo viśvasmān no aṁhaso niṣ pipartana ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • narāśaṁsaṁ: The one who is worthy of praise or the best among men.
    • vājinaṁ: The one who possesses strength or vigor.
    • vājayann: The one who brings forth strength or energy.
    • iha: Here or in this world.
    • kṣayadvīram: The one who is a hero in overcoming obstacles.
    • pūṣaṇaṁ: The nourisher or the one who provides sustenance.
    • sumnair: With good intentions or auspiciousness.
    • īmahe: We invoke or we seek.
    • rathaṁ: The chariot or vehicle.
    • na: Not.
    • durgād: From difficulties or obstacles.
    • vasavaḥ: The deities or the forces of nature.
    • sudānavo: The ones who are well-disposed or favorable.
    • viśvasmān: The one who is all-pervading or universal.
    • no: Our.
    • aṁhaso: Of the light or energy.
    • niṣ pipartana: May they not take away or diminish.
  • Overall Meaning: This mantra is a prayer invoking the qualities of strength, nourishment, and protection. It seeks the blessings of a divine force that is praised for its heroic qualities and ability to overcome challenges. The mantra emphasizes the importance of auspiciousness and the desire for a chariot (symbolizing progress or journey) that is free from obstacles, guided by benevolent forces.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, when embarking on a new project, teams often seek the support of mentors or leaders who embody the qualities of strength and guidance, similar to the divine qualities invoked in this mantra.
    • During challenging times, individuals may turn to spiritual practices or community support that embodies the nourishing and protective aspects of this mantra, helping them to overcome difficulties.
    • In sports, athletes often invoke their inner strength and seek the support of their coaches and teammates, paralleling the invocation of strength and protection in this mantra.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for strength, nourishment, and protection in the face of challenges. It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal struggles to professional endeavors, reminding us of the importance of seeking support and embodying heroic qualities. By invoking these divine attributes, individuals can foster resilience and navigate through life's obstacles with confidence and grace.
