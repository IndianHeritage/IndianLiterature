एषा दिवो दुहिता प्रत्य अदर्शि ज्योतिर वसाना समना पुरस्तात् |ऋतस्य पन्थाम् अन्व एति साधु प्रजानतीव न दिशो मिनाति ||
eṣā divo duhitā praty adarśi jyotir vasānā samanā purastāt | ṛtasya panthām anv eti sādhu prajānatīva na diśo mināti ||
  • eṣā divo duhitā: This phrase translates to "this daughter of the sky." In a broader sense, it can be interpreted as a representation of divine inspiration or enlightenment that comes from a higher realm.
  • praty adarśi: This means "has shown" or "revealed." It signifies the act of unveiling knowledge or truth that was previously hidden.
  • jyotir vasānā: Translated as "the light that dwells," this refers to the essence of illumination, both literally and metaphorically. It can symbolize wisdom, knowledge, or spiritual insight.
  • samanā purastāt: This means "equal in all directions" or "present everywhere." It suggests the omnipresence of this divine light or knowledge.
  • ṛtasya panthām: This translates to "the path of truth." It emphasizes the importance of following a righteous and truthful path in life.
  • anv eti sādhu: This means "follows the good." It indicates the pursuit of goodness and virtue in one's actions and decisions.
  • prajānatīva: This can be interpreted as "the one who knows" or "the wise one." It refers to the enlightened individual who understands the deeper truths of existence.
  • na diśo mināti: This translates to "does not stray from the directions." It signifies the importance of staying true to one's path and not being distracted by falsehoods or temptations.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of seeking divine knowledge and truth, represented by the "daughter of the sky." It encourages individuals to pursue a righteous path, guided by wisdom and virtue. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied to personal development, decision-making, and ethical living. By striving for enlightenment and adhering to the path of truth, individuals can navigate life's challenges with clarity and purpose. This mantra serves as a reminder to remain focused on one's goals and values, ensuring that one does not deviate from the path of righteousness.
