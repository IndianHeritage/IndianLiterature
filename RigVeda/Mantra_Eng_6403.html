देवेभिर देव्य अदिते 'रिष्टभर्मन्ना गहि |स्मत सूरिभिः पुरुप्रिय सुशर्मभिः ||
devebhir devy adite 'riṣṭabharmann ā gahi | smat sūribhiḥ purupriye suśarmabhiḥ ||
  • Mantra: "devebhir devy adite 'riṣṭabharmann ā gahi | smat sūribhiḥ purupriye suśarmabhiḥ ||"
  • Translation: This mantra can be translated as: "O Divine Ones, come to us with your blessings, and grant us the protection of the righteous. May we be favored by the strong and the benevolent."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • Devebhir: By the divine beings or gods.
    • Devy: Divine or godly.
    • Adite: A reference to Aditi, the mother of gods, symbolizing boundless nature and cosmic order.
    • 'Riṣṭabharmann: In the context of righteousness and protection.
    • Ā gahi: Come to us or approach us.
    • Smat: With our thoughts or intentions.
    • Sūribhiḥ: By the strong or powerful.
    • Purupriye: To those who are dear to many or beloved.
    • Suśarmabhiḥ: With auspiciousness or good fortune.
  • Contextual Meaning:
    • This mantra invokes the divine for blessings and protection, emphasizing the importance of righteousness and strength in life.
    • It reflects a desire for divine intervention in achieving success and well-being, highlighting the interconnectedness of human efforts and divine grace.
    • In real life, this can be seen in various situations such as seeking guidance before starting a new venture, praying for safety during travels, or asking for support in challenging times.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • Before a significant event, such as a wedding or a business launch, individuals often seek blessings from higher powers, similar to the invocation in this mantra.
    • In times of crisis, people may turn to prayer or meditation, asking for strength and protection, resonating with the essence of this mantra.
    • Communities often come together in rituals or ceremonies to invoke divine blessings for collective well-being, reflecting the communal aspect of seeking divine favor.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the human aspiration for divine support in the pursuit of righteousness and success.
    • It is applicable in various life scenarios where individuals or communities seek strength, protection, and auspiciousness.
    • Ultimately, it serves as a reminder of the importance of aligning one's actions with higher principles and the belief in the power of divine grace in achieving one's goals.
