तां आशीराम पुरोळाशम् इन्द्रेमं सोमं श्रीणीहि |रेवन्तं हि त्वा श्रिणोमि ||
tām̐ āśiram puroḻāśam indremaṁ somaṁ śrīṇīhi | revantaṁ hi tvā śṛṇomi ||
  • tām̐ āśiram: This phrase can be interpreted as "that which is auspicious" or "that which brings prosperity." In real life, this can refer to positive environments or situations that foster growth and well-being, such as a supportive family or a nurturing workplace.
  • puroḻāśam: This term signifies "the one who is the source of all wealth" or "the one who provides sustenance." In practical terms, this could relate to a mentor or leader who guides others towards success, much like a teacher who inspires students to achieve their potential.
  • indremaṁ: This word can be understood as "the divine" or "the celestial." It represents higher powers or forces that influence our lives. For example, one might see this in the context of nature, where the beauty and complexity of the universe inspire awe and reverence.
  • somaṁ: Soma is often associated with a sacred drink that grants immortality and enlightenment. In modern terms, this could symbolize experiences or knowledge that elevate our consciousness, such as meditation or profound learning experiences that transform our understanding of life.
  • śrīṇīhi: This term means "to listen" or "to pay attention." It emphasizes the importance of being receptive to wisdom and guidance. In everyday life, this can be seen in the practice of active listening in conversations, which fosters better relationships and understanding.
  • revantaṁ: This word can be interpreted as "the one who brings joy" or "the one who is radiant." In real life, this could refer to individuals who spread positivity and happiness, such as friends or community leaders who uplift others through their actions and words.
  • hi tvā śṛṇomi: This phrase translates to "I hear you" or "I acknowledge you." It signifies the importance of recognition and validation in relationships. In practical terms, this can be applied in workplaces where employees feel valued and heard, leading to increased morale and productivity.

Conclusion: The mantra "tām̐ āśiram puroḻāśam indremaṁ somaṁ śrīṇīhi | revantaṁ hi tvā śṛṇomi ||" encapsulates the essence of seeking auspiciousness, wisdom, and joy in life. Its significance lies in the reminder to cultivate positive environments, listen to divine guidance, and acknowledge the contributions of others. In real-life scenarios, this mantra encourages us to foster supportive relationships, embrace learning experiences, and spread positivity, ultimately leading to a more harmonious and fulfilling existence.
