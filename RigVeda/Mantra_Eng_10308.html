एता त्या ते श्रुत्यानि केवला यद एक एकम् अकृत्नोर अयज्ञम् |मासां विधानम् अदधा अधि dyavi त्वया विभिन्नम् भरति प्रधानम् पिता ||
etā tyā te śrutyāni kevalā yad eka ekam akṛṇor ayajñam | māsāṁ vidhānam adadhā adhi dyavi tvayā vibhinnam bharati pradhim pitā ||
  • etā tyā te śrutyāni: This phrase can be translated as "these are your teachings." It emphasizes the importance of knowledge and wisdom passed down through generations. In real life, this can be likened to the teachings of mentors or educators who guide individuals in their personal and professional lives.
  • kevalā yad eka ekam akṛṇor ayajñam: This part means "only that which is done alone, without any sacrifice." It suggests the significance of individual effort and self-reliance. For example, in a workplace, an employee who takes initiative and works independently can achieve success without relying solely on team efforts.
  • māsāṁ vidhānam adadhā: This translates to "the arrangement of the months." It signifies the cyclical nature of time and the importance of planning and organization. In everyday life, this can be seen in how people plan their schedules, set goals, and manage their time effectively to achieve desired outcomes.
  • adhi dyavi tvayā vibhinnam: This means "in the heavens, you are distinct." It highlights the uniqueness of each individual and their contributions to the universe. In real life, this can be related to how each person has their own talents and abilities that can make a difference in the world, much like how diverse skills in a team lead to greater innovation.
  • bharati pradhim pitā: This phrase translates to "the father who nourishes." It symbolizes the nurturing aspect of leadership and guidance. In practical terms, this can be seen in parental roles or mentorship, where individuals provide support and encouragement to help others grow and succeed.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of knowledge, individual effort, time management, uniqueness, and nurturing guidance. In real life, these principles can be applied in various scenarios, such as education, workplace dynamics, personal development, and leadership. By embodying these values, individuals can foster a more productive and harmonious environment, ultimately leading to personal and collective success.
