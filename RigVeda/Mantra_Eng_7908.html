एष इन्द्राय वायवे स्वर्जित परि शिच्यते |पवित्रे दक्षसाधनः ||
eṣa indrāya vāyave svarjit pari ṣicyate | pavitre dakṣasādhanaḥ ||
  • eṣa: This word translates to "this" or "this one," indicating a specific focus or subject.
  • indrāya: Referring to Indra, the king of the gods in Vedic tradition, symbolizing strength, power, and leadership.
  • vāyave: This means "to Vayu," the god of wind, representing movement, breath, and the vital force of life.
  • svarjit: This term means "illuminated" or "made bright," suggesting enlightenment or clarity.
  • pari: This translates to "around" or "in all directions," indicating a comprehensive or holistic approach.
  • ṣicyate: This means "is poured" or "is offered," suggesting a ritualistic act of giving or dedication.
  • pavitre: This word means "pure" or "sacred," emphasizing the importance of purity in actions and intentions.
  • dakṣasādhanaḥ: This translates to "skillful means" or "capable actions," highlighting the significance of skill and competence in achieving goals.

  • Meaning: The mantra speaks to the act of offering or dedicating something pure and skillful to the divine forces represented by Indra and Vayu. It emphasizes the importance of clarity, purity, and competence in our actions.
  • Real-life Example: In a professional setting, this mantra can be applied when one approaches a project with clarity of purpose (svarjit) and a commitment to ethical practices (pavitre). For instance, a team working on a community service project may dedicate their efforts to the betterment of society, ensuring that their actions are both skillful (dakṣasādhanaḥ) and pure in intention.

  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for a holistic approach to life, where one dedicates their actions to higher ideals while maintaining purity and skill. It serves as a reminder that our efforts should be illuminated by clarity and directed towards the greater good. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various fields such as leadership, community service, and personal development, encouraging individuals to act with integrity and competence.
