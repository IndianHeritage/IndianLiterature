मादयस्व सुत सचा शवसे शूर राधसे |विद्मा हि त्वा पुरूवसुम उप कामान ससृज्महे 'था नो 'विता भव ||
mādayasva sute sacā śavase śūra rādhase | vidmā hi tvā purūvasum upa kāmān sasṛjmahe 'thā no 'vitā bhava ||
  • mādayasva: This term can be interpreted as "may you be pleased" or "may you be joyful." It signifies a call for happiness and contentment.
  • sute: This word means "in the offspring" or "in the progeny." It emphasizes the importance of lineage and the continuation of life through generations.
  • sacā: This translates to "with truth" or "with sincerity." It highlights the value of honesty and integrity in our actions and relationships.
  • śavase: This term refers to "in strength" or "in vigor." It denotes the importance of physical and mental strength in overcoming challenges.
  • śūra: This means "brave" or "valiant." It calls for courage and valor in facing life's adversities.
  • rādhase: This can be interpreted as "to the one who is worthy" or "to the one who is deserving." It suggests recognizing and honoring those who have earned respect.
  • vidmā hi tvā: This phrase means "we know you indeed." It expresses a deep understanding and recognition of the divine or the higher power being addressed.
  • purūvasum: This term refers to "the one who is abundant" or "the one who is prosperous." It signifies the aspiration for abundance and prosperity in life.
  • upa kāmān: This translates to "towards desires" or "for our wishes." It indicates the pursuit of fulfilling one's desires and aspirations.
  • sasṛjmahe: This means "we create" or "we generate." It emphasizes the act of creation and manifestation in our lives.
  • 'thā no 'vitā bhava: This phrase can be interpreted as "may you not abandon us" or "may you not forsake us." It is a plea for support and guidance from the divine.

Conclusion: This mantra encapsulates a profound message about the importance of joy, truth, strength, bravery, and abundance in our lives. It serves as a reminder to seek happiness and fulfillment while remaining grounded in honesty and courage. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as personal development, leadership, and community building. For instance, in a workplace setting, fostering a culture of integrity and support can lead to a more productive and harmonious environment. Similarly, in personal relationships, embodying these values can strengthen bonds and create a nurturing atmosphere. Ultimately, this mantra encourages us to strive for a life filled with purpose, connection, and resilience, while seeking divine guidance along the way.
