वयं जयेम तवया युजाऽवृतम अस्माकं अंशं उद अवा भरे-भरे |अस्मभ्यं इन्द्र वरिवः सुगं कृधि प्र शत्रूणां मघवन वृष्ण्यां रुजा ||
vayaṁ jayema tvayā yujā vṛtam asmākam aṁśam ud avā bhare-bhare | asmabhyam indra varivaḥ sugaṁ kṛdhi pra śatrūṇām maghavan vṛṣṇyā ruja ||
  • Transliteration: vayaṁ jayema tvayā yujā vṛtam asmākam aṁśam ud avā bhare-bhare | asmabhyam indra varivaḥ sugaṁ kṛdhi pra śatrūṇām maghavan vṛṣṇyā ruja ||
  • Meaning:
    • vayaṁ jayema: "May we conquer" - This phrase expresses a desire for victory and success.
    • tvayā yujā: "with your support" - It emphasizes the importance of divine assistance in achieving goals.
    • vṛtam asmākam aṁśam: "the portion of ours that is protected" - This refers to the blessings and protection that one seeks from the divine.
    • ud avā bhare-bhare: "may you bestow abundantly" - A plea for abundance and prosperity in life.
    • asmabhyam indra: "to us, O Indra" - Indra is the king of gods in Vedic literature, symbolizing strength and power.
    • varivaḥ sugaṁ kṛdhi: "create for us a good fortune" - A request for favorable circumstances and good luck.
    • pra śatrūṇām: "against our enemies" - This indicates a desire for protection and victory over adversaries.
    • maghavan vṛṣṇyā ruja: "O Maghavan, remove our pain" - Maghavan refers to a deity associated with wealth and abundance, asking for relief from suffering.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In competitive environments, such as sports or business, individuals often seek support from mentors or divine forces to achieve success, similar to the plea for divine assistance in the mantra.
    • During challenging times, people may pray for protection and guidance, reflecting the desire for abundance and safety expressed in the mantra.
    • In community gatherings, invoking blessings for collective success and protection against adversities mirrors the communal aspect of the mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of seeking divine support in overcoming challenges and achieving success.
    • It serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of individual efforts and divine grace in the pursuit of goals.
    • In real life, it encourages individuals to remain hopeful and proactive, while also recognizing the importance of community and support systems in overcoming obstacles.
