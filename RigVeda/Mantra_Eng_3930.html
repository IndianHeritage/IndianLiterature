वधूर् इयम् पतिम् इच्छन्त्य् एति य इं वहाते महिषीम् इषिराम् |आस्य श्रवस्याद् रथ आ च घोषात् पुरू सहस्राः परिवर्तयाते ||
vadhūr iyam patim icchanty eti ya īṁ vahāte mahiṣīm iṣirām | āsya śravasyād ratha ā ca ghoṣāt purū sahasrā pari vartayāte ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • vadhūr: A bride or a woman.
    • iyam: This.
    • patim: Husband.
    • icchanty: Desires or wishes.
    • eti: Goes or moves.
    • ya: Who.
    • īṁ: The divine or auspicious.
    • vahāte: Carries or brings.
    • mahiṣīm: The great or powerful.
    • iṣirām: The strong or mighty.
    • āsya: Of this.
    • śravasyād: Of fame or glory.
    • ratha: Chariot.
    • ā: From.
    • ca: And.
    • ghoṣāt: From the sound or call.
    • purū: Many or numerous.
    • sahasrā: Thousands.
    • pari: Around or about.
    • vartayāte: Move or revolve.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of a bride who desires her husband and moves towards him, embodying the essence of love and companionship.
    • The reference to the divine and powerful suggests that the relationship is not just physical but also spiritual, emphasizing the importance of mutual respect and support.
    • The imagery of a chariot and the sound of many voices indicates a celebration, perhaps a wedding or a gathering, where the couple is surrounded by family and friends, symbolizing community support.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern weddings, the bride often walks down the aisle, symbolizing her journey towards her partner, much like the mantra describes.
    • The idea of community support can be seen in how families come together to celebrate a marriage, reinforcing the bonds of love and friendship.
    • In relationships, the desire for companionship and mutual support is crucial, reflecting the essence of this mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of love, companionship, and community in relationships.
    • It serves as a reminder of the importance of mutual respect and support in a partnership.
    • In real life, it encourages individuals to cherish their relationships and the bonds they share with their loved ones, fostering a sense of unity and celebration.
