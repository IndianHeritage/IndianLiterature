सं गच्छस्व पितृभिः सं यमेनेष्टापूर्तेन परम व्योमन् |हित्वायावद्यं पुनर अस्तम एहि सं गच्छस्व तंवा सुवर्चाः ||
saṁ gacchasva pitṛbhiḥ saṁ yameneṣṭāpūrtena parame vyoman | hitvāyāvadyam punar astam ehi saṁ gacchasva tanvā suvarcāḥ ||
  • saṁ gacchasva pitṛbhiḥ: This phrase translates to "go together with the ancestors." It emphasizes the importance of familial and ancestral connections. In real life, this can be seen in family gatherings, where individuals come together to honor their heritage and maintain bonds with their relatives.
  • saṁ yameneṣṭāpūrtena: This means "with self-control and through the fulfillment of desires." It suggests that one should pursue their goals with discipline and integrity. For example, a student studying diligently for exams embodies this principle by balancing their desires for leisure with the need for academic success.
  • parame vyoman: This translates to "in the supreme sky" or "in the highest realm." It signifies the pursuit of higher knowledge and spiritual enlightenment. In practical terms, this can relate to individuals seeking wisdom through education, meditation, or philosophical inquiry.
  • hitvāyāvadyam: This phrase means "having abandoned the unworthy." It encourages individuals to let go of negative influences or harmful habits. For instance, someone overcoming addiction exemplifies this by choosing a healthier lifestyle and distancing themselves from toxic relationships.
  • punar astam ehi: This translates to "come back again." It suggests a cycle of renewal or rebirth. In real life, this can be seen in the context of personal growth, where individuals learn from their past mistakes and strive to improve themselves continuously.
  • saṁ gacchasva tanvā suvarcāḥ: This means "go together with a body that is radiant." It emphasizes the importance of health and vitality. In practical terms, this can relate to maintaining physical fitness and mental well-being, which are essential for a fulfilling life.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of living a life that honors one's heritage while striving for personal growth and spiritual enlightenment. It encourages individuals to maintain strong familial ties, pursue their goals with discipline, and let go of negativity. In today's fast-paced world, these principles are applicable in various scenarios, such as family dynamics, personal development, and the pursuit of knowledge. By embodying the teachings of this mantra, individuals can lead a more meaningful and fulfilling life, fostering connections with their past while striving for a brighter future.
