मन्त्र: एतो न्व इन्द्रं स्तवाम शुद्धं शुद्धेन साम्ना |शुद्धैर उक्थैर वावृद्ध्वांसं शुद्ध आशीर्वान ममत्तु ||
eto nv indraṁ stavāma śuddhaṁ śuddhena sāmnā | śuddhair ukthair vāvṛdhvāṁsaṁ śuddha āśīrvān mamattu ||
  • Mantra: "eto nv indraṁ stavāma śuddhaṁ śuddhena sāmnā | śuddhair ukthair vāvṛdhvāṁsaṁ śuddha āśīrvān mamattu ||"
  • Translation: "We invoke Indra, the pure one, with pure hymns; may he, the pure one, grant us pure blessings."
  • Explanation:
    • Indra: In Vedic literature, Indra is the king of the gods and the god of rain and thunderstorms. He symbolizes strength, power, and the ability to overcome obstacles. In real life, we can relate to Indra as a representation of leadership and the pursuit of excellence.
    • Stavāma: This term means "we praise" or "we invoke." It signifies the act of calling upon a higher power for assistance or guidance. In our daily lives, this can be seen in practices such as prayer or meditation, where individuals seek support from a divine source.
    • Śuddhaṁ: This word translates to "pure." Purity in this context refers to both the intentions behind the invocation and the nature of the hymns used. In real life, purity can be associated with honesty, integrity, and clarity of purpose in our actions and thoughts.
    • Sāmnā: This term means "hymns" or "songs." It emphasizes the importance of using uplifting and positive expressions when seeking blessings. In everyday life, this can be reflected in the way we communicate with others, promoting positivity and encouragement.
    • Āśīrvān: This word means "blessings." The mantra expresses a desire for blessings that are pure and beneficial. In practical terms, this can relate to seeking good fortune, health, and success in various endeavors.
  • Significance: The mantra emphasizes the importance of purity in thought, word, and deed when invoking divine assistance. It teaches us that our intentions and the quality of our expressions matter when seeking help or blessings. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios, such as:
    • In personal relationships, approaching others with pure intentions fosters trust and understanding.
    • In professional settings, maintaining integrity and clarity in communication can lead to better teamwork and collaboration.
    • In spiritual practices, focusing on purity of thought and action can enhance one's connection to the divine and lead to more meaningful experiences.
