ऊर्जं नो द्यौश्च पृथिवी च पिन्वताम् पिता माता विश्वविदा सुदंशसा |संराणे रोधसी विश्वशम्भुवाऽ सनिं वाजं रयिमस्मे सम इन्भताम् ||
ūrjaṁ no dyauś ca pṛthivī ca pinvatām pitā mātā viśvavidā sudaṁsasā | saṁrarāṇe rodasī viśvaśambhuvā saniṁ vājaṁ rayim asme sam invatām ||
  • Meaning of the Mantra:
    • ūrjaṁ: Energy or strength.
    • no: Our.
    • dyauś: Sky or heaven.
    • ca: And.
    • pṛthivī: Earth.
    • ca: And.
    • pinvatām: May we receive or may it be bestowed upon us.
    • pitā: Father.
    • mātā: Mother.
    • viśvavidā: All-knowing or omniscient.
    • sudaṁsasā: With good intentions or auspiciousness.
    • saṁrarāṇe: In the midst of the world.
    • rodasī: The two realms (heaven and earth).
    • viśvaśambhuvā: All-pervading or universal prosperity.
    • saniṁ: Together or in unity.
    • vājaṁ: Strength or vigor.
    • rayim: Wealth or abundance.
    • asme: For us.
    • sam invatām: May it come to us or may we be blessed with.
  • Detailed Explanation:
    • This mantra invokes the energies of both the sky and the earth, emphasizing the importance of balance and harmony in our lives.
    • It acknowledges the nurturing aspects of both the father and mother, symbolizing the duality of creation and the need for both masculine and feminine energies.
    • The phrase "viśvavidā sudaṁsasā" highlights the importance of wisdom and good intentions in our actions, suggesting that knowledge and benevolence lead to prosperity.
    • By calling upon the "rodasī" (the two realms), the mantra signifies the interconnectedness of all existence, urging us to recognize our place within the universe.
    • The invocation of "vājaṁ rayim" emphasizes the desire for strength and abundance, which can be interpreted as a call for both physical and spiritual wealth.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a family setting, this mantra can be reflected in the way parents nurture their children, providing them with both emotional support (mother) and guidance (father).
    • In a community, the mantra can inspire collective efforts towards prosperity, where individuals come together to support one another, fostering a sense of unity and shared purpose.
    • In personal development, the mantra encourages individuals to seek knowledge and act with good intentions, leading to a fulfilling and prosperous life.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its holistic approach to life, emphasizing the balance of energies, the importance of wisdom, and the pursuit of abundance.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from family dynamics to community building and personal growth.
    • By embodying the principles of this mantra, individuals can cultivate a life of harmony, prosperity, and fulfillment.
