तं अह्यन भुरिजोर्धियā संवासानं विवस्वतः |पतिं वाचो अदाभ्यम् ||
tam ahyan bhurijor dhiyā saṁvasānaṁ vivasvataḥ | patiṁ vāco adābhyam ||
  • tam ahyan: This phrase can be translated as "that which is bright" or "that which is illuminating." It signifies the pursuit of knowledge and enlightenment.
  • bhurijor: This term refers to "the one who is rich" or "the one who possesses abundance." It emphasizes the importance of wealth, not just in material terms but also in wisdom and virtues.
  • dhiyā: This word means "with intellect" or "with understanding." It highlights the role of intellect in guiding one's actions and decisions.
  • saṁvasānaṁ: This can be interpreted as "coming together" or "gathering." It suggests the importance of community and collaboration in achieving goals.
  • vivasvataḥ: This term refers to "the sun" or "the radiant one." It symbolizes the source of life, energy, and clarity, representing the ultimate truth and enlightenment.
  • patiṁ: This word means "lord" or "master." It signifies authority and guidance, suggesting the need for a leader or a guiding principle in life.
  • vāco: This translates to "speech" or "words." It emphasizes the power of communication and expression in human relationships.
  • adābhyam: This term can be understood as "without obstacles" or "unhindered." It signifies the importance of clear communication and the removal of barriers in understanding.

  • Significance: This mantra emphasizes the pursuit of knowledge, the importance of community, and the power of communication. It encourages individuals to seek enlightenment and wisdom while fostering collaboration and understanding among people.
  • Real-life Application: In today's world, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as in educational settings where collaboration and open communication are essential for learning. It can also be relevant in workplaces where teamwork and clear communication lead to success. Furthermore, in personal relationships, the principles of understanding and shared knowledge can strengthen bonds and resolve conflicts.

  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of knowledge, community, and communication. By embodying these principles, individuals can navigate life's challenges more effectively and contribute positively to society.
