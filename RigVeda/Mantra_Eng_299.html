विष्वेभिर अग्ने अग्निभिर इमं यज्ञं इदं वचः |चनो धाः सहसो यहो ||
viśvebhir agne agnibhir imaṁ yajñam idaṁ vacaḥ | cano dhāḥ sahaso yaho ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • viśvebhir: By all, universally
    • agne: O Agni (the fire god)
    • agnibhir: By the fires
    • imaṁ: This
    • yajñam: Sacrifice or offering
    • idaṁ: This
    • vacaḥ: Word or speech
    • cano: May we sing or praise
    • dhāḥ: May we hold or maintain
    • sahaso: With strength or power
    • yaho: O, may it be so
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes Agni, the fire god, who is considered a mediator between humans and the divine.
    • It emphasizes the importance of collective participation in rituals and sacrifices, suggesting that all beings should come together in harmony.
    • The phrase "may we sing or praise" indicates a call for unity in devotion and respect towards the divine.
    • The mantra also highlights the significance of words and intentions in rituals, suggesting that the way we articulate our prayers and offerings is crucial.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern contexts, this mantra can be related to community gatherings where people come together for a common cause, such as environmental clean-up drives or charity events.
    • In family settings, it can be seen during festivals where families unite to perform rituals, reinforcing bonds and shared values.
    • In workplaces, it can symbolize teamwork and collaboration, where collective efforts lead to achieving common goals.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of unity, collective effort, and the power of words in rituals and daily life.
    • It teaches us that when we come together with a shared purpose and intention, we can create a positive impact in our communities and lives.
    • By invoking Agni, we are reminded of the transformative power of fire, symbolizing purification and the energy needed to achieve our goals.
    • Overall, this mantra encourages us to maintain strength and harmony in our actions and words, fostering a sense of togetherness in all endeavors.
