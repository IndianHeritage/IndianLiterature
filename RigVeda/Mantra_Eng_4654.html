त्वं तदुक्तम् इन्द्र बर्हणा कः प्र यच्छतā सहस्रā शूर दर्षि |अव गिरेर दासं शम्बरं हन् प्रावो दिवोदासं चित्राभिर ऊती ||
tvaṁ tad uktham indra barhaṇā kaḥ pra yac chatā sahasrā śūra darṣi | ava girer dāsaṁ śambaraṁ han prāvo divodāsaṁ citrābhir ūtī ||
  • tvaṁ - You
  • tad - That
  • uktham - Word or hymn
  • indra - Indra, the king of gods
  • barhaṇā - With great glory
  • kaḥ - Who
  • pra - Indeed
  • yac - What
  • chatā - Thousand
  • sahasrā - Thousand
  • śūra - Brave or valiant
  • darṣi - Seer or observer
  • ava - Down
  • girer - Of the mountain
  • dāsaṁ - Servant or devotee
  • śambaraṁ - The demon or obstacle
  • han - Kill or destroy
  • prāvo - Powerful
  • divodāsaṁ - The one who is a servant of the heavens
  • citrābhir - With various or colorful
  • ūtī - Praise or offerings

This mantra is a call to Indra, the king of gods, to bestow his blessings and strength upon the devotee. It emphasizes the power of words and hymns in invoking divine assistance. The mantra highlights the importance of bravery and the ability to overcome obstacles, represented by the demon Śambara. The devotee seeks the strength to defeat challenges and attain glory.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios where individuals face significant challenges or obstacles. For instance:

  • In a competitive work environment, one might recite this mantra to seek the courage and strength to excel and overcome professional hurdles.
  • Students preparing for exams can use this mantra to invoke focus and determination, helping them to conquer their academic challenges.
  • In personal struggles, such as health issues or emotional turmoil, reciting this mantra can serve as a source of motivation to face and overcome difficulties.

Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its ability to inspire courage and resilience. It serves as a reminder that with divine support and inner strength, one can overcome any obstacle. By invoking the blessings of Indra, individuals can find the motivation to face challenges head-on, making it applicable in various aspects of life, from personal struggles to professional endeavors.
