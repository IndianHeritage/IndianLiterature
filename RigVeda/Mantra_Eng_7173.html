यच चिद्धि शाश्वतामसीन्द्र साधारणस्त्वम् |तं त्वा वयं हवामहे ||
yac cid dhi śaśvatām asīndra sādhāraṇas tvam | taṁ tvā vayaṁ havāmahe ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yac: that which
    • cid: indeed
    • dhi: intellect or understanding
    • śaśvatām: of eternity or permanence
    • asīndra: O Indra (the king of gods)
    • sādhāraṇas: common or universal
    • tvam: you
    • taṁ: him or that
    • tvā: you (accusative)
    • vayaṁ: we
    • havāmahe: we invoke or we call upon
  • Overall Meaning: This mantra can be interpreted as a call to the divine, specifically to Indra, acknowledging his eternal nature and universal significance. It expresses a desire to invoke his presence and blessings.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • Invocation in Rituals: In many Hindu rituals, invoking deities like Indra is common. For instance, during a Yajna (sacrificial fire ceremony), priests chant mantras to invite divine energies, similar to how this mantra calls upon Indra.
    • Seeking Guidance: In personal life, individuals often seek guidance from higher powers during challenging times. This mantra serves as a reminder to turn to the divine for wisdom and support, much like how people pray for clarity in decision-making.
    • Community and Unity: The term "sādhāraṇas" emphasizes the universal aspect of the divine. In community gatherings, such as festivals, people come together to celebrate and invoke blessings, reflecting the collective spirit of seeking divine favor.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its invocation of divine wisdom and support. It teaches us the importance of recognizing the eternal and universal aspects of the divine in our lives. By calling upon Indra, we acknowledge the need for guidance and strength, applicable in various real-life scenarios such as rituals, personal challenges, and community gatherings. This mantra encourages us to foster a connection with the divine, reminding us that we are not alone in our endeavors and that seeking higher wisdom is a path to fulfillment and success.
