उत नो वाजसातये पवस्व बृहतीर इषः |द्यमद इन्दो सुवीर्यम् ||
uta no vājasātaye pavasva bṛhatīr iṣaḥ | dyumad indo suvīryam ||
  • uta - This word can be translated as "may" or "let us." It signifies a request or a desire for something to happen.
  • no - This means "our," indicating possession or a collective aspect.
  • vājasātaye - This term refers to "for the purpose of strength" or "for the attainment of vigor." It emphasizes the pursuit of power and energy.
  • pavasva - This is a verb meaning "to flow" or "to move." It suggests a dynamic action, often associated with the movement of energy or blessings.
  • bṛhatīr - This word means "great" or "vast," indicating something of significant magnitude or importance.
  • iṣaḥ - This translates to "the will" or "the intention," highlighting the importance of purpose and desire in achieving goals.
  • dyumad - This term means "shining" or "radiant," often associated with light and brilliance.
  • indo - This refers to "Indra," the king of gods in Vedic literature, symbolizing strength, power, and leadership.
  • suvīryam - This means "good strength" or "excellent vigor," emphasizing the quality of strength that is desirable.

  • Meaning: The mantra expresses a collective desire for strength and vigor, invoking the blessings of the divine for greatness and radiance. It is a prayer for the flow of energy and power in our lives, seeking the support of Indra, the god of strength.
  • Real-life examples:
    • In a team setting, this mantra can be applied when a group of individuals comes together to achieve a common goal, such as completing a project. They may invoke this energy to enhance their collaboration and effectiveness.
    • In personal development, individuals may recite this mantra as a form of motivation to cultivate their inner strength and resilience, especially during challenging times.

  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for collective strength and the pursuit of greatness. It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from teamwork to personal growth, reminding us of the importance of intention and the divine support we can seek in our endeavors. By embracing the qualities of vigor and radiance, we can navigate life's challenges with greater resilience and purpose.
