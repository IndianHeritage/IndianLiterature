गम्भीरैण न उरुणामात्रिन् प्रेषो यन्द्धि सुतपावन वाजान् |स्थā ū षु ūर्ध्व ūtī अरिषण्यन्न अक्तोर व्युष्टौ परितकम्यायाम् ||
gambhīreṇa na uruṇāmatrin preṣo yandhi sutapāvan vājān | sthā ū ṣu ūrdhva ūtī ariṣaṇyann aktor vyuṣṭau paritakmyāyām ||
  • gambhīreṇa: This word translates to "with depth" or "profoundly." It signifies the importance of deep understanding and wisdom in our actions and thoughts.
  • na uruṇāmatrin: This phrase can be interpreted as "not being distracted by the trivial." It emphasizes the need to focus on what truly matters in life, avoiding superficial concerns.
  • preṣo yandhi: This means "the one who sends forth." It refers to the divine or higher power that guides and directs our actions and intentions.
  • sutapāvan: This term signifies "well-prepared" or "purified." It suggests that one should approach life with a clear mind and a pure heart, ready to face challenges.
  • vājān: This word means "strength" or "power." It highlights the importance of inner strength and resilience in overcoming obstacles.
  • sthā ū: This can be interpreted as "stand firm." It encourages stability and steadfastness in one's beliefs and actions.
  • ṣu ūrdhva: This phrase translates to "upward" or "higher." It signifies the aspiration to rise above challenges and strive for greater achievements.
  • ūtī: This means "to overcome" or "to conquer." It emphasizes the importance of perseverance and determination in achieving one's goals.
  • ariṣaṇyann: This term refers to "the one who is free from fear." It encourages individuals to face their fears and uncertainties with courage.
  • aktor: This word means "the doer" or "the one who acts." It signifies the importance of taking action and being proactive in life.
  • vyuṣṭau: This translates to "in the light" or "in clarity." It emphasizes the need for clarity of thought and purpose in our endeavors.
  • paritakmyāyām: This phrase can be interpreted as "in the midst of challenges." It signifies the importance of maintaining focus and determination even when faced with difficulties.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates profound wisdom about navigating life with depth, focus, and strength. It teaches us to remain steadfast in our beliefs, to act with clarity, and to overcome challenges with courage. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various situations, such as during personal struggles, professional challenges, or when pursuing goals. By embodying the principles of this mantra, individuals can cultivate resilience, clarity, and a sense of purpose, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling and meaningful life.
