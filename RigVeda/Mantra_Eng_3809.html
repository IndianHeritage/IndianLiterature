तं त्वा शोचिष्ठ दीदिवः सुम्नाय नूनम इमहे सकिभ्यः ||
taṁ tvā śociṣṭha dīdivaḥ sumnāya nūnam īmahe sakhibhyaḥ ||
  • taṁ - This word translates to "that" or "him," referring to a divine entity or a higher power.
  • tvā - This means "you," indicating a direct address to the divine or the object of worship.
  • śociṣṭha - This term can be interpreted as "the most radiant" or "the most brilliant," suggesting a quality of purity and enlightenment.
  • dīdivaḥ - This word means "of the heavens" or "celestial," indicating a connection to the divine or the cosmic order.
  • sumnāya - This translates to "for auspiciousness" or "for good fortune," emphasizing the desire for positive outcomes.
  • nūnam - This word means "indeed" or "certainly," reinforcing the conviction behind the statement.
  • īmahe - This translates to "we meditate" or "we contemplate," indicating a practice of reflection or worship.
  • sakhibhyaḥ - This means "to friends" or "to companions," suggesting a communal aspect of worship or shared intentions.

The mantra can be understood as a call to a divine entity, acknowledging its brilliance and celestial nature, while expressing a desire for auspiciousness and good fortune. The speaker, along with their companions, engages in meditation or contemplation, seeking blessings and positive outcomes.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios:

  • Personal Growth: Individuals can use this mantra during meditation to focus on their aspirations and seek clarity in their life goals.
  • Group Gatherings: In communal settings, such as family gatherings or team meetings, reciting this mantra can foster a sense of unity and shared purpose, encouraging positive interactions.
  • Rituals and Ceremonies: This mantra can be incorporated into rituals for auspicious beginnings, such as weddings or new ventures, to invoke blessings and good fortune.

In conclusion, this mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking divine guidance and auspiciousness in our lives. It highlights the significance of community and shared intentions in the pursuit of enlightenment and positive outcomes. By meditating on this mantra, individuals can cultivate a deeper connection with the divine and enhance their personal and communal experiences.
