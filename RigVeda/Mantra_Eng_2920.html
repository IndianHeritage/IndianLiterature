प्रति धानां भारत तुयम् अस्मै पुरोळाशं वीरतमाय नृणाम् |दिवे-दिवे सदृशीः इन्द्र तुभ्यं वर्धन्तु त्वा सोमपेयाय धृष्णो ||
prati dhānā bharata tūyam asmai puroḻāśaṁ vīratamāya nṛṇām | dive-dive sadṛśīr indra tubhyaṁ vardhantu tvā somapeyāya dhṛṣṇo ||
  • Mantra: "prati dhānā bharata tūyam asmai puroḻāśaṁ vīratamāya nṛṇām | dive-dive sadṛśīr indra tubhyaṁ vardhantu tvā somapeyāya dhṛṣṇo ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • prati: towards
    • dhānā: wealth or prosperity
    • bharata: O Bharata (a term often referring to the people of India)
    • tūyam: your
    • asmai: to him
    • puroḻāśaṁ: the one who is endowed with strength
    • vīratamāya: for the brave or the heroic
    • nṛṇām: of men
    • dive-dive: day by day
    • sadṛśīr: similar or like
    • indra: O Indra (the king of gods)
    • tubhyaṁ: to you
    • vardhantu: may they increase
    • tvā: you
    • somapeyāya: for the one who drinks soma (a sacred drink)
    • dhṛṣṇo: of the brave
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra is a prayer directed towards Indra, the king of gods in Vedic tradition, asking for prosperity and strength.
    • The phrase "prati dhānā bharata" emphasizes the importance of directing wealth and prosperity towards the people, particularly the Bharata, symbolizing the collective identity of the Indian populace.
    • By invoking "vīratamāya nṛṇām," the mantra highlights the valor and bravery of men, suggesting that true strength comes from courage and heroism.
    • The repetition of "dive-dive" signifies a daily invocation, indicating that the blessings of prosperity and strength are sought continuously, not just in times of need.
    • In the context of "sadṛśīr indra," the mantra seeks blessings that are akin to those granted to Indra, the epitome of power and success.
    • The concluding part, "vardhantu tvā somapeyāya dhṛṣṇo," reinforces the idea that those who are brave and partake in the sacred (symbolized by soma) should be blessed with growth and abundance.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, this mantra can be applied to encourage teamwork and bravery among employees, fostering an environment where everyone works towards common goals with courage and integrity.
    • In personal life, individuals can recite this mantra to seek strength and prosperity in their endeavors, whether it be in education, career, or personal relationships.
    • Communities can use this mantra during festivals or gatherings to invoke blessings for collective prosperity and strength, reinforcing the bonds of unity and courage among members.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the importance of seeking strength and prosperity not just for oneself, but for the community as a whole.
    • Its significance lies in the continuous pursuit of bravery and abundance, encouraging individuals to embody these qualities in their daily lives.
    • By invoking divine blessings, it inspires a collective effort towards achieving greatness, making it applicable in various aspects of life, from personal growth to community development.
