शुक्रेभिरङ्गैः राजा आततन्वान् क्रतुम्पुनानः कविभिः पवित्रैः | शोचिर्वसानः पर्यायुरपां श्रियो मिमीते बृहतीरअनूनाः ||
śukrebhir aṅgai raja ātatanvān kratum punānaḥ kavibhiḥ pavitraiḥ | śocir vasānaḥ pary āyur apāṁ śriyo mimīte bṛhatīr anūnāḥ ||
  • śukrebhir aṅgai raja ātatanvān: This phrase can be translated to "with pure limbs, the one who is radiant." Here, "śukrebhir" refers to purity and brightness, while "aṅgai" means limbs or parts of the body. In real life, this can symbolize a person who is healthy and virtuous, embodying qualities that are admirable and inspiring.
  • kratum punānaḥ kavibhiḥ pavitraiḥ: This translates to "the intellect that is purified by the wise." "Kratu" refers to intellect or will, and "punānaḥ" means purified. This suggests that wisdom and knowledge are essential for personal growth. For example, in a workplace, a team that values learning and ethical practices tends to perform better and create a positive environment.
  • śocir vasānaḥ pary āyur apāṁ: This can be interpreted as "the one who is adorned with purity, surrounded by longevity and wealth." "Śocir" means adorned or decorated, while "āyur" refers to life or longevity. This implies that a life lived with integrity and purity attracts prosperity. In everyday life, individuals who practice honesty and kindness often find themselves surrounded by supportive relationships and opportunities.
  • śriyo mimīte bṛhatīr anūnāḥ: This phrase translates to "the great one who brings forth abundance." "Śriyo" refers to wealth or prosperity, and "bṛhatīr" means greatness. This suggests that those who embody these virtues not only achieve personal success but also contribute to the well-being of others. For instance, community leaders who work selflessly for the betterment of society often inspire others to follow suit, creating a ripple effect of positive change.

  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of purity, wisdom, and integrity in achieving personal and communal prosperity. It teaches that a virtuous life leads to not only individual success but also the enrichment of the community. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various contexts, such as in personal relationships, professional environments, and community service. By embodying these qualities, individuals can create a positive impact on their surroundings, fostering a culture of respect, support, and growth.
