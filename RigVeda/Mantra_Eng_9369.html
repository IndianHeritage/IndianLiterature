आ त एतु मनः पुनः क्रत्वे दक्षाय जीवसे |ज्योत् च सूर्यं दृष्टे ||
ā ta etu manaḥ punaḥ kratve dakṣāya jīvase | jyok ca sūryaṁ dṛśe ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā: This can be interpreted as "from" or "out of."
    • ta: This means "that" or "those."
    • etu: This translates to "come" or "arrive."
    • manaḥ: This refers to the "mind" or "intellect."
    • punaḥ: This means "again" or "once more."
    • kratve: This relates to "sacrifice" or "effort."
    • dakṣāya: This means "to the skilled" or "to the capable."
    • jīvase: This translates to "for life" or "for living."
    • jyok: This means "shine" or "radiate."
    • ca: This means "and."
    • sūryaṁ: This refers to the "sun."
    • dṛśe: This means "to the sight" or "to the vision."
  • Overall Meaning: The mantra can be interpreted as a call to the mind to come forth again with skill and effort for the purpose of life, while also invoking the radiance of the sun to illuminate our vision. It emphasizes the importance of mental clarity and skillful action in achieving a fulfilling life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • Mindfulness and Focus: In today's fast-paced world, practicing mindfulness can help individuals focus their minds on the present moment, enhancing their ability to make skilled decisions. For instance, a student preparing for exams can use techniques like meditation to clear their mind and improve concentration.
    • Skill Development: The mantra encourages individuals to hone their skills. For example, a musician dedicating time to practice can relate to the idea of "kratve dakṣāya," as they put in effort to master their craft, leading to a more fulfilling artistic expression.
    • Seeking Guidance: Just as the sun provides light, seeking guidance from mentors or teachers can illuminate one's path. A young professional might seek advice from experienced colleagues to navigate their career effectively.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its holistic approach to life, emphasizing the interplay between the mind, skill, and the guiding light of knowledge. It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal development to professional growth. By invoking the essence of this mantra, individuals can strive for clarity, skillfulness, and purpose in their lives, ultimately leading to a more enlightened and fulfilling existence.
