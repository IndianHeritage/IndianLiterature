स्वस्ति पन्थाम् अनु चरेम सूर्याचन्द्रमसाव इव |पुनर ददताघ्नता जानता सं गमेमहि ||
svasti panthām anu carema sūryācandramasāv iva | punar dadatāghnatā jānatā saṁ gamemahi ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • svasti: auspiciousness, well-being
    • panthām: path, way
    • anu: after, following
    • carema: may we move, may we proceed
    • sūrya: sun
    • candra: moon
    • masā: month, time
    • iva: like, as if
    • punar: again, anew
    • dadatā: giving, providing
    • ghnatā: destruction, harm
    • jānatā: knowing, understanding
    • saṁ: together, in union
    • gamemahi: we may go, we may reach
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra expresses a prayer for auspiciousness and guidance on the path of life.
    • It invokes the celestial bodies, the sun and the moon, symbolizing light, knowledge, and the cyclical nature of time.
    • The phrase "may we move like the sun and the moon" suggests a desire for steady progress and harmony in life.
    • The latter part of the mantra emphasizes the importance of understanding and overcoming obstacles, as well as the need for unity in our endeavors.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, this mantra can be applied when embarking on a new project. Teams can invoke this prayer for guidance and success, ensuring they work together harmoniously.
    • In personal life, individuals may recite this mantra during significant transitions, such as starting a new job or moving to a new city, seeking auspiciousness and clarity in their journey.
    • In educational contexts, students can use this mantra before exams or important presentations, wishing for the wisdom and clarity represented by the sun and moon.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for auspiciousness and guidance in all aspects of life.
    • It serves as a reminder to seek knowledge, work together, and overcome challenges with understanding and unity.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be a source of inspiration and motivation, encouraging individuals and groups to pursue their goals with clarity and purpose.
