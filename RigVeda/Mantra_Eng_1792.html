भूरिणि भद्राऽनार्येषु बाहुषु वक्षःसु रुक्मा रभसासो अञ्जयः |अंसेष्वेताः पविषु क्षुराऽधि वयो न पक्षान व्यनु श्रीयो धिरे ||
bhūrīṇi bhadrā naryeṣu bāhuṣu vakṣaḥsu rukmā rabhasāso añjayaḥ | aṁseṣv etāḥ paviṣu kṣurā adhi vayo na pakṣān vy anu śriyo dhire ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • bhūrīṇi: abundant, plentiful
    • bhadrā: auspicious, good
    • naryeṣu: among women
    • bāhuṣu: in the arms
    • vakṣaḥsu: in the chest
    • rukmā: golden, radiant
    • rabhasāso: swift, energetic
    • añjayaḥ: those who are adorned
    • aṁseṣv: in the limbs
    • etāḥ: these
    • paviṣu: may they enter
    • kṣurā: sharp, keen
    • adhi: upon
    • vayo: age, life
    • na: not
    • pakṣān: wings
    • vy: in the direction of
    • anu: following
    • śriyo: prosperity, wealth
    • dhire: with steadiness, calmness
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the blessings of abundance and auspiciousness, particularly focusing on women and their strength.
    • The imagery of "golden" and "swift" suggests a celebration of vitality and energy, emphasizing the importance of these qualities in life.
    • The phrase "may they enter" indicates a desire for these qualities to manifest in one's life, particularly in the context of relationships and community.
    • The mention of "sharp" and "wings" can symbolize the need for clarity and direction in one's pursuits, urging individuals to be focused and determined.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace setting, this mantra can inspire teamwork and collaboration, where the strengths of individuals (especially women) are recognized and celebrated.
    • In personal relationships, it encourages partners to support each other’s ambitions and to cultivate a nurturing environment that fosters growth.
    • In community initiatives, it can serve as a reminder to uplift and empower women, ensuring their voices and contributions are valued.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for recognizing and harnessing the strengths of individuals, particularly women, in various aspects of life.
    • It is applicable in real-life scenarios such as promoting gender equality, fostering supportive relationships, and encouraging personal and communal growth.
    • By embodying the qualities of abundance, auspiciousness, and determination, individuals can create a more harmonious and prosperous environment for themselves and others.
