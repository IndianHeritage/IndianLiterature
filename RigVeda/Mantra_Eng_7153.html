बळ ऋत्वियाय धाम्न ऋक्वभिः शूर नोनुमः |जेषामिन्द्र त्वया युजा ||
baḻ ṛtviyāya dhāmna ṛkvabhiḥ śūra nonumaḥ | jeṣāmendra tvayā yujā ||
  • baḻ ṛtviyāya: This phrase can be interpreted as "to the one who is endowed with the qualities of a priest or a sage." In real life, this can refer to individuals who possess wisdom and knowledge, guiding others in their spiritual or moral journeys.
  • dhāmna: This word means "by the power" or "by the essence." It signifies the strength or energy that comes from a higher source, which can be seen in leaders or mentors who inspire others through their actions and beliefs.
  • ṛkvabhiḥ: This term refers to "with hymns" or "with praises." It highlights the importance of expressing gratitude and reverence, which is applicable in everyday life when we acknowledge the contributions of others, whether in personal relationships or in professional settings.
  • śūra: This word translates to "brave" or "valiant." It represents courage and strength, qualities that are essential in overcoming challenges in life. For example, a person standing up for justice or advocating for a cause demonstrates this bravery.
  • nonumaḥ: This means "we bow down" or "we offer our respects." It emphasizes humility and the importance of recognizing the greatness in others, which can be practiced in various situations, such as showing respect to elders or mentors.
  • jeṣāmendra: This phrase translates to "O Indra, the leader of those who have conquered." Indra is often seen as a symbol of strength and leadership. In real life, this can relate to individuals who lead by example, inspiring others to achieve their goals.
  • tvayā yujā: This means "by you, united." It signifies the importance of unity and collaboration in achieving success. In a team setting, for instance, working together harmoniously can lead to greater accomplishments than individual efforts.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of wisdom, strength, humility, and unity. It serves as a reminder of the qualities we should aspire to embody in our lives. By recognizing and respecting the contributions of others, demonstrating courage in the face of adversity, and working collaboratively, we can create a more harmonious and successful environment in both personal and professional spheres. This mantra encourages us to seek guidance from wise leaders and to strive for greatness while remaining humble and united in our endeavors.
