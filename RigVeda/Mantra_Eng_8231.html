या आरजीकेषु कृतवसु ये मध्य पस्त्यानाम् |ये वा जनेषु पञ्चसु ||
ya ārjīkeṣu kṛtvasu ye madhye pastyānām | ye vā janeṣu pañcasu ||
  • Mantra: "ya ārjīkeṣu kṛtvasu ye madhye pastyānām | ye vā janeṣu pañcasu ||"
  • Translation: "Those who are engaged in righteous actions, who are in the midst of the people, and those who are among the five."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • ya: Those who
    • ārjīkeṣu: In righteous actions or deeds
    • kṛtvasu: Engaged or involved
    • ye: Who
    • madhye: In the midst
    • pastyānām: Of the people
    • vā: Or
    • janeṣu: Among the people
    • pañcasu: The five (often referring to the five elements or senses)
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of engaging in righteous actions (ārjīkeṣu) while being part of the community (madhye pastyānām).
    • The phrase "among the five" (pañcasu) can be interpreted in various ways, such as referring to the five elements (earth, water, fire, air, and space) or the five senses (sight, hearing, touch, taste, and smell), highlighting the interconnectedness of human actions with the natural world.
    • In real life, this mantra can be seen as a call to action for individuals to contribute positively to society while being aware of their environment and the impact of their actions.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • A community leader who organizes clean-up drives is embodying the essence of this mantra by engaging in righteous actions while being in the midst of the people.
    • Environmental activists who advocate for sustainable practices are also acting in accordance with this mantra, as they work towards the betterment of society and the planet.
    • In a workplace, a team member who promotes collaboration and ethical practices contributes to a positive environment, reflecting the principles of this mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the significance of righteous actions in our daily lives and the importance of being part of a community.
    • It encourages individuals to be mindful of their actions and their impact on others and the environment.
    • By embodying the principles of this mantra, we can foster a more harmonious and responsible society, where each individual plays a vital role in the collective well-being.
