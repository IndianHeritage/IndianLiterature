मन्त्र: कद् वो अद्य महानां देवानाम् अवो वृणे | तमनā च दसमवर्चसाम् ||
kad vo adya mahānāṁ devānām avo vṛṇe | tmanā ca dasmavarcasām ||
  • Word Breakdown:
    • kad: When
    • vo: You (plural)
    • adya: Today
    • mahānāṁ: Of the great ones
    • devānām: Of the gods
    • avo: May we obtain
    • vṛṇe: I choose
    • tmanā: With our mind
    • ca: And
    • dasmavarcasām: Of those who have great brilliance
  • Meaning:
    • This mantra expresses a desire to connect with the divine and seek blessings from the great gods.
    • The speaker is asking when they can choose to receive the brilliance and blessings of the divine beings.
    • It emphasizes the importance of intention and mental focus in seeking divine guidance and support.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a moment of decision-making, one might recite this mantra to invoke clarity and wisdom from higher powers.
    • During a significant life event, such as a wedding or a new job, individuals may reflect on this mantra to seek blessings for success and brilliance in their new journey.
    • In meditation practices, this mantra can be used to focus the mind on the divine, enhancing spiritual connection and understanding.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine guidance and the importance of mental focus in achieving one's goals.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios where individuals seek clarity, support, and blessings from higher powers.
    • By invoking this mantra, one can cultivate a deeper connection with the divine, enhancing their spiritual journey and personal growth.
