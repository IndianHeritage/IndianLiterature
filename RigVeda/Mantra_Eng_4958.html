पुषणं न्व अजयाश्वम् उप स्तोषाम वाजिनम् | स्वसुर यो जार उच्यते ||
pūṣaṇaṁ nv a1jāśvam upa stoṣāma vājinam | svasur yo jāra ucyate ||
  • pūṣaṇaṁ: This term refers to the Sun God, who is considered a nourisher and sustainer of life. In real life, the sun is essential for agriculture, providing energy for plants to grow, which in turn sustains human and animal life.
  • nv: This is a particle that can be interpreted as "indeed" or "certainly," emphasizing the importance of the following words.
  • ajāśvam: This word can be broken down into "aja" (unborn) and "aśva" (horse). It symbolizes strength and vitality. In a real-life context, this can be likened to the energy and vigor found in youth or in a thriving community.
  • upa: This means "near" or "approaching," indicating a call to the divine or a request for assistance.
  • stoṣāma: This term means "we praise" or "we worship," reflecting the act of devotion and recognition of the divine forces in our lives.
  • vājinam: This refers to "the one who possesses strength" or "the powerful one." In everyday life, this can be associated with leaders or individuals who inspire and empower others.
  • svasur: This word means "of the family" or "of the household," indicating a connection to one's roots and community.
  • yo: This means "who," referring to the subject of the mantra.
  • jāra: This term can be interpreted as "the one who brings forth" or "the creator," symbolizing the generative forces in life.
  • ucyate: This means "is spoken" or "is declared," indicating the importance of communication and expression in recognizing the divine.

Conclusion: This mantra emphasizes the significance of the Sun God as a source of nourishment, strength, and vitality. It calls upon divine forces to support and empower individuals and communities. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios, such as seeking guidance in personal growth, community development, or even in leadership roles. By recognizing and honoring these divine qualities, individuals can foster a sense of unity and purpose in their lives and the lives of those around them.
