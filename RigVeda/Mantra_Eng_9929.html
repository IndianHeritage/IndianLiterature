आ देवो दूतः अजिरश्च चिकीत्वान् त्वद् देवापे अभि मां अगच्छत् |प्रतīचीनः प्रति मां आ ववृत्स्व दधामि ते द्युमतीं वाचम् आसन् ||
ā devo dūto ajiraś cikitvān tvad devāpe abhi mām agacchat | pratīcīnaḥ prati mām ā vavṛtsva dadhāmi te dyumatīṁ vācam āsan ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā: from
    • devo: the god
    • dūto: messenger
    • ajiraś: swift
    • cikitvān: knowing
    • tvad: your
    • devāpe: to the gods
    • abhi: towards
    • mām: me
    • agacchat: came
    • pratīcīnaḥ: from the west
    • prati: towards
    • mām: me
    • ā: from
    • vavṛtsva: may you protect
    • dadhāmi: I give
    • te: to you
    • dyumatīṁ: brilliant
    • vācam: speech
    • āsan: have
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the divine messenger, asking for guidance and protection.
    • The speaker acknowledges the swiftness and wisdom of the divine, seeking their presence and assistance.
    • The phrase "from the west" can symbolize a journey or a new beginning, indicating the arrival of new opportunities.
    • The speaker expresses a desire to offer brilliant speech, which can be interpreted as a wish for eloquence and clarity in communication.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, one might recite this mantra before a presentation, seeking clarity and confidence in their speech.
    • During times of transition, such as moving to a new city or starting a new job, individuals may reflect on this mantra to invite guidance and support from the divine.
    • In personal relationships, invoking this mantra can help in fostering open and honest communication, ensuring that one's words are impactful and meaningful.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of divine guidance in our lives, particularly in communication and decision-making.
    • It serves as a reminder to seek wisdom and clarity, especially during times of change or uncertainty.
    • By incorporating this mantra into daily practices, individuals can cultivate a deeper connection with the divine and enhance their ability to express themselves effectively.
