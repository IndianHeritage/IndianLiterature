आर्यो विश्वां गातुर एति प्र यद आनड दिवो अन्तान् |कविर अभ्रं दीद्यानाḥ ||
aryo viśāṁ gātur eti pra yad ānaḍ divo antān | kavir abhraṁ dīdyānaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • aryo: noble, virtuous
    • viśāṁ: of the people, community
    • gātur: the one who leads, the guide
    • eti: goes, moves towards
    • pra: towards, in the direction of
    • yad: that which
    • ānaḍ: bliss, joy
    • divo: of the heavens, celestial
    • antān: the end, the ultimate
    • kavir: the wise one, the poet
    • abhraṁ: the cloud, the covering
    • dīdyānaḥ: shining, illuminating
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of a noble leader who guides the community towards joy and bliss.
    • The phrase "gātur eti" emphasizes the role of a leader who moves towards the ultimate joy, suggesting that leadership is not just about authority but about guiding others towards happiness.
    • The reference to "dīdyānaḥ" indicates that wisdom and illumination are essential qualities of a leader, akin to how a cloud can bring rain, nourishing the earth and fostering growth.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, a manager who inspires and motivates their team to achieve collective goals embodies the essence of this mantra.
    • In community service, a volunteer leader who organizes efforts to help those in need demonstrates the qualities of a noble guide, leading others towards a common purpose of joy and fulfillment.
    • In education, a teacher who nurtures and inspires students to reach their potential reflects the wisdom and illumination described in the mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its emphasis on virtuous leadership and the pursuit of collective happiness.
    • It serves as a reminder that true leadership is about guiding others towards joy and fulfillment, illuminating their paths with wisdom.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as in workplaces, communities, and educational institutions, where the qualities of a noble leader can foster a positive and productive environment.
