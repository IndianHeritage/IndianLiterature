अग्निषोमाव इमानी नो युवं हव्याः जुजोषतम् |आ यातम् उप नः सचा ||
agnīṣomāv imāni no yuvaṁ havyā jujoṣatam | ā yātam upa naḥ sacā ||
  • Mantra: "agnīṣomāv imāni no yuvaṁ havyā jujoṣatam | ā yātam upa naḥ sacā ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • agnīṣomāv: Referring to the divine forces of Agni (fire) and Soma (nectar or moon).
    • imāni: These.
    • no: Our.
    • yuvaṁ: Young or youthful.
    • havyā: Offerings or that which is to be offered.
    • jujoṣatam: May they be pleased or may they accept.
    • ā: May.
    • yātam: Come or arrive.
    • upa: Near or close to.
    • naḥ: Us or our.
    • sacā: With truth or with sincerity.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the energies of Agni and Soma, symbolizing the dual aspects of life: the fiery, dynamic energy of Agni and the calming, nurturing essence of Soma.
    • The phrase "may they be pleased" indicates a desire for these divine forces to accept our offerings, which can be interpreted as our actions, intentions, and prayers.
    • The call for these energies to "come near to us with truth" emphasizes the importance of sincerity and authenticity in our lives.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, this mantra can be applied when we seek guidance or support in our endeavors, whether in personal relationships, career choices, or spiritual practices.
    • For instance, when starting a new project, one might recite this mantra to invoke the energies of creativity (Agni) and intuition (Soma) to ensure a balanced approach.
    • In moments of conflict, invoking these energies can remind us to approach situations with both passion and calmness, fostering resolution and understanding.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of balance between dynamic action and nurturing support in our lives.
    • It serves as a reminder to approach our goals with sincerity and to seek the blessings of divine energies in our endeavors.
    • By integrating the essence of this mantra into our daily practices, we can cultivate a harmonious existence that honors both our ambitions and our connections with others.
