मां नो निदे च वक्तवे 'र्यो रन्धीर अराव्णे |त्वे अपि क्रतुर मम ||
mā no nide ca vaktave 'ryo randhīr arāvṇe | tve api kratur mama ||
  • mā no nide ca vaktave: This phrase can be translated as "Do not let us be misled by false speech." It emphasizes the importance of truthfulness and integrity in communication. In real life, this can be seen in situations where honesty is crucial, such as in legal matters or personal relationships. For example, a lawyer must present the truth in court to ensure justice is served.
  • 'ryo randhīr arāvṇe: This part can be interpreted as "Let not the deceitful or the unworthy speak against us." It highlights the need to protect oneself from negative influences and harmful words. In everyday life, this can relate to avoiding toxic relationships or environments where negativity prevails. For instance, a person might choose to distance themselves from friends who constantly bring them down.
  • tve api kratur mama: This translates to "You are also my intention or will." It signifies the alignment of one's intentions with a higher purpose or divine will. In practical terms, this can be applied in goal-setting and personal development. For example, when someone sets a goal to improve their health, they may seek guidance or inspiration from a mentor or spiritual leader to ensure their intentions are pure and aligned with their values.

  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of truth, protection from negativity, and alignment of intentions. In real life, these principles can guide individuals in their personal and professional lives, fostering integrity, resilience, and purpose. By adhering to these values, one can navigate challenges more effectively and cultivate a positive environment for themselves and others.
