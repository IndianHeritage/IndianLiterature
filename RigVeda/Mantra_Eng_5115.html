जīmūतस्येव भवति प्रतीकं यद वर्मī याति समदाम उपस्थे |अनाविद्धया तन्मा जय त्वं स त्वा वर्मणो महिमा पिपर्तु ||
jīmūtasyeva bhavati pratīkaṁ yad varmī yāti samadām upasthe | anāviddhayā tanvā jaya tvaṁ sa tvā varmaṇo mahimā pipartu ||
  • jīmūtasyeva bhavati pratīkaṁ: This phrase can be translated as "like the cloud, it becomes a symbol." Here, the cloud represents something that is vast, encompassing, and capable of providing nourishment, much like the blessings or protection one seeks in life.
  • yad varmī yāti samadām upasthe: This translates to "whatever the protector (varmī) goes towards, it is in harmony with the surroundings." The term "varmī" refers to a protector or guardian, suggesting that true protection is not just about defense but also about harmony and balance with the environment.
  • anāviddhayā tanvā: This means "with an unpierced body." It signifies resilience and strength, suggesting that one can remain unharmed and intact despite challenges or adversities.
  • jaya tvaṁ sa tvā varmaṇo mahimā pipartu: This can be interpreted as "victory to you; may the greatness of the protector (varmaṇo) nourish you." It emphasizes the importance of acknowledging and honoring the protective forces in our lives, which can be both spiritual and physical.

  • Significance: This mantra encapsulates the essence of protection, resilience, and harmony. It serves as a reminder that true strength lies not just in defense but in the ability to coexist peacefully with our surroundings.
  • Real-life Application: In everyday life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as:
    • Personal Growth: When facing challenges, one can draw strength from the idea of being like a cloud—adaptable and nurturing, providing support to oneself and others.
    • Community Harmony: In community settings, the mantra encourages individuals to work together harmoniously, recognizing the importance of collective protection and support.
    • Environmental Awareness: The reference to harmony with the surroundings can inspire individuals to protect the environment, understanding that our well-being is interconnected with nature.

  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the importance of protection, resilience, and harmony in our lives. By embodying these principles, we can navigate challenges more effectively and contribute positively to our communities and the environment.
