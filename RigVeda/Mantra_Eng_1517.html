युवोर विश्वा अधि श्रीयः पृक्षश्च विश्ववेदसः |पुरुषायन्ते वाम पवयो हिरण्यये रथे दास्राः हिरण्यये ||
yuvor viśvā adhi śriyaḥ pṛkṣaś ca viśvavedasā | pruṣāyante vām pavayo hiraṇyaye rathe dasrā hiraṇyaye ||
  • yuvor viśvā adhi śriyaḥ: This phrase can be translated as "the two young ones (or the two divine beings) are the essence of all prosperity." Here, "yuvor" refers to youth or vitality, symbolizing new beginnings and potential. In real life, this can be related to the energy and enthusiasm of youth, which often drives innovation and progress in society.
  • pṛkṣaś ca viśvavedasā: This translates to "and they are the ones who know everything." "Viśvavedasā" implies a comprehensive understanding of the universe. In practical terms, this can be likened to the pursuit of knowledge and wisdom in various fields, such as science, philosophy, and spirituality, which are essential for personal and societal growth.
  • pruṣāyante vām pavayo: This means "they are the ones who bring forth the best of the best." It emphasizes the idea of excellence and the importance of striving for the highest standards in our endeavors. In everyday life, this can be seen in the dedication of individuals who aim for excellence in their careers, education, or personal development.
  • hiraṇyaye rathe dasrā hiraṇyaye: This can be interpreted as "in the golden chariot, they move forward." The "golden chariot" symbolizes prosperity and success. This phrase encourages us to embrace opportunities that lead to wealth and abundance. In real life, this can be reflected in the pursuit of goals that lead to financial stability and personal fulfillment.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of youth, knowledge, excellence, and prosperity. It signifies that by harnessing the energy of youth and the pursuit of wisdom, individuals can achieve greatness and contribute positively to society. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire people to strive for their goals, seek knowledge, and work towards creating a prosperous future for themselves and their communities. It serves as a reminder that the journey towards success is not just about material wealth but also about personal growth and the impact one has on the world.
