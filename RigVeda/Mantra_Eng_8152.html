एते असृग्रम् आशवोऽतिः हवरांसी बभ्रवः |सोमा ऋतस्य धारया ||
ete asṛgram āśavo 'ti hvarāṁsi babhravaḥ | somā ṛtasya dhārayā ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ete: these
    • asṛgram: the ones who are full of vigor or energy
    • āśavo: the swift or quick ones
    • 'ti: indeed
    • hvarāṁsi: the offerings or sacrifices
    • babhravaḥ: have been made or have come forth
    • somā: the soma (a sacred drink)
    • ṛtasya: of truth or cosmic order
    • dhārayā: by holding or sustaining
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the energetic and swift beings (possibly referring to deities or forces of nature) that are associated with offerings and sacrifices.
    • The mention of "soma" indicates a connection to a sacred ritual drink that symbolizes immortality and divine inspiration.
    • The phrase "ṛtasya dhārayā" suggests the importance of sustaining truth and cosmic order, which can be interpreted as a call to uphold righteousness in our lives.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, the mantra can be applied to the importance of teamwork and collaboration. Just as the swift beings work together to create harmony, employees must work together to achieve common goals.
    • In personal life, the idea of sustaining truth can be reflected in maintaining honesty in relationships. Just as the mantra emphasizes the importance of cosmic order, individuals can strive to create balance and integrity in their interactions.
    • During festivals or rituals, the offering of soma can be likened to the sharing of food and drink among family and friends, symbolizing unity and celebration of life.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of energy, collaboration, and the sustenance of truth in our lives.
    • It serves as a reminder to uphold righteousness and work together harmoniously, whether in personal relationships or community settings.
    • By embodying the principles of this mantra, individuals can contribute to a more balanced and truthful existence, fostering a sense of unity and purpose in their lives.
