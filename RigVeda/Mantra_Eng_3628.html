मन्त्र: अरवाचि सुभगे भव सीते वन्दामहे त्वा |यथा नः सुभगाससि यथा नः सुफलाससि ||
arvācī subhage bhava sīte vandāmahe tvā | yathā naḥ subhagāsasi yathā naḥ suphalāsasi ||
  • Mantra: "arvācī subhage bhava sīte vandāmahe tvā | yathā naḥ subhagāsasi yathā naḥ suphalāsasi ||"
  • Translation: "O auspicious one, we bow to you, may you be favorable to us, just as you are auspicious and fruitful for us."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • arvācī: Refers to the direction or aspect of the east, symbolizing new beginnings and opportunities.
    • subhage: Means auspicious or fortunate, indicating positivity and good fortune.
    • bhava: Translates to "become" or "may you be," expressing a wish or prayer.
    • sīte: Refers to the goddess of prosperity and abundance, often associated with wealth and fertility.
    • vandāmahe: Means "we bow" or "we offer our respects," indicating reverence and humility.
    • yathā: Means "as" or "just as," used to draw a comparison.
    • subhagāsasi: Refers to being auspicious or fortunate, emphasizing the desired qualities.
    • suphalāsasi: Means "fruitful" or "yielding good results," highlighting the importance of positive outcomes.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be invoked during the opening of a new venture, seeking prosperity and success in the endeavor.
    • During personal milestones, such as weddings or childbirth, individuals may recite this mantra to invite blessings of happiness and abundance into their lives.
    • In educational settings, students may chant this mantra before exams or important presentations, wishing for favorable outcomes and success.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its invocation of auspiciousness and prosperity. It serves as a reminder to seek positivity and fruitful outcomes in various aspects of life. By reciting this mantra, individuals can cultivate a mindset of gratitude and openness to the blessings that come their way. It is applicable in real-life scenarios where one seeks to enhance their fortune, whether in personal, professional, or spiritual endeavors. The mantra encourages a connection with the divine, fostering a sense of hope and positivity in the journey of life.
