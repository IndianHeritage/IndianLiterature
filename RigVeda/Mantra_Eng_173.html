तयोरिदवसावयं सनेम नि च धीमहि |स्यादुत प्ररेचनम् ||
tayor id avasā vayaṁ sanema ni ca dhīmahi | syād uta prarecanam ||
  • Mantra: "tayor id avasā vayaṁ sanema ni ca dhīmahi | syād uta prarecanam ||"
  • Translation: This mantra can be translated as: "May we be united in our thoughts and actions; may we be endowed with wisdom and understanding; may we be inspired to speak the truth."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • tayor: of those (referring to a higher power or divine beings)
    • id: this
    • avasā: united or together
    • vayaṁ: we
    • sanema: may we be endowed or blessed
    • ni: and
    • ca: also
    • dhīmahi: may we think or contemplate
    • syād: may there be
    • uta: or
    • prarecanam: inspiration or encouragement
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • Teamwork: In a workplace setting, this mantra emphasizes the importance of collaboration. For instance, when a team works together on a project, they must align their thoughts and actions to achieve a common goal. This unity can lead to innovative solutions and successful outcomes.
    • Community Building: In a community, this mantra can be applied to foster harmony among diverse groups. When individuals come together with a shared purpose, such as organizing a charity event, they can create a positive impact and strengthen community bonds.
    • Personal Relationships: In personal relationships, whether familial or friendships, this mantra encourages open communication and understanding. By being united in thoughts and actions, individuals can resolve conflicts and build deeper connections.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for unity, wisdom, and truthfulness. In real-life scenarios, it serves as a reminder of the power of collective effort and the importance of aligning our thoughts and actions towards a common purpose. By embodying these principles, individuals and communities can foster a more harmonious and productive environment, ultimately leading to personal growth and societal progress.
