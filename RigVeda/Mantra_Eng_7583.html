अव द्रप्सो अंशुमतीं अतिष्ठद इयानः कृष्णो दशभिः सहस्रैः |आवत तं इन्द्रः शच्यां धमन्तम अप स्नेहितीर नृमणा अधत्त ||
ava drapso aṁśumatīm atiṣṭhad iyānaḥ kṛṣṇo daśabhiḥ sahasraiḥ | āvat tam indraḥ śacyā dhamantam apa snehitīr nṛmaṇā adhatta ||
  • ava drapso aṁśumatīm: This phrase can be interpreted as "to the one who is endowed with brilliance." In real life, this can refer to individuals who possess wisdom, knowledge, or exceptional qualities that illuminate their surroundings, much like a leader or a mentor who guides others.
  • atiṣṭhad iyānaḥ: This translates to "who has established a path." It signifies the importance of setting a direction or purpose in life. For example, a person who decides to pursue a career in medicine establishes a path that not only benefits themselves but also contributes to society by saving lives.
  • kṛṣṇo daśabhiḥ sahasraiḥ: This can be understood as "the dark one (Kṛṣṇa) with ten thousand." Kṛṣṇa, often associated with divine playfulness and wisdom, represents the idea that even in darkness, there is abundance and potential. In real life, this can be seen in situations where individuals face challenges but find strength in community support, much like a team overcoming obstacles together.
  • āvat tam indraḥ śacyā dhamantam: This phrase means "may Indra, the powerful one, protect him." Indra is a deity associated with strength and protection. In a modern context, this can relate to the support systems we have in our lives, such as family, friends, or mentors who provide guidance and protection during difficult times.
  • apa snehitīr nṛmaṇā adhatta: This translates to "he who is affectionate and compassionate." This emphasizes the importance of kindness and empathy in human interactions. For instance, a teacher who shows compassion towards their students fosters a nurturing environment that encourages learning and growth.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of wisdom, purpose, community support, protection, and compassion. Its significance lies in the reminder that in our journey through life, we should strive to embody these qualities. Whether in personal relationships, professional endeavors, or community involvement, applying the principles of brilliance, direction, strength, protection, and kindness can lead to a more fulfilling and harmonious existence. This mantra serves as a guiding light, encouraging individuals to cultivate these attributes in themselves and in their interactions with others.
