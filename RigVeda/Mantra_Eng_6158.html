उत त्यद् आश्वश्व्यं यद् इन्द्र नाहुषीष्व आ |अग्रे विक्षु प्रदीदयत ||
uta tyad āśvaśvyaṁ yad indra nāhuṣīṣv ā | agre vikṣu pradīdayat ||
  • Mantra: "uta tyad āśvaśvyaṁ yad indra nāhuṣīṣv ā | agre vikṣu pradīdayat ||"
  • Translation: "May that which is like a horse, which Indra, the son of Nahusha, has given, shine forth in the front."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • uta: a particle often used to denote 'and' or 'also'.
    • tyad: means 'that' or 'that which'.
    • āśvaśvyaṁ: derived from 'āśva', meaning 'horse', symbolizing strength, speed, and vitality.
    • yad: another form of 'that' or 'which'.
    • indra: the king of gods in Vedic tradition, representing power and authority.
    • nāhuṣīṣv: refers to 'Nahusha', a king who became a god, symbolizing transformation and elevation.
    • agre: means 'in the front' or 'ahead'.
    • vikṣu: means 'to shine' or 'to illuminate'.
    • pradīdayat: means 'to make shine' or 'to cause to shine'.

  • Contextual Significance:
    • This mantra invokes the blessings of Indra, emphasizing the importance of strength and vitality in life.
    • The horse symbolizes not just physical strength but also the speed and agility needed to overcome challenges.
    • Indra, as a deity, represents the power of leadership and the ability to transform situations, much like Nahusha's journey from a mortal king to a divine being.

  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In competitive sports, athletes often channel the energy of strength and speed, akin to the horse, to achieve their goals.
    • In leadership roles, individuals can draw inspiration from Indra's authority to lead with confidence and decisiveness.
    • In personal development, the transformation of Nahusha serves as a reminder that anyone can elevate their status through hard work and dedication.

  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the qualities of strength, leadership, and transformation.
    • It encourages individuals to seek the blessings of divine energy to shine in their endeavors.
    • Applicable in various life scenarios, from personal growth to professional achievements, it inspires one to harness their inner strength and lead with purpose.
