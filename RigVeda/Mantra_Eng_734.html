मन्त्र: चित्रैः अञ्जिभिर वपुषे व्य अञ्जते वक्षःसु रुक्मां अधि येतिरे शुभे |अंसेष्व एषां नि मिमृक्षुर ऋष्टयः साकं जज्निरे स्वधया दिवो नरः ||
citrair añjibhir vapuṣe vy añjate vakṣaḥsu rukmām̐ adhi yetire śubhe | aṁseṣv eṣāṁ ni mimṛkṣur ṛṣṭayaḥ sākaṁ jajñire svadhayā divo naraḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • citrair: with various forms
    • añjibhir: adorned
    • vapuṣe: with the body
    • vy: they (the divine beings)
    • añjate: are invoked
    • vakṣaḥsu: in the chests
    • rukmām̐: golden
    • adhi: upon
    • yetire: they reside
    • śubhe: auspicious
    • aṁseṣv: in the limbs
    • eṣāṁ: of these
    • ni: not
    • mimṛkṣur: they are to be taken
    • ṛṣṭayaḥ: the desires
    • sākaṁ: together
    • jajñire: they are born
    • svadhayā: by their own will
    • divo: of the heavens
    • naraḥ: man
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the divine beings who are adorned with various forms and are invoked in the hearts of individuals.
    • The "golden" aspect signifies purity and auspiciousness, suggesting that these divine qualities reside within us.
    • It emphasizes the importance of our desires and how they are born from our own will, indicating that we have the power to shape our destinies.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a personal development context, this mantra can be related to the idea of self-empowerment. Just as the divine beings are invoked, individuals can invoke their inner strength and qualities to achieve their goals.
    • In a community setting, the mantra can be seen as a call for collective action, where the desires and aspirations of individuals come together to create a harmonious society.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its reminder of the divine potential within each individual. It encourages us to recognize and cultivate our inner qualities and desires.
    • In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as personal growth, community building, and spiritual practices, where individuals strive to align their actions with their higher selves and collective aspirations.
