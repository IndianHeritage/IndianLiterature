प्र हिन्वानास इन्दवोऽच्छा समुद्रमाशवः | धियाऽजूताऽसृक्षत ||
pra hinvānāsa indavo 'cchā samudram āśavaḥ | dhiyā jūtā asṛkṣata ||
  • pra hinvānāsa: This phrase can be interpreted as "those who are moving forward" or "those who are advancing." It signifies progress and the pursuit of knowledge or enlightenment.
  • indavo: This term refers to "the divine" or "the gods." In the context of the mantra, it suggests a connection to higher powers or spiritual beings that guide and inspire humanity.
  • 'cchā: This word means "to cover" or "to envelop." It indicates a protective or nurturing aspect, suggesting that the divine presence provides shelter and support.
  • samudram: Translated as "ocean," it symbolizes vastness, depth, and the source of life. The ocean can represent the collective consciousness or the infinite possibilities that exist in the universe.
  • āśavaḥ: This term can be understood as "the essence" or "the vital energy." It refers to the life force that permeates all beings and connects them to the divine.
  • dhiyā: Meaning "with intellect" or "with understanding," this word emphasizes the importance of wisdom and discernment in our actions and thoughts.
  • jūtā: This term means "joined" or "united." It signifies the coming together of individuals or forces for a common purpose, highlighting the power of collaboration and unity.
  • asṛkṣata: This word translates to "they protected" or "they safeguarded." It indicates the act of preserving and nurturing, suggesting that the divine and collective efforts lead to safety and well-being.

In conclusion, this mantra emphasizes the significance of divine guidance and the importance of unity and intellect in our journey through life. It teaches us that by advancing together with the support of higher powers, we can navigate the vast ocean of existence, drawing upon the vital energy that connects us all. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as teamwork in professional settings, community building, and personal growth. By fostering collaboration and seeking wisdom, we can create a harmonious environment that nurtures both individual and collective well-being.
