प्र वो महे महिवृधे भरध्वं प्रचेतसे प्र सुमतिं कृतुध्वं |विशः पूर्वीः प्र चराः चरषणिप्राः ||
pra vo mahe mahivṛdhe bharadhvam pracetase pra sumatiṁ kṛṇudhvam | viśaḥ pūrvīḥ pra carā carṣaṇiprāḥ ||
  • pra vo mahe mahivṛdhe: This phrase can be translated as "We invoke you, the great one, the one who is vast." Here, "mahivṛdhe" refers to the vastness of the universe and the greatness of the divine. In real life, this can be seen as a call to recognize the larger forces at play in our lives, such as nature, the cosmos, or the collective human experience.
  • bharadhvam pracetase: This means "May you bring forth wisdom." "Bharadhvam" implies the act of bringing forth or manifesting, while "pracetase" refers to intelligence or wisdom. In practical terms, this can be related to seeking knowledge and understanding in our daily lives, whether through education, experience, or introspection.
  • pra sumatiṁ kṛṇudhvam: This translates to "May you grant us good thoughts." "Sumati" signifies good or auspicious thoughts. In a real-world context, this can be applied to the importance of positive thinking and mental well-being, encouraging individuals to cultivate a mindset that fosters creativity, compassion, and resilience.
  • viśaḥ pūrvīḥ: This phrase means "The beings of the past." It acknowledges the wisdom and experiences of those who came before us. In life, this can be seen in the value of history, tradition, and the lessons learned from previous generations, which can guide our actions and decisions today.
  • pra carā carṣaṇiprāḥ: This translates to "May you move forward with strength." "Carā" means to move or progress, while "carṣaṇiprāḥ" refers to the strength or vigor in that movement. In a practical sense, this can be related to personal growth, perseverance, and the pursuit of goals, emphasizing the importance of taking action and moving forward in life.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a profound invocation for wisdom, good thoughts, and strength, while also recognizing the vastness of existence and the importance of learning from the past. Its significance lies in its applicability to everyday life, encouraging individuals to seek knowledge, maintain a positive mindset, and strive for personal growth. By embodying these principles, one can navigate life's challenges with greater clarity and purpose, ultimately contributing to a more harmonious existence within the larger tapestry of life.
