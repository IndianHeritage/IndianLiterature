ऊर्ध्वो ग्रावा बृहद् अग्निः समिद्धः प्रियाः धामान्य अदितेर उपस्थे |पुरोहिताव ऋत्विजा यज्ञे अस्मिन विदुष्टरा द्रविणम् आ यजेताम् ||
ūrdhvo grāvā bṛhad agniḥ samiddhaḥ priyā dhāmāny aditer upasthe | purohitāv ṛtvijā yajñe asmin viduṣṭarā draviṇam ā yajethām ||
  • ūrdhvo grāvā bṛhad agniḥ: This phrase translates to "the great fire that rises upwards." Here, "ūrdhvo" means upward, "grāvā" refers to the gathering or assembly, and "bṛhad" signifies greatness. In real life, this can be likened to the aspirations and ambitions of individuals or communities that aim for higher achievements, much like a fire that seeks to rise.
  • samiddhaḥ priyā dhāmāny aditer upasthe: This part means "kindled and dear, dwelling in the presence of Aditi." "Samiddhaḥ" refers to something that is kindled or ignited, "priyā" means dear or beloved, and "Aditi" is often associated with the infinite or the mother of gods. This can be related to the nurturing aspects of life, such as family and community support, which are essential for personal growth and success.
  • purohitāv ṛtvijā yajñe asmin: This translates to "the priests and the officiants in this sacrifice." "Purohitāv" refers to the priests, "ṛtvijā" means the officiants or those who perform rituals, and "yajñe" refers to the sacrifice or offering. In modern contexts, this can be seen as the importance of guidance and mentorship in our lives, where experienced individuals help us navigate through challenges.
  • viduṣṭarā draviṇam ā yajethām: This means "may they invoke the wealth of knowledge." "Viduṣṭarā" refers to the knowledgeable or wise, "draviṇam" means wealth, and "yajethām" implies invoking or offering. This highlights the significance of knowledge and wisdom as the true wealth in life, which can lead to prosperity and success.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of striving for greatness, the nurturing support of community and family, the role of guidance from mentors, and the value of knowledge as a form of wealth. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios such as personal development, education, and community building. By recognizing the significance of these elements, individuals can work towards achieving their goals while fostering a supportive environment that values wisdom and collaboration.
