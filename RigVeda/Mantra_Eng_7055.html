आ नो विश्वेषां रसं मध्वः सिञ्चन्त्व अद्रयः |ये परावती सुन्विरे जनेष्व आ ये अरवातिंदवः ||
ā no viśveṣāṁ rasam madhvaḥ siñcantv adrayaḥ | ye parāvati sunvire janeṣv ā ye arvāvatīndavaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā: May
    • no: us
    • viśveṣāṁ: of all beings
    • rasam: essence or flavor
    • madhvaḥ: sweet or pleasant
    • siñcantv: may they pour or bestow
    • adrayaḥ: the waters or the streams
    • ye: those who
    • parāvati: who are elevated or exalted
    • sunvire: may they be invoked or called upon
    • janeṣv: among people
    • ā: may
    • ye: those who
    • arvāvatīndavaḥ: who are like the flowing rivers
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer for the essence of all beings to be sweetened and enriched.
    • It calls upon the waters, symbolizing nourishment and life, to bestow their blessings upon humanity.
    • The reference to "those who are elevated" suggests a desire for wisdom and enlightenment to flow among people.
    • The mention of "flowing rivers" signifies the continuous and abundant nature of knowledge and resources.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community setting, this mantra can be applied during gatherings where people seek to share knowledge and experiences, enriching each other's lives.
    • In educational institutions, it can symbolize the aspiration for a nurturing environment where students and teachers exchange wisdom freely.
    • In personal relationships, it encourages individuals to foster sweetness and understanding, enhancing the quality of interactions.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for unity, nourishment, and the sharing of wisdom among all beings.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from community gatherings to personal relationships, emphasizing the importance of collaboration and mutual support.
    • By invoking this mantra, individuals and communities can aspire to create a harmonious environment where knowledge and kindness flow freely, much like the rivers that sustain life.
