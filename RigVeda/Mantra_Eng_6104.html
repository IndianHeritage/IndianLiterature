मन्त्र: उता नो गोमतीर इषा उता सातीः अहर्विदा | विपथः सातये सितम् ||
uta no gomatīr iṣa uta sātīr aharvidā | vi pathaḥ sātaye sitam ||
  • Mantra: "uta no gomatīr iṣa uta sātīr aharvidā | vi pathaḥ sātaye sitam ||"
  • Translation: This mantra can be translated as: "May we attain the wealth of the earth, may we achieve the strength of the day; may we find the path that leads us to prosperity."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • uta: a word of invocation, often translated as "may" or "let us."
    • no: means "our," indicating possession or belonging.
    • gomatīr: derived from "goma," meaning "wealth" or "prosperity," often associated with cattle in Vedic culture, symbolizing abundance.
    • iṣa: refers to "strength" or "power," indicating a desire for vigor and capability.
    • sātīr: relates to "day" or "light," symbolizing clarity, knowledge, and enlightenment.
    • aharvidā: translates to "of the day," emphasizing the importance of daily efforts and actions.
    • vi: means "through" or "by," indicating a means to an end.
    • pathaḥ: means "path," representing the journey or direction one takes in life.
    • sātaye: refers to "prosperity" or "success," indicating the desired outcome of the journey.
    • sitam: means "white" or "pure," often symbolizing clarity, truth, and righteousness.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be applied to the pursuit of success and wealth. For instance, a startup may invoke this mantra to seek clarity and strength in their business decisions, aiming for prosperity in their ventures.
    • In personal life, individuals may reflect on this mantra when setting goals for self-improvement, seeking the strength to overcome challenges and the clarity to choose the right path in their careers or relationships.
    • In community settings, this mantra can inspire collective efforts towards achieving common goals, such as community development projects that aim to enhance the prosperity and well-being of all members.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its holistic approach to life, emphasizing the importance of wealth, strength, and clarity in our daily pursuits. It serves as a reminder to seek prosperity not just in material terms but also in personal growth and community well-being. By invoking this mantra, individuals and groups can align their actions with their aspirations, fostering a sense of purpose and direction in their lives. In essence, it encourages us to be mindful of the paths we choose and the efforts we make towards achieving our goals.
