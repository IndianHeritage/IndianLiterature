उद īर्ध्वं जीवो असुर न आगाद अप प्रागात तम आ ज्योतिरेति |आरैक पन्थां यातवे सूर्यायागन्म यत्र प्रतिरन्त आयुः ||
ud īrdhvaṁ jīvo asur na āgād apa prāgāt tama ā jyotir eti | āraik panthāṁ yātave sūryāyāganma yatra pratiranta āyuḥ ||
  • ud īrdhvaṁ: This phrase translates to "upward" or "higher." It signifies the aspiration to rise above challenges and limitations.
  • jīvo: Meaning "the living being" or "soul," it emphasizes the essence of life and consciousness.
  • asur: Referring to "the demon" or "negative forces," it represents obstacles or adversities that one may face in life.
  • na āgād: This means "should not go" or "should not be led." It suggests a warning against succumbing to negativity or despair.
  • apa prāgāt: Translated as "from the past," it indicates the importance of learning from previous experiences and not repeating mistakes.
  • tama: Meaning "darkness," it symbolizes ignorance or confusion that can cloud judgment.
  • ā jyotir eti: This translates to "towards the light," representing the pursuit of knowledge, wisdom, and enlightenment.
  • āraik: This word means "to follow" or "to adhere to," suggesting the importance of a guiding principle or path.
  • panthāṁ: Meaning "path," it signifies the journey one undertakes in life.
  • yātave: This translates to "to the one who travels," indicating the seeker or the individual on a quest for truth.
  • sūryāyāganma: Referring to "the sun," it symbolizes vitality, energy, and the source of life.
  • yatra: Meaning "where," it points to the destination or goal of the journey.
  • pratiranta: This translates to "in every direction," indicating the all-encompassing nature of the journey.
  • āyuḥ: Meaning "life" or "longevity," it emphasizes the importance of a fulfilling and meaningful existence.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a profound message about the journey of life. It encourages individuals to rise above negativity and ignorance, to seek enlightenment, and to follow a righteous path. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various contexts such as personal development, overcoming challenges, and pursuing knowledge. For instance, when faced with difficulties at work or in personal relationships, one can reflect on this mantra to find the strength to rise above and seek a path towards positivity and growth. Ultimately, it serves as a reminder that the pursuit of light and knowledge is essential for a fulfilling life.
