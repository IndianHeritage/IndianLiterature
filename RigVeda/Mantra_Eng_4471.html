मन्त्र: नानाह्य अग्नेऽवसे स्पर्धन्ते रायो आर्यः | तूर्वन्तो दस्युम आयवो व्रतैः शिक्षन्तो अव्रतम् ||
nānā hy a1gne 'vase spardhante rāyo aryaḥ | tūrvanto dasyum āyavo vrataiḥ sīkṣanto avratam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • nānā: various or diverse
    • hy: indeed
    • a1gne: O Agni (the fire god)
    • 'vase: in your control
    • spardhante: they strive or compete
    • rāyo: for wealth or prosperity
    • aryaḥ: noble or honorable ones
    • tūrvanto: those who are strong or powerful
    • dasyum: the enemy or adversary
    • āyavo: those who are capable or able
    • vrataiḥ: with vows or commitments
    • sīkṣanto: they learn or are trained
    • avratam: without vows or commitments
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra addresses Agni, the fire god, emphasizing the diversity of noble individuals who strive for prosperity under his guidance.
    • It highlights the importance of strength and commitment in overcoming adversities and enemies.
    • The phrase "they learn with vows" suggests that true strength comes from dedication and discipline, while "without vows" indicates a lack of commitment leads to weakness.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, diverse teams often compete for resources and recognition. The most successful teams are those that work together under strong leadership (Agni) and are committed to their goals.
    • In sports, athletes train rigorously (sīkṣanto) with a commitment to their sport (vrataiḥ), which helps them overcome challenges (dasyum) and achieve success.
    • In personal development, individuals who set clear goals and commit to their personal growth often find themselves more capable of facing life's adversities.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of diversity, strength, and commitment in achieving prosperity and overcoming challenges.
    • It teaches that true power lies in unity and dedication, encouraging individuals to strive for their goals while remaining committed to their values.
    • In real life, this can be applied in various fields such as business, sports, and personal development, where commitment and teamwork lead to success.
