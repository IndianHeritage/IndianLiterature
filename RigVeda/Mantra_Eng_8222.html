आ कलशा अनूषतेन्दो धाराभिर ओजसा |एन्द्रस्य पीतये विश ||
ā kalaśā anūṣatendo dhārābhir ojasā | endrasya pītaye viśa ||
  • Word Breakdown:
    • ā: A prefix indicating direction or movement towards.
    • kalaśā: Refers to a pot or vessel, often symbolizing abundance or nourishment.
    • anūṣatendo: This can be interpreted as the one who is eternal or everlasting.
    • dhārābhir: Refers to streams or flows, often symbolizing the flow of energy or resources.
    • ojasā: Represents vitality, strength, or energy.
    • endrasya: Pertaining to Indra, the king of gods in Vedic tradition, symbolizing power and authority.
    • pītaye: Refers to the act of drinking or consuming.
    • viśa: Can mean poison or something that is potent, often used in a metaphorical sense.
  • Meaning:
    • This mantra speaks of the eternal flow of energy and abundance, symbolized by the pot (kalaśā) that nourishes and sustains life.
    • The reference to Indra suggests a connection to divine power and authority, emphasizing the importance of strength and vitality in our lives.
    • The term "viśa" can be interpreted as a reminder of the duality of existence, where both nourishment and potential dangers coexist.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In agriculture, the concept of a pot (kalaśā) can be likened to the importance of water reservoirs that nourish crops, symbolizing the flow of resources necessary for sustenance.
    • In personal development, the idea of ojasā (vitality) can be related to maintaining physical and mental health through balanced nutrition and exercise, which empowers individuals to face challenges.
    • The duality of "viśa" can be seen in everyday life where we must navigate both opportunities and risks, such as in business decisions where potential rewards must be weighed against possible losses.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of recognizing the flow of energy and resources in our lives, urging us to cultivate strength and vitality while being aware of the potential dangers that may arise.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal health to professional endeavors, reminding us to seek balance and make informed decisions.
    • Ultimately, it encourages a holistic approach to life, where we appreciate the abundance around us while remaining vigilant and empowered to navigate challenges.
