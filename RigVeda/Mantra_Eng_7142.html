सत्यं इद् वा उ तं वयं इन्द्रं स्तवाम नानृतम् |महाम् असुन्वतो वधो भूरी ज्योतिंषि सुन्वतो भद्राः इन्द्रस्य रातयः ||
satyam id vā u taṁ vayam indraṁ stavāma nānṛtam | mahām̐ asunvato vadho bhūri jyotīṁṣi sunvato bhadrā indrasya rātayaḥ ||
  • Translation: "We invoke Indra, the embodiment of truth; we do not speak falsehood. May the great and powerful Indra, who bestows abundant light and blessings, protect us and guide us towards prosperity."
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • satyam: Truth
    • id: This
    • vā: Or
    • u: And
    • taṁ: That
    • vayam: We
    • indraṁ: Indra (the king of gods)
    • stavāma: We praise/invoke
    • nānṛtam: Not falsehood
    • mahām̐: Great
    • asunvato: Bestowing
    • vadho: Protection
    • bhūri: Abundant
    • jyotīṁṣi: Light
    • sunvato: Bestowing
    • bhadrā: Blessings
    • indrasya: Of Indra
    • rātayaḥ: Guidance
  • Contextual Explanation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of truthfulness and integrity in our lives. In a world where misinformation can easily spread, the call to uphold truth is more relevant than ever.
    • Indra, as a deity, symbolizes strength, power, and the ability to overcome obstacles. By invoking Indra, we seek his blessings for courage and clarity in our endeavors.
    • The phrase "we do not speak falsehood" serves as a reminder to maintain honesty in our words and actions, which is crucial in personal relationships, professional settings, and societal interactions.
    • The invocation of light and blessings signifies the desire for enlightenment and prosperity, which can be interpreted as a call for wisdom in decision-making and success in our pursuits.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate environment, leaders who prioritize transparency and honesty foster trust among their teams, leading to a more productive and positive workplace.
    • In personal relationships, being truthful strengthens bonds and creates a foundation of trust, which is essential for long-lasting connections.
    • In community settings, advocating for truth and integrity can inspire collective action towards social justice and ethical practices.
  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the significance of truth and integrity in our lives. By invoking the blessings of Indra, we not only seek protection and guidance but also commit ourselves to uphold these values in our daily actions. In a world filled with challenges, this mantra encourages us to strive for honesty and to illuminate our paths with wisdom and blessings, ultimately leading to a more harmonious and prosperous existence.
