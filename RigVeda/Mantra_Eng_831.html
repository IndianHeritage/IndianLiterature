विपृक्षो अग्नेमघवानो अश्युर विसूरयो ददतो विश्वमायुः |सनम वाजं समिथेष्व आर्यो भागं देवेषु श्रवसे दधानाः ||
vi pṛkṣo agne maghavāno aśyur vi sūrayo dadato viśvam āyuḥ | sanema vājaṁ samitheṣv aryo bhāgaṁ deveṣu śravase dadhānāḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • vi pṛkṣo: "O Agni, the one who is seen or perceived."
    • agne: "O Fire, or O Divine Light."
    • maghavāno: "the powerful or the mighty."
    • aśyur: "the one who is full of life or vitality."
    • vi sūrayo: "with the heroes or the strong ones."
    • dadato: "who gives or bestows."
    • viśvam āyuḥ: "the universal life or longevity."
    • sanema: "let us come together or unite."
    • vājaṁ: "the wealth or prosperity."
    • samitheṣv: "in the gatherings or assemblies."
    • aryo: "the noble or the worthy."
    • bhāgaṁ: "the share or portion."
    • deveṣu: "among the gods."
    • śravase: "in the glory or fame."
    • dadhānāḥ: "who are endowed or who possess."
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra invokes Agni, the divine fire, symbolizing energy, transformation, and the divine presence in our lives.
    • It emphasizes the importance of unity and collaboration among individuals, especially those who are strong and noble.
    • The mantra suggests that by coming together, we can achieve prosperity and longevity, both individually and collectively.
    • In real life, this can be seen in community efforts, teamwork in workplaces, or collaborative projects where diverse strengths contribute to a common goal.
    • For example, in a corporate setting, when employees unite their skills and efforts, they can drive innovation and success, reflecting the essence of this mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for unity, strength, and the pursuit of shared goals.
    • It teaches us that by working together and supporting one another, we can enhance our lives and the lives of those around us.
    • This principle is applicable in various aspects of life, including family, community, and professional environments, where collaboration leads to greater achievements.
    • Ultimately, it reminds us of the power of collective effort in achieving prosperity and longevity, both materially and spiritually.
