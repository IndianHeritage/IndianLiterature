मन्त्र: समना तूर्णिर उप यासि यज्ञं आ नासत्यā सख्याय वक्षि |वासव्यां इन्द्र धारयः सहस्राश्विना शूर ददतुर मगानि ||
samanā tūrṇir upa yāsi yajñam ā nāsatyā sakhyāya vakṣi | vasāvyām indra dhārayaḥ sahasrāśvinā śūra dadatur maghāni ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • samanā: similar, united
    • tūrṇir: quickly, swiftly
    • upa: towards, near
    • yāsi: you go, you approach
    • yajñam: sacrifice, offering
    • ā: from, out of
    • nāsatyā: O Nasatyas (the twin deities)
    • sakhyāya: for friendship, for companionship
    • vakṣi: you will speak, you will declare
    • vasāvyām: in the abode, in the dwelling
    • indra: O Indra (the king of gods)
    • dhārayaḥ: may you hold, may you sustain
    • sahasrāśvinā: with the two Ashvins (twin gods)
    • śūra: brave, valiant
    • dadatur: may they give, may they bestow
    • maghāni: great blessings, riches
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of unity and collaboration in achieving goals.
    • The invocation of the twin deities, Nasatyas, symbolizes the need for companionship and support in endeavors.
    • Indra, as the king of gods, represents strength and leadership, suggesting that divine support is essential for success.
    • The mention of "great blessings" indicates that the outcomes of collective efforts can lead to prosperity and abundance.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, teamwork is crucial for project success. When team members work together harmoniously, they can achieve results that are greater than the sum of their individual efforts.
    • In community service, individuals coming together for a common cause can create significant positive change, reflecting the essence of this mantra.
    • In sports, a team that collaborates effectively, supporting each other, often outperforms a group of talented individuals who do not work together.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of unity, collaboration, and divine support in achieving success.
    • It serves as a reminder that working together with a shared purpose can lead to greater outcomes and blessings in various aspects of life.
    • In real-life scenarios, whether in personal relationships, professional environments, or community efforts, the principles embodied in this mantra can guide individuals towards collective success and fulfillment.
