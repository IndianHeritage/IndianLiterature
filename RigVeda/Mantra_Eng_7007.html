ऋदूदरेण सख्या सचेय यो मां न रिष्येद धार्यश्व पीतः |अयं यः सोमो न्य अधाय्य अस्मे तस्मा इन्द्रं प्रतिरमेम्य आयुः ||
ṛdūdareṇa sakhyā saceya yo mā na riṣyed dharyaśva pītaḥ | ayaṁ yaḥ somo ny adhāyy asme tasmā indram pratiram emy āyuḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ṛdūdareṇa: With the strength of the righteous.
    • sakhyā: In friendship or companionship.
    • saceya: May he who is capable.
    • yo: Who.
    • mā: Not.
    • na: Not.
    • riṣyed: Be disturbed or agitated.
    • dharyaśva: Hold firm or steady.
    • pītaḥ: The one who is imbibed or drunk.
    • ayaṁ: This.
    • yaḥ: Who.
    • somo: The moon or soma (nectar).
    • ny adhāyy: Has been placed or established.
    • asme: For us.
    • tasmā: Therefore.
    • indram: Indra, the king of gods.
    • pratiram: As a protector or support.
    • emy: May I come or arrive.
    • āyuḥ: Life or longevity.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of strength derived from righteousness and companionship.
    • It calls upon the divine to ensure that one remains undisturbed and steady in life.
    • The reference to "soma" symbolizes nourishment and vitality, suggesting that spiritual and physical sustenance is essential for a fulfilling life.
    • By invoking Indra, the mantra seeks divine protection and support, highlighting the need for guidance in our endeavors.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace, fostering a culture of teamwork and support can lead to greater productivity and job satisfaction, reflecting the essence of "sakhyā."
    • During challenging times, individuals often turn to their friends and family for emotional support, embodying the idea of holding firm and steady.
    • Practicing mindfulness and meditation can help individuals remain undisturbed by external chaos, aligning with the mantra's call for steadiness.
    • In health and wellness, consuming nourishing foods (symbolized by "soma") is crucial for maintaining physical and mental well-being.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of strength, companionship, and divine support in our lives.
    • It encourages individuals to seek righteousness and stability, both personally and in their relationships.
    • By invoking the divine for protection and nourishment, we can navigate life's challenges with resilience and purpose.
    • Ultimately, the mantra highlights the significance of community, spirituality, and the pursuit of a balanced life.
