आ वृषस्व महामह महे नृतम rādhase |दृḻहश चिद दृह्य मगहन मगत्तये ||
ā vṛṣasva mahāmaha mahe nṛtama rādhase | dṛḻhaś cid dṛhya maghavan maghattaye ||
  • ā vṛṣasva: This phrase can be interpreted as "may you be strong" or "may you be powerful." It invokes the idea of strength and vitality, which is essential in both physical and mental aspects of life.
  • mahāmaha: This term translates to "great" or "immense." It emphasizes the vastness of the qualities being invoked, suggesting that the strength and power sought are not just ordinary but extraordinary.
  • mahe nṛtama: This can be understood as "to the great man" or "to the supreme being." It signifies reverence towards a higher power or authority, acknowledging the importance of guidance from those who are wise and powerful.
  • rādhase: This word can be interpreted as "to the one who is worthy" or "to the one who is deserving." It highlights the importance of merit and righteousness in seeking strength and support.
  • dṛḻhaś cid: This phrase means "with firm resolve" or "with steadfastness." It emphasizes the need for determination and unwavering focus in one's pursuits.
  • dṛhya: This term translates to "to hold" or "to grasp." It suggests the importance of holding onto one's goals and aspirations with tenacity.
  • maghavan: This word refers to "the generous one" or "the one who bestows blessings." It signifies the importance of generosity and the sharing of resources and knowledge.
  • maghattaye: This can be interpreted as "for greatness" or "for abundance." It emphasizes the pursuit of greatness and the aspiration for a life filled with abundance and prosperity.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a powerful invocation for strength, determination, and the pursuit of greatness. It serves as a reminder that in our quest for success, we must seek guidance from those who are wise and deserving, while also embodying qualities of generosity and steadfastness. In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios such as personal development, leadership, and community service. For instance, when facing challenges in a career, one can draw upon the strength and resolve mentioned in the mantra to persevere and achieve their goals. Additionally, in leadership roles, embodying the qualities of generosity and wisdom can inspire others and foster a supportive environment. Overall, this mantra encourages individuals to strive for greatness while remaining grounded in values of strength, determination, and generosity.
