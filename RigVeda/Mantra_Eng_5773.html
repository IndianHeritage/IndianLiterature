मन्त्र: प्रतिकेतवः प्रथमादृश्रन्न ऊर्ध्वा अस्याः अञ्जयो विश्रयन्ते | उषो अरवाचाः बृहतां रथेन ज्योतिर्मता वामम् अस्मभ्यं वक्षि ||
prati ketavaḥ prathamā adṛśrann ūrdhvā asyā añjayo vi śrayante | uṣo arvācā bṛhatā rathena jyotiṣmatā vāmam asmabhyaṁ vakṣi ||
  • Mantra: "prati ketavaḥ prathamā adṛśrann ūrdhvā asyā añjayo vi śrayante | uṣo arvācā bṛhatā rathena jyotiṣmatā vāmam asmabhyaṁ vakṣi ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • prati: towards
    • ketavaḥ: the wise or knowledgeable ones
    • prathamā: first or foremost
    • adṛśrann: unseen or hidden
    • ūrdhvā: upward
    • asyā: of this
    • añjayo: those who are endowed or blessed
    • vi: indeed
    • śrayante: they rely or depend
    • uṣo: dawn or light
    • arvācā: from the east
    • bṛhatā: great or vast
    • rathena: with a chariot
    • jyotiṣmatā: with brightness or light
    • vāmam: auspicious or favorable
    • asmabhyaṁ: for us
    • vakṣi: you will bestow or grant
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra speaks of the wise individuals who are the first to perceive the unseen aspects of life.
    • It emphasizes the importance of looking upward and relying on the blessings of those who are enlightened.
    • The dawn is depicted as a powerful force that brings light and clarity, symbolizing new beginnings and opportunities.
    • The mention of a chariot suggests movement and progress, indicating that with the right guidance, one can navigate through life’s challenges.
    • The mantra concludes with a request for blessings that are favorable and beneficial for us, highlighting the importance of divine support in our endeavors.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, individuals who are knowledgeable and experienced often guide newcomers, helping them navigate the complexities of their roles.
    • During challenging times, people often look towards mentors or spiritual leaders for guidance, much like relying on the dawn for light in darkness.
    • In personal growth, one may seek enlightenment through education and self-reflection, akin to the upward journey mentioned in the mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of wisdom, guidance, and the pursuit of enlightenment in our lives.
    • It teaches us to seek the unseen truths and rely on the support of those who have attained knowledge.
    • In real-life scenarios, it encourages us to embrace new beginnings with optimism and to recognize the divine forces that aid us in our journey.
