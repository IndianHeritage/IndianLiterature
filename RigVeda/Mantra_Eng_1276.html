तिस्रः क्षपस् त्रिर अहातिव्रजद्भिर नासत्याः भुज्युम ऊहथुः पतङ्गैः |समुद्रस्य धन्वन् आर्द्रस्य पारि त्रिभिः रथैः शतपद्भिः षलश्वैः ||
tisraḥ kṣapas trir ahātivrajadbhir nāsatyā bhujyum ūhathuḥ pataṁgaiḥ | samudrasya dhanvann ārdrasya pāre tribhī rathaiḥ śatapadbhiḥ ṣaḻaśvaiḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • tisraḥ: three
    • kṣapas: dawns
    • trir: thrice
    • ahātivrajadbhir: with those who have not gone away
    • nāsatyā: the Asvins, divine twin horsemen
    • bhujyum: the one who is strong
    • ūhathuḥ: they have taken
    • pataṁgaiḥ: with the wings
    • samudrasya: of the ocean
    • dhanvann: with a bow
    • ārdrasya: of the moist
    • pāre: on the shore
    • tribhī: with three
    • rathaiḥ: chariots
    • śatapadbhiḥ: with a hundred feet
    • ṣaḻaśvaiḥ: with six horses
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the three dawns that come forth, symbolizing the arrival of new beginnings and opportunities.
    • The mention of the Asvins, who are known for their healing and protective qualities, suggests a call for divine assistance in overcoming challenges.
    • The imagery of chariots and horses evokes a sense of movement and progress, indicating that one should harness their strengths and resources to navigate through life's journey.
    • The ocean represents vastness and depth, symbolizing the challenges and experiences one may encounter, while the moist shore signifies the nurturing aspects of life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional context, this mantra can be applied when embarking on a new project or career path, reminding individuals to seek support and utilize their skills effectively.
    • In personal life, it can serve as a reminder to embrace new beginnings, such as starting a family or moving to a new place, while also being open to help from others.
    • In times of adversity, invoking the Asvins can symbolize seeking guidance and healing, whether through community support or personal resilience.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of recognizing new opportunities and the support available from both divine and earthly sources.
    • It encourages individuals to be proactive in their endeavors, harnessing their strengths and seeking assistance when needed.
    • Ultimately, it serves as a reminder that life is a journey filled with challenges and opportunities, and with the right mindset and support, one can navigate through it successfully.
