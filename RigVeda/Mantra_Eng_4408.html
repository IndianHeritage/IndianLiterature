नū नो अग्नि 'वृकेभिः स्वस्ति वेषि रायाḥ पथिभिः पर्ष्य अंहः |तां सूरिभ्यो गृणते रासी सुम्नम मदेम शतहिमाः सुवीराः ||
nū no agne 'vṛkebhiḥ svasti veṣi rāyaḥ pathibhiḥ parṣy aṁhaḥ | tā sūribhyo gṛṇate rāsi sumnam madema śatahimāḥ suvīrāḥ ||
  • nū no agne: This phrase translates to "O Agni," where Agni represents the fire god, a significant deity in Vedic literature symbolizing energy, transformation, and purification.
  • 'vṛkebhiḥ: This word means "with the wolves," which can symbolize strength, ferocity, and the ability to overcome challenges.
  • svasti veṣi: This translates to "may you bring well-being," indicating a prayer for health, prosperity, and overall good fortune.
  • rāyaḥ: This means "wealth" or "prosperity," emphasizing the importance of material and spiritual abundance in life.
  • pathibhiḥ: This word means "by the paths," suggesting the various routes or choices one can take in life.
  • parṣy aṁhaḥ: This translates to "may we be protected," indicating a desire for safety and security in our endeavors.
  • tā sūribhyo gṛṇate: This means "those who are strong sing," highlighting the power of strength and resilience in achieving success.
  • rāsi sumnam: This translates to "may we have a collection of good fortune," emphasizing the importance of gathering positive experiences and blessings.
  • madema: This means "let us be joyful," indicating a desire for happiness and contentment in life.
  • śatahimāḥ: This translates to "a hundredfold," suggesting the wish for abundance and prosperity in all aspects of life.
  • suvīrāḥ: This means "with good heroes," indicating the importance of companionship and support from strong, virtuous individuals.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda encapsulates a prayer for well-being, prosperity, and protection. It emphasizes the significance of strength, both personal and communal, in navigating life's challenges. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as during significant life events like weddings, new ventures, or even daily rituals, where individuals seek blessings for success and happiness. The invocation of Agni symbolizes the transformative power of fire, urging individuals to embrace change and growth while fostering a supportive community around them. Ultimately, this mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of strength, prosperity, and well-being in our lives.
