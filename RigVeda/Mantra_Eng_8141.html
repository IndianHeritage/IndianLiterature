अभि गव्यानीं वीतये नृम्णा पुनानो अर्षसि |सन्द्वाजः परि श्रव ||
abhi gavyāni vītaye nṛmṇā punāno arṣasi | sanadvājaḥ pari srava ||
  • Mantra: "abhi gavyāni vītaye nṛmṇā punāno arṣasi | sanadvājaḥ pari srava ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • abhi: towards, for
    • gavyāni: the cows, the essence of nourishment
    • vītaye: for the purpose of liberation or prosperity
    • nṛmṇā: by men, through human effort
    • punāno: purifying, renewing
    • arṣasi: you (the divine) are present
    • sanadvājaḥ: with good banners, auspicious signs
    • pari: all around, completely
    • srava: flow, abundance
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the divine presence to bless the efforts of humanity.
    • The mention of "gavyāni" signifies the importance of cows, which are often seen as symbols of wealth and nourishment in Vedic culture.
    • The phrase "nṛmṇā punāno" emphasizes the role of human actions in achieving prosperity and purification.
    • "Sanadvājaḥ" suggests that auspicious signs and blessings are essential for success.
    • The overall essence of the mantra is a call for divine support in human endeavors, particularly in the pursuit of prosperity and well-being.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In agriculture, farmers often pray for a good harvest, recognizing that their hard work needs divine support for success.
    • In business, entrepreneurs may seek blessings for their ventures, understanding that while they put in effort, external factors also play a significant role.
    • Communities often come together in rituals to invoke prosperity and abundance, reflecting the collective effort and divine assistance in achieving common goals.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the interconnectedness of human effort and divine grace.
    • It serves as a reminder that while we strive for success, we should also seek blessings and maintain a sense of humility.
    • In modern life, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as personal growth, community development, and environmental sustainability, where collective efforts are essential for achieving prosperity.
