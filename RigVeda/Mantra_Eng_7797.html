अभि विप्राः अनूषत गावो वत्सं न मातरः | इन्द्रं सोमस्य पीतये ||
abhi viprā anūṣata gāvo vatsaṁ na mātaraḥ | indraṁ somasya pītaye ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • abhi: towards, for
    • viprā: the wise, learned, or enlightened ones
    • anūṣata: may they be nourished or sustained
    • gāvo: the cows (symbolizing wealth and nourishment)
    • vatsaṁ: the calf (representing new beginnings or offspring)
    • na: not
    • mātaraḥ: mothers (referring to the nurturing aspect)
    • indraṁ: Indra (the king of gods, representing strength and power)
    • somasya: of Soma (a sacred drink, symbolizing immortality and divine inspiration)
    • pītaye: for drinking or consumption
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the wise and learned individuals, asking for their nourishment and support.
    • The mention of cows and calves symbolizes prosperity, sustenance, and the nurturing aspect of life.
    • Indra, as the king of gods, represents strength and the ability to overcome challenges, while Soma signifies divine inspiration and the quest for immortality.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community, the wise individuals (like teachers or leaders) play a crucial role in guiding and nurturing the younger generation, much like how cows nurture their calves.
    • In agriculture, the health of livestock (cows) is essential for the prosperity of farmers, symbolizing the importance of nurturing resources for growth.
    • In personal development, seeking wisdom and inspiration (like that from Indra and Soma) can lead to overcoming life's challenges and achieving one's goals.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of wisdom, nurturing, and strength in achieving prosperity and overcoming challenges.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, such as education, community building, and personal growth, highlighting the interconnectedness of knowledge, sustenance, and divine inspiration.
    • By invoking these qualities, individuals and communities can strive for a balanced and prosperous life.
