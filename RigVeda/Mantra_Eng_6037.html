स्तुतश्च यास त्वा वर्धन्ति महे राधसे नृम्णाय |इन्द्र कारिणं वृधन्तः ||
stutaś ca yās tvā vardhanti mahe rādhase nṛmṇāya | indra kāriṇaṁ vṛdhantaḥ ||
  • stutaś ca yās tvā - "Those who praise you" - This phrase emphasizes the importance of recognition and appreciation. In real life, this can be seen in various contexts such as workplaces where employees are acknowledged for their hard work, leading to increased motivation and productivity.
  • vardhanti - "increase" or "enhance" - This word signifies growth and development. For example, when a community comes together to support local businesses, it leads to economic growth and prosperity for all involved.
  • mahe rādhase - "to the great benefactor" - This refers to a powerful figure or deity who provides support and blessings. In a modern context, this could relate to leaders or mentors who guide and uplift others, fostering a sense of community and shared purpose.
  • nṛmṇāya - "for the benefit of mankind" - This phrase highlights the altruistic aspect of actions taken for the greater good. An example can be seen in charitable organizations that work towards improving the lives of underprivileged individuals, showcasing humanity's capacity for compassion.
  • indra kāriṇaṁ - "the doer of great deeds" - This refers to someone who takes action and achieves significant accomplishments. In real life, this could be likened to innovators and leaders who drive change and inspire others through their actions.
  • vṛdhantaḥ - "those who grow" - This word signifies the idea of continuous growth and improvement. In personal development, individuals who seek knowledge and skills through education and experience exemplify this concept.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of praise, growth, and altruism in the context of human actions and divine support. It highlights how recognizing and appreciating the contributions of others can lead to collective growth and benefit mankind. In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios such as community service, workplace dynamics, and personal development, reminding us of the importance of uplifting one another for the greater good.
