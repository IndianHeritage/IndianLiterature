आ विश्वदेवं सत्पतिं सूक्तैर अद्य वृणीमहे |सत्यसवं सवितारम् ||
ā viśvadevaṁ satpatiṁ sūktair adyā vṛṇīmahe | satyasavaṁ savitāram ||
  • Mantra: "ā viśvadevaṁ satpatiṁ sūktair adyā vṛṇīmahe | satyasavaṁ savitāram ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā: To, towards
    • viśvadevam: The universal deities or gods
    • satpatiṁ: The lord of truth or the one who embodies truth
    • sūktair: With hymns or praises
    • adyā: Today or in the present
    • vṛṇīmahe: We choose or we invoke
    • satyasavam: The one who is full of truth
    • savitāram: The sun or the source of life
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to the universal deities, invoking their presence and guidance.
    • It emphasizes the importance of truth and integrity, as it calls upon the lord of truth.
    • The phrase "with hymns" indicates that the invocation is made with sincere devotion and respect.
    • By saying "today," it highlights the immediacy and relevance of the prayer in our current lives.
    • The mention of the sun symbolizes enlightenment, vitality, and the source of all life, reinforcing the connection between truth and life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, leaders can use this mantra to inspire a culture of transparency and honesty, encouraging employees to uphold ethical standards.
    • In personal relationships, invoking the essence of this mantra can remind individuals to communicate openly and truthfully, fostering trust and understanding.
    • In educational environments, teachers can encourage students to seek knowledge and wisdom, aligning with the pursuit of truth as a guiding principle.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the significance of truth in all aspects of life.
    • It encourages individuals and communities to seek divine guidance in their pursuit of honesty and integrity.
    • By invoking the universal deities and the essence of truth, we align ourselves with higher principles that can lead to personal and collective growth.
    • In essence, this mantra is applicable in various real-life scenarios, promoting a harmonious and truthful existence.
