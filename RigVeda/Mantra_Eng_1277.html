अनारम्भणे तद अवीरयेताम् अनास्थाने अग्रभणे समुद्रे |यद अश्विनाः ऊहथुर भुज्युम अस्तं शतारित्रां नावम् आतस्थिवांसम् ||
anārambhaṇe tad avīrayethām anāsthāne agrabhaṇe samudre | yad aśvinā ūhathur bhujyum astaṁ śatāritrāṁ nāvam ātasthivāṁsam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • anārambhaṇe: In the context of not starting or initiating.
    • tad: That.
    • avīrayethām: May it not be disturbed or disrupted.
    • anāsthāne: In a state of instability or uncertainty.
    • agrabhaṇe: In the forefront or leading position.
    • samudre: In the ocean or vastness.
    • yad: That which.
    • aśvinā: By the Aśvins, the twin deities associated with health and medicine.
    • ūhathur: They have provided or bestowed.
    • bhujyum: Strength or power.
    • astaṁ: To the setting or decline.
    • śatāritrāṁ: A hundred stars or celestial bodies.
    • nāvam: The boat or vessel.
    • ātasthivāṁsam: They have placed or anchored.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks to the importance of stability and strength in the face of uncertainty.
    • The reference to the ocean symbolizes the vast challenges and unpredictability of life.
    • The Aśvins, as divine figures, represent the support and guidance we can receive during difficult times.
    • The imagery of a boat anchored amidst a hundred stars suggests finding direction and purpose even when surrounded by chaos.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, launching a new product can be daunting. The mantra reminds entrepreneurs to remain stable and not rush into decisions without careful planning.
    • In personal life, during times of emotional turmoil, seeking guidance from trusted friends or mentors can provide the strength needed to navigate through challenges.
    • In education, students facing exams may feel overwhelmed. This mantra encourages them to find their focus and not be swayed by distractions.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for stability and strength amidst uncertainty.
    • It teaches us to seek guidance and support from others, much like the Aśvins, who provide strength and direction.
    • In real-life scenarios, whether in personal challenges, professional endeavors, or academic pursuits, this mantra serves as a reminder to remain grounded and focused, ensuring that we do not lose our way in the vast ocean of life.
