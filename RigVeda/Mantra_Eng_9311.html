एवां देवां इन्द्रो विव्ये नृन् प्र च्यौतनेन मगवां सत्यराधाः |विश्वे ता ते हरिवः शचीवोऽभि तुरासः स्वयशो गृणन्ति ||
evā devām̐ indro vivye nṝn pra cyautnena maghavā satyarādhāḥ | viśvet tā te harivaḥ śacīvo 'bhi turāsaḥ svayaśo gṛṇanti ||
  • evā devām̐ indro vivye: This phrase can be translated as "Thus, Indra, the god, is invoked." Indra is often regarded as the king of the gods in Vedic literature, representing strength and power. In real life, this can be seen as a call for strength and guidance in challenging situations, much like how people seek inspiration from leaders or mentors.
  • nṝn pra cyautnena maghavā satyarādhāḥ: This translates to "For the men, through the power of truth, may the mighty one (Indra) bestow his blessings." Here, the emphasis is on the importance of truth and righteousness. In everyday life, this can be related to the idea that honesty and integrity lead to success and respect in society, similar to how businesses thrive on trust with their customers.
  • viśvet tā te harivaḥ: This means "All beings, may they be favorable to you." It reflects the interconnectedness of all life and the importance of harmony. In a real-world context, this can be seen in community efforts where individuals come together for a common cause, promoting unity and cooperation for the greater good.
  • śacīvo 'bhi turāsaḥ svayaśo gṛṇanti: This translates to "The powerful ones, who are full of energy, sing your praises." This highlights the idea of recognizing and celebrating strength and achievements. In modern life, this can be likened to how we celebrate achievements in various fields, such as sports or academics, encouraging others to strive for excellence.

  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of invoking divine strength, the power of truth, and the importance of community and recognition. In real life, it serves as a reminder to seek guidance from higher powers, uphold integrity, foster unity, and celebrate achievements. This mantra can be applicable in various scenarios, such as during personal challenges, community gatherings, or even in professional settings where teamwork and recognition play a crucial role in success.
