रथीव कशयाश्वां अभिक्षिपन्न आविर्दूतान कृतुते वर्ष्याम् अह |दूरात सिंहस्य स्तनथा उद ईरते यत वर्ष्यं नभः ||
rathīva kaśayāśvām̐ abhikṣipann āvir dūtān kṛṇute varṣyā3m̐ aha | dūrāt siṁhasya stanathā ud īrate yat parjanyaḥ kṛṇute varṣya1ṁ nabhaḥ ||
  • Mantra: "rathīva kaśayāśvām̐ abhikṣipann āvir dūtān kṛṇute varṣyā3m̐ aha | dūrāt siṁhasya stanathā ud īrate yat parjanyaḥ kṛṇute varṣya1ṁ nabhaḥ ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • rathīva: like a chariot
    • kaśayāśvām̐: with swift horses
    • abhikṣipann: having been sent forth
    • āvir: manifest
    • dūtān: messengers
    • kṛṇute: brings forth
    • varṣyām: in the rainy season
    • aha: I
    • dūrāt: from afar
    • siṁhasya: of the lion
    • stanathā: from the roar
    • ud īrate: rises up
    • yat: that which
    • parjanyaḥ: the rain-bringer
    • kṛṇute: brings forth
    • varṣyaṁ: rain
    • nabhaḥ: the sky
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra invokes the imagery of a chariot driven by swift horses, symbolizing speed and power. In real life, this can be likened to the rapid advancements in technology that propel society forward.
    • The mention of messengers being sent forth signifies communication and the dissemination of knowledge. For example, in today's world, the internet serves as a messenger, connecting people and ideas across vast distances.
    • The reference to the rainy season highlights the importance of rain for agriculture and sustenance. Just as rain nourishes the earth, education and knowledge nourish the mind and spirit.
    • The lion's roar represents strength and authority. In a corporate setting, a strong leader can inspire and motivate a team, much like the lion commands respect in the animal kingdom.
    • The rain-bringer, or Parjanya, symbolizes abundance and prosperity. In a broader context, this can relate to the idea of abundance in life, whether it be in relationships, career, or personal growth.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the interconnectedness of speed, communication, strength, and abundance. It serves as a reminder that just as the elements of nature work together to create life, so too must we harness our strengths and communicate effectively to thrive in our personal and professional lives.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire individuals to embrace change, seek knowledge, and cultivate strength within themselves and their communities. It encourages us to be proactive in our pursuits, much like the swift chariot racing towards its destination.
