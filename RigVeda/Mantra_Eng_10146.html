तं मर्ता अमर्त्यं घृतेनाग्निं सपर्यत |अदाभ्यं गृहपतिम् ||
tam martā amartyaṁ ghṛtenāgniṁ saparyata | adābhyaṁ gṛhapatim ||
  • tam martā amartyaṁ: This phrase can be translated as "that mortal (martā) becomes immortal (amartya)." It suggests the idea of transcending mortality through certain actions or rituals.
  • ghṛtenāgniṁ: This translates to "with ghee (ghṛta) and fire (agni)." In Vedic rituals, ghee is often used as an offering to the sacred fire, symbolizing purity and the essence of life.
  • saparyata: This means "worship" or "to be revered." It indicates the importance of honoring the divine or the sacred elements in our lives.
  • adābhyaṁ: This can be interpreted as "without obstacles" or "free from hindrances." It emphasizes the need for a clear and unobstructed path in spiritual practices.
  • gṛhapatim: This refers to the "lord of the house" or "householder." It symbolizes the importance of family and domestic life in the pursuit of spiritual goals.

  • Real-Life Example: In modern life, the mantra can be seen as a call to balance the material and spiritual aspects of existence. For instance, a person who dedicates time to family (gṛhapatim) while also engaging in spiritual practices (saparyata) can find a sense of immortality through their legacy and values passed on to future generations.
  • Significance: The mantra emphasizes the importance of rituals and offerings (ghṛtenāgniṁ) in achieving a higher state of being. It suggests that through devotion and the right actions, one can transcend the limitations of mortality (tam martā amartyaṁ).
  • Application: This mantra can be applied in daily life by encouraging individuals to engage in practices that honor their heritage and values, such as family gatherings, rituals, or community service, which can foster a sense of connection and continuity beyond one's lifetime.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of living a life that harmonizes the mortal and the immortal, the material and the spiritual. By recognizing the significance of our actions and the importance of honoring our roots, we can create a meaningful existence that resonates through time, ultimately leading to a form of immortality through the impact we have on others and the world around us.
