याव इथ्था श्लोकम् आ दिवो ज्योतिर्जनाय चक्रतुः |आ न ऊर्जं वहतम अश्विनाय युवम् ||
yāv itthā ślokam ā divo jyotir janāya cakrathuḥ | ā na ūrjaṁ vahatam aśvinā yuvam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yāv: As long as
    • itthā: Thus
    • ślokam: Verse or hymn
    • ā: From
    • dīvo: The sky or heaven
    • jyotir: Light or illumination
    • janāya: For the people or for the community
    • cakrathuḥ: May they achieve or may they attain
    • ā: From
    • na: Not
    • ūrjaṁ: Energy or strength
    • vahatam: To carry or to bear
    • aśvinā: O Ashvins (the twin deities)
    • yuvam: You two (referring to the Ashvins)
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the divine light from the heavens, emphasizing the importance of illumination and knowledge for the community.
    • The mention of the Ashvins, who are known as the divine physicians, signifies a request for health, vitality, and the energy needed to thrive.
    • The phrase "may they achieve" suggests a collective aspiration for success and well-being, highlighting the interconnectedness of individuals within a society.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community setting, this mantra can be recited during gatherings or festivals to invoke blessings for collective health and prosperity.
    • In educational institutions, it can serve as a reminder of the importance of knowledge and enlightenment, encouraging students to seek wisdom for the betterment of society.
    • In healthcare settings, invoking the Ashvins can symbolize the hope for healing and the importance of medical advancements for the welfare of the community.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of divine guidance in achieving collective goals and well-being.
    • It serves as a reminder that the pursuit of knowledge, health, and energy is essential for the progress of society.
    • By invoking the blessings of the Ashvins, individuals and communities can aspire to attain a harmonious and prosperous existence.
