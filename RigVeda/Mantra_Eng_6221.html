आ यातं नहुषस पर्य आन्तरिक्षात सुवृक्तिभिः |पिबाथो अश्विनां मधु कण्वानां सवने सुतम् ||
ā yātaṁ nahuṣas pary āntarikṣāt suvṛktibhiḥ | pibātho aśvinā madhu kaṇvānāṁ savane sutam ||
  • Mantra: "ā yātaṁ nahuṣas pary āntarikṣāt suvṛktibhiḥ | pibātho aśvinā madhu kaṇvānāṁ savane sutam ||"
  • Translation: "O Ashvins, may you come to us from the heavens, bringing us the sweet nectar of life, the essence of joy and prosperity."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • ā yātaṁ: "come to us" - a call for divine presence.
    • nahuṣas: "of Nahusha" - referring to a legendary king, symbolizing strength and virtue.
    • pary: "from all around" - indicating a universal appeal.
    • āntarikṣāt: "from the heavens" - a reference to celestial beings or divine forces.
    • suvṛktibhiḥ: "with sweet words" - suggesting the importance of positive communication and blessings.
    • pibātho: "may you drink" - an invitation to partake in the blessings offered.
    • aśvinā: "O Ashvins" - invoking the twin deities associated with health and vitality.
    • madhu: "nectar" - symbolizing sweetness, joy, and abundance.
    • kaṇvānāṁ: "of the Kanva" - referring to a lineage or a group known for their wisdom.
    • savane: "in the sacrifice" - indicating the context of rituals and offerings.
    • sutam: "the essence" - representing the core of what is being offered or sought.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a modern context, this mantra can be likened to the way we seek guidance and blessings in our lives, whether through prayer, meditation, or community gatherings.
    • For instance, during a wedding ceremony, couples often invoke blessings for a prosperous and joyful life together, similar to how the mantra calls for divine presence and sweetness in life.
    • In corporate settings, leaders may use motivational speeches to inspire their teams, akin to the "sweet words" mentioned in the mantra, fostering a positive and productive environment.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of seeking divine blessings and the importance of positive communication in our lives.
    • It serves as a reminder that we can invite joy, prosperity, and health into our lives through our intentions and actions.
    • In various aspects of life, from personal relationships to professional endeavors, the essence of this mantra can guide us to cultivate a harmonious and fulfilling existence.
