न तं अंहो न दुरितं कुतश्चन नारातयस् तितिरुर न द्वयाविनः |विश्वा इद अस्माद ध्वरसो वि बाधसे यं सुगोपाऽ रक्षसि ब्रह्मणस पते ||
na tam aṁho na duritaṁ kutaś cana nārātayas titirur na dvayāvinaḥ | viśvā id asmād dhvaraso vi bādhase yaṁ sugopā rakṣasi brahmaṇas pate ||
  • Translation: The mantra can be translated as: "Neither the evil nor the misfortune can touch us; we are not afraid of any adversities. All beings are free from fear, and you, O protector of the universe, safeguard us from all harm."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • na: not
    • tam: that
    • aṁho: evil
    • duritaṁ: misfortune
    • kutaś cana: from anywhere
    • nārātayas: beings (often referring to humans)
    • titirur: free from fear
    • na: not
    • dvayāvinaḥ: those who are dual-minded or fearful
    • viśvā: all beings
    • id: this
    • asmād: from us
    • dhvaraso: from the obstacles
    • vi: indeed
    • bādhase: you protect
    • yaṁ: whom
    • sugopā: good protector
    • rakṣasi: protect
    • brahmaṇas: of the universe
    • pate: O Lord
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In times of personal crisis, such as losing a job or facing health issues, individuals often recite this mantra to invoke a sense of protection and to dispel fear.
    • During community gatherings or religious ceremonies, this mantra can be chanted to foster a collective sense of security and unity among participants.
    • In educational settings, students may use this mantra to overcome exam anxiety, reminding themselves that they are protected from failure and misfortune.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its powerful affirmation of protection and fearlessness. It serves as a reminder that, despite the adversities we may face, we can find strength in our faith and community. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as personal challenges, communal support, and mental well-being, reinforcing the belief that we are safeguarded by a higher power. By embracing this mantra, individuals can cultivate resilience and a positive outlook, enabling them to navigate life's uncertainties with confidence.
