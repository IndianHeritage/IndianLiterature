रथवाहनं हविर अस्य नाम यत्रायुधं निहितं अस्य वर्म |तत्रा रथम उप शग्मं सदेम विष्वाहा वयं सुमनस्यमानाः ||
rathavāhanaṁ havir asya nāma yatrāyudhaṁ nihitam asya varma | tatrā ratham upa śagmaṁ sadema viśvāhā vayaṁ sumanasyamānāḥ ||
  • Mantra: "rathavāhanaṁ havir asya nāma yatrāyudhaṁ nihitam asya varma | tatrā ratham upa śagmaṁ sadema viśvāhā vayaṁ sumanasyamānāḥ ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • rathavāhanaṁ: The chariot bearer or the one who drives the chariot.
    • haviḥ: Offering or sacrifice.
    • asya: Of this.
    • nāma: Name or identity.
    • yatra: Where.
    • ayudhaṁ: Weapons or tools.
    • nihitam: Hidden or placed.
    • asya: Of this.
    • varma: Armor or protection.
    • tatrā: There.
    • ratham: Chariot.
    • upa: Near or close to.
    • śagmaṁ: Strength or power.
    • sadema: May we be together.
    • viśvāhā: All beings or universal.
    • vayaṁ: We.
    • sumanasyamānāḥ: With joyful minds or auspicious thoughts.
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra speaks of the chariot as a symbol of strength and protection. In ancient times, chariots were crucial in battles, representing power and the ability to overcome challenges.
    • The phrase "rathavāhanaṁ" signifies the importance of having a strong foundation or support system in life, akin to having a reliable vehicle that takes you forward.
    • The mention of "haviḥ" and "offering" indicates the need for sacrifice and dedication in achieving one's goals, similar to how athletes train rigorously to excel in their sports.
    • "yatra ayudhaṁ nihitam" suggests that true strength and weapons are often hidden within us, much like the potential that individuals possess but may not always recognize.
    • The call for "sadema viśvāhā" emphasizes unity and collective strength, akin to teamwork in a corporate environment where collaboration leads to success.
    • Finally, "sumanasyamānāḥ" reflects the importance of maintaining a positive mindset, which is essential in overcoming obstacles, much like how a positive attitude can help individuals navigate through life's challenges.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of strength, unity, and a positive mindset in overcoming life's challenges.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, such as teamwork in professional settings, personal development, and even in sports where collective effort and individual potential play a crucial role.
    • By embracing the teachings of this mantra, individuals can cultivate resilience, foster collaboration, and maintain a positive outlook, ultimately leading to success and fulfillment in their endeavors.
