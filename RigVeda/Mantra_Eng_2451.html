उपक्षेतारस तव सुप्रणीत 'अग्ने विश्वानि धन्या दधानाः |सुरेतसाऽश्रवसा तुञ्जमानाऽभि ष्याम पृतनायूम् रदेवान् ||
upakṣetāras tava supraṇīte 'gne viśvāni dhanyā dadhānāḥ | suretasā śravasā tuñjamānā abhi ṣyāma pṛtanāyūm̐r adevān ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • upakṣetāras: Those who are devoted or dedicated.
    • tava: Your.
    • supraṇīte: Well-arranged or well-prepared.
    • agne: O Fire (a reference to Agni, the fire god).
    • viśvāni: All or universal.
    • dhanyā: Blessed or prosperous.
    • dadhānāḥ: Holding or possessing.
    • suretasā: With good intellect or wisdom.
    • śravasā: With fame or glory.
    • tuñjamānā: Being praised or celebrated.
    • abhi: Towards or in the direction of.
    • ṣyāma: We may become or we may be.
    • pṛtanāyūm̐r: In the path of righteousness or truth.
    • adevān: Divine beings or deities.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the blessings of Agni, the fire god, symbolizing purity, transformation, and the divine presence in our lives.
    • It emphasizes the importance of being dedicated and well-prepared in our endeavors, suggesting that those who are blessed and possess wisdom will be recognized and celebrated.
    • The mantra encourages individuals to align themselves with righteousness and truth, seeking the guidance of divine forces in their actions.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, a team that is well-prepared and dedicated to their goals often achieves success and recognition, much like the blessings invoked in the mantra.
    • Students who study diligently and seek knowledge with wisdom are often celebrated for their achievements, reflecting the essence of being "tuñjamānā."
    • In personal relationships, those who act with integrity and truthfulness tend to build stronger bonds, aligning with the idea of "pṛtanāyūm̐r."
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the significance of dedication, preparation, and righteousness in our lives.
    • It highlights the importance of seeking divine guidance and wisdom in our actions, encouraging us to strive for prosperity and recognition through our efforts.
    • In essence, it teaches us that by aligning ourselves with higher principles and being devoted to our paths, we can achieve success and fulfillment in various aspects of life.
