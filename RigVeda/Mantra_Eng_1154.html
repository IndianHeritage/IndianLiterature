सम् मां तापन्य अभितः sapatnīr इव पर्शवः |मूषो न शिश्ना व्य अदन्ति माध्यः स्तोतारं ते शतक्रतो वित्तं मे अस्य रोदसी ||
sam mā tapanty abhitaḥ sapatnīr iva parśavaḥ | mūṣo na śiśnā vy adanti mādhyaḥ stotāraṁ te śatakrato vittam me asya rodasī ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sam: together, in harmony
    • mā: not
    • tapanty: they burn, they shine
    • abhitaḥ: around, in all directions
    • sapatnīr: like rivals, competitors
    • iva: like, as if
    • parśavaḥ: the sides, the edges
    • mūṣo: of the mouse, the small creature
    • na: not
    • śiśnā: to be frightened, to be scared
    • vy: away, apart
    • adanti: they bite, they harm
    • mādhyaḥ: in the middle, the center
    • stotāraṁ: the one who praises, the worshipper
    • te: your
    • śatakrato: O hundred-fold, referring to Indra
    • vittam: wealth, riches
    • me: my
    • asya: of this
    • rodasī: the two worlds, the heavens and the earth
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the harmony and balance in life, emphasizing the importance of unity and cooperation.
    • The imagery of rivals (sapatnīr) suggests that competition is a natural part of existence, yet it should not lead to conflict.
    • The reference to the mouse (mūṣo) indicates that even small fears or challenges should not deter one from their path.
    • The phrase "O hundred-fold" (śatakrato) invokes the power of Indra, symbolizing strength and abundance.
    • The mention of wealth (vittam) signifies the importance of prosperity in both material and spiritual realms.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace, collaboration among team members can lead to greater success than individual competition.
    • In personal relationships, understanding and supporting each other can help overcome challenges, rather than allowing rivalries to create discord.
    • In financial matters, seeking balance between saving and spending can lead to a more prosperous life.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra teaches us the significance of unity, cooperation, and the pursuit of wealth in a balanced manner.
    • It encourages individuals to face their fears and challenges without being overwhelmed, promoting resilience.
    • In real life, applying these principles can lead to harmonious relationships, successful teamwork, and a fulfilling life.
