प्रिय पदानि पश्वो न पाही विश्वायुर अग्नि गुहा गुहं गाः ||
priyā padāni paśvo ni pāhi viśvāyur agne guhā guhaṁ gāḥ ||
  • Mantra: "priyā padāni paśvo ni pāhi viśvāyur agne guhā guhaṁ gāḥ ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • priyā: dear, beloved
    • padāni: paths, ways
    • paśvo: of the animals, creatures
    • ni: down, below
    • pāhi: protect, guard
    • viśvāyur: all beings, universal life
    • agne: O Agni, the fire god
    • guhā: cave, hidden place
    • guhaṁ: secret, concealed
    • gāḥ: lead, guide
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Agni, the fire god, asking for protection and guidance.
    • The phrase "priyā padāni" suggests a desire for safe and beloved paths, indicating the importance of choosing the right direction in life.
    • "paśvo ni pāhi" emphasizes the need to protect all living beings, highlighting our responsibility towards animals and nature.
    • "viśvāyur agne" acknowledges Agni as the source of universal life, representing the interconnectedness of all existence.
    • The mention of "guhā guhaṁ" points to the hidden aspects of life, suggesting that there are deeper truths and wisdom to be discovered.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In our daily lives, we often face choices that can lead us down different paths. This mantra encourages us to seek guidance and protection in making those choices.
    • As stewards of the environment, we are reminded of our duty to protect animals and nature, reflecting the mantra's call for safeguarding all beings.
    • In moments of uncertainty, we can look for the hidden wisdom in our experiences, much like the "guhā guhaṁ" suggests, leading us to deeper understanding.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of guidance, protection, and responsibility in our lives.
    • It encourages us to seek the right paths, care for all living beings, and explore the hidden truths within ourselves and the world around us.
    • In a broader sense, it highlights the interconnectedness of life and the divine presence that guides us through our journeys.
