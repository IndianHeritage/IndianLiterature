स सुष्ठुभा स ऋक्वता गणेन वलं रुरोज फालिगं रवेण | बृहस्पतिर उष्रियā हव्यसूदः कनिक्रदद वावशतीर उद आजत ||
sa suṣṭubhā sa ṛkvatā gaṇena valaṁ ruroja phaligaṁ raveṇa | bṛhaspatir usriyā havyasūdaḥ kanikradad vāvaśatīr ud ājat ||
  • sa suṣṭubhā: This phrase can be translated as "that which is well-structured" or "that which is excellent." It signifies the importance of order and harmony in the universe.
  • sa ṛkvatā: This means "that which is composed of verses" or "that which is poetic." It highlights the significance of creativity and expression in life.
  • gaṇena: This translates to "with the group" or "by the assembly." It emphasizes the value of community and collective effort.
  • valaṁ ruroja: This can be interpreted as "the protective force that arises." It suggests the emergence of strength and protection in times of need.
  • phaligaṁ raveṇa: This means "the fruit of the sun." It symbolizes the rewards and benefits that come from hard work and dedication.
  • bṛhaspatir: Referring to the deity Brihaspati, the lord of wisdom and eloquence, this signifies the importance of knowledge and guidance.
  • usriyā havyasūdaḥ: This phrase can be understood as "the one who brings forth offerings." It highlights the significance of giving and sharing in society.
  • kanikradad: This means "the one who calls out" or "the one who invokes." It signifies the power of communication and the importance of expressing one's thoughts and feelings.
  • vāvaśatīr ud ājat: This can be translated as "the one who has emerged victorious." It emphasizes the triumph of good over evil and the importance of perseverance.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda encapsulates the essence of harmony, creativity, community, strength, knowledge, generosity, communication, and victory. In real life, these principles can be applied in various scenarios, such as teamwork in a workplace, the importance of education and wisdom in decision-making, and the value of community support during challenging times. By embodying these qualities, individuals can contribute to a more balanced and prosperous society, ultimately leading to personal and collective success.
