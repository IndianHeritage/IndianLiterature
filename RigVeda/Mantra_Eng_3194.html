यच चिद धि ते पुरुषत्रा यविष्ठाचित्तिभिः चक्रमा कच चिद आगः |कृधī स्व अ1स्मां अदिτερ अनागान व्य एनांसी शिश्रथो विश्वग अग्ने ||
yac cid dhi te puruṣatrā yaviṣṭhācittibhiś cakṛmā kac cid āgaḥ | kṛdhī ṣv a1smām̐ aditer anāgān vy enāṁsi śiśratho viṣvag agne ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yac: whatever
    • cid: indeed
    • dhi: thought or intellect
    • te: your
    • puruṣatrā: the one who is the protector of beings
    • yaviṣṭhācittibhiś: with the best of intentions
    • cakṛmā: we have done
    • kac: whether
    • cid: indeed
    • āgaḥ: come
    • kṛdhī: may you be pleased
    • ṣv: with us
    • asmām̐: us
    • aditer: of Aditi (the mother of gods)
    • anāgān: the innocent or sinless
    • vy: may you protect
    • enāṁsi: our beings
    • śiśratho: from all directions
    • viṣvag: all
    • agne: O Agni (the fire god)
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes Agni, the fire god, who is seen as a protector and a guide.
    • It emphasizes the importance of pure intentions and thoughts in our actions.
    • The phrase "may you be pleased with us" reflects a desire for divine favor and blessings.
    • The reference to Aditi signifies the nurturing aspect of the universe, suggesting that we seek protection for the innocent and those who are pure of heart.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, leaders often invoke the spirit of teamwork and integrity, similar to how this mantra calls for pure intentions in actions.
    • In personal relationships, the mantra can be a reminder to approach interactions with kindness and sincerity, ensuring that our intentions are clear and positive.
    • During community gatherings or rituals, people may seek blessings for protection and goodwill, akin to the invocation of Agni for safeguarding the innocent.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the significance of purity in thought and action.
    • It highlights the importance of seeking divine guidance and protection, especially for those who are innocent and pure.
    • In real life, it encourages individuals to act with integrity and to foster a sense of community and support for one another.
    • Ultimately, it teaches us that our intentions shape our actions and that seeking blessings can lead to a harmonious existence.
