त्वं दाता प्रथमो राधसाम अस्य असि सत्य ईशानकृत् |तुविद्युम्नस्य युज्या वृणीमहे पुत्रस्य शवसो महः ||
tvaṁ dātā prathamo rādhasām asy asi satya īśānakṛt | tuvidyumnasya yujyā vṛṇīmahe putrasya śavaso mahaḥ ||
  • tvaṁ dātā: This phrase translates to "You are the giver." It emphasizes the role of a divine entity or a higher power that provides and sustains life. In real life, this can be likened to parents or mentors who nurture and support individuals in their growth.
  • prathamo rādhasām: This means "the first among the givers." It signifies the importance of being the foremost in generosity and support. For example, in a community, the first person to offer help during a crisis often inspires others to follow suit.
  • asy asi satya īśānakṛt: This translates to "You are the truth, the creator of the universe." It highlights the connection between truth and creation. In everyday life, this can be seen in the pursuit of honesty and integrity, which are foundational for building trust in relationships and communities.
  • tuvidyumnasya: This means "of the one who is knowledgeable." It refers to the importance of wisdom and knowledge in guiding actions and decisions. In real life, this can be exemplified by educators or leaders who use their knowledge to uplift others.
  • yujyā vṛṇīmahe: This translates to "we seek your connection." It expresses a desire for a relationship with the divine or a higher power. In practical terms, this can be seen in the way people seek spiritual guidance or community support during challenging times.
  • putrasya śavaso mahaḥ: This means "the greatness of the son of the divine." It signifies the potential and greatness that lies within individuals, especially those who are guided by higher principles. In real life, this can be observed in young leaders or activists who embody the values of their mentors and strive to make a positive impact.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of divine generosity, truth, knowledge, and the pursuit of connection with a higher power. Its significance lies in the reminder that we are all part of a larger cosmic order, where our actions and intentions can lead to greater good. In real life, this mantra encourages individuals to embody these values, fostering a sense of community, integrity, and purpose in their lives. By recognizing the importance of giving, seeking knowledge, and striving for truth, we can create a more harmonious and supportive environment for ourselves and others.
