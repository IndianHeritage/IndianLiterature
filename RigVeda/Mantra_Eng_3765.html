प्र वेधसे कवये वेद्या गीरं भरे यशसे पूर्व्याय |घृतप्रसत्तो असुरः सुशेवो रयो धार्ता धरुणो वस्वो अग्निः ||
pra vedhase kavaye vedyāya giram bhare yaśase pūrvyāya | ghṛtaprasatto asuraḥ suśevo rāyo dhartā dharuṇo vasvo agniḥ ||
  • pra vedhase: This phrase can be translated as "to the one who knows" or "to the seer." It signifies the importance of knowledge and wisdom in guiding actions and decisions.
  • kavaye: This term refers to a "poet" or "sage," someone who possesses deep insight and understanding. It emphasizes the role of creativity and expression in conveying profound truths.
  • vedyāya: This word means "to be known" or "to be understood." It highlights the pursuit of knowledge and the importance of comprehension in our lives.
  • giram bhare: Translated as "let the words be filled," this phrase suggests the power of speech and communication. It underscores the significance of articulating thoughts and ideas effectively.
  • yaśase pūrvyāya: This means "to the one who is glorious" or "to the one who has achieved fame." It points to the value of honor and reputation in society.
  • ghṛtaprasatto: This term refers to "one who is nourished by ghee," symbolizing abundance and prosperity. It reflects the idea that sustenance and wealth are essential for a fulfilling life.
  • asuraḥ: This word can mean "divine" or "powerful." It signifies strength and the ability to overcome challenges.
  • suśevo: Translated as "well-doer," this term emphasizes the importance of good deeds and ethical behavior in achieving success.
  • rāyo dhartā: This phrase means "the one who holds wealth." It highlights the significance of managing resources wisely and responsibly.
  • dharuṇo: This term refers to "the strong" or "the steadfast." It signifies resilience and the ability to endure hardships.
  • vasvo agniḥ: Translated as "the fire of the divine," this phrase symbolizes purity, transformation, and the energy that drives life.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of knowledge, creativity, ethical behavior, and resilience in achieving success and prosperity. In real-life scenarios, it can be applied in various contexts such as education, where the pursuit of knowledge is paramount; in professional settings, where effective communication and good deeds lead to a positive reputation; and in personal development, where resilience and ethical living contribute to a fulfilling life. Overall, this mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of wisdom, action, and the pursuit of a meaningful existence.
