युधेन्द्रो मह्ना वरिवश चकार देवभ्यः सत्पतिश चर्षणिप्राह् |विवस्वतः सदने अस्य तानि विप्राः उक्तेभिः कवयो गृणन्ति ||
yudhendro mahnā varivaś cakāra devebhyaḥ satpatiś carṣaṇiprāḥ | vivasvataḥ sadane asya tāni viprā ukthebhiḥ kavayo gṛṇanti ||
  • yudhendro mahnā varivaś cakāra: This phrase can be translated as "Yudhendra, with great power, created." Here, "Yudhendra" refers to a powerful figure, often associated with leadership and strength. In real life, this can be likened to a leader or a visionary who brings about significant change or innovation in society.
  • devebhyaḥ satpatiś carṣaṇiprāḥ: This translates to "the lord of the seven realms, who is revered by the gods." The term "satpati" signifies a ruler or a master, while "carṣaṇiprāḥ" refers to those who are wise and knowledgeable. This can be related to individuals in positions of authority who are respected for their wisdom, such as educators or community leaders.
  • vivasvataḥ sadane asya: This means "in the abode of Vivasvat." Vivasvat is often associated with the sun and represents enlightenment and knowledge. This can be seen in real life as the pursuit of knowledge and truth, akin to the quest for education and understanding in various fields.
  • tāni viprā ukthebhiḥ kavayo gṛṇanti: This translates to "the wise ones, the seers, sing of these." Here, "viprā" refers to the learned or wise individuals, and "kavayo" refers to poets or seers. This can be related to the role of scholars and artists in society who express and share knowledge through their works, influencing culture and thought.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of leadership, wisdom, and the pursuit of knowledge. It signifies that true power lies in the ability to create and inspire, much like a leader who guides their community towards enlightenment. In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as in educational institutions where leaders and educators work together to foster a culture of learning and respect for knowledge. It also highlights the role of artists and thinkers in shaping society through their insights and creativity, reminding us of the interconnectedness of leadership, wisdom, and the arts in our daily lives.
