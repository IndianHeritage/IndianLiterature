उत दासाः परिविषे स्मद्दिष्टिः गोपरिणसाः |यदुस तुर्वश च मामहे ||
uta dāsā pariviṣe smaddiṣṭī goparīṇasā | yadus turvaś ca māmahe ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • uta: and
    • dāsā: the servants or helpers
    • pariviṣe: surround or encompass
    • smaddiṣṭī: with our vision or perception
    • goparīṇasā: with the strength of the protectors (often referring to cows or guardians)
    • yadus: the Yadu clan or lineage
    • turvaś: the powerful or strong
    • ca: and
    • māmahe: we invoke or we call upon
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of invoking the strength and protection of those who serve and surround us.
    • The mention of "goparīṇasā" highlights the importance of guardianship, which can be interpreted as the protective forces in our lives, such as family, friends, or even spiritual guides.
    • The reference to the Yadu clan suggests a lineage of strength and valor, emphasizing the importance of heritage and community in providing support and protection.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, the mantra can be seen as a call for teamwork and collaboration, where each member plays a role in supporting one another to achieve common goals.
    • In personal life, it can reflect the importance of family and friends who surround us with love and protection, especially during challenging times.
    • In a spiritual context, it can be interpreted as a prayer for divine protection and guidance from higher powers or deities.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of community, support, and protection in our lives.
    • It serves as a reminder to appreciate those who surround us and contribute to our well-being.
    • In real-life scenarios, invoking this mantra can inspire individuals to foster stronger relationships and seek the strength of their community, ultimately leading to a more harmonious and supportive environment.
