त्वां उ जातवेदसं विश्ववारं गृणे धियाः | अग्ने देवां आ वह नः प्रियव्रतान मृळीकाय प्रियव्रतान ||
tvām u jātavedasaṁ viśvavāraṁ gṛṇe dhiyā | agne devām̐ ā vaha naḥ priyavratān mṛḻīkāya priyavratān ||
  • tvām - This word translates to "you," referring to the divine or the fire god Agni.
  • u - This is a particle that can mean "also" or "indeed," emphasizing the connection to Agni.
  • jātavedasaṁ - This term means "the one who knows all births," highlighting Agni's role as a mediator between the earthly and the divine, as well as his omniscience.
  • viśvavāraṁ - This translates to "the protector of all," indicating Agni's protective nature over all beings and the universe.
  • gṛṇe - This means "I sing" or "I praise," showing the act of devotion and reverence towards Agni.
  • dhiyā - This word means "with intellect" or "with understanding," suggesting that the praise is not just mechanical but comes from a place of wisdom.
  • agne - This is a direct address to Agni, the fire god, invoking his presence.
  • devām̐ - This means "divine," reinforcing Agni's status as a deity.
  • ā vaha - This translates to "bring to us," indicating a request for Agni to bestow his blessings.
  • naḥ - This means "to us," emphasizing the communal aspect of the prayer.
  • priyavratān - This term means "those who are dear" or "beloved," referring to the devotees or those who follow the righteous path.
  • mṛḻīkāya - This word can be interpreted as "to the one who is gentle" or "to the one who is kind," suggesting a plea for compassion and gentleness.
  • priyavratān - Repeated for emphasis, reinforcing the connection to the beloved and righteous followers.

Conclusion: This mantra is a heartfelt invocation to Agni, the fire god, who symbolizes purity, transformation, and the divine connection between the human and the celestial. It emphasizes the importance of wisdom and understanding in devotion, as well as the communal aspect of seeking blessings. In real life, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as during rituals, prayers, or moments of reflection where individuals seek guidance, protection, and blessings for themselves and their loved ones. It serves as a reminder of the importance of approaching the divine with sincerity, intellect, and a sense of community, fostering a deeper connection with the spiritual realm and promoting harmony in our lives.
