अहन्न अहिम परिशयानम् अर्ण ओजायमानं तुविजात तव्यān |न ते महित्वम् अनु भूद अध द्यौर यद अन्यया स्पिग्याः क्षाम अवस्थाः ||
ahann ahim pariśayānam arṇa ojāyamānaṁ tuvijāta tavyān | na te mahitvam anu bhūd adha dyaur yad anyayā sphigyā3 kṣām avasthāḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ahann: I am
    • ahim: not (or without)
    • pariśayānam: the gathering or collection
    • arṇa: of the ocean or vastness
    • ojāyamānaṁ: being energized or empowered
    • tuvijāta: you who are born or created
    • tavyān: to be done or accomplished
    • na te: not yours
    • mahitvam: greatness or magnificence
    • anu bhūd: following or in accordance with
    • adha: below or beneath
    • dyaur: the sky or heaven
    • yad: that which
    • anyayā: by any other means
    • sphigyā: to be manifested or revealed
    • kṣām: patience or endurance
    • avasthāḥ: states or conditions
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks to the essence of self-awareness and the rejection of negativity.
    • It emphasizes the importance of recognizing one's own power and potential, while also acknowledging the vastness of existence.
    • The phrase "I am not without the gathering of the ocean" suggests that one is part of a larger whole, interconnected with the universe.
    • The mantra encourages individuals to embrace their inherent strength and to act in ways that align with their true nature.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, this mantra can inspire individuals to recognize their contributions to a team, understanding that their efforts are part of a larger goal.
    • In personal development, it can serve as a reminder to cultivate self-awareness and to reject self-doubt, empowering individuals to pursue their passions.
    • In moments of adversity, the mantra encourages patience and resilience, reminding us that challenges are part of the journey and can lead to growth.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for self-empowerment and awareness of one's place in the universe.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal growth to teamwork, and serves as a reminder to embrace one's strengths while remaining connected to the larger whole.
    • By internalizing the message of this mantra, individuals can foster a sense of purpose and resilience, navigating life's challenges with confidence and clarity.
