त्वं ह्य अग्ने प्रथमो मनोतास्य धियो अभवो दसम होता |त्वं सīm वृषन्न अकृत्नोर दुष्टरीतुः सहो विश्वस्मै सहसे सहध्यै ||
tvaṁ hy agne prathamo manotāsyā dhiyo abhavo dasma hotā | tvaṁ sīṁ vṛṣann akṛṇor duṣṭarītu saho viśvasmai sahase sahadhyai ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • tvaṁ: You
    • hy: indeed
    • agne: O Agni (the fire god)
    • prathamo: the first
    • manotā: of thought or intention
    • asyā: of this
    • dhiyo: intellects or minds
    • abhavo: may you become
    • dasma: from this
    • hotā: the one who is invoked or worshipped
  • tvaṁ: You
  • sīṁ: the one who is strong
  • vṛṣann: the one who brings prosperity
  • akṛṇor: who does not create
  • duṣṭarītu: difficult paths or obstacles
  • saho: may you help
  • viśvasmai: to all beings
  • sahase: with strength
  • sahadhyai: with wisdom
  • Overall Meaning: This mantra is a prayer to Agni, the fire god, acknowledging his role as the first source of thought and intention. It seeks his blessings for intellect, strength, and the ability to overcome obstacles, while also asking for his guidance in helping all beings with wisdom and prosperity.

    • Real-Life Examples:
      • In a corporate setting, leaders often invoke the spirit of Agni when making decisions that require clarity and foresight. They seek to align their intentions with the greater good of the organization, much like the mantra's call for Agni to guide thoughts and actions.
      • In personal life, individuals may recite this mantra during challenging times, asking for strength and wisdom to navigate through difficulties, similar to how one might seek guidance from a mentor or a trusted friend.

    • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine guidance in thought, action, and overcoming challenges. It emphasizes the importance of intellect and strength in achieving prosperity and well-being. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as decision-making processes, personal challenges, and collective efforts for the betterment of society. By invoking Agni's blessings, individuals and communities can strive for clarity, strength, and wisdom in their endeavors.
