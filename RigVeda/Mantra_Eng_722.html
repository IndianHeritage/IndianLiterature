त्वं महां इन्द्र यो ह शुष्मैर dyāvā जजñāनः पृथिवी अमे धाः |यद धा ते विश्वा गिरयश चिद अभ्वा भियā दृढासः किरणा नजिन ||
tvam mahām̐ indra yo ha śuṣmair dyāvā jajñānaḥ pṛthivī ame dhāḥ | yad dha te viśvā girayaś cid abhvā bhiyā dṛḻhāsaḥ kiraṇā naijan ||
  • tvam - You
  • mahām̐ - Great
  • indra - Lord or King, often associated with strength and power
  • yo - Who
  • ha - Indeed
  • śuṣmair - With brilliance or radiance
  • dyāvā - The sky or heavens
  • jajñānaḥ - Born or created
  • pṛthivī - The earth
  • ame - Here or in this place
  • dhāḥ - Support or hold
  • yad - That which
  • dha - To hold or maintain
  • te - Your
  • viśvā - All or universal
  • girayaś - Songs or hymns
  • cid - Consciousness or awareness
  • abhvā - Without fear
  • bhiya - Fear
  • dṛḻhāsaḥ - Firm or steadfast
  • kiraṇā - Rays or beams
  • naijan - Not to be diminished

This mantra can be interpreted as a prayer or invocation to Indra, the great lord of the heavens, who is associated with strength, power, and the natural elements. It acknowledges the connection between the sky (dyāvā) and the earth (pṛthivī), emphasizing the unity of the cosmos. The mantra speaks of the brilliance and radiance of Indra, who supports and holds the universe, and it expresses a desire for fearlessness and steadfastness in the face of challenges.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios:

  • Personal Strength: Just as Indra represents strength and power, individuals can draw inspiration from this mantra to cultivate their inner strength when facing difficulties.
  • Unity with Nature: The connection between the sky and earth reminds us of our relationship with nature. It encourages us to respect and protect our environment.
  • Fearlessness: The invocation for fearlessness can be a guiding principle in overcoming personal fears, whether in professional settings, relationships, or personal growth.

Conclusion: This mantra signifies the importance of strength, unity, and fearlessness in our lives. By invoking the qualities of Indra, we can strive to embody these traits, fostering resilience and harmony within ourselves and with the world around us. It serves as a reminder that we are part of a larger cosmic order, encouraging us to live with purpose and courage.
