त्वं इन्द्र सजोषसम् अर्कम् बिभर्षि बाह्वोः |वज्रम् शिशान ओजसा ||
tvam indra sajoṣasam arkam bibharṣi bāhvoḥ | vajraṁ śiśāna ojasā ||
  • tvam - This word translates to "you" or "thou," indicating a direct address to a divine entity.
  • indra - Indra is the king of the gods in Vedic mythology, often associated with rain, thunderstorms, and strength. He represents power and authority.
  • sajoṣasam - This term can be broken down into "saj" (together) and "oṣasam" (with energy or vigor), suggesting a collective strength or energy.
  • arkam - This means "light" or "radiance," often associated with the sun or divine illumination, symbolizing knowledge and enlightenment.
  • bibharṣi - This translates to "you bear" or "you carry," indicating the act of holding or sustaining something significant.
  • bāhvoḥ - This means "of the arms," referring to strength and capability, often symbolizing protection and support.
  • vajraṁ - Vajra means "thunderbolt" or "diamond," symbolizing indestructibility and immense power.
  • śiśāna - This term means "to shine" or "to illuminate," indicating the presence of brilliance or clarity.
  • ojasā - This translates to "with energy" or "with strength," emphasizing vitality and dynamism.

The mantra can be interpreted as a call to Indra, acknowledging his strength and power, and asking for his support and energy. It emphasizes the importance of collective strength and the illumination that comes from divine guidance.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios:

  • Leadership: Just as Indra is a leader among the gods, individuals in positions of authority can draw inspiration from this mantra to lead with strength and wisdom.
  • Teamwork: The concept of collective energy can be applied in team settings, where collaboration and mutual support lead to greater achievements.
  • Personal Strength: Individuals can invoke the qualities of Indra to find inner strength during challenging times, reminding themselves of their own capabilities.

Conclusion: This mantra signifies the importance of divine strength and guidance in our lives. It encourages individuals to seek support from higher powers while also recognizing their own potential. In various aspects of life, whether in leadership, teamwork, or personal challenges, the essence of this mantra serves as a reminder of the strength that comes from unity and divine illumination.
