ते घेद अग्ने स्वाध्योऽहā विश्वा नृचक्षसः |तरन्तः स्याम दुर्गहा ||
te ghed agne svādhyo 'hā viśvā nṛcakṣasaḥ | tarantaḥ syāma durgahā ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • te: you (plural)
    • ghed: may you be invoked
    • agne: O Agni (the fire god)
    • svādhyo: who is worthy of worship
    • 'hā: indeed
    • viśvā: all
    • nṛcakṣasaḥ: of the human beings
    • tarantaḥ: may we cross
    • syāma: we may become
    • durgahā: difficult to cross
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Agni, the fire god, who is considered a mediator between humans and the divine.
    • It expresses a desire for divine assistance in overcoming challenges and obstacles in life.
    • The invocation of Agni signifies the importance of fire in rituals, symbolizing purity, transformation, and the energy that fuels life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a challenging situation, such as preparing for an important exam or a job interview, individuals often seek motivation and strength. This mantra can serve as a reminder to invoke inner strength and resilience.
    • During times of personal crisis, such as loss or failure, people may turn to spiritual practices or rituals that invoke divine support, similar to how this mantra seeks the blessings of Agni.
    • In community gatherings or rituals, invoking Agni can symbolize unity and collective strength, as people come together to overcome shared challenges.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine assistance in navigating life's difficulties.
    • It emphasizes the importance of faith and the belief that with divine support, one can overcome even the most daunting obstacles.
    • This mantra is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal struggles to communal challenges, reminding individuals and communities to seek strength and guidance from the divine.
