वि नो वाजाऽऋभुक्षणः पथश्चितन यष्टवे |अस्मभ्यं सूरयः स्तुताः विश्वा आशास तारिषाणि ||
vi no vājā ṛbhukṣaṇaḥ pathaś citana yaṣṭave | asmabhyaṁ sūrayaḥ stutā viśvā āśās tarīṣaṇi ||
  • vi no vājā ṛbhukṣaṇaḥ pathaś citana yaṣṭave
    • vi no - "for us" or "to us"
    • vājā - "the best of the best" or "the most excellent"
    • ṛbhukṣaṇaḥ - "the one who provides nourishment" or "the giver of sustenance"
    • pathaś - "on the path" or "in the journey"
    • citana - "to be awakened" or "to be enlightened"
    • yaṣṭave - "to the one who is worshipped" or "to the one who is revered"
  • asmabhyaṁ sūrayaḥ stutā
    • asmabhyaṁ - "to us" or "for us"
    • sūrayaḥ - "the powerful ones" or "the strong beings"
    • stutā - "are praised" or "are worshipped"
  • viśvā āśās tarīṣaṇi
    • viśvā - "all" or "universal"
    • āśās - "hopes" or "aspirations"
    • tarīṣaṇi - "to cross over" or "to transcend"

This mantra from the Rigveda emphasizes the importance of seeking nourishment and enlightenment on our life’s journey. It calls upon the powerful beings to bless us with their strength and praises them for their support. The mantra suggests that by aligning ourselves with these divine forces, we can transcend our limitations and achieve our aspirations.


In real life, this can be seen in various contexts:

  • Personal Growth: Just as the mantra seeks enlightenment, individuals often pursue education and self-improvement to awaken their potential.
  • Community Support: The call for powerful beings can be likened to seeking support from mentors or community leaders who guide us on our paths.
  • Spiritual Practices: Many people engage in rituals or prayers to connect with higher powers, seeking strength and guidance in their endeavors.

Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its universal message of seeking nourishment, strength, and enlightenment. It encourages individuals to recognize the support available to them and to strive for their aspirations with the help of divine forces. In everyday life, this can manifest through education, community engagement, and spiritual practices, ultimately leading to personal and collective growth.
