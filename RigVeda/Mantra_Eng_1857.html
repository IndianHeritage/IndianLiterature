शेषन् नु त इन्द्र सस्मिन् योनौ प्रशस्तये पवीरवस्य मह्ना |सृजद अर्णांस्य अव यद युधा गास तिष्ठद धरी धृतता मृष्टा वाजान ||
śeṣan nu ta indra sasmin yonau praśastaye pavīravasya mahnā | sṛjad arṇāṁsy ava yad yudhā gās tiṣṭhad dharī dhṛṣatā mṛṣṭa vājān ||
  • śeṣan nu ta indra: This phrase can be interpreted as "O Indra, the one who is supreme." Indra is often regarded as the king of the gods in Vedic literature, symbolizing strength and power.
  • sasmin yonau praśastaye: This translates to "in this great source of energy." It refers to the cosmic energy or the source from which all creation emerges, emphasizing the importance of harnessing this energy for positive outcomes.
  • pavīravasya mahnā: This means "of the great and powerful." It highlights the significance of strength and valor, suggesting that one should aspire to be powerful in their endeavors.
  • sṛjad arṇāṁsy: This phrase translates to "create the waters." It symbolizes the act of creation and the importance of nurturing life, akin to how water is essential for survival.
  • ava yad yudhā gās: This means "when the warriors stand ready." It signifies preparedness and the importance of being ready to face challenges, much like soldiers preparing for battle.
  • tiṣṭhad dharī dhṛṣatā: This translates to "standing firm with courage." It emphasizes the need for resilience and bravery in the face of adversity.
  • mṛṣṭa vājān: This means "the well-prepared steeds." It symbolizes the importance of being well-equipped and ready for action, akin to having the right tools and resources in any endeavor.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda encapsulates themes of strength, creation, and preparedness. It serves as a reminder of the importance of harnessing cosmic energy and being courageous in the face of challenges. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios such as leadership, where one must be strong and prepared to guide others, or in personal endeavors where resilience and readiness are key to success. The mantra encourages individuals to cultivate their inner strength and to be proactive in creating positive outcomes in their lives.
