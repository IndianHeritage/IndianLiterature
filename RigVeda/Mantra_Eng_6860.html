अग्निं धीभिर मनीषिणो मेधिरासो विपश्चितः |अद्मसद्याय हिन्विरे ||
agniṁ dhībhir manīṣiṇo medhirāso vipaścitaḥ | admasadyāya hinvire ||
  • Agniṁ: This word refers to "Agni," the fire god in Vedic literature, symbolizing energy, transformation, and purification.
  • Dhībhir: This translates to "with intellect" or "with wisdom," indicating the importance of mental clarity and understanding.
  • Manīṣiṇo: Meaning "those who think deeply" or "wise ones," it emphasizes the value of thoughtful contemplation and insight.
  • Medhirāso: This word means "with intelligence" or "with understanding," reinforcing the idea of using one's intellect to navigate life.
  • Vipaścitaḥ: This translates to "the wise" or "the knowledgeable," highlighting the importance of wisdom in decision-making.
  • Admasadyāya: This means "to the house" or "to the dwelling," suggesting a focus on the home or community as a place of gathering and support.
  • Hinvi: This word means "to bring" or "to lead," indicating an action of guiding or directing.

The mantra can be interpreted as a call to invoke the divine energy of Agni, urging wise individuals to use their intellect and understanding to lead and support their community or household. It emphasizes the importance of wisdom and thoughtful action in creating a harmonious environment.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as:

  • Leadership: Leaders in any field can embody the qualities of Agni by using their wisdom to guide their teams effectively.
  • Education: Teachers can inspire students to think deeply and use their intellect to solve problems, fostering a culture of learning.
  • Community Building: Individuals can come together to support one another, using their collective wisdom to create a nurturing environment.

Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for wisdom and thoughtful action in our lives. By invoking the qualities of Agni, we can transform our surroundings and relationships, leading to a more harmonious and supportive community. It serves as a reminder that intellect and understanding are essential in guiding our actions and decisions, ultimately contributing to the well-being of ourselves and those around us.
