गन्थिं न वि ष्य ग्रथितं पुनान ऋजुं च गातुं वृजिनं च सोम |अत्यो न क्रदो हरिर आसृजनो मर्यो देव धन्व पस्त्यावान ||
granthiṁ na vi ṣya grathitam punāna ṛjuṁ ca gātuṁ vṛjinaṁ ca soma | atyo na krado harir ā sṛjāno maryo deva dhanva pastyāvān ||
  • Granthiṁ na viṣya grathitam: This phrase can be interpreted as "do not let the knots bind." In life, we often encounter situations that create mental or emotional knots, such as stress, anxiety, or unresolved conflicts. For example, a person may feel overwhelmed by work pressures, leading to a mental block. The mantra encourages individuals to seek clarity and release these bindings.
  • Punāna ṛjuṁ ca gātuṁ: This translates to "to purify and to walk straight." It emphasizes the importance of maintaining integrity and righteousness in one's actions. In real life, this can be seen in the context of ethical decision-making, where a person chooses to act honestly even when faced with temptation. For instance, a business leader who prioritizes ethical practices over profit maximization embodies this principle.
  • Vṛjinaṁ ca soma: Here, "vṛjinaṁ" refers to obstacles or impurities, while "soma" symbolizes nourishment and vitality. This part of the mantra suggests overcoming challenges to attain a state of well-being. A practical example could be someone facing health issues who decides to adopt a healthier lifestyle, thus overcoming their physical and mental barriers.
  • Atyo na krado harir ā sṛjāno: This can be interpreted as "beyond the limits, the divine creates." It speaks to the idea of transcending limitations and embracing the divine potential within us. In everyday life, this could relate to personal growth, where individuals push beyond their perceived limits, such as an athlete training hard to break their own records.
  • Maryo deva dhanva pastyāvān: This phrase can be understood as "the divine warrior sees the bow." It symbolizes the readiness to face challenges with courage and strength. In real life, this can be exemplified by someone who stands up for justice in their community, embodying the warrior spirit in the face of adversity.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a profound message about overcoming obstacles, maintaining integrity, and embracing one's divine potential. Its significance lies in its applicability to various aspects of life, from personal struggles to ethical dilemmas. By internalizing these teachings, individuals can navigate their lives with greater clarity, purpose, and strength, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling existence.
