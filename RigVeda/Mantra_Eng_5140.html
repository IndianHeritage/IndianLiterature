विष्वा अग्नि 'प दहारेतीर येभिस तपोभिर अधो जरूथम | प्र निस्वरं चातयस्वामिवाम् ||
viśvā agne 'pa dahārātīr yebhis tapobhir adaho jarūtham | pra nisvaraṁ cātayasvāmīvām ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • viśvā: all, universal
    • agne: O Agni (the fire god)
    • 'pa: protect
    • dahārātīr: from the burning, or from the heat
    • yebhis: with which
    • tapobhir: through austerities or penances
    • adaho: from below, or from the earth
    • jarūtham: the one who is old or ancient
    • pra: forth, or forward
    • nisvaraṁ: without a master, or independent
    • cātayasvāmīvām: may you be invoked or called upon
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Agni, the fire god, who represents energy, transformation, and purification.
    • The invocation seeks protection from the universal fire, which can symbolize both the physical fire and the metaphorical fire of challenges and adversities in life.
    • The mention of "austerities" suggests that through disciplined practices, one can attain a higher state of being and overcome difficulties.
    • The phrase "without a master" indicates a state of independence and self-sufficiency, encouraging individuals to seek their own path and wisdom.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, when facing challenges such as stress or anxiety, one might engage in meditation or yoga (austerities) to find inner peace and clarity.
    • In a professional setting, the mantra can inspire individuals to take charge of their careers, seeking knowledge and skills independently rather than relying solely on external guidance.
    • During difficult times, such as loss or failure, invoking the spirit of Agni can serve as a reminder to embrace transformation and rise from the ashes, much like a phoenix.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of inner strength, self-reliance, and the transformative power of fire, both literally and metaphorically.
    • Its significance lies in the encouragement to face life's challenges with courage and to seek personal growth through disciplined practices.
    • In real-life scenarios, it serves as a reminder that we have the power to protect ourselves from adversities and to invoke our inner strength to navigate through life's complexities.
