यद ध क्राणā इरध्यै दक्षं सचन्त ऊतयः |सध्रीचीनā नियुतो दावने धिय उप ब्रुवत īं धियः ||
yad dha krāṇā iradhyai dakṣaṁ sacanta ūtayaḥ | sadhrīcīnā niyuto dāvane dhiya upa bruvata īṁ dhiyaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yad: that which
    • dha: to hold or to support
    • krāṇā: actions or deeds
    • iradhyai: with skill or capability
    • dakṣaṁ: dexterous or competent
    • sacanta: those who are united or gathered
    • ūtayaḥ: those who are elevated or uplifted
    • sadhrīcīnā: those who are in harmony or agreement
    • niyuto: those who are directed or guided
    • dāvane: in the act of burning or purifying
    • dhiya: intellect or understanding
    • upa: towards or near
    • bruvata: those who speak or express
    • īṁ: a particle indicating a request or invocation
    • dhiyaḥ: of the intellect or understanding
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of collective action and the power of skilled individuals working together.
    • It suggests that when people unite with a common purpose and utilize their skills, they can achieve great things.
    • The reference to "dāvane" indicates a process of purification, suggesting that through collaboration and shared understanding, one can elevate their intellect and actions.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace, when team members collaborate effectively, leveraging each other's strengths, they can complete projects more efficiently and creatively.
    • In community service, individuals coming together for a common cause, such as environmental clean-up, can create a significant impact on their surroundings.
    • In education, group studies allow students to share knowledge and insights, enhancing their understanding of complex subjects.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the power of unity and collaboration in achieving common goals.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from professional environments to community initiatives, highlighting that collective efforts can lead to greater outcomes.
    • By fostering a spirit of cooperation and utilizing individual skills, we can uplift not only ourselves but also those around us, leading to a more harmonious and productive society.
