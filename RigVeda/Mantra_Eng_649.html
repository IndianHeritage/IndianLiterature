तुभ्येद एते बहुला अद्रिदुग्धाश चामूषदश कमसā इन्द्रपानाः |व्य अशनुहि तर्पया कामम् एषाम अथ मनो वसुदेया कृष्व ||
tubhyed ete bahulā adridugdhāś camūṣadaś camasā indrapānāḥ | vy aśnuhi tarpayā kāmam eṣām athā mano vasudeyāya kṛṣva ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • tubhyed: to you
    • ete: these
    • bahulā: abundant
    • adridugdhāś: rich in milk
    • camūṣadaś: those who are like a herd
    • camasā: with cups
    • indrapānāḥ: those who drink the nectar of Indra
    • vy: indeed
    • aśnuhi: fulfill
    • tarpayā: by satisfying
    • kāmam: desires
    • eṣām: of these
    • athā: then
    • mano: mind
    • vasudeyāya: for the wealth of the earth
    • kṛṣva: make it happen
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer directed towards divine entities, asking for abundance and fulfillment of desires.
    • The phrase "tubhyed ete bahulā" indicates a request for blessings from the divine, emphasizing the abundance that is being sought.
    • "Adridugdhāś camūṣadaś" refers to the richness and nourishment that comes from nature, akin to how a herd provides sustenance.
    • The mention of "indrapānāḥ" symbolizes the divine nectar, representing spiritual fulfillment and enlightenment.
    • By saying "vy aśnuhi tarpayā kāmam eṣām," the mantra expresses a desire for the divine to fulfill the needs and desires of the devotees.
    • Finally, "athā mano vasudeyāya kṛṣva" calls for the manifestation of wealth and prosperity in the material world, linking spiritual and material abundance.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In agricultural communities, this mantra can be recited during harvest festivals to invoke blessings for a bountiful crop yield.
    • In personal life, individuals may chant this mantra during times of need, seeking fulfillment of their aspirations and desires.
    • Businesses may use this mantra in rituals to attract prosperity and success, aligning their goals with spiritual intentions.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the interconnectedness of spiritual and material wealth, highlighting the importance of divine blessings in achieving abundance.
    • It serves as a reminder that while we seek material success, it is essential to align our desires with higher spiritual values.
    • In real life, this mantra can be applicable in various scenarios, from personal aspirations to community rituals, reinforcing the belief that fulfillment comes from both divine grace and our own efforts.
