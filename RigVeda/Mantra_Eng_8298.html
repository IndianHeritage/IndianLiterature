अलाय्यस्य परशुर ननाश तं आपवस्व देव सोम |आखुं चिदेव देव सोम ||
alāyyasya paraśur nanāśa tam ā pavasva deva soma | ākhuṁ cid eva deva soma ||
  • Mantra: "alāyyasya paraśur nanāśa tam ā pavasva deva soma | ākhuṁ cid eva deva soma ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • alāyyasya: of the one who is in need or in a state of distress
    • paraśur: axe or weapon
    • nanāśa: to remove or to destroy
    • tam: that
    • ā: come
    • pavasva: purify or cleanse
    • deva: divine or god
    • soma: the sacred drink or essence
    • ākhuṁ: to be present or to exist
    • cid: consciousness or awareness
    • eva: indeed
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the divine essence of Soma, asking for purification and the removal of obstacles.
    • The reference to "the one who is in need" suggests a plea for help in times of distress, akin to seeking support during challenging life situations.
    • The "axe" symbolizes the cutting away of negativity or difficulties, much like how one might need to make tough decisions to overcome personal challenges.
    • The call for purification indicates a desire for clarity and renewal, similar to how individuals often seek mental and emotional cleansing through meditation or therapy.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, when facing a crisis, leaders may invoke this mantra metaphorically by seeking to "cut away" inefficiencies and "purify" the work environment to foster better productivity.
    • In personal life, someone going through a tough breakup might reflect on this mantra as a way to seek emotional healing and clarity, aiming to remove the pain and negativity associated with the past relationship.
    • During community gatherings, this mantra can be a reminder for collective efforts to overcome societal issues, encouraging members to work together to "cut" through challenges and "purify" their intentions for a better future.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking divine assistance in overcoming obstacles and achieving purification in various aspects of life.
    • Its significance lies in the universal need for support during difficult times, highlighting the human experience of seeking clarity and renewal.
    • In real-life scenarios, whether in personal struggles, professional challenges, or community issues, the essence of this mantra serves as a guiding principle for resilience and transformation.
