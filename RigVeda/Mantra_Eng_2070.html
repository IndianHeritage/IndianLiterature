अन्तर ह्यग्न इयसे विद्वाँ जनमोभय कवे |दूतो जन्येव मित्र्यः ||
antar hy agna īyase vidvāñ janmobhayā kave | dūto janyeva mitryaḥ ||
  • antar hy agna īyase: This phrase can be translated as "within, indeed, you (Agni) move." Here, Agni refers to the sacred fire, which symbolizes energy, transformation, and the divine presence in the world. In real life, this can be likened to the inner drive or motivation that propels individuals to pursue their goals and aspirations.
  • vidvāñ janmobhayā kave: This translates to "the wise one, born of both (parents), is a poet." This highlights the importance of wisdom and creativity, suggesting that true understanding comes from a blend of experiences and knowledge. In contemporary life, this can be seen in individuals who draw from diverse backgrounds and experiences to create art, literature, or innovative solutions.
  • dūto janyeva mitryaḥ: This means "like a messenger, born of friendship." It emphasizes the role of communication and relationships in our lives. Just as a messenger conveys important information, individuals often act as conduits of knowledge and support within their communities. In real life, this can be observed in how friends and colleagues share insights and help each other grow.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of inner motivation, the value of wisdom derived from diverse experiences, and the significance of communication and relationships. In real life, these principles can guide individuals in their personal and professional journeys, encouraging them to seek knowledge, foster connections, and pursue their passions with determination. By embodying these qualities, one can contribute positively to society and inspire others along the way.
