देवानामिदवो महत् तदावृणीमहे वयं |वृष्णामस्मभ्यमूतये ||
devānām id avo mahat tad ā vṛṇīmahe vayam | vṛṣṇām asmabhyam ūtaye ||
  • devānām: This word translates to "of the gods" or "divine beings." It signifies the higher powers or deities that govern the universe.
  • id: This means "here" or "now," indicating the present moment and the immediacy of the invocation.
  • avo: This can be interpreted as "we call upon" or "we invoke," suggesting a plea or request directed towards the divine.
  • mahat: This word means "great" or "mighty," referring to the vastness and power of the divine forces.
  • tad: This translates to "that," pointing towards the greatness of the divine that is being invoked.
  • ā: This means "to" or "towards," indicating directionality in the invocation.
  • vṛṇīmahe: This translates to "we choose" or "we select," expressing the act of making a conscious choice or decision.
  • vayam: This means "we," indicating the collective nature of the invocation, emphasizing community and unity.
  • vṛṣṇām: This refers to "of the Vṛṣṇi," a clan associated with strength and valor, often linked to Lord Krishna.
  • asmabhyam: This translates to "for us," indicating a personal or communal request for blessings or support.
  • ūtaye: This means "for prosperity" or "for nourishment," suggesting a desire for abundance and well-being.

The mantra can be understood as a collective invocation to the divine beings, expressing a desire for their blessings and support in achieving greatness and prosperity. It emphasizes the importance of community and the shared aspiration for well-being.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as:

  • Community Gatherings: When people come together for a common purpose, such as festivals or ceremonies, invoking the divine for blessings can enhance the sense of unity and shared goals.
  • Personal Aspirations: Individuals can recite this mantra when seeking guidance or support in their personal endeavors, whether in career, education, or relationships.
  • Teamwork: In professional settings, teams can use this mantra to foster collaboration and invoke a spirit of cooperation and success.

In conclusion, this mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the interconnectedness of individuals and the divine. It highlights the significance of seeking support from higher powers while emphasizing the importance of community and shared aspirations in achieving prosperity and greatness.
