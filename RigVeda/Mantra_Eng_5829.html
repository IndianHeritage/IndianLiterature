आयं सु तुभ्यं वरुण स्वधावो हृदि स्तोमा उपशृतश्चिद अस्तु |शं नः क्षेमे शम् उ योगे नो अस्तु यूयम् पात स्वस्तिभिः सदा नः ||
ayaṁ su tubhyaṁ varuṇa svadhāvo hṛdi stoma upaśritaś cid astu | śaṁ naḥ kṣeme śam u yoge no astu yūyam pāta svastibhiḥ sadā naḥ ||
  • ayaṁ su tubhyaṁ varuṇa: This phrase can be translated as "This is good for you, O Varuna." Varuna is the Vedic deity associated with water, cosmic order, and moral authority. In real life, this can be seen as an appeal to higher powers or principles for guidance and support in our actions.
  • svadhāvo hṛdi: This means "may it dwell in the heart." It emphasizes the importance of internalizing values and principles. For example, in daily life, one might strive to embody honesty and integrity in their heart, influencing their decisions and interactions with others.
  • stoma upaśritaś cid astu: This translates to "may the praise be established." It suggests the significance of acknowledging and celebrating virtues and achievements. In a practical sense, this can be applied in workplaces where recognizing team efforts fosters a positive environment and motivates individuals.
  • śaṁ naḥ kṣeme: This means "may peace be with us." It reflects a universal desire for peace and well-being. In real life, this can be seen in community efforts to promote harmony, such as conflict resolution programs or peace initiatives that aim to bring people together.
  • śam u yoge no astu: This translates to "may there be no disturbance in our union." It highlights the importance of unity and cooperation. In practical terms, this can be applied in family settings, where maintaining harmony and understanding among members is crucial for a healthy relationship.
  • yūyam pāta svastibhiḥ sadā naḥ: This means "may you protect us always with auspiciousness." It is a call for divine protection and blessings. In everyday life, this can be reflected in rituals or practices that seek blessings for safety and prosperity, such as family gatherings or community festivals.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a deep yearning for guidance, peace, unity, and protection from higher powers. Its significance lies in its ability to inspire individuals and communities to strive for moral integrity, celebrate achievements, and foster harmonious relationships. In real-life scenarios, the principles embedded in this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as personal development, community building, and workplace dynamics, ultimately leading to a more balanced and fulfilling life.
