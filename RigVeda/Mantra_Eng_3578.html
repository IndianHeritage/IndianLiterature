कुवित स देविः सनयो नवो वायमो बभूयाद उषसो वो अद्य |येना नवग्वे अङ्गिरे दशग्वे सप्ताश्ये रेवती रेवद ऊषा ||
kuvit sa devīḥ sanayo navo vā yāmo babhūyād uṣaso vo adya | yenā navagve aṅgire daśagve saptāsye revatī revad ūṣa ||
  • kuvit sa devīḥ sanayo navo vā yāmo babhūyād uṣaso vo adya
    • kuvit: This term can be interpreted as "may it be well" or "may it be auspicious." It sets a positive tone for the mantra.
    • sa devīḥ: Refers to "the goddesses" or "divine beings," indicating a connection to the divine feminine energy.
    • sanayo: This word means "together" or "in harmony," suggesting unity among the divine forces.
    • nava: Translates to "new" or "fresh," symbolizing renewal and new beginnings.
    • vā: Means "or," indicating a choice or alternative.
    • yāmo: Refers to "the time" or "the season," emphasizing the cyclical nature of time.
    • babhūyād: This means "may it become" or "may it manifest," expressing a desire for transformation.
    • uṣaso: Refers to "the dawn," symbolizing enlightenment, hope, and the arrival of new opportunities.
    • vo: Means "to you," indicating a direct address to the divine or the audience.
    • adya: Translates to "today," emphasizing the immediacy of the request or intention.
  • yenā navagve aṅgire daśagve saptāsye revatī revad ūṣa
    • yenā: Means "by whom," indicating a source or cause.
    • navagve: Refers to "the new," reinforcing the theme of renewal.
    • aṅgire: This term can refer to "the Angiras," a group of sages known for their wisdom and knowledge.
    • daśagve: Refers to "the ten," possibly indicating completeness or a full cycle.
    • saptāsye: Means "the seven," which often symbolizes spiritual awakening and the chakras in various traditions.
    • revatī: Refers to "the prosperous" or "the wealthy," indicating abundance.
    • revad: Means "to be prosperous," reinforcing the theme of abundance and success.
    • ūṣa: Refers again to "the dawn," symbolizing new beginnings and enlightenment.

Conclusion:

The mantra speaks to the themes of renewal, unity, and the divine feminine, invoking the blessings of the goddesses for prosperity and enlightenment. It emphasizes the importance of new beginnings and the cyclical nature of time, encouraging individuals to embrace change and seek harmony in their lives.

In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied during significant life transitions, such as starting a new job, moving to a new place, or embarking on a new project. It serves as a reminder to seek divine guidance and support, fostering a sense of hope and positivity as one navigates through life's challenges.

Overall, the mantra encourages individuals to connect with their inner strength and the divine, promoting a harmonious and prosperous existence.
