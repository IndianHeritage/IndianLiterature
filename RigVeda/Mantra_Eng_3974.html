उद īरय कवीतमं कवीनाम् अनत्तैनम् अभि मध्वा घृतेन |स नो वसूनि प्रयताः हितानि चन्द्राणि देवः सविता सुवाति ||
ud īraya kavitamaṁ kavīnām unattainam abhi madhvā ghṛtena | sa no vasūni prayatā hitāni candrāṇi devaḥ savitā suvāti ||
  • ud īraya: This phrase can be translated as "rise up" or "elevate." It signifies the call to elevate one's consciousness or awareness.
  • kavitamaṁ: This word means "the best of poets" or "the most creative." It refers to the divine inspiration that guides creativity and expression.
  • kavīnām: This translates to "of the poets" or "of the wise." It emphasizes the collective wisdom and creativity of those who seek knowledge and truth.
  • unattainam: Meaning "unattainable," this word suggests the pursuit of higher ideals or truths that may seem beyond reach.
  • abhi madhvā ghṛtena: This phrase can be interpreted as "with sweet (madhvā) ghee (ghṛtena)." Ghee is often associated with purity and nourishment, symbolizing the essence of life and creativity.
  • sa no vasūni: This means "may he (the divine) grant us wealth." It is a prayer for abundance and prosperity in various forms.
  • prayatā hitāni: This translates to "with effort, may they be beneficial." It emphasizes the importance of effort and intention in achieving positive outcomes.
  • candrāṇi: This word means "the moon" or "lunar." The moon is often associated with calmness, reflection, and the cycles of life.
  • devaḥ savitā: This refers to "the divine Savitar," a solar deity representing light, energy, and creation.
  • suvāti: This means "may he (the divine) bless us." It is a call for divine blessings and guidance in our endeavors.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda encapsulates a profound aspiration for creativity, wisdom, and divine blessings. It encourages individuals to elevate their consciousness and seek inspiration from the divine, while also recognizing the importance of effort and intention in achieving prosperity and beneficial outcomes. In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as in creative pursuits, personal development, and spiritual practices. By invoking these ideals, individuals can strive for higher goals, foster creativity, and seek the blessings of the divine in their daily lives.
