त्वां अग्नि पितरम् इष्टिभिर् नरस् त्वां भ्रातृआय शम्य तनूरुचम् |त्वं पुत्रो भवसि यस् ते 'विदत त्वं सखा सुशेवः पाश्य आधृषः ||
tvām agne pitaram iṣṭibhir naras tvām bhrātrāya śamyā tanūrucam | tvam putro bhavasi yas te 'vidhat tvaṁ sakhā suśevaḥ pāsy ādhṛṣaḥ ||
  • tvām agne - "O Agni," refers to the divine fire, symbolizing purity, energy, and the presence of the divine in our lives.
  • pitaram - "father," indicating a paternal figure or a source of guidance and support.
  • iṣṭibhir - "with offerings," suggesting the importance of rituals and offerings in establishing a connection with the divine.
  • naraḥ - "humans," emphasizing the relationship between the divine and humanity.
  • bhrātrāya - "to the brother," highlighting the significance of familial bonds and relationships.
  • śamyā - "with harmony," indicating the need for peace and balance in relationships.
  • tanūrucam - "with a pleasing body," referring to the physical form and the importance of health and vitality.
  • tvam putro - "you become a son," suggesting the idea of becoming a part of a family or community.
  • bhavasi - "you are," indicating existence and presence.
  • yas te 'vidhat - "who has not been created by you," pointing to the idea of individuality and uniqueness.
  • tvaṁ sakhā - "you are a friend," emphasizing the importance of friendship and companionship.
  • suśevaḥ - "well-wisher," indicating the role of supportive relationships in our lives.
  • pāsy - "to be seen," suggesting visibility and recognition in relationships.
  • ādhṛṣaḥ - "the one who is strong," referring to strength and resilience in character.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda emphasizes the interconnectedness of divine presence, familial bonds, and the importance of relationships in our lives. It highlights how we can seek guidance from the divine (Agni) while nurturing our relationships with family and friends. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied to foster harmony in family dynamics, strengthen friendships, and encourage individuals to seek divine support in their endeavors. It serves as a reminder of the significance of community, support, and the divine in our daily lives, urging us to cultivate positive relationships and seek balance and strength in our interactions.
