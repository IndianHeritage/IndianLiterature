ममच चन ते मगवन व्यंसो निविविध्वाम् अप हनू जगहान् |अधā निविद्ध उत्तरो बभूवाञ् चिरो दासस्य सम पिणग वधेन ||
mamac cana te maghavan vyaṁso nivividhvām̐ apa hanū jaghāna | adhā nividdha uttaro babhūvāñ chiro dāsasya sam piṇag vadhena ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • mamac: my
    • cana: or
    • te: your
    • maghavan: O bountiful one (referring to a deity)
    • vyaṁso: of wealth or prosperity
    • nivividhvām̐: may we be free from obstacles
    • apa: away from
    • hanū: harm or destruction
    • jaghāna: may it be removed
    • adhā: below
    • nividdha: bound or tied
    • uttaro: higher or elevated
    • babhūvāñ: may it become
    • chiro: long-lasting
    • dāsasya: of the servant or devotee
    • sam: together
    • piṇag: with strength
    • vadhena: by the means of sacrifice or offering
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer for protection and prosperity. The speaker is addressing a divine entity, asking for the removal of obstacles and harm.
    • The phrase "O bountiful one" signifies a plea to a deity who is known for generosity and abundance.
    • The request to be free from harm and obstacles reflects a common human desire for safety and success in life.
    • The mention of being bound below and aspiring to be elevated indicates a journey from a lower state to a higher, more prosperous one.
    • The reference to a servant or devotee emphasizes humility and the importance of devotion in seeking divine assistance.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be recited by entrepreneurs seeking success and protection from failures. For instance, a startup founder may chant this mantra before a crucial pitch to investors, hoping to attract prosperity and ward off obstacles.
    • In personal life, individuals facing challenges such as health issues or relationship problems may find solace in this mantra, using it as a tool for meditation and focus on overcoming difficulties.
    • During community gatherings or religious ceremonies, this mantra can be invoked to seek collective well-being and harmony, reinforcing the idea of unity and shared aspirations.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking divine intervention for prosperity and protection. It highlights the importance of faith, humility, and the desire for elevation in life.
    • Its significance lies in its universal applicability, reminding individuals that through devotion and the right intentions, they can overcome obstacles and achieve their goals.
    • In a world filled with challenges, this mantra serves as a beacon of hope, encouraging individuals to remain steadfast in their pursuits while seeking the blessings of the divine.
