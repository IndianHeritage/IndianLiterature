न मा इन्द्रेण सख्यं वियोषद अस्मभ्यम अस्य दक्षिणा दूहिता |उप ज्येष्ठे वरूथे गभस्तौ प्राय-प्राय जिगीवांसाḥ स्याम ||
na ma indreṇa sakhyaṁ vi yoṣad asmabhyam asya dakṣiṇā duhīta | upa jyeṣṭhe varūthe gabhastau prāye-prāye jigīvāṁsaḥ syāma ||
  • na ma indreṇa sakhyaṁ: This phrase translates to "May we not be deprived of friendship with Indra." Indra is the king of the gods in Vedic literature, often associated with strength, rain, and thunder. In real life, this can be interpreted as a plea for maintaining strong and supportive relationships, akin to the importance of teamwork in a corporate environment where collaboration leads to success.
  • vi yoṣad asmabhyam asya dakṣiṇā duhīta: This part means "May the daughter of the earth (or the goddess of wealth) grant us her blessings." Here, "yoṣad" refers to a nurturing figure, possibly symbolizing prosperity and abundance. In practical terms, this can relate to seeking opportunities for growth and success in one's career or personal life, much like how businesses seek favorable conditions for expansion.
  • upa jyeṣṭhe varūthe: This translates to "In the presence of the elder or the most revered." This emphasizes the importance of respecting elders and seeking their guidance. In modern society, this can be seen in mentorship programs where experienced individuals guide younger generations, fostering knowledge transfer and personal development.
  • gabhastau prāye-prāye: This means "May we attain the light or brilliance." Light symbolizes knowledge, wisdom, and enlightenment. In everyday life, this can be related to the pursuit of education and self-improvement, where individuals strive to gain insights and understanding to navigate life's challenges effectively.
  • jigīvāṁsaḥ syāma: This translates to "May we live long." It is a wish for longevity and vitality. In a real-world context, this can be connected to the importance of health and well-being, where individuals engage in healthy lifestyles and practices to ensure a long and fulfilling life.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a holistic approach to life, emphasizing the significance of relationships, prosperity, respect for elders, the pursuit of knowledge, and the desire for longevity. In real-life scenarios, these themes are applicable in various contexts, such as personal development, professional growth, and community building. By embodying these principles, individuals can create a balanced and fulfilling life, fostering connections and striving for continuous improvement.
