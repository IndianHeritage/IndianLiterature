समिद्धो अग्निर दिवि शोचिर अश्रेत प्रत्यङ्ग उषसम उर्वियā वि भाति |एति प्राची विश्ववारा नमोभिर देवाम् ईळाना हविषा घृताची ||
samiddho agnir divi śocir aśret pratyaṅṅ uṣasam urviyā vi bhāti | eti prācī viśvavārā namobhir devām̐ īḻānā haviṣā ghṛtācī ||
  • Mantra: "samiddho agnir divi śocir aśret pratyaṅṅ uṣasam urviyā vi bhāti | eti prācī viśvavārā namobhir devām̐ īḻānā haviṣā ghṛtācī ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • samiddho: kindled or ignited
    • agnir: fire
    • divi: in the heavens
    • śocir: shining or illuminating
    • aśret: has settled or established
    • pratyaṅṅ: towards the east
    • uṣasam: dawn or morning
    • urviyā: widely or broadly
    • vi bhāti: shines or radiates
    • eti: comes or approaches
    • prācī: from the east
    • viśvavārā: all-encompassing or universal
    • namobhir: with reverence or salutations
    • devām̐: to the gods
    • īḻānā: with offerings or oblations
    • haviṣā: with sacred offerings
    • ghṛtācī: with clarified butter or ghee
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra invokes the fire (Agni) as a divine entity that is kindled and shines brightly in the heavens.
    • The imagery of dawn (uṣasam) signifies new beginnings, hope, and the dispelling of darkness.
    • The phrase "urviyā vi bhāti" suggests that this divine light radiates widely, illuminating all around.
    • By addressing the gods with reverence and offering ghee, the mantra emphasizes the importance of rituals and offerings in connecting with the divine.
    • In real life, this can be likened to the way we seek enlightenment and guidance in our daily lives, often through rituals, prayers, or meditative practices.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its representation of the eternal cycle of life, light, and the divine connection we seek.
    • It teaches us the importance of starting each day with gratitude and reverence, acknowledging the light that guides us.
    • In practical terms, this mantra can be applied in daily rituals, morning prayers, or any moment when one seeks clarity and guidance.
    • Ultimately, it serves as a reminder of the divine presence in our lives and the importance of nurturing that connection through our actions and intentions.
