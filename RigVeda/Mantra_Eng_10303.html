तव त्य इन्द्र सख्येषु वह्नय ऋतम मन्वानां व्य अदर्दिरुर वलम् |यत्रा दशस्यन्न उषसो रिणन्न अपः कुत्साय मन्मन्न अह्यश च दंसयः ||
tava tya indra sakhyeṣu vahnaya ṛtam manvānā vy adardirur valam | yatrā daśasyann uṣaso riṇann apaḥ kutsāya manmann ahyaś ca daṁsayaḥ ||
  • tava tya indra sakhyeṣu: This phrase can be translated as "Your friendship, O Indra." Here, Indra is addressed as a friend, highlighting the importance of camaraderie and support in relationships.
  • vahnaya ṛtam manvānā: This means "the truth that is pursued by the fire." Fire symbolizes transformation and purification, suggesting that truth is something that requires effort and dedication to attain.
  • vy adardirur valam: This translates to "they have not been defeated." It emphasizes resilience and strength in the face of challenges, indicating that true friends and allies stand strong together.
  • yatrā daśasyann uṣaso riṇann: This can be interpreted as "where the ten (or many) are nourished by the dawn." It signifies the importance of new beginnings and nourishment, both physical and spiritual, that comes with the dawn of a new day.
  • apaḥ kutsāya manmann: This means "the waters are directed towards the good." Water is often associated with life and purity, suggesting that positive intentions and actions lead to beneficial outcomes.
  • ahyaś ca daṁsayaḥ: This translates to "and the day brings forth." It indicates the arrival of new opportunities and the potential for growth and success as the day unfolds.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda emphasizes the significance of friendship, truth, resilience, nourishment, positive intentions, and the promise of new beginnings. In real life, these themes can be applied in various scenarios, such as fostering strong relationships with friends and family, pursuing truth in our endeavors, and remaining resilient in the face of adversity. The mantra serves as a reminder that with the support of friends and a commitment to truth, we can navigate life's challenges and embrace new opportunities for growth and success.
