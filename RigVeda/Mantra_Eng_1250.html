आवहन्ती पोष्या वार्याणि चित्रं केतुं कृत्नुते चेकिताना |ईयुषीणाम उपमा शाश्वतीनां विभातीनां प्रथमोषा व्य अश्वैत ||
āvahantī poṣyā vāryāṇi citraṁ ketuṁ kṛṇute cekitānā | īyuṣīṇām upamā śaśvatīnāṁ vibhātīnām prathamoṣā vy aśvait ||
  • Mantra: "āvahantī poṣyā vāryāṇi citraṁ ketuṁ kṛṇute cekitānā |
  • Translation: "The one who brings forth nourishment, who is adorned with various qualities, creates a banner of distinction for the wise."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • āvahantī: The one who brings forth or nourishes.
    • poṣyā: Nourishing or sustaining.
    • vāryāṇi: Various or diverse qualities.
    • citraṁ: Colorful or distinct.
    • ketuṁ: Banner or flag, symbolizing identity or achievement.
    • kṛṇute: Creates or establishes.
    • cekitānā: The wise or knowledgeable individuals.
  • Meaning: This mantra emphasizes the importance of nourishment and the diverse qualities that contribute to the identity and achievements of wise individuals. It suggests that those who provide sustenance and support are instrumental in creating a distinct identity or banner for the knowledgeable.
  • Real-Life Example:
    • In a community, a teacher (the nourisher) plays a crucial role in shaping the minds of students (the wise). The teacher's diverse teaching methods (various qualities) help students develop their unique identities (banner of distinction).
    • In a corporate setting, a mentor (the one who nourishes) guides employees (the wise) in their professional development, helping them to stand out in their careers (creating a banner of achievement).
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its recognition of the vital role that nourishment—be it intellectual, emotional, or physical—plays in the development of individuals. It highlights that the qualities we cultivate and the support we provide can lead to the creation of a distinct identity and success in various aspects of life. This mantra is applicable in educational settings, workplaces, and personal relationships, reminding us of the importance of nurturing others to help them achieve their potential.
