युध्मो अनर्वा खजकृत समद्वा शूरः सत्राशाड जनुशेम अशाल्हः |व्य आस इन्द्रः पृथनाḥ स्वोजा अधा विश्वं शत्रूयन्तं जग्हाना ||
yudhmo anarvā khajakṛt samadvā śūraḥ satrāṣāḍ januṣem aṣāḻhaḥ | vy āsa indraḥ pṛtanāḥ svojā adhā viśvaṁ śatrūyantaṁ jaghāna ||
  • yudhmo anarvā khajakṛt: This phrase can be interpreted as "the one who engages in battle without fear." It emphasizes the importance of courage and valor in facing challenges. In real life, this can be seen in individuals who confront their fears, such as a soldier going into battle or a person standing up for their beliefs despite opposition.
  • samadvā śūraḥ: This translates to "the brave one who is equal in all situations." It highlights the quality of maintaining composure and bravery regardless of circumstances. For example, a leader who remains calm during a crisis exemplifies this quality, inspiring confidence in others.
  • satrāṣāḍ januṣem aṣāḻhaḥ: This can be understood as "the one who overcomes obstacles and challenges." It signifies resilience and the ability to rise above difficulties. In everyday life, this could refer to someone who perseveres through personal struggles, such as a student overcoming academic challenges to achieve their goals.
  • vy āsa indraḥ pṛtanāḥ: This phrase means "may Indra, the king of gods, grant us strength." Indra symbolizes power and authority, and invoking his name suggests seeking divine support in our endeavors. In practical terms, this could be likened to individuals seeking guidance or inspiration from mentors or role models in their lives.
  • svojā adhā viśvaṁ: This translates to "may we be united in our efforts." It emphasizes the importance of collaboration and teamwork. In real life, this can be seen in community projects where individuals come together to achieve a common goal, such as organizing a charity event or working on environmental conservation efforts.
  • śatrūyantaṁ jaghāna: This means "may we conquer our enemies." It signifies the triumph over adversities and challenges. In a broader context, this can refer to overcoming personal demons, such as addiction or negative thoughts, and emerging victorious in the journey of self-improvement.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of courage, resilience, unity, and the pursuit of strength in the face of challenges. Its significance lies in its applicability to various aspects of life, from personal struggles to collective efforts in communities. By embodying the qualities expressed in this mantra, individuals can navigate their battles with bravery and emerge victorious, fostering a spirit of collaboration and support in their endeavors.
