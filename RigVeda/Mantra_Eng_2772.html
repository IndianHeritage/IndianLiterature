इन्द्रस्तुजो बरहणा आ विवेश नृवद दधानो नार्यां पुरूणि |अचेतयद् हि य इमा जरीत्रे प्रेमं वर्णम् अतिरच् शुक्रम् आसाम् ||
indras tujo barhaṇā ā viveśa nṛvad dadhāno naryā purūṇi | acetayad dhiya imā jaritre premaṁ varṇam atirac chukram āsām ||
  • Indras tujo barhaṇā: This phrase refers to Indra, the king of the gods in Vedic literature, who is often associated with strength and power. "Barhaṇā" signifies the act of bestowing or granting something valuable.
  • Ā viveśa nṛvad dadhāno naryā purūṇi: This part can be interpreted as Indra entering the realm of humans, bringing forth the qualities of leadership and guidance. "Nṛvad" suggests a connection to humanity, while "purūṇi" indicates the abundance or richness of life.
  • Acetayad dhiya imā jaritre: Here, "acetayad" implies a state of awareness or consciousness. "Dhiya" refers to intellect or wisdom, and "jaritre" can be understood as the act of creation or manifestation. This suggests that through awareness and wisdom, one can create a meaningful existence.
  • Premaṁ varṇam atirac chukram āsām: "Premaṁ" translates to love or affection, while "varṇam" refers to color or quality. "Atirac" indicates something that is beyond measure, and "chukram" signifies purity or brightness. This line emphasizes the importance of love and purity in life, suggesting that these qualities elevate human experience.

  • Significance: This mantra encapsulates the essence of divine guidance, human potential, and the transformative power of love and wisdom. It highlights the relationship between the divine and humanity, suggesting that through awareness and love, individuals can achieve greatness.
  • Real-life Application: In practical terms, this mantra can be applied in various aspects of life. For instance, in leadership roles, one can embody the qualities of Indra by guiding others with wisdom and compassion. In personal relationships, fostering love and purity can lead to deeper connections and fulfillment.

  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of the divine and human experience. It encourages individuals to cultivate awareness, wisdom, and love in their lives, ultimately leading to a more enriched and meaningful existence.
