परावतो न सामा तद यत्रा रणन्ति धीतयः |त्रिधातुभिर अरुषीभिर वयो दधे रोचमानो वयो दधे ||
parāvato na sāma tad yatrā raṇanti dhītayaḥ | tridhātubhir aruṣībhir vayo dadhe rocamāno vayo dadhe ||
  • Mantra: "parāvato na sāma tad yatrā raṇanti dhītayaḥ | tridhātubhir aruṣībhir vayo dadhe rocamāno vayo dadhe ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • parāvato: of the supreme or higher realms
    • na: not
    • sāma: song or hymn
    • tad: that
    • yatrā: where
    • raṇanti: they fight or engage
    • dhītayaḥ: with wisdom or intellect
    • tridhātubhir: with three elements or aspects
    • aruṣībhir: with red or fiery qualities
    • vayo: age or life
    • dadhe: establishes or supports
    • rocamāno: shining or illuminating
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the higher realms where wisdom prevails over conflict.
    • It emphasizes the importance of engaging in battles of intellect rather than physical confrontations.
    • The mention of "tridhātubhir" suggests a balance of three essential elements—perhaps mind, body, and spirit—that contribute to a person's overall strength.
    • The "aruṣībhir" indicates a fiery passion or energy that drives one to act wisely and courageously.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, rather than resorting to aggressive competition, companies can engage in strategic thinking and innovation to outsmart their rivals.
    • In personal relationships, resolving conflicts through open communication and understanding reflects the wisdom mentioned in the mantra.
    • In education, students are encouraged to engage in debates and discussions, fostering a culture of intellectual engagement rather than hostility.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of wisdom and intellect in overcoming challenges.
    • It serves as a reminder that true strength lies in the ability to think critically and act with purpose.
    • In today's world, where conflicts often arise, applying the principles of this mantra can lead to more harmonious and constructive outcomes in various aspects of life.
