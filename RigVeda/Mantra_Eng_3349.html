प्र सु ष विभ्यो मरुतो विर अस्तु प्र श्येनः श्येनेभ्य आशीपत्वा |अचक्रया यत स्वधया सुपर्णो हव्यं भरन मनवे देवजुष्टम् ||
pra su ṣa vibhyo maruto vir astu pra śyenaḥ śyenebhya āśupatvā | acakrayā yat svadhayā suparṇo havyam bharan manave devajuṣṭam ||
  • pra su ṣa vibhyo maruto vir astu: This phrase can be interpreted as a call to the Maruts, the storm deities, to bestow their strength and vigor. The term "pra su" suggests a plea for abundance and prosperity, while "vibhyo" indicates a connection to the divine forces of nature.
  • pra śyenaḥ śyenebhya āśupatvā: Here, "pra śyenaḥ" refers to the swift and powerful nature of the hawk, symbolizing speed and agility. "Śyenebhya" connects to the idea of soaring high, representing ambition and the pursuit of goals. "Āśupatvā" emphasizes the importance of quickness in achieving one's objectives.
  • acakrayā yat svadhayā suparṇo: "Acakrayā" suggests a state of being unencumbered or free from obstacles. "Svadhayā" refers to self-empowerment and the ability to act according to one's own will. "Suparṇo" symbolizes the well-formed or auspicious, indicating a positive outcome when one is true to oneself.
  • havyam bharan manave devajuṣṭam: This line speaks to the act of offering (havyam) to the divine. "Bharan" implies carrying or bringing forth, while "manave" refers to humanity or the human spirit. "Devajuṣṭam" indicates that these offerings are pleasing to the gods, suggesting a reciprocal relationship between humans and the divine.

  • Significance: This mantra emphasizes the importance of invoking divine forces for strength, agility, and prosperity. It highlights the need for self-empowerment and the pursuit of one's goals with determination and speed.
  • Real-life Application: In modern life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios such as:
    • In business, when seeking success, one can invoke the qualities of the Maruts for strength and resilience in the face of challenges.
    • In personal development, the mantra encourages individuals to act swiftly and decisively in pursuing their goals, embodying the spirit of the hawk.
    • In spiritual practices, it serves as a reminder to offer gratitude and seek blessings from the divine, fostering a sense of connection and reciprocity.

  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the interconnectedness between humans and the divine. By invoking the qualities of strength, agility, and self-empowerment, individuals can navigate life's challenges more effectively. It encourages a proactive approach to achieving one's goals while maintaining a respectful relationship with the divine forces that guide and support us.
