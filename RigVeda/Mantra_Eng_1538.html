अभī नो अग्न उक्तम् इज जुगुर्याः dyāvākṣāmā सिंधवश्च स्वगूर्ताः |गव्यं यव्यं यन्तो दीर्घाहेषं वरम् अरुण्यो वरन्त ||
abhī no agna uktham ij juguryā dyāvākṣāmā sindhavaś ca svagūrtāḥ | gavyaṁ yavyaṁ yanto dīrghāheṣaṁ varam aruṇyo varanta ||
  • abhī no agna uktham: This phrase can be translated as "O Agni, may our utterances reach you." Here, Agni represents the divine fire and is often seen as a mediator between humans and the gods. In real life, this can be likened to the importance of communication and connection in our relationships, whether personal or professional.
  • ij juguryā dyāvākṣāmā: This part means "we invoke you, O Agni, who is the source of strength." It emphasizes the idea of invoking divine energy or strength in our endeavors. For example, when starting a new project or facing challenges, people often seek motivation or inspiration, akin to invoking Agni for strength.
  • sindhavaś ca svagūrtāḥ: This translates to "and the rivers that flow with their own strength." Rivers symbolize the flow of life and the ability to adapt and overcome obstacles. In real life, this can be seen in how individuals navigate through challenges, much like a river carves its path through the landscape.
  • gavyaṁ yavyaṁ yanto: This phrase means "those who seek the best of the best." It reflects the human aspiration for excellence and quality in life. In practical terms, this can relate to striving for the best in education, career, or personal growth.
  • dīrghāheṣaṁ varam: This translates to "long-lasting blessings." It signifies the desire for enduring prosperity and well-being. In everyday life, this can be related to the pursuit of sustainable practices, whether in business or personal life, to ensure long-term benefits.
  • aruṇyo varanta: This means "may the auspicious ones grant us." It highlights the importance of seeking blessings from positive and auspicious forces in life. In real life, this can be seen in rituals, prayers, or positive affirmations that people engage in to attract good fortune and success.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking divine guidance, strength, and blessings in our lives. It emphasizes the importance of communication, the pursuit of excellence, and the desire for long-lasting prosperity. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire individuals to invoke their inner strength, adapt to challenges, and strive for the best outcomes in their personal and professional lives. By recognizing the divine forces at play and seeking their blessings, individuals can navigate their journeys with purpose and positivity.
