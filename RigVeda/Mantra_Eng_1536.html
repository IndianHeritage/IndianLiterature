इदं अग्नि सुधितं दुर्धिताद् अधि प्रियाद् उ चिन् मनमनः प्रेयो अस्तु ते |यत् ते शुक्रं तंवो रोचते शुचि तेनास्मभ्यं वनसे रत्नम आ त्वम् ||
idam agne sudhitaṁ durdhitād adhi priyād u cin manmanaḥ preyo astu te | yat te śukraṁ tanvo3 rocate śuci tenāsmabhyaṁ vanase ratnam ā tvam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • idam: this
    • agne: O Agni (the fire god)
    • sudhitaṁ: well-prepared or purified
    • durdhitād: from difficulties or troubles
    • adhi: upon
    • priyād: from what is dear
    • u: and
    • cin: may you gather
    • manmanaḥ: our thoughts or minds
    • preyo: dear or beloved
    • astu: may it be
    • te: to you
    • yat: whatever
    • te: your
    • śukraṁ: pure or bright
    • tanvoḥ: of the body
    • rocate: shines or is pleasing
    • śuci: pure
    • tena: by that
    • asmbhyaṁ: to us
    • vanase: in the forest or wilderness
    • ratnam: jewel or treasure
    • ā: to
    • tvam: you
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Agni, the fire god, asking for his blessings.
    • It emphasizes the importance of purification and the gathering of positive thoughts.
    • The reference to "difficulties" suggests a desire for overcoming challenges with the help of divine energy.
    • The mantra also highlights the significance of purity and brightness, both in a physical and spiritual sense.
    • It seeks to attract valuable treasures or blessings into one’s life, symbolized by the "jewel" in the wilderness.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, individuals often face challenges that require inner strength and clarity. This mantra can be recited during meditation to invoke the energy of Agni for overcoming such obstacles.
    • In a professional setting, when facing tough decisions or projects, one might reflect on the mantra to seek clarity and purity of thought, ensuring that their actions are aligned with their values.
    • During times of stress or confusion, this mantra serves as a reminder to focus on what is dear and valuable, helping individuals to prioritize their goals and aspirations.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine assistance in achieving purity, clarity, and overcoming difficulties.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal challenges to professional endeavors, serving as a source of inspiration and strength.
    • By invoking Agni, individuals can cultivate a mindset that attracts positivity and valuable experiences, much like a jewel found in the wilderness.
