आ हर्यताया धृष्णवे धनुस तंवन्ति पौंस्यम् |शुक्रां वयन्त्य असुराय निर्णिजं विपाम अग्रे महीयुवः ||
ā haryatāya dhṛṣṇave dhanus tanvanti pauṁsyam | śukrāṁ vayanty asurāya nirṇijaṁ vipām agre mahīyuvaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā haryatāya: "to the one who is swift" or "to the one who moves quickly"
    • dhṛṣṇave: "to the strong" or "to the courageous"
    • dhanus: "bow" (as in a weapon)
    • tanvanti: "they stretch" or "they extend"
    • pauṁsyam: "of the man" or "of the masculine"
    • śukrāṁ: "the bright" or "the pure"
    • vayanty: "they go" or "they proceed"
    • asurāya: "to the demon" or "to the adversary"
    • nirṇijaṁ: "without a cause" or "without a reason"
    • vipām: "to the wise" or "to the knowledgeable"
    • agre: "in front" or "ahead"
    • mahīyuvaḥ: "the great youth" or "the powerful one"
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks to the qualities of strength, courage, and the ability to act decisively.
    • The imagery of a bow being drawn suggests readiness for action, much like a warrior preparing for battle.
    • The reference to "the bright" and "the wise" indicates the importance of knowledge and clarity in overcoming challenges.
    • In real life, this can be seen in situations where individuals must confront obstacles, whether in personal challenges, professional endeavors, or societal issues.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • A student preparing for an important exam must embody the qualities of courage and strength, much like the warrior with a drawn bow, ready to face the challenge ahead.
    • In a corporate setting, a leader who must make tough decisions for the benefit of the team exemplifies the "great youth" who acts with wisdom and clarity.
    • Activists fighting for social justice often face adversaries and must draw upon their inner strength and knowledge to advocate for change.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of courage, strength, and wisdom in overcoming challenges.
    • It serves as a reminder that in the face of adversity, one must be prepared to act decisively and with clarity.
    • In various aspects of life, whether personal, professional, or societal, embodying these qualities can lead to success and positive change.
