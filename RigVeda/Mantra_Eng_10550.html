सँ गच्छध्वं सँ वदध्वं सँ वो मनांसी जानताम् |देवा भागं यथा पूर्वे सँजानानां उपासते ||
saṁ gacchadhvaṁ saṁ vadadhvaṁ saṁ vo manāṁsi jānatām | devā bhāgaṁ yathā pūrve saṁjānānā upāsate ||
  • saṁ gacchadhvaṁ: This phrase translates to "Come together." It emphasizes the importance of unity and collective action. In real life, this can be seen in team sports where players must work together to achieve a common goal, such as winning a game.
  • saṁ vadadhvaṁ: This means "Speak together." It highlights the significance of communication and dialogue. For instance, in a workplace setting, effective communication among team members can lead to better collaboration and innovation.
  • saṁ vo manāṁsi jānatām: This translates to "Let your minds be in harmony." It suggests that shared understanding and mutual respect are essential for progress. In a community, when individuals share their thoughts and ideas respectfully, it fosters a sense of belonging and cooperation.
  • devā bhāgaṁ yathā pūrve: This means "As the gods have shared their portion." It refers to the idea of sharing resources and blessings. In modern society, this can be reflected in charitable acts where individuals or organizations share their wealth or knowledge to uplift others.
  • saṁjānānā upāsate: This translates to "They worship together." It signifies the importance of collective worship and spiritual practices. In real life, this can be seen in religious gatherings or community events where people come together to celebrate their faith and values.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of unity, communication, harmony, sharing, and collective worship. In today's world, these principles are applicable in various scenarios, such as teamwork in professional settings, community service, and social gatherings. By embodying these values, individuals can contribute to a more cohesive and supportive society, fostering an environment where everyone can thrive together.
