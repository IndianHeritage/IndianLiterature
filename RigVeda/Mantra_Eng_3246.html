आयं वृतश्चातयते समीचीर य आजीषु मगवाऽश्रिण्व एकः |आयं वाजं भरति यं सनोत्य अस्य प्रियासः सख्ये स्याम ||
ayaṁ vṛtaś cātayate samīcīr ya ājiṣu maghavā śṛṇva ekaḥ | ayaṁ vājam bharati yaṁ sanoty asya priyāsaḥ sakhye syāma ||
  • ayaṁ vṛtaś cātayate samīcīr: This phrase can be interpreted as "this truth binds together in harmony." The word "vṛta" refers to truth or reality, while "cātayate" means to bind or connect. In real life, this can be seen in communities where shared values and truths bring people together, fostering unity and cooperation.
  • ya ājiṣu maghavā śṛṇva ekaḥ: Here, "maghavā" refers to the powerful or the mighty, and "śṛṇva" means to listen. This line suggests that the powerful one listens to the collective voice. In a modern context, this can be related to leaders who take the time to listen to their constituents, ensuring that their decisions reflect the needs and desires of the people.
  • ayaṁ vājam bharati: This translates to "this one carries the strength." "Vājam" refers to strength or power, and "bharati" means to carry or bear. In everyday life, this can be exemplified by individuals who take on responsibilities and challenges, demonstrating resilience and strength in their actions.
  • yaṁ sanoty asya priyāsaḥ sakhye syāma: This line means "whoever nurtures this, may we be friends." "Sanoty" refers to nurturing or fostering, and "priyāsaḥ" means beloved or dear. This emphasizes the importance of nurturing relationships and friendships. In real life, it highlights the value of building and maintaining strong bonds with others, whether in personal or professional settings.

  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of truth, unity, strength, and nurturing relationships. In a world where division and conflict are prevalent, this mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of coming together in harmony, listening to one another, and fostering strong connections. It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, such as community building, leadership, and personal relationships, encouraging individuals to embody these values in their daily lives.
