क्व नूनं सुदानवो मदथा वृक्तबरहिषः |ब्रह्मा को वः सपर्यति ||
kva nūnaṁ sudānavo madathā vṛktabarhiṣaḥ | brahmā ko vaḥ saparyati ||
  • kva nūnaṁ: This phrase translates to "where indeed" or "where is it." It expresses a sense of inquiry or searching for something significant.
  • sudānavo: This term refers to "good drink" or "nourishing beverage," often symbolizing the essence of life or spiritual sustenance.
  • madathā: This word means "in the intoxication" or "in the state of bliss," suggesting a heightened state of awareness or ecstasy.
  • vṛktabarhiṣaḥ: This can be interpreted as "those who are well-versed in the sacred hymns" or "those who are knowledgeable in spiritual matters," indicating a connection to wisdom and divine knowledge.
  • brahmā: Referring to the ultimate reality or the cosmic spirit, this term signifies the source of all creation and existence.
  • ko vaḥ: This translates to "who among you" or "who is it that," indicating a call to the audience or the seekers of truth.
  • saparyati: This means "worships" or "offers reverence," suggesting an act of devotion or acknowledgment of the divine.

  • Meaning: The mantra poses a profound question about the source of spiritual nourishment and the nature of divine worship. It invites individuals to reflect on where true sustenance lies and who among them is engaged in the act of reverence towards the ultimate reality.
  • Real-Life Example: In a modern context, this mantra can be related to the search for meaning in life. For instance, individuals often seek fulfillment through various means such as relationships, careers, or spiritual practices. The mantra encourages introspection about what truly nourishes the soul and how one can engage in meaningful worship or gratitude towards the universe.

  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for self-exploration and understanding of one's spiritual needs. It emphasizes the importance of recognizing the sources of true nourishment in life, whether they be spiritual, emotional, or intellectual. In real-life scenarios, this can be applicable in moments of crisis or confusion, prompting individuals to seek deeper connections with themselves and the world around them. By engaging in practices that honor the divine, individuals can cultivate a sense of peace and fulfillment, ultimately leading to a more enriched life.
