आ नो दिव आ पृथिव्या ऋजिषिन्न इदं बर्हिः सोमपेयाय याहि |वहन्तु त्वा हरयो मद्र्यञ्चम् आङ्गूषम् अच्छा तवसम मदाय ||
ā no diva ā pṛthivyā ṛjīṣinn idam barhiḥ somapeyāya yāhi | vahantu tvā harayo madryañcam āṅgūṣam acchā tavasam madāya ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā no diva: "May we be blessed from the heavens"
    • ā pṛthivyā: "and from the earth"
    • ṛjīṣinn: "with righteousness"
    • idam barhiḥ: "this offering"
    • somapeyāya: "for the drink of Soma"
    • yāhi: "come forth"
    • vahantu: "may they carry"
    • tvā: "you"
    • harayo: "the shining ones"
    • madryañcam: "with joy and delight"
    • āṅgūṣam: "with strength"
    • acchā: "good"
    • tavasam: "of your power"
    • madāya: "for the intoxication"
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra is a prayer for blessings from both the heavens and the earth, emphasizing the importance of divine and earthly support in our lives.
    • The term "ṛjīṣinn" signifies the pursuit of righteousness, which is a fundamental aspect of living a virtuous life.
    • The mention of "Soma" refers to a sacred drink in Vedic rituals, symbolizing spiritual enlightenment and the joy that comes from divine connection.
    • The phrase "may they carry you" suggests a desire for divine beings to support and uplift the individual, highlighting the interconnectedness of all beings.
    • The overall tone of the mantra is one of reverence and gratitude, acknowledging the sources of strength and joy in life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, this mantra can be reflected in moments of gratitude, such as giving thanks for the support of family and friends, akin to the blessings sought from the heavens and earth.
    • In professional settings, pursuing righteousness can be seen in ethical decision-making, where individuals strive to act with integrity and fairness.
    • During celebrations or significant life events, invoking joy and strength can be paralleled with the communal sharing of food and drink, fostering connections and happiness among participants.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking divine blessings and the importance of righteousness in our lives.
    • Its significance lies in the reminder to remain connected to both the spiritual and earthly realms, fostering a balanced and fulfilling life.
    • In real-life scenarios, it encourages individuals to cultivate gratitude, pursue ethical paths, and celebrate the joys of life, ultimately leading to a harmonious existence.
