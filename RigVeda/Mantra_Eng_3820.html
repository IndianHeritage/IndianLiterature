तं त्वा घृतस्नव इमहे चित्रभानो स्वर्दृशम् |देवाम् ā वीतये वह ||
taṁ tvā ghṛtasnav īmahe citrabhāno svardṛśam | devām̐ ā vītaye vaha ||
  • taṁ tvā - This phrase translates to "We invoke you" or "We call upon you." It signifies a deep respect and a call for divine presence.
  • ghṛtasnav - This word means "one who is rich in ghee" or "the one who is nourishing." Ghee is often associated with purity and nourishment in Vedic traditions, symbolizing abundance and prosperity.
  • īmahe - This translates to "we meditate" or "we contemplate." It indicates a focus on spiritual practice and the importance of meditation in connecting with the divine.
  • citrabhāno - This term means "the one with brilliant rays" or "the radiant one." It refers to a deity or divine force that brings light and clarity, illuminating the path for devotees.
  • svardṛśam - This translates to "the one who has a golden vision" or "the one who sees the truth." It emphasizes the importance of wisdom and insight in understanding the world and oneself.
  • devām̐ - This means "to the gods" or "to the divine." It acknowledges the presence of higher powers and the need for their guidance and blessings.
  • ā vītaye - This phrase translates to "for the purpose of liberation" or "for the purpose of protection." It signifies a desire for spiritual freedom and safety from worldly troubles.
  • vaha - This means "carry" or "bring forth." It is a call to the divine to manifest blessings and support in the lives of the devotees.

Conclusion: The mantra "taṁ tvā ghṛtasnav īmahe citrabhāno svardṛśam | devām̐ ā vītaye vaha ||" is a powerful invocation that emphasizes the importance of calling upon divine forces for nourishment, wisdom, and protection. In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as during times of uncertainty or when seeking guidance in decision-making. For instance, individuals may recite this mantra during meditation or prayer to cultivate a sense of peace and clarity in their lives. Its significance lies in the recognition of the divine's role in providing support and enlightenment, encouraging individuals to seek a deeper connection with the spiritual realm for personal growth and liberation.
