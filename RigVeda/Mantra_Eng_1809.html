यज्ञा-यज्ञा वः समना तुतुर्वणिर धियं-धियं वो देवयā उ दधिध्वे |आ वो 'र्वाचः सुविताया रोदस्योर महे ववृत्याम अवसे सुवृक्तिभिः ||
yajñā-yajñā vaḥ samanā tuturvaṇir dhiyaṁ-dhiyaṁ vo devayā u dadhidhve | ā vo 'rvācaḥ suvitāya rodasyor mahe vavṛtyām avase suvṛktibhiḥ ||
  • yajñā-yajñā vaḥ samanā: This phrase refers to the concept of sacrifice or offerings. In Vedic tradition, yajña signifies a ritual of offerings to the deities, which is believed to create harmony and balance in the universe.
  • tuturvaṇir dhiyaṁ-dhiyaṁ: This part emphasizes the importance of collective thought and intention. It suggests that the minds of the participants should be aligned, working together towards a common goal.
  • vo devayā u dadhidhve: Here, the mantra invokes the divine presence, asking for blessings and support from the gods. It signifies the need for divine guidance in our endeavors.
  • ā vo 'rvācaḥ suvitāya: This line speaks to the importance of good speech and communication. It highlights that the words we use should be beneficial and uplifting, fostering positive relationships and understanding.
  • rodasyor mahe vavṛtyām: This phrase refers to the vastness of the universe and the importance of expanding our horizons. It encourages us to think beyond our immediate surroundings and consider the greater cosmos.
  • avase suvṛktibhiḥ: This final part emphasizes the need for good actions and deeds. It suggests that our efforts should be directed towards positive outcomes, benefiting ourselves and others.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda encapsulates the essence of community, intention, and divine connection. It teaches us that through collective efforts, aligned thoughts, and positive communication, we can achieve greater harmony in our lives. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various contexts such as teamwork in professional settings, community service, or family gatherings where mutual respect and understanding are essential. By embodying the principles of this mantra, we can foster a more harmonious and prosperous environment for ourselves and those around us.
