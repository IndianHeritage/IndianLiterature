त्वं दूतस्त्वमु नः परस्पास त्वं वस्य आ वृषभ प्रणेता |अग्नि तोकस्य नस् तने तनूनाम् अप्रयुच्छन् दीद्यद् बोधि गोपाः ||
tvaṁ dūtas tvam u naḥ paraspās tvaṁ vasya ā vṛṣabha praṇetā | agne tokasya nas tane tanūnām aprayucchan dīdyad bodhi gopāḥ ||
  • tvaṁ dūtas: This phrase translates to "You are the messenger." In a real-life context, this can refer to individuals who act as intermediaries or communicators, such as diplomats or mediators in conflicts.
  • tvam u naḥ paraspās: This means "You are our protector." This can be likened to the role of a guardian or a mentor in our lives, someone who provides guidance and support during challenging times.
  • tvaṁ vasya ā vṛṣabha praṇetā: This translates to "You are the one who leads us." In everyday life, this can refer to leaders in various fields, such as teachers, coaches, or community leaders who inspire and guide others towards a common goal.
  • agne: This word means "O Fire" and symbolizes energy, transformation, and purification. In practical terms, fire can represent the passion and drive we have in our pursuits, whether in our careers or personal lives.
  • tokasya nas tane tanūnām: This translates to "May you grant us the strength of our bodies." This can be related to the importance of health and well-being in our lives, emphasizing the need for physical fitness and mental resilience.
  • aprayucchan dīdyad bodhi gopāḥ: This means "May you not withhold the light of knowledge." In real life, this can be interpreted as the importance of education and enlightenment, encouraging individuals to seek knowledge and share it with others.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of guidance, protection, and the pursuit of knowledge in our lives. It highlights the roles of leaders, mentors, and the importance of health and well-being. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as in educational settings where teachers guide students, in workplaces where leaders inspire their teams, and in personal relationships where individuals support each other. Ultimately, it serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of our roles in society and the importance of nurturing our physical and intellectual capacities for a fulfilling life.
