अहम् एव वात इव प्र वाम्य आरभमानां भुवनानि विश्वा |परो दिवा पर एन पृतिव्यैतावती महिना सम बभूव ||
aham eva vāta iva pra vāmy ārabhamāṇā bhuvanāni viśvā | paro divā para enā pṛthivyaitāvatī mahinā sam babhūva ||
  • Translation: "I am indeed like the wind; I move through all worlds. I am greater than the day, greater than the earth; thus I have become powerful."
  • Word-by-Word Explanation:
    • aham eva: "I am indeed" - This emphasizes the speaker's self-awareness and existence.
    • vāta: "wind" - Represents movement, change, and the unseen forces that influence life.
    • iva: "like" - A simile that draws a comparison between the speaker and the wind.
    • pra vāmy: "I move" - Indicates action and the ability to traverse different realms.
    • ārabhamāṇā: "beginning" - Suggests initiation or the act of starting something new.
    • bhuvanāni: "worlds" - Refers to various realms of existence, both physical and metaphysical.
    • viśvā: "all" - Denotes inclusivity, encompassing everything.
    • paro: "greater" - Indicates superiority or a higher state of being.
    • divā: "day" - Symbolizes light, knowledge, and consciousness.
    • pṛthivyaitāvatī: "earth" - Represents the physical realm and material existence.
    • mahinā: "powerful" - Signifies strength and influence.
    • sam babhūva: "thus I have become" - Indicates transformation and realization of one's potential.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • Just as the wind moves freely and influences the environment, individuals can navigate through life's challenges and impact the world around them.
    • In personal development, recognizing one's own power and potential can lead to significant changes, much like the wind that initiates movement in nature.
    • In leadership, a person who embodies the qualities of the wind—adaptability, strength, and influence—can inspire and guide others effectively.
  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of self-awareness, the power of movement, and the ability to influence one's surroundings. It serves as a reminder that, like the wind, we have the potential to traverse different realms of existence and make a significant impact. In real life, this can be applied in various contexts such as personal growth, leadership, and the pursuit of knowledge. Embracing our inner strength and recognizing our interconnectedness with the world can lead to profound transformations in our lives and the lives of others.
