वृक्षाश्चिन मे अभिपित्वे अरारणुः |गाम्भजन्त मेहनाश्वं भजन्त मेहनाः ||
vṛkṣāś cin me abhipitve arāraṇuḥ | gām bhajanta mehanāśvam bhajanta mehanā ||
  • Mantra: "vṛkṣāś cin me abhipitve arāraṇuḥ | gām bhajanta mehanāśvam bhajanta mehanā ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • vṛkṣāś: trees
    • cin: may they be
    • me: for me
    • abhipitve: in my protection
    • arāraṇuḥ: may they flourish
    • gām: the earth
    • bhajanta: may they serve
    • mehanāśvam: the powerful horse
    • bhajanta: may they serve
    • mehanā: the powerful one
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra expresses a deep connection with nature, particularly trees, and invokes their protection and flourishing.
    • The mention of "gām" (earth) signifies a reverence for the planet and its resources, suggesting that the earth and its elements should serve humanity.
    • The reference to "mehanāśvam" (powerful horse) symbolizes strength, vitality, and the ability to overcome challenges, indicating a desire for empowerment and support from nature.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern environmental movements, the mantra's call for the flourishing of trees can be seen in reforestation efforts, where communities come together to plant trees and restore ecosystems.
    • The idea of the earth serving humanity resonates with sustainable practices, such as organic farming, where the health of the soil and plants is prioritized to ensure long-term benefits for both people and the environment.
    • The powerful horse can be likened to the strength found in teamwork and collaboration, as seen in community projects that aim to uplift and empower individuals through shared resources and support.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of harmony between humans and nature, advocating for the protection and flourishing of the environment.
    • Its significance lies in the reminder that our well-being is intertwined with the health of the earth and its resources.
    • In real-life scenarios, it encourages individuals and communities to engage in practices that promote sustainability, environmental stewardship, and collective empowerment.
