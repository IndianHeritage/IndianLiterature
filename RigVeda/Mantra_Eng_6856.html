स त्वं विप्राय दाशुषे रयिं देहि सहस्रिणम् |अग्नि वीरवतिम् इषम् ||
sa tvaṁ viprāya dāśuṣe rayiṁ dehi sahasriṇam | agne vīravatīm iṣam ||
  • sa tvaṁ: This phrase translates to "You are" or "You, indeed." It addresses a divine entity, often interpreted as Agni, the fire god, symbolizing energy and transformation.
  • viprāya: This term refers to the learned or wise ones, often the priests or sages who possess knowledge and understanding of the Vedas. It emphasizes the importance of wisdom in seeking blessings.
  • dāśuṣe: This word means "to the one who gives" or "to the generous." It highlights the act of giving and the importance of generosity in spiritual and material pursuits.
  • rayiṁ: This translates to "wealth" or "prosperity." It signifies not just material wealth but also spiritual abundance and well-being.
  • dehi: This means "give" or "bestow." It is a plea for the divine to grant blessings and abundance.
  • sahasriṇam: This term means "thousands" or "many." It suggests a desire for abundant blessings, emphasizing the wish for a multitude of wealth and prosperity.
  • agne: This is a direct invocation to Agni, the fire god, representing purification, transformation, and the divine spark within all beings.
  • vīravatīm: This word means "with strength" or "powerful." It signifies the strength and valor that one seeks in life, both in physical and spiritual realms.
  • iṣam: This translates to "the power" or "the force." It indicates the desire for the divine to grant strength and capability to achieve one's goals.

  • Significance: This mantra is a prayer for abundance, wisdom, and strength. It reflects the human desire for prosperity and the importance of seeking divine blessings in achieving one's goals.
  • Real-life Application: In everyday life, this mantra can be invoked during rituals, personal prayers, or meditation to seek guidance and support in endeavors, whether they are related to career, relationships, or personal growth.
  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking divine assistance in attaining wealth and strength. It serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of wisdom, generosity, and the pursuit of prosperity, encouraging individuals to cultivate these qualities in their lives.
