उता ब्रुवन्तु जन्तव उद अग्निर वृत्रहाजनि |धनञ्जयो रणे-रणे ||
uta bruvantu jantava ud agnir vṛtrahājani | dhanaṁjayo raṇe-raṇe ||
  • Mantra: "uta bruvantu jantava ud agnir vṛtrahājani | dhanaṁjayo raṇe-raṇe ||"
  • Translation: "Let the beings speak out; may Agni, the slayer of Vritra, be invoked. May we be victorious in every battle."

  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • uta: Let (us) or may (we)
    • bruvantu: speak or express
    • jantava: beings or creatures
    • ud: up or forth
    • agnir: Agni, the fire god
    • vṛtrahājani: slayer of Vritra (a demon representing chaos and obstacles)
    • dhanaṁjayo: victorious or successful in wealth or battle
    • raṇe-raṇe: in every battle or conflict

  • Contextual Explanation:
    • This mantra invokes Agni, the fire god, who is often seen as a purifying force and a protector against chaos and negativity.
    • Vritra represents obstacles and challenges in life. By invoking Agni, the mantra seeks to overcome these challenges.
    • The phrase "Let the beings speak out" emphasizes the importance of communication and unity among individuals in facing adversities.
    • The call for victory in battles signifies the human struggle against various forms of conflict, whether they be personal, social, or spiritual.

  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, teams often face challenges that can be likened to battles. This mantra can inspire team members to communicate openly and work together to overcome obstacles.
    • In personal life, individuals may face conflicts in relationships or career paths. The invocation of Agni can symbolize the inner strength and clarity needed to navigate these challenges.
    • In community settings, this mantra can serve as a reminder for collective action and support in times of crisis, encouraging people to unite and speak out against injustices.

  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of unity, communication, and the pursuit of victory over challenges in various aspects of life.
    • It serves as a reminder that invoking inner strength and collective support can lead to overcoming obstacles and achieving success.
    • In essence, it encourages individuals and communities to face their battles with courage and determination, fostering a spirit of resilience and triumph.
