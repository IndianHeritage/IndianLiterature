अग्निर् नेताऽभग इव क्षितीनां दैवीनां देव ऋतुपा ऋतावाः |स वृत्रहा सनयो विश्ववेदाः परिषद् विश्वाति दुरितागृणन्तम् ||
agnir netā bhaga iva kṣitīnāṁ daivīnāṁ deva ṛtupā ṛtāvā | sa vṛtrahā sanayo viśvavedāḥ parṣad viśvāti duritā gṛṇantam ||
  • Agnir netā bhaga iva kṣitīnāṁ: This phrase translates to "Agni (the fire) is the leader, like the wealth of the earth." Here, Agni symbolizes not just physical fire but also the divine energy that leads and guides. In real life, this can be likened to a leader in a community or organization who inspires and motivates others, much like how a fire provides warmth and light.
  • Daivīnāṁ deva ṛtupā ṛtāvā: This means "the divine gods who uphold the cosmic order." The term "ṛtupā" refers to those who maintain righteousness and truth. In a practical sense, this can be seen in individuals or groups who work towards justice and ethical standards in society, such as activists or community leaders who fight for human rights.
  • Sa vṛtrahā sanayo viśvavedāḥ: This translates to "He who destroys the obstacles, the one who knows all." Here, "vṛtrahā" refers to the one who removes barriers or challenges. In everyday life, this can be related to mentors or guides who help individuals overcome personal or professional challenges, enabling them to achieve their goals.
  • Parṣad viśvāti duritā gṛṇantam: This means "the assembly that chants away misfortunes." This phrase emphasizes the power of collective effort and community support in overcoming difficulties. In real life, this can be seen in support groups or community gatherings where people come together to share their struggles and uplift one another, creating a sense of belonging and resilience.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of leadership, divine guidance, the removal of obstacles, and the power of community in overcoming challenges. Its significance lies in the recognition of the interconnectedness of individuals and the divine, encouraging us to seek guidance, support, and righteousness in our lives. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire individuals to take on leadership roles, uphold ethical standards, seek mentorship, and foster community support, ultimately leading to a more harmonious and prosperous society.
