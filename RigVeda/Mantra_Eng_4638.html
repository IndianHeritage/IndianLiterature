न वीजावे नमते न स्थिराया न शर्धते दस्युजूताया स्तवान् |अज्रā इन्द्रस्य गिरयश्चिद ऋष्वा गम्भीरे चिद भवति गाधम अस्मै ||
na vīḻave namate na sthirāya na śardhate dasyujūtāya stavān | ajrā indrasya girayaś cid ṛṣvā gambhīre cid bhavati gādham asmai ||
  • na vīḻave namate: This phrase can be interpreted as "not bowing down" or "not yielding." It signifies strength and resilience in the face of challenges. In real life, this can be seen in individuals who stand firm in their beliefs and values, even when faced with opposition.
  • na sthirāya: This means "not to the stable" or "not to the firm." It suggests a rejection of complacency or stagnation. For example, in a professional setting, this could relate to individuals who continuously seek growth and improvement rather than settling into a routine.
  • na śardhate dasyujūtāya stavān: This can be translated as "not to the one who is weak or cowardly." It emphasizes the importance of courage and strength. In everyday life, this can be reflected in people who take risks to pursue their dreams, despite the fear of failure.
  • ajrā indrasya girayaś: "Indra's unyielding strength" refers to the power and might of the deity Indra, often associated with thunder and rain. This can symbolize the natural forces that are beyond human control. In real life, this can be seen in how people respect and adapt to nature's power, such as preparing for natural disasters.
  • cid ṛṣvā gambhīre: This phrase means "in the deep and profound." It suggests a connection to deeper truths and wisdom. In practical terms, this can relate to individuals who seek knowledge and understanding beyond superficial appearances, such as philosophers or scientists.
  • cid bhavati gādham asmai: This translates to "may it be granted to him." It implies a prayer or wish for strength and resilience to be bestowed upon someone. In real life, this can be seen in communities coming together to support individuals facing hardships, offering encouragement and strength.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of strength, resilience, and the pursuit of deeper truths. It encourages individuals to stand firm in their beliefs, seek continuous growth, and embrace courage in the face of adversity. In real life, these principles can be applied in various scenarios, such as personal development, professional growth, and community support. By embodying the essence of this mantra, individuals can navigate life's challenges with grace and determination, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling and meaningful existence.
