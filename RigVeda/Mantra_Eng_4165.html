अश्वा इवेद अरुषासः स्बन्धवः शूरा इव प्रयुधः प्रोत युयुधुः |मर्याऽ इव सुवृद्धो वावृद्धुर नरः सूर्यस्य चक्षुः प्र मिनन्ति वृष्टिभिः ||
aśvā ived aruṣāsaḥ sabandhavaḥ śūrā iva prayudhaḥ prota yuyudhuḥ | maryā iva suvṛdho vāvṛdhur naraḥ sūryasya cakṣuḥ pra minanti vṛṣṭibhiḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • aśvā: horses
    • ived: have come
    • aruṣāsaḥ: red or fiery
    • sabandhavaḥ: with bonds or connections
    • śūrā: the brave or the strong
    • iva: like
    • prayudhaḥ: in battle
    • prota: woven or connected
    • yuyudhuḥ: they fought
    • maryā: the noble or the honorable
    • iva: like
    • suvṛdho: well-nourished or well-cultivated
    • vāvṛdhur: they have grown or increased
    • naraḥ: men or humans
    • sūryasya: of the sun
    • cakṣuḥ: eye or vision
    • pra: towards
    • minanti: they aim or direct
    • vṛṣṭibhiḥ: with rains or abundance
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the strength and valor of horses, symbolizing power and energy.
    • The imagery of "red or fiery" horses suggests vitality and passion, often associated with warriors in battle.
    • The phrase "like the brave in battle" emphasizes the courage and determination required in challenging situations.
    • The connection of "woven" suggests unity and teamwork, highlighting the importance of collaboration in achieving goals.
    • The mention of "noble men" indicates the significance of honor and integrity in human actions.
    • The reference to the "eye of the sun" symbolizes clarity, vision, and enlightenment, guiding individuals towards their objectives.
    • The term "with rains" signifies abundance and prosperity, suggesting that hard work and valor lead to fruitful outcomes.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In sports, a team that works together with courage and determination can achieve victory, much like the "brave in battle."
    • In business, collaboration among team members can lead to innovative solutions, reflecting the "woven" nature of their efforts.
    • In personal development, individuals who cultivate their skills and maintain integrity often find success, akin to the "well-nourished" men.
    • In environmental efforts, communities that unite to protect nature can create abundance, similar to the "rains" that bring prosperity.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of strength, unity, and integrity in both personal and communal endeavors.
    • It serves as a reminder that courage and collaboration can lead to success and abundance in various aspects of life.
    • By embodying these values, individuals and communities can navigate challenges effectively and achieve their goals.
