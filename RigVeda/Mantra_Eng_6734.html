मन्त्र: न हि षस तव नो मम शास्त्रे अन्यस्य रण्यति |यो अस्मान वीर आनयत ||
nahi ṣas tava no mama śāstre anyasya raṇyati | yo asmān vīra ānayat ||
  • Translation: "There is no weapon of yours or mine that can harm another; only he who brings us heroes can prevail."

  • Word Breakdown:
    • nahi: not
    • ṣas: weapon
    • tava: your
    • no: my
    • mama: mine
    • śāstre: in the scriptures or teachings
    • anyasya: of another
    • raṇyati: can harm or injure
    • yo: who
    • asmān: us
    • vīra: hero
    • ānayat: brings or leads

  • Meaning and Explanation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the idea that physical weapons or force are not the ultimate means of achieving victory or success.
    • It suggests that true strength lies in the ability to inspire and lead others, particularly heroes or courageous individuals.
    • The phrase "there is no weapon of yours or mine" indicates that conflict and aggression are not the solutions to problems.
    • Instead, the focus should be on unity and the collective strength that comes from bringing together capable and brave individuals.

  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace setting, a manager who fosters teamwork and collaboration among employees can achieve better results than one who relies solely on authority and control.
    • In community building, leaders who inspire and mobilize people towards a common goal, such as social justice or environmental protection, can create significant change without resorting to conflict.
    • In sports, a coach who motivates and develops the skills of their players can lead the team to victory, demonstrating that leadership and inspiration are more powerful than mere tactics.

  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra teaches us the importance of leadership, inspiration, and unity over conflict and aggression.
    • It is applicable in various aspects of life, including personal relationships, professional environments, and community initiatives.
    • By focusing on bringing out the best in others and fostering a spirit of collaboration, we can achieve greater success and harmony in our lives.
