त्वं रयिम पुरुवीरम् अग्ने दाशुषे मर्ताय | प्र णो नय वस्यो अच्छ ||
tvaṁ rayim puruvīram agne dāśuṣe martāya | pra ṇo naya vasyo accha ||
  • tvaṁ - This word translates to "you," referring to the divine or the deity being addressed, in this case, Agni, the fire god.
  • rayim - This means "wealth" or "prosperity." It signifies the abundance that one seeks in life.
  • puruvīram - This can be interpreted as "of great valor" or "heroic." It emphasizes strength and courage.
  • agne - This directly refers to "Agni," the fire god, who is a central figure in Vedic rituals and represents energy and transformation.
  • dāśuṣe - This word means "to the one who is invoked" or "to the one who is worshipped." It indicates the act of devotion towards Agni.
  • martāya - This translates to "for the mortal" or "for humanity," highlighting the connection between the divine and human beings.
  • pra ṇo - This means "our prayer" or "our invocation," indicating a request or a plea directed towards Agni.
  • naya - This translates to "lead" or "guide," suggesting a desire for direction and support from the divine.
  • vasyo - This means "to be made favorable" or "to be made accessible," indicating a wish for blessings and positive outcomes.
  • accha - This translates to "good" or "well," signifying the desired state of being or outcome.

The mantra can be understood as a prayer to Agni, seeking his guidance and blessings for prosperity, strength, and favorable outcomes in life. It reflects a deep connection between the divine and human existence, emphasizing the importance of invoking divine support in our endeavors.


In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts:

  • Personal Growth: Individuals can recite this mantra during times of challenge, seeking the strength and courage to overcome obstacles.
  • Business Ventures: Entrepreneurs may use this mantra to invoke prosperity and favorable outcomes in their business endeavors.
  • Community Support: In communal gatherings or rituals, this mantra can be recited to seek collective strength and prosperity for the community.

In conclusion, this mantra signifies the importance of seeking divine guidance and blessings in our lives. It serves as a reminder that, while we strive for success and prosperity, invoking the support of the divine can lead to favorable outcomes and a more fulfilling existence. The essence of this mantra is applicable in various aspects of life, encouraging individuals to connect with the divine for strength, guidance, and abundance.
