वि तर्तूर्यन्ते मगवन विपश्चितो 'र्यो विपो जनानाम् |उप क्रमस्व पुरुरूपम् आ भर वाजं नेदिष्टम् ऊतये ||
vi tartūryante maghavan vipaścito 'ryo vipo janānām | upa kramasva pururūpam ā bhara vājaṁ nediṣṭham ūtaye ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • vi: apart, separately
    • tartūryante: they are being drawn or attracted
    • maghavan: O bountiful one (often referring to the divine or a deity)
    • vipaścito: wise, knowledgeable
    • 'ryo: those who are noble or honorable
    • vipo: of the wise
    • janānām: of the people
    • upa: towards, near
    • kramasva: take your position, step forward
    • pururūpam: of many forms, diverse
    • ā: from, out of
    • bhara: fill, bestow
    • vājam: strength, power, or wealth
    • nediṣṭham: the most desirable, the best
    • ūtaye: for the purpose of prosperity or well-being
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the divine, asking for the blessings of abundance and wisdom.
    • It emphasizes the importance of knowledge and nobility among people, suggesting that those who are wise should take initiative and lead.
    • The phrase "pururūpam" indicates the diversity of forms and paths in life, acknowledging that there are many ways to achieve prosperity and well-being.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, this mantra can be applied when leaders encourage their teams to embrace diverse ideas and perspectives to drive innovation.
    • In community service, wise individuals can take the lead in organizing efforts that benefit the community, showcasing the importance of noble actions.
    • In personal development, individuals can reflect on their unique strengths and forms, using them to contribute positively to society.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of wisdom, nobility, and diversity in achieving prosperity.
    • It serves as a reminder that by embracing our unique qualities and taking initiative, we can contribute to the well-being of ourselves and others.
    • In real life, it encourages individuals and communities to work together, leveraging their strengths for collective growth and success.
