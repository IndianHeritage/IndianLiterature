यस्ते स्तनः शशयो यो मयोभूर येन विश्वा पुष्यसि वार्याणि |यो रत्नधा वसुविद यः सुदात्रः सरस्वति तं इह धातवे कः ||
yas te stanaḥ śaśayo yo mayobhūr yena viśvā puṣyasi vāryāṇi | yo ratnadhā vasuvid yaḥ sudatraḥ sarasvati tam iha dhātave kaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yas: who
    • te: your
    • stanaḥ: breast or source
    • śaśayo: of the moon
    • yo: who
    • mayobhūr: who is born of my mind
    • yena: by whom
    • viśvā: all
    • puṣyasi: you nourish
    • vāryāṇi: the best things
    • yo: who
    • ratnadhā: who possesses jewels
    • vasuvid: who knows wealth
    • yaḥ: who
    • sudatraḥ: who is well endowed
    • sarasvati: Sarasvati, the goddess of knowledge
    • tam: him
    • iha: here
    • dhātave: to be given
    • kaḥ: who
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the divine qualities associated with abundance, knowledge, and nourishment.
    • The reference to "your breast of the moon" symbolizes the nurturing aspect of the divine, akin to how the moon influences the tides and growth in nature.
    • The phrase "who is born of my mind" suggests the power of thought and intention in manifesting reality.
    • "By whom all the best things are nourished" emphasizes the importance of divine support in achieving prosperity and well-being.
    • The mention of "possessing jewels" and "knowing wealth" indicates the value of wisdom and material abundance in life.
    • Finally, invoking Sarasvati highlights the significance of knowledge and creativity in attaining success.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In personal development, this mantra can inspire individuals to focus on their intentions and thoughts, leading to positive outcomes in their lives.
    • In business, the principles of nurturing relationships and seeking knowledge can lead to prosperity and success.
    • In education, invoking the blessings of Sarasvati can motivate students to pursue knowledge and creativity, enhancing their learning experiences.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of thought, intention, and divine support in achieving abundance and knowledge.
    • Its significance lies in the encouragement to cultivate a mindset that attracts prosperity and nurtures creativity.
    • In real-life scenarios, it can be applied in personal growth, business practices, and educational pursuits, fostering a holistic approach to success.
