प्रिय तष्टानि मे कपिर व्यक्ता व्य अदूदुषत् |शिरो nv अस्य राविषं न सुगं दुष्कृतं भुवं विश्वस्माद इन्द्र उत्तरः ||
priyā taṣṭāni me kapir vyaktā vy adūduṣat | śiro nv asya rāviṣaṁ na sugaṁ duṣkṛte bhuvaṁ viśvasmād indra uttaraḥ ||
  • Mantra: "priyā taṣṭāni me kapir vyaktā vy adūduṣat | śiro nv asya rāviṣaṁ na sugaṁ duṣkṛte bhuvaṁ viśvasmād indra uttaraḥ ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • priyā: dear, beloved
    • taṣṭāni: those who are established, those who stand firm
    • me: my
    • kapir: the wise one, the clever one
    • vyaktā: manifest, evident
    • vy: not
    • adūduṣat: has not been harmed, has not been wronged
    • śiro: head, mind
    • nv: indeed
    • asya: of this
    • rāviṣaṁ: radiance, brilliance
    • na: not
    • sugaṁ: easy, pleasant
    • duṣkṛte: by evil deeds, by wrong actions
    • bhuvaṁ: in the world, in existence
    • viśvasmād: from all, from the universe
    • indra: the king of gods, a powerful deity
    • uttaraḥ: the best, the highest
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra expresses a plea for protection and guidance from the divine, specifically invoking Indra, the king of gods.
    • The speaker seeks the presence of beloved and wise beings who are established and manifest in their goodness.
    • It emphasizes the importance of maintaining a pure mind and avoiding evil actions, suggesting that a clear and untainted mind leads to a better existence.
    • The mantra reflects a desire for clarity and brilliance in thought, urging the individual to rise above negativity and harmful deeds.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace setting, this mantra can be applied when seeking guidance from mentors or leaders who embody wisdom and integrity.
    • In personal relationships, it encourages individuals to surround themselves with positive influences and to strive for clarity in communication.
    • In moments of decision-making, reflecting on this mantra can help individuals avoid actions that may lead to negative consequences.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the significance of purity in thought and action, and the importance of seeking guidance from wise and benevolent forces.
    • It is applicable in various aspects of life, including personal growth, professional development, and interpersonal relationships.
    • By embodying the principles of this mantra, individuals can cultivate a life that is not only successful but also harmonious and fulfilling.
