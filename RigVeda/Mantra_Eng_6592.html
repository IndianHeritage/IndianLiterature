अघ्नते विष्णवे वयं अरिष्यन्तः सुदानवे |श्रुधि स्वयावन सिन्धो पूर्वचित्तये ||
aghnate viṣṇave vayam ariṣyantaḥ sudānave | śrudhi svayāvan sindho pūrvacittaye ||
  • Mantra: "aghnate viṣṇave vayam ariṣyantaḥ sudānave |
  • śrudhi svayāvan sindho pūrvacittaye ||"

  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • aghnate: To the one who is invincible or powerful.
    • viṣṇave: To Vishnu, the preserver and protector in Hinduism.
    • vayam: We or us.
    • ariṣyantaḥ: Who are free from enemies or obstacles.
    • sudānave: To the one who is well-versed or knowledgeable.
    • śrudhi: Listen or hear.
    • svayāvan: By our own efforts or actions.
    • sindho: Of the ocean or water.
    • pūrvacittaye: For the purpose of the past or previous intentions.

  • Explanation:
    • This mantra invokes the divine qualities of Vishnu, emphasizing the importance of strength and knowledge.
    • The phrase "aghnate viṣṇave" suggests a plea for protection and guidance from Vishnu, who embodies the qualities of preservation and stability.
    • By stating "vayam ariṣyantaḥ," it highlights the aspiration to be free from adversities and challenges, which is a common desire in human life.
    • "sudānave" indicates the importance of wisdom and knowledge in overcoming life's obstacles, akin to how education and experience help individuals navigate challenges.
    • The call to "śrudhi" emphasizes the need to listen and learn, which is crucial in both personal and professional growth.
    • "svayāvan sindho" reflects the idea of self-effort and the vastness of potential, similar to how one must navigate the vast ocean of opportunities in life.
    • Finally, "pūrvacittaye" suggests that our past intentions and actions shape our present, reminding us of the importance of reflection and learning from our experiences.

  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the significance of divine guidance, personal effort, and the pursuit of knowledge in overcoming life's challenges.
    • In real-life scenarios, it can be applied in various contexts such as education, career development, and personal growth, where individuals seek wisdom and strength to navigate their paths.
    • Ultimately, it encourages individuals to reflect on their past actions and intentions, fostering a mindset of continuous learning and improvement.
