ऋतं च सत्यं चाभिद्धात तपसो 'ध्य अजायत |ततो रात्र्य अजायत ततः समुद्रो अर्णवः ||
ṛtaṁ ca satyaṁ cābhīddhāt tapaso 'dhy ajāyata | tato rātry ajāyata tataḥ samudro arṇavaḥ ||
  • ṛtaṁ: This term refers to the cosmic order or truth that governs the universe. It signifies the natural law and the principle of harmony in existence.
  • satyaṁ: Meaning "truth," this word emphasizes the importance of honesty and integrity in both thought and action.
  • cābhīddhāt: This can be interpreted as "from the essence of" or "from the source of," indicating that both ṛta and satya arise from a fundamental source.
  • tapaso: This word means "austerity" or "penance," suggesting that through disciplined effort and self-control, one can attain higher truths.
  • 'dhy: This is a form of the verb "to be," indicating existence or emergence.
  • ajāyata: Meaning "was born" or "came into being," it signifies the manifestation of the universe from the principles of ṛta and satya.
  • tato: This means "thereafter," indicating a sequence of events or a progression in the unfolding of creation.
  • rātry: This translates to "night," symbolizing the unknown or the potential for new beginnings.
  • samudro: Meaning "ocean," it represents vastness, depth, and the collective consciousness of existence.
  • arṇavaḥ: This term refers to "the sea" or "the abyss," often symbolizing the source of life and the depths of the universe.

  • Significance: This mantra encapsulates the essence of creation, emphasizing that the universe is born from the principles of truth and cosmic order. It highlights the importance of discipline and effort in realizing these truths.
  • Real-life Application: In our daily lives, this mantra can inspire us to seek truth and harmony in our actions. For example, in a workplace setting, adhering to ethical practices and fostering a collaborative environment reflects the principles of ṛta and satya. Similarly, personal relationships thrive on honesty and mutual respect, embodying the essence of this mantra.

  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of truth, order, and the universe. By aligning our lives with these principles, we can contribute to a more harmonious existence, both individually and collectively. It encourages us to engage in self-discipline and strive for higher understanding, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling life.
