इह त्या पुरुभूतमा देवाः नमोभिर अश्विनाः |अर्वाचीनाः स्व अवसे करामहे गन्ताराः दाशुषो गृहम् ||
iha tyā purubhūtamā devā namobhir aśvinā | arvācīnā sv avase karāmahe gantārā dāśuṣo gṛham ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • iha: here, in this place
    • tyā: with that, by that
    • purubhūtamā: the most abundant, the one who has many forms
    • devā: gods, divine beings
    • namobhir: with salutations, with reverence
    • aśvinā: O Ashvins, the twin gods of medicine and health
    • arvācīnā: from the past, ancient
    • sv: our, self
    • avase: we invoke, we call upon
    • karāmahe: we perform, we do
    • gantārā: those who lead, the guides
    • dāśuṣo: of the ten, the ten directions
    • gṛham: home, dwelling
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to the Ashvins, the divine twin brothers known for their healing powers and ability to bring prosperity.
    • The invocation emphasizes the importance of reverence and gratitude towards the divine forces that govern health and well-being.
    • It suggests a call to these deities to bless our homes and lives, ensuring abundance and guidance in our endeavors.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern life, this mantra can be recited during health-related rituals or when seeking guidance in difficult situations, such as during medical treatments or family crises.
    • People often invoke divine blessings in their homes, especially during festivals or significant life events, to ensure harmony and prosperity.
    • Healthcare professionals may find inspiration in the Ashvins' attributes, striving to embody their qualities of care and healing in their practice.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine assistance and the recognition of the interconnectedness of health, abundance, and guidance.
    • It serves as a reminder to honor the forces that support our well-being and to actively seek their presence in our lives.
    • In a broader context, it encourages individuals to cultivate a sense of gratitude and reverence for the blessings they receive, fostering a positive and harmonious environment.
