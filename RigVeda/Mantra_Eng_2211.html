नूनं सा ते प्रति वरं जरीत्रे दूहीयद इन्द्र दक्षिणा मघोनी |शिक्षा स्तोत्रिभ्यो माती धग भगो नो बृहद वदेम विदथे सुवीराः ||
nūnaṁ sā te prati varaṁ jaritre duhīyad indra dakṣiṇā maghonī | śikṣā stotṛbhyo māti dhag bhago no bṛhad vadema vidathe suvīrāḥ ||
  • nūnaṁ sā te prati varaṁ - This phrase can be interpreted as "Indeed, that is your excellent gift." It suggests a recognition of the divine blessings that one receives.
  • jaritre duhīyad indra - Here, "Indra" refers to the king of gods, often associated with strength and power. "Jaritre" can mean "in the act of creation" or "in the process of nurturing." This part emphasizes the importance of divine support in our endeavors.
  • dakṣiṇā maghonī - "Dakṣiṇā" refers to offerings or gifts, while "maghonī" relates to wealth or abundance. This line highlights the significance of giving and receiving in the context of prosperity.
  • śikṣā stotṛbhyo - "Śikṣā" means "instruction" or "teaching," and "stotṛbhyo" refers to those who praise or worship. This suggests that learning and reverence are essential for spiritual growth.
  • māti dhag bhago - "Māti" means "intellect" or "wisdom," while "bhago" refers to wealth or prosperity. This line emphasizes the importance of wisdom in achieving success and abundance.
  • no bṛhad vadema - "No" means "we," and "bṛhad" translates to "great" or "vast." This part expresses a collective aspiration for greatness and the pursuit of higher ideals.
  • vidathe suvīrāḥ - "Vidathe" refers to "in the assembly" or "in the gathering," and "suvīrāḥ" means "brave" or "valiant." This line underscores the importance of community and courage in achieving goals.

Conclusion:

This mantra from the Rigveda encapsulates the essence of divine blessings, the importance of wisdom, community, and the pursuit of prosperity. In real life, it can be applied in various scenarios such as:

  • In personal development, recognizing the gifts and support we receive from the universe can motivate us to strive for excellence.
  • In professional settings, the mantra encourages collaboration and the sharing of knowledge, fostering a culture of learning and growth.
  • In community initiatives, it highlights the significance of collective efforts and bravery in overcoming challenges.

Overall, this mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of wisdom, community, and divine support in our journey towards success and fulfillment.
