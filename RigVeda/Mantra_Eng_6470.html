त उग्रासो वृषण उग्रबाहवो नकीष तनूषु यतिरे |स्थिरा धन्वान्य आयुधा रथेषु वो 'नीकेष्व अधि श्रीयः ||
ta ugrāso vṛṣaṇa ugrabāhavo nakiṣ ṭanūṣu yetire | sthirā dhanvāny āyudhā ratheṣu vo 'nīkeṣv adhi śriyaḥ ||
  • ta ugrāso: This phrase can be translated as "those who are powerful" or "those who are fierce." It signifies strength and vigor, qualities that are often admired in leaders and warriors.
  • vṛṣaṇa: This word means "the one who is strong" or "the one who is capable." It emphasizes the importance of capability and resilience in overcoming challenges.
  • ugrabāhavo: This term refers to "those with strong arms" or "those who are mighty." It highlights the physical strength and prowess necessary for success in various endeavors.
  • nakiṣ ṭanūṣu yetire: This can be interpreted as "who move swiftly in their bodies." It suggests agility and the ability to adapt quickly to changing circumstances, which is crucial in both personal and professional life.
  • sthirā dhanvāny: This phrase translates to "those who have firm bows." It symbolizes stability and readiness, indicating that one must be prepared and steadfast in their pursuits.
  • āyudhā: Meaning "with weapons," this word signifies the tools and resources one possesses to achieve their goals. In a modern context, this could refer to skills, knowledge, or technology.
  • ratheṣu: This translates to "in chariots," which can be seen as a metaphor for the journey of life. It emphasizes the importance of having a reliable means to navigate through challenges.
  • vo 'nīkeṣv adhi śriyaḥ: This phrase means "may you achieve glory in your endeavors." It is a blessing for success and recognition in one's efforts, encouraging individuals to strive for excellence.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of strength, agility, preparedness, and the pursuit of glory. In real life, it serves as a reminder to cultivate these qualities in our personal and professional lives. Whether in sports, business, or personal challenges, embodying the attributes of power, capability, and readiness can lead to success and recognition. This mantra encourages individuals to harness their inner strength and strive for excellence in all their endeavors, ultimately leading to a fulfilling and accomplished life.
