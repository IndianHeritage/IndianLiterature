अजैष्माद्यासनाम चाभूमानागसो वयम् |जाग्रत्स्वप्नः संकल्पः पापो यं द्विष्मस्तं स ऋच्छतु यो नो द्वेष्टि तं ऋच्छतु ||
ajaiṣmādyāsanāma cābhūmānāgaso vayam | jāgratsvapnaḥ saṁkalpaḥ pāpo yaṁ dviṣmas taṁ sa ṛcchatu yo no dveṣṭi tam ṛcchatu ||
  • Mantra: "ajaiṣmādyāsanāma cābhūmānāgaso vayam | jāgratsvapnaḥ saṁkalpaḥ pāpo yaṁ dviṣmas taṁ sa ṛcchatu yo no dveṣṭi tam ṛcchatu ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ajaiṣmā: We are not defeated
    • dyāsanāma: By the name of the sky or the heavens
    • cābhūmānāgaso: We are not bound by earthly matters
    • vayam: We
    • jāgratsvapnaḥ: In the waking state of dreams
    • saṁkalpaḥ: The resolve or intention
    • pāpo: Sinful or negative
    • yaṁ: Whom
    • dviṣmas: We hate
    • taṁ: That
    • sa ṛcchatu: May it be removed
    • yo: Who
    • no: Our
    • dveṣṭi: Hates
    • tam ṛcchatu: May it be removed
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra expresses a powerful affirmation of resilience and strength. It emphasizes that we are not defeated by external circumstances or negative influences.
    • The phrase "ajaiṣmā" signifies a declaration of victory and strength, suggesting that we can overcome challenges.
    • "cābhūmānāgaso vayam" indicates a detachment from earthly troubles, encouraging a higher perspective on life’s challenges.
    • The reference to "jāgratsvapnaḥ" suggests that our intentions and dreams can manifest in reality, highlighting the importance of conscious thought and resolve.
    • The latter part of the mantra addresses negativity and hatred, indicating a desire to remove such influences from our lives. It serves as a reminder to focus on positivity and to let go of negative emotions.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, when faced with competition or challenges, one can recite this mantra to reinforce their determination and focus on their goals, rather than being bogged down by negativity.
    • In personal relationships, if there are feelings of resentment or hatred, this mantra can serve as a reminder to release those feelings and foster a more positive environment.
    • During times of stress or anxiety, individuals can use this mantra to cultivate a mindset of resilience, reminding themselves that they are not defined by their circumstances.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra holds significant relevance in today's fast-paced world, where negativity and challenges are prevalent.
    • By affirming our strength and detachment from negativity, we can cultivate a more positive and resilient mindset.
    • It encourages individuals to focus on their intentions and dreams, promoting a proactive approach to life’s challenges.
    • Ultimately, this mantra serves as a powerful tool for personal empowerment and emotional well-being, applicable in various aspects of life.
