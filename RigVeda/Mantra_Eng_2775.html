सत्रासाहं वरेण्यं सहोदां ससवांसाṁ स्वर अपश च देविः |ससाना यः पृथिवीं द्यां उतेमां इन्द्रं मदन्त्य अनु धीरणासः ||
satrāsāhaṁ vareṇyaṁ sahodāṁ sasavāṁsaṁ svar apaś ca devīḥ | sasāna yaḥ pṛthivīṁ dyām utemām indram madanty anu dhīraṇāsaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • satrāsāhaṁ: I am the one who overcomes enemies.
    • vareṇyaṁ: Worthy of worship or reverence.
    • sahodāṁ: Together with brothers or companions.
    • sasavāṁsaṁ: With the essence of life or vitality.
    • svar: The celestial or heavenly realm.
    • apaś: The divine or the gods.
    • ca: And.
    • devīḥ: The goddesses or divine beings.
    • sasāna: He who rules or governs.
    • yaḥ: Who.
    • pṛthivīṁ: The earth.
    • dyām: The sky or heavens.
    • utemām: This or that (referring to the divine or celestial).
    • indram: Indra, the king of gods.
    • madanty: Who brings joy or intoxication.
    • anu: Following or in accordance with.
    • dhīraṇāsaḥ: The wise or the prudent.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the divine power that overcomes adversities and enemies.
    • It emphasizes the importance of unity and companionship in achieving greatness.
    • The reference to Indra signifies strength, leadership, and the ability to bring joy and prosperity.
    • It highlights the connection between the earthly realm and the divine, suggesting that divine support is essential for success.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a team setting, the mantra can be applied to emphasize collaboration and support among team members to overcome challenges.
    • In personal struggles, invoking the essence of this mantra can inspire individuals to seek divine guidance and strength to face adversities.
    • In leadership roles, it serves as a reminder to lead with wisdom and to bring joy and prosperity to those being led.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of divine support, unity, and wisdom in overcoming challenges.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, including teamwork, personal struggles, and leadership.
    • By embodying the principles of this mantra, individuals can cultivate resilience and foster a supportive environment that leads to success and joy.
