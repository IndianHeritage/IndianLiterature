सचस्व नः पराक आ सचस्वास्तमीक आ |पाहि नो दूराद आराद अभिष्टिभिः सदा पाह्य अभिष्टिभिः ||
sacasva naḥ parāka ā sacasvāstamīka ā | pāhi no dūrād ārād abhiṣṭibhiḥ sadā pāhy abhiṣṭibhiḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sacasva: May you be strong or powerful.
    • naḥ: Our.
    • parāka: In the highest or supreme way.
    • ā: O.
    • sacasvāstamīka: May you be endowed with strength.
    • pāhi: Protect or safeguard.
    • no: Us.
    • dūrād: From afar or from a distance.
    • ārād: From the obstacles or difficulties.
    • abhiṣṭibhiḥ: With blessings or auspiciousness.
    • sadā: Always.
    • pāhy: May you protect.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer for strength and protection. It calls upon a higher power to bestow strength upon us and to safeguard us from challenges and adversities.
    • In real life, this can be likened to seeking support during difficult times, such as when facing personal challenges like illness, financial difficulties, or emotional distress. For instance, someone going through a tough phase may recite this mantra to invoke strength and protection.
    • The phrase "from afar" suggests that the protection sought is not just immediate but also from distant threats, which can be interpreted as seeking guidance and strength from the universe or divine forces.
  • Significance:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of inner strength and the need for external support during challenging times.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, such as during personal crises, in professional settings when facing competition, or even in community challenges where collective strength is needed.
    • By reciting this mantra, individuals can cultivate a sense of resilience and hope, reinforcing the belief that they are not alone in their struggles.
