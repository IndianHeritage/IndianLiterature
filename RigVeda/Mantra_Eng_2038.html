ईळितो अग्ने मनसा नो अर्हन् देवान् यक्षि मानवात् पूर्वो अद्य |स आ वह मरुतां शर्धो अच्युतम् इन्द्रं नरो बरहिषदं यजध्वम् ||
īḻito agne manasā no arhan devān yakṣi mānuṣāt pūrvo adya | sa ā vaha marutāṁ śardho acyutam indraṁ naro barhiṣadaṁ yajadhvam ||
  • īḻito agne: This phrase refers to the invocation of Agni, the fire god, who is considered a mediator between humans and the divine. In real life, fire symbolizes transformation and purification, much like how we use fire in rituals or cooking to transform raw ingredients into nourishing meals.
  • manasā no arhan devān: This translates to "with our minds, may we honor the gods." It emphasizes the importance of intention and mindfulness in our actions. For example, when we perform any task, whether at work or in personal life, doing so with a focused and respectful mindset can lead to better outcomes.
  • yakṣi mānuṣāt pūrvo adya: This can be interpreted as "may we be blessed by the divine as we were in the past." It suggests a continuity of blessings and support from the divine. In practical terms, this can be seen in how traditions and values passed down through generations continue to guide us in our daily lives.
  • sa ā vaha marutāṁ: This phrase means "may he bring us the Maruts," referring to the storm gods who represent strength and vitality. In real life, this can be likened to seeking support from friends and family during challenging times, as they provide the strength needed to overcome obstacles.
  • śardho acyutam indraṁ: This translates to "the unchanging Indra," who is the king of the gods and represents power and authority. In our lives, we often look up to leaders or mentors who embody strength and wisdom, guiding us through our personal and professional journeys.
  • naro barhiṣadaṁ yajadhvam: This phrase means "let us worship the divine with our offerings." It highlights the importance of gratitude and giving back. In real life, this can be seen in acts of charity or community service, where we contribute to the well-being of others as a form of worship.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking divine blessings through mindful actions, honoring traditions, and fostering community support. Its significance lies in the reminder that our intentions and actions can create a positive impact in our lives and the lives of others. In real life, this mantra encourages us to approach our daily tasks with respect, to seek strength from our relationships, and to give back to our communities, ultimately leading to a more harmonious existence.
