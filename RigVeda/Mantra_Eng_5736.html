आ गुमता नासत्याऽ रथेनाश्वावतā पुरुषचन्द्रेण यातम् |अभि वां विश्व नियुतः साचन्ते स्पार्हया श्रिया तन्वा शुभाना ||
ā gomatā nāsatyā rathenāśvāvatā puruścandreṇa yātam | abhi vāṁ viśvā niyutaḥ sacante spārhayā śriyā tanvā śubhānā ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā gomatā: "from the one who possesses wealth" - refers to a person or entity that is rich in resources or virtues.
    • nāsatyā: "O Nāsatyas" - Nāsatyas are the twin deities in Vedic mythology, often associated with healing and truth.
    • rathenā: "by the chariot" - symbolizes movement, progress, or journey in life.
    • āśvāvatā: "with horses" - represents strength, speed, and the power to overcome obstacles.
    • puruścandreṇa: "by the man who is like the moon" - signifies a person of great beauty, wisdom, or calmness.
    • yātam: "may we go" - a call for guidance and direction in our journey.
  • Contextual Meaning:
    • This mantra invokes the blessings of the Nāsatyas, asking for guidance and support in our endeavors.
    • The imagery of a chariot and horses suggests a journey filled with challenges, where one seeks strength and wisdom to navigate through life.
    • The reference to a man like the moon indicates the importance of having a calm and wise presence in our lives, guiding us through difficulties.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be applied when embarking on a new project. The wealth represents resources, the chariot symbolizes the project itself, and the horses signify the team working together.
    • In personal life, when facing challenges, one can seek the qualities of the Nāsatyas—truth and healing—to find solutions and maintain integrity.
    • In education, students can invoke this mantra for guidance in their studies, seeking wisdom and clarity to navigate their academic journey.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking divine guidance and support in our journeys, whether personal or professional.
    • It highlights the need for strength, wisdom, and resources to overcome challenges and achieve success.
    • In essence, it serves as a reminder to remain grounded in truth and integrity while pursuing our goals, making it applicable in various aspects of life.
