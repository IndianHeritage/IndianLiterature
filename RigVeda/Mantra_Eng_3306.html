वृषा वृषन्धिं चतुरश्रिम अस्यन्न उग्रो बाहुभ्यां नृतमः शचिवान् | श्रीये परुष्णीम् उषमाण ऊर्णां यस्याः पर्वाणि सख्याय विव्ये ||
vṛṣā vṛṣandhiṁ caturaśrim asyann ugro bāhubhyāṁ nṛtamaḥ śacīvān | śriye paruṣṇīm uṣamāṇa ūrṇāṁ yasyāḥ parvāṇi sakhyāya vivye ||
  • vṛṣā vṛṣandhiṁ: This phrase can be interpreted as "the strong and powerful." In real life, this can refer to individuals or entities that exhibit strength and resilience, such as athletes or leaders who overcome challenges.
  • caturaśrim: This term signifies "the one who possesses fourfold prosperity." In practical terms, it can relate to a balanced life that includes wealth, health, knowledge, and relationships. For example, a successful entrepreneur who maintains a healthy work-life balance embodies this concept.
  • asyann ugro bāhubhyāṁ: Translated, this means "with strong arms." This can symbolize the power of teamwork and collaboration. In a workplace, when colleagues come together with their strengths, they can achieve remarkable results, much like a sports team working together to win a game.
  • nṛtamaḥ śacīvān: This phrase can be understood as "the best among men, endowed with strength." It highlights the importance of moral and ethical strength in leadership. A real-life example could be a community leader who stands up for justice and inspires others to do the same.
  • śriye paruṣṇīm: This translates to "the one who brings prosperity." In everyday life, this could refer to individuals who contribute positively to their communities, such as philanthropists or volunteers who work to improve the lives of others.
  • uṣamāṇa ūrṇāṁ: This can be interpreted as "the one who brings forth abundance." This concept is applicable in various scenarios, such as farmers who cultivate the land to produce food for society, thereby ensuring sustenance and abundance for all.
  • yasyāḥ parvāṇi sakhyāya vivye: This phrase means "whose qualities are shared among friends." It emphasizes the importance of friendship and community. In real life, this can be seen in how friends support each other through difficult times, showcasing the strength of their bond.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of strength, prosperity, and community. It signifies the importance of collaboration, ethical leadership, and the sharing of resources and qualities among individuals. In real life, these principles can be applied in various contexts, such as in workplaces, communities, and personal relationships, fostering a sense of unity and collective growth. By embodying these values, individuals can contribute to a more harmonious and prosperous society.
