पूर्वीर अस्य निष्सिधो मार्त्येषु पुरू वसूनि पृथिवी बिभर्ति |इन्द्राय द्यावा ओषधीर उतापो रयिं रक्षन्ति जीरयो वनानि ||
pūrvīr asya niṣṣidho martyeṣu purū vasūni pṛthivī bibharti | indrāya dyāva oṣadhīr utāpo rayiṁ rakṣanti jīrayo vanāni ||
  • pūrvīr asya niṣṣidho: This phrase can be translated as "the ancient ones are not obstructed." It suggests that the wisdom and knowledge of the past are always accessible and can guide us in our present lives.
  • martyeṣu purū vasūni: This means "the many treasures are present among mortals." It indicates that humans possess various resources and potentials that can be harnessed for growth and prosperity.
  • pṛthivī bibharti: Translated as "the Earth supports," this part emphasizes the nurturing aspect of nature, highlighting how the Earth provides sustenance and resources for all living beings.
  • indrāya dyāva: This phrase refers to "Indra, the lord of the heavens." Indra symbolizes strength and power, representing the divine forces that protect and guide humanity.
  • oṣadhīr utāpo: This translates to "the herbs and plants rise." It signifies the importance of nature and its healing properties, reminding us of the value of natural resources in our lives.
  • rayiṁ rakṣanti: Meaning "they protect wealth," this part highlights the significance of safeguarding our resources and wealth, both material and spiritual.
  • jīrayo vanāni: This translates to "the forests that are alive." It emphasizes the vitality of nature and the interconnectedness of all living beings within ecosystems.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the relationship between humanity, nature, and the divine. It signifies the importance of respecting ancient wisdom, recognizing the treasures within ourselves, and valuing the Earth as a source of sustenance. In real life, this mantra can inspire individuals to seek knowledge from the past, utilize their inherent potential, and protect the environment. It serves as a reminder that by harmonizing with nature and acknowledging the divine forces, we can achieve prosperity and well-being in our lives.
