यस्त इन्द्र महीर अपः स्तभूयमान आशयत् |नि तं पद्यासु शिष्णथः ||
yas ta indra mahīr apaḥ stabhūyamāna āśayat | ni tam padyāsu śiśnathaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yas: who
    • ta: that
    • indra: Indra, the king of gods
    • mahīr: of the great
    • apaḥ: waters
    • stabhūyamāna: being held or restrained
    • āśayat: dwelling or residing
    • ni: not
    • tam: that
    • padyāsu: in the paths
    • śiśnathaḥ: you may know or understand
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the divine presence of Indra, who is associated with strength and power.
    • The phrase "mahīr apaḥ" refers to the great waters, symbolizing abundance and life.
    • The idea of "stabhūyamāna" suggests a state of being held back or restrained, which can be interpreted as the challenges or obstacles one faces in life.
    • The mantra encourages individuals to seek understanding and clarity in their paths, despite the challenges they may encounter.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, an employee may face obstacles in their career advancement. This mantra can serve as a reminder to seek guidance and clarity from mentors (symbolized by Indra) to navigate these challenges.
    • In personal relationships, individuals may experience misunderstandings or conflicts. The mantra encourages open communication and understanding to overcome these hurdles.
    • In times of uncertainty, such as during a health crisis, individuals can draw strength from the idea of divine support and the abundance of resources available to them.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking divine guidance and understanding in the face of life's challenges.
    • It serves as a reminder that even when faced with obstacles, one can find strength and clarity through introspection and seeking support.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied to various aspects of life, including career, relationships, and personal growth, encouraging individuals to remain resilient and open to understanding.
