रथो न यातः शिक्वभिः कृतो dyām अङ्गेभिर अरुशेभिर ईयते |आद अस्य ते कृष्णासो दक्षिण सूरयः शूरस्येव त्वेषथाद ईषते वयः ||
ratho na yātaḥ śikvabhiḥ kṛto dyām aṅgebhir aruṣebhir īyate | ād asya te kṛṣṇāso dakṣi sūrayaḥ śūrasyeva tveṣathād īṣate vayaḥ ||
  • Mantra: "ratho na yātaḥ śikvabhiḥ kṛto dyām aṅgebhir aruṣebhir īyate | ād asya te kṛṣṇāso dakṣi sūrayaḥ śūrasyeva tveṣathād īṣate vayaḥ ||"
  • Translation: This mantra speaks of the chariot (ratha) that moves swiftly, driven by the powerful forces (śikvabhiḥ) that have been created. It describes how the sky (dyām) is traversed by the vibrant energies (aruṣebhir) and how the strong (sūrayaḥ) are guided by the divine (kṛṣṇāso) to achieve their goals.
  • Word Breakdown:
    • Ratha: Chariot, symbolizing the journey of life and the means to achieve one's goals.
    • Yātaḥ: To move or travel, indicating progress and movement in life.
    • Śikvabhiḥ: Forces or energies that propel us forward.
    • Dyām: The sky, representing the vast possibilities and aspirations.
    • Aṅgebhir: Energies or elements that are vibrant and dynamic.
    • Aruṣebhir: The bright or radiant forces that illuminate our path.
    • Kṛṣṇāso: Divine guidance or support.
    • Sūrayaḥ: The strong or courageous individuals who strive for success.
    • Śūrasyeva: Like the brave, indicating the qualities of valor and determination.
    • Vayaḥ: Age or time, suggesting the importance of timing in our endeavors.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, the mantra can be likened to a team working together (the chariot) to achieve a common goal, where each member's strengths (energies) contribute to the success of the project.
    • In personal development, it reflects the journey of self-improvement, where one harnesses their inner strengths and seeks guidance (divine support) to overcome challenges.
    • In sports, athletes embody the spirit of this mantra as they train hard (energies) and rely on their coaches (divine guidance) to reach their peak performance (success).
  • Conclusion: This mantra emphasizes the importance of movement, strength, and divine guidance in our lives. It teaches us that while we may have the power to drive our own chariots, the support and energies we harness from our surroundings and the divine can significantly enhance our journey. In real life, it encourages us to seek collaboration, embrace our strengths, and remain open to guidance as we navigate through various challenges and aspirations.
