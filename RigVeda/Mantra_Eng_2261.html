तस्मा इद् विश्वे धुनयन्त सिंधवोऽच्छिद्राः शर्म दधिरे पुरूणि |देवानां सुम्ने सुभगः स एधते यं-यं युजं कृत्नुते ब्रह्मणस् पतिः ||
tasmā id viśve dhunayanta sindhavo 'cchidrā śarma dadhire purūṇi | devānāṁ sumne subhagaḥ sa edhate yaṁ-yaṁ yujaṁ kṛṇute brahmaṇas patiḥ ||
  • tasmā id viśve dhunayanta sindhavo: This phrase can be translated as "Therefore, all the rivers, flowing together, bring forth." Here, the rivers symbolize the collective efforts of nature and humanity working in harmony. In real life, this can be likened to teamwork in organizations where diverse talents come together to achieve a common goal.
  • 'cchidrā śarma dadhire purūṇi: This translates to "They hold the unbroken shelter of the ancient." This suggests that there is a protective and nurturing aspect to the ancient wisdom and traditions that guide us. For example, family traditions often provide a sense of security and identity, much like the ancient wisdom referred to in this mantra.
  • devānāṁ sumne subhagaḥ sa edhate: This means "The auspicious one among the gods is the one who is invoked." It emphasizes the importance of invoking positive energies and divine blessings in our endeavors. In practical terms, this can be seen in rituals or prayers before starting significant tasks, such as a wedding or a new business venture, where people seek divine favor for success.
  • yaṁ-yaṁ yujaṁ kṛṇute brahmaṇas patiḥ: This translates to "Whichever path the Lord of creation chooses." It highlights the idea of surrendering to a higher power and trusting in the divine plan. In everyday life, this can be reflected in moments of uncertainty where individuals must trust their intuition or faith when making decisions, such as choosing a career path or navigating personal challenges.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of unity, protection, divine invocation, and trust in a higher power. Its significance lies in the reminder that we are part of a larger whole, and by working together, respecting traditions, seeking divine guidance, and trusting the journey, we can navigate life's complexities. In real-life scenarios, this mantra encourages collaboration in communities, reliance on family values, and the importance of spiritual practices in achieving personal and collective goals.
