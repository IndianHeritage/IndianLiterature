ऐषु द्यावापृथिवी धातम् महद अस्मे वीरेषु विश्वचर्षणि श्रवः | पृक्षं वाजस्य सातये पृक्षं रायत तुर्वणे ||
aiṣu dyāvāpṛthivī dhātam mahad asme vīreṣu viśvacarṣaṇi śravaḥ | pṛkṣaṁ vājasya sātaye pṛkṣaṁ rāyota turvaṇe ||
  • aiṣu - This word can be interpreted as "may we attain" or "let us achieve." It signifies a desire for accomplishment and success.
  • dyāvāpṛthivī - This refers to "heaven and earth," symbolizing the duality of existence and the balance between the spiritual and material worlds.
  • dhātam - Meaning "to establish" or "to support," this word emphasizes the importance of a strong foundation in life.
  • mahad - Translated as "great" or "mighty," it signifies the pursuit of greatness and the aspiration to achieve significant goals.
  • asme - This means "to us," indicating a collective aspiration or desire for the community or group.
  • vīreṣu - This word translates to "in the heroes" or "among the brave," highlighting the importance of courage and valor in achieving one's goals.
  • viśvacarṣaṇi - Meaning "universal energy" or "cosmic power," it represents the interconnectedness of all beings and the energy that drives the universe.
  • śravaḥ - This translates to "fame" or "glory," indicating the desire for recognition and respect in society.
  • pṛkṣaṁ - Meaning "to strive" or "to seek," it emphasizes the importance of effort and determination in achieving one's goals.
  • vājasya - This word means "of strength" or "of power," signifying the importance of inner strength and resilience.
  • sātaye - Translated as "for the purpose of," it indicates a goal-oriented approach to life.
  • rāyota - This means "prosperity" or "wealth," highlighting the aspiration for abundance and success in material aspects of life.
  • turvaṇe - This word signifies "to the one who is strong" or "to the powerful," emphasizing the importance of strength in achieving one's desires.

In conclusion, this mantra from the Rigveda encapsulates a profound aspiration for greatness, strength, and recognition in both personal and communal contexts. It emphasizes the importance of striving for success while maintaining a balance between the spiritual and material aspects of life. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various situations, such as in personal development, leadership, and teamwork. By embodying the qualities of courage, determination, and a strong foundation, individuals and communities can work towards achieving their goals and attaining prosperity. The mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of all beings and the universal energy that drives our aspirations.
