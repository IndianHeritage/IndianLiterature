वंस्वा नो वार्याऽपुरु वंस्व रायः पुरुस्पृहः |सुवीर्यस्य प्रजावतः यशस्वतः ||
vaṁsvā no vāryā puru vaṁsva rāyaḥ puruspṛhaḥ | suvīryasya prajāvato yaśasvataḥ ||
  • vaṁsvā no vāryā puru vaṁsva rāyaḥ puruspṛhaḥ
    • vaṁsvā: This term refers to "strength" or "vigor." It signifies the essence of vitality and robustness.
    • no: This is a pronoun meaning "our," indicating a collective or communal aspect.
    • vāryā: This word translates to "protection" or "defense," suggesting the need for safeguarding and security.
    • puru: Meaning "many" or "abundant," it emphasizes the idea of plurality and richness.
    • vaṁsva: This can be interpreted as "lineage" or "heritage," connecting to the idea of ancestry and cultural roots.
    • rāyaḥ: This term means "wealth" or "prosperity," indicating the importance of material and spiritual abundance.
    • puruspṛhaḥ: This translates to "desire for many," reflecting the aspirations and ambitions of individuals or communities.
  • suvīryasya prajāvato yaśasvataḥ
    • suvīryasya: This term means "of the strong" or "of the valiant," highlighting the qualities of courage and strength.
    • prajāvato: This translates to "of the progeny" or "of the offspring," emphasizing the importance of lineage and the continuation of life.
    • yaśasvataḥ: This word means "of fame" or "of glory," indicating the pursuit of honor and recognition in society.

Conclusion:

The mantra encapsulates the essence of strength, protection, lineage, and the pursuit of prosperity and glory. It emphasizes the importance of community and the collective aspirations of individuals. In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios such as:

  • Community Building: It encourages individuals to come together, fostering a sense of unity and shared purpose.
  • Family Values: The emphasis on lineage and progeny highlights the importance of family and the values passed down through generations.
  • Personal Aspirations: The desire for strength and prosperity can motivate individuals to strive for their goals, whether in career, education, or personal development.
  • Social Responsibility: The call for protection and defense can inspire individuals to engage in social causes, advocating for the welfare of their communities.

Overall, this mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of strength, heritage, and the pursuit of a prosperous and honorable life.
