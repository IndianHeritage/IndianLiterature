त्वं अग्नि वानुष्यतो न पाही त्वं उ नः सहसावन्न अवद्यāt |सं त्वा ध्वस्मन्वद अभ्य एतु पाठः सं रयिः स्पृहयाय्यः सहस्री ||
tvam agne vanuṣyato ni pāhi tvam u naḥ sahasāvann avadyāt | saṁ tvā dhvasmanvad abhy etu pāthaḥ saṁ rayiḥ spṛhayāyyaḥ sahasrī ||
  • tvam agne - "You, O Agni" - This refers to Agni, the fire god, who is a central figure in Vedic rituals and symbolizes energy, transformation, and purification.
  • vanuṣyato ni pāhi - "protect us from all that is harmful" - This is a plea for protection from negative influences and dangers, emphasizing the need for safety and security in life.
  • tvam u naḥ sahasāvann avadyāt - "may you grant us abundant wealth and prosperity" - This line expresses a desire for material and spiritual abundance, highlighting the importance of prosperity in human life.
  • saṁ tvā dhvasmanvad abhy etu pāthaḥ - "may our paths be illuminated by your light" - This signifies the need for guidance and clarity in our journeys, both literal and metaphorical.
  • saṁ rayiḥ spṛhayāyyaḥ sahasrī - "may we be blessed with countless riches" - This reinforces the aspiration for wealth and success, indicating that abundance is a shared goal.

  • Significance: This mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking divine guidance and protection while aspiring for prosperity and abundance in life. It reflects the human desire for safety, wealth, and clarity in one's path.
  • Real-life Application: In modern life, this mantra can be invoked during times of uncertainty or when embarking on new ventures, such as starting a business or moving to a new place. It serves as a reminder to seek guidance and protection from higher powers while striving for success.

  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the interconnectedness of spiritual and material aspirations. By seeking divine protection and guidance, individuals can navigate life's challenges more effectively, leading to a fulfilling and prosperous existence.
