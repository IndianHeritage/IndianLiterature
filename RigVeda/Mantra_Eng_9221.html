आ वाम अगन सुमतिर वाजिनीवासू न अश्विनाः हृत्सु कामाः अयंषत् | अभूतं गोपा मिथुना शुभस पतिः प्रियाः आर्यम्णो दुर्यां अशीमहि ||
ā vām agan sumatir vājinīvasū ny aśvinā hṛtsu kāmā ayaṁsata | abhūtaṁ gopā mithunā śubhas patī priyā aryamṇo duryām̐ aśīmahi ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā vām: From you both
    • agan: We have come
    • sumatir: With good thoughts
    • vājinīvasū: Like the horses of the sun
    • ny aśvinā: O Ashvins (the twin deities)
    • hṛtsu: In our hearts
    • kāmā: Desires
    • ayaṁsata: May we attain
    • abhūtaṁ: That which is not
    • gopā: The protector
    • mithunā: In pairs
    • śubhas: Auspicious
    • patī: Lord
    • priyā: Beloved
    • aryamṇo: Of the noble
    • duryām̐: Difficult to attain
    • aśīmahi: We invoke
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to the divine twin deities, the Ashvins, who are often associated with health, vitality, and the blessings of life.
    • The invocation begins with a call to the Ashvins, asking for their presence and assistance in achieving good thoughts and desires.
    • The phrase "like the horses of the sun" symbolizes speed, strength, and the ability to overcome obstacles, suggesting that the blessings sought are powerful and swift.
    • The mantra emphasizes the importance of having auspicious and noble intentions in our hearts, which aligns with the idea that our thoughts and desires shape our reality.
    • The concluding lines highlight the aspiration to attain that which is difficult, invoking the protection and guidance of the divine in our endeavors.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, this mantra can be applied when seeking clarity and good intentions in decision-making. For instance, a team may recite this mantra before a crucial meeting to align their thoughts and intentions.
    • In personal relationships, invoking the Ashvins can serve as a reminder to approach interactions with love and respect, fostering a nurturing environment.
    • During challenging times, individuals may reflect on this mantra to inspire resilience and the pursuit of noble goals, reminding themselves that with divine support, they can overcome difficulties.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the importance of good thoughts, noble intentions, and the pursuit of auspicious outcomes in life.
    • Its significance lies in the belief that divine support is available to those who seek it with sincerity and clarity of purpose.
    • In real-life scenarios, it encourages individuals to cultivate positive thoughts and intentions, fostering a harmonious and prosperous existence.
