अग्न ओजिष्टम् आ भर द्युम्नम् अस्मभ्यम अध्रिगो |प्र नो रायाऽ परीनसā रत्सि वाजाय पन्थाम् ||
agna ojiṣṭham ā bhara dyumnam asmabhyam adhrigo | pra no rāyā parīṇasā ratsi vājāya panthām ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • agna: O Fire
    • ojiṣṭham: the most powerful
    • ā bhara: may you bring
    • dyumnam: glory or brilliance
    • asmabhyam: to us
    • adhirigo: the one who is worthy
    • pra no: for us
    • rāyā: wealth or prosperity
    • parīṇasā: with abundance
    • ratsi: may you protect
    • vājāya: for the one who is strong
    • panthām: the path
  • Overall Meaning: This mantra is a prayer to Agni, the fire god, asking for strength, glory, and prosperity. It emphasizes the importance of fire as a purifying and transformative force in life. The invocation seeks blessings for abundance and protection on the path of life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • Invocation of Strength: In challenging situations, such as during exams or competitions, individuals often seek inner strength and clarity. This mantra can be recited to invoke the energy of Agni, symbolizing the fire within that drives one to succeed.
    • Celebration of Achievements: During festivals or significant life events, lighting a fire or a candle can symbolize the glory and brilliance one wishes to achieve. This aligns with the mantra's call for glory and brilliance in our lives.
    • Seeking Prosperity: In business or personal endeavors, individuals may recite this mantra to attract wealth and abundance, reflecting the desire for prosperity in their ventures.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its holistic approach to life, emphasizing the need for strength, glory, and prosperity. It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal challenges to communal celebrations. By invoking Agni, individuals can align themselves with the transformative power of fire, seeking to enhance their lives with strength and abundance. This mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of our desires and the divine forces that can aid us in achieving them.
