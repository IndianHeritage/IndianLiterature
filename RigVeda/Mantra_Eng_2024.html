अभि त्वा नक्तीर उषसो ववाशिरे 'ग्ने वत्सं न स्वसरेषु धेनवः |दिव इवेद अरतिर मानवश युगाः क्षपो भासि पूर्वार संयतः ||
abhi tvā naktīr uṣaso vavāśire 'gne vatsaṁ na svasareṣu dhenavaḥ | diva ived aratir mānuṣā yugā kṣapo bhāsi puruvāra saṁyataḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • abhi: towards
    • tvā: you
    • naktīr: nights
    • uṣaso: dawns
    • vavāśire: have come forth
    • 'gne: O Agni (the fire god)
    • vatsaṁ: calf
    • na: not
    • svasareṣu: in the mothers
    • dhenavaḥ: cows
    • diva: in the sky
    • ived: indeed
    • aratir: the path
    • mānuṣā: of humans
    • yugā: ages
    • kṣapo: of the twilight
    • bhāsi: shines
    • puruvāra: of the ancient
    • saṁyataḥ: controlled
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes Agni, the fire god, symbolizing the divine light and energy that brings forth the dawn and dispels the darkness of night.
    • The phrase "abhi tvā naktīr uṣaso vavāśire" suggests a reverence towards the arrival of dawn and the light it brings, indicating a transition from darkness to light, both literally and metaphorically.
    • The mention of "vatsaṁ na svasareṣu dhenavaḥ" can be interpreted as a reflection on nurturing and protection, akin to how a mother cow cares for her calf, symbolizing the importance of care and sustenance in life.
    • "Diva ived aratir mānuṣā" emphasizes the human journey through various ages, highlighting the significance of guidance and the paths we choose in life.
    • The concluding part "kṣapo bhāsi puruvāra saṁyataḥ" suggests that the light of knowledge and wisdom shines through the ages, controlled and directed towards a higher purpose.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, the transition from night to dawn can symbolize new beginnings, such as starting a new job or embarking on a new project, where one seeks the guidance of wisdom (Agni) to illuminate the path ahead.
    • The nurturing aspect of the mantra can be seen in parenting, where caregivers provide love and support to their children, ensuring they grow and thrive, much like the mother cow with her calf.
    • The concept of choosing paths in life resonates with individuals making decisions about their careers, relationships, or personal growth, where the wisdom of past experiences guides their choices.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking light and guidance in life, emphasizing the importance of nurturing, wisdom, and the choices we make throughout our journey.
    • Its significance lies in the reminder that, like the dawn that follows the night, every challenge can lead to new opportunities, and with the right guidance, we can navigate through life's complexities.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra encourages individuals to embrace change, seek knowledge, and nurture relationships, fostering a harmonious existence in both personal and communal spheres.
