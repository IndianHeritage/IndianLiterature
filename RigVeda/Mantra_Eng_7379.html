तवे वसूनि संगता विश्वा च सोम सौभागा |सुदात्व अपरीहृतā ||
tve vasūni saṁgatā viśvā ca soma saubhagā | sudātv aparihvṛtā ||
  • tve - This word translates to "to you" or "for you," indicating a direction of reverence or devotion towards a divine entity.
  • vasūni - Refers to "wealth" or "prosperity." In the context of the mantra, it signifies the abundance and blessings that one seeks.
  • saṁgatā - This means "gathered" or "assembled." It suggests a coming together of energies or forces, which can be interpreted as unity in purpose or community.
  • viśvā - Translates to "all" or "universal." It emphasizes inclusivity and the interconnectedness of all beings.
  • ca - A simple conjunction meaning "and," linking the ideas presented in the mantra.
  • soma - Refers to the sacred drink in Vedic tradition, symbolizing nourishment, immortality, and divine inspiration.
  • saubhagā - This word means "auspicious" or "fortunate," indicating the blessings of good fortune and success.
  • sudātv - Translates to "well-being" or "goodness," suggesting a state of health and prosperity.
  • aparihṛtā - Means "unobstructed" or "unhindered," indicating a flow of positive energy and blessings that are not blocked by negativity or obstacles.

The mantra can be interpreted as a prayer or invocation for prosperity, unity, and well-being. It expresses a desire for the divine to bestow wealth and auspiciousness upon all beings, emphasizing the importance of community and collective good.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios:

  • Community Gatherings: When people come together for a common purpose, such as festivals or social events, invoking this mantra can enhance the sense of unity and shared prosperity.
  • Business Ventures: Entrepreneurs can recite this mantra to attract success and good fortune in their endeavors, fostering a collaborative spirit among team members.
  • Personal Well-being: Individuals can use this mantra as a daily affirmation to invite positivity and abundance into their lives, focusing on health and happiness.

Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for collective prosperity and well-being. It serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of all beings and the importance of seeking blessings not just for oneself, but for the community as a whole. By embodying the principles of unity, abundance, and auspiciousness, individuals can create a more harmonious and prosperous environment in their personal and communal lives.
