तं देवासो जुषेरत विश्वे अद्य सजोषसः |तथा राजानां करथो यद ईमहा ऋतावानां यद ईमहे ||
taṁ devāso juṣerata viśve adya sajoṣasaḥ | tathā rājānā karatho yad īmaha ṛtāvānā yad īmahe ||
  • taṁ devāso juṣerata: This phrase can be translated as "May the gods be pleased with us." It emphasizes the importance of seeking divine favor and blessings in our endeavors. In real life, this can be seen in various cultures where people pray or perform rituals to seek blessings from higher powers before starting significant tasks, such as a new business or a journey.
  • viśve adya sajoṣasaḥ: This translates to "All the gods, today, are united." It signifies the idea of unity among divine forces. In a contemporary context, this can be related to the concept of teamwork and collaboration, where individuals come together for a common goal, much like how different departments in a company work together to achieve success.
  • tathā rājānā karatho: This means "Thus, may the kings (or leaders) act." It highlights the role of leadership and governance. In today's world, this can be applied to the responsibilities of leaders in various sectors, such as politics, business, or community organizations, where their actions significantly impact the lives of others.
  • yad īmaha ṛtāvānā: This translates to "What we desire, may it be fulfilled." It reflects the human aspiration for achieving goals and desires. In real life, this can be seen in personal ambitions, such as pursuing education, career goals, or personal development, where individuals strive to fulfill their dreams.
  • yad īmahe: This phrase means "What we seek." It emphasizes the importance of intention and focus in our pursuits. In practical terms, this can relate to setting clear goals in life, whether in personal relationships, career paths, or spiritual journeys, and actively working towards them.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking divine blessings, unity, leadership, and the fulfillment of desires. Its significance lies in the reminder that our actions should be aligned with higher principles and that collaboration and intention are key to achieving our goals. In real life, this mantra can inspire individuals and communities to work together harmoniously, seek guidance from higher powers, and strive for their aspirations with clarity and purpose.
