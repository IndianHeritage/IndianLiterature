नū मर्तो दयते सनीष्यन् यो विष्णव उरुगायाय दाशत् |प्र यः सत्राच मनसा यजाता एतावन्तं नार्यम आविवासात् ||
nū marto dayate saniṣyan yo viṣṇava urugāyāya dāśat | pra yaḥ satrācā manasā yajāta etāvantaṁ naryam āvivāsāt ||
  • nū marto dayate saniṣyan: This phrase can be translated to "Indeed, the mortal who is steadfast." Here, "marto" refers to a human being or mortal, and "saniṣyan" implies being firm or steadfast. In real life, this can be related to individuals who remain resolute in their beliefs and actions despite challenges.
  • yo viṣṇava urugāyāya dāśat: This translates to "who worships the great Vishnu." "Viṣṇava" refers to the deity Vishnu, who is often associated with preservation and protection. "Urugāyāya" means one who is of great renown or glory. This can be seen in the context of people who dedicate their lives to spiritual practices or community service, embodying the qualities of Vishnu.
  • pra yaḥ satrācā manasā yajāta: This means "may he who is devoted to the sacrifice with his mind." "Satrācā" refers to sacrifice or offerings, which are central to many spiritual traditions. This can be exemplified by individuals who engage in selfless acts for the benefit of others, such as volunteering or charitable work.
  • etāvantaṁ naryam āvivāsāt: This translates to "may he attain the highest state of humanity." "Naryam" refers to the highest qualities of a human being, and "āvivāsāt" implies achieving or realizing. This can be related to personal growth and the pursuit of excellence in one’s character and actions, such as striving for integrity, compassion, and wisdom.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of steadfastness, devotion, and selfless action in the pursuit of a higher state of being. It signifies that by embodying these qualities, individuals can attain a more profound understanding of their humanity and contribute positively to society. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various contexts, such as in leadership, community service, and personal development, where individuals strive to be resilient, compassionate, and dedicated to the greater good.
