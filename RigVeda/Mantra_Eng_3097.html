कथा ह तद वरुणाय त्वम् अग्ने कथा दिवे गर्हसे कन न आगः |कथा मित्राय मीळ्हुसे पृथिव्यै ब्रवः कद आर्यम्णे कद भगाय ||
kathā ha tad varuṇāya tvam agne kathā dive garhase kan na āgaḥ | kathā mitrāya mīḻhuṣe pṛthivyai bravaḥ kad aryamṇe kad bhagāya ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • Kathā: How
    • Ha: Indeed
    • Tad: That
    • Varuṇāya: To Varuna (the god of water and cosmic order)
    • Tvam: You
    • Agne: O Agni (the fire god)
    • Kathā: How
    • Dive: In the sky
    • Garhase: You are praised
    • Kan: Who
    • Na: Not
    • Āgaḥ: To be approached
    • Kathā: How
    • Mitrāya: To Mitra (the god of friendship and contracts)
    • Mīḻhuṣe: You are pleasing
    • Pṛthivyai: To the Earth
    • Bravaḥ: Speak
    • Kad: When
    • Aryamṇe: To Aryaman (the god of social order)
    • Kad: When
    • Bhagāya: To the blessed one
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a call to the deities Varuna, Agni, and Mitra, seeking their guidance and blessings.
    • The repetition of "kathā" emphasizes the quest for understanding and clarity in life.
    • It reflects the interconnectedness of natural elements (fire, water, earth) and their divine representations.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, individuals often seek guidance from higher powers or principles when faced with challenges, similar to how the mantra seeks divine insight.
    • In a community setting, invoking the spirit of friendship and cooperation (Mitra) can lead to harmonious relationships, akin to the mantra's call for unity.
    • Environmental awareness can be linked to the reverence for Earth (Pṛthivyai), encouraging sustainable practices that honor the planet.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of seeking wisdom and guidance from the divine in various aspects of life.
    • It highlights the need for harmony between humans and nature, as well as among individuals in society.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire individuals to foster relationships, respect the environment, and seek clarity in their actions and decisions.
