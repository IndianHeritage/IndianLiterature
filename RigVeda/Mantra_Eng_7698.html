तं īं हिन्वन्त्य अग्रुवो धामन्ति बाकुरं दृतिम् |त्रिधातु वारणं मधु ||
tam īṁ hinvanty agruvo dhamanti bākuraṁ dṛtim | tridhātu vāraṇam madhu ||
  • tam: This word translates to "that" or "him," referring to a divine entity or force.
  • īṁ: This is a sound or syllable often associated with spiritual energy or power.
  • hinvanty: This means "to bring forth" or "to create," indicating the act of manifestation.
  • agruvo: This term can be interpreted as "the one who is foremost" or "the leader," suggesting a guiding force.
  • dhamanti: This translates to "to support" or "to uphold," indicating stability and strength.
  • bākuraṁ: This word refers to "the mind" or "intellect," emphasizing the importance of mental clarity.
  • dṛtim: This means "determination" or "resolve," highlighting the need for steadfastness in one's pursuits.
  • tridhātu: This term refers to "three elements" or "three realms," which can symbolize the physical, mental, and spiritual aspects of existence.
  • vāraṇam: This translates to "the protector" or "the guardian," indicating a force that safeguards and nurtures.
  • madhu: This word means "honey," often symbolizing sweetness, nourishment, and the rewards of life.

The mantra can be interpreted as a call to a divine force that brings forth creation, supports the mind with determination, and protects the three realms of existence, ultimately leading to the sweetness of life.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios:

  • Personal Growth: The emphasis on determination and mental clarity can inspire individuals to pursue their goals with focus and resilience.
  • Leadership: The idea of a guiding force can be relevant in leadership roles, where one must support and uplift others while maintaining a clear vision.
  • Spiritual Practice: The reference to the three realms encourages a holistic approach to life, integrating physical, mental, and spiritual well-being.
  • Community Support: The protective aspect of the mantra can be seen in community efforts to safeguard and nurture each other, fostering a sense of belonging and care.

In conclusion, this mantra signifies the importance of divine guidance, mental clarity, and determination in navigating life's challenges. It serves as a reminder to seek balance across different aspects of existence and to support one another in the journey of life, ultimately leading to a fulfilling and sweet experience.
