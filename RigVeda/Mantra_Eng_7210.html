शश्वन्तं हि प्रचेतसः प्रत्यन्तं चिदेनसः |देवाः कृणुथ जिवसे ||
śaśvantaṁ hi pracetasaḥ pratiyantaṁ cid enasaḥ | devāḥ kṛṇutha jīvase ||
  • śaśvantaṁ: This word can be translated to "everlasting" or "eternal." It signifies something that is permanent and unchanging.
  • hi: This is a particle that emphasizes the preceding word, often translated as "indeed" or "certainly."
  • pracetasaḥ: This term refers to "those who are wise" or "intelligent beings." It highlights the importance of wisdom and understanding in life.
  • pratiyantaṁ: This means "approaching" or "coming towards." It suggests a movement or a journey towards something significant.
  • cid: This word translates to "consciousness" or "awareness." It represents the essence of being aware and understanding one's surroundings.
  • enasaḥ: This can be interpreted as "life" or "vital energy." It signifies the life force that sustains all beings.
  • devāḥ: This word means "gods" or "divine beings." It indicates the presence of higher powers or forces in the universe.
  • kṛṇutha: This is a verb that means "to create" or "to make." It emphasizes the act of bringing something into existence.
  • jīvase: This translates to "for life" or "for living." It signifies the purpose of existence and the importance of life itself.

  • Meaning of the Mantra: The mantra speaks to the eternal nature of wisdom and consciousness. It emphasizes that wise beings approach the essence of life with awareness, and it calls upon the divine to create and sustain life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a personal context, one might reflect on the importance of wisdom in decision-making. For instance, when faced with a career choice, seeking advice from experienced mentors can lead to a more fulfilling path.
    • In a broader societal context, communities that prioritize education and awareness tend to thrive. For example, countries that invest in education often see advancements in technology and quality of life.

  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for wisdom and consciousness in the pursuit of life. It reminds us that by seeking knowledge and understanding, we can create a more meaningful existence. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various aspects, such as personal growth, community development, and even in spiritual practices, where the focus is on cultivating awareness and making informed choices for a better life.
