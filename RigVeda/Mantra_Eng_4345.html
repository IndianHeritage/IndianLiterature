दृढा चिद्यावनस्पतीं क्षमया दर्धर्ष्योजसा |यत् ते अभ्रस्य विद्युतो दिवो वर्षन्ति वर्ष्टयः ||
dṛḻhā cid yā vanaspatīn kṣmayā dardharṣy ojasā | yat te abhrasya vidyuto divo varṣanti vṛṣṭayaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • dṛḻhā: firm, strong
    • cid: indeed, certainly
    • yā: who, that
    • vanaspatīn: trees, plants
    • kṣmayā: with patience, endurance
    • dardharṣy: to bear, to support
    • ojasā: with energy, strength
    • yat: that which
    • te: your
    • abhrasya: of the clouds
    • vidyuto: lightning
    • dive: of the sky
    • varṣanti: rain, pour
    • vṛṣṭayaḥ: showers, downpours
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the strength and resilience of nature, particularly trees and plants, which endure and thrive through various challenges.
    • The phrase "dṛḻhā cid yā vanaspatīn" emphasizes the steadfastness of trees, symbolizing stability and support in the ecosystem.
    • "kṣmayā dardharṣy ojasā" suggests that with patience and strength, these natural elements can withstand adversities.
    • The latter part of the mantra, "yat te abhrasya vidyuto divo varṣanti vṛṣṭayaḥ," connects the strength of nature to the life-giving rains that come from the clouds, highlighting the interdependence of elements in nature.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In agriculture, farmers rely on the resilience of crops (vanaspatīn) to withstand droughts or floods, showcasing the importance of strong plants in sustaining food supply.
    • Urban green spaces, such as parks and gardens, serve as reminders of the strength of nature amidst concrete jungles, providing a refuge for both people and wildlife.
    • Environmental conservation efforts aim to protect forests, recognizing that strong ecosystems can endure climate change and provide essential resources like clean air and water.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of resilience and strength in nature, reminding us of our connection to the environment.
    • It teaches us to appreciate the patience and endurance of natural elements, which can inspire us in our personal lives to face challenges with strength.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra encourages sustainable practices and respect for nature, emphasizing that our well-being is intertwined with the health of our environment.
