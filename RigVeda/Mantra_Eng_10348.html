उत्तानपर्णे सुभगे देवजूते सहस्वति |सपत्नीं मे परा धमा पतिम मे केवलं कुरु ||
uttānaparṇe subhage devajūte sahasvati | sapatnīm me parā dhama patim me kevalaṁ kuru ||
  • uttānaparṇe: This term can be broken down into two parts: "uttāna," meaning "uplifted" or "raised," and "parṇe," which refers to "leaves" or "foliage." Together, it symbolizes the idea of growth, elevation, and the flourishing of life.
  • subhage: This word translates to "auspicious" or "fortunate." It denotes a state of well-being and prosperity, suggesting that the subject is blessed with good fortune.
  • devajūte: This term means "in the presence of the divine" or "among the gods." It emphasizes the connection to the spiritual realm and the importance of divine blessings in one's life.
  • sahasvati: This word translates to "powerful" or "strong." It signifies strength, vitality, and the ability to overcome challenges.
  • sapatnīm: This term means "rival" or "competitor." It can refer to challenges or obstacles that one may face in life, particularly in relationships or endeavors.
  • me: This simply means "my," indicating a personal connection to the following requests or desires.
  • parā dhama: This phrase translates to "the highest good" or "ultimate virtue." It signifies the pursuit of the best possible outcome or the highest moral standards.
  • patim: This word means "husband" or "lord." It can symbolize a partner or a guiding force in one's life.
  • me kevalaṁ kuru: This phrase translates to "make it solely for me" or "do this exclusively for me." It expresses a desire for a focused and dedicated outcome.

In summary, this mantra invokes a plea for divine assistance in achieving personal growth and prosperity while overcoming challenges. It emphasizes the importance of spiritual connection and the pursuit of the highest good in one's life. The mantra can be applied in real-life scenarios such as:


  • Seeking guidance in personal relationships, ensuring that one remains focused on positive outcomes despite rivalries or challenges.
  • Striving for personal and professional growth, invoking strength and divine support to overcome obstacles.
  • Fostering a mindset of positivity and well-being, recognizing the importance of divine blessings in achieving one's goals.

In conclusion, this mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of personal aspirations, divine support, and the pursuit of virtue. It encourages individuals to seek strength and guidance in their endeavors, ultimately leading to a fulfilling and prosperous life.
