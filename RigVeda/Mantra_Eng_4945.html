सम पूषण विदुषा नय यो अञ्जसानुशासति |य एवेदमिति ब्रवत ||
sam pūṣan viduṣā naya yo añjasānuśāsati | ya evedam iti bravat ||
  • Translation: "O Pusha, the all-knowing, guide us swiftly on the right path; he who speaks this truth."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • sam: Together, in harmony.
    • pūṣan: A name for the Sun God, representing nourishment and growth.
    • viduṣā: The all-knowing or wise one.
    • naya: To guide or lead.
    • yo: Who.
    • añjasā: Swiftly or easily.
    • anuśāsati: To instruct or command.
    • ya: He who.
    • evedam: This indeed.
    • iti: Thus or so.
    • bravat: Speaks or declares.
  • Contextual Meaning:
    • This mantra invokes Pusha, the Sun God, who symbolizes nourishment and guidance.
    • It emphasizes the importance of seeking wisdom and guidance in our lives.
    • The phrase "guide us swiftly on the right path" reflects the human desire for direction and clarity in decision-making.
    • It acknowledges the power of truth and the importance of speaking it in our lives.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, this mantra can be applied when seeking guidance for career decisions, such as choosing the right job or project.
    • In personal relationships, it can serve as a reminder to communicate truthfully and seek understanding.
    • In education, students can invoke this mantra when looking for clarity in their studies or when facing challenges in learning.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of seeking guidance and wisdom in all aspects of life.
    • It encourages individuals to embrace truth and clarity in their thoughts and actions.
    • By invoking the essence of Pusha, one can find the strength to navigate life's challenges with confidence and integrity.
