ऋतस्य हि सदसो धीतिर अद्यौत सं गार्ष्टेयो वृषभो गोभिर आनट् |उद अतिṣṭhat तविषेणाऽ रवेण महान्ति चित सं विव्याचाऽ राजांसी ||
ṛtasya hi sadaso dhītir adyaut saṁ gārṣṭeyo vṛṣabho gobhir ānaṭ | ud atiṣṭhat taviṣeṇā raveṇa mahānti cit saṁ vivyācā rajāṁsi ||
  • ṛtasya hi sadaso dhītir: This phrase can be translated as "the understanding of truth is indeed the foundation." Here, "ṛta" refers to cosmic order or truth, while "dhītir" signifies wisdom or understanding. In real life, this can be related to the importance of having a strong moral compass and understanding the truth in our actions and decisions.
  • adyaut saṁ gārṣṭeyo vṛṣabho: This part means "today, the powerful bull has arisen." The "bull" symbolizes strength and vitality. In practical terms, this can be seen in the context of personal empowerment and the emergence of new opportunities. For instance, when someone overcomes challenges and rises to a position of strength, they embody this mantra.
  • gobhir ānaṭ: This translates to "with the cows." Cows in Vedic literature often symbolize wealth, nourishment, and abundance. In modern life, this can be interpreted as the importance of community and support systems that provide sustenance and growth, much like how a family or a group of friends can uplift an individual.
  • ud atiṣṭhat taviṣeṇā: This means "it has risen with strength." This can be related to the idea of resilience and the ability to rise above difficulties. For example, individuals who face adversity but continue to strive for their goals exemplify this strength.
  • raveṇa mahānti cit: This translates to "with the great light of the sun." The sun symbolizes enlightenment, knowledge, and clarity. In real life, this can be seen in the pursuit of education and wisdom, where individuals seek to illuminate their paths and those of others.
  • saṁ vivyācā rajāṁsi: This means "it has explained the great realms." This can refer to the dissemination of knowledge and understanding across different domains. In a contemporary context, this can be likened to the sharing of information through technology and education, which helps in bridging gaps between various fields of knowledge.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of truth, strength, community, resilience, enlightenment, and the sharing of knowledge. In real life, it serves as a reminder to seek truth in our actions, to rise with strength in the face of challenges, to value our communities, and to pursue knowledge. This mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as personal development, leadership, and community building, encouraging individuals to embody these principles in their daily lives.
