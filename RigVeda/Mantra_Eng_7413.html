आ त्व अśत्रव ā गहि न्य उ1क्तानि च हूयसे |उपमे रोचने दिवः ||
ā tv aśatrav ā gahi ny u1kthāni ca hūyase | upame rocane divaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā: from, away from
    • tv: you
    • aśatrav: without enemies
    • ā: to, towards
    • gahi: grasp, hold
    • ny: not
    • u1kthāni: the utterances, the hymns
    • ca: and
    • hūyase: you are invoked, you are called
    • upame: in comparison, in similarity
    • rocane: in brilliance, in radiance
    • dvaḥ: of the heavens
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra can be understood as a call to a divine entity, asking it to come forth and be present.
    • The phrase "without enemies" suggests a state of peace and harmony, indicating a desire for a conflict-free existence.
    • The invocation of hymns signifies the importance of words and prayers in connecting with the divine.
    • The reference to "brilliance of the heavens" implies a connection to higher realms, enlightenment, and divine wisdom.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a personal context, this mantra can be recited during meditation or prayer to seek peace and clarity in one's life.
    • In community gatherings, invoking this mantra can foster a sense of unity and collective harmony, encouraging participants to set aside differences.
    • In conflict resolution scenarios, the essence of this mantra can be applied to promote dialogue and understanding, aiming for a peaceful resolution.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for peace, unity, and divine presence in our lives.
    • It serves as a reminder of the power of words and intentions in shaping our reality and relationships.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be a guiding principle for fostering harmony, whether in personal relationships, community interactions, or broader societal contexts.
