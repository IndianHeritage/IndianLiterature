त्यम ū षु वाजिनं देवजूतं सहावानं तरुतारं रथानाम् |अरिष्टनेमिम पृथनाजम् आशुं स्वस्तये तार्क्ष्यम इह हुवेम ||
tyam ū ṣu vājinaṁ devajūtaṁ sahāvānaṁ tarutāraṁ rathānām | ariṣṭanemim pṛtanājam āśuṁ svastaye tārkṣyam ihā huvema ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • tyam: that
    • ū: and
    • ṣu: in the best
    • vājinaṁ: of the chariots
    • devajūtaṁ: divine, born of the gods
    • sahāvānaṁ: together with companions
    • tarutāraṁ: swift, like the wind
    • rathānām: of the chariots
    • ariṣṭanemim: the one who removes obstacles
    • pṛtanājam: born of the earth
    • āśuṁ: quickly
    • svastaye: for well-being
    • tārkṣyam: the eagle, symbolizing strength and speed
    • ihā: here
    • huvema: we invoke
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes a divine force that is swift and powerful, akin to a chariot that moves with great speed and efficiency.
    • The mention of "ariṣṭanemim" signifies the removal of obstacles, which can be related to overcoming challenges in life.
    • The reference to "tārkṣyam" symbolizes strength and agility, qualities that are essential for success in various endeavors.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a competitive environment, such as a corporate setting, invoking the qualities of speed and strength can be likened to a team working together efficiently to meet deadlines and achieve goals.
    • In sports, athletes often seek to embody the qualities of swiftness and agility, much like the "tārkṣyam," to outperform their competitors.
    • In personal development, individuals may face obstacles that require them to summon their inner strength and resilience, akin to the invocation of "ariṣṭanemim."
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of divine support in overcoming challenges and achieving success.
    • It serves as a reminder that with the right mindset and qualities, one can navigate through life's obstacles effectively.
    • In practical terms, it can be applied in various aspects of life, including personal growth, professional endeavors, and teamwork, encouraging individuals to invoke their inner strength and work collaboratively towards common goals.
