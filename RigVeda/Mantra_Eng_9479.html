इन्द्राग्नि वृत्रहत्येषु सत्पतिं मिथो हिन्वाना तंवा समोकसा |अन्तरिक्षं मह्य ā पप्रुर ओजसा सोमो घृतश्रीर महिमानम् ईरयन ||
indrāgnī vṛtrahatyeṣu satpatī mitho hinvānā tanvā3 samokasā | antarikṣam mahy ā paprur ojasā somo ghṛtaśrīr mahimānam īrayan ||
  • Indrāgnī: This refers to the combined powers of Indra, the king of gods, and Agni, the fire god. In real life, this can symbolize the collaboration of strength and energy in overcoming challenges.
  • Vṛtrahatyeṣu: This term means "in the slaying of Vritra," where Vritra represents obstacles or demons. In our lives, Vritra can be seen as the challenges we face, such as fear or doubt.
  • Satpatī: This translates to "the true path" or "the righteous way." It emphasizes the importance of following a moral and ethical path in life.
  • Mitho hinvānā: This phrase means "together they inspire." It highlights the power of unity and collaboration in achieving goals.
  • Tanvā3 samokasā: This can be interpreted as "with a strong body" or "with vigor." It signifies the importance of physical and mental strength in overcoming life's challenges.
  • Antarikṣam: This word means "the space" or "the atmosphere." It can represent the vast opportunities and possibilities that exist around us.
  • Mahy ā paprur: This translates to "they have approached me." It signifies the importance of seeking knowledge and wisdom from the universe.
  • Ojasā: This means "with energy" or "with power." It emphasizes the need for vitality and enthusiasm in our endeavors.
  • Somo: Referring to the moon or soma, which symbolizes nourishment and rejuvenation. In real life, this can represent the importance of rest and self-care.
  • Ghṛtaśrīr: This term means "the glory of ghee" or "purity." It signifies the value of purity and clarity in our actions and thoughts.
  • Mahimānam: This translates to "greatness" or "majesty." It reflects the aspiration for greatness in our pursuits.
  • Īrayan: This means "to invoke" or "to praise." It emphasizes the importance of gratitude and recognition of the forces that aid us.

Conclusion: This mantra encapsulates the essence of collaboration, strength, and the pursuit of righteousness in overcoming life's challenges. It teaches us that by uniting our energies and following a true path, we can conquer obstacles and achieve greatness. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios, such as teamwork in professional settings, community efforts in social causes, or personal growth through self-discipline and moral integrity. The significance lies in recognizing the interconnectedness of our actions and the support we receive from the universe, encouraging us to strive for excellence while remaining grounded in our values.
