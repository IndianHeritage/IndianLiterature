स रायस्खाम उप सृजा गृणानः पुरुषचन्द्रस्य त्वं इन्द्र वस्वः |पतिर बभूथासमो जनानाम् एको विश्वस्य भुवनस्य राजा ||
sa rāyas khām upa sṛjā gṛṇānaḥ puruścandrasya tvam indra vasvaḥ | patir babhūthāsamo janānām eko viśvasya bhuvanasya rājā ||
  • sa rāyas khām upa sṛjā gṛṇānaḥ: This phrase can be translated as "O Indra, who creates wealth and prosperity." Here, "sa" refers to "that," "rāyas" means "wealth" or "prosperity," "khām" refers to "the sky" or "the heavens," and "upa sṛjā" means "who creates" or "who brings forth." In real life, this can be related to the idea of seeking abundance and success in various endeavors, such as business or personal growth.
  • puruścandrasya tvam indra vasvaḥ: This translates to "You are the Indra of the moon among men." "Puruṣa" means "man," "chandra" means "moon," and "tvam" means "you." This line signifies the importance of leadership and guidance, akin to how the moon illuminates the night. In practical terms, this can be seen in leaders who inspire and guide their teams, much like a mentor or a coach.
  • patir babhūthāsamo janānām: This means "You have become the lord equal to the people." "Patir" means "lord" or "master," "babhūtha" means "have become," and "sama" means "equal." This line emphasizes the idea of a leader being relatable and approachable. In a real-world context, this can be observed in leaders who maintain humility and connect with their followers, fostering a sense of community and trust.
  • eko viśvasya bhuvanasya rājā: This translates to "You are the one king of the entire universe." "Eko" means "one," "viśvasya" means "of the universe," "bhuvanasya" means "of the worlds," and "rājā" means "king." This line highlights the unity and interconnectedness of all beings under a single divine authority. In everyday life, this can be reflected in the idea of global citizenship, where individuals recognize their role in the larger community and work towards collective well-being.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of leadership, prosperity, and unity. It signifies the importance of a leader who is not only powerful but also relatable and connected to the people. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios, such as in business leadership, community service, and personal relationships. By embodying the qualities of guidance, humility, and a sense of shared purpose, individuals can create a positive impact in their surroundings, fostering an environment of growth and collaboration.
