तं इन्द्रं दानम् ईमहे शवसानम् अभीरवम् |ईशानं राया ईमहे ||
tam indraṁ dānam īmahe śavasānam abhīrvam | īśānaṁ rāya īmahe ||
  • tam indraṁ: This phrase refers to Indra, the king of the gods in Vedic mythology, who is often associated with strength, power, and the ability to overcome obstacles. In real life, this can symbolize the pursuit of leadership qualities and the strength to face challenges.
  • dānam īmahe: "Dānam" means giving or generosity. This part of the mantra emphasizes the importance of being generous and sharing with others. In everyday life, this can be seen in acts of charity, community service, or simply helping a friend in need.
  • śavasānam abhīrvam: "Śavasānam" can be interpreted as the one who brings vitality or life. "Abhīrvam" suggests a sense of protection or safety. Together, this can be understood as invoking the energy that brings life and security. In practical terms, this could relate to creating a safe and nurturing environment for family and community.
  • īśānaṁ rāya: "Īśānaṁ" refers to a lord or master, while "rāya" means wealth or prosperity. This part of the mantra calls upon the divine for guidance in achieving prosperity and success. In real life, this can be applied to seeking wisdom in financial decisions or striving for success in one's career.
  • īmahe: This term means "we invoke" or "we seek." It signifies a collective effort to reach out for divine assistance and blessings. In a community context, this can be seen in group prayers or collective actions aimed at achieving a common goal.

Conclusion: The mantra "tam indraṁ dānam īmahe śavasānam abhīrvam | īśānaṁ rāya īmahe ||" encapsulates the essence of seeking strength, generosity, vitality, and prosperity through divine invocation. Its significance lies in the reminder to cultivate leadership qualities, practice generosity, create safe environments, and strive for prosperity while seeking divine guidance. In real life, this mantra can inspire individuals and communities to work together towards common goals, fostering a spirit of cooperation, support, and shared success.
