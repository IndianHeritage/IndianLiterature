प्रेरय सूरो अर्थं न पारं ये अस्य कामं जनिधा इव गमन् |गिरश्च ये ते तुविजात पूर्वीर नर इन्द्र प्रतिशिक्षन्त्यन्नैः ||
preraya sūro arthaṁ na pāraṁ ye asya kāmaṁ janidhā iva gman | giraś ca ye te tuvijāta pūrvīr nara indra pratiśikṣanty annaiḥ ||
  • Mantra: "preraya sūro arthaṁ na pāraṁ ye asya kāmaṁ janidhā iva gman | giraś ca ye te tuvijāta pūrvīr nara indra pratiśikṣanty annaiḥ ||"
  • Translation: "O Indra, inspire the brave to achieve their goals, and may those who desire wealth and prosperity be guided by the wisdom of the ancients."

  • Word-by-Word Explanation:
    • preraya: Inspire or motivate.
    • sūro: The brave or the strong.
    • arthaṁ: Goals or objectives, often related to wealth and prosperity.
    • na pāraṁ: Not beyond or not unattainable.
    • ye: Those who.
    • asya kāmaṁ: This desire or aspiration.
    • janidhā: Like a source or origin.
    • iva: Like or as if.
    • gman: To move or progress.
    • giraś: Wisdom or knowledge.
    • ca: And.
    • ye: Those who.
    • te: They.
    • tuvijāta: Guided or directed.
    • pūrvīr: By the ancients or predecessors.
    • nara: Man or human.
    • indra: A deity, often representing strength and leadership.
    • pratiśikṣanty: They teach or instruct.
    • annaiḥ: With food or sustenance.

  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, a leader may inspire their team to achieve ambitious sales targets, emphasizing that these goals are attainable with hard work and collaboration.
    • In education, teachers often draw upon the wisdom of past scholars to guide students in their learning journey, showing them that knowledge is built upon the foundations laid by those before them.
    • In personal development, individuals may seek mentorship from experienced professionals who can provide guidance and support in achieving their career aspirations.

  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of inspiration and guidance in achieving one's goals. It highlights that aspirations are not beyond reach and that wisdom from the past can illuminate the path forward.
    • In real life, this can be applied in various contexts such as leadership, education, and personal growth, where motivation and mentorship play crucial roles in success.
    • Ultimately, the mantra serves as a reminder that with the right inspiration and guidance, individuals can overcome challenges and achieve their desires.
