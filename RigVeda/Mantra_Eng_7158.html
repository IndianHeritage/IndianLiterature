एहि प्रेहि क्षयो दिव्य आगोषाणां | ओभे पृणासि रोधसी ||
ehi prehi kṣayo divy ā3ghoṣañ carṣaṇīnām | obhe pṛṇāsi rodasī ||
  • Mantra: "ehi prehi kṣayo divy ā3ghoṣañ carṣaṇīnām | obhe pṛṇāsi rodasī ||"
  • Translation: This mantra can be translated as: "Come, approach, and let the divine sound of the celestial beings resonate; may you fill the two worlds (heavens and earth) with abundance."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • ehi: Come or approach.
    • prehi: Go or proceed.
    • kṣayo: Destruction or loss, but in this context, it can imply the removal of obstacles.
    • divy: Divine or celestial.
    • ā3ghoṣañ: Sound or resonance, often associated with the sacred sounds of the universe.
    • carṣaṇīnām: Of the celestial beings or divine entities.
    • obhe: Both or two.
    • pṛṇāsi: You fill or you nourish.
    • rodasī: The two worlds, typically referring to the heavens and the earth.
  • Contextual Explanation:
    • This mantra invokes the presence of divine beings and encourages a connection between the earthly and celestial realms.
    • It emphasizes the importance of approaching the divine for guidance and support in overcoming challenges.
    • The idea of filling the two worlds with abundance suggests a holistic approach to life, where spiritual and material needs are met.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In times of difficulty, individuals often seek spiritual guidance through prayer or meditation, similar to the invocation in this mantra.
    • Communities coming together for rituals or celebrations can be seen as a way to resonate with divine energies, filling their lives with joy and abundance.
    • Environmental initiatives that aim to nourish the earth and promote sustainability reflect the essence of filling the world with abundance.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of connecting with the divine to seek support and abundance in life.
    • It serves as a reminder of the interconnection between the spiritual and material worlds, encouraging individuals to seek balance.
    • In practical terms, it can inspire people to engage in community service, environmental stewardship, and personal growth, fostering a sense of unity and abundance in their lives.
