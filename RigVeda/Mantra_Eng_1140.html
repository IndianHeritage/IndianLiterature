अव तमनā भारत केतवेदा अव तमनā भारत फेनम् उदन | क्षीरेंण स्नातः कुयवस्य योषे हते ते स्याताम् प्रवणे शिफायाः ||
ava tmanā bharate ketavedā ava tmanā bharate phenam udan | kṣīreṇa snātaḥ kuyavasya yoṣe hate te syātām pravaṇe śiphāyāḥ ||
  • Translation: The mantra can be translated as: "With the mind focused, may we attain the knowledge of the wise; with the mind focused, may we receive the essence of the waters. Those who are purified by the milk, may they be free from the troubles of the world."
  • Word-by-Word Explanation:
    • ava tmanā: With the mind focused or directed.
    • bharate: To attain or to achieve.
    • ketavedā: Knowledge of the wise or enlightened beings.
    • phenam udan: The essence of the waters, symbolizing purity and nourishment.
    • kṣīreṇa: By the milk, representing nourishment and sustenance.
    • snātaḥ: Purified or cleansed.
    • kuyavasya: Of the troubles or difficulties.
    • yoṣe: In the context of women or feminine energy.
    • hate: May be free or liberated.
    • te syātām: May they be.
    • pravaṇe: In the flow or movement.
    • śiphāyāḥ: Of the arrows or challenges.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a stressful work environment, focusing the mind can help individuals achieve clarity and make better decisions, akin to "ava tmanā bharate." For instance, a manager who practices mindfulness can lead their team more effectively.
    • In personal relationships, understanding and nurturing the essence of communication (phenam udan) can lead to healthier interactions, similar to how milk nourishes the body.
    • When facing challenges, such as health issues or emotional turmoil, seeking purification (kṣīreṇa snātaḥ) through practices like yoga or meditation can help individuals overcome their difficulties.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its emphasis on mental focus, the pursuit of wisdom, and the importance of purification in overcoming life's challenges. In real-life scenarios, it encourages individuals to cultivate mindfulness, seek knowledge, and maintain purity in thoughts and actions to navigate through difficulties effectively. This mantra serves as a reminder that with a focused mind and a pure heart, one can achieve personal growth and harmony in life.
