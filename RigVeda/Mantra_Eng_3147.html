तव त्ये अग्ने हरितो घृतस्ना रोहितासा ऋज्वञ्चः स्वञ्चः |अरुषासो वृषण ऋजुमुष्का आ देवतातिम अह्वन्त दसमाः ||
tava tye agne harito ghṛtasnā rohitāsa ṛjvañcaḥ svañcaḥ | aruṣāso vṛṣaṇa ṛjumuṣkā ā devatātim ahvanta dasmāḥ ||
  • tava tye agne: This phrase translates to "O Agni, you are the one." Agni is the fire god in Vedic literature, symbolizing energy, transformation, and purification. In real life, this can be related to the role of fire in cooking, warmth, and rituals.
  • harito ghṛtasnā: This means "green and full of ghee." Ghee, or clarified butter, is considered sacred and is often used in offerings and rituals. The term "green" can symbolize prosperity and abundance. In modern life, this can be seen in the importance of healthy, nourishing food that sustains life.
  • rohitāsa ṛjvañcaḥ svañcaḥ: "Rohita" refers to the red or the dawn, symbolizing new beginnings. "Ṛjvañcaḥ" and "svañcaḥ" can be interpreted as "those who are righteous and pure." This can be related to the idea of starting fresh each day, embracing new opportunities, and living a life of integrity.
  • aruṣāso vṛṣaṇa ṛjumuṣkā: "Aruṣāso" refers to the red dawn, while "vṛṣaṇa" can mean strength or power. "Ṛjumuṣkā" suggests a connection to righteousness. This can be likened to the strength found in moral values and the power of a community that stands together for justice and righteousness.
  • ā devatātim ahvanta dasmāḥ: This translates to "we invoke the divine." It emphasizes the importance of calling upon the divine for guidance and support. In everyday life, this can be seen in the practice of prayer or meditation, where individuals seek connection with a higher power for wisdom and strength.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of invoking Agni, the fire god, as a symbol of transformation, purity, and new beginnings. It highlights the importance of righteousness, strength, and divine guidance in our lives. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in rituals, personal reflections, and community gatherings, reminding us to embrace change, seek nourishment—both physical and spiritual—and uphold moral values in our daily actions. The significance of this mantra lies in its ability to inspire individuals to live a life of purpose, integrity, and connection to the divine.
