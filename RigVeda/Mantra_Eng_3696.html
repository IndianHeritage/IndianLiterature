इला सरस्वती मही तिस्रो देवीर मयोभुवः |बरहिः सीदन्त्व अस्रिधः ||
iḻā sarasvatī mahī tisro devīr mayobhuvaḥ | barhiḥ sīdantv asridhaḥ ||
  • iḻā: This term refers to the concept of 'speech' or 'expression'. In a broader sense, it symbolizes communication and the power of words in shaping thoughts and ideas.
  • sarasvatī: Sarasvatī is the goddess of knowledge, music, art, wisdom, and learning. She represents the pursuit of knowledge and the importance of education in human life.
  • mahī: This word means 'earth' or 'the great one'. It signifies the physical world and the nurturing aspect of nature that supports life.
  • tisro: This translates to 'three'. In various contexts, it can represent the three realms of existence: the physical, the mental, and the spiritual.
  • devīr: This term means 'goddesses' or 'divine beings'. It emphasizes the presence of divine forces in the world that guide and protect humanity.
  • mayobhuvaḥ: This can be interpreted as 'may we be blessed' or 'may we be enriched'. It reflects a desire for abundance and prosperity in life.
  • barhiḥ: This word refers to 'sacred grass' or 'the seat of the divine'. It symbolizes the connection between the earthly and the divine, often used in rituals.
  • sīdantv: This means 'may they sit' or 'may they dwell'. It expresses a wish for the divine presence to reside among us.
  • asridhaḥ: This term can be understood as 'those who are nourished' or 'those who are supported'. It highlights the importance of sustenance and support in life.

In conclusion, this mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking knowledge, divine guidance, and the nurturing aspects of the earth. It emphasizes the importance of communication and the pursuit of wisdom, while also acknowledging the divine forces that enrich our lives. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in educational settings, during rituals for knowledge and prosperity, or in moments of reflection where one seeks guidance and support from the universe. It serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of knowledge, nature, and the divine, encouraging individuals to strive for growth and enlightenment in their lives.
