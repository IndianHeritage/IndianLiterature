तयोरिदमवाच्छवस्तिग्मा दिद्युः मग्नोः |प्रतिद्रुणा गभस्त्योर्गवां वृतघ्न एषते ||
tayor id amavac chavas tigmā didyun maghonoḥ | prati druṇā gabhastyor gavāṁ vṛtraghna eṣate ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • tayor: of those (referring to the divine or cosmic forces)
    • id: this
    • amavac: the unyielding or firm
    • chavas: strength or power
    • tigmā: sharp or piercing
    • didyun: they shine or illuminate
    • maghonoḥ: of the mighty or powerful
    • prati: towards
    • druṇā: the path or way
    • gabhastyor: of the luminous or radiant
    • gavāṁ: of the cows (symbolizing wealth and nourishment)
    • vṛtraghna: the slayer of Vritra (a demon representing obstacles)
    • eṣate: is present or exists
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the divine forces that are unyielding and powerful, illuminating the path towards prosperity and abundance.
    • The reference to "cows" symbolizes wealth, nourishment, and sustenance, which are essential for life.
    • The term "vṛtraghna" refers to the slayer of Vritra, representing the overcoming of obstacles and challenges in life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be applied to the pursuit of success. Just as the divine forces illuminate the path, a clear vision and determination can guide entrepreneurs through challenges.
    • In personal life, when facing difficulties, one can draw strength from this mantra, reminding themselves that obstacles can be overcome with resilience and support from the community.
    • In agriculture, the symbolism of cows can relate to the importance of sustainable practices that nourish both the land and the people, ensuring a prosperous future.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of divine guidance and strength in overcoming life's challenges.
    • It serves as a reminder that with determination and the right support, one can achieve prosperity and well-being.
    • In various aspects of life, whether in business, personal growth, or community development, the principles encapsulated in this mantra can inspire individuals to strive for success and harmony.
