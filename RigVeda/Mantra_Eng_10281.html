मन्त्र: नकीर देवाऽ मिनीमसि नकीर आ योपयामसि मन्त्रश्रुत्यं चरामसि |पक्षेभिर अपिकक्षेभिर अत्राभि सं रभामहे ||
nakir devā minīmasi nakir ā yopayāmasi mantraśrutyaṁ carāmasi | pakṣebhir apikakṣebhir atrābhi saṁ rabhāmahe ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • nakir: not (or no)
    • devā: gods
    • minīmasi: we are diminished (or we are not small)
    • ā: from
    • yopayāmasi: we are not separated (or we do not depart)
    • mantraśrutyaṁ: by the hearing of the mantra
    • carāmasi: we move (or we wander)
    • pakṣebhir: with wings
    • apikakṣebhir: with those who have wings
    • atrābhi: here (or in this place)
    • saṁ: together
    • rabhāmahe: we grasp (or we hold)
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks to the unity and strength derived from the divine.
    • It emphasizes that despite challenges, we are not diminished by the presence of the gods.
    • The phrase "by the hearing of the mantra" suggests the power of sound and intention in spiritual practice.
    • The imagery of wings symbolizes freedom and the ability to rise above difficulties.
    • It conveys a sense of community and collective strength, as indicated by "we grasp together."
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a team setting, when individuals come together with a shared purpose, they can achieve more than they could alone, reflecting the mantra's theme of unity.
    • In moments of personal struggle, reciting affirmations or mantras can provide strength and a sense of connection to something greater, similar to the divine presence mentioned.
    • In nature, birds flying in formation demonstrate the power of collective effort, akin to the "wings" referenced in the mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of unity, strength, and the power of collective intention.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, such as teamwork, personal growth, and community building.
    • By recognizing the divine presence and the strength we derive from it, we can navigate challenges more effectively and foster a sense of belonging.
