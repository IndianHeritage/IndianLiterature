प्र वाचम् इन्दुर इष्यति समुद्रस्याधि विष्टपी |जिन्वन् कोशम् मधुश्चुतम् ||
pra vācam indur iṣyati samudrasyādhi viṣṭapi | jinvan kośam madhuścutam ||
  • pra vācam: This phrase can be translated as "the speech that is forthcoming" or "the utterance that is to be expressed." It signifies the importance of communication and the sharing of knowledge.
  • indur: This word means "the moon." In many cultures, the moon symbolizes calmness, reflection, and the passage of time. It can also represent the mind and emotions.
  • iṣyati: This term translates to "is desired" or "is sought after." It indicates a longing or aspiration towards something valuable or significant.
  • samudrasyādhi: This means "above the ocean." The ocean often symbolizes vastness, depth, and the unknown. It can represent the collective unconscious or the source of all life.
  • viṣṭapi: This word refers to "the expanse" or "the wide space." It suggests a sense of openness and the potential for growth and exploration.
  • jinvan: This translates to "having conquered" or "victorious." It implies overcoming challenges and achieving success.
  • kośam: This word means "treasure" or "wealth." It signifies the valuable resources or knowledge that one possesses.
  • madhuścutam: This phrase can be interpreted as "the sweet essence" or "the nectar." It represents the ultimate reward or the most cherished outcome of one's efforts.

In summary, this mantra speaks to the importance of communication and the pursuit of knowledge and wisdom. It emphasizes the journey of seeking understanding and the treasures that come from overcoming challenges. The imagery of the moon and the ocean suggests a deep connection to the universe and the vast potential that lies within each individual.


Significance and Real-Life Application:

  • This mantra can be applied in various aspects of life, such as personal growth, education, and professional development. It encourages individuals to seek knowledge and communicate effectively with others.
  • In a real-life scenario, a student striving for academic excellence can embody the essence of this mantra by actively participating in discussions, seeking guidance from mentors, and overcoming obstacles in their learning journey.
  • In the workplace, this mantra can inspire teamwork and collaboration, where individuals share their insights and experiences to achieve common goals, ultimately leading to collective success.

Overall, the mantra serves as a reminder of the value of communication, the pursuit of knowledge, and the rewards that come from perseverance and collaboration in our daily lives.
