प्रास्मा ऊर्जं घृतश्चुतम अश्विनाऽयच्छतं युवम् |यो वां सुम्नाय तुष्टवद वसूयाद दानुनस पतिः ||
prāsmā ūrjaṁ ghṛtaścutam aśvinā yacchataṁ yuvam | yo vāṁ sumnāya tuṣṭavad vasūyād dānunas patī ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • prāsmā: From the depths or from the core.
    • ūrjaṁ: Energy or strength.
    • ghṛtaścutam: The essence of ghee or clarified butter.
    • aśvinā: O Ashvins, the divine twin horsemen.
    • yacchataṁ: Bestow or grant.
    • yuvam: You two (referring to the Ashvins).
    • yo: Who.
    • vāṁ: You (plural).
    • sumnāya: For auspiciousness or well-being.
    • tuṣṭavad: Satisfied or content.
    • vasūyād: From wealth or abundance.
    • dānunas: Of the giver or generous one.
    • patī: Lord or master.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the Ashvins, who are considered the divine physicians and bringers of health and vitality.
    • The phrase "prāsmā ūrjaṁ" suggests a call for deep, intrinsic energy, indicating a desire for strength that comes from within.
    • "ghṛtaścutam" symbolizes nourishment and sustenance, as ghee is often associated with purity and health in Vedic traditions.
    • The mantra emphasizes the importance of divine blessings for well-being and prosperity, as it requests the Ashvins to bestow their gifts upon the devotees.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern life, this mantra can be related to seeking inner strength during challenging times, such as overcoming personal or professional obstacles.
    • It can also be applied in health practices, where individuals may invoke the Ashvins for healing and vitality, similar to how people seek holistic treatments today.
    • In community settings, this mantra can inspire collective efforts towards well-being, where groups come together to support each other, reflecting the idea of shared abundance and generosity.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine assistance in achieving strength, health, and prosperity.
    • It serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of well-being, both individually and collectively, and the importance of seeking divine guidance in our endeavors.
    • In real-life scenarios, it encourages individuals to cultivate inner strength and to be generous and supportive within their communities, fostering a spirit of abundance and gratitude.
