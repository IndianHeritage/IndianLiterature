यस्मै त्वं सुद्रविणो ददाशो 'नागास्त्वम अदिते सर्वताता |यम् भद्रेण शवसा चोदयासि प्रजावतां राधसा ते स्याम ||
yasmai tvaṁ sudraviṇo dadāśo 'nāgāstvam adite sarvatātā | yam bhadreṇa śavasā codayāsi prajāvatā rādhasā te syāma ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yasmai: To whom
    • tvaṁ: You
    • sudraviṇo: Of good wealth
    • dadāśo: Bestow
    • ānāgāstvam: You are free from sin
    • adite: O Aditi (the mother of gods)
    • sarvatātā: All-pervading
    • yam: Whom
    • bhadreṇa: With auspiciousness
    • śavasā: With strength
    • codayāsi: You inspire
    • prajāvatā: With progeny
    • rādhasā: With abundance
    • te: Your
    • syāma: May we become
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Aditi, the mother of the gods, asking for blessings of wealth, strength, and progeny.
    • The phrase "yasmai tvaṁ sudraviṇo dadāśo" signifies the desire for divine wealth and prosperity.
    • "ānāgāstvam adite sarvatātā" acknowledges Aditi's purity and omnipresence, emphasizing the importance of moral integrity in seeking blessings.
    • The invocation of strength and auspiciousness in "yam bhadreṇa śavasā codayāsi" reflects the need for divine inspiration to achieve success and well-being.
    • Finally, "prajāvatā rādhasā te syāma" expresses the hope for abundance and fertility, both in terms of material wealth and family.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern life, individuals often seek blessings for prosperity through rituals and prayers, similar to the invocation in this mantra.
    • Businesses may recite this mantra during auspicious ceremonies to attract wealth and success.
    • Families may chant this mantra when seeking blessings for children, reflecting the desire for progeny and abundance.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of seeking divine blessings for wealth, strength, and family.
    • It teaches the importance of moral integrity and auspiciousness in our pursuits.
    • In real life, it serves as a reminder to align our actions with ethical values while striving for success and abundance.
