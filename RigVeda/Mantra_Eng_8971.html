उच्छ्वञ्चस्व पृथिवी मा निबाधथा: सूपायनास्मै भव सूपवञ्चना |माता पुत्रं यथा सिचाभ्य एनं भूम उर्णुहि ||
uc chvañcasva pṛthivi mā ni bādhathāḥ sūpāyanāsmai bhava sūpavañcanā | mātā putraṁ yathā sicābhy enam bhūma ūrṇuhi ||
  • uc chvañcasva: This phrase can be interpreted as "rise up" or "ascend." It signifies the call for elevation or upliftment, both physically and spiritually.
  • pṛthivi: This word means "earth" or "the world." It represents the physical realm we inhabit, symbolizing stability and grounding.
  • mā ni bādhathāḥ: This translates to "do not obstruct" or "do not hinder." It is a plea for freedom from obstacles, whether they be physical, emotional, or spiritual.
  • sūpāyanāsmai: This can be understood as "to the one who is well-prepared" or "to the one who is ready." It emphasizes the importance of being prepared for the journey ahead.
  • bhava sūpavañcanā: This phrase means "may there be good fortune" or "may there be auspiciousness." It is a wish for positive outcomes and blessings in life.
  • mātā putraṁ yathā sicābhy enam: This translates to "as a mother nourishes her child." It highlights the nurturing aspect of relationships, emphasizing care, love, and support.
  • bhūma ūrṇuhi: This means "may the earth embrace" or "may the earth support." It signifies a desire for the earth to provide sustenance and support in our endeavors.

Conclusion:

The mantra encapsulates a profound message about the journey of life, emphasizing the importance of rising above challenges, being prepared, and seeking blessings for a fruitful existence. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as:

  • In personal development, it encourages individuals to overcome obstacles and strive for growth.
  • In parenting, it serves as a reminder of the nurturing role parents play in their children's lives.
  • In community building, it highlights the importance of supporting one another and fostering a sense of belonging.

Overall, this mantra serves as a guiding principle for living a life filled with purpose, support, and positive intentions, reminding us of the interconnectedness of all beings and the nurturing role of the earth.
