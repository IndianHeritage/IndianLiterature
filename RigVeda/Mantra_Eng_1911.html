अभूद इदं वयुनम ओ षु भूषतां रथो वृषण्वान मदता मनिषिणः |धियं जिन्वा धिष्ण्या विश्पलावसू दिवो नपातā सुखृतें शुचिव्रता ||
abhūd idaṁ vayunam o ṣu bhūṣatā ratho vṛṣaṇvān madatā manīṣiṇaḥ | dhiyaṁjinvā dhiṣṇyā viśpalāvasū divo napātā sukṛte śucivratā ||
  • abhūd idaṁ vayunam: This phrase can be interpreted as "this has come into being" or "this has manifested." It signifies the emergence of something significant in the universe.
  • o ṣu bhūṣatā: This translates to "O you who are adorned" or "O you who are blessed." It addresses a divine or powerful entity, acknowledging their grace and presence.
  • ratho vṛṣaṇvān madatā manīṣiṇaḥ: This part can be understood as "the chariot of the strong, wise, and intoxicated." It symbolizes the journey of life, driven by strength and wisdom, often influenced by the intoxication of knowledge or divine inspiration.
  • dhiyaṁjinvā dhiṣṇyā: This means "with the mind that is creative and powerful." It emphasizes the importance of a strong and imaginative mind in achieving one's goals.
  • viśpalāvasū: This can be interpreted as "the one who is well-protected and prosperous." It suggests a state of security and abundance, which is essential for a fulfilling life.
  • divo napātā: This translates to "the offspring of the heavens." It signifies a connection to the divine or celestial forces that guide and influence our lives.
  • sukṛte śucivratā: This means "those who are pure in their actions and vows." It highlights the importance of righteousness and purity in one's deeds and intentions.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of creation, strength, wisdom, and purity. It serves as a reminder of the divine forces at play in our lives and the importance of maintaining a strong, creative mind while adhering to righteous actions. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as personal development, leadership, and spiritual growth. By embodying the principles of strength, wisdom, and purity, individuals can navigate their journeys with grace and purpose, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling and prosperous life.
