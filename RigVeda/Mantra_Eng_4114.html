व्यक्तून रुद्राः व्यहानी शिक्वसो व्य अन्तरिक्षं वि राजांसि धूतयः |वि यद अज्रां अजात नावा ईं यथा वि दुर्गाणि मरुतो नाह रिष्यथा ||
vy a1ktūn rudrā vy ahāni śikvaso vy a1ntarikṣaṁ vi rajāṁsi dhūtayaḥ | vi yad ajrām̐ ajatha nāva īṁ yathā vi durgāṇi maruto nāha riṣyatha ||
  • Mantra: "vy a1ktūn rudrā vy ahāni śikvaso vy a1ntarikṣaṁ vi rajāṁsi dhūtayaḥ | vi yad ajrām̐ ajatha nāva īṁ yathā vi durgāṇi maruto nāha riṣyatha ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • vy a1ktūn: "We invoke" or "We call upon."
    • rudrā: Referring to "Rudra," a form of the divine associated with storms and the hunt.
    • vy ahāni: "In the days" or "In the times."
    • śikvaso: "Of the shining" or "Of the brilliant."
    • vy a1ntarikṣaṁ: "In the atmosphere" or "In the space."
    • vi rajāṁsi: "The rays" or "The lights."
    • dhūtayaḥ: "That are purified" or "That are cleansed."
    • vi yad: "Whatever" or "That which."
    • ajrām̐: "Is not old" or "Is eternal."
    • ajatha: "Is not created" or "Is not made."
    • nāva: "The boat" or "The vessel."
    • īṁ: "May it protect" or "May it guide."
    • yathā: "As" or "In the way."
    • vi durgāṇi: "From the dangers" or "From the obstacles."
    • maruto: "The winds" or "The storm gods."
    • nāha: "May not" or "Do not."
    • riṣyatha: "Harm" or "Disturb."
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra is a call to Rudra, seeking protection and guidance in times of difficulty.
    • It emphasizes the importance of purity and light, suggesting that one should strive to maintain clarity and righteousness in their actions.
    • The reference to "the boat" symbolizes the journey of life, where one seeks safe passage through challenges and adversities.
    • In real life, this can be likened to seeking help during tough times, such as during a personal crisis or when facing obstacles in one's career.
    • For example, when someone is going through a difficult phase, they might turn to spiritual practices or community support to find strength and guidance.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of seeking divine assistance and maintaining purity in thoughts and actions.
    • It is applicable in real-life scenarios where individuals face challenges, reminding them to seek help and remain steadfast in their values.
    • By invoking the protective energies of Rudra, one can navigate through life's storms with resilience and clarity.
