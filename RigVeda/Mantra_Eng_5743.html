अहेम यज्ञं पथाम उराणां इमां सुवृक्तिं वृषणा जुषेथाम् |श्रुष्टिवेवं प्रेषितो वाम अबोधि प्रति स्तोमैर जरामाणो वसिष्ठः ||
ahema yajñam pathām urāṇā imāṁ suvṛktiṁ vṛṣaṇā juṣethām | śruṣṭīveva preṣito vām abodhi prati stomair jaramāṇo vasiṣṭhaḥ ||
  • Mantra: "ahema yajñam pathām urāṇā imāṁ suvṛktiṁ vṛṣaṇā juṣethām |
  • Translation: "May we, through our righteous actions, be blessed with the best of intentions and wisdom, and may we be guided on the path of truth."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • ahema: signifies a call for blessings or auspiciousness.
    • yajñam: refers to sacrifice or offering, often associated with rituals that promote harmony.
    • pathām: means 'path' or 'way', indicating a journey or direction.
    • urāṇā: implies 'ancient' or 'eternal', suggesting wisdom that transcends time.
    • suvṛktiṁ: translates to 'good conduct' or 'righteousness', emphasizing moral integrity.
    • vṛṣaṇā: refers to 'strength' or 'power', often associated with divine energy.
    • juṣethām: means 'may we enjoy' or 'may we be blessed with'.
    • śruṣṭīveva: indicates 'as if created', suggesting a manifestation of divine will.
    • preṣito: means 'sent forth' or 'guided', indicating divine guidance.
    • vām: translates to 'you' or 'your', referring to the divine or higher power.
    • abodhi: means 'awareness' or 'knowledge', emphasizing enlightenment.
    • prati: means 'towards' or 'in accordance with'.
    • stomair: refers to 'praises' or 'hymns', indicating the importance of expression in devotion.
    • jaramāṇo: means 'aging' or 'decaying', reflecting the transient nature of life.
    • vasiṣṭhaḥ: refers to 'the best' or 'the most excellent', often associated with the sage Vasistha.

  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, this mantra can be applied to promote ethical practices and integrity among employees, encouraging them to act with righteousness in their dealings.
    • In personal relationships, it serves as a reminder to maintain good conduct and intentions, fostering trust and harmony.
    • In community service, individuals can embody the principles of this mantra by engaging in selfless acts that benefit others, thus creating a positive impact.

  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of righteous actions and the pursuit of wisdom in our lives.
    • Its significance lies in the reminder that our intentions and conduct shape our experiences and relationships.
    • By embodying the principles of this mantra, individuals can navigate their lives with purpose, integrity, and a sense of connection to the divine.
    • Ultimately, it encourages us to seek guidance and blessings in our journey, ensuring that we remain aligned with truth and righteousness.
