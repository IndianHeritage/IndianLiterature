मां त्वा रुद्र चुक्रुधामां नमोभिर मां दुष्टुती वृषभ मां सहूति |उन् नो वीरां अर्पय भेषजैभिर भीषक्तमं त्वा भीषजां श्रिणोमि ||
mā tvā rudra cukrudhāmā namobhir mā duṣṭutī vṛṣabha mā sahūtī | un no vīrām̐ arpaya bheṣajebhir bhiṣaktamaṁ tvā bhiṣajāṁ śṛṇomi ||
  • mā tvā rudra cukrudhāmā: This phrase can be translated as "May we not anger you, O Rudra." Rudra is a deity associated with storms and the hunt, often seen as a fierce protector. In real life, this can be understood as a plea for maintaining harmony and respect in our relationships with powerful forces, whether they be natural or social.
  • namobhir mā duṣṭutī vṛṣabha: This translates to "With our salutations, may we not be harmed by your wrath." The term "vṛṣabha" refers to a bull, symbolizing strength and power. This line emphasizes the importance of humility and respect towards those who hold power, reminding us to approach authority with reverence to avoid conflict.
  • mā sahūtī: This means "May we not be led astray." It serves as a reminder to stay true to our values and not be swayed by negative influences. In everyday life, this can relate to peer pressure or societal expectations that may lead us away from our true path.
  • un no vīrām̐ arpaya bheṣajebhir: This translates to "Grant us strength and health." The word "vīrām̐" signifies courage and valor, while "bheṣajebhir" refers to remedies or healing. This line highlights the importance of seeking strength and well-being, which is essential in facing life's challenges, whether they be physical, emotional, or spiritual.
  • bhiṣaktamaṁ tvā bhiṣajāṁ śṛṇomi: This means "I hear you, O greatest healer." It acknowledges the divine as a source of healing and support. In real life, this can be interpreted as recognizing the importance of seeking help from those who are knowledgeable and skilled in healing, whether they are medical professionals, spiritual guides, or supportive friends and family.

Conclusion: The mantra serves as a profound reminder of the importance of respect, humility, and the pursuit of strength and healing in our lives. It emphasizes the need to maintain harmonious relationships with powerful forces, whether they be deities, nature, or authority figures. In a world filled with challenges, this mantra encourages us to seek guidance and support from those who can help us navigate our paths, ultimately leading to a more balanced and fulfilling life.
