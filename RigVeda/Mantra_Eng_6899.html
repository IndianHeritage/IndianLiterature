अग्नि धृतव्रताया ते समुद्रायेव सिन्धवः |गिरो वाश्रासा ईरते ||
agne dhṛtavratāya te samudrāyeva sindhavaḥ | giro vāśrāsa īrate ||
  • Agne: This word refers to Agni, the fire god in Vedic literature, symbolizing energy, light, and purity.
  • Dhṛtavratāya: This term means "to the one who is steadfast in vows" or "to the one who is committed." It signifies dedication and determination.
  • Te: This is a pronoun meaning "to you," indicating a direct address to Agni.
  • Samudrāyeva: This translates to "like the ocean," suggesting vastness, depth, and abundance.
  • Sindhavaḥ: This word means "rivers," which can symbolize the flow of life, nourishment, and connectivity.
  • Giro: This term refers to "words" or "speech," indicating communication and expression.
  • Vāśrāsa: This means "to be well-versed" or "to be knowledgeable," emphasizing wisdom and understanding.
  • īrate: This word means "to be invoked" or "to be worshipped," indicating reverence and respect.

The mantra can be interpreted as a prayer to Agni, asking for steadfastness in our commitments and the ability to communicate effectively. It draws a parallel between the qualities of Agni and the vastness of the ocean and rivers, suggesting that just as these bodies of water are essential for life, so too is the energy and commitment represented by Agni.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios:

  • Personal Development: Just as Agni represents energy and commitment, individuals can draw inspiration from this mantra to remain dedicated to their personal goals, whether in education, career, or relationships.
  • Communication: The emphasis on words and knowledge encourages individuals to communicate effectively and wisely, fostering better relationships and understanding in both personal and professional settings.
  • Environmental Awareness: The reference to rivers and oceans can inspire a sense of responsibility towards nature, encouraging actions that protect and preserve our water bodies and ecosystems.

Conclusion: This mantra signifies the importance of dedication, effective communication, and respect for nature. It serves as a reminder to embody these qualities in our daily lives, fostering personal growth, better relationships, and environmental stewardship.
