मन्त्र: नपातो दुर्गहस्य मे सहस्रेण सुराधसः | श्रवो देवेष्व अकृत ||
napāto durgahasya me sahasreṇa surādhasaḥ | śravo deveṣv akrata ||
  • napāto: This word can be interpreted as "not falling" or "not declining." It signifies stability and resilience.
  • durgahasya: This translates to "of the difficult or hard to reach." It refers to challenges or obstacles that may be present in life.
  • me: This means "my" or "to me," indicating a personal connection or ownership.
  • sahasreṇa: This word means "with a thousand" or "thousands," suggesting abundance or a multitude of resources.
  • surādhasaḥ: This can be interpreted as "of the divine or celestial beings," referring to higher powers or spiritual entities.
  • śravo: This translates to "fame" or "glory," indicating recognition or honor.
  • deveṣv: This means "among the gods," suggesting a connection to the divine or spiritual realm.
  • akrata: This translates to "not done" or "not created," implying a state of being that is untouched or unblemished.

The mantra can be understood as a plea for stability and support in the face of challenges, invoking the divine for assistance and recognition. It emphasizes the importance of resilience and the abundance of resources available to overcome difficulties.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as:

  • Personal Challenges: When facing difficult situations, such as job loss or health issues, one can recite this mantra to seek strength and support from higher powers.
  • Academic Pursuits: Students can use this mantra during exams or challenging projects to invoke focus and clarity, ensuring they do not falter in their efforts.
  • Professional Growth: In the workplace, this mantra can serve as a reminder to remain resilient in the face of competition and to seek recognition for one’s hard work.

In conclusion, this mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the importance of resilience, divine support, and the pursuit of recognition in our lives. By invoking these principles, individuals can navigate challenges more effectively and strive for success in various aspects of life.
