अज्रे चिद अस्मै कृणुथा न्यञ्चनं दुर्गे चिद आ सुसरणम् |एषा चिद अस्माद अशनिः परो नु सास्रेदन्ती वि नश्यतु ||
ajre cid asmai kṛṇuthā nyañcanaṁ durge cid ā susaraṇam | eṣā cid asmād aśaniḥ paro nu sāsredhantī vi naśyatu ||
  • ajre cid asmai kṛṇuthā nyañcanaṁ: This phrase can be interpreted as a call to remove obstacles and difficulties. The word "ajre" signifies something that is difficult or challenging, while "cid" implies consciousness or awareness. "Asmai" refers to the individual or the seeker, and "kṛṇuthā" means to make or create. "Nyañcanaṁ" suggests the act of removing or dispelling.
  • durge cid ā susaraṇam: Here, "durge" refers to a difficult situation or a place of distress. "Susaraṇam" means a good path or a safe route. This line emphasizes the importance of finding a safe and beneficial path even in challenging circumstances.
  • eṣā cid asmād aśaniḥ: "Eṣā" means this or that, while "asmād" refers to 'from this' or 'from here'. "Aśaniḥ" can be interpreted as lightning or a powerful force. This line suggests that from the current situation, there is a powerful force that can be harnessed for positive change.
  • paro nu sāsredhantī vi naśyatu: "Paro" means beyond or higher, "nu" is an affirmation, and "sāsredhantī" refers to something that is sustaining or supportive. "Vi naśyatu" implies that it should not perish or be lost. This line conveys the idea that the higher forces or energies should support the seeker and ensure their well-being.

  • Significance: This mantra emphasizes the importance of overcoming obstacles and finding a safe path in life. It encourages individuals to seek higher energies and forces that can aid them in their journey.
  • Real-life Application: In real life, this mantra can be applied in various situations such as:
    • During challenging times in personal or professional life, individuals can recite this mantra to seek strength and guidance.
    • In moments of decision-making, it can help in finding clarity and a safe path forward.
    • In spiritual practices, it can be used to connect with higher energies for support and protection.

  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a reminder that even in the face of difficulties, there is always a path to safety and support. By invoking higher energies, individuals can navigate through life's challenges with confidence and resilience.
