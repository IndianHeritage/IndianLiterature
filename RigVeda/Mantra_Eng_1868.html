शुष्मिन्तमो हि ते मदो द्युम्निन्तमा उता क्रतुः |वृत्रघ्ना वरिवोविदा मंसिष्ठा अश्वसातमः ||
śuṣmintamo hi te mado dyumnintama uta kratuḥ | vṛtraghnā varivovidā maṁsīṣṭhā aśvasātamaḥ ||
  • śuṣmintamo hi te mado: This phrase can be translated as "Your intoxication is the most blissful." Here, "śuṣmin" refers to a state of bliss or joy, while "mado" signifies intoxication or a state of heightened awareness. In real life, this can be likened to moments of deep happiness or enlightenment that one experiences, such as during meditation or when achieving a personal goal.
  • dyumnintama uta kratuḥ: This translates to "Your brilliance is the greatest, and so is your intellect." "Dyumnintama" refers to the highest form of brilliance or radiance, while "kratuḥ" signifies intellect or mental strength. In practical terms, this can be seen in individuals who inspire others through their wisdom and knowledge, such as great leaders or thinkers who illuminate paths for others.
  • vṛtraghnā varivovidā: This means "You are the slayer of Vritra, the one who knows the essence of all." "Vṛtraghnā" refers to the one who conquers obstacles (Vritra symbolizes chaos or obstacles), and "varivovidā" means the one who understands the essence of everything. In everyday life, this can be related to overcoming personal challenges or societal issues, akin to activists or reformers who fight against injustice.
  • maṁsīṣṭhā aśvasātamaḥ: This translates to "You are the best among the strong." "Maṁsīṣṭhā" refers to being the best or the most powerful, while "aśvasātamaḥ" signifies the strongest among the strong. This can be exemplified by individuals who exhibit exceptional strength, whether physical, mental, or emotional, such as athletes or those who have endured significant hardships and emerged stronger.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the qualities of bliss, brilliance, intellect, strength, and the ability to overcome obstacles. Its significance lies in the recognition of these virtues as essential for personal growth and societal progress. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire individuals to seek joy in their endeavors, strive for knowledge, confront challenges head-on, and cultivate strength in various aspects of life. By embodying these qualities, one can contribute positively to their community and lead a fulfilling life.
