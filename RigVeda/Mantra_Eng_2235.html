तेजिष्ठया तपनी रक्षस tapa ये त्वा निदे दधिरे दृष्टवीर्यम् |आविस तात कृष्व यद असत त उक्त्यम् बृहस्पते वि परिरापो अर्दय ||
tejiṣṭhayā tapanī rakṣasas tapa ye tvā nide dadhire dṛṣṭavīryam | āvis tat kṛṣva yad asat ta ukthya1m bṛhaspate vi parirāpo ardaya ||
  • tejiṣṭhayā: This word can be broken down into "tejas," meaning brilliance or energy, and "ṭhayā," which implies a state of being. Together, it refers to the state of being filled with energy or brilliance.
  • tapanī: This term is derived from "tapa," which means heat or austerity. "Tapanī" can be understood as something that brings warmth or illumination.
  • rakṣasas: This refers to demons or malevolent beings in Vedic literature. It symbolizes obstacles or negative forces that can hinder progress.
  • tapa ye: This phrase translates to "those who perform austerities." It emphasizes the importance of discipline and self-control in overcoming challenges.
  • tvā nide dadhire: This means "they have placed you." It suggests a sense of support or foundation provided by the energies or forces at play.
  • dṛṣṭavīryam: This term can be understood as "the power that is seen." It refers to the visible strength or energy that can be harnessed.
  • āvis tat kṛṣva: This translates to "bring forth that energy." It is a call to action, urging one to channel their inner strength.
  • yad asat: This means "what is not true" or "the false." It signifies the need to discern between reality and illusion.
  • ta ukthya1m: This can be interpreted as "that which is spoken." It emphasizes the power of words and intentions in shaping reality.
  • bṛhaspate: Referring to the deity of wisdom and knowledge, Bṛhaspati represents guidance and enlightenment.
  • vi parirāpo ardaya: This phrase means "may you protect and support." It is a plea for divine assistance in overcoming challenges.

Conclusion: This mantra encapsulates the essence of harnessing inner strength and energy to overcome obstacles and negative forces in life. It emphasizes the importance of discipline, discernment, and the power of words in manifesting one's intentions. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various contexts such as personal development, spiritual practices, and even in professional settings where one faces challenges. By invoking the energies represented in this mantra, individuals can seek guidance and support to navigate through difficulties, ultimately leading to personal growth and enlightenment.
