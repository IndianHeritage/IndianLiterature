तत् सु नः सविता भगो वरुणो मित्रो आर्यमाः |शर्म यच्छन्तु सप्रथो यद् इमहे ||
tat su naḥ savitā bhago varuṇo mitro aryamā | śarma yacchantu sapratho yad īmahe ||
  • tat - This word translates to "that" or "that which." It signifies a connection to a higher reality or divine essence.
  • su - Meaning "good" or "well," it emphasizes positivity and auspiciousness.
  • naḥ - This means "our," indicating a sense of community and collective identity.
  • savitā - Referring to the "Sun" or "the divine light," it symbolizes enlightenment, knowledge, and life-giving energy.
  • bhago - This word means "wealth" or "prosperity," suggesting abundance and richness in life.
  • varuṇo - Referring to "Varuna," the god of cosmic order and water, it represents the moral and ethical dimensions of existence.
  • mitro - Meaning "friend," it signifies companionship, support, and harmony among individuals.
  • aryamā - This refers to a deity associated with the sky and the heavens, symbolizing protection and guidance.
  • śarma - Meaning "shelter" or "protection," it emphasizes the need for safety and security in life.
  • yacchantu - This translates to "may they grant," indicating a prayerful request for blessings.
  • sapratho - Meaning "together" or "in unison," it highlights the importance of unity and collective effort.
  • yad - This means "what" or "that which," referring to the desires or aspirations of the community.
  • īmahe - Meaning "we meditate" or "we contemplate," it signifies the act of reflection and seeking deeper understanding.

This mantra can be interpreted as a prayer for collective well-being, prosperity, and protection. It invokes the divine forces of the Sun, Varuna, and Aryama, asking for their blessings and guidance. The emphasis on community and unity suggests that individual success is intertwined with the welfare of others.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios:

  • Community Initiatives: When working on community projects, such as environmental conservation or social welfare, this mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of collective effort and shared goals.
  • Teamwork in Professional Settings: In workplaces, fostering a sense of camaraderie and support among colleagues can lead to greater productivity and job satisfaction, echoing the mantra's call for unity and friendship.
  • Personal Growth: On an individual level, meditating on this mantra can inspire one to seek knowledge, prosperity, and protection, encouraging a balanced and fulfilling life.

Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its holistic approach to life, emphasizing the interconnectedness of individuals within a community. It encourages us to seek blessings for ourselves and others, fostering a spirit of cooperation and mutual support. By embodying the principles of this mantra, we can create a more harmonious and prosperous society.
