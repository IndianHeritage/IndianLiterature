स यो वृषा वृष्ण्येभिः समोका महो दिवः पृथिव्याश च सम्राट् |सतीनसत्वा हव्यो भरेषु मरुत्वान नो भवत्व इन्द्र ऊती ||
sa yo vṛṣā vṛṣṇyebhiḥ samokā maho divaḥ pṛthivyāś ca samrāṭ | satīnasatvā havyo bhareṣu marutvān no bhavatv indra ūtī ||
  • sa yo vṛṣā vṛṣṇyebhiḥ: This phrase can be interpreted as "He who is strong among the strong." It emphasizes the idea of strength and power, suggesting that true strength comes from unity and collaboration.
  • samokā maho divaḥ: This translates to "the one who is equal to the great heavens." It signifies a connection to the divine and the vastness of the universe, indicating that the strength mentioned earlier is not just physical but also spiritual.
  • pṛthivyāś ca samrāṭ: This means "and the ruler of the earth." It highlights the importance of leadership and governance, suggesting that true leaders are those who are strong and just, ruling with wisdom and fairness.
  • satīnasatvā havyo: This can be interpreted as "the one who is worthy of offerings." It implies that those who possess strength and wisdom are deserving of respect and reverence, and that they should be honored in society.
  • bhareṣu marutvān: This phrase translates to "may the winds bring forth." It symbolizes the idea of change and movement, suggesting that strength and leadership should lead to progress and transformation in society.
  • no bhavatv indra ūtī: This means "may the blessings of Indra be upon us." Indra is often seen as a deity of rain and thunder, representing abundance and prosperity. This line calls for divine blessings to support the strength and leadership mentioned earlier.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of strength, leadership, and divine blessings. It emphasizes that true power lies in unity and the ability to lead with wisdom. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various contexts such as community leadership, organizational management, and even personal development. By embodying the qualities of strength and fairness, individuals can inspire others and create a positive impact in their surroundings. The call for divine blessings serves as a reminder that while human efforts are essential, seeking guidance and support from a higher power can lead to greater success and fulfillment.
