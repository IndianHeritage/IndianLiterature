एता ते अग्न उचथानि वेधो जुष्टानि सन्तु मनसे हृदे च |शकेम रायः सुदुरो यमं ते 'धि श्रवो देवभक्तं दधानाः ||
etā te agna ucathāni vedho juṣṭāni santu manase hṛde ca | śakema rāyaḥ sudhuro yamaṁ te 'dhi śravo devabhaktaṁ dadhānāḥ ||
  • etā te agna ucathāni vedho: This phrase can be translated as "These are the invocations to you, O Agni, the divine fire." Agni is a significant deity in the Rigveda, representing fire, energy, and transformation. In real life, fire symbolizes purification and the transformative power of energy, much like how challenges can lead to personal growth.
  • juṣṭāni santu manase hṛde ca: This means "May these invocations be pleasing to your mind and heart." It emphasizes the importance of intention and sincerity in our prayers and actions. In everyday life, this can be related to how we approach our goals and relationships, ensuring that our intentions are pure and heartfelt.
  • śakema rāyaḥ sudhuro yamaṁ te: This translates to "May we attain wealth and prosperity, and may we overcome difficulties." This line highlights the aspiration for success and the ability to navigate challenges. In practical terms, this can be seen in the pursuit of education or career goals, where perseverance and hard work lead to rewards.
  • 'dhi śravo devabhaktaṁ dadhānāḥ: This means "Holding the divine knowledge and devotion." It signifies the importance of wisdom and devotion in achieving our desires. In real life, this can be reflected in the pursuit of knowledge through education, spirituality, or personal development, which guides us toward our goals.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking divine guidance and support in our endeavors. It emphasizes the importance of intention, sincerity, and the pursuit of knowledge in overcoming challenges and achieving prosperity. In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as setting personal or professional goals, engaging in spiritual practices, or fostering meaningful relationships. By invoking the qualities of Agni, we can transform our challenges into opportunities for growth and success.
