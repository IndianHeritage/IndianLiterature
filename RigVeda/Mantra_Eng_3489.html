आशुं दधिक्रां तं उ नु ष्टवाम दिवस पृथिव्या उता चर्किराम |उच्छन्तीर माम उषसः सूदयन्त्व अति विश्वानि दुरितानि पर्वषण ||
āśuṁ dadhikrāṁ tam u nu ṣṭavāma divas pṛthivyā uta carkirāma | ucchantīr mām uṣasaḥ sūdayantv ati viśvāni duritāni parṣan ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • āśuṁ: quickly, swiftly
    • dadhikrāṁ: may we obtain, may we achieve
    • tam: that
    • u: indeed
    • nu: now, let us
    • ṣṭavāma: praise, worship
    • divas: day, sky
    • pṛthivyā: earth
    • uta: and, also
    • carkirāma: we revolve, we move
    • ucchantīr: may they rise, may they elevate
    • mām: me, my
    • uṣasaḥ: of the dawn, of the light
    • sūdayantv: may they purify, may they cleanse
    • ati: exceedingly, very
    • viśvāni: all, every
    • duritāni: misfortunes, obstacles
    • parṣan: may they be removed, may they be dispelled
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra expresses a desire for swift achievement and the elevation of one's spirit.
    • It emphasizes the importance of praising the divine forces that govern the day and the earth.
    • The invocation of dawn symbolizes new beginnings and the dispelling of darkness, both literally and metaphorically.
    • It seeks purification from misfortunes and obstacles, highlighting the need for spiritual and emotional cleansing.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, this mantra can be applied when starting a new project. The team can invoke the spirit of collaboration and positivity to achieve their goals swiftly.
    • During personal challenges, such as overcoming a health issue, individuals can recite this mantra to seek strength and purification from negative influences.
    • In educational pursuits, students can use this mantra to focus their energies on learning and overcoming obstacles in their studies.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the power of intention and the importance of seeking divine assistance in our endeavors.
    • Its significance lies in the belief that through praise and positive action, one can overcome challenges and achieve success.
    • In daily life, it encourages individuals to embrace new beginnings, seek purification from negativity, and strive for personal and collective growth.
