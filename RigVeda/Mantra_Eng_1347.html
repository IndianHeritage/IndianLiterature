विपृच्छामि पाक्यां न देवान वषट्कृतस्याद्भुतस्य दश्राः |पातं च सह्यसो युवं च रभ्यसो नः ||
vi pṛcchāmi pākyā3 na devān vaṣaṭkṛtasyādbhutasya dasrā | pātaṁ ca sahyaso yuvaṁ ca rabhyaso naḥ ||
  • Translation: "I ask the divine beings, who are the creators of wonders, to grant us the strength and vitality of youth, and to protect us from harm."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • vi pṛcchāmi: "I ask" - This indicates a quest for knowledge or assistance.
    • pākyā: "divine beings" - Refers to the gods or celestial entities that are revered in Vedic tradition.
    • na devān: "not the gods" - Suggests a distinction or a specific focus on certain divine aspects.
    • vaṣaṭkṛtasyādbhutasya: "creators of wonders" - Highlights the extraordinary nature of these divine beings.
    • dasrā: "strength" - Represents physical and mental power.
    • pātaṁ: "protection" - The act of safeguarding or shielding from harm.
    • ca sahyaso: "and vitality" - Refers to the energy and liveliness associated with youth.
    • yuvaṁ: "youth" - Symbolizes freshness, vigor, and potential.
    • rabhyaso: "from harm" - Indicates a plea for safety and security.
    • naḥ: "to us" - A direct appeal for the blessings to be bestowed upon the speaker and their community.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a modern context, this mantra can be likened to individuals seeking guidance and support from mentors or spiritual leaders when facing challenges in life.
    • In health and wellness, people often turn to holistic practices or spiritual rituals to invoke strength and protection, similar to the plea for divine assistance in the mantra.
    • In community settings, groups may come together to pray or meditate for collective strength and safety, reflecting the communal aspect of the mantra's request.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its invocation of divine support for strength, vitality, and protection. It emphasizes the importance of seeking help from higher powers in times of need, which is applicable in various real-life scenarios such as personal challenges, health issues, and community well-being. By acknowledging the need for divine assistance, individuals can foster a sense of connection to something greater than themselves, promoting resilience and hope in their lives.
