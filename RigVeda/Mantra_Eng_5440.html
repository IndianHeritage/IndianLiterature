प्र शुक्रैतु देवी मनीषा अस्मत सुतष्टो रथो न वाजी ||
pra śukraitu devī manīṣā asmat sutaṣṭo ratho na vājī ||
  • pra - This prefix often denotes 'before' or 'in front of'. It can signify the importance of something that comes first or is primary.
  • śukraitu - This term can be interpreted as 'bright' or 'clear'. In a broader sense, it can refer to purity or clarity of thought and intention.
  • devī - This word means 'goddess' or 'divine'. It represents the feminine aspect of divinity, often associated with power, wisdom, and nurturing.
  • manīṣā - This term translates to 'intellect' or 'wisdom'. It emphasizes the importance of knowledge and understanding in decision-making.
  • asmat - This word means 'our' or 'us', indicating a collective identity or community.
  • sutaṣṭo - This can be interpreted as 'well-pleased' or 'satisfied'. It reflects a state of contentment and fulfillment.
  • ratho - This word means 'chariot'. In a metaphorical sense, it can represent the journey of life or the means by which one moves forward.
  • na - This is a negation, meaning 'not'. It can signify the absence of something or a refusal.
  • vājī - This term can be understood as 'swift' or 'powerful'. It often refers to strength and capability in achieving goals.

The mantra "pra śukraitu devī manīṣā asmat sutaṣṭo ratho na vājī" can be interpreted as a call for divine guidance and clarity of thought in our collective journey. It emphasizes the importance of wisdom and satisfaction in our endeavors, while also highlighting the need for strength and determination.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios:

  • Decision Making: When faced with important choices, invoking the clarity and wisdom of the divine can help individuals and communities make informed decisions.
  • Community Support: The emphasis on 'our' signifies the importance of collective effort. In community projects, working together with a clear purpose can lead to greater satisfaction and success.
  • Personal Growth: The mantra encourages individuals to seek clarity and wisdom in their personal journeys, fostering a sense of fulfillment and strength in overcoming challenges.

Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine guidance, clarity of thought, and the importance of community. It serves as a reminder that wisdom and strength are essential in navigating life's journey, and that collective efforts can lead to greater satisfaction and success. By embodying these principles, individuals and communities can strive for a more harmonious and fulfilling existence.
