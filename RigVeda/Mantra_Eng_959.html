प्रैषाम् अज्मेषु विथुरेव रेजते भूमिर्यामेषु यद् ध युञ्जते शुभे |ते क्रीळयो धुनयो भ्राजदृष्टयः स्वयम् महित्वं पनयन्त धूतयः ||
praiṣām ajmeṣu vithureva rejate bhūmir yāmeṣu yad dha yuñjate śubhe | te krīḻayo dhunayo bhrājadṛṣṭayaḥ svayam mahitvam panayanta dhūtayaḥ ||
  • praiṣām ajmeṣu: This phrase can be interpreted as "the one who is in the midst of the people." It signifies the importance of community and collective effort.
  • vithureva rejate: This means "the earth shines brightly." It symbolizes prosperity and abundance that comes from harmony and cooperation among individuals.
  • bhūmir yāmeṣu: Here, "bhūmir" refers to the earth, and "yāmeṣu" can be understood as "in the journeys." This suggests that the earth supports us in our endeavors and travels, highlighting the connection between nature and human activities.
  • yad dha yuñjate śubhe: This translates to "when they unite in auspiciousness." It emphasizes the power of unity and collaboration in achieving positive outcomes.
  • te krīḻayo dhunayo: This phrase can be interpreted as "they who are engaged in joyful activities." It reflects the idea that joy and happiness arise from meaningful interactions and shared experiences.
  • bhrājadṛṣṭayaḥ: This means "with radiant vision." It signifies clarity of thought and purpose, suggesting that those who work together with a clear vision can achieve greatness.
  • svayam mahitvam: This translates to "greatness in itself." It indicates that true greatness comes from within and is nurtured through collective efforts.
  • panayanta dhūtayaḥ: This can be understood as "they who bring forth purity." It suggests that through collaboration and unity, individuals can create a pure and harmonious environment.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of community, unity, and collaboration in achieving prosperity and greatness. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various contexts such as teamwork in workplaces, community service projects, or even family gatherings. When individuals come together with a shared purpose and positive intentions, they can create a supportive environment that fosters growth and success. The mantra serves as a reminder that our collective efforts can lead to a brighter and more prosperous future for all.
