अस्य देवस्य मीळ्हुषो वयाऽ विष्णोः एषस्य प्रभृतः हविर्भिः |विदे हि रुद्रः रुद्रियं महित्वं यासिष्टं वर्तिर अश्विनाव् इरावत् ||
asya devasya mīḻhuṣo vayā viṣṇor eṣasya prabhṛthe havirbhiḥ | vide hi rudro rudriyam mahitvaṁ yāsiṣṭaṁ vartir aśvināv irāvat ||
  • Mantra: "asya devasya mīḻhuṣo vayā viṣṇor eṣasya prabhṛthe havirbhiḥ | vide hi rudro rudriyam mahitvaṁ yāsiṣṭaṁ vartir aśvināv irāvat ||"
  • Translation: This mantra can be translated as: "With the offerings of this divine being, may we attain the strength of Vishnu. Indeed, Rudra, the great one, is the essence of all that is powerful, and may the Ashvins, the divine horsemen, bring us prosperity."
  • Word-by-Word Explanation:
    • asya: of this
    • devasya: divine being
    • mīḻhuṣo: strength or power
    • vayā: may we attain
    • viṣṇor: of Vishnu, the preserver
    • eṣasya: of this
    • prabhṛthe: with offerings
    • havirbhiḥ: with oblations
    • vide: indeed
    • hi: for
    • rudro: Rudra, the fierce one
    • rudriyam: essence of Rudra
    • mahitvaṁ: greatness
    • yāsiṣṭaṁ: may you attain
    • vartir: existence or presence
    • aśvināv: of the Ashvins, the twin deities
    • irāvat: prosperity or abundance
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of divine strength and support in our lives. For instance, when facing challenges at work, one might invoke the strength of Vishnu to overcome obstacles and achieve success.
    • The reference to Rudra signifies the need for resilience and courage. In personal struggles, such as health issues, individuals may draw inspiration from Rudra's essence to remain strong and hopeful.
    • The Ashvins symbolize healing and prosperity. In times of financial difficulty, people may recite this mantra to seek blessings for abundance and well-being.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its invocation of divine powers to attain strength, resilience, and prosperity. It serves as a reminder that in our daily lives, we can seek support from higher powers to navigate challenges and achieve our goals. Whether in personal struggles, professional endeavors, or seeking abundance, this mantra encourages individuals to connect with the divine for guidance and strength. Its applicability is universal, resonating with anyone seeking empowerment and support in their journey through life.
