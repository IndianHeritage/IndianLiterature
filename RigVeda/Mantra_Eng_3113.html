ऊर्ध्वो भव प्रतिविध्याध्य अस्मद आविष कृत्स्व दैव्याण्य अग्ने |अव स्थिरा तनुहि यातुजूनां जामिम अजामिम प्र मृणीहि शत्रून् ||
ūrdhvo bhava prati vidhyādhy asmad āviṣ kṛṇuṣva daivyāny agne | ava sthirā tanuhi yātujūnāṁ jāmim ajāmim pra mṛṇīhi śatrūn ||
  • ūrdhvo bhava: This phrase translates to "be elevated" or "rise high." It signifies the aspiration for spiritual and moral elevation in life.
  • prati vidhyādhy: This means "may you direct" or "may you guide." It emphasizes the importance of guidance and direction in one's life journey.
  • asmad āviṣ kṛṇuṣva: This translates to "may you manifest in us." It indicates a desire for divine presence and influence in our lives.
  • daivyāny agne: This means "O divine Agni." Agni, the fire god, represents purity, transformation, and the divine spark within us.
  • ava sthirā tanuhi: This translates to "may you make our bodies stable." It reflects the need for physical and mental stability in life.
  • yātujūnāṁ: This means "of the enemies." It refers to the challenges and adversities we face in life.
  • jāmim ajāmim: This translates to "the offspring of the womb." It signifies the importance of progeny and continuity of life.
  • pra mṛṇīhi: This means "may you destroy." It expresses a plea for the removal of obstacles and negativity.
  • śatrūn: This translates to "the foes." It refers to both external enemies and internal struggles.

In conclusion, this mantra encapsulates a profound prayer for spiritual elevation, guidance, and divine presence in our lives. It acknowledges the need for stability in our physical and mental states while seeking protection from adversities and challenges. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied during times of personal struggle, when one seeks clarity and strength to overcome obstacles, whether they be in relationships, career, or personal growth. By invoking the divine and seeking guidance, individuals can find the resilience to rise above their challenges and lead a more fulfilling life.
