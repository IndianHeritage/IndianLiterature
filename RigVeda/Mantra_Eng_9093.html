प्र देवत्रा ब्रह्मणे गातुर एत्व अपो अच्छा मनसो न प्रयुक्ति |महिम् मित्रस्य वरुणस्य धासिम पृथुज्रयसे रीरधाऽ सुवृक्तिम् ||
pra devatrā brahmaṇe gātur etv apo acchā manaso na prayukti | mahīm mitrasya varuṇasya dhāsim pṛthujrayase rīradhā suvṛktim ||
  • pra devatrā: This phrase can be translated as "to the divine beings" or "to the deities." It signifies a call or invocation to the higher powers that govern the universe.
  • brahmaṇe: This word refers to "Brahman," the ultimate reality or cosmic spirit in Hindu philosophy. It represents the source of all creation and the essence of the universe.
  • gātur etv: This can be interpreted as "may we attain" or "may we reach." It expresses a desire for connection or realization of the divine.
  • apo acchā: Translated as "the waters are pure," this phrase emphasizes the importance of purity and clarity, both in a physical and spiritual sense.
  • manaso na prayukti: This means "the mind should not be distracted." It highlights the need for focus and concentration in spiritual practices and daily life.
  • mahīm mitrasya varuṇasya: This refers to "the greatness of Mitra and Varuna," two deities associated with truth, order, and cosmic law. It signifies the importance of righteousness and moral integrity.
  • dhāsim pṛthujrayase: This can be understood as "to the one who supports the earth." It reflects the idea of stability and support in life, both physically and metaphorically.
  • rīradhā suvṛktim: This phrase means "may we attain prosperity and well-being." It expresses a wish for abundance and success in life.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of connecting with the divine, maintaining purity of mind, and striving for righteousness and prosperity. In real life, this can be applied by fostering a sense of spirituality, focusing on moral values, and seeking clarity in our thoughts and actions. It encourages individuals to cultivate a disciplined mind and to seek support from higher powers in their endeavors, ultimately leading to a fulfilling and prosperous life.
