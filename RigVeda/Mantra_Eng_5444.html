अभि प्र स्थाताहेवा यज्ञं यातेव पत्मन् तमनā हिनोत ||
abhi pra sthātāheva yajñaṁ yāteva patman tmanā hinota ||
  • Mantra: "abhi pra sthātāheva yajñaṁ yāteva patman tmanā hinota"
  • Translation: This mantra can be translated as "May the one who is the essence of sacrifice (yajña) inspire us to perform our duties with dedication and purpose."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • Abhi: Towards, in the direction of.
    • Pra: Forth, forward.
    • Sthātā: One who stands, or is established.
    • Yajña: Sacrifice, offering, or ritual.
    • Yāteva: Just as, or like.
    • Patman: Lotus, symbolizing purity and enlightenment.
    • Tmanā: With the mind or consciousness.
    • Hinota: To inspire or to motivate.
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of yajña, which is not just a ritual but a way of life that involves selfless action and dedication to a higher purpose.
    • The reference to "patman" (lotus) signifies the idea of rising above challenges and maintaining purity of intention, much like a lotus flower that blooms beautifully despite growing in muddy waters.
    • In real life, this can be seen in the actions of individuals who dedicate themselves to social causes, environmental conservation, or community service, embodying the spirit of yajña by working for the greater good without seeking personal gain.
  • Significance:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder that our actions should be driven by a sense of duty and purpose, encouraging us to contribute positively to society.
    • It highlights the interconnectedness of all beings and the importance of performing our roles with sincerity and commitment.
    • In a world where self-interest often prevails, this mantra calls for a shift towards collective well-being and harmony.
  • Application in Real Life:
    • In professional settings, this mantra can inspire teamwork and collaboration, urging individuals to work together towards common goals.
    • In personal life, it encourages individuals to engage in acts of kindness and service, fostering a sense of community and support.
    • In spiritual practices, it can motivate individuals to pursue their spiritual paths with dedication, understanding that their efforts contribute to a larger cosmic order.
