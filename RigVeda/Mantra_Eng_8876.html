मन्त्र: किम भ्रातासद यद अनाथं भवाति किम उ स्वसā यन् निरृतिर निगच्छात् |काममूतā बह्व एतद् रपामि तन्बा मे तन्बं सम पिपृग्धि ||
kim bhrātāsad yad anātham bhavāti kim u svasā yan nirṛtir nigacchāt | kāmamūtā bahv e3tad rapāmi tanvā me tanva1ṁ sam pipṛgdhi ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • kim: what
    • bhrātāsad: brotherly
    • yad: that which
    • anātham: without support or helpless
    • bhavāti: becomes
    • kim: what
    • u: or
    • svasā: sister
    • yan: that which
    • nirṛtir: misfortune or calamity
    • nigacchāt: approaches or comes
    • kāmamūtā: desires or wishes
    • bahv: many
    • etad: this
    • rapāmi: I offer or I give
    • tanvā: with my body
    • me: my
    • tanvaṁ: body
    • sam: together
    • pipṛgdhi: may you nourish or may you strengthen
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra reflects on the nature of relationships and the support we seek from our loved ones.
    • It questions the essence of brotherhood and sisterhood, pondering what happens when one becomes helpless or when misfortune approaches.
    • The mantra emphasizes the importance of desires and the need for support in overcoming challenges.
    • It expresses a wish for nourishment and strength, both physically and emotionally, from the divine or from the universe.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In times of personal crisis, such as losing a job or facing health issues, individuals often turn to family and friends for support, embodying the essence of brotherhood and sisterhood.
    • During natural disasters, communities come together to help those affected, showcasing the collective strength and support that can be drawn from relationships.
    • In a workplace setting, colleagues often support each other during stressful projects, reflecting the idea of nourishing one another's efforts and well-being.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of human relationships and the importance of support in overcoming life's challenges.
    • It highlights the significance of nurturing bonds with family and friends, as well as the need for collective strength in facing adversities.
    • In real life, it encourages individuals to seek and offer support, fostering a sense of community and resilience.
