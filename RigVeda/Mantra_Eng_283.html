मन्त्र: उता यो मानवेषु आ यशश चक्रे असाम्य आ | अस्माकं उदरेषु आ ||
uta yo mānuṣeṣv ā yaśaś cakre asāmy ā | asmākam udareṣv ā ||
  • Mantra: "uta yo mānuṣeṣv ā yaśaś cakre asāmy ā | asmākam udareṣv ā ||"
  • Translation: "May he who has created fame among men, grant us prosperity."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • uta: a particle often used to express a wish or desire.
    • yo: who.
    • mānuṣeṣv: among men or in human society.
    • ā: a particle indicating direction or purpose.
    • yaśaś: fame, glory, or reputation.
    • cakre: has created or established.
    • asāmy ā: without disparity or inequality.
    • asmākam: our or belonging to us.
    • udareṣv: in our endeavors or pursuits.
    • ā: again, indicating direction or purpose.
  • Meaning and Explanation:
    • This mantra expresses a prayer or a wish for the creation of fame and prosperity.
    • The phrase "who has created fame among men" suggests a divine or higher power that bestows recognition and success.
    • The term "asāmy ā" emphasizes the importance of equality and fairness in the distribution of fame and prosperity, indicating that these blessings should not be limited to a select few but should be accessible to all.
    • In real life, this can be related to the idea of meritocracy, where individuals are recognized and rewarded based on their efforts and contributions rather than their background or status.
    • For example, in a workplace setting, a company that values equal opportunity and recognizes the hard work of all employees fosters a culture of motivation and success.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of fame and prosperity as collective goals that should be pursued with fairness and equality.
    • It serves as a reminder that success should be shared and that everyone has the potential to achieve greatness.
    • In contemporary society, this mantra can inspire individuals and communities to work together towards common goals, ensuring that recognition and rewards are distributed justly.
    • Ultimately, it encourages a harmonious society where everyone can thrive and contribute to the greater good.
