ऊर्ध्व ऊ शु ण औतये तिष्ठा देवो न सविता |ऊर्ध्वो वाजस्य सन्तता यद अञ्जिभिर वाघद्भिर विह्वयामहे ||
ūrdhva ū ṣu ṇa ūtaye tiṣṭhā devo na savitā | ūrdhvo vājasya sanitā yad añjibhir vāghadbhir vihvayāmahe ||
  • ūrdhva: This word translates to "upward" or "higher." It signifies the aspiration to rise above the mundane and reach for higher ideals or spiritual goals.
  • ū ṣu ṇa: This phrase can be interpreted as "the one who is auspicious" or "the one who brings good fortune." It emphasizes the importance of positive energy and blessings in our lives.
  • ūtaye: This means "to elevate" or "to uplift." It suggests a call to action, encouraging individuals to strive for improvement and growth.
  • tiṣṭhā: This word means "to stand" or "to remain." It implies stability and perseverance in the face of challenges.
  • devo: This translates to "god" or "divine being." It represents the higher powers or forces that guide and protect us.
  • na savitā: "Savitā" refers to the sun or the source of life. The phrase can be understood as a plea for divine guidance and illumination in our lives.
  • ūrdhvo vājasya: This can be interpreted as "the higher strength" or "the elevated power." It signifies the pursuit of strength and vitality in our endeavors.
  • sanitā: This word means "to be nourished" or "to be sustained." It highlights the importance of support and sustenance in our journey.
  • yad añjibhir: This phrase translates to "that which is achieved through effort." It emphasizes the value of hard work and determination in achieving our goals.
  • vāghadbhir: This means "with words" or "through speech." It signifies the power of communication and expression in our lives.
  • vihvayāmahe: This translates to "we invoke" or "we call upon." It represents the act of seeking divine assistance and guidance in our endeavors.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a profound message about striving for higher ideals, seeking divine guidance, and the importance of perseverance and effort in our lives. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as personal development, spiritual growth, and professional endeavors. For instance, when facing challenges at work, one can invoke the essence of this mantra to seek strength and clarity, reminding oneself to remain steadfast and uplifted in pursuit of success. Ultimately, it serves as a reminder that with determination, positive energy, and divine support, we can elevate ourselves and achieve our aspirations.
