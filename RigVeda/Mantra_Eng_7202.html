मां नो मृचां रिपुणां वृजिनानाम् अविष्यवः |देवा अभि प्र मृक्षत ||
mā no mṛcā ripūṇāṁ vṛjinānām aviṣyavaḥ | devā abhi pra mṛkṣata ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • mā: May we not
    • no: Our
    • mṛcā: Be harmed
    • ripūṇāṁ: By our enemies
    • vṛjinānām: By obstacles or afflictions
    • aviṣyavaḥ: May we be free from
    • devā: The gods
    • abhi: May they
    • pra: Protect us
    • mṛkṣata: From harm
  • Overall Meaning: This mantra is a prayer for protection and freedom from harm caused by enemies and obstacles. It expresses a desire for divine intervention to safeguard individuals from negative influences and adversities.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • Personal Safety: In daily life, individuals often face challenges and adversities, whether in personal relationships, work environments, or health issues. This mantra can be recited as a form of meditation or affirmation to seek protection and strength against these challenges.
    • Community Well-being: Communities can come together to recite this mantra during festivals or gatherings, invoking divine protection against social issues such as crime or natural disasters, fostering a sense of unity and safety.
    • Conflict Resolution: In situations of conflict, whether in families or workplaces, this mantra can serve as a reminder to seek peaceful resolutions and to protect oneself from negative energies that may arise from disputes.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its universal appeal for protection and peace. It emphasizes the importance of seeking divine support in overcoming adversities and maintaining harmony in life. By incorporating this mantra into daily practices, individuals can cultivate a mindset of resilience and positivity, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling and secure life.
