चक्रं यदस्याप्स्वा निषत्तम उतो तदस्मै मध्वि च चच्छद्यात् | पृथिव्यां अतिषितं यदूधः पयो गोष्व अदधा ओषधिषु ||
cakraṁ yad asyāpsv ā niṣattam uto tad asmai madhv ic cacchadyāt | pṛthivyām atiṣitaṁ yad ūdhaḥ payo goṣv adadhā oṣadhīṣu ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • cakraṁ: wheel or cycle
    • yad: that which
    • asyāpsv: in the waters
    • ā: towards
    • niṣattam: the most powerful
    • uto: or
    • tad: that
    • asmai: to him
    • madhv: sweetness
    • ic: may
    • cacchadyāt: be cut off
    • pṛthivyām: on the earth
    • atiṣitaṁ: that which is established
    • yad: that which
    • ūdhaḥ: from above
    • payo: milk
    • goṣv: in the cows
    • adadhā: has been given
    • oṣadhīṣu: in the plants
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the interconnectedness of nature and the divine.
    • The "cakraṁ" symbolizes the cyclical nature of life, much like the cycles of seasons or the water cycle.
    • The phrase "yad asyāpsv" refers to the waters, which can be seen as a source of life, similar to how rivers nourish the land.
    • "Pṛthivyām atiṣitaṁ" indicates the establishment of life on earth, emphasizing the importance of stability and sustenance.
    • The mention of "payo goṣv" highlights the nurturing aspect of nature, as milk from cows represents nourishment and sustenance for humanity.
    • Overall, the mantra reflects a deep respect for the natural world and its resources.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In agriculture, understanding the cycles of nature (like planting and harvesting) is crucial for successful farming.
    • Water conservation practices can be seen as a modern application of respecting the "waters" mentioned in the mantra.
    • In holistic health, the use of natural resources (like milk and herbs) for nourishment aligns with the teachings of this mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of harmony with nature and the importance of sustainable practices.
    • It serves as a reminder to respect the resources provided by the earth and to live in balance with the environment.
    • In today's context, it encourages us to adopt eco-friendly practices and to appreciate the interconnectedness of all life forms.
