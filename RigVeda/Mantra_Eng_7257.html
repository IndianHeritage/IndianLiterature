आ पप्राथ महिना वृष्ण्या वृषण विश्वा शविष्ट शवसा |अस्मां अव मघवन गोमती व्रजे वज्रीं चित्राभिर ऊतिभिः ||
ā paprātha mahinā vṛṣṇyā vṛṣan viśvā śaviṣṭha śavasā | asmām̐ ava maghavan gomati vraje vajriñ citrābhir ūtibhiḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā paprātha: "I have spoken" or "I have declared."
    • mahinā: "with greatness" or "with power."
    • vṛṣṇyā: "of the Vṛṣṇi" or "of the strong."
    • vṛṣan: "the strong one" or "the powerful."
    • viśvā: "all" or "universal."
    • śaviṣṭha: "the best" or "the most excellent."
    • śavasā: "with strength" or "with vigor."
    • asmām̐: "from us" or "among us."
    • ava: "down" or "towards."
    • maghavan: "the bountiful" or "the generous."
    • gomati: "wealthy" or "prosperous."
    • vraje: "in the pasture" or "in the field."
    • vajriñ: "the one who wields power" or "the strong."
    • citrābhir: "with various" or "with colorful."
    • ūtibhiḥ: "with offerings" or "with praises."
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of a declaration made with great power and strength, emphasizing the importance of unity and collective strength.
    • The reference to "Vṛṣṇi" symbolizes a lineage of strength and valor, suggesting that the speaker is invoking the qualities of bravery and excellence.
    • The phrase "from us" indicates a communal aspect, highlighting the importance of togetherness in achieving greatness.
    • The mention of "the bountiful" and "wealthy" suggests a connection to prosperity and abundance, which can be interpreted as a call for blessings in various aspects of life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, this mantra can be applied to team dynamics where collective effort and strength lead to success. For instance, a project team that collaborates effectively can achieve remarkable results.
    • In community service, the mantra reflects the idea that when individuals come together with a common purpose, they can create a significant impact, such as organizing a food drive or a charity event.
    • In personal development, the invocation of strength and excellence can inspire individuals to pursue their goals with determination and resilience, much like athletes training for a competition.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of collective strength, unity, and the pursuit of excellence. It serves as a reminder that greatness is often achieved through collaboration and shared purpose.
    • In real-life scenarios, whether in professional, communal, or personal contexts, the principles embodied in this mantra can guide individuals and groups towards achieving their goals and fostering a sense of community and prosperity.
