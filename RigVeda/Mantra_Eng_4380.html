मन्तर: नृवद वसो सदम इद धेहि अस्मे भूरी टोकाय तनयाय पश्वः | पूर्वीर इषो बृहतीर आर्यघा अस्मे भद्रा सौश्रवसानि सन्तु ||
nṛvad vaso sadam id dhehy asme bhūri tokāya tanayāya paśvaḥ | pūrvīr iṣo bṛhatīr āre-aghā asme bhadrā sauśravasāni santu ||
  • nṛvad vaso sadam id dhehy asme bhūri tokāya tanayāya paśvaḥ
    • nṛvad: This term refers to the essence of humanity or the human condition.
    • vaso: This means 'to dwell' or 'to reside'.
    • sadam: This translates to 'in a good place' or 'in a pleasant abode'.
    • id dhehy: This phrase can be interpreted as 'may this be established' or 'let this be realized'.
    • asme: This means 'for us' or 'to us'.
    • bhūri: This signifies 'abundant' or 'plentiful'.
    • tokāya: This refers to 'offspring' or 'progeny'.
    • tanayāya: This also means 'to the children' or 'to the descendants'.
    • paśvaḥ: This translates to 'cattle' or 'wealth'.
  • pūrvīr iṣo bṛhatīr āre-aghā asme bhadrā sauśravasāni santu
    • pūrvīr: This means 'of the ancients' or 'of the predecessors'.
    • iṣo: This refers to 'desires' or 'wishes'.
    • bṛhatīr: This translates to 'great' or 'vast'.
    • āre-aghā: This can be interpreted as 'free from sin' or 'without wrongdoing'.
    • asme: Again, this means 'for us' or 'to us'.
    • bhadrā: This signifies 'auspicious' or 'good'.
    • sauśravasāni: This refers to 'good fame' or 'renown'.
    • santu: This means 'may they be' or 'let them be'.

Meaning:

This mantra expresses a prayer for a prosperous and harmonious life. It seeks the establishment of a good dwelling for humanity, filled with abundance and wealth, particularly for future generations. The invocation of the ancestors' desires emphasizes the importance of continuity and the blessings of those who came before us. The mantra concludes with a wish for auspiciousness and good fame, suggesting that a life lived in accordance with these principles will lead to a positive legacy.


Real-Life Examples:

  • In a family setting, this mantra can be reflected in the way parents strive to provide a nurturing environment for their children, ensuring they have the resources and support to thrive.
  • In a community, it can be seen in initiatives aimed at preserving cultural heritage and values, ensuring that the wisdom of the ancestors is passed down to future generations.
  • In personal development, individuals may interpret this mantra as a call to cultivate a life of integrity and purpose, seeking to leave a positive impact on society.

Conclusion:

The significance of this mantra lies in its holistic approach to life, emphasizing the interconnectedness of humanity, prosperity, and legacy. It serves as a reminder to cultivate a life that honors the past while building a better future. In real-life scenarios, it encourages individuals and communities to focus on values that promote well-being, abundance, and a positive reputation, ultimately leading to a fulfilling and meaningful existence.
