मन्त्र: उता स्य देवो भुवनस्य सक्षणिस त्वष्टा ज्ञाभिः सजोषा जूजुवद रथम् | इळा भागो बृहद्विवोता रोडसी पूषा पुरंधिर अश्विनाव अधा पती ||
uta sya devo bhuvanasya sakṣaṇis tvaṣṭā gnābhiḥ sajoṣā jūjuvad ratham | iḻā bhago bṛhaddivota rodasī pūṣā puraṁdhir aśvināv adhā patī ||
  • uta sya devo bhuvanasya: This phrase can be translated as "the divine being of the universe." It signifies the omnipresence of divinity in all aspects of life and nature.
  • sakṣaṇis tvaṣṭā: Referring to the creator or the divine craftsman, Tvaṣṭā is associated with the act of creation and manifestation. This can be likened to an artist who brings a canvas to life, shaping it with intention and skill.
  • gnābhiḥ sajoṣā jūjuvad ratham: This part speaks to the idea of knowledge and wisdom guiding the journey of life, much like a charioteer guiding a chariot. In real life, this can be seen in the role of mentors or educators who steer individuals towards their goals.
  • iḻā bhago bṛhaddivota: Here, 'Iḻā' refers to the earth or the material world, while 'bhago' signifies prosperity. This suggests that true wealth comes from a harmonious relationship with the earth and its resources, akin to sustainable living practices that respect nature.
  • rodasī pūṣā puraṁdhir: This phrase can be interpreted as the nurturing aspect of the universe, akin to a mother providing for her children. In modern contexts, this can relate to community support systems that foster growth and well-being.
  • aśvināv adhā patī: The Aśvins are twin deities associated with health and medicine. This highlights the importance of well-being and the pursuit of health in our lives, reflecting the contemporary focus on holistic health practices.
  • Conclusion: This mantra encapsulates the interconnectedness of divinity, creation, knowledge, prosperity, nurturing, and health. Its significance lies in the reminder that our lives are shaped by these fundamental principles. In real life, it encourages us to seek balance and harmony in our actions, to respect the earth, to pursue knowledge, and to care for our health and the well-being of our communities. By embodying these values, we can lead more fulfilling and meaningful lives.
