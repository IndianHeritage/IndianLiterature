इयं ते नव्यसी मतिर्ग्ने अधाय्य अस्मद आ |मन्द्र सुजात सुक्रतो 'मूर दसमातिथे ||
iyaṁ te navyasī matir agne adhāyy asmad ā | mandra sujāta sukrato 'mūra dasmātithe ||
  • iyaṁ te navyasī matir agne adhāyy asmad ā
    • iyaṁ - This word translates to "this" or "this one". It signifies a focus on the present moment or the current situation.
    • te - Meaning "to you\ it indicates a direction or intention towards a divine entity.
    • navyasī - This term can be interpreted as "new" or "fresh\ suggesting a sense of renewal or innovation.
    • matir - Translates to "intellect" or "thought\ emphasizing the importance of wisdom and understanding.
    • agne - Refers to "Agni\ the fire god, symbolizing energy, transformation, and purification.
    • adhāyy - This means "may it be directed" or "may it lead\ indicating a desire for guidance.
    • asmad - Meaning "us\ it signifies a collective identity or community.
    • ā - A preposition that can mean "towards" or "from\ indicating direction.
  • mandra sujāta sukrato 'mūra dasmātithe
    • mandra - This word means "hymn" or "song\ suggesting the power of words and expressions in invoking the divine.
    • su-jāta - Translates to "well-born" or "noble\ indicating purity and high moral standing.
    • su-kṛta - Meaning "well-done" or "good deeds\ emphasizing the importance of righteous actions.
    • 'mūra - This term can mean "not foolish" or "wise\ highlighting the value of wisdom in decision-making.
    • dasmātithe - This can be interpreted as "from the tenth\ which may refer to a higher state of consciousness or a deeper understanding.

Conclusion:

The mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking divine guidance and wisdom in our lives. It calls upon Agni, the fire god, to inspire fresh thoughts and noble actions within us. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various contexts such as:

  • In personal development, individuals can reflect on their intentions and seek to cultivate a mindset that is open to new ideas and wisdom.
  • In leadership, the mantra serves as a reminder for leaders to act with integrity and to inspire their teams through noble actions and wise decisions.
  • In community building, it encourages collective efforts towards a common goal, fostering a sense of unity and shared purpose.

Overall, this mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the significance of wisdom, integrity, and the pursuit of higher ideals in our daily lives.
