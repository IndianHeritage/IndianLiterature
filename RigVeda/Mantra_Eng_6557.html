विश्वानि विश्वमनसो धियā नो वृत्रहन्तमः |उग्र प्रणेतर अधि शू वसो गहि ||
viśvāni viśvamanaso dhiyā no vṛtrahantama | ugra praṇetar adhi ṣū vaso gahi ||
  • viśvāni: This word translates to "all" or "universal." It signifies inclusiveness and the idea of unity among all beings.
  • viśvamanaso: This means "with universal minds" or "with all minds." It emphasizes the importance of collective consciousness and shared understanding.
  • dhiyā: This translates to "with wisdom" or "with intellect." It highlights the role of knowledge and understanding in achieving harmony.
  • no: This is a pronoun meaning "our" or "us," indicating a collective identity.
  • vṛtrahantama: This term refers to "the slayer of Vritra," where Vritra symbolizes obstacles or ignorance. It signifies the removal of barriers to knowledge and enlightenment.
  • ugra: This word means "powerful" or "fierce." It denotes strength and determination in overcoming challenges.
  • praṇetar: This translates to "the one who leads" or "the guide." It signifies the importance of leadership and guidance in the pursuit of wisdom.
  • adhi: This means "above" or "over," indicating a higher perspective or understanding.
  • ṣū: This word can mean "to be" or "to exist," emphasizing the essence of being.
  • vaso: This translates to "of the dwelling" or "of the home," symbolizing a sense of belonging and community.
  • gahi: This means "to hold" or "to grasp," indicating the act of embracing or accepting wisdom and guidance.

In essence, this mantra calls for a collective pursuit of wisdom and understanding, urging individuals to come together with a unified mind to overcome ignorance and obstacles. It emphasizes the importance of strong leadership and guidance in this journey, encouraging a sense of community and belonging.


Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for unity, wisdom, and strength in overcoming challenges. In real-life scenarios, it can be applied in various contexts such as teamwork in professional settings, community building, and educational environments. By fostering a collective mindset and embracing guidance, individuals can work together to achieve common goals and overcome obstacles, leading to a more harmonious and enlightened society.
