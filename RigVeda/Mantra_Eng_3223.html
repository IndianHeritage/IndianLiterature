अपो वृतं वव्रिवांसं पराहन् प्रावत ते वज्रम पृथिवी सचेता: |प्रार्णांसी समुद्रियाण्य ऐनो: पतिर्भवञ्च चवासां शूर धृष्णो ||
apo vṛtraṁ vavrivāṁsam parāhan prāvat te vajram pṛthivī sacetāḥ | prārṇāṁsi samudriyāṇy ainoḥ patir bhavañ chavasā śūra dhṛṣṇo ||
  • Mantra: "apo vṛtraṁ vavrivāṁsam parāhan prāvat te vajram pṛthivī sacetāḥ | prārṇāṁsi samudriyāṇy ainoḥ patir bhavañ chavasā śūra dhṛṣṇo ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • apo: from the waters
    • vṛtraṁ: the obstacle or the demon
    • vavrivāṁsam: having been conquered
    • parāhan: to be removed or dispelled
    • prāvat: may you come forth
    • te: your
    • vajram: thunderbolt or strength
    • pṛthivī: earth
    • sacetāḥ: with consciousness or awareness
    • prārṇāṁsi: the great waters
    • samudriyāṇy: of the oceans
    • ainoḥ: of the two
    • patir: lord or master
    • bhavañ: may you become
    • chavasā: with strength
    • śūra: brave or valiant
    • dhṛṣṇo: with courage
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra invokes the power of water to overcome obstacles, represented by the demon Vritra. In real life, this can be seen as a metaphor for overcoming challenges and difficulties.
    • The mention of "vajram" symbolizes strength and resilience, akin to how individuals face adversities in their lives, drawing upon their inner strength to prevail.
    • The phrase "may you come forth" suggests a call to action, encouraging individuals to actively seek solutions and not passively wait for change.
    • The reference to "the great waters" and "oceans" can be interpreted as the vast potential and resources available to us, which we can tap into when facing challenges.
    • The qualities of being "brave" and "courageous" highlight the importance of mental fortitude in overcoming life's hurdles, much like a soldier facing the battlefield with valor.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of inner strength, awareness, and the ability to confront and overcome obstacles in life.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, such as personal struggles, professional challenges, and even societal issues where courage and resilience are required to bring about change.
    • By invoking the essence of this mantra, individuals can find inspiration to harness their inner power and face their challenges with determination and bravery.
