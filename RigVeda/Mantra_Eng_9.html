स नः पितेव सूनवेऽग्नि सूपायनो भव |सचस्वा नः स्वस्तये ||
sa naḥ piteva sūnave 'gne sūpāyano bhava | sacasvā naḥ svastaye ||
  • sa naḥ - "May he (the divine) be with us" - This phrase invokes the presence of a higher power, suggesting a connection between the individual and the divine.
  • piteva - "like a father" - This simile emphasizes the nurturing and protective qualities of the divine, akin to a father's role in a family.
  • sūnave - "to the son" - This indicates a relationship, where the divine is seen as a guiding force for the individual, much like a father guiding his son.
  • agne - "O Agni" - Agni, the fire god, symbolizes energy, transformation, and the divine spark within us. Fire is often associated with purification and enlightenment.
  • sūpāyano - "may you be well-disposed" - This is a plea for the divine to be favorable and supportive, indicating a desire for guidance and blessings.
  • bhava - "be" - A call for the divine presence to manifest in our lives, reinforcing the importance of divine intervention.
  • sacasvā - "may we be endowed" - This phrase expresses a wish for the blessings and virtues that lead to prosperity and well-being.
  • naḥ - "for us" - This emphasizes the collective aspect of the mantra, indicating that the blessings are sought not just for the individual but for the community.
  • svastaye - "for our welfare" - This signifies the ultimate goal of the mantra, which is the well-being and prosperity of all.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda encapsulates a deep yearning for divine guidance and support, akin to the nurturing relationship between a father and son. It highlights the importance of seeking blessings not just for oneself but for the community as a whole. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as during family gatherings, community events, or personal reflections, where individuals seek harmony, prosperity, and well-being. By invoking the divine presence, individuals can foster a sense of unity and purpose, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling and harmonious life.
