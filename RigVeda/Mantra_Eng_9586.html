वासूनां वा चर्कृष इयक्षन् धियā वा यज्ञैर वा रोडस्योः |अर्वन्तो वा ये रयिमन्तः सातौ वनुं वा ये सुश्रुणं सुश्रुतो धुः ||
vasūnāṁ vā carkṛṣa iyakṣan dhiyā vā yajñair vā rodasyoḥ | arvanto vā ye rayimantaḥ sātau vanuṁ vā ye suśruṇaṁ suśruto dhuḥ ||
  • Vasūnāṁ vā carkṛṣa: This phrase can be interpreted as "the wealth of the Vasus" or "the essence of the divine beings." The Vasus are considered elemental deities in Vedic literature, representing various aspects of nature and prosperity.
  • Iyaksan dhiyā vā yajñair vā rodasyoḥ: This part suggests the importance of wisdom (dhiyā) and sacrifices (yajñair) in achieving harmony with the cosmic order (rodasyoḥ). It emphasizes that through wisdom and rituals, one can connect with the divine.
  • Arvanto vā ye rayimantaḥ: This line refers to those who are rich in resources (rayimantaḥ) and are capable of achieving abundance (arvanto). It highlights the idea that true wealth comes from both material and spiritual abundance.
  • Sātau vanuṁ vā ye suśruṇaṁ suśruto dhuḥ: This concluding part speaks to the importance of listening (suśruṇaṁ) and understanding (suśruto) the teachings of the wise (sātau). It suggests that those who are attentive to wisdom will find their path to prosperity and fulfillment.

  • Real-Life Example: In modern life, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as in business, where wisdom and ethical practices lead to sustainable success. For instance, a company that prioritizes ethical sourcing and community engagement often finds itself more prosperous than one that focuses solely on profit.
  • Significance: The mantra emphasizes the interconnectedness of wisdom, sacrifice, and abundance. It teaches that true prosperity is not just about material wealth but also about spiritual and ethical richness.
  • Application: In personal life, individuals can apply this mantra by seeking knowledge, engaging in community service, and practicing mindfulness. This holistic approach can lead to a more fulfilling and prosperous life.

  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a reminder that prosperity is achieved through a balance of wisdom, ethical actions, and a connection to the divine. By embodying these principles, individuals and communities can thrive in harmony with the universe.
