अच्छा यो गन्ता नाधमानम् ऊती इत्याऽ विप्रं हवमानं गृणन्तम् |उप त्मनि दधानो धुर्य ā3शून सहस्राणि शतानि वज्रबाहुः ||
acchā yo gantā nādhamānam ūtī itthā vipraṁ havamānaṁ gṛṇantam | upa tmani dadhāno dhury ā3śūn sahasrāṇi śatāni vajrabāhuḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • acchā: Good, auspicious
    • yo: Who
    • gantā: Goes, travels
    • nādhamānam: Without any hindrance
    • ūtī: Strength, power
    • itthā: Thus, in this manner
    • vipraṁ: The wise one, the learned
    • havamānaṁ: Offering, invocation
    • gṛṇantam: Singing, chanting
    • upa: Near, towards
    • tmani: In oneself
    • dadhāno: Placing, establishing
    • dhury: Burden, responsibility
    • ā3śūn: Quickly, swiftly
    • sahasrāṇi: Thousands
    • śatāni: Hundreds
    • vajrabāhuḥ: One with strong arms, powerful
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the auspicious journey of a wise individual who moves forward without any obstacles.
    • The phrase "good who goes without hindrance" suggests that a person who is wise and knowledgeable can navigate life's challenges effectively.
    • The invocation of strength and power indicates that such wisdom is supported by inner strength and the ability to overcome difficulties.
    • The mention of "singing" and "chanting" emphasizes the importance of expressing gratitude and devotion, which can be seen in various cultures through prayers and hymns.
    • The idea of placing responsibilities upon oneself and doing so swiftly reflects the importance of taking action and being proactive in life.
    • The reference to "thousands and hundreds" signifies abundance and the potential for great achievements when one is guided by wisdom and strength.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • A student preparing for exams embodies the essence of this mantra by studying diligently (wisdom) and overcoming distractions (hindrances).
    • A leader who inspires their team to work together towards a common goal demonstrates the power of collective strength and wisdom.
    • In a community, individuals who come together to support each other during difficult times exemplify the idea of placing responsibilities on oneself and acting swiftly to help others.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of wisdom, strength, and proactive action in overcoming life's challenges.
    • It serves as a reminder that with the right mindset and support, individuals can achieve great things and navigate through obstacles.
    • In real life, applying the principles of this mantra can lead to personal growth, community support, and successful endeavors.
