स्वस्ति नो दिवो अग्नि पृथिव्याः विश्वायुर धेहि यजथाय देव |सचेमहि तव दसम प्रकेतैर उरुष्याण न उरुभिः देव शंसैः ||
svasti no divo agne pṛthivyā viśvāyur dhehi yajathāya deva | sacemahi tava dasma praketair uruṣyā ṇa urubhir deva śaṁsaiḥ ||
  • svasti no divo agne: This phrase translates to "May there be well-being from the divine Agni (fire)." Agni is often seen as a symbol of purity and transformation. In real life, this can be related to the importance of fire in rituals, cooking, and even in the context of energy and warmth in our homes.
  • pṛthivyā viśvāyur dhehi: This means "Grant us the universal life force from the earth." Here, Pṛthivī (the Earth) is personified, emphasizing the connection between humanity and nature. In practical terms, this can be seen in sustainable living practices, where we seek to live in harmony with the earth, ensuring that we respect and preserve our environment for future generations.
  • yajathāya deva: This translates to "For the sake of the divine offering." It highlights the importance of rituals and offerings in connecting with the divine. In modern life, this can be reflected in acts of charity and community service, where individuals offer their time and resources for the greater good.
  • sacemahi tava dasma praketair: This means "We invoke your presence with our offerings." This line emphasizes the act of invocation and the importance of intention behind our actions. In everyday life, this can be related to setting intentions in our personal and professional endeavors, ensuring that we approach tasks with purpose.
  • uruṣyā ṇa urubhir deva śaṁsaiḥ: This translates to "May we be blessed with your abundant grace." It signifies the desire for divine blessings and support. In real life, this can be seen in the way people seek guidance and support from their communities, mentors, or spiritual practices during challenging times.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a deep yearning for well-being, connection with nature, and divine blessings. Its significance lies in the reminder of our interdependence with the earth and the divine. In real life, it encourages us to engage in sustainable practices, perform acts of kindness, set meaningful intentions, and seek support from our communities. By embodying these principles, we can create a harmonious existence that honors both our spiritual and earthly connections.
