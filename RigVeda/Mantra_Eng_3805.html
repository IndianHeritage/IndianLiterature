स हि ष्मा विश्वचर्षणिर अभिमाति सहो दधे |अग्न एषु क्षयेष्वा रेवन् नः शुक्र दीदिहि द्युमत पावक दीदिहि ||
sa hi ṣmā viśvacarṣaṇir abhimāti saho dadhe | agna eṣu kṣayeṣv ā revan naḥ śukra dīdihi dyumat pāvaka dīdihi ||
  • sa hi ṣmā viśvacarṣaṇir abhimāti saho dadhe: This line can be translated to "Indeed, the one who is the universal mover, may he grant us strength."
    • sa hi: "Indeed" or "truly," indicating affirmation.
    • ṣmā: Refers to "the one" or "that being."
    • viśvacarṣaṇir: "Universal mover" or "one who moves all," symbolizing a force that drives the universe.
    • abhimāti: "May he grant" or "may he bestow," a request for blessings.
    • saho dadhe: "Strength" or "power," emphasizing the need for inner strength.
  • agna eṣu kṣayeṣv ā revan naḥ: This translates to "O Agni, in the moments of decline, may you shine upon us."
    • agna: Refers to "Agni," the fire god, symbolizing energy, transformation, and purification.
    • eṣu: "In these" or "in these moments," referring to specific situations.
    • kṣayeṣv: "Of decline" or "in times of loss," indicating challenging times.
    • ā revan naḥ: "May you shine upon us," a plea for guidance and support.
  • śukra dīdihi dyumat pāvaka dīdihi: This means "Shine brightly, O pure one, O luminous fire."
    • śukra: "Pure" or "bright," referring to the purity of Agni.
    • dīdihi: "Shine" or "illuminate," a call for enlightenment.
    • dyumat: "Luminous" or "radiant," emphasizing the brilliance of Agni.
    • pāvaka: Another name for Agni, meaning "the purifier," highlighting the transformative power of fire.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda emphasizes the importance of seeking strength and guidance from the divine, particularly in challenging times. It acknowledges the universal forces that drive existence and the need for inner strength to navigate life's difficulties. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied during moments of crisis or uncertainty, reminding individuals to seek inner resilience and divine support. It encourages a connection with the transformative power of fire, symbolizing purification and renewal, which can be relevant in personal growth, overcoming obstacles, and finding clarity in difficult situations.
