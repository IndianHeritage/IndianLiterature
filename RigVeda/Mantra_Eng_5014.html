प्र याम महिम्ना महिनासु चेकिते द्युम्नेभिर अन्याऽऽपसाम् अपस्तमा |रथ इव बृहती विभ्वने कृतोपस्तुत्या चिकीषुषा सरस्वती ||
pra yā mahimnā mahināsu cekite dyumnebhir anyā apasām apastamā | ratha iva bṛhatī vibhvane kṛtopastutyā cikituṣā sarasvatī ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • pra: forward, towards
    • yā: who, which
    • mahimnā: by greatness, by glory
    • mahināsu: in the great ones
    • cekite: has been known, has been recognized
    • dyumnebhir: by the shining ones, by the luminous
    • anyā: other, different
    • apasām: of the waters
    • apastamā: the best of waters
    • ratha: chariot
    • iva: like, as
    • bṛhatī: the great one, the vast
    • vibhvane: in the vastness, in the expanse
    • kṛtopastutyā: by the act of praise, by the offering of homage
    • cikituṣā: by the one who knows, by the wise
    • sarasvatī: Saraswati, the goddess of knowledge and wisdom
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the goddess Saraswati, who embodies knowledge, wisdom, and the arts.
    • The phrase "pra yā mahimnā" suggests a call to the greatness that is recognized in the divine.
    • It emphasizes the importance of knowledge and wisdom in achieving greatness, akin to how a chariot (ratha) moves forward with purpose and direction.
    • The reference to "dyumnebhir" indicates the illumination that knowledge brings, similar to how the sun brightens the day.
    • By acknowledging the "best of waters" (apastamā), it signifies the purity and depth of knowledge that nourishes the mind and spirit.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In education, students who seek knowledge with dedication often achieve great success, much like the chariot that moves swiftly towards its destination.
    • Professionals who continuously learn and adapt to new information can navigate their careers effectively, reflecting the wisdom of Saraswati.
    • Artists and musicians draw inspiration from the goddess, channeling creativity and knowledge into their work, leading to cultural enrichment.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of knowledge and wisdom in our lives, urging us to seek greatness through learning and understanding.
    • It is applicable in various scenarios, from personal development to professional growth, emphasizing that wisdom is a guiding force.
    • By invoking Saraswati, we remind ourselves of the importance of education, creativity, and the pursuit of truth in our journey through life.
