यः शाश्वतो मह्य एनो दधानान अमन्यमानान् चर्वा जग्हान | यः शर्द्धते नानुददाति श्रिध्यां यो दस्योर हन्ता स जनास इन्द्रः ||
yaḥ śaśvato mahy eno dadhānān amanyamānāñ charvā jaghāna | yaḥ śardhate nānudadāti śṛdhyāṁ yo dasyor hantā sa janāsa indraḥ ||
  • yaḥ - He who
  • śaśvato - is eternal
  • mahy - to me
  • eno - this one
  • dadhānān - who holds
  • amanyamānāñ - without any doubt
  • charvā - the one who destroys
  • jaghāna - has struck down
  • yaḥ - he who
  • śardhate - supports
  • nānudadāti - does not abandon
  • śṛdhyāṁ - in the assembly
  • yo - who
  • dasyor - of the enemies
  • hantā - the slayer
  • sa - he
  • janāsa - of the people
  • indraḥ - is Indra

This mantra speaks of Indra, the king of the gods in Vedic literature, who is portrayed as an eternal protector and a slayer of enemies. The mantra emphasizes the qualities of Indra as a deity who is unwavering in his support for those who have faith in him and who actively engages in the protection of his devotees.


In real life, this can be interpreted as a reminder of the importance of having steadfast support in our lives, whether it be from friends, family, or a higher power. Just as Indra is depicted as a protector, individuals can find strength in their relationships and communities. For example, during challenging times, having a supportive network can help one overcome obstacles, much like how Indra aids his followers in battle against adversaries.


The mantra also highlights the idea of courage and resilience. Indra does not abandon those who are in need, which can inspire individuals to stand firm in their convictions and support others in their struggles. This can be seen in various social movements where individuals come together to fight against injustice, embodying the spirit of Indra as a protector and slayer of negativity.


Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its portrayal of unwavering support and protection. It serves as a reminder that, like Indra, we can be sources of strength for ourselves and others. In real-life scenarios, this mantra encourages individuals to cultivate strong relationships, stand up against challenges, and support one another in the face of adversity. By embodying these qualities, we can create a more resilient and compassionate community.
