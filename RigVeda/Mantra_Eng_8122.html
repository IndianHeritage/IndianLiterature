असाव्य अंशुर मदायाप्सु दक्षो गिरिष्ठाः |श्येनो न योनिम आसदत ||
asāvy aṁśur madāyāpsu dakṣo giriṣṭhāḥ | śyeno na yonim āsadat ||
  • asāvy: This term can be interpreted as "unfettered" or "free." It suggests a state of being unbound or liberated.
  • aṁśur: This word means "ray" or "light." It symbolizes knowledge, enlightenment, or divine energy.
  • madāyāpsu: This can be broken down into "madā" (intoxication or bliss) and "āpsu" (in the waters). Together, it conveys a sense of being immersed in blissful waters, representing emotional or spiritual fulfillment.
  • dakṣo: This term means "skilled" or "capable." It refers to someone who is adept and proficient in their actions.
  • giriṣṭhāḥ: This word translates to "the highest" or "the best." It denotes superiority or excellence.
  • śyeno: This means "hawk" or "eagle," symbolizing sharp vision, focus, and the ability to rise above challenges.
  • na: This is a negation, meaning "not." It indicates the absence of something.
  • yonim: This word means "womb" or "source." It represents the origin or the place from which something emerges.
  • āsadat: This means "to sit" or "to dwell." It implies a state of being or existence in a particular condition.

The mantra can be interpreted as a call for liberation and enlightenment, emphasizing the importance of skill and excellence while also highlighting the need for focus and vision. The imagery of being immersed in blissful waters suggests a deep emotional or spiritual state that one can achieve through skillful actions and a clear vision.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios:

  • Personal Growth: Just as the mantra speaks of being skilled and capable, individuals can strive to develop their talents and abilities, leading to personal fulfillment and success.
  • Professional Excellence: In a work environment, being "dakṣo" (skilled) can lead to career advancement. Professionals can focus on honing their skills to achieve the "giriṣṭhāḥ" (highest) standards in their field.
  • Mindfulness and Clarity: The imagery of the "śyeno" (hawk) encourages individuals to maintain focus and clarity in their goals, avoiding distractions that may lead them away from their true purpose.

In conclusion, this mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of liberation, skill, and vision in achieving a fulfilling life. It encourages individuals to immerse themselves in their passions and strive for excellence, ultimately leading to a state of bliss and enlightenment.
