मन्त्र: पाहि नो अग्न एकया पाह्य उष्ण दवितीयया | पाहि गीरभिः तिसृभिर ऊर्जाम् पते पाहि चतसृभिर वसो ||
pāhi no agna ekayā pāhy u1ta dvitīyayā | pāhi gīrbhis tisṛbhir ūrjām pate pāhi catasṛbhir vaso ||
  • pāhi no agna: This phrase translates to "O Agni, protect us." Agni, the fire god, symbolizes energy, transformation, and purification. In real life, this can be seen as a call for guidance and protection in our endeavors, much like how we seek the support of mentors or leaders in our lives.
  • ekayā pāhy u1ta dvitīyayā: This means "protect us with your single form and your dual form." Here, the "single form" can represent unity and focus, while the "dual form" may signify balance and adaptability. In practical terms, this can relate to the importance of being both steadfast and flexible in our personal and professional lives, adapting to challenges while maintaining our core values.
  • pāhi gīrbhis tisṛbhir ūrjām pate: This translates to "protect us with your threefold energy, O Lord." The "threefold energy" can be interpreted as the physical, mental, and spiritual aspects of life. In everyday scenarios, this emphasizes the need for a holistic approach to well-being, where we nurture our body, mind, and spirit to achieve overall harmony and success.
  • pāhi catasṛbhir vaso: This means "protect us with your fourfold wealth." The "fourfold wealth" can refer to the four goals of life in Hindu philosophy: Dharma (righteousness), Artha (prosperity), Kama (pleasure), and Moksha (liberation). In real life, this mantra encourages us to seek a balanced life that encompasses ethical living, financial stability, enjoyment, and spiritual growth.

Conclusion: The mantra serves as a profound invocation for protection and guidance from Agni, symbolizing the importance of energy, balance, and holistic well-being in our lives. It reminds us to seek support in our endeavors, maintain focus while being adaptable, and strive for a balanced approach to life’s goals. In a world filled with challenges, this mantra can be a source of inspiration, encouraging individuals to cultivate their inner strength and seek harmony in all aspects of life.
