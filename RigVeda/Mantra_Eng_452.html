त्यं चिद्घा दीर्घं पृथुम् मिहो नपातम् अमृध्रम् |प्र च्यावयन्ति यामभिः ||
tyaṁ cid ghā dīrgham pṛthum miho napātam amṛdhram | pra cyāvayanti yāmabhiḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • tyaṁ: that
    • cid: indeed
    • ghā: to be bright or to shine
    • dīrgham: long
    • pṛthum: wide or vast
    • miho: of the sun
    • napātam: offspring or progeny
    • amṛdhram: immortal or eternal
    • pra: forth or forward
    • cyāvayanti: they bring or they lead
    • yāmabhiḥ: with the Yama (the god of death)
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the eternal and vast nature of existence, symbolized by the sun and its offspring.
    • The phrase "that indeed shines long and wide" can be interpreted as a reference to the enduring nature of truth and wisdom.
    • The mention of "immortal" suggests a connection to the soul or the essence of life that transcends physical existence.
    • The invocation of Yama indicates a recognition of the cycle of life and death, emphasizing the importance of living a righteous life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In our daily lives, we often seek wisdom and truth, much like the sun that provides light and guidance. For instance, in decision-making, we strive to make choices that reflect our values and beliefs.
    • The concept of immortality can be seen in the legacies we leave behind, such as the impact of great leaders or thinkers whose ideas continue to influence generations.
    • Understanding the role of Yama can help us appreciate the importance of ethical living, as it reminds us that our actions have consequences beyond our immediate existence.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra encapsulates the essence of life, urging us to seek truth and wisdom while acknowledging the inevitability of death.
    • Its significance lies in the reminder to live a life of purpose, integrity, and awareness of the greater cosmic order.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire individuals to reflect on their values, make conscious choices, and strive for a legacy that contributes positively to society.
