अव त्या बृहतीर इषो विश्वचन्द्रा अमित्रहन |शचीभिः शक्र धूनुहीन्द्र विश्वाभिर ऊतिभिर देवी जनित्री अजीजनद भद्रा जनित्री अजीजनत ||
ava tyā bṛhatīr iṣo viśvaścandrā amitrahan | śacībhiḥ śakra dhūnuhīndra viśvābhir ūtibhir devī janitry ajījanad bhadrā janitry ajījanat ||
  • ava tyā bṛhatīr: This phrase can be interpreted as "may you bring forth greatness." It signifies the aspiration for abundance and prosperity in life.
  • iṣo viśvaścandrā: This translates to "the moon of all powers." The moon is often associated with calmness, reflection, and the nurturing aspects of life, suggesting a holistic approach to power.
  • amitrahan: Meaning "destroyer of enemies," this term emphasizes the importance of overcoming obstacles and negativity in life, whether they be external adversaries or internal doubts.
  • śacībhiḥ: This word refers to "with strength" or "with energy." It highlights the necessity of inner strength and resilience in achieving one's goals.
  • śakra dhūnuhīndra: This can be interpreted as "Indra, the powerful one." Indra is a deity associated with rain, storms, and strength, symbolizing the power to overcome challenges.
  • viśvābhir ūtibhir: This phrase means "with all forms of abundance." It suggests that one should seek prosperity in all aspects of life, including health, wealth, and happiness.
  • devī janitry ajījanad: This translates to "the goddess who gives birth." It signifies the nurturing and creative aspects of the divine feminine, emphasizing the importance of creation and sustenance in life.
  • bhadrā janitry ajījanat: This means "the auspicious mother who gives birth." It reinforces the idea of nurturing and the positive aspects of creation, suggesting that good things come from a supportive and loving environment.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda encapsulates the essence of seeking greatness, overcoming challenges, and nurturing creativity and abundance in life. It is applicable in real-life scenarios such as personal development, where individuals strive to achieve their goals with strength and resilience. In a broader context, it can be seen in community efforts to foster growth and support, emphasizing the importance of collaboration and nurturing relationships. The mantra serves as a reminder that with the right mindset and support, one can overcome obstacles and create a prosperous and fulfilling life.
