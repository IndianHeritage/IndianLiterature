मृळा नो रुद्रोता नो मयस् कृधि क्षयद्वीराय नमसā विद्हेम ते |यच छं च योश च मनुर आयेजे पिता तद अश्यामा तव रुद्र प्रणीतिषु ||
mṛḻā no rudrota no mayas kṛdhi kṣayadvīrāya namasā vidhema te | yac chaṁ ca yoś ca manur āyeje pitā tad aśyāma tava rudra praṇītiṣu ||
  • mṛḻā no rudrota no mayas kṛdhi: This phrase can be interpreted as a plea for peace and tranquility. The term "mṛḻā" refers to calmness or gentleness, while "rudrota" signifies the fierce aspect of Rudra, a deity associated with storms and destruction. The invocation here is to balance these opposing forces, seeking a state of harmony.
  • kṣayadvīrāya namasā vidhema te: This part translates to honoring the powerful and brave. "Kṣayadvīrāya" refers to those who are valiant and capable of overcoming challenges. "Namasā" indicates reverence, suggesting that we should pay homage to such strength and valor.
  • yac chaṁ ca yoś ca manur āyeje: This line speaks to the universal connection and the role of the divine in guiding humanity. "Manur" refers to the progenitor of mankind, indicating a lineage or a guiding force that shapes human destiny.
  • pitā tad aśyāma tava rudra praṇītiṣu: Here, "pitā" means father, suggesting a protective and nurturing aspect of the divine. The phrase implies a request for guidance and support from Rudra in our endeavors, emphasizing the importance of divine intervention in our lives.

  • Significance: This mantra encapsulates the duality of existence—peace and chaos, strength and vulnerability. It serves as a reminder that in our lives, we often face challenges that require both inner calm and external strength.
  • Real-life Application: In modern life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios such as:
    • Conflict Resolution: When faced with disputes, invoking the essence of this mantra can guide individuals towards finding a peaceful resolution while acknowledging the strength needed to confront issues.
    • Leadership: Leaders can draw inspiration from this mantra to balance assertiveness with empathy, ensuring they lead with both strength and compassion.
    • Personal Growth: Individuals can reflect on their own challenges, using the mantra as a meditation tool to cultivate inner peace while striving for personal strength and resilience.

  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a profound reminder of the balance required in life. By acknowledging both the gentle and fierce aspects of existence, we can navigate our paths with wisdom and strength, ultimately leading to a more harmonious life.
