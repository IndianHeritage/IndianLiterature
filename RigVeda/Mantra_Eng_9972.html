आ वो धियं यज्ञियां वर्त ऊतये देवā देवीं यजतां यज्ञियाम इह |सā नो दुधीयद यवसेव गत्वी सहस्रधारा पयसा मही गौः ||
ā vo dhiyaṁ yajñiyāṁ varta ūtaye devā devīṁ yajatāṁ yajñiyām iha | sā no duhīyad yavaseva gatvī sahasradhārā payasā mahī gauḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā vo: To you all
    • dhiyaṁ: Intelligence or understanding
    • yajñiyāṁ: In the context of sacrifice or worship
    • varta: May it be present or may it exist
    • ūtaye: For the purpose of prosperity
    • devā: Gods
    • devīṁ: Goddesses
    • yajatāṁ: Who are being worshipped
    • yajñiyām: In the sacrificial context
    • iha: Here or in this world
    • sā: That
    • no: Our
    • duhīyad: May it nourish or sustain
    • yavaseva: For the purpose of strength or vigor
    • gatvī: Having gone or having reached
    • sahasradhārā: With a thousand streams or flows
    • payasā: With nourishment or milk
    • mahī: Great or vast
    • gauḥ: Cow, symbolizing wealth and nourishment
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the divine presence of gods and goddesses, asking for their blessings in the form of intelligence and prosperity.
    • It emphasizes the importance of worship and sacrifice in achieving a fulfilling life, suggesting that through devotion, one can attain strength and nourishment.
    • The reference to "sahasradhārā payasā" symbolizes abundance and the nurturing qualities associated with the divine, akin to how a cow provides milk, a vital source of sustenance.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern life, this mantra can be applied in various contexts such as education, where students seek divine guidance for intelligence and wisdom in their studies.
    • In business, entrepreneurs may recite this mantra to invoke prosperity and success in their ventures, emphasizing the need for hard work and dedication.
    • During family rituals or gatherings, this mantra can be chanted to seek blessings for health, wealth, and harmony within the household.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine support in achieving a balanced and prosperous life.
    • It serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of worship, intelligence, and nourishment, encouraging individuals to seek higher understanding and strength through devotion.
    • In essence, it highlights the importance of spiritual practices in enhancing one's life and the lives of those around them, making it applicable in various real-life scenarios.
