मन्त्र: स्तुहि स्तुहीद एते घा ते मंहिष्ठासो मघोनाम् | निन्दिताश्वः प्रपथी परमज्या मघस्य मेध्यातिथे ||
stuhi stuhīd ete ghā te maṁhiṣṭhāso maghonām | ninditāśvaḥ prapathī paramajyā maghasya medhyātithe ||
  • stuhi: This word can be translated as "praise" or "worship." It signifies the act of honoring or acknowledging the divine or the forces of nature.
  • stuhīd: This is a form of the verb that means "to praise" or "to extol." It emphasizes the importance of expressing gratitude and reverence.
  • ete: This means "these" and refers to the entities or forces being praised.
  • ghā: This word can be interpreted as "to call upon" or "to invoke," indicating a desire to connect with the divine or the higher powers.
  • te: This means "you," addressing the divine or the forces being invoked.
  • maṁhiṣṭhāso: This term can be understood as "the most excellent" or "the highest," referring to the supreme qualities of the divine or the forces of nature.
  • maghonām: This translates to "of the mighty" or "of the powerful," indicating the strength and influence of the forces being praised.
  • ninditāśvaḥ: This can be interpreted as "the one who is not to be criticized" or "the one who is beyond reproach," suggesting the inviolable nature of the divine.
  • prapathī: This word means "the path" or "the way," symbolizing guidance and direction in life.
  • paramajyā: This translates to "the supreme light" or "the highest radiance," representing enlightenment and wisdom.
  • maghasya: This means "of the wealth" or "of the abundance," indicating prosperity and richness in life.
  • medhyātithe: This can be understood as "the guest of honor" or "the revered guest," emphasizing the importance of hospitality and respect towards others.

In conclusion, this mantra emphasizes the significance of praise, reverence, and the acknowledgment of divine forces in our lives. It teaches us to express gratitude for the abundance and guidance we receive. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various ways, such as:

  • Practicing gratitude daily, recognizing the contributions of others in our lives.
  • Seeking guidance and wisdom in decision-making, reflecting on the paths we choose.
  • Emphasizing the importance of hospitality and respect towards guests, treating them as revered individuals.

The mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of all beings and the importance of honoring the divine presence in our lives, leading to a more harmonious and fulfilling existence.
