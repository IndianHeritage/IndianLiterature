आ याहि पर्वतेभ्यः समुद्रस्याधि विष्टपः |दिवो अमुष्य शासतो दिवं यया दिवावसो ||
ā yāhi parvatebhyaḥ samudrasyādhi viṣṭapaḥ | divo amuṣya śāsato divaṁ yaya divāvaso ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā yāhi: Come here
    • parvatebhyaḥ: From the mountains
    • samudrasyādhi: Above the ocean
    • viṣṭapaḥ: The one who is established
    • divo: Of the sky
    • amuṣya: Of this
    • śāsato: Commanding
    • divaṁ: Heaven
    • yaya: By which
    • divāvaso: Dwelling in the day
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invites a divine presence to come from the mountains, symbolizing strength and stability, to the ocean, which represents depth and vastness.
    • The phrase "the one who is established" suggests a grounding force that connects the heavens and the earth.
    • The reference to "the sky" and "heaven" indicates a higher spiritual realm, emphasizing the connection between the earthly and the divine.
    • The mantra can be seen as a call for guidance and support from higher powers, especially in times of need.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In times of personal crisis, individuals often seek solace in nature, such as mountains and oceans, reflecting the need for grounding and connection to something greater.
    • People may turn to spiritual practices, such as meditation or prayer, to invite divine guidance into their lives, paralleling the call for heavenly support in the mantra.
    • In leadership, the idea of being "established" can relate to the importance of having a strong foundation and clear vision, akin to the stability of mountains guiding the vastness of the ocean.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of seeking divine guidance and support from higher realms, especially during challenging times.
    • It emphasizes the interconnectedness of nature, spirituality, and human experience, reminding us to look beyond ourselves for strength and direction.
    • In real life, this can be applicable in various scenarios, such as personal struggles, leadership challenges, or spiritual quests, where one seeks a deeper connection to the universe and its guiding forces.
