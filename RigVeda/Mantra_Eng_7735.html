उदातैर जितते बृहद् द्वारो देवीर हिरण्ययीः |पवमानेन सुश्रुताः ||
ud ātair jihate bṛhad dvāro devīr hiraṇyayīḥ | pavamānena suṣṭutāḥ ||
  • ud ātair: This phrase can be translated as "from above" or "from the heights." It signifies a divine or elevated perspective, suggesting that the source of inspiration or guidance comes from a higher realm.
  • jihate: This word means "to strive" or "to reach out." It indicates an active pursuit or effort towards something significant.
  • bṛhad: Translated as "great" or "vast," this term emphasizes the magnitude of what is being sought or achieved. It reflects the idea of aiming for something substantial and meaningful.
  • dvāro: This means "door" or "gateway." In a metaphorical sense, it represents opportunities or thresholds that one must cross to achieve their goals.
  • devīr: This word refers to "goddesses" or divine beings. It suggests the presence of divine support or guidance in the endeavors being undertaken.
  • hiraṇyayīḥ: This term translates to "golden" or "wealthy." It symbolizes prosperity, abundance, and the richness of life experiences.
  • pavamānena: This word means "by the purifying" or "by the sacred." It indicates a process of cleansing or sanctifying the efforts and intentions behind one's actions.
  • suṣṭutāḥ: This translates to "well praised" or "well spoken." It implies that the actions taken are commendable and worthy of recognition.

The mantra as a whole can be interpreted as a call to strive for greatness and to seek opportunities that lead to prosperity, guided by divine support and purification of intentions. It emphasizes the importance of aiming high and recognizing the divine forces that assist in our endeavors.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios:

  • Career Aspirations: When pursuing a career, individuals can take inspiration from this mantra to aim for significant achievements, seeking opportunities that align with their values and aspirations.
  • Personal Growth: The mantra encourages individuals to strive for personal development, emphasizing the importance of setting high goals and working towards them with a pure heart.
  • Community Service: In the context of helping others, this mantra can inspire individuals to engage in community service, aiming to create a positive impact and uplift those around them.

Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its encouragement to pursue greatness with divine guidance and a pure intention. It serves as a reminder that striving for meaningful goals, supported by higher powers, can lead to a fulfilling and prosperous life. By applying its teachings, individuals can navigate their paths with purpose and clarity, ultimately contributing to their own growth and the well-being of their communities.
