यस्ते अग्नि सुमतिमर्तो अक्षत सहसः सूनो अति स प्र श्रिण्वे |इषं दधानो वहमानो अश्वैर आ स द्युमाम् अमवान भूषति द्यून ||
yas te agne sumatim marto akṣat sahasaḥ sūno ati sa pra śṛṇve | iṣaṁ dadhāno vahamāno aśvair ā sa dyumām̐ amavān bhūṣati dyūn ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yas: who
    • te: your
    • agne: O Agni (the fire god)
    • sumatim: good understanding or wisdom
    • marto: a mortal or human
    • akṣat: uninjured or unharmed
    • sahasaḥ: with strength or power
    • sūno: of the son
    • ati: exceedingly
    • sa: he
    • pra: forth or out
    • śṛṇve: may hear
    • iṣaṁ: the desire or wish
    • dadhāno: holding or possessing
    • vahamāno: carrying or bearing
    • aśvair: with horses (symbolizing speed and strength)
    • ā: towards
    • sa: he
    • dyumām̐: radiant or shining
    • amavān: the one who is immortal
    • bhūṣati: adorns or embellishes
    • dyūn: the heavens or celestial realms
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes Agni, the fire god, symbolizing purity, transformation, and the divine spark within humans.
    • It emphasizes the importance of wisdom and strength in human life, suggesting that a person who possesses these qualities can achieve greatness.
    • The phrase "may hear" indicates a desire for divine guidance and understanding, highlighting the need for communication with the divine.
    • The imagery of horses represents speed and power, suggesting that with the right qualities, one can swiftly achieve their goals.
    • The reference to the "radiant" and "immortal" signifies the aspiration for enlightenment and eternal truth.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, a leader who embodies wisdom and strength can inspire their team to achieve remarkable results, much like how Agni inspires transformation.
    • In personal development, individuals who seek knowledge and understanding often find themselves on a path to success, akin to the blessings of Agni.
    • In education, students who cultivate wisdom and strength are more likely to excel, reflecting the qualities praised in this mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of wisdom, strength, and divine guidance in our lives.
    • It encourages individuals to seek enlightenment and to embody the qualities that lead to success and fulfillment.
    • In various aspects of life, from personal growth to professional achievements, the principles outlined in this mantra can be applied to foster a deeper connection with the divine and to enhance one's journey through life.
