सद अस्य मादे सद वास्य पीताव इन्द्रः सद अस्य सख्ये चकार |रणा वा ये निषदि सत ते अस्य पुरा विविध्रे सद उ नूतनासः ||
sad asya made sad v asya pītāv indraḥ sad asya sakhye cakāra | raṇā vā ye niṣadi sat te asya purā vividre sad u nūtanāsaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sad: good, auspicious, or beneficial
    • asya: of this
    • made: in my mind or heart
    • pītāv: the one who is to be revered or worshipped
    • indraḥ: Indra, the king of gods, associated with strength and power
    • sakhye: friendship or companionship
    • cakāra: has created or established
    • raṇā: in battle or conflict
    • vā: or
    • niṣadi: those who sit or are present
    • sat: truth or reality
    • te: those
    • purā: in the past
    • vividre: have been separated or divided
    • nūtanāsaḥ: new or renewed
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the auspiciousness and goodness that is created in the mind and heart.
    • It acknowledges Indra, the powerful deity, as a source of strength and companionship.
    • The mantra emphasizes the importance of truth and the bonds of friendship, especially in times of conflict.
    • It reflects on the past divisions and the hope for renewal and unity.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace setting, fostering a culture of collaboration and support can lead to greater productivity and morale, akin to the friendship and strength mentioned in the mantra.
    • During conflicts, whether personal or professional, seeking truth and understanding can help mend relationships and create a renewed sense of unity.
    • In community settings, coming together to support one another in times of need reflects the essence of companionship and strength that this mantra embodies.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for unity, strength, and the importance of truth and friendship.
    • It serves as a reminder that in both personal and communal aspects of life, fostering good relationships and seeking truth can lead to renewal and harmony.
    • This mantra can be applied in various real-life scenarios, from personal relationships to community engagements, emphasizing the timeless values of companionship and strength in overcoming challenges.
