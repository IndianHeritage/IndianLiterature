मन्त्र: द्वादश प्रधयश चक्रं एकं त्रिणि नाभ्याणि क उ तच चिकेट | तस्मिन साकं त्रिशता न शङ्कवोऽर्पिताः षष्टिर न चलाचलासः ||
dvādaśa pradhayaś cakram ekaṁ trīṇi nabhyāni ka u tac ciketa | tasmin sākaṁ triśatā na śaṅkavo 'rpitāḥ ṣaṣṭir na calācalāsaḥ ||
  • dvādaśa pradhayaś cakram ekaṁ: This phrase translates to "the one wheel with twelve spokes." In a real-life context, this can be likened to the concept of a well-rounded life, where each spoke represents different aspects such as health, relationships, career, spirituality, etc. Just as a wheel needs all its spokes to function properly, a balanced life requires attention to all these areas.
  • trīṇi nabhyāni: This means "three hubs." The hubs can symbolize the core values or principles that support our lives. For example, in a business context, these could be integrity, innovation, and customer focus. Each hub is essential for the stability and success of the overall structure.
  • ka u tac ciketa: This translates to "who knows that?" It raises a question about knowledge and awareness. In today's world, where information is abundant, it emphasizes the importance of discernment and wisdom in understanding what truly matters in life.
  • tasmin sākaṁ triśatā na śaṅkavo 'rpitāḥ: This means "in that, together, there are no doubts." It suggests that when one is aligned with their core values and balanced in life, doubts and uncertainties diminish. For instance, a person who is clear about their goals and values is less likely to be swayed by external opinions or fears.
  • ṣaṣṭir na calācalāsaḥ: This translates to "the sixty (or the essence) is not unstable." It implies that a strong foundation leads to stability. In practical terms, this can be seen in organizations that have a clear mission and vision; they tend to be more resilient in the face of challenges.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of balance, core values, and stability in life. It teaches us that just as a wheel needs all its spokes to function effectively, our lives require attention to various aspects to achieve harmony. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in personal development, business strategies, and even in community building. By understanding and implementing these principles, individuals and organizations can navigate life's complexities with greater confidence and clarity.
