ऐषु धा वीरवत् यश उषो मगhoni सूरीषु |ये नो राधांस्य अह्रयाः मगवाणो अरासत सुजाते अश्वसूऋते ||
aiṣu dhā vīravad yaśa uṣo maghoni sūriṣu | ye no rādhāṁsy ahrayā maghavāno arāsata sujāte aśvasūnṛte ||
  • aiṣu dhā: This phrase can be interpreted as "may we be endowed with" or "may we possess." It signifies a desire for blessings and positive attributes.
  • vīravad: This word translates to "brave" or "heroic." It emphasizes the importance of courage and valor in one's life.
  • yaśa: Meaning "fame" or "glory," this term highlights the aspiration for recognition and respect in society.
  • uṣo: This refers to "dawn" or "morning," symbolizing new beginnings, hope, and the dispelling of darkness.
  • maghoni: This word can be understood as "power" or "strength," indicating the importance of inner and outer strength in achieving one's goals.
  • sūriṣu: This translates to "among the heroes" or "in the company of the brave," suggesting the value of companionship and support from like-minded individuals.
  • ye no rādhāṁsy: This phrase means "those who bring us prosperity" or "who bestow upon us wealth," emphasizing the importance of abundance and prosperity in life.
  • ahrayā: This can be interpreted as "who lead" or "who guide," indicating the significance of guidance and leadership in one's journey.
  • maghavāno: This word refers to "the powerful" or "the mighty," reinforcing the idea of strength and capability.
  • arāsata: This translates to "with good fortune" or "with auspiciousness," highlighting the desire for favorable circumstances.
  • sujāte: Meaning "well-born" or "noble," this term signifies the importance of good character and integrity.
  • aśvasūnṛte: This phrase can be interpreted as "in the company of horses" or "with swiftness," symbolizing speed, agility, and the ability to overcome obstacles.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a profound aspiration for bravery, glory, strength, and prosperity. It emphasizes the importance of companionship, guidance, and noble character in achieving one's goals. In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as in personal development, leadership roles, and community building. By embodying the qualities of courage and integrity, individuals can inspire others and create a positive impact in their surroundings. The mantra serves as a reminder to seek strength and support from others while striving for personal and collective success.
