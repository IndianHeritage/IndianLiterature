ते हि स्थिरस्य शवसः सखायः सन्ति धृष्णुया |ते याम्न्ना धृषद्विनस्तमनā पान्ति शाश्वतः ||
te hi sthirasya śavasaḥ sakhāyaḥ santi dhṛṣṇuyā | te yāmann ā dhṛṣadvinas tmanā pānti śaśvataḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • te: they
    • hi: indeed
    • sthirasya: of the stable
    • śavasaḥ: of the body or the physical form
    • sakhāyaḥ: friends or companions
    • santi: exist
    • dhṛṣṇuyā: with courage or strength
    • yāmann: those who
    • ā: towards
    • dhṛṣadvinas: those who are strong or powerful
    • tmanā: with the self
    • pānti: protect or sustain
    • śaśvataḥ: eternally or forever
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the importance of companionship and support among those who are stable and strong.
    • It emphasizes that true friends or companions are those who provide strength and protection, especially in times of need.
    • The idea of "dhṛṣṇuyā" suggests that courage is essential in maintaining these relationships and in facing life's challenges.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace, colleagues who support each other during stressful projects exemplify the essence of this mantra. Their collaboration and mutual encouragement lead to success.
    • In personal life, friends who stand by each other during difficult times, such as illness or loss, embody the concept of "sakhāyaḥ" as they provide emotional and physical support.
    • In sports teams, players who uplift and motivate each other demonstrate the strength of companionship, showcasing how teamwork can lead to victory.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of strong relationships and the role of friends in providing support and courage.
    • It teaches us that in both personal and professional spheres, having reliable companions can lead to resilience and success.
    • In real life, applying the principles of this mantra can foster a sense of community, enhance teamwork, and promote emotional well-being.
