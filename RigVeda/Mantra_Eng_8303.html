विओ ममे यम्या संयतī मदः साकंवृद्धा पयसा पिन्वद अक्षिता |महī अपारे रजसी विवेदिद अभिव्रजनन अक्षितं पाजा आ ददे ||
vi yo mame yamyā saṁyatī madaḥ sākaṁvṛdhā payasā pinvad akṣitā | mahī apāre rajasī vivevidad abhivrajann akṣitam pāja ā dade ||
  • Translation: The mantra can be translated as: "He who, with his might, brings forth the essence of strength, nourished by the abundant flow of energy, may he grant us the unyielding power to overcome obstacles and achieve greatness."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • vi yo: He who
    • mame: my
    • yamyā: with strength
    • saṁyatī: brings forth
    • madaḥ: essence or intoxication
    • sākaṁvṛdhā: nourished by abundance
    • payasā: flow of energy or nourishment
    • pinvad: may he grant
    • akṣitā: unyielding or steadfast
    • mahī: great or vast
    • apāre: boundless
    • rajasī: energy or strength
    • vivevidad: to overcome or conquer
    • abhivrajann: achieving or attaining
    • akṣitam: unyielding or firm
    • pāja: power or strength
    • ā dade: may he grant us
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, a leader who embodies the essence of strength and resilience can inspire their team to overcome challenges and achieve collective goals.
    • In personal development, individuals who seek to harness their inner strength and energy can face life's obstacles with determination and confidence.
    • In sports, athletes often draw upon their mental and physical strength to push through barriers and achieve peak performance.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for strength, resilience, and the ability to overcome challenges. It emphasizes the importance of harnessing one's inner power and energy to achieve greatness. In real-life scenarios, whether in personal growth, leadership, or athletic pursuits, this mantra serves as a reminder that with determination and the right mindset, one can conquer obstacles and reach their full potential. It encourages individuals to seek nourishment from their experiences and to remain steadfast in their pursuits, ultimately leading to success and fulfillment.
