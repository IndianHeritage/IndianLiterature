यद योढया महतो मन्यमानान् साक्षाम तान बाहुभिः शाशदानान् |यद वा नृभिर वृता इन्द्राभियुध्यास तं त्वयाजिं साउश्रवसं जयेम ||
yad yodhayā mahato manyamānān sākṣāma tān bāhubhiḥ śāśadānān | yad vā nṛbhir vṛta indrābhiyudhyās taṁ tvayājiṁ sauśravasaṁ jayema ||
  • Mantra: "yad yodhayā mahato manyamānān sākṣāma tān bāhubhiḥ śāśadānān | yad vā nṛbhir vṛta indrābhiyudhyās taṁ tvayājiṁ sauśravasaṁ jayema ||"
  • Translation: "Whatever great beings, who are considered powerful, are to be fought against, may we conquer them with our strong arms. And if we are surrounded by men, let us fight against them with the strength of Indra, and may we be victorious with your auspicious blessings."

  • Word-by-Word Explanation:
    • yad: Whatever
    • yodhayā: By fighting
    • mahato: Great beings
    • manyamānān: Who are considered powerful
    • sākṣāma: Strong
    • tān: Them
    • bāhubhiḥ: With arms
    • śāśadānān: Who are enduring
    • yad vā: And if
    • nṛbhir: By men
    • vṛta: Surrounded
    • indrābhiyudhyās: Fight with the strength of Indra
    • taṁ: That
    • tvayājiṁ: With your blessings
    • sauśravasaṁ: Auspicious
    • jayema: May we be victorious

  • Real-Life Examples:
    • This mantra can be applied in situations where individuals face challenges or adversities, such as in competitive environments like sports or business. For instance, a sports team may recite this mantra before a crucial match, invoking strength and unity to overcome their opponents.
    • In personal life, when facing conflicts or difficult decisions, individuals can draw inspiration from this mantra to gather their inner strength and confront challenges head-on, much like a student preparing for an important exam.

  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for strength and unity in the face of adversity. It emphasizes the importance of collective effort and the invocation of divine blessings to achieve victory.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra serves as a reminder that challenges can be overcome through determination, teamwork, and the support of higher powers or principles. It encourages individuals to confront their fears and strive for success, making it a powerful tool for motivation and resilience.
