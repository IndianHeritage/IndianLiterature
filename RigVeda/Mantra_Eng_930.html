को अद्य युङ्क्ते धुरी गा ऋतस्य शिमीवत भामिनो दुरहृणायून |आसन्निषून हृत्स्वसो मयोभून य एषाम् भृत्यां ऋणधत स जीवात ||
ko adya yuṅkte dhuri gā ṛtasya śimīvato bhāmino durhṛṇāyūn | āsanniṣūn hṛtsvaso mayobhūn ya eṣām bhṛtyām ṛṇadhat sa jīvāt ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ko: who
    • adya: today
    • yuṅkte: will unite
    • dhuri: in the burden
    • gā: song
    • ṛtasya: of truth
    • śimīvato: of the one who is auspicious
    • bhāmino: of the one who is radiant
    • durhṛṇāyūn: with difficult to bear burdens
    • āsanniṣūn: those who are near
    • hṛtsvaso: of the heart
    • mayobhūn: those who are filled with my essence
    • ya: who
    • eṣām: of them
    • bhṛtyām: of the servants
    • ṛṇadhat: who bears the debt
    • sa: he
    • jīvāt: lives
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra poses a question about who will unite today in the burdens of truth and auspiciousness.
    • It reflects on the nature of support and companionship in facing life's challenges.
    • The mention of "difficult to bear burdens" signifies the struggles that individuals encounter in their lives.
    • It emphasizes the importance of community and support systems, suggesting that those who are near and filled with essence can help alleviate these burdens.
    • The phrase "who bears the debt" indicates a sense of responsibility and the interconnectedness of individuals in a community.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace, team members often unite to share the workload, especially during challenging projects, reflecting the essence of this mantra.
    • In families, members support each other during tough times, such as illness or financial difficulties, embodying the spirit of shared burdens.
    • Community service initiatives, where individuals come together to help those in need, illustrate the principle of collective support and responsibility.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of unity, support, and shared responsibility in overcoming life's challenges.
    • It serves as a reminder that we are not alone in our struggles and that collaboration can lead to resilience and strength.
    • In real-life scenarios, whether in personal relationships, workplaces, or communities, the essence of this mantra encourages individuals to come together, support one another, and share the burdens of life.
