अभि द्युम्नं बृहद् यश इषस् पते दिदीहि देव देवयुः |विकोषं मध्यं युवा ||
abhi dyumnam bṛhad yaśa iṣas pate didīhi deva devayuḥ | vi kośam madhyamaṁ yuva ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • abhi: towards, for
    • dyumnam: glory, brilliance
    • bṛhad: great, vast
    • yaśa: fame, renown
    • iṣas: of power, strength
    • pate: O Lord, master
    • didīhi: grant, bestow
    • deva: divine, god
    • devayuḥ: of the divine beings
    • vi: in, within
    • kośam: treasure, wealth
    • madhyamaṁ: middle, central
    • yuva: youthful, young
  • Overall Meaning: This mantra is a prayer to the divine for the bestowal of great glory and fame, emphasizing the importance of strength and power. It seeks blessings for the wealth of knowledge and wisdom that resides in the central aspect of youthfulness.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, individuals often seek recognition and success. This mantra can be interpreted as a call for divine support in achieving one's career goals, emphasizing the importance of hard work and integrity.
    • In education, students may recite this mantra to invoke clarity of thought and wisdom, aiming for excellence in their studies and future endeavors.
    • In personal development, the focus on youthfulness can be seen as a reminder to maintain a fresh perspective and adaptability in life, encouraging individuals to embrace change and growth.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its holistic approach to life, encouraging individuals to seek divine blessings for strength, wisdom, and recognition. It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from professional aspirations to personal growth, reminding us that with divine support and our own efforts, we can achieve greatness and contribute positively to society.
