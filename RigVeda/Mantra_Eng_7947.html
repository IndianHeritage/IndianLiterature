अभि ब्रह्मीर अनूषत यह्वीः ऋतस्य मातरः |मार्मृज्यन्ते दिवः शिशुम् ||
abhi brahmīr anūṣata yahvīr ṛtasya mātaraḥ | marmṛjyante divaḥ śiśum ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • abhi: towards, in the direction of
    • brahmīr: the ultimate reality, the cosmic spirit
    • anūṣata: who is eternal, who exists
    • yahvīr: the one who is powerful, the one who is strong
    • ṛtasya: of truth, of order, of righteousness
    • mātaraḥ: the mothers, the nurturing forces
    • marmṛjyante: are being wiped away, are being cleansed
    • divaḥ: of the heavens, of the divine
    • śiśum: the child, the young one
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks to the relationship between the ultimate reality (Brahman) and the nurturing forces of the universe.
    • The phrase "abhi brahmīr anūṣata" suggests a movement towards the ultimate truth, indicating a journey or aspiration towards understanding the divine.
    • "Yahvīr ṛtasya mātaraḥ" emphasizes the strength and nurturing aspect of truth, portraying it as a motherly force that supports and sustains life.
    • The latter part, "marmṛjyante divaḥ śiśum," indicates a cleansing or purification process that occurs in the divine realm, particularly for the innocent or the young, symbolizing renewal and protection.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In personal growth, individuals often seek truth and understanding, akin to the journey towards Brahman. For instance, someone may engage in meditation or philosophical study to gain deeper insights into their existence.
    • The nurturing aspect of truth can be seen in parenting, where parents guide their children with love and wisdom, embodying the "mātaraḥ" aspect of the mantra.
    • In community service, organizations that support the vulnerable and young, such as orphanages or shelters, reflect the cleansing and protective qualities described in the mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of seeking truth and understanding in our lives, portraying it as a nurturing force that guides and protects us.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal development to community support, reminding us of the importance of truth and nurturing in our journey through life.
    • Ultimately, it encourages us to aspire towards higher truths while recognizing the protective and nurturing forces that aid us along the way.
