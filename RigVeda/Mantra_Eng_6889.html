यो अग्निं तंवोऽदामे देवम् मार्तः सपर्णति |तस्मा इद् दीदायद् वसु ||
yo agniṁ tanvo3 dame devam martaḥ saparyati | tasmā id dīdayad vasu ||
  • Word Breakdown:
    • yo: who
    • agniṁ: fire
    • tanvo: of the body
    • dame: in the house
    • devam: the divine
    • martaḥ: mortals
    • saparyati: worships
    • tasmā: therefore
    • id: this
    • dīdayad: shines
    • vasu: wealth
  • Meaning:
    • This mantra speaks about the relationship between fire (Agni), the divine, and mortals.
    • It emphasizes the importance of worshipping the divine presence in our lives, symbolized by fire, which is a purifying and transformative element.
    • It suggests that those who worship the divine with sincerity will be blessed with wealth and prosperity.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In many cultures, fire is used in rituals and ceremonies to symbolize purification and the presence of the divine. For instance, in Hindu weddings, the sacred fire (Agni) is a witness to the vows taken by the couple.
    • In modern life, the mantra can be interpreted as a reminder to cultivate a sense of gratitude and reverence for the resources we have, leading to a more fulfilling and prosperous life.
    • People often create a space in their homes for meditation or prayer, where they can connect with the divine, much like the 'dame' or house mentioned in the mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of recognizing and honoring the divine in our daily lives.
    • By fostering a sense of worship and gratitude, we can attract positive energies and abundance into our lives.
    • It serves as a reminder that our actions and intentions can lead to prosperity, both materially and spiritually.
