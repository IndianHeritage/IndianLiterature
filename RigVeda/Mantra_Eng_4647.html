अध स्मा ते चर्षणयो यद एजान इन्द्र त्रातोभव वरूताः |अस्माकासो ये नृतमासो आर्य इन्द्र सूरयो दधिरे पुरो नः ||
adha smā te carṣaṇayo yad ejān indra trātota bhavā varūtā | asmākāso ye nṛtamāso arya indra sūrayo dadhire puro naḥ ||
  • Adha smā te carṣaṇayo: This phrase can be translated as "Here we are, O Indra, with our offerings." It signifies the act of presenting oneself and one's intentions before a higher power, in this case, Indra, who is the king of gods in Vedic literature.
  • Yad ejān indra trātota bhavā varūtā: This translates to "May you, Indra, protect us from all that is harmful." It emphasizes the need for divine protection and guidance in overcoming obstacles and adversities in life.
  • Asmākāso ye nṛtamāso arya: This means "Those who are truthful and noble among us." It highlights the importance of truthfulness and righteousness in our actions and interactions with others.
  • Indra sūrayo dadhire puro naḥ: This can be interpreted as "Indra, the strong, grant us a place of honor." It reflects the desire for recognition and respect, not just from the divine but also within the community.

  • Significance: This mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking divine assistance and protection while emphasizing the values of truth and nobility. It serves as a reminder of the importance of aligning oneself with higher principles and seeking guidance in times of need.
  • Real-life Application: In everyday life, this mantra can be applied in various situations such as:
    • During challenging times, individuals can recite this mantra to invoke a sense of protection and support from a higher power.
    • In personal and professional relationships, embodying the values of truthfulness and nobility can lead to stronger bonds and mutual respect.
    • In community settings, this mantra can inspire collective efforts towards achieving common goals while upholding ethical standards.

  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the interconnectedness of the divine, the individual, and the community. By seeking protection from Indra and committing to truthfulness and nobility, individuals can navigate life's challenges with strength and integrity. This mantra encourages a holistic approach to life, where spiritual, ethical, and communal values are harmoniously integrated.
