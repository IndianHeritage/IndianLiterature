को वो महान्ति महतामुद अश्नवत कस्काव्यामरुतः को ह पौंस्याः |यूयं ह भूमिं किरणं न रेजथा प्र यद भरध्वे सुविताया दावने ||
ko vo mahānti mahatām ud aśnavat kas kāvyā marutaḥ ko ha pauṁsyā | yūyaṁ ha bhūmiṁ kiraṇaṁ na rejatha pra yad bharadhve suvitāya dāvane ||
  • ko vo mahānti mahatām ud aśnavat: This phrase can be translated to "Who among the great ones is the most powerful?" It poses a question about the nature of greatness and power, inviting contemplation on what it means to be truly significant in the grand scheme of things.
  • kas kāvyā marutaḥ: This translates to "Who among the poets of the winds?" Here, "marutaḥ" refers to the winds, often personified in Vedic literature. It suggests a connection between creativity (poetry) and the natural forces of the world.
  • ko ha pauṁsyā: This means "Who is the one among the men?" This line emphasizes the human aspect, questioning the role of humanity in the context of greatness and power.
  • yūyaṁ ha bhūmiṁ kiraṇaṁ na rejatha: Translated, this means "You all do not shine upon the earth like the rays of light." It suggests that there is a lack of brilliance or impact among those being addressed, contrasting the potential for greatness with the current state of being.
  • pra yad bharadhve suvitāya dāvane: This can be interpreted as "When you all gather for the good of the world." It calls for unity and collective action towards a beneficial purpose, highlighting the importance of collaboration in achieving greatness.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda reflects on the themes of greatness, power, and the role of humanity in the universe. It encourages individuals to reflect on their own contributions and the impact they have on the world around them. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios such as leadership, teamwork, and community service. By recognizing the potential for greatness within ourselves and others, we can strive to work together for the greater good, much like how diverse elements of nature come together to create harmony. The mantra serves as a reminder that true significance comes not just from individual power, but from collective efforts towards a common goal.
