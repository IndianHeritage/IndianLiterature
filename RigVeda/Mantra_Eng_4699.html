नूनं न इन्द्रापराय च स्याभवामृळीक उता नो अभिष्टौ |इथ्था गृणन्तो महिनस्य शर्मन् दिवि ष्यामा पार्ये गोषतमाः ||
nūnaṁ na indrāparāya ca syā bhavā mṛḻīka uta no abhiṣṭau | itthā gṛṇanto mahinasya śarman divi ṣyāma pārye goṣatamāḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • nūnaṁ: indeed, certainly
    • na: not
    • indrāparāya: the one who is beyond Indra (the king of gods)
    • ca: and
    • syā: may be
    • bhavā: you (or your presence)
    • mṛḻīka: the one who is gentle or kind
    • uta: or
    • no: our
    • abhiṣṭau: in our aspirations or desires
    • itthā: thus
    • gṛṇanto: singing or praising
    • mahinasya: of the great one (often referring to the divine)
    • śarman: joy, comfort, or protection
    • divi: in the heavens
    • ṣyāma: may we be
    • pārye: in the company of
    • goṣatamāḥ: the best of the cows (symbolizing wealth and prosperity)
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra expresses a deep yearning for divine presence and blessings.
    • The speaker acknowledges the greatness of the divine and seeks comfort and joy in their presence.
    • It emphasizes the importance of kindness and gentleness in our aspirations.
    • The reference to "the best of the cows" symbolizes abundance and prosperity, which are often sought in life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a personal context, one might recite this mantra during times of uncertainty, seeking divine guidance and comfort.
    • In a community setting, it can be used during rituals or gatherings to invoke blessings for prosperity and harmony.
    • In professional life, individuals may reflect on the qualities of kindness and gentleness while pursuing their goals, ensuring that their ambitions do not overshadow their values.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of seeking divine support in our endeavors.
    • It highlights the significance of maintaining kindness and gentleness in our aspirations, ensuring that we remain grounded and connected to our values.
    • In real life, it encourages individuals to cultivate a sense of community and shared joy, fostering an environment of abundance and prosperity.
