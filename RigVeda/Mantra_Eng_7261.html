त्वं न इन्द्र ऋतयुस् त्वानिदो न तृम्पसि |मध्य वसिष्व तुविनृम्णोर्वोर न दासं शिष्णाथो हठैः ||
tvaṁ na indra ṛtayus tvānido ni tṛmpasi | madhye vasiṣva tuvinṛmṇorvor ni dāsaṁ śiśnatho hathaiḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • tvaṁ: You
    • na: Not
    • indra: O Indra (the king of gods)
    • ṛtayus: Of truth or righteousness
    • tvānido: You are the one who is invoked
    • ni: Down
    • tṛmpasi: You are the one who is satisfied
    • madhye: In the midst
    • vasiṣva: Dwell
    • tuvinṛmṇor: Of the two (or dual) forces
    • vor: Of the two
    • ni: Down
    • dāsaṁ: Servant or devotee
    • śiśnatho: With strength
    • hathaiḥ: With blows or strikes
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra addresses Indra, the deity associated with strength, rain, and thunder. It emphasizes the importance of truth and righteousness.
    • The phrase "You are the one who is invoked" suggests a call for divine assistance and presence in the midst of challenges.
    • The mention of "servant" indicates humility and devotion, recognizing the need for divine support in overcoming obstacles.
    • The dual forces could symbolize the balance of opposing elements in life, such as good and evil, or strength and weakness.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In times of personal crisis, individuals often seek divine guidance or support, similar to invoking Indra for strength and clarity.
    • In leadership, the balance of opposing forces is crucial; a leader must navigate between different interests and values, much like the dual forces mentioned in the mantra.
    • In community settings, the mantra can be seen as a call for unity and collective strength, where individuals come together to support one another in times of need.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of invoking divine support in the pursuit of truth and righteousness.
    • It highlights the need for balance in life, recognizing the dualities we face and the strength that comes from unity and devotion.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire individuals to seek guidance, foster community support, and strive for ethical living amidst challenges.
