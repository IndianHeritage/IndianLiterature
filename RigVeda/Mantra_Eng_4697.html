त्वं तां इन्द्रोभयाम् अमित्रान् दासाः वृत्राण्य् आर्याः च शूरः | वधीर वनेव सुदितेभिर् अटकैर् आ पृत्सु दर्षि नृणां नृतम ||
tvaṁ tām̐ indrobhayām̐ amitrān dāsā vṛtrāṇy āryā ca śūra | vadhīr vaneva sudhitebhir atkair ā pṛtsu darṣi nṛṇāṁ nṛtama ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • tvaṁ: You
    • tām̐: That
    • indrobhayām̐: O Indra, the powerful
    • amitrān: From enemies
    • dāsā: Servants or followers
    • vṛtrāṇy: The obstacles or foes
    • āryā: Noble or honorable
    • ca: And
    • śūra: Brave or valiant
    • vadhīr: To kill or overcome
    • vaneva: Like a forest
    • sudhitebhir: With good or auspicious things
    • atkair: With strength or power
    • ā: From
    • pṛtsu: In the battles
    • darṣi: To see or perceive
    • nṛṇāṁ: Of men
    • nṛtama: The best or the most excellent
  • Overall Meaning: This mantra is a prayer to Indra, the king of the gods, seeking his strength and support in overcoming obstacles and enemies. It emphasizes the importance of bravery and nobility in facing challenges, and it invokes the divine to aid in achieving victory in battles, both literal and metaphorical.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • Overcoming Challenges: Just as a student may face academic challenges, invoking the spirit of this mantra can inspire them to seek help and work hard to overcome difficulties.
    • Facing Adversity: In a workplace scenario, an employee may encounter competition or conflict. This mantra serves as a reminder to remain noble and brave, seeking support from colleagues to navigate through tough situations.
    • Personal Growth: Individuals facing personal struggles, such as health issues or emotional turmoil, can draw strength from this mantra, encouraging them to confront their fears and seek healing.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine assistance in overcoming adversities and achieving excellence. It teaches us the values of bravery, nobility, and the importance of seeking help when faced with challenges. In real life, this mantra can be applied in various situations, from personal struggles to professional challenges, reminding us that with strength and support, we can overcome any obstacle.
