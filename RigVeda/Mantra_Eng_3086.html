अधā ह यद वयं अग्ने त्वायā पद्भिर हस्तेभिः चक्रमा तनूबिः | रथं न क्रन्तो अपसā भुरिजोर ऋतं येमुः सुध्या आशुषाणाः ||
adhā ha yad vayam agne tvāyā paḍbhir hastebhiś cakṛmā tanūbhiḥ | rathaṁ na kranto apasā bhurijor ṛtaṁ yemuḥ sudhya āśuṣāṇāḥ ||
  • adhā ha yad vayam agne: This phrase translates to "Here, indeed, we invoke you, O Agni." Agni, the fire god, represents energy, transformation, and the divine spark within all beings. In real life, invoking Agni can symbolize the pursuit of knowledge and enlightenment.
  • tvāyā paḍbhir hastebhiś cakṛmā tanūbhiḥ: This means "We have made you our own with our hands and bodies." This signifies the importance of personal effort and dedication in achieving one's goals. For example, in a professional setting, one must work hard and apply their skills to succeed.
  • rathaṁ na kranto apasā bhurijor: This translates to "May we not be hindered in our journey." The 'ratha' or chariot symbolizes the journey of life. This line emphasizes the need for perseverance and resilience in the face of challenges. In everyday life, this can relate to overcoming obstacles in personal or professional endeavors.
  • ṛtaṁ yemuḥ sudhya āśuṣāṇāḥ: This means "May we attain the truth and the essence of life." 'Rta' refers to cosmic order and truth. This line encourages individuals to seek truth and righteousness in their actions. For instance, in ethical decision-making, one should strive to align their choices with moral values.

  • Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda emphasizes the importance of invoking divine energy (Agni) in our lives, the necessity of personal effort, the significance of perseverance in our journeys, and the pursuit of truth. In real-life scenarios, these principles can guide individuals in their personal growth, professional endeavors, and ethical decision-making. By embodying these values, one can lead a more fulfilling and purposeful life.
