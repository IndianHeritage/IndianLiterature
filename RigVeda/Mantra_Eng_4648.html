अनु ते दायि मह इन्द्रियाय सत्रां ते विश्वं अनु वृत्रहत्ये |अनु क्षत्रं अनु सहो यजत्रेन्द्र देवैभिर अनु ते नृषह्ये ||
anu te dāyi maha indriyāya satrā te viśvam anu vṛtrahatye | anu kṣatram anu saho yajatrendra devebhir anu te nṛṣahye ||
  • anu te dāyi maha indriyāya: This phrase can be translated as "to you, the great one, who grants strength." Here, "anu" means "to" or "towards," "dāyi" refers to "the giver," "maha" means "great," and "indriyāya" relates to "strength" or "power." In real life, this can be seen as a call for empowerment and support in our endeavors, much like seeking guidance from a mentor or a leader who inspires us to achieve our goals.
  • satrā te viśvam anu vṛtrahatye: This translates to "may all beings be in harmony with you, as you conquer obstacles." "Satrā" means "all beings," "te" means "you," "viśvam" refers to "the universe," and "vṛtrahatye" means "conquering obstacles." This can be related to teamwork in a workplace where collaboration leads to overcoming challenges, similar to how a sports team works together to win a game.
  • anu kṣatram anu saho: This means "to the strength and support." "Kṣatram" refers to "strength" or "power," and "saho" means "support." In everyday life, this can be seen in the context of community support systems, where individuals come together to provide strength to one another during difficult times, such as during natural disasters or personal crises.
  • yajatrendra devebhir anu te nṛṣahye: This translates to "may we be blessed by the divine, as we strive for success." "Yajatrendra" refers to "the one who performs sacrifices," "devebhir" means "by the gods," and "nṛṣahye" means "to achieve success." This can be likened to the idea of hard work and dedication leading to success, where individuals often seek blessings or guidance from higher powers, whether through prayer, meditation, or personal reflection.

  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking strength, support, and harmony in our lives. It highlights the interconnectedness of individuals and the divine, suggesting that through collaboration and empowerment, we can overcome obstacles and achieve success. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as in personal relationships, professional environments, and community efforts, where collective strength and support lead to greater achievements and a harmonious existence.
