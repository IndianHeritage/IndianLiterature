त्वं वलस्य गोमतोऽपावर अद्रिवो बिलम् |त्वां देवाः अभिभ्युषस तुज्यमानास आविषुः ||
tvaṁ valasya gomato 'pāvar adrivo bilam | tvāṁ devā abibhyuṣas tujyamānāsa āviṣuḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • tvaṁ: You
    • valasya: of the strong
    • gomato: of the cow (or wealth)
    • 'pāvar: the one who is powerful
    • adrivo: of the mountain
    • bilam: the hole or cave
    • devā: the gods
    • abibhyuṣas: have approached
    • tujyamānāsa: being invoked
    • āviṣuḥ: have come forth
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks to the divine presence and strength associated with the natural world, particularly in relation to wealth and power.
    • The phrase "You of the strong" suggests a connection to strength and resilience, which can be seen in real life through individuals or communities that overcome challenges.
    • The reference to "the cow" symbolizes wealth and nourishment, reflecting how agriculture and livestock are vital for sustenance and economic stability.
    • The mention of "the mountain" and "the cave" can be interpreted as the challenges and shelters we encounter in life, representing both obstacles and safe havens.
    • The invocation of the gods indicates a call for divine assistance and blessings, which can be related to prayer or rituals performed in times of need.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In agriculture, farmers often pray for a good harvest, invoking divine blessings to ensure their crops thrive, much like the invocation in the mantra.
    • Communities facing natural disasters may come together to seek strength and support, reflecting the mantra's emphasis on resilience and divine assistance.
    • In personal challenges, individuals may find strength in their faith or community, paralleling the mantra's call for divine support in overcoming obstacles.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of strength, resilience, and divine support in our lives.
    • It serves as a reminder that we can invoke higher powers in times of need, whether through prayer, community support, or personal reflection.
    • In a broader sense, it highlights the interconnectedness of nature, wealth, and spirituality, encouraging us to seek balance and harmony in our lives.
