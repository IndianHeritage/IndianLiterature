स्वस्तिर इद धि प्रपत्थे श्रेष्ठा रेक्णस्वत्य अभि या वामम् एति |सा नो अमासो अरणे नि पातु स्वावेशा भवतु देवगोपाः ||
svastir id dhi prapathe śreṣṭhā rekṇasvaty abhi yā vāmam eti | sā no amā so araṇe ni pātu svāveśā bhavatu devagopā ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • Svastir: Well-being, auspiciousness
    • id: This
    • dhi: Indeed
    • prapathe: To the path, to the way
    • śreṣṭhā: The best, the most excellent
    • rekṇasvaty: With good fortune, with prosperity
    • abhi: Towards, in the direction of
    • yā: Who, that
    • vāmam: Your, of you
    • eti: Comes, approaches
    • sā: She, that
    • no: Us, our
    • amā: Not, without
    • so: He, that
    • araṇe: In the shelter, in protection
    • ni: Down, in
    • pātu: Protect, guard
    • svāveśā: By the divine presence, by the divine will
    • bhavatu: May it be, let it be
    • devagopā: The divine protector, the guardian of the gods
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes well-being and auspiciousness on the path of life.
    • It emphasizes the importance of following the best and most prosperous path.
    • The mantra seeks protection and guidance from divine forces, ensuring that one is shielded from negativity and harm.
    • It highlights the significance of divine presence in our lives, suggesting that with faith and devotion, we can navigate challenges effectively.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, individuals may recite this mantra before starting a new project to invoke success and protection against obstacles.
    • During significant life events, such as weddings or new ventures, people often seek blessings for auspicious beginnings, resonating with the essence of this mantra.
    • In times of uncertainty or crisis, individuals may turn to spiritual practices, including chanting this mantra, to find solace and guidance.
  • Conclusion:
    • The mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of seeking divine guidance and protection in our daily lives.
    • It encourages individuals to pursue paths that lead to prosperity and well-being, reinforcing the belief that with faith, one can overcome challenges.
    • In essence, this mantra is applicable in various life scenarios, from personal growth to professional endeavors, emphasizing the interconnectedness of spirituality and daily life.
