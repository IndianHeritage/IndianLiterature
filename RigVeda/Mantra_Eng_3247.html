आयं श्रिण्वे अध जयन्न उता घ्नन्नayam उता प्र कृतुते युधा गाः |यदा सत्यं कृतुते मन्युम इन्द्रो विश्वं दृढम् भयत एजद अस्मात ||
ayaṁ śṛṇve adha jayann uta ghnann ayam uta pra kṛṇute yudhā gāḥ | yadā satyaṁ kṛṇute manyum indro viśvaṁ dṛḻham bhayata ejad asmāt ||
  • ayaṁ śṛṇve: This phrase translates to "this one hears." It emphasizes the importance of listening and being receptive to knowledge and wisdom. In real life, active listening is crucial in communication, whether in personal relationships or professional settings.
  • adha jayann uta ghnann: This means "overcoming obstacles and striking down." It signifies the power to conquer challenges and adversities. For example, in a business context, a company may face market competition, but through strategic planning and innovation, it can overcome these challenges.
  • ayam uta pra kṛṇute yudhā gāḥ: This translates to "this one also creates the paths of battle." It suggests the idea of preparing for challenges and conflicts. In life, this can be seen in how individuals prepare for exams or competitions, strategizing their approach to achieve success.
  • yadā satyaṁ kṛṇute: This means "when truth is established." It highlights the significance of truth and integrity in our actions. In real life, honesty in business dealings fosters trust and long-term relationships, which are essential for success.
  • manyum indro: This translates to "Indra, the lord of might." Indra symbolizes strength and power. In a real-life scenario, this can be related to leaders who inspire and empower their teams to achieve greatness through their strength and vision.
  • viśvaṁ dṛḻham bhayata ejad asmāt: This means "the whole world is firmly established, free from fear." It conveys a sense of security and stability. In today's world, creating a safe and supportive environment, whether at home or in the workplace, is vital for fostering growth and collaboration.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of listening, overcoming challenges, preparing for conflicts, establishing truth, and fostering strength and security. In real life, these principles can be applied in various scenarios, such as personal development, leadership, and community building. By embodying these values, individuals can navigate life's challenges more effectively and contribute positively to their surroundings.
