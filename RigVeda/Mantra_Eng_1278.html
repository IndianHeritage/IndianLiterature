यम अश्विनाऽ ददतुः श्वेतम अश्वम् अघाश्वाय शाश्वद इति स्वस्ति |तद वां दात्रं महि कीर्तेन्यं भूत पैद्वो वाजी सदा मिद धव्यः आर्यः ||
yam aśvinā dadathuḥ śvetam aśvam aghāśvāya śaśvad it svasti | tad vāṁ dātram mahi kīrtenyam bhūt paidvo vājī sadam id dhavyo aryaḥ ||
  • yam aśvinā dadathuḥ: This phrase translates to "May the Ashvins grant us." The Ashvins are twin deities in Vedic mythology, often associated with health, medicine, and the dawn. They symbolize the duality of life and the balance between opposites.
  • śvetam aśvam aghāśvāya: This means "a white horse for the one who is without horses." The white horse is a symbol of purity and strength. In a real-life context, this can represent the idea of receiving blessings or resources even when one feels lacking or in need.
  • śaśvad it svasti: This translates to "may there be constant well-being." It emphasizes the importance of health and prosperity in life. In modern terms, this can relate to the pursuit of wellness, both physically and mentally, in our daily lives.
  • tad vāṁ dātram mahi kīrtenyam: This means "that which is great, may we sing its praises." It suggests the importance of recognizing and celebrating the greatness in our lives, whether it be achievements, nature, or relationships. For example, acknowledging the efforts of healthcare workers during a pandemic can be seen as a modern application of this sentiment.
  • bhūt paidvo vājī sadam id dhavyo aryaḥ: This translates to "may we be endowed with strength and wealth." It reflects the desire for abundance and the ability to overcome challenges. In real life, this can be seen in the pursuit of education and career growth, where individuals seek to empower themselves and their communities.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a prayer for health, prosperity, and the recognition of greatness in life. Its significance lies in the holistic approach to well-being, urging individuals to seek balance, celebrate achievements, and strive for abundance. In today's world, this mantra can inspire us to focus on personal growth, community support, and the pursuit of a fulfilling life, reminding us that even in times of scarcity, blessings can manifest in unexpected ways.
