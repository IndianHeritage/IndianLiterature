येन ज्योतिंष्य आयवे मनवे च विवेधिता |मन्दानो अस्य बरहिषो वि राजसि ||
yena jyotīṁṣy āyave manave ca viveditha | mandāno asya barhiṣo vi rājasi ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yena: by whom
    • jyotīṁṣy: the lights or illuminations
    • āyave: for the purpose of life or existence
    • manave: for the mind or intellect
    • ca: and
    • viveditha: have been distinguished or separated
    • mandāno: the one who is adorned or decorated
    • asya: of this
    • barhiṣo: of the sacred grass or offerings
    • vi: in
    • rājasi: you reign or rule
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the divine source that illuminates the mind and life.
    • It emphasizes the importance of knowledge and wisdom in distinguishing between various aspects of existence.
    • The reference to "mandāno asya barhiṣo" suggests the sacredness of offerings and the reverence in which they are held.
    • It implies that the divine presence reigns over the offerings made, symbolizing the connection between the material and spiritual realms.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, this mantra can be related to the pursuit of knowledge and understanding. For instance, students who seek wisdom through education embody the essence of "jyotīṁṣy āyave."
    • In a corporate setting, leaders who make informed decisions based on clear insights reflect the idea of "viveditha," distinguishing between various options for the betterment of the organization.
    • During rituals or ceremonies, the offerings made to deities can be seen as a representation of "mandāno asya barhiṣo," where the sacredness of the act is acknowledged and revered.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of divine illumination in guiding our thoughts and actions.
    • It serves as a reminder to seek knowledge and wisdom in our lives, allowing us to make informed choices.
    • In spiritual practices, it reinforces the importance of reverence in offerings, acknowledging the connection between the material and spiritual worlds.
    • Overall, it encourages individuals to recognize the divine presence in their lives and to act with intention and clarity.
