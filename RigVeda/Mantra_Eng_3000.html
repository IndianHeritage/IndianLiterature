अच्छा विवक्ष्मि रोडसी सुमेके ग्राव्णो युजानो अध्वरे मनिषा |इमा उ ते मनवे भूर्वारा ऊर्ध्वा भवन्ति दर्शता यजत्राः ||
acchā vivakmi rodasī sumeke grāvṇo yujāno adhvare manīṣā | imā u te manave bhūrivārā ūrdhvā bhavanti darśatā yajatrāḥ ||
  • acchā vivakmi rodasī: This phrase can be translated as "I speak well of the heavens." Here, "acchā" means good or well, "vivakmi" means I speak, and "rodasī" refers to the heavens or the sky. This suggests a positive acknowledgment of the celestial realm.
  • sumeke grāvṇo yujāno adhvare manīṣā: This part translates to "the well-formed, connected with the sacrifice, is the thought." "Sumeke" means well-formed or auspicious, "grāvṇo" refers to being connected or joined, "yujāno" means to be united, "adhvare" relates to sacrifice, and "manīṣā" means thought or intellect. This emphasizes the importance of unity and auspiciousness in the context of rituals and sacrifices.
  • imā u te manave bhūrivārā: This translates to "these are for you, O Manava, of great wealth." "Imā" means these, "u" means for, "te" means you, "manave" refers to humanity or mankind, and "bhūrivārā" means of great wealth or abundance. This indicates that the blessings or offerings are directed towards humanity, signifying the importance of collective well-being.
  • ūrdhvā bhavanti darśatā yajatrāḥ: This can be interpreted as "those who see the offerings rise upwards." "Ūrdhvā" means upwards, "bhavanti" means become or rise, "darśatā" refers to seeing or vision, and "yajatrāḥ" relates to the offerings or sacrifices. This suggests that the offerings made with sincerity and unity rise to the heavens, symbolizing a connection between the earthly and the divine.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of unity, positive intentions, and the connection between humanity and the divine through rituals and sacrifices. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various contexts such as community gatherings, family functions, or any collective effort where the intention is to create a positive impact. By fostering unity and goodwill, individuals can contribute to a greater purpose, ensuring that their actions resonate positively in the world around them. The mantra serves as a reminder that our thoughts and actions, when aligned with a higher purpose, can lead to abundance and well-being for all.
