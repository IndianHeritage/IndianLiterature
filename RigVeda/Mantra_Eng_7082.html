आयं वाम भागो निहितो यजत्रेमā गिरो नासत्योप यातम् |पिबतं सोमं मधुमन्तं अस्मे प्र दाश्वांसम अवतं शचीभिः ||
ayaṁ vām bhāgo nihito yajatremā giro nāsatyopa yātam | pibataṁ somam madhumantam asme pra dāśvāṁsam avataṁ śacībhiḥ ||
  • ayaṁ vām bhāgo nihito: This phrase translates to "this portion of yours is established." It signifies the idea of sharing and the importance of community. In real life, this can be seen in communal gatherings where resources, knowledge, and experiences are shared among individuals, fostering unity and cooperation.
  • yajatremā giro: This means "the words of the worshippers." It emphasizes the power of prayer and intention in rituals. For example, in modern life, people often gather for collective prayers or meditations, believing that their combined intentions can manifest positive outcomes.
  • nāsatyopa yātam: This translates to "may we be led by the truthful." It highlights the value of truthfulness and integrity. In everyday scenarios, this can be reflected in ethical decision-making in business or personal relationships, where honesty is prioritized for long-term success and trust.
  • pibataṁ somam madhumantam: This phrase means "drink the sweet soma." Soma is often associated with divine nectar or bliss. In contemporary terms, this can relate to enjoying the fruits of one's labor, such as celebrating achievements with friends and family, which brings joy and fulfillment.
  • asme pra dāśvāṁsam avataṁ śacībhiḥ: This translates to "may we be protected by the powerful." It signifies the need for support and protection from higher forces or the community. In real life, this can be seen in the importance of social support systems, such as family, friends, and community organizations that provide assistance during challenging times.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates themes of community, truth, joy, and protection. Its significance lies in the reminder of the interconnectedness of individuals and the importance of collective efforts in achieving common goals. In real life, these principles can be applied in various scenarios, such as community service, ethical practices in business, and the celebration of shared successes, ultimately fostering a harmonious and supportive society.
