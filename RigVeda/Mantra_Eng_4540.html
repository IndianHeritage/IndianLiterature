आ ते अग्न ऋचा हविः हृदा तष्टं भरामसि |ते ते भवन्तूक्षण ऋषभासो वशा उता ||
ā te agna ṛcā havir hṛdā taṣṭam bharāmasi | te te bhavantūkṣaṇa ṛṣabhāso vaśā uta ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā te: To you
    • agna: O Fire
    • ṛcā: With hymns
    • havir: Offering
    • hṛdā: With the heart
    • taṣṭam: We have placed
    • bharāmasi: We are invoking
    • te: You
    • bhavantū: May you become
    • kṣaṇa: Moment
    • ṛṣabhāso: O Lord of the Light
    • vaśā: Under your control
    • uta: And
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Agni, the Vedic fire god, symbolizing the divine light and energy.
    • It emphasizes the importance of heartfelt offerings and the connection between the devotee and the divine.
    • The invocation of Agni signifies the desire for spiritual enlightenment and guidance.
    • Real-life example: Just as one lights a candle to dispel darkness, invoking Agni represents the quest for knowledge and clarity in life.
  • Significance:
    • This mantra highlights the importance of sincerity in worship and the need for divine guidance in our actions.
    • It teaches us to approach our goals with a pure heart and a focused mind, seeking the blessings of higher powers.
    • In practical terms, this can be applied in daily life by setting intentions for our actions, whether in personal relationships, professional endeavors, or spiritual practices.
  • Conclusion:
    • The mantra serves as a reminder of the significance of inner purity and the power of intention in our lives.
    • By invoking Agni, we acknowledge the need for guidance and clarity, which can lead to personal growth and enlightenment.
    • In essence, this mantra encourages us to align our actions with our higher purpose, fostering a deeper connection with the divine and the world around us.
