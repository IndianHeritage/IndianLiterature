स्वनो न वो 'मवान् रेजयद वृषा त्वेषो ययिस तविष एवयामरुत् |येना सहन्त ऋञ्जत स्वरोजिषः स्थारश्मानो हिरण्ययाः स्वायुधासा इष्मिणः ||
svano na vo 'mavān rejayad vṛṣā tveṣo yayis taviṣa evayāmarut | yenā sahanta ṛñjata svarociṣaḥ sthāraśmāno hiraṇyayāḥ svāyudhāsa iṣmiṇaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • svano: sound, voice
    • na: not
    • vo: your
    • 'mavān: may I not be deprived
    • rejayad: may it shine, may it be bright
    • vṛṣā: strong, powerful
    • tveṣo: your energy, your power
    • yayis: by which
    • taviṣa: strength, might
    • evayāmarut: O Maruts, the storm gods
    • yenā: by whom
    • sahanta: endure, withstand
    • ṛñjata: to be purified
    • svarociṣaḥ: shining, radiant
    • sthāraśmāno: with firm rays
    • hiraṇyayāḥ: golden
    • svāyudhāsa: with your own weapons
    • iṣmiṇaḥ: in this place
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the Maruts, the storm gods, asking for their powerful energy and strength.
    • The speaker expresses a desire to not be deprived of the divine sound and brightness that the Maruts bring.
    • It emphasizes the importance of endurance and purification through the divine forces represented by the Maruts.
    • The imagery of "golden rays" and "weapons" symbolizes protection and strength in facing challenges.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In times of personal struggle, individuals often seek strength and resilience, much like the invocation of the Maruts for support.
    • In a community facing natural disasters, the collective strength and unity can be seen as a modern reflection of the Maruts' power.
    • In sports, athletes often draw on their inner strength and the support of their team, akin to calling upon divine energies for victory.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of seeking divine support and strength in overcoming life's challenges.
    • It teaches us to endure hardships and strive for purification and brightness in our lives.
    • In real-life scenarios, it encourages individuals and communities to harness their inner strength and work together to face adversities.
