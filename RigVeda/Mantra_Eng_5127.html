आ जङ्घन्ति सान्व एषां जगहनाम् उप जिघ्नते | अश्वाजनि प्रचेतसोऽश्वान समत्सु चोदय ||
ā jaṅghanti sānv eṣāṁ jaghanām̐ upa jighnate | aśvājani pracetaso 'śvān samatsu codaya ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā jaṅghanti: They (the horses) move or advance.
    • sānv eṣāṁ: This is the essence of those (the horses).
    • jaghanām̐: In the context of the body or the lower part.
    • upa jighnate: They strike or attack.
    • aśvājani: O horse-born (or related to horses).
    • pracetaso: With intelligence or awareness.
    • 'śvān: The horses.
    • samatsu: In the assembly or gathering.
    • codaya: Inspire or motivate.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the movement and essence of horses, which are often symbolic of strength, speed, and vitality in Vedic literature.
    • The phrase "ā jaṅghanti" suggests a dynamic action, indicating that the horses are not static but are advancing towards a goal.
    • The mention of "pracetaso" implies that these horses are not just physical beings but are endowed with intelligence, suggesting a deeper connection between the physical and the mental realms.
    • The call to "codaya" indicates a desire for motivation and inspiration, which can be applied to various aspects of life, such as leadership, teamwork, and personal growth.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In sports, a coach might use the essence of this mantra to inspire athletes to push their limits, emphasizing teamwork and collective strength.
    • In a corporate setting, leaders can draw from this mantra to motivate their teams, encouraging them to advance towards common goals with intelligence and strategy.
    • In personal development, individuals can reflect on the mantra to inspire themselves to move forward in life, harnessing their inner strength and intelligence to overcome challenges.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra from the Rigveda highlights the importance of movement, intelligence, and motivation in achieving goals.
    • Its significance lies in its applicability to various aspects of life, from personal growth to teamwork and leadership.
    • By embodying the qualities of strength and awareness, individuals and groups can navigate challenges and inspire one another towards success.
