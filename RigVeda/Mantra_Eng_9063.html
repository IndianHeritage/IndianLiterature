मन्त्र: सप्त वीरासो अधराद उद आयन्न अष्टोत्तरात्तात सम अजग्मिरन् ते |नव पश्चातात स्थिविमन्त आयन दश प्राक सानु विटिरन्त्य अशनः ||
sapta vīrāso adharād ud āyann aṣṭottarāttāt sam ajagmiran te | nava paścātāt sthivimanta āyan daśa prāk sānu vi tiranty aśnaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • Sapta: Seven
    • Vīrāso: Heroes or warriors
    • Adharād: From below or from the earth
    • Ud āyann: Rising up or emerging
    • Aṣṭottarāttāt: From the eight directions
    • Sam ajagmiran: They have come together or assembled
    • Te: They
    • Nava: Nine
    • Paścātāt: From behind or after
    • Sthivimanta: Firm or stable
    • Āyan: Coming or arriving
    • Daśa: Ten
    • Prāk: Before
    • Sānu: With support or assistance
    • Vi tiranty: They cross over or transcend
    • Aśnaḥ: The eaters or those who consume
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of a collective emergence of strength and unity, represented by the seven heroes rising from the earth.
    • The mention of "eight directions" signifies the comprehensive nature of their strength, suggesting that they are prepared to face challenges from all sides.
    • The "nine" that follow indicate a further layer of support or strength, emphasizing the importance of community and collaboration.
    • The "ten" signifies completeness or wholeness, suggesting that with unity and strength, one can overcome obstacles.
    • The phrase "they cross over" implies overcoming difficulties, which can be interpreted as achieving goals or aspirations through collective effort.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a team setting, such as in sports or business, the mantra reflects how individuals come together, each contributing their unique strengths to achieve a common goal.
    • In community service, various groups may unite (the seven heroes) to address social issues, demonstrating the power of collective action.
    • In personal development, the mantra can inspire individuals to seek support from friends and family (the nine and ten) to overcome personal challenges.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its emphasis on unity, strength, and collective effort. It teaches that challenges can be overcome when individuals come together, supporting one another.
    • In real-life scenarios, whether in professional settings, community initiatives, or personal struggles, the principles of collaboration and mutual support are essential for success.
    • This mantra serves as a reminder that we are stronger together, and by recognizing and harnessing our collective strengths, we can transcend obstacles and achieve our goals.
