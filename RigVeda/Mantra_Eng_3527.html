मन्त्र: इहेह यद वां समना पपृक्षे सेयम अस्मे सुमतिर वाजरत्ना |उरुष्यतं जरीतारं युवं ह श्रितः कामो नासत्याऽ युवद्रिक ||
iheha yad vāṁ samanā papṛkṣe seyam asme sumatir vājaratnā | uruṣyataṁ jaritāraṁ yuvaṁ ha śritaḥ kāmo nāsatyā yuvadrik ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • iheha: here and now
    • yad: whatever
    • vāṁ: your
    • samanā: equal, harmonious
    • papṛkṣe: I ask, I inquire
    • seyam: this, that
    • asme: for us
    • sumatir: good thought, wisdom
    • vājaratnā: like a diamond, precious
    • uruṣyataṁ: may it be vast, expansive
    • jaritāraṁ: of the one who brings forth
    • yuvaṁ: young, youthful
    • ha: indeed
    • śritaḥ: supported, based upon
    • kāmo: desire, wish
    • nāsatyā: not false, truthful
    • yuvadrik: youthful vision, perspective
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking wisdom and understanding in the present moment.
    • It suggests that one should inquire about what is harmonious and beneficial for oneself and others.
    • The reference to "good thought" and "precious like a diamond" indicates the value of wisdom and clarity in decision-making.
    • The mantra encourages a youthful perspective, suggesting that one should approach life with curiosity and openness.
    • It also highlights the significance of truthfulness in desires and aspirations.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace setting, this mantra can inspire employees to seek collaborative solutions that benefit the entire team, fostering a harmonious work environment.
    • In personal relationships, it encourages open communication and understanding, allowing individuals to express their desires truthfully while considering the feelings of others.
    • In education, students can apply this mantra by asking questions that lead to deeper understanding and wisdom, rather than accepting information at face value.
    • In community service, individuals can embody this mantra by seeking to understand the needs of others and providing support that is both truthful and beneficial.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder to live in the present, seek wisdom, and approach life with a youthful and truthful perspective.
    • Its significance lies in promoting harmony, understanding, and clarity in various aspects of life, from personal relationships to professional environments.
    • By embodying the principles of this mantra, individuals can cultivate a more meaningful and fulfilling existence, contributing positively to their communities and relationships.
