विषा होत्रा विश्वम् अश्नोति वार्यम् बृहस्पतिर अरमतिḥ पानीयसी |ग्रावा यत्र मधुशूद उच्यते बृहद अवीवशन्त मतीभिर मनीषिणः ||
vi ṣā hotrā viśvam aśnoti vāryam bṛhaspatir aramatiḥ panīyasī | grāvā yatra madhuṣud ucyate bṛhad avīvaśanta matibhir manīṣiṇaḥ ||
  • Translation: The mantra can be translated as: "The one who is the hotar (the priest) of the viṣā (the community) knows everything; the great lord Bṛhaspati, who is the bestower of wealth, is pleased with the offerings. Where the sweet nectar is spoken of, there the wise ones, endowed with great intellect, gather."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • viṣā: Refers to a community or a group of people.
    • hotrā: The priest or the one who performs rituals.
    • viśvam: Everything or all-encompassing.
    • aśnoti: To know or to understand.
    • vāryam: Wealth or abundance.
    • bṛhaspatir: Refers to Bṛhaspati, the deity of wisdom and eloquence.
    • aramatiḥ: To be pleased or satisfied.
    • panīyasī: Sweet or nectar-like.
    • grāvā: Where or in which place.
    • madhuṣud: Referring to the sweet or nectar-like essence.
    • ucyate: Is spoken or is said.
    • bṛhad: Great or vast.
    • avīvaśanta: Those who are endowed with wisdom.
    • matibhir: With intellect or understanding.
    • manīṣiṇaḥ: The wise or the learned ones.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of community and collective wisdom. In modern society, we see this in collaborative efforts, such as community service projects or group decision-making in organizations.
    • The reference to Bṛhaspati highlights the value of knowledge and wisdom. In real life, this can be seen in educational institutions where the pursuit of knowledge is revered, and wise mentors guide students.
    • The idea of sweet nectar can be likened to the rewards of hard work and cooperation, such as the success achieved through teamwork in a corporate environment or the joy of shared experiences in friendships.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for unity, wisdom, and the pursuit of knowledge within a community. It teaches us that true understanding and wealth come from collective efforts and the sharing of wisdom. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as teamwork in workplaces, community building, and educational settings, where collaboration and shared knowledge lead to greater achievements and fulfillment.
