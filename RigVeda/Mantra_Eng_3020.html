मित्रो देवेषु आयुषु जनाय वृतबरहिषे |इषा इष्टव्रता अकाḥ ||
mitro deveṣv āyuṣu janāya vṛktabarhiṣe | iṣa iṣṭavratā akaḥ ||
  • Mitro: This word translates to "friend" or "friendly." In the context of the mantra, it signifies the importance of friendship and harmony among people.
  • Deveṣv: This means "among the gods." It indicates a divine aspect, suggesting that the relationships we cultivate should be in alignment with higher principles.
  • Āyuṣu: This translates to "in life" or "in longevity." It emphasizes the value of life and the desire for a long, fulfilling existence.
  • Janāya: This means "for the people" or "for humanity." It highlights the collective aspect of human existence and the importance of community.
  • Vṛktabarhiṣe: This term can be interpreted as "in the sacred fire" or "in the context of offerings." It signifies the rituals and practices that connect us to the divine and to each other.
  • Iṣa: This word means "desire" or "wish." It reflects the aspirations and intentions that guide our actions.
  • Iṣṭavratā: This translates to "with a sacred vow" or "with a determined purpose." It emphasizes the importance of commitment to our goals and values.
  • Akaḥ: This word means "not" or "without." It suggests the absence of negativity or obstacles in the pursuit of our desires.

The mantra as a whole can be interpreted as a prayer for friendship and harmony among people, invoking divine blessings for a long and fulfilling life. It emphasizes the importance of community and collective well-being, encouraging individuals to pursue their desires with a sense of purpose and commitment, free from negativity.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios:

  • Community Building: In our daily lives, fostering friendships and community ties can lead to a more harmonious society. For example, participating in local events or volunteering can strengthen bonds among individuals.
  • Workplace Harmony: In professional settings, promoting a friendly and collaborative environment can enhance productivity and job satisfaction. Team-building activities can help cultivate this spirit.
  • Personal Relationships: In personal relationships, practicing kindness and understanding can lead to deeper connections. This mantra encourages us to approach our interactions with love and respect.

In conclusion, this mantra serves as a reminder of the significance of friendship, community, and purpose in our lives. By embodying these principles, we can create a more positive and fulfilling existence for ourselves and those around us.
