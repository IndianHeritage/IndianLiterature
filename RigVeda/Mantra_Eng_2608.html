मन्त्र: भवā नो अग्ने सुमना उपेतौ सखेव सख्ये पितरेव साधुः |पुरुद्रुहो हि क्षितयो जनानाम् प्रति प्रतīचीर दहताद् अरातिः ||
bhavā no agne sumanā upetau sakheva sakhye pitareva sādhuḥ | purudruho hi kṣitayo janānām prati pratīcīr dahatād arātīḥ ||
  • bhavā no agne: This phrase translates to "May you be present, O Agni." Agni, the fire god, symbolizes energy, transformation, and the divine spark within us. In real life, this can be seen as a call to harness our inner strength and passion to achieve our goals.
  • sumanā upetau: This means "may we be endowed with good thoughts." It emphasizes the importance of positive thinking and mental clarity. For example, in a workplace, fostering a positive environment can lead to increased productivity and creativity among employees.
  • sakheva sakhye: This translates to "like a friend to a friend." It highlights the value of friendship and support in our relationships. In real life, having a supportive network can help individuals navigate challenges more effectively, much like how friends uplift each other during tough times.
  • pitareva sādhuḥ: This means "like a father to a son." It signifies guidance, protection, and nurturing. In a family context, a father figure often provides wisdom and support, which can be mirrored in mentorship relationships in professional settings.
  • purudruho hi kṣitayo janānām: This phrase translates to "for the many who dwell on the earth." It acknowledges the collective experience of humanity. In real life, this can be seen in community efforts where individuals come together to support a common cause, such as environmental conservation or social justice.
  • prati pratīcīr dahatād arātīḥ: This means "may he burn away the obstacles." It is a prayer for the removal of difficulties and challenges. In practical terms, this can relate to overcoming personal struggles, such as addiction or mental health issues, where seeking help can lead to healing and growth.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking divine guidance, fostering positive thoughts, and nurturing relationships. Its significance lies in the reminder that we are interconnected and that our actions can influence the collective well-being of society. In real life, this mantra encourages individuals to cultivate inner strength, support one another, and work towards overcoming challenges together, ultimately leading to a harmonious and prosperous community.
