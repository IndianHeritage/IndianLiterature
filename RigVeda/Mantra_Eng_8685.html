स नो हरिणाम् पत इन्दो देवप्सरसतमः |सखेव सख्ये नार्यो रुचे भव ||
sa no harīṇām pata indo devapsarastamaḥ | sakheva sakhye naryo ruce bhava ||
  • sa no: This phrase translates to "may he (the divine) be with us." It expresses a desire for divine presence and guidance in our lives.
  • harīṇām: Referring to "the divine beings" or "the gods," this word emphasizes the importance of seeking blessings from higher powers.
  • pata: This means "path" or "way." It signifies the journey we undertake in life, both physically and spiritually.
  • indo: This word can be interpreted as "Indra," the king of gods, symbolizing strength, power, and protection.
  • devapsarastamaḥ: This term refers to "the most divine and celestial beings," highlighting the aspiration to connect with the highest forms of divinity.
  • sakheva: Meaning "like a friend," this word emphasizes the importance of companionship and support in our relationships.
  • sakhye: This translates to "in friendship," reinforcing the theme of camaraderie and mutual respect.
  • naryo: Referring to "women," this word acknowledges the role of women in society and their importance in relationships.
  • ruce: This means "brightness" or "radiance," symbolizing positivity, joy, and enlightenment.
  • bhava: This translates to "become" or "may you become," indicating a wish for transformation and growth.

In summary, this mantra is a prayer for divine guidance and support in our lives, emphasizing the importance of friendship, companionship, and the role of women. It encourages us to seek the blessings of the divine while fostering positive relationships with those around us.


Significance and Real-Life Application:

  • This mantra can be recited during personal challenges, seeking strength and guidance from the divine.
  • In relationships, it serves as a reminder to value friendship and support, fostering a sense of community and togetherness.
  • It highlights the importance of women in our lives, encouraging respect and appreciation for their contributions.
  • In professional settings, this mantra can inspire teamwork and collaboration, promoting a positive work environment.

Overall, the mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the interconnectedness of life, the importance of divine guidance, and the value of nurturing relationships in our journey.
