पश्चात् पुरस्ताद् अधराद् उदक्तात् कविः काव्येन परि पाहि राजन् |सखे सखायम् अजरो जरीम्णे 'ग्ने मर्तांऽ अमर्त्यस् त्वं नः ||
paścāt purastād adharād udaktāt kaviḥ kāvyena pari pāhi rājan | sakhe sakhāyam ajaro jarimṇe 'gne martām̐ amartyas tvaṁ naḥ ||
  • paścāt purastād: This phrase translates to "behind and in front." It signifies the omnipresence of the divine or the universal consciousness that surrounds us from all directions.
  • adharād udaktāt: This means "from below and from above." It emphasizes the idea that the divine influence is not only present in the heavens but also in the earth, suggesting a holistic view of existence.
  • kaviḥ kāvyena: Here, "kaviḥ" refers to the poet or seer, and "kāvyena" means through poetry or creative expression. This indicates that the divine can be understood and expressed through art and creativity.
  • pari pāhi rājan: This translates to "protect us, O King." It is a plea for guidance and protection from a higher power, recognizing the need for support in our lives.
  • sakhe sakhāyam: "O friend, friend" suggests a deep bond and connection, highlighting the importance of relationships and companionship in our journey.
  • ajaro jarimṇe: This phrase means "immortal and aging." It reflects the duality of existence, where the soul is eternal while the physical body is subject to aging and decay.
  • agne martām̐ amartyas: "O Agni, the mortal and the immortal" refers to Agni, the fire god, symbolizing transformation and the connection between the material and spiritual realms.
  • tvaṁ naḥ: This means "you are ours," indicating a sense of belonging and connection to the divine.

Conclusion: This mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking divine protection and guidance in all aspects of life. It emphasizes the interconnectedness of existence, the importance of creativity, and the dual nature of life—immortal spirit versus mortal body. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in moments of uncertainty or challenge, reminding individuals to seek support from their community and the divine. It encourages creative expression as a means to connect with the higher self and navigate the complexities of life. Ultimately, it serves as a reminder of the omnipresence of the divine and the importance of relationships in our journey through life.
