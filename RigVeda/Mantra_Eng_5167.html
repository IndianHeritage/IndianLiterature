तन् नस् तुरीपम् अध पोषयित्नु देव तवष्टर वि रराणः स्यस्व |यतो वीरः कर्मण्यः सुदक्षो युक्तग्रावा जायते देवकामः ||
tan nas turīpam adha poṣayitnu deva tvaṣṭar vi rarāṇaḥ syasva | yato vīraḥ karmaṇyaḥ sudakṣo yuktagrāvā jāyate devakāmaḥ ||
  • tan nas turīpam: This phrase can be translated as "may that which is excellent for us." It signifies a request for the best or the most beneficial aspects of life.
  • adha poṣayitnu: This means "nurture us." It is a plea for divine support and sustenance, emphasizing the importance of care and nourishment in our lives.
  • deva tvaṣṭar: Referring to the divine craftsman or creator, Tvaṣṭar represents the aspect of divinity that shapes and molds our existence.
  • vi rarāṇaḥ syasva: This can be interpreted as "may you be present in our actions." It suggests a desire for divine guidance and presence in our endeavors.
  • yato vīraḥ: This translates to "where the hero is." It points to the idea of valor and strength, indicating that true courage is essential in life.
  • karmaṇyaḥ sudakṣo: Meaning "skilled in actions," this phrase highlights the importance of being adept and proficient in our tasks and responsibilities.
  • yuktagrāvā: This can be understood as "those who are united." It emphasizes the significance of collaboration and unity in achieving goals.
  • jāyate devakāmaḥ: This means "born of divine desire." It suggests that our aspirations and achievements should align with higher, divine intentions.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a profound request for divine guidance, nourishment, and support in our endeavors. It emphasizes the importance of excellence, skill, unity, and alignment with divine will in our actions. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios such as teamwork in professional settings, nurturing relationships, and pursuing personal goals with a sense of purpose. By invoking these principles, individuals can strive for a life that is not only successful but also meaningful and aligned with higher values.
