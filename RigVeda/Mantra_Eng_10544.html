अन्तश्चरति रोचनास्य प्राणाद अपानती |व्याख्यन महिषो दिवम् ||
antaś carati rocanāsya prāṇād apānatī | vy akhyan mahiṣo divam ||
  • antaś - This word translates to "within" or "inside." It signifies the inner self or the core of one's being.
  • carati - Meaning "moves" or "travels," this word indicates the dynamic nature of existence and consciousness.
  • rocanāsya - This can be interpreted as "of the radiant one" or "of the luminous." It refers to the divine light or the essence that illuminates our lives.
  • prāṇād - Translated as "from the life force," this word emphasizes the importance of vitality and energy that sustains life.
  • apānatī - This means "withdraws" or "departs," suggesting a transition or a return to the source.
  • vy akhyan - This phrase can be understood as "has manifested" or "has revealed," indicating the unfolding of existence.
  • mahiṣo - Meaning "the great one" or "the powerful," this term often refers to a divine or supreme entity.
  • divam - Translated as "heaven" or "the celestial realm," it signifies the ultimate state of bliss or enlightenment.

The mantra can be interpreted as a reflection on the journey of the inner self, moving through the radiant essence of life, and ultimately returning to the divine source. It emphasizes the importance of understanding our inner nature and the life force that drives us.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios:

  • In meditation practices, individuals often seek to connect with their inner selves, moving beyond the distractions of the external world to find peace and clarity.
  • In personal development, understanding one's life force and inner radiance can lead to greater self-awareness and fulfillment.
  • In spiritual contexts, the journey towards enlightenment is often described as moving from the material to the divine, echoing the themes of this mantra.

Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its profound exploration of the inner journey of the self. It encourages individuals to recognize the life force within, to embrace their inner light, and to seek a connection with the divine. This understanding is applicable in various aspects of life, including personal growth, meditation, and spiritual exploration, guiding individuals towards a more meaningful existence.
