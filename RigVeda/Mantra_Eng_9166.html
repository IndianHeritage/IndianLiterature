द्युश्च नः पृथिवी च प्रचेतसा ऋतावरī रक्षताम अंहसो ऋषः |मा दुर्विदात्रा निरृतिर न ईशत तद देवानाम् अवो अद्य वृणीमहे ||
dyauś ca naḥ pṛthivī ca pracetasa ṛtāvarī rakṣatām aṁhaso riṣaḥ | mā durvidatrā nirṛtir na īśata tad devānām avo adyā vṛṇīmahe ||
  • dyauś ca naḥ - "May the heavens be with us"
  • pṛthivī ca - "and the earth as well"
  • pracetasa - "with wisdom and intelligence"
  • ṛtāvarī - "may the truth protect us"
  • rakṣatām - "may it safeguard us"
  • aṁhaso - "from all that is harmful"
  • riṣaḥ - "and from all adversities"
  • mā durvidatrā - "may we not be led into difficulties"
  • nirṛtir - "may misfortune stay away"
  • na īśata - "and not have power over us"
  • tad devānām - "that of the divine beings"
  • avo adyā - "we invoke today"
  • vṛṇīmahe - "we choose and seek"

This mantra from the Rigveda is a prayer for protection and guidance. It emphasizes the importance of the heavens and the earth, invoking their presence in our lives. The plea for wisdom and intelligence highlights the need for discernment in our actions and decisions. The mantra seeks the safeguarding of truth, which is essential for a harmonious life, free from harm and adversity.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios:

  • Personal Challenges: When facing difficulties, one can recite this mantra to seek protection and guidance, reminding oneself to stay grounded and wise in decision-making.
  • Community Well-being: In community gatherings or during festivals, this mantra can be chanted to invoke collective strength and protection against misfortunes.
  • Spiritual Practices: During meditation or prayer, this mantra serves as a reminder to focus on truth and wisdom, fostering a deeper connection with the divine.

Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its universal appeal for protection, wisdom, and truth. It serves as a reminder that in our journey through life, we should seek the support of the divine and remain steadfast in our pursuit of righteousness. By invoking these qualities, we can navigate challenges with grace and resilience, ensuring a harmonious existence both individually and collectively.
