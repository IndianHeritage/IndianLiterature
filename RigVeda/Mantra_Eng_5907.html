देवी देवस्य रोदसी जनित्री बृहस्पतिं वावृद्धतुर महित्वा |दक्षाय्याय दक्षता सखायः करद ब्रह्मणे सुतरा सुगाधा ||
devī devasya rodasī janitrī bṛhaspatiṁ vāvṛdhatur mahitvā | dakṣāyyāya dakṣatā sakhāyaḥ karad brahmaṇe sutarā sugādhā ||
  • devī devasya rodasī: This phrase translates to "the goddess of the divine, the celestial." It signifies the presence of a divine feminine energy that nurtures and supports the cosmos. In real life, this can be seen in the reverence for nature and the feminine aspects of creation, such as the Earth Mother in various cultures.
  • janitrī bṛhaspatiṁ: Here, "janitrī" means "mother" or "creator," and "bṛhaspati" refers to the deity associated with wisdom and knowledge. This line emphasizes the nurturing aspect of wisdom, suggesting that true knowledge is born from a caring and supportive environment. For example, in education, a teacher who nurtures their students fosters a love for learning.
  • vāvṛdhatur mahitvā: This means "may she increase in greatness." It reflects the aspiration for growth and expansion in wisdom and virtue. In practical terms, this can be related to personal development, where individuals strive to enhance their skills and character, much like a business that aims for growth and success through innovation.
  • dakṣāyyāya dakṣatā: "Dakṣāyyāya" refers to skillfulness, while "dakṣatā" means competence or capability. This line highlights the importance of being skilled and capable in one's endeavors. In everyday life, this can be seen in professions where expertise is crucial, such as in medicine or engineering, where skilled practitioners are essential for success.
  • sakhāyaḥ karad brahmaṇe: This translates to "friends of the divine, acting with intention." It suggests that collaboration and friendship are vital in achieving divine or higher goals. In real life, this can be observed in teamwork settings, where individuals come together to achieve a common purpose, such as in community service projects or corporate teamwork.
  • sutarā sugādhā: This means "with great fragrance" or "with excellent qualities." It implies that the actions taken should be virtuous and beneficial. In practical terms, this can relate to ethical practices in business, where companies strive to operate with integrity and social responsibility, creating a positive impact on society.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of divine feminine energy, wisdom, skillfulness, collaboration, and virtue. Its significance lies in the reminder that growth and success are not just individual pursuits but are deeply rooted in nurturing relationships, ethical practices, and a commitment to personal and communal development. In real life, this mantra can inspire individuals and organizations to foster environments that promote learning, collaboration, and ethical behavior, ultimately leading to a more harmonious and prosperous society.
