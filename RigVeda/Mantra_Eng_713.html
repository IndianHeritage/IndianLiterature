गृणानो अङ्गिरोभिर दसम वि वर उषसा सूर्येण गोभिर अन्धः |वि भूम्या अप्रथय इन्द्र सानु दिवो रज उपरम अस्तभायः ||
gṛṇāno aṅgirobhir dasma vi var uṣasā sūryeṇa gobhir andhaḥ | vi bhūmyā aprathaya indra sānu divo raja uparam astabhāyaḥ ||
  • gṛṇāno aṅgirobhir: This phrase can be translated as "singing with the Angiras". The Angiras are a group of sages in Vedic literature known for their wisdom and knowledge. In real life, this can be seen as the importance of community and collaboration in achieving knowledge and wisdom.
  • dasma vi var uṣasā: This translates to "from here, may we attain the dawn". The dawn symbolizes new beginnings and opportunities. In everyday life, this can be related to the start of a new day, where one can set new goals and aspirations.
  • sūryeṇa gobhir andhaḥ: This means "with the sun, may we dispel darkness". The sun represents light, knowledge, and clarity, while darkness symbolizes ignorance and confusion. In practical terms, this can be applied to education and enlightenment, where knowledge helps to overcome challenges and uncertainties.
  • vi bhūmyā aprathaya: This translates to "may we not be separated from the earth". This emphasizes the connection to nature and the importance of grounding oneself. In real life, this can be seen in the growing movement towards sustainability and environmental awareness.
  • indra sānu divo: This means "may Indra, the king of the gods, be with us". Indra is often associated with strength and power. In a modern context, this can be interpreted as seeking strength and support from higher powers or the universe in times of need.
  • raja uparam astabhāyaḥ: This translates to "may we attain the highest state of existence". This signifies the pursuit of excellence and higher consciousness. In real life, this can be related to personal development and striving for one's best self.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of community, new beginnings, knowledge, connection to nature, divine support, and the pursuit of excellence. In our daily lives, we can apply these principles by collaborating with others, embracing new opportunities, seeking knowledge, being mindful of our environment, finding strength in our beliefs, and striving for personal growth. This mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of all aspects of life and the importance of a holistic approach to living. It encourages us to seek light over darkness and to remain grounded while aspiring for higher ideals.
