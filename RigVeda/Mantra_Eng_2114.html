सनेमा ये त ऊतिभिः तरन्तो विश्वाः स्पृधा आर्येण दस्यून् |अस्मभ्यं तत त्वाष्ट्रं विश्वरूपम् अरन्धयः साख्यस्य तृताय ||
sanema ye ta ūtibhis taranto viśvāḥ spṛdha āryeṇa dasyūn | asmabhyaṁ tat tvāṣṭraṁ viśvarūpam arandhayaḥ sākhyasya tritāya ||
  • Sanema ye ta ūtibhis: This phrase can be interpreted as "We seek those who are endowed with strength." It emphasizes the importance of seeking out individuals or forces that possess power and capability.
  • Taranto viśvāḥ spṛdha āryeṇa dasyūn: This part translates to "May all beings, with the help of the noble, overcome the enemies." Here, the term "dasyūn" refers to adversaries or obstacles, while "āryeṇa" signifies the noble or virtuous. This suggests that with the support of the righteous, one can conquer challenges.
  • Asmabhyaṁ tat tvāṣṭraṁ viśvarūpam: This can be understood as "That universal form, we invoke for ourselves." It indicates a desire to connect with a higher, universal essence or power that can aid in our endeavors.
  • Arandhayaḥ sākhyasya tritāya: This phrase means "May we be united in the third bond." The term "sākhyasya" refers to friendship or alliance, suggesting the importance of unity and collaboration in achieving goals.

  • Significance: This mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking strength, overcoming obstacles with the help of noble beings, and fostering unity among individuals. It highlights the interconnectedness of individuals in their pursuit of common goals.
  • Real-life Application: In a practical context, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios such as teamwork in a corporate environment, community building, or even in personal relationships. For instance, in a workplace, employees can draw strength from each other to overcome challenges, fostering a culture of support and collaboration.
  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a reminder of the power of unity, the importance of seeking strength from noble sources, and the collective effort required to overcome adversities. It encourages individuals to work together harmoniously, leveraging each other's strengths to achieve common objectives.
