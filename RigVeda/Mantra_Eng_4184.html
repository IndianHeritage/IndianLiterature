मन्त्र: उता घा नेमो अस्तुतः pumām̐ इति ब्रुवे पाणिः |स वैरदेय इति समः ||
uta ghā nemo astutaḥ pumām̐ iti bruve paṇiḥ | sa vairadeya it samaḥ ||
  • Translation: The mantra can be translated as: "May we be blessed with strength and valor; let us speak of the noble and the brave." This invocation emphasizes the importance of courage and strength in our lives.
  • Word Breakdown:
    • uta: A term often used to express a wish or desire, indicating a plea for blessings.
    • ghā: Refers to strength or valor, highlighting the qualities one seeks.
    • nemo: This word can imply a sense of protection or safeguarding.
    • astutaḥ: Meaning 'noble' or 'brave', it signifies the qualities of a person one aspires to embody.
    • pumām̐: Refers to a man or a person, emphasizing the human aspect of the qualities being invoked.
    • iti: A term used to denote 'thus' or 'so', indicating the conclusion of a thought.
    • bruve: Meaning 'to speak' or 'to express', it highlights the importance of communication.
    • paṇiḥ: Refers to a person or a being, often used in the context of a noble individual.
    • sa: Meaning 'that' or 'he', referring to the qualities or the person being discussed.
    • vairadeya: This term can be interpreted as 'strength' or 'power', reinforcing the theme of valor.
    • it: A particle that can mean 'there' or 'that', linking the qualities to the person.
    • samaḥ: Meaning 'equal' or 'balanced', suggesting the importance of harmony in possessing these qualities.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, a leader who embodies strength and bravery can inspire their team to overcome challenges, much like a captain steering a ship through a storm.
    • In personal relationships, being noble and brave can mean standing up for loved ones in difficult situations, akin to a friend defending another in a conflict.
    • In community service, individuals who exhibit these qualities often lead initiatives that uplift others, similar to activists fighting for social justice.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for strength, bravery, and nobility. In real-life scenarios, these qualities are essential for personal growth, leadership, and community building. By embodying the essence of this mantra, individuals can contribute positively to their surroundings, fostering an environment of courage and support. It serves as a reminder that true strength is not just physical but also moral and ethical, guiding us to act with integrity and valor in our daily lives.
