समिद्धो अग्न आ वह देवां अद्य यतस्रुच् |तन्तुं तनुष्व पूर्व्यं सुतसोमाय दाशुषे ||
samiddho agna ā vaha devām̐ adya yatasruce | tantuṁ tanuṣva pūrvyaṁ sutasomāya dāśuṣe ||
  • Mantra: "samiddho agna ā vaha devām̐ adya yatasruce | tantuṁ tanuṣva pūrvyaṁ sutasomāya dāśuṣe ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • samiddho: kindled, ignited
    • agna: O Fire
    • ā vaha: bring forth, carry
    • devām̐: the divine beings, gods
    • adya: today, now
    • yatasruce: from whom the light arises
    • tantuṁ: the thread, the connection
    • tanuṣva: may you expand, may you grow
    • pūrvyaṁ: the ancient, the previous
    • sutasomāya: to the one who is the source of joy
    • dāśuṣe: for the one who serves
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra invokes the sacred fire (Agni), which is a significant element in Vedic rituals, symbolizing purity and the divine connection between the earthly and the celestial.
    • The phrase "samiddho agna" suggests that the fire is already kindled, indicating readiness and the presence of divine energy.
    • "ā vaha devām̐" calls upon the divine beings to come forth, highlighting the importance of inviting positive energies and deities into our lives.
    • "adya yatasruce" emphasizes the immediacy of the request, asking for divine light and guidance to manifest in the present moment.
    • The term "tantuṁ tanuṣva" signifies the desire for growth and expansion, akin to how we seek personal development and deeper connections in our lives.
    • "pūrvyaṁ sutasomāya" refers to the ancient wisdom and joy that can be derived from the past, reminding us to learn from our history and experiences.
    • "dāśuṣe" indicates a service-oriented approach, suggesting that we should be of service to others and the divine, fostering a sense of community and support.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a personal context, this mantra can be applied during moments of decision-making or when seeking clarity. For instance, lighting a candle or a small fire while reciting this mantra can help focus intentions and invite positive energies.
    • In community gatherings or rituals, invoking Agni can symbolize unity and collective growth, encouraging participants to share their experiences and wisdom.
    • In professional settings, the mantra can inspire teamwork and collaboration, reminding individuals to serve each other and work towards common goals.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the interconnectedness of life, the importance of inviting divine energies, and the need for personal and communal growth.
    • Its significance lies in its ability to inspire individuals to seek clarity, foster connections, and serve others, making it applicable in various aspects of life, from personal development to community engagement.
    • By embodying the principles of this mantra, individuals can cultivate a more meaningful and purposeful existence, aligning themselves with the divine and the greater good.
