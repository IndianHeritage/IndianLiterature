इन्द्रे अग्ना नमो बृहत् सुवृक्तिम एरयामहे |धियā धेनā अवस्यवः ||
indre agnā namo bṛhat suvṛktim erayāmahe | dhiyā dhenā avasyavaḥ ||
  • Indre: This refers to Indra, the king of the gods in Vedic mythology, often associated with rain, thunder, and strength.
  • Agnā: This word means "to ignite" or "to inspire." It signifies the act of invoking or calling upon divine energy.
  • Namo: A term of reverence, meaning "I bow" or "I offer my salutations." It indicates respect and humility towards the divine.
  • Bṛhat: This translates to "great" or "vast," suggesting the expansive nature of the divine or the universe.
  • Suvṛktim: Meaning "well-spoken" or "good speech," it emphasizes the importance of positive communication and wisdom.
  • Erayāmahe: This means "we strive" or "we seek," indicating a collective effort towards a goal or aspiration.
  • Dhiyā: This word refers to "intellect" or "understanding," highlighting the role of wisdom in our endeavors.
  • Dhenā: Meaning "nourishment" or "wealth," it symbolizes the abundance and resources that support our lives.
  • Avasyavaḥ: This translates to "we must attain" or "we are destined to achieve," indicating a sense of purpose and inevitability in our pursuits.

The mantra can be interpreted as a prayer or invocation to Indra, seeking his blessings for wisdom, strength, and abundance. It emphasizes the importance of good communication and collective effort in achieving our goals. The invocation of divine energy is a reminder of the interconnectedness of all beings and the need for guidance in our endeavors.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as:

  • Teamwork: In a workplace setting, invoking the spirit of collaboration and good communication can lead to successful project outcomes.
  • Personal Growth: Individuals can use this mantra as a reminder to seek wisdom and understanding in their personal journeys, striving for self-improvement.
  • Community Building: In community initiatives, the collective effort towards a common goal can be inspired by the principles outlined in this mantra.

In conclusion, this mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the importance of invoking divine guidance, fostering good communication, and striving for collective goals. It encourages individuals and communities to seek wisdom and abundance in their pursuits, ultimately leading to a more harmonious and prosperous existence.
