युवं भुज्युम अवविद्धं समुद्र उद ūहथुर अर्णसो अस्रिधानैः |पत्रत्रिभिर अश्रमैर अव्यथिभिर दंशनाभिर अश्विनाऽ पारयन्ता ||
yuvam bhujyum avaviddhaṁ samudra ud ūhathur arṇaso asridhānaiḥ | patatribhir aśramair avyathibhir daṁsanābhir aśvinā pārayantā ||
  • yuvam - This word translates to "you two," referring to the twin deities, the Ashvins, who are often associated with health, medicine, and the dawn.
  • bhujyum - This means "to be nourished" or "to be supported," indicating the importance of sustenance and care.
  • avaviddhaṁ - This term can be interpreted as "unharmed" or "unscathed," suggesting protection and safety.
  • samudra - Translated as "ocean," it symbolizes vastness, depth, and the source of life.
  • ud ūhathur - This phrase means "they have lifted up," indicating an action of elevation or support.
  • arṇaso - This refers to "the waves" or "the waters," which can represent the challenges and changes in life.
  • asridhānaiḥ - This word means "with the help of," suggesting collaboration and assistance.
  • patatribhir - This translates to "with wings," symbolizing freedom, elevation, and the ability to rise above challenges.
  • aśramair - This means "with efforts" or "with diligence," emphasizing the importance of hard work.
  • avyathibhir - This term means "without distress," indicating a state of peace and tranquility.
  • daṁsanābhir - This translates to "with bites" or "with sharpness," which can symbolize challenges or obstacles that need to be overcome.
  • aśvinā - Referring again to the Ashvins, the divine twins who are known for their healing powers and assistance.
  • pārayantā - This means "they who carry" or "they who support," indicating the role of the Ashvins in providing help and guidance.

Conclusion: This mantra emphasizes the themes of nourishment, protection, and support in the face of life's challenges. The Ashvins, as divine figures, symbolize the importance of health, healing, and assistance. In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as seeking help during difficult times, valuing the support of loved ones, and recognizing the importance of health and well-being. It serves as a reminder that, like the Ashvins, we can find strength and support in our relationships and communities, helping us navigate the vast ocean of life with resilience and grace.
