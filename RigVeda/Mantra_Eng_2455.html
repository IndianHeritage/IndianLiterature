एता ते अग्नि जनिमाऽसनानि प्र पूर्व्याय नूतनानि वाचम् |महान्ति वृष्णे सवनाः कृतेमाऽजनन्म-जनमन निहितो जातवेदाः ||
etā te agne janimā sanāni pra pūrvyāya nūtanāni vocam | mahānti vṛṣṇe savanā kṛtemā janmañ-janman nihito jātavedāḥ ||
  • etā te agne: This phrase translates to "these (offerings) are for you, O Agni." Agni, the fire god, represents the divine energy that connects the physical and spiritual realms.
  • janimā sanāni: This means "the births (or creations) that are ancient." It signifies the timeless nature of creation and existence, suggesting that the essence of life has been present since the beginning of time.
  • pra pūrvyāya nūtanāni vocam: This translates to "I speak of the new (things) that are born from the ancient." It emphasizes the continuity of life and the idea that new experiences and creations arise from the wisdom of the past.
  • mahānti vṛṣṇe: This means "to the great (or powerful) one." It refers to the supreme being or the ultimate reality, highlighting the importance of reverence and respect towards the divine.
  • savanā kṛtemā: This translates to "the offerings made with intention." It signifies the importance of sincerity and purpose in our actions, especially in rituals and offerings.
  • janmañ-janman nihito jātavedāḥ: This means "the one who is born in every birth, O Jātavedas." Jātavedas refers to Agni as the one who is present in every cycle of life, symbolizing the eternal nature of existence and the interconnectedness of all beings.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda encapsulates the essence of creation, the significance of honoring the divine, and the importance of intention in our actions. In real life, it serves as a reminder to respect our roots and the wisdom of the past while embracing new experiences. It encourages us to approach life with reverence, recognizing the divine presence in our daily actions and the interconnectedness of all beings. This mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as during rituals, personal reflections, or even in moments of decision-making, where one seeks to align their actions with a higher purpose and understanding of existence.
