येनावा तुर्वशं यदुं येन कण्वं धनस्पृतम् |राय सु तस्य धीमहि ||
yenāva turvaśaṁ yaduṁ yena kaṇvaṁ dhanaspṛtam | rāye su tasya dhīmahi ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yenāva: By whom
    • turvaśaṁ: The strong or powerful
    • yaduṁ: The wealth or prosperity
    • yena: By whom
    • kaṇvaṁ: The wise or learned
    • dhanaspṛtam: The one who is rich or prosperous
    • rāye: For wealth or prosperity
    • su: Good or auspicious
    • tasya: Of that
    • dhīmahi: We meditate or contemplate
  • Overall Meaning: This mantra expresses a prayer for wisdom and prosperity. It acknowledges the source of strength and wealth, invoking the divine for guidance and understanding in achieving success and abundance in life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • Business Success: Entrepreneurs often seek wisdom and guidance when starting a new venture. This mantra can be a reminder to meditate on the principles of integrity and hard work, which can lead to prosperity.
    • Personal Growth: Individuals may use this mantra during meditation to focus on their goals, seeking clarity and strength to overcome challenges in their personal or professional lives.
    • Community Development: In community projects, leaders can invoke this mantra to inspire collective efforts towards achieving prosperity and well-being for all members, emphasizing the importance of wisdom in decision-making.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for wisdom and prosperity. It serves as a reminder that true wealth is not just material but also encompasses knowledge and strength. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as business, personal development, and community initiatives, encouraging individuals and groups to seek guidance and meditate on their paths to success and abundance.
