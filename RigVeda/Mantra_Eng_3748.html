ऋतं चिकीत्व ऋतमिच चिकीद्ध्य ऋतस्य धाराऽनु तृन्धि पूर्वीः |नाहं यातुं सहसा न द्वयेना ऋतं सपाम्य अरुषस्य वृष्णः ||
ṛtaṁ cikitva ṛtam ic cikiddhy ṛtasya dhārā anu tṛndhi pūrvīḥ | nāhaṁ yātuṁ sahasā na dvayena ṛtaṁ sapāmy aruṣasya vṛṣṇaḥ ||
  • Meaning of the Mantra:
    • ṛtaṁ cikitva: This phrase refers to the understanding or realization of "ṛta," which signifies cosmic order, truth, and righteousness.
    • ṛtam ic: This implies the desire or intention to uphold and follow the cosmic order.
    • cikiddhy: This suggests the act of knowing or comprehending.
    • ṛtasya dhārā: This refers to the flow or stream of righteousness and truth.
    • anu tṛndhi pūrvīḥ: This can be interpreted as following the path laid down by the ancients or predecessors.
    • nāhaṁ yātuṁ sahasā: This indicates a reluctance or inability to act hastily or without thought.
    • na dvayena: This means not being divided or conflicted.
    • ṛtaṁ sapāmy: This suggests the affirmation or establishment of truth and order.
    • aruṣasya vṛṣṇaḥ: This refers to the divine or celestial aspect, often associated with the sun or light.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, understanding and adhering to ethical practices can be seen as following "ṛta." For instance, a company that prioritizes transparency and honesty in its dealings embodies the principles of cosmic order.
    • In personal relationships, the mantra encourages individuals to communicate openly and truthfully, avoiding misunderstandings and conflicts, thus maintaining harmony.
    • In environmental conservation, recognizing the interconnectedness of all life and acting responsibly to protect nature reflects the essence of "ṛta." For example, sustainable practices in agriculture can be seen as following the ancient wisdom of living in harmony with the earth.
  • Conclusion and Significance:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of understanding and adhering to the principles of truth and righteousness in all aspects of life.
    • It serves as a reminder to act thoughtfully and with intention, avoiding hasty decisions that may lead to conflict or disorder.
    • In a world often filled with chaos and uncertainty, this mantra encourages individuals and communities to seek harmony and balance by following the path of truth laid down by those who came before.
    • Ultimately, the mantra is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal conduct to societal norms, promoting a life lived in alignment with universal principles of order and righteousness.
