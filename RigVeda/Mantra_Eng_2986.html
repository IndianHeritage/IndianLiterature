महī सम ऐरच चंवā समīचī उभे ते अस्य वसनā न्यृषṭे |श्रिण्वे वीरो विन्दमानो वसūनि महद देवानाम असुरत्वम् एकम् ||
mahī sam airac camvā samīcī ubhe te asya vasunā nyṛṣṭe | śṛṇve vīro vindamāno vasūni mahad devānām asuratvam ekam ||
  • Translation: The mantra can be translated as: "May the earth and the sky, both of them, be filled with your wealth. The hero who hears this, finds the great wealth of the gods, the unity of the divine."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • mahī: Earth or the physical realm.
    • sam: Together or in harmony.
    • airac: Sky or the celestial realm.
    • camvā: Wealth or abundance.
    • samīcī: Both or duality.
    • ubhe: Both of them.
    • te: Your or belonging to you.
    • asya: This or of this.
    • vasunā: Wealth or riches.
    • nyṛṣṭe: In the act of giving or bestowing.
    • śṛṇve: Hear or listen.
    • vīro: Hero or brave one.
    • vindamāno: Finding or discovering.
    • vasūni: Wealths or riches.
    • mahad: Great or significant.
    • devānām: Of the gods.
    • asuratvam: Unity or oneness.
    • ekam: One or singular.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of harmony between the earth and the sky, which can be seen in sustainable living practices. For instance, communities that engage in organic farming respect both the land (earth) and the atmosphere (sky) to produce healthy food.
    • The concept of a hero who listens and finds wealth can be related to entrepreneurs who pay attention to market needs and innovate accordingly. For example, a tech entrepreneur who listens to user feedback can create a product that meets the needs of the community, thus finding 'wealth' in the form of success and profit.
    • The unity of the divine can be reflected in interfaith dialogues where different religious communities come together to find common ground, promoting peace and understanding.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for harmony between the physical and spiritual realms, encouraging individuals to seek wealth not just in material terms but also in spiritual unity and understanding. In real life, this can be applied in various contexts such as environmental sustainability, entrepreneurship, and community building. By fostering a sense of unity and listening to the needs of others, we can create a more prosperous and harmonious society.
