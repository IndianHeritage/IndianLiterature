ईयुरर्थं न न्यर्थं परुष्णीमाशुश्चनेदभिपित्वं जगाम | सुदास इन्द्रः सुतुकाम् अमित्रान् अरन्धयन मानवसे वध्रिवाचः ||
īyur arthaṁ na nyartham paruṣṇīm āśuś caned abhipitvaṁ jagāma | sudāsa indraḥ sutukām̐ amitrān arandhayan mānuṣe vadhrivācaḥ ||
  • īyur arthaṁ na nyartham: This phrase can be translated to "May we attain the desired goals and not the undesired." It emphasizes the importance of setting clear intentions in life. For example, in a career context, one might aim for a promotion (desired) rather than facing job loss (undesired).
  • paruṣṇīm āśuś caned abhipitvaṁ jagāma: This part suggests that one should swiftly overcome obstacles and challenges. In real life, this can be seen in athletes who face injuries but work hard to recover and return to their sport, demonstrating resilience and determination.
  • sudāsa indraḥ sutukām̐: Here, "Indra" refers to the king of gods, symbolizing strength and power. "Sudāsa" can be interpreted as a person who is well-versed in righteousness. This can relate to leaders in society who strive to make ethical decisions for the greater good, such as community leaders advocating for social justice.
  • amitrān arandhayan: This phrase means "to overcome enemies." In a modern context, this could refer to individuals or organizations that face competition and strive to innovate and improve their offerings, thereby overcoming their rivals.
  • mānuṣe vadhrivācaḥ: This translates to "the words of the wise among humans." It highlights the importance of wisdom and guidance in human interactions. For instance, mentors in professional settings provide valuable advice that can shape the careers of their mentees.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of striving for positive outcomes while overcoming challenges and adversities. It emphasizes the significance of wisdom, ethical leadership, and resilience in achieving one's goals. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various fields such as personal development, leadership, and community building, encouraging individuals to focus on their aspirations while navigating the complexities of life.
