अहं सप्तहां नहुषो नहुष्टरः प्राश्रावयं शवसां तुर्वशं यदुम् |अहं न्य अन्यं सहसा सहस करं नव व्राधतो नवतिं च वक्षयम् ||
ahaṁ saptahā nahuṣo nahuṣṭaraḥ prāśrāvayaṁ śavasā turvaśaṁ yadum | ahaṁ ny a1nyaṁ sahasā sahas karaṁ nava vrādhato navatiṁ ca vakṣayam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ahaṁ: I
    • saptahā: seven
    • nahuṣo: of Nahusha (a legendary king)
    • nahuṣṭaraḥ: the best among Nahusha
    • prāśrāvayaṁ: I proclaim
    • śavasā: with strength
    • turvaśaṁ: of the Turvaśa (a tribe)
    • yadum: the one who is strong
    • ny a1nyaṁ: not another
    • sahasā: with force
    • sahas karaṁ: with great effort
    • nava: new
    • vrādhato: of the one who is growing
    • navatiṁ: ninety
    • ca: and
    • vakṣayam: I will destroy
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the speaker's identity and strength, drawing a connection to the legendary king Nahusha, who is often associated with power and authority.
    • The mention of "seven" could symbolize completeness or perfection, as the number seven is often seen as sacred in various cultures.
    • The phrase "I proclaim with strength" indicates a declaration of intent or purpose, emphasizing the importance of confidence and assertiveness in one's actions.
    • The reference to "Turvaśa" suggests a connection to a community or tribe, highlighting the significance of collective strength and unity.
    • The latter part of the mantra emphasizes the idea of overcoming challenges and obstacles ("I will destroy") through effort and determination.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, a leader may embody the spirit of this mantra by confidently proclaiming a new vision for the company, rallying their team to work together towards a common goal.
    • In personal development, an individual may draw inspiration from this mantra to overcome personal challenges, using their inner strength and determination to achieve their goals.
    • In community initiatives, groups can come together, much like the Turvaśa tribe, to support one another in achieving collective objectives, showcasing the power of unity.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the importance of self-identity, strength, and community in overcoming challenges.
    • Its significance lies in the encouragement to proclaim one's purpose with confidence and to harness collective strength for greater achievements.
    • In real-life scenarios, it can inspire individuals and groups to take decisive action, fostering resilience and unity in the face of adversity.
