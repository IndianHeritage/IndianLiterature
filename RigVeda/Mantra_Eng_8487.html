स ईं रथो न भुरिषाल् अयोिज महः पुरूणि सातये वसूनि |आद ईं विश्वा नहुष्याणि जाताऽस्वर्षाता वन ऊर्ध्वा नवन्त ||
sa īṁ ratho na bhuriṣāḻ ayoji mahaḥ purūṇi sātaye vasūni | ād īṁ viśvā nahuṣyāṇi jātā svarṣātā vana ūrdhvā navanta ||
  • sa īṁ ratho na bhuriṣāḻ ayoji mahaḥ purūṇi sātaye vasūni
    • sa: This word translates to "that" or "he," indicating a connection to a higher power or divine entity.
    • īṁ: This is a sound that represents energy or life force, often associated with the divine.
    • ratho: Meaning "chariot," it symbolizes the journey of life and the movement towards goals.
    • na: This means "not," indicating negation or absence.
    • bhuriṣāḻ: This term refers to "great strength" or "power," suggesting the importance of resilience.
    • ayojī: This means "united" or "connected," emphasizing the importance of harmony.
    • mahaḥ: Translates to "great" or "mighty," indicating the significance of greatness in our pursuits.
    • purūṇi: This means "ancient" or "eternal," suggesting the timeless nature of wisdom.
    • sātaye: This translates to "for the purpose of truth," highlighting the importance of truthfulness in our actions.
    • vasūni: Refers to "wealth" or "prosperity," indicating the blessings that come from living a righteous life.
  • ād īṁ viśvā nahuṣyāṇi jātā svarṣātā vana ūrdhvā navanta
    • ād: This means "beginning" or "first," indicating the importance of starting with intention.
    • īṁ: Again, this represents energy or life force.
    • viśvā: This translates to "all" or "universal," suggesting inclusivity and unity.
    • nahuṣyāṇi: Refers to "those who are powerful," indicating the strength found in unity.
    • jātā: This means "born" or "created," emphasizing the idea of new beginnings.
    • svarṣātā: This translates to "from the heavens," indicating divine influence.
    • vana: Meaning "forest" or "nature," it symbolizes growth and the natural world.
    • ūrdhvā: This means "upward" or "higher," suggesting aspirations and elevation.
    • navanta: This translates to "new" or "fresh," indicating renewal and transformation.

Conclusion:

The mantra emphasizes the importance of unity, strength, and truth in our lives. It encourages individuals to embark on their journeys with a sense of purpose and connection to the divine. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various contexts such as teamwork in professional settings, where collaboration leads to greater achievements, or in personal relationships, where honesty and support foster deeper connections. The mantra serves as a reminder that by aligning ourselves with higher principles and working together, we can achieve greatness and experience renewal in our lives.
