तं वो धियā नव्यस्यā शविष्टम् प्रत्नम् प्रत्नवत् परितंस्यध्यै |स नो वक्षद अनिमानः सुवह्मिन्द्रो विश्वानि अति दुर्गहाणि ||
taṁ vo dhiyā navyasyā śaviṣṭham pratnam pratnavat paritaṁsayadhyai | sa no vakṣad animānaḥ suvahmendro viśvāny ati durgahāṇi ||
  • taṁ vo dhiyā: This phrase translates to "that (knowledge) for you with intellect." It emphasizes the importance of seeking wisdom and understanding through intellect.
  • navyasyā: This word means "new" or "fresh." It signifies the pursuit of new knowledge and insights that can lead to growth and development.
  • śaviṣṭham: This term refers to "the best" or "the most excellent." It highlights the aspiration to attain the highest form of knowledge or understanding.
  • pratnam pratnavat: This can be interpreted as "the one who is worthy of praise." It suggests that the pursuit of knowledge and wisdom is a noble endeavor.
  • paritaṁsayadhyai: This phrase means "to the one who is all-knowing." It indicates a reverence for the divine or universal knowledge that encompasses all aspects of life.
  • sa no vakṣad: This translates to "may he inspire us." It is a call for divine guidance and inspiration in our quest for knowledge.
  • animānaḥ: This word means "the one who is limitless." It signifies the boundless nature of knowledge and the infinite possibilities it presents.
  • suvahmendro: This term refers to "the benevolent lord." It represents a higher power or deity that supports and nurtures our pursuit of wisdom.
  • viśvāny ati durgahāṇi: This phrase means "all obstacles that are difficult to overcome." It acknowledges the challenges we face in life and the need for strength and support to overcome them.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of seeking knowledge and wisdom through intellect and divine guidance. It encourages individuals to pursue new insights and strive for excellence in their understanding. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in educational settings, personal development, and professional growth, where the pursuit of knowledge is essential. By invoking the divine for inspiration and support, individuals can overcome challenges and obstacles in their journey towards enlightenment and success.
