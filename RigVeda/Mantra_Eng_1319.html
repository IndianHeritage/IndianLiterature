आथर्वणायाश्विनादधिचेऽश्व्यं शिरः प्रत्य एयरयतम् |स वाम् मधु प्र वोचद ऋतायन त्वाष्ट्रं यद दसराव अपिकक्ष्यं वाम् ||
ātharvaṇāyāśvinā dadhīce 'śvyaṁ śiraḥ praty airayatam | sa vām madhu pra vocad ṛtāyan tvāṣṭraṁ yad dasrāv apikakṣyaṁ vām ||
  • Mantra: "ātharvaṇāyāśvinā dadhīce 'śvyaṁ śiraḥ praty airayatam | sa vām madhu pra vocad ṛtāyan tvāṣṭraṁ yad dasrāv apikakṣyaṁ vām ||"
  • Translation: This mantra invokes the divine Ashvins, the twin deities of health and medicine, asking them to bestow their blessings and wisdom. It emphasizes the importance of knowledge and the pursuit of truth.
  • Word Breakdown:
    • ātharvaṇāyāśvinā: Referring to the Ashvins, who are associated with healing and well-being.
    • dadhīce: To bestow or grant.
    • śvyaṁ: This word can imply 'your' or 'of you'.
    • śiraḥ: Head or intellect, symbolizing wisdom.
    • praty: Towards or in the direction of.
    • airayatam: To inspire or enlighten.
    • madhu: Sweetness or nectar, often symbolizing the essence of life.
    • pra vocad: To speak forth or declare.
    • ṛtāyan: The embodiment of truth or righteousness.
    • tvāṣṭraṁ: A reference to the divine craftsman or creator.
    • yad: That which.
    • dasrāv: To be heard or listened to.
    • apikakṣyaṁ: To be desired or sought after.
    • vām: You or your.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • This mantra can be recited during health-related rituals or ceremonies, invoking the Ashvins for healing and wellness.
    • In modern medicine, the pursuit of knowledge and truth is paramount, similar to the wisdom sought from the Ashvins in this mantra.
    • In personal development, individuals can reflect on the importance of seeking knowledge and truth in their lives, akin to the blessings of the Ashvins.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine guidance in the pursuit of health, wisdom, and truth. It emphasizes the importance of seeking knowledge and the blessings of the divine in our lives. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applicable in health rituals, personal growth, and the quest for knowledge, reminding us to seek the sweet essence of life and truth in our endeavors.
