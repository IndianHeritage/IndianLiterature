मन्त्र: चित्तिम् अचित्तिं चिनवत् विद्वान् पृष्ठेव वीताऽवृजिना च मर्तान् |रायः च नः स्वपत्याय देव दितिं च रास्वादितिम् उरुष्य ||
cittim acittiṁ cinavad vi vidvān pṛṣṭheva vītā vṛjinā ca martān | rāye ca naḥ svapatyāya deva ditiṁ ca rāsvāditim uruṣya ||
  • cittim acittiṁ: This phrase can be interpreted as the duality of consciousness. "Cittim" refers to the conscious mind, while "acittiṁ" refers to the unconscious or the state of being unaware. In real life, this can be related to the balance we seek between our conscious decisions and our subconscious influences, such as habits or instincts.
  • cinavad vi vidvān: "Cinavad" means to perceive or understand, and "vidvān" refers to a wise person or a learned individual. This suggests that true wisdom comes from the ability to perceive beyond the surface. For example, a wise leader in a community understands not just the spoken words of their people but also the underlying emotions and needs.
  • pṛṣṭheva vītā vṛjinā ca martān: This line can be interpreted as a call to overcome obstacles ("vṛjinā") and to rise above mortality ("martān"). In practical terms, this can relate to personal challenges we face in life, such as health issues or financial struggles, and the importance of resilience and perseverance in overcoming them.
  • rāye ca naḥ svapatyāya: "Rāye" refers to wealth or prosperity, and "svapatyāya" suggests a desire for it to come to us. This can be seen in the context of striving for success in our careers or personal lives, where we seek abundance and fulfillment. For instance, a business owner may work tirelessly to ensure their company thrives.
  • deva ditiṁ ca rāsvāditim: Here, "deva" refers to the divine or higher powers, while "ditiṁ" can be interpreted as a form of protection or guidance. This line emphasizes the importance of seeking divine support in our endeavors. In real life, many people turn to spirituality or prayer during difficult times, seeking guidance and strength.
  • uruṣya: This term can be understood as the vastness or the expansive nature of existence. It reminds us of the interconnectedness of all beings and the universe. In practical terms, this can be reflected in our relationships and the importance of community and collaboration in achieving common goals.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the journey of self-awareness, wisdom, and the pursuit of prosperity while acknowledging the divine and the interconnectedness of life. Its significance lies in the reminder to balance our conscious and unconscious selves, to seek wisdom in our actions, to overcome challenges, and to strive for abundance with the support of higher powers. In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as personal development, leadership, and community building, encouraging individuals to reflect on their inner selves and their place in the world.
