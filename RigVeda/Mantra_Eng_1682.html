यन् निर्णिजा रेक्णसां प्रावृतस्य रातिं गृभीताम् मुखतो नयन्ति |सुप्राङ् अजो मेम्यद विश्वरूप इन्द्रापूष्णोः प्रियम् अप्य एति पाठः ||
yan nirṇijā rekṇasā prāvṛtasya rātiṁ gṛbhītām mukhato nayanti | suprāṅ ajo memyad viśvarūpa indrāpūṣṇoḥ priyam apy eti pāthaḥ ||
  • yan nirṇijā rekṇasā prāvṛtasya rātiṁ gṛbhītām mukhato nayanti
    • yan - that which
    • nirṇijā - is not disturbed or shaken
    • rekṇasā - by the force of the wind
    • prāvṛtasya - of the one who has come forth
    • rātiṁ - joy or happiness
    • gṛbhītām - that which is grasped or held
    • mukhato - from the mouth
    • nayanti - they lead or guide
  • suprāṅ ajo memyad viśvarūpa indrāpūṣṇoḥ priyam apy eti pāthaḥ
    • suprāṅ - well-formed or auspicious
    • ajo - unborn or eternal
    • memyad - that which is desired
    • viśvarūpa - of universal form
    • indrāpūṣṇoḥ - of Indra and Pūṣṇa (deities representing strength and nourishment)
    • priyam - dear or beloved
    • apy - even
    • eti - goes or reaches
    • pāthaḥ - the path or way

This mantra speaks to the idea of stability and guidance in the face of life's challenges. The first part emphasizes that true joy is not easily shaken by external forces, much like a tree that stands firm against the wind. In real life, this can be likened to individuals who maintain their composure and happiness despite facing adversities, such as a person who remains positive during difficult times, drawing strength from their inner resilience.


The second part of the mantra highlights the importance of seeking the auspicious and eternal aspects of life, represented by the deities Indra and Pūṣṇa. This can be seen in the pursuit of goals that align with one's values and the greater good, such as community service or environmental conservation. Just as these deities provide nourishment and strength, individuals can find fulfillment in actions that benefit others.


Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its encouragement to cultivate inner strength and seek a path that is both auspicious and aligned with universal values. In real life, it serves as a reminder to remain steadfast in the face of challenges and to pursue actions that contribute positively to the world around us. By embodying these principles, individuals can lead a life that is not only fulfilling for themselves but also beneficial to others, creating a harmonious existence.
