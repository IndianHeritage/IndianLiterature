ओमासश्चरषणिधृतो विश्वे देवासा आगत |दाश्वांसो दाशुषः सुतम् ||
omāsaś carṣaṇīdhṛto viśve devāsa ā gata | dāśvāṁso dāśuṣaḥ sutam ||
  • Omāsaś: This term can be interpreted as "may we be united" or "together." It signifies the importance of unity and collective effort.
  • Carṣaṇī: This word refers to "the earth" or "the ground." It symbolizes stability and the foundation upon which life is built.
  • Dhṛto: Meaning "held" or "supported," this term emphasizes the idea of being upheld by a higher power or the community.
  • Viśve devāsa: Translated as "all the gods," this phrase acknowledges the presence and influence of divine forces in our lives.
  • Ā gata: This means "have come" or "arrived," indicating the manifestation of divine blessings or guidance.
  • Dāśvāṁso: This term can be understood as "the tenfold" or "the multitude," representing the various aspects of life and existence.
  • Dāśuṣaḥ: This word refers to "the ten," which can symbolize completeness or wholeness in various contexts.
  • Sutam: Meaning "well-born" or "noble," this term highlights the importance of good lineage, virtues, and moral values.

The mantra can be interpreted as a prayer for unity and support from the divine, emphasizing the importance of community and the blessings that come from a higher power. It calls upon all gods to provide guidance and strength, ensuring that individuals are well-supported in their endeavors.


In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts:

  • Community Building: In a society where collaboration and unity are essential, this mantra serves as a reminder of the strength found in working together towards common goals.
  • Support Systems: It highlights the importance of having a support system, whether it be family, friends, or community, to help individuals navigate challenges.
  • Spiritual Guidance: The invocation of divine presence encourages individuals to seek spiritual guidance and remain connected to their beliefs.

In conclusion, this mantra signifies the importance of unity, support, and divine guidance in our lives. It serves as a reminder that we are not alone in our journeys and that collective efforts, along with spiritual blessings, can lead to success and fulfillment. By embracing these principles, individuals can foster a sense of community and purpose in their everyday lives.
