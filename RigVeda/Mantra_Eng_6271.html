स त्वं अस्मद अप द्विषो युयोधि जातवेदः |अदेवीर अग्नि अरातिः ||
sa tvam asmad apa dviṣo yuyodhi jātavedaḥ | adevīr agne arātīḥ ||
  • sa tvam: This phrase translates to "You are" or "You indeed." It signifies the presence of a divine or powerful entity.
  • asmad: This means "us" or "our," indicating a collective identity or community.
  • apa: This translates to "from" or "away from," suggesting a separation or distancing from something undesirable.
  • dviṣo: This word means "enemies" or "those who hate," referring to adversaries or negative forces.
  • yuyodhi: This means "fight" or "battle," indicating a call to action or a plea for strength in overcoming challenges.
  • jātavedaḥ: This term refers to "the one who knows the origin" or "the wise one," often associated with Agni, the fire god, symbolizing knowledge and enlightenment.
  • adevīr: This translates to "without the divine" or "without the gods," suggesting a state of being devoid of divine support.
  • agne: This is a direct address to Agni, the fire god, representing energy, transformation, and purification.
  • arātīḥ: This means "obstacles" or "hindrances," referring to the challenges and difficulties one faces in life.

The mantra can be interpreted as a plea for divine assistance in overcoming adversities and enemies. It acknowledges the presence of a higher power (Agni) and seeks strength and guidance to combat negativity and obstacles in life.


In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts:

  • Personal Challenges: When facing difficulties in personal life, such as health issues or emotional struggles, one can recite this mantra to invoke strength and clarity.
  • Professional Obstacles: In a competitive work environment, this mantra can serve as a reminder to stay focused and seek guidance to overcome professional challenges.
  • Conflict Resolution: During conflicts or disputes, reciting this mantra can help individuals find the courage to address issues and seek resolution.

Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine support in overcoming adversities. It emphasizes the importance of seeking strength from a higher power while acknowledging the presence of challenges. In various aspects of life, whether personal, professional, or relational, this mantra serves as a reminder to confront obstacles with courage and wisdom, ultimately leading to growth and transformation.
