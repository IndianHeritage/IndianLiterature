श्रुतं वो वृत्रहन्तमम् प्र शर्धं चर्षणीनाम् |आ शुशे राधसे महे ||
śrutaṁ vo vṛtrahantamam pra śardhaṁ carṣaṇīnām | ā śuṣe rādhase mahe ||
  • śrutaṁ: This word translates to "hearing" or "listening." It emphasizes the importance of being receptive to knowledge and wisdom.
  • vo: This means "to you," indicating a direct address to the audience or the divine.
  • vṛtrahantamam: This compound word can be broken down into "vṛtra," which refers to obstacles or demons, and "hantamam," meaning "the one who destroys." Together, it signifies the destroyer of obstacles.
  • pra: This prefix means "forth" or "forward," suggesting a movement towards something positive or beneficial.
  • śardhaṁ: This word means "together" or "in unity," highlighting the importance of collective effort.
  • carṣaṇīnām: This refers to "the wise" or "the learned," indicating those who possess knowledge and understanding.
  • ā: This is a prefix that can mean "towards" or "in the direction of," suggesting a goal or aspiration.
  • śuṣe: This word means "to prosper" or "to thrive," indicating a positive outcome or success.
  • rādhase: This can be interpreted as "to the one who is worthy" or "to the one who deserves," suggesting respect and recognition for merit.
  • mahe: This means "great" or "mighty," emphasizing the significance of the subject being addressed.

In summary, this mantra can be interpreted as a call to listen and be receptive to the wisdom that helps in overcoming obstacles. It emphasizes the importance of unity and collective effort among the wise, aiming for prosperity and recognizing the greatness of those who deserve it.


Significance and Real-Life Application:

  • This mantra encourages individuals to seek knowledge and wisdom actively, which is applicable in educational settings where students are encouraged to listen and learn from their teachers.
  • In a workplace, it highlights the importance of teamwork and collaboration, where collective efforts lead to overcoming challenges and achieving success.
  • On a personal level, it serves as a reminder to be open to advice and guidance from those who have more experience, fostering personal growth and development.

Ultimately, the mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the importance of wisdom, unity, and the pursuit of prosperity in various aspects of life.
