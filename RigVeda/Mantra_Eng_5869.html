तां सानसी शवसानां हि भूतं साकंवृद्धा शवसा शूषुवांसा |क्षयन्तौ रायो यवसस्य भूरेः पृङ्क्तं वाजस्य स्थविरस्य घृष्वेः ||
tā sānasī śavasānā hi bhūtaṁ sākaṁvṛdhā śavasā śūśuvāṁsā | kṣayantau rāyo yavasasya bhūreḥ pṛṅktaṁ vājasya sthavirasya ghṛṣveḥ ||
  • tā sānasī: This phrase can be interpreted as "that which is established" or "that which is firm." It signifies stability and permanence in life.
  • śavasānā hi bhūtaṁ: This translates to "the essence of existence." It refers to the fundamental nature of being and the interconnectedness of all life forms.
  • sākaṁvṛdhā: This means "growing together." It emphasizes the importance of collaboration and mutual growth among individuals and communities.
  • śavasā: This can be understood as "the vital force" or "energy." It represents the life force that drives all living beings.
  • śūśuvāṁsā: This term refers to "the nourishing aspect." It highlights the importance of sustenance and support in life.
  • kṣayantau rāyo: This phrase means "the diminishing of wealth." It suggests that material wealth can be transient and should not be the sole focus of life.
  • yavasasya bhūreḥ: This translates to "the abundance of grains." It symbolizes prosperity and the importance of agriculture and sustenance in human life.
  • pṛṅktaṁ vājasya: This means "the strength of the horse." In ancient times, horses were symbols of power and vitality, representing the strength needed to overcome challenges.
  • sthavirasya ghṛṣveḥ: This can be interpreted as "the enduring nature of the strong." It emphasizes resilience and the ability to withstand adversities.

  • Conclusion: This mantra encapsulates the essence of life, emphasizing stability, interconnectedness, collaboration, and the importance of sustenance. In real-life scenarios, it can be applied to various aspects such as community building, teamwork in professional settings, and the significance of nurturing relationships. It serves as a reminder that while material wealth may diminish, the strength derived from unity and mutual support is enduring. This mantra encourages individuals to focus on collective growth and resilience, fostering a harmonious existence.
