मन्त्र: अश्वप्नजस् तरणयः सुशेवा अतन्द्रासोऽवृका अश्रमिष्टाः |ते पायवः सध्रियञ्चो निषद्याग्ने तव नः पान्त्व अमूर ||
asvapnajas taraṇayaḥ suśevā atandrāso 'vṛkā aśramiṣṭhāḥ | te pāyavaḥ sadhryañco niṣadyāgne tava naḥ pāntv amūra ||
  • asvapnajas taraṇayaḥ: This phrase can be interpreted as "those who are born from the non-sleeping" or "those who are awake and alert." In real life, this can refer to individuals who are proactive and aware of their surroundings, much like a vigilant leader or a dedicated student who is always ready to learn and adapt.
  • suśevā atandrāso 'vṛkā: "suśevā" means "well-protected" or "well-nurtured," while "atandrāso" refers to "those who do not tire" and "'vṛkā" can mean "the wolves" or "the strong." This can symbolize a community or a group of people who are resilient and well-cared for, akin to a family that supports each other through challenges, ensuring that no one feels exhausted or abandoned.
  • aśramiṣṭhāḥ: This term can be understood as "those who are engaged in spiritual practice" or "those who are dedicated." In a modern context, this could relate to individuals who are committed to personal growth, such as someone who practices mindfulness or meditation regularly to enhance their mental well-being.
  • te pāyavaḥ sadhryañco: "te pāyavaḥ" translates to "may they protect us," while "sadhryañco" means "with good fortune." This can be seen in real life as a call for support from friends, family, or even mentors who provide guidance and protection during difficult times, much like a coach who encourages their team to strive for success.
  • niṣadyāgne tava naḥ pāntv amūra: "niṣadyāgne" refers to "the seated fire" or "the divine presence," and "tava naḥ pāntv amūra" means "may it protect us from all sides." This can be likened to the idea of a safe space or a sanctuary where one feels secure and at peace, such as a home filled with love and understanding, or a community that fosters inclusivity and support.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of vigilance, resilience, and spiritual dedication in our lives. It calls for protection and support from those around us, highlighting the significance of community and personal growth. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied to various aspects, such as fostering strong relationships, creating safe environments, and encouraging proactive behavior in both personal and professional settings. By embodying the principles of this mantra, individuals can cultivate a life filled with awareness, support, and spiritual fulfillment.
