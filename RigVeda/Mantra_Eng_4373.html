त्वां वर्धन्ति क्षितयः पृथिव्यां त्वां राया उभयासो जनानाम् |त्वं त्राताऽतरणे चेत्यो भूः पिता माता सदमिन मानवाणाम् ||
tvāṁ vardhanti kṣitayaḥ pṛthivyāṁ tvāṁ rāya ubhayāso janānām | tvaṁ trātā taraṇe cetyo bhūḥ pitā mātā sadam in mānuṣāṇām ||
  • tvāṁ vardhanti kṣitayaḥ pṛthivyāṁ: This phrase translates to "The earth (kṣitayaḥ) nourishes you (tvāṁ)". It signifies the nurturing aspect of the earth, which provides sustenance and support to all living beings. In real life, this can be seen in agriculture, where the soil and environment play a crucial role in growing food that sustains human life.
  • tvāṁ rāya ubhayāso janānām: This means "You (tvāṁ) are the wealth (rāya) of both (ubhayāso) the people (janānām)". Here, the mantra emphasizes the importance of resources and wealth that benefit society. For example, community resources such as parks, libraries, and public services enhance the quality of life for all individuals.
  • tvaṁ trātā taraṇe cetyo bhūḥ: This translates to "You (tvaṁ) are the protector (trātā) in the journey (taraṇe) of existence (bhūḥ)". This highlights the role of a guardian or protector in life’s journey. In real life, this can be related to the role of parents, mentors, or leaders who guide and protect individuals through challenges and uncertainties.
  • pitā mātā sadam in mānuṣāṇām: This means "Father (pitā) and Mother (mātā) are always (sadam) present in humanity (mānuṣāṇām)". This phrase underscores the fundamental roles of parental figures in human life, symbolizing love, care, and guidance. In real life, this can be observed in family structures where parents provide emotional and financial support to their children, shaping their future.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of nurturing, protection, and the importance of community and familial bonds. It signifies that the earth provides for us, that wealth is not just material but also encompasses the support of our community, and that parental figures are vital in guiding us through life. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied to promote environmental sustainability, community welfare, and the value of family, encouraging individuals to appreciate and contribute to the well-being of their surroundings and loved ones.
