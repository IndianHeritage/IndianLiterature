न युष्मे वाजबन्धवो निनित्सुश्चन मार्त्यः |अवद्यं अधि दीधरत ||
na yuṣme vājabandhavo ninitsuś cana martyaḥ | avadyam adhi dīdharat ||
  • na yuṣme: This phrase translates to "not for you" or "not to you." It indicates a sense of separation or distinction from the subject being addressed.
  • vājabandhavo: This term can be broken down into "vāja," meaning "strength" or "power," and "bandhavo," which refers to "friends" or "allies." Together, it suggests "friends of strength" or "allies of power."
  • ninitsuś: This word means "to be defeated" or "to be overcome." It implies a sense of struggle or conflict.
  • cana martyaḥ: "cana" means "any" or "at all," and "martyaḥ" refers to "mortal" or "human." This phrase can be interpreted as "any mortal" or "any human being."
  • avadyam: This term means "without fault" or "pure." It signifies something that is unblemished or free from defects.
  • adhi dīdharat: "adhi" means "above" or "over," and "dīdharat" can be understood as "to hold" or "to bear." Together, it suggests the idea of holding something above or maintaining a higher standard.

The mantra can be interpreted as a declaration of the strength and purity of one's allies, emphasizing that no mortal can defeat them, and that they hold themselves to a higher standard of conduct.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as:

  • Teamwork: In a team setting, the mantra encourages individuals to recognize the strength of their collective efforts. Just as the allies in the mantra are undeterred by challenges, a team can overcome obstacles when they work together with integrity and strength.
  • Personal Integrity: The emphasis on being "without fault" serves as a reminder to maintain personal integrity in our actions. In professional settings, this can translate to ethical decision-making and accountability.
  • Resilience: The idea that no mortal can defeat the strength of one's allies can inspire resilience in the face of adversity. It encourages individuals to lean on their support systems during tough times.

Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its powerful message about the strength of unity, integrity, and resilience. It serves as a reminder that when we align ourselves with trustworthy allies and uphold high standards, we can face challenges with confidence and emerge victorious. This mantra can be a guiding principle in both personal and professional realms, encouraging individuals to foster strong relationships and maintain their values.
