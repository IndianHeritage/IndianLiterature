मन्त्र: परि द्युक्षः सानद्रयिर भरद वाजं नो अन्धसा | सुवानो अर्ष पवित्र आ ||
pari dyukṣaḥ sanadrayir bharad vājaṁ no andhasā | suvāno arṣa pavitra ā ||
  • Mantra: "pari dyukṣaḥ sanadrayir bharad vājaṁ no andhasā | suvāno arṣa pavitra ā ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • pari: around, all around
    • dyukṣaḥ: shining, bright
    • sanadrayir: with good intentions, with a pure heart
    • bharad: may you bring, may you bestow
    • vājaṁ: strength, power, or nourishment
    • no: for us
    • andhasā: from darkness, from ignorance
    • suvāno: auspicious, good
    • arṣa: to the seers, to the wise
    • pavitra: pure, sacred
    • ā: may it come, may it arrive
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the divine forces to surround us with brightness and purity.
    • It emphasizes the importance of good intentions and a pure heart in our actions.
    • It seeks strength and nourishment for our endeavors, while also asking to be guided away from ignorance and darkness.
    • The mention of "suvāno" and "pavitra" highlights the significance of auspiciousness and purity in our lives.
    • It acknowledges the wisdom of the seers, suggesting that we should aspire to their level of understanding and insight.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, this mantra can be applied when starting a new project. By focusing on good intentions and seeking guidance, one can create a positive work environment.
    • In personal relationships, approaching interactions with purity of heart can lead to stronger bonds and mutual respect.
    • In education, students can invoke this mantra to seek clarity and wisdom, aiming to overcome ignorance and achieve knowledge.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of purity, good intentions, and the pursuit of knowledge in our lives.
    • Its significance lies in its ability to inspire individuals to seek strength and guidance while navigating through life's challenges.
    • By embodying the principles of this mantra, one can foster a more harmonious and enlightened existence, both personally and collectively.
