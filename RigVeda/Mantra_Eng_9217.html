युवं ह कृषं युवम अश्विनां शयुं युवं विधन्तं विधवाम उरुष्यतः |युवं सनीभ्यः स्तनयन्तम अश्विनाप व्रजम् ऊर्णुथः सप्ताश्यम् ||
yuvaṁ ha kṛśaṁ yuvam aśvinā śayuṁ yuvaṁ vidhantaṁ vidhavām uruṣyathaḥ | yuvaṁ sanibhyaḥ stanayantam aśvināpa vrajam ūrṇuthaḥ saptāsyam ||
  • Mantra: "yuvaṁ ha kṛśaṁ yuvam aśvinā śayuṁ yuvaṁ vidhantaṁ vidhavām uruṣyathaḥ | yuvaṁ sanibhyaḥ stanayantam aśvināpa vrajam ūrṇuthaḥ saptāsyam ||"
  • Translation: This mantra invokes the divine Aśvins, the twin deities of health and medicine, asking them to bring forth the young and the strong, to provide nourishment and support, and to bless the community with abundance and vitality.
  • Word Breakdown:
    • yuvaṁ: young, youthful
    • ha: indeed, certainly
    • kṛśaṁ: weak, frail
    • yuvam: the young one
    • aśvinā: by the Aśvins
    • śayuṁ: bed, resting place
    • vidhantaṁ: creating, forming
    • vidhavām: the one who is bereaved
    • uruṣyathaḥ: may you be strong, may you thrive
    • sanibhyaḥ: from the close ones, from the relatives
    • stanayantam: nourishing, providing sustenance
    • vrajam: the community, the group
    • ūrṇuthaḥ: may you bring forth, may you produce
    • saptāsyam: sevenfold, in abundance
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • This mantra can be related to the importance of youth in society. For instance, in many cultures, young people are seen as the future and are often encouraged to take on leadership roles.
    • The invocation of the Aśvins can be likened to modern healthcare practices where we seek the best medical care and support for the young and vulnerable in our communities.
    • The idea of nourishment can be connected to the importance of nutrition in child development, emphasizing the need for healthy food to support growth and vitality.
  • Significance:
    • This mantra highlights the importance of youth and vitality in the community, emphasizing the need for support and nourishment.
    • It serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of individuals within a community, where the well-being of the young is crucial for the overall health of society.
    • In a broader sense, it encourages us to seek divine blessings for health, strength, and abundance, which are essential for a thriving community.
