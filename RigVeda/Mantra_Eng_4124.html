युयं रयिम मरुतः स्पार्हवीरं युयम ऋषिम अवथ सामविप्रम् |युयम अर्वन्तम भरताय वाजं युयं धत्त राजानं श्रुष्टिमन्तम् ||
yūyaṁ rayim marutaḥ spārhavīraṁ yūyam ṛṣim avatha sāmavipram | yūyam arvantam bharatāya vājaṁ yūyaṁ dhattha rājānaṁ śruṣṭimantam ||
  • yūyaṁ: This word translates to "you all" or "you together," indicating a collective group.
  • rayim: This means "wealth" or "prosperity," often referring to both material and spiritual abundance.
  • marutaḥ: Refers to the "Maruts," the storm deities in Vedic literature, symbolizing strength and power.
  • spārhavīraṁ: This can be interpreted as "the valiant" or "the brave," emphasizing courage and valor.
  • ṛṣim: This word means "sage" or "seer," representing wisdom and knowledge.
  • avatha: This translates to "you protect" or "you uphold," indicating a sense of guardianship.
  • sāmavipram: This refers to "the one who is skilled in the Sāma Veda," highlighting the importance of musical and poetic knowledge.
  • arvantam: This means "the one who is worthy" or "the one who is deserving," suggesting merit and value.
  • bharatāya: This translates to "for the Bharata," referring to the people or the nation, often associated with the idea of unity.
  • vājaṁ: This means "strength" or "power," often in the context of victory or success.
  • dhattha: This translates to "you give" or "you bestow," indicating generosity and support.
  • rājānaṁ: This means "kings" or "rulers," symbolizing leadership and authority.
  • śruṣṭimantam: This refers to "the creator" or "the one who brings forth," emphasizing creativity and innovation.

The mantra essentially calls upon a collective group (the Maruts) to bestow wealth, strength, and protection upon the community. It emphasizes the importance of wisdom, bravery, and leadership in achieving prosperity and success. The invocation of the Maruts signifies a request for divine assistance in overcoming challenges and achieving collective goals.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as:

  • Community Development: When a community comes together to work towards a common goal, invoking the spirit of unity and strength can lead to successful outcomes.
  • Leadership Initiatives: Leaders can draw inspiration from this mantra to foster courage and wisdom in their decision-making processes, ensuring they protect and uplift their people.
  • Personal Growth: Individuals can reflect on the qualities of bravery, wisdom, and generosity in their own lives, striving to embody these traits in their daily actions.

In conclusion, this mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the importance of collective strength, wisdom, and leadership in achieving prosperity and overcoming challenges. It encourages individuals and communities to come together, support one another, and strive for a common purpose, ultimately leading to a harmonious and prosperous society.
