आ नो भद्राः क्रतवो यन्तु विश्वतो 'दब्धासो अपरीतास उद्भिदः |देवा नो यथा सदमिद्वृधे असन्नप्रायुवो रक्षितारो दिवेदिवे ||
ā no bhadrāḥ kratavo yantu viśvato 'dabdhāso aparītāsa udbhidaḥ | devā no yathā sadam id vṛdhe asann aprāyuvo rakṣitāro dive-dive ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā: May
    • no: our
    • bhadrāḥ: auspicious, good
    • kratavo: actions, efforts
    • yantu: may go, may proceed
    • viśvato: from all directions
    • 'dabdhāso: unburnt, untouched
    • aparītāsa: unbroken, undiminished
    • udbhidaḥ: arising, emerging
    • devā: gods, divine beings
    • no: our
    • yathā: as, in the way
    • sad: true, real
    • am: this
    • id: here, now
    • vṛdhe: in growth, in prosperity
    • asann: not, without
    • aprāyuvo: without obstacles, unhindered
    • rakṣitāro: protectors, guardians
    • dive-dive: day by day
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer for auspicious actions and efforts to come to us from all directions.
    • It emphasizes the importance of divine protection and guidance in our daily lives.
    • The phrase "unburnt" signifies purity and resilience, suggesting that our efforts should remain untouched by negativity.
    • It calls for the divine to ensure that our growth and prosperity are free from obstacles.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be invoked to seek success in ventures, ensuring that efforts are fruitful and free from hindrances.
    • In personal life, individuals may recite this mantra to seek protection and guidance in their daily activities, fostering a sense of security and positivity.
    • In community gatherings, it can be used to promote collective well-being and harmony, encouraging everyone to work together towards common goals.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of divine support in our endeavors, reminding us that with the right intentions and actions, we can achieve our goals.
    • It serves as a reminder to remain resilient and pure in our efforts, ensuring that we are protected and guided in our daily lives.
    • By incorporating this mantra into our routines, we can cultivate a mindset of positivity and openness to the blessings that come our way.
