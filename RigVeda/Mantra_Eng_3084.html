कविं शशासुः कवयोऽदब्धा निधारयन्तो दुर्याश्वायोः |अतस्त्वं दृष्ट्याम् अग्न एतान् पद्भिः पश्येर अद्भुताम् आर्य एविः ||
kaviṁ śaśāsuḥ kavayo 'dabdhā nidhārayanto duryāsv āyoḥ | atas tvaṁ dṛśyām̐ agna etān paḍbhiḥ paśyer adbhutām̐ arya evaiḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • kaviṁ: The seer or wise one.
    • śaśāsuḥ: Of the moon or lunar aspect.
    • kavayo: The wise or learned ones.
    • 'dabdhā: Bound or restrained.
    • nidhārayanto: Those who hold or maintain.
    • duryāsv: Difficult to attain or hard to reach.
    • āyoḥ: Of the two (or duality).
    • atas: Therefore or hence.
    • tvaṁ: You.
    • dṛśyām̐: In sight or visible.
    • agna: O Agni (the fire or divine energy).
    • etān: These.
    • paḍbhiḥ: By the words or hymns.
    • paśyer: May see or perceive.
    • adbhutām̐: Wonderful or extraordinary.
    • arya: Noble or honorable.
    • evaiḥ: Indeed or certainly.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the wise seers who are able to perceive the deeper truths of existence.
    • It emphasizes the importance of knowledge and wisdom in understanding the complexities of life.
    • The reference to Agni symbolizes the divine energy that illuminates the path of knowledge.
    • The mantra suggests that through the power of words and hymns, one can attain a vision of the extraordinary aspects of life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern education, teachers and mentors act as the 'kaviṁ' or wise ones, guiding students to understand complex subjects.
    • In personal development, individuals often seek knowledge through books and experiences, akin to the 'kavayo' who hold wisdom.
    • In spiritual practices, meditation and rituals can be seen as ways to connect with the divine energy of Agni, helping individuals to gain insight and clarity.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of wisdom and knowledge in navigating life's challenges.
    • It encourages individuals to seek understanding and clarity through learning and introspection.
    • In real-life scenarios, applying the principles of this mantra can lead to personal growth, better decision-making, and a deeper appreciation of the extraordinary aspects of existence.
