यथा चिद वृद्धम अतसाम अग्नि संजूर्वसि क्षमि |एवा दह मित्रमहो यो अस्मध्रुग दुरमनमा कश्च वेनति ||
yathā cid vṛddham atasam agne saṁjūrvasi kṣami | evā daha mitramaho yo asmadhrug durmanmā kaś ca venati ||
  • yathā cid vṛddham: This phrase can be translated as "as if it were grown" or "in the manner of growth." It signifies the idea of development and maturity, suggesting that one should strive for growth in all aspects of life.
  • atasam agne: "Atasam" refers to "in the presence of," and "agne" means "O Fire" or "O Divine Light." This invokes the imagery of fire as a purifying and transformative force, often associated with knowledge and enlightenment.
  • saṁjūrvasi kṣami: "Saṁjūrvasi" means "you unite" or "you bring together," while "kṣami" translates to "forgive" or "be patient." This suggests the importance of unity and forgiveness in relationships and communities.
  • evā daha mitramaho: "Evā" means "thus," "daha" translates to "burn," and "mitramaho" refers to "O friend." This can be interpreted as a call to burn away negativity and foster friendship and camaraderie.
  • yo asmadhrug: "Yo" means "who," and "asmadhrug" translates to "who sees us." This implies a recognition of the divine presence that observes and guides us in our actions.
  • durmanmā: This term means "ill thoughts" or "negative intentions." It highlights the need to be aware of and overcome negative thoughts that can hinder personal and communal growth.
  • kaś ca venati: "Kaś ca" means "whoever" and "venati" translates to "invokes" or "calls upon." This suggests that anyone who seeks to connect with the divine or higher consciousness can do so.

Conclusion:

The mantra emphasizes the importance of personal growth, unity, forgiveness, and the pursuit of positive intentions. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various contexts such as:

  • In personal relationships, fostering understanding and forgiveness can lead to stronger bonds and a more harmonious environment.
  • In community settings, promoting unity and collective growth can help overcome challenges and create a supportive atmosphere.
  • In professional environments, encouraging positive intentions and collaboration can enhance teamwork and productivity.

Overall, this mantra serves as a reminder to cultivate positivity, seek growth, and maintain a connection with the divine, which can lead to a more fulfilling and harmonious life.
