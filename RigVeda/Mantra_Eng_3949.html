मां माम इमं तव संतम अत्र इरस्याद्रुग्धो भियसां नि गारीत |त्वं मित्रोऽसि सत्यराधास तौ मेहावतम् वरुणश्च राजा ||
mā mām imaṁ tava santam atra irasyā drugdho bhiyasā ni gārīt | tvam mitro asi satyarādhās tau mehāvataṁ varuṇaś ca rājā ||
  • mā mām imaṁ tava santam atra irasyā drugdho bhiyasā ni gārīt
    • mā mām: This phrase translates to "do not let me." It expresses a plea or a request for protection or guidance.
    • imaṁ: This means "this" or "this one," referring to the speaker's current state or situation.
    • tava santam: "Your peace" or "your tranquility," indicating a desire for the divine presence or blessings.
    • atra: Meaning "here" or "in this place," it emphasizes the immediacy of the speaker's situation.
    • irasyā drugdho: This can be interpreted as "from the wrath" or "from the anger," suggesting a fear of negative consequences.
    • bhiyasā ni gārīt: "May I not be harmed," a plea for safety and protection from harm.
  • tvam mitro asi satyarādhās tau mehāvataṁ varuṇaś ca rājā
    • tvam: "You," referring to the divine or a higher power.
    • mitro asi: "You are a friend," indicating a relationship of trust and support with the divine.
    • satyarādhās: "You who are established in truth," highlighting the importance of truthfulness and integrity.
    • tau mehāvataṁ: "May you grant me greatness," a request for blessings and prosperity.
    • varuṇaś ca rājā: "Varuna, the king," referring to Varuna, the Vedic deity associated with cosmic order and moral authority.

Conclusion:

This mantra encapsulates a deep yearning for divine protection and guidance. It reflects the human condition of seeking safety from harm while acknowledging the presence of a benevolent higher power. In real life, this can be applicable in various situations such as during times of uncertainty, fear, or when facing challenges. For instance, individuals may recite this mantra during stressful events like exams, job interviews, or personal crises, seeking the comfort of divine friendship and the assurance of truth and justice. The significance lies in the recognition of a supportive universe that stands by those who seek truth and righteousness, encouraging individuals to remain steadfast in their values while navigating life's complexities.
