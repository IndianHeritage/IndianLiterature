यस् ते साधिष्ठोऽवसे ते स्याम भरेषु ते |वयं होत्राभिरुत देवहूतिभिः ससवांसो मनामहे ||
yas te sādhiṣṭho 'vase te syāma bhareṣu te | vayaṁ hotrābhir uta devahūtibhiḥ sasavāṁso manāmahe ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yas: who
    • te: your
    • sādhiṣṭho: the best, the most excellent
    • 'vase: may we dwell, may we reside
    • te: your
    • syāma: may we become
    • bhareṣu: in the offerings
    • te: your
    • vayaṁ: we
    • hotrābhir: with our invocations
    • uta: and
    • devahūtibhiḥ: with divine offerings
    • sasavāṁso: may we be endowed
    • manāmahe: we meditate, we contemplate
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra expresses a desire to connect with the divine and to be in harmony with the best aspects of existence.
    • The phrase "may we dwell in your excellence" suggests a longing to embody the virtues and qualities that are considered supreme.
    • It emphasizes the importance of offerings, both material and spiritual, as a means to connect with the divine.
    • The invocation of "we meditate" indicates a collective effort to seek wisdom and understanding through contemplation and prayer.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community gathering, people may come together to offer prayers and share their best qualities, fostering a sense of unity and purpose.
    • In personal life, individuals may strive to embody excellence in their actions, whether in their careers, relationships, or personal growth.
    • During rituals or ceremonies, offerings made to deities can symbolize gratitude and a desire for blessings, reflecting the essence of this mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of striving for excellence and connecting with the divine through offerings and meditation.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, such as community gatherings, personal development, and spiritual practices.
    • By embodying the qualities of excellence and engaging in collective invocations, individuals and communities can foster a deeper connection with the divine and enhance their spiritual journey.
