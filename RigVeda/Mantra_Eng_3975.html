सम इन्द्र णो मनसा नेषि गोभिः सं सूरिभिर हरिवः सं स्वस्ति |सम ब्रह्मणा देवहितं यद अस्ति सं देवानां सुमत्यां यज्ञियानाम् ||
sam indra ṇo manasā neṣi gobhiḥ saṁ sūribhir harivaḥ saṁ svasti | sam brahmaṇā devahitaṁ yad asti saṁ devānāṁ sumatyā yajñiyānām ||
  • Translation: "May Indra, with his mind, lead us together with the cows, along with the strong ones, towards prosperity. May the divine blessings that exist through Brahman be with us, and may we have good thoughts from the gods for our sacrifices."
  • Word-by-Word Explanation:
    • sam: together, in harmony
    • indra: the king of gods, representing strength and power
    • ṇo: our
    • manasā: with the mind, intellect
    • neṣi: lead, guide
    • gobhiḥ: with the cows, symbolizing wealth and nourishment
    • saṁ: together, in unity
    • sūribhir: with the strong ones, representing strength and valor
    • harivaḥ: towards prosperity, abundance
    • svasti: well-being, auspiciousness
    • brahmaṇā: through Brahman, the ultimate reality
    • devahitaṁ: divine blessings, favor of the gods
    • yad: that which
    • asti: exists, is present
    • devānāṁ: of the gods
    • sumatyā: good thoughts, wisdom
    • yajñiyānām: for our sacrifices, rituals
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community gathering, when people come together to celebrate a festival, they embody the essence of "sam" by uniting in joy and purpose.
    • In a corporate setting, a leader guiding their team towards a common goal reflects the idea of Indra leading with intellect and strength.
    • When families come together to share a meal, they symbolize the nourishment and wealth represented by "gobhiḥ."
    • In personal development, seeking divine blessings through meditation or prayer can be seen as striving for "devahitaṁ" in our lives.
  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of unity, guidance, and divine blessings in achieving prosperity and well-being. It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal growth to community bonding, reminding us that collective strength and good intentions lead to a harmonious and prosperous life. By invoking the blessings of the divine and striving for unity, we can navigate challenges and foster a supportive environment in our personal and communal lives.
