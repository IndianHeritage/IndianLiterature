अग्नि मृळ महाम् आसी य इम आ देवयुं जनम् |इयेत भर्हिर आसदम् ||
agne mṛḻa mahām̐ asi ya īm ā devayuṁ janam | iyetha barhir āsadam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • Agne: O Agni (Fire), representing the divine energy and the sacred fire.
    • Mṛḻa: May you be pleased or may you be propitiated.
    • Mahām̐: Great or mighty.
    • Asi: You are.
    • Ya: Who.
    • Īm: May we attain or may we reach.
    • Ā: To or towards.
    • Devayuṁ: Divine or godly.
    • Janam: People or beings.
    • Iyetha: May you come or may you arrive.
    • Barhir: On the altar or on the seat.
    • Āsadam: To sit or to dwell.
  • Overall Meaning: This mantra is a prayer to Agni, the fire god, asking for his blessings and presence among the people. It emphasizes the importance of divine energy in our lives and the need for spiritual guidance and support.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In Hindu rituals, Agni is often invoked during ceremonies such as weddings and yajnas (sacrificial offerings) to bless the participants and sanctify the event.
    • In daily life, the concept of Agni can be seen in the importance of maintaining a positive and energetic environment, akin to the warmth and light provided by fire.
    • In a metaphorical sense, Agni represents the inner fire or passion that drives individuals to pursue their goals and aspirations, highlighting the significance of motivation and determination in achieving success.
  • Conclusion: The mantra signifies the importance of divine energy and guidance in our lives. It serves as a reminder to seek blessings from higher powers and to cultivate a positive and energetic environment. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied by engaging in spiritual practices, fostering a supportive community, and nurturing one's inner drive to achieve personal and collective goals.
