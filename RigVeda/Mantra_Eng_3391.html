वामं-वामं त आदुरे देवो ददात्व अर्यमाः |वामं पूषा वामं भगो वामं देवः करूषती ||
vāmaṁ-vāmaṁ ta ādure devo dadātv aryamā | vāmam pūṣā vāmam bhago vāmaṁ devaḥ karūḻatī ||
  • vāmaṁ-vāmaṁ: This phrase can be interpreted as "the auspicious" or "the good." It emphasizes the importance of positivity and well-being in life.
  • ta ādure: This translates to "may he be far away." It suggests a desire for distance from negativity or harmful influences.
  • devo dadātv aryamā: Here, "devo" means "the divine" or "God," and "dadātv" means "may grant." "Aryamā" refers to a deity associated with the sun and prosperity. This part of the mantra expresses a prayer for divine blessings and guidance.
  • vāmam pūṣā: "Pūṣā" is a deity associated with nourishment and growth. This phrase signifies the importance of sustenance and support in our lives.
  • vāmam bhago: "Bhago" refers to wealth or prosperity. This part emphasizes the desire for abundance and success.
  • vāmaṁ devaḥ karūḻatī: This translates to "may the divine bestow blessings." It is a call for divine intervention and support in achieving one's goals.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a prayer for positivity, divine blessings, nourishment, and prosperity. In real life, it can be applied in various scenarios such as during challenging times when one seeks strength and support, or when embarking on new ventures that require guidance and abundance. By reciting this mantra, individuals can cultivate a mindset focused on positivity and attract favorable outcomes in their personal and professional lives.
