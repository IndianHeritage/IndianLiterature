अग्नि शर्धन्तम आगणं पीष्टं रुक्मेभिर अञ्जिभिः |विशो अद्य मरुताम अव ह्वये दिवश्चिद रोचनाद अधि ||
agne śardhantam ā gaṇam piṣṭaṁ rukmebhir añjibhiḥ | viśo adya marutām ava hvaye divaś cid rocanād adhi ||
  • Agne: This word refers to Agni, the fire god in Vedic literature, symbolizing energy, light, and purity.
  • Śardhantam: This term can be interpreted as "to be invoked" or "to be called upon," indicating a plea for divine assistance.
  • Ā gaṇam: This means "to come together" or "to assemble," suggesting unity and collective strength.
  • Piṣṭaṁ: This word translates to "ground" or "crushed," often referring to the preparation of offerings or the essence of something refined.
  • Rukmebhir: This term means "with golden" or "with brilliance," symbolizing wealth, prosperity, and auspiciousness.
  • Añjibhiḥ: This refers to "with offerings" or "with oblations," indicating the act of giving and devotion.
  • Viśo: This word means "of the community" or "of the people," emphasizing the importance of collective well-being.
  • Adya: This translates to "today," signifying immediacy and relevance in the present moment.
  • Marutām: This refers to the Maruts, the storm deities, representing power, strength, and the forces of nature.
  • Ava hvaye: This means "I invoke" or "I call upon," indicating a desire to connect with the divine.
  • Divaś: This translates to "of the heavens," suggesting a connection to the divine or celestial realms.
  • Cid: This word means "consciousness" or "awareness," highlighting the importance of mindfulness and presence.
  • Rocanād: This term means "from the light" or "from the brilliance," indicating the source of illumination and knowledge.
  • Adhi: This means "above" or "over," suggesting a higher perspective or divine guidance.

Conclusion: The mantra invokes Agni, the fire god, and calls upon the collective strength of the community, emphasizing the importance of unity, offerings, and divine connection. In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios such as community gatherings, rituals, or ceremonies where collective energy and intention are needed. It serves as a reminder of the significance of coming together for a common purpose, invoking divine blessings, and fostering a sense of awareness and mindfulness in our actions. The mantra encourages individuals to seek guidance from higher powers while contributing positively to their communities, thus promoting harmony and prosperity.
