आच्याः जानु दक्षिणतो निषद्येमं यज्ञं अभि गृणीत विश्वे |मा हिंसिष्ट पितरः केन चित् नो यद् वा आगः पुरुषता कराम ||
ācyā jānu dakṣiṇato niṣadyemaṁ yajñam abhi gṛṇīta viśve | mā hiṁsiṣṭa pitaraḥ kena cin no yad va āgaḥ puruṣatā karāma ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ācyā: O, the one who is revered
    • jānu: knee
    • dakṣiṇato: from the south
    • niṣadyemaṁ: we sit here
    • yajñam: the sacrifice or offering
    • abhi: towards
    • gṛṇīta: may we sing or chant
    • viśve: all
    • mā: do not
    • hiṁsiṣṭa: harm
    • pitaraḥ: ancestors or forefathers
    • kena: by whom
    • cin: at all
    • no: us
    • yad: whatever
    • va: or
    • āgaḥ: comes
    • puruṣatā: humanity or human nature
    • karāma: we perform
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes a sense of reverence and respect towards the divine and the ancestors.
    • It emphasizes the importance of performing rituals and sacrifices with a pure heart and intention.
    • The phrase "do not harm our ancestors" suggests a deep connection to lineage and the respect owed to those who came before us.
    • It also highlights the collective nature of human existence, where the actions of one can affect the whole community.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In many cultures, rituals are performed to honor ancestors, such as the Day of the Dead in Mexico, where families remember and celebrate their deceased loved ones.
    • In modern society, the concept of sustainability can be seen as a way to honor future generations by taking care of the environment today.
    • Community service and charitable acts can be viewed as a way to contribute positively to society, reflecting the mantra's call for collective well-being.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of respect, community, and the interconnectedness of life.
    • It serves as a reminder to perform our duties with integrity and to honor those who have paved the way for us.
    • In real life, it encourages us to act with compassion and mindfulness, ensuring that our actions contribute positively to our families, communities, and the world at large.
