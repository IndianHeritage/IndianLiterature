बोधिन्मनसाऽ रथ्येषिरा हवनश्रुता | विभिश्च्यवानमश्विनानियाथो अद्वयाविनं माध्वी मम श्रुतं हवम् ||
bodhinmanasā rathyeṣirā havanaśrutā | vibhiś cyavānam aśvinā ni yātho advayāvinam mādhvī mama śrutaṁ havam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • bodhinmanasā: With a mind that is enlightened or awakened.
    • rathyeṣirā: The charioteer of the path or journey.
    • havanaśrutā: The one who is invoked or called upon during the sacrificial offering.
    • vibhiś: With great power or strength.
    • cyavānam: The one who is moving or wandering.
    • aśvinā: The divine twin horsemen, often associated with health and medicine.
    • ni yātho: Do not stray or deviate.
    • advayāvinam: The one who is unique or unparalleled.
    • mādhvī: Sweet or pleasant.
    • mama: My.
    • śrutaṁ: The knowledge or wisdom that is heard or learned.
    • havam: The offering or sacrifice.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of an enlightened mind in guiding one's journey through life.
    • The reference to the Aśvins, divine twins, symbolizes health, vitality, and the duality of existence.
    • The plea to not stray from the path signifies the need for focus and determination in achieving one's goals.
    • The sweetness of the offering suggests that the outcomes of our actions should be pleasant and beneficial.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, an enlightened mind can lead to better decision-making, much like a skilled charioteer navigating a complex path.
    • In personal relationships, invoking the qualities of the Aśvins can lead to healthier interactions and mutual support.
    • Staying focused on one's goals, akin to not straying from the path, can be seen in athletes who train rigorously to achieve their dreams.
    • The sweetness of our actions can be reflected in community service, where the outcomes benefit not just the individual but society as a whole.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the significance of an enlightened mindset in navigating life's challenges.
    • It encourages individuals to seek health, vitality, and focus while ensuring that their actions lead to positive outcomes.
    • In essence, it highlights the interconnectedness of knowledge, health, and purposeful living, applicable in various aspects of life, from personal growth to community engagement.
