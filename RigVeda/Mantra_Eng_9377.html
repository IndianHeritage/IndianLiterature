यत ते मरīचिः प्रवतो मनो जगाम दूरकम् |तत त ā वर्तयामसिह क्षयाय जीवसे ||
yat te marīcīḥ pravato mano jagāma dūrakam | tat ta ā vartayāmasīha kṣayāya jīvase ||
  • yat te marīcīḥ: This phrase can be translated as "that which is your light" or "that which is your radiance." In a broader sense, it refers to the divine light or knowledge that guides individuals in their lives.
  • pravato mano jagāma dūrakam: This part means "the mind that travels far." It signifies the journey of the mind, suggesting that through contemplation and meditation, one can reach distant realms of understanding and consciousness.
  • tat ta ā vartayāmasīha: This translates to "that we may attain." It expresses a desire to reach or achieve something significant, often related to spiritual or personal growth.
  • kṣayāya jīvase: This phrase means "for the sake of life" or "for the preservation of life." It emphasizes the importance of sustaining life and the pursuit of knowledge and enlightenment for the betterment of existence.

  • Real-Life Example: Consider a student who is striving for academic excellence. The "light" in this context could represent knowledge and wisdom. As the student dedicates time to study (pravato mano jagāma dūrakam), they expand their understanding and reach new heights in their education (tat ta ā vartayāmasīha). This pursuit is not just for grades but for the enrichment of their life and future (kṣayāya jīvase).
  • Significance: This mantra highlights the importance of seeking knowledge and enlightenment as a means to enhance life. It encourages individuals to look beyond the immediate and strive for deeper understanding and wisdom.
  • Application in Real Life: In various aspects of life, such as personal development, career growth, or spiritual journeys, this mantra serves as a reminder to pursue knowledge and enlightenment. It can be applied in meditation practices, educational endeavors, or even in professional settings where continuous learning is essential.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking light and knowledge as a pathway to a fulfilling life. It encourages individuals to engage their minds in the pursuit of wisdom, ultimately leading to a richer and more meaningful existence. By applying the principles of this mantra, one can navigate life's challenges with greater clarity and purpose.
