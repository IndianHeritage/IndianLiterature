व्य अञ्जते दिवो अन्तेष्व् अक्तून् विश्वो न युक्ता उषसो यतन्ते |सं ते गावस् तम आ वर्तयन्ति ज्योतिर यच्छन्ति सवितेव बाहू ||
vy añjate divo anteṣv aktūn viśo na yuktā uṣaso yatante | saṁ te gāvas tama ā vartayanti jyotir yacchanti saviteva bāhū ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • vy añjate: They are connected or joined.
    • divo anteṣv: In the realms of the sky.
    • aktūn: The powers or energies.
    • viśo: The community or the people.
    • na yuktā: Not united or not in harmony.
    • uṣaso: The dawn or the new beginning.
    • yatante: They strive or they make efforts.
    • saṁ: Together or in unity.
    • te gāvas: Those who are like cows (symbolizing nourishment and abundance).
    • tama: Darkness or ignorance.
    • ā vartayanti: They turn away or dispel.
    • jyotir: Light or illumination.
    • yacchanti: They give or bestow.
    • saviteva: Like the sun (Savita being a form of the sun).
    • bāhū: Arms or strength.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the interconnectedness of the community and the divine forces in the universe.
    • It highlights the importance of unity among people, especially in striving towards a common goal.
    • The metaphor of cows represents nourishment, suggesting that when people come together, they can create abundance and prosperity.
    • The reference to darkness and light symbolizes the journey from ignorance to knowledge, emphasizing the need for enlightenment.
    • Just as the sun dispels darkness with its rays, collective efforts can illuminate the path towards progress and understanding.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace, when team members collaborate effectively, they can achieve greater results than when working in isolation.
    • Community initiatives, such as local clean-up drives, demonstrate how collective action can lead to a healthier environment.
    • In education, group studies often lead to better understanding and retention of knowledge among students.
    • Social movements, like those advocating for climate change, show how united efforts can bring about significant change in society.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the power of unity and collective effort in overcoming challenges and achieving goals.
    • It encourages individuals to work together, fostering a sense of community and shared purpose.
    • In real life, applying the principles of this mantra can lead to personal growth, societal progress, and a more harmonious existence.
    • Ultimately, it teaches that through collaboration and striving for enlightenment, we can dispel ignorance and create a brighter future for all.
