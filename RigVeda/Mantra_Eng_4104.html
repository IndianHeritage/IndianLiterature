तं वः शर्धं रथानां त्वेषं गणं मारुतं नव्यसीनाम् |अनु प्र यन्ति वृष्टयः ||
taṁ vaḥ śardhaṁ rathānāṁ tveṣaṁ gaṇam mārutaṁ navyasīnām | anu pra yanti vṛṣṭayaḥ ||
  • taṁ vaḥ: This phrase translates to "that for you" or "that which is for you." It indicates a sense of direction or purpose, suggesting that there is something significant being referred to.
  • śardhaṁ: This word means "together" or "in unity." It emphasizes the importance of collective effort and harmony among individuals.
  • rathānāṁ: This translates to "of the chariots." In the context of the mantra, it symbolizes movement, progress, and the journey of life.
  • tveṣaṁ: This means "your" or "of you," indicating possession or connection to the subject being discussed.
  • gaṇam: This word means "group" or "assembly." It signifies the importance of community and collaboration.
  • mārutaṁ: This refers to "the winds" or "the forces of nature." It symbolizes the dynamic and ever-changing aspects of life.
  • navyasīnām: This translates to "new" or "fresh." It suggests innovation, renewal, and the potential for new beginnings.
  • anu pra yanti: This phrase means "they go forth" or "they proceed." It indicates movement towards a goal or destination.
  • vṛṣṭayaḥ: This word means "rains" or "showers." It symbolizes nourishment, abundance, and the blessings of nature.

The mantra can be interpreted as a call for unity and collective effort in the pursuit of progress and renewal. It emphasizes the importance of working together as a community, harnessing the forces of nature, and moving forward towards a common goal. The mention of "rains" signifies the blessings that come from this collective effort, suggesting that when individuals unite, they can achieve abundance and prosperity.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as:

  • Community Projects: When a community comes together to work on a project, such as building a school or organizing a festival, the collective effort can lead to successful outcomes and a sense of shared accomplishment.
  • Teamwork in Organizations: In a workplace, when team members collaborate effectively, they can innovate and achieve goals that would be difficult to reach individually. This reflects the idea of harnessing the "winds" of creativity and effort.
  • Environmental Initiatives: The mantra's reference to nature can inspire collective action towards environmental sustainability, where communities work together to protect natural resources and promote ecological balance.

In conclusion, this mantra serves as a reminder of the power of unity and collaboration. It highlights that when individuals come together with a shared purpose, they can navigate the challenges of life, harness the blessings of nature, and create a prosperous future. Its significance lies in its applicability to various aspects of life, encouraging us to work together for the greater good.
