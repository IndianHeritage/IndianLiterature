अविप्रे चिद वयो दधद अनाशुना चिद अरवतā |इन्द्रो जेता हितं धनम् ||
avipre cid vayo dadhad anāśunā cid arvatā | indro jetā hitaṁ dhanam ||
  • Mantra: "avipre cid vayo dadhad anāśunā cid arvatā | indro jetā hitaṁ dhanam ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • avipre: without hindrance or obstruction
    • cid: indeed or certainly
    • vayo: of the wind or life force
    • dadhad: holding or maintaining
    • anāśunā: without decay or deterioration
    • cid: indeed or certainly
    • arvatā: of the strong or powerful
    • indro: Indra, the king of gods
    • jetā: victorious or conqueror
    • hitaṁ: beneficial or auspicious
    • dhanam: wealth or riches
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the unobstructed flow of life force, akin to the wind, which is essential for vitality and growth.
    • The phrase "holding without decay" suggests the importance of maintaining one's strength and resources over time, much like how a tree must be nurtured to grow strong and resilient.
    • Indra, as the king of gods, symbolizes power and authority, and the mantra invokes his blessings for victory and prosperity.
    • The idea of "beneficial wealth" emphasizes that true riches are not just material but also encompass health, happiness, and spiritual fulfillment.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be applied to the importance of sustainable practices. Just as the wind sustains life, businesses should operate in a way that supports the environment and community, ensuring long-term success.
    • In personal life, maintaining one's health and well-being can be seen as holding onto one's life force. Regular exercise, a balanced diet, and mental wellness practices are ways to embody this principle.
    • In relationships, the mantra encourages nurturing connections without allowing them to decay. This can be achieved through open communication, trust, and mutual support.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of maintaining strength and vitality in various aspects of life, whether it be personal health, business practices, or relationships.
    • Its application in real life serves as a reminder to seek balance and sustainability, ensuring that our actions lead to beneficial outcomes for ourselves and those around us.
    • Ultimately, invoking the blessings of Indra for victory and prosperity encourages us to strive for success that is not only material but also enriching to our lives and the lives of others.
