स्वादवः सोमाऽयाहि श्रीताः सोमाऽयाहि |शिप्रिन्न ऋषिवः शचिवो नायमच्छा सदहमादम् ||
svādavaḥ somā ā yāhi śrītāḥ somā ā yāhi | śiprinn ṛṣīvaḥ śacīvo nāyam acchā sadhamādam ||
  • svādavaḥ somā ā yāhi: This phrase can be translated as "O Soma, who is sweet, come here." In the Vedic context, Soma is both a deity and a sacred drink that is believed to confer immortality and divine inspiration. The sweetness of Soma symbolizes the pleasant and beneficial aspects of life.
  • śrītāḥ somā ā yāhi: This translates to "O Soma, who is well-prepared, come here." This emphasizes the importance of preparation and the right conditions for achieving success and prosperity. Just as Soma must be prepared correctly to be effective, so too must our efforts in life be well-planned and executed.
  • śiprinn ṛṣīvaḥ: This can be interpreted as "like the swift sages." Here, the term "sages" refers to wise individuals who possess deep knowledge and understanding. The phrase suggests that one should aspire to be as swift and insightful as these sages in their pursuits.
  • śacīvo nāyam acchā: This means "may we not be deprived of strength." It highlights the importance of strength, both physical and mental, in overcoming challenges. In real life, this can relate to the resilience needed to face adversities, whether in personal or professional contexts.
  • sadhamādam: This term can be understood as "the best of pleasures." It signifies the ultimate joy and fulfillment that one seeks in life. This can be related to achieving one's goals, finding happiness in relationships, or experiencing contentment in one's endeavors.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking divine blessings for sweetness, preparation, wisdom, strength, and ultimate joy in life. In real-life scenarios, it serves as a reminder to approach our goals with the right mindset, preparation, and resilience. Whether in personal growth, professional achievements, or spiritual pursuits, invoking these qualities can lead to a more fulfilling and successful life. The significance of this mantra lies in its holistic approach to life, encouraging individuals to strive for balance and harmony in all aspects of their existence.
