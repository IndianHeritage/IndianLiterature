ये ते पन्थाः सवितः पूर्व्यासो 'रेणवः सुक्रता अन्तरिक्षे |तेभिर नो अद्य पथिभिः सुगेभी रक्षां च नो अधि च ब्रूहि देव ||
ye te panthāḥ savitaḥ pūrvyāso 'reṇavaḥ sukṛtā antarikṣe | tebhir no adya pathibhiḥ sugebhī rakṣā ca no adhi ca brūhi deva ||
  • Mantra: "ye te panthāḥ savitaḥ pūrvyāso 'reṇavaḥ sukṛtā antarikṣe | tebhir no adya pathibhiḥ sugebhī rakṣā ca no adhi ca brūhi deva ||"
  • Translation: "O Savitar, the divine light, those paths that are ancient and full of merit, may they protect us today, and may you speak to us about the paths that are auspicious."

  • Word-by-Word Explanation:
    • ye te: "those who" - Referring to the paths or ways that are being addressed.
    • panthāḥ: "paths" - Symbolizing the various routes or choices in life.
    • savitaḥ: "of Savitar" - Savitar is the Sun God, representing light, energy, and enlightenment.
    • pūrvyāso: "ancient" - Indicating the timeless wisdom and traditions that guide us.
    • 'reṇavaḥ: "full of merit" - Suggesting that these paths are virtuous and beneficial.
    • sukṛtā: "good deeds" - Emphasizing the importance of righteous actions.
    • antarikṣe: "in the sky" - Referring to the celestial realm, symbolizing higher consciousness.
    • tebhir: "by them" - Indicating the paths that lead to safety and well-being.
    • no: "us" - A plea for protection and guidance.
    • adya: "today" - Highlighting the immediacy of the request.
    • pathibhiḥ: "by the paths" - Reinforcing the idea of guidance through various choices.
    • sugebhī: "auspicious" - Signifying paths that lead to success and happiness.
    • rakṣā: "protection" - A request for safety and security.
    • ca: "and" - Connecting the ideas of protection and guidance.
    • no: "us" - Again, a plea for assistance.
    • adhi: "above" - Suggesting a higher level of protection or guidance.
    • brūhi: "speak" - A call for divine communication and wisdom.
    • deva: "O God" - Addressing the divine presence, seeking help and guidance.

  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In decision-making, individuals often seek guidance from mentors or spiritual leaders, akin to asking for the "auspicious paths" in life.
    • In times of uncertainty, people may turn to prayer or meditation, invoking divine protection and clarity, similar to the essence of this mantra.
    • In a professional context, teams may rely on established best practices (the "ancient paths") to ensure successful project outcomes.

  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking guidance and protection from divine forces in our daily lives.
    • It highlights the value of ancient wisdom and virtuous actions as pathways to success and safety.
    • In real-life scenarios, it serves as a reminder to align our choices with ethical principles and to seek support from higher powers during challenging times.
