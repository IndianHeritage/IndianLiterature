उत नो देवाव अश्विनाऽ शुभस पती धामभिर मित्रावरुणा उरुष्यताम् |महः स राया एषतेऽति धन्वेव दुरिता ||
uta no devāv aśvinā śubhas patī dhāmabhir mitrāvaruṇā uruṣyatām | mahaḥ sa rāya eṣate 'ti dhanveva duritā ||
  • Translation: "May the divine Aśvins, the auspicious lords, come to us with their bountiful blessings. May they, along with Mitra and Varuna, bestow upon us great wealth and remove our obstacles, like a powerful bow dispels darkness."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • uta: May (a call for blessings)
    • no: us (the speaker and the community)
    • devāv: divine beings (referring to the Aśvins)
    • aśvinā: Aśvins (the twin gods of medicine and health)
    • śubhas: auspicious (bringing good fortune)
    • patī: lords (rulers or protectors)
    • dhāmabhir: with their bountiful blessings (prosperity)
    • mitrāvaruṇā: Mitra and Varuna (gods of friendship and cosmic order)
    • uruṣyatām: may they bestow (a request for generosity)
    • mahaḥ: great (signifying abundance)
    • sa: that (indicating connection)
    • rāya: wealth (material and spiritual prosperity)
    • eṣate: may it come (a hopeful invocation)
    • 'ti: like (comparison)
    • dhanveva: like a bow (symbol of strength)
    • duritā: obstacles (challenges or difficulties)
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In times of difficulty, individuals often seek blessings from divine entities, similar to how this mantra invokes the Aśvins for support. For instance, during a challenging medical situation, people may pray for healing and guidance.
    • Communities come together to celebrate festivals, invoking the blessings of deities for prosperity and well-being, reflecting the collective spirit of seeking divine favor as expressed in this mantra.
    • In business, entrepreneurs often seek blessings for success and the removal of obstacles, akin to the invocation of wealth and prosperity in this mantra.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine intervention in overcoming life's challenges and achieving prosperity. It emphasizes the importance of community, health, and wealth, making it applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal struggles to collective celebrations. By invoking the blessings of divine beings, individuals and communities can find strength and support in their endeavors, fostering a sense of hope and resilience in the face of adversity.
