स व्राधतो नहुषो दṁसुजूतः शर्धस्तरो नरां गूर्तश्रवाः |विसृष्टरातिर याती बालयसृत्वा विश्वासु पृत्सु सदमिच्छूरः ||
sa vrādhato nahuṣo daṁsujūtaḥ śardhastaro narāṁ gūrtaśravāḥ | visṛṣṭarātir yāti bāḻhasṛtvā viśvāsu pṛtsu sadam ic chūraḥ ||
  • sa vrādhato nahuṣo: This phrase can be interpreted as "he who is endowed with strength and power." In real life, this can refer to individuals who possess leadership qualities, such as a successful entrepreneur or a community leader who inspires others through their actions.
  • daṁsujūtaḥ: This term means "one who is adorned with the qualities of wisdom and knowledge." For example, a teacher or mentor who imparts knowledge and guides others can be seen as embodying this quality.
  • śardhastaro narāṁ: This translates to "the one who brings together people." In a modern context, this could refer to a social activist or a team leader who fosters collaboration and unity among diverse groups of people.
  • gūrtaśravāḥ: This means "one who is renowned for their deeds." A real-life example could be a philanthropist whose charitable actions have earned them respect and admiration in society.
  • visṛṣṭarātir: This phrase can be understood as "the one who has the ability to overcome obstacles." In everyday life, this could refer to an athlete who perseveres through challenges to achieve their goals, demonstrating resilience and determination.
  • yāti bāḻhasṛtvā: This means "one who moves forward with strength." This can be likened to a person who, despite facing setbacks, continues to strive for success, such as a student who works hard to improve their grades after failing an exam.
  • viśvāsu pṛtsu: This translates to "in all aspects of life." It emphasizes the holistic approach to living, where one seeks balance and fulfillment in various areas, such as career, relationships, and personal growth.
  • sadam ic chūraḥ: This phrase means "the one who desires the ultimate truth." In real life, this can refer to individuals who seek knowledge and understanding, such as philosophers or scientists who explore the mysteries of existence.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of strength, wisdom, unity, resilience, and the pursuit of truth. Its significance lies in the encouragement it provides to individuals to embody these qualities in their lives. In a world where challenges and obstacles are prevalent, this mantra serves as a reminder to strive for personal growth, foster collaboration, and seek knowledge. By applying these principles, individuals can contribute positively to their communities and lead fulfilling lives.
