ईशे ह्य अग्निर अमृतस्य भूरेर ईशे रायः सुवीर्यस्य दातोः |मा त्वा वयं सहसावन्न अवीराः माप्सवः परि शदाम माादुवः ||
īśe hy a1gnir amṛtasya bhūrer īśe rāyaḥ suvīryasya dātoḥ | mā tvā vayaṁ sahasāvann avīrā māpsavaḥ pari ṣadāma māduvaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • īśe: To the Lord or the Divine
    • hy: Indeed
    • a1gnir: Fire (Agni)
    • amṛtasya: Of immortality
    • bhūrer: Of the earth
    • īśe: To the Lord
    • rāyaḥ: Wealth or prosperity
    • suvīryasya: Of the strong or valiant
    • dātoḥ: Giver or bestower
    • mā: Not
    • tvā: You
    • vayaṁ: We
    • sahasāvann: With strength or might
    • avīrā: Without fear
    • māpsavaḥ: Waters or the essence of life
    • pari: Around
    • ṣadāma: We shall protect or surround
    • māduvaḥ: Sweet or pleasant
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes Agni, the fire god, symbolizing purity, transformation, and the divine presence in our lives.
    • It emphasizes the importance of seeking the blessings of the divine for prosperity and strength.
    • The phrase "mā tvā vayaṁ sahasāvann avīrā" suggests a plea for protection and courage, indicating that we seek divine support to face challenges without fear.
    • The reference to "māpsavaḥ" highlights the essential nature of water, representing life and sustenance, which we wish to surround ourselves with.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In times of personal or professional challenges, individuals often seek spiritual guidance or perform rituals to invoke divine support, similar to the invocation of Agni in this mantra.
    • In a corporate setting, leaders may emphasize the importance of teamwork and collective strength, akin to the plea for courage and protection in the mantra.
    • During significant life events, such as weddings or housewarming ceremonies, rituals involving fire and water are performed to seek blessings for prosperity and harmony.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of divine support in achieving prosperity, strength, and fearlessness in life.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal challenges to communal gatherings, where individuals seek blessings for success and protection.
    • By invoking the divine, we remind ourselves of the interconnectedness of life, the importance of courage, and the pursuit of a prosperous existence.
