हन्तो नु किम आससे प्रथमं नो रथं कृधि |उपमं वाजयु श्रवः ||
hanto nu kim āsase prathamaṁ no rathaṁ kṛdhi | upamaṁ vājayu śravaḥ ||
  • Mantra: "hanto nu kim āsase prathamaṁ no rathaṁ kṛdhi | upamaṁ vājayu śravaḥ ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • hanto: "O slayer" or "O destroyer"
    • nu: "indeed" or "certainly"
    • kim: "what"
    • āsase: "you desire" or "you wish"
    • prathamaṁ: "first" or "foremost"
    • no: "our"
    • rathaṁ: "chariot" or "vehicle"
    • kṛdhi: "make" or "create"
    • upamaṁ: "comparison" or "simile"
    • vājayu: "of the swift" or "of the strong"
    • śravaḥ: "fame" or "glory"
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra can be seen as a call to action, asking for the creation of a powerful vehicle (ratha) that can lead to success and glory.
    • The phrase "O slayer, what do you desire?" suggests a dialogue with a divine or powerful entity, seeking guidance or assistance in achieving one's goals.
    • The emphasis on "first" indicates the importance of prioritizing one's objectives and taking the necessary steps to achieve them.
    • The mention of "comparison" implies that one should strive to be the best, drawing inspiration from the strong and the swift.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a competitive environment, such as a corporate setting, this mantra can inspire individuals to seek out the best strategies (the "chariot") to achieve their career goals.
    • A student preparing for exams might interpret this as a call to focus on their studies and seek the best resources to ensure their success.
    • A sports team could use this mantra to motivate themselves to train harder and aim for victory, drawing comparisons to successful teams or athletes.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of intention, action, and striving for excellence in all endeavors.
    • Its significance lies in the reminder to seek guidance, prioritize goals, and draw inspiration from those who have achieved greatness.
    • In real life, it serves as a motivational tool, encouraging individuals to take charge of their aspirations and work diligently towards their success.
