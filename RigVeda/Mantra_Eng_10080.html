सचन्त यद उषसः सूर्येण चित्राम अस्य केतवो राम अविन्दन |आ यन् नक्षत्रं ददृशे दिवो न पुनर यतो नकि रद्धा नु वेद ||
sacanta yad uṣasaḥ sūryeṇa citrām asya ketavo rām avindan | ā yan nakṣatraṁ dadṛśe divo na punar yato nakir addhā nu veda ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sacanta: They who are auspicious or beneficial.
    • yad: When.
    • uṣasaḥ: Of the dawn.
    • sūryeṇa: With the sun.
    • citrām: Colorful or brilliant.
    • asya: Of this.
    • ketavo: The stars or celestial bodies.
    • rām: Joy or delight.
    • avindan: They found or discovered.
    • ā: From.
    • yan: That which.
    • nakṣatraṁ: The star or constellation.
    • dadṛśe: They saw or perceived.
    • divo: Of the sky.
    • na: Not.
    • punar: Again.
    • yato: From where.
    • nakir: No one.
    • addhā: Truly or indeed.
    • nu: Now.
    • veda: Knows or understands.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the auspiciousness that comes with the dawn, symbolized by the sun.
    • It highlights the beauty and brilliance of celestial bodies, suggesting that they bring joy and discovery.
    • The phrase "they found" indicates a sense of realization or enlightenment that comes with the arrival of light.
    • It also reflects on the idea that once something is perceived or understood, it cannot be unlearned or forgotten.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, the dawn represents new beginnings. For instance, starting a new job or a new chapter in life can be seen as a 'dawn' of opportunities.
    • The sun's rising can symbolize hope and positivity, much like how people often feel rejuvenated and motivated at the start of a new day.
    • In a broader sense, the discovery of knowledge or truth can be likened to the light of the sun dispelling darkness, leading to enlightenment.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of light, knowledge, and the joy that comes with discovery.
    • It teaches us to appreciate the beauty of new beginnings and the insights that come with them.
    • In real life, it encourages us to embrace change and seek knowledge, as these are the forces that illuminate our paths and lead us to greater understanding.
