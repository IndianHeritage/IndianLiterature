तं वः सखायो मदाया पुनानम अभि गायत |शिशुं न यज्ञैः स्वदयन्त गूर्तिभिः ||
taṁ vaḥ sakhāyo madāya punānam abhi gāyata | śiśuṁ na yajñaiḥ svadayanta gūrtibhiḥ ||
  • taṁ vaḥ sakhāyo: This phrase translates to "those friends of yours." It emphasizes the importance of friendship and companionship in life. In real life, having supportive friends can significantly impact our mental and emotional well-being.
  • madāya: This word means "for joy" or "for happiness." It suggests that the purpose of gathering with friends is to celebrate and share joy. For example, friends coming together for a celebration or a festival enhances the sense of community and happiness.
  • punānam abhi gāyata: This translates to "singing praises again and again." It highlights the significance of expressing gratitude and appreciation. In real life, regularly acknowledging the contributions of friends and loved ones fosters stronger relationships.
  • śiśuṁ: This word means "child" or "young one." It signifies innocence and the future generation. In a practical sense, nurturing children and providing them with a supportive environment is crucial for their development.
  • na yajñaiḥ svadayanta: This phrase means "not by sacrifices do they nourish." It suggests that true nourishment comes from love and companionship rather than material offerings. In modern life, emotional support and care are often more valuable than material gifts.
  • gūrtibhiḥ: This word refers to "with wisdom" or "with understanding." It implies that relationships should be built on mutual respect and understanding. In real life, wise communication and empathy are essential for maintaining healthy relationships.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of friendship, joy, and emotional nourishment over material sacrifices. It teaches us that true happiness comes from our relationships and the love we share with others. In real life, this can be applied by fostering strong bonds with friends and family, nurturing the younger generation, and prioritizing emotional connections over materialistic pursuits. By doing so, we create a supportive community that thrives on mutual respect and understanding.
