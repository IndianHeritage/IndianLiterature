जिह्मश्ये चरतवे मघोन्याभोगय इष्टये राया उ त्वम् |दभ्रम पश्यद्भ्य उर्वियā विचक्षु उषा अजीगर भुवनानि विश्वा ||
jihmaśye3 caritave maghony ābhogaya iṣṭaye rāya u tvam | dabhram paśyadbhya urviyā vicakṣa uṣā ajīgar bhuvanāni viśvā ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • jihmaśye: of the one who is clever or skillful
    • caritave: in actions or deeds
    • maghony: of the wealthy or prosperous
    • ābhogaya: to enjoy or to experience
    • iṣṭaye: for the purpose of worship or offerings
    • rāya: wealth or prosperity
    • u: and
    • tvam: you
    • dabhram: the one who is strong or powerful
    • paśyadbhya: to those who see or perceive
    • urviyā: with great expanse or vastness
    • vicakṣa: wise or knowledgeable
    • uṣā: dawn or new beginnings
    • ajīgar: to encompass or embrace
    • bhuvanāni: the worlds or realms
    • viśvā: all or everything
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks to the qualities of cleverness and skill in one's actions, emphasizing the importance of being wise and perceptive in the pursuit of prosperity.
    • The mention of "wealth" and "prosperity" suggests that the mantra is a call for blessings in material and spiritual abundance.
    • The reference to "dawn" symbolizes new beginnings, indicating that every day brings opportunities for growth and success.
    • The phrase "to those who see" implies that wisdom and perception are crucial in recognizing and seizing opportunities.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • A business leader who navigates challenges with clever strategies exemplifies the essence of "jihmaśye caritave." Their ability to adapt and innovate leads to prosperity for their company.
    • In personal finance, individuals who are knowledgeable about investments and market trends can make informed decisions that enhance their wealth, reflecting the wisdom of "vicakṣa."
    • Starting a new venture at the beginning of a year or season can be likened to the "uṣā" or dawn, representing fresh opportunities and the potential for success.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of wisdom, skill, and perception in achieving prosperity and success in life.
    • It encourages individuals to embrace new beginnings and to be aware of the opportunities that surround them.
    • In a broader sense, it highlights the interconnectedness of all realms and the potential for collective growth and abundance.
