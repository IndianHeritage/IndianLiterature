बाधसे जनान वृषभेव मन्युनā घृषौ मीळ्ह ऋचिषम |अस्माकं बोध्य अवितā महाधने तनूष्व अप्सु सूर्ये ||
bādhase janān vṛṣabheva manyunā ghṛṣau mīḻha ṛcīṣama | asmākam bodhy avitā mahādhane tanūṣv apsu sūrye ||
  • Translation: "You are troubled by the people like a bull by anger; may you be freed from this, and may we be endowed with great wealth, with our bodies in the waters of the sun."
  • Word-by-Word Explanation:
    • bādhase: You are troubled or afflicted.
    • janān: By people or beings.
    • vṛṣabheva: Like a bull; symbolizes strength and power.
    • manyunā: By anger or wrath.
    • ghṛṣau: May you be freed or liberated.
    • mīḻha: May we be endowed or blessed.
    • ṛcīṣama: With great wealth or prosperity.
    • asmākam: Our or ours.
    • bodhy: To be aware or enlightened.
    • avitā: May it protect or preserve.
    • mahādhane: In great wealth or abundance.
    • tanūṣv: With our bodies or forms.
    • apsu: In the waters.
    • sūrye: Of the sun.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace, individuals may feel troubled by colleagues or competition, akin to the bull's struggle against anger. This mantra can serve as a reminder to seek peace and resolution rather than conflict.
    • In personal relationships, when misunderstandings arise, one can reflect on the need to be freed from anger and to focus on building wealth in terms of emotional connections and understanding.
    • In financial endeavors, the aspiration for great wealth can be pursued with the understanding that one must remain aware and enlightened, ensuring that actions are taken with integrity and purpose.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for liberation from anger and conflict, promoting a path towards enlightenment and prosperity. It emphasizes the importance of maintaining peace in relationships and striving for abundance in all aspects of life. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied to personal growth, conflict resolution, and the pursuit of wealth, both materially and spiritually. By embodying the essence of this mantra, individuals can navigate challenges with strength and wisdom, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling existence.
