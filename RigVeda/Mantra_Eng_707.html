अस्मा इद उ त्यद अनु दाय्य एषाम एको यद वाव्ने भूरेर ईशानः |प्रैतशं सूर्ये पस्पृधानाṁ सauvश्व्ये सुष्विम आवद इन्द्रः ||
asmā id u tyad anu dāyy eṣām eko yad vavne bhūrer īśānaḥ | praitaśaṁ sūrye paspṛdhānaṁ sauvaśvye suṣvim āvad indraḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • asmā: from us
    • id: this
    • u: indeed
    • tyad: that
    • anu: after
    • dāyy: to give
    • eṣām: of these
    • eko: one
    • yad: who
    • vavne: to be praised
    • bhūrer: of the earth
    • īśānaḥ: the lord
    • praitaśaṁ: from all sides
    • sūrye: in the sun
    • paspṛdhānaṁ: who is spreading
    • sauvaśvye: in the auspicious
    • suṣvim: good
    • āvad: may he speak
    • indraḥ: Indra, the king of gods
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the divine presence, asking for blessings and guidance.
    • It emphasizes the unity and oneness of existence, suggesting that despite the diversity of life, there is a singular divine essence that connects all.
    • The reference to Indra signifies strength, power, and the ability to overcome obstacles, which can be seen in real-life scenarios where individuals seek courage and support in challenging times.
    • The mention of the sun symbolizes enlightenment, knowledge, and the life-giving force, reminding us of the importance of wisdom and clarity in our lives.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In moments of uncertainty, individuals often turn to spiritual practices or mantras for strength and guidance, similar to how this mantra seeks the blessings of Indra.
    • Communities coming together for a common cause reflect the idea of unity and collective strength, akin to the oneness expressed in the mantra.
    • In educational settings, the pursuit of knowledge and enlightenment can be likened to the sun's role in spreading wisdom, as mentioned in the mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of all beings and the divine support available to us.
    • Its significance lies in its ability to inspire individuals to seek strength, unity, and enlightenment in their lives.
    • In practical terms, it encourages us to embrace challenges with courage and to recognize the divine presence in our everyday experiences.
