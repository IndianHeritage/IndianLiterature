यो वृत्राय सिन् अत्राभरिष्यत प्र तं जनित्री विदुषा उवाच |पथो रदन्तीर अनु जोषम् अस्मै दिवे-दिवे धुनयो यन्त्य अर्थम् ||
yo vṛtrāya sinam atrābhariṣyat pra taṁ janitrī viduṣa uvāca | patho radantīr anu joṣam asmai dive-dive dhunayo yanty artham ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yo: who
    • vṛtrāya: to the one who is a foe or obstacle
    • sinam: the one who is to be overcome
    • atrābhariṣyat: here, may be invoked or called upon
    • pra: for
    • taṁ: that
    • janitrī: the mother or source
    • viduṣa: of the wise or knowledgeable
    • uvāca: spoke or said
    • patho: of the paths
    • radantīr: those that are radiant or shining
    • anu: following
    • joṣam: the joy or delight
    • asmai: to him
    • dive-dive: day by day
    • dhunayo: the ones who are moving or flowing
    • yanty: go or lead
    • artham: towards the purpose or meaning
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of invoking a higher power or wisdom to overcome obstacles in life.
    • The term "vṛtrāya" symbolizes challenges or adversities that one faces, akin to personal struggles such as job loss or health issues.
    • The phrase "janitrī viduṣa" suggests seeking guidance from a nurturing source, which can be interpreted as family, mentors, or spiritual leaders.
    • "Patho radantīr" refers to the radiant paths of life that one can follow, emphasizing the importance of choosing the right direction.
    • The mantra encourages a daily pursuit of joy and purpose, reminding us that each day presents new opportunities to align with our goals.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • When facing a difficult decision, one might reflect on this mantra to seek clarity and guidance from trusted advisors.
    • In moments of despair, individuals can find solace in the idea that obstacles can be overcome with wisdom and perseverance.
    • Professionals may use this mantra as a reminder to stay focused on their goals and to embrace the journey, regardless of the challenges encountered.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the importance of seeking wisdom and guidance in overcoming life's challenges.
    • It emphasizes the significance of nurturing relationships and the pursuit of joy and purpose in our daily lives.
    • By applying the teachings of this mantra, individuals can cultivate resilience and a positive outlook, ultimately leading to personal growth and fulfillment.
