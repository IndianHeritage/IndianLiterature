मन्त्र: एते शमीभिः सुशमी अभूवन ये हिन्विरे तन्वः सोम उक्थैः | नृवद वदन्न उप नो माहि वाजान दिवि श्रवो दधिषे नाम वीरः ||
ete śamībhiḥ suśamī abhūvan ye hinvire tanva1ḥ soma ukthaiḥ | nṛvad vadann upa no māhi vājān divi śravo dadhiṣe nāma vīraḥ ||
  • Translation: "These beautiful trees have flourished, those who have been invoked with the Soma hymns. O hero, who speaks like a man, come to us with your bountiful gifts; you who are renowned in the heavens, grant us your name."
  • Word-by-Word Explanation:
    • ete: these
    • śamībhiḥ: with beautiful trees
    • suśamī: flourishing or well-formed
    • abhūvan: have become or have flourished
    • ye: those who
    • hinvire: have been invoked
    • tanvaḥ: with the Soma hymns
    • soma: Soma, a sacred drink
    • ukthaiḥ: with hymns
    • nṛvad: like a man
    • vadann: speaking
    • upa: come to us
    • no: our
    • māhi: with bountiful gifts
    • vājān: of wealth or strength
    • divi: in the heavens
    • śravo: renowned or famous
    • dadhiṣe: grant or bestow
    • nāma: name
    • vīraḥ: O hero
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • The invocation of nature and its elements, as seen in agricultural practices where farmers pray for a good harvest, reflects the essence of this mantra. Just as the trees flourish, so too do crops when nurtured properly.
    • In modern contexts, the idea of invoking strength and support can be seen in team sports, where players come together, invoking their collective strength to achieve victory.
    • The concept of seeking blessings from a higher power for success and prosperity is prevalent in various cultures, such as during festivals or rituals where communities come together to pray for abundance.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for the flourishing of nature and the invocation of divine support for prosperity and strength. It emphasizes the interconnectedness of humanity with nature and the divine. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as agriculture, teamwork, and community gatherings, where the collective effort and divine blessings are sought for success and abundance. It serves as a reminder of the importance of harmony with nature and the need for support from higher powers in our endeavors.
