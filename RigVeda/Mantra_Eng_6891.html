उद अग्नि शुचयस् तव शुक्रा भ्राजन्त इरते |तव ज्योतिंष्य अर्चयः ||
ud agne śucayas tava śukrā bhrājanta īrate | tava jyotīṁṣy arcayaḥ ||
  • ud agne: This phrase translates to "O Agni," where Agni represents the fire god in Vedic literature. Fire is often seen as a purifying force and a medium for offerings in rituals.
  • śucayas: This means "pure" or "sacred." It emphasizes the purity associated with Agni, suggesting that the fire is not just a physical element but also a spiritual one.
  • tava: This translates to "your," indicating possession or belonging. It signifies that the qualities being described belong to Agni.
  • śukrā: This word means "bright" or "radiant." It highlights the brilliance of Agni, symbolizing knowledge, enlightenment, and divine presence.
  • bhrājanta: This means "shining" or "illuminating." It suggests that Agni not only possesses brightness but also spreads light, which can be interpreted as wisdom or guidance.
  • īrate: This translates to "we invoke" or "we worship." It indicates an act of reverence and calling upon Agni for blessings.
  • tava jyotīṁṣy: This phrase means "your lights" or "your radiances." It refers to the various forms of light and energy that Agni embodies, representing different aspects of life and existence.
  • arcayaḥ: This means "we offer" or "we praise." It signifies the act of devotion and the importance of acknowledging the divine presence in our lives.

In summary, this mantra is a prayer to Agni, the fire god, invoking his pure and radiant qualities. It emphasizes the importance of light, purity, and divine guidance in our lives. The act of worship and offering signifies a deep respect for the forces of nature and the divine, recognizing their role in our existence.


Significance and Real-Life Application:

  • This mantra can be applied in daily life as a reminder to seek purity and clarity in our thoughts and actions, much like the purifying nature of fire.
  • In rituals and ceremonies, invoking Agni symbolizes the importance of intention and devotion, encouraging individuals to approach their goals with sincerity and dedication.
  • The concept of light in this mantra can be related to the pursuit of knowledge and wisdom, inspiring individuals to seek enlightenment in their personal and professional lives.
  • In a broader sense, this mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of all elements in nature, urging us to respect and honor the environment and the forces that sustain life.
