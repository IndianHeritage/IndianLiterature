मन्त्र: हिरण्यपाणिम ऊतये सवितारम उप हवये | स चित्त देवता पदम् ||
hiraṇyapāṇim ūtaye savitāram upa hvaye | sa cettā devatā padam ||
  • Mantra: "hiraṇyapāṇim ūtaye savitāram upa hvaye | sa cettā devatā padam ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • hiraṇyapāṇim: One who has golden hands; symbolizes prosperity and abundance.
    • ūtaye: For the purpose of nourishment or sustenance.
    • savitāram: The Sun God, representing light, energy, and life.
    • upa hvaye: I invoke or call upon.
    • sa: He or that (referring to the deity).
    • cettā: If he (the deity) is pleased or grants.
    • devatā: The divine or deity.
    • padam: The goal, place, or state of being.
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to the Sun God, Savitā, who is often associated with life-giving energy and prosperity.
    • The phrase "hiraṇyapāṇim" suggests a connection to wealth and abundance, indicating that the Sun God is a source of nourishment not just for the body but also for the soul.
    • By invoking Savitā, the speaker seeks blessings for sustenance and prosperity in life.
    • The latter part of the mantra emphasizes the importance of divine favor; it suggests that if the deity is pleased, one can attain their desired goals or states of being.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In agriculture, farmers often pray to the Sun for good weather and a bountiful harvest, reflecting the essence of this mantra where the Sun is seen as a provider of sustenance.
    • In personal life, individuals may recite this mantra during times of financial difficulty, seeking divine intervention for prosperity and abundance.
    • In spiritual practices, this mantra can be used during meditation to invoke positive energy and clarity, aligning oneself with the life-giving force of the Sun.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of divine blessings in achieving prosperity and sustenance in life.
    • It serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of nature and spirituality, encouraging individuals to seek harmony with the forces that sustain them.
    • In real-life scenarios, it can be applied in various contexts, from agriculture to personal growth, emphasizing the importance of invoking divine support for achieving one's goals.
