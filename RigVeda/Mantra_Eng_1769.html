कस्य ब्रह्माणि जुजुषुर युवानः को अध्वरे मरुत आ ववर्त |श्येनाम् इव ध्रजतो अन्तरिक्षे केन महा मनसा रीरामाम ||
kasya brahmāṇi jujuṣur yuvānaḥ ko adhvare maruta ā vavarta | śyenām̐ iva dhrajato antarikṣe kena mahā manasā rīramāma ||
  • kasya - Whose
  • brahmāṇi - In the context of the divine or the ultimate reality
  • jujuṣur - Desiring or seeking
  • yuvānaḥ - The youthful or the young
  • ko - Who
  • adhvare - In the sacrificial context or in the act of worship
  • maruta - The winds or the storm gods
  • ā vavarta - Have turned or have come
  • śyenām̐ - Like the eagle
  • iva - Like or as
  • dhrajato - In the sky or in the atmosphere
  • antarikṣe - In the space or the heavens
  • kena - By whom
  • mahā - Great
  • manasā - With the mind or intellect
  • rīramāma - Shall we be delighted or shall we rejoice

This mantra can be interpreted as a profound inquiry into the nature of existence and the divine. It poses questions about the source of creation and the forces that govern the universe. The youthful seekers are depicted as desiring to understand the ultimate reality, represented by the term "brahmāṇi." The reference to the winds (maruta) suggests the dynamic and ever-changing nature of life and the universe.


In real life, this mantra can be applied to the quest for knowledge and understanding. For instance, young individuals embarking on their educational journeys often seek answers to profound questions about life, purpose, and the universe. Just as the mantra asks, "By whom shall we rejoice?" it reflects the human desire to connect with something greater than ourselves, whether through spirituality, philosophy, or science.


Moreover, the imagery of the eagle soaring in the sky symbolizes the pursuit of higher knowledge and the aspiration to rise above mundane concerns. In a practical sense, this can be seen in the lives of those who strive for excellence in their fields, whether in academics, arts, or social service, aiming to make a meaningful impact on the world.


Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for introspection and the pursuit of wisdom. It encourages individuals to seek deeper truths and to understand their place in the cosmos. In a world filled with distractions, this mantra serves as a reminder to focus on our aspirations and the greater questions of existence, ultimately guiding us towards a more fulfilling and enlightened life.
