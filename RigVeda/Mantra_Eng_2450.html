ईळे च त्वा यजमानो हविर्भिर ईळे सखित्वं सुमतिं निकामः |देवैर अवो मिमीहि सं जरीत्रे रक्षां च नो दाम्येभिर अनीकैः ||
īḻe ca tvā yajamāno havirbhir īḻe sakhitvaṁ sumatiṁ nikāmaḥ | devair avo mimīhi saṁ jaritre rakṣā ca no damyebhir anīkaiḥ ||
  • īḻe ca tvā yajamāno havirbhir īḻe sakhitvaṁ sumatiṁ nikāmaḥ
    • īḻe ca tvā: This phrase can be interpreted as "I invoke you" or "I praise you." It signifies the act of calling upon a divine presence or energy.
    • yajamāno: Refers to the one who is performing the ritual or sacrifice, indicating the importance of intention and devotion in spiritual practices.
    • havirbhir: This word means "with offerings." It emphasizes the significance of offerings in rituals, which can be seen as a way to express gratitude and seek blessings.
    • sakhitvaṁ: This term translates to "friendship" or "companionship," suggesting a relationship of trust and support between the devotee and the divine.
    • sumatiṁ nikāmaḥ: This phrase means "may I attain good understanding" or "may I achieve wisdom." It highlights the desire for clarity and insight in one's life.
  • devair avo mimīhi saṁ jaritre rakṣā ca no damyebhir anīkaiḥ
    • devair: This word means "by the gods," indicating the divine forces that are being invoked for assistance.
    • avo mimīhi: This can be interpreted as "may you protect" or "may you guide us," showing a plea for divine intervention and support.
    • saṁ jaritre: This phrase refers to "in the journey of life," suggesting that the mantra is relevant to the experiences and challenges faced in life.
    • rakṣā ca no: This means "and protect us," emphasizing the need for safety and security in one's endeavors.
    • damyebhir anīkaiḥ: This phrase translates to "from those who are harmful," indicating a request for protection from negative influences or adversities.

Conclusion:

The mantra encapsulates a deep spiritual invocation for guidance, wisdom, and protection. It reflects the importance of establishing a connection with the divine through rituals and offerings, fostering a sense of companionship and trust. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied during challenging times when one seeks clarity and support, such as during personal crises, decision-making processes, or when facing adversities. By reciting this mantra, individuals can cultivate a mindset of seeking wisdom and protection, reinforcing their resilience and ability to navigate life's complexities.
