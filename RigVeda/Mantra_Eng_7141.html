अहं च त्वं च वृत्रहन् सं युज्यावा सनीभ्य आ |अरातिवा चिद अद्रिवोऽनु नौ शूर मंसते भद्राः इन्द्रस्य रातयः ||
ahaṁ ca tvaṁ ca vṛtrahan saṁ yujyāva sanibhya ā | arātīvā cid adrivo 'nu nau śūra maṁsate bhadrā indrasya rātayaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ahaṁ - I
    • ca - and
    • tvaṁ - you
    • ca - and
    • vṛtrahan - the slayer of Vritra (a demon)
    • saṁ - together
    • yujyāva - we are united
    • sanibhya - in harmony
    • ā - towards
    • arātīvā - the enemies
    • cid - indeed
    • adrivo - the non-violent
    • 'nu - indeed
    • nau - we
    • śūra - the brave
    • maṁsate - may we be nourished
    • bhadrā - auspicious
    • indrasya - of Indra (the king of gods)
    • rātayaḥ - the realms
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the unity between the individual (I) and the divine (you), suggesting a partnership in overcoming challenges.
    • The reference to "vṛtrahan" symbolizes the triumph over obstacles and adversities, akin to facing personal demons in life.
    • The phrase "we are united in harmony" highlights the importance of collaboration and support among individuals in achieving common goals.
    • It calls for bravery and nourishment, indicating that courage and sustenance are essential for success in any endeavor.
    • The invocation of Indra signifies seeking divine blessings and guidance in our pursuits.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a team project at work, the mantra can be applied by fostering collaboration and unity among team members to overcome challenges.
    • In personal relationships, it encourages partners to support each other in facing life's difficulties, reinforcing the idea of being "together" in adversity.
    • In community service, it can inspire individuals to come together to help those in need, embodying the spirit of bravery and nourishment for the greater good.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the power of unity, courage, and divine support in overcoming life's challenges.
    • Its significance lies in the encouragement to work together harmoniously, face adversities bravely, and seek blessings for success.
    • In real-life scenarios, it can be applied in various contexts, from personal relationships to professional teamwork, emphasizing the importance of collaboration and mutual support.
