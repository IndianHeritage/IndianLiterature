अविष्टो अस्मान् विश्वासु विक्ष्व अद्युः कृत्नोति शंसं निनित्सोः ||
aviṣṭo asmān viśvāsu vikṣv adyuṁ kṛṇota śaṁsaṁ ninitsoḥ ||
  • Aviṣṭo: This term can be translated as "unconquered" or "unstoppable." It signifies a state of being that is beyond defeat, representing resilience and strength.
  • Asmān: This word means "us" or "we," indicating a collective identity or community. It emphasizes the importance of unity and togetherness.
  • Viśvāsu: Translated as "in all worlds" or "in all realms," this term suggests a universal presence or influence, highlighting the interconnectedness of all existence.
  • Vikṣv: This word means "to see" or "to perceive." It implies awareness and understanding, encouraging individuals to be observant and mindful of their surroundings.
  • Adyuṁ: This term translates to "today" or "in the present." It emphasizes the importance of the current moment and the actions taken in the here and now.
  • Kṛṇota: Meaning "to make" or "to create," this word signifies the act of bringing something into existence, whether it be ideas, actions, or relationships.
  • Śaṁsaṁ: This term can be understood as "blessings" or "well-being." It represents the positive outcomes and benefits that arise from collective efforts and unity.
  • Ninitsoḥ: This word refers to "the one who is praised" or "the one who is celebrated." It highlights the importance of recognition and appreciation within a community.

In conclusion, the mantra "aviṣṭo asmān viśvāsu vikṣv adyuṁ kṛṇota śaṁsaṁ ninitsoḥ" encapsulates themes of resilience, unity, awareness, and the importance of the present moment. It encourages individuals to work together towards common goals, to be mindful of their actions, and to create positive outcomes for themselves and their communities. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as teamwork in professional settings, community service initiatives, or even personal relationships where collaboration and mutual support are essential for success and well-being. Its significance lies in fostering a sense of belonging and shared purpose, ultimately leading to a harmonious and prosperous existence for all.
