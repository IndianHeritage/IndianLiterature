इमं रथम अधि ये सप्त तस्थुः सप्तचक्रं सप्त वहन्त्य अश्वाः |सप्त स्वसारो अभि सं नवन्ते यत्र गवां निहिता सप्त नाम ||
imaṁ ratham adhi ye sapta tasthuḥ saptacakraṁ sapta vahanty aśvāḥ | sapta svasāro abhi saṁ navante yatra gavāṁ nihitā sapta nāma ||
  • Translation: "Those who are seated on this chariot, may they be blessed. The seven wheels are drawn by seven horses. The seven sisters are united, where the seven names of the cows are hidden."

  • Breakdown of Key Terms:
    • Ratham: Chariot - Symbolizes the journey of life and the vehicle that carries us through our experiences.
    • Sapta: Seven - Represents completeness and the various aspects of existence.
    • Aśvāḥ: Horses - Symbolize strength, energy, and the driving forces in our lives.
    • Svasāro: Sisters - Represents unity and collaboration among different elements or forces.
    • Gavāṁ: Cows - Symbolize nourishment, wealth, and the sustenance we derive from our environment.

  • Real-Life Examples:
    • The chariot can be likened to an individual's life journey, where the choices we make and the paths we take are akin to steering a chariot.
    • The seven horses can represent different aspects of our personality or life roles, such as being a parent, professional, friend, etc., all working together to move us forward.
    • The concept of unity among the seven sisters can be seen in teamwork in a workplace, where collaboration leads to success.
    • The cows symbolize the resources we rely on, such as family, education, and community support, which nourish our growth and development.

  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of unity, strength, and the interconnectedness of various elements in our lives. It teaches us that just as a chariot requires all its parts to function effectively, our lives depend on the harmonious collaboration of different aspects. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios such as teamwork in professional settings, family dynamics, and community involvement. Recognizing and nurturing these connections can lead to a more fulfilling and balanced life.
