मन्त्र: उता वा यः सहस्यम् प्रविद्वान् मर्तो मर्तं मार्चयति द्वयेना |अतः पाहि स्तवमान स्तुवन्तम् अग्ने माकिर नो दुरिताय धायिः ||
uta vā yaḥ sahasya pravidvān marto martam marcayati dvayena | ataḥ pāhi stavamāna stuvantam agne mākir no duritāya dhāyīḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • uta vā: or indeed
    • yaḥ: who
    • sahasya: with strength
    • pravidvān: knowing well
    • marto: the mortal
    • martam: death
    • marcayati: overcomes
    • dvayena: with duality
    • ataḥ: therefore
    • pāhi: protect
    • stavamāna: praising
    • stuvantam: one who is praised
    • agne: O Fire
    • mākir: may you make us
    • no: for us
    • duritāya: from misfortune
    • dhāyīḥ: grant
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks to the duality of existence, where the mortal being, despite facing death, can overcome it through strength and knowledge.
    • The invocation of Agni (Fire) symbolizes the transformative power of energy and purity, which can help in overcoming life's challenges.
    • It emphasizes the importance of seeking protection and guidance from higher powers while acknowledging our own vulnerabilities.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In moments of crisis, such as facing a serious illness or personal loss, individuals often seek strength and guidance from spiritual practices, much like invoking Agni for protection.
    • In professional settings, when facing challenges, teams often come together to support each other, drawing on collective strength to overcome obstacles.
    • In educational contexts, students may face the 'death' of failure but can overcome it through knowledge and perseverance, akin to the mantra's message of overcoming mortality.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the resilience of the human spirit and the importance of seeking support from both within and from higher powers.
    • Its significance lies in the acknowledgment of life's dualities—strength and vulnerability, life and death—and the continuous journey of overcoming challenges.
    • In real life, it encourages individuals to embrace their struggles, seek guidance, and strive for transformation, ultimately leading to personal growth and enlightenment.
