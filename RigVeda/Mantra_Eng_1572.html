मन्त्र: अप्रयुच्छन्न अप्रयुच्छद्भिर अग्नि शिवेभिर नः पायुभिः पाहि शग्मैः | अदब्देभिर अदृप्तिभिर इष्टे 'निमिषद्भिः परि पाहि नो जाः ||
aprayucchann aprayucchadbhir agne śivebhir naḥ pāyubhiḥ pāhi śagmaiḥ | adabdhebhir adṛpitebhir iṣṭe 'nimiṣadbhiḥ pari pāhi no jāḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • aprayucchann: Not being separated or not being distant.
    • aprayucchadbhir: By those who do not separate.
    • agne: O Agni (the divine fire or energy).
    • śivebhir: By auspicious or benevolent forces.
    • naḥ: Our.
    • pāyubhiḥ: By the protectors or guardians.
    • pāhi: Protect.
    • śagmaiḥ: By the auspicious or favorable ones.
    • adabdhebhir: By those who are not disturbed.
    • adṛpitebhir: By those who are not obstructed.
    • iṣṭe: In the worship or offerings.
    • 'nimiṣadbhiḥ: By those who do not blink or are vigilant.
    • pari: All around or completely.
    • pāhi: Protect.
    • no: Us.
    • jāḥ: From the enemies or obstacles.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Agni, the divine fire, seeking protection and guidance.
    • It emphasizes the importance of unity and support from benevolent forces in overcoming challenges.
    • The repetition of "pāhi" signifies a strong plea for safeguarding from all forms of disturbances and adversities.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In times of personal crisis, individuals often seek the support of family and friends, akin to invoking Agni for protection.
    • In a workplace setting, teamwork and collaboration can be seen as a modern interpretation of the mantra, where collective efforts lead to overcoming obstacles.
    • During community gatherings or rituals, the collective prayers for safety and prosperity reflect the essence of this mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for unity, protection, and the importance of auspicious energies in our lives.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal challenges to communal efforts, reminding us of the strength found in togetherness and divine support.
    • By invoking such energies, individuals can foster a sense of security and resilience in the face of adversity.
