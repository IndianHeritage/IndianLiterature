मन्त्र: नासत्याभ्यां बर्हिर इव प्र वृञ्जे स्तोमां इयर्म्य अभ्रियेव वातः |याव अर्बगाय विमदाय जायाṁ सेनाजुवां न्यूहतू रथेन ||
nāsatyābhyām barhir iva pra vṛñje stomām̐ iyarmy abhriyeva vātaḥ | yāv arbhagāya vimadāya jāyāṁ senājuvā nyūhatū rathena ||
  • nāsatyābhyām: This term refers to the twin deities, the Aśvins, who are often associated with health, medicine, and the dawn. They symbolize the duality of existence and the balance between opposites.
  • barhir iva: "Like a covering" or "like a blanket." This phrase suggests protection or a nurturing aspect, akin to how a blanket provides warmth and security.
  • pra vṛñje: This means "I invoke" or "I call forth." It indicates a desire to bring forth something beneficial or auspicious.
  • stomām̐: Refers to a hymn or praise. It signifies the act of offering reverence or gratitude, often in a ritualistic context.
  • iyarmy: This can be interpreted as "to the one who is worthy" or "to the one who deserves." It emphasizes the importance of recognizing and honoring those who contribute positively to our lives.
  • abhriyeva vātaḥ: "Like the wind that brings rain." This phrase symbolizes the nurturing and life-giving aspects of nature, suggesting that just as the wind brings rain, so too can our actions bring forth prosperity and growth.
  • yāv arbhagāya: "As long as there is a child." This highlights the importance of nurturing the next generation and ensuring their well-being.
  • vimadāya jāyāṁ: "For the one who is joyful." This phrase suggests that joy and happiness are essential components of life and should be cultivated.
  • senājuvā: "With the strength of an army." This indicates the power of unity and collective effort in achieving goals.
  • nyūhatū rathena: "May we be carried forward on the chariot." This symbolizes progress and the journey of life, suggesting that we should strive to move forward with purpose and direction.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda emphasizes the importance of invoking divine blessings for protection, prosperity, and joy. It highlights the significance of nurturing the next generation and recognizing the contributions of those around us. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various contexts, such as family dynamics, community building, and personal growth. By fostering a sense of unity and collective strength, we can navigate life's challenges and move forward with purpose, much like a chariot that carries us toward our goals. The mantra serves as a reminder to honor the dualities of life, seek joy, and support one another in our journeys.
