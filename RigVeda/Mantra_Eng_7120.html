त्वं पुरू सहस्राणि शतानि च यूथा दानाय मंहसे |आ पुरंदरणं चक्रिम विप्रवचस इन्द्रं गायन्तो 'वसे ||
tvam purū sahasrāṇi śatāni ca yūthā dānāya maṁhase | ā puraṁdaraṁ cakṛma vipravacasa indraṁ gāyanto 'vase ||
  • tvam purū sahasrāṇi: This phrase translates to "You are the many thousands." Here, "tvam" means "you," and "purū" refers to "many" or "numerous." This can be interpreted as recognizing the vastness and omnipresence of a divine entity or force in our lives.
  • śatāni ca yūthā: This means "and hundreds of groups." The word "śatāni" signifies "hundreds," while "yūthā" refers to "groups" or "masses." This can be seen as a representation of unity and collective strength, emphasizing the importance of community and collaboration in achieving goals.
  • dānāya maṁhase: This translates to "for the great gift." "Dānāya" means "for the gift" or "for the offering," and "maṁhase" refers to "great" or "mighty." This suggests that the divine or the greater force provides us with significant blessings or gifts, which we should recognize and appreciate.
  • ā puraṁdaraṁ cakṛma: This means "we have made the Purandara." "Puraṁdara" is often associated with the deity Indra, who is known as the destroyer of obstacles. "Cakṛma" means "we have made" or "we have created." This can be interpreted as the act of overcoming challenges and creating a path forward in life.
  • vipravacasa indraṁ: This translates to "the wise Indra." "Vipravacasa" refers to "wise" or "intelligent," and "Indra" is the name of the deity. This highlights the importance of wisdom and intelligence in leadership and decision-making, suggesting that we should seek guidance from wise figures in our lives.
  • gāyanto 'vase: This means "singing praises." "Gāyanto" refers to "singing" or "chanting," and "'vase" means "to invoke" or "to call upon." This emphasizes the significance of expressing gratitude and reverence towards the divine or the forces that guide us.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of recognizing the vastness of divine presence, the strength found in community, and the importance of wisdom in overcoming challenges. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios such as teamwork in professional settings, where collective efforts lead to success, or in personal growth, where seeking wisdom from mentors can help navigate life's obstacles. The act of expressing gratitude through praise and acknowledgment of the gifts we receive fosters a positive mindset and strengthens our connection to the greater forces in our lives.
