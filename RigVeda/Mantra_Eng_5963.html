यदि वाहम् अनृतदेव आस मोघं वा देवाम् अप्यूहे अग्ने |किम अस्मभ्यं जातवेदो हृणीषे द्रोघवाचस ते निरृतं सचन्ताम् ||
yadi vāham anṛtadeva āsa moghaṁ vā devām̐ apyūhe agne | kim asmabhyaṁ jātavedo hṛṇīṣe droghavācas te nirṛthaṁ sacantām ||
  • yadi vāham anṛtadeva āsa moghaṁ vā devām̐ apyūhe agne
    • yadi vāham - "If I am" or "If I become"
    • anṛtadeva - "the deity of untruth" or "falsehood"
    • āsa - "to be" or "to exist"
    • moghaṁ - "in vain" or "fruitless"
    • vā - "or"
    • devām̐ - "to the gods"
    • apyūhe - "I do not invoke"
    • agne - "O Agni" (the fire god)
  • kim asmabhyaṁ jātavedo hṛṇīṣe
    • kim - "what"
    • asmabhyaṁ - "to us"
    • jātavedo - "O Jātavedas" (another name for Agni, meaning "the one who knows all births")
    • hṛṇīṣe - "do you grant" or "do you bestow"
  • droghavācas te nirṛthaṁ sacantām
    • droghavācas - "with deceitful words"
    • te - "your"
    • nirṛthaṁ - "without purpose" or "in vain"
    • sacantām - "may they not come to us"

Meaning:

This mantra expresses a plea to Agni, the fire god, to not let the speaker be associated with falsehood or deceit. The speaker is essentially asking Agni to ensure that their actions and words are truthful and meaningful. The invocation of Agni signifies the importance of purity and truth in one's life, as fire is often seen as a purifying element.


Real-life Examples:

  • In a professional setting, honesty in communication is crucial. For instance, during a job interview, a candidate should present their qualifications truthfully, as exaggerating or lying can lead to negative consequences.
  • In personal relationships, trust is built on honesty. If one partner lies to another, it can lead to a breakdown of the relationship, highlighting the importance of truthful communication.
  • In a broader societal context, leaders and public figures are expected to uphold truthfulness. When they engage in deceitful practices, it can lead to public distrust and societal unrest.

Conclusion:

The significance of this mantra lies in its emphasis on truthfulness and integrity. It serves as a reminder to individuals to strive for honesty in their thoughts, words, and actions. In real-life scenarios, whether in personal relationships, professional environments, or societal interactions, the principles of truth and integrity are foundational for building trust and fostering positive connections. By invoking Agni's blessings, one seeks to align themselves with these values, ensuring that their life is not led in vain but with purpose and authenticity.
