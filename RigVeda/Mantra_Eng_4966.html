आ ते स्वस्तिम इमहा आर्यघाम उपावसुम् |अद्याऽ च सर्वतातये श्वश च सर्वतातये ||
ā te svastim īmaha āreaghām upāvasum | adyā ca sarvatātaye śvaś ca sarvatātaye ||
  • Mantra: "ā te svastim īmaha āreaghām upāvasum | adyā ca sarvatātaye śvaś ca sarvatātaye ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā te: to you
    • svastim: well-being, prosperity, or auspiciousness
    • īmaha: we invoke or we seek
    • āreaghām: for the purpose of overcoming obstacles or difficulties
    • upāvasum: we shall dwell or reside
    • adyā: today
    • ca: and
    • sarvatātaye: in all directions or universally
    • śvaś: auspiciousness or good fortune
    • ca: and
    • sarvatātaye: in all directions or universally
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer for well-being and auspiciousness directed towards a higher power or divine entity.
    • The invocation of "ā te svastim" signifies a request for blessings and prosperity.
    • The phrase "āreaghām upāvasum" indicates a desire to overcome obstacles and reside in a state of peace and stability.
    • By stating "adyā ca sarvatātaye," the mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking these blessings universally and in all aspects of life.
    • The repetition of "śvaś ca sarvatātaye" reinforces the idea of spreading auspiciousness in every direction, highlighting the interconnectedness of all beings.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a personal context, one might recite this mantra before starting a new venture, such as a business or a project, to invoke success and remove any potential hurdles.
    • In community gatherings or rituals, this mantra can be chanted to promote harmony and collective well-being among participants.
    • During times of crisis or uncertainty, individuals may use this mantra as a source of comfort and hope, seeking divine intervention for peace and stability.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its universal appeal for well-being and auspiciousness, making it relevant in various aspects of life.
    • It serves as a reminder of the importance of seeking blessings not just for oneself but for the greater good of all.
    • In practical terms, this mantra can be applied in daily life through meditation, prayer, or during significant life events, fostering a sense of community and shared prosperity.
