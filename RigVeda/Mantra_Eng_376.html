नदं न भिन्नम् अमुयā शयानम् मनो रुहाणā अति यन्त्य आपः |याश चिद वृत्रो महिना पर्यतिष्ठत तासाम् अहिः पात्सुतःशीर बभूव ||
nadaṁ na bhinnam amuyā śayānam mano ruhāṇā ati yanty āpaḥ | yāś cid vṛtro mahinā paryatiṣṭhat tāsām ahiḥ patsutaḥśīr babhūva ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • nadaṁ: sound, vibration
    • na: not
    • bhinnam: broken, divided
    • amuyā: by that which is not
    • śayānam: resting, lying down
    • mano: mind
    • ruhāṇā: rising, elevating
    • ati: beyond, surpassing
    • yanty: they go, they move
    • āpaḥ: waters, streams
    • yāś: those who
    • cid: indeed, certainly
    • vṛtro: the obstacle, the demon
    • mahinā: with great strength
    • paryatiṣṭhat: has established, has stood
    • tāsām: of them
    • ahiḥ: serpent, a symbol of energy
    • patsutaḥ: of the one who is powerful
    • śīr: glory, brilliance
    • babhūva: has become
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the unity of sound and the mind, emphasizing that true understanding and elevation come from a harmonious state.
    • The phrase "nadaṁ na bhinnam" suggests that sound is not broken; it is a continuous flow that connects all things.
    • The reference to "āpaḥ" (waters) symbolizes the flow of consciousness and knowledge, which nourishes the mind and spirit.
    • The mention of "vṛtro" represents obstacles or challenges that one faces in life, which can be overcome with strength and determination.
    • The "ahiḥ" symbolizes the powerful energy within us that can be harnessed to achieve greatness.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In meditation practices, the focus on sound (like chanting or mantras) helps in unifying the mind and elevating consciousness.
    • In personal development, overcoming obstacles (vṛtro) can be likened to facing challenges in career or relationships, where inner strength (ahiḥ) is essential for success.
    • In nature, the flow of water (āpaḥ) can be seen as a metaphor for the flow of life and experiences, teaching us to adapt and move forward.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of unity, strength, and the continuous flow of energy in overcoming life's challenges.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, such as personal growth, meditation, and facing adversities.
    • By understanding and embodying the principles in this mantra, individuals can cultivate resilience and harmony in their lives.
