अति श्रिती तिरश्चता गव्याऽजिगात्य अण्व्या। वग्नुम इयर्ति यं विदे॥
ati śritī tiraścatā gavyā jigāty aṇvyā | vagnum iyarti yaṁ vide ||
  • ati śritī: This phrase can be translated as "beyond the limits" or "transcending boundaries." It suggests a state of being that goes beyond ordinary experiences.
  • tiraścatā: This term refers to "crossing over" or "transversing." It implies movement or transition from one state to another, often indicating a journey or transformation.
  • gavyā: This word is associated with "cattle" or "wealth." In the context of the Rigveda, it symbolizes prosperity, abundance, and the nurturing aspects of life.
  • jigāty: This can be interpreted as "to strive" or "to exert effort." It emphasizes the importance of action and determination in achieving one's goals.
  • aṇvyā: This term can mean "with care" or "with attention." It highlights the need for mindfulness and consideration in our actions and decisions.
  • vagnum: This word refers to "speech" or "word." It signifies the power of communication and expression in shaping our reality.
  • iyarti: This can be translated as "to lead" or "to guide." It suggests the role of leadership and direction in navigating life's challenges.
  • yaṁ: This is a pronoun meaning "who" or "that." It connects the previous concepts to a specific subject or entity.
  • vide: This term means "to know" or "to understand." It emphasizes the importance of knowledge and awareness in our journey.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of transcending limitations and striving for prosperity through mindful actions and effective communication. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios such as personal development, leadership roles, and community building. For instance, a leader who transcends boundaries and communicates effectively can inspire their team to strive for greater achievements. Similarly, individuals who approach their goals with care and determination can create a path toward success and abundance. The significance of this mantra lies in its call for awareness, effort, and the transformative power of knowledge in our lives.
