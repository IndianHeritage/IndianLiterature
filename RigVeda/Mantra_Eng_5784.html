एषा स्याऽनव्यम् आयुर् दधाना गूढ्वी तमो ज्योतिषोऽषा अबोधि |अग्र एति युवतिर् अह्रयाणां प्राचिकितत सूर्यं यज्ञम् अग्निम् ||
eṣā syā navyam āyur dadhānā gūḍhvī tamo jyotiṣoṣā abodhi | agra eti yuvatir ahrayāṇā prācikitat sūryaṁ yajñam agnim ||
  • eṣā syā navyam āyur dadhānā: This phrase translates to "This is a new life, endowed with longevity." It signifies the importance of health and vitality in life. In real life, this can be related to the modern emphasis on wellness, where people invest in healthy diets, exercise, and mental well-being to ensure a long and fulfilling life.
  • gūḍhvī tamo jyotiṣoṣā abodhi: This part means "May the hidden darkness be dispelled by light." It reflects the idea of overcoming ignorance and confusion through knowledge and enlightenment. For example, in education, when students learn and gain knowledge, they are metaphorically dispelling the darkness of ignorance.
  • agra eti yuvatir ahrayāṇā: This translates to "The young woman moves forward with determination." It emphasizes the strength and resolve of youth, particularly women, in pursuing their goals. In contemporary society, this can be seen in the rise of women in leadership roles and their contributions to various fields, breaking barriers and stereotypes.
  • prācikitat sūryaṁ yajñam agnim: This means "Let us worship the Sun and the Fire." The Sun represents energy and life, while Fire symbolizes transformation and purification. In real life, this can be related to environmental awareness and the importance of sustainable energy sources, as well as the rituals and practices that honor nature and its elements.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of life, knowledge, determination, and reverence for nature. Its significance lies in the holistic approach to living a meaningful life, where health, enlightenment, empowerment, and environmental consciousness are intertwined. In today's world, this mantra can inspire individuals to seek balance in their lives, pursue knowledge, empower themselves and others, and respect the natural world, ultimately leading to a harmonious existence.
