सम इति तं अगम् अश्नवाद दुःशंसम् मर्त्यं रिपुम् |यो अस्मत्रा दुर्हणावां उप द्वयुः ||
sam it tam agham aśnavad duḥśaṁsam martyaṁ ripum | yo asmatrā durhaṇāvām̐ upa dvayuḥ ||
  • Mantra: "sam it tam agham aśnavad duḥśaṁsam martyaṁ ripum | yo asmatrā durhaṇāvām̐ upa dvayuḥ ||"
  • Translation: This mantra can be translated as: "May we overcome the evil and the adversary that is difficult to conquer. He who is our protector, may he lead us to safety."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • sam: Together, in harmony.
    • it: To go or to move towards.
    • tam: That, referring to the adversary or evil.
    • agham: Sin or evil.
    • aśnavad: Like a horse, symbolizing strength and speed.
    • duḥśaṁsam: Difficult to conquer or overcome.
    • martyaṁ: Mortal or human.
    • ripum: Enemy or adversary.
    • yo: He who.
    • asmatrā: Our protector.
    • durhaṇāvām̐: Difficult to conquer.
    • upa: Near or close to.
    • dvayuḥ: Safety or refuge.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a personal context, this mantra can be invoked during challenging times, such as facing a difficult situation at work or in relationships. For instance, when dealing with a toxic colleague, one can recite this mantra to seek strength and protection.
    • In a broader societal context, communities facing adversity, such as natural disasters or social injustices, can use this mantra to unite and seek collective strength against the challenges they face.
    • In mental health, individuals struggling with anxiety or depression may find solace in this mantra, using it as a reminder to seek help and protection from their inner adversities.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for unity and strength against adversities. It emphasizes the importance of seeking protection and guidance in times of trouble. In real-life scenarios, whether personal, communal, or societal, this mantra serves as a powerful reminder to confront challenges with courage and to seek support from those who can help us navigate through difficulties. By invoking this mantra, individuals and communities can foster resilience and hope, ultimately leading to a path of safety and harmony.
