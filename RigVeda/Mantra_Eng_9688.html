उद īर्ष्वातो विश्वावसो नमसेळा महे त्वा |अन्यां इच्छ प्रफर्व्यं सं जायाṁ पत्या सृजा ||
ud īrṣvāto viśvāvaso namaseḻā mahe tvā | anyām iccha prapharvya1ṁ saṁ jāyām patyā sṛja ||
  • ud īrṣvāto: This term can be broken down into two parts: "ud" meaning "up" or "out" and "īrṣvāto" which relates to "to rise" or "to elevate." This suggests a call to elevate oneself or to rise above challenges.
  • viśvāvaso: This word can be interpreted as "all-pervading" or "universal." It signifies the presence of a divine or universal force that encompasses everything.
  • namaseḻā: This term refers to "salutation" or "offering." It indicates a gesture of respect or reverence towards the divine or the universal force.
  • mahe tvā: "mahe" means "great" and "tvā" means "you." Together, this phrase expresses reverence towards a great entity, possibly a deity or a universal principle.
  • anyām iccha: "anyām" translates to "other" and "iccha" means "wish" or "desire." This suggests a longing for something beyond the current state or a desire for a different experience.
  • prapharvyaṁ: This term can be understood as "to spread" or "to expand." It indicates a desire for growth or the expansion of one's horizons.
  • saṁ jāyām: "saṁ" means "together" and "jāyām" refers to "to be born" or "to arise." This phrase emphasizes the importance of unity and collective growth.
  • patyā sṛja: "patyā" means "by the lord" or "by the master," and "sṛja" means "to create" or "to bring forth." This indicates the act of creation or manifestation guided by a higher power.

In summary, this mantra speaks to the aspiration of rising above challenges with the support of a universal force. It emphasizes the importance of reverence, unity, and the desire for growth and transformation. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios such as personal development, teamwork, and spiritual practices. For instance, in a workplace setting, individuals can strive to elevate their skills and work collaboratively towards a common goal, invoking a sense of respect and unity among team members. The mantra serves as a reminder of the power of collective effort and the importance of seeking guidance from a higher source in our endeavors.
