वाश्रां अर्षन्तीन्दवोऽभि वत्सं न धेनवः |दधन्विरे गभस्त्योः ||
vāśrā arṣantīndavo 'bhi vatsaṁ na dhenavaḥ | dadhanvire gabhastyoḥ ||
  • vāśrā: This term can be interpreted as "those who are powerful" or "those who are strong." It signifies strength and resilience.
  • arṣantīndavo: This word can be broken down into "arṣanti," meaning "to be strong" or "to be powerful," and "indavaḥ," which refers to "the moon" or "the divine." Together, it suggests a connection between strength and divine power.
  • 'bhi: This is a preposition that can mean "with" or "together with," indicating a sense of unity or collaboration.
  • vatsaṁ: This term translates to "calf" or "young one," often symbolizing new beginnings, growth, and nurturing.
  • na: This is a negation, meaning "not," which can imply a sense of caution or avoidance.
  • dhenavaḥ: This word means "cows" or "nourishing beings," representing abundance, sustenance, and the nurturing aspect of life.
  • dadhanvire: This term can be understood as "those who possess" or "those who hold," indicating ownership or guardianship.
  • gabhastyoḥ: This word refers to "light" or "brightness," often symbolizing knowledge, clarity, and enlightenment.

The mantra can be interpreted as a call for strength and unity among powerful beings, emphasizing the importance of nurturing and protecting new beginnings while avoiding negativity. It highlights the significance of collaboration and the role of nurturing entities in fostering growth and enlightenment.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as:

  • Community Building: Just as the mantra emphasizes unity and strength, communities can thrive when individuals come together to support and nurture one another, fostering a sense of belonging and growth.
  • Parenting and Mentorship: The nurturing aspect represented by "vatsaṁ" and "dhenavaḥ" can be seen in parenting and mentorship, where the focus is on guiding and supporting the younger generation to help them grow and succeed.
  • Leadership: Leaders who embody the qualities of strength and nurturing can create environments that promote collaboration, innovation, and enlightenment, leading to overall success.

In conclusion, this mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of strength, unity, and nurturing in our lives. It encourages us to work together, support one another, and foster growth in ourselves and those around us, ultimately leading to a brighter and more enlightened future.
