तां कर्माषटरास्मै प्र च्युत्नानि देवयन्तो भरण्ते |जुजोषद इन्द्रो दसमवर्चा नासत्येव सुग्म्यो रथेष्टाः ||
tā karmāṣatarāsmai pra cyautnāni devayanto bharante | jujoṣad indro dasmavarcā nāsatyeva sugmyo ratheṣṭhāḥ ||
  • tā karmāṣatarāsmai: This phrase can be translated as "those who perform actions with great effort." It emphasizes the importance of diligent work and commitment in achieving one's goals. In real life, this can be seen in the dedication of athletes training for competitions or students studying hard for exams.
  • pra cyautnāni devayanto bharante: This part means "the divine beings who carry forth the results of actions." It suggests that there are higher powers or forces that support and guide individuals in their endeavors. For example, many people believe in the concept of karma, where good actions lead to positive outcomes, reflecting the idea that divine forces are at play in our lives.
  • jujoṣad indro dasmavarcā: This translates to "Indra, the powerful one, who is full of strength." Indra is often regarded as a deity of rain and thunderstorms, symbolizing strength and power. In a modern context, this can relate to leaders or influential figures who inspire others through their strength and capabilities, such as a CEO leading a company through challenges.
  • nāsatyeva sugmyo ratheṣṭhāḥ: This phrase can be interpreted as "like the swift chariots of the gods." It signifies speed, efficiency, and the ability to overcome obstacles. In everyday life, this can be likened to technological advancements that allow for rapid communication and transportation, enabling people to achieve their goals more efficiently.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of hard work, divine support, strength, and efficiency in achieving success. It teaches that while individual effort is crucial, there are also greater forces at play that can aid in our journeys. In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as in personal development, professional endeavors, and even in community service, where collective efforts and divine guidance can lead to meaningful outcomes. By embodying the principles of this mantra, individuals can strive for excellence while remaining open to the support of higher powers in their pursuits.
