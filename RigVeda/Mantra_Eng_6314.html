तं अह्वे वाजसातय इन्द्रं भराय शुष्मिणम् |भव नः सुम्ने अन्तमः सखा वृधे ||
tam ahve vājasātaya indram bharāya śuṣmiṇam | bhavā naḥ sumne antamaḥ sakhā vṛdhe ||
  • tam ahve vājasātaya indram bharāya śuṣmiṇam
    • tam: This word translates to "that" or "him," referring to a divine entity.
    • ahve: This means "I invoke" or "I call upon," indicating a plea or request for divine assistance.
    • vājasātaya: This term can be understood as "the one who grants strength" or "the one who provides vigor," often associated with the divine qualities of Indra.
    • indram: Refers to Indra, the king of the gods in Vedic literature, symbolizing power, strength, and leadership.
    • bharāya: This means "to bestow" or "to grant," suggesting a request for the divine to provide something valuable.
    • śuṣmiṇam: This word can be interpreted as "the one who is radiant" or "the one who shines," emphasizing the brilliance and glory of the divine.
  • bhavā naḥ sumne antamaḥ sakhā vṛdhe
    • bhavā: This translates to "may you become" or "may you be," indicating a desire for the divine presence.
    • naḥ: This means "for us," suggesting a collective plea or invocation on behalf of a group.
    • sumne: This can be understood as "auspicious" or "favorable," indicating a wish for positive outcomes.
    • antamaḥ: This translates to "the ultimate" or "the best," signifying a desire for the highest form of blessings.
    • sakhā: This means "friend" or "ally," indicating a relationship of support and companionship with the divine.
    • vṛdhe: This translates to "may you grow" or "may you increase," suggesting a wish for prosperity and abundance.

Conclusion:

This mantra is a powerful invocation to Indra, seeking his blessings for strength, prosperity, and auspiciousness. In real life, it can be applied in various scenarios such as:

  • During challenging times, individuals can recite this mantra to invoke strength and support from a higher power.
  • In communal gatherings or rituals, this mantra can be used to foster unity and collective strength among participants.
  • In personal endeavors, such as starting a new project or facing a significant life change, this mantra serves as a reminder to seek divine guidance and support.

The significance of this mantra lies in its ability to connect individuals with the divine, encouraging them to seek strength and companionship in their journeys, ultimately leading to growth and prosperity.
