अनु त्वा रोडसी उभे चक्रं न वर्त्य एतशम् |अनु सुवानास इन्दवः ||
anu tvā rodasī ubhe cakraṁ na varty etaśam | anu suvānāsa indavaḥ ||
  • Mantra: "anu tvā rodasī ubhe cakraṁ na varty etaśam | anu suvānāsa indavaḥ ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • anu: after, following
    • tvā: you
    • rodasī: the two worlds (heavens and earth)
    • ubhe: both
    • cakraṁ: wheel, cycle
    • na: not
    • varty: to revolve, to turn
    • etaśam: this, here
    • suvānāsa: well-formed, auspicious
    • indavaḥ: the moon, or the divine
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the connection between the earthly and heavenly realms.
    • The phrase "anu tvā rodasī ubhe" suggests a following or a connection to both worlds, indicating a unity between the material and spiritual.
    • "Cakraṁ na varty etaśam" implies that the cycles of life and existence do not revolve in the same way here, hinting at a deeper understanding of existence beyond mere physicality.
    • The mention of "suvānāsa indavaḥ" can be interpreted as a call for auspiciousness and divine blessings, often associated with the moon, which symbolizes calmness and serenity.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, this mantra can be reflected in the balance we seek between our material pursuits (like career and wealth) and our spiritual growth (like mindfulness and compassion).
    • For instance, a person may work hard to achieve success in their career while also engaging in meditation or community service, embodying the essence of both worlds.
    • In relationships, this mantra can remind us to maintain harmony between our personal desires and the needs of others, fostering a sense of unity and understanding.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for balance and harmony between the physical and spiritual realms.
    • It encourages individuals to recognize the interconnectedness of all aspects of life, promoting a holistic approach to existence.
    • In real-life scenarios, applying the teachings of this mantra can lead to a more fulfilling and meaningful life, where one can thrive both materially and spiritually.
