उद् द्यां इवे तृष्णजो नाथितासो 'दीधयुर दाशराज्ञे वृतासः | वसिष्ठस्य स्तुवत इन्द्रो अश्रोद उरुं तृत्सुभ्यो अकृतोद उ लोकम् ||
ud dyām ivet tṛṣṇajo nāthitāso 'dīdhayur dāśarājñe vṛtāsaḥ | vasiṣṭhasya stuvata indro aśrod uruṁ tṛtsubhyo akṛṇod u lokam ||
  • ud dyām ivet: This phrase can be translated as "like the sky above." It signifies vastness and the limitless nature of the universe.
  • tṛṣṇajo nāthitāso: This means "born of thirst, not bound." It suggests a state of desire or longing that is not confined or restricted.
  • 'dīdhayur dāśarājñe vṛtāsaḥ: This can be interpreted as "they who are protected by the king of the Dashas." Here, 'Dāśarājña' refers to a ruler or protector, symbolizing authority and guidance.
  • vasiṣṭhasya stuvata: This translates to "praising Vasiṣṭha." Vasiṣṭha is a revered sage in Hindu tradition, representing wisdom and knowledge.
  • indro aśrod: This means "Indra, the lord of strength." Indra is a significant deity in Vedic literature, often associated with power and leadership.
  • uruṁ tṛtsubhyo akṛṇod: This can be understood as "he created a vast world for the thirsty." It implies the act of creation and provision for those in need.
  • u lokam: This translates to "the world." It signifies the physical and spiritual realms that we inhabit.

  • Significance: This mantra emphasizes the themes of vastness, desire, protection, wisdom, strength, and creation. It reflects the interconnectedness of the universe and the divine forces that guide and support humanity.
  • Real-life Application: In our daily lives, we often experience desires and longings that drive us to seek fulfillment. This mantra reminds us to look beyond our immediate needs and recognize the broader universe that supports us. It encourages us to seek wisdom and guidance from those who are knowledgeable, much like how we turn to mentors or leaders in our lives.
  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of seeking knowledge and strength while acknowledging the vastness of existence. It encourages us to appreciate the support we receive from the universe and to strive for a deeper understanding of our desires and the world around us.
