यदā वज्रं हिरण्यं इद अथā रथं हरī यम अस्य वहतो वि सूरिभिः |आ तिष्ठति मगवā सनश्रुत इन्द्रो वाजस्य दीर्घश्रवसस पतिḥ ||
yadā vajraṁ hiraṇyam id athā rathaṁ harī yam asya vahato vi sūribhiḥ | ā tiṣṭhati maghavā sanaśruta indro vājasya dīrghaśravasas patiḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yadā: When
    • vajraṁ: the thunderbolt
    • hiraṇyam: golden
    • id: this
    • athā: and then
    • rathaṁ: the chariot
    • harī: the divine
    • yam: whom
    • asya: of this
    • vahato: who carries
    • vi: with
    • sūribhiḥ: the heroes
    • ā: towards
    • tiṣṭhati: stands
    • maghavā: the powerful
    • sanaśruta: of great fame
    • indro: Indra
    • vājasya: of strength
    • dīrghaśravasas: of long renown
    • patiḥ: lord
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the powerful and divine forces that govern strength and success.
    • The "vajraṁ" symbolizes strength and resilience, akin to how a person might face challenges in life with determination.
    • The "hiraṇyam" represents prosperity and abundance, similar to achieving financial stability or personal growth.
    • The "rathaṁ" signifies the journey or path one takes, much like pursuing a career or personal goals.
    • The mention of "sūribhiḥ" highlights the importance of community and support, as seen in teamwork in workplaces or family support during tough times.
    • Indra, as the lord of strength, can be likened to a leader or mentor who guides individuals towards success.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, a team working together to achieve a project goal embodies the essence of "sūribhiḥ," where collaboration leads to success.
    • In personal life, overcoming obstacles, such as health issues or financial struggles, reflects the strength of "vajraṁ" and the pursuit of "hiraṇyam."
    • Sports teams often rely on the strength of their players and the guidance of their coaches, paralleling the themes of this mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of strength, community, and guidance in achieving success and prosperity.
    • It serves as a reminder that with resilience and support, individuals can navigate their paths effectively.
    • In real life, applying the principles of this mantra can lead to personal growth, successful teamwork, and the attainment of goals.
