त्वतो वाज्य अह्रयोऽभि पूर्वस्माद् अपरः |प्र दाश्वाम् अग्ने अस्तात् ||
tvoto vājy ahrayo 'bhi pūrvasmād aparaḥ | pra dāśvām̐ agne asthāt ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • tvoto: From you
    • vājy: The one who is worthy of praise
    • ahrayo: The one who is to be invoked
    • 'bhi: With
    • pūrvasmād: From the past
    • aparaḥ: The future
    • pra: Forth
    • dāśvām̐: To grant
    • agne: O Agni (the fire god)
    • asthāt: Has stood or has been established
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes Agni, the fire god, who is considered a mediator between humans and the divine.
    • The phrase "from you" signifies a direct appeal to Agni, acknowledging his power and presence.
    • By referring to Agni as "worthy of praise," it emphasizes the respect and reverence that should be given to divine forces.
    • The mention of "from the past" and "the future" suggests a continuity of blessings and guidance that Agni provides throughout time.
    • The call to "grant" indicates a request for support, guidance, or blessings in one's endeavors.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a modern context, this mantra can be recited during significant life events such as weddings or housewarming ceremonies, where the blessings of Agni are sought for prosperity and harmony.
    • In daily life, individuals may invoke this mantra during meditation or prayer, seeking clarity and guidance in their personal and professional decisions.
    • In a corporate setting, leaders may use the essence of this mantra to inspire teamwork and collaboration, emphasizing the importance of collective effort and respect for each member's contributions.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of invoking divine support in our lives, recognizing the continuity of blessings from the past to the future.
    • It serves as a reminder of the importance of respect, gratitude, and the pursuit of guidance in our endeavors.
    • By incorporating such invocations into our daily practices, we can foster a deeper connection with the divine and enhance our spiritual and material well-being.
