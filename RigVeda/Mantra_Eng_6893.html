त्वां अग्ने मनिषिणस त्वां हिन्वन्ति चित्तिभिः |त्वां वर्धन्तु नो गिरः ||
tvām agne manīṣiṇas tvāṁ hinvanti cittibhiḥ | tvāṁ vardhantu no giraḥ ||
  • tvām - This word translates to "you" and refers to Agni, the fire god in Vedic literature. Agni is often seen as a mediator between humans and the divine.
  • agne - This is the vocative form of Agni, directly addressing the deity. It signifies the importance of invoking Agni in rituals and prayers.
  • manīṣiṇas - This term means "wise" or "intelligent." It suggests that those who are wise and knowledgeable are calling upon Agni for guidance and support.
  • tvāṁ hinvanti - This phrase means "they invoke you." It indicates the act of calling upon Agni, emphasizing the need for divine assistance in human endeavors.
  • cittibhiḥ - This word refers to "thoughts" or "intellect." It implies that the invocation of Agni is not just a ritualistic act but is also rooted in deep contemplation and understanding.
  • tvāṁ vardhantu - This translates to "may they enhance you." It expresses a desire for Agni to grow and flourish, which can be interpreted as a wish for prosperity and well-being.
  • no giraḥ - This means "our words" or "our utterances." It signifies the importance of speech and communication in the invocation of Agni, highlighting the role of language in spiritual practices.

The mantra can be understood as a prayer to Agni, asking for wisdom and guidance. It emphasizes the importance of invoking divine support through thoughtful and intelligent means. The wise individuals are calling upon Agni, seeking to enhance their lives and the lives of others through their words and actions.


In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts:

  • Personal Growth: Just as the mantra invokes Agni for wisdom, individuals can seek knowledge and understanding in their personal and professional lives. For example, a student may recite this mantra before an exam, hoping to gain clarity and insight.
  • Community Support: The invocation of Agni can symbolize the collective effort of a community to enhance their well-being. Community leaders may use this mantra in gatherings to inspire unity and shared purpose.
  • Rituals and Ceremonies: In traditional ceremonies, this mantra can be recited to invoke blessings for prosperity and success. For instance, during a housewarming ceremony, families may chant this mantra to seek divine favor for their new home.

In conclusion, this mantra signifies the importance of invoking divine wisdom and support in our lives. It teaches us that through thoughtful communication and collective effort, we can enhance our existence and the world around us. The significance of this mantra lies in its ability to inspire individuals and communities to seek guidance from higher powers, fostering a sense of connection and purpose.
