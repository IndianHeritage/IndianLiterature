धामन ते विश्वं भुवनम् अधि श्रितम् अन्तः समुद्रे हृद्य अन्तर आयुषि |अपाम अनीक समिथे य आभृतस् तं अश्याम मधुमन्तं त उर्मिम् ||
dhāman te viśvam bhuvanam adhi śritam antaḥ samudre hṛdy a1ntar āyuṣi | apām anīke samithe ya ābhṛtas tam aśyāma madhumantaṁ ta ūrmim ||
  • dhāman te viśvam bhuvanam adhi śritam: This phrase can be translated as "Your abode, O divine, is the entire universe." It signifies the omnipresence of the divine, suggesting that the essence of the divine is present in all aspects of the universe.
  • antaḥ samudre hṛdy a1ntar āyuṣi: This translates to "Within the depths of the ocean, in the heart of life." Here, the ocean symbolizes the vastness of existence, while the heart represents the core of life and consciousness. It emphasizes the idea that the divine is not only in the external world but also within us.
  • apām anīke samithe ya ābhṛtas: This means "In the gathering of waters, where the streams converge." This line reflects the interconnectedness of all life forms and the importance of unity in diversity. Just as rivers come together to form a larger body of water, individuals and communities must unite for a greater purpose.
  • tam aśyāma madhumantaṁ ta ūrmim: This can be interpreted as "That which is dark, sweet, and full of waves." The term "dark" may refer to the unknown or the mysteries of life, while "sweet" symbolizes the joys and pleasures that life offers. The "waves" represent the constant changes and movements in life, reminding us to embrace both the calm and turbulent times.

  • Significance: This mantra encapsulates the essence of the divine's omnipresence, the interconnectedness of life, and the duality of existence. It serves as a reminder that the divine is both within us and around us, urging us to seek unity and harmony in our lives.
  • Real-life Application: In our daily lives, this mantra encourages us to recognize the divine in every aspect of our existence. For example, in moments of conflict, we can remember the importance of unity and seek common ground. In times of uncertainty, we can find solace in the understanding that the divine is present within us, guiding us through challenges.
  • Conclusion: The mantra teaches us to appreciate the beauty of life, the importance of connection, and the presence of the divine in all things. By embodying these principles, we can lead more fulfilling lives, fostering peace and understanding in our communities.
