वसुर वसुपतिर हि कमस्य अग्ने विभावसुः |स्याम ते सुमताव अपि ||
vasur vasupatir hi kam asy agne vibhāvasuḥ | syāma te sumatāv api ||
  • Vasur: This term refers to wealth or abundance. In a broader sense, it signifies the various forms of prosperity that one can attain in life.
  • Vasupatir: This translates to the lord of wealth. It indicates a divine figure or force that governs prosperity and abundance.
  • Hi: This word is often used for emphasis, meaning "indeed" or "certainly." It reinforces the importance of the preceding terms.
  • Kam: This means "what" or "which," often used to inquire or express a desire for something.
  • Asy: This translates to "of this" or "of this one," referring to the divine or the energy being invoked.
  • Agne: This is a direct address to Agni, the fire god, who symbolizes energy, transformation, and the divine spark in all beings.
  • Vibhāvasuḥ: This term means "the one who shines brightly" or "the one who is radiant." It emphasizes the illuminating and transformative power of the divine.
  • Syāma: This means "may you be" or "let there be," indicating a wish or prayer for a certain state of being.
  • Te: This translates to "your," referring to the divine presence being addressed.
  • Sumatāv: This means "with good thoughts" or "with auspicious intentions," highlighting the importance of positive thinking and intentions in one's life.
  • Api: This word means "also" or "even," suggesting inclusivity in the prayer or wish being expressed.

Conclusion: The mantra "vasur vasupatir hi kam asy agne vibhāvasuḥ | syāma te sumatāv api ||" is a prayer for abundance and prosperity, invoking the divine energy of Agni. It emphasizes the importance of good thoughts and intentions in attracting wealth and success. In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as during financial endeavors, business ventures, or personal growth. By focusing on positive intentions and invoking divine support, individuals can align themselves with the energies of abundance and prosperity, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling and prosperous life.
