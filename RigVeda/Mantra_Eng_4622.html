यद वा दिवि पार्ये सुष्विम इन्द्र वृत्रहत्ये 'वसि शूरसातौ |यद वा दक्षस्य बिभ्युषो अबिभ्यद अरन्धयः शर्धता इन्द्र दस्यून ||
yad vā divi pārye suṣvim indra vṛtrahatye 'vasi śūrasātau | yad vā dakṣasya bibhyuṣo abibhyad arandhayaḥ śardhata indra dasyūn ||
  • Mantra: "yad vā divi pārye suṣvim indra vṛtrahatye 'vasi śūrasātau | yad vā dakṣasya bibhyuṣo abibhyad arandhayaḥ śardhata indra dasyūn ||"
  • Translation: This mantra invokes Indra, the king of gods, highlighting his prowess in overcoming obstacles and enemies. It speaks of his strength in battle and his ability to protect and empower those who are worthy.
  • Word Breakdown:
    • yad vā: "whatever" or "that which"
    • divi: "in the heavens" or "in the sky"
    • pārye: "in the midst" or "surrounded by"
    • suṣvim: "excellent" or "auspicious"
    • indra: "Indra," the king of gods
    • vṛtrahatye: "in the slaying of Vritra," a demon representing chaos and drought
    • 'vasi: "you who control" or "you who govern"
    • śūrasātau: "in the company of the brave"
    • dakṣasya: "of the skilled" or "of the capable"
    • bibhyuṣo: "who fears" or "who is afraid"
    • abibhyad: "did not fear"
    • arandhayaḥ: "the unyielding" or "the strong"
    • śardhata: "in unity" or "together"
    • dasyūn: "the enemies" or "the foes"
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • This mantra can be related to situations where individuals face challenges or adversities, such as in competitive environments like sports or business. Just as Indra overcomes Vritra, individuals can draw strength from this mantra to confront their own challenges.
    • In a community setting, when people unite against a common threat, they embody the essence of "śardhata" (togetherness) to overcome obstacles, similar to how Indra leads the brave in battle.
    • In personal development, the mantra serves as a reminder to cultivate courage and skill (dakṣasya) in the face of fear, encouraging individuals to act decisively rather than succumb to doubt.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for strength, unity, and courage in the face of adversity. It emphasizes the importance of overcoming fears and challenges through collective effort and individual bravery. In real-life scenarios, whether in personal struggles, community challenges, or competitive situations, this mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the potential within each individual to rise above difficulties and emerge victorious.
