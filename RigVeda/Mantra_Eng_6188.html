युश्मां उ नक्तम् ऊतये युश्मान दिवा हवामहे |युश्मान प्रयत्य अध्वरे ||
yuṣmām̐ u naktam ūtaye yuṣmān divā havāmahe | yuṣmān prayaty adhvare ||
  • yuṣmām̐ - This word translates to "to you" or "for you," indicating a direct address to a divine entity or a higher power.
  • u - This is a conjunction meaning "and," linking the thoughts in the mantra.
  • naktam - This means "night," symbolizing the time of darkness, often associated with challenges or ignorance.
  • ūtaye - This translates to "for the dawn" or "for the light," representing hope, knowledge, and the arrival of a new beginning.
  • yuṣmān - Again, this means "to you," reinforcing the personal address to the divine.
  • divā - This means "by day," symbolizing clarity, enlightenment, and the presence of the divine in our daily lives.
  • havāmahe - This translates to "we invoke" or "we call upon," indicating a plea or prayer for assistance or blessings.
  • prayaty - This means "to strive" or "to exert effort," emphasizing the importance of human effort in conjunction with divine support.
  • adhvare - This refers to "the sacrifice" or "the ritual," highlighting the significance of rituals in connecting with the divine.

The mantra can be interpreted as a prayer that acknowledges the transition from darkness (night) to light (dawn), invoking the divine presence both during the night and the day. It emphasizes the importance of striving and effort in our spiritual practices and daily lives.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios:

  • During challenging times, individuals can recite this mantra to seek guidance and strength, reminding themselves that after every night, a new dawn will come.
  • In moments of confusion or ignorance, this mantra serves as a reminder to seek knowledge and clarity, encouraging individuals to strive for enlightenment.
  • In the context of rituals, this mantra can be used to invoke blessings during ceremonies, reinforcing the connection between the divine and human efforts.

Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its ability to inspire hope and resilience. It teaches us that while we may face darkness in our lives, there is always the promise of light and new beginnings. By invoking the divine and putting in our efforts, we can navigate through challenges and emerge stronger, making it applicable in both spiritual and everyday contexts.
