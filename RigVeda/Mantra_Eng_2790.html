इमां ऊषु प्रभृतिं सातये धाः शाश्वत-शाश्वद ऊतिभिर यादमानः |सुत- सुत वावृद्धे वर्धनेभिर यः कर्मभिर महद्भिः सुश्रुतो भूत्वा ||
imām ū ṣu prabhṛtiṁ sātaye dhāḥ śaśvac-chaśvad ūtibhir yādamānaḥ | sute-sute vāvṛdhe vardhanebhir yaḥ karmabhir mahadbhiḥ suśruto bhūt ||
  • imām ū ṣu prabhṛtiṁ: This phrase can be translated as "this (knowledge) is the beginning." It signifies the initiation of a journey or understanding.
  • sātaye dhāḥ: This means "for the purpose of truth." It emphasizes the importance of seeking and adhering to truth in all endeavors.
  • śaśvac-chaśvad ūtibhir: This translates to "with eternal and everlasting strength." It suggests that the strength derived from truth and knowledge is enduring.
  • yādamānaḥ: This word means "being remembered." It indicates the significance of knowledge and actions that are worthy of remembrance.
  • sute-sute vāvṛdhe: This can be interpreted as "in every good deed, it flourishes." It highlights the idea that good actions lead to growth and prosperity.
  • vardhanebhir: This means "by those who nurture." It refers to the importance of nurturing and supporting good deeds and knowledge.
  • yaḥ karmabhir mahadbhiḥ: This translates to "those great actions." It emphasizes the value of significant and impactful actions in life.
  • suśruto bhūt: This means "well-heard and well-known." It signifies that the teachings and actions rooted in truth and goodness are recognized and respected.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of initiating a journey towards truth and knowledge, nurturing good deeds, and recognizing the significance of impactful actions. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios such as education, personal development, and community service. For instance, in a workplace, fostering a culture of honesty and integrity can lead to a more productive and harmonious environment. Similarly, in personal relationships, valuing truth and nurturing positive interactions can strengthen bonds and create lasting connections. Ultimately, the mantra serves as a reminder that our actions, rooted in truth and goodness, have the power to create a meaningful and lasting impact in the world.
