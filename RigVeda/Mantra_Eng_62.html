इन्द्र इद धर्योः सचा सम्मिश्ला आ वचोयुजा |इन्द्रो वज्री हिरण्ययः ||
indra id dharyoḥ sacā sammiśla ā vacoyujā | indro vajrī hiraṇyayaḥ ||
  • Indra: Indra is the king of the gods in Vedic mythology, often associated with thunder, rain, and war. He represents strength and power.
  • id: This word can be interpreted as "this" or "here," indicating a focus on the present moment or situation.
  • dharyoḥ: This term refers to "the one who holds" or "the one who supports," suggesting stability and strength.
  • sacā: This word means "with truth" or "with reality," emphasizing the importance of honesty and integrity.
  • sammiśla: This can be understood as "to unite" or "to bring together," indicating the importance of collaboration and harmony.
  • ā: A preposition that can mean "from" or "out of," often used to indicate a source or origin.
  • vacoyujā: This term translates to "with speech" or "with words," highlighting the power of communication and expression.
  • vajrī: This word means "the one who wields the thunderbolt," symbolizing strength and the ability to overcome obstacles.
  • hiraṇyayaḥ: This term refers to "golden" or "wealthy," often associated with prosperity and abundance.

  • Meaning: The mantra can be interpreted as a call to Indra, the powerful deity, to support and unite us with truth and effective communication. It emphasizes the importance of strength, collaboration, and integrity in our endeavors.

  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace setting, this mantra can be applied when teams come together to work on a project. The strength of collaboration (sammiśla) and honest communication (vacoyujā) can lead to successful outcomes.
    • In personal relationships, embodying the qualities of Indra—strength, truth, and unity—can help resolve conflicts and build stronger bonds.
    • In community initiatives, invoking the spirit of this mantra can inspire collective action towards a common goal, fostering a sense of belonging and shared purpose.

  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for strength, unity, and truthfulness in our actions and communications. It serves as a reminder that by embodying these qualities, we can overcome challenges and achieve prosperity in various aspects of life. Whether in personal relationships, professional environments, or community efforts, the principles encapsulated in this mantra are universally applicable and can lead to harmonious and successful outcomes.
