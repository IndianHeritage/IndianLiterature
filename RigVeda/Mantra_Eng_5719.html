यो ह स्य वां रथिरा वस्ता उष्रा रथो युजानाḥ पर्याती वर्तिः |तेन नः शं योर उषसो व्युष्टौ न्य अश्विनाः वहतं यज्ञे अस्मिन ||
yo ha sya vāṁ rathirā vasta usrā ratho yujānaḥ pariyāti vartiḥ | tena naḥ śaṁ yor uṣaso vyuṣṭau ny aśvinā vahataṁ yajñe asmin ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yo: who
    • ha: indeed
    • sya: is
    • vāṁ: your
    • rathirā: chariot
    • vasta: dwelling
    • usrā: bright
    • ratho: chariot
    • yujānaḥ: joined
    • pariyāti: goes around
    • vartiḥ: the one who moves
    • tena: by that
    • naḥ: our
    • śaṁ: peace
    • yor: of both
    • uṣaso: of the dawn
    • vyuṣṭau: in the light
    • ny: not
    • aśvinā: O Ashvins (the twin deities)
    • vahataṁ: carrying
    • yajñe: in the sacrifice
    • asmin: in this
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the divine charioteers, the Ashvins, who are known for their speed and healing abilities.
    • The chariot symbolizes the journey of life, where the soul is the driver, navigating through various experiences.
    • The phrase "by that" suggests that the divine guidance of the Ashvins is essential for achieving peace and harmony in our lives.
    • The mention of dawn signifies new beginnings, hope, and the illumination of knowledge.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In times of uncertainty or challenges, individuals often seek guidance, much like how the mantra seeks the help of the Ashvins for direction and support.
    • In a corporate setting, teams may invoke the spirit of collaboration and innovation, akin to the Ashvins working together to achieve a common goal.
    • During personal struggles, people may turn to meditation or prayer, seeking the light of dawn to bring clarity and peace, similar to the invocation of the mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of divine guidance in our lives, particularly during transitions and challenges.
    • It serves as a reminder to seek harmony and peace through collaboration and support from others, much like the Ashvins who assist in the journey of life.
    • In practical terms, it encourages individuals to embrace new beginnings with hope and to rely on the wisdom of others to navigate through life's complexities.
