एतं मे स्तोमं तना न सूर्ये द्युतद्यमानं वावृद्धन्त नृणाम् |संवननं नाश्व्यं तष्टेवानपच्युतम् ||
etam me stomaṁ tanā na sūrye dyutadyāmānaṁ vāvṛdhanta nṛṇām | saṁvananaṁ nāśvyaṁ taṣṭevānapacyutam ||
  • Translation: This mantra can be translated as: "This my hymn, which is not to be diminished, is shining like the sun, and it is increasing among men. It is a source of unity and is not to be lost."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • etam: This
    • me: my
    • stomaṁ: hymn or praise
    • tanā: body or essence
    • na: not
    • sūrye: in the sun
    • dyutadyāmānaṁ: shining or radiant
    • vāvṛdhanta: increasing or growing
    • nṛṇām: among men
    • saṁvananaṁ: source of unity
    • nāśvyaṁ: not to be lost
    • taṣṭe: to be established
    • vānapacyutam: not to be diminished
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of unity and collective growth. For instance, in a community project, when individuals come together with a shared vision, their collective efforts shine brightly, much like the sun.
    • In a corporate setting, a company's mission statement can be seen as a modern equivalent of this hymn. It serves to unite employees and guide their actions, ensuring that the company's values are not diminished over time.
    • In personal relationships, the essence of this mantra can be reflected in the commitment to nurture and grow the bond, ensuring that it remains strong and radiant, akin to the sun's light.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for unity, growth, and the preservation of essence. It teaches us that our collective efforts, whether in communities, workplaces, or personal relationships, should be aimed at fostering a radiant and enduring connection. By embodying the principles of this hymn, we can create environments where individuals thrive together, ensuring that our shared values and goals are never lost or diminished.
