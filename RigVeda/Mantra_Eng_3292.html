मां नो मर्दीर आ भरā दद्धि तन् नः प्र दाशुषे दातवे भूरे यत ते |नव्ये देश्णे शस्ते अस्मिन त उक्ते प्र ब्रवाम वयं इन्द्र स्तुवन्तः ||
mā no mardhīr ā bharā daddhi tan naḥ pra dāśuṣe dātave bhūri yat te | navye deṣṇe śaste asmin ta ukthe pra bravāma vayam indra stuvantaḥ ||
  • mā no mardhīr: This phrase translates to "May you not harm us." It expresses a plea for protection and safety. In real life, this can be likened to seeking safety in various situations, such as during natural disasters or conflicts.
  • ā bharā daddhi tan naḥ: This means "May you bestow upon us strength." It signifies a request for empowerment and resilience. For example, in challenging times, individuals often seek strength from their community or spiritual beliefs to overcome obstacles.
  • pra dāśuṣe dātave bhūri yat te: This translates to "For the one who gives abundantly." It acknowledges the generosity of a higher power or the universe. In everyday life, this can be seen in acts of kindness and charity, where people share their resources with those in need.
  • navye deṣṇe: This phrase means "In the new vision." It suggests looking forward to new possibilities and perspectives. In a practical sense, this can relate to innovation and adapting to change, such as embracing new technologies or ideas in a workplace.
  • śaste asmin ta ukthe: This translates to "In this auspicious moment." It emphasizes the importance of recognizing and seizing favorable opportunities. For instance, when one is presented with a job offer or a chance to pursue a passion, it is crucial to act decisively.
  • pra bravāma vayam indra stuvantaḥ: This means "We proclaim, we praise you, O Indra." It reflects the act of expressing gratitude and reverence towards a divine force or inspiration. In real life, this can be seen in various forms of worship, gratitude practices, or simply acknowledging the support one receives from others.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a deep yearning for protection, strength, and abundance while recognizing the auspicious moments in life. Its significance lies in the universal human experience of seeking safety, empowerment, and gratitude. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as during personal challenges, community support, and moments of celebration. By embodying the essence of this mantra, individuals can cultivate resilience, foster generosity, and appreciate the blessings in their lives.
