इन्द्रं वृत्राय हन्तवे देवासो दधिरे पुरः |इन्द्रं वाणीर अनूषतां सम ओजसे ||
indraṁ vṛtrāya hantave devāso dadhire puraḥ | indraṁ vāṇīr anūṣatā sam ojase ||
  • Indraṁ: Refers to Indra, the king of the gods in Vedic mythology, symbolizing strength, power, and leadership.
  • Vṛtrāya: Vṛtra is a demon or serpent that represents obstacles, chaos, or ignorance that hinders progress and prosperity.
  • Hantave: Means "to slay" or "to defeat," indicating the action of overcoming challenges.
  • Devāso: Refers to the gods or divine beings, emphasizing the collective support of divine forces.
  • Dadhire: Means "to uphold" or "to establish," suggesting the act of creating a foundation or support.
  • Puraḥ: Means "in front" or "before," indicating the presence of the divine in guiding actions.
  • Vāṇīr: Refers to the voice or speech, symbolizing communication and expression.
  • Anūṣatā: Means "with devotion" or "with respect," highlighting the importance of sincerity in actions.
  • Sam: Means "together" or "with unity," emphasizing the collective effort.
  • Ojase: Refers to "strength" or "energy," indicating the power that comes from unity and divine support.

This mantra can be interpreted as a call to Indra, seeking his strength and support in overcoming obstacles represented by Vṛtra. It emphasizes the importance of divine assistance and collective effort in achieving success. The invocation of Indra signifies the need for leadership and courage in facing challenges, while the mention of the gods highlights the role of divine forces in human endeavors.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as:

  • Leadership: In a corporate setting, a leader may invoke the qualities of Indra to inspire their team to overcome challenges and achieve goals.
  • Community Efforts: In community projects, the collective effort of individuals can be likened to the divine support mentioned in the mantra, where unity leads to success.
  • Personal Challenges: Individuals facing personal obstacles can recite this mantra as a reminder to seek strength and guidance from higher powers and to work together with others for support.

Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its reminder of the power of divine support and collective effort in overcoming life's challenges. It encourages individuals to seek strength from higher forces and to work together harmoniously to achieve their goals. By embodying the qualities of Indra, one can navigate obstacles with courage and determination, ultimately leading to success and prosperity.
