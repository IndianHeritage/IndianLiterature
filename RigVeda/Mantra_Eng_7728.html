अभ्य आर्षाणमपच्युतो रयिं समत्सु सासहिḥ |अथा नो वस्यसस कृधि ||
abhy a1rṣānapacyuto rayiṁ samatsu sāsahiḥ | athā no vasyasas kṛdhi ||
  • Mantra: "abhy a1rṣānapacyuto rayiṁ samatsu sāsahiḥ | athā no vasyasas kṛdhi ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • abhy: towards, in the direction of
    • a1rṣāna: of the seers or sages
    • apacyuto: not falling away, not deviating
    • rayiṁ: wealth, prosperity, or abundance
    • samatsu: in harmony, among the good
    • sāsahiḥ: with strength, with power
    • athā: therefore, thus
    • no: our
    • vasyasas: of those who are controlled, of those who are subdued
    • kṛdhi: make, create, or establish
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the blessings of the sages and seers, emphasizing the importance of wisdom and guidance in achieving prosperity.
    • The phrase "not falling away" suggests a steadfastness in purpose and intention, which is crucial in both personal and professional life.
    • Wealth and prosperity are sought in a harmonious environment, indicating that collaboration and unity are essential for success.
    • The call to "make our controlled" implies a desire for stability and order in our lives, which can be applied to various aspects such as family, work, and community.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, this mantra can be reflected in team dynamics where collaboration leads to shared success, emphasizing the need for unity and collective effort.
    • In personal relationships, the mantra highlights the importance of maintaining harmony and understanding, which can lead to a prosperous and fulfilling life.
    • In community initiatives, the call for strength and control can be seen in efforts to create organized and supportive environments that foster growth and development.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the significance of wisdom, unity, and strength in achieving prosperity.
    • It encourages individuals to seek guidance from wise sources and to work harmoniously with others to create a stable and prosperous life.
    • In essence, it teaches that true wealth is not just material but also encompasses relationships, community, and personal growth.
