वयं उ त्वा पथस पते रथम न वाजसातये |धिये पूषन्न अयुज्महि ||
vayam u tvā pathas pate rathaṁ na vājasātaye | dhiye pūṣann ayujmahi ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • vayam: We
    • u: indeed
    • tvā: you
    • pathas: of the path
    • pate: O Lord
    • rathaṁ: the chariot
    • na: not
    • vājasātaye: for the purpose of strength or power
    • dhiye: in our intellect
    • pūṣann: O nourisher
    • ayujmahi: we invoke or we unite
  • Overall Meaning:
    • This mantra expresses a prayer for guidance and strength.
    • The speaker is addressing a divine entity, asking for support on the path of life.
    • It emphasizes the importance of intellect and nourishment in achieving one's goals.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, when embarking on a new project, teams often seek guidance and clarity to ensure they are on the right path. This mantra can be likened to a team meeting where members align their goals and seek collective wisdom.
    • In personal life, individuals may face challenges that require them to seek advice from mentors or spiritual leaders, similar to invoking the divine for support and direction.
  • Significance and Application:
    • This mantra highlights the importance of seeking divine guidance in our endeavors.
    • It teaches us to value intellect and nourishment, both physically and mentally, as we navigate through life's challenges.
    • In modern life, this can be applied in various scenarios such as decision-making, leadership, and personal growth, reminding us to seek wisdom and support from higher powers or collective knowledge.
