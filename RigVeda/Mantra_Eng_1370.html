मां साते अस्मत्सुमतिर् विदसद् वाजप्रमहः समिषो वरन्त || आ नो भज मघवन गोष्व् आर्यो मंहिष्ठास् ते सद्हमादः स्याम ||
mā sā te asmat sumatir vi dasad vājapramahaḥ sam iṣo varanta | ā no bhaja maghavan goṣv aryo maṁhiṣṭhās te sadhamādaḥ syāma ||
  • mā sā te asmat sumatir vi dasad vājapramahaḥ sam iṣo varanta
    • mā sā te: This phrase can be interpreted as "may that not be for us." It expresses a desire to avoid negativity or obstacles.
    • asmat: This means "from us" or "our," indicating a collective identity or community.
    • sumatir: Translated as "good thought" or "wisdom," it emphasizes the importance of positive thinking and intelligence.
    • vi dasad: This can mean "to be far away" or "to be distant," suggesting a wish to keep harmful influences at bay.
    • vājapramahaḥ: This term refers to "the one who is powerful in strength" or "the one who is victorious." It signifies the importance of strength and victory in life.
    • sam iṣo varanta: This phrase can be understood as "may we be united in our desires." It highlights the significance of unity and shared goals.
  • ā no bhaja maghavan goṣv aryo maṁhiṣṭhās te sadhamādaḥ syāma
    • ā no bhaja: This translates to "may we be blessed" or "may we receive." It expresses a desire for divine blessings and support.
    • maghavan: Referring to "the generous one" or "the one who bestows wealth," it signifies abundance and prosperity.
    • goṣv: This term means "in the herds" or "among the cattle," which can symbolize wealth and sustenance in agrarian societies.
    • aryo: This can be interpreted as "noble" or "worthy," indicating a call for high moral standards and integrity.
    • maṁhiṣṭhās: This means "the best" or "the most excellent," emphasizing the pursuit of excellence in all endeavors.
    • te sadhamādaḥ syāma: This phrase translates to "may we be in harmony with you," highlighting the importance of maintaining good relationships and harmony with others.

Conclusion:

The mantra emphasizes the significance of positive thinking, unity, and the pursuit of excellence while seeking divine blessings and prosperity. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various contexts such as:

  • In personal development, fostering a positive mindset can lead to better decision-making and overall well-being.
  • In teamwork and collaboration, the mantra encourages individuals to work together harmoniously towards common goals.
  • In community building, it highlights the importance of supporting one another and striving for collective prosperity.

Overall, this mantra serves as a reminder of the values of wisdom, strength, and unity, which are essential for personal and communal growth.
