अवोऽ इथ्था वां छर्दिषो अभिष्टौ युवोर मित्रावरुणाव अस्कृधोयु |अनु यद गावाḥ स्फुरान ऋजिप्यं धृष्णुं यद रणे वृषणं युनजन ||
avor itthā vāṁ chardiṣo abhiṣṭau yuvor mitrāvaruṇāv askṛdhoyu | anu yad gāvaḥ sphurān ṛjipyaṁ dhṛṣṇuṁ yad raṇe vṛṣaṇaṁ yunajan ||
  • Mantra: "avor itthā vāṁ chardiṣo abhiṣṭau yuvor mitrāvaruṇāv askṛdhoyu | anu yad gāvaḥ sphurān ṛjipyaṁ dhṛṣṇuṁ yad raṇe vṛṣaṇaṁ yunajan ||"
  • Translation: This mantra can be interpreted as a call for the blessings of the divine forces, specifically Mitra and Varuna, to bestow strength and protection in times of conflict and to ensure the prosperity of cattle and resources.
  • Word Breakdown:
    • avor: a call or invocation
    • itthā: thus or in this manner
    • vāṁ: your (plural)
    • chardiṣo: to the directions or realms
    • abhiṣṭau: blessings or boons
    • yuvor: of the two (referring to Mitra and Varuna)
    • mitrāvaruṇāv: Mitra and Varuna, the deities of friendship and cosmic order
    • askṛdhoyu: may they protect or guard
    • anu: following or in accordance with
    • yad: that which
    • gāvaḥ: cattle or wealth
    • sphurān: may they flourish or thrive
    • ṛjipyaṁ: in righteousness or truth
    • dhṛṣṇuṁ: strength or courage
    • yad: that which
    • raṇe: in battle or conflict
    • vṛṣaṇaṁ: the strong or powerful
    • yunajan: may they unite or come together
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • This mantra can be recited by farmers seeking blessings for their livestock, ensuring that their cattle thrive and produce well, which is crucial for their livelihood.
    • In a corporate setting, teams can invoke this mantra for unity and strength in facing challenges, promoting collaboration and resilience among team members.
    • During times of conflict, such as in community disputes or even in personal relationships, this mantra serves as a reminder to seek divine guidance for resolution and harmony.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its invocation of divine protection and prosperity. It emphasizes the importance of unity, strength, and righteousness in both personal and communal endeavors. In real-life scenarios, it can be applied in various contexts, from agriculture to corporate teamwork, and even in resolving conflicts, reminding us of the need for divine support in our pursuits and the importance of maintaining harmony and strength in our relationships.
