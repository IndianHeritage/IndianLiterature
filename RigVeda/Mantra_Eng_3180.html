आभिषṭे अद्य गीरभिर गृणन्तो 'अग्नि दाशेम |प्र ते दिवो न स्तनयन्ति शुष्माः ||
ābhiṣ ṭe adya gīrbhir gṛṇanto 'gne dāśema | pra te divo na stanayanti śuṣmāḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ābhiṣṭe: To invoke or call upon.
    • adya: Today.
    • gīrbhir: With hymns or songs.
    • gṛṇanto: Singing or praising.
    • agne: O Fire (Agni, the deity of fire).
    • dāśema: May we attain or achieve.
    • pra: For or towards.
    • te: Your.
    • divo: Of the heavens.
    • na: Not.
    • stanayanti: They who support or uphold.
    • śuṣmāḥ: The radiant or luminous ones.
  • Overall Meaning:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Agni, the fire god, asking for his blessings and support.
    • It emphasizes the importance of invoking divine energies through hymns and praises.
    • The mantra expresses a desire to achieve prosperity and well-being through the grace of Agni.
    • It acknowledges the celestial beings that support and uphold the universe.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern rituals, lighting a fire during ceremonies symbolizes the presence of Agni, representing purity and the divine.
    • People often sing hymns or mantras during significant life events, such as weddings or housewarming ceremonies, to invoke blessings.
    • In daily life, individuals may seek to achieve their goals by invoking positive energies through meditation or prayer, similar to the invocation in this mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of invoking divine energies for support and guidance in our lives.
    • It teaches us the value of gratitude and recognition of the forces that sustain us, both seen and unseen.
    • In a broader context, it encourages individuals to seek harmony and prosperity through spiritual practices, fostering a connection with the divine.
