न ता नशन्ति न दभाति तस्करो नासाम अमित्रो व्यथिर आ दधर्षति |देवाम्श च याभिर यजते ददाति च ज्योग इत ताभिः सचते गोपतिः सह ||
na tā naśanti na dabhāti taskaro nāsām āmitro vyathir ā dadharṣati | devām̐ś ca yābhir yajate dadāti ca jyog it tābhiḥ sacate gopatiḥ saha ||
  • na tā naśanti: This phrase translates to "they do not perish." It signifies the eternal nature of certain truths or principles that remain unaffected by time or circumstances.
  • na dabhāti taskaro: This means "the thief does not harm." It suggests that true wealth or spiritual riches cannot be taken away by external forces, such as theft or deceit.
  • nāsām āmitro vyathir ā dadharṣati: This translates to "the friend does not cause distress." It emphasizes the importance of genuine friendship and support in life, where true friends uplift rather than bring sorrow.
  • devām̐ś ca yābhir yajate: This means "those who worship the divine." It highlights the significance of devotion and the act of worship in connecting with higher powers or spiritual truths.
  • dadāti ca jyog it tābhiḥ: This translates to "they give and are united." It signifies the idea of sharing and community, where individuals come together in harmony and mutual support.
  • sacate gopatiḥ saha: This means "the lord of the cows (gopati) resides with them." In a broader sense, it refers to the divine presence or blessings that accompany those who live in accordance with these principles.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates profound truths about the nature of existence, relationships, and spirituality. It teaches that true wealth and security come from within and are not subject to external circumstances. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios, such as fostering genuine friendships, engaging in acts of kindness, and maintaining a spiritual practice. By embodying these principles, individuals can cultivate a life of peace, fulfillment, and connection with the divine. The significance of this mantra lies in its ability to guide individuals towards a harmonious existence, emphasizing the importance of inner strength, community, and spiritual devotion.
