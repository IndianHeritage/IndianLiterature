विषूचो अश्वान युजुजे वनेजा ऋजीतिभी रशनाभिर गृभीतान् |चक्षदे मित्रो वसुभिः सुजातः सम आनृधे पर्वभिर वावृद्धानः ||
viṣūco aśvān yuyuje vanejā ṛjītibhī raśanābhir gṛbhītān | cakṣade mitro vasubhiḥ sujātaḥ sam ānṛdhe parvabhir vāvṛdhānaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • viṣūco: Of the swift ones, often referring to the horses.
    • aśvān: Horses, symbolizing strength and speed.
    • yuyuje: To unite or harness.
    • vanejā: In the forest or wilderness.
    • ṛjītibhī: With the help of the righteous or the just.
    • raśanābhir: With the ropes or bonds.
    • gṛbhītān: Having been seized or taken.
    • cakṣade: May he see or perceive.
    • mitro: Friend or ally.
    • vasubhiḥ: With the wealth or resources.
    • sujātaḥ: Well-born or noble.
    • sam: Together or in harmony.
    • ānṛdhe: Without deceit or falsehood.
    • parvabhir: With the mountains or peaks.
    • vāvṛdhānaḥ: May he increase or grow.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the unity and strength derived from righteous actions and the bonds of friendship.
    • The imagery of horses being harnessed in the wilderness symbolizes the potential and energy that can be directed towards a noble cause.
    • The mention of "mitro" and "vasubhiḥ" emphasizes the importance of alliances and resources in achieving goals.
    • It suggests that when we act with integrity and in harmony with others, we can achieve great heights, akin to mountains.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a team project, the collaboration of diverse skills (like the horses) can lead to successful outcomes when everyone works together (yuyuje).
    • In community service, individuals coming together for a righteous cause can create significant positive change, reflecting the essence of "ṛjītibhī."
    • In business, forming partnerships based on trust and shared values can lead to mutual growth and success, akin to "sujātaḥ sam ānṛdhe."
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of unity, integrity, and collaboration in achieving success.
    • It serves as a reminder that when we harness our strengths and work together without deceit, we can overcome challenges and reach new heights.
    • In real life, whether in personal relationships, professional settings, or community efforts, the principles embodied in this mantra can guide us towards meaningful accomplishments.
