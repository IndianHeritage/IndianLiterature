प्रत्याग्निरुषसश्चेकितानोऽबोधिविप्रः पदवीः कवीनाम् |पृतुपाजादेवयद्भिः समिद्धोऽप द्वारातमसो वह्निरावः ||
praty agnir uṣasaś cekitāno 'bodhi vipraḥ padavīḥ kavīnām | pṛthupājā devayadbhiḥ samiddho 'pa dvārā tamaso vahnir āvaḥ ||
  • Mantra: "praty agnir uṣasaś cekitāno 'bodhi vipraḥ padavīḥ kavīnām | pṛthupājā devayadbhiḥ samiddho 'pa dvārā tamaso vahnir āvaḥ ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • praty: towards, in the direction of
    • agnir: fire
    • uṣasaś: of the dawn
    • cekitāno: the one who is aware, knowledgeable
    • 'bodhi: to awaken, to enlighten
    • vipraḥ: the wise, the learned
    • padavīḥ: paths, ways
    • kavīnām: of the poets, the seers
    • pṛthupājā: well-off, prosperous
    • devayadbhiḥ: by the divine beings
    • samiddho: kindled, ignited
    • 'pa: may you come
    • dvārā: through the doors
    • tamaso: of darkness
    • vahnir: fire
    • āvaḥ: may you come, may you be present
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra invokes the fire of knowledge and enlightenment, symbolized by "agnir" and "uṣasaś," representing the dawn of understanding.
    • The phrase "vipraḥ padavīḥ kavīnām" emphasizes the importance of wise individuals and poets who illuminate the paths of life with their insights.
    • The invocation of "pṛthupājā devayadbhiḥ" suggests that prosperity and divine blessings are sought through the kindling of this inner fire.
    • By asking "tamaso vahnir āvaḥ," the mantra seeks to dispel darkness and ignorance, inviting the light of knowledge and wisdom into one’s life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a personal context, this mantra can be applied when one is seeking clarity in decision-making. For instance, a student preparing for exams may recite this mantra to invoke focus and understanding.
    • In a professional setting, leaders can use the essence of this mantra to inspire their teams, encouraging them to seek knowledge and innovative solutions to challenges.
    • In community gatherings, this mantra can serve as a reminder to foster wisdom and enlightenment among members, promoting discussions that lead to collective growth.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for enlightenment and the dispelling of ignorance through the fire of knowledge.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal growth to professional development and community engagement.
    • By embracing the teachings of this mantra, individuals can cultivate a deeper understanding of themselves and the world around them, leading to a more enlightened and prosperous life.
