प्र हि रिरिक्ष ओजसा दिवो अन्तेभ्यस् परि |न त्वा विव्याच रज इन्द्र पार्थिवम् अनु स्वधां ववक्षिथ ||
pra hi ririkṣa ojasā divo antebhyas pari | na tvā vivyāca raja indra pārthivam anu svadhāṁ vavakṣitha ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • pra hi: indeed, truly
    • ririkṣa: to desire, to seek
    • ojasā: with strength, with energy
    • dīvo: of the heavens
    • antebhyas: from the end, from the limits
    • pari: around, in all directions
    • na: not
    • tvā: you
    • vivyāca: to reveal, to disclose
    • raja: the atmosphere, the sky
    • indra: O Indra, the king of gods
    • pārthivam: earthly, pertaining to the earth
    • anu: following, in accordance with
    • svadhāṁ: self, one's own
    • vavakṣitha: you have spoken, you have declared
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra expresses a deep yearning for strength and energy that comes from the heavens.
    • It emphasizes the importance of seeking divine support and guidance in all endeavors.
    • The phrase "not you have revealed" suggests that there are truths or energies that remain hidden, waiting to be discovered.
    • Indra, as the king of gods, symbolizes power and authority, indicating that divine intervention is crucial for success.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a challenging situation, such as preparing for an important exam, students often seek strength and clarity from their spiritual beliefs, akin to invoking the energy of the heavens.
    • In leadership roles, individuals may look to divine guidance to make ethical decisions, reflecting the need for higher wisdom in earthly matters.
    • During times of uncertainty, people may engage in meditation or prayer, seeking to connect with a higher power for strength and direction.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of seeking divine strength and guidance in our lives.
    • It reminds us that while we strive for success, there are greater forces at play that can aid us in our journey.
    • In practical terms, it encourages individuals to remain open to spiritual insights and support, especially in times of need.
    • Ultimately, it teaches us the value of humility and the recognition that we are part of a larger cosmic order.
