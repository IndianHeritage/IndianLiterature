पुनर एहि वृषाकपे सुविता कल्पयावहै |य एष स्वप्ननंशनोऽस्तम एषि पथā पुनर विश्वस्माद इन्द्र उत्तरः ||
punar ehi vṛṣākape suvitā kalpayāvahai | ya eṣa svapnanaṁśano 'stam eṣi pathā punar viśvasmād indra uttaraḥ ||
  • Mantra: "punar ehi vṛṣākape suvitā kalpayāvahai |
  • Translation: "Come again, O Vṛṣākape, with your well-prepared offerings."
  • Meaning of 'punar ehi': The phrase 'punar ehi' translates to 'come again' or 'return'. This signifies a call for renewal or a return of blessings.
  • Meaning of 'vṛṣākape': 'Vṛṣākape' refers to a deity associated with strength and power, often linked to Indra, the king of gods in Vedic literature. This can symbolize the call for divine intervention or support.
  • Meaning of 'suvitā kalpayāvahai': 'Suvitā' means 'well-prepared' or 'auspicious', and 'kalpayāvahai' refers to the act of bestowing or offering. Together, this suggests a plea for the divine to bring forth well-prepared blessings or prosperity.
  • Meaning of 'ya eṣa svapnanaṁśano': This part translates to 'who is the one that consumes dreams'. It can be interpreted as a reference to the divine power that influences our aspirations and dreams.
  • Meaning of 'astam eṣi pathā punar viśvasmād indra uttaraḥ': This translates to 'as the sun sets, may you return again, O Indra, from the path of the universe'. It signifies the cyclical nature of life and the hope for the return of light and blessings after darkness.

  • Real-Life Example: In daily life, this mantra can be related to the cycles of challenges and triumphs we face. For instance, after a period of struggle, one may seek renewal in their career or personal life, invoking the strength and support of higher powers or the universe.
  • Application: This mantra can be recited during times of transition, such as starting a new job, moving to a new place, or embarking on a new project. It serves as a reminder to seek divine guidance and to prepare oneself for the blessings that may come.

  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for renewal, strength, and divine support. It emphasizes the importance of being well-prepared for the blessings we seek and acknowledges the cyclical nature of life. In real-life scenarios, it can inspire individuals to remain hopeful and proactive during challenging times, trusting that support and prosperity will return.
