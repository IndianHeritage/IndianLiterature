वयं अग्नि वानुयाम तवोतā वसūयवो हविषा बुद्ध्यमानाः |वयं समर्ये विदथेष्व अह्नां वयं राया सहसस् पुत्र मर्तान ||
vayam agne vanuyāma tvotā vasūyavo haviṣā budhyamānāḥ | vayaṁ samarye vidatheṣv ahnāṁ vayaṁ rāyā sahasas putra martān ||
  • vayam agne: This phrase translates to "we, O Agni (the fire god)". Agni is often seen as a divine force that represents energy, transformation, and purification.
  • vanuyāma: This means "we seek" or "we desire". It indicates a longing or aspiration towards something greater.
  • tvotā: This translates to "your praise" or "your worship". It signifies the act of honoring and acknowledging the divine.
  • vasūyavo: This word means "wealthy" or "prosperous". It reflects the desire for abundance and prosperity in life.
  • haviṣā: This refers to "offerings" or "sacrifices". In the context of the mantra, it suggests the importance of giving and sharing.
  • budhyamānāḥ: This translates to "being aware" or "being enlightened". It emphasizes the importance of knowledge and consciousness in our actions.
  • vayaṁ samarye: This means "we are united" or "we come together". It highlights the significance of community and collaboration.
  • vidatheṣv ahnāṁ: This translates to "in the assemblies of the day". It signifies the importance of gathering and sharing wisdom during the day.
  • vayaṁ rāyā: This means "we seek wealth" or "we desire prosperity". It reflects the aspiration for success and abundance.
  • sahasas: This translates to "with strength" or "with vigor". It emphasizes the need for energy and determination in achieving our goals.
  • putra martān: This means "sons of the mortal" or "children of humanity". It signifies our connection to the human experience and the legacy we leave behind.

Conclusion: This mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking divine guidance and prosperity through collective effort and awareness. It emphasizes the importance of community, knowledge, and the pursuit of wealth in a righteous manner. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios such as teamwork in professional settings, community service, and family gatherings where individuals come together to support one another and strive for common goals. The mantra serves as a reminder that through unity, awareness, and respect for the divine, we can achieve abundance and fulfillment in our lives.
