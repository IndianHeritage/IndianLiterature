आ शर्म पर्वतानाम् ओतापां वृणीमहे |द्यावाक्षामारे अस्मद् रपस कृतम् ||
ā śarma parvatānām otāpāṁ vṛṇīmahe | dyāvākṣāmāre asmad rapas kṛtam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā: from
    • śarma: comfort, joy, or protection
    • parvatānām: of the mountains
    • otāpāṁ: the heights or peaks
    • vṛṇīmahe: we choose or we invoke
    • dyāvākṣāmāre: the sky and the earth
    • asmad: our
    • rapas: strength or energy
    • kṛtam: created or made
  • Overall Meaning: This mantra can be interpreted as a prayer or invocation for the protection and comfort that comes from the mountains, as well as a call to the sky and earth for strength and energy that has been created for us. It emphasizes the connection between nature and human existence, highlighting the importance of seeking refuge in the natural world.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • Nature as a Source of Comfort: Many people find solace in nature, especially in mountainous regions. For instance, hikers often seek the tranquility of the mountains to escape the stresses of daily life, reflecting the essence of seeking comfort from the peaks.
    • Environmental Awareness: The mantra can also be seen as a reminder of our responsibility to protect the environment. By invoking the strength of the earth and sky, we are encouraged to engage in sustainable practices that honor the natural world.
    • Spiritual Retreats: Many spiritual retreats are held in mountainous areas, where individuals seek to connect with nature and find inner peace. This aligns with the idea of invoking the protective and comforting aspects of the mountains.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for harmony between humanity and nature. It serves as a reminder that we can find strength and comfort in the natural world, and it encourages us to seek refuge in the mountains while also recognizing our role in preserving the environment. In real life, this mantra can inspire individuals to engage in outdoor activities, promote environmental stewardship, and seek mental and spiritual rejuvenation through nature.
