अर्वाञ्चम् अद्य यय्यं नृवाहणं रथं युञ्जाथाम इह वां विमोचनम् |पृङ्क्तं हविंषि मधुनā हि कं गतम् अथā सोमं पिबतं वाजिनीवासू ||
arvāñcam adya yayyaṁ nṛvāhaṇaṁ rathaṁ yuñjāthām iha vāṁ vimocanam | pṛṅktaṁ havīṁṣi madhunā hi kaṁ gatam athā somam pibataṁ vājinīvasū ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • arvāñcam: The one who is swift or quick.
    • adya: Today or in the present moment.
    • yayyaṁ: To whom or for whom.
    • nṛvāhaṇaṁ: The charioteer or the one who drives the chariot.
    • rathaṁ: Chariot.
    • yuñjāthām: May you unite or connect.
    • iha: Here or in this place.
    • vāṁ: Your or you all.
    • vimocanam: Liberation or release.
    • pṛṅktaṁ: The offerings or the oblations.
    • havīṁṣi: The sacred offerings.
    • madhunā: With sweetness or with honey.
    • hi: Indeed or surely.
    • kaṁ: Who or whom.
    • gatam: Gone or departed.
    • athā: Then or thereafter.
    • somam: The soma, a sacred drink.
    • pibataṁ: Drink or consume.
    • vājinīvasū: The best of the strong or the powerful.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the swift charioteer, asking for unity and liberation in the present moment.
    • It emphasizes the importance of offerings and the sweetness of life, symbolized by honey.
    • The reference to soma suggests a connection to divine energy and vitality, encouraging the consumption of this sacred drink for strength.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a competitive environment, such as a sports event, athletes may invoke this mantra to seek unity and strength among their team members, aiming for victory.
    • During a challenging project at work, a team can use the essence of this mantra to foster collaboration and support, ensuring that everyone contributes to the common goal.
    • In personal life, individuals may reflect on this mantra during meditation to seek liberation from stress and to embrace the sweetness of life.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of unity, strength, and the pursuit of liberation in various aspects of life.
    • It is applicable in real-life scenarios where teamwork, collaboration, and the celebration of life's sweetness are essential for success and fulfillment.
    • By embodying the principles of this mantra, individuals and groups can navigate challenges more effectively and achieve their goals with a sense of purpose and harmony.
