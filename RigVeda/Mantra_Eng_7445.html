नू मे गिरो नासत्याश्विनाऽ प्रावतम् युवम् |मध्वः सोमस्य पीतये ||
nū me giro nāsatyāśvinā prāvataṁ yuvam | madhvaḥ somasya pītaye ||
  • Translation: "O Nāsatyas, may my words reach you, the youthful ones; may they be sweet for the consumption of the Soma."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • nū: A call or invocation, often used to express urgency or a plea.
    • me: "my," indicating possession or personal connection.
    • giro: "words" or "speech," referring to the power of communication.
    • nāsatya: Referring to the Nāsatyas, the twin deities associated with healing and truth.
    • āśvinā: "youthful ones," highlighting the vitality and energy of the deities.
    • prāvataṁ: "may reach" or "may come to," indicating a desire for connection.
    • yuvam: "you," addressing the deities directly.
    • madhvaḥ: "sweet" or "pleasant," often used to describe something desirable.
    • somasya: "of Soma," referring to the sacred drink that symbolizes immortality and divine connection.
    • pītaye: "for drinking," indicating the act of consuming or partaking.
  • Contextual Significance:
    • The mantra invokes the Nāsatyas, who are often seen as divine healers and bringers of truth. This reflects the human desire for healing and clarity in life.
    • The reference to "words" emphasizes the importance of communication in seeking divine guidance and support. In real life, effective communication can lead to better relationships and understanding.
    • The mention of "Soma" symbolizes the quest for spiritual nourishment and enlightenment. Just as Soma is consumed for its divine qualities, individuals seek experiences that elevate their consciousness.
  • Real-Life Application:
    • In personal development, this mantra can inspire individuals to seek clarity in their thoughts and words, promoting honest communication in relationships.
    • In health and wellness, invoking the Nāsatyas can serve as a reminder to seek healing, whether through physical, emotional, or spiritual means.
    • In spiritual practices, the mantra encourages individuals to connect with the divine, seeking guidance and nourishment for the soul.
  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking divine connection through words and the pursuit of sweetness in life. It highlights the importance of communication, healing, and spiritual nourishment, making it relevant in various aspects of modern life, from personal relationships to spiritual growth.
