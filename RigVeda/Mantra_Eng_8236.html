आ ते दक्षं मयोभुवं वह्निम अद्य वृणीमहे |पान्तम आ पुरुष्पृहं ||
ā te dakṣam mayobhuvaṁ vahnim adyā vṛṇīmahe | pāntam ā puruspṛham ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā te: From you
    • dakṣam: Skilled or capable
    • mayobhuvaṁ: May I be endowed with strength or energy
    • vahnim: Fire
    • adyā: Today
    • vṛṇīmahe: We choose or invoke
    • pāntam: The one who protects or nourishes
    • ā: From
    • puruspṛham: The one who is full of vigor or strength
  • Overall Meaning: This mantra is a prayer for the invocation of divine energy and strength. The speaker is seeking the blessings of a powerful force (often interpreted as Agni, the fire god) to bestow upon them the capability and vigor needed to achieve their goals. It emphasizes the importance of divine support in human endeavors.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • Personal Development: When someone embarks on a journey of self-improvement, they often seek motivation and strength. This mantra can be a reminder to invoke inner strength and capabilities to overcome challenges.
    • Professional Aspirations: In a work environment, individuals may face obstacles in achieving their career goals. Reciting this mantra can serve as a source of inspiration to harness their skills and seek divine guidance in their professional journey.
    • Health and Wellness: When dealing with health issues, individuals may pray for strength and resilience. This mantra can be a way to invoke the energy needed to heal and maintain well-being.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine assistance in cultivating strength and capability. It serves as a reminder that while personal effort is essential, seeking support from a higher power can enhance one's ability to navigate life's challenges. In various real-life scenarios, whether in personal growth, professional endeavors, or health, this mantra encourages individuals to recognize their potential and the importance of divine energy in achieving their aspirations.
