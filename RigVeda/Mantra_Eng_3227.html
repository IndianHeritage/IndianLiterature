यासि कुत्सेन सारथम् अवश्युस् तोडो वातस्य हर्योः ईशानः |ऋज्रā वाजं न गध्यं युयूषन् कविर यद अहन् पार्याय भूषात् ||
yāsi kutsena saratham avasyus todo vātasya haryor īśānaḥ | ṛjrā vājaṁ na gadhyaṁ yuyūṣan kavir yad ahan pāryāya bhūṣāt ||
  • yāsi kutsena: This phrase can be interpreted as "you who go with a purpose" or "you who travel with intent." It emphasizes the importance of having a clear goal or direction in life.
  • saratham avasyus: This translates to "with the chariot of necessity." It suggests that one must navigate through life using the tools and resources available, akin to how a chariot is used for travel.
  • todo vātasya haryor: This can be understood as "the wind of the two horses." In a metaphorical sense, it represents the dual forces or energies in life that propel us forward, much like how horses pull a chariot.
  • īśānaḥ: This word means "the lord" or "the ruler." It signifies the divine or higher power that guides our journey.
  • ṛjrā vājaṁ: This phrase translates to "the swift chariot." It symbolizes the speed and efficiency with which we should pursue our goals.
  • na gadhyaṁ: This means "not to be hindered." It encourages us to overcome obstacles and challenges that may come our way.
  • yuyūṣan kavir: This can be interpreted as "the wise one who desires." It highlights the importance of wisdom and intention in our pursuits.
  • yad ahan pāryāya bhūṣāt: This translates to "what I have achieved through effort." It emphasizes the value of hard work and perseverance in attaining success.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of purposeful living, emphasizing the importance of direction, resources, divine guidance, and the pursuit of goals with wisdom and determination. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios such as career planning, personal development, and overcoming challenges. For instance, when embarking on a new project, having a clear goal (yāsi kutsena) and utilizing available resources (saratham avasyus) can lead to success. The mantra serves as a reminder that with the right mindset and effort, one can navigate through life's journey effectively, achieving their aspirations while remaining resilient against obstacles.
