तव ब्रह्मास आश्यया पतन्त्य अनु स्पृश धृतता शोशुचानः |तपुंष्य अग्ने जुह्वा पतङ्गान असंनिदितो वि सृजा विश्वग उल्काः ||
tava bhramāsa āśuyā patanty anu spṛśa dhṛṣatā śośucānaḥ | tapūṁṣy agne juhvā pataṁgān asaṁdito vi sṛja viṣvag ulkāḥ ||
  • tava bhramāsa āśuyā: This phrase can be translated as "by your divine power" or "through your creative energy." It signifies the immense strength and influence of the divine, particularly in the context of Agni, the fire god.
  • patanty anu spṛśa: This means "they fall and touch." It suggests the idea of something descending or manifesting in the physical realm, indicating the connection between the divine and the earthly.
  • dhṛṣatā śośucānaḥ: This can be interpreted as "with courage and purity." It emphasizes the qualities of bravery and cleanliness, both physical and spiritual, that are essential for those who seek to connect with the divine.
  • tapūṁṣy agne: This translates to "O Agni, the one who is the source of heat." Agni is often seen as a purifying force, representing transformation and the energy that fuels life.
  • juhvā pataṁgān: This phrase means "offering the winged ones." It symbolizes the act of sacrifice or offering, which is a central theme in Vedic rituals, where offerings are made to the gods to seek their blessings.
  • asaṁdito vi sṛja: This can be understood as "create without hesitation." It encourages the idea of manifesting one's desires and intentions boldly and without fear.
  • viṣvag ulkāḥ: This translates to "the universal sparks." It signifies the interconnectedness of all beings and the divine energy that flows through everything in the universe.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda highlights the relationship between the divine and the material world, emphasizing the importance of courage, purity, and the act of offering. In real life, it can be applied in various scenarios, such as in personal growth, where one must harness their inner strength and purity to manifest their goals. It also reflects the significance of rituals and offerings in many cultures, where individuals seek to connect with a higher power for guidance and blessings. The mantra serves as a reminder of the divine energy that surrounds us and the importance of approaching life with bravery and intention.
