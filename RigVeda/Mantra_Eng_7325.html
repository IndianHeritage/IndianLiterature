सां ते अग्ने शान्तमा चनिष्ठा भवतु प्रियाः |तया वर्धस्व सुश्रुतः ||
sā te agne śaṁtamā caniṣṭhā bhavatu priyā | tayā vardhasva suṣṭutaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sā: that
    • te: your
    • agne: O Agni (the fire god)
    • śaṁtamā: the most peaceful
    • caniṣṭhā: may it be established
    • bhavatu: may it become
    • priyā: dear
    • tayā: with that
    • vardhasva: may you grow
    • suṣṭutaḥ: well praised
  • Overall Meaning:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Agni, the fire god, asking for peace and prosperity.
    • It expresses a desire for Agni to become a source of affection and growth.
    • The invocation of Agni signifies the importance of fire in rituals, symbolizing purity and transformation.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a wedding ceremony, the sacred fire (Agni) is invoked to bless the couple with love and harmony.
    • During festivals like Diwali, lighting lamps and candles symbolizes the welcoming of peace and prosperity into homes.
    • In daily life, the act of cooking can be seen as a way to honor Agni, as it transforms raw ingredients into nourishing meals.
  • Significance and Application:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of peace and growth in our lives.
    • It can be applied in various situations where harmony and prosperity are desired, such as in family gatherings, business ventures, or personal development.
    • By invoking the qualities of Agni, individuals can seek to cultivate a peaceful environment and foster positive relationships.
