कं उष्विद अस्य सेनयाग्नेर अपाकचक्षसः |पणिं गोषु तारामहे ||
kam u ṣvid asya senayāgner apākacakṣasaḥ | paṇiṁ goṣu starāmahe ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • kam: Who
    • u: or
    • ṣvid: to be known
    • asya: of this
    • senayā: by the army
    • agner: of the fire
    • apākacakṣasaḥ: of the one who sees (or perceives) the light
    • paṇiṁ: the wealth
    • goṣu: in the cows (or in the animals)
    • starāmahe: we strive or we aim
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra can be understood as a call to seek knowledge and wealth, particularly in the context of community and collective strength.
    • The phrase "kam u ṣvid" suggests a quest for understanding or enlightenment, asking "Who can reveal this?"
    • "Senayā agner" indicates the power of unity and strength, likening it to the energy of fire, which is both destructive and transformative.
    • The term "apākacakṣasaḥ" refers to the ability to perceive or understand the deeper truths of existence, akin to enlightenment.
    • "Paṇiṁ goṣu" emphasizes the importance of wealth, not just in material terms but also in the richness of life experiences, relationships, and community.
    • "Starāmahe" reflects the aspiration to achieve these goals collectively, highlighting the importance of teamwork and collaboration.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, this mantra can be applied to team dynamics where collective effort leads to innovation and success. For instance, a project team working together to solve a complex problem embodies the essence of "starāmahe."
    • In community service, individuals coming together to support a cause, such as building homes for the underprivileged, reflects the unity and strength symbolized by "senayā agner."
    • In personal development, seeking knowledge through education and mentorship can be seen as a pursuit of "apākacakṣasaḥ," where one strives to gain deeper insights into life.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of collective strength, the pursuit of knowledge, and the importance of wealth in various forms.
    • It teaches us that by working together and seeking enlightenment, we can achieve greater goals and enrich our lives.
    • In today's world, where collaboration and community are vital, the teachings of this mantra are highly applicable, encouraging us to strive for unity and shared success.
