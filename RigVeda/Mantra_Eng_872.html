अवā नो अग्न उतीभिर गायतृस्य प्रभर्मणि |विश्वासु धिषु वन्द्य ||
avā no agna ūtibhir gāyatrasya prabharmaṇi | viśvāsu dhīṣu vandya ||
  • Mantra: "avā no agna ūtibhir gāyatrasya prabharmaṇi | viśvāsu dhīṣu vandya ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • avā: May we be protected
    • no: us
    • agna: O Fire (Agni, the divine fire)
    • ūtibhir: with your powers
    • gāyatrasya: of the Gāyatrī (a sacred verse)
    • prabharmaṇi: in the brilliance or radiance
    • viśvāsu: in all
    • dhīṣu: in intellects or minds
    • vandya: worthy of worship or reverence
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Agni, the divine fire, which symbolizes purity, transformation, and the divine presence in rituals.
    • The phrase "avā no" indicates a plea for protection and guidance, suggesting that the speaker seeks the divine's safeguarding in their endeavors.
    • By invoking "ūti," the mantra emphasizes the importance of divine powers and energies that can aid in achieving one's goals.
    • The reference to "gāyatrasya prabharmaṇi" connects the mantra to the Gāyatrī mantra, which is revered for its spiritual significance and ability to illuminate the mind.
    • The term "viśvāsu dhīṣu" highlights the universality of wisdom and intellect, suggesting that the divine presence should be acknowledged in all aspects of life.
    • Finally, "vandya" signifies the respect and reverence owed to the divine, reinforcing the idea that worship and gratitude are essential components of spiritual practice.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, individuals often seek guidance and protection in challenging situations, such as during exams or important decisions. This mantra serves as a reminder to invoke higher powers for support.
    • In educational settings, students can recite this mantra to enhance their focus and clarity of thought, aligning their intellect with divine wisdom.
    • During rituals and ceremonies, the invocation of Agni symbolizes the transformation of intentions into actions, emphasizing the importance of purity and dedication in one's pursuits.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking divine guidance and protection in all aspects of life.
    • Its significance lies in the acknowledgment of the divine presence in our thoughts and actions, promoting a sense of reverence and gratitude.
    • By applying the principles of this mantra, individuals can cultivate a deeper connection with their spiritual selves, enhancing their intellectual pursuits and overall well-being.
