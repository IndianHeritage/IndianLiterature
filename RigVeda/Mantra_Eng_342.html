आ घ त्वावान तमनाप्तः स्तोत्रिभ्यो धृष्णव इयानः |ऋणोर अक्षं न चक्र्योः ||
ā gha tvāvān tmanāptaḥ stotṛbhyo dhṛṣṇav iyānaḥ | ṛṇor akṣaṁ na cakryoḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā: from
    • gha: to be fulfilled
    • tvāvān: you (the one who is addressed)
    • tmanā: with your own self
    • ptaḥ: having attained
    • stotṛbhyo: to the worshippers
    • dhṛṣṇav: with strength
    • iyānaḥ: may you go
    • ṛṇor: of the debt
    • akṣaṁ: the eye
    • na: not
    • cakryoḥ: may you be bound
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks to the idea of fulfillment and the importance of self-awareness in achieving one's goals.
    • It emphasizes the need to approach worship and devotion with strength and sincerity.
    • The phrase "may you go" suggests a journey or progression towards a higher state of being.
    • The reference to "the debt" implies that there are responsibilities or obligations that one must navigate in life.
    • Overall, it conveys a message of liberation from the bonds of material debt or obligations.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, this mantra can be applied to the pursuit of career goals. One must be self-aware and strong in their efforts to achieve success while also being mindful of their responsibilities to others.
    • In personal relationships, the idea of fulfilling one's obligations while maintaining a sense of self can lead to healthier interactions and mutual respect.
    • In spiritual practices, this mantra encourages individuals to engage in worship with a strong heart and mind, recognizing their own journey and the debts they carry.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for self-awareness, strength, and the importance of fulfilling one's obligations without being bound by them.
    • It serves as a reminder that while we may have debts and responsibilities, we can navigate them with strength and purpose.
    • In real life, this mantra can inspire individuals to pursue their goals with integrity and awareness, leading to a more fulfilling and liberated existence.
