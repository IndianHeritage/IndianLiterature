प्रातर्यावणः सहस्कृत सोमपेयाय सन्त्य |इहाद्य दैव्यम् जनम् बर्हिर आ सादयाऽ वसो ||
prātaryāvṇaḥ sahaskṛta somapeyāya santya | ihādya daivyaṁ janam barhir ā sādayā vaso ||
  • prātaryāvṇaḥ: This term can be broken down into "prātah" meaning "morning" and "yāvṇaḥ" which refers to "the one who brings forth." Together, it signifies the arrival of a new day or the dawn.
  • sahaskṛta: This word means "prepared" or "made ready." It implies a sense of readiness and organization, suggesting that one should be prepared for the opportunities that a new day brings.
  • somapeyāya: "Soma" refers to a sacred drink in Vedic tradition, often associated with immortality and divine inspiration. "Peyāya" means "to drink." Thus, this phrase can be interpreted as partaking in divine or spiritual nourishment.
  • santya: This term translates to "truth" or "purity." It emphasizes the importance of seeking truth and maintaining purity in thoughts and actions.
  • ihādya: This means "here and now," indicating the importance of the present moment and the actions taken in it.
  • daivyaṁ janam: "Daivyaṁ" means "divine" and "janam" means "people" or "community." This phrase refers to a community that embodies divine qualities or virtues.
  • barhir: This term means "feather" or "sacred grass," often used in rituals. It symbolizes purity and the connection to the divine.
  • ā sādayā: "Ā" means "to" or "towards," and "sādayā" refers to "to bring together" or "to unite." This suggests a call for unity and harmony among people.
  • vaso: This term means "wealth" or "prosperity." It signifies the abundance that comes from living in harmony and truth.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of starting each day with readiness, seeking divine inspiration, and maintaining truth and purity in our actions. It calls for unity among people, fostering a community that embodies divine qualities. In real life, this mantra can be applied by individuals who strive to begin their day with intention, engage in spiritual practices, and work towards creating a harmonious and prosperous community. By embodying these principles, one can contribute to a more peaceful and fulfilling life, both personally and collectively.
