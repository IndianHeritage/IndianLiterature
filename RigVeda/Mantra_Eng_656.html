स इन महानी समिथानि मजनना कृत्नोति युध्म ओजसा जनैभ्यः |अधा चन श्रद दधति त्विषीमता इन्द्राय वज्रं निघनिघ्नते वधम् ||
sa in mahāni samithāni majmanā kṛṇoti yudhma ojasā janebhyaḥ | adhā cana śrad dadhati tviṣīmata indrāya vajraṁ nighanighnate vadham ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sa: He/That
    • in: In/Within
    • mahāni: Great/Significant
    • samithāni: Assemblies/Meetings
    • majmanā: By the one who is engaged in action
    • kṛṇoti: Creates/Performs
    • yudhma: Battle/Conflict
    • ojasā: With strength/Power
    • janebhyaḥ: For the people/Among the people
    • adhā: Therefore/Thus
    • cana: Also/Indeed
    • śrad: Faith/Trust
    • dadhati: Bestows/Grants
    • tviṣīmata: Of the radiant/Illuminated
    • indrāya: To Indra (the king of gods)
    • vajraṁ: Thunderbolt/Weapon
    • nighanighnate: Strikes/Defeats
    • vadham: Death/Destruction
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of a powerful entity (often interpreted as Indra) who, within great assemblies, creates actions that lead to victory in battles.
    • The phrase "with strength for the people" emphasizes the importance of collective strength and unity in overcoming challenges.
    • It highlights the role of faith and trust in the divine, suggesting that such faith can lead to empowerment and success.
    • The reference to Indra and his thunderbolt symbolizes the power to overcome obstacles and adversaries.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, this mantra can be likened to a team leader who inspires trust and unity among team members, leading them to successfully complete a challenging project.
    • In community service, it reflects how collective efforts, driven by faith in a common cause, can lead to significant social change.
    • In personal life, it can be seen in situations where individuals rely on their inner strength and faith to overcome personal struggles or conflicts.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of unity, strength, and faith in achieving success in various aspects of life.
    • It teaches that through collective effort and trust in a higher power, individuals and communities can overcome challenges and achieve their goals.
    • In essence, it serves as a reminder of the power of collaboration and belief in the face of adversity.
