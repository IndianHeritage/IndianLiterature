अयं देवः सहसा जायमान इन्द्रेण युजा पणिम अस्तभायत् |अयं स्वस्य पितुर आयुधानीन्दुर अमुष्णाद अशिवस्य मायाः ||
ayaṁ devaḥ sahasā jāyamāna indreṇa yujā paṇim astabhāyat | ayaṁ svasya pitur āyudhānīndur amuṣṇād aśivasya māyāḥ ||
  • ayaṁ devaḥ: This phrase translates to "this deity" or "this divine being." It signifies the presence of a divine force or energy that is being invoked.
  • sahasā jāyamāna: This means "born with great power" or "emerging with strength." It suggests the idea of a powerful force coming into existence, which can be related to the birth of new ideas or movements in life.
  • indreṇa yujā: This translates to "united with Indra." Indra is a prominent deity in Vedic literature, often associated with rain, storms, and strength. This phrase indicates a connection or alignment with this powerful force.
  • paṇim astabhāyat: This means "with hands that are strong." It symbolizes the ability to act and create, emphasizing the importance of taking action in the world.
  • ayaṁ svasya pitur: This translates to "this is from one's own father." It signifies a connection to lineage, heritage, and the wisdom passed down through generations.
  • āyudhānīndur: This means "the moon of weapons." It symbolizes strength and protection, suggesting that one is equipped with the necessary tools to face challenges.
  • amuṣṇād: This translates to "not to be harmed." It indicates a state of safety and security, reinforcing the idea of divine protection.
  • aśivasya māyāḥ: This means "the illusion of the unholy." It suggests the presence of distractions or negative influences that can lead one away from their true path.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of divine strength, protection, and the connection to one's heritage. In real life, it can be applied in various scenarios such as seeking guidance during challenging times, invoking strength when pursuing goals, or maintaining focus amidst distractions. It serves as a reminder that we are not alone in our endeavors; we are supported by divine forces and our ancestral wisdom. This mantra encourages individuals to harness their inner strength and act decisively, while also being aware of the illusions that may lead them astray.
