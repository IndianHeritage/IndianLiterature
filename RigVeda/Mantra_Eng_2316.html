उत त्ये देवी सुभगे मिथूदृशोषासनक्ता जगताम अपीजुवाः |स्तुषे यद वाम पृथिवि नव्यसां वचः स्थातुश च वयस् त्रिवया उपस्तिरे ||
uta tye devī subhage mithūdṛśoṣāsānaktā jagatām apījuvā | stuṣe yad vām pṛthivi navyasā vacaḥ sthātuś ca vayas trivayā upastire ||
  • uta tye devī subhage: This phrase can be translated as "O auspicious goddess." Here, "devī" refers to a divine feminine energy or goddess, and "subhage" signifies auspiciousness or good fortune. In real life, this can be related to the reverence given to women in society, recognizing their role as bearers of life and prosperity.
  • mithūdṛśoṣāsānaktā: This term can be interpreted as "who sees with a kind eye." It emphasizes the importance of compassion and understanding in our interactions. For example, in a workplace, a manager who approaches their team with kindness and empathy fosters a positive environment, leading to better productivity and morale.
  • jagatām apījuvā: This translates to "the one who nourishes the world." It highlights the nurturing aspect of the divine feminine, akin to a mother who provides for her children. In real life, this can be seen in the efforts of caregivers, teachers, and community leaders who work tirelessly to support and uplift those around them.
  • stuṣe yad vām pṛthivi: This means "when we praise you, O Earth." It signifies the importance of gratitude and respect towards nature. In contemporary society, this can be reflected in environmental movements that advocate for the protection of our planet, recognizing it as a source of life and sustenance.
  • navyasā vacaḥ: This phrase translates to "new words." It suggests the idea of innovation and fresh perspectives. In real life, this can be related to the importance of creativity in problem-solving, where new ideas can lead to breakthroughs in various fields, such as technology, art, and social change.
  • sthātuś ca vayas trivayā upastire: This can be interpreted as "may you stand firm and support us in three ways." The "three ways" could symbolize the physical, mental, and spiritual aspects of life. In practice, this can be seen in holistic approaches to well-being, where individuals seek balance in their physical health, mental clarity, and spiritual fulfillment.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of reverence towards the divine feminine, emphasizing qualities such as compassion, nourishment, gratitude, innovation, and holistic support. Its significance lies in the reminder to honor and embody these values in our daily lives. By recognizing the importance of kindness, nurturing, and balance, we can create a more harmonious and supportive society, ultimately leading to personal and collective growth.
