यā वो भेषजाः मरुतः शुचीनि यā शान्तमā वृषणो यā मयोभुः | यāनि मनुर अवृणीतā पिता नस् ता शान्त च योश च रुद्रस्य वश्मि ||
yā vo bheṣajā marutaḥ śucīni yā śaṁtamā vṛṣaṇo yā mayobhu | yāni manur avṛṇītā pitā nas tā śaṁ ca yoś ca rudrasya vaśmi ||
  • yā vo bheṣajā marutaḥ śucīni: This phrase refers to the healing powers or remedies that the Maruts (the storm deities) possess. In real life, this can be likened to the various natural remedies and medicines that are derived from nature, emphasizing the importance of holistic healing.
  • yā śaṁtamā vṛṣaṇo: This part speaks of the peaceful and calming aspects of the Maruts. In our daily lives, we often seek peace and tranquility, whether through meditation, yoga, or simply spending time in nature. This mantra reminds us of the importance of finding calmness amidst chaos.
  • yā mayobhu: This phrase can be interpreted as the blessings or gifts that are bestowed upon us. In practical terms, it can refer to the opportunities and resources we receive in life, which we should recognize and be grateful for.
  • yāni manur avṛṇītā: This line indicates the choices made by Manu, the progenitor of humanity. It highlights the significance of decision-making in our lives. Every choice we make shapes our future, and this mantra encourages us to make wise and thoughtful decisions.
  • pitā nas tā: This phrase refers to our ancestors or forefathers. It emphasizes the importance of honoring our roots and the wisdom passed down through generations. In real life, this can be seen in family traditions and values that guide our actions and beliefs.
  • śaṁ ca yoś ca rudrasya vaśmi: This final part speaks of the connection to Rudra, a deity associated with storms and transformation. It signifies the power of transformation in our lives. Just as storms can bring change, we too can embrace change and transformation as a part of our growth.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of healing, peace, gratitude, wise decision-making, honoring our ancestors, and embracing transformation. In real life, it serves as a reminder to seek balance and harmony in our lives, to appreciate the gifts we receive, and to make choices that align with our values and the wisdom of those who came before us. By integrating these principles into our daily lives, we can cultivate a more fulfilling and meaningful existence.
