सनायुवो नमसā नव्यो अर्कैर वसूयवो मतयो दसम दद्रुः |पतिं न पत्नीर उषतीर उषन्तं स्पृशन्ति त्वां शवसावन मनिषाः ||
sanāyuvo namasā navyo arkair vasūyavo matayo dasma dadruḥ | patiṁ na patnīr uśatīr uśantaṁ spṛśanti tvā śavasāvan manīṣāḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sanāyuvo: Those who are long-lived or have longevity.
    • namasā: With reverence or salutation.
    • navyo: New or fresh.
    • arkair: With rays or light.
    • vasūyavo: Abundant or prosperous.
    • matayo: With thoughts or intentions.
    • dasma: From here or from this place.
    • dadruḥ: To be established or to be firm.
    • patiṁ: Lord or master.
    • na: Not.
    • patnīr: Wives or partners.
    • uśatīr: Those who are strong or powerful.
    • uśantaṁ: The one who is awake or alert.
    • spṛśanti: They touch or connect.
    • tvā: You.
    • śavasāvan: With the essence of life or vitality.
    • manīṣāḥ: Thoughts or wisdom.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of reverence towards those who are long-lived and prosperous, emphasizing the importance of new beginnings and fresh intentions.
    • The mention of "arkair" suggests the significance of light and knowledge, which can be related to enlightenment in our lives.
    • It highlights the connection between the divine (patiṁ) and the community (patnīr), indicating that strong relationships are essential for a prosperous life.
    • The phrase "uśatīr uśantaṁ" points to the importance of being alert and aware, which can be applied in our daily lives to make informed decisions.
    • Finally, the mantra encourages us to connect with the essence of life and wisdom, suggesting that our thoughts and intentions shape our reality.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace, fostering a culture of respect and collaboration can lead to longevity and prosperity for the organization.
    • Starting a new project with fresh ideas and intentions can lead to successful outcomes, much like the "navyo" aspect of the mantra.
    • In personal relationships, being alert and aware of each other's needs can strengthen bonds and create a supportive environment.
    • Practicing mindfulness and being present in the moment can help individuals connect with their inner wisdom and make better life choices.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of life, emphasizing the importance of respect, awareness, and intention in our actions.
    • Its significance lies in guiding individuals towards a prosperous and fulfilling life by fostering strong relationships and being mindful of one's thoughts and actions.
    • In real-life scenarios, applying the principles of this mantra can lead to personal growth, improved relationships, and a deeper understanding of one's purpose.
