अनमीवासा इळयामदन्तो मितज्ञवो वरिमन्ना पृथिव्याः |आदित्यस्य व्रतमुपक्षियन्तो वयं मित्रस्य सुमतौ स्याम ||
anamīvāsa iḻayā madanto mitajñavo varimann ā pṛthivyāḥ | ādityasya vratam upakṣiyanto vayam mitrasya sumatau syāma ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • anamīvāsa: "We are like the unbroken flow" - signifies continuity and unity.
    • iḻayā: "with the support" - indicates reliance on a higher power or community.
    • madanto: "in joy" - represents happiness and fulfillment.
    • mitajñavo: "those who know the truth" - refers to wise individuals or enlightened beings.
    • varimann: "the one who is powerful" - denotes strength and capability.
    • ā pṛthivyāḥ: "from the earth" - symbolizes our connection to the physical world.
    • ādityasya: "of the sun" - represents light, knowledge, and divinity.
    • vratam: "the vow" - signifies commitment and dedication.
    • upakṣiyanto: "we are protected" - indicates safety and security.
    • vayam: "we" - emphasizes collective identity.
    • mitrasya: "of the friend" - refers to camaraderie and support.
    • sumatau: "in good thoughts" - signifies positivity and constructive thinking.
    • syāma: "may we be" - expresses a desire for a certain state of being.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of unity and collective strength. Just as a river flows continuously, individuals should work together harmoniously.
    • The reference to joy and truth highlights the significance of wisdom and happiness in our lives. For example, in a workplace, a team that values truth and joy is likely to be more productive and innovative.
    • The connection to the earth and the sun symbolizes our grounding in reality and the pursuit of knowledge. In real life, this can be seen in sustainable practices that respect nature while seeking enlightenment through education.
    • The vow mentioned in the mantra signifies commitment to a cause or community. For instance, individuals who volunteer for social causes demonstrate this commitment, working together for a common good.
    • The idea of being protected and supported by friends emphasizes the importance of relationships. In times of crisis, having a supportive network can provide the strength needed to overcome challenges.
    • Finally, the aspiration for good thoughts reflects the power of positive thinking. In personal development, maintaining a positive mindset can lead to better outcomes in various aspects of life.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of individuals and the importance of community, wisdom, and positivity in our lives.
    • Its significance lies in promoting harmony, commitment, and support among people, which is applicable in various real-life scenarios such as teamwork, community service, and personal relationships.
    • By embodying the principles outlined in this mantra, individuals can contribute to a more harmonious and enlightened society.
