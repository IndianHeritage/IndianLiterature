आ देवानाम् अग्रयावेह यातु नराशंसो विश्वरूपेभिर अश्वैः |ऋतस्य पथā नमसā मियेदो देविभ्यो देवतमः सुशूदत ||
ā devānām agrayāveha yātu narāśaṁso viśvarūpebhir aśvaiḥ | ṛtasya pathā namasā miyedho devebhyo devatamaḥ suṣūdat ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā: from
    • devānām: of the gods
    • agrayā: foremost
    • yātu: may go
    • narāśaṁso: of the human beings
    • viśvarūpebhir: with various forms
    • aśvaiḥ: with horses
    • ṛtasya: of truth
    • pathā: on the path
    • namasā: with reverence
    • miyedho: may I attain
    • devebhyo: from the gods
    • devatamaḥ: the supreme deity
    • suṣūdat: may I be nourished
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the blessings of the gods, asking for guidance and support in the journey of life.
    • The phrase "may go" signifies a desire for progress and movement towards higher goals.
    • It emphasizes the importance of truth and righteousness ("ṛtasya pathā") as the guiding principles in life.
    • The mention of "various forms" suggests the diversity of experiences and paths that one may encounter.
    • By invoking the "supreme deity," the mantra acknowledges a higher power that nourishes and sustains life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, this mantra can be applied when seeking guidance for career advancement, emphasizing the importance of integrity and hard work.
    • In personal relationships, it can serve as a reminder to approach interactions with respect and reverence, fostering harmony and understanding.
    • During challenging times, individuals may recite this mantra to seek strength and nourishment from a higher power, reinforcing their faith and resilience.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine guidance and the pursuit of truth in all aspects of life.
    • It serves as a reminder that, regardless of the challenges faced, one can seek support from the divine and strive for righteousness.
    • Incorporating this mantra into daily life can inspire individuals to remain focused on their goals while honoring the diverse paths they may encounter.
