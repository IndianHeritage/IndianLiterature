नाव न क्षोढः प्रदिशः पृथिव्याः स्वस्तिभिर अति दुर्गाणि विश्वा |स्वाम प्रजाम बृहदुक्तो महित्वावरेष्व अदधाद आ परेषु ||
nāvā na kṣodaḥ pradiśaḥ pṛthivyāḥ svastibhir ati durgāṇi viśvā | svām prajām bṛhaduktho mahitvāvareṣv adadhād ā pareṣu ||
  • nāvā na kṣodaḥ: This phrase can be interpreted as "may we not be harmed" or "may we not suffer." It emphasizes the importance of safety and protection in our lives.
  • pradiśaḥ pṛthivyāḥ: This translates to "in all directions of the earth." It signifies the universal nature of the plea for safety and well-being, extending beyond personal boundaries to encompass the entire world.
  • svastibhir ati durgāṇi viśvā: Here, "svastibhir" refers to auspiciousness or well-being, while "ati durgāṇi" means "extremely difficult situations." This part of the mantra expresses a desire for protection from all forms of adversity and challenges that may arise in life.
  • svām prajām bṛhaduktho: This can be understood as "may our progeny be great." It highlights the importance of future generations and the hope for their prosperity and success.
  • mahitvāvareṣv adadhād ā pareṣu: This phrase suggests "may we be established in greatness among others." It reflects the aspiration for recognition and respect in society, as well as the desire to contribute positively to the community.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a profound wish for safety, well-being, and prosperity not just for oneself but for all of humanity. It emphasizes the interconnectedness of individuals and the collective responsibility we have towards each other. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as during community gatherings, family prayers, or even in personal reflections. It serves as a reminder to seek harmony and support in overcoming challenges, fostering a sense of unity and shared purpose. By invoking this mantra, individuals can cultivate a mindset focused on resilience, compassion, and the betterment of society as a whole.
