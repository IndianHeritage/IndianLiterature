तन् नो वि वोचो यदि ते पुरा चिज्जरितारा आनशुः सुम्नम इन्द्र |कस ते भागः किम् वयो दुध्र खिद्वः पुरुहूत पुरूवसो 'सुरघ्नः ||
tan no vi voco yadi te purā cij jaritāra ānaśuḥ sumnam indra | kas te bhāgaḥ kiṁ vayo dudhra khidvaḥ puruhūta purūvaso 'suraghnaḥ ||
  • tan no vi voco: This phrase can be translated as "may we speak of you." It signifies the importance of communication and the sharing of knowledge. In real life, this can be seen in the context of education and mentorship, where sharing wisdom is crucial for growth.
  • yadi te purā cij jaritāra ānaśuḥ sumnam indra: This part translates to "if you, O Indra, have ever been invoked." Indra is a deity associated with strength and power. This line emphasizes the need for invoking divine support in our endeavors. For example, in sports, athletes often invoke a higher power for strength and guidance before competitions.
  • kas te bhāgaḥ kiṁ vayo dudhra: This translates to "what is your share, what is your age?" It reflects on the concepts of fortune and the passage of time. In a real-life scenario, this can relate to how individuals assess their achievements and the opportunities they have received throughout their lives.
  • khidvaḥ puruhūta purūvaso 'suraghnaḥ: This can be interpreted as "O many-named one, slayer of the asuras." It highlights the multifaceted nature of divinity and the triumph over obstacles. In everyday life, this can be seen in the struggles people face and how they overcome challenges, much like a hero in a story.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking divine guidance, understanding one's place in the world, and overcoming challenges. Its significance lies in the reminder that communication, invocation of strength, and reflection on one's journey are vital in both personal and communal contexts. In real life, this mantra can be applied in various situations, such as during significant life events, challenges, or when seeking clarity in decision-making. It encourages individuals to connect with their inner strength and the support of the universe in their pursuits.
