कन्येव तन्मा शाशदानाम् एषि देवी देवम् इयक्षमानम् |संस्मयमानā युवतिः पुरस्ताद आविर्वक्षांसी कृणुषे विभाति ||
kanyeva tanvā3 śāśadānām̐ eṣi devi devam iyakṣamāṇam | saṁsmayamānā yuvatiḥ purastād āvir vakṣāṁsi kṛṇuṣe vibhātī ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • Kanyeva: Like a young woman
    • Tanvā: With a body
    • Śāśadānām: Of the eternal beings
    • Eṣi: You come
    • Devi: O Goddess
    • Devam: To the God
    • Iyakṣamāṇam: Who is capable
    • Saṁsmayamānā: With a smile
    • Yuvatiḥ: The young woman
    • Purastād: In front
    • Āvir: Manifest
    • Vakṣāṁsi: In the bosom
    • Kṛṇuṣe: You create
    • Vibhātī: You shine
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the divine feminine energy, represented as a young woman, who embodies beauty, grace, and strength.
    • The phrase "like a young woman" suggests vitality and freshness, indicating the importance of youthfulness in spirit and approach to life.
    • The reference to "eternal beings" signifies the timeless nature of the divine and its connection to the universe.
    • The invocation of the Goddess and God emphasizes the duality and balance of masculine and feminine energies in creation.
    • The act of manifesting and shining in the bosom symbolizes nurturing and the essence of life, suggesting that the divine is present in all aspects of existence.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In personal development, embracing the qualities of the "young woman" can inspire individuals to approach challenges with enthusiasm and creativity.
    • In relationships, recognizing the balance of energies can lead to healthier dynamics, where both partners appreciate and respect each other's strengths.
    • In nature, the cycle of life reflects the eternal beings, reminding us of the interconnectedness of all living things and the importance of nurturing our environment.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the divine presence in our lives and the importance of embracing both our youthful spirit and the nurturing aspects of existence.
    • It encourages us to manifest our true selves, shine in our endeavors, and maintain a balance between the masculine and feminine energies within us.
    • In practical terms, this mantra can be applied in daily life through self-reflection, fostering relationships, and engaging with the world around us with a sense of wonder and responsibility.
