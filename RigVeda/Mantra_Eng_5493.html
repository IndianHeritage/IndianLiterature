मन्त्र: सन्तीषि प्रवतो दाशुषे चिद्याभिर् विवेषो हर्यश्व धीभिः |ववन्मा नु ते युज्याभिर् ऊती कदा न इन्द्र राया आ दाशस्येः ||
sanitāsi pravato dāśuṣe cid yābhir viveṣo haryaśva dhībhiḥ | vavanmā nu te yujyābhir ūtī kadā na indra rāya ā daśasyeḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sanitāsi: You are purified or made clean.
    • pravato: By the wind or through the breeze.
    • dāśuṣe: For the one who is a servant or devotee.
    • cid: Consciousness or awareness.
    • yābhir: By which or through which.
    • viveṣo: You enter or penetrate.
    • haryaśva: With the swift horses or with great speed.
    • dhībhiḥ: With intellect or wisdom.
    • vavanmā: May we be blessed or may we receive.
    • nu: Indeed or truly.
    • te: Your.
    • yujyābhir: By the means of connection or union.
    • ūtī: Prosperity or abundance.
    • kadā: When.
    • na: Not.
    • indra: O Indra, the king of gods.
    • rāya: Wealth or riches.
    • ā: To or towards.
    • dāśasyeḥ: Of the ten or the multitude.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the divine presence of Indra, the king of gods, asking for purification and blessings.
    • The reference to "swift horses" symbolizes speed and efficiency, suggesting that the blessings sought should come quickly and effectively.
    • The emphasis on "intellect" indicates that wisdom is essential in receiving and utilizing these blessings.
    • The phrase "may we be blessed" reflects a desire for abundance and prosperity in life, which can be interpreted as a call for both material and spiritual wealth.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be recited by entrepreneurs seeking success and rapid growth in their ventures, emphasizing the need for wisdom in decision-making.
    • In personal life, individuals may use this mantra during times of transition or challenge, seeking clarity and guidance to navigate their circumstances effectively.
    • In community gatherings, it can be a call for collective prosperity, encouraging members to work together with wisdom and speed towards common goals.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the importance of purification, wisdom, and the swift attainment of blessings in various aspects of life.
    • Its significance lies in the reminder that with the right mindset and intentions, individuals can attract prosperity and success.
    • In real-life scenarios, it serves as a motivational tool, encouraging people to seek divine guidance while actively working towards their goals.
