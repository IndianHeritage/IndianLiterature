मन्त्र: इन्दो व्यव्यं अर्षसि वि श्रवाण्सि वि सौभगा | वि वाजान सोम गोमतः ||
indo vy avyam arṣasi vi śravāṁsi vi saubhagā | vi vājān soma gomataḥ ||
  • Indo: Refers to the Moon, often associated with nourishment and the mind. In real life, the Moon influences tides and can symbolize emotional balance.
  • Vy avyam: Translates to "may you be prosperous" or "may you be successful." This can relate to personal goals, such as achieving success in one's career or personal life.
  • Arṣasi: Means "to shine" or "to radiate." This can be likened to individuals who inspire others through their actions and achievements, much like a leader or a mentor.
  • Vi śravāṁsi: Refers to "with fame" or "with glory." In a modern context, this can relate to public recognition, such as awards or accolades received for one's contributions in various fields.
  • Vi saubhagā: Translates to "with good fortune" or "with auspiciousness." This can be seen in life events that bring joy and prosperity, such as weddings or the birth of a child.
  • Vi vājān: Means "with strength" or "with vigor." This can be related to physical fitness or mental resilience, emphasizing the importance of health and well-being in achieving one's goals.
  • Soma: Refers to the sacred drink that symbolizes immortality and divine inspiration. In contemporary life, this can be likened to the pursuit of knowledge and enlightenment through education and self-improvement.
  • Gomataḥ: Means "from the cow," which symbolizes nourishment and sustenance. In real life, this can relate to agriculture and the importance of sustainable practices for food security.

Conclusion: This mantra emphasizes the interconnectedness of prosperity, fame, strength, and nourishment in achieving a fulfilling life. It serves as a reminder that success is not solely about personal achievements but also about the impact one has on others and the environment. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire individuals to pursue their goals while maintaining a balance between personal success and contributing positively to society. It highlights the significance of nurturing relationships, seeking knowledge, and fostering a sense of community, ultimately leading to a harmonious and prosperous existence.
