वरेथे अग्निम आतपो वदते वल्ग्वात्रये |अन्ति षड्भूत वाम अवः ||
varethe agnim ātapo vadate valgv atraye | anti ṣad bhūtu vām avaḥ ||
  • Varethe: This word can be interpreted as "I choose" or "I invoke." It signifies the act of selecting or calling upon something with intention.
  • Agnim: Refers to "Agni," the fire god in Vedic literature. Agni symbolizes energy, transformation, and the divine spark within all beings.
  • Ātapo: This translates to "heat" or "radiance." It represents the life-giving warmth of the sun and the transformative power of energy.
  • Vadate: Means "speaks" or "declares." This indicates communication or the expression of thoughts and intentions.
  • Valgv: This term can be understood as "the one who is powerful" or "the one who is capable." It emphasizes strength and capability.
  • Atraye: This word can be interpreted as "to us" or "for us," indicating a personal connection or appeal.
  • Anti: Means "to bring" or "to lead." It suggests guidance or direction towards a desired outcome.
  • Ṣad: This translates to "six," which may symbolize completeness or a holistic approach.
  • Bhūtu: Refers to "elements" or "beings." It encompasses all forms of existence, both physical and spiritual.
  • Vām: This means "you" or "your," indicating a direct address to the divine or the universe.
  • Avaḥ: Translates to "bring" or "carry." It signifies the act of receiving or welcoming something into one’s life.

The mantra can be interpreted as a powerful invocation to Agni, the fire god, asking for his blessings and guidance. It emphasizes the importance of choosing the right path, harnessing energy, and seeking transformation in life. The speaker expresses a desire for strength and capability, inviting the divine to bring forth the elements necessary for growth and fulfillment.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as:

  • Personal Growth: When facing challenges, one can invoke the energy of Agni to transform obstacles into opportunities for growth.
  • Decision Making: The act of choosing wisely in life can be guided by the principles of this mantra, encouraging individuals to seek clarity and strength in their choices.
  • Spiritual Practices: In meditation or rituals, this mantra can be recited to connect with the divine energy and seek guidance for one’s journey.

In conclusion, this mantra holds significant meaning in the context of personal empowerment, transformation, and spiritual connection. It serves as a reminder to invoke the divine energy within and around us, guiding us towards our highest potential and harmonious existence.
