ईळितो अग्न आ वहेंद्रं चित्रं इह प्रियं |सुखै रथेभिर ऊतये ||
īḻito agna ā vahendraṁ citram iha priyam | sukhai rathebhir ūtaye ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • īḻito: Invoked or called upon
    • agna: O Agni (the fire god)
    • ā: To or towards
    • vahendraṁ: The lord of the heavens or Indra
    • citram: Wonderful or diverse
    • iha: Here or in this world
    • priyam: Beloved or dear
    • sukhai: With happiness or joy
    • rathebhir: By the chariots
    • ūtaye: For the purpose of elevation or upliftment
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Agni, the fire god, who is often seen as a mediator between the divine and human realms.
    • It invokes Agni to bring forth the blessings of Indra, the king of the gods, emphasizing the importance of divine support in our endeavors.
    • The term "citram" suggests the beauty and diversity of life, indicating that the blessings sought are not just material but also encompass a rich and fulfilling existence.
    • The mention of "sukhai rathebhir" implies that joy and happiness are essential components of our journey, and that we should strive to achieve our goals with a sense of fulfillment.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a modern context, this mantra can be related to the pursuit of success in various fields, such as career, education, or personal relationships. Just as Agni is invoked for blessings, individuals often seek guidance and support from mentors or spiritual leaders.
    • The idea of "sukhai rathebhir" can be seen in team sports, where players work together towards a common goal, celebrating their victories and learning from their defeats, all while fostering a sense of camaraderie and joy.
    • In personal development, this mantra encourages individuals to seek happiness in their journey, reminding them that the process of achieving goals is as important as the goals themselves.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of invoking divine support in our lives, particularly in our pursuits and endeavors.
    • It serves as a reminder that joy and fulfillment should accompany our efforts, and that the journey itself is valuable.
    • In real-life scenarios, whether in professional settings, personal growth, or community endeavors, the principles encapsulated in this mantra can guide individuals towards a more meaningful and enriched existence.
