अधां nv अस्य संदृशं जगन्वान अग्नेर अनीकं वरुणस्य मंसी |स्वर यद अश्मन्न अधिपा उ अन्धो 'भि मां वपुर दृष्टये निनीयात ||
adhā nv asya saṁdṛśaṁ jaganvān agner anīkaṁ varuṇasya maṁsi | sva1r yad aśmann adhipā u andho 'bhi mā vapur dṛśaye ninīyāt ||
  • adhā nv asya saṁdṛśaṁ: This phrase can be translated as "downward towards this sight." It suggests a focus on perception and understanding, indicating that one should look deeper into the essence of things.
  • jaganvān agner anīkaṁ: This means "the vastness of the world, the multitude of fire." Here, "agni" (fire) symbolizes energy, transformation, and the divine presence in the universe. It reflects the idea that everything is interconnected through this energy.
  • varuṇasya maṁsi: This translates to "in the realm of Varuna." Varuna is the god of cosmic order and water, representing the moral and ethical aspects of the universe. This phrase emphasizes the importance of adhering to cosmic laws and principles.
  • sva1r yad aśmann: This can be interpreted as "the light that is in the stone." It signifies the hidden potential and wisdom that can be found in the most unassuming places, encouraging us to seek knowledge and enlightenment in all aspects of life.
  • adhipā u andho 'bhi: This means "the rulers and the blind." It suggests that those in power may sometimes lack vision or understanding, highlighting the need for awareness and insight in leadership.
  • vapur dṛśaye ninīyāt: This translates to "may the form lead to vision." It implies that our physical existence and experiences should guide us towards greater understanding and enlightenment.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of perception, interconnectedness, and the pursuit of knowledge. In real life, it encourages individuals to look beyond the surface, recognize the energy that binds the universe, and adhere to moral principles. It serves as a reminder for leaders to remain aware and insightful, ensuring that their actions align with the greater cosmic order. This mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as in personal development, leadership, and ethical decision-making, guiding individuals to seek deeper truths and foster a harmonious existence with the world around them.
