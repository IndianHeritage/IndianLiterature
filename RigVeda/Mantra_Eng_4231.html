बळ इथ्था देव निष्कृतम् आदित्य यजतम् बृहत् |वरुण मित्रार्यमन् वर्षिष्ठं क्षत्रम् आशाथे ||
baḻ itthā deva niṣkṛtam ādityā yajatam bṛhat | varuṇa mitrāryaman varṣiṣṭhaṁ kṣatram āśāthe ||
  • baḻ itthā: This phrase can be interpreted as "thus" or "in this manner." It sets the tone for the invocation, indicating a specific way of approaching the divine.
  • deva niṣkṛtam: "Deva" refers to the divine or gods, while "niṣkṛtam" means "liberated" or "freed." Together, this suggests a call to the divine for liberation or assistance in achieving freedom from worldly bonds.
  • ādityā: This term refers to the Adityas, a group of solar deities in Vedic tradition. They symbolize light, truth, and cosmic order.
  • yajatam: This means "to be worshipped" or "to be honored." It emphasizes the importance of devotion and reverence in spiritual practices.
  • bṛhat: This word translates to "great" or "vast," indicating the expansive nature of the divine or the universe.
  • varuṇa: Varuṇa is a deity associated with cosmic order, water, and the celestial sphere. He represents the moral and ethical aspects of the universe.
  • mitrāryaman: This term combines "Mitra," the god of friendship and contracts, with "Aryaman," a deity associated with hospitality and social order. Together, they signify the importance of relationships and community.
  • varṣiṣṭhaṁ: This means "the best" or "the most excellent," indicating a pursuit of the highest ideals or virtues.
  • kṣatram: This term refers to "power" or "strength," often associated with the warrior class in Vedic society. It emphasizes the importance of courage and protection.
  • āśāthe: This can be interpreted as "in the absence of" or "without," suggesting a plea for support in times of difficulty or adversity.

Conclusion: This mantra encapsulates a profound invocation to the divine, emphasizing the importance of liberation, worship, and the pursuit of excellence in various aspects of life. It highlights the interconnectedness of divine forces, community, and moral order. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in moments of seeking guidance, strength, and support, particularly during challenging times. It serves as a reminder to honor relationships, uphold ethical values, and strive for greatness in our endeavors, fostering a sense of unity and purpose in both personal and communal contexts.
