सं दक्षिणेन मनसा जायते कविर ऋतस्य गर्भो निहितो यमा परः |यूनाऽ ह सन्ता प्रथमं वि जजñतुर गुहा हितं जनिम नेमम उद्यतम् ||
saṁ dakṣeṇa manasā jāyate kavir ṛtasya garbho nihito yamā paraḥ | yūnā ha santā prathamaṁ vi jajñatur guhā hitaṁ janima nemam udyatam ||
  • saṁ dakṣeṇa manasā: This phrase can be translated as "with a skillful mind." It emphasizes the importance of mental acuity and wisdom in achieving one's goals. In real life, this can be seen in professionals who use their intellect and creativity to solve complex problems, such as engineers designing innovative solutions or artists creating impactful works.
  • jāyate kavir ṛtasya: This means "the poet of truth is born." Here, "kavir" refers to a wise or enlightened individual, while "ṛtasya" signifies truth or cosmic order. This suggests that those who seek and express truth are revered. For example, journalists and authors who uncover and share truths about society play a crucial role in shaping public opinion and fostering awareness.
  • garbho nihito yamā paraḥ: This translates to "the essence is hidden in the depths." It implies that true understanding and wisdom often lie beneath the surface and require exploration to uncover. In everyday life, this can be likened to personal growth, where individuals must delve into their inner selves to discover their true potential and purpose.
  • yūnā ha santā prathamaṁ vi jajñatur: This phrase means "the first among the united ones is born." It highlights the significance of unity and collaboration in achieving success. In a practical context, this can be observed in team sports, where players must work together harmoniously to win games, or in business, where teamwork drives innovation and productivity.
  • guhā hitaṁ janima nemam udyatam: This translates to "the birth that is hidden in the cave is not easily seen." It suggests that profound truths and insights may not be immediately apparent and require patience and perseverance to uncover. In real life, this can be related to the journey of education, where knowledge is often gained gradually through study and experience.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of wisdom, truth, unity, and the exploration of deeper meanings in life. It teaches us that success and understanding often come from a combination of mental skill, collaboration, and the willingness to look beyond the surface. In various real-life scenarios, such as in personal development, teamwork, and the pursuit of knowledge, these principles can guide individuals toward achieving their goals and realizing their potential. The significance of this mantra lies in its timeless relevance, encouraging us to cultivate a thoughtful and united approach to life’s challenges.
