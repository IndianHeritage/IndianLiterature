समानं नीलं वृषणो वसानाḥ सं जग्मिरे महिषा अर्वतीभिः |ऋतस्य पदं कवयो न पान्ति गुहा नामानि दधिरे पराणि ||
samānaṁ nīḻaṁ vṛṣaṇo vasānāḥ saṁ jagmire mahiṣā arvatībhiḥ | ṛtasya padaṁ kavayo ni pānti guhā nāmāni dadhire parāṇi ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • samānaṁ: similar, equal
    • nīḻaṁ: blue, dark
    • vṛṣaṇo: of the strong, powerful
    • vasānāḥ: dwelling, residing
    • saṁ jagmire: they have gone together
    • mahiṣā: the great ones, often referring to powerful beings or forces
    • arvatībhiḥ: with strength, might
    • ṛtasya: of truth, order, or cosmic law
    • padaṁ: path, way
    • kavayo: the wise, seers
    • ni pānti: they protect, guard
    • guhā: cave, secret place
    • nāmāni: names, identities
    • dadhire: they hold, maintain
    • parāṇi: the ultimate, supreme
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the unity and strength found in the collective, emphasizing how powerful beings or forces come together in harmony.
    • The reference to "blue" or "dark" can symbolize depth and the unknown, suggesting that true strength often lies in understanding and embracing the mysteries of life.
    • The "wise" or "seers" are those who understand the deeper truths of existence and guide others along the path of righteousness and order.
    • The "cave" or "secret place" can represent the inner self or the subconscious, where true knowledge and identity are held.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In teamwork, individuals with different strengths come together to achieve a common goal, much like the powerful beings in the mantra.
    • In nature, ecosystems thrive on the interdependence of various species, showcasing how unity leads to strength and sustainability.
    • In personal development, understanding one's inner self and embracing both strengths and weaknesses can lead to a more fulfilling life.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of unity, strength, and the pursuit of truth in our lives.
    • It teaches us that by coming together and understanding our deeper selves, we can navigate the complexities of life more effectively.
    • In real-life scenarios, whether in personal relationships, professional environments, or community efforts, the principles of collaboration and mutual support are essential for success and harmony.
