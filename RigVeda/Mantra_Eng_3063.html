स जायत प्रथमः पस्त्यासु महो बुद्ध्ने रजसो अस्य योनौ |अपाद अशीर्षा गुहमानो अन्तायोयुवानो वृषभस्य नीलि ||
sa jāyata prathamaḥ pastyāsu maho budhne rajaso asya yonau | apād aśīrṣā guhamāno antāyoyuvāno vṛṣabhasya nīḻe ||
  • sa jāyata prathamaḥ: This phrase translates to "He is born first." It signifies the emergence of a fundamental principle or entity, often interpreted as the creation of the universe or the first manifestation of consciousness.
  • pastyāsu maho budhne: This can be understood as "in the vastness of the great ocean." Here, the "great ocean" symbolizes the infinite possibilities and the primordial state of existence before creation.
  • rajaso asya yonau: This means "in the womb of the creative energy." It refers to the source of creation, where the potential for all things resides, emphasizing the importance of creativity and energy in the process of manifestation.
  • apād aśīrṣā: This translates to "without feet and without a head." It suggests a state of being that transcends physical limitations, representing the formless and the abstract aspects of existence.
  • guhamāno antāyoyuvāno: This phrase can be interpreted as "hidden, yet youthful." It indicates the idea that the essence of creation is often concealed and not immediately visible, yet it is vibrant and full of potential.
  • vṛṣabhasya nīḻe: This translates to "in the abode of the bull." The bull is often a symbol of strength and fertility in various cultures, representing the power of creation and the nurturing aspect of the universe.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda encapsulates the essence of creation, the interplay of energy, and the hidden potential within the universe. It emphasizes the importance of recognizing the formless aspects of existence and the creative energies that drive manifestation. In real life, this can be applied to various scenarios such as artistic creation, innovation in technology, or personal growth. Understanding that great ideas often emerge from a state of potential and creativity can inspire individuals to explore their own hidden talents and capabilities, leading to a more fulfilling and productive life.
