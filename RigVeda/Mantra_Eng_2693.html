यदाद्य त्वा प्रयति यज्ञे अस्मिन्होतश्चिकित्वोऽवृणीमहीह |ध्रुवमयाध्रुवमुताशमिष्टाः प्रजानन् विद्वां उप याहि सोमम् ||
yad adya tvā prayati yajñe asmin hotaś cikitvo 'vṛṇīmahīha | dhruvam ayā dhruvam utāśamiṣṭhāḥ prajānan vidvām̐ upa yāhi somam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yad: whatever
    • adya: today
    • tvā: you
    • prayati: are invoked
    • yajñe: in the sacrifice
    • asmin: in this
    • hotaś: O priest
    • cikitvo: with understanding
    • 'vṛṇīmahīha: we choose
    • dhruvam: certain
    • ayā: by this
    • dhruvam: certain
    • utā: and also
    • śamiṣṭhāḥ: the best
    • prajānan: knowing
    • vidvām̐: wise
    • upa: towards
    • yāhi: come
    • somam: the soma (sacred drink)
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a call to the divine, specifically invoking the presence of the deity during a sacrificial ritual.
    • The speaker expresses a desire to choose the best offerings and blessings, emphasizing the importance of wisdom and understanding in the process.
    • The mention of "soma" signifies the sacred drink that is believed to confer immortality and divine insight, highlighting the spiritual aspect of the ritual.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern life, this mantra can be related to the importance of making informed decisions. For instance, when starting a new project, one might seek guidance and clarity to ensure the best outcomes.
    • In personal relationships, invoking understanding and wisdom can lead to healthier communication and stronger bonds, akin to the ritualistic invocation of divine presence in the mantra.
    • In a corporate setting, leaders may use this principle to foster a culture of collaboration and informed decision-making, ensuring that all voices are heard and the best ideas are chosen.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its emphasis on the importance of wisdom, understanding, and the conscious choice of the best offerings in life.
    • It serves as a reminder to approach life's challenges with clarity and purpose, invoking the divine or higher wisdom to guide our decisions.
    • In various aspects of life, from personal growth to professional endeavors, the principles encapsulated in this mantra can lead to more meaningful and fulfilling experiences.
