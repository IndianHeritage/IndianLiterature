स नः पृथु श्रवाय्यम् अच् छा देव विवाससि |बृहद अग्नि सुवीर्यम् ||
sa naḥ pṛthu śravāyyam acchā deva vivāsasi | bṛhad agne suvīryam ||
  • sa naḥ: This phrase translates to "may he (the divine) be with us." It signifies a call for divine presence and support in our lives.
  • pṛthu: This word means "broad" or "vast." It suggests the expansiveness of the divine qualities we seek.
  • śravāyyam: This term refers to "fame" or "renown." It indicates the importance of being recognized for our virtues and actions.
  • acchā: This means "good" or "auspicious." It emphasizes the desire for positive outcomes and blessings.
  • deva: This word means "god" or "divine being." It represents the higher powers we invoke for guidance and support.
  • vivāsasi: This translates to "you reside" or "you dwell." It indicates the divine's presence in our lives.
  • bṛhad: This means "great" or "mighty." It refers to the immense power and strength of the divine.
  • agne: This word means "O Agni," referring to the fire god, symbolizing energy, transformation, and purification.
  • suviryam: This translates to "good strength" or "valiant." It signifies the qualities of courage and resilience that we aspire to embody.

The mantra as a whole can be interpreted as a prayer for the divine presence to be with us, granting us broad fame and auspiciousness, while invoking the mighty Agni to bless us with strength and valor. It reflects a deep yearning for divine support in our endeavors, encouraging us to strive for greatness and recognition in our actions.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as:

  • Personal Growth: When embarking on a new journey, whether it be in career or personal development, reciting this mantra can serve as a reminder to seek divine guidance and strength.
  • Community and Leadership: Leaders can use this mantra to inspire their teams, fostering a sense of unity and purpose while striving for collective recognition and success.
  • Challenges and Adversity: During difficult times, invoking the qualities of Agni can help individuals find the courage to face challenges and emerge stronger.

In conclusion, this mantra emphasizes the significance of divine support in our lives, encouraging us to seek greatness and strength in our actions. It serves as a reminder that with the right intentions and divine guidance, we can achieve our goals and make a positive impact in the world.
