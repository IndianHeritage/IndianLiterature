आ तु षिञ्च हरिम ईं द्रोर उपस्थे वाशीभिः तक्षताश्मनमयीभिः |परि ष्वजध्वं दश कक्ष्याभिर उभे धुरौ प्रति वह्निं युनक्त ||
ā tū ṣiñca harim īṁ dror upasthe vāśībhis takṣatāśmanmayībhiḥ | pari ṣvajadhvaṁ daśa kakṣyābhir ubhe dhurau prati vahniṁ yunakta ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā: from, out of
    • tū: you
    • ṣiñca: sprinkle, pour
    • harim: the divine, often referring to Lord Vishnu
    • īṁ: a syllable representing the divine
    • dror: in the flow, in the stream
    • upasthe: in the presence, near
    • vāśībhis: with the powerful, the strong
    • takṣatāśmanmayībhiḥ: with the strength of the divine, made of stone
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the divine presence, asking for blessings and strength.
    • The act of sprinkling or pouring signifies the sharing of divine energy and grace.
    • The mention of "harim" emphasizes the connection to a higher power, often associated with protection and sustenance.
    • The phrase "pari ṣvajadhvaṁ" suggests embracing or welcoming the divine energy into our lives.
    • The reference to "daśa kakṣyābhir" indicates the ten directions or realms, symbolizing completeness and wholeness.
    • "prati vahniṁ yunakta" can be interpreted as the divine fire or energy that fuels our actions and intentions.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily rituals, individuals may recite this mantra to seek guidance and strength in their endeavors, whether in personal or professional life.
    • During significant life events, such as weddings or new ventures, this mantra can be chanted to invoke blessings for success and harmony.
    • In meditation practices, this mantra can be used to focus the mind and connect with the divine, fostering a sense of peace and purpose.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of seeking divine guidance and strength in our lives.
    • Its significance lies in the belief that by connecting with the divine, we can navigate challenges and embrace opportunities with confidence.
    • In real-life scenarios, it encourages individuals to cultivate a sense of spirituality and mindfulness, enhancing their overall well-being and purpose.
