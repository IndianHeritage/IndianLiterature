अभि त्वा देव सवितर ईशानं वार्याणाम् |सदावन भागम् इमहे ||
abhi tvā deva savitar īśānaṁ vāryāṇām | sadāvan bhāgam īmahe ||
  • Mantra: "abhi tvā deva savitar īśānaṁ vāryāṇām | sadāvan bhāgam īmahe ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • abhi: towards, for
    • tvā: you
    • deva: O divine one, god
    • savitā: the sun, the source of life
    • īśānaṁ: lord, master
    • vāryāṇām: of the strong, powerful
    • sadāvan: always, eternally
    • bhāgam: portion, share
    • īmahe: we meditate, we invoke
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer directed towards Savitar, the sun god, who is considered the source of life and energy.
    • It expresses a desire to receive a share of the divine blessings and strength that Savitar embodies.
    • The invocation of "īśānaṁ vāryāṇām" highlights the reverence for the powerful and the divine, suggesting that one seeks guidance and strength from a higher power.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, individuals often seek inspiration and strength from various sources, such as mentors, leaders, or spiritual figures. This mantra can be likened to seeking guidance from a trusted advisor when facing challenges.
    • In moments of uncertainty or difficulty, people may turn to meditation or prayer, similar to the invocation in this mantra, to find clarity and strength.
    • In a corporate setting, a team may invoke the spirit of collaboration and strength to achieve a common goal, paralleling the essence of seeking divine support in this mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine guidance and strength, emphasizing the importance of seeking support from higher powers in our endeavors.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal challenges to collective efforts in teams, reminding us of the interconnectedness of our actions with the divine.
    • Ultimately, this mantra encourages individuals to cultivate a sense of humility and openness to receiving blessings, fostering a deeper connection with the universe and its energies.
