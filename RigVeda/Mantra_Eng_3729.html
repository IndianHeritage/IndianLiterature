उत स्म यं शिशुं यथा नवं जनिष्टारणी |धर्तारम मानवश्च viśाम अग्निं स्वध्वरम् ||
uta sma yaṁ śiśuṁ yathā navaṁ janiṣṭāraṇī | dhartāram mānuṣīṇāṁ viśām agniṁ svadhvaram ||
  • uta sma yaṁ śiśuṁ yathā navaṁ janiṣṭāraṇī: This phrase can be translated to "May we bring forth a new child, like a newborn." Here, the term "śiśuṁ" refers to a child or offspring, and "navaṁ" signifies something new or fresh. The mantra emphasizes the importance of new beginnings and the potential that comes with the birth of a child.
  • dhartāram mānuṣīṇāṁ viśām agniṁ svadhvaram: This part translates to "May we uphold the fire of humanity, the divine essence." The word "dhartāram" means to uphold or support, while "agniṁ" refers to fire, which is often seen as a symbol of purity, energy, and transformation in Vedic literature. "Svadhvaram" indicates something that is self-generated or inherent.

  • Significance: This mantra highlights the dual themes of new life and the nurturing of human values. It suggests that with the arrival of a new child, there is a responsibility to cultivate and uphold the values that sustain humanity. The fire symbolizes the inner strength and spirit that must be maintained for the well-being of society.
  • Real-life Application: In contemporary life, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as:
    • Parenting: Parents can reflect on the importance of nurturing their children not just physically but also morally and ethically, ensuring they grow up with strong values.
    • Community Building: Communities can take inspiration from this mantra to foster environments that support new generations, emphasizing education, compassion, and cooperation.
    • Personal Growth: Individuals can interpret the mantra as a call to embrace new beginnings in their own lives, whether it be through personal development, career changes, or new relationships.

  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a reminder of the cyclical nature of life and the importance of nurturing the new while upholding the values that bind society together. It encourages us to recognize the potential in every new life and the collective responsibility we share in fostering a harmonious and supportive environment for future generations.
