वामी वामस्य धूतयः प्रणीतिर्मस्तु सूनृताः |देवस्य वा मरुतो मार्त्यस्य वेजानस्य प्रयज्यवः ||
vāmī vāmasya dhūtayaḥ praṇītir astu sūnṛtā | devasya vā maruto martyasya vejānasya prayajyavaḥ ||
  • vāmī vāmasya dhūtayaḥ: This phrase can be translated as "the one who is the messenger of the good." Here, "vāmī" refers to a benevolent or good entity, while "dhūtayaḥ" signifies the act of conveying or delivering. In real life, this can be likened to individuals or organizations that promote positive messages, such as non-profit organizations advocating for social justice or environmental protection.
  • praṇītir astu sūnṛtā: This translates to "may there be a well-formed intention." "Praṇītir" refers to intention or purpose, and "sūnṛtā" means well-formed or righteous. This can be seen in the context of setting goals in life, where having a clear and righteous intention is crucial for achieving success. For example, a student who aims to excel in academics with the intention of contributing positively to society embodies this principle.
  • devasya vā maruto: This part means "of the divine or the winds." "Devasya" refers to the divine or gods, while "maruto" refers to the winds, often associated with strength and movement. In a real-life scenario, this can be interpreted as the forces of nature that guide us, such as the wind that can symbolize change and the divine guidance we seek in our lives. For instance, when embarking on a new journey, one might seek blessings from a higher power for guidance.
  • martyasya vejānasya prayajyavaḥ: This translates to "the strength of mortals." "Martyasya" refers to mortals or human beings, "vejānasya" signifies strength or vigor, and "prayajyavaḥ" means to be endowed with. This can be related to the human spirit and resilience in overcoming challenges. For example, individuals facing adversity, such as illness or personal loss, often draw upon their inner strength to persevere and thrive.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of good intentions, divine guidance, and the strength of humanity. It serves as a reminder that our actions should be rooted in benevolence and righteousness, and that we can draw upon both divine support and our inner strength to navigate life's challenges. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire individuals to pursue their goals with a clear purpose, seek guidance in times of uncertainty, and harness their resilience in the face of adversity. Its significance lies in promoting a harmonious balance between our intentions, actions, and the support we seek from the universe.
