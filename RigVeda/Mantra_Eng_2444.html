पितुश्चिदूदहरजनुषा विवेद व्यस्य धारासृजद्विधेनाः |गुहा चरन्तं सकिभिः शिवेभिर दिवो यह्वीभिर न गुहा बभूव ||
pituś cid ūdhar januṣā viveda vy asya dhārā asṛjad vi dhenāḥ | guhā carantaṁ sakhibhiḥ śivebhir divo yahvībhir na guhā babhūva ||
  • pituś cid ūdhar: This phrase can be translated as "from the belly of the father." It signifies the origin or source of life, emphasizing the importance of paternal lineage and the nurturing aspect of a father.
  • januṣā viveda: This means "he who has been born." It highlights the act of birth and the beginning of existence, suggesting that life is a journey that starts from a specific point.
  • vy asya dhārā asṛjad vi dhenāḥ: This translates to "the streams of this one have been made to flow." It indicates the flow of life and resources, akin to how rivers nourish the land and sustain life, much like a mother’s nurturing role.
  • guhā carantaṁ: This means "the one who moves in the cave." The cave symbolizes the womb or a place of safety and growth, representing the protective environment necessary for development.
  • sakhibhiḥ śivebhir: This translates to "with friends who are auspicious." It emphasizes the importance of companionship and community in one’s life journey, suggesting that positive relationships contribute to well-being.
  • divo yahvībhir: This means "with the rays of heaven." It signifies divine blessings and the connection between the earthly and the celestial, suggesting that life is enriched by spiritual guidance.
  • na guhā babhūva: This translates to "there was no cave." It implies a state of openness and clarity, suggesting that enlightenment comes from understanding and awareness.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda encapsulates the essence of life, emphasizing the roles of lineage, nurturing, community, and divine connection. In real life, it can be applied to the importance of family bonds, the nurturing role of parents, and the significance of friendships and community support in personal growth. It also highlights the need for spiritual awareness and clarity in one’s life journey. By recognizing these elements, individuals can foster a more fulfilling and enriched life, grounded in strong relationships and a connection to something greater than themselves.
