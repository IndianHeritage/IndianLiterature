आ ते शुष्मो वृषभ एतु पश्चाद उत्तराद अधराद आ पुरस्तात् |आ विश्वतो अभि सम एत्व अर्वाङ इन्द्र द्युम्नं स्वर्वद धेहि अस्मे ||
ā te śuṣmo vṛṣabha etu paścād ottarād adharād ā purastāt | ā viśvato abhi sam etv arvāṅ indra dyumnaṁ svarvad dhehy asme ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā te: from you
    • śuṣmo: the best or the most excellent
    • vṛṣabha: the bull, symbolizing strength and power
    • etu: may come
    • paścād: from behind
    • ottarād: from above
    • adharād: from below
    • ā purastāt: from the front
    • ā viśvato: from all sides
    • abhi: towards
    • sam: together
    • etv: may come
    • arvāṅ: all beings
    • indra: the god Indra, representing power and leadership
    • dyumnaṁ: glory or fame
    • svarvad: in all directions
    • dhehy: may be established
    • asme: for us
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the divine presence and strength from all directions—front, back, above, below, and from all sides.
    • It emphasizes the importance of seeking blessings and support from the divine, particularly from Indra, who symbolizes power and leadership.
    • The mention of "arvāṅ" signifies the inclusivity of all beings, suggesting that the blessings sought are not just for the individual but for the collective.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a challenging situation, such as a team project at work, invoking this mantra can serve as a reminder to seek support and collaboration from all team members, ensuring that everyone contributes their strengths.
    • During personal struggles, such as health issues, one might recite this mantra to invoke strength and healing from all aspects of life—physical, emotional, and spiritual.
    • In community gatherings or rituals, this mantra can be used to foster unity and collective strength, encouraging participants to support one another.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for holistic support and strength from the divine and the universe.
    • It teaches us the value of community, collaboration, and the interconnectedness of all beings.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied to foster teamwork, seek divine guidance, and promote collective well-being, reminding us that we are never alone in our endeavors.
