अवā कल्पेषु नः पुमस तमांसी सोम योद्ध्या |तानि पुनान जङ्घनः ||
avā kalpeṣu naḥ pumas tamāṁsi soma yodhyā | tāni punāna jaṅghanaḥ ||
  • Mantra: "avā kalpeṣu naḥ pumas tamāṁsi soma yodhyā | tāni punāna jaṅghanaḥ ||"
  • Translation: "In the various cycles of existence, may the divine Soma, the essence of life, protect us from darkness and grant us strength to overcome challenges."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • avā: May it protect
    • kalpeṣu: In the cycles of existence
    • naḥ: Us
    • pumas: The essence or strength
    • tamāṁsi: Darkness or ignorance
    • soma: A divine drink, symbolizing life and vitality
    • yodhyā: To fight or overcome
    • tāni: Those
    • punāna: Purifying or cleansing
    • jaṅghanaḥ: The thighs or the foundation of strength
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra invokes the protective qualities of Soma, which is often associated with nourishment and vitality. In real life, this can be likened to seeking support from friends and family during tough times.
    • The reference to "darkness" symbolizes ignorance or challenges we face in life. For example, when someone is going through a difficult phase, such as a job loss or personal crisis, this mantra serves as a reminder to seek strength and clarity.
    • The idea of "fighting" against darkness can be related to the struggles individuals face in their careers or personal lives. It encourages resilience and the pursuit of knowledge and understanding to overcome obstacles.
    • The mention of "purifying" suggests the importance of self-reflection and growth. For instance, after a failure, one might take time to learn from the experience and emerge stronger, much like how a phoenix rises from its ashes.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of seeking divine support and inner strength to navigate life's challenges. It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, such as during personal struggles, professional setbacks, or moments of uncertainty.
    • By reciting this mantra, individuals can cultivate a mindset of resilience, encouraging them to confront their fears and emerge victorious, much like a warrior in battle.
    • Ultimately, it serves as a reminder that with the right support and determination, one can overcome any darkness and achieve clarity and strength in life.
