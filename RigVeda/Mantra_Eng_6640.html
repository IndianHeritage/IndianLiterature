इदां हि व उपस्तुतिम इदां वामस्य भक्तये |उप वो विश्ववेदसो नमस्युर आम् असृक्ष्य अन्याम् इव ||
idā hi va upastutim idā vāmasya bhaktaye | upa vo viśvavedaso namasyur ām̐ asṛkṣy anyām iva ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • idā: Here, in this context.
    • hi: Indeed, certainly.
    • va: Or, either.
    • upastutim: Praise, worship, or adoration.
    • vāmasya: Of the left, often associated with gentleness or nurturing.
    • bhaktaye: For devotion or worship.
    • upa: Near, close to.
    • vo: Your, of you.
    • viśvavedaso: All-knowing, omniscient.
    • namasyur: They bow down, they offer respect.
    • ām̐: A sacred sound, often used in mantras.
    • asṛkṣy: To protect, to safeguard.
    • anyām: Other, different.
    • iva: Like, as if.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of devotion and worship.
    • It suggests that through sincere praise and adoration, one can connect with the divine.
    • The reference to "vāmasya" indicates a nurturing aspect of the divine, which can be related to the feminine energy in nature.
    • The phrase "viśvavedaso" highlights the omniscience of the divine, suggesting that all knowledge and wisdom are accessible through devotion.
    • The act of bowing down signifies humility and respect, essential qualities in any spiritual practice.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, individuals often express gratitude and reverence through rituals, such as lighting a lamp or offering flowers, which can be seen as a form of "upastutim."
    • In relationships, showing respect and nurturing qualities can strengthen bonds, akin to the "vāmasya" aspect of the mantra.
    • In moments of uncertainty, seeking knowledge and wisdom through prayer or meditation reflects the essence of "viśvavedaso."
    • Acts of kindness and humility in community service can be viewed as a practical application of the teachings in this mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the significance of devotion and respect in our lives.
    • It encourages individuals to cultivate a relationship with the divine through praise and humility.
    • In a world often filled with chaos, the principles of nurturing, respect, and seeking wisdom can lead to personal growth and community harmony.
    • Ultimately, the mantra teaches that through sincere devotion, one can find protection and guidance in life's journey.
