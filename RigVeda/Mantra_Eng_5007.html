त्वं देवी सरस्वती अवा वाजेषु वाजिनी |रदा पूषेवा नः सन्निम ||
tvaṁ devi sarasvaty avā vājeṣu vājini | radā pūṣeva naḥ sanim ||
  • tvaṁ - This word translates to "you," referring to the divine feminine energy or goddess.
  • devi - This means "goddess," indicating a revered and powerful feminine presence.
  • sarasvaty - This refers to "Saraswati," the goddess of knowledge, music, art, wisdom, and learning.
  • avā - This means "may you protect" or "may you bestow," indicating a plea for guidance and support.
  • vājeṣu - This translates to "in the offerings" or "in the rewards," suggesting the context of abundance and prosperity.
  • vājini - This means "the one who grants rewards" or "the bestower of wealth," emphasizing the goddess's role in providing blessings.
  • radā - This can be interpreted as "like a charioteer" or "like a guide," symbolizing direction and leadership.
  • pūṣeva - This means "like a nourisher" or "like a provider," indicating the nurturing aspect of the goddess.
  • naḥ - This translates to "for us," indicating a collective plea or request for blessings.
  • sanim - This means "may you grant us" or "may you bestow upon us," reinforcing the desire for divine favor.

The mantra can be understood as a prayer to Goddess Saraswati, seeking her blessings for knowledge, prosperity, and guidance. It emphasizes the importance of divine support in achieving success and abundance in life.


In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applicable in various contexts:

  • Education: Students can recite this mantra before exams or while studying to seek the goddess's blessings for wisdom and clarity of thought.
  • Creative Pursuits: Artists, musicians, and writers may invoke this mantra to inspire creativity and enhance their skills.
  • Professional Growth: Individuals seeking career advancement can use this mantra to attract opportunities and success in their endeavors.

Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its invocation of divine feminine energy, particularly that of Goddess Saraswati, to seek knowledge, prosperity, and guidance. It serves as a reminder of the importance of nurturing one's intellect and creativity while recognizing the need for divine support in achieving one's goals. By incorporating this mantra into daily life, individuals can cultivate a mindset of abundance and wisdom, ultimately leading to personal and professional growth.
