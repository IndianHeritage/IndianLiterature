इमं ऊषु त्वं अस्माकं सनिं गायत्रं नव्यांसम् |अग्नि देवेषु प्र वोचः ||
imam ū ṣu tvam asmākaṁ saniṁ gāyatraṁ navyāṁsam | agne deveṣu pra vocaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • imam: this
    • ū: indeed
    • ṣu: good
    • tvam: you
    • asmākaṁ: of us
    • saniṁ: together
    • gāyatraṁ: the song or hymn
    • navyāṁsam: new or fresh
    • agne: O Fire (Agni)
    • deveṣu: among the gods
    • pra: forth
    • vocaḥ: speak or declare
  • Overall Meaning: This mantra is a call to Agni, the divine fire, to bless and inspire us with a new hymn that we can sing together. It emphasizes the importance of unity and the divine presence in our lives.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • Unity in Community: Just as the mantra calls for collective singing, communities often come together in festivals or gatherings to celebrate unity and shared values, such as Diwali or Thanksgiving.
    • Seeking Inspiration: In moments of challenge, individuals often seek inspiration from a higher power or from nature, similar to how the mantra invokes Agni for guidance and strength.
    • New Beginnings: The reference to "new" can be related to starting new projects or phases in life, such as a new job or a new relationship, where one seeks blessings for success.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its invocation of divine energy and the importance of community. It teaches us that in our personal and collective journeys, we can seek guidance and inspiration from higher powers, fostering unity and shared purpose. This mantra can be applicable in various life scenarios, such as during communal prayers, celebrations, or personal reflections, reminding us of the strength found in togetherness and the divine.
