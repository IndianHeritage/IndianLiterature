अत्यं हविः सचते सच्च धातु चारीष्टगातुः स होतā सहोभरीः |प्रसर्स्राणो अनु बरहिर वृषा शिशुर मध्य युवाजरो विस्रुहा हितः ||
atyaṁ haviḥ sacate sac ca dhātu cāriṣṭagātuḥ sa hotā sahobhariḥ | prasarsrāṇo anu barhir vṛṣā śiśur madhye yuvājaro visruhā hitaḥ ||
  • atyaṁ haviḥ: This phrase can be interpreted as "the supreme offering" or "the ultimate sacrifice." In real life, this can relate to the idea of giving one's best effort in any endeavor, whether it be in work, relationships, or personal growth.
  • sacate sac ca: This translates to "truth and truthfulness." In everyday life, this emphasizes the importance of honesty and integrity in our actions and words, fostering trust in relationships and communities.
  • dhātu cāriṣṭagātuḥ: This can be understood as "the essence of existence" or "the fundamental nature of reality." It reminds us to connect with our core values and the essence of what it means to be human, encouraging self-reflection and mindfulness.
  • sa hotā sahobhariḥ: This phrase refers to "the one who invokes" or "the priest who offers." In a modern context, this can symbolize leadership and the role of individuals who inspire and guide others towards a common goal or purpose.
  • prasarsrāṇo anu barhir: This translates to "flowing forth from the earth." It signifies the connection between humanity and nature, reminding us of our responsibility to care for the environment and live sustainably.
  • vṛṣā śiśur madhye: This can be interpreted as "the young one in the midst." It highlights the importance of nurturing the younger generation, ensuring they are equipped with the knowledge and values needed to thrive in society.
  • yuvājaro visruhā hitaḥ: This phrase means "the youthful and beneficial." It encourages us to embrace innovation and new ideas, recognizing that progress often comes from the energy and creativity of the youth.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates profound wisdom about the interconnectedness of truth, leadership, nature, and the nurturing of future generations. Its significance lies in its applicability to our daily lives, urging us to strive for excellence, uphold integrity, care for our environment, and invest in the youth. By embodying these principles, we can contribute to a more harmonious and sustainable world.
