ऋतम् ऋतेन सपन्तेशिरं दक्षम् आशाते |अद्रुहा देवौ वर्धते ||
ṛtam ṛtena sapanteṣiraṁ dakṣam āśāte | adruhā devau vardhete ||
  • Meaning of the Mantra:
    • ṛtam: This term refers to the cosmic order or truth. It signifies the underlying principle that governs the universe, representing harmony and balance.
    • ṛtena: This is the instrumental form of "ṛtam," indicating the means or method through which the cosmic order is maintained.
    • sapanteṣiraṁ: This word can be interpreted as "those who are in harmony" or "those who follow the truth." It suggests a collective effort towards maintaining the cosmic order.
    • dakṣam: This term means "skilled" or "capable." It implies that those who adhere to the cosmic order are endowed with the ability to achieve their goals effectively.
    • āśāte: This word translates to "they desire" or "they aspire." It indicates the aspirations of individuals or groups who align themselves with the cosmic order.
    • adruhā: This term means "without enmity" or "harmonious." It emphasizes the importance of peaceful coexistence and collaboration among individuals.
    • devau: This refers to the divine or celestial beings. It signifies the higher powers that support and bless those who follow the path of truth.
    • vardhete: This means "they flourish" or "they prosper." It indicates that those who align with the cosmic order and maintain harmony will experience growth and success.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace, when employees work collaboratively and adhere to ethical practices (ṛtam), they create a positive environment (sapanteṣiraṁ) that leads to greater productivity (dakṣam) and job satisfaction (āśāte).
    • In community living, when individuals resolve conflicts peacefully (adruhā) and support each other, the community thrives (vardhete) and becomes a better place for everyone.
    • In personal relationships, practicing honesty and transparency (ṛtena) fosters trust and strengthens bonds, leading to a fulfilling life (āśāte).
  • Conclusion:
    • The mantra emphasizes the significance of aligning oneself with the cosmic order (ṛtam) and the benefits that arise from such alignment. It teaches that through cooperation, skill, and peaceful interactions, individuals and communities can flourish.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra serves as a guiding principle for ethical behavior, teamwork, and harmonious living, reminding us that our actions have a direct impact on our success and well-being.
    • Ultimately, the mantra encourages us to aspire for a life rooted in truth and harmony, leading to prosperity and fulfillment in all aspects of life.
