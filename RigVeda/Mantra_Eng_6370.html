सत्रा त्वं पुरुष्तुतमं एको वृत्राणि तोषसे |नान्य इन्द्रात करणं भूय इन्बति ||
satrā tvam puruṣṭutam̐ eko vṛtrāṇi tośase | nānya indrāt karaṇam bhūya invati ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • satrā: In the context of the mantra, it refers to the "enemy" or "obstacle."
    • tvam: This means "you," addressing a divine entity.
    • puruṣṭutam̐: This translates to "the most praised" or "the one who is worthy of worship."
    • eko: This means "one" or "the only one."
    • vṛtrāṇi: This refers to "the obstacles" or "the forces of obstruction."
    • tośase: This means "to be pleased" or "to grant satisfaction."
    • nānya: This translates to "no other" or "none else."
    • indrāt: This refers to "Indra," the king of gods, symbolizing power and strength.
    • karaṇam: This means "action" or "deed."
    • bhūya: This translates to "again" or "more."
    • invati: This means "to invoke" or "to call upon."
  • Overall Meaning:
    • The mantra addresses a divine entity, acknowledging its supreme power in overcoming obstacles.
    • It emphasizes the singularity of this divine force, suggesting that it is the only one capable of providing assistance against adversities.
    • By invoking this power, one seeks to gain strength and support in overcoming challenges.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a personal context, when facing challenges such as job loss or health issues, individuals may recite this mantra to seek divine support and strength to overcome these obstacles.
    • In a community setting, during times of crisis, such as natural disasters, people may come together to invoke this divine power for collective strength and resilience.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of recognizing a higher power in overcoming life's challenges.
    • It teaches that in times of difficulty, turning to a divine source can provide the strength and support needed to navigate through obstacles.
    • In practical terms, it encourages individuals and communities to seek unity and strength in faith, fostering resilience in the face of adversity.
