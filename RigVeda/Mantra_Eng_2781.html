उपो नयस्व वृषणा तपुष्पोतेम अव तवं वृषभ स्वधावः |ग्रसेताम अश्वा वि मुच्हेह शोणā दिवे-दिवे सदृशीर अधि धानाः ||
upo nayasva vṛṣaṇā tapuṣpotem ava tvaṁ vṛṣabha svadhāvaḥ | grasetām aśvā vi muceha śoṇā dive-dive sadṛśīr addhi dhānāḥ ||
  • Upo nayasva: This phrase can be interpreted as "lead us" or "guide us." It signifies the need for direction and support in our lives.
  • Vṛṣaṇā: This term refers to strength or power. It embodies the idea of harnessing inner strength to overcome challenges.
  • Tapuṣpotem: This word can be understood as "the one who is radiant" or "the one who shines." It suggests the importance of enlightenment and wisdom in our journey.
  • Ava tvaṁ vṛṣabha svadhāvaḥ: This phrase translates to "may you, O bull, grant us your essence." The bull symbolizes strength and stability, representing the qualities we seek to embody.
  • Grasetām aśvā: This means "may the horses consume." Horses often symbolize energy and movement, indicating the need for vitality in our pursuits.
  • Vi muceha śoṇā: This can be interpreted as "may we be freed from obstacles." It emphasizes the desire for liberation from challenges that hinder our progress.
  • Dive-dive: This phrase means "day by day," suggesting a continuous journey of growth and improvement.
  • Sadṛśīr addhi dhānāḥ: This translates to "may we attain similar qualities." It reflects the aspiration to develop virtues that lead to success and fulfillment.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a profound message about seeking guidance, strength, and enlightenment in our daily lives. It emphasizes the importance of overcoming obstacles and striving for continuous growth. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as personal development, leadership, and teamwork. For instance, in a corporate setting, a leader may invoke this mantra to inspire their team to work together harmoniously, harness their strengths, and overcome challenges collectively. Ultimately, it serves as a reminder of the virtues we should aspire to embody in our journey through life.
