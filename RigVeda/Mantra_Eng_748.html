ऋतस्य देवाः अनु व्रता गुरु भुवत् परिष्टिः द्यौः न भूमः ||
ṛtasya devā anu vratā gur bhuvat pariṣṭir dyaur na bhūma ||
  • Meaning of the Mantra:
    • ṛtasya: Refers to "truth" or "cosmic order." In real life, this can be seen as the principles of honesty and integrity that govern our actions.
    • devā: Means "gods" or "divine beings." This can symbolize higher ideals or forces that guide us, such as moral values or spiritual beliefs.
    • anu: Translates to "following" or "in accordance with." This suggests the importance of aligning our actions with the truth or cosmic order.
    • vratā: Refers to "vows" or "commitments." In everyday life, this can relate to the promises we make to ourselves and others, emphasizing the importance of keeping our word.
    • gur: Means "great" or "important." This highlights the significance of the principles we follow and the values we uphold.
    • bhuvat: Translates to "becoming" or "manifesting." This indicates the process of bringing our commitments and values into reality through our actions.
    • pariṣṭir: Refers to "surrounding" or "encompassing." This can be understood as the environment or community that supports our journey towards truth and integrity.
    • dyaur: Means "heaven" or "sky." This symbolizes the higher aspirations and goals we strive for in life.
    • na: Translates to "not." This negation emphasizes the importance of avoiding actions that go against the cosmic order or truth.
    • bhūma: Refers to "earth" or "ground." This represents our physical existence and the reality we live in, reminding us to stay grounded while pursuing higher ideals.
  • Significance and Real-Life Application:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of living in accordance with truth and integrity. In a world where dishonesty can often prevail, it serves as a reminder to uphold our values and commitments.
    • In professional settings, this can translate to ethical business practices, where companies prioritize transparency and accountability, fostering trust among employees and customers.
    • In personal relationships, it encourages individuals to be truthful and reliable, strengthening bonds and creating a supportive community.
    • On a broader scale, this mantra can inspire social movements that advocate for justice and equality, aligning collective actions with the principles of truth and righteousness.
  • Conclusion:
    • The mantra "ṛtasya devā anu vratā gur bhuvat pariṣṭir dyaur na bhūma" serves as a profound reminder of the significance of truth and integrity in our lives.
    • By aligning our actions with these principles, we can create a more harmonious and just society, both in our personal lives and in the larger community.
    • Ultimately, it encourages us to strive for higher ideals while remaining grounded in our reality, fostering a balanced approach to life.
