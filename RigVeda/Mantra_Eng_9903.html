आ त्वा हर्यन्तं प्रयुजो जनानां रथे वहन्तु हरिशिप्रम इन्द्र |पिबा यथा प्रतिभृतस्य मध्वो हर्यन यज्ञं सदहमादे दशोणिम ||
ā tvā haryantam prayujo janānāṁ rathe vahantu hariśipram indra | pibā yathā pratibhṛtasya madhvo haryan yajñaṁ sadhamāde daśoṇim ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā: O
    • tvā: you
    • haryantam: who is joyful or brings joy
    • prayujo: may be invoked or called upon
    • janānāṁ: of the people
    • rathe: in the chariot
    • vahantu: may carry or lead
    • hariśipram: swiftly, like a horse
    • indra: O Indra, the king of gods
    • pibā: drink
    • yathā: as
    • pratibhṛtasya: of the one who is filled with delight
    • madhvo: of the sweet (nectar)
    • haryan: joyful
    • yajñaṁ: the sacrifice or offering
    • sadhamāde: with good results
    • daśoṇim: tenfold or abundant
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Indra, the king of gods, asking him to bring joy and prosperity to the people.
    • The invocation of Indra signifies the importance of divine support in human endeavors, especially in communal activities like sacrifices (yajñas).
    • The reference to a chariot symbolizes the journey of life, where divine guidance is sought to navigate challenges.
    • The act of drinking the sweet nectar represents the enjoyment of the fruits of one's labor and the blessings that come from divine favor.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community festival, people often pray for blessings and prosperity, similar to how this mantra seeks divine intervention for joy and abundance.
    • During significant life events, such as weddings or births, families may perform rituals invoking divine blessings, akin to the yajña mentioned in the mantra.
    • In modern contexts, individuals may seek guidance and support from higher powers or mentors when embarking on new ventures, reflecting the essence of invoking Indra for assistance.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of divine support in achieving joy and prosperity in life.
    • It highlights the importance of community and collective efforts in seeking blessings for success.
    • In real-life scenarios, it serves as a reminder to seek guidance and support from higher powers or the community when facing challenges, reinforcing the interconnectedness of individuals and the divine.
