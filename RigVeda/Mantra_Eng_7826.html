एष रुक्मिभिर ईयते वाजी शुभ्रेभिर अंशुभिः |पतिः सिंधूनाम भवन ||
eṣa rukmibhir īyate vājī śubhrebhir aṁśubhiḥ | patiḥ sindhūnām bhavan ||
  • eṣa rukmibhir īyate: This phrase can be translated to "this one moves with golden rays." The term "rukmibhir" refers to something that is golden or radiant, symbolizing prosperity and abundance. In real life, this can be likened to the sun, which provides light and energy, essential for life and growth.
  • vājī śubhrebhir aṁśubhiḥ: This translates to "the one who is endowed with auspicious qualities." The word "vājī" can be associated with strength and vitality, while "śubhrebhir" refers to auspiciousness or good fortune. This can be seen in individuals who possess qualities that lead to success, such as determination, kindness, and intelligence. For example, a leader who inspires their team through positive actions and decisions embodies these qualities.
  • patiḥ sindhūnām bhavan: This means "the lord of the rivers." Here, "patiḥ" signifies lordship or mastery, and "sindhūnām" refers to rivers. This can be interpreted as a metaphor for someone who guides and nurtures, much like a river that sustains life along its banks. In a real-life context, this could represent a mentor or a parent who provides guidance and support to their children or mentees, helping them navigate through challenges.

  • Significance of the Mantra: This mantra emphasizes the importance of radiance, auspicious qualities, and guidance. It highlights the interconnectedness of prosperity, strength, and nurturing leadership.
  • Real-Life Application: In everyday life, this mantra can inspire individuals to cultivate their inner qualities, seek prosperity, and become guiding figures in their communities. For instance, a teacher who encourages students to shine in their own unique ways embodies the essence of this mantra.
  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a reminder of the values of radiance, auspiciousness, and guidance. By embodying these qualities, individuals can contribute positively to their surroundings, fostering a sense of community and support that benefits everyone.
