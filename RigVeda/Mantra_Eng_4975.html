यास ते पूषण नावो अन्तः समुद्रे हिरण्यवीर अन्तरिक्षे चरन्ति |ताभिर्यासी दूत्यां सूर्यस्य कामेन कृत श्रव इच्छमानः ||
yās te pūṣan nāvo antaḥ samudre hiraṇyayīr antarikṣe caranti | tābhir yāsi dūtyāṁ sūryasya kāmena kṛta śrava icchamānaḥ ||
  • yās te pūṣan: This phrase refers to the divine being Pūṣan, who is often associated with nourishment and prosperity. In real life, this can be likened to the role of a provider or caretaker in a family or community, ensuring that everyone has what they need to thrive.
  • nāvo antaḥ samudre: This translates to "the ship within the ocean." It symbolizes the journey of life, where we navigate through various challenges and experiences. For example, consider a person starting a new job; they are like a ship setting sail into the vast ocean of opportunities and uncertainties.
  • hiraṇyayīr antarikṣe caranti: This means "they move in the golden space." Here, "golden" can represent the richness of experiences and knowledge we gain as we navigate life. In a practical sense, this could refer to individuals pursuing education or personal growth, moving towards their goals with ambition and hope.
  • tābhir yāsi dūtyāṁ: This phrase means "with them, you go as a messenger." It emphasizes the importance of communication and connection. In everyday life, this can be seen in how we share ideas and collaborate with others, whether in a workplace or community setting.
  • sūryasya kāmena: This translates to "desiring the sun." The sun symbolizes energy, vitality, and enlightenment. In real life, this can be interpreted as striving for success and happiness, much like how plants reach for sunlight to grow. For instance, an athlete training for a competition embodies this desire for excellence.
  • kṛta śrava icchamānaḥ: This means "seeking the fame that is earned." It highlights the value of hard work and the pursuit of recognition through one's efforts. In modern contexts, this can relate to professionals who seek to build their reputations through dedication and skill, such as artists or entrepreneurs.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the journey of life, emphasizing the importance of nourishment, communication, ambition, and the pursuit of earned recognition. It serves as a reminder that, like a ship navigating the ocean, we must embrace our experiences, seek knowledge, and strive for our goals while maintaining connections with others. In real life, this mantra can inspire individuals to approach their personal and professional lives with purpose and determination, fostering growth and success in their endeavors.
