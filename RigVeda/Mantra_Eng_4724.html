एवाऽजज्ञानं सहसे असामि वावृद्धानं राधसे च श्रुताय |महाम उग्रं अवसे विप्र नूनम आ विवासेम वृततूर्येषु ||
evā jajñānaṁ sahase asāmi vāvṛdhānaṁ rādhase ca śrutāya | mahām ugram avase vipra nūnam ā vivāsema vṛtratūryeṣu ||
  • evā jajñānaṁ: This phrase can be translated as "thus I have been born" or "thus I have come into existence." It signifies the acknowledgment of one's origin and the journey of life.
  • sahase asāmi: This means "I am indeed strong" or "I am powerful." It reflects the inner strength and resilience that individuals possess.
  • vāvṛdhānaṁ: This term refers to "growth" or "expansion." It emphasizes the importance of personal and spiritual development throughout life.
  • rādhase ca śrutāya: This can be interpreted as "to the one who is worthy of praise and knowledge." It highlights the value of wisdom and the pursuit of knowledge in one's life.
  • mahām ugram: Translated as "great and fierce," this phrase suggests the power and intensity of the divine or universal forces that govern existence.
  • avase vipra: This means "the wise one" or "the learned." It signifies the importance of wisdom and intellect in navigating life's challenges.
  • nūnam ā vivāsema: This can be understood as "surely we shall live" or "we shall indeed thrive." It conveys a sense of optimism and determination to succeed.
  • vṛtratūryeṣu: This term refers to "in the face of obstacles" or "in the presence of challenges." It acknowledges the difficulties one may encounter in life.

In conclusion, this mantra from the Rigveda encapsulates the essence of human existence, emphasizing the journey of life, the strength within, the pursuit of knowledge, and the resilience to overcome challenges. It serves as a reminder that despite the obstacles we face, we possess the inner power to grow and thrive. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as personal development, education, and overcoming adversity. It encourages individuals to embrace their origins, harness their strengths, seek wisdom, and remain determined in the face of challenges, ultimately leading to a fulfilling and meaningful life.
