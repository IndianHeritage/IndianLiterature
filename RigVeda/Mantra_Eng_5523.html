भग एव भगवाम् अस्तु देवास् तेन वयं भगवन्तः स्याम |तं त्वा भग सर्व इज जोहवीति स नो भग पुरेतā भविह ||
bhaga eva bhagavām̐ astu devās tena vayam bhagavantaḥ syāma | taṁ tvā bhaga sarva ij johavīti sa no bhaga puraetā bhaveha ||
  • Translation: "May the divine wealth (bhaga) be ours, may we be blessed with prosperity. We invoke you, O wealth, may we be endowed with abundance. May this wealth come to us from all directions."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • Bhaga: Refers to wealth, prosperity, or divine abundance.
    • Bhagavām̐: The one who possesses wealth or is blessed.
    • Devās: The divine beings or gods.
    • Vayam: We or us.
    • Syāma: May we become or may we be.
    • Ij: To invoke or to call upon.
    • Johavīti: To offer or to present.
    • Puraetā: From all directions or from all sides.
    • Bhaveha: May it come to us or may it manifest.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be recited during the opening of a new venture, invoking prosperity and success in the enterprise.
    • During festivals or family gatherings, individuals may chant this mantra to seek blessings for abundance and well-being for all members of the family.
    • In personal finance, one might reflect on this mantra while setting financial goals, reminding themselves to seek not just wealth, but a holistic abundance that includes health and happiness.
  • Significance:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of divine blessings in achieving prosperity. It acknowledges that while hard work is essential, the grace of the divine plays a crucial role in attaining wealth.
    • It serves as a reminder to be grateful for the abundance one has and to seek blessings for continued prosperity.
    • The mantra can be applicable in various life scenarios, such as starting new projects, seeking career advancements, or even during personal challenges where one seeks support and abundance in life.
