तस्मा अग्निर भारतः शर्मा यं सज ज्योक पश्यात सूर्यं उच्छरन्तम् |य इन्द्राय सुनवामेति आह नरे नार्याय नृतमाय नृत्नाम् ||
tasmā agnir bhārataḥ śarma yaṁsaj jyok paśyāt sūryam uccarantam | ya indrāya sunavāmety āha nare naryāya nṛtamāya nṛṇām ||
  • tasmā agnir bhārataḥ: This phrase can be translated as "therefore, the fire (Agnir) is the source of prosperity (Bhārata)." In real life, fire symbolizes energy, transformation, and the spark of life. For example, in cooking, fire transforms raw ingredients into nourishing meals, which is essential for sustenance.
  • śarma yaṁsaj: This means "the shelter or protection that is obtained." In a modern context, this can refer to the safety and security we seek in our homes and communities. For instance, a family home provides emotional and physical shelter to its members.
  • jyok paśyāt sūryam uccarantam: This translates to "may we see the sun (Sūrya) shining brightly." The sun is often associated with knowledge, enlightenment, and vitality. In everyday life, seeking knowledge and enlightenment can be likened to pursuing education and personal growth, which illuminate our paths.
  • ya indrāya: This phrase means "to Indra," who is the king of the gods and represents strength and power. In real life, this can be seen in leadership roles where individuals strive to embody strength and wisdom, guiding others towards success.
  • sunavāmety āha: This can be interpreted as "may we speak with clarity and purpose." In communication, clarity is vital for effective relationships, whether in personal or professional settings. For example, clear communication in a workplace can lead to better teamwork and project outcomes.
  • nare naryāya nṛtamāya nṛṇām: This translates to "for the benefit of humanity (nṛṇām)." It emphasizes the importance of actions that serve the greater good. In real life, this can be seen in community service and social initiatives aimed at improving the lives of others, such as volunteering at local shelters or participating in environmental conservation efforts.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking prosperity, protection, enlightenment, strength, clarity in communication, and the welfare of humanity. Its significance lies in the holistic approach it advocates for living a balanced and purposeful life. In real-life scenarios, these principles can guide individuals in their personal and professional endeavors, encouraging them to contribute positively to society while pursuing their own growth and well-being.
