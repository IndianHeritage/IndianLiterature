यस् ते रथो मनसो जवीयान इन्द्र तेन सोमपेयाय याहि |तूयम् आ ते हरयः प्र द्रवन्तु येभिर यासि वृषभिर मंदमानः ||
yas te ratho manaso javīyān endra tena somapeyāya yāhi | tūyam ā te harayaḥ pra dravantu yebhir yāsi vṛṣabhir mandamānaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yas: who
    • te: your
    • ratho: chariot
    • manaso: of the mind
    • javīyān: swift
    • endram: Indra (the king of gods)
    • tena: by that
    • somapeyāya: for the sake of drinking Soma (a sacred drink)
    • yāhi: come
    • tūyam: you
    • ā: indeed
    • te: your
    • harayaḥ: horses
    • pra: forward
    • dravantu: may they run
    • yebhir: with which
    • yāsi: you go
    • vṛṣabhir: with the strong ones
    • mandamānaḥ: slowly moving
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the swift chariot of the mind, symbolizing the speed and agility of thought and intention.
    • It calls upon Indra, the god of rain and thunder, to bless the seeker with the strength and energy to pursue their goals.
    • The reference to Soma indicates a desire for spiritual nourishment and enlightenment, suggesting that the journey is not just physical but also spiritual.
    • The horses represent the vital energies or forces that propel us forward in life, urging them to move swiftly towards our aspirations.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a competitive environment, such as a corporate setting, this mantra can be seen as a reminder to harness one's mental agility and focus on achieving professional goals.
    • In personal development, it encourages individuals to seek spiritual growth and clarity, much like how one would seek knowledge through meditation or study.
    • In sports, athletes often visualize their goals and invoke a sense of speed and strength, akin to the swift chariot and strong horses mentioned in the mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of mental clarity, spiritual nourishment, and the strength to pursue one's goals.
    • Its significance lies in the reminder that both physical and mental energies are essential for success in any endeavor.
    • In real life, it can be applied in various contexts, from personal aspirations to professional achievements, encouraging individuals to harness their inner strength and clarity of thought.
