मन्त्र: आय निजघ्निर ओजसा रथसङ्गे धन हिते | स्तवाऽबिभ्युषा हृदा ||
ayā nijaghnir ojasā rathasaṁge dhane hite | stavā abibhyuṣā hṛdā ||
  • ayā - This word can be interpreted as "this" or "that," indicating a focus on a specific subject or entity.
  • nijaghnir - Translated as "the one who strikes" or "the one who kills," it refers to a powerful force or energy that can overcome obstacles.
  • ojasā - Meaning "with strength" or "with vigor," this word emphasizes the importance of energy and vitality in achieving goals.
  • rathasaṁge - This term translates to "in the company of the chariot," symbolizing movement, progress, and the journey of life.
  • dhane - Meaning "wealth" or "prosperity," it signifies the material and spiritual abundance one seeks in life.
  • hite - This word means "for the good" or "for welfare," indicating actions taken for the benefit of oneself and others.
  • stavā - Translated as "praise" or "hymn," it reflects the importance of acknowledging and honoring the divine or the forces of nature.
  • abibhyuṣā - This term means "without fear" or "fearless," suggesting a state of confidence and courage in the face of challenges.
  • hṛdā - Meaning "with the heart," it emphasizes the significance of emotions, intentions, and sincerity in one's actions.

The mantra can be interpreted as a call to harness one's inner strength and energy to overcome challenges and achieve prosperity, all while acting with a pure heart and without fear. It encourages individuals to recognize the importance of their journey in life, supported by the forces of nature and the divine.


In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts:

  • Personal Growth: When facing obstacles in personal or professional life, one can draw upon their inner strength and courage to navigate challenges, much like a charioteer guiding their chariot through difficult terrain.
  • Community Welfare: The emphasis on actions taken for the good of others can inspire individuals to engage in community service or social initiatives, promoting collective prosperity.
  • Spiritual Practice: The act of praising and honoring the divine can be integrated into daily rituals, fostering a sense of gratitude and connection to something greater than oneself.

In conclusion, this mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the importance of inner strength, courage, and the pursuit of prosperity for oneself and others. It encourages individuals to approach life with a fearless heart, recognizing the divine support available in their journey.
