अभिभुवे 'भिभङ्गाय वन्तते 'षाल्हाय सहमानाय वेधसे |तुविग्रये वह्नये दुष्टरीतवे सत्रासाहे नम इन्द्राय वाचत ||
abhibhuve 'bhibhaṅgāya vanvate 'ṣāḻhāya sahamānāya vedhase | tuvigraye vahnaye duṣṭarītave satrāsāhe nama indrāya vocata ||
  • Mantra: "abhibhuve 'bhibhaṅgāya vanvate 'ṣāḻhāya sahamānāya vedhase | tuvigraye vahnaye duṣṭarītave satrāsāhe nama indrāya vocata ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • abhibhuve: To the one who is all-pervading or omnipresent.
    • 'bhibhaṅgāya: To the one who is powerful and capable of overcoming obstacles.
    • vanvate: To the one who is worthy of praise.
    • 'ṣāḻhāya: To the one who is strong and steadfast.
    • sahamānāya: To the one who is supportive and helps others.
    • vedhase: To the one who is knowledgeable and wise.
    • tuvigraye: To the one who is fierce and powerful.
    • vahnaye: To the one who is like fire, symbolizing energy and transformation.
    • duṣṭarītave: To the one who can overcome difficult situations.
    • satrāsāhe: To the one who is a protector and a source of strength.
    • nama: Salutations or reverence.
    • indrāya: To Indra, the king of gods, representing power and authority.
    • vocata: We call upon or invoke.
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Indra, the god of rain and thunderstorms, who is often associated with strength, power, and protection.
    • The invocation highlights various attributes of Indra, such as his omnipresence, ability to overcome obstacles, and his role as a protector.
    • In real life, this mantra can be seen as a call for strength and support in challenging situations, much like how people seek guidance from mentors or leaders during difficult times.
    • For example, in a corporate setting, a team may invoke the spirit of Indra when facing a tough project deadline, seeking the strength and wisdom to overcome challenges.
    • Similarly, individuals facing personal struggles may recite this mantra to find inner strength and resilience.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its ability to inspire courage and determination in the face of adversity.
    • It serves as a reminder that one can call upon higher powers or inner strength to navigate through life's challenges.
    • In various real-life scenarios, whether in personal or professional contexts, this mantra encourages individuals to seek support and embody the qualities of strength, wisdom, and resilience.
    • Ultimately, it emphasizes the importance of community and divine support in overcoming obstacles and achieving success.
