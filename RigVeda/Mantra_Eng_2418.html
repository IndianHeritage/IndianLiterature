इन्द्रो अङ्ग महद् भयम् अभि षद् अप चुच्यवत् |स हि स्थिरो विचर्षणिः ||
indro aṅga mahad bhayam abhī ṣad apa cucyavat | sa hi sthiro vicarṣaṇiḥ ||
  • Indro: Refers to Indra, the king of the gods in Vedic mythology, symbolizing strength, power, and leadership.
  • Aṅga: Means 'part' or 'aspect', indicating that the mantra addresses a specific quality or characteristic.
  • Mahad: Translates to 'great' or 'mighty', emphasizing the significance of the quality being discussed.
  • Bhayam: Means 'fear' or 'danger', suggesting a context of overcoming challenges or threats.
  • Abhī: Translates to 'without fear' or 'fearless', indicating a state of courage and confidence.
  • Ṣad: Means 'six', which could symbolize completeness or a set of qualities.
  • Apa: Means 'away' or 'from', indicating a removal or distancing from something.
  • Cucyavat: Refers to 'having fallen' or 'having been cast away', suggesting a release from fear or danger.
  • Sa hi: Means 'he indeed', affirming the subject being discussed.
  • Sthiro: Translates to 'steady' or 'firm', indicating stability and resilience.
  • Vicarṣaṇiḥ: Refers to 'the one who moves' or 'the one who travels', suggesting a dynamic aspect of existence.

This mantra can be interpreted as a call for strength and fearlessness in the face of challenges. It emphasizes the importance of being steady and resilient, much like a leader who must navigate through difficulties while maintaining composure. For example, in a corporate setting, a manager facing a crisis must embody the qualities of Indra—being decisive and courageous, while also ensuring that their team feels secure and supported.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as:

  • Personal Challenges: When facing fears or uncertainties, one can invoke the qualities of fearlessness and steadiness to overcome obstacles.
  • Leadership: Leaders can draw inspiration from this mantra to guide their teams through turbulent times, ensuring that they remain focused and united.
  • Emotional Resilience: In moments of anxiety or stress, recalling the essence of this mantra can help individuals cultivate a sense of calm and strength.

Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its ability to inspire courage and stability in the face of fear and uncertainty. It serves as a reminder that, like Indra, we can embody strength and resilience, allowing us to navigate life's challenges with confidence. By applying these principles in our daily lives, we can foster a sense of empowerment and support for ourselves and those around us.
