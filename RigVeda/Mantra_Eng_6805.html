न्य अग्ने नव्यसा वचस तनूषु शंसम एषाम् |न्य अरातीर रराव्णां विश्वा आर्यो अरातीर इतो युच्छन्त्व आमुरो नभन्ताम् अन्यके समे ||
ny agne navyasā vacas tanūṣu śaṁsam eṣām | ny arātī rarāvṇāṁ viśvā aryo arātīr ito yucchantv āmuro nabhantām anyake same ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ny: not
    • agne: O Agni (the fire god)
    • navyasā: with new energy
    • vacas: speech or words
    • tanūṣu: in the bodies
    • śaṁsam: to praise or to glorify
    • eṣām: of these
    • arātī: obstacles or hindrances
    • rarāvṇāṁ: those who are obstructed
    • viśvā: all
    • aryo: noble or worthy
    • arātīr: obstacles
    • ito: from here
    • yucchantv: may they be removed
    • āmuro: immortal
    • nabhantām: from the heavens
    • anyake: other
    • same: in the same way
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Agni, the fire god, asking for the removal of obstacles and the enhancement of positive energy.
    • The invocation of "new energy" signifies a fresh start or rejuvenation, which can be related to personal growth or new beginnings in life.
    • The mention of "speech" and "bodies" indicates the importance of communication and physical presence in overcoming challenges.
    • By addressing "all noble ones," the mantra emphasizes the collective effort needed to overcome difficulties, suggesting that support from the community is vital.
    • The plea for the removal of obstacles can be seen as a universal desire for peace and harmony in various aspects of life, such as relationships, work, and personal endeavors.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, when a team faces challenges in a project, invoking the spirit of collaboration and communication can help in overcoming those hurdles.
    • During personal crises, individuals often seek support from friends and family, reflecting the mantra's call for collective strength to remove obstacles.
    • In community gatherings or rituals, the collective prayer for peace and harmony resonates with the essence of this mantra, aiming to create a supportive environment.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of seeking divine assistance in overcoming life's challenges while emphasizing the importance of community and communication.
    • Its application in real life can be seen in various scenarios where individuals or groups strive to achieve harmony and success by removing obstacles.
    • Ultimately, it serves as a reminder that with collective effort and divine guidance, one can navigate through difficulties and emerge stronger.
