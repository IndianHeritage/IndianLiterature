मक्षू देववत रथः शूरो वा पृत्सु कासु चित् |देवानां य इन् मनो यजमान इयक्षत्य अभीद अयजनो भुवत ||
makṣū devavato rathaḥ śūro vā pṛtsu kāsu cit | devānāṁ ya in mano yajamāna iyakṣaty abhīd ayajvano bhuvat ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • makṣū: a term often associated with the divine or celestial beings.
    • devavato: of the divine or godly.
    • rathaḥ: chariot, symbolizing movement and progress.
    • śūro: brave or heroic.
    • vā: or.
    • pṛtsu: in the fields or in the world.
    • kāsu: where or in which.
    • cit: indeed or certainly.
    • devānāṁ: of the gods.
    • ya: who.
    • in: in.
    • mano: mind or consciousness.
    • yajamāna: the one who performs the sacrifice or offering.
    • iyakṣaty: who protects or guards.
    • abhīd: may he be praised or honored.
    • ayajvano: of the non-sacrificer.
    • bhuvat: may he become or may he be.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks to the relationship between the divine and the human, emphasizing the importance of bravery and action in the world.
    • The chariot (rathaḥ) symbolizes the journey of life, where one must be courageous (śūro) in facing challenges.
    • It suggests that those who are aligned with divine principles and who act with intention (yajamāna) will be protected and guided.
    • The mention of the gods (devānāṁ) indicates that divine support is available to those who seek it through righteous actions.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, an employee who takes initiative and demonstrates courage in their work may find that they are supported by their colleagues and superiors, akin to the divine protection mentioned in the mantra.
    • In personal relationships, being brave enough to communicate openly can lead to stronger bonds, reflecting the idea of divine guidance in human interactions.
    • In community service, individuals who act selflessly for the benefit of others often find that their efforts are recognized and rewarded, similar to the praise (abhīd) given to those who honor the divine through their actions.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of courage, intention, and divine support in our lives.
    • It serves as a reminder that by aligning our actions with higher principles and being brave in our endeavors, we can invoke divine assistance and protection.
    • In real-life scenarios, whether in personal growth, professional challenges, or community involvement, the essence of this mantra encourages us to act with purpose and seek the support of the divine in our journeys.
