एता धियं कृतवामाः सखायोऽप यां मातां ऋणुत व्रजं गोः |यया मनुर विशिषिप्रं जिगाय यया वणिग वङ्कुर आपा पुरीषम् ||
etā dhiyaṁ kṛṇavāmā sakhāyo 'pa yā mātām̐ ṛṇuta vrajaṁ goḥ | yayā manur viśiśipraṁ jigāya yayā vaṇig vaṅkur āpā purīṣam ||
  • etā dhiyaṁ kṛṇavāmā: This phrase can be translated as "We create this thought" or "We establish this intention." It emphasizes the power of intention and thought in shaping our reality.
  • sakhāyo 'pa yā mātām̐: This part means "friends, who are like a mother." It highlights the importance of nurturing relationships and the support system we have in our lives.
  • ṛṇuta vrajaṁ goḥ: This translates to "may we attain the wealth of the cows." In Vedic culture, cows symbolize prosperity and abundance, representing the material and spiritual wealth one seeks.
  • yayā manur viśiśipraṁ jigāya: This means "by which Manu (the progenitor of mankind) attained greatness." It refers to the wisdom and knowledge that leads to success and fulfillment in life.
  • yayā vaṇig vaṅkur āpā purīṣam: This translates to "by which the merchant achieves prosperity." It signifies the importance of commerce and trade in society, and how ethical practices lead to success.

  • Significance: This mantra encapsulates the essence of intention, community, prosperity, and ethical living. It serves as a reminder that our thoughts and relationships shape our reality and that success comes from wisdom and ethical practices.
  • Real-life Application: In today's world, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios such as:
    • In personal development, focusing on positive intentions can lead to better outcomes in life.
    • In business, fostering supportive relationships and ethical practices can enhance success and reputation.
    • In community building, nurturing friendships and support systems can lead to a more prosperous and harmonious society.

  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a guiding principle for individuals and communities, emphasizing the importance of intention, relationships, and ethical practices in achieving prosperity and fulfillment. By embodying these values, one can navigate life's challenges and contribute positively to society.
