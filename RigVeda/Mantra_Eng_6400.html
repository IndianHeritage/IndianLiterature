इदं ह नूनम एषां सुम्नं भिक्षेत मार्त्यः |आदित्यानाम अपूर्व्यं सवीमनि ||
idaṁ ha nūnam eṣāṁ sumnam bhikṣeta martyaḥ | ādityānām apūrvyaṁ savīmani ||
  • idaṁ - This word translates to "this" or "here." It signifies the present moment or the immediate context.
  • ha - This is an emphatic particle, often used to assert or emphasize a statement.
  • nūnam - This means "indeed" or "certainly," reinforcing the certainty of the statement being made.
  • eṣāṁ - This translates to "of these" or "of those," referring to a specific group or category.
  • sumnam - This word means "auspiciousness" or "well-being," indicating a positive state or condition.
  • bhikṣeta - This means "may obtain" or "may receive," suggesting an action of seeking or acquiring.
  • martyaḥ - This translates to "the mortal" or "human being," referring to the human condition.
  • ādityānām - This means "of the Adityas," referring to the solar deities or celestial beings in Vedic tradition.
  • apūrvyaṁ - This translates to "unprecedented" or "extraordinary," indicating something unique or exceptional.
  • savīmani - This means "with all" or "together," suggesting a collective aspect or unity.

The mantra can be interpreted as a prayer or invocation where the speaker expresses a desire for auspiciousness and well-being. It emphasizes the importance of seeking positive outcomes in life, particularly for mortals, through the blessings of the celestial beings, specifically the Adityas.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as:

  • Personal Growth: Individuals can recite this mantra during meditation or reflection to cultivate a mindset focused on positivity and well-being.
  • Community Gatherings: It can be used in group settings, such as family gatherings or community events, to invoke collective blessings and harmony.
  • Rituals and Ceremonies: The mantra can be incorporated into rituals aimed at seeking prosperity, health, and auspicious beginnings, such as weddings or housewarming ceremonies.

Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for auspiciousness and well-being, emphasizing the role of divine blessings in human life. It serves as a reminder to seek positivity and unity, both individually and collectively, in our daily lives. By invoking the blessings of the celestial beings, we can aspire to achieve extraordinary outcomes and foster a harmonious existence.
