अदाभ्यो भुवनानि प्रचाकशद व्रतानि देवः सविताभिः रक्षते |प्रास्राग बाहू भुवनस्य प्रजाभ्यो धृतव्रतो महो अज्मस्य राजति ||
adābhyo bhuvanāni pracākaśad vratāni devaḥ savitābhi rakṣate | prāsrāg bāhū bhuvanasya prajābhyo dhṛtavrato maho ajmasya rājati ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • adābhyo: From the unmanifested
    • bhuvanāni: The worlds or realms
    • pracākaśad: He who brings forth or manifests
    • vratāni: The vows or commitments
    • devaḥ: The divine or god
    • savitābhi: By the sun or the solar deity
    • rakṣate: Protects or safeguards
    • prāsrāg: With outstretched arms
    • bāhū: Arms
    • bhuvanasya: Of the world
    • prajābhyo: To the beings or creatures
    • dhṛtavrato: With steadfast vows
    • maho: Great
    • ajmasya: Of the unborn or eternal
    • rājati: Reigns or shines
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the divine force that brings forth the worlds from the unmanifested state.
    • It emphasizes the protective nature of the divine, particularly the solar deity, Savita, who safeguards the commitments and vows made by beings.
    • The imagery of outstretched arms symbolizes the embrace of the divine, offering protection and support to all creatures in the universe.
    • The phrase "dhṛtavrato" highlights the importance of steadfastness in one's commitments, suggesting that true strength lies in the dedication to one's vows.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In personal life, individuals often make commitments, such as marriage vows or promises to family and friends. This mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of honoring those commitments and the protection that comes from a higher power when one is steadfast in their promises.
    • In a professional context, businesses often have missions and values that they commit to uphold. The mantra can inspire organizations to remain true to their values, ensuring that they operate with integrity and purpose.
    • In a broader societal context, communities can draw strength from collective commitments to social justice, environmental protection, or cultural preservation, reflecting the protective nature of the divine as described in the mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra encapsulates the essence of divine protection and the significance of steadfast commitments in various aspects of life.
    • It encourages individuals and communities to recognize the divine support available when they remain true to their vows and commitments.
    • Ultimately, it serves as a reminder that the strength of the universe is behind those who act with integrity and dedication, fostering a sense of security and purpose in their endeavors.
