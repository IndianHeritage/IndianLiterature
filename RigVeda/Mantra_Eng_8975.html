मन्त्र: निवार्तध्वं मानु गातास्मान सिषक्त रेवतीः |अग्निषोमा पुनर्वसू अस्मे धारयतं रयिम ||
ni vartadhvam mānu gātāsmān siṣakta revatīḥ | agnīṣomā punarvasū asme dhārayataṁ rayim ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ni vartadhvam: "Do not turn back" or "Do not retreat."
    • mānu: "Human beings" or "people."
    • gātāsmān: "Lead us" or "Guide us."
    • siṣakta: "With strength" or "with power."
    • revatīḥ: "Prosperity" or "wealth."
    • agnīṣomā: "The divine forces" or "the energies of fire and light."
    • punarvasū: "The benevolent ones" or "the auspicious beings."
    • asme: "For us" or "to us."
    • dhārayataṁ: "May they bestow" or "may they grant."
    • rayim: "Abundance" or "wealth."
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of perseverance and strength in the face of challenges.
    • It calls upon divine energies to guide humanity towards prosperity and abundance.
    • The invocation of "agnīṣomā" and "punarvasū" suggests a reliance on higher powers for support and blessings.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, when facing obstacles in a project, a team can embody the spirit of "ni vartadhvam" by not giving up and seeking innovative solutions.
    • In personal life, individuals may face financial difficulties; invoking the essence of this mantra can inspire them to seek help and work towards regaining stability.
    • Communities can come together in times of crisis, drawing on collective strength and support, reflecting the mantra's call for unity and resilience.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the strength found in unity and the importance of perseverance.
    • It highlights the role of divine support in achieving prosperity and overcoming challenges.
    • In real life, it encourages individuals and communities to remain steadfast in their pursuits, fostering a sense of hope and collective strength.
