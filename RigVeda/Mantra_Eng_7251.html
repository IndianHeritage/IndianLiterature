अनु प्रात्नस्यौकसः प्रियमेधास एषाम् |पूर्वाम् अनु प्रयतिं वृक्तबरहिषो हितप्रयसा आशत ||
anu pratnasyaukasaḥ priyamedhāsa eṣām | pūrvām anu prayatiṁ vṛktabarhiṣo hitaprayasa āśata ||
  • anu pratnasyaukasaḥ priyamedhāsa eṣām: This phrase can be broken down into several components:
    • anu: meaning "after" or "following."
    • pratnasyaukasaḥ: refers to "the one who is wise" or "the one who has understanding."
    • priyamedhāsa: translates to "those who have dear thoughts" or "those who are affectionate in their understanding."
    • eṣām: means "of them" or "of those."
  • pūrvām anu prayatiṁ: This part can be interpreted as:
    • pūrvām: meaning "previous" or "earlier."
    • anu prayatiṁ: translates to "following the path" or "continuing on the journey."
  • vṛktabarhiṣo hitaprayasa āśata: This phrase can be understood as:
    • vṛktabarhiṣo: refers to "those who are engaged in sacred actions" or "those who perform rituals."
    • hitaprayasa: translates to "those who strive for the good" or "those who work for beneficial outcomes."
    • āśata: means "may they be blessed" or "may they attain."

Conclusion:

This mantra emphasizes the importance of wisdom, affection, and the pursuit of good actions. It suggests that those who follow the path of understanding and engage in sacred actions are blessed and achieve beneficial outcomes. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios such as:

  • In personal relationships, fostering understanding and affection leads to stronger bonds.
  • In professional settings, striving for ethical practices and good intentions can result in a positive work environment.
  • In community service, engaging in actions that benefit others can create a more harmonious society.

Overall, the mantra serves as a reminder to cultivate wisdom and compassion in our actions, leading to a more fulfilling and harmonious life.
