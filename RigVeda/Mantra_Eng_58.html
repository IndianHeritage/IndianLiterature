अनवद्यैर अभिद्युभिर् मखः सहस्वद् आर्चति |गणैर इन्द्रस्य काम्यैः ||
anavadyair abhidyubhir makhaḥ sahasvad arcati | gaṇair indrasya kāmyaiḥ ||
  • Mantra: "anavadyair abhidyubhir makhaḥ sahasvad arcati | gaṇair indrasya kāmyaiḥ ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • anavadyaiḥ: Without blemish or fault
    • abhidyubhiḥ: With offerings or sacrifices
    • makhaḥ: The sacrifice or ritual
    • sahasvad: With great abundance or multitude
    • arcati: Worships or honors
    • gaṇaiḥ: By groups or assemblies
    • indrasya: Of Indra, the king of gods
    • kāmyaiḥ: For desired outcomes or wishes
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of a ritual or sacrifice that is performed without any faults or blemishes.
    • The act of worship is done in great abundance, indicating a collective effort by groups or communities.
    • Indra, as the king of gods, is invoked for fulfilling desires and wishes, highlighting the importance of divine blessings in achieving goals.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern contexts, this mantra can be related to community gatherings where people come together to celebrate festivals or significant events, such as weddings or religious ceremonies, ensuring that the rituals are performed with purity and intention.
    • For instance, during a wedding ceremony, families often perform rituals that are meant to invoke blessings for a prosperous and happy married life, reflecting the essence of this mantra.
    • In corporate settings, team-building activities can be seen as a form of collective effort where employees come together to achieve common goals, invoking a sense of unity and purpose.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its emphasis on purity, collective effort, and the invocation of divine blessings for desired outcomes.
    • It teaches us the importance of performing our duties and rituals with sincerity and without faults, whether in personal life, community activities, or professional endeavors.
    • By understanding and applying the principles of this mantra, individuals and communities can foster a sense of unity and purpose, leading to successful outcomes in various aspects of life.
