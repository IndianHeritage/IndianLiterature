सम भानुना यतते सूर्यस्याजुह्वानो घृतपृष्ठः स्वञ्चाः |तस्मा अमृतध्राः उषसो व्य उच्छान य इन्द्राय सुनवामेत्य आह ||
sam bhānunā yatate sūryasyājuhvāno ghṛtapṛṣṭhaḥ svañcāḥ | tasmā amṛdhrā uṣaso vy ucchān ya indrāya sunavāmety āha ||
  • Mantra: "sam bhānunā yatate sūryasyājuhvāno ghṛtapṛṣṭhaḥ svañcāḥ | tasmā amṛdhrā uṣaso vy ucchān ya indrāya sunavāmety āha ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sam: together, in harmony
    • bhānunā: with the light, radiance
    • yatate: strives, works hard
    • sūryasya: of the sun
    • ajuhvāno: invoking, calling upon
    • ghṛtapṛṣṭhaḥ: with clarified butter, representing purity and nourishment
    • svañcāḥ: one's own, personal
    • tasmā: therefore, for that reason
    • amṛdhrā: immortal, eternal
    • uṣaso: of the dawn, representing new beginnings
    • vy ucchān: having risen, elevated
    • ya: who
    • indrāya: to Indra, the king of gods
    • sunavāmety: we offer, we invoke
    • āha: he said, it is said
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra speaks of the harmonious effort of invoking the sun's light and energy.
    • The phrase "sam bhānunā yatate" emphasizes the collective effort to harness the sun's radiance, which can be likened to teamwork in a workplace where individuals come together to achieve a common goal.
    • "ghṛtapṛṣṭhaḥ svañcāḥ" suggests that this effort is nourished by purity and personal dedication, similar to how a chef uses pure ingredients to create a delicious meal.
    • The mention of "amṛdhrā uṣaso" signifies the dawn of new opportunities and the eternal nature of life, akin to how each day brings new chances for growth and learning.
    • Finally, invoking Indra symbolizes seeking divine support and guidance in our endeavors, much like how individuals seek mentorship or inspiration from leaders in their fields.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the importance of collective effort, purity of intention, and the pursuit of new beginnings.
    • In real life, it encourages individuals to work together harmoniously, maintain integrity in their actions, and embrace each day as a fresh start.
    • Its significance lies in the reminder that with dedication and divine support, one can achieve greatness and navigate the challenges of life.
