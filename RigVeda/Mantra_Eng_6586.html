सं यां दानूनि येमथुर दिव्याः पार्थिवीर इषः |नभस्वतीर आ वां चरन्तु वृष्टयः ||
saṁ yā dānūni yemathur divyāḥ pārthivīr iṣaḥ | nabhasvatīr ā vāṁ carantu vṛṣṭayaḥ ||
  • saṁ yā dānūni yemathur: This phrase can be translated as "those who are endowed with divine gifts." It refers to individuals or entities that possess qualities or resources that are considered sacred or beneficial.
  • divyāḥ pārthivīr iṣaḥ: This part means "the divine and earthly powers." It highlights the connection between the celestial and terrestrial realms, suggesting that both have their own strengths and influences.
  • nabhasvatīr ā vāṁ: This translates to "may the celestial beings come to you." It implies a call for divine intervention or support from higher powers.
  • carantu vṛṣṭayaḥ: This means "may the rains flow." It symbolizes abundance, fertility, and the nurturing aspects of nature, which are essential for life and prosperity.

The mantra as a whole can be interpreted as a prayer or invocation for divine blessings and abundance. It emphasizes the importance of both spiritual and material wealth, recognizing that true prosperity comes from a harmonious relationship with the divine and the natural world.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios:

  • Agriculture: Farmers can recite this mantra to invoke favorable weather conditions, such as rain, which is crucial for crop growth.
  • Personal Growth: Individuals seeking success in their endeavors can use this mantra to attract positive energies and divine guidance in their pursuits.
  • Community Well-being: Communities can come together to chant this mantra during festivals or gatherings to promote unity and collective prosperity.

In conclusion, this mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of the divine and earthly realms. It encourages individuals and communities to seek blessings for abundance and to recognize the importance of nurturing both spiritual and material aspects of life. By invoking divine support, one can foster a more prosperous and harmonious existence.
