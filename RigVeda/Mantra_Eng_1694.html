निक्रमणं निषदानं विवर्तनं यच च पड्बीशम् अरवतः |यच च पपौ यच च घासिं जग्हासा सर्वा ता ते अपि देवेष्व अस्तु ||
nikramaṇaṁ niṣadanaṁ vivartanaṁ yac ca paḍbīśam arvataḥ | yac ca papau yac ca ghāsiṁ jaghāsa sarvā tā te api deveṣv astu ||
  • Nikramṇaṁ: This term refers to the act of stepping out or moving forward. In a real-life context, it can symbolize taking initiative or making a significant change in one’s life.
  • Niṣadanaṁ: This word means to sit down or to settle. It can represent the importance of finding stability and peace in one’s life, akin to establishing a home or a safe space.
  • Vivartanaṁ: This translates to turning or transformation. It signifies the process of change, which is essential in personal growth and development, much like how a caterpillar transforms into a butterfly.
  • Yac ca paḍbīśam arvataḥ: This phrase can be interpreted as referring to the offerings or sacrifices made to the divine. In everyday life, this can relate to the concept of giving back to the community or engaging in acts of kindness.
  • Yac ca papau: This means 'what has been consumed' or 'what has been taken in.' It can symbolize the knowledge and experiences we gather throughout our lives, much like how we learn from our interactions and challenges.
  • Yac ca ghāsiṁ: This refers to 'what has been devoured' or 'what has been absorbed.' It can represent the lessons learned from hardships or struggles, akin to how we grow stronger through adversity.
  • Jaghāsa: This term means 'to overcome' or 'to conquer.' It signifies the triumph over challenges, much like how individuals face and overcome personal obstacles in their lives.
  • Sarvā tā te api deveṣv astu: This concluding phrase can be interpreted as a prayer or wish for all these actions and transformations to be accepted by the divine. It emphasizes the importance of seeking blessings and guidance from a higher power in our endeavors.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the journey of life, highlighting the significance of taking initiative, finding stability, embracing transformation, and seeking divine blessings. In real-life scenarios, it serves as a reminder to actively engage in personal growth, contribute positively to society, and overcome challenges with resilience. By embodying these principles, individuals can lead a more fulfilling and purposeful life, aligning their actions with a higher spiritual calling.
