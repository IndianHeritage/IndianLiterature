इषुर न श्रीय इषुदेर असना गोषाः शतसां न रंहिः |अवीरे क्रतौ विडविद्युतन नोरां न मायुं चितयन्त धुनयः ||
iṣur na śriya iṣudher asanā goṣāḥ śatasā na raṁhiḥ | avīre kratau vi davidyutan norā na māyuṁ citayanta dhunayaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • iṣur: arrow or weapon
    • na: not
    • śriya: wealth or prosperity
    • iṣudher: of the bow or weapon
    • asanā: not seated or not established
    • goṣāḥ: cows or herds
    • śatasā: by hundreds
    • na: not
    • raṁhiḥ: to be harmed or injured
    • avīre: without fear
    • kratau: in the sacrifices or rituals
    • vi: in a different way
    • davidyutan: not divided or not separated
    • norā: not to be disturbed
    • na: not
    • māyuṁ: illusion or deception
    • citayanta: those who are aware or conscious
    • dhunayaḥ: of the sound or vibration
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the power of unity and the importance of being undivided in purpose.
    • The mention of "iṣur" and "iṣudher" suggests that weapons or challenges cannot harm those who are united in their goals.
    • The reference to "goṣāḥ" indicates the abundance of resources (like cattle) that come from a harmonious community.
    • It emphasizes that fearlessness in rituals and actions leads to prosperity and success.
    • The phrase "not to be disturbed" highlights the need for focus and clarity in one's intentions.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, a team that works together harmoniously, without internal conflict, is more likely to achieve its goals and succeed in projects.
    • In community service, groups that unite for a common cause, such as environmental conservation, can create a significant impact, demonstrating the power of collective action.
    • In personal relationships, open communication and shared goals can prevent misunderstandings and foster a supportive environment.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the strength found in unity and the importance of collective effort in overcoming challenges.
    • It teaches that fearlessness and clarity of purpose can lead to prosperity and success in various aspects of life.
    • In real-life scenarios, whether in personal relationships, community efforts, or professional environments, the principles outlined in this mantra can guide individuals towards achieving their goals while fostering a sense of togetherness.
