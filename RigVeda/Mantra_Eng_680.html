भवā वरूथं गृणते विभावो भवā मघवन मघवद्भ्यः शर्म |उरुश्याग्ने अंहसो गृणन्तं प्रातर मक्षू धियावसुर जगम्यāt ||
bhavā varūthaṁ gṛṇate vibhāvo bhavā maghavan maghavadbhyaḥ śarma | uruṣyāgne aṁhaso gṛṇantam prātar makṣū dhiyāvasur jagamyāt ||
  • bhavā varūthaṁ: This phrase can be interpreted as "may the divine presence" or "may the essence of existence." It signifies the importance of recognizing the divine in all aspects of life.
  • gṛṇate vibhāvo: This translates to "sings the praises of the glorious." It emphasizes the act of acknowledging and celebrating the beauty and magnificence of the universe.
  • bhavā maghavan: Here, "bhavā" refers to existence or being, while "maghavan" denotes abundance or wealth. This suggests that true wealth comes from a deep understanding of existence.
  • maghavadbhyaḥ śarma: This can be understood as "from the abundant, may we receive protection." It highlights the need for security and safety that comes from a bountiful life.
  • uruṣyāgne: This term refers to the "great fire" or "sacred fire," symbolizing transformation and purification. Fire is often seen as a source of energy and life.
  • aṁhaso gṛṇantam: This means "the one who sings of the light." It signifies the importance of enlightenment and knowledge in our lives.
  • prātar makṣū: This translates to "in the morning, may we awaken." It symbolizes new beginnings and the potential that each day holds.
  • dhiyāvasur jagamyāt: This phrase can be interpreted as "with wisdom, may we attain our goals." It emphasizes the role of intellect and understanding in achieving success.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a profound understanding of existence, abundance, and the pursuit of knowledge. It encourages individuals to recognize the divine in their lives, celebrate the beauty around them, and seek wisdom as a guiding force. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in daily practices such as meditation, gratitude, and the pursuit of knowledge. By embodying these principles, individuals can cultivate a life of abundance, security, and enlightenment, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling existence.
