योनीषṭ इन्द्र सदने अकारी तं ā नृभिः पुरुहूत प्र याहि |असो यथा नो 'विता वृधे च ददो वसूनि ममदश च सोमैः ||
yoniṣ ṭa indra sadane akāri tam ā nṛbhiḥ puruhūta pra yāhi | aso yathā no 'vitā vṛdhe ca dado vasūni mamadaś ca somaiḥ ||
  • Yoniṣ ṭa Indra sadane akāri: This phrase can be interpreted as a call to Indra, the king of gods, to come to his abode or dwelling. The term "yoniṣ" refers to the source or origin, while "Indra" symbolizes strength and power. In real life, this can be seen as a plea for divine intervention in times of need.
  • Tam ā nṛbhiḥ puruhūta pra yāhi: Here, "tam" refers to that divine presence, and "nṛbhiḥ" means by humans or people. "Puruhūta" signifies one who is invoked or called upon. This line emphasizes the importance of human connection with the divine, suggesting that we should actively seek the blessings of the gods in our lives.
  • Aso yathā no 'vitā vṛdhe ca dado: "Aso" means 'may it be so' or 'let it happen.' The phrase expresses a desire for prosperity and growth. "Vṛdhe" refers to increase or abundance, and "dado" means to give. This can be related to the modern concept of manifestation, where individuals focus on their goals and desires to attract positive outcomes.
  • Vasūni mamadaś ca somaiḥ: "Vasūni" refers to wealth or resources, while "mamadaś" can be interpreted as the essence of joy or happiness. "Somaiḥ" relates to the divine nectar or elixir, often associated with prosperity. This line highlights the importance of seeking not just material wealth but also spiritual fulfillment and joy in life.

  • Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda encapsulates the essence of seeking divine blessings for prosperity, strength, and joy. It emphasizes the relationship between humans and the divine, urging individuals to actively seek support and guidance. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various contexts such as during challenging times, when pursuing personal goals, or in rituals and prayers for abundance. The significance lies in the belief that through devotion and connection with the divine, one can achieve a fulfilling and prosperous life.
