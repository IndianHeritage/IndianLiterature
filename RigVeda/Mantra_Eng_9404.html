न्यग वातोऽव वाति न्यक तपति सूर्यः |नीचीनामघ्न्यादुहे न्यग भवतु ते रपः ||
nya1g vāto 'va vāti nyak tapati sūryaḥ | nīcīnam aghnyā duhe nyag bhavatu te rapaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • nya1g: a term indicating a sense of direction or movement.
    • vāto: refers to the wind or air.
    • 'va: signifies 'like' or 'as'.
    • vāti: means 'blows' or 'moves'.
    • nyak: indicates 'downward' or 'beneath'.
    • tapati: means 'shines' or 'burns'.
    • sūryaḥ: refers to the sun.
    • nīcīnam: means 'lowly' or 'humble'.
    • aghnyā: refers to 'non-violent' or 'harmless'.
    • duhe: means 'to nourish' or 'to provide'.
    • nyag: indicates 'in a good way' or 'properly'.
    • bhavatu: means 'may it be' or 'let it be'.
    • te: refers to 'your' or 'of you'.
    • rapaḥ: signifies 'wealth' or 'prosperity'.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the natural elements and their harmonious interaction.
    • The wind (vāto) is likened to a force that moves and influences the environment.
    • The sun (sūryaḥ) is depicted as a source of light and energy that shines down.
    • It emphasizes the importance of nurturing the humble and non-violent (nīcīnam aghnyā) aspects of life.
    • The mantra calls for the blessings of prosperity (rapaḥ) to come in a good and proper way (nyag).
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In nature, the wind and sun work together to create a balanced ecosystem, which can be seen in how plants grow and thrive.
    • In human interactions, nurturing those who are humble and non-violent can lead to a more peaceful and prosperous community.
    • In business, fostering a culture that values ethical practices and supports the well-being of all can lead to sustainable success.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of harmony in nature and society.
    • It teaches us to appreciate the forces that nurture life and to act with kindness towards others.
    • In real life, applying the principles of this mantra can lead to personal growth, community well-being, and overall prosperity.
