बहवः सूरचक्षसो 'ग्निजिह्वा ऋतावृद्धः |त्रीणि ये येमुर विदथानि धीतिभिर विश्वानि परिभूतिभिः ||
bahavaḥ sūracakṣaso 'gnijihvā ṛtāvṛdhaḥ | trīṇi ye yemur vidathāni dhītibhir viśvāni paribhūtibhiḥ ||
  • bahavaḥ: This word translates to "many" or "numerous." It signifies abundance and diversity.
  • sūracakṣaso: This term refers to "those with divine vision" or "those who see with the eyes of the gods." It implies a higher understanding or insight.
  • 'gnijihvā: This means "with fiery tongues" or "those who speak with the intensity of fire." It symbolizes powerful communication and expression.
  • ṛtāvṛdhaḥ: This can be interpreted as "those who are nourished by truth" or "who grow in righteousness." It emphasizes the importance of truth and moral integrity.
  • trīṇi: This word means "three." It often represents completeness or a triad of important elements.
  • ye: This translates to "who" or "those who," referring to the subjects of the mantra.
  • yemur: This means "have attained" or "have achieved," indicating accomplishment or realization.
  • vidathāni: This refers to "the realms" or "the places of action," suggesting various domains of existence or endeavors.
  • dhītibhir: This translates to "with wisdom" or "through understanding," highlighting the role of intellect and insight.
  • viśvāni: This means "all" or "universal," indicating inclusivity and the entirety of existence.
  • paribhūtibhiḥ: This can be interpreted as "with all forms of prosperity" or "through various means of abundance," emphasizing the different ways in which prosperity can manifest.

In summary, this mantra speaks to the idea of many individuals with divine insight and powerful communication, who are nourished by truth and have achieved success in various realms through wisdom. It highlights the importance of understanding, moral integrity, and the diverse paths to prosperity.


Significance and Real-Life Application:

  • This mantra encourages individuals to seek wisdom and truth in their lives, reminding us that success is not just about material wealth but also about moral and intellectual growth.
  • In a real-life scenario, this can be applied in professional settings where teamwork and diverse perspectives lead to innovative solutions. For example, a project team that values each member's insights and experiences is likely to achieve greater success.
  • Moreover, it emphasizes the importance of communication. In personal relationships, expressing oneself with clarity and honesty can lead to deeper connections and understanding.
  • Ultimately, the mantra serves as a reminder that true prosperity encompasses not just material gains but also the richness of wisdom, integrity, and the collective strength of diverse individuals working together.
