स्वना न यस्य भामासः पवन्ते रोचमानस्य बृहतः सुदिवः |ज्येष्ठेभिर यस तेजिष्ठैः क्रीळुमद्भिर वर्षिष्ठेभिर भानुभिर नक्षति dyām ||
svanā na yasya bhāmāsaḥ pavante rocamānasya bṛhataḥ sudivaḥ | jyeṣṭhebhir yas tejiṣṭhaiḥ krīḻumadbhir varṣiṣṭhebhir bhānubhir nakṣati dyām ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • svanā: sound, voice
    • na: not
    • yasya: whose
    • bhāmāsaḥ: brilliance, radiance
    • pavante: blowing, moving
    • rocamānasya: shining, illuminating
    • bṛhataḥ: great, vast
    • sudivaḥ: of good heavens, auspicious
    • jyeṣṭhebhir: by the elder, superior
    • yas: who
    • tejiṣṭhaiḥ: by the most powerful
    • krīḻumadbhir: by those who play, engage
    • varṣiṣṭhebhir: by the most excellent
    • bhānubhir: by the sun, light
    • nakṣati: shines, illuminates
    • dyām: the sky, heavens
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the divine radiance and the powerful forces of nature.
    • It emphasizes the importance of sound and voice in the manifestation of brilliance and illumination.
    • The reference to "good heavens" suggests a connection to auspiciousness and positivity in life.
    • The mention of "elder" and "most powerful" indicates respect for wisdom and strength, which can be seen in leadership roles in society.
    • In real life, this can be related to how leaders or influential figures can inspire and illuminate the path for others through their actions and words.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, a CEO who communicates effectively can inspire employees, leading to a more productive and positive work environment.
    • In education, a teacher who uses their voice to engage students can illuminate their understanding and foster a love for learning.
    • In community leadership, individuals who advocate for social causes can bring about significant change, much like the "powerful" forces mentioned in the mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of communication, leadership, and the power of nature in our lives.
    • It serves as a reminder that our words and actions can have a profound impact on those around us.
    • In essence, it encourages us to harness our inner strength and wisdom to illuminate the paths of others, creating a more harmonious and enlightened society.
