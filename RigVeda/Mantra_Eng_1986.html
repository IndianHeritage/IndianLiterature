अस्य श्लोको दिवीयते पृथिव्याम् अत्यो न यंसद यक्षभृत् विचेताः |मृगाणां न हेतयो यन्ति चेमा बृहस्पतेर अहिमायाम् अभि द्यून् ||
asya śloko divīyate pṛthivyām atyo na yaṁsad yakṣabhṛd vicetāḥ | mṛgāṇāṁ na hetayo yanti cemā bṛhaspater ahimāyām̐ abhi dyūn ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • asya: of this
    • śloko: verse or mantra
    • divīyate: shines or is illuminated
    • pṛthivyām: on the earth
    • atyō: beyond or surpassing
    • na: not
    • yaṁsad: those who are wise or knowledgeable
    • yakṣabhṛd: those who possess wealth or prosperity
    • vicetāḥ: who are perceptive or insightful
    • mṛgāṇāṁ: of the creatures or beings
    • na: not
    • hetayo: the causes or reasons
    • yanti: go or lead
    • cemā: these
    • bṛhaspater: of Brihaspati, the lord of wisdom
    • ahimāyām̐: in the realm of the great
    • abhi: towards
    • dyūn: the heavens or celestial realms
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of a verse that shines brightly on earth, surpassing the knowledge of the wise and wealthy.
    • It suggests that true insight and understanding are not easily attained, even by those who are materially prosperous.
    • The reference to creatures indicates that the wisdom of the universe is not limited to human understanding; it encompasses all beings.
    • The mention of Brihaspati emphasizes the importance of wisdom and knowledge in navigating life's complexities.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, a successful business leader may have wealth and resources, but true leadership requires wisdom and insight that goes beyond mere financial success.
    • In education, students may excel academically but must also cultivate critical thinking and emotional intelligence to truly understand the world around them.
    • In personal relationships, individuals may possess material wealth, yet the depth of understanding and empathy is what truly enriches connections with others.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of wisdom over material wealth, reminding us that true understanding and insight are essential for navigating life.
    • It encourages individuals to seek knowledge and wisdom, as these qualities lead to a more fulfilling and meaningful existence.
    • In real-life scenarios, applying this wisdom can lead to better decision-making, deeper relationships, and a more profound appreciation of the world.
