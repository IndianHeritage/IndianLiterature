यस्ते अग्ने नमसā यज्ञं ईटṭ ऋतं स पात्य अरुषस्य वृष्णः |तस्य क्षयः पृतुर आ साधुर एतु प्रसर्स्राणस्य नहुषस्य शेषः ||
yas te agne namasā yajñam īṭṭa ṛtaṁ sa pāty aruṣasya vṛṣṇaḥ | tasya kṣayaḥ pṛthur ā sādhur etu prasarsrāṇasya nahuṣasya śeṣaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yas: who
    • te: your
    • agne: O Agni (the fire god)
    • namasā: with reverence
    • yajñam: the sacrifice
    • īṭṭa: that is desired
    • ṛtaṁ: the truth or order
    • sa: he
    • pāty: who nourishes
    • aruṣasya: of the red (often referring to the sun or fire)
    • vṛṣṇaḥ: the strong or powerful
  • Meaning of the Mantra:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Agni, the fire god, who is considered a mediator between humans and the divine.
    • It expresses a desire for the blessings that come from performing sacrifices with reverence and truth.
    • The mantra emphasizes the importance of sincerity and the right intention in rituals, suggesting that true nourishment comes from a pure heart.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern life, this mantra can be related to the concept of integrity in our actions. For instance, when we engage in community service, doing so with genuine intent and respect for the cause can lead to more meaningful outcomes.
    • In professional settings, approaching work with dedication and honesty, akin to performing a yajña (sacrifice), can yield success and recognition, much like the blessings sought from Agni.
  • Conclusion and Significance:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of intention and reverence in our actions, whether in spiritual practices or daily life.
    • It teaches us that true strength and nourishment come from aligning our actions with truth and sincerity.
    • In a world often driven by superficial goals, this mantra serves as a reminder to seek deeper meaning and connection in our endeavors.
