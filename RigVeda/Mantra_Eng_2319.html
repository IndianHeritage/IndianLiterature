अस्य मे द्यावापृथिवी ऋतायतो भूतम अवित्री वचसः सिषासतः |ययोः आयुः प्रतरं ते इदं पुर उपस्तुते वसूयुर वाम महो दधे ||
asya me dyāvāpṛthivī ṛtāyato bhūtam avitrī vacasaḥ siṣāsataḥ | yayor āyuḥ prataraṁ te idam pura upastute vasūyur vām maho dadhe ||
  • Mantra: "asya me dyāvāpṛthivī ṛtāyato bhūtam avitrī vacasaḥ siṣāsataḥ | yayor āyuḥ prataraṁ te idam pura upastute vasūyur vām maho dadhe ||"
  • Translation: "May the heavens and the earth, united in truth, grant me the strength of speech and the power of thought. May they bestow upon me a long life, and may I be blessed with abundance and greatness."

  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • asya: of this
    • me: my
    • dyāvāpṛthivī: heavens and earth
    • ṛtāyato: united in truth
    • bhūtam: grant
    • avitrī: strength
    • vacasaḥ: of speech
    • siṣāsataḥ: and thought
    • yayor: of whom
    • āyuḥ: life
    • prataraṁ: long
    • te: to you
    • idam: this
    • pura: ancient
    • upastute: blessed
    • vasūyur: abundance
    • vām: you
    • maho: greatness
    • dadhe: bestow

  • Significance: This mantra emphasizes the importance of harmony between the heavens and the earth, suggesting that our existence is supported by a balance of natural forces. It highlights the need for truth and integrity in our thoughts and speech, which are essential for personal growth and societal well-being.
  • Real-Life Application:
    • In personal development, this mantra can inspire individuals to seek truth in their actions and words, fostering better relationships and communication.
    • In a professional setting, it encourages teamwork and collaboration, as understanding and respecting diverse perspectives can lead to greater success.
    • In community building, it serves as a reminder to work towards collective well-being, ensuring that the needs of all are met through honest dialogue and cooperation.

  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the interconnectedness of life and the importance of truthfulness in our interactions. By embodying these principles, we can cultivate a life filled with abundance, longevity, and greatness, both personally and collectively.
