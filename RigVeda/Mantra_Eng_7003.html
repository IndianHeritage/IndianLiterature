अग्निं न मां मथितं सं दिदीपः प्र चक्षय कृतुहि वस्यसो नः |अथा हि ते मद आ सोम मन्ये रेवाम् इव प्र चरां पुष्टिम् अच् ||
agniṁ na mā mathitaṁ saṁ didīpaḥ pra cakṣaya kṛṇuhi vasyaso naḥ | athā hi te mada ā soma manye revām̐ iva pra carā puṣṭim accha ||
  • Agniṁ na mā mathitaṁ: This phrase translates to "O Agni, do not be disturbed." Agni, the fire god, represents energy, transformation, and purification. In real life, this can be seen as a call to maintain focus and clarity in our endeavors, much like how a fire needs to be tended to in order to burn brightly.
  • Saṁ didīpaḥ pra cakṣaya: This means "let your light shine forth." It emphasizes the importance of enlightenment and knowledge. In practical terms, this can relate to the pursuit of education and wisdom, encouraging individuals to share their insights and illuminate the paths of others.
  • Kṛṇuhi vasyaso naḥ: This translates to "make us your own." It signifies a desire for connection and guidance from the divine. In everyday life, this can be interpreted as seeking mentorship or support from those who inspire us, fostering a sense of community and belonging.
  • Athā hi te mada ā soma: This phrase means "for indeed, your joy is like Soma." Soma is a sacred drink that symbolizes immortality and divine bliss. This can be related to the pursuit of happiness and fulfillment in life, suggesting that true joy comes from spiritual and emotional well-being.
  • Manye revām̐ iva pra carā: This translates to "I think of you as a flowing river." This metaphor highlights the idea of continuous movement and change. In real life, it can remind us to embrace change and adaptability, much like a river that carves its path through the landscape.
  • Puṣṭim accha: This means "bring us nourishment." It signifies the need for sustenance, both physical and spiritual. In practical terms, this can relate to the importance of maintaining a balanced diet and nurturing our mental and emotional health through positive relationships and experiences.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a deep yearning for divine guidance, enlightenment, and nourishment in our lives. Its significance lies in the reminder to seek knowledge, maintain focus, and embrace change while fostering connections with others. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire individuals to pursue their passions, seek mentorship, and cultivate a sense of community, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling and harmonious existence.
