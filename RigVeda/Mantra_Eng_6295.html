यद वाऽशक्र परावती समुद्रे अधि मन्दसे |अस्माकं इति सुते रणा सम इन्दुभिः ||
yad vā śakra parāvati samudre adhi mandase | asmākam it sute raṇā sam indubhiḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yad: whatever
    • vā: or
    • śakra: O Indra, the powerful
    • parāvati: who is supreme
    • samudre: in the ocean
    • adhi: above
    • mandase: the one who is calm or tranquil
    • asmākam: our
    • it: here
    • sute: in the offerings
    • raṇā: in battles
    • sam: together
    • indubhiḥ: with the moon (or with the light)
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the powerful deity Indra, who is often associated with strength, rain, and the heavens.
    • The phrase "yad vā" suggests a sense of inquiry or contemplation about the nature of power and tranquility.
    • By referencing the ocean, it symbolizes vastness and depth, indicating that true strength lies in calmness and composure.
    • The mantra emphasizes the importance of unity and collective strength in overcoming challenges, particularly in battles or conflicts.
    • The mention of "indubhiḥ" can be interpreted as a call for illumination and guidance, akin to the moonlight that provides clarity in darkness.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, leaders often face challenges that require both strength and calmness. This mantra can serve as a reminder to approach conflicts with a composed mindset, fostering teamwork and collaboration.
    • In personal relationships, the idea of facing challenges together resonates with the importance of unity and support. Couples can draw inspiration from this mantra to navigate difficulties with mutual respect and understanding.
    • In environmental discussions, the reference to the ocean can symbolize the need for sustainable practices. Just as the ocean is vast and powerful, so too is the impact of collective human action on the planet.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of strength combined with tranquility, urging individuals to seek unity in the face of challenges.
    • Its application in real life can be seen in various contexts, from personal relationships to professional environments, emphasizing the importance of collaboration and calmness.
    • Ultimately, it serves as a reminder that true power is not just in might, but in the ability to remain composed and work together towards common goals.
