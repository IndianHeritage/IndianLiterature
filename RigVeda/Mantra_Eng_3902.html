उत त्ये मापौरुकुत्स्यस्य सूरेस त्रसदस्योर हिरणिनो रराणाः |वहन्तु मा दश श्येतासो अस्य गैरिक्षितस्य क्रतुभिर नु सश्चे ||
uta tye mā paurukutsyasya sūres trasadasyor hiraṇino rarāṇāḥ | vahantu mā daśa śyetāso asya gairikṣitasya kratubhir nu saśce ||
  • uta tye: This phrase can be interpreted as "may they come" or "let them arrive." It signifies a call for the arrival of positive energies or forces.
  • mā paurukutsyasya: Referring to "the strength of the Purukutsa," this part emphasizes the importance of strength and valor in overcoming challenges.
  • sūres trasadasyor: "Sūres" refers to the divine or celestial beings, while "trasadasyor" can be understood as "those who are fearful." This suggests a plea for divine support against fear and adversity.
  • hiraṇino rarāṇāḥ: "Hiraṇino" means "of gold" or "wealth," and "rarāṇāḥ" refers to "those who are prosperous." This indicates a desire for prosperity and abundance in life.
  • vahantu mā: This translates to "may they carry" or "may they bring." It is a request for the divine to bring forth blessings.
  • daśa śyetāso: "Daśa" means "ten," and "śyetāso" refers to "those who are wise." This part emphasizes the importance of wisdom and intelligence in achieving success.
  • asya gairikṣitasya: "Asya" means "of this," and "gairikṣitasya" refers to "the one who is protected." This suggests a sense of security and protection in one's endeavors.
  • kratubhir nu saśce: "Kratubhir" refers to "sacrifices" or "efforts," while "nu saśce" can be interpreted as "may they be fulfilled." This indicates that through dedicated efforts and sacrifices, one can achieve their goals.

Conclusion: This mantra encapsulates a profound message about the importance of strength, wisdom, and divine support in overcoming challenges and achieving prosperity. In real life, it can be applied in various scenarios such as personal development, business endeavors, or community initiatives. By invoking the qualities of courage and intelligence, and seeking divine blessings, individuals can navigate through difficulties and work towards their goals with confidence. The mantra serves as a reminder that with the right mindset and support, success is attainable.
