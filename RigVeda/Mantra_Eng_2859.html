आ याहि पूर्वीर अति चर्षणीर आमं आर्य आशीष उप नो हरिभ्यां |इमा हि त्वा मतयः स्तोमतष्टा इन्द्र हवन्ते सख्यं जुषाणाः ||
ā yāhi pūrvīr ati carṣaṇīr ām̐ arya āśiṣa upa no haribhyām | imā hi tvā matayaḥ stomataṣṭā indra havante sakhyaṁ juṣāṇāḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā yāhi: Come here
    • pūrvīr: From the ancients
    • ati carṣaṇīr: Beyond the limits
    • ām̐ arya: O noble one
    • āśiṣa: Blessings
    • upa no: Come to us
    • haribhyām: From the divine beings
    • imā hi: Indeed these
    • tvā: You
    • matayaḥ: Thoughts
    • stomataṣṭā: Praise
    • indra: O Indra (the king of gods)
    • havante: Invoked
    • sakhyaṁ: Friendship
    • juṣāṇāḥ: Enjoying
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a call to the divine, specifically to Indra, asking for blessings and guidance.
    • The phrase "Come here from the ancients" suggests a connection to wisdom and knowledge passed down through generations.
    • It emphasizes the importance of divine blessings in achieving success and harmony in life.
    • The invocation of friendship ("sakhyaṁ") highlights the significance of community and support in our endeavors.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, teams often invoke the spirit of collaboration and support to achieve common goals, similar to how this mantra seeks divine assistance.
    • During significant life events, such as weddings or new ventures, people often seek blessings from elders or spiritual leaders, reflecting the essence of this mantra.
    • In educational institutions, students may call upon the wisdom of past scholars to guide their learning, paralleling the call to ancient wisdom in the mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of seeking divine guidance and blessings in our lives.
    • It teaches us to value the wisdom of the past and the support of our community.
    • In real-life scenarios, whether in personal or professional contexts, invoking such blessings can foster a sense of unity and purpose, leading to greater success and fulfillment.
