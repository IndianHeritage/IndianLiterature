युक्तो ह यद वां तौग्र्याय पेरुर विथ्ये अर्णसो धायि पज्रः |उप वाम अवः शरणं गमेयं शूरो नाज्म पतयद्भिर एवः ||
yukto ha yad vāṁ taugryāya perur vi madhye arṇaso dhāyi pajraḥ | upa vām avaḥ śaraṇaṁ gameyaṁ śūro nājma patayadbhir evaiḥ ||
  • yukto ha yad vāṁ taugryāya: This phrase can be interpreted as "those who are united or connected." The term "yukto" signifies a state of being in harmony or alignment. In real life, this can be seen in teamwork or collaboration, where individuals come together for a common goal, such as a sports team working together to win a game.
  • perur vi madhye arṇaso: This translates to "in the midst of the waters." The "arṇasa" refers to the ocean or water, which can symbolize the vastness of life and the challenges we face. For example, navigating through life's difficulties can be likened to sailing through turbulent waters, where one must remain focused and resilient.
  • dhāyi pajraḥ: This means "the one who holds the strength." "Pajraḥ" can be associated with power or might. In a real-life context, this could refer to a leader or a person who possesses the strength to guide others through challenges, much like a mentor who supports their mentees in overcoming obstacles.
  • upa vām avaḥ śaraṇaṁ: This phrase translates to "may we approach the refuge." It signifies seeking safety or protection. In everyday life, this can be seen when individuals seek comfort in their families or communities during times of distress, highlighting the importance of support systems.
  • gameyaṁ śūro nājma patayadbhir evaiḥ: This can be interpreted as "the brave one who is not swayed by the stars." It suggests that true courage comes from within and is not determined by external circumstances. For instance, a person who pursues their dreams despite societal pressures exemplifies this bravery, showing that inner strength is crucial for success.

  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of unity, strength, and seeking refuge in times of need. It teaches us that collaboration and support from others can help us navigate life's challenges. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various contexts, such as teamwork in professional settings, the importance of community support during crises, and the need for inner strength to pursue one's goals. Ultimately, it serves as a reminder that we are stronger together and that true courage lies in our ability to face challenges head-on while supporting one another.
