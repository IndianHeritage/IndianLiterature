गोभिर मिमिक्षुं दधिरे सुपारम् इन्द्रं ज्यैष्ठ्याय धायसे गृणानाः |मन्दानः सोमं पपिवां ṛजिषिन सम अस्मभ्यम् पुरुधा गा इषण्य ||
gobhir mimikṣuṁ dadhire supāram indraṁ jyaiṣṭhyāya dhāyase gṛṇānāḥ | mandānaḥ somam papivām̐ ṛjīṣin sam asmabhyam purudhā gā iṣaṇya ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • gobhir: with the cows (representing wealth and nourishment)
    • mimikṣuṁ: the one who is to be praised or invoked
    • dadhire: they have established or placed
    • supāram: the well-prepared or excellent
    • indraṁ: Indra, the king of gods and god of rain and thunder
    • jyaiṣṭhyāya: for the highest or supreme
    • dhāyase: you who are sustaining or supporting
    • gṛṇānāḥ: singing or chanting
    • mandānaḥ: the one who is sweet or pleasant
    • somam: the soma, a sacred drink
    • papivām̐: we have drunk
    • ṛjīṣin: the one who is righteous or virtuous
    • sam: together
    • asmabhyam: for us
    • purudhā: in many ways
    • gā: song or hymn
    • iṣaṇya: to be desired or sought after
  • Overall Meaning:
    • This mantra invokes Indra, the chief deity associated with rain, thunder, and strength, emphasizing the importance of community and collective effort in seeking blessings.
    • The reference to cows symbolizes wealth and sustenance, indicating that prosperity is a communal goal.
    • The act of singing praises and drinking soma signifies the importance of rituals and shared experiences in fostering unity and spiritual growth.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In agricultural communities, the collective worship of deities like Indra during monsoon seasons is crucial for ensuring good rainfall and bountiful harvests.
    • Community gatherings, such as festivals, where people come together to celebrate and share food, echo the sentiments of unity and shared prosperity expressed in the mantra.
    • In modern contexts, corporate teamwork and collaboration can be seen as a reflection of this mantra, where collective efforts lead to success and growth.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of community, shared rituals, and the pursuit of collective well-being.
    • It teaches us that prosperity and success are best achieved when individuals come together, support one another, and engage in shared practices.
    • In today's world, the principles of collaboration, unity, and mutual support remain relevant, reminding us of the importance of working together for common goals.
