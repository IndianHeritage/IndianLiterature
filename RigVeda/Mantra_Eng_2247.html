ऋतावानः प्रतिचक्ष्याणृतā पुनराता आतस्तुḥ कवयो महस् पथः |ते बाहुभ्यां धमितम् अग्निम अश्मनि नकिḥ षो अस्त्य अरणो जहुर हि तम् ||
ṛtāvānaḥ praticakṣyānṛtā punar āta ā tasthuḥ kavayo mahas pathaḥ | te bāhubhyāṁ dhamitam agnim aśmani nakiḥ ṣo asty araṇo jahur hi tam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ṛtāvānaḥ: One who is endowed with truth or righteousness.
    • praticakṣyān: Those who perceive or understand.
    • anṛtā: Falsehood or untruth.
    • punar: Again or once more.
    • āta: To come or to approach.
    • ā: This or that.
    • tasthuḥ: They who are established or firm.
    • kavayo: The wise or the poets.
    • mahas: Great or vast.
    • pathaḥ: Path or way.
    • te: They.
    • bāhubhyāṁ: With their arms.
    • dhamitam: Having been kindled or ignited.
    • agnim: The fire.
    • aśmani: In the stone or on the earth.
    • nakiḥ: Who is there?
    • ṣo: Six or the six.
    • asty: There is.
    • araṇo: A refuge or shelter.
    • jahur: They have abandoned or left.
    • hi: Indeed.
    • tam: That.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the importance of truth and righteousness in understanding the world.
    • The wise individuals (kavayo) who are established in truth (ṛtāvānaḥ) are able to perceive the reality around them.
    • It emphasizes the need to abandon falsehood (anṛtā) and to seek the path of righteousness (mahas pathaḥ).
    • The imagery of fire (agnim) suggests purification and transformation, indicating that through truth, one can find refuge (araṇo) and strength.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, a company that values transparency and honesty is more likely to build trust with its employees and customers, leading to long-term success.
    • In personal relationships, being truthful fosters deeper connections and understanding, while deceit can lead to conflict and separation.
    • In social movements, leaders who advocate for truth and justice often inspire others to join their cause, creating a collective effort towards positive change.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of truth and righteousness in navigating life’s complexities.
    • It serves as a reminder that embracing truth leads to clarity, strength, and a sense of refuge in a world often filled with uncertainty.
    • By applying the principles of this mantra in our daily lives, we can foster a more honest, compassionate, and just society.
