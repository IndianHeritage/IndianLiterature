यो अस्मै हव्यैर घृतवत्भिर अविदत प्र तं प्राच नयति ब्रह्मणस् पतिः |उरुश्यतीं अम्हसो रक्षती रिषोऽंहोश् चिद अस्मा उरुचक्रिर अद्भुतः ||
yo asmai havyair ghṛtavadbhir avidhat pra tam prācā nayati brahmaṇas patiḥ | uruṣyatīm aṁhaso rakṣatī riṣo3 'ṁhoś cid asmā urucakrir adbhutaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yo: who
    • asmai: to him
    • havyair: with offerings
    • ghṛtavadbhir: with ghee
    • avidhat: has arranged
    • pra: forward
    • tam: that
    • prācā: the ancient
    • nayati: leads
    • brahmaṇas: of the divine
    • patiḥ: lord
    • uruṣyatīm: of great strength
    • aṁhaso: of the light
    • rakṣatī: protects
    • riṣo3: of the seers
    • 'ṁhoś: of the wise
    • cid: indeed
    • asmā: from us
    • urucakrir: of great deeds
    • adbhutaḥ: wonderful
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of a divine entity who is the lord of the universe and is approached with offerings, particularly ghee, which is considered sacred in Vedic rituals.
    • The phrase "who leads that ancient divine" suggests guidance and wisdom that transcends time, indicating that this entity is a source of strength and protection.
    • The mention of "great deeds" and "wonderful" highlights the extraordinary nature of this divine being, emphasizing the importance of recognizing and honoring such forces in our lives.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern life, this mantra can be related to the concept of seeking guidance from higher powers or mentors in our personal and professional journeys. Just as offerings are made to the divine, individuals often seek wisdom from those who have more experience.
    • The idea of protection can be seen in the way communities come together to support one another during difficult times, reflecting the strength and unity that the mantra speaks of.
    • In a corporate setting, leaders who embody the qualities of wisdom and strength can inspire their teams to achieve great deeds, much like the divine being described in the mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of recognizing and honoring the divine forces in our lives that guide, protect, and inspire us.
    • It encourages individuals to seek wisdom and strength from higher powers, whether through spiritual practices, mentorship, or community support.
    • In essence, it serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of all beings and the divine guidance available to us in our pursuit of greatness and fulfillment.
