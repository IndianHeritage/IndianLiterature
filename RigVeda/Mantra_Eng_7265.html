भूरिभिः समहा ऋषिभिर बर्हिष्मद्भिः स्तविष्यसे |यद इत्तम एकमेकम इच्छर वत्सान पराददः ||
bhūribhiḥ samaha ṛṣibhir barhiṣmadbhiḥ staviṣyase | yad ittham ekam-ekam ic chara vatsān parādadaḥ ||
  • bhūribhiḥ: This word can be translated to "with abundance" or "with plenty." It signifies the richness of life and the universe.
  • samaha: This means "equal" or "balanced." It suggests harmony and equilibrium in nature and human life.
  • ṛṣibhir: Referring to "sages" or "wise ones," this term emphasizes the importance of wisdom and knowledge in guiding human actions.
  • barhiṣmadbhiḥ: This word relates to "sacred offerings" or "rituals." It highlights the significance of rituals in connecting with the divine and the universe.
  • staviṣyase: This translates to "you will be praised" or "you will be honored." It indicates the recognition of the divine or the forces of nature.
  • yad: Meaning "that" or "which," it introduces a conditional or causal relationship.
  • ittham: This word means "thus" or "in this way," indicating a manner of action or existence.
  • ekam-ekam: This translates to "one by one," suggesting a methodical or individual approach to understanding or experiencing life.
  • ic: Meaning "to desire" or "to wish," it reflects human aspirations and intentions.
  • chara: This means "to move" or "to go," indicating action and progress.
  • vatsān: Referring to "calves" or "young ones," it symbolizes new beginnings, growth, and nurturing.
  • parādadaḥ: This translates to "to be given" or "to be bestowed," indicating the act of receiving or being granted something valuable.

The mantra emphasizes the interconnectedness of abundance, wisdom, and ritual in achieving harmony in life. It suggests that through the guidance of sages and the performance of sacred rituals, one can attain recognition and fulfillment. The phrase "one by one" highlights the importance of individual actions and desires in the broader context of existence.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as:

  • Personal Growth: Individuals can focus on their personal development by seeking knowledge and wisdom, taking small steps towards their goals, and recognizing the abundance in their lives.
  • Community Rituals: Engaging in community rituals and celebrations can foster a sense of belonging and connection, promoting harmony and balance within the group.
  • Environmental Awareness: The emphasis on abundance can inspire people to appreciate and protect nature, recognizing the importance of sustainable practices for future generations.

In conclusion, this mantra serves as a reminder of the significance of balance, wisdom, and ritual in our lives. By embracing these principles, we can cultivate a deeper understanding of ourselves and our place in the universe, leading to a more harmonious existence.
