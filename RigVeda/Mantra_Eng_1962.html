मन्त्र: करम्भ ओषधि भव पीवो वृक्क उदारथिः | वातापे पीव इद भव ||
karambha oṣadhe bhava pīvo vṛkka udārathiḥ | vātāpe pīva id bhava ||
  • Mantra: "karambha oṣadhe bhava pīvo vṛkka udārathiḥ | vātāpe pīva id bhava ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • karambha: A term that can be interpreted as "to be fruitful" or "to produce."
    • oṣadhe: Refers to "herbs" or "plants," symbolizing nature and its bounty.
    • bhava: Means "become" or "may you become," indicating a wish or invocation.
    • pīvo: Translates to "nourishing" or "sustaining," often associated with abundance.
    • vṛkka: Refers to "trees," representing strength, stability, and longevity.
    • udārathiḥ: Means "generous" or "noble," suggesting a quality of giving and openness.
    • vātāpe: Refers to "the wind" or "air," symbolizing movement and change.
    • id: Means "this," indicating a focus on the present or immediate context.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the essence of nature, calling upon plants and trees to be fruitful and nourishing.
    • It emphasizes the importance of generosity and nobility in our actions, suggesting that we should strive to be like the trees that provide shelter and sustenance.
    • The mention of the wind signifies the ever-changing nature of life and the need to adapt while remaining grounded in our values.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In agriculture, this mantra can inspire farmers to cultivate their land with care, ensuring that their crops are abundant and nourishing for the community.
    • In personal relationships, embodying the qualities of generosity and support can lead to stronger bonds, much like how trees provide shelter and resources to various forms of life.
    • In environmental conservation, recognizing the importance of trees and plants can motivate individuals and communities to engage in reforestation and sustainable practices.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of our interconnectedness with nature and the importance of nurturing both the environment and our relationships.
    • Its significance lies in promoting a lifestyle that values sustainability, generosity, and adaptability, which are essential in today's rapidly changing world.
    • By embodying the principles expressed in this mantra, individuals can contribute to a more harmonious and balanced existence, both personally and collectively.
