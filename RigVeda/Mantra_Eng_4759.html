उद् अभ्राणिव स्तनयन्न इयर्तीन्द्रो राधांस्य अश्व्यानी गव्याः |त्वमसि प्रदिवः कारुधायाः मा त्वादामान आ दभन मग्नोः ||
ud abhrāṇīva stanayann iyartīndro rādhāṁsy aśvyāni gavyā | tvam asi pradivaḥ kārudhāyā mā tvādāmāna ā dabhan maghonaḥ ||
  • ud abhrāṇīva: This phrase can be interpreted as "like the clouds that rise." It symbolizes the potential for growth and abundance, much like how clouds bring rain, which is essential for life.
  • stanayann: This word means "nourishing" or "sustaining." It emphasizes the importance of providing sustenance, akin to how a mother nourishes her child.
  • iyartīndro: This term refers to "Indra," the king of gods in Vedic literature, who is often associated with strength and power. Here, it signifies the divine force that drives change and progress.
  • rādhāṁsy: This can be understood as "the desires" or "the aspirations." It reflects the human condition of yearning for fulfillment and success.
  • aśvyāni gavyā: This phrase translates to "the swift-moving cows." Cows are revered in Vedic culture, symbolizing wealth and prosperity. The swiftness indicates the urgency and importance of achieving one's goals.
  • tvam asi: Meaning "you are," this phrase addresses the divine or the higher self, suggesting a connection between the individual and the universal consciousness.
  • pradivaḥ: This term means "the one who leads." It signifies guidance and direction, much like a mentor or a leader who helps others navigate their paths.
  • kārudhāyā: This can be interpreted as "the one who brings forth." It emphasizes the act of creation and manifestation, akin to an artist bringing their vision to life.
  • mā tvādāmāna: This phrase means "do not be distant from us." It expresses a desire for closeness and connection, highlighting the importance of community and support.
  • ā dabhan maghonaḥ: This translates to "may you grant us strength." It is a plea for empowerment and resilience, essential qualities for overcoming challenges.

Conclusion: This mantra encapsulates the essence of growth, nourishment, and the pursuit of aspirations. It emphasizes the interconnectedness of individuals with the divine and the importance of guidance and support in achieving one's goals. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios, such as in personal development, where individuals seek mentorship and community support to realize their dreams. It also highlights the significance of nurturing relationships and fostering a sense of belonging, which are crucial for emotional and spiritual well-being. Ultimately, the mantra serves as a reminder of the divine presence in our lives, encouraging us to seek strength and connection as we navigate our journeys.
