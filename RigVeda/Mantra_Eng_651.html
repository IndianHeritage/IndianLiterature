स शेवृधम अधि धा द्युम्नम अस्मे महि क्षत्रं जनाशाल इन्द्र तव्यं |रक्षा च नो मग्होनः पाहि सूरिन रायें च नः स्वपत्याऽइषे धाः ||
sa śevṛdham adhi dhā dyumnam asme mahi kṣatraṁ janāṣāḻ indra tavyam | rakṣā ca no maghonaḥ pāhi sūrīn rāye ca naḥ svapatyā iṣe dhāḥ ||
  • sa śevṛdham: This phrase can be interpreted as "that which is well-established" or "that which is firm." It signifies stability and strength in one's endeavors.
  • adhi dhā dyumnam: This translates to "may we attain glory." It emphasizes the pursuit of success and recognition in our actions.
  • asme mahi kṣatraṁ: This means "may we have a great dominion." It reflects the desire for power and influence, not just for oneself but for the community or society.
  • janāṣāḻ indra tavyam: This can be understood as "the people are worthy of Indra's blessings." Indra, in Vedic literature, is often associated with strength and leadership, suggesting that the community deserves divine support.
  • rakṣā ca no maghonaḥ: This translates to "protect us from harm." It is a plea for safety and security, highlighting the importance of protection in our lives.
  • pāhi sūrīn: This means "guard the heroes." It emphasizes the need to protect those who are brave and noble, recognizing their contributions to society.
  • rāye ca naḥ svapatyā: This translates to "may we have wealth and prosperity." It reflects the aspiration for abundance and success in material and spiritual pursuits.
  • iṣe dhāḥ: This can be interpreted as "may we be steadfast in our intentions." It signifies the importance of determination and focus in achieving one's goals.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda encapsulates the essence of seeking strength, glory, protection, and prosperity for oneself and the community. In real-life scenarios, it can be applied in various contexts such as leadership, community service, and personal development. For instance, a leader may invoke this mantra to inspire their team towards a common goal, ensuring that they work together with strength and determination. Additionally, individuals can reflect on these principles in their daily lives, striving for stability, success, and the protection of their loved ones. The significance of this mantra lies in its holistic approach to life, emphasizing the interconnectedness of personal aspirations and communal well-being.
