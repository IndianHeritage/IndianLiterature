अयोदंष्ट्रो अर्चिषा यातुधानान उप स्पृश जातवेदः समिद्धः |आ जिह्वया मूरेदेवान रभस्व क्रव्यादो वृक्त्व्य अपि धत्स्वासन ||
ayodaṁṣṭro arciṣā yātudhānān upa spṛśa jātavedaḥ samiddhaḥ | ā jihvayā mūradevān rabhasva kravyādo vṛktvy api dhatsvāsan ||
  • ayodaṁṣṭro: This term can be broken down into "ayo" meaning "not" and "daṁṣṭra" meaning "tooth" or "fang". It signifies the absence of harmful or aggressive forces.
  • arciṣā: This word translates to "with light" or "radiance". It represents the illuminating power of knowledge and wisdom.
  • yātudhānān: Refers to "evil spirits" or "malevolent beings". This term highlights the presence of negative influences in the world.
  • upa spṛśa: This phrase means "come close" or "approach". It indicates a call for protection or assistance from higher powers.
  • jātavedaḥ: This term refers to "the one who knows all births" or "the all-knowing". It is often associated with Agni, the fire god, symbolizing knowledge and enlightenment.
  • samiddhaḥ: Meaning "kindled" or "lit\ it signifies the activation of energy or consciousness.
  • ā jihvayā: This translates to "with the tongue". It can imply the power of speech and communication.
  • mūradevān: Refers to "the divine beings" or "gods". It emphasizes the connection to the divine and the spiritual realm.
  • rabhasva: This term means "be powerful" or "be strong". It is a call for strength and vigor in the face of challenges.
  • kravyādo: Refers to "flesh-eating" or "carnivorous". It symbolizes the darker aspects of nature and existence.
  • vṛktvy: This term means "to be driven away" or "to be expelled". It indicates the removal of negativity or harmful influences.
  • api dhatsvāsan: This phrase translates to "also grant us protection". It is a plea for safety and security from malevolent forces.

Conclusion: This mantra serves as a powerful invocation for protection against negative influences and malevolent beings. It emphasizes the importance of knowledge, strength, and divine assistance in overcoming challenges. In real life, this can be applicable in various scenarios such as facing personal struggles, dealing with toxic relationships, or seeking guidance in difficult situations. By reciting this mantra, individuals can cultivate a sense of inner strength and clarity, allowing them to navigate through life's adversities with confidence and resilience.
