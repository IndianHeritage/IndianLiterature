एदु मध्वो मदिन्तरं सिञ्च वाध्वर्यो अन्धसः |एवाहि वीरः स्तवते सदावृद्धः ||
ed u madhvo madintaraṁ siñca vādhvaryo andhasaḥ | evā hi vīraḥ stavate sadāvṛdhaḥ ||
  • ed - This word can be interpreted as "this" or "here," indicating a presence or a focus on the current moment.
  • u - This is often used as a conjunction meaning "and," connecting thoughts or ideas.
  • madhvo - Refers to "sweetness" or "honey," symbolizing nourishment and the essence of life.
  • madintaraṁ - This can be understood as "the one who is intoxicated" or "the one who is filled with joy," suggesting a state of bliss or ecstasy.
  • siñca - Means "to pour" or "to sprinkle," indicating the act of bestowing or sharing something valuable.
  • vādhvaryo - Refers to "the priest" or "the one who performs rituals," highlighting the importance of spiritual guidance and tradition.
  • andhasaḥ - Translates to "of the blind" or "darkness," which can symbolize ignorance or lack of awareness.
  • evā - Means "indeed" or "certainly," reinforcing the truth of the statement that follows.
  • hi - A particle that emphasizes the preceding statement, often translated as "for" or "because."
  • vīraḥ - Refers to "the hero" or "the brave one," symbolizing strength, valor, and courage.
  • stavate - Means "to praise" or "to worship," indicating an act of reverence or acknowledgment.
  • sadāvṛdhaḥ - Translates to "always growing" or "ever-increasing," suggesting a continuous development or enhancement.

The mantra can be interpreted as a call to recognize and celebrate the sweetness of life, the joy that comes from spiritual practices, and the importance of guidance in overcoming ignorance. It emphasizes the role of the priest or spiritual leader in pouring forth blessings and wisdom, which leads to the growth of courage and valor in individuals.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as:

  • In personal development, individuals can seek guidance from mentors or spiritual leaders to overcome challenges and grow in their journey.
  • In community settings, rituals and celebrations can be organized to honor and appreciate the contributions of individuals who embody courage and strength.
  • In moments of difficulty, one can reflect on the sweetness of life and the joy that can be found even in challenging circumstances, fostering resilience and positivity.

In conclusion, this mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of joy, guidance, and growth. It encourages individuals to seek wisdom, celebrate life, and cultivate courage, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling and enriched existence.
