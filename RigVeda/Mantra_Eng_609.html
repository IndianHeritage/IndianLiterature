तक्षद यत त उशनā सहसा सहो वि रोडसी मजनā बाधते शवः |आ त्वā वातस्य नृमणो मनोयुज आ पूर्यमाणम अवहन्न अभि श्रवः ||
takṣad yat ta uśanā sahasā saho vi rodasī majmanā bādhate śavaḥ | ā tvā vātasya nṛmaṇo manoyuja ā pūryamāṇam avahann abhi śravaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • takṣad: He who creates or shapes
    • yat: that which
    • ta: that
    • uśanā: with brilliance or light
    • sahasā: suddenly or swiftly
    • saho: with strength or power
    • vi: in the midst of
    • rodasī: the two worlds or realms
    • majmanā: the one who is being worshipped or revered
    • bādhate: obstructs or hinders
    • śavaḥ: the dead or lifeless
    • ā: to or towards
    • tvā: you
    • vātasya: of the wind or air
    • nṛmaṇo: of the human or man
    • manoyuja: born of the mind or mental
    • ā: to or towards
    • pūryamāṇam: being filled or fulfilled
    • avahann: brings forth or carries
    • abhi: towards or in the direction of
    • śravaḥ: fame or glory
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the creative force that shapes existence and the brilliance that comes from it.
    • It emphasizes the power of the mind and the human spirit in overcoming obstacles and achieving greatness.
    • The reference to "the two worlds" suggests a connection between the physical and spiritual realms, indicating that our actions in one affect the other.
    • The mantra also highlights the importance of mental strength and clarity in manifesting our desires and goals.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In personal development, individuals often face obstacles that can feel insurmountable. This mantra serves as a reminder that with mental strength and clarity, one can overcome these challenges.
    • In the context of leadership, a leader who embodies the qualities of strength and brilliance can inspire their team to achieve great things, much like the creative force described in the mantra.
    • In spiritual practices, the connection between the physical and spiritual realms can be seen in meditation, where individuals seek to align their thoughts and actions with their higher purpose.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its emphasis on the power of creation, mental strength, and the interconnectedness of all realms of existence.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal growth to leadership and spiritual practices, encouraging individuals to harness their inner strength and brilliance.
    • By embodying the principles of this mantra, one can navigate life's challenges with resilience and purpose, ultimately leading to a fulfilling and impactful life.
