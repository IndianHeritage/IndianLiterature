अस्मभ्यं त्वा वसुविदम् अभि वाणीर अनूषत |गोभिष ṭे वर्णम् अभि वासयामसि ||
asmabhyaṁ tvā vasuvidam abhi vāṇīr anūṣata | gobhiṣ ṭe varṇam abhi vāsayāmasi ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • asmabhyaṁ: for us
    • tvā: you
    • vasuvidam: the one who knows wealth or prosperity
    • abhi: towards
    • vāṇīr: speech or communication
    • anūṣata: may we be guided
    • gobhiṣ: with the cows (symbolizing wealth and nourishment)
    • ṭe: your
    • varṇam: color or essence
    • abhi: towards
    • vāsayāmasi: may we dwell or reside
  • Overall Meaning: This mantra expresses a prayer for guidance and prosperity. It seeks the blessings of a divine entity who is knowledgeable about wealth and communication, asking for a harmonious relationship with abundance (symbolized by cows) and the essence of that abundance.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be invoked by entrepreneurs seeking guidance in their ventures, asking for clarity in communication and prosperity in their endeavors.
    • In personal life, individuals may recite this mantra during times of financial uncertainty, hoping to attract wealth and improve their communication skills to foster better relationships.
    • In community gatherings, this mantra can be used to promote collective prosperity and harmony, encouraging members to work together towards common goals.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its emphasis on the interconnectedness of communication, prosperity, and guidance. It highlights the importance of seeking wisdom in our pursuits and fostering a positive environment for growth. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applicable in various aspects, including business, personal relationships, and community development, serving as a reminder to align our intentions with the pursuit of abundance and understanding.
