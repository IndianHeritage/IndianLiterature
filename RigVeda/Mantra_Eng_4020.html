यो जागार तं ऋचः कामयन्ते यो जागार तं उ सामानि यन्ति |यो जागार तं अयं सोम आह तवाहम अस्मि सख्ये न्योकाः ||
yo jāgāra tam ṛcaḥ kāmayante yo jāgāra tam u sāmāni yanti | yo jāgāra tam ayaṁ soma āha tavāham asmi sakhye nyokāḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yo: who
    • jāgāra: awakens or is awake
    • tam: that
    • ṛcaḥ: hymns or verses
    • kāmayante: desire or wish for
    • u: and
    • sāmāni: melodies or songs
    • yanti: go or lead
    • ayaṁ: this
    • soma: the divine drink or nectar
    • āha: speaks or says
    • tavāham: I am yours
    • sakhye: in friendship
    • nyokāḥ: I offer or dedicate
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the divine presence that awakens and inspires.
    • It emphasizes the connection between the seeker and the divine, highlighting the importance of hymns and melodies in spiritual practice.
    • The reference to "soma" symbolizes the essence of life and the divine nectar that nourishes the soul.
    • The phrase "I am yours in friendship" signifies a deep bond and trust between the devotee and the divine.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, this mantra can be reflected in moments of inspiration, such as when an artist creates a masterpiece or a musician composes a beautiful song, feeling a connection to something greater.
    • In relationships, the idea of being "yours in friendship" can be seen in the bonds of trust and support we share with friends and loved ones.
    • During meditation or prayer, one may feel a sense of awakening and clarity, akin to the "awakening" mentioned in the mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of awakening to the divine presence in our lives and recognizing the beauty of connection through hymns and friendship.
    • It encourages individuals to seek inspiration and nourishment from the divine, fostering a sense of community and support.
    • In real-life scenarios, it serves as a reminder to cultivate relationships based on trust and to remain open to the divine guidance that inspires creativity and connection.
