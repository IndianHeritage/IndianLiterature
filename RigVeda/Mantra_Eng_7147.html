आद ऊ नु ते अनु क्रतुं स्वाहा वरस्य यज्यवः |श्वात्रम् अर्का अनूषतेंद्र गोत्रस्य दावने ||
ād ū nu te anu kratuṁ svāhā varasya yajyavaḥ | śvātram arkā anūṣatendra gotrasya dāvane ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ād: "from the beginning" or "first"
    • ū: "to you"
    • nu: "indeed" or "certainly"
    • te: "your"
    • anu: "following" or "after"
    • kratuṁ: "intellect" or "willpower"
    • svāhā: "may it be so" or "offering"
    • varasya: "of the divine" or "of the chosen one"
    • yajyavaḥ: "those who perform sacrifices"
    • śvātram: "the black" or "dark"
    • arkā: "the light" or "radiance"
    • anūṣat: "to be followed" or "to be pursued"
    • indra: "the king of gods" or "powerful"
    • gotrasya: "of the lineage" or "of the family"
    • dāvane: "in the fire" or "in the sacrifice"
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of intention and willpower in spiritual practices.
    • The phrase "from the beginning" suggests that the pursuit of knowledge and enlightenment is a continuous journey.
    • It acknowledges the divine presence and the significance of performing rituals or sacrifices with a pure heart.
    • The reference to "light" and "dark" symbolizes the duality of existence, where one must navigate through challenges to reach enlightenment.
    • Indra, as the king of gods, represents strength and power, indicating that divine support is essential in our endeavors.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In personal development, setting clear intentions and following through with determination can lead to success, much like the mantra suggests.
    • In community rituals, the collective effort of individuals performing sacrifices or prayers can create a sense of unity and purpose.
    • In facing life's challenges, recognizing the balance between light and dark can help individuals make informed decisions and grow from experiences.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the significance of intention, divine support, and the journey of self-discovery.
    • It is applicable in various aspects of life, including personal growth, community engagement, and spiritual practices.
    • By embodying the principles of this mantra, individuals can cultivate a deeper connection with themselves and the universe, leading to a more fulfilling life.
