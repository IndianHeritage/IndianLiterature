रथं ये चक्रुः सुवृतं सुचेतसोऽविह्वरन्तं मनसस्स परिध्यायाः |तां ऊं व आस्य सवनस्य पीतय आ वो वाजाः ऋभवो वेदयामसि ||
rathaṁ ye cakruḥ suvṛtaṁ sucetaso 'vihvarantam manasas pari dhyayā | tām̐ ū nv a1sya savanasya pītaya ā vo vājā ṛbhavo vedayāmasi ||
  • Mantra: "rathaṁ ye cakruḥ suvṛtaṁ sucetaso 'vihvarantam manasas pari dhyayā | tām̐ ū nv a1sya savanasya pītaya ā vo vājā ṛbhavo vedayāmasi ||"
  • Translation: "Those who have constructed the chariot with good intentions and clear minds, who are engaged in meditation, may we invoke the essence of this chariot, which is filled with the essence of the sun, and may we attain the strength of the horses."

  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • rathaṁ: chariot
    • ye: those who
    • cakruḥ: have constructed
    • suvṛtaṁ: with good intentions
    • sucetaso: with clear minds
    • avihvarantam: engaged in meditation
    • manasas: of the mind
    • pari dhyayā: may we meditate upon
    • tām̐: that
    • ū nv: may we invoke
    • asya: of this
    • savanasy: essence of the sun
    • pītaya: filled with
    • ā vo: may we attain
    • vājā: strength
    • ṛbhavo: of the horses
    • vedayāmasi: we invoke

  • Significance:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of intention and clarity of mind in our actions. Just as a chariot is constructed with care and purpose, our lives should be lived with similar mindfulness.
    • The chariot symbolizes the journey of life, and the horses represent the strength and energy we harness to move forward. This can be related to how we channel our thoughts and emotions to achieve our goals.
    • In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios such as setting personal goals, engaging in teamwork, or even in spiritual practices where focus and intention are crucial.

  • Real-Life Application:
    • In a professional setting, when a team comes together to work on a project, they must have a clear vision and shared intentions, much like the construction of a chariot. This ensures that everyone is aligned and working towards a common goal.
    • In personal development, individuals can meditate on their intentions and visualize their goals, invoking the strength and energy needed to achieve them, akin to invoking the essence of the sun for vitality.
    • In spiritual practices, this mantra can serve as a reminder to maintain a focused and clear mind, allowing for deeper meditation and connection with one's inner self.

  • Conclusion:
    • The mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the significance of intention, clarity, and focus in our lives. By embodying these principles, we can navigate our journeys with purpose and strength, ultimately leading to personal and collective success.
    • In essence, it encourages us to construct our own 'chariots' in life, filled with positive energy and directed towards meaningful goals.
