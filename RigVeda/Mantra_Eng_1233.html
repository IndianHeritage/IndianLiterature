याभिः कुत्सम आरजुनेयं शतक्रतुः प्र तुर्वीतिम प्र च दभीतिम आवतम् |याभिर ध्वसन्तिम पुरुषान्तिम आवतं ताभिर ऊषु ऊतिभिर अश्विनां गतम् ||
yābhiḥ kutsam ārjuneyaṁ śatakratū pra turvītim pra ca dabhītim āvatam | yābhir dhvasantim puruṣantim āvataṁ tābhir ū ṣu ūtibhir aśvinā gatam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yābhiḥ: By whom
    • kutsam: The one who is criticized or insulted
    • ārjuneyaṁ: The one who is strong or powerful
    • śatakratū: The one who has performed a hundred sacrifices
    • pra: Forward or towards
    • turvītim: The one who is capable of overcoming obstacles
    • pra ca: And also
    • dabhītim: The one who is fearless
    • āvatam: Protect
    • yābhir: By whom
    • dhvasantim: The one who is destroyed or eliminated
    • puruṣantim: The one who is a person or human
    • āvataṁ: Protect
    • tābhir: By whom
    • ū: O
    • ṣu: Good or auspicious
    • ūtibhir: By the auspicious ones
    • aśvinā: O Ashvins (the twin deities)
    • gatam: Gone or departed
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the divine powers of the Ashvins, who are known for their healing and protective qualities.
    • It emphasizes the importance of overcoming criticism and obstacles with strength and fearlessness.
    • The mantra suggests that through divine intervention, one can be protected from destruction and negativity.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, when facing criticism or challenges, one can draw strength from this mantra to remain focused and fearless.
    • In personal life, during times of emotional turmoil or when feeling overwhelmed, reciting this mantra can serve as a reminder to seek protection and support from higher powers.
    • In health-related issues, invoking the Ashvins can symbolize the quest for healing and recovery, encouraging individuals to seek medical help while also relying on spiritual strength.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of divine support in overcoming life's challenges.
    • It teaches us to be resilient in the face of adversity and to seek protection from negative influences.
    • In real-life scenarios, it serves as a powerful reminder of the strength that comes from faith and the importance of maintaining a positive outlook.
