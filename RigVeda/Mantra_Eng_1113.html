यः शूरैः हव्यो यश्च भीरुभिः यो धावद्भिः हूयते यश्च जिग्युभिः | इन्द्रं यं विश्वा भुवनाभि संधधुर मरुत्वन्तं सख्याय हवामहे ||
yaḥ śūrebhir havyo yaś ca bhīrubhir yo dhāvadbhir hūyate yaś ca jigyubhiḥ | indraṁ yaṁ viśvā bhuvanābhi saṁdadhur marutvantaṁ sakhyāya havāmahe ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yaḥ: who
    • śūrebhir: by the brave
    • havya: worthy of offerings
    • yaś: who
    • ca: and
    • bhīrubhir: by the strong
    • yo: who
    • dhāvadbhir: by those who run
    • hūyate: is invoked
    • yaś: who
    • ca: and
    • jigyubhiḥ: by those who strive
    • indraṁ: Indra (the king of gods)
    • yaṁ: whom
    • viśvā: all
    • bhuvanābhi: by the worlds
    • saṁdadhur: they unite
    • marutvantaṁ: the powerful (Maruts)
    • sakhyāya: for friendship
    • havāmahe: we invoke
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of invoking Indra, the king of gods, who is associated with strength, bravery, and the power to overcome challenges.
    • The mantra highlights the importance of unity and collaboration among the brave and strong individuals who strive for a common goal.
    • It emphasizes the idea that when all beings unite in purpose, they can invoke divine support and strength.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, a team working together towards a common project can be seen as embodying the spirit of this mantra. When team members support each other and combine their strengths, they can achieve remarkable results.
    • In community service, individuals coming together to help those in need exemplify the unity and strength described in the mantra. Their collective efforts can bring about significant change.
    • In sports, a team that works cohesively, with each player contributing their unique skills, can overcome formidable opponents, reflecting the essence of invoking strength through unity.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for unity, strength, and collaboration. It teaches us that by coming together and supporting one another, we can invoke greater powers to achieve our goals.
    • In real life, this mantra is applicable in various scenarios, such as teamwork in professional environments, community initiatives, and collaborative efforts in sports and other fields.
    • Ultimately, it serves as a reminder that collective strength and purpose can lead to success and fulfillment, both individually and as a community.
