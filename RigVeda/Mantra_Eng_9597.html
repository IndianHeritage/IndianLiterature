तृष्टामय प्रथमं यातवे सजूः सुसर्त्वा रसया श्वेत्या त्या |त्वं सिन्धो कुबहया गोमतीं कृमुम मेहत्न्वा सरथं याभिर ईयसे ||
tṛṣṭāmayā prathamaṁ yātave sajūḥ susartvā rasayā śvetyā tyā | tvaṁ sindho kubhayā gomatīṁ krumum mehatnvā sarathaṁ yābhir īyase ||
  • tṛṣṭāmayā: This word can be interpreted as "thirsty" or "desiring." It signifies a longing or a quest for something essential, much like how individuals seek knowledge or fulfillment in life.
  • prathamaṁ: Meaning "first" or "foremost," this word emphasizes the importance of prioritizing one's desires or needs, akin to how we often prioritize our goals in life.
  • yātave: This term can be understood as "to the one who travels" or "to the one who journeys." It reflects the idea of a seeker or a traveler on the path of life, similar to how we all embark on personal journeys to discover our purpose.
  • sajūḥ: This word means "with great effort" or "with determination." It highlights the importance of perseverance in achieving one's goals, much like athletes training rigorously to excel in their sport.
  • susartvā: This can be translated as "well spoken" or "with good words." It suggests the significance of communication and the power of words in shaping our relationships and experiences, similar to how effective communication can resolve conflicts.
  • rasayā: Meaning "with essence" or "with flavor," this word signifies the richness of experiences in life. It can be compared to how diverse experiences, like traveling or learning new skills, add depth to our lives.
  • śvetyā: This term translates to "white" or "pure," symbolizing clarity and purity of intention. It reflects the importance of having a clear and honest purpose in our actions, much like how transparency in business builds trust.
  • tvam: Meaning "you," this word personalizes the mantra, addressing the divine or the universal consciousness. It reminds us of our connection to a greater whole, similar to how community involvement fosters a sense of belonging.
  • sindho: This word refers to "ocean" or "river," symbolizing abundance and the flow of life. It can be likened to how rivers nourish the land, representing the resources and opportunities available to us.
  • kubhayā: This term can be interpreted as "with strength" or "with power." It emphasizes the importance of harnessing our inner strength to overcome challenges, much like how individuals face adversities with resilience.
  • gomatīṁ: This word means "wealthy" or "prosperous," symbolizing abundance in life. It reflects the aspiration for prosperity, akin to how people strive for financial stability and success.
  • krumum: This term can be understood as "great" or "mighty," indicating the significance of greatness in our endeavors. It can be compared to how leaders inspire greatness in their teams.
  • mehatnvā: This word signifies "with greatness" or "with importance," highlighting the value of significant actions in life. It reflects how impactful decisions can shape our future, similar to how a single choice can alter the course of our lives.
  • sarathaṁ: Meaning "with chariots" or "with vehicles," this term symbolizes the means to achieve our goals. It can be likened to how technology serves as a vehicle for progress in modern society.
  • yābhir: This word translates to "by which" or "through which," indicating the methods or paths we take in life. It reflects the various approaches we adopt to reach our objectives, similar to how different strategies can lead to success in business.
  • īyase: Meaning "you move" or "you go," this term emphasizes action and movement. It signifies the importance of taking steps towards our goals, much like how consistent effort leads to achievement.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of human experience, emphasizing the importance of desire, determination, communication, and action in our journeys. It serves as a reminder that life is a quest for fulfillment, where clarity of purpose and the richness of experiences play crucial roles. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire individuals to pursue their goals with perseverance, communicate effectively, and harness their inner strength to navigate challenges. Ultimately, it highlights the interconnectedness of our journeys and the significance of striving for greatness in all our endeavors.
