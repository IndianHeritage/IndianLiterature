आ द्वाभ्यां हरिभ्यां इन्द्र याह्य आ चतुर्भिर आ षड्भिर हूयमानः |आष्ठाभिर दशभिः सोमपेयं अयं सुतः सुमख मा मृधस कः ||
ā dvābhyāṁ haribhyām indra yāhy ā caturbhir ā ṣaḍbhir hūyamānaḥ | āṣṭābhir daśabhiḥ somapeyam ayaṁ sutaḥ sumakha mā mṛdhas kaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā dvābhyāṁ: From the two (or two paths)
    • haribhyām: To the gods (or the divine)
    • indra: O Indra (the king of gods)
    • yāhy: You who go
    • ā: From
    • caturbhir: With four
    • ā ṣaḍbhir: And six
    • hūyamānaḥ: Being invoked
    • āṣṭābhir: With eight
    • daśabhiḥ: And ten
    • somapeyam: The one who drinks soma
    • ayaṁ: This
    • sutaḥ: Son (or offspring)
    • sumakha: Well-formed or auspicious
    • mā: Not
    • mṛdhas: From the earth
    • kaḥ: Who?
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes Indra, the king of gods, asking for guidance and blessings from the divine.
    • The mention of "two paths" can symbolize choices in life, emphasizing the importance of making wise decisions.
    • The numbers "four," "six," "eight," and "ten" may represent various aspects of life, such as the four directions, six seasons, eight virtues, and ten commandments, highlighting the holistic approach to living.
    • The reference to "soma" signifies the pursuit of spiritual enlightenment and the importance of rituals in connecting with the divine.
    • The phrase "sumakha mā mṛdhas kaḥ" suggests a plea for protection from negative influences or harm, emphasizing the need for safety and well-being.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In decision-making scenarios, such as choosing a career path or a life partner, one can reflect on the "two paths" and seek guidance through meditation or prayer.
    • During festivals or rituals, the invocation of Indra can be a way to connect with the divine and seek blessings for prosperity and happiness.
    • In times of uncertainty or danger, reciting this mantra can serve as a reminder to seek protection and make choices that lead to safety and well-being.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra from the Rigveda emphasizes the importance of divine guidance in navigating life's choices and challenges.
    • Its significance lies in the holistic approach to life, encouraging individuals to seek spiritual connection and protection.
    • In real-life scenarios, it serves as a reminder to make wise decisions, seek blessings, and maintain a connection with the divine for overall well-being.
