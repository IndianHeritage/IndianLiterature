तागृणीहि नमस्येभिः शूषैः सुम्नेभिर इन्द्रावरुणा चकाना |वज्रेणान्यः शवसां हन्ति वृत्रं सिषक्त्य अन्यो वृजनेषु विप्रः ||
tā gṛṇīhi namasyebhiḥ śūṣaiḥ sumnebhir indrāvaruṇā cakānā | vajreṇānyaḥ śavasā hanti vṛtraṁ siṣakty anyo vṛjaneṣu vipraḥ ||
  • tā gṛṇīhi: This phrase translates to "sing praises to them." It emphasizes the importance of acknowledging and honoring the divine forces in our lives.
  • namasyebhiḥ: This means "with reverence." It suggests that our approach to the divine should be filled with respect and humility.
  • śūṣaiḥ: This word can be interpreted as "with the wise" or "with the learned." It indicates the value of seeking knowledge and wisdom in our spiritual practices.
  • sumnebhir: This translates to "with auspiciousness." It highlights the importance of positive intentions and actions in our lives.
  • indrāvaruṇā: Referring to the divine forces Indra and Varuna, this phrase signifies the duality of power and control in nature, representing strength and order.
  • cakānā: This means "who have created" or "who have established." It acknowledges the creative forces of the universe.
  • vajreṇānyaḥ: This translates to "with the thunderbolt." It symbolizes strength and the ability to overcome obstacles.
  • śavasā: Meaning "with the dead" or "with the lifeless," it can refer to the challenges or adversities we face in life.
  • hanti vṛtraṁ: This means "destroys the Vritra." Vritra is often seen as a dragon or serpent representing chaos and obstacles. This phrase signifies the triumph over challenges.
  • siṣakty: This translates to "with power" or "with strength." It emphasizes the importance of inner strength and resilience.
  • anyo vṛjaneṣu vipraḥ: This means "the wise one in the matters of overcoming." It suggests that wisdom is crucial in navigating life's challenges.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda encapsulates the essence of reverence, wisdom, and strength in overcoming life's challenges. It teaches us to honor the divine forces, seek knowledge, and approach life with positive intentions. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various situations such as facing personal challenges, seeking guidance in difficult times, or striving for success in endeavors. By embodying the principles of respect, wisdom, and resilience, we can navigate our lives more effectively and harmoniously.
