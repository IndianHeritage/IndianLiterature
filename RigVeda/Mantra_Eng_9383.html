यत् ते भूतं च भव्यम् च मनो जगाम दूरकम् |तत् ता आ वर्तयामसिह क्षयाय जीवसे ||
yat te bhūtaṁ ca bhavyaṁ ca mano jagāma dūrakam | tat ta ā vartayāmasīha kṣayāya jīvase ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yat: that which
    • te: of you
    • bhūtaṁ: the past
    • ca: and
    • bhavyaṁ: the future
    • ca: and
    • mano: mind
    • jagāma: has gone
    • dūrakam: far away
    • tat: that
    • ta: you
    • ā: indeed
    • vartayāmasi: we are bringing back
    • ha: indeed
    • kṣayāya: for destruction
    • jīvase: for life
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks to the connection between the past and the future, emphasizing the role of the mind in navigating these realms.
    • It suggests that while the mind may wander far from the present, there is a conscious effort to bring it back to a state of awareness and purpose.
    • The phrase "for destruction" can be interpreted as a reminder that life is transient and that we must focus on what truly matters.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In meditation practices, individuals often find their minds drifting to past regrets or future anxieties. This mantra encourages practitioners to gently guide their thoughts back to the present moment.
    • In the context of personal growth, one might reflect on past experiences to learn from them while also setting intentions for the future, thus embodying the essence of this mantra.
    • In leadership, a leader may need to consider historical lessons (bhūtaṁ) while planning for future goals (bhavyaṁ), ensuring that the team remains focused and aligned.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its reminder of the importance of mindfulness and awareness in our daily lives.
    • It teaches us to acknowledge our past and future while emphasizing the necessity of living in the present moment.
    • In practical terms, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as mindfulness practices, personal development, and effective leadership, guiding individuals to lead a more balanced and purposeful life.
