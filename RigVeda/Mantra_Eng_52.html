युñjanty asya kāmyā हरī विपक्षसā रथे |शोणā धृष्णū नृवहसā ||
yuñjanty asya kāmyā harī vipakṣasā rathe | śoṇā dhṛṣṇū nṛvāhasā ||
  • yuñjanty: This word can be translated as "they who unite" or "they who join together." It signifies the importance of collaboration and unity in achieving goals.
  • asya: Meaning "of this" or "of this one," it refers to the subject or the focus of the mantra, which can be interpreted as the collective effort or the community.
  • kāmyā: This translates to "desired" or "wishful." It indicates the aspirations or goals that the community or individuals are striving to achieve.
  • harī: Often associated with the divine or auspiciousness, "harī" can refer to a deity or a positive force that aids in the fulfillment of desires.
  • vipakṣasā: This term means "against adversaries" or "against opposition." It highlights the challenges and obstacles that may arise in the pursuit of goals.
  • rathe: Translated as "in the chariot," it symbolizes the journey or the path taken towards achieving one's desires, often implying movement and progress.
  • śoṇā: This word means "red" and can symbolize vitality, energy, or strength. It may also represent the passion and fervor needed to pursue one's goals.
  • dhṛṣṇū: Meaning "those who are bold" or "those who are courageous," it emphasizes the importance of bravery in facing challenges.
  • nṛvāhasā: This term refers to "human beings" or "mankind," indicating that the mantra is relevant to all people and their collective endeavors.

In summary, this mantra emphasizes the significance of unity, aspiration, and courage in the face of challenges. It suggests that when individuals come together with a common goal, supported by divine forces, they can overcome obstacles and achieve their desires. The imagery of a chariot signifies the journey of life, where collaboration and boldness are essential for success.


In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as:

  • Teamwork in Organizations: In a corporate setting, employees working together towards a common goal can achieve greater success than individuals working in isolation.
  • Community Initiatives: Local communities coming together to address social issues, such as environmental conservation or public health, exemplify the power of collective action.
  • Personal Relationships: In personal relationships, mutual support and understanding can help individuals navigate challenges and achieve shared aspirations.

Ultimately, the mantra serves as a reminder of the strength found in unity and the importance of courage in pursuing our goals, making it a timeless principle applicable in various aspects of life.
