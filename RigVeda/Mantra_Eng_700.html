अस्मा इद उ ग्नाश चिद देवपत्नीः इन्द्रायार्कम् अहिहत्य ऊवुः |परि dyāvāpṛthivī जभ्र उर्वी नास्य ते महिमानम् परि ष्टः ||
asmā id u gnāś cid devapatnīr indrāyārkam ahihatya ūvuḥ | pari dyāvāpṛthivī jabhra urvī nāsya te mahimānam pari ṣṭaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • asmā: from us
    • id: this
    • u: indeed
    • gnāś: knowing
    • cid: indeed
    • devapatnīr: divine consorts
    • indrāyārkam: to Indra and the Sun
    • ahihatya: without hindrance
    • ūvuḥ: they have spoken
    • pari: around
    • dyāvāpṛthivī: heaven and earth
    • jabhra: have embraced
    • urvī: the vastness
    • nāsya: of this
    • te: your
    • mahimānam: greatness
    • pari: around
    • ṣṭaḥ: established
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of a divine connection and the acknowledgment of the greatness of the universe.
    • It emphasizes the importance of unity between heaven (dyāvā) and earth (pṛthivī), suggesting that both realms are interconnected.
    • The reference to Indra and the Sun signifies the importance of these deities in providing strength and illumination in our lives.
    • The phrase "without hindrance" suggests that divine blessings and knowledge are accessible to those who seek them earnestly.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, this mantra can be applied to the pursuit of knowledge and wisdom. For instance, students who seek knowledge with dedication often find that their efforts are rewarded.
    • The concept of unity between heaven and earth can be seen in environmental conservation efforts, where people recognize the importance of protecting nature for future generations.
    • In relationships, the idea of embracing the vastness of life can encourage individuals to appreciate the diversity of experiences and perspectives that others bring.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of all things and the importance of seeking knowledge and understanding.
    • Its significance lies in the encouragement to embrace the divine and the vastness of life, fostering a sense of unity and purpose.
    • In real-life scenarios, it can inspire individuals to pursue their goals with determination, appreciate the world around them, and cultivate meaningful relationships.
