अध्वर्यवो यो दृष्भीकं जग्हान यो गा उदाजद अप हि वलं वः |तस्मा एतम अन्तरिक्षे न वातं इन्द्रं सोमैर ओर्णुत जूर न वस्त्रैः ||
adhvaryavo yo dṛbhīkaṁ jaghāna yo gā udājad apa hi valaṁ vaḥ | tasmā etam antarikṣe na vātam indraṁ somair orṇuta jūr na vastraiḥ ||
  • adhvaryavo: This term refers to the priests who perform the rituals and sacrifices in the Vedic tradition. They are responsible for the proper execution of the rites.
  • yo dṛbhīkaṁ jaghāna: This phrase can be interpreted as "who has struck down the obstacles." It signifies overcoming challenges and difficulties in life.
  • yo gā udājad: This means "who has brought forth the song." It symbolizes the expression of joy and celebration, often through music and art.
  • apa hi valaṁ vaḥ: This translates to "remove the obstacles from us." It is a plea for divine assistance in overcoming hindrances that may impede progress.
  • tasmā etam antarikṣe: This phrase means "therefore, in this space." It refers to the cosmic or spiritual realm where divine energies are believed to reside.
  • na vātam indraṁ: This can be interpreted as "may the wind not disturb Indra." Indra is the king of the gods, and this line emphasizes the need for stability and protection from chaos.
  • somair orṇuta: This means "may we be nourished by the soma." Soma is a sacred drink in Vedic rituals, symbolizing nourishment and vitality.
  • jūr na vastraiḥ: This translates to "may we be clothed in strength." It signifies the importance of being fortified and protected in our endeavors.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda emphasizes the importance of overcoming obstacles, seeking divine assistance, and fostering joy and strength in our lives. In real-life scenarios, it can be applied to various situations such as facing challenges in personal or professional life, seeking support during difficult times, and celebrating achievements. The invocation of divine energies and the emphasis on community and support highlight the interconnectedness of individuals in their journeys. By reciting this mantra, one can cultivate a sense of resilience and hope, encouraging a proactive approach to life's challenges.
