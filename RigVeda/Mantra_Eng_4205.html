ऋतस्य गोपाव अधि तिष्ठथो रथं सत्यधर्माणां परम व्योमनि |यम् अत्र मित्रावरुणावथो युवं तस्मै वृष्टिः मधुमत् पिन्वते दिवः ||
ṛtasya gopāv adhi tiṣṭhatho rathaṁ satyadharmāṇā parame vyomani | yam atra mitrāvaruṇāvatho yuvaṁ tasmai vṛṣṭir madhumat pinvate divaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ṛtasya: of truth or order
    • gopāv: the protectors or guardians
    • adhi: upon or above
    • tiṣṭhatho: standing or established
    • rathaṁ: the chariot
    • satyadharmāṇā: of those who follow truth and righteousness
    • parame: the highest or supreme
    • vyomani: in the sky or heavens
    • yam: whom
    • atra: here
    • mitrāvaruṇāv: Mitra and Varuna (deities representing friendship and cosmic order)
    • atho: and
    • yuvaṁ: youthful or vibrant
    • tasmai: to that
    • vṛṣṭir: rain or abundance
    • madhumat: sweet or pleasant
    • pinvate: may bestow or shower
    • divaḥ: of the heavens
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the guardians of truth and order, who are established in the highest realms.
    • It invokes the deities Mitra and Varuna, representing the principles of friendship and cosmic order, asking for their blessings in the form of abundance and prosperity.
    • The imagery of a chariot suggests movement and progress, indicating that those who adhere to truth and righteousness are on a path towards higher goals.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, leaders who uphold ethical practices and transparency can be seen as the "guardians" of their organizations, guiding their teams towards success.
    • In personal relationships, individuals who foster trust and honesty, akin to Mitra and Varuna, create a supportive environment that encourages growth and abundance.
    • In environmental efforts, communities that prioritize sustainable practices embody the principles of truth and righteousness, contributing to the well-being of the planet.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of truth and righteousness in achieving prosperity and harmony in life.
    • It serves as a reminder that by aligning ourselves with these principles, we can invoke blessings and abundance in our endeavors.
    • In various aspects of life—be it personal, professional, or communal—adhering to these values can lead to a more fulfilling and harmonious existence.
