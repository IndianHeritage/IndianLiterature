उभे सोमावचाकशन मृगो न तक्तो अर्षसि |सीदन्न ऋतस्य योनिम आ ||
ubhe somāvacākaśan mṛgo na takto arṣasi | sīdann ṛtasya yonim ā ||
  • Mantra: "ubhe somāvacākaśan mṛgo na takto arṣasi | sīdann ṛtasya yonim ā ||"
  • Translation: "Both the deer, having drunk the soma, do not fear; they sit in the womb of truth."
  • Word-by-Word Explanation:
    • ubhe: Both
    • somāvacākaśan: Having drunk the soma (a sacred drink)
    • mṛgo: Deer (symbolizing innocence and grace)
    • na: Not
    • takto: Fearful or afraid
    • arṣasi: You will be (or are)
    • sīdann: Sitting or dwelling
    • ṛtasya: Of truth or righteousness
    • yonim: Womb or source
    • ā: To or towards
  • Contextual Significance:
    • The mantra speaks to the idea of fearlessness and tranquility that comes from a state of truth and righteousness.
    • The deer, often seen as gentle and vulnerable creatures, symbolize purity and the ability to live harmoniously with nature.
    • The "soma" represents a divine elixir that grants wisdom and enlightenment, suggesting that through knowledge and truth, one can overcome fear.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a stressful work environment, individuals who maintain integrity and adhere to their values often find peace and confidence, much like the deer in the mantra.
    • In personal relationships, those who communicate openly and truthfully foster a sense of security and trust, allowing them to thrive without fear.
    • In times of crisis, communities that come together based on shared values and truth can support each other, creating a safe space for all members.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of living in alignment with truth and righteousness, which leads to a fearless and peaceful existence.
    • It serves as a reminder that by embracing knowledge and integrity, individuals can navigate life's challenges with grace and confidence.
    • In various aspects of life, from personal to professional, the principles highlighted in this mantra can guide individuals towards a more fulfilling and harmonious existence.
