अग्नि स क्षेषद ऋतपाऽ ऋतेजाऽ उरु ज्योतिः नशते देवयुष्टे |यं त्वं मित्रेण वरुणः सजोषा देव पाशि त्यजसा मर्तम अंहः ||
agne sa kṣeṣad ṛtapā ṛtejā uru jyotir naśate devayuṣ ṭe | yaṁ tvam mitreṇa varuṇaḥ sajoṣā deva pāsi tyajasā martam aṁhaḥ ||
  • Agne: This word refers to Agni, the fire god, symbolizing energy, light, and purity.
  • Sa kṣeṣad: This phrase can be interpreted as "may he protect" or "may he guard," indicating a plea for protection.
  • Ṛtapā: This term signifies "truth" or "righteousness," emphasizing the importance of living a life aligned with moral values.
  • Ṛtejā: This word means "radiance" or "brilliance," suggesting the illumination that comes from knowledge and virtue.
  • Uru jyotir: Translated as "vast light," it represents the expansive nature of divine knowledge and wisdom.
  • Naśate: This term means "to shine" or "to illuminate," reinforcing the idea of enlightenment.
  • Devayuṣ ṭe: This phrase can be understood as "may the divine life protect you," invoking the blessings of the divine.
  • Yaṁ: This word means "whom," referring to the divine or the higher power being addressed.
  • Tvam: This means "you," indicating a direct address to the divine.
  • Mitreṇa: This term refers to "with friendship," suggesting a relationship based on trust and camaraderie.
  • Varuṇaḥ: Varuna is the god of cosmic order and water, symbolizing the moral and ethical aspects of life.
  • Sajoṣā: This means "with harmony," indicating the importance of unity and cooperation.
  • Deva pāsi: This phrase translates to "may the gods protect," a plea for divine safeguarding.
  • Tyajasā: This term means "by abandoning," suggesting the need to let go of negativity or harmful influences.
  • Martam aṁhaḥ: This phrase can be interpreted as "the mortal body," referring to human existence and the physical form.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda emphasizes the importance of seeking divine protection and guidance in our lives. It highlights the values of truth, righteousness, and the pursuit of knowledge. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in moments of uncertainty or fear, where individuals seek strength and clarity. For instance, before embarking on a new journey or facing challenges, reciting this mantra can serve as a reminder to align oneself with higher principles and seek the support of the divine. Its significance lies in fostering a sense of connection with the universe and encouraging individuals to live harmoniously with others while striving for personal growth and enlightenment.
