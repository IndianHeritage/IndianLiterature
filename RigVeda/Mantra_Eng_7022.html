यथा कण्वे मगवन्त्रासदस्यविः यथा पक्ते दशव्रजः |यथा गोशार्ये असनोः ऋजिश्वनीन्द्र गोमद् धिरण्यवत् ||
yathā kaṇve maghavan trasadasyavi yathā pakthe daśavraje | yathā gośarye asanor ṛjiśvanīndra gomad dhiraṇyavat ||
  • yathā kaṇve maghavan trasadasyavi: This phrase can be interpreted as "like the powerful Maghavan (Indra), who is swift and strong." Here, "Maghavan" refers to Indra, the king of the gods in Vedic literature, known for his strength and valor. In real life, this can symbolize the qualities of leadership and courage that one should aspire to embody.
  • yathā pakthe daśavraje: This translates to "like the one who is established in the tenfold path." The "tenfold path" can be seen as a metaphor for a disciplined and righteous life. In modern terms, this could relate to the importance of following ethical principles and guidelines in our daily lives, such as the Ten Commandments in Christianity or the Eightfold Path in Buddhism.
  • yathā gośarye asanor ṛjiśvanīndra: This part means "like the one who is in the midst of the cows, the strong and righteous Indra." Cows are often seen as symbols of wealth and prosperity in Vedic culture. This can be related to the idea of nurturing and protecting one's resources and community, akin to how a leader should care for their people and environment.
  • gomad dhiraṇyavat: This translates to "like the one who is rich in resources." This emphasizes the importance of abundance and prosperity, not just in material wealth but also in wisdom and relationships. In real life, this can be reflected in how we cultivate our skills and connections to create a fulfilling life.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of strength, righteousness, and prosperity. It serves as a reminder to embody the qualities of a strong leader, to follow a disciplined path, to nurture our resources, and to strive for abundance in all aspects of life. In today's world, these principles can be applied in various scenarios, such as in leadership roles, community service, and personal development, guiding individuals to lead a life of purpose and integrity.
