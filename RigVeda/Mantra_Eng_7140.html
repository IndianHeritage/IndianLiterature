उज जातम इन्द्र ते शव उत त्वाम उत तव क्रतुम् |भूरिगो भूरी वावृद्धुर मगवन तव शर्मणि भद्रा इन्द्रस्य रातयः ||
uj jātam indra te śava ut tvām ut tava kratum | bhūrigo bhūri vāvṛdhur maghavan tava śarmaṇi bhadrā indrasya rātayaḥ ||
  • uj jātam indra te śava: This phrase can be translated as "O Indra, the one who is born from the energy of the universe." Here, Indra represents the divine force or energy that drives creation and sustains life. In real life, this can be likened to the concept of motivation or inspiration that propels individuals to achieve their goals.
  • ut tvām ut tava kratum: This means "we invoke you and your intentions." It signifies the importance of aligning one's actions with a higher purpose or intention. In practical terms, this can be seen in leadership, where a leader's vision and intentions guide the team towards success.
  • bhūrigo bhūri vāvṛdhur: This translates to "may you be enriched with abundance." It emphasizes the idea of prosperity and growth. In everyday life, this can relate to the pursuit of knowledge, wealth, or personal development, where individuals seek to enrich their lives and the lives of others.
  • maghavan tava śarmaṇi: This means "O Maghavan, may your protection be with us." Maghavan refers to the one who grants blessings and protection. In real life, this can be interpreted as seeking guidance and support from mentors, family, or community during challenging times.
  • bhadrā indrasya rātayaḥ: This translates to "may we have auspicious journeys under Indra's guidance." It signifies the importance of embarking on endeavors with a positive mindset and divine support. In practical terms, this can be seen in the way people approach new ventures, such as starting a business or a new relationship, with hope and optimism.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking divine guidance, aligning intentions with higher purposes, and striving for abundance and protection in our lives. Its significance lies in the reminder that we are not alone in our journeys; we can invoke higher energies and intentions to support our endeavors. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applicable in various contexts, such as personal development, leadership, and community building, where individuals seek to create a positive impact while being guided by a higher purpose.
