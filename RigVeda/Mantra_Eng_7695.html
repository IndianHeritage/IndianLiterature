त्वाम् अच् छा चरामसि तद् इद् अर्थं दिवे-दिवे |इन्दो त्वे न आशसः ||
tvām acchā carāmasi tad id arthaṁ dive-dive | indo tve na āśasaḥ ||
  • tvām - This word translates to "you" or "to you," indicating a direct address to a divine entity or a higher power.
  • acchā - This term means "we approach" or "we come near," suggesting a sense of reverence and intention in seeking connection.
  • carāmasi - This means "we move" or "we travel," indicating a journey or progression, both physically and spiritually.
  • tad - This translates to "that," referring to the purpose or goal of the journey.
  • id - This means "this," emphasizing the immediacy of the action or intention.
  • arthaṁ - This word means "meaning" or "purpose," highlighting the significance of the journey and the intention behind it.
  • dive-dive - This phrase translates to "day by day," indicating a continuous process or daily practice.
  • indo - This refers to "Indra," the king of gods in Vedic tradition, symbolizing strength, power, and protection.
  • tve - This means "to you," reinforcing the direct address to Indra.
  • na - This translates to "not," indicating a negation or absence.
  • āśasaḥ - This means "of desires" or "of wishes," suggesting a focus on the divine will rather than personal desires.

The mantra can be interpreted as a prayer or invocation where the speaker expresses their intention to approach the divine (Indra) with a purpose that is meaningful and significant, emphasizing a daily commitment to this journey. It reflects a desire to align one's actions with a higher purpose, seeking guidance and strength from the divine.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as:

  • Personal Growth: Individuals can use this mantra as a reminder to pursue their goals with intention and purpose, reflecting on their daily actions and aligning them with their values.
  • Spiritual Practice: It can serve as a daily affirmation in meditation or prayer, encouraging a connection with the divine and a focus on spiritual growth.
  • Community Engagement: In a communal setting, this mantra can inspire collective efforts towards a common goal, fostering unity and shared purpose.

In conclusion, this mantra signifies the importance of approaching life with intention and purpose, seeking guidance from a higher power, and committing to a continuous journey of growth and understanding. It encourages individuals to reflect on their desires and align them with a greater good, making it applicable in various aspects of life, from personal development to community involvement.
