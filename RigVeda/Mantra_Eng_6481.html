मरतः मारुतस्य न आ भेषजस्य वहता सुदानवः |यूयं सखायः सप्तयः ||
maruto mārutasya na ā bheṣajasya vahatā sudānavaḥ | yūyaṁ sakhāyaḥ saptayaḥ ||
  • Mantra: "maruto mārutasya na ā bheṣajasya vahatā sudānavaḥ | yūyaṁ sakhāyaḥ saptayaḥ ||"
  • Translation: "O Maruts, you who carry the healing powers, may you not be hindered by the obstacles. You are the seven friends (sakhāyaḥ) who bring forth prosperity."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • Maruto: Refers to the Maruts, the storm deities in Vedic literature, symbolizing strength and power.
    • Mārutasya: Of the Maruts, indicating their divine nature and connection to the forces of nature.
    • Bheṣajasya: Healing or medicinal powers, suggesting the importance of health and well-being.
    • Vahatā: To carry or to bear, indicating the action of the Maruts in delivering blessings.
    • Sudānavaḥ: Prosperous or beneficial, emphasizing the positive outcomes associated with the Maruts.
    • Yūyaṁ: You all, addressing the collective group of Maruts.
    • Sakhāyaḥ: Friends, highlighting the camaraderie and support among the Maruts.
    • Saptayaḥ: Seven, referring to the seven Maruts, which can symbolize completeness and wholeness.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • The Maruts can be seen as a metaphor for teamwork and collaboration in our daily lives. Just as the seven Maruts work together to bring prosperity, individuals in a team can achieve greater success by supporting each other.
    • In healthcare, the reference to healing powers can be related to the importance of community health initiatives that aim to provide support and resources for well-being, emphasizing that collective efforts can lead to better health outcomes.
    • In environmental contexts, the Maruts symbolize the forces of nature. Their invocation can remind us of the need to respect and protect our natural resources, as they are essential for our survival and prosperity.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of unity, healing, and the power of nature. It serves as a reminder that we are not alone in our struggles and that collective efforts can lead to prosperity and well-being.
    • In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as teamwork in professional settings, community health initiatives, and environmental conservation efforts. By recognizing the importance of collaboration and support, we can create a more harmonious and prosperous society.
