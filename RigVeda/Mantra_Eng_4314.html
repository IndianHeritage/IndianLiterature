एषा जनं दर्शता बोधयन्ती सुगान पथः कृत्वती याति अग्रे | बृहद्रथा बृहती विश्वमिन्वोषा ज्योतिः यच्छत्य अग्रे अह्नाम् ||
eṣā janaṁ darśatā bodhayantī sugān pathaḥ kṛṇvatī yāty agre | bṛhadrathā bṛhatī viśvaminvoṣā jyotir yacchaty agre ahnām ||
  • eṣā janaṁ darśatā bodhayantī: This phrase can be translated as "This (knowledge) shows the people and enlightens them." It emphasizes the role of knowledge and wisdom in guiding individuals and communities. For example, in modern society, education systems aim to enlighten students, helping them understand the world around them and make informed decisions.
  • sugān pathaḥ kṛṇvatī yāty agre: This means "It creates good paths and leads forward." This suggests that wisdom not only illuminates the way but also actively creates beneficial paths for progress. In real life, this can be seen in the work of social reformers who create new opportunities and pathways for marginalized communities, leading them towards a better future.
  • bṛhadrathā bṛhatī viśvaminvoṣā: This translates to "The great chariot, the vast one, is the light of the universe." Here, the 'great chariot' symbolizes a powerful force or vehicle of progress and enlightenment. In contemporary terms, this could refer to technological advancements that serve as vehicles for global connectivity and knowledge sharing, such as the internet.
  • jyotir yacchaty agre ahnām: This means "The light that shines forth in the day." It signifies the importance of light as a metaphor for knowledge and truth that dispels darkness (ignorance). In everyday life, this can be related to the pursuit of truth in journalism, where uncovering facts brings clarity to public discourse.

  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the transformative power of knowledge and wisdom in guiding individuals and societies towards enlightenment and progress. It highlights the importance of creating positive pathways and the role of light (knowledge) in dispelling ignorance. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various fields such as education, social reform, technology, and journalism, where the pursuit of knowledge leads to better decision-making and societal advancement.
