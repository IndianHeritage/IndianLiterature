अभि क्षिपः सम अग्मता मरजयन्तीर इषस पतिम् |पृष्ठा गृभ्णता वाजिनः ||
abhi kṣipaḥ sam agmata marjayantīr iṣas patim | pṛṣṭhā gṛbhṇata vājinaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • abhi: towards, in the direction of
    • kṣipaḥ: swift, quick
    • sam: together, in harmony
    • agmata: has come, arrived
    • marjayantīr: purifying, cleansing
    • iṣas: of the bow, or the power
    • patim: lord, master
    • pṛṣṭhā: from behind, at the back
    • gṛbhṇata: grasping, holding
    • vājinaḥ: of the steeds, or the powerful
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of a swift and harmonious arrival of a powerful force or energy.
    • The term "marjayantīr" suggests a cleansing or purifying aspect, indicating that this force brings about renewal or purification.
    • The phrase "pṛṣṭhā gṛbhṇata vājinaḥ" implies that this energy is grasped or held from behind, suggesting support and strength from unseen forces.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a team setting, when a group works together harmoniously towards a common goal, they can achieve swift results, akin to the "swift" arrival mentioned in the mantra.
    • In personal development, individuals often seek to cleanse their minds and spirits through practices like meditation or yoga, which aligns with the purifying aspect of the mantra.
    • Support systems, such as family or friends, can provide the unseen strength that helps individuals overcome challenges, reflecting the idea of grasping strength from behind.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of unity, purification, and the unseen support that aids in achieving goals.
    • Its significance lies in reminding us that when we come together with a clear purpose and seek to cleanse our intentions, we can harness powerful energies to propel us forward.
    • In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios, such as teamwork, personal growth, and the importance of community support in overcoming obstacles.
