मन्त्र: एते विश्वानि वार्याः पवमानास आषत || हिताः न सप्तयो रथे ||
ete viśvāni vāryā pavamānāsa āśata | hitā na saptayo rathe ||
  • Translation: "These (sacred) powers, which are all-pervading, are to be invoked. They are the seven (spheres of influence) that are beneficial, like the horses of a chariot."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • ete: These
    • viśvāni: All-pervading or universal
    • vāryā: Powers or energies
    • pavamānāsa: Invoked or called upon
    • āśata: To be established or made firm
    • hitā: Beneficial or good
    • na: Not
    • saptayo: Seven
    • rathe: Chariot
  • Contextual Meaning:
    • This mantra emphasizes the invocation of universal powers that are beneficial and essential for progress.
    • The reference to "seven" can symbolize completeness, as in the seven days of the week or the seven colors of the rainbow, indicating a holistic approach to life.
    • The metaphor of a chariot suggests that these powers drive us forward, much like how a chariot is propelled by horses.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In personal development, one might invoke the "powers" of determination, focus, and resilience to achieve their goals, akin to harnessing the energy of the seven beneficial influences.
    • In teamwork, the "chariot" can represent a project or goal, where each team member contributes their unique strengths (the horses) to move the project forward.
    • In spiritual practices, individuals may meditate on these universal energies to align themselves with a greater purpose, seeking harmony and balance in their lives.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of all energies and the importance of invoking them for personal and collective growth.
    • It highlights the significance of harnessing beneficial influences in our lives, whether in personal endeavors, relationships, or community efforts.
    • By recognizing and invoking these universal powers, we can navigate life's challenges more effectively, ensuring that we are propelled forward on our journey.
