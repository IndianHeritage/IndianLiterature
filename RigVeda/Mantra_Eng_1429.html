प्र यः पुरूणि गाहते तक्षद वनेव शोचिषा |स्थिरा चिदन्ना न रिणात्य ओजसा न स्थिराणि चिद ओजसा ||
pra yaḥ purūṇi gāhate takṣad vaneva śociṣā | sthirā cid annā ni riṇāty ojasā ni sthirāṇi cid ojasā ||
  • pra yaḥ purūṇi gāhate takṣad vaneva śociṣā
    • pra yaḥ: This can be interpreted as "those who" or "whoever." It indicates a subject or a group of people.
    • purūṇi: This word means "the ancient" or "the old." It refers to the wisdom or knowledge that has been passed down through generations.
    • gāhate: This translates to "sing" or "chant." It signifies the act of expressing or sharing knowledge through vocal means.
    • takṣad: This means "to create" or "to build." It implies the act of making something new from the old wisdom.
    • vaneva: This translates to "like a forest." It symbolizes a place of growth, diversity, and life.
    • śociṣā: This means "with brightness" or "with radiance." It indicates the illumination that comes from knowledge and wisdom.
  • sthirā cid annā ni riṇāty ojasā ni sthirāṇi cid ojasā
    • sthirā: This means "stable" or "firm." It refers to something that is enduring and reliable.
    • cid: This word can mean "indeed" or "certainly," emphasizing the truth of the statement.
    • annā: This translates to "food" or "nourishment." It symbolizes sustenance for the body and mind.
    • ni riṇāty: This means "does not diminish" or "does not decrease." It indicates abundance and continuity.
    • ojasā: This word means "energy" or "vitality." It represents the life force that sustains all beings.
    • ni sthirāṇi: This translates to "the stable ones." It refers to those who are grounded and secure.

Conclusion:

The mantra emphasizes the importance of ancient wisdom and the act of sharing it through expression, much like the growth and diversity found in a forest. It highlights the significance of stability and nourishment in life, suggesting that true vitality comes from a firm foundation of knowledge and sustenance. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios such as education, where sharing knowledge creates a nurturing environment for growth, or in personal development, where a stable foundation of values and beliefs leads to a fulfilling life. The mantra serves as a reminder that wisdom, when shared and built upon, can lead to a vibrant and enduring existence.
