बृहद इन्द्राय गायत मरुतो वृत्रहन्तमम् |येन ज्योतिर्जजनयन ऋतावृधो देवं देवाय जागृवि ||
bṛhad indrāya gāyata maruto vṛtrahaṁtamam | yena jyotir ajanayann ṛtāvṛdho devaṁ devāya jāgṛvi ||
  • Translation: The mantra can be translated as: "Sing to the great Indra, the slayer of Vritra, the Maruts (storm deities), who brought forth light, and who is the embodiment of truth, awakening the divine."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • bṛhad: Great or vast, indicating the immense power and significance of Indra.
    • indrāya: To Indra, the king of gods, associated with rain, thunder, and strength.
    • gāyata: Sing or chant, suggesting the importance of devotion and praise.
    • maruto: Referring to the Maruts, the storm deities, symbolizing the forces of nature.
    • vṛtrahaṁtamam: The slayer of Vritra, a demon representing chaos and drought, highlighting the victory of order over disorder.
    • yena: By whom, indicating the agency of Indra in creation.
    • jyotir: Light, symbolizing knowledge, enlightenment, and clarity.
    • ajanayann: Who brought forth or generated, emphasizing the creative aspect of the divine.
    • ṛtāvṛdho: Embodiment of truth, righteousness, and cosmic order.
    • devaṁ: The divine or god, referring to the higher spiritual reality.
    • devāya: To the divine, indicating the direction of worship and reverence.
    • jāgṛvi: Awakening or arousing, suggesting a call to consciousness and awareness.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In times of crisis, such as natural disasters, people often turn to prayer and rituals to invoke divine protection and guidance, similar to how the mantra calls upon Indra for strength and support.
    • The concept of light as knowledge can be seen in educational pursuits, where individuals seek enlightenment through learning, paralleling the invocation of light in the mantra.
    • In environmental movements, the Maruts can symbolize the forces of nature that need to be respected and preserved, echoing the mantra's reverence for natural elements.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its invocation of divine forces to overcome chaos and darkness in life. It emphasizes the importance of truth, knowledge, and the interconnectedness of nature and spirituality. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire individuals to seek strength in adversity, pursue knowledge, and respect the natural world, fostering a sense of harmony and balance in their lives.
