मन्त्र: विराट् मित्रावरुणयोः अभिश्रीः इन्द्रस्य त्रिष्टुप् इह भागो अहनः | विश्वान् देवाः जगत्याः आविवेश तेन चाक्ल्प्र ऋषयः मनुष्याः ||
virāṇ mitrāvaruṇayor abhiśrīr indrasya triṣṭub iha bhāgo ahnaḥ | viśvān devāñ jagaty ā viveśa tena cākḷpra ṛṣayo manuṣyāḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • virāṇ: of the heroes
    • mitrāvaruṇayor: of Mitra and Varuna
    • abhiśrīr: the auspiciousness
    • indrasya: of Indra
    • triṣṭub: in the meter of Trishtubh
    • iha: here
    • bhāgo: the share
    • ahnaḥ: of the day
    • viśvān: all
    • devāñ: the gods
    • jagaty: in the world
    • ā: into
    • viveśa: entered
    • tena: by that
    • cākḷpra: the seers
    • ṛṣayo: the sages
    • manuṣyāḥ: the humans
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the divine qualities of heroes and the auspiciousness bestowed by the deities Mitra and Varuna.
    • Mitra and Varuna are often associated with friendship, harmony, and cosmic order, emphasizing the importance of unity and cooperation in society.
    • Indra, the king of gods, represents strength and power, suggesting that these qualities are essential for achieving success in life.
    • The mantra highlights the interconnectedness of all beings, as it mentions that all gods have entered the world, indicating a shared existence and purpose.
    • The reference to sages and humans signifies the role of wisdom and knowledge in navigating life’s challenges.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community project, the collaboration of individuals (heroes) can lead to successful outcomes, reflecting the essence of Mitra and Varuna.
    • In personal relationships, fostering harmony and understanding can strengthen bonds, akin to the auspiciousness mentioned in the mantra.
    • In leadership, embodying the qualities of Indra—strength, decisiveness, and protection—can inspire and guide others effectively.
    • In education, the pursuit of knowledge by sages can be mirrored in the continuous learning journey of individuals, emphasizing the importance of wisdom.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of unity, strength, and wisdom in our lives.
    • It encourages individuals to embody the qualities of cooperation and auspiciousness in their interactions with others.
    • In a broader sense, it highlights the significance of divine guidance and the interconnectedness of all beings in the pursuit of a harmonious existence.
    • By applying these principles, individuals can contribute to a more balanced and prosperous society.
