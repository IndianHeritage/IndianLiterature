आदित्यैर इन्द्रः सगणो मरुद्भिर अस्माकं भूत्व अवितā तनूनाम् ||
ādityair indraḥ sagaṇo marudbhir asmākam bhūtv avitā tanūnām ||
  • Mantra: "ādityair indraḥ sagaṇo marudbhir asmākam bhūtv avitā tanūnām"
  • Translation: "May Indra, along with the Adityas and the Maruts, protect our bodies."

  • Word Breakdown:
    • Ādityaiḥ: Refers to the Adityas, a group of solar deities representing various aspects of the sun.
    • Indraḥ: The king of the gods, associated with rain, thunderstorms, and strength.
    • Sagaṇo: Together with or accompanied by.
    • Marudbhir: Refers to the Maruts, storm deities associated with wind and storms.
    • Asmākam: Our or belonging to us.
    • Bhūtv: Being or existing.
    • Avitā: Protect or safeguard.
    • Tanūnām: Of our bodies or physical forms.

  • Contextual Explanation:
    • This mantra invokes the protection of powerful deities, specifically Indra, the Adityas, and the Maruts, emphasizing the importance of divine safeguarding in our lives.
    • In real life, this can be related to seeking protection and strength in challenging situations, such as during natural disasters or personal crises.
    • For example, during a storm, people often pray for safety and protection, reflecting the essence of this mantra where the Maruts, as storm deities, are called upon for their power.
    • Similarly, in times of uncertainty or fear, individuals may look to higher powers for guidance and support, akin to invoking Indra's strength.

  • Significance:
    • This mantra highlights the interconnectedness of divine forces and human existence, reminding us that we are not alone in our struggles.
    • It serves as a reminder to seek help from higher powers, whether through prayer, meditation, or community support.
    • In a broader sense, it encourages individuals to recognize the importance of collective strength and unity in facing life's challenges.

  • Real-Life Application:
    • In daily life, this mantra can be recited during moments of stress or fear, providing a sense of comfort and reassurance.
    • It can also be used in group settings, such as family gatherings or community events, to invoke a sense of unity and collective strength.
    • Moreover, it can inspire individuals to take proactive steps in safeguarding their well-being, both physically and mentally, by fostering a supportive environment.
