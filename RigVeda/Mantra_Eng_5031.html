अधि श्रीये दुहिताऽ सूर्यस्य रथं तस्थौ पुरुभुजा शतोतिम् |प्र मायाभिर मायिना भूतमात्र नराऽ नृतू जनिमान यज्ञियानाम् ||
adhi śriye duhitā sūryasya rathaṁ tasthau purubhujā śatotim | pra māyābhir māyinā bhūtam atra narā nṛtū janiman yajñiyānām ||
  • adhi śriye: This phrase can be translated as "towards the glory" or "in the direction of prosperity." It signifies the pursuit of excellence and success.
  • duhitā sūryasya: This means "the daughter of the Sun." In many cultures, the Sun is a symbol of life, energy, and enlightenment. The daughter of the Sun can represent a person or entity that embodies these qualities.
  • rathaṁ tasthau: This translates to "stood on the chariot." The chariot is often a metaphor for the journey of life, with its ups and downs, and standing on it signifies readiness to face challenges.
  • purubhujā: This means "with many enjoyments" or "with abundant pleasures." It suggests a life filled with joy and fulfillment.
  • śatotim: This translates to "a hundredfold." It emphasizes the idea of abundance and prosperity in various aspects of life.
  • pra māyābhir: This means "with the help of illusions" or "through the power of creativity." It highlights the role of imagination and creativity in achieving goals.
  • māyinā bhūtam: This translates to "the one who creates." It refers to the creative force that brings things into existence, emphasizing the importance of action and initiative.
  • atra narā: This means "here, among men." It signifies the human experience and the collective journey we share.
  • nṛtū janiman: This translates to "born of the dance." Dance is often a metaphor for life’s rhythm and the joy of existence, suggesting that life is a celebration.
  • yajñiyānām: This means "of those who perform sacrifices." It refers to the idea of selflessness and the importance of contributing to the greater good.

Conclusion: This mantra encapsulates the essence of striving for prosperity and joy in life while recognizing the importance of creativity, community, and selflessness. It teaches us that life is a journey filled with challenges and opportunities, and by embracing our creative potential and working together, we can achieve abundance and fulfillment. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire individuals to pursue their goals with determination, foster collaboration in communities, and engage in acts of kindness and generosity, ultimately leading to a more harmonious and prosperous society.
