असृग्न देववीतये वाजयन्तो रथा इव ||
asṛgran devavītaye vājayanto rathā iva ||
  • Mantra: "asṛgran devavītaye vājayanto rathā iva"
  • Translation: This mantra can be translated as "They who are endowed with strength, like chariots, are invoking the divine for prosperity."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • asṛgran: This term refers to those who are endowed with strength or power.
    • devavītaye: This signifies the invocation of the divine or the gods, seeking their blessings.
    • vājayanto: This word means to invoke or call upon, often associated with the idea of bringing forth abundance or prosperity.
    • rathā iva: This phrase translates to "like chariots," symbolizing strength, speed, and the ability to carry forth one's intentions.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, a company may invoke this mantra by seeking divine guidance and strength to achieve prosperity and success in their ventures.
    • In personal life, individuals may recite this mantra during times of challenge, asking for strength and support from a higher power to overcome obstacles.
    • During community gatherings or festivals, people may come together to chant this mantra, symbolizing unity and collective strength in seeking blessings for the community's prosperity.
  • Significance:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of strength and divine support in achieving success and prosperity.
    • It serves as a reminder that invoking the divine can provide the necessary strength to overcome challenges, much like a chariot that carries its passengers forward.
    • In a broader sense, it highlights the interconnectedness of human effort and divine grace in the pursuit of goals.
  • Conclusion:
    • The mantra "asṛgran devavītaye vājayanto rathā iva" is a powerful invocation for strength and prosperity.
    • Its application in real life can be seen in various contexts, from personal challenges to collective community efforts.
    • By recognizing the role of divine support in our endeavors, we can cultivate a mindset that embraces both effort and faith, leading to a more fulfilling and prosperous life.
