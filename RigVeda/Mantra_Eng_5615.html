कृत्ये चिदत्र मरुतो रणन्तानवद्यासः शुचयः पावकाः | प्र णोऽवात सुमतिभिर्यजत्राः प्र वाजेभिः तिरत पूष्यसे नः ||
kṛte cid atra maruto raṇantānavadyāsaḥ śucayaḥ pāvakāḥ | pra ṇo 'vata sumatibhir yajatrāḥ pra vājebhis tirata puṣyase naḥ ||
  • kṛte cid atra maruto: This phrase refers to the Maruts, the storm deities in Vedic literature, who are known for their strength and vitality. The term "kṛte" implies action or purpose, suggesting that the Maruts are invoked for a specific reason or task.
  • raṇantānavadyāsaḥ: This part describes the Maruts as "raṇantā," meaning they are engaged in battle or conflict. "Anavadyāsaḥ" indicates that they are pure or faultless. This can be interpreted as a call for the divine forces to assist in overcoming challenges in life.
  • śucayaḥ pāvakāḥ: "Śucayaḥ" means pure or sacred, while "pāvakāḥ" refers to the purifying aspect of fire. Together, they symbolize the cleansing and transformative power of the divine, which can help individuals overcome impurities in their lives.
  • pra ṇo 'vata sumatibhir: This phrase is a plea for guidance and wisdom. "Pra ṇo" refers to life force or energy, and "sumatibhir" means with good thoughts or intentions. It emphasizes the importance of seeking divine wisdom to navigate life's complexities.
  • yajatrāḥ pra vājebhis: "Yajatrāḥ" refers to those who perform sacrifices or rituals, while "pra vājebhis" suggests the invocation of strength and abundance. This highlights the significance of rituals in connecting with the divine and attracting positive energies.
  • tirata puṣyase naḥ: This concluding phrase translates to "may you nourish us." It is a request for sustenance and support from the divine forces, emphasizing the need for spiritual and material nourishment in life.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda encapsulates the essence of invoking divine forces for strength, purity, and guidance in overcoming life's challenges. In real-life scenarios, it can be applied during times of struggle or uncertainty, reminding individuals to seek wisdom and support from higher powers. The significance lies in the understanding that through rituals and a pure heart, one can attract positive energies and navigate life's battles with clarity and purpose.
