ऋतस्य हि शुरुधः सन्ति पूर्वीर ऋतस्य धीतिर वृजिनानि हन्ति |ऋतस्य श्लोको बधिरा तातर्द कर्णा बुधानः शुचमान आयोः ||
ṛtasya hi śurudhaḥ santi pūrvīr ṛtasya dhītir vṛjināni hanti | ṛtasya śloko badhirā tatarda karṇā budhānaḥ śucamāna āyoḥ ||
  • Meaning of the Mantra:
    • ṛtasya hi śurudhaḥ santi: This phrase suggests that those who are wise and virtuous are indeed the ones who uphold the cosmic order (ṛta). In real life, this can be seen in individuals who act with integrity and fairness, contributing positively to society.
    • pūrvīr ṛtasya dhītir vṛjināni hanti: The wisdom of the ancients (pūrvīr) in understanding and following the principles of righteousness (ṛta) helps to eliminate obstacles and negativity (vṛjināni). For example, leaders who follow ethical practices can inspire their communities to overcome challenges.
    • ṛtasya śloko badhirā tatarda: The verses (śloko) of righteousness (ṛta) are often ignored (badhirā) by those who are not attentive. This can be likened to people who disregard moral teachings, leading to chaos in their lives.
    • karṇā budhānaḥ śucamāna āyoḥ: Those who are wise (budhānaḥ) and pure (śucamāna) listen (karṇā) to the teachings of righteousness and act upon them. In everyday life, this can be seen in individuals who seek knowledge and apply it to make ethical decisions.
  • Significance of the Mantra:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of wisdom, righteousness, and the adherence to moral principles in life.
    • It highlights that true understanding and application of these principles can lead to the removal of negativity and obstacles.
    • In a broader context, it serves as a reminder for individuals and societies to listen to and act upon the teachings of righteousness to foster harmony and progress.
  • Real-Life Application:
    • In personal life, individuals can apply this mantra by striving to be ethical in their decisions, promoting honesty and integrity in their relationships.
    • In professional settings, leaders can embody these principles by creating a culture of transparency and accountability, which can lead to a more productive and positive work environment.
    • On a societal level, communities can come together to uphold moral values, ensuring that the collective wisdom of the past guides their actions towards a better future.
