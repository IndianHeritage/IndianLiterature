तस्मा अर्षन्ति दिव्या असश्चतः स सत्वभिः प्रथमो गोषु गच्छति |अनिभृत्स्ततविषिर हन्त्य ओजसा यं-यं युजं कृत्नुते ब्रह्मणस पतिः ||
tasmā arṣanti divyā asaścataḥ sa satvabhiḥ prathamo goṣu gacchati | anibhṛṣṭataviṣir hanty ojasā yaṁ-yaṁ yujaṁ kṛṇute brahmaṇas patiḥ ||
  • tasmā arṣanti divyā: This phrase can be translated as "therefore, the divine beings are pleased." It signifies the importance of divine approval and blessings in our endeavors. In real life, this can be seen in various cultures where people seek blessings from higher powers before starting significant tasks, such as a wedding or a new business.
  • asaścataḥ sa satvabhiḥ: This means "with the essence of truth and vitality." It emphasizes the value of truthfulness and integrity in our actions. For example, in a workplace, employees who uphold honesty and transparency are often more trusted and respected, leading to a healthier work environment.
  • prathamo goṣu gacchati: Translated, this means "the first among the cows goes." Cows are often seen as symbols of wealth and nourishment in many cultures, particularly in India. This line can be interpreted as the idea that those who are virtuous and righteous will be rewarded first, akin to how the best resources are allocated to those who deserve them.
  • anibhṛṣṭataviṣir hanty ojasā: This phrase translates to "the one who is not burdened by obstacles destroys with strength." It highlights the power of resilience and determination. In real life, individuals who face challenges head-on, such as entrepreneurs overcoming financial difficulties, exemplify this strength.
  • yaṁ-yaṁ yujaṁ kṛṇute brahmaṇas patiḥ: This means "whatever the Lord of creation connects." It suggests that the divine orchestrates connections and relationships in our lives. For instance, networking in professional settings often leads to opportunities that seem serendipitous, reflecting this divine connection.

  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of divine blessings, truthfulness, resilience, and the interconnectedness of life. In real-life scenarios, it serves as a reminder to seek higher guidance, maintain integrity, face challenges with strength, and recognize the divine connections that shape our paths. By embodying these principles, individuals can lead more fulfilling and purposeful lives.
