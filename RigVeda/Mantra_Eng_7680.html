प्र यं राये निनिषसि मार्तो यस ते वसो दाशत् |स वीरं धत्ते अग्न उक्तशंसिनं तमनā सहस्रपोषिणम् ||
pra yaṁ rāye ninīṣasi marto yas te vaso dāśat | sa vīraṁ dhatte agna ukthaśaṁsinaṁ tmanā sahasrapoṣiṇam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • pra: forward, towards
    • yaṁ: who
    • rāye: wealth, prosperity
    • ninīṣasi: you are seeking
    • marto: mortal, human
    • yas: who
    • te: your
    • vaso: dwelling, abode
    • dāśat: to give, to bestow
    • sa: he
    • vīraṁ: hero, brave one
    • dhatte: holds, carries
    • agna: O Agni, the fire god
    • ukthaśaṁsinaṁ: who is praised in hymns
    • tmanā: with the mind
    • sahasrapoṣiṇam: who nourishes a thousand
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra addresses Agni, the fire god, and speaks to the human desire for wealth and prosperity.
    • The phrase "pra yaṁ rāye ninīṣasi" indicates the pursuit of wealth and abundance, which is a common aspiration in human life.
    • It acknowledges the mortal condition ("marto") and the need for divine assistance in achieving one's goals.
    • The mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking blessings from Agni, who is depicted as a hero ("vīraṁ") that supports and nourishes those who honor him.
    • In real life, this can be seen in the way people seek guidance and blessings from higher powers or deities when striving for success in their endeavors.
  • Significance and Real-Life Application:
    • This mantra highlights the relationship between humans and the divine, illustrating how individuals seek support in their pursuits.
    • It serves as a reminder that while humans work hard for their goals, invoking divine blessings can enhance their efforts.
    • In practical terms, this can be applied in various situations such as starting a new business, pursuing education, or embarking on any significant life journey where success is desired.
    • By reciting this mantra, individuals can cultivate a mindset of gratitude and openness to receiving support from the universe or higher powers.
