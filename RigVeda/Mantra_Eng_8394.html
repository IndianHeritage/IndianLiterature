मन्त्र: इन्द्रस्य कुक्षा पवते मदिन्तम ऊर्जं वसानः श्रवसे सुमङ्गलः |प्रत्यक्षं स विś्वा भुवनाभि पप्रथे क्रील्हन हरिर अत्यः स्यन्दते वृषा ||
endrasya kukṣā pavate madintama ūrjaṁ vasānaḥ śravase sumaṅgalaḥ | pratyaṅ sa viśvā bhuvanābhi paprathe krīḻan harir atyaḥ syandate vṛṣā ||
  • Endrasya kukṣā: This phrase refers to the "womb of Indra," symbolizing the source of strength and power. Indra is often seen as the king of the gods and a deity of rain and thunderstorms. In real life, this can be likened to the nurturing aspect of nature that provides sustenance and strength to all living beings.
  • Pavate madintama ūrjaṁ: This translates to "the most powerful energy that flows." It signifies the vital energy or life force that permeates the universe. In practical terms, this can be compared to the energy we derive from food, sunlight, and our environment, which fuels our daily activities and growth.
  • Vasānaḥ śravase sumaṅgalaḥ: This means "dwelling in auspiciousness and fame." It emphasizes the importance of living a life that is not only successful but also brings good fortune and positive recognition. In everyday life, this can be seen in individuals who strive for excellence in their careers while also contributing positively to their communities.
  • Pratyaṅ sa viśvā bhuvanābhi paprathe: This phrase suggests "he who pervades all worlds." It indicates a universal presence and influence. In a real-world context, this can be related to the interconnectedness of all beings and the impact of our actions on the broader community and environment.
  • Krīḻan harir atyaḥ syandate vṛṣā: This translates to "the swift-moving force that brings forth abundance." It highlights the dynamic nature of life and the continuous flow of resources and opportunities. In practical terms, this can be observed in the cycles of nature, such as the changing seasons, which bring forth new life and abundance.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of strength, energy, auspiciousness, universality, and abundance. Its significance lies in the reminder of our connection to the divine and the natural world. In real-life scenarios, it encourages individuals to harness their inner strength, seek positive energy, and contribute to the well-being of others. By embodying these principles, one can lead a fulfilling life that resonates with the greater good, fostering harmony and prosperity in both personal and communal spheres.
