येषाम् अज्मेषु पृथिवी जुजुर्वाम् इव विश्वपतिः |भीया यामेषु रेजते ||
yeṣām ajmeṣu pṛthivī jujurvām̐ iva viśpatiḥ | bhiyā yāmeṣu rejate ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yeṣām: of whom
    • ajmeṣu: in the horses (or steeds)
    • pṛthivī: the earth
    • jujurvām̐: as if (or like) the lord
    • iva: like
    • viśpatiḥ: the lord of all
    • bhiyā: with fear
    • yāmeṣu: in the nights
    • rejate: shines or glows
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the relationship between the earth and the divine, represented by the lord (viśpatiḥ).
    • The phrase "yeṣām ajmeṣu pṛthivī" suggests a connection between the earthly realm and the divine, indicating that those who possess strength (like horses) are under the protection of a higher power.
    • The comparison "jujurvām̐ iva viśpatiḥ" implies that the divine presence is akin to a lord who oversees and governs the world.
    • The latter part, "bhiyā yāmeṣu rejate," indicates that even in the face of fear or darkness (symbolized by night), there is a light or hope that shines, suggesting resilience and divine guidance.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In competitive sports, athletes often draw strength from their training and the support of their coaches, akin to the "horses" in the mantra. They may feel a divine presence or motivation that drives them to excel, especially in challenging situations.
    • In times of crisis, such as natural disasters, communities often come together, finding strength in unity and support, reflecting the idea of divine protection and guidance amidst fear and uncertainty.
    • In personal struggles, individuals may find solace in their faith or belief in a higher power, which helps them navigate through dark times, much like the light that "shines" in the night.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of strength, divine guidance, and resilience in the face of adversity.
    • It serves as a reminder that even in challenging times, there is a higher power that watches over and supports us, encouraging us to shine brightly despite fears and uncertainties.
    • In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios, from personal challenges to collective struggles, reinforcing the belief that we are never alone in our journeys.
