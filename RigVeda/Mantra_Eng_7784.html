समीचीनास आसते होतारः सप्तजामयः |पदं एकस्य पिप्रतः ||
samīcīnāsa āsate hotāraḥ saptajāmayaḥ | padam ekasya piprataḥ ||
  • Mantra: "samīcīnāsa āsate hotāraḥ saptajāmayaḥ | padam ekasya piprataḥ ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • samīcīnāsa: Those who are united or in harmony.
    • āsate: They sit or reside.
    • hotāraḥ: The priests or those who invoke.
    • saptajāmayaḥ: The seven kinds or forms of existence.
    • padam: The goal or the state.
    • ekasya: Of the one or singular.
    • piprataḥ: They are nourishing or providing.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the importance of unity and harmony among individuals who are engaged in spiritual practices or rituals.
    • The term "hotāraḥ" refers to those who perform rituals, indicating that the collective effort of these individuals is essential for achieving a higher state of existence.
    • The phrase "saptajāmayaḥ" suggests that there are multiple dimensions or forms of life that are interconnected, emphasizing the diversity of existence.
    • The mantra concludes with the idea that through unity and collective effort, one can attain a singular goal or state of nourishment, both spiritually and materially.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community setting, when people come together for a common cause, such as environmental conservation, their collective efforts can lead to significant positive changes, reflecting the essence of "samīcīnāsa."
    • In a workplace, teamwork and collaboration among employees can lead to innovative solutions and a harmonious work environment, embodying the spirit of "hotāraḥ."
    • In family life, nurturing relationships and supporting each other can create a strong foundation for personal growth and happiness, resonating with "padam ekasya piprataḥ."
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of unity, collaboration, and the interconnectedness of all forms of existence.
    • It serves as a reminder that through collective efforts and harmony, individuals can achieve greater goals and nourish each other spiritually and materially.
    • In real-life scenarios, whether in communities, workplaces, or families, the principles outlined in this mantra can guide individuals towards creating a more harmonious and fulfilling existence.
