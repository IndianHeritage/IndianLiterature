न ते सखा सख्यं वष्ट्य एतत् सलक्ष्मा यद विषुरूपा भवाति |महस पुत्रासो असुरस्य वीराः दिवो धार्तार उर्वियाः परि ख्यन् ||
na te sakhā sakhyaṁ vaṣṭy etat salakṣmā yad viṣurūpā bhavāti | mahas putrāso asurasya vīrā divo dhartāra urviyā pari khyan ||
  • na te sakhā sakhyaṁ vaṣṭy etat salakṣmā: This phrase can be translated to "You are not a friend, nor is friendship established here, O Salakṣmā." Here, "sakhā" refers to a friend, and "sakhyaṁ" refers to friendship. The term "Salakṣmā" can be interpreted as a representation of prosperity or abundance.
  • yad viṣurūpā bhavāti: This translates to "that which becomes multifaceted or diverse." The term "viṣurūpā" suggests the idea of something that takes on various forms or appearances, indicating the complexity of existence and the many roles one can play in life.
  • mahas putrāso asurasya vīrā: This means "the great sons of the Asura, the heroes." "Mahas" refers to greatness, "putrāso" means sons, and "asurasya" refers to the Asura, often associated with powerful beings or deities. This line emphasizes the strength and valor of those who are born from such powerful entities.
  • divo dhartāra urviyā pari khyan: This translates to "the bearers of the heavens, who uphold the vastness." "Divo" refers to the heavens, "dhartāra" means bearers or upholders, and "urviyā" signifies vastness or expansiveness. This line highlights the responsibility of those who maintain the order of the universe.

Conclusion: The mantra reflects on the nature of relationships, the complexity of existence, and the responsibilities of those who hold power. In real life, it can be applied to various scenarios, such as leadership, where one must navigate friendships and alliances while also embracing the multifaceted nature of their role. It serves as a reminder that true strength lies not just in power but in the ability to maintain harmony and uphold the greater good. This mantra encourages individuals to reflect on their relationships and responsibilities, fostering a deeper understanding of their place in the world.
