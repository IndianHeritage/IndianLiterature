नानाहि त्वा हवमानाऽजनाऽइमे धनानां धर्तर अवसाऽविपण्यवः |अस्माकं स्मारथम् आतिष्ठ सातये जैत्रं हीन्द्र निभृतम् मनस तव ||
nānā hi tvā havamānā janā ime dhanānāṁ dhartar avasā vipanyavaḥ | asmākaṁ smā ratham ā tiṣṭha sātaye jaitraṁ hīndra nibhṛtam manas tava ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • nānā: various, many
    • hi: indeed, certainly
    • tvā: you
    • havamānā: being invoked, called upon
    • janā: people, beings
    • ime: these
    • dhanānāṁ: of wealth, riches
    • dhartar: the one who holds, sustains
    • avasā: in the midst, surrounded
    • vipanyavaḥ: those who are in distress, troubled
    • asmākaṁ: our
    • smā: here, in this place
    • ratham: chariot, vehicle
    • ā: to, towards
    • tiṣṭha: stand, remain
    • sātaye: for victory, success
    • jaitraṁ: triumph, victory
    • hīndra: O Indra, the deity
    • nibhṛtam: firmly, securely
    • manas: mind, thought
    • tava: your
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a call to the divine, specifically to Indra, the king of gods in Vedic tradition.
    • The speaker acknowledges the various people who are invoking the divine for assistance, particularly in times of need.
    • It emphasizes the importance of wealth and resources, suggesting that the divine sustains those who are in distress.
    • The mantra requests the divine to ensure that their chariot (symbolizing progress and success) stands firm for victory.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In times of economic hardship, communities often come together to invoke blessings for prosperity and support, similar to the invocation in this mantra.
    • During significant life events, such as weddings or new ventures, people seek divine guidance and support, reflecting the essence of this mantra.
    • In sports, teams often invoke a sense of unity and strength, calling upon their collective spirit to achieve victory, akin to the request for the chariot to stand firm for success.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the collective human experience of seeking divine support in times of need.
    • It highlights the importance of community, wealth, and the pursuit of success, reminding us that we are not alone in our struggles.
    • In real life, it encourages individuals and communities to come together, invoke higher powers, and strive for victory in their endeavors, reinforcing the belief in divine assistance and the strength of unity.
