इन्द्रासोमा दुष्कृतो वव्रे अन्तर अनारम्भणे तमसि प्र विद्यातम् |यथा नातः पुनर एकश्चनुदयत तद्वाम अस्तु सहसे मन्युमच्छवः ||
indrāsomā duṣkṛto vavre antar anārambhaṇe tamasi pra vidhyatam | yathā nātaḥ punar ekaś canodayat tad vām astu sahase manyumac chavaḥ ||
  • Indrāsomā: This term refers to the divine forces of Indra and Soma. Indra is the king of the gods and represents strength, power, and the ability to overcome obstacles. Soma, on the other hand, symbolizes the elixir of life, vitality, and the essence of joy.
  • Duṣkṛto: This word means "the one who has committed wrongs" or "the one who has done bad deeds." It signifies the challenges and negative actions that can hinder progress.
  • Vavre: This term can be interpreted as "to choose" or "to select." It indicates the act of making a decision or taking a stand against negativity.
  • Antar anārambhaṇe: This phrase translates to "in the midst of uninitiated actions." It suggests a state of confusion or lack of direction, where one may feel lost or unsure about their path.
  • Tamasi pra vidhyatam: This means "let there be illumination in darkness." It emphasizes the need for clarity and understanding in challenging situations.
  • Yathā nātaḥ punar ekaś canodayat: This can be interpreted as "just as the sun rises again after the night." It symbolizes hope, renewal, and the cyclical nature of life.
  • Tad vām astu sahase manyumac chavaḥ: This phrase translates to "may that strength and courage be with you." It is a blessing for empowerment and resilience in the face of adversity.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda encapsulates the journey from darkness to light, emphasizing the importance of strength, clarity, and resilience. In real life, it can be applied in various scenarios such as overcoming personal challenges, facing adversities in professional life, or navigating through periods of confusion. The invocation of divine forces like Indra and Soma serves as a reminder that with the right mindset and support, one can rise above difficulties and emerge stronger. This mantra encourages individuals to seek clarity in their actions and to embrace the cyclical nature of life, where every setback can lead to a new beginning.
