युजे वाम् ब्रह्म पूर्व्यं नमोभिर् वि श्लोक एतु पथ्येव सूरः |श्रिण्वन्तु विश्वे अमृतस्य पुत्रा आ ये धामानि दिव्यानी तस्थुः ||
yuje vām brahma pūrvyaṁ namobhir vi śloka etu pathyeva sūreḥ | śṛṇvantu viśve amṛtasya putrā ā ye dhāmāni divyāni tasthuḥ ||
  • yuje vām brahma pūrvyaṁ: This phrase can be translated as "I unite you with the ancient Brahman." Here, "yuje" means to unite or connect, "vām" refers to 'you', and "brahma" signifies the ultimate reality or cosmic spirit. The term "pūrvyaṁ" indicates something that is ancient or primordial.
  • namobhir vi śloka etu pathyeva sūreḥ: This part translates to "With salutations, may the verses lead you on the right path." "Namobhir" refers to salutations or reverence, "vi śloka" means 'with verses', and "pathyeva sūreḥ" implies 'to the righteous path of the divine'. This emphasizes the importance of respect and devotion in seeking guidance.
  • śṛṇvantu viśve amṛtasya putrā: This translates to "Let all beings listen, O children of immortality." "śṛṇvantu" means 'let them listen', "viśve" refers to 'all beings', and "amṛtasya putrā" signifies 'children of immortality', indicating a connection to the eternal or divine essence within all living beings.
  • ā ye dhāmāni divyāni tasthuḥ: This can be interpreted as "Those who dwell in the divine realms, may they thrive." "ā ye" means 'those who', "dhāmāni" refers to 'realms' or 'abodes', and "divyāni" signifies 'divine'. "tasthuḥ" implies 'may they thrive' or 'may they prosper'. This highlights the aspiration for well-being and prosperity in the divine realms.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda emphasizes the importance of unity with the ultimate reality, respect for divine guidance, and the interconnectedness of all beings as children of immortality. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios such as:

  • In personal development, individuals can seek to connect with their higher selves and strive for spiritual growth.
  • In community settings, fostering respect and cooperation among diverse groups can lead to a harmonious society.
  • In environmental conservation, recognizing the interconnectedness of all life can inspire actions that protect our planet.

Overall, the mantra serves as a reminder of our shared existence and the pursuit of a righteous path, encouraging us to listen, learn, and thrive together in harmony with the divine.
