विप्रथतां देवजुष्टं तिरश्चा दीर्घं द्राघ्मा सुरभि भूत्व अस्मे |अहेळता मनसा देव बर्हिर इन्द्रज्येष्ठां उषतो यक्षि देवान् ||
vi prathatāṁ devajuṣṭaṁ tiraścā dīrghaṁ drāghmā surabhi bhūtv asme | aheḻatā manasā deva barhir indrajyeṣṭhām̐ uśato yakṣi devān ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • vi prathatāṁ: "to spread out" or "to expand."
    • devajuṣṭaṁ: "blessed by the gods."
    • tiraścā: "and across" or "in various directions."
    • dīrghaṁ: "long" or "extended."
    • drāghmā: "of great strength" or "powerful."
    • surabhi: "fragrant" or "pleasing."
    • bhūtv: "becoming" or "manifesting."
    • asme: "for us."
    • aheḻatā: "with great effort" or "with determination."
    • manasā: "with the mind."
    • deva: "god" or "divine."
    • barhir: "the covering" or "the outer layer."
    • indrajyeṣṭhām̐: "the highest power" or "the supreme strength."
    • uśato: "of the auspicious" or "of the good."
    • yakṣi: "to bestow" or "to grant."
    • devān: "to the gods."
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the divine blessings that spread out in various directions, symbolizing the omnipresence of divine energy.
    • The reference to "long" and "powerful" suggests the enduring nature of these blessings, akin to the lasting impact of positive actions in our lives.
    • The phrase "with great effort" emphasizes the importance of determination and mental focus in achieving our goals, similar to how athletes train rigorously to excel in their sports.
    • The invocation of the gods signifies a call for divine support in our endeavors, much like how people seek guidance through prayer or meditation in challenging times.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be applied when launching a new product. The effort put into planning and execution, combined with seeking blessings for success, can lead to fruitful outcomes.
    • In personal development, individuals often visualize their goals and work diligently towards them, invoking a sense of divine support to manifest their aspirations.
    • In community service, when people come together to help others, the collective effort and intention can create a powerful impact, akin to the blessings described in the mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of divine blessings and the power of focused effort in achieving success.
    • It serves as a reminder that while we strive for our goals, seeking support from a higher power can enhance our journey.
    • In real life, it encourages individuals to combine their efforts with a sense of purpose and spirituality, leading to a more fulfilling and impactful existence.
