विदुं दद्राणं समने बहूनां युवानं संतं पलितो जगारा |देवस्य पश्य काव्यं महित्वाद्यां ममार स ह्यः सम आना ||
vidhuṁ dadrāṇaṁ samane bahūnāṁ yuvānaṁ santam palito jagāra | devasya paśya kāvyam mahitvādyā mamāra sa hyaḥ sam āna ||
  • Vidhuṁ dadrāṇaṁ: This phrase can be interpreted as "the one who sees the moon." In a metaphorical sense, the moon often represents knowledge, enlightenment, and the illumination of the mind. Just as the moon lights up the night, knowledge enlightens our understanding.
  • Samane bahūnāṁ: This translates to "among many who are equal." It suggests a sense of community and equality, emphasizing the importance of collective wisdom and shared experiences.
  • Yuvānaṁ santam: This means "the youthful and serene." It highlights the qualities of youthfulness, vitality, and tranquility, which are essential for personal growth and harmony in life.
  • Palito jagāra: This can be understood as "the one who has been adorned or decorated." It signifies the importance of nurturing and enhancing one's qualities and virtues, much like how one adorns themselves for a special occasion.
  • Devasya paśya: This translates to "behold the divine." It encourages individuals to recognize and appreciate the divine presence in their lives, which can manifest in various forms, such as nature, relationships, or inner peace.
  • Kāvyam mahitvādyā: This means "the greatness of poetry or art." It emphasizes the significance of creativity and expression in human life, suggesting that art has the power to elevate our spirits and connect us to deeper truths.
  • Mamāra sa hyaḥ sam āna: This can be interpreted as "the one who has attained the ultimate truth." It signifies the journey towards self-realization and understanding one's place in the universe.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of knowledge, community, youthfulness, and the divine. It serves as a reminder of the importance of seeking enlightenment and recognizing the beauty in art and creativity. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as education, where the pursuit of knowledge is paramount; in community building, where equality and shared experiences foster growth; and in personal development, where nurturing one's qualities leads to a fulfilling life. Ultimately, it encourages individuals to seek the divine within themselves and in the world around them, promoting a harmonious existence.
