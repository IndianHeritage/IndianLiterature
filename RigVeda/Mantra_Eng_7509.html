पराकात्ताच्चिदद्रिवस्त्वां नक्षन्त नो गिरः |अरं गमाम ते वयम् ||
parākāttāc cid adrivas tvāṁ nakṣanta no giraḥ | araṁ gamāma te vayam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • parākāttāt: From beyond, transcending
    • cid: Consciousness, awareness
    • adrivas: Without obstacles, unhindered
    • tvāṁ: You (addressing the divine)
    • nakṣanta: We do not abandon, we do not forsake
    • no: Our
    • giraḥ: Words, speech
    • araṁ: We move, we progress
    • gamāma: We go, we advance
    • te: Your
    • vayam: We
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks to the idea of transcending limitations and obstacles through consciousness.
    • It emphasizes the importance of not forsaking the divine or the higher consciousness in our speech and actions.
    • The phrase "we do not abandon you" signifies a commitment to maintaining a connection with the divine, regardless of the challenges faced.
    • The call to "move towards you" suggests a journey of spiritual growth and progress, urging individuals to seek a deeper understanding and connection with the divine.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, when faced with challenges, individuals can draw upon their inner strength and awareness to navigate obstacles, much like the mantra suggests transcending limitations.
    • In personal relationships, maintaining open communication (giraḥ) and not abandoning the connection with loved ones can lead to stronger bonds and understanding.
    • In moments of doubt or fear, individuals can remind themselves of their higher purpose and consciousness, allowing them to move forward with confidence and clarity.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for spiritual awareness and the importance of maintaining a connection with the divine.
    • It serves as a reminder that despite the challenges we face, we have the power to transcend them through consciousness and commitment.
    • This mantra can be applicable in various aspects of life, including personal growth, relationships, and professional endeavors, encouraging individuals to seek higher understanding and connection.
