ऊर्ध्वो वां गातुर अध्वरे अकARY ऊर्ध्वा शोचींषि प्रस्थिता राजांसी |दिवो वा नाभा न्य असादि होतā स्तृणीमहि देवव्यचā वि बरहिः ||
ūrdhvo vāṁ gātur adhvare akāry ūrdhvā śocīṁṣi prasthitā rajāṁsi | divo vā nābhā ny asādi hotā stṛṇīmahi devavyacā vi barhiḥ ||
  • ūrdhvo vāṁ gātur adhvare akāry: This phrase can be translated as "May we ascend to the higher realms through our actions." The word "ūrdhvo" means "upward" or "higher," indicating a spiritual or elevated state. In real life, this can be related to personal growth and striving for excellence in our endeavors, whether in our careers, education, or personal relationships.
  • ūrdhvā śocīṁṣi prasthitā rajāṁsi: This part translates to "May the shining ones (the enlightened) lead us on our path." Here, "śocīṁṣi" refers to those who are enlightened or wise. In practical terms, this can be seen in the guidance we receive from mentors, teachers, or spiritual leaders who help us navigate life's challenges.
  • divo vā nābhā ny asādi hotā: This translates to "From the heavens, may we receive the blessings." The term "dīvo" refers to the divine or celestial realm. This can be interpreted as seeking inspiration and support from higher powers or the universe, akin to how people often pray or meditate for guidance and strength in difficult times.
  • stṛṇīmahi devavyacā vi barhiḥ: This means "We invoke the divine presence to bless our offerings." The word "barhiḥ" refers to sacred grass used in rituals, symbolizing purity and the connection to the divine. In everyday life, this can be related to the importance of intention and gratitude in our actions, whether we are celebrating a milestone or simply appreciating the small joys in life.

  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of striving for higher ideals, seeking guidance from enlightened beings, and invoking divine blessings in our lives. Its significance lies in the reminder that our actions should be directed towards personal and spiritual growth, and that we are not alone in our journey. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire individuals to pursue their goals with integrity, seek mentorship, and maintain a sense of gratitude and connection to the greater universe. It serves as a powerful reminder of the interconnectedness of our actions, intentions, and the divine forces that guide us.
