विः ये भ्राजन्ते सुमखास ऋष्टिभिः प्रच्यावयन्तो अच्युताः चिद् ओजसा |मनोजुवो यन् मरुतो रथेषु आवृषव्रातासः पृषतीर आयुग्ध्वम् ||
vi ye bhrājante sumakhāsa ṛṣṭibhiḥ pracyāvayanto acyutā cid ojasā | manojuvo yan maruto ratheṣv ā vṛṣavrātāsaḥ pṛṣatīr ayugdhvam ||
  • Translation: "Those who shine with beautiful forms, who bring forth the fruits of our actions, may they, with their unchanging energy, inspire our minds. O Maruts, who are swift like the wind, may you grant us strength and protection in our endeavors."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • vi ye bhrājante: "Those who shine" - Refers to divine beings or forces that illuminate our path.
    • sumakhāsa: "with beautiful forms" - Suggests the aesthetic and harmonious nature of these forces.
    • ṛṣṭibhiḥ: "fruits of our actions" - Indicates the results or outcomes of our deeds.
    • pracyāvayanto: "bring forth" - The act of manifesting or producing results.
    • acyutā: "unchanging" - Represents stability and reliability in the divine forces.
    • cid ojasā: "with energy" - Refers to the vital energy that drives actions and thoughts.
    • manojuvo: "inspire our minds" - The influence these forces have on our thoughts and creativity.
    • yan maruto: "O Maruts" - Addressing the Maruts, the storm deities associated with strength and vitality.
    • ratheṣv: "in our endeavors" - Refers to the various paths or journeys we undertake.
    • ā vṛṣavrātāsaḥ: "grant us strength" - A plea for empowerment and support.
    • pṛṣatīr: "protection" - Seeking safety and guidance in our pursuits.
    • ayugdhvam: "may you grant" - A call for divine assistance and blessings.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, when embarking on a new project, individuals often seek inspiration and guidance from mentors or leaders who embody the qualities of the "beautiful forms" mentioned in the mantra.
    • During challenging times, people may turn to spiritual practices or rituals to invoke the "unchanging energy" of divine forces, seeking strength and clarity in their decision-making.
    • In sports, athletes often visualize their goals and draw upon their inner strength, akin to the "Maruts" who provide the energy and motivation needed to excel.
  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of divine guidance and strength in our lives. It serves as a reminder that we are not alone in our endeavors; there are forces that inspire and protect us. By acknowledging these influences, we can cultivate a mindset that embraces creativity, resilience, and purpose. In real-life scenarios, whether in personal growth, professional challenges, or spiritual journeys, invoking the essence of this mantra can lead to greater clarity and empowerment.
