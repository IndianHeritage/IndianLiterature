देवासस त्वा वरुणो मित्रो आर्यमā सं दूतं प्रात्नम इन्दते |विश्वं सो अग्ने जयति त्वया धनं यस् ते ददाश मार्त्यः ||
devāsas tvā varuṇo mitro aryamā saṁ dūtam pratnam indhate | viśvaṁ so agne jayati tvayā dhanaṁ yas te dadāśa martyaḥ ||
  • devāsas tvā: This phrase translates to "the gods (devāsas) are calling upon you (tvā)." It signifies the invocation of divine powers, suggesting that the speaker is reaching out to the gods for assistance or blessings.
  • varuṇo mitro aryamā: Here, Varuna, Mitra, and Aryama are deities associated with cosmic order, friendship, and the moral law, respectively. Varuna represents the cosmic order and the moral authority, Mitra symbolizes friendship and harmony, while Aryama is associated with the path of righteousness. This part emphasizes the importance of these divine qualities in human life.
  • saṁ dūtam pratnam indhate: This can be interpreted as "the messenger (dūtam) of the gods (saṁ) is invoked (pratnam) to bring forth (indhate)." It highlights the role of divine messengers in conveying blessings and guidance to humanity.
  • viśvaṁ so agne: This phrase means "O Agni (the fire god), you are the one who encompasses everything (viśvaṁ)." Agni is often seen as a purifying force and a mediator between humans and the divine. This part signifies the all-encompassing nature of divine energy and its role in life.
  • jayati tvayā: This translates to "victory (jayati) is achieved through you (tvayā)." It emphasizes that success and triumph in life are possible through divine support and guidance.
  • dhanaṁ yas te dadāśa martyaḥ: This means "the wealth (dhanaṁ) that you (te) bestow (dadāśa) upon mortals (martyaḥ)." It signifies the blessings of prosperity and abundance that the divine grants to humanity, highlighting the importance of divine grace in achieving material and spiritual wealth.

Conclusion:

This mantra from the Rigveda encapsulates the essence of divine invocation, emphasizing the interconnectedness of humanity and the divine. It serves as a reminder of the importance of moral values, friendship, and the pursuit of righteousness in our lives. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied during rituals, prayers, or moments of reflection, where individuals seek guidance, support, and blessings from the divine. It encourages individuals to recognize the role of divine forces in their achievements and to cultivate a sense of gratitude for the abundance they receive. Ultimately, it signifies that true success and prosperity come from aligning oneself with higher principles and seeking the blessings of the divine in all endeavors.
