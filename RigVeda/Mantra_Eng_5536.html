एव नो अग्नि विक्ष्वा दशस्य त्वया वयं सहसावन्न आस्क्राः |राया युजा सदहमादो अरिष्टा युयम पात स्वस्तिभिः सदा नः ||
evā no agne vikṣv ā daśasya tvayā vayaṁ sahasāvann āskrāḥ | rāyā yujā sadhamādo ariṣṭā yūyam pāta svastibhiḥ sadā naḥ ||
  • evā no agne: This phrase translates to "O Agni (the fire god), may you be with us." Agni represents not just physical fire but also the divine energy that purifies and transforms. In real life, this can be likened to the role of fire in cooking, warmth, and rituals, symbolizing transformation and sustenance.
  • vikṣv ā daśasya: This means "in all directions, in all ten directions." It signifies the omnipresence of Agni, suggesting that divine energy is everywhere around us. For example, in our daily lives, we can see the influence of energy in nature, technology, and human interactions, reminding us of the interconnectedness of all things.
  • tvayā vayaṁ sahasāvann āskrāḥ: This translates to "we, who are endowed with strength, are protected by you." It emphasizes the need for divine protection and guidance. In practical terms, this can be related to seeking support from mentors, family, or community in times of need, highlighting the importance of collective strength.
  • rāyā yujā: This means "may we be united with prosperity." It reflects the desire for abundance and success in life. In a real-world context, this can be seen in the pursuit of education, career goals, and financial stability, where individuals strive for a prosperous life through hard work and collaboration.
  • sadhamādo: This translates to "with good fortune." It signifies the aspiration for positive outcomes in our endeavors. For instance, when starting a new project or venture, people often seek blessings for success, which can be seen in various cultural practices around the world.
  • ariṣṭā: This means "free from obstacles." It conveys the hope for a smooth journey in life. In real life, this can be related to overcoming challenges and adversities, such as health issues or financial difficulties, and the importance of resilience and determination in achieving one's goals.
  • yūyam pāta svastibhiḥ sadā naḥ: This translates to "may you always protect us with auspiciousness." It is a plea for continuous blessings and safety. In everyday life, this can be reflected in the rituals and prayers people perform for the well-being of their loved ones, emphasizing the human desire for security and peace.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a deep yearning for divine guidance, protection, and prosperity in all aspects of life. Its significance lies in the recognition of the interconnectedness of all beings and the importance of seeking support from the divine and the community. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire individuals to pursue their goals with determination while remaining open to the blessings and guidance that come from both spiritual and communal sources. It serves as a reminder that, in our journey through life, we are not alone, and with the right support and mindset, we can overcome obstacles and achieve prosperity.
