आ नः सोम सहो जु्वो रूपं न वर्चसे भर |सुष्वाणो देववीतये ||
ā naḥ soma saho juvo rūpaṁ na varcase bhara | suṣvāṇo devavītaye ||
  • ā naḥ: This phrase translates to "may it be ours" or "let it come to us." It signifies a desire for something beneficial to be granted to the speaker and their community.
  • soma: Soma refers to a sacred drink in Vedic tradition, often associated with immortality and divine inspiration. It symbolizes the essence of life and spiritual enlightenment.
  • saho: This word means "with strength" or "with vigor." It emphasizes the importance of resilience and power in achieving one's goals.
  • juvo: This term can be interpreted as "to bring forth" or "to produce." It indicates the act of creation or manifestation of desires.
  • rūpaṁ: Meaning "form" or "appearance," this word highlights the significance of the physical manifestation of one's aspirations.
  • na varcase: This phrase translates to "not to be diminished" or "not to lose its brilliance." It suggests the importance of maintaining the integrity and vitality of one's pursuits.
  • bhara: This word means "to fill" or "to bestow." It conveys the idea of abundance and the act of receiving blessings.
  • suṣvāṇo: This term means "well-formed" or "auspicious." It indicates the quality of what is being sought, emphasizing the importance of positive attributes in one's endeavors.
  • devavītaye: This phrase can be interpreted as "for the divine purpose" or "for the sake of the gods." It signifies the connection between human actions and divine will, suggesting that one's efforts should align with higher principles.

Conclusion: The mantra "ā naḥ soma saho juvo rūpaṁ na varcase bhara | suṣvāṇo devavītaye ||" encapsulates a profound desire for divine blessings, strength, and the manifestation of auspicious forms in life. It emphasizes the importance of resilience and the pursuit of goals that align with higher values. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as during personal challenges, community endeavors, or spiritual practices. By invoking this mantra, individuals can seek the strength to overcome obstacles, the clarity to manifest their desires, and the wisdom to align their actions with a greater purpose. Ultimately, it serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of human aspirations and divine support, encouraging individuals to strive for excellence while remaining grounded in their spiritual beliefs.
