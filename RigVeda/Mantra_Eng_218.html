आ ग्ना अग्न इहावसे होत्रां यविष्ट भаратīm |वरूत्रीं धिषणाṁ वह ||
ā gnā agna ihāvase hotrāṁ yaviṣṭha bhāratīm | varūtrīṁ dhiṣaṇāṁ vaha ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā: from
    • gnā: knowledge or wisdom
    • agna: O Fire (Agni, the deity of fire)
    • ihāvase: you are present here
    • hotrāṁ: the one who offers (the priest)
    • yaviṣṭha: the best or most excellent
    • bhāratīm: the one who brings prosperity
    • varūtrīṁ: the one who removes obstacles
    • dhiṣaṇāṁ: the one who grants wisdom
    • vaha: carry or bring forth
  • Overall Meaning: This mantra invokes Agni, the fire deity, as a source of knowledge and wisdom. It emphasizes the importance of the priest's role in offering to Agni, who is seen as the bringer of prosperity and remover of obstacles. The mantra calls for the divine to carry forth wisdom and prosperity into the lives of the devotees.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a traditional Hindu wedding, the sacred fire (Agni) is invoked as a witness to the vows taken by the couple. This reflects the idea of Agni as a source of wisdom and prosperity in their new life together.
    • In daily life, individuals often seek knowledge and guidance through various means, such as education, mentorship, or spiritual practices. This mantra can serve as a reminder to seek wisdom and clarity in decision-making.
    • In times of difficulty, people may perform rituals or prayers to invoke divine assistance, similar to how this mantra calls upon Agni to remove obstacles and bring prosperity.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its invocation of Agni as a symbol of knowledge, prosperity, and the removal of obstacles. It highlights the importance of seeking wisdom in our lives and recognizing the divine presence in our endeavors. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire individuals to pursue knowledge, overcome challenges, and foster a sense of gratitude for the blessings they receive. By acknowledging the role of divine wisdom, one can navigate life's complexities with greater clarity and purpose.
