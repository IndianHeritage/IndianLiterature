आ नो महीम अरमतिं सजोषा ज्ञां देवीं नमसां रातहव्यां |मधोर मदाया बृहतिम ऋतज्ञां आग्ने वह पथिभिर देवयानैः ||
ā no mahīm aramatiṁ sajoṣā gnāṁ devīṁ namasā rātahavyām | madhor madāya bṛhatīm ṛtajñām āgne vaha pathibhir devayānaiḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā no: May we be blessed
    • mahīm: with greatness or abundance
    • aramatiṁ: with joy or delight
    • sajoṣā: with harmony or togetherness
    • gnāṁ: knowledge or wisdom
    • devīṁ: divine or goddess
    • namasā: with reverence or salutation
    • rātahavyām: to the one who is worthy of offerings
    • madhor: of sweetness or pleasantness
    • madāya: for the intoxication or joy
    • bṛhatīm: the vast or great
    • ṛtajñām: the one who knows the truth or righteousness
    • āgne: O Agni (the fire god)
    • vaha: carry or bring
    • pathibhir: by the paths or ways
    • devayānaiḥ: of the divine beings or gods
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra is a prayer invoking blessings and abundance in life.
    • It emphasizes the importance of joy, harmony, and knowledge, suggesting that these qualities lead to a fulfilling life.
    • The reference to Agni, the fire god, symbolizes transformation, purification, and the divine presence in our lives.
    • In real life, this mantra can be seen as a call to seek wisdom and joy in our actions, fostering a sense of community and reverence for the divine.
    • For example, in a workplace, promoting teamwork and respect can lead to a more productive and harmonious environment.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its holistic approach to life, encouraging individuals to seek greatness through joy, knowledge, and divine connection.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, such as personal development, community building, and spiritual practices.
    • By embodying the principles of this mantra, individuals can create a more harmonious and fulfilling existence, both personally and collectively.
