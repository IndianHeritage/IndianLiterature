तं आर्यमाभि रक्षत्य ऋजून्तम अनु व्रतम् |उक्तैः य एनोः परिभूषति व्रतं स्तोमैराभूषति व्रतम् ||
tam aryamābhi rakṣaty ṛjūyantam anu vratam | ukthair ya enoḥ paribhūṣati vrataṁ stomair ābhūṣati vratam ||
  • tam aryamābhi rakṣaty ṛjūyantam anu vratam
    • tam - This word translates to "that" or "him," referring to a divine entity or principle.
    • aryamā - This term signifies a noble or revered being, often associated with the sun or a guiding force.
    • abhi - This means "with" or "towards," indicating a direction or focus.
    • rakṣaty - This translates to "protects," suggesting a safeguarding presence.
    • ṛjūyantam - This word means "straight" or "righteous," emphasizing the importance of integrity and truthfulness.
    • anu - This means "following" or "in accordance with," indicating adherence to a path or principle.
    • vratam - This refers to a vow or commitment, often of a spiritual or moral nature.
  • ukthair ya enoḥ paribhūṣati vrataṁ stomair ābhūṣati vratam
    • ukthair - This means "with hymns" or "through chants," indicating the power of words and prayers.
    • ya - This translates to "who," referring to the subject of the action.
    • enoḥ - This term can mean "of the one" or "of the divine," pointing to a higher power.
    • paribhūṣati - This means "adorns" or "decorates," suggesting enhancement or beautification.
    • vrataṁ - Again refers to a vow or commitment.
    • stomair - This means "with praises" or "through hymns," reinforcing the idea of worship through vocal expression.
    • ābhūṣati - This means "embellishes" or "enhances," indicating the importance of devotion in enriching one's life.

Conclusion:

This mantra emphasizes the significance of protection through righteousness and the power of devotion. It suggests that by adhering to a noble path and making commitments (vratam), one can receive divine protection (rakṣaty) and adorn their life with spiritual richness (ābhūṣati). In real-life scenarios, this can be applied to personal integrity, ethical living, and the importance of community and spiritual practices. For instance, individuals who commit to honesty and ethical behavior in their professional lives often find themselves supported by their peers and community, reflecting the protective and enhancing qualities described in the mantra. Thus, the mantra serves as a reminder of the interplay between personal commitment, ethical living, and divine support in achieving a fulfilling life.
