तं वो दीर्घायुशोचिषं गिरा हुवे मघोनाम् |अरीष्टो येषां रथो व्य अश्वदावन्न ईयते ||
taṁ vo dīrghāyuśociṣaṁ girā huve maghonām | ariṣṭo yeṣāṁ ratho vy aśvadāvann īyate ||
  • taṁ vo - This phrase translates to "to you" or "for you." It indicates a dedication or offering to the divine or the audience.
  • dīrghāyuśociṣaṁ - This compound word means "long life and brilliance." It signifies the desire for longevity and a life filled with radiance and positivity.
  • girā - This word means "by speech" or "through words." It emphasizes the power of communication and the importance of expressing one's thoughts and prayers.
  • huve - This translates to "I invoke" or "I call upon." It indicates an act of prayer or invocation, seeking blessings or assistance from the divine.
  • maghonām - This means "of the wealthy" or "of the prosperous." It refers to those who are blessed with abundance and success.
  • ariṣṭo - This word means "without obstacles" or "free from harm." It signifies a desire for a life that is free from difficulties and challenges.
  • yeṣāṁ - This translates to "of whom" or "whose." It refers to the subjects of the mantra, possibly the divine or the audience being addressed.
  • ratho - This means "chariot." In Vedic literature, the chariot often symbolizes the journey of life and the means to achieve one's goals.
  • vy - This is a prefix that can mean "in" or "with," indicating the manner in which something is done.
  • aśvadāvann - This translates to "like a horse." Horses in Vedic culture symbolize strength, speed, and vitality.
  • īyate - This means "is driven" or "is moved." It indicates action and the dynamic nature of life.

In summary, this mantra is a prayer for a long, prosperous, and obstacle-free life, invoking the blessings of the divine through the power of words. It emphasizes the importance of communication and the desire for a life filled with vitality and success.


Significance and Real-Life Application:

  • This mantra can be recited during personal prayers or rituals to seek blessings for health and longevity.
  • In a professional context, it can be used to invoke success and prosperity in one's career or business endeavors.
  • It serves as a reminder of the importance of clear communication and positive expression in relationships and interactions.
  • In times of challenge, reciting this mantra can provide encouragement and a sense of protection against obstacles.

Overall, the mantra encapsulates a holistic approach to life, encouraging individuals to seek not only material success but also spiritual and emotional well-being.
