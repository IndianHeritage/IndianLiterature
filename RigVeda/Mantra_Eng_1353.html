अश्विनोर आसनं रथम अनश्वं वाजिनीवतोः |तेनाहं भूरी चाकन ||
aśvinor asanaṁ ratham anaśvaṁ vājinīvatoḥ | tenāham bhūri cākana ||
  • Mantra: "aśvinor asanaṁ ratham anaśvaṁ vājinīvatoḥ | tenāham bhūri cākana ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • aśvinor: of the Ashvins (the twin gods of medicine and health)
    • asanaṁ: seat or position
    • ratham: chariot or vehicle
    • anaśvaṁ: without horses
    • vājinīvatoḥ: of the swift steeds
    • tena: by that
    • aham: I
    • bhūri: abundant or plentiful
    • cākana: to obtain or to achieve
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the Ashvins, who are known for their speed and healing abilities.
    • The reference to "asanaṁ ratham anaśvaṁ" suggests a chariot or vehicle that is not drawn by horses, symbolizing a state of being that is not reliant on conventional means.
    • The phrase "vājinīvatoḥ" emphasizes the swiftness and agility of the Ashvins, indicating that the speaker seeks their assistance in achieving something significant.
    • Overall, the mantra expresses a desire for abundance and success through divine intervention.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a modern context, this mantra can be related to seeking help in overcoming obstacles in life, such as health issues or challenges in personal or professional endeavors.
    • For instance, an athlete may recite this mantra before a competition, invoking the Ashvins for strength and speed, akin to how they are believed to assist in achieving excellence.
    • Similarly, someone facing a critical decision in their career might use this mantra to seek clarity and guidance, hoping for a swift resolution to their dilemma.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the importance of divine support in our pursuits, reminding us that while we may strive for success through our efforts, seeking guidance from higher powers can lead to greater achievements.
    • It is applicable in various life scenarios, from health and wellness to career advancements, emphasizing the need for balance between personal effort and spiritual assistance.
    • Ultimately, the mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of our actions and the divine, encouraging us to remain open to receiving help in our journeys.
