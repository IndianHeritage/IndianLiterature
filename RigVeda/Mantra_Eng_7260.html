उद ū षु णो वसो महे मृशस्व शूर राधसे |उद ū षु मह्यै मघवन मघत्तय उद इन्द्र श्रवसे महे ||
ud ū ṣu ṇo vaso mahe mṛśasva śūra rādhase | ud ū ṣu mahyai maghavan maghattaya ud indra śravase mahe ||
  • ud ū ṣu ṇo vaso: This phrase can be interpreted as a call to the divine for abundance and prosperity. The term "ud" signifies elevation or rising, while "ūṣu" refers to the dawn or light. "Vaso" implies wealth or resources. Together, this can be understood as a plea for the rise of wealth and prosperity in one’s life.
  • mahe mṛśasva: "Mahe" means great or powerful, and "mṛśasva" can be translated as "to be gracious" or "to bestow." This part of the mantra emphasizes the importance of divine grace in achieving greatness and success in life.
  • śūra rādhase: "Śūra" means brave or valiant, and "rādhase" refers to the one who is worthy of praise. This phrase highlights the value of courage and valor in one’s endeavors, suggesting that bravery is essential for achieving one’s goals.
  • ud ū ṣu mahyai: This reiterates the call for elevation and abundance, specifically directed towards the divine. "Mahyai" indicates a connection to the great or powerful, reinforcing the idea of seeking blessings from a higher power.
  • maghavan maghattaya: "Maghavan" refers to the divine being associated with wealth and abundance, while "maghattaya" signifies the essence of greatness or prosperity. This part of the mantra acknowledges the source of wealth and the importance of recognizing and honoring it.
  • ud indra śravase mahe: "Indra" is often regarded as the king of gods and a symbol of strength and power. "Śravase" means fame or glory, and "mahe" again emphasizes greatness. This phrase calls upon Indra for strength and recognition, suggesting that divine support is crucial for achieving fame and success.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda encapsulates the essence of seeking divine blessings for prosperity, courage, and greatness. It emphasizes the importance of recognizing the sources of wealth and strength in our lives. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied during times of challenge or when embarking on new ventures, serving as a reminder to seek support from higher powers and to embody bravery in pursuit of our goals. It encourages individuals to strive for greatness while remaining humble and grateful for the abundance they receive.
