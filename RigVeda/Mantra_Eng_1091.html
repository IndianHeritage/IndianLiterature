मन्त्र: दिवो न यस्य रेतसो दुधानाः पन्थासो यन्ति शवसापरीताः |तरद्वेषाः सासहिः पौंस्येभिर मरुत्वान नो भवत्व इन्द्र ऊती ||
divo na yasya retaso dughānāḥ panthāso yanti śavasāparītāḥ | taraddveṣāḥ sāsahiḥ pauṁsyebhir marutvān no bhavatv indra ūtī ||
  • Mantra: "divo na yasya retaso dughānāḥ panthāso yanti śavasāparītāḥ | taraddveṣāḥ sāsahiḥ pauṁsyebhir marutvān no bhavatv indra ūtī ||"
  • Translation: "May the paths of those who are nourished by the essence of the heavens lead us to the divine. May the powerful Indra, who is free from enmity, grant us strength and prosperity."

  • Word-by-Word Explanation:
    • Divo: Of the heavens or divine realm.
    • Na: Not.
    • Yasya: Whose.
    • Retaso: Essence or nourishment.
    • Dughānāḥ: Those who are nourished or sustained.
    • Panthāso: Paths or ways.
    • Yanti: Lead or go.
    • Śavasāparītāḥ: Surrounded by strength or power.
    • Taraddveṣāḥ: Free from enmity or hostility.
    • Sāsahiḥ: Granting strength or support.
    • Pauṁsyebhir: By the powerful or strong.
    • Marutvān: The mighty or powerful (often referring to the wind gods).
    • No: Us.
    • Bhavatv: May it be or may it become.
    • Indra: The king of gods, representing strength and power.
    • Ūtī: Prosperity or abundance.

  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, this mantra can be invoked during meetings to foster a spirit of collaboration and unity among team members, encouraging them to work together without hostility.
    • In personal relationships, it can serve as a reminder to approach conflicts with understanding and to seek paths that lead to mutual growth and support.
    • In community gatherings, this mantra can inspire collective efforts towards prosperity and well-being, emphasizing the importance of harmony and strength in numbers.

  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for unity, strength, and the pursuit of divine paths free from enmity. It emphasizes the importance of collaboration and support in achieving prosperity. In real-life scenarios, whether in personal relationships, professional environments, or community efforts, invoking this mantra can inspire individuals to work together harmoniously, fostering an atmosphere of mutual respect and shared goals. By aligning with the essence of the heavens and the strength of Indra, individuals can navigate challenges and achieve greater success collectively.
