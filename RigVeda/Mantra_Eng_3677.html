इमे यामासस त्वद्रिग अभूवन वसवे वा तद इद आगो अवाचि |नाहायम अग्निर अभिशस्तये नो न रीषते वावृद्धानः परा दात ||
ime yāmāsas tvadrig abhūvan vasave vā tad id āgo avāci | nāhāyam agnir abhiśastaye no na rīṣate vāvṛdhānaḥ parā dāt ||
  • ime yāmāsas: This phrase can be interpreted as "these who are here" or "these beings present." It signifies the acknowledgment of the existence of various entities around us, both seen and unseen.
  • tvadrig abhūvan: This translates to "your power has manifested." It emphasizes the divine energy or force that is present in the universe, which can be related to the natural elements and their influence on life.
  • vasave vā: This means "for the one who is worthy." It suggests that there is a deserving entity or force that is to be revered, which can be likened to the respect we give to nature or higher powers in our lives.
  • tad id āgo avāci: This can be understood as "that which has been spoken." It refers to the wisdom or knowledge that has been passed down through generations, akin to the teachings we receive from elders or sacred texts.
  • nāhāyam agnir abhiśastaye: This translates to "this fire does not diminish." Fire here symbolizes energy, passion, and transformation, indicating that the essence of life and its vigor remains constant despite challenges.
  • no na rīṣate: This means "it does not wane." It signifies resilience and the enduring nature of certain truths or values in life, much like the unwavering spirit of individuals facing adversity.
  • vāvṛdhānaḥ parā dāt: This can be interpreted as "the supreme giver of abundance." It highlights the idea of generosity and the importance of sharing resources, akin to how communities thrive when they support one another.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of recognizing the divine presence in our lives, the importance of wisdom, resilience, and the value of generosity. In real-life scenarios, it encourages individuals to appreciate the interconnectedness of all beings, respect the natural world, and uphold values that foster community and support. This mantra serves as a reminder to remain steadfast in our pursuits and to share our blessings with others, ultimately leading to a harmonious existence.
