मयो दधे मेधिरः पूतदक्षो दिवः सुभन्धुर जनुषा पृथिव्याः |अविन्दन्नु दर्शतम अप्स्व अन्तर देवासो अग्निम अपसि स्वसृणाम् ||
mayo dadhe medhiraḥ pūtadakṣo divaḥ subandhur januṣā pṛthivyāḥ | avindann u darśatam apsv a1ntar devāso agnim apasi svasṝṇām ||
  • mayo dadhe medhiraḥ: This phrase can be translated as "May we be endowed with wisdom." The term "medhiraḥ" refers to intelligence or wisdom, which is essential for making sound decisions in life. For example, a student preparing for exams seeks wisdom to understand complex subjects better.
  • pūtadakṣo divaḥ: This translates to "the pure and capable one from the heavens." Here, "pūtadakṣo" signifies purity and capability, suggesting that divine qualities are essential for achieving success. In real life, a leader who embodies these qualities can inspire their team to achieve great results.
  • subandhur januṣā pṛthivyāḥ: This means "the well-connected one among the beings of the earth." "Subandhur" implies good relationships and connections. In a practical sense, networking and building relationships are crucial in professional settings, as they can lead to new opportunities and collaborations.
  • avindann u darśatam apsv: This can be interpreted as "may we find and see the best in the waters." The "waters" here symbolize knowledge and resources. In everyday life, this can relate to seeking out the best information and resources available to us, such as using libraries or online platforms for research.
  • antardevāso agnim apasi svasṛṇām: This translates to "the divine beings reveal the fire within." "Agnim" represents fire, which symbolizes energy, passion, and transformation. In real life, this can be seen in individuals who harness their inner passion to drive change, such as activists fighting for social justice.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of wisdom, purity, strong relationships, the pursuit of knowledge, and the inner fire that drives transformation. In real life, these qualities are applicable in various scenarios, such as education, leadership, networking, and personal development. By embodying these principles, individuals can navigate life's challenges more effectively and contribute positively to society.
