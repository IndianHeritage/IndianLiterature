अग्निं वः पूर्व्यं गिरा देवम् ईळे वसूनाम् |सपर्ण्यन्तः पुरुप्रियं मित्रं न क्षेत्रसाधसम् ||
agniṁ vaḥ pūrvyaṁ girā devam īḻe vasūnām | saparyantaḥ purupriyam mitraṁ na kṣetrasādhasam ||
  • Agniṁ vaḥ pūrvyaṁ girā: This phrase translates to "We invoke Agni, the divine fire, with our words." Agni is not just a physical fire but symbolizes energy, transformation, and the divine presence in rituals. In real life, this can be likened to the way we invoke passion and motivation in our endeavors, much like how a fire ignites warmth and light.
  • Devaṁ īḻe vasūnām: This means "I meditate upon the divine, the source of all wealth." Here, 'Deva' refers to the divine or godly aspect, while 'vasūnām' signifies wealth or abundance. In practical terms, this can be seen in how individuals seek spiritual or moral wealth through meditation, prayer, or community service, aiming for a life rich in values rather than just material possessions.
  • Saparyantaḥ purupriyam: This translates to "Those who are devoted to the beloved of the people." This can be interpreted as the importance of community and relationships. In everyday life, this reflects how we cherish and nurture our connections with family, friends, and society, emphasizing the value of love and support in our lives.
  • Mitraṁ na kṣetrasādhasam: This means "May we have the friendship that is not dependent on material possessions." 'Mitra' signifies friendship, while 'kṣetrasādhasam' refers to the material or worldly gains. This highlights the idea that true friendship transcends material wealth. In real life, it can be observed in friendships that endure through hardships, where emotional support and understanding take precedence over financial or social status.

  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of invoking divine energy (Agni) and seeking spiritual wealth (Deva) while fostering genuine relationships (Mitra) that are not contingent on material possessions. In today's fast-paced world, where materialism often overshadows spiritual and emotional connections, this mantra serves as a reminder to prioritize inner values, community bonds, and the pursuit of a meaningful life. It encourages individuals to cultivate passion, meditate on higher ideals, and build friendships based on mutual respect and love, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling existence.
