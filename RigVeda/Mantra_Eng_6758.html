विष्वैर देवैस त्रिभिर एकादशैर इहाद्भिर मरुद्भिर भृगुभिः सचाभुवाः |सजोषसां उषसा सूर्येण च सोमं पिबताम अश्विनाः ||
viśvair devais tribhir ekādaśair ihādbhir marudbhir bhṛgubhiḥ sacābhuvā | sajoṣasā uṣasā sūryeṇa ca somam pibatam aśvinā ||
  • Translation: This mantra from the Rigveda invokes the divine forces, asking for the blessings of various deities, including the Maruts (storm gods), the Bhrigus (a family of sages), and the Aśvins (twin gods associated with health and medicine). It emphasizes the unity of these divine beings and their collective power.
  • Word Breakdown:
    • viśvair: with all, universal
    • devais: by the gods
    • tribhir: by three
    • ekādaśair: eleven
    • ihādbhir: here, in this world
    • marudbhir: by the Maruts
    • bhṛgubhiḥ: by the Bhrigus
    • sacābhuvā: and with abundance
    • sajoṣasā: with joy
    • uṣasā: with the dawn
    • sūryeṇa: with the sun
    • ca: and
    • somam: the moon
    • pibatam: may drink
    • aśvinā: by the Aśvins
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • This mantra can be recited during rituals or ceremonies to invoke the blessings of health and prosperity, similar to how people pray for good health before a major event or competition.
    • In modern life, the concept of unity among different forces can be seen in teamwork, where diverse skills and talents come together to achieve a common goal, much like the collective power of the deities mentioned.
    • The reference to the sun and moon symbolizes the balance of energies in life, reminding us of the importance of maintaining harmony between work (sun) and rest (moon).
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for unity and collaboration among various divine forces, reflecting the interconnectedness of all aspects of life. It teaches us to seek blessings from multiple sources and to appreciate the diversity of energies that contribute to our well-being. In real-life scenarios, this mantra encourages us to work together harmoniously, respect different perspectives, and strive for a balanced life, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling existence.
