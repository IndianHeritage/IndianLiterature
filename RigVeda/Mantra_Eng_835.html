अर्वद्भिर अग्ने अरवतोन नृभिर नृण वीरैर वीरान वनुयामाऽत्वोताः |ईशानासः पितृवित्तस्य रायो वि सूरयः शतहिमाः नो अश्युः ||
arvadbhir agne arvato nṛbhir nṝn vīrair vīrān vanuyāmā tvotāḥ | īśānāsaḥ pitṛvittasya rāyo vi sūrayaḥ śatahimā no aśyuḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • arvadbhir: With the help of the wise or learned.
    • agne: O Agni (the fire god).
    • arvato: From the heights or the elevated.
    • nṛbhir: By men or people.
    • nṝn: Of men.
    • vīrair: By heroes or brave ones.
    • vīrān: The brave or heroic.
    • vanuyāmā: We invoke or call upon.
    • tvotāḥ: Your praises.
    • īśānāsaḥ: The lord of wealth or prosperity.
    • pitṛvittasya: Of the ancestral wealth.
    • rāyo: Of riches or prosperity.
    • vi: Indeed.
    • sūrayaḥ: The strong or powerful.
    • śatahimā: A hundredfold or abundant.
    • no: For us.
    • aśyuḥ: Quickly or swiftly.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Agni, the fire god, seeking his blessings and support.
    • It emphasizes the importance of wisdom and bravery in achieving success and prosperity.
    • The invocation of Agni signifies the need for divine guidance in human endeavors.
    • The mention of ancestral wealth highlights the value of heritage and the blessings passed down through generations.
    • The desire for abundance and swift success reflects a common human aspiration for prosperity and fulfillment.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, leaders often invoke the spirit of teamwork and bravery to inspire their teams to achieve ambitious goals.
    • Families may honor their ancestors during festivals, seeking blessings for prosperity and success in their endeavors.
    • Individuals may turn to spiritual practices, such as meditation or prayer, to seek guidance and wisdom in making important life decisions.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of wisdom, bravery, and divine support in the pursuit of success.
    • It highlights the significance of honoring one's heritage and the blessings that come from it.
    • In real life, the principles embodied in this mantra can guide individuals and communities in their quest for prosperity, encouraging them to seek wisdom and support from both the divine and their ancestors.
