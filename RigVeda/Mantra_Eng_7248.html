अर्भको न कुमारको 'धि तिष्ठन् नवं रथम् |स पक्षन् महिषं मृगं पित्रे मात्रे विभुकृतुम् ||
arbhako na kumārako 'dhi tiṣṭhan navaṁ ratham | sa pakṣan mahiṣam mṛgam pitre mātre vibhukratum ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • arbhako: a young child or infant
    • na: not
    • kumārako: a young boy or lad
    • 'dhi: indeed
    • tiṣṭhan: standing or existing
    • navaṁ: new
    • ratham: chariot or vehicle
    • sa: he or that
    • pakṣan: wings
    • mahiṣam: buffalo or a strong animal
    • mṛgam: animal or creature
    • pitre: to the father
    • mātre: to the mother
    • vibhukratum: to be endowed or to possess
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the essence of childhood and the potential that comes with it.
    • The phrase "arbhako na kumārako" suggests that the child is not merely a young boy but embodies the purity and potential of infancy.
    • The "navaṁ ratham" symbolizes new beginnings and the journey of life, akin to a chariot that carries one forward.
    • The mention of "pakṣan" indicates the ability to soar or rise above challenges, much like a bird taking flight.
    • Furthermore, "mahiṣam mṛgam" can be interpreted as the strength and resilience found in nature, representing the nurturing aspects of both parents.
    • Finally, the invocation to "pitre mātre vibhukratum" emphasizes the importance of parental guidance and blessings in nurturing a child's growth.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern parenting, this mantra can be reflected in the way parents encourage their children to explore new opportunities, much like a chariot leading them on their journey.
    • Educational systems that focus on holistic development embody the essence of nurturing a child's potential, allowing them to "soar" in their pursuits.
    • Community programs that support families in raising children highlight the importance of collective responsibility, akin to the blessings of both mother and father.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of childhood as a phase of immense potential and growth.
    • It reminds us of the vital role that parents and guardians play in shaping a child's future, providing them with the tools to navigate life's challenges.
    • In real life, it encourages us to foster environments where children can thrive, explore, and develop their unique abilities, ensuring a brighter future for society as a whole.
