ऊर्ध्वं भानुं सविता देवो अश्रेद द्रप्सं दविद्वद गविषो न सत्वा |अनु व्रतं वरुणो यन्ति मित्रो यत सूर्यं दिव्य आरोहयन्ति ||
ūrdhvam bhānuṁ savitā devo aśred drapsaṁ davidhvad gaviṣo na satvā | anu vrataṁ varuṇo yanti mitro yat sūryaṁ divy ārohayanti ||
  • ūrdhvam bhānuṁ: This phrase translates to "the radiant sun above." It signifies the importance of the sun as a source of light and energy in our lives.
  • savitā devo: "Savitā" refers to the sun god, representing the divine aspect of the sun that nourishes and sustains life.
  • ašred drapsaṁ: This can be interpreted as "the one who brings forth abundance." It emphasizes the sun's role in agriculture and the growth of crops, which is essential for sustenance.
  • davidhvad gaviṣo: This phrase suggests "the one who pervades the earth." It highlights the sun's omnipresence and its influence on all living beings.
  • na satvā: This means "not to be neglected." It serves as a reminder of the importance of acknowledging and respecting the forces of nature.
  • anu vrataṁ: This translates to "following the path." It signifies the importance of adhering to righteous conduct and moral values in life.
  • varuṇo yanti mitro: "Varuṇa" is the god of cosmic order, and "mitro" refers to friendship. This phrase suggests that those who follow the path of righteousness are guided by divine forces.
  • yat sūryaṁ divy ārohayanti: This means "those who elevate the divine sun." It emphasizes the significance of worship and reverence towards the sun as a source of life and energy.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda encapsulates the reverence for the sun and its vital role in sustaining life on Earth. It teaches us to respect nature, follow a righteous path, and acknowledge the divine forces that guide us. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied by promoting sustainable practices, engaging in environmental conservation, and fostering a sense of community and friendship. By recognizing the interconnectedness of all life and the importance of moral values, we can create a harmonious existence that honors the natural world and its divine elements.
