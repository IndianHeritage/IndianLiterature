ते सूनवः स्वपसः सुदंससो मही जज्ञुर मातरा पूर्वचित्तये |स्थातुश्च सत्यं जगतश्च धर्मणि पुत्रस्य पाथः पदम् अद्वयाविनः ||
te sūnavaḥ svapasaḥ sudaṁsaso mahī jajñur mātarā pūrvacittaye | sthātuś ca satyaṁ jagataś ca dharmaṇi putrasya pāthaḥ padam advayāvinaḥ ||
  • te sūnavaḥ: This phrase translates to "they who are the sons" or "the offspring." It signifies the importance of lineage and the connection between generations.
  • svapasaḥ: This means "of the wise" or "of the enlightened." It emphasizes the value of wisdom and knowledge in guiding one's actions and decisions.
  • sudaṁsaso: This term can be interpreted as "of good conduct" or "of noble character." It highlights the significance of ethical behavior and moral integrity.
  • mahī jajñur: This translates to "the great ones have been born." It suggests the emergence of significant individuals or leaders who can influence society positively.
  • mātarā pūrvacittaye: This means "by the mothers of the past." It acknowledges the role of mothers and ancestral wisdom in shaping the future.
  • sthātuś ca: This phrase translates to "and those who stand firm." It refers to individuals who are steadfast and resilient in their beliefs and actions.
  • satyaṁ: This means "truth." It underscores the importance of truthfulness in all aspects of life.
  • jagataś ca dharmaṇi: This translates to "in the world and in righteousness." It emphasizes the need for ethical conduct in both personal and societal contexts.
  • putrasya pāthaḥ: This means "the path of the son." It signifies the journey and responsibilities that come with being a part of a lineage.
  • padam advayāvinaḥ: This translates to "the path of the non-dual." It suggests a journey towards unity and understanding beyond dualities.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda encapsulates the essence of lineage, wisdom, ethical conduct, and the importance of truth in life. It signifies that the values instilled by previous generations, particularly by mothers, play a crucial role in shaping the character and path of future generations. In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as parenting, education, and leadership. It encourages individuals to uphold truth and righteousness, fostering a society that values wisdom and moral integrity. By embodying these principles, one can contribute to a harmonious and progressive community, ensuring that the legacy of good conduct and wisdom continues through generations.
