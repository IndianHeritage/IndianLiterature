आ वां वहिष्ठा इह ते वहन्तु रथा अश्वासा उषसो व्युष्टौ |इमे हि वाम मधुपेयाया सोमाऽस्मिन यज्ञे वृषणा माद्येथाम् ||
ā vāṁ vahiṣṭhā iha te vahantu rathā aśvāsa uṣaso vyuṣṭau | ime hi vām madhupeyāya somā asmin yajñe vṛṣaṇā mādayethām ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā vāṁ: May we (us)
    • vahiṣṭhā: the best or the most excellent
    • iha: here
    • te: your
    • vahantu: may they carry or bring
    • rathā: chariots
    • aśvāsa: horses
    • uṣaso: of the dawn
    • vyuṣṭau: having risen or arisen
    • ime: these
    • hi: indeed
    • vām: to you
    • madhupeyāya: for the sweet drink (nectar)
    • somā: the soma (sacred drink)
    • asmin: in this
    • yajñe: sacrifice or offering
    • vṛṣaṇā: with strength or vigor
    • mādayethām: may they make us joyful
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer for blessings and prosperity. It invokes the divine to bring forth the best chariots and horses, symbolizing strength and speed.
    • The mention of "dawn" signifies new beginnings and the hope that comes with the arrival of light after darkness.
    • The reference to "soma" and "madhupeyāya" indicates a desire for spiritual nourishment and joy, suggesting that the offerings made in rituals should lead to happiness and fulfillment.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a modern context, this mantra can be related to the aspirations of individuals or communities seeking success in their endeavors, such as starting a new business or embarking on a new project.
    • For instance, during a wedding ceremony, couples may recite similar prayers to invoke blessings for a prosperous and joyful life together.
    • In sports, athletes often seek blessings for strength and success, akin to the invocation of chariots and horses in the mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking divine blessings for strength, prosperity, and joy in life.
    • Its significance lies in the universal desire for success and happiness, making it applicable in various life scenarios, from personal aspirations to communal celebrations.
    • By invoking these blessings, individuals can cultivate a mindset of positivity and hope, essential for overcoming challenges and achieving their goals.
