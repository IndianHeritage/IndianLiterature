अष्टा महो दिव आदो हरि इह द्युम्नासाहम अभि योधाना उत्सम् |हरिं यत ते मन्दिनं दुःखन वृधे गोरभसम अद्रिभिर वाताप्यम् ||
aṣṭā maho diva ādo harī iha dyumnāsāham abhi yodhāna utsam | hariṁ yat te mandinaṁ dukṣan vṛdhe gorabhasam adribhir vātāpyam ||
  • Mantra: "aṣṭā maho diva ādo harī iha dyumnāsāham abhi yodhāna utsam | hariṁ yat te mandinaṁ dukṣan vṛdhe gorabhasam adribhir vātāpyam ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • aṣṭā: Eight
    • maho: Great
    • diva: Heaven
    • ādo: From here
    • harī: The one who removes obstacles
    • iha: Here
    • dyumnāsāham: I am filled with glory
    • abhi: Towards
    • yodhāna: Warriors
    • utsam: To be invoked
    • hariṁ: The remover of sins
    • yat: That which
    • te: Your
    • mandinaṁ: Wise
    • dukṣan: Difficulties
    • vṛdhe: In growth
    • gorabhasam: The strength of the bull
    • adribhir: By the mountains
    • vātāpyam: By the winds
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra invokes the divine forces to aid in overcoming challenges and obstacles.
    • The mention of "eight great heavens" signifies the vastness of divine support available to us.
    • "Harī" represents the divine energy that helps in removing difficulties, akin to how a mentor or guide assists us in our personal or professional lives.
    • The phrase "I am filled with glory" reflects self-empowerment and confidence, essential for success in any endeavor.
    • In real life, this can be compared to a team of warriors (yodhāna) working together to achieve a common goal, such as a sports team striving for victory.
    • The reference to "the strength of the bull" symbolizes resilience and determination, qualities necessary to face life's challenges.
    • Finally, "by the mountains and winds" suggests that nature itself supports our endeavors, reminding us of the interconnectedness of all things.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of divine support, self-confidence, and teamwork in overcoming life's challenges.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, such as in personal growth, professional challenges, and community efforts.
    • By invoking this mantra, individuals can cultivate a mindset of resilience and empowerment, enabling them to navigate difficulties with grace and strength.
    • Ultimately, it serves as a reminder that we are not alone in our struggles; divine forces and the support of our community are always with us.
