इदं उ त्यान महि महाम अनीकं यद उष्रियā साचत पूर्व्यं गौः |ऋतस्य पदे अधि दीद्यानाṁ गुहा रघुष्यद रघुयद विवेद ||
idam u tyan mahi mahām anīkaṁ yad usriyā sacata pūrvyaṁ gauḥ | ṛtasya pade adhi dīdyānaṁ guhā raghuṣyad raghuyad viveda ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • idam: this
    • u: and
    • tyan: that
    • mahi: great
    • mahām: of the great
    • anīkaṁ: multitude
    • yad: which
    • usriyā: by the light
    • sacata: is made manifest
    • pūrvyaṁ: ancient
    • gauḥ: cow (symbolizing wealth and nourishment)
    • ṛtasya: of truth
    • pade: in the path
    • adhi: upon
    • dīdyānaṁ: shining
    • guhā: cave (symbolizing hidden knowledge)
    • raghuṣyad: of the swift
    • raghuyad: of the noble
    • viveda: has understood
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the great multitude and the ancient truths that are revealed through light.
    • The cow, a symbol of wealth and nourishment, represents the abundance that comes from understanding these truths.
    • The path of truth is illuminated, suggesting that knowledge and wisdom guide us through life.
    • The cave symbolizes the depths of knowledge that may be hidden but can be accessed through understanding.
    • The swift and noble qualities refer to the virtues that one should embody in the pursuit of truth and knowledge.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern life, the pursuit of knowledge can be likened to education. Just as the mantra emphasizes the importance of understanding ancient truths, education helps us uncover the wisdom of the past.
    • The cow, representing nourishment, can be seen in the context of sustainable living and agriculture, where understanding the balance of nature leads to abundance.
    • The idea of a path illuminated by truth can be related to ethical decision-making in business, where transparency and integrity lead to long-term success.
    • Exploring hidden knowledge can be compared to scientific research, where discoveries often come from delving into the unknown.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of seeking knowledge and truth in our lives.
    • It encourages us to embody noble qualities and to pursue understanding, which can lead to personal growth and societal benefit.
    • In a world filled with distractions, this mantra serves as a reminder to focus on what is truly important: the pursuit of wisdom and the nurturing of our inner selves.
    • Ultimately, it teaches us that by understanding the ancient truths and embodying virtuous qualities, we can navigate life with clarity and purpose.
