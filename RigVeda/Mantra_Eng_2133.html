सध्रीं आ यन्ति परि बिभ्रतीः पयो विश्वाप्स्याय प्र भरन्त भोजनोम् |समानो अध्वा प्रवताम अनुश्यदे यस् ताकृत्नोः प्रथमं सास्य उक्त्यः ||
sadhrīm ā yanti pari bibhratīḥ payo viśvapsnyāya pra bharanta bhojanam | samāno adhvā pravatām anuṣyade yas tākṛṇoḥ prathamaṁ sāsy ukthyaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sadhrīm: Together, in harmony
    • ā yanti: They go, they proceed
    • pari: All around, in every direction
    • bibhratīḥ: Bearing, carrying
    • payo: Milk, nourishment
    • viśvapsnyāya: For the universal well-being
    • pra bharanta: They offer, they provide
    • bhojanam: Food, sustenance
    • samāno: Similar, united
    • adhvā: Path, journey
    • pravatām: They flow, they move
    • anuṣyade: Following, in accordance with
    • yas: Who
    • tākṛṇoḥ: Of the creators, of the makers
    • prathamaṁ: First, foremost
    • sāsy: Grain, harvest
    • ukthyaḥ: Praise, invocation
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of unity and harmony in the pursuit of nourishment and sustenance.
    • It suggests that when individuals come together, they can create a nurturing environment that benefits everyone.
    • The reference to "milk" symbolizes nourishment, both physical and spiritual, which is essential for growth and well-being.
    • The phrase "for the universal well-being" indicates that the actions taken should not only benefit the individual but also the community and the world at large.
    • The mantra also highlights the significance of following a common path, suggesting that collaboration and shared goals lead to prosperity.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community garden, individuals from different backgrounds come together to cultivate plants. This collaboration not only provides food but also fosters relationships and a sense of belonging.
    • In a workplace, teamwork and cooperation among employees can lead to innovative solutions and a productive environment, benefiting both the organization and its members.
    • During a crisis, such as a natural disaster, communities often unite to provide aid and support, demonstrating the power of collective action for the greater good.
  • Conclusion:
    • The mantra serves as a reminder of the significance of unity and collaboration in achieving common goals.
    • It encourages individuals to work together for the well-being of all, highlighting that true nourishment comes from shared efforts and mutual support.
    • In today's world, where individualism often prevails, this mantra is a call to action to foster community spirit and collective responsibility.
