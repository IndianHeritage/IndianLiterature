मन्त्र: विश्वानि नो दुर्गहा जातवेदः सिंधुं न नाव दुर्गताति पर्षि | अग्ने अत्रिवन नमसाऽगृणानोऽस्माकं बोध्य अवितā तनूनाम् ||
viśvāni no durgahā jātavedaḥ sindhuṁ na nāvā duritāti parṣi | agne atrivan namasā gṛṇāno3 'smākam bodhy avitā tanūnām ||
  • viśvāni no durgahā jātavedaḥ: This phrase can be translated to "O Jātaveda, may you make all our paths free from obstacles." Here, "viśvāni" refers to all aspects of life, and "durgahā" means difficult or obstructed. Jātaveda is a name for Agni, the fire god, who is often seen as a mediator between humans and the divine.
  • sindhuṁ na nāvā duritāti parṣi: This translates to "May we not be overwhelmed by the waves of difficulties." "Sindhu" refers to the ocean or water, symbolizing the vast challenges we face, while "nāvā" means boat, representing our journey through life. "Duritāti" refers to troubles or misfortunes, and "parṣi" means to protect or save.
  • agne atrivan namasā gṛṇāno: This means "O Agni, we offer our salutations to you with devotion." "Agne" is a direct address to Agni, and "namasā" signifies respect or reverence. "Gṛṇāno" indicates the act of singing praises or offering hymns.
  • smākam bodhy avitā tanūnām: This translates to "May you enlighten and protect our bodies." "Smākam" means our, "bodhy" refers to enlightenment or awareness, and "avitā" means to protect. "Tanūnām" refers to our physical forms or bodies.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda emphasizes the importance of seeking divine guidance and protection in overcoming life's challenges. It highlights the role of Agni as a source of light and clarity, helping individuals navigate through difficulties. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied during times of uncertainty or hardship, such as facing personal struggles, professional challenges, or health issues. By invoking the essence of this mantra, individuals can cultivate a sense of resilience and hope, trusting that they will be guided and protected on their journey. The significance lies in the belief that with divine support, one can overcome obstacles and achieve a harmonious existence.
