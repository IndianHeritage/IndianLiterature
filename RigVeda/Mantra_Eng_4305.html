सां नो अद्याभरद्वसुर व्य उच्छा दूहितर दिवः |यो व्य उच्छः सहीयसि सत्यश्रवसि वाय्ये सुजाते अश्वसूऋते ||
sā no adyābharadvasur vy ucchā duhitar divaḥ | yo vy aucchaḥ sahīyasi satyaśravasi vāyye sujāte aśvasūnṛte ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sā: that
    • no: for us
    • adyā: today
    • abharad: may bring
    • vasur: wealth
    • vy: may be
    • ucchā: elevated
    • duhitar: daughter
    • dvaḥ: of heaven
    • yo: who
    • vy: may be
    • aucchaḥ: elevated
    • sahīyasi: you endure
    • satyaśravasi: of true fame
    • vāyye: in the wind
    • sujāte: well-born
    • aśvasūnṛte: in the truth of horses
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer for prosperity and well-being.
    • The speaker invokes a divine force to bring wealth and blessings into their life.
    • The mention of "daughter of heaven" symbolizes purity and divine grace.
    • The phrase "who may be elevated" suggests a desire for upliftment and success.
    • It emphasizes the importance of truth and integrity in achieving one's goals.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be recited by entrepreneurs seeking success and ethical growth.
    • Students may chant this mantra before exams to invoke clarity of thought and success in their endeavors.
    • Families may use this mantra during rituals to seek blessings for prosperity and harmony in their household.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine assistance in achieving prosperity and success through truthfulness.
    • It serves as a reminder that integrity and ethical behavior are essential in all aspects of life.
    • This mantra can be applicable in various real-life scenarios, including personal growth, professional success, and family well-being.
