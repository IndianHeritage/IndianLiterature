याभिर अङ्गिरो मनसा निरण्यथो 'ग्रम् गच्छथो विवरे गोर्णसः |याभिर मनुं शूरम् इषा समावतं ताभिर ऊषु ऊतिभिर अश्विनाऽ गतम् ||
yābhir aṅgiro manasā niraṇyatho 'graṁ gacchatho vivare goarṇasaḥ | yābhir manuṁ śūram iṣā samāvataṁ tābhir ū ṣu ūtibhir aśvinā gatam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yābhir: By whom
    • aṅgiro: The Angiras (a group of sages)
    • manasā: With the mind
    • niraṇyatho: Without any hindrance
    • 'graṁ: To the highest
    • gacchatho: You go
    • vivare: In the open space
    • goarṇasaḥ: The cows (or wealth)
    • yābhir: By whom
    • manuṁ: The man (or human)
    • śūram: The brave
    • iṣā: With power
    • samāvataṁ: To be gathered
    • tābhir: By those
    • ū: O
    • ṣu: With
    • ūtibhir: With strength
    • aśvinā: By the Ashvins (the twin gods)
    • gatam: Having gone
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the divine guidance provided by the Angiras sages and the Ashvins, who are known for their healing and protective qualities.
    • It emphasizes the importance of mental clarity and focus, suggesting that with the right mindset, one can overcome obstacles and reach their highest potential.
    • The reference to "cows" symbolizes wealth and prosperity, indicating that divine blessings can lead to abundance.
    • The mantra encourages individuals to seek strength and bravery, aligning themselves with divine forces to achieve their goals.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, this mantra can inspire individuals to approach challenges with a clear mind and determination, much like a team working together to achieve a common goal.
    • In personal development, it can serve as a reminder to harness one's inner strength and seek guidance from mentors or wise individuals, akin to how athletes train under the guidance of coaches.
    • In times of adversity, invoking the spirit of the Ashvins can motivate individuals to find innovative solutions, similar to how entrepreneurs adapt to market changes.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of divine guidance, mental clarity, and the pursuit of strength in overcoming life's challenges.
    • Its application in real life is vast, from personal growth to professional success, reminding us that with the right mindset and support, we can achieve our highest aspirations.
    • Ultimately, it encourages a harmonious relationship with the divine, fostering a sense of community and shared purpose in our endeavors.
