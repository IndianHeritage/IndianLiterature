एकं चमसं चतुरः कृतोतन तद् वो देवाः अब्रुवन् तद् वा आगमम् |सौधन्वना यद्येवाकरिष्यथ साकं देवैर्यज्ञीयासो भविष्यथ ||
ekaṁ camasaṁ caturaḥ kṛṇotana tad vo devā abruvan tad va āgamam | saudhanvanā yady evā kariṣyatha sākaṁ devair yajñiyāso bhaviṣyatha ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ekaṁ: one
    • camasaṁ: cup or vessel
    • caturaḥ: four
    • kṛṇotana: they create or make
    • tad: that
    • vo: for you
    • devā: the gods
    • abruvan: have spoken
    • tad: that
    • va: or
    • āgamam: the coming or arrival
    • saudhanvanā: with wealth or prosperity
    • yady: if
    • evā: indeed
    • kariṣyatha: you will perform
    • sākaṁ: together
    • devair: with the gods
    • yajñiyāso: of the sacrifices
    • bhaviṣyatha: you will become
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about unity and collaboration in the context of rituals and offerings to the divine.
    • The phrase "ekaṁ camasaṁ caturaḥ" suggests the idea of a single vessel being filled by four, symbolizing the importance of collective effort.
    • The gods have communicated that if one performs their duties together with them, they will be blessed with prosperity and abundance.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, a team working together on a project can be seen as "one vessel" where each member contributes their unique skills (the "four") to achieve a common goal.
    • In community service, when individuals come together to help those in need, they embody the essence of this mantra by pooling their resources and efforts for a greater cause.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its emphasis on unity, collaboration, and the importance of performing actions with a sense of purpose and togetherness.
    • In real life, it teaches us that by working together and aligning our efforts with a higher purpose, we can achieve greater success and prosperity.
    • This mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as teamwork in professional environments, community initiatives, and even family gatherings where collective effort leads to shared joy and fulfillment.
