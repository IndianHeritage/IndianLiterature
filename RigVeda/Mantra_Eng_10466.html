वातस्य नु महिमानं रथस्य रुजन्न एति स्तनयन्न अस्य घोषः |दिव्स्पृग यात्य अरुणानि कृत्वन्न उतो एति पृथिव्याः रेणुम अस्यन् ||
vātasya nu mahimānaṁ rathasya rujann eti stanayann asya ghoṣaḥ | divispṛg yāty aruṇāni kṛṇvann uto eti pṛthivyā reṇum asyan ||
  • vātasya nu mahimānaṁ: This phrase refers to the greatness or magnificence of the wind (vāta). In real life, we can relate this to the power of nature, such as how strong winds can shape landscapes, influence weather patterns, and even affect human activities.
  • rathasya rujann eti: This part speaks about the chariot (ratha) that moves swiftly. Chariots symbolize progress and movement in life. For example, in modern terms, this can be likened to vehicles that facilitate our journeys, representing the drive towards our goals and aspirations.
  • stanayann asya ghoṣaḥ: This translates to the sound or roar that arises from the movement of the chariot. The sound can be seen as a metaphor for the impact of our actions. In life, the consequences of our decisions often resonate, much like the sound of a chariot moving through the land.
  • divispṛg yāty aruṇāni kṛṇvann: This phrase indicates the journey towards the heavens or the divine (divispṛg) and the creation of dawn (aruṇāni). It symbolizes hope and new beginnings. In everyday life, this can be related to the pursuit of knowledge and enlightenment, akin to the dawn of a new day bringing light and clarity.
  • uto eti pṛthivyā reṇum asyan: This part refers to the earth (pṛthivyā) and the dust (reṇum) that is stirred up. It signifies the connection between the heavens and the earth, illustrating how our actions on earth can have celestial significance. In practical terms, this can be seen in how our environmental actions impact the planet and future generations.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the interplay between nature, human endeavor, and the cosmos. It emphasizes the significance of our actions and their reverberations in the world around us. In real life, this can be applied to our daily choices, encouraging us to act with awareness and responsibility, recognizing that our movements—like the chariot—create ripples that affect both our immediate environment and the broader universe. This mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of all things and the importance of striving for progress while being mindful of our impact on the world.
