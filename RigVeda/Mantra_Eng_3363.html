आ नः स्तुत उप वाजेभिर ऊती इन्द्र याहि हरिभिर मंदसानः |तिरश्चिद आर्यः सवना पुरूण्य आङ्गूषेभिर गृणानः सत्यराधाः ||
ā naḥ stuta upa vājebhir ūtī indra yāhi haribhir mandasānaḥ | tiraś cid aryaḥ savanā purūṇy āṅgūṣebhir gṛṇānaḥ satyarādhāḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā naḥ: "to us" or "for us"
    • stuta: "praised" or "worshipped"
    • upa: "near" or "approaching"
    • vājebhir: "with offerings" or "with rewards"
    • ūtī: "prosperity" or "wealth"
    • indra: "O Indra" (the king of gods)
    • yāhi: "come" or "approach"
    • haribhir: "with the swift ones" (referring to horses or swift beings)
    • mandasānaḥ: "seated gently" or "calmly"
    • tiraś cid: "from all sides" or "from every direction"
    • aryaḥ: "noble" or "worthy"
    • savanā: "sacrificial offerings" or "praises"
    • purūṇy: "many" or "abundant"
    • āṅgūṣebhir: "with our fingers" (symbolizing action)
    • gṛṇānaḥ: "singing" or "chanting"
    • satyarādhāḥ: "those who are established in truth"
  • Overall Meaning: This mantra is a prayer to Indra, the king of gods, asking for blessings and prosperity. It emphasizes the importance of approaching the divine with offerings and praises, seeking abundance and truth in our lives. The invocation of Indra signifies a call for strength and support in our endeavors, while the mention of "noble" and "truth" highlights the values of integrity and righteousness.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be applied when seeking success in ventures. Entrepreneurs often pray for guidance and prosperity, similar to how this mantra seeks blessings from Indra.
    • In personal life, individuals may recite this mantra during challenging times, asking for strength and support to overcome obstacles, much like how one would seek help from a mentor or a wise friend.
    • During community gatherings or festivals, this mantra can be chanted to invoke collective blessings, fostering a sense of unity and shared purpose among participants.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine support and the pursuit of truth and prosperity. It serves as a reminder to approach life with humility and sincerity, seeking blessings not just for oneself but for the community as a whole. In real-life scenarios, it encourages individuals to act with integrity, to seek help when needed, and to celebrate the abundance of life through collective efforts and shared values.
