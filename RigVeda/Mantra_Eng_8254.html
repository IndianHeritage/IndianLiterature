महाम् असि सोम ज्येष्ठ उग्राणाम् इन्द ओजिष्टः |युध्वा सञ्ज चश्वज जिगेथ ||
mahām̐ asi soma jyeṣṭha ugrāṇām inda ojiṣṭhaḥ | yudhvā sañ chaśvaj jigetha ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • mahām̐ - great, powerful
    • asi - you are
    • soma - the divine nectar or drink, often associated with immortality
    • jyeṣṭha - the eldest or the most powerful
    • ugrāṇām - of the fierce or strong
    • inda - the lord or the deity
    • ojiṣṭhaḥ - the most energetic or powerful
    • yudhvā - by fighting or engaging
    • sañ - together
    • chaśvaj - with strength or vigor
    • jigetha - may conquer or overcome
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the essence of strength and power, emphasizing the importance of unity and collective effort in overcoming challenges.
    • The reference to "soma" symbolizes the pursuit of higher consciousness and spiritual nourishment, suggesting that true strength comes from both physical and spiritual realms.
    • The phrase "yudhvā sañ chaśvaj jigetha" highlights the idea that through collaboration and shared strength, one can conquer adversities.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate environment, teams that work together harmoniously often achieve greater success than individuals working in isolation. This reflects the mantra's emphasis on collective strength.
    • In sports, teams that train together and support each other are more likely to win championships, illustrating the power of unity and shared effort.
    • In community service, groups that come together to address social issues can create significant change, embodying the spirit of overcoming challenges through collaboration.
  • Conclusion:
    • The mantra signifies the importance of harnessing both individual and collective strength to overcome obstacles in life.
    • It teaches us that true power lies not just in personal prowess but in the ability to unite and work towards a common goal.
    • This principle is applicable in various aspects of life, including personal relationships, professional settings, and community initiatives, encouraging us to seek collaboration and support in our endeavors.
