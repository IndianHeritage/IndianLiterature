तव शरीरं पतयिष्ण्वर्वन तव चित्तं वात इव ध्रजीमान् |तव श्रिङ्गाणि विष्ठिता पुरुत्रारण्येषु जर्भुराणां चरन्ति ||
tava śarīram patayiṣṇv arvan tava cittaṁ vāta iva dhrajīmān | tava śṛṅgāṇi viṣṭhitā purutrāraṇyeṣu jarbhurāṇā caranti ||
  • tava śarīram patayiṣṇv arvan: This phrase can be translated as "Your body, O Arvan, is like a vessel." Here, "tava" means "your," "śarīram" means "body," and "patayiṣṇv" refers to something that is stable or firm. "Arvan" is a name that can refer to a deity or a person, often associated with strength and valor.
  • tava cittaṁ vāta iva dhrajīmān: This translates to "Your mind is like the wind, strong and powerful." "Cittaṁ" means "mind," "vāta" means "wind," and "dhrajīmān" refers to something that is mighty or powerful. This suggests that the mind has the ability to be both gentle and forceful, much like the wind.
  • tava śṛṅgāṇi viṣṭhitā purutrāraṇyeṣu: This can be interpreted as "Your horns are established in all directions." "Śṛṅgāṇi" means "horns," "viṣṭhitā" means "established," and "purutrāraṇyeṣu" refers to being present in various places or directions. This symbolizes strength and the ability to protect oneself from all sides.
  • jarbhurāṇā caranti: This phrase means "the creatures that move about." "Jarbhurāṇā" refers to beings or creatures, and "caranti" means "they move." This indicates the dynamic nature of life and the constant movement of beings in the world.

Conclusion: This mantra emphasizes the interconnectedness of body, mind, and the environment. It highlights the strength and stability of the physical form, the power of the mind, and the protective nature of one's presence in the world. In real life, this can be applied to various scenarios such as personal development, where one must cultivate a strong body and mind to navigate life's challenges. It also speaks to the importance of being aware of one's surroundings and the need for protection and strength in facing adversities. The mantra serves as a reminder to harness inner strength and remain grounded while being adaptable like the wind, ultimately leading to a balanced and resilient life.
