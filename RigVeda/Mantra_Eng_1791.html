विष्वानि भद्रां मरुतो रथेषु वो मिथस्पृध्येव तविषाण्य आहिताः |अंसेष्वा वः प्रपतेषु खादयोऽक्षो वश चक्रा समया वि वावृते ||
viśvāni bhadrā maruto ratheṣu vo mithaspṛdhyeva taviṣāṇy āhitā | aṁseṣv ā vaḥ prapatheṣu khādayo 'kṣo vaś cakrā samayā vi vāvṛte ||
  • Translation: "O Maruts, may you, who are endowed with strength, grant us all auspiciousness. Just as the rays of the sun spread out, may your blessings be upon us, and may we be protected from all harm."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • viśvāni: all, universal
    • bhadrā: auspicious, good
    • maruto: referring to the Maruts, the storm deities
    • ratheṣu: in the chariots, symbolizing movement and progress
    • mithaspṛdhyeva: like the rays of the sun, indicating brightness and positivity
    • taviṣāṇy: strength, power
    • āhitā: placed, established
    • aṁseṣv: in the limbs, suggesting protection
    • khādayo: to consume, to take away
    • 'kṣo: harm, injury
    • vaś: under control, in command
    • cakrā: wheel, symbolizing cycles and continuity
    • samayā: in time, at the right moment
    • vi vāvṛte: may it manifest, may it come forth
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community gathering, invoking the blessings of the Maruts can symbolize the collective strength and unity of the group, aiming for auspicious outcomes in their endeavors.
    • During a challenging project at work, a team might recite this mantra to seek protection from obstacles and to ensure that their efforts lead to success.
    • In personal life, individuals may chant this mantra during times of uncertainty or fear, seeking strength and auspiciousness in their decisions and actions.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its invocation of strength and auspiciousness from the Maruts, emphasizing the importance of collective effort and divine support in overcoming challenges. It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal struggles to communal endeavors, reminding us that with strength and positivity, we can navigate through life's obstacles and achieve our goals. The mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the interconnectedness of our actions and the blessings we seek in our journey.
