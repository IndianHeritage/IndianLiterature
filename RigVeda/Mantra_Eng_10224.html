ओर्व अप्रा अमर्त्याऽ निवतो देव्य उ1द्वतः |ज्योतिषा बाधते तमः ||
orv aprā amartyā nivato devy u1dvataḥ | jyotiṣā bādhate tamaḥ ||
  • Mantra: "orv aprā amartyā nivato devy u1dvataḥ | jyotiṣā bādhate tamaḥ ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • orv: This can be interpreted as "to the heights" or "to the vastness."
    • aprā: Means "unfailing" or "without limitation."
    • amartyā: Refers to "immortal" or "eternal."
    • nivato: Translates to "calm" or "peaceful."
    • devy: Pertains to "divine" or "goddess-like."
    • u1dvataḥ: Can be understood as "rising" or "ascending."
    • jyotiṣā: Means "by light" or "through illumination."
    • bādhate: Translates to "overcomes" or "dispels."
    • tamaḥ: Refers to "darkness" or "ignorance."
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks to the journey towards enlightenment and the overcoming of ignorance.
    • The phrase "to the heights" suggests striving for higher knowledge and understanding.
    • "Unfailing" and "immortal" indicate that true knowledge and enlightenment are eternal pursuits.
    • The mention of "calm" and "divine" suggests that this journey is not just about knowledge but also about attaining peace and a higher state of being.
    • "By light" signifies that knowledge and wisdom illuminate the path, dispelling the darkness of ignorance.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In education, the pursuit of knowledge can be seen as a journey to "the heights," where students strive to overcome ignorance through learning.
    • In personal development, individuals often seek "calm" and "peace" through practices like meditation, which aligns with the idea of attaining a divine state of being.
    • In a broader societal context, movements that promote awareness and understanding, such as environmental activism, can be viewed as efforts to "overcome darkness" by spreading knowledge and fostering enlightenment.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of seeking knowledge and enlightenment as a means to overcome ignorance and darkness in our lives.
    • It encourages individuals to strive for higher understanding and to embrace the light of knowledge, which can lead to personal and collective growth.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various fields such as education, personal development, and social awareness, highlighting the importance of continuous learning and the pursuit of peace and understanding.
