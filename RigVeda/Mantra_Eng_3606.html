को वस त्राताऽवसवः को वरूताऽद्यावाभूमी अदिते त्रासीतां नः |सहीयसो वरुण मित्र मर्तात को वोऽध्वरे वरिवो धाति देवाः ||
ko vas trātā vasavaḥ ko varūtā dyāvābhūmī adite trāsīthāṁ naḥ | sahīyaso varuṇa mitra martāt ko vo 'dhvare varivo dhāti devāḥ ||
  • Translation: The mantra can be translated as: "Who is the protector, the Vasus? Who is the one who brings us together, the heavens and the earth? O Aditi, may you protect us from fear. O Varuna and Mitra, who among you is the one who grants us strength? Who among the gods supports our rituals?"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ko: Who
    • vas: Protector
    • trātā: To save or protect
    • vasavaḥ: The Vasus, a group of deities associated with natural elements
    • ko: Who
    • varūtā: The one who brings together
    • dyāvābhūmī: Heavens and earth
    • adite: Aditi, the mother of gods
    • trāsīthāṁ: May you protect us from fear
    • naḥ: Us
    • sahīyaso: Of those who are strong
    • varuṇa: Varuna, the god of cosmic order
    • mitra: Mitra, the god of friendship and contracts
    • martāt: From mortality
    • ko: Who
    • vo: To you
    • 'dhvare: In the rituals
    • varivo: The one who grants strength
    • dhāti: Supports
    • devāḥ: The gods
  • Significance:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of divine protection and support in our lives.
    • It acknowledges the interconnectedness of the universe, represented by the heavens and the earth.
    • The invocation of Aditi, Varuna, and Mitra highlights the need for strength, unity, and cosmic order.
  • Real-Life Application:
    • In times of uncertainty or fear, individuals can recite this mantra to seek protection and reassurance from higher powers.
    • In community gatherings or rituals, invoking the support of deities can foster a sense of unity and collective strength.
    • This mantra can also serve as a reminder of the importance of maintaining harmony with nature and the universe.
  • Conclusion:
    • The mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the need for divine guidance and protection in our lives.
    • It encourages individuals to seek strength from the universe and to foster connections with others.
    • Ultimately, it highlights the significance of rituals and the role of the divine in our everyday experiences.
