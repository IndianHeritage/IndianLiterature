नानानं वा उ नो धियो वि व्रतानि जनानाम् |तक्षाऽरिष्टं ऋतं भीषग् ब्रह्मा सुन्वन्तम् इच्छतीन्द्रायन्दो परि श्रव ||
nānānaṁ vā u no dhiyo vi vratāni janānām | takṣā riṣṭaṁ rutam bhiṣag brahmā sunvantam icchatīndrāyendo pari srava ||
  • nānānaṁ vā u no dhiyo vi vratāni janānām: This phrase can be translated to "Let our thoughts be diverse, yet unified in purpose." It emphasizes the importance of having varied perspectives and ideas while working towards a common goal. In real life, this can be seen in team projects where individuals bring different skills and viewpoints to achieve a shared objective.
  • takṣā riṣṭaṁ rutam bhiṣag: This part translates to "The skilled craftsman creates the right path." It highlights the value of expertise and craftsmanship in shaping outcomes. For example, in construction, skilled architects and engineers collaborate to design and build structures that are both functional and aesthetically pleasing.
  • brahmā sunvantam icchatīndrāyendo pari srava: This can be interpreted as "May the divine essence flow abundantly." It suggests a desire for prosperity and abundance in life. In practical terms, this can relate to the pursuit of knowledge, wealth, and well-being, where individuals seek to enrich their lives and the lives of others through their actions and intentions.

  • Significance: This mantra encapsulates the essence of collaboration, skill, and the pursuit of abundance. It serves as a reminder that while diversity in thought is valuable, unity in purpose is essential for success.
  • Real-life Application: In various fields such as business, education, and community service, this mantra can be applied to foster teamwork, encourage innovation, and promote a shared vision. For instance, in a corporate setting, diverse teams can brainstorm ideas that lead to innovative products, while in community projects, varied skills can help address local issues effectively.
