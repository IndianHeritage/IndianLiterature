अस्य प्रजावतī गृहे 'सश्चन्ती दिवे-दिवे |इला धेनुमती दुहे ||
asya prajāvatī gṛhe 'saścantī dive-dive | iḻā dhenumatī duhe ||
  • Mantra: "asya prajāvatī gṛhe 'saścantī dive-dive | iḻā dhenumatī duhe ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • asya: of this
    • prajāvatī: fruitful, prosperous, or one who bears offspring
    • gṛhe: in the home
    • saścantī: with peace or tranquility
    • dive-dive: day by day
    • iḻā: the goddess of speech or eloquence
    • dhenumatī: one who is rich in cows or wealth
    • duhe: to milk or to extract
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes a sense of prosperity and abundance in the household.
    • The phrase "asya prajāvatī gṛhe" suggests that the home is blessed with fruitful offspring and prosperity.
    • "saścantī dive-dive" emphasizes the importance of peace and tranquility that should be present in the home every day.
    • The mention of "iḻā dhenumatī" indicates the presence of wealth and resources, symbolized by cows, which were considered valuable in ancient times.
    • Overall, the mantra reflects a desire for a harmonious and prosperous life, where wealth and peace coexist.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a family setting, this mantra can be seen as a blessing for children to grow up in a nurturing environment, where they can thrive and contribute positively to society.
    • In a business context, it can symbolize the growth of a company that fosters a peaceful work environment, leading to productivity and success.
    • In community gatherings, invoking this mantra can promote unity and cooperation among members, ensuring that everyone works together for common prosperity.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of nurturing a peaceful and prosperous environment in our homes and communities.
    • Its significance lies in the holistic approach to life, where material wealth, emotional well-being, and social harmony are interconnected.
    • By applying the principles of this mantra in daily life, individuals can strive for a balanced existence that promotes growth, peace, and abundance.
