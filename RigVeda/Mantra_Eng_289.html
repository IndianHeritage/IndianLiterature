उद उत्तमं मुमुग्धि नो विपाशं मध्यं चृत || अवाधमानी जीवसे ||
ud uttamam mumugdhi no vi pāśam madhyamaṁ cṛta | avādhamāni jīvase ||
  • ud uttamam: This phrase can be translated as "to the highest" or "to the best." It signifies the aspiration towards excellence and the pursuit of the highest ideals in life.
  • mumugdhi: This term refers to "liberation" or "freedom." It emphasizes the importance of seeking liberation from the bonds that hold us back, whether they are physical, mental, or emotional.
  • no vi pāśam: This translates to "may we not be bound" or "may we not be caught in the net." It suggests a desire to avoid entrapment in life's challenges or distractions that prevent us from achieving our goals.
  • madhyamaṁ cṛta: This phrase means "the middle path" or "the balanced approach." It highlights the significance of moderation and balance in our actions and decisions.
  • avādhamāni: This term can be understood as "to be free from obstacles" or "to be unencumbered." It reflects the idea of overcoming barriers that hinder our progress.
  • jīvase: This word means "to live" or "to exist." It emphasizes the importance of living a life that is meaningful and aligned with our highest aspirations.

In conclusion, this mantra encapsulates a profound message about striving for the highest ideals while seeking liberation from the constraints that bind us. It encourages individuals to pursue a balanced and moderate approach in life, avoiding extremes that can lead to difficulties. The significance of this mantra is applicable in various real-life scenarios, such as:


  • In personal development, it inspires individuals to set high goals while remaining grounded and balanced in their pursuits.
  • In professional settings, it serves as a reminder to seek excellence in work while avoiding burnout and maintaining a healthy work-life balance.
  • In relationships, it encourages open communication and understanding, helping individuals to navigate conflicts without becoming trapped in negativity.

Ultimately, this mantra serves as a guiding principle for living a fulfilling and liberated life, encouraging us to rise above challenges and strive for our highest potential.
