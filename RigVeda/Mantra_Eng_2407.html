विष्वान्य अन्यो भुवना जजाना विश्वम् अन्यो अभिचक्षाण एति |सोमापूषणाव अवतं धियम मे युवाभ्यां विश्वाः पृतना जयेम ||
viśvāny anyo bhuvanā jajāna viśvam anyo abhicakṣāṇa eti | somāpūṣaṇāv avataṁ dhiyam me yuvābhyāṁ viśvāḥ pṛtanā jayema ||
  • Translation: The mantra can be translated as: "All beings are born from the one, and the one sees all beings. May the two, the nourisher of the soma, protect my understanding, so that we may conquer all realms together."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • viśvāny: All beings or all entities.
    • anyo: Another or different.
    • bhuvanā: Worlds or realms.
    • jajāna: Born or created.
    • viśvam: The whole or entirety.
    • abhicakṣāṇa: Sees or perceives.
    • eti: Comes or approaches.
    • somāpūṣaṇāv: Nourisher of soma (a sacred drink).
    • avataṁ: Protect or guard.
    • dhiyaṁ: Understanding or intellect.
    • me: My.
    • yuvābhyāṁ: Together or in unity.
    • viśvāḥ: All or everything.
    • pṛtanā: Conquer or overcome.
    • jayema: We may conquer or achieve victory.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • This mantra emphasizes the interconnectedness of all beings. For instance, in a community, every individual contributes to the collective well-being, much like how different parts of a body work together for overall health.
    • The idea of "nourisher of soma" can be likened to the role of mentors or guides in our lives who help us grow and understand our potential, similar to how a teacher nourishes a student's intellect.
    • The call for protection of understanding can be seen in the context of seeking wisdom in decision-making, where one relies on the guidance of experienced individuals to navigate complex situations.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for unity and collective strength. It teaches us that while we may be different, we are all part of a greater whole. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios such as teamwork in workplaces, community service, and even in family dynamics. By recognizing our interconnectedness and supporting one another, we can achieve greater success and harmony in our lives. The mantra encourages us to seek wisdom and protection for our understanding, reminding us that collaboration and mutual support are essential for overcoming challenges and achieving our goals.
