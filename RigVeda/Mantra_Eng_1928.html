अस्मे सा वाम माध्वी रातिर अस्तु स्तोमं हिनोतम मान्यस्य कारोः |अनु यद वां श्रवस्याऽसुदानू सुवीर्याय चर्षणयो मदन्ति ||
asme sā vām mādhvī rātir astu stomaṁ hinotam mānyasya kāroḥ | anu yad vāṁ śravasyā sudānū suvīryāya carṣaṇayo madanti ||
  • asme sā vām mādhvī rātir astu: This phrase can be translated to "May this sweet night be ours." Here, "mādhvī" refers to something sweet or pleasant, and "rātir" means night. In real life, this can symbolize the importance of having peaceful and joyful moments in our lives, akin to enjoying a serene evening with loved ones.
  • stomaṁ hinotam mānyasya kāroḥ: This translates to "Let the best praise be offered to the one who is worthy." "Stomaṁ" refers to praise or hymn, and "mānyasya kāroḥ" indicates someone who is deserving of respect. In practical terms, this can relate to recognizing and appreciating the efforts of individuals in our lives, such as teachers, mentors, or leaders who guide us.
  • anu yad vāṁ śravasyā sudānū: This means "As you are known for your glorious deeds." "Śravasyā" refers to fame or glory, and "sudānū" implies good or noble actions. This can be seen in real life when we acknowledge the achievements of those around us, such as community leaders or activists who work tirelessly for the betterment of society.
  • suvīryāya carṣaṇayo madanti: This translates to "May they inspire us with their strength." "Suvīryāya" refers to strength or valor, and "carṣaṇayo" indicates the act of inspiring. In everyday life, this can be related to how we draw inspiration from athletes, artists, or anyone who demonstrates resilience and determination in the face of challenges.

  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of appreciating the sweetness of life, recognizing the contributions of worthy individuals, celebrating glorious deeds, and drawing inspiration from strength. In real life, it encourages us to cultivate gratitude, respect, and motivation, fostering a positive environment in our personal and communal lives. By embodying these principles, we can create a harmonious and uplifting atmosphere that benefits everyone around us.
