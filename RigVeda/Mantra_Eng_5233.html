त्रिश्चिदक्तोः प्र चिकीतुर्वसूनि त्वे अन्तर दाशुषे मार्त्याय |मनुष्वद अग्न इह यक्षि देवान भवानो दूतो अभिशस्तिपाव ||
triś cid aktoḥ pra cikitur vasūni tve antar dāśuṣe martyāya | manuṣvad agna iha yakṣi devān bhavā no dūto abhiśastipāvā ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • triś: three
    • cid: indeed
    • aktoḥ: of the one who is not to be approached
    • pra: towards
    • cikitur: has created
    • vasūni: wealth or good things
    • tve: for you
    • antar: within
    • dāśuṣe: for the one who is mortal
    • martyāya: to the mortal
    • manuṣvad: like a human
    • agna: O Agni (Fire)
    • iha: here
    • yakṣi: may you bestow
    • devān: the gods
    • bhavā: may you become
    • no: for us
    • dūto: a messenger
    • abhiśastipāvā: who is pure and auspicious
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes Agni, the fire god, who is seen as a mediator between humans and the divine.
    • The phrase "three indeed of the one who is not to be approached" suggests the presence of a higher power that is beyond human comprehension.
    • "Wealth or good things" indicates the blessings and prosperity that one seeks in life.
    • The reference to "the mortal" emphasizes the human condition and the need for divine assistance in navigating life's challenges.
    • By calling upon Agni, the mantra seeks to establish a connection with the divine, asking for blessings and guidance.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In times of difficulty, individuals often seek spiritual guidance or blessings, similar to how this mantra seeks the favor of Agni.
    • In various cultures, fire is used in rituals to symbolize purification and the connection between the earthly and the divine.
    • People may light candles or perform rituals to invoke blessings for health, prosperity, and protection, reflecting the essence of this mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the importance of seeking divine support in our mortal lives.
    • It signifies the belief that through spiritual practices, one can attract positive energies and blessings.
    • In real-life scenarios, it encourages individuals to maintain a connection with the divine, especially during challenging times, fostering hope and resilience.
