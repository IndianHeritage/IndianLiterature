मन्त्र: उता स्मास्य तण्यतोर्व इव द्योः ऋघायतो अभियुजो भयन्ते |यदा सहस्रं अभि शीम अयोधीद दुर्वर्तुः स्मा भवति भीम ऋञ्जन ||
uta smāsya tanyator iva dyor ṛghāyato abhiyujo bhayante | yadā sahasram abhi ṣīm ayodhīd durvartuḥ smā bhavati bhīma ṛñjan ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • uta: and
    • smāsya: of the dead
    • tanyator: of the body
    • iva: like
    • dyor: of the sky
    • ṛghāyato: of the powerful
    • abhiyujo: those who are united
    • bhayante: they become fearful
    • yadā: when
    • sahasram: a thousand
    • abhi: towards
    • ṣīm: protection
    • ayodhīd: of the warriors
    • durvartuḥ: difficult to overcome
    • smā: indeed
    • bhavati: becomes
    • bhīma: terrible
    • ṛñjan: causing fear
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the fear that arises when powerful forces unite against a common enemy.
    • The imagery of the body of the dead being compared to the sky suggests a vastness and an overwhelming presence.
    • It highlights the strength of unity among warriors and the fear that can be instilled in adversaries when they face a united front.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, when employees come together to tackle a challenging project, their collective effort can lead to success that may intimidate competitors.
    • In sports, a team that plays cohesively can often overpower a more talented but disorganized opponent, demonstrating the power of unity.
    • Historically, nations that have united against a common threat have often been able to overcome formidable challenges, such as during World War II.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its emphasis on the strength found in unity and the fear it can instill in adversaries.
    • It serves as a reminder that when individuals come together with a common purpose, they can achieve great things and overcome significant obstacles.
    • This principle is applicable in various aspects of life, including personal relationships, professional environments, and community efforts, reinforcing the idea that collaboration is key to success.
