एव ते अग्नि सुमतिं चकानो नविष्टाय नवमं त्रसदस्युः |यो मे गिरस तुविजातस्य पूर्वीर युक्तेनाभि त्र्यरुणो गृणाति ||
evā te agne sumatiṁ cakāno naviṣṭhāya navamaṁ trasadasyuḥ | yo me giras tuvijātasya pūrvīr yuktenābhi tryaruṇo gṛṇāti ||
  • evā te agne: This phrase translates to "O Agni," referring to the Vedic fire god, Agni, who symbolizes energy, light, and the divine messenger between humans and the gods.
  • sumatiṁ cakāno: This means "I have created good thoughts." It emphasizes the importance of positive thinking and intentions in one's life.
  • naviṣṭhāya navamaṁ trasadasyuḥ: This can be interpreted as "to the one who is the ninth, the one who is the protector." It suggests a connection to a higher power or divine protection that guides and supports individuals.
  • yo me giras: This translates to "who speaks to me." It indicates a communication or connection with the divine, highlighting the importance of listening to spiritual guidance.
  • tuvijātasya pūrvīr: This means "of the one who is born from the divine." It signifies the idea of being connected to a higher source of wisdom and knowledge.
  • yuktenābhi: This translates to "with the right connection." It emphasizes the need for alignment and harmony in one's actions and thoughts.
  • tryaruṇo gṛṇāti: This means "the one who sings the praises." It reflects the importance of expressing gratitude and reverence towards the divine and the forces of nature.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda encapsulates the essence of seeking divine guidance, fostering positive thoughts, and maintaining a connection with higher powers. In real life, it serves as a reminder to cultivate good intentions, listen to our inner voice, and express gratitude. By embodying these principles, individuals can navigate challenges with a sense of purpose and clarity, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling and harmonious life. The significance of this mantra lies in its ability to inspire individuals to align their thoughts and actions with their higher selves, promoting personal growth and spiritual development.
