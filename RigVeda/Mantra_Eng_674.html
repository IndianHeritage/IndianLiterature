क्राणā रुद्रैभिर वसुभिः पुरोहितो होतā निषत्तो रयिषाल् अमर्त्यः | रथो न विक्ष्व ऋञ्जसाना आयुषु व्य āनुषग वारीय देव ऋण्वति ||
krāṇā rudrebhir vasubhiḥ purohito hotā niṣatto rayiṣāḻ amartyaḥ | ratho na vikṣv ṛñjasāna āyuṣu vy ānuṣag vāryā deva ṛṇvati ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • krāṇā: (by) the actions
    • rudrebhir: (of) the Rudras
    • vasubhiḥ: (with) the Vasus
    • purohito: (the) priest or mediator
    • hotā: (the) one who invokes or calls
    • niṣatto: (with) firm resolve
    • rayiṣāḻ: (with) wealth or prosperity
    • amartyaḥ: (the) immortal
    • ratho: (the) chariot
    • na: (not)
    • vikṣv: (to be) disturbed
    • ṛñjasāna: (by) the swift
    • āyuṣu: (in) life
    • vy: (with) the divine
    • ānuṣag: (to) follow
    • vāryā: (the) powerful
    • deva: (the) gods
    • ṛṇvati: (to) grant or bestow
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the powers of the Rudras and Vasus, who are deities associated with storm and wealth, respectively.
    • The phrase "purohito hotā" emphasizes the role of a priest or mediator who connects the divine with the earthly realm, highlighting the importance of rituals in seeking blessings.
    • The mantra speaks of firm resolve and the pursuit of wealth, suggesting that one should approach life with determination and a clear purpose.
    • The mention of "amartyaḥ" indicates a quest for immortality or eternal values, which can be interpreted as striving for lasting impact and legacy.
    • The imagery of a chariot ("ratho") symbolizes the journey of life, where one should remain undisturbed and focused despite challenges.
    • The concluding part emphasizes the divine support in one's endeavors, suggesting that with the right intentions and actions, one can achieve prosperity and fulfillment.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, this mantra can be applied to the pursuit of success where teamwork (Rudras and Vasus) and leadership (purohito) are essential for achieving goals.
    • In personal life, it encourages individuals to maintain a strong resolve in their ambitions, whether it be in education, career, or personal development.
    • During challenging times, such as economic downturns, this mantra serves as a reminder to stay focused and undisturbed, seeking divine guidance and support.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra from the Rigveda encapsulates the essence of seeking divine support in our endeavors while emphasizing the importance of determination and focus.
    • Its significance lies in the reminder that with the right intentions and actions, one can navigate life's challenges and achieve prosperity.
    • In real life, it encourages individuals to engage in rituals, maintain resolve, and seek divine blessings in their personal and professional journeys.
