अर्वाण्चो अद्याऽ भवता यजत्रा आ वो हार्दि भयमानो व्ययेयम् |त्राध्वं नो देवाऽ निजुरो वृक्षस्य त्राध्वं कर्ताद अवपदो यजत्राः ||
arvāñco adyā bhavatā yajatrā ā vo hārdi bhayamāno vyayeyam | trādhvaṁ no devā nijuro vṛkasya trādhvaṁ kartād avapado yajatrāḥ ||
  • Mantra: "arvāñco adyā bhavatā yajatrā ā vo hārdi bhayamāno vyayeyam | trādhvaṁ no devā nijuro vṛkasya trādhvaṁ kartād avapado yajatrāḥ ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • arvāñco: of the strong or powerful
    • adyā: today
    • bhavatā: by you (the divine)
    • yajatrā: the one who performs the sacrifice
    • ā: to
    • vo: you (plural)
    • hārdi: in the heart
    • bhayamāno: being fearful
    • vyayeyam: may we be liberated
    • trādhvaṁ: protect us
    • no: our
    • devā: O gods
    • nijuro: from the evil
    • vṛkasya: of the wolf
    • kartād: from the doer
    • avapado: from the fall
    • yajatrāḥ: the one who performs the sacrifice
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra is a prayer for protection and liberation from fear and evil.
    • The invocation of the divine emphasizes the importance of seeking help from higher powers in times of distress.
    • The reference to "the wolf" symbolizes danger or malevolent forces that threaten safety.
    • In real life, this mantra can be related to situations where individuals face challenges or fears, such as during a difficult period in life, a health crisis, or when facing adversities in relationships or work.
    • For example, someone starting a new job may recite this mantra to seek divine protection from the uncertainties and pressures of the new environment.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its universal appeal for safety and guidance.
    • It teaches us to acknowledge our fears and seek support from the divine, reminding us that we are not alone in our struggles.
    • In practical terms, this mantra can be applied in daily life by reciting it during moments of anxiety or when facing challenges, fostering a sense of peace and reassurance.
    • Ultimately, it encourages a proactive approach to overcoming fears and adversities through faith and divine assistance.
