उद्वाम् पृक्षासो मधुमन्त इरते रथा अश्वास उषसो व्युष्टिषु |अपोर्णुवन्तस तमा आ परिवृतं स्वर्ण न शुक्रं तन्वन्त आ रजः ||
ud vām pṛkṣāso madhumanta īrate rathā aśvāsa uṣaso vyuṣṭiṣu | aporṇuvantas tama ā parīvṛtaṁ sva1r ṇa śukraṁ tanvanta ā rajaḥ ||
  • ud vām pṛkṣāso: This phrase can be interpreted as "may we rise up" or "let us elevate ourselves." It signifies the aspiration for growth and enlightenment.
  • madhumanta īrate: "Madhumanta" refers to sweetness or pleasantness, while "īrate" means to be engaged or to act. Together, it suggests engaging in sweet or positive actions.
  • rathā aśvāsa: "Rathā" means chariot, and "aśvāsa" refers to horses. This can symbolize the journey of life, where the chariot represents our path and the horses symbolize the energy and drive needed to move forward.
  • uṣaso vyuṣṭiṣu: "Uṣaso" means dawn or new beginnings, and "vyuṣṭiṣu" refers to the rising or emergence. This phrase emphasizes the importance of new beginnings and the opportunities they bring.
  • aporṇuvantas: This term can be understood as "those who are fulfilled" or "those who are complete." It highlights the idea of achieving completeness in life.
  • tama ā parīvṛtaṁ: "Tama" means darkness, and "parīvṛtaṁ" means surrounded or enveloped. This suggests the idea of overcoming darkness or ignorance.
  • sva1r ṇa śukraṁ: "Sva1r" refers to one's own or self, and "śukraṁ" means brightness or purity. This indicates the pursuit of one's own inner light or truth.
  • tanvanta ā rajaḥ: "Tanvanta" means to become or to manifest, and "rajaḥ" refers to energy or activity. This phrase encourages the manifestation of positive energy in our lives.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda encapsulates the essence of personal growth, positivity, and the pursuit of enlightenment. It encourages individuals to rise above challenges, engage in positive actions, and embrace new beginnings. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios such as personal development, professional growth, and spiritual journeys. By focusing on our inner light and striving for completeness, we can overcome darkness and manifest positive energy in our lives. This mantra serves as a reminder of the potential within each individual to create a fulfilling and meaningful life.
