तत् सवितुर्वृणीमहे वयं देवस्य भोजन् |श्रेष्टं सर्वधातमं तुरं भगस्य धीमहि ||
tat savitur vṛṇīmahe vayaṁ devasya bhojanam | śreṣṭhaṁ sarvadhātamaṁ turam bhagasya dhīmahi ||
  • tat: This word translates to "that," referring to the divine or the ultimate reality.
  • savitur: This means "of Savitar," the Sun God, symbolizing light, energy, and life.
  • vṛṇīmahe: This translates to "we choose" or "we invoke," indicating a conscious decision to seek the divine.
  • vayaṁ: This means "we," emphasizing the collective nature of the invocation.
  • devasya: This translates to "of the deity," referring to the divine presence or energy.
  • bhojanam: This means "nourishment" or "sustenance," indicating the need for spiritual and physical sustenance.
  • śreṣṭhaṁ: This translates to "the best" or "the highest," signifying the pursuit of the highest truth or goodness.
  • sarvadhātamaṁ: This means "the one who pervades all," indicating the omnipresence of the divine.
  • turam: This translates to "swift" or "quick," suggesting the immediacy of divine assistance.
  • bhagasya: This means "of fortune" or "prosperity," indicating the blessings that come from the divine.
  • dhīmahi: This translates to "we meditate" or "we contemplate," emphasizing the importance of reflection and meditation.

The mantra can be understood as a collective invocation to the divine light of Savitar, seeking nourishment and guidance. It emphasizes the importance of choosing the highest truth and meditating on the divine presence that pervades all aspects of life.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios:

  • Morning Rituals: Reciting this mantra during morning prayers can help individuals set a positive intention for the day, seeking clarity and guidance.
  • Decision Making: When faced with important decisions, reflecting on this mantra can encourage individuals to choose paths that align with higher values and truths.
  • Community Gatherings: In group settings, such as family or community gatherings, invoking this mantra can foster a sense of unity and shared purpose.

Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine guidance and nourishment. It serves as a reminder to seek the highest truth and to meditate on the omnipresent divine energy that sustains us. By incorporating this mantra into daily life, individuals can cultivate a deeper connection with the divine and enhance their spiritual journey.
