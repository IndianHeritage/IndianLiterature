अभूदु भा उ अंशवे हिरण्यं प्रति सूर्यः |व्याख्यज जिह्वयासितः ||
abhūd u bhā u aṁśave hiraṇyam prati sūryaḥ | vy akhyaj jihvayāsitaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • abhūd: It means "to become" or "to manifest."
    • u: This is a conjunction meaning "and."
    • bhā: It translates to "light" or "brightness."
    • u: Again, a conjunction meaning "and."
    • aṁśave: This means "to the one who is a part" or "to the one who is a portion."
    • hiraṇyam: This translates to "gold" or "wealth."
    • prati: It means "towards" or "in the direction of."
    • sūryaḥ: This refers to "the sun."
    • vy: This is a prefix that can mean "to spread" or "to manifest."
    • akhyaj: It means "to declare" or "to announce."
    • jihvayāsitaḥ: This translates to "with the tongue" or "through speech."
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the manifestation of light and wealth, symbolized by gold, in relation to the sun.
    • The sun is often seen as a source of life and energy, representing knowledge, clarity, and enlightenment.
    • The phrase "with the tongue" suggests the importance of speech and communication in declaring or manifesting one's intentions and desires.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be applied to the pursuit of success and prosperity. Just as the sun brings light and energy, a clear vision and effective communication can lead to fruitful outcomes.
    • In personal development, the idea of manifesting one's desires through positive speech and affirmations can be seen as a practical application of this mantra. By speaking positively about our goals, we can attract the resources and opportunities needed to achieve them.
    • In relationships, the importance of clear and honest communication can be likened to the sun's role in providing clarity and warmth, fostering deeper connections and understanding.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of light, wealth, and the power of speech in manifesting our desires and intentions.
    • It serves as a reminder that clarity, communication, and a positive mindset are essential in our journey towards success and fulfillment.
    • In various aspects of life, from personal growth to professional endeavors, the principles encapsulated in this mantra can guide us in achieving our goals and enhancing our experiences.
