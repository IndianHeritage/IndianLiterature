रायः समुद्राम्ś चतुरोऽस्मभ्यं सोम विश्वतः |आ पवस्व सहस्रिणः ||
rāyaḥ samudrām̐ś caturo 'smabhyaṁ soma viśvataḥ | ā pavasva sahasriṇaḥ ||
  • Mantra: "rāyaḥ samudrām̐ś caturo 'smabhyaṁ soma viśvataḥ | ā pavasva sahasriṇaḥ ||"
  • Translation: "O Soma, may you pour forth your fourfold wealth upon us from all sides, and may you flow abundantly."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • rāyaḥ: Wealth or prosperity.
    • samudrām̐ś: From the ocean, symbolizing vastness and abundance.
    • caturo: Fourfold, indicating completeness or totality.
    • 'smabhyaṁ: To us, indicating a plea or request directed towards the divine.
    • soma: A sacred drink, often associated with immortality and divine blessings.
    • viśvataḥ: From all sides, suggesting an all-encompassing nature.
    • ā pavasva: May you flow or may you pour forth.
    • sahasriṇaḥ: Abundantly or in a thousand ways, emphasizing the richness of the blessings sought.
  • Significance:
    • This mantra is a prayer for abundance and prosperity, invoking the divine essence of Soma to bless the seeker with wealth from all directions.
    • The fourfold wealth can be interpreted as physical, emotional, spiritual, and intellectual prosperity, highlighting the holistic approach to well-being.
    • In real life, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as during rituals for new beginnings, business ventures, or personal growth, where one seeks blessings for success and abundance.
    • For example, an entrepreneur might recite this mantra before launching a new product, hoping to attract prosperity and success from all sides.
  • Conclusion:
    • The mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking divine blessings for a prosperous life, reminding us that true wealth encompasses more than just material possessions.
    • It encourages individuals to cultivate a mindset of abundance and gratitude, recognizing the various forms of wealth that enrich our lives.
    • By invoking this mantra, one can align themselves with the energies of prosperity and abundance, fostering a positive environment for growth and success.
