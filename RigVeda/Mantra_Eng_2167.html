प्र वः सतां ज्येष्ठतमाया सुष्टुतिम अग्नाव इव समिधाने हविर भरे |इन्द्रं अजुर्म्यं जरयन्तं उक्षितं सनाद युवानम् अवसे हवामहे ||
pra vaḥ satāṁ jyeṣṭhatamāya suṣṭutim agnāv iva samidhāne havir bhare | indram ajuryaṁ jarayantam ukṣitaṁ sanād yuvānam avase havāmahe ||
  • pra vaḥ satāṁ jyeṣṭhatamāya suṣṭutim: This phrase can be translated as "We invoke the best praise of the wise." Here, "pra vaḥ" means to invoke or call forth, "satāṁ" refers to the wise or learned, "jyeṣṭhatamāya" means the best or foremost, and "suṣṭutim" signifies praise or commendation. In real life, this can be seen in situations where we seek the guidance and wisdom of experienced individuals, such as mentors or leaders, to help us make important decisions.
  • agnāv iva samidhāne havir bhare: This translates to "As offerings are made to the fire." "Agnāv" refers to fire, "iva" means like or as, "samidhāne" indicates the act of offering, and "havir bhare" means to fill with offerings. This can be related to rituals and ceremonies in various cultures where offerings are made to a sacred fire, symbolizing the connection between the material and spiritual worlds. For example, in Hindu weddings, the sacred fire (Agni) is a witness to the vows taken by the couple.
  • indram ajuryaṁ jarayantam ukṣitaṁ: This means "Indra, the strong, who is ever youthful." "Indram" refers to the deity Indra, "ajuryaṁ" signifies strength or vigor, "jarayantam" means ever youthful or rejuvenating, and "ukṣitaṁ" indicates being nourished or sustained. In modern contexts, this can be likened to the idea of maintaining one's vitality and strength through healthy living and continuous learning, much like how athletes or professionals strive to stay at the top of their game.
  • sanād yuvānam avase havāmahe: This translates to "We invoke the youthful one." "Sanād" means from the beginning or eternally, "yuvānam" refers to youth or youthful energy, and "avase havāmahe" means we invoke or call upon. This can be interpreted as a call to embrace the energy and enthusiasm of youth, which is applicable in various life scenarios, such as encouraging innovation and creativity in workplaces or educational institutions.

  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of invoking wisdom, strength, and youthful energy in our lives. It signifies the value of seeking guidance from the wise, making offerings to the greater good, and maintaining vitality through continuous growth and learning. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire individuals to seek mentorship, engage in community service, and foster an environment of innovation and enthusiasm, ultimately leading to personal and collective growth.
