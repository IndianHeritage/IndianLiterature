यं त्वं विप्र मेधसाताव अग्ने हिनोषि धनाय | स तवोती गोषु गन्ता ||
yaṁ tvaṁ vipra medhasātāv agne hinoṣi dhanāya | sa tavotī goṣu gantā ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yaṁ: whom
    • tvaṁ: you
    • vipra: O wise one, learned person
    • medhasātāv: with intelligence, wisdom
    • agne: O Agni, the fire god
    • hinoṣi: you bestow, you grant
    • dhanāya: for wealth, prosperity
    • sa: he, that
    • tavotī: your, of you
    • goṣu: in the cows, in the wealth
    • gantā: to go, to move
  • Overall Meaning: This mantra is a prayer to Agni, the fire god, asking for wisdom and prosperity. The devotee acknowledges Agni as a source of intelligence and wealth, seeking his blessings to achieve success and abundance in life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • Seeking Guidance: Just as individuals often seek guidance from mentors or wise figures in their lives, this mantra emphasizes the importance of wisdom in achieving one's goals. For instance, a student may seek advice from a teacher to excel in their studies.
    • Wealth and Prosperity: In modern life, people often pray for financial stability and success in their careers. This mantra can be seen as a reminder to focus on ethical means of acquiring wealth, much like a businessperson who prioritizes integrity in their dealings.
    • Community and Support: The reference to "cows" symbolizes wealth and sustenance in ancient times. Today, this can be related to the importance of community support in achieving personal and professional goals, similar to how a farmer relies on the community for resources and assistance.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for wisdom and prosperity through divine blessings. It teaches us that intelligence and ethical pursuits are essential for achieving success in life. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire individuals to seek knowledge, act with integrity, and rely on community support to navigate their paths toward prosperity. By invoking Agni's blessings, one can cultivate a mindset that values wisdom and ethical wealth creation, ultimately leading to a fulfilling and prosperous life.
