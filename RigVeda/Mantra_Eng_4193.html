एतं मे स्तोमं ऊर्म्ये दार्भ्याय परा वह |गिरो देवी रथीर इव ||
etam me stomam ūrmye dārbhyāya parā vaha | giro devi rathīr iva ||
  • Translation: The mantra translates to "O Devi, carry my hymn like a chariot." This invocation is directed towards a divine feminine energy, asking for the hymn to be carried forth with strength and grace.
  • Word Breakdown:
    • etam: This means "this" or "this hymn," indicating a personal connection to the words being spoken.
    • me: Translates to "my," signifying ownership and personal appeal.
    • stomam: Refers to a "hymn" or "praise," emphasizing the importance of vocal expression in spiritual practice.
    • ūrmye: This word can be interpreted as "like a wave" or "with strength," suggesting a powerful and dynamic quality.
    • dārbhyāya: This term means "to carry" or "to bear," indicating a request for support and assistance.
    • parā: Meaning "supreme" or "transcendent," it highlights the divine nature of the energy being invoked.
    • vaha: This means "to carry" or "to bring forth," reinforcing the request for the hymn to be taken to a higher realm.
    • giro: Refers to "words" or "speech," emphasizing the power of verbal expression in spiritual contexts.
    • devi: This means "goddess," directly addressing the divine feminine energy.
    • rathīr: Translates to "like a chariot," symbolizing strength, movement, and the ability to traverse obstacles.
    • iva: Meaning "like" or "as," it draws a comparison to the chariot, enhancing the imagery of the hymn being carried forth.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, individuals often seek guidance and support from higher powers or spiritual entities when facing challenges. This mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of invoking divine assistance in our endeavors.
    • In a professional setting, one might recite this mantra before a presentation or important meeting, asking for clarity and strength in communication, much like a chariot that carries one forward with purpose.
    • During personal struggles, such as health issues or emotional turmoil, individuals may find solace in reciting this mantra, seeking the divine's help to navigate through their difficulties.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its invocation of divine support and the power of words. It emphasizes the importance of expressing one's thoughts and prayers, asking for them to be carried forth with strength and purpose. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in moments of need, whether in personal challenges, professional endeavors, or spiritual practices. By recognizing the divine feminine energy and seeking its assistance, individuals can find empowerment and guidance in their journeys.
