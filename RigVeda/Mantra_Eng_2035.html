ये स्तोत्रिभ्यो गोआग्राम अश्वपेśसम अग्ने रातिम उपसृजन्ति सूरयः |अस्माञ्च तांश्च प्र हि नेषि वस्य आ बृहद् वदेम विदथे सुवीराः ||
ye stotṛbhyo goagrām aśvapeśasam agne rātim upasṛjanti sūrayaḥ | asmāñ ca tām̐ś ca pra hi neṣi vasya ā bṛhad vadema vidathe suvīrāḥ ||
  • Mantra: "ye stotṛbhyo goagrām aśvapeśasam agne rātim upasṛjanti sūrayaḥ | asmāñ ca tām̐ś ca pra hi neṣi vasya ā bṛhad vadema vidathe suvīrāḥ ||"
  • Translation: "Those who praise the divine, who are like the swift horses, and who bring forth delight, O Agni, may the strong ones lead us to that which is vast and beneficial."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • ye: Those who
    • stotṛbhyo: To the praisers
    • goagrām: Like the swift horses
    • aśvapeśasam: Bringing forth delight
    • agne: O Agni (the divine fire)
    • rātim: Delight or joy
    • upasṛjanti: They bring forth
    • sūrayaḥ: The strong ones
    • asmāñ: Us
    • ca: And
    • tām̐ś: That
    • pra hi: Lead us to
    • neṣi: To that which is vast
    • vasya: Beneficial
    • ā: To
    • bṛhad: Vast
    • vadema: May we speak
    • vidathe: In the assembly
    • suvīrāḥ: The strong ones
  • Contextual Explanation:
    • This mantra invokes Agni, the fire god, symbolizing energy, transformation, and the divine presence in rituals.
    • The reference to "swift horses" can be likened to the speed and efficiency of modern transportation, emphasizing the importance of progress and movement in life.
    • The idea of "bringing forth delight" resonates with the pursuit of happiness and fulfillment in our daily lives, whether through personal achievements or communal celebrations.
    • The call for the "strong ones" to lead us to vast and beneficial outcomes reflects the need for leadership and guidance in various aspects of life, such as in business, community service, or personal development.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of divine guidance and the pursuit of joy and fulfillment in life.
    • It is applicable in real-life scenarios where individuals seek inspiration and strength to overcome challenges, whether in personal endeavors, professional settings, or community initiatives.
    • By invoking the qualities of Agni and the strong ones, we are reminded to strive for excellence and to seek out the vast opportunities that life presents.
