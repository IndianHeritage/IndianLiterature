प्र च्यवानाज जुजुरुषो वव्रिम अटकं न मुंचथः |युवा यदी कृतः पुनर आ कामम् ऋण्वे वध्वः ||
pra cyavānāj jujuruṣo vavrim atkaṁ na muñcathaḥ | yuvā yadī kṛthaḥ punar ā kāmam ṛṇve vadhvaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • pra: forward, towards
    • cyavānāj: those who have fallen away
    • jujuruṣo: those who are striving or making efforts
    • vavrim: I will speak or declare
    • atkaṁ: the goal or purpose
    • na: not
    • muñcathaḥ: let go or release
    • yuvā: you (plural)
    • yadī: if
    • kṛthaḥ: you have done
    • punar: again
    • ā: towards
    • kāmam: desire or wish
    • ṛṇve: in debt or obligation
    • vadhvaḥ: to those who are bound or tied
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks to the importance of perseverance and commitment in the face of challenges.
    • The phrase "pra cyavānāj" suggests that even if one has fallen away or faced setbacks, there is a call to strive forward.
    • "Yuvā yadī kṛthaḥ punar ā kāmam" implies that if you have acted with intention and purpose, you can reclaim your desires and goals.
    • The mantra emphasizes not to let go of your aspirations, even when faced with difficulties.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, an employee who faces failure in a project may feel disheartened. This mantra encourages them to learn from the experience and strive for success in future endeavors.
    • A student who struggles with a subject may be tempted to give up. The mantra serves as a reminder to keep working hard and to seek help, reinforcing the idea that perseverance can lead to mastery.
    • In personal relationships, when conflicts arise, this mantra can inspire individuals to communicate openly and work through their issues rather than abandoning the relationship.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its encouragement to maintain determination and resilience.
    • It teaches that setbacks are a part of life, but they should not deter one from pursuing their goals.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, including career, education, and personal relationships, reminding individuals to stay committed and strive for their aspirations despite challenges.
