प्र कृष्टिहेव शूषा एति रोर्वद आसुर्यं वर्णं नि रिणीते अस्य तम् |जहाति वव्रिम पितुर एति निष्कृतम् उपप्रुतं कृतुते निर्णिजं तना ||
pra kṛṣṭiheva śūṣa eti roruvad asurya1ṁ varṇaṁ ni riṇīte asya tam | jahāti vavrim pitur eti niṣkṛtam upaprutaṁ kṛṇute nirṇijaṁ tanā ||
  • pra kṛṣṭiheva: This phrase can be interpreted as "towards the highest excellence" or "to the utmost brilliance." It signifies the pursuit of the highest ideals and virtues in life.
  • śūṣa eti: This translates to "the one who is thirsty" or "the one who seeks." It represents the human condition of longing for knowledge, fulfillment, and spiritual enlightenment.
  • roruvad: This word means "to cry out" or "to express." It indicates the importance of vocalizing one's desires and aspirations, suggesting that expression is a vital part of the human experience.
  • asuryaṁ varṇaṁ: This can be understood as "the color of the sun" or "radiance." It symbolizes enlightenment, positivity, and the pursuit of truth and knowledge.
  • ni riṇīte asya tam: This phrase means "he does not turn away from it." It emphasizes the importance of steadfastness and commitment to one's goals and ideals.
  • jahāti vavrim: This translates to "he abandons the darkness." It signifies the act of letting go of ignorance and negativity, moving towards a more enlightened state of being.
  • pitur eti niṣkṛtam: This means "the father leads to liberation." It suggests that guidance from elders or mentors is crucial in the journey towards self-realization and freedom from worldly attachments.
  • upaprutaṁ kṛṇute: This can be interpreted as "he creates a path." It signifies the importance of actively shaping one's destiny and the role of individual agency in achieving one's goals.
  • nirṇijaṁ tanā: This phrase means "the body that is free from bondage." It represents the ultimate goal of spiritual practice: liberation from the physical and mental constraints that bind us.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the journey of self-discovery and the pursuit of enlightenment. It emphasizes the importance of striving for excellence, expressing one's desires, and seeking guidance from mentors. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios, such as in education, where students seek knowledge and guidance from teachers, or in personal development, where individuals strive to overcome ignorance and negativity. The mantra serves as a reminder that the path to liberation and fulfillment requires commitment, expression, and the courage to abandon darkness in favor of light. It encourages us to actively shape our destinies and seek the highest ideals in our lives.
