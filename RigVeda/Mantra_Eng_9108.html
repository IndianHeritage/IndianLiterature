आ नो देवानामुप वेतु शंसो विश्वेभिः तुरैर अवसे यजत्रः |तेभिर वयं सुषखायो भवेम तरन्तो विश्वा दुरिता स्याम ||
ā no devānām upa vetu śaṁso viśvebhis turair avase yajatraḥ | tebhir vayaṁ suṣakhāyo bhavema taranto viśvā duritā syāma ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā no: May we be led
    • devānām: by the gods
    • upa vetu: to the knowledge
    • śaṁso: of peace
    • viśvebhis: with all beings
    • turair: with swift actions
    • avase: may we be protected
    • yajatraḥ: in our sacrifices
    • tebhir: with them
    • vayaṁ: we
    • suṣakhāyo: may we be well-off
    • bhavema: may we become
    • taranto: crossing over
    • viśvā: all
    • duritā: misfortunes
    • syāma: may we be free from
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra is a prayer for guidance and protection from the divine.
    • It emphasizes the importance of seeking knowledge and peace through the blessings of the gods.
    • The phrase "may we be led by the gods" signifies a desire for divine intervention in our lives.
    • In real life, this can be seen in the context of individuals seeking guidance in difficult situations, such as making important life decisions or facing challenges.
    • The call for "swift actions" suggests the need for timely responses to life's challenges, akin to how one might act quickly in a crisis.
    • The mantra also highlights the importance of community and collective effort, as it mentions "with all beings," indicating that we are not alone in our struggles.
    • In practical terms, this can relate to teamwork in workplaces or collaboration in community projects.
    • The aspiration to "cross over misfortunes" reflects a universal human desire to overcome obstacles, which can be applied in various contexts, such as personal growth, recovery from illness, or overcoming financial difficulties.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of life and the importance of seeking divine guidance.
    • Its significance lies in the hope for protection and prosperity, not just for oneself but for the community as a whole.
    • In real-life scenarios, it encourages individuals to seek support from others and to act with intention and purpose.
    • Ultimately, it inspires a collective journey towards overcoming challenges and achieving peace and well-being.
