न ता arvā रेणुककाटो अशनुते न संस्कृतत्रम् उप यन्ति ता अभि |उरुगायम् अभयं तस्य ता अनु गावो मर्तस्य विचरन्ति यज्वनः ||
na tā arvā reṇukakāṭo aśnute na saṁskṛtatram upa yanti tā abhi | urugāyam abhayaṁ tasya tā anu gāvo martasya vi caranti yajvanaḥ ||
  • na tā arvā reṇukakāṭo aśnute: This phrase can be translated to "They do not consume the dust of the earth." Here, "arvā" refers to the earth or ground, and "reṇukakāṭo" signifies the dust or particles that arise from it. This can symbolize purity and the avoidance of negativity or harmful influences.
  • na saṁskṛtatram upa yanti tā abhi: This means "They do not approach the refined or the elevated." The term "saṁskṛtatram" refers to something that is refined or elevated, indicating a state of higher consciousness or purity. This suggests that those who are pure do not seek out superficial or materialistic pursuits.
  • urugāyam abhayaṁ tasya: Translated, this means "The vast and fearless one." "Urugāyam" refers to something that is expansive or vast, while "abhayaṁ" means fearless. This can be interpreted as a representation of divine qualities or the ultimate truth that is vast and free from fear.
  • tā anu gāvo martasya vi caranti yajvanaḥ: This translates to "The cows of the mortal beings wander in the presence of the worshippers." Here, "gāvo" refers to cows, which are often seen as symbols of nourishment and abundance in Vedic literature. "Martasya" refers to mortals, and "yajvanaḥ" indicates those who perform rituals or worship. This suggests that those who are devoted and worshipful attract abundance and prosperity.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of purity, fearlessness, and devotion in one's life. It teaches that by avoiding negativity and superficial pursuits, one can attain a higher state of consciousness and attract abundance. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied by focusing on personal growth, spiritual practices, and maintaining a positive environment. By embodying these qualities, individuals can lead a more fulfilling and prosperous life, fostering a sense of community and connection with others who share similar values.
