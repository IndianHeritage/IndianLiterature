वृतेव यन्तं बहुभिर् वसव्यैष तवे रयिं जागृवांसो अनु गमन् |रुशन्तं अग्निं दर्शतम् बृहन्तं वपावन्तं विश्वहा दीदिवांसम् ||
vṛteva yantam bahubhir vasavyai3s tve rayiṁ jāgṛvāṁso anu gman | ruśantam agniṁ darśatam bṛhantaṁ vapāvantaṁ viśvahā dīdivāṁsam ||
  • Mantra: "vṛteva yantam bahubhir vasavyaiḥ tve rayiṁ jāgṛvāṁso anu gman | ruśantam agniṁ darśatam bṛhantaṁ vapāvantaṁ viśvahā dīdivāṁsam ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • vṛteva: like a protector or guardian
    • yantam: the one who is directed or guided
    • bahubhir: by many or numerous
    • vasavyaiḥ: with wealth or prosperity
    • tve: to you
    • rayiṁ: wealth or abundance
    • jāgṛvāṁso: those who are awake or alert
    • anu: following or in accordance with
    • gman: to go or to move
    • ruśantam: the one who is shining or radiant
    • agniṁ: fire or energy
    • darśatam: the one who is visible or manifest
    • bṛhantaṁ: the great or vast one
    • vapāvantaṁ: the one who is powerful or strong
    • viśvahā: the one who carries all or universal
    • dīdivāṁsam: the one who is brilliant or luminous
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra invokes the essence of guidance and protection, emphasizing the importance of wealth and prosperity in life.
    • The phrase "like a protector" suggests that one should seek guidance from those who are wise and experienced, much like how a mentor guides a student.
    • The mention of "wealth" can be interpreted not just in material terms but also in terms of knowledge, relationships, and spiritual growth.
    • In real life, this can be seen in the way communities come together to support each other, sharing resources and wisdom to uplift everyone.
    • The reference to "fire" symbolizes energy and transformation, akin to how challenges in life can lead to personal growth and enlightenment.
    • Just as fire is essential for cooking and warmth, the mantra highlights the necessity of harnessing one's inner strength and potential to achieve greatness.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of seeking guidance and support from the community while also recognizing one's own potential.
    • It teaches us that wealth is multifaceted and that true prosperity comes from collaboration, wisdom, and inner strength.
    • In practical terms, this can be applied in various aspects of life, such as in teamwork, mentorship, and personal development.
    • Ultimately, it encourages individuals to be both givers and receivers of knowledge and support, fostering a harmonious and prosperous environment.
