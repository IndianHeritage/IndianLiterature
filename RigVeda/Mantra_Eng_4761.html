अस्य मादे पूरु वर्पांसि विद्वान् इन्द्रो वृत्राण्य अप्रती जग्हान |तम उ प्र होषि मधुमन्तम अस्मै सोमं वीराय शिप्रिणे पिबध्यै ||
asya made puru varpāṁsi vidvān indro vṛtrāṇy apratī jaghāna | tam u pra hoṣi madhumantam asmai somaṁ vīrāya śipriṇe pibadhyai ||
  • Mantra: "asya made puru varpāṁsi vidvān indro vṛtrāṇy apratī jaghāna | tam u pra hoṣi madhumantam asmai somaṁ vīrāya śipriṇe pibadhyai ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • asya: of this
    • made: in the intoxication
    • puru: many
    • varpāṁsi: forms or bodies
    • vidvān: the wise one
    • indro: Indra, the king of gods
    • vṛtrāṇy: the obstacles or enemies
    • apratī: without resistance
    • jaghāna: has struck down
    • tam: that
    • u: indeed
    • pra: for the sake of
    • hoṣi: you may invoke
    • madhumantam: the sweet one
    • asmai: for him
    • somaṁ: the soma (sacred drink)
    • vīrāya: for the hero
    • śipriṇe: who is strong
    • pibadhyai: may drink
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra speaks of the divine qualities of Indra, who is often associated with strength, courage, and the ability to overcome obstacles.
    • The phrase "asya made" suggests a state of heightened awareness or intoxication, which can be likened to moments in life when one feels empowered or inspired, such as during a significant achievement or celebration.
    • Indra's action of "jaghāna" signifies the removal of challenges or enemies, which can be related to personal struggles we face in life, such as overcoming fears or adversities.
    • The invocation of "madhumantam" and "somaṁ" symbolizes the pursuit of joy and vitality, akin to seeking happiness and fulfillment in our daily lives.
    • The call for the hero to "pibadhyai" reflects the importance of nurturing strength and resilience, much like how athletes or leaders prepare themselves mentally and physically to face challenges.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of divine support in overcoming life's challenges and the importance of inner strength and joy.
    • In real-life scenarios, it can be applied to moments of struggle, encouraging individuals to seek help, embrace their inner strength, and celebrate their victories.
    • Ultimately, it serves as a reminder that with wisdom and courage, one can navigate through obstacles and emerge victorious, much like the hero's journey in various narratives.
