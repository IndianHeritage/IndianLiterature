के मे मर्यकं वि यवन्त गोभिर न येषां गोपा अरणश्चिदास || य इं जगृभुर अव ते सृजन्त्व आजाति पश्व उप नश्चिकित्वान् ||
ke me maryakaṁ vi yavanta gobhir na yeṣāṁ gopā araṇaś cid āsa | ya īṁ jagṛbhur ava te sṛjantv ājāti paśva upa naś cikitvān ||
  • Mantra: "ke me maryakaṁ vi yavanta gobhir na yeṣāṁ gopā araṇaś cid āsa | ya īṁ jagṛbhur ava te sṛjantv ājāti paśva upa naś cikitvān ||"
  • Translation: This mantra can be interpreted as a call to the divine for protection and guidance. It speaks of the guardians (gopā) who are aware of the needs of the devotees and the importance of nurturing and safeguarding life.
  • Word Breakdown:
    • ke: who
    • me: my
    • maryakaṁ: protector or guardian
    • vi: indeed
    • yavanta: those who are strong or powerful
    • gobhir: with the cows (symbolizing wealth and nourishment)
    • na: not
    • yeṣāṁ: of whom
    • gopā: the protectors or shepherds
    • araṇaś: refuge or shelter
    • cid: consciousness or awareness
    • āsa: hope or desire
    • ya: who
    • īṁ: this
    • jagṛbhur: have grasped or understood
    • ava: down or below
    • te: your
    • sṛjantv: may they create or manifest
    • ājāti: born or produced
    • paśva: cattle or livestock
    • upa: near or close
    • naś: us
    • cikitvān: being aware or knowledgeable
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • This mantra can be recited by farmers seeking protection for their livestock, emphasizing the importance of guardianship in agriculture.
    • In a broader sense, it can be applied by individuals seeking guidance and protection in their personal and professional lives, invoking the idea of a higher power watching over them.
    • Communities can come together to recite this mantra during festivals or gatherings, reinforcing the collective hope for safety and prosperity.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its invocation of protection and awareness from the divine. It emphasizes the role of guardianship in nurturing life and the importance of community support. In real-life scenarios, it serves as a reminder to seek guidance and protection, whether in personal endeavors, agricultural practices, or communal activities. By acknowledging the presence of a higher power, individuals and communities can foster a sense of security and hope in their lives.
