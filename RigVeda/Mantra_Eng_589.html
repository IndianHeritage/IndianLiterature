अदृश्रम अस्य केतवो वि रश्मयो जनाम् अनु |भ्राजन्तो अग्नयो यथा ||
adṛśram asya ketavo vi raśmayo janām̐ anu | bhrājanto agnayo yathā ||
  • adṛśram: This word translates to "invisible" or "unseen." It signifies the aspects of life or the universe that are not immediately perceptible to the senses.
  • asya: Meaning "of this" or "of this world," it refers to the current existence or reality we inhabit.
  • ketavo: This term can be interpreted as "the guiding lights" or "the stars." It symbolizes guidance, direction, and higher knowledge.
  • vi raśmayo: "vi" means "through" or "by," and "raśmayo" refers to "rays" or "beams." Together, they suggest the illumination or enlightenment that comes from these guiding lights.
  • janām̐: This word means "people" or "human beings." It emphasizes the collective experience of humanity.
  • anu: This translates to "following" or "after," indicating a sequence or a journey.
  • bhrājanto: Meaning "shining" or "radiating," it describes the quality of the guiding lights or knowledge that illuminates the path.
  • agnayo: This word refers to "the fires" or "the divine energy." In a broader sense, it can symbolize passion, creativity, and transformation.
  • yathā: Meaning "as" or "like," it draws a comparison or analogy.

  • Interpretation: The mantra speaks about the unseen forces and guiding lights in our lives that lead us through the darkness. It suggests that while we may not always see the path ahead, there are divine energies and knowledge that illuminate our journey.
  • Real-life Example: Consider a person facing a difficult decision in their career. They may feel lost and uncertain (adṛśram), but through introspection, guidance from mentors (ketavo), and their own inner strength (agnayo), they find clarity and direction (bhrājanto). This process reflects the journey of following the light of knowledge and wisdom (anu) to make informed choices.

  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its reminder that even in times of uncertainty, there are unseen forces and guiding lights that can lead us to clarity and understanding. It encourages individuals to seek knowledge, trust their intuition, and recognize the divine energies at play in their lives. This mantra is applicable in various real-life scenarios, such as personal growth, decision-making, and navigating challenges, reinforcing the idea that we are never truly alone on our journey.
