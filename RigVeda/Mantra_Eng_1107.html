विष्वाहेंद्रो अधिवक्तā नो अस्त्व अपरिहृताः सनुयाम वाजम् |तन नो मित्रो वरुणो मामहन्ताम् अदितिः सिन्धुः पृथिवी उता द्यौः ||
viśvāhendro adhivaktā no astv aparihvṛtāḥ sanuyāma vājam | tan no mitro varuṇo māmahantām aditiḥ sindhuḥ pṛthivī uta dyauḥ ||
  • Translation: "May the universal Lord, who is the speaker, not be obstructed; may He grant us strength and abundance. May the divine friends, Mitra and Varuna, protect us, along with Aditi, the ocean, the earth, and the sky."
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • viśvāhendro: universal Lord or the supreme being.
    • adhivaktā: the speaker or the one who communicates.
    • no: us or our.
    • astv: may there be.
    • aparihṛtāḥ: not obstructed or hindered.
    • sanuyāma: grant us strength or support.
    • vājam: abundance or wealth.
    • tan: that or may that.
    • mitro: Mitra, the deity of friendship and contracts.
    • varuṇo: Varuna, the deity of cosmic order and water.
    • māmahantām: protect us or may they safeguard us.
    • aditiḥ: Aditi, the mother of gods and the infinite.
    • sindhuḥ: ocean or water body.
    • pṛthivī: earth.
    • uta: and.
    • dyauḥ: sky or heaven.
  • Significance:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of divine support and protection in our lives.
    • It reflects the interconnectedness of various elements of nature and the divine, highlighting the need for harmony and balance.
    • In real life, this can be seen in the way communities come together to support one another, much like the deities mentioned in the mantra.
    • For example, during times of crisis, such as natural disasters, people often unite to provide assistance, embodying the spirit of Mitra and Varuna.
  • Real-Life Application:
    • This mantra can be recited during personal or communal prayers to seek guidance and strength in challenging times.
    • It serves as a reminder to foster relationships based on trust and cooperation, akin to the friendship represented by Mitra.
    • In a corporate setting, it can inspire teamwork and collaboration, ensuring that all members work towards a common goal without obstruction.
  • Conclusion:
    • The mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking divine guidance and support in all aspects of life.
    • It encourages individuals to recognize the importance of community, nature, and the divine in achieving harmony and success.
    • By embodying the principles of this mantra, one can navigate life's challenges with resilience and unity.
