मन्त्र: कदु प्रेष्टाव इषां रयीणाम अध्वर्यन्ता यद उन्निनीतो अपाम् |अयं वां यज्ञो अकृत प्रशस्तिं वसुधिती अवितारा जनानाम् ||
kad u preṣṭāv iṣāṁ rayīṇām adhvaryantā yad unninītho apām | ayaṁ vāṁ yajño akṛta praśastiṁ vasudhitī avitārā janānām ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • kad: when
    • u: or
    • preṣṭāv: in the best or most excellent
    • iṣāṁ: of the rulers or leaders
    • rayīṇām: of wealth or prosperity
    • adhvaryantā: while performing the rituals
    • yad: that
    • unninītho: which is raised or elevated
    • apām: of the waters
    • ayaṁ: this
    • vāṁ: your
    • yajño: sacrifice or worship
    • akṛta: not done or unperformed
    • praśastiṁ: praise or glory
    • vasudhitī: of the earth
    • avitārā: without division or separation
    • janānām: of the people
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra reflects on the relationship between leadership, wealth, and the performance of rituals.
    • It suggests that when the best leaders perform rituals, they elevate the prosperity of the community.
    • The mention of "waters" symbolizes the source of life and abundance, indicating that rituals can invoke blessings from nature.
    • The phrase "your sacrifice" emphasizes the collective effort of the community in worship, highlighting the importance of unity.
    • It also points out that without proper rituals, the glory and praise of the earth and its people may remain unfulfilled.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern society, community rituals such as festivals or collective prayers can bring people together, fostering unity and shared purpose.
    • Leadership in organizations often involves guiding teams through rituals of recognition and celebration, which can enhance morale and productivity.
    • Environmental rituals, such as tree planting or clean-up drives, can be seen as modern interpretations of invoking blessings from nature for the prosperity of the community.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of leadership and collective effort in achieving prosperity and harmony.
    • It teaches that rituals and sacrifices are not merely traditional practices but essential for invoking blessings and fostering unity among people.
    • In real life, it encourages individuals and communities to engage in collective actions that promote well-being and prosperity, reinforcing the idea that together, we can achieve greater heights.
