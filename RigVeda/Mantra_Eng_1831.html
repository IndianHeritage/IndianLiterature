त्वं ईशिषे वसुपते वसूनां त्वं मित्राणां मित्रपते धेष्ठः | इन्द्र त्वं मरुद्भिः सं वदस्वाध प्राशान ऋतुथा हवींषि ||
tvam īśiṣe vasupate vasūnāṁ tvam mitrāṇām mitrapate dheṣṭhaḥ | indra tvam marudbhiḥ saṁ vadasvādha prāśāna ṛtuthā havīṁṣi ||
  • tvam - You
  • īśiṣe - O Lord
  • vasupate - Lord of wealth
  • vasūnāṁ - of all beings
  • tvam - You
  • mitrāṇām - of friends
  • mitrapate - O Lord of friends
  • dheṣṭhaḥ - the best
  • indra - O Indra
  • tvam - You
  • marudbhiḥ - with the Maruts (storm deities)
  • saṁ - together
  • vadasvādha - speak auspiciously
  • prāśāna - grant us
  • ṛtuthā - in accordance with truth
  • havīṁṣi - our offerings

This mantra can be translated as: "O Lord of wealth, you are the best among friends. O Indra, together with the Maruts, speak auspiciously and grant us our offerings in accordance with truth."


The mantra addresses multiple divine entities, primarily focusing on Indra, the king of gods, and the Maruts, who are storm deities. It emphasizes the importance of wealth, friendship, and truth in our lives.


  • Wealth and Prosperity: The invocation of "vasupate" signifies the importance of wealth in our lives. In real life, this can be related to the pursuit of financial stability and abundance, which allows individuals to support their families and communities.
  • Friendship and Community: The mention of "mitrāṇām" highlights the value of friendship. In today's world, building strong relationships and networks can lead to personal and professional growth. For example, collaboration in a workplace often leads to innovative solutions and success.
  • Truth and Integrity: The phrase "in accordance with truth" underscores the significance of honesty and integrity. In real life, this can be seen in ethical business practices, where companies that prioritize transparency often gain consumer trust and loyalty.

In conclusion, this mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of wealth, friendship, and truth. It encourages individuals to seek prosperity while fostering strong relationships and adhering to ethical principles. In a world where these values are often challenged, the mantra's teachings are applicable in various aspects of life, from personal relationships to professional endeavors, guiding us towards a harmonious and fulfilling existence.
