तत्-तद् इद अस्य पौंस्यं गृणीमसीनस्य त्रातुर अवृकस्य मीळ्हुषः |यः पार्थिवानि त्रिभिर इद विगामभिर उरु क्रमिष्टोरुगायाय जीवसे ||
tat-tad id asya pauṁsyaṁ gṛṇīmasīnasya trātur avṛkasya mīḻhuṣaḥ | yaḥ pārthivāni tribhir id vigāmabhir uru kramiṣṭorugāyāya jīvase ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • tat-tad: that, this
    • id: indeed
    • asya: of this
    • pauṁsyaṁ: manliness, strength
    • gṛṇīmasī: we sing, we praise
    • nasya: of the one
    • trātur: protector
    • avṛkasya: of the wolf, symbolizing danger
    • mīḻhuṣaḥ: of the one who is strong
    • yaḥ: who
    • pārthivāni: earthly, worldly
    • tribhir: by three
    • id: indeed
    • vigāmabhir: by the paths
    • uru: vast
    • kramiṣṭo: the one who moves
    • rugāyāya: for the sake of life
    • jīvase: for living
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the strength and protection that comes from a higher power.
    • The phrase "we sing of the strength of this manliness" suggests a celebration of courage and valor.
    • The reference to "the protector of the one who is strong" indicates a divine guardian that aids those who embody strength.
    • The mention of "earthly paths" and "the one who moves" signifies the journey of life and the various challenges faced along the way.
    • It emphasizes the importance of resilience and the support one receives from the divine in overcoming life's obstacles.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In times of personal crisis, individuals often seek strength through prayer or meditation, invoking a sense of divine protection.
    • In a community facing adversity, collective strength and support can be seen as a manifestation of this mantra, where people come together to uplift one another.
    • Sports teams often embody this spirit, where the strength of the team is celebrated, and the support from coaches and fans acts as a protective force.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the strength that lies within us and the divine support that is available in our journey through life.
    • It encourages individuals to embrace their inner strength while recognizing the importance of community and divine guidance.
    • In practical terms, it can be applied in various life situations, from personal challenges to collective efforts in communities, reinforcing the idea that we are never alone in our struggles.
