मन्त्र: कर्हि स्वित तद इन्द्र यन् नृभिर नृन वीरैर वीरान नीलयासे जयाजीन | त्रिधातु गा अधि जयासि गोष्व इन्द्र द्युम्नं स्वर्वद धेहि अस्मे ||
karhi svit tad indra yan nṛbhir nṝn vīrair vīrān nīḻayāse jayājīn | tridhātu gā adhi jayāsi goṣv indra dyumnaṁ svarvad dhehy asme ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • karhi: when
    • svit: indeed
    • tad: that
    • indra: O Indra (the king of gods)
    • yan: which
    • nṛbhir: by men
    • nṝn: of men
    • vīrair: by heroes
    • vīrān: the heroes
    • nīḻayāse: you are established
    • jayājīn: victorious
    • tridhātu: in three realms
    • gā: go
    • adhi: above
    • jayāsi: you are victorious
    • goṣv: in the herds
    • indra: O Indra
    • dyumnaṁ: glory
    • svarvad: in all worlds
    • dhehy: may you bestow
    • asme: upon us
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Indra, the god of rain and thunder, who is often associated with strength and victory.
    • The invocation begins with a call to Indra, asking when he will establish his presence among men and heroes, signifying the importance of divine support in human endeavors.
    • The phrase "tridhātu" refers to the three realms: the physical, the mental, and the spiritual, indicating that victory is sought in all aspects of life.
    • The mantra emphasizes the need for divine blessings in achieving glory and success in various pursuits, whether they be personal, communal, or spiritual.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a competitive environment, such as a sports event, athletes often seek blessings for strength and victory, similar to how this mantra seeks Indra's support.
    • In business, entrepreneurs may invoke divine guidance to achieve success and overcome challenges, paralleling the call for Indra's assistance in the mantra.
    • During significant life events, such as weddings or new ventures, individuals may perform rituals to seek blessings for prosperity and happiness, reflecting the essence of this mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its universal appeal for strength, victory, and divine support in all realms of life.
    • It serves as a reminder that while human effort is essential, seeking higher guidance can lead to greater success and fulfillment.
    • This mantra can be applicable in various real-life scenarios, encouraging individuals to strive for excellence while remaining humble and open to divine assistance.
