सं यद धनन्त मण्युभिर जनासः शूरा यह्वीष्व ओषधिषु विक्षु |अध स्मा नो मरुतो रुद्रियासस त्रातारो भूत पृतनास्व आर्यः ||
saṁ yad dhananta manyubhir janāsaḥ śūrā yahvīṣv oṣadhīṣu vikṣu | adha smā no maruto rudriyāsas trātāro bhūta pṛtanāsv aryaḥ ||
  • saṁ yad dhananta manyubhir janāsaḥ: This phrase can be translated to "When the people, endowed with wealth and strength, are united." It emphasizes the importance of community and collective strength. In real life, this can be seen in various social movements where individuals come together for a common cause, such as environmental activism or community service.
  • śūrā yahvīṣv oṣadhīṣu vikṣu: This part means "the brave ones who are like the herbs in the forest." Here, the comparison to herbs signifies resilience and the ability to thrive in challenging conditions. For example, consider how certain communities adapt and flourish despite economic hardships, much like resilient plants that grow in adverse environments.
  • adha smā no maruto rudriyāsas: This translates to "May the Maruts, the storm gods, protect us." The Maruts symbolize strength and protection. In a real-life context, this can relate to the need for protection from natural disasters or societal challenges, where communities often rely on local governance and support systems to safeguard their well-being.
  • trātāro bhūta pṛtanāsv aryaḥ: This means "O noble ones, be our protectors." It calls upon the noble and virtuous to offer their protection and guidance. In modern society, this can be seen in the role of leaders, mentors, and community figures who provide support and guidance to those in need, fostering a sense of security and belonging.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of unity, strength, and protection within a community. It highlights the significance of collective effort and the role of noble individuals in safeguarding the well-being of society. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied to various contexts, such as community building, social justice movements, and disaster preparedness. By fostering unity and seeking protection from those who are strong and virtuous, communities can thrive and overcome challenges together.
