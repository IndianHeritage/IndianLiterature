प्र नूनं जाताताम आयं मनुस् तोकमेव रोहतु |यः सहस्रं शताश्वं सद्यो दानाय मंहते ||
pra nūnaṁ jāyatām ayam manus tokmeva rohatu | yaḥ sahasraṁ śatāśvaṁ sadyo dānāya maṁhate ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • pra: forward, towards
    • nūnaṁ: indeed, certainly
    • jāyatām: may be born, may arise
    • ayam: this
    • manus: human, mankind
    • tokmeva: like a sprout, as a new growth
    • rohatu: may it grow, may it flourish
    • yaḥ: who
    • sahasraṁ: thousand
    • śatāśvaṁ: hundred horses
    • sadyo: immediately, at once
    • dānāya: for giving, for donation
    • maṁhate: may it be powerful, may it be great
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra expresses a desire for the flourishing of humanity, akin to a sprout that grows strong and healthy.
    • The mention of "thousand horses" symbolizes immense strength and resources, suggesting that the growth of mankind should be powerful and abundant.
    • The phrase "for giving" indicates a focus on generosity and the importance of sharing one's resources and strengths with others.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community setting, this mantra can be reflected in initiatives that promote education and empowerment, allowing individuals to grow and contribute positively to society.
    • In business, companies that prioritize corporate social responsibility embody the essence of this mantra by using their resources to uplift communities and support sustainable development.
    • On a personal level, individuals can apply this mantra by nurturing their talents and sharing their knowledge with others, fostering a culture of growth and collaboration.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for the flourishing of humanity through strength, generosity, and collaboration.
    • It serves as a reminder that true growth is not just about individual success but also about uplifting others and contributing to the greater good.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire actions that lead to community development, social responsibility, and personal growth, ultimately creating a more harmonious and prosperous society.
