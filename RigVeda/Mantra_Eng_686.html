प्र नू महित्वं वृषभस्य वोचं यं पूरवो वृत्रहणं साचन्ते |वैश्वानरो दस्युम अग्निर जगहन्वां अधूनोत काष्ठा अव शम्बरं भेत्त ||
pra nū mahitvaṁ vṛṣabhasya vocaṁ yam pūravo vṛtrahaṇaṁ sacante | vaiśvānaro dasyum agnir jaghanvām̐ adhūnot kāṣṭhā ava śambaram bhet ||
  • pra nū mahitvaṁ: This phrase can be translated as "let us speak of the great power." It emphasizes the importance of acknowledging and discussing the greatness of a divine force or entity.
  • vṛṣabhasya vocaṁ: This means "the voice of the bull." In Vedic literature, the bull often symbolizes strength, fertility, and the divine. It can represent the power of nature and the universe.
  • yam pūravo vṛtrahaṇaṁ sacante: This translates to "who, in the past, has slain the demon Vritra." Vritra is a serpent or dragon-like figure in Vedic mythology, representing chaos and obstruction. The act of slaying Vritra symbolizes the triumph of order over chaos.
  • vaiśvānaro: This term refers to the universal fire or the divine fire that sustains life. It is often associated with the energy that connects all beings.
  • dasyum agnir jaghanvām̐: This means "the fire that destroys the enemy." Here, fire symbolizes purification and the removal of negativity or obstacles in life.
  • adhūnot kāṣṭhā: This translates to "he who has removed the wood." In this context, wood can symbolize the material aspects of life that can hinder spiritual progress.
  • ava śambaram bhet: This means "let us break the barriers." It signifies the importance of overcoming obstacles and limitations in our lives.

  • Significance: This mantra encapsulates the essence of overcoming challenges and recognizing the divine power that aids in this process. It serves as a reminder of the strength within us and the universal forces that support our journey.
  • Real-life Application: In everyday life, this mantra can be applied when facing difficulties or obstacles. For instance, when dealing with personal challenges such as health issues, financial struggles, or emotional turmoil, one can invoke the spirit of this mantra to find strength and clarity. It encourages individuals to seek the inner fire that drives them to overcome adversities.

  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a powerful invocation of strength, resilience, and the divine support available to us. By recognizing and embracing this power, we can navigate through life's challenges with confidence and determination. It encourages a proactive approach to overcoming obstacles, reminding us that we are not alone in our struggles.
