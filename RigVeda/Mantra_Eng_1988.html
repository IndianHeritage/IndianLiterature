मन्त्र: सुप्रैतुः सूयवसो न पन्था दुरनियन्तुः परिप्रीतो न मित्रः |अनर्वाणो अभि ये चक्षते नो 'पिवृता अपोर्णुवन्तो अस्तुः ||
supraituḥ sūyavaso na panthā durniyantuḥ pariprīto na mitraḥ | anarvāṇo abhi ye cakṣate no 'pīvṛtā aporṇuvanto asthuḥ ||
  • supraituḥ: This word can be interpreted as "may they go forth" or "may they progress." It signifies a desire for advancement and success in endeavors.
  • sūyavaso: This term refers to "those who are well-versed" or "knowledgeable." It emphasizes the importance of wisdom and understanding in achieving goals.
  • na panthā: Translated as "there is no path," it suggests that without guidance or direction, one may struggle to find their way.
  • durniyantuḥ: This means "difficult to navigate" or "hard to traverse." It highlights the challenges one may face in life.
  • pariprīto: This word means "to be satisfied" or "content." It reflects the importance of finding fulfillment in one's journey.
  • na mitraḥ: Translated as "not a friend," it suggests that in times of difficulty, one may feel isolated or unsupported.
  • anarvāṇo: This term can be understood as "those who are unrestrained" or "free." It signifies the potential for liberation and the ability to overcome obstacles.
  • abhi ye cakṣate: This phrase means "those who see" or "those who perceive." It emphasizes the value of awareness and insight in navigating life's challenges.
  • no 'pīvṛtā: Translated as "not turned away," it suggests the importance of remaining open and receptive to opportunities.
  • aporṇuvanto: This term means "those who are complete" or "fulfilled." It reflects the idea of achieving wholeness in one's life.
  • asthuh: This word can be interpreted as "may they be" or "let them be." It signifies a wish for positive outcomes and success.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the journey of life, emphasizing the importance of knowledge, guidance, and fulfillment. It acknowledges the challenges one may face but also highlights the potential for liberation and success. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied to personal growth, career advancement, and overcoming obstacles. By seeking knowledge, remaining open to opportunities, and striving for fulfillment, individuals can navigate their paths more effectively and achieve their goals.
