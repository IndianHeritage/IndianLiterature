शग्धि वाजस्य शुभग प्रजावतोऽग्ने बृहतो अध्वरे |सं राया भूयसा सृज मयोभुना तुविद्युम्न यशस्वता ||
śagdhi vājasya subhaga prajāvato 'gne bṛhato adhvare | saṁ rāyā bhūyasā sṛja mayobhunā tuvidyumna yaśasvatā ||
  • śagdhi vājasya: This phrase can be interpreted as "the one who brings forth the essence of strength." In real life, this can relate to individuals or leaders who inspire and empower others, much like a coach who motivates a team to achieve their best.
  • subhaga prajāvato: This translates to "the auspicious one who brings forth progeny." This can be seen in the context of family and community, where nurturing and supportive environments lead to the growth of future generations, similar to how a gardener tends to plants to ensure they flourish.
  • ‘gne bṛhato adhvare: Here, 'gne' refers to "O Fire," and 'bṛhato adhvare' means "in the great sacrifice." Fire is often a symbol of transformation and purification. In everyday life, this can be likened to the challenges we face that ultimately lead to personal growth, much like how a phoenix rises from its ashes.
  • saṁ rāyā bhūyasā sṛja: This means "may you create abundance." This can be applied in various contexts, such as in business, where innovation and creativity lead to prosperity, or in personal life, where positive actions and thoughts can lead to a fulfilling life.
  • mayobhunā tuvidyumna: This phrase can be interpreted as "with the power of the mind, may we achieve success." This emphasizes the importance of mental strength and focus in achieving goals, akin to how athletes visualize their success before a competition.
  • yaśasvatā: This translates to "with glory." It signifies the pursuit of honor and recognition through one's actions. In real life, this can be seen in individuals who strive for excellence in their fields, whether in academics, sports, or arts, and are celebrated for their contributions.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of strength, prosperity, and the transformative power of fire, urging individuals to harness their mental capabilities to create abundance and achieve glory. Its significance lies in its applicability to various aspects of life, from personal development to community building. By embodying the principles outlined in this mantra, individuals can strive for a life filled with purpose, success, and recognition, ultimately contributing to the greater good of society.
