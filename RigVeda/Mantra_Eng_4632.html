ततुरिर वीरो नार्यो विचेताः श्रोताः हवं गृणत उर्व्यूतिः |वसुः शंसो नरां कारुधायाऽ वाजी स्तुतो विदथे दाति वाजम् ||
taturir vīro naryo vicetāḥ śrotā havaṁ gṛṇata urvyūtiḥ | vasuḥ śaṁso narāṁ kārudhāyā vājī stuto vidathe dāti vājam ||
  • taturir vīro naryo vicetāḥ: This phrase can be translated as "the hero, the man, who is wise and discerning." Here, "taturir" refers to a hero or a strong individual, "vīro" emphasizes valor, and "vicetāḥ" indicates wisdom and discernment. In real life, this can be seen in leaders or individuals who make thoughtful decisions in challenging situations, such as a firefighter who bravely assesses a dangerous situation before acting.
  • śrotā havaṁ gṛṇata urvyūtiḥ: This part means "the one who listens and praises the vastness." "Śrotā" refers to a listener, "havaṁ" means to praise or invoke, and "urvyūtiḥ" signifies vastness or expansiveness. This can be related to someone who appreciates the beauty of nature, like a poet who listens to the sounds of the environment and expresses them through their art, thereby connecting with the vastness of the world.
  • vasuḥ śaṁso narāṁ kārudhāyā: This translates to "the good one, who brings peace to men." "Vasuḥ" means good or wealth, "śaṁso" refers to peace or prosperity, and "narāṁ" indicates men or humanity. This can be exemplified by community leaders who work towards social harmony and welfare, such as those who organize peace talks in conflict zones.
  • vājī stuto vidathe dāti vājam: This phrase means "the one who is praised in the assembly gives strength." "Vājī" refers to strength or power, "stuto" means praised, "vidathe" indicates an assembly or gathering, and "vājam" signifies strength or resources. This can be seen in a corporate setting where a leader who is recognized for their contributions inspires their team, leading to increased productivity and morale.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the qualities of heroism, wisdom, appreciation of nature, peace, and strength in community. It signifies the importance of these traits in individuals who contribute positively to society. In real life, these attributes are applicable in various scenarios, such as leadership, community service, and personal development. By embodying these qualities, individuals can foster a more harmonious and prosperous environment, ultimately leading to collective growth and well-being.
