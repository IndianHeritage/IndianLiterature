ब्रह्मन् वीर ब्रह्मकृतिं जुषाणो 'र्वाचीनो हरिभिर याहि तुयम् |अस्मिन्न ऊषु सवने मादयस्वोप ब्रह्माणि श्रिणव इमा नः ||
brahman vīra brahmakṛtiṁ juṣāṇo 'rvācīno haribhir yāhi tūyam | asminn ū ṣu savane mādayasvopa brahmāṇi śṛṇava imā naḥ ||
  • Translation: The mantra can be translated as: "O Brahman, the mighty one, who is the creator, come to us with your divine presence. In this sacred offering, may we be filled with joy and may the divine essence hear our prayers."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • Brahman: Refers to the ultimate reality or cosmic spirit in Hindu philosophy.
    • Vīra: Means 'mighty' or 'heroic', indicating strength and valor.
    • Brahmakṛtiṁ: Refers to the creative aspect of Brahman, the one who brings forth creation.
    • Juṣāṇo: Means 'enjoying' or 'delighting in', suggesting a sense of fulfillment and joy.
    • Haribhir: Refers to the divine beings or deities, often associated with Vishnu.
    • Tūyam: Means 'to us', indicating a call for divine presence.
    • Asminn ū ṣu: Refers to 'in this offering' or 'in this sacred act'.
    • Savane: Refers to the act of offering or sacrifice.
    • Mādayasv: Means 'may we be filled with joy' or 'may we rejoice'.
    • Brahmāṇi: Refers to the divine essence or the ultimate reality.
    • Śṛṇava: Means 'hear' or 'listen', indicating a plea for attention from the divine.
    • Imā naḥ: Means 'these prayers of ours', indicating the offerings or prayers being made.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a personal context, when facing challenges, individuals may recite this mantra to invoke strength and creativity from the universe, seeking guidance and support.
    • In community rituals, this mantra can be chanted during festivals or ceremonies to invite divine presence and blessings, fostering a sense of unity and joy among participants.
    • In meditation practices, practitioners may use this mantra to focus their minds on the divine, enhancing their spiritual connection and promoting inner peace.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its invocation of the divine presence and creative energy. It emphasizes the importance of joy, community, and connection with the ultimate reality. In real-life scenarios, it can be applied in personal struggles, community gatherings, and spiritual practices, serving as a reminder of the strength and support available from the universe when one seeks it with sincerity and devotion.
