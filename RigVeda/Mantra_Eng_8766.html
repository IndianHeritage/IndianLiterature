अनु हि त्वा सुतं सोम मदामसि महे समर्यराज्ये |वाजाम् अभि पवमान प्र गाहसे ||
anu hi tvā sutaṁ soma madāmasi mahe samaryarājye | vājām̐ abhi pavamāna pra gāhase ||
  • anu hi tvā: This phrase translates to "Indeed, we follow you." It signifies a sense of devotion and commitment to a higher power or principle.
  • sutaṁ soma: "Soma" refers to a sacred drink in Vedic tradition, often associated with immortality and divine inspiration. "Sutaṁ" means "son" or "offspring," indicating a connection to divine lineage.
  • madāmasi: This means "we are intoxicated" or "we are filled with joy." It reflects a state of bliss or ecstasy that comes from spiritual experiences or divine blessings.
  • mahe samaryarājye: "Mahā" means "great," and "samaryarājye" refers to "in the great kingdom" or "in the realm of greatness." This suggests a connection to a higher state of existence or a divine realm.
  • vājām̐ abhi: "Vājām̐" means "strength" or "power," and "abhi" indicates "towards" or "in the direction of." This implies a pursuit of strength and empowerment.
  • pavamāna: This term refers to "the purifying one" or "the one who brings purity." It is often associated with the divine or spiritual forces that cleanse and elevate.
  • pra gāhase: This means "you are invoked" or "you are called upon." It indicates a plea for divine assistance or presence in one's life.

In conclusion, this mantra encapsulates a deep yearning for divine connection and empowerment. It reflects the human desire to seek guidance from higher powers, to be filled with joy and strength, and to aspire towards greatness. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applicable in moments of seeking inspiration, during rituals, or when one is in need of strength to overcome challenges. It serves as a reminder of the importance of spiritual connection and the pursuit of higher ideals in our daily lives.
