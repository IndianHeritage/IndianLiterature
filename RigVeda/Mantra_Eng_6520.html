सुप्रावर्गं सुवीर्यं सुष्टु वार्यम अनाधृष्टं रक्षस्विना |अस्मिन्न ā वाम āयाने वाजिनीवासू विश्वा वामानि धीमहि ||
suprāvargaṁ suvīryaṁ suṣṭhu vāryam anādhṛṣṭaṁ rakṣasvinā | asminn ā vām āyāne vājinīvasū viśvā vāmāni dhīmahi ||
  • suprāvargaṁ: This term can be broken down into "su" meaning good or auspicious, and "prāvargaṁ" which refers to a path or direction. Thus, it signifies a good or auspicious path.
  • suvīryaṁ: "Su" again means good, and "vīryaṁ" refers to strength or valor. This word emphasizes the importance of having good strength or valor in one's endeavors.
  • suṣṭhu: This word means well or properly, indicating that actions should be performed with care and precision.
  • vāryam: This term refers to something that is to be protected or preserved. It highlights the need for safeguarding what is valuable.
  • anādhṛṣṭaṁ: This can be interpreted as something that is not to be disregarded or neglected. It emphasizes the importance of attention and respect towards certain aspects of life.
  • rakṣasvinā: This word refers to being protected or guarded. It suggests the presence of protective forces or energies in our lives.
  • asminn: This means "in this" or "in this context," indicating a specific situation or environment.
  • ā vām: This phrase can be understood as "to you" or "towards you," suggesting a direction or intention towards a particular goal or entity.
  • āyāne: This term refers to a journey or a path, indicating movement towards a destination.
  • vājinīvasū: This can be interpreted as "the best of the best," referring to the highest qualities or attributes one can possess.
  • viśvā: This word means "all" or "universal," indicating inclusivity and the collective nature of existence.
  • vāmāni: This term refers to "your qualities" or "your attributes," emphasizing the importance of recognizing and valuing one's own strengths.
  • dhīmahi: This means "we contemplate" or "we meditate," suggesting a reflective and thoughtful approach to life.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of following a good and auspicious path while embodying strength and valor. It encourages individuals to act with care and protect what is valuable in their lives. The call for attention and respect towards certain aspects of life, along with the notion of being guarded and protected, highlights the significance of community and support systems. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts such as personal development, leadership, and teamwork. By contemplating our qualities and striving for the best, we can create a harmonious and prosperous environment for ourselves and those around us. This mantra serves as a reminder to be mindful of our actions and the paths we choose, ultimately guiding us towards a fulfilling and meaningful life.
