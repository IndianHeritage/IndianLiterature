एव नः सोम परिषिच्यमानो वयो दधच चित्रतमम् पवस्व | अद्वेषे dyावापृथिवी हुवेम देवाः धत्त रयिम अस्मे सुवीरम् ||
evā naḥ soma pariṣicyamāno vayo dadhac citratamam pavasva | adveṣe dyāvāpṛthivī huvema devā dhatta rayim asme suvīram ||
  • evā naḥ soma pariṣicyamāno: This phrase can be translated as "May the Soma, which is being poured upon us." Soma is a sacred drink in Vedic tradition, often associated with immortality and divine inspiration. In real life, this can symbolize the nourishment we receive from nature and the universe, akin to how we seek inspiration and sustenance from our surroundings.
  • vayo dadhac citratamam pavasva: This translates to "May you, O Soma, bestow upon us the most excellent vitality." Here, "vayo" refers to vitality or life force, and "citratamam" means the best or most excellent. This can be related to the importance of health and well-being in our lives. For example, engaging in physical activities, consuming nutritious food, and maintaining mental health can be seen as ways to invite this vitality into our lives.
  • adveṣe dyāvāpṛthivī huvema: This means "We invoke the heavens and the earth without enmity." It emphasizes harmony and peace between the elements of nature. In a real-life context, this can be reflected in our relationships with the environment and each other. For instance, promoting sustainable practices and fostering community relationships can embody this principle of non-enmity.
  • devā dhatta rayim asme suvīram: This translates to "May the gods grant us wealth and strength." "Rayim" refers to wealth or prosperity, and "suvīram" means strength or valor. In practical terms, this can be interpreted as a prayer for success in our endeavors, whether in our careers, personal lives, or community contributions. For example, striving for excellence in our work and supporting one another can lead to collective prosperity.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a profound message of seeking divine nourishment, vitality, harmony with nature, and the blessings of wealth and strength. Its significance lies in its call for a balanced and prosperous life, where we draw inspiration from the universe, maintain our health, foster peaceful relationships, and strive for success. In real-life scenarios, this mantra encourages us to live mindfully, respect our environment, and support each other in our journeys, ultimately leading to a fulfilling and harmonious existence.
