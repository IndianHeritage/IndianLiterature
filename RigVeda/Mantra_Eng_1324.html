त्रिवन्धुरेण त्रिवृतā रथेन त्रिचक्रेण सुवृतā यातम् अर्वाक् |पिन्वतं गा जिन्वतम अर्वतो नो वर्धयतं अश्विनā वीरम् अस्मे ||
trivandhureṇa trivṛtā rathena tricakreṇa suvṛtā yātam arvāk | pinvataṁ gā jinvatam arvato no vardhayatam aśvinā vīram asme ||
  • Mantra: "trivandhureṇa trivṛtā rathena tricakreṇa suvṛtā yātam arvāk | pinvataṁ gā jinvatam arvato no vardhayatam aśvinā vīram asme ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • trivandhureṇa: With three bindings or connections.
    • trivṛtā: Threefold or triple.
    • rathena: By the chariot.
    • tricakreṇa: With three wheels.
    • suvṛtā: Well-directed or well-formed.
    • yātam: Let us go or proceed.
    • arvāk: Towards the front or forward.
    • pinvataṁ: Let us enjoy or partake.
    • gā: Cows or wealth.
    • jinvatam: Victorious or conquering.
    • arvato: From the front or forward.
    • no: Our.
    • vardhayatam: May it increase or grow.
    • aśvinā: By the Ashvins (the twin gods of medicine and health).
    • vīram: Courage or valor.
    • asme: To us or for us.
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra invokes the Ashvins, who are known for their healing powers and ability to bring prosperity.
    • The phrase "trivandhureṇa trivṛtā" suggests a journey or endeavor that is well-supported and stable, akin to a chariot with three strong bindings.
    • The "tricakreṇa" indicates a vehicle that is well-equipped, symbolizing the importance of having the right tools and support in life.
    • In real life, this can be compared to a well-planned project where all aspects are considered, ensuring a smooth journey towards success.
    • The call to "pinvataṁ gā" emphasizes the importance of enjoying the fruits of one's labor, akin to celebrating achievements in personal or professional life.
    • The mantra encourages us to seek victory and growth, reminding us that with the right support and direction, we can overcome challenges and achieve our goals.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of support, direction, and the pursuit of success in life.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, such as in business ventures, personal goals, and health journeys.
    • By invoking the Ashvins, we seek their blessings for courage and prosperity, reminding us to appreciate our achievements and strive for continuous growth.
