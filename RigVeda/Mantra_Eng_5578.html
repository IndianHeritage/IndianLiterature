अमीवाहा वास्तोष पते विश्वा रूपाण्य आविशन् |सखा सुशेवा एधि नः ||
amīvahā vāstoṣ pate viśvā rūpāṇy āviśan | sakhā suśeva edhi naḥ ||
  • amīvahā: This term can be interpreted as "the one who brings together" or "the unifier." It signifies the importance of unity and harmony in various aspects of life.
  • vāstoṣ pate: This phrase translates to "O Lord of the dwelling" or "O Lord of the house." It refers to the divine presence that resides in our homes and environments, emphasizing the sanctity of our living spaces.
  • viśvā rūpāṇy: This means "all forms" or "universal forms." It suggests the diversity and multiplicity of existence, highlighting that everything in the universe has its unique form and purpose.
  • āviśan: This word translates to "have entered" or "have come into being." It indicates the manifestation of these forms in our lives and surroundings.
  • sakhā: This term means "friend" or "companion." It emphasizes the importance of relationships and companionship in our journey through life.
  • suśeva: This translates to "well-being" or "goodness." It signifies the pursuit of positive outcomes and the importance of nurturing a good environment.
  • edhi: This word means "may you grow" or "may you prosper." It is a blessing for growth, prosperity, and success in various endeavors.
  • naḥ: This means "for us" or "to us," indicating a collective aspect, suggesting that the blessings and growth are sought not just for the individual but for the community as a whole.

Conclusion:

The mantra "amīvahā vāstoṣ pate viśvā rūpāṇy āviśan | sakhā suśeva edhi naḥ ||" encapsulates the essence of unity, divine presence, diversity, companionship, and well-being. In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios such as:

  • In family settings, it encourages harmony and togetherness, reminding us to create a nurturing environment for all members.
  • In community gatherings, it emphasizes the importance of friendship and collective well-being, fostering a sense of belonging and support.
  • In workplaces, it highlights the significance of collaboration and mutual growth, promoting a positive and productive atmosphere.

Overall, this mantra serves as a reminder to seek unity and well-being in all aspects of life, encouraging us to create spaces that are not only physically comfortable but also spiritually enriching.
