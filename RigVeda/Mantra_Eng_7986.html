आ योनिम अरुणो रुहद गमद इन्द्रं वृषा सुतः |ध्रुवे सदसि सीदति ||
ā yonim aruṇo ruhad gamad indraṁ vṛṣā sutaḥ | dhruve sadasi sīdati ||
  • ā yonim: This phrase can be interpreted as "from the womb" or "from the source." It signifies the origin or the beginning of something.
  • aruṇo: This word means "the reddish one" or "the dawn." It symbolizes new beginnings, hope, and the arrival of light after darkness.
  • ruhad: This term translates to "great" or "vast." It denotes something of significant magnitude or importance.
  • gamad: This means "to go" or "to move." It indicates action, progress, or the journey towards a goal.
  • indraṁ: Referring to "Indra," the king of gods in Vedic literature, associated with strength, power, and rain. Indra represents the force that drives away obstacles.
  • vṛṣā sutaḥ: This translates to "the son of strength" or "the powerful offspring." It emphasizes the idea of inheriting strength or power.
  • dhruve: This word means "steadfast" or "constant." It signifies stability and reliability.
  • sadasi: This term refers to "in the assembly" or "in the gathering." It indicates a collective or community aspect.
  • sīdati: This means "to sit" or "to reside." It suggests a sense of belonging or presence in a particular space.

The mantra can be interpreted as a call to the divine for strength and guidance, emphasizing the importance of community and the journey towards achieving greatness. It reflects the transition from darkness to light, symbolizing hope and new beginnings.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios:

  • In personal development, it encourages individuals to recognize their origins and strengths, motivating them to pursue their goals with determination.
  • In community settings, it highlights the importance of collective strength and support, reminding us that we can achieve more together than alone.
  • In times of challenge, invoking the qualities of Indra can inspire resilience and the ability to overcome obstacles.

Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its ability to inspire individuals and communities to embrace their origins, harness their strengths, and work together towards a brighter future. It serves as a reminder that with steadfastness and unity, one can navigate the challenges of life and emerge victorious.
