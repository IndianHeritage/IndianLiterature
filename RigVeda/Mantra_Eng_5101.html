उद ū आयाम् उपवक्तेव बाहू हिरण्यया सविता सुप्रतीका |दिवो रोहांस्य अरुहत पृथिव्या आरीरमत पतयत कच चिद अभ्वम् ||
ud ū ayām̐ upavakteva bāhū hiraṇyayā savitā supratīkā | divo rohāṁsy aruhat pṛthivyā arīramat patayat kac cid abhvam ||
  • ud ū ayām̐ upavakteva bāhū hiraṇyayā savitā supratīkā
    • ud ū ayām̐: This phrase can be interpreted as "rise up" or "elevate." It signifies the call for awakening or enlightenment.
    • upavakteva: This means "as if speaking" or "as if proclaiming." It suggests a communication or declaration of intent.
    • bāhū: Translated as "arms," it symbolizes strength and capability.
    • hiraṇyayā: Meaning "golden," it represents purity, value, and auspiciousness.
    • savitā: Referring to the Sun God, Savitā embodies life, energy, and illumination.
    • supratīkā: This term means "well-formed" or "well-structured," indicating a sense of order and harmony.
  • divo rohāṁsy aruhat pṛthivyā:
    • divo: Meaning "of the heavens," it connects the mantra to celestial or divine realms.
    • rohāṁsy: This translates to "the heights" or "the elevated places," suggesting aspirations or goals.
    • aruhat: Meaning "ascended" or "climbed," it indicates progress or achievement.
    • pṛthivyā: Referring to "the earth," it symbolizes the material world and our physical existence.
  • arīramat patayat kac cid abhvam:
    • arīramat: This can be interpreted as "to be free" or "to be liberated," indicating a state of release from bondage.
    • patayat: Meaning "to fly" or "to soar," it suggests freedom and the ability to transcend limitations.
    • kac cid: This phrase means "perhaps" or "maybe," indicating uncertainty or contemplation.
    • abhvam: Translated as "to be" or "to exist," it emphasizes the essence of being.

Conclusion:

The mantra encapsulates a profound message of awakening, strength, and aspiration. It calls upon the divine energy of the Sun to elevate our spirits and guide us towards achieving our goals. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios such as personal development, where one seeks to rise above challenges and attain a higher state of consciousness. It encourages individuals to harness their inner strength and strive for excellence while remaining grounded in reality. The significance of this mantra lies in its ability to inspire and motivate individuals to pursue their dreams with clarity and purpose, ultimately leading to a harmonious existence both within themselves and in the world around them.
