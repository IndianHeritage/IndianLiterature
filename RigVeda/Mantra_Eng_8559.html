मन्त्र: भद्रā वस्त्रā समन्यā वसानो महān कविर निवचनानि शंसन |आ वच्यस्व चाम्वोः पूयमानो विचक्षणो जागृविर देववीतौ ||
bhadrā vastrā samanyā3 vasāno mahān kavir nivacanāni śaṁsan | ā vacyasva camvoḥ pūyamāno vicakṣaṇo jāgṛvir devavītau ||
  • bhadrā vastrā: This phrase translates to "auspicious garments." In a broader sense, it signifies the importance of purity and positivity in our lives. Just as we choose our clothes carefully, we should also choose our thoughts and actions.
  • samanyā3 vasāno: This means "wearing common attire." It suggests humility and the idea that one should not be overly attached to material possessions or status. In real life, this can be seen in individuals who prioritize community and equality over personal gain.
  • mahān kavir: Translated as "the great poet" or "the wise one," this refers to a person of wisdom and insight. In our lives, this could be a mentor or a teacher who guides us through challenges with their knowledge.
  • nivacanāni śaṁsan: This means "speaking words of truth." It emphasizes the importance of honesty and integrity in communication. For example, in a workplace, transparent communication fosters trust and collaboration among team members.
  • ā vacyasva: This translates to "speak out." It encourages individuals to express their thoughts and beliefs openly. In a democratic society, this is crucial for the exchange of ideas and the promotion of social justice.
  • camvoḥ pūyamāno: This phrase means "being filled with the essence of the divine." It suggests that one should strive to embody virtues such as compassion and kindness. In everyday life, this can be seen in acts of charity and community service.
  • vicakṣaṇo: This translates to "the wise one." It highlights the value of wisdom in decision-making. For instance, a wise leader considers the long-term effects of their actions on the community.
  • jāgṛvir devavītau: This means "the awakened ones, the divine." It signifies the importance of being aware and conscious of our surroundings and actions. In real life, mindfulness practices can help individuals stay present and make better choices.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of wisdom, humility, and truthfulness in our lives. It encourages us to be mindful of our actions and to strive for a life filled with positive intentions and community spirit. In real-life scenarios, these principles can guide us in personal relationships, professional environments, and societal interactions, ultimately leading to a more harmonious and fulfilling existence.
