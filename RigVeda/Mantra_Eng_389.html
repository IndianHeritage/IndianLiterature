अयुयुत्सन्न अनवद्यस्य सेनाम् आयातयन्त क्षितयो नवग्वाः |वृषायुधो न वध्रयो निरष्टाः प्रवाद्भिर इन्द्राच् चितयन्त आयन ||
ayuyutsann anavadyasya senām ayātayanta kṣitayo navagvāḥ | vṛṣāyudho na vadhrayo niraṣṭāḥ pravadbhir indrāc citayanta āyan ||
  • ayuyutsann anavadyasya: This phrase can be translated to "the one who is unblemished and does not engage in conflict." In real life, this can refer to individuals who strive for peace and harmony, avoiding unnecessary disputes. For example, a mediator in a community conflict who seeks to resolve issues without taking sides embodies this quality.
  • senām ayātayanta: This means "those who lead the army." In a modern context, this could refer to leaders or managers who guide their teams towards achieving common goals. For instance, a project manager who effectively coordinates their team to meet deadlines exemplifies this leadership.
  • kṣitayo navagvāḥ: This translates to "the new steeds of the earth." This can symbolize new beginnings or fresh opportunities. In everyday life, this could relate to starting a new job or embarking on a new venture, representing hope and potential for growth.
  • vṛṣāyudho na vadhrayo: This phrase means "the one who wields the powerful weapon." In contemporary terms, this could refer to individuals who possess knowledge or skills that empower them to make significant changes. For example, a scientist developing a new technology that addresses climate change wields a powerful weapon in the fight for environmental sustainability.
  • niraṣṭāḥ pravadbhir indrāc: This can be interpreted as "those who are not abandoned by Indra." Indra, in Vedic literature, is often seen as a protector and a source of strength. This suggests that those who act with integrity and righteousness are supported by higher powers. In real life, this could be seen in individuals who, despite challenges, find support from their community or mentors when they uphold their values.
  • citayanta āyan: This translates to "those who gather and create paths." This can refer to innovators and visionaries who pave the way for future generations. For instance, entrepreneurs who create new industries or social movements that inspire change are actively creating paths for others to follow.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of integrity, leadership, and the pursuit of peace. It highlights the significance of individuals who lead with strength and righteousness, supported by a higher purpose. In real life, these qualities are applicable in various scenarios, such as in leadership roles, community service, and personal development. By embodying these principles, individuals can contribute positively to society and inspire others to do the same, ultimately leading to a more harmonious and progressive world.
