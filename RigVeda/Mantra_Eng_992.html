त्वं सोमासि सत्पतिस् त्वं राजोऽत वृत्रहा |त्वं भद्रोऽसि क्रतुः ||
tvaṁ somāsi satpatis tvaṁ rājota vṛtrahā | tvam bhadro asi kratuḥ ||
  • tvaṁ somāsi satpatis: This phrase translates to "You are the essence of Soma, the lord of the seven." In Vedic literature, Soma is often associated with a divine drink that grants immortality and is linked to the moon. The term "satpatis" refers to the seven sages or the seven principles of existence. This suggests that the essence of life and consciousness is interconnected with divine wisdom.
  • tvaṁ rājota vṛtrahā: This means "You are the king and the slayer of Vritra." Vritra is a serpent or dragon in Vedic mythology that represents chaos and obstruction. By referring to the divine as the "slayer of Vritra," it emphasizes the power of the divine to overcome obstacles and restore order. In real life, this can be seen as a metaphor for overcoming personal challenges and adversities.
  • tvam bhadro asi kratuḥ: This translates to "You are the auspicious one, the embodiment of intention." The word "bhadro" signifies auspiciousness and goodness, while "kratuḥ" refers to intention or will. This highlights the importance of having a positive and purposeful mindset in our actions. In everyday life, this can be applied to setting goals and striving for positive outcomes in various endeavors.

  • Significance of the Mantra: This mantra encapsulates the essence of divine wisdom, the power to overcome obstacles, and the importance of positive intention. It serves as a reminder that the divine is not only a source of strength but also a guiding force in our lives.
  • Real-Life Application: In practical terms, this mantra can be invoked during challenging times when one faces obstacles, whether in personal life, career, or health. By meditating on the qualities of the divine as described in the mantra, individuals can find the strength to overcome their challenges and align their intentions with positive outcomes.
  • Conclusion: The mantra encourages individuals to recognize the divine presence in their lives, to seek wisdom, and to cultivate a positive mindset. By embodying these principles, one can navigate life's challenges with resilience and purpose, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling existence.
