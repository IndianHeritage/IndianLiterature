मक्षू कनायाः सख्यं नवग्वा ऋतं वदन्त ऋतयुक्तिम अग्मन |द्विबर्हसो य उप गोपम् आगुर अदक्षिणासो अच्युताऽदुदुक्षन् ||
makṣū kanāyāḥ sakhyaṁ navagvā ṛtaṁ vadanta ṛtayuktim agman | dvibarhaso ya upa gopam āgur adakṣiṇāso acyutā dudukṣan ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • makṣū: Refers to the "mighty" or "powerful".
    • kanāyāḥ: Means "of the one who is connected" or "of the one who is united".
    • sakhyaṁ: Translates to "friendship" or "companionship".
    • navagvā: Refers to "new" or "fresh" and can also imply "newly born".
    • ṛtaṁ: Means "truth" or "cosmic order".
    • vadanta: Translates to "speaking" or "saying".
    • ṛtayuktim: Refers to "the principle of truth" or "the path of righteousness".
    • agman: Means "they have come" or "they have arrived".
    • dvibarhaso: Refers to "two-fold" or "dual".
    • ya: Means "who" or "that".
    • upa: Translates to "near" or "approaching".
    • gopam: Refers to "the protector" or "the guardian".
    • āgur: Means "they have invoked" or "they have called upon".
    • adakṣiṇāso: Refers to "those who are not inclined" or "those who do not turn away".
    • acyutā: Means "the infallible" or "the eternal".
    • dudukṣan: Refers to "they have sought" or "they have desired".
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the power of unity and friendship, emphasizing the importance of coming together to speak the truth and uphold righteousness.
    • The phrase "newly born" suggests the idea of renewal and fresh beginnings, which can be applied in various life situations, such as starting a new project or entering a new phase in life.
    • The mention of "the protector" indicates the need for guidance and support in our endeavors, highlighting the role of mentors or leaders in our lives.
    • The concept of "the infallible" suggests a higher truth or principle that remains constant, which can be related to moral values or universal truths that guide human behavior.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace setting, this mantra can be applied to foster teamwork and collaboration, where individuals come together to achieve a common goal while upholding ethical standards.
    • In personal relationships, it emphasizes the importance of friendship and support, encouraging individuals to be there for one another during challenging times.
    • In community service, the idea of invoking the protector can relate to leaders who guide and inspire others to work towards the greater good.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of unity, truth, and support in various aspects of life.
    • It encourages individuals to seek companionship and guidance while remaining committed to righteousness and moral values.
    • In essence, it serves as a reminder that through collaboration and adherence to truth, we can navigate life's challenges more effectively.
