यद अश्वस्य क्रविषो मक्षिकाश यद वा स्वरौ स्वधितौ रिप्तम अस्ति |यद धस्तयोः शमितुर यन् नखेषु सर्वा ता ते अपि देवेष्व अस्तु ||
yad aśvasya kraviṣo makṣikāśa yad vā svarau svadhitau riptam asti | yad dhastayoḥ śamitur yan nakheṣu sarvā tā te api deveṣv astu ||
  • Mantra: "yad aśvasya kraviṣo makṣikāśa yad vā svarau svadhitau riptam asti | yad dhastayoḥ śamitur yan nakheṣu sarvā tā te api deveṣv astu ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yad: whatever
    • aśvasya: of the horse
    • kraviṣo: of the one who is strong or powerful
    • makṣikāśa: of the insects or small creatures
    • yad vā: or whatever
    • svarau: of the sounds or voices
    • svadhitau: of the self or one's own
    • riptam: that which is filled or complete
    • asti: exists
    • yad: whatever
    • dhastayoḥ: of the hands
    • śamitur: of peace or tranquility
    • yan: that which
    • nakheṣu: in the nails
    • sarvā: all
    • tā: those
    • te: to you
    • api: also
    • deveṣv: among the gods
    • astu: may it be
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks to the interconnectedness of all beings and the divine.
    • It emphasizes that whatever exists in the powerful horse, in the small insects, in the sounds, and in the hands, is also present among the gods.
    • The mantra suggests a holistic view of existence, where every element, no matter how small or large, contributes to the greater whole.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In nature, the role of every creature, from the largest mammal to the smallest insect, is crucial for maintaining ecological balance.
    • In human society, every individual's contribution, regardless of their position, is essential for the functioning of the community.
    • In personal development, recognizing the value of every experience, whether positive or negative, helps in achieving a well-rounded perspective on life.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its reminder of the unity and interdependence of all forms of life.
    • It encourages respect for all beings and an understanding that every action has a ripple effect in the universe.
    • This perspective can be applied in daily life by fostering compassion, promoting environmental sustainability, and valuing diversity in communities.
