नेह भद्रं रक्षस्विन नावेय नोपयाय उता |गवे च भद्रं धेनवे वीराय च श्रवस्यते 'नेहसो व ऊतयः सूतयः व ऊतयः ||
neha bhadraṁ rakṣasvine nāvayai nopayā uta | gave ca bhadraṁ dhenave vīrāya ca śravasyate 'nehaso va ūtayaḥ suūtayo va ūtayaḥ ||
  • Translation: The mantra can be translated as: "May we not be harmed by the protectors; may we not be obstructed by the obstacles. May the auspiciousness of the cows and the valor of the heroes be with us, and may the auspiciousness of the heavens be with us."
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • neha: here, in this context
    • bhadraṁ: auspiciousness, goodness
    • rakṣasvine: protectors, guardians
    • nā: not
    • vayai: by us, to us
    • nopayā: not obstructed
    • uta: or, and
    • gave: of the cows
    • ca: and
    • dhenave: of the milch cows
    • vīrāya: to the hero, the brave
    • ca: and
    • śravasyate: may it be renowned, may it be praised
    • 'nehaso: of the heavens
    • va: or
    • ūtayaḥ: auspiciousness, blessings
    • suūtayo: good auspiciousness
    • va: or
    • ūtayaḥ: auspiciousness, blessings
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • This mantra can be recited before starting any new venture, such as a business or a project, to invoke protection and auspiciousness.
    • In a family setting, it can be chanted during rituals or ceremonies to ensure the well-being and safety of family members.
    • In a community gathering, it can be used to foster unity and strength among participants, encouraging them to support each other against challenges.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its invocation of protection and auspiciousness. It emphasizes the importance of community, valor, and the blessings of nature (represented by cows). In real life, it serves as a reminder to seek support from protectors, whether they are family, friends, or the environment, and to strive for goodness and valor in our actions. By reciting this mantra, individuals can cultivate a sense of security and positivity in their lives, making it applicable in various scenarios, from personal endeavors to communal activities.
