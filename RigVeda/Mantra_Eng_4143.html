रथं नु मारुतं वयं श्रवस्युम आ हुवामहे |आ यस्मिन तस्थौ सुराणां बिभ्रती सचा मरुत्सु रोधसी ||
rathaṁ nu mārutaṁ vayaṁ śravasyum ā huvāmahe | ā yasmin tasthau suraṇāni bibhratī sacā marutsu rodasī ||
  • Translation: "We invoke the Maruts, the powerful ones, who carry the chariot; in whom the divine beings reside, and who hold the heavens and the earth together."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • rathaṁ: Chariot - Symbolizes movement, progress, and the journey of life.
    • mārutaṁ: Maruts - The storm deities, representing power, energy, and the forces of nature.
    • vayaṁ: We - Indicates collective invocation, emphasizing community and shared purpose.
    • śravasyum: Invoking - The act of calling upon divine forces for assistance and guidance.
    • ā huvāmahe: We call upon - A plea for connection with the divine, seeking their presence in our lives.
    • yasmin: In whom - Refers to the divine beings residing within the Maruts.
    • suraṇāni: Divine beings - Represents the higher powers and spiritual entities.
    • bibhratī: Holding - Suggests stability and support provided by these divine forces.
    • sacā: With truth - Emphasizes the importance of truth and righteousness in our endeavors.
    • marutsu: In the storms - Symbolizes challenges and the dynamic nature of life.
    • rodasī: Heavens and earth - Represents the duality of existence and the interconnectedness of all things.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community project, when people come together to achieve a common goal, they invoke the spirit of collaboration and support, much like calling upon the Maruts for strength.
    • During challenging times, such as natural disasters, communities often seek divine intervention or support, reflecting the invocation of the Maruts to help navigate through difficulties.
    • In personal growth, individuals may call upon their inner strength and the support of their community, akin to the Maruts holding the heavens and earth together, to overcome obstacles.
  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of invoking divine support and recognizing the interconnectedness of all beings. It teaches us that in times of need, we can call upon higher powers and the strength of our community to navigate life's challenges. This invocation is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal struggles to collective efforts in society, reminding us of the power of unity, truth, and divine guidance in our journeys.
