सदृशीर अद्य सदृशीर इदु श्वो दीर्घं साचन्ते वरुणस्य धाम |अनवद्यास त्रिंशतं योजनान्य एकैकां क्रतुम् पर्य यन्ति सद्यः ||
sadṛśīr adya sadṛśīr id u śvo dīrghaṁ sacante varuṇasya dhāma | anavadyās triṁśataṁ yojanāny ekaikā kratum pari yanti sadyaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sadṛśīr: similar, alike
    • adya: today
    • id: this
    • u: and
    • śvo: tomorrow
    • dīrghaṁ: long, extensive
    • sacante: they attain, they reach
    • varuṇasya: of Varuna (the god of cosmic order)
    • dhāma: abode, realm
    • anavadyās: without blemish, pure
    • triṁśataṁ: thirty
    • yojanāny: units of measurement (approximately 8 miles each)
    • ekaikā: each
    • kratum: intention, resolve
    • pari: around, encompassing
    • yanti: they go, they proceed
    • sadyaḥ: immediately, swiftly
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the alignment of the present and the future, emphasizing the importance of intentions and actions.
    • It suggests that today and tomorrow are interconnected, and through pure intentions, one can reach the divine abode of Varuna.
    • The mention of "thirty yojanas" signifies a vast distance, symbolizing the extensive journey one undertakes in life through their actions and intentions.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, setting clear intentions and goals can lead to success. For instance, a project manager who outlines a clear vision for a project can inspire the team to work towards that goal, leading to successful project completion.
    • In personal relationships, having pure intentions and open communication can strengthen bonds. For example, a couple that communicates their feelings and intentions can build a stronger, more resilient relationship.
    • In spiritual practices, individuals who meditate with a clear intention often find a deeper connection to their spiritual beliefs, akin to reaching the divine realm mentioned in the mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of intentions and actions in shaping our present and future.
    • It teaches that by maintaining purity in our thoughts and actions, we can navigate the vast journey of life and reach our goals, both material and spiritual.
    • In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios, from personal development to professional success, emphasizing the importance of clarity and purity in our intentions.
