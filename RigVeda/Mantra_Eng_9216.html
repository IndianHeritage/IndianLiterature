युवं ह भुज्युं युवम अश्विनाऽवशं युवं शiñजाम उषणाम उपारथुः |युवो ररावाऽपि सख्यम आसते युवोर अहम् अवसाऽसुम्नम् आ चके ||
yuvaṁ ha bhujyuṁ yuvam aśvinā vaśaṁ yuvaṁ śiñjāram uśanām upārathuḥ | yuvo rarāvā pari sakhyam āsate yuvor aham avasā sumnam ā cake ||
  • Mantra: "yuvaṁ ha bhujyuṁ yuvam aśvinā vaśaṁ yuvaṁ śiñjāram uśanām upārathuḥ | yuvo rarāvā pari sakhyam āsate yuvor aham avasā sumnam ā cake ||"
  • Translation: This mantra can be interpreted as a call for youthful energy and vitality, invoking the divine Aśvins (the twin gods of health and medicine) to bestow their blessings. It emphasizes the importance of friendship, support, and the collective strength of youth.
  • Word Breakdown:
    • yuvaṁ: youth or youthful energy
    • bhujyuṁ: strength or power
    • aśvinā: referring to the Aśvins, deities associated with health and well-being
    • vaśaṁ: control or influence
    • śiñjāram: a term that can imply harmony or balance
    • uśanām: referring to the light or illumination, often associated with knowledge
    • upārathuḥ: to come together or unite
    • rarāvā: to be strong or powerful
    • sakhyam: friendship or companionship
    • āsate: to sit or dwell
    • aham: I or self
    • avasā: to be in a state of readiness or preparedness
    • sumnam: auspiciousness or good fortune
    • ā cake: to achieve or attain
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • This mantra can be applied in the context of youth empowerment programs, where the focus is on harnessing the energy and creativity of young people to bring about positive change in society.
    • In team sports, the mantra reflects the importance of teamwork and camaraderie, where each member's strength contributes to the overall success of the team.
    • In educational settings, it emphasizes the role of mentorship and guidance from experienced individuals (akin to the Aśvins) to help youth navigate challenges and achieve their goals.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its celebration of youth, strength, and the power of unity. It serves as a reminder that when individuals come together, they can achieve great things. In real life, this mantra can inspire initiatives that promote collaboration, health, and well-being among young people, fostering an environment where they can thrive and contribute positively to society.
