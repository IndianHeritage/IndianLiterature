उद उष्रियाः सृजते सूर्यः सच्चाम् उद्यान नक्षत्रम् आर्चिवत् |तवेद उषो व्युषि सूर्यस्य च सम भक्तेन गमेमहि ||
ud usriyāḥ sṛjate sūryaḥ sacām̐ udyan nakṣatram arcivat | taved uṣo vyuṣi sūryasya ca sam bhaktena gamemahi ||
  • ud usriyāḥ sṛjate sūryaḥ: This phrase translates to "the dawns create the sun." Here, "ud usriyāḥ" refers to the dawns or the early morning light, while "sṛjate sūryaḥ" means to bring forth or create the sun. This can be understood as the transition from darkness to light, symbolizing new beginnings and hope.
  • sacām̐ udyan nakṣatram arcivat: This part means "the stars shine brightly as the sun rises." "Sacām̐" refers to the stars, "udyān" means to rise, and "arcivat" implies shining or illuminating. This illustrates the idea that even as the sun rises, the stars still hold their beauty, representing the coexistence of different forms of light and guidance in our lives.
  • taved uṣo vyuṣi sūryasya ca: This translates to "may we attain the dawn and the sun." "Taved" means may we attain, "uṣo" refers to the dawn, "vyuṣi" means to rise or emerge, and "sūryasya ca" refers to the sun. This line expresses a desire for enlightenment and clarity, both literally and metaphorically.
  • sam bhaktena gamemahi: This final phrase means "we move forward with devotion." "Sam" implies together or in harmony, "bhaktena" refers to devotion or faith, and "gamemahi" means we move or progress. This emphasizes the importance of unity and devotion in our journey towards enlightenment and success.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of transformation, enlightenment, and the importance of unity and devotion in our lives. It signifies the transition from darkness to light, both in a physical and spiritual sense. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied to personal growth, where individuals strive to overcome challenges (darkness) and achieve their goals (light). It also highlights the significance of community and support, as we often rely on each other’s strengths and guidance to navigate through life’s journey. The mantra serves as a reminder to embrace new beginnings, appreciate the beauty around us, and move forward with faith and unity.
