कण्वेभिर धृष्णव आ धृषद वाजं दर्षि सहस्रिणम् |पिषङ्गरूपम् मगवन विचर्षणे मक्षू गोमन्तम् ईमहे ||
kaṇvebhir dhṛṣṇav ā dhṛṣad vājaṁ darṣi sahasriṇam | piśaṅgarūpam maghavan vicarṣaṇe makṣū gomantam īmahe ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • kaṇvebhir: By the Kanva sages
    • dhṛṣṇav: With courage or strength
    • ā: To
    • dhṛṣad: The one who is strong
    • vājaṁ: The prize or wealth
    • darṣi: To see or perceive
    • sahasriṇam: Of the thousand
    • piśaṅgarūpam: Of yellowish form
    • maghavan: O Maghavan (a name for Indra)
    • vicarṣaṇe: In the act of moving or roaming
    • makṣū: The one who is swift
    • gomantam: The one who possesses cows
    • īmahe: We invoke or we meditate upon
  • Overall Meaning: This mantra is a prayer invoking the blessings of the divine, particularly Indra, the god of rain and thunder, who is often associated with strength and abundance. The mantra emphasizes the importance of courage and strength in achieving wealth and prosperity, symbolized by the "vājaṁ" (prize or wealth) and "gomantam" (the one who possesses cows, a symbol of wealth in Vedic culture).
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be seen as a reminder for entrepreneurs to approach their ventures with courage and strength, seeking the rewards of their hard work.
    • In personal development, individuals can reflect on the importance of resilience and determination in overcoming challenges to achieve their goals.
    • In community settings, this mantra can inspire collective efforts towards prosperity, encouraging members to support each other in their endeavors.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for strength and courage in the pursuit of wealth and prosperity. It serves as a reminder that success often requires not just hard work, but also the bravery to face challenges and the wisdom to recognize opportunities. In real-life scenarios, whether in personal growth, business, or community efforts, invoking the spirit of this mantra can inspire individuals and groups to strive for their goals with determination and unity.
