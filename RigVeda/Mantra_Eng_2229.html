मन्त्र: त्रातारं त्वा तनूनां हवामहे 'वस्पर्तर अधिवक्तारम् अस्मयुम् | बृहस्पते देवनिदो नि बरहय मा दुरेव उत्तरं सुम्नम् उन्नशन ||
trātāraṁ tvā tanūnāṁ havāmahe 'vaspartar adhivaktāram asmayum | bṛhaspate devanido ni barhaya mā durevā uttaraṁ sumnam un naśan ||
  • Mantra: "trātāraṁ tvā tanūnāṁ havāmahe 'vaspartar adhivaktāram asmayum | bṛhaspate devanido ni barhaya mā durevā uttaraṁ sumnam un naśan ||"
  • Translation: "We invoke you, O protector of our bodies, O Bṛhaspati, the divine speaker, may you grant us strength and prosperity. May you, O divine one, not let us stray far from the path of good fortune."

  • Word Breakdown:
    • trātāraṁ: Protector or savior, referring to a divine entity that offers safety and support.
    • tvā: You, addressing the divine directly.
    • tanūnāṁ: Of our bodies, indicating a connection to physical existence and well-being.
    • havāmahe: We invoke or call upon, a plea for divine intervention.
    • bṛhaspate: A name for the deity associated with wisdom, knowledge, and prosperity.
    • devanido: Divine speaker, emphasizing the importance of communication and guidance from the divine.
    • ni barhaya: May you grant or bestow, a request for blessings.
    • mā durevā: Not let us stray far, a plea for guidance to stay on the right path.
    • uttaraṁ: Good fortune or prosperity, indicating the desired outcome.
    • sumnam: Auspiciousness or well-being, a state of positive existence.
    • un naśan: May it not be taken away, a desire to retain blessings and good fortune.

  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In times of personal crisis, individuals often seek protection and guidance, similar to invoking a higher power for support. For instance, someone facing health issues may recite this mantra to seek strength and healing.
    • In a professional setting, when embarking on a new project, teams may invoke the spirit of collaboration and wisdom, akin to calling upon Bṛhaspati for guidance to ensure success and prosperity.
    • During significant life events, such as weddings or new ventures, people often seek blessings for a prosperous future, reflecting the essence of this mantra in their prayers and rituals.

  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of divine protection, guidance, and the pursuit of prosperity. It serves as a reminder that in our daily lives, we can seek support from higher powers, whether through prayer, meditation, or positive affirmations. By invoking the qualities of wisdom and protection, we align ourselves with the energies that foster well-being and success. This mantra is applicable in various life scenarios, encouraging individuals to remain grounded and focused on their goals while seeking divine assistance in their endeavors.
