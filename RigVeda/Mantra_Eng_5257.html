अग्नेरक्षाणोऽम्हसः प्रतिष्म देवरीषतः। तपिष्ठैरजरो दह।
agne rakṣā ṇo aṁhasaḥ prati ṣma deva rīṣataḥ | tapiṣṭhair ajaro daha ||
  • Agne: This word refers to Agni, the fire god in Vedic literature. Fire symbolizes purity, transformation, and energy.
  • Rakṣā: This means protection or safeguarding. It signifies the need for safety and security in life.
  • ṇo: This is a pronoun meaning "our," indicating a personal connection to the divine.
  • Aṁhasaḥ: This term can be interpreted as "from the darkness" or "from the obscurity," suggesting a desire to move from ignorance to knowledge.
  • Prati: This means "towards" or "in the direction of," indicating a purposeful approach to seeking protection.
  • Ṣma: This word means "to us," reinforcing the personal aspect of the mantra.
  • Deva: This translates to "god" or "divine being," emphasizing the spiritual aspect of the plea for protection.
  • Rīṣataḥ: This term means "to be pleased" or "to be favorable," indicating a desire for divine favor.
  • Tapiṣṭhair: This word refers to "with great heat" or "intensity," suggesting a fervent plea or request.
  • Ajaro: This means "immortal" or "eternal," indicating a longing for lasting protection and guidance.
  • Daha: This translates to "burn" or "consume," which can symbolize the transformative power of fire.

The mantra can be understood as a heartfelt invocation to Agni, seeking protection from darkness and ignorance while requesting divine favor and guidance. It emphasizes the importance of spiritual enlightenment and the transformative power of fire, which can purify and protect.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios:

  • Personal Growth: Just as fire transforms materials, individuals can seek to transform their lives through knowledge and self-improvement.
  • Safety and Security: In times of uncertainty or danger, invoking protection can provide a sense of security and peace of mind.
  • Spiritual Practices: This mantra can be used in meditation or rituals to connect with the divine and seek guidance in one's life journey.

Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine protection and guidance in the pursuit of knowledge and personal transformation. It serves as a reminder of the importance of seeking enlightenment and the protective forces that can aid us in our journey through life.
