दिवश्चिद् आवोऽमवत् तरेभ्यो विभ्वना चिद् आश्वपस्तरेभ्यः |वायोश्चिद् आसोमरभस्तरेभ्योऽग्नेश्चिद् आर्च पितुकृत्तरेभ्यः ||
divaś cid ā vo 'mavattarebhyo vibhvanā cid āśvapastarebhyaḥ | vāyoś cid ā somarabhastarebhyo 'gneś cid arca pitukṛttarebhyaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • Divaś: Of the heavens
    • Cid: Indeed
    • Ā: To us
    • Vo: Your
    • 'Mavattarebhyo: From the most excellent
    • Vibhvanā: From the one who is all-pervading
    • Cid: Indeed
    • Āśvapastarebhyaḥ: From the horses that are swift
    • Vāyoś: Of the wind
    • Cid: Indeed
    • Ā: To us
    • Somarabhastarebhyo: From the invigorating soma
    • 'Agnēś: Of Agni (the fire)
    • Cid: Indeed
    • Arca: Worship
    • Pitukṛttarebhyaḥ: From the offerings made to the ancestors
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the divine forces of the heavens, wind, and fire, seeking blessings and support from these elemental powers.
    • The reference to "swift horses" symbolizes speed and strength, suggesting a desire for rapid progress and success in endeavors.
    • The mention of "soma" indicates a connection to vitality and rejuvenation, often associated with health and well-being.
    • Agni, the fire god, represents transformation and purification, highlighting the importance of spiritual and physical cleansing.
    • The offerings to ancestors signify respect for lineage and the importance of familial bonds in seeking blessings.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern life, this mantra can be recited during significant life events such as weddings or new ventures, invoking divine support for a prosperous journey.
    • In a corporate setting, leaders may use the essence of this mantra to inspire teams, emphasizing the need for swift action and collective effort towards a common goal.
    • Health practitioners might draw on the vitality aspect of soma to encourage holistic wellness practices, promoting physical and mental rejuvenation.
    • During family gatherings, honoring ancestors can strengthen familial ties and instill a sense of belonging and purpose among members.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking divine guidance and support from elemental forces in our lives.
    • Its significance lies in the acknowledgment of the interconnectedness of nature, spirituality, and human endeavors.
    • By invoking these energies, individuals can align themselves with the greater cosmic order, fostering a sense of purpose and direction in their lives.
    • Ultimately, it serves as a reminder of the importance of respect for our roots, the power of transformation, and the pursuit of excellence in all aspects of life.
