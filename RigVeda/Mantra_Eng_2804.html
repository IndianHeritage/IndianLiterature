पुरुष्टुतस्य धामभिः शतेन महयामसि |इन्द्रस्य चर्षणीधृतः ||
puruṣṭutasya dhāmabhiḥ śatena mahayāmasi | indrasya carṣaṇīdhṛtaḥ ||
  • Puruṣṭutasya: This term refers to the "one who is endowed with strength" or "the powerful one." In a real-life context, this can be likened to individuals who exhibit great resilience and capability in overcoming challenges, such as leaders in various fields who inspire others through their strength of character.
  • Dhāmabhiḥ: This word means "with the abode" or "with the dwelling." It signifies a place of stability and security. For example, a family home can be seen as a dhāma, providing comfort and support to its members.
  • Śatena: This translates to "with a hundred" or "a multitude." It emphasizes abundance and the idea of collective strength. In modern life, this can be observed in community efforts where many individuals come together to achieve a common goal, such as charity events or social movements.
  • Mahayāmasi: This means "we are great" or "we are powerful." It reflects a sense of pride and confidence in one's abilities. In a practical sense, this can be seen in team sports where players work together, believing in their collective strength to win games.
  • Indrasya: Referring to "Indra," the king of the gods in Vedic tradition, symbolizing authority and power. In contemporary society, this can be related to influential leaders or figures who guide and protect their communities, much like a CEO leading a company.
  • Carṣaṇīdhṛtaḥ: This term can be interpreted as "upheld by the support of the earth" or "sustained by the foundation." It signifies the importance of having a strong base or support system. In real life, this can be seen in the role of mentors or family members who provide guidance and stability to individuals as they pursue their goals.

Conclusion: The mantra "puruṣṭutasya dhāmabhiḥ śatena mahayāmasi | indrasya carṣaṇīdhṛtaḥ ||" encapsulates the essence of strength, community, and support. It highlights the significance of collective power and the importance of a stable foundation in achieving greatness. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied to various aspects such as teamwork, leadership, and the value of having a supportive environment. It serves as a reminder that true strength lies not just in individual capabilities but in the unity and support of those around us.
