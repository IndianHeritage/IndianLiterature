मन्त्र: चक्रिर यो विश्वा भुवनाभि सासहिष चक्रिर देवेष्व आ दुवः |आ देवेषु यतत आ सुवीर्य आ शंस उता नृणाम ||
cakrir yo viśvā bhuvanābhi sāsahiś cakrir deveṣv ā duvaḥ | ā deveṣu yatata ā suvīrya ā śaṁsa uta nṛṇām ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • cakrir: He who acts or performs
    • yo: who
    • viśvā: all or universal
    • bhuvanābhi: in the worlds or realms
    • sāsahiś: with strength or power
    • cakrir: has acted
    • deveṣv: among the gods
    • ā: indeed
    • duvaḥ: to be desired or sought
    • ā: indeed
    • deveṣu: among the gods
    • yatata: striving or making effort
    • ā: indeed
    • suvīrya: with great strength
    • ā: indeed
    • śaṁsa: praise or commendation
    • uta: or
    • nṛṇām: of men or humans
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the universal actions of a powerful being who acts with strength in all realms.
    • It emphasizes the importance of striving for greatness and the pursuit of commendation among both divine and human realms.
    • The repetition of "ā" signifies affirmation and the importance of these actions being recognized and desired.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, a leader who acts decisively and with strength can inspire their team to strive for excellence, much like the divine actions described in the mantra.
    • In sports, athletes who push their limits and seek recognition for their efforts embody the essence of this mantra, striving for greatness and earning praise.
    • In personal development, individuals who work hard to achieve their goals and seek commendation from their peers reflect the striving and strength mentioned in the mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of action, strength, and striving for recognition in both divine and human contexts.
    • It encourages individuals to act with purpose and seek commendation through their efforts, highlighting the interconnectedness of human actions and divine recognition.
    • In real life, this can be applied in various fields such as leadership, sports, and personal growth, reminding us that our actions have significance and can lead to greater achievements.
