याभिः शान्ताति भवथो ददाशुषे भुज्युं याभिर अवथो याभिर अध्रिगुम |ओम्यावतिं सुभराम ऋतस्तुभं ताभिर ऊषु ऊतिभिर अश्विनां गतम् ||
yābhiḥ śaṁtātī bhavatho dadāśuṣe bhujyuṁ yābhir avatho yābhir adhrigum | omyāvatīṁ subharām ṛtastubhaṁ tābhir ū ṣu ūtibhir aśvinā gatam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yābhiḥ: By whom
    • śaṁtātī: Peaceful or tranquil
    • bhavatho: Your (of the divine)
    • dadāśuṣe: To the one who gives
    • bhujyuṁ: Strength or power
    • yābhir: By whom
    • avatho: You are protected
    • yābhir: By whom
    • adhrigum: The one who is unshaken or stable
    • omyāvatīṁ: O, the one who is all-pervading
    • subharām: Auspicious or beneficial
    • ṛtastubhaṁ: The truth or righteousness
    • tābhir: By whom
    • ū: O
    • ṣu: Good or auspicious
    • ūtibhir: By the powers
    • aśvinā: O Ashvins (the divine twin horsemen)
    • gatam: Gone or arrived
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the divine forces, particularly the Ashvins, who are known for their healing and protective qualities.
    • It emphasizes the importance of peace, strength, and stability in life, suggesting that these qualities are bestowed by the divine.
    • The repetition of "yābhir" signifies the various ways in which divine protection and strength can manifest in our lives.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In times of personal crisis, such as illness or loss, individuals often seek peace and strength through prayer or meditation, similar to the invocation in this mantra.
    • Communities coming together to support one another during disasters exemplify the collective strength and stability that can be drawn from divine or spiritual beliefs.
    • In professional settings, leaders who embody peace and strength can inspire their teams, reflecting the qualities sought in this mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the divine support available to us in our pursuit of peace, strength, and stability.
    • It encourages individuals to seek these qualities not only within themselves but also through their connections with the divine and the community.
    • In real life, the principles of this mantra can be applied in various situations, from personal challenges to collective efforts, highlighting the significance of divine guidance in achieving harmony and resilience.
