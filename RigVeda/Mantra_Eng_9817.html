मन्त्र: सुदक्षो दाक्षैः क्रतुनासी सुक्रतुर अग्ने कविः काव्येनासी विश्ववित् |वासुर वसूनां क्षयसी त्वम् एक इद् dyāvā च यानि पृथिवी च पुष्यतः ||
sudakṣo dakṣaiḥ kratunāsi sukratur agne kaviḥ kāvyenāsi viśvavit | vasur vasūnāṁ kṣayasi tvam eka id dyāvā ca yāni pṛthivī ca puṣyataḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sudakṣo: Well-skilled or highly capable
    • dakṣaiḥ: With skill or dexterity
    • kratunā: By the power of resolve or intention
    • si: You are
    • sukratur: Having good intentions or auspicious thoughts
    • agne: O Agni (the fire god)
    • kaviḥ: The poet or seer
    • kāvyenā: By poetry or creative expression
    • viśvavit: Knower of all
    • vasur: Wealth or abundance
    • vasūnāṁ: Of the wealth or riches
    • kṣayasi: You diminish or reduce
    • tvam: You
    • eka: Alone or singular
    • id: Here or now
    • dyāvā: The sky
    • ca: And
    • yāni: Which
    • pṛthivī: The earth
    • ca: And
    • puṣyataḥ: Flourishing or thriving
  • Overall Meaning:
    • This mantra praises Agni, the fire god, highlighting his skills and capabilities.
    • It emphasizes the importance of good intentions and creative expression in achieving success.
    • Agni is recognized as the one who knows everything and is responsible for the abundance in the universe.
    • The mantra suggests that Agni, through his singular power, nourishes both the sky and the earth.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, a skilled leader (like Agni) can inspire their team through clear intentions and creative problem-solving.
    • In personal life, individuals who cultivate good intentions and express themselves creatively often find fulfillment and success.
    • Environmentalists can relate to the idea of nurturing both the earth and sky, emphasizing the importance of sustainability for future generations.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the power of skill, intention, and creativity in achieving abundance and success.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal growth to professional achievements and environmental stewardship.
    • By embodying the qualities of Agni, individuals can contribute positively to their surroundings and foster a thriving community.
