प्र णो धन्वन्त्विन्दवो मदाच्युतो धनावाऽयेबHIR अर्वतो जुनिमासि |तिरो मर्तस्य कस्य चित्परिह्वृतिं वयं धनानि विश्वधाभरेमहि ||
pra ṇo dhanvantv indavo madacyuto dhanā vā yebhir arvato junīmasi | tiro martasya kasya cit parihvṛtiṁ vayaṁ dhanāni viśvadhā bharemahi ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • pra: forward, towards
    • ṇa: life, vitality
    • dhanvantv: the physician, healer
    • indavo: the divine beings, gods
    • madacyuto: intoxicated with joy, blissful
    • dhanā: wealth, prosperity
    • vā: or
    • yebhir: by whom
    • arvato: from all sides, everywhere
    • junīmasi: we invoke, we call upon
    • tiro: beyond, away from
    • martasya: of the mortal, of death
    • kasya: whose
    • cit: indeed, certainly
    • parihvṛtiṁ: protection, shelter
    • vayaṁ: we
    • dhanāni: wealth, riches
    • viśvadhā: in all directions, universally
    • bharemahi: we may carry, we may bear
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer for health, wealth, and protection.
    • It invokes the divine beings to bless us with vitality and prosperity.
    • The phrase "we invoke" signifies a call to the divine for assistance in our lives.
    • It emphasizes the importance of seeking protection from mortality and the challenges of life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In times of illness, individuals often recite this mantra to seek healing and strength.
    • During financial hardships, people may chant this mantra to attract wealth and abundance.
    • In moments of uncertainty or fear, invoking this mantra can provide a sense of security and comfort.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its holistic approach to life, encompassing health, wealth, and protection.
    • It serves as a reminder to seek divine support in all aspects of life, encouraging a balanced and prosperous existence.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied during personal challenges, whether they are related to health, finances, or emotional well-being.
