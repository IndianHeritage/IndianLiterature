आ मध्वो अस्मा असिचन्न अमत्रं इन्द्राय पूर्‍णं स हि सत्यराधाः |स वावृधे वरिमन् आ पृथिव्याः अभि क्रत्वा नर्यः पौंस्यैश्च ||
ā madhvo asmā asicann amatram indrāya pūrṇaṁ sa hi satyarādhāḥ | sa vāvṛdhe varimann ā pṛthivyā abhi kratvā naryaḥ pauṁsyaiś ca ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā: from
    • madhvo: sweetness or delight
    • asmā: us
    • asicann: having been made or created
    • amatram: immortality or eternal life
    • indrāya: to Indra (the king of gods)
    • pūrṇaṁ: full or complete
    • sa: he
    • hi: indeed
    • satyarādhāḥ: those who are established in truth
    • sa: he
    • vāvṛdhe: has increased or grown
    • varimann: the one who is powerful
    • ā: from
    • pṛthivyā: the earth
    • abhi: towards
    • kratvā: through action or effort
    • naryaḥ: men or heroes
    • pauṁsyaiś: with strength or vigor
    • ca: and
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the divine connection between humans and the cosmic forces, particularly through the figure of Indra, who represents strength, power, and the fulfillment of desires.
    • The phrase "ā madhvo asmā asicann amatram" suggests that from the sweetness of life, we are created to seek immortality, indicating a pursuit of higher truths and eternal values.
    • By addressing Indra, the mantra acknowledges the need for divine support in achieving completeness and fulfillment in life.
    • The mention of "satyarādhāḥ" emphasizes the importance of truthfulness and integrity in our endeavors, suggesting that those who are grounded in truth will find growth and success.
    • The concluding part, "abhi kratvā naryaḥ pauṁsyaiś ca," highlights the role of human effort and strength in achieving one's goals, reinforcing the idea that while divine support is essential, personal action is equally important.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, individuals often seek guidance and support from mentors (akin to Indra) while striving to achieve their career goals. The mantra reminds us to remain truthful and dedicated in our work.
    • In personal relationships, the pursuit of honesty and integrity can lead to deeper connections and fulfillment, reflecting the essence of "satyarādhāḥ."
    • In community service, individuals can draw inspiration from the mantra to work collectively towards a common goal, using their strengths and efforts to uplift others.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra encapsulates the significance of seeking divine guidance while emphasizing the importance of truth and personal effort in achieving fulfillment and immortality.
    • It serves as a reminder that our actions, grounded in truth and supported by divine forces, can lead to a life of purpose and completeness.
    • In various aspects of life—be it personal, professional, or communal—the principles outlined in this mantra can guide individuals towards a more meaningful existence.
