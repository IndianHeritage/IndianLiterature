एता उ त्याः प्रत्य अदृश्रन् पुरस्ताज ज्योतिर यच्छन्तीर उषसो विभातिः |अजीजनन सूर्यं यज्ञम् अग्निम अपाचीनं तमो अगाद अजुष्टम् ||
etā u tyāḥ praty adṛśran purastāj jyotir yacchantīr uṣaso vibhātīḥ | ajījanan sūryaṁ yajñam agnim apācīnaṁ tamo agād ajuṣṭam ||
  • etā u tyāḥ praty adṛśran: This phrase can be translated as "These (lights) are indeed seen in front." It signifies the presence of light and knowledge that dispels darkness.
  • purastāj jyotir yacchantīr uṣaso vibhātīḥ: This part means "The light that shines forth from the dawn." It emphasizes the importance of dawn as a symbol of new beginnings and enlightenment.
  • ajījanan sūryaṁ: Translated as "They have given birth to the Sun." This indicates the Sun's vital role in sustaining life and is often associated with energy and vitality.
  • yajñam agnim: This means "the fire of sacrifice." In Vedic tradition, fire is a sacred element, representing purification and the divine connection between humans and the cosmos.
  • apācīnaṁ tamo agād: This translates to "They have driven away the ancient darkness." It signifies the triumph of light over darkness, knowledge over ignorance.
  • ajuṣṭam: This word can be interpreted as "unharmed" or "untouched," suggesting the resilience and purity of the light and knowledge that emerges.

  • Real-Life Examples:
    • The concept of dawn can be related to personal growth. Just as the dawn brings light, individuals often experience moments of clarity and understanding that lead to personal transformation.
    • The Sun's role in agriculture is a practical example of how light sustains life. Farmers rely on sunlight for crops to grow, symbolizing the essential nature of light in our daily lives.
    • In modern contexts, the idea of "driving away darkness" can be seen in educational initiatives aimed at reducing ignorance and promoting knowledge, such as literacy programs.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the eternal struggle between light and darkness, knowledge and ignorance. It serves as a reminder of the importance of enlightenment in our lives. In various real-life scenarios, whether through personal growth, the sustenance of life through nature, or the pursuit of knowledge, the principles expressed in this mantra are applicable. It encourages individuals to seek knowledge, embrace new beginnings, and contribute positively to the world around them, ultimately leading to a more enlightened society.
