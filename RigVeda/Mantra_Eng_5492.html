त्वं इन्द्र स्वयशा ऋभुक्शा वाजो न साधुर अस्तम एष्य ऋक्वा |वयं नु ते दाश्वांसाḥ स्याम ब्रह्म कृत्वन्तो हरिवो वसिष्ठाḥ ||
tvam indra svayaśā ṛbhukṣā vājo na sādhur astam eṣy ṛkvā | vayaṁ nu te dāśvāṁsaḥ syāma brahma kṛṇvanto harivo vasiṣṭhāḥ ||
  • tvam indra: This phrase translates to "You, Indra," referring to the king of the gods in Vedic tradition, symbolizing strength, power, and leadership.
  • svayaśā: Meaning "self-illuminated" or "self-glorious," it emphasizes the inherent greatness and brilliance of Indra.
  • ṛbhukṣā: This term can be interpreted as "the one who provides nourishment" or "the one who grants abundance," highlighting the role of Indra as a provider.
  • vājo na sādhur astam eṣy ṛkvā: This can be understood as "may the strong one not come to harm," indicating a plea for protection and strength in the face of adversity.
  • vayaṁ nu te dāśvāṁsaḥ syāma: This translates to "we may become your servants," expressing a desire for devotion and service to Indra.
  • brahma kṛṇvanto: Meaning "those who create the ultimate reality," it refers to the pursuit of higher knowledge and truth.
  • harivo vasiṣṭhāḥ: This phrase can be interpreted as "the best of the best," indicating a call for excellence and the highest virtues.

  • Significance: This mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking strength, protection, and guidance from a higher power. It reflects the human desire for support in overcoming challenges and striving for excellence.
  • Real-life Application: In daily life, this mantra can be invoked during times of difficulty or uncertainty, such as facing a challenging situation at work or personal struggles. It serves as a reminder to seek strength from within and from the universe, encouraging individuals to strive for their best selves.

  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of divine guidance and the pursuit of higher ideals. By recognizing the strength and nourishment provided by a higher power, individuals can navigate life's challenges with resilience and purpose. It encourages a mindset of service, excellence, and the quest for truth, applicable in various aspects of life, from personal growth to community service.
