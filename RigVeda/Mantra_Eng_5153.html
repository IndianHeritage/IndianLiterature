नू मे ब्रह्माण्य अग्न उच छशाधि त्वं देव मगवद्भ्यः सुषूदः |रातौ स्यामोभयासा आ ते यूयम् पात स्वस्तिभिः सदा नः ||
nū me brahmāṇy agna uc chaśādhi tvaṁ deva maghavadbhyaḥ suṣūdaḥ | rātau syāmobhayāsa ā te yūyam pāta svastibhiḥ sadā naḥ ||
  • nū me brahmāṇy agna: This phrase can be translated as "O Agni, the divine fire, I invoke you." Agni is often seen as a mediator between humans and the divine, representing the sacred fire used in rituals.
  • uc chaśādhi tvaṁ deva: This means "May you shine forth, O God." It emphasizes the brilliance and purity of Agni, symbolizing enlightenment and knowledge.
  • maghavadbhyaḥ suṣūdaḥ: This translates to "You who are the bestower of wealth." Agni is associated with prosperity and abundance, suggesting that invoking him can lead to material and spiritual wealth.
  • rātau syāmobhayāsa: This phrase means "May I be in both the day and the night." It signifies the desire for balance and harmony in life, encompassing both the light and dark aspects of existence.
  • ā te yūyam pāta: This translates to "May you protect us." It is a plea for divine protection and guidance, highlighting the importance of seeking help from higher powers.
  • svastibhiḥ sadā naḥ: This means "Always grant us well-being." It reflects the aspiration for health, happiness, and prosperity in life.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a deep reverence for Agni, the divine fire, as a source of enlightenment, prosperity, and protection. In real life, this mantra can be applicable in various scenarios, such as during rituals, prayers for well-being, or moments of seeking guidance in challenging times. By invoking Agni, individuals can aspire for balance in their lives, recognizing the interplay of light and darkness, and seek divine blessings for a prosperous and harmonious existence. The significance of this mantra lies in its ability to connect the individual with the divine, fostering a sense of security and abundance in both material and spiritual realms.
