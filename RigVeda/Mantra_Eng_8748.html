दिवो धार्तासि शुक्रः पीयूषः सत्ये विधर्मन् वाजी पवस्व ||
divo dhartāsi śukraḥ pīyūṣaḥ satye vidharman vājī pavasva ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • Divo: Of the heavens or celestial.
    • Dhartāsi: You support or uphold.
    • Śukraḥ: Bright, pure, or excellent.
    • Pīyūṣaḥ: Nectar or essence.
    • Satye: In truth or reality.
    • Vidharman: The one who establishes or maintains order.
    • Vājī: The one who brings strength or vigor.
    • Pavasva: May you flow or may you be active.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the divine support from the celestial realm, emphasizing the importance of truth and order in life.
    • The term "Divo dhartāsi" suggests that the divine is a sustaining force, much like how the sun supports life on Earth.
    • "Śukraḥ pīyūṣaḥ" refers to the purity and essence of life, akin to how clean water is essential for survival.
    • "Satye vidharman" highlights the significance of truth and righteousness, similar to how laws in society maintain order and justice.
    • "Vājī pavasva" encourages the flow of strength and vitality, reminiscent of how motivation and energy drive individuals to achieve their goals.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community, leaders who uphold truth and justice create a harmonious environment, much like the divine order suggested in the mantra.
    • In personal life, individuals who seek purity in their actions and thoughts often find clarity and purpose, reflecting the essence of "śukraḥ pīyūṣaḥ."
    • During challenging times, drawing strength from one's beliefs and values can be likened to "vājī pavasva," where inner strength propels one forward.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the divine support available to those who seek truth and righteousness in their lives.
    • Its significance lies in the encouragement to uphold values that foster order and strength, applicable in personal, social, and professional contexts.
    • By embodying the principles of this mantra, individuals can navigate life's challenges with clarity and purpose, ultimately contributing to a more harmonious society.
