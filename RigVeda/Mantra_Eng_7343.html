कुवित सु नो गविष्टये 'अग्ने संवेषिषो रयिम् |उरुकृत उरु णस कृतहि ||
kuvit su no gaviṣṭaye 'gne saṁveṣiṣo rayim | urukṛd uru ṇas kṛdhi ||
  • kuvit su no gaviṣṭaye: This phrase can be interpreted as a call for the divine to grant us the best of resources and wealth. The word "kuvit" suggests a desire for good fortune, while "gaviṣṭaye" refers to the abundance of cows, which in ancient times symbolized wealth and prosperity.
  • 'gne saṁveṣiṣo rayim: Here, "agne" refers to Agni, the fire god, who is often seen as a mediator between humans and the divine. "Saṁveṣiṣo" implies the gathering or collection of resources, and "rayim" signifies wealth or prosperity. This line emphasizes the importance of invoking divine blessings to attract wealth and resources into our lives.
  • urukṛd uru ṇas kṛdhi: The term "urukṛd" means to create or manifest widely, while "uru ṇas" refers to the vastness or abundance of resources. "Kṛdhi" is a call to action, urging the divine to help us in our endeavors. This line suggests that we should strive for abundance in all aspects of life, with the help of divine forces.

  • Real-Life Example: In modern contexts, this mantra can be related to the pursuit of success in various fields such as business, education, or personal development. For instance, an entrepreneur may recite this mantra while seeking to attract investment and resources for their startup, invoking the blessings of Agni to ensure prosperity.
  • Significance: The mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking divine assistance in our endeavors. It serves as a reminder that while hard work is essential, the blessings of the divine can play a crucial role in achieving success and abundance.
  • Application: This mantra can be applied in daily life through practices such as meditation, prayer, or rituals that honor the divine. By focusing on gratitude and the intention to attract positive resources, individuals can align themselves with the energies of abundance and prosperity.

  • Conclusion: The mantra "kuvit su no gaviṣṭaye 'gne saṁveṣiṣo rayim | urukṛd uru ṇas kṛdhi ||" serves as a powerful invocation for wealth and abundance. Its significance lies in the recognition of the interplay between human effort and divine grace. By incorporating this mantra into our lives, we can cultivate a mindset of abundance and attract the resources we need to thrive.
