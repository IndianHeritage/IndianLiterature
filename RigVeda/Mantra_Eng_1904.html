आ वां रथो 'वनिर न प्रवत्वान सृप्रवन्धुरः सुविताया गम्याः |वृष्णः स्थातारा मनसो जवीयान अहम्पूर्वो यजतो धिष्ण्या यः ||
ā vāṁ ratho 'vanir na pravatvān sṛpravandhuraḥ suvitāya gamyāḥ | vṛṣṇaḥ sthātārā manaso javīyān ahampūrvo yajato dhiṣṇyā yaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā vāṁ: "from us" or "from our side"
    • ratho: "chariot"
    • 'vanir: "may it not be obstructed"
    • na pravatvān: "may it not be hindered"
    • sṛpravandhuraḥ: "with good connections" or "with auspicious ties"
    • suvitāya: "to the well-formed" or "to the well-prepared"
    • gamyāḥ: "to be reached" or "to be attained"
    • vṛṣṇaḥ: "the strong" or "the powerful"
    • sthātārā: "the one who stands firm"
    • manaso: "of the mind"
    • javīyān: "the swift" or "the quick"
    • ahampūrvo: "I, the first" or "I, the foremost"
    • yajato: "to the one who performs sacrifice"
    • dhiṣṇyā: "in the place of worship" or "in the sacred space"
    • yaḥ: "who" or "that"
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of a collective aspiration for success and auspiciousness.
    • The "chariot" symbolizes the journey of life, and the desire for it to be unobstructed reflects the human wish for a smooth path.
    • The phrase "with good connections" emphasizes the importance of relationships and support systems in achieving goals.
    • The reference to "the strong" and "the swift" suggests that mental strength and agility are crucial for overcoming challenges.
    • The invocation of "the one who performs sacrifice" highlights the value of dedication and effort in spiritual and worldly pursuits.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, this mantra can be applied when embarking on a new project. The team may seek to ensure that their efforts are well-coordinated and that they have the necessary support to succeed.
    • In personal relationships, the mantra can serve as a reminder to nurture connections that provide strength and encouragement during difficult times.
    • In sports, athletes often visualize their goals and prepare mentally, embodying the qualities of swiftness and strength mentioned in the mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra encapsulates the essence of striving for success while maintaining strong connections and mental agility.
    • Its significance lies in the reminder that our journeys are often influenced by our relationships and our mental preparedness.
    • In real life, it encourages individuals to seek harmony in their endeavors, ensuring that they are well-prepared and supported as they pursue their goals.
