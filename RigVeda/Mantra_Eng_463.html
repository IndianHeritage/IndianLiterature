सत्यं तवेषा अमवान्तो धन्वञ्चिदा रुद्रियासः |मिहं कृत्वन्त्य अवाताम् ||
satyaṁ tveṣā amavanto dhanvañ cid ā rudriyāsaḥ | mihaṁ kṛṇvanty avātām ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • satyaṁ: Truth
    • tveṣā: Your
    • amavanto: Those who are immortal
    • dhanvañ: Wealth or riches
    • cid: Indeed
    • ā: O
    • rudriyāsaḥ: Those who are connected to Rudra (a form of Shiva)
    • mihaṁ: Here
    • kṛṇvanty: Create or manifest
    • avātām: Protection or shelter
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of truth and the connection to the divine.
    • It suggests that those who embody truth and are aligned with the divine (Rudra) are blessed with wealth and protection.
    • The phrase "mihaṁ kṛṇvanty avātām" indicates that in this world, those who uphold truth create a protective environment for themselves and others.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, a leader who practices transparency and honesty fosters trust among employees, leading to a productive and harmonious workplace.
    • In personal relationships, being truthful strengthens bonds and creates a safe space for open communication.
    • Communities that prioritize truth and integrity often experience lower crime rates and higher levels of cooperation among members.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the power of truth in our lives.
    • It highlights that aligning oneself with truth and the divine can lead to prosperity and protection.
    • In real-life scenarios, whether in personal, professional, or communal contexts, the principles of honesty and integrity can create a foundation for success and security.
