मां श्रेधत सोमिनो दक्षता महे कृणुध्वं राया आतुजे |तरणिर इज जयति क्षेति पुष्यति न देवासः कवतनवे ||
mā sredhata somino dakṣatā mahe kṛṇudhvaṁ rāya ātuje | taraṇir ij jayati kṣeti puṣyati na devāsaḥ kavatnave ||
  • mā sredhata: This phrase can be interpreted as "do not be disturbed" or "do not be hindered." It emphasizes the importance of maintaining focus and composure in the face of challenges.
  • somino dakṣatā: Referring to the "skill of the moon" or "the ability of the moon," this part highlights the significance of wisdom and calmness, akin to the moon's steady presence in the night sky.
  • mahe kṛṇudhvaṁ: This translates to "let us strive for greatness" or "let us achieve great things." It encourages individuals to aim high and work towards significant accomplishments.
  • rāya ātuje: Meaning "may prosperity come to us," this phrase invokes the blessings of abundance and success in endeavors.
  • taraṇir ij: This can be understood as "the one who conquers" or "the one who overcomes." It signifies the strength and determination needed to face obstacles.
  • jayati kṣeti: This translates to "victory is achieved" or "success is attained." It reinforces the idea that perseverance leads to triumph.
  • puṣyati: Meaning "to flourish" or "to prosper," this word emphasizes growth and the positive outcomes of hard work.
  • na devāsaḥ kavatnave: This phrase can be interpreted as "the gods do not abandon us" or "the divine does not forsake us." It suggests that divine support is always present in our endeavors.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a powerful message about resilience, ambition, and divine support. In real-life scenarios, it can be applied in various contexts such as personal development, professional growth, and spiritual journeys. For instance, when facing challenges in a career, one can draw inspiration from this mantra to remain focused, strive for greatness, and trust in the support of the universe. It serves as a reminder that with determination and the right mindset, success is achievable, and one is never alone in their pursuits.
