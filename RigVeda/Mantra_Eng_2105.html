अरोरवीद् वृष्णो अस्य वज्रोऽमानुषं यन् मानुषो निजूर्वात् |नि मायिनो दानवस्य मायाऽपादयत् पपिवान सुतस्य ||
aroravīd vṛṣṇo asya vajro 'mānuṣaṁ yan mānuṣo nijūrvāt | ni māyino dānavasya māyā apādayat papivān sutasya ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • aroravīd: One who is powerful or strong.
    • vṛṣṇo: Of Vishnu or the divine.
    • asya: This.
    • vajro: Thunderbolt or weapon.
    • 'mānuṣaṁ: Human or pertaining to humans.
    • yan: That which.
    • mānuṣo: Human being.
    • nijūrvāt: From his own strength or power.
    • ni: Not.
    • māyino: Of the illusionist or deceiver.
    • dānavasya: Of the demon or evil being.
    • māyā: Illusion or magic.
    • apādayat: Removed or dispelled.
    • papivān: Drank or consumed.
    • sutasya: Of the son or offspring.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the strength and power of the divine, particularly in the context of human beings.
    • It emphasizes that the divine can protect humans from the illusions and deceptions created by evil forces.
    • The reference to "vajro" symbolizes the strength and protection that can be invoked against negativity.
    • It suggests that through divine strength, one can overcome the challenges posed by malevolent forces.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In times of personal crisis, individuals often seek strength through prayer or meditation, invoking a higher power to help them overcome their struggles.
    • In a community facing adversity, collective faith and support can act as a protective shield against negative influences.
    • People often turn to spiritual practices to dispel feelings of fear or doubt, much like the mantra suggests dispelling the illusions of evil.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of divine strength in overcoming life's challenges and the illusions created by negativity.
    • It is applicable in real-life scenarios where individuals or communities face difficulties, reminding them of the power of faith and resilience.
    • By invoking this strength, one can find the courage to confront and dispel the negative forces in their lives.
