युवं भुज्युं समुद्र आ रजसः पार ईङ्खितम् |यातम् अच्छा पतत्रिभिर नासत्याऽसातये कृतम् ||
yuvam bhujyuṁ samudra ā rajasaḥ pāra īṅkhitam | yātam acchā patatribhir nāsatyā sātaye kṛtam ||
  • yuvam - This word translates to "you both" or "you two," indicating a duality or partnership.
  • bhujyuṁ - This term can be interpreted as "to be nourished" or "to be sustained," suggesting a sense of support and sustenance.
  • samudra - Meaning "ocean," it symbolizes vastness, depth, and the collective unconscious or the source of life.
  • ā - A preposition that can mean "from" or "out of," indicating a source or origin.
  • rajasaḥ - This word refers to "the qualities of passion" or "activity," often associated with the material world and its dynamics.
  • pāra - Meaning "beyond" or "across," it suggests a journey or transition from one state to another.
  • īṅkhitam - This term can be understood as "to be marked" or "to be designated," indicating a specific purpose or direction.
  • yātam - Translates to "having gone" or "having traveled," implying movement or progress.
  • acchā - Meaning "good" or "well," it denotes positivity and favorable conditions.
  • patatribhir - This word refers to "with wings" or "by those who fly," symbolizing freedom, elevation, and transcendence.
  • nāsatyā - Referring to "the Asvins," the twin deities known for their healing and protective qualities, representing duality and balance.
  • sātaye - Meaning "for the sake of truth" or "for the purpose of reality," emphasizing the importance of authenticity.
  • kṛtam - This term means "done" or "accomplished," indicating the completion of an action or intention.

The mantra can be interpreted as a call for partnership and support in navigating the vastness of life (symbolized by the ocean) while transcending the material passions and challenges we face. It emphasizes the importance of truth and authenticity in our actions, guided by the protective and healing qualities of the Asvins.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as:

  • In relationships, it encourages mutual support and nourishment, reminding partners to uplift each other through life's challenges.
  • In professional settings, it highlights the importance of collaboration and teamwork, where individuals work together to achieve common goals.
  • In personal growth, it serves as a reminder to seek truth and authenticity in one's actions, fostering a sense of purpose and direction.

In conclusion, this mantra signifies the importance of partnership, support, and authenticity in navigating life's vast ocean. It encourages individuals to transcend material challenges and work together towards a common truth, making it applicable in various aspects of life, from personal relationships to professional endeavors.
