अस्मा इद उ तवष्टा तक्षद वज्रं स्वपस्तमं स्वर्यं रणाय |वृत्रस्य चिद विदद येन मरम तुजन्न ईशानस तुजता कियेदहः ||
asmā id u tvaṣṭā takṣad vajraṁ svapastamaṁ svarya1ṁ raṇāya | vṛtrasya cid vidad yena marma tujann īśānas tujatā kiyedhāḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • asmā: from us
    • id: this
    • u: indeed
    • tvaṣṭā: the divine craftsman or creator
    • takṣad: has fashioned or created
    • vajraṁ: the thunderbolt or powerful weapon
    • svapastamaṁ: the best of the best
    • svaryaṁ: the heavenly or divine
    • raṇāya: for battle or conflict
    • vṛtrasya: of Vritra (the cosmic serpent or demon)
    • cid: indeed
    • vidad: has given or provided
    • yena: by whom
    • marma: the secret or essence
    • tujann: piercing or penetrating
    • īśānas: the lord or master
    • tujatā: by piercing
    • kiyedhāḥ: how many or how much
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the divine craftsman, Tvaṣṭā, who is responsible for creating powerful weapons, symbolized by the vajra (thunderbolt).
    • It emphasizes the importance of divine intervention in overcoming obstacles, represented by Vritra, a demon that embodies chaos and ignorance.
    • The mantra suggests that through divine creation and guidance, one can achieve victory in life's battles, whether they are physical, emotional, or spiritual.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, when facing challenges at work, one might seek inspiration and guidance from mentors or leaders, akin to invoking Tvaṣṭā for support.
    • In personal struggles, such as overcoming fears or anxieties, individuals may turn to spiritual practices or community support, reflecting the essence of seeking divine assistance.
    • In social justice movements, the fight against oppression can be seen as a battle against modern-day Vritras, where collective action and divine inspiration lead to change.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of divine creation and support in overcoming life's challenges.
    • It serves as a reminder that with the right guidance and inner strength, one can confront and conquer obstacles.
    • In real life, it encourages individuals to seek help, whether through community, spirituality, or personal development, to achieve their goals and navigate conflicts.
