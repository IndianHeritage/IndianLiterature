अवसृष्टा परा पत शरव्ये ब्रह्मसंशिते |गच्छामित्रान प्र पद्यस्व मामीषां कं चनोच्छिषः ||
avasṛṣṭā parā pata śaravye brahmasaṁśite | gacchāmitrān pra padyasva māmīṣāṁ kaṁ canoc chiṣaḥ ||
  • avasṛṣṭā: This word translates to "released" or "liberated." It signifies the act of letting go or freeing oneself from constraints.
  • parā: Meaning "supreme" or "ultimate," this term refers to the highest state of being or consciousness.
  • pata: This can be interpreted as "to fly" or "to soar," suggesting movement towards a higher state or goal.
  • śaravye: This word relates to "the one who is protected" or "the one who is safe," indicating a sense of security in the journey.
  • brahmasaṁśite: This term means "in the realm of the divine" or "in accordance with the cosmic order," emphasizing alignment with universal principles.
  • gacchāmi: Translated as "I go" or "I move," it indicates an active pursuit of a goal or destination.
  • mitrān: This means "friends" or "allies," suggesting the importance of companionship and support in one's journey.
  • pra padyasva: This phrase translates to "take refuge" or "seek shelter," indicating a need for safety and guidance.
  • māmīṣāṁ: This can be interpreted as "my desires" or "my intentions," reflecting personal aspirations and goals.
  • kaṁ canoc: Meaning "anyone" or "anyone at all," it suggests inclusivity and openness to possibilities.
  • chiṣaḥ: This word refers to "remnants" or "leftovers," which can symbolize the lessons learned or experiences gained along the way.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a journey of liberation and self-discovery. It emphasizes the importance of moving towards a higher state of being while being supported by friends and allies. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios such as personal growth, spiritual journeys, or even in professional settings where collaboration and support are essential. The mantra encourages individuals to let go of their fears and constraints, seek refuge in their aspirations, and embrace the journey with the help of those around them. It serves as a reminder that the path to fulfillment is often shared and that the lessons learned along the way are invaluable.
