स सत्यसत्त्वान महते रणाय रथम आ तिष्ठ तुविनृम्ण भीमम् |याहि प्रपथिन्न अवसोपा मद्रिक प्र च श्रुत श्रावय चर्षणिभ्यः ||
sa satyasatvan mahate raṇāya ratham ā tiṣṭha tuvinṛmṇa bhīmam | yāhi prapathinn avasopa madrik pra ca śruta śrāvaya carṣaṇibhyaḥ ||
  • sa satyasatvan mahate raṇāya: This phrase can be translated as "to the one who is true and powerful, for the great battle." Here, "satyasatvan" refers to truth and strength, indicating the importance of integrity and valor in facing challenges.
  • ratham ā tiṣṭha: This means "stand firm in the chariot." The chariot symbolizes the journey of life, and standing firm suggests resilience and determination in the face of adversity.
  • tuvinṛmṇa bhīmam: This translates to "to the terrifying one." It signifies the acknowledgment of fearsome challenges or adversaries that one may encounter.
  • yāhi prapathinn avasopa madrik: This can be interpreted as "go forth on the path, do not be hindered." It encourages individuals to pursue their goals without being deterred by obstacles.
  • pra ca śruta śrāvaya carṣaṇibhyaḥ: This means "let the sound of your voice be heard." It emphasizes the importance of communication and sharing one's truth with others, fostering connection and understanding.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of courage, truth, and perseverance in the face of life's battles. It serves as a reminder to stand firm in one's beliefs and values while navigating challenges. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as personal struggles, professional challenges, or social injustices. By embodying the principles of truth and resilience, individuals can inspire others and create a positive impact in their communities. The significance of this mantra lies in its ability to motivate and empower individuals to confront their fears and pursue their paths with unwavering determination.
