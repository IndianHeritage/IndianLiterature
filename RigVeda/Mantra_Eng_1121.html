तं स्मा रथम मगवन प्राव सातये जैत्रं यं ते अनुमदाम संगमे |आजा न इन्द्र मनसा पुरुष्टुत त्वायद्भ्यो मगवञ्च छर्म यच्छ नः ||
taṁ smā ratham maghavan prāva sātaye jaitraṁ yaṁ te anumadāma saṁgame | ājā na indra manasā puruṣṭuta tvāyadbhyo maghavañ charma yaccha naḥ ||
  • taṁ smā ratham maghavan: This phrase can be translated as "We invoke the chariot of the powerful one." Here, "maghavan" refers to a deity, often associated with wealth and abundance, such as Indra. The chariot symbolizes the journey of life and the pursuit of goals.
  • prāva sātaye jaitraṁ: This means "to achieve victory and success." It emphasizes the importance of striving for success in our endeavors, whether in personal or professional life. For example, a student preparing for exams must focus on their studies to achieve academic success.
  • yaṁ te anumadāma saṁgame: This translates to "whom we praise in unity." It highlights the significance of community and collaboration. In real life, teamwork in a workplace or community projects exemplifies this unity, where collective efforts lead to greater achievements.
  • ājā na indra manasā: This phrase means "O Indra, do not be distant in thought." It calls for the divine presence to be close to us, guiding our thoughts and actions. In daily life, this can be seen in moments of prayer or meditation, where individuals seek guidance and clarity.
  • puruṣṭuta tvāyadbhyo: This translates to "to you, O powerful one." It acknowledges the strength and influence of the divine. In real life, recognizing the power of nature or a higher force can inspire individuals to act with humility and respect towards the environment and others.
  • maghavañ charma yaccha naḥ: This means "grant us the protection of your armor." It symbolizes the need for safety and security in our lives. For instance, individuals often seek protection through community support, legal systems, or personal relationships that provide a sense of security.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda emphasizes the importance of seeking divine guidance, striving for success, and the power of unity in achieving our goals. It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, such as in education, teamwork, and personal growth. By invoking the strength of the divine and recognizing the importance of community, individuals can navigate life's challenges with resilience and purpose. The mantra serves as a reminder to remain connected to our goals and to seek support from both the divine and our communities.
