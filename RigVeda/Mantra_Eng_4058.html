विश्वो देवस्य नेतुर मार्तो वूरीत सख्यम् |विश्वो राया इषुध्यति dyumnaṁ वृणीत पुष्यसे ||
viśvo devasya netur marto vurīta sakhyam | viśvo rāya iṣudhyati dyumnaṁ vṛṇīta puṣyase ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • viśvo: universal, all-encompassing
    • devasya: of the divine, pertaining to the gods
    • netur: leader, guide
    • marto: mortal, human
    • vurīta: to be endowed, to be blessed
    • sakhyam: friendship, companionship
    • rāya: wealth, prosperity
    • iṣudhyati: to be purified, to be made strong
    • dyumnaṁ: glory, fame
    • vṛṇīta: to choose, to select
    • puṣyase: to flourish, to prosper
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks to the universal nature of divinity and the relationship between mortals and the divine.
    • It emphasizes the importance of friendship and companionship among humans, suggesting that these bonds are blessed by the divine.
    • The mantra also highlights the pursuit of wealth and prosperity, indicating that these should be sought with purity and strength.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, teamwork and collaboration can be seen as a manifestation of the "sakhyam" (friendship) aspect, where individuals work together towards a common goal, leading to collective success.
    • In personal relationships, nurturing friendships can lead to emotional support and prosperity in life, reflecting the blessings of the divine.
    • In community service, individuals coming together to help others can embody the universal aspect of divinity, promoting a sense of unity and shared purpose.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the interconnectedness of all beings and the divine, encouraging individuals to foster friendships and seek prosperity through purity and strength.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, such as in personal relationships, professional environments, and community engagements, reminding us of the importance of unity and collaboration.
    • Ultimately, it serves as a reminder that our actions, guided by friendship and a pursuit of collective well-being, can lead to a flourishing and prosperous life.
