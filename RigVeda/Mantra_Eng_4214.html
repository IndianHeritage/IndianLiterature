यन्नूनमश्यां गतिमित्रस्य यायाम्पथाः |अस्य प्रियस्य शर्मण्य अहिंसानस्य सश्चिरे ||
yan nūnam aśyāṁ gatim mitrasya yāyām pathā | asya priyasya śarmaṇy ahiṁsānasya saścire ||
  • yan nūnam aśyāṁ gatim mitrasya yāyām pathā
    • yan nūnam: This phrase can be translated as "indeed" or "truly," indicating a strong affirmation.
    • aśyāṁ: This word refers to "goodness" or "well-being." It signifies a state of positivity and harmony.
    • gatim: This means "path" or "direction," suggesting a journey or a way of life.
    • mitrasya: This translates to "of the friend" or "of the ally," emphasizing the importance of relationships and companionship.
    • yāyām: This word means "to go" or "to proceed," indicating movement towards a goal.
    • pathā: This means "by the path," reinforcing the idea of following a specific way or direction.
  • asya priyasya śarmaṇy ahiṁsānasya saścire
    • asya: This means "of this" or "of that," referring to the subject at hand.
    • priyasya: This translates to "beloved" or "dear," indicating something cherished or valued.
    • śarmaṇy: This word means "in happiness" or "in comfort," suggesting a state of peace and contentment.
    • ahiṁsānasya: This refers to "non-violence" or "harmlessness," a core principle in many philosophies, especially in Hinduism.
    • saścire: This means "may it be long-lasting" or "may it endure," expressing a desire for permanence and stability.

Conclusion:

The mantra emphasizes the importance of following a path of goodness and friendship, leading to a state of happiness and non-violence. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios, such as:

  • Building and maintaining strong relationships based on trust and mutual respect.
  • Choosing a career or life path that aligns with one's values and promotes well-being for oneself and others.
  • Practicing non-violence in thoughts, words, and actions, contributing to a more peaceful society.

Ultimately, this mantra serves as a reminder to seek a life filled with positive relationships, ethical choices, and a commitment to peace, which can lead to lasting happiness and fulfillment.
