उपो षु श्रिणुही गिरो मगवन मातथा इव |यदा नः सूनृतावतः कर आद अर्थयास इद योजा nv इन्द्र ते हरि ||
upo ṣu śṛṇuhī giro maghavan mātathā iva | yadā naḥ sūnṛtāvataḥ kara ād arthayāsa id yojā nv indra te harī ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • upo: near, close
    • ṣu: good, auspicious
    • śṛṇuhī: listen, hear
    • giro: words, speech
    • maghavan: O Maghavan (Indra), the powerful one
    • mātathā: as if, like a mother
    • iva: like, as
    • yadā: when
    • naḥ: our
    • sūnṛtāvataḥ: of those who speak the truth
    • kara: actions, deeds
    • ād: from, out of
    • arthayāsa: for the purpose, for the benefit
    • id: this
    • yojā: connection, union
    • nv: indeed, surely
    • indra: O Indra, the king of gods
    • te: your
    • harī: blessings, grace
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer directed towards Indra, the king of gods, asking him to listen to our words and grant us his blessings.
    • The phrase "as if a mother" signifies the nurturing and protective qualities associated with divine blessings.
    • It emphasizes the importance of truthfulness and righteous actions, suggesting that our deeds should align with our words.
    • The mantra calls for a connection with the divine, seeking guidance and support in our endeavors.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, this mantra can be applied when seeking guidance for ethical decision-making. Leaders can reflect on their words and actions, ensuring they align with the values of truth and integrity.
    • In personal relationships, the mantra serves as a reminder to communicate openly and honestly, fostering trust and understanding among family and friends.
    • During challenging times, individuals can recite this mantra to invoke a sense of divine support, encouraging them to act with righteousness and seek the blessings of higher powers.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of truthfulness, the power of words, and the importance of divine connection in our lives.
    • It serves as a reminder that our actions should reflect our intentions, and that seeking blessings from the divine can guide us in our pursuits.
    • In essence, it encourages individuals to live a life of integrity, fostering a harmonious relationship with themselves, others, and the divine.
