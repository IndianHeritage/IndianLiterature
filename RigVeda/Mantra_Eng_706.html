अस्येदु भियाऽगिरयश्च दृढा dyāvā च भूमाऽजनुषस्तुजेत || उपो वेनस्य जोगुवान ओणिं सद्यो भूवद वीर्याय नोधाः ||
asyed u bhiyā girayaś ca dṛḻhā dyāvā ca bhūmā januṣas tujete | upo venasya joguvāna oṇiṁ sadyo bhuvad vīryāya nodhāḥ ||
  • Mantra: "asyed u bhiyā girayaś ca dṛḻhā dyāvā ca bhūmā januṣas tujete | upo venasya joguvāna oṇiṁ sadyo bhuvad vīryāya nodhāḥ ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • asyed: of this
    • u: indeed
    • bhiyā: with fear
    • girayaś: mountains
    • ca: and
    • dṛḻhā: strong
    • dyāvā: sky
    • ca: and
    • bhūmā: earth
    • januṣas: of beings
    • tujete: they strive
    • upo: towards
    • venasya: of the wind
    • joguvāna: the one who is powerful
    • oṇiṁ: the essence
    • sadyo: immediately
    • bhuvad: becomes
    • vīryāya: for strength
    • nodhāḥ: not to be obstructed
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra speaks about the interconnectedness of nature and the strength derived from it.
    • The phrase "bhiyā girayaś ca dṛḻhā dyāvā ca bhūmā" emphasizes the strength of the mountains and the sky, suggesting that these natural elements are powerful and stable.
    • The mention of "januṣas tujete" indicates that all beings strive towards these powerful elements, seeking strength and stability in their lives.
    • "upo venasya joguvāna oṇiṁ" refers to the essence of the wind, which symbolizes movement and change, suggesting that one must harness this energy to achieve their goals.
    • The concluding part "sadyo bhuvad vīryāya nodhāḥ" implies that by aligning oneself with these natural forces, one can gain strength that is not obstructed by any obstacles.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In personal development, individuals often look to nature for inspiration. For instance, a person may find solace in the mountains, using the strength of the landscape to overcome personal challenges.
    • In team dynamics, organizations can draw parallels to this mantra by fostering a culture that encourages collaboration and harnesses the collective strength of its members, much like the stability of the earth and sky.
    • Environmental awareness can also be linked to this mantra, as it highlights the importance of respecting and preserving natural elements that provide strength and stability to life.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of recognizing and aligning with the powerful forces of nature to achieve strength and resilience in life.
    • It teaches us that by understanding our connection to the earth and sky, we can strive towards our goals with greater determination and clarity.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in personal growth, teamwork, and environmental stewardship, reminding us of the strength that comes from unity with nature.
