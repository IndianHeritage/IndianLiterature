न तिष्ठन्ति न निमिषन्त्य एते देवानां स्पर्श इह ये चरन्ति |अन्येन मद आहनो याहि तुयं तेन वि वृहा रथ्येव चक्रा ||
na tiṣṭhanti na ni miṣanty ete devānāṁ spaśa iha ye caranti | anyena mad āhano yāhi tūyaṁ tena vi vṛha rathyeva cakrā ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • na tiṣṭhanti: do not stand still
    • na ni miṣanty: do not blink
    • ete: these
    • devānāṁ: of the gods
    • spaśa: touch or contact
    • iha: here
    • ye: who
    • caranti: move or wander
    • anyena: by another
    • mad: my
    • āhano: offering or sacrifice
    • yāhi: go
    • tūyaṁ: to you
    • tena: by that
    • vi vṛha: may it grow
    • rathyeva: like a chariot
    • cakrā: wheel
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the divine beings (gods) who are always in motion and do not remain stagnant.
    • The phrase "na tiṣṭhanti" suggests that the gods are dynamic and ever-active, symbolizing the importance of progress and movement in life.
    • The reference to "mad āhano" indicates a personal offering or sacrifice, suggesting that one should seek to connect with the divine through actions and intentions.
    • The imagery of a chariot wheel ("cakrā") signifies the cyclical nature of life and the importance of maintaining momentum in our endeavors.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, this mantra can inspire individuals to continuously strive for growth and improvement, much like how companies innovate to stay relevant in the market.
    • In personal development, the idea of not standing still can motivate individuals to pursue lifelong learning and self-improvement, akin to how athletes train consistently to enhance their performance.
    • In relationships, the mantra encourages active engagement and communication, reminding us that stagnant relationships can lead to disconnect, while dynamic interactions foster deeper connections.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its emphasis on movement, growth, and connection with the divine.
    • It serves as a reminder that life is a journey that requires active participation and continuous effort.
    • In real-life scenarios, applying the principles of this mantra can lead to personal fulfillment, professional success, and enriched relationships.
    • Ultimately, it encourages us to embrace change and seek divine guidance in our pursuits, ensuring that we remain aligned with our higher purpose.
