ममत्तु त्वा दिव्यः सोम इन्द्र ममत्तु यः सूयते पार्थिवेषु |ममत्तु येन वरिवश चकर्थ ममत्तु येन निरिणासि शत्रून् ||
mamattu tvā divyaḥ soma indra mamattu yaḥ sūyate pārthiveṣu | mamattu yena varivaś cakartha mamattu yena niriṇāsi śatrūn ||
  • Translation: "May that divine Soma of yours, O Indra, protect me; may that which is poured out on the earth protect me. May that which you have made powerful protect me; may that which you have made to conquer enemies protect me."
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • Mamattu: May it protect me
    • Tvā: You (referring to Indra)
    • Divyaḥ: Divine
    • Soma: A sacred drink, often associated with immortality and divine inspiration
    • Indra: The king of gods, associated with strength and power
    • Sūyate: That which is poured out
    • Pārthiveṣu: On the earth
    • Yena: By which
    • Varivaś: Powerful or strong
    • Cakartha: You have made
    • Niriṇāsi: You conquer or defeat
    • Śatrūn: Enemies
  • Context and Significance:
    • This mantra is a prayer for protection and strength, invoking the divine qualities of Indra and the sacred Soma.
    • In real life, this can be seen as a call for support in challenging situations, such as during conflicts or competitions, where one seeks the strength to overcome obstacles.
    • For example, a student preparing for an important exam might recite this mantra to seek focus and clarity, hoping for divine guidance to succeed.
    • In a broader sense, it reflects the human desire for protection and empowerment in various aspects of life, whether in personal struggles, professional challenges, or societal conflicts.
  • Conclusion:
    • The mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking divine assistance in overcoming adversities and achieving success.
    • It serves as a reminder that strength can come from both within and from higher powers, encouraging individuals to cultivate resilience and seek support when needed.
    • In contemporary life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as during personal challenges, community issues, or even global crises, reinforcing the belief in the power of collective strength and divine guidance.
