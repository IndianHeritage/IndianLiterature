तच चित्रं राधा आ भरोषो यद दीर्घश्रुत्तमम् |यत ते दिवो दुधितर मर्तभोजनं तद रास्व भुनजामहै ||
tac citraṁ rādha ā bharoṣo yad dīrghaśruttamam | yat te divo duhitar martabhojanaṁ tad rāsva bhunajāmahai ||
  • tac citraṁ rādha ā bharoṣo: This phrase can be translated as "that which is colorful and vibrant, may it be filled with abundance." Here, "tac" refers to "that," "citraṁ" means "colorful" or "varied," and "rādha" implies "abundance" or "prosperity." In real life, this can be related to the idea of seeking a life filled with diverse experiences and richness, much like a garden full of various flowers.
  • yad dīrghaśruttamam: This translates to "that which is of the longest and best hearing." "Yad" means "that," "dīrgha" means "long," and "śruttamam" refers to "the best hearing." This can symbolize the importance of listening and understanding in our lives. For example, in effective communication, listening is as crucial as speaking, as it fosters better relationships and understanding.
  • yat te divo duhitar: This means "that which is your daughter of the sky." "Te" means "your," "divo" refers to "of the sky," and "duhitar" means "daughter." This can be interpreted as a metaphor for aspirations or goals that are lofty and divine. In real life, this could relate to pursuing dreams that elevate us, much like aiming for the stars.
  • martabhojanaṁ tad rāsva bhunajāmahai: This translates to "may we partake in the sustenance of the mortal." "Martabhojanaṁ" refers to "sustenance of the mortal," "tad" means "that," "rāsva" means "short," and "bhunajāmahai" means "may we enjoy." This emphasizes the importance of enjoying the simple pleasures of life while being grounded in reality. For instance, appreciating small moments of joy, like sharing a meal with loved ones, can bring fulfillment.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking abundance, valuing communication, aspiring for lofty goals, and enjoying the simple pleasures of life. Its significance lies in the holistic approach it advocates for living a fulfilling life. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire individuals to embrace diversity in experiences, prioritize listening in relationships, pursue their dreams, and find joy in everyday moments. By embodying these principles, one can lead a balanced and enriched life.
