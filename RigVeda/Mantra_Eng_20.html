अश्विनाऽपुरुदंससां नराः शवीरयā धियā |धिष्ण्यā वनतं गिरः ||
aśvinā purudaṁsasā narā śavīrayā dhiyā | dhiṣṇyā vanataṁ giraḥ ||
  • Mantra: "aśvinā purudaṁsasā narā śavīrayā dhiyā | dhiṣṇyā vanataṁ giraḥ ||"
  • Translation: This mantra can be translated as: "O Ashvins, with your powerful and divine energy, may you inspire the minds of men with your wisdom and grant us the strength to express our thoughts."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • Aśvinā: Refers to the Ashvins, the twin Vedic deities associated with health, medicine, and the dawn.
    • Purudaṁsasā: Means "with great strength" or "powerful," indicating the divine energy of the Ashvins.
    • Narā: Translates to "men" or "humans," representing the audience or the seekers of wisdom.
    • Śavīrayā: Implies "with strength" or "with vigor," emphasizing the need for mental and physical strength.
    • Dhiya: Refers to "intellect" or "wisdom," highlighting the importance of mental clarity and insight.
    • Dhiṣṇyā: Means "with intention" or "with purpose," suggesting a focused approach to thought and action.
    • Vanataṁ: Translates to "to express" or "to articulate," indicating the act of communication.
    • Giraḥ: Refers to "words" or "speech," emphasizing the power of language and expression.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, this mantra can be invoked to seek clarity and strength in communication during important meetings or presentations.
    • Students can recite this mantra before exams or presentations to enhance their mental focus and articulate their thoughts clearly.
    • In personal relationships, it can be used to foster open and honest communication, ensuring that thoughts and feelings are expressed effectively.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine assistance in enhancing human intellect and communication. In real-life scenarios, it serves as a reminder of the importance of clarity of thought and strength in expression. By invoking the blessings of the Ashvins, individuals can strive for better understanding and articulation in their personal and professional lives, ultimately leading to more meaningful interactions and decisions.
