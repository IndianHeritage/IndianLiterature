मर्ता अमर्त्यस्य ते भूरी नाम मनामहे |विप्रासो जातवेदसः ||
martā amartyasya te bhūri nāma manāmahe | viprāso jātavedasaḥ ||
  • Translation: "We meditate upon the name of the mortal, who is the embodiment of immortality; O Jātavedas, the wise ones."

  • Word Breakdown:
    • martā: Mortal, referring to beings that are subject to death.
    • amartyasya: Immortal, representing the eternal aspect of existence.
    • te: Your, indicating a connection or reverence to the divine.
    • bhūri: Abundant or plentiful, suggesting richness in qualities or blessings.
    • nāma: Name, which holds significance in many cultures as a representation of identity and essence.
    • manāmahe: We meditate or contemplate, indicating a deep, reflective practice.
    • viprāso: Wise or learned ones, often referring to sages or enlightened beings.
    • jātavedasaḥ: Jātavedas, a name for Agni, the fire god, symbolizing knowledge and transformation.

  • Meaning and Interpretation:
    • This mantra reflects the duality of existence, acknowledging the mortal nature of life while simultaneously recognizing the eternal essence that transcends death.
    • It emphasizes the importance of meditation and contemplation on the divine, particularly through the lens of wisdom and knowledge represented by Agni.
    • In real life, this can be seen in practices such as mindfulness and meditation, where individuals seek to connect with a higher consciousness while acknowledging their own mortality.
    • For example, in moments of grief or loss, people often turn to spiritual practices to find solace and understanding of the cycle of life and death.

  • Significance:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the transient nature of life and the importance of seeking deeper truths beyond physical existence.
    • It encourages individuals to cultivate wisdom and understanding, much like the sages who have come before us.
    • In a practical sense, this mantra can be applied in various life situations, such as during personal challenges, health crises, or existential reflections, guiding individuals to seek inner peace and clarity.
    • Ultimately, it highlights the journey of life as a path towards enlightenment, urging us to embrace both our mortal experiences and the quest for immortality through knowledge and spiritual growth.
