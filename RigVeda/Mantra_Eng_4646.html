स पत्यता उभयोर नृम्णम अयोः यदी वेधसः समिथे हवन्ते |वृत्रे वा महो नृवति क्षये वा व्यचस्वन्ता यदि वितन्तसैते ||
sa patyata ubhayor nṛmṇam ayor yadī vedhasaḥ samithe havante | vṛtre vā maho nṛvati kṣaye vā vyacasvantā yadi vitantasaite ||
  • sa patyata: This phrase can be translated as "may it be established" or "may it be firm." It signifies the desire for stability and strength in one's endeavors.
  • ubhayor nṛmṇam: This means "of both men." It emphasizes the collective effort and unity among people in achieving a common goal.
  • ayor yadī vedhasaḥ: This translates to "if they are wise." It suggests that wisdom and knowledge are crucial in guiding actions and decisions.
  • samithe havante: This means "in the assembly of offerings." It refers to the importance of community and collaboration in rituals or endeavors.
  • vṛtre vā maho nṛvati: This can be interpreted as "whether against the great adversary." It highlights the challenges and obstacles that may arise in life.
  • kṣaye vā vyacasvantā: This translates to "or in the destruction." It signifies the potential for loss or failure in various pursuits.
  • yadi vitantasaite: This means "if they are extended." It suggests the idea of reaching out or expanding one's efforts and influence.

In summary, this mantra from the Rigveda emphasizes the importance of unity, wisdom, and collective effort in overcoming challenges and achieving stability. It reflects the idea that when individuals come together with a shared purpose and wisdom, they can confront adversities, whether they be great challenges or the potential for failure.


In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as:

  • Community Projects: When a group of people comes together to work on a community project, the principles of unity and collective effort are essential for success.
  • Teamwork in Organizations: In a workplace, collaboration and shared wisdom among team members can lead to innovative solutions and overcoming obstacles.
  • Conflict Resolution: The mantra can also be applied in resolving conflicts, where understanding and wisdom are necessary to navigate challenges effectively.

Overall, the significance of this mantra lies in its timeless message about the power of unity, wisdom, and collective action in facing life's challenges and achieving meaningful goals.
