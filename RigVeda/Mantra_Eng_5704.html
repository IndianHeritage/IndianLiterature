असश्चतां मगवद्भ्यो हि भूतं ये राया मगदेयं जुनन्ति |प्र ये बन्धुं सूनृताभिस तिरन्ते गव्यां पृञ्चन्तो अश्व्या मगानि ||
asaścatā maghavadbhyo hi bhūtaṁ ye rāyā maghadeyaṁ junanti | pra ye bandhuṁ sūnṛtābhis tirante gavyā pṛñcanto aśvyā maghāni ||
  • asaścatā: This term can be interpreted as "those who are wise" or "those who are knowledgeable." It emphasizes the importance of wisdom in decision-making and life choices.
  • maghavadbhyo: Refers to the "wealthy" or "prosperous." This highlights the significance of abundance and prosperity in life.
  • hi bhūtaṁ: Translates to "indeed, the being" or "the essence of existence." It suggests a focus on the fundamental nature of life and existence.
  • ye rāyā: Means "those who possess wealth" or "those who have resources." This points to the idea that wealth can be a tool for achieving greater goals.
  • maghadeyaṁ: This can be understood as "the great wealth" or "the ultimate prosperity." It signifies the highest form of success and abundance.
  • junanti: Translates to "they unite" or "they come together." This indicates the importance of collaboration and unity in achieving goals.
  • pra ye bandhuṁ: Means "those who are friends" or "those who are connected." It emphasizes the value of relationships and community.
  • sūnṛtābhis: Refers to "with truth" or "with righteousness." This highlights the importance of integrity and honesty in all endeavors.
  • tirante: Translates to "they cross over" or "they transcend." This suggests overcoming obstacles and challenges in life.
  • gavyā: Refers to "cows" or "nourishment." In a broader sense, it symbolizes sustenance and the nurturing aspects of life.
  • pṛñcanto: Means "seeking" or "inquiring." This indicates the pursuit of knowledge and understanding.
  • aśvyā: Refers to "horses" or "strength." It symbolizes power, energy, and the drive to achieve one's goals.
  • maghāni: Translates to "great blessings" or "abundant gifts." This signifies the rewards that come from hard work and righteousness.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of wisdom, unity, and integrity in the pursuit of prosperity and success. It teaches that true wealth is not just material but also encompasses relationships, knowledge, and moral values. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios such as teamwork in a workplace, community building, and personal development. By fostering connections, acting with integrity, and seeking knowledge, individuals can transcend challenges and achieve their goals, leading to a fulfilling and prosperous life.
