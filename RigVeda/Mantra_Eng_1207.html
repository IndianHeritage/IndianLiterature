आ नो यज्ञाय तक्षत ऋभुमद वयः क्रत्वे दक्षाय सुप्रजावतिम् इषम् |यथा क्षयाम सर्ववीरया विशा तन् नः शर्धाय धासथा स्व इन्द्रियम् ||
ā no yajñāya takṣata ṛbhumad vayaḥ kratve dakṣāya suprajāvatīm iṣam | yathā kṣayāma sarvavīrayā viśā tan naḥ śardhāya dhāsathā sv indriyam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā: towards
    • no: our
    • yajñāya: for the sacrifice
    • takṣata: may you create
    • ṛbhumad: with the essence of truth
    • vayaḥ: our strength
    • kratve: for the power
    • dakṣāya: skilled
    • suprajāvatīm: with good progeny
    • iṣam: the goal
    • yathā: as
    • kṣayāma: we may diminish
    • sarvavīrayā: all strength
    • viśā: of the community
    • tan: that
    • naḥ: for us
    • śardhāya: with unity
    • dhāsathā: may you support
    • sv: our
    • indriyam: senses
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of collective effort and unity in achieving goals.
    • It calls for the creation of strength and skill for the purpose of sacrifice, which can be interpreted as selfless service or contribution to the community.
    • The mention of "good progeny" suggests a focus on nurturing future generations, ensuring they are equipped with the right values and skills.
    • The phrase "as we may diminish all strength" indicates a recognition of the challenges faced by the community and the need for support and solidarity.
    • Finally, the mantra seeks the support of the senses, highlighting the importance of awareness and perception in our actions.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace, this mantra can be applied to foster teamwork. When employees work together towards a common goal, they create a supportive environment that enhances productivity.
    • In a family setting, parents can embody this mantra by nurturing their children with values of cooperation and responsibility, ensuring they grow up to be skilled and compassionate individuals.
    • In community service, volunteers can take inspiration from this mantra by coming together to address social issues, pooling their strengths and resources for a greater impact.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for unity, strength, and the nurturing of future generations.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal relationships to professional environments and community initiatives.
    • By embodying the principles of this mantra, individuals and groups can work towards a harmonious and prosperous society, where collective efforts lead to shared success.
