यस्मिन् देवाः मनमनि संचरन्त्य अपीच्ये न वयं अस्य विद्मः |मित्रो नो अत्रादितिर अनागान सविता देवो वरुणाय वाचत ||
yasmin devā manmani saṁcaranty apīcye3 na vayam asya vidma | mitro no atrāditir anāgān savitā devo varuṇāya vocat ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yasmin: in whom
    • devā: the gods
    • manmani: in their minds
    • saṁcaranty: move or travel
    • apīcye: even in this
    • na: not
    • vayam: we
    • asya: of this
    • vidma: know
    • mitro: friend
    • no: our
    • atrāditir: the beginning
    • anāgān: without sin
    • savitā: the sun
    • devo: god
    • varuṇāya: to Varuna
    • vocat: speaks
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the divine presence in the minds of the gods, suggesting that they are aware of our thoughts and actions.
    • It emphasizes the idea that we may not fully understand the divine workings around us, yet we seek guidance and friendship from the divine.
    • The mention of "Mitro" (friend) and "Varuna" (the god of cosmic order) indicates a relationship of trust and moral integrity.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In moments of decision-making, individuals often seek divine guidance, reflecting the essence of this mantra. For instance, a student may pray for clarity before an important exam.
    • In community gatherings, people invoke the presence of the divine to foster unity and understanding, akin to the friendship mentioned in the mantra.
    • When facing moral dilemmas, individuals may reflect on their actions, seeking to align with the cosmic order represented by Varuna.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the interconnectedness of human thoughts and divine awareness, reminding us that our intentions are observed and understood by the divine.
    • It encourages individuals to cultivate a sense of moral integrity and seek friendship with the divine, fostering a harmonious relationship with the universe.
    • In real life, this mantra can be applied in moments of reflection, decision-making, and community building, guiding individuals towards a path of righteousness and understanding.
