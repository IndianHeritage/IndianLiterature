मन्तर: नराशंसं सुदृष्टमम् अपश्यं सप्रथस्तमम् | दिवो न सद्ममखसम ||
narāśaṁsaṁ sudhṛṣṭamam apaśyaṁ saprathastamam | divo na sadmamakhasam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • narāśaṁsaṁ: Refers to a noble or virtuous person, often interpreted as a hero or a divine being.
    • sudhṛṣṭamam: Means the most excellent or the best in sight or perception.
    • apaśyaṁ: Translates to 'not seen' or 'unseen'.
    • saprathastamam: Indicates something that is most powerful or has great strength.
    • divo: Means 'of the heavens' or 'divine'.
    • na: Means 'not'.
    • sadmamakhasam: Refers to a dwelling or abode that is not obstructed or is free from barriers.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of a noble being who is the best in perception and is powerful, yet remains unseen.
    • It suggests the existence of divine qualities that may not always be visible to the human eye, emphasizing the importance of inner virtues over outward appearances.
    • The reference to the heavens indicates a connection to the divine or spiritual realm, suggesting that true strength and nobility come from a higher source.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In leadership, a true leader may not always be in the spotlight (unseen), but their virtues, such as integrity and wisdom, make them powerful and respected.
    • In personal relationships, individuals who embody kindness and compassion may not always seek recognition, yet their impact is profound and transformative.
    • In nature, the most powerful forces, like love and compassion, are often invisible yet can create significant change in the world.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of recognizing and valuing the unseen virtues and strengths in ourselves and others.
    • It encourages individuals to cultivate inner qualities that contribute to personal growth and societal well-being.
    • In a world often focused on external appearances and achievements, this mantra serves as a reminder to seek and appreciate the deeper, more meaningful aspects of life.
