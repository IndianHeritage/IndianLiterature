स्वस्तिदा विशस् पतिर वृत्रहा विमृधो वशी | वृषेन्द्रः पुर एतु नः सोमपा अभयंकरः ||
svastidā viśas patir vṛtrahā vimṛdho vaśī | vṛṣendraḥ pura etu naḥ somapā abhayaṁkaraḥ ||
  • svastidā: This word can be translated as "bringing well-being" or "auspiciousness." It signifies the importance of health and prosperity in life.
  • viśas patir: This means "lord of the universe" or "ruler of all beings." It emphasizes the divine authority and the interconnectedness of all life.
  • vṛtrahā: This term refers to "the slayer of Vritra," where Vritra symbolizes obstacles or challenges. It represents the overcoming of difficulties and the triumph of good over evil.
  • vimṛdho: This can be interpreted as "one who is powerful" or "mighty." It highlights the strength and capability to face adversities.
  • vaśī: This word means "one who has control" or "master." It suggests the ability to govern oneself and one's circumstances.
  • vṛṣendraḥ: This translates to "the lord of strength" or "the powerful one." It signifies the importance of inner strength and resilience.
  • pura etu naḥ: This phrase means "may he come to us." It is a call for divine presence and guidance in our lives.
  • somapā: This refers to "the one who drinks soma," which is a symbol of immortality and divine inspiration. It represents the pursuit of higher knowledge and enlightenment.
  • abhayaṁkaraḥ: This means "one who grants fearlessness." It signifies the importance of courage and the removal of fears in our lives.

In conclusion, this mantra encapsulates a profound message of seeking well-being, strength, and divine guidance. It emphasizes the importance of overcoming obstacles, mastering oneself, and pursuing higher knowledge. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied during challenging times, such as facing personal or professional difficulties, where one seeks strength and courage. It serves as a reminder to cultivate resilience and to call upon divine support for guidance and protection. By embodying the principles of this mantra, individuals can navigate life's challenges with confidence and grace.
