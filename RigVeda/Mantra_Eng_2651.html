प्र यन्तु वाजास तविषीभिर अग्नयः शुभे सम्मिश्लाः पृषतीर आयुक्षत | बृहदुक्षो मरुतो विश्ववेदसः प्र वेपयन्ति पर्वताम् अदाभ्यः ||
pra yantu vājās taviṣībhir agnayaḥ śubhe sammiślāḥ pṛṣatīr ayukṣata | bṛhadukṣo maruto viśvavedasaḥ pra vepayanti parvatām̐ adābhyāḥ ||
  • Translation: "May the swift and powerful Agni, who is auspicious, lead us to the right path. The mighty Maruts, who are knowledgeable in all, shake the mountains with their strength."
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • pra: forward, towards
    • yantu: may they go
    • vājās: swift, powerful
    • taviṣībhir: with strength
    • agnayaḥ: Agni (the fire god)
    • śubhe: auspicious, good
    • sammiślāḥ: lead us together
    • pṛṣatīr: to the right path
    • ayukṣata: they have harnessed
    • bṛhadukṣo: mighty, strong
    • maruto: Maruts (storm deities)
    • viśvavedasaḥ: knowledgeable in all
    • pra: forward
    • vepayanti: they shake
    • parvatām̐: mountains
    • adābhyāḥ: with their strength
  • Significance: This mantra emphasizes the importance of guidance and strength in our lives. Agni, representing knowledge and purity, is invoked to lead us on the right path, while the Maruts symbolize the power of nature and the forces that can shake the very foundations of our existence.
  • Real-Life Application:
    • In personal development, this mantra can inspire individuals to seek guidance from mentors or spiritual leaders who can help them navigate life's challenges.
    • In leadership, it highlights the importance of strong and knowledgeable leaders who can guide their teams towards success.
    • In environmental awareness, it serves as a reminder of the power of nature and the need to respect and protect our natural resources.
  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the need for guidance and strength in our lives. By invoking the energies of Agni and the Maruts, we can aspire to lead a life that is not only successful but also aligned with the greater good of humanity and nature.
