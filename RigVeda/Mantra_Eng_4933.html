अग्नीपर्जन्याव अवतं धियम मे 'स्मिन हव सुहवा सुश्रुतिं नः |इळाम अन्यो जनयद गर्भं अन्यः प्रजावतीर इषा आ धत्तम अस्मे ||
agnīparjanyāv avataṁ dhiyam me 'smin have suhavā suṣṭutiṁ naḥ | iḻām anyo janayad garbham anyaḥ prajāvatīr iṣa ā dhattam asme ||
  • Agnīparjanyāv: This term refers to the divine forces of fire (Agni) and rain (Parjanya). In Vedic literature, Agni symbolizes energy, transformation, and purification, while Parjanya represents nourishment and abundance.
  • Avataṁ dhiyam me: This phrase translates to "may my intellect be protected." It emphasizes the importance of safeguarding one's wisdom and understanding.
  • 'smin have suhavā suṣṭutiṁ naḥ: This part means "in this, we offer our beautiful praise." It highlights the act of devotion and the significance of expressing gratitude and reverence.
  • Iḻām anyo janayad garbham: This translates to "may another give birth to prosperity." It signifies the desire for growth and the flourishing of life.
  • Anyaḥ prajāvatīr: This means "another who brings forth progeny." It reflects the importance of community and the continuation of life through generations.
  • Iṣa ā dhattam asme: This phrase translates to "may the divine grant us strength." It is a plea for empowerment and support from the divine forces.

In conclusion, this mantra from the Rigveda encapsulates the essence of seeking divine protection for one's intellect, expressing gratitude, and desiring prosperity and strength. It is applicable in real-life scenarios such as:

  • In personal development, individuals can recite this mantra to invoke clarity of thought and wisdom in decision-making.
  • In community gatherings, it can be used to foster a sense of unity and collective growth, encouraging members to support one another.
  • In rituals or ceremonies, it serves as a reminder to express gratitude for the abundance and blessings received in life.

The significance of this mantra lies in its holistic approach to life, emphasizing the interconnectedness of intellect, community, and divine support in achieving prosperity and well-being.
