कथा देवानां कटमस्य यामनि सुमन्तु नाम श्रिण्वताम मनामहे |को मृळाति कटमो नो मयस् करात कटमा ऊती अभ्य आववर्तति ||
kathā devānāṁ katamasya yāmani sumantu nāma śṛṇvatām manāmahe | ko mṛḻāti katamo no mayas karat katama ūtī abhy ā vavartati ||
  • Mantra: "kathā devānāṁ katamasya yāmani sumantu nāma śṛṇvatām manāmahe | ko mṛḻāti katamo no mayas karat katama ūtī abhy ā vavartati ||"
  • Translation: This mantra can be translated as: "How do we invoke the divine beings? Which of them is the most auspicious? We meditate upon the name of the benevolent. Who is the one that brings forth prosperity? Which of them is the one that leads us towards our goals?"
  • Word-by-Word Explanation:
    • kathā: How
    • devānāṁ: of the gods
    • katamasya: which of them
    • yāmani: is invoked
    • sumantu: auspicious or benevolent
    • nāma: name
    • śṛṇvatām: of those who listen
    • manāmahe: we meditate upon
    • ko: who
    • mṛḻāti: brings forth prosperity
    • katamo: which of them
    • no: our
    • mayas: actions
    • karat: leads
    • katama: which
    • ūtī: goal or success
    • abhy: towards
    • āvavartati: manifests or comes forth
  • Significance: This mantra emphasizes the importance of invoking divine guidance and understanding which divine forces are at play in our lives. It encourages individuals to seek the benevolent aspects of the divine and to meditate on their names, suggesting that through this practice, one can align themselves with positive energies that lead to prosperity and success.
  • Real-Life Application:
    • In daily life, individuals can use this mantra during meditation or prayer to focus on their intentions and seek guidance from higher powers.
    • In decision-making scenarios, such as career choices or personal relationships, reflecting on this mantra can help individuals consider which actions align with their higher purpose and lead to positive outcomes.
    • In community settings, this mantra can be used to foster a sense of unity and collective intention, encouraging members to work together towards common goals with divine support.
  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of our actions and the divine. By invoking the auspicious and benevolent aspects of the divine, we can navigate our lives with greater clarity and purpose, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling existence.
