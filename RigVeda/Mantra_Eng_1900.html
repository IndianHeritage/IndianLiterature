प्र यद वहते महिना रथस्य प्र स्यन्द्रा याथो मनुषो न होता | धत्तं सूरिभ्य उता वा स्वश्व्यं नासत्याः रयिषा चः स्याम ||
pra yad vahethe mahinā rathasya pra syandrā yātho manuṣo na hotā | dhattaṁ sūribhya uta vā svaśvyaṁ nāsatyā rayiṣācaḥ syāma ||
  • Translation: "As the great chariot moves, may we be endowed with strength; may we not be hindered by the obstacles that come our way. Let us be blessed with wealth and prosperity, and may we attain the blessings of the divine."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • pra yad vahethe: "As the great chariot moves" - This symbolizes the journey of life and the progress we make.
    • mahinā rathasya: "of the great chariot" - The chariot represents strength, power, and the ability to navigate through life's challenges.
    • pra syandrā yātho: "may we not be hindered" - A plea for resilience and the ability to overcome obstacles.
    • manuṣo na hotā: "by the obstacles that come our way" - Acknowledges the challenges faced by humans in their journey.
    • dhattaṁ sūribhya: "let us be blessed with strength" - A call for divine support and empowerment.
    • uta vā svaśvyaṁ: "and wealth" - Emphasizes the importance of prosperity in life.
    • nāsatyā rayiṣācaḥ syāma: "may we attain the blessings of the divine" - A request for divine grace and abundance.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, this mantra can be invoked when embarking on a new project or venture, symbolizing the hope for success and the strength to overcome challenges.
    • During personal struggles, such as health issues or financial difficulties, individuals may reflect on this mantra to seek resilience and divine support.
    • In community gatherings or rituals, this mantra can be recited to foster a sense of unity and collective strength among participants, encouraging them to support one another in their journeys.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its universal appeal for strength, resilience, and prosperity. It serves as a reminder that while challenges are an inherent part of life, with divine support and inner strength, one can navigate through them successfully. In real-life scenarios, whether in personal endeavors, professional pursuits, or community efforts, this mantra encourages individuals to seek empowerment and blessings, fostering a mindset of positivity and determination.
