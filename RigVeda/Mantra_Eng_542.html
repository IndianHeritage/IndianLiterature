एषो उषा अपूर्व्या व्य उच्छति प्रियā दिवः |स्तुषे वाम अश्विनā बृहत् ||
eṣo uṣā apūrvyā vy ucchati priyā divaḥ | stuṣe vām aśvinā bṛhat ||
  • eṣo uṣā: This phrase translates to "this dawn" or "this morning." It signifies the arrival of a new day, symbolizing hope and new beginnings.
  • apūrvyā: Meaning "unprecedented" or "unique," this word emphasizes the special nature of each dawn, suggesting that every day brings something new and different.
  • vy ucchati: This translates to "rises" or "ascends," indicating the action of the dawn breaking and illuminating the world.
  • priyā divaḥ: This means "beloved of the heavens," suggesting that the dawn is cherished and revered, not just by humans but also by the divine.
  • stuṣe: This word means "to praise" or "to extol," indicating an act of reverence towards the dawn.
  • vām: This translates to "your" or "of you," referring to the Aśvins, the twin deities associated with health, medicine, and the dawn.
  • aśvinā: Referring to the Aśvins, who are often invoked for their benevolence and healing powers, representing the duality of light and darkness.
  • bṛhat: This means "great" or "vast," indicating the expansive nature of the blessings that come with the dawn.

In summary, this mantra celebrates the arrival of dawn as a unique and beloved phenomenon, worthy of praise. It acknowledges the Aśvins, who are seen as bringers of health and vitality, and emphasizes the significance of each new day as a source of hope and renewal.


Significance and Real-Life Application:

  • The mantra serves as a reminder to appreciate the beauty of each new day, encouraging mindfulness and gratitude.
  • In real life, this can be applied by starting each day with a moment of reflection or meditation, acknowledging the opportunities that lie ahead.
  • It can also inspire individuals to seek healing and wellness, both physically and mentally, by invoking the blessings of the Aśvins in their daily lives.
  • Furthermore, it highlights the importance of community and connection, as the dawn is a shared experience that brings people together.
