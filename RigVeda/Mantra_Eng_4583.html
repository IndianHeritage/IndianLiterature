जनं वज्रिन महि चिन मन्यमानम् एभ्यो नृभ्यो रन्धया येष्व अस्मि |अधाऽ हि त्वां पृथिव्यां शूरसातौ हवामहे तनये गोष्व आप्सु ||
janaṁ vajrin mahi cin manyamānam ebhyo nṛbhyo randhayā yeṣv asmi | adhā hi tvā pṛthivyāṁ śūrasātau havāmahe tanaye goṣv apsu ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • janaṁ: people, community
    • vajrin: powerful, strong
    • mahi: great, vast
    • cin: thinking, contemplating
    • manyamānam: considering, reflecting
    • ebhyo: from these, from them
    • nṛbhyo: from men, from humans
    • randhayā: to unite, to bring together
    • yeṣv: in whom, in which
    • asmi: I am, I exist
    • adhā: therefore, thus
    • hi: indeed, surely
    • tvā: you
    • pṛthivyāṁ: in the earth, in the world
    • śūrasātau: in the strength of heroes
    • havāmahe: we invoke, we call upon
    • tanaye: offspring, progeny
    • goṣv: in cattle, in wealth
    • apsu: in waters, in the essence
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks to the strength and unity of the community, invoking the powerful essence of the earth and the strength of heroes.
    • It emphasizes the importance of collective thought and action, suggesting that when people come together with a common purpose, they can achieve great things.
    • The reference to "cattle" and "waters" symbolizes wealth and sustenance, indicating that prosperity comes from unity and strength.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern society, community initiatives such as local clean-up drives or charity events exemplify the power of collective action. When individuals unite for a common cause, they can create significant positive change.
    • In business, teamwork and collaboration among employees can lead to innovative solutions and increased productivity, reflecting the mantra's emphasis on unity and strength.
    • Environmental movements often rely on the collective efforts of individuals who are passionate about sustainability, showcasing how a united community can address global challenges.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for unity and strength among people. It serves as a reminder that when individuals come together, they can harness their collective power to achieve common goals.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied to various aspects of life, including community service, teamwork in professional settings, and collaborative efforts in social movements.
    • Ultimately, it encourages us to reflect on the importance of community and the strength that arises from working together towards a shared vision.
