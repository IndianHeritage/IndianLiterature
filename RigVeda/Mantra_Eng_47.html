आ त्वा विशन्त्वाशवः सोमास इन्द्र गिरवणः |शं ते सन्तु प्रचेतसे ||
ā tvā viśantv āśavaḥ somāsa indra girvaṇaḥ | śaṁ te santu pracetase ||
  • Mantra: "ā tvā viśantv āśavaḥ somāsa indra girvaṇaḥ | śaṁ te santu pracetase ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā: towards
    • tvā: you
    • viśantv: may they enter
    • āśavaḥ: the invigorating (or life-giving)
    • somāsa: the soma (a sacred drink associated with immortality)
    • indra: Indra (the king of gods, associated with strength and rain)
    • girvaṇaḥ: the singers or poets (those who chant or recite)
    • śaṁ: peace or well-being
    • te: to you
    • santu: may they be
    • pracetase: for the wise or intelligent
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the presence of divine energy and strength, represented by Indra, and seeks to invite invigorating forces into one's life.
    • The mention of "soma" symbolizes the pursuit of higher consciousness and spiritual awakening, akin to how people today seek mindfulness and clarity through meditation or yoga.
    • The phrase "may they enter" suggests an openness to receiving blessings and wisdom, which can be related to how individuals today often seek guidance from mentors or spiritual leaders.
    • The call for peace and well-being ("śaṁ te santu") emphasizes the importance of harmony in personal and communal life, reflecting modern values of mental health and community support.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, teams often engage in rituals or practices that promote collaboration and creativity, similar to how the mantra invites invigorating energies.
    • During personal challenges, individuals may turn to meditation or prayer, seeking the "invigorating" forces of peace and wisdom, akin to the essence of this mantra.
    • Community gatherings, such as festivals or spiritual retreats, embody the spirit of this mantra by fostering a sense of unity and shared well-being.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of inviting positive energies and wisdom into our lives.
    • Its significance lies in the universal quest for peace, strength, and higher consciousness, which resonates across cultures and time.
    • In real life, it encourages individuals and communities to seek harmony, support one another, and remain open to the transformative powers of spirituality and wisdom.
