कविर वेधस्य पर्येक्षि माहिनम अत्यो न मृष्टो अभि वाजम् अर्षसि |अपसेधन दुरिता सोम मृळय घृतं वसानः परि यासि निर्णिजम् ||
kavir vedhasyā pary eṣi māhinam atyo na mṛṣṭo abhi vājam arṣasi | apasedhan duritā soma mṛḻaya ghṛtaṁ vasānaḥ pari yāsi nirṇijam ||
  • kavir vedhasyā pary eṣi: This phrase can be translated as "The wise one (Kavi) who knows the essence of the Vedas." Here, "Kavi" refers to a seer or a poet, someone who has deep insight and understanding. In real life, this can be likened to individuals who possess profound knowledge in their fields, such as scientists, philosophers, or spiritual leaders who guide others with their wisdom.
  • māhinam atyo na mṛṣṭo abhi vājam arṣasi: This part means "You, who are beyond the reach of the ordinary, do not let the falsehoods affect you." It emphasizes the importance of staying true to oneself and not being swayed by deceit or negativity. In everyday life, this can be seen in leaders who remain steadfast in their values despite external pressures or misinformation.
  • apasedhan duritā soma mṛḻaya: This translates to "Remove the obstacles and difficulties, O Soma." Soma is often associated with the divine nectar or the essence of life. This line signifies the need to overcome challenges and seek clarity. In practical terms, this can relate to personal struggles, such as overcoming addiction or mental health issues, where one seeks help to clear the path to a better life.
  • ghṛtaṁ vasānaḥ pari yāsi nirṇijam: This means "You who are enveloped in ghee (purity), go forth and achieve your goals." Ghee symbolizes purity and nourishment. This line encourages individuals to embrace purity in their thoughts and actions to achieve their aspirations. In real life, this can be reflected in the pursuit of a healthy lifestyle, where one focuses on physical and mental well-being to reach their full potential.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of wisdom, integrity, overcoming obstacles, and purity in achieving one's goals. It serves as a reminder to seek knowledge, remain true to oneself, and strive for clarity and purity in life. In a world filled with distractions and challenges, this mantra can guide individuals in their personal and professional journeys, encouraging them to rise above difficulties and pursue their aspirations with integrity and clarity.
