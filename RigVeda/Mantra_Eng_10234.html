मह्यं यजन्तु मम यानि हव्याकूतिः सत्याः मनसो मे अस्तु |एनो मा नि गां कतमच चनाहं विश्वे देवासो अधि वोचत नः ||
mahyaṁ yajantu mama yāni havyākūtiḥ satyā manaso me astu | eno mā ni gāṁ katamac canāhaṁ viśve devāso adhi vocatā naḥ ||
  • Translation: "Let them offer to me the oblations that are due to me; may my mind be filled with truth. May I not be deprived of any of the offerings; may all the gods speak to us."
  • Word-by-Word Explanation:
    • mahyaṁ: to me
    • yajantu: let them offer
    • mama: my
    • yāni: those which
    • havyākūtiḥ: offerings or oblations
    • satyā: with truth
    • manaso: of the mind
    • me: my
    • astu: may it be
    • eno: may I not be deprived
    • mā: not
    • ni: of
    • gāṁ: any
    • katamac: any of
    • canāhaṁ: I may not
    • viśve: all
    • devāso: gods
    • adhi: upon
    • vocatā: speak
    • naḥ: to us
  • Significance:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of offerings and the connection between the devotee and the divine. It reflects a desire for truth and clarity in one's thoughts and actions.
    • In real life, this can be seen in various rituals and practices where individuals seek blessings and guidance from higher powers, ensuring that their intentions are pure and aligned with truth.
    • For example, in a corporate setting, a leader may invoke this mantra to ensure that their decisions are made with integrity and that they remain open to guidance from their team and stakeholders.
  • Application in Real Life:
    • This mantra can be applied in daily life by encouraging individuals to reflect on their intentions before making decisions, ensuring that they are rooted in truth and honesty.
    • In personal relationships, it can serve as a reminder to communicate openly and truthfully, fostering a deeper connection with others.
    • In spiritual practices, reciting this mantra can help individuals focus their minds and intentions, aligning them with their higher purpose.
  • Conclusion:
    • The mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the importance of truthfulness and the need for divine guidance in our lives.
    • By incorporating its teachings into our daily practices, we can cultivate a more meaningful and connected existence, both personally and collectively.
