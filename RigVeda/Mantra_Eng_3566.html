बृहस्पते यां परमां परावद अत आ त ऋतस्पृशो नि षेदुः |तुभ्यं खाताऽवताद्रिदुग्धा मध्वः श्चोतन्त्याभितो विरप्शम् ||
bṛhaspate yā paramā parāvad ata ā ta ṛtaspṛśo ni ṣeduḥ | tubhyaṁ khātā avatā adridugdhā madhvaḥ ścotanty abhito virapśam ||
  • Mantra: "bṛhaspate yā paramā parāvad ata ā ta ṛtaspṛśo ni ṣeduḥ | tubhyaṁ khātā avatā adridugdhā madhvaḥ ścotanty abhito virapśam ||"
  • Translation: This mantra is a prayer to Bṛhaspati, the deity of wisdom and eloquence, invoking his supreme power and blessings.
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • bṛhaspate: O Bṛhaspati, the lord of speech and wisdom.
    • yā: who.
    • paramā: the supreme.
    • parāvad: beyond all.
    • ata: thus.
    • ā: come.
    • ta: that.
    • ṛtaspṛśo: who touches the truth.
    • ni ṣeduḥ: may reside.
    • tubhyaṁ: to you.
    • khātā: from the sky.
    • avatā: may descend.
    • adridugdhā: with the essence of the mountains.
    • madhvaḥ: sweet.
    • ścotanty: may shine forth.
    • abhito: around.
    • virapśam: the one who is powerful.
  • Significance: This mantra emphasizes the importance of wisdom, truth, and the divine blessings that come from Bṛhaspati. It highlights the need for guidance in our lives, urging us to seek knowledge and clarity in our thoughts and actions.
  • Real-Life Application:
    • In education, students can recite this mantra to invoke clarity and understanding in their studies, seeking the wisdom of Bṛhaspati to help them grasp complex concepts.
    • In decision-making, individuals can reflect on this mantra to remind themselves to seek the truth and wisdom before making important choices, ensuring that their actions are aligned with higher principles.
    • In leadership, this mantra can serve as a reminder for leaders to act with wisdom and integrity, guiding their teams with clarity and purpose.
  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the significance of wisdom and truth in our lives. By invoking the blessings of Bṛhaspati, we can strive for clarity in our thoughts and actions, leading to a more enlightened and purposeful existence.
