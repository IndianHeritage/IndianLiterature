तव प्र यक्षि संदृशम उता क्रतुं सुदानवः |विश्वे जुषन्त कामिनः ||
tava pra yakṣi saṁdṛśam uta kratuṁ sudānavaḥ | viśve juṣanta kāminaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • Tava: Your
    • Pra: To bring forth or manifest
    • Yakṣi: To see or perceive
    • Saṁdṛśam: In the presence or sight
    • Uta: And also
    • Kratuṁ: Intellect or will
    • Sudānavaḥ: Well-formed or well-structured
    • Viśve: All or universal
    • Juṣanta: Enjoying or delighting
    • Kāminaḥ: Desiring or wishing
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks to the divine presence and the ability to perceive and manifest desires through intellect and will.
    • The phrase "tava pra yakṣi" suggests a connection to a higher power, indicating that one's desires and intentions are seen and acknowledged.
    • "Viśve juṣanta kāminaḥ" emphasizes the universal nature of desires, suggesting that all beings share in the experience of wanting and seeking fulfillment.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, when a team works together towards a common goal, they often invoke a sense of collective intention and purpose, akin to the "kratuṁ sudānavaḥ" aspect of the mantra.
    • In personal relationships, understanding and acknowledging each other's desires can lead to deeper connections, reflecting the "viśve juṣanta kāminaḥ" sentiment.
    • In spiritual practices, meditation and mindfulness can help individuals align their intentions with their higher self, resonating with the idea of perceiving and manifesting one's true desires.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of recognizing and manifesting desires through a connection with the divine and the collective.
    • It serves as a reminder that our intentions are powerful and can lead to fulfillment when aligned with a higher purpose.
    • In real life, this can be applied in various contexts, such as personal growth, teamwork, and spiritual practices, encouraging individuals to be mindful of their desires and the impact they have on themselves and others.
