तक्षद यदī मनसो वेनतो वाग ज्येष्ठस्य वा धर्मणि क्षोर अनीकें |आद इम आयन वरम् आ वावशानां जुष्टं पतिं कलशे गावा इन्दुम ||
takṣad yadī manaso venato vāg jyeṣṭhasya vā dharmaṇi kṣor anīke | ād īm āyan varam ā vāvaśānā juṣṭam patiṁ kalaśe gāva indum ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • takṣad: to create or to fashion
    • yadī: if
    • manaso: of the mind
    • venato: of the one who is devoted or inclined
    • vāg: speech or word
    • jyeṣṭhasya: of the elder or the most important
    • vā: or
    • dharmaṇi: in righteousness or in duty
    • kṣor: sharp or keen
    • anīke: in the group or assembly
    • ād: to lead or to guide
    • īm: to us or for us
    • āyan: a place or abode
    • varam: a boon or blessing
    • ā: from
    • vāvaśānā: to be desired or sought after
    • juṣṭam: filled or endowed
    • patiṁ: lord or master
    • kalaśe: in the pot or vessel
    • gāva: of the cows or wealth
    • indum: the moon or nectar
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the creation and the importance of the mind and speech in the pursuit of righteousness.
    • It emphasizes the need for devotion and the role of the elder or wise in guiding the community.
    • The reference to "sharpness" suggests the need for clarity and precision in thought and action.
    • The mantra also highlights the significance of seeking blessings and the importance of community in achieving goals.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace, effective communication (vāg) and the guidance of experienced leaders (jyeṣṭhasya) are crucial for achieving team goals.
    • In personal relationships, being mindful (manaso) and respectful of each other's thoughts and feelings can lead to stronger bonds.
    • In community service, working together (anīke) towards a common goal while upholding ethical values (dharmaṇi) can create a positive impact.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of mental clarity, effective communication, and ethical conduct in both personal and communal life.
    • It teaches us that by seeking wisdom and guidance from those who are experienced, we can navigate challenges more effectively.
    • In real life, applying these principles can lead to harmonious relationships, successful teamwork, and a fulfilling life.
