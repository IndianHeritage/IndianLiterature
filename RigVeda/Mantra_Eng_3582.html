तā ā चरन्ति समानā पुरस्तात् समानतः समानā पप्रथानाः |ऋतस्य देविः सदसो बुद्धानां गवां न सर्गा उषसो जारन्ते ||
tā ā caranti samanā purastāt samānataḥ samanā paprathānāḥ | ṛtasya devīḥ sadaso budhānā gavāṁ na sargā uṣaso jarante ||
  • tā ā caranti samanā purastāt: This phrase can be translated to "They move together, united, in front." It signifies the importance of unity and collective action. In real life, this can be seen in team sports where players must work together to achieve a common goal, such as winning a game.
  • samānataḥ samanā paprathānāḥ: This means "In equality, they spread out together." It emphasizes the idea of equality and shared purpose. For example, in a workplace, when all employees are treated equally and work collaboratively, it fosters a positive environment that can lead to greater productivity and innovation.
  • ṛtasya devīḥ sadaso budhānā: This translates to "The goddesses of truth, the wise ones, are present." This highlights the significance of truth and wisdom in our lives. In everyday scenarios, this can be reflected in ethical decision-making, where individuals choose to act with integrity and honesty, leading to trust and respect in relationships.
  • gavāṁ na sargā uṣaso jarante: This phrase means "The cows do not give birth to the dawn." It symbolizes the idea that certain things are not born from others, indicating the uniqueness of each entity. In real life, this can be related to the concept of individuality, where each person has their own path and contributions to make, much like how different species contribute to biodiversity.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of unity, equality, truth, and individuality. In a world that often promotes division and competition, this mantra serves as a reminder of the power of collaboration and shared values. It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, such as teamwork in sports, ethical practices in business, and the celebration of individual uniqueness in society. By embodying these principles, we can create a more harmonious and productive environment in our personal and professional lives.
