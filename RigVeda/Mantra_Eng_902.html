एते त इन्द्र जन्तवो विश्वं पुष्यन्ति वार्यम् |अन्तर हि ख्यो जनानां आर्यो वेदो अदाशुषां तेषां नो वेद आ भर ||
ete ta indra jantavo viśvam puṣyanti vāryam | antar hi khyo janānām aryo vedo adāśuṣāṁ teṣāṁ no veda ā bhara ||
  • Translation: "These, O Indra, are the beings that nourish the entire universe; they are the ones who bring forth strength. Indeed, within the hearts of all beings, the noble knowledge resides; may it not be hidden from us."
  • Word-by-Word Explanation:
    • ete: these
    • ta: O
    • indra: Indra, the king of gods and the god of rain and thunderstorms
    • jantavo: beings or creatures
    • viśvam: the entire universe
    • puṣyanti: nourish or provide sustenance
    • vāryam: strength or power
    • antar: within
    • hi: indeed
    • khyo: hearts or minds
    • janānām: of all beings
    • aryo: noble or worthy
    • vedo: knowledge or wisdom
    • adāśuṣāṁ: that which is not hidden
    • teṣāṁ: from them
    • no: us
    • veda: may it be known
    • ā bhara: may it not be concealed
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of knowledge and wisdom in our lives. For instance, in education, teachers play a crucial role in nourishing the minds of students, much like the beings mentioned in the mantra.
    • In a community, leaders who promote understanding and cooperation among members can be seen as embodying the essence of this mantra, as they bring forth strength and unity.
    • In personal development, individuals who seek knowledge and strive to understand themselves and others are practicing the principles outlined in this mantra, ensuring that noble knowledge is not hidden from them.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for the pursuit of knowledge and the recognition of the interconnectedness of all beings. It reminds us that true strength comes from understanding and nurturing one another. In real-life scenarios, whether in education, community building, or personal growth, the principles of nourishment through knowledge and the importance of not hiding wisdom are applicable. By embracing these values, we can foster a more enlightened and harmonious society.
