सत्यं ऊचुर नर एव हि चक्रुर अनु स्वधाम ऋभवो जग्मुर एताम् |विभ्राजमानांश चमसां अहेवावेनत त्वष्टा चतुरो ददृश्वान् ||
satyam ūcur nara evā hi cakrur anu svadhām ṛbhavo jagmur etām | vibhrājamānām̐ś camasām̐ ahevāvenat tvaṣṭā caturo dadṛśvān ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • satyam: Truth
    • ūcur: They spoke
    • nara: Men or humans
    • evā: Indeed
    • hi: For
    • cakrur: They created
    • anu: Following
    • svadhām: Their own nature
    • ṛbhavo: The wise or enlightened ones
    • jagmur: They went
    • etām: To this
    • vibhrājamānām̐ś: Shining or radiating
    • camasām̐: Of the beings or creatures
    • ahevāvenat: Not to be neglected
    • tvaṣṭā: The creator or craftsman
    • caturo: Four
    • dadṛśvān: Having seen
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the essence of truth and the role of humans in creating their own reality.
    • It emphasizes that enlightened beings (ṛbhavo) follow their own nature (svadhām) and contribute to the world.
    • The phrase "vibhrājamānām̐ś" suggests that these beings shine brightly, indicating their wisdom and knowledge.
    • The mention of "tvaṣṭā" refers to the creator, highlighting the importance of creation and craftsmanship in life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace, employees who embody truth and integrity often inspire others, leading to a positive and productive environment.
    • Artists and creators who follow their true nature often produce work that resonates deeply with others, showcasing the power of authenticity.
    • In personal relationships, being truthful and transparent fosters trust and strengthens bonds, reflecting the essence of the mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of truth and authenticity in human life.
    • It teaches that by following our true nature and embracing our creative potential, we can contribute positively to the world around us.
    • In various aspects of life—be it personal, professional, or artistic—this mantra serves as a reminder to uphold truth and integrity, leading to a more harmonious existence.
