यद वा तृक्षौ मघवन द्रुह्याव आ जन एत पूरु कच च वृष्ण्यम् |अस्मभ्यं तद रिरिः सं नृषाह्ये 'मित्रान पृत्सु तुर्वणे ||
yad vā tṛkṣau maghavan druhyāv ā jane yat pūrau kac ca vṛṣṇyam | asmabhyaṁ tad rirīhi saṁ nṛṣāhye 'mitrān pṛtsu turvaṇe ||
  • Mantra: "yad vā tṛkṣau maghavan druhyāv ā jane yat pūrau kac ca vṛṣṇyam | asmabhyaṁ tad rirīhi saṁ nṛṣāhye 'mitrān pṛtsu turvaṇe ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yad: whatever
    • vā: or
    • tṛkṣau: the strong
    • maghavan: O Maghavan (a name for Indra, the god of rain and thunder)
    • druhya: enemies
    • ā: to
    • jane: the people
    • yat: that which
    • pūrau: the prosperous
    • kac: or
    • ca: and
    • vṛṣṇyam: the strength
    • asmabhyaṁ: for us
    • tad: that
    • rirīhi: may you bestow
    • saṁ: together
    • nṛṣāhye: in the battle
    • 'mitrān: friends
    • pṛtsu: in the fields
    • turvaṇe: in the strong
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to the deity Maghavan (Indra), seeking strength and prosperity.
    • The phrase "yad vā tṛkṣau" suggests a request for whatever strength or power is available.
    • It acknowledges the presence of enemies ("druhya") and the need for protection against them.
    • The mantra emphasizes the importance of community ("ā jane") and the collective strength of the people.
    • By invoking "pūrau" and "vṛṣṇyam," it highlights the desire for prosperity and strength in all endeavors.
    • The concluding part of the mantra, "asmabhyaṁ tad rirīhi," is a plea for blessings and support in overcoming challenges, particularly in battles or conflicts.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, this mantra can be likened to a team seeking collective strength to overcome competition and achieve success.
    • In personal life, it can represent individuals coming together to support each other in times of crisis, such as during natural disasters or personal hardships.
    • In community initiatives, it reflects the importance of unity and collaboration to achieve common goals, such as environmental conservation or social justice.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for unity, strength, and support in the face of adversity.
    • It serves as a reminder that collective efforts can lead to prosperity and protection against challenges.
    • In real-life scenarios, whether in personal relationships, community efforts, or professional environments, the essence of this mantra encourages collaboration and resilience.
