दृशानो रुक्म उर्वियā व्य अद्यौद दुरमार्षम् आयुः श्रीये रुचानः |अग्निर अमृतो अभवत वयोभिर यद एनं द्यौर जनयत सुरेताः ||
dṛśāno rukma urviyā vy adyaud durmarṣam āyuḥ śriye rucānaḥ | agnir amṛto abhavad vayobhir yad enaṁ dyaur janayat suretāḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • dṛśāno: Of the seer or the one who perceives.
    • rukma: Golden or radiant.
    • urviyā: With vastness or expansiveness.
    • vy: Indeed or truly.
    • adyaud: Today or in this moment.
    • durmarṣam: Unassailable or invincible.
    • āyuḥ: Life or longevity.
    • śriye: For prosperity or wealth.
    • rucānaḥ: Radiant or shining.
    • agnir: Fire or the divine energy.
    • amṛto: Immortal or eternal.
    • abhavad: Became or manifested.
    • vayobhir: By the forces of life or energies.
    • yad: That which.
    • enaṁ: This or him.
    • dyaur: Sky or heaven.
    • janayat: Generates or gives birth to.
    • suretāḥ: The divine or the wise.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the perception of the divine and the radiant qualities of life.
    • The phrase "dṛśāno rukma urviyā" suggests that those who can see or perceive the divine are blessed with a golden, expansive vision.
    • "Adyaud durmarṣam āyuḥ" emphasizes the importance of living a life that is invincible and prosperous in the present moment.
    • The invocation of "agnir amṛto" signifies the eternal nature of divine energy, which is essential for life and prosperity.
    • The mantra concludes with the idea that the sky or heaven generates this divine energy, which is a source of wisdom and enlightenment.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, individuals who perceive opportunities (dṛśāno) and act upon them with a positive and expansive mindset (rukma urviyā) often achieve success.
    • In personal life, focusing on the present moment (adyaud) and cultivating resilience (durmarṣam) can lead to a fulfilling and prosperous life.
    • Practices such as meditation can help individuals connect with their inner divine energy (agnir) and foster a sense of immortality (amṛto) through spiritual growth.
    • Communities that come together to support each other in wisdom and prosperity (suretāḥ) can create a nurturing environment for all members.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of perception, resilience, and the divine energy that sustains life.
    • It encourages individuals to embrace the present, cultivate a radiant mindset, and connect with the eternal aspects of existence.
    • In real life, applying the principles of this mantra can lead to personal growth, community support, and a deeper understanding of one's purpose.
