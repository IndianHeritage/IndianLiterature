या इन्दोः पवमानस्यानु धामान्य अक्रमीत् |तम आहुः सुप्रजा इति यस् ते सोमाविधान मन इन्द्रायन्दो परि श्रव ||
ya indoḥ pavamānasyānu dhāmāny akramīt | tam āhuḥ suprajā iti yas te somāvidhan mana indrāyendo pari srava ||
  • ya indoḥ pavamānasyānu dhāmāny akramīt: This phrase can be translated as "He who follows the path of Indra, the one who moves swiftly." Here, "Indra" represents the king of the gods, symbolizing strength, power, and leadership. The term "pavamānasyānu" refers to the swift movement or the path that is followed, indicating a journey or a quest for knowledge and enlightenment.
  • tam āhuḥ suprajā iti: This translates to "They call him the one with good progeny." The term "suprajā" signifies prosperity and the ability to produce good offspring, which can be interpreted as the ability to create a positive legacy or impact in the world. This can relate to individuals who contribute positively to society, nurturing future generations.
  • yas te somāvidhan mana indrāyendo pari srava: This part means "Whose mind is filled with the essence of Soma, and who is dedicated to Indra." "Soma" is often associated with a divine nectar that grants immortality and wisdom. This suggests that a person who is devoted to higher ideals and imbibes wisdom is revered and respected.

  • Real-Life Example: Consider a leader in a community who embodies the qualities of Indra. This leader works tirelessly for the welfare of the people, ensuring that their needs are met and that they thrive. By following the path of righteousness and wisdom, they create a legacy of good governance and inspire future leaders.
  • Application in Daily Life: The mantra encourages individuals to pursue knowledge and wisdom, much like the pursuit of education and personal growth. It emphasizes the importance of nurturing relationships and contributing positively to society, whether through family, community service, or professional endeavors.

  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call to action for individuals to embody the qualities of leadership, wisdom, and responsibility. It serves as a reminder that by following a righteous path and contributing positively to the world, one can create a lasting impact. In real life, this mantra can inspire individuals to strive for excellence in their personal and professional lives, fostering a sense of community and legacy.
