स घा नः सूनुः शवसाऽ पृथुप्रगामा सुशेवः |मीढ्वाम् अस्माकम् बभूयात् ||
sa ghā naḥ sūnuḥ śavasā pṛthupragāmā suśevaḥ | mīḍhvām̐ asmākam babhūyāt ||
  • sa - This word translates to "that" or "he," indicating a connection to a higher power or divine entity.
  • ghā - This means "to speak" or "to call," suggesting communication or invocation.
  • naḥ - This translates to "our," indicating a sense of belonging or community.
  • sūnuḥ - Meaning "son," it symbolizes lineage, continuity, and the future generation.
  • śavasā - This word means "with strength" or "powerful," indicating the importance of strength in our endeavors.
  • pṛthupragāmā - This translates to "one who moves forward" or "progressive," emphasizing the need for advancement and growth.
  • suśevaḥ - Meaning "well-being" or "good fortune," it highlights the aspiration for prosperity and positive outcomes.
  • mīḍhvām̐ - This word means "may we be" or "let us be," expressing a desire or intention.
  • asmākam - This translates to "our," reinforcing the collective aspect of the mantra.
  • babhūyāt - Meaning "may it become" or "may it manifest," it indicates a wish for realization or fulfillment.

The mantra can be interpreted as a prayer or invocation for strength, progress, and well-being for the community and future generations. It emphasizes the importance of collective growth and the aspiration for a prosperous future.


In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts:

  • Family Gatherings: During family events, this mantra can be recited to invoke blessings for the well-being and success of all family members, especially the younger generation.
  • Community Events: In community gatherings, it can serve as a reminder of the collective responsibility towards progress and prosperity.
  • Personal Reflection: Individuals can use this mantra for personal motivation, focusing on their strength and aspirations for growth and success.

Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for unity, strength, and progress. It serves as a reminder that our actions and intentions should be directed towards the well-being of our community and future generations. By embodying these principles, we can create a positive impact in our lives and the lives of those around us.
