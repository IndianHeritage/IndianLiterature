ऋचां कपोतं नुदत प्राणोदं इषं मदन्तः परि गां नयध्वम् |संयोगयन्तो दुरितानि विश्वा हित्वा न ऊर्जं प्र पतात पतिष्ठः ||
ṛcā kapotaṁ nudata praṇodam iṣam madantaḥ pari gāṁ nayadhvam | saṁyopayanto duritāni viśvā hitvā na ūrjam pra patāt patiṣṭhaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ṛcā: By the hymns or verses
    • kapotaṁ: The dove or messenger
    • nudata: To be urged or inspired
    • praṇodam: To lead or guide
    • iṣam: The goal or aim
    • madantaḥ: Those who are intoxicated or filled with joy
    • pari: All around or completely
    • gāṁ: The earth or the world
    • nayadhvam: Lead us or guide us
    • saṁyopayanto: Bringing together or uniting
    • duritāni: Difficulties or obstacles
    • viśvā: All or everything
    • hitvā: Leaving behind or overcoming
    • na: Not
    • ūrjam: Energy or strength
    • pra: Forward or towards
    • patāt: Falling or descending
    • patiṣṭhaḥ: Stand firm or be established
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the power of hymns to guide and inspire individuals, much like how a dove symbolizes peace and communication.
    • It emphasizes the importance of unity and collective effort in overcoming obstacles, akin to teamwork in a corporate environment where diverse skills come together to achieve a common goal.
    • The phrase "hitvā na ūrjam pra patāt" suggests that by overcoming difficulties, one can maintain their strength and energy, similar to athletes who push through challenges to achieve their best performance.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community project, individuals from different backgrounds come together, inspired by a shared vision, to create a positive impact, reflecting the essence of this mantra.
    • In personal development, individuals may face various challenges, but by focusing on their goals and supporting each other, they can overcome these hurdles and emerge stronger.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the power of collective effort, guidance, and the importance of overcoming obstacles in life.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, such as teamwork, community building, and personal growth, reminding us that through unity and perseverance, we can achieve our goals and maintain our strength.
