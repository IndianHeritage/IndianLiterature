परी चिन् मार्तो द्रविणं ममण्याद ऋतस्य पथā नमसā विवासेत् |उत स्वेन क्रतुनā सं वदेत श्रेयान्सं दक्षं मनसā जगृभ्यात् ||
pari cin marto draviṇam mamanyād ṛtasya pathā namasā vivāset | uta svena kratunā saṁ vadeta śreyāṁsaṁ dakṣam manasā jagṛbhyāt ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • pari: around, in all directions
    • cin: to know, to perceive
    • marto: mortal, human
    • draviṇam: wealth, resources
    • mamanyād: my, of mine
    • ṛtasya: of truth, of order
    • pathā: path, way
    • namasā: with reverence, with respect
    • vivāset: may he speak, may he express
    • uta: or, alternatively
    • svena: by his own
    • kratunā: with determination, with will
    • saṁ: together, in union
    • vadeta: may he speak, may he say
    • śreyāṁsaṁ: the best, the most auspicious
    • dakṣam: capable, skilled
    • manasā: with the mind
    • jagṛbhyāt: may he grasp, may he attain
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of understanding and perceiving the world around us.
    • It speaks to the mortal human condition, highlighting the need for resources and wealth in our lives.
    • The phrase "ṛtasya pathā" suggests that there is a righteous path or order that one should follow.
    • By showing reverence ("namasā"), we are encouraged to express our thoughts and intentions clearly.
    • The mantra also emphasizes the power of determination and will ("kratunā") in achieving our goals.
    • It suggests that by uniting our minds and efforts, we can attain the best outcomes ("śreyāṁsaṁ").
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, understanding market dynamics and resources is crucial for success. This mantra can inspire entrepreneurs to seek knowledge and act with determination.
    • In personal relationships, showing respect and clear communication can lead to better understanding and harmony.
    • In education, students are encouraged to grasp knowledge with a focused mind, which aligns with the idea of attaining the best outcomes through effort and unity.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of knowledge, respect, determination, and unity in achieving success.
    • Its significance lies in its applicability across various aspects of life, from personal growth to professional endeavors.
    • By embodying the principles outlined in this mantra, individuals can navigate their paths with clarity and purpose, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling life.
