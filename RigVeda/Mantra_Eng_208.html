तेन सत्येन जागृतम अधि प्रचेतुने पदे |इन्द्राग्नि शर्म यच्छतम ||
tena satyena jāgṛtam adhi pracetune pade | indrāgnī śarma yacchatam ||
  • Mantra: "tena satyena jāgṛtam adhi pracetune pade | indrāgnī śarma yacchatam ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • tena: by that
    • satyena: with truth
    • jāgṛtam: awakened or enlightened
    • adhi: upon or in accordance with
    • pracetune: the wise or intelligent
    • pade: in the path or way
    • indrāgnī: Indra and Agni (deities representing power and fire)
    • śarma: comfort or protection
    • yacchatam: may grant or provide
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of truth as a guiding principle in life.
    • It suggests that by adhering to truth, one can achieve enlightenment and wisdom.
    • The invocation of Indra and Agni symbolizes the request for strength and protection in one's endeavors.
    • In practical terms, this can be seen in situations where individuals seek guidance in their personal or professional lives, relying on honesty and integrity to navigate challenges.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, a leader who practices transparency and honesty fosters a culture of trust, leading to better teamwork and productivity.
    • In personal relationships, being truthful can strengthen bonds and resolve conflicts, as partners feel secure and valued.
    • In education, students who embrace honesty in their studies often find greater success and fulfillment, as they build a solid foundation of knowledge.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the power of truth in achieving clarity and wisdom.
    • It highlights the significance of seeking divine support (Indra and Agni) in our pursuits, ensuring that we are protected and guided along our path.
    • In essence, the mantra encourages individuals to live authentically and seek strength through truth, making it applicable in various aspects of life, from personal growth to professional integrity.
