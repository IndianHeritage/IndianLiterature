अश्वावतीर गोमतीर न उषासो वीरवतीः सदम उचन्तु भद्राः |घृतं दूहानां विश्वतः प्रपीता यूम पात स्वस्तिभिः सदा नः ||
aśvāvatīr gomatīr na uṣāso vīravatīḥ sadam ucchantu bhadrāḥ | ghṛtaṁ duhānā viśvataḥ prapītā yūyam pāta svastibhiḥ sadā naḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • aśvāvatīr: One who is like a horse, symbolizing strength and vitality.
    • gomatīr: One who is rich in cows, representing wealth and prosperity.
    • na uṣāso: Not the dawn, indicating a time of transition or change.
    • vīravatīḥ: Those who are brave or heroic.
    • sadam: A place of dwelling or abode.
    • ucchantu: May they rise or ascend.
    • bhadrāḥ: Auspicious or beneficial.
    • ghṛtaṁ: Ghee or clarified butter, symbolizing nourishment and purity.
    • duhānā: From the churners, indicating effort and hard work.
    • viśvataḥ: From all directions or universally.
    • prapītā: Having been drunk or consumed.
    • yūyam: You all, addressing a group.
    • pāta: May you protect or safeguard.
    • svastibhiḥ: With auspiciousness or well-being.
    • sadā: Always or forever.
    • naḥ: Us or our.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes strength, prosperity, and auspiciousness. It emphasizes the importance of bravery and the collective well-being of a community.
    • The imagery of horses and cows symbolizes vitality and wealth, suggesting that these qualities are essential for a thriving life.
    • The call for auspiciousness and protection indicates a desire for safety and prosperity in all endeavors.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be applied to the idea of teamwork and collaboration, where each member contributes their strength (aśvāvatīr) and resources (gomatīr) to achieve common goals.
    • In personal life, it can inspire individuals to be brave (vīravatīḥ) in facing challenges, while also seeking the support of their community for collective well-being (bhadrāḥ).
    • During significant life transitions, such as starting a new job or moving to a new place, this mantra serves as a reminder to embrace change and seek auspicious beginnings.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its holistic approach to life, emphasizing strength, prosperity, and community support.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal growth to professional endeavors, reminding us of the importance of collaboration and auspiciousness in our pursuits.
    • By invoking these qualities, individuals and communities can strive for a harmonious and prosperous existence.
