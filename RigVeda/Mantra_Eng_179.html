मां नः शंसो अररुषो धूर्तिः प्रणङ मार्त्यस्य |रक्षा णो ब्रह्मणस पते ||
mā naḥ śaṁso araruṣo dhūrtiḥ praṇaṅ martyasya | rakṣā ṇo brahmaṇas pate ||
  • mā naḥ: This phrase translates to "may we not" or "let us not." It expresses a desire for protection or avoidance of negative outcomes.
  • śaṁso: This word means "to be harmed" or "to suffer." It indicates the potential for adversity or misfortune.
  • araruṣo: This term can be interpreted as "the one who is angry" or "the one who causes distress." It suggests the presence of a force or entity that may bring about challenges.
  • dhūrtiḥ: This word means "deception" or "trickery." It highlights the idea of being misled or encountering falsehoods.
  • praṇaṅ: This translates to "life" or "vitality." It signifies the essence of existence and the importance of preserving it.
  • martyasya: This term refers to "of mortals" or "of human beings." It emphasizes the human condition and the vulnerabilities that come with it.
  • rakṣā: This word means "protection" or "safeguarding." It conveys the need for security and defense against harm.
  • ṇo: This is a form of "us" or "our," indicating a collective plea for protection.
  • brahmaṇas: This term refers to "the divine" or "the ultimate reality." It signifies a higher power or cosmic order that governs existence.
  • pate: This word means "lord" or "master." It denotes reverence towards a higher authority or divine being.

Conclusion: The mantra "mā naḥ śaṁso araruṣo dhūrtiḥ praṇaṅ martyasya | rakṣā ṇo brahmaṇas pate ||" is a profound invocation for protection against harm, deception, and the challenges of mortal life. It reflects a universal human desire for safety and guidance from a higher power. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applicable in various contexts, such as during times of personal crisis, when facing difficult decisions, or when seeking clarity in a world filled with uncertainties. By reciting this mantra, individuals can cultivate a sense of peace and resilience, trusting in the divine to safeguard their journey through life.
