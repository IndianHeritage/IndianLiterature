स नः पवस्व वाजयुश चक्राणश चारुम अध्वरम् |बरहिष्मां ā विवासति ||
sa naḥ pavasva vājayuś cakrāṇaś cārum adhvaram | barhiṣmām̐ ā vivāsati ||
  • sa naḥ - This phrase translates to "may He (the divine) be with us." It signifies a call for divine presence and support in our endeavors.
  • pavasva - This word means "purify" or "make pure." It indicates a desire for spiritual cleansing and the removal of impurities from our thoughts and actions.
  • vājayuś - This term refers to "strength" or "vigor." It embodies the idea of empowerment and the ability to achieve one's goals with determination.
  • cakrāṇaś - This word can be interpreted as "those who are engaged in action" or "those who are active." It emphasizes the importance of being proactive and involved in the world around us.
  • cārum - Meaning "beautiful" or "auspicious," this word highlights the significance of positive and harmonious actions in life.
  • adhvaram - This term refers to "sacrifice" or "ritual." It signifies the importance of dedication and commitment to one's duties and responsibilities.
  • barhiṣmām̐ - This word relates to "sacred grass" or "the place of worship." It symbolizes the sanctity of the environment in which spiritual practices are conducted.
  • ā vivāsati - This phrase means "may He (the divine) come forth" or "may He manifest." It expresses a longing for divine intervention and guidance in our lives.

Conclusion: The mantra "sa naḥ pavasva vājayuś cakrāṇaś cārum adhvaram | barhiṣmām̐ ā vivāsati ||" encapsulates a profound spiritual aspiration. It calls for divine presence and purity in our actions, encouraging us to engage actively and beautifully in our lives while remaining committed to our responsibilities. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as during personal challenges, community service, or any endeavor where one seeks strength and guidance. By invoking this mantra, individuals can cultivate a sense of purpose, clarity, and connection to the divine, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling and harmonious life.
