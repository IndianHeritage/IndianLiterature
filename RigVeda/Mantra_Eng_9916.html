यस्यौषधिः प्रसर्पथाङ्गमाङ्गम परुष्परुः |ततो यक्ष्मं वि बाधध्व उग्रो मध्यमशीर इव ||
yasyauṣadhīḥ prasarpathāṅgam-aṅgam paruṣ-paruḥ | tato yakṣmaṁ vi bādhadhva ugro madhyamaśīr iva ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yasyauṣadhīḥ: "whose medicines" - Refers to the healing properties of herbs and plants.
    • prasarpathāṅgam-aṅgam: "the path of the body and its parts" - Indicates the interconnectedness of the body and its various functions.
    • paruṣ-paruḥ: "harsh and strong" - Suggests the challenges and difficulties faced in life.
    • tato: "therefore" - A transition word indicating a conclusion or result.
    • yakṣmaṁ: "disease" - Refers to ailments or illnesses that affect the body.
    • vi bādhadhva: "do not obstruct" - A call to avoid hindrances or barriers to health.
    • ugro: "fierce" - Represents the intensity of challenges or diseases.
    • madhyamaśīr: "in the middle" - Suggests a balanced state or moderation.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of natural remedies and the healing power of plants.
    • It highlights the need to maintain balance in life, especially when facing difficulties or health challenges.
    • The mantra serves as a reminder to seek harmony within the body and to avoid actions that may obstruct health.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern medicine, herbal remedies are often used alongside conventional treatments to enhance healing.
    • Practices like yoga and meditation promote balance and well-being, aligning with the mantra's call for moderation.
    • In times of stress or illness, individuals are encouraged to seek natural solutions, such as herbal teas or supplements, to support their health.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the significance of natural healing and the importance of maintaining balance in our lives.
    • It encourages individuals to be mindful of their health and to seek remedies that promote well-being without obstruction.
    • In a world filled with challenges, this mantra inspires a holistic approach to health, integrating both physical and mental well-being.
