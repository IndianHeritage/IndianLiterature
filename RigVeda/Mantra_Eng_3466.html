ऋभुतो रयिः प्रथमश्रवस्तमो वाजश्रुतासो यं अजीजनन नरः | विभ्वतष्टो विदथेषु प्रवाच्यो यं देवासोऽवथā स विचर्षणिः ||
ṛbhuto rayiḥ prathamaśravastamo vājaśrutāso yam ajījanan naraḥ | vibhvataṣṭo vidatheṣu pravācyo yaṁ devāso 'vathā sa vicarṣaṇiḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ṛbhuto: From the wise or the learned.
    • rayiḥ: Wealth or prosperity.
    • prathama: First or foremost.
    • śravastamo: The one with the greatest fame or glory.
    • vājaśrutāso: Those who are known for their strength or valor.
    • yam: Whom.
    • ajījanan: Have given birth to or have produced.
    • naraḥ: Man or human being.
    • vibhvataṣṭo: The one who is powerful or mighty.
    • vidatheṣu: In the assemblies or gatherings.
    • pravācyo: To be spoken of or mentioned.
    • yaṁ: Whom.
    • devāso: The gods or divine beings.
    • 'vathā: Have protected or supported.
    • sa: He or that one.
    • vicarṣaṇiḥ: The one who is wise or knowledgeable.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the qualities of a wise and prosperous individual.
    • It emphasizes the importance of wisdom and strength in achieving success and recognition.
    • The mantra suggests that those who are wise and strong are revered by both humans and divine beings.
    • It highlights the idea that true wealth comes from wisdom and the ability to lead and inspire others.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • A successful leader in a company who combines knowledge with strength to guide their team effectively.
    • A community leader who is respected for their wisdom and ability to bring people together for a common cause.
    • Individuals who use their talents and knowledge to uplift others, thereby gaining recognition and respect.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of wisdom, strength, and prosperity in human life.
    • It teaches us that true success is not just about material wealth but also about the respect and recognition earned through knowledge and leadership.
    • In real life, applying the principles of this mantra can lead to personal growth, community respect, and a fulfilling life.
