इमा इन्द्राय सुण्विरे सोमासो दध्याशिरः |तां ā मदाया वज्रहस्त पीतये हरिभ्यां याह्य ओक आ ||
ima indrāya sunvire somāso dadhyāśiraḥ | tām̐ ā madāya vajrahasta pītaye haribhyāṁ yāhy oka ā ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ima: these
    • indrāya: for Indra (the king of gods)
    • sunvire: who is praised
    • somāso: of the soma (a sacred drink)
    • dadhyāśiraḥ: may we have a good mind
    • tām̐: that
    • ā: for
    • madāya: intoxication or joy
    • vajrahasta: with a thunderbolt in hand
    • pītaye: for drinking
    • haribhyāṁ: for the gods
    • yāhy: may we go
    • oka: to the abode
    • ā: to
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Indra, the god of rain and thunderstorms, asking for his blessings.
    • The reference to "soma" indicates a desire for spiritual and physical nourishment, as soma was considered a divine drink that grants immortality and enlightenment.
    • The phrase "vajrahasta" symbolizes strength and power, as Indra is often depicted wielding a thunderbolt, representing his ability to overcome obstacles.
    • The mantra expresses a wish for joy and intoxication, not in a literal sense, but as a metaphor for spiritual ecstasy and fulfillment.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern life, this mantra can be related to seeking strength and guidance in challenging situations, much like how individuals turn to mentors or leaders for support.
    • The concept of "soma" can be likened to the pursuit of knowledge and experiences that enrich our lives, such as education, travel, or spiritual practices.
    • Just as the mantra invokes Indra for blessings, people often seek divine or spiritual support through prayer or meditation in times of need.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking divine guidance and strength in our lives.
    • It highlights the significance of spiritual nourishment and the pursuit of joy and fulfillment.
    • In a broader context, it serves as a reminder to connect with higher powers or principles, whether through spirituality, community, or personal growth, to navigate life's challenges effectively.
