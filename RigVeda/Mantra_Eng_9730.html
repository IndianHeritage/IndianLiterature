न सेशे यस्य रोमशं निषेदुषो विजृम्भते |सेद ईशे यस्य राम्बते 'न्तरā शक्त्या कप्रद विश्वस्माद इन्द्र उत्तरः ||
na seśe yasya romaśaṁ niṣeduṣo vijṛmbhate | sed īśe yasya rambate 'ntarā sakthyā3 kapṛd viśvasmād indra uttaraḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • na seśe: not remaining
    • yasya: whose
    • romaśaṁ: hair or fine particles
    • niṣeduṣo: in the place of rest
    • vijṛmbhate: expands or awakens
    • sed: but
    • īśe: in the lord
    • rambate: rejoices or flourishes
    • antarā: in between
    • sakthyā: with power
    • kapṛd: the one who is capable
    • viśvasmād: from all
    • indra: the supreme god or ruler
    • uttaraḥ: the higher or superior
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the divine presence and power of a higher entity, often interpreted as Indra, who is a symbol of strength and authority.
    • The phrase "na seśe yasya romaśaṁ niṣeduṣo vijṛmbhate" suggests that nothing remains stagnant in the presence of this divine power; everything is in a state of expansion and awakening.
    • The second part, "sed īśe yasya rambate 'ntarā sakthyā," indicates that in the realm of the divine, there is flourishing and joy, powered by the capabilities of the higher being.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, a leader who inspires and motivates their team can be seen as embodying the qualities of Indra. Their presence can lead to a dynamic and productive work environment where ideas flourish.
    • In personal life, a mentor or a guiding figure can awaken potential in individuals, leading them to grow and expand their horizons, much like the divine influence described in the mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of divine influence in our lives, highlighting how higher powers can lead to growth, joy, and expansion.
    • It serves as a reminder to seek inspiration from those who embody strength and wisdom, whether in leadership, mentorship, or personal relationships.
    • In practical terms, it encourages individuals to recognize and harness the power of positive influences in their lives, leading to personal and communal flourishing.
