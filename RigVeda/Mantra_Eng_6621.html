यद अदो दिवो अर्णव इषो वा मदथो गृहे | श्रुतं इन मे अमर्त्या ||
yad ado divo arṇava iṣo vā madatho gṛhe | śrutam in me amartyā ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yad: whatever
    • ado: from below
    • dīvo: of the heavens
    • arṇava: ocean or vastness
    • iṣo: of the ruler or lord
    • vā: or
    • madatho: from the intoxication or delight
    • gṛhe: in the home
    • śrutam: heard or understood
    • in: in
    • me: me
    • amartyā: immortal or divine
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks to the connection between the earthly and the divine.
    • It suggests that whatever comes from the vastness of the heavens or the depths of the ocean, whether it is a blessing or a challenge, is to be embraced within the home.
    • The phrase "from the intoxication or delight" indicates that joy and fulfillment can be found in the mundane aspects of life.
    • The mention of "immortal" signifies the eternal truths and values that guide human existence.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a family setting, this mantra can be applied to the acceptance of both joys and sorrows. For instance, during a celebration, the family can acknowledge the happiness that comes from their shared experiences, while also recognizing the challenges they face together.
    • In a workplace, this mantra can inspire a culture of resilience. Employees can be encouraged to embrace both successes and setbacks, understanding that each experience contributes to their growth and development.
    • In personal relationships, it can remind individuals to appreciate the depth of their connections, recognizing that both the highs and lows are essential to building a strong bond.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of embracing all aspects of life, both divine and earthly.
    • Its significance lies in the reminder that every experience, whether joyful or challenging, contributes to our understanding of existence.
    • In real-life scenarios, it encourages individuals to cultivate resilience, acceptance, and appreciation for the journey of life, fostering a deeper connection with themselves and others.
