रथिरासो हरयो ये ते अस्रिधा ओजो वातस्य पिप्रति |येभिर् नि दस्युम् मनुषो निघोषयो येभिः स्वः परीयसे ||
rathirāso harayo ye te asridha ojo vātasya piprati | yebhir ni dasyum manuṣo nighoṣayo yebhiḥ svaḥ parīyase ||
  • Mantra: "rathirāso harayo ye te asridha ojo vātasya piprati | yebhir ni dasyum manuṣo nighoṣayo yebhiḥ svaḥ parīyase ||"
  • Translation: This mantra speaks of the divine forces that drive the chariot of life, the energy that sustains us, and the protection against adversities.
  • Word Breakdown:
    • rathirāso: Refers to the chariot or the vehicle of life, symbolizing the journey we undertake.
    • harayo: Represents the divine or celestial beings that guide and protect us.
    • asridha: Means to be steadfast or firm, indicating resilience in the face of challenges.
    • ojo: Refers to energy or vitality, essential for sustaining life.
    • vātasya: Relates to the wind or breath, symbolizing the life force that moves us.
    • piprati: Means to nourish or sustain, highlighting the importance of support in our lives.
    • yebhir: Refers to those who, or by whom, indicating the collective strength of community.
    • ni dasyum: Means to overcome obstacles or adversities.
    • manuṣo: Refers to humanity, emphasizing our shared experiences.
    • nighoṣayo: Means to conquer or defeat, indicating triumph over challenges.
    • svaḥ: Refers to the heavens or the higher realms, symbolizing aspirations and goals.
    • parīyase: Means to surround or encompass, indicating the protective nature of divine forces.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In our daily lives, we often face challenges that can feel overwhelming, much like a chariot facing rough terrain. This mantra reminds us that we have divine support and energy to navigate these challenges.
    • Consider a student preparing for exams. The stress and pressure can be likened to obstacles. By invoking the essence of this mantra, the student can find the strength and resilience to overcome these hurdles.
    • In a community setting, when people come together to support one another during difficult times, they embody the collective strength mentioned in the mantra, helping each other to overcome adversities.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its reminder of the divine support and energy available to us in our life's journey. It teaches us about resilience, the importance of community, and the ability to overcome challenges. In real-life scenarios, whether in personal struggles, academic pressures, or community support, this mantra serves as a powerful affirmation of strength and protection, encouraging us to embrace our journey with confidence and faith in the divine forces that guide us.
