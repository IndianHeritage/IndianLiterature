हव्यवाळ अग्निर अजरः पिता नो विभुर विभावाऽस्मे |सुगार्हपत्या सम इषो दिदीहि अस्मद्र्यः सम मिमीहि श्रवांसि ||
havyavāḻ agnir ajaraḥ pitā no vibhur vibhāvā sudṛśīko asme | sugārhapatyāḥ sam iṣo didīhy asmadrya1k sam mimīhi śravāṁsi ||
  • havyavāḻ agnir ajaraḥ: This phrase refers to the "everlasting fire" or "immortal fire." In a real-life context, this can symbolize the eternal nature of knowledge and wisdom, which continues to illuminate our paths regardless of the challenges we face.
  • pitā no vibhur vibhāvā: This translates to "may the father (or divine) be generous to us." Here, "father" can be interpreted as a guiding force or a higher power that provides support and abundance. In everyday life, this can relate to the support we receive from our parents, mentors, or even the community that nurtures us.
  • sudṛśīko asme: This means "may we be endowed with good vision." This can be understood as a call for clarity and insight in our decisions and actions. In practical terms, it emphasizes the importance of having a clear perspective in life, akin to making informed choices in our careers or personal relationships.
  • sugārhapatyāḥ sam iṣo didīhy: This phrase can be interpreted as "may we strive for the best outcomes." It encourages us to aim for excellence in our endeavors. In real life, this can be seen in the pursuit of education, career goals, or personal development, where striving for the best leads to success and fulfillment.
  • asmadrya1k sam mimīhi śravāṁsi: This translates to "may our offerings be accepted." This signifies the importance of our contributions and efforts being recognized. In a practical sense, it can relate to the workplace, where our hard work and dedication are acknowledged and rewarded, fostering a sense of belonging and purpose.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a profound message about the eternal nature of wisdom, the importance of support from higher powers, the need for clarity in vision, the pursuit of excellence, and the recognition of our contributions. In real life, these themes are applicable in various scenarios, such as personal growth, professional development, and community engagement. By embodying these principles, individuals can lead more fulfilling lives, contribute positively to society, and foster a sense of interconnectedness with the world around them.
