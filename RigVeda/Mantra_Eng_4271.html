को वाम अद्य पुरूणाम आ वव्ने मार्त्यानाम् |को विप्रो विप्रवाहसः को यज्ञैर वाजिनिवासू ||
ko vām adya purūṇām ā vavne martyānām | ko vipro vipravāhasā ko yajñair vājinīvasū ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ko: Who
    • vām: You
    • adya: Today
    • purūṇām: Of the ancient ones
    • ā: To
    • vavne: Speak or declare
    • martyānām: Of mortals
    • vipro: The wise or learned
    • vipravāhasā: Who leads the wise
    • yajñair: By sacrifices
    • vājinīvasū: The wealth of the strong
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra poses a series of questions, seeking to understand who among the wise and learned can speak or declare the truths of the ancient ones today.
    • It reflects a quest for knowledge and wisdom, emphasizing the importance of guidance from those who are knowledgeable.
    • The mention of "martyānām" indicates a focus on human beings and their experiences, suggesting that the wisdom sought is relevant to our mortal lives.
    • The reference to "yajñair" highlights the significance of rituals and sacrifices in attaining knowledge and prosperity, indicating that spiritual practices are essential for personal growth.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern education, we often seek mentors or teachers who can guide us through complex subjects, much like the wise individuals referenced in the mantra.
    • In corporate settings, leaders who possess wisdom and experience are crucial for guiding teams towards success, paralleling the idea of "vipravāhasā."
    • Spiritual practices, such as meditation or community service, can be seen as modern-day "yajñair," where individuals engage in activities that promote personal and collective well-being.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking wisdom and guidance from knowledgeable individuals in our lives.
    • It serves as a reminder that the pursuit of knowledge is a continuous journey, relevant to both ancient traditions and modern contexts.
    • By engaging in spiritual practices and valuing the insights of the wise, we can navigate our mortal existence with greater understanding and purpose.
