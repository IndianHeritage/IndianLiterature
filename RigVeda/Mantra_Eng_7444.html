त्रिवन्धुरेण त्रिवृतā रथेन यातम अश्विनā |मध्वः सोमस्य पीतये ||
trivandhureṇa trivṛtā rathenā yātam aśvinā | madhvaḥ somasya pītaye ||
  • Mantra: "trivandhureṇa trivṛtā rathenā yātam aśvinā | madhvaḥ somasya pītaye ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • trivandhureṇa: With three bindings or connections
    • trivṛtā: Threefold or triple
    • rathenā: By the chariot
    • yātam: May it go or travel
    • aśvinā: O Ashvins (the twin deities associated with health and medicine)
    • madhvaḥ: Sweet or pleasant
    • somasya: Of the soma (a sacred drink in Vedic rituals)
    • pītaye: For drinking or consumption
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the Ashvins, who are known for their healing powers and are often depicted as divine physicians.
    • The phrase "trivandhureṇa trivṛtā" suggests a journey or movement that is supported by threefold connections, which can symbolize stability and strength in one's endeavors.
    • The mention of "rathenā" (by the chariot) indicates a vehicle or means of progress, suggesting that the journey is not just physical but also metaphorical, representing life's journey.
    • "Madhvaḥ somasya pītaye" refers to the sweetness of the soma, which is often associated with enlightenment and divine knowledge, indicating that the journey undertaken is towards a higher understanding or state of being.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional context, this mantra can be applied to teamwork where individuals are connected through shared goals (the three bindings) and work together (the chariot) to achieve success (the journey).
    • In personal development, it can symbolize the importance of having a strong foundation (the threefold connections) in one's life, such as mental, emotional, and physical well-being, to navigate challenges effectively.
    • In health and wellness, invoking the Ashvins can remind individuals to seek holistic healing and balance in their lives, much like the soma represents nourishment for the soul.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of connections and support systems in our journeys, whether they be personal, professional, or spiritual.
    • It highlights the significance of seeking knowledge and healing, encouraging individuals to pursue a balanced and fulfilling life.
    • In essence, it serves as a reminder that with the right support and understanding, one can navigate the complexities of life and reach a state of enlightenment and well-being.
