युयं देवाः प्रमतिर युयम् ओजो युयं द्वेषांसी सन्तर युयोत |अभिक्षत्तारो अभि च क्षमध्वम् अद्या च नो मृळयतापरं च ||
yūyaṁ devāḥ pramatir yūyam ojo yūyaṁ dveṣāṁsi sanutar yuyota | abhikṣattāro abhi ca kṣamadhvam adyā ca no mṛḻayatāparaṁ ca ||
  • yūyaṁ devāḥ: This phrase translates to "You, the gods." It addresses the divine beings, acknowledging their presence and power.
  • pramatir: This word means "wisdom" or "intellect." It signifies the importance of understanding and knowledge in our lives.
  • yūyam ojo: Here, "ojo" refers to "energy" or "vitality." This emphasizes the need for strength and vigor in our endeavors.
  • yūyaṁ dveṣāṁsi: This translates to "You, who dispel enmity." It highlights the role of the divine in overcoming hatred and conflict.
  • sanutar yuyota: This means "may you unite us." It is a plea for harmony and togetherness among people.
  • abhikṣattāro: This word can be interpreted as "those who are benevolent." It calls upon the gods to be kind and generous.
  • abhi ca kṣamadhvam: This translates to "and be forgiving." It emphasizes the importance of forgiveness in human relationships.
  • adyā ca no mṛḻayatāparaṁ ca: This means "today, do not let us be harmed." It is a request for protection and safety from any harm.

  • Significance: This mantra encapsulates a deep spiritual and ethical message. It calls upon the divine for wisdom, strength, unity, benevolence, and protection. Each element of the mantra serves as a reminder of the values that are essential for a harmonious life.
  • Real-life Application: In everyday life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios. For instance, in a workplace setting, invoking the spirit of unity and collaboration can lead to a more productive environment. In personal relationships, embracing forgiveness can help mend rifts and foster deeper connections. Additionally, in times of conflict, remembering the call to dispel enmity can guide individuals towards peaceful resolutions.

  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the interconnectedness of wisdom, strength, unity, and compassion. By embodying these principles, individuals can navigate life's challenges more effectively and contribute to a more harmonious society. Its relevance transcends time and culture, making it applicable in various aspects of life, from personal relationships to community interactions.
