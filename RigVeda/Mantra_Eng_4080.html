ते स्यन्द्रासो नोक्षणोऽति ष्कन्दन्ति शर्वरीः |मरुतामधाम महो दिवि क्षमा च मनमहः ||
te syandrāso nokṣaṇo 'ti ṣkandanti śarvarīḥ | marutām adhā maho divi kṣamā ca manmahe ||
  • te syandrāso: This phrase can be interpreted as "those who are powerful" or "those who are strong." It signifies individuals or forces that possess great strength and capability.
  • nokṣaṇo: This term means "not diminished" or "not reduced." It emphasizes the idea of resilience and the ability to withstand challenges without losing strength.
  • 'ti ṣkandanti: This can be translated as "they do not fall" or "they do not waver." It suggests a steadfastness in the face of adversity, highlighting the importance of perseverance.
  • śarvarīḥ: This word refers to "the darkness" or "the night." In a metaphorical sense, it can represent challenges, ignorance, or obstacles that one faces in life.
  • marutām: This term refers to "the winds" or "the storm." It symbolizes the forces of nature or the tumultuous events in life that can disrupt peace and stability.
  • adhā maho: This phrase means "in the great" or "in the vast." It indicates a connection to something larger than oneself, perhaps the universe or the collective human experience.
  • divi: This word translates to "in the sky" or "in the heavens." It can signify higher aspirations, spiritual goals, or the pursuit of enlightenment.
  • kṣamā: This term means "forgiveness" or "patience." It highlights the importance of these virtues in overcoming difficulties and maintaining harmony.
  • ca manmahe: This phrase translates to "and we meditate" or "and we reflect." It emphasizes the practice of contemplation and mindfulness in one's life.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of strength, resilience, and the importance of maintaining one's composure in the face of challenges. It teaches us that, like the powerful winds that can both create and destroy, we must navigate the storms of life with patience and forgiveness. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied when facing personal or professional challenges, reminding us to remain steadfast and reflective. By embodying these qualities, we can overcome obstacles and strive for higher aspirations, ultimately leading to personal growth and enlightenment.
