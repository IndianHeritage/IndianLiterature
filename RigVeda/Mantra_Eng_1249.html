व्याञ्जिभिर दिव आतास्व अद्यौद अप कृष्णां निर्णिजं देव्य आवः |प्रबोधयन्त्य अरुणैभिर अश्वैर ओषा याति सुयुजा रथेन ||
vy a1ñjibhir diva ātāsv adyaud apa kṛṣṇāṁ nirṇijaṁ devy āvaḥ | prabodhayanty aruṇebhir aśvair oṣā yāti suyujā rathena ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • vy a1ñjibhir: With the help of the celestial beings
    • diva: In the sky or heaven
    • ātāsv: May they come
    • adyaud: Today
    • apa: From here
    • kṛṣṇāṁ: The black or dark ones (often referring to the night or darkness)
    • nirṇijaṁ: Without any obstacles
    • devy: Divine or goddess-like
    • āvaḥ: May they be invoked or called
    • prabodhayanty: Awakening or enlightening
    • aruṇebhir: With the red or dawn-like ones
    • aśvair: With the white or bright ones
    • oṣā: The dawn or light
    • yāti: Goes or travels
    • suyujā: Well-yoked or well-connected
    • rathena: By the chariot
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes celestial beings to come down from the heavens today, removing obstacles and bringing divine energy.
    • The reference to "kṛṣṇāṁ" suggests a desire to overcome darkness or ignorance, while "devy āvaḥ" emphasizes the importance of divine guidance.
    • The mention of "aruṇebhir" and "aśvair" symbolizes the transition from darkness to light, representing knowledge and enlightenment.
    • The "suyujā rathena" indicates a well-prepared journey, suggesting that with divine assistance, one can navigate life's challenges effectively.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, this mantra can be applied when facing challenges or uncertainties. For instance, a student preparing for exams may recite this mantra to seek clarity and focus.
    • In a professional setting, an individual may invoke this mantra before a crucial meeting or presentation, hoping to eliminate obstacles and gain divine support for success.
    • During times of personal struggle, such as dealing with loss or emotional turmoil, this mantra serves as a reminder to seek enlightenment and guidance from higher powers.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine intervention and guidance in overcoming obstacles and achieving enlightenment.
    • It emphasizes the importance of seeking help from higher powers, whether in moments of darkness or during significant life events.
    • In essence, this mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the interconnectedness of the divine and human experience, encouraging individuals to embrace the journey towards knowledge and light.
