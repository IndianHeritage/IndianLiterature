समुद्रम आसाम अव तस्थे अग्रिमा न ऋश्यति सवनं यस्मिन्न आयता |अत्रा न हार्दि क्रवणस्य रेजते यत्रा मतिर्विद्यते पूतबन्धनी ||
samudram āsām ava tasthe agrimā na riṣyati savanaṁ yasminn āyatā | atrā na hārdi kravaṇasya rejate yatrā matir vidyate pūtabandhanī ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • samudram: ocean or sea
    • āsām: of the waters
    • ava: down or below
    • tasthe: stood or remained
    • agrimā: foremost or leading
    • na: not
    • riṣyati: is disturbed or shaken
    • savanaṁ: offering or sacrifice
    • yasminn: in which
    • āyatā: extended or spread out
    • atrā: here
    • na: not
    • hārdi: in the heart
    • kravaṇasya: of the sacrifice
    • rejate: shines or radiates
    • yatrā: where
    • matir: intellect or understanding
    • vidyate: exists or is present
    • pūtabandhanī: pure or sacred bond
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the ocean as a metaphor for the vastness of knowledge and wisdom.
    • The phrase "samudram āsām" suggests that the waters (knowledge) are abundant and deep.
    • "Agrimā na riṣyati" indicates that true wisdom remains undisturbed, even amidst chaos.
    • The reference to "savanaṁ" implies that offerings made with pure intentions lead to enlightenment.
    • "Atrā na hārdi kravaṇasya rejate" emphasizes that the essence of sacrifice shines in the heart, where true understanding resides.
    • "Yatrā matir vidyate pūtabandhanī" suggests that where there is pure intellect, there exists a sacred bond with the universe.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a chaotic work environment, maintaining a calm and focused mindset can lead to better decision-making, akin to the undisturbed ocean.
    • When engaging in community service, the intention behind the action (the offering) is what truly matters, reflecting the essence of the mantra.
    • In personal relationships, understanding and empathy create a "pure bond," allowing for deeper connections and harmony.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of wisdom, intention, and purity in our actions and thoughts.
    • It teaches us that true understanding and connection with the universe come from a place of calmness and clarity.
    • In real life, applying these principles can lead to personal growth, improved relationships, and a more profound sense of purpose.
