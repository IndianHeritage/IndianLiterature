ये स्तोत्रिभ्यो गोआग्राम अश्वपेśसम अग्ने रातिम उपसृजन्ति सूरयः |अस्माञ्च तांश्च प्र हि नेषि वस्य आ बृहद् वदेम विदथे सुवीराः ||
ye stotṛbhyo goagrām aśvapeśasam agne rātim upasṛjanti sūrayaḥ | asmāñ ca tām̐ś ca pra hi neṣi vasya ā bṛhad vadema vidathe suvīrāḥ ||
  • Mantra: "ye stotṛbhyo goagrām aśvapeśasam agne rātim upasṛjanti sūrayaḥ | asmāñ ca tām̐ś ca pra hi neṣi vasya ā bṛhad vadema vidathe suvīrāḥ ||"
  • Translation: "Those who praise the divine, who are like the herds of cattle, who bring forth the energy of the sun, may they grant us delight, O Agni. May we, along with them, be led to prosperity and may we speak great words in the assembly of the brave."

  • Word-by-Word Explanation:
    • ye: Those who
    • stotṛbhyo: To the praisers or worshippers
    • goagrām: Like herds of cattle, symbolizing abundance and prosperity
    • aśvapeśasam: Bringing forth the energy of the sun, representing vitality and strength
    • agne: O Agni, the fire god, symbolizing transformation and purification
    • rātim: Delight or joy
    • upasṛjanti: They bring forth or bestow
    • sūrayaḥ: The brave or the strong
    • asmāñ: Us
    • ca: And
    • tām̐ś: Those
    • ca: And
    • pra: Lead or guide
    • hi: Indeed
    • neṣi: To lead
    • vasya: To prosperity or success
    • ā: To
    • bṛhad: Great or vast
    • vadema: May we speak
    • vidathe: In the assembly or gathering
    • suvīrāḥ: Of the brave or the strong

  • Real-Life Examples:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of community and collaboration. For instance, in a workplace, when team members praise each other's efforts and work together, they create a positive environment that fosters productivity and success.
    • The reference to "herds of cattle" can be likened to the idea of collective resources. Just as cattle provide sustenance, a community that supports one another can lead to shared prosperity.
    • The invocation of Agni symbolizes the transformative power of fire. In real life, this can be seen in how challenges (like fire) can lead to personal growth and new opportunities when approached with courage and determination.

  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of unity, strength, and the pursuit of joy through collective efforts. It teaches us that by praising and supporting one another, we can achieve greater heights and navigate life's challenges more effectively.
    • In real-life scenarios, whether in personal relationships, workplaces, or communities, the principles of collaboration, mutual respect, and shared goals can lead to a more fulfilling and prosperous existence.
