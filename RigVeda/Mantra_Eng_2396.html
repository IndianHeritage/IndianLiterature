प्रातर्यावाण रथ्येव वीराजेव यमा वरम् आ सचेथे |मेने इव तन्वा शुम्भमाने दम्पतीव क्रतुविदा जनेषु ||
prātaryāvāṇā rathyeva vīrājeva yamā varam ā sacethe | mene iva tanvā3 śumbhamāne dampatīva kratuvidā janeṣu ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • prātaryāvāṇā: In the early morning or at dawn
    • rathyeva: Like a chariot
    • vīrājeva: Like a hero
    • yamā: The one who controls or restrains
    • varam: The best or the most excellent
    • ā sacethe: May he attain or achieve
    • mene: To think or to consider
    • iva: Like or as
    • tanvā: With a body or form
    • śumbhamāne: When being praised or honored
    • dampatīva: Like a couple or partners
    • kratuvidā: Knowledgeable or wise in rituals
    • janeṣu: Among people or in society
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the dawn, a time of new beginnings and opportunities.
    • It compares the qualities of a hero and the control one has over their life, suggesting that one should strive for excellence.
    • The phrase "may he attain" indicates a prayer for success and fulfillment in life.
    • It emphasizes the importance of being recognized and honored in society, akin to a couple who support each other.
    • The mention of "knowledgeable in rituals" highlights the value of wisdom and understanding in navigating life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, starting a new project at dawn can symbolize fresh ideas and the potential for success.
    • A hero in a community could be someone who takes initiative and leads others towards a common goal, embodying the qualities of bravery and control.
    • Couples who support each other in their endeavors reflect the partnership and mutual respect highlighted in the mantra.
    • Individuals who seek knowledge and understanding in their fields, much like those knowledgeable in rituals, can navigate challenges more effectively.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of new beginnings, striving for excellence, and the value of support and wisdom in our lives.
    • It encourages individuals to embrace their potential and work towards being recognized for their contributions in society.
    • In real life, applying the principles of this mantra can lead to personal growth, stronger relationships, and a more fulfilling existence.
