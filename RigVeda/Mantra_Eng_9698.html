मां विदन परिपन्थिनो य आसीदन्ति दम्पती |सुगेभिर दुर्गम अतीताम अप द्रान्त्व अरातयः ||
mā vidan paripanthino ya āsīdanti dampatī | sugebhir durgam atītām apa drāntv arātayaḥ ||
  • mā vidan paripanthino: This phrase translates to "Do not let the obstacles (or enemies) come near." It emphasizes the importance of protection from negative influences or adversaries in life.
  • ya āsīdanti dampatī: This refers to "those who are united (like a couple)." It highlights the strength found in unity and partnership, suggesting that together, individuals can overcome challenges.
  • sugebhir durgam atītām: This means "with good qualities, they have crossed the difficult path." It signifies that possessing virtuous qualities can help one navigate through life's challenges successfully.
  • apa drāntv arātayaḥ: This translates to "may they be removed from the path of the unworthy." It calls for the removal of negative influences or unworthy individuals from one's life, ensuring a clear path forward.

In essence, this mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the importance of unity, virtue, and protection from negativity. It encourages individuals to cultivate good qualities and to seek supportive relationships that can help them overcome obstacles.


Real-Life Application:

  • In personal relationships, this mantra can inspire couples to work together to face challenges, reinforcing the idea that unity is strength.
  • In professional settings, it can motivate teams to support one another, fostering a collaborative environment that helps everyone succeed.
  • On a personal level, individuals can reflect on their own qualities and strive to improve themselves, ensuring they are equipped to handle life's difficulties.

Conclusion:

This mantra is significant as it encapsulates the essence of resilience through unity and virtue. In a world filled with challenges, it serves as a guiding principle for individuals and communities to support one another, cultivate positive qualities, and remove negativity from their lives. By embodying these teachings, one can navigate through life's complexities with grace and strength.
