आ त्वा बृहन्तो हरयो युजानां अर्वाग इन्द्र सदहमादो वहन्तु |प्र ये द्विता दिव ऋञ्जन्त्य आताः सुसम्मृष्टासो वृषभस्य मूषाः ||
ā tvā bṛhanto harayo yujānā arvāg indra sadhamādo vahantu | pra ye dvitā diva ṛñjanty ātāḥ susammṛṣṭāso vṛṣabhasya mūrāḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā: from, away
    • tvā: you
    • bṛhanto: great, powerful
    • harayo: the shining ones, often referring to deities or divine beings
    • yujānā: united, connected
    • arvāg: from below, from the earth
    • indra: Indra, the king of gods, associated with rain and thunderstorms
    • sadhamādo: with good fortune, auspiciousness
    • vahantu: may they carry, may they bring
    • pra: forth, forward
    • ye: who
    • dvitā: the two, duality
    • diva: heaven, sky
    • ṛñjanty: they shine, they illuminate
    • ātāḥ: those who have come
    • susammṛṣṭāso: well-assembled, well-formed
    • vṛṣabhasya: of the bull, often symbolizing strength and power
    • mūrāḥ: forms, shapes
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the powerful deities, asking them to unite and bring forth auspiciousness.
    • It emphasizes the connection between the divine and earthly realms, suggesting that the blessings of the gods can manifest in our lives.
    • Indra, as the king of gods, represents strength and the ability to overcome challenges, which is a reminder of the importance of resilience in our lives.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In times of difficulty, such as during a natural disaster, communities often come together to support one another, reflecting the unity and strength called for in this mantra.
    • In personal challenges, individuals may seek guidance and support from mentors or spiritual leaders, embodying the connection between the divine and human efforts.
    • In professional settings, teamwork and collaboration can lead to successful outcomes, mirroring the idea of united efforts bringing forth good fortune.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of all beings and the importance of seeking divine guidance in our endeavors.
    • It highlights the significance of unity, strength, and auspiciousness in achieving our goals, both individually and collectively.
    • In real life, it encourages us to foster relationships, seek support, and remain resilient in the face of challenges, ultimately leading to a more harmonious existence.
