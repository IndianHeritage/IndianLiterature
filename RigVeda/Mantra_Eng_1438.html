स न ऊर्जाम उपाभृत्य आय कृत्पा न जूर्यति |यम् मातरिश्वा मनवे परावतो देवम् भाः परावतः ||
sa na ūrjām upābhṛty ayā kṛpā na jūryati | yam mātariśvā manave parāvato devam bhāḥ parāvataḥ ||
  • sa na ūrjām upābhṛty ayā kṛpā na jūryati
    • sa - This word translates to "that" or "he," referring to a divine entity or force.
    • na - Means "not," indicating negation.
    • ūrjām - Refers to "energy" or "vitality," symbolizing strength and power.
    • upābhṛty - This can be interpreted as "support" or "nurturing," suggesting a source of sustenance.
    • ayā - Translates to "by this" or "through this," indicating a means or method.
    • kṛpā - Means "compassion" or "grace," highlighting the importance of kindness.
    • na jūryati - Translates to "does not diminish," suggesting that the energy or grace remains intact.
  • yam mātariśvā manave parāvato devam bhāḥ parāvataḥ
    • yam - Means "whom," referring to a divine being or force.
    • mātariśvā - This term is often associated with the wind or the sky, symbolizing freedom and movement.
    • manave - Refers to "Manu," the progenitor of humanity, indicating a connection to human existence.
    • parāvato - Means "the highest" or "supreme," suggesting a divine elevation.
    • devam - Translates to "god" or "deity," indicating a higher power.
    • bhāḥ - Refers to "light" or "radiance," symbolizing knowledge and enlightenment.
    • parāvataḥ - Means "the highest" or "supreme," reinforcing the idea of divine elevation.

Conclusion:

This mantra emphasizes the importance of divine energy and compassion in our lives. It suggests that through the nurturing support of a higher power, our vitality and grace remain undiminished. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied to the way we seek strength and support from our communities, relationships, and spiritual beliefs. The invocation of divine energy can inspire individuals to act with compassion and kindness, fostering a sense of unity and strength among people. Ultimately, this mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of all beings and the importance of seeking higher wisdom and support in our daily lives.
