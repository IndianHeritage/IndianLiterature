प्रतिष्ठ यस्य वीरकर्मम इष्णद अनुस्थितं नु नार्यो अपौहत् |पुनस्तद आवृहति यत्कनायाऽदुहितुर आ अनुभृतम् अनर्वा ||
prathiṣṭa yasya vīrakarmam iṣṇad anuṣṭhitaṁ nu naryo apauhat | punas tad ā vṛhati yat kanāyā duhitur ā anubhṛtam anarvā ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • prathiṣṭa: establishment, foundation
    • yasya: of whom
    • vīrakarmam: heroic deeds, valorous actions
    • iṣṇad: performed, executed
    • anuṣṭhitaṁ: accomplished, fulfilled
    • nu: indeed, certainly
    • naryo: women
    • apauhat: not deprived, not harmed
    • punas: again, furthermore
    • tad: that
    • ā: from, out of
    • vṛhati: increases, grows
    • yat: which
    • kanāyā: of the daughter
    • duhitur: of the mother
    • ā: from
    • anubhṛtam: experienced, undergone
    • anarvā: without any hindrance, unobstructed
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the establishment of a foundation based on heroic deeds and actions that are performed with integrity.
    • It emphasizes the importance of women being unharmed and empowered, suggesting that their well-being is crucial for the growth and prosperity of society.
    • The reference to the daughter and mother indicates the significance of familial bonds and the nurturing role of women in society.
    • It suggests that when women are supported and not deprived, they contribute positively to the community, leading to growth and abundance.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern society, initiatives that empower women through education and economic opportunities can be seen as a reflection of this mantra. For instance, programs that provide scholarships for girls promote their growth and potential.
    • Organizations that focus on women's health and safety ensure that women are not deprived of their rights and can thrive in their communities.
    • In families, supporting daughters in their ambitions and respecting their choices can lead to a more harmonious and prosperous household.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for the recognition and empowerment of women as essential contributors to society.
    • It highlights the interconnectedness of individual actions and societal growth, suggesting that when women are supported, the entire community flourishes.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra can guide policies and personal actions that promote gender equality, ensuring a balanced and thriving society.
