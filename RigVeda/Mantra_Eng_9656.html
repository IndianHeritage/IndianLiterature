त्वं हि मन्यो अभिभूत्योजाः स्वयम्भूर् भामो अभिमातिषाहः |विश्वचर्षणिः सहुरिः सहावान् अस्मास्व् ओजः पृतनासु धेहि ||
tvaṁ hi manyo abhibhūtyojāḥ svayambhūr bhāmo abhimātiṣāhaḥ | viśvacarṣaṇiḥ sahuriḥ sahāvān asmāsv ojaḥ pṛtanāsu dhehi ||
  • tvaṁ hi manyo abhibhūtyojāḥ: This phrase can be translated as "You are indeed the one who is worthy of reverence and who possesses great power." Here, "tvaṁ" refers to the divine or the ultimate reality, while "manyo" signifies respect and reverence. In real life, this can be likened to how we regard leaders or figures of great wisdom and strength, such as a mentor or a teacher who guides us through challenges.
  • svayambhūr bhāmo abhimātiṣāhaḥ: This part means "the self-existent, the glorious, and the one who possesses supreme strength." "Svayambhūr" indicates self-existence, suggesting that this divine presence is not dependent on anything else. In our lives, we often encounter individuals who embody self-sufficiency and confidence, inspiring us to cultivate similar qualities.
  • viśvacarṣaṇiḥ sahuriḥ sahāvān: This translates to "the one who moves through the universe, who is powerful and capable." "Viśvacarṣaṇiḥ" implies a universal presence, while "sahuriḥ" and "sahāvān" denote strength and capability. This can be related to the idea of resilience in the face of adversity, where individuals or communities come together to overcome challenges, much like how societies unite during crises.
  • asmāsv ojaḥ pṛtanāsu dhehi: This phrase means "bestow upon us strength and vigor in our endeavors." "Ojaḥ" refers to energy or strength, and "pṛtanāsu" relates to efforts or actions. In practical terms, this can be seen in how we seek motivation and support from others when pursuing our goals, whether in personal development, education, or professional endeavors.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of recognizing and revering the divine or powerful forces in our lives, whether they be spiritual, personal, or communal. It encourages us to seek strength and guidance from these forces as we navigate our journeys. In real-life scenarios, this can manifest in our relationships with mentors, leaders, and the collective strength of communities. By acknowledging these influences, we can cultivate resilience, confidence, and a sense of purpose in our actions.
