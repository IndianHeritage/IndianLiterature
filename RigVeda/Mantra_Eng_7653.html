माता रुद्राणां दुहितā वसूनां स्वसादित्यनाम अमृतस्य नाभिः |प्र नु वोचं चिकीषे जनाय मा गाम अनागाम अदितिं वधिष्ठ ||
mātā rudrāṇāṁ duhitā vasūnāṁ svasādityānām amṛtasya nābhiḥ | pra nu vocaṁ cikituṣe janāya mā gām anāgām aditiṁ vadhiṣṭa ||
  • mātā rudrāṇāṁ: This phrase translates to "Mother of the Rudras." In Hindu mythology, Rudras are often associated with storm and wind, representing the fierce aspects of nature. The term "Mother" signifies nurturing and protective qualities.
  • duhitā vasūnāṁ: This means "Daughter of the Vasus." The Vasus are a group of deities representing various natural elements, such as earth, water, and fire. The term "Daughter" indicates a connection to the fundamental aspects of existence.
  • svasādityānām: This translates to "of the Adityas." The Adityas are solar deities, symbolizing light, truth, and cosmic order. This phrase emphasizes the importance of light and truth in life.
  • amṛtasya nābhiḥ: This means "the navel of immortality." In this context, it refers to the source of eternal life and sustenance. The navel is often seen as a point of connection and nourishment.
  • pra nu vocaṁ: This translates to "let us speak forth." It signifies the act of communication and expression, highlighting the importance of sharing knowledge and wisdom.
  • cikituṣe janāya: This means "for the benefit of the people." It emphasizes the mantra's purpose of serving humanity and promoting well-being.
  • mā gām anāgām: This translates to "may I not go to the unworthy." It reflects a desire to avoid negative influences and seek a righteous path in life.
  • aditiṁ vadhiṣṭa: This means "may I attain Aditi." Aditi is the mother of the gods and represents boundlessness and infinity. This phrase expresses a longing for spiritual elevation and connection to the divine.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a deep reverence for the divine feminine and the interconnectedness of nature, truth, and immortality. It serves as a reminder to seek knowledge, communicate for the benefit of others, and strive for a righteous path while aspiring for spiritual growth. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire individuals to embrace their nurturing qualities, promote harmony in their communities, and pursue a life aligned with truth and virtue. It encourages us to reflect on our actions and their impact on the world around us, fostering a sense of responsibility and connection to the greater cosmos.
