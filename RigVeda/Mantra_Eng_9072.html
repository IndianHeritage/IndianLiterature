सां ते जीवातुर उता तस्य विद्धि मा स्मैतादृग अप गूहः समर्ये |आविः स्वः कृतुते गूहते बसं स पादुर अस्य निर्णिजो न मुच्यते ||
sā te jīvātur uta tasya viddhi mā smaitādṛg apa gūhaḥ samarye | āviḥ svaḥ kṛṇute gūhate busaṁ sa pādur asya nirṇijo na mucyate ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sā: that
    • te: to you
    • jīvātur: may you live
    • uta: or
    • tasya: of that
    • viddhi: know
    • mā: not
    • smaitādṛg: such a vision
    • apa: away
    • gūhaḥ: hidden
    • samarye: in the battle
    • āviḥ: the one who is present
    • svaḥ: heaven
    • kṛṇute: creates
    • gūhate: hidden
    • busaṁ: the one who is strong
    • sa: he
    • pādur: the one who is steadfast
    • asya: of this
    • nirṇijo: unshaken
    • na: not
    • mucyate: is released
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks to the essence of life and the strength required to navigate challenges.
    • The phrase "may you live" emphasizes the importance of vitality and existence.
    • It suggests that one should seek knowledge and understanding, as indicated by "know of that."
    • The mention of "hidden" and "in the battle" implies that life is full of challenges that may not be immediately visible.
    • The mantra encourages resilience, as indicated by "the one who is steadfast" and "unshaken."
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, this mantra can be applied to individuals facing tough competition or challenges. For instance, a startup founder navigating the complexities of launching a new product can draw strength from this mantra, reminding them to remain steadfast and seek knowledge.
    • In personal life, someone dealing with health issues may find solace in the idea of resilience and the importance of living fully despite challenges.
    • Students facing academic pressures can relate to the mantra by understanding that knowledge and perseverance are key to overcoming obstacles.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of resilience, knowledge, and steadfastness in the face of life's challenges.
    • Its significance lies in its ability to inspire individuals to confront their struggles with courage and determination.
    • In various aspects of life—be it personal, professional, or academic—this mantra encourages a proactive approach to overcoming difficulties and achieving one's goals.
