एको द्वे वसुमती समीचि इन्द्र आ पप्रौ पृथिवीं उता द्याम् |उतान्तरिक्षाद अभि नः समीका इषो रथीः सयुजः शूर वाजान् ||
eko dve vasumatī samīcī indra ā paprau pṛthivīm uta dyām | utāntarikṣād abhi naḥ samīka iṣo rathīḥ sayujaḥ śūra vājān ||
  • eko dve vasumatī samīcī: This phrase can be translated as "one and two, the earth is well-distributed." It suggests a unity in diversity, where the earth (vasumatī) is shared among all beings. In real life, this can be seen in communities where resources are shared equitably, promoting harmony and cooperation.
  • indra ā paprau pṛthivīm uta dyām: Here, Indra, the king of gods, is invoked to bestow blessings upon the earth (pṛthivīm) and the sky (dyām). This reflects the human desire for divine intervention in ensuring prosperity and balance in nature. For example, farmers often pray for rain, symbolizing their reliance on divine forces for a good harvest.
  • utāntarikṣād abhi naḥ samīka: This line speaks to the connection between the earth and the sky (antarikṣa). It emphasizes the importance of both realms in sustaining life. In modern contexts, this can relate to environmental awareness, where the health of the earth is directly linked to the atmosphere, highlighting the need for sustainable practices.
  • iṣo rathīḥ sayujaḥ śūra vājān: This part refers to the chariots (rathīḥ) of strength (śūra) and speed (vājān). It symbolizes the power and swiftness needed to achieve goals. In everyday life, this can be likened to the pursuit of ambitions, where individuals must harness their strengths and resources to succeed.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of unity, divine blessings, and the interconnectedness of nature. Its significance lies in the reminder that we are all part of a larger ecosystem, and our actions impact the world around us. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire individuals and communities to work together for common goals, respect nature, and seek harmony in diversity. It encourages a holistic approach to life, where spiritual, environmental, and social aspects are intertwined, promoting a balanced and prosperous existence.
