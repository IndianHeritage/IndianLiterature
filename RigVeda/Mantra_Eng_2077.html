द्र्वन्नः सर्पिरासुतिः प्रात्नो होताऽवरेण्यः |सहसस् पुत्रो अद्भुतः ||
drvannaḥ sarpirāsutiḥ pratno hotā vareṇyaḥ | sahasas putro adbhutaḥ ||
  • drvannaḥ: This word can be interpreted as "the one who is strong" or "the one who is powerful." In real life, this can refer to individuals or entities that exhibit strength, resilience, or authority, such as leaders or influential figures in society.
  • sarpirāsutiḥ: This term translates to "the essence of ghee" or "the quality of nourishment." Ghee is often associated with purity and health in Indian culture. In a modern context, this can symbolize the importance of nutrition and wellness in our lives, emphasizing the need for a balanced diet.
  • pratno: This word means "the one who is invoked" or "the one who is called upon." It signifies the act of seeking guidance or blessings from a higher power. In everyday life, this can be seen in practices such as prayer or meditation, where individuals seek support in challenging times.
  • hotā: This term refers to "the priest" or "the one who offers sacrifices." In contemporary terms, it can represent those who facilitate connections, such as community leaders or mentors who guide others through difficult situations.
  • vareṇyaḥ: This word means "the one who is worthy of praise" or "the one who is revered." It highlights the importance of recognizing and honoring those who contribute positively to society, such as teachers, healthcare workers, and activists.
  • sahasas: This translates to "with strength" or "with vigor." It emphasizes the idea of perseverance and determination in achieving goals. In real life, this can be seen in athletes or individuals who overcome obstacles through hard work and dedication.
  • putro: This word means "son" or "child." It signifies the importance of family and the next generation. In modern society, this can relate to the responsibilities of parents in nurturing and educating their children.
  • adbhutaḥ: This term translates to "wonderful" or "extraordinary." It reflects the beauty and marvels of life, encouraging individuals to appreciate the extraordinary aspects of their existence, such as nature, art, and human connections.

Conclusion: The mantra "drvannaḥ sarpirāsutiḥ pratno hotā vareṇyaḥ | sahasas putro adbhutaḥ ||" encapsulates themes of strength, nourishment, reverence, and the extraordinary nature of life. Its significance lies in the reminder to seek strength and guidance, appreciate the nourishing aspects of life, and honor those who contribute positively to our communities. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire individuals to cultivate resilience, prioritize health and wellness, and foster meaningful relationships, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling and harmonious existence.
