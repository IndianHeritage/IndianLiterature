श्रिये जातः श्रिया आ निर इयाय श्रियं वयो जरतृभ्यो दधाति |श्रियं वसाना अमृतत्वं आयन भवन्ति सत्याः समिता मितद्रौ ||
śriye jātaḥ śriya ā nir iyāya śriyaṁ vayo jaritṛbhyo dadhāti | śriyaṁ vasānā amṛtatvam āyan bhavanti satyā samithā mitadrau ||
  • śriye jātaḥ: This phrase can be translated as "born of prosperity" or "originating from wealth." It signifies the importance of prosperity in life.
  • śriya ā nir iyāya: This means "may prosperity not depart." It emphasizes the desire to retain wealth and abundance in one's life.
  • śriyaṁ vayo jaritṛbhyo dadhāti: This translates to "prosperity grants longevity to those who are wise." It suggests that true wealth is not just material but also includes wisdom and longevity.
  • śriyaṁ vasānā: This means "those who dwell in prosperity." It indicates that living in a state of abundance is essential for a fulfilling life.
  • amṛtatvam āyan: This translates to "attaining immortality." It signifies the ultimate goal of life, which is to achieve a state beyond physical existence.
  • bhavanti satyā samithā mitadrau: This means "they become true and united." It highlights the importance of truth and unity in achieving prosperity and immortality.

In conclusion, this mantra from the Rigveda emphasizes the significance of prosperity, wisdom, and unity in life. It teaches that true wealth encompasses not just material possessions but also the wisdom to use them wisely and the unity with others to create a harmonious existence. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various ways, such as:

  • In personal finance, it encourages individuals to seek not just wealth but also the knowledge to manage it effectively.
  • In community building, it highlights the importance of working together towards common goals, fostering unity and shared prosperity.
  • In health and wellness, it reminds us that true prosperity includes physical and mental well-being, leading to a longer, more fulfilling life.

Overall, the mantra serves as a guiding principle for living a balanced and prosperous life, where material wealth is harmonized with wisdom, truth, and community.
