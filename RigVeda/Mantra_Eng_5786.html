प्रत्यु अदर्श्य आयत्य उ1च्छन्ती दुहितā दिवः |अपो महि व्ययति चक्षसे तमो ज्योतिष कृत्नोति सूनरी ||
praty u adarśy āyaty u1cchantī duhitā divaḥ | apo mahi vyayati cakṣase tamo jyotiṣ kṛṇoti sūnarī ||
  • Mantra: "praty u adarśy āyaty u1cchantī duhitā divaḥ | apo mahi vyayati cakṣase tamo jyotiṣ kṛṇoti sūnarī ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • praty: towards, in the direction of
    • u: indeed, certainly
    • adarśy: unseen, not visible
    • āyaty: extending, reaching out
    • u1cchantī: she who is rising, elevating
    • duhitā: daughter
    • divaḥ: of the heavens, celestial
    • apo: water
    • mahi: great, vast
    • vyayati: flows, moves
    • cakṣase: for the eye, for vision
    • tamo: darkness
    • jyotiṣ: light, brightness
    • kṛṇoti: creates, brings forth
    • sūnarī: the beautiful one, the auspicious
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of a divine daughter who is reaching out towards the unseen, symbolizing aspirations and goals that may not yet be visible but are within reach.
    • The mention of "water" signifies the flow of life and knowledge, suggesting that wisdom and clarity can emerge from the depths of the unknown.
    • The contrast between "darkness" and "light" highlights the journey from ignorance to knowledge, emphasizing the importance of enlightenment in our lives.
    • In real life, this can be related to personal growth, where individuals strive to achieve their dreams despite challenges and uncertainties.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of seeking knowledge and enlightenment, encouraging individuals to pursue their aspirations even when the path is not clear.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, such as education, career development, and personal relationships, where one must navigate through uncertainties to achieve success.
    • Ultimately, it serves as a reminder that through perseverance and the pursuit of wisdom, one can transform darkness into light and realize their true potential.
