मिमातु द्यौर अदितिर्वीतये नः सं दानुचित्रा उषसो यतन्ताम् |आचुच्यवुर दिव्यं कोशं एता ऋषे रुद्रस्य मरुतो गृणानाः ||
mimātu dyaur aditir vītaye naḥ saṁ dānucitrā uṣaso yatantām | ācucyavur divyaṁ kośam eta ṛṣe rudrasya maruto gṛṇānāḥ ||
  • mimātu dyaur: This phrase can be interpreted as "may the sky (or heaven) support us." In real life, this can be seen as a call for divine protection and guidance, similar to how people seek support from higher powers during challenging times.
  • aditir vītaye naḥ: "Aditi" refers to the mother of the gods and symbolizes boundlessness and freedom. "Vītaye" means to liberate or free us. This part of the mantra expresses a desire for liberation from limitations, akin to how individuals strive for personal growth and freedom from societal constraints.
  • saṁ dānucitrā uṣaso yatantām: "Saṁ" means together, "dānucitrā" refers to the beautiful dawn, and "uṣaso" means dawn. This line emphasizes the collective effort to welcome the dawn, representing new beginnings and opportunities. In everyday life, this can be related to teamwork and collaboration in achieving common goals.
  • ācucyavur divyaṁ kośam eta: "Ācucyavur" can be understood as "may we attain," and "divyaṁ kośam" refers to the divine treasure or wealth. This suggests a pursuit of spiritual or material abundance. In practical terms, it can relate to the quest for knowledge, wisdom, and prosperity in life.
  • ṛṣe rudrasya maruto gṛṇānāḥ: "ṛṣe" means sage, "rudrasya" refers to Rudra (a form of Shiva), and "maruto" signifies the storm gods. This line invokes the blessings of these divine entities, highlighting the importance of seeking guidance from wise and powerful forces. In real life, this can be seen in how people turn to mentors or spiritual leaders for advice and support.

Conclusion: This mantra encapsulates a profound desire for divine support, liberation, and the pursuit of abundance. It emphasizes the importance of collective effort and the guidance of wise forces in our lives. In a real-life context, it serves as a reminder to seek support from higher powers, work together towards common goals, and strive for personal and spiritual growth. The significance of this mantra lies in its ability to inspire individuals to embrace new beginnings, seek wisdom, and foster a sense of community in their endeavors.
