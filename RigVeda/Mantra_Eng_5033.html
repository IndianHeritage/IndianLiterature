आ वां वयो 'श्वासो वहिष्ठा अभि प्रयो नासत्याः वहन्तु |प्र वां रथो मनोजवाः असर्जिषः पृक्ष इषिधो अनु पूर्वीः ||
ā vāṁ vayo 'śvāso vahiṣṭhā abhi prayo nāsatyā vahantu | pra vāṁ ratho manojavā asarjīṣaḥ pṛkṣa iṣidho anu pūrvīḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā vāṁ: May we (us)
    • vayo: of age (youth)
    • 'śvāso: breath (life)
    • vahiṣṭhā: the best (most excellent)
    • abhi: towards (to)
    • prayo: the goal (aim)
    • nāsatyā: not false (truthful)
    • vahantu: may they carry (bring)
  • pra vāṁ: May we (us)
  • ratho: chariot (vehicle)
  • manojavā: swift (quick)
  • asarjīṣaḥ: not obstructed (unhindered)
  • pṛkṣa: to see (to observe)
  • iṣidho: those who are skilled (capable)
  • anu: after (following)
  • pūrvīḥ: the ancients (previous ones)

  • Overall Meaning: This mantra expresses a prayer for the best of life and truth to guide us. It seeks the blessings of swift and unhindered progress, akin to a chariot that moves forward without obstacles. The invocation of the ancients suggests a connection to wisdom and knowledge that has been passed down through generations.

  • Real-Life Examples:
    • Personal Growth: In our lives, we often seek guidance and support to achieve our goals. This mantra can be a reminder to pursue truth and excellence in our endeavors, whether in education, career, or personal relationships.
    • Teamwork: In a team setting, the mantra emphasizes the importance of collaboration and swift action. Just as a chariot requires a team of horses to move efficiently, successful projects often depend on the collective effort of individuals working towards a common goal.
    • Spiritual Journey: The invocation of the ancients can inspire individuals on their spiritual paths, encouraging them to seek wisdom from past teachings and apply those lessons in their lives.

  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for truth, excellence, and swift progress. It serves as a guiding principle in various aspects of life, encouraging individuals to strive for their goals while remaining grounded in truth and wisdom. By embodying these values, one can navigate life's challenges more effectively and achieve a fulfilling existence.
