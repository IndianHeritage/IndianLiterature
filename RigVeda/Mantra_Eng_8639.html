मन्त्र: परि ते जिज्युषो यथा धाराऽसुतस्य धावति |रंहमाणां व्य अव्ययं वारं वाजीव सानसिः ||
pari te jigyuṣo yathā dhārā sutasya dhāvati | raṁhamāṇā vy a1vyayaṁ vāraṁ vājīva sānasiḥ ||
  • Mantra: "pari te jigyuṣo yathā dhārā sutasya dhāvati | raṁhamāṇā vy a1vyayaṁ vāraṁ vājīva sānasiḥ ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • pari: around, in all directions
    • te: your
    • jigyuṣo: of the one who is striving or seeking
    • yathā: as, like
    • dhārā: flow, stream
    • sutasya: of the son, offspring
    • dhāvati: runs, flows
    • raṁhamāṇā: being joyful, full of delight
    • vy a1vyayaṁ: imperishable, eternal
    • vāraṁ: protection, refuge
    • vājīva: one who is strong, powerful
    • sānasiḥ: in the assembly, gathering
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the flow of energy and strength that surrounds those who are striving for their goals.
    • It draws a parallel between the natural flow of water and the pursuit of one's aspirations, suggesting that just as water flows effortlessly, so too should our efforts be directed towards our objectives.
    • The mention of joy and delight indicates that the journey towards achieving one's goals should be fulfilling and enriching.
    • The idea of imperishability suggests that true strength and protection come from within and are not easily lost.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • Consider a student preparing for an important exam. The mantra encourages them to focus on their studies with joy and determination, much like a river flowing towards the sea.
    • A professional striving for success in their career can relate to the idea of surrounding themselves with positive energy and support, akin to the protective and empowering aspects mentioned in the mantra.
    • In a community setting, individuals working together towards a common goal can embody the essence of this mantra, creating a joyful and supportive environment that fosters growth and achievement.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of striving towards one's goals with joy and determination, while also highlighting the significance of community and support.
    • Its teachings are applicable in various aspects of life, including education, career, and personal development, reminding us that the journey is as important as the destination.
    • By embodying the principles of this mantra, individuals can cultivate a sense of purpose and resilience, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling life.
