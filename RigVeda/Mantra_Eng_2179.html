अधā यो विश्वा भुवनाभि मजनेशानकृत प्रवयā अभ्य अवर्धत |आद रोदसी ज्योतिषा वह्निर आतनोत सिव्यन तमांसी दुधिता सम अव्ययत ||
adhā yo viśvā bhuvanābhi majmaneśānakṛt pravayā abhy avardhata | ād rodasī jyotiṣā vahnir ātanot sīvyan tamāṁsi dudhitā sam avyayat ||
  • adhā yo viśvā bhuvanābhi majmaneśānakṛt pravayā abhy avardhata
    • adhā - downwards or below
    • yo - who
    • viśvā - all or universal
    • bhuvanābhi - by the worlds
    • majmaneśānakṛt - the one who is the master of the universe
    • pravayā - the path or direction
    • abhy avardhata - has been elevated or uplifted
  • ād rodasī jyotiṣā vahnir ātanot sīvyan tamāṁsi dudhitā sam avyayat
    • ād - from
    • rodasī - the two worlds or realms
    • jyotiṣā - by the light
    • vahnir - of fire
    • ātanot - has spread or extended
    • sīvyan - auspicious or beneficial
    • tamāṁsi - darkness
    • dudhitā - dispelled or removed
    • sam avyayat - completely or entirely

This mantra speaks to the universal principles of elevation and enlightenment. It describes a process where the master of the universe uplifts all beings and dispels darkness through the light of fire. The imagery of light overcoming darkness is a powerful metaphor for knowledge and wisdom triumphing over ignorance and confusion.


In real life, this can be seen in various contexts:

  • Education: Just as the mantra suggests the elevation of beings, education serves to uplift individuals and societies, providing them with knowledge that dispels ignorance.
  • Personal Growth: Individuals often go through phases of darkness in their lives, whether it be through mental health struggles or personal challenges. The journey towards self-improvement and enlightenment mirrors the uplifting process described in the mantra.
  • Community Support: Communities that come together to support one another in times of need exemplify the idea of collective upliftment, where the strength of the group helps dispel the darkness of despair.

Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its universal message of hope, enlightenment, and the power of collective upliftment. It encourages individuals and communities to seek knowledge, support one another, and strive for a brighter future, thereby applying its teachings in everyday life.
