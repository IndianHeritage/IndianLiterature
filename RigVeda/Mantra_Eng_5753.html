महें नो अद्य सुविताया बोध्य उषो महे सौभागाया प्र यन्धि |चित्रं रयिं यशसं धेहि अस्मे देवी मार्तेषु मानविषु श्रवस्युम् ||
mahe no adya suvitāya bodhy uṣo mahe saubhagāya pra yandhi | citraṁ rayiṁ yaśasaṁ dhehy asme devi marteṣu mānuṣi śravasyum ||
  • mahe no adya suvitāya: This phrase can be translated as "May we today attain great wisdom." The term "mahe" signifies greatness, while "suvitāya" refers to good or auspicious knowledge. In real life, this can be related to the pursuit of education and personal growth, where individuals strive to gain knowledge that benefits themselves and society.
  • bodhy uṣo mahe saubhagāya: This translates to "May the dawn of wisdom bring us great fortune." Here, "bodhy" refers to enlightenment or awakening, and "uṣo" means dawn. This can be likened to the beginning of a new day, symbolizing new opportunities. For instance, starting a new job or embarking on a new project can be seen as a dawn of wisdom and fortune.
  • pra yandhi: This means "lead us forth." It is a call for guidance and direction. In everyday life, this can be interpreted as seeking mentorship or guidance from experienced individuals in various fields, such as career development or personal relationships.
  • citraṁ rayiṁ yaśasaṁ dhehy asme: This translates to "May we be endowed with diverse wealth and fame." "Citraṁ" signifies diversity, "rayiṁ" refers to wealth, and "yaśasaṁ" means fame or glory. This can be related to the idea of achieving success in various aspects of life, such as financial stability, recognition in one's profession, or contributions to the community.
  • devi marteṣu mānuṣi śravasyum: This means "O Goddess, grant us renown among mortals." The term "devi" refers to the divine or goddess, while "mānuṣi" means human beings. This can be seen as a prayer for legacy and respect in society. For example, individuals who contribute positively to their communities, such as philanthropists or activists, often seek to be remembered for their good deeds.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking wisdom, guidance, and prosperity in life. It highlights the aspiration for enlightenment and the pursuit of diverse forms of wealth and recognition. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire individuals to strive for personal and professional growth, seek mentorship, and contribute positively to society, ultimately leading to a fulfilling and respected life. The significance of this mantra lies in its universal appeal to human aspirations for knowledge, success, and legacy.
