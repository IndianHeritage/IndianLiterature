अद्रोघम् आवहोषतो यविष्ट्य देवाम् अजस्र वीतये |अभि प्रयांसी सुदिताः वसो गहि मन्दस्व धीतिभिर हितः ||
adrogham ā vahośato yaviṣṭhya devām̐ ajasra vītaye | abhi prayāṁsi sudhitā vaso gahi mandasva dhītibhir hitaḥ ||
  • adrogham: This word translates to "without deceit" or "free from harm." It emphasizes the importance of honesty and integrity in our actions.
  • ā vahośato: This phrase means "may we carry forth" or "may we bring forth." It suggests a proactive approach to life, encouraging individuals to take initiative.
  • yaviṣṭhya: This term can be interpreted as "the best" or "the most excellent." It highlights the pursuit of excellence in all endeavors.
  • devām̐: This word refers to "the divine" or "gods." It signifies the connection between human actions and divine blessings.
  • ajasra: Meaning "everlasting" or "constant," this word underscores the importance of consistency in our efforts and values.
  • vītaye: This translates to "for the purpose of prosperity" or "for the sake of wealth." It indicates that our actions should aim for growth and abundance.
  • abhi: This word means "towards" or "in the direction of," suggesting a focus on goals and aspirations.
  • prayāṁsi: This term refers to "efforts" or "endeavors," emphasizing the need for action in achieving our desires.
  • sudhitā: Meaning "well thought out" or "wise," this word highlights the importance of wisdom in decision-making.
  • vaso: This translates to "wealth" or "prosperity," reinforcing the idea that our efforts should lead to abundance.
  • gahi: This word means "to grasp" or "to hold," indicating the need to take control of our circumstances.
  • mandasva: This term can be interpreted as "gentle" or "calm," suggesting that a peaceful approach is beneficial.
  • dhītibhir: Meaning "with wisdom" or "with intelligence," this word emphasizes the role of intellect in our actions.
  • hitaḥ: This translates to "beneficial" or "good," indicating that our actions should ultimately lead to positive outcomes.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a holistic approach to life, emphasizing the importance of integrity, excellence, and wisdom in our actions. It encourages individuals to strive for prosperity while maintaining a connection to the divine. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as in personal development, business ethics, and community service. By embodying these principles, individuals can create a positive impact in their lives and the lives of others, fostering a harmonious and prosperous society.
