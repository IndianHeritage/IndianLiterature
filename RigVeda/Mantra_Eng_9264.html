श्रीणाम उदारो धरुणो रयीणाम मनीषाणाम प्रार्पणः सोमगोपाः |वसुः सूनुः सहसो अप्सु राजा विभात्य अग्र उषसाम इधानः ||
śrīṇām udāro dharuṇo rayīṇām manīṣāṇām prārpaṇaḥ somagopāḥ | vasuḥ sūnuḥ sahaso apsu rājā vi bhāty agra uṣasām idhānaḥ ||
  • śrīṇām udāro dharuṇo: This phrase can be translated as "the noble one among the prosperous." It signifies the importance of nobility and virtue in achieving prosperity. In real life, this can be seen in leaders who prioritize ethical practices and social responsibility, leading to sustainable success.
  • rayīṇām manīṣāṇām prārpaṇaḥ: This translates to "the offering of the wise among the prosperous." It emphasizes the value of wisdom in the distribution of wealth. For example, philanthropic efforts by successful individuals or organizations that aim to uplift society reflect this principle.
  • somagopāḥ: This term refers to "the protectors of the soma," which symbolizes nourishment and vitality. In contemporary terms, this can relate to those who safeguard natural resources and promote health and well-being in communities, such as environmental activists or healthcare professionals.
  • vasuḥ sūnuḥ sahaso apsu: This phrase means "the son of wealth, strong in the waters." It suggests a connection between wealth and strength, particularly in nurturing environments. In real life, this can be illustrated by businesses that thrive in resource-rich areas or families that support each other through challenges, drawing strength from their shared resources.
  • rājā vi bhāty agra uṣasām idhānaḥ: This translates to "the king shines foremost among the dawns." It signifies leadership and the emergence of new beginnings. In practical terms, this can be seen in visionary leaders who inspire change and innovation, leading their communities or organizations into a brighter future.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of noble leadership, wisdom in wealth distribution, and the importance of nurturing environments. Its significance lies in the reminder that true prosperity is not just about material wealth but also about ethical practices, community support, and visionary leadership. In real-life scenarios, these principles can be applied in various fields such as business, governance, and social activism, encouraging individuals and organizations to strive for a balanced and responsible approach to success.
