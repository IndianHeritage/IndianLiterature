आ नो अद्य समनसो गन्ता विश्वे सजोषसः |ऋचा गिरा मरुतो देव्य अदिते सदने पस्त्ये महि ||
ā no adya samanaso gantā viśve sajoṣasaḥ | ṛcā girā maruto devy adite sadane pastye mahi ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā: towards
    • no: us
    • adya: today
    • samanaso: united, harmonious
    • gantā: may go, may proceed
    • viśve: all, everyone
    • sajoṣasaḥ: with joy, with enthusiasm
    • ṛcā: with hymns
    • girā: with speech, with words
    • maruto: O Maruts (the storm deities)
    • devy: divine, godly
    • adite: O Aditi (the mother of gods)
    • sadane: in the abode, in the home
    • pastye: in the nourishment, in the sustenance
    • mahi: great, vast
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra calls for unity and harmony among all beings.
    • It emphasizes the importance of collective effort and joy in achieving common goals.
    • The invocation of the Maruts and Aditi signifies a plea for divine support and guidance.
    • It highlights the significance of words and hymns in connecting with the divine and fostering community.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace, team-building activities can foster unity and collaboration, leading to better productivity.
    • Community gatherings, such as festivals or cultural events, promote joy and togetherness among diverse groups.
    • In educational settings, collaborative projects encourage students to work harmoniously towards a common goal.
    • Spiritual gatherings or prayer meetings can serve as a platform for collective invocation of divine blessings.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the power of unity, joy, and divine support in our lives.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal relationships to community interactions.
    • By embracing the essence of this mantra, individuals and groups can cultivate a harmonious environment that fosters growth and well-being.
    • Ultimately, it reminds us of the importance of coming together with a shared purpose and the strength that lies in collective action.
