चर्दिर्यन्तमदाभ्यं विप्राय स्तुवते नराः |मध्वः सोमस्य पीतये ||
chardir yantam adābhyaṁ viprāya stuvate narā | madhvaḥ somasya pītaye ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • chardir: This can be interpreted as "the one who is moving" or "the one who is going forth."
    • yantam: This means "the one who is directed" or "the one who is guided."
    • adābhyaṁ: This refers to "the one who is free from obstacles" or "the one who is unobstructed."
    • viprāya: This translates to "to the wise" or "to the learned."
    • stuvate: This means "they praise" or "they extol."
    • narā: This refers to "men" or "humans."
    • madhvaḥ: This can be interpreted as "sweet" or "pleasant."
    • somasya: This refers to "of the soma," which is a sacred drink in Vedic tradition.
    • pītaye: This means "for drinking" or "for consumption."
  • Overall Meaning:
    • This mantra speaks about the act of praising the divine or the higher consciousness that guides and supports humanity.
    • It emphasizes the importance of wisdom and the role of learned individuals in recognizing and extolling the divine qualities.
    • The reference to "soma" suggests a connection to spiritual nourishment and the sweetness of divine experiences.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, leaders who guide their teams with wisdom and clarity can be seen as embodying the qualities described in this mantra. Their ability to navigate challenges and inspire others reflects the "unobstructed" path.
    • In personal relationships, individuals who support and uplift their loved ones through praise and encouragement exemplify the essence of this mantra, fostering a nurturing environment.
    • In spiritual practices, the consumption of "soma" can be likened to engaging in activities that nourish the soul, such as meditation, prayer, or community service, which bring sweetness and fulfillment to life.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of wisdom, guidance, and the importance of praising the divine in our lives.
    • It serves as a reminder to seek and recognize the higher consciousness that supports us, encouraging us to embody these qualities in our daily interactions.
    • In essence, it calls for a life lived with purpose, wisdom, and a commitment to uplifting ourselves and others, making it applicable in various real-life scenarios.
