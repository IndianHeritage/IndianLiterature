विओ राजाṁस्य अमिमीत सुक्रतुर वैश्वानरो विडिवो रोचनā कविः |परी यो विश्वा भुवनानि पप्रथे 'दब्धो गोपा अमृतस्य रक्षितā ||
vi yo rajāṁsy amimīta sukratur vaiśvānaro vi divo rocanā kaviḥ | pari yo viśvā bhuvanāni paprathe 'dabdho gopā amṛtasya rakṣitā ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • vi: who
    • yo: that
    • rajāṁsy: kings
    • amimīta: unmeasured
    • sukratur: of good intention
    • vaiśvānaro: the universal being
    • vi: who
    • divo: of the heavens
    • rocanā: brilliance
    • kaviḥ: the poet or seer
    • pari: all around
    • yo: who
    • viśvā: all
    • bhuvanāni: worlds
    • paprathe: has spread
    • 'dabdho: unshaken
    • gopā: protector
    • amṛtasya: of immortality
    • rakṣitā: the one who protects
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of a universal king or being who is unmeasured and possesses good intentions.
    • The term "vaiśvānaro" refers to the universal aspect of divinity, suggesting a connection to all beings and the cosmos.
    • The "brilliance of the heavens" indicates a divine light or knowledge that illuminates the path for humanity.
    • The mantra emphasizes the idea of a protector who safeguards the essence of immortality, representing the eternal truths and values that guide human life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In leadership, a good leader embodies the qualities of the "universal king," making decisions that benefit all, much like Mahatma Gandhi, who led with compassion and integrity.
    • The concept of "brilliance" can be seen in the pursuit of knowledge and enlightenment, as exemplified by scientists and thinkers like Albert Einstein, who illuminated the world with their discoveries.
    • In our daily lives, we can act as "gopā" by protecting and nurturing our relationships, values, and the environment, ensuring a legacy of goodness and sustainability for future generations.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of universal values, good intentions, and the protection of eternal truths in our lives.
    • It encourages individuals to embody these qualities in their actions, fostering a sense of community and responsibility.
    • In a world often filled with chaos, this mantra serves as a reminder to seek wisdom, act with integrity, and protect what is truly valuable, thus contributing to a harmonious existence.
