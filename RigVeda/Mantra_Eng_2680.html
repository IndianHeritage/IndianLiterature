उत्तानायाम अव भरā चिकीत्वान सद्यः प्रवीतā वृषणं जजाना |अरुषस्तूपो रुशद अस्य पाजा इḻायास पुत्रो वयुने 'जनीषṭा ||
uttānāyām ava bharā cikitvān sadyaḥ pravītā vṛṣaṇaṁ jajāna | aruṣastūpo ruśad asya pāja iḻāyās putro vayune 'janiṣṭa ||
  • uttānāyām: This word can be interpreted as "in the upward direction" or "to rise." It signifies the aspiration for growth and elevation in life.
  • ava bharā: This phrase translates to "may you bring forth" or "may you provide." It indicates a call for abundance and support from the divine or the universe.
  • cikitvān: Meaning "wise" or "thoughtful," this word emphasizes the importance of wisdom and intelligence in our actions and decisions.
  • sadyaḥ: This term means "immediately" or "without delay," suggesting the urgency and immediacy of the desires or actions being invoked.
  • pravītā: This word translates to "having been produced" or "having been created," indicating the manifestation of desires or goals.
  • vṛṣaṇaṁ: This term refers to "strength" or "power," often associated with the ability to overcome challenges and achieve success.
  • jajāna: Meaning "to be born" or "to arise," this word signifies the emergence of new opportunities or ideas.
  • aruṣastūpo: This phrase can be interpreted as "the red one" or "the one who shines," symbolizing brightness, energy, and vitality.
  • ruśad: This word means "to roar" or "to be powerful," indicating a strong presence or impact in the world.
  • asya pāja: This translates to "of this offering" or "of this sacrifice," suggesting the importance of giving and sharing in the pursuit of goals.
  • iḻāyās: This term can be interpreted as "of the earth" or "of the ground," symbolizing stability and grounding in one's endeavors.
  • putro: Meaning "son" or "child," this word signifies new beginnings, growth, and the continuation of legacy.
  • vayune: This term translates to "in the strength" or "in the power," emphasizing the importance of harnessing one's inner strength.
  • 'janiṣṭa: This word means "was born" or "came into being," indicating the realization of potential and the birth of new ideas or projects.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of aspiration, wisdom, and the manifestation of strength in our lives. It serves as a reminder to seek growth and elevation while being grounded in our actions. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as personal development, professional growth, and community building. By invoking the principles of wisdom, strength, and immediate action, individuals can create a positive impact in their lives and the lives of others. The significance of this mantra lies in its ability to inspire individuals to rise above challenges, embrace their potential, and contribute meaningfully to the world around them.
