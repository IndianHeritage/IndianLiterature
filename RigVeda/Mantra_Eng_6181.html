त्रीणि शतान्य अरवतां सहस्रā दश गोनाम् |ददुष् पज्राय सām्ने ||
trīṇi śatāny arvatāṁ sahasrā daśa gonām | daduṣ pajrāya sāmne ||
  • Mantra: "trīṇi śatāny arvatāṁ sahasrā daśa gonām | daduṣ pajrāya sāmne ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • trīṇi: three
    • śatāny: hundreds
    • arvatāṁ: of the strong or powerful
    • sahasrā: thousands
    • daśa: ten
    • gonām: of the herds or groups
    • daduṣ: may grant or bestow
    • pajrāya: for prosperity or wealth
    • sāmne: in front or in the presence
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of abundance and prosperity, invoking the blessings of strength and wealth.
    • The mention of "three hundred" and "ten thousand" suggests a vast quantity, emphasizing the idea of plentiful resources.
    • It can be interpreted as a prayer for the bestowal of strength and prosperity in various aspects of life, such as health, wealth, and success.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be seen as a call for abundance in sales and profits, where the "three hundred" and "ten thousand" represent targets or goals that a business aims to achieve.
    • In personal life, individuals may recite this mantra while seeking strength to overcome challenges, whether they be health-related, financial, or emotional.
    • Communities may use this mantra during festivals or gatherings to invoke blessings for collective prosperity and well-being.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its invocation of strength and abundance, making it applicable in various real-life scenarios where individuals or groups seek to enhance their prosperity and overcome obstacles.
    • By reciting this mantra, one can cultivate a mindset of abundance, encouraging positive actions that lead to success and fulfillment in life.
    • Ultimately, it serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of strength, wealth, and community, urging individuals to strive for collective well-being.
