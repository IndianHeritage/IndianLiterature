प्र वः पान्तं रघुमन्यवोऽन्धो यज्ञं रुद्राय मीळ्हुषे भरध्वम् |दिवो अस्तोष्य असुरस्य वीरैः इषुध्येवा मरुतो रोदस्यः ||
pra vaḥ pāntaṁ raghumanyavo 'ndho yajñaṁ rudrāya mīḻhuṣe bharadhvam | divo astoṣy asurasya vīrair iṣudhyeva maruto rodasyoḥ ||
  • pra vaḥ: This phrase can be interpreted as "let us go forth" or "let us move forward." It signifies a call to action, encouraging individuals to take initiative and progress in their endeavors.
  • pāntaṁ raghumanyavo: "pāntaṁ" refers to the path or journey, while "raghumanyavo" can be understood as "the descendants of Raghu," a lineage known for its valor and righteousness. This part emphasizes the importance of following a noble path, inspired by great ancestors.
  • ‘ndho yajñaṁ: "‘ndho" means "blind" or "unseen," and "yajñaṁ" refers to a sacrifice or offering. This suggests that even when the path is not clear, one should continue to perform their duties and sacrifices, trusting in the process.
  • rudrāya mīḻhuṣe bharadhvam: "rudrāya" refers to Rudra, a deity associated with storms and transformation, while "mīḻhuṣe" means "to be united" or "to come together." "bharadhvam" translates to "let us carry" or "let us bear." This part encourages unity and collective effort in the face of challenges.
  • divo astoṣy: "divo" means "of the heavens" or "divine," and "astoṣy" can be interpreted as "may we be satisfied" or "may we be fulfilled." This signifies a connection to the divine and the aspiration for spiritual fulfillment.
  • asurasya vīrair: "asurasya" refers to the "asuras," often seen as powerful beings, and "vīrair" means "by the heroes." This suggests that strength and valor are essential in overcoming obstacles posed by powerful adversaries.
  • iṣudhyeva maruto rodasyoḥ: "iṣudhyeva" means "like arrows," "maruto" refers to the storm deities, and "rodasyoḥ" means "of the two worlds." This part emphasizes the idea of being swift and decisive, like arrows, in navigating the challenges of both the material and spiritual realms.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda encapsulates themes of action, unity, and divine connection. It encourages individuals to pursue their paths with courage and determination, even when faced with uncertainty. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied to various situations such as teamwork in a corporate environment, where collective effort is essential for success, or in personal challenges where one must remain steadfast and trust in their journey. The significance of this mantra lies in its timeless message of resilience, collaboration, and the pursuit of higher ideals, reminding us that we are part of a larger cosmic order and that our actions contribute to the greater good.
