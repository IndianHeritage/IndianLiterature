अवन्तमात्रये गृहं कृतुं युवम् अश्विनाः |अन्ति षड्भूतु वाम अवः ||
avantam atraye gṛhaṁ kṛṇutaṁ yuvam aśvinā | anti ṣad bhūtu vām avaḥ ||
  • Avantam: This word can be interpreted as "to bring forth" or "to create." It signifies the act of initiation or the beginning of something new.
  • Atraye: This term refers to "the one who is worthy" or "the one who is deserving." It emphasizes the importance of merit and righteousness in one's actions.
  • Gṛhaṁ: This translates to "home" or "house." In a broader sense, it represents a place of safety, comfort, and belonging.
  • Kṛṇutaṁ: This means "to make" or "to establish." It indicates the act of creation or construction, whether it be physical or metaphorical.
  • Yuvam: This word means "young" or "youthful." It symbolizes vitality, energy, and the potential for growth and development.
  • Aśvinā: Referring to the Aśvins, the twin deities in Vedic mythology associated with health, medicine, and the dawn. They represent the duality of life and the balance between opposites.
  • Anti: This word can be understood as "to lead" or "to guide." It suggests a direction or purpose in one's actions.
  • Ṣad: This translates to "six." In this context, it may symbolize completeness or the totality of existence.
  • Bhūtu: This term refers to "beings" or "entities." It encompasses all forms of life and existence.
  • Vām: Meaning "you" or "your," it indicates a personal connection and responsibility towards others.
  • Avaḥ: This means "to bring" or "to carry." It signifies the act of delivering or manifesting something into reality.

The mantra "avantam atraye gṛhaṁ kṛṇutaṁ yuvam aśvinā | anti ṣad bhūtu vām avaḥ ||" can be interpreted as a call to the divine Aśvins to help create a worthy home filled with vitality and completeness, guiding all beings towards a better existence.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios:

  • Creating a Supportive Environment: Just as the mantra calls for the establishment of a home, individuals can strive to create supportive environments in their families, workplaces, or communities. This can involve fostering relationships based on mutual respect and understanding.
  • Encouraging Youth Empowerment: The emphasis on youthfulness highlights the importance of empowering the younger generation. This can be done through education, mentorship, and providing opportunities for growth.
  • Holistic Well-being: The invocation of the Aśvins, deities of health, suggests a focus on holistic well-being. This can translate into promoting physical, mental, and emotional health in oneself and others.

In conclusion, this mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of creating a nurturing and supportive environment, empowering the youth, and striving for holistic well-being. It encourages individuals to take responsibility for their actions and to work towards the betterment of themselves and their communities.
