पतिर भव वृत्रहन् सूनृतानां गिरां विश्वायुर वृषभो वयोधाः |आ नो गहि सख्येभिः शिवेभिर महान महीभिर ऊतिभिः सरण्यन ||
patir bhava vṛtrahan sūnṛtānāṁ girāṁ viśvāyur vṛṣabho vayodhāḥ | ā no gahi sakhyebhiḥ śivebhir mahān mahībhir ūtibhiḥ saraṇyan ||
  • patir bhava: This phrase translates to "be the lord" or "be the master." It signifies the importance of leadership and guidance in various aspects of life.
  • vṛtrahan: This means "the slayer of Vritra," referring to the overcoming of obstacles and challenges. In real life, this can be likened to facing and conquering personal or professional challenges.
  • sūnṛtānāṁ girāṁ: This translates to "of the truthful words." It emphasizes the value of honesty and integrity in communication. For example, in a workplace, transparent communication fosters trust among team members.
  • viśvāyur: This means "universal life" or "all-encompassing vitality." It reflects the interconnectedness of all living beings and the importance of nurturing life in all its forms.
  • vṛṣabho: This translates to "the bull," symbolizing strength and power. In real life, this can represent the strength of character needed to face adversities.
  • vayodhāḥ: This means "the warriors of life," indicating the need for courage and resilience in the face of life's battles.
  • ā no gahi: This translates to "may you come to us," suggesting a call for unity and collaboration. In a community setting, this can be seen in collective efforts to achieve common goals.
  • sakhyebhiḥ: This means "with friends," highlighting the importance of companionship and support in our endeavors.
  • śivebhir: This translates to "with auspiciousness," indicating the desire for positive outcomes and blessings in our actions.
  • mahān mahībhir: This means "with great greatness," suggesting the pursuit of excellence and high ideals in life.
  • ūtibhiḥ: This translates to "with abundance," reflecting the aspiration for prosperity and well-being.
  • saraṇyan: This means "the refuge," indicating the importance of seeking shelter and support during difficult times.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of leadership, strength, truthfulness, and the importance of community and support. It serves as a reminder to embrace challenges, communicate honestly, and strive for excellence while seeking refuge in companionship and auspiciousness. In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as in personal development, teamwork, and community building, encouraging individuals to be resilient and supportive of one another in their journeys.
