आ ते सपर्युः जवसे युनज्मि ययोः अनु प्रदिवः श्रुष्टिम आवः |इह त्वा धेयुर हरयः सुशिप्र पिबा त्व अस्य सुषुतस्य चारोः ||
ā te saparyū javase yunajmi yayor anu pradivaḥ śruṣṭim āvaḥ | iha tvā dheyur harayaḥ suśipra pibā tv a1sya suṣutasya cāroḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā te: To you
    • saparyū: With reverence
    • javase: Swiftly
    • yunajmi: I connect or unite
    • yayor: Of whom
    • anu: Following
    • pradivaḥ: The heavens
    • śruṣṭim: Creation
    • āvaḥ: May you bring
    • iha: Here
    • tvā: You
    • dheyur: May they be established
    • harayaḥ: The divine beings
    • suśipra: Quickly
    • pibā: Drink
    • tv: You
    • asya: Of this
    • suṣutasya: Well-being
    • cāroḥ: Of the one who is good
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra expresses a deep reverence and a desire for connection with the divine.
    • The speaker seeks to unite with the divine essence that governs creation and existence.
    • It emphasizes the importance of divine beings in bringing forth well-being and prosperity.
    • The invocation of swiftness suggests a yearning for immediate blessings and support from the divine.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, individuals often seek guidance and support from higher powers during challenging times, similar to the invocation in this mantra.
    • People may perform rituals or prayers to connect with the divine, asking for blessings in their endeavors, much like the desire for divine beings to establish well-being.
    • In moments of urgency, such as during a crisis, individuals may call upon their faith to swiftly bring about resolution and peace.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of connecting with the divine for guidance, support, and well-being.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, such as seeking help during difficult times, performing rituals for blessings, and fostering a sense of community through shared faith.
    • Ultimately, it reminds us of the interconnectedness of all beings and the divine, encouraging us to seek harmony and support in our lives.
