उशन्नु षु णः सुमना उपाके सोमस्य नु सुषुतस्य स्वधावः |पा इन्द्र प्रतिभृतस्य मध्वः सम अन्धसा ममदः पृष्ठ्येन ||
uśann u ṣu ṇaḥ sumanā upāke somasya nu suṣutasya svadhāvaḥ | pā indra pratibhṛtasya madhvaḥ sam andhasā mamadaḥ pṛṣṭhyena ||
  • uśann u ṣu ṇaḥ: This phrase can be interpreted as a call for awakening and enlightenment. It signifies the importance of being aware and conscious in our actions and thoughts.
  • sumanā upāke: This translates to having a good mind or intellect. It emphasizes the value of wisdom and understanding in decision-making.
  • somasya nu suṣutasya svadhāvaḥ: Here, 'soma' refers to a divine nectar or essence, often associated with immortality and bliss. This part of the mantra suggests the pursuit of happiness and well-being through virtuous actions.
  • pā indra pratibhṛtasya madhvaḥ: 'Indra' is a deity representing strength and power. This line calls for the blessings of strength and prosperity, highlighting the need for support in our endeavors.
  • sam andhasā mamadaḥ pṛṣṭhyena: This can be interpreted as a plea for clarity and guidance in times of confusion or darkness. It signifies the importance of seeking help and wisdom from others when faced with challenges.

  • Significance: This mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking enlightenment, wisdom, and strength in our lives. It serves as a reminder to cultivate a good mind, pursue happiness through virtuous actions, and seek guidance when needed.
  • Real-life Application: In everyday life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios such as:
    • In decision-making processes, one can reflect on the importance of having a clear and wise mindset.
    • During challenging times, individuals can seek support from mentors or friends, embodying the call for guidance.
    • In the pursuit of personal goals, the mantra encourages individuals to act with integrity and virtue, leading to true happiness.

  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a profound reminder of the interconnectedness of awareness, wisdom, and strength in our lives. By embodying these principles, we can navigate life's challenges more effectively and lead a fulfilling life.
