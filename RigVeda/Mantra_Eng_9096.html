यो अनिध्मो दीदायद अप्स्व अन्तर यं विप्रास इळते अध्वरेषु |अपां नपान मधुमतीर अपो दा याभिर इन्द्रो वावृधे वीर्याय ||
yo anidhmo dīdayad apsv a1ntar yaṁ viprāsa īḻate adhvareṣu | apāṁ napān madhumatīr apo dā yābhir indro vāvṛdhe vīryāya ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yo: who
    • anidhmo: who is not asleep
    • dīdayad: shining or illuminating
    • apsv: in the waters
    • antar: within
    • yaṁ: whom
    • viprāsa: the wise or learned ones
    • īḻate: invoke or praise
    • adhvareṣu: in the sacrifices
    • apāṁ: of the waters
    • napān: the drink or sustenance
    • madhumatīr: sweet or nourishing
    • apo: waters
    • dā: give
    • yābhir: by which
    • indro: Indra, the king of gods
    • vāvṛdhe: has increased
    • vīryāya: for strength or valor
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of a divine presence that is awake and illuminating, residing within the waters.
    • It emphasizes the importance of wise individuals who invoke this divine presence during rituals and sacrifices.
    • The mantra also highlights the nourishing qualities of water, which is essential for life and sustenance.
    • Indra, as the king of gods, is invoked to increase strength and valor through the blessings of the waters.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, this mantra can be related to the importance of being aware and conscious, akin to being "awake" in our actions and decisions.
    • Just as wise individuals seek knowledge and invoke higher powers for guidance, we too can seek wisdom in our personal and professional lives.
    • The nourishing aspect of water can be seen in our need for hydration and the role of clean water in health and well-being.
    • In rituals or ceremonies, invoking blessings for strength and valor can be paralleled with seeking motivation and courage in challenging situations.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the connection between the divine, nature, and human consciousness.
    • It teaches us the value of awareness, wisdom, and the essential role of water in our lives.
    • In real-life scenarios, it encourages us to seek strength and nourishment from both the physical and spiritual realms.
    • Ultimately, it reminds us to remain vigilant and invoke the divine presence in our endeavors for a fulfilling and empowered life.
