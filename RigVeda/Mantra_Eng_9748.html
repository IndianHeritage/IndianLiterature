तद अग्ने चक्षुः प्रति धेहि रेबे शफारुजं येन पश्यसि यातुधानम् |अथर्ववज ज्योतिषा दैव्यम् सत्यं धूर्वन्तम् अचितं न्योषा ||
tad agne cakṣuḥ prati dhehi rebhe śaphārujaṁ yena paśyasi yātudhānam | atharvavaj jyotiṣā daivyena satyaṁ dhūrvantam acitaṁ ny oṣa ||
  • tad agne: This phrase translates to "O Agni," referring to the Vedic fire god, symbolizing energy, light, and purity.
  • cakṣuḥ prati dhehi: This means "bestow upon us sight," indicating a request for clarity and understanding.
  • rebhe śaphārujaṁ: This can be interpreted as "the one who shines brightly," emphasizing the brilliance and illuminating nature of Agni.
  • yena paśyasi yātudhānam: This translates to "by whom you see the evil spirits," suggesting the protective aspect of Agni, helping to ward off negativity and malevolence.
  • atharvavaj: This term refers to the Atharva Veda, which is associated with knowledge and wisdom.
  • jyotiṣā: Meaning "by light," this emphasizes the importance of illumination, both literally and metaphorically.
  • daivyena satyaṁ: This translates to "divine truth," highlighting the significance of truth in spiritual and worldly matters.
  • dhūrvantam acitaṁ: This phrase can be understood as "the one who is not obscured," indicating clarity and purity of thought.
  • ny oṣa: This can be interpreted as "may we be protected," reinforcing the theme of seeking safety and guidance.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking clarity, protection, and divine truth in our lives. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various contexts such as decision-making, personal growth, and spiritual practices. For instance, when faced with challenges or negativity, invoking the qualities of Agni can inspire individuals to seek inner strength and wisdom. This mantra serves as a reminder to pursue truth and clarity in our actions and thoughts, fostering a sense of safety and enlightenment in our daily lives.
