अजीतये 'हतये पवस्व स्वस्तये सर्वतातये बृहते |तद उशन्ति विश्व इमे सखायस तद अहं वश्मि पवमान सोम ||
ajītaye 'hataye pavasva svastaye sarvatātaye bṛhate | tad uśanti viśva ime sakhāyas tad ahaṁ vaśmi pavamāna soma ||
  • ajītaye: This term can be interpreted as "to the unconquered" or "to the invincible." It signifies strength and resilience in the face of challenges.
  • 'hataye: This word means "to the one who has been defeated" or "to the one who has fallen." It reflects the idea of overcoming adversity and rising again.
  • pavasva: This translates to "may you purify" or "may you cleanse." It suggests a call for purification, both physically and spiritually.
  • svastaye: This means "for well-being" or "for prosperity." It emphasizes the importance of health and happiness in life.
  • sarvatātaye: This term can be understood as "to the all-pervading" or "to the universal." It indicates a connection to the divine or the universal consciousness.
  • bṛhate: This translates to "to the great" or "to the vast." It signifies the importance of greatness in thoughts, actions, and aspirations.
  • tad uśanti: This phrase means "that brings peace" or "that grants tranquility." It reflects the desire for inner peace and harmony.
  • viśva ime sakhāyas: This translates to "these friends of all" or "these companions of the universe." It emphasizes the importance of friendship and unity among all beings.
  • tad ahaṁ vaśmi: This means "I am under its control" or "I am in its power." It signifies surrendering to a higher power or the divine.
  • pavamāna soma: This refers to the "purifying soma," which is often associated with the divine nectar or elixir that grants immortality and enlightenment.

Conclusion: This mantra encapsulates themes of resilience, purification, well-being, and unity. It serves as a reminder that despite challenges and defeats, one can rise again and seek purification and peace. In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as during times of personal struggle, when seeking mental clarity, or when fostering relationships and community. It encourages individuals to connect with the universal consciousness and strive for greatness while maintaining harmony with others. The significance of this mantra lies in its ability to inspire hope, promote well-being, and foster a sense of unity among all beings.
