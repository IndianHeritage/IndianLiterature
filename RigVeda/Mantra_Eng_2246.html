अभिनक्षन्तो अभि ये तं आनशुर निधिम पाणीनाम परमं गुहा हितम् |ते विद्वांसः प्रतिचक्ष्यानृतā पुनर यत उ आयन तद उद ईयुर आविशम् ||
abhinakṣanto abhi ye tam ānaśur nidhim paṇīnām paramaṁ guhā hitam | te vidvāṁsaḥ praticakṣyānṛtā punar yata u āyan tad ud īyur āviśam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • abhinakṣanto: Those who are aware or knowledgeable.
    • abhi: Towards or in relation to.
    • ye: Who.
    • tam: That.
    • ānaśur: The one who is not greedy or does not desire.
    • nidhim: Treasure or wealth.
    • paṇīnām: Of the skilled or artisans.
    • paramaṁ: Supreme or ultimate.
    • guhā: Cave or hidden place.
    • hitam: Beneficial or good.
    • te: They.
    • vidvāṁsaḥ: The wise or knowledgeable ones.
    • praticakṣyānṛtā: They reveal the truth.
    • punar: Again or once more.
    • yata: Because.
    • u: And.
    • āyan: Coming or arriving.
    • tad: That.
    • ud: Up or out.
    • īyur: They may go.
    • āviśam: Enter or dwell.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the wise individuals who are aware of the ultimate treasure that lies hidden.
    • It emphasizes the importance of knowledge and wisdom over material greed.
    • The "cave" symbolizes the depths of understanding and insight that can be achieved through learning and experience.
    • Those who are knowledgeable reveal the truth and guide others towards it, encouraging a cycle of enlightenment.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, a wise leader who prioritizes ethical practices over profit maximization can be seen as embodying the principles of this mantra.
    • In education, teachers who inspire students to seek knowledge for its own sake, rather than for grades or accolades, reflect the essence of this teaching.
    • Philosophers and thinkers who delve into the depths of human experience and share their insights contribute to the collective wisdom of society.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of wisdom and the pursuit of knowledge as the true treasures of life.
    • It encourages individuals to seek deeper understanding and to share their insights with others, fostering a community of learning and growth.
    • In real life, applying the teachings of this mantra can lead to more ethical decision-making, personal fulfillment, and a more enlightened society.
