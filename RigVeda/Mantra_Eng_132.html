इह त्वष्टारम अग्रियं विश्वरूपम् उप हवये |अस्माकम् अस्तु केवलः ||
iha tvaṣṭāram agriyaṁ viśvarūpam upa hvaye | asmākam astu kevalaḥ ||
  • iha - Here, in this world.
  • tvaṣṭāram - O Tvaṣṭar, the divine craftsman or creator.
  • agriyaṁ - The foremost or the best.
  • viśvarūpam - The universal form or the all-encompassing nature.
  • upa hvaye - I invoke or call upon.
  • asmākam - Our, belonging to us.
  • astu - Let there be or may it be.
  • kevalaḥ - Only, solely, or exclusively.

This mantra can be translated as: "Here, I invoke Tvaṣṭar, the foremost creator of the universal form. May there be only that which belongs to us."
The mantra addresses Tvaṣṭar, a deity associated with creation and craftsmanship in Vedic literature. By invoking Tvaṣṭar, the speaker seeks the blessings of the divine craftsman to manifest the best and most universal aspects of creation in their life.
In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts:

  • Creativity and Innovation: Just as Tvaṣṭar is the divine craftsman, individuals can invoke their creative potential in fields such as art, music, and technology. For example, an artist may recite this mantra before starting a new painting, seeking inspiration and the ability to create something unique and impactful.
  • Personal Growth: The invocation of the "foremost" creator can symbolize the pursuit of excellence in personal endeavors. A student preparing for exams might use this mantra to focus their mind and channel their efforts towards achieving their best results.
  • Community and Collaboration: The phrase "may there be only that which belongs to us" emphasizes unity and collective well-being. In a team setting, this mantra can inspire collaboration and a shared vision, encouraging team members to work together towards a common goal.

Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine assistance in the pursuit of excellence and creativity. It serves as a reminder that we can invoke higher powers to guide our actions and intentions. In various aspects of life, whether in personal development, creative endeavors, or collaborative efforts, this mantra encourages individuals to seek the best outcomes while fostering a sense of unity and purpose. By integrating this invocation into daily practices, one can cultivate a mindset geared towards achieving greatness and contributing positively to the world around them.
