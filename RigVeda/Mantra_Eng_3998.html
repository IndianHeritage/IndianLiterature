प्र तव्यसो नामुक्तिं तुरस्याहं पूष्ण उता वायोर अधिक्षि |या राधसां चोदितारा मतीनां या वाजस्य द्रविणोदा उता तमन ||
pra tavyaso namaüktiṁ turasyāham pūṣṇa uta vāyor adikṣi | yā rādhasā coditārā matīnāṁ yā vājasya draviṇodā uta tman ||
  • pra tavyaso namaüktiṁ: This phrase can be interpreted as a call to the divine for guidance and support. The term "namaüktiṁ" refers to the act of offering salutations or prayers. In real life, this can be seen as a reminder to seek help from higher powers or wisdom when faced with challenges.
  • turasyāham pūṣṇa uta vāyor adikṣi: Here, "turasyāham" signifies a sense of urgency or a call to action. "Pūṣṇa" refers to nourishment or sustenance, while "vāyor" relates to the wind or breath. This can be understood as a plea for the vital energy that sustains life. In practical terms, it emphasizes the importance of seeking nourishment—both physical and spiritual—in our daily lives.
  • yā rādhasā coditārā matīnāṁ: This line speaks to the inspiration and motivation that comes from divine sources. "Rādhasā" can be associated with the divine feminine energy, while "coditārā" suggests being spurred into action. This can be likened to moments in life when we feel inspired to pursue our passions or goals, often attributed to a higher calling or inner drive.
  • yā vājasya draviṇodā uta tman: The term "vājasya" refers to strength or power, and "draviṇodā" relates to wealth or resources. This line emphasizes the importance of harnessing our inner strength and resources to achieve our goals. In real life, this can be seen in individuals who leverage their skills and talents to create opportunities for themselves and others.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a profound message about seeking divine guidance, nourishment, and inspiration while harnessing our inner strength and resources. Its significance lies in the reminder that we are not alone in our endeavors; we can call upon higher powers for support and motivation. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied during times of uncertainty or when pursuing personal and professional goals, encouraging individuals to remain connected to their spiritual roots while actively engaging with the world around them.
