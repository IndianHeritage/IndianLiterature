तन् मा ऋतम् इन्द्र शूर चित् र पात्व अपो न वज्रिन दुरिताति पर्षि भूरी |कद न इन्द्र राया आ दशस्येर विश्वाप्सन्यस्य स्पृहयाय्यस्य राजन् ||
tan ma ṛtam indra śūra citra pātv apo na vajrin duritāti parṣi bhūri | kadā na indra rāya ā daśasyer viśvapsnyasya spṛhayāyyasya rājan ||
  • tan ma ṛtam indra śūra citra pātv: This phrase can be translated as "May Indra, the powerful and valiant one, grant us the truth." Here, Indra represents strength and valor, often associated with leadership and protection. In real life, this can be seen in leaders who embody courage and integrity, inspiring their followers to pursue truth and justice.
  • apo na vajrin duritāti parṣi bhūri: This translates to "May we be free from obstacles and may we receive abundant blessings." The term "vajrin" refers to being strong and unyielding, while "duritāti" signifies obstacles or difficulties. In everyday life, this can relate to overcoming challenges in personal or professional endeavors, where resilience and support from others can lead to success.
  • kadā na indra rāya ā daśasyer: This means "When will Indra, the king, bestow his grace upon us?" It reflects a longing for divine intervention and support in times of need. In practical terms, this can be likened to seeking guidance or help from mentors or spiritual leaders during critical moments in life.
  • viśvapsnyasya spṛhayāyyasya rājan: This translates to "O king, may we aspire for the universal wealth." The term "viśvapsnyasya" refers to universal abundance, indicating a desire for prosperity that benefits all. In real life, this can be seen in community initiatives aimed at uplifting the less fortunate, promoting a sense of shared wealth and responsibility.

  • Significance and Application: This mantra emphasizes the importance of strength, truth, and the pursuit of collective well-being. It serves as a reminder that in our quest for success and prosperity, we should seek the support of strong leaders and work towards overcoming obstacles together.
  • Real-Life Scenario: In a corporate setting, this mantra can inspire teams to work collaboratively, overcoming challenges with the guidance of strong leadership. In community service, it can motivate individuals to strive for a better society, ensuring that everyone has access to resources and opportunities.
  • Conclusion: The essence of this mantra lies in the interconnectedness of strength, truth, and collective prosperity. By embodying these principles, individuals and communities can navigate life's challenges more effectively, fostering an environment where everyone can thrive.
