इह प्र ब्रूहि यतमह् सो अग्ने यो यातुधानो य इदं कृतोति |तम आ रभस्व समिधा यविष्ठ नृचक्षसश् चक्षुषे रन्धयैनम् ||
iha pra brūhi yatamaḥ so agne yo yātudhāno ya idaṁ kṛṇoti | tam ā rabhasva samidhā yaviṣṭha nṛcakṣasaś cakṣuṣe randhayainam ||
  • Mantra: "iha pra brūhi yatamaḥ so agne yo yātudhāno ya idaṁ kṛṇoti | tam ā rabhasva samidhā yaviṣṭha nṛcakṣasaś cakṣuṣe randhayainam ||"
  • Translation: "Here, speak out, O Agni, who is the one that strives; who is the one that creates this. Ignite him with your flame, O most excellent one, and blind the eyes of the enemies."

  • Word-by-Word Explanation:
    • iha: Here, in this context or situation.
    • pra brūhi: Speak out or declare openly.
    • yatamaḥ: The one who strives or makes an effort.
    • so: He or that one.
    • agne: O Agni, the fire god, representing energy and transformation.
    • yo: Who or that which.
    • yātudhāno: The one who creates or brings forth.
    • ya: Who or that which.
    • idaṁ: This or here.
    • kṛṇoti: Creates or makes.
    • tam: Him or that one.
    • ā rabhasva: Ignite or set ablaze.
    • samidhā: With your flame or fire.
    • yaviṣṭha: Most excellent or supreme.
    • nṛcakṣasaś: Of the enemies or those who oppose.
    • cakṣuṣe: Eyes or vision.
    • randhayainam: Blind or obscure him.

  • Significance:
    • This mantra invokes Agni, the fire god, symbolizing energy, transformation, and purification.
    • It emphasizes the importance of striving and effort in achieving one's goals.
    • The plea to blind the enemies signifies the need for protection against negativity and obstacles in life.
  • Real-Life Application:
    • In personal development, this mantra can be a reminder to speak out and declare one's intentions clearly, just as one would strive for success in their career or personal life.
    • In challenging situations, invoking the energy of Agni can symbolize the need to transform obstacles into opportunities, much like how a fire can transform raw materials into something useful.
    • In a competitive environment, the request to blind the enemies can be interpreted as a call for focus and clarity, ensuring that distractions and negativity do not hinder progress.

  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the importance of effort, clarity of purpose, and the transformative power of energy in overcoming challenges.
    • By invoking Agni, individuals can seek strength and protection in their endeavors, making it applicable in various aspects of life, from personal growth to professional challenges.
