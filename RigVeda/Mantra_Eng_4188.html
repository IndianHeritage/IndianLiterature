येषां श्रीयाधि रोडसी विभ्राजन्ते रथेष्वा |दिवि रुक्म इवोपरी ||
yeṣāṁ śriyādhi rodasī vibhrājante ratheṣv ā | divi rukma ivopari ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yeṣāṁ: of those
    • śriyā: with prosperity or wealth
    • adhi: above or over
    • rodasī: the two worlds (heaven and earth)
    • vibhrājante: shine or radiate
    • ratheṣv: in chariots
    • ā: indeed
    • divi: in the sky or heaven
    • rukma: golden
    • iva: like
    • upari: above
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of those who possess wealth and prosperity, which shines brightly in the two realms of existence—heaven and earth.
    • The imagery of chariots suggests movement, progress, and the journey of life, while the reference to gold signifies purity, value, and divine blessings.
    • It implies that those who are prosperous and virtuous radiate their wealth and goodness, influencing both the material and spiritual worlds.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • Consider a successful entrepreneur who not only achieves financial success but also contributes to society through philanthropy. Their wealth shines as a beacon of hope and inspiration for others.
    • In a community, individuals who share their resources and knowledge can uplift others, creating a cycle of prosperity that benefits everyone.
    • In personal relationships, those who embody kindness and generosity often create a positive environment that encourages growth and happiness.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of wealth that is not just material but also spiritual and ethical.
    • It teaches us that true prosperity is reflected in our actions and the positive impact we have on the world around us.
    • In real life, it encourages individuals to strive for success while also being mindful of their responsibilities towards others, fostering a harmonious existence.
