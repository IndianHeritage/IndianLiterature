तं ā नूनं वृजनम् अन्यथा चिच्छूरो यच् चक्र विडुरो गृणीषे |मा निर अरं शुक्रदुग्धस्य धेनोर आङ्गिरसां ब्रह्मणा विप्र जिन्‍व ||
tam ā nūnaṁ vṛjanam anyathā cic chūro yac chakra vi duro gṛṇīṣe | mā nir araṁ śukradughasya dhenor āṅgirasān brahmaṇā vipra jinva ||
  • tam ā nūnaṁ vṛjanam: This phrase can be interpreted as "that indeed is the one who is free from sin or fault." It emphasizes the importance of purity and righteousness in one's actions.
  • anyathā cic shūro: This translates to "otherwise, the brave one." It suggests that true bravery is not just about physical strength but also about moral integrity and the courage to uphold righteousness.
  • yac chakra vi duro gṛṇīṣe: This means "whatever you have created or established." It highlights the significance of creation and the responsibility that comes with it, urging individuals to be mindful of their contributions to the world.
  • mā nir araṁ: This can be understood as "do not let there be any harm." It serves as a reminder to avoid causing harm to others, promoting a sense of compassion and empathy.
  • śukradughasya dhenor: This phrase refers to "the cow that gives the milk of wisdom." Cows are often seen as symbols of nourishment and abundance, and this line emphasizes the value of wisdom and knowledge as essential sustenance for life.
  • āṅgirasān brahmaṇā: This translates to "by the Brahman, the wise one." It signifies the connection between wisdom and the divine, suggesting that true knowledge comes from a higher source.
  • vipra jinva: This means "may the learned one be victorious." It encourages the pursuit of knowledge and wisdom, asserting that those who seek understanding will ultimately triumph.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda encapsulates the essence of righteousness, wisdom, and the importance of moral integrity. In real-life scenarios, it serves as a guiding principle for individuals to strive for purity in their actions, to be brave in upholding truth, and to contribute positively to society. It emphasizes the need for compassion and the pursuit of knowledge, reminding us that true victory lies in the wisdom we acquire and share. By embodying these values, we can create a more harmonious and enlightened world.
