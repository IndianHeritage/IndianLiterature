यदा ते विष्णुर ओजसा त्रिणि पदा विचक्रमे |आदित ते हर्यतां हरि ववक्षतुः ||
yadā te viṣṇur ojasā trīṇi padā vicakrame | ād it te haryatā harī vavakṣatuḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yadā: When
    • te: Your
    • viṣṇu: Vishnu (the preserver in Hinduism)
    • ojasā: With strength or energy
    • trīṇi: Three
    • padā: Steps or feet
    • vicakrame: Moved or traversed
    • ād: May
    • it: This
    • te: Your
    • haryatā: With swiftness or speed
    • harī: The one who removes obstacles
    • vavakṣatuḥ: May it be revealed or manifest
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the divine energy of Vishnu, who is known for his protective and sustaining qualities.
    • The phrase "When your Vishnu moves with strength" suggests a moment of divine intervention or support.
    • The "three steps" can symbolize the three realms of existence: physical, mental, and spiritual.
    • The invocation of swiftness and the removal of obstacles indicates a plea for help in overcoming challenges in life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In times of personal crisis, such as losing a job or facing health issues, individuals often seek divine support, much like the invocation in this mantra.
    • In a competitive environment, such as exams or job interviews, people may pray for the strength and guidance of a higher power to help them succeed.
    • During significant life transitions, such as marriage or moving to a new city, individuals may reflect on the need for divine presence to navigate these changes smoothly.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of divine support and strength in overcoming life's challenges.
    • It serves as a reminder that, in moments of difficulty, one can seek guidance and energy from a higher power.
    • In practical terms, it encourages individuals to remain hopeful and proactive, trusting that they are not alone in their struggles.
    • Ultimately, the mantra highlights the interconnectedness of the physical, mental, and spiritual realms, urging us to seek balance and support in all aspects of life.
