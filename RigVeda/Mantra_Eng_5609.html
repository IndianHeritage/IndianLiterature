अस्मे वीरो मरुतः शुष्म्य अस्तु जनानां यो असुरो विधर्ता |अपो येन सुख्षितये तरेमाध svं ओको अभि वः स्याम ||
asme vīro marutaḥ śuṣmy astu janānāṁ yo asuro vidhartā | apo yena sukṣitaye taremādha svam oko abhi vaḥ syāma ||
  • asme vīro marutaḥ: This phrase can be translated to "may the heroes of the Maruts be with us." The Maruts are storm deities in Vedic literature, often associated with strength and valor. In real life, this can be interpreted as a call for courage and support from powerful forces in times of need.
  • ṣuṣmy astu janānāṁ: This means "may there be prosperity for the people." It emphasizes the importance of well-being and abundance for the community. In contemporary society, this can relate to social welfare programs aimed at improving the quality of life for all individuals.
  • yo asuro vidhartā: This translates to "who is the sustainer of the divine." It refers to a higher power or force that nurtures and sustains life. In everyday life, this can be seen in the context of nature and the environment, where the balance of ecosystems sustains human life.
  • apo yena sukṣitaye: This phrase means "by whom we are protected." It signifies the protective forces in our lives, whether they be spiritual, familial, or societal. For example, the role of community support systems can be seen as a protective force that helps individuals navigate challenges.
  • taremādha svam oko: This translates to "may we reach our own abode." It suggests a journey towards one's goals or aspirations. In real life, this can be related to personal growth and the pursuit of dreams, emphasizing the importance of striving for one's own path.
  • abhi vaḥ syāma: This means "may we be united." It highlights the significance of unity and collaboration among people. In modern contexts, this can be applied to teamwork in workplaces or community efforts to achieve common goals.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a profound message of seeking strength, prosperity, protection, personal growth, and unity. Its significance lies in its applicability to various aspects of life, from individual aspirations to collective well-being. In a world where challenges are abundant, this mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of community support, the pursuit of goals, and the strength derived from unity. By embodying these principles, individuals and societies can work towards a harmonious and prosperous existence.
