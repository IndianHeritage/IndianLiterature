व्रजं कृणुध्वं स हि वो नृपाणो वर्म सिव्यध्वं बहुला पृथूनी |पुरः कृणुध्वं आयसीर अधृष्टा मा वः सुस्रोच चमसो दृष्णता तं ||
vrajaṁ kṛṇudhvaṁ sa hi vo nṛpāṇo varma sīvyadhvam bahulā pṛthūni | puraḥ kṛṇudhvam āyasīr adhṛṣṭā mā vaḥ susroc camaso dṛṁhatā tam ||
  • Mantra: "vrajaṁ kṛṇudhvaṁ sa hi vo nṛpāṇo varma sīvyadhvam bahulā pṛthūni | puraḥ kṛṇudhvam āyasīr adhṛṣṭā mā vaḥ susroc camaso dṛṁhatā tam ||"
  • Translation: "O rulers, weave together the protective armor; may you create a vast and strong shield. Let the iron be forged before you, and may you not be hindered by any obstacles."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • vrajaṁ: to gather or come together
    • kṛṇudhvaṁ: you should create or weave
    • nṛpāṇo: O rulers or kings
    • varma: armor or protection
    • sīvyadhvam: weave or construct
    • bahulā: vast or abundant
    • pṛthūni: strong or sturdy
    • puraḥ: before or in front
    • āyasīr: iron or metal
    • adhṛṣṭā: not hindered or obstructed
    • susroc: well-formed or well-structured
    • camaso: obstacles or hindrances
    • dṛṁhatā: to be strong or fortified
    • tam: that or it
  • Contextual Explanation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of unity and collaboration among leaders (nṛpāṇo) to create a strong defense (varma) for their people.
    • The act of weaving armor symbolizes the collective effort required to build resilience against challenges.
    • In real life, this can be seen in various contexts such as community building, teamwork in organizations, or even nations coming together for a common cause.
    • For example, during natural disasters, local governments and organizations often collaborate to provide aid and support, embodying the essence of this mantra.
  • Significance:
    • The mantra serves as a reminder of the strength found in unity and the importance of preparing for challenges ahead.
    • It encourages leaders to take proactive measures in safeguarding their communities, whether through policy-making, disaster preparedness, or fostering social cohesion.
    • In a broader sense, it highlights the need for collective responsibility in facing societal issues, such as climate change or public health crises.
