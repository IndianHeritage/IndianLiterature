कथा त एतदहम् आ चिकेटं गृत्सस्य पाकस् तवसो मनिषाम् |त्वं नो विद्वाम् ऋतुथा वि वोचो यम् अर्धं ते मगहन क्षेम्याः धूः ||
kathā ta etad aham ā ciketaṁ gṛtsasya pākas tavaso manīṣām | tvaṁ no vidvām̐ ṛtuthā vi voco yam ardhaṁ te maghavan kṣemyā dhūḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • kathā: How
    • ta: That
    • etad: This
    • aham: I
    • ā: To
    • ciketaṁ: Understand
    • gṛtsasya: Of the one who is pure
    • pākas: Cooked or prepared
    • tavaso: Of your strength
    • manīṣām: Of wisdom or thought
    • tvaṁ: You
    • no: For us
    • vidvām̐: Knowledgeable
    • ṛtuthā: In truth
    • vi: Indeed
    • voco: I speak
    • yam: Whom
    • ardhaṁ: Half
    • te: Your
    • maghavan: O bountiful one
    • kṣemyā: May I be protected
    • dhūḥ: From the troubles
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra expresses a quest for understanding and knowledge.
    • The speaker is seeking clarity on how to comprehend the essence of purity and strength.
    • It emphasizes the importance of wisdom and truth in achieving one's goals.
    • The invocation of "maghavan" refers to a divine figure associated with abundance and protection.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, this mantra can be applied when seeking guidance on a complex project. One might ask for clarity and understanding from a mentor or leader.
    • In personal relationships, it can signify the importance of open communication and understanding each other's perspectives to strengthen bonds.
    • In educational pursuits, students may reflect on this mantra when striving to grasp difficult concepts, seeking wisdom and truth in their studies.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for understanding and wisdom in all aspects of life.
    • It encourages individuals to seek knowledge and clarity, emphasizing the role of truth in achieving success.
    • In real-life scenarios, applying the principles of this mantra can lead to better decision-making, improved relationships, and a deeper understanding of oneself and the world.
