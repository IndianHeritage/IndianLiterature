प्रातः रत्नं प्रातरित्वा दधाति तं चिकीत्वान् प्रतिगृह्य न धत्ते |तेन प्रजां वर्धयमान आयू रायस पोषेण सचते सुवीरः ||
prātā ratnam prātaritvā dadhāti taṁ cikitvān pratigṛhyā ni dhatte | tena prajāṁ vardhayamāna āyū rāyas poṣeṇa sacate suvīraḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • prātā: In the morning
    • ratnam: Jewel or precious thing
    • prātaritvā: Having been brought forth in the morning
    • dadhāti: Bestows or gives
    • taṁ: That
    • cikitvān: Knowing or understanding
    • pratigṛhyā: Having accepted
    • ni dhatte: Does not hold back
    • tena: By that
    • prajāṁ: Offspring or people
    • vardhayamāna: Being nourished or increased
    • āyū: Life or longevity
    • rāyas: Wealth or prosperity
    • poṣeṇa: By nurturing or fostering
    • sacate: Flourishes or thrives
    • suvīraḥ: The one who is strong or valiant
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the morning as a time of renewal and the bestowal of blessings.
    • The "jewel" symbolizes the precious gifts of life, knowledge, and prosperity that are available to us each day.
    • Understanding and accepting these gifts leads to the flourishing of life and community.
    • The mantra emphasizes the importance of nurturing and fostering growth, both in ourselves and in others.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a personal context, starting each day with gratitude can be seen as a way of recognizing the "jewel" of life.
    • In a community setting, leaders who nurture their people and resources can lead to a thriving society.
    • In business, companies that invest in their employees' growth and well-being often see increased productivity and loyalty.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of recognizing and accepting the blessings of life each day.
    • It teaches us that by nurturing ourselves and others, we can foster growth, prosperity, and strength in our communities.
    • In real life, applying the principles of this mantra can lead to a more fulfilling and prosperous existence, both individually and collectively.
