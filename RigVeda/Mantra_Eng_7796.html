सोमा असृग्रम इन्दवः सुतā ऋतस्य साधने | इन्द्राय मधुमत्तमाः ||
somā asṛgram indavaḥ sutā ṛtasya sādane | indrāya madhumattamāḥ ||
  • Mantra: "somā asṛgram indavaḥ sutā ṛtasya sādane | indrāya madhumattamāḥ ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • somā: Refers to the sacred drink, often associated with the moon and immortality.
    • asṛgram: Means "to flow" or "to pour out," indicating abundance.
    • indavaḥ: Refers to the "Indus" or "the divine," often associated with the gods.
    • sutā: Means "sons" or "offspring," often referring to the results or products of a process.
    • ṛtasya: Refers to "truth" or "cosmic order," indicating the fundamental principles of the universe.
    • sādane: Means "in the abode" or "in the place," suggesting a location or context.
    • indrāya: Refers to "to Indra," the king of gods, symbolizing power and strength.
    • madhumattamāḥ: Means "the sweetest" or "the most delightful," indicating the best or most desirable quality.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the essence of the sacred drink, Soma, which is believed to bestow immortality and divine qualities.
    • The phrase "asṛgram indavaḥ sutā" suggests the flowing abundance of divine blessings that come from the sacred drink.
    • By referring to "ṛtasya sādane," the mantra emphasizes the importance of aligning with cosmic order and truth in our lives.
    • The invocation to "Indra" highlights the need for strength and power in overcoming challenges, while "madhumattamāḥ" signifies the pursuit of the highest and sweetest experiences in life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a personal context, one might seek to embody the qualities of truth and abundance in their daily life, striving for honesty in relationships and generosity in actions.
    • In a professional setting, aligning with the principles of "ṛta" can mean adhering to ethical practices and fostering a harmonious work environment.
    • During celebrations or rituals, invoking the essence of Soma can symbolize the sharing of joy and prosperity among family and friends, much like sharing a drink during a festive occasion.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of abundance, truth, and divine strength in our lives.
    • Its significance lies in the encouragement to seek out and embrace the sweetness of life while remaining grounded in truth and cosmic order.
    • In real-life scenarios, it can inspire individuals to cultivate a mindset of abundance, ethical living, and the pursuit of joy, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling existence.
