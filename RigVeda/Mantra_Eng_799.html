अद्रौ चिदस्मा अन्तरदुरोणे विश्वां न विश्वो अमृतः स्वाधीः ||
adrau cid asmā antar duroṇe viśāṁ na viśvo amṛtaḥ svādhīḥ ||
  • Word Breakdown:
    • adrau: This can be interpreted as "in the beginning" or "at the start."
    • cid: This means "if" or "when."
    • asmā: Translates to "us" or "we."
    • antar: Means "within" or "inside."
    • duroṇe: Refers to "the great expanse" or "the vastness."
    • viśāṁ: This means "of the worlds" or "of the universe."
    • na: Translates to "not."
    • viśvo: Means "all" or "everything."
    • amṛtaḥ: Refers to "immortal" or "eternal."
    • svādhīḥ: This can be interpreted as "self-controlled" or "self-possessed."
  • Meaning:
    • The mantra speaks about the essence of existence and the interconnectedness of all beings.
    • It suggests that within the vastness of the universe, there is a profound truth that is eternal and unchanging.
    • The phrase "not all is immortal" indicates the transient nature of life, while "self-controlled" emphasizes the importance of self-mastery in navigating this reality.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In the context of personal growth, this mantra can be applied to the journey of self-discovery. Just as the universe is vast and complex, so too are our inner worlds.
    • For instance, when facing challenges, one can reflect on the idea of self-control and resilience, recognizing that while circumstances may change, our inner strength can remain constant.
    • In relationships, understanding that not everything is eternal can help us appreciate the present moment and foster deeper connections with others.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the transient nature of life and the importance of self-awareness and control.
    • It encourages individuals to look within and recognize their own power in shaping their experiences, despite the vastness of the external world.
    • In practical terms, it can be applied in meditation, mindfulness practices, and personal development, guiding individuals to find balance and peace amidst life's uncertainties.
