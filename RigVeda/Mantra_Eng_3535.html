एष स्य भानुर उद इयर्ति युज्यते रथः परिज्मा दिवो अस्य सानविः |पृक्षासो अस्मिन मिथुना अधि त्रयो दृतिस तुरीयो मधुनो विग्रप्शते ||
eṣa sya bhānur ud iyarti yujyate rathaḥ parijmā divo asya sānavi | pṛkṣāso asmin mithunā adhi trayo dṛtis turīyo madhuno vi rapśate ||
  • eṣa sya bhānur ud iyarti: This phrase can be translated as "This sun rises high." Here, "eṣa" means "this," "sya" refers to "of this," "bhānur" means "sun," "ud" means "up," and "iyarti" means "rises." The sun is often seen as a symbol of enlightenment and knowledge in many cultures.
  • yujyate rathaḥ parijmā divo asya sānavi: This translates to "The chariot is harnessed to the sky." "Yujyate" means "is harnessed," "rathaḥ" means "chariot," "parijmā" refers to "surrounding," "divo" means "of the sky," and "asya sānavi" means "of this." The chariot symbolizes the journey of life, and the sky represents the vast possibilities and aspirations we have.
  • pṛkṣāso asmin mithunā adhi trayo: This can be interpreted as "In this, the three are united." "Pṛkṣāso" means "in this," "asmin" means "this," "mithunā" means "united," and "adhi trayo" means "the three." This suggests the importance of unity and collaboration among different elements or forces.
  • dṛtis turīyo madhuno vi rapśate: This translates to "The steadfastness of the four is like honey." "Dṛtis" means "steadfastness," "turīyo" refers to "four," "madhuno" means "honey," and "vi rapśate" means "shines." Here, steadfastness is compared to honey, which is sweet and nourishing, indicating that perseverance leads to rewarding outcomes.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda emphasizes the themes of enlightenment, unity, and steadfastness. In real life, it can be applied to various scenarios such as teamwork in a professional setting, where individuals must harness their strengths (the chariot) to achieve a common goal (the sky). It also highlights the importance of perseverance in the face of challenges, suggesting that those who remain steadfast will ultimately reap the rewards of their efforts, much like the sweetness of honey. This mantra serves as a reminder to seek knowledge, work together harmoniously, and stay committed to our goals for a fulfilling life.
