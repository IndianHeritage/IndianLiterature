विष्वे ह्यस्मै यजताय धृष्णवे क्रतुम्भरन्ति वृषभाय सश्चते |वृषा यजस्व हविषा विदुष्टारः पिबेन्द्र सोमं वृषभेण भानुना ||
viśve hy asmai yajatāya dhṛṣṇave kratum bharanti vṛṣabhāya saścate | vṛṣā yajasva haviṣā viduṣṭaraḥ pibendra somaṁ vṛṣabheṇa bhānunā ||
  • viśve hy asmai yajatāya: This phrase can be translated as "All beings indeed worship him." It emphasizes the universality of worship and the interconnectedness of all life. In real life, this can be seen in various cultures where people come together to honor a higher power or the essence of life itself, such as during festivals or communal prayers.
  • dhṛṣṇave kratum bharanti: This means "the resolute intentions are fulfilled." It suggests that strong will and determination lead to the realization of one's goals. For example, in a business context, a startup that remains focused and determined in its mission is more likely to succeed than one that lacks clarity and resolve.
  • vṛṣabhāya saścate: This translates to "to the strong one, they offer." The term "vṛṣabha" often refers to strength or a bull, symbolizing power and vitality. In everyday life, this can be related to how people often look up to strong leaders or mentors who guide them through challenges, offering their respect and support.
  • vṛṣā yajasva haviṣā viduṣṭaraḥ: This phrase means "O strong one, perform the sacrifice with offerings." It highlights the importance of dedication and effort in achieving success. In practical terms, this can be seen in the context of education, where students must put in hard work and dedication (sacrifice) to achieve their academic goals.
  • pibendra somaṁ vṛṣabheṇa bhānunā: This translates to "Drink the soma, O strong one, with the light of the sun." Soma is often associated with divine nectar or inspiration. This can be interpreted as seeking knowledge and enlightenment (the light of the sun) to empower oneself. In real life, this can be likened to the pursuit of education and wisdom, which nourishes the mind and spirit.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of worship, determination, strength, sacrifice, and the pursuit of knowledge. Its significance lies in the reminder that all beings are interconnected in their quest for higher truths and that through resolute intentions and efforts, one can achieve their goals. In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as personal development, leadership, and community building, where individuals come together to support each other in their journeys towards growth and enlightenment.
