क्रीळन् नो रश्मा आ भुवः सम भस्मना वायुनā वेविदानः |तā अस्य सन् धृषजो न तिग्माः सुसंशितā वक्ष्यो वक्षणेष्ठाः ||
krīḻan no raśma ā bhuvaḥ sam bhasmanā vāyunā vevidānaḥ | tā asya san dhṛṣajo na tigmāḥ susaṁśitā vakṣyo vakṣaṇesthāḥ ||
  • Translation: The mantra can be translated as: "May the rays of light shine upon us, and may the winds carry us forward. Those who are steadfast and courageous, may they be protected and supported."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • krīḻan: To shine or illuminate.
    • no: Our or us.
    • raśma: Rays or beams of light.
    • ā bhuvaḥ: May they come forth or manifest.
    • sam: Together or in harmony.
    • bhasmanā: With brilliance or radiance.
    • vāyunā: By the winds or through the air.
    • vevidānaḥ: Those who are knowledgeable or wise.
    • tā: Those.
    • asya: Of this or this.
    • san: Being or existence.
    • dhṛṣajo: Courageous or steadfast.
    • na: Not.
    • tigmāḥ: Sharp or intense.
    • susaṁśitā: Well-protected or well-guarded.
    • vakṣyo: Those who are to be spoken of.
    • vakṣaṇesthāḥ: Those who are established in their purpose.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, this mantra can be applied to teams working together harmoniously, where the "rays of light" symbolize innovative ideas and collaboration that lead to success.
    • In personal development, individuals can reflect on the courage and steadfastness required to pursue their goals, drawing strength from the idea of being "well-protected" by their own determination and support systems.
    • In community building, the mantra emphasizes the importance of collective wisdom and courage, encouraging members to support each other in overcoming challenges.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for illumination, courage, and support. It serves as a reminder that in our personal and collective journeys, we should seek knowledge, work together harmoniously, and stand firm in the face of challenges. This mantra is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal growth to teamwork and community support, highlighting the importance of unity and resilience in achieving our goals.
