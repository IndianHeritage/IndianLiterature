अभि गावो अनूषत योषा जारम् इव प्रियम् |अगन्न आजिं यथा हितम् ||
abhi gāvo anūṣata yoṣā jāram iva priyam | agann ājiṁ yathā hitam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • abhi: towards, in the direction of
    • gāvo: cows, representing wealth and prosperity
    • anūṣata: to be cherished or loved
    • yoṣā: woman, symbolizing nurturing and care
    • jāram: old, aged, or wise
    • iva: like, as if
    • priyam: dear, beloved
    • agann: to eat, to consume
    • ājiṁ: food, nourishment
    • yathā: as, in the manner of
    • hitam: beneficial, good
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks to the importance of cherishing what is dear to us, whether it be wealth, relationships, or wisdom.
    • The imagery of cows represents prosperity and abundance, which are to be valued and nurtured.
    • The mention of a woman signifies the nurturing aspect of life, emphasizing the role of care and love in our relationships.
    • The comparison of the old to something beloved suggests that wisdom and experience are to be honored and respected.
    • Finally, the mantra highlights the importance of consuming what is beneficial, both in terms of physical nourishment and emotional or spiritual sustenance.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a family setting, the mantra can be applied by valuing the elderly members who possess wisdom and experience, ensuring they are cared for and respected.
    • In a community, cherishing resources like livestock (cows) can symbolize the importance of sustainable practices that promote prosperity for all.
    • In personal relationships, nurturing connections with loved ones, akin to how one would care for a beloved pet or cherished possession, can lead to deeper bonds and mutual support.
    • In a professional context, this mantra can remind us to focus on beneficial practices and decisions that contribute to the well-being of the organization and its members.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call to appreciate and nurture what is valuable in our lives, whether it be relationships, wisdom, or resources.
    • It encourages us to create a harmonious environment where love, care, and respect are paramount, leading to a fulfilling and prosperous life.
    • In essence, this mantra serves as a reminder to consume and engage with what is truly beneficial, fostering a life of abundance and joy.
