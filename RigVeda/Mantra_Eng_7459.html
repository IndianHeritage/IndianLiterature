न त्वा बृहन्तो अद्रयो वरन्त इन्द्र वीळवः |यद दित्ससि स्तुवते मातवे वसु नकीष टद आमिनाति ते ||
na tvā bṛhanto adrayo varanta indra vīḻavaḥ | yad ditsasi stuvate māvate vasu nakiṣ ṭad ā mināti te ||
  • na tvā bṛhanto adrayo varanta indra vīḻavaḥ
    • na tvā - "not you" or "not for you"
    • bṛhanto - "the great ones" or "the powerful"
    • adrayo - "mountains" or "hills"
    • varanta - "who are strong" or "who are mighty"
    • indra - "Indra," the king of gods, associated with strength and power
    • vīḻavaḥ - "the ones who are victorious" or "the conquerors"
  • yad ditsasi stuvate māvate vasu nakiṣ ṭad ā mināti te
    • yad - "what" or "that which"
    • ditsasi - "you desire" or "you wish for"
    • stuvate - "they praise" or "they extol"
    • māvate - "to the one who is great" or "to the mighty one"
    • vasu - "wealth" or "prosperity"
    • nakiṣ - "not" or "no"
    • ṭad - "that" or "it"
    • ā mināti - "does not diminish" or "does not reduce"
    • te - "for you" or "to you"

This mantra can be interpreted as a prayer or invocation to Indra, acknowledging his power and strength while also expressing a desire for prosperity and abundance. The speaker emphasizes that the great ones, like the mountains, do not diminish the wealth or blessings that one seeks from Indra.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various contexts:

  • Personal Aspirations: Just as the mantra expresses a desire for wealth and prosperity, individuals can use it as a reminder to pursue their goals without fear of external limitations.
  • Community Support: The invocation of Indra can symbolize the collective strength of a community. When individuals come together to support each other, they can achieve greater prosperity.
  • Overcoming Challenges: The reference to mountains signifies obstacles. This mantra encourages individuals to seek strength and support from higher powers or the community when facing challenges.

Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for strength, support, and the pursuit of prosperity. It serves as a reminder that while challenges may arise, the desire for abundance and the support of the divine or community can lead to success. In everyday life, it encourages individuals to remain steadfast in their aspirations and to seek help when needed, reinforcing the idea that true strength comes from unity and faith.
