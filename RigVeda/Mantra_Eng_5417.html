शिक्षेयं इन महायते दिवे-दिवे राया आ कुहचिद्विदे |नहि त्वद अन्यन मघवान न आप्यं वस्यो अस्ति पिता चन ||
śikṣeyam in mahayate dive-dive rāya ā kuhacidvide | nahi tvad anyan maghavan na āpyaṁ vasyo asti pitā cana ||
  • śikṣeyam: This word translates to "I shall learn" or "I shall be educated." It emphasizes the importance of acquiring knowledge and wisdom.
  • in mahayate: This phrase means "in the great" or "in the vastness." It suggests that the pursuit of knowledge is a grand endeavor that encompasses the universe.
  • dive-dive: This term refers to "day by day" or "each day." It highlights the continuous nature of learning and growth.
  • rāya: This word means "wealth" or "prosperity." It signifies that knowledge is a form of wealth that enriches one's life.
  • ā kuhacidvide: This phrase can be interpreted as "wherever it may be." It suggests that knowledge can be found in various places and forms.
  • nahi tvad anyan: This translates to "there is no other than you." It emphasizes the idea that the ultimate source of knowledge and wisdom is within oneself or in a higher power.
  • maghavan: This word refers to "the generous one" or "the bountiful." It signifies the abundance that comes from knowledge and understanding.
  • na āpyaṁ: This means "there is no limit" or "there is no boundary." It indicates that the pursuit of knowledge is limitless.
  • vasyo asti: This phrase translates to "is under control" or "is governed." It suggests that knowledge allows one to have control over their life and circumstances.
  • pitā cana: This means "like a father" or "in a paternal way." It implies that knowledge nurtures and guides individuals, much like a father figure.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of continuous learning and the pursuit of knowledge as a form of wealth that enriches our lives. It teaches us that knowledge is not confined to a single source but can be found everywhere, and it is essential for personal growth and empowerment. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in educational settings, professional development, and personal endeavors where the quest for knowledge leads to greater understanding and control over one's life. By embracing the idea of lifelong learning, individuals can navigate challenges and opportunities with wisdom and confidence.
