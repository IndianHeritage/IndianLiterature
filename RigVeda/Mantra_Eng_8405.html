जायेव पत्याव अधि शेव मंहसे पज्रायā गर्भ श्रिणुहि ब्रवीमि ते |अन्तर वाणिषु प्र चरā सु जीवसे 'निन्द्यो वृजन सोम जागृहि ||
jāyeva patyāv adhi śeva maṁhase pajrāyā garbha śṛṇuhi bravīmi te | antar vāṇīṣu pra carā su jīvase 'nindyo vṛjane soma jāgṛhi ||
  • jāyeva patyāv adhi śeva maṁhase: This phrase can be interpreted as a call for the divine to bless the union of a couple, emphasizing the importance of harmony and love in a relationship. In real life, this can be seen in wedding ceremonies where couples seek blessings for a prosperous and loving marriage.
  • pajrāyā garbha śṛṇuhi bravīmi te: This part refers to the nurturing aspect of life, particularly the womb (garbha) and the blessings of fertility. It signifies the importance of family and the continuation of life. For example, in many cultures, the birth of a child is celebrated as a significant event, symbolizing hope and new beginnings.
  • antar vāṇīṣu pra carā su jīvase: This line speaks to the inner voice and the importance of communication in relationships. It suggests that one should listen to their inner self and communicate effectively with others. In everyday life, this can be applied in personal relationships, where open and honest communication fosters understanding and connection.
  • 'nindyo vṛjane soma jāgṛhi: This phrase can be interpreted as a call to awaken the divine essence (soma) within oneself, urging individuals to rise above negativity and embrace positivity. In practical terms, this can relate to personal development and self-improvement, where individuals strive to overcome challenges and cultivate a positive mindset.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates themes of love, family, communication, and personal growth. Its significance lies in its holistic approach to life, emphasizing the interconnectedness of relationships and the importance of nurturing both oneself and others. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as marriage, parenting, and personal development, encouraging individuals to seek harmony, communicate openly, and strive for positivity in their lives.
