अस्मभ्यं वाजिनीवासू मघवद्भ्यश्च सप्रथः |छर्दिर यन्तम अदाभ्यम् ||
asmabhyaṁ vājinīvasū maghavadbhyaś ca saprathaḥ | chardir yantam adābhyam ||
  • Mantra: "asmabhyaṁ vājinīvasū maghavadbhyaś ca saprathaḥ | chardir yantam adābhyam ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • asmabhyaṁ: for us
    • vājinīvasū: the powerful, the strong
    • maghavadbhyaś: from the wealthy, the prosperous
    • ca: and
    • saprathaḥ: together, in unity
    • chardir: prosperity, wealth
    • yantam: that which is moving or flowing
    • adābhyam: without obstacles, unhindered
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra invokes the blessings of strength and prosperity for the collective well-being of a group.
    • The term asmabhyaṁ emphasizes a communal approach, suggesting that the benefits are sought not just for the individual but for all.
    • vājinīvasū refers to the strength and power that can be harnessed when individuals come together.
    • maghavadbhyaś highlights the importance of wealth and prosperity, indicating that these blessings are desired from those who are already prosperous.
    • saprathaḥ signifies unity, suggesting that collective efforts yield better results.
    • chardir represents the wealth and prosperity that one seeks, while yantam indicates the flow of these blessings.
    • Finally, adābhyam assures that these blessings should come without any hindrances or obstacles.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, this mantra can be applied when teams work together towards a common goal, emphasizing the importance of collaboration and shared success.
    • In community projects, such as building a school or a hospital, the mantra reflects the collective effort of individuals coming together to create something beneficial for all.
    • In personal relationships, it can signify the importance of supporting one another in achieving mutual goals, whether they are financial, emotional, or social.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the power of unity and collective effort in achieving prosperity and overcoming obstacles.
    • Its significance lies in the understanding that individual success is often intertwined with the success of the community.
    • By invoking this mantra, individuals and groups can foster a spirit of collaboration, ensuring that their pursuits are fruitful and unhindered.
