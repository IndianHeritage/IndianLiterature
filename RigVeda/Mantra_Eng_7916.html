एष वृषा कणिक्रदद दशभिर जामिभिर यतः |अभि द्रोणानि धावति ||
eṣa vṛṣā kanikradad daśabhir jāmibhir yataḥ | abhi droṇāni dhāvati ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • eṣa: this
    • vṛṣā: bull or strength
    • kanikradad: roaring or making a sound
    • daśabhir: with ten
    • jāmibhir: with the offspring or progeny
    • yataḥ: from where
    • abhi: towards
    • droṇāni: the containers or vessels
    • dhāvati: runs or moves
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of a powerful entity, often interpreted as a bull, which is associated with strength and vitality.
    • The phrase "kanikradad" suggests a loud roar, symbolizing the assertiveness and dominance of this strength.
    • The mention of "daśabhir jāmibhir" indicates that this strength is supported by ten offspring, which can be seen as a metaphor for various forms of support or resources that enhance one's power.
    • The term "abhi droṇāni dhāvati" implies that this strength is directed towards certain goals or containers, suggesting a purposeful movement towards achieving objectives.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, a strong leader (the bull) may lead a team (the ten offspring) towards achieving a significant project (the containers) with determination and assertiveness (the roar).
    • In sports, a team that works cohesively (the offspring) under a strong coach (the bull) can achieve victory (the containers) through strategic plays and teamwork (the roar).
    • In personal development, an individual may harness their inner strength (the bull) and utilize their skills and resources (the offspring) to pursue their goals (the containers) with confidence and determination (the roar).
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of strength, support, and purposeful action in achieving one's goals.
    • It serves as a reminder that true power often comes from a combination of individual strength and the support of others.
    • In real life, whether in personal endeavors, professional settings, or community efforts, harnessing one's strength and working collaboratively can lead to significant achievements.
