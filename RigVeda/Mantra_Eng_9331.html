मां देवाऽ दधिरे हव्यवाहम् अपाम्लुक्तम् बहु कृत्स्ना चरन्तम् | अग्निर विद्वान् यज्ञं नः कल्पयाति पञ्चयामं त्रिवृतं सप्ततन्तुम् ||
māṁ devā dadhire havyavāham apamluktam bahu kṛcchrā carantam | agnir vidvān yajñaṁ naḥ kalpayāti pañcayāmaṁ trivṛtaṁ saptatantum ||
  • māṁ devā dadhire: This phrase translates to "the gods have placed me." It signifies the divine support and guidance that one receives in life. In real life, this can be seen in moments when individuals feel a sense of purpose or direction, often attributed to a higher power or the universe guiding them.
  • havyavāham apamluktam: This means "the one who carries offerings, untainted." It emphasizes purity in intentions and actions. For example, in a community service project, when volunteers work selflessly, they embody this principle by offering their time and effort without any ulterior motives.
  • bahu kṛcchrā carantam: This translates to "wandering through many difficulties." It acknowledges the struggles and challenges faced in life. A real-life example could be an individual overcoming personal hardships, such as financial difficulties or health issues, demonstrating resilience and perseverance.
  • agnir vidvān yajñaṁ naḥ kalpayāti: This means "the wise fire prepares the sacrifice for us." Here, 'fire' symbolizes transformation and energy. In practical terms, this can relate to how individuals channel their passions and efforts into productive endeavors, such as pursuing education or career goals, which ultimately lead to personal growth and success.
  • pañcayāmaṁ trivṛtaṁ saptatantum: This phrase can be interpreted as "we aim for the fivefold, the threefold, and the seventy." It suggests a holistic approach to life, encompassing various aspects such as physical, mental, and spiritual well-being. For instance, a balanced lifestyle that includes exercise, mindfulness, and social connections reflects this principle.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of divine guidance, purity of intention, resilience in the face of challenges, the transformative power of passion, and the importance of a balanced life. Its significance lies in reminding individuals to seek divine support, act with integrity, embrace challenges, channel their energies wisely, and strive for holistic well-being. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire people to lead purposeful lives, contribute positively to their communities, and cultivate personal growth amidst adversity.
