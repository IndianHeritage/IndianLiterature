अस्मिन न इन्द्र पृत्सुतौ यशस्वति शिमीवति क्रन्दसि प्राव सातये |यत्र गोषाटां धृतिषु खादिषु विश्वक पतन्ति दिद्यवो नृषाह्ये ||
asmin na indra pṛtsutau yaśasvati śimīvati krandasi prāva sātaye | yatra goṣātā dhṛṣiteṣu khādiṣu viṣvak patanti didyavo nṛṣāhye ||
  • Mantra: "asmin na indra pṛtsutau yaśasvati śimīvati krandasi prāva sātaye | yatra goṣātā dhṛṣiteṣu khādiṣu viṣvak patanti didyavo nṛṣāhye ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • asmin: in this
    • na: not
    • indra: Indra, the king of gods
    • pṛtsutau: in the great deeds
    • yaśasvati: glorious
    • śimīvati: who is auspicious
    • krandasi: you cry out
    • prāva: for the sake of
    • sātaye: truth or righteousness
    • yatra: where
    • goṣātā: the cows (or wealth)
    • dhṛṣiteṣu: in the brave
    • khādiṣu: in the eaters
    • viṣvak: all
    • patanti: fall or descend
    • didyavo: the powerful
    • nṛṣāhye: among men
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the presence of Indra, the deity associated with strength, rain, and thunder, emphasizing the importance of glorious deeds and auspiciousness.
    • The phrase "you cry out for the sake of truth" suggests a call for righteousness and integrity in actions.
    • The mention of "where the cows (wealth) are in the brave" indicates that true wealth and prosperity come to those who are courageous and act with valor.
    • The overall essence of the mantra is a plea for divine support in achieving success through righteous actions and bravery.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, a leader who embodies the qualities of Indra—strength, courage, and integrity—can inspire their team to achieve great success. For instance, a CEO who stands firm in ethical practices during a crisis can lead their company to emerge stronger.
    • In personal life, individuals who face challenges with bravery and uphold their values often find themselves rewarded, whether through personal growth or community respect. For example, a whistleblower who exposes corruption may face difficulties but ultimately contributes to a more just society.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the significance of courage and righteousness in our lives. It encourages individuals to seek divine guidance in their endeavors and to act with integrity.
    • In real-life scenarios, whether in leadership, personal challenges, or community involvement, embodying the principles of bravery and truth can lead to meaningful success and fulfillment.
    • Ultimately, the mantra highlights that true prosperity is not just material wealth but also the richness of character and the impact one has on others.
