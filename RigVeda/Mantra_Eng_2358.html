इमं स्वस्मै हृदा आसुतष्टं मन्त्रं वोचेम कुविदस्य वेदत् |अपां नपाद असुर्यस्य मह्ना विश्वानि आर्यो भुवना जजाना ||
imaṁ sv asmai hṛda ā sutaṣṭam mantraṁ vocema kuvid asya vedat | apāṁ napād asuryasya mahnā viśvāny aryo bhuvanā jajāna ||
  • imaṁ sv asmai hṛda ā sutaṣṭam: This phrase can be translated to "This mantra, we speak with a heart that is well-prepared." It emphasizes the importance of intention and purity of heart when reciting mantras. In real life, this can be likened to the idea that when we approach any task or challenge with sincerity and a clear mind, we are more likely to succeed.
  • mantraṁ vocema kuvid asya vedat: This means "We speak this mantra, which is known to those who understand." It suggests that knowledge and understanding are crucial for the effective use of mantras. In practical terms, this can be related to the importance of education and wisdom in making informed decisions in life.
  • apāṁ napād asuryasya mahnā: This translates to "The great one, the sun, who is the source of all waters." Here, the sun symbolizes life and energy, while water represents sustenance and nourishment. In everyday life, this can be seen in the way sunlight and water are essential for agriculture, which in turn sustains human life.
  • viśvāny aryo bhuvanā jajāna: This means "All noble beings have been born in this world." It highlights the idea that every individual has the potential for greatness and nobility. In a real-world context, this can be related to the belief in the inherent worth and potential of every person, encouraging us to uplift and support one another.

  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of intention, knowledge, and the interconnectedness of life. It teaches us that approaching life with a pure heart and a quest for understanding can lead to greater fulfillment and success. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various aspects such as personal growth, education, and community support, reminding us of our shared humanity and the potential for greatness within each individual.
