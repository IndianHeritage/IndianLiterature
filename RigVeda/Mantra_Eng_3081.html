यस्तुभ्यं अग्नेमृताय दाशद दुवास तवे कृत्नवते यतस्रुक् |न स राया शशमानो वियोषन् नैनम अंहः परि वरद अग्हायोः ||
yas tubhyam agne amṛtāya dāśad duvas tve kṛṇavate yatasruk | na sa rāyā śaśamāno vi yoṣan nainam aṁhaḥ pari varad aghāyoḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yas: who
    • tubhyam: to you
    • agne: O Agni (the fire god)
    • amṛtāya: for immortality
    • dāśad: grant
    • duvas: two days
    • tve: to you
    • kṛṇavate: who brings
    • yata: from where
    • sruk: the offerings
    • na: not
    • sa: he
    • rāyā: wealth
    • śaśamāno: who is prosperous
    • vi: indeed
    • yoṣan: woman
    • nainam: not this
    • aṁhaḥ: darkness
    • pari: around
    • varad: grant
    • aghāyoḥ: of the sin
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Agni, the fire god, seeking blessings for immortality and prosperity.
    • The phrase "O Agni, grant us two days" can be interpreted as a request for a short period of time to achieve our goals, emphasizing the importance of time in our endeavors.
    • The mention of "not being surrounded by darkness" signifies the desire for clarity and enlightenment in life, steering clear of ignorance and negativity.
    • The reference to "wealth" and "prosperity" highlights the importance of material and spiritual abundance in one's life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be applied when seeking success in a new venture. Entrepreneurs often pray for guidance and clarity to navigate challenges, much like the invocation to Agni for blessings.
    • In personal life, individuals may recite this mantra during significant life transitions, such as starting a new job or moving to a new city, to seek clarity and prosperity.
    • In spiritual practices, this mantra can be used in rituals to invoke positive energy and dispel negativity, fostering an environment conducive to growth and success.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its holistic approach to life, encompassing the physical, emotional, and spiritual realms.
    • It serves as a reminder to seek clarity and prosperity while being mindful of the time we have to achieve our goals.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra encourages individuals to cultivate a positive mindset, embrace opportunities, and strive for both material and spiritual wealth.
