तवायम् भाग ऋत्वियः सरश्मिः सूर्ये सचा |अध्वर्युभिर भरमानā अयंसत वायो शुक्रा अयंसत ||
tavāyam bhāga ṛtviyaḥ saraśmiḥ sūrye sacā | adhvaryubhir bharamāṇā ayaṁsata vāyo śukrā ayaṁsata ||
  • tavāyam bhāga ṛtviyaḥ: This phrase can be translated as "This is your share, O Radiant One." Here, "tavāyam" refers to "your" and "bhāga" means "share" or "portion." The term "ṛtviyaḥ" signifies something that is in accordance with the cosmic order or truth.
  • saraśmiḥ sūrye sacā: This part translates to "the rays of the sun." "Saraśmiḥ" refers to "rays" or "light," and "sūrye" means "of the sun." The word "sacā" can be interpreted as "and also," indicating a connection to the divine light.
  • adhvaryubhir bharamāṇā: This phrase means "being carried by the priests." "Adhvaryubhir" refers to the priests who perform rituals, and "bharamāṇā" means "being carried" or "supported." This indicates the importance of rituals in channeling divine energy.
  • ayaṁsata vāyo śukrā: This translates to "may the pure winds come." "Ayaṁsata" means "may they come," "vāyo" refers to "winds" or "air," and "śukrā" signifies "pure" or "bright." This suggests a desire for purity and clarity in life.
  • ayaṁsata: The repetition of "ayaṁsata" emphasizes the urgency and importance of the request for the arrival of these pure winds.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda emphasizes the connection between the divine, the natural elements, and the rituals performed by priests. It signifies the importance of seeking divine blessings and the purity of intentions in our actions. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios such as:

  • In personal rituals or prayers, where individuals seek clarity and purity in their thoughts and actions.
  • In environmental practices, where the "pure winds" can symbolize clean air and a healthy environment, urging us to take care of nature.
  • In community gatherings or ceremonies, where the collective energy and intentions can lead to positive outcomes.

This mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of all elements in life and the importance of maintaining harmony with the universe.
