आ वंसते मगवाऽ वीरवत् यशः समिद्धो द्युम्न्य आहुतः |कुविन नो अस्य सुमतिर नवीयस्य अच् छा वाजेभिर आगमत् ||
ā vaṁsate maghavā vīravad yaśaḥ samiddho dyumny āhutaḥ | kuvin no asya sumatir navīyasy acchā vājebhir āgamat ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā vaṁsate: "He who is born from us" or "He who is derived from us."
    • maghavā: "the powerful" or "the mighty one."
    • vīravad: "like a hero" or "with valor."
    • yaśaḥ: "fame" or "glory."
    • samiddho: "kindled" or "ignited."
    • dyumny: "in brilliance" or "in splendor."
    • āhutaḥ: "invoked" or "called upon."
    • kuvin: "may we have" or "may we receive."
    • no: "our."
    • asya: "of this."
    • sumatir: "good thought" or "wise understanding."
    • navīyasy: "of the new" or "of the fresh."
    • acchā: "may it come" or "may it arrive."
    • vājebhir: "with strength" or "with vigor."
    • āgamat: "may it come" or "may it arrive."
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of invoking a powerful and glorious force that is born from the collective strength and valor of individuals.
    • It emphasizes the importance of good thoughts and wisdom, suggesting that these qualities are essential for achieving success and glory.
    • The phrase "kindled in brilliance" indicates that this force is not only powerful but also radiant and inspiring.
    • By calling upon this force, the mantra encourages individuals to seek strength and wisdom in their endeavors.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a team setting, such as a workplace or sports team, the collective effort and valor of each member can lead to shared success. The mantra can be seen as a reminder to harness this collective strength.
    • In personal development, focusing on good thoughts and wise decisions can lead to a more fulfilling and successful life. The mantra encourages individuals to cultivate these qualities.
    • In community initiatives, invoking the spirit of collaboration and shared goals can lead to significant achievements, reflecting the essence of this mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for collective strength, wisdom, and the pursuit of glory through good thoughts and actions.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, including teamwork, personal growth, and community efforts, reminding us of the power of unity and positive thinking.
    • By embodying the principles of this mantra, individuals and groups can strive for greatness and achieve their goals with valor and wisdom.
