आयांसम अग्ने सुख्षितिं जनायायांसम उ मघवद्भ्यः सुवृक्तिम् |विश्वं तद भद्रं यद अवन्ति देवाः बृहद् वदेम विदथे सुवीराः ||
ayāṁsam agne sukṣitiṁ janāyāyāṁsam u maghavadbhyaḥ suvṛktim | viśvaṁ tad bhadraṁ yad avanti devā bṛhad vadema vidathe suvīrāḥ ||
  • ayāṁsam agne: This phrase can be translated as "O Agni," where Agni represents the fire god, a central figure in Vedic rituals and symbolism. Fire is often associated with purification and transformation.
  • sukṣitiṁ janāyā: This means "the well-being of the people." It emphasizes the importance of collective welfare and prosperity in society.
  • u maghavadbhyaḥ: This translates to "and to the mighty ones." It refers to the powerful beings or deities who are capable of bestowing blessings and abundance.
  • suvṛktim: This word means "good speech" or "auspicious utterance." It highlights the significance of positive communication and the power of words in shaping reality.
  • viśvaṁ tad bhadraṁ: This translates to "that which is universal and auspicious." It signifies the interconnectedness of all beings and the importance of promoting good for everyone.
  • yad avanti devā: This means "that which the gods protect." It suggests that divine forces are involved in safeguarding the well-being of humanity.
  • bṛhad vadema: This translates to "let us speak broadly." It encourages expansive thinking and the sharing of wisdom and knowledge.
  • vidathe suvīrāḥ: This means "in the assembly of the brave." It refers to gatherings where courageous individuals come together to discuss and act for the common good.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of collective well-being, positive communication, and the role of divine forces in protecting humanity. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in community gatherings, discussions aimed at social welfare, and initiatives that promote unity and prosperity. By invoking the blessings of Agni and the mighty ones, individuals and communities can strive for a harmonious and prosperous existence, fostering an environment where good speech and collective action lead to positive outcomes for all.
