ते मन्वत प्रथमं नाम धेनोस त्रिः सप्त मातुः परमाणि विन्दन |तज जानतीर अभ्य अनूषत व्राऽ आविर्भुवद अरुणीर यशसा गोः ||
te manvata prathamaṁ nāma dhenos triḥ sapta mātuḥ paramāṇi vindan | taj jānatīr abhy anūṣata vrā āvir bhuvad aruṇīr yaśasā goḥ ||
  • te manvata prathamaṁ: This phrase can be translated as "they who are the first among the thinkers." It emphasizes the importance of intellect and wisdom in guiding actions and decisions.
  • nāma dhenos triḥ sapta: This part refers to "the name of the cow, thrice seven." In Vedic culture, the cow is a symbol of wealth, nourishment, and abundance. The number seven often signifies completeness or perfection.
  • mātuḥ paramāṇi vindan: This translates to "finding the ultimate essence of the mother." Here, 'mother' symbolizes the source of life and sustenance, which can be interpreted as nature or the Earth itself.
  • taj jānatīr abhy anūṣata: This means "those who know that, do not stray." It highlights the importance of knowledge and understanding in maintaining a righteous path in life.
  • vrā āvir bhuvad aruṇīr: This can be interpreted as "may the radiant one manifest." It suggests the emergence of enlightenment and clarity, guiding individuals towards truth and righteousness.
  • yaśasā goḥ: This phrase translates to "by the glory of the cow." It reinforces the idea that prosperity and success are linked to respect for nature and its resources.

  • Significance: This mantra encapsulates the essence of wisdom, respect for nature, and the pursuit of knowledge. It serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of life and the importance of nurturing our environment.
  • Real-life Application: In today's world, this mantra can inspire individuals to engage in sustainable practices, such as organic farming, conservation efforts, and promoting animal welfare. It encourages a lifestyle that honors the Earth and its resources, leading to a more harmonious existence.

  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the value of wisdom, the significance of nature, and the pursuit of knowledge. By applying these principles in our daily lives, we can foster a deeper connection with the world around us, leading to personal growth and a more sustainable future.
