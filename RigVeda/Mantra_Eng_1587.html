समानं वत्सम् अभि संचरन्ती विश्वग् धेनू वि चरतः सुमेके |अनपवृज्यां अध्वनो मिमाने विश्वान केतां अधि महो दधाने ||
samānaṁ vatsam abhi saṁcarantī viṣvag dhenū vi carataḥ sumeke | anapavṛjyām̐ adhvano mimāne viśvān ketām̐ adhi maho dadhāne ||
  • samānaṁ vatsam abhi saṁcarantī: This phrase can be translated as "the cows (or beings) that move together." Here, "samānaṁ" means "together" or "similar," and "vatsam" refers to "calves" or "young ones." This can symbolize unity and cooperation in a community.
  • viṣvag dhenū vi carataḥ sumeke: This translates to "the universal cows that roam freely." "Viṣvag" means "universal" or "all-encompassing," while "dhenū" refers to "cows" or "nourishing beings." This can represent the idea of abundance and the nurturing aspects of nature that provide for all living beings.
  • anapavṛjyām̐ adhvano mimāne: This means "on the unimpeded path." "Anapavṛjyām̐" signifies something that is unobstructed or free from hindrance, while "adhvano" refers to "path" or "way." This can symbolize the journey of life that is clear and free from obstacles.
  • viśvān ketām̐ adhi maho dadhāne: This translates to "those who hold the greatness of all." "Viśvān" means "all" or "universal," "ketām̐" refers to "greatness" or "intelligence," and "maho" means "great." This can signify the importance of wisdom and understanding in achieving greatness in life.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the themes of unity, abundance, unobstructed paths, and the pursuit of greatness. In real life, these concepts can be applied in various scenarios such as teamwork in professional settings, community support during challenging times, and the importance of maintaining a clear vision in personal goals. By fostering cooperation and nurturing relationships, individuals can create a harmonious environment that leads to collective success and fulfillment. This mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of all beings and the significance of wisdom in navigating life's journey.
