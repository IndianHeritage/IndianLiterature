अहं सोऽस्मि यः पुरा सुते वदामि काणि चित् |तं मां व्यन्त्याध्यो वृको न तृष्णजं मृगं वित्तं मे अस्य रोधसी ||
ahaṁ so asmi yaḥ purā sute vadāmi kāni cit | tam mā vyanty ādhyo3 vṛko na tṛṣṇajam mṛgaṁ vittam me asya rodasī ||
  • ahaṁ so asmi: This phrase translates to "I am that." It signifies a deep connection between the self and the universe, suggesting that the individual is not separate from the cosmos but is an integral part of it.
  • yaḥ purā sute vadāmi: This means "who I speak of in the past." It indicates a reference to ancient wisdom or knowledge that has been passed down through generations.
  • kāni cit: This translates to "whatever it may be." It reflects an openness to all possibilities and experiences, emphasizing the importance of acceptance in life.
  • tam mā vyanty: This means "may that not abandon me." It expresses a desire for connection and support from the divine or the universe, highlighting the human need for guidance.
  • ādhyo3 vṛko: This translates to "the rich wolf." The wolf often symbolizes strength, intelligence, and the ability to navigate challenges. In this context, it may represent the inner strength one must harness to overcome obstacles.
  • na tṛṣṇajam mṛgaṁ: This means "not the thirsting creature." It suggests a rejection of insatiable desires and urges, advocating for a more balanced and content approach to life.
  • vittam me asya rodasī: This translates to "wealth is mine in this world." It signifies the importance of recognizing and appreciating the abundance that exists in one's life, both materially and spiritually.

Conclusion: This mantra encapsulates the essence of self-awareness, connection to the universe, and the pursuit of inner strength while rejecting insatiable desires. In real-life scenarios, it encourages individuals to recognize their place in the cosmos, embrace their experiences, and seek guidance from the universe. It serves as a reminder to appreciate the wealth of knowledge and abundance around us, fostering a sense of gratitude and contentment. By applying these principles, one can navigate life's challenges with resilience and a deeper understanding of their interconnectedness with all beings.
