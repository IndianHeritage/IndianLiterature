शशमानस्य वा नरः स्वेदस्य सत्यशवसः |विदा कामस्य वेनतः ||
śaśamānasya vā naraḥ svedasya satyaśavasaḥ | vidā kāmasya venataḥ ||
  • śaśamānasya: This term can be broken down into "śaśa," meaning "hare" or "rabbit," and "mānasya," which refers to "mind" or "thought." Together, it suggests a state of mind that is gentle, soft, or peaceful, akin to the nature of a hare.
  • vā: This is a conjunction meaning "or," indicating an alternative or choice in the context of the mantra.
  • naraḥ: This word means "man" or "human being." It signifies the subject of the mantra, focusing on human qualities and experiences.
  • svedasya: Derived from "sveda," which means "sweat" or "effort." This term implies the physical exertion or labor that a person undergoes.
  • satyaśavasaḥ: This compound word consists of "satya," meaning "truth," and "śavasaḥ," which can be interpreted as "possessor" or "one who embodies." Thus, it refers to someone who embodies truthfulness or integrity.
  • vidā: This term means "knowledge" or "wisdom." It emphasizes the importance of understanding and insight in human life.
  • kāmasya: This word translates to "of desire" or "of wishes." It highlights the human experience of longing and aspiration.
  • venataḥ: This term can be understood as "of the one who is wise" or "of the one who is knowledgeable." It suggests a connection to wisdom and discernment in fulfilling desires.

In summary, the mantra "śaśamānasya vā naraḥ svedasya satyaśavasaḥ | vidā kāmasya venataḥ ||" speaks to the qualities of a human being who embodies a gentle and peaceful mind, engages in effort, and possesses truthfulness. It emphasizes the importance of wisdom in navigating desires and aspirations.


Significance and Real-Life Application:

  • This mantra encourages individuals to cultivate a peaceful and gentle mindset, akin to the nature of a hare, which can be applied in stressful situations where calmness is needed.
  • It highlights the value of hard work and effort, reminding us that success often requires dedication and perseverance.
  • The emphasis on truthfulness serves as a moral compass, guiding individuals to act with integrity in their personal and professional lives.
  • Lastly, the mantra underscores the importance of wisdom in fulfilling desires, suggesting that one should seek knowledge and understanding before pursuing their aspirations.

Overall, this mantra serves as a reminder of the qualities that lead to a fulfilling and meaningful life, encouraging individuals to embody peace, truth, and wisdom in their daily actions and decisions.
