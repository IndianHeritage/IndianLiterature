अभिभूर् अहम् आगमं विश्वकर्मेण धाम्ना |आ वश् चित्तम् आ वो व्रतम् आ वोऽहम् समितिं ददे ||
abhibhūr aham āgamaṁ viśvakarmeṇa dhāmnā | ā vaś cittam ā vo vratam ā vo 'haṁ samitiṁ dade ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • abhibhūr: I am the one who overcomes or prevails.
    • aham: I.
    • āgamaṁ: The arrival or coming.
    • viśvakarmeṇa: With all actions or deeds.
    • dhāmnā: With the essence or abode.
    • ā: May.
    • vaś: Control or mastery.
    • cittam: Mind or consciousness.
    • ā: May.
    • vo: Your.
    • vratam: Vow or commitment.
    • ā: May.
    • vo: Your.
    • haṁ: I.
    • samitiṁ: Assembly or gathering.
    • dade: I give or bestow.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra expresses a sense of empowerment and control over one's actions and thoughts.
    • The speaker asserts their ability to overcome challenges and emphasizes the importance of collective commitment and unity.
    • It highlights the significance of aligning one's mind and actions with a higher purpose or vow.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, a leader may use this mantra to inspire their team to work together towards a common goal, emphasizing the importance of unity and shared commitment.
    • In personal development, individuals can reflect on this mantra to cultivate a mindset of resilience and determination, reminding themselves of their ability to overcome obstacles.
    • In community initiatives, this mantra can serve as a rallying cry for collective action, encouraging individuals to come together and support a cause.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for self-empowerment, unity, and commitment to a higher purpose.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal growth to teamwork and community efforts.
    • By embracing the essence of this mantra, individuals and groups can foster a sense of purpose and resilience in their endeavors.
