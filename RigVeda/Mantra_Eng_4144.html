तं वः शर्धं रथे शुभं त्वेषं पनस्युम् आ हुवे |यस्मिन् सुजातā सुभगा महीयते सचा मरुत्सु मीळ्हुषी ||
taṁ vaḥ śardhaṁ ratheśubhaṁ tveṣam panasyum ā huve | yasmin sujātā subhagā mahīyate sacā marutsu mīḻhuṣī ||
  • taṁ vaḥ - This phrase translates to "that we invoke" or "we call upon you." It signifies a plea or invocation to a higher power or divine entity.
  • śardhaṁ - This word means "together" or "in unity." It emphasizes the importance of collective effort and harmony among individuals.
  • ratheśubhaṁ - This term can be broken down into "ratha" (chariot) and "śubha" (auspicious or good). It refers to the auspiciousness of the chariot, symbolizing progress and movement towards a positive goal.
  • tveṣam - This means "your" or "to you," indicating a direct connection to the divine or the entity being invoked.
  • panasyum - This word can be interpreted as "prosperity" or "wealth." It highlights the desire for abundance and success in life.
  • ā huve - This translates to "we call" or "we invoke," reinforcing the act of seeking blessings or guidance from the divine.
  • yasmin - This means "in which" or "where," setting the context for the following qualities or attributes.
  • sujātā - This term means "well-born" or "noble," referring to qualities of virtue and righteousness.
  • subhagā - This translates to "auspicious" or "fortunate," indicating a state of well-being and positivity.
  • mahīyate - This means "is magnified" or "is exalted," suggesting the elevation of the qualities mentioned before.
  • sacā - This word means "and" or "with," linking the qualities together.
  • marutsu - This term refers to "the winds" or "the forces of nature," symbolizing the dynamic and ever-changing aspects of life.
  • mīḻhuṣī - This means "to be nourished" or "to thrive," indicating growth and prosperity in life.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of unity, auspiciousness, and the invocation of divine blessings for prosperity and well-being. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various contexts such as teamwork in professional settings, community gatherings, or family functions where collective effort and harmony are essential for achieving common goals. By invoking positive energies and seeking blessings, individuals can foster an environment conducive to growth and success, both personally and collectively.
