आ यद योनीं हिरण्ययं वरुण मित्र सदथः |धर्तारा चर्षणीनां यन्तं सुम्नं ऋशादसाः ||
ā yad yoniṁ hiraṇyayaṁ varuṇa mitra sadathaḥ | dhartārā carṣaṇīnāṁ yantaṁ sumnaṁ riśādasā ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā yad: Whatever
    • yoniṁ: Source or womb
    • hiraṇyayaṁ: Golden or precious
    • varuṇa: The deity associated with cosmic order and water
    • mitra: Friend or ally
    • sadathaḥ: With stability or support
    • dhartārā: Those who uphold or support
    • carṣaṇīnāṁ: Of the earth or the ground
    • yantaṁ: That which is directed or guided
    • sumnaṁ: Auspicious or favorable
    • riśādasā: Those who are free from obstacles or difficulties
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks to the interconnectedness of all beings and the divine forces that guide and support them.
    • The mention of "hiraṇyayaṁ" suggests the value of purity and richness in our intentions and actions.
    • "Varuṇa" and "Mitra" symbolize the cosmic order and friendship, indicating the importance of harmony in relationships.
    • The phrase "dhartārā carṣaṇīnāṁ" emphasizes the role of those who support and nurture the earth, highlighting environmental stewardship.
    • The mantra encourages individuals to seek auspiciousness and to be free from obstacles, promoting a positive mindset.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community, when people come together to support each other, they embody the essence of "Mitra" and "Varuṇa," fostering a sense of belonging and cooperation.
    • Environmental initiatives, such as tree planting or conservation efforts, reflect the idea of "dhartārā," as individuals work to uphold and protect the earth.
    • In personal development, focusing on positive intentions and actions can lead to a more fulfilling life, aligning with the mantra's call for auspiciousness and overcoming obstacles.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of community, environmental responsibility, and positive intentions in our lives.
    • Its significance lies in promoting harmony, support, and auspiciousness, which are essential for personal and collective well-being.
    • In real-life scenarios, applying the principles of this mantra can lead to stronger relationships, a healthier planet, and a more positive outlook on life.
