देवान् हुवे बृहच्छ्रवसः स्वस्तये ज्योतिष्कृतो अध्वरस्य प्रचेतसः |ये वावृधुः प्रतरं विश्ववेदस इन्द्रज्येष्ठासो अमृताः ऋतावृधः ||
devān huve bṛhacchravasaḥ svastaye jyotiṣkṛto adhvarasya pracetasaḥ | ye vāvṛdhuḥ prataraṁ viśvavedasa indrajyeṣṭhāso amṛtā ṛtāvṛdhaḥ ||
  • devān huve bṛhacchravasaḥ: This phrase can be translated as "I invoke the great and glorious deities." Here, the speaker is calling upon the divine beings, emphasizing their greatness and the power they hold.
  • svastaye: This word means "for well-being" or "for prosperity." It signifies the intention behind the invocation, which is to seek blessings for health, happiness, and success.
  • jyotiṣkṛto adhvarasya pracetasaḥ: This translates to "the one who is the source of light and the one who is aware of the rituals." It highlights the importance of knowledge and enlightenment in performing rituals, suggesting that true understanding leads to successful outcomes.
  • ye vāvṛdhuḥ prataraṁ viśvavedasa: This means "those who have attained the knowledge of the universe." It refers to individuals who have gained wisdom and insight, indicating that knowledge is a key aspect of spiritual and worldly success.
  • indrajyeṣṭhāso amṛtā ṛtāvṛdhaḥ: This can be interpreted as "those who are the best among the powerful, who are immortal and who thrive in truth." It emphasizes the qualities of strength, immortality, and adherence to truth, which are essential for achieving greatness.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a profound invocation for divine blessings aimed at achieving well-being, knowledge, and strength. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various contexts such as personal development, community welfare, and spiritual practices. For instance, individuals may recite this mantra during rituals or meditation to seek guidance and support from higher powers, fostering a sense of connection with the divine. Additionally, it serves as a reminder of the importance of knowledge and truth in our pursuits, encouraging us to strive for enlightenment and integrity in our actions. Ultimately, the mantra signifies the interconnectedness of divine support, personal growth, and the pursuit of a meaningful life.
