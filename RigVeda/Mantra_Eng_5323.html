एष स्तोमो अचिक्रदद वृषा त उता स्तामुर मगवन्न अक्रपीष्ठा |रायस कामो जरीतारं त आगन त्वं अङ्ग शक्र वस्व आ शक नः ||
eṣa stomo acikradad vṛṣā ta uta stāmur maghavann akrapiṣṭa | rāyas kāmo jaritāraṁ ta āgan tvam aṅga śakra vasva ā śako naḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • eṣa: this
    • stomo: hymn or praise
    • acikradad: has cried out or called
    • vṛṣā: strong or powerful
    • ta: that
    • uta: or
    • stāmur: the one who is steadfast
    • maghavann: the mighty one
    • akrapiṣṭa: who is not to be defeated
    • rāyas: wealth or prosperity
    • kāmo: desire or wish
    • jaritāraṁ: the one who brings forth
    • ta: that
    • āgan: come
    • tvam: you
    • aṅga: O being
    • śakra: powerful or strong
    • vasva: all or entire
    • ā: for
    • śako: capable
    • naḥ: of us
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes a powerful hymn that calls upon a strong and mighty force.
    • The phrase "this hymn has cried out" suggests a deep yearning for connection with the divine or the powerful forces of nature.
    • It emphasizes the importance of steadfastness and resilience in the face of challenges.
    • The mention of wealth and desire indicates a longing for prosperity and fulfillment in life.
    • By addressing the divine as "O being," it acknowledges the presence of a higher power that can aid in achieving one's desires.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In times of struggle, individuals often turn to prayers or mantras to seek strength and guidance, similar to how this mantra calls upon a powerful force.
    • People may recite this mantra during rituals or ceremonies aimed at attracting prosperity and success in their endeavors.
    • In personal development, the concepts of resilience and steadfastness can be applied when facing obstacles in career or relationships.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the strength and support available to us from the divine and the universe.
    • It highlights the significance of resilience, the pursuit of desires, and the importance of invoking higher powers for assistance.
    • In real life, it encourages individuals to remain steadfast in their pursuits, seek prosperity, and connect with the divine for guidance and strength.
