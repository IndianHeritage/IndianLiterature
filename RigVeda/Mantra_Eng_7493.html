आयाम धīvato धियो 'र्वद्भिः शक्र गोदरे |जयेम पृत्सु वज्रिवः ||
ayāma dhīvato dhiyo 'rvadbhiḥ śakra godare | jayema pṛtsu vajrivaḥ ||
  • ayāma: This word can be interpreted as "let us" or "may we." It signifies a collective intention or desire.
  • dhīvato: This term refers to "those who are wise" or "intelligent." It emphasizes the importance of wisdom in decision-making and actions.
  • dhiyo: This word means "intellects" or "minds." It highlights the role of mental faculties in achieving goals.
  • 'rvadbhiḥ: This can be translated as "with strength" or "with vigor." It suggests the necessity of strength, both mental and physical, in overcoming challenges.
  • śakra: This term refers to "power" or "might." In a broader sense, it can also denote divine power or energy.
  • godare: This word means "in the waters" or "in the depths." It symbolizes the challenges or obstacles that one may face.
  • jayema: This translates to "we may conquer" or "we may triumph." It expresses a desire for victory and success.
  • pṛtsu: This word means "in the battles" or "in the struggles." It indicates the context in which one seeks victory.
  • vajrivaḥ: This term can be interpreted as "with the strength of a thunderbolt." It symbolizes immense power and resilience.

The mantra can be understood as a collective invocation for wisdom, strength, and victory in the face of challenges. It encourages individuals to harness their intellect and vigor to overcome obstacles and achieve success.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as:

  • Academic Pursuits: Students can invoke this mantra while preparing for exams, seeking wisdom and strength to conquer their academic challenges.
  • Professional Challenges: In the workplace, individuals can use this mantra to inspire teamwork and collective effort in overcoming project hurdles.
  • Personal Growth: Individuals facing personal struggles can reflect on this mantra to find the inner strength and wisdom needed to navigate life's difficulties.

In conclusion, this mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the importance of wisdom, strength, and collective effort in achieving success. It encourages individuals to face challenges with resilience and determination, making it applicable in various aspects of life, from education to personal development and professional endeavors.
