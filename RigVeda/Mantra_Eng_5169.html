आ याह्य अग्ने समिधानो अर्वाङ इन्द्रेण देवैः सरथं तुरेबिः |बरHIR न आस्ताम् अदितिः सुपुत्रा स्वाहा देवाः अमृताः मादयन्ताम् ||
ā yāhy agne samidhāno arvāṅ indreṇa devaiḥ sarathaṁ turebhiḥ | barhir na āstām aditiḥ suputrā svāhā devā amṛtā mādayantām ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā yāhy: O Agni, may you come forth.
    • agne: O Fire (Agni), the divine energy.
    • samidhāno: who is kindled or ignited.
    • arvāṅ: with strength or vigor.
    • indreṇa: by Indra, the king of gods.
    • devaiḥ: with the divine beings.
    • sarathaṁ: with chariots or vehicles.
    • turebhiḥ: with swift ones.
    • barhir: the sacred grass or the altar.
    • na āstām: may it not be absent.
    • aditiḥ: Aditi, the mother of gods.
    • suputrā: with good sons.
    • svāhā: an invocation or offering.
    • devā: the gods.
    • amṛtā: immortal or eternal.
    • mādayantām: may they be pleased or delighted.
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Agni, the fire god, who represents energy, transformation, and purification.
    • It invokes Agni to come forth with strength and vigor, symbolizing the importance of fire in rituals and daily life.
    • Indra, the king of gods, is mentioned, indicating the interconnectedness of divine forces in achieving goals.
    • The reference to "chariots" and "swift ones" suggests the need for speed and efficiency in our endeavors.
    • The mantra emphasizes the importance of family and lineage, as seen in the mention of Aditi and her good sons, highlighting the value of nurturing and supporting one another.
    • The invocation of the gods to be pleased signifies the importance of seeking divine blessings in our actions.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a modern context, this mantra can be related to the importance of teamwork and collaboration in achieving success, much like how the divine beings work together.
    • In personal life, it can be a reminder to nurture family relationships and support one another, akin to the blessings of Aditi.
    • In professional settings, invoking the spirit of Agni can symbolize the drive for innovation and transformation, encouraging individuals to embrace change and pursue their goals with vigor.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the interconnectedness of divine energies, the importance of family, and the need for strength and support in our endeavors.
    • Its significance lies in its ability to inspire individuals to seek divine blessings, foster relationships, and pursue their goals with determination and enthusiasm.
    • In real life, it encourages us to embrace transformation, work collaboratively, and nurture our connections with others, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling and harmonious existence.
