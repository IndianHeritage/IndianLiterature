दशावनिभ्यो दशकक्ष्येभ्यो दशयोग्त्रेभ्यो दशयोजनिभ्यः |दशाभीशुभ्यो अर्चताजरेभ्यो दश धुरो दश युक्ता वहद्भ्यः ||
daśāvanibhyo daśakakṣyebhyo daśayoktrebhyo daśayojanebhyaḥ | daśābhīśubhyo arcatājarebhyo daśa dhuro daśa yuktā vahadbhyaḥ ||
  • Mantra: "daśāvanibhyo daśakakṣyebhyo daśayoktrebhyo daśayojanebhyaḥ | daśābhīśubhyo arcatājarebhyo daśa dhuro daśa yuktā vahadbhyaḥ ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • daśāvanibhyo: From the ten directions
    • daśakakṣyebhyo: From the ten realms
    • daśayoktrebhyo: From the ten utterances
    • daśayojanebhyaḥ: From the ten journeys
    • daśābhīśubhyo: From the ten auspiciousness
    • arcatājarebhyo: To the worshippers
    • daśa: Ten
    • dhuro: Burdens
    • daśa yuktā: Ten who are united
    • vahadbhyaḥ: To those who carry
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra speaks of the ten directions, realms, utterances, and journeys, emphasizing the importance of unity and auspiciousness.
    • In real life, this can be related to teamwork and collaboration, where individuals from different backgrounds and skills come together to achieve a common goal.
    • For example, in a corporate setting, a project team may consist of members from various departments (marketing, finance, development) who contribute their unique perspectives and expertise to successfully complete a project.
    • The mention of "worshippers" signifies the importance of respect and acknowledgment of contributions from all members, fostering a positive environment.
    • The concept of "burdens" and "those who carry" can be interpreted as the responsibilities shared among team members, highlighting the need for support and cooperation.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the power of unity and collaboration in achieving success.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, such as workplaces, community projects, and even family dynamics, where collective effort leads to greater outcomes.
    • By recognizing and valuing each individual's contribution, we can create a harmonious and productive environment that benefits everyone involved.
