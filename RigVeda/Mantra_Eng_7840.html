अत्यूर्मिर matsaro मदः सोमः पवित्रे अर्षति |विघ्नान् रक्षांसी देवयुः ||
atyūrmir matsaro madaḥ somaḥ pavitre arṣati | vighnan rakṣāṁsi devayuḥ ||
  • atyūrmir: This word can be broken down into "atyūrm," which means "long-lived" or "immortal." It signifies the desire for longevity and vitality in life.
  • matsaro: This term translates to "jealous" or "envious." It reflects the human tendency to feel envy towards others' successes or possessions.
  • madaḥ: This word means "intoxication" or "ecstasy." It can refer to the blissful state that comes from spiritual experiences or the joy of life.
  • somaḥ: Soma is a sacred drink in Vedic tradition, often associated with immortality and divine inspiration. It symbolizes the pursuit of higher consciousness and spiritual awakening.
  • pavitre: This word means "pure" or "sacred." It emphasizes the importance of purity in thoughts, actions, and intentions.
  • arṣati: This term can be interpreted as "to shine" or "to radiate." It suggests the idea of enlightenment and the illumination of the mind.
  • vighnan: This word means "obstacles" or "hindrances." It acknowledges the challenges and difficulties one may face in life.
  • rakṣāṁsi: This term translates to "protection" or "guardians." It refers to the divine forces or energies that safeguard individuals from harm.
  • devayuḥ: This word means "divine beings" or "gods." It signifies the presence of higher powers that guide and support humanity.

In summary, this mantra speaks to the human experience of seeking longevity and joy while acknowledging the presence of envy and obstacles. It emphasizes the importance of purity and the pursuit of spiritual enlightenment, while also recognizing the protective forces of the divine. In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as:


  • In personal development, individuals can reflect on their desires for longevity and happiness, striving to overcome jealousy and embrace a more fulfilling life.
  • In spiritual practices, the mantra can serve as a reminder to seek purity in thoughts and actions, fostering a deeper connection with the divine.
  • In facing challenges, individuals can invoke the protective energies of the divine, seeking guidance and support in overcoming obstacles.

Overall, the significance of this mantra lies in its holistic approach to life, encouraging individuals to pursue a balanced existence filled with joy, purity, and divine protection.
