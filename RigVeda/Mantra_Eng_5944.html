यद एषाम् अन्यो अन्यस्य वाचं शक्तस्येव वदति शिक्षमानः |सर्वं तद एषां समृद्धेव पर्व यत सुवाचो वदथनाध्य अप्सु ||
yad eṣām anyo anyasya vācaṁ śāktasyeva vadati śikṣamāṇaḥ | sarvaṁ tad eṣāṁ samṛdheva parva yat suvāco vadathanādhy apsu ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yad: whatever
    • eṣām: of these
    • anyo: another
    • anyasya: of another
    • vācaṁ: speech
    • śāktasyeva: like the powerful
    • vadati: speaks
    • śikṣamāṇaḥ: learning
    • sarvaṁ: all
    • tad: that
    • eṣāṁ: of these
    • samṛdheva: like prosperity
    • parva: occasion
    • yat: which
    • suvāco: good speech
    • vadathanādhy: speaking
    • apsu: in the waters
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of speech and communication.
    • It suggests that when one learns to speak powerfully and effectively, they can influence others positively.
    • The reference to "waters" symbolizes the flow of knowledge and ideas, indicating that good communication can lead to prosperity and harmony.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace, effective communication can lead to better teamwork and project success. For instance, a manager who articulates goals clearly can inspire their team to achieve higher productivity.
    • In personal relationships, the ability to express oneself thoughtfully can resolve conflicts and strengthen bonds. For example, couples who communicate openly about their feelings tend to have healthier relationships.
    • In public speaking, leaders who convey their messages with clarity and passion can mobilize communities and drive social change, as seen in historical figures like Martin Luther King Jr.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its advocacy for the power of speech and communication.
    • It teaches us that mastering the art of communication can lead to personal and collective prosperity.
    • In real life, this mantra is applicable in various scenarios, from professional settings to personal relationships, highlighting that effective communication is essential for success and harmony.
