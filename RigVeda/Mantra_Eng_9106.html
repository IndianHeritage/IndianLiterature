एमा अग्मन रेवतीर जीवधन्या अध्वर्यवः साधयतā सखायः |नि बरहिषि धत्तन सोम्यासोऽपां नप्त्रā संविदानास एनाḥ ||
emā agman revatīr jīvadhanyā adhvaryavaḥ sādayatā sakhāyaḥ | ni barhiṣi dhattana somyāso 'pāṁ naptrā saṁvidānāsa enāḥ ||
  • emā agman revatīr: This phrase can be interpreted as "these (offerings) have come forth, bringing prosperity." It signifies the arrival of abundance and well-being.
  • jīvadhanyā adhvaryavaḥ: Translated, this means "the priests who are engaged in the rituals of life and wealth." It highlights the role of spiritual leaders in facilitating prosperity through rituals.
  • sādayatā sakhāyaḥ: This can be understood as "they who bring together friends." It emphasizes the importance of community and relationships in achieving success and happiness.
  • ni barhiṣi dhattana: This phrase means "in the sacred space, they hold the offerings." It reflects the significance of sacred rituals and the environment in which they are performed.
  • somyāso 'pāṁ naptrā: This translates to "the soothing essence of the offerings." It suggests that the offerings made during rituals have a calming and beneficial effect.
  • saṁvidānāsa enāḥ: This can be interpreted as "those who are aware of the divine order." It points to the understanding of cosmic laws and the interconnectedness of all beings.

  • Significance: This mantra encapsulates the essence of community, prosperity, and the role of rituals in enhancing life. It emphasizes the importance of spiritual practices in fostering relationships and well-being.
  • Real-life Application: In modern life, this mantra can be applied in various contexts such as community gatherings, family rituals, and even corporate events where teamwork and collaboration are essential. For instance, during a wedding ceremony, the rituals performed not only celebrate the union of two individuals but also strengthen the bonds among families and friends.

  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of life, the importance of community, and the role of rituals in achieving prosperity and harmony. By engaging in collective practices and fostering relationships, individuals can create a supportive environment that nurtures growth and well-being.
