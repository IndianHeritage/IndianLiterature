अभि प्र भरा धृतता धृत्संमनः श्रवश् चित ते असद् बृहत् |अर्षन्त्व् आपो जवासां व मातरो हनो वृतं जयास्वः ||
abhi pra bhara dhṛṣatā dhṛṣanmanaḥ śravaś cit te asad bṛhat | arṣantv āpo javasā vi mātaro hano vṛtraṁ jayā svaḥ ||
  • abhi pra bhara dhṛṣatā: This phrase can be interpreted as "with courage, bring forth abundance." It emphasizes the importance of courage in achieving prosperity and success. In real life, this can be seen in entrepreneurs who take risks to start their businesses, demonstrating courage to pursue their dreams.
  • dhṛṣanmanaḥ: This translates to "with a determined mind." It highlights the significance of mental strength and focus. For instance, athletes often train their minds to stay focused on their goals, which is crucial for their performance in competitions.
  • śravaś cit te asad bṛhat: This can be understood as "may your consciousness be vast and enlightened." It suggests the pursuit of knowledge and wisdom. In everyday life, this is applicable in educational settings where individuals strive to expand their understanding and awareness of the world around them.
  • arṣantv āpo javasā: This phrase means "may the waters flow swiftly." It symbolizes the flow of life and the importance of adaptability. In nature, rivers that flow freely represent resilience and the ability to overcome obstacles, which can be mirrored in human experiences when facing challenges.
  • vi mātaro hano vṛtraṁ: This translates to "may the mothers (nurturers) remove obstacles." It signifies the role of nurturing forces in overcoming difficulties. In real life, this can be seen in the support systems provided by family and friends who help individuals navigate through tough times.
  • jayā svaḥ: This means "victory and well-being." It encapsulates the ultimate goal of achieving success and a fulfilling life. In practical terms, this can be reflected in personal achievements, such as completing a degree or reaching a career milestone, which contribute to one's overall well-being.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the interplay of courage, determination, knowledge, adaptability, nurturing support, and the pursuit of victory in life. Its significance lies in the reminder that success is not just about individual effort but also about the collective support and wisdom we gather along the way. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire individuals to face challenges with courage, seek knowledge, and rely on their support systems to achieve their goals, ultimately leading to a fulfilling and victorious life.
