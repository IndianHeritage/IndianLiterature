नकीष त्वद रथीतरो हरि यद इन्द्र यच्छसे |नकीष त्वानु मजनना नकीः स्वश्व आनाše ||
nakiṣ ṭvad rathītaro harī yad indra yacchase | nakiṣ ṭvānu majmanā nakiḥ svaśva ānaśe ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • nakiṣ: Not (or no)
    • ṭvad: Your
    • rathītaro: Chariot (or vehicle) of the highest kind
    • harī: O Lord (often referring to Indra)
    • yad: Whatever
    • indra: Indra, the king of gods
    • yacchase: You bestow or grant
    • ṭvānu: Your own
    • majmanā: Of the worshipper or devotee
    • nakiḥ: Not (or no)
    • svaśva: Own or self
    • ānaśe: To be nourished or sustained
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra expresses a plea or invocation to Indra, the deity associated with strength, rain, and thunder.
    • The speaker acknowledges that there is no chariot greater than that which belongs to Indra, emphasizing his supreme power and authority.
    • The mantra suggests a relationship between the devotee and Indra, where the devotee seeks blessings and sustenance from the divine.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In times of difficulty, individuals often turn to higher powers or deities for guidance and support, similar to how the mantra seeks the blessings of Indra.
    • In a corporate setting, leaders may invoke the spirit of teamwork and collaboration, akin to seeking the 'chariot' of collective strength to overcome challenges.
    • During natural calamities, communities often come together to pray for divine intervention, reflecting the essence of seeking sustenance and protection from a higher power.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of recognizing the power of the divine in our lives and the importance of seeking blessings for strength and sustenance.
    • It serves as a reminder that in our pursuits, whether personal or professional, we should remain humble and acknowledge the greater forces at play.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire individuals to cultivate faith and resilience, encouraging them to seek support from their community and higher powers during challenging times.
