ये ह त्ये ते सहमानाऽयासस्त्वेषासो अग्ने अर्चयश्चरन्ति |श्येनासो न दुवसनासो अर्थं तुविष्वणसो मारुतं न शर्धः ||
ye ha tye te sahamānā ayāsas tveṣāso agne arcayaś caranti | śyenāso na duvasanāso arthaṁ tuviṣvaṇaso mārutaṁ na śardhaḥ ||
  • Translation: The mantra can be translated as: "Those who are united with you, O Agni, who are your companions, they who are strong and powerful, may they come to us. The swift ones, the strong ones, may they bring us wealth and prosperity, and may they not be hindered in their endeavors."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • ye ha tye te: "Those who are united with you" - This refers to the individuals or entities that are in harmony with Agni, the fire god, symbolizing unity and cooperation.
    • sahamānā: "companions" - This emphasizes the importance of companionship and collective strength.
    • ayāsas: "strong and powerful" - This highlights the qualities of strength and resilience.
    • tveṣāso: "may they come to us" - A call for the presence and blessings of these strong companions.
    • agne: "O Agni" - Directly addressing the fire god, who represents energy, transformation, and purification.
    • arcayaś: "may they bring" - A request for offerings or blessings.
    • caranti: "they who move" - Refers to those who are active and dynamic in their pursuits.
    • śyenāso: "the swift ones" - Symbolizes speed and agility in achieving goals.
    • na duvasanāso: "may they not be hindered" - A plea for the removal of obstacles in their path.
    • arthaṁ: "wealth and prosperity" - The ultimate goal of the endeavors, representing material and spiritual abundance.
    • tuviṣvaṇaso: "the strong ones" - Again emphasizing strength and capability.
    • mārutaṁ: "may they bring" - A continuation of the request for blessings.
    • na śardhaḥ: "may they not be hindered" - Reiterating the desire for unobstructed progress.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, this mantra can be applied to teamwork. When colleagues work together harmoniously, supporting each other, they can achieve greater success and overcome challenges more effectively.
    • In personal relationships, the mantra emphasizes the importance of having strong, supportive friends and family who can help navigate life's difficulties and bring joy and prosperity.
    • In community initiatives, the call for unity and strength can inspire collective action towards common goals, such as environmental conservation or social justice, where the combined efforts of many can lead to significant change.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its emphasis on unity, strength, and the pursuit of prosperity through collective effort. It serves as a reminder that in both personal and communal endeavors, the support of strong companions is essential for overcoming obstacles and achieving success. In real life, this mantra can inspire individuals and groups to foster collaboration, seek out supportive relationships, and work together towards shared goals, ultimately leading to a more prosperous and harmonious existence.
