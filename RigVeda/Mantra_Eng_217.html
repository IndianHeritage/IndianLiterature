अग्नि पत्नीर इह वह देवानामुशतीर उप |त्वष्टारं सोमपीतये ||
agne patnīr ihā vaha devānām uśatīr upa | tvaṣṭāraṁ somapītaye ||
  • Agne: This word refers to Agni, the fire god in Vedic literature. Fire is often seen as a purifying and transformative force.
  • Patnīr: This translates to "wife" or "spouse," indicating a relationship or partnership.
  • ihā: Meaning "here" or "in this world," it emphasizes the earthly context of the mantra.
  • Vaha: This means "to bring" or "to carry," suggesting an action of delivery or manifestation.
  • Devānām: This translates to "of the gods," indicating a divine connection or influence.
  • Uśatīr: This word means "prosperity" or "well-being," highlighting the desired outcome of the invocation.
  • Upa: Meaning "towards" or "near," it suggests a movement or approach towards something.
  • Tvaṣṭāraṁ: This refers to "the creator" or "the one who shapes," often associated with the divine craftsman or architect.
  • Somapītaye: This translates to "for the enjoyment of Soma," where Soma is a sacred drink that symbolizes immortality and divine ecstasy.

  • Meaning: The mantra can be interpreted as a prayer to Agni, asking him to bring prosperity and well-being in this world, while also invoking the divine creator for the enjoyment of the sacred Soma. It emphasizes the importance of relationships, divine blessings, and the transformative power of fire.
  • Real-life Example: In a wedding ceremony, couples often light a sacred fire (Agni) as a witness to their vows, symbolizing the transformative power of their union and the blessings they seek from the divine. This mantra can be recited to invoke prosperity and harmony in their relationship.

  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its invocation of divine forces to bring prosperity and well-being into our lives. It highlights the importance of relationships and the blessings that come from the divine. In real-life scenarios, such as weddings, rituals, or any significant life events, this mantra can be used to seek divine guidance and blessings for a prosperous and harmonious life.
