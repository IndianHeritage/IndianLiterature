मन्त्र: सुविज्ञानं चिकीषे जनाय सच्चासच्च वचसी पस्पृधाते |तयोः यत् सत्यं यतरद ऋजीयस तदित सोमो 'वति हन्त्य आसत् ||
suvijñānaṁ cikituṣe janāya sac cāsac ca vacasī paspṛdhāte | tayor yat satyaṁ yatarad ṛjīyas tad it somo 'vati hanty āsat ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • suvijñānaṁ: Good knowledge or wisdom
    • cikituṣe: For the well-being or benefit
    • janāya: To the people or humanity
    • sat: Truth
    • asat: Untruth or falsehood
    • vacasī: Speech or words
    • paspṛdhāte: To be made manifest or to be expressed
    • tayor: Of those two (truth and untruth)
    • yat: Whatever
    • satyaṁ: The ultimate truth
    • yatarad: Where it is
    • ṛjīyas: Straightforwardness or righteousness
    • tad: That
    • it: Indeed
    • somo: The moon or a source of nourishment
    • 'vati: Flows or moves
    • hanty: Kills or destroys
    • āsat: Falsehood or untruth
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of good knowledge and wisdom for the benefit of humanity.
    • It highlights the duality of truth (sat) and untruth (asat), suggesting that words should express the truth and not falsehood.
    • The mantra suggests that the ultimate truth is straightforward and nourishes like the moon, while falsehood is destructive.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In education, promoting critical thinking and good knowledge can lead to informed decision-making among students.
    • In communication, being truthful in our words fosters trust and strengthens relationships, while deceit can lead to conflict and misunderstanding.
    • In leadership, a leader who embodies truth and righteousness inspires their followers and creates a positive environment.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the significance of truth and wisdom in our lives.
    • It encourages individuals to seek knowledge and express it truthfully, contributing to the well-being of society.
    • In a world filled with misinformation, this mantra is particularly relevant, urging us to discern truth from falsehood and to communicate with integrity.
