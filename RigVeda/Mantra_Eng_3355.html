अव यच छ्येनो अश्वनीद अध द्योः विय यद यदि वात ऊहुः पुरंढिम् |सृजद यद अस्मा अव ह क्षिपज ज्यां कृशानुर अस्ताम् मनसा भुरण्यन् ||
ava yac chyeno asvanīd adha dyor vi yad yadi vāta ūhuḥ puraṁdhim | sṛjad yad asmā ava ha kṣipaj jyāṁ kṛśānur astā manasā bhuraṇyan ||
  • ava yac chyeno asvanīd: This phrase can be interpreted as "whatever is obstructing or hindering." In real life, this can relate to challenges or obstacles we face in our personal or professional lives, such as fear of failure or lack of resources.
  • adha dyor vi yad yadi vāta ūhuḥ puraṁdhim: This translates to "under the sky, whatever the winds may bring." This can symbolize the unpredictability of life and the external factors that influence our journey, much like how we cannot control the weather but must adapt to it.
  • sṛjad yad asmā ava ha kṣipaj: This means "create what we desire." It emphasizes the power of intention and action in manifesting our goals. For example, setting clear objectives in a career and working diligently towards them can lead to success.
  • jyāṁ kṛśānur astā manasā bhuraṇyan: This can be interpreted as "may our minds be filled with strength." It highlights the importance of mental resilience and positivity. In real life, maintaining a strong mindset during tough times, such as during a health crisis or personal loss, is crucial for recovery and growth.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of overcoming obstacles, adapting to life's unpredictability, actively creating our desired outcomes, and fostering mental strength. In real life, these principles can be applied in various scenarios, such as personal development, career advancement, and navigating challenges. By embodying these teachings, individuals can cultivate a more fulfilling and resilient life, ultimately leading to greater success and happiness.
