शतधारं वायुम् अर्कं स्वरविदं नृचक्षसस् ते अभि चक्षते हविः |ये पृणन्ति प्र च यच्छन्ति सङ्गमे ते दक्षिणां दूहते सप्तमातरम् ||
śatadhāraṁ vāyum arkaṁ svarvidaṁ nṛcakṣasas te abhi cakṣate haviḥ | ye pṛṇanti pra ca yacchanti saṁgame te dakṣiṇāṁ duhate saptamātaram ||
  • śatadhāraṁ vāyum: This phrase can be translated as "the wind that carries a hundred streams." Here, "śatadhāraṁ" signifies abundance and diversity, while "vāyum" refers to the wind, symbolizing movement and change. In real life, this can be likened to the various paths and opportunities that life presents to us, much like the wind that carries seeds to different places for growth.
  • arkaṁ svarvidaṁ: "Arkaṁ" means "light" or "radiance," and "svarvidaṁ" refers to "knowledge of the heavens." Together, they suggest enlightenment and the pursuit of higher knowledge. This can be related to the quest for education and understanding in our lives, where we seek to illuminate our minds with wisdom, akin to how the sun brings light to the world.
  • nṛcakṣasas te abhi cakṣate haviḥ: This translates to "the beings with human-like eyes perceive your offerings." "Nṛcakṣasas" refers to beings that have human-like qualities, indicating a connection to humanity. "Haviḥ" means offerings or sacrifices. This can be understood as the recognition of our efforts and contributions by society, similar to how we appreciate the hard work of others in our communities.
  • ye pṛṇanti pra ca yacchanti: This means "those who nourish and give." It emphasizes the importance of generosity and support in our relationships. In real life, this can be seen in acts of kindness, where individuals or communities come together to help one another, such as during natural disasters or in charitable endeavors.
  • saṁgame te dakṣiṇāṁ duhate saptamātaram: This phrase can be interpreted as "in unity, they bring forth the nurturing mother." "Saṁgame" signifies togetherness, while "dakṣiṇāṁ" refers to nurturing or support. This highlights the significance of collaboration and teamwork in achieving common goals. For instance, in a workplace, when team members unite their strengths, they can accomplish tasks more effectively, much like a mother nurturing her children.

  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the interconnectedness of life, the pursuit of knowledge, the recognition of contributions, the importance of generosity, and the power of unity. In real-life scenarios, these themes are applicable in various contexts, such as community service, teamwork in professional settings, and the quest for personal growth. By embodying these principles, individuals can foster a more harmonious and supportive environment, ultimately leading to collective progress and well-being.
