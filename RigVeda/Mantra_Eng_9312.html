प्र वो महे मंदमानायान्धसो 'र्चा विश्वानराय विश्वाभुवे | इन्द्रस्य यस्य सुमखं सहो महि श्रवो नृम्णं च रोधसी सपर्यतः ||
pra vo mahe mandamānāyāndhaso 'rcā viśvānarāya viśvābhuve | indrasya yasya sumakhaṁ saho mahi śravo nṛmṇaṁ ca rodasī saparyataḥ ||
  • pra vo mahe mandamānāyāndhaso 'rcā viśvānarāya viśvābhuve
    • pra vo mahe: This phrase can be interpreted as "we invoke you" or "we call upon you." It signifies a deep respect and a plea for divine intervention.
    • mandamānāyāndhaso: This refers to the "one who is wise and knowledgeable." It emphasizes the importance of wisdom in guiding our actions and decisions.
    • 'rcā viśvānarāya: This can be translated as "the one who is the source of all light and knowledge." It highlights the divine aspect of enlightenment and understanding.
    • viśvābhuve: This means "to all beings" or "universal." It suggests that the divine presence is accessible to everyone, transcending individual limitations.
  • indrasya yasya sumakhaṁ saho mahi śravo nṛmṇaṁ ca rodasī saparyataḥ
    • indrasya: Referring to Indra, the king of gods in Vedic tradition, symbolizing strength and power.
    • yasya sumakhaṁ: This translates to "whose auspiciousness is great." It indicates the blessings and positive energy that come from the divine.
    • saho mahi: This means "with great strength." It emphasizes the importance of resilience and courage in facing life's challenges.
    • śravo nṛmṇaṁ: This can be interpreted as "the fame of humanity." It suggests that human actions and virtues are recognized and celebrated.
    • ca rodasī saparyataḥ: This phrase means "and the earth is revered." It signifies the importance of respecting and honoring our planet and its resources.

Conclusion:

  • The mantra emphasizes the significance of invoking divine wisdom and strength in our lives.
  • It teaches us to seek knowledge and enlightenment, which can guide us in making better decisions.
  • In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in moments of uncertainty or when facing challenges, reminding us to draw upon our inner strength and the support of the universe.
  • Moreover, it highlights the importance of respecting our environment and recognizing the interconnectedness of all beings.
  • Ultimately, this mantra serves as a reminder of the divine presence in our lives and the power of collective consciousness in achieving harmony and balance.
