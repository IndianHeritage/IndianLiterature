ते घा राजानो अमृतस्य मन्द्रा आर्यमा मित्रो वरुणः परिज्मा |कद रुद्रो नृणां स्तुतो मरुतः पूषणो भागः ||
te ghā rājāno amṛtasya mandrā aryamā mitro varuṇaḥ parijmā | kad rudro nṛṇāṁ stuto marutaḥ pūṣaṇo bhagaḥ ||
  • te ghā rājāno: This phrase can be translated as "those who are the kings." In a real-life context, this can refer to leaders or individuals in positions of authority who guide and protect their communities.
  • amṛtasya mandrā: This means "the essence of immortality." It signifies the pursuit of eternal truths and values that transcend the physical realm. For example, in modern life, this can relate to the quest for knowledge, wisdom, and spiritual growth.
  • aryamā: This term refers to a deity associated with friendship and harmony. In everyday life, it can symbolize the importance of building strong, supportive relationships with others.
  • mitro varuṇaḥ: "Mitro" means friend, and "Varuṇa" is a god associated with cosmic order and moral authority. This highlights the significance of friendship and ethical conduct in maintaining societal balance. In practice, this can be seen in community service and ethical leadership.
  • parijmā: This word suggests the idea of encompassing or surrounding. It can represent the protective nature of community and family, emphasizing the need for support systems in our lives.
  • kad rudro nṛṇāṁ stuto: This translates to "when Rudra is praised by men." Rudra is a deity associated with storms and transformation. This can be interpreted as the importance of acknowledging and respecting the forces of nature and change in our lives.
  • marutaḥ pūṣaṇo bhagaḥ: "Maruta" refers to the storm gods, "Pūṣaṇa" is a deity of nourishment, and "bhaga" signifies prosperity. Together, this emphasizes the interconnectedness of nature, nourishment, and abundance. In real life, this can relate to sustainable living and the importance of respecting our environment for future generations.

Conclusion: This mantra encapsulates the essence of leadership, friendship, ethical conduct, and the interconnectedness of life. It serves as a reminder of the values that should guide our actions and relationships. In today's world, these principles can be applied in various scenarios, such as community building, environmental stewardship, and personal development. By embodying these ideals, we contribute to a harmonious and prosperous society.
