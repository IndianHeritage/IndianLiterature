अश्वावतीर गोमतीर विश्वसुविदो भूरी च्यवन्त वस्तवे |उदीरय प्रतिमासूनृताऽ उषश्चोद राधो मघोनाम ||
aśvāvatīr gomatīr viśvasuvido bhūri cyavanta vastave | ud īraya prati mā sūnṛtā uṣaś coda rādho maghonām ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • aśvāvatīr: One who is like a horse, symbolizing strength and speed.
    • gomatīr: One who possesses wealth or abundance.
    • viśvasuvido: All-knowing or wise in all aspects.
    • bhūri: Abundant or plentiful.
    • cyavanta: Those who are moving or flowing.
    • vastave: In the realm of existence or reality.
    • ud īraya: Rise up or elevate.
    • prati: Towards or in response to.
    • mā: Not or do not.
    • sūnṛtā: Falsehood or deceit.
    • uṣaś: Dawn or new beginnings.
    • coda: Inspire or motivate.
    • rādho: To be prosperous or successful.
    • maghonām: Of the wealthy or those who possess abundance.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the qualities of strength, wisdom, and abundance.
    • It emphasizes the importance of rising above falsehood and deceit, encouraging individuals to seek truth and integrity.
    • The reference to dawn symbolizes new beginnings and the potential for growth and prosperity.
    • In a real-life context, this mantra can be applied to personal development, where one strives to embody strength and wisdom while avoiding dishonesty.
    • For example, in a business setting, a leader can use these principles to foster a culture of transparency and ethical practices, leading to long-term success.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for individuals to embrace strength, wisdom, and abundance while rejecting falsehood.
    • It serves as a reminder that true prosperity comes from integrity and ethical behavior.
    • In everyday life, this mantra can guide individuals in their personal and professional journeys, encouraging them to rise above challenges and pursue truth and success.
    • Ultimately, it highlights the interconnectedness of strength, wisdom, and ethical living in achieving a fulfilling and prosperous life.
