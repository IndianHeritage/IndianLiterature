मां ते गोदात्र निर अराम राधसा इन्द्र मां ते गृहामहि |दृढा चिद आर्यः प्र मृषाभ्य आ भर न ते दामान आदभे ||
mā te godatra nir arāma rādhasa indra mā te gṛhāmahi | dṛḻhā cid aryaḥ pra mṛśābhy ā bhara na te dāmāna ādabhe ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • mā te: May you not
    • godatra: be deprived of wealth
    • nir arāma: without any discomfort
    • rādhasa: in your endeavors
    • indra: O Indra (the king of gods)
    • mā te: may you not
    • gṛhāmahi: we seek your protection
    • dṛḻhā: strong
    • cid: indeed
    • aryaḥ: noble ones
    • pra: for
    • mṛśābhy: from falsehood
    • ā bhara: may you bring
    • na te: not for you
    • dāmāna: bonds
    • ādabhe: may they not bind
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Indra, the deity associated with strength, rain, and the heavens, asking for protection and abundance.
    • The speaker expresses a desire to be free from discomfort and deprivation, seeking the blessings of Indra to ensure prosperity in their endeavors.
    • The mention of "noble ones" indicates a call for integrity and truthfulness, emphasizing the importance of these values in achieving success.
    • The plea to be free from bonds suggests a desire for liberation from negative influences or constraints that hinder progress.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be applied when seeking success in ventures. Entrepreneurs often pray for guidance and protection against failures and obstacles.
    • In personal life, individuals may recite this mantra during challenging times, hoping for strength and clarity to overcome difficulties.
    • In community settings, leaders may invoke this mantra to foster unity and integrity among members, promoting a culture of truthfulness and support.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its holistic approach to life, emphasizing the need for divine support in achieving prosperity while adhering to noble values.
    • It serves as a reminder that success is not just about material wealth but also about maintaining integrity and being free from negative influences.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire individuals and communities to strive for excellence while fostering a supportive and truthful environment.
