मां नो हृणीताम् अतिथिर वसुर अग्निः पुरुप्रशस्त एषः |यः सुहोतā स्वध्वरः ||
mā no hṛṇītām atithir vasur agniḥ purupraśasta eṣaḥ | yaḥ suhotā svadhvaraḥ ||
  • mā no hṛṇītām: This phrase translates to "may the guest not take away from us." In a real-life context, this can be understood as a plea for hospitality and the importance of treating guests with respect and generosity. For example, in many cultures, offering food and shelter to guests is a sacred duty, reflecting the value placed on community and relationships.
  • atithir vasur agniḥ: Here, "atithir" means "guest," "vasur" refers to "wealth" or "prosperity," and "agniḥ" signifies "fire." This can be interpreted as the guest being a source of wealth and prosperity, akin to the warmth and light provided by fire. In practical terms, this can relate to how welcoming guests can lead to fruitful relationships and opportunities, much like how a warm fire brings people together.
  • purupraśasta eṣaḥ: This means "this one is praised in the highest." It emphasizes the importance of honoring those who are worthy, such as guests or individuals who contribute positively to society. In everyday life, this can be seen in how we recognize and celebrate the achievements of others, fostering a culture of appreciation and respect.
  • yaḥ suhotā svadhvaraḥ: This translates to "who is a good host and has the right to offer." It highlights the qualities of a good host, which include generosity, kindness, and the ability to create a welcoming environment. In real life, this can be exemplified by individuals who go out of their way to ensure that their guests feel comfortable and valued, such as a friend who organizes a gathering and pays attention to everyone's needs.

  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of hospitality, respect for guests, and the virtues of a good host. In today's world, where individualism often prevails, this mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of community, generosity, and the bonds that are formed through shared experiences. It encourages us to cultivate an environment where everyone feels welcome and valued, ultimately leading to stronger relationships and a more harmonious society.
