मन्त्र: रायस्कामो वज्रहस्तं सुदक्षिणं पुत्रो न पितरं हुवे ||
rāyaskāmo vajrahastaṁ sudakṣiṇam putro na pitaraṁ huve ||
  • Mantra: "rāyaskāmo vajrahastaṁ sudakṣiṇam putro na pitaraṁ huve"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • rāyaskāmo: One who desires wealth or prosperity.
    • vajrahastaṁ: One who has a hand like a thunderbolt; symbolizes strength and power.
    • sudakṣiṇam: Well-skilled or proficient; denotes capability and expertise.
    • putro: Son or offspring; represents the next generation.
    • na: Not; negation.
    • pitaraṁ: Father; signifies the parental figure.
    • huve: I invoke or I call upon; a term used in prayers or invocations.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra expresses a desire for prosperity and strength, invoking a powerful and skilled entity.
    • The phrase "putro na pitaraṁ huve" suggests a call for the blessings of a son, emphasizing the importance of the next generation in achieving success.
    • It highlights the relationship between the individual and their lineage, indicating that while one may seek wealth and strength, the support and blessings of the family, particularly the offspring, are crucial.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, an entrepreneur may seek the blessings of their family and children while striving for success, recognizing that their efforts will benefit future generations.
    • In personal development, individuals often invoke the support of their mentors or family members, understanding that their guidance is essential for achieving personal goals.
    • In cultural practices, many rituals involve invoking blessings for prosperity and strength, reflecting the values of family and community support.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the interconnectedness of personal ambition and familial support.
    • It serves as a reminder that while one may strive for individual success, the blessings and support of family, especially the next generation, play a vital role in achieving true prosperity.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as business, personal growth, and cultural practices, emphasizing the importance of community and family in the pursuit of success.
