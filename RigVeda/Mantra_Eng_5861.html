यां वां शतं नियुतो याः सहस्रं इन्द्रवायू विश्ववाराः साचन्ते |आभिर यातं सुविदात्राभिर अर्वाक् पातं नराः प्रतिभृतस्य मध्वः ||
yā vāṁ śataṁ niyuto yāḥ sahasram indravāyū viśvavārāḥ sacante | ābhir yātaṁ suvidatrābhir arvāk pātaṁ narā pratibhṛtasya madhvaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yā: who/that
    • vāṁ: your
    • śataṁ: hundred
    • niyuto: controlled/regulated
    • yāḥ: who/that
    • sahasram: thousand
    • indravāyū: like Indra's wind
    • viśvavārāḥ: all-protecting
    • sacante: they who are capable
    • ābhir: with light
    • yātaṁ: come forth
    • suvidatrābhir: with good fortune
    • arvāk: from all sides
    • pātaṁ: protection
    • narā: men/humans
    • pratibhṛtasya: of the one who is endowed
    • madhvaḥ: with sweetness
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the divine forces that protect and guide individuals.
    • The reference to "hundred" and "thousand" signifies abundance and strength in numbers.
    • Indra, a prominent deity in Vedic literature, symbolizes power and protection, akin to the wind that can be both gentle and fierce.
    • The phrase "with light" suggests enlightenment and clarity, indicating that divine guidance leads to wisdom.
    • The mantra emphasizes the importance of community and collective strength, as it mentions "men" who are endowed with sweetness, suggesting harmony and cooperation.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, teamwork can be likened to the "hundred" and "thousand" working together towards a common goal, where each member contributes their unique strengths.
    • In personal relationships, the mantra can be applied to the support systems we build, where friends and family act as protective forces, much like the divine energies described.
    • In community service, the collective effort of individuals coming together to help those in need reflects the essence of this mantra, showcasing the power of unity and shared purpose.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of divine protection and guidance in our lives.
    • It teaches us that strength lies in unity and that we should seek and offer support to one another.
    • In real-life scenarios, whether in personal, professional, or community contexts, the principles of cooperation, protection, and enlightenment are vital for achieving success and harmony.
