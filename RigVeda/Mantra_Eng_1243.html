परायतीनाम् अन्व एति पाठ आयातीनाम् प्रथमाः शाश्वतीनाम् |व्युञ्चन्ती जीवम् उदीरयन्त्य उषा मृतं कं चन बोधयन्ती ||
parāyatīnām anv eti pātha āyatīnām prathamā śaśvatīnām | vyucchantī jīvam udīrayanty uṣā mṛtaṁ kaṁ cana bodhayantī ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • parāyatīnām: of those who are devoted or dedicated
    • anv eti: follows or pursues
    • pātha: the path or way
    • āyatīnām: of those who are eternal or everlasting
    • prathamā: the first or foremost
    • śaśvatīnām: of those who are permanent or unchanging
    • vyucchantī: liberating or freeing
    • jīvam: life or living being
    • udīrayanty: awakening or rousing
    • uṣā: dawn or light
    • mṛtaṁ: dead or lifeless
    • kaṁ cana: anyone or anything
    • bodhayantī: enlightening or bringing to awareness
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the pursuit of a higher path by those who are dedicated to eternal truths.
    • The dawn (uṣā) symbolizes new beginnings, enlightenment, and the awakening of consciousness.
    • It emphasizes the importance of liberation from ignorance and the awakening of life, even in those who may seem lifeless or unaware.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a personal context, this mantra can be applied to individuals who strive for self-improvement and enlightenment, such as those who engage in meditation or spiritual practices.
    • In a societal context, it can reflect movements that aim to awaken consciousness about social issues, such as environmental activism or education reform, where the goal is to liberate society from ignorance and promote awareness.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call to pursue a path of enlightenment and liberation.
    • It encourages individuals to awaken their consciousness and strive for a deeper understanding of life, ultimately leading to personal and collective growth.
    • This mantra is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal development to social awakening, reminding us of the power of knowledge and awareness in transforming lives.
