त्वां देवेषु प्रथमं हवामहे त्वं बभूथा पृतनासु सासहिḥ |सेमं नः कारुम उपमन्युम उद्भिदं इन्द्रः कृणोतु प्रसवे रथम पुरः ||
tvāṁ deveṣu prathamaṁ havāmahe tvam babhūtha pṛtanāsu sāsahiḥ | semaṁ naḥ kārum upamanyum udbhidam indraḥ kṛṇotu prasave ratham puraḥ ||
  • tvāṁ deveṣu prathamaṁ havāmahe: This phrase translates to "We invoke you first among the gods." It signifies the importance of prioritizing divine presence in our lives. In real life, this can be seen in rituals where individuals or communities begin their prayers or ceremonies by acknowledging a higher power, setting a tone of reverence and intention.
  • tvam babhūtha pṛtanāsu sāsahiḥ: This means "You have become the support in our struggles." It highlights the idea that divine support is crucial during challenging times. For example, when someone faces difficulties in their career or personal life, they often seek guidance or strength from a higher power, reflecting this sentiment of reliance on divine support.
  • semaṁ naḥ kārum upamanyum udbhidam: This translates to "May Indra grant us a prosperous path." Here, 'Indra' represents a powerful deity associated with strength and prosperity. In practical terms, this can relate to seeking success in endeavors such as starting a new business or embarking on a significant life change, where individuals pray for favorable outcomes and guidance.
  • indraḥ kṛṇotu prasave ratham puraḥ: This means "May Indra lead our chariot forward." The chariot symbolizes progress and movement towards goals. In everyday life, this can be likened to the journey of pursuing education, career advancements, or personal growth, where one hopes for divine guidance to navigate challenges and achieve success.

  • Conclusion: This mantra emphasizes the importance of invoking divine presence, seeking support during struggles, and aspiring for prosperity and guidance in our endeavors. Its significance lies in the acknowledgment of a higher power in our lives, which can provide strength and direction. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applicable during personal challenges, community rituals, or significant life transitions, reminding individuals to seek divine assistance and maintain faith in their journey.
