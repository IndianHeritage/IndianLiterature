अदेवेना मनसा यो ऋषण्यति शासाम उग्रो मन्यमानो जिघांसति |बृहस्पते मा प्राणक तस्य नो वधो न कर्म मन्युः दुरेवस्य शर्धतः ||
adevena manasā yo riṣaṇyati śāsām ugro manyamāno jighāṁsati | bṛhaspate mā praṇak tasya no vadho ni karma manyuṁ durevasya śardhataḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • adevena: by the unworthy or unfit
    • manasā: with the mind
    • yo: who
    • riṣaṇyati: causes to be disturbed
    • śāsām: the command or authority
    • ugro: fierce or powerful
    • manyamāno: considering or thinking
    • jighāṁsati: desires to harm
    • bṛhaspate: O Brihaspati (the deity of wisdom and eloquence)
    • mā: do not
    • praṇak: let loose or allow
    • tasya: of him
    • no: our
    • vadho: destruction or killing
    • ni: not
    • karma: action or deed
    • manyuṁ: anger or wrath
    • durevasya: of the distant or powerful
    • śardhataḥ: with strength or might
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra addresses the concept of mental disturbance caused by unworthy thoughts or actions.
    • It highlights the importance of controlling one's mind and actions, especially when faced with powerful negative influences.
    • The invocation of Brihaspati signifies a plea for wisdom and guidance to overcome harmful intentions.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace setting, an employee may face jealousy or rivalry from colleagues. This mantra serves as a reminder to maintain focus and not let negative thoughts affect productivity.
    • In personal relationships, one might encounter misunderstandings or conflicts. The mantra encourages individuals to seek wisdom and avoid actions driven by anger or resentment.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its emphasis on mental discipline and the pursuit of wisdom.
    • It teaches us to be aware of our thoughts and actions, especially in challenging situations, and to seek guidance from higher wisdom.
    • In real life, applying the principles of this mantra can lead to better decision-making, improved relationships, and a more peaceful mind.
