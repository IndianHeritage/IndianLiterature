मन्त्र: आय ते अग्ने समिधा विद्हेम प्रति स्तोमं शस्यमानं गृभाय | दहाशसो रक्षसः पाह्य अस्मान द्रुहो निदो मित्रमहो अवद्यात् ||
ayā te agne samidhā vidhema prati stomaṁ śasyamānaṁ gṛbhāya | dahāśaso rakṣasaḥ pāhy a1smān druho nido mitramaho avadyāt ||
  • ayā te agne samidhā vidhema: This phrase can be translated as "We offer our prayers to you, O Agni, the divine fire." Agni is often seen as a mediator between humans and the divine, representing purity and the transformative power of fire.
  • prati stomaṁ śasyamānaṁ gṛbhāya: This means "May we receive the blessings that come from your offerings." Here, "stoma" refers to the hymns or praises offered to Agni, and "śasyamānaṁ" indicates the abundance or prosperity that is sought through these offerings.
  • dahāśaso rakṣasaḥ pāhy a1smān: This translates to "Protect us from the evil forces." The term "rakṣasaḥ" refers to malevolent beings or forces that can disrupt harmony and well-being. The invocation seeks protection from such negative influences.
  • druho nido mitramaho avadyāt: This can be interpreted as "May we be free from deceit and may friendship prevail." "Druho" refers to treachery or deceit, while "mitramaho" emphasizes the importance of friendship and unity among people.

  • Significance: This mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking divine guidance and protection in our lives. It highlights the role of Agni as a purifying force and a protector against negativity and deceit.
  • Real-life Application: In modern life, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as:
    • Personal Growth: Just as Agni transforms offerings into energy, individuals can seek to transform their challenges into opportunities for growth.
    • Community Harmony: The call for protection against deceit and the emphasis on friendship can be seen in efforts to build trust and cooperation within communities.
    • Spiritual Practices: Many people still perform rituals involving fire, symbolizing the purification of the mind and spirit, and invoking protection from negative influences.

  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a reminder of the need for spiritual guidance, protection from negativity, and the importance of fostering positive relationships. In a world filled with challenges, invoking the essence of Agni can inspire individuals to seek clarity, strength, and unity in their lives.
