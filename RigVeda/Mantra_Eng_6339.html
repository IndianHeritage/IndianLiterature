अभि स्वरण्तु ये तव रुद्रासः सक्षत श्रीयम् |उतो मरुत्वतीर विषो अभि प्रयः ||
abhi svarantu ye tava rudrāsaḥ sakṣata śriyam | uto marutvatīr viśo abhi prayaḥ ||
  • Mantra: "abhi svarantu ye tava rudrāsaḥ sakṣata śriyam | uto marutvatīr viśo abhi prayaḥ ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • abhi: towards, in the direction of
    • svarantu: may they sing, may they call
    • ye: those who
    • tava: your
    • rudrāsaḥ: O Rudras (the fierce deities associated with storms and wind)
    • sakṣata: may they be present, may they manifest
    • śriyam: prosperity, wealth, glory
    • uto: and
    • marutvatīr: O Maruts (the storm deities)
    • viśo: of the people, of the community
    • abhi: towards, in the direction of
    • prayaḥ: effort, endeavor
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to the Rudras and Maruts, invoking their presence and blessings.
    • The Rudras are often associated with the destructive aspects of nature, while the Maruts represent the beneficial forces of storms and winds.
    • The mantra emphasizes the importance of community and collective effort in seeking prosperity and well-being.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community setting, this mantra can be relevant during festivals or gatherings where people come together to celebrate and seek blessings for prosperity.
    • In a corporate environment, teams can invoke the spirit of collaboration and collective effort to achieve common goals, much like the invocation of the Rudras and Maruts for communal prosperity.
    • During natural calamities, communities often come together to support each other, reflecting the essence of this mantra in seeking divine protection and strength.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of divine blessings in achieving prosperity and the power of collective effort.
    • It teaches us to seek harmony with nature and the divine forces that govern it, reminding us that our endeavors are strengthened when we work together as a community.
    • In real life, it encourages us to foster unity, collaboration, and a sense of shared purpose in our personal and professional lives.
