परā याहि मगवन्न ā च याहīन्द्र भ्रातर उभयत्रा ते अर्थम् | यत्रा रथस्य बृहतो निधानं विमोचनं वाजिनो रासभस्य ||
parā yāhi maghavann ā ca yāhīndra bhrātar ubhayatrā te artham | yatrā rathasya bṛhato nidhānaṁ vimocanaṁ vājino rāsabhasya ||
  • Mantra: "parā yāhi maghavann ā ca yāhīndra bhrātar ubhayatrā te artham | yatrā rathasya bṛhato nidhānaṁ vimocanaṁ vājino rāsabhasya ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • parā: supreme, beyond
    • yāhi: come, approach
    • maghavan: O Maghavan (a name for Indra, the king of gods)
    • ā: also
    • yāhīndra: O Indra
    • bhrātar: brother
    • ubhayatrā: in both ways
    • te: for you
    • artham: purpose, meaning
    • yatrā: where
    • rathasya: of the chariot
    • bṛhato: of the great
    • nidhānaṁ: treasure, wealth
    • vimocanaṁ: liberation, release
    • vājino: of the swift
    • rāsabhasya: of the strong
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the supreme powers of Indra and Maghavan, asking them to come forth and assist in achieving a higher purpose.
    • The mention of "brother" signifies unity and collaboration, suggesting that divine assistance is sought not just for oneself but for the collective good.
    • The reference to the chariot symbolizes the journey of life, where the "great treasure" represents the wisdom and resources needed to navigate challenges.
    • The idea of "liberation" indicates a desire to free oneself from obstacles, whether they be material, emotional, or spiritual.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, this mantra can be applied when seeking guidance and support from colleagues or mentors to achieve a common goal.
    • In personal relationships, it emphasizes the importance of unity and collaboration, encouraging individuals to work together for mutual benefit.
    • In times of crisis, invoking the essence of this mantra can serve as a reminder to seek help and resources to overcome difficulties.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of divine support and the importance of community in achieving one's goals.
    • It serves as a reminder that we are not alone in our struggles and that seeking help from both the divine and our peers can lead to liberation and success.
    • In essence, it encourages a holistic approach to life, where spiritual and communal aspects are intertwined for a fulfilling journey.
