समिधाना उ सन्त्य शुक्रशोच इहा वह |चिकित्वान दैव्यं जनम् ||
samidhāna u santya śukraśoca ihā vaha | cikitvān daivyaṁ janam ||
  • Mantra: "samidhāna u santya śukraśoca ihā vaha | cikitvān daivyaṁ janam ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • samidhāna: This can be interpreted as "offering" or "sacrifice," often associated with the act of giving or sharing.
    • u: A conjunction meaning "and," indicating a connection between ideas.
    • santya: This refers to "truth" or "purity," emphasizing the importance of honesty and integrity.
    • śukraśoca: This can be translated as "bright" or "radiant," often associated with knowledge, wisdom, or enlightenment.
    • ihā: Meaning "here" or "in this world," grounding the mantra in the present reality.
    • vaha: This means "to carry" or "to bring forth," suggesting the act of delivering or manifesting something.
    • cikitvān: This translates to "thoughtful" or "wise," indicating a level of awareness and consideration.
    • daivyaṁ: Meaning "divine" or "heavenly," referring to a higher power or spiritual essence.
    • janam: This means "people" or "humanity," indicating the collective of individuals.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of offering and sharing in a truthful and pure manner.
    • It suggests that by being thoughtful and wise, one can bring forth divine qualities into the world.
    • The mantra encourages individuals to act with integrity and to contribute positively to society.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community service project, volunteers come together to offer their time and resources to help those in need, embodying the spirit of "samidhāna."
    • A teacher who imparts knowledge with honesty and clarity exemplifies "santya" and "śukraśoca," guiding students towards enlightenment.
    • In a workplace, fostering a culture of open communication and integrity reflects the essence of this mantra, as employees work together thoughtfully for a common goal.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the significance of selfless actions and the impact of integrity in our daily lives.
    • It is applicable in various scenarios, from personal relationships to professional environments, encouraging individuals to act with purpose and mindfulness.
    • By embodying the principles of this mantra, we can contribute to a more harmonious and enlightened society.
