यथा ह त्यद वसवो गौर्यं चित पदि षिताम अमुञ्चतां यजत्राः |एवो ष्व अस्मन् मुञ्चतां व्यंहः प्र तारी अग्ने प्रतरं न आयुः ||
yathā ha tyad vasavo gauryaṁ cit padi ṣitām amuñcatā yajatrāḥ | evo ṣv a1sman muñcatā vy aṁhaḥ pra tāry agne prataraṁ na āyuḥ ||
  • Mantra: "yathā ha tyad vasavo gauryaṁ cit padi ṣitām amuñcatā yajatrāḥ | evo ṣv a1sman muñcatā vy aṁhaḥ pra tāry agne prataraṁ na āyuḥ ||"
  • Translation: "As the Vasus (deities of abundance) release their wealth, may the offerings be made with clarity and purpose. Just as they free the essence of life, may we also be liberated from our burdens, and may Agni (the fire god) guide us towards longevity."

  • Explanation of Key Terms:
    • Vasavo: Refers to the Vasus, a group of deities associated with natural elements and abundance.
    • Gauryaṁ: Signifies wealth or prosperity, often linked to the blessings of the deities.
    • Cit: Represents clarity or consciousness, emphasizing the importance of intention in offerings.
    • Yajatrāḥ: Refers to those who perform rituals or sacrifices, highlighting the role of human agency in spiritual practices.
    • Agni: The fire god, symbolizing transformation, purification, and the connection between the earthly and divine realms.

  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a modern context, the mantra can be related to the act of giving and sharing resources. For instance, community service initiatives where individuals come together to support those in need reflect the essence of the Vasus releasing their wealth.
    • In personal development, the idea of freeing oneself from burdens can be seen in practices like mindfulness and meditation, where individuals seek clarity and liberation from stress and anxiety.
    • In rituals, lighting a fire during a ceremony symbolizes the invocation of Agni, representing the transformation of intentions into reality, much like how the mantra calls for guidance towards longevity and prosperity.

  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for clarity, intention, and the liberation from burdens through divine guidance. It emphasizes the interconnectedness of human actions and divine blessings, reminding us that our efforts in rituals and community service can lead to personal and collective prosperity. In real life, this mantra encourages us to act with purpose, seek clarity in our intentions, and recognize the abundance that can be shared with others, ultimately guiding us towards a fulfilling and meaningful existence.
