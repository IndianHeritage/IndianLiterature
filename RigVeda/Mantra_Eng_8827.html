एकः समुद्रो धरणो रयीणाम् अस्मद धृदो भूर्जन्मा वि चष्टे |सिषक्त्यूदहर निण्योः उपस्थ उतस्य मध्यम् निहितम् पदं वेः ||
ekaḥ samudro dharuṇo rayīṇām asmad dhṛdo bhūrijanmā vi caṣṭe | siṣakty ūdhar niṇyor upastha utsasya madhye nihitam padaṁ veḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ekaḥ: one
    • samudro: ocean
    • dharuṇo: of the bearer
    • rayīṇām: of riches or wealth
    • asmad: our
    • dhṛdo: firm or strong
    • bhūrijanmā: of abundant birth or creation
    • vi caṣṭe: indeed moves or acts
    • siṣakty: with power
    • ūdhar: uplift
    • niṇyor: of the two
    • upastha: near or close
    • utsasya: having been sown
    • madhye: in the midst
    • nihitam: placed or hidden
    • padaṁ: foot or path
    • veḥ: of the wind or movement
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the unity and strength found in the vastness of the ocean, symbolizing the collective wealth and resources that can be harnessed when individuals come together.
    • The phrase "ekaḥ samudro" suggests that despite individual differences, there is a singular essence that binds us, much like the ocean that connects various shores.
    • The term "rayīṇām" refers to wealth, which can be interpreted not just in material terms but also in knowledge, relationships, and experiences that enrich our lives.
    • The mantra emphasizes the importance of collaboration and unity ("asmad dhṛdo bhūrijanmā"), indicating that when we work together, we can achieve greater things.
    • The imagery of "siṣakty ūdhar" conveys the idea of uplifting each other, suggesting that through support and encouragement, we can rise above challenges.
    • The mention of "madhye nihitam padaṁ veḥ" implies that the path to success and fulfillment is often hidden within the collective efforts and shared experiences of a community.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace, when team members collaborate effectively, they can achieve goals that would be impossible individually, reflecting the essence of "ekaḥ samudro."
    • In community service, individuals coming together to support a cause can create significant change, demonstrating the power of collective action.
    • In education, group projects allow students to combine their strengths and knowledge, leading to richer learning experiences.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of unity, collaboration, and the shared journey towards common goals.
    • Its teachings are applicable in various aspects of life, including personal relationships, professional environments, and community engagements.
    • By embracing the principles outlined in this mantra, individuals can foster a sense of belonging and purpose, ultimately leading to a more harmonious and prosperous existence.
