मन्त्र: क्रत्वा महां अनुष्वधं भीमा आ वावृधे शवः | श्रीय ऋष्व उपाकयोः नि शिप्री हरिवान दधे हस्तयोः वज्रम आयसम ||
kratvā mahām̐ anuṣvadham bhīma ā vāvṛdhe śavaḥ | śriya ṛṣva upākayor ni śiprī harivān dadhe hastayor vajram āyasam ||
  • Kratvā: This term refers to the act of performing or executing a task with determination and strength. In real life, this can be likened to the dedication one shows in their profession or personal goals, such as a student studying diligently for exams.
  • Mahām̐: This word signifies greatness or vastness. It can be associated with the vast potential within each individual to achieve greatness, much like an athlete training for the Olympics, pushing their limits to reach new heights.
  • Anuṣvadham: This term can be interpreted as the act of following or adhering to a path. In a practical sense, it can relate to someone who follows a disciplined lifestyle, such as a person adhering to a strict fitness regimen to achieve health goals.
  • Bhīma: This word means formidable or powerful. It can be seen in the context of a leader who inspires strength and courage in their team, similar to a coach motivating athletes to perform at their best during a crucial game.
  • Ā vāvṛdhe: This phrase suggests the idea of growth or increase. In everyday life, this can be reflected in personal development, such as someone who continuously seeks knowledge and skills to advance in their career.
  • Śavaḥ: This term refers to the body or physical form. It emphasizes the importance of the physical aspect of life, akin to how individuals must take care of their health and well-being to lead a fulfilling life.
  • Śriya: This word denotes prosperity or wealth. It can be related to the pursuit of financial stability and success, much like an entrepreneur striving to build a successful business.
  • Ṛṣva: This term can be interpreted as strength or vigor. In real life, it can be seen in individuals who demonstrate resilience in the face of challenges, such as a person overcoming personal hardships to achieve their dreams.
  • Upākayor: This word refers to the act of supporting or aiding. It can be likened to community service, where individuals come together to help those in need, fostering a sense of unity and support.
  • Ni: This prefix often indicates negation or absence. In a practical sense, it can remind us of the importance of eliminating negativity from our lives, such as distancing ourselves from toxic relationships.
  • Śiprī: This term can be associated with swiftness or agility. It can be reflected in the fast-paced nature of modern life, where adaptability and quick decision-making are crucial for success.
  • Harivān: This word signifies auspiciousness or good fortune. It can be related to the idea of attracting positive energy and opportunities, much like a person who maintains a positive outlook and attracts success.
  • Dadhe: This term means to place or establish. In real life, it can be seen in the context of setting goals and working towards them, such as a student establishing a study plan to achieve academic success.
  • Hastayor: This word refers to hands, symbolizing action and effort. It emphasizes the importance of taking initiative and being proactive in life, akin to a craftsman who skillfully creates art with their hands.
  • Vajram: This term means thunderbolt or strength. It can be associated with the idea of resilience and power, much like a person who stands firm in their beliefs despite facing opposition.
  • Āyasam: This word refers to iron or metal, symbolizing durability and strength. In real life, it can represent the importance of building a strong foundation in any endeavor, such as a business that is built on solid principles and ethics.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of strength, determination, and the pursuit of greatness in various aspects of life. It serves as a reminder that through dedication, resilience, and a positive mindset, individuals can overcome challenges and achieve their goals. The principles outlined in this mantra are applicable in everyday scenarios, from personal development to professional success, encouraging individuals to harness their inner strength and strive for excellence in all endeavors.
