यो राजांसि विममे पार्थिवानि त्रिश्चिद विष्णुर मनवे बाधिताय |तस्य ते शर्मन्न उपदद्यमाने राया मदेम तंवा तना च ||
yo rajāṁsi vimame pārthivāni triś cid viṣṇur manave bādhitāya | tasya te śarmann upadadyamāne rāyā madema tanvā3 tanā ca ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yo: who
    • rajāṁsi: kings
    • vimame: has considered
    • pārthivāni: earthly
    • triś: three
    • cid: indeed
    • viṣṇur: Vishnu
    • manave: to Manu (the progenitor of mankind)
    • bādhitāya: for the one who is troubled
    • tasya: of him
    • te: your
    • śarmann: happiness or protection
    • upadadyamāne: when being given
    • rāyā: wealth or prosperity
    • madema: may we attain
    • tanvā: with our bodies
    • tanā: with our essence
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the divine consideration of kings and earthly rulers by Vishnu, who is seen as a protector and sustainer.
    • It emphasizes the importance of seeking protection and happiness from the divine, especially in times of trouble.
    • The reference to Manu highlights the connection between divine guidance and human governance, suggesting that rulers should act in accordance with higher principles.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern governance, leaders often seek guidance from ethical and moral frameworks, akin to how Vishnu is invoked for wisdom and protection.
    • During challenging times, such as economic downturns or natural disasters, communities often turn to spiritual practices for solace and guidance, reflecting the essence of this mantra.
    • In personal life, individuals may seek blessings for prosperity and protection in their endeavors, resonating with the call for divine support in the mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of divine guidance in leadership and personal life.
    • It serves as a reminder that true prosperity and happiness come from aligning oneself with higher principles and seeking protection from the divine.
    • In real-life scenarios, it encourages individuals and leaders alike to reflect on their actions and seek wisdom in their decisions, fostering a harmonious and prosperous existence.
