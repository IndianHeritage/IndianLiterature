पुनः पत्नीम् अग्निर अदाद आयुषा सह वर्चसा |दीर्घायुर अस्य यः पतिर्जīvāti शरदः शतम् ||
punaḥ patnīm agnir adād āyuṣā saha varcasā | dīrghāyur asyā yaḥ patir jīvāti śaradaḥ śatam ||
  • Mantra: "punaḥ patnīm agnir adād āyuṣā saha varcasā | dīrghāyur asyā yaḥ patir jīvāti śaradaḥ śatam ||"
  • Translation: "May Agni (the fire god) bestow upon us a wife again, along with longevity and brilliance. May the husband who possesses her live for a hundred autumns."
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • punaḥ: again
    • patnīm: wife
    • agnir: Agni (the fire god)
    • adād: bestow
    • āyuṣā: with longevity
    • saha: together
    • varcasā: with brilliance
    • dīrghāyur: long life
    • asyā: of this (wife)
    • yaḥ: who
    • patir: husband
    • jīvāti: lives
    • śaradaḥ: autumns
    • śatam: hundred
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra invokes Agni, the deity associated with fire, which symbolizes purity, transformation, and energy.
    • The phrase "punaḥ patnīm" suggests a desire for a new beginning or renewal in relationships, particularly in the context of marriage.
    • The mention of "āyuṣā saha varcasā" emphasizes the importance of longevity and brilliance in life, indicating that a fulfilling life is characterized by both vitality and radiance.
    • The concluding part, "dīrghāyur asyā yaḥ patir jīvāti śaradaḥ śatam," expresses a wish for the husband to live a long and prosperous life, ideally for a hundred years, which reflects the cultural value placed on longevity and stability in relationships.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern weddings, couples often seek blessings for a long and happy life together, similar to the wishes expressed in this mantra.
    • In many cultures, the idea of renewal in relationships can be seen in practices such as vow renewals, where couples reaffirm their commitment to each other.
    • Health and wellness practices that promote longevity, such as yoga and meditation, resonate with the mantra's emphasis on a long life filled with vitality.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of relationships, the desire for renewal, and the importance of longevity and brilliance in life.
    • It serves as a reminder of the values of commitment, vitality, and the blessings sought in marital life.
    • In real life, it encourages individuals to cherish their relationships, seek renewal when needed, and aspire for a life filled with health and happiness.
