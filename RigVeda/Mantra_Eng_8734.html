सहस्रधारं वृषभं पयोवृद्धं प्रियं देवाय जनमने |ऋतेन य यृतजातो विवावृद्धे राजा देव ऋतं बृहत् ||
sahasradhāraṁ vṛṣabham payovṛdham priyaṁ devāya janmane | ṛtena ya ṛtajāto vivāvṛdhe rājā deva ṛtam bṛhat ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sahasradhāraṁ: One with a thousand streams or sources.
    • vṛṣabham: The bull, symbolizing strength and power.
    • payovṛdham: One who increases nourishment or abundance.
    • priyaṁ: Dear or beloved.
    • devāya: To the divine or to the gods.
    • janmane: At birth or in existence.
    • ṛtena: By truth or through righteousness.
    • ya: Who.
    • ṛtajāto: Born of truth or righteousness.
    • vivāvṛdhe: Who has grown or expanded.
    • rājā: King or ruler.
    • deva: God or divine being.
    • ṛtam: The principle of order, truth, or cosmic law.
    • bṛhat: Great or vast.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of a divine entity that embodies strength, abundance, and truth.
    • The phrase "sahasradhāraṁ vṛṣabham" suggests a powerful force that has multiple sources of strength, akin to a leader or a guiding principle in life.
    • "Payovṛdham" indicates the nurturing aspect, reminding us of the importance of sustenance and support in our lives.
    • The mantra emphasizes the significance of truth and righteousness ("ṛtena") as foundational elements for growth and prosperity.
    • It portrays the divine as a ruler ("rājā") who governs the principles of order and truth in the universe.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In leadership, a successful leader often embodies the qualities of strength and nurturing, guiding their team with integrity and support.
    • In personal life, individuals who adhere to truth and righteousness often find themselves in positions of respect and authority, much like the "rājā" in the mantra.
    • Communities that prioritize truth and support for one another tend to thrive, reflecting the abundance suggested by "payovṛdham."
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of strength, nurturing, and truth in our lives.
    • It encourages individuals to embody these qualities in their personal and professional lives, fostering growth and prosperity.
    • By aligning ourselves with these principles, we can create a harmonious existence that reflects the vastness and greatness of the divine order.
