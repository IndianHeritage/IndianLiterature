आ सूर्यो अरुहच चुक्रम अर्णो 'युक्त यद धारितो वीटपृष्ठाः |उद्ना न नावम अनयन्त धीराः आशृण्वतीर आपो अर्वाग अतिष्ठन ||
ā sūryo aruhac chukram arṇo 'yukta yad dharito vītapṛṣṭhāḥ | udnā na nāvam anayanta dhīrā āśṛṇvatīr āpo arvāg atiṣṭhan ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā: from
    • sūryo: the sun
    • aruhac: has risen
    • chukram: bright or pure
    • arṇo: of the ocean
    • 'yukta: connected or united
    • yad: which
    • dhariṭo: has been held or supported
    • vītapṛṣṭhāḥ: with the back of the tree
    • udnā: the waters
    • na: not
    • nāvam: the boat
    • anayanta: they carried
    • dhīrā: the wise or courageous
    • āśṛṇvatīr: those who listen
    • āpo: waters
    • arvāg: flowing down
    • atiṣṭhan: have stood or remained
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the sun rising and illuminating the world with its pure light.
    • The connection between the sun and the ocean signifies the unity of different elements of nature.
    • The wise individuals who listen to the teachings of nature are compared to boats that navigate through the waters of life.
    • The waters are depicted as flowing down, symbolizing the continuous flow of knowledge and wisdom.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • Just as the sun rises each day, bringing light and warmth, we can draw parallels to the new opportunities that each day presents in our lives.
    • The ocean represents the vastness of knowledge and experiences we encounter, while the sun symbolizes enlightenment and understanding.
    • In a team setting, the wise individuals who listen and learn from each other can be seen as the boats that navigate through challenges, ensuring collective success.
    • The continuous flow of water can be likened to the ongoing journey of personal growth and learning, where we must remain open to new ideas and experiences.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of enlightenment, unity, and the pursuit of knowledge.
    • It teaches us to appreciate the interconnectedness of nature and our lives, encouraging us to be wise and attentive to the lessons around us.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire individuals to embrace new beginnings, foster teamwork, and remain open to continuous learning.
    • Ultimately, it serves as a reminder that wisdom and understanding are essential for navigating the complexities of life.
