आ त्वा मदाच्युतā हरī श्येनम् पक्षेव वक्षतः |दिवो अमुष्य शासतो दिवं यया दिवावसो ||
ā tvā madacyutā harī śyenam pakṣeva vakṣataḥ | divo amuṣya śāsato divaṁ yaya divāvaso ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā: From
    • tvā: You
    • madacyutā: Unfailing in intoxication
    • harī: The divine or the golden one
    • śyenam: Like a falcon
    • pakṣeva: Like a wing
    • vakṣataḥ: Protecting or guarding
    • divo: Of the sky
    • amuṣya: Of this
    • śāsato: By the command
    • divaṁ: Heaven
    • yaya: By which
    • divāvaso: Dwelling in the day
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes a divine presence, asking for protection and guidance.
    • The imagery of a falcon suggests swiftness and keen vision, symbolizing the ability to see beyond the ordinary.
    • The phrase "unfailing in intoxication" can be interpreted as a call for a state of bliss or heightened awareness, often sought in spiritual practices.
    • The reference to the sky and heaven indicates a connection to the divine and the higher realms of existence.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, individuals often seek guidance and protection in challenging situations, much like the invocation in this mantra.
    • For instance, a student preparing for an important exam may recite this mantra to seek clarity and focus, akin to the falcon's sharp vision.
    • In moments of uncertainty, people may turn to spiritual practices or meditation, aiming to achieve a state of bliss and heightened awareness.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking divine guidance and protection in our lives.
    • It serves as a reminder to cultivate awareness and clarity, much like the falcon that navigates the skies with precision.
    • In a broader sense, it encourages individuals to connect with the divine and seek higher understanding, applicable in various life scenarios such as education, personal challenges, and spiritual growth.
