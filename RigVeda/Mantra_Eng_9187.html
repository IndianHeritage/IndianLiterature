यस्य ते विश्वा भुवनानि केतुन pra चेरते न च विश्वन्ते अक्तुभिः |अनागास्त्वेन हरिकेश सूर्याह्नाह्ना नो वस्यसां-वस्यसोधि इहि ||
yasya te viśvā bhuvanāni ketunā pra cerate ni ca viśante aktubhiḥ | anāgāstvena harikeśa sūryāhnāhnā no vasyasā-vasyasod ihi ||
  • Mantra: "yasya te viśvā bhuvanāni ketunā pra cerate ni ca viśante aktubhiḥ | anāgāstvena harikeśa sūryāhnāhnā no vasyasā-vasyasod ihi ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yasya: of whom
    • te: your
    • viśvā: all
    • bhuvanāni: worlds
    • ketunā: by the flag or banner
    • pra cerate: move forward
    • ni ca: and also
    • viśante: enter
    • aktubhiḥ: with strength or power
    • anāgāstvena: without sin or fault
    • harikeśa: O Lord with green hair (a name for Lord Vishnu)
    • sūryāhnāhnā: of the sun
    • no: not
    • vasyasā-vasyasod: under control or influence
    • ihi: here
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra speaks of the divine presence that governs all worlds and the power that allows beings to move forward and enter into new realms.
    • The phrase "yasya te viśvā bhuvanāni ketunā" suggests that the divine (often associated with Lord Vishnu) is the guiding force behind all existence, akin to a flag that leads an army.
    • The idea of moving forward "pra cerate" indicates progress and evolution, much like how individuals strive for personal growth in their lives.
    • The mention of "anāgāstvena" emphasizes purity and the absence of sin, which can be related to ethical living and moral integrity in real life.
    • In a practical sense, this mantra can be seen as a reminder for individuals to pursue their goals with integrity and to seek guidance from a higher power or moral compass.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, leaders often act as guiding forces for their teams, much like the divine presence mentioned in the mantra. They set the vision (the flag) and inspire their teams to move forward.
    • In personal development, individuals may seek to overcome challenges and move into new phases of life, embodying the essence of "pra cerate" as they strive for self-improvement.
    • Ethical dilemmas in everyday life can be approached with the mindset of "anāgāstvena," encouraging individuals to act with integrity and avoid actions that may lead to regret.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the divine guidance available to us as we navigate through life.
    • It emphasizes the importance of moving forward with purpose, integrity, and a sense of connection to a higher power.
    • In essence, it encourages individuals to align their actions with ethical principles and to seek progress in their personal and communal lives.
