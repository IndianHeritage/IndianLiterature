आ यः पप्रौ भानुना रोडसी उभे धूमेन धावते दिवि |तिरस तमो ददृश उर्म्याश्व आ श्यावाश्व अरुषो वृषा श्याव अरुषो वृषा ||
ā yaḥ paprau bhānunā rodasī ubhe dhūmena dhāvate divi | tiras tamo dadṛśa ūrmyāsv ā śyāvāsv aruṣo vṛṣā śyāvā aruṣo vṛṣā ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā yaḥ: He who
    • paprau: has spread out
    • bhānunā: with light
    • rodasī: the two worlds (heavens and earth)
    • ubhe: both
    • dhūmena: with smoke
    • dhāvate: runs or moves
    • divi: in the sky
    • tiras: across
    • tamo: darkness
    • dadṛśa: has seen
    • ūrmyāsv: in the waves
    • ā: from
    • śyāvāsv: the dark
    • aruṣo: the red
    • vṛṣā: the bull
    • śyāvā: dark
    • aruṣo: red
    • vṛṣā: bull
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of a divine entity that illuminates the two realms of existence, the earthly and the celestial.
    • The imagery of light spreading across the worlds signifies knowledge, enlightenment, and the dispelling of ignorance.
    • The mention of smoke suggests the presence of challenges or obstacles that may obscure clarity, yet the divine light continues to shine through.
    • The reference to darkness and waves indicates the tumultuous nature of life, where one must navigate through confusion and uncertainty.
    • The colors mentioned, dark and red, symbolize different aspects of existence—darkness representing ignorance or challenges, and red symbolizing vitality, strength, and passion.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In personal development, individuals often face challenges (smoke) that cloud their judgment. Seeking knowledge (light) helps them navigate through these difficulties.
    • In a professional setting, a leader may encounter obstacles (darkness) but must inspire their team (light) to overcome these challenges and achieve success.
    • In relationships, misunderstandings (smoke) can create distance, but open communication (light) can bridge the gap and foster connection.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking knowledge and enlightenment in the face of challenges and darkness.
    • It serves as a reminder that despite the obstacles we encounter, the pursuit of understanding and clarity can lead us to a more fulfilling existence.
    • In real life, applying the principles of this mantra can help individuals and communities navigate through difficulties, fostering resilience and growth.
