यं त्वा पूर्वम् ईळितो वध्र्यश्वः समीदहे अग्ने स इदं जुषस्व |स नः स्तिपा उता भवā तनूपा दात्रं रक्षस्व यद इदं ते अस्मे ||
yaṁ tvā pūrvam īḻito vadhryaśvaḥ samīdhe agne sa idaṁ juṣasva | sa naḥ stipā uta bhavā tanūpā dātraṁ rakṣasva yad idaṁ te asme ||
  • Mantra: "yaṁ tvā pūrvam īḻito vadhryaśvaḥ samīdhe agne sa idaṁ juṣasva | sa naḥ stipā uta bhavā tanūpā dātraṁ rakṣasva yad idaṁ te asme ||"
  • Translation: "O Agni, whom we have invoked before, may you accept this offering. May you, who are strong and powerful, grant us protection and support, and may you keep us safe from harm."
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yaṁ: whom
    • tvā: you
    • pūrvam: before
    • īḻito: invoked
    • vadhryaśvaḥ: strong and powerful
    • samīdhe: may accept
    • agne: O Agni (the fire god)
    • sa: may he
    • idaṁ: this
    • juṣasva: enjoy
    • sa: may he
    • naḥ: our
    • stipā: protection
    • uta: and
    • bhavā: may you be
    • tanūpā: supportive
    • dātraṁ: grant
    • rakṣasva: protect
    • yad: that
    • idaṁ: this
    • te: of you
    • asme: to us
  • Significance:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Agni, the fire god, who symbolizes energy, transformation, and purification.
    • It emphasizes the importance of invoking divine support and protection in our lives.
    • In real life, this can be related to seeking guidance and strength during challenging times, such as during a personal crisis or when facing difficult decisions.
    • For example, when starting a new venture or project, individuals often seek blessings for success and safety, akin to invoking Agni's protection.
  • Conclusion:
    • The mantra serves as a reminder of the need for divine assistance and the importance of maintaining a connection with higher powers.
    • It encourages individuals to seek strength and support from the universe, especially in times of uncertainty.
    • By reciting this mantra, one can cultivate a sense of security and resilience, fostering a positive mindset in the face of life's challenges.
