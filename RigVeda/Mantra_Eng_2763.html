ओ षु स्वसारः कारवे श्रिणोत ययौ वो दूराद अनसा रथेन |नि षू नमध्वम् भवता सुपारा अधोक्षाः सिन्धवः श्रोत्याभिः ||
o ṣu svasāraḥ kārave śṛṇota yayau vo dūrād anasā rathena | ni ṣū namadhvam bhavatā supārā adhoakṣāḥ sindhavaḥ srotyābhiḥ ||
  • Mantra: "o ṣu svasāraḥ kārave śṛṇota yayau vo dūrād anasā rathena | ni ṣū namadhvam bhavatā supārā adhoakṣāḥ sindhavaḥ srotyābhiḥ ||"
  • Translation: "O rivers, may you hear our call from afar, as you flow swiftly on your course. May you bring us prosperity and abundance, and may your waters be pure and nourishing."
  • Word-by-Word Explanation:
    • O: A call or invocation.
    • ṣu: Good or auspicious.
    • svasāraḥ: Referring to the rivers or flowing waters.
    • kārave: To hear or listen.
    • śṛṇota: May you hear.
    • yayau: To go or flow.
    • vo: To you (the rivers).
    • dūrād: From afar.
    • anasā: Swiftly or without obstruction.
    • rathena: Like a chariot.
    • ni: Not or do not.
    • ṣū: To be obstructed.
    • namadhvam: May you be honored or revered.
    • bhavatā: By you (the rivers).
    • supārā: Abundant or prosperous.
    • adhokṣāḥ: Pure or untainted.
    • sindhavaḥ: Rivers or streams.
    • srotyābhiḥ: With your waters.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In agriculture, rivers are vital for irrigation. Farmers often pray for the rivers to flow abundantly, ensuring their crops receive enough water.
    • Communities living near rivers depend on them for drinking water and sanitation. The mantra reflects the reverence for these water bodies that sustain life.
    • In modern times, environmentalists advocate for the protection of rivers, recognizing their importance in maintaining ecological balance and supporting biodiversity.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its acknowledgment of the life-giving properties of rivers. It serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of nature and humanity. By invoking the rivers, we express gratitude and seek their blessings for prosperity and purity. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire sustainable practices, respect for natural resources, and a commitment to preserving water bodies for future generations. It emphasizes the importance of maintaining a harmonious relationship with nature, which is crucial for our survival and well-being.
