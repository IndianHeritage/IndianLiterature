विषेण भङ्गुरावतः प्रति स्म रक्षसो दह |अग्ने तिग्मेन शोचिषा तपुरग्राभिर ऋष्टिभिः ||
viṣeṇa bhaṅgurāvataḥ prati ṣma rakṣaso daha | agne tigmena śociṣā tapuragrābhir ṛṣṭibhiḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • viṣeṇa: with the poison or harmful forces
    • bhaṅgurāvataḥ: may we be protected from the unstable or dangerous
    • prati: against
    • ṣma: may we be safe
    • rakṣaso: from the demons or evil forces
    • daha: burn or destroy
    • agne: O Agni (the fire god)
    • tigmena: with great brilliance or intensity
    • śociṣā: with light or radiance
    • tapuragrābhir: with the power of austerity
    • ṛṣṭibhiḥ: with blessings or boons
  • Overall Meaning: This mantra is a prayer for protection from harmful forces and evil entities. It invokes Agni, the fire god, to burn away negativity and provide safety through his radiant and powerful presence. The mantra emphasizes the importance of spiritual strength and the blessings that come from righteous living and austerity.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a challenging work environment, individuals may recite this mantra to seek protection from negative influences and to foster a positive atmosphere.
    • During times of personal crisis or emotional turmoil, people might use this mantra as a form of meditation to invoke inner strength and clarity.
    • In community gatherings or rituals, this mantra can be chanted to collectively seek protection from external threats and to promote harmony among participants.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine protection and the removal of negativity. It serves as a reminder of the power of spiritual practices and the importance of maintaining a positive mindset in the face of adversity. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as personal challenges, community support, and workplace dynamics, reinforcing the belief that spiritual strength can help overcome obstacles and foster a safe and harmonious environment.
