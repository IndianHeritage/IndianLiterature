एव ते गृत्समदाः शूर मन्मावस्यवो न वयुनानि तक्षुः |ब्रह्मण्यन्त इन्द्र ते नवीय इषम् ऊर्जं सुख्षितिं सुम्नम अश्युः ||
evā te gṛtsamadāḥ śūra manmāvasyavo na vayunāni takṣuḥ | brahmaṇyanta indra te navīya iṣam ūrjaṁ sukṣitiṁ sumnam aśyuḥ ||
  • evā te gṛtsamadāḥ: This phrase can be translated as "Thus, may we be endowed with strength." The term "gṛtsamadāḥ" refers to those who are strong and capable, suggesting a desire for empowerment and resilience.
  • śūra manmāvasyavo: Here, "śūra" means "brave" or "valiant," and "manmāvasyavo" can be interpreted as "those who are devoted to me." This indicates a call for courage and loyalty, emphasizing the importance of bravery in the face of challenges.
  • na vayunāni takṣuḥ: This translates to "may we not be hindered by obstacles." The word "vayunāni" refers to difficulties or barriers, while "takṣuḥ" implies the ability to overcome them. This part of the mantra encourages perseverance and the ability to navigate through life's challenges.
  • brahmaṇyanta indra te: "Brahmaṇyanta" refers to the ultimate reality or the divine, and "indra" is often associated with the king of gods, representing power and authority. This phrase suggests a connection to the divine for guidance and strength.
  • navīya iṣam: "Navīya" means "new" or "fresh," and "iṣam" refers to "desire" or "wish." This indicates a longing for new opportunities and aspirations, highlighting the importance of renewal and growth in life.
  • ūrjaṁ sukṣitiṁ sumnam aśyuḥ: "ūrjaṁ" means "energy" or "vitality," "sukṣitiṁ" refers to "well-being," and "sumnam" means "auspiciousness." "aśyuḥ" implies speed or swiftness. This part of the mantra expresses a desire for quick attainment of energy, well-being, and auspiciousness in life.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda encapsulates a profound message about strength, bravery, overcoming obstacles, divine guidance, renewal, and the pursuit of well-being. In real-life scenarios, it can be applied in various contexts such as personal development, leadership, and community building. For instance, individuals facing challenges in their careers can draw inspiration from this mantra to cultivate resilience and seek divine guidance. Similarly, leaders can embody the qualities of bravery and loyalty to inspire their teams, while communities can come together to support one another in overcoming obstacles. Ultimately, this mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of strength, courage, and the pursuit of a fulfilling life.
