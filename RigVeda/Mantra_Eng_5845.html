यद एमी प्रस्पुरन्न इव दृतिर न ध्मातो अद्रिवः |मृळा सुख्षात्र मृळय ||
yad emi prasphurann iva dṛtir na dhmāto adrivaḥ | mṛḻā sukṣatra mṛḻaya ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yad: that which
    • emi: I attain
    • prasphurann: shining or illuminating
    • iva: like
    • dṛtir: determination or resolve
    • na: not
    • dhmāto: blown or driven
    • adrivaḥ: by the wind or external forces
    • mṛḻā: gentle or soft
    • sukṣatra: auspicious or favorable
    • mṛḻaya: may you be gentle or soft
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the inner strength and determination that one can attain, which is not swayed by external forces.
    • The phrase "yad emi prasphurann iva dṛtir" suggests that the resolve one develops is akin to a shining light, illuminating the path ahead.
    • It emphasizes the importance of inner strength that remains unaffected by external challenges, represented by "na dhmāto adrivaḥ".
    • The concluding part, "mṛḻā sukṣatra mṛḻaya," calls for gentleness and auspiciousness, indicating that true strength is complemented by kindness and positivity.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, an individual may face numerous challenges and pressures. The mantra encourages them to maintain their resolve and not be swayed by external negativity, focusing instead on their goals.
    • In personal relationships, one might encounter conflicts or misunderstandings. The mantra serves as a reminder to approach these situations with gentleness and understanding, fostering a positive environment.
    • During times of stress or anxiety, individuals can reflect on this mantra to cultivate inner peace and resilience, allowing them to navigate difficulties with grace.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its powerful message about the balance between inner strength and gentleness.
    • It teaches that true determination is not just about being strong but also about being kind and positive in the face of adversity.
    • This mantra can be applied in various aspects of life, including personal growth, professional challenges, and interpersonal relationships, guiding individuals to remain steadfast yet gentle.
