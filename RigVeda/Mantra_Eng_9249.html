आ यात्व इन्द्रः स्वपतिर मदाय यो धर्मणा तूतुजानस तुविष्मान् |प्रत्वक्षाणो अति विश्वा सहांस्य अपारेण महता वृष्ण्येन ||
ā yātv indraḥ svapatir madāya yo dharmaṇā tūtujānas tuviṣmān | pratvakṣāṇo ati viśvā sahāṁsy apāreṇa mahatā vṛṣṇyena ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā yātv: May he come forth
    • indraḥ: Indra, the king of gods
    • svapatir: The one who is the lord of dreams
    • madāya: For the sake of joy or intoxication
    • yo: Who
    • dharmaṇā: By righteousness or duty
    • tūtujānas: Those who are devoted or worshippers
    • tuviṣmān: The one who is powerful or mighty
    • pratvakṣāṇo: The one who sees clearly
    • ati: Beyond
    • viśvā: All or universal
    • sahāṁsy: With strength or support
    • apāreṇa: Without limits or boundaries
    • mahatā: By the great
    • vṛṣṇyena: By the power of the Vrishnis (a clan of warriors)
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes Indra, the king of gods, asking him to come forth and bless the devotees with joy and strength.
    • It emphasizes the importance of righteousness (dharma) in achieving one's goals and the power of devotion.
    • The phrase "pratvakṣāṇo ati viśvā" suggests that true vision and understanding come from a place of righteousness and devotion.
    • The mention of "apāreṇa mahatā vṛṣṇyena" indicates that the blessings and strength derived from divine powers are limitless and can empower individuals to overcome challenges.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, a leader who embodies righteousness and supports their team can inspire loyalty and drive success, much like Indra's blessings.
    • In personal life, individuals who practice ethical behavior and remain devoted to their values often find themselves supported by their community, reflecting the mantra's essence.
    • During challenging times, people often turn to spiritual practices or community support, seeking the strength and clarity that this mantra invokes.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of divine support in our endeavors and the role of righteousness in achieving success.
    • It teaches that true strength comes from a clear vision and ethical living, encouraging individuals to seek divine guidance in their pursuits.
    • In real life, this mantra can be applied in various situations, from personal challenges to professional endeavors, reminding us to act with integrity and seek support from higher powers.
