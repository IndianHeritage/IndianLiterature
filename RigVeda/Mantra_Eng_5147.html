मन्त्र: सेद अग्निर अग्नीम्̐र अत्य अस्त्व अन्यान यत्र वाजी तनयो वीळुपाणिः | सहस्रपाथा अक्षरा समेति ||
sed agnir agnīm̐r aty astv anyān yatra vājī tanayo vīḻupāṇiḥ | sahasrapāthā akṣarā sameti ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sed: but
    • agnir: fire
    • agnīm̐r: the fire (as a deity)
    • aty: beyond
    • astv: may it be
    • anyān: other
    • yatra: where
    • vājī: the one who is strong or powerful
    • tanayo: the son
    • vīḻupāṇiḥ: with many paths
    • sahasrapāthā: with a thousand paths
    • akṣarā: imperishable
    • sameti: comes together
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the divine aspect of fire, representing both physical and spiritual illumination.
    • The phrase "but fire, may it be beyond other" suggests that the essence of fire transcends ordinary existence, symbolizing purity and transformation.
    • The mention of "the one who is strong" indicates the power of fire to provide energy and warmth, akin to how a strong leader can guide and protect.
    • "With many paths" and "with a thousand paths" signifies the various ways in which fire can manifest in life, such as in cooking, rituals, and as a source of energy.
    • The term "imperishable" emphasizes the eternal nature of fire, paralleling the enduring qualities of knowledge and wisdom.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In cooking, fire transforms raw ingredients into nourishing meals, much like how knowledge transforms ignorance into wisdom.
    • In rituals, fire is often used as a medium to connect with the divine, symbolizing purification and the offering of intentions.
    • In nature, fire plays a crucial role in ecosystems, promoting new growth after a burn, similar to how challenges in life can lead to personal growth.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of fire as a symbol of transformation, strength, and the interconnectedness of life.
    • Its application in real life can be seen in various aspects, from personal development to spiritual practices.
    • Understanding the deeper meanings of such mantras can inspire individuals to harness their inner strength and navigate life's challenges with resilience.
