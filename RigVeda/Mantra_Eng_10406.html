आग्ने स्थूरं रयिम भर पृतुं गोमन्तम अश्विनम् |अङ्धि खं वर्तयाऽ पाणिम् ||
āgne sthūraṁ rayim bhara pṛthuṁ gomantam aśvinam | aṅdhi khaṁ vartayā paṇim ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • āgne: O Agni (the fire god)
    • sthūraṁ: substantial, abundant
    • rayim: wealth, prosperity
    • bhara: may you bring, fill
    • pṛthuṁ: vast, great
    • gomantam: possessing cows (symbolizing wealth)
    • aśvinam: O Aśvins (the twin gods of medicine and health)
    • aṅdhi: may you remove, dispel
    • khaṁ: the sky, space
    • vartayā: by your action, through your influence
    • paṇim: my hand, my effort
  • Overall Meaning: This mantra is a prayer to Agni, the fire god, asking him to bestow abundant wealth and prosperity. It also invokes the Aśvins to remove obstacles and enhance one's efforts, symbolized by the hand.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, invoking Agni can symbolize the desire for success and growth. For instance, a startup may recite this mantra during a launch event to seek blessings for prosperity.
    • In personal life, individuals may chant this mantra during significant life events, such as weddings or housewarming ceremonies, to invite abundance and dispel negativity.
    • In health and wellness, the mention of Aśvins can be related to seeking good health and vitality, encouraging individuals to take care of their physical and mental well-being.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its invocation of divine forces to attract wealth, health, and prosperity while removing obstacles. It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, such as business ventures, personal milestones, and health practices. By reciting this mantra, individuals can align their intentions with the energies of abundance and well-being, fostering a positive environment for growth and success.
