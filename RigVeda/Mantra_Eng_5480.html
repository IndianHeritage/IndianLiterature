प्र ब्रह्मैतु सदनाद ऋतस्य वि रश्मिभिः ससृजे सूर्यो गाः |वि सानुनā पृथिवी सस्र उर्वी पृथु प्रतीकम अध्य एधे अग्निः ||
pra brahmaitu sadanād ṛtasya vi raśmibhiḥ sasṛje sūryo gāḥ | vi sānunā pṛthivī sasra urvī pṛthu pratīkam adhy edhe agniḥ ||
  • pra brahmaitu sadanād: This phrase can be interpreted as "from the ultimate reality or the supreme consciousness." In real life, this can be related to the pursuit of knowledge and understanding of the universe, akin to scientific exploration or philosophical inquiry.
  • ṛtasya vi raśmibhiḥ: This translates to "by the rays of truth." Here, "ṛta" signifies truth or cosmic order. In everyday life, this can be seen in the importance of honesty and integrity in our actions and decisions, reflecting how truth illuminates our path.
  • sasṛje sūryo gāḥ: This means "the sun created the cows." Symbolically, cows represent nourishment and sustenance. In a practical sense, this can relate to how the sun is essential for agriculture, providing the energy needed for crops to grow, which in turn sustains life.
  • vi sānunā pṛthivī: This translates to "with the support of the earth." It emphasizes the interconnectedness of all life. In real life, this can be seen in sustainable practices that respect and nurture the environment, ensuring that we live in harmony with nature.
  • sasra urvī pṛthu pratīkam: This means "the vast earth is the foundation." It highlights the importance of a stable and nurturing environment. In modern contexts, this can relate to the significance of land conservation and the need for a solid foundation in our lives, whether it be emotional, financial, or social.
  • adhy edhe agniḥ: This translates to "may the fire rise." Fire symbolizes energy, transformation, and purification. In real life, this can be seen in the drive for innovation and change, where the 'fire' represents our passion and motivation to improve ourselves and our surroundings.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of creation, truth, and the interconnectedness of life. It signifies the importance of understanding our place in the universe, the role of truth in guiding our actions, and the necessity of nurturing our environment. In real life, these principles can be applied in various ways, such as promoting sustainability, fostering honesty in relationships, and embracing change for personal growth. By reflecting on this mantra, individuals can find inspiration to lead a life that is harmonious with the cosmos and beneficial to all living beings.
