न त्वा गभीरः पुरुहूत सिन्दुर नाद्रयः परि षन्तो वरन्त |इत्त्था सकिभ्य इषितो यद इन्द्रा दृढं चिद अरुजो गव्यं ऊर्वम् ||
na tvā gabhīraḥ puruhūta sindhur nādrayaḥ pari ṣanto varanta | itthā sakhibhya iṣito yad indrā dṛḻhaṁ cid arujo gavyam ūrvam ||
  • na tvā gabhīraḥ puruhūta sindhur: This phrase can be translated as "the deep ocean, O many-named one, does not conceal you." Here, the "deep ocean" symbolizes the vast and often unfathomable aspects of life and existence. In real life, this can relate to the challenges and complexities we face, which may seem overwhelming but do not hide the essence of our being.
  • nādrayaḥ pari ṣanto varanta: This translates to "the waters do not hold you back." It signifies that despite the obstacles (represented by water), one can still move forward. In practical terms, this can be seen in situations where individuals face difficulties, such as in career challenges or personal struggles, yet find ways to overcome them and achieve their goals.
  • itthā sakhibhya iṣito yad indrā: This means "thus, O Indra, you are invoked by friends." Indra, in Vedic literature, is often seen as a deity of strength and power. This line emphasizes the importance of community and support from friends in overcoming life's challenges. In real life, this can be illustrated by the support systems we build, such as friendships, family, or professional networks that help us navigate tough times.
  • dṛḻhaṁ cid arujo gavyam ūrvam: This translates to "the strong, unblemished, and nourishing earth." It symbolizes stability and sustenance. In everyday life, this can be related to the foundational aspects of our lives, such as our values, beliefs, and the environment we create around us, which provide us with strength and nourishment to thrive.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of resilience, community support, and the importance of a strong foundation in life. It teaches us that while we may face deep and challenging waters, we are not alone, and with the support of friends and a solid grounding in our values, we can navigate through life's complexities. This mantra is applicable in various real-life scenarios, such as during personal crises, professional challenges, or when seeking to build a supportive community. It serves as a reminder that strength lies not just within ourselves but also in the connections we foster with others and the stability we create in our lives.
