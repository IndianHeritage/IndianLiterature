त्रिर्यातुधानः प्रसितिं त एत्व ऋतं यो अग्ने अनृतेन हन्ति |तमर्चिषा स्फूर्जयञ जातवेदः समक्षम एनं गृणते न वृङ्धि ||
trir yātudhānaḥ prasitiṁ ta etv ṛtaṁ yo agne anṛtena hanti | tam arciṣā sphūrjayañ jātavedaḥ samakṣam enaṁ gṛṇate ni vṛṅdhi ||
  • Mantra: "trir yātudhānaḥ prasitiṁ ta etv ṛtaṁ yo agne anṛtena hanti | tam arciṣā sphūrjayañ jātavedaḥ samakṣam enaṁ gṛṇate ni vṛṅdhi ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • trir: three times
    • yātudhānaḥ: the one who is a sorcerer or a malevolent being
    • prasitiṁ: the power or strength
    • ta: that
    • etv: indeed
    • ṛtaṁ: the truth or cosmic order
    • yo: who
    • agne: O Agni (the fire god)
    • anṛtena: by falsehood or deceit
    • hanti: destroys
    • tam: that
    • arciṣā: with light or brilliance
    • sphūrjayañ: may he shine forth
    • jātavedaḥ: the one who knows all beings (Agni)
    • samakṣam: in front of us
    • enaṁ: him
    • gṛṇate: praises
    • ni vṛṅdhi: do not obstruct
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra invokes Agni, the fire god, who is often seen as a mediator between humans and the divine.
    • The phrase "trir yātudhānaḥ" suggests a recognition of the threefold nature of malevolent forces that can disrupt harmony.
    • It emphasizes the importance of truth ("ṛtaṁ") and the destructive power of deceit ("anṛtena hanti").
    • The mantra calls for Agni to shine forth with brilliance, illuminating the path of truth and righteousness.
    • It serves as a reminder to not obstruct the divine light and guidance that Agni provides.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace, this mantra can be applied to encourage transparency and honesty among colleagues, fostering a positive environment.
    • In personal relationships, it highlights the importance of trust and truthfulness, warning against the destructive nature of lies.
    • In community settings, it can serve as a call to action against malevolent influences, urging individuals to stand for justice and integrity.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the eternal struggle between truth and deceit, urging individuals to seek the light of truth in their lives.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, reminding us of the importance of integrity, honesty, and the pursuit of righteousness.
    • By invoking Agni, we are encouraged to illuminate our paths and resist the forces that seek to lead us astray.
