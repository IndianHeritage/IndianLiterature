तं वां रथं वयम अद्याहुवेमा स्तोमैर अश्विनाऽ सुविताय नव्यम् |अरीष्टनेमिम् परि द्यां इयाणं विद्यामेषं वृजनं जीरदानुम् ||
taṁ vāṁ rathaṁ vayam adyā huvema stomair aśvinā suvitāya navyam | ariṣṭanemim pari dyām iyānaṁ vidyāmeṣaṁ vṛjanaṁ jīradānum ||
  • taṁ vāṁ rathaṁ vayam adyā huvema: This phrase can be translated to "We invoke that chariot of yours today." Here, the "chariot" symbolizes the vehicle of progress and success. In real life, this can be likened to the aspirations and goals we set for ourselves, which propel us forward in our personal and professional journeys.
  • stomair aśvinā suvitāya navyam: This translates to "With hymns, we call upon the Ashvins for the new." The Ashvins are twin deities associated with health, medicine, and the dawn. This part emphasizes the importance of seeking divine assistance in our endeavors, especially when embarking on new ventures. For example, when starting a new job or project, one might seek guidance and support from mentors or spiritual practices.
  • ariṣṭanemim pari dyām iyānaṁ: This means "May this chariot lead us to the heavens." It signifies the aspiration to reach higher states of being or consciousness. In practical terms, this can relate to personal growth, education, and the pursuit of knowledge. For instance, a student striving for academic excellence is metaphorically riding this chariot towards their goals.
  • vidyāmeṣaṁ vṛjanaṁ jīradānum: This translates to "This knowledge is the source of our vitality." Knowledge is portrayed as essential for life and progress. In today's world, continuous learning and adaptation are crucial for success in various fields, such as technology, healthcare, and business. For example, professionals often engage in lifelong learning to stay relevant in their careers.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of invoking divine support for our journeys, emphasizing the importance of knowledge and aspiration. In real life, it serves as a reminder to seek guidance, embrace new opportunities, and continuously strive for personal and professional growth. By recognizing the significance of our goals and the support we can receive, we can navigate life's challenges with greater confidence and purpose.
