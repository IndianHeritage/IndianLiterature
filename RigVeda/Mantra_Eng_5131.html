यत्र बाणाः सम्पतन्ति कुमाराः विशिखा इव |तत्र नो ब्रह्मणस पतिर अदितिः शर्मा यच्छतु विश्वाहा शर्मा यच्छतु ||
yatra bāṇāḥ sampatanti kumārā viśikhā iva | tatrā no brahmaṇas patir aditiḥ śarma yacchatu viśvāhā śarma yacchatu ||
  • Mantra: "yatra bāṇāḥ sampatanti kumārā viśikhā iva | tatrā no brahmaṇas patir aditiḥ śarma yacchatu viśvāhā śarma yacchatu ||"
  • Translation: "Where the arrows (of knowledge) strike like the arrows of a young warrior, there may the Lord of Brahman grant us protection, and may Aditi (the infinite) bestow upon us all forms of well-being."

  • Word-by-Word Explanation:
    • yatra: where
    • bāṇāḥ: arrows
    • sampatanti: strike or reach
    • kumārā: young warriors
    • viśikhā: like arrows
    • tatrā: there
    • no: for us
    • brahmaṇas: of Brahman (the ultimate reality)
    • patir: lord or master
    • aditiḥ: Aditi (the infinite)
    • śarma: protection or well-being
    • yacchatu: may grant
    • viśvāhā: all forms

  • Contextual Significance:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of knowledge and wisdom, symbolized by arrows, which can strike effectively when aimed correctly, much like a young warrior in battle.
    • The invocation of Brahman and Aditi signifies a plea for divine protection and well-being, suggesting that true strength comes from a higher power.

  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a competitive environment, such as a workplace or academic setting, individuals often need to harness their skills (arrows) effectively to achieve success. This mantra serves as a reminder to seek guidance and protection from a higher source, whether through mentorship, spirituality, or community support.
    • In personal challenges, such as health issues or emotional struggles, invoking the essence of this mantra can encourage individuals to seek holistic well-being, recognizing that support from the universe (Aditi) can manifest in various forms, including love, friendship, and professional help.

  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra encapsulates the idea that while we may strive to achieve our goals with our own efforts (arrows), it is essential to acknowledge and seek the blessings of a higher power for protection and well-being.
    • In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios, from personal growth to professional endeavors, reminding us that we are not alone in our journeys and that divine support is always available.
