यानीन्द्राग्नि चक्रथुर वीर्याणि यानि रूपाण्य उता वृष्ण्याणि |या वाम प्रत्नानि सख्याः शिवानि तैभिः सोमस्य पिबतं सुतस्य ||
yānīndrāgnī cakrathur vīryāṇi yāni rūpāṇy uta vṛṣṇyāni | yā vām pratnāni sakhyā śivāni tebhiḥ somasya pibataṁ sutasya ||
  • yānīndrāgnī: This term refers to the divine forces or energies associated with Indra and Agni, two significant deities in Vedic literature. Indra is often seen as the king of gods and a god of rain and thunderstorms, while Agni represents fire and is a vital element in rituals.
  • cakrathur: This word can be interpreted as "they who have created or established." It signifies the act of creation or manifestation of strength and power.
  • vīryāṇi: This translates to "strengths" or "valors." It emphasizes the importance of courage and valor in human endeavors.
  • yāni rūpāṇy uta vṛṣṇyāni: This phrase refers to the various forms and manifestations of strength and vitality. It highlights the diversity of power and beauty in the universe.
  • yā vām pratnāni sakhyā śivāni: This part speaks to the auspicious and harmonious relationships among beings. It suggests that there are blessings and friendships that contribute to well-being.
  • tebhiḥ somasya pibataṁ sutasya: This line invites the listener to partake in the essence of Soma, a sacred drink associated with immortality and divine inspiration. It symbolizes the nourishment of the spirit and the pursuit of higher knowledge.

Conclusion: This mantra encapsulates the essence of divine energies, strength, and harmonious relationships. It emphasizes the importance of courage and the diverse manifestations of power in our lives. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied to personal growth, teamwork, and the pursuit of knowledge. By recognizing the divine forces at play and fostering positive relationships, individuals can cultivate a sense of purpose and strength in their endeavors. The invocation of Soma symbolizes the quest for spiritual nourishment, encouraging individuals to seek wisdom and inspiration in their daily lives.
