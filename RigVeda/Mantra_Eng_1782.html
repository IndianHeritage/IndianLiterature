एष वः स्तोमो मरुत इयं गीर मांदार्यस्य मान्यस्य कारोः |एषा यासिष्ट तंवे वयां विद्यामेषं वृजनं जीरदानुम् ||
eṣa vaḥ stomo maruta iyaṁ gīr māndāryasya mānyasya kāroḥ | eṣā yāsīṣṭa tanve vayāṁ vidyāmeṣaṁ vṛjanaṁ jīradānum ||
  • eṣa vaḥ stomo maruta: This phrase can be translated as "This hymn is for you, O Maruts." The Maruts are a group of storm deities in Vedic literature, often associated with the power of the wind and storms. They symbolize strength and vitality.
  • iyaṁ gīr māndāryasya mānyasya kāroḥ: This translates to "This song is from the revered and honored one." Here, "gīr" refers to a song or hymn, indicating the importance of vocal expression in worship and communication with the divine.
  • eṣā yāsīṣṭa tanve vayāṁ: This means "This is the one that has been desired for our bodies." It emphasizes the connection between the physical form and spiritual aspirations, suggesting that the divine blessings sought are meant to enhance our physical existence.
  • vidyāmeṣaṁ vṛjanaṁ jīradānum: This can be interpreted as "We seek knowledge and the essence of life." The term "vidyā" refers to knowledge or wisdom, while "jīradānum" suggests the vital essence or life force. This highlights the importance of knowledge in achieving a fulfilling life.

  • Significance: This mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking divine blessings for strength, knowledge, and vitality. It reflects the Vedic belief in the interconnectedness of the physical and spiritual realms.
  • Real-life Application: In contemporary life, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as during personal challenges or when seeking guidance in decision-making. For instance, individuals may recite this mantra before important events, like exams or job interviews, to invoke strength and clarity of mind.
  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of seeking divine support in our endeavors. It encourages individuals to strive for knowledge and strength, fostering a holistic approach to life that integrates both physical and spiritual well-being.
