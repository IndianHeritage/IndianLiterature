सम अज्र्यां पर्वत्या वसूनि दासाः वृत्राण्य आर्याः जिगेथ || शूर इव धृष्णुश्च्यवनानां त्वं अग्नि पृतनायूम् अभि ष्याः ||
sam ajryā parvatyā3 vasūni dāsā vṛtrāṇy āryā jigetha | śūra iva dhṛṣṇuś cyavano janānāṁ tvam agne pṛtanāyūm̐r abhi ṣyāḥ ||
  • Translation: "O Agni, you who are the conqueror of the powerful, who vanquishes the enemies, like a hero, you are the one who brings forth the wealth of the mountains and the treasures of the earth."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • Agni: The fire god, representing energy, transformation, and the divine messenger.
    • Vasūni: Wealth or treasures, often associated with material and spiritual abundance.
    • Dāsā: Enemies or obstacles that one must overcome.
    • Vṛtrāṇy: The powerful or formidable forces that stand in the way of progress.
    • Śūra: A hero or brave person, symbolizing courage and strength.
    • Dhṛṣṇuś: The one who is determined and resolute.
    • Cyavano: A reference to the act of overcoming or conquering.
    • Janānāṁ: Of the people or community.
    • Pṛtanāyūm̐r: The one who brings forth or generates.
    • Abhi ṣyāḥ: To be invoked or called upon.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, Agni can represent the entrepreneurial spirit that drives individuals to overcome challenges and achieve success. For instance, a startup founder who faces numerous obstacles but remains determined to bring their innovative product to market embodies the essence of Agni.
    • In personal development, the mantra can inspire individuals to confront their fears and limitations, much like a hero facing a daunting challenge. For example, someone overcoming a significant personal setback, such as recovering from an illness or pursuing a new career path, reflects the qualities of courage and determination highlighted in the mantra.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call to harness inner strength and resilience in the face of adversity. It emphasizes the importance of courage, determination, and the pursuit of wealth—both material and spiritual. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied to various challenges, encouraging individuals and communities to rise above obstacles, embrace their inner hero, and strive for abundance and success. By invoking Agni, one can seek guidance and support in their endeavors, fostering a mindset of victory and prosperity.
