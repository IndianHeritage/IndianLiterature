अभि प्रियाणि काव्यā विश्व चक्षाणो अर्षति |हरिस तुञ्जान आयुधा ||
abhi priyāṇi kāvyā viśvā cakṣāṇo arṣati | haris tuñjāna āyudhā ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • abhi: towards
    • priyāṇi: beloved, dear
    • kāvyā: poetic, literary
    • viśvā: all, universal
    • cakṣāṇo: seeing, perceiving
    • arṣati: to be inspired, to be moved
    • haris: the divine, often referring to Vishnu or a deity
    • tuñjāna: to be joyful, to rejoice
    • āyudhā: with weapons, tools, or means
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the connection between the beloved aspects of poetry and the universal vision that inspires creativity.
    • The phrase "abhi priyāṇi kāvyā" suggests that poetry and literature are cherished and should be approached with love and reverence.
    • "Viśvā cakṣāṇo arṣati" indicates that through this beloved poetry, one can gain a universal perspective, leading to inspiration and enlightenment.
    • The mention of "haris tuñjāna āyudhā" implies that the divine presence brings joy and empowerment, equipping individuals with the tools needed to express themselves and connect with the world.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In literature classes, students often explore beloved texts that inspire them to think critically and creatively, reflecting the essence of "abhi priyāṇi kāvyā."
    • Writers and poets often draw inspiration from universal themes found in nature, love, and human experience, embodying "viśvā cakṣāṇo arṣati."
    • Artistic expressions, such as music and dance, can be seen as modern-day interpretations of this mantra, where joy and divine inspiration come together to create something beautiful.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of poetry and literature as a means to connect with the divine and gain a broader understanding of life.
    • It encourages individuals to approach creative endeavors with love and reverence, recognizing the joy and empowerment that comes from such expressions.
    • In real life, this mantra can be applied in various fields such as education, art, and personal development, reminding us of the transformative power of creativity and inspiration.
