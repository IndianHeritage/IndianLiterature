उत त्ये नो मरुतो मन्दसाना धियं तोकं च वाजिनो 'वन्तु |मा नः परि ख्यद अक्षरा चरन्त्य अवीवृधन युज्यं ते रयिं नः ||
uta tye no maruto mandasānā dhiyaṁ tokaṁ ca vājino 'vantu | mā naḥ pari khyad akṣarā caranty avīvṛdhan yujyaṁ te rayiṁ naḥ ||
  • Translation: "May the Maruts, the swift ones, bring us intelligence and strength; may they not let our words be lost, and may they unite us with wealth."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • Uta: A call for blessings or assistance.
    • Tye: Refers to the Maruts, the storm deities associated with strength and vitality.
    • No: Us, indicating a collective appeal.
    • Mandasānā: Those who are swift or quick, referring to the Maruts.
    • Dhiyaṁ: Intelligence or wisdom.
    • Tokaṁ: Strength or vigor.
    • Vājino: Wealth or prosperity.
    • Avantu: May they come or may they arrive.
    • Mā: Not, a negation.
    • Naḥ: Our, indicating possession.
    • Pari: Around or about.
    • Khyad: To be lost or to disappear.
    • Akṣarā: Words or syllables.
    • Caranty: To move or to flow.
    • Avīvṛdhan: Not to be obstructed or hindered.
    • Yujyaṁ: To unite or to connect.
    • Te: Your, referring to the Maruts.
    • Rayiṁ: Wealth or abundance.
    • Naḥ: Our, again indicating possession.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, this mantra can be invoked during team meetings to foster collaboration and ensure that all voices are heard, promoting a culture of unity and shared goals.
    • In personal relationships, it can serve as a reminder to communicate openly and supportively, ensuring that misunderstandings do not arise and that connections remain strong.
    • During community gatherings, this mantra can be recited to encourage collective efforts towards prosperity and well-being, emphasizing the importance of working together for common goals.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for unity, intelligence, and prosperity. It emphasizes the importance of collective strength and the need for clear communication. In real-life scenarios, whether in personal relationships, workplaces, or community settings, invoking this mantra can inspire collaboration, enhance understanding, and promote a shared vision for success. By recognizing the power of unity and the importance of wisdom, individuals and groups can navigate challenges more effectively and achieve greater heights together.
