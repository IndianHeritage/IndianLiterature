सिषासतू रयीणां वाजेष्वर्वतामिव |भरेषु जिग्युषाम् असि ||
siṣāsatū rayīṇāṁ vājeṣv arvatām iva | bhareṣu jigyuṣām asi ||
  • siṣāsatū: This term can be interpreted as "to be strong" or "to be powerful." It signifies the importance of strength and resilience in life.
  • rayīṇāṁ: This word translates to "wealth" or "prosperity." It emphasizes the value of material and spiritual abundance.
  • vājeṣv: This can be understood as "in the context of chariots" or "in the realm of victory." It relates to the idea of success and triumph in endeavors.
  • arvatām: This term means "of the strong" or "of the powerful." It highlights the qualities of strength and capability.
  • iva: This word translates to "like" or "as." It is used for comparison, indicating a similarity or analogy.
  • bhareṣu: This means "in the burdens" or "in the responsibilities." It refers to the challenges and duties one must bear in life.
  • jigyuṣām: This term can be interpreted as "of those who strive" or "of those who are victorious." It signifies the efforts made to achieve success.
  • asi: This translates to "you are" or "you exist." It affirms existence and presence, often in a divine or powerful context.

The mantra can be understood as a call for strength and prosperity in the face of challenges. It emphasizes the importance of resilience and the pursuit of success, likening the journey to that of a powerful chariot moving towards victory.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios:

  • Personal Growth: Individuals facing challenges in their careers or personal lives can draw inspiration from this mantra to cultivate strength and resilience.
  • Business Success: Entrepreneurs can relate to the idea of striving for prosperity and overcoming obstacles, much like a chariot racing towards victory.
  • Community Support: In a community setting, this mantra can inspire collective strength and support, encouraging members to uplift one another in times of need.

In conclusion, this mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the importance of strength, resilience, and the pursuit of prosperity. It encourages individuals to embrace their challenges and strive for success, reinforcing the idea that with determination and support, one can overcome any obstacle and achieve their goals.
