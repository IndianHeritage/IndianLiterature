आरण्गरेव मध्वेरयेत सागरहेव गवि नीचीनबारे |कीनारेव स्वेदम आसिष्विदानां क्षामेवोर्जा सूयवसात सचेते ||
āraṅgareva madhv erayethe sāragheva gavi nīcīnabāre | kīnāreva svedam āsiṣvidānā kṣāmevorjā sūyavasāt sacethe ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • āraṅgareva: Like the sweet (madhura) essence
    • madhv: Sweetness
    • erayethe: May it flow
    • sāragheva: Like the river
    • gavi: In the cow
    • nīcīnabāre: In the lowly or humble
    • kīnāreva: Like the shore
    • svedam: Sweat or effort
    • āsiṣvidānā: Bestowing blessings
    • kṣāmevorjā: The energy of forgiveness
    • sūyavasāt: From the source of prosperity
    • sacethe: May it be so
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the essence of sweetness and the flow of blessings, likening them to natural elements such as rivers and cows, which are vital in Vedic culture.
    • The reference to "madhv" (sweetness) suggests the importance of positive qualities in life, such as kindness and compassion.
    • By comparing blessings to a river, it emphasizes the idea that blessings should flow freely and abundantly, nourishing all aspects of life.
    • The mention of "nīcīnabāre" (in the lowly) indicates that blessings and sweetness can be found even in humble circumstances, promoting humility and gratitude.
    • The phrase "kṣāmevorjā" (the energy of forgiveness) highlights the significance of forgiveness in achieving peace and prosperity.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community setting, acts of kindness and generosity can create a positive environment, much like the sweetness described in the mantra.
    • In personal relationships, practicing forgiveness can lead to stronger bonds and a more harmonious life, reflecting the energy of forgiveness mentioned.
    • In professional settings, fostering a culture of support and collaboration can lead to collective success, akin to the flowing blessings of the river.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of sweetness, humility, and forgiveness in our lives.
    • Its significance lies in encouraging individuals to cultivate positive qualities and to recognize the blessings that flow from acts of kindness and forgiveness.
    • In real-life scenarios, applying the teachings of this mantra can lead to personal growth, improved relationships, and a more compassionate society.
