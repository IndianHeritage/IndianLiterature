तस्य द्युमाम् असद् रथो देवजूतः स शूशुवत् |विश्वा वन्नन् अमित्रिया ||
tasya dyumām̐ asad ratho devajūtaḥ sa śūśuvat | viśvā vanvann amitriyā ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • tasya: of that
    • dyumām̐: radiant or shining
    • asad: not existing or unreal
    • ratho: chariot
    • devajūtaḥ: born of the gods
    • sa: he
    • śūśuvat: like a roaring sound
    • viśvā: all or universal
    • vanvann: flourishing or thriving
    • amitriyā: without enmity or hostility
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of a radiant chariot that is not merely a physical vehicle but symbolizes the journey of life and the pursuit of higher knowledge.
    • The phrase "devajūtaḥ" indicates that this journey is divinely inspired, suggesting that our aspirations and endeavors should align with a higher purpose.
    • The roaring sound "śūśuvat" can be interpreted as the powerful energy that drives us forward, akin to the motivation we feel when pursuing our dreams.
    • The term "viśvā vanvann" emphasizes the importance of flourishing in all aspects of life, promoting growth and prosperity.
    • Finally, "amitriyā" suggests that this journey should be undertaken without hostility, advocating for peace and harmony in our interactions with others.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, this mantra can inspire individuals to pursue their careers with passion and integrity, ensuring that their work contributes positively to society.
    • In personal relationships, it encourages individuals to foster connections based on mutual respect and understanding, avoiding conflicts and promoting harmony.
    • In community service, it can motivate people to engage in activities that promote the well-being of all, reflecting the idea of flourishing together without enmity.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of aligning our personal journeys with a higher purpose, embracing growth, and fostering peaceful relationships.
    • Its significance lies in its universal applicability, encouraging individuals to reflect on their actions and their impact on the world around them.
    • By embodying the principles outlined in this mantra, we can create a more harmonious and prosperous society, where everyone thrives together.
