आ नो भर व्यञ्जनं गाम अश्वम् अभ्यञ्जनम् |सचā मनā हिरण्यया ||
ā no bhara vyañjanaṁ gām aśvam abhyañjanam | sacā manā hiraṇyayā ||
  • Mantra: ā no bhara vyañjanaṁ gām aśvam abhyañjanam | sacā manā hiraṇyayā ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā: to us
    • no: our
    • bhara: bring or carry
    • vyañjanaṁ: nourishment or sustenance
    • gām: the cow or the community
    • aśvam: the horse or strength
    • abhyañjanam: to be adorned or decorated
    • sacā: and with
    • manā: mind or intellect
    • hiraṇyayā: golden or prosperous
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer for abundance and prosperity.
    • It calls for the nourishment of both the community (symbolized by the cow) and strength (symbolized by the horse).
    • The phrase "sacā manā hiraṇyayā" emphasizes the importance of a prosperous and enlightened mind.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community setting, this mantra can be applied to encourage collaboration and support among members to achieve common goals.
    • In personal development, it can inspire individuals to seek knowledge and wisdom, leading to a prosperous life.
    • In business, it can be a reminder to focus on ethical practices that benefit both the company and the community.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its holistic approach to prosperity, emphasizing the balance between material wealth and intellectual growth.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal development to community building and ethical business practices.
    • By invoking this mantra, individuals and communities can strive for a harmonious and prosperous existence, fostering both physical and mental well-being.
