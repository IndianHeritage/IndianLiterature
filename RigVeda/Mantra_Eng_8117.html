अस्य ते सख्ये वयं तवेन्दो द्युम्न उत्तमे |सासह्यामा पृथन्यतः ||
asya te sakhye vayaṁ tavendo dyumna uttame | sāsahyāma pṛtanyataḥ ||
  • Mantra: "asya te sakhye vayaṁ tavendo dyumna uttame | sāsahyāma pṛtanyataḥ ||"
  • Translation: "In friendship, we are united with you, O Lord; may we attain the highest glory and strength together."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • asya: of this
    • te: your
    • sakhye: in friendship
    • vayaṁ: we
    • tavendo: O Lord
    • dyumna: glory or brilliance
    • uttame: highest or supreme
    • sāsahyāma: may we attain strength
    • pṛtanyataḥ: together or in unity
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of friendship and unity in achieving greatness.
    • It acknowledges a higher power (referred to as "O Lord") that guides and supports the bond of friendship.
    • The phrase "may we attain the highest glory" suggests that through collaboration and mutual support, individuals can achieve remarkable success.
    • Real-life example: In a team project, when members work together harmoniously, they can produce outstanding results that surpass individual efforts.
  • Significance:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the strength found in unity and the power of collective effort.
    • It can be applied in various scenarios, such as in workplaces, communities, and personal relationships, where collaboration is essential for success.
    • In essence, it encourages individuals to foster strong bonds and work together towards common goals, leading to shared achievements and mutual growth.
