यद गायत्रे अधि गायत्रम आहितं त्रैषṭुभाद वा त्रैषṭुभं निरतक्षत |यद वा जगज जगत्य आहितं पदं य इत तद विदुस ते अमृतत्वं आनशुḥ ||
yad gāyatre adhi gāyatram āhitaṁ traiṣṭubhād vā traiṣṭubhaṁ niratakṣata | yad vā jagaj jagaty āhitam padaṁ ya it tad vidus te amṛtatvam ānaśuḥ ||
  • Mantra: "yad gāyatre adhi gāyatram āhitaṁ traiṣṭubhād vā traiṣṭubhaṁ niratakṣata | yad vā jagaj jagaty āhitam padaṁ ya it tad vidus te amṛtatvam ānaśuḥ ||"
  • Translation: This mantra speaks of the essence of the Gayatri mantra and its connection to the universe. It suggests that those who understand the nature of existence and the ultimate reality attain immortality.
  • Word Breakdown:
    • yad: that which
    • gāyatre: in the Gayatri (a sacred meter)
    • adhi: above or beyond
    • āhitaṁ: placed or established
    • traiṣṭubhād: from the Trishtubh meter
    • vā: or
    • jagaj: the universe
    • jagaty: of the world
    • āhitam: established
    • padaṁ: the state or position
    • ya: who
    • it: this
    • tad: that
    • vidus: know
    • te: for you
    • amṛtatvam: immortality
    • ānaśuḥ: attain or achieve
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • The Gayatri mantra is often recited for spiritual growth and enlightenment. For instance, individuals may chant it during meditation to connect with a higher consciousness.
    • In a practical sense, understanding the interconnectedness of all beings can lead to a more compassionate and harmonious society. For example, environmental movements emphasize the importance of preserving nature, reflecting the idea of unity in existence.
    • In personal development, the pursuit of knowledge and wisdom can be seen as a path to achieving a deeper understanding of life, akin to the immortality mentioned in the mantra.
  • Conclusion: This mantra emphasizes the significance of understanding the deeper truths of existence and the universe. It teaches that through knowledge and awareness, one can transcend the limitations of the physical world and attain a state of immortality. In real life, this can be applied through practices of meditation, environmental consciousness, and personal growth, leading to a more fulfilling and enlightened existence.
