स रेतोधा वृषभः शाश्वतीनां तस्मिन्न आत्मा जगतस तस्थुषश च |तन म ऋतम पातु शतशारदाय यूम पात स्वस्तिभिः सदा नः ||
sa retodhā vṛṣabhaḥ śaśvatīnāṁ tasminn ātmā jagatas tasthuṣaś ca | tan ma ṛtam pātu śataśāradāya yūyam pāta svastibhiḥ sadā naḥ ||
  • sa retodhā vṛṣabhaḥ: This phrase can be translated as "He who is the bull of the waters." In this context, "bull" symbolizes strength and fertility, while "waters" represent the source of life and nourishment. In real life, this can be likened to a strong leader or a nurturing figure who provides support and sustenance to their community.
  • śaśvatīnāṁ tasminn ātmā jagatas tasthuṣaś ca: This translates to "In Him, the essence of the universe and the source of all beings resides." This highlights the interconnectedness of all life and the divine presence within it. For example, in nature, every living being plays a role in the ecosystem, demonstrating how interconnectedness sustains life.
  • tan ma ṛtam pātu: This means "May that truth protect me." Here, "truth" signifies righteousness and moral integrity. In everyday life, this can be seen in the importance of honesty and ethical behavior in personal and professional relationships, where truth serves as a foundation for trust and respect.
  • śataśāradāya yūyam pāta svastibhiḥ sadā naḥ: This translates to "May you protect us with auspiciousness always." The term "auspiciousness" refers to good fortune and positive outcomes. In practical terms, this can be related to the idea of seeking blessings for success in endeavors, whether in business, education, or personal growth.

  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of strength, interconnectedness, truth, and auspiciousness in our lives. It serves as a reminder to seek protection and guidance from higher powers while embodying these virtues in our daily actions. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as in leadership roles, community building, ethical decision-making, and striving for success in personal and professional pursuits. By embodying the principles outlined in this mantra, individuals can contribute to a harmonious and prosperous society.
