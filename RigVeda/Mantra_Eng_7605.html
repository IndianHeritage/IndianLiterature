त्वं पुर इन्द्र चिकीद एनां व्योऽजसा शविष्ठ शक्र नाशयध्यै |त्वद विश्वानि भुवनानि वज्रिन dyावा रेजते पृथिवी च भीषा ||
tvam pura indra cikid enā vy ojasā śaviṣṭha śakra nāśayadhyai | tvad viśvāni bhuvanāni vajrin dyāvā rejete pṛthivī ca bhīṣā ||
  • tvam pura indra cikid enā vy: This phrase translates to "You, O Indra, are the one who knows everything." Here, Indra is recognized as the king of the gods and a symbol of wisdom and knowledge. In real life, this can be likened to leaders or mentors who possess deep understanding and insight, guiding others through challenges.
  • ojasā śaviṣṭha śakra nāśayadhyai: This means "with your powerful energy, O mighty one, dispel all obstacles." It emphasizes the importance of strength and resilience in overcoming difficulties. For example, in personal life, individuals often face challenges that require inner strength and determination to overcome, much like athletes training hard to achieve their goals.
  • tvad viśvāni bhuvanāni vajrin: This translates to "all worlds are under your protection, O wielder of the thunderbolt." It signifies the protective nature of divine forces in our lives. In a real-world context, this can be seen in the role of community leaders or organizations that work to safeguard the welfare of their members, much like how a parent protects their child.
  • dyāvā rejete pṛthivī ca bhīṣā: This means "the heavens shine, and the earth is filled with fear." This line reflects the duality of existence, where divine light coexists with fear and uncertainty. In everyday life, this can be observed in moments of crisis, where hope and fear coexist, such as during natural disasters when communities come together to support each other while facing the unknown.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of divine guidance, strength, and protection. It serves as a reminder of the importance of wisdom in leadership, the power of resilience in overcoming challenges, and the protective nature of community. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire individuals to seek knowledge, harness their inner strength, and support one another in times of need, ultimately fostering a sense of unity and purpose in the face of adversity.
