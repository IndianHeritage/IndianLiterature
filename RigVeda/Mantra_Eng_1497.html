मन्त्र: एते वाम अभ्य असृक्षत तिरः पवित्रम् आशवः |युवायवोऽति रोमान्य अव्यया सोमासो अत्य अव्यया ||
ete vām abhy asṛkṣata tiraḥ pavitram āśavaḥ | yuvāyavo 'ti romāṇy avyayā somāso aty avyayā ||
  • Translation: "These (soma) have poured forth for you, pure and invigorating; the youthful ones, who are beyond decay, are the immortal ones, the soma."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • ete: These
    • vām: for you
    • abhy asṛkṣata: have poured forth
    • tiraḥ: pure
    • pavitram: invigorating
    • āśavaḥ: youthful ones
    • yuvāyavo: beyond decay
    • avyayā: immortal
    • somāso: the soma
    • aty: very
    • avyayā: immortal
  • Contextual Meaning:
    • The mantra speaks of the sacred drink 'soma,' which is often associated with immortality and divine inspiration in Vedic literature.
    • The phrase "have poured forth for you" suggests a divine offering, indicating that the blessings of life and vitality are available to those who seek them.
    • "Pure and invigorating" highlights the importance of purity in both physical and spiritual practices, akin to how we seek clean and healthy food for our bodies today.
    • The reference to "youthful ones" and "beyond decay" can be interpreted as a metaphor for the eternal aspects of life, such as knowledge, wisdom, and spiritual growth, which remain unaffected by the passage of time.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern wellness practices, the emphasis on detoxification and consuming pure, organic foods mirrors the idea of seeking 'pavitram' or purity for physical health.
    • Spiritual practices such as meditation and yoga can be seen as ways to connect with the 'immortal' aspects of ourselves, promoting mental clarity and emotional stability.
    • In the context of personal development, the pursuit of knowledge and wisdom can be viewed as striving for the 'youthful' and 'immortal' qualities that enrich our lives.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of seeking purity and vitality in our lives, both physically and spiritually.
    • It encourages individuals to recognize the eternal aspects of existence, such as knowledge and wisdom, which transcend the limitations of the physical world.
    • In real-life scenarios, the teachings of this mantra can inspire us to pursue a lifestyle that values health, spiritual growth, and the quest for deeper understanding, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling life.
