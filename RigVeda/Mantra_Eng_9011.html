मक्षू ता त इन्द्र दानाप्नस आक्षाणे शूर वज्रिवः |यद ह ध शुष्णस्य दम्भयो जातं विश्वं सयावभिः ||
makṣū tā ta indra dānāpnasa ākṣāṇe śūra vajrivaḥ | yad dha śuṣṇasya dambhayo jātaṁ viśvaṁ sayāvabhiḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • makṣū: (to be) strong, powerful
    • tā: that
    • ta: you (Indra)
    • indra: the god Indra, often associated with strength and rain
    • dānāpnasa: grant us, bestow upon us
    • ākṣāṇe: in the sight, vision
    • śūra: brave, valiant
    • vajrivaḥ: wielding the thunderbolt, powerful
    • yad: that which
    • dha: to hold, to maintain
    • śuṣṇasya: of the scorching, intense
    • dambhayo: of the deceitful, false
    • jātaṁ: born, created
    • viśvaṁ: the universe, all
    • sayāvabhiḥ: with protection, safeguarding
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes Indra, the god of strength and rain, asking for his blessings and protection.
    • The phrase "makṣū tā ta indra" emphasizes the need for strength and power in our endeavors.
    • It highlights the importance of being brave and valiant ("śūra vajrivaḥ") in the face of challenges.
    • The reference to "śuṣṇasya dambhayo" suggests a caution against deceit and falsehood, urging us to seek truth and authenticity.
    • Overall, the mantra calls for divine support in overcoming obstacles and maintaining integrity.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a competitive work environment, one may face challenges that require both strength and bravery to overcome. This mantra serves as a reminder to seek inner strength and remain true to oneself.
    • In personal relationships, the mantra encourages individuals to be honest and authentic, avoiding deceitful behavior that can harm connections.
    • During times of crisis, such as natural disasters or personal hardships, invoking the strength of Indra can symbolize the need for resilience and support from a higher power.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra from the Rigveda emphasizes the significance of strength, bravery, and truthfulness in our lives.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, reminding us to seek divine support while facing challenges and to uphold integrity in our actions.
    • By reflecting on this mantra, individuals can cultivate a mindset of resilience and authenticity, essential for personal growth and harmonious relationships.
