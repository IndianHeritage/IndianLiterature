न वेपसā न तण्यतेन्द्रं वृत्रो वि बीभयत् | अभ्य एनं वज्र आयसः सहस्रभृष्टिर आयतार्चन्न अनु स्वराज्यम् ||
na vepasā na tanyatendraṁ vṛtro vi bībhayat | abhy enaṁ vajra āyasaḥ sahasrabhṛṣṭir āyatārcann anu svarājyam ||
  • na vepasā: This phrase translates to "without trembling" or "without fear." It signifies a state of confidence and composure in the face of challenges.
  • na tanyatendraṁ: This means "not being disturbed by the mighty." It suggests resilience against powerful adversities or forces that may threaten one's peace or stability.
  • vṛtro vi bībhayat: Here, "Vṛtra" refers to a demon or obstacle, and "bībhayat" means "to be afraid." This part emphasizes overcoming fears associated with formidable challenges or enemies.
  • abhy enaṁ vajra āyasaḥ: "Abhy" means "towards," "enaṁ" refers to "this," "vajra" means "thunderbolt" or "weapon," and "āyasaḥ" means "iron." This phrase suggests that one should approach challenges with the strength and power of a thunderbolt, symbolizing determination and resilience.
  • sahasrabhṛṣṭir: This term means "thousandfold strength" or "great power." It indicates an abundance of strength and capability to face adversities.
  • āyatārcann: This can be interpreted as "having a long-lasting glow" or "radiance." It signifies the enduring nature of one's strength and resolve.
  • anu svarājyam: "Anu" means "following" or "after," and "svarājyam" refers to "sovereignty" or "kingdom." This phrase suggests that by embodying these qualities, one can attain a state of sovereignty or mastery over their circumstances.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of courage, resilience, and strength in the face of challenges. It teaches us to remain unshaken by fears and obstacles, encouraging us to approach difficulties with the power of determination and confidence. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various situations such as facing personal challenges, overcoming fears in professional settings, or dealing with adversities in relationships. By embodying the qualities expressed in this mantra, individuals can cultivate a mindset that allows them to navigate life's challenges with grace and strength, ultimately leading to personal empowerment and success.
