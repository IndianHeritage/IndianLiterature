त्वं सोम महे भगं त्वं यूना ऋतायते |दक्षं दधासि जीवसे ||
tvaṁ soma mahe bhagaṁ tvaṁ yūna ṛtāyate | dakṣaṁ dadhāsi jīvase ||
  • tvaṁ - This word translates to "You" and signifies the divine presence or the essence of the universe.
  • soma - Soma refers to the sacred drink in Vedic tradition, symbolizing immortality and divine inspiration.
  • mahe - Meaning "great" or "mighty," it emphasizes the power and significance of the divine.
  • bhagaṁ - This term means "wealth" or "prosperity," often associated with blessings and abundance.
  • tvaṁ - Reiterating the divine presence, it reinforces the connection between the seeker and the divine.
  • yūna - This word can be interpreted as "united" or "joined," indicating the importance of harmony and connection.
  • ṛtāyate - Meaning "to the truth" or "to righteousness," it highlights the pursuit of truth and moral integrity.
  • dakṣaṁ - This translates to "skilled" or "capable," suggesting the importance of competence and ability in one's actions.
  • dadhāsi - Meaning "you bestow" or "you grant," it indicates the act of giving or providing blessings.
  • jīvase - This word means "to life" or "for living," emphasizing the connection between the divine and the essence of life.

The mantra can be interpreted as a prayer or invocation to the divine, acknowledging its greatness and seeking blessings for prosperity, truth, and skill in life. It reflects a deep reverence for the divine presence that sustains and enriches life.


In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts:

  • Personal Growth: Individuals can recite this mantra to seek divine guidance in their personal and professional endeavors, fostering a sense of purpose and direction.
  • Community Harmony: In group settings, such as family gatherings or community events, this mantra can be used to promote unity and collective well-being.
  • Spiritual Practices: During meditation or spiritual rituals, this mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of all beings and the importance of living in accordance with truth and righteousness.

In conclusion, this mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking divine blessings for a prosperous and meaningful life. It emphasizes the importance of truth, skill, and unity, making it relevant in various aspects of daily life, from personal development to community engagement.
