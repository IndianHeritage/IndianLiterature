स पुनान उप सूरे न धातोभे अप्रा रोडसी वि षा आवः |प्रियाऽचिद यस्य प्रियसास ऊती स तु धनं कारिणे न प्र यंषत ||
sa punāna upa sūre na dhātobhe aprā rodasī vi ṣa āvaḥ | priyā cid yasya priyasāsa ūtī sa tū dhanaṁ kāriṇe na pra yaṁsat ||
  • sa punāna upa sūre: This phrase can be interpreted as "He who purifies and approaches the divine." In real life, this can relate to individuals who seek spiritual growth and enlightenment through practices such as meditation, yoga, or community service.
  • na dhātobhe aprā rodasī: This translates to "not bound by the elements, beyond the horizon." It signifies a state of being that transcends physical limitations. For example, in modern life, this can be seen in the pursuit of knowledge and wisdom that goes beyond material possessions, such as the quest for understanding through education or philosophical inquiry.
  • vi ṣa āvaḥ: This means "may we be protected from poison." In a contemporary context, this can refer to the need for protection from negative influences, whether they be toxic relationships, harmful environments, or detrimental habits. It emphasizes the importance of surrounding oneself with positivity and support.
  • priyā cid yasya priyasāsa ūtī: This phrase can be understood as "the one who is dear, whose essence is love." It highlights the significance of love and compassion in our lives. In real life, this can be reflected in the relationships we cultivate with family, friends, and community, emphasizing the importance of empathy and kindness.
  • sa tū dhanaṁ kāriṇe na pra yaṁsat: This translates to "may he who acts with purpose not be deprived of wealth." This can be interpreted as a call for prosperity and success for those who work diligently and with intention. In practical terms, it encourages individuals to pursue their goals with integrity and dedication, leading to both material and spiritual abundance.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a profound message about the pursuit of spiritual purity, the transcendence of physical limitations, the importance of protection from negativity, the essence of love, and the rewards of purposeful action. In real life, it serves as a guiding principle for individuals seeking to lead a balanced and meaningful life, encouraging them to cultivate positive relationships, pursue knowledge, and act with integrity. By embodying these values, one can navigate the complexities of life while striving for both personal and collective well-being.
