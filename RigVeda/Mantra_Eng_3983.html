दमूनसो अपसो ये सुहस्ताः वृष्णः पत्नीर नद्यो विभ्वतष्टाः |सरस्वती बृहद्विवोता राका दशस्यन्तीर वरिवस्यन्तु शुभ्राः ||
damūnaso apaso ye suhastā vṛṣṇaḥ patnīr nadyo vibhvataṣṭāḥ | sarasvatī bṛhaddivota rākā daśasyantīr varivasyantu śubhrāḥ ||
  • damūnaso apaso ye suhastā vṛṣṇaḥ: This phrase can be interpreted as a call to the divine forces that govern the waters and the rivers. The term "damūnaso" refers to the essence or the spirit of the waters, while "suhastā" signifies the benevolent or auspicious nature of these forces. In real life, this can be related to the importance of clean and flowing water in our lives, which is essential for health and prosperity.
  • patnīr nadyo vibhvataṣṭāḥ: Here, "patnīr" refers to the wives or partners, and "nadyo" means rivers. This line suggests a connection between the rivers and the nurturing aspect of life, akin to how a partner supports and sustains a family. In a modern context, this can be seen in the way rivers provide resources for agriculture, drinking water, and transportation, thus nurturing communities.
  • sarasvatī bṛhaddivota: "Sarasvatī" is the goddess of knowledge, music, and arts, while "bṛhaddivota" refers to the vastness of the divine. This line emphasizes the importance of wisdom and creativity in our lives. In practical terms, this can be related to the pursuit of education and the arts, which enrich our lives and societies.
  • rākā daśasyantīr varivasyantu śubhrāḥ: "Rākā" refers to the moon, symbolizing calmness and serenity, while "varivasyantu" means to be blessed or protected. This line suggests that the blessings of the divine should bring peace and purity into our lives. In everyday life, this can be interpreted as the need for tranquility and positivity in our environments, whether at home or in our communities.

  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the interconnectedness of nature, the divine, and human life. It highlights the significance of water, wisdom, and tranquility in fostering a prosperous and harmonious existence. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied by advocating for environmental conservation, pursuing knowledge and creativity, and fostering peaceful relationships within our communities. By recognizing and honoring these elements, we can create a more balanced and fulfilling life.
