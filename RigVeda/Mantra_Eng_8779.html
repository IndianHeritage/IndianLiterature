अग्मन्न् उक्तानि पौंस्येन्द्रं जैत्राय हर्षयन् |वज्रश्च यद्भवथो अनपच्युताऽ समत्स्व अनपच्युताऽ ||
agmann ukthāni pauṁsyendraṁ jaitrāya harṣayan | vajraś ca yad bhavatho anapacyutā samatsv anapacyutā ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • agmann: "to come" or "arrival"
    • ukthāni: "utterances" or "words spoken"
    • pauṁsyendraṁ: "the lord of men" or "the supreme being among humans"
    • jaitrāya: "for victory" or "to achieve success"
    • harṣayan: "to inspire joy" or "to bring happiness"
    • vajraś: "thunderbolt" or "indestructible"
    • ca: "and"
    • yad: "that which"
    • bhavatho: "of you" or "your"
    • anapacyutā: "unfailing" or "everlasting"
    • samatsv: "in harmony" or "in balance"
    • anapacyutā: "unfailing" or "everlasting" (repeated for emphasis)
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the arrival of divine words that inspire joy and victory.
    • The "lord of men" signifies a higher power or leader who guides humanity towards success.
    • The mention of "vajra" symbolizes strength and resilience, suggesting that the blessings received are indestructible.
    • The repetition of "anapacyutā" emphasizes the idea of everlasting support and guidance.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, a leader who inspires their team with motivational speeches can be seen as embodying the essence of this mantra.
    • In personal life, a mentor or a parent who provides unwavering support and guidance can be viewed as a "lord of men," helping individuals achieve their goals.
    • In community service, volunteers who work tirelessly to uplift others reflect the spirit of bringing joy and harmony to society.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of divine guidance and the power of words in achieving success and happiness.
    • It teaches us to seek inspiration from higher powers and to be resilient in our pursuits.
    • In real life, it encourages us to be leaders who uplift others, fostering a sense of community and harmony.
    • Ultimately, it reminds us that with the right support and mindset, we can achieve our goals and inspire joy in ourselves and others.
