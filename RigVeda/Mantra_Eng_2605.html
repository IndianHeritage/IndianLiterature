त्रीण्य आयूषं तव जातवेदस तिस्रा आजानीर उषस ते अग्ने |ताभिर देवानाम् अवो यक्षि विद्वान् अथ भव यजमानाय शं योः ||
trīṇy āyūṁṣi tava jātavedas tisra ājānīr uṣasas te agne | tābhir devānām avo yakṣi vidvān athā bhava yajamānāya śaṁ yoḥ ||
  • Mantra: "trīṇy āyūṁṣi tava jātavedas tisra ājānīr uṣasas te agne | tābhir devānām avo yakṣi vidvān athā bhava yajamānāya śaṁ yoḥ ||"
  • Translation: "O Jātavedas (the one who knows all births), grant us threefold life; may your three radiant forms (uṣasas) shine upon us, O Agni. With these, may we attract the attention of the gods; may you, O wise one, bestow blessings upon the worshipper."

  • Word-by-Word Explanation:
    • trīṇy: threefold
    • āyūṁṣi: lives or lifespans
    • tava: your
    • jātavedas: O Agni, the one who knows all births
    • tisra: three
    • ājānīr: radiant forms or aspects
    • uṣasas: dawns or illuminations
    • te: your
    • agne: O Agni
    • tābhir: with these
    • devānām: of the gods
    • avo: may we attract
    • yakṣi: bestow or grant
    • vidvān: wise one
    • athā: and thus
    • bhava: become
    • yajamānāya: for the worshipper
    • śaṁ: blessings or peace
    • yoḥ: of whom

  • Significance:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Agni, the fire god, who symbolizes knowledge, transformation, and the divine connection between humans and the gods.
    • The request for "threefold life" can be interpreted as a desire for physical, mental, and spiritual well-being, emphasizing the holistic approach to life.
    • The mention of "three radiant forms" signifies the importance of dawns or new beginnings, suggesting that each day brings new opportunities for growth and enlightenment.

  • Real-Life Application:
    • In daily life, this mantra can inspire individuals to seek balance in their lives, focusing on physical health, mental clarity, and spiritual growth.
    • For example, someone might practice yoga (physical), meditation (mental), and engage in community service (spiritual) to embody the threefold life.
    • Additionally, the mantra encourages individuals to recognize and seize new opportunities, much like the dawn brings light to a new day.

  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of life, the importance of seeking blessings from the divine, and the pursuit of a balanced existence.
    • By invoking Agni, individuals can cultivate a deeper understanding of their purpose and the blessings that come from living a life aligned with wisdom and service.
