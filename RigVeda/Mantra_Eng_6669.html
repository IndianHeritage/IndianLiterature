ते नस् त्राध्वं तेऽवता त उ नोऽधि वोकत |मा नः पथः पितृयान् मानवाद् अधि दूरं नैष्ठ परावतः ||
te nas trādhvaṁ te 'vata ta u no adhi vocata | mā naḥ pathaḥ pitryān mānavād adhi dūraṁ naiṣṭa parāvataḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • te: you (plural)
    • nas: us
    • trādhvaṁ: protect
    • te: you (plural)
    • 'vata: may you grant
    • ta: that
    • u: and
    • no: us
    • adhi: above
    • vocata: speak
    • mā: not
    • naḥ: our
    • pathaḥ: path
    • pitryān: ancestral
    • mānavād: human
    • adhi: above
    • dūraṁ: far away
    • naiṣṭa: not
    • parāvataḥ: beyond
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a plea for protection and guidance.
    • It emphasizes the importance of community and collective well-being.
    • The speaker asks for divine protection from negative influences and for the right path to be illuminated.
    • It reflects a desire for connection with ancestral wisdom and human values.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a family setting, this mantra can be recited during gatherings to invoke blessings for unity and protection.
    • In a community meeting, it can serve as a reminder to prioritize collective welfare and support each other.
    • During challenging times, individuals may use this mantra to seek guidance and clarity in their decisions.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of seeking protection and guidance from higher powers.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, such as family gatherings, community events, and personal challenges.
    • By invoking this mantra, individuals and communities can foster a sense of unity, purpose, and connection to their roots.
