मन्त्र: प्रो त्ये अग्नयो 'ग्निषु विश्वं पुष्यन्ति वार्यम् |ते हिन्विरे त इन्बिरे त इषाण्यन्त्य आनुषग इषं स्तोत्रिभ्य आ भर ||
pro tye agnayo 'gniṣu viśvam puṣyanti vāryam | te hinvire ta invire ta iṣaṇyanty ānuṣag iṣaṁ stotṛbhya ā bhara ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • pro tye: For you (to you)
    • agnayo: O Agni (the fire god)
    • 'gniṣu: In the fires
    • viśvam: All, the universe
    • puṣyanti: Nourish, sustain
    • vāryam: The powerful, the strong
    • te: They
    • hinvire: Lead, guide
    • ta: Those
    • iṣaṇyanty: They desire, they wish
    • ānuṣag: Following, in accordance with
    • iṣaṁ: The desire, the wish
    • stotṛbhya: To the worshippers
    • ā bhara: Bring forth, provide
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra addresses Agni, the fire god, who is often seen as a mediator between humans and the divine.
    • It emphasizes the role of Agni in nourishing and sustaining all aspects of life, symbolizing the universal energy that supports existence.
    • The phrase "they lead" suggests that the forces of nature, represented by Agni, guide humanity towards fulfillment and prosperity.
    • The mantra also highlights the importance of desire and intention in achieving one's goals, indicating that aligning with divine forces can help manifest those desires.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, individuals often seek guidance and support from higher powers or the universe when making important decisions, similar to how the mantra invokes Agni for assistance.
    • In various cultures, fire rituals are performed to seek blessings for prosperity and well-being, reflecting the nourishing aspect of Agni.
    • People often set intentions or goals, akin to the "desire" mentioned in the mantra, and work towards them, believing that their efforts will be supported by the universe.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the interconnectedness of all life and the importance of seeking guidance from higher energies.
    • It teaches that by aligning our desires with the universal forces, we can achieve our goals and contribute to the greater good.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire individuals to engage in practices that honor nature and the divine, fostering a sense of community and shared purpose.
