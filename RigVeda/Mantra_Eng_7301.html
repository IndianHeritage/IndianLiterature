मन्त्र: निमिषश्चिज्जवीयसा रथेनायातमश्विनाः |अन्ति षड्भूतवामवः ||
nimiṣaś cij javīyasā rathenā yātam aśvinā | anti ṣad bhūtu vām avaḥ ||
  • Mantra: "nimiṣaś cij javīyasā rathenā yātam aśvinā | anti ṣad bhūtu vām avaḥ ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • nimiṣaś: blink or moment
    • cij: indeed or certainly
    • javīyasā: with great speed or swiftness
    • rathenā: by the chariot
    • yātam: may you go or travel
    • aśvinā: O Ashvins (the twin deities of health and medicine)
    • anti: may you come
    • ṣad: six
    • bhūtu: beings or entities
    • vām: to you
    • avaḥ: may you bring or bestow
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the Ashvins, who are known for their speed and healing abilities.
    • The phrase "nimiṣaś cij javīyasā" emphasizes the importance of swift action, suggesting that even a moment's delay can be significant.
    • The reference to "rathenā" indicates the use of a chariot, symbolizing the journey of life and the need for guidance and support.
    • By calling upon the Ashvins, the mantra seeks their assistance in navigating challenges and ensuring the well-being of all beings.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a medical emergency, the swift arrival of healthcare professionals can be likened to the speed of the Ashvins, highlighting the importance of timely intervention.
    • In sports, athletes often rely on quick reflexes and rapid decision-making, paralleling the mantra's emphasis on speed and action.
    • In daily life, making prompt decisions in business or personal matters can lead to better outcomes, reflecting the mantra's call for swift movement and guidance.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of speed, guidance, and well-being in our lives.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from health and medicine to decision-making and personal growth.
    • By invoking the Ashvins, we seek their blessings to navigate life's challenges with swiftness and support, ensuring the well-being of ourselves and others.
