आसस्राणासः शवसानम् अच्छेन्द्रं सुचक्रे रथ्यासो अश्वाः |अभि श्रव ऋज्यन्तो वहेयर नू चिन न वायोर अमृतं विगदस्येत् ||
āsasrāṇāsaḥ śavasānam acchendraṁ sucakre rathyāso aśvāḥ | abhi śrava ṛjyanto vaheyur nū cin nu vāyor amṛtaṁ vi dasyet ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • āsasrāṇāsaḥ: Those who are swift or quick.
    • śavasānam: The one who has a body or form.
    • acchendraṁ: The one who is like a king or ruler.
    • sucakre: Well-formed or well-structured.
    • rathyāso: Those who are on the path or journey.
    • aśvāḥ: Horses, symbolizing strength and speed.
    • abhi: Towards or in the direction of.
    • śrava: Fame or glory.
    • ṛjyanto: Those who are engaged or involved.
    • vaheyur: They may carry or bear.
    • nū: Indeed or certainly.
    • cin: To know or to understand.
    • nu: A particle used for emphasis.
    • vāyor: Of the wind or air.
    • amṛtaṁ: Immortal or eternal.
    • vi dasyet: May be revealed or shown.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of swift beings who embody strength and purpose.
    • The mention of horses symbolizes not just physical strength but also the speed and agility required to navigate life's challenges.
    • The phrase "those who are on the path" suggests a journey, indicating that life is a continuous process of growth and learning.
    • The reference to "immortal" signifies the pursuit of eternal truths and values that transcend the physical realm.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a competitive environment, such as sports or business, individuals or teams that are quick and agile often outperform their slower counterparts.
    • In personal development, those who actively seek knowledge and engage in self-improvement are akin to the "swift beings" mentioned in the mantra.
    • In relationships, being open to understanding and communication can lead to deeper connections, much like the "immortal truths" that bind people together.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of speed, strength, and purpose in our lives.
    • It encourages us to be proactive in our pursuits, whether in personal growth, professional endeavors, or relationships.
    • By embodying the qualities of the swift and strong, we can navigate the complexities of life and strive towards eternal values that enrich our existence.
