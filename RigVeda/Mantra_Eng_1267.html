चित्रं देवानामुदगादनीकं चक्षुरमित्रस्य वरुणस्याग्निः |आप्रा dyāvāpṛthivī अन्तरिक्षं सूर्य आत्मा जगतस्तस्मुषश्च ||
citraṁ devānām ud agād anīkaṁ cakṣur mitrasya varuṇasyāgneḥ | āprā dyāvāpṛthivī antarikṣaṁ sūrya ātmā jagatas tasthuṣaś ca ||
  • citraṁ devānām ud agād anīkaṁ: This phrase can be translated as "the diverse and splendid assembly of the gods." Here, "citraṁ" refers to the variety and beauty, while "devānām" signifies the gods. In real life, this can be likened to a diverse community where different individuals come together, each contributing their unique qualities to create a harmonious society.
  • cakṣur mitrasya varuṇasyāgneḥ: This translates to "the eye of Mitra, Varuna, and Agni." In this context, "cakṣur" symbolizes vision or insight, while "Mitra," "Varuna," and "Agni" are deities representing friendship, cosmic order, and fire, respectively. This can be related to the importance of having a clear vision and understanding in our relationships and endeavors, much like how these deities oversee the balance of nature and human affairs.
  • āprā dyāvāpṛthivī antarikṣaṁ: This means "the vastness of the sky, the earth, and the space in between." "Āprā" signifies abundance, while "dyāvāpṛthivī" refers to the sky and earth. This can be seen in our daily lives as a reminder of the interconnectedness of all elements of nature and how they support life. For example, the balance of ecosystems is crucial for sustaining life on Earth.
  • sūrya ātmā jagatas tasthuṣaś ca: This translates to "the sun is the soul of the universe." Here, "sūrya" represents the sun, which is essential for life, while "ātma" signifies the soul or essence. This can be compared to how the sun provides energy and sustenance to all living beings, emphasizing the importance of nurturing and protecting our environment for future generations.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of unity in diversity, the importance of vision and insight, the interconnectedness of nature, and the vital role of the sun in sustaining life. In real-life scenarios, it serves as a reminder to appreciate the diversity in our communities, to seek clarity in our relationships, to recognize our dependence on the environment, and to honor the natural forces that support life. By embodying these principles, we can foster a more harmonious and sustainable world.
