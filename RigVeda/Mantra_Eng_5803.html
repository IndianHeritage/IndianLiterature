यत्रा नरः समयन्ते कृतध्वजो यस्मिन्न आजा भवति किं चन प्रियं |यत्रा भयन्ते भुवना स्वदृशस् तत्रा न इन्द्रावरुणाधि वोचतम् ||
yatrā naraḥ samayante kṛtadhvajo yasminn ājā bhavati kiṁ cana priyam | yatrā bhayante bhuvanā svardṛśas tatrā na indrāvaruṇādhi vocatam ||
  • Mantra: "yatrā naraḥ samayante kṛtadhvajo yasminn ājā bhavati kiṁ cana priyam | yatrā bhayante bhuvanā svardṛśas tatrā na indrāvaruṇādhi vocatam ||"
  • Translation: "Wherever men unite with a common purpose, there is a flag of victory; in that place, whatever is dear is achieved. Where fear is dispelled, the world shines brightly; there, neither Indra nor Varuna can speak."

  • Explanation:
    • yatrā naraḥ samayante: This phrase emphasizes the importance of unity among people. It suggests that when individuals come together with a shared goal, they can achieve great things.
    • kṛtadhvajo: The term "kṛtadhvajo" refers to a flag or symbol of victory. It signifies that unity leads to success and recognition.
    • yasminn ājā bhavati kiṁ cana priyam: This part indicates that in such a united effort, anything that is cherished or desired can be attained.
    • yatrā bhayante bhuvanā svardṛśas: Here, the mantra speaks about the dispelling of fear. When people are united, they can overcome challenges and fears that may hinder progress.
    • tatrā na indrāvaruṇādhi vocatam: This suggests that in a place where fear is absent and unity prevails, even the powerful deities like Indra and Varuna cannot intervene negatively. It highlights the strength of collective human effort.

  • Real-Life Examples:
    • Community Projects: When a community comes together to work on a project, such as building a school or organizing a festival, they often achieve more than individuals working alone. The collective effort leads to a successful outcome, symbolized by the "flag of victory."
    • Team Sports: In sports, teams that work together harmoniously often outperform those with individual stars. The unity and shared purpose can lead to championships, illustrating the mantra's message about achieving what is dear through collaboration.
    • Social Movements: Historical movements, such as civil rights or environmental activism, demonstrate how collective action can overcome fear and societal barriers, leading to significant change and progress.

  • Conclusion:
    • The mantra emphasizes the power of unity and collective action. It teaches that when individuals come together with a common purpose, they can achieve their goals and overcome fears.
    • In real life, this principle is applicable in various scenarios, from community initiatives to team dynamics in workplaces and social movements. The significance lies in recognizing that collaboration can lead to success and positive change.
