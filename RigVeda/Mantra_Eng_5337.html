बोधा सु मे मगवन वाचम् एमाṁ यां ते वसिष्ठो अर्चति प्रशस्तिम् |इमा ब्रह्म सदहमादे जुषस्व ||
bodhā su me maghavan vācam emāṁ yāṁ te vasiṣṭho arcati praśastim | imā brahma sadhamāde juṣasva ||
  • Translation: The mantra can be translated as: "O Maghavan (Indra), may you grant me the wisdom (bodhā) that I speak, which Vasiṣṭha praises as excellent (praśastim). May these words (vācam) be pleasing to you, and may I attain the highest (brahma) through them."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • bodhā: Wisdom or understanding.
    • maghavan: A name for Indra, the king of gods, associated with strength and abundance.
    • vācam: Speech or words.
    • vasiṣṭho: Referring to the sage Vasiṣṭha, known for his wisdom and knowledge.
    • praśastim: Praise or excellence.
    • imā: These.
    • brahma: The ultimate reality or highest truth.
    • sadhamāde: To attain or reach the highest state.
    • juṣasva: May you enjoy or may it be pleasing.
  • Contextual Significance:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of wisdom and the power of words. It suggests that the right speech, imbued with wisdom, can lead to higher understanding and fulfillment.
    • Indra, as a deity, represents strength and the ability to overcome obstacles. By invoking him, the speaker seeks divine support in their quest for wisdom.
    • The reference to Vasiṣṭha highlights the value of learned individuals and the tradition of seeking guidance from wise sages.
  • Real-Life Application:
    • In everyday life, this mantra can be applied in situations where effective communication is crucial, such as in negotiations, public speaking, or teaching. The wisdom behind our words can influence outcomes significantly.
    • In personal development, it encourages individuals to seek knowledge and understanding, fostering a mindset that values learning and growth.
    • In leadership, it serves as a reminder that wise and thoughtful communication can inspire and motivate others, leading to a more harmonious and productive environment.
  • Conclusion:
    • The mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking wisdom through speech and the importance of divine guidance in this pursuit. It serves as a reminder that our words hold power and that by aligning them with wisdom, we can achieve greater truths and fulfillment in life.
    • In a world where communication is key, this mantra encourages us to reflect on the quality of our speech and the wisdom we impart, ultimately leading to personal and collective growth.
