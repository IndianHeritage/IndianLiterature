मन्त्र: अवश्यते स्तुवते कृष्णियाय ऋजुयते नासत्याः शचीभिः |पशुं न नष्टम् इव दर्शनाय विष्णाप्वं ददथुर विश्वकाय ||
avasyate stuvate kṛṣṇiyāya ṛjūyate nāsatyā śacībhiḥ | paśuṁ na naṣṭam iva darśanāya viṣṇāpvaṁ dadathur viśvakāya ||
  • Mantra: "avasyate stuvate kṛṣṇiyāya ṛjūyate nāsatyā śacībhiḥ | paśuṁ na naṣṭam iva darśanāya viṣṇāpvaṁ dadathur viśvakāya ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • avasyate: is assured or certain
    • stuvate: praises or worships
    • kṛṣṇiyāya: to the one who is black or dark, often referring to Lord Krishna
    • ṛjūyate: is straight or righteous
    • nāsatyā: not false or deceitful
    • śacībhiḥ: with the powerful or capable
    • paśuṁ: the animal or living being
    • na naṣṭam: not lost or destroyed
    • iva: like or as if
    • darśanāya: for the purpose of vision or sight
    • viṣṇāpvaṁ: to the one who is like Vishnu
    • dadathur: they have given
    • viśvakāya: to all beings or creatures
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra speaks of the assurance and praise directed towards a divine figure, often interpreted as Lord Krishna or Vishnu.
    • The phrase "avasyate stuvate" indicates that those who worship with sincerity will surely receive blessings.
    • It emphasizes the importance of righteousness ("ṛjūyate") and truthfulness ("nāsatyā"), suggesting that divine grace is bestowed upon those who embody these qualities.
    • The mention of "paśuṁ na naṣṭam" implies that even beings that seem lost or in distress are not forsaken, highlighting the protective nature of the divine.
    • In real life, this can be seen in situations where individuals or communities come together to support those in need, ensuring that no one is left behind.
    • The mantra encourages a sense of community and collective responsibility, reminding us that through righteous actions and support, we can uplift others.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of devotion, righteousness, and community support in our lives.
    • It teaches us that through sincere worship and ethical living, we can attain divine blessings and ensure the well-being of all beings.
    • In practical terms, it can inspire individuals to engage in charitable acts, promote social justice, and foster a sense of unity among diverse groups.
    • Ultimately, it serves as a reminder that the divine presence is always with us, guiding and protecting those who strive for truth and compassion.
