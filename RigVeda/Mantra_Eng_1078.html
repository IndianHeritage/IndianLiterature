सुखेत्रियाऽसुगातुयाऽवसूयाच यजामहे |अप नः शोशुचदघम् ||
sukṣetriyā sugātuyā vasūyā ca yajāmahe | apa naḥ śośucad agham ||
  • Mantra: "sukṣetriyā sugātuyā vasūyā ca yajāmahe | apa naḥ śośucad agham ||"
  • Translation: "We invoke the auspicious and benevolent forces; may they remove our sins and obstacles."

  • Word Breakdown:
    • sukṣetriyā: Derived from "sukṣetra," meaning auspicious or beneficial. It refers to positive energies or forces that promote well-being.
    • sugātuyā: From "sugātu," meaning benevolent or good-natured. This signifies the importance of kindness and positive intentions in our actions.
    • vasūyā: Refers to wealth or abundance. It symbolizes the material and spiritual prosperity that one seeks in life.
    • yajāmahe: A form of the verb "yaj," meaning to worship or invoke. It indicates the act of calling upon these positive forces.
    • apa naḥ: Translates to "may they remove from us." It expresses a plea for the removal of negativity.
    • śośucad: Meaning "purify" or "cleanse." It emphasizes the need for purification of the mind and spirit.
    • agham: Refers to sins or obstacles. It highlights the challenges and negative aspects that hinder our progress.

  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, individuals often face challenges that can be seen as "agham." For instance, stress from work or personal relationships can create obstacles in achieving peace and happiness.
    • When starting a new venture, such as a business, invoking "sukṣetriyā" and "sugātuyā" can be likened to seeking positive energy and support from mentors or a supportive community.
    • In moments of conflict, whether in personal relationships or professional settings, focusing on benevolence ("sugātuyā") can lead to more constructive resolutions.

  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of invoking positive energies and benevolence in our lives. It serves as a reminder to seek purification from negativity and to focus on the good.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as during personal challenges, in community building, or while embarking on new journeys.
    • Ultimately, it encourages individuals to cultivate a mindset of positivity and to actively seek the removal of obstacles, leading to a more fulfilling and prosperous life.
