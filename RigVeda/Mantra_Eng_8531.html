अपाम इवेदūrमयस्तर्तुराणः प्र मनिषा ईरते सोममच्छ | नमस्यन्तीर उप च यन्ति सं चा च विशन्त्य उषतीर उषन्तम् ||
apām ived ūrmayas tarturāṇāḥ pra manīṣā īrate somam accha | namasyantīr upa ca yanti saṁ cā ca viśanty uśatīr uśantam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • apām: of waters
    • ived: I have known
    • ūrmayas: the waves or currents
    • tarturāṇāḥ: those who are crossing
    • pra: towards
    • manīṣā: the mind or intellect
    • īrate: to worship or to honor
    • somam: the soma (a sacred drink)
    • accha: good or beneficial
    • namasyantīr: those who bow down
    • upa: near or towards
    • ca: and
    • yanti: they go
    • saṁ: together
    • cā: and
    • viśanty: they enter
    • uśatīr: the good or auspicious
    • uśantam: the one who is auspicious
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the waters and their currents, symbolizing the flow of life and consciousness.
    • The act of crossing the currents can be seen as overcoming challenges or obstacles in life.
    • The reference to worshiping the soma indicates the importance of spiritual nourishment and the pursuit of higher knowledge.
    • Those who bow down and approach the divine are acknowledged, suggesting humility and reverence are essential in spiritual practice.
    • The mantra emphasizes the collective journey towards goodness and auspiciousness, highlighting the importance of community and shared values.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a personal context, when facing difficulties, one might seek guidance through meditation or prayer, akin to crossing the currents of life.
    • In a community setting, collective efforts in social service can be seen as a way of entering the realm of goodness, where individuals come together to uplift each other.
    • In professional life, collaboration and teamwork can be viewed as a journey where individuals bow down to the greater good of the project, leading to successful outcomes.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of understanding the flow of life and the need for spiritual and communal support.
    • It teaches us to honor the divine and seek knowledge while navigating through life's challenges.
    • In real life, it encourages humility, collaboration, and the pursuit of goodness, making it applicable in various aspects of personal, social, and professional life.
