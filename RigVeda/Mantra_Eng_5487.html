प्र वो महीम अरमतिं कृतुध्वं प्र पूषणं विदथ्यं न वीरम् |भागं धियोऽवितारं नो अस्याः सातौ वाजं रातिषाचं पुरंधिम् ||
pra vo mahīm aramatiṁ kṛṇudhvam pra pūṣaṇaṁ vidathya1ṁ na vīram | bhagaṁ dhiyo 'vitāraṁ no asyāḥ sātau vājaṁ rātiṣācam puraṁdhim ||
  • pra vo mahīm aramatiṁ kṛṇudhvam: This phrase can be translated as "Let us create a great and prosperous life together." The word "mahīm" signifies greatness, while "aramatiṁ" refers to a state of joy or happiness. This encourages collective effort towards achieving a fulfilling life.
  • pra pūṣaṇaṁ vidathyaṁ na vīram: Here, "pūṣaṇaṁ" refers to nourishment or sustenance, and "vidathyaṁ" means to be well-prepared or organized. The phrase suggests that we should prepare ourselves to receive nourishment and strength, emphasizing the importance of being ready to embrace opportunities that come our way.
  • bhagaṁ dhiyo 'vitāraṁ no asyāḥ: "Bhagaṁ" denotes wealth or prosperity, while "dhiyo" refers to intellect or wisdom. This line implies that true wealth comes from wisdom and understanding, and that we should seek to expand our knowledge and insights.
  • sātau vājaṁ rātiṣācam puraṁdhim: "Sātau" means in the realm of truth, "vājaṁ" refers to strength or power, and "puraṁdhim" signifies a well-structured or fortified place. This suggests that by adhering to truth and wisdom, we can build a strong foundation for our lives.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of collective effort, preparation, wisdom, and truth in achieving a prosperous and fulfilling life. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various contexts such as teamwork in professional settings, community building, and personal development. By working together, being prepared, seeking knowledge, and adhering to truth, individuals and communities can create a strong and prosperous future. This mantra serves as a reminder that our collective actions and intentions shape our reality, encouraging us to strive for greatness together.
