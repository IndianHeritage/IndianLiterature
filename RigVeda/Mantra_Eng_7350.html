आयम् इन्द्रो मरुत्सखाः वि वृत्रस्याभिनच् चिरः |वज्रेण शतपर्वणा ||
ayam indro marutsakhā vi vṛtrasyābhinac chiraḥ | vajreṇa śataparvaṇā ||
  • Ayam Indro: This phrase refers to Indra, the king of the gods in Vedic mythology, symbolizing strength, power, and leadership.
  • Marutsakhā: This term means "friends of the Maruts," who are storm deities associated with wind and storms. It signifies the importance of camaraderie and collective strength.
  • Vi Vṛtrasyābhinac Chiraḥ: This part translates to "to vanquish Vritra for a long time." Vritra is a serpent or dragon that represents chaos and obstacles. The mantra emphasizes overcoming challenges and adversities.
  • Vajreṇa: This word means "with the thunderbolt," symbolizing the power and might of Indra's weapon, which is often associated with divine intervention and strength.
  • Śataparvaṇā: This term translates to "with a hundred folds," indicating abundance, protection, and the multifaceted nature of strength and resilience.

The mantra as a whole can be interpreted as a call to Indra, invoking his strength and the support of the Maruts to overcome obstacles and challenges in life. It highlights the importance of unity and collective effort in facing adversities.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as:

  • Teamwork in Professional Settings: Just as Indra and the Maruts work together, individuals in a workplace can achieve greater success through collaboration and mutual support.
  • Overcoming Personal Challenges: When facing difficulties, invoking the spirit of Indra can inspire individuals to harness their inner strength and resilience, much like how one might seek motivation from a mentor or leader.
  • Community Support: In times of crisis, communities can come together, much like the Maruts, to support one another and overcome challenges collectively.

In conclusion, this mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the strength found in unity and the importance of overcoming obstacles with courage and support from others. It encourages individuals to seek help, work together, and draw upon their inner strength to face life's challenges.
