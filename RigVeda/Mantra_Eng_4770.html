अयम् अकृत्नोद उषसः सुपत्नीर अयं सूर्ये अदधाज ज्योतिर अन्तः |अयं त्रिधातु दिवि रोचनेषु त्रितेषु विन्दद अमृतं निगूढम् ||
ayam akṛṇod uṣasaḥ supatnīr ayaṁ sūrye adadhāj jyotir antaḥ | ayaṁ tridhātu divi rocaneṣu triteṣu vindad amṛtaṁ nigūḻham ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ayam: this
    • akṛṇod: has created
    • uṣasaḥ: of the dawn
    • supatnīr: well-prepared
    • ayaṁ: this
    • sūrye: in the sun
    • adadhāj: has placed
    • jyotir: light
    • antaḥ: within
    • ayaṁ: this
    • tridhātu: three realms
    • divi: in the sky
    • rocaneṣu: in the shining
    • triteṣu: in the three
    • vindad: finds
    • amṛtaṁ: immortality
    • nigūḻham: hidden
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the creation of dawn and the placement of light within the sun.
    • It emphasizes the interconnectedness of the three realms: the physical, the mental, and the spiritual.
    • The search for immortality is highlighted, suggesting that it can be found in the hidden aspects of existence.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, the dawn represents new beginnings. Just as the sun rises each day, we have the opportunity to start anew.
    • The concept of light within the sun can be likened to knowledge and enlightenment that we seek in our lives.
    • The three realms can be seen in our own lives as we navigate physical challenges, mental struggles, and spiritual quests.
    • The pursuit of immortality can be interpreted as striving for a legacy or impact that lasts beyond our physical existence.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of creation, light, and the quest for deeper understanding in life.
    • It encourages us to recognize the potential for renewal and growth in our daily experiences.
    • By understanding the interconnectedness of our existence, we can find meaning and purpose, ultimately leading us to discover the hidden aspects of life that contribute to our sense of immortality.
