स इधानो वसुष कविर अग्निर ईḻेन्यो गिरा |रेवद अस्मभ्यम पूर्वणीका दीदिहि ||
sa idhāno vasuṣ kavir agnir īḻenyo girā | revad asmabhyam purvaṇīka dīdihi ||
  • sa idhāno: This phrase can be interpreted as "he who is present" or "he who is here." It signifies the presence of a divine or powerful entity.
  • vasuṣ: This word refers to "wealth" or "prosperity." In a broader sense, it can also denote the abundance of resources and blessings in life.
  • kavir: Translated as "the wise one" or "the poet," this term emphasizes the importance of wisdom and creativity in understanding the world.
  • agnir: This word means "fire," which is often associated with energy, transformation, and purification. Fire is a vital element in many cultures, symbolizing both destruction and renewal.
  • īḻenyo: This term can be understood as "through the song" or "by means of the hymn." It highlights the power of words and music in invoking divine presence and blessings.
  • girā: This word translates to "speech" or "voice," reinforcing the idea that communication and expression are essential in connecting with the divine.
  • revad: This can be interpreted as "to grant" or "to bestow," indicating a request for blessings or gifts from the divine.
  • asmabhyam: This term means "to us," suggesting a plea or invocation directed towards the divine for assistance or favor.
  • purvaṇīka: This word can be understood as "the ancient" or "the primordial," referring to the timeless wisdom and traditions that guide human existence.
  • dīdihi: This is a call for illumination or enlightenment, asking for clarity and understanding in life.

Conclusion: The mantra "sa idhāno vasuṣ kavir agnir īḻenyo girā | revad asmabhyam purvaṇīka dīdihi ||" encapsulates a profound invocation for divine presence, wisdom, and prosperity. It emphasizes the significance of communication, creativity, and the transformative power of fire in our lives. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as during rituals for prosperity, seeking guidance in difficult times, or invoking inspiration in creative endeavors. By recognizing the interconnectedness of wisdom, wealth, and divine presence, individuals can cultivate a more meaningful and enriched life.
