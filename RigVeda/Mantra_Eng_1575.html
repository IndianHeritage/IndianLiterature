युयूषतः सवयसा तदिद्वपुः समानम् अर्थं वितरित्रता मिथः |आद इम भगो न हव्यः सम अस्मद आवोऽल्हुर न रश्मीन सम अयंस्त सारथिḥ ||
yuyūṣataḥ savayasā tad id vapuḥ samānam arthaṁ vitaritratā mithaḥ | ād īm bhago na havyaḥ sam asmad ā voḻhur na raśmīn sam ayaṁsta sārathiḥ ||
  • yuyūṣataḥ: This term can be understood as "those who are striving" or "those who are seeking." It emphasizes the importance of effort and intention in achieving one's goals.
  • savayasā: This word refers to "those who are united" or "together." It highlights the significance of community and collaboration in the pursuit of common objectives.
  • tad id vapuḥ: This phrase translates to "that form" or "that essence." It suggests a focus on the true nature of things, urging individuals to look beyond superficial appearances.
  • samānam arthaṁ: This means "the same purpose" or "shared meaning." It indicates the importance of having a common goal or understanding among individuals.
  • vitaritratā mithaḥ: This can be interpreted as "sharing together" or "distributing among each other." It emphasizes the value of generosity and mutual support.
  • ād īm bhago: This phrase can be understood as "may we attain prosperity" or "may we achieve success." It reflects a desire for abundance and well-being.
  • na havyaḥ: This means "not to be offered" or "not to be sacrificed." It suggests a caution against wasting resources or efforts on unworthy pursuits.
  • sam asmad: This translates to "together with us" or "alongside us." It reinforces the idea of unity and collective action.
  • ā voḻhur: This can be interpreted as "may you come" or "may you arrive." It expresses an invitation for positive forces or energies to join the collective effort.
  • na raśmīn: This means "not the rays" or "not the light." It may symbolize the avoidance of distractions or illusions that can lead one astray.
  • sam ayaṁsta sārathiḥ: This phrase translates to "may this charioteer be with us." It signifies the need for guidance and direction in our endeavors.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of collective effort, shared goals, and mutual support in achieving prosperity and success. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various contexts such as teamwork in a workplace, community projects, or even family dynamics. By fostering unity and collaboration, individuals can work towards common objectives, ensuring that their efforts are not wasted on unworthy pursuits. The invocation of guidance symbolizes the need for wise leadership and direction in our collective journeys, reminding us that we are stronger together.
