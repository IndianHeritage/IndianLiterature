मरतः यद धा वो बलं जनाम् अचुच्यवीतन |गिरिम् अचुच्यवीतन ||
maruto yad dha vo balaṁ janām̐ acucyavītana | girīm̐r acucyavītana ||
  • Mantra: "maruto yad dha vo balaṁ janām̐ acucyavītana | girīm̐r acucyavītana ||"
  • Translation: "O Maruts, may your strength, which is unyielding, be bestowed upon us; may it be as unyielding as the mountains."
  • Explanation:
    • Maruts: The Maruts are a group of storm deities in Vedic literature, often associated with strength, power, and the forces of nature. They symbolize the dynamic and uncontrollable aspects of life.
    • Balaṁ: This word translates to "strength" or "power." It signifies not just physical strength but also mental and spiritual resilience.
    • Janām̐: This refers to "people" or "humanity." The mantra is a plea for strength not just for the individual but for the collective community.
    • Acucyavītana: This term means "unyielding" or "unfaltering." It emphasizes the idea of enduring strength that does not waver in the face of challenges.
    • Girīm̐: This translates to "mountains," which are often seen as symbols of stability, permanence, and strength in various cultures.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In times of personal crisis, such as losing a job or facing health issues, individuals often seek inner strength to overcome these challenges. This mantra serves as a reminder to draw upon one's inner resilience.
    • Communities facing natural disasters, like hurricanes or earthquakes, can embody the spirit of the Maruts by coming together to support one another, showcasing collective strength and unity.
    • In sports, teams often invoke the idea of strength and resilience to overcome their opponents, much like the Maruts, who represent the power of nature.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for strength and resilience, both individually and collectively. It teaches us that in the face of adversity, we can draw upon the unyielding strength of nature and the community around us. This mantra is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal struggles to communal challenges, reminding us that we are capable of enduring and overcoming obstacles with the right mindset and support.
