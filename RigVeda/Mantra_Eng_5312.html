सद्यश्चिन नु ते मगवन्न अभिष्टौ नरः शंसन्त्य उक्तशास उक्ताः |ये ते हवैभिर विपणिम् अदाशन् अस्मान वृणीष्व युज्याय तस्मै ||
sadyaś cin nu te maghavann abhiṣṭau naraḥ śaṁsanty ukthaśāsa ukthā | ye te havebhir vi paṇīm̐r adāśann asmān vṛṇīṣva yujyāya tasmai ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sadyaś: immediately, right now
    • cin: indeed, truly
    • nu: now, certainly
    • te: your
    • maghavann: O powerful one, O great one
    • abhiṣṭau: in the offerings, in the prayers
    • naraḥ: man, human being
    • śaṁsanty: praise, glorify
    • ukthaśāsa: the one who is praised
    • ukthā: the utterance, the hymn
    • ye: those who
    • te: your
    • havebhir: with offerings
    • vi: through, by means of
    • paṇīm̐r: hands, actions
    • adāśann: have given, have bestowed
    • asmān: us, our
    • vṛṇīṣva: may you choose, may you grant
    • yujyāya: for connection, for union
    • tasmai: to that one, to him
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer directed towards a powerful deity, asking for blessings and guidance.
    • The speaker acknowledges the importance of offerings and praises, emphasizing the role of human actions in connecting with the divine.
    • It reflects the belief that through sincere offerings and prayers, one can receive divine support and guidance in their endeavors.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, employees may come together to offer their best efforts and teamwork, seeking the support of a higher purpose or vision to achieve their goals.
    • In personal life, individuals may engage in rituals or practices that symbolize their intentions and desires, such as lighting candles or meditating, to invite positive energy and guidance into their lives.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of intention, action, and connection with the divine in achieving one's goals.
    • It teaches that through sincere efforts and offerings, individuals can seek blessings and support in their personal and professional lives.
    • In essence, it encourages a proactive approach to life, where one actively seeks to align their actions with their aspirations, fostering a sense of purpose and fulfillment.
