कन् नव्यो अतसीनां तुरो गृणीत मर्त्यः |नहī nv अस्य महिमानम् इन्द्रियं स्वर गृणन्त आना्शुः ||
kan navyo atasīnāṁ turo gṛṇīta martyaḥ | nahī nv asya mahimānam indriyaṁ svar gṛṇanta ānaśuḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • kan: who
    • navyo: new, fresh
    • atasīnāṁ: of those who are seated, established
    • turo: swiftly, quickly
    • gṛṇīta: may sing, may praise
    • martyaḥ: mortal, human being
    • nahī: indeed, not
    • nv: indeed, not
    • asya: of this, of it
    • mahimānam: greatness, glory
    • indriyaṁ: senses, faculties
    • svar: heaven, celestial realm
    • gṛṇanta: singing, praising
    • ānaśuḥ: without delay, without hesitation
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks to the essence of human existence and the pursuit of divine qualities.
    • The phrase "kan navyo atasīnāṁ" suggests a call to those who are established in their lives to recognize the new and fresh opportunities around them.
    • "Turo gṛṇīta martyaḥ" emphasizes the importance of swiftly praising and acknowledging the divine or the greater truths of life.
    • The latter part, "nahī nv asya mahimānam indriyaṁ," indicates that the true greatness of this existence cannot be fully grasped by our senses alone.
    • Finally, "svar gṛṇanta ānaśuḥ" encourages us to sing praises of the divine without hesitation, highlighting the importance of devotion and recognition of the higher powers.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, a leader who recognizes and praises the contributions of their team fosters a positive environment, encouraging innovation and collaboration.
    • In personal relationships, acknowledging the efforts of loved ones can strengthen bonds and create a supportive atmosphere.
    • In spiritual practices, individuals who meditate or engage in prayer often find a deeper connection to the divine, transcending the limitations of their physical senses.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of recognizing the divine in our lives and the need to express gratitude and praise.
    • It highlights that while our senses may limit our understanding, the pursuit of spiritual knowledge and connection is essential.
    • In real-life scenarios, whether in personal, professional, or spiritual contexts, the act of acknowledging and praising the greater forces at play can lead to a more fulfilling and enriched existence.
