मन्त्र: उरु ते ज्रयः पर्य एति बुध्नं वीरोचमानं महिषस्य धाम | विश्वेभिर अग्ने स्वयशोभिर इद्धो 'दब्धेभिः पायुभिः पाह्य अस्मान् ||
uru te jrayaḥ pary eti budhnaṁ virocamānam mahiṣasya dhāma | viśvebhir agne svayaśobhir iddho 'dabdhebhiḥ pāyubhiḥ pāhy asmān ||
  • uru te jrayaḥ: This phrase can be translated as "the vastness of your strength." It signifies the immense power and energy that one possesses. In real life, this can be related to individuals who harness their inner strength to overcome challenges, such as athletes training rigorously to achieve their goals.
  • pary eti budhnaṁ: This means "approaching the awakened one." It suggests a journey towards enlightenment or knowledge. In practical terms, this can be seen in the pursuit of education, where students seek knowledge and wisdom from their teachers and mentors.
  • virocamānam mahiṣasya dhāma: This translates to "the radiant abode of the great one." It refers to a place of brilliance and greatness. In everyday life, this could symbolize a successful career or a fulfilling home life where one feels a sense of achievement and happiness.
  • viśvebhir agne: This phrase means "with all, O Fire." Fire here symbolizes energy, transformation, and purification. In real life, this can be likened to teamwork and collaboration, where individuals come together to achieve a common goal, much like a group project in a workplace.
  • svayaśobhir iddho 'dabdhebhiḥ: This translates to "by your own glory, unassailable." It emphasizes self-reliance and the strength that comes from within. This can be seen in entrepreneurs who build their businesses from the ground up, relying on their vision and determination.
  • pāyubhiḥ pāhy asmān: This means "protect us with your strength." It is a plea for safety and support. In real life, this can be related to the importance of community and support systems, such as family and friends who provide emotional and physical support during tough times.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates themes of strength, knowledge, collaboration, self-reliance, and protection. Its significance lies in the reminder that we all possess inner strength and the potential for greatness. In real life, these principles can be applied in various scenarios, such as personal development, teamwork, and seeking support from our communities. By embodying these values, individuals can navigate life's challenges more effectively and achieve their aspirations.
