वीलौ सतीर अभि धीराऽतृन्दन प्राचाहिन्वन् मनसा सप्त विप्राः |विश्वाम अविन्दन पथ्याम ऋतस्य प्रजानन् इति ता नमसā विवेश ||
vīḻau satīr abhi dhīrā atṛndan prācāhinvan manasā sapta viprāḥ | viśvām avindan pathyām ṛtasya prajānann it tā namasā viveśa ||
  • vīḻau satīr abhi dhīrā: This phrase can be interpreted as "the wise and steadfast ones." Here, "vīḻau" refers to those who are strong or powerful, "satīr" signifies truthfulness or righteousness, and "abhi dhīrā" indicates those who are courageous and wise.
  • atṛndan prācāhinvan manasā sapta viprāḥ: This part translates to "the seven seers who have attained knowledge through their minds." "Atṛndan" refers to those who have transcended limitations, "prācāhinvan" means those who have reached a higher understanding, and "sapta viprāḥ" signifies the seven sages or wise individuals.
  • viśvām avindan pathyām ṛtasya: This can be understood as "they discovered the universal path of truth." "Viśvām" means universal or all-encompassing, "avindan" refers to discovering or finding, and "pathyām ṛtasya" indicates the path of righteousness or truth.
  • prajānann it tā namasā viveśa: This phrase translates to "they entered into the essence of creation with reverence." "Prajānann" refers to the act of creation or birth, "it tā" means 'thus' or 'therefore,' and "namasā viveśa" signifies entering with respect or reverence.

  • Significance: This mantra emphasizes the importance of wisdom, courage, and the pursuit of truth. It highlights the role of enlightened individuals (the seven sages) in discovering universal truths and guiding others on the righteous path.
  • Real-life Application: In today's world, this mantra can inspire individuals to seek knowledge and wisdom in their lives. For example, educators and mentors can embody the qualities of the "wise and steadfast ones" by guiding students towards truth and understanding. Additionally, leaders in various fields can strive to be like the "seven sages," using their knowledge to create a positive impact on society.
  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a reminder of the value of wisdom, courage, and the pursuit of truth in our lives. By embodying these qualities, we can contribute to a more enlightened and harmonious society, just as the sages did in ancient times.
