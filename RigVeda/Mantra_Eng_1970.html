प्रथमā हि सुवाचसā होतारā दैव्यā कवī |यज्ञं नो यक्षताम इमम् ||
prathamā hi suvācasā hotārā daivyā kavī | yajñaṁ no yakṣatām imam ||
  • Mantra: "prathamā hi suvācasā hotārā daivyā kavī | yajñaṁ no yakṣatām imam ||"
  • Translation: "First, indeed, with excellent speech, the divine poet (or seer) shall invoke; may this sacrifice be accepted by us."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • prathamā: First, primary, or foremost.
    • suvācasā: With excellent or auspicious speech.
    • hotārā: The one who invokes or calls upon (often referring to a priest or seer).
    • daivyā: Divine or heavenly.
    • kavī: Poet, seer, or wise person.
    • yajñaṁ: Sacrifice, offering, or ritual.
    • no: Our or us.
    • yakṣatām: May it be accepted or may it be granted.
    • imam: This or here.
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of the spoken word and the role of the divine in rituals.
    • The invocation of the divine through excellent speech signifies the power of words in shaping reality and invoking blessings.
    • In real life, this can be seen in various contexts such as public speaking, where the choice of words can inspire and motivate others.
    • For example, a leader addressing a crowd can uplift spirits and encourage unity through powerful and positive language.
    • The concept of sacrifice (yajña) can be interpreted beyond physical offerings; it can also refer to the sacrifices we make in our daily lives for the greater good, such as volunteering or helping others.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of intention and the power of words in our actions.
    • It teaches us that invoking the divine through our speech and actions can lead to positive outcomes and acceptance of our efforts.
    • In a broader sense, it encourages us to communicate effectively and with purpose, whether in personal relationships, professional settings, or community engagements.
    • Ultimately, it serves as a reminder that our words and actions, when aligned with a higher purpose, can create a meaningful impact in the world around us.
