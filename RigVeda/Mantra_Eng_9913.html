इष्कृतिर नाम वो माताथो युयम् स्थ निष्कृतिः |सीराः पतत्रिणीः स्थन यद आमयति निष कृत ||
iṣkṛtir nāma vo mātātho yūyaṁ stha niṣkṛtīḥ | sīrāḥ patatriṇīḥ sthana yad āmayati niṣ kṛtha ||
  • iṣkṛtir nāma vo mātātho yūyaṁ stha niṣkṛtīḥ
    • iṣkṛtir: This term refers to the act of creation or formation. It signifies the essence of bringing something into existence.
    • nāma: Meaning "name," it indicates the identity or the essence of what is being referred to.
    • vo: This is a form of address, meaning "to you" or "for you," indicating a connection to the audience or the divine.
    • mātātho: Translated as "mother," it signifies nurturing, care, and the source of life.
    • yūyaṁ: Meaning "you all," it addresses a collective group, emphasizing community or unity.
    • stha: This term means "to stand" or "to be established," indicating stability and permanence.
    • niṣkṛtīḥ: Refers to "liberation" or "release," suggesting a state of being free from bondage or suffering.
  • sīrāḥ patatriṇīḥ sthana yad āmayati niṣ kṛtha
    • sīrāḥ: This word means "wings," symbolizing freedom, elevation, and the ability to transcend limitations.
    • patatriṇīḥ: Refers to "birds," which are often seen as messengers or symbols of the soul's journey.
    • sthana: Meaning "place" or "position," it indicates a specific location or state of being.
    • yad: Translated as "that which," it introduces a clause that describes a condition or action.
    • āmayati: This term means "to bring forth" or "to produce," indicating the act of creation or manifestation.
    • niṣ kṛtha: Meaning "be free" or "be liberated," it emphasizes the importance of achieving freedom from constraints.

Conclusion:

The mantra speaks to the themes of creation, nurturing, community, and liberation. It emphasizes the importance of standing firm in one's identity and the collective journey towards freedom. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios such as:

  • In personal development, recognizing one's potential and striving for liberation from self-imposed limitations.
  • In community building, fostering a sense of unity and support among individuals to create a nurturing environment.
  • In environmental conservation, understanding our role as caretakers of the earth, akin to a mother nurturing her children.

This mantra serves as a reminder of our interconnectedness and the importance of striving for liberation and growth, both individually and collectively.
