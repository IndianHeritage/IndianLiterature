जिघर्म्य अग्निं हविषा घृतेन प्रतिक्षियन्तं भुवनानि विश्वा |पृतुं तिरश्चा वयसा बृहन्तं व्यचिष्टम् अन्नै रभसं दृष्टानम् ||
jigharmy agniṁ haviṣā ghṛtena pratikṣiyantam bhuvanāni viśvā | pṛthuṁ tiraścā vayasā bṛhantaṁ vyaciṣṭham annai rabhasaṁ dṛśānam ||
  • jigharmy agniṁ: This phrase translates to "I seek the fire." In the context of the Rigveda, fire symbolizes purity, transformation, and the divine presence. In real life, fire can represent passion and the drive to achieve one's goals.
  • haviṣā ghṛtena: This means "with offerings and clarified butter." Offerings are a way to show gratitude and respect to the divine. In modern life, this can be likened to acts of kindness and generosity towards others, fostering a sense of community and connection.
  • pratikṣiyantam bhuvanāni viśvā: This translates to "the worlds that are being sustained." It emphasizes the interconnectedness of all existence. In today's world, this can be seen in environmental efforts where individuals and communities work together to sustain the planet.
  • pṛthuṁ tiraścā vayasā bṛhantaṁ: This phrase means "the vast and mighty." It refers to the strength and resilience found in nature and humanity. In real life, this can be observed in the perseverance of individuals facing challenges, showcasing human strength and adaptability.
  • vyaciṣṭham annai rabhasaṁ dṛśānam: This translates to "the abundant food that is seen." Food is a fundamental necessity for life, and this line highlights the importance of nourishment. In contemporary society, this can relate to the significance of food security and the efforts to provide for those in need.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates themes of seeking divine guidance, the importance of offerings, the interconnectedness of life, human strength, and the necessity of nourishment. Its significance lies in reminding us of our responsibilities towards ourselves, each other, and the environment. In real-life scenarios, it encourages us to pursue our passions, engage in acts of kindness, work towards sustainability, demonstrate resilience, and ensure that everyone has access to basic needs like food. By embodying these principles, we can create a more harmonious and supportive world.
