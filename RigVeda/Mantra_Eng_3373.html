यत्रोता मार्त्याय कम अरिणा इन्द्र सूर्यं |प्रावः शचीभिर एतशम् ||
yatrota martyāya kam ariṇā indra sūryam | prāvaḥ śacībhir etaśam ||
  • Mantra: "yatrota martyāya kam ariṇā indra sūryam | prāvaḥ śacībhir etaśam ||"
  • Translation: "Wherever the mortal (martyāya) is, may Indra (the god of strength) and the Sun (sūryam) protect him with their power (ariṇā) and blessings (śacībhir)."

  • Word Breakdown:
    • yatra: wherever
    • martyāya: mortal, referring to human beings
    • kam: who
    • ariṇā: with strength or power
    • indra: the king of gods, symbolizing strength and power
    • sūryam: the Sun, representing light, energy, and life
    • prāvaḥ: may protect or guide
    • śacībhir: with blessings or divine grace
    • etaśam: this power or strength

  • Contextual Meaning:
    • This mantra emphasizes the need for divine protection and strength in the life of a mortal being.
    • Indra and the Sun are invoked as powerful entities that can provide guidance and support.
    • The mantra reflects a universal desire for safety, strength, and blessings in the face of life's challenges.

  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In times of personal crisis, such as illness or loss, individuals often seek strength and support from higher powers, similar to invoking Indra and the Sun.
    • During significant life events, like weddings or new ventures, people may recite prayers or mantras to seek blessings and protection, mirroring the essence of this RigVeda mantra.
    • In various cultures, rituals are performed to invoke divine protection for travelers, akin to the plea for safety and strength in this mantra.

  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of seeking strength and protection from higher powers in our lives.
    • It highlights the interconnectedness of human beings with the divine, emphasizing that we are not alone in our struggles.
    • In practical terms, it encourages individuals to cultivate a sense of faith and resilience, drawing on both spiritual and personal resources to navigate life's challenges.
