अग्नि सहन्तम आ भर द्युम्नस्य प्रासहā रयिम् |विश्वा यश चर्षणीर अभ्य आ३सā वाजेषु सासहत् ||
agne sahantam ā bhara dyumnasya prāsahā rayim | viśvā yaś carṣaṇīr abhy ā3sā vājeṣu sāsahat ||
  • Agne: This word refers to Agni, the fire god in Vedic literature, symbolizing energy, light, and purity.
  • Sahantam: This term means 'to bear' or 'to support', indicating the strength and resilience of Agni.
  • Ā bhara: Translated as 'bring forth' or 'bestow', it signifies the act of providing or generating something valuable.
  • Dyumnasya: This word means 'of glory' or 'of brilliance', referring to the radiance and splendor associated with success and achievement.
  • Prāsahā: This term can be interpreted as 'wealth' or 'prosperity', emphasizing the material and spiritual abundance that one seeks.
  • Rayim: This word signifies 'wealth' or 'prosperity', reinforcing the desire for abundance in life.
  • Viśvā: Meaning 'all' or 'universal', it indicates inclusiveness and the collective nature of existence.
  • Yaś: This translates to 'fame' or 'renown', highlighting the importance of reputation and recognition in society.
  • Carṣaṇīr: This term refers to 'fields' or 'pastures', symbolizing growth, nourishment, and the fruits of labor.
  • Abhy ā3sā: Meaning 'to strive' or 'to endeavor', it emphasizes the effort required to achieve one's goals.
  • Vājeṣu: This word means 'in the offerings' or 'in the sacrifices', indicating the importance of rituals and contributions to the community.
  • Sāsahat: This term can be interpreted as 'to be strong' or 'to be powerful', reinforcing the idea of resilience and capability.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a prayer to Agni, seeking his blessings for strength, prosperity, and success in all endeavors. It emphasizes the importance of striving for glory and wealth, not just for oneself but for the collective good. In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios such as starting a new venture, seeking success in personal or professional life, or during rituals that aim to invoke blessings for abundance. It serves as a reminder that with effort, resilience, and the right intentions, one can achieve greatness and contribute positively to society.
