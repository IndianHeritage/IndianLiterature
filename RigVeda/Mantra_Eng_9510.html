हंसैर इव सकिभिर वावदद्भिर अश्मनमयानी नहनां व्यस्यन् | बृहस्पतिर अभिकनिक्रदद गा उता प्रास्तौद उच च विद्वांं अगायत ||
haṁsair iva sakhibhir vāvadadbhir aśmanmayāni nahanā vyasyan | bṛhaspatir abhikanikradad gā uta prāstaud uc ca vidvām̐ agāyat ||
  • haṁsair iva sakhibhir: This phrase can be translated as "like swans with their companions." The swan is often seen as a symbol of purity and grace in various cultures. In real life, this can be related to the importance of companionship and community in achieving harmony and success.
  • vāvadadbhir aśmanmayāni: This translates to "with voices that resonate like stones." Here, the imagery of stones suggests strength and permanence. In life, this can be interpreted as the power of strong, resonant communication that can withstand the test of time and challenges.
  • nahanā vyasyan: This means "casting away the burdens." It signifies the act of letting go of difficulties or obstacles. In practical terms, this can relate to the importance of resilience and the ability to overcome challenges in our daily lives.
  • bṛhaspatir abhikanikradad: This phrase refers to "Bṛhaspati, the lord of wisdom." Bṛhaspati is often associated with knowledge and guidance. In real life, this can be seen as the pursuit of wisdom and the importance of seeking guidance from knowledgeable sources in our decision-making processes.
  • gā uta prāstaud uc ca: This translates to "singing and rising high." It emphasizes the idea of elevation through expression and creativity. In everyday life, this can be related to the importance of self-expression and the uplifting power of art, music, and creativity.
  • vidvām̐ agāyat: This means "the wise one sang." It highlights the role of wisdom in guiding actions and thoughts. In practical terms, this can be interpreted as the significance of wisdom in leadership and the impact of wise decisions on the community.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of community, communication, resilience, wisdom, and creativity. It signifies the importance of surrounding oneself with supportive companions, the strength of effective communication, the necessity of overcoming burdens, the pursuit of knowledge, and the uplifting power of self-expression. In real life, these principles can be applied in various scenarios, such as teamwork in professional settings, the importance of mentorship, and the role of creativity in personal growth. By embodying these values, individuals can foster a harmonious and successful life, contributing positively to their communities.
