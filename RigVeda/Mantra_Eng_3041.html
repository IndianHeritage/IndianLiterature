इयं ते पूषन्नाघृणे सुष्टुतिर देव नव्यसी |अस्माभिः तुभ्यं शस्यते ||
iyaṁ te pūṣann āghṛṇe suṣṭutir deva navyasī | asmābhis tubhyaṁ śasyate ||
  • iyaṁ te pūṣann: This phrase can be translated as "This is for you, O Pusha." Pusha is a deity associated with nourishment and prosperity, often invoked for abundance in agriculture and livestock.
  • āghṛṇe: This word means "to be invoked" or "to be called upon." It signifies the act of seeking blessings or assistance from the divine.
  • suṣṭutir: This term translates to "well-uttered praise" or "excellent praise." It emphasizes the importance of expressing gratitude and reverence towards the divine.
  • deva navyasī: This means "O divine one, who is new." It suggests the idea of renewal and freshness, indicating that the divine presence is ever-rejuvenating.
  • asmābhis tubhyaṁ: This translates to "from us to you." It indicates a relationship of devotion and offering from the worshippers to the deity.
  • śasyate: This word means "may it be fruitful" or "may it yield." It expresses the desire for abundance and prosperity in the endeavors of the worshippers.

In summary, this mantra is a prayer to the deity Pusha, seeking blessings for nourishment, prosperity, and abundance. It emphasizes the importance of invoking the divine with sincere praise and gratitude. The mantra reflects a deep connection between humans and the divine, highlighting the need for spiritual support in achieving material success.


Significance and Real-Life Application:

  • This mantra can be applied in agricultural practices, where farmers may chant it to seek blessings for a bountiful harvest.
  • In personal life, individuals can use this mantra during times of need, invoking divine support for prosperity in their careers or personal endeavors.
  • It serves as a reminder of the importance of gratitude and reverence in our daily lives, encouraging individuals to appreciate the abundance they receive.

Ultimately, the mantra teaches us that by acknowledging and honoring the divine, we can cultivate a fruitful and prosperous life, both materially and spiritually.
