सां वह योक्शभिर अवातोषो वरं वहसि जोषम् अनु |त्वं दिवो दुतितर या ह देवि पूर्वहूतौ मंहनā दर्शता भूः ||
sā vaha yokṣabhir avātoṣo varaṁ vahasi joṣam anu | tvaṁ divo duhitar yā ha devī pūrvahūtau maṁhanā darśatā bhūḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sā: that
    • vaha: carry or bring
    • yokṣabhir: with the powers or energies
    • avātoṣo: not displeased or not angry
    • varaṁ: the best or the boon
    • vahasi: you carry or bring
    • joṣam: joy or delight
    • anu: following or after
    • tvaṁ: you
    • divo: of the sky or heaven
    • duhitar: daughter
    • yā: who
    • ha: indeed
    • devī: goddess
    • pūrvahūtau: in the earlier invocations
    • maṁhanā: with great strength
    • darśatā: showing or revealing
    • bhūḥ: the earth or the world
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes a divine presence, asking for the blessings and joy that come from the goddess, who is seen as a daughter of the heavens.
    • The phrase "sā vaha" suggests a request for the divine to bring forth blessings and positive energies into our lives.
    • The mention of "avātoṣo" indicates a desire for harmony and the absence of negativity, emphasizing the importance of a peaceful existence.
    • By referring to the goddess as "duhitar," it highlights the nurturing and protective qualities associated with feminine divinity.
    • The mantra also acknowledges the past invocations ("pūrvahūtau"), suggesting a continuity of tradition and the importance of remembering our roots.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, this mantra can be recited during moments of stress or conflict, inviting peace and joy into one's surroundings.
    • In family settings, invoking the nurturing aspect of the goddess can help strengthen bonds and promote understanding among family members.
    • During significant life events, such as weddings or new beginnings, this mantra can serve as a reminder of the blessings and joy that one seeks from the divine.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its ability to connect individuals with divine energies that promote joy, peace, and strength.
    • It serves as a reminder of the importance of invoking positive energies in our lives and the power of tradition in fostering a sense of community and continuity.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be a source of comfort and guidance, encouraging individuals to seek harmony and joy in their daily interactions and experiences.
