मन्त्र: उता ब्रह्मण्याऽवयं तुभ्यम् प्रवृद्ध वज्रिवः |विप्राः अतक्ष्म जīvसे ||
uta brahmaṇyā vayaṁ tubhyam pravṛddha vajrivaḥ | viprā atakṣma jīvase ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • uta: and, also
    • brahmaṇyā: to the divine, to the ultimate reality
    • vayaṁ: we
    • tubhyam: to you
    • pravṛddha: increased, enhanced
    • vajrivaḥ: like the thunderbolt, powerful
    • viprā: the wise, the learned
    • atakṣma: not to be harmed, invulnerable
    • jīvase: for life, for living
  • Overall Meaning:
    • This mantra expresses a prayer or invocation to the divine, acknowledging the power and strength that comes from it.
    • It emphasizes the relationship between the divine and the seekers (the wise), highlighting the importance of wisdom and strength in life.
    • The phrase "like the thunderbolt" signifies immense power and resilience, suggesting that those who are connected to the divine are fortified against challenges.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In times of adversity, individuals often seek strength through spirituality or faith, much like the invocation in this mantra. For instance, someone facing a personal crisis may turn to prayer or meditation to find inner strength.
    • In leadership, wise leaders often draw upon their values and principles (akin to the divine) to guide their decisions, ensuring they remain resilient and effective, just as the mantra suggests a connection to a higher power for strength.
    • In the context of community, when people come together with shared wisdom and purpose, they can create a powerful force for positive change, reflecting the collective strength mentioned in the mantra.
  • Conclusion and Significance:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of seeking strength and wisdom from a higher source, especially in challenging times.
    • It highlights the idea that true resilience comes from a connection to the divine and the wisdom of the learned, which can be applied in various aspects of life, including personal growth, leadership, and community building.
    • Ultimately, it encourages individuals to cultivate their inner strength and wisdom, enabling them to navigate life's challenges with confidence and grace.
