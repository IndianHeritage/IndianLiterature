आ संयतम इन्द्र णः स्वस्तिं शत्रुतूर्याय बृहतीं अमृतध्राम् | यया दासान्य आर्याणि वृत्रā करो वज्रिन सुतुकां नाहुषाणि ||
ā saṁyatam indra ṇaḥ svastiṁ śatrutūryāya bṛhatīm amṛdhrām | yayā dāsāny āryāṇi vṛtrā karo vajrin sutukā nāhuṣāṇi ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā: O
    • saṁyatam: controlled, restrained
    • indra: Indra, the king of gods
    • ṇaḥ: for us
    • svastiṁ: well-being, prosperity
    • śatrutūryāya: for the destruction of enemies
    • bṛhatīm: great, vast
    • amṛdhrām: immortal, eternal
    • yayā: by which
    • dāsāny: servants, followers
    • āryāṇi: noble ones
    • vṛtrā: the demon Vritra
    • karo: who causes
    • vajrin: the one with the thunderbolt (Indra)
    • sutukā: who is strong
    • nāhuṣāṇi: the descendants of Nahusha
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Indra, seeking his blessings for prosperity and the destruction of enemies.
    • The term "saṁyatam" emphasizes the importance of self-control and restraint in achieving one's goals.
    • Indra is invoked as a powerful deity who can provide strength and support in overcoming obstacles.
    • The mention of "dāsāny āryāṇi" highlights the importance of noble followers and the collective effort in achieving success.
    • The reference to "vṛtrā" symbolizes the challenges and adversities one faces, which can be overcome with divine assistance.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be applied when seeking success in a competitive market. Just as Indra is called upon for strength, entrepreneurs often seek guidance and support from mentors and advisors.
    • In personal life, individuals may face challenges such as health issues or relationship problems. The mantra serves as a reminder to maintain self-control and seek help from supportive friends and family.
    • In community settings, this mantra can inspire collective action against social injustices, emphasizing the need for unity and strength in numbers.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of divine support, self-restraint, and collective effort in overcoming challenges.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal struggles to professional endeavors, reminding us that with the right mindset and support, we can achieve our goals and overcome adversities.
    • Ultimately, it encourages individuals to seek strength from higher powers while also emphasizing the value of community and noble intentions in the pursuit of success.
