आ यन् मां वेना अरुहन्न ऋतस्यं एकं आसीनं हर्यतस्य पीष्ठे |मनश्चिन मे हृदा आ प्रत्य अवोचद अचिक्रदण् चिशुमन्तः सखायः ||
ā yan mā venā aruhann ṛtasyam̐ ekam āsīnaṁ haryatasya pṛṣṭhe | manaś cin me hṛda ā praty avocad acikradañ chiśumantaḥ sakhāyaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā yan: "from where" or "from which"
    • mā: "not"
    • venā: "to be disturbed" or "to be shaken"
    • aruhann: "they ascend" or "they rise"
    • ṛtasyam̐: "of truth" or "of righteousness"
    • ekam: "one" or "the singular"
    • āsīnaṁ: "sitting" or "being seated"
    • haryatasya: "of the horse" or "of the swift"
    • pṛṣṭhe: "on the back" or "upon the back"
    • manaś: "of the mind" or "of thought"
    • cin: "to seek" or "to find"
    • me: "my"
    • hṛda: "heart" or "soul"
    • ā: "to" or "towards"
    • praty: "in response" or "in return"
    • avocad: "spoke" or "declared"
    • acikradañ: "to be aware" or "to understand"
    • chiśumantaḥ: "of the young" or "of the child"
    • sakhāyaḥ: "friends" or "companions"
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the quest for truth and the importance of a stable mind.
    • The phrase "from where not to be disturbed" suggests a state of mental peace that is essential for understanding and wisdom.
    • The reference to "one sitting on the back of the horse" symbolizes the idea of control and mastery over one's thoughts and emotions.
    • The mantra emphasizes the importance of friendship and companionship in the journey of life, particularly in the pursuit of truth.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a stressful work environment, maintaining a calm and focused mind can lead to better decision-making and productivity.
    • In personal relationships, having supportive friends can help individuals navigate challenges and seek truth in their interactions.
    • Practices such as meditation and mindfulness can help individuals achieve the mental state described in the mantra, allowing them to rise above distractions.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of mental stability, the pursuit of truth, and the value of companionship.
    • In real life, it serves as a reminder to cultivate a peaceful mind, seek truth in our actions, and cherish the support of friends.
    • By embodying these principles, individuals can navigate life's challenges with greater clarity and purpose.
