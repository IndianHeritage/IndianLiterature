विवृतम् पर्वशो ययुर् विपर्वताम् अराजिनः |चक्राणां वृष्णि पौंस्यम् ||
vi vṛtram parvaśo yayur vi parvatām̐ arājinaḥ | cakrāṇā vṛṣṇi pauṁsyam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • vi: apart, away
    • vṛtram: the obstacle, the demon
    • parvaśo: of the powerful, the strong
    • yayur: they have gone, they have moved
    • vi: apart, away
    • parvatām̐: to the mountains, the peaks
    • arājinaḥ: without kings, without rulers
    • cakrāṇā: of the wheel, of the cycle
    • vṛṣṇi: of the Vṛṣhṇi, a clan or lineage
    • pauṁsyam: of manhood, of masculinity
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about overcoming obstacles and challenges (vṛtram) that are powerful (parvaśo).
    • It suggests a movement away from these obstacles towards higher aspirations (parvatām̐).
    • The mention of "arājinaḥ" indicates a state of being without rulers, which can symbolize freedom from oppression or control.
    • The reference to "cakrāṇā" and "vṛṣṇi" can be interpreted as a call to embrace the cycles of life and the strength of community or lineage.
    • Overall, it emphasizes the importance of unity and strength in overcoming life's challenges.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In personal development, individuals often face obstacles that seem insurmountable. This mantra can inspire one to move away from these challenges and seek higher goals, much like a mountain climber who perseveres despite the difficulties.
    • In a community context, when a group faces oppression or lack of leadership, this mantra can serve as a reminder to unite and find strength in their collective identity, similar to social movements that rise against injustice.
    • In the corporate world, teams often encounter significant challenges. This mantra can be a motivational tool to encourage collaboration and resilience, akin to a sports team rallying together to overcome a tough opponent.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its powerful message of overcoming obstacles through unity and strength.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal struggles to community challenges, reminding us that we can rise above difficulties by working together and striving for higher aspirations.
    • Ultimately, it encourages a mindset of resilience and collective strength, which is essential in navigating the complexities of life.
