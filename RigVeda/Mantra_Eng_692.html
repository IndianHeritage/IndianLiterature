तं त्वा वयम् पतिम अग्ने रयीणाम् प्र शंसामो मतीभिर गोतमासः |आशुं न वाजम्भरम् मरजयन्तः प्रातर मक्षू धियावसुर जगम्यत् ||
taṁ tvā vayam patim agne rayīṇām pra śaṁsāmo matibhir gotamāsaḥ | āśuṁ na vājambharam marjayantaḥ prātar makṣū dhiyāvasur jagamyāt ||
  • taṁ tvā vayam patim agne rayīṇām: This phrase translates to "We praise you, O Agni, the lord of wealth." Here, Agni represents the divine fire and is often associated with prosperity and abundance. In real life, this can be likened to the importance of nurturing our resources and wealth, whether it be financial, emotional, or spiritual.
  • pra śaṁsāmo matibhir gotamāsaḥ: This means "We extol you with our thoughts, O Agni." It emphasizes the role of intention and mindfulness in our actions. For example, when we set goals in our careers or personal lives, it is essential to align our thoughts and intentions with our aspirations to achieve success.
  • āśuṁ na vājambharam marjayantaḥ: This translates to "Quickly, may we remove obstacles." This line highlights the importance of overcoming challenges in our lives. In a practical sense, this could refer to tackling procrastination or addressing issues in relationships, thereby allowing us to move forward more effectively.
  • prātar makṣū dhiyāvasur jagamyāt: This means "In the morning, may we attain our goals with wisdom." It signifies the value of starting each day with purpose and clarity. For instance, establishing a morning routine that includes meditation or planning can help set a positive tone for the day ahead.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of Agni as a symbol of wealth and prosperity, the power of intention, the necessity of overcoming obstacles, and the importance of starting each day with wisdom and purpose. In real life, these principles can guide us in our personal and professional endeavors, encouraging us to cultivate a mindset that attracts abundance, fosters resilience, and promotes mindful living. By embodying these teachings, we can navigate life's challenges more effectively and achieve our goals with clarity and determination.
