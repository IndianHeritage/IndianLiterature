स नश्चित्राभिरद्रिवोऽनवद्याभिरूतिभिः |अनाधृष्टाभिरागहि ||
sa naś citrābhir adrivo 'navadyābhir ūtibhiḥ | anādhṛṣṭābhir ā gahi ||
  • sa naś - "May we" or "Let us" - This phrase indicates a collective intention or desire for well-being and prosperity.
  • citrābhir - "with bright" or "with splendid" - This word suggests brilliance, clarity, and positivity, often associated with knowledge and enlightenment.
  • adrivo - "from the mountains" - Mountains symbolize strength, stability, and the heights of achievement. They can also represent challenges that one must overcome.
  • anavadyābhir - "with faultless" or "with pure" - This term emphasizes purity and perfection, suggesting that the intentions and actions should be without blemish.
  • ūtibhiḥ - "with energies" or "with powers" - This refers to the various forms of energy or strength that can be harnessed for positive outcomes.
  • anādhṛṣṭābhir - "with unshaken" or "with unwavering" - This indicates steadfastness and resilience, qualities essential for achieving goals.
  • ā gahi - "may you grasp" or "may you hold" - This is an invitation to take hold of the blessings and strengths being offered.

The mantra can be interpreted as a collective prayer or aspiration for strength, purity, and unwavering resolve in the pursuit of knowledge and success. It encourages individuals to seek brilliance and positivity while remaining steadfast in their efforts.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios:

  • Education: Students can recite this mantra before exams or important presentations, invoking clarity of thought and purity of intention.
  • Personal Development: Individuals striving for self-improvement can use this mantra to remind themselves to remain steadfast and focused on their goals.
  • Teamwork: In a collaborative environment, teams can chant this mantra to foster unity, strength, and a shared vision for success.

Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for collective strength, purity of purpose, and unwavering resolve. It serves as a reminder that by harnessing our inner energies and maintaining a positive outlook, we can overcome challenges and achieve our aspirations. Whether in personal endeavors, educational pursuits, or collaborative efforts, this mantra encourages us to strive for excellence and hold onto our goals with determination.
