अपान्यद एत्य अभ्य अन्यद एति विषुरूपे अहनी सं चरते |परिक्षितो तमो अन्याऽ गुहाकर अद्यौद उषाः शोशुचता रथेन ||
apānyad ety abhy a1nyad eti viṣurūpe ahanī saṁ carete | parikṣitos tamo anyā guhākar adyaud uṣāḥ śośucatā rathena ||
  • Mantra: "apānyad ety abhy a1nyad eti viṣurūpe ahanī saṁ carete | parikṣitos tamo anyā guhākar adyaud uṣāḥ śośucatā rathena ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • apānyad: not going away
    • ety: goes
    • abhy: towards
    • anyad: another
    • eti: goes
    • viṣurūpe: in various forms
    • ahanī: day or light
    • saṁ carete: moves together
    • parikṣitos: surrounded or enveloped
    • tamo: darkness
    • anyā: another
    • guhākar: cave-like or hidden
    • adyau: today
    • uṣāḥ: dawn or light
    • śośucatā: shining or illuminating
    • rathena: by the chariot
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the duality of existence, where one does not stray away from their path while simultaneously exploring different forms of life.
    • The phrase "apānyad ety" suggests a steadfastness in one's journey, akin to how a person remains committed to their goals despite distractions.
    • The mention of "viṣurūpe" indicates the various forms and experiences one encounters, similar to how individuals adapt to different roles in life, such as being a parent, a professional, or a friend.
    • "Ahanī saṁ carete" reflects the idea of moving together with the light of knowledge and wisdom, much like how a community thrives when its members support each other.
    • The reference to "parikṣitos tamo" highlights the challenges and darkness one faces, akin to personal struggles or societal issues that require resilience and courage to overcome.
    • "Guhākar adyaud uṣāḥ" symbolizes the dawn breaking through the darkness, representing hope and new beginnings, similar to how individuals find strength after overcoming hardships.
    • The final part, "śośucatā rathena," can be likened to the journey of life being a chariot that carries us through various experiences, emphasizing the importance of direction and purpose.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra encapsulates the essence of life's journey, emphasizing the balance between steadfastness and exploration.
    • Its significance lies in encouraging individuals to remain committed to their paths while being open to new experiences and forms of existence.
    • In real-life scenarios, this can be applied to personal growth, career development, and community engagement, where one must navigate challenges while seeking knowledge and connection.
    • Ultimately, it serves as a reminder that even in darkness, there is always the potential for light and renewal, urging us to embrace both our struggles and triumphs.
