त्वं पवित्रे राजसो विधर्मणि देवभ्यः सोम पवमान पूयसे |त्वां उषिजः प्रथमā अगृभ्णत तुभ्यमā विश्व भुवनानि येमिरे ||
tvam pavitre rajaso vidharmaṇi devebhyaḥ soma pavamāna pūyase | tvām uśijaḥ prathamā agṛbhṇata tubhyemā viśvā bhuvanāni yemire ||
  • tvam pavitre rajaso vidharmaṇi: This phrase translates to "You, O Soma, are pure and uphold righteousness." Here, Soma is personified as a divine entity that embodies purity and moral order. In real life, this can be likened to individuals or leaders who strive to maintain integrity and ethical standards in their actions.
  • devebhyaḥ soma pavamāna pūyase: This means "You, O Soma, are purified by the gods." It signifies the divine nature of Soma, suggesting that purity is not just a personal attribute but is also recognized and enhanced by divine forces. In everyday life, this can be seen in the way communities come together to support and uplift individuals who embody virtuous qualities.
  • tvām uśijaḥ prathamā agṛbhṇata: This translates to "The wise ones first grasp you." It implies that those who are knowledgeable and wise are the first to recognize and appreciate the essence of purity and righteousness. In practical terms, this can be observed in how mentors or leaders are often the first to identify and nurture talent and virtue in others.
  • tubhyemā viśvā bhuvanāni yemire: This means "All worlds are drawn to you." It suggests that the influence of purity and righteousness extends beyond individual lives to encompass entire communities and societies. In real life, this can be seen in how ethical practices in business or governance attract positive attention and foster trust among people.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of purity, righteousness, and the recognition of these qualities by both divine and human entities. It highlights the importance of integrity in leadership and the collective admiration that arises from virtuous actions. In contemporary society, this mantra can serve as a guiding principle for individuals and organizations striving to create a positive impact, reminding us that purity of intention and action can lead to broader societal harmony and respect.
