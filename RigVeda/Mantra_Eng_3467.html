स वाज्य अर्वा स ऋषिर वचस्यया स शूरो अस्तā पृतनासु दुष्टरः |स रायस पोषं स सुवीर्यं दधे यं वाजो विभ्वाम् ऋभवो यम् आविषुः ||
sa vājy arvā sa ṛṣir vacasyayā sa śūro astā pṛtanāsu duṣṭaraḥ | sa rāyas poṣaṁ sa suvīryaṁ dadhe yaṁ vājo vibhvām̐ ṛbhavo yam āviṣuḥ ||
  • sa vājy arvā: This phrase can be interpreted as "he who is strong and powerful." In real life, this can refer to individuals who exhibit strength in various forms, such as physical strength, mental resilience, or emotional stability. For example, a leader who guides their team through challenges demonstrates this strength.
  • sa ṛṣir vacasyayā: This translates to "the sage who speaks wisely." This emphasizes the importance of wisdom in communication. In everyday life, this can be seen in mentors or teachers who provide guidance based on their experiences and knowledge, helping others navigate their paths.
  • sa śūro astā pṛtanāsu duṣṭaraḥ: This means "the brave one who overcomes difficulties." It highlights the value of courage in facing challenges. For instance, a person who stands up against injustice or advocates for change in their community embodies this bravery.
  • sa rāyas poṣaṁ: This phrase refers to "the one who nourishes wealth." It signifies the importance of nurturing resources, whether they are financial, emotional, or social. In real life, this can be seen in entrepreneurs who invest in their businesses or individuals who support their families and communities.
  • sa suvīryaṁ dadhe: This translates to "he who possesses great strength." This can relate to individuals who demonstrate exceptional skills or talents in their fields, such as athletes, artists, or scientists who push the boundaries of what is possible.
  • yaṁ vājo vibhvām̐ ṛbhavo yam āviṣuḥ: This phrase can be interpreted as "the one who is surrounded by abundance and prosperity." It reflects the idea that those who embody strength, wisdom, and courage attract success and fulfillment in their lives. For example, a community leader who fosters collaboration and support among members often sees their community thrive.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the qualities of strength, wisdom, bravery, and the nurturing of resources. In real life, these attributes are essential for personal growth and community development. By embodying these qualities, individuals can overcome challenges, support others, and create a prosperous environment. This mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of these virtues in achieving success and fulfillment in various aspects of life.
