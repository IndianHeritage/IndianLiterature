त्वां tsārī दसमानो भागम् īṭṭे तक्ववीये |त्वं विश्वस्माद् भुवनात् पाशी धर्मणासूर्यात् पाशी धर्मणा ||
tvāṁ tsārī dasamāno bhagam īṭṭe takvavīye | tvaṁ viśvasmād bhuvanāt pāsi dharmaṇāsuryāt pāsi dharmaṇā ||
  • tvāṁ tsārī dasamāno bhagam īṭṭe takvavīye: This line can be interpreted as a call to a divine entity, seeking protection and guidance. The term "tvāṁ" refers to "you," indicating a direct address to a deity or higher power. "Dasamāno" suggests a connection to the ten directions or realms, symbolizing completeness and universality. "Bhagam" refers to fortune or blessings, while "īṭṭe" implies a request or invocation. "Takvavīye" can be understood as a plea for strength or support.
  • tvaṁ viśvasmād bhuvanāt pāsi dharmaṇāsuryāt pāsi dharmaṇā: In this line, "tvaṁ" again refers to the divine entity being addressed. "Viśvasmād bhuvanāt" translates to "from all worlds," indicating a desire for protection that encompasses all realms of existence. "Pāsi" means "to protect," and "dharmaṇāsuryāt" refers to the principles of righteousness or dharma. The repetition of "pāsi dharmaṇā" emphasizes the importance of being safeguarded by the forces of righteousness.

  • Real-Life Example: In modern life, this mantra can be seen as a reflection of the human quest for guidance and protection in a complex world. For instance, individuals often seek spiritual support during challenging times, such as illness or personal crises. Just as the mantra invokes divine protection, people may turn to prayer, meditation, or community support to find strength and resilience.
  • Significance: The mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking divine guidance and protection in all aspects of life. It highlights the interconnectedness of all beings and the need for righteousness in our actions. In a world filled with challenges, this mantra serves as a reminder to align ourselves with higher principles and seek support from the universe.
  • Application: This mantra can be applied in various real-life scenarios, such as during personal challenges, community conflicts, or even global issues. By invoking the essence of this mantra, individuals can cultivate a sense of peace, purpose, and connection to something greater than themselves. It encourages a proactive approach to living a life aligned with dharma, fostering harmony and balance in both personal and communal spheres.
