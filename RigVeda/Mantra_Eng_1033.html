युवम् एतानि दिवि रोचनान्य अग्निश च सोम सक्रतू अधत्तम् |युवं सिंधूम् अभिशस्तेर अवद्याद अग्नīषोमाव अमुञ्चतं गृभीतान् ||
yuvam etāni divi rocanāny agniś ca soma sakratū adhattam | yuvaṁ sindhūm̐r abhiśaster avadyād agnīṣomāv amuñcataṁ gṛbhītān ||
  • yuvam etāni divi rocanāny: This phrase can be translated as "You two, who shine in the heavens." Here, "yuvam" refers to the duality of forces or entities, often interpreted as the divine or celestial beings. In real life, this can symbolize the balance of opposites, such as light and darkness, or male and female energies.
  • agniś ca soma sakratū adhattam: This translates to "Agni and Soma, you who are powerful." Agni represents fire, which is often associated with energy, transformation, and purification, while Soma symbolizes the moon and is linked to nourishment and vitality. In everyday life, this can be seen in the balance of energy (Agni) and calmness (Soma) that we strive for in our lives.
  • yuvaṁ sindhūm̐r abhiśaster avadyād: This means "May you, the youthful ones, protect us from harm." The term "sindhūm̐r" refers to the rivers, which are often seen as life-giving forces. This can be related to the importance of nature and water in sustaining life. In a practical sense, it emphasizes the need for environmental conservation and respect for natural resources.
  • agnīṣomāv amuñcataṁ gṛbhītān: This translates to "May Agni and Soma release their blessings upon us." This line signifies the desire for divine blessings and support in our endeavors. In real life, it can be interpreted as a call for guidance and protection in our daily activities, whether in personal relationships, work, or spiritual pursuits.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of balance between opposing forces, the importance of nature, and the need for divine blessings in our lives. It teaches us to seek harmony between energy and calmness, to respect and protect our natural resources, and to remain open to receiving guidance and support from the universe. In a world that often feels chaotic, this mantra serves as a reminder to cultivate balance and seek the blessings of the divine in our everyday lives.
