अयम् अग्निः वध्र्यश्वस्य वृत्रहा सनकात् प्रेढो नमसोपवाक्यः |स नो अजामिम् उता वा विजामीन अभि तिष्ठ शर्धतो वाध्र्यश्व ||
ayam agnir vadhryaśvasya vṛtrahā sanakāt preddho namasopavākyaḥ | sa no ajāmīm̐r uta vā vijāmīn abhi tiṣṭha śardhato vādhryaśva ||
  • Mantra: "ayam agnir vadhryaśvasya vṛtrahā sanakāt preddho namasopavākyaḥ | sa no ajāmīm̐r uta vā vijāmīn abhi tiṣṭha śardhato vādhryaśva ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ayam: this
    • agnir: fire
    • vadhryaśvasya: of the strong horse
    • vṛtrahā: slayer of Vritra (the demon of drought)
    • sanakāt: from the wise
    • preddho: having been invoked
    • namasopavākyaḥ: with respectful words
    • sa: he
    • no: for us
    • ajāmīm̐r: the one who gives birth (often referring to the cow)
    • uta: or
    • vā: or
    • vijāmīn: the one who is wise
    • abhi: towards
    • tiṣṭha: may you stand
    • śardhato: with strength
    • vādhryaśva: O strong horse
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra invokes the divine essence of fire, symbolizing energy, transformation, and purification.
    • The reference to "vadhryaśvasya" (the strong horse) signifies strength and vitality, often associated with the power of nature and the universe.
    • "Vṛtrahā" refers to the slayer of Vritra, representing the overcoming of obstacles and challenges, akin to how we face difficulties in life.
    • The invocation of "ajāmīm̐r" (the one who gives birth) can be seen as a metaphor for nurturing and growth, similar to how a mother nurtures her child.
    • The phrase "abhi tiṣṭha" encourages standing strong and firm, which can be applied in real-life situations where resilience is required, such as facing personal or professional challenges.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of strength, resilience, and the transformative power of energy in our lives.
    • It serves as a reminder to invoke our inner strength and wisdom when faced with challenges, much like how we draw upon our resources during tough times.
    • In real life, this mantra can be applicable in various scenarios, such as during difficult decisions, personal growth journeys, or when striving for success in our endeavors.
    • Ultimately, it teaches us to respect the forces of nature and the divine, encouraging us to harness these energies for our personal and collective growth.
