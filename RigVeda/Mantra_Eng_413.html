याति देवः प्रवता यात्य उद्वता याति शुभ्राभ्यां यजतो हरिभ्यां |आ देवो याति सविता परावतः 'प वि विश्वा दुरिता बाधमानः ||
yāti devaḥ pravatā yāty udvatā yāti śubhrābhyāṁ yajato haribhyām | ā devo yāti savitā parāvato 'pa viśvā duritā bādhamānaḥ ||
  • yāti devaḥ pravatā: This phrase can be translated as "the divine beings move forward." It signifies the movement of celestial or divine forces in the universe. In real life, this can be likened to the way natural phenomena, such as the changing of seasons or the cycle of day and night, reflect the divine order of the universe.
  • yāty udvatā: This means "they ascend." It suggests a rise or elevation, which can be interpreted as the pursuit of higher knowledge or spiritual growth. For example, individuals who strive for personal development and enlightenment can be seen as ascending in their life journey.
  • yāti śubhrābhyāṁ yajato haribhyām: This translates to "they move towards the auspicious, worshipping the divine." It emphasizes the importance of worship and devotion in achieving a positive and prosperous life. In practical terms, this can be observed in various cultures where rituals and prayers are performed to seek blessings for health, wealth, and happiness.
  • ā devo yāti savitā parāvato: This phrase means "the divine sun moves towards the highest." It symbolizes the pursuit of ultimate truth and enlightenment. In real life, this can be related to the quest for knowledge and understanding, as individuals seek to illuminate their minds and lives with wisdom.
  • 'pa viśvā duritā bādhamānaḥ: This translates to "may all obstacles be removed." It expresses a desire for the removal of difficulties and challenges. In everyday life, this can be seen in the prayers and intentions set by individuals facing hardships, hoping for guidance and support to overcome their struggles.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of divine guidance, the pursuit of higher knowledge, and the importance of worship in overcoming life's challenges. Its significance lies in the reminder that by aligning ourselves with divine forces and seeking spiritual growth, we can navigate through obstacles and achieve a fulfilling life. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire individuals to engage in practices such as meditation, prayer, and self-reflection, fostering a deeper connection with the universe and promoting personal development.
