न्य अस्मै देवी स्वधितिः जिहीत इन्द्राय गातुर उषतीव येमे |सं यद ओजो युवते विश्वम् आभिर अनु स्वधाव्ने क्षितयो नमन्त ||
ny asmai devī svadhitir jihīta indrāya gātur uśatīva yeme | saṁ yad ojo yuvate viśvam ābhir anu svadhāvne kṣitayo namanta ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ny - to him
    • asmai - to that one
    • devī - goddess
    • svadhitir - self-rule or self-control
    • jihīta - may it be favorable
    • indrāya - to Indra (the king of gods)
    • gātur - of the song or hymn
    • uśatīva - like a powerful force
    • yeme - those who
    • saṁ - together
    • yad - whatever
    • ojo - energy or strength
    • yuvate - youthful or vigorous
    • viśvam - the whole universe
    • ābhir - with all
    • anu - following
    • svadhāvne - in self-governance
    • kṣitayo - of the earth
    • namanta - bow down or pay homage
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the goddess of self-control and self-rule, asking for her blessings to Indra, the king of gods.
    • It emphasizes the importance of strength and energy in achieving harmony in the universe.
    • The phrase "like a powerful force" suggests that the goddess's influence is vital for overcoming challenges.
    • It encourages individuals to recognize and honor the interconnectedness of all beings and the earth.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In personal development, practicing self-control can lead to better decision-making and success in various aspects of life, such as career and relationships.
    • In community building, recognizing the strength of collective efforts can lead to more effective teamwork and collaboration.
    • In environmental conservation, honoring the earth and its resources can inspire sustainable practices that benefit all living beings.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of self-governance, strength, and respect for the universe.
    • It is applicable in real-life scenarios where individuals and communities strive for harmony, balance, and sustainability.
    • By invoking the goddess of self-control, we are reminded of the power of our choices and the importance of honoring our interconnectedness with the world around us.
