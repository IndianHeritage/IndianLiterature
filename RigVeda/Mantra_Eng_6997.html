अजैष्माद्यासनाम चाभूमानागसो वयम् |उषो यस्माद दुष्ष्वप्न्याद अभैष्माप तद उच्छत्व अनहसो व उतयः सुतयः व उतयः ||
ajaiṣmādyāsanāma cābhūmānāgaso vayam | uṣo yasmād duṣṣvapnyād abhaiṣmāpa tad ucchatv anehaso va ūtayaḥ suūtayo va ūtayaḥ ||
  • Mantra: "ajaiṣmādyāsanāma cābhūmānāgaso vayam | uṣo yasmād duṣṣvapnyād abhaiṣmāpa tad ucchatv anehaso va ūtayaḥ suūtayo va ūtayaḥ ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ajaiṣmā: We have not been defeated
    • dyāsanāma: by the power of the heavens
    • cābhūmānāgaso: we are not bound by earthly limitations
    • vayam: we
    • uṣo: of the dawn
    • yasmād: from which
    • duṣṣvapnyād: from bad dreams
    • abhaiṣmāpa: we are free
    • tad: that
    • ucchatv: may rise
    • anehaso: of the powerful
    • va: or
    • ūtayaḥ: the rising
    • suūtayo: the well-risen
    • va: or
    • ūtayaḥ: the rising
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra speaks to the resilience and strength of the human spirit.
    • It emphasizes that we are not defeated by external circumstances, particularly those that are beyond our control, such as the challenges posed by the material world.
    • The reference to "the dawn" symbolizes new beginnings and hope, suggesting that from darkness (bad dreams) we can emerge into light (freedom and strength).
    • In real life, this can be likened to overcoming personal struggles, such as mental health challenges or difficult life situations. For example, someone who has faced adversity, such as losing a job, can find strength in this mantra to rise again and seek new opportunities.
    • The mantra encourages individuals to seek liberation from negative thoughts and experiences, much like how one might practice mindfulness or meditation to clear the mind and focus on positive outcomes.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its powerful message of resilience and hope.
    • It serves as a reminder that we have the strength to overcome challenges and that new beginnings are always possible.
    • In practical terms, this mantra can be applied in various life scenarios, such as during times of stress, anxiety, or uncertainty, encouraging individuals to rise above their circumstances and embrace the dawn of new possibilities.
    • Ultimately, it teaches us that we are not bound by our past or our fears, but rather we have the power to shape our future.
