महाम् इन्द्रो नृवद आ चर्षणिप्रात उता द्विभराः अमिनः सहोभिः |अस्मद्र्यग वावृधे वीर्यायोऽरुḥ पृथुः सुक्रतः कर्तृभिर भूत्व् ||
mahām̐ indro nṛvad ā carṣaṇiprā uta dvibarhā aminaḥ sahobhiḥ | asmadryag vāvṛdhe vīryāyoruḥ pṛthuḥ sukṛtaḥ kartṛbhir bhūt ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • mahām̐: Great or mighty
    • indro: Indra, the king of gods
    • nṛvad: In the form of a man
    • ā: To
    • carṣaṇiprā: The one who is invoked or called upon
    • uta: And
    • dvibarhā: With two wings or two powers
    • aminaḥ: With strength or might
    • sahobhiḥ: Together with companions
    • asmadryag: Our strength or power
    • vāvṛdhe: May it increase or grow
    • vīryāyoruḥ: The strength of the powerful
    • pṛthuḥ: Vast or great
    • sukṛtaḥ: Good deeds or actions
    • kartṛbhir: By the doers or creators
    • bhūt: Beings or entities
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the mighty Indra, who is often associated with strength, power, and leadership.
    • It emphasizes the importance of collective strength and the role of companions in achieving greatness.
    • The phrase "may it increase our strength" suggests a desire for growth and enhancement of one's capabilities.
    • It highlights the significance of good deeds and actions in the pursuit of strength and success.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, a team working together can achieve greater results than individuals working in isolation. The mantra reflects the importance of collaboration.
    • In sports, a team that supports each other and works towards a common goal exemplifies the essence of this mantra, showcasing how collective effort leads to victory.
    • In personal development, individuals who engage in community service or support groups often find their strengths amplified through shared experiences and good deeds.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of invoking strength and power through collective effort and good actions.
    • It serves as a reminder that true strength is not just individual but is enhanced through collaboration and shared goals.
    • In real life, whether in personal relationships, professional environments, or community engagements, the principles of this mantra can guide individuals towards achieving greater success and fulfillment.
