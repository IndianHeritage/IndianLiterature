दुहीयन् मित्रधितये युवाकु राये च नो मिमीतं वाजवत्यम् |इषे च नो मिमीतं धेनुमत्यै ||
duhīyan mitradhitaye yuvāku rāye ca no mimītaṁ vājavatyai | iṣe ca no mimītaṁ dhenumatyai ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • duhīyan: To be milked or to extract nourishment.
    • mitradhitaye: For the sake of friendship or for the benefit of allies.
    • yuvāku: Young or youthful.
    • rāye: Wealth or prosperity.
    • ca: And.
    • no: Our.
    • mimītaṁ: May it be fulfilled or may it be realized.
    • vājavatyai: For the one who is rich in resources or strength.
    • iṣe: For the purpose or for the aim.
    • dhenumatyai: For the one who is rich in cows or wealth.
  • Overall Meaning:
    • This mantra expresses a prayer for the nourishment and prosperity that comes from friendship and alliances.
    • It emphasizes the importance of youth and vitality in achieving wealth and success.
    • The invocation of strength and resources suggests a desire for abundance and support from allies.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be related to forming partnerships that enhance mutual growth and prosperity.
    • In personal relationships, it highlights the significance of nurturing friendships that provide emotional and material support.
    • In community settings, it reflects the idea of collective strength and resources leading to shared success.
  • Conclusion:
    • The mantra signifies the importance of collaboration and support in achieving prosperity.
    • It is applicable in various aspects of life, including personal relationships, business partnerships, and community building.
    • By fostering strong connections and working together, individuals and groups can achieve greater success and abundance.
