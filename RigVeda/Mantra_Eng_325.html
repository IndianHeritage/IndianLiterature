ससन्तु त्यारातयो बोधन्तु शूर रातयः |आ तु न इन्द्र शंसय गोष्व अश्वेषु शुभ्रिषु सहस्रेṣu तुवीमघ ||
sasantu tyā arātayo bodhantu śūra rātayaḥ | ā tū na indra śaṁsaya goṣv aśveṣu śubhriṣu sahasreṣu tuvīmagha ||
  • Translation: "May the enemies be subdued, may the brave ones be awakened. O Indra, do not hold back; grant us a thousand blessings in our cattle and horses."
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • Sasantu: May they be subdued
    • Tyā: By that
    • Arātayo: Enemies
    • Bodhantu: May they be awakened
    • Śūra: Brave
    • Rātayaḥ: Warriors
    • Ā: O
    • Tū: You
    • Na: Not
    • Indra: The deity, often associated with strength and power
    • Śaṁsaya: Hold back
    • Goṣv: In our cattle
    • Aśveṣu: In our horses
    • Śubhriṣu: In auspicious things
    • Sahasreṣu: A thousand
    • Tuvīmagha: Grant us blessings
  • Contextual Significance:
    • This mantra is a prayer for strength and prosperity, invoking the deity Indra to bestow blessings upon the community.
    • It emphasizes the importance of overcoming adversaries and awakening the courage within individuals, particularly in times of conflict or challenge.
    • The mention of cattle and horses symbolizes wealth and resources, which were vital for sustenance and status in ancient times.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a competitive business environment, this mantra can be interpreted as a call to overcome challenges and competitors, encouraging teams to harness their inner strength and work collaboratively.
    • In personal development, individuals may use this mantra as a motivational tool to face fears and obstacles, awakening their inner bravery to pursue goals.
    • In community settings, it can serve as a rallying cry for collective action against social injustices, promoting unity and strength among members.
  • Conclusion:
    • The mantra encapsulates the essence of resilience, courage, and the pursuit of prosperity.
    • Its application in modern life is relevant in various contexts, from personal growth to community empowerment.
    • By invoking the blessings of strength and abundance, individuals and communities can strive to overcome challenges and achieve their aspirations.
