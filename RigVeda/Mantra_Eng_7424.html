वयं इद वः सुदानवः क्षियन्तो यान्तो अध्वन्ना |देवाः वृधाय हूमहे ||
vayam id vaḥ sudānavaḥ kṣiyanto yānto adhvann ā | devā vṛdhāya hūmahe ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • vayam: We
    • id: This
    • vaḥ: Your
    • sudānavaḥ: Good and noble
    • kṣiyanto: Diminishing or reducing
    • yānto: Going or moving
    • adhvann: On the path
    • ā: To
    • devā: The gods
    • vṛdhāya: For growth or increase
    • hūmahe: We invoke or call upon
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra expresses a collective aspiration for growth and prosperity.
    • It emphasizes the importance of unity and collaboration in achieving noble goals.
    • The invocation of the gods signifies a plea for divine support in our endeavors.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, a team may come together to work on a project, invoking the spirit of collaboration to achieve success.
    • In community service, individuals may unite to address social issues, calling upon higher ideals to guide their efforts.
    • In personal development, one may seek the support of mentors or spiritual guides to foster growth and overcome challenges.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of collective effort and divine guidance in the pursuit of noble objectives.
    • It is applicable in various aspects of life, including personal growth, teamwork, and community initiatives.
    • By invoking the support of the divine, individuals and groups can enhance their potential and work towards a greater purpose.
