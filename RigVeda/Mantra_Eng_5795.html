युवामिद्युत्सु पृतनासु वह्नयो युवां क्षेमस्य प्रसवे मितज्ञवः |ईशानां वस्व उभयस्य कारव इन्द्रावरुणा सुहवा हवामहे ||
yuvām id yutsu pṛtanāsu vahnayo yuvāṁ kṣemasya prasave mitajñavaḥ | īśānā vasva ubhayasya kārava indrāvaruṇā suhavā havāmahe ||
  • yuvām id yutsu pṛtanāsu vahnayo: This phrase can be translated as "You two, in the battles of the flames." Here, "yuvām" refers to the two entities or forces, and "vahnayo" signifies the flames or fire, which can symbolize challenges or trials in life.
  • yuvāṁ kṣemasya prasave mitajñavaḥ: This translates to "You two, who are the creators of safety and well-being." The term "kṣemasya" refers to safety or well-being, indicating that these forces are responsible for providing security and prosperity.
  • īśānā vasva ubhayasya kārava: This means "You are the lords of all, the creators of both." "īśānā" refers to the rulers or lords, while "ubhayasya" indicates the duality of existence, suggesting that these forces govern both the material and spiritual realms.
  • indrāvaruṇā suhavā havāmahe: This can be interpreted as "We invoke you, Indra and Varuna, who are pleasing." "Indra" and "Varuna" are significant deities in Vedic literature, representing strength and cosmic order, respectively. "Suhavā" implies that they are benevolent and favorable.

Conclusion:

This mantra emphasizes the duality of existence and the importance of invoking divine forces for safety, well-being, and prosperity. In real life, it can be applied in various scenarios such as:

  • In times of personal or communal challenges, individuals can recite this mantra to seek strength and guidance from higher powers.
  • In rituals or ceremonies aimed at promoting peace and harmony, this mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of all beings and the need for balance.
  • In the context of leadership, it highlights the responsibility of leaders to create a safe and prosperous environment for their communities.

Overall, the mantra serves as a powerful invocation for support and guidance in navigating the complexities of life, encouraging individuals to seek harmony and strength from the divine.
