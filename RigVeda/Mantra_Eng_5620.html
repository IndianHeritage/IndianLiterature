बृहद् वयो मगवद्भ्यो दधातु जुजोषन्न इन मरुतः सुष्टुतिं नः |गतो नाध्वा वि तिराति जन्तुम प्र णः स्पार्हाभिर ऊतिभिस तिरेत ||
bṛhad vayo maghavadbhyo dadhāta jujoṣann in marutaḥ suṣṭutiṁ naḥ | gato nādhvā vi tirāti jantum pra ṇaḥ spārhābhir ūtibhis tireta ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • bṛhad: great, vast
    • vayo: age, life
    • maghavadbhyo: to the powerful, the mighty
    • dadhāta: may grant, bestow
    • jujoṣann: while being nourished, sustained
    • in: in, within
    • marutaḥ: of the winds, storm gods
    • suṣṭutiṁ: good praise, commendation
    • naḥ: to us
    • gato: having gone, traveled
    • nādhvā: not on the path
    • vi: through, across
    • tirāti: crosses, overcomes
    • jantum: beings, creatures
    • pra ṇaḥ: our breath, life force
    • spārhābhir: with desires, aspirations
    • ūtibhis: with blessings, abundance
    • tireta: may cross, may overcome
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the great powers of the universe, particularly the forces of nature represented by the Maruts, the storm gods.
    • It emphasizes the importance of being nourished and sustained by these divine forces, suggesting that life and vitality come from a connection to the greater cosmos.
    • The phrase "gato nādhvā vi tirāti jantum" indicates that even when one is not on the right path, the divine can help one overcome obstacles and challenges in life.
    • The mantra also highlights the significance of desires and aspirations, suggesting that with the right blessings, one can achieve their goals and navigate through life's difficulties.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, when facing challenges at work, one might seek guidance and support from mentors or colleagues, akin to invoking the blessings of the Maruts for overcoming obstacles.
    • In personal life, during times of uncertainty or hardship, individuals often turn to spiritual practices or community support to find strength and direction, reflecting the essence of this mantra.
    • In health and wellness, the idea of being nourished can relate to seeking a balanced lifestyle, where physical, mental, and spiritual well-being are interconnected, much like the sustenance provided by divine forces.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of life and the universe, encouraging individuals to seek support from higher powers and to recognize the importance of aspirations and desires.
    • Its significance lies in the understanding that even when faced with challenges, one can find a way to overcome them through faith, support, and the right intentions.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire individuals to cultivate resilience, seek guidance, and remain hopeful in their pursuits, ultimately leading to personal growth and fulfillment.
