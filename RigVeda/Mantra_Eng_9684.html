पूर्वापरं चरतो माययैतौ शिशू क्रीळन्तौ परि यातो अध्वरम् |विश्वान्य अन्यो भुवनाभिचष्ट ऋतूम् अन्यो विदधज जायते पुनः ||
pūrvāparaṁ carato māyayaitau śiśū krīḻantau pari yāto adhvaram | viśvāny anyo bhuvanābhicaṣṭa ṛtūm̐r anyo vidadhaj jāyate punaḥ ||
  • pūrvāparaṁ carato: This phrase can be translated as "the one who moves in the past and the future." It signifies the cyclical nature of time and existence, where past experiences shape future actions.
  • māyayaitau: This refers to the illusion or the play of the universe. It suggests that life is a manifestation of divine play, where reality may not be as it seems.
  • śiśū krīḻantau: Translated as "the children are playing," this evokes the innocence and joy of childhood, symbolizing purity and the untainted nature of existence.
  • pari yāto adhvaram: This can be interpreted as "they move around the sacred ritual." It highlights the importance of rituals in life, which serve as a means to connect with the divine and the cosmos.
  • viśvāny anyo bhuvanābhicaṣṭa: This phrase means "the one who sees all worlds." It emphasizes the interconnectedness of all beings and the universe, suggesting a broader perspective on existence.
  • ṛtūm̐r anyo vidadhaj: This translates to "the one who creates the seasons." It signifies the cyclical nature of life and the importance of change and adaptation in our lives.
  • jāyate punaḥ: Meaning "is born again," this phrase reflects the concept of rebirth and renewal, indicating that life is a continuous cycle of creation and destruction.

  • Significance: This mantra encapsulates the essence of life as a cycle of experiences, emphasizing the importance of understanding our past to navigate our future. It highlights the playfulness of existence, the significance of rituals, and the interconnectedness of all beings.
  • Real-life Application: In our daily lives, we can apply the teachings of this mantra by reflecting on our past experiences to make informed decisions for the future. Engaging in rituals, whether cultural or personal, can help us connect with our roots and the universe. Embracing change and recognizing the cyclical nature of life can lead to personal growth and resilience.
