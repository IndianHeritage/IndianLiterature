भुवो जनस्य दिव्यस्य राजा पार्थिवस्य जगतस् त्वेषसंदृक् |धिष्व वज्रं दक्षिण इन्द्र हस्ते विश्वा अजुर्य दयसे वि मायाः ||
bhuvo janasya divyasya rājā pārthivasya jagatas tveṣasaṁdṛk | dhiṣva vajraṁ dakṣiṇa indra haste viśvā ajurya dayase vi māyāḥ ||
  • Mantra: "bhuvo janasya divyasya rājā pārthivasya jagatas tveṣasaṁdṛk | dhiṣva vajraṁ dakṣiṇa indra haste viśvā ajurya dayase vi māyāḥ ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • bhuvo: of the earth
    • janasya: of the people
    • divyasya: divine
    • rājā: king
    • pārthivasya: of the earthly
    • jagatas: of the world
    • tveṣasaṁdṛk: with your glance
    • dhiṣva: may you hear
    • vajraṁ: the thunderbolt
    • dakṣiṇa: right-hand
    • indra: Indra, the king of gods
    • haste: in your hand
    • viśvā: all
    • ajurya: strength
    • dayase: grant
    • vi: indeed
    • māyāḥ: illusions
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra invokes the divine king, Indra, who is often associated with strength, power, and protection.
    • The phrase "bhuvo janasya divyasya" emphasizes the connection between the divine and the earthly, suggesting that the welfare of the people is intertwined with divine grace.
    • "Dhiṣva vajraṁ dakṣiṇa indra haste" calls upon Indra to wield his thunderbolt, symbolizing the dispelling of negativity and obstacles in life.
    • The mantra seeks strength ("ajurya") and the ability to overcome illusions ("māyāḥ"), which can be interpreted as the challenges and distractions of everyday life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In leadership, a ruler or manager can embody the qualities of Indra by making decisions that benefit the community, ensuring that their actions are guided by wisdom and strength.
    • In personal life, individuals can invoke this mantra during challenging times, seeking clarity and strength to overcome obstacles, much like how one might turn to a mentor or a source of inspiration.
    • In a community setting, this mantra can be recited during gatherings or ceremonies to invoke collective strength and unity, reminding everyone of their shared purpose and the divine support available to them.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of divine support in achieving strength and overcoming life's challenges.
    • It serves as a reminder that the welfare of the community is essential and that leaders should act with wisdom and strength.
    • In real-life scenarios, it can be applied in personal development, leadership, and community building, encouraging individuals to seek divine guidance and strength in their endeavors.
