यद अद्याश्विनाव अहं हुवेयं वाजसातये |यत पृथ्सु तुर्वणे सहस तच श्रेष्टम अश्विनोर अवः ||
yad adyāśvināv ahaṁ huveya vājasātaye | yat pṛtsu turvaṇe sahas tac chreṣṭham aśvinor avaḥ ||
  • Mantra: "yad adyāśvināv ahaṁ huveya vājasātaye | yat pṛtsu turvaṇe sahas tac chreṣṭham aśvinor avaḥ ||"
  • Translation: "Whatever I invoke today for the Ashvins, for the sake of strength; that which is most excellent, may it come to me swiftly from the Ashvins."

  • Explanation of Key Terms:
    • Ashvins: The Ashvins are twin deities in Hindu mythology, often associated with health, medicine, and the dawn. They symbolize the duality of life and the balance between opposites.
    • Huveya: This term refers to the act of invoking or calling upon the divine. It signifies a plea for assistance or blessings.
    • Vājasātaye: This word relates to strength or vigor, indicating a desire for empowerment and vitality.
    • Pṛtsu: This term can be understood as 'in the world' or 'in the realm of existence,' emphasizing the practical application of the invocation.
    • Turvaṇe: This refers to the swift arrival or manifestation of what is being invoked, highlighting the urgency and importance of the request.
    • Shrestham: Meaning 'the best' or 'the most excellent,' it indicates the quality of what is being sought.

  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a modern context, this mantra can be likened to someone seeking help from a mentor or a coach. Just as the speaker calls upon the Ashvins for strength, individuals often reach out to their support systems for guidance and empowerment in challenging situations.
    • In the realm of health and wellness, people may invoke the Ashvins' blessings when embarking on a new fitness journey, hoping for strength and resilience to achieve their goals.
    • In a corporate setting, a team may invoke the spirit of collaboration and excellence when launching a new project, seeking the best outcomes and swift success.

  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine assistance in achieving strength and excellence. It serves as a reminder that invoking higher powers or seeking support from others can lead to positive outcomes in various aspects of life. Whether in personal endeavors, professional projects, or health pursuits, the essence of this mantra encourages individuals to seek help and strive for the best, reinforcing the belief that we are not alone in our journeys. The Ashvins symbolize the balance of dualities, reminding us that strength can come from both within and from external sources, and that invoking such energies can lead to swift and favorable results.
