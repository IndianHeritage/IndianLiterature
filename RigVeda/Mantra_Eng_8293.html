उभाभ्यां देव सवितः पवित्रेण सवेना च |माम पुनिहि विश्वतः ||
ubhābhyāṁ deva savitaḥ pavitreṇa savena ca | mām punīhi viśvataḥ ||
  • Ubhābhyāṁ: This word translates to "from both" or "from the two." It signifies duality and the importance of balance in life.
  • Deva: This means "God" or "divine." It refers to the higher powers or deities that govern the universe.
  • Savitaḥ: This term refers to "the Sun" or "the solar deity." The Sun is often seen as a source of life, energy, and illumination.
  • Pavitreṇa: This means "with purity" or "pure." It emphasizes the importance of purity in thoughts, actions, and intentions.
  • Savena: This translates to "with the essence" or "with the energy." It indicates the vital force that sustains life.
  • Mām: This means "me" or "myself." It indicates a personal plea or invocation.
  • Punīhi: This translates to "purify" or "cleanse." It is a request for spiritual or physical purification.
  • Viśvataḥ: This means "from all sides" or "in all directions." It signifies a holistic approach to life and spirituality.

The mantra can be interpreted as a prayer to the divine forces, particularly the Sun, to purify the individual from all sides, emphasizing the need for both spiritual and physical cleanliness. It reflects the belief that divine energy can cleanse and rejuvenate the soul.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios:

  • Personal Growth: Just as the Sun provides light and energy, individuals can seek enlightenment and growth through self-reflection and purification of thoughts and actions.
  • Health and Wellness: The emphasis on purity can be related to maintaining a healthy lifestyle, both physically and mentally. This includes clean eating, mindfulness, and emotional well-being.
  • Spiritual Practices: In meditation or yoga, practitioners often seek to purify their minds and bodies, aligning with the essence of this mantra.

Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for holistic purification and balance in life. It serves as a reminder to seek divine guidance and maintain purity in all aspects of existence. By invoking this mantra, individuals can strive for a harmonious life, enriched by the divine energy of the universe.
