उषः प्रतिची भुवनानि विश्वोर्ध्वा तिष्ठस्य अमृतस्य केतुः | समानम् अर्थं चरणी्यमाना चक्रम् इव नव्यस्य आ ववृत्स्व ||
uṣaḥ pratīcī bhuvanāni viśvordhvā tiṣṭhasy amṛtasya ketuḥ | samānam arthaṁ caraṇīyamānā cakram iva navyasy ā vavṛtsva ||
  • uṣaḥ pratīcī: This phrase refers to the dawn or the arrival of light. In a metaphorical sense, it symbolizes new beginnings and opportunities. Just as dawn brings light to the world, it represents hope and the potential for growth.
  • bhuvanāni viśvordhvā: This translates to "the worlds above and below." It signifies the interconnectedness of all realms of existence, both physical and spiritual. In real life, this can be seen in how our actions impact not just ourselves but also the community and environment around us.
  • tiṣṭhasy amṛtasya ketuḥ: This means "you stand as the banner of immortality." It suggests a pursuit of eternal truths and values. In practical terms, this can relate to striving for lasting legacies, such as contributions to society or the environment that endure beyond our lifetimes.
  • samānam arthaṁ: This phrase means "the same purpose." It emphasizes unity and collective goals. In everyday life, this can be observed in teamwork, where individuals come together for a common objective, such as in a workplace or community project.
  • caraṇīyamānā: This translates to "moving forward." It signifies progress and the journey of life. In real life, this can be seen in personal development, where individuals strive to improve themselves and their circumstances.
  • cakram iva navyasy ā vavṛtsva: This means "like a wheel that turns." It symbolizes the cyclical nature of life and the continuous flow of time and experiences. In practical terms, this can be related to the seasons of life, where each phase brings its own challenges and opportunities.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of life, emphasizing the importance of new beginnings, interconnectedness, the pursuit of lasting values, unity in purpose, progress, and the cyclical nature of existence. In real-life scenarios, it encourages individuals to embrace change, work collaboratively towards common goals, and recognize the impact of their actions on the broader world. This mantra serves as a reminder to live with intention and awareness, fostering a sense of responsibility towards oneself and the community.
