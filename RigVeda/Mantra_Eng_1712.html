ईर्मान्तासः सिलिकमध्यमासः सं शूरणासो दिव्यासो अत्याः |हंसा इव श्रेणीशो यतन्ते यद आक्षिषुर दिव्यम अज्मम अश्वाः ||
īrmāntāsaḥ silikamadhyamāsaḥ saṁ śūraṇāso divyāso atyāḥ | haṁsā iva śreṇiśo yatante yad ākṣiṣur divyam ajmam aśvāḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • īrmāntāsaḥ: Those who are strong or powerful.
    • silikamadhyamāsaḥ: Those who are in the middle of the path or the essence.
    • saṁ: Together or in unity.
    • śūraṇāso: The brave or the valiant.
    • divyāso: The divine or celestial beings.
    • atyāḥ: Beyond or transcendent.
    • haṁsā: Like swans, symbolizing purity and wisdom.
    • iva: Like or as.
    • śreṇiśo: The leaders or guides.
    • yatante: Striving or making efforts.
    • yad: That which.
    • ākṣiṣur: With eyes or vision.
    • divyam: Divine or heavenly.
    • ajmam: The essence or the core.
    • aśvāḥ: Horses, often symbolizing strength and speed.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of powerful beings who are united in their strength and bravery.
    • It emphasizes the importance of striving together towards a divine goal, much like a team working towards a common objective.
    • The imagery of swans suggests purity and wisdom, indicating that those who strive together should also embody these qualities.
    • The mention of horses symbolizes the energy and speed required to achieve one's goals, highlighting the need for both strength and agility in our endeavors.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, a team that works collaboratively, leveraging each member's strengths, can achieve remarkable results, akin to the powerful beings described in the mantra.
    • In sports, a team that trains together, supports each other, and strives for a common victory embodies the essence of this mantra.
    • In community service, individuals coming together to address social issues reflect the unity and bravery mentioned, striving for a divine purpose of betterment.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the power of unity, strength, and divine purpose in our lives.
    • It teaches us that by working together with wisdom and purity of intention, we can transcend challenges and achieve greatness.
    • In real-life scenarios, whether in personal relationships, professional environments, or community efforts, the principles outlined in this mantra can guide us towards success and fulfillment.
