सं ते पयांसी समु यन्तु वाजाः सं वृष्ण्याञ्य अभिमातिषाहः |आप्यायमानो अमृताय सोम दिवि श्रवांस्य उत्तमानि धिष्व ||
saṁ te payāṁsi sam u yantu vājāḥ saṁ vṛṣṇyāny abhimātiṣāhaḥ | āpyāyamāno amṛtāya soma divi śravāṁsy uttamāni dhiṣva ||
  • saṁ te payāṁsi sam u yantu vājāḥ: This phrase can be translated to "May your nourishing waters flow together, and may the steeds (vājāḥ) be united." Here, "payāṁsi" refers to nourishing waters, symbolizing abundance and sustenance. In real life, this can be likened to the importance of collaboration and unity in achieving common goals, much like how rivers converge to create a larger body of water.
  • saṁ vṛṣṇyāny abhimātiṣāhaḥ: This translates to "May the powerful (vṛṣṇyā) be endowed with strength and valor." The term "vṛṣṇyā" refers to strength and valor, which can be seen in leaders or individuals who inspire others through their actions. For example, a community leader who rallies people for a cause demonstrates this strength, encouraging collective action for the betterment of society.
  • āpyāyamāno amṛtāya soma: This means "May the soma, which grants immortality (amṛtāya), be made available." Soma is often associated with divine nectar or elixir, symbolizing spiritual nourishment and enlightenment. In a real-life context, this can be related to the pursuit of knowledge and wisdom, which can lead to personal growth and a lasting impact on others.
  • divi śravāṁsy uttamāni dhiṣva: This translates to "In the heavens, may the best glories (uttamāni) be attained." This phrase emphasizes the aspiration for higher achievements and recognition. In practical terms, this can be seen in the pursuit of excellence in various fields, such as science, art, or social service, where individuals strive to leave a positive legacy.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of unity, strength, spiritual nourishment, and the pursuit of excellence. Its significance lies in the reminder that collaboration and shared goals can lead to greater achievements, while the quest for knowledge and virtue can elevate individuals and communities. In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as teamwork in professional settings, community service initiatives, and personal development journeys, encouraging individuals to work together towards a common purpose while striving for greatness.
