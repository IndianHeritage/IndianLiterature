ऋभुर न इन्द्रः शवसां नवीयान ऋभुर वाजेभिर वसुभिर वसुर ददिः |युष्माकं देवाः अवसाहनि प्रियेऽभि तिष्ठेम पृत्सुतीर असुन्वताम् ||
ṛbhur na indraḥ śavasā navīyān ṛbhur vājebhir vasubhir vasur dadiḥ | yuṣmākaṁ devā avasāhani priye3 'bhi tiṣṭhema pṛtsutīr asunvatām ||
  • ṛbhur na indraḥ: This phrase can be translated as "O Indra, the one who is rich in resources." Indra is often regarded as the king of the gods in Vedic literature, symbolizing strength and power. In real life, this can be likened to leaders or individuals who possess abundant resources and capabilities to support their communities.
  • śavasā navīyān: This means "newly born with strength." It signifies the idea of renewal and the potential for growth. In a practical sense, this can relate to new beginnings in life, such as starting a new job, moving to a new city, or embarking on a new project that requires vigor and enthusiasm.
  • ṛbhur vājebhir vasubhir vasur dadiḥ: This translates to "bestow upon us wealth and prosperity." Here, "vājebhir" refers to wealth or resources, while "vasubhir" indicates abundance. In everyday life, this can be seen in the pursuit of financial stability, career success, or the desire for a prosperous family life.
  • yuṣmākaṁ devā avasāhani: This means "may the gods protect you." It emphasizes the importance of divine protection and guidance in our lives. In real life, this can be reflected in the support we seek from our community, family, or even spiritual beliefs during challenging times.
  • priye3 'bhi tiṣṭhema: This translates to "may we stand together in love." It highlights the significance of unity and cooperation among individuals. In practical terms, this can be observed in teamwork, friendships, and family bonds that help us overcome obstacles together.
  • pṛtsutīr asunvatām: This means "may we be blessed with longevity and health." It underscores the desire for a long, healthy life. In real life, this can relate to the importance of maintaining physical and mental well-being through healthy habits, regular exercise, and a balanced diet.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a profound message of seeking strength, prosperity, protection, unity, and health. Its significance lies in the holistic approach to life, encouraging individuals to strive for personal growth while fostering connections with others. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as during personal challenges, community building, or even in corporate settings where teamwork and collaboration are essential for success. By embodying the principles outlined in this mantra, individuals can work towards a fulfilling and prosperous life, supported by the strength of their relationships and the abundance of resources around them.
