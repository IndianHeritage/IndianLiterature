अषाढो अग्ने वृषभो दिदीहि पुरो विश्वाः सौभागा संजीवन् |यज्ञस्य नेता प्रथमस्य पायोर जातवेदो बृहतः सुप्रणीते ||
aṣāḻho agne vṛṣabho didīhi puro viśvāḥ saubhagā saṁjigīvān | yajñasya netā prathamasya pāyor jātavedo bṛhataḥ supraṇīte ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • aṣāḻho: Unwavering, steadfast
    • agne: O Agni (the fire god)
    • vṛṣabho: The bull, symbolizing strength and power
    • didīhi: Grant, bestow
    • puro: In the front, in the beginning
    • viśvāḥ: All, everything
    • saubhagā: Auspicious, good fortune
    • saṁjigīvān: Living together, united
    • yajñasya: Of the sacrifice
    • netā: Leader, guide
    • prathamasya: First, foremost
    • pāyor: Of the waters
    • jātavedo: O Jātavedas (the one who knows all births)
    • bṛhataḥ: Great, vast
    • supraṇīte: Well-directed, well-ordered
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes Agni, the fire god, who is seen as a powerful and steadfast force.
    • Agni is often associated with the sacrificial fire, which is central to Vedic rituals.
    • The phrase "vṛṣabho didīhi" emphasizes the strength and power of Agni, asking him to bestow auspiciousness and good fortune.
    • It highlights the importance of unity and collective living ("saṁjigīvān") in achieving success and prosperity.
    • The mantra also acknowledges Agni as the leader of sacrifices, guiding the devotees towards their goals.
    • By referring to "jātavedo," it recognizes Agni's omniscience and ability to understand all aspects of existence.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern life, this mantra can be related to the importance of teamwork and collaboration in achieving common goals, such as in a workplace or community project.
    • The invocation of Agni can be seen in various cultural rituals where fire is used to symbolize purification and the offering of intentions to a higher power.
    • In personal development, the qualities of steadfastness and strength can be applied to overcoming challenges and pursuing one's aspirations.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of invoking divine guidance and strength in our endeavors.
    • It teaches us the value of unity and collective effort in achieving success and prosperity.
    • In real-life scenarios, it encourages individuals to seek support from others and to remain steadfast in their pursuits, while also recognizing the greater forces at play in their lives.
