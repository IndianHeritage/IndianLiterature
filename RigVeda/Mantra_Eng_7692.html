मन्त्र: रक्षोहा विश्वचर्षणिर अभि योनिम् अयोहतम् |द्रुणा सदस्थम् आसदत ||
rakṣohā viśvacarṣaṇir abhi yonim ayohatam | druṇā sadhastham āsadat ||
  • Mantra: "rakṣohā viśvacarṣaṇir abhi yonim ayohatam | druṇā sadhastham āsadat ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • rakṣohā: of the demons or negative forces
    • viśvacarṣaṇir: the one who moves everywhere, often referring to the universal energy or consciousness
    • abhi: towards or in the direction of
    • yonim: the source or womb, often symbolizing creation or origin
    • ayohatam: the most powerful or supreme
    • druṇā: with strength or firmness
    • sadhastham: seated in the assembly or gathering
    • āsadat: may they sit or may they be present
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the protective energies against negative forces (rakṣohā) that may disrupt harmony.
    • The term "viśvacarṣaṇir" suggests a universal presence, indicating that the energy we seek is omnipresent and can be accessed from anywhere.
    • By directing our intentions "abhi yonim," we are calling upon the source of creation, emphasizing the importance of aligning with our origins to manifest positive outcomes.
    • The phrase "ayohatam" highlights the supreme power of this energy, reminding us that we are tapping into a force greater than ourselves.
    • "Druṇā sadhastham" signifies the need for strength and unity in our endeavors, suggesting that collective efforts in a supportive environment can lead to success.
    • Finally, "āsadat" implies a call for this energy to be present in our lives, reinforcing the idea that we must invite and welcome positive influences.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace setting, this mantra can be applied when teams face challenges. By invoking collective strength and focusing on a common goal, teams can overcome obstacles and foster a supportive environment.
    • In personal life, individuals can use this mantra during meditation or prayer to seek protection from negative influences, encouraging a mindset of positivity and resilience.
    • In community gatherings, the essence of this mantra can be reflected in the way people come together to support one another, creating a safe space for growth and collaboration.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the importance of seeking protection from negative forces while embracing the universal energy that surrounds us.
    • Its significance lies in the call for unity, strength, and the welcoming of positive influences in our lives.
    • By applying the teachings of this mantra in real-life scenarios, individuals and communities can cultivate resilience, harmony, and a supportive atmosphere conducive to growth and success.
