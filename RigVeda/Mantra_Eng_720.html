सनााद एव तव रायो गभस्तौ न क्षीयन्ते नोप दस्यन्ति दसम | द्यमाम् असी क्रतुमाम् इन्द्र धीरः शिक्षां शचीवास तव नः शचीभिः ||
sanād eva tava rāyo gabhastau na kṣīyante nopa dasyanti dasma | dyumām̐ asi kratumām̐ indra dhīraḥ śikṣā śacīvas tava naḥ śacībhiḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sanād: From the beginning, or from the ancient times.
    • eva: Indeed, or certainly.
    • tava: Your.
    • rāyo: Wealth or prosperity.
    • gabhastau: Radiance or brilliance.
    • na: Not.
    • kṣīyante: Diminish or decrease.
    • nopa: Nor.
    • dasyanti: Harm or trouble.
    • dasma: From this place or from here.
    • dyumām̐: Shining or luminous.
    • asi: You are.
    • kratumām̐: Of strong will or determination.
    • indra: Indra, the king of gods.
    • dhīraḥ: Wise or courageous.
    • śikṣā: Instruction or teaching.
    • śacīvas: Of the powerful or strong.
    • tava: Your.
    • naḥ: Our.
    • śacībhiḥ: With strength or power.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the eternal nature of prosperity and brilliance that comes from the divine.
    • It suggests that true wealth and radiance do not diminish or cause harm, but rather, they are everlasting gifts from the divine.
    • The invocation of Indra signifies the importance of strength, wisdom, and guidance in achieving success and prosperity.
    • The mantra calls for divine instruction and support to help individuals harness their inner strength and wisdom.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be applied to the pursuit of ethical practices that lead to sustainable success, where the wealth generated does not harm others.
    • In personal development, individuals can reflect on their inner strengths and seek guidance from mentors or spiritual leaders to achieve their goals.
    • In community building, the mantra can inspire collective efforts to create a supportive environment where everyone thrives without diminishing others' well-being.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of seeking divine guidance and wisdom in our pursuits.
    • It highlights that true prosperity is not just about material wealth but also about the strength and brilliance that comes from within.
    • By embodying the principles of this mantra, individuals and communities can work towards a harmonious existence where everyone benefits and flourishes.
