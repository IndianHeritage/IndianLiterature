वार्ण त्वा यव्याभिर वर्धन्ति शूर ब्रह्माणि |वावृद्ध्वांसं चिद अद्रिवो दिवे-दिवे ||
vār ṇa tvā yavyābhir vardhanti śūra brahmāṇi | vāvṛdhvāṁsaṁ cid adrivo dive-dive ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • vār ṇa: O Lord, or O Divine Being
    • tvā: You
    • yavyābhir: By the means of the strong
    • vardhanti: They increase or enhance
    • śūra: The brave or the valiant
    • brahmāṇi: In the realm of the divine or the ultimate reality
    • vāvṛdhvāṁsaṁ: May we be endowed with strength
    • cid: Consciousness or awareness
    • adrivo: Like a mountain or immovable
    • dive-dive: Day by day or continuously
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the divine presence, asking for strength and enhancement through the means of bravery and valor.
    • It emphasizes the importance of courage in achieving spiritual and material growth.
    • The reference to "brahmāṇi" suggests a connection to the ultimate reality, indicating that true strength comes from a higher source.
    • The phrase "day by day" signifies the continuous nature of this growth and the need for persistent effort.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, an individual may face challenges that require bravery and resilience. By embodying the qualities of "śūra," they can overcome obstacles and achieve their goals.
    • In personal development, one may seek to enhance their skills and knowledge continuously. The mantra serves as a reminder to pursue growth with courage and determination.
    • In community service, individuals who act bravely to help others embody the essence of this mantra, contributing to the greater good and fostering a sense of unity.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of courage and divine support in the journey of life.
    • It teaches us that true strength is not just physical but also mental and spiritual, derived from a connection to the divine.
    • In real-life scenarios, applying the principles of this mantra can lead to personal growth, resilience in the face of challenges, and a commitment to helping others.
    • Ultimately, it encourages us to seek continuous improvement and to face each day with bravery and awareness.
