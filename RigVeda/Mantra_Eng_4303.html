महें नो अद्य बोधयोषो राये दिवित्मती |यथा चिन् नो अबोधयः सत्यश्रवसि वाय्ये सुजाते अश्वसूत्रे ||
mahe no adya bodhayoṣo rāye divitmatī | yathā cin no abodhayaḥ satyaśravasi vāyye sujāte aśvasūnṛte ||
  • mahe no adya bodhayoṣo rāye divitmatī: This phrase can be translated to "May we today awaken to great glory and wealth." Here, "mahe" signifies greatness, "no" means "our," "adya" translates to "today," "bodhayoṣo" refers to "awakening to glory," and "rāye divitmatī" means "wealth that is prosperous." This part of the mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking and recognizing greatness and prosperity in our lives.
  • yathā cin no abodhayaḥ: This translates to "As if we are not awakened." The term "yathā" means "as," "cin" translates to "if," and "no abodhayaḥ" refers to "we are not awakened." This suggests a state of unawareness or ignorance that we must overcome to achieve our goals.
  • satyaśravasi vāyye sujāte aśvasūnṛte: This can be interpreted as "In truth, may we be born in auspiciousness and righteousness." "Satyaśravasi" means "in truth," "vāyye" translates to "may we be," "sujāte" refers to "born in auspiciousness," and "aśvasūnṛte" means "in righteousness." This part of the mantra highlights the significance of truth and righteousness in our lives, suggesting that these qualities lead to a prosperous and fulfilling existence.

  • Significance: This mantra emphasizes the importance of awakening to greatness and prosperity while being rooted in truth and righteousness. It serves as a reminder to strive for personal and collective growth, urging individuals to recognize their potential and work towards achieving it.
  • Real-life Application: In practical terms, this mantra can be applied in various aspects of life, such as:
    • Personal Development: Individuals can use this mantra as a daily affirmation to inspire themselves to pursue their goals and aspirations with a sense of purpose and integrity.
    • Community Building: In a community context, this mantra can encourage collective efforts towards achieving common goals, fostering an environment of support and collaboration.
    • Business Ethics: In the business world, the principles of truth and righteousness can guide ethical decision-making, leading to sustainable success and a positive impact on society.

  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the values of awareness, truth, and righteousness. By embodying these principles, individuals and communities can work towards a prosperous and fulfilling life, ultimately contributing to the greater good of society.
