तिस्रो देवीर बर्हिर इदं वरिय आ सीदत चक्रमा वः स्योनम् |मनुष्वद यज्ञं सुदिता हवīmṣīḻा देवī घृतपदी जुषन्त ||
tisro devīr barhir idaṁ varīya ā sīdata cakṛmā vaḥ syonam | manuṣvad yajñaṁ sudhitā havīṁṣīḻā devī ghṛtapadī juṣanta ||
  • tisro devīr: This phrase translates to "three goddesses." In the context of the Rigveda, these goddesses often represent various aspects of life and nature, such as prosperity, knowledge, and strength.
  • barhir idaṁ varīya: This means "this offering is worthy." It emphasizes the importance of the offerings made to the deities, suggesting that they should be of high quality and significance.
  • ā sīdata: This translates to "may they sit down." It indicates a call for the goddesses to accept the offerings and be present during the ritual.
  • cakṛmā vaḥ syonam: This means "we create for you." It signifies the act of creation and dedication of the offerings to the deities, highlighting the relationship between humans and the divine.
  • manuṣvad yajñaṁ: This translates to "the human sacrifice." It refers to the rituals performed by humans to honor the gods, emphasizing the importance of human participation in spiritual practices.
  • sudhitā havīṁṣīḻā: This means "well-prepared offerings." It stresses the need for careful preparation and intention behind the offerings made to the deities.
  • devī ghṛtapadī juṣanta: This translates to "the goddesses who enjoy the clarified butter." Clarified butter (ghee) is a significant offering in Vedic rituals, symbolizing purity and nourishment.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda highlights the significance of offerings and rituals in connecting with the divine. It emphasizes the importance of intention, quality, and participation in spiritual practices. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied to various aspects of life, such as the importance of dedication and preparation in any endeavor, whether it be in personal goals, community service, or professional projects. By recognizing the divine in our efforts and ensuring that we approach our tasks with sincerity and care, we can create a meaningful impact in our lives and the lives of others.
