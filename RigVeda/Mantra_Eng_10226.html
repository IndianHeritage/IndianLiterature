सां नो अद्य यस्यां वयं न ते याम्न्न अविक्ष्महि |वृक्षे न वसतिं वयः ||
sā no adya yasyā vayaṁ ni te yāmann avikṣmahi | vṛkṣe na vasatiṁ vayaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sā: that
    • no: our
    • adya: today
    • yasyā: whose
    • vayaṁ: we
    • ni: not
    • te: you
    • yāmann: who
    • avikṣmahi: we do not see
    • vṛkṣe: in the tree
    • na: not
    • vasatiṁ: dwelling
    • vayaḥ: our life
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra expresses a plea for divine guidance and protection.
    • It emphasizes the importance of community and collective well-being.
    • The reference to "tree" symbolizes stability, growth, and shelter, suggesting that our lives should be rooted in strong values and support systems.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace, this mantra can be applied to foster teamwork and collaboration, ensuring that everyone feels supported and valued.
    • In a family setting, it highlights the importance of nurturing relationships and creating a safe environment for all members.
    • In environmental contexts, the tree symbolizes the need for sustainable practices that protect our natural resources for future generations.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for unity and the recognition of our interconnectedness.
    • It serves as a reminder to seek support from one another and to create spaces where everyone can thrive.
    • In real life, applying the principles of this mantra can lead to stronger communities, healthier relationships, and a more sustainable world.
