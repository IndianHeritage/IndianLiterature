के ते नर इन्द्र ये त इषे ये ते सुम्नं सधन्यं इयक्षान् |के ते वाजायासूर्याय हिन्विरे के अप्सु स्वासूरवरासु पौंस्ये ||
ke te nara indra ye ta iṣe ye te sumnaṁ sadhanya1m iyakṣān | ke te vājāyāsuryāya hinvire ke apsu svāsūrvarāsu pauṁsye ||
  • Mantra: "ke te nara indra ye ta iṣe ye te sumnaṁ sadhanya1m iyakṣān | ke te vājāyāsuryāya hinvire ke apsu svāsūrvarāsu pauṁsye ||"
  • Translation: This mantra can be interpreted as a call to the divine, seeking blessings and strength. It addresses Indra, the king of gods, and inquires about the nature of those who are powerful and prosperous.
  • Word Breakdown:
    • ke te: Who are you?
    • nara: Men or humans.
    • indra: Indra, the king of gods, symbolizing strength and power.
    • ye ta: Those who.
    • iṣe: Are capable or powerful.
    • sumnaṁ: Auspiciousness or well-being.
    • sadhanya: Prosperity or success.
    • iyakṣān: Those who are strong or mighty.
    • vājāyāsuryāya: To the one who is like the sun, representing brilliance and vitality.
    • hinvire: Who brings forth or bestows.
    • apsu: In the waters, symbolizing purity and life.
    • svāsūrvarāsu: In the best of the waters or the most excellent.
    • pauṁsye: In strength or vigor.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • This mantra can be recited in times of uncertainty or when seeking guidance in decision-making, much like how individuals consult mentors or leaders for wisdom.
    • In a corporate setting, leaders may invoke the spirit of Indra to inspire strength and unity among their teams, fostering a culture of collaboration and success.
    • During personal challenges, individuals may reflect on the qualities of strength and prosperity, using this mantra as a reminder to seek auspiciousness in their endeavors.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking divine guidance and strength in our lives.
    • It serves as a reminder that we can call upon higher powers to assist us in achieving our goals and overcoming obstacles.
    • In real-life scenarios, whether in personal growth, professional challenges, or community building, invoking the essence of this mantra can inspire individuals to strive for prosperity and well-being.
