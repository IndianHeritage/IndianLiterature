मम देवाः विहवे सन्तु सर्व इन्द्रवन्तो मरुतः विष्णुः अग्निः |ममान्तरिक्षं उरुलोकं अस्तु मह्यं वातः पवतां कामे अस्मिन् ||
mama devā vihave santu sarva indravanto maruto viṣṇur agniḥ | mamāntarikṣam urulokam astu mahyaṁ vātaḥ pavatāṁ kāme asmin ||
  • Translation: "May all the gods be pleased with me; may the Indras, the Maruts, Vishnu, and Agni be favorable to me. May the space above and the vast worlds be mine; may the wind blow towards my desires in this life."
  • Breakdown of Key Terms:
    • mama devā: "my gods" - This signifies a personal connection with the divine, indicating that the speaker seeks the blessings of the deities.
    • vihave santu: "may they be pleased" - A request for the gods' favor, highlighting the importance of divine approval in one's endeavors.
    • sarva indravanto: "all Indras" - Refers to the various manifestations of power and authority, suggesting a desire for strength and leadership.
    • maruto: "the Maruts" - The storm gods, representing the forces of nature, indicating a wish for their support in overcoming challenges.
    • viṣṇur agniḥ: "Vishnu and Agni" - Vishnu symbolizes preservation and protection, while Agni represents fire and transformation, both essential for life and progress.
    • mamāntarikṣam urulokam: "the space above and the vast worlds" - A desire for cosmic connection and abundance, reflecting aspirations for a fulfilling life.
    • mahyaṁ vātaḥ pavatāṁ: "may the wind blow towards my desires" - A metaphor for the forces of nature aligning with one's intentions, emphasizing the importance of harmony with the environment.
    • kāme asmin: "in this life" - A reminder that these aspirations are grounded in the present, focusing on the here and now.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In personal endeavors, such as starting a new business, one might recite this mantra to invoke the support of divine forces, seeking guidance and favor from the gods.
    • During significant life events, like weddings or new beginnings, individuals may chant this mantra to ensure that their journey is blessed and that they receive the strength and support needed to succeed.
    • In times of challenge, such as facing health issues or personal struggles, this mantra serves as a reminder to seek divine assistance and to align one's desires with the greater forces of nature.
  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a profound desire for divine favor and support in all aspects of life. It emphasizes the interconnectedness of the individual with the cosmos and the importance of seeking blessings from higher powers. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied during personal challenges, significant life events, or when embarking on new ventures, serving as a reminder to align one's intentions with the greater forces of nature and the divine. By invoking the blessings of the gods, individuals can cultivate a sense of purpose and direction in their lives, fostering resilience and positivity in their pursuits.
