मां जस्वने वृषभ नो ररीथा मां ते रेवतः सख्ये रिषाम |पूर्वीष टा इन्द्र निष्सिधो जनेषु जह्य असुष्वीन प्र वृहापृणतः ||
mā jasvane vṛṣabha no rarīthā mā te revataḥ sakhye riṣāma | pūrvīṣ ṭa indra niṣṣidho janeṣu jahy asuṣvīn pra vṛhāpṛṇataḥ ||
  • mā jasvane vṛṣabha no rarīthā: This phrase can be interpreted as a plea for strength and resilience. The term "jasvane" refers to vigor or vitality, while "vṛṣabha" signifies a strong bull, symbolizing power and robustness. In real life, this can relate to the importance of maintaining physical and mental strength in the face of challenges.
  • mā te revataḥ sakhye riṣāma: Here, "revataḥ" refers to prosperity or wealth, and "sakhye" means friendship or companionship. This line emphasizes the desire for harmonious relationships that foster mutual support and prosperity. In everyday life, this can be seen in the importance of building strong networks and friendships that contribute to personal and professional growth.
  • pūrvīṣ ṭa indra niṣṣidho janeṣu: "Indra" is often associated with leadership and authority, while "niṣṣidho" means to be restrained or held back. This line suggests a call for the removal of obstacles that hinder progress. In practical terms, this can relate to overcoming barriers in one’s career or personal life, such as societal norms or personal fears that prevent one from achieving their goals.
  • jahy asuṣvīn pra vṛhāpṛṇataḥ: "Asuṣvīn" refers to health and well-being, while "vṛhāpṛṇataḥ" means to be filled with abundance. This line highlights the importance of health as a foundation for a prosperous life. In real life, this can be exemplified by the need to prioritize physical and mental health to ensure a fulfilling and successful life.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a holistic approach to life, emphasizing the significance of strength, supportive relationships, overcoming obstacles, and prioritizing health. In real-life scenarios, these elements are crucial for personal development and success. By embodying the principles outlined in this mantra, individuals can navigate challenges more effectively, build meaningful connections, and ultimately lead a more prosperous and fulfilling life.
