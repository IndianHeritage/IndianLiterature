ऋजीपी श्येनो ददमानो अंशुम परावतः शकुनो मन्द्रं मदम् |सोमं भरद दादृहानो देवावान दिवो अमुष्माद उत्तराद आदाय ||
ṛjīpī śyeno dadamāno aṁśum parāvataḥ śakuno mandram madam | somam bharad dādṛhāṇo devāvān divo amuṣmād uttarād ādāya ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ṛjīpī: One who is righteous or virtuous.
    • śyeno: The golden eagle, symbolizing strength and power.
    • dadamāno: Bestowing or giving.
    • aṁśum: Light or radiance.
    • parāvataḥ: From the heights or the heavens.
    • śakuno: The bird, often representing freedom and transcendence.
    • mandram: Gentle or soothing.
    • madam: Joy or bliss.
    • somam: The moon or soma, often associated with nourishment and immortality.
    • bharad: Carrying or bringing forth.
    • dādṛhāṇo: Firm or steadfast.
    • devāvān: Divine or godly.
    • divo: Of the sky or heaven.
    • amuṣmād: From that place or source.
    • uttarād: From above or higher realms.
    • ādāya: Taking or receiving.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the divine qualities of righteousness and strength, symbolized by the golden eagle.
    • The act of bestowing light and radiance signifies the importance of knowledge and enlightenment in our lives.
    • The reference to the soothing and joyful aspects of life suggests that true happiness comes from divine blessings and a connection to the higher realms.
    • The mention of the moon (soma) indicates nourishment, both physical and spiritual, which is essential for growth and well-being.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, a leader who embodies righteousness and strength can inspire their team, much like the golden eagle soaring high.
    • In personal relationships, bestowing light and joy can create a nurturing environment, fostering love and understanding.
    • Practicing mindfulness and seeking divine guidance can help individuals navigate challenges, drawing strength from higher realms.
    • Engaging in community service can be seen as a way to carry forth the blessings of nourishment and joy to those in need.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of righteousness, strength, and divine blessings in our lives.
    • It encourages individuals to seek enlightenment and joy, fostering a connection with the divine.
    • In real-life scenarios, applying the principles of this mantra can lead to personal growth, harmonious relationships, and a positive impact on society.
    • Ultimately, it serves as a reminder to embrace the virtues of strength and compassion, guiding us towards a fulfilling and meaningful life.
