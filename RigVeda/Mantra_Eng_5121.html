तīv्रान घोषान कृत्न्वते वृषपाणयोऽश्वा रथेभिः सह वाजयन्तः |अवक्रामन्तः प्रपदैर अमित्रान क्षिणन्ति शत्रूम् अनपव्ययन्तः ||
tīvrān ghoṣān kṛṇvate vṛṣapāṇayo 'śvā rathebhiḥ saha vājayantaḥ | avakrāmantaḥ prapadair amitrān kṣiṇanti śatrūm̐r anapavyayantaḥ ||
  • tīvrān ghoṣān kṛṇvate: This phrase translates to "they create intense sounds." Here, "tīvrān" means intense or powerful, and "ghoṣān" refers to sounds or noises. In real life, this can be likened to the sounds made during a sports event or a concert, where the energy and excitement create a powerful atmosphere.
  • vṛṣapāṇayo 'śvā rathebhiḥ saha vājayantaḥ: This part means "the horses, along with the charioteers, are making their way." "Vṛṣapāṇayo" refers to the charioteers or those who control the horses, while "śvā rathebhiḥ" indicates the horses and chariots. This can be compared to a racing event where horses and their riders work in harmony to achieve victory.
  • avakrāmantaḥ prapadair amitrān: This translates to "they advance with their steps against the enemies." "Avakrāmantaḥ" means to advance or move forward, and "amitrān" refers to enemies. In a real-life context, this can be seen in competitive scenarios, such as business or sports, where one must strategically advance against competitors.
  • kṣiṇanti śatrūm̐r anapavyayantaḥ: This means "they diminish the enemies without any loss." "Kṣiṇanti" refers to diminishing or reducing, and "anapavyayantaḥ" means without loss. This can be related to overcoming challenges in life, such as financial difficulties or personal struggles, where one finds ways to reduce problems without incurring further losses.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the themes of strength, teamwork, and strategic advancement against challenges. It signifies the importance of collaboration and determination in overcoming obstacles, whether in competitive sports, business, or personal endeavors. In real life, this mantra can inspire individuals to work together, harness their strengths, and face adversities with confidence and resilience, ultimately leading to success and victory.
