मन्त्र: न त्वां अग्ने मनुर दधे ज्योतिर्जनाय शाश्वते |दीदेथा कण्व ऋतजात उक्षितो यं नमस्यन्ति कृष्टयः ||
ni tvām agne manur dadhe jyotir janāya śaśvate | dīdetha kaṇva ṛtajāta ukṣito yaṁ namasyanti kṛṣṭayaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ni: to you
    • tvām: you
    • agne: O Agni (the fire god)
    • manur: the mind or thought
    • dadhe: I place or establish
    • jyotir: light or brilliance
    • janāya: for the people or for the community
    • śaśvate: eternal or everlasting
    • dīdetha: may you shine or illuminate
    • kaṇva: O Kaṇva (a sage or seer)
    • ṛtajāta: born of truth or righteousness
    • ukṣito: having been nourished or stimulated
    • yaṁ: whom
    • namasyanti: they honor or worship
    • kṛṣṭayaḥ: the cultivators or those who work the land
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra addresses Agni, the fire god, symbolizing the divine light and energy that sustains life.
    • The speaker expresses a desire to establish their thoughts and intentions in alignment with Agni's eternal brilliance for the benefit of the community.
    • The invocation of Kaṇva, a sage, emphasizes the importance of wisdom and truth in guiding one's actions.
    • The reference to cultivators highlights the connection between spiritual practices and the physical labor that sustains society.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern society, this mantra can be related to the importance of community service and collective well-being. For instance, community gardens not only provide food but also foster a sense of unity among neighbors.
    • In personal development, individuals can reflect on their thoughts and intentions, ensuring they align with their values and contribute positively to their surroundings.
    • In professional settings, leaders can embody the principles of Agni by illuminating the path for their teams, encouraging collaboration, and nurturing a culture of respect and honor.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of aligning one's thoughts and actions with higher principles for the benefit of the community.
    • It serves as a reminder to honor the wisdom of the past while nurturing the present, ensuring that our efforts contribute to a sustainable and harmonious future.
    • In essence, it encourages individuals to be mindful of their impact on others and to cultivate a spirit of cooperation and respect in all endeavors.
