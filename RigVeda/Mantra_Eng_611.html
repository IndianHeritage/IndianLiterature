आ स्मा रथं वृषपाणेषु तिष्ठसि शार्यतस्य प्रभरिता येषु मंदसे |इन्द्र यथा सुतसोमेṣu चाकनो 'नर्वाणं श्लोकम आ रोहसे दिवि ||
ā smā rathaṁ vṛṣapāṇeṣu tiṣṭhasi śāryātasya prabhṛtā yeṣu mandase | indra yathā sutasomeṣu cākano 'narvāṇaṁ ślokam ā rohase divi ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā smā: From us
    • rathaṁ: Chariot
    • vṛṣapāṇeṣu: In the hands of the strong
    • tiṣṭhasi: You stand
    • śāryātasya: Of the one who is powerful
    • prabhṛtā: Those who are endowed
    • yeṣu: In whom
    • mandase: In the wise
    • indra: Indra (the king of gods)
    • yathā: As
    • sutasomeṣu: In the sons of the moon
    • cākano: The one who is strong
    • 'narvāṇaṁ: The ultimate goal
    • ślokam: Verse
    • ā rohase: You ascend
    • divi: In the heavens
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the strength and power of Indra, the king of gods, who is often associated with thunder, rain, and war.
    • The chariot symbolizes the journey of life, and standing in the hands of the strong signifies the support of powerful forces in our endeavors.
    • The reference to the wise indicates the importance of wisdom and intelligence in achieving success.
    • Indra's connection to the sons of the moon suggests a lineage of strength and nobility, emphasizing the idea that greatness can be inherited or cultivated.
    • The ultimate goal ('narvāṇaṁ') signifies the aspiration for spiritual enlightenment and liberation.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a competitive environment, such as a corporate setting, individuals often rely on their mentors (the strong) to guide them through challenges, much like the chariot being steered by a capable driver.
    • In personal development, wisdom plays a crucial role; individuals who seek knowledge and understanding are more likely to achieve their goals, akin to the wise in the mantra.
    • In spiritual practices, the pursuit of enlightenment can be seen as a journey where one aspires to ascend to higher states of consciousness, similar to the ascent to the heavens mentioned in the mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of strength, wisdom, and the pursuit of higher goals in life.
    • It serves as a reminder that we are supported by powerful forces and that wisdom is essential in navigating our journeys.
    • In real-life scenarios, whether in personal growth, professional endeavors, or spiritual quests, the principles embodied in this mantra can guide individuals toward success and fulfillment.
