रथीतमं कपर्दिनं ईशानं राधसो महः |रायः सखायं इमहे ||
rathītamaṁ kapardinam īśānaṁ rādhaso mahaḥ | rāyaḥ sakhāyam īmahe ||
  • rathītamaṁ: This term can be broken down into "rathi," meaning charioteer or one who drives a chariot, and "tama," which signifies the best or the highest. Thus, "rathītamaṁ" refers to the supreme charioteer, symbolizing guidance and leadership.
  • kapardinam: This word is often associated with Lord Shiva, who is depicted with matted hair (kaparda). It signifies the divine aspect of creation and destruction, representing the balance of life.
  • īśānaṁ: This term means the lord or the ruler. It denotes a higher power or authority, often referring to a divine being or a spiritual guide.
  • rādhaso: This word can be interpreted as "of Radha," who is often seen as the divine feminine energy and a symbol of love and devotion in Hindu mythology.
  • mahaḥ: This means great or mighty, emphasizing the power and significance of the subject being referred to.
  • rāyaḥ: This term translates to wealth or prosperity, indicating abundance and success in various aspects of life.
  • sakhāyam: This means friend or companion, highlighting the importance of relationships and support systems in life.
  • īmahe: This is a call to invoke or meditate upon, suggesting a desire to connect with the divine or higher consciousness.

The mantra can be interpreted as a prayer or invocation to the supreme charioteer, who is also a divine ruler, to bless us with prosperity and companionship, drawing upon the energies of love and devotion represented by Radha. It emphasizes the importance of guidance, divine support, and the nurturing of relationships in our pursuit of success and abundance.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios:

  • In leadership roles, one can seek guidance from the "supreme charioteer" to navigate challenges effectively.
  • In personal relationships, invoking the energy of Radha can enhance love, compassion, and understanding among friends and partners.
  • In times of seeking prosperity, this mantra serves as a reminder to cultivate supportive relationships that contribute to our success.

Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its holistic approach to life, emphasizing the interconnectedness of guidance, love, and prosperity. By meditating on these concepts, individuals can foster a balanced and fulfilling life, drawing upon divine energies to support their journey.
