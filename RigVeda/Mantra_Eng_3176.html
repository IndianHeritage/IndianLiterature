परी ते दूलभो रथोऽस्मां अशनतु विश्वतः |येन रक्षसि दाशुषः ||
pari te dūḻabho ratho 'smām̐ aśnotu viśvataḥ | yena rakṣasi dāśuṣaḥ ||
  • Mantra: "pari te dūḻabho ratho 'smām̐ aśnotu viśvataḥ | yena rakṣasi dāśuṣaḥ ||"
  • Translation: "May the chariot that is difficult to attain come to us from all sides; by which you protect us from all adversities."

  • Word Breakdown:
    • pari: around, from all sides
    • dūḻabho: difficult to attain, rare
    • ratho: chariot, symbolizing progress or movement
    • 'smām̐: to us, for us
    • aśnotu: may it come, may it arrive
    • viśvataḥ: from all directions, universally
    • yena: by which, through which
    • rakṣasi: protect, safeguard
    • dāśuṣaḥ: from adversities, troubles

  • Meaning and Explanation:
    • This mantra expresses a desire for a chariot that is hard to attain, symbolizing the pursuit of goals that may seem out of reach.
    • The chariot represents progress, success, and the journey of life, which can be filled with challenges.
    • The phrase "from all sides" indicates a holistic approach to receiving blessings and support from the universe.
    • The invocation for protection from adversities highlights the importance of safety and security in our endeavors.

  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional context, an individual may strive for a promotion that seems difficult to achieve. This mantra can serve as a reminder to seek support from colleagues and mentors, emphasizing the importance of teamwork.
    • In personal life, someone may face challenges in relationships or health. The mantra encourages seeking help from friends and family, reinforcing the idea that support is available from all directions.
    • In a broader societal context, communities facing adversity, such as natural disasters, can invoke this mantra to unite and seek collective strength and resources for recovery.

  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of striving for goals that may seem unattainable while recognizing the need for support and protection from challenges.
    • It teaches us to be open to receiving help from various sources and to remain resilient in the face of difficulties.
    • In real life, it can be applied in various scenarios, from personal aspirations to collective efforts in communities, reminding us that with determination and support, we can overcome obstacles.
