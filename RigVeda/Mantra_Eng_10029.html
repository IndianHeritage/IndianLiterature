पज्रेवा चरचरं जारम् मरायु क्षदमेवार्थेषु तर्तरीथा उग्रा |ऋभू नापत खरामज्रा खरज्रुर वायुर न परफरत क्षयद रयीणाम ||
pajreva carcaraṁ jāram marāyu kṣadmevārtheṣu tartarītha ugrā | ṛbhū nāpat kharamajrā kharajrur vāyur na parpharat kṣayad rayīṇām ||
  • pajreva carcaraṁ jāram: This phrase can be interpreted as a call to the forces of nature, emphasizing the importance of movement and vitality. In real life, this can relate to the idea of staying active and engaged in our surroundings, much like how a river flows continuously, symbolizing life and energy.
  • marāyu kṣadmevārtheṣu tartarītha ugrā: Here, "marāyu" refers to the life force or energy, while "kṣadmevārtheṣu" suggests the importance of purpose in our actions. This can be seen in the context of pursuing goals with determination. For example, athletes train rigorously to achieve their dreams, embodying the essence of this mantra.
  • ṛbhū nāpat kharamajrā: This part speaks to the idea of overcoming obstacles. "ṛbhū" can be understood as a call for strength and resilience. In everyday life, this can be likened to individuals facing challenges, such as students preparing for exams or professionals navigating career hurdles, demonstrating perseverance.
  • kharajrur vāyur na parpharat: This phrase highlights the significance of the wind, symbolizing change and adaptability. In real life, this can be related to how businesses must adapt to market changes or how individuals must adjust to new circumstances, much like how trees bend with the wind but do not break.
  • kṣayad rayīṇām: This concluding part refers to the idea of abundance and prosperity. It suggests that through resilience and adaptability, one can achieve success and wealth. For instance, entrepreneurs who innovate and adapt their strategies often find greater success in their ventures.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of life, emphasizing movement, purpose, resilience, adaptability, and the pursuit of abundance. Its significance lies in its applicability to various aspects of life, from personal growth to professional success. By embodying the principles outlined in this mantra, individuals can navigate challenges and strive for their goals, ultimately leading to a fulfilling and prosperous life.
