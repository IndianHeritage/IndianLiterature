तं त्वा हविष्मतीर विष उप ब्रुवत ūtaye |उरुज्रयसम इन्दुभिः ||
taṁ tvā haviṣmatīr viśa upa bruvata ūtaye | urujrayasam indubhiḥ ||
  • taṁ - This word translates to "that" or "him," referring to a divine entity or a higher power.
  • tvā - This means "you," indicating a direct address to the divine or the object of worship.
  • haviṣmatīr - This term can be broken down into "haviṣ" (offering) and "matīr" (intellect or thought), suggesting a connection between offerings and wisdom.
  • viśa - This word means "to all" or "universal," indicating inclusivity and the reach of the divine.
  • upa - This translates to "near" or "approaching," suggesting a closeness to the divine.
  • bruvata - This means "speaking" or "proclaiming," indicating the act of communication or invocation.
  • ūtaye - This term means "for the purpose of" or "for the benefit of," indicating intention behind the invocation.
  • urujrayasam - This can be interpreted as "great strength" or "powerful," referring to the might of the divine.
  • indubhiḥ - This word means "with the moon" or "by the moon," which can symbolize calmness, serenity, and the nurturing aspects of the divine.

The mantra can be understood as a prayer or invocation directed towards a divine entity, asking for wisdom and strength. It emphasizes the importance of offerings and the intention behind them, suggesting that true wisdom comes from a sincere heart and a desire to connect with the divine.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios:

  • Spiritual Practices: Individuals can recite this mantra during meditation or prayer to seek guidance and strength in their lives.
  • Community Gatherings: It can be used in communal rituals where people come together to offer their intentions and seek collective wisdom.
  • Personal Reflection: The mantra encourages individuals to reflect on their intentions and the offerings they make in their daily lives, whether it be in relationships, work, or personal growth.

Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for a deeper connection with the divine through sincere offerings and intentions. It serves as a reminder that wisdom and strength are accessible to all who seek them with an open heart. In a world filled with distractions, this mantra encourages individuals to pause, reflect, and approach the divine for guidance and support in their life journeys.
