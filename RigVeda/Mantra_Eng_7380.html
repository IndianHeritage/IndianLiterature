त्वामिद्यवयुरममकामो गव्युरहिरण्ययुः |त्वामश्वयुरेषते ||
tvām id yavayur mama kāmo gavyur hiraṇyayuḥ | tvām aśvayur eṣate ||
  • tvām - This word translates to "you" or "to you," indicating a direct address to a divine entity or a higher power.
  • id - This means "now," suggesting immediacy and relevance in the present moment.
  • yavayur - This can be interpreted as "the one who is nourishing" or "the one who provides sustenance," often associated with the earth or nature.
  • mama - This means "my," indicating a personal connection or possession.
  • kāmo - This translates to "desire" or "wish," reflecting human aspirations and needs.
  • gavyur - This word refers to "cattle" or "wealth," symbolizing prosperity and abundance.
  • hiraṇyayuḥ - This means "golden" or "wealthy," often associated with richness and prosperity.
  • aśvayur - This translates to "the one who is like a horse," symbolizing strength, speed, and vitality.
  • eṣate - This means "is present" or "is here," indicating the presence of the divine or the desired qualities.

The mantra can be understood as a prayer or invocation to a divine force, expressing a desire for nourishment, prosperity, and strength. It emphasizes the importance of connection to the divine in fulfilling one's needs and aspirations.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios:

  • Personal Aspirations: When individuals seek to achieve their goals, they can recite this mantra to invoke the support of a higher power, reminding themselves of their desires and the abundance they wish to attract.
  • Business and Wealth: Entrepreneurs and business owners can use this mantra to focus on their intentions for prosperity and success, aligning their efforts with the divine energy of abundance.
  • Health and Vitality: The reference to strength and nourishment can be a reminder to prioritize health and well-being, encouraging individuals to seek balance in their lives.

Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its ability to connect individuals with their desires and the divine source of abundance. By invoking these qualities, one can cultivate a mindset of prosperity, strength, and fulfillment in various aspects of life. It serves as a reminder that our aspirations are supported by a greater force, encouraging us to pursue our goals with confidence and faith.
