यā जामयो वृष्ण इच्छन्ति शक्तिं नमस्यन्तीर जानते गर्भम् अस्मिन् |अच्छा पुत्रं धेनवो वावशानां महश्चरन्ति बिभ्रतं वपूंषि ||
yā jāmayo vṛṣṇa icchanti śaktiṁ namasyantīr jānate garbham asmin | acchā putraṁ dhenavo vāvaśānā mahaś caranti bibhrataṁ vapūṁṣi ||
  • yā jāmayo vṛṣṇa icchanti: This phrase can be translated as "those who desire strength and prosperity." Here, "jāmayo" refers to the desires or aspirations, while "vṛṣṇa" signifies strength or prosperity. In real life, this can relate to individuals or communities striving for success, whether in personal goals, careers, or collective endeavors.
  • śaktiṁ namasyantīr: This means "who honor the power." "Śakti" refers to power or energy, and "namasyantīr" indicates respect or reverence. In practical terms, this can be seen in how people respect and acknowledge the forces that drive their achievements, such as hard work, education, or community support.
  • jānate garbham asmin: This translates to "who know the essence within." "Garbham" refers to the essence or the core of something, while "asmin" means "in this." This can be interpreted as understanding one's inner potential or the fundamental truths of life. For example, self-awareness and personal growth are crucial in realizing one's capabilities and purpose.
  • acchā putraṁ dhenavo vāvaśānā: This phrase means "may we have good offspring, like cows." "Acchā" signifies good or auspicious, "putraṁ" refers to offspring, and "dhenavo" relates to cows, which are often seen as symbols of wealth and nourishment in many cultures. This can be applied to the desire for nurturing future generations who are healthy, prosperous, and contribute positively to society.
  • mahaś caranti bibhrataṁ vapūṁṣi: This translates to "the great ones who carry the forms." "Mahaś" refers to greatness, "caranti" means to move or act, and "vapūṁṣi" signifies forms or bodies. This can be understood as the influential individuals or leaders who embody and promote values, ethics, and strength in their actions. In real life, this can be seen in leaders who inspire others through their deeds and character.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of strength, respect for power, self-awareness, the nurturing of future generations, and the role of great leaders in society. Its significance lies in the holistic approach to life, encouraging individuals to strive for personal and communal prosperity while honoring the forces that contribute to their success. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as personal development, community building, and leadership, guiding individuals and societies toward a prosperous and harmonious existence.
