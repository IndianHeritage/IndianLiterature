आ नो गहि सख्येभिः शिवेभिर महान महीभिर ऊतिभिः सरण्यन |अस्मे रयिम बहुलं संतरुत्रम सुवाचं भागं यशसं कृधī नः ||
ā no gahi sakhyebhiḥ śivebhir mahān mahībhir ūtibhiḥ saraṇyan | asme rayim bahulaṁ saṁtarutraṁ suvācam bhāgaṁ yaśasaṁ kṛdhī naḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā no: May we be guided
    • gahi: by the friends
    • sakhyebhiḥ: who are united
    • śivebhir: with auspiciousness
    • mahān: great
    • mahībhir: with the vastness
    • ūtibhiḥ: with abundance
    • saraṇyan: as a refuge
    • asme: for us
    • rayim: wealth
    • bahulaṁ: plentiful
    • saṁtarutraṁ: crossing over obstacles
    • suvācam: with good speech
    • bhāgaṁ: fortune
    • yaśasaṁ: fame
    • kṛdhī: may we attain
    • naḥ: for us
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra expresses a desire for guidance and support from friends who are united in auspiciousness.
    • It emphasizes the importance of community and collaboration in achieving greatness and abundance.
    • The plea for wealth and fortune reflects a universal human aspiration for prosperity and success.
    • The mention of "crossing over obstacles" signifies resilience and the ability to overcome challenges with the help of supportive relationships.
    • Good speech and communication are highlighted as essential for fostering positive relationships and achieving one's goals.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace setting, teamwork and collaboration can lead to greater success. For instance, a project team that communicates effectively and supports each other is more likely to meet deadlines and achieve high-quality results.
    • In personal relationships, friends who uplift and encourage one another can help each other navigate life's challenges, whether it's through emotional support during tough times or celebrating each other's successes.
    • In community initiatives, collective efforts can lead to significant change. For example, a neighborhood coming together to improve local parks or organize events fosters a sense of belonging and shared purpose.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the power of unity, support, and communication in achieving personal and collective goals.
    • Its significance lies in the acknowledgment that we are stronger together, and that our aspirations for wealth, fortune, and success can be realized through collaboration and mutual support.
    • In real life, applying the principles of this mantra can lead to more fulfilling relationships, successful teamwork, and a greater sense of community, ultimately enriching our lives and the lives of those around us.
