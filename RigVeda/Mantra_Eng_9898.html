अरं कामाय हरयो दधन्विरे स्थिराय हिन्वन हरयो हरī तुरा |अर्वद्भिर यो हरिभिर जोषम् ईयते सो अस्य कामं हरिवन्तम् आनशे ||
araṁ kāmāya harayo dadhanvire sthirāya hinvan harayo harī turā | arvadbhir yo haribhir joṣam īyate so asya kāmaṁ harivantam ānaśe ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • araṁ: for the purpose
    • kāmāya: of desires
    • harayo: the divine beings (often associated with Vishnu)
    • dadhanvire: who wield the bow
    • sthirāya: for stability
    • hinvan: who strike down
    • harayo: the divine beings
    • harī: the divine or auspicious
    • turā: swiftly
    • arvadbhir: with great strength
    • yo: who
    • haribhir: with the divine beings
    • joṣam: the energy or vigor
    • īyate: is invoked
    • so: he
    • asya: of this
    • kāmaṁ: desire
    • harivantam: the one who fulfills desires
    • ānaśe: may he grant
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks to the divine beings, particularly those associated with Vishnu, who are invoked for the fulfillment of desires.
    • The phrase "for the purpose of desires" indicates that the divine is called upon to assist in achieving one's goals and aspirations.
    • The imagery of "wielding the bow" symbolizes strength and the ability to overcome obstacles, suggesting that divine support is crucial in the pursuit of stability and success.
    • The mantra emphasizes the importance of invoking divine energy and vigor, which can be seen in real-life scenarios such as seeking motivation in challenging times or during significant life changes.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, individuals may recite this mantra before a big presentation or project, seeking divine guidance and strength to achieve their goals.
    • During personal challenges, such as health issues or relationship struggles, people may turn to this mantra for emotional support and stability.
    • In times of uncertainty, such as starting a new venture or moving to a new place, invoking this mantra can provide a sense of reassurance and purpose.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its ability to connect individuals with divine energies that aid in the pursuit of desires and stability.
    • It serves as a reminder that seeking support from higher powers can empower individuals to overcome challenges and achieve their aspirations.
    • In everyday life, this mantra can be applicable in various situations, from personal growth to professional endeavors, reinforcing the belief that divine assistance is always available for those who seek it.
