ईशिषे वार्यस्य हि दात्रस्याग्ने स्वर्पतिः |स्तोता स्यां तव शर्मणि ||
īśiṣe vāryasya hi dātrasyāgne svarpatiḥ | stotā syāṁ tava śarmaṇi ||
  • īśiṣe: This word translates to "to the powerful" or "to the one who has authority." It signifies respect and acknowledgment of strength and capability.
  • vāryasya: This means "of the one who is worthy" or "of the one who is capable." It emphasizes the importance of recognizing those who have the ability to provide and protect.
  • hi: This is a particle that can be translated as "indeed" or "certainly," reinforcing the statement that follows.
  • dātrasyāgne: "dātrasyāgne" refers to "the giver" or "the one who bestows," specifically in the context of Agni, the fire god. Agni is often seen as a mediator between humans and the divine, representing the transformative power of fire.
  • svarpatiḥ: This term can be understood as "the one who is the lord of the heavens" or "the celestial being." It indicates a connection to higher realms and divine authority.
  • stotā: This means "the one who praises" or "the one who offers hymns." It highlights the act of devotion and recognition of the divine.
  • syāṁ: This is a form of the verb "to be," suggesting a state of existence or presence.
  • tava: This translates to "your," indicating possession or belonging, often used in the context of addressing the divine.
  • śarmaṇi: This word means "in protection" or "in refuge." It signifies safety, comfort, and the assurance of divine support.

The mantra can be interpreted as a prayer or invocation directed towards Agni, acknowledging his power and role as a giver and protector. It emphasizes the importance of recognizing and praising the divine forces that provide safety and support in our lives.


In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts:

  • Personal Growth: Just as Agni transforms offerings into energy, individuals can harness their inner strength and capabilities to overcome challenges and achieve personal goals.
  • Community Support: The mantra highlights the importance of recognizing and appreciating those in our communities who provide support and protection, such as leaders, mentors, and caregivers.
  • Spiritual Practice: Regular recitation of this mantra can serve as a reminder to seek refuge in the divine and to express gratitude for the blessings and protection received in life.

Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call to recognize and honor the divine forces that guide and protect us. By acknowledging the power of Agni and the importance of community and personal strength, we can cultivate a sense of gratitude and resilience in our lives. This mantra serves as a reminder to seek refuge in the divine and to appreciate the support we receive from both the spiritual and earthly realms.
