भराय सु भरत भागं ऋत्वियम प्र वायवे शुचिपे क्रन्ददिष्टये |गौरस्य यः पयसाḥ पीतिम आनश आ सर्वतातिम अदितिं वृणीमहे ||
bharāya su bharata bhāgam ṛtviyam pra vāyave śucipe krandadiṣṭaye | gaurasya yaḥ payasaḥ pītim ānaśa ā sarvatātim aditiṁ vṛṇīmahe ||
  • Translation: "We invoke the bountiful and nourishing essence of the Earth, the sacred offerings that flow like the pure waters of the sky. We seek the abundance of the cow's milk, which grants us sustenance and prosperity, and we call upon the infinite, the boundless, the nurturing force of the universe."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • bharāya: To nourish or sustain.
    • su: Good or auspicious.
    • bharata: The essence or wealth.
    • bhāgam: Portion or share.
    • ṛtviyam: Sacred or divine.
    • pra: Forth or forward.
    • vāyave: To the wind or air.
    • śucipe: Pure or clean.
    • krandadiṣṭaye: To the one who cries out or calls.
    • gaurasya: Of the cow or the divine.
    • payasaḥ: Of milk.
    • pītim: Nourishment or sustenance.
    • ānaśa: To consume or partake.
    • ā: From or out of.
    • sarvatātim: All-encompassing or infinite.
    • aditiṁ: The boundless or nurturing.
    • vṛṇīmahe: We choose or invoke.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • The mantra emphasizes the importance of nature and its resources, similar to how modern agriculture relies on the health of the soil and the purity of water to yield crops.
    • In a spiritual context, individuals may recite this mantra during rituals to invoke prosperity and abundance in their lives, reflecting the universal desire for sustenance and well-being.
    • In community gatherings, this mantra can be a reminder of the interconnectedness of all beings and the importance of sharing resources, akin to how communities come together to support one another in times of need.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its invocation of abundance and nourishment from both the Earth and the cosmos. It serves as a reminder of our dependence on nature and the divine for sustenance. In real-life scenarios, it encourages individuals and communities to appreciate and protect their resources, fostering a sense of gratitude and responsibility towards the environment. By recognizing the interconnectedness of all life, we can cultivate a more harmonious existence, ensuring that we not only seek our own prosperity but also contribute to the well-being of others and the planet.
