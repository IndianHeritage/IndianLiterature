चाक्लप्रे तेन ऋषयो मनुष्याः यज्ञे जाते पितरो नः पुराणे |पश्यन् मन्ये मनसा चक्षसा तान य इमं यज्ञं अयजन्त पूर्वे ||
cākḷpre tena ṛṣayo manuṣyā yajñe jāte pitaro naḥ purāṇe | paśyan manye manasā cakṣasā tān ya imaṁ yajñam ayajanta pūrve ||
  • cākḷpre tena: This phrase can be interpreted as "by that which is established" or "by that which is known." It suggests a connection to a higher knowledge or truth that guides human actions.
  • ṛṣayo manuṣyā: Refers to the "sages" or "wise men" among humans. This highlights the importance of wisdom and knowledge in human life.
  • yajñe jāte: This translates to "in the act of sacrifice" or "in the performance of rituals." It emphasizes the significance of rituals and sacrifices in connecting with the divine.
  • pitaro naḥ purāṇe: This means "our ancestors of old." It acknowledges the role of ancestors in guiding and influencing the present generation.
  • paśyan manye: This can be understood as "seeing with understanding." It implies a deeper perception and awareness of the world around us.
  • manasā cakṣasā: Translates to "with the mind's eye." This suggests that true understanding comes from inner insight rather than just external observation.
  • tān ya imaṁ yajñam ayajanta pūrve: This means "those who performed this sacrifice in the past." It reflects on the continuity of traditions and the importance of learning from those who came before us.

  • Significance: This mantra emphasizes the importance of wisdom, rituals, and the connection to our ancestors. It suggests that by understanding and performing rituals, we can connect with the divine and gain insights into our lives.
  • Real-life Application: In modern life, this mantra can be applied in various ways. For instance, individuals can engage in rituals that honor their ancestors, such as family gatherings during festivals or memorials. Additionally, seeking wisdom from elders and sages in the community can guide personal and collective decision-making.

  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of tradition, wisdom, and the interconnectedness of past and present. By acknowledging our roots and the knowledge passed down through generations, we can navigate our lives with greater awareness and purpose.
