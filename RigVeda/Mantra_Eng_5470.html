शं न इन्द्रो वसुभिर देवो अस्तु शम् आदित्येभिर वरुणः सुशंसः |शं नो रुद्रो रुद्रैः जलाशः शं नस् त्वष्टा ज्ञाभिर इह श्रिणोतु ||
śaṁ na indro vasubhir devo astu śam ādityebhir varuṇaḥ suśaṁsaḥ | śaṁ no rudro rudrebhir jalāṣaḥ śaṁ nas tvaṣṭā gnābhir iha śṛṇotu ||
  • śaṁ na indro vasubhir devo astu: This line invokes Indra, the king of the gods, asking for his blessings and peace. Indra is often associated with strength and power, and in real life, this can be seen as a call for leadership and guidance in our endeavors.
  • śam ādityebhir varuṇaḥ suśaṁsaḥ: Here, the mantra seeks the blessings of Varuna, the god of cosmic order, and the Adityas, who represent various aspects of the sun. This can be interpreted as a request for harmony and balance in our lives, akin to how the sun provides light and sustenance to the earth.
  • śaṁ no rudro rudrebhir jalāṣaḥ: This line calls upon Rudra, a form of Shiva, associated with storms and transformation. It signifies the need for purification and renewal, much like how storms cleanse the atmosphere. In our lives, this can represent the importance of overcoming challenges to emerge stronger.
  • śaṁ nas tvaṣṭā gnābhir iha śṛṇotu: The final part invokes Tvaṣṭā, the divine craftsman, asking for his wisdom and guidance. This can be related to the creative processes in our lives, whether in art, science, or personal development, emphasizing the need for skill and knowledge in our pursuits.

  • Significance: This mantra is a prayer for peace, harmony, and guidance from various deities, reflecting the interconnectedness of different aspects of life.
  • Real-life Application: In daily life, this mantra can be recited during times of uncertainty or when seeking clarity in decision-making. It serves as a reminder to seek balance, strength, and wisdom from the universe.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking divine support in our lives. It highlights the importance of community, leadership, and the need for inner strength and wisdom. By invoking these deities, we align ourselves with the greater cosmic order, fostering a sense of peace and purpose in our actions.
