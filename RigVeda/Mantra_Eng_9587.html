हव एषाम असुरो नक्षत द्यां श्रवस्यता मनसा निंसत क्षाम |चक्षाणां यत्र सुविताया देवाः द्यौर न वारेभिः कृतवन्त स्वैः ||
hava eṣām asuro nakṣata dyāṁ śravasyatā manasā niṁsata kṣām | cakṣāṇā yatra suvitāya devā dyaur na vārebhiḥ kṛṇavanta svaiḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • hava: O, invoke
    • eṣām: of these
    • asuro: the divine or powerful
    • nakṣata: shining or bright
    • dyāṁ: in the sky or heavens
    • śravasyatā: through fame or glory
    • manasā: with the mind
    • niṁsata: to be directed or focused
    • kṣām: to be capable or strong
    • cakṣāṇā: with the eyes
    • yatra: where
    • suvitāya: well-nourished or well-cultivated
    • devā: the gods
    • dyaur: the sky or heaven
    • na: not
    • vārebhiḥ: by the powerful
    • kṛṇavanta: who create or make
    • svaiḥ: with their own
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the divine powers that shine in the heavens.
    • It emphasizes the importance of focusing the mind and directing one's thoughts towards achieving strength and capability.
    • The reference to the gods suggests a connection to higher consciousness and divine guidance.
    • The phrase "well-nourished" indicates the need for a cultivated mind and spirit to receive divine blessings.
    • It also implies that true creation and strength come from within, rather than relying solely on external forces.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, focusing one's mind on goals and aspirations can lead to success, much like how the mantra encourages mental clarity and strength.
    • In personal development, cultivating a positive mindset and nurturing one's inner self can lead to greater achievements and fulfillment.
    • In spiritual practices, meditating on divine qualities can help individuals connect with their higher selves and find guidance in their lives.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of mental focus, inner strength, and divine connection in achieving one's goals.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, including personal growth, professional success, and spiritual development.
    • By invoking the divine and nurturing our inner capabilities, we can create a fulfilling and empowered life.
