विघ्नन्तो दुरिता पुरु सुगा टोकाय वाजिनः |तनाः कृत्वन्तो अर्वते ||
vighnanto duritā puru sugā tokāya vājinaḥ | tanā kṛṇvanto arvate ||
  • Mantra: "vighnanto duritā puru sugā tokāya vājinaḥ | tanā kṛṇvanto arvate ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • vighnanto: removing obstacles
    • duritā: difficulties or misfortunes
    • puru: many or numerous
    • sugā: good or auspicious
    • tokāya: for the benefit or welfare
    • vājinaḥ: those who are strong or powerful
    • tanā: bodies or forms
    • kṛṇvanto: creating or establishing
    • arvate: for the purpose of achieving or attaining
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the removal of obstacles and difficulties that hinder progress.
    • It calls upon powerful beings or forces to aid in creating a favorable environment for success.
    • The focus is on collective strength and the importance of working together to overcome challenges.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a team project at work, members may face various challenges. By collaborating and supporting each other, they can overcome these obstacles and achieve their goals.
    • In personal life, individuals may encounter hardships such as financial difficulties. Seeking help from family and friends can provide the necessary support to navigate through tough times.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the power of unity and collective effort in overcoming life's challenges.
    • It is applicable in various scenarios, from personal struggles to professional endeavors, highlighting the importance of seeking support and working together.
    • By invoking the essence of this mantra, individuals can foster resilience and create a positive environment conducive to success.
