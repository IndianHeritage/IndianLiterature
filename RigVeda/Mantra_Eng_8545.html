यथापवथā मनवे वयोधā अमित्रहā वरिवोविद धविष्मान् |एवā पवस्व द्रविणं दधाना इन्द्रे सं तिष्ठ जनयायुधानि ||
yathāpavathā manave vayodhā amitrahā varivovid dhaviṣmān | evā pavasva draviṇaṁ dadhāna indre saṁ tiṣṭha janayāyudhāni ||
  • Mantra: "yathāpavathā manave vayodhā amitrahā varivovid dhaviṣmān | evā pavasva draviṇaṁ dadhāna indre saṁ tiṣṭha janayāyudhāni ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yathā: as, in the way
    • apavathā: you have been directed
    • manave: to the mind or intellect
    • vayodhā: the forces of life or energy
    • amitrahā: the destroyer of enemies
    • varivovid: the one who knows the vastness
    • dhaviṣmān: the one who is powerful
    • evā: thus, in this manner
    • pavasva: may you purify
    • draviṇaṁ: wealth or resources
    • dadhāna: holding or possessing
    • indre: to Indra, the king of gods
    • saṁ: together, in unity
    • tiṣṭha: stand, remain
    • janayāyudhāni: the weapons of creation or progeny
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra invokes the divine forces to guide the mind and intellect in a way that is beneficial and protective.
    • The phrase "yathāpavathā manave" suggests that one should follow the path laid out by the divine, aligning one's thoughts and actions with higher wisdom.
    • "Vayodhā amitrahā" emphasizes the importance of overcoming adversities and enemies, which can be interpreted as both external challenges and internal doubts.
    • The mention of "varivovid dhaviṣmān" highlights the need for knowledge and strength to navigate life's complexities.
    • "Evā pavasva draviṇaṁ dadhāna" calls for the purification and proper utilization of resources, which can be seen in real life as a reminder to manage wealth and resources wisely.
    • Finally, "indre saṁ tiṣṭha janayāyudhāni" signifies the importance of standing united in the pursuit of creation and progress, invoking the blessings of Indra for success in endeavors.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, this mantra can be applied to encourage teamwork and collaboration, where individuals align their goals with the organization's vision.
    • In personal life, it serves as a reminder to overcome personal challenges and adversities, focusing on self-improvement and resilience.
    • In community initiatives, it emphasizes the importance of pooling resources and working together for a common cause, such as environmental conservation or social welfare.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra is significant as it encapsulates the essence of guidance, strength, and unity in the face of challenges.
    • Its application in real life encourages individuals to seek wisdom, manage resources effectively, and work collaboratively towards common goals.
    • By invoking the divine blessings of Indra, it inspires confidence and fortitude in personal and collective endeavors.
