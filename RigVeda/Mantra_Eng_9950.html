अस्य स्तोमेभिर औशिज ऋजिष्वा व्रजं दारयद वृषभेण पिप्रोः |सुत्वा यद यजतो दीदयद गीः पुर इयानो अभि वर्पसां भूत्व् ||
asya stomebhir auśija ṛjiśvā vrajaṁ darayad vṛṣabheṇa piproḥ | sutvā yad yajato dīdayad gīḥ pura iyāno abhi varpasā bhūt ||
  • Mantra: "asya stomebhir auśija ṛjiśvā vrajaṁ darayad vṛṣabheṇa piproḥ | sutvā yad yajato dīdayad gīḥ pura iyāno abhi varpasā bhūt ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • asya: of this
    • stomebhir: with hymns
    • auśija: of the seers or sages
    • ṛjiśvā: straight or righteous
    • vrajaṁ: the herd or group
    • darayad: may he protect
    • vṛṣabheṇa: with strength or might
    • piproḥ: may he nourish
    • sutvā: having been invoked
    • yad: that
    • yajato: of those who perform sacrifices
    • dīdayad: may he shine or illuminate
    • gīḥ: the song or hymn
    • pura: in the city or place
    • iyāno: coming forth
    • abhi: towards
    • varpasā: with brilliance or radiance
    • bhūt: may it be or exist
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the power of hymns and the importance of righteous actions.
    • The phrase "asya stomebhir" suggests that through the recitation of hymns, one can connect with divine energies.
    • "Vrajaṁ darayad" indicates the protective nature of these hymns, akin to how a shepherd protects his flock.
    • The mention of "vṛṣabheṇa" symbolizes strength, which can be related to how individuals draw strength from their community or support systems in real life.
    • "Yajato dīdayad gīḥ" reflects the idea that those who engage in spiritual practices or sacrifices will be illuminated, similar to how a dedicated student shines in their field of study.
    • The mantra concludes with a call for brilliance and existence, which can be interpreted as a desire for prosperity and well-being in one's life.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of community, strength, and spiritual practice in achieving personal and collective well-being.
    • In real life, it can be applied in various scenarios such as teamwork in professional settings, where collaboration leads to success.
    • It also highlights the significance of rituals and traditions in fostering a sense of belonging and purpose.
    • Ultimately, the mantra serves as a reminder that through righteous actions and collective efforts, individuals can achieve illumination and prosperity.
