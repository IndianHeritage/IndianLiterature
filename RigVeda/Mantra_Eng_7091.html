इन्द्रावरुणा यद ऋषिभ्यो मनिषां वाचो मतीं श्रुतम अदत्तम अग्रे | यानि स्थानानि असृजन्त धीराः यज्ञं तन्मानास तपसाभ्य अपश्यम् ||
indrāvaruṇā yad ṛṣibhyo manīṣāṁ vāco matiṁ śrutam adattam agre | yāni sthānāny asṛjanta dhīrā yajñaṁ tanvānās tapasābhy apaśyam ||
  • Indrāvaruṇā: This refers to the divine pair Indra and Varuna, representing the forces of nature and cosmic order. Indra is often associated with rain and thunderstorms, while Varuna embodies the moral and cosmic order.
  • Yad ṛṣibhyo: "What the sages (ṛṣis) have discovered." This emphasizes the importance of wisdom and knowledge gained through meditation and contemplation.
  • Manīṣāṁ vāco matiṁ: "The thoughts and words of wisdom." This highlights the significance of thoughtful speech and the power of words in shaping reality.
  • Śrutam adattam agre: "What has been heard and not given away." This suggests the value of knowledge that is retained and cherished, rather than shared indiscriminately.
  • Yāni sthānāny asṛjanta dhīrā: "Those who have created the various realms." This refers to the wise individuals who have the ability to manifest different aspects of existence through their understanding and actions.
  • Yajñaṁ tanvānās: "The sacrifice that is made with a pure heart." This indicates that true offerings are made with sincerity and devotion, not merely as rituals.
  • Tapasābhy apaśyam: "Through austerity, they have seen." This emphasizes the importance of discipline and self-control in gaining deeper insights into life and the universe.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda encapsulates the essence of wisdom, the power of words, and the significance of disciplined living. It teaches us that true knowledge comes from contemplation and that our thoughts and words can shape our reality. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios such as effective communication in relationships, the importance of mindful speech in professional settings, and the value of self-discipline in personal growth. By embodying these principles, individuals can create a harmonious and meaningful existence, contributing positively to the world around them.
