अवेयम् अश्वैद युवतिः पुरस्ताद युङ्क्ते गवाम् अरुणानाम् अनीकम् |विनूनम् उच्छाद असति प्र केतुर्गृहं-गृहम् उप तिष्ठाते अग्निः ||
aveyam aśvaid yuvatiḥ purastād yuṅkte gavām aruṇānām anīkam | vi nūnam ucchād asati pra ketur gṛhaṁ-gṛham upa tiṣṭhāte agniḥ ||
  • aveyam aśvaid yuvatiḥ purastād: This phrase can be interpreted as "the youthful maiden is present in front." Here, "yuvatiḥ" refers to youth or a young woman, symbolizing vitality and potential. In real life, this can represent the energy and enthusiasm of youth, which is often seen in young individuals embarking on new journeys or experiences.
  • yuṅkte gavām aruṇānām anīkam: This translates to "she unites with the red cows." The "red cows" can symbolize prosperity and abundance, as cows are often associated with wealth in many cultures. This part of the mantra emphasizes the importance of community and collaboration in achieving prosperity. For example, in a business setting, teamwork and cooperation among colleagues can lead to greater success.
  • vi nūnam ucchād asati pra ketur: This can be understood as "indeed, the fire rises, illuminating the path." Here, "agniḥ" (fire) symbolizes knowledge, enlightenment, and transformation. In real life, this can be likened to the pursuit of education and self-improvement, where knowledge acts as a guiding light in one's life journey.
  • gṛhaṁ-gṛham upa tiṣṭhāte agniḥ: This means "the fire resides in every home." This signifies that the essence of knowledge and enlightenment is present in every household. It suggests that every family has the potential for growth and learning. For instance, families that prioritize education and open discussions create an environment where knowledge thrives.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of youth, community, knowledge, and the potential for growth within every household. It highlights how collaboration and the pursuit of knowledge can lead to prosperity and enlightenment. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios, such as in educational settings, workplaces, and family dynamics, where the collective effort and the nurturing of knowledge can lead to a brighter future for individuals and communities alike.
