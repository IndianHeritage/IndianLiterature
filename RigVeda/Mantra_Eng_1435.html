मन्त्र: प्रति यद īṁ हविष्मान विश्वासु क्षासु जोगुवे |अग्रे रेब्हो न जरत ऋषूणां जूर्णिर होता ऋषूणाम् ||
prati yad īṁ haviṣmān viśvāsu kṣāsu joguve | agre rebho na jarata ṛṣūṇāṁ jūrṇir hota ṛṣūṇām ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • prati: towards, in relation to
    • yad: whatever, that which
    • īṁ: a form of the divine, often associated with power or energy
    • haviṣmān: the one who offers, the one who is worthy of offerings
    • viśvāsu: in all, everywhere
    • kṣāsu: in the realms, in the spaces
    • joguve: may he be invoked, may he be called upon
    • agre: in the forefront, at the beginning
    • rebho: of the offerings, of the sacrifices
    • na: not
    • jarata: aging, decaying
    • ṛṣūṇāṁ: of the sages, of the seers
    • jūrṇir: the one who is old, the one who has aged
    • hota: the priest, the one who invokes
    • ṛṣūṇām: of the sages, of the seers
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks to the idea of invoking divine energy and offerings in a way that transcends decay and aging.
    • It emphasizes the importance of calling upon the divine in all aspects of life, suggesting that the divine presence is everywhere and in everything.
    • The reference to sages indicates that wisdom and knowledge are crucial in this invocation, and that the guidance of wise individuals is essential.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, this mantra can be applied to the idea of innovation and creativity. Just as the mantra invokes divine energy, businesses can invoke new ideas and approaches to stay relevant and avoid stagnation.
    • In personal life, individuals can use this mantra as a reminder to seek wisdom and guidance from mentors or elders, ensuring that their actions are grounded in knowledge and experience.
    • In community gatherings or rituals, this mantra can be recited to invoke a sense of unity and collective purpose, reminding participants of the importance of wisdom and shared goals.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call to recognize the divine in all aspects of life and to seek wisdom in our actions.
    • It serves as a reminder that while we may face challenges and the passage of time, invoking the right energies and guidance can lead to renewal and growth.
    • In real-life scenarios, whether in personal development, professional endeavors, or community engagement, this mantra encourages a proactive approach to invoking wisdom and divine energy to navigate life's complexities.
