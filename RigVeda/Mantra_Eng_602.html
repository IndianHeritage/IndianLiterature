त्वं गोत्रं अङ्गिरोभ्यो 'वृणोर अपोतात्रये शतदुरेषु गातुवित् |ससेन चिद् विमदायावहो वस्व आजाव अद्रिं वावसानस्य नर्तयन ||
tvaṁ gotram aṅgirobhyo 'vṛṇor apotātraye śatadureṣu gātuvit | sasena cid vimadāyāvaho vasv ājāv adriṁ vāvasānasya nartayan ||
  • tvaṁ gotram: This phrase translates to "You are the lineage" or "You are the family." It signifies the importance of one's heritage and the values passed down through generations.
  • aṅgirobhyo: Referring to the Angiras, a group of sages known for their wisdom and knowledge. This highlights the connection to learned individuals and the pursuit of knowledge.
  • 'vṛṇor: This means "to choose" or "to select." It emphasizes the act of making conscious choices in life.
  • apotātraye: This can be interpreted as "in the three worlds" or "across the realms." It suggests a universal perspective, encompassing all aspects of existence.
  • śatadureṣu: Meaning "in a hundred ways" or "in many forms," this indicates the diversity of experiences and paths available in life.
  • gātuvit: This refers to "the one who knows the songs" or "the one who understands the hymns." It symbolizes the importance of wisdom and understanding in navigating life's challenges.
  • sasena: This translates to "with an army" or "with strength." It signifies the need for support and strength in one's endeavors.
  • cid vimadāyāvaho: This phrase can be interpreted as "bringing forth consciousness" or "leading to awareness." It emphasizes the importance of mindfulness and awareness in our actions.
  • vasv ājāv: Meaning "all beings" or "all creatures," this highlights the interconnectedness of life and the importance of compassion towards all living beings.
  • adriṁ vāvasānasya: This translates to "the mountain of the one who is established." It symbolizes stability and strength, akin to a mountain that stands firm against the elements.
  • nartayan: Meaning "to dance" or "to celebrate," this signifies the joy of life and the importance of celebrating our existence and achievements.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of life, emphasizing the importance of heritage, knowledge, conscious choices, and interconnectedness. In real-life scenarios, it encourages individuals to embrace their lineage, seek wisdom, make mindful decisions, and celebrate their existence. This mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as family gatherings, educational pursuits, and community celebrations, reminding us of our roots and the collective journey of humanity.
