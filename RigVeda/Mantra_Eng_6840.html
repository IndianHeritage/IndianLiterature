यथा वामात्रिर अश्विनाऽगीर्भिर विप्रो अजोहवीत् |नासत्यā सोमपीतये नभन्ताम् अन्यके समे ||
yathā vām atrir aśvinā gīrbhir vipro ajohavīt | nāsatyā somapītaye nabhantām anyake same ||
  • yathā - This word translates to "as" or "like," indicating a comparison or a way of understanding.
  • vām - Meaning "you," it addresses the divine or the audience directly.
  • atir - This can be interpreted as "the one who is beyond" or "the one who transcends," often referring to a higher state of being or consciousness.
  • aśvinā - Refers to the Aśvins, the twin deities in Vedic mythology known for their healing and medicinal qualities, symbolizing health and well-being.
  • gīrbhir - This means "with hymns" or "with praises," indicating the importance of words and expressions in connecting with the divine.
  • vipro - Translates to "the wise one" or "the learned," often referring to a priest or sage who possesses knowledge and insight.
  • ajohavīt - This can be understood as "may he be invoked" or "may he be called upon," suggesting a plea for divine presence or assistance.
  • nāsatyā - Refers to the Aśvins again, emphasizing their role in the mantra.
  • somapītaye - This means "for the sake of the drinker of Soma," where Soma is a sacred drink believed to confer immortality and divine insight.
  • nabhantām - This can be interpreted as "to the heavens" or "to the sky," indicating a connection to the divine or higher realms.
  • anyake - Meaning "other" or "different," suggesting a distinction or a choice among various paths or entities.
  • same - This translates to "in harmony" or "in unity," indicating the importance of balance and coherence in life.

Conclusion: This mantra emphasizes the invocation of divine energies, particularly those represented by the Aśvins, to seek wisdom, health, and harmony in life. It highlights the significance of words and hymns in connecting with the divine and the importance of seeking higher states of consciousness. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in rituals, meditation practices, or moments of reflection where individuals seek guidance, healing, or a deeper understanding of their existence. It serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of all beings and the pursuit of harmony in our lives.
