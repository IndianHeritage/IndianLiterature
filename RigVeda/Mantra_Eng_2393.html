भगं धियं वाजयन्तः पुरंढिं नराशंसो ज्ञास्पतिर नो व्याह् |आये वामस्य संगथे रयीणाम प्रिय देवस्य सवितुः स्याम ||
bhagaṁ dhiyaṁ vājayantaḥ puraṁdhiṁ narāśaṁso gnāspatir no avyāḥ | āye vāmasya saṁgathe rayīṇām priyā devasya savituḥ syāma ||
  • bhagaṁ dhiyaṁ: This phrase can be translated as "the wealth of intellect" or "prosperity of thought." It emphasizes the importance of wisdom and understanding in achieving success.
  • vājayantaḥ: This term means "those who bring forth strength" or "those who are powerful." It signifies the role of strength and vigor in overcoming challenges.
  • puraṁdhiṁ: This word refers to "the creator" or "the architect." It symbolizes the divine force that shapes our destinies and the world around us.
  • narāśaṁso: This term translates to "the best among men" or "noble beings." It highlights the qualities of virtue and righteousness that are essential for a fulfilling life.
  • gnāspatir: This means "the lord of knowledge" or "the deity of wisdom." It represents the pursuit of knowledge and the importance of learning in our lives.
  • no avyāḥ: This phrase translates to "may we not be deprived" or "let us not lack." It expresses a desire for abundance and fulfillment in life.
  • āye vāmasya: This means "in the auspicious gathering" or "in the company of the good." It emphasizes the significance of community and collaboration in achieving goals.
  • saṁgathe rayīṇām: This phrase translates to "in the union of wealth" or "in the gathering of resources." It signifies the importance of collective effort and shared resources for prosperity.
  • priyā devasya: This means "beloved of the deity" or "dear to the divine." It highlights the connection between human efforts and divine blessings.
  • savituḥ syāma: This translates to "may we be blessed by the sun" or "may we receive the light of the sun." It symbolizes the hope for enlightenment, vitality, and growth.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking wisdom, strength, and community in our lives. It emphasizes the importance of intellectual wealth and the divine guidance that supports our endeavors. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as in educational pursuits, teamwork in professional settings, and personal development. By fostering a mindset of collaboration and seeking knowledge, individuals can navigate challenges more effectively and achieve their goals. The invocation of divine blessings serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of human efforts and the greater cosmic order, encouraging us to strive for excellence while remaining grounded in our values.
