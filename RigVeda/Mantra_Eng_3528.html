तं वां रथं वयम अद्याहुवेमा पृथुज्रयम् अश्विनां संगतिं गोः |यः सूर्यां वहति वन्धुरायुर गिर्वाहसम पुरुतमं वसूयुम् ||
taṁ vāṁ rathaṁ vayam adyā huvema pṛthujrayam aśvinā saṁgatiṁ goḥ | yaḥ sūryāṁ vahati vandhurāyur girvāhasam purutamaṁ vasūyum ||
  • taṁ vāṁ rathaṁ vayam adyā huvema: This phrase can be translated as "We invoke that chariot of yours today." Here, the "chariot" symbolizes a vehicle of progress or a means to achieve goals. In real life, this can be likened to the aspirations and ambitions we pursue, such as education, career, or personal growth.
  • pṛthujrayam aśvinā saṁgatiṁ goḥ: This part means "the connection with the Ashvins, the divine twins." The Ashvins are often associated with health, medicine, and the dawn. This can be related to the importance of seeking guidance and support from mentors or professionals in our lives, akin to how we rely on doctors for health or teachers for knowledge.
  • yaḥ sūryāṁ vahati: Translated, this means "who carries the sun." The sun represents energy, vitality, and enlightenment. In a practical sense, this can refer to individuals who inspire and uplift others, much like leaders or role models who illuminate the path for others.
  • vandhurāyur girvāhasam purutamaṁ vasūyum: This can be interpreted as "the one who is the best among the wise, who brings forth prosperity." This emphasizes the value of wisdom and intelligence in achieving success. In everyday life, this can be seen in how we value education and knowledge in our careers and personal lives.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of invoking divine guidance and support in our pursuits. It highlights the significance of connections with wise individuals and the pursuit of knowledge and prosperity. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios, such as seeking mentorship in our careers, valuing education, and striving for personal growth. By recognizing the divine and wise influences in our lives, we can navigate our paths more effectively and achieve our goals with greater clarity and purpose.
