तव श्रीये व्य अजिहीत पर्वतो गवां गोत्रम् उदसृजो यद अंगिरः | इन्द्रेण युजा तमसा परिवृतम् बृहस्पते निर अपाम औब्जो अर्णवम् ||
tava śriye vy ajihīta parvato gavāṁ gotram udasṛjo yad aṅgiraḥ | indreṇa yujā tamasā parīvṛtam bṛhaspate nir apām aubjo arṇavam ||
  • tava śriye vy ajihīta: This phrase can be translated as "May your glory be exalted." Here, "tava" means "your," "śriye" refers to "glory" or "prosperity," and "ajihīta" means "may it be exalted." This reflects the aspiration for divine blessings and recognition of one's achievements.
  • parvato gavāṁ gotram udasṛjo: This part translates to "the mountain of the cattle's lineage." "Parvato" means "mountain," "gavāṁ" refers to "of the cattle," and "gotram" means "lineage" or "family." This signifies strength and stability, akin to a mountain, and the importance of lineage and heritage in one's life.
  • yad aṅgiraḥ: This translates to "as the Angiras." The Angiras are a group of sages in Vedic literature known for their wisdom and knowledge. This phrase suggests a connection to wisdom and the pursuit of knowledge.
  • indreṇa yujā tamasā parīvṛtam: This means "surrounded by darkness, united with Indra." "Indreṇa" refers to the god Indra, "yujā" means "united," "tamasā" means "darkness," and "parīvṛtam" means "surrounded." This indicates the idea of overcoming ignorance and darkness through divine guidance and strength.
  • bṛhaspate nir apām aubjo arṇavam: This translates to "O Brihaspati, may we cross the ocean of waters." "Bṛhaspate" refers to the deity of wisdom and eloquence, "nir" means "without," "apām" means "of waters," and "aubjo arṇavam" means "the ocean." This signifies the desire for guidance and support in navigating life's challenges, akin to crossing a vast ocean.

Conclusion: This mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking divine blessings for glory, wisdom, and strength. It emphasizes the importance of lineage and the pursuit of knowledge while recognizing the challenges of ignorance and darkness. In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as during significant life transitions, seeking guidance in education or career paths, or when facing challenges that require resilience and wisdom. By invoking these divine qualities, individuals can aspire to overcome obstacles and achieve their goals with the support of higher powers.
