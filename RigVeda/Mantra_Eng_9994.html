अभि गोत्राणि सहसाऽ गाहमानोऽदयो वीरः शतमन्युर इन्द्रः |दुश्च्यवनः पृतनाशाल् आयुध्योऽस्माकं सेना अवतु प्र युत्सु ||
abhi gotrāṇi sahasā gāhamāno 'dayo vīraḥ śatamanyur indraḥ | duścyavanaḥ pṛtanāṣāḻ ayudhyo3 'smākaṁ senā avatu pra yutsu ||
  • Mantra: "abhi gotrāṇi sahasā gāhamāno 'dayo vīraḥ śatamanyur indraḥ | duścyavanaḥ pṛtanāṣāḻ ayudhyo3 'smākaṁ senā avatu pra yutsu ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • abhi: towards, in the direction of
    • gotrāṇi: clans, families
    • sahasā: with great force, powerfully
    • gāhamāno: entering, approaching
    • 'dayo: the compassionate, the benevolent
    • vīraḥ: the hero, the brave one
    • śatamanyur: with a hundred-fold energy, strength
    • indraḥ: Indra, the king of gods
    • duścyavanaḥ: the one who is difficult to conquer
    • pṛtanāṣāḻ: the one who is victorious in battles
    • ayudhyo: without weapons, unarmed
    • smākaṁ: our, belonging to us
    • senā: army, forces
    • avatu: may protect, safeguard
    • pra: in the direction of, towards
    • yutsu: in battles, in conflicts
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra invokes the blessings of Indra, the king of gods, who is often associated with strength, power, and victory.
    • The phrase "abhi gotrāṇi sahasā gāhamāno" suggests a powerful approach towards various clans or families, indicating unity and strength in diversity.
    • The term "vīraḥ" emphasizes the qualities of bravery and heroism, which are essential in overcoming challenges.
    • "śatamanyur indraḥ" highlights the immense energy and strength that Indra embodies, suggesting that with divine support, one can achieve great feats.
    • The mantra also acknowledges the challenges represented by "duścyavanaḥ" and "pṛtanāṣāḻ," indicating that while obstacles may be formidable, victory is attainable.
    • Finally, the plea for protection of "smākaṁ senā" signifies the importance of safeguarding one's community or group in times of conflict.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, this mantra can be likened to a team coming together to face a challenging project. The unity and collective strength of the team can lead to successful outcomes.
    • In sports, a team that embodies the spirit of bravery and resilience, much like the qualities of Indra, can overcome tough opponents and achieve victory.
    • In personal life, individuals facing adversities can draw inspiration from this mantra to seek strength and support from their community, emphasizing the importance of collective effort.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the power of unity, bravery, and divine support in overcoming challenges.
    • Its significance lies in the encouragement it provides to individuals and communities to stand strong together in the face of adversity.
    • In real-life scenarios, whether in personal struggles, professional challenges, or community efforts, the essence of this mantra can inspire resilience and collective strength.
