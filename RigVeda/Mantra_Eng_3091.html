अकर्म ते स्वपसो अभूम ऋतम आवस्रन्न उषसो विभातिः |अनूनम् अग्निम पुरुधा सुचन्द्रं देवस्य मार्मृजतश्चारु चक्षुः ||
akarma te svapaso abhūma ṛtam avasrann uṣaso vibhātīḥ | anūnam agnim purudhā suścandraṁ devasya marmṛjataś cāru cakṣuḥ ||
  • Akarma te svapaso abhūma: This phrase can be interpreted as "We are not bound by your actions, O Sleep." Here, 'akarma' refers to inaction or the state of being free from the consequences of actions. In real life, this can relate to the idea of mindfulness and being present, where one is not overly attached to the outcomes of their actions.
  • Ṛtam avasrann uṣaso vibhātīḥ: This translates to "The truth has descended, and the dawn shines forth." 'Ṛtam' signifies truth or cosmic order, while 'uṣaso' refers to dawn. This can be likened to moments in life when clarity and understanding emerge after confusion, similar to how dawn brings light after the darkness of night.
  • Anūnam agnim purudhā suścandraṁ: This means "The fire, in its many forms, is not diminished." 'Agnim' represents fire, which can symbolize energy, passion, or transformation. In everyday life, this can be seen in how individuals harness their inner strength and creativity to overcome challenges, much like how fire can be used for warmth, cooking, or forging tools.
  • Devasya marmṛjataś cāru cakṣuḥ: This translates to "The beautiful eyes of the divine are ever-watchful." 'Cakṣuḥ' refers to eyes, symbolizing perception and awareness. This can be related to the idea of being observant and aware of the world around us, akin to how one might notice the beauty in nature or the kindness in others.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates themes of mindfulness, truth, transformation, and divine awareness. Its significance lies in encouraging individuals to remain present and aware, to seek truth in their lives, and to harness their inner strength. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various contexts such as personal growth, conflict resolution, and fostering a deeper connection with oneself and the universe. By embodying these principles, one can navigate life's challenges with grace and clarity.
