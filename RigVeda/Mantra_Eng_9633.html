अग्नेर अप्नसः समिद अस्तु भद्राग्निर मही रोडसी आ विवेश |अग्निर एकं चोदयत समत्स्व अग्निर वृत्राणि दयते पुरूणि ||
agner apnasaḥ samid astu bhadrāgnir mahī rodasī ā viveśa | agnir ekaṁ codayat samatsv agnir vṛtrāṇi dayate purūṇi ||
  • Mantra: "agner apnasaḥ samid astu bhadrāgnir mahī rodasī ā viveśa | agnir ekaṁ codayat samatsv agnir vṛtrāṇi dayate purūṇi ||"
  • Translation: "May the fire (Agni) that is kindled, be auspicious; may it enter the vast expanse of the earth and sky. May this fire inspire us to act together; may it dispel our obstacles and grant us abundance."
  • Word-by-Word Explanation:
    • Agni: Represents fire, a symbol of energy, transformation, and purity.
    • Apnasaḥ: Refers to the act of kindling or igniting.
    • Samid: The fuel or wood that is used to sustain the fire.
    • Bhadrāgnir: The auspicious or beneficial fire.
    • Mahī: The vastness of the earth.
    • Rodasī: The expanse of the sky.
    • Ā Viveśa: To enter or to pervade.
    • Ekaṁ: One or singular.
    • Codayat: To inspire or motivate.
    • Samatsv: Together or in harmony.
    • Vṛtrāṇi: Obstacles or challenges.
    • Dayate: To dispel or remove.
    • Purūṇi: Abundance or prosperity.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community gathering, when a fire is lit for a ritual, it symbolizes unity and the collective spirit of the participants, much like the mantra emphasizes the importance of coming together.
    • In personal development, the mantra can inspire individuals to overcome their challenges (vṛtrāṇi) and strive for abundance (purūṇi) in their lives through collective effort and motivation.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for unity, inspiration, and the dispelling of obstacles through the auspicious energy of fire. It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, such as community events, personal growth, and collaborative projects, where the collective effort is essential for overcoming challenges and achieving prosperity. The mantra serves as a reminder of the power of unity and the transformative energy that can be harnessed when individuals come together with a common purpose.
