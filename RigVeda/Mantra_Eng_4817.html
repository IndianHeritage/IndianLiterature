यद इन्द्र सर्गे अर्वतश्चोदयासे महाधने |असमने अध्वनि वृजिने पथि श्येनाम् iva श्रवस्यतः ||
yad indra sarge arvataś codayāse mahādhane | asamane adhvani vṛjine pathi śyenām̐ iva śravasyataḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yad: that which
    • indra: Indra, the king of gods
    • sarge: in the creation
    • arvataś: of the mountains
    • codayāse: you inspire or motivate
    • mahādhane: in the great wealth
    • asamane: in the unequal
    • adhvani: in the path
    • vṛjine: in the victory
    • pathi: on the way
    • śyenām̐: like the eagle
    • iva: as if
    • śravasyataḥ: of the glorious
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes Indra, the deity associated with strength, rain, and the heavens, emphasizing his role in creation and prosperity.
    • The phrase "you inspire or motivate" suggests that Indra's influence is crucial in guiding individuals towards success and abundance.
    • The reference to "the mountains" symbolizes stability and strength, indicating that true wealth is built on a solid foundation.
    • The comparison to "the eagle" signifies vision and the ability to soar above challenges, highlighting the importance of perspective in achieving one's goals.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, leaders often draw inspiration from successful figures (akin to Indra) to motivate their teams towards achieving great wealth and success.
    • In personal development, individuals may seek mentors who provide guidance and support, helping them navigate the "unequal paths" of life.
    • In nature, the eagle is often seen as a symbol of freedom and vision, reminding us to maintain a broad perspective when facing life's challenges.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of divine inspiration and guidance in the pursuit of success and prosperity.
    • It teaches us to seek strength and stability in our endeavors while maintaining a visionary outlook, much like the eagle.
    • In real life, applying the principles of this mantra can lead to personal and professional growth, encouraging individuals to rise above challenges and strive for greatness.
