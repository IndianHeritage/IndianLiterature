न यस्य द्यावापृथिवी न धन्वा नान्तरिक्षं नाद्रयः सोमो अक्षाः |यद अस्य मन्युर अधिनीयमानः श्रिणाति वीळु रुजति स्थिराणि ||
na yasya dyāvāpṛthivī na dhanva nāntarikṣaṁ nādrayaḥ somo akṣāḥ | yad asya manyur adhinīyamānaḥ śṛṇāti vīḻu rujati sthirāṇi ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • na - not
    • yasya - whose
    • dyāvāpṛthivī - heaven and earth
    • na - not
    • dhanva - wealth
    • nāntarikṣaṁ - nor the space (sky)
    • nādrayaḥ - nor the waters
    • somo - the moon
    • akṣāḥ - the eyes
    • yat - that which
    • asya - of this
    • manyur - anger
    • adhinīyamānaḥ - being controlled
    • śṛṇāti - hears
    • vīḻu - the wise
    • rujati - suffers
    • sthirāṇi - the stable (things)
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra reflects on the insignificance of material possessions and worldly elements in comparison to the inner state of being.
    • It emphasizes that even the vastness of heaven and earth, wealth, and the celestial bodies do not hold value if one is consumed by anger.
    • The wise person, who hears and understands this, recognizes that true stability and peace come from controlling one's emotions, particularly anger.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate environment, a leader who allows anger to dictate their decisions may lose the respect of their team, regardless of their position or wealth.
    • In personal relationships, individuals who let anger control their actions often find themselves isolated, even if they possess material wealth or social status.
    • Practices such as meditation and mindfulness can help individuals manage their emotions, leading to better decision-making and healthier relationships.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of emotional regulation and the pursuit of inner peace over material gains.
    • It is applicable in various aspects of life, including personal relationships, professional settings, and spiritual growth.
    • By focusing on controlling anger and fostering wisdom, individuals can achieve a more stable and fulfilling life.
