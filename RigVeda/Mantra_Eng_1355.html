अध स्वप्नस्य निर विदेऽभुञ्जतश्च रेवतः |उभा ता बस्रि नश्यतः ||
adha svapnasya nir vide 'bhuñjataś ca revataḥ | ubhā tā basri naśyataḥ ||
  • Adha svapnasya nir vide 'bhuñjataś ca revataḥ
    • Adha: This word translates to "below" or "under." It signifies a state of being grounded or connected to the earth.
    • Svapnasya: This means "of dreams." It refers to the realm of dreams, aspirations, and the subconscious mind.
    • Nir vide: This can be interpreted as "without separation" or "without division." It suggests a state of unity or wholeness.
    • Bhuñjataś: This word means "enjoying" or "experiencing." It indicates an active engagement with life and its offerings.
    • Ca: This simply means "and," connecting the ideas presented in the mantra.
    • Revataḥ: This term can be understood as "prosperous" or "abundant." It implies a state of richness in life, both materially and spiritually.
  • Ubhā tā basri naśyataḥ
    • Ubhā: This means "both" or "together." It emphasizes the duality or partnership in existence.
    • Tā: This translates to "that" or "those," referring to the elements or aspects being discussed.
    • Basri: This can be interpreted as "to be strong" or "to endure." It signifies resilience and strength in facing challenges.
    • Naśyataḥ: This means "to perish" or "to be destroyed." It suggests the importance of preservation and continuity in life.

Conclusion:

The mantra "adha svapnasya nir vide 'bhuñjataś ca revataḥ ubhā tā basri naśyataḥ" encapsulates the essence of grounding oneself in reality while embracing dreams and aspirations. It emphasizes the importance of unity and abundance in life, encouraging individuals to enjoy their experiences without losing sight of their roots. The duality of strength and preservation is also highlighted, reminding us that while we strive for prosperity, we must also be resilient and protect what is valuable to us.


This mantra is applicable in real-life scenarios such as personal development, where one must balance ambition with practicality. For instance, in pursuing a career, an individual should remain grounded in their values and experiences while also dreaming big and striving for success. It serves as a reminder that true prosperity comes from a harmonious blend of aspiration and reality, strength and preservation.
