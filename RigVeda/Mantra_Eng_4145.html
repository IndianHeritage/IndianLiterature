आ रुद्रास इन्द्रवन्तः सजोषसो हिरण्यरथाः सुविताया गन्तना |इयं वो अस्मत प्रति हर्यते मतिः तृष्णजे न दिव उत्सा उदन्यवे ||
ā rudrāsa indravantaḥ sajoṣaso hiraṇyarathāḥ suvitāya gantana | iyaṁ vo asmat prati haryate matis tṛṣṇaje na diva utsā udanyave ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā: from
    • rudrāsa: of the Rudras (a group of deities associated with storms and wind)
    • indravantaḥ: possessing the qualities of Indra (the king of gods)
    • sajoṣaso: with joy or happiness
    • hiraṇyarathāḥ: golden chariots
    • suvitāya: for the well-being or prosperity
    • gantana: going or moving
    • iyaṁ: this
    • vo: for you
    • asmat: our
    • prati: towards
    • haryate: is being drawn or attracted
    • matis: intellect or understanding
    • tṛṣṇaje: born of desire or thirst
    • na: not
    • diva: of the heavens
    • utsā: enthusiasm or energy
    • udanyave: for the one who rises or ascends
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the Rudras, who are associated with the forces of nature, particularly storms and transformation.
    • It emphasizes the qualities of Indra, suggesting a connection to strength, leadership, and the ability to overcome challenges.
    • The mention of "golden chariots" symbolizes prosperity and the journey towards success.
    • The phrase "this is being drawn towards you" indicates that the speaker is seeking a connection or favor from the divine, particularly in terms of intellect and understanding.
    • The reference to "desire" highlights the human condition of longing and aspiration, while the caution against being overly attached to these desires is also present.
    • Finally, the mantra encourages enthusiasm and energy for those who strive to rise above their circumstances.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, individuals may invoke this mantra to seek guidance and strength when facing challenges at work, much like a leader would rally their team.
    • Students preparing for exams can relate to the desire for knowledge and understanding, using the mantra to inspire their study efforts and maintain enthusiasm.
    • In personal development, individuals may reflect on their aspirations and the importance of balancing desires with a sense of purpose, drawing on the wisdom of the mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of human desires, divine guidance, and the pursuit of knowledge.
    • Its significance lies in encouraging individuals to seek strength and understanding while navigating the complexities of life.
    • In real-life scenarios, it can be applied to various aspects such as personal growth, professional challenges, and the quest for wisdom, making it a timeless invocation for those on a journey of self-discovery and achievement.
