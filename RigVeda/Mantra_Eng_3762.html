अग्निर जातो अरोचत घ्नन् दस्यूँज ज्योतिषा तमः |अविन्दद गा अपः स्वः ||
agnir jāto arocata ghnan dasyūñ jyotiṣā tamaḥ | avindad gā apaḥ svaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • agnir: Fire
    • jāto: Born or generated
    • arocata: Shone or illuminated
    • ghnan: Destroying or vanquishing
    • dasyūñ: The wicked or evil ones
    • jyotiṣā: By light or illumination
    • tamaḥ: Darkness
    • avindad: Found or discovered
    • gā: The waters
    • apaḥ: The waters or streams
    • svaḥ: The heavens or the sky
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the power of fire (Agni) as a source of light and energy.
    • Agni is depicted as having been born and shining brightly, symbolizing knowledge and enlightenment.
    • The phrase "ghnan dasyūñ" indicates the ability of this light to vanquish evil or ignorance, represented by the "dasyūñ."
    • It suggests that through illumination, darkness (tamaḥ) can be dispelled, leading to clarity and understanding.
    • The mention of "gā apaḥ svaḥ" connects the idea of fire with the waters and the heavens, indicating a holistic view of nature and the interconnectedness of elements.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a practical sense, this mantra can be related to the pursuit of knowledge and education. Just as fire illuminates a dark space, education enlightens the mind, helping individuals overcome ignorance.
    • In personal development, one can draw parallels to overcoming challenges and adversities (the "dasyūñ") in life through the light of wisdom and understanding.
    • In environmental contexts, the balance of fire (energy) and water (life) reflects the need for sustainable practices that respect nature's interconnected systems.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of light, knowledge, and the power to overcome darkness and ignorance.
    • It serves as a reminder of the importance of seeking enlightenment in various aspects of life, whether through education, personal growth, or environmental awareness.
    • In essence, it encourages individuals to harness the power of knowledge and wisdom to create a brighter, more harmonious existence.
