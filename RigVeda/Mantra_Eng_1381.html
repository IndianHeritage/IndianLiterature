अध ग्मन्ता नहुषो हवं सूरः श्रोताः राजानो अमृतस्य मन्द्राः |नभोजुवो यन् निरवस्य राधः प्रशस्तये महिना रथवते ||
adha gmantā nahuṣo havaṁ sūreḥ śrotā rājāno amṛtasya mandrāḥ | nabhojuvo yan niravasya rādhaḥ praśastaye mahinā rathavate ||
  • Adha gmantā nahuṣo havaṁ sūreḥ: This phrase can be interpreted as a call to the divine, invoking the energy of the sun (Sūrya) and the strength of Nahuṣa, a legendary king known for his wisdom and power. It signifies the importance of seeking guidance from higher powers in our endeavors.
  • Śrotā rājāno amṛtasya mandrāḥ: Here, "Śrotā" refers to the listeners or those who are receptive to wisdom. "Rājāno" means kings or leaders, and "amṛtasya mandrāḥ" suggests the essence of immortality or eternal truth. This line emphasizes the role of leaders in understanding and disseminating wisdom that leads to a fulfilling life.
  • Nabhojuvo yan niravasya rādhaḥ: This part speaks of the vastness of the sky (Nabhojuvo) and the importance of being grounded (niravasya). It suggests that while we aspire for greatness, we must remain connected to our roots and the reality around us.
  • Praśastaye mahinā rathavate: "Praśastaye" means for praise or commendation, and "mahinā rathavate" refers to the strength of a chariot. This line indicates that our actions should be directed towards achieving greatness and that we should strive for commendable deeds that propel us forward, much like a chariot that moves with purpose.

  • Significance: This mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking divine guidance, the importance of wisdom in leadership, the balance between aspiration and grounding, and the pursuit of commendable actions. It serves as a reminder that in our quest for success, we must remain connected to our values and the wisdom of those who came before us.
  • Real-life Application: In today's world, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios such as:
    • Leadership: Leaders in organizations can draw inspiration from this mantra to seek wisdom and guidance, ensuring that their decisions are informed and beneficial for all.
    • Personal Growth: Individuals can reflect on their aspirations while remaining grounded in their values, ensuring that their pursuits are meaningful and aligned with their true selves.
    • Community Engagement: The mantra encourages collaboration and the sharing of wisdom within communities, fostering an environment where collective growth is prioritized.
