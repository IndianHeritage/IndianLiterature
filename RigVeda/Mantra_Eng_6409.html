अपामिवाम अप श्रिधम् अप सेधत दुरमतिम् |आदित्यासो युयोतना नो अंहसः ||
apāmīvām apa sridham apa sedhata durmatim | ādityāso yuyotanā no aṁhasaḥ ||
  • Mantra: "apāmīvām apa sridham apa sedhata durmatim | ādityāso yuyotanā no aṁhasaḥ ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • apāmīvām: "from the waters" or "of the waters"
    • apa: "away" or "from"
    • sridham: "prosperity" or "wealth"
    • apa: "away" or "from"
    • sedhata: "may you remove" or "may you lead away"
    • durmatim: "bad thoughts" or "evil intentions"
    • ādityāso: "of the sun" or "of the solar deities"
    • yuyotanā: "may they unite" or "may they come together"
    • no: "for us"
    • aṁhasaḥ: "from the darkness" or "from ignorance"
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the waters, symbolizing purity and life, asking them to remove obstacles and negative thoughts.
    • The repetition of "apa" emphasizes the desire to distance oneself from negativity and harmful influences.
    • The mention of "ādityāso" connects the mantra to the sun, representing enlightenment, clarity, and positive energy.
    • The overall plea is for unity and support from divine forces to overcome ignorance and negativity.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, individuals often face challenges that can lead to negative thinking. This mantra serves as a reminder to seek clarity and positivity, much like how one might meditate or practice mindfulness to clear the mind.
    • In a community setting, this mantra can be applied during group activities or discussions, encouraging participants to focus on constructive thoughts and collaboration, rather than divisive or harmful ideas.
    • In personal relationships, invoking the essence of this mantra can help individuals communicate better, fostering understanding and unity, while dispelling misunderstandings and negative emotions.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for purification and the removal of negativity, both personally and collectively.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, such as personal growth, community harmony, and relationship building.
    • By embracing the essence of this mantra, individuals can cultivate a more positive mindset and foster a supportive environment, leading to overall well-being and enlightenment.
