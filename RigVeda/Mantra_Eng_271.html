विमृलीकाय ते मनो रथीर अश्वं न संदितम् |गीर्भिर वरुण सीमहि ||
vi mṛḻīkāya te mano rathīr aśvaṁ na saṁditam | gīrbhir varuṇa sīmahi ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • vi: away, apart
    • mṛḻīkāya: to the one who is gentle or soft
    • te: your
    • mano: mind
    • rathīr: charioteer
    • aśvaṁ: horse
    • na: not
    • saṁditam: disturbed or agitated
    • gīrbhir: with hymns or words
    • varuṇa: the deity associated with cosmic order and water
    • sīmahi: we invoke or we praise
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra can be understood as a prayer or invocation to Varuṇa, the god of cosmic order and water, asking for a calm and focused mind.
    • The phrase "vi mṛḻīkāya te mano" suggests a desire for a gentle and peaceful state of mind, free from disturbances.
    • The reference to "rathīr aśvaṁ" symbolizes the control of one's thoughts and actions, akin to a charioteer guiding a horse, which represents the mind.
    • By invoking Varuṇa with "gīrbhir varuṇa sīmahi," the speaker seeks divine assistance in maintaining mental clarity and harmony.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In stressful situations, such as during exams or public speaking, individuals often seek to calm their minds. This mantra serves as a reminder to focus and maintain composure.
    • In meditation practices, practitioners aim to achieve a state of tranquility and clarity, paralleling the essence of this mantra.
    • In leadership roles, the ability to remain calm and guide a team effectively is crucial. This mantra can inspire leaders to cultivate a peaceful mindset while making decisions.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for mental peace and clarity, essential for navigating life's challenges.
    • It emphasizes the importance of controlling one's thoughts and emotions, akin to a charioteer guiding a horse, which is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal development to professional settings.
    • By invoking the divine presence of Varuṇa, individuals can seek support in achieving a balanced and harmonious state of mind, ultimately leading to better decision-making and overall well-being.
