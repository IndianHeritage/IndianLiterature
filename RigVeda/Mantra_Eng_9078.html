एवाऽहि मां तवसं वर्धयन्ति दिवश्चिन मे बृहता उत्तरां धूः |पुरू सहस्राणि शिशामि साकं अशत्रुं हि मां जनिता जजाना ||
evā hi māṁ tavasaṁ vardhayanti divaś cin me bṛhata uttarā dhūḥ | purū sahasrā ni śiśāmi sākam aśatruṁ hi mā janitā jajāna ||
  • evā hi māṁ tavasaṁ vardhayanti: This phrase can be translated to "Indeed, they increase my strength." Here, "tavasaṁ" refers to strength or power, and "vardhayanti" means to enhance or increase. In real life, this can be likened to the support one receives from friends, family, or mentors that helps in personal growth and empowerment.
  • divaś cin me bṛhata uttarā dhūḥ: This translates to "May the heavens grant me great prosperity." "Divaś" refers to the heavens or divine realms, "bṛhata" means great or vast, and "uttarā dhūḥ" can be interpreted as blessings or auspiciousness. This can be seen in the context of seeking blessings for success in endeavors, such as starting a new business or pursuing education.
  • purū sahasrā ni śiśāmi: This means "I am surrounded by many thousands." "Purū" refers to many or numerous, and "sahasrā" means thousands. This can be related to the idea of community support, where an individual is backed by a large network of people, such as colleagues in a workplace or members of a community organization.
  • sākam aśatruṁ hi mā janitā jajāna: This translates to "With no enemies, I am born." "Sākam" means together or in unity, "aśatruṁ" refers to without enemies, and "janitā" means born or created. This can be applied to the concept of harmony and peace in society, where collaboration and understanding lead to a lack of conflict, fostering a nurturing environment for growth.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of strength, divine blessings, community support, and harmony in life. It signifies that personal growth is often a collective effort, supported by the divine and the community around us. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applicable in various contexts such as teamwork in professional settings, seeking blessings for new ventures, and promoting peace and collaboration in society. By embodying these principles, individuals can cultivate a fulfilling and prosperous life.
