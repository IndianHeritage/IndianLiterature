धर्ता दिवो रजसस् पृष्ट ऊर्ध्वो रथो न वायुर वसुभिर नियुत्वान् |क्षपां वस्तां जनिता सूर्यस्य विभक्तां भागं धिषणेव वाजम् ||
dhartā divo rajasas pṛṣṭa ūrdhvo ratho na vāyur vasubhir niyutvān | kṣapāṁ vastā janitā sūryasya vibhaktā bhāgaṁ dhiṣaṇeva vājam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • dhartā: the supporter or sustainer
    • divo: of the heavens
    • rajasas: of the atmosphere or the sky
    • pṛṣṭa: the back or the upper part
    • ūrdhvo: upward or above
    • ratho: the chariot
    • na: not
    • vāyur: the wind
    • vasubhir: with the wealth or the elements
    • niyutvān: having been directed or guided
    • kṣapāṁ: the night
    • vastā: the abode or dwelling
    • janitā: the creator or originator
    • sūryasya: of the sun
    • vibhaktā: divided or distributed
    • bhāgaṁ: the portion or share
    • dhiṣaṇeva: like a command or decree
    • vājam: the strength or power
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the divine support and structure of the universe.
    • The "chariot" symbolizes the journey of life, which is guided by the forces of nature.
    • The reference to the sun indicates the source of energy and life, emphasizing the importance of light and warmth in our existence.
    • The mention of "night" and "wealth" suggests a balance between different aspects of life, such as light and darkness, material and spiritual wealth.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In our daily lives, we often rely on the support of family and friends, much like the "dhartā" who sustains us.
    • The journey of life can be likened to a chariot ride, where we navigate through various challenges and experiences, guided by our values and beliefs.
    • Just as the sun rises each day, bringing light and energy, we too can find motivation and purpose in our daily routines.
    • The balance between work (material wealth) and rest (night) is crucial for our well-being, reminding us to take time for self-care and reflection.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the interconnectedness of all elements in the universe and the importance of divine guidance in our lives.
    • It serves as a reminder to seek balance, embrace the journey, and recognize the support we have from the universe and those around us.
    • In practical terms, it encourages us to appreciate the cycles of life, the importance of community, and the need for both material and spiritual fulfillment.
