न तं अंहो न दुरितं देवासो अष्ट मर्त्यम् |सजोषसो यं आर्यमा मित्रो नयन्ति वरुणो अति द्विषः ||
na tam aṁho na duritaṁ devāso aṣṭa martyam | sajoṣaso yam aryamā mitro nayanti varuṇo ati dviṣaḥ ||
  • na tam aṁho: This phrase can be translated as "not that which is dark" or "not that which is negative." It suggests a rejection of negativity or harmful influences in life.
  • na duritaṁ: This means "not the difficult" or "not the sinful." It emphasizes the importance of avoiding actions that lead to suffering or moral degradation.
  • devāso: Referring to the "gods" or divine beings, this term highlights the role of higher powers in guiding and protecting individuals.
  • aṣṭa martyam: This translates to "the eight mortals," which can symbolize the various aspects of human existence or the different paths one can take in life.
  • sajoṣaso: This means "together with joy" or "in harmony." It signifies the importance of community and shared happiness in life.
  • yam aryamā: This phrase refers to "that which is noble" or "the righteous one." It encourages individuals to aspire towards noble qualities and actions.
  • mitro: Meaning "friend," this term emphasizes the value of friendship and supportive relationships in one's journey.
  • nayanti: This translates to "they lead" or "they guide," indicating the role of divine or noble beings in guiding individuals towards the right path.
  • varuṇo: Referring to Varuna, the god of cosmic order and water, this term symbolizes the importance of maintaining balance and harmony in life.
  • ati dviṣaḥ: This means "beyond enmity" or "free from hatred." It encourages individuals to rise above conflicts and negative emotions.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of rejecting negativity and sin, while seeking guidance from divine beings and noble qualities. It highlights the significance of community, friendship, and harmony in life. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied to personal development, encouraging individuals to cultivate positive relationships, aspire towards righteousness, and maintain a balanced and harmonious life. By embodying these principles, one can navigate the complexities of life with grace and integrity.
