मन्त्र: इयं न उष्रा प्रथमā सुदेव्यं रेवत सनीभ्यो रेवती व्य उच्छतु |आरे मन्युः दुर्विदात्रस्य धीमहि स्वस्त्य अग्निं समिधानम् ईमहे ||
iyaṁ na usrā prathamā sudevyaṁ revat sanibhyo revatī vy ucchatu | āre manyuṁ durvidatrasya dhīmahi svasty a1gniṁ samidhānam īmahe ||
  • iyaṁ na usrā prathamā sudevyaṁ revat sanibhyo revatī vy ucchatu: This line can be interpreted as a call to the divine forces to rise and shine. The term "usrā" refers to the dawn or light, while "sudevyaṁ" signifies something that is auspicious or divine. The phrase suggests a yearning for the divine light to illuminate our path.
  • āre manyuṁ durvidatrasya dhīmahi: Here, "āre" is an invocation, and "manyuṁ" refers to the mind or intellect. "Durvidatrasya" means difficult to understand or comprehend. This line emphasizes the importance of focusing our intellect on overcoming challenges that may seem insurmountable.
  • svasty a1gniṁ samidhānam īmahe: This part translates to a prayer for well-being and the invocation of fire ("agni"). "Svasty" signifies health and prosperity, while "samidhānam" refers to the act of kindling or igniting. This line expresses a desire to ignite the fire of well-being and prosperity in our lives.

  • Real-Life Example of Invocation of Divine Light: Just as the dawn brings light to the world, individuals often seek guidance and clarity in their lives. For instance, someone facing a difficult decision may pray for divine insight to help them choose the right path.
  • Overcoming Challenges: The line about focusing the intellect on difficult challenges can be seen in the context of education. Students often face tough subjects that require perseverance and mental strength. By concentrating their efforts and seeking help, they can overcome these academic hurdles.
  • Igniting Prosperity: The invocation of fire symbolizes transformation and energy. In a business context, entrepreneurs often seek to "ignite" their ventures by fostering innovation and creativity, leading to growth and success.

  • Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda emphasizes the significance of seeking divine guidance, focusing the mind on overcoming challenges, and igniting the fire of prosperity in our lives. It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal growth and education to business and spiritual pursuits. By embodying these principles, individuals can navigate their paths with clarity, resilience, and purpose.
