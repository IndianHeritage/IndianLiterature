अभि विश्वानि वार्याभि देवाम ऋतावृद्धः |सोमः पुनानो अर्षति ||
abhi viśvāni vāryābhi devām̐ ṛtāvṛdhaḥ | somaḥ punāno arṣati ||
  • Mantra: "abhi viśvāni vāryābhi devām̐ ṛtāvṛdhaḥ | somaḥ punāno arṣati ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • abhi: towards, in the direction of
    • viśvāni: all, universal
    • vāryābhi: with strength, with power
    • devām̐: to the gods, divine beings
    • ṛtāvṛdhaḥ: who increases righteousness, who promotes truth
    • somaḥ: the sacred drink, often associated with immortality and divine inspiration
    • punāno: purifying, renewing
    • arṣati: shines, radiates
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the universal powers and divine beings, asking for their strength and support.
    • It emphasizes the importance of righteousness and truth, suggesting that these qualities are essential for a harmonious life.
    • The mention of "soma" symbolizes the quest for spiritual enlightenment and renewal, indicating that through divine connection, one can achieve purity and clarity.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, this mantra can be applied when a team seeks to work together harmoniously, invoking the strength of collaboration and truthfulness in their dealings.
    • In personal relationships, it can serve as a reminder to uphold honesty and integrity, fostering trust and mutual respect.
    • During times of personal crisis, one might meditate on this mantra to seek divine guidance and renewal, helping to restore balance and clarity in life.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of seeking divine support in all endeavors, emphasizing the need for strength, truth, and righteousness.
    • It is applicable in various aspects of life, including personal growth, relationships, and professional environments, encouraging individuals to strive for purity and enlightenment.
    • By invoking these qualities, one can create a more harmonious and fulfilling life, aligned with universal principles.
