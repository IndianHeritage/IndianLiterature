तव त्ये सोम शक्तिभिर निकामासो व्य ऋण्विरे |गृत्सस्य धीरास तवसो वि वो मदे व्रजं गोमन्तं अश्विनं विवक्षसे ||
tava tye soma śaktibhir nikāmāso vy ṛṇvire | gṛtsasya dhīrās tavaso vi vo made vrajaṁ gomantam aśvinaṁ vivakṣase ||
  • tava tye soma: This phrase can be interpreted as "your power, O Soma." Soma is often associated with a divine drink that grants immortality and is a symbol of vitality and strength.
  • śaktibhir nikāmāso vy ṛṇvire: This translates to "with your powers, we invoke you." Here, the emphasis is on the invocation of divine energies or powers that are believed to aid in achieving one's goals.
  • gṛtsasya dhīrās: This means "the wise ones of the gṛts," where gṛts refers to the sacred offerings or the essence of life. It suggests that wisdom and understanding are essential in the pursuit of spiritual and material goals.
  • tavaso vi vo made: This can be understood as "may we be filled with your essence." It reflects a desire for divine inspiration and the infusion of spiritual energy into one's life.
  • vrajaṁ gomantam: This phrase translates to "the one who leads us to prosperity." It signifies the importance of guidance and support in achieving success and abundance.
  • aśvinaṁ vivakṣase: This means "O Ashvins, we call upon you." The Ashvins are twin deities associated with health, medicine, and the dawn, symbolizing new beginnings and healing.

Conclusion: This mantra emphasizes the invocation of divine powers and wisdom to achieve prosperity and well-being. In real-life scenarios, it can be applied in various contexts such as:

  • In personal development, individuals can use this mantra to seek guidance and strength in their endeavors, whether in career, relationships, or health.
  • In community settings, it can serve as a reminder of the importance of collective wisdom and support in achieving common goals.
  • In spiritual practices, chanting this mantra can help individuals connect with divine energies, fostering a sense of peace and purpose.

Overall, the significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine assistance and the recognition of the interconnectedness of wisdom, strength, and prosperity in our lives.
