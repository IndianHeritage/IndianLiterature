आ वायो भूष शुचिपा उप नः सहस्रं ते नियुतो विश्ववारा |उपो ते अन्धो मद्यम आयामि यस्य देव दधिषे पूर्वपेयं ||
ā vāyo bhūṣa śucipā upa naḥ sahasraṁ te niyuto viśvavāra | upo te andho madyam ayāmi yasya deva dadhiṣe pūrvapeyam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā vāyo: O Wind
    • bhūṣa: adornment or decoration
    • śucipā: pure or sacred
    • upa naḥ: come to us
    • sahasraṁ: a thousand
    • te: your
    • niyuto: directed or guided
    • viśvavāra: protector of all
    • upo: come near
    • te andho: your darkness
    • madyam: middle or center
    • ayāmi: I go
    • yasya: whose
    • deva: O God
    • dadhiṣe: you bestow
    • pūrvapeyam: the previous drink or offering
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the Wind as a divine force, asking it to come and bless us with purity and protection.
    • The reference to "a thousand" signifies abundance and the vastness of blessings that can be bestowed upon us.
    • It emphasizes the importance of seeking guidance and protection from the divine, particularly in times of uncertainty or darkness.
    • The phrase "O God, you bestow the previous drink" suggests a connection to past blessings and the continuity of divine grace in our lives.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, we often seek guidance and protection in various forms, such as through meditation, prayer, or rituals that invoke divine energies.
    • For instance, before embarking on a new journey or project, individuals may recite this mantra to seek blessings for success and purity of purpose.
    • In moments of confusion or darkness, people may turn to spiritual practices that resonate with the essence of this mantra, seeking clarity and direction.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of invoking divine forces for guidance, protection, and purity in our lives.
    • It serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of all beings and the importance of seeking blessings from the universe.
    • In real-life scenarios, it encourages individuals to cultivate a sense of spirituality and connection to the divine, fostering a deeper understanding of their purpose and path.
