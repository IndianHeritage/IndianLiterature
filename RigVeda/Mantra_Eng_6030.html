आ तु षिञ्च कण्वमन्तं न घा विद्म शवासानात् |यशस्तरं शतमूतेः ||
ā tū ṣiñca kaṇvamantaṁ na ghā vidma śavasānāt | yaśastaraṁ śatamūteḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā: from
    • tū: you
    • ṣiñca: sprinkle or pour
    • kaṇvamantaṁ: the essence of the mind or intellect
    • na: not
    • ghā: to know
    • vidma: we know
    • śavasānāt: from the body of the dead
    • yaśastaraṁ: the glory or fame
    • śatamūteḥ: of the hundredfold or abundant
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra can be understood as a prayer or invocation for the divine to bestow wisdom and knowledge upon us.
    • The phrase "ā tū ṣiñca" suggests a request for the divine to pour forth knowledge and understanding.
    • "kaṇvamantaṁ" refers to the essence of intellect, indicating that the knowledge sought is not superficial but profound.
    • The line "na ghā vidma" implies a recognition of our limitations in understanding the deeper truths of existence.
    • "śavasānāt" connects the quest for knowledge with the transient nature of life, reminding us that our physical existence is temporary.
    • "yaśastaraṁ" emphasizes the importance of glory and fame, suggesting that true knowledge leads to a lasting legacy.
    • "śatamūteḥ" indicates abundance, implying that the knowledge we seek is not just for ourselves but can benefit many.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, this mantra can be applied when seeking guidance for making important decisions. For instance, a leader may invoke this wisdom to ensure that their choices benefit the entire team.
    • In education, students can reflect on this mantra when studying, reminding themselves that true understanding goes beyond rote memorization and leads to deeper insights.
    • In personal relationships, individuals can use this mantra to seek clarity and understanding, fostering better communication and connection with others.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of seeking wisdom and understanding in all aspects of life.
    • Its significance lies in acknowledging our limitations while striving for knowledge that can lead to personal growth and the betterment of society.
    • In real-life scenarios, invoking this mantra can inspire individuals to pursue knowledge with humility and a sense of purpose, ultimately contributing to a more enlightened and harmonious world.
