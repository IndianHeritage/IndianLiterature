भूयाम ते सुमतौ वाजिनो वयं मा नः स्तर अभिमातये |अस्माण् चित्राभिर अवताद अभिष्टिभिर आ नः सुम्नेषु यामय ||
bhūyāma te sumatau vājino vayam mā naḥ star abhimātaye | asmāñ citrābhir avatād abhiṣṭibhir ā naḥ sumneṣu yāmaya ||
  • bhūyāma: This word translates to "may we become" or "let us be." It signifies a desire for growth and enhancement in various aspects of life.
  • te: This means "to you," indicating a direction of intention or prayer towards a higher power or divine entity.
  • sumatau: This translates to "with good thoughts" or "with auspicious intellect." It emphasizes the importance of positive thinking and wisdom in our endeavors.
  • vājino: This word means "of the strong" or "of the powerful." It refers to strength, vitality, and the ability to achieve one's goals.
  • vayam: This means "we," indicating a collective aspiration or intention among a group.
  • mā: This translates to "may not," suggesting a plea or a wish to avoid negativity or obstacles.
  • naḥ: This means "our," indicating possession or belonging to the group that is praying.
  • star: This word can be interpreted as "to shine" or "to rise," symbolizing aspirations and achievements.
  • abhimātaye: This translates to "for our desires" or "for our intentions," indicating a focus on fulfilling one's aspirations.
  • asmāñ: This means "us," reinforcing the collective nature of the prayer.
  • citrābhir: This translates to "with various" or "with colorful," suggesting diversity and richness in experiences.
  • avatād: This means "may we be protected" or "may we be guided," indicating a desire for divine protection and guidance.
  • abhiṣṭibhir: This translates to "with blessings" or "with auspiciousness," emphasizing the importance of positive outcomes.
  • ā: This means "to" or "towards," indicating directionality in the prayer.
  • naḥ: Again, this means "our," reinforcing the collective aspect.
  • sumneṣu: This translates to "in good things" or "in auspicious matters," focusing on positive outcomes.
  • yāmaya: This means "may we attain" or "may we reach," indicating a desire for achievement and fulfillment.

Conclusion: The mantra expresses a collective aspiration for growth, strength, and positive outcomes in life. It emphasizes the importance of good thoughts, divine guidance, and the pursuit of one's desires. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as during group endeavors, team projects, or community initiatives where collective intention and positive thinking are crucial for success. By invoking this mantra, individuals and groups can foster an environment of support, encouragement, and shared aspirations, ultimately leading to a more harmonious and successful outcome in their pursuits.
