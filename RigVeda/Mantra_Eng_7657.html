त्वया ह स्विद युजा वयं चोदिष्टेन यविष्ट्या |अभि ष्मो वाजसातये ||
tvayā ha svid yujā vayaṁ codiṣṭhena yaviṣṭhya | abhi ṣmo vājasātaye ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • tvayā: by you
    • ha: indeed
    • svid: where
    • yujā: with the connection
    • vayaṁ: we
    • codiṣṭhena: by the command
    • yaviṣṭhya: having been established
    • abhi: towards
    • ṣmo: we shall be
    • vājasātaye: for the purpose of strength or power
  • Overall Meaning:
    • This mantra expresses a plea or invocation to a higher power, asking for guidance and strength.
    • It emphasizes the importance of connection and command in achieving one's goals.
    • The phrase "we shall be for the purpose of strength" indicates a collective aspiration for empowerment and success.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a team setting, such as a sports team or a project group, members often rely on each other’s strengths and guidance to achieve a common goal. This mantra can be seen as a reminder of the importance of collaboration and mutual support.
    • In personal development, individuals may seek mentorship or guidance from experienced individuals to navigate challenges and enhance their capabilities, reflecting the essence of seeking strength through connection.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for unity and strength through guidance. It highlights the importance of seeking help and establishing connections to achieve greater goals.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as teamwork, mentorship, and personal growth, reminding us that we are stronger together and that seeking guidance is a vital part of our journey towards success.
