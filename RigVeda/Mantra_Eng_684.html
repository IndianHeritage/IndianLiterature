बृहती इव सूनवे रोधसी गिरो होता मनुष्यः न दक्षः |स्वर्वते सत्यशुष्माय पूर्वीर वैश्वानराय नृतमाय यह्वीः ||
bṛhatī iva sūnave rodasī giro hotā manuṣyo3 na dakṣaḥ | svarvate satyaśuṣmāya pūrvīr vaiśvānarāya nṛtamāya yahvīḥ ||
  • Mantra: "bṛhatī iva sūnave rodasī giro hotā manuṣyo3 na dakṣaḥ | svarvate satyaśuṣmāya pūrvīr vaiśvānarāya nṛtamāya yahvīḥ ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • bṛhatī: vast, great
    • iva: like, as if
    • sūnave: to the son, offspring
    • rodasī: of the two worlds, the heavens and the earth
    • giro: of the words, speech
    • hotā: the priest, the one who invokes
    • manuṣyo: human beings
    • na: not
    • dakṣaḥ: skilled, capable
    • svarvate: to the one who speaks, uttering
    • satyaśuṣmāya: to the one who is true and auspicious
    • pūrvīr: from the ancients, the wise
    • vaiśvānarāya: to the universal being
    • nṛtamāya: to the one who is truthful
    • yahvīḥ: who is invoked, called upon
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra speaks of the vastness and greatness of the universe, likening it to the offspring of the divine.
    • It emphasizes the importance of words and speech, particularly in the context of invoking the divine through rituals.
    • The mention of humans not being skilled suggests a call for humility and recognition of our limitations in understanding the divine.
    • It highlights the significance of truth and auspiciousness, urging individuals to align their actions and words with these principles.
    • The reference to the ancients and the universal being indicates a connection to wisdom that transcends time and space.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern life, this mantra can be applied in the context of communication. For instance, in a workplace, clear and truthful communication is essential for collaboration and success.
    • In personal relationships, being truthful and auspicious in our words can strengthen bonds and foster trust.
    • During rituals or significant life events, invoking the divine with sincerity and humility can enhance the spiritual experience.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the vastness of existence and the importance of aligning our speech and actions with truth and auspiciousness.
    • It encourages individuals to recognize their limitations and seek wisdom from the ancients while invoking the divine in their lives.
    • Ultimately, it highlights the significance of communication, truthfulness, and humility in fostering a harmonious existence.
