शतम अहं तिरिन्दिरे सहस्रं पार्शाव आददे |राधांसी याद्वानाम् ||
śatam ahaṁ tirindire sahasram parśāv ā dade | rādhāṁsi yādvānām ||
  • śatam: This word translates to "hundred." It signifies abundance and the idea of multiplicity.
  • ahaṁ: Meaning "I" or "myself," it indicates the speaker's personal involvement or presence.
  • tirindire: This term can be interpreted as "to the one who is strong" or "to the powerful." It reflects strength and capability.
  • sahasram: This means "thousand," further emphasizing the concept of vastness and plenty.
  • parśāv: This word refers to "sides" or "flanks," which can symbolize support or protection.
  • ā dade: This phrase translates to "gives" or "provides," indicating an act of generosity or bestowal.
  • rādhāṁsi: This term can be understood as "the desires" or "the aspirations," pointing towards human ambitions and goals.
  • yādvānām: This means "of those who are wise" or "of the knowledgeable," suggesting a connection to wisdom and understanding.

  • Meaning and Interpretation: The mantra speaks of a powerful entity (possibly a deity or a universal force) that provides abundance and support to those who are wise and have aspirations. The repetition of "hundred" and "thousand" emphasizes the vastness of what is being offered, suggesting that the blessings are not limited but rather plentiful.
  • Real-Life Example: In a practical sense, this mantra can be related to the idea of mentorship or guidance in one's career. For instance, a mentor who has achieved success can provide numerous opportunities (hundreds and thousands) to their mentee, helping them navigate their aspirations and goals.

  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its emphasis on the relationship between strength, wisdom, and abundance. It teaches us that those who are wise and have clear aspirations can receive immense support and resources. In real life, this can be applicable in various scenarios such as education, career development, and personal growth, where seeking guidance and support from knowledgeable individuals can lead to success and fulfillment.
