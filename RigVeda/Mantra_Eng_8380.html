आयं नो विद्वान् वनवद वनुष्यत इन्दुः सत्राच मनसा पुरुष्टुतः |इन्स्य यः सदने गर्भम् आदधे गवाम उरुजम् अभ्य अर्षति व्रजम् ||
ayaṁ no vidvān vanavad vanuṣyata induḥ satrācā manasā puruṣṭutaḥ | inasya yaḥ sadane garbham ādadhe gavām urubjam abhy arṣati vrajam ||
  • ayaṁ no vidvān: This phrase translates to "this knowledgeable one." It signifies the importance of wisdom and understanding in our lives.
  • vanavad vanuṣyata: This can be interpreted as "like a forest, may he flourish." It suggests the idea of growth and abundance, akin to a thriving forest ecosystem.
  • induḥ satrācā manasā puruṣṭutaḥ: Here, "induḥ" refers to the moon or a source of light, while "satrācā" implies a connection to the sacred or divine. This part emphasizes the illumination of the mind and spirit through knowledge and divine guidance.
  • inasya yaḥ sadane: This translates to "he who dwells in the abode of the divine." It highlights the importance of seeking a higher purpose and connection with the divine.
  • garbham ādadhe: This means "he who holds the womb." It symbolizes the nurturing aspect of creation and the importance of protecting and fostering life.
  • gavām urubjam: This phrase refers to "the vastness of cows," which in Vedic culture symbolizes wealth, nourishment, and prosperity.
  • abhy arṣati vrajam: This translates to "he who leads the herd." It signifies leadership, guidance, and the responsibility of taking care of others.

In conclusion, this mantra encapsulates the essence of wisdom, growth, divine connection, nurturing, prosperity, and leadership. It serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of knowledge and life, urging individuals to seek wisdom and guidance while nurturing their surroundings and communities. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as in leadership roles where one must guide and nurture their team, or in personal development where the pursuit of knowledge and understanding leads to a more fulfilling life. It emphasizes the significance of being a responsible and wise individual in society, contributing positively to the collective well-being.
