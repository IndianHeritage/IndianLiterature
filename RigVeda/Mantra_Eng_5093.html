यो वाम ऋजवे क्रमणाया रोडसी मार्तो ददाश धिषणे स साधति |प्र प्रजाभिर जायते धर्मणस परी युवोः सिक्तā विषुरूपाणि सव्रता ||
yo vām ṛjave kramaṇāya rodasī marto dadāśa dhiṣaṇe sa sādhati | pra prajābhir jāyate dharmaṇas pari yuvoḥ siktā viṣurūpāṇi savratā ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yo: who
    • vām: your
    • ṛjave: to the righteous
    • kramaṇāya: for the purpose of action
    • rodasī: in the two worlds (heaven and earth)
    • marto: the mortal
    • dadāśa: gives
    • dhiṣaṇe: to the one who is wise
    • sa: he
    • sādhati: accomplishes
    • pra: through
    • prajābhir: progeny
    • jāyate: is born
    • dharmaṇas: of righteousness
    • pari: around
    • yuvoḥ: of the young
    • siktā: sprinkled
    • viṣurūpāṇi: with various forms
    • savratā: with vows
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the connection between righteousness and action.
    • It emphasizes that those who act righteously in both worlds (heaven and earth) are rewarded.
    • The wise are those who understand the importance of righteousness and act accordingly.
    • It suggests that through righteous actions, one can create a legacy (progeny) that embodies these values.
    • The mention of "young" indicates the importance of instilling these values in the next generation.
    • The idea of being "sprinkled with various forms" suggests diversity in approaches to righteousness.
    • Finally, the term "with vows" indicates a commitment to these principles.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • A teacher who instills values of honesty and integrity in their students is acting in accordance with this mantra.
    • A community leader who promotes social justice and equality is embodying the principles of righteousness.
    • Parents who teach their children the importance of ethical behavior are ensuring that these values are passed on to future generations.
    • Organizations that operate with transparency and fairness reflect the essence of this mantra in their practices.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of righteousness in our actions and its impact on both our lives and the lives of others.
    • It serves as a reminder that our actions have consequences that extend beyond ourselves, influencing future generations.
    • In a world where ethical dilemmas are common, this mantra encourages individuals to act with integrity and purpose.
    • Ultimately, it calls for a commitment to righteousness, fostering a society that values ethical behavior and social responsibility.
