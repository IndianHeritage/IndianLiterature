महī dyāvāpṛthivī iha jyeṣṭhe rucā bhavatāṁ śucayadbhir arkaiḥ |yat sīṁ variṣṭhe bṛhatī viminvan ruvad dhokṣā paprathānebhir evaiḥ ||
mahī dyāvāpṛthivī iha jyeṣṭhe rucā bhavatāṁ śucayadbhir arkaiḥ | yat sīṁ variṣṭhe bṛhatī viminvan ruvad dhokṣā paprathānebhir evaiḥ ||
  • Translation: "O Earth and Sky, may your brilliance shine forth here, with the pure rays of light. Whatever is the most excellent and vast, may it be proclaimed with the powerful sounds of the sacred hymns."

  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • mahī: Earth
    • dyāvā: Sky or Heaven
    • pṛthivī: Another term for Earth
    • iha: Here
    • jyeṣṭhe: Most excellent or supreme
    • rucā: Brilliance or light
    • bhavatāṁ: May you become
    • śucayadbhir: With pure or sacred
    • arkaiḥ: Rays or light
    • yat: Whatever
    • sīṁ: Most excellent
    • variṣṭhe: Vast or great
    • bṛhatī: The great or vast
    • viminvan: Proclaimed or declared
    • ruvad: With powerful sounds
    • dhokṣā: Sacred hymns
    • paprathānebhir: With the sounds of proclamation
    • evaiḥ: Thus or in this way

  • Significance: This mantra emphasizes the connection between the Earth and the Sky, highlighting the importance of purity and brilliance in our lives. It calls for the manifestation of excellence and greatness through sacred sounds and hymns.

  • Real-Life Application:
    • This mantra can be applied in daily life by encouraging individuals to seek purity in their thoughts and actions, much like striving for excellence in personal and professional endeavors.
    • In a community setting, it can inspire collective efforts to promote harmony and brilliance, akin to how a team works together to achieve a common goal.
    • In spiritual practices, reciting this mantra can serve as a reminder to connect with the divine and seek enlightenment through sacred sounds and rituals.

  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the interconnectedness of the Earth and Sky, urging us to embrace purity and excellence in our lives. It encourages us to proclaim our aspirations and achievements through sacred expressions, fostering a sense of unity and purpose in our journey.
