एतं उ त्यं दश क्षिपो मृजन्ति सप्त धीतयः |स्वायुधम् मदिन्तमम् ||
etam u tyaṁ daśa kṣipo mṛjanti sapta dhītayaḥ | svāyudham madintamam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • etam: this
    • u: and
    • tyaṁ: that
    • daśa: ten
    • kṣipo: swift or quick
    • mṛjanti: they cleanse or purify
    • sapta: seven
    • dhītayaḥ: thoughts or intellects
    • svāyudham: armed with one's own weapons
    • madintamam: the most excellent or supreme
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the power of thought and intellect.
    • The phrase "ten swift ones" suggests that there are ten aspects or forces that act quickly in our lives.
    • The "seven thoughts" indicate the importance of having a well-rounded and diverse mindset.
    • "Armed with one's own weapons" implies that we should utilize our own strengths and skills to navigate challenges.
    • The term "the most excellent" emphasizes the pursuit of the highest ideals and excellence in our endeavors.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, a team may consist of ten members, each bringing unique skills (the swift ones) to achieve a common goal.
    • In personal development, one might cultivate seven different areas of intelligence, such as emotional, social, and analytical, to enhance overall effectiveness.
    • When facing challenges, individuals can draw upon their own experiences and skills (their weapons) to overcome obstacles.
    • Striving for excellence can be seen in athletes who train rigorously to achieve their personal best, embodying the essence of "the most excellent."
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of harnessing our thoughts and intellect to navigate life's challenges.
    • It encourages individuals to recognize their strengths and apply them effectively in various situations.
    • In a world filled with distractions and challenges, this mantra serves as a reminder to focus on personal growth and the pursuit of excellence.
    • Ultimately, it teaches us that by cleansing our thoughts and aligning them with our strengths, we can achieve greater success and fulfillment in life.
