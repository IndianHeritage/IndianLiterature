मन्त्र: दिवि मे अन्यः पक्षोऽधो अन्यं अचीकृषम् |कुवित सोमस्यापाम इति ||
divi me anyaḥ pakṣo3 'dho anyam acīkṛṣam | kuvit somasyāpām iti ||
  • Mantra: "divi me anyaḥ pakṣo3 'dho anyam acīkṛṣam | kuvit somasyāpām iti ||"
  • Translation: "In the heavens, there is another wing; below, there is another that is not black. Where is the nectar of the moon?"
  • Word-by-Word Explanation:
    • divi: in the heavens or sky
    • me: my
    • anyaḥ: another or different
    • pakṣo: wing
    • adhah: below or beneath
    • anyam: another
    • acīkṛṣam: not black or dark
    • kuvit: where
    • somasyāpām: of the moon's nectar
    • iti: thus or so
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of duality and the existence of different realms or states of being.
    • The "wing" can symbolize freedom, potential, or the ability to transcend limitations.
    • The reference to "not black" suggests a contrast between light and darkness, knowledge and ignorance.
    • The inquiry about the "nectar of the moon" can be interpreted as a quest for wisdom, enlightenment, or spiritual nourishment.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In personal development, individuals often seek to rise above their current circumstances (the "wing") and strive for a better understanding of themselves and the world around them.
    • In a corporate setting, a company may look for innovative solutions (the "nectar") that can lead to growth and success, contrasting with outdated practices (the "darkness").
    • In education, students are encouraged to explore different perspectives (the "another wing") to gain a comprehensive understanding of subjects, moving beyond rote learning (the "not black").
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking knowledge and understanding beyond the obvious or the conventional.
    • It encourages individuals to explore different dimensions of life, whether in personal growth, professional endeavors, or spiritual journeys.
    • Ultimately, it serves as a reminder that enlightenment and wisdom are attainable through exploration and the pursuit of higher truths.
