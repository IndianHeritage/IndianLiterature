ऊर्ध्वा धीतिः प्रत्यस्य प्रयामन्य अधायि शस्मन् सम अयन्त आ दिशः |स्वदामि घर्मम् प्रति यन्त्य ऊतय आ वाम ऊर्जानी रथम अश्विनारुहत् ||
ūrdhvā dhītiḥ praty asya prayāmany adhāyi śasman sam ayanta ā diśaḥ | svadāmi gharmam prati yanty ūtaya ā vām ūrjānī ratham aśvināruhat ||
  • ūrdhvā dhītiḥ: This phrase can be translated as "the intellect or understanding that is elevated or uplifted." It signifies the importance of having a higher level of consciousness and awareness in our thoughts and actions.
  • praty asya prayāmany: This means "in relation to this, the efforts or endeavors." It emphasizes the need for focused efforts in achieving one's goals and aspirations.
  • adhāyi śasman: This translates to "the one who is grounded or stable." It suggests the importance of being rooted in one's values and principles while pursuing ambitions.
  • sam ayanta ā diśaḥ: This phrase means "they who direct towards the right path." It indicates the guidance and support one receives from others in their journey.
  • svadāmi gharmam: This can be interpreted as "I enjoy the warmth or comfort." It reflects the idea of finding joy and contentment in one's surroundings and experiences.
  • prati yanty ūtaya: This means "they who move towards the beneficial." It highlights the importance of making choices that lead to positive outcomes and well-being.
  • ā vām ūrjānī: This translates to "to you, the energies or strengths." It signifies the acknowledgment of the power and potential within oneself and others.
  • ratham aśvināruhat: This phrase means "the chariot that is driven by the Ashvins." The Ashvins are twin deities associated with health and vitality, symbolizing the journey of life that is supported by divine forces.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of elevated understanding, focused efforts, and the importance of being grounded in one's values while seeking guidance. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios such as personal development, career growth, and relationships. By striving for higher awareness and making conscious choices, individuals can navigate their paths more effectively, leading to a fulfilling and meaningful life. The invocation of divine support, as represented by the Ashvins, reminds us that we are not alone in our journeys and that there are forces that aid us in our pursuits.
