विपूषन्नारयातुदपणेरिच्छहृदिप्रियम् |अथेमस्मभ्यंरन्धय ||
vi pūṣann ārayā tuda paṇer iccha hṛdi priyam | athem asmabhyaṁ randhaya ||
  • vi pūṣann: The term "vi" can be interpreted as "in many ways" or "in various forms," while "pūṣann" refers to the divine energy or nourishment. This can be seen as a call to the universal life force that sustains all beings.
  • ārayā: This word means "to protect" or "to guide." It signifies the need for divine guidance and protection in our lives.
  • tuda: This term can be understood as "to bestow" or "to grant." It emphasizes the act of giving or providing something valuable.
  • paṇer: This word translates to "of the wealth" or "of the riches." It highlights the importance of material and spiritual wealth in our lives.
  • iccha: Meaning "desire" or "wish," this term reflects the human tendency to aspire for various goals and aspirations.
  • hṛdi: This word means "in the heart." It signifies the emotional and spiritual center of a person, where true desires and intentions reside.
  • priyam: This translates to "beloved" or "dear." It indicates the things or people that we hold close to our hearts.
  • athem: This term can be interpreted as "here" or "in this place," suggesting a focus on the present moment and our current circumstances.
  • asmabhyaṁ: This means "to us" or "for us," indicating a collective aspect, emphasizing community and shared experiences.
  • randhaya: This word translates to "to fulfill" or "to complete." It signifies the act of bringing desires to fruition and achieving one's goals.

Conclusion: The mantra "vi pūṣann ārayā tuda paṇer iccha hṛdi priyam | athem asmabhyaṁ randhaya ||" encapsulates a profound message about the interconnectedness of life, the importance of divine guidance, and the fulfillment of desires. In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as seeking clarity in decision-making, nurturing relationships, and striving for personal and communal growth. It serves as a reminder to align our desires with our heart's true intentions and to seek the nourishment and protection of the divine in our endeavors. By embracing this mantra, individuals can cultivate a deeper understanding of their aspirations and the collective journey of life, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling existence.
