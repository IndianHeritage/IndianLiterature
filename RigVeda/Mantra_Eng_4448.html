नū नश् चित्रं पूर्ववाजाभिर् ऊती अग्ने रयिम् मघवद्भ्यश् च धेहि |ये राधसां श्रवसां चात्य अन्यान् सुवीर्येभिश् चाभि सन्ति जनान् ||
nū naś citram puruvājābhir ūtī agne rayim maghavadbhyaś ca dhehi | ye rādhasā śravasā cāty anyān suvīryebhiś cābhi santi janān ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • nū: indeed
    • naś: our
    • citram: wonderful
    • puruvājābhir: with the previous invocations
    • ūtī: blessings
    • agne: O Agni (the fire god)
    • rayim: wealth
    • maghavadbhyaś: to the powerful ones
    • ca: and
    • dhehi: grant
    • ye: those who
    • rādhasā: with prosperity
    • śravasā: with fame
    • cāty: and more
    • anyān: others
    • suvīryebhiś: with great strength
    • cābhi: and also
    • santi: exist
    • janān: people
  • Overall Meaning: This mantra is a prayer to Agni, the fire god, asking for blessings and wealth. It acknowledges the power of previous invocations and requests that Agni bestow prosperity and fame upon those who are strong and capable, as well as upon others.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be seen as a call for success and prosperity in ventures. Entrepreneurs often seek blessings for their endeavors, hoping to attract wealth and recognition.
    • In personal life, individuals may recite this mantra during significant life events, such as weddings or new beginnings, to invoke blessings for a prosperous and fulfilling life.
    • In community gatherings, this mantra can be used to inspire collective strength and unity, encouraging members to support one another in achieving common goals.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its invocation of divine blessings for prosperity, strength, and recognition. It emphasizes the importance of community and collective strength, reminding us that success is often a shared journey. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, from personal aspirations to communal efforts, highlighting the universal desire for growth and achievement. By seeking the blessings of Agni, individuals and communities can foster an environment conducive to success and well-being.
