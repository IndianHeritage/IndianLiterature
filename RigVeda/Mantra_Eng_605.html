त्वं कुत्सं शुष्णहत्येष्व आविथारन्धयो 'तिथिग्वाय शम्बरम् | महान्तं चिद् अर्बुदं नि क्रमीर् पदाऽसनाद एव दस्युहत्याय जजñिषे ||
tvaṁ kutsaṁ śuṣṇahatyeṣv āvithārandhayo 'tithigvāya śambaram | mahāntaṁ cid arbudaṁ ni kramīḥ padā sanād eva dasyuhatyāya jajñiṣe ||
  • tvaṁ - This word translates to "you," indicating a direct address to a divine entity or force.
  • kutsaṁ - Refers to "Kutsa," a sage or a divine figure, symbolizing wisdom and knowledge.
  • śuṣṇahatyeṣv - This can be broken down into "śuṣṇa," meaning "to burn" or "to destroy," and "hatyeṣv," which implies "in the enemies." Together, it suggests the destruction of adversaries or negativity.
  • āvithārandhayo - This term can be interpreted as "to cover" or "to protect," indicating a shield or safeguard against harm.
  • 'tithigvāya - This word relates to "Tithigva," which can be understood as a reference to a divine protector or guardian.
  • śambaram - Refers to "Shambhu," another name for Lord Shiva, symbolizing auspiciousness and benevolence.
  • mahāntaṁ - This means "great" or "magnificent," often used to describe something of immense power or significance.
  • cid - Translates to "consciousness" or "awareness," indicating a higher state of being or understanding.
  • arbudaṁ - This term can mean "a multitude" or "a vast number," suggesting abundance or a large presence.
  • ni kramīḥ - This phrase implies "do not step back" or "do not falter," encouraging perseverance and strength.
  • padā - Refers to "foot" or "path," symbolizing the journey or the way one should take in life.
  • sanād - This can be interpreted as "eternal" or "everlasting," indicating something that transcends time.
  • eva - Means "only" or "just," emphasizing the exclusivity of the statement.
  • dasyuhatyāya - This term relates to "the destruction of enemies," reinforcing the theme of overcoming obstacles.
  • jajñiṣe - This translates to "you have been born" or "you have manifested," indicating the emergence of a powerful force or entity.

Conclusion: This mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking protection and guidance from divine forces against adversities. It emphasizes the importance of wisdom, strength, and consciousness in overcoming challenges. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied when facing difficulties, whether personal or professional. By invoking the qualities represented in this mantra, individuals can find the courage to confront their fears and adversaries, ensuring they remain steadfast on their path. The significance lies in the belief that divine support is always available, encouraging individuals to seek higher consciousness and strength in their endeavors.
