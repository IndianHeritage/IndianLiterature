आ यस्मिन सप्त रश्मयस् तता यज्ञस्य नेतरी |मनुष्वद दैव्यम अष्टमम् पोतā विश्वं तद इन्बति ||
ā yasmin sapta raśmayas tatā yajñasya netari | manuṣvad daivyam aṣṭamam potā viśvaṁ tad invati ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā: from
    • yasmin: in which
    • sapta: seven
    • raśmayas: rays
    • tatā: thus
    • yajñasya: of the sacrifice
    • netari: the leader
    • manuṣvad: human-like
    • daivyam: divine
    • aṣṭamam: eighth
    • potā: the one who carries
    • viśvaṁ: the universe
    • tad: that
    • invati: leads or guides
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of a divine force that is connected to the concept of sacrifice (yajña) and the cosmic order.
    • The "seven rays" can be interpreted as the seven colors of light or the seven chakras in the human body, symbolizing completeness and harmony.
    • The "eighth" aspect refers to a higher, divine principle that transcends the human experience, suggesting a connection between the material and spiritual realms.
    • The phrase "human-like divine" indicates that this divine force is accessible and relatable to humans, bridging the gap between the earthly and the celestial.
    • The mantra emphasizes the importance of sacrifice and the role it plays in maintaining the balance of the universe.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern life, the concept of sacrifice can be seen in acts of charity, where individuals give up their resources for the benefit of others, reflecting the essence of yajña.
    • The idea of "seven rays" can be related to the diversity of cultures and beliefs that coexist in society, promoting harmony and understanding among different communities.
    • In personal development, the journey of self-improvement can be likened to the alignment of the seven chakras, leading to a more balanced and fulfilling life.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the interconnectedness of all beings and the importance of sacrifice in achieving harmony in the universe.
    • It serves as a reminder that our actions, whether in personal growth or community service, contribute to the greater cosmic order.
    • By understanding and applying the principles of this mantra, individuals can foster a sense of purpose and connection in their lives, ultimately leading to a more harmonious existence.
