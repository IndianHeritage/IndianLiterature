प्र त्वा दूतं वृणीमहे होतारं विश्ववेदसम् |महस ते सतो वि चरन्त्यर्चयो दिवि स्पृशन्ति भानवः ||
pra tvā dūtaṁ vṛṇīmahe hotāraṁ viśvavedasam | mahas te sato vi caranty arcayo divi spṛśanti bhānavaḥ ||
  • pra tvā dūtaṁ vṛṇīmahe: This phrase translates to "We choose you, O messenger." In this context, the term "dūta" refers to a messenger or envoy, symbolizing the importance of communication and the role of intermediaries in conveying messages between the divine and humanity. In real life, this can be likened to the role of leaders or representatives who communicate the needs and aspirations of their communities to higher authorities.
  • hotāraṁ viśvavedasam: This means "the one who is the priest of all knowledge." The term "hotā" refers to a priest or one who offers sacrifices, while "viśvavedasam" signifies the embodiment of universal knowledge. This highlights the significance of wisdom and knowledge in guiding actions and decisions. For example, educators and mentors in our lives serve as "hotāraṁ," imparting knowledge that shapes our understanding of the world.
  • mahas te sato vi caranty: This translates to "the great ones move in truth." Here, "mahas" refers to greatness or the divine, and "sato" signifies truth. This line emphasizes the importance of integrity and truthfulness in our actions. In everyday life, this can be seen in the actions of individuals who stand by their principles, such as whistleblowers who expose wrongdoing for the greater good.
  • arcayo divi spṛśanti bhānavaḥ: This means "the worshippers touch the divine light." "Arcayo" refers to worshippers or those who offer reverence, "divi" means divine or heavenly, and "bhānavaḥ" signifies light or radiance. This line illustrates the connection between human devotion and the divine. In real life, this can be observed in various forms of worship, where individuals seek to connect with a higher power through rituals, prayers, or acts of kindness.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of communication with the divine, the pursuit of knowledge, the importance of truth, and the connection between worshippers and the divine light. Its significance lies in reminding us of our roles as messengers and seekers of truth, encouraging us to act with integrity and reverence in our daily lives. This mantra can be applied in various real-life scenarios, such as in leadership, education, and spiritual practices, guiding individuals to foster a deeper connection with themselves, their communities, and the divine.
