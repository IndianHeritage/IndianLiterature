तं युवं देवाव अश्विनां कुमारं साहदेव्यं |दीर्घायुषं कृणोतन ||
taṁ yuvaṁ devāv aśvinā kumāraṁ sāhadevyam | dīrghāyuṣaṁ kṛṇotana ||
  • taṁ - This word translates to "that" or "him," referring to the subject of the mantra, which is a young divine being.
  • yuvaṁ - Meaning "young" or "youthful," it emphasizes the vitality and energy associated with youth.
  • devāv - This means "gods," indicating the divine nature of the beings being referred to.
  • aśvinā - Referring to the Aśvins, the twin gods of medicine and health in Vedic mythology, symbolizing healing and well-being.
  • kumāraṁ - This translates to "young one" or "youth," reinforcing the idea of a youthful divine presence.
  • sāhadevyam - This term can be interpreted as "with the help of the divine," suggesting a connection to divine assistance or blessings.
  • dīrghāyuṣaṁ - Meaning "long life," it expresses a desire for longevity and vitality.
  • kṛṇotana - This translates to "may he grant" or "may he bestow," indicating a wish or prayer for the blessings mentioned.

The mantra can be understood as a prayer or invocation to the youthful divine beings, specifically the Aśvins, to grant long life and vitality. It reflects a deep reverence for the divine and a desire for health and longevity, which are essential aspects of human existence.


In real life, this mantra can be applicable in various scenarios:

  • Health and Wellness: Individuals may recite this mantra during health rituals or when seeking recovery from illness, invoking the Aśvins for their healing powers.
  • Celebrating Milestones: During birthdays or significant life events, this mantra can be chanted to bless the individual with a long and prosperous life.
  • Spiritual Practices: In meditation or yoga sessions, practitioners may use this mantra to focus on the themes of youthfulness and vitality, enhancing their spiritual connection.

In conclusion, this mantra signifies the importance of divine blessings for health and longevity. It serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness between the divine and human experiences, encouraging individuals to seek support from higher powers in their journey of life. By invoking the Aśvins, one acknowledges the significance of health and vitality, which are crucial for a fulfilling life.
