आ नो गव्येभिर अश्व्यैः वसव्यैः उप गच्छताम् |सखायौ देवौ सख्याय शम्भुवेन्द्राग्नीतां हवामहे ||
ā no gavyebhir aśvyair vasavyai3r upa gacchatam | sakhāyau devau sakhyāya śambhuvendrāgnī tā havāmahe ||
  • Mantra: "ā no gavyebhir aśvyair vasavyai3r upa gacchatam | sakhāyau devau sakhyāya śambhuvendrāgnī tā havāmahe ||"
  • Translation: "May we be led by the divine forces, by the auspicious and powerful energies, towards prosperity and harmony. We invoke the divine companions, the deities, for friendship and support, especially Agni, the fire god, who is the embodiment of transformation and purity."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • ā no: "May we" - a call for guidance and support.
    • gavyebhir: "by the auspicious" - referring to positive energies or forces.
    • aśvyair: "by the powerful" - invoking strength and vigor.
    • vasavyai3r: "towards prosperity" - a desire for abundance and well-being.
    • upa gacchatam: "may we be led" - a plea for direction and guidance.
    • sakhāyau: "companions" - referring to allies or friends.
    • devau: "deities" - invoking divine presence.
    • sakhyāya: "for friendship" - emphasizing the importance of camaraderie.
    • śambhuvendrāgnī: "Agni, the fire god" - representing transformation, purification, and energy.
    • tā havāmahe: "we invoke" - a call to action, seeking the presence of the divine.

  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, this mantra can be applied when teams come together for a project. The invocation of divine support can symbolize the need for collaboration and shared goals, leading to successful outcomes.
    • In personal relationships, the essence of friendship and support can be seen in how individuals come together during challenging times, invoking the spirit of unity and strength.
    • During rituals or ceremonies, such as weddings or housewarming events, this mantra can be recited to seek blessings for harmony and prosperity in the new journey.

  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine guidance and the importance of friendship and collaboration in achieving prosperity. In real-life scenarios, whether in personal relationships, professional environments, or communal gatherings, the principles of unity, support, and the invocation of positive energies can lead to transformative experiences and successful outcomes. By embracing these values, individuals and communities can foster a sense of belonging and shared purpose, ultimately leading to a more harmonious existence.
