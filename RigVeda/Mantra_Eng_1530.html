आद अस्य ते ध्वसयन्तो वृथेरते कृष्णम् अभ्वम् महि वर्पः करिकृतः |यत सिम महीम अवनिम प्राभि मर्मृशद अभिश्वसन स्तनयन्न एति नानदत ||
ād asya te dhvasayanto vṛtherate kṛṣṇam abhvam mahi varpaḥ karikrataḥ | yat sīm mahīm avanim prābhi marmṛśad abhiśvasan stanayann eti nānadat ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ād: From
    • asya: This
    • te: Your
    • dhvasayanto: Destroying
    • vṛtherate: Of the powerful
    • kṛṣṇam: Black or dark (often referring to Lord Krishna)
    • abhvam: Non-existent or absent
    • mahi: Great
    • varpaḥ: Abode or dwelling
    • karikrataḥ: Having been made or created
    • yat: That
    • sīm: Boundary or limit
    • mahīm: Earth or the vastness
    • avanim: Earth
    • prābhi: With light or brilliance
    • marmṛśad: Touching or affecting
    • abhiśvasan: Trusting or relying upon
    • stanayann: Nourishing or sustaining
    • eti: Goes or moves
    • nānadat: Not rejoicing or not celebrating
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the divine power that can destroy negativity and darkness.
    • It emphasizes the importance of trust and reliance on the divine for sustenance and support.
    • The reference to "kṛṣṇam" suggests a connection to Lord Krishna, who embodies love, compassion, and guidance.
    • The mantra also highlights the vastness of the earth and the importance of boundaries, both physical and spiritual.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In times of personal crisis, individuals often turn to spiritual practices or prayers, similar to the reliance on divine support mentioned in the mantra.
    • Communities facing challenges may come together, trusting in a higher power to guide them through difficulties, reflecting the essence of the mantra.
    • Environmental movements emphasize the importance of caring for the earth, resonating with the mantra's reference to the vastness of the earth and our responsibility towards it.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the power of faith and trust in the divine, especially during challenging times.
    • It encourages individuals to seek guidance and support from a higher power, fostering a sense of community and shared purpose.
    • In a broader context, it highlights the importance of environmental stewardship and the interconnectedness of all beings.
