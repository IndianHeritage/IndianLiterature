एना मंदानो जहि शूर शत्रूञ जामिम अजामिम मगवन्न अमित्रान् | अभिषेणाम् अभ्य आ3देदिशानान् पराच इन्द्र प्र मृणां जही च ||
enā mandāno jahi śūra śatrūñ jāmim ajāmim maghavann amitrān | abhiṣeṇām̐ abhy ā3dediśānān parāca indra pra mṛṇā jahī ca ||
  • Mantra: "enā mandāno jahi śūra śatrūñ jāmim ajāmim maghavann amitrān | abhiṣeṇām̐ abhy ā3dediśānān parāca indra pra mṛṇā jahī ca ||"
  • Translation: This mantra can be interpreted as a call to the divine for strength and protection against adversaries. It invokes Indra, the king of gods, to help overcome obstacles and enemies.
  • Word Breakdown:
    • enā: with this
    • mandāno: weak or feeble
    • jahi: conquer or defeat
    • śūra: brave or valiant
    • śatrūñ: enemies
    • jāmim: born or produced
    • ajāmim: unborn or eternal
    • maghavann: powerful or mighty
    • amitrān: foes or adversaries
    • abhiṣeṇām̐: with blessings or anointing
    • abhy: towards or in the direction of
    • ā3dediśānān: the directions or realms
    • parāca: beyond or superior
    • indra: the king of gods
    • pra: forth or forward
    • mṛṇā: to kill or to slay
    • jahī: let go or abandon
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a competitive work environment, individuals often face challenges and adversaries. This mantra can serve as a reminder to seek inner strength and resilience to overcome these challenges.
    • In personal conflicts, invoking the spirit of bravery and seeking divine assistance can help individuals navigate through difficult relationships or situations.
    • During times of societal unrest or conflict, communities can come together, reciting this mantra to invoke unity and strength against common adversaries.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine intervention and strength in the face of adversity. It emphasizes the importance of courage and resilience, encouraging individuals to confront their fears and challenges head-on. In real-life scenarios, whether in personal struggles, professional challenges, or societal issues, this mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the strength that can be drawn from faith and the collective spirit of bravery. By invoking the blessings of Indra, individuals can find the courage to overcome obstacles and emerge victorious in their endeavors.
