ऋतस्य जिव्हा पवते मधु प्रियं वक्ता पतिर्हियोऽस्य अदाभ्यः |दधाति पुत्रः पितरोर अपिच्यं नाम तृतीयं अधि रोचने दिवः ||
ṛtasya jihvā pavate madhu priyaṁ vaktā patir dhiyo asyā adābhyaḥ | dadhāti putraḥ pitror apīcya1ṁ nāma tṛtīyam adhi rocane divaḥ ||
  • ṛtasya jihvā: This phrase translates to "the tongue of truth." In this context, it emphasizes the importance of speaking truthfully and the power of words. For example, in a courtroom, the truth spoken by witnesses can significantly impact the outcome of a case.
  • pavate madhu priyaṁ: This means "the sweet winds blow." It symbolizes the pleasant and beneficial aspects of nature. In real life, this can be likened to the refreshing feeling of a gentle breeze on a hot day, which brings comfort and joy.
  • vaktā patir dhiyo asyā adābhyaḥ: This translates to "the speaker is the lord of intellect." It highlights the idea that those who communicate effectively and wisely hold a position of authority and respect. For instance, a leader who articulates a vision clearly can inspire and mobilize people towards a common goal.
  • dadhāti putraḥ pitror apīcyaṁ: This means "the son supports the father." It signifies the importance of familial bonds and responsibilities. In modern society, this can be seen in the way children care for their aging parents, ensuring their well-being and honoring their sacrifices.
  • nāma tṛtīyam adhi rocane divaḥ: This translates to "the third name shines in the heavens." It suggests that there is a higher truth or understanding that transcends ordinary perception. In real life, this can be related to the pursuit of knowledge and enlightenment, where individuals seek deeper meanings in life beyond material existence.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of truth, effective communication, familial responsibilities, and the pursuit of higher knowledge. In real life, these principles can guide individuals in their personal and professional lives, fostering integrity, compassion, and a quest for understanding. By embodying these values, one can contribute positively to society and create harmonious relationships with others.
