येनादशग्वमध्रिगुं वेपयन्तं स्वर्णरम् |येनासमुद्रमाविधा तमीमहे ||
yenā daśagvam adhriguṁ vepayantaṁ svarṇaram | yenā samudram āvithā tam īmahe ||
  • Mantra: "yenā daśagvam adhriguṁ vepayantaṁ svarṇaram |
  • Translation: "By whom the tenfold (or the ten) are made to tremble, the golden (or the radiant) one; by whom we invoke the covered ocean."

  • Word Breakdown:
    • yenā: By whom
    • daśagvam: The tenfold or ten (often interpreted as the ten directions or aspects of existence)
    • adhriguṁ: Made to tremble or shake
    • vepayantaṁ: Shaking or trembling
    • svarṇaram: The golden or radiant one (often referring to the divine or the sun)
    • samudram: Ocean
    • āvithā: Covered or concealed
    • tam: That
    • īmahe: We invoke or we call upon

  • Meaning and Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks to the power of a divine force that can influence the fundamental aspects of existence, represented by the "tenfold." This can be seen as a metaphor for the various dimensions of life, including physical, emotional, and spiritual realms.
    • The "trembling" of the tenfold signifies the impact of this divine force on the universe, suggesting that it has the ability to instigate change and movement in all aspects of life.
    • The reference to the "golden one" can be interpreted as the sun or a divine entity that brings light, warmth, and life, emphasizing the importance of this force in sustaining existence.
    • The invocation of the "covered ocean" symbolizes the depths of the unconscious or the unknown, suggesting that this divine force also has the power to reveal hidden truths and insights.

  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a personal context, when individuals face challenges or uncertainties, invoking a higher power or seeking guidance can lead to transformative experiences, akin to the "trembling" of the tenfold.
    • In nature, the sun's rays penetrate the ocean's surface, revealing the vibrant life beneath, which parallels the idea of uncovering hidden knowledge or potential.
    • In a community setting, collective efforts to address social issues can be seen as invoking a greater force for change, leading to significant shifts in societal dynamics.

  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of divine influence in our lives, encouraging individuals to recognize and invoke the power that can lead to transformation and enlightenment.
    • It serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of all aspects of existence and the potential for change when we align ourselves with a higher purpose.
    • In practical terms, this mantra can be applied in daily life through meditation, prayer, or reflection, fostering a deeper connection with the universe and the forces that shape our reality.
