अमाद एषाम भियसां भूमिर एजति नौर न पूर्णा क्षरति व्यथिर यती |दूरदृष्टो ये चितयन्ता एम्बभिर अन्तर महे विदथे यतिरे नरः ||
amād eṣām bhiyasā bhūmir ejati naur na pūrṇā kṣarati vyathir yatī | dūredṛśo ye citayanta emabhir antar mahe vidathe yetire naraḥ ||
  • amād eṣām: This phrase can be interpreted as "the essence of these" or "the nature of these." It suggests a focus on understanding the fundamental qualities of existence.
  • bhiyasā: This word means "with great effort" or "with intensity." It emphasizes the importance of striving and putting in effort to achieve one's goals.
  • bhūmir ejati: Translated as "the earth moves" or "the ground is active," this part signifies the dynamic nature of the world and the constant changes that occur in life.
  • naur na pūrṇā: This translates to "the boat is not full." It symbolizes incompleteness or the idea that one must continue to strive for fulfillment and understanding.
  • kṣarati vyathir yatī: This can be interpreted as "the one who is troubled or distressed." It highlights the struggles and challenges that individuals face in their journey.
  • dūredṛśo: Meaning "those who see from afar," this phrase refers to individuals who have a broader perspective or insight into life.
  • ye citayanta: This translates to "those who contemplate" or "those who think deeply." It emphasizes the importance of reflection and thoughtfulness in understanding life.
  • emabhir: This word means "with these" or "with such things," indicating the tools or means available for contemplation and understanding.
  • antar mahe: Translated as "within the great," this suggests that profound understanding and wisdom lie within the depths of existence.
  • vidathe yetire naraḥ: This phrase means "the people who strive in the great assembly." It signifies the collective effort of humanity in seeking knowledge and truth.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of understanding the essence of life, the need for effort in our pursuits, and the dynamic nature of existence. It encourages individuals to reflect deeply and strive for fulfillment, recognizing that challenges are a part of the journey. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as personal growth, education, and community efforts. It serves as a reminder that through contemplation and collective striving, individuals can achieve greater understanding and contribute positively to society.
