एतावतस्ते वसो विद्यामा शूर नव्यसः |यथा प्राव एतशं कृत्व्ये धनं यथा वशं दशव्रजे ||
etāvatas te vaso vidyāma śūra navyasaḥ | yathā prāva etaśaṁ kṛtvye dhane yathā vaśaṁ daśavraje ||
  • etāvatas: This term can be understood as "of this much" or "to this extent." It signifies a measure or limit.
  • te: This means "your" or "of you," indicating possession or relation.
  • vaso: This word translates to "wealth" or "prosperity," referring to material and spiritual abundance.
  • vidyāma: This means "we know" or "we understand," indicating knowledge or wisdom.
  • śūra: This term translates to "brave" or "valiant," referring to courage and strength.
  • navyasaḥ: This can be interpreted as "new" or "fresh," suggesting innovation or renewal.
  • yathā: This means "as" or "in the way that," indicating a comparison or manner.
  • prāva: This term can mean "to achieve" or "to attain," suggesting accomplishment.
  • etaśaṁ: This translates to "this" or "these," referring to something specific.
  • kṛtvye: This means "to do" or "to perform," indicating action or effort.
  • dhane: This translates to "wealth" or "riches," similar to "vaso," emphasizing material prosperity.
  • yathā: Again, this means "as" or "in the way that," reinforcing the comparative aspect.
  • vaśaṁ: This means "control" or "influence," indicating mastery over circumstances.
  • daśavraje: This term can be interpreted as "in the tenfold path" or "in the realm of ten," suggesting a comprehensive approach or a complete system.

The mantra essentially speaks about the extent of knowledge and bravery that leads to prosperity and control over wealth. It emphasizes that with the right understanding and courage, one can achieve success and mastery in life.


In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts:

  • Education and Knowledge: The importance of acquiring knowledge and understanding in order to navigate life's challenges effectively. For example, a student who dedicates time to study and understand their subjects will likely perform better in exams and future endeavors.
  • Entrepreneurship: The mantra highlights the need for courage and innovation in business. An entrepreneur who is brave enough to take risks and innovate can create successful ventures, much like how startups disrupt traditional industries.
  • Personal Development: It encourages individuals to take action towards their goals. For instance, someone looking to improve their health must take actionable steps, such as exercising and eating well, to achieve their desired outcome.

Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its holistic approach to life, emphasizing the interplay between knowledge, courage, and prosperity. It serves as a reminder that with the right mindset and actions, one can achieve mastery over their circumstances and attain success in various aspects of life.
