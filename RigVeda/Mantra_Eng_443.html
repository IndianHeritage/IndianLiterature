ये पृषतीभिर ऋष्टिभिः साकं वाशीभिर अञ्जिभिः |अजायन्त स्वभानवः ||
ye pṛṣatībhir ṛṣṭibhiḥ sākaṁ vāśībhir añjibhiḥ | ajāyanta svabhānavaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ye: those who
    • pṛṣatībhir: with the powers of the mind
    • ṛṣṭibhiḥ: with the sacred utterances
    • sākaṁ: together
    • vāśībhir: with the controlling forces
    • añjibhiḥ: with the powers of action
    • ajāyanta: they were born
    • svabhānavaḥ: of their own nature
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the collective power of individuals who harness their mental faculties and sacred knowledge.
    • The term "pṛṣatībhir" suggests that the mind's capabilities are crucial in achieving goals.
    • "ṛṣṭibhiḥ" indicates that sacred utterances or mantras have a significant role in shaping reality.
    • The phrase "sākaṁ vāśībhir añjibhiḥ" emphasizes the importance of collaboration and control over one's actions and thoughts.
    • Finally, "ajāyanta svabhānavaḥ" implies that these individuals manifest their true nature through their combined efforts and powers.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a team project, individuals bring their unique skills and knowledge together, much like the mantra suggests. For instance, in a software development team, programmers, designers, and project managers collaborate to create a successful product.
    • In personal development, individuals often use affirmations (sacred utterances) to shape their mindset and achieve their goals, reflecting the power of "ṛṣṭibhiḥ."
    • In community service, people come together (sākaṁ) to address social issues, demonstrating the collective strength and control over their actions to create positive change.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of collective effort, mental power, and sacred knowledge in achieving one's true potential.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, such as teamwork, personal growth, and community initiatives, where collaboration and focused action lead to meaningful outcomes.
    • Ultimately, it teaches us that by harnessing our individual strengths and working together, we can manifest our true nature and achieve greater goals.
