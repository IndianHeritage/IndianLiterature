त्वं अग्नि प्रमतिस् त्वं पिताऽसि नस्त्वं वयस्कृत तव जामयो वयं |सं त्वा रायः शतिनः सं सहस्रिणः सुवीरं यन्ति व्रतपाम अदाभ्यः ||
tvam agne pramatis tvam pitāsi nas tvaṁ vayaskṛt tava jāmayo vayam | saṁ tvā rāyaḥ śatinaḥ saṁ sahasriṇaḥ suvīraṁ yanti vratapām adābhya ||
  • tvam agne - "You, O Agni" - This refers to Agni, the fire god, who is considered a divine messenger and a symbol of purity and transformation.
  • pramatis - "the one who is to be praised" - This highlights the importance of Agni as a deity worthy of reverence and worship.
  • tvam pitāsi - "You are the father" - This signifies Agni's role as a nurturing and protective force, akin to a father figure in our lives.
  • nas tvaṁ vayaskṛt - "You are our strength" - This emphasizes the idea that Agni provides us with the energy and vitality needed to thrive.
  • tava jāmayo vayam - "We are your offspring" - This suggests a deep connection between humans and Agni, indicating that we are all part of a larger cosmic family.
  • saṁ tvā rāyaḥ - "Together with you, we seek prosperity" - This line expresses the desire for abundance and success in life, with Agni as a guiding force.
  • śatinaḥ - "of the wealthy" - This refers to the blessings of wealth and prosperity that come from aligning oneself with divine energies.
  • saṁ sahasriṇaḥ - "together with thousands" - This indicates the collective effort of the community in seeking blessings from Agni.
  • suvīraṁ - "the strong and valiant" - This highlights the qualities of courage and strength that Agni embodies and inspires in us.
  • yanti vratapām adābhya - "they who follow the vows and are free from sin" - This emphasizes the importance of living a righteous life, adhering to one's commitments, and seeking purity in thoughts and actions.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of Agni as a divine force that nurtures, protects, and empowers individuals and communities. It signifies the importance of seeking strength, prosperity, and righteousness in our lives. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as during rituals, prayers, or personal reflections, where individuals seek guidance and blessings for their endeavors. By invoking Agni, one can aspire to cultivate virtues like courage, integrity, and a sense of community, ultimately leading to a fulfilling and prosperous life.
