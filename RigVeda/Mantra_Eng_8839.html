सं यस्मिन विश्वा वसूनि जग्मुर वाजे नाश्वाः सप्तिवन्त एविः |अस्मे ऊतीर इन्द्रवाततमā arvācīnā अग्न आ कृणुष्व ||
saṁ yasmin viśvā vasūni jagmur vāje nāśvāḥ saptīvanta evaiḥ | asme ūtīr indravātatamā arvācīnā agna ā kṛṇuṣva ||
  • saṁ yasmin - "In whom" or "In which": This phrase indicates a connection or a relationship with a higher power or divine entity.
  • viśvā vasūni - "All the treasures" or "All the wealth": This refers to the abundance and prosperity that exists in the universe.
  • jagmur - "Have come" or "Have arrived": This signifies the arrival or manifestation of these treasures in our lives.
  • vāje - "In strength" or "In power": This word emphasizes the strength and vigor associated with the treasures mentioned.
  • nāśvāḥ - "The horses" or "The steeds": Horses are often symbolic of speed, power, and energy, representing the dynamic forces in life.
  • saptīvanta - "Sevenfold" or "Seven": This number often symbolizes completeness and perfection in Vedic literature.
  • evaiḥ - "Indeed" or "Certainly": This word adds emphasis to the preceding statements.
  • asme - "For us" or "To us": This indicates a personal connection, suggesting that the blessings are meant for the speaker and their community.
  • ūtīr - "Bestow" or "Grant": A call for divine intervention to provide blessings.
  • indravātatamā - "The best of Indra's winds": This phrase invokes the power of Indra, the king of gods, and his winds, which symbolize strength and protection.
  • arvācīnā - "Recent" or "New": This suggests a freshness or renewal in the blessings being sought.
  • agna - "O Fire" or "O Agni": Agni is the fire god, representing transformation, energy, and the divine spark within.
  • ā kṛṇuṣva - "Make it happen" or "Bring it forth": A plea for the divine to manifest the desired outcomes.

Conclusion: This mantra is a powerful invocation for abundance, strength, and divine blessings. It emphasizes the interconnectedness of all treasures in the universe and calls upon divine forces to bestow these blessings upon us. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios such as seeking success in endeavors, invoking prosperity in business, or even during personal challenges where one seeks strength and support. The mantra serves as a reminder of the abundance available to us and encourages us to connect with the divine to manifest our desires. It highlights the importance of faith and the belief that we can attract positive energies and outcomes in our lives.
