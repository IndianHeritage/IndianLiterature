उद व उर्मिः शम्याः हन्त्व आपो योक्त्राणि मुंचत |मादुष्कृतौ व्येनसाघ्न्यौ शूनं आरताम् ||
ud va ūrmiḥ śamyā hantv āpo yoktrāṇi muñcata | māduṣkṛtau vyenasāghnyau śūnam āratām ||
  • ud va ūrmiḥ: This phrase can be translated as "rise up, O waves." Here, "ud" means "up" or "rise," and "ūrmiḥ" refers to "waves" or "currents." This can symbolize the idea of overcoming obstacles or challenges in life, much like how waves rise and fall in the ocean.
  • śamyā hantv āpo: This translates to "may the waters, like the calmness of the shami tree, be pacified." The "śamyā" refers to the shami tree, which is known for its stability and strength. "Hantv" means "to pacify," and "āpo" refers to "waters." This suggests a desire for tranquility and peace, akin to the calmness one seeks in turbulent times.
  • yoktrāṇi muñcata: This means "release the bindings." "Yoktrāṇi" refers to "bindings" or "ties," and "muñcata" means "to release." This can be interpreted as a call to let go of the things that hold us back, whether they are fears, doubts, or negative influences.
  • māduṣkṛtau vyenasāghnyau: This phrase translates to "may the sweet actions be victorious." "Māduṣkṛtau" refers to "sweet actions" or "good deeds," while "vyenasāghnyau" means "may they be victorious." This emphasizes the importance of positive actions and their ability to triumph over negativity.
  • śūnam āratām: This translates to "may the void be filled." "Śūnam" means "void" or "emptiness," and "āratām" refers to "to be filled." This suggests a desire for fulfillment and completeness in life, encouraging individuals to seek purpose and meaning.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a profound message about overcoming challenges, seeking peace, releasing burdens, promoting positive actions, and striving for fulfillment. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios such as personal growth, conflict resolution, and community building. For instance, when faced with difficulties, one can remember to rise above them like waves, seek tranquility in stressful situations, and let go of negative influences. By focusing on good deeds and striving for a sense of purpose, individuals can create a more harmonious and fulfilling life. This mantra serves as a reminder of the power of resilience and positivity in navigating the complexities of existence.
