वधीद इन्द्रो वरशिखस्य शेषोऽभ्यावर्तिने चायमानाय शिक्षन् |वृचिवतो यद धरीयूपीयायं हन् पूर्वे अर्धे भियसापरो दर्त ||
vadhīd indro varaśikhasya śeṣo 'bhyāvartine cāyamānāya śikṣan | vṛcīvato yad dhariyūpīyāyāṁ han pūrve ardhe bhiyasāparo dart ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • vadhīd: May he (Indra) protect or guide.
    • indro: Indra, the king of gods, associated with strength and rain.
    • varaśikhasya: Of the one who possesses the best or the most excellent.
    • śeṣo: The remaining or the leftover.
    • 'bhyāvartine: To the one who turns or revolves.
    • cāyamānāya: To the one who is being invoked or called upon.
    • śikṣan: For the purpose of teaching or instruction.
    • vṛcīvato: Of the one who is strong or powerful.
    • yad: That which.
    • dhariyūpīyāyāṁ: In the context of holding or bearing.
    • han: To strike or to defeat.
    • pūrve: In the past or earlier.
    • ardhe: In the half or part.
    • bhiyasāparo: One who is capable of overcoming fear or obstacles.
    • dart: To conquer or to win.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes Indra, the powerful deity, asking for his guidance and protection.
    • It emphasizes the importance of learning and instruction, suggesting that knowledge is a powerful tool in overcoming challenges.
    • The reference to overcoming fear and conquering obstacles highlights the need for courage and strength in facing life's difficulties.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a student's life, this mantra can be applied when facing exams or challenges in education. Seeking guidance and knowledge can help them overcome their fears and perform better.
    • In a professional setting, employees may invoke this mantra when dealing with difficult projects or tasks, reminding themselves to seek knowledge and support from colleagues to succeed.
    • In personal life, individuals facing hardships can reflect on this mantra to find strength and courage to overcome their fears and challenges.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of divine guidance, the pursuit of knowledge, and the strength to overcome obstacles.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, encouraging individuals to seek wisdom and support in their endeavors.
    • Ultimately, it serves as a reminder that with the right guidance and knowledge, one can conquer fears and achieve success.
