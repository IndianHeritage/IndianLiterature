इन्द्र पिब प्रतिकामं सुतस्य प्रातःसावस तव हि पूर्वपीतिः |हर्षस्व हन्तवे शूर शत्रून उक्तेभिष ṭे वीर्याः प्र ब्रवाम ||
indra piba pratikāmaṁ sutasya prātaḥsāvas tava hi pūrvapītiḥ | harṣasva hantave śūra śatrūn ukthebhiṣ ṭe vīryā3 pra bravāma ||
  • Indra: Refers to the king of the gods in Vedic tradition, symbolizing strength, power, and leadership.
  • Piba: Means "drink," suggesting the act of consuming or absorbing something beneficial.
  • Pratikāmaṁ: Translates to "with desire" or "with intention," indicating a purposeful action.
  • Sutasya: Refers to "the son" or "the offspring," often symbolizing the fruits of one's labor or efforts.
  • Prātaḥsāvas: Means "in the morning," symbolizing new beginnings, freshness, and the start of a new day.
  • Tava hi pūrvapītiḥ: Translates to "for you is the ancient drink," suggesting a connection to tradition and the wisdom of the past.
  • Harṣasva: Means "rejoice" or "be joyful," encouraging a positive and uplifting attitude.
  • Hantave: Refers to "to the slayer" or "to the conqueror," indicating overcoming challenges or adversaries.
  • Śūra: Means "brave" or "valiant," highlighting the importance of courage in facing difficulties.
  • Śatrūn: Refers to "enemies" or "opponents," representing obstacles in life.
  • Ukthebhiṣ: Means "by the words spoken," indicating the power of speech and intention.
  • Ṭe vīryā3 pra bravāma: Translates to "we proclaim your strength," emphasizing the importance of recognizing and celebrating one's own power and capabilities.

Conclusion: This mantra emphasizes the importance of drawing strength from tradition and the wisdom of the past while facing challenges in life. It encourages individuals to embrace new beginnings with joy and courage, recognizing their own power to overcome obstacles. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various contexts such as personal growth, leadership, and teamwork. For instance, in a corporate setting, a leader can inspire their team by invoking the spirit of Indra, encouraging them to face challenges with bravery and a positive mindset, ultimately leading to success and victory over competitors.
