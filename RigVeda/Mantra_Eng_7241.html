मन्त्र: आर्चत प्राऽर्चत प्रियमेधासो आर्चत |आर्चन्तु पुत्रका उता पुरं न धृष्ण्व आर्चत ||
arcata prārcata priyamedhāso arcata | arcantu putrakā uta puraṁ na dhṛṣṇv arcata ||
  • arcata: This term can be understood as "to worship" or "to offer." It signifies the act of devotion and reverence towards the divine.
  • prārcata: This word means "to invoke" or "to call upon." It emphasizes the importance of seeking the presence and blessings of the divine in our lives.
  • priyamedhāso: This term refers to "those who have a loving and wise mind." It highlights the qualities of affection and intelligence in our thoughts and actions.
  • putrakā: This word translates to "sons" or "children." It signifies the importance of family and the next generation in the context of blessings and prosperity.
  • uta: This is a conjunction meaning "and" or "also," connecting thoughts and ideas within the mantra.
  • puraṁ: This term means "the city" or "the dwelling place." It can symbolize the community or society we live in.
  • na dhṛṣṇv: This phrase translates to "without fear" or "without hesitation." It encourages confidence and courage in our actions.

The mantra essentially calls for worship and invocation of the divine, particularly focusing on those who possess a loving and wise mind. It emphasizes the importance of blessings for children and the community, urging individuals to act without fear or hesitation.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios:

  • Family Gatherings: During family events, invoking blessings for children and the family unit can strengthen bonds and promote harmony.
  • Community Service: When engaging in community work, this mantra serves as a reminder to act with love and wisdom, fostering a supportive environment.
  • Personal Growth: In personal endeavors, the mantra encourages individuals to pursue their goals with confidence and a loving mindset, overcoming fears that may hinder progress.

In conclusion, this mantra signifies the importance of devotion, wisdom, and courage in our lives. It serves as a guiding principle for nurturing relationships, fostering community spirit, and encouraging personal growth. By embodying these qualities, individuals can create a more harmonious and prosperous life for themselves and those around them.
