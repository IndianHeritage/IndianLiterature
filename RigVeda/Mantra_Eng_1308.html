सूनोर मानेनाश्विनाऽ गृणानां वाजं विप्राय भुरणां रदन्ता |अगस्त्ये ब्रह्मणा वावृद्धानां सं विश्वलां नासत्यारिणीतम् ||
sūnor mānenāśvinā gṛṇānā vājaṁ viprāya bhuraṇā radantā | agastye brahmaṇā vāvṛdhānā saṁ viśpalāṁ nāsatyāriṇītam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sūnor: of the sons
    • mānena: with respect
    • āśvinā: O Ashvins (the twin deities)
    • gṛṇānā: singing
    • vājaṁ: the wealth or prosperity
    • viprāya: to the wise or learned
    • bhuraṇā: bestowing
    • radantā: who are roaring or resounding
    • agastye: to Agastya (a sage)
    • brahmaṇā: with the knowledge or wisdom
    • vāvṛdhānā: who are increasing
    • saṁ: together
    • viśpalāṁ: the well-protected
    • nāsatyāriṇītam: the truth of the Nāsatyas (the Ashvins)
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the Ashvins, who are considered the divine physicians and bringers of health and prosperity.
    • The phrase "sūnor mānenā" emphasizes the importance of respect and reverence towards the divine and wise individuals in our lives.
    • By singing praises and acknowledging the wealth and prosperity bestowed by the Ashvins, we align ourselves with positive energies.
    • The mention of Agastya signifies the importance of wisdom and knowledge in enhancing our lives and the lives of others.
    • The mantra encourages a collective effort ("saṁ") to protect and nurture the well-being of the community.
    • The reference to "nāsatyāriṇītam" highlights the significance of truth and integrity in our pursuits.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, respecting and valuing the contributions of team members can lead to a more harmonious and productive work environment.
    • In personal relationships, acknowledging the wisdom of elders and mentors can guide individuals towards better decision-making.
    • Community initiatives that focus on collective well-being, such as health camps or educational programs, embody the spirit of this mantra.
    • Practicing honesty and integrity in daily interactions fosters trust and strengthens relationships, reflecting the essence of "nāsatyāriṇītam."
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of respect, wisdom, and truth in our lives.
    • It emphasizes the importance of collective efforts in nurturing prosperity and well-being.
    • By embodying the principles outlined in this mantra, individuals can contribute to a more harmonious and prosperous society.
    • Ultimately, it encourages us to seek knowledge, respect the divine, and uphold truth in all our endeavors.
