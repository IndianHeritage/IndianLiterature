आ रोडसी हर्यमाणो महित्वा नव्यं-नव्यं हर्यसि मन्म नु प्रियं |प्र पस्त्यम असुर हर्यतं गोर आविष कृतहि हरये सूर्याय ||
ā rodasī haryamāṇo mahitvā navyaṁ-navyaṁ haryasi manma nu priyam | pra pastyam asura haryataṁ gor āviṣ kṛdhi haraye sūryāya ||
  • ā rodasī: This phrase can be interpreted as "O Earth" or "O the vast expanse." It signifies the importance of the Earth as a nurturing and sustaining force in our lives.
  • haryamāṇo: This term means "the one who is moving or traveling." It reflects the dynamic nature of existence and the constant movement in life.
  • mahitvā: This word translates to "greatness" or "majesty." It emphasizes the grandeur of the universe and the forces that govern it.
  • navyaṁ-navyaṁ: This phrase means "new and new." It suggests the idea of renewal and the cyclical nature of life, where every moment brings forth new opportunities and experiences.
  • haryasi manma nu priyam: This can be understood as "may you bring forth what is dear to my heart." It expresses a desire for the universe to manifest one's innermost wishes and aspirations.
  • pra pastyam: This term means "to the one who is the protector." It refers to the divine or cosmic forces that safeguard and guide us through life.
  • asura haryataṁ: This phrase can be interpreted as "the one who is powerful and divine." It acknowledges the presence of both benevolent and formidable forces in the universe.
  • gor āviṣ: This means "the one who is strong and mighty." It highlights the strength and resilience found in nature and within ourselves.
  • kṛdhi haraye sūryāya: This can be translated as "may you create for the sun." It signifies the importance of the sun as a source of life, energy, and illumination.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of life, emphasizing the interconnectedness of nature, the cyclical nature of existence, and the importance of our desires and aspirations. In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as during moments of reflection, meditation, or when seeking guidance in decision-making. It serves as a reminder to appreciate the beauty of the world around us, to embrace change, and to align our intentions with the greater forces of the universe. By acknowledging the majesty of life and the power of our thoughts, we can cultivate a deeper connection with ourselves and the world, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling existence.
