अस्मा इद उ प्र तवसे तुराय प्रयो न हरमि स्तोमं माहिनाय |ऋचिषमायाध्रिगव ओहम इन्द्राय ब्रह्माणि राततमाः ||
asmā id u pra tavase turāya prayo na harmi stomam māhināya | ṛcīṣamāyādhrigava oham indrāya brahmāṇi rātatamā ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • asmā: from us
    • id: this
    • u: indeed
    • pra: for the purpose of
    • tavase: strength or power
    • turāya: swift or quick
    • prayo: to bring forth
    • na: not
    • harmi: harm or injury
    • stomam: praise or hymn
    • māhināya: for the great one
    • ṛcīṣamāyādhrigava: the one who is the source of hymns
    • oham: I am
    • indrāya: to Indra (the king of gods)
    • brahmāṇi: the ultimate reality or the divine
    • rātatamā: the most excellent or supreme
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra expresses a plea for strength and power from the divine, specifically directed towards Indra, who is often associated with rain, thunder, and strength.
    • The invocation of "asmā id u pra tavase" suggests a collective effort or unity in seeking divine assistance.
    • The phrase "not harmi" indicates a desire for protection from harm, emphasizing the importance of safety and well-being.
    • By addressing Indra as "brahmāṇi," the mantra acknowledges the connection between the divine and the ultimate reality, suggesting that true strength comes from a higher source.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In times of personal or communal crisis, such as natural disasters or conflicts, individuals may turn to spiritual practices or prayers similar to this mantra, seeking strength and protection.
    • In a corporate setting, teams may invoke collective strength and unity when facing challenges, drawing parallels to the mantra's emphasis on coming together for a common purpose.
    • During significant life events, such as weddings or new ventures, people often seek blessings for strength and protection, mirroring the essence of this mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of seeking divine support and strength in our lives.
    • It highlights the significance of unity and collective effort in overcoming challenges and achieving goals.
    • In a broader context, it encourages individuals to recognize the divine presence in their pursuits and to seek protection from harm, fostering a sense of security and resilience.
