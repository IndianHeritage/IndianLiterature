मन्त्र: श्यावाश्वस्य रेबतस् तथा श्रिणु यथाश्रिणोर atreḥ कर्माणि कृत्वतः | प्र त्रसदस्युम आविथ तवम् एक इन नृषाह्य इन्द्र क्षत्राणि वर्धयन ||
śyāvāśvasya rebhatas tathā śṛṇu yathāśṛṇor atreḥ karmāṇi kṛṇvataḥ | pra trasadasyum āvitha tvam eka in nṛṣāhya indra kṣatrāṇi vardhayan ||
  • śyāvāśvasya rebhatas: This phrase can be translated as "the swift horses of the dark one." In this context, "śyāvāśva" refers to powerful, swift horses, often symbolizing strength and speed. In real life, this can be likened to the importance of having strong and reliable resources or support systems in our endeavors.
  • tathā śṛṇu yathāśṛṇor atreḥ: This translates to "thus listen, as the hearer does." It emphasizes the importance of listening and understanding. In our daily lives, effective communication and active listening are crucial in building relationships and resolving conflicts.
  • karmāṇi kṛṇvataḥ: This means "those who perform actions." It highlights the significance of taking action and being proactive. In a practical sense, this can be seen in the context of work ethic; individuals who take initiative and work diligently are often more successful.
  • pra trasadasyum āvitha tvam: This can be interpreted as "may you be covered by the protective shield." It signifies the need for protection and support in our lives. For example, in a corporate environment, having a mentor or a supportive team can act as a protective shield against challenges.
  • eka in nṛṣāhya: This phrase translates to "one among men." It suggests the idea of unity and collaboration among individuals. In real life, teamwork and collaboration are essential in achieving common goals, whether in sports, business, or community service.
  • indra kṣatrāṇi vardhayan: This means "Indra, who increases the powers of the warriors." Indra is often seen as a deity of strength and power. This can be related to the idea of empowerment in our lives, where leaders or mentors inspire and uplift others to reach their full potential.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of strength, action, protection, unity, and empowerment. In real life, these principles can be applied in various scenarios, such as in personal development, teamwork, and leadership. By embodying these qualities, individuals can navigate challenges more effectively and contribute positively to their communities. The significance of this mantra lies in its timeless wisdom, encouraging us to harness our strengths, listen actively, take action, support one another, and empower those around us.
