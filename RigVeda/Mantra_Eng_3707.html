नवā नो अग्न ā भर स्तोत्रिभ्यः सुख्षितीर इषः |ते स्याम य āनृचुस त्वादूतासो दमे-दम इषं स्तोत्रिभ्य ā भर ||
navā no agna ā bhara stotṛbhyaḥ sukṣitīr iṣaḥ | te syāma ya ānṛcus tvādūtāso dame-dama iṣaṁ stotṛbhya ā bhara ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • navā: new, fresh
    • no: our
    • agna: O Agni (the fire god)
    • ā bhara: may you bring forth
    • stotṛbhyaḥ: to the worshippers
    • sukṣitīr: auspicious, beneficial
    • iṣaḥ: power, strength
    • te: to you
    • syāma: may we become
    • ya: who
    • ānṛcus: those who sing praises
    • tvādūtāso: your messengers
    • dame-dama: in the home, in the household
    • iṣaṁ: strength, power
    • stotṛbhya: to the worshippers
    • ā bhara: may you bring forth
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Agni, the fire god, asking for his blessings and auspiciousness.
    • The invocation of "new" signifies a fresh start or new beginnings, which can be related to various life situations such as starting a new job, moving to a new place, or beginning a new relationship.
    • The phrase "may you bring forth" emphasizes the desire for Agni to provide strength and support to the worshippers, akin to seeking guidance and protection in challenging times.
    • The mention of "those who sing praises" highlights the importance of community and collective worship, reminding us of the value of support from friends and family in our endeavors.
    • The reference to "in the home" suggests that the blessings of Agni are not just for individual pursuits but also for the well-being of the household, emphasizing harmony and prosperity within family life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • When starting a new business, entrepreneurs often seek blessings for success and stability, similar to invoking Agni for auspicious beginnings.
    • In family gatherings or rituals, people often pray for the well-being of their loved ones, reflecting the communal aspect of worship highlighted in the mantra.
    • During festivals, lighting a fire or lamp symbolizes the presence of Agni, representing purity and the dispelling of darkness, which can be seen as a metaphor for overcoming challenges.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of seeking divine blessings for strength, auspiciousness, and community support in various aspects of life.
    • It is applicable in real-life scenarios where individuals or families seek guidance, protection, and prosperity, reinforcing the idea that spiritual practices can provide comfort and motivation.
    • Ultimately, the invocation of Agni serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of our personal aspirations with the well-being of our communities and households.
