अग्नि भ्रातः सहस्कृत रोहिदश्व शुचिव्रत |इमं स्तोमं जुषस्व मे ||
agne bhrātaḥ sahaskṛta rohidaśva śucivrata | imaṁ stomaṁ juṣasva me ||
  • Agne: This word refers to Agni, the fire god in Vedic literature, symbolizing energy, light, and purity.
  • Bhrātaḥ: This translates to "brother," indicating a sense of kinship and unity.
  • Sahaskṛta: Meaning "well-prepared" or "skillfully done," it emphasizes the importance of effort and readiness.
  • Rohidaśva: This term can be interpreted as "the one with red horses," often associated with strength and vitality.
  • Śucivrata: This means "one who is pure in vows," highlighting the significance of integrity and commitment.
  • Imam: This word means "this," referring to the offering or the hymn being presented.
  • Stomaṁ: This translates to "hymn" or "praise," indicating the act of worship and reverence.
  • Juṣasva: Meaning "enjoy" or "accept," it expresses a desire for the divine to receive the offering.
  • Me: This means "my," indicating a personal connection and intention behind the offering.

The mantra can be understood as a prayer to Agni, asking him to accept the hymn and bless the devotee with strength, purity, and unity. It emphasizes the importance of preparation and sincerity in one's actions and intentions.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios:

  • Personal Growth: Just as one prepares for a significant event, this mantra reminds us to be well-prepared in our endeavors, whether in education, career, or personal relationships.
  • Community and Relationships: The reference to "brother" signifies the importance of unity and support within communities. It encourages individuals to foster strong bonds and work together for common goals.
  • Integrity in Actions: The emphasis on purity and commitment serves as a reminder to uphold integrity in our promises and actions, whether in professional settings or personal commitments.

Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for strength, unity, and purity in our actions. It serves as a guiding principle for personal and communal growth, encouraging individuals to prepare diligently, act with integrity, and foster strong relationships. By embodying these values, one can navigate life's challenges with resilience and purpose.
