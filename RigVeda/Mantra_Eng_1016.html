अतारीष्म तमसस् पारम् अस्योषा उच्छन्ती वयुनā कृत्नोति |श्रिये छन्दो न स्मयते विभाति सुप्रतीका सौम्नसायाजिगः ||
atāriṣma tamasas pāram asyoṣā ucchantī vayunā kṛṇoti | śriye chando na smayate vibhātī supratīkā saumanasāyājīgaḥ ||
  • atāriṣma: This word can be interpreted as "to transcend" or "to go beyond." It signifies the act of overcoming obstacles or limitations.
  • tamasas: This translates to "darkness" or "ignorance." It represents the challenges and difficulties that one faces in life.
  • pāram: Meaning "to the other side" or "beyond," it indicates reaching a state of enlightenment or clarity.
  • asyoṣā: This can be understood as "the dawn" or "the light." It symbolizes new beginnings and the arrival of knowledge.
  • ucchantī: This word means "to rise" or "to elevate." It reflects the idea of rising above challenges.
  • vayunā: This translates to "with strength" or "with vigor." It emphasizes the importance of determination and energy in overcoming obstacles.
  • kṛṇoti: Meaning "to create" or "to make," it signifies the act of bringing forth new possibilities and opportunities.
  • śriye: This word means "prosperity" or "wealth." It represents the rewards of overcoming challenges.
  • chando: This translates to "the rhythm" or "the structure." It indicates the importance of harmony and balance in life.
  • na smayate: Meaning "does not smile," it suggests that one should not be complacent or take things lightly.
  • vibhātī: This word means "to shine" or "to illuminate." It represents the clarity and understanding that comes from overcoming ignorance.
  • supratīkā: This translates to "well-formed" or "well-structured." It emphasizes the importance of a solid foundation in life.
  • saumanasāyājīgaḥ: This can be interpreted as "with a peaceful mind" or "with a calm disposition." It highlights the significance of mental tranquility in achieving success.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the journey of overcoming darkness and ignorance to reach a state of enlightenment and prosperity. It emphasizes the importance of strength, determination, and a peaceful mind in navigating life's challenges. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied to personal growth, professional endeavors, and spiritual journeys. For instance, when facing difficulties in a career, one can draw inspiration from this mantra to transcend obstacles and strive for success with a calm and focused mindset. Ultimately, it serves as a reminder that with perseverance and clarity, one can achieve their goals and illuminate their path in life.
