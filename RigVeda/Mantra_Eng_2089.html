सैनानीकन सुविदात्रो अस्मे यष्टा देवाम् आयजिष्टः स्वस्ति |अदब्धो गोपा उता नः परस्पा अग्ने द्युमद उता रेवद दिदीहि ||
sainānīkena suvidatro asme yaṣṭā devām̐ āyajiṣṭhaḥ svasti | adabdho gopā uta naḥ paraspā agne dyumad uta revad didīhi ||
  • Mantra: "sainānīkena suvidatro asme yaṣṭā devām̐ āyajiṣṭhaḥ svasti | adabdho gopā uta naḥ paraspā agne dyumad uta revad didīhi ||"
  • Translation: "With the guidance of the leader, may we be well-placed; may the worshipper of the gods be the best among us. O Agni, grant us protection and prosperity, and may you shine brightly, bringing us wealth and success."
  • Word-by-Word Explanation:
    • sainānīkena: With the guidance of the leader
    • suvidatro: May we be well-placed or well-situated
    • asme: For us
    • yaṣṭā: The worshipper or the one who offers
    • devām̐: To the gods
    • āyajiṣṭhaḥ: The best or the most excellent
    • svasti: Prosperity or well-being
    • adabdho: Unharmed or protected
    • gopā: Guardian or protector
    • uta: And
    • naḥ: Our
    • paraspā: For us from others
    • agne: O Agni (the fire god)
    • dyumad: Shining or radiant
    • uta: And
    • revad: Wealth or prosperity
    • didīhi: Grant or bestow

  • Real-Life Examples:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of leadership and guidance in achieving success. For instance, in a corporate setting, a good leader can steer the team towards achieving their goals effectively.
    • The call for protection and prosperity can be seen in personal life, where individuals seek blessings for their families and careers, often through rituals or prayers.
    • In community settings, this mantra can be applied when groups come together for a common cause, seeking the best outcomes through collective effort and divine blessings.

  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of divine guidance and the role of leadership in achieving prosperity and protection. It highlights the interconnectedness of individuals within a community and the need for collective efforts towards common goals.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applicable in various contexts such as family gatherings, community events, and organizational meetings, where the focus is on seeking blessings for success and well-being.
    • Ultimately, it serves as a reminder to acknowledge the divine forces at play in our lives and to strive for excellence in our endeavors while supporting one another.
