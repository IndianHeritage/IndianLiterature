यां ते दिद्युद अवसृष्टा दिवस परि क्षमया चरति परि सा वृणक्तु नः |सहस्रं ते स्वपिवात भेषजाः मा नस् तोकेषु तनयेषु रीरिषः ||
yā te didyud avasṛṣṭā divas pari kṣmayā carati pari sā vṛṇaktu naḥ | sahasraṁ te svapivāta bheṣajā mā nas tokeṣu tanayeṣu rīriṣaḥ ||
  • yā te didyud avasṛṣṭā: This phrase can be interpreted as "that which shines forth from you." It signifies the divine light or energy that emanates from a higher power, often associated with knowledge and enlightenment.
  • divas pari kṣmayā carati: This translates to "that which moves around the heavens with grace." It suggests a sense of harmony and balance in the universe, where celestial bodies and energies interact in a purposeful manner.
  • pari sā vṛṇaktu naḥ: This means "may that energy choose us." It expresses a desire for divine blessings and guidance, indicating a yearning for connection with the higher forces of the universe.
  • sahasraṁ te svapivāta bheṣajā: This phrase translates to "a thousand remedies from your breath." It symbolizes the abundance of healing and nurturing energies available to us, suggesting that the divine provides numerous ways to restore balance and health.
  • mā nas tokeṣu tanayeṣu rīriṣaḥ: This means "may we not suffer in our offspring." It reflects a concern for future generations, emphasizing the importance of well-being and prosperity for our children and their descendants.

  • Significance: This mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking divine guidance and blessings for oneself and future generations. It highlights the interconnectedness of all beings and the importance of harmony in the universe.
  • Real-life Application: In daily life, this mantra can be invoked during moments of uncertainty or when seeking clarity in decision-making. For instance, a parent may recite this mantra while praying for the health and happiness of their children, or an individual may use it as a meditation focus to attract positive energies and healing into their life.

  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a reminder of the divine support available to us and the importance of nurturing our relationships with both the spiritual and physical realms. By acknowledging the blessings we receive and expressing our desires for the well-being of ourselves and our loved ones, we align ourselves with the greater cosmic order and invite abundance into our lives.
