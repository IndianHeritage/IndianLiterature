तद् देवस्य सवितुर्वार्यम् महद्वृणीमहे असुरस्य प्रचेतसः |छर्दिर्जेन दाशुषे यच्छति तन्मना तन्नो महां उदयन् देवो अक्तुभिः ||
tad devasya savitur vāryam mahad vṛṇīmahe asurasya pracetasaḥ | chardir yena dāśuṣe yacchati tmanā tan no mahām̐ ud ayān devo aktubhiḥ ||
  • tad devasya savitur vāryam: This phrase can be translated as "that divine light of Savitar, the Sun God, which is worthy of worship." Savitar represents the life-giving energy of the sun, symbolizing illumination and enlightenment.
  • mahad vṛṇīmahe: This means "we meditate upon the great one." It emphasizes the importance of focusing our thoughts and intentions on the divine and the greater good.
  • asurasya pracetasaḥ: This translates to "the wise among the divine beings." It suggests that there are enlightened beings who guide us and help us in our spiritual journey.
  • chardir yena dāśuṣe: This phrase means "by whom the light shines upon the worshippers." It indicates that the divine light provides guidance and blessings to those who seek it.
  • yacchati tmanā: This translates to "that which is given by the self." It implies that true understanding and enlightenment come from within, and we must connect with our inner selves to receive divine wisdom.
  • tan no mahām̐ ud ayān: This means "may that great one inspire us." It is a prayer for divine inspiration and guidance in our lives.
  • devo aktubhiḥ: This translates to "the divine being with his powers." It signifies the omnipotence of the divine and the ability to manifest blessings in our lives.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda emphasizes the importance of seeking divine guidance and enlightenment through meditation and self-awareness. In real-life scenarios, it can be applied in various ways:

  • In personal development, individuals can meditate on their inner selves to gain clarity and wisdom, much like how one might seek guidance from a mentor or a spiritual leader.
  • In community settings, this mantra can inspire collective efforts towards a common goal, encouraging individuals to work together for the greater good, similar to how a team collaborates to achieve success.
  • In moments of uncertainty, reciting this mantra can serve as a reminder to seek inner strength and divine support, akin to how people often turn to prayer or meditation during challenging times.

Overall, the significance of this mantra lies in its call for introspection, unity, and the pursuit of higher knowledge, making it relevant in both personal and communal aspects of life.
