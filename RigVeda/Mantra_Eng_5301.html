मन्त्र: चत्वारो मा पैजवनस्य दानाः स्मद्दिष्टयः कृशनिनो निरेकः | ऋज्रासो मा पृथिविष्ठाः सुदासस तोकं तोकाय श्रवसे वहन्ति ||
catvāro mā paijavanasya dānāḥ smaddiṣṭayaḥ kṛśanino nireke | ṛjrāso mā pṛthiviṣṭhāḥ sudāsas tokaṁ tokāya śravase vahanti ||
  • catvāro mā paijavanasya dānāḥ: This phrase can be translated as "Four are the gifts of the one who is the giver." Here, "catvāro" refers to the number four, and "dānāḥ" signifies gifts or offerings. In a real-life context, this can be understood as the four essential aspects of life that one should focus on: physical health, mental well-being, social relationships, and spiritual growth.
  • smaddiṣṭayaḥ kṛśanino nireke: This translates to "Those who are wise and knowledgeable are not separate from the essence." This emphasizes the importance of wisdom and knowledge in understanding the true nature of existence. For example, in modern education, the pursuit of knowledge is not just about acquiring facts but understanding their application in life.
  • ṛjrāso mā pṛthiviṣṭhāḥ: This can be interpreted as "May the wise ones be grounded in the earth." It suggests the importance of being rooted and connected to the world around us. In practical terms, this can relate to environmental awareness and the need to respect and care for our planet.
  • sudāsas tokaṁ tokāya śravase vahanti: This means "The good ones carry the essence of prosperity." It highlights the idea that those who are virtuous and righteous contribute to the well-being and prosperity of society. In real life, this can be seen in community service and charitable actions that uplift others.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of wisdom, groundedness, and the importance of contributing positively to society. In real-life scenarios, it encourages individuals to focus on holistic development—balancing physical, mental, social, and spiritual aspects. It also calls for environmental consciousness and the value of virtuous actions that lead to collective prosperity. By embodying these principles, individuals can create a harmonious and thriving community, reflecting the essence of the mantra in everyday life.
