आ नो भरा भागं इन्द्र द्युमन्तं नीते देश्णस्य धीमहि प्ररेके |ऊर्वा इव पप्रथे कामो अस्मे तं आ पृण वसुपते वसूनाम् ||
ā no bhara bhagam indra dyumantaṁ ni te deṣṇasya dhīmahi prareke | ūrva iva paprathe kāmo asme tam ā pṛṇa vasupate vasūnām ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā no: May we be blessed
    • bhara: with abundance
    • bhagam: of wealth
    • indra: O Indra (the king of gods)
    • dyumantaṁ: shining, radiant
    • ni: may we
    • te: your
    • deṣṇasya: vision, insight
    • dhīmahi: we contemplate
    • prareke: in the beginning
    • ūrva: like the earth
    • iva: as if
    • paprathe: we are nourished
    • kāmo: desires
    • asme: may we have
    • tam: that
    • ā: may we attain
    • pṛṇa: to fulfill
    • vasupate: O Lord of wealth
    • vasūnām: of all riches
  • Overall Meaning:
    • This mantra is a prayer for abundance and prosperity, invoking the blessings of Indra, the king of gods, and the Lord of wealth.
    • It emphasizes the importance of vision and insight in achieving one's desires and goals.
    • The imagery of being nourished like the earth suggests a deep connection to nature and the sustenance it provides.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be recited by entrepreneurs seeking success and prosperity in their ventures, reminding them to focus on their vision and the abundance they wish to create.
    • In personal life, individuals may use this mantra during times of uncertainty, seeking clarity and abundance in their relationships and personal growth.
    • Communities can come together to chant this mantra during festivals or gatherings, fostering a collective spirit of abundance and shared prosperity.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for abundance, insight, and the fulfillment of desires.
    • It serves as a reminder to focus on our goals and the vision we hold for our lives, encouraging us to seek the blessings of higher powers in our endeavors.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be a source of inspiration and motivation, guiding individuals and communities towards prosperity and fulfillment.
