तां न आवोळ्हम् अश्विनाऽ रयिम पीषङ्गसंदृशम् |धिष्ण्या वरिवोविदम् ||
tā na ā voḻham aśvinā rayim piśaṅgasaṁdṛśam | dhiṣṇyā varivovidam ||
  • tā na ā voḻham: This phrase can be translated as "May we not be deprived of." It expresses a desire for abundance and prosperity, emphasizing the importance of not losing out on opportunities or resources.
  • aśvinā: Refers to the Aśvins, the twin deities in Vedic mythology known for their healing and benevolent qualities. They symbolize health, vitality, and the ability to overcome obstacles.
  • rayim: This word means "wealth" or "prosperity." It signifies not just material wealth but also spiritual and emotional richness.
  • piśaṅgasaṁdṛśam: This term describes something that is golden or yellowish in color, often associated with the sun. It symbolizes brightness, positivity, and the life-giving force of the sun.
  • dhiṣṇyā: This word means "through the dwelling" or "in the home." It indicates the importance of a stable and nurturing environment for growth and prosperity.
  • varivovidam: This can be interpreted as "the one who knows the way" or "the one who is knowledgeable." It emphasizes the value of wisdom and guidance in achieving success and well-being.

In summary, this mantra is a prayer for abundance and prosperity, invoking the blessings of the Aśvins to ensure that we are not deprived of wealth and well-being. It highlights the significance of a supportive environment and the need for wisdom in navigating life's challenges.


Real-Life Application: This mantra can be applied in various aspects of life, such as:

  • Personal Growth: Individuals can recite this mantra to attract positive energy and opportunities in their personal and professional lives.
  • Community Well-Being: Communities can come together to chant this mantra during gatherings or festivals to invoke collective prosperity and harmony.
  • Business Success: Entrepreneurs may use this mantra as a form of affirmation to foster a mindset of abundance and attract success in their ventures.

Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its holistic approach to prosperity, emphasizing the need for a nurturing environment, wisdom, and divine blessings. By incorporating its teachings into daily life, individuals and communities can strive for a balanced and prosperous existence.
