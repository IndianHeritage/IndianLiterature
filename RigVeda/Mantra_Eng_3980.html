विसर्माणं कृणुहि वित्तमेषां ये भुञ्जते अपृणन्तो न उक्थैः |अपव्रतान प्रसवे वावृद्धानान ब्रह्मद्विषः सूर्याद यावयस्व ||
visarmāṇaṁ kṛṇuhi vittam eṣāṁ ye bhuñjate apṛṇanto na ukthaiḥ | apavratān prasave vāvṛdhānān brahmadviṣaḥ sūryād yāvayasva ||
  • visarmāṇaṁ kṛṇuhi: This phrase can be interpreted as a call to create or establish a state of comfort and security. In real life, this can relate to the importance of providing a safe environment for individuals, families, or communities. For example, a community center that offers resources and support can be seen as a manifestation of this idea.
  • vittam eṣāṁ ye bhuñjate: This translates to "those who enjoy wealth." It emphasizes the idea of prosperity and the responsible use of resources. In modern contexts, this can refer to individuals or organizations that utilize their wealth for the betterment of society, such as philanthropists who donate to education or healthcare initiatives.
  • apṛṇanto na ukthaiḥ: This phrase suggests that those who do not share or give back are not truly prosperous. It highlights the importance of generosity and community support. For instance, businesses that engage in corporate social responsibility (CSR) practices exemplify this principle by contributing to social causes and supporting their local communities.
  • apavratān prasave vāvṛdhānān: This can be interpreted as a call for the growth and flourishing of those who are open and receptive. In real life, this could relate to educational institutions that foster an environment of learning and growth, encouraging students to explore their potential and contribute positively to society.
  • brahmadviṣaḥ sūryād yāvayasva: This phrase can be understood as a plea for protection from negativity and harmful influences, invoking the power of the sun (Sūrya) as a source of light and positivity. In practical terms, this can relate to the importance of surrounding oneself with positive influences, whether through friendships, mentorships, or community involvement.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of creating a secure and prosperous environment, the importance of sharing wealth and resources, and the value of personal growth and positive influences. In real life, these principles can be applied in various contexts, such as community development, corporate responsibility, and personal relationships. By embodying these values, individuals and communities can work towards a more harmonious and prosperous existence, fostering a culture of support, generosity, and growth.
