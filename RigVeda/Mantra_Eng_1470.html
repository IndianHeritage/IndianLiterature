मन्त्र: चकार्थ कारम् एभ्यः पृथनासु प्रवन्तवे |ते अन्याम्-अन्यां नद्यम् सनीष्णत श्रवस्यन्तः सनीष्णत ||
cakartha kāram ebhyaḥ pṛtanāsu pravantave | te anyām-anyāṁ nadyaṁ saniṣṇata śravasyantaḥ saniṣṇata ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • cakartha: They have done
    • kāram: The action or deed
    • ebhyaḥ: From these
    • pṛtanāsu: In the streams or channels
    • pravantave: Flowing or moving
    • te: They
    • anyām-anyāṁ: Other than each other
    • nadyaṁ: Rivers or streams
    • saniṣṇata: Being established or settled
    • śravasyantaḥ: Those who are renowned or famous
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the actions taken by individuals or entities that lead to the flow of life, much like rivers that nourish the land.
    • The phrase "cakartha kāram ebhyaḥ" suggests that the deeds performed by these entities are significant and impactful.
    • The mention of "pṛtanāsu pravantave" indicates the dynamic nature of these actions, akin to rivers that continuously flow and change the landscape.
    • Furthermore, "te anyām-anyāṁ nadyaṁ" implies that these actions can lead to different outcomes or paths, just as rivers can diverge and create new routes.
    • The concluding part "saniṣṇata śravasyantaḥ" emphasizes the importance of being established in one's actions, leading to recognition and respect in society.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community, when individuals come together to work on a project, their collective actions can lead to significant changes, much like how rivers shape the land.
    • In a business context, a company's innovative actions can create new markets and opportunities, similar to how rivers can carve new paths through terrain.
    • In personal life, the choices we make can lead us down different paths, influencing our future, akin to how rivers can branch off into various streams.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of actions and their consequences in life.
    • It teaches us that our deeds can lead to various outcomes, and being mindful of our actions can establish our reputation and legacy.
    • In real life, this mantra encourages individuals to act with intention and awareness, understanding that their contributions can have a lasting impact on their surroundings and society.
