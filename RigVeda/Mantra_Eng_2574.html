इन्द्राग्नीतविषाणि वां सदस्थानि प्रयांसी च |युवोर अप्तूर्यं हितम् ||
indrāgnī taviṣāṇi vāṁ sadhasthāni prayāṁsi ca | yuvor aptūryaṁ hitam ||
  • Indrāgnī: This term refers to the combined powers of Indra, the king of gods, and Agni, the fire god. Indra symbolizes strength, power, and leadership, while Agni represents energy, transformation, and purification.
  • Taviṣāṇi: This word can be interpreted as "the powerful" or "the strong." It emphasizes the qualities of strength and vigor that are essential for overcoming challenges.
  • Vāṁ: This term means "your" or "of you," indicating a personal connection or relationship with the divine forces being invoked.
  • Sadhasthāni: This word translates to "those who are gathered" or "the assembly." It signifies the importance of community and collective strength in achieving goals.
  • Prayāṁsi: This term means "the offerings" or "the sacrifices." It highlights the significance of giving and sharing in the pursuit of success and harmony.
  • Yuvor: This word means "of the young" or "of the new." It suggests a focus on youth, innovation, and the potential for growth and change.
  • Aptūryaṁ: This term can be interpreted as "the fulfillment" or "the completion." It signifies the achievement of goals and the realization of potential.
  • Hitam: This word means "beneficial" or "good." It emphasizes the importance of actions and outcomes that lead to well-being and prosperity.

Conclusion: The mantra "indrāgnī taviṣāṇi vāṁ sadhasthāni prayāṁsi ca | yuvor aptūryaṁ hitam ||" encapsulates the essence of invoking divine strength and energy for the benefit of the community. It highlights the importance of collective effort, the power of youth, and the significance of beneficial actions. In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios such as team projects, community initiatives, and personal growth endeavors. By recognizing the strength in unity and the transformative power of energy, individuals and groups can work towards achieving their goals while fostering a sense of well-being and harmony in their surroundings.
