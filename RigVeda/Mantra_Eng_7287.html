उतो nv अस्य यन् महद अश्वावद योजने बृहद् |दामाऽ रथस्य ददृशे ||
uto nv asya yan mahad aśvāvad yojanam bṛhad | dāmā rathasya dadṛśe ||
  • Mantra: "uto nv asya yan mahad aśvāvad yojanam bṛhad | dāmā rathasya dadṛśe ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • uto: may it be
    • nv: indeed
    • asya: of this
    • yan: which
    • mahad: great
    • aśvāvad: like horses
    • yojanam: the journey or path
    • bṛhad: vast or large
    • dāmā: the reins or control
    • rathasya: of the chariot
    • dadṛśe: I have seen or perceived
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the journey of life, comparing it to a chariot that is driven by horses.
    • The "great journey" signifies the path one takes in life, filled with challenges and opportunities.
    • The mention of "reins" indicates the need for control and guidance in navigating this journey.
    • Just as a charioteer must skillfully manage the reins to steer the horses, individuals must exercise control over their thoughts and actions to achieve their goals.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, a person may face various challenges (akin to the horses) while pursuing a career path (the journey). Effective management of time and resources (the reins) is crucial for success.
    • In personal life, individuals often encounter obstacles that require careful planning and decision-making to navigate effectively, similar to how a charioteer must guide their chariot through difficult terrain.
    • In education, students must control their study habits and time management to succeed in their academic journey, paralleling the control needed in driving a chariot.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of control and guidance in our life's journey.
    • It serves as a reminder that while the path may be vast and filled with challenges, with the right mindset and management, one can navigate through life successfully.
    • In real-life scenarios, whether in career, personal growth, or education, the principles of control and direction are universally applicable, encouraging individuals to take charge of their journeys.
