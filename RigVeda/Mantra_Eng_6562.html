मन्त्र: नह्य अङ्ग नृतो त्वद अन्यं विन्दामि राधसे |रायेद्युम्नाय शवसे च गिर्वणः ||
nahy a1ṅga nṛto tvad anyaṁ vindāmi rādhase | rāye dyumnāya śavase ca girvaṇaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • nahy: not
    • a1ṅga: limb or part
    • nṛto: dance or movement
    • tvad: your
    • anyaṁ: another
    • vindāmi: I find or I seek
    • rādhase: for the purpose of prosperity or wealth
    • rāye: for wealth or riches
    • dyumnāya: for glory or fame
    • śavase: for the purpose of strength or vitality
    • ca: and
    • girvaṇaḥ: of the singers or poets
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra expresses a deep sense of devotion and the quest for prosperity and strength.
    • The speaker acknowledges that they do not find any part of their existence outside of the divine presence.
    • It emphasizes the importance of seeking wealth, glory, and vitality through a connection with the divine.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional context, individuals often seek guidance and support from mentors or spiritual leaders to achieve success and prosperity.
    • In personal life, people may turn to prayer or meditation to find strength and clarity in challenging situations.
    • Communities often come together to celebrate achievements, recognizing the divine influence in their collective success.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of recognizing the divine in all aspects of life, particularly in the pursuit of wealth, strength, and glory.
    • It serves as a reminder that true prosperity comes from a connection with the divine and that our achievements are intertwined with a higher purpose.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire individuals to seek balance between material pursuits and spiritual growth, fostering a holistic approach to success.
