प्र वां निचेरुः ककुहो वशांऽ अनु पीशङ्गरूपः सदनानि गम्याः |हरि अन्यस्य पीपयन्त वाजैर मथ्रा राजांस्य अश्विनाः वि घोषैः ||
pra vāṁ niceruḥ kakuho vaśām̐ anu piśaṅgarūpaḥ sadanāni gamyāḥ | harī anyasya pīpayanta vājair mathrā rajāṁsy aśvinā vi ghoṣaiḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • pra vāṁ: "to you" or "for you"
    • niceruḥ: "the one who is pure" or "the one who is clean"
    • kakuho: "the one who is sweet-sounding" or "the one who is melodious"
    • vaśām̐: "to be controlled" or "to be governed"
    • anu: "following" or "after"
    • piśaṅgarūpaḥ: "of golden hue" or "having a golden appearance"
    • sadanāni: "the abodes" or "the homes"
    • gamyāḥ: "to be reached" or "to be attained"
    • harī: "the divine" or "the godly"
    • anyasya: "of another" or "of others"
    • pīpayanta: "nourishing" or "sustaining"
    • vājair: "with strength" or "with vigor"
    • mathrā: "by the mother" or "through nurturing"
    • rajāṁsy: "the kings" or "the rulers"
    • aśvinā: "by the Ashvins" (the twin gods)
    • vi ghoṣaiḥ: "with sounds" or "with voices"
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the divine qualities of purity, sweetness, and strength.
    • It emphasizes the importance of nurturing and sustaining life, akin to how a mother cares for her children.
    • The reference to the Ashvins, who are known for their healing and protective qualities, suggests a call for divine assistance in achieving well-being.
    • The mantra can be seen as a prayer for guidance and support in our endeavors, whether personal or communal.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community setting, this mantra can be recited during gatherings to invoke harmony and cooperation among members.
    • In personal life, individuals may use this mantra during meditation or reflection to seek clarity and strength in their decisions.
    • In healthcare, practitioners may recite this mantra to promote healing and well-being for their patients, drawing on the nurturing aspect of the divine.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine support in achieving purity, strength, and nurturing qualities in our lives.
    • It serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of all beings and the importance of community and support.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, from personal growth to community well-being, emphasizing the need for divine guidance and nurturing in our journeys.
