मन्त्र: परि सोम प्र धन्वा स्वस्तये नृभिः पुनानो अभि वासयाशिरम् |ये ते मदाऽआहनसो विहायसस तेभिर इन्द्रं चोदय दातवे मघम् ||
pari soma pra dhanvā svastaye nṛbhiḥ punāno abhi vāsayāśiram | ye te madā āhanaso vihāyasas tebhir indraṁ codaya dātave magham ||
  • Mantra: "pari soma pra dhanvā svastaye nṛbhiḥ punāno abhi vāsayāśiram | ye te madā āhanaso vihāyasas tebhir indraṁ codaya dātave magham ||"
  • Translation: This mantra can be translated as a call to the divine, invoking the essence of Soma (a sacred drink) for prosperity and well-being. It seeks the blessings of Indra, the king of gods, to bestow wealth and abundance upon the worshippers.
  • Word Breakdown:
    • pari: around, in all directions
    • soma: a sacred drink, symbolizing immortality and divine inspiration
    • pra: for, towards
    • dhanvā: wealth, prosperity
    • svastaye: for well-being, auspiciousness
    • nṛbhiḥ: by men, by people
    • punāno: purifying, renewing
    • abhi: towards, upon
    • vāsayāśiram: to dwell, to reside
    • ye: those who
    • te: your
    • madā: intoxication, joy
    • āhanaso: of the mighty
    • vihāyasas: of the heavens
    • tebhir: with these
    • indraṁ: Indra, the king of gods
    • codaya: inspire, encourage
    • dātave: to give, to bestow
    • magham: wealth, abundance
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a modern context, this mantra can be recited during rituals or ceremonies aimed at invoking prosperity, such as housewarming ceremonies or business inaugurations.
    • Individuals may chant this mantra while performing meditation or yoga, focusing on attracting positive energy and abundance into their lives.
    • Communities may come together to recite this mantra during festivals, seeking collective blessings for prosperity and well-being.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its invocation of divine blessings for wealth and well-being. It emphasizes the importance of community and collective efforts in seeking prosperity. In real-life scenarios, it serves as a reminder to focus on positive intentions and actions that lead to abundance, whether in personal life, business, or community endeavors. By reciting this mantra, individuals and communities can cultivate a mindset of gratitude and openness to receiving blessings, ultimately enhancing their overall quality of life.
