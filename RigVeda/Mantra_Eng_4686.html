त्वद्भीयेंद्र पार्थिवानि विश्वाच्युता चिच्च्यावयन्ते राजांसि | dyावाक्षामाः पर्वतासो वनानि विश्वं दृढं भयते अजन्मन् आ ते ||
tvad bhiyendra pārthivāni viśvācyutā cic cyāvayante rajāṁsi | dyāvākṣāmā parvatāso vanāni viśvaṁ dṛḻham bhayate ajmann ā te ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • tvad: Your
    • bhiyendra: Fearlessness
    • pārthivāni: Earthly beings
    • viśvācyutā: All beings
    • cic: Consciousness
    • cyāvayante: They are protected
    • rajāṁsi: Kings
    • dyāvākṣāmā: The heavens
    • parvatāso: Mountains
    • vanāni: Forests
    • viśvaṁ: All
    • dṛḻham: Firmly
    • bhayate: Fear
    • ajmann: Of the soul
    • ā te: To you
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the protective nature of a higher power, suggesting that fearlessness is bestowed upon all beings.
    • The phrase "tvad bhiyendra" indicates that through the divine, individuals can overcome their fears.
    • It emphasizes the interconnectedness of all beings, as "viśvācyutā" refers to the universal consciousness that binds us.
    • The mention of "rajāṁsi" signifies that even kings, who are often seen as powerful, are under the protection of this divine force.
    • The natural elements like "dyāvākṣāmā" (heavens), "parvatāso" (mountains), and "vanāni" (forests) symbolize the vastness of the universe that is also under this divine protection.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In times of crisis, such as natural disasters, communities often come together, drawing strength from each other and a higher belief, reflecting the essence of this mantra.
    • Individuals facing personal challenges, such as illness or loss, may find solace in the idea that they are not alone and that a greater force is watching over them.
    • In leadership, the mantra can be applied to inspire confidence and courage in others, reminding them that they are supported by a collective strength.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of fearlessness and the protective nature of the divine in our lives.
    • It teaches us that we are all interconnected and supported by a universal consciousness, which can help us overcome our fears.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be a source of inspiration and strength, encouraging individuals and communities to face challenges with courage and unity.
