युवं तासां दिव्यस्य प्रशासने विश्वां क्षयथो अमृतस्य मजनना |याभिर धेनुम अश्वं पिन्वथो नराः ताभिर ऊषु ऊतिभिर अश्विनाः गतम् ||
yuvaṁ tāsāṁ divyasya praśāsane viśāṁ kṣayatho amṛtasya majmanā | yābhir dhenum asva1m pinvatho narā tābhir ū ṣu ūtibhir aśvinā gatam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yuvaṁ: youthful, young
    • tāsāṁ: of those
    • divyasya: divine, celestial
    • praśāsane: in the governance, control
    • viśāṁ: of the people, community
    • kṣayatho: for the destruction, downfall
    • amṛtasya: of immortality, eternal
    • majmanā: of the mind, intellect
    • yābhir: by which
    • dhenum: the cow (symbolizing wealth and nourishment)
    • asvaṁ: the horse (symbolizing strength and power)
    • pinvatho: you may milk, extract
    • narā: men, humans
    • tābhir: by those
    • ū: and
    • ṣu: good, auspicious
    • ūtibhir: with the blessings, benefits
    • aśvinā: O Ashvins (the divine twin horsemen)
    • gatam: gone, achieved
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the youthful and divine governance over the community.
    • It emphasizes the importance of intellect and the pursuit of immortality.
    • The mention of milking the cow and horse symbolizes the extraction of wealth and strength from life.
    • The Ashvins, as divine figures, represent the blessings and auspiciousness that come from such endeavors.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern society, the youth are often seen as the driving force behind innovation and change, much like the "yuvaṁ" in the mantra.
    • Communities thrive when they are governed wisely, reflecting the "praśāsane" aspect of the mantra.
    • The pursuit of knowledge and wisdom can lead to a form of immortality through legacy, akin to "amṛtasya majmanā."
    • In agriculture, the cow represents sustenance and wealth, while the horse symbolizes labor and strength, both essential for a thriving community.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of youthful energy and divine guidance in the governance of society.
    • It encourages individuals to seek knowledge and strength, ensuring the prosperity of their community.
    • In real life, it serves as a reminder of the importance of nurturing both intellectual and physical resources for a balanced and prosperous existence.
