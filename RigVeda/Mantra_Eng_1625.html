प्रस्तुतिर वां धाम न प्रयुक्तिर अयामि मित्रावरुणा सुवृक्तिः |अनक्ति यद वां विदथेṣu होतā सुम्नं वां सूरीर वृषणाव इयक्षन ||
prastutir vāṁ dhāma na prayuktir ayāmi mitrāvaruṇā suvṛktiḥ | anakti yad vāṁ vidatheṣu hotā sumnaṁ vāṁ sūrir vṛṣaṇāv iyakṣan ||
  • Prastutir vāṁ dhāma: This phrase can be interpreted as "the presentation of your essence." It emphasizes the importance of expressing one's true self and values in a way that is clear and impactful.
  • Na prayuktir ayāmi: This translates to "there is no misuse of your power." It suggests that one should use their abilities and resources wisely and ethically, avoiding any form of exploitation or harm.
  • Mitrāvaruṇā suvṛktiḥ: This refers to "the auspiciousness of the divine forces, Mitra and Varuna." Mitra represents friendship and harmony, while Varuna symbolizes cosmic order and truth. Together, they signify the importance of maintaining balance and integrity in relationships and actions.
  • Anakti yad vāṁ vidatheṣu hotā: This means "the one who invokes you in the gatherings." It highlights the significance of community and collective efforts, where individuals come together to support and uplift each other.
  • Sumnaṁ vāṁ sūrir vṛṣaṇāv iyakṣan: This can be interpreted as "may the wise ones bring you prosperity and blessings." It emphasizes the role of wisdom and guidance in achieving success and well-being.

  • Real-Life Example: In a workplace setting, this mantra can be applied to team dynamics. When team members present their ideas (prastutir vāṁ dhāma) clearly and ethically (na prayuktir ayāmi), they foster a collaborative environment (mitrāvaruṇā suvṛktiḥ). By supporting each other in meetings (anakti yad vāṁ vidatheṣu hotā), they can achieve collective goals and receive guidance from experienced colleagues (sumnaṁ vāṁ sūrir vṛṣaṇāv iyakṣan).

  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for authenticity, ethical behavior, community support, and the pursuit of wisdom. In real-life scenarios, it serves as a reminder to individuals and groups to act with integrity, collaborate harmoniously, and seek guidance from those with experience. By embodying these principles, one can create a positive impact in their personal and professional lives.
