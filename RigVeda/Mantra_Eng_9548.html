मन्त्र: दिवो वा सानु स्पृशतां वरियः पृथिव्यां वा मातृया वि श्रयध्वम् | उषतीर द्वारो महिना महद्भिर देवं रथं रथयुर धारणध्वम् ||
divo vā sānu spṛśatā varīyaḥ pṛthivyā vā mātrayā vi śrayadhvam | uśatīr dvāro mahinā mahadbhir devaṁ rathaṁ rathayur dhārayadhvam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • Divo: Of the heavens
    • vā: or
    • Sānu: touching
    • spṛśatā: with great strength
    • varīyaḥ: the powerful
    • Pṛthivyā: of the earth
    • vā: or
    • Mātrayā: with the motherly
    • vi: indeed
    • śrayadhvam: may you support
    • Uśatīr: the auspicious
    • dvāro: doors
    • mahinā: with greatness
    • mahadbhir: with the mighty
    • Devaṁ: the divine
    • Rathaṁ: the chariot
    • Rathayur: of the charioteers
    • dhārayadhvam: may you hold
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the divine forces of the heavens and the earth, emphasizing the importance of strength and support.
    • The phrase "divo vā sānu spṛśatā varīyaḥ" suggests a connection between the celestial and the earthly realms, highlighting the need for powerful influences to touch our lives.
    • "Pṛthivyā vā mātrayā vi śrayadhvam" indicates the nurturing aspect of the earth, urging us to seek support from both the divine and the maternal forces present in nature.
    • The mention of "uśatīr dvāro" refers to the auspicious doors or opportunities that open up when we align ourselves with these powerful energies.
    • Finally, "devaṁ rathaṁ rathayur dhārayadhvam" calls upon the divine charioteers to guide and support us in our journey through life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In personal development, this mantra can be applied when seeking guidance and strength during challenging times. For instance, when facing a difficult decision, one might meditate on this mantra to invoke clarity and support.
    • In community building, the essence of this mantra can inspire collective efforts to create a nurturing environment, where individuals support each other like the earth supports its inhabitants.
    • In leadership, the idea of divine charioteers can be reflected in how leaders guide their teams, ensuring that they are supported and empowered to achieve their goals.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the interconnectedness of the divine and earthly realms, emphasizing the importance of strength, support, and auspicious opportunities in our lives.
    • It serves as a reminder to seek guidance from higher powers while also recognizing the nurturing aspects of our environment.
    • In real-life scenarios, it encourages individuals and communities to foster supportive relationships and to embrace the strength that comes from unity and divine guidance.
