अस्येदेषा सुमतिः पप्रथानाभवत् पूर्व्या भूमानā गौः |अस्य सनीळा असुरस्य योनौ समान आ भरणे बिभ्रमाणाः ||
asyed eṣā sumatiḥ paprathānābhavat pūrvyā bhūmanā gauḥ | asya sanīḻā asurasya yonau samāna ā bharaṇe bibhramāṇāḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • asyed: this (referring to the subject of the mantra)
    • eṣā: this (feminine form, indicating a quality or state)
    • sumatiḥ: good understanding or wisdom
    • paprathānābhavat: has become widely known or has spread
    • pūrvyā: from the past or ancient
    • bhūmanā: great or vast
    • gauḥ: cow (symbolizing wealth and nourishment)
    • asya: of this (referring to the subject again)
    • sanīḻā: of the divine or celestial
    • asurasya: of the asuras (often representing powerful beings)
    • yonau: in the womb or source
    • samāna: equal or similar
    • ā: towards
    • bharaṇe: in the act of bearing or carrying
    • bibhramāṇāḥ: those who are holding or possessing
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the emergence of wisdom and understanding that has been passed down from ancient times.
    • The reference to the cow symbolizes nourishment and prosperity, indicating that wisdom is a source of sustenance for life.
    • The mention of asuras suggests a balance between divine and powerful forces, emphasizing the importance of wisdom in navigating life's challenges.
    • It highlights the idea that wisdom is not just an individual trait but a collective heritage that has been nurtured over time.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern society, the importance of wisdom can be seen in leadership. Leaders who draw from historical knowledge and collective experiences tend to make better decisions.
    • In education, teaching students about past philosophies and wisdom can help them develop critical thinking skills and a deeper understanding of the world.
    • In personal relationships, wisdom gained from past experiences can guide individuals in making better choices and fostering healthier connections.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of wisdom as a guiding force in life, rooted in ancient knowledge and collective experience.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from leadership and education to personal relationships, emphasizing that wisdom is essential for growth and prosperity.
    • By embracing the teachings of the past and recognizing the value of wisdom, individuals and societies can navigate challenges more effectively and cultivate a more harmonious existence.
