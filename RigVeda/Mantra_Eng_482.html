उत तिष्ठ ब्रह्मणस पते देवयन्तस त्वेमहे |उप प्र यन्तु मरुतः सुदानव इन्द्र प्राशूर भव सचा ||
ut tiṣṭha brahmaṇas pate devayantas tvemahe | upa pra yantu marutaḥ sudānava indra prāśūr bhavā sacā ||
  • ut tiṣṭha: This phrase translates to "arise" or "get up." It signifies the call to action, urging individuals to awaken to their true potential and responsibilities.
  • brahmaṇas pate: This means "O Lord of the Brahman." Here, "Brahman" refers to the ultimate reality or cosmic spirit in Hindu philosophy, while "pate" denotes lordship. This phrase acknowledges a higher power guiding and overseeing the universe.
  • devayantas tvemahe: This translates to "we invoke you, O divine ones." It is a call to the divine forces or deities, seeking their presence and assistance in our endeavors.
  • upa pra yantu marutaḥ: This means "may the Maruts come near." The Maruts are storm deities associated with strength and power. This line expresses a desire for the arrival of these powerful forces to aid in our pursuits.
  • sudānava: This term can be interpreted as "well-born" or "noble." It emphasizes the importance of noble qualities and virtues in our actions and intentions.
  • indra prāśūr bhavā: This translates to "may Indra, the powerful one, be with us." Indra is the king of the gods and represents strength, courage, and leadership. Invoking Indra signifies a request for strength and guidance in overcoming challenges.
  • sacā: This word means "with truth" or "with sincerity." It highlights the importance of honesty and integrity in our actions and interactions.

Conclusion: The mantra serves as a powerful invocation for awakening, divine assistance, and the embodiment of noble qualities. In real-life scenarios, it can be applied in various contexts such as:

  • In personal development, it encourages individuals to rise to their potential and act with purpose.
  • In leadership roles, it emphasizes the importance of seeking guidance from higher principles and embodying strength and integrity.
  • In community and teamwork, it fosters a spirit of collaboration and the pursuit of noble goals, invoking the support of collective energies.

This mantra is significant as it encapsulates the essence of striving for excellence, seeking divine guidance, and acting with truthfulness, making it relevant in both personal and communal aspects of life.
