नू नो रयिम पूर्वीरम् बृहन्तं दसरā मिमाथाम उभयेष्व अस्मे |नरो यद वाम अश्विनā स्तोमम् आवन सधस्तुतिम आजमीळ्हासो अग्मन् ||
nū no rayim puruvīram bṛhantaṁ dasrā mimāthām ubhayeṣv asme | naro yad vām aśvinā stomam āvan sadhastutim ājamīḻhāso agman ||
  • nū no rayim puruvīram bṛhantaṁ: This phrase can be translated to "We seek the abundant wealth and strength." Here, "rayim" refers to wealth or prosperity, while "puruvīram" signifies strength or valor. In real life, this can be related to the pursuit of success in various fields, such as career, education, or personal development.
  • dasrā mimāthām ubhayeṣv asme: This part means "May we be endowed with both forms of prosperity." The term "dasrā" implies duality or twofold aspects, suggesting that prosperity can come in various forms, such as material wealth and spiritual fulfillment. For example, a person may achieve financial success while also nurturing relationships and personal growth.
  • naro yad vām aśvinā stomam: This translates to "O Ashvins, grant us your praise." The Ashvins are twin deities associated with health, medicine, and well-being. This line emphasizes the importance of seeking divine blessings for health and vitality. In everyday life, this can be seen in the practice of maintaining physical and mental health through balanced living, exercise, and mindfulness.
  • āvan sadhastutim ājamīḻhāso agman: This can be interpreted as "May we attain the highest praise and be free from obstacles." The word "sadhastutim" refers to achieving success and recognition, while "ājamīḻhāso" suggests overcoming challenges. In practical terms, this encourages individuals to strive for excellence in their endeavors while remaining resilient in the face of difficulties.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a holistic approach to life, emphasizing the pursuit of wealth, strength, health, and success while acknowledging the need for divine support. Its significance lies in the reminder that true prosperity encompasses both material and spiritual dimensions. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire individuals to seek balance in their lives, striving for success in their careers while also nurturing their health and relationships. It encourages a mindset of resilience and gratitude, fostering a harmonious existence.
