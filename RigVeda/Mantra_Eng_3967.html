कथा दाशेम नमसां सुदानून एवया मरुतो अच्छोक्तौ प्रश्रवसो मरुतो अच्छोक्तौ |मा नो 'हिर बुध्न्यो ऋषे धाद अस्माकं भूद उपमातिवनिः ||
kathā dāśema namasā sudānūn evayā maruto acchoktau praśravaso maruto acchoktau | mā no 'hir budhnyo riṣe dhād asmākam bhūd upamātivaniḥ ||
  • Mantra: "kathā dāśema namasā sudānūn evayā maruto acchoktau praśravaso maruto acchoktau | mā no 'hir budhnyo riṣe dhād asmākam bhūd upamātivaniḥ ||"
  • Translation: This mantra can be interpreted as a prayer for guidance and support from the Maruts, the storm deities, asking them to bless us with their strength and wisdom.
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • kathā: How
    • dāśema: May we attain
    • namasā: With reverence
    • sudānūn: The good and noble
    • evayā: In this way
    • maruto: O Maruts (storm deities)
    • acchoktau: With strength
    • praśravaso: With great fame
    • mā: May not
    • no: Us
    • 'hir: To be harmed
    • budhnyo: By the wise
    • riṣe: By the sage
    • dhād: May grant
    • asmākam: Our
    • bhūd: Be
    • upamātivaniḥ: Like the best
  • Significance: This mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking divine guidance and support in our endeavors. The Maruts symbolize strength, vitality, and the power of nature, reminding us that we are part of a larger universe and that we can draw upon its energies for our growth and success.
  • Real-Life Application:
    • In challenging situations, such as during exams or important presentations, individuals can recite this mantra to invoke confidence and clarity of thought.
    • In times of uncertainty, such as starting a new job or moving to a new place, this mantra serves as a reminder to seek guidance and support from both the divine and the community.
    • In personal development, the mantra encourages individuals to strive for nobility and goodness in their actions, fostering a sense of purpose and direction.
  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the interconnectedness of life and the importance of seeking strength and wisdom from higher powers. By incorporating this mantra into daily life, individuals can cultivate resilience, clarity, and a sense of purpose, ultimately leading to personal and communal growth.
