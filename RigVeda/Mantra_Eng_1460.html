इता ऊतीर आयुñजत समानम् अर्थम् अक्षितम् |धेनूर इव मनवे विश्वदोहसो जनाय विश्वदोहसः ||
ita ūtīr ayuñjata samānam artham akṣitam | dhenūr iva manave viśvadohaso janāya viśvadohasaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ita: here, in this context
    • ūtīr: to strive, to work together
    • ayuñjata: to unite, to join
    • samānam: similar, equal
    • artham: purpose, meaning
    • akṣitam: unbroken, eternal
    • dhenūr: like a cow, nurturing
    • iva: like, as if
    • manave: to mankind, to humanity
    • viśvadohaso: of universal well-being
    • janāya: for the people, for the community
    • viśvadohasaḥ: of universal nourishment
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of unity and collaboration among people.
    • It suggests that when individuals come together with a common purpose, they can achieve greater things, much like a nurturing cow that provides sustenance.
    • The phrase "samānam artham akṣitam" indicates that the shared goals and values are eternal and unbreakable.
    • It highlights the significance of working towards the well-being of the community and humanity as a whole.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace, when team members collaborate effectively, they can achieve project goals more efficiently, demonstrating the power of unity.
    • Community service initiatives, where individuals come together to help those in need, reflect the essence of this mantra.
    • In family settings, when members support each other, they create a nurturing environment that fosters growth and happiness.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the strength found in unity and collaboration.
    • Its significance lies in promoting a sense of community and shared purpose, which is essential for the well-being of society.
    • In real-life scenarios, applying the principles of this mantra can lead to more harmonious relationships, successful teamwork, and a supportive environment for all.
