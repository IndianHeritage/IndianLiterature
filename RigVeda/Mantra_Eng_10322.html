प्र नो यच्छत् आर्यमाऽ प्र भगः प्र बृहस्पतिः |प्र देवाः प्रोत सूनृताः रायो देवी ददातु नः ||
pra no yacchatv aryamā pra bhagaḥ pra bṛhaspatiḥ | pra devāḥ prota sūnṛtā rāyo devī dadātu naḥ ||
  • pra no yacchatv aryamā: This phrase can be interpreted as a call for the divine to bestow upon us the qualities of Aryama, who is often associated with nobility and virtue. In real life, this can be seen as a request for guidance and support in our moral and ethical endeavors.
  • pra bhagaḥ: The term "bhaga" refers to prosperity and abundance. This part of the mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking blessings for wealth and success. For example, in a business context, this could relate to the desire for fruitful ventures and financial stability.
  • pra bṛhaspatiḥ: Bṛhaspati is the deity of wisdom and knowledge. This phrase signifies the importance of seeking wisdom in our actions and decisions. In everyday life, this can be applied to the pursuit of education and the importance of making informed choices.
  • pra devāḥ prota sūnṛtā: This line invokes the deities to grant us the quality of "sūnṛtā," which means truthfulness and righteousness. In practical terms, this can be seen in the importance of honesty in personal relationships and professional dealings.
  • rāyo devī dadātu naḥ: This concluding part of the mantra is a plea for the divine to grant us the blessings of the goddess of wealth (Lakshmi). It highlights the aspiration for prosperity and well-being in our lives. In real life, this can be reflected in our efforts to create a stable and prosperous environment for ourselves and our families.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a holistic approach to life, emphasizing the importance of virtue, wisdom, truthfulness, and prosperity. Its significance lies in the reminder to seek divine guidance in our moral and ethical pursuits while striving for success and abundance. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire individuals to act with integrity, pursue knowledge, and work towards creating a prosperous life, both personally and professionally.
