व्यश्वस् त्वा वसुविदम् उक्षण्युर अप्रीणाद ऋषिः |महो राये तं उ त्वा सम इधीमहि ||
vyaśvas tvā vasuvidam ukṣaṇyur aprīṇād ṛṣiḥ | maho rāye tam u tvā sam idhīmahi ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • vyaśvas: A term that can be interpreted as "one who is all-pervading" or "universal."
    • tvā: "You" or "to you," indicating a direct address.
    • vasuvidam: Refers to "the one who knows the essence of wealth" or "the giver of prosperity."
    • ukṣaṇyur: "The one who is full of strength" or "the powerful one."
    • aprīṇād: "Not to be displeased" or "one who is always pleased."
    • ṛṣiḥ: "The sage" or "the seer," indicating wisdom and knowledge.
    • maho: "Great" or "mighty," often used to denote something of significant importance.
    • rāye: "Wealth" or "prosperity," referring to material and spiritual abundance.
    • tam: "That" or "him," referring to the divine or the object of worship.
    • u: "And" or "also," used to connect thoughts.
    • tvā: Again, "you," emphasizing the focus on the divine.
    • sam: "Together" or "in unity," indicating collective intention.
    • idhīmahi: "We meditate" or "we contemplate," suggesting a deep focus on the divine.
  • Overall Meaning:
    • This mantra is a prayer directed towards a divine entity, acknowledging its universal presence and power.
    • It emphasizes the importance of wealth, both material and spiritual, and the need for collective meditation and focus on the divine.
    • The invocation of the sage suggests that wisdom and knowledge are essential in understanding and attaining prosperity.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, this mantra can be applied during team meetings where members focus on collective goals, emphasizing unity and shared prosperity.
    • In personal life, individuals can use this mantra during meditation to seek guidance and strength in their endeavors, fostering a sense of abundance and wisdom.
    • Communities can come together to chant this mantra during festivals or gatherings, reinforcing the idea of collective prosperity and spiritual growth.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of all beings and the importance of seeking wisdom and unity in the pursuit of prosperity.
    • Its significance lies in its ability to inspire individuals and communities to focus on collective goals, fostering a sense of abundance and spiritual growth.
    • By meditating on this mantra, one can cultivate a mindset of gratitude and openness to the wealth of experiences life has to offer.
