तं अग्नि प्रीतनाशं रयिं सहस्व आ भर |त्वं हि सत्यो अद्भुतो दाता वाजस्य गोमताḥ ||
tam agne pṛtanāṣahaṁ rayiṁ sahasva ā bhara | tvaṁ hi satyo adbhuto dātā vājasya gomataḥ ||
  • tam agne: This phrase translates to "O Agni," where Agni represents the fire god in Vedic literature. Fire is often seen as a purifying force and a symbol of energy and transformation.
  • pṛtanāṣahaṁ: This can be interpreted as "I offer" or "I invoke." It signifies the act of devotion and the desire to connect with the divine.
  • rayiṁ: This word means "wealth" or "prosperity." In a broader sense, it can refer to all forms of abundance, including spiritual, emotional, and material wealth.
  • sahasva: This translates to "may you be strong" or "may you be powerful." It emphasizes the importance of strength and vitality in achieving one's goals.
  • ā bhara: This means "bring forth" or "bestow." It is a call for the divine to provide blessings and abundance.
  • tvaṁ hi: This translates to "for you are." It acknowledges the divine presence and attributes qualities to Agni.
  • satyo: This means "truth" or "truthful." It highlights the importance of honesty and integrity in one's life.
  • adbhuto: This translates to "wonderful" or "extraordinary." It signifies the awe-inspiring nature of the divine and the universe.
  • dātā: This means "giver" or "donor." It emphasizes the role of Agni as a provider of blessings and resources.
  • vājasya: This translates to "of strength" or "of vigor." It relates to the idea of power and energy in life.
  • gomataḥ: This means "of the cow," which is a symbol of nourishment and sustenance in Vedic culture. Cows are often associated with wealth and prosperity.

Conclusion: The mantra "tam agne pṛtanāṣahaṁ rayiṁ sahasva ā bhara | tvaṁ hi satyo adbhuto dātā vājasya gomataḥ ||" is a powerful invocation to Agni, seeking blessings of wealth, strength, and truth. In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as during rituals for prosperity, in moments of seeking guidance, or when striving for personal growth. It serves as a reminder of the importance of integrity, the pursuit of abundance, and the recognition of the divine forces that support us in our endeavors. By invoking Agni, individuals can cultivate a sense of connection to the greater universe and draw upon its energy to manifest their desires and aspirations.
