अधā त्वं हि नस् करो विश्वा अस्मभ्यं सुख्षितीः |वाजद्रविणसो गिरः ||
adhā tvaṁ hi nas karo viśvā asmabhyaṁ sukṣitīḥ | vājadraviṇaso giraḥ ||
  • adhā tvaṁ hi nas karo viśvā asmabhyaṁ sukṣitīḥ
    • adhā - This word translates to "down" or "below," indicating a sense of grounding or humility.
    • tvaṁ - This means "you," referring to a divine entity or force.
    • hi - This is an emphatic particle, often translated as "indeed" or "certainly."
    • nas - This means "us" or "to us," indicating a collective or community aspect.
    • karo - This translates to "do" or "make," suggesting action or creation.
    • viśvā - This means "all" or "universal," indicating inclusivity.
    • asmabhyaṁ - This translates to "for us," reinforcing the communal aspect.
    • sukṣitīḥ - This means "well-being" or "prosperity," referring to a state of health and happiness.
  • vājadraviṇaso giraḥ
    • vājadraviṇaso - This term can be broken down into "vāja," meaning "strength" or "power," and "draviṇa," meaning "wealth" or "resources."
    • giraḥ - This translates to "speech" or "words," indicating the power of communication and expression.

The mantra can be interpreted as a prayer or invocation for divine assistance in achieving universal well-being and prosperity through collective action and communication. It emphasizes the importance of humility and community in the pursuit of strength and resources.


Real-Life Examples:

  • In a community project aimed at improving local infrastructure, the mantra's essence can be seen in how individuals come together, grounded in humility, to create a better environment for all.
  • In a corporate setting, this mantra can inspire teamwork, where employees work collaboratively to enhance the company's resources and overall well-being.
  • In personal relationships, the mantra encourages open communication and mutual support, fostering a sense of belonging and prosperity among friends and family.

Conclusion:

This mantra signifies the importance of collective effort, humility, and effective communication in achieving well-being and prosperity. It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from community initiatives to personal relationships, reminding us that our actions, grounded in a sense of unity and purpose, can lead to a more prosperous and harmonious existence.
