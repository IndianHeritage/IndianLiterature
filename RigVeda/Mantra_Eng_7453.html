आ वां विश्वाभिर ऊतिभिः प्रियमेधा अहूषत |तां वर्तिर यातम उप वृक्तबरिषो जुष्टं यज्ञं दिविष्टिषु ||
ā vāṁ viśvābhir ūtibhiḥ priyamedhā ahūṣata | tā vartir yātam upa vṛktabarhiṣo juṣṭaṁ yajñaṁ diviṣṭiṣu ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā vāṁ: From us
    • viśvābhir: With all (the powers)
    • ūtibhiḥ: With offerings
    • priyamedhā: With a loving mind
    • ahūṣata: They have called
    • tā: Those
    • vartir: The path
    • yātam: May we go
    • upa: Near
    • vṛktabarhiṣo: To the well-prepared offerings
    • juṣṭaṁ: Enjoyed
    • yajñaṁ: The sacrifice
    • diviṣṭiṣu: In the heavenly realms
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of collective effort and unity in spiritual practices.
    • The phrase "from us with all the powers" suggests that when individuals come together with a common purpose, their collective energy can create a powerful impact.
    • The term "loving mind" indicates that the intention behind actions is crucial; actions performed with love and devotion yield better results.
    • The call to "may we go near the well-prepared offerings" signifies the importance of preparation and sincerity in rituals and offerings.
    • Finally, the reference to "in the heavenly realms" implies that such sincere efforts can lead to spiritual elevation and fulfillment.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In community service, when people come together with a shared goal, such as cleaning a park or helping the needy, their combined efforts can lead to significant positive changes in the community.
    • In a workplace, teams that collaborate with a positive mindset and mutual respect often achieve better results than those that work in isolation or with negative attitudes.
    • In personal relationships, approaching conflicts with love and understanding can lead to resolution and deeper connections.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra teaches us the significance of unity, intention, and preparation in our actions.
    • It is applicable in various aspects of life, including community work, professional environments, and personal relationships.
    • By embodying the principles of collective effort and loving intention, we can create a more harmonious and fulfilling existence, both individually and collectively.
