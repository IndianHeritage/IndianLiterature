मन्त्र: पूर्वो देवाः भवतु सुन्वतो रथोऽस्माकं शंसो अभ्यस्तु दूद्यः |तद् आ जानीतोऽत पूष्यतां वचोऽग्ने सख्ये मा ऋषामाः वयं तव ||
pūrvo devā bhavatu sunvato ratho 'smākaṁ śaṁso abhy astu dūḍhyaḥ | tad ā jānītota puṣyatā vaco 'gne sakhye mā riṣāmā vayaṁ tava ||
  • pūrvo devā bhavatu: This phrase translates to "May the ancient gods be present." It emphasizes the importance of invoking the blessings of the deities who have been revered since time immemorial. In real life, this can be seen in various cultures where people seek the blessings of their ancestors or spiritual guides before embarking on new ventures.
  • sunvato ratho 'smākaṁ: This means "may the chariot of our desires be fulfilled." The chariot symbolizes the journey of life and the aspirations we hold. In modern terms, this can relate to personal goals, such as career ambitions or family aspirations, where one hopes for success and fulfillment.
  • śaṁso abhy astu dūḍhyaḥ: This translates to "may there be prosperity and abundance." It reflects a desire for wealth and well-being. In everyday life, this can be related to financial stability, health, and overall happiness, which are common aspirations for individuals and families.
  • tad ā jānītota puṣyatā vaco: This means "may we understand the fruitful words." It suggests the importance of wisdom and knowledge in making decisions. In real life, this can be applied to the pursuit of education and learning, where understanding and applying knowledge leads to better outcomes.
  • 'agne sakhye mā riṣāmā vayaṁ tava: This translates to "O Agni, may we not be angry with you." Agni, the fire god, represents transformation and purification. This line emphasizes the need for harmony and respect in relationships, whether personal or professional. In contemporary life, it can be seen in the importance of maintaining good relationships and resolving conflicts amicably.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda encapsulates the essence of seeking divine blessings, prosperity, wisdom, and harmonious relationships. Its significance lies in the universal human desire for success, understanding, and peace. In real life, it serves as a reminder to honor our roots, pursue our goals with integrity, and foster positive connections with others. By embodying these principles, individuals can navigate their journeys with purpose and fulfillment.
