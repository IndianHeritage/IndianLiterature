सम अन्या यन्त्य उप यन्त्य अन्याḥ समानम् ऊर्वं नद्यः पृणन्ति |तम ū शुचिं शुचयो दीदिवांसम् अपां नपातम् परि तस्थुर आपः ||
sam anyā yanty upa yanty anyāḥ samānam ūrvaṁ nadyaḥ pṛṇanti | tam ū śuciṁ śucayo dīdivāṁsam apāṁ napātam pari tasthur āpaḥ ||
  • Mantra: "sam anyā yanty upa yanty anyāḥ samānam ūrvaṁ nadyaḥ pṛṇanti | tam ū śuciṁ śucayo dīdivāṁsam apāṁ napātam pari tasthur āpaḥ ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sam: together, in harmony
    • anyā: others, different
    • yanty: go, move
    • upa: towards, near
    • ūrvaṁ: the earth, the ground
    • nadyaḥ: rivers
    • pṛṇanti: fill, nourish
    • tam: that, him
    • ū: indeed, certainly
    • śuciṁ: pure, clean
    • śucayo: those who are pure
    • dīdivāṁsam: shining, radiant
    • apāṁ: of waters
    • napātam: offspring, progeny
    • pari: around, all around
    • tasthur: they have established
    • āpaḥ: waters
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra speaks about the interconnectedness of different elements in nature, particularly focusing on rivers and waters.
    • The phrase "sam anyā yanty" suggests that while different rivers may flow in various directions, they ultimately contribute to the same purpose of nourishing the earth.
    • The rivers, described as "pṛṇanti," symbolize the life-giving force of water, which is essential for all living beings.
    • The mantra emphasizes purity and radiance, as seen in "śuciṁ śucayo dīdivāṁsam," indicating that purity in thought and action leads to a brighter existence.
    • In real life, this can be related to how diverse communities and cultures can coexist and contribute to a harmonious society, much like rivers that flow together towards a common destination.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its reminder of the unity in diversity. It teaches us that while we may have different paths and identities, we all contribute to the greater good.
    • In practical terms, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as promoting teamwork in workplaces, fostering community spirit, and encouraging environmental conservation efforts.
    • By recognizing our interconnectedness, we can work towards a more harmonious and sustainable world, much like the rivers that nourish the earth together.
