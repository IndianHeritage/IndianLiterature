मन्त्र: नि गव्यवोऽनवो द्रुह्यवश्च षष्टिः शताऽसुशुपुः षट् सहस्राः |षष्टिर् वीरासोऽधि षड् दुवोयु विश्वेद् इन्द्रस्य वीर्याणि कृतानि ||
ni gavyavo 'navo druhyavaś ca ṣaṣṭiḥ śatā suṣupuḥ ṣaṭ sahasrā | ṣaṣṭir vīrāso adhi ṣaḍ duvoyu viśved indrasya vīryā kṛtāni ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ni gavyavo: "not to be harmed" or "not to be injured."
    • 'navo: "new" or "fresh."
    • druhyavaś: "those who are hostile" or "enemies."
    • ca: "and."
    • ṣaṣṭiḥ: "sixty."
    • śatā: "hundred."
    • suṣupuḥ: "well-protected" or "well-guarded."
    • ṣaṭ: "six."
    • sahasrā: "thousand."
    • ṣaṣṭir: "the sixth."
    • vīrāso: "of the brave" or "of the heroes."
    • adhi: "above" or "over."
    • ṣaḍ: "six."
    • duvoyu: "twofold" or "double."
    • viśved: "all" or "universal."
    • indrasya: "of Indra" (the king of gods in Vedic tradition).
    • vīryā: "strengths" or "powers."
    • kṛtāni: "accomplished" or "done."
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the protection and strength that comes from unity and bravery.
    • The mention of "sixty" and "hundred" signifies abundance and the idea of collective strength.
    • It emphasizes the importance of being well-guarded against enemies and challenges.
    • The reference to Indra suggests invoking divine strength and support in overcoming obstacles.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, this mantra can be applied to teamwork, where the collective strength of a team can overcome challenges that an individual might face alone.
    • In personal life, it can be seen in community support during difficult times, where the unity of individuals provides protection and strength.
    • In sports, a team working together with a common goal exemplifies the idea of collective bravery and strength against opponents.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of unity, strength, and protection against adversities.
    • It teaches us that by coming together, we can achieve greater accomplishments and safeguard ourselves from harm.
    • In real life, whether in personal relationships, community efforts, or professional environments, the principles of collaboration and mutual support are essential for success and resilience.
