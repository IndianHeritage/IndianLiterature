पितā यत् स्वां दुहितरम् अधिष्कन् क्षमया रेतः संजग्मानो नि शिञ्चत् |स्वाध्यो जनयन ब्रह्म देवā वास्तोष पतिं व्रतपां निर अतक्षन् ||
pitā yat svāṁ duhitaram adhiṣkan kṣmayā retaḥ saṁjagmāno ni ṣiñcat | svādhyo 'janayan brahma devā vāstoṣ patiṁ vratapāṁ nir atakṣan ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • pitā: father
    • yat: who
    • svāṁ: his own
    • duhitaram: daughter
    • adhiṣkan: to protect or support
    • kṣmayā: with kindness or compassion
    • retaḥ: seed or essence
    • saṁjagmāno: having come together
    • ni ṣiñcat: to nourish or sustain
    • svādhyo: self-born or self-created
    • ajanayan: giving birth
    • brahma: the ultimate reality or cosmic spirit
    • devā: gods or divine beings
    • vāstoṣ: of the dwelling or home
    • patiṁ: lord or master
    • vratapāṁ: of those who observe vows
    • nir: without
    • atakṣan: being disturbed or harmed
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the role of a father in nurturing and protecting his daughter.
    • It highlights the importance of compassion and kindness in familial relationships.
    • The act of nourishing and supporting one's family is seen as a divine duty.
    • It suggests that through love and care, one can create a harmonious and sacred environment.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • A father who actively participates in his daughter's education and emotional well-being exemplifies the essence of this mantra.
    • In a community, when families come together to support each other, they embody the spirit of compassion and unity described in the mantra.
    • Organizations that promote family welfare and support systems for women reflect the nurturing aspect of this teaching.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of familial bonds and the responsibilities that come with them.
    • It is applicable in real-life scenarios where compassion, support, and nurturing are essential for personal and communal growth.
    • By embodying the principles of this mantra, individuals can contribute to a more harmonious society, fostering love and understanding within families and communities.
