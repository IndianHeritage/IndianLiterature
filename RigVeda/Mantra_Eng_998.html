मन्त्र: सोम गीरभिष् त्वा वयं वर्धयामो वचोविदः | सुमृळीको न आ विश ||
soma gīrbhiṣ ṭvā vayaṁ vardhayāmo vacovidaḥ | sumṛḻīko na ā viśa ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • soma: Refers to the sacred drink or elixir, often associated with immortality and divine inspiration.
    • gīrbhiṣ: Means "with hymns" or "through songs," indicating the use of vocal expressions or prayers.
    • ṭvā: Translates to "you," addressing a divine entity or force.
    • vayaṁ: Means "we," indicating a collective group or community.
    • vardhayāmo: Translates to "we increase" or "we enhance," suggesting growth or elevation.
    • vacovidaḥ: Refers to "those who know speech" or "those who are knowledgeable in words," indicating wise individuals or sages.
    • sumṛḻīko: Means "pleasant" or "sweet," often referring to the nature of the speech or communication.
    • na: Translates to "not," indicating negation.
    • ā viśa: Means "may you not enter" or "may you not come," suggesting a plea for protection or avoidance of negativity.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the divine essence of Soma, emphasizing the importance of sacred communication and the collective effort to enhance wisdom and knowledge.
    • It highlights the role of hymns and prayers in connecting with the divine and seeking blessings for growth and positivity.
    • The plea for protection against negativity signifies the desire for a harmonious and uplifting environment, both spiritually and socially.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community gathering, people often come together to share stories, songs, and prayers, enhancing their collective spirit and fostering a sense of unity.
    • In educational settings, the importance of communication and knowledge sharing is paramount. Teachers and students engage in discussions that promote growth and understanding.
    • In personal relationships, expressing oneself through kind words and supportive communication can strengthen bonds and create a positive atmosphere.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its emphasis on the power of collective communication and the pursuit of wisdom.
    • It serves as a reminder of the importance of uplifting speech and the need to protect oneself and the community from negativity.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as community gatherings, educational environments, and personal relationships, promoting harmony and growth.
