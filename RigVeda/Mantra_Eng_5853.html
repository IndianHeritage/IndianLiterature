ते सत्येन मनसा दीध्यानाḥ स्वेन युक्तासः क्रतुनā वहन्ति |इन्द्रवायू वीरवाहं रथं वाम ईशानयोः अभि पृक्षः साचन्ते ||
te satyena manasā dīdhyānāḥ svena yuktāsaḥ kratunā vahanti | indravāyū vīravāhaṁ rathaṁ vām īśānayor abhi pṛkṣaḥ sacante ||
  • te satyena manasā dīdhyānāḥ: This phrase can be translated as "They, with their minds focused on truth." Here, "satyena" refers to truth, and "manasā" refers to the mind. It emphasizes the importance of aligning one's thoughts with truthfulness.
  • svena yuktāsaḥ kratunā vahanti: This means "They carry their intentions with their own wisdom." "Svena" means one's own, "yuktāsaḥ" refers to being wise or intelligent, and "kratunā" means intention or resolve. This suggests that individuals should act with their own understanding and determination.
  • indravāyū vīravāhaṁ rathaṁ: This translates to "The chariot of the brave, driven by Indra and Vayu." "Indra" is the king of gods, representing strength and power, while "Vayu" symbolizes the wind and movement. This line signifies the importance of courage and divine support in one's endeavors.
  • vām īśānayor abhi pṛkṣaḥ sacante: This can be interpreted as "You, who are the masters of the two, observe the truth." "Vām" refers to the two, possibly indicating duality in nature or aspects of life, while "īśānayor" means masters or controllers. "Pṛkṣaḥ" means to observe, and "sacante" refers to the truth. This line encourages individuals to be vigilant and aware of the truth in their lives.

Conclusion: This mantra emphasizes the significance of truth, wisdom, courage, and awareness in our lives. In real-life scenarios, it can be applied in various contexts such as personal development, leadership, and decision-making. For instance, a leader who embodies these qualities can inspire their team to pursue goals with integrity and determination. By focusing on truth and wisdom, individuals can navigate challenges effectively, making informed choices that lead to success. Ultimately, this mantra serves as a reminder to align our thoughts and actions with higher principles, fostering a life of purpose and fulfillment.
