तवेमे सप्त सिन्धवः प्राशिषं सोम सिस्रते |तुभ्यं धावन्ति धेनवः ||
taveme sapta sindhavaḥ praśiṣaṁ soma sisrate | tubhyaṁ dhāvanti dhenavaḥ ||
  • Mantra: "taveme sapta sindhavaḥ praśiṣaṁ soma sisrate | tubhyaṁ dhāvanti dhenavaḥ ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • taveme: "to you" or "for you"
    • sapta: "seven"
    • sindhavaḥ: "rivers"
    • praśiṣaṁ: "blessings" or "prosperity"
    • soma: "soma" (a sacred drink in Vedic rituals)
    • sisrate: "flow" or "stream"
    • tubhyaṁ: "to you" or "for you"
    • dhāvanti: "run" or "flow"
    • dhenavaḥ: "cows" (symbolizing wealth and nourishment)
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the divine, expressing a desire for the blessings of prosperity and abundance.
    • The "seven rivers" symbolize the flow of wealth and resources, akin to the rivers that nourish the land.
    • The mention of "soma" indicates a connection to spiritual nourishment and the importance of rituals in achieving harmony and prosperity.
    • The "cows" represent not just physical wealth but also the nurturing aspect of life, emphasizing the importance of sustenance and care.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In agriculture, the concept of rivers can be likened to irrigation systems that provide water to crops, essential for a bountiful harvest.
    • In business, the flow of resources and wealth can be compared to the successful management of finances, where investments yield fruitful returns.
    • In personal life, nurturing relationships can be seen as the "cows" that provide emotional and spiritual sustenance, leading to a fulfilling life.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the interconnectedness of spiritual and material wealth, highlighting the importance of both in achieving a balanced life.
    • It serves as a reminder to seek blessings and prosperity not just for oneself but for the greater good, fostering a sense of community and shared abundance.
    • In modern contexts, this mantra can inspire individuals and communities to work towards sustainable practices that ensure the flow of resources while nurturing relationships and spiritual well-being.
