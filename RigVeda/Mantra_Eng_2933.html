मन्त्र: विश्वामित्रा अरासत ब्रह्मेन्द्राय वज्रीणि |करदिन् नः सुराधसः ||
viśvāmitrā arāsata brahmendrāya vajriṇe | karad in naḥ surādhasaḥ ||
  • viśvāmitrā: This term refers to the sage Vishwamitra, who is known for his deep spiritual knowledge and his role in the Vedic tradition. He represents the pursuit of wisdom and enlightenment.
  • arāsata: This word can be interpreted as "to be free from obstacles" or "to be unimpeded." It signifies the removal of barriers in the path of spiritual and material progress.
  • brahmendrāya: This term refers to Brahma, the creator god in Hinduism, and Indra, the king of the gods. Together, they symbolize the ultimate authority and power in the universe.
  • vajriṇe: This word means "the one who wields the thunderbolt" or "the powerful." It signifies strength, protection, and the ability to overcome challenges.
  • karad in naḥ: This phrase translates to "may you grant us" or "may you bestow upon us." It is a plea for blessings and support from the divine.
  • surādhasaḥ: This term refers to "the divine beings" or "the gods." It emphasizes the connection between humans and the divine, highlighting the importance of seeking guidance from higher powers.

  • Meaning: The mantra can be understood as a prayer or invocation to the divine, specifically to the sage Vishwamitra and the powerful deities Brahma and Indra. It expresses a desire for the removal of obstacles and the granting of blessings for success and strength in life.

  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, individuals may face various challenges and obstacles in their careers. This mantra can serve as a reminder to seek wisdom and guidance from mentors (akin to Vishwamitra) and to remain resilient (like the thunderbolt) in the face of adversity.
    • In personal life, when facing difficulties such as health issues or relationship problems, one can recite this mantra to invoke divine support and strength, encouraging a mindset of overcoming challenges.

  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine assistance in overcoming obstacles and achieving success. It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal struggles to professional challenges. By invoking the wisdom of sages and the strength of divine beings, individuals can find the courage and guidance needed to navigate life's complexities.
