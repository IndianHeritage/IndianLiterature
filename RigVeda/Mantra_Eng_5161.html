ईळेन्यं वो असुरं सुदक्षम् अन्तर दूतं रोधसी सत्यवाचम् | मनुष्वद अग्निम मनुना समिद्धं सम अध्वराय सदम इन महेम ||
īḻenyaṁ vo asuraṁ sudakṣam antar dūtaṁ rodasī satyavācam | manuṣvad agnim manunā samiddhaṁ sam adhvarāya sadam in mahema ||
  • īḻenyaṁ vo asuraṁ sudakṣam: This phrase can be translated as "O Asuras, may we have the skillful one." Here, "Asuras" refers to powerful beings or deities, and "sudakṣam" means skillful or adept. In real life, this can be related to seeking guidance from knowledgeable and capable individuals in our endeavors.
  • antar dūtaṁ rodasī satyavācam: This translates to "the messenger within the sky who speaks the truth." "Dūtaṁ" means messenger, and "satyavācam" refers to speaking the truth. This can be likened to the importance of honest communication in our relationships and interactions, emphasizing the need for transparency and integrity.
  • manuṣvad agnim manunā samiddhaṁ: This means "the fire that is kindled by humans." "Agnim" refers to fire, which symbolizes energy, passion, and transformation. This can be seen in real life as the drive and motivation we have to pursue our goals, igniting our inner fire to achieve success.
  • sam adhvarāya sadam in mahema: This translates to "for the sacred rituals, may we have a place of abundance." "Adhvarāya" refers to rituals or sacrifices, and "mahema" means abundance or prosperity. This highlights the significance of rituals in our lives, which can be seen in various cultural practices that bring communities together and foster a sense of belonging.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking guidance from skilled individuals, the value of honest communication, the inner drive to pursue our passions, and the role of rituals in fostering community and abundance. In real life, these principles can be applied in various scenarios, such as in professional settings where teamwork and integrity are crucial, in personal relationships where open communication is key, and in cultural practices that enhance social bonds. By embodying these values, we can create a more harmonious and prosperous life.
