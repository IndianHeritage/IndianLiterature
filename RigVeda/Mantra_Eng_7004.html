इषिरेण ते मनसा सुतस्य भक्षीमहि पितृयस्येव रायः |सोम राजा प्र ण आयूँषि तारीर अहानिव सूर्यो वासराणि ||
iṣireṇa te manasā sutasya bhakṣīmahi pitryasyeva rāyaḥ | soma rājan pra ṇa āyūṁṣi tārīr ahānīva sūryo vāsarāṇi ||
  • iṣireṇa te manasā: This phrase can be translated as "with the power of your mind." It emphasizes the importance of mental strength and focus. In real life, this can be related to how individuals harness their thoughts and intentions to achieve their goals, such as a student concentrating on studies to excel in exams.
  • sutasya bhakṣīmahi: This means "we consume the essence of your creation." It signifies the act of taking in or absorbing the benefits of what has been created. For example, when we eat food, we are consuming the essence of nature's bounty, which nourishes our bodies and sustains life.
  • pitryasyeva rāyaḥ: This translates to "like the wealth of the ancestors." It highlights the value of heritage and the blessings passed down through generations. In modern contexts, this can be seen in how families cherish traditions and values that shape their identity and guide their actions.
  • soma rājan: This phrase refers to "the king of Soma," which symbolizes the divine nectar or elixir of life. Soma is often associated with immortality and spiritual enlightenment. In everyday life, this can be likened to the pursuit of knowledge and wisdom, which enriches our lives and helps us grow.
  • pra ṇa āyūṁṣi: This means "may we attain long life." It reflects the human desire for longevity and vitality. In practical terms, this can relate to healthy living practices, such as a balanced diet and regular exercise, which contribute to a longer, healthier life.
  • tārīr ahānīva sūryo vāsarāṇi: This translates to "like the sun that rises every day." It symbolizes consistency, hope, and the cyclical nature of life. In real life, this can be seen in the daily routines we establish, which provide structure and stability, much like the rising sun brings a new day.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of mental strength, the importance of heritage, the pursuit of knowledge, and the desire for longevity. It serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of our thoughts, actions, and the natural world. In real-life scenarios, it encourages individuals to focus their minds on positive outcomes, cherish their roots, seek wisdom, and maintain a healthy lifestyle, all while embracing the cyclical nature of existence. This mantra can be applied in various aspects of life, from personal development to community building, fostering a holistic approach to living.
