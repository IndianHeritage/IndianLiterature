तं उ त्वा वाजसातमम् अंगिरस्वद धवामहे |द्यम्नैर अभि प्रणोनुमः ||
tam u tvā vājasātamam aṅgirasvad dhavāmahe | dyumnair abhi pra ṇonumaḥ ||
  • tam - This word translates to "that" or "him," indicating a focus on a specific entity or concept.
  • u - This is a conjunction meaning "and," used to connect thoughts or ideas.
  • tvā - This means "you," addressing the divine or a higher power directly.
  • vājasātamam - This term can be broken down into "vāja," meaning "strength" or "power," and "sātamam," meaning "the best" or "the most excellent." Together, it refers to the "most excellent strength" or "supreme power."
  • aṅgirasvad - This word relates to "Aṅgiras," a sage or divine figure in Vedic literature, symbolizing wisdom and knowledge.
  • dhavāmahe - This translates to "we invoke" or "we call upon," indicating a plea or request for divine assistance.
  • dyumnair - This word means "with glory" or "with brilliance," suggesting a desire for recognition and honor.
  • abhi - This means "towards" or "in the direction of," indicating a movement or intention towards something.
  • pra - This prefix means "forth" or "forward," enhancing the action of the following word.
  • ṇonumaḥ - This can be interpreted as "we bow" or "we offer our respects," a gesture of reverence and humility.

The mantra can be understood as a call to a divine power, invoking the supreme strength and wisdom of the Aṅgiras sage. It expresses a desire for glory and recognition while humbly bowing in respect. The essence of the mantra is about seeking strength and wisdom from a higher source, acknowledging the importance of divine guidance in achieving excellence.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios:

  • Personal Growth: Individuals can recite this mantra when seeking strength and wisdom in their personal or professional lives, especially during challenging times.
  • Leadership: Leaders can use this invocation to remind themselves of the importance of humility and the need for guidance in their decision-making processes.
  • Community Support: In group settings, such as team meetings or community gatherings, this mantra can serve as a reminder to seek collective strength and wisdom for the benefit of all.

Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine strength and wisdom, emphasizing the importance of humility and respect in our pursuits. It serves as a reminder that true power comes from acknowledging and invoking higher forces, whether in personal endeavors or collective efforts. By integrating this mindset into our daily lives, we can foster a sense of purpose and direction, ultimately leading to greater achievements and fulfillment.
