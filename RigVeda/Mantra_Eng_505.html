अति नः सश्चतो नय सुगा नः सुपथा कृणु |पुषन्न इह क्रतुम् विदः ||
ati naḥ saścato naya sugā naḥ supathā kṛṇu | pūṣann iha kratuṁ vidaḥ ||
  • ati naḥ saścato naya: This phrase can be translated to "Lead us on the right path." Here, "ati" means "beyond" or "further," "naḥ" means "us," "saścato" refers to "with good," and "naya" means "lead." This part emphasizes the importance of guidance in our lives, urging a higher power to lead us towards righteousness.
  • sugā naḥ supathā kṛṇu: This translates to "Make our path easy and good." "Sugā" means "easy" or "pleasant," "naḥ" again means "us," "supathā" refers to "good path," and "kṛṇu" means "make" or "create." This line expresses a desire for a smooth journey in life, both physically and spiritually.
  • pūṣann iha kratuṁ vidaḥ: This can be interpreted as "O Pusha, grant us strength and knowledge." "Pūṣann" refers to the deity Pusha, who is associated with nourishment and prosperity, "iha" means "here," "kratuṁ" refers to "strength" or "intellect," and "vidaḥ" means "to know" or "to understand." This line calls upon divine assistance for wisdom and strength in our endeavors.

Conclusion:

  • The mantra emphasizes the need for guidance, support, and wisdom in our lives. It reflects a universal human desire to seek a righteous path and to be aided in our journey.
  • In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applicable in various situations such as starting a new venture, making important life decisions, or even during challenging times when one seeks clarity and direction.
  • By reciting this mantra, individuals can cultivate a mindset of seeking guidance from higher powers, fostering a sense of community and shared purpose, and encouraging a positive outlook towards life's challenges.
