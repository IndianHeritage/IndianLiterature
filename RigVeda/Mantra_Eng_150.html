अग्नि देवां इह वह सादय योनिषु त्रिषु |परि भूष पिब ऋतुनाः ||
agne devām̐ ihā vaha sādayā yoniṣu triṣu | pari bhūṣa piba ṛtunā ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • Agne: O Fire (Agni), representing the divine energy and illumination.
    • Devām̐: Divine beings or deities.
    • ihā: Here, in this world.
    • vaha: Carry or bring forth.
    • sādayā: With satisfaction or fulfillment.
    • yoniṣu: In the wombs or sources of creation.
    • triṣu: In the three realms or worlds.
    • pari: All around or encompassing.
    • bhūṣa: Adorn or embellish.
    • piba: Drink or imbibe.
    • ṛtunā: With the seasons or cycles.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes Agni, the fire god, symbolizing energy, transformation, and purification.
    • It emphasizes the presence of divine beings in our lives and the importance of their blessings.
    • The phrase "here in this world" suggests a focus on the material and spiritual coexistence.
    • By asking Agni to bring forth satisfaction from the sources of creation, it highlights the interconnectedness of life and the divine.
    • The reference to the three realms signifies the holistic nature of existence—physical, mental, and spiritual.
    • To "adorn" and "imbibe" with the seasons indicates the need to embrace change and the cyclical nature of life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, we can relate to the concept of Agni as the driving force behind our ambitions and goals, much like how fire fuels our physical needs.
    • In moments of challenge, invoking the energy of Agni can inspire us to transform obstacles into opportunities.
    • Celebrations and rituals often involve fire, symbolizing purification and the presence of divine energy, reminding us of our connection to the universe.
    • Understanding the cycles of nature, such as seasons, can help us appreciate the importance of adaptability and resilience in our personal and professional lives.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the divine energy present in our lives and the importance of seeking fulfillment and satisfaction.
    • It encourages us to recognize the interconnectedness of all beings and the cycles of life, urging us to embrace change and transformation.
    • In practical terms, it can be applied in rituals, meditation, and daily affirmations to invoke positivity and divine blessings.
    • Ultimately, it signifies the importance of aligning ourselves with the divine energies to lead a fulfilling and harmonious life.
