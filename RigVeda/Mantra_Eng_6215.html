ओ षु वृष्णः प्रयज्यून आ नव्यसे सुविताया |ववृत्यां चित्रवाजान ||
o ṣu vṛṣṇaḥ prayajyūn ā navyase suvitāya | vavṛtyāṁ citravājān ||
  • Mantra: "o ṣu vṛṣṇaḥ prayajyūn ā navyase suvitāya | vavṛtyāṁ citravājān ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • o: An invocation or call, often used to address a deity.
    • ṣu: Good or auspicious.
    • vṛṣṇaḥ: Referring to the divine or the one who is strong, often associated with Lord Vishnu or the concept of strength.
    • prayajyūn: To offer or to present; it implies a sense of giving or sacrifice.
    • ā: A preposition meaning 'to' or 'towards'.
    • navyase: New or fresh; it signifies renewal or new beginnings.
    • suvitāya: Well-prepared or well-formed; it indicates something that is beneficial or auspicious.
    • vavṛtyāṁ: To manifest or to come forth; it suggests the idea of emergence or realization.
    • citravājān: Colorful or diverse; it can refer to various forms or manifestations.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra can be seen as a prayer for auspiciousness and strength in new endeavors.
    • The invocation of "o ṣu vṛṣṇaḥ" suggests a call to a divine force for support and guidance.
    • The phrase "prayajyūn ā navyase" emphasizes the importance of offering oneself or one's efforts towards new beginnings.
    • "Suvitāya" indicates that these new beginnings should be well-prepared and beneficial.
    • The concluding part "vavṛtyāṁ citravājān" suggests that these efforts will manifest in diverse and colorful ways, leading to a rich and fulfilling outcome.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • When starting a new business, one might recite this mantra to seek divine guidance and ensure that their efforts are well-prepared and auspicious.
    • In personal life, before embarking on a new journey, such as marriage or parenthood, individuals may invoke this mantra to bless their new beginnings.
    • Students may use this mantra before exams or new academic pursuits to seek strength and clarity in their endeavors.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of divine support in new ventures and the importance of preparation and auspiciousness.
    • It serves as a reminder that with the right intentions and efforts, new beginnings can lead to diverse and fulfilling outcomes.
    • In real life, it can be applied in various situations where individuals seek strength, guidance, and success in their new endeavors.
