आ वो रुवण्युम औशिजो हुवध्यै घोषेवा शंसम अर्जुनस्य नंशे | प्र वह पूष्णे दावन आम्ँ अच्छा वोचेय वसुतातिम अग्नेः ||
ā vo ruvaṇyum auśijo huvadhyai ghoṣeva śaṁsam arjunasya naṁśe | pra vaḥ pūṣṇe dāvana ām̐ acchā voceya vasutātim agneḥ ||
  • Word Breakdown:
    • ā vo: A call to the divine, inviting blessings.
    • ruvaṇyum: Refers to the essence of prosperity and abundance.
    • auśijo: Those who are pure and virtuous.
    • huvadhyai: To invoke or call upon.
    • ghoṣeva: Like a sound or call that resonates.
    • śaṁsam: Praise or commendation.
    • arjunasya: Referring to the noble or the righteous.
    • naṁśe: In the context of offering or sacrifice.
    • pra vaḥ: To lead or guide forward.
    • pūṣṇe: To the nourisher or provider.
    • dāvana: Referring to the divine fire or energy.
    • ām̐: A sacred sound, often used in invocations.
    • acchā: To protect or safeguard.
    • voceya: To speak or declare.
    • vasutātim: Abundance or wealth.
    • agneḥ: To Agni, the fire god, symbolizing energy and transformation.
  • Meaning and Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a call to the divine forces, inviting them to bless the speaker with prosperity and abundance.
    • It emphasizes the importance of purity and virtue in one's actions, suggesting that only those who are righteous can truly invoke divine blessings.
    • The invocation of Agni symbolizes the transformative power of fire, which can purify and energize one's intentions.
    • By praising and acknowledging the divine, the speaker seeks guidance and protection in their endeavors.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be recited by entrepreneurs seeking success and abundance in their ventures, emphasizing ethical practices and integrity.
    • During personal challenges, individuals may use this mantra to invoke strength and guidance, reminding themselves to stay virtuous and focused on their goals.
    • In community gatherings, this mantra can be a reminder of collective responsibility towards nurturing and supporting each other, fostering a sense of unity and purpose.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine blessings and the emphasis on righteousness and purity in one's actions.
    • It serves as a reminder that true prosperity comes from virtuous living and the ability to invoke higher energies for guidance and protection.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various aspects, including personal growth, business ethics, and community support, reinforcing the idea that our intentions and actions shape our reality.
