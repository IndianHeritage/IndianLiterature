अस्माकम् अद्य वाम अयं स्तोमो वाहिष्ठो अन्तमः |युवाभ्याम भूत्व अश्विनाः ||
asmākam adya vām ayaṁ stomo vāhiṣṭho antamaḥ | yuvābhyām bhūtv aśvinā ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • asmākam: our
    • adya: today
    • vām: your
    • ayaṁ: this
    • stomo: hymn or praise
    • vāhiṣṭho: the best or most excellent
    • antamaḥ: the ultimate or the last
    • yuvābhyām: by the two of you (referring to the Ashvins)
    • bhūtv: becoming or manifesting
    • aśvinā: O Ashvins (the twin deities)
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer directed towards the Ashvins, the twin gods of health and medicine in Vedic literature.
    • The speaker expresses a desire for their hymn or praise to be recognized and accepted by the Ashvins, highlighting the importance of divine acknowledgment in spiritual practices.
    • The phrase "our today" signifies a sense of immediacy and relevance, suggesting that the speaker seeks blessings in the present moment.
    • The term "the best hymn" indicates a yearning for the highest form of praise, emphasizing the quality and sincerity of the offering.
    • By invoking the Ashvins, the speaker is not only seeking their blessings but also aiming to embody their qualities of vitality and healing.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern life, this mantra can be applied in situations where individuals seek healing or support, such as during illness or challenging times.
    • For instance, someone undergoing medical treatment might recite this mantra to invoke the blessings of health and recovery, believing in the power of divine intervention.
    • Additionally, it can be used in rituals or ceremonies aimed at promoting well-being, where participants collectively seek the best outcomes for their health and happiness.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine assistance and the recognition of the interconnectedness of human efforts and divine grace.
    • It serves as a reminder that in our pursuits, whether for health, success, or happiness, we can seek support from higher powers.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra encourages individuals to remain hopeful and proactive in their healing journeys, fostering a sense of community and shared intention.
