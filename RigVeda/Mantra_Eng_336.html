आ घा गमद यदि श्रवत सहस्रीणिभिर ऊतिभिः |वाजेभिर उप नो हवम् ||
ā ghā gamad yadi śravat sahasriṇībhir ūtibhiḥ | vājebhir upa no havam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā: from
    • ghā: to be strong or powerful
    • gamad: to come or arrive
    • yadi: if
    • śravat: flowing or abundant
    • sahasriṇībhir: with thousands
    • ūtibhiḥ: with offerings or praises
    • vājebhir: with strength or vigor
    • upa: towards or near
    • no: our
    • havam: invocation or prayer
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the arrival of strength and abundance when invoked with sincere offerings.
    • The phrase "ā ghā gamad" suggests a call for powerful forces to come forth, indicating a desire for strength in various aspects of life.
    • "Yadi śravat" implies that if there is a flow of abundance, it should be recognized and welcomed.
    • The mention of "sahasriṇībhir ūtibhiḥ" emphasizes the importance of collective efforts and praises, suggesting that when many come together in devotion, the results are magnified.
    • "Vājebhir upa no havam" indicates that we seek strength and vigor in our prayers, highlighting the importance of intention and energy behind our invocations.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community setting, when people come together to support a cause, such as a charity event, their collective strength can lead to significant outcomes, much like the invocation of strength in the mantra.
    • In personal life, when an individual faces challenges, invoking strength through meditation or prayer can help them overcome obstacles, reflecting the essence of this mantra.
    • In business, teamwork and collaboration can lead to greater success, akin to the idea of many voices coming together to create a powerful invocation.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for strength and abundance through collective effort and sincere invocation.
    • It teaches us that when we come together with intention and purpose, we can invoke powerful forces that aid us in our endeavors.
    • This mantra is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal challenges to community efforts, reminding us of the strength found in unity and shared purpose.
