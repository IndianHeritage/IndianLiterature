मन्त्र: अग्रे सिन्धूनाम् पवमानो अर्षति अग्रे वाचो अग्रियो गोषु गच्छति | अग्रे वाजस्य भजते महाधनं स्वायुधः सोत्रिभिः पूयते वृषा ||
agre sindhūnām pavamāno arṣaty agre vāco agriyo goṣu gacchati | agre vājasya bhajate mahādhanaṁ svāyudhaḥ sotṛbhiḥ pūyate vṛṣā ||
  • Agre sindhūnām pavamāno arṣaty: This phrase can be translated to "The one who moves forward among the rivers." Here, "sindhūnām" refers to rivers, symbolizing the flow of life and knowledge. In real life, this can be likened to the pursuit of wisdom and the journey of self-discovery, where one must navigate through various experiences, much like a river flows through different terrains.
  • Agre vāco agriyo goṣu gacchati: This means "The one who goes ahead in speech and among the cows." The term "vāco" refers to speech, and "goṣu" refers to cows, which are often seen as symbols of wealth and nourishment in Vedic culture. This can be interpreted as the importance of communication and leadership in guiding others, akin to a leader who speaks wisely and leads their community towards prosperity.
  • Agre vājasya bhajate mahādhanaṁ: This translates to "The one who worships the great wealth of strength." "Vājasya" refers to strength or power, and "mahādhanaṁ" signifies great wealth. In a modern context, this can be related to the idea of harnessing one's inner strength and resources to achieve success, whether in personal endeavors or professional pursuits.
  • Svāyudhaḥ sotṛbhiḥ pūyate vṛṣā: This means "The one who is adorned with their own weapons is purified by the singers." "Svāyudhaḥ" refers to being equipped with one's own tools or strengths, while "sotṛbhiḥ" refers to those who sing or praise. This can be seen as a reminder that self-reliance and the acknowledgment of one's capabilities lead to personal growth and recognition by others, similar to how a skilled individual earns respect through their achievements.

  • Conclusion: This mantra emphasizes the importance of progress, communication, strength, and self-reliance. In real life, it serves as a guiding principle for individuals to strive for personal growth, lead with wisdom, and harness their inner strengths. It is applicable in various scenarios, such as in leadership roles, personal development, and community building, where the qualities of foresight, effective communication, and self-empowerment are essential for success and harmony.
