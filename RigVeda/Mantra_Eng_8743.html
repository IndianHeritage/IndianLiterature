मन्त्र: परि प्र धन्वेन्द्राय सोम स्वादुर मित्राय पूष्णे भगाय ||
pari pra dhanvendrāya soma svādur mitrāya pūṣṇe bhagāya ||
  • Mantra: "pari pra dhanvendrāya soma svādur mitrāya pūṣṇe bhagāya"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • pari: around, encompassing
    • pra: forth, forward
    • dhanvendrāya: to the lord of wealth (Dhanvantari)
    • soma: the moon, often associated with nectar or immortality
    • svādur: sweet, pleasant
    • mitrāya: to the friend, often referring to the sun or a benevolent force
    • pūṣṇe: to the nourisher, the one who provides sustenance
    • bhagāya: to the blessed one, the one who is prosperous
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes various divine aspects, primarily focusing on wealth, nourishment, and friendship.
    • The phrase "pari pra" suggests a holistic approach, indicating that one should seek wealth and nourishment from all directions.
    • "Dhanvendrāya" refers to the divine aspect of wealth, emphasizing the importance of not just material wealth but also spiritual richness.
    • "Soma svādur" highlights the sweetness of life and the joy that comes from abundance and prosperity.
    • "Mitrāya pūṣṇe" signifies the importance of friendship and nourishment in our lives, suggesting that true wealth comes from relationships and support.
    • "Bhagāya" indicates the blessings that come from being prosperous and fulfilled.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be applied to the pursuit of success where one seeks not only financial gain but also the support of colleagues and partners.
    • In personal life, it can be a reminder to cherish friendships and relationships that provide emotional and spiritual nourishment.
    • In health and wellness, it emphasizes the importance of a balanced life where physical, emotional, and spiritual needs are met.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of wealth, nourishment, and relationships.
    • It encourages individuals to seek a holistic approach to life, where material success is balanced with emotional and spiritual well-being.
    • In practical terms, it can be applied in various aspects of life, from personal relationships to professional endeavors, highlighting the importance of community and support in achieving true prosperity.
