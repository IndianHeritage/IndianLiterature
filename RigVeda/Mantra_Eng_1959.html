यद अदो पितो अजगन् विवस्वा पर्वतानाम् |अत्रा चिन् नो मधो पितो 'राम भक्षाय गम्याः ||
yad ado pito ajagan vivasva parvatānām | atrā cin no madho pito 'ram bhakṣāya gamyāḥ ||
  • Mantra: "yad ado pito ajagan vivasva parvatānām | atrā cin no madho pito 'ram bhakṣāya gamyāḥ ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yad: that which
    • ado: here
    • pito: the father or source
    • ajagan: of the universe
    • vivasva: shining or radiant
    • parvatānām: of the mountains
    • atrā: here
    • cin: if
    • no: our
    • madho: of sweetness or joy
    • pito: the father or source
    • 'ram: delight or pleasure
    • bhakṣāya: for consumption or enjoyment
    • gamyāḥ: to be attained or reached
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the divine source that is both the father and the radiant essence of the universe.
    • The reference to "mountains" symbolizes stability, strength, and the eternal nature of the divine.
    • The phrase "if our source of sweetness" suggests a longing for connection with this divine essence, which brings joy and fulfillment.
    • The mantra implies that through devotion and seeking, one can attain the divine joy and nourishment that is essential for spiritual growth.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, individuals often seek stability and strength in their relationships, akin to the mountains mentioned in the mantra. For instance, a supportive family or community can provide the foundation needed to navigate life's challenges.
    • People may also find joy and fulfillment in their passions, such as art, music, or nature, which can be seen as a reflection of the divine sweetness referred to in the mantra.
    • Practices like meditation and mindfulness can help individuals connect with their inner source of joy, leading to a more fulfilling life.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of recognizing and connecting with the divine source of joy and strength in our lives.
    • It encourages individuals to seek stability and nourishment from both the external world and their inner selves.
    • In real-life scenarios, this can manifest as building strong relationships, pursuing passions, and engaging in spiritual practices that foster a sense of connection and fulfillment.
