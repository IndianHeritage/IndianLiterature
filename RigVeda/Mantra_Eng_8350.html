सम्यक् सम्यञ्चो महिषा अहेषत सिंधोर ऊर्माव अधि वेना अवीविपन् |मधोर धाराभिर जनयन्तो अर्कम् इति प्रियाम इन्द्रस्य तन्वम् अवीवृधन् ||
samyak samyañco mahiṣā aheṣata sindhor ūrmāv adhi venā avīvipan | madhor dhārābhir janayanto arkam it priyām indrasya tanvam avīvṛdhan ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • samyak: rightly, properly
    • samyañco: in harmony, in balance
    • mahiṣā: the great, the powerful
    • aheṣata: the serpent, often symbolizing energy or potential
    • sindhor: of the ocean, representing vastness and depth
    • ūrmāv: waves, symbolizing movement and change
    • adhi: above, over
    • venā: the flute, often associated with music and harmony
    • avīvipan: to bring forth, to manifest
    • madhor: of sweetness, representing joy and positivity
    • dhārābhir: with streams, indicating flow and abundance
    • janayanto: generating, creating
    • arkam: light, often associated with the sun or enlightenment
    • it: this
    • priyām: beloved, dear
    • indrasya: of Indra, the king of gods, representing power and authority
    • tanvam: body, form
    • avīvṛdhan: expanding, growing
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the importance of harmony and balance in life.
    • The mention of the ocean and its waves symbolizes the vast experiences and emotions we encounter.
    • The flute represents the beauty of music and the importance of creativity in achieving balance.
    • Generating light and sweetness signifies the positive outcomes of living in harmony.
    • Indra, as a symbol of power, suggests that true strength comes from unity and balance.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a team setting, when members work in harmony, they can achieve greater results, much like the waves of the ocean working together to create a powerful force.
    • In personal relationships, communication and creativity (like music) can help resolve conflicts and foster deeper connections.
    • In nature, ecosystems thrive when all elements are in balance, demonstrating the importance of harmony in sustaining life.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of balance and harmony in all aspects of life.
    • It teaches us that by embracing creativity and positivity, we can generate light and growth in our lives.
    • In real-life scenarios, applying these principles can lead to stronger relationships, successful teamwork, and a more fulfilling existence.
