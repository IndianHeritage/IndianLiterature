एव वस्व इन्द्रः सत्यः सम्राड् धन्ता वृतं वरिवः पूरवे कः |पुरुष्टुत क्रत्वा नः शग्धि रायो भक्षीय ते 'वसो दैवस्य ||
evā vasva indraḥ satyaḥ samrāḍ ḍhantā vṛtraṁ varivaḥ pūrave kaḥ | puruṣṭuta kratvā naḥ śagdhi rāyo bhakṣīya te 'vaso daivyasya ||
  • evā vasva indraḥ: This phrase can be translated as "Indeed, all-pervading Indra." Indra is a prominent deity in Vedic literature, often associated with rain, thunder, and strength. In real life, this can symbolize the omnipresence of divine energy or power in our lives.
  • satyaḥ samrāḍ: This means "the truthful ruler." It emphasizes the importance of truth and righteousness in leadership. In contemporary society, this can be related to ethical leadership in politics and business, where leaders are expected to uphold truth and integrity.
  • ḍhantā vṛtraṁ varivaḥ pūrave kaḥ: This translates to "who has subdued Vritra, the obstructer." Vritra is often seen as a dragon or serpent that represents obstacles or challenges. In our lives, this can be interpreted as overcoming personal or societal challenges, akin to facing fears or adversities.
  • puruṣṭuta kratvā naḥ: This means "by the strength of our actions." It highlights the significance of our efforts and deeds in achieving success. In real life, this can be seen in the context of hard work and perseverance leading to personal and professional achievements.
  • śagdhi rāyo bhakṣīya te 'vaso daivyasya: This translates to "may we be blessed with wealth and prosperity." It reflects the desire for abundance and well-being. In modern terms, this can relate to the pursuit of financial stability and the importance of sharing wealth for the greater good.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of divine presence, truth in leadership, overcoming obstacles, the importance of actions, and the pursuit of prosperity. Its significance lies in guiding individuals towards a life of integrity, resilience, and abundance. In real-life scenarios, it encourages us to embody these values in our personal and professional lives, fostering a community that thrives on truth, strength, and shared prosperity.
