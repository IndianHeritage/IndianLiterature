उवाशोषा उच्छाच्च नु देवी जीराऽ रथानाम् |ये अस्याऽ आचरनेषु दध्रिरे समुद्रे न श्रवस्यवः ||
uvāsoṣā ucchāc ca nu devī jīrā rathānām | ye asyā ācaraṇeṣu dadhrire samudre na śravasyavaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • uvāsoṣā: Refers to the dawn or the early morning light.
    • ucchāc: Means 'high' or 'elevated'.
    • ca: Translates to 'and'.
    • nu: A particle often used for emphasis, meaning 'indeed' or 'truly'.
    • devī: Refers to the divine or goddess.
    • jīrā: Means 'old' or 'ancient'.
    • rathānām: Refers to 'chariots' or 'vehicles'.
    • ye: Means 'those who'.
    • asyā: Refers to 'this' or 'of this'.
    • ācaraṇeṣu: Means 'in the actions' or 'in the practices'.
    • dadhrire: Means 'upheld' or 'supported'.
    • samudre: Refers to 'the ocean' or 'the vastness'.
    • na: Means 'not'.
    • śravasyavaḥ: Refers to 'those who are renowned' or 'those who have fame'.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the dawn, symbolizing new beginnings and the divine presence that elevates our actions.
    • The mention of 'chariots' can be interpreted as the vehicles of life, representing our journeys and endeavors.
    • The phrase 'those who uphold' suggests the importance of maintaining integrity and purpose in our actions.
    • The reference to the ocean signifies vastness and depth, indicating that our actions should be profound and meaningful.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, the dawn can represent the start of a new day, encouraging individuals to set intentions and goals.
    • Just as chariots are used to travel, our choices and actions propel us forward in life, emphasizing the need for thoughtful decision-making.
    • In a professional setting, upholding values and ethics can lead to a reputation that is respected and renowned, much like the 'fame' mentioned in the mantra.
    • The ocean can symbolize the vast opportunities available to us, reminding us to explore and engage deeply with our surroundings.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of new beginnings, the importance of our actions, and the pursuit of integrity and depth in life.
    • It serves as a reminder to approach each day with purpose, to uphold our values, and to explore the vast opportunities that life presents.
    • In essence, it encourages us to be mindful of our journey and the impact of our actions on ourselves and the world around us.
