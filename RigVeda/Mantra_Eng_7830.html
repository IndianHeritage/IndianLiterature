प्र ते सोतार ओण्यो रसम् मदाय घृष्वये |सर्गो न तक्त्य एतशः ||
pra te sotāra oṇyo3 rasam madāya ghṛṣvaye | sargo na takty etaśaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • pra: for you, towards you
    • te: your
    • sotāra: the one who brings forth, the creator
    • oṇya: other, different
    • rasam: essence, flavor, taste
    • madāya: intoxicated, filled with joy
    • ghṛṣvaye: for the one who is worthy, deserving
    • sargo: creation, birth
    • na: not
    • takty: to be taken away, to be diminished
    • etaśaḥ: from this, from here
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra can be understood as a prayer or invocation directed towards a divine entity, asking for the essence of creation and joy to be bestowed upon the devotee.
    • The phrase "for you, the creator" emphasizes the relationship between the devotee and the divine, suggesting a deep reverence and acknowledgment of the creator's role in the universe.
    • The mention of "other essence" indicates the diversity of experiences and flavors of life, highlighting the richness of existence.
    • "Intoxicated with joy" suggests a state of bliss that comes from recognizing and embracing the beauty of creation.
    • The concluding part, "not to be diminished from here," implies a desire for the divine essence and joy to remain constant and undiminished in one's life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, this mantra can be reflected in moments of gratitude, where individuals acknowledge the beauty and diversity of their experiences, much like appreciating different flavors in a meal.
    • During celebrations, such as festivals or family gatherings, people often feel a sense of joy and connection, akin to being "intoxicated with joy," which resonates with the essence of this mantra.
    • In creative pursuits, artists and musicians often seek inspiration from the divine, hoping to capture the "essence" of their experiences in their work, which aligns with the invocation of the creator's essence in the mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of recognizing and celebrating the divine essence in our lives, encouraging individuals to seek joy and fulfillment in their experiences.
    • It serves as a reminder to appreciate the richness of life and to maintain a connection with the divine, ensuring that the essence of joy and creation remains undiminished.
    • In practical terms, this mantra can be applied in daily rituals, meditation, or moments of reflection, fostering a sense of gratitude and connection to the larger universe.
