उप त्वा कर्मन्नूतये स नो युवोग्रश्चक्राम यो धृतश् |त्वामिद्ध्य अवितारं ववृमहे सखाय इन्द्र सानसिम ||
upa tvā karmann ūtaye sa no yuvograś cakrāma yo dhṛṣat | tvām id dhy avitāraṁ vavṛmahe sakhāya indra sānasim ||
  • upa tvā karmann ūtaye: This phrase can be translated as "We invoke you for the purpose of action." Here, the speaker is calling upon a divine force, likely Indra, to assist in their endeavors. In real life, this can be likened to seeking guidance or support from a mentor or a higher power when embarking on a new project or undertaking.
  • sa no yuvograś cakrāma: This translates to "May we be strong and powerful." The term "yuvograś" refers to youthfulness and vigor, suggesting a desire for energy and strength in one's actions. In everyday life, this can be seen in athletes who train rigorously to achieve peak performance or in students who strive for excellence in their studies.
  • yo dhṛṣat: This means "who is courageous." Courage is a vital trait in overcoming challenges. For instance, individuals who stand up for their beliefs or take risks in their careers exemplify this quality. The mantra encourages the cultivation of bravery in the face of adversity.
  • tvām id dhy avitāraṁ vavṛmahe: This can be interpreted as "We meditate upon you, O Indra." It signifies a deep reverence and contemplation of the divine. In practical terms, this can relate to practices like meditation or prayer, where individuals seek clarity and connection with a higher power to guide their actions.
  • sakhāya indra sānasim: This phrase translates to "O Indra, our friend." It emphasizes the relationship between the divine and the devotee, portraying Indra as a supportive ally. In real life, this can be compared to the importance of having supportive friends or community members who uplift and encourage us in our pursuits.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking divine assistance in our actions, fostering strength and courage, and nurturing a supportive relationship with the divine. Its significance lies in the reminder that we are not alone in our endeavors; we can call upon higher powers for guidance and support. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various contexts, such as during challenging times in personal or professional life, where invoking strength and courage can lead to success and fulfillment. The mantra serves as a powerful affirmation of our potential and the importance of community and divine support in our journeys.
