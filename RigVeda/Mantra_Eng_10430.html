मुञ्चामि त्वा हविषा जीवनाय कम अज्ञातयक्ष्माद उता राजयक्ष्मात् |ग्राहिर जग्राह यदि वैतद एनं तस्य इन्द्राग्नि प्र मुमुक्तम एनम् ||
muñcāmi tvā haviṣā jīvanāya kam ajñātayakṣmād uta rājayakṣmāt | grāhir jagrāha yadi vaitad enaṁ tasyā indrāgnī pra mumuktam enam ||
  • Translation: "I release you with this offering for the sake of life, from the unknown wealth and from the wealth of kings. If the seizer has seized you, may Indra and Agni free you from that."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • muñcāmi: I release or liberate.
    • tvā: you (referring to the entity being addressed).
    • haviṣā: with this offering or sacrifice.
    • jīvanāya: for the sake of life.
    • kam: from the unknown.
    • ajñātayakṣmād: from the wealth that is unknown or hidden.
    • uta: and from.
    • rājayakṣmāt: from the wealth of kings.
    • grāhir: the seizer or captor.
    • jagrāha: has seized.
    • yadi: if.
    • vaitad: this.
    • enaṁ: you (referring to the entity).
    • tasyā: from that.
    • indrāgnī: may Indra and Agni.
    • pra: free or liberate.
    • mumuktam: may liberate you.
  • Contextual Significance:
    • This mantra is a plea for liberation and protection from unseen forces that may hinder one's life and prosperity.
    • It reflects the ancient belief in the power of deities like Indra and Agni to provide safety and freedom from obstacles.
    • In real life, this can be related to seeking help in times of distress, whether it be financial, emotional, or spiritual.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a situation where someone is facing financial difficulties due to unforeseen circumstances, they might recite this mantra as a way to seek relief and support.
    • During a challenging phase in life, such as illness or personal loss, individuals may turn to spiritual practices, including mantras, to find strength and liberation from their suffering.
    • In a community setting, this mantra can be used in rituals to invoke blessings for collective well-being and protection from negative influences.
  • Conclusion:
    • The mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking liberation from both known and unknown challenges in life.
    • It serves as a reminder of the protective forces available to individuals, encouraging them to reach out for help in times of need.
    • In essence, it highlights the interconnectedness of life, wealth, and spiritual support, making it relevant in various aspects of human experience.
