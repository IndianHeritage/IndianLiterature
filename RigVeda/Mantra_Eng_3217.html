आ सत्यो यातु मगवाम् ऋजीषी द्रवन्त्व अस्य हरय उप नः |तस्मा इद अन्धः सुशुमा सुदक्षम् इहाभिपित्वं कराते गृणानः ||
ā satyo yātu maghavām̐ ṛjīṣī dravantv asya haraya upa naḥ | tasmā id andhaḥ suṣumā sudakṣam ihābhipitvaṁ karate gṛṇānaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā: from
    • satyo: truth
    • yātu: may it go
    • maghavām̐: to the powerful
    • ṛjīṣī: the righteous
    • dravantv: may they flow
    • asya: of this
    • haraya: the divine beings
    • upa: near
    • naḥ: to us
    • tasmā: therefore
    • id: this
    • andhaḥ: blind
    • suṣumā: the good path
    • sudakṣam: skillful
    • ihābhipitvaṁ: here, may it be attained
    • karate: they do
    • gṛṇānaḥ: singing
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the essence of truth and righteousness, asking for the blessings of powerful divine beings to guide us.
    • The phrase "may it go to the powerful" suggests a desire for strength and support from higher powers in our endeavors.
    • The call for the divine beings to "flow" towards us indicates a yearning for their presence and guidance in our lives.
    • The mention of "blind" and "good path" signifies the importance of clarity and wisdom in our journey, urging us to seek enlightenment and avoid ignorance.
    • By referring to "skillful," the mantra emphasizes the need for competence and capability in our actions.
    • The concluding part, "here, may it be attained," reinforces the idea that these qualities are not just abstract concepts but are to be actively pursued in our current lives.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, this mantra can be applied when seeking guidance for a project. One might invoke the principles of truth and righteousness to ensure ethical practices while striving for success.
    • In personal relationships, the mantra serves as a reminder to communicate openly and honestly, fostering trust and understanding among individuals.
    • During challenging times, individuals can reflect on this mantra to seek clarity and direction, encouraging them to choose the right path and make informed decisions.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra from the RigVeda encapsulates the essence of seeking truth, guidance, and skillfulness in our lives.
    • Its significance lies in its ability to inspire individuals to pursue righteousness and clarity, both in personal and professional realms.
    • By embodying the principles expressed in this mantra, one can navigate life's challenges with wisdom and integrity, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling existence.
