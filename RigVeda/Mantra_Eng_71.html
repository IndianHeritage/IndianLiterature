मन्त्र: इन्द्र सानसिं रयिं सजित्वानं सदासहं | वर्षिष्ठं ऊतये भर ||
endra sānasiṁ rayiṁ sajitvānaṁ sadāsaham | varṣiṣṭham ūtaye bhara ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • Endra: Refers to Indra, the king of gods in Vedic literature, symbolizing power and strength.
    • Sānasiṁ: Means 'to be seated' or 'to be established', indicating stability and presence.
    • Rayiṁ: Translates to 'wealth' or 'prosperity', often associated with material and spiritual abundance.
    • Sajitvānaṁ: Means 'having conquered' or 'having achieved', suggesting success and accomplishment.
    • Sadāsaham: Implies 'always' or 'eternally', indicating a sense of permanence.
    • Varṣiṣṭham: Means 'the best' or 'the most excellent', denoting superiority and excellence.
    • Ūtaye: Refers to 'for the purpose of nourishment' or 'for sustenance', indicating support and sustenance.
    • Bhara: Means 'to fill' or 'to bestow', suggesting the act of providing or granting.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes Indra, seeking his blessings for prosperity and success.
    • It emphasizes the importance of being established in strength and stability while achieving wealth.
    • The phrase suggests that true prosperity comes from conquering challenges and being steadfast in one's pursuits.
    • It highlights the need for nourishment, both materially and spiritually, to sustain success.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be applied to entrepreneurs who strive to establish their ventures with strength and resilience, overcoming obstacles to achieve financial success.
    • In personal life, individuals may seek to cultivate inner strength and stability, ensuring that their relationships and personal growth are nourished and supported.
    • In community settings, leaders can embody the principles of this mantra by fostering an environment of support and abundance, encouraging collective growth and prosperity.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of strength, stability, and the pursuit of excellence in all aspects of life.
    • It encourages individuals to seek blessings for prosperity while remaining grounded and focused on their goals.
    • By embodying the principles of this mantra, one can cultivate a life filled with abundance, resilience, and meaningful achievements.
