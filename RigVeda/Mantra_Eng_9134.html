अधि पुत्रोपमश्रवो नपान मित्रातिथेर इहि |पितुṣ ṭे अस्मि वन्दिता ||
adhi putropamaśravo napān mitrātither ihi | pituṣ ṭe asmi vanditā ||
  • Adhi: This word translates to "above" or "over." It signifies a higher state or a superior position.
  • Putropama: This term means "like a son." It emphasizes the bond and affection that exists in familial relationships, particularly between a father and son.
  • Śravo: This word means "fame" or "renown." It indicates the importance of reputation and the legacy one leaves behind.
  • Napān: This term refers to "nourishment" or "sustenance." It highlights the need for support and care in relationships.
  • Mitrātither: This word can be broken down into "Mitra," meaning "friend," and "āthithya," meaning "hospitality." It emphasizes the importance of friendship and welcoming others.
  • Ihi: This word means "here" or "in this place," indicating the immediacy and relevance of the message.
  • Pituṣ: This term means "father." It signifies the paternal figure and the respect associated with it.
  • Ṭe: This word translates to "your," indicating possession or belonging.
  • Asmi: This means "I am," affirming existence and identity.
  • Vanditā: This term means "worshipped" or "honored," indicating reverence and respect.

The mantra can be interpreted as a call to recognize and honor the relationships that nourish us, particularly the bond between a father and son. It emphasizes the importance of friendship and hospitality, suggesting that these values are essential for a fulfilling life.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios:

  • Family Relationships: It encourages individuals to appreciate and honor their familial bonds, particularly the father-son relationship, which can be a source of strength and support.
  • Friendship: The emphasis on friendship and hospitality reminds us to cultivate meaningful relationships and to be welcoming to others, fostering a sense of community.
  • Legacy and Reputation: The mention of fame and renown highlights the importance of leaving a positive legacy, encouraging individuals to act with integrity and kindness.

Conclusion: This mantra serves as a reminder of the significance of nurturing relationships, honoring our roots, and fostering a sense of community. It encourages us to live with respect and gratitude towards those who support us, ultimately leading to a more harmonious and fulfilling life.
