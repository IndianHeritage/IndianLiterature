आ रोडसी बृहती वेविदानाः प्र रुद्रियाः जब्रिरे यज्ञियासः |विदान मर्तो नेमधिता चिकीत्वान अग्निम पदे परम तस्थिवांसम् ||
ā rodasī bṛhatī vevidānāḥ pra rudriyā jabhrire yajñiyāsaḥ | vidan marto nemadhitā cikitvān agnim pade parame tasthivāṁsam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā rodasī: From the vast expanse of the universe
    • bṛhatī: The great or the expansive
    • vevidānāḥ: Those who know various paths or ways
    • pra: Indeed or truly
    • rudriyā: The Rudras, often associated with storm and transformation
    • jabhrire: They have invoked or called upon
    • yajñiyāsaḥ: The offerings or sacrifices
    • vidan: Knowing or understanding
    • marto: The mortal or human
    • nemadhitā: Not being restrained or limited
    • cikitvān: Being wise or thoughtful
    • agnim: The fire, often symbolizing energy or divine presence
    • pade: In the realm or path
    • parame: The supreme or ultimate
    • tasthivāṁsam: One who stands or resides
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the vastness of the universe and the various paths of knowledge that exist within it.
    • It emphasizes the importance of invoking divine energies (Rudras) through offerings and sacrifices, which can be seen as a metaphor for dedication and commitment in our lives.
    • The mention of the mortal being who is wise and unrestrained suggests that human beings have the potential to transcend limitations through knowledge and understanding.
    • The fire (Agni) symbolizes transformation, energy, and the divine spark within us, indicating that by following the right path, we can connect with the supreme reality.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern life, this mantra can be related to the pursuit of knowledge and self-improvement. For instance, individuals who seek education and personal growth are embodying the essence of "vevidānāḥ."
    • The act of making sacrifices for the greater good, such as volunteering or contributing to community service, reflects the idea of "yajñiyāsaḥ."
    • In a corporate setting, a leader who encourages innovation and creativity among team members is invoking the spirit of "agnim" by fostering an environment where ideas can flourish.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of knowledge, dedication, and the pursuit of higher truths in our lives.
    • It teaches us that by understanding the vastness of existence and invoking the divine through our actions, we can transcend our limitations and connect with the ultimate reality.
    • In essence, it serves as a reminder to embrace wisdom, make meaningful sacrifices, and strive for personal and collective growth in our journey through life.
