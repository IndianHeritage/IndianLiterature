अग्निः सप्तिं वाजम्भरं ददात्य अग्निर वीरं श्रुत्यं कर्मनिःष्ठाम् | अग्नī रोदसी वि चरत समञ्जन्न अग्निर नारीं वीरकुक्षिम पुरन्धिम ||
agniḥ saptiṁ vājambharaṁ dadāty agnir vīraṁ śrutyaṁ karmaniḥṣṭhām | agnī rodasī vi carat samañjann agnir nārīṁ vīrakukṣim puraṁdhim ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • Agniḥ: Fire, representing energy and transformation.
    • Saptiṁ: Seven, often symbolizing completeness or perfection.
    • Vājambharaṁ: Bestowing strength or vigor.
    • Dadāty: Gives or bestows.
    • Vīraṁ: Hero or warrior, symbolizing courage and valor.
    • Śrutyaṁ: Knowledge or wisdom.
    • Karmaniḥṣṭhām: Firmness in action or dedication to duty.
    • Agnī: Again refers to fire, emphasizing its importance.
    • Rodasī: The heavens or the sky.
    • Vi: In or within.
    • Carat: Moves or travels.
    • Samañjann: Harmoniously or in balance.
    • Nārīṁ: Woman, representing femininity and nurturing.
    • Vīrakukṣim: The womb of the hero, symbolizing the source of strength.
    • Puraṁdhim: The creator or the one who brings forth.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes Agni, the fire god, who is seen as a source of strength and energy.
    • The mention of "seven" signifies completeness, suggesting that Agni bestows all necessary qualities for success.
    • Agni is portrayed as a giver of valor and wisdom, emphasizing the importance of knowledge in achieving one's goals.
    • The mantra also highlights the balance between the masculine and feminine energies, with references to both the hero and the nurturing woman.
    • It suggests that true strength comes from a harmonious blend of action, wisdom, and nurturing qualities.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, a leader who embodies the qualities of Agni can inspire their team to achieve goals with vigor and dedication.
    • In personal life, individuals can draw on the wisdom of Agni to face challenges with courage and a balanced approach, integrating both strength and compassion.
    • In community building, the mantra can serve as a reminder of the importance of collaboration between different genders and roles to create a supportive environment.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of fire as a transformative force in life, representing energy, strength, and wisdom.
    • It teaches us that true success is achieved through a balance of action, knowledge, and nurturing qualities.
    • In real-life scenarios, the principles embodied in this mantra can guide individuals and communities towards harmonious growth and achievement.
