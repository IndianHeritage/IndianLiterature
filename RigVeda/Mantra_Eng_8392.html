सोमस्य धाराऽपवते नृचक्षस ऋतेन देवान् हवते दिवस् परि |बृहस्पते रवथेनाऽविधिद्युत समुद्रासो न सवनाानि विव्यचुः ||
somasya dhārā pavate nṛcakṣasa ṛtena devān havate divas pari | bṛhaspate ravathenā vi didyute samudrāso na savanāni vivyacuḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • somasya: of the soma (a sacred drink in Vedic rituals)
    • dhārā: flow or stream
    • pavate: flows or moves
    • nṛcakṣasa: of the human beings or mortals
    • ṛtena: by the truth or righteousness
    • devān: the gods
    • havate: invoke or call upon
    • divas: day or sky
    • pari: all around or everywhere
    • bṛhaspate: O Bṛhaspati (the lord of speech and wisdom)
    • ravathenā: with the light or radiance
    • vi: apart or separately
    • didyute: shone or illuminated
    • samudrāso: of the oceans
    • na: not
    • savanāni: offerings or sacrifices
    • vivyacuḥ: they have poured forth
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the flow of soma, which symbolizes the divine nectar or energy that nourishes both the gods and mortals.
    • The invocation of the gods through the truth signifies the importance of righteousness in our actions and thoughts.
    • Bṛhaspati, as the lord of wisdom, is called upon to illuminate our path, guiding us with knowledge and understanding.
    • The reference to the oceans suggests the vastness of divine energy and the interconnectedness of all beings.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, the flow of soma can be likened to the positive energy we receive from nature, relationships, and spiritual practices.
    • Righteousness (ṛta) can be seen in ethical decision-making in business, where honesty and integrity lead to long-term success.
    • Seeking wisdom from mentors or spiritual leaders can illuminate our paths, much like Bṛhaspati's guidance.
    • The oceans represent the collective consciousness of humanity, reminding us that our actions impact the greater whole.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of divine energy, truth, and wisdom in our lives.
    • It teaches us to invoke positive energies and act with righteousness, fostering a harmonious existence.
    • In real-life scenarios, applying these principles can lead to personal growth, ethical living, and a deeper connection with the universe.
    • Ultimately, it serves as a reminder of our interconnectedness and the importance of nurturing both our spiritual and material lives.
