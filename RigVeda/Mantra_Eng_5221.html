स सुक्रतुर यो विदुरः पाणीनाम् पुनानो अर्कम् पुरुभोजसां नः |होता मन्द्रो विश्वां दामूनास् तिरस् तमो ददृशे रम्याणाम् ||
sa sukratur yo vi duraḥ paṇīnām punāno arkam purubhojasaṁ naḥ | hotā mandro viśāṁ damūnās tiras tamo dadṛśe rāmyāṇām ||
  • sa sukratur: This phrase can be translated as "he who is of good thought" or "the one who has good intentions." It emphasizes the importance of positive thinking and intentions in our actions.
  • yo vi duraḥ: This means "who is far from evil." It suggests that one should strive to distance themselves from negativity and harmful influences.
  • paṇīnām: This refers to "the hands" or "the actions." It signifies that our actions should be guided by good thoughts and intentions.
  • punāno arkam: This translates to "renewing the light" or "reviving the energy." It indicates the importance of rejuvenation and the pursuit of enlightenment in life.
  • purubhojasaṁ naḥ: This means "to us who are nourished by abundance." It highlights the idea of being grateful for the resources and abundance we receive in life.
  • hotā mandro: This can be interpreted as "the one who invokes" or "the priest." It signifies the role of a guide or leader in invoking positive energies and blessings.
  • viśāṁ damūnās: This translates to "of the people, the one who brings peace." It emphasizes the importance of harmony and peace within communities.
  • tiras tamo dadṛśe: This means "who sees through darkness." It suggests the ability to overcome ignorance and negativity, seeking knowledge and truth.
  • rāmyāṇām: This refers to "of the beautiful" or "of the pleasant." It signifies the pursuit of beauty and positivity in life.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda encapsulates the essence of living a life guided by positive thoughts, good intentions, and actions that promote peace and harmony. In real-life scenarios, it can be applied in various contexts such as personal development, community building, and leadership. For instance, a leader who embodies these qualities can inspire their team to work towards common goals with a positive mindset, fostering an environment of collaboration and growth. Additionally, individuals can reflect on their intentions and actions, striving to distance themselves from negativity and embrace a path of enlightenment and abundance. Ultimately, this mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of thoughts, actions, and the pursuit of a harmonious life.
