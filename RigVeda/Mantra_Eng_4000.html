आ नो दिवो बृहतः पर्वताद् आ सरस्वती यजतā गन्तु यज्ञम् |हवं देवी जुजुषाणā घृताचī शग्मां नो वाचम् उषती श्रिणोतु ||
ā no divo bṛhataḥ parvatād ā sarasvatī yajatā gantu yajñam | havaṁ devī jujuṣāṇā ghṛtācī śagmāṁ no vācam uśatī śṛṇotu ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā no: May we be blessed
    • divo: from the heavens
    • bṛhataḥ: the great
    • parvatād: from the mountains
    • ā: from
    • sarasvatī: the goddess Saraswati
    • yajatā: who is worshipped
    • gantu: to go
    • yajñam: to the sacrifice
    • havaṁ: our invocation
    • devī: O goddess
    • jujuṣāṇā: who is desirous
    • ghṛtācī: with clarified butter
    • śagmāṁ: may she grant us
    • no: our
    • vācam: speech
    • uśatī: auspicious
    • śṛṇotu: may she hear
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer for blessings and guidance from the divine, particularly from the goddess Saraswati, who represents knowledge, wisdom, and the arts.
    • The invocation seeks to draw inspiration and support from the heavens and the mountains, symbolizing strength and stability.
    • The mention of "yajñam" indicates a ritual or sacrifice, emphasizing the importance of devotion and offerings in seeking divine favor.
    • The desire for auspicious speech reflects the significance of communication and expression in human interactions and spiritual practices.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In academic settings, students often invoke Saraswati's blessings before exams or important presentations, seeking clarity and eloquence in their speech.
    • Artists and musicians may recite this mantra to inspire creativity and enhance their artistic expressions.
    • During festivals like Vasant Panchami, devotees perform rituals to honor Saraswati, symbolizing the pursuit of knowledge and wisdom.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking divine guidance in our endeavors, particularly in the realms of knowledge and communication.
    • Its significance lies in the recognition of the interconnectedness of the divine, nature, and human efforts, encouraging individuals to approach their pursuits with reverence and dedication.
    • In real-life scenarios, it serves as a reminder to cultivate wisdom, articulate our thoughts clearly, and honor the divine presence in our daily lives.
