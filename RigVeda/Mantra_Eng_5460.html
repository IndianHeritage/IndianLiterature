प्रति नः स्तोमं तवष्टा जुषेत स्याद अस्मे अरमतिर वसूयुः ||
prati naḥ stomaṁ tvaṣṭā juṣeta syād asme aramatir vasūyuḥ ||
  • Mantra: "prati naḥ stomaṁ tvaṣṭā juṣeta syād asme aramatir vasūyuḥ"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • prati: towards, in relation to
    • naḥ: our
    • stomaṁ: praise, hymn, or song
    • tvaṣṭā: the divine craftsman or creator
    • juṣeta: may he enjoy, may he accept
    • syād: may there be
    • asme: for us
    • aramatir: the best of the best, excellence
    • vasūyuḥ: wealth, abundance, prosperity
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer directed towards Tvaṣṭā, the divine craftsman, asking him to accept our hymns and praises.
    • It emphasizes the desire for excellence and abundance in our lives, suggesting that through divine grace, we can achieve prosperity.
    • The invocation of Tvaṣṭā highlights the importance of creation and craftsmanship in achieving our goals.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, this mantra can be applied when seeking success in a project. For instance, a team may recite this mantra before a presentation to invoke creativity and excellence in their work.
    • In personal life, individuals may use this mantra when embarking on new ventures, such as starting a business or pursuing education, to attract prosperity and divine support.
    • During festivals or rituals, people may chant this mantra to seek blessings for abundance and success in their endeavors.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine assistance in achieving excellence and prosperity.
    • It serves as a reminder that our efforts, when aligned with divine will, can lead to fruitful outcomes.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra encourages individuals to seek higher guidance and to strive for excellence in all their pursuits, fostering a mindset of abundance and creativity.
