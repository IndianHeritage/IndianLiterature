मन्त्र: इमे ये नार्वाङ् न परश्चरन्ति न ब्राह्मणासो न सुतेकरसः | ता एते वाचम् अभिपद्य पापयā सिरīs तन्त्रं तन्वते अप्रजज्ञयः ||
ime ye nārvāṅ na paraś caranti na brāhmaṇāso na sutekarāsaḥ | ta ete vācam abhipadya pāpayā sirīs tantraṁ tanvate aprajajñayaḥ ||
  • ime ye nārvāṅ: This phrase can be interpreted as "those who are not aware" or "those who do not understand." It suggests a state of ignorance or lack of knowledge.
  • na paraś caranti: This translates to "do not wander elsewhere." It implies a focus on one's own path or purpose, rather than being distracted by external influences.
  • na brāhmaṇāso: Referring to "not the learned ones" or "not the wise." This indicates that the mantra is addressing those who may lack wisdom or understanding, regardless of their social status.
  • na sutekarāsaḥ: This can be understood as "not those who are skilled in rituals." It emphasizes that mere ritualistic knowledge is insufficient without true understanding.
  • ta ete vācam abhipadya: This means "these words are directed towards them." It suggests that the following message is intended for those who are unaware or misguided.
  • pāpayā: This translates to "by sin" or "through wrongdoing." It indicates that the actions or thoughts of the individuals in question may lead to negative consequences.
  • sirīs tantraṁ tanvate: This phrase can be interpreted as "they weave a web of confusion." It suggests that ignorance leads to a complex entanglement of thoughts and actions that can be difficult to navigate.
  • aprajajñayaḥ: This means "without true knowledge" or "lacking understanding." It reinforces the idea that the individuals being addressed are not fully aware of the implications of their actions.

Conclusion:

The significance of this mantra lies in its emphasis on the importance of true knowledge and understanding over mere ritualistic practices or superficial wisdom. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios, such as in education, where the focus should be on critical thinking and comprehension rather than rote learning. It also serves as a reminder to seek deeper truths in our actions and decisions, encouraging individuals to reflect on their choices and the potential consequences of ignorance. By fostering awareness and understanding, we can navigate life's complexities more effectively and avoid the pitfalls of misguided actions.
