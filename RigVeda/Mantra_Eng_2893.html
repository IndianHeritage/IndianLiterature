सहावापृत्सु तरणिर्नार्वा व्यानशी रोडसी मेहनावान् |भगो न कारे हव्यो मतीनाम् पितेव चारुः सुहवो वयोधाः ||
sahāvā pṛtsu taraṇir nārvā vyānaśī rodasī mehanāvān | bhago na kāre havyo matīnām piteva cāruḥ suhavo vayodhāḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sahāvā: Together, in harmony
    • pṛtsu: In the waters, in the vastness
    • taraṇir: The one who crosses, the one who traverses
    • nārvā: Not to be hindered, unimpeded
    • vyānaśī: The one who moves, the one who flows
    • rodasī: The two worlds, the heavens and the earth
    • mehanāvān: The one who is powerful, the one who is strong
    • bhago: Wealth, prosperity
    • na: Not
    • kāre: To do, to act
    • havyo: Worthy of offerings, deserving
    • matīnām: Of the wise, of the intelligent
    • piteva: Like a father
    • cāruḥ: Beautiful, pleasing
    • suhavo: Pleasant, agreeable
    • vayodhāḥ: The strong, the powerful
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of unity and harmony in achieving strength and prosperity.
    • The imagery of traversing waters suggests overcoming obstacles and challenges in life.
    • The reference to the two worlds signifies the connection between the material and spiritual realms.
    • Wealth and prosperity are portrayed as outcomes of wise actions and harmonious relationships.
    • The comparison to a father highlights the nurturing and protective aspects of strength and prosperity.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a team setting, collaboration and mutual support can lead to greater success, much like the mantra suggests unity leads to strength.
    • In personal relationships, nurturing bonds can create a supportive environment that fosters growth and prosperity.
    • In business, wise decision-making and ethical practices can lead to sustainable success, reflecting the mantra's emphasis on intelligence and harmony.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the significance of unity, wisdom, and strength in our lives.
    • It encourages individuals to work together harmoniously to overcome challenges and achieve prosperity.
    • In various aspects of life, from personal relationships to professional endeavors, the principles outlined in this mantra can guide us toward a more fulfilling and successful existence.
