सत्याम् आशिषं कृतात् वयोधै कीरिं चिद् ध्य अवथ स्वेभिर एविः |पश्चा मृधो अप भवन्तु विश्वास तद् rodasī श्रिणुतं विश्वमिन्वे ||
satyām āśiṣaṁ kṛṇutā vayodhai kīriṁ cid dhy avatha svebhir evaiḥ | paścā mṛdho apa bhavantu viśvās tad rodasī śṛṇutaṁ viśvaminve ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • satyām: Truth
    • āśiṣaṁ: Blessings
    • kṛṇutā: May you create
    • vayodhai: With the strength of the wind
    • kīriṁ: Fame or glory
    • cid: Consciousness
    • dhy: Meditate
    • avatha: May you protect
    • svebhir: With your own
    • evaiḥ: Indeed
    • paścā: Afterwards
    • mṛdho: From the earth
    • apa: May they go away
    • bhavantu: May they become
    • viśvās: Trust or faith
    • tad: That
    • rodasī: The two worlds
    • śṛṇutaṁ: Listen
    • viśvaminve: To all beings
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of truth and the blessings that come from it.
    • It calls upon the strength of the wind, symbolizing the power of nature and the universe to aid in achieving glory.
    • The invocation of consciousness suggests a deep meditative state where one connects with the divine.
    • It highlights the need for protection and support from one's own resources and abilities.
    • The latter part of the mantra speaks to the importance of trust and faith, urging listeners to pay attention to the interconnectedness of all beings.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, this mantra can be applied when embarking on a new project. Emphasizing truth and integrity can lead to a successful outcome, supported by the collective strength of the team.
    • In personal relationships, fostering trust and open communication aligns with the essence of this mantra, creating a strong bond that can withstand challenges.
    • In environmental efforts, recognizing the power of nature and working in harmony with it can lead to sustainable practices that benefit all beings.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the significance of truth, trust, and the interconnectedness of all life.
    • Its application in various aspects of life—be it personal, professional, or environmental—highlights the universal principles that guide human interactions and the pursuit of goals.
    • By embodying the teachings of this mantra, individuals can cultivate a life of integrity, harmony, and mutual respect, ultimately contributing to a more peaceful and prosperous world.
