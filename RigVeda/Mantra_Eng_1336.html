सं यन् मिथः पस्पृधाना सो अग्मता शुभे मखाऽमिता जायवो रणे |युवोर अह प्रवनें चेकिते रथो यद अश्विनाऽ वहतः सूरीम् आ वरम् ||
saṁ yan mithaḥ paspṛdhānāso agmata śubhe makhā amitā jāyavo raṇe | yuvor aha pravaṇe cekite ratho yad aśvinā vahathaḥ sūrim ā varam ||
  • saṁ yan mithaḥ paspṛdhānāso: This phrase can be interpreted as "when those who are united and working together." It emphasizes the importance of collaboration and unity among individuals or groups.
  • agmata śubhe makhā amitā jāyavo raṇe: This part translates to "may they achieve the auspicious and boundless results in the battle." It signifies the desire for success and prosperity in endeavors, particularly in challenging situations.
  • yuvor aha pravaṇe cekite: This can be understood as "may the young and energetic ones be vigilant." It highlights the need for awareness and readiness among the youth, who are often the driving force in any society.
  • ratho yad aśvinā vahathaḥ sūrim ā varam: This phrase means "as the Ashvins (the twin deities) carry the brave one in their chariot." The Ashvins symbolize health, vitality, and the ability to overcome obstacles, suggesting that divine support is essential for success.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda encapsulates the essence of teamwork, vigilance, and divine assistance in achieving success. In real-life scenarios, it can be applied in various contexts such as:

  • Teamwork in Organizations: Just as the mantra emphasizes unity, modern workplaces thrive on collaboration. Successful projects often result from diverse teams working together towards a common goal.
  • Youth Empowerment: The call for vigilance among the youth resonates today as they are the future leaders. Encouraging young individuals to be proactive and aware of their surroundings can lead to positive societal changes.
  • Overcoming Challenges: The reference to divine support in battles can be likened to seeking guidance and strength during difficult times. Many people turn to spiritual practices or community support to navigate challenges in life.

Overall, this mantra serves as a reminder of the power of unity, the importance of being alert and prepared, and the belief in higher support during our endeavors.
