तā हि क्षत्रं धारयेत् अनु द्यून दृष्णुते सानुम उपमाद् इव द्योः | दृष्णो नक्षत्र उता विश्वदेवो भूमिम आतान द्यां धासिनायोः ||
tā hi kṣatraṁ dhārayethe anu dyūn dṛṁhethe sānum upamād iva dyoḥ | dṛḻho nakṣatra uta viśvadevo bhūmim ātān dyāṁ dhāsināyoḥ ||
  • tā hi kṣatraṁ dhārayethe: This phrase can be translated as "they indeed hold the power." Here, "kṣatra" refers to power or authority, often associated with rulers or warriors. In real life, this can be seen in leaders who uphold justice and protect their communities.
  • anu dyūn dṛṁhethe: This means "following the heavens." It suggests a connection between earthly actions and celestial guidance. For example, when individuals or leaders make decisions based on ethical or moral principles, they are metaphorically following the heavens.
  • sānum upamād iva dyoḥ: This translates to "like the heavens in their greatness." It emphasizes the vastness and importance of the heavens, paralleling the responsibilities of those in power. In a practical sense, this can relate to leaders who aspire to greatness and strive to serve their people with integrity.
  • dṛḻho nakṣatra uta viśvadevo: This phrase means "the firm star and the universal deities." It signifies stability and the guiding forces in the universe. In everyday life, this can be likened to the role of mentors or guiding figures who provide direction and support in times of uncertainty.
  • bhūmim ātān dyāṁ dhāsināyoḥ: This translates to "the earth is held by the servants of the heavens." It implies that those who serve with dedication and humility are the true custodians of the earth. In a real-world context, this can refer to environmental activists or community leaders who work tirelessly to protect the planet and its resources.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of leadership, responsibility, and the connection between earthly actions and celestial guidance. It serves as a reminder that true power lies in serving others and upholding justice. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios, such as in governance, community service, and environmental stewardship, where individuals and leaders are called to act with integrity and purpose for the greater good.
