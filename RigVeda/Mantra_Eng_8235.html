तं त्वा सुतेष्वाभुवो हिन्विरे देवतातये |स पवस्वानयाय रुचाः ||
taṁ tvā suteṣv ābhuvo hinvire devatātaye | sa pavasvānayā rucā ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • taṁ: that (referring to a divine entity or force)
    • tvā: you (addressing the divine)
    • suteṣv: in the offerings (suteṣv refers to the offerings made to the gods)
    • ābhuvo: may we be blessed or may we attain
    • hinvire: may we be inspired or guided
    • devatātaye: for the purpose of the divine or in the service of the divine
    • sa: he (referring to the divine force)
    • pavasvānayā: with the power of purification or with the energy of the divine
    • rucā: with brilliance or radiance
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer for divine guidance and inspiration.
    • It emphasizes the importance of offerings and devotion in seeking blessings from the divine.
    • The invocation of the divine is a call for enlightenment and clarity in one's life.
    • The phrase "with the power of purification" suggests a need for inner cleansing and spiritual growth.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a personal context, someone may recite this mantra during meditation to seek clarity and guidance in making important life decisions.
    • In a community setting, this mantra could be chanted during rituals or ceremonies to invoke blessings for collective well-being and harmony.
    • In educational settings, students may use this mantra to focus their minds and seek inspiration for their studies and creative endeavors.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine assistance and the pursuit of spiritual enlightenment.
    • It serves as a reminder of the importance of devotion, offerings, and the quest for inner purity.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as personal growth, community rituals, and educational pursuits, fostering a deeper connection with the divine and enhancing one's spiritual journey.
