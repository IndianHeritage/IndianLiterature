ये त्वा देवोश्रिकं मन्यमानाः पापाः भद्रम उपजीवन्ति पज्राः |न दूद्येऽनु ददासि वामं बृहस्पते चयसा इति प्रियारुम् ||
ye tvā devosrikam manyamānāḥ pāpā bhadram upajīvanti pajrāḥ | na dūḍhye3 anu dadāsi vāmam bṛhaspate cayasa it piyārum ||
  • Translation: "Those who, considering you as the divine, live in harmony and righteousness, do not abandon you, O Bṛhaspati, and they receive your blessings."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • ye: Those who
    • tvā: You
    • devosrikam: Divine or God-like
    • manyamānāḥ: Considering or believing
    • pāpā: Sins or wrongdoings
    • bhadram: Goodness or auspiciousness
    • upajīvanti: Live or thrive
    • pajrāḥ: Righteous or virtuous
    • na: Not
    • dūḍhye: Abandon or forsake
    • anu: After or following
    • dadāsi: You give
    • vāmam: Your blessings
    • bṛhaspate: O Bṛhaspati (the deity of wisdom and eloquence)
    • cayasa: With strength or power
    • it: This
    • piyārum: Sweet or pleasant
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of recognizing and honoring the divine presence in our lives.
    • It suggests that those who live righteously and maintain a connection with the divine will be blessed and supported.
    • For example, in a workplace, employees who work with integrity and respect for one another often find themselves in a positive environment, leading to success and harmony.
    • In personal relationships, individuals who value honesty and kindness tend to build stronger bonds, reflecting the essence of this mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for righteousness and the recognition of divine guidance in our lives.
    • It teaches us that by living a life of virtue and acknowledging the divine, we can attract blessings and positive outcomes.
    • This principle is applicable in various real-life scenarios, such as in our personal conduct, professional ethics, and community interactions.
    • Ultimately, it serves as a reminder that our actions and beliefs shape our experiences and the world around us.
