वनस्पतिर अवसृजन उप स्थाद अग्निर हविः सूदयाति प्र धीभिः |त्रिधा समक्तं नयतु प्रजानन देवभ्यो दैव्यम् शमितोप हव्यम् ||
vanaspatir avasṛjann upa sthād agnir haviḥ sūdayāti pra dhībhiḥ | tridhā samaktaṁ nayatu prajānan devebhyo daivyaḥ śamitopa havyam ||
  • Vanaspatir avasṛjann: This phrase refers to the "lord of the forest" or "the plant life" that brings forth abundance. In real life, this can be likened to the importance of nature and biodiversity in sustaining life on Earth. For example, forests provide oxygen, habitat for wildlife, and resources for humans.
  • Upa sthād agnir haviḥ: Here, "Agnir" refers to fire, which is a symbol of energy and transformation. "Haviḥ" signifies offerings or sacrifices. This can be related to how fire is used in various cultures for cooking, warmth, and rituals. For instance, in many Indian households, the sacred fire (Havan) is lit during ceremonies to invoke blessings.
  • Sūdayāti pra dhībhiḥ: This part suggests that through wisdom and understanding, one can attain clarity and enlightenment. In modern life, this can be seen in the pursuit of knowledge through education and self-improvement, leading to better decision-making and personal growth.
  • Tridhā samaktaṁ nayatu prajānan: This phrase implies a harmonious balance in three aspects (possibly referring to body, mind, and spirit) and encourages guiding the community (prajā) towards a common goal. In real life, this can be observed in teamwork and collaboration in workplaces or community projects where diverse skills and perspectives come together for a shared purpose.
  • Devebhyo daivyaḥ śamitopa havyam: This line emphasizes the divine nature of offerings made to the gods. It reflects the idea of gratitude and respect towards higher powers or the universe. In contemporary life, this can be seen in various forms of gratitude practices, such as mindfulness and appreciation for the resources and opportunities we have.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the interconnectedness of nature, energy, wisdom, community, and divinity. Its significance lies in reminding us of our responsibilities towards the environment, the importance of knowledge, and the value of collaboration. In real life, these principles can guide us in sustainable living, fostering community spirit, and cultivating a sense of gratitude, ultimately leading to a more harmonious existence.
