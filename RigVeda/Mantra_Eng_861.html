अभि त्वा गोतमā गिरा जातवेदो विचर्षणे |द्यम्नैर अभि प्र णोनुमः ||
abhi tvā gotamā girā jātavedo vicarṣaṇe | dyumnair abhi pra ṇonumaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • abhi: towards, for
    • tvā: you
    • gotamā: O Gotama (a name often associated with the sage Vasishtha)
    • girā: with words, through speech
    • jātavedo: O Jātavedas (a name for Agni, the fire god)
    • vicarṣaṇe: in the act of moving, in the process of wandering
    • dyumnair: with glory, with brilliance
    • abhi: towards, for
    • pra: forth, out
    • ṇonumaḥ: we bow, we offer our homage
  • Overall Meaning: This mantra is a prayer directed towards Agni, the fire god, invoking his presence and brilliance. The speaker addresses Agni as "Gotama," indicating a relationship of respect and reverence. The mantra emphasizes the importance of words and speech in connecting with the divine, suggesting that through our words, we can reach out to the higher powers.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • Communication: Just as the mantra highlights the power of words, in our daily lives, effective communication can bridge gaps between individuals, fostering understanding and connection.
    • Rituals and Offerings: In many cultures, offerings to deities are made with words of praise and gratitude, similar to how this mantra is an offering to Agni. For instance, during Diwali, people light lamps and recite prayers to invite prosperity and well-being.
    • Personal Growth: The invocation of Agni can symbolize the inner fire or passion that drives individuals towards their goals. Just as Agni is a purifying force, individuals can harness their inner strength to overcome challenges.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its recognition of the power of words and the divine connection through speech. It teaches us that our intentions, expressed through language, can invoke higher energies and facilitate personal and communal growth. In real-life scenarios, this mantra encourages us to communicate effectively, honor our traditions, and harness our inner strength to achieve our aspirations.
