अबोध्य अग्निः समिधा जनानाम् प्रति धेनुमिवायतिम् उषासम् |यःवा इव प्र वयाम् उज्जिहानाः प्र भानवः सिस्रते नाकम् अच् च ||
abodhy agniḥ samidhā janānām prati dhenum ivāyatīm uṣāsam | yahvā iva pra vayām ujjihānāḥ pra bhānavaḥ sisrate nākam accha ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • abodhy: awaken or inspire
    • agniḥ: fire
    • samidhā: kindled or ignited
    • janānām: of the people
    • prati: towards
    • dhenum: the cow (symbolizing wealth and nourishment)
    • ivāyatīm: like a generous or bountiful
    • uṣāsam: dawn or morning
    • yahvā: who
    • iva: like
    • pra: forth or forward
    • vayām: we
    • ujjihānāḥ: rising or ascending
    • pra bhānavaḥ: shining or illuminating
    • sisrate: flow or move
    • nākam: towards the sky or heaven
    • accha: good or auspicious
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the fire (Agni) as a source of inspiration and awakening for the people.
    • It likens the fire to the dawn, which brings light and hope, suggesting that just as the dawn nourishes the earth, fire nourishes the spirit.
    • The reference to the cow symbolizes abundance and prosperity, indicating that the blessings of Agni lead to a fruitful life.
    • The mantra emphasizes the importance of rising and shining, encouraging individuals to strive for higher goals and aspirations.
    • It also suggests a collective movement towards goodness and auspiciousness, highlighting the interconnectedness of people in their pursuit of enlightenment.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community gathering, lighting a fire can symbolize unity and the sharing of knowledge, much like how Agni is invoked for collective inspiration.
    • In personal development, the idea of 'rising with the dawn' can be applied to setting daily goals and striving for personal growth each day.
    • In business, the mantra can inspire leaders to create a nurturing environment that fosters creativity and abundance, akin to the nurturing qualities of the cow.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of inspiration, unity, and the pursuit of higher ideals in life.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal growth to community building, emphasizing the role of collective effort in achieving prosperity and enlightenment.
    • By invoking the qualities of Agni, individuals and communities can strive towards a brighter, more auspicious future.
