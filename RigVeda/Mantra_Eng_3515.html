मां नरः स्वश्वा वाजयन्तो मां वृताḥ समरणे हवन्ते |कृणोम्य आजिम मगवाहम इन्द्र इयर्मि रेणुम अभिभूत्योजाः ||
māṁ naraḥ svaśvā vājayanto māṁ vṛtāḥ samaraṇe havante | kṛṇomy ājim maghavāham indra iyarmi reṇum abhibhūtyojāḥ ||
  • māṁ naraḥ svaśvā vājayanto: This phrase can be translated to "Men, who are strong and powerful, are calling me." Here, "naraḥ" refers to men or people, "svaśvā" implies strength or power, and "vājayanto" means to call or summon. This suggests a collective strength and unity among individuals.
  • mṛtāḥ samaraṇe havante: This translates to "Those who are united in battle invoke me." "Mṛtāḥ" refers to those who are united or gathered, "samaraṇe" means in battle or conflict, and "havante" indicates invocation or calling upon. This emphasizes the importance of unity and collaboration in facing challenges.
  • kṛṇomy ājim maghavāham indra: This can be interpreted as "I create strength, I am the powerful Indra." "Kṛṇomy" means to create or make, "ājim" refers to strength or power, "maghavāham" indicates being powerful, and "Indra" is a significant deity representing strength and leadership. This highlights the role of leadership in fostering strength and resilience.
  • iyarmi reṇum abhibhūtyojāḥ: This translates to "I overcome obstacles with my strength." "Iyarmi" means to overcome, "reṇum" refers to obstacles or challenges, and "abhibhūtyojāḥ" indicates the use of strength to conquer. This signifies the ability to face and overcome difficulties through inner strength and determination.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of unity, strength, and leadership in overcoming challenges. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various contexts such as teamwork in professional settings, community efforts in social causes, or even personal struggles where support from others is crucial. The invocation of collective strength and the role of a leader in guiding and empowering individuals can lead to successful outcomes in any endeavor. This mantra serves as a reminder that together, with strength and unity, we can face and conquer the battles of life.
