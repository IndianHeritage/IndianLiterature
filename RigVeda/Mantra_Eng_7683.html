अश्वं न गीरभिः रथ्यं सुदानवो मर्मृज्यन्ते देवयवः |उभे तोके तनये दसम विश्वते परषि राधो मघोनाम ||
aśvaṁ na gīrbhī rathyaṁ sudānavo marmṛjyante devayavaḥ | ubhe toke tanaye dasma viśpate parṣi rādho maghonām ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • aśvaṁ: horse
    • na: not
    • gīrbhī: by the songs or hymns
    • rathyaṁ: chariot
    • sudānavo: well-fed or prosperous
    • marmṛjyante: are wiped away or removed
    • devayavaḥ: divine beings or deities
    • ubhe: both
    • toke: in the offspring
    • tanaye: in the progeny
    • dasma: to us
    • viśpate: O Lord of the Universe
    • parṣi: protect or guard
    • rādho: grant or bestow
    • maghonām: of the wealthy or prosperous
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the importance of divine blessings in ensuring prosperity and protection.
    • The mention of "aśvaṁ" (horse) and "rathyaṁ" (chariot) symbolizes strength, speed, and the journey of life.
    • The phrase "sudānavo marmṛjyante" suggests that even the well-fed or prosperous can face challenges that need divine intervention.
    • It emphasizes the role of divine beings in safeguarding both the individual and their offspring, ensuring a legacy of prosperity.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern life, this mantra can be related to the importance of seeking guidance and blessings from higher powers during significant life events, such as starting a new business or family.
    • For instance, many people perform rituals or prayers before embarking on a new venture, believing that divine support will lead to success and protection.
    • Additionally, parents often pray for the well-being and success of their children, reflecting the desire for divine protection over future generations.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of divine blessings in achieving prosperity and protection in life.
    • It serves as a reminder that while we may strive for success, the support of divine forces is crucial in overcoming obstacles and ensuring a prosperous future.
    • In essence, it encourages individuals to maintain a connection with the divine, fostering a sense of security and hope for themselves and their descendants.
