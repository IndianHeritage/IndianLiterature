अस्य प्र जातवेदसो विप्रवीरस्य मीळ्हुषः |महिम् इयर्मि सुश्रुतिम् ||
asya pra jātavedaso vipravīrasya mīḻhuṣaḥ | mahīm iyarmi suṣṭutim ||
  • Mantra: "asya pra jātavedaso vipravīrasya mīḻhuṣaḥ | mahīm iyarmi suṣṭutim ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • asya: of this
    • pra: before, in front of
    • jātavedaso: O Jātavedas (the one who knows all births, a name for Agni)
    • vipravīrasya: of the noble and brave
    • mīḻhuṣaḥ: of the one who is sweet, pleasant
    • mahīm: greatness, vastness
    • iyarmi: I invoke, I call upon
    • suṣṭutim: good praise, excellent hymn
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Agni, the fire god, who is considered the mediator between humans and the divine.
    • The phrase "asya pra jātavedaso" acknowledges Agni as the one who is aware of all births and the essence of life.
    • "vipravīrasya mīḻhuṣaḥ" refers to the noble and brave individuals who embody sweetness and pleasantness in their actions and thoughts.
    • The invocation of "mahīm iyarmi suṣṭutim" signifies a call to recognize and praise the greatness of Agni, emphasizing the importance of expressing gratitude and reverence.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, we often seek guidance and support from mentors or leaders who embody the qualities of bravery and nobility, similar to how Agni is revered in this mantra.
    • During significant life events, such as weddings or festivals, fire is often invoked as a symbol of purity and transformation, reflecting the essence of Agni as a divine presence.
    • In moments of challenge, individuals may call upon their inner strength and courage, akin to the "vipravīrasya" aspect, to overcome obstacles and achieve their goals.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of recognizing the divine in our lives and the importance of invoking qualities such as bravery, nobility, and sweetness.
    • It serves as a reminder to appreciate the greatness around us and to express gratitude for the guidance we receive from both the divine and those who inspire us.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra encourages individuals to embody these qualities, fostering a sense of community and connection with the divine and each other.
