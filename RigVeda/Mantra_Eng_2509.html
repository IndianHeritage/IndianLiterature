द्युश्च तव प्रथिवी यज्ञियासो नि होतारं साधयन्ते दमाय |यदी विश्वो मानवशीर देवयन्तीः प्रयस्वतीर ईळते शुक्रं आर्चिः ||
dyauś ca tvā pṛthivī yajñiyāso ni hotāraṁ sādayante damāya | yadī viśo mānuṣīr devayantīḥ prayasvatīr īḻate śukram arciḥ ||
  • dyauś ca tvā pṛthivī: This phrase translates to "Heaven and Earth." In many cultures, including Hinduism, Heaven represents the divine or spiritual realm, while Earth symbolizes the physical and material world. This duality reflects the balance between the spiritual and the material aspects of life.
  • yajñiyāso ni hotāraṁ sādayante damāya: This part can be interpreted as "the priests who perform the sacrifices (yajñas) are the ones who bring forth the offerings." In real life, this can be seen in various rituals and ceremonies where offerings are made to deities or ancestors, emphasizing the importance of gratitude and respect in our lives.
  • yadī viśo mānuṣīr devayantīḥ: This translates to "if the community of humans (mānuṣīr) is devoted to the divine (devayantīḥ)." This highlights the significance of community and collective devotion. In modern society, this can be reflected in community service, where people come together to support a common cause, fostering unity and cooperation.
  • prayasvatīr īḻate śukram arciḥ: This phrase means "the efforts (prayasvatīr) shine forth as pure light (śukram arciḥ)." It signifies that sincere efforts and hard work lead to positive outcomes and enlightenment. In everyday life, this can be seen in the pursuit of education or personal goals, where dedication and perseverance result in success and fulfillment.

  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the interconnectedness of the spiritual and material worlds, the importance of community and collective efforts, and the rewards of hard work and dedication. In real life, it serves as a reminder to maintain a balance between our spiritual practices and our material pursuits, to work together for the common good, and to strive for excellence in our endeavors. This mantra can be applicable in various scenarios, such as community gatherings, educational pursuits, and personal development, encouraging individuals to reflect on their actions and their impact on the world around them.
