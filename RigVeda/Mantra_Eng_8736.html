आ वाच्यस्व सुदक्ष चाम्वोः सुतो विश्वां वाह्निर न विश्वपतिḥ | वृष्टिं दिवः पवस्व रीतिं अपां जिन्वा गविष्टये धियः ||
ā vacyasva sudakṣa camvoḥ suto viśāṁ vahnir na viśpatiḥ | vṛṣṭiṁ divaḥ pavasva rītim apāṁ jinvā gaviṣṭaye dhiyaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā vacyasva: "Come forth" or "become manifest."
    • sudakṣa: "Well-skilled" or "expert."
    • camvoḥ: "Of the camels" or "of the strong."
    • suto: "Son" or "offspring."
    • viśāṁ: "Of the people" or "of the community."
    • vahnir: "Fire" or "the divine energy."
    • na viśpatiḥ: "Not the lord of all" or "not the ruler."
    • vṛṣṭiṁ: "Rain" or "abundance."
    • divaḥ: "Of the heavens" or "from the sky."
    • pavasva: "Let it flow" or "let it come."
    • rītim: "Prosperity" or "wealth."
    • apāṁ: "Of the waters" or "of the fluids."
    • jinvā: "Conquer" or "overcome."
    • gaviṣṭaye: "For the benefit of the cows" or "for nourishment."
    • dhiyaḥ: "Intellect" or "wisdom."
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the divine energies to manifest abundance and prosperity.
    • It emphasizes the importance of skilled actions and the role of community in achieving collective goals.
    • The reference to "fire" symbolizes transformation and the energy needed to bring about change.
    • The call for rain signifies the need for nourishment and sustenance, both physically and spiritually.
    • By mentioning "cows," it highlights the significance of agriculture and livestock in sustaining life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In agriculture, farmers pray for rain to ensure a good harvest, reflecting the need for divine intervention in their efforts.
    • In a corporate setting, teamwork and skilled individuals coming together can lead to successful projects, akin to the community aspect of the mantra.
    • In personal development, individuals seek wisdom and guidance to overcome challenges, paralleling the call for intellect in the mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the interconnectedness of divine energy, community effort, and the natural world.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from agriculture to personal growth, emphasizing the need for collaboration and divine support.
    • Ultimately, it serves as a reminder of the importance of nurturing both our physical and spiritual needs for a prosperous life.
