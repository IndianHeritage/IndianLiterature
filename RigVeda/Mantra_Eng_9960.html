ऊर्ध्वो ग्रावा वसवोऽस्तु सोतरी विश्वा द्वेषांसी सन्तर युयोत |स नो देवः सविता पायुर ईड्य आ सर्वतातिम अदितिं वृणीमहे ||
ūrdhvo grāvā vasavo 'stu sotari viśvā dveṣāṁsi sanutar yuyota | sa no devaḥ savitā pāyur īḍya ā sarvatātim aditiṁ vṛṇīmahe ||
  • ūrdhvo grāvā vasavo 'stu: This phrase can be translated as "May the powers of the heavens be exalted." Here, "ūrdhvo" means "upward" or "higher," and "vasavo" refers to the divine powers or deities associated with prosperity and abundance. In real life, this can be seen as a call to elevate our aspirations and seek higher ideals in our lives, much like striving for personal growth or success in our careers.
  • sotari viśvā dveṣāṁsi sanutar yuyota: This part translates to "May the universal forces unite and overcome all enmities." "Sotari" refers to the act of overcoming or conquering, while "dveṣāṁsi" means "enmities" or "hostilities." This can be related to real-life situations where individuals or communities come together to resolve conflicts, promoting peace and understanding, such as in community service or conflict resolution initiatives.
  • sa no devaḥ savitā pāyur īḍya: This translates to "May the divine Savita protect us and be worthy of our praise." "Savita" is a deity associated with the sun and life-giving energy. In practical terms, this can be seen as a reminder to appreciate and respect the natural forces that sustain us, such as the environment and the sun's energy, which are crucial for our survival and well-being.
  • ā sarvatātim aditiṁ vṛṇīmahe: This means "We choose the limitless and the boundless." "Aditi" represents the concept of infinity and the mother of all gods. In everyday life, this can symbolize the pursuit of limitless possibilities and the importance of thinking beyond boundaries, whether in personal ambitions, creativity, or innovation.

  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of striving for higher ideals, unity in overcoming conflicts, reverence for divine forces, and the pursuit of limitless possibilities. In real-life scenarios, it encourages individuals and communities to work together for peace, appreciate the natural world, and aspire to achieve their fullest potential. This mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as personal development, community building, and environmental conservation, highlighting the interconnectedness of all aspects of life.
