आ तु गहि प्र तु द्रव मत्स्वा सुतस्य गोमता: |तन्तुं तनुष्व पूर्व्यं यथा विदे ||
ā tū gahi pra tu drava matsvā sutasya gomataḥ | tantuṁ tanuṣva pūrvyaṁ yathā vide ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā: from
    • tū: you
    • gahi: hold or grasp
    • pra: forward or in front
    • tu: but
    • drava: flowing or liquid
    • matsvā: of the mind or intellect
    • sutasya: of the son or offspring
    • gomataḥ: of the cow or the one who possesses cows
    • tantuṁ: the thread or connection
    • tanuṣva: make thin or subtle
    • pūrvyaṁ: the previous or ancient
    • yathā: as or in the way
    • vide: to know or to understand
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks to the relationship between the individual and the divine, emphasizing the importance of grasping the essence of life and knowledge.
    • The phrase "ā tū gahi" suggests a call to the divine or a higher power to hold or guide us in our journey.
    • "Drava matsvā" indicates the need for clarity of thought and intellect, akin to how water flows freely and clearly.
    • The reference to "gomataḥ" symbolizes wealth and nourishment, as cows were considered sacred and a source of sustenance in ancient times.
    • "Tantuṁ tanuṣva pūrvyaṁ" implies the importance of connecting with our roots and understanding our past to navigate our present and future.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern life, this mantra can be applied in the context of seeking guidance during challenging times, much like how individuals turn to mentors or spiritual leaders for support.
    • The emphasis on clarity of thought can be seen in practices such as meditation or mindfulness, which help individuals achieve mental clarity and emotional balance.
    • The connection to one's roots can be reflected in family traditions and cultural practices that help individuals understand their identity and values.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of seeking guidance, maintaining clarity of thought, and connecting with our heritage.
    • Its significance lies in its applicability to everyday life, encouraging individuals to reflect on their journey, seek wisdom, and nurture their connections with both the past and the present.
    • By embodying the principles of this mantra, one can cultivate a more meaningful and purposeful existence.
