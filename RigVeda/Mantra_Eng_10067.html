व्यचस्वतीर उर्वियā वि श्रयन्ताम् पतिभ्यः न जनयः शुम्भमानाः |देवीर द्वारो बृहतीर विश्वमिन्वा देवभ्यः भवत सुप्रायणाः ||
vyacasvatīr urviyā vi śrayantām patibhyo na janayaḥ śumbhamānāḥ | devīr dvāro bṛhatīr viśvaminvā devebhyo bhavata suprāyaṇāḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • vyacasvatīr: Those who are wise or knowledgeable.
    • urviyā: With great abundance or vastness.
    • vi: In this context, it can mean 'with' or 'together with.'
    • śrayantām: May they support or uphold.
    • patibhyo: To the partners or companions.
    • na: Not.
    • janayaḥ: Those who create or generate.
    • śumbhamānāḥ: Those who are in a state of abundance or prosperity.
    • devīr: The goddesses or divine beings.
    • dvāro: Doors or gateways.
    • bṛhatīr: The great or vast.
    • viśvaminvā: In all directions or universally.
    • devebhyo: To the gods or divine entities.
    • bhavata: May you become or may you be.
    • suprāyaṇāḥ: Well-guided or auspiciously led.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of wisdom and abundance in life.
    • It calls upon divine support for individuals and their relationships, urging them to avoid creating negativity or conflict.
    • The mention of goddesses and divine beings suggests a plea for guidance and protection in all endeavors.
    • The idea of doors or gateways symbolizes opportunities and pathways that lead to prosperity and success.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be applied to foster teamwork and collaboration, ensuring that all partners support each other in achieving common goals.
    • In personal relationships, it serves as a reminder to avoid creating conflicts and to nurture a supportive environment.
    • In community settings, it encourages individuals to work together for the greater good, promoting harmony and abundance for all.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for wisdom, support, and divine guidance in all aspects of life.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal relationships to professional collaborations, emphasizing the importance of unity and positivity.
    • By invoking this mantra, individuals can seek to create a harmonious environment that fosters growth, prosperity, and well-being.
