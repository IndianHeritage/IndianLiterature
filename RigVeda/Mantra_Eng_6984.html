परीणो वृणजननघा दुर्गाणि रथ्यो यथा |स्यामेद इन्द्रस्य शर्मण्य आदित्यानामुतावस्य अनहसो वूतयः सुतयः वूतयः ||
pari ṇo vṛṇajann aghā durgāṇi rathyo yathā | syāmed indrasya śarmaṇy ādityānām utāvasy anehaso va ūtayaḥ suūtayo va ūtayaḥ ||
  • Mantra: "pari ṇo vṛṇajann aghā durgāṇi rathyo yathā | syāmed indrasya śarmaṇy ādityānām utāvasy anehaso va ūtayaḥ suūtayo va ūtayaḥ ||"
  • Translation: "May we choose the auspicious paths, free from obstacles, as the chariots of Indra and the Adityas move swiftly and safely."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • pari: around, encompassing
    • ṇo: us, our
    • vṛṇajann: may we choose
    • aghā: free from obstacles, sin
    • durgāṇi: difficult paths
    • rathyo: chariots
    • yathā: as, like
    • syāmed: may we be
    • indrasya: of Indra
    • śarmaṇy: in safety, protection
    • ādityānām: of the Adityas (solar deities)
    • utāvasy: swift, rapid
    • anehaso: without hindrance
    • va: or
    • ūtayaḥ: may they move
    • suūtayo: well-moving
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of choosing the right path in life, one that is free from obstacles and difficulties.
    • It draws a parallel between our journey and the swift, unhindered movement of chariots, symbolizing progress and success.
    • Indra, the king of gods, represents strength and protection, while the Adityas symbolize light and truth.
    • In real life, this can be related to making wise decisions in our careers, relationships, and personal growth.
    • For example, when faced with a difficult choice, one might seek guidance from mentors or trusted friends, ensuring that the path chosen is beneficial and free from negative consequences.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for clarity and wisdom in decision-making.
    • It encourages individuals to seek paths that lead to safety, success, and fulfillment, much like the divine chariots of Indra and the Adityas.
    • In everyday life, this mantra can serve as a reminder to reflect on our choices and strive for a life that is harmonious and prosperous.
    • By invoking this mantra, one can cultivate a mindset that prioritizes positive outcomes and the pursuit of one's goals with confidence and clarity.
