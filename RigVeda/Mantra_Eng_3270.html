मन्त्र: कस ते मातरं विधवाम् अचक्रच छयुं कस त्वाम् अजिघांसच चरन्तम् |कस ते देवो अधि मार्डीका आसीद यत प्राक्षिणाः पितरम् पादगृह्य ||
kas te mātaraṁ vidhavām acakrac chayuṁ kas tvām ajighāṁsac carantam | kas te devo adhi mārḍīka āsīd yat prākṣiṇāḥ pitaram pādagṛhya ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • kas: Who
    • te: Your
    • mātaraṁ: Mother
    • vidhavām: Who is bereaved
    • acakrac: Who is without a protector
    • shayuṁ: Who is sleeping
    • kas: Who
    • tvām: You
    • ajighāṁsac: Who desires to harm
    • carantam: Moving or wandering
    • kas: Who
    • te: Your
    • devo: God or divine being
    • adhi: Above or over
    • mārḍīka: A term often associated with a protective force
    • āsīd: Was or existed
    • yat: That
    • prākṣiṇāḥ: Ancestors or forefathers
    • pitaram: Father
    • pādagṛhya: Holding the feet or seeking refuge
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra poses a series of questions, reflecting a deep inquiry into the nature of protection, motherhood, and divine presence.
    • The repeated questioning of "Who" emphasizes the search for understanding and clarity in times of uncertainty.
    • The mention of a bereaved mother and a wandering individual suggests themes of vulnerability and the need for support.
    • The reference to divine beings and ancestors indicates a connection to spiritual guidance and the importance of lineage in providing strength and protection.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In times of personal crisis, individuals often seek the support of family and friends, much like the bereaved mother in the mantra. This reflects the universal need for community and connection.
    • When facing challenges, people may turn to their cultural or spiritual beliefs for guidance, similar to seeking the protection of divine beings mentioned in the mantra.
    • The concept of honoring ancestors can be seen in various cultures where rituals and traditions are practiced to remember and seek blessings from forefathers.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of seeking protection and understanding in a world filled with uncertainties.
    • It encourages individuals to reflect on their relationships with their mothers, divine beings, and ancestors, recognizing the support systems that exist in their lives.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of community, spiritual guidance, and the need for protection in our journeys.
