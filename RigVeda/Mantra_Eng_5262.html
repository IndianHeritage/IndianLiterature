उद अस्य शोचिर अस्ताद आजुह्वानस्य मीळ्हुषः |उद धूमासो अरुषासो दिविष्पृशः सम अग्निम इन्दते नरः ||
ud asya śocir asthād ājuhvānasya mīḻhuṣaḥ | ud dhūmāso aruṣāso divispṛśaḥ sam agnim indhate naraḥ ||
  • ud asya: This phrase can be interpreted as "from above" or "from the heights." It signifies a connection to the divine or higher realms.
  • śocir asthād: "śocir" means "bright" or "radiant," while "asthād" can be understood as "has established." Together, they convey the idea of a radiant presence that has taken its place.
  • ājuhvānasya: This term refers to "the one who calls" or "invokes." It suggests an active engagement with the divine or the forces of nature.
  • mīḻhuṣaḥ: This word can be translated as "the one who is powerful" or "the strong." It emphasizes strength and capability.
  • ud dhūmāso: "ud" again means "from above," while "dhūmāso" refers to "smoky" or "cloudy." This could symbolize the rising of smoke, often associated with offerings or sacrifices.
  • aruṣāso: This term means "red" or "fiery," indicating a vibrant energy or passion.
  • divispṛśaḥ: "divispṛśaḥ" translates to "touching the heavens" or "reaching for the divine." It signifies aspiration and the pursuit of higher goals.
  • sam agnim: "sam" means "together" or "with," and "agnim" refers to "fire." This phrase suggests a collective invocation of fire, which is often a symbol of transformation and purification.
  • indhate: This word means "ignites" or "kindles." It indicates the act of bringing forth energy or life.
  • naraḥ: "naraḥ" means "man" or "human." It emphasizes the role of humanity in the invocation and connection to the divine.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda emphasizes the relationship between humanity and the divine, highlighting the importance of invoking higher energies and the transformative power of fire. In real life, this can be seen in rituals, ceremonies, and personal practices where individuals seek to connect with their higher selves or the universe. The significance lies in the recognition of our potential to reach for the divine, to invoke strength and brightness in our lives, and to engage actively with the forces that shape our existence. Whether through meditation, prayer, or community gatherings, this mantra serves as a reminder of our capacity to ignite change and aspire towards greater heights.
