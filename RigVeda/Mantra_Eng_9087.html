मन्त्र: कस्ते मद इन्द्र रन्त्यो भूद दुरो गिरो अभ्य उग्रो वि धावा |कद्वाहो अर्वाग उप मां मनिषा आ त्वा शक्यां उपमं राधो अन्नैः ||
kas te mada indra rantyo bhūd duro giro abhy u1gro vi dhāva | kad vāho arvāg upa mā manīṣā ā tvā śakyām upamaṁ rādho annaiḥ ||
  • kas te mada indra rantyo bhūd: This phrase can be translated to "Who is the one that brings joy, O Indra?" Here, Indra is addressed as the deity of rain and thunder, symbolizing strength and power. In real life, this can be likened to leaders or influential figures who bring happiness and prosperity to their communities.
  • duro giro abhy u1gro vi dhāva: This translates to "May the strong words and actions of the mighty be effective." It emphasizes the importance of powerful speech and decisive action. In a modern context, this can relate to effective communication in leadership, where strong words can inspire and mobilize people towards a common goal.
  • kad vāho arvāg upa mā manīṣā: This means "When will the wise one come to us?" It reflects a yearning for wisdom and guidance. In everyday life, this can be seen in the search for mentors or wise leaders who can provide direction in times of uncertainty.
  • ā tvā śakyām upamaṁ rādho annaiḥ: This translates to "We are capable of praising you with offerings." It signifies the act of devotion and gratitude towards the divine. In practical terms, this can be related to the importance of gratitude in our lives, where acknowledging the contributions of others can foster stronger relationships and community bonds.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking strength, wisdom, and guidance from higher powers, while also emphasizing the importance of effective communication and gratitude. In real life, it serves as a reminder to look for inspiration in leaders, to communicate effectively, and to express gratitude towards those who contribute to our well-being. This mantra can be applicable in various scenarios, such as in leadership training, community building, and personal development, highlighting the timeless relevance of these themes in our lives.
