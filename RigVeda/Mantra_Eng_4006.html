सम अश्विनोर अवसां नूतनेन मयोभुवा सुप्रणीति गमेमा |आ नो रयिं वहतम ओत वीरान आ विश्वानि अमृतā सौभगानि ||
sam aśvinor avasā nūtanena mayobhuvā supraṇītī gamema | ā no rayiṁ vahatam ota vīrān ā viśvāny amṛtā saubhagāni ||
  • Translation: The mantra can be translated as: "May we, with the new strength of the Aśvins, attain the well-directed path. May we carry the wealth of the brave and may all beings be blessed with immortality and prosperity."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • sam: together, in harmony
    • aśvinor: of the Aśvins, the twin deities associated with health and medicine
    • avasā: strength, support
    • nūtanena: with newness, freshness
    • mayobhuvā: may we be endowed with strength
    • supraṇītī: well-directed, auspicious path
    • gamema: may we go, may we attain
    • ā no: may we, for us
    • rayiṁ: wealth, prosperity
    • vahatam: carrying, bringing
    • ota: of the brave, the valiant
    • vīrān: heroes, warriors
    • ā: may
    • viśvāny: all beings, all creatures
    • amṛtā: immortal, eternal
    • saubhagāni: auspiciousness, prosperity
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of community and collaboration. For instance, in a workplace, when team members work together harmoniously, they can achieve greater success, much like the Aśvins who symbolize cooperation and support.
    • The call for new strength can be seen in personal development. Individuals often seek new skills or knowledge to enhance their capabilities, akin to the "nūtanena" aspect of the mantra.
    • The idea of carrying wealth and blessings can be related to charitable actions. When individuals or organizations support those in need, they embody the spirit of bringing prosperity to all beings.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for unity, strength, and the pursuit of a prosperous and auspicious life. It encourages individuals to seek new beginnings and to work together for the common good. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as community service, teamwork in professional settings, and personal growth endeavors. By embodying the principles of this mantra, individuals can contribute to a more harmonious and prosperous society.
