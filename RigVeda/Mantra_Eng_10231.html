ममाग्ने वर्चो विहवेष्व अस्तु वयं त्वेन्धानास तन्मं पुषेम |मह्यं नमन्ताम् प्रदिशश चतस्रस् त्वयाध्यक्षेण पृतना जयेम ||
mamāgne varco vihaveṣv astu vayaṁ tvendhānās tanvam puṣema | mahyaṁ namantām pradiśaś catasras tvayādhyakṣeṇa pṛtanā jayema ||
  • Translation: "O Agni, may our radiance be in the forefront; may we, through your guidance, attain strength. With your direction, may we conquer our enemies and achieve victory."
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • mamāgne: O Agni (the divine fire or energy)
    • varco: radiance or brilliance
    • vihaveṣv: may it be in the forefront or may it shine forth
    • astu: let it be
    • vayaṁ: we
    • tvendhānās: through your guidance
    • tanvam: strength or body
    • puṣema: may we attain or may we grow
    • mahyaṁ: for me or for us
    • namantām: may we bow or may we honor
    • pradiśaś: in all directions
    • catasras: four (often referring to the four cardinal directions)
    • tvayādhyakṣeṇa: under your supervision or guidance
    • pṛtanā: may we conquer or may we win
    • jayema: may we achieve victory
  • Contextual Significance:
    • This mantra invokes Agni, the deity representing fire, which symbolizes energy, transformation, and purification.
    • In real life, this can be related to the pursuit of knowledge and enlightenment, where one seeks the light of wisdom to guide their actions.
    • The idea of radiance being in the forefront can be likened to striving for excellence in one’s endeavors, whether in academics, career, or personal growth.
    • Conquering enemies can be interpreted metaphorically as overcoming challenges and obstacles in life, such as fears, doubts, or external competition.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking guidance from a higher power (Agni) to achieve strength and success.
    • It teaches us to honor and respect the forces that guide us, acknowledging that our victories are often a result of collective effort and divine support.
    • In practical terms, this mantra can be applied in daily life by setting clear intentions, seeking guidance in difficult times, and striving for personal and communal growth.
