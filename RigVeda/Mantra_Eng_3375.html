एतद्घेदुत वीर्यं इन्द्र चकर्थ पौंस्यं |स्त्रियं यद दुर्धणायुवं वधीर दुहितरं दिवः ||
etad ghed uta vīrya1m indra cakartha pauṁsyam | striyaṁ yad durhaṇāyuvaṁ vadhīr duhitaraṁ divaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • etad: this
    • ghed: to be known or understood
    • uta: or
    • vīryam: strength or valor
    • indra: Indra, the king of gods
    • cakartha: has accomplished or performed
    • pauṁsyam: of manliness or masculinity
    • striyaṁ: woman or female
    • yad: that which
    • durhaṇāyu: difficult to conquer or hard to attain
    • vadhīr: killer or destroyer
    • duhitaraṁ: daughter
    • divaḥ: of the heavens or divine
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the strength and valor associated with Indra, the king of gods, and how he has accomplished feats of masculinity.
    • It also touches upon the concept of women and the challenges they face, particularly in the context of their relationships and societal roles.
    • The mention of "durhaṇāyu" suggests that there are formidable challenges in life that require strength and resilience to overcome.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern society, the mantra can be related to the empowerment of women, where they strive to overcome societal challenges and assert their strength.
    • For instance, women in leadership roles often face significant obstacles, yet they embody the strength and valor akin to Indra, achieving great accomplishments in their fields.
    • Additionally, the mantra can be applied to personal relationships, where individuals must navigate complex dynamics, requiring both strength and understanding.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its recognition of both masculine and feminine strengths, emphasizing the importance of resilience in the face of challenges.
    • It serves as a reminder that both men and women have roles to play in overcoming difficulties and achieving greatness.
    • In real life, this mantra encourages individuals to embrace their strengths, support one another, and strive for personal and collective growth.
