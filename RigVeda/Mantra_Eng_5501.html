अभि ये मिथो वानुषः सपन्ते रातिं दिवो रातिषाचः पृथिव्याः |अहिर बुद्ध्युत नः श्रिणोतु वरूत्र्य एकधेनुभिर नि पातु ||
abhi ye mitho vanuṣaḥ sapante rātiṁ divo rātiṣācaḥ pṛthivyāḥ | ahir budhnya uta naḥ śṛṇotu varūtry ekadhenubhir ni pātu ||
  • Translation: "May these beings, who are united, bring us joy; may the joy of the heavens and the joy of the earth be ours. May the serpent of the deep hear us; may Varuna protect us with his many gifts."
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • abhi: towards, for
    • ye: those who
    • mitho: together, united
    • vanuṣaḥ: beings, creatures
    • sapante: bring, provide
    • rātiṁ: joy, happiness
    • divo: of the heavens
    • rātiṣācaḥ: and of the earth
    • pṛthivyāḥ: of the earth
    • ahir: serpent, often symbolizing the deep or the unknown
    • budhnya: of the deep, the ocean
    • uta: and, also
    • naḥ: us, our
    • śṛṇotu: may hear, listen
    • varūtry: Varuna, the deity of cosmic order
    • ekadhenubhir: with many gifts
    • ni: down, from
    • pātu: protect, guard
  • Contextual Significance:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of unity and collective joy among beings. In a world often divided by differences, it serves as a reminder that togetherness can lead to shared happiness.
    • The reference to the heavens and the earth signifies the interconnectedness of all aspects of life. Just as the sky and the ground are essential for life, so too is the harmony among people.
    • The invocation of Varuna, a deity associated with cosmic order and moral authority, highlights the need for protection and guidance in our endeavors. It suggests that divine support is crucial for achieving our goals.
  • Real-Life Applications:
    • In community gatherings, such as festivals or family reunions, this mantra can be recited to invoke a sense of unity and shared joy among participants.
    • In workplaces, promoting teamwork and collaboration can be inspired by the essence of this mantra, encouraging employees to work together harmoniously for common goals.
    • In personal relationships, the mantra serves as a reminder to nurture connections and support one another, fostering an environment of love and understanding.
  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of unity, joy, and divine protection. Its significance lies in its ability to inspire individuals and communities to come together, celebrate life, and seek guidance from higher powers. In various real-life scenarios, whether in personal relationships, community events, or professional settings, the principles embodied in this mantra can lead to a more harmonious and fulfilling existence.
