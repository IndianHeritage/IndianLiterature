पुराग्ने दुरितेभ्यः पुरा मृध्रेब्यः कवे | प्राण आयुर वसो तिर ||
purāgne duritebhyaḥ purā mṛdhrebhyaḥ kave | pra ṇa āyur vaso tira ||
  • Mantra: "purāgne duritebhyaḥ purā mṛdhrebhyaḥ kave | pra ṇa āyur vaso tira ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • purāgne: O Agni (the divine fire), who is ancient
    • duritebhyaḥ: from difficulties or misfortunes
    • purā: from the past
    • mṛdhrebhyaḥ: from obstacles or hindrances
    • kave: O wise one or poet
    • pra: may we attain
    • ṇa: life or longevity
    • āyur: life or lifespan
    • vaso: of wealth or prosperity
    • tira: may we cross or overcome
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Agni, the fire god, who symbolizes purity, transformation, and the divine presence in rituals.
    • It seeks protection and guidance from past difficulties and obstacles, emphasizing the importance of overcoming challenges in life.
    • The invocation of "kave" suggests a call for wisdom and insight, highlighting the need for knowledge in navigating life's trials.
    • The mantra expresses a desire for longevity and prosperity, indicating that overcoming difficulties leads to a fruitful and fulfilling life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In personal life, individuals often face challenges such as health issues, financial difficulties, or relationship problems. This mantra serves as a reminder to seek strength and wisdom to overcome these obstacles.
    • In a professional context, employees may encounter setbacks in their careers. The mantra encourages them to learn from past experiences and strive for success with resilience and determination.
    • In community settings, this mantra can inspire collective efforts to address social issues, urging members to work together to overcome challenges and achieve prosperity for all.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its universal message of resilience, wisdom, and the pursuit of a prosperous life.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, reminding individuals and communities to seek guidance and strength in the face of adversity.
    • By invoking the divine presence of Agni, the mantra encourages a transformative approach to life's challenges, fostering growth and well-being.
