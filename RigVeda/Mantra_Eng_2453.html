मन्त्र: न दुरोणे अमृतो मर्याणां राजा ससाद विदथानि साधन | घृतप्रतिका उर्वियā व्य अद्यौद अग्निर विश्वानि काव्याणि विद्वान् ||
ni duroṇe amṛto martyānāṁ rājā sasāda vidathāni sādhan | ghṛtapratīka urviyā vy adyaud agnir viśvāni kāvyāni vidvān ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ni: not
    • duroṇe: from the difficult or hard
    • amṛto: immortal
    • martyānāṁ: of mortals
    • rājā: king
    • sasāda: sat or dwelled
    • vidathāni: in the assemblies or gatherings
    • sādhan: achievements or accomplishments
    • ghṛtapratīka: symbolized by ghee or clarified butter
    • urviyā: with vastness or abundance
    • vy: indeed
    • adyaud: today
    • agnir: fire
    • viśvāni: all or universal
    • kāvyāni: poetic or artistic
    • vidvān: the wise or knowledgeable
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the relationship between the mortal and the immortal, emphasizing that even in the face of difficulties, the essence of life (immortality) can be realized.
    • The "king" represents leadership and authority, suggesting that true leadership is found in gatherings where achievements are celebrated.
    • The mention of "ghee" symbolizes nourishment and purity, indicating that the essence of life is enriched by wisdom and creativity.
    • The reference to "fire" signifies transformation and energy, which are essential for growth and enlightenment.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, a leader who fosters collaboration and celebrates team achievements embodies the essence of this mantra. They create an environment where creativity and wisdom thrive.
    • In personal life, individuals who face challenges but seek knowledge and wisdom can transform their experiences into opportunities for growth, much like the fire that transforms raw materials into something valuable.
    • In community gatherings, when people come together to share their experiences and achievements, they create a space where the immortal essence of human connection and creativity is celebrated.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of overcoming difficulties through wisdom, creativity, and collaboration.
    • It teaches us that true leadership and achievement are found in unity and shared experiences, which can lead to personal and collective growth.
    • In real life, applying the principles of this mantra can lead to a more fulfilling and enriched existence, where challenges are met with resilience and creativity.
