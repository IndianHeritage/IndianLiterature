आ नो द्युम्नैर आ श्रवोभिर आ रायाऽयातम अश्विनाः |पुरुश्चन्द्रा नासत्याः ||
ā no dyumnair ā śravobhir ā rāyā yātam aśvinā | puruścandrā nāsatyā ||
  • Mantra: "ā no dyumnair ā śravobhir ā rāyā yātam aśvinā | puruścandrā nāsatyā ||"
  • Translation: "May we be blessed with brilliance and fame, may we attain prosperity through the Aśvins, the divine twin horsemen; may we be like the moon among men, and may we not be deceived."
  • Word-by-Word Explanation:
    • ā no: "May we" - a call for blessings and positive outcomes.
    • dyumnair: "with brilliance" - refers to radiance, intelligence, and enlightenment.
    • ā śravobhir: "with fame" - signifies the desire for recognition and respect in society.
    • ā rāyā: "with prosperity" - indicates a wish for wealth and abundance.
    • yātam: "may we attain" - a hopeful expression of achieving the desired qualities.
    • aśvinā: "through the Aśvins" - invoking the divine twins known for their healing and benevolence.
    • puruścandrā: "like the moon among men" - a metaphor for being distinguished and admired.
    • nāsatyā: "may we not be deceived" - a plea for clarity and truthfulness in life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, individuals often seek recognition for their hard work. This mantra encourages striving for brilliance and fame through dedication and integrity.
    • In personal relationships, being like the moon signifies the importance of being a source of light and positivity for others, fostering admiration and respect.
    • In times of uncertainty, the plea to not be deceived resonates with the need for discernment and wisdom in decision-making, whether in business or personal choices.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its holistic approach to life, emphasizing the pursuit of brilliance, fame, and prosperity while maintaining integrity and clarity. It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, such as career aspirations, personal relationships, and ethical decision-making. By invoking the blessings of the Aśvins, individuals are reminded to strive for excellence and truthfulness, ultimately leading to a fulfilling and respected life.
