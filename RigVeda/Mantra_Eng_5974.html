प्रति चक्ष्वा वि चक्ष्वेन्द्रश्च सोम जागृतम् |रक्षोभ्यो वधम अस्यतम अशनिं यातुमद्भ्यः ||
prati cakṣva vi cakṣvendraś ca soma jāgṛtam | rakṣobhyo vadham asyatam aśaniṁ yātumadbhyaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • prati: towards, in the direction of
    • cakṣva: to see, to perceive
    • vi: apart, separately
    • cakṣvendraś: the lord of vision, referring to the divine or higher perception
    • ca: and
    • soma: the moon, often symbolizing nourishment and tranquility
    • jāgṛtam: awakened, alert
    • rakṣobhyo: from the protectors, guardians
    • vadham: destruction, harm
    • asyatam: the most powerful
    • aśaniṁ: lightning, symbolizing power and energy
    • yātumadbhyaḥ: to those who are in need, to those who seek
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra calls for a heightened perception and awareness, urging individuals to look towards the divine and the nourishing aspects of life.
    • The mention of "soma" signifies the importance of tranquility and nourishment, which can be related to how we seek peace in our daily lives through meditation or nature.
    • The phrase "rakṣobhyo vadham asyatam" emphasizes the need for protection from harm, which can be seen in real-life scenarios where individuals seek safety and security in their environments.
    • The reference to "aśaniṁ" suggests the harnessing of energy and power, akin to how we channel our inner strength to overcome challenges.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a stressful work environment, one might meditate or practice mindfulness to achieve a state of "jāgṛtam," or alertness, allowing for better decision-making.
    • During times of crisis, communities often come together to protect one another, embodying the essence of "rakṣobhyo" as they seek to safeguard their members.
    • Individuals may engage in physical activities or sports to channel their "aśaniṁ," using their energy to achieve personal goals and overcome obstacles.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of awareness, protection, and harnessing one's inner strength.
    • Its significance lies in its applicability to everyday life, encouraging individuals to seek clarity, safety, and empowerment in their actions.
    • By embodying the principles of this mantra, one can navigate life's challenges with a sense of purpose and resilience.
