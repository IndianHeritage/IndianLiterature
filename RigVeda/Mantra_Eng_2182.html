अमाजूर इव पित्रोः सचा सती समानाद आ सदसस त्वाम इये भागम् |कृधि प्रकेतम् उप मास्य आ भर दद्धि भागं तन्वो येन मामहः ||
amājūr iva pitroḥ sacā satī samānād ā sadasas tvām iye bhagam | kṛdhi praketam upa māsy ā bhara daddhi bhāgaṁ tanvo3 yena māmahaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • amājūr: Unharmed, unscathed
    • iva: Like, as if
    • pitroḥ: Of the ancestors, of the forefathers
    • sacā: And with, along with
    • satī: The virtuous, the true
    • samānād: From the same source, equally
    • ā: To, towards
    • sadasas: Of the assembly, of the gathering
    • tvām: You
    • iye: To come, to approach
    • bhagam: Wealth, fortune, prosperity
    • kṛdhi: Make, create
    • praketam: With a clear mind, with wisdom
    • upa: Near, close to
    • māsy: May we be, let us be
    • ā: To, towards
    • bhara: Fill, bestow
    • daddhi: Give, grant
    • bhāgaṁ: Share, portion
    • tanvoḥ: Of the body, of the being
    • yena: By which, through which
    • māmahaḥ: We may attain, we may achieve
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the blessings of the ancestors and the divine for protection and prosperity.
    • It emphasizes the importance of being unharmed and virtuous, drawing strength from the lineage and community.
    • The call to create wealth and wisdom suggests a proactive approach to life, encouraging individuals to seek fortune through righteous means.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a family business, the mantra can be a reminder to honor the values and teachings of previous generations while striving for success.
    • In personal relationships, it can signify the importance of mutual respect and support, drawing on shared values to navigate challenges.
    • In community service, it can inspire individuals to contribute positively, ensuring that their actions benefit not just themselves but also the collective.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the interconnectedness of individuals with their ancestors and community.
    • Its significance lies in the call for protection, prosperity, and wisdom, urging individuals to act with integrity and purpose.
    • In real-life scenarios, it encourages a holistic approach to success, where personal achievements are aligned with the well-being of others.
