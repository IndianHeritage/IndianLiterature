जोहूत्रो अग्निः प्रथमः पितेवेलास पदे मनुषा यत समिद्धः |श्रियं वसानो अमृतो विचेतामर्मृजेन्यः श्रवस्यः स वाजी ||
johūtro agniḥ prathamaḥ piteveḻas pade manuṣā yat samiddhaḥ | śriyaṁ vasāno amṛto vicetā marmṛjenyaḥ śravasya1ḥ sa vājī ||
  • johūtro agniḥ: This phrase refers to the "first fire" or "Agni," which is a significant deity in Vedic literature. Fire symbolizes purity, transformation, and the divine presence in rituals.
  • prathamaḥ piteveḻas pade: This translates to "the first in the abode of the father." It signifies the importance of Agni as a central figure in familial and societal rituals, representing the connection between the divine and the earthly.
  • manuṣā yat samiddhaḥ: This means "when the fire is kindled by humans." It emphasizes the role of human agency in invoking divine presence through rituals, highlighting the relationship between humans and the divine.
  • śriyaṁ vasāno: This translates to "dwelling in prosperity." It suggests that the presence of Agni brings wealth, abundance, and well-being to individuals and communities.
  • amṛto vicetā: This means "the immortal seer." It refers to the eternal wisdom and knowledge that Agni embodies, guiding humans towards enlightenment and understanding.
  • marmṛjenyaḥ: This term can be interpreted as "the one who knows the essence." It signifies the importance of understanding the deeper truths of existence, which Agni represents.
  • śravasyaḥ sa vājī: This translates to "he who is the source of fame." It indicates that Agni is not only a source of material wealth but also of spiritual and social recognition.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of Agni as a divine force that connects humans with the spiritual realm. It highlights the importance of rituals in invoking prosperity, wisdom, and recognition. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as during family ceremonies, community gatherings, or personal reflections, where individuals seek to connect with the divine for guidance and blessings. The essence of this mantra encourages individuals to recognize their role in the universe and the importance of their actions in shaping their destiny.
