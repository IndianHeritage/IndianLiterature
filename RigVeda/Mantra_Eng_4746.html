यस्य गा अन्तर अश्मनो मदे दृढा आवसृजः |अयं स सोम इन्द्र ते सुतः पिब ||
yasya gā antar aśmano made dṛḻhā avāsṛjaḥ | ayaṁ sa soma indra te sutaḥ piba ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yasya: of whom
    • gā: the song or hymn
    • antar: within
    • aśmano: of the stones (often referring to the earth or the physical realm)
    • made: in the intoxication or ecstasy
    • dṛḻhā: firm or strong
    • avāsṛjaḥ: released or let go
    • ayaṁ: this
    • sa: he or that
    • soma: the divine nectar or drink, often associated with immortality
    • indra: the king of gods, representing strength and power
    • te: your
    • sutaḥ: offspring or son
    • piba: drink
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of a divine connection and the act of drinking the soma, which symbolizes the essence of life and vitality.
    • The phrase "yasya gā antar aśmano made dṛḻhā avāsṛjaḥ" suggests that within the firm and strong essence of the earth, there is a song or hymn that is released, indicating a deep connection between nature and the divine.
    • The invocation of Indra, the king of gods, emphasizes the importance of strength and power in our lives, suggesting that we should seek divine support in our endeavors.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In moments of celebration, such as weddings or festivals, people often partake in rituals that involve offerings and prayers, similar to the act of drinking soma, to invoke blessings and strength from the divine.
    • In nature, when we experience the beauty of a sunrise or the sound of a river, it can evoke a sense of ecstasy and connection to the earth, akin to the "song" mentioned in the mantra.
    • In personal challenges, seeking strength through meditation or prayer can be seen as a modern-day equivalent of invoking Indra's power to overcome obstacles.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of connecting with the divine and nature to gain strength and vitality in our lives.
    • It teaches us to appreciate the beauty and power of the natural world while recognizing the importance of spiritual nourishment.
    • In real-life scenarios, it encourages us to seek divine guidance and strength in our personal and communal endeavors, fostering a sense of unity and purpose.
