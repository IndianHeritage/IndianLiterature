यā ओषधिः पूर्वा जातā देवभ्यस त्रियुगं पुरा |मनै नु बभ्रूणाम अहं शतं धामानि सप्त च ||
yā oṣadhīḥ pūrvā jātā devebhyas triyugam purā | manai nu babhrūṇām ahaṁ śataṁ dhāmāni sapta ca ||
  • yā oṣadhīḥ pūrvā jātā devebhyas triyugam purā: This line refers to the ancient herbs or plants that were created for the deities in the past. The term "oṣadhīḥ" signifies medicinal plants or herbs, while "pūrvā" indicates their origin or ancient nature. "Devebhyas" means 'for the gods', and "triyugam purā" suggests that these herbs were known and utilized in the three ages (Yugas) of time.
  • manai nu babhrūṇām ahaṁ śataṁ dhāmāni sapta ca: This part speaks to the speaker's connection to the divine and the abundance of blessings. "Manai" refers to the mind or intellect, "babhrūṇām" means 'of the wise', and "ahaṁ" translates to 'I'. "Śataṁ dhāmāni" indicates 'hundreds of abodes' or 'places of wealth', while "sapta ca" means 'and seven', which can symbolize completeness or fullness.

  • Real-Life Example: The reference to ancient herbs can be likened to the modern resurgence of herbal medicine and natural remedies. For instance, turmeric, which has been used for centuries in Ayurvedic medicine, is now recognized globally for its anti-inflammatory properties. This reflects the timeless wisdom of utilizing nature for healing, as mentioned in the mantra.
  • Significance: The mantra emphasizes the importance of nature and its resources, particularly in relation to spirituality and health. It suggests that the wisdom of the past can guide present and future practices, especially in holistic health and wellness.
  • Application in Real Life: In contemporary society, this mantra can inspire individuals to seek natural remedies and to respect the environment. It encourages a lifestyle that harmonizes with nature, promoting sustainability and the use of traditional knowledge in modern health practices.

  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of nature, spirituality, and human well-being. It highlights the significance of ancient wisdom in contemporary life, urging us to honor and utilize the gifts of nature for our health and spiritual growth. By embracing these teachings, we can cultivate a more balanced and harmonious existence.
