जुहुरे वि चितयन्तोऽनिमिषं नृम्णं पान्ति |आ दृढां पुरं विविशुः ||
juhure vi citayanto 'nimiṣaṁ nṛmṇam pānti | ā dṛḻhām puraṁ viviśuḥ ||
  • Mantra: "juhure vi citayanto 'nimiṣaṁ nṛmṇam pānti | ā dṛḻhām puraṁ viviśuḥ ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • juhure: They offer or invoke.
    • vi: Indeed or truly.
    • citayanto: By meditating or contemplating.
    • 'nimiṣaṁ: In a moment or blink of an eye.
    • nṛmṇam: The human or mankind.
    • pānti: They protect or sustain.
    • ā: From or towards.
    • dṛḻhām: Firm or strong.
    • puraṁ: The city or abode.
    • viviśuḥ: They entered or penetrated.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the act of offering and invoking divine energies through meditation.
    • The phrase "in a moment" suggests the immediacy and power of these spiritual practices.
    • It emphasizes the protection and sustenance provided to humanity by these divine forces.
    • The "firm abode" refers to a stable and secure environment, which can be interpreted as both physical and spiritual safety.
    • The act of entering the "city" symbolizes the integration of divine wisdom into our lives.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern life, meditation practices such as mindfulness or yoga can be seen as contemporary forms of "juhure vi citayanto," where individuals invoke peace and clarity.
    • Community gatherings for prayer or collective meditation can be viewed as a way of sustaining and protecting the collective spirit of humanity.
    • In times of crisis, people often turn to spiritual practices to find strength and stability, reflecting the "firm abode" that the mantra speaks of.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of spiritual practices in providing protection and sustenance to humanity.
    • It encourages individuals to engage in meditation and contemplation, fostering a sense of community and shared strength.
    • In real-life scenarios, the principles of this mantra can be applied to enhance personal well-being and collective harmony, making it relevant in today's fast-paced world.
