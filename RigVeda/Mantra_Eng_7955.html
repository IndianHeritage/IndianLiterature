आ नः पवस्व धारया पवमान रयिम पृथुम् |यया ज्योतिर्विदासि नः ||
ā naḥ pavasva dhārayā pavamāna rayim pṛthum | yayā jyotir vidāsi naḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā naḥ: "O, our" - a call to a divine entity, indicating possession or a plea for assistance.
    • pavasva: "may you purify" - a request for purification, both physically and spiritually.
    • dhārayā: "by holding" - signifies the act of sustaining or supporting.
    • pavamāna: "the purifying one" - refers to a divine force or energy that cleanses and elevates.
    • rayim: "wealth" or "prosperity" - denotes material and spiritual abundance.
    • pṛthum: "broad" or "vast" - suggests expansiveness, indicating a wide-reaching impact.
    • yayā: "by which" - introduces a means or method.
    • jyotir: "light" - symbolizes knowledge, enlightenment, and clarity.
    • vidāsi: "you grant" - indicates the act of bestowing or providing.
    • naḥ: "to us" - again emphasizes the collective plea or request.
  • Overall Meaning: This mantra is a prayer for purification and sustenance from a divine source. It seeks the blessings of prosperity and enlightenment, asking for the divine to grant clarity and abundance in life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, a team may recite this mantra before a major project, seeking clarity and success in their endeavors.
    • Individuals may use this mantra during meditation to focus on personal growth, seeking to purify their thoughts and attract positive energy.
    • In community gatherings, this mantra can be invoked to foster unity and collective prosperity, emphasizing the importance of shared goals and enlightenment.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine assistance in achieving purity, prosperity, and enlightenment. It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal development to collective endeavors, reminding us of the importance of seeking higher guidance and clarity in our pursuits. By invoking this mantra, individuals and communities can align themselves with positive energies, fostering an environment conducive to growth and success.
