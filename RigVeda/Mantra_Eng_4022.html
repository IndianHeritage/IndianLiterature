विदा दिवो विष्यन्न अद्रिम उक्थैर आयत्याः उषसो आर्चिनो गुः |अपावृत व्रजिनीः उत स्वर गाद विडुरो मानवशीः देव आवः ||
vidā divo viṣyann adrim ukthair āyatyā uṣaso arcino guḥ | apāvṛta vrajinīr ut svar gād vi duro mānuṣīr deva āvaḥ ||
  • vidā divo: This phrase translates to "knowing the heavens" or "the knowledge of the sky." It signifies the pursuit of higher knowledge and understanding of the universe.
  • viṣyann adrim: This means "the one who spreads out the mountains." It can be interpreted as the force that shapes the earth and creates the landscape, symbolizing strength and stability.
  • ukthair āyatyā: This translates to "with the rays of light." It refers to the illumination and enlightenment that comes from knowledge and wisdom, akin to the dawn breaking after a long night.
  • uṣaso arcino guḥ: This means "the hidden light of the dawn." It emphasizes the idea that true understanding often lies beneath the surface, waiting to be discovered.
  • apāvṛta vrajinīr: This translates to "the one who removes obstacles." It signifies the divine force that helps individuals overcome challenges and barriers in their lives.
  • ut svar gād: This means "to the shining light." It represents the aspiration towards enlightenment and the pursuit of truth.
  • vi duro mānuṣīr: This translates to "the divine that is beyond human." It suggests that there are forces in the universe that transcend human understanding and capabilities.
  • deva āvaḥ: This means "O divine, come to us." It is a call for divine presence and guidance in our lives.

Conclusion: This mantra from the RigVeda encapsulates the essence of seeking knowledge, overcoming obstacles, and aspiring towards enlightenment. In real life, it can be applied in various scenarios such as education, personal growth, and spiritual development. For instance, when facing challenges in life, one can invoke the spirit of this mantra to seek guidance and strength. It encourages individuals to look beyond the surface and strive for deeper understanding, reminding us that the pursuit of knowledge and truth is a divine journey that enriches our lives.
