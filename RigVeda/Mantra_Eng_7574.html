मन्त्र: मन्ये त्वा यज्ञियं यज्ञियानाम् मन्ये त्वा च्यवनम् अच्युतानाम् |मन्ये त्वा सत्वनाम् इन्द्र केतुम् मन्ये त्वा वृषभं चर्षणीनाम् ||
manye tvā yajñiyaṁ yajñiyānām manye tvā cyavanam acyutānām | manye tvā satvanām indra ketum manye tvā vṛṣabhaṁ carṣaṇīnām ||
  • manye tvā yajñiyaṁ yajñiyānām: This phrase translates to "I consider you, O Indra, as the most worthy of all offerings." Here, Indra is recognized as the king of the gods and the deity of rain and thunderstorms. In real life, this can be likened to how we honor and respect leaders or figures of authority in our communities, acknowledging their contributions and seeking their blessings for prosperity.
  • manye tvā cyavanam acyutānām: This means "I consider you, O Indra, as the one who is never defeated." This highlights the idea of resilience and strength. In everyday life, this can be seen in individuals who overcome challenges and adversities, embodying the spirit of perseverance. For example, a student who fails an exam but continues to study hard and eventually succeeds reflects this quality.
  • manye tvā satvanām indra ketum: This translates to "I consider you, O Indra, as the banner of the strong." This signifies leadership and guidance. In a corporate setting, a strong leader who inspires their team to achieve goals can be seen as a 'banner' under which the team rallies. This can also apply to community leaders who guide their people through difficult times.
  • manye tvā vṛṣabhaṁ carṣaṇīnām: This means "I consider you, O Indra, as the bull among the strong." The bull symbolizes strength and power. In real life, this can be related to individuals or entities that are seen as powerful forces in their respective fields, such as a successful entrepreneur who drives innovation and economic growth.

  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the qualities of strength, resilience, leadership, and the importance of honoring those who embody these traits. In our daily lives, we can apply the teachings of this mantra by recognizing and respecting the leaders and strong figures around us, striving to embody resilience in the face of challenges, and supporting those who guide us towards success. It serves as a reminder to appreciate the divine qualities in ourselves and others, fostering a sense of community and strength.
