मन्त्र: स्तोता यत ते अनुव्रत उक्तानि ऋतुथा दधे |शुचिः पावक उच्यते सो अद्भुतः ||
stotā yat te anuvrata ukthāny ṛtuthā dadhe | śuciḥ pāvaka ucyate so adbhutaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • stotā: The one who praises or worships.
    • yat: That which.
    • te: Your.
    • anuvrata: Following or adhering to.
    • ukthāny: The hymns or praises.
    • ṛtuthā: In accordance with truth or righteousness.
    • dadhe: Establishes or places.
    • śuciḥ: Pure or clean.
    • pāvaka: Sacred or purifying.
    • ucyate: Is called or is known as.
    • so: He or that.
    • adbhutaḥ: Wonderful or extraordinary.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the power of praise and devotion.
    • It emphasizes the importance of following a righteous path and adhering to truth.
    • The one who praises (stotā) is recognized for their devotion and commitment (anuvrata) to the divine.
    • The hymns (ukthāny) that are sung in praise are established in truth (ṛtuthā), highlighting the significance of sincerity in worship.
    • The mantra also mentions purity (śuciḥ) and the sacredness (pāvaka) of the divine, suggesting that true devotion leads to a state of purity.
    • Ultimately, the one who embodies these qualities is considered extraordinary (adbhutaḥ).
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, individuals who practice gratitude and express appreciation towards others often foster positive relationships and create a harmonious environment.
    • In a workplace setting, employees who adhere to ethical practices and support their colleagues contribute to a culture of trust and collaboration.
    • Spiritual practices such as meditation and prayer can lead to a sense of inner peace and clarity, aligning with the idea of purity and sacredness.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the significance of devotion, truth, and purity in our lives.
    • It encourages individuals to engage in practices that promote righteousness and to express gratitude, leading to personal and communal well-being.
    • By embodying these principles, one can experience a transformative journey, becoming a source of inspiration and positivity in the world.
