द्यौः न य इन्द्राभि भूमार्यस् तस्थौ रयिः शवसां पृत्सु जनान् |तं नः सहस्रभरम् उर्वरासां दद्धि सूनो सहसो वृत्रतुरम् ||
dyaur na ya indrābhi bhūmāryas tasthau rayiḥ śavasā pṛtsu janān | taṁ naḥ sahasrabharam urvarāsāṁ daddhi sūno sahaso vṛtraturam ||
  • Mantra: "dyaur na ya indrābhi bhūmāryas tasthau rayiḥ śavasā pṛtsu janān | taṁ naḥ sahasrabharam urvarāsāṁ daddhi sūno sahaso vṛtraturam ||"
  • Translation: "May the heavens, which Indra has established, grant us wealth and strength among the people. May that which is rich and abundant, like the earth, bestow upon us the strength to overcome obstacles."
  • Word-by-Word Explanation:
    • dyaur: heavens or sky
    • na: not
    • ya: who
    • indrābhi: by Indra, the king of gods
    • bhūmāryas: established on earth
    • tasthau: has stood or remained
    • rayiḥ: wealth or prosperity
    • śavasā: with strength
    • pṛtsu: among the people
    • janān: people or beings
    • taṁ: that
    • naḥ: for us
    • sahasrabharam: abundant or plentiful
    • urvarāsāṁ: like the earth
    • daddhi: may grant or bestow
    • sūno: strength or power
    • sahaso: with vigor
    • vṛtraturam: the one who overcomes obstacles
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be interpreted as a call for prosperity and success. For instance, a startup seeking funding may recite this mantra to invoke the blessings of abundance and strength in overcoming challenges.
    • In personal life, individuals facing difficulties may use this mantra as a source of motivation, reminding themselves to seek strength and support from the universe to overcome their obstacles.
    • In community gatherings, this mantra can be recited to foster unity and collective strength among members, encouraging them to work together for common goals.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its invocation of divine support for wealth, strength, and the ability to overcome challenges. It emphasizes the importance of seeking blessings from higher powers, whether in personal endeavors, community efforts, or business pursuits. By reciting this mantra, individuals and groups can cultivate a mindset of abundance and resilience, making it applicable in various real-life scenarios where strength and prosperity are desired.
