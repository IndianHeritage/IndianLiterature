इन्दुं रिहन्ति महिषा अदब्धाः पदे रेब्हन्ति कवयो न गृध्राः |हिन्वन्ति धीरा दशभिः क्षिपाभिः सम अञ्जते रूपम् अपां रसना ||
induṁ rihanti mahiṣā adabdhāḥ pade rebhanti kavayo na gṛdhrāḥ | hinvanti dhīrā daśabhiḥ kṣipābhiḥ sam añjate rūpam apāṁ rasena ||
  • Induṁ rihanti mahiṣā adabdhāḥ: This phrase can be translated to "The powerful ones (mahiṣā) who are unyielding (adabdhāḥ) enjoy the moon (induṁ)." Here, "induṁ" refers to the moon, which symbolizes nourishment and abundance. The term "mahiṣā" can be interpreted as those who are strong or influential, while "adabdhāḥ" signifies resilience and steadfastness.
  • Pade rebhanti kavayo na gṛdhrāḥ: This translates to "The wise (kavayo) do not act like vultures (gṛdhrāḥ) but instead move forward (rebhanti) on the path (pade)." This suggests that true wisdom involves taking the right path and not resorting to scavenging or opportunistic behavior, as vultures do.
  • Hinavanti dhīrā daśabhiḥ kṣipābhiḥ: This means "The courageous (dhīrā) propel (hinvanti) with ten (daśabhiḥ) swift (kṣipābhiḥ) actions." It emphasizes the importance of decisive and bold actions taken by those who are wise and courageous.
  • Sam añjate rūpam apāṁ rasena: This can be interpreted as "They (sam) unite (añjate) the form (rūpam) of the waters (apāṁ) with essence (rasena)." This suggests a harmonious blending of elements, indicating that wisdom and courage lead to the creation of something beautiful and essential.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of wisdom, strength, and the importance of taking the right path in life. It teaches us that true power lies in resilience and the ability to act wisely rather than opportunistically. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various contexts, such as leadership, decision-making, and personal growth. For instance, a leader who remains steadfast in their values and makes thoughtful decisions, rather than acting out of desperation or greed, embodies the principles of this mantra. Ultimately, it signifies that through courage and wisdom, one can create a meaningful and harmonious existence.
