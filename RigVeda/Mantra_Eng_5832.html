परी स्पर्शो वरुणस्य स्मदिष्टा उभे पश्यन्ति रोडसी सुमेके |ऋतावानः कवयो यज्ञधीराḥ प्रचेतसो य इषयन्त मनमा ||
pari spaśo varuṇasya smadiṣṭā ubhe paśyanti rodasī sumeke | ṛtāvānaḥ kavayo yajñadhīrāḥ pracetaso ya iṣayanta manma ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • pari: around, encompassing
    • spaśo: touch, contact
    • varuṇasya: of Varuna, the god of cosmic order and water
    • smadiṣṭā: those who are wise or knowledgeable
    • ubhe: both
    • paśyanti: see, perceive
    • rodasī: the two realms, often interpreted as the sky and the earth
    • sumeke: in harmony, well-ordered
    • ṛtāvānaḥ: those who are established in truth
    • kavayo: the wise, the poets
    • yajñadhīrāḥ: those who are devoted to sacrifice or worship
    • pracetaso: with a clear mind, intelligent
    • ya: who
    • iṣayanta: control, govern
    • manma: my mind, thoughts
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the omnipresence of Varuna, who represents the cosmic order and the moral law.
    • It emphasizes the importance of wisdom and perception, suggesting that those who are knowledgeable can see the harmony between the realms of existence.
    • The phrase "ṛtāvānaḥ kavayo" highlights the role of wise individuals who are grounded in truth and engage in acts of devotion.
    • It suggests that a clear mind and understanding can lead to the governance of one's thoughts and actions.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, leaders who embody the qualities of "ṛtāvānaḥ" can create a culture of integrity and transparency, guiding their teams towards ethical practices.
    • In personal relationships, individuals who strive for understanding and harmony, akin to "sumeke," can foster deeper connections and resolve conflicts effectively.
    • In environmental conservation, recognizing the interconnectedness of all life, as suggested by "ubhe rodasī," can inspire collective action towards sustainability.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of wisdom, truth, and harmony in our lives.
    • It encourages individuals to cultivate a clear mind and to engage in practices that promote understanding and ethical behavior.
    • By embodying these principles, we can navigate the complexities of life with greater awareness and purpose, ultimately contributing to a more harmonious society.
