नū चिन् न इन्द्रो मगवाः सहूतī दानो वाजं न यमते न ऊतī |अनूनां यस्य दक्षिणा पीपाय वामं नृभ्यो अभिवीताः सखिभ्यः ||
nū cin na indro maghavā sahūtī dāno vājaṁ ni yamate na ūtī | anūnā yasya dakṣiṇā pīpāya vāmaṁ nṛbhyo abhivītā sakhibhyaḥ ||
  • Translation: The mantra can be translated as: "May Indra, the powerful one, grant us strength and wealth; may he not withhold his gifts from us. May we be protected from those who wish us harm, and may we befriend those who are our allies."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • nū: A particle expressing a wish or desire.
    • cin: To be aware or to know.
    • na: Not; a negation.
    • indro: Referring to Indra, the king of gods in Vedic tradition, symbolizing power and strength.
    • maghavā: The mighty or powerful one.
    • sahūtī: Granting or bestowing.
    • dāno: Gifts or wealth.
    • vājaṁ: Strength or vigor.
    • ni yamate: Not to withhold or restrain.
    • na ūtī: Not to be denied.
    • anūnā: Unharmed or unscathed.
    • yasya: Whose or of whom.
    • dakṣiṇā: Right or favorable; often associated with blessings.
    • pīpāya: To protect or to nourish.
    • vāmaṁ: Left or unfavorable; often associated with danger.
    • nṛbhyo: From men or humans.
    • abhivītā: Being afraid or fearful.
    • sakhibhyaḥ: To friends or allies.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, this mantra can be invoked by teams seeking success in their projects. They may wish for the strength to overcome challenges and the support of their colleagues.
    • In personal relationships, individuals may use this mantra to foster goodwill and protection among friends, ensuring that they are surrounded by supportive and trustworthy people.
    • During times of conflict or competition, such as in sports or business, this mantra serves as a reminder to seek strength and not to be deterred by adversaries.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for strength, protection, and the fostering of positive relationships. It emphasizes the importance of community and support in overcoming challenges. In real life, it can be applied in various scenarios, from personal endeavors to professional collaborations, reminding individuals to seek strength and allyship while remaining resilient against negativity. By invoking this mantra, one can cultivate a mindset of empowerment and unity, essential for achieving success and well-being in any aspect of life.
