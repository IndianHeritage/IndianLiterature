उज जाताम् परशुर ज्योतिषा सह भूयाऽऋतस्य सुदुघा पुराणवत् |विरोचताम् अरुषो भानुना शुचिः स्वर्ण न शुक्रं शुशुचिता सत्पतिः ||
uj jāyatām paraśur jyotiṣā saha bhūyā ṛtasya sudughā purāṇavat | vi rocatām aruṣo bhānunā śuciḥ sva1r ṇa śukraṁ śuśucīta satpatiḥ ||
  • uj jāyatām: This phrase can be translated as "may we be born" or "may we arise." It signifies the desire for new beginnings and the emergence of positive qualities in life.
  • paraśur: This word means "with the axe" or "with strength." It symbolizes the power to cut through obstacles and challenges that one may face.
  • jyotiṣā: Translated as "with light" or "with illumination," it represents knowledge, wisdom, and enlightenment that guide us through life.
  • saha bhūyā: This means "together" or "in unity." It emphasizes the importance of collaboration and community in achieving goals.
  • ṛtasya: This word refers to "truth" or "cosmic order." It signifies the fundamental principles that govern the universe and our lives.
  • sudughā purāṇavat: This phrase can be interpreted as "the well-guarded ancient wisdom." It suggests that the knowledge we seek is preserved through time and is valuable for our growth.
  • vi rocatām: This means "may it shine forth" or "may it be revealed." It expresses the hope for clarity and understanding in our lives.
  • aruṣo bhānunā: Translated as "with the red sun," it symbolizes vitality, energy, and the life-giving force of the sun.
  • śuciḥ: This word means "pure" or "clean." It represents the importance of purity in thoughts, actions, and intentions.
  • sva1r ṇa: This can be interpreted as "in our own nature" or "in our essence." It emphasizes self-awareness and understanding one's true self.
  • śukraṁ: This word means "bright" or "radiant." It signifies the pursuit of excellence and brilliance in our endeavors.
  • śuśucīta: This means "to be well-pleased" or "to rejoice." It reflects the joy that comes from achieving one's goals and living in harmony.
  • satpatiḥ: Translated as "the true lord" or "the righteous ruler," it signifies the ideal leader or guiding force in our lives.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a profound message about the journey of life, emphasizing the importance of unity, truth, and enlightenment. It encourages individuals to seek knowledge, embrace purity, and strive for excellence while overcoming obstacles. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as personal development, leadership, and community building. By embodying the principles outlined in this mantra, individuals can foster a harmonious and prosperous environment, both for themselves and for those around them.
