अभि नो वाजसातमं रयिमर्ष पुरुस्पृहं |इन्दो सहस्रभार्णसं तुविद्युम्नं विभ्वासहम् ||
abhi no vājasātamaṁ rayim arṣa puruspṛham | indo sahasrabharṇasaṁ tuvidyumnaṁ vibhvāsaham ||
  • abhi no vājasātamaṁ: This phrase can be translated as "May we attain the best of strength." The word "vājasātamaṁ" refers to the highest or best strength, often associated with vitality and energy.
  • rayim arṣa puruspṛham: Here, "rayim" means wealth or prosperity, while "arṣa" signifies something that is elevated or exalted. "Puruspṛham" refers to the desire for abundance. Together, this line expresses a yearning for elevated prosperity and abundance.
  • indo sahasrabharṇasaṁ: "Indo" refers to the moon or a divine being, often associated with nourishment and fertility. "Sahasrabharṇa" means "having a thousand rays," symbolizing brilliance and illumination. This part of the mantra invokes the divine for enlightenment and nourishment.
  • tuvidyumnaṁ vibhvāsaham: "Tuvidyumnaṁ" can be interpreted as "one who is victorious" or "one who has achieved success." "Vibhvāsaham" means "one who shines brightly." This line emphasizes the importance of success and radiance in one's endeavors.

  • Significance: This mantra encapsulates a prayer for strength, prosperity, enlightenment, and success. It reflects the holistic approach of the RigVeda, where physical, spiritual, and material well-being are interconnected.
  • Real-life Application: In modern life, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as:
    • Personal Development: Individuals can recite this mantra as a daily affirmation to cultivate strength and resilience in facing challenges.
    • Business and Career: Entrepreneurs and professionals may use this mantra to invoke prosperity and success in their ventures, reminding themselves of their goals and aspirations.
    • Spiritual Growth: Practitioners of yoga and meditation can incorporate this mantra into their routines to seek enlightenment and clarity in their spiritual journeys.

  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the interconnectedness of strength, prosperity, and success. By invoking these qualities, individuals can strive for a balanced and fulfilling life, aligning their actions with their aspirations. It encourages a proactive approach to personal and professional growth, fostering a mindset of abundance and achievement.
