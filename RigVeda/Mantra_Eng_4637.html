न यं जरण्ति शरदो न मासा न द्यावा इन्द्रं अवकर्षयन्ति |वृद्धस्य चिद् वर्धताम् अस्य तनूः स्तोमेभिरुक्तैश्च शस्यमानाः ||
na yaṁ jaranti śarado na māsā na dyāva indram avakarśayanti | vṛddhasya cid vardhatām asya tanūḥ stomebhir ukthaiś ca śasyamānā ||
  • na yaṁ jaranti śarado: This phrase translates to "that which does not age with the seasons." It signifies the eternal nature of certain aspects of life, such as wisdom, knowledge, and the soul. In real life, this can be related to the pursuit of knowledge and personal growth, which remain relevant regardless of the passage of time.
  • na māsā na dyāva indram avakarśayanti: This means "neither the months nor the heavens can diminish it." It emphasizes the idea that true value and essence cannot be affected by time or external circumstances. For example, a person's character or integrity remains intact despite the challenges they face over time.
  • vṛddhasya cid vardhatām asya tanūḥ: This translates to "may the body of the aged grow in strength." It reflects the desire for vitality and strength in old age. In a real-life context, this can be seen in the importance of health and wellness programs for the elderly, promoting physical activity and mental engagement to enhance their quality of life.
  • stomebhir ukthaiś ca śasyamānā: This means "may it be praised with hymns and offerings." It highlights the significance of gratitude and recognition for the blessings one receives. In everyday life, this can be related to the practice of expressing gratitude, whether through prayer, meditation, or simply acknowledging the good in our lives.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of eternal values, the resilience of the human spirit, and the importance of honoring our experiences and growth. Its significance lies in the reminder that while physical aspects may change with time, the core values of wisdom, integrity, and gratitude remain timeless. In real-life scenarios, this mantra encourages individuals to focus on personal development, maintain their character, and appreciate the journey of life, regardless of age or circumstances.
