अद्रिभिः सुतः पवसे पवित्र आम̐ इन्दव इन्द्रस्य जटहरेष्व आविशन् |त्वं नृचक्षाऽभवो विचक्षण सोम गोत्रम् अङ्गिरोभ्योऽवृणोर अप ||
adribhiḥ sutaḥ pavase pavitra ām̐ indav indrasya jaṭhareṣv āviśan | tvaṁ nṛcakṣā abhavo vicakṣaṇa soma gotram aṅgirobhyo 'vṛṇor apa ||
  • adribhiḥ sutaḥ: This phrase translates to "born from the mountains." In a real-life context, this can be understood as the natural resources and rivers that originate from the mountains, which are vital for sustaining life and agriculture.
  • pavase pavitra: This means "purified by the wind." It signifies the cleansing and purifying forces of nature, akin to how fresh air revitalizes our environment and health.
  • ām̐ indav: This refers to "the moon." The moon is often associated with calmness and tranquility, influencing tides and agricultural cycles, much like how it affects human emotions and behaviors.
  • indrasya jaṭhareṣv āviśan: This translates to "they entered the womb of Indra." In mythology, Indra is the king of gods, representing strength and power. This can symbolize the nurturing aspect of nature, where all life is born and nurtured.
  • tvaṁ nṛcakṣā abhavo: This means "you are the one with human-like eyes." It suggests a connection between the divine and humanity, emphasizing the importance of perception and awareness in our lives.
  • vicakṣaṇa: This translates to "wise" or "perceptive." In real life, wisdom is crucial for making informed decisions, whether in personal life or in leadership roles.
  • soma gotram: This refers to the "lineage of Soma," which is a sacred drink in Vedic tradition, symbolizing immortality and divine inspiration. It can be related to the pursuit of knowledge and enlightenment in our lives.
  • aṅgirobhyo 'vṛṇor apa: This means "from the Angiras, I choose." The Angiras are ancient sages known for their wisdom and knowledge. This signifies the importance of seeking guidance from wise individuals in our lives.

Conclusion: This mantra encapsulates the essence of nature, divinity, and human experience. It highlights the interconnectedness of all life forms and the importance of wisdom and purity. In real life, it serves as a reminder to respect nature, seek knowledge, and strive for a harmonious existence. By understanding our roots and the forces that shape our lives, we can make better choices and contribute positively to our communities and the environment.
