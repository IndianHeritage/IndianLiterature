तस्य वज्रः क्रन्दति स्मत स्वर्षा दिवो न त्वेषो रवथः शिमिवान् |तं सचन्ते सानयस् तं धनानि मरुत्वान् नो भवत्व इन्द्र ऊती ||
tasya vajraḥ krandati smat svarṣā divo na tveṣo ravathaḥ śimīvān | taṁ sacante sanayas taṁ dhanāni marutvān no bhavatv indra ūtī ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • tasya: of him
    • vajraḥ: the thunderbolt or powerful
    • krandati: cries or calls out
    • smat: from us
    • svarṣā: the sound or voice
    • divo: of the sky or heaven
    • na: not
    • tveṣo: your
    • ravathaḥ: shining or radiating
    • śimīvān: the one who is auspicious
    • taṁ: him
    • sacante: may we attain
    • sanayas: the wealth or prosperity
    • dhanāni: riches or resources
    • marutvān: the powerful or strong
    • no: for us
    • bhavatv: may it be
    • indra: Indra, the king of gods
    • ūtī: strength or support
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the powerful presence of Indra, the deity associated with strength, rain, and thunder.
    • The phrase "tasya vajraḥ krandati" suggests that the powerful force (vajra) is calling out from the heavens, indicating a divine intervention or support.
    • The mention of "svarṣā divo" emphasizes the connection between the earthly realm and the divine, highlighting the importance of seeking blessings from higher powers.
    • By saying "taṁ sacante sanayas," the mantra expresses a desire to attain wealth and prosperity, which can be interpreted as a call for abundance in various aspects of life, including material wealth, health, and spiritual growth.
    • The invocation of "marutvān" signifies the strength and vigor that one seeks in life, suggesting that with divine support, one can overcome challenges and achieve success.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In times of difficulty, individuals often seek divine guidance or support, similar to how this mantra calls upon Indra for strength and prosperity.
    • In business, entrepreneurs may recite this mantra to invoke success and abundance in their ventures, believing that divine blessings can lead to fruitful outcomes.
    • During significant life events, such as weddings or new beginnings, people may chant this mantra to seek blessings for a prosperous and harmonious future.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness between the divine and human realms, emphasizing the importance of seeking support and strength from higher powers.
    • Its significance lies in the belief that with divine blessings, one can attain prosperity, overcome challenges, and lead a fulfilling life.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applicable in various contexts, such as personal growth, business endeavors, and significant life events, reinforcing the idea that seeking divine guidance can lead to success and abundance.
