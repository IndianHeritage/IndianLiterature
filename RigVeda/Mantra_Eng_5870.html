उपो ह यद् विदथं वाजिनो गुरु धीरभिर् विप्राः प्रमतिम् इच्छमानाः |अर्वन्तो न काष्ठां नक्षमानाः इन्द्राग्नि जोहुवतो नरस् ते ||
upo ha yad vidathaṁ vājino gur dhībhir viprāḥ pramatim icchamānāḥ | arvanto na kāṣṭhāṁ nakṣamāṇā indrāgnī johuvato naras te ||
  • Mantra: "upo ha yad vidathaṁ vājino gur dhībhir viprāḥ pramatim icchamānāḥ | arvanto na kāṣṭhāṁ nakṣamāṇā indrāgnī johuvato naras te ||"
  • Translation: "Those who, with their wise intellect, seek the path of the strong and powerful, may they, with their offerings, invoke Indra and Agni, the mighty ones, to grant them strength and wisdom."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • Upo: Towards, in the direction of
    • Ha: Indeed, certainly
    • Yad: What, that which
    • Vidathaṁ: The path, the way
    • Vājino: Of the strong, powerful
    • Gur: Great, wise
    • Dhībhir: With intellect, wisdom
    • Viprāḥ: The learned, the wise
    • Pramatim: Understanding, insight
    • Icchamānāḥ: Desiring, wishing for
    • Arvanto: Strong, powerful
    • Na: Not
    • Kāṣṭhāṁ: Wood, material
    • Nakṣamāṇā: Able to endure, withstand
    • Indrāgnī: Indra and Agni, the deities of strength and fire
    • Johuvato: Invoking, calling upon
    • Naras: Men, humans
    • Te: Your, of you
  • Significance: This mantra emphasizes the importance of wisdom and strength in human endeavors. It encourages individuals to seek guidance from powerful deities like Indra and Agni, who symbolize strength and energy. The invocation of these deities signifies a call for support in achieving one's goals through intellect and understanding.
  • Real-Life Application:
    • In a professional setting, individuals often seek mentorship and guidance from experienced leaders (akin to invoking Indra and Agni) to navigate challenges and achieve success.
    • Students may invoke this mantra metaphorically when they seek knowledge and wisdom from their teachers to excel in their studies.
    • In personal development, individuals may reflect on their strengths and seek to enhance their wisdom through self-improvement practices, aligning with the essence of the mantra.
  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a reminder of the synergy between strength and wisdom. It highlights the importance of seeking guidance and understanding in our pursuits, whether in personal growth, education, or professional endeavors. By invoking the qualities of Indra and Agni, individuals can aspire to achieve their goals with clarity and power.
