मनीषिणः प्र भरध्वं मनीषां यथा-यथा मतयः सन्ति नृणाम् |इन्द्रं सत्यैर एरयामाः कृतैभिः स हि वीरो गिरवणस्युर विदानः ||
manīṣiṇaḥ pra bharadhvam manīṣāṁ yathā-yathā matayaḥ santi nṛṇām | indraṁ satyair erayāmā kṛtebhiḥ sa hi vīro girvaṇasyur vidānaḥ ||
  • manīṣiṇaḥ pra bharadhvam: This phrase can be translated as "O wise ones, let us bring forth." It calls upon the wise individuals to contribute their thoughts and ideas. In real life, this can be seen in collaborative environments such as workplaces or community gatherings where diverse perspectives are valued.
  • manīṣāṁ yathā-yathā matayaḥ santi nṛṇām: This translates to "as many thoughts as there are among men." It emphasizes the richness of human thought and the variety of opinions that exist. For example, in a democratic society, the multitude of opinions during elections reflects this diversity of thought.
  • indraṁ satyair erayāmā kṛtebhiḥ: This means "let us invoke Indra with truthful deeds." Indra, in Vedic literature, is often associated with strength and leadership. This line suggests that actions based on truth and integrity are essential for invoking strength and support. In real life, this can be seen in leadership roles where ethical behavior fosters trust and loyalty among followers.
  • sa hi vīro girvaṇasyur vidānaḥ: This translates to "for he is the hero, the knower of the sound." It highlights the importance of knowledge and wisdom in achieving greatness. In modern contexts, this can relate to educators or mentors who guide others through their knowledge, helping them to become 'heroes' in their own right.

  • Conclusion: This mantra emphasizes the importance of wisdom, collaboration, and integrity in human endeavors. It signifies that by bringing together diverse thoughts and acting truthfully, individuals can invoke strength and achieve greatness. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios such as teamwork in organizations, community decision-making, and ethical leadership. The mantra serves as a reminder that collective wisdom and integrity are foundational to success and progress in any field.
