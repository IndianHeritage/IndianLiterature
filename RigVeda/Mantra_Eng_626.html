त्वमस्य पारे रजसो व्योमनाḥ स्वभूत्योजाऽवसे धृत्स्मनः |चक्रिषे भूमिम प्रतिमानम् ओजसोऽपः स्वः परिभूर एष्याऽदिवम् ||
tvam asya pāre rajaso vyomanaḥ svabhūtyojā avase dhṛṣanmanaḥ | cakṛṣe bhūmim pratimānam ojaso 'paḥ svaḥ paribhūr eṣy ā divam ||
  • tvam asya pāre rajaso vyomanaḥ: This phrase can be translated as "You are the one who exists beyond the realm of the material world." Here, "tvam" refers to the divine or the ultimate reality, while "pāre" indicates the transcendental nature of this reality. In real life, this can be understood as the pursuit of higher consciousness or spiritual awakening, where individuals seek to connect with something greater than themselves.
  • svabhūtyojā avase dhṛṣanmanaḥ: This translates to "Your inherent nature is to be fearless and to possess a strong mind." "Svabhūtyojā" refers to the innate qualities of the self, while "dhṛṣanmanaḥ" emphasizes courage and mental strength. In practical terms, this can be seen in individuals who face challenges with resilience, such as activists fighting for social justice or individuals overcoming personal hardships.
  • cakṛṣe bhūmim pratimānam ojaso 'paḥ: This means "You have created the earth as a reflection of your energy." "Cakṛṣe" indicates creation, while "bhūmim" refers to the earth. This can be related to environmental stewardship, where individuals and communities work to protect and preserve the planet, recognizing that their actions reflect their values and energy.
  • svaḥ paribhūr eṣy ā divam: This translates to "You encompass the heavens and will reach the divine." "Svaḥ" refers to the sky or heavens, and "divam" signifies the divine realm. This can be interpreted as the aspiration for spiritual growth and enlightenment, where individuals strive to elevate their consciousness and connect with the divine, much like those who engage in meditation or spiritual practices.

  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the connection between the individual and the divine, highlighting the importance of courage, mental strength, and environmental consciousness. In real life, it serves as a reminder to seek higher consciousness, face challenges with resilience, and take responsibility for the world we inhabit. By embodying these principles, individuals can contribute to a more harmonious and enlightened society.
