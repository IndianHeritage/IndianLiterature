एव ते अग्नि विमदो मनिषाम ऊर्जो नपाद अमृतैः सजोषाः |गिरा आ वक्षत सुमतीर इयान इषं ऊर्जं सुख्षितिं विश्वम आभाः ||
evā te agne vimado manīṣām ūrjo napād amṛtebhiḥ sajoṣāḥ | gira ā vakṣat sumatīr iyāna iṣam ūrjaṁ sukṣitiṁ viśvam ābhāḥ ||
  • evā te agne: This phrase translates to "O Agni," referring to the Vedic fire god, symbolizing energy, light, and purity.
  • vimado manīṣām: This means "with a joyful mind," indicating a state of happiness and clarity in thought.
  • ūrjo napād: This translates to "with strength and vigor," emphasizing the importance of energy in our actions.
  • amṛtebhiḥ sajoṣāḥ: This means "with the immortal and joyful," suggesting a connection to eternal truths and happiness.
  • gira ā vakṣat: This translates to "let the words be spoken," highlighting the power of speech and communication.
  • sumatīr iyāna: This means "may we have good thoughts," emphasizing the significance of positive thinking.
  • iṣam ūrjaṁ: This translates to "the strength of the mind," indicating the importance of mental fortitude.
  • sukṣitiṁ: This means "well-being," which is essential for a fulfilling life.
  • viśvam ābhāḥ: This translates to "the light of the universe," symbolizing universal knowledge and enlightenment.

In conclusion, this mantra emphasizes the importance of energy, positive thinking, and effective communication in achieving well-being and enlightenment. It encourages individuals to cultivate a joyful mindset, harness their inner strength, and express their thoughts clearly. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various contexts such as personal development, leadership, and teamwork. For instance, in a workplace setting, fostering a positive environment and encouraging open communication can lead to increased productivity and harmony among team members. Overall, the mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of thought, speech, and action in the pursuit of a fulfilling life.
