मन्त्र: होतā देवो अमर्त्यः पुरस्ताद् एति मायया | विधाथानि प्रचोदयन् ||
hotā devo amartyaḥ purastād eti māyayā | vidathāni pracodayan ||
  • hotā: This term refers to the priest or the one who invokes the deities during a ritual. In a broader sense, it symbolizes the role of a guide or a leader who calls upon higher powers for assistance.
  • devo: This word means 'divine' or 'godly'. It signifies the presence of higher spiritual forces that govern the universe.
  • amartyaḥ: This translates to 'immortal' or 'eternal'. It represents the aspect of divinity that transcends the physical realm and is everlasting.
  • purastād: Meaning 'in front' or 'before', this word indicates the direction from which divine energy or guidance is sought.
  • eti: This means 'comes' or 'arrives', suggesting the arrival of divine influence or inspiration.
  • māyayā: This term refers to 'illusion' or 'magic'. It highlights the complexities and challenges of the material world that can obscure spiritual truths.
  • vidathāni: This word means 'the various paths' or 'ways of action'. It signifies the different choices and decisions one can make in life.
  • pracodayan: This translates to 'inspiring' or 'motivating'. It indicates the act of encouraging or guiding individuals towards their goals or higher purposes.

The mantra can be interpreted as a call for divine guidance and inspiration in navigating the complexities of life. It emphasizes the importance of invoking higher powers to help us make wise decisions and overcome the illusions that may hinder our spiritual growth.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as:

  • Decision Making: When faced with difficult choices, individuals can recite this mantra to seek clarity and divine guidance, helping them to choose the right path.
  • Leadership: Leaders can use this mantra to inspire their teams, invoking a sense of purpose and direction that aligns with higher values.
  • Spiritual Growth: Practitioners can meditate on this mantra to connect with their inner selves and the divine, helping them to navigate the illusions of the material world.

Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its ability to connect individuals with divine guidance, encouraging them to seek clarity and inspiration in their lives. By invoking higher powers, one can navigate the complexities of existence with wisdom and purpose, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling and spiritually aligned life.
