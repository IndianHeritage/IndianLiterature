आयां धियाऽ च गव्यया पूरुणामन् पुरुष्टुत || यत सोमेशोमा आबवः ||
ayā dhiyā ca gavyayā puruṇāman puruṣṭuta | yat some-soma ābhavaḥ ||
  • ayā dhiyā: This phrase can be translated as "with that intellect" or "with that understanding." It emphasizes the importance of wisdom and discernment in our actions and thoughts.
  • ca gavyayā: "and with the strength of the cow." In Vedic culture, cows are considered sacred and symbolize wealth, nourishment, and abundance. This part of the mantra suggests that one should harness the strength and resources available to them.
  • puruṇāman puruṣṭuta: This translates to "the one who is praised by the wise." It indicates that true wisdom and understanding are recognized and celebrated by those who are knowledgeable.
  • yat some-soma ābhavaḥ: This can be interpreted as "that which is derived from the soma." Soma is a sacred drink in Vedic rituals, often associated with immortality and divine inspiration. This part of the mantra suggests that the results of our actions, when guided by wisdom and strength, lead to higher states of being or enlightenment.

  • Significance: This mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking wisdom and strength in our endeavors. It highlights the importance of using our intellect and resources wisely to achieve a higher purpose.
  • Real-life Application: In everyday life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as:
    • In education, students can strive to learn with understanding and utilize their resources effectively to achieve academic success.
    • In business, entrepreneurs can focus on making informed decisions that leverage their strengths and resources to create sustainable growth.
    • In personal relationships, individuals can seek to understand and communicate wisely, fostering stronger connections with others.

  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a reminder to approach life with wisdom and strength, recognizing the value of knowledge and the resources at our disposal. By embodying these principles, we can aspire to achieve greater fulfillment and contribute positively to the world around us.
