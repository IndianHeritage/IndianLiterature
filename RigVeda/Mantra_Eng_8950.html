आयुर विष्वायुः परि पासति त्वा पूषा त्वा पातु प्रपत्थे पुरस्तात् |यत्रासते सुक्रतो यत्र ते ययुस् तत्र त्वा देवः सविता दधातु ||
āyur viśvāyuḥ pari pāsati tvā pūṣā tvā pātu prapathe purastāt | yatrāsate sukṛto yatra te yayus tatra tvā devaḥ savitā dadhātu ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • āyur: Life
    • viśvāyuḥ: Universal vitality
    • pari: All around
    • pāsati: Protects
    • tvā: You
    • pūṣā: Nourisher or provider
    • pātu: May protect
    • prapathe: On the path
    • purastāt: In front
    • yatrāsate: Where there is
    • sukṛto: Good deeds
    • yatra: Where
    • te: You
    • yayus: May thrive
    • tatra: There
    • tvā: You
    • devaḥ: God
    • savitā: The Sun God
    • dadhātu: May grant
  • Overall Meaning:
    • This mantra invokes the divine energy of life and vitality, asking for protection and nourishment from the Sun God, Savitā.
    • It emphasizes the importance of good deeds and the paths that lead to a fulfilling life.
    • The mantra suggests that where there is righteousness and virtue, divine blessings will be present.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, this mantra can be reflected in the way we care for our health and well-being, ensuring we lead a life filled with positive actions and intentions.
    • For instance, someone who volunteers in their community or helps others in need embodies the essence of "sukṛto" (good deeds), which aligns with the mantra's teachings.
    • Additionally, individuals who practice mindfulness and gratitude can experience a sense of vitality and protection in their lives, resonating with the mantra's call for divine nourishment.
  • Conclusion and Significance:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of life, virtue, and divine support.
    • It encourages individuals to engage in righteous actions, fostering a life that is not only fulfilling but also protected by higher powers.
    • In a broader context, it can be applied in various life situations, such as during challenges, where one seeks strength and guidance through positive actions and thoughts.
    • Ultimately, the mantra highlights the importance of living a life aligned with goodness, ensuring that we attract divine blessings and vitality in our journey.
