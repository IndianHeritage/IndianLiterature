अस्य वो ह्य अवसā पान्तो दाक्षसाधनम् |यः सूरिषु श्रवो बृहद् दधे स्वर्ण न हर्यतः ||
asya vo hy avasā pānto dakṣasādhanam | yaḥ sūriṣu śravo bṛhad dadhe sva1r ṇa haryataḥ ||
  • Mantra: "asya vo hy avasā pānto dakṣasādhanam | yaḥ sūriṣu śravo bṛhad dadhe sva1r ṇa haryataḥ ||"
  • Translation: This mantra can be translated as: "This is indeed your path, the means of skill; he who bestows great fame upon the heroes, may he protect us."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • asya: This, or of this.
    • vo: Your.
    • hy: Indeed.
    • avasā: Path or way.
    • pānto: Means or skill.
    • dakṣasādhanam: Skillful means or resources.
    • yaḥ: He who.
    • sūriṣu: Among the heroes.
    • śravo: Fame or glory.
    • bṛhad: Great or vast.
    • dadhe: Bestows or grants.
    • sva1r: Our or of us.
    • ṇa: May.
    • haryataḥ: Protect or guard.
  • Contextual Explanation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of following a righteous path and utilizing skillful means to achieve success.
    • In real life, this can be seen in various professions where individuals must navigate challenges with skill and integrity, such as in leadership roles, where guiding a team effectively is crucial.
    • The reference to "heroes" can be interpreted as those who strive for excellence and contribute positively to society, such as activists, educators, or healthcare workers.
    • The invocation for protection signifies the need for support and guidance in our endeavors, reminding us that we are not alone in our pursuits.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the significance of choosing the right path and employing our skills wisely.
    • It encourages individuals to seek greatness not just for themselves but for the benefit of others, fostering a sense of community and shared purpose.
    • In practical terms, it can be applied in daily life by setting personal goals that align with ethical values and striving to make a positive impact in the world.
    • Ultimately, it highlights the interconnectedness of our actions and the importance of seeking guidance and support in our journeys.
