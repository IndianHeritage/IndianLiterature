एहि वां विमुचो नपाद आगृह्णे सं सचावहै | रथीर ऋतस्य नो भव ||
ehi vāṁ vimuco napād āghṛṇe saṁ sacāvahai | rathīr ṛtasya no bhava ||
  • Mantra: "ehi vāṁ vimuco napād āghṛṇe saṁ sacāvahai | rathīr ṛtasya no bhava ||"
  • Translation: "Come here, release us from the bonds; let us be united. May we be the charioteers of truth."
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ehi: Come here
    • vāṁ: you (plural)
    • vimuco: release or liberate
    • napād: from the bonds or ties
    • āghṛṇe: let us be united or come together
    • saṁ: together
    • sacāvahai: may we be the charioteers or guides
    • rathīr: charioteers
    • ṛtasya: of truth or righteousness
    • no: our
    • bhava: may it be or may we become
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra calls for unity and liberation from the constraints that bind individuals or communities.
    • The phrase "Come here" signifies an invitation for collective action and support.
    • "Release us from the bonds" suggests a desire to break free from limitations, whether they are social, emotional, or spiritual.
    • The idea of being "united" emphasizes the importance of collaboration and harmony among people.
    • Being the "charioteers of truth" implies taking responsibility for guiding oneself and others towards righteousness and ethical living.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community setting, this mantra can be applied when people come together to address social issues, such as poverty or injustice, emphasizing the need for collective effort to overcome challenges.
    • In personal relationships, it can serve as a reminder to communicate openly and work together to resolve conflicts, fostering unity and understanding.
    • In a workplace, it encourages teamwork and collaboration, where individuals support each other to achieve common goals, thus embodying the spirit of being "charioteers of truth."
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of unity, liberation, and the pursuit of truth in various aspects of life.
    • It serves as a powerful reminder that by coming together and supporting one another, we can overcome obstacles and strive for a more just and harmonious existence.
    • In essence, it calls for a collective journey towards enlightenment and righteousness, applicable in personal, social, and professional realms.
