मन्त्र: पौरं चिद्ध्युदप्रुतं पौर पौराय जिवथः | यद ईं गृभीततातये सिंहमिव द्रुहस्पद ||
pauraṁ cid dhy udaprutam paura paurāya jinvathaḥ | yad īṁ gṛbhītatātaye siṁham iva druhas pade ||
  • Mantra: "pauraṁ cid dhy udaprutam paura paurāya jinvathaḥ |
  • Translation: "May the city (paura) that is filled with consciousness (cid) rise up (udaprutam) and may the people (paurāya) thrive (jinvathaḥ) in it."
  • Imagery: The mantra evokes the image of a vibrant city, alive with awareness and energy, where its inhabitants flourish.

  • Key Terms:
    • Pauraṁ: Refers to a city or community, symbolizing a collective space where people live and interact.
    • Cid: Represents consciousness or awareness, indicating that the city is not just a physical space but a place of mindful existence.
    • Udaprutam: Means to rise or elevate, suggesting growth and progress within the community.
    • Paurāya: Pertains to the people of the city, emphasizing the importance of community and social bonds.
    • Jinvathaḥ: Translates to thriving or flourishing, highlighting the aspiration for prosperity and well-being.

  • Real-Life Examples:
    • Urban Development: In modern cities, urban planners aim to create spaces that foster community engagement and consciousness, such as parks, community centers, and cultural hubs.
    • Social Initiatives: Programs that promote mental health awareness and community support can be seen as efforts to elevate the consciousness of the populace, leading to a thriving society.
    • Environmental Awareness: Initiatives aimed at sustainable living and environmental consciousness contribute to the well-being of the community, ensuring that the city rises in harmony with nature.

  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of a conscious and thriving community. It serves as a reminder that the growth of a city is not merely about physical infrastructure but also about the collective awareness and well-being of its inhabitants. In real life, this mantra can inspire urban development, social initiatives, and environmental consciousness, ultimately leading to a more harmonious and prosperous society.
