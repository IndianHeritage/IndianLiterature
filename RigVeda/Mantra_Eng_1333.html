आ श्येनस्य जवासानूतनेनास्मे यातं नासत्याः सजोषाः |हवे हि वाम अश्विनाः रातहव्यः शाश्वत्तमायाः उषसो व्युष्टौ ||
ā śyenasya javasā nūtanenāsme yātaṁ nāsatyā sajoṣāḥ | have hi vām aśvinā rātahavyaḥ śaśvattamāyā uṣaso vyuṣṭau ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā: from
    • śyenasya: of the swift one (often associated with the sun or a bird)
    • javasā: with speed
    • nūtanen: with newness
    • āsme: we are
    • yātaṁ: going
    • nāsatyā: with the truthful ones
    • sajoṣāḥ: with joy
    • have: we invoke
    • hi: indeed
    • vām: your
    • aśvinā: O Ashvins (the twin deities)
    • rātahavyaḥ: who are worthy of invocation
    • śaśvattamāyā: with everlasting qualities
    • uṣaso: of the dawn
    • vyuṣṭau: who have risen
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the Ashvins, the twin deities associated with health, medicine, and the dawn.
    • The phrase "ā śyenasya javasā" suggests a call for speed and swiftness, possibly in achieving goals or overcoming obstacles.
    • "Nūtanenāsme" emphasizes the importance of new beginnings and fresh perspectives in life.
    • By mentioning "nāsatyā sajoṣāḥ," the mantra highlights the value of truthfulness and joy in our endeavors.
    • The invocation of the Ashvins signifies a request for divine assistance in attaining health, prosperity, and enlightenment.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, one might recite this mantra before embarking on a new project, seeking the swiftness and clarity of the Ashvins to ensure success.
    • During personal challenges, invoking the qualities of truth and joy can help individuals navigate through difficulties with a positive mindset.
    • In health-related matters, this mantra can be a reminder to seek holistic well-being, aligning oneself with the divine energies of healing represented by the Ashvins.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine support in achieving speed, truth, and joy in our lives.
    • It serves as a reminder to embrace new beginnings and to seek assistance from higher powers when facing challenges.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various aspects, including personal growth, professional endeavors, and health, encouraging individuals to align with positive energies and strive for success.
