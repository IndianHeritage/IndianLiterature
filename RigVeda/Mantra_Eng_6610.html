दस्रां हि विश्वं आनुशङ मक्षूबिः परिदीयतः |धियं जिन्वा मधुवर्णा शुभस पतिः ||
dasrā hi viśvam ānuṣaṅ makṣūbhiḥ paridīyathaḥ | dhiyaṁjinvā madhuvarṇā śubhas patī ||
  • Dasrā hi viśvam ānuṣaṅ makṣūbhiḥ paridīyathaḥ
    • Dasrā: This term can be interpreted as "to be quick" or "to be swift." It signifies the importance of speed and efficiency in actions.
    • Viśvam: This means "the whole" or "universe." It emphasizes the interconnectedness of all beings and the cosmos.
    • Ānuṣaṅ: This can be understood as "to follow" or "to pursue." It suggests a sense of continuity and the importance of following a path or tradition.
    • Makṣūbhiḥ: This word refers to "with the bees" or "with the buzzing." It symbolizes the collective effort and harmony found in nature.
    • Paridīyathaḥ: This means "to be surrounded" or "to be encompassed." It indicates the idea of being enveloped by a greater force or community.
  • Dhiyaṁjinvā madhuvarṇā śubhas patī
    • Dhiyaṁjinvā: This term refers to "the one who is wise" or "the one who has understanding." It highlights the value of wisdom and intellect in guiding actions.
    • Madhuvarṇā: This translates to "honey-colored" or "sweet." It symbolizes positivity, beauty, and the sweetness of life.
    • Śubhas: This means "auspicious" or "good." It emphasizes the importance of good fortune and positive outcomes.
    • Patī: This term means "lord" or "master." It signifies authority and leadership, suggesting that wisdom and goodness should guide those in power.

Conclusion:

The mantra emphasizes the significance of wisdom, collective effort, and the interconnectedness of all beings. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios such as teamwork in a workplace, where swift and efficient actions lead to success. It also highlights the importance of leadership that is guided by wisdom and positivity, which can be seen in effective governance or community leadership. The mantra serves as a reminder that when individuals come together with a shared purpose, guided by wisdom and positivity, they can create a harmonious and prosperous environment for all.
