तं एव ऋषिं तं उ ब्रह्माणम आहुर यज्ञन्यं सामगाम उक्तशासम् |स शुक्रस्य तन्भो वेद तिस्रो यः प्रथमो दक्षिणया रराध ||
tam eva ṛṣiṁ tam u brahmāṇam āhur yajñanyaṁ sāmagām ukthaśāsam | sa śukrasya tanvo veda tisro yaḥ prathamo dakṣiṇayā rarādha ||
  • tam eva ṛṣiṁ: This phrase translates to "that very sage." In the context of the Rigveda, a ṛṣi is a seer or sage who has attained a deep understanding of the universe and its laws. This can be likened to modern-day philosophers or scientists who seek to understand the fundamental truths of existence.
  • tam u brahmāṇam āhur: This means "they call him the Brahman." Brahman in Vedic philosophy refers to the ultimate reality or cosmic spirit. In real life, this can be compared to the pursuit of truth in various fields, such as science, spirituality, or philosophy, where individuals strive to understand the essence of existence.
  • yajñanyaṁ sāmagām ukthaśāsam: This translates to "the one who is the essence of sacrifice and the hymns of the Samaveda." Yajña (sacrifice) is a central theme in Vedic rituals, symbolizing selflessness and the importance of community. In contemporary life, this can be seen in acts of charity, community service, or any endeavor where individuals contribute for the greater good.
  • sa śukrasya tanvo veda: This means "he knows the essence of the bright one." The term "śukra" can refer to brightness or purity. This can be related to the pursuit of knowledge and enlightenment in our lives, where individuals seek clarity and understanding in their personal and professional journeys.
  • tisro yaḥ prathamo dakṣiṇayā rarādha: This translates to "the one who first attained the threefold path through the southern direction." The "threefold path" can symbolize the various paths one can take in life—be it knowledge, action, or devotion. The southern direction may represent a journey towards wisdom and understanding. In real life, this can be seen in the choices we make in our careers, relationships, and personal growth.

Conclusion: This mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking knowledge, understanding the ultimate reality, and the importance of selfless actions in life. It emphasizes the pursuit of truth and the interconnectedness of all beings through sacrifice and community. In today's world, this mantra serves as a reminder to strive for enlightenment, contribute positively to society, and recognize the deeper truths that govern our existence. Whether through education, community service, or personal reflection, the teachings of this mantra can guide individuals towards a more meaningful and fulfilling life.
