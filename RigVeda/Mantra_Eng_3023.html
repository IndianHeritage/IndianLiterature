इन्द्रस्य सख्यम ऋभवः सम आनशुर मनो नपातो अपसो दधन्विरे |सौधन्वनासो अमृतत्वम् एरिरे विष्टीः शमीभिः सुक्रतः सुक्रत्यया ||
indrasya sakhyam ṛbhavaḥ sam ānaśur manor napāto apaso dadhanvire | saudhanvanāso amṛtatvam erire viṣṭvī śamībhiḥ sukṛtaḥ sukṛtyayā ||
  • Indrasya sakhyam: This phrase refers to the friendship or alliance with Indra, the king of the gods in Vedic mythology. Indra is often associated with strength, rain, and thunder. In real life, this can symbolize the importance of forming strong alliances and friendships that empower us.
  • Ṛbhavaḥ sam ānaśur: This can be interpreted as the one who is noble and possesses great strength. It emphasizes the qualities of righteousness and valor. In everyday life, this can relate to individuals who stand up for justice and integrity, inspiring others through their actions.
  • Manor napāto apaso dadhanvire: This line suggests the idea of overcoming obstacles and challenges. It can be seen as a call to harness one's mental strength and willpower to navigate through life's difficulties. For example, a student facing academic challenges can draw on their inner strength to persevere and succeed.
  • Saudhanvanāso amṛtatvam erire: This phrase speaks to the attainment of immortality or eternal life through virtuous actions. In a modern context, this can be interpreted as leaving a lasting legacy through good deeds and contributions to society, much like influential leaders or philanthropists who are remembered for their positive impact.
  • Viṣṭvī śamībhiḥ sukṛtaḥ sukṛtyayā: This line emphasizes the importance of good deeds and righteous actions. It suggests that through virtuous living, one can achieve peace and harmony. In real life, this can be seen in community service or charitable acts that foster a sense of unity and well-being among people.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of strength, friendship, righteousness, and the pursuit of virtuous living. Its significance lies in the encouragement to build strong relationships, overcome challenges, and engage in good deeds. In today's world, this mantra can inspire individuals to strive for personal growth, contribute positively to their communities, and leave a meaningful legacy that transcends their lifetime.
