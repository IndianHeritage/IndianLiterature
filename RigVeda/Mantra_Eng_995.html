त्वं नः सोम विश्वतो रक्षां राजन् अग्हायतः |न रिष्येत तवावतः सखा ||
tvaṁ naḥ soma viśvato rakṣā rājann aghāyataḥ | na riṣyet tvāvataḥ sakhā ||
  • tvaṁ naḥ soma viśvato rakṣā: This phrase translates to "You, O Soma, protect us from all sides." Here, "Soma" refers to a sacred drink in Vedic tradition, symbolizing nourishment, immortality, and divine protection. In real life, this can be likened to the protective and nurturing aspects of nature or a supportive community that surrounds us.
  • rājann aghāyataḥ: This means "O King, do not let us be harmed." The term "Rājann" signifies a ruler or king, representing authority and governance. This can be related to the need for strong leadership in society that ensures the safety and well-being of its people, much like how a good leader protects their community from harm.
  • na riṣyet tvāvataḥ sakhā: This translates to "May we not be distressed, O Friend." The word "sakhā" means friend, indicating a plea for companionship and support. In everyday life, this can be seen in the importance of friendships and relationships that provide emotional support during challenging times.

  • Significance: This mantra emphasizes the need for protection, support, and companionship in life. It reflects the human desire for safety and the importance of community and relationships.
  • Real-life Application: In modern society, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as:
    • In personal relationships, where friends and family provide emotional and physical support during difficult times.
    • In community settings, where leaders and organizations work to ensure the safety and well-being of their members.
    • In environmental contexts, where the protection of nature and resources is crucial for sustaining life and health.

  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of individuals within a community and the importance of seeking protection and support from both divine and earthly sources. It encourages us to foster relationships and create environments where safety and companionship thrive, ultimately leading to a harmonious and fulfilling life.
