तस्मिन हि सन्त्यूतयो विश्वा अभीरवः सचा |तम आ वहन्तु सप्तयः पुरूवसुम मदाय हरयः सुतम् ||
tasmin hi santy ūtayo viśvā abhīravaḥ sacā | tam ā vahantu saptayaḥ purūvasum madāya harayaḥ sutam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • Tasmin: In that (context or situation)
    • Hi: Indeed
    • Santy: There is peace or tranquility
    • Ūtayo: The forces or energies
    • Viśvā: All or universal
    • Abhīravaḥ: Those who are fearless or courageous
    • Sacā: With truth or sincerity
    • Tam: That (referring to the divine or higher power)
    • Ā vahantu: May they bring or carry
    • Saptayaḥ: The seven (often referring to the seven sages or energies)
    • Purūvasum: The one who is abundant or prosperous
    • Madāya: For joy or delight
    • Harayaḥ: The divine or the remover of obstacles
    • Sutam: The offspring or the result
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of a state where peace and universal energies coexist.
    • It emphasizes the importance of fearlessness and truthfulness in achieving harmony.
    • The invocation of divine forces suggests a reliance on higher powers for guidance and support.
    • The mention of "saptayaḥ" can be interpreted as a call to the seven sages or energies that guide humanity.
    • It highlights the significance of joy and abundance in life, suggesting that these qualities are essential for spiritual and material prosperity.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace, fostering a culture of openness and truth can lead to a more harmonious environment, where employees feel fearless in expressing their ideas.
    • In personal relationships, practicing honesty and courage can strengthen bonds and create a sense of peace.
    • In community settings, collective efforts towards joy and abundance can lead to initiatives that uplift everyone, such as community gardens or cooperative businesses.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of peace, truth, and abundance in our lives.
    • It encourages individuals to embrace fearlessness and sincerity as pathways to achieving harmony and prosperity.
    • In real-life scenarios, applying the principles of this mantra can lead to more fulfilling relationships, successful collaborations, and a deeper sense of community.
