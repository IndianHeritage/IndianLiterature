उवे अम्ब सुलाभिके यथेवाङ्ग भविष्यति |भसन मे अम्ब शक्ति मे शिरो मे वीव हृष्यति विश्वस्माद इन्द्र उत्तरः ||
uve amba sulābhike yathevāṅga bhaviṣyati | bhasan me amba sakthi me śiro me vīva hṛṣyati viśvasmād indra uttaraḥ ||
  • uve amba sulābhike: This phrase can be translated as "O Mother, who is easily accessible." Here, "amba" refers to the divine feminine, often seen as a nurturing and protective figure. The term "sulābhike" suggests that this divine presence is approachable and ready to assist those who seek her.
  • yathevāṅga bhaviṣyati: This translates to "as it will be in the future." It implies a sense of destiny or the unfolding of events that are yet to come. This can be related to the idea of hope and faith in the future, where one believes that positive outcomes will manifest.
  • bhasan me amba: This means "speak to me, O Mother." It indicates a plea for guidance and communication from the divine. In real life, this can be seen in moments of prayer or meditation where individuals seek clarity and direction in their lives.
  • sakthi me: This translates to "my strength." It signifies the empowerment that comes from the divine. In practical terms, this can be related to the inner strength individuals draw upon during challenging times, often feeling a connection to a higher power that supports them.
  • śiro me vīva hṛṣyati: This means "may my head be filled with joy." It reflects a desire for happiness and fulfillment. In everyday life, this can be associated with moments of achievement, love, or contentment that bring joy to one's existence.
  • viśvasmād indra uttaraḥ: This translates to "from all, may Indra be supreme." Indra is often regarded as the king of the gods and a symbol of power and authority. This phrase suggests a call for the highest form of strength and protection from the divine, which can be seen in situations where individuals seek support in overcoming obstacles or challenges.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a deep yearning for divine connection, strength, and guidance. It emphasizes the importance of seeking support from a higher power, especially during uncertain times. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applicable in moments of prayer, meditation, or reflection, where individuals seek clarity, empowerment, and joy. It serves as a reminder that one can always turn to the divine for assistance and that the future holds promise and hope, reinforcing the belief in a supportive universe.
