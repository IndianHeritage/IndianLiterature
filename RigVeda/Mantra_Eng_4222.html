मित्रोऽंहोश्चिदादुरुक्षयाय गातुं वनते। मित्रस्य हि प्रतूर्वतः सुमतिरस्ति विधतः॥
mitro aṁhoś cid ād uru kṣayāya gātuṁ vanate | mitrasya hi pratūrvataḥ sumatir asti vidhataḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • Mitro: Friend or ally
    • Aṁhoś: Of the heavens or celestial
    • Cid: Consciousness or awareness
    • Ād: Beginning or origin
    • Uru: Vast or expansive
    • Kṣayāya: For destruction or dissolution
    • Gātuṁ: To go or to move
    • Vanate: To flourish or to thrive
  • Overall Meaning:
    • This mantra speaks of the divine friendship and support that comes from the celestial realms.
    • It emphasizes the importance of consciousness and awareness in navigating the vastness of life.
    • The mantra suggests that through friendship and divine guidance, one can overcome challenges and thrive.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In personal relationships, having supportive friends can help individuals face life's challenges, much like the divine friendship mentioned in the mantra.
    • In a professional setting, teamwork and collaboration can lead to greater success, reflecting the idea of flourishing through collective effort.
    • In spiritual practices, cultivating awareness and consciousness can lead to personal growth and a deeper understanding of one's purpose.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of friendship, awareness, and divine support in overcoming obstacles.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, such as personal relationships, teamwork, and spiritual growth.
    • By embracing the principles of this mantra, individuals can foster a sense of community and thrive in their endeavors.
