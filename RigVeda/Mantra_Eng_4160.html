हये नरो मरुतो मृळतां नस् तुवीमघासो अमृताः ऋतज्ञाः |सत्यश्रुतः कवयो युवानो बृहद्गिरयो बृहद् उक्षमानाः ||
haye naro maruto mṛḻatā nas tuvīmaghāso amṛtā ṛtajñāḥ | satyaśrutaḥ kavayo yuvāno bṛhadgirayo bṛhad ukṣamāṇāḥ ||
  • haye naro maruto mṛḻatā: This phrase can be interpreted as a call to the Maruts, the storm deities, to come forth. The term "haye" signifies a call or invocation, while "naro" refers to men or humans. "Maruto" indicates the Maruts, and "mṛḻatā" suggests the act of coming or moving. In real life, this can be seen as a metaphor for seeking help or guidance from powerful forces in times of need.
  • nas tuvīmaghāso amṛtā ṛtajñāḥ: Here, "nas" means 'to us', "tuvīmaghāso" can be interpreted as 'those who are rich in resources', and "amṛtā ṛtajñāḥ" refers to those who are immortal and knowledgeable about the truth. This part emphasizes the importance of seeking wisdom and abundance in life. For example, in a business context, this could relate to seeking mentorship or guidance from experienced individuals to achieve success.
  • satyaśrutaḥ kavayo yuvāno: "Satyaśrutaḥ" means 'those who are known for their truth', "kavayo" refers to poets or wise individuals, and "yuvāno" means 'young or youthful'. This suggests that wisdom and truth are often found in the words of the young and creative thinkers. In modern society, this can be seen in how young innovators and thinkers challenge the status quo and bring fresh perspectives to age-old problems.
  • bṛhadgirayo bṛhad ukṣamāṇāḥ: "Bṛhadgirayo" translates to 'those who have great songs or hymns', and "bṛhad ukṣamāṇāḥ" means 'those who are great in their endeavors'. This highlights the significance of creativity and ambition in achieving greatness. In real life, this can be related to artists, musicians, and leaders who inspire others through their work and vision.

  • Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda emphasizes the importance of invoking divine forces, seeking wisdom, and valuing the contributions of the young and creative. It serves as a reminder that in our pursuit of success and truth, we should not hesitate to call upon greater powers, learn from those who have more experience, and embrace the innovative spirit of youth. In practical terms, this mantra can be applied in various aspects of life, such as personal growth, professional development, and community building, encouraging individuals to seek collaboration, mentorship, and inspiration from diverse sources.
