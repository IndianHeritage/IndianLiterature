प्र स्वानासो रथा इवार्वन्तो न श्रवस्यवः |सोमासो रायें अक्रमुः ||
pra svānāso rathā ivārvanto na śravasyavaḥ | somāso rāye akramuḥ ||
  • pra svānāso: This phrase can be interpreted as "may we be awakened" or "may we be alert." It signifies the importance of being aware and conscious in our actions and thoughts.
  • rathā ivārvanto: Translated as "like the swift chariots," this part emphasizes speed and efficiency. In real life, this can relate to how we should approach our goals with determination and agility, much like a chariot racing towards its destination.
  • na śravasyavaḥ: This means "not being deprived of glory." It suggests that one should strive for recognition and honor in their endeavors, ensuring that their efforts are acknowledged and valued.
  • somāso: Referring to "the soma," which is often associated with vitality and strength. In a modern context, this can symbolize the pursuit of health and well-being, akin to how people today seek nourishment and wellness through various means.
  • rāye akramuḥ: This translates to "may we attain prosperity." It highlights the aspiration for success and abundance in life, which is a universal desire that drives individuals to work hard and achieve their dreams.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a profound message about awareness, speed in action, the pursuit of glory, vitality, and the quest for prosperity. In real life, it serves as a reminder to remain alert and proactive in our endeavors, to strive for recognition in our achievements, to prioritize our health, and to work diligently towards our goals. This mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as in personal development, career advancement, and even in community service, where the collective effort towards a common goal can lead to shared prosperity and recognition. By embodying the principles of this mantra, individuals can navigate their lives with purpose and intention, ultimately leading to a fulfilling and successful existence.
