अहम् एतान् चाश्वसतो द्वा-द्वेन्द्रं ये वज्रं युधये 'कृत्वता |आह्वयमानाम् अव हन्मनाहनं दृढा वदन्न अनमस्युर नमस्विनः ||
aham etāñ chāśvasato dvā-dvendraṁ ye vajraṁ yudhaye 'kṛṇvata | āhvayamānām̐ ava hanmanāhanaṁ dṛḻhā vadann anamasyur namasvinaḥ ||
  • Translation: "I invoke the two Indras, who wield the thunderbolt in battle, those who are called upon, who are strong and powerful, and who are worthy of our reverence."

  • Word Breakdown:
    • aham: I
    • etāñ: these
    • chāśvasato: of the two
    • dvā-dvendraṁ: Indras, the dual powers
    • ye: who
    • vajraṁ: thunderbolt
    • yudhaye: in battle
    • 'kṛṇvata: who perform
    • āhvayamānām̐: being invoked
    • ava: down
    • hanmanāhanaṁ: strong and powerful
    • dṛḻhā: firm
    • vadann: speaking
    • anamasyur: worthy of reverence
    • namasvinaḥ: of the revered ones

  • Contextual Meaning:
    • This mantra is a call to the dual aspects of Indra, the Vedic god associated with rain, storms, and battle. Indra represents strength, courage, and the ability to overcome obstacles.
    • The invocation of Indra signifies a plea for strength and support in challenging situations, much like a soldier seeking the blessings of a deity before going into battle.
    • In real life, this can be likened to individuals facing significant challenges, such as athletes preparing for a competition or students gearing up for exams, where they seek motivation and strength to succeed.

  • Significance:
    • The mantra emphasizes the importance of invoking divine support in our endeavors, highlighting the belief that strength and success come from both within and from higher powers.
    • It serves as a reminder that in times of struggle, one can seek guidance and strength from external sources, whether they be spiritual, communal, or personal.
    • This invocation can be applicable in various scenarios, such as during personal challenges, professional hurdles, or even in collective efforts like community service or social movements.

  • Conclusion:
    • The mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking strength and support in the face of adversity. It encourages individuals to recognize their own power while also acknowledging the influence of external forces.
    • In a world filled with challenges, this mantra serves as a source of inspiration, reminding us that we are not alone in our struggles and that invoking strength can lead to overcoming obstacles.
