विजेषकृत इन्द्र इवानवब्रवोऽस्माकं मन्यो अधिपा भविह |प्रियं ते नाम सहुरे गृणीमसि विद्मा तं उत्सं यत आबभूथा ||
vijeṣakṛd indra ivānavabravo3 'smākam manyo adhipā bhaveha | priyaṁ te nāma sahure gṛṇīmasi vidmā tam utsaṁ yata ābabhūtha ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • vijeṣakṛd: One who is victorious or a conqueror.
    • indra: The king of gods, often associated with strength and power.
    • ivānavabravo: As if he has come forth or manifested.
    • smākam: Our or belonging to us.
    • manyo: Mind or thought.
    • adhipā: Lord or ruler.
    • bhaveha: May you become or may you be.
    • priyaṁ: Dear or beloved.
    • te: Your.
    • nāma: Name.
    • sahure: We sing or we praise.
    • gṛṇīmasi: We sing or we chant.
    • vidmā: We know.
    • tam: That.
    • utsaṁ: Strength or power.
    • yata: From which or because of which.
    • ābabhūtha: Has arisen or has come into being.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the strength and power of Indra, the king of gods, asking for his blessings and guidance.
    • It emphasizes the importance of unity and collective thought, as it speaks of "our mind" and "our strength," suggesting that together we can achieve greatness.
    • The phrase "may you become our beloved lord" indicates a desire for a strong leader or guiding force in our lives.
    • By singing praises and acknowledging the power that has arisen, the mantra encourages recognition of divine strength in our endeavors.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a team setting, such as a workplace or sports team, invoking the spirit of Indra can symbolize the collective effort to achieve a common goal, where each member contributes their strength.
    • In personal development, one might reflect on the qualities of Indra—leadership, strength, and decisiveness—when striving to overcome challenges or lead others.
    • During community gatherings or rituals, this mantra can be recited to foster a sense of unity and shared purpose, encouraging participants to work together harmoniously.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for strength, unity, and leadership. It serves as a reminder that we can draw upon divine qualities to enhance our lives and communities.
    • In real-life scenarios, whether in personal challenges, team dynamics, or community efforts, invoking the essence of this mantra can inspire individuals to harness their collective power and strive for success.
    • Ultimately, it teaches us the value of collaboration and the importance of recognizing and celebrating the strengths that arise from unity.
