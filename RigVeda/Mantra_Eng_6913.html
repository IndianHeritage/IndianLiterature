अस्माकं सु रथम पुर इन्द्रः कृतोतु सातये |न यं धूर्वन्ति धूर्तयः ||
asmākaṁ su ratham pura indraḥ kṛṇotu sātaye | na yaṁ dhūrvanti dhūrtayaḥ ||
  • Translation: "May Indra, the great god, create for us a good chariot for our success; may it not be disturbed by the wicked."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • asmākaṁ: "for us" - indicating a collective desire or prayer.
    • su: "good" - referring to something beneficial or positive.
    • ratham: "chariot" - symbolizing a vehicle or means to achieve goals.
    • pura: "may create" - a request for divine intervention.
    • indraḥ: "Indra" - the king of gods in Vedic tradition, representing strength and power.
    • kṛṇotu: "may he create" - a plea for action from the divine.
    • sātaye: "for our success" - emphasizing the purpose of the chariot.
    • na: "not" - a negation, indicating a desire to avoid something.
    • yaṁ: "which" - referring to the chariot or the path to success.
    • dhūrvanti: "disturb" - to interfere or create obstacles.
    • dhūrtayaḥ: "wicked" - referring to those who may wish harm or disruption.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, this mantra can be interpreted as a prayer for a successful project. For instance, a team working on a crucial presentation may seek the support of their leaders (symbolized by Indra) to ensure that their efforts are fruitful and not hindered by negative influences or office politics.
    • In personal life, an individual may recite this mantra when embarking on a new venture, such as starting a business or entering a new relationship, hoping for a smooth journey free from negativity.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its invocation of divine support for success while simultaneously seeking protection from negativity. It emphasizes the importance of a strong foundation (the chariot) in achieving one's goals and the need for external support in overcoming challenges. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as career advancements, personal growth, and community endeavors, reminding individuals to seek both inner strength and external guidance in their pursuits.
