यत् पाञ्चजन्यया विशन्द्रे घोषा असृक्षत |अस्तृणाद् बरहणा विपोऽर्यो मानस्य स क्षयः ||
yat pāñcajanyayā viśendre ghoṣā asṛkṣata | astṛṇād barhaṇā vipo3 'ryo mānasya sa kṣayaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yat: that which
    • pāñcajanyayā: by the sound of the conch (Pāñcajanya)
    • viśendre: in the presence of the divine (Indra)
    • ghoṣā: the sound or call
    • asṛkṣata: was heard or resonated
    • astṛṇād: it spread or expanded
    • barhaṇā: with feathers (often symbolic of purity or elevation)
    • vipo3 'ryo: of the noble or the wise
    • mānasya: of the mind or intellect
    • sa: that
    • kṣayaḥ: destruction or decline
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the powerful sound of the conch, which is often associated with auspicious beginnings and the call to action in Hindu rituals.
    • The invocation of Indra, the king of gods, signifies the importance of divine presence and support in our endeavors.
    • The spreading of the sound indicates the far-reaching impact of this divine call, resonating in the minds of the wise and noble.
    • The mention of "mānasya" suggests that this sound influences the intellect and consciousness, guiding individuals towards wisdom and away from decline.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In many cultures, the sound of a conch is used to mark significant events, such as weddings or religious ceremonies, symbolizing purity and the call to gather.
    • In a corporate setting, a leader's call to action can be likened to the sound of the conch, rallying the team towards a common goal and inspiring them to rise above challenges.
    • In personal development, the mantra can be seen as a reminder to listen to the inner voice of wisdom, guiding one away from negative thoughts and towards constructive actions.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of divine guidance and the power of sound in shaping our thoughts and actions.
    • It serves as a reminder to seek wisdom and clarity in our decisions, ensuring that we are aligned with higher principles and values.
    • In real life, it encourages individuals to be mindful of the influences around them and to harness the power of positive communication and intention in their pursuits.
