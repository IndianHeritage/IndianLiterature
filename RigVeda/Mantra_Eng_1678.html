सम्मील्य यद्भुवना पर्यसर्पत क्व स्वित तात्या पितरा वा आसतुः |अशपत यः करसनं वा आददे यः प्राब्रवीत प्रो तस्मा अब्रवीतना ||
sammīlya yad bhuvanā paryasarpata kva svit tātyā pitarā va āsatuḥ | aśapata yaḥ karasnaṁ va ādade yaḥ prābravīt pro tasmā abravītana ||
  • Mantra: "sammīlya yad bhuvanā paryasarpata kva svit tātyā pitarā va āsatuḥ | aśapata yaḥ karasnaṁ va ādade yaḥ prābravīt pro tasmā abravītana ||"
  • Translation: This mantra can be interpreted as a call for unity and harmony among all beings. It reflects on the interconnectedness of the universe and the importance of collective existence.
  • Word Breakdown:
    • sammīlya: coming together, uniting
    • yad: that which
    • bhuvanā: worlds or realms
    • paryasarpata: pervading or encompassing
    • kva svit: where indeed
    • tātyā: father or ancestors
    • pitarā: parents or forefathers
    • va: or
    • āsatuḥ: may dwell or reside
    • aśapata: he who curses or speaks ill
    • yaḥ: who
    • karasnaṁ: with hands or actions
    • va ādade: or takes
    • yaḥ prābravīt: who has spoken
    • pro: thus
    • tasmā: from that
    • abravītana: he has spoken or declared
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community setting, this mantra emphasizes the importance of coming together for a common cause, such as environmental conservation. When individuals unite to protect their local ecosystem, they embody the essence of "sammīlya."
    • In family dynamics, the mantra highlights the significance of respecting and honoring one's ancestors and parents. For instance, celebrating family traditions can strengthen bonds and foster a sense of belonging.
    • In workplaces, promoting teamwork and collaboration reflects the idea of "bhuvanā paryasarpata," where diverse talents and perspectives come together to achieve a shared goal.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for unity and respect for the interconnectedness of all beings. It serves as a reminder that our actions and words can impact others, urging us to foster harmony in our relationships and communities. In real-life scenarios, whether in family, community, or workplace settings, embracing the principles of this mantra can lead to a more cohesive and supportive environment, ultimately contributing to the well-being of all.
