अवन्तु नः पितरः सुप्रवाचनाऽउत देवī देवपुत्रे ऋतावृद्धा | रथं न दुर्गाद वसवः सुदानवो विश्वस्मान नो अंहसो निः पिपर्तन ||
avantu naḥ pitaraḥ supravācanā uta devī devaputre ṛtāvṛdhā | rathaṁ na durgād vasavaḥ sudānavo viśvasmān no aṁhaso niṣ pipartana ||
  • avantu naḥ pitaraḥ - "May our fathers protect us": This phrase invokes the blessings and protection of our ancestors. In real life, this can be seen in the way families honor their forebears, seeking guidance and support from their wisdom and experiences.
  • supravācanā uta - "with good words": This emphasizes the importance of positive and constructive communication. In modern contexts, this can relate to how we communicate in our personal and professional lives, fostering an environment of respect and understanding.
  • devī devaputre ṛtāvṛdhā - "the goddess, the divine offspring, who increases righteousness": This line highlights the role of divine feminine energy in promoting dharma (righteousness). In everyday life, this can be reflected in the empowerment of women and the recognition of their contributions to society.
  • rathaṁ na durgād - "may the chariot not be obstructed": The chariot symbolizes progress and movement. This can be interpreted as a wish for smooth journeys in life, free from obstacles. In practical terms, it can relate to pursuing goals without hindrance, whether in career, education, or personal aspirations.
  • vasavaḥ sudānavo - "the Vasus, the good ones": The Vasus are deities representing various aspects of nature and prosperity. This phrase can be seen as a call for abundance and well-being in our lives, akin to seeking prosperity in our endeavors.
  • viśvasmān no aṁhaso niṣ pipartana - "may all energies not take away our strength": This line serves as a reminder to maintain our inner strength and resilience against external challenges. In real life, this can be related to mental health and the importance of self-care in facing life's adversities.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a profound plea for protection, positive communication, righteousness, unobstructed progress, abundance, and resilience. Its significance lies in its holistic approach to life, encouraging individuals to seek blessings from their ancestors, communicate positively, empower the divine feminine, pursue their goals without obstacles, and maintain their strength against challenges. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in family gatherings, community events, and personal reflections, serving as a guiding principle for harmonious living and personal growth.
