अरं दासो न मीळ्हुṣe कराण्य अहं देवाय भूर्णये 'नागाः |अचेतयद अचितो देवो आर्यो गृत्सं राये कवितरो जुनाति ||
araṁ dāso na mīḻhuṣe karāṇy ahaṁ devāya bhūrṇaye 'nāgāḥ | acetayad acito devo aryo gṛtsaṁ rāye kavitaro junāti ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • araṁ: to the one who is worthy
    • dāso: servant or devotee
    • na: not
    • mīḻhuṣe: to be confused or misled
    • karāṇy: actions or deeds
    • ahaṁ: I
    • devāya: to the divine
    • bhūrṇaye: for the purpose of abundance or prosperity
    • 'nāgāḥ: the great ones or the powerful
    • acetayad: not to be neglected or ignored
    • acito: the one who is beyond comprehension
    • devo: the divine being
    • aryo: noble or honorable
    • gṛtsaṁ: the one who is wise or knowledgeable
    • rāye: for wealth or prosperity
    • kavitaro: the poets or seers
    • junāti: brings together or unites
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks to the relationship between the devotee and the divine, emphasizing the importance of serving the divine without confusion or misdirection.
    • It highlights the need for clarity in one's actions and intentions, ensuring that they align with the divine purpose of abundance and prosperity.
    • The reference to "nāgāḥ" suggests a connection to powerful forces or beings, indicating that the divine is not to be taken lightly and should be approached with respect and understanding.
    • The mantra also acknowledges the wisdom of the divine, suggesting that true knowledge and prosperity come from a connection with the divine and the noble qualities it embodies.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, this mantra can be applied by ensuring that one's work is aligned with ethical practices and a higher purpose, avoiding confusion in decision-making.
    • In personal relationships, it encourages individuals to act with clarity and intention, fostering connections that are respectful and nurturing.
    • In community service, it serves as a reminder to approach service with a clear understanding of the needs of others, ensuring that actions lead to genuine abundance and support.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of serving the divine with clarity and purpose, ensuring that one's actions contribute to the greater good.
    • It is applicable in various aspects of life, including personal growth, professional integrity, and community engagement.
    • By embodying the principles outlined in this mantra, individuals can cultivate a life of abundance, wisdom, and meaningful connections with others and the divine.
