यā वो मायā अभिद्रुहे यजत्राः पाशा आदित्यā रिपवे विच्छृत्ताः |अश्वीव तां अति येषं रथेनारीष्टा उराव ā शर्मन् स्याम ||
yā vo māyā abhidruhe yajatrāḥ pāśā ādityā ripave vicṛttāḥ | aśvīva tām̐ ati yeṣaṁ rathenāriṣṭā urāv ā śarman syāma ||
  • yā vo māyā abhidruhe: This phrase can be translated as "that which is your illusion or deception." In real life, this can refer to the distractions and illusions that prevent individuals from seeing the truth or achieving their goals. For example, in a corporate setting, employees may be distracted by office politics, which can hinder their productivity and focus.
  • yajatrāḥ pāśā ādityā: This translates to "the bonds of the sun (Aditya)." Here, the sun symbolizes clarity, truth, and enlightenment. In life, this can be seen in the pursuit of knowledge and wisdom, where individuals seek to break free from ignorance. For instance, a student striving for academic excellence can be seen as breaking the bonds of ignorance through education.
  • ripave vicṛttāḥ: This means "cut off from enemies." In a practical sense, this can refer to overcoming obstacles or adversaries in life. For example, an athlete may face fierce competition, but through determination and hard work, they can overcome these challenges and achieve victory.
  • aśvīva tām̐ ati yeṣaṁ: This can be interpreted as "like the swift horses." This phrase emphasizes speed and agility in achieving one's goals. In real life, this can be likened to a business that adapts quickly to market changes, ensuring its success in a competitive environment.
  • rathenāriṣṭā urāv: This translates to "with the chariot, may we overcome obstacles." The chariot symbolizes progress and movement towards a goal. In everyday life, this can be seen in the journey of personal development, where individuals use their skills and resources (the chariot) to navigate through life's challenges.
  • ā śarman syāma: This means "may we attain peace and happiness." This is a universal aspiration for all individuals. In real life, this can be reflected in the pursuit of mental well-being, where practices like meditation and mindfulness help individuals achieve a state of peace amidst chaos.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the journey of overcoming illusions and obstacles in pursuit of truth, knowledge, and peace. It emphasizes the importance of clarity, determination, and adaptability in achieving one's goals. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios, such as personal growth, professional success, and the quest for inner peace. By understanding and embodying the principles of this mantra, individuals can navigate their lives more effectively, leading to a more fulfilling existence.
