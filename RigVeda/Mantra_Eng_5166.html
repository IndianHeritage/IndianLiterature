आ भारतī भारतिभिः सजोषा इळा देवैर मनुष्येभिर अग्निः |सरस्वती सारस्वतेभिर अर्वाक् तिस्रो देवीर बर्हिर एदं सदन्तु ||
ā bhāratī bhāratībhiḥ sajoṣā iḻā devair manuṣyebhir agniḥ | sarasvatī sārasvatebhir arvāk tisro devīr barhir edaṁ sadantu ||
  • Mantra: "ā bhāratī bhāratībhiḥ sajoṣā iḻā devair manuṣyebhir agniḥ |
  • Translation: "May the divine energy of Bharati, along with the deities and humans, unite with Agni (the fire)."
  • Explanation:
    • Bharati: Refers to the goddess of speech, knowledge, and learning. In real life, this can be seen in the pursuit of education and communication, where knowledge is shared and valued.
    • Agni: Represents fire, which is a symbol of transformation and purification. In everyday life, fire is used in rituals, cooking, and as a source of energy, signifying the importance of transformation in our lives.
    • Unity of Deities and Humans: This highlights the importance of collaboration between divine forces and human efforts. For example, in community service, when people come together for a common cause, they embody this unity.
  • Next Part: "sarasvatī sārasvatebhir arvāk tisro devīr barhir edaṁ sadantu ||"
  • Translation: "May Sarasvati, along with her divine forms, bless this offering with her presence."
  • Explanation:
    • Sarasvati: The goddess of wisdom, music, and arts. In real life, this can be seen in the importance of creativity and knowledge in various fields such as education, music, and art.
    • Divine Blessings: The mantra calls for blessings on offerings, which can be interpreted as seeking divine guidance in our endeavors, whether in academics, arts, or personal growth.
    • Three Goddesses: The mention of three goddesses signifies the multifaceted nature of knowledge and creativity, reminding us that wisdom can be approached from various angles and disciplines.
  • Conclusion: This mantra emphasizes the significance of knowledge, unity, and divine blessings in our lives. It encourages individuals to seek wisdom and collaborate with others, recognizing the transformative power of knowledge and creativity. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in educational settings, community projects, and personal development, where the pursuit of knowledge and collaboration leads to growth and enlightenment.
