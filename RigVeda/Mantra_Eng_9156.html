अनमीवाऽ उषसा आ चरन्तु न उद अग्नयो जिहतां ज्योतिषा बृहत् |आयुक्षातां अश्विनाऽ तूतुजिं रथं स्वस्त्य अग्निं समिधानम् ईमहे ||
anamīvā uṣasa ā carantu na ud agnayo jihatāṁ jyotiṣā bṛhat | āyukṣātām aśvinā tūtujiṁ rathaṁ svasty a1gniṁ samidhānam īmahe ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • anamīvā: Like a non-existent or invisible force
    • uṣasa: Dawn or the light of the morning
    • ā carantu: May they move or travel
    • na: Not
    • ud: Upwards or above
    • agnayo: Of the fires or the divine energies
    • jihataṁ: May they shine or blaze
    • jyotiṣā: With light or illumination
    • bṛhat: Great or vast
    • āyukṣātām: For longevity or long life
    • aśvinā: O Ashvins (the twin deities of health and medicine)
    • tūtujiṁ: May we attain or achieve
    • rathaṁ: Chariot or vehicle
    • svasty: Well-being or auspiciousness
    • agniṁ: Fire or divine energy
    • samidhānam: The act of kindling or igniting
    • īmahe: We invoke or we meditate upon
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the dawn, symbolizing new beginnings and the dispelling of darkness.
    • It emphasizes the importance of light and energy in our lives, urging us to seek illumination and knowledge.
    • The mention of the Ashvins highlights the significance of health and well-being, suggesting that we should strive for a long and prosperous life.
    • The chariot symbolizes the journey of life, and the mantra encourages us to seek auspiciousness and divine guidance in our endeavors.
    • By invoking Agni, the fire, we are reminded of the transformative power of energy and the importance of nurturing our inner fire for growth and success.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, this mantra can be applied during morning rituals or meditation, focusing on the arrival of a new day filled with opportunities.
    • When facing challenges, one can reflect on the mantra to seek clarity and guidance, much like the dawn dispels the darkness of night.
    • In health practices, invoking the Ashvins can serve as a reminder to prioritize physical and mental well-being through balanced living.
    • In professional settings, the idea of a chariot can symbolize teamwork and collaboration, encouraging individuals to work together towards common goals.
    • In personal development, the concept of igniting one's inner fire can inspire individuals to pursue their passions and aspirations with vigor.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the cyclical nature of life, the importance of seeking light and knowledge, and the need for health and well-being.
    • Its significance lies in its ability to inspire individuals to embrace new beginnings, cultivate their inner strength, and strive for a harmonious and prosperous life.
    • By incorporating the essence of this mantra into daily practices, one can foster a sense of purpose and direction, ultimately leading to a fulfilling existence.
