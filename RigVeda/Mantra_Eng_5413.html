मघोनः स्म वृत्रहत्येषु कोदया ये ददति प्रियाः वसु |तव प्रणीती हर्यश्व सूरिभिर विश्वा तरेमा दुरिता ||
maghonaḥ sma vṛtrahatyeṣu codaya ye dadati priyā vasu | tava praṇītī haryaśva sūribhir viśvā tarema duritā ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • maghonaḥ: of the powerful
    • sma: indeed
    • vṛtrahatyeṣu: in the slaying of Vritra (the demon of drought)
    • codaya: inspire or motivate
    • ye: those who
    • dadati: give
    • priyā: beloved
    • vasu: wealth or prosperity
    • tava: your
    • praṇītī: guidance or leadership
    • haryaśva: with swift horses
    • sūribhir: by the brave or heroic
    • viśvā: all or universal
    • tarema: may we cross or overcome
    • duritā: difficulties or obstacles
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra invokes the powerful forces that aid in overcoming challenges, particularly symbolized by the slaying of Vritra, a demon representing obstacles and drought.
    • The phrase "inspire those who give beloved wealth" emphasizes the importance of generosity and the role of community in achieving prosperity.
    • The mention of "your guidance with swift horses" suggests the need for divine or higher guidance in our endeavors, emphasizing speed and efficiency in overcoming life's challenges.
    • The call to "cross all difficulties" reflects a universal desire to overcome personal and collective struggles, resonating with anyone facing adversity.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be applied to motivate teams to work together towards a common goal, overcoming market challenges through collaboration and shared resources.
    • In personal life, individuals facing health issues may find strength in community support, echoing the mantra's call for collective effort in overcoming difficulties.
    • During times of natural disasters, communities often come together to provide aid and support, embodying the spirit of this mantra by overcoming the 'Vritra' of calamity through unity and generosity.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the strength found in community, the importance of guidance, and the collective effort required to overcome life's obstacles.
    • Its significance lies in its universal applicability, encouraging individuals and communities to seek support, act generously, and strive for prosperity while facing challenges.
    • In essence, it teaches that with the right motivation and guidance, we can navigate through difficulties and emerge victorious, making it relevant in various aspects of life, from personal struggles to communal efforts.
