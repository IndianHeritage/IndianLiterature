नव्यं तदुक्त्यं हितं देवासः सुप्रवाचनम् |ऋतं अर्षन्ति सिन्धवः सत्यं तातान सूर्यो वित्तं मे अस्य रोदसी ||
navyaṁ tad ukthyaṁ hitaṁ devāsaḥ supravācanam | ṛtam arṣanti sindhavaḥ satyaṁ tātāna sūryo vittam me asya rodasī ||
  • navyaṁ tad ukthyaṁ hitaṁ devāsaḥ supravācanam: This phrase can be translated to mean "that which is new, that which is beneficial, the divine beings speak well." Here, "navyaṁ" refers to something new or fresh, "ukthyaṁ" relates to speech or utterance, "hitaṁ" means beneficial or good, "devāsaḥ" refers to the divine beings or gods, and "supravācanam" indicates well-spoken or eloquent words. In real life, this can be seen in the context of innovation and positive communication, where new ideas and beneficial discussions lead to progress and harmony in society.
  • ṛtam arṣanti sindhavaḥ: This translates to "the rivers flow with truth." "ṛtam" signifies truth or order, "arṣanti" means to flow or move, and "sindhavaḥ" refers to rivers. This can be related to the idea that just as rivers flow continuously and provide sustenance, truth and honesty are essential for the sustenance of relationships and communities. For example, in a business setting, transparency and truthfulness can lead to trust and long-term success.
  • satyaṁ tātāna sūryo vittam me asya rodasī: This can be interpreted as "the sun, which is the source of wealth, shines with truth upon me." "satyaṁ" means truth, "tātāna" refers to shining or radiating, "sūryo" is the sun, "vittam" means wealth or prosperity, and "rodasī" refers to the horizon or the world. This emphasizes the idea that truth and integrity are foundational to achieving prosperity and success. In everyday life, this can be seen in how ethical practices in business lead to sustainable growth and a good reputation.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of new and beneficial ideas, the flow of truth, and the relationship between truth and prosperity. In real life, it serves as a reminder that innovation, honesty, and integrity are crucial for personal and communal growth. By embracing these values, individuals and societies can foster a harmonious and prosperous environment, leading to a better quality of life for all.
