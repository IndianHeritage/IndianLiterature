मन्त्र: महि त्रिणाम् अवोऽस्तु द्युक्षम् मित्रस्यार्यम्णः | दुराधर्षं वरुणस्य ||
mahi trīṇām avo 'stu dyukṣam mitrasyāryamṇaḥ | durādharṣaṁ varuṇasya ||
  • Translation: "May the three realms be auspicious for us, may we be protected by the friendship of Mitra and Varuna, who are difficult to be overcome."

  • Word Breakdown:
    • Mahi: Great or auspicious.
    • Trīṇām: Of the three realms (heaven, earth, and the atmosphere).
    • Avo: May we be protected or may it come to us.
    • Dyukṣam: Auspicious or favorable.
    • Mitrasyāryamṇaḥ: By the friendship of Mitra, the god of friendship and contracts.
    • Durādharṣaṁ: Difficult to overcome or resist.
    • Varuṇasya: Of Varuna, the god of cosmic order and water.

  • Contextual Explanation:
    • This mantra invokes the blessings of two significant deities in Vedic tradition: Mitra and Varuna. Mitra represents friendship, harmony, and contracts, while Varuna embodies the cosmic order, truth, and the moral law.
    • The phrase "three realms" refers to the holistic view of existence in Vedic philosophy, encompassing the physical, mental, and spiritual dimensions of life.
    • The invocation for protection signifies a desire for safety and well-being in all aspects of life, emphasizing the importance of community and divine support.

  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, the principles of Mitra can be applied to foster teamwork and collaboration among employees, ensuring that contracts and agreements are honored with integrity.
    • In personal relationships, invoking the spirit of Mitra can help maintain harmony and trust, while Varuna's influence can guide individuals to act in accordance with moral values and social responsibilities.
    • In environmental contexts, Varuna's association with water can inspire efforts to protect natural resources, emphasizing the interconnectedness of all life and the need for sustainable practices.

  • Conclusion: The mantra highlights the significance of friendship, moral order, and the interconnectedness of all realms of existence. In real life, it serves as a reminder to cultivate harmonious relationships, uphold ethical standards, and seek divine guidance in our endeavors. By embodying these principles, individuals and communities can navigate challenges with resilience and foster a more harmonious world.
