नू त्वाम अग्न इमहे वसिष्ठा ईशानं सूनो सहसो वसूनाम् |इषं स्तोत्रिभ्यो मगवद्भ्य आनाḍ यूम पात स्वस्तिभिः सदा नः ||
nū tvām agna īmahe vasiṣṭhā īśānaṁ sūno sahaso vasūnām | iṣaṁ stotṛbhyo maghavadbhya ānaḍ yūyam pāta svastibhiḥ sadā naḥ ||
  • nū tvām agna īmahe: This phrase translates to "We invoke you, O Agni." Agni, the fire god, represents energy, transformation, and the divine messenger between humans and the gods. In real life, invoking Agni can symbolize the importance of seeking guidance and inspiration in our endeavors, much like how we seek motivation from mentors or leaders.
  • vasiṣṭhā īśānaṁ sūno: This means "the best of the best, the lord of the wealth." Here, "Vasiṣṭha" refers to the sage known for his wisdom and wealth of knowledge. In a modern context, this can relate to the pursuit of knowledge and wisdom in our lives, emphasizing the value of education and learning from those who are knowledgeable.
  • sahaso vasūnām: This translates to "the powerful among the wealth." It highlights the idea that true wealth is not just material but also includes strength, wisdom, and the ability to influence positively. In everyday life, this can be seen in leaders who use their resources to uplift others, demonstrating that power should be used for the greater good.
  • iṣaṁ stotṛbhyo maghavadbhya ānaḍ: This phrase means "to those who praise and to the generous." It emphasizes the importance of gratitude and recognition of those who contribute positively to our lives. In real life, this can be reflected in how we appreciate our friends, family, and community members who support us, fostering a culture of gratitude.
  • yūyam pāta svastibhiḥ sadā naḥ: This translates to "May you protect us always with auspiciousness." This is a prayer for protection and well-being. In practical terms, it can be related to the importance of seeking safety and security in our lives, whether through community support, personal relationships, or spiritual practices.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a deep reverence for Agni, the divine force that represents transformation, wisdom, and protection. Its significance lies in the reminder to seek guidance, value knowledge, appreciate generosity, and strive for auspiciousness in our lives. In real-life scenarios, this mantra encourages us to cultivate positive relationships, pursue wisdom, and use our resources for the benefit of others, ultimately leading to a harmonious and prosperous existence.
