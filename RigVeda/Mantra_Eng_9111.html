नित्यश्चाकन्यात्मा स्वपतिर्मदूमना यस्मादु देवः सविता जजाना |भागो वा गोभिरार्यमेम अनज्यात्मा सोऽस्मै चारुश्चादयदुत स्यात् ||
nityaś cākanyāt svapatir damūnā yasmā u devaḥ savitā jajāna | bhago vā gobhir aryamem anajyāt so asmai cāruś chadayad uta syāt ||
  • nityaś cākanyāt svapatir damūnā: This phrase can be translated as "the eternal one, who is the lord of the night, who is the protector." Here, "nitya" means eternal, "svapatir" refers to the lord of the night, and "damūnā" signifies protection. In real life, this can be likened to the role of a guardian or a mentor who provides guidance and safety in times of uncertainty.
  • yasmā u devaḥ savitā jajāna: This translates to "from whom the divine Savita was born." Savita is often associated with the sun and creation. This line emphasizes the source of life and energy. In practical terms, it can be related to the sun's role in agriculture, where sunlight is essential for the growth of crops, symbolizing sustenance and vitality.
  • bhago vā gobhir aryamem anajyāt: This can be interpreted as "may the divine blessings come to us from the cows." Cows are considered sacred in many cultures, particularly in India, symbolizing nourishment and abundance. This line highlights the importance of gratitude towards nature and its resources, reminding us to respect and care for the environment that sustains us.
  • so asmai cāruś chadayad uta syāt: This means "may he bestow upon us beautiful blessings." The term "cāruś" refers to beauty or goodness. This line signifies the desire for positive outcomes and prosperity in life. In a real-life context, it can be seen in the aspirations of individuals and communities striving for a better future, whether through education, health, or social welfare.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates themes of protection, creation, gratitude, and the pursuit of beauty and prosperity. Its significance lies in the acknowledgment of divine forces that govern our lives and the natural world. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire individuals to seek guidance from mentors, appreciate the resources provided by nature, and strive for a harmonious and prosperous existence. It serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of life and the importance of nurturing both our inner selves and the environment around us.
