आदित्सन्तं चिदाघृणे पूषण दानाय चोदय |पणेश चिद्विम्रदामनः ||
aditsantaṁ cid āghṛṇe pūṣan dānāya codaya | paṇeś cid vi mradā manaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • aditsantaṁ: The one who is the source or origin.
    • cid: Consciousness or awareness.
    • āghṛṇe: To be invoked or called upon.
    • pūṣan: A deity associated with nourishment and prosperity.
    • dānāya: For the purpose of giving or donation.
    • codaya: Inspire or motivate.
    • paṇeś: The one who is the master of wealth or resources.
    • cid: Consciousness or awareness.
    • vi: Indeed or truly.
    • mradā: Soft or gentle.
    • manaḥ: Mind or intellect.
  • Overall Meaning: This mantra invokes the divine consciousness, asking for inspiration and guidance from the deity Pūṣan, who is associated with nourishment and prosperity. It emphasizes the importance of a gentle and aware mind in the pursuit of wealth and resources, encouraging a mindset that is open to giving and sharing.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be applied when seeking inspiration for ethical practices in wealth generation. For instance, a company that prioritizes corporate social responsibility embodies the essence of this mantra by focusing on giving back to the community.
    • In personal life, individuals can reflect on this mantra when making decisions about charitable donations or community service, reminding themselves of the importance of a generous and conscious mindset.
    • In relationships, the gentle approach suggested by "mradā manaḥ" can be applied to foster understanding and compassion, leading to healthier interactions.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for a conscious and gentle approach to wealth and resources. It encourages individuals to seek inspiration from divine consciousness, promoting a mindset that values giving and sharing. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can guide ethical business practices, charitable actions, and compassionate relationships, ultimately leading to a more harmonious and prosperous existence.
