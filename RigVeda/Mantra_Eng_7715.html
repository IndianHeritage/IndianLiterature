एष देवो रथार्यति पवमानो दशस्यति |आविष कृणोति वाग्वनुम् ||
eṣa devo ratharyati pavamāno daśasyati | āviṣ kṛṇoti vagvanum ||
  • eṣa: This word translates to "this" or "he," indicating a specific deity or divine presence.
  • devo: Meaning "god" or "deity," it refers to a divine being that holds power and significance.
  • ratharyati: This term can be broken down into "ratha" (chariot) and "yati" (to move or drive), suggesting the movement or guidance of a divine chariot.
  • pavamāno: This word means "purifying" or "the one who purifies," often associated with the wind or breath of life, symbolizing the essence of purity and clarity.
  • daśasyati: This can be interpreted as "he who is tenfold" or "the one who encompasses all," indicating a vast and comprehensive nature.
  • āviṣ: Meaning "to cover" or "to envelop," it suggests a protective or encompassing quality.
  • kṛṇoti: This translates to "he makes" or "he creates," indicating the active role of the deity in shaping or manifesting reality.
  • vagvanum: This term refers to "speech" or "word," highlighting the importance of communication and expression in the divine context.

The mantra can be interpreted as a call to a divine presence that guides and purifies, moving like a chariot through the world. It emphasizes the protective and creative aspects of the deity, who envelops and manifests through the power of speech and communication.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios:

  • Guidance in Decision-Making: Just as a chariot is guided by its driver, individuals can seek divine guidance in making important life decisions, trusting that a higher power will lead them on the right path.
  • Purification of Thoughts and Actions: The concept of purification can be applied to personal growth, where individuals strive to cleanse their thoughts and actions, fostering a more positive and constructive mindset.
  • Communication and Expression: The emphasis on speech highlights the importance of clear and honest communication in relationships, whether personal or professional, fostering understanding and connection.

In conclusion, this mantra signifies the interplay between divine guidance, purification, and the power of communication. It serves as a reminder of the importance of seeking higher wisdom, striving for personal clarity, and expressing oneself authentically in all aspects of life.
