तपुरजम्भो वन आ वातचोदितो यूथे न साह्वाम् अव वाति वंसगः |अभिव्रजनन अक्षितं पाजसा रजः स्थातुश्चरतं भयते पतत्रिणः ||
tapurjambho vana ā vātacodito yūthe na sāhvām̐ ava vāti vaṁsagaḥ | abhivrajann akṣitam pājasā rajaḥ sthātuś caratham bhayate patatriṇaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • tapuḥ: heat, energy, or austerity
    • jambho: the one who is great or vast
    • vana: forest or wilderness
    • ā: towards
    • vātacodito: driven by the wind
    • yūthe: in a group or herd
    • na: not
    • sāhvām: our strength or power
    • ava: down or away
    • vāti: blows or moves
    • vaṁsagaḥ: the one who is from the lineage or family
    • abhivrajann: moving forward or advancing
    • akṣitam: unbroken or intact
    • pājasā: with glory or radiance
    • rajaḥ: dust or particles
    • sthātuś: to stand or remain
    • caratham: moving or traveling
    • bhayate: is afraid or fearful
    • patatriṇaḥ: of the birds or winged creatures
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the power of energy and the vastness of nature.
    • It emphasizes the importance of unity and strength in a group, suggesting that when individuals come together, they can withstand challenges.
    • The reference to the wind indicates the dynamic nature of life and how external forces can influence our journey.
    • The mention of birds signifies freedom and the ability to rise above fears, suggesting that one should not be afraid to pursue their path.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, teamwork is essential. Just as the mantra suggests unity, successful projects often rely on collaboration, where each member contributes their strengths.
    • In nature, flocks of birds often fly together, showcasing how collective movement can lead to safety and efficiency, mirroring the mantra's message of strength in numbers.
    • In personal development, individuals often face fears and challenges. The mantra encourages overcoming these fears, much like how a bird must take the leap to fly.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the power of unity, the importance of resilience, and the need to embrace change.
    • Its significance lies in its applicability to various aspects of life, from personal growth to teamwork and overcoming challenges.
    • By embodying the principles outlined in this mantra, individuals can navigate their paths with courage and strength, ultimately leading to success and fulfillment.
