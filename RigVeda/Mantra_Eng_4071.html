सजूर मित्रावरुणाभ्यां सजूः सोमेन विष्णुना |आ याह्य अग्ने अत्रिवत सुत रेण ||
sajūr mitrāvaruṇābhyāṁ sajūḥ somena viṣṇunā | ā yāhy agne atrivat sute raṇa ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • sajūr: Together, united
    • mitrāvaruṇābhyāṁ: With Mitra and Varuna (the deities representing friendship and cosmic order)
    • sajūḥ: Together, united
    • somena: With the Soma (a sacred drink, symbolizing immortality and divine inspiration)
    • viṣṇunā: With Vishnu (the preserver and protector in Hinduism)
    • ā yāhy: May we go, may we proceed
    • agne: O Agni (the fire god, representing energy and transformation)
    • atrivat: Like this, in this manner
    • sute: In the offering, in the sacrifice
    • raṇa: In battle, in conflict
  • Overall Meaning: This mantra invokes the unity of divine forces—Mitra, Varuna, Soma, and Vishnu—while seeking the blessings of Agni. It emphasizes the importance of collaboration and harmony among these deities to achieve success in endeavors, particularly in challenging situations like battles or conflicts.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a team project at work, the collaboration of different team members (akin to the unity of the deities) can lead to successful outcomes. Just as the mantra calls for unity, effective teamwork often requires individuals to work together harmoniously.
    • In personal relationships, the principles of friendship (Mitra) and maintaining order (Varuna) are crucial. This mantra can serve as a reminder to nurture these values in our interactions with others.
    • During challenging times, such as facing a difficult decision or conflict, invoking the spirit of this mantra can inspire individuals to seek guidance and support from their community, much like the divine forces coming together to provide strength.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for unity and collaboration among divine forces, which can be mirrored in our daily lives. By embodying the principles of cooperation, friendship, and seeking divine guidance, we can navigate challenges more effectively. This mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the importance of working together, whether in personal relationships, professional settings, or during times of conflict, ultimately leading to greater success and harmony in life.
