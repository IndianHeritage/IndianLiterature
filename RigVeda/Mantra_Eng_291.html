मन्त्र: नि नो होतार वरेण्यः सदा यविष्ट मन्यमभिः |अग्ने दिवित्मता वचः ||
ni no hotā vareṇyaḥ sadā yaviṣṭha manmabhiḥ | agne divitmatā vacaḥ ||
  • Mantra: "ni no hotā vareṇyaḥ sadā yaviṣṭha manmabhiḥ | agne divitmatā vacaḥ ||"
  • Translation: "O Agni, the most excellent and worthy of worship, may you always be our priest, and may your divine words bring us prosperity."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • ni no: "to us" or "for us"
    • hotā: "priest" or "one who invokes"
    • vareṇyaḥ: "most excellent" or "worthy of worship"
    • sadā: "always"
    • yaviṣṭha: "the best" or "the most beneficial"
    • manmabhiḥ: "by our thoughts" or "through our minds"
    • agne: "O Agni" (the fire god)
    • divitmatā: "divine" or "of the heavens"
    • vacaḥ: "words" or "speech"
  • Context and Significance:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Agni, the fire god, who is considered a mediator between humans and the divine in Vedic literature.
    • Agni represents not only physical fire but also the spiritual fire of knowledge and enlightenment.
    • The invocation of Agni as a priest signifies the importance of guidance and wisdom in our lives.
    • The phrase "most excellent and worthy of worship" emphasizes the reverence for divine forces that guide us.
    • By asking for Agni's divine words to bring prosperity, the mantra highlights the connection between spiritual wisdom and material success.
  • Real-Life Application:
    • In daily life, this mantra can be a reminder to seek wisdom and guidance in our decisions, much like consulting a mentor or a trusted advisor.
    • It encourages individuals to cultivate a mindset of reverence for knowledge and the pursuit of truth, which can lead to personal and professional growth.
    • In moments of uncertainty, reciting this mantra can serve as a source of inspiration, helping individuals to align their thoughts with higher ideals.
    • In communal settings, such as family gatherings or ceremonies, invoking Agni can symbolize the collective aspiration for prosperity and harmony.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking divine guidance and wisdom in our lives.
    • It serves as a reminder of the importance of spiritual values in achieving material success and personal fulfillment.
    • By integrating the principles of this mantra into our daily lives, we can foster a deeper connection with the divine and enhance our overall well-being.
