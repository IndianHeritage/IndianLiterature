भवाद्युम्नī वाध्र्यश्वोऽत गोपामात्वा तārīd अभिमातिर जनानाम् |शूर इव धृष्णुश्च्यवनः सुमित्रः प्र नु वोचं वाध्र्यश्वस्य नाम ||
bhavā dyumnī vādhryaśvota gopā mā tvā tārīd abhimātir janānām | śūra iva dhṛṣṇuś cyavanaḥ sumitraḥ pra nu vocaṁ vādhryaśvasya nāma ||
  • bhavā dyumnī vādhryaśvota gopā mā tvā tārīd abhimātir janānām
    • bhavā - This word can be interpreted as "be" or "existence." It signifies the essence of being and the importance of presence in the world.
    • dyumnī - This translates to "glorious" or "illustrious." It emphasizes the value of honor and reputation in society.
    • vādhryaśvota - This term refers to "the one who is strong and powerful." It symbolizes strength and resilience, qualities that are essential for overcoming challenges.
    • gopā - Meaning "protector" or "guardian," it highlights the role of individuals who safeguard others, akin to a shepherd protecting their flock.
    • mā tvā tārīd - This phrase can be understood as "may you not be forsaken." It reflects a desire for support and companionship in times of need.
    • abhimātir janānām - This translates to "the desire of the people." It signifies the collective aspirations and wishes of society.
  • śūra iva dhṛṣṇuś cyavanaḥ sumitraḥ pra nu vocaṁ vādhryaśvasya nāma
    • śūra - This means "brave" or "valiant." It represents courage and the spirit of heroism that inspires individuals to act for the greater good.
    • iva - Translated as "like" or "as," it draws a comparison, indicating that the qualities being discussed are akin to those of a hero.
    • dhṛṣṇuś - This term refers to "the one who is determined." It emphasizes the importance of perseverance and steadfastness in achieving goals.
    • cyavanaḥ - A name that signifies wisdom and insight, representing the value of knowledge and understanding in navigating life's challenges.
    • sumitraḥ - Meaning "good friend" or "well-wisher," it highlights the significance of supportive relationships in our lives.
    • pra nu vocaṁ - This phrase can be interpreted as "let us speak" or "let us express." It encourages open communication and sharing of thoughts and feelings.
    • vādhryaśvasya nāma - This translates to "the name of the strong and powerful." It reinforces the idea of recognizing and honoring strength and resilience.

Conclusion:

The mantra emphasizes the importance of strength, courage, and community support in our lives. It calls for the recognition of individual and collective aspirations, urging us to be guardians of one another and to foster relationships built on trust and mutual respect. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as:

  • In leadership roles, where the qualities of bravery and determination are essential for guiding teams and organizations.
  • In personal relationships, where being a protector and a good friend can strengthen bonds and create a supportive environment.
  • In community initiatives, where collective aspirations can lead to positive change and development.

Ultimately, this mantra serves as a reminder of the values that bind us together and the strength we can draw from one another in our journey through life.
