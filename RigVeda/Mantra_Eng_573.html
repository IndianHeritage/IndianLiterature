एषायुक्त परावतः सूर्यस्योदयनाद अधि |शतम् रथेभिः सुभगोषा इयं वि यात्य अभि मानवशान् ||
eṣāyukta parāvataḥ sūryasyodayanād adhi | śataṁ rathebhiḥ subhagoṣā iyaṁ vi yāty abhi mānuṣān ||
  • eṣāyukta: This term can be translated as "this is connected" or "this is united." It signifies the importance of unity and connection in various aspects of life.
  • parāvataḥ: This word refers to "the highest" or "the supreme." It indicates the pursuit of higher ideals and aspirations.
  • sūryasyodayanād: This phrase means "from the rising of the sun." The sun is often associated with knowledge, enlightenment, and the dispelling of darkness.
  • adhi: This word translates to "above" or "over." It suggests a sense of elevation or transcendence.
  • śataṁ: Meaning "hundred," this term can symbolize abundance and prosperity.
  • rathebhiḥ: This refers to "chariots" or "vehicles." In a metaphorical sense, it can represent the means or tools we use to achieve our goals.
  • subhagoṣā: This term means "auspicious sound" or "good fortune." It emphasizes the importance of positive energy and good vibrations in our lives.
  • iyaṁ: This word translates to "this" and points to the present moment or the current situation.
  • vi yāty: This phrase means "goes forth" or "moves ahead." It signifies progress and the journey of life.
  • abhi mānuṣān: This translates to "towards humans" or "for mankind." It highlights the mantra's relevance to human existence and the collective journey of humanity.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of unity, higher aspirations, and the pursuit of knowledge. It encourages individuals to harness their tools and resources (like chariots) to move forward positively in life. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various contexts, such as teamwork in a workplace, where collaboration and shared goals lead to success. It also resonates in personal development, where individuals strive for enlightenment and growth, moving towards a brighter future for themselves and society. The mantra serves as a reminder that our journey is interconnected, and by uplifting ourselves, we contribute to the greater good of humanity.
