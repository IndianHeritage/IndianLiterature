ते 'रुणेभिर वरम्āpिṣaṅगैः शुभे कं यान्ति रथतूरभिर अश्वैः |रुक्मो न चित्रः स्वधितीवान पव्यā रथस्य जङ्घनन्त भूमा ||
te 'ruṇebhir varam ā piśaṅgaiḥ śubhe kaṁ yānti rathatūrbhir aśvaiḥ | rukmo na citraḥ svadhitīvān pavyā rathasya jaṅghananta bhūma ||
  • te 'ruṇebhir varam ā piśaṅgaiḥ: This phrase can be translated as "May they (the horses) come to us, adorned with beautiful colors." Here, "te" refers to the horses, "ruṇebhir" means adorned or decorated, "varam" signifies the best or the finest, and "piśaṅgaiḥ" refers to the beautiful colors, often associated with the golden or yellow hues.
  • śubhe kaṁ yānti rathatūrbhir aśvaiḥ: This part translates to "They who are auspicious, who go towards the chariot with the horses." "śubhe" means auspicious or good, "kaṁ" refers to whom or what, "yānti" means to go or move, "rathatūrbhir" refers to the chariot, and "aśvaiḥ" means with horses.
  • rukmo na citraḥ svadhitīvān: This can be interpreted as "The one who is golden and not colorful, who is self-illuminated." "rukmo" means golden, "na citraḥ" means not colorful or not varied, and "svadhitīvān" refers to being self-illuminated or having its own light.
  • pavyā rathasya jaṅghananta bhūma: This translates to "The one who is the foundation of the chariot, the one who supports it." "pavyā" means foundation or support, "rathasya" refers to the chariot, "jaṅghananta" means the one who is the base or the legs, and "bhūma" means earth or ground.

  • Significance: This mantra emphasizes the importance of strength, beauty, and auspiciousness in the context of a chariot, which symbolizes progress and movement in life. The horses represent energy and vitality, while the chariot signifies the journey of life.
  • Real-life Application: In real life, this mantra can be applied to various aspects such as personal growth, career advancement, and spiritual journeys. For instance, when embarking on a new project or phase in life, one can invoke the qualities of strength and auspiciousness to ensure a successful journey. Just as a chariot needs strong horses to move forward, individuals need determination and support to achieve their goals.

  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a reminder to seek strength and auspiciousness in our endeavors. It encourages individuals to recognize the importance of a solid foundation and the right energy to propel them forward in their life's journey. By embodying these qualities, one can navigate through challenges and achieve success.
