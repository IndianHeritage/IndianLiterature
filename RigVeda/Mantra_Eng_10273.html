वयं इन्द्र त्वायवः सखित्वं आ रभामहे |ऋतस्य नः पथā नयाति विश्वानि दुरितानि नभन्ताम् अन्यकेषां ज्याकाऽधि धन्वसु ||
vayam indra tvāyavaḥ sakhitvam ā rabhāmahe | ṛtasya naḥ pathā nayāti viśvāni duritā nabhantām anyakeṣāṁ jyākā adhi dhanvasu ||
  • Vayam Indra tvāyavaḥ: This phrase translates to "We, O Indra, are your friends." Here, Indra is addressed as a powerful deity, symbolizing strength and leadership. In real life, this can be seen in the context of teamwork and collaboration, where individuals come together to support a common leader or cause.
  • Sakhitvam ā rabhāmahe: This means "We seek friendship." It emphasizes the importance of building relationships and alliances. In modern scenarios, this can relate to networking in professional environments, where forming friendships can lead to opportunities and mutual support.
  • Ṛtasya naḥ pathā: This translates to "May the path of truth lead us." Here, 'ṛta' signifies truth and order. In everyday life, this can be applied to ethical decision-making, where individuals strive to follow a path of honesty and integrity in their actions.
  • Nayāti viśvāni duritā: This means "May it lead us away from all obstacles." It reflects the desire for guidance in overcoming challenges. In real life, this can be seen in personal development, where individuals seek mentorship or guidance to navigate difficulties in their lives.
  • Nabhantām anyakeṣāṁ: This translates to "From the heavens and from all other directions." It signifies the universal nature of support and guidance. In practical terms, this can relate to seeking help from various sources, whether it be friends, family, or community resources.
  • Jyākā adhi dhanvasu: This means "May we be victorious in our endeavors." It expresses a wish for success and triumph. In real life, this can be applied to personal goals, where individuals aspire to achieve their dreams and ambitions through hard work and perseverance.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking friendship, truth, guidance, and success. Its significance lies in the universal principles of collaboration, ethical living, and the pursuit of goals. In real life, it serves as a reminder to foster relationships, uphold integrity, and strive for victory in our personal and communal endeavors. By embodying these values, individuals can create a supportive environment that leads to collective growth and achievement.
