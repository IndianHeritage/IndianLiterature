अभ्यारम् इद अद्रयो निषिक्तं पुष्करे मधु |अवतस्य विसर्जने ||
abhyāram id adrayo niṣiktam puṣkare madhu | avatasya visarjane ||
  • Mantra: "abhyāram id adrayo niṣiktam puṣkare madhu | avatasya visarjane ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • abhyāram: This can be interpreted as "to be offered" or "to be dedicated."
    • id: This means "this" or "here."
    • adrayo: Refers to "mountains" or "hills."
    • niṣiktam: Means "anointed" or "smeared."
    • puṣkare: Translates to "in the lotus" or "in the pure."
    • madhu: Means "honey," symbolizing sweetness or nourishment.
    • avatasya: Refers to "of the one who is seated" or "of the one who is resting."
    • visarjane: Means "in the act of releasing" or "in the act of letting go."
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of offering something sweet and nourishing (madhu) that has been anointed (niṣiktam) on the mountains (adrayo) and is associated with purity (puṣkare).
    • The act of releasing (visarjane) suggests a process of letting go or surrendering, which can be interpreted as a spiritual practice or a ritualistic offering.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a spiritual context, this mantra can be used during rituals where offerings are made to deities, symbolizing the dedication of one's efforts and the sweetness of life.
    • In personal development, the idea of "letting go" can be applied when individuals release negative thoughts or past experiences, allowing for personal growth and healing.
    • In nature, the act of offering honey can symbolize the sweetness of life and the importance of nurturing relationships, much like how bees contribute to the ecosystem.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of offerings, purity, and the act of letting go. It serves as a reminder to appreciate the sweetness in life while also recognizing the importance of releasing burdens that no longer serve us.
    • In real-life scenarios, it encourages individuals to engage in practices that promote spiritual growth, emotional healing, and the nurturing of relationships, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling life.
