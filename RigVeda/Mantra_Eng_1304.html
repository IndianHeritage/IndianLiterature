युवं नराः स्तुवते कृष्णियाय विष्णुपवं ददथुर विश्वकाय |घोषायै चित् पितृषदे दुरोणे पतिं जूर्यन्त्याः अश्विनाव् अदत्तम् ||
yuvaṁ narā stuvate kṛṣṇiyāya viṣṇāpvaṁ dadathur viśvakāya | ghoṣāyai cit pitṛṣade duroṇe patiṁ jūryantyā aśvināv adattam ||
  • yuvaṁ narā stuvate: This phrase translates to "the young men praise." It signifies the importance of youth and their role in society. In real life, this can be seen in various cultures where young people are encouraged to participate in community activities, showcasing their talents and energy.
  • kṛṣṇiyāya viṣṇāpvaṁ: This part refers to the "black (or dark) one, Vishnu." Vishnu is often associated with preservation and protection. In modern contexts, this can relate to environmental conservation efforts, where individuals or groups work to protect nature, akin to how Vishnu preserves the universe.
  • dadathur viśvakāya: This means "they give to all." It emphasizes generosity and the act of sharing. In today's world, this can be reflected in charitable organizations and community service, where people come together to support those in need.
  • ghoṣāyai cit pitṛṣade: This translates to "for the call of the ancestors." It highlights the importance of honoring one's heritage and ancestors. In real life, many cultures celebrate festivals or rituals that pay homage to their forebears, fostering a sense of identity and continuity.
  • duroṇe patiṁ: This phrase means "the lord of the strong." It signifies strength and leadership. In contemporary society, this can be seen in leaders who inspire and empower others, whether in politics, business, or community initiatives.
  • jūryantyā aśvināv adattam: This translates to "the two Ashvins, who are the givers." The Ashvins are twin deities associated with health and medicine. This can be related to modern healthcare professionals who dedicate their lives to healing and improving the well-being of others.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the values of youth, preservation, generosity, honoring ancestors, strength in leadership, and the importance of health. In real life, these principles can guide individuals and communities in fostering a supportive and thriving society. By embodying these values, we can create a world that respects its past while actively working towards a better future for all.
