याभिरन्तकं जसमानमआरणे भुज्युं याभिरव्यथिभिरजिजिन्वतुः |याभिः कर्कन्धुं वय्यं च जिन्वथस्ताभिरूषु ऊतिभिरअश्विनागतम् ||
yābhir antakaṁ jasamānam āraṇe bhujyuṁ yābhir avyathibhir jijinvathuḥ | yābhiḥ karkandhuṁ vayyaṁ ca jinvathas tābhir ū ṣu ūtibhir aśvinā gatam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yābhir: By whom
    • antakaṁ: The destroyer (often referring to death)
    • jasamānam: The one who is engaged in battle or conflict
    • āraṇe: In the forest or wilderness
    • bhujyuṁ: The one who is strong or powerful
    • avyathibhir: Without fear or anxiety
    • jijinvathuḥ: May they live or thrive
    • karkandhuṁ: The one who is fierce or aggressive
    • vayyaṁ: The one who is capable or able
    • jinvathas: May they live or thrive
    • tābhir: By those
    • ū: With strength
    • ṣu: Good or auspicious
    • ūtibhir: With vigor or energy
    • aśvinā: O Ashvins (the twin deities)
    • gatam: Having gone or having passed
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the divine powers of the Ashvins, who are known as the twin gods of health and medicine in Vedic literature.
    • It speaks about overcoming fear and thriving in the face of challenges, symbolized by the fierce and powerful entities in the wilderness.
    • The mantra emphasizes the importance of strength, courage, and the ability to confront adversities without fear.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, employees may face challenges such as tight deadlines or difficult projects. This mantra can inspire them to approach these challenges with confidence and resilience.
    • In personal life, individuals may encounter health issues or emotional struggles. The invocation of strength and fearlessness can motivate them to seek help and overcome their difficulties.
    • In sports, athletes often face intense competition. This mantra can serve as a reminder to maintain focus and determination, pushing through obstacles to achieve their goals.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for strength and fearlessness in the face of life's challenges.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, encouraging individuals to confront their fears and thrive despite adversities.
    • By invoking the blessings of the Ashvins, one can cultivate resilience, health, and the ability to navigate through life's battles with courage.
