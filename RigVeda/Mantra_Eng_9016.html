यजामहे इन्द्रं वज्रदक्षिणं हरिणां रथ्यं विवृतानाम् |प्र श्मश्रु डोधुवद ऊर्ध्वथा भूद् वि सेनाभिर दयमानो वि राधसाः ||
yajāmaha indraṁ vajradakṣiṇaṁ harīṇāṁ rathya1ṁ vivratānām | pra śmaśru dodhuvad ūrdhvathā bhūd vi senābhir dayamāno vi rādhasā ||
  • yajāmaha: This term translates to "we worship" or "we invoke." It signifies the act of devotion and reverence towards a deity.
  • indraṁ: Refers to Indra, the king of the gods in Vedic mythology, often associated with rain, thunder, and strength. Indra represents power and the ability to overcome obstacles.
  • vajradakṣiṇaṁ: This means "with the thunderbolt in his hand." The thunderbolt symbolizes strength and the ability to destroy evil or negativity.
  • harīṇāṁ: This term means "of the horses." In Vedic culture, horses are often associated with speed, power, and nobility.
  • rathyaṁ: Refers to "the chariot." The chariot symbolizes movement, progress, and the journey of life.
  • vivratānām: This means "of those who are open" or "who have revealed." It signifies transparency and honesty in actions.
  • pra śmaśru: This term translates to "with the beard." It can symbolize wisdom and maturity.
  • dodhuvad: Means "like a cow." Cows are often seen as symbols of nourishment and abundance in Vedic culture.
  • ūrdhvathā: This means "upward" or "towards the higher." It signifies aspiration and the pursuit of higher goals.
  • bhūd: This translates to "may it be" or "let it be." It expresses a wish or desire for something to manifest.
  • vi senābhir: This means "with the armies." It signifies strength in numbers and the importance of community and support.
  • dayamāno: This translates to "being compassionate." It emphasizes the importance of kindness and empathy in our actions.
  • vi rādhasā: This means "with the radiance." It signifies brilliance, positivity, and the light of knowledge.

Conclusion: This mantra is a powerful invocation to Indra, seeking his blessings for strength, progress, and compassion. It emphasizes the importance of community, transparency, and the pursuit of higher goals. In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as during challenging times when one seeks strength and support from others, or when striving for personal growth and enlightenment. It serves as a reminder to be compassionate and to work together towards common goals, embodying the values of unity and strength in our daily lives.
