अतीदु शक्र ओहात इन्द्रो विश्वा अति द्विषः |भिनत कनीन ओदनं पच्यमानं परो गिरा ||
atīd u śakra ohata indro viśvā ati dviṣaḥ | bhinat kanīna odanam pacyamānam paro girā ||
  • atīd u śakra: This phrase can be translated as "O Indra, the powerful one." Here, "Indra" refers to the king of the gods in Vedic tradition, symbolizing strength and authority.
  • ohata indro viśvā ati dviṣaḥ: This translates to "who overcomes all enemies." It emphasizes the idea of triumph over adversaries, suggesting that with strength and wisdom, one can conquer challenges.
  • bhinat kanīna odanam: This means "breaking the bonds of the weak." It signifies the act of liberating oneself or others from limitations or obstacles that hinder progress.
  • pacyamānam paro girā: This translates to "cooked by the supreme word." It implies that through divine speech or wisdom, one can achieve transformation and growth.

The mantra as a whole can be interpreted as a call to Indra, seeking strength and guidance to overcome obstacles and adversaries. It highlights the importance of divine support in achieving success and breaking free from limitations.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios:

  • Personal Challenges: When facing difficulties in life, such as career setbacks or personal struggles, invoking the strength of Indra can serve as a reminder to seek inner strength and resilience.
  • Conflict Resolution: In situations of conflict, whether in personal relationships or professional environments, the mantra encourages individuals to rise above negativity and find constructive solutions.
  • Self-Improvement: The idea of breaking free from limitations can inspire individuals to pursue personal growth, whether through education, skill development, or overcoming fears.

In conclusion, this mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the strength that lies within and the importance of seeking divine guidance in overcoming life's challenges. It encourages individuals to harness their inner power, confront adversities, and strive for personal and collective growth.
