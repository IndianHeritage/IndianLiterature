मात्सि नो वस्यैष्टय इन्द्रं इन्दो वृषा विशा |ऋघायमान इन्बसि शत्रुम अन्ति न विन्दसि ||
matsi no vasyaïṣṭaya indram indo vṛṣā viśa | ṛghāyamāṇa invasi śatrum anti na vindasi ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • matsi: "May we be endowed" or "May we be blessed."
    • no: "Our."
    • vasyaïṣṭaya: "With the best of strength" or "With the best of power."
    • indram: "Indra," the king of gods, representing strength and power.
    • indo: "Of Indra," indicating a connection to divine strength.
    • vṛṣā: "The one who is strong" or "The powerful one."
    • viśa: "In the world" or "In the universe."
    • ṛghāyamāṇa: "Invoking" or "Calling upon."
    • invasi: "You come" or "You arrive."
    • śatrum: "The enemy" or "Adversary."
    • anti: "Against" or "In opposition to."
    • na: "Not."
    • vindasi: "You find" or "You achieve."
  • Overall Meaning:
    • This mantra is a prayer for strength and protection. It seeks the blessings of Indra, the god of rain and thunder, who symbolizes power and victory.
    • The invocation is aimed at overcoming adversities and enemies, asking for divine support to ensure that one does not encounter defeat.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a competitive environment, such as a workplace or academic setting, individuals often seek strength and support to overcome challenges. This mantra can be recited to invoke confidence and resilience.
    • During times of personal struggle, such as health issues or emotional distress, individuals may find solace in this mantra, using it as a reminder to seek inner strength and divine assistance.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine strength and protection against adversities. It emphasizes the importance of seeking help from higher powers in times of need.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applicable in various situations where individuals face challenges, whether in personal life, professional endeavors, or during times of crisis.
    • By reciting this mantra, one can cultivate a sense of empowerment and resilience, fostering a belief that with divine support, obstacles can be overcome.
