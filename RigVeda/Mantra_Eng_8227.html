अर्षा सोम द्युमत्तमोऽभि द्रोणानि रोर्वत् |सीदञ्च छ्येनो न योनिम् आ ||
arṣā soma dyumattamo 'bhi droṇāni roruvat | sīdañ chyeno na yonim ā ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • arṣā: from the heavens or celestial realms
    • soma: the sacred drink, often associated with immortality and divine inspiration
    • dyumattamo: the most radiant or luminous
    • 'bhi: with or by
    • droṇāni: the containers or vessels
    • roruvat: that which is flowing or streaming
    • sīdañ: to sit or settle
    • chyeno: to be cut off or separated
    • na: not
    • yonim: the womb or source
    • ā: towards or to
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the celestial essence of Soma, representing divine inspiration and the life-giving properties of nature.
    • The phrase "arṣā soma dyumattamo" suggests a connection to the highest, most radiant aspects of the cosmos, emphasizing the importance of seeking enlightenment and higher knowledge.
    • "Droṇāni roruvat" refers to the vessels that contain this divine essence, symbolizing the various forms through which inspiration and life flow into our existence.
    • The latter part, "sīdañ chyeno na yonim ā," indicates a desire to remain connected to the source of life and creativity, avoiding separation from it.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, this mantra can be related to the pursuit of knowledge and wisdom. For instance, students and scholars often seek inspiration from various sources, much like the vessels that contain Soma.
    • Artists and creators may find their muse in nature or the cosmos, drawing from the "dyumattamo" to produce works that resonate with deeper truths.
    • In moments of meditation or reflection, individuals may strive to connect with their inner selves, ensuring they do not become "chyeno" or cut off from their creative source.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of connecting with the divine and the sources of inspiration in our lives.
    • It serves as a reminder to seek enlightenment and to remain grounded in our creative pursuits, ensuring that we do not lose touch with the essence of life.
    • In practical terms, it encourages individuals to cultivate a relationship with nature, knowledge, and creativity, fostering a holistic approach to personal and spiritual growth.
