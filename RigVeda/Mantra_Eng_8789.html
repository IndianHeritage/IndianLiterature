यत्र ब्रह्मा पवमान चन्दस्यां वाचं वदन |ग्राव्णा सोमे महीयते सोमेानन्दं जनयन्न इन्द्रायन्दो परि श्रव ||
yatra brahmā pavamāna chandasyā3ṁ vācaṁ vadan | grāvṇā some mahīyate somenānandaṁ janayann indrāyendo pari srava ||
  • yatra - where
  • brahmā - the creator or the ultimate reality
  • pavamāna - the purifying or the sacred
  • chandasyāṁ - in the verses or the poetic expressions
  • vācaṁ - speech or words
  • vadan - speaking or uttering
  • grāvṇā - through the offerings or the sacrifices
  • some - the moon or the soma (a sacred drink)
  • mahīyate - becomes great or is exalted
  • somenānandaṁ - through the joy of the soma
  • janayann - generating or producing
  • indrāya - for Indra, the king of gods
  • indo - of Indra
  • pari - all around or completely
  • srava - flow or abundance

This mantra speaks of a sacred space where the ultimate reality (Brahman) is present, and where the purifying essence of speech is expressed through poetic verses. It emphasizes the importance of offerings, particularly the soma, which symbolizes joy and divine connection. The mantra invokes Indra, the king of gods, highlighting the significance of divine blessings and abundance in our lives.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various contexts:

  • Spiritual Practices: Engaging in meditation or prayer where one seeks to connect with the divine and purify their thoughts and words.
  • Celebrations and Rituals: During festivals or significant life events, offerings can be made to invoke blessings and joy, similar to the offerings of soma in ancient rituals.
  • Creative Expression: The importance of words and poetry in expressing emotions and connecting with others can be seen in literature, music, and art.

Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for purity, joy, and divine connection. It reminds us of the power of words and offerings in our spiritual and everyday lives. By embracing these principles, we can cultivate a sense of abundance and connection with the divine, leading to a more fulfilling existence.
