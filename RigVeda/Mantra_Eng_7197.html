मन्त्र: महि वो महताम् अवो वरुण मित्रार्यमन् |अवांस्य आ वृणीमहे ||
mahi vo mahatām avo varuṇa mitrāryaman | avāṁsy ā vṛṇīmahe ||
  • Word Breakdown:
    • mahi: great, vast, or powerful.
    • vo: your.
    • mahatām: of the great ones.
    • avo: may we invoke or call upon.
    • varuṇa: the god of cosmic order and water.
    • mitrāryaman: the divine beings associated with friendship and harmony.
    • avāṁsy: our desires or aspirations.
    • ā: towards or for.
    • vṛṇīmahe: we choose or we select.
  • Meaning:
    • This mantra is a prayer invoking the great powers and deities, specifically Varuna and Mitra, to guide and bless our aspirations.
    • It emphasizes the importance of seeking the support of higher powers in our endeavors and the significance of friendship and harmony in achieving our goals.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, a team may recite this mantra before a major project to invoke the support of higher powers for success and to foster teamwork and collaboration.
    • During personal challenges, individuals may reflect on this mantra to remind themselves of the importance of seeking guidance and maintaining harmonious relationships with others.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine assistance and the recognition of the interconnectedness of all beings.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, such as in personal growth, teamwork, and community building, where invoking higher powers and fostering harmony can lead to success and fulfillment.
