प्र स्तोषद उप गासिषच छ्रवत साम गीयमानम् |अभि राधसाज जुगुरत ||
pra stoṣad upa gāsiṣac chravat sāma gīyamānam | abhi rādhasā jugurat ||
  • pra stoṣad: This phrase can be interpreted as "praise" or "to be praised." It signifies the act of acknowledging and honoring the divine or the forces of nature.
  • upa gāsiṣac: This translates to "coming near" or "approaching." It suggests a closeness or intimacy with the divine or the spiritual realm.
  • chravat: This word means "to flow" or "to move." It can be associated with the flow of energy, thoughts, or even the flow of life itself.
  • sāma gīyamānam: This refers to "the song of the Sāma Veda," which is a collection of melodies and hymns. It emphasizes the importance of music and sound in spiritual practices.
  • abhi rādhasā: This can be interpreted as "towards the abundance" or "towards prosperity." It indicates a direction towards growth and fulfillment.
  • jugurat: This word means "to be strong" or "to be powerful." It signifies the strength that comes from devotion and connection to the divine.

The mantra as a whole can be understood as a call to praise and connect with the divine through music and song, while also seeking abundance and strength in life. It emphasizes the importance of approaching the divine with reverence and the transformative power of sound and melody.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios:

  • Spiritual Practices: Individuals can use this mantra during meditation or prayer to enhance their connection with the divine and to cultivate a sense of peace and abundance in their lives.
  • Music and Healing: The significance of sound and music in healing practices can be drawn from this mantra. For example, sound therapy or chanting can be used to promote emotional and spiritual well-being.
  • Community Gatherings: In communal settings, such as festivals or religious ceremonies, this mantra can inspire collective singing and celebration, fostering a sense of unity and shared purpose.

In conclusion, this mantra highlights the significance of praise, connection, and the transformative power of sound in our lives. It serves as a reminder to approach the divine with reverence, to seek abundance, and to harness the strength that comes from spiritual practices. By incorporating these elements into our daily lives, we can cultivate a deeper sense of fulfillment and connection to the world around us.
