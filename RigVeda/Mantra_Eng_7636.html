यद वाग् वदन्त्य अविचेतनानि राष्ट्री देवानां निषसाद मन्द्रा |चतस्र उर्जं दुदुहे पयांसि क्व स्विद अस्याः परमं जगाम ||
yad vāg vadanty avicetanāni rāṣṭrī devānāṁ niṣasāda mandrā | catasra ūrjaṁ duduhe payāṁsi kva svid asyāḥ paramaṁ jagāma ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yad: whatever
    • vāg: speech or words
    • vadanty: they speak
    • avicetanāni: those who are not conscious or aware
    • rāṣṭrī: of the nation or community
    • devānāṁ: of the gods
    • niṣasāda: sat down or settled
    • mandrā: in the assembly or gathering
    • catasra: four
    • ūrjaṁ: energy or strength
    • duduhe: they drew out or extracted
    • payāṁsi: the waters or nourishment
    • kva: where
    • svid: or where
    • asyāḥ: of this
    • paramaṁ: the highest or supreme
    • jagāma: went or reached
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the power of words and the collective consciousness of a community.
    • It suggests that even those who are not aware or conscious can contribute to the collective energy of a nation.
    • The mention of "four energies" can symbolize the four directions or elements that are essential for balance and harmony.
    • The act of drawing nourishment from the waters signifies the importance of seeking sustenance from the environment and the divine.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community meeting, even the silent members can influence the outcome through their presence and energy.
    • In environmental movements, the collective voice of individuals, even those who may not be actively participating, can lead to significant change.
    • The concept of drawing nourishment can be seen in practices like sustainable living, where communities work together to utilize natural resources responsibly.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of collective consciousness and the power of words in shaping a community.
    • It teaches us that every individual, regardless of their awareness, contributes to the larger whole.
    • In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios such as teamwork, community service, and environmental conservation, where the collective effort leads to greater outcomes.
