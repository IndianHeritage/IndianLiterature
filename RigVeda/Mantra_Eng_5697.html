अशोच्य अग्निः समिधानो अस्मे उपो अदृश्रन् तमसश्चिदन्ताः |अचेति केतुर्वुषसः पुरस्ताच्छ्रियेदिवो दूहितुरजायमानः ||
aśocy agniḥ samidhāno asme upo adṛśran tamasaś cid antāḥ | aceti ketur uṣasaḥ purastāc chriye divo duhitur jāyamānaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • aśocy: Not to be lamented
    • agniḥ: Fire
    • samidhāno: Kindled or ignited
    • asme: For us
    • upo: Near
    • adṛśran: Unseen or hidden
    • tamasaś: Of darkness
    • cid: Consciousness or awareness
    • antāḥ: Within
    • aceti: Not to be known
    • ketur: A guiding star or banner
    • uṣasaḥ: Of dawn
    • purastāt: In front or ahead
    • chriye: In the light
    • divo: Of the sky or heaven
    • duhitur: Of the mother
    • jāyamānaḥ: Being born or emerging
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the fire (Agni) that is not to be lamented, suggesting a transformative power that brings light and awareness.
    • The phrase "samidhāno asme" indicates that this fire is kindled for us, symbolizing the inner light or knowledge that guides us through darkness.
    • The reference to "tamasaś cid antāḥ" implies that within the darkness, there is consciousness, hinting at the idea that even in ignorance, awareness exists.
    • "Aceti ketur uṣasaḥ purastāc" suggests that the guiding light of dawn is ahead of us, representing hope and new beginnings.
    • The mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking knowledge and enlightenment, as it is the light that dispels darkness.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In personal development, individuals often face periods of confusion or darkness. This mantra can serve as a reminder that within those struggles, there is potential for growth and enlightenment.
    • In a professional setting, when teams encounter challenges, the "fire" of collaboration and innovation can lead to new solutions, illuminating the path forward.
    • In spiritual practices, the mantra can be chanted to invoke inner strength and clarity, helping practitioners to navigate through life's uncertainties.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its message of hope and the transformative power of knowledge and awareness.
    • It teaches us that even in the darkest times, there is a guiding light that can lead us to clarity and understanding.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra encourages individuals to seek knowledge, embrace change, and trust in the process of emergence and growth.
