अप्नस्वतीं अश्विना वाचं अस्मे कृतं नो दसरा वृषणा मनिषाम् | अद्यूत्ये 'वसे न ह्वये वां वृद्धे च नो भवतं वाजसातौ ||
apnasvatīm aśvinā vācam asme kṛtaṁ no dasrā vṛṣaṇā manīṣām | adyūtye 'vase ni hvaye vāṁ vṛdhe ca no bhavataṁ vājasātau ||
  • Mantra: "apnasvatīm aśvinā vācam asme kṛtaṁ no dasrā vṛṣaṇā manīṣām | adyūtye 'vase ni hvaye vāṁ vṛdhe ca no bhavataṁ vājasātau ||"
  • Translation: This mantra can be translated as a prayer for the blessings of the Ashvins, the divine twin horsemen, asking for their guidance and wisdom in our endeavors.
  • Word Breakdown:
    • apnasvatīm: Our own, personal.
    • aśvinā: To the Ashvins, the deities associated with health and medicine.
    • vācam: Speech or words.
    • kṛtaṁ: Done or created.
    • no: Our.
    • dasrā: Bright or shining.
    • vṛṣaṇā: With strength or power.
    • manīṣām: Wisdom or intellect.
    • adyūtye: In this moment or today.
    • 'vase: To be invoked or called upon.
    • ni hvaye: I call upon you.
    • vāṁ: You (plural).
    • vṛdhe: To grow or increase.
    • ca: And.
    • no: Our.
    • bhavataṁ: You (plural) may become.
    • vājasātau: In the context of strength and valor.
  • Significance: This mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking divine guidance and wisdom in our actions. The Ashvins are known for their healing powers and ability to bring prosperity. By invoking their blessings, we aim to enhance our intellect and strength in our endeavors.
  • Real-Life Application:
    • In personal development, individuals can recite this mantra to seek clarity and wisdom in decision-making, especially during challenging times.
    • In a professional setting, teams can use this mantra to foster collaboration and invoke creativity, ensuring that their collective efforts lead to successful outcomes.
    • In health and wellness practices, this mantra can be used to promote healing and well-being, aligning oneself with the positive energies of the Ashvins.
  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of our actions and the divine forces that guide us. By acknowledging and invoking these forces, we can enhance our personal and collective journeys, leading to growth, wisdom, and success in various aspects of life.
