इदं उ त्यत् पुरुतमम् पुरस्ताज् ज्योतिः तमसो वयुनावद् अस्तात् |नूनं दिवो दुधितरो विभातीर गातुं कृत्वन्न उषसो जनाय ||
idam u tyat purutamam purastāj jyotis tamaso vayunāvad asthāt | nūnaṁ divo duhitaro vibhātīr gātuṁ kṛṇavann uṣaso janāya ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • idam: this
    • u: indeed
    • tyat: that
    • purutamam: the most excellent
    • purastāt: in front
    • jyotis: light
    • tamaso: of darkness
    • vayunā: by the power
    • avad: has stood
    • asthāt: established
    • nūnam: surely
    • divo: of heaven
    • duhitaro: daughters
    • vibhātīr: shining
    • gātuṁ: to go
    • kṛṇavann: creating
    • uṣaso: of dawn
    • janāya: for the people
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the transition from darkness to light, symbolizing knowledge and enlightenment.
    • The phrase "purutamam purastāj jyotis tamaso" emphasizes the supremacy of light over darkness, suggesting that knowledge and wisdom are paramount.
    • The mention of "divo duhitaro vibhātīr" refers to the daughters of heaven, which can be interpreted as the various forms of enlightenment or knowledge that illuminate our lives.
    • The mantra encourages the pursuit of knowledge and the creation of a better world for all, as indicated by "janāya." This reflects the idea that enlightenment should benefit society.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In education, the pursuit of knowledge is akin to moving from darkness (ignorance) to light (understanding). Schools and universities serve as beacons of light, guiding students towards enlightenment.
    • In personal development, individuals often seek self-improvement and enlightenment through various means such as reading, meditation, or engaging in discussions, reflecting the essence of this mantra.
    • In community service, initiatives aimed at uplifting the underprivileged can be seen as creating light in the lives of those in darkness, embodying the spirit of "kṛṇavann uṣaso janāya."
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of knowledge, enlightenment, and the collective responsibility to create a better world.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from education to personal growth and community service, reminding us that the pursuit of knowledge is a noble endeavor that benefits all.
    • Ultimately, it encourages us to strive for light in our lives and the lives of others, fostering a society that values wisdom and understanding.
