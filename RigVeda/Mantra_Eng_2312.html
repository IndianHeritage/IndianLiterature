अस्माकं मित्रावरुणावतं रथमादित्यै रुद्रैः वसुभिः साचाभुवाः |प्र यद वयो न पप्तन वस्मनसः परि श्रवस्यवो हृषीवन्तो वनर्षदः ||
asmākam mitrāvaruṇāvataṁ ratham ādityai rudrair vasubhiḥ sacābhuvā | pra yad vayo na paptan vasmanas pari śravasyavo hṛṣīvanto vanarṣadaḥ ||
  • Translation: "May our chariot, driven by Mitra and Varuna, be blessed by the Adityas, Rudras, and Vasus. As we do not fall into the snares of old age, may we be filled with strength and vigor, like the deer in the forest."
  • Key Deities:
    • Mitra: Represents friendship, harmony, and the social order.
    • Varuna: Symbolizes cosmic order, truth, and the moral law.
    • Adityas: A group of solar deities representing various aspects of life and light.
    • Rudras: Associated with storms and the destructive aspects of nature, but also with healing.
    • Vasus: Represent the elemental forces of nature, such as earth, water, and fire.
  • Imagery of the Chariot: The chariot in this mantra symbolizes the journey of life. Just as a chariot needs strong horses and a skilled driver, our lives require support from divine forces and our own efforts to navigate challenges.
  • Real-Life Example: In a modern context, this mantra can be likened to the support systems we build in our lives—friends, family, mentors, and community. For instance, when starting a new business, having a network of supportive individuals can help steer the venture towards success, much like the divine forces guiding the chariot.
  • Concept of Aging: The phrase "as we do not fall into the snares of old age" emphasizes the desire for vitality and longevity. It reflects a universal human aspiration to maintain health and vigor throughout life.
  • Real-Life Application: This can be seen in the practices of yoga and meditation, which many people adopt to enhance their physical and mental well-being, aiming to live a fulfilling life free from the limitations often associated with aging.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its invocation of divine support for a prosperous and vigorous life journey. It teaches us the importance of community, the pursuit of truth, and the need for strength in facing life's challenges. In real life, it encourages us to seek harmony in our relationships and to cultivate resilience, ensuring that we navigate our paths with grace and vitality.
