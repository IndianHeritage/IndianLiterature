अदब्दस्य स्वधावतो दूतस्य रेबतः सदा |अग्नेः सख्यं वृणीमहे ||
adabdhasya svadhāvato dūtasya rebhataḥ sadā | agneḥ sakhyaṁ vṛṇīmahe ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • adabdhasya: of the one who is unblemished or pure
    • svadhāvato: of the one who moves in its own way or is self-driven
    • dūtasya: of the messenger or envoy
    • rebhataḥ: of the one who is always present or active
    • sadā: always
    • agneḥ: of Agni, the fire god
    • sakhyaṁ: friendship or companionship
    • vṛṇīmahe: we choose or we invoke
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of invoking the divine presence of Agni, the fire god, who is seen as a pure and self-driven force.
    • Agni is often regarded as a messenger between humans and the divine, symbolizing the connection and communication between the two realms.
    • The phrase "of the one who is unblemished" suggests a call for purity in intentions and actions, emphasizing the importance of integrity in our endeavors.
    • By invoking Agni's friendship, the mantra highlights the significance of building relationships based on trust and mutual respect.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, this mantra can be applied when forming partnerships or collaborations. Just as Agni serves as a messenger, effective communication is crucial in any relationship.
    • In personal relationships, the emphasis on purity and integrity can guide individuals to foster honest and supportive connections with friends and family.
    • In spiritual practices, invoking Agni can symbolize the purification of one's thoughts and actions, encouraging individuals to seek clarity and purpose in their lives.
  • Conclusion:
    • The mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of purity, integrity, and communication in our lives.
    • By invoking Agni, we seek to establish a connection with the divine that is based on friendship and mutual respect.
    • In real-life scenarios, this mantra encourages individuals to approach their relationships—be it personal, professional, or spiritual—with sincerity and a commitment to fostering positive connections.
