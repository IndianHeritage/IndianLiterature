आयā पवā पवस्वैना वसūनि मām̐श्चत्व इन्दो सरसि प्र धन्वा |ब्रध्नश्चिदात्र वातो न जūतः पुरुमेधश्चित्तकवे नरं दात ||
ayā pavā pavasvainā vasūni mām̐ścatva indo sarasi pra dhanva | bradhnaś cid atra vāto na jūtaḥ purumedhaś cit takave naraṁ dāt ||
  • ayā pavā pavasvainā: This phrase can be interpreted as a call to the wind or the life force. "Ayā" signifies a request or invocation, while "pavā" refers to the wind or air, which is often associated with vitality and movement.
  • vasūni mām̐ścatva: "Vasūni" refers to the wealth or abundance, and "mām̐ścatva" can be understood as a plea for these riches to come to the speaker. This reflects a desire for prosperity and well-being.
  • indo sarasi pra dhanva: "Indo" refers to the moon or a divine being, while "sarasi" means in the lake or water. "Pra dhanva" suggests a request for the best or the most valuable. This line can symbolize seeking divine blessings for abundance and clarity.
  • bradhnaś cid atra vāto na jūtaḥ: "Bradhnaś" refers to the strong or powerful, while "cid" means consciousness or awareness. "Atra vāto na jūtaḥ" implies that the wind or life force is not bound here. This can signify the importance of being free and aware in one's actions.
  • purumedhaś cit takave naraṁ dāt: "Purumedhaś" refers to the wise or enlightened person, "cit" means consciousness, and "takave naraṁ dāt" can be interpreted as a request to grant wisdom or insight to a person. This emphasizes the value of wisdom in navigating life.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda encapsulates a deep yearning for vitality, prosperity, and wisdom. It highlights the interconnectedness of nature, the divine, and human consciousness. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts such as:

  • In personal development, invoking the essence of the wind can symbolize the pursuit of freedom and vitality in one's life.
  • In financial endeavors, the plea for "vasūni" can be a reminder to seek abundance and prosperity through hard work and ethical means.
  • In spiritual practices, the call for divine blessings can serve as a meditation on the importance of wisdom and awareness in decision-making.

Overall, this mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of seeking balance between material wealth, spiritual wisdom, and the natural forces that influence our lives.
