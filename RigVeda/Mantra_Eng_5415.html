त्वं विश्वस्य धनदाऽसि श्रुतो य इम्भवन्त्य आजयः |तवायं विश्वः पुरूहूत पार्थिवोऽवस्युर्नाम भिक्षते ||
tvaṁ viśvasya dhanadā asi śruto ya īm bhavanty ājayaḥ | tavāyaṁ viśvaḥ puruhūta pārthivo 'vasyur nāma bhikṣate ||
  • tvaṁ - You
  • viśvasya - of the universe
  • dhanadā - the giver of wealth
  • asi - are
  • śruto - known
  • ya - who
  • īm - those who
  • bhavanty - become
  • ājayaḥ - victorious
  • tavāyaṁ - your
  • viśvaḥ - the whole world
  • puruhūta - many names
  • pārthivo - earthly
  • 'vasyur - is under your control
  • nāma - name
  • bhikṣate - seeks

This mantra can be translated as: "You are the giver of wealth of the universe, known by those who become victorious. Your name is sought by the whole world, which is under your control."
The mantra emphasizes the divine nature of abundance and victory, suggesting that the source of wealth and success is a higher power. It acknowledges the interconnectedness of the universe and the role of the divine in providing for all beings.


In real life, this can be seen in various contexts:

  • Wealth and Prosperity: Many people turn to spirituality or prayer when seeking financial stability or success in their careers. For example, entrepreneurs often invoke blessings before starting a new venture, believing that divine support can lead to prosperity.
  • Victory in Challenges: Athletes and students often seek motivation and strength from a higher power when facing competition or exams. The belief that they are supported by a greater force can instill confidence and lead to success.
  • Global Unity: The mantra speaks to the idea that the entire world is under divine control, which can inspire individuals to work towards global harmony and cooperation, recognizing that we are all part of a larger whole.

Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its reminder of the divine source of wealth and victory. It encourages individuals to seek connection with the higher power that governs the universe, fostering a sense of gratitude and responsibility. In practical terms, it can inspire people to approach their endeavors with faith, recognizing that their efforts are supported by a greater force, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling and prosperous life.
