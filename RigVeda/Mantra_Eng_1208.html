आ तक्षत सात्म asmभ्यम ऋभवः सातीं रथाय सात्म अर्वते नरः |सातीं नो जैत्रीं सम महेत विश्वहा जामिम अजामिम पृथनासु सक्षणिम ||
ā takṣata sātim asmabhyam ṛbhavaḥ sātiṁ rathāya sātim arvate naraḥ | sātiṁ no jaitrīṁ sam maheta viśvahā jāmim ajāmim pṛtanāsu sakṣaṇim ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā takṣata: "Do not break" or "Do not harm."
    • sātim: "Truth" or "Righteousness."
    • asmabhyam: "For us" or "To us."
    • ṛbhavaḥ: "Be favorable" or "Be kind."
    • sātiṁ: "Truth" or "Righteousness" (repeated for emphasis).
    • rathāya: "For the chariot" or "For the journey."
    • sātim: "Truth" or "Righteousness" (repeated).
    • arvate: "For the one who is strong" or "For the powerful."
    • naraḥ: "Man" or "Human."
    • sātiṁ: "Truth" or "Righteousness" (repeated).
    • no: "Our" or "To us."
    • jaitrīṁ: "Victory" or "Success."
    • sam: "Together" or "In unity."
    • maheta: "May we achieve" or "May we attain."
    • viśvahā: "All" or "Universal."
    • jāmim: "The born" or "The created."
    • ajāmim: "The uncreated" or "The eternal."
    • pṛtanāsu: "In the struggles" or "In the challenges."
    • sakṣaṇim: "With strength" or "With power."
  • Overall Meaning:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of truth and righteousness in our lives.
    • It calls for protection and favor from the divine, urging that we do not stray from the path of truth.
    • The repetition of "sātim" signifies the centrality of truth in achieving success and strength.
    • It highlights the collective journey of humanity, suggesting that together we can attain victory and overcome challenges.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace, maintaining integrity and truthfulness can lead to a positive environment and collective success.
    • In personal relationships, being truthful fosters trust and strengthens bonds, allowing individuals to navigate challenges together.
    • In community efforts, when people unite with a commitment to truth and righteousness, they can achieve significant social change.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the power of truth and righteousness in our lives.
    • It encourages individuals and communities to seek favor and strength through unity and integrity.
    • In a world often filled with challenges, adhering to these principles can lead to personal and collective victories.
