सं जागृवद्भिर जरामाण इध्यते दमे दमुना इषयन्न इळस पदे |विश्वस्य होता हविषो वरेण्यो विभुर विभावा सुशखā सखीयते ||
saṁ jāgṛvadbhir jaramāṇa idhyate dame damūnā iṣayann iḻas pade | viśvasya hotā haviṣo vareṇyo vibhur vibhāvā suṣakhā sakhīyate ||
  • saṁ jāgṛvadbhir: This phrase can be translated as "with those who are awake" or "with the vigilant ones." It emphasizes the importance of awareness and consciousness in our actions and decisions.
  • jaramāṇa idhyate: This means "aging is overcome" or "the process of aging is transcended." It suggests that through awareness and vigilance, one can overcome the limitations imposed by time and aging.
  • dame damūnā: This translates to "with control and restraint." It highlights the significance of self-discipline and control over one's desires and impulses.
  • iṣayann iḻas pade: This phrase means "in the place of power." It indicates that through vigilance and control, one can attain a position of strength and influence.
  • viśvasya hotā: This translates to "the one who offers to all." It signifies a universal benefactor or a person who contributes to the well-being of everyone.
  • haviṣo vareṇyo: This means "the most excellent offerings." It suggests that the offerings made should be of the highest quality and intention.
  • vibhur vibhāvā: This translates to "the one who is abundant and prosperous." It indicates a state of richness and fulfillment in life.
  • suṣakhā sakhīyate: This means "is well-favored and friendly." It emphasizes the importance of relationships and harmony in life.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of living a conscious and disciplined life. It teaches us that by being vigilant and exercising self-control, we can transcend the limitations of aging and achieve a position of power and influence. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios such as personal development, leadership, and community service. For instance, a leader who is aware of their actions and maintains self-discipline can inspire others and create a positive impact in their community. The mantra encourages us to make excellent contributions to the world around us, fostering relationships that are harmonious and beneficial. Ultimately, it signifies the importance of awareness, control, and generosity in leading a fulfilling life.
