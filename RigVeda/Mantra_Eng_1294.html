शरस्य चिदार्चत्कस्यावताद् आनिचाद् उच्चाचक्रथुः पातवे वाह् |शयवे चिन् नासत्याः शचीभिर् जसुरये स्तर्यम् पिप्यथुर् गाम् ||
śarasya cid ārcatkasyāvatād ā nīcād uccā cakrathuḥ pātave vāḥ | śayave cin nāsatyā śacībhir jasuraye staryam pipyathur gām ||
  • śarasya cid ārcatkasyāvatād: This phrase can be interpreted as a call to the divine or the higher powers to bestow their blessings. It signifies the importance of seeking guidance and support from a higher source in times of need.
  • ā nīcād uccā cakrathuḥ: This part emphasizes the transition from a lower state to a higher one. It suggests that one should strive to elevate themselves from their current circumstances, overcoming challenges and obstacles.
  • pātave vāḥ: This can be understood as a plea for strength and resilience. It highlights the need for inner strength to face adversities and to remain steadfast in one’s pursuits.
  • śayave cin nāsatyā: This phrase refers to the importance of truth and integrity. It suggests that one should remain honest and true to their values, even when faced with difficulties.
  • śacībhir jasuraye: This part speaks to the power of collective strength and support. It indicates that working together with others can lead to greater achievements and success.
  • staryam pipyathur gām: This can be interpreted as a call to action, urging individuals to strive for progress and to seek out new opportunities. It encourages a proactive approach to life.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking divine guidance, striving for personal growth, and the importance of truth and collective strength. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios such as personal development, teamwork in professional settings, and maintaining integrity in relationships. By embodying these principles, individuals can navigate challenges more effectively and work towards achieving their goals with resilience and support from others.
