देवान वसिष्ठो अमृतान ववन्दे ये विश्वा भुवनाभि प्रतस्थुः |ते नो रासन्ताम उरुगायम अद्य यूयम् पात स्वस्तिभिः सदा नः ||
devān vasiṣṭho amṛtān vavande ye viśvā bhuvanābhi pratasthuḥ | te no rāsantām urugāyam adya yūyam pāta svastibhiḥ sadā naḥ ||
  • devān vasiṣṭho amṛtān vavande: This phrase can be translated as "the divine beings, the most excellent, who are immortal, we honor." Here, "devān" refers to the gods or divine beings, "vasiṣṭho" signifies the best or most excellent, and "amṛtān" means immortal. In real life, this can be seen as a call to recognize and respect the higher powers or principles that guide our existence, such as love, truth, and justice.
  • ye viśvā bhuvanābhi pratasthuḥ: This translates to "those who have established themselves in all worlds." "Viśvā" means all or universal, "bhuvanābhi" refers to worlds or realms, and "pratasthuḥ" means to stand or establish. This can be related to individuals or leaders who strive to create a positive impact across various aspects of life, such as community service or environmental conservation.
  • te no rāsantām urugāyam: This means "may they grant us abundance and prosperity." "Te" refers to them (the divine beings), "no" means us, "rāsantām" signifies abundance, and "urugāyam" refers to the vast or expansive. In practical terms, this can be interpreted as a prayer for blessings in our endeavors, whether in personal growth, career success, or community well-being.
  • adya yūyam pāta svastibhiḥ sadā naḥ: This translates to "today, may you protect us with your auspiciousness always." "Adya" means today, "yūyam" refers to you (the divine), "pāta" means protect, "svastibhiḥ" signifies auspiciousness or well-being, and "sadā naḥ" means always for us. This can be seen as a reminder to seek protection and guidance from higher powers in our daily lives, whether through meditation, prayer, or acts of kindness.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of recognizing and honoring divine forces and principles that guide our lives. It serves as a reminder to seek abundance, protection, and auspiciousness in our daily endeavors. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied through community engagement, personal development, and spiritual practices that foster a sense of connection with the greater universe. By embodying these principles, individuals can contribute to a more harmonious and prosperous society.
