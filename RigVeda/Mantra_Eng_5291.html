वि साद्यो विश्वा दृष्मितान्य एषाम इन्द्रः पुरः सहसा सप्त दर्धः |व्य आनवस्य तृत्सवे गयम् भाग जेष्म पूरुं विदथे मृध्रवाचम् ||
vi sadyo viśvā dṛṁhitāny eṣām indraḥ puraḥ sahasā sapta dardaḥ | vy ānavasya tṛtsave gayam bhāg jeṣma pūruṁ vidathe mṛdhravācam ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • vi: indeed, truly
    • sadyo: immediately, at once
    • viśvā: all, universal
    • dṛṁhitāny: those who are established, firm
    • eṣām: of these
    • indraḥ: Indra, the king of gods
    • puraḥ: in front, before
    • sahasā: with great force, suddenly
    • sapta: seven
    • dardaḥ: powerful, strong
    • vy: you (plural)
    • ānavasya: of the one who is humble
    • tṛtsave: for the one who seeks
    • gayam: the song, hymn
    • bhāg: share, portion
    • jeṣma: we shall conquer
    • pūruṁ: the strong, powerful
    • vidathe: in the assembly, gathering
    • mṛdhravācam: with sweet words, pleasant speech
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the presence of Indra, the chief deity associated with rain, thunder, and war, emphasizing his role in establishing strength and unity among all beings.
    • The phrase "vi sadyo viśvā dṛṁhitāny" suggests that all beings are firmly established and united under the guidance of Indra.
    • The mention of "sahasā sapta dardaḥ" indicates the powerful and swift nature of Indra's actions, symbolizing the immediate impact of divine intervention in human affairs.
    • The latter part of the mantra, "vy ānavasya tṛtsave gayam bhāg jeṣma pūruṁ vidathe mṛdhravācam," highlights the importance of humility and the pursuit of knowledge, suggesting that those who seek with a humble heart will be rewarded with strength and success in their endeavors.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, a leader who embodies the qualities of Indra—strength, decisiveness, and unity—can inspire their team to work together effectively, leading to successful project outcomes.
    • In personal relationships, approaching conflicts with humility and a willingness to listen can lead to stronger bonds and resolutions that benefit all parties involved.
    • In community service, individuals who come together with a shared purpose and a humble attitude can create significant positive change, reflecting the collective strength mentioned in the mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the power of unity, humility, and divine guidance in achieving success and strength in various aspects of life.
    • Its significance lies in the encouragement to seek strength through collaboration and to approach challenges with a humble heart, ensuring that we remain open to the wisdom and support of others.
    • In real-life scenarios, whether in leadership, personal relationships, or community efforts, the principles embodied in this mantra can lead to transformative outcomes and a deeper sense of connection among individuals.
