उग्रं युयुज्म पृतनासु सासहिम ऋणकातिम अदाभ्यम् |वेदा भृमं चित सनितामो रथीतमो वाजिनं यम् इद ū नशत ||
ugraṁ yuyujma pṛtanāsu sāsahim ṛṇakātim adābhyam | vedā bhṛmaṁ cit sanitā rathītamo vājinaṁ yam id ū naśat ||
  • ugraṁ: This word translates to "mighty" or "powerful." It signifies strength and intensity.
  • yuyujma: This means "we unite" or "we come together." It emphasizes the importance of collaboration and unity.
  • pṛtanāsu: This refers to "in the battles" or "in the struggles." It indicates the challenges and conflicts one faces in life.
  • sāsahim: This can be interpreted as "with strength" or "with vigor." It suggests the need for resilience and determination.
  • ṛṇakātim: This word means "the debt" or "the obligation." It highlights the responsibilities we carry in our lives.
  • adābhyam: This translates to "without fear" or "without hesitation." It encourages facing challenges boldly.
  • vedā: This means "knowledge" or "wisdom." It signifies the importance of understanding and learning.
  • bhṛmaṁ: This can be interpreted as "the great" or "the vast." It refers to the expansive nature of knowledge and experience.
  • cit: This word means "consciousness" or "awareness." It emphasizes the need for mindfulness in our actions.
  • sanitā: This translates to "the one who is pure" or "the one who is virtuous." It signifies the importance of integrity and righteousness.
  • rathītamo: This means "the best charioteer" or "the one who leads." It symbolizes guidance and leadership.
  • vājinaṁ: This translates to "the one who brings victory" or "the victorious." It signifies success and achievement.
  • yam: This means "who" or "that." It refers to the subject of the mantra.
  • id: This translates to "this" or "here." It emphasizes the immediacy of the message.
  • ū naśat: This means "we shall not be defeated" or "we shall not fail." It conveys a strong message of resilience and determination.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of strength, unity, and resilience in the face of challenges. It encourages individuals to come together, face their responsibilities without fear, and strive for knowledge and virtue. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as teamwork in professional settings, community building, and personal development. By embodying the principles of collaboration, courage, and wisdom, individuals can navigate life's battles more effectively and achieve success together. The significance of this mantra lies in its timeless message of empowerment and collective strength, reminding us that we are stronger when we unite and support one another.
