रासी क्षयं रासी मित्रम् अस्मे रासी शर्ध इन्द्र मारुतं नः |सजोषसो ये च मंदसानाः प्र वायवः पान्त्य अग्रणीतिम ||
rāsi kṣayaṁ rāsi mitram asme rāsi śardha indra mārutaṁ naḥ | sajoṣaso ye ca mandasānāḥ pra vāyavaḥ pānty agraṇītim ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • rāsi: abundance, wealth, or resources
    • kṣayaṁ: decrease or loss
    • mitram: friend or ally
    • asme: to us or for us
    • śardha: strength or power
    • indra: the king of gods, representing power and authority
    • mārutaṁ: the winds or the forces of nature
    • naḥ: our or us
    • sajoṣaso: those who are united or in harmony
    • ye: who
    • ca: and
    • mandasānāḥ: those who are gentle or mild
    • pra: before or in front of
    • vāyavaḥ: the winds or air
    • pānty: leading or guiding
    • agraṇītim: the leader or chief
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the balance of abundance and loss, emphasizing the importance of friendship and unity.
    • It invokes Indra, the god of rain and thunderstorms, symbolizing strength and authority, and the Mārutas, the winds, representing the forces of nature.
    • The mantra suggests that those who are united and gentle can lead and guide us through challenges.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be applied to teamwork. When team members work together harmoniously, they can overcome challenges and achieve greater success.
    • In personal relationships, the mantra highlights the importance of maintaining friendships and support systems, especially during difficult times.
    • In environmental contexts, it can remind us of the need to respect and work with nature (the Mārutas) to ensure sustainability and balance.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for unity, strength, and the importance of relationships in overcoming life's challenges.
    • It teaches us that by fostering friendships and working together, we can navigate through difficulties and maintain balance in our lives.
    • This mantra is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal relationships to professional environments, emphasizing the power of collaboration and harmony.
