याभिः शचीभिः चमसां अपिंशत यया धियā गाम अरिणीत चरमणः |येना हरī मनसा निरतक्षत तेन देवत्वं ऋभवः सम आनश ||
yābhiḥ śacībhiś camasām̐ apiṁśata yayā dhiyā gām ariṇīta carmaṇaḥ | yena harī manasā niratakṣata tena devatvam ṛbhavaḥ sam ānaśa ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yābhiḥ: By whom
    • śacībhiś: With the powers
    • camasām̐: Of the offerings
    • apiṁśata: They are made
    • yayā: By which
    • dhiyā: With intellect
    • gām: The goal
    • ariṇīta: They attain
    • carmaṇaḥ: Of the body
    • yena: By whom
    • harī: The divine
    • manasā: With the mind
    • niratakṣata: Remains steadfast
    • tena: By that
    • devatvam: Divinity
    • ṛbhavaḥ: Becomes manifest
    • sam ānaśa: Together with
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the power of intellect and divine connection.
    • It emphasizes that through the right offerings and intentions, one can achieve their goals.
    • The phrase "by whom the divine remains steadfast" suggests that a focused mind can lead to spiritual realization.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, a team that works together with a clear goal and shared intentions can achieve success, much like the mantra suggests.
    • In personal development, individuals who set clear intentions and remain focused on their goals often find greater success and fulfillment.
    • In spiritual practices, meditation and mindfulness can help individuals connect with their inner divinity, reflecting the essence of this mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of intention, focus, and divine connection in achieving one's goals.
    • It is applicable in various aspects of life, including personal growth, professional success, and spiritual development.
    • By understanding and applying the principles of this mantra, individuals can cultivate a more meaningful and purposeful life.
