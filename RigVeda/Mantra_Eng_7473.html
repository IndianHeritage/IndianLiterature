त्वं हि सत्यो मगवन्न अनानतो वृत्राभूरे न्यृञ्जसे |स तवं शविष्ठ वज्रहस्त दाशुषे 'र्वाञ्चं रयिम आ कृधि ||
tvaṁ hi satyo maghavann anānato vṛtrā bhūri nyṛñjase | sa tvaṁ śaviṣṭha vajrahasta dāśuṣe 'rvāñcaṁ rayim ā kṛdhi ||
  • tvaṁ hi satyo - "You are indeed the truth": This phrase emphasizes the importance of truth in our lives. In real life, truth is the foundation of trust in relationships, whether personal or professional. For example, a business that operates transparently and honestly is more likely to gain customer loyalty.
  • maghavann anānato - "O Mighty One, without limits": This refers to the boundless nature of divine power. In our daily lives, we often face challenges that seem insurmountable. Recognizing that there is a greater force at work can provide comfort and strength. For instance, during difficult times, people may turn to their faith or spirituality for guidance and support.
  • vṛtrā bhūri nyṛñjase - "You who vanquish the obstacles": This line highlights the ability to overcome challenges. In practical terms, this can be seen in the stories of individuals who have triumphed over adversity, such as athletes who overcome injuries or entrepreneurs who succeed after multiple failures.
  • sa tvaṁ śaviṣṭha vajrahasta - "You, with the strongest hand, wielding the thunderbolt": The imagery of a thunderbolt symbolizes strength and power. In real life, this can be likened to leaders who take decisive action in times of crisis, inspiring others to follow their lead. For example, a community leader who mobilizes resources during a natural disaster exemplifies this strength.
  • dāśuṣe 'rvāñcaṁ rayim ā kṛdhi - "Bestow upon us wealth and prosperity": This plea for abundance reflects a universal desire for well-being. In modern society, this can be related to the pursuit of financial stability and success. Many people engage in practices such as budgeting, investing, and continuous learning to achieve their goals of prosperity.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking truth, strength, and prosperity in life. Its significance lies in the reminder that we can overcome obstacles with the help of a higher power and that our pursuit of truth and abundance is a noble endeavor. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire individuals to face challenges with courage, seek honesty in their dealings, and strive for personal and communal growth. By embodying these principles, we can create a more harmonious and prosperous society.
