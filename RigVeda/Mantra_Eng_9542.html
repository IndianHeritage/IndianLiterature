मन्त्र: शाश्वद अग्निर वध्र्यश्वस्य शत्रून नृभिर जिगाय सुतसोमवत्भिः |समनं चिद अदहश चित्रभानोऽव व्राधन्तम अभिनद वृद्धश चित ||
śaśvad agnir vadhryaśvasya śatrūn nṛbhir jigāya sutasomavadbhiḥ | samanaṁ cid adahaś citrabhāno 'va vrādhantam abhinad vṛdhaś cit ||
  • śaśvad agnir: This phrase translates to "everlasting fire." In a real-life context, this can symbolize the eternal nature of knowledge and wisdom, much like the fire that sustains life and provides warmth.
  • vadhryaśvasya: This means "of the strong." It refers to strength and resilience, qualities that are essential in overcoming challenges in life.
  • śatrūn nṛbhir jigāya: This translates to "the enemies are defeated by men." This can be interpreted as the triumph of human effort and unity in overcoming adversities, akin to teamwork in a corporate setting where collective effort leads to success.
  • sutasomavadbhiḥ: This means "like the Soma of the sons." Soma is a sacred drink in Vedic tradition, symbolizing nourishment and vitality. In modern life, this can represent the importance of nurturing relationships and community support.
  • samanaṁ cid adahaś: This translates to "the same, indeed, does not burn." It suggests that true essence or spirit remains unscathed by external challenges, similar to how a person's core values remain intact despite life's trials.
  • citrabhāno 'va: This means "like the bright sun." The sun is a universal symbol of hope and enlightenment, representing the pursuit of knowledge and truth in our lives.
  • vrādhantam abhinad: This translates to "the one who is nourished is protected." It emphasizes the importance of care and support in fostering growth, much like how mentorship in professional settings can lead to personal and career development.
  • vṛdhaś cit: This means "the wise one." It signifies the value of wisdom and experience, which are crucial in making informed decisions in life.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of resilience, unity, and the pursuit of knowledge. It highlights the importance of strength in overcoming challenges, the value of community support, and the enduring nature of wisdom. In real-life scenarios, these principles can be applied in various contexts, such as teamwork in professional environments, nurturing relationships in personal life, and the continuous quest for knowledge and self-improvement. The significance of this mantra lies in its timeless wisdom, encouraging individuals to remain steadfast and united in the face of adversity while cherishing the light of knowledge and experience.
