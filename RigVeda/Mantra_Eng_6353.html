इन्द्रेण रोचनादिवो दृढानि दृष्मितानि च |स्थिराणि न पराणुदे ||
indreṇa rocanā divo dṛḻhāni dṛṁhitāni ca | sthirāṇi na parāṇude ||
  • Indreṇa: This word refers to Indra, the king of the gods in Vedic mythology, often associated with strength, power, and the forces of nature.
  • Rocanā: This translates to "brightness" or "radiance," symbolizing clarity, enlightenment, and the illumination of knowledge.
  • Divo: Meaning "of the sky" or "heaven," it signifies the divine realm and the higher consciousness.
  • Dṛḻhāni: This word means "firm" or "stable," indicating strength and resilience in one's character or circumstances.
  • Dṛṁhitāni: This translates to "established" or "set," suggesting a sense of permanence and reliability.
  • Ca: This simply means "and," connecting the ideas presented in the mantra.
  • Sthirāṇi: Meaning "steady" or "unwavering," it emphasizes the importance of stability in life.
  • Na: This means "not," indicating negation or absence.
  • Parāṇude: This can be interpreted as "to be disturbed" or "to be shaken," suggesting vulnerability or instability.

The mantra can be interpreted as a prayer or invocation for strength and stability, asking for the divine blessings of Indra to bring brightness and firmness to one's life. It emphasizes the importance of being grounded and resilient, even in the face of challenges.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios:

  • Personal Growth: Individuals can use this mantra as a reminder to seek inner strength and clarity in their personal journeys, especially during difficult times.
  • Leadership: Leaders can embody the qualities of Indra by being firm and stable, providing guidance and support to their teams while fostering a bright and positive environment.
  • Community Building: In community settings, this mantra can inspire collective resilience and stability, encouraging members to support one another and maintain a steady course in the face of adversity.

Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine support in achieving stability and clarity in life. By invoking the qualities of Indra, individuals and communities can cultivate resilience, ensuring that they remain steadfast and undisturbed by external challenges. This mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the importance of inner strength and the pursuit of enlightenment in our daily lives.
