तां विद्वांसः हवामहे वां ता नो विद्वांसः मनमा वाचेतम् अद्य |प्रार्चद दयमानो युवाकुः ||
tā vidvāṁsā havāmahe vāṁ tā no vidvāṁsā manma vocetam adya | prārcad dayamāno yuvākuḥ ||
  • tā vidvāṁsā havāmahe vāṁ: This phrase can be translated as "We invoke the wise ones." Here, "tā" refers to "those," "vidvāṁsā" means "the wise" or "the knowledgeable," and "havāmahe" translates to "we invoke" or "we call upon." This suggests a reverence for wisdom and knowledge, emphasizing the importance of seeking guidance from those who possess understanding.
  • tā no vidvāṁsā manma vocetam adya: This part means "May those wise ones speak to us today." "No" means "to us," "manma" translates to "our mind" or "our thoughts," and "vocetam" means "may they speak." This indicates a desire for communication and enlightenment from the wise, highlighting the need for wisdom in decision-making and daily life.
  • prārcad dayamāno yuvākuḥ: This translates to "The one who is compassionate and youthful." "Prārcad" refers to "compassionate," "dayamāno" means "showing kindness," and "yuvākuḥ" translates to "youthful" or "energetic." This emphasizes the qualities of compassion and vitality, suggesting that wisdom should be accompanied by kindness and a youthful spirit.

  • Significance: This mantra emphasizes the importance of invoking wisdom and seeking guidance from knowledgeable individuals. It highlights the need for communication and understanding in our lives, as well as the value of compassion and youthful energy in our interactions.
  • Real-life Application: In practical terms, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as:
    • Education: Students can invoke the wisdom of their teachers and mentors, seeking guidance in their studies and personal growth.
    • Decision-making: Individuals facing important life choices can reflect on the wisdom of experienced friends or family members, allowing their insights to guide their decisions.
    • Community Engagement: In community settings, invoking the wisdom of elders can foster a sense of unity and shared knowledge, promoting compassionate actions that benefit all.

  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a reminder of the value of wisdom, communication, and compassion in our lives. By invoking the wise and seeking their guidance, we can navigate challenges more effectively and foster a more harmonious existence. It encourages us to remain open to learning and to approach life with a youthful spirit and a compassionate heart.
