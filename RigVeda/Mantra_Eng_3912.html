सं यज जनौ सुदहनौ विश्वशर्धसाव अवेद इन्द्रो मगवाऽ गोषु शुभ्रिषु |युजं ह्य अन्यम् अकृत प्रवेपन्य उद ईं गव्यं सृजते सत्वभिर धुनिः ||
saṁ yaj janau sudhanau viśvaśardhasāv aved indro maghavā goṣu śubhriṣu | yujaṁ hy a1nyam akṛta pravepany ud īṁ gavyaṁ sṛjate satvabhir dhuniḥ ||
  • saṁ yaj janau sudhanau: This phrase can be interpreted as "those who are engaged in righteous actions." In real life, this can refer to individuals or communities that work together for the common good, such as volunteers in a charity organization.
  • viśvaśardhasāv aved: This translates to "the one who knows the essence of all." This can be likened to a wise leader or mentor who understands the needs and strengths of their team, guiding them towards success.
  • indro maghavā goṣu śubhriṣu: Here, "Indra" represents strength and power, while "maghavā" refers to abundance. This can be seen in a business context where a strong leader (Indra) drives the company towards prosperity (maghavā) through ethical practices (goṣu śubhriṣu).
  • yujaṁ hy a1nyam akṛta pravepany: This phrase suggests the importance of unity and collaboration. In a real-life scenario, this can be observed in team sports where players must work together harmoniously to achieve victory.
  • ud īṁ gavyaṁ sṛjate: This can be interpreted as "creating a favorable environment." For instance, in a workplace, fostering a positive culture can lead to increased productivity and employee satisfaction.
  • satvabhir dhuniḥ: This phrase emphasizes the power of truth and righteousness. In everyday life, this can be seen in individuals who stand up for justice and integrity, inspiring others to do the same.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of collaboration, wisdom, strength, and righteousness. Its significance lies in the reminder that when individuals come together with a shared purpose and ethical values, they can create a positive impact in their communities and beyond. This mantra is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from teamwork in professional settings to community service initiatives, highlighting the importance of unity and moral integrity in achieving collective goals.
