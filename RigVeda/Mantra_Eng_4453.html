धन्याऽ cid dhi tve धिषणा वष्टि प्र देवाञ जन्म गृणते यजध्यै |वेपिष्टो अङ्गिरसां यद धा विप्रो मधु छन्दो भनति रेब इष्टौ ||
dhanyā cid dhi tve dhiṣaṇā vaṣṭi pra devāñ janma gṛṇate yajadhyai | vepiṣṭho aṅgirasāṁ yad dha vipro madhu cchando bhanati rebha iṣṭau ||
  • Translation: The mantra can be translated as: "O Divine Ones, may we be blessed with the wisdom and knowledge that leads to prosperity. The wise among us, who are like the Angiras, may they inspire us with sweet words and lead us to our goals."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • dhanyā: Blessed or prosperous.
    • cid: Indeed or truly.
    • dhi: Wisdom or intellect.
    • dhiṣaṇā: Knowledge or understanding.
    • vaṣṭi: To be invoked or called upon.
    • pra: For or towards.
    • devāñ: Divine beings or gods.
    • janma: Birth or creation.
    • gṛṇate: To sing or praise.
    • yajadhyai: For the purpose of sacrifice or worship.
    • vepiṣṭho: The best or most excellent.
    • aṅgirasāṁ: Of the Angiras, a group of wise sages.
    • yad: That which.
    • dha: To hold or possess.
    • vipro: The wise or learned one.
    • madhu: Sweet or pleasant.
    • cchando: Verses or hymns.
    • bhanati: To speak or declare.
    • rebha: To inspire or motivate.
    • iṣṭau: Desirable or worthy.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, this mantra can be applied when teams come together to brainstorm ideas. The invocation of wisdom and knowledge can lead to innovative solutions that benefit the organization.
    • In educational institutions, teachers can embody the essence of this mantra by inspiring students with sweet words of encouragement, fostering a positive learning environment.
    • During community gatherings, leaders can invoke the blessings of divine wisdom to guide discussions and decisions that affect the welfare of the community.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine wisdom and guidance in all endeavors. It emphasizes the importance of knowledge and the role of wise individuals in leading others towards prosperity. In real-life scenarios, whether in personal growth, professional settings, or community development, invoking such wisdom can lead to fruitful outcomes and harmonious relationships. By recognizing the value of wisdom and the power of positive communication, individuals and groups can achieve their goals and contribute to the greater good.
