स सुक्रतुर ऋतचिद अस्तु होतā य आदित्य शवसां नमस्वान् |आववर्तद अवसे वां हविष्मान् असद इत स सुविताय प्रयस्वान् ||
sa sukratur ṛtacid astu hotā ya āditya śavasā vāṁ namasvān | āvavartad avase vāṁ haviṣmān asad it sa suvitāya prayasvān ||
  • sa sukratur: This phrase translates to "may he who is of good thought" or "he who has good intentions." It emphasizes the importance of positive thinking and intentions in our actions.
  • ṛtacid astu hotā: This means "may the one who knows the truth be the priest." Here, "ṛti" refers to truth or cosmic order, and "hotā" refers to the priest or the one who offers sacrifices. This highlights the significance of truthfulness and righteousness in leadership and guidance.
  • ya āditya: This translates to "who is like the sun." The sun symbolizes enlightenment, knowledge, and vitality. It suggests that one should aspire to be as illuminating and life-giving as the sun.
  • śavasā vāṁ namasvān: This means "to you, who are full of strength, we bow." It acknowledges the power and strength of the divine or the higher forces in our lives, encouraging humility and reverence.
  • āvavartad avase vāṁ haviṣmān: This translates to "may he who is full of offerings come to us." It signifies the importance of generosity and the act of giving, suggesting that abundance and blessings come to those who are open to sharing.
  • asad it sa suvitāya prayasvān: This means "may he who is wise and skilled in action lead us away from falsehood." It emphasizes the need for wisdom and skill in navigating life, steering clear of deception and untruths.

Conclusion:

The mantra encapsulates the essence of positive thinking, truthfulness, reverence, generosity, and wisdom. In real-life scenarios, these principles can be applied in various contexts:

  • In personal relationships, fostering good intentions and truthfulness can lead to stronger bonds and trust.
  • In leadership roles, embodying the qualities of enlightenment and strength can inspire others and create a positive environment.
  • In community service, being generous and open to giving can enhance social cohesion and support those in need.
  • In decision-making, applying wisdom and skill can help individuals navigate challenges effectively and avoid pitfalls.

Overall, this mantra serves as a guiding principle for living a life of integrity, compassion, and enlightenment, encouraging individuals to strive for personal and collective growth.
