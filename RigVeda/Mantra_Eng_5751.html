प्र ये ययुर अवृकासो रथा इव नृपातारो जनानाम् |उत स्वेन शवसा शूशुवुर नर उता क्षियन्ति सुख्सितिम् ||
pra ye yayur avṛkāso rathā iva nṛpātāro janānām | uta svena śavasā śūśuvur nara uta kṣiyanti sukṣitim ||
  • pra ye yayur avṛkāso rathā iva nṛpātāro janānām
    • pra ye yayur: This phrase can be translated as "those who have gone forth" or "those who have moved ahead." It signifies progress and the journey of life.
    • avṛkāso: This term refers to "without obstacles" or "unhindered." It emphasizes the importance of a clear path in achieving one's goals.
    • rathā iva: "like chariots" suggests speed, strength, and the ability to navigate through challenges. Chariots are often symbols of power and movement in ancient texts.
    • nṛpātāro: This word means "kings" or "rulers." It indicates leadership and the responsibility that comes with it.
    • janānām: This translates to "of the people." It highlights the connection between leaders and the community they serve.
  • uta svena śavasā śūśuvur nara uta kṣiyanti sukṣitim
    • uta: This word can mean "or" or "also," suggesting an alternative or additional thought.
    • svena: This means "by their own" or "through their own efforts," emphasizing self-reliance and personal responsibility.
    • śavasā: This term refers to "strength" or "power." It indicates the importance of inner strength in overcoming challenges.
    • śūśuvur: This can be interpreted as "they have achieved" or "they have accomplished," signifying success and achievement.
    • nara: This means "man" or "human," representing humanity and the collective experience of people.
    • uta kṣiyanti: This phrase translates to "they diminish" or "they reduce," indicating the idea of overcoming obstacles or challenges.
    • sukṣitim: This means "prosperity" or "well-being," highlighting the ultimate goal of success and fulfillment in life.

Conclusion:

The mantra emphasizes the journey of life, the importance of leadership, and the role of personal effort in achieving success. It suggests that both leaders and individuals must navigate their paths without obstacles, relying on their strength and determination. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios such as:

  • In personal development, individuals can strive to overcome challenges by setting clear goals and working diligently towards them.
  • In leadership, it serves as a reminder for leaders to guide their communities effectively, ensuring that they are moving forward together.
  • In teamwork, it highlights the importance of collaboration and mutual support in achieving common objectives.

Overall, the mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the interconnectedness of individual efforts, leadership, and the pursuit of prosperity in life.
