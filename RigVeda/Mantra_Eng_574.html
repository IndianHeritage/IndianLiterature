विश्वम् अस्य नानाम चक्षसे जगज ज्योतिष कृणोति सूनरी |अप द्वेषो मगहोनी दुहिता दिव उषा उच्छद अप श्रीधः ||
viśvam asyā nānāma cakṣase jagaj jyotiṣ kṛṇoti sūnarī | apa dveṣo maghonī duhitā diva uṣā ucchad apa sridhaḥ ||
  • viśvam asyā nānāma cakṣase: This phrase can be translated as "All of this is seen through the many eyes." It signifies the interconnectedness of all beings and the perception of the universe through various perspectives. In real life, this can be related to the idea of empathy and understanding different viewpoints in a diverse society.
  • jagaj jyotiṣ kṛṇoti sūnarī: This translates to "The world is illuminated by the beautiful light." Here, 'jagaj' refers to the world, 'jyotiṣ' means light, and 'sūnarī' implies beauty. This can be likened to the impact of knowledge and wisdom in our lives, which enlightens our path. For example, education can be seen as a light that guides individuals towards better opportunities and understanding.
  • apa dveṣo maghonī duhitā: This means "Without enmity, the daughter of abundance." It emphasizes the importance of harmony and the absence of hatred in fostering prosperity. In practical terms, this can be observed in communities that thrive on cooperation and mutual respect, leading to collective growth and success.
  • diva uṣā ucchad apa sridhaḥ: This translates to "The dawn reveals the wealth." 'Dawn' symbolizes new beginnings and opportunities, while 'wealth' can refer to both material and spiritual richness. This can be related to the idea of starting fresh each day, where every sunrise brings new chances to achieve our goals and enrich our lives.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of unity, enlightenment, harmony, and new beginnings. Its significance lies in the reminder that we are all interconnected, and by embracing diverse perspectives, we can illuminate our paths and foster a prosperous environment. In real-life scenarios, this mantra encourages individuals and communities to work together, respect one another, and seize the opportunities that each new day brings, ultimately leading to a more harmonious and enriched existence.
