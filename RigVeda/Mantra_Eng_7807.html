पवन्ते वाजसातये सोमाः सहस्रपाजसः |गृणाना देववीतये ||
pavante vājasātaye somāḥ sahasrapājasaḥ | gṛṇānā devavītaye ||
  • pavante: This word translates to "the wind" or "the purifying force." In a broader sense, it can represent the vital energy or life force that sustains all living beings.
  • vājasātaye: This term means "for the purpose of strength" or "for the attainment of vigor." It signifies the pursuit of power, energy, and vitality in life.
  • somāḥ: Referring to "the soma," which is often associated with a divine drink that grants immortality and enlightenment. It symbolizes the essence of life and spiritual awakening.
  • sahasrapājasaḥ: This phrase means "with a thousand rays" or "with immense brilliance." It indicates abundance, prosperity, and the multifaceted nature of existence.
  • gṛṇānā: This word translates to "singing" or "chanting." It emphasizes the importance of expression, devotion, and the act of invoking divine energies through sound.
  • devavītaye: This term means "for the purpose of pleasing the gods" or "to attain divine favor." It highlights the relationship between humans and the divine, emphasizing the need for reverence and gratitude.

The mantra can be interpreted as a call to harness the purifying energy of the wind for the purpose of gaining strength and vitality, while invoking the divine essence of life represented by soma. It emphasizes the importance of expressing gratitude and devotion to the divine forces that govern existence.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios:

  • Personal Growth: Just as the wind purifies and revitalizes, individuals can seek to cleanse their minds and spirits through meditation and self-reflection, aiming for personal strength and resilience.
  • Health and Wellness: The pursuit of vitality can be likened to adopting a healthy lifestyle, where one focuses on physical fitness, nutrition, and mental well-being, akin to the rejuvenating effects of soma.
  • Community and Relationships: Singing and expressing gratitude can strengthen bonds within communities, fostering a sense of unity and shared purpose, much like the collective worship and reverence for the divine.
  • Environmental Awareness: The reference to the wind can also serve as a reminder of the importance of nature and the environment, encouraging individuals to respect and protect the natural world that sustains life.

In conclusion, this mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking strength and vitality through purification and devotion. It serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of life, the importance of gratitude, and the pursuit of personal and communal well-being. By embodying these principles, individuals can navigate their lives with purpose and harmony.
