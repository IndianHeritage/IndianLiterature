अपामिदं न्ययनं समुद्रस्य निवेशनम् |अन्यं कृणुष्वेतः पन्थां तेन याहि वशाम् अनु ||
apām idaṁ nyayanaṁ samudrasya niveśanam | anyaṁ kṛṇuṣvetaḥ panthāṁ tena yāhi vaśām̐ anu ||
  • Mantra: "apām idaṁ nyayanaṁ samudrasya niveśanam | anyaṁ kṛṇuṣvetaḥ panthāṁ tena yāhi vaśām̐ anu ||"
  • Translation: "This is the path of the waters, the abode of the ocean; create another path, and go along that, following it."

  • Word Breakdown:
    • apām: of waters
    • idaṁ: this
    • nyayanaṁ: path or direction
    • samudrasya: of the ocean
    • niveśanam: abode or dwelling
    • anyaṁ: another
    • kṛṇuṣvetaḥ: create or make
    • panthāṁ: path
    • tena: by that
    • yāhi: go
    • vaśām̐: under control or in harmony
    • anu: following

  • Meaning and Explanation:
    • This mantra speaks about the path of waters, symbolizing the flow of life and the natural order of things.
    • The "abode of the ocean" can be interpreted as the vastness of existence, where all life originates and returns.
    • The call to "create another path" suggests the importance of innovation and finding new ways to navigate life's challenges.
    • In real life, this can be seen in various contexts, such as:
      • Environmental Conservation: Just as rivers and oceans have their paths, we must create sustainable paths for our environment, ensuring that we protect our water bodies.
      • Personal Growth: When faced with obstacles, we are encouraged to find new paths in our careers or personal lives, adapting to changes and challenges.
      • Community Development: In society, creating new paths can mean developing new policies or programs that better serve the community's needs.

  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its encouragement to embrace change and seek new directions in life.
    • It teaches us that while we may have established paths (like the waters of the ocean), there is always room for innovation and new journeys.
    • In practical terms, this mantra can be applied in various aspects of life, from personal development to environmental stewardship, reminding us to be adaptable and proactive in our pursuits.
