स सुन्वत इन्द्रः सूर्यं आ देवो रिणङ मार्त्याय स्तवान् |आ यद रयिं गुहदवद्यं अस्मै भरद अंशं नैतशो दशस्यन् ||
sa sunvata indraḥ sūryam ā devo riṇaṅ martyāya stavān | ā yad rayiṁ guhadavadyam asmai bharad aṁśaṁ naitaśo daśasyan ||
  • sa sunvata indraḥ: This phrase can be translated as "May Indra, the king of gods, listen." Indra is often associated with strength, rain, and the heavens. In real life, this can be seen as a call for divine attention and support in our endeavors.
  • sūryam ā devo riṇaṅ martyāya stavān: This translates to "The divine sun, may it shine upon mortals." The sun symbolizes life, energy, and growth. In practical terms, this can be related to seeking enlightenment and guidance in our daily lives, much like how sunlight nurtures plants.
  • ā yad rayiṁ guhadavadyam: This means "Whatever wealth is hidden." This can refer to the potential and opportunities that are not immediately visible. In a real-life context, it encourages individuals to seek out hidden talents or opportunities that may lead to success.
  • asmai bharad aṁśaṁ: This translates to "May he bestow upon us a portion." This reflects the idea of sharing and receiving blessings. In everyday life, it can be seen in the context of community support, where individuals help each other to achieve common goals.
  • naitaśo daśasyan: This means "Let it not be taken away." This emphasizes the importance of preserving what is valuable. In real life, this can relate to safeguarding relationships, knowledge, and resources that contribute to our well-being.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a prayer for divine support, enlightenment, and the pursuit of hidden opportunities. Its significance lies in the acknowledgment of a higher power that guides and protects us in our endeavors. In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as seeking guidance in difficult times, recognizing and nurturing our potential, and fostering a sense of community and support among individuals. By invoking these principles, we can strive for a more fulfilling and prosperous life.
