आयं ते शर्यणावति सुशोमायाम् अधि प्रियः |आरजीकीये मदिन्तमः ||
ayaṁ te śaryaṇāvati suṣomāyām adhi priyaḥ | ārjīkīye madintamaḥ ||
  • ayaṁ te - This phrase translates to "this is yours" or "this belongs to you." It signifies a sense of possession or connection to something sacred or valuable.
  • śaryaṇāvati - This term can be interpreted as "the one who is well-protected" or "the one who is surrounded by auspiciousness." It suggests a state of safety and well-being.
  • suṣomāyām - This word refers to "in the beautiful" or "in the pleasant." It evokes imagery of beauty and positivity, indicating a favorable environment or situation.
  • adhi priyaḥ - This means "dear to you" or "beloved." It emphasizes the importance of relationships and connections that are cherished.
  • ārjīkīye - This term can be understood as "in the realm of righteousness" or "in the domain of virtue." It highlights the significance of moral values and ethical living.
  • madintamaḥ - This translates to "the most delightful" or "the supreme joy." It signifies the ultimate state of happiness and fulfillment.

The mantra as a whole can be interpreted as a call to recognize and embrace the beauty and protection that comes from cherished relationships and virtuous living. It encourages individuals to seek joy in their connections and to cultivate an environment that is both safe and beautiful.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios:

  • In personal relationships, it reminds us to value and nurture our connections with loved ones, ensuring that they feel cherished and protected.
  • In community settings, it encourages the creation of a supportive and beautiful environment where everyone feels safe and valued.
  • In professional life, it highlights the importance of ethical practices and fostering a positive workplace culture that promotes well-being and joy among colleagues.

Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its emphasis on the interconnectedness of beauty, protection, and cherished relationships. By embodying these principles in our daily lives, we can create a harmonious existence that fosters joy and fulfillment. It serves as a reminder to prioritize what is truly important and to cultivate a life that reflects our values and connections.
