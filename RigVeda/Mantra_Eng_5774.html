प्रति शीम अग्निर जरते समिद्धः प्रति विप्रासो मतीभिर गृणन्तः |उषा याति ज्योतिषा बादहमाना विश्वा तमांसि दुरिताप देवी ||
prati ṣīm agnir jarate samiddhaḥ prati viprāso matibhir gṛṇantaḥ | uṣā yāti jyotiṣā bādhamānā viśvā tamāṁsi duritāpa devī ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • prati: towards
    • ṣīm: the direction or the goal
    • agnir: fire
    • jarate: is born or arises
    • samiddhaḥ: fully kindled or ignited
    • prati: towards
    • viprāso: the wise or learned ones
    • matibhir: with thoughts or intellect
    • gṛṇantaḥ: singing or praising
    • uṣā: dawn
    • yāti: goes or moves
    • jyotiṣā: with light or illumination
    • bādhamānā: dispelling or driving away
    • viśvā: all or universal
    • tamāṁsi: darkness
    • duritāpa: obstacles or difficulties
    • devī: goddess
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the emergence of fire, symbolizing knowledge and enlightenment, which is fully ignited and directed towards the wise.
    • The wise individuals, through their intellect and understanding, sing praises to this fire, representing the pursuit of knowledge and truth.
    • The dawn (uṣā) is depicted as a force that brings light, dispelling darkness and obstacles, symbolizing hope and new beginnings.
    • The mantra emphasizes the importance of wisdom and collective effort in overcoming challenges and achieving enlightenment.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a classroom setting, when students collaborate and share knowledge, they create an environment where ideas flourish, akin to the fire that is kindled.
    • In personal development, individuals often seek mentors (the wise) who guide them through their challenges, helping them to dispel their doubts and fears.
    • In community initiatives, collective efforts to address social issues can be seen as the dawn that brings light to darkness, fostering hope and progress.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the power of knowledge, collaboration, and the pursuit of enlightenment in overcoming life's challenges.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, such as education, personal growth, and community development, where wisdom and collective effort lead to positive change.
    • Ultimately, it encourages individuals to seek knowledge and work together to dispel ignorance and obstacles, paving the way for a brighter future.
