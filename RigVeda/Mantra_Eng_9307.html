अहं सूर्यस्य परि याम्य आशुभिः प्रैतशेबHIR वहमान ओजसा |यन् मां सावो मनुष आह निर्णिज ऋधक कृषे दासं कृत्व्यं हठैः ||
ahaṁ sūryasya pari yāmy āśubhiḥ praitaśebhir vahamāna ojasā | yan mā sāvo manuṣa āha nirṇija ṛdhak kṛṣe dāsaṁ kṛtvyaṁ hathaiḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ahaṁ: I
    • sūryasya: of the Sun
    • pari: around
    • yāmy: I move
    • āśubhiḥ: with evil or negativity
    • praitaśebhir: with those who are departing
    • vahamāna: carrying
    • ojasā: with energy or strength
    • yan: that
    • mā: not
    • sāvo: the good ones
    • manuṣa: human beings
    • āha: speak
    • nirṇija: without hesitation
    • ṛdhak: prosperity
    • kṛṣe: in agriculture
    • dāsaṁ: servant
    • kṛtvyaṁ: to be done
    • hathaiḥ: with hands
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra expresses the speaker's intention to move around the Sun, symbolizing the pursuit of enlightenment and positivity.
    • The phrase "with evil or negativity" suggests a desire to overcome obstacles and negativity in life.
    • By "carrying with energy," it emphasizes the importance of strength and vitality in one's endeavors.
    • The speaker seeks to ensure that the good ones (humans) are not left behind or neglected in their journey.
    • The mantra also highlights the importance of speaking without hesitation about prosperity, especially in the context of agriculture and labor.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, this mantra can be applied to teamwork, where individuals strive to uplift each other and work towards common goals, overcoming challenges together.
    • In personal development, it encourages individuals to focus on positive energy and to actively work against negativity in their lives, such as toxic relationships or harmful habits.
    • In agriculture, it can be seen as a call to action for farmers to work diligently and collaboratively to ensure prosperity and sustainability in their practices.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of striving for positivity and enlightenment while actively working against negativity.
    • It emphasizes the value of community, collaboration, and hard work in achieving prosperity.
    • In real life, it serves as a reminder to focus on uplifting oneself and others, ensuring that no one is left behind in the pursuit of success and well-being.
