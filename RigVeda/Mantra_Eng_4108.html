अतीयाम निदस तिरः स्वस्तिभिर हित्वावद्यं अरातिः |वृष्ट्वी शं योर आप उष्री भेषजं स्याम मरुतः सह ||
atīyāma nidas tiraḥ svastibhir hitvāvadyam arātīḥ | vṛṣṭvī śaṁ yor āpa usri bheṣajaṁ syāma marutaḥ saha ||
  • atīyāma: This word can be interpreted as "we strive" or "we aim." It signifies the human endeavor to reach higher goals or aspirations.
  • nidas: This term refers to "peace" or "tranquility." It emphasizes the importance of a calm and serene environment for achieving one's goals.
  • tiraḥ: Meaning "beyond" or "across," it suggests looking beyond immediate challenges and obstacles.
  • svastibhir: This word translates to "with auspiciousness" or "with well-being." It highlights the need for positive energy and good fortune in our endeavors.
  • hitvāvadyam: This phrase means "having abandoned negativity" or "leaving behind what is unworthy." It encourages individuals to let go of harmful thoughts and actions.
  • arātīḥ: This term can be understood as "enemies" or "obstacles." It signifies the challenges we face in our journey.
  • vṛṣṭvī: This word means "rain" or "nourishment." It symbolizes the essential resources and support we need to thrive.
  • śaṁ yor: This phrase translates to "of peace" or "of well-being." It emphasizes the importance of harmony in our lives.
  • āpa: Meaning "water," it represents the life-giving force and the necessity of sustenance.
  • usri: This term can be interpreted as "healing" or "remedy." It signifies the need for solutions and support in overcoming difficulties.
  • bheṣajaṁ: This word means "medicine" or "cure." It highlights the importance of healing and recovery in our lives.
  • syāma: This term translates to "may we become" or "let us be." It expresses a desire for transformation and growth.
  • marutaḥ: Referring to "the winds" or "the forces of nature," it symbolizes the dynamic and ever-changing aspects of life.
  • saha: This word means "together" or "with." It emphasizes the importance of collaboration and unity in achieving our goals.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of striving for peace and well-being while overcoming obstacles. It encourages individuals to aim high, abandon negativity, and seek nourishment and healing in their lives. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as personal development, teamwork, and community building. By fostering a positive environment and working together, we can navigate challenges and achieve our aspirations, ultimately leading to a harmonious and fulfilling life.
