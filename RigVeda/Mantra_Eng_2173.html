प्र ते नावं न समने वचस्युवं ब्रह्मणा यामि सवनेषु दाधृषिः |कुविन नो अस्य वचसो निबोधिषद इन्द्रं उत्सं न वसुनः सिचामहे ||
pra te nāvaṁ na samane vacasyuvam brahmaṇā yāmi savaneṣu dādhṛṣiḥ | kuvin no asya vacaso nibodhiṣad indram utsaṁ na vasunaḥ sicāmahe ||
  • pra te nāvaṁ na samane vacasyuvam: This phrase can be translated as "We seek your guidance, O divine force, in our endeavors." Here, "nāvaṁ" refers to a boat or vessel, symbolizing the journey of life, while "vacasyuvam" indicates the power of speech or communication. This suggests that we are asking for divine assistance in navigating the challenges we face.
  • brahmaṇā yāmi savaneṣu dādhṛṣiḥ: This part means "With the support of the divine, I move forward in my endeavors." "Brahmaṇā" refers to the ultimate reality or cosmic spirit, while "savaneṣu" relates to the act of offering or sacrifice. This emphasizes the importance of aligning our actions with a higher purpose and seeking divine blessings in our pursuits.
  • kuvin no asya vacaso nibodhiṣad: Translated, this means "May we understand the essence of this divine speech." "Kuvin" refers to wisdom or knowledge, and "nibodhiṣad" suggests the act of comprehending or grasping. This highlights the significance of understanding the deeper meanings behind our words and actions, encouraging us to seek wisdom in our communication.
  • indram utsaṁ na vasunaḥ sicāmahe: This phrase translates to "We invoke the strength of Indra, the divine warrior." "Indra" is a prominent deity in the Rigveda, symbolizing power and strength, while "utsaṁ" refers to vigor or energy. This signifies the importance of invoking strength and courage in our lives, especially when facing challenges.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of seeking divine guidance and wisdom in our endeavors. It teaches us to align our actions with a higher purpose and to invoke strength when facing challenges. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various situations such as starting a new project, making important decisions, or overcoming obstacles. By recognizing the need for divine support and understanding the deeper meanings of our actions, we can navigate life's journey with greater clarity and strength.
