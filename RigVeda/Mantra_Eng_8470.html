सो अग्रे अह्नां हरिर हर्यतो मदः प्र चेतसā चेतयते अनु द्युिभिः |द्वा जनā यातयन्न अन्तर ईयते नरā च शंसं दैव्यं च धार्तरि ||
so agre ahnāṁ harir haryato madaḥ pra cetasā cetayate anu dyubhiḥ | dvā janā yātayann antar īyate narā ca śaṁsaṁ daivyaṁ ca dhartari ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • so: that
    • agre: in the beginning
    • ahnāṁ: of the day
    • harir: the sun or the divine
    • haryato: of the one who brings joy
    • madaḥ: intoxication or bliss
    • pra: indeed
    • cetasā: with the mind
    • cetayate: inspires or awakens
    • anu: after
    • dyubhiḥ: the heavens
    • dvā: two
    • janā: beings or people
    • yātayann: leading or guiding
    • antar: within
    • īyate: is moving or going
    • narā: men or humans
    • ca: and
    • śaṁsaṁ: praise or glory
    • daivyaṁ: divine
    • ca: and
    • dhartari: in the sustainer or the earth
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the divine presence that inspires and awakens the mind, particularly in the context of the sun's rising and the joy it brings.
    • The reference to "two beings" suggests a duality in existence, possibly representing the balance between the material and spiritual worlds.
    • The mantra emphasizes the importance of divine guidance in our lives, as it leads us towards praise and glory, both in our personal endeavors and in our connection to the universe.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, one can relate this mantra to the experience of waking up to a new day, where the rising sun symbolizes hope and new beginnings.
    • In moments of decision-making, the "divine guidance" can be interpreted as intuition or a sense of inner knowing that helps individuals navigate challenges.
    • In relationships, the "two beings" can represent partnerships where mutual support and understanding lead to personal growth and shared joy.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of divine inspiration and the joy that comes from recognizing the interconnectedness of all beings.
    • It serves as a reminder to seek guidance from within and from the universe, fostering a sense of purpose and harmony in our lives.
    • In practical terms, it encourages individuals to embrace each day with gratitude and to acknowledge the divine presence in their journey, leading to a more fulfilling and meaningful existence.
