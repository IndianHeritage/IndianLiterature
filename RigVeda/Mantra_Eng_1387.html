पूर्वा विश्वस्माद bhuvanād abोधि जयन्ती वाजम् बृहती सन्तु्री |उच्चा व्य अख्यद युवतिः पुनर्भूर ओषा अगन प्रथमā पूर्वहूतौ ||
pūrvā viśvasmād bhuvanād abodhi jayantī vājam bṛhatī sanutrī | uccā vy akhyad yuvatiḥ punarbhūr oṣā agan prathamā pūrvahūtau ||
  • pūrvā viśvasmād bhuvanād abodhi: This phrase can be translated as "From the ancient, all-encompassing universe, the awakening." It signifies the idea of enlightenment and the emergence of knowledge from the vastness of existence.
  • jayantī vājam bṛhatī sanutrī: This translates to "The victorious one, the great speech, the nurturing." Here, "jayantī" refers to victory, while "vājam bṛhatī" indicates the power of speech or communication that is profound and nurturing.
  • uccā vy akhyad yuvatiḥ punarbhūr: This can be interpreted as "The young woman, elevated, has proclaimed again." It suggests a renewal or a resurgence of ideas or values, often associated with youth and vitality.
  • oṣā agan prathamā pūrvahūtau: This means "The light has come forth first from the ancient." It emphasizes the importance of light, knowledge, and the initial spark of creation or understanding that comes from the past.

  • Significance: This mantra encapsulates the essence of knowledge, communication, and the cyclical nature of life and enlightenment. It highlights the importance of drawing wisdom from the past to foster growth and understanding in the present.
  • Real-life Application: In today's world, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as education, where learning from history and ancient wisdom can guide current practices. For instance, educators can draw upon traditional teaching methods that have proven effective over time, integrating them with modern techniques to enhance student engagement and understanding.
  • Moreover, in personal development, individuals can reflect on their past experiences to gain insights that inform their future decisions, embodying the idea of "awakening" from past lessons.

  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of knowledge across time. It encourages individuals and societies to honor their roots while striving for progress. By recognizing the value of ancient wisdom, we can cultivate a more enlightened and nurturing environment for future generations.
