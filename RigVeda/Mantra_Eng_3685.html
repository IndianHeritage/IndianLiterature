अस्माकं अग्नि अध्वरं जुषस्व सहसः सूनो त्रिषधस्थ हव्यं |वयं देवेषु सुक्रतः स्याम शर्माṇā नस् त्रिवरूथेन पाहि ||
asmākam agne adhvaraṁ juṣasva sahasaḥ sūno triṣadhastha havyam | vayaṁ deveṣu sukṛtaḥ syāma śarmaṇā nas trivarūthena pāhi ||
  • asmākam agne: This phrase translates to "O Agni, our fire." Agni is a significant deity in Vedic literature, representing fire, energy, and transformation. In real life, fire symbolizes warmth, light, and the energy that fuels our daily activities.
  • adhvaraṁ juṣasva: This means "please accept our offerings." In a practical sense, this can be seen as a call for divine acceptance of our efforts and actions, much like how we seek validation in our personal and professional lives.
  • sahasaḥ sūno triṣadhastha havyam: This translates to "the offerings that are made with strength and devotion." It emphasizes the importance of sincerity and effort in our endeavors. For example, in a workplace, a project that is approached with dedication and hard work is more likely to succeed.
  • vayaṁ deveṣu sukṛtaḥ syāma: This means "may we be virtuous among the gods." It reflects the aspiration to lead a life of righteousness and integrity. In everyday life, this can be related to ethical behavior in our interactions with others, striving to be honest and just.
  • śarmaṇā nas trivarūthena pāhi: This translates to "protect us with your threefold shelter." This can be interpreted as a plea for safety and security in various aspects of life—physical, emotional, and spiritual. For instance, having a supportive family, a stable job, and a strong community can provide this threefold protection.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a deep yearning for divine guidance, protection, and acceptance in our lives. It emphasizes the importance of sincerity in our actions, the pursuit of virtue, and the need for security in various forms. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can inspire individuals to approach their goals with dedication, seek ethical paths, and cultivate supportive relationships, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling and harmonious existence.
