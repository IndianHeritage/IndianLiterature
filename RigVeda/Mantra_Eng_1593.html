यो नो अग्नि अररिवां अघायुर अरातिवा मर्चयति द्वयेना |मन्त्रः गुरुः पुनर अस्तु सो अस्मानु मृक्षीष्ट तंवं दुरुक्तैः ||
yo no agne ararivām̐ aghāyur arātīvā marcayati dvayena | mantro guruḥ punar astu so asmā anu mṛkṣīṣṭa tanvaṁ duruktaiḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yo: who
    • no: our
    • agne: O Agni (the fire god)
    • ararivām̐: who is the one who brings forth
    • aghāyur: free from sin or evil
    • arātīvā: who is the one who protects
    • marcayati: who inspires or motivates
    • dvayena: with two (aspects or qualities)
    • mantro: the mantra or sacred utterance
    • guruḥ: the teacher or guide
    • punar: again
    • astu: may it be
    • so: he
    • asmā: for us
    • anu: following
    • mṛkṣīṣṭa: may we attain or achieve
    • tanvaṁ: the body or form
    • duruktaiḥ: with harsh or unkind words
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes Agni, the fire god, who is seen as a purifying force and a protector.
    • It emphasizes the importance of being free from sin and evil, suggesting that purity of heart and intention is crucial.
    • The mention of "two aspects" could refer to the dual nature of existence, such as good and evil, or the physical and spiritual realms.
    • The role of the guru (teacher) is highlighted, indicating that guidance is essential in navigating life's challenges.
    • The plea for protection from harsh words signifies the need for compassion and understanding in communication.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace setting, invoking the qualities of Agni can inspire teamwork and collaboration, promoting a positive environment free from negativity.
    • In personal relationships, striving for purity of intention and communication can lead to healthier interactions and conflict resolution.
    • In educational contexts, the role of a mentor or teacher is crucial in guiding students through their learning journey, emphasizing the importance of wisdom and support.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of purity, guidance, and compassionate communication in our lives.
    • Its significance lies in the acknowledgment of the dualities we face and the need for a guiding force to help us navigate them.
    • By embodying the principles of this mantra, individuals can foster a more harmonious and understanding environment in their personal and professional lives.
