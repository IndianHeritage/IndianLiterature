बृहत् स्वच्छन्द्रम् अमवद् यद् उक्त्यं अकृत्वता भियसां रोहणं दिवः |यन् मानवप्रधना इन्द्रं ऊतयः स्वर नृशाचो मरुतोऽमदन्न अनु ||
bṛhat svaścandram amavad yad ukthya1m akṛṇvata bhiyasā rohaṇaṁ divaḥ | yan mānuṣapradhanā indram ūtayaḥ svar nṛṣāco maruto 'madann anu ||
  • Mantra: "bṛhat svaścandram amavad yad ukthya1m akṛṇvata bhiyasā rohaṇaṁ divaḥ | yan mānuṣapradhanā indram ūtayaḥ svar nṛṣāco maruto 'madann anu ||"
  • Translation: This mantra speaks of the vastness of the moon and the celestial realms, invoking the divine energies that govern the universe. It highlights the importance of human effort in reaching out to the divine, particularly through the worship of Indra, the king of gods, and the Maruts, the storm deities.
  • Word Breakdown:
    • bṛhat: vast, great
    • svaścandram: the moon, representing illumination and knowledge
    • amavad: to speak or declare
    • ukthya: hymns or praises
    • akṛṇvata: those who perform or create
    • bhiyasā: with strength or power
    • rohaṇaṁ: ascent or elevation
    • divaḥ: of the heavens or sky
    • mānuṣapradhanā: those who are foremost among humans
    • indram: Indra, the king of gods
    • ūtayaḥ: to invoke or call upon
    • svar: the celestial realm or heaven
    • nṛṣāco: the human beings
    • maruto: the Maruts, storm deities
    • 'madann: who partake or consume
    • anu: after or following
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • This mantra can be related to the pursuit of knowledge and enlightenment in our lives. Just as the moon illuminates the night, individuals seek knowledge to enlighten their paths.
    • The invocation of Indra can be seen in the context of leadership and strength. In modern society, leaders are often called upon to guide their communities through challenges, much like Indra's role in the Vedic texts.
    • The Maruts symbolize the forces of nature and the importance of respecting and understanding these forces in our daily lives, such as in environmental conservation efforts.
  • Conclusion: This mantra emphasizes the significance of human effort in connecting with the divine and the natural world. It teaches us that through our actions, prayers, and respect for nature, we can elevate ourselves and invoke the blessings of the divine. In real life, this can be applied in various contexts, such as personal growth, leadership, and environmental stewardship, reminding us of our interconnectedness with the universe.
