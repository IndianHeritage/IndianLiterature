मन्त्र: नि शुष्मम् इन्दव एषाम् पुरुहूत जनानाम् |यो अस्मां ādideśati ||
ni śuṣmam indav eṣām puruhūta janānām | yo asmām̐ ādideśati ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ni: not
    • śuṣmam: dry, barren
    • indav: of the moon, lunar
    • eṣām: of these
    • puruhūta: many invocations, many offerings
    • janānām: of the people, of the beings
    • yo: who
    • asmām̐: to us
    • ādideśati: gives direction, commands
  • Overall Meaning: This mantra can be interpreted as a call for the divine to not let the people be dry or barren, but instead to provide them with the nourishing light of the moon. It emphasizes the importance of divine guidance and the need for spiritual sustenance in the lives of individuals and communities.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • Spiritual Guidance: Just as the moon provides light in the darkness, individuals often seek guidance from spiritual leaders or mentors during challenging times. For instance, a person facing a difficult decision may consult a trusted advisor to gain clarity and direction.
    • Community Support: In a community setting, this mantra can reflect the collective need for support and nourishment. For example, during a crisis, community members may come together to provide resources and emotional support, ensuring that no one feels isolated or barren.
    • Personal Growth: On a personal level, individuals may engage in practices such as meditation or prayer to seek inner peace and clarity, akin to receiving the moon's light in their lives. This can help them navigate through life's challenges with a sense of purpose.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its reminder of the importance of divine guidance and community support in overcoming life's challenges. It encourages individuals to seek nourishment—both spiritual and emotional—so that they do not feel dry or barren in their lives. In real-life scenarios, this can manifest through seeking mentorship, fostering community connections, and engaging in personal growth practices. Ultimately, it highlights the interconnectedness of individuals and the divine, urging us to remain open to guidance and support from both spiritual and communal sources.
