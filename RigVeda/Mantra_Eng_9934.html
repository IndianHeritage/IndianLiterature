यद देवापिः शान्तनवे पुरोहितो होत्राय वृत्तः कृपयन्न अदीदhet |देवश्रुतं वृष्टिवणिं रराणो बृहस्पतिर वाचं अस्मा अयच्छत ||
yad devāpiḥ śaṁtanave purohito hotrāya vṛtaḥ kṛpayann adīdhet | devaśrutaṁ vṛṣṭivaniṁ rarāṇo bṛhaspatir vācam asmā ayacchat ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yad: whatever
    • devāpiḥ: the gods
    • śaṁtanave: in the lineage
    • purohito: the priest or the one who performs rituals
    • hotrāya: for the one who offers sacrifices
    • vṛtaḥ: chosen or selected
    • kṛpayann: showing compassion
    • adīdhet: may he inspire or bless
    • devaśrutaṁ: the divine sound or voice
    • vṛṣṭivaniṁ: the one who brings rain
    • rarāṇo: having been invoked
    • bṛhaspatir: the great teacher or guru
    • vācam: speech or words
    • asmā: for us
    • ayacchat: may he grant or bestow
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the divine connection between the gods and the rituals performed by priests.
    • It emphasizes the importance of compassion and blessings from the divine, particularly in the context of rituals that seek to invoke prosperity and abundance.
    • The mention of "bṛhaspatir" refers to the deity associated with wisdom and knowledge, suggesting that through divine guidance, one can attain clarity and understanding.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern life, this mantra can be related to the practice of seeking guidance and blessings before starting any significant endeavor, such as a new business or project.
    • For instance, many people perform rituals or prayers before a wedding or a major life event, invoking divine blessings for success and harmony.
    • In agriculture, farmers often pray for rain and good harvests, reflecting the invocation of "vṛṣṭivaniṁ" to ensure prosperity.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of divine intervention and the role of rituals in connecting with higher powers.
    • It serves as a reminder of the importance of compassion, wisdom, and the need for guidance in our lives.
    • In essence, it encourages individuals to seek blessings and to be mindful of the spiritual dimensions of their actions, fostering a sense of community and shared purpose.
