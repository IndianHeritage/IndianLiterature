सर्वं परिक्रोशं जहि जम्बह्या कृकदाश्वम् |आ तु न इन्द्र शंसय गोष्व अश्वेषु शुभ्रिषु सहस्रेषु तुवीमघ ||
sarvam parikrośaṁ jahi jambhayā kṛkadāśvam | ā tū na indra śaṁsaya goṣv aśveṣu śubhriṣu sahasreṣu tuvīmagha ||
  • Translation: "O Indra, drive away all fears and obstacles, like a lion chasing away a deer. May you grant us strength and abundance, as you bestow blessings upon thousands of cows and horses."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • Sarvam: All, everything.
    • Parikrośaṁ: Fear, anxiety, or obstacles.
    • Jahi: Drive away, remove.
    • Jambhayā: Like a lion, symbolizing strength and courage.
    • Kṛkadāśvam: A metaphorical reference, possibly indicating a powerful force.
    • Ā tū: May you grant.
    • Na: Not.
    • Indra: The king of gods, representing power and authority.
    • Śaṁsaya: Doubt or uncertainty.
    • Goṣv: Cows, symbolizing wealth and prosperity.
    • Aśveṣu: Horses, representing strength and valor.
    • Śubhriṣu: Auspicious or beneficial.
    • Sahasreṣu: Thousands, indicating abundance.
    • Tuvīmagha: Bestow blessings or wealth.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In times of personal crisis, such as losing a job or facing health issues, individuals often seek strength and support from their community or spiritual beliefs, akin to invoking Indra's blessings to drive away fears.
    • In business, when facing competition or market challenges, leaders may draw inspiration from this mantra to overcome doubts and pursue success with confidence, much like a lion asserting its dominance.
    • During significant life events, such as weddings or new ventures, people often perform rituals to invoke blessings for prosperity and abundance, reflecting the essence of this mantra.
  • Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of overcoming fears and doubts with strength and confidence. It serves as a reminder that, like Indra, we can seek support from higher powers or our inner strength to navigate challenges. In real life, this mantra can be applied in various situations, from personal struggles to professional endeavors, encouraging individuals to pursue their goals with courage and to seek abundance in all aspects of life.
