मन्त्र: निः येन मुष्ठिहत्यया नि वृत्राः रुṇधामहै | त्वोतासो न्य अरवताः ||
ni yena muṣṭihatyayā ni vṛtrā ruṇadhāmahai | tvotāso ny arvatā ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ni: not
    • yena: by whom
    • muṣṭihatyayā: with a fist (or strength)
    • ni: not
    • vṛtrā: the obstructors or enemies
    • ruṇadhāmahai: who make noise or create disturbance
    • tvotāso: your praises
    • ny: indeed
    • arvatā: of the strong or powerful
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about overcoming obstacles and enemies through strength and determination.
    • The phrase "by whom strength is not used" suggests that true power does not rely solely on physical force.
    • The mention of "obstructors" indicates challenges or adversities that one may face in life.
    • The reference to "your praises" implies that acknowledging and celebrating one's strengths can lead to empowerment.
    • Overall, the mantra emphasizes the importance of inner strength and resilience in the face of challenges.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, an employee may face challenges from competitors (the "obstructors"). Instead of resorting to unethical practices (using strength), they can rely on their skills and creativity to succeed.
    • In personal relationships, conflicts may arise (the "noise"). Instead of reacting with anger, one can choose to communicate effectively and resolve issues peacefully.
    • In sports, athletes often encounter tough opponents. Instead of relying solely on physical strength, they train their minds and strategies to overcome these challenges.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder that true strength lies not in physical might but in resilience, wisdom, and the ability to navigate life's challenges with grace.
    • It encourages individuals to focus on their inner qualities and to celebrate their achievements, fostering a sense of empowerment.
    • In real-life scenarios, applying the principles of this mantra can lead to personal growth, improved relationships, and success in various endeavors.
