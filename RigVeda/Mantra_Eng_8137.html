आविषन् कलशं सुतो विश्वा अर्षन्न अभि श्रीयः |शूरो न गोषु तिष्ठति ||
āviśan kalaśaṁ suto viśvā arṣann abhi śriyaḥ | śūro na goṣu tiṣṭhati ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • āviśan: Entered or pervaded
    • kalaśaṁ: The pot or vessel
    • suto: The son or offspring
    • viśvā: All or universal
    • arṣann: Having attained or achieved
    • abhi: Towards or in the direction of
    • śriyaḥ: Prosperity or wealth
    • śūro: The brave or the hero
    • na: Not
    • goṣu: In the cows or among the cattle
    • tiṣṭhati: Stands or remains
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of a divine presence that enters and pervades the vessel (kalaśa), symbolizing abundance and prosperity.
    • The phrase "suto viśvā" suggests that this presence is universal and encompasses all aspects of life, much like a nurturing parent who provides for all their children.
    • The term "arṣann abhi śriyaḥ" indicates that this divine presence is directed towards prosperity, suggesting that it brings wealth and success to those who embrace it.
    • Finally, the line "śūro na goṣu tiṣṭhati" implies that true bravery and strength do not reside solely in material possessions (like cattle) but in the spiritual and moral virtues that one embodies.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be interpreted as a reminder that true success comes from ethical practices and a commitment to the greater good, rather than just financial gain.
    • In personal life, it encourages individuals to cultivate inner strength and virtues, suggesting that real bravery is about standing up for what is right, rather than merely accumulating wealth.
    • In community settings, it can inspire collective efforts towards prosperity, emphasizing that when individuals work together with a shared purpose, they can achieve greater success than when they act alone.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of spiritual wealth over material wealth, urging individuals to seek a deeper understanding of prosperity that encompasses ethical living and communal harmony.
    • Its application in real life is profound, as it serves as a guiding principle for personal development, business ethics, and community engagement.
    • Ultimately, it teaches that true strength lies in the virtues we cultivate and the positive impact we have on the world around us.
