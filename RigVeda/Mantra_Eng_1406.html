स्वसा स्वस्रे ज्यायस्यै योनिम आरिग अपैत्य अस्याः प्रतिचक्ष्येवा |व्युञ्चन्ती रश्मिभिः सूर्यस्याञ्ज्य अङ्क्ते समनगाऽइव व्राः ||
svasā svasre jyāyasyai yonim āraig apaity asyāḥ praticakṣyeva | vyucchantī raśmibhiḥ sūryasyāñjy aṅkte samanagā iva vrāḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • svasā: sister
    • svasre: to the sister
    • jyāyasyai: of greater strength
    • yonim: womb or source
    • āraig: to be born or to arise
    • apaity: to go away or to depart
    • asyāḥ: of this
    • praticakṣyeva: as if to see
    • vyucchantī: separating or dispersing
    • raśmibhiḥ: by rays
    • sūryasyāñjy: of the sun
    • aṅkte: to embrace or to cover
    • samanagā: like the united or together
    • iva: like or as
    • vrāḥ: the brave or the warriors
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the bond between sisters, emphasizing the strength and unity that exists within familial relationships.
    • The phrase "svasā svasre" highlights the importance of sisterhood, suggesting that sisters are a source of strength and support for one another.
    • The imagery of rays from the sun ("raśmibhiḥ sūryasyāñjy") symbolizes enlightenment and guidance, indicating that sisters can illuminate each other's paths in life.
    • The idea of separation ("vyucchantī") can be interpreted as the challenges and distances that may arise in relationships, yet the mantra suggests that these can be overcome through love and unity.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In many cultures, sisters often play a crucial role in each other's lives, providing emotional support during difficult times, much like the mantra suggests.
    • Consider a scenario where one sister is going through a tough breakup; the other sister may step in to offer comfort and advice, embodying the strength and unity described in the mantra.
    • In professional settings, sisters may collaborate on projects, leveraging their unique strengths to achieve common goals, reflecting the idea of "greater strength" in unity.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of sisterhood and the bonds that tie family members together.
    • It serves as a reminder of the strength found in unity and the importance of supporting one another through life's challenges.
    • In real-life scenarios, the principles outlined in this mantra can be applied to foster stronger relationships, encourage collaboration, and promote emotional well-being among siblings and family members.
