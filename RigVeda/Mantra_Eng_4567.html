आ सहस्रं पथिभिर इन्द्र रायाऽ तुविद्युम्न तुविवाजेभिर अर्वाक् | याहि सूनो सहसो यस्य नू चिद अदेव ईशे पुरुहूत यतोः ||
ā sahasram pathibhir indra rāyā tuvidyumna tuvivājebhir arvāk | yāhi sūno sahaso yasya nū cid adeva īśe puruhūta yotoḥ ||
  • Transliteration: ā sahasram pathibhir indra rāyā tuvidyumna tuvivājebhir arvāk
  • Translation: "O Indra, with a thousand paths, may you come to us, endowed with strength and power, and may you grant us the wealth that is sought after."
  • Meaning of Key Terms:
    • Indra: The king of gods in Vedic tradition, representing strength, power, and the force of nature.
    • Sahasram: Thousand; symbolizes abundance and infinite possibilities.
    • Pathibhir: Paths; refers to the various ways or methods to achieve goals.
    • Tuvidyumna: Endowed with strength; signifies the importance of power and capability in achieving success.
    • Tuvivājebhir: Granting wealth; emphasizes the desire for prosperity and resources.
    • Arvāk: May you come; a call for divine intervention and support.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be seen as a call for guidance and support in navigating the complexities of the market. Just as Indra is invoked for strength, entrepreneurs often seek mentorship and resources to help them succeed.
    • In personal development, individuals may reflect on the paths they can take to achieve their goals, invoking the spirit of Indra to provide clarity and strength in their endeavors.
    • In community initiatives, leaders may use this mantra to rally support and resources for projects aimed at improving the lives of others, seeking the collective strength of the community.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its invocation of divine strength and guidance in the pursuit of wealth and success. It teaches us that while we may have many paths to choose from, the support of higher powers and the strength within ourselves are crucial in achieving our goals. In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, from personal aspirations to collective community efforts, reminding us to seek strength and clarity in our pursuits.
