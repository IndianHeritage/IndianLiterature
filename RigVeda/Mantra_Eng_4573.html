इन्द्रं एव धिषणा सातये धाद बृहन्तं ऋष्वम् अजरो युवानम् |अशाल्हेन शवसा शूशुवांसं सद्यश्चिद्यो वावृधे असामि ||
indram eva dhiṣaṇā sātaye dhād bṛhantam ṛṣvam ajaraṁ yuvānam | aṣāḻhena śavasā śūśuvāṁsaṁ sadyaś cid yo vāvṛdhe asāmi ||
  • Indram eva dhiṣaṇā: This phrase refers to Indra, the king of the gods in Vedic literature, who represents strength, power, and leadership. The term "dhiṣaṇā" implies a sense of guidance or direction.
  • Sātaye dhād: "Sātaye" means for the purpose of truth or righteousness, while "dhād" suggests the act of establishing or supporting. Together, this indicates the importance of establishing truth and righteousness in our lives.
  • Bṛhantam ṛṣvam ajaraṁ yuvānam: "Bṛhantam" means great or vast, "ṛṣvam" refers to strength or vigor, "ajaraṁ" signifies immortality, and "yuvānam" means youthful. This line emphasizes the qualities of greatness, strength, and eternal youthfulness, which are essential for a fulfilling life.
  • Aṣāḻhena śavasā: "Aṣāḻhena" means without deceit or falsehood, and "śavasā" refers to the essence or core of being. This suggests that one should strive to be genuine and true to oneself.
  • Śūśuvāṁsaṁ: This term can be interpreted as the essence of life or vitality. It emphasizes the importance of nurturing our inner strength and vitality.
  • Sadyaś cid yo vāvṛdhe asāmi: "Sadyaś" means immediately or promptly, "cid" refers to consciousness or awareness, and "yo vāvṛdhe" implies that which has grown or developed. "Asāmi" suggests a state of being or existence. This line highlights the importance of being aware and present in the moment, allowing for growth and development in our lives.

Conclusion: This mantra encapsulates the essence of strength, truth, and authenticity. It encourages individuals to embody the qualities of Indra, striving for greatness and righteousness while remaining true to themselves. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in leadership roles, personal development, and relationships. By embracing these principles, one can foster a life of integrity, vitality, and continuous growth, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling existence.
