विज्ञानं छ्यावाः शितिपादो अक्ष्यन रथं हिरण्यप्रौगं वहन्तः |शाश्वद् विषः सवितुर्दैव्यस्योपस्थे विश्वा भुवनानि तस्थुः ||
vi janāñ chyāvāḥ śitipādo akhyan rathaṁ hiraṇyapraügaṁ vahantaḥ | śaśvad viśaḥ savitur daivyasyopasthe viśvā bhuvanāni tasthuḥ ||
  • vi janāñ chyāvāḥ: This phrase can be interpreted as "those who are aware" or "those who understand." It emphasizes the importance of knowledge and awareness in life.
  • śitipādo akhyan: "The one who has a firm foundation" or "the one who is stable." This suggests the significance of having a strong base or grounding in one's life, akin to having a solid education or moral values.
  • rathaṁ hiraṇyapraügaṁ vahantaḥ: This translates to "carrying the golden chariot." The chariot symbolizes progress and movement towards goals, while "golden" signifies value and prosperity. In real life, this can be likened to pursuing one's dreams with dedication and integrity.
  • śaśvad viśaḥ: Meaning "may there be eternal peace" or "may there be everlasting harmony." This reflects the desire for a peaceful existence, which is essential in personal relationships and community living.
  • savitur daivyasyopasthe: This phrase can be understood as "on the lap of the divine light." It signifies the nurturing aspect of divine energy, akin to the support we receive from our families and mentors in our lives.
  • viśvā bhuvanāni tasthuḥ: This means "may all worlds be established." It conveys a sense of universal well-being and interconnectedness, reminding us that our actions impact not just ourselves but the entire community and environment.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of knowledge, stability, progress, peace, divine support, and universal well-being. In real life, it serves as a reminder to seek knowledge, build a strong foundation, pursue our goals with integrity, foster peace in our relationships, appreciate the support we receive, and recognize our responsibility towards the greater community. By embodying these principles, we can lead a fulfilling and harmonious life, contributing positively to the world around us.
