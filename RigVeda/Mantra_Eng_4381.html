मन्त्र: पुरूण्य अग्ने पुरुधा त्वायां वसूनि राजन् वसुतां ते अश्यां | पुरूणि हि त्वे पुरुवारा सन्त्य अग्ने वसु विधते राजनि त्वे ||
purūṇy agne purudhā tvāyā vasūni rājan vasutā te aśyām | purūṇi hi tve puruvāra santy agne vasu vidhate rājani tve ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • purūṇy: abundant, plentiful
    • agne: O Agni (the fire god)
    • purudhā: in various forms
    • tvāyā: to you
    • vasūni: wealth, riches
    • rājan: O king
    • vasutā: through your wealth
    • te: your
    • aśyām: auspicious, favorable
    • purūṇi: abundant
    • hi: indeed
    • tve: you
    • puruvāra: the one who is the best
    • santy: exist
    • agne: O Agni
    • vasu: wealth
    • vidhate: bestows
    • rājani: O ruler
    • tve: you
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra addresses Agni, the fire god, who is often seen as a mediator between humans and the divine.
    • The invocation highlights the abundance and various forms of wealth that Agni can bestow upon the devotee.
    • It emphasizes the importance of Agni in providing auspiciousness and prosperity in life.
    • The repetition of "purūṇy" signifies the endless nature of blessings and wealth that can be received.
    • By calling Agni "rājan" (king), it acknowledges his supreme authority and power in the cosmic order.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, invoking Agni can symbolize the pursuit of success and prosperity through hard work and dedication.
    • In personal life, it can represent the quest for a fulfilling and abundant life, where one seeks to cultivate positive relationships and opportunities.
    • During rituals or ceremonies, this mantra can be recited to seek blessings for wealth and auspiciousness in new ventures, such as starting a new job or launching a project.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of seeking divine blessings for abundance and prosperity in all aspects of life.
    • It highlights the role of Agni as a facilitator of wealth and auspiciousness, encouraging individuals to cultivate a relationship with the divine for guidance and support.
    • In practical terms, it can be applied in daily life by fostering a mindset of gratitude and openness to receiving blessings, while also putting in the necessary effort to achieve one's goals.
