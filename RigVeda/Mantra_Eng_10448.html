मन्त्र: भद्रं वै वरं वृणते भद्रं युञ्जन्ति दक्षिणम् |भद्रं वैवस्वते चक्षुर बहुत्रा जीवतो मनः ||
bhadraṁ vai varaṁ vṛṇate bhadraṁ yuñjanti dakṣiṇam | bhadraṁ vaivasvate cakṣur bahutrā jīvato manaḥ ||
  • bhadraṁ vai varaṁ vṛṇate: This phrase translates to "the auspicious one chooses the best." Here, "bhadraṁ" signifies auspiciousness or goodness, while "varaṁ" refers to the best or the most excellent. In real life, this can be seen in situations where individuals or communities strive to make choices that lead to positive outcomes, such as selecting a career path that aligns with their values and passions.
  • bhadraṁ yuñjanti dakṣiṇam: This means "the auspicious one unites with the right hand." The right hand is often associated with action and positivity in many cultures. This can be interpreted as the importance of taking positive actions and making decisions that lead to harmony and prosperity. For example, in a business setting, collaborating with trustworthy partners can lead to successful ventures.
  • bhadraṁ vaivasvate cakṣur: This translates to "the auspicious eye of the sun." "Vaivasvate" refers to the sun, which is a symbol of light, knowledge, and clarity. This can be related to the idea of enlightenment and awareness in our lives. For instance, gaining knowledge through education can illuminate our paths and help us make informed decisions.
  • bahutrā jīvato manaḥ: This means "the mind of the living is manifold." It suggests that the human mind is complex and capable of diverse thoughts and emotions. In real life, this can be observed in how individuals navigate their feelings and thoughts in various situations, such as dealing with stress or making life choices. It emphasizes the importance of mental health and self-awareness.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of making wise and auspicious choices in life. It encourages individuals to act positively, seek knowledge, and be aware of the complexities of their minds. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in personal decision-making, professional collaborations, and the pursuit of knowledge. By embodying the principles of this mantra, individuals can foster a life filled with positivity, clarity, and harmony.
