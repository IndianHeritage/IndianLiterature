आयाः चित्तो विपानया हरिः पवस्व धारणया |युजं वाजेषु चोदय ||
ayā citto vipānayā hariḥ pavasva dhārayā | yujaṁ vājeṣu codaya ||
  • ayā - This word can be interpreted as "come" or "arrive." It signifies an invitation or a call to a higher power or energy.
  • citto - This refers to the "mind" or "consciousness." It emphasizes the importance of mental clarity and focus.
  • vipānayā - This can be understood as "to remove" or "to dispel." It suggests the act of clearing away obstacles or distractions.
  • hariḥ - This term is often associated with the divine, particularly Lord Vishnu or Krishna. It represents the ultimate source of energy and sustenance.
  • pavasva - This means "to purify" or "to cleanse." It indicates a desire for spiritual purification and the removal of impurities from the mind and soul.
  • dhārayā - This translates to "to hold" or "to support." It signifies the need for stability and strength in one's life.
  • yujaṁ - This word means "to unite" or "to join." It emphasizes the importance of connection, whether with oneself, others, or the divine.
  • vājeṣu - This refers to "in the powers" or "in the forces." It highlights the strength and energy present in the universe.
  • codaya - This means "to inspire" or "to motivate." It signifies the call for divine guidance and encouragement in one's endeavors.

The mantra can be interpreted as a prayer for mental clarity and spiritual purification. It calls upon the divine to arrive and assist in dispelling distractions, purifying the mind, and providing strength and inspiration. The essence of this mantra is about seeking divine support in achieving unity and clarity in life.


In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts:

  • Meditation and Mindfulness: Practicing this mantra during meditation can help individuals focus their minds, dispel negative thoughts, and achieve a state of inner peace.
  • Personal Challenges: When facing difficulties, reciting this mantra can serve as a reminder to seek divine guidance and support, helping individuals to stay grounded and motivated.
  • Teamwork and Collaboration: In group settings, this mantra can inspire unity and cooperation, encouraging team members to work together harmoniously towards a common goal.

In conclusion, this RigVeda mantra holds significant importance as it encapsulates the essence of seeking divine assistance for mental clarity, purification, and strength. It serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of all beings and the importance of unity in overcoming life's challenges.
