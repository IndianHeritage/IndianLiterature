देवाश्चित्ते असूर्य प्रचेतसो बृहस्पते यज्ञियम भागम आनाśुḥ |उष्रा इव सूर्यो ज्योतीषां महो विश्वेषां इज जनिता ब्रह्मणाम् असि ||
devāś cit te asurya pracetaso bṛhaspate yajñiyam bhāgam ānaśuḥ | usrā iva sūryo jyotiṣā maho viśveṣām ij janitā brahmaṇām asi ||
  • Translation: "O Devas, you who are wise and powerful, grant us the share of the offerings that is due to us, O Bṛhaspati. Like the sun, you shine with great light, and you are the source of all creation."
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • devāś: O Devas (gods)
    • cit: wise, intelligent
    • te: your
    • asurya: powerful, radiant
    • pracetaso: of the wise
    • bṛhaspate: O Bṛhaspati (the deity of wisdom and knowledge)
    • yajñiyam: pertaining to offerings or sacrifices
    • bhāgam: share, portion
    • ānaśuḥ: grant, bestow
    • usrā: like the sun
    • iva: as, like
    • sūryo: sun
    • jyotiṣā: with light
    • maho: great
    • viśveṣām: of all beings
    • ij: who is born
    • janitā: creator
    • brahmaṇām: of the Brahman (ultimate reality)
    • asi: you are
  • Significance:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of divine wisdom and the role of Bṛhaspati as a guide in our lives.
    • It highlights the need for seeking blessings from the divine for the right share of offerings, which can be interpreted as seeking guidance and support in our endeavors.
    • The comparison to the sun signifies the illuminating power of knowledge and wisdom, which dispels ignorance and leads to enlightenment.
  • Real-Life Application:
    • In daily life, this mantra can be invoked during moments of decision-making or when seeking clarity in complex situations.
    • It serves as a reminder to seek wisdom from knowledgeable sources, whether they be mentors, books, or spiritual teachings.
    • In a broader sense, it encourages individuals to contribute positively to society, akin to making offerings that benefit the community.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking divine guidance and wisdom in our lives.
    • By acknowledging the role of higher powers and striving for knowledge, we can navigate life's challenges more effectively.
    • Ultimately, it teaches us the value of humility and the importance of sharing our knowledge and resources for the greater good.
