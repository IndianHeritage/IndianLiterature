एतेनाग्ने ब्रह्मणा वावृधस्व शक्ती वा यत ते चक्रिमा विदा वा |उत प्र णेष्य अभि वस्यो अस्मान सं नः सृजा सुमत्यां वाजवत्या ||
etenāgne brahmaṇā vāvṛdhasva śaktī vā yat te cakṛmā vidā vā | uta pra ṇeṣy abhi vasyo asmān saṁ naḥ sṛja sumatyā vājavatyā ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • etenā: By this
    • agne: O Fire
    • brahmaṇā: With the power of the divine
    • vāvṛdhasva: May you grow or expand
    • śaktī: Energy or strength
    • vā: Or
    • yat: Whatever
    • te: Your
    • cakṛmā: We perform or create
    • vidā: Knowledge or wisdom
    • uta: And
    • pra ṇeṣy: May we be led
    • abhi: Towards
    • vasyo: Under control
    • asmān: Us
    • saṁ: Together
    • naḥ: Our
    • sṛja: Create or produce
    • sumatyā: Good thoughts
    • vājavatyā: Abundant wealth or strength
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Agni, the fire god, asking for the expansion of divine energy and strength.
    • It emphasizes the importance of knowledge and wisdom in our actions and creations.
    • The mantra seeks guidance and control over our thoughts and actions, aiming for collective well-being and prosperity.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, leaders can invoke this mantra to inspire their teams to work together harmoniously, fostering a culture of collaboration and innovation.
    • In personal life, individuals can recite this mantra during meditation to focus their thoughts on positive outcomes and personal growth.
    • In community gatherings, this mantra can be used to promote unity and shared goals, encouraging members to contribute their strengths for the common good.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for the harnessing of divine energy and wisdom to create a prosperous and harmonious life.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal development to community building, emphasizing the need for collective effort and positive thinking.
    • By invoking the essence of this mantra, individuals and groups can strive for a balanced and fruitful existence, guided by knowledge and shared intentions.
