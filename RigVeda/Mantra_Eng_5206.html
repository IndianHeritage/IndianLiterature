प्र वो देवं चित सहसानम् अग्निम अश्वं न वाजिनं हिषे नमोभिः | भवā नो दूतो अध्वरस्य विद्वान् त्मना देवेषु विविदे मितद्रुः ||
pra vo devaṁ cit sahasānam agnim aśvaṁ na vājinaṁ hiṣe namobhiḥ | bhavā no dūto adhvarasya vidvān tmanā deveṣu vivide mitadruḥ ||
  • pra vo devaṁ cit: This phrase can be translated as "to you, O divine ones, with intention." It signifies a call to the deities, inviting their presence and attention. In real life, this can be seen as a reminder to acknowledge and respect the forces of nature and the universe that influence our lives.
  • citas sahasānam: This means "the one who is aware of the vastness." It emphasizes the importance of awareness and understanding of the greater cosmos. In practical terms, this can relate to mindfulness practices that encourage individuals to be present and aware of their surroundings and actions.
  • agnim aśvaṁ na vājinaṁ hiṣe: This translates to "the fire, the horse, and the swift ones." Fire symbolizes energy and transformation, while the horse represents strength and speed. The swift ones can refer to the forces that aid in achieving goals. In everyday life, this can be interpreted as harnessing our inner energy and strength to pursue our ambitions effectively.
  • namobhiḥ: This means "with reverence." It highlights the importance of humility and respect in our endeavors. In real life, showing respect to others, whether in personal relationships or professional settings, can lead to more harmonious interactions and successful collaborations.
  • bhavā no dūto adhvarasya vidvān: This phrase translates to "may you be our messenger, O wise one of the sacrifice." It suggests the need for guidance and wisdom in our actions. In practical terms, this can be seen in seeking mentors or advisors who can provide insight and direction in our personal and professional lives.
  • tmanā deveṣu vivide mitadruḥ: This means "with our minds, may we connect with the diverse deities." It emphasizes the importance of intention and connection with the divine or higher principles. In real life, this can relate to the practice of setting intentions and aligning our actions with our values and beliefs.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a profound understanding of the interconnectedness of life, the importance of awareness, respect, and the pursuit of wisdom. Its significance lies in its applicability to daily life, encouraging individuals to be mindful, respectful, and intentional in their actions. By invoking the energies of the universe and seeking guidance, we can navigate our paths more effectively, fostering personal growth and harmonious relationships with others and the world around us.
