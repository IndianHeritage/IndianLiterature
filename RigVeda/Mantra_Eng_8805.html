आ हि द्यावापृथिवी अग्न उभे सदा पुत्रो न मातरा ततान्थ |प्र याह्य अछोषतो यविष्ठाथा वह सहस्येह देवान ||
ā hi dyāvāpṛthivī agna ubhe sadā putro na mātarā tatantha | pra yāhy acchośato yaviṣṭhāthā vaha sahasyeha devān ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā hi: indeed, truly
    • dyāvāpṛthivī: heaven and earth
    • agna: O Agni (the fire god)
    • ubhe: both
    • sadā: always
    • putro: son
    • na: not
    • mātarā: by the mother
    • tatantha: have been born
    • pra: for
    • yāhy: I go
    • acchośato: of the one who is pure
    • yaviṣṭhāthā: who is established
    • vaha: carry
    • sahasyeha: with strength
    • devān: the gods
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes Agni, the fire god, representing the divine energy that connects heaven and earth.
    • The phrase "both heaven and earth" signifies the unity of the spiritual and material worlds.
    • It emphasizes the idea that true strength and purity come from a divine source, and that one should seek guidance from the gods.
    • The mention of "son" and "mother" reflects the importance of familial bonds and the nurturing aspect of nature.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, this mantra can be applied to the concept of seeking balance between our spiritual aspirations and material responsibilities.
    • For instance, when faced with challenges, one might meditate on this mantra to find inner strength and clarity, invoking the support of divine energies.
    • In family dynamics, it can remind individuals of the importance of nurturing relationships and the role of love and support in personal growth.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for unity between the spiritual and material realms, encouraging individuals to seek divine guidance in their lives.
    • It serves as a reminder of the strength that comes from purity and the importance of familial and divine connections.
    • In practical terms, it can be used in meditation, rituals, or as a daily affirmation to foster resilience and harmony in one's life.
