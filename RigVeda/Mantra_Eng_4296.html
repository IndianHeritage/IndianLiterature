मन्त्र: अश्विनाऽ वाजिनीवासू जुषेथां यज्ञं इष्टये | हंसाव इव पततम् आ सुतां उप ||
aśvinā vājinīvasū juṣethāṁ yajñam iṣṭaye | haṁsāv iva patatam ā sutām̐ upa ||
  • Mantra: "aśvinā vājinīvasū juṣethāṁ yajñam iṣṭaye | haṁsāv iva patatam ā sutām̐ upa ||"
  • Translation: "May the Ashvins, the divine horsemen, partake in the sacrifice for our benefit, like swans that swiftly approach the offerings."
  • Explanation:
    • Ashvins: The Ashvins are twin deities in Hindu mythology, often associated with health, medicine, and the dawn. They are depicted as horsemen who bring light and healing. In real life, this can be likened to the role of doctors or healers who bring wellness and hope to individuals.
    • Vājinīvasū: This term refers to the Ashvins as bestowers of strength and vitality. In a modern context, this can be related to the importance of physical fitness and mental strength in achieving personal goals.
    • Yajñam: Yajna refers to a ritual sacrifice or offering, symbolizing the act of giving and sharing. In everyday life, this can be seen in charitable acts or community service, where individuals contribute to the welfare of others.
    • Haṁsāv iva patatam: This phrase compares the Ashvins to swans that gracefully approach. Swans are often symbols of beauty and purity. This can be related to the idea of approaching life’s challenges with grace and elegance, much like how one should handle difficult situations.
    • Sutām̐: This refers to the offerings or the results of the sacrifices. In a practical sense, it can be seen as the fruits of one’s labor, whether in personal endeavors or professional achievements.
  • Significance: This mantra emphasizes the importance of divine intervention and support in our endeavors. It highlights the need for strength, health, and the act of giving in our lives. By invoking the Ashvins, we seek their blessings for success in our sacrifices and efforts.
  • Real-Life Application: In daily life, this mantra can inspire individuals to seek help and guidance from mentors or spiritual figures when facing challenges. It encourages a mindset of generosity and community involvement, reminding us that our actions can have a positive impact on others.
