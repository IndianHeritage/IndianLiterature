भीमो विवेषायुधेभिर एषाम अपांसी विश्व नaryाणि विद्वान् | इन्द्रः पुरो जर्हृषाणो वि दूदोध वि वज्रहस्तो महिना जग्हान ||
bhīmo viveṣāyudhebhir eṣām apāṁsi viśvā naryāṇi vidvān | indraḥ puro jarhṛṣāṇo vi dūdhod vi vajrahasto mahinā jaghāna ||
  • bhīmo viveṣāyudhebhir: The term "bhīmo" refers to something that is formidable or powerful. "viveṣāyudhebhir" can be interpreted as engaging in battle or conflict. This suggests a scenario where one must confront challenges with strength and courage.
  • eṣām apāṁsi viśvā naryāṇi vidvān: "eṣām" means 'of these', "apāṁsi" refers to waters or resources, "viśvā" means 'all', and "naryāṇi" can be understood as 'men' or 'humans'. "vidvān" means 'the wise one' or 'the knowledgeable'. This part of the mantra emphasizes the importance of wisdom in utilizing resources effectively for the benefit of all.
  • indraḥ puro jarhṛṣāṇo: "indraḥ" refers to the king of gods, symbolizing power and authority. "puro" means 'in the front' or 'before', and "jarhṛṣāṇo" can be interpreted as one who is revered or honored. This indicates the presence of divine support and guidance in overcoming obstacles.
  • vi dūdhod vi vajrahasto mahinā jaghāna: "vi dūdhod" suggests the act of nourishing or providing sustenance, while "vi vajrahasto" refers to one who wields a thunderbolt, symbolizing strength and decisiveness. "mahinā jaghāna" means 'with great power, he struck down'. This signifies the importance of using one's strength wisely to achieve victory and success.

  • Significance: This mantra encapsulates the essence of facing challenges with courage, wisdom, and divine support. It highlights the importance of being formidable in the face of adversity while also being wise in the use of resources.
  • Real-life Application: In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios such as:
    • Leadership: A leader must be strong and courageous while also being wise in decision-making, ensuring that resources are utilized for the benefit of all.
    • Conflict Resolution: When faced with conflicts, one should approach the situation with strength and wisdom, seeking to resolve issues in a manner that is beneficial to all parties involved.
    • Personal Challenges: Individuals facing personal challenges can draw inspiration from this mantra to confront their difficulties with courage and seek knowledge to navigate through tough times.

  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a reminder of the balance between strength and wisdom. It encourages individuals to face challenges head-on while being mindful of the resources at their disposal. By embodying these principles, one can achieve success and harmony in both personal and communal aspects of life.
