आ नो अश्विनाः त्रिवृत रथेनार्वाञ्चं रयिं वहतं सुवीरम् |श्रिण्वन्ता वाम अवसे जोहवीमि वृधे च नो भवतं वाजसातौ ||
ā no aśvinā trivṛtā rathenārvāñcaṁ rayiṁ vahataṁ suvīram | śṛṇvantā vām avase johavīmi vṛdhe ca no bhavataṁ vājasātau ||
  • Mantra: "ā no aśvinā trivṛtā rathenārvāñcaṁ rayiṁ vahataṁ suvīram | śṛṇvantā vām avase johavīmi vṛdhe ca no bhavataṁ vājasātau ||"
  • Translation: "O Ashvins, bring us the threefold wealth on your swift chariot, and grant us the excellent strength. Listening to us, we invoke you; may you bestow upon us abundance and prosperity."
  • Explanation:
    • ā no aśvinā: This phrase calls upon the Ashvins, the twin deities of health and medicine in Vedic tradition, symbolizing duality and balance.
    • trivṛtā: Refers to the "threefold" aspect, which can signify various forms of wealth—spiritual, material, and intellectual.
    • rathenārvāñcaṁ: "On your swift chariot" indicates the speed and efficiency with which the Ashvins can deliver blessings and prosperity.
    • rayiṁ vahataṁ: "Bring us wealth" emphasizes the desire for abundance in life, which can be interpreted as financial stability, health, and happiness.
    • suvīram: "Excellent strength" signifies the importance of resilience and vigor in achieving one's goals.
    • śṛṇvantā vām: "Listening to us" suggests a two-way communication with the divine, where the deities are attentive to human prayers.
    • avase johavīmi: "We invoke you" indicates the act of calling upon the divine for assistance and blessings.
    • vṛdhe ca no bhavataṁ: "May you bestow upon us abundance" reinforces the request for prosperity and growth in various aspects of life.
    • vājasātau: "In the context of strength" connects the blessings sought with the idea of empowerment and capability.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be recited before starting a new venture, invoking the Ashvins for guidance and success.
    • During personal challenges, individuals may use this mantra to seek strength and resilience, reminding themselves of the support available from the divine.
    • In community gatherings, this mantra can be a call for collective prosperity, encouraging members to work together for mutual benefit.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its holistic approach to wealth and strength. It emphasizes the importance of divine support in achieving prosperity and resilience in life. By invoking the Ashvins, individuals and communities can foster a sense of connection to the divine, encouraging a mindset of abundance and collaboration. This mantra is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal challenges to communal efforts, reminding us of the power of intention and the support available from higher realms.
