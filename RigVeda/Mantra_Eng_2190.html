आशीत्यानवत्या याह्यर्वाङ आ शतेन हरिभिरुह्यमानः |अयं हि ते शुनहोत्रेषु सोम इन्द्र त्वायाऽपरिषिक्तो मदाय ||
āśītyā navatyā yāhy arvāṅ ā śatena haribhir uhyamānaḥ | ayaṁ hi te śunahotreṣu soma indra tvāyā pariṣikto madāya ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • āśītyā: With the blessings or grace.
    • navatyā: Ninety.
    • yāhy: Who is coming or arriving.
    • arvāṅ: With all beings or creatures.
    • ā: This or that.
    • śatena: With a hundred.
    • haribhir: By the divine or the gods.
    • uhyamānaḥ: Being invoked or called upon.
    • ayaṁ: This.
    • hi: Indeed.
    • te: Your.
    • śunahotreṣu: In the offerings or sacrifices.
    • soma: The sacred drink or elixir.
    • indra: The king of gods.
    • tvāyā: For you.
    • pariṣikto: Anointed or consecrated.
    • madāya: For intoxication or bliss.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the blessings that come from the divine, emphasizing the importance of community and collective effort.
    • The mention of "ninety" can symbolize completeness or a full cycle, suggesting that the blessings are abundant and holistic.
    • It highlights the significance of invoking the divine presence in rituals and offerings, particularly in the context of the sacred drink, Soma, which is associated with spiritual enlightenment and divine connection.
    • The reference to Indra, the king of gods, signifies the importance of leadership and guidance in achieving collective goals.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community gathering or festival, the collective prayers and rituals can be seen as a modern-day invocation of blessings, similar to the mantra's call for divine grace.
    • In corporate settings, teamwork and collaboration can be likened to the "arvāṅ" aspect, where every member contributes to the success of a project, invoking a sense of unity and shared purpose.
    • During significant life events, such as weddings or births, families often perform rituals to seek blessings, paralleling the essence of this mantra.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking divine blessings through collective efforts and rituals.
    • Its significance lies in the reminder that we are all interconnected, and our actions, when aligned with a higher purpose, can lead to greater fulfillment and joy.
    • In real life, it encourages individuals and communities to come together, invoke blessings, and celebrate the abundance of life, fostering a sense of unity and shared joy.
