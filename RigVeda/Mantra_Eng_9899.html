हरिष्मशारुर हरिकेश आयसस तुरस्पेये यो हरिपा अवर्धत |अर्वद्भिर यो हरिभिर वाजिनीवासुर अति विश्वा दुरिता पारिषद धरी ||
hariśmaśārur harikeśa āyasas turaspeye yo haripā avardhata | arvadbhir yo haribhir vājinīvasur ati viśvā duritā pāriṣad dharī ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • hariśmaśārur: One who is radiant and auspicious.
    • harikeśa: The one with beautiful hair, often referring to Lord Vishnu.
    • āyasas: Strength or power.
    • turaspeye: The one who is swift and powerful.
    • yo: Who.
    • haripā: The one who is a protector or savior.
    • avardhata: Increases or enhances.
    • arvadbhir: By the strong or powerful.
    • yo: Who.
    • haribhir: By the devotees of Hari (Vishnu).
    • vājinīvasur: The one who is like a charioteer or guide.
    • ati: Extremely or very.
    • viśvā: All or universal.
    • duritā: Difficulties or obstacles.
    • pāriṣad: The assembly or group.
    • dharī: Holds or supports.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the divine qualities of strength, protection, and auspiciousness.
    • It emphasizes the importance of divine guidance in overcoming obstacles and challenges in life.
    • The reference to "haripā" suggests that the divine is a protector, enhancing the strength of those who seek refuge in it.
    • The mantra can be seen as a call for support from the divine in times of difficulty, encouraging individuals to seek strength from a higher power.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In times of personal crisis, such as loss or failure, individuals often turn to spiritual practices or prayers for strength and guidance.
    • In a professional setting, when facing challenges, teams may invoke collective strength and support, akin to the divine assembly mentioned in the mantra.
    • During community gatherings or rituals, the invocation of divine protection can foster a sense of unity and resilience among participants.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the power of faith and the importance of seeking divine assistance in overcoming life's challenges.
    • It highlights the significance of community and collective strength in facing difficulties.
    • In real life, the principles embodied in this mantra can inspire individuals to cultivate resilience, seek support, and remain hopeful in the face of adversity.
