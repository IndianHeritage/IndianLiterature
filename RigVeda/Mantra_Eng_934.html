मां ते राधांसी मां त उटयो वसोऽस्मान कदाचन दभन |विश्वा च न उपमिमही मानव वसूनी चरषणिभ्य आ ||
mā te rādhāṁsi mā ta ūtayo vaso 'smān kadā canā dabhan | viśvā ca na upamimīhi mānuṣa vasūni carṣaṇibhya ā ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • mā: not
    • te: your
    • rādhāṁsi: desires
    • mā: not
    • ta: those
    • ūtayo: who are in distress
    • vaso: dwellings or homes
    • 'smān: us
    • kadā: when
    • canā: at any time
    • dabhan: to harm or trouble
    • viśvā: all
    • ca: and
    • na: not
    • upamimīhi: compare or equate
    • mānuṣa: human
    • vasūni: wealth or possessions
    • carṣaṇibhya: from the earth
    • ā: towards
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra expresses a plea for protection against harm and distress.
    • It emphasizes the importance of not allowing one's desires to lead to suffering or trouble.
    • The speaker asks for a state where their home and well-being are safeguarded from any form of distress.
    • It also highlights the idea of not comparing oneself to others, particularly in terms of material wealth or possessions.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace setting, this mantra can be applied when individuals face competition or stress. It encourages focusing on personal growth rather than comparing oneself to colleagues.
    • In personal relationships, it serves as a reminder to prioritize emotional well-being and avoid letting desires lead to conflicts or misunderstandings.
    • During challenging times, such as financial difficulties, this mantra can inspire individuals to seek inner peace and resilience rather than succumbing to despair.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call for self-awareness and protection from external and internal disturbances.
    • It encourages individuals to cultivate a sense of contentment and to focus on their own journey without being swayed by societal pressures or comparisons.
    • In real life, applying the teachings of this mantra can lead to a more harmonious existence, fostering resilience and emotional stability in the face of challenges.
