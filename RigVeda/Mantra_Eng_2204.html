त्वं न इन्द्र त्वाभिर ऊती त्वायतो अभिष्टिपासी जनान् |त्वं इनो दाशुषो वरूतेत्त्वाधीर अभि यो नक्षति त्वा ||
tvaṁ na indra tvābhir ūtī tvāyato abhiṣṭipāsi janān | tvam ino dāśuṣo varūtetthādhīr abhi yo nakṣati tvā ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • tvaṁ: You
    • na: Not
    • indra: O Indra (a deity, often associated with strength and power)
    • tvābhir: With you
    • ūtī: Praise or invocation
    • tvāyato: From you
    • abhiṣṭipāsi: You protect or guard
    • janān: People or beings
    • tvam: You
    • ino: Our
    • dāśuṣo: Of the faithful or devoted
    • varūte: In the realm of Varuna (another deity associated with cosmic order)
    • etthā: Here
    • dhīr: Wise or intelligent
    • abhi: Towards
    • yo: Who
    • nakṣati: Protects or watches over
    • tvā: You
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra addresses the deity Indra, invoking his strength and protection.
    • It emphasizes the relationship between the divine and the people, highlighting the need for guidance and support.
    • The mention of Varuna suggests a connection to cosmic order and moral law, indicating that divine protection is not just physical but also ethical.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In times of crisis, people often turn to spiritual practices or prayers for protection and guidance, similar to how this mantra seeks the favor of Indra.
    • Communities may come together to invoke blessings for safety and prosperity, reflecting the collective aspect of seeking divine support.
    • In leadership, the wise and ethical guidance of leaders can be likened to the divine protection mentioned in the mantra, where leaders are expected to safeguard the interests of their people.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of divine protection and guidance in human affairs.
    • It is applicable in real-life scenarios where individuals or communities seek strength, wisdom, and ethical guidance in their endeavors.
    • By invoking the divine, it reminds us of our interconnectedness and the need for moral integrity in leadership and community life.
