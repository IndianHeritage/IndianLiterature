अग्न आयूषं पवसा आ सुवोर्जम इषं च नः |आरे बाधस्व दुष्चुनाम ||
agna āyūṁṣi pavasa ā suvorjam iṣaṁ ca naḥ | āre bādhasva ducchunām ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • agna: O Agni (the fire god)
    • āyūṁṣi: may you grant us longevity
    • pavasa: may you purify
    • ā: and
    • suvorjam: may you bestow us with wealth
    • iṣaṁ: and strength
    • ca: and
    • naḥ: to us
    • āre: O Agni
    • bādhasva: may you remove
    • ducchunām: the obstacles or difficulties
  • Overall Meaning: This mantra is a prayer to Agni, the fire god, asking for longevity, purification, wealth, strength, and the removal of obstacles. It reflects a deep reverence for the divine and the belief in the power of fire as a purifying and transformative force.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In many cultures, fire is seen as a symbol of purification. For instance, in a traditional Indian wedding, the couple walks around a sacred fire, symbolizing their commitment and the purification of their union.
    • In modern life, people often seek to "purify" their lives by removing negative influences, much like the mantra requests Agni to remove obstacles. This can be seen in practices like decluttering one's home or engaging in mindfulness and meditation to clear the mind.
    • Wealth and strength can be interpreted in various ways. For example, financial stability can be seen as a form of wealth, while emotional resilience can be viewed as strength. Many individuals set goals to achieve these aspects in their lives, reflecting the desires expressed in the mantra.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its holistic approach to life. It emphasizes the importance of spiritual, physical, and material well-being. In real-life scenarios, it serves as a reminder to seek balance and harmony in our lives, to strive for personal growth, and to overcome challenges with the help of divine forces. By invoking Agni, we acknowledge the need for purification and strength in our journey, making this mantra relevant in various aspects of life, from personal development to community well-being.
