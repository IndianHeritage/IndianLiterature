आ सुष्वयन्ती यजते उपाके उषासानक्ता सदतां नि योनौ |दिव्ये योषणे बृहती सुरुक्मे अधि श्रीयं शुक्रपीशं दधाने ||
ā suṣvayantī yajate upāke uṣāsānaktā sadatāṁ ni yonau | divye yoṣaṇe bṛhatī surukme adhi śriyaṁ śukrapiśaṁ dadhāne ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā suṣvayantī: "She who is auspicious" or "the one who brings good fortune."
    • yajate: "is worshipped" or "is offered reverence."
    • upāke: "in the presence of" or "alongside."
    • uṣāsānaktā: "the dawn" or "the morning light."
    • sadatāṁ: "always" or "eternally."
    • ni yonau: "in the womb" or "in the source."
    • divye: "divine" or "heavenly."
    • yoṣaṇe: "in the feminine" or "in the woman."
    • bṛhatī: "the great" or "the vast."
    • surukme: "beautiful" or "well-formed."
    • adhi: "upon" or "over."
    • śriyaṁ: "prosperity" or "wealth."
    • śukrapiśaṁ: "the bright" or "the pure."
    • dadhāne: "who holds" or "who possesses."
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the divine feminine energy, often personified as the dawn or the goddess of dawn, who is revered for her auspiciousness and ability to bring light and prosperity.
    • The phrase "ā suṣvayantī" emphasizes the importance of invoking positive energies and blessings in our lives, akin to how we seek good fortune in our daily endeavors.
    • The mention of "uṣāsānaktā" signifies the transition from darkness to light, representing hope and new beginnings, much like how each day brings a fresh start.
    • In a real-life context, this can be related to rituals or practices that individuals perform to seek blessings for success, such as starting a new business or embarking on a new journey.
    • The reference to "divye yoṣaṇe" highlights the reverence for women and the divine feminine, suggesting that nurturing and supportive energies are essential for growth and prosperity.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra encapsulates the significance of invoking divine energies for prosperity and success in life.
    • It serves as a reminder of the importance of positivity, hope, and the nurturing aspects of life, which can be applied in various real-life scenarios, such as personal growth, relationships, and professional endeavors.
    • By recognizing and honoring these energies, individuals can create a more harmonious and prosperous existence, aligning themselves with the natural rhythms of life.
