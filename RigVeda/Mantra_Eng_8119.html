मन्त्र: एते असृग्रम इन्दवस् तिरः पवित्रम् आशवः |विश्वानि अभि सौभगाः ||
ete asṛgram indavas tiraḥ pavitram āśavaḥ | viśvāny abhi saubhagā ||
  • Mantra: "ete asṛgram indavas tiraḥ pavitram āśavaḥ | viśvāny abhi saubhagā ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ete: these
    • asṛgram: the ones who are pure or untainted
    • indavas: the ones who are like the moon or possess lunar qualities
    • tiraḥ: beyond or across
    • pavitram: pure or sacred
    • āśavaḥ: those who are full of life or vitality
    • viśvāny: all or universal
    • abhi: towards or in the direction of
    • saubhagā: auspiciousness or good fortune
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of a group of beings or entities that are pure and possess qualities akin to the moon, suggesting a sense of calmness, serenity, and illumination.
    • The term "pavitram" emphasizes the importance of purity, which can be interpreted in various contexts, such as moral integrity, spiritual cleanliness, or physical health.
    • The phrase "viśvāny abhi saubhagā" indicates a universal aspiration towards good fortune and auspiciousness, suggesting that these qualities are not just for a select few but are available to all who seek them.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a community setting, individuals who embody purity and positivity can inspire others, creating an environment where everyone strives for collective well-being and success.
    • In personal development, one might focus on cultivating purity of thought and action, leading to a more fulfilling and prosperous life.
    • In environmental contexts, the mantra can be seen as a call to maintain the purity of nature, encouraging sustainable practices that benefit all living beings.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of purity and auspiciousness in our lives. It encourages individuals to strive for these qualities, not just for personal gain but for the benefit of the community and the world at large.
    • Its significance lies in the universal applicability of its message, urging us to seek purity in our thoughts, actions, and interactions with others.
    • By embodying the principles outlined in this mantra, we can contribute to a more harmonious and prosperous society, where good fortune is accessible to all.
