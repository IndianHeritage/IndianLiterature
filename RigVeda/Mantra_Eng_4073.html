स्वस्ति नो मिमीताम् अश्विनाः भगः स्वस्ति देव्या अदितिः अनर्वणः |स्वस्ति पूषा असुरो दधातु नः स्वस्ति dyावापृथिवी सुचेतुनā ||
svasti no mimītām aśvinā bhagaḥ svasti devy aditir anarvaṇaḥ | svasti pūṣā asuro dadhātu naḥ svasti dyāvāpṛthivī sucetunā ||
  • svasti no mimītām aśvinā bhagaḥ: This phrase can be translated as "May the Ashvins, the divine twin horsemen, bestow upon us well-being." The Ashvins are considered deities of health and medicine in Vedic literature. In real life, this can be related to seeking good health and wellness through various means such as proper diet, exercise, and medical care.
  • svasti devy aditir anarvaṇaḥ: This translates to "May the goddess Aditi, the mother of the gods, grant us well-being." Aditi represents the infinite and the nurturing aspect of the universe. In practical terms, this can be seen in the importance of maternal figures in our lives who provide support and guidance, emphasizing the need for nurturing relationships.
  • svasti pūṣā asuro dadhātu naḥ: This means "May the god Pūṣa, the nourisher, grant us well-being." Pūṣa is associated with nourishment and prosperity. In everyday life, this can be reflected in the importance of community support and resources that help individuals thrive, such as access to food, education, and social services.
  • svasti dyāvāpṛthivī sucetunā: This translates to "May the heavens and the earth, with their pure consciousness, grant us well-being." This line emphasizes the connection between the cosmos and our existence. In a real-world context, it can be interpreted as the need for environmental awareness and sustainability, recognizing that our well-being is intertwined with the health of our planet.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a holistic approach to well-being, invoking divine blessings for health, nourishment, and harmony with the universe. Its significance lies in the reminder that well-being is not just an individual pursuit but a collective one, involving community, nature, and the divine. In real-life scenarios, this mantra encourages us to seek balance in our lives, prioritize health, nurture relationships, and care for our environment, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling and harmonious existence.
