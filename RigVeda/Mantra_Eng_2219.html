अध त्विषीमां अभ्य ओजसा कृविं युधाभवद आ रोडसी अपृणद अस्य मजनना प्र वावृधे |अधत्तान्यं जठरे प्रेम अरिच्यत सैनाṁ सश्चद देवो देवं सत्यं इन्द्रं सत्य इन्दुः ||
adha tviṣīmām̐ abhy ojasā kriviṁ yudhābhavad ā rodasī apṛṇad asya majmanā pra vāvṛdhe | adhattānyaṁ jaṭhare prem aricyata sainaṁ saścad devo devaṁ satyam indraṁ satya induḥ ||
  • Adha tviṣīmām̐ abhy ojasā: This phrase can be translated as "With great energy, I invoke the divine light." Here, "tviṣīmām̐" refers to the divine light or brilliance, while "ojasā" signifies energy or strength. In real life, this can be seen as a call to harness one's inner strength and clarity in challenging situations, much like athletes preparing for a competition.
  • Kriviṁ yudhābhavad: This means "may the battle be won." It signifies the importance of striving for victory in life's challenges. For example, in a business context, this could relate to overcoming competition or achieving success in a project.
  • Ā rodasī apṛṇad asya majmanā pra vāvṛdhe: This translates to "The two worlds (heaven and earth) have been made to flourish." It emphasizes the interconnectedness of the universe and the importance of balance. In everyday life, this can be reflected in sustainable practices that benefit both the environment and society.
  • Adhattānyaṁ jaṭhare prem aricyata: This phrase means "In the womb of the universe, love is born." It highlights the idea that love and compassion are fundamental to existence. In real life, this can be seen in the nurturing relationships we build, whether in families or communities.
  • Sainaṁ saścad devo devaṁ satyam indraṁ satya induḥ: This can be interpreted as "The divine truth is the ultimate reality." It emphasizes the pursuit of truth and integrity. In practical terms, this can relate to ethical decision-making in personal and professional life, where honesty and transparency are valued.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of invoking divine energy, striving for victory, nurturing love, and pursuing truth. Its significance lies in its applicability to various aspects of life, from personal growth and relationships to ethical practices in society. By embodying these principles, individuals can lead more fulfilling and harmonious lives, contributing positively to the world around them.
