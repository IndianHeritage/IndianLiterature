मन्त्र: स्तोत्रं राधानाम् पते गिर्वाहो वीर यस्य ते |विभूतिर अस्तु सूनृताः ||
stotraṁ rādhānām pate girvāho vīra yasya te | vibhūtir astu sūnṛtā ||
  • stotraṁ: This word translates to "hymn" or "praise." It signifies the act of offering reverence or devotion through words.
  • rādhānām: Refers to "of Radha," who is often considered the divine feminine energy and beloved of Lord Krishna in Hindu mythology. Radha symbolizes love, devotion, and the soul's yearning for the divine.
  • pate: This means "O Lord" or "O Master," indicating a respectful address to a deity or revered figure.
  • girvāho: This term can be interpreted as "of the songs" or "of the praises," suggesting a connection to the divine through music and poetry.
  • vīra: Translates to "hero" or "brave one," often used to describe someone who embodies courage and valor.
  • yasya: This means "whose," indicating possession or association.
  • te: Translates to "your," further emphasizing the connection to the divine.
  • vibhūtir: This word means "glory" or "wealth," referring to the abundance and magnificence associated with the divine.
  • astu: This means "may it be" or "let it be," expressing a wish or blessing.
  • sūnṛtā: This term can be interpreted as "truth" or "righteousness," highlighting the importance of honesty and virtue in one's life.

The mantra can be understood as a heartfelt hymn praising Radha, the divine feminine, and expressing a desire for her glory and truth to manifest in the devotee's life. It emphasizes the importance of devotion, courage, and the pursuit of righteousness.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios:

  • Devotion in Relationships: Just as Radha's love for Krishna is unwavering, individuals can strive to cultivate deep, loving relationships based on trust and devotion.
  • Courage in Adversity: The mention of "vīra" encourages individuals to embody bravery in challenging situations, whether in personal struggles or professional challenges.
  • Pursuit of Truth: The call for "sūnṛtā" serves as a reminder to uphold honesty and integrity in all aspects of life, fostering a sense of righteousness.

In conclusion, this mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the virtues of love, courage, and truth. By invoking the essence of Radha, individuals can aspire to embody these qualities in their daily lives, leading to personal growth and a deeper connection with the divine.
