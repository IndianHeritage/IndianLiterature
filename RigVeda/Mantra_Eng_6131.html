युवं मृगं जागृवांसं स्वदथो वा वृषण्वसू |तां नः पृङ्क्तम इषा रयिम ||
yuvam mṛgaṁ jāgṛvāṁsaṁ svadatho vā vṛṣaṇvasū | tā naḥ pṛṅktam iṣā rayim ||
  • yuvam - This word translates to "you" in the plural form, indicating a collective or community aspect.
  • mṛgaṁ - This means "deer" or "animal," often symbolizing grace, agility, and the natural world.
  • jāgṛvāṁsaṁ - This can be interpreted as "awakened" or "alert," suggesting a state of awareness and readiness.
  • svadatho - This word means "enjoying" or "delighting," indicating a sense of pleasure or satisfaction.
  • vā - This translates to "or," presenting an alternative or choice.
  • vṛṣaṇvasū - This term can be understood as "the one who brings prosperity" or "the one who is strong," often associated with strength and abundance.
  • tā - This means "that," referring to something specific that has been mentioned.
  • naḥ - This translates to "to us," indicating a personal or communal connection.
  • pṛṅktam - This word means "array" or "group," suggesting a collective gathering or assembly.
  • iṣā - This can be interpreted as "power" or "authority," indicating a force that can influence or direct.
  • rayim - This means "wealth" or "prosperity," often associated with abundance and well-being.

The mantra can be understood as a call to a collective group, invoking a sense of alertness and enjoyment in the pursuit of prosperity and abundance. It emphasizes the importance of community and shared goals, suggesting that when individuals come together with awareness and intention, they can achieve greater wealth and success.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as:

  • Teamwork in Organizations: Just as the mantra calls for collective awareness and enjoyment, in a workplace, teams that work together with a shared vision and alertness can achieve greater success. For example, a marketing team brainstorming ideas for a campaign can draw on each member's strengths to create a more impactful strategy.
  • Community Initiatives: Local communities can come together to address common issues, such as environmental conservation or social welfare. By being alert to the needs of the community and enjoying the process of collaboration, they can create a more prosperous environment for all.
  • Personal Relationships: In personal relationships, being aware and engaged with one another can lead to deeper connections and shared happiness. Couples who actively communicate and support each other's goals often find greater fulfillment in their relationship.

In conclusion, this mantra signifies the power of collective awareness and the joy of working together towards common goals. It highlights the importance of community, alertness, and the pursuit of prosperity, making it applicable in various aspects of life, from professional settings to personal relationships. By embracing these principles, individuals and groups can foster a more abundant and fulfilling existence.
