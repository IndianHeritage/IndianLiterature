मन्त्र: आयā ते अग्ने विधेमोर्जो नपाद अश्वमिष्टे | एनā सूक्तेन सुजात ||
ayā te agne vidhemorjo napād aśvamiṣṭe | enā sūktena sujāta ||
  • ayā te agne: This phrase translates to "O Agni," where Agni represents the fire god in Vedic literature. Fire is often seen as a purifying force and a medium for offerings in rituals.
  • vidhemorjo: This can be interpreted as "we offer our prayers" or "we invoke." It signifies the act of devotion and the importance of intention in spiritual practices.
  • napād: This word means "to the path" or "to the way." It suggests a direction or guidance, indicating that the prayers and offerings are aimed towards a higher purpose.
  • aśvamiṣṭe: This term refers to "the best of horses" or "the most excellent." In Vedic culture, horses symbolize strength, speed, and prosperity, often associated with success and victory.
  • enā sūktena: This translates to "with this hymn" or "through this sacred verse." It emphasizes the power of words and hymns in invoking divine blessings.
  • sujāta: This means "well-born" or "auspicious." It indicates the desired outcome of the prayers, which is to achieve a state of well-being and prosperity.

In summary, this mantra is a prayer to Agni, invoking his blessings for guidance and prosperity. It highlights the significance of intention in spiritual practices and the power of sacred words. The reference to horses symbolizes strength and success, suggesting that the offerings made with devotion can lead to favorable outcomes.


Significance and Real-Life Application:

  • This mantra can be recited during rituals or ceremonies to seek blessings for success in endeavors, much like how individuals seek guidance before starting a new project or venture.
  • In daily life, it serves as a reminder to approach tasks with intention and devotion, ensuring that one's actions are aligned with a higher purpose.
  • The invocation of Agni can be seen as a metaphor for igniting one's inner strength and passion, encouraging individuals to pursue their goals with vigor and determination.

Ultimately, this mantra teaches us the importance of devotion, intention, and the pursuit of excellence in our lives, guiding us towards a path of prosperity and well-being.
