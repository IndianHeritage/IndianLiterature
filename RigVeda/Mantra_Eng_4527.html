अग्निर्वृत्राणि जङ्घनद् द्रविणस्युर विपण्यया |समिद्धः शुक्र आहुतः ||
agnir vṛtrāṇi jaṅghanad draviṇasyur vipanyayā | samiddhaḥ śukra āhutaḥ ||
  • Agnir: This word refers to "Agni," the fire god in Vedic literature. Fire symbolizes purity, transformation, and the divine presence in rituals.
  • Vṛtrāṇi: This term relates to "Vṛtra," a demon or obstacle that represents chaos and ignorance. The mantra suggests overcoming these obstacles.
  • Jaṅghanad: This word can be interpreted as "the one who roars" or "the one who is powerful." It signifies strength and the ability to overcome challenges.
  • Draviṇasyur: This term refers to "wealth" or "prosperity." It indicates the importance of material and spiritual abundance in life.
  • Vipanyayā: This word means "through the offering" or "by means of sacrifice." It emphasizes the significance of selfless actions and contributions to achieve goals.
  • Samiddhaḥ: This term means "kindled" or "ignited." It symbolizes the awakening of potential and the readiness to act.
  • Śukra: This word translates to "pure" or "bright." It represents clarity, wisdom, and the pursuit of knowledge.
  • Āhutaḥ: This term means "offered" or "sacrificed." It signifies the act of giving and the importance of offerings in spiritual practices.

The mantra "agnir vṛtrāṇi jaṅghanad draviṇasyur vipanyayā | samiddhaḥ śukra āhutaḥ ||" encapsulates the essence of overcoming obstacles through the divine presence of fire (Agni) and the importance of selfless offerings. It highlights the journey from chaos (Vṛtra) to prosperity (Draviṇasyur) by igniting one's potential (Samiddhaḥ) and pursuing purity and wisdom (Śukra).


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as:

  • Personal Growth: Just as Agni transforms raw materials into energy, individuals can transform their challenges into opportunities for growth through dedication and effort.
  • Community Service: The concept of offering (Āhutaḥ) can be seen in acts of charity and community service, where individuals contribute to the welfare of others, leading to collective prosperity.
  • Spiritual Practices: The mantra encourages individuals to engage in rituals and practices that promote clarity and wisdom, helping them navigate life's complexities.

In conclusion, this mantra serves as a reminder of the power of transformation, the importance of overcoming obstacles, and the value of selfless contributions. It encourages individuals to embrace their potential and strive for a life filled with purpose, clarity, and abundance.
