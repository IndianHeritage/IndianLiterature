मन्त्र: द्रवतां त उषसा वाजयन्ती अग्ने वातस्य पथ्याभिरच्छ || यत सिम अञ्जन्ति पूर्व्यं हविभिरा वन्धुरेव तस्थतुर दुरोणे ||
dravatāṁ ta uṣasā vājayantī agne vātasya pathyābhir accha | yat sīm añjanti pūrvyaṁ havirbhir ā vandhureva tasthatur duroṇe ||
  • Dravatāṁ ta uṣasā vājayantī: This phrase refers to the dawn (Uṣas) that brings forth abundance and prosperity. The word "dravatāṁ" signifies the flow or abundance, suggesting that the dawn is a time of new beginnings and opportunities.
  • Agne vātasya pathyābhir accha: Here, "Agne" refers to Agni, the fire god, symbolizing energy and transformation. "Vātasya pathyābhir" indicates the paths of the wind, suggesting that the energy of Agni is guided by the forces of nature. This can be interpreted as the importance of aligning our actions with natural forces to achieve success.
  • Yat sīm añjanti pūrvyaṁ: This line speaks to the idea of offerings (havirbhir) made to the divine. "Sīm añjanti" implies that these offerings are made with intention and purpose, reflecting the importance of dedication in our endeavors.
  • Havirbhir ā vandhureva tasthatur duroṇe: This part emphasizes the significance of rituals and offerings in connecting with the divine. "Duroṇe" suggests stability and strength, indicating that through our rituals, we can establish a strong foundation for our lives.

  • Significance: This mantra encapsulates the essence of new beginnings, the importance of aligning with natural energies, and the power of intention in our actions. It highlights the role of rituals in establishing a connection with the divine and creating a stable foundation for our lives.
  • Real-life Application: In practical terms, this mantra can be applied in various aspects of life. For instance, starting a new project or venture can be seen as a new dawn, where one should harness the energy of the moment and align their actions with their goals. Rituals, whether in personal or professional settings, can help in focusing intentions and creating a supportive environment for success.
  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a reminder of the cyclical nature of life, where every dawn brings new opportunities. By recognizing the abundance around us, aligning with natural energies, and engaging in meaningful rituals, we can navigate our paths with purpose and strength.
