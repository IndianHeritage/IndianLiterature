युवम् अत्रये 'वनीताया तप्तम् ऊर्जम् ओमानम् अश्विनाव अधत्तम् |युवं कण्वायापिरिप्ताया चक्षुः प्रत्य अधत्तं सुश्रुतिं जुजुषाणाः ||
yuvam atraye 'vanītāya taptam ūrjam omānam aśvināv adhattam | yuvaṁ kaṇvāyāpiriptāya cakṣuḥ praty adhattaṁ suṣṭutiṁ jujuṣāṇā ||
  • Mantra: "yuvam atraye 'vanītāya taptam ūrjam omānam aśvināv adhattam | yuvaṁ kaṇvāyāpiriptāya cakṣuḥ praty adhattaṁ suṣṭutiṁ jujuṣāṇā ||"
  • Translation: This mantra invokes the divine energies of the Ashvins, the twin deities of health and medicine, asking them to bestow their blessings of vitality and insight.
  • Word Breakdown:
    • yuvam: You (plural), referring to the divine or the deities.
    • atraye: Here, indicating presence or invocation.
    • vanītāya: To the one who is revered or honored.
    • taptam: Heated or energized, symbolizing vitality.
    • ūrjam: Energy or strength.
    • omānam: Acknowledgment or offering.
    • aśvināv: To the Ashvins, the divine twin physicians.
    • adhattam: Bestow or grant.
    • kaṇvāyā: To the one who is wise or knowledgeable.
    • piriptāya: To the one who is complete or fulfilled.
    • cakṣuḥ: Eyes or vision, symbolizing insight.
    • praty: Towards or in the direction of.
    • adhattaṁ: Grant or bestow.
    • suṣṭutiṁ: Praise or commendation.
    • jujuṣāṇā: Desiring or seeking.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a medical context, this mantra can be recited by healthcare professionals seeking the blessings of the Ashvins for healing and health in their patients.
    • Students or individuals pursuing knowledge can chant this mantra to seek clarity and insight in their studies or decision-making processes.
    • During rituals or ceremonies, this mantra can be used to invoke positive energy and vitality for the participants, enhancing their experience.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its invocation of divine energies for health, insight, and vitality. It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, such as healthcare, education, and personal growth. By reciting this mantra, individuals can align themselves with the blessings of the Ashvins, fostering a sense of well-being and clarity in their lives.
