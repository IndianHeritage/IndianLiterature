उप वाऽएषे नमसां जिगीषोषासानक्तā सुदुगहेव धेनुः |समाने अहन विमिमानो अर्कं विषुरूपे पयसी सस्मिन्न ऊधन ||
upa va eṣe namasā jigīṣoṣāsānaktā sudugheva dhenuḥ | samāne ahan vimimāno arkaṁ viṣurūpe payasi sasminn ūdhan ||
  • upa va eṣe namasā: This phrase can be translated as "We bow down with reverence." It signifies the importance of humility and respect in our approach to the divine and to the forces of nature.
  • jigīṣoṣāsānaktā: This term refers to the "desire to conquer" or "to achieve victory." It reflects the human aspiration to overcome challenges and attain success in various endeavors.
  • sudugheva dhenuḥ: This translates to "like a cow that gives rich milk." Cows are often seen as symbols of nourishment and abundance in many cultures, representing the rewards of hard work and dedication.
  • samāne ahan vimimāno: This phrase means "in the same way, we strive for the light." It emphasizes the pursuit of knowledge, enlightenment, and truth, akin to seeking the sun's rays for guidance.
  • arkaṁ viṣurūpe: "The radiant light in its many forms." This highlights the diversity of knowledge and wisdom available to us, suggesting that truth can manifest in various ways.
  • payasi sasminn ūdhan: This can be interpreted as "in the waters of that essence." It signifies the idea of immersing oneself in the richness of life and the universe, drawing sustenance from it.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda encapsulates the essence of reverence, aspiration, and the pursuit of knowledge. It teaches us the importance of humility while striving for success and enlightenment. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various contexts, such as in education, where students bow to the knowledge they seek, or in professional settings, where individuals respect their mentors and strive to achieve their goals. The metaphor of the cow symbolizes the rewards of hard work, reminding us that dedication leads to abundance. Overall, this mantra encourages us to seek wisdom and nourishment from the world around us, fostering a sense of interconnectedness and respect for all forms of life.
