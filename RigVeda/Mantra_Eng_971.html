तान पूर्वया निविदा हूमहे वयं भागं मित्रं अदितिं दक्षं अश्रिधम् |आर्यमणं वरुणं सोमं अश्विनां सरस्वती नः शुभगा मयस् करत ||
tān pūrvayā nividā hūmahe vayam bhagam mitram aditiṁ dakṣam asridham | aryamaṇaṁ varuṇaṁ somam aśvinā sarasvatī naḥ subhagā mayas karat ||
  • tān pūrvayā nividā hūmahe: This phrase can be translated as "We invoke those who have been invoked before." It emphasizes the importance of tradition and the wisdom of ancestors. In real life, this can be seen in practices such as honoring one's heritage during festivals or family gatherings, where stories and teachings of previous generations are shared.
  • vayam bhagam mitram aditiṁ dakṣam asridham: This part means "We seek the blessings of the divine, the friend, the boundless, the skilled, and the protector." Here, "bhagam" refers to blessings or fortune, while "mitram" signifies friendship. In everyday life, this can relate to seeking support from friends and family during challenging times, highlighting the importance of community and relationships.
  • aryamaṇaṁ varuṇaṁ somam: This translates to "the noble Aryaman, Varuna, and Soma." Aryaman represents social order and harmony, Varuna symbolizes cosmic law and moral authority, and Soma is associated with divine inspiration and ecstasy. In practical terms, this can be reflected in the pursuit of justice, ethical living, and the appreciation of art and culture in society.
  • aśvinā sarasvatī naḥ subhagā mayas karat: This means "May the Ashvins, Sarasvati, and the auspicious bring us prosperity." The Ashvins are twin deities representing health and well-being, while Sarasvati is the goddess of knowledge and wisdom. This can be applied in real life by valuing education and health, and striving for a balanced life that fosters both intellectual and physical well-being.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a holistic approach to life, emphasizing the importance of invoking the wisdom of the past, seeking blessings from divine forces, and striving for harmony, knowledge, and prosperity. Its significance lies in the reminder to honor our roots, value our relationships, and pursue a balanced and ethical life. In contemporary society, this mantra can inspire individuals to cultivate community, prioritize education, and maintain a connection with their cultural heritage, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling and harmonious existence.
