तेषां हि चित्रम् उक्त्यं वरूथम अस्ति दाशुषे |आदित्यनाम अरंक्रते ||
teṣāṁ hi citram ukthya1ṁ varūtham asti dāśuṣe | ādityānām araṁkṛte ||
  • teṣāṁ hi - "Indeed, for them" - This phrase indicates a focus on a specific group or individuals, suggesting that what follows is particularly relevant to them.
  • citram - "wonderful" or "extraordinary" - This word conveys a sense of amazement or uniqueness, implying that the subject being discussed is remarkable in some way.
  • ukthyaṁ - "utterance" or "saying" - This refers to a spoken word or mantra, highlighting the power of speech and expression in conveying thoughts and intentions.
  • varūtham - "protection" or "safeguard" - This term signifies a form of security or shelter, suggesting that the utterance provides a form of divine protection.
  • asti - "exists" or "is" - This word affirms the presence or reality of what is being discussed, emphasizing its significance.
  • dāśuṣe - "for the devotee" or "for the worshipper" - This phrase indicates that the message is directed towards those who are devoted or engaged in worship, highlighting the importance of faith and devotion.
  • ādityānām - "of the Adityas" - Referring to the celestial deities associated with the sun, this term connects the mantra to divine forces and their influence in the world.
  • araṁkṛte - "in the absence of" or "without" - This word suggests a condition or state where something is lacking, indicating a need for the protection or blessings being discussed.

Conclusion: This mantra emphasizes the significance of spoken words and their power to provide protection and guidance, particularly for devotees. In real-life scenarios, it can be applied to the practice of prayer and meditation, where individuals seek divine support and security in their lives. The mantra serves as a reminder of the extraordinary nature of faith and the importance of expressing one's intentions through words. It highlights the belief that through devotion and the right utterances, individuals can invoke the protection of higher powers, especially in challenging times.
