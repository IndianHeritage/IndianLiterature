प्रियो नो अस्तु विश्वपितर होता मन्द्रो वरेण्यः |प्रियाः स्वग्नयो वयम् ||
priyo no astu viśpatir hotā mandro vareṇyaḥ | priyāḥ svagnayo vayam ||
  • Mantra: "priyo no astu viśpatir hotā mandro vareṇyaḥ | priyāḥ svagnayo vayam ||"
  • Translation: "May the beloved Lord of all, the worthy Hotar (priest), be favorable to us; may we be dear to the gods and may our offerings be accepted."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • priyo: beloved, dear
    • no: to us
    • astu: may it be, let it be
    • viśpatir: Lord of all, universal ruler
    • hotā: priest, one who offers sacrifices
    • mandro: worthy, commendable
    • vareṇyaḥ: worthy of worship, deserving
    • priyāḥ: dear, beloved
    • svagnayo: our offerings
    • vayam: we
  • Context and Explanation:
    • This mantra is a prayer for divine favor and acceptance of offerings. It emphasizes the importance of being in harmony with the divine and the community.
    • The term "viśpatir" signifies a universal ruler, which can be related to the idea of a leader or guiding force in our lives, such as a mentor or a community leader.
    • The "hotā" refers to the priest who performs rituals, symbolizing the role of individuals who facilitate connections between the divine and the community, much like teachers or spiritual guides in modern society.
    • The phrase "may we be dear to the gods" reflects the human desire for acceptance and love, akin to how we seek approval and affection from our peers and loved ones.
    • In real life, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as during family gatherings, community events, or personal rituals where individuals seek blessings and positive energy.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of seeking divine favor and the importance of community and relationships in our lives.
    • It serves as a reminder to cultivate positive relationships with others and to honor the guiding forces in our lives, whether they be spiritual, familial, or communal.
    • In practical terms, it encourages us to engage in acts of kindness, to offer our best selves to others, and to seek harmony in our interactions, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling and connected life.
