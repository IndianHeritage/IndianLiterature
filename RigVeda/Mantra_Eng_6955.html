यो दुष्टरो विश्ववारा श्रवाय्यो वाजेष्व अस्ति तरुताः | स नः शविष्ठ सवना वसो गहि गमेमा गोमती व्रजे ||
yo duṣṭaro viśvavāra śravāyyo vājeṣv asti tarutā | sa naḥ śaviṣṭha savanā vaso gahi gamema gomati vraje ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yo: who
    • duṣṭaro: the one who is wicked or harmful
    • viśvavāra: protector of all
    • śravāyyo: worthy of praise
    • vājeṣv: in the context of strength or power
    • asti: exists
    • tarutā: of the trees or nature
    • sa: he
    • naḥ: our
    • śaviṣṭha: the best or most excellent
    • savanā: offerings or sacrifices
    • vaso: of wealth or abundance
    • gahi: may you hold or grasp
    • gamema: may we go or move
    • gomati: with intelligence or wisdom
    • vraje: in the path or journey
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes a higher power or divine entity who is both a protector and a source of strength.
    • It acknowledges the existence of both good and evil, emphasizing the need for protection from harmful influences.
    • The reference to nature (trees) suggests a connection to the environment and the importance of harmony with it.
    • The plea for guidance in our journey signifies the human quest for wisdom and direction in life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In times of personal crisis, individuals often seek protection and guidance, much like the invocation in this mantra. For instance, someone facing a difficult decision may pray for clarity and strength.
    • Environmental movements often echo the sentiment of this mantra by advocating for the protection of nature, recognizing its role in our well-being.
    • In leadership, the idea of being a protector and a source of strength resonates with those who strive to lead with integrity and wisdom.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of seeking protection from negativity while embracing the strength and wisdom that comes from nature and the divine.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, such as personal challenges, environmental stewardship, and ethical leadership.
    • Ultimately, it serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of life, urging us to seek balance and guidance in our journeys.
