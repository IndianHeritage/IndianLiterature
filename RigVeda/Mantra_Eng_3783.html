मन्त्र: चित्रा वा येषु दीधितिर आसन्न उक्ताऽपान्ति ये |स्तीर्यं बरहिः स्वर्णरे श्रवांसि दधिरे परि ||
citrā vā yeṣu dīdhitir āsann ukthā pānti ye | stīrṇam barhiḥ svarṇare śravāṁsi dadhire pari ||
  • citrā vā yeṣu dīdhitir āsann ukthā pānti ye: This phrase can be translated to "Those who are near, who are radiant and who are nourished by the offerings." Here, "citrā" refers to brightness or radiance, indicating a state of enlightenment or clarity. "Dīdhitir" signifies illumination or brilliance, suggesting the presence of divine or spiritual energy. "Ukthā" refers to offerings or hymns, which are essential in Vedic rituals.
  • stīrṇam barhiḥ svarṇare śravāṁsi dadhire pari: This part translates to "They who have established the sacred grass, who hold the golden offerings, are the ones who possess the essence." "Stīrṇam barhiḥ" refers to the sacred grass used in rituals, symbolizing purity and sanctity. "Svarṇare" means golden, representing wealth and prosperity. "Śravāṁsi" refers to the essence or the core of something, indicating that those who perform rituals with sincerity and devotion attain true wealth and spiritual fulfillment.

  • Significance: This mantra emphasizes the importance of rituals and offerings in connecting with the divine. It highlights that those who approach these practices with sincerity and clarity will receive blessings and nourishment from the spiritual realm.
  • Real-life Application: In modern life, this mantra can be applied to various aspects, such as:
    • Spiritual Practices: Engaging in meditation, prayer, or rituals with a clear mind and heart can lead to personal growth and enlightenment.
    • Community and Relationships: Just as offerings nourish the divine, acts of kindness and support within communities can foster strong relationships and collective well-being.
    • Personal Development: The pursuit of knowledge and clarity in one's life can lead to greater success and fulfillment, akin to the radiance described in the mantra.

  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a reminder of the significance of intention and purity in our actions. By approaching life with clarity and sincerity, we can cultivate a deeper connection with ourselves and the world around us, leading to spiritual and material abundance.
