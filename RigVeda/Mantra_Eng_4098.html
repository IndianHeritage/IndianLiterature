ये अञ्जिषु ये वाशीषु स्वभानवः स्रक्षु रुक्मेषु खादिषु |श्रायाऽ रथेṣu धन्वसु ||
ye añjiṣu ye vāśīṣu svabhānavaḥ srakṣu rukmeṣu khādiṣu | śrāyā ratheṣu dhanvasu ||
  • Translation: The mantra can be translated as: "Those who are in the heavens, those who are in the earth, who are the creators of the light, who are in the golden realms, who are in the food, may they grant us strength in our chariots and in our endeavors."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • ye añjiṣu: "Those who are in the heavens" - Refers to celestial beings or divine entities.
    • ye vāśīṣu: "Those who are in the earth" - Indicates the presence of divine or powerful beings on earth.
    • svabhānavaḥ: "Creators of light" - Symbolizes knowledge, wisdom, and enlightenment.
    • srakṣu: "In the golden realms" - Represents prosperity and abundance.
    • rukmeṣu: "In the food" - Suggests nourishment and sustenance.
    • khādiṣu: "May they grant us strength" - A plea for empowerment and support.
    • śrāyā: "In our chariots" - Symbolizes our journeys and endeavors in life.
    • ratheṣu dhanvasu: "In our endeavors" - Refers to all our efforts and pursuits.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, this mantra can be invoked to seek guidance and strength from higher powers when embarking on a new project or venture.
    • In personal life, individuals may recite this mantra during challenging times, asking for support and clarity in their decisions.
    • During community gatherings or rituals, this mantra can be used to invoke blessings for collective strength and prosperity.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine support and strength in all aspects of life. It emphasizes the interconnectedness of the celestial and earthly realms, reminding us that we are not alone in our endeavors. By acknowledging the presence of higher powers, we can find courage and guidance in our pursuits, whether they be personal, professional, or communal. This mantra serves as a reminder to seek wisdom and strength from the universe, fostering a sense of unity and purpose in our lives.
