मन्त्र: अदितिर्न उरुष्यतव् अदितिः शर्म यच्छतु | माताऽमित्रस्य रेवतोऽर्यम्णो वरुणस्य चानेहसो वा ऊतयः सूतयः वा ऊतयः ||
aditir na uruṣyatv aditiḥ śarma yacchatu | mātā mitrasya revato 'ryamṇo varuṇasya cānehaso va ūtayaḥ suūtayo va ūtayaḥ ||
  • Mantra: "aditir na uruṣyatv aditiḥ śarma yacchatu | mātā mitrasya revato 'ryamṇo varuṇasya cānehaso va ūtayaḥ suūtayo va ūtayaḥ ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • Aditi: The infinite, the mother of all gods.
    • Na: Not.
    • Uruṣyatv: To be oppressed or burdened.
    • Śarma: Shelter, comfort, or protection.
    • Yacchatu: May grant or provide.
    • Mātā: Mother.
    • Mitrasya: Of the friend or ally.
    • Revato: Of the radiant or bright one.
    • Aryamṇa: Of the noble or honorable one.
    • Varuṇasya: Of Varuna, the god of cosmic order.
    • Cānehaso: Of the one who is without darkness.
    • Va: Or.
    • Ūtayaḥ: Blessings or boons.
    • Suūtayo: Good blessings or auspicious boons.
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra invokes Aditi, the mother of all gods, asking her not to let anyone be burdened or oppressed.
    • It seeks her blessings for shelter and comfort, emphasizing the importance of protection in life.
    • The mention of various deities like Mitra, Varuna, and Aryamṇa highlights the interconnectedness of divine forces in providing support and guidance.
    • In real life, this can be related to seeking help from friends, family, or community during difficult times, emphasizing the need for a supportive network.
    • The mantra also reflects the idea of cosmic order and harmony, suggesting that when we align ourselves with these principles, we can receive blessings and protection.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of divine protection and the need for a supportive environment in our lives.
    • It is applicable in real-life scenarios where individuals face challenges, reminding them to seek help and comfort from their community and the divine.
    • By invoking these blessings, one can cultivate a sense of security and well-being, fostering a harmonious existence.
