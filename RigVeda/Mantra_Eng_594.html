मन्त्र: सप्त त्वा हरितो रथे वहन्ति देव सूर्यः |शोचिष्केशं विचक्षण ||
sapta tvā harito rathe vahanti deva sūrya | śociṣkeśaṁ vicakṣaṇa ||
  • Mantra: "sapta tvā harito rathe vahanti deva sūrya | śociṣkeśaṁ vicakṣaṇa ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • Sapta: Seven
    • Tvā: You
    • Harito: Green or vibrant
    • Rathe: In the chariot
    • Vahanti: Carry or drive
    • Deva: Gods or divine beings
    • Sūrya: Sun
    • Śociṣkeśaṁ: Radiant or bright-haired
    • Vicakṣaṇa: Wise or perceptive
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the divine forces that guide and support us, represented by the "seven" vibrant energies or aspects.
    • The "chariot" symbolizes the journey of life, where the divine (Deva) and the sun (Sūrya) play crucial roles in illuminating our path.
    • The term "śociṣkeśaṁ" refers to the radiant qualities of the divine, suggesting that wisdom and brightness are essential for navigating life's challenges.
    • "Vicakṣaṇa" emphasizes the importance of being perceptive and wise in our decisions, highlighting the need for discernment in our actions.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, the "seven vibrant energies" can represent teamwork, where each member brings unique strengths to the project, driving it forward like a chariot.
    • In personal development, seeking wisdom and guidance from mentors can be likened to the divine forces that illuminate our path, helping us make informed decisions.
    • In times of uncertainty, reflecting on the "radiant qualities" of positivity and resilience can empower individuals to overcome obstacles, much like the sun dispelling darkness.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of divine guidance and wisdom in our lives.
    • It teaches us to recognize the vibrant energies around us and to harness them for our journey.
    • In real-life scenarios, applying the principles of discernment, teamwork, and seeking guidance can lead to personal and collective success.
    • Ultimately, it encourages us to embrace the light of knowledge and wisdom as we navigate through life's challenges.
