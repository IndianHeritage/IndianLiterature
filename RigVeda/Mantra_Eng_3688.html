यस्मै त्वं सुखृत जातवेद उ लोकं अग्ने कृणवः स्योनम् |अश्विनं स पुत्रिणं वीरवंतं गोमंतं रयं नशते स्वस्ति ||
yasmai tvaṁ sukṛte jātaveda u lokam agne kṛṇavaḥ syonam | aśvinaṁ sa putriṇaṁ vīravantaṁ gomantaṁ rayiṁ naśate svasti ||
  • yasmai tvaṁ sukṛte jātaveda: This phrase can be translated as "To whom you, O Jātaveda (the all-knowing), grant your blessings." Here, "Jātaveda" refers to Agni, the fire god, who is often seen as a mediator between the divine and human realms. In real life, this can be likened to seeking guidance or blessings from a higher power or a wise mentor in our endeavors.
  • u lokam agne kṛṇavaḥ syonam: This translates to "May Agni bring us prosperity in this world." The word "lokam" refers to the world or realm we inhabit. This line emphasizes the importance of seeking prosperity and well-being in our lives. For example, in a business context, this could mean striving for success and ethical practices to ensure growth and stability.
  • aśvinaṁ sa putriṇaṁ vīravantaṁ: This part means "May we be blessed with the strength of the Aśvins, the divine twin horsemen." The Aśvins symbolize health, vitality, and swiftness. In everyday life, this can be interpreted as a desire for good health and the ability to overcome challenges with vigor and resilience, much like athletes training for a competition.
  • gomantaṁ rayiṁ naśate svasti: This translates to "May we attain wealth and prosperity." "Gomantaṁ" refers to cattle, which were a symbol of wealth in ancient times. This line highlights the aspiration for material and spiritual abundance. In modern terms, it can relate to achieving financial stability and success in one's career or personal life.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a prayer for blessings, prosperity, strength, and well-being. It signifies the importance of seeking divine guidance in our pursuits and the aspiration for a fulfilling life. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applicable in various contexts, such as during personal challenges, business ventures, or health-related endeavors. By invoking the blessings of Agni and the Aśvins, individuals can cultivate a mindset focused on growth, resilience, and ethical prosperity, ultimately leading to a harmonious and successful life.
