अभि प्रियाणि पवते चनोहितो नामानि यह्वो अधि येषु वर्धते |आ सूर्यस्य बृहतो बृहन्न अधि रथं विश्वञ्चम् अरुहद् विचक्षणः ||
abhi priyāṇi pavate canohito nāmāni yahvo adhi yeṣu vardhate | ā sūryasya bṛhato bṛhann adhi rathaṁ viṣvañcam aruhad vicakṣaṇaḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • abhi: towards
    • priyāṇi: beloved or dear things
    • pavate: flows or moves
    • canohito: well-placed or auspicious
    • nāmāni: names or identities
    • yahvo: those who
    • adhi: upon or over
    • yeṣu: in whom
    • vardhate: flourish or grow
    • ā: from
    • sūryasya: of the sun
    • bṛhato: great or vast
    • bṛhann: the great one
    • adhi: upon or over
    • rathaṁ: chariot
    • viṣvañcam: all beings or everything
    • aruhad: has ascended or risen
    • vicakṣaṇaḥ: wise or perceptive
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the flow of beloved things and auspicious names that thrive in the presence of the great sun.
    • It emphasizes the importance of wisdom and perception in understanding the vastness of existence.
    • The imagery of the sun and chariot suggests a journey through life, guided by knowledge and the pursuit of what is dear and meaningful.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In personal relationships, the mantra can be seen as a reminder to cherish and nurture the connections that bring joy and positivity into our lives.
    • In a professional context, it encourages individuals to seek out and promote ideas and projects that are beneficial and well-received, much like the flow of auspicious names.
    • In education, the pursuit of knowledge and wisdom is akin to the journey of the chariot, where one must be perceptive and aware to navigate through life's challenges.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its call to recognize and embrace the positive aspects of life, guided by wisdom and understanding.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from personal relationships to professional endeavors, reminding us to focus on what is truly valuable and to navigate our paths with insight.
    • Ultimately, it encourages a harmonious existence, where beloved things flourish under the light of knowledge and perception.
