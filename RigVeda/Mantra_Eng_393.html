न ये दिवः पृथिव्यां अन्तम आपुर न मायाभिर धनदाम पर्यभूवन |युजं वज्रं वृषभश चक्र इन्द्रो निर ज्योतिषा तमसो गा अदुक्षत ||
na ye divaḥ pṛthivyā antam āpur na māyābhir dhanadām paryabhūvan | yujaṁ vajraṁ vṛṣabhaś cakra indro nir jyotiṣā tamaso gā adukṣat ||
  • na ye divaḥ pṛthivyā antam āpur: This phrase translates to "Those who do not reach the end of the heavens and the earth." It suggests the vastness of the universe and the idea that there are limits to human understanding and experience.
  • na māyābhir dhanadām paryabhūvan: This means "They are not enveloped by illusions or wealth." It emphasizes the importance of transcending material desires and illusions that can cloud one's judgment and understanding.
  • yujaṁ vajraṁ vṛṣabhaś cakra indro: This part can be interpreted as "Join the powerful, like the thunderbolt, the bull, and the wheel of Indra." Here, "vajra" symbolizes strength and resilience, while "Indra" represents power and authority. It encourages individuals to align themselves with strength and wisdom.
  • nir jyotiṣā tamaso gā adukṣat: This translates to "Without light, they have gone beyond darkness." It signifies the journey towards enlightenment and knowledge, moving away from ignorance and confusion.

  • Significance: This mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking truth and wisdom beyond the material world. It encourages individuals to look beyond superficial desires and to strive for deeper understanding and enlightenment.
  • Real-life Application: In today's fast-paced world, people often get caught up in materialism and distractions. This mantra serves as a reminder to focus on personal growth, knowledge, and spiritual enlightenment. For example, in a corporate setting, one might prioritize ethical practices and long-term goals over short-term gains, embodying the principles of this mantra.

  • Conclusion: The mantra teaches us the importance of transcending illusions and material desires to seek true knowledge and strength. It is applicable in various aspects of life, including personal development, professional ethics, and spiritual growth. By embodying these principles, individuals can lead more fulfilling and meaningful lives.
