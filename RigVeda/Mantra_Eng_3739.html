नू नो अग्न उतये सबाद्हसश च रातये |अस्माकाशश च सूरयो विश्वा आशास तरिषाणि ||
nū no agna ūtaye sabādhasaś ca rātaye | asmākāsaś ca sūrayo viśvā āśās tarīṣaṇi ||
  • nū no agna ūtaye: This phrase can be translated as "O Agni, may you arise for us." Agni, the fire god, symbolizes energy, transformation, and the divine spark within us. In real life, this can be seen as a call for inspiration and motivation in our endeavors.
  • sabādhasaś ca rātaye: This translates to "and may you bring us prosperity." Here, "sabādhasaś" refers to abundance and wealth, while "rātaye" signifies the attainment of goals. In practical terms, this can relate to seeking success in our careers or personal projects, emphasizing the importance of hard work and dedication.
  • asmākāsaś ca sūrayo: This means "and may our heroes be strong." "Asmākāsaś" refers to our own people or community, while "sūrayo" signifies strength and valor. This can be applied to teamwork and collaboration in real life, where the strength of a community or group can lead to greater achievements.
  • viśvā āśās tarīṣaṇi: This translates to "may all our hopes be fulfilled." "Viśvā" means all or universal, "āśās" refers to hopes or aspirations, and "tarīṣaṇi" signifies the act of crossing over or achieving. This can be seen in the context of setting goals and striving for personal growth, where the fulfillment of our aspirations leads to a sense of accomplishment.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking inspiration, prosperity, strength in community, and the fulfillment of hopes. In real life, it serves as a reminder to pursue our goals with determination, support one another, and remain hopeful for the future. By invoking these qualities, we can create a positive impact in our lives and the lives of those around us, fostering a sense of unity and shared purpose.
