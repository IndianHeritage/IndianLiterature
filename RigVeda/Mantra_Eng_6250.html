यद अद्य वां नासत्योक्थैर आचुच्युवीमहि |यद वा वाणीभिर अश्विनेवेत काणवस्य बोधतम ||
yad adya vāṁ nāsatyokthair ācucyuvīmahi | yad vā vāṇībhir aśvinevet kāṇvasya bodhatam ||
  • Mantra: "yad adya vāṁ nāsatyokthair ācucyuvīmahi | yad vā vāṇībhir aśvinevet kāṇvasya bodhatam ||"
  • Translation: This mantra can be translated as: "Whatever we may be seeking today, may we attain it through the blessings of the Nāsatyas. Whatever we may be speaking, may the Ashvins, the divine physicians, enlighten us with their wisdom."
  • Key Terms:
    • Nāsatyas: The twin deities in Vedic mythology, often associated with truth and healing.
    • Ashvins: The divine horsemen and physicians of the gods, symbolizing health, knowledge, and enlightenment.
    • Vāṇī: Refers to speech or communication, indicating the importance of words in our lives.
  • Meaning and Explanation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking divine guidance in our endeavors. It acknowledges that our efforts alone may not be sufficient without the blessings of higher powers.
    • The invocation of the Nāsatyas suggests a quest for truth and healing, which can be applied in various aspects of life, such as personal growth, relationships, and health.
    • The mention of the Ashvins highlights the significance of knowledge and wisdom in our pursuits. It encourages us to seek enlightenment and understanding in our actions and decisions.
    • In real life, this mantra can be reflected in situations where individuals seek clarity in their goals, such as students preparing for exams, professionals aiming for career advancement, or anyone facing challenges in life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • A student preparing for an important exam may recite this mantra to invoke clarity and focus, hoping to gain the wisdom needed to succeed.
    • A person facing a health crisis might reflect on the blessings of the Nāsatyas, seeking healing and truth in their medical journey.
    • In a corporate setting, a team may use this mantra during a meeting to foster open communication and collective wisdom, aiming for successful project outcomes.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of our efforts and the divine forces that guide us. It encourages humility in our pursuits and the recognition that wisdom and healing often come from beyond ourselves.
    • Incorporating this mantra into daily life can inspire individuals to seek truth, knowledge, and healing, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling and enlightened existence.
