अभ्यर्ष स्वायुध सोम द्विभरसं रयिम् |अथा नो वस्यसस कृधि ||
abhy arṣa svāyudha soma dvibarhasaṁ rayim | athā no vasyasas kṛdhi ||
  • Mantra: "abhy arṣa svāyudha soma dvibarhasaṁ rayim | athā no vasyasas kṛdhi ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • abhy: towards, for
    • arṣa: the seer, sage, or one who has attained knowledge
    • svāyudha: with one's own weapons or resources
    • soma: a sacred drink, often associated with immortality and divine inspiration
    • dvibarhasaṁ: double strength or power
    • rayim: wealth, prosperity, or abundance
    • athā: then, therefore
    • no: our
    • vasyasas: of those who are controlled or subdued
    • kṛdhi: make, create, or establish
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the blessings of the seers and sages, asking for strength and prosperity.
    • The phrase "svāyudha soma" suggests utilizing one's own resources and inner strength, akin to relying on personal skills and knowledge in real-life challenges.
    • "dvibarhasaṁ rayim" emphasizes the desire for abundant wealth and prosperity, which can be interpreted as striving for success in various aspects of life, such as career, relationships, and personal growth.
    • The concluding part, "athā no vasyasas kṛdhi," indicates a plea for the establishment of control over one's circumstances, suggesting the importance of self-discipline and focus in achieving goals.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • A student preparing for exams can relate to this mantra by harnessing their own knowledge and study techniques (svāyudha) to achieve academic success (rayim).
    • An entrepreneur may use their skills and resources to build a successful business, reflecting the essence of self-reliance and the pursuit of prosperity.
    • In personal relationships, individuals can apply the teachings of this mantra by fostering strong connections through understanding and mutual respect, thereby creating a harmonious environment.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of self-reliance, inner strength, and the pursuit of prosperity in life.
    • It encourages individuals to utilize their own resources and knowledge to overcome challenges and achieve their goals.
    • In a broader context, it serves as a reminder that true wealth and success come from within, and that self-discipline and focus are essential in navigating life's complexities.
