त्वं नो अग्नि अधराद उदक्तात त्वं पश्चाद उता रक्षां पुरस्तात् |प्रतिऽते ते अजरासस तपिष्ठा अघशंसं शोशुचतो दहन्तु ||
tvaṁ no agne adharād udaktāt tvam paścād uta rakṣā purastāt | prati te te ajarāsas tapiṣṭhā aghaśaṁsaṁ śośucato dahantu ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • tvaṁ: You
    • no: for us
    • agne: O Fire (Agni)
    • adharād: from below
    • udaktāt: from above
    • tvam: you
    • paścād: behind
    • uta: and
    • rakṣā: protection
    • purastāt: in front
    • prati: towards
    • te: your
    • te: your
    • ajarāsas: imperishable
    • tapiṣṭhā: the most powerful
    • aghaśaṁsaṁ: free from sin
    • śośucato: of the pure
    • dahantu: may they burn
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Agni, the Vedic fire god, who symbolizes energy, transformation, and purification.
    • The invocation seeks Agni's protection from all directions—below, above, behind, and in front—indicating a holistic approach to safety and well-being.
    • The terms "ajarāsas" and "tapiṣṭhā" emphasize the eternal and powerful nature of Agni, suggesting that divine energy is both unchanging and immensely strong.
    • The phrase "aghaśaṁsaṁ" refers to the purity and sinlessness of Agni, highlighting the importance of moral integrity and spiritual cleanliness.
    • The concluding request "dahantu" signifies a desire for the purifying fire to consume any negativity or impurities, reinforcing the transformative power of fire.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In daily life, this mantra can be applied during rituals or ceremonies where fire is used, such as weddings or homas, to invoke blessings and protection.
    • It can also serve as a reminder to seek purity in thoughts and actions, encouraging individuals to live a life free from negativity and sin.
    • In a metaphorical sense, the mantra can inspire people to embrace change and transformation in their lives, much like fire transforms materials through heat.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of seeking divine protection and purity in all aspects of life.
    • It serves as a reminder of the eternal and powerful nature of spiritual energy, encouraging individuals to cultivate a life of integrity and transformation.
    • In a broader context, it highlights the importance of community and collective well-being, as the invocation is for the benefit of all.
