उषिक् पावकः अरतिः सुमेधा मार्तेषु अग्निः अमृतो न धायि | इयर्ति धूमम् अरुषम् भरिभ्रद् उच् चुक्रेण शोचिषा dyām इनक्षन् ||
uśik pāvako aratiḥ sumedhā marteṣv agnir amṛto ni dhāyi | iyarti dhūmam aruṣam bharibhrad uc chukreṇa śociṣā dyām inakṣan ||
  • uśik pāvako aratiḥ: This phrase can be translated as "the fire that is pure and free from obstruction." Here, "uśik" refers to purity, "pāvako" means fire, and "aratiḥ" signifies freedom from obstacles. In real life, this can be likened to the pursuit of knowledge and truth, where one seeks clarity and purity in thoughts and actions, much like a clear flame that burns without hindrance.
  • sumedhā marteṣv agnir amṛto ni dhāyi: This part translates to "the wise one, the fire that is immortal, resides among mortals." "Sumedhā" refers to wisdom, "marteṣv" means among mortals, "agnir" is fire, and "amṛto" signifies immortality. This can be related to the idea that wisdom and knowledge are eternal and can guide humans through their lives, much like a guiding light that remains constant amidst the chaos of life.
  • iyarti dhūmam aruṣam bharibhrad: This translates to "it drives away the smoke that is red and heavy." "Iyarti" means to drive away, "dhūmam" refers to smoke, "aruṣam" means red, and "bharibhrad" signifies heavy. In a practical sense, this can be seen as the removal of negativity or confusion in our lives, akin to clearing the air in a room filled with smoke, allowing for clarity and understanding.
  • uc chukreṇa śociṣā dyām inakṣan: This phrase means "with the bright light, it illuminates the sky." "Uc" means high, "chukreṇa" refers to brightness, "śociṣā" means illuminating, "dyām" refers to the sky, and "inakṣan" signifies seeing. This can be interpreted as the pursuit of enlightenment and knowledge, where the light represents understanding that brightens our path and helps us see the truth in the world around us.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of purity, wisdom, and enlightenment in our lives. It signifies that just as fire is essential for warmth and light, so too is knowledge and clarity essential for a fulfilling life. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts such as education, personal development, and spiritual growth. It encourages individuals to seek wisdom, clear away negativity, and strive for enlightenment, ultimately leading to a more meaningful and purposeful existence.
