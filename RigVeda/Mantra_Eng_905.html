सुसंदृशं त्वा वयम् मघवन वन्दिषीमहि |प्र नूनं पूर्णवन्धुरः स्तुतो याहि वशाम् अनु योजा nv इन्द्र ते हरि ||
susaṁdṛśaṁ tvā vayam maghavan vandiṣīmahi | pra nūnam pūrṇavandhuraḥ stuto yāhi vaśām̐ anu yojā nv indra te harī ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • susaṁdṛśaṁ: Beautifully adorned or well-formed.
    • tvā: You (referring to a deity, often Indra).
    • vayam: We.
    • maghavan: O bountiful one (another name for Indra, the god of rain and thunder).
    • vandiṣīmahi: We praise or worship.
    • pra: Indeed or truly.
    • nūnam: Surely or certainly.
    • pūrṇavandhuraḥ: Full of abundance or wealth.
    • stuto: Praised or glorified.
    • yāhi: Come or approach.
    • vaśām̐: Under control or in our power.
    • anu: Following or in accordance with.
    • yojā: Join or unite.
    • nv: Indeed or certainly.
    • indra: Indra, the king of gods.
    • te: Your.
    • harī: The one who brings prosperity or wealth.
  • Overall Meaning: This mantra is a prayer to Indra, expressing admiration and seeking his blessings. The devotees acknowledge Indra's beauty and abundance, praising him for his generosity and power. They request his presence and assistance, emphasizing the importance of his role in bringing prosperity and wealth to their lives.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In agricultural communities, people often pray to Indra for rain, which is essential for crops. This mantra can be recited during rituals to invoke his blessings for a bountiful harvest.
    • In personal endeavors, individuals may use this mantra to seek guidance and support in achieving their goals, believing that divine intervention can lead to success.
    • During festivals, such as Indra Jatra, communities come together to celebrate and honor Indra, reciting mantras like this one to express gratitude and seek continued blessings.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its invocation of divine support and abundance. It serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness between humans and the divine, encouraging individuals to seek help in their pursuits. In real-life scenarios, it can be applied in various contexts, from agricultural practices to personal aspirations, reinforcing the belief that with faith and devotion, one can attract prosperity and success into their lives.
