प्र मंहिष्ठाय बृहत बृहद्रये सत्यशुश्माय तवसे मतिं भरे | अपाम इव प्रवण यस्य दुर्धरं राधो विश्वायु शवसे अपावृतम् ||
pra maṁhiṣṭhāya bṛhate bṛhadraye satyaśuṣmāya tavase matim bhare | apām iva pravaṇe yasya durdharaṁ rādho viśvāyu śavase apāvṛtam ||
  • pra maṁhiṣṭhāya: This phrase can be interpreted as "to the one who is the greatest" or "to the supreme being." It signifies reverence towards a higher power or divine entity.
  • bṛhate bṛhadraye: "bṛhate" means "great" or "vast," and "bṛhadraye" refers to "the one who possesses greatness." This emphasizes the vastness and omnipotence of the divine.
  • satyaśuṣmāya: This term translates to "the one who embodies truth." It highlights the importance of truthfulness and integrity in life.
  • tavase matim bhare: "tavase" refers to "strength" or "power," while "matim bhare" means "may my intellect be filled." This suggests a prayer for wisdom and strength to navigate life's challenges.
  • apām iva pravaṇe: This phrase can be understood as "like the flowing waters." It symbolizes the continuous flow of life and the need for adaptability.
  • yasya durdharaṁ: "yasya" means "of whom," and "durdharaṁ" refers to "that which is difficult to attain." This indicates the challenges one faces in life.
  • rādho viśvāyu: "rādho" can be interpreted as "to achieve" or "to attain," while "viśvāyu" refers to "the universal energy." This signifies the pursuit of universal knowledge and energy.
  • śavase apāvṛtam: "śavase" means "in the realm of life," and "apāvṛtam" refers to "uncovered" or "revealed." This suggests the unveiling of life's mysteries and truths.

Conclusion: This mantra from the Rigveda encapsulates the essence of seeking wisdom, strength, and truth from a higher power. It emphasizes the importance of recognizing the vastness of the universe and the challenges that come with it. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in moments of decision-making, personal growth, and spiritual exploration. By invoking these qualities, individuals can navigate their lives with greater clarity and purpose, striving for truth and understanding in their actions and thoughts.
