स वेद सुष्टुतीनाम इन्दुर न पूषा वृषा |अभि प्सुरः प्रुषायति व्रजं न आ प्रुषायति ||
sa veda suṣṭutīnām indur na pūṣā vṛṣā | abhi psuraḥ pruṣāyati vrajaṁ na ā pruṣāyati ||
  • sa veda suṣṭutīnām: This phrase can be translated to "He knows the well-spoken praises." Here, "sa" refers to a divine entity or a higher power, while "veda" means knowledge or wisdom. "Suṣṭutīnām" refers to the well-spoken or well-uttered praises, indicating the importance of recognizing and honoring the divine through sincere and thoughtful expressions.
  • indur na pūṣā vṛṣā: "Indur" translates to "the moon," which symbolizes calmness, serenity, and the nurturing aspect of nature. "Na pūṣā" means "not the nourisher," and "vṛṣā" refers to strength or vigor. This line suggests a contrast between the gentle, nurturing qualities of the moon and the more vigorous, life-sustaining aspects of the sun or other forces in nature.
  • abhi psuraḥ pruṣāyati: This can be interpreted as "the one who is invoked or called upon." "Abhi" means "towards" or "in the direction of," while "psuraḥ" refers to the act of invoking or calling. "Pruṣāyati" indicates the act of manifesting or bringing forth. This line emphasizes the importance of invoking the divine presence in our lives.
  • vrajaṁ na ā pruṣāyati: "Vrajaṁ" means "the flock" or "the community," while "na ā pruṣāyati" translates to "does not manifest." This suggests that without the divine invocation, the community or flock may not thrive or reach its full potential. It highlights the necessity of spiritual guidance and support for collective well-being.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of recognizing and honoring the divine through well-spoken praises. It highlights the nurturing qualities of nature, represented by the moon, and the importance of invoking divine presence for the prosperity of the community. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various contexts such as community gatherings, spiritual practices, and personal reflections. By acknowledging the divine and seeking guidance, individuals and communities can foster a sense of unity, strength, and purpose, ultimately leading to a more harmonious existence.
