ब्रह्मा च ते जातवेदो नमश्चेयं च गीः सदमिद् वर्धनी भूत्व् |रक्षा णो अग्ने तनयानि तोका रक्षोत नस्तन्वोऽप्रयुच्छन् ||
brahma ca te jātavedo namaś ceyaṁ ca gīḥ sadam id vardhanī bhūt | rakṣā ṇo agne tanayāni tokā rakṣota nas tanvo3 aprayucchan ||
  • Brahma ca te jātavedo: This phrase refers to the divine essence or the ultimate reality (Brahman) and acknowledges Agni, the fire god, as the one who knows all. In real life, this can be seen as recognizing the fundamental truths that govern our existence, much like how scientists seek to understand the laws of nature.
  • namaś ceyaṁ ca gīḥ: This translates to offering salutations and praises. In everyday life, this can be likened to showing respect and gratitude towards those who contribute positively to our lives, such as mentors, teachers, or even nature itself.
  • sadam id vardhanī bhūt: This part signifies the desire for prosperity and growth. In practical terms, it can be related to personal development and the pursuit of success in various aspects of life, such as career, relationships, and health.
  • rakṣā ṇo agne: Here, the mantra seeks protection from Agni. In a real-world context, this can be interpreted as seeking safety and security in our lives, whether it be physical safety, emotional well-being, or financial stability.
  • tanayāni tokā: This phrase refers to offspring or progeny. It emphasizes the importance of family and the continuation of lineage. In modern society, this can be seen in the value placed on nurturing the next generation and ensuring their well-being and education.
  • rakṣota nas tanvo3 aprayucchan: This is a plea for Agni to protect our bodies and lives. In real life, this can be understood as a call for health and wellness, highlighting the importance of taking care of our physical and mental health through proper lifestyle choices.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a holistic approach to life, emphasizing the interconnectedness of spiritual, physical, and familial aspects. Its significance lies in the recognition of divine guidance and protection while striving for personal growth and the well-being of loved ones. In today's fast-paced world, this mantra serves as a reminder to seek balance, show gratitude, and prioritize health and family, ultimately leading to a fulfilling and harmonious life.
