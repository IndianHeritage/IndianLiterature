तेषां हि मह्ना महताम् अनर्वणां स्तोमां इयर्म्य ऋतज्ञा ऋतावृद्धाम् |ये अप्सवम् अर्णवम् चित्रराधसस् ते नो रासन्ताम् महये सुमित्र्याः ||
teṣāṁ hi mahnā mahatām anarvaṇāṁ stomām̐ iyarmy ṛtajñā ṛtāvṛdhām | ye apsavam arṇavaṁ citrarādhasas te no rāsantām mahaye sumitryāḥ ||
  • teṣāṁ hi mahnā mahatām: This phrase can be translated as "Indeed, among the great ones, the most powerful." It emphasizes the importance of strength and greatness in the context of divine or cosmic forces.
  • anarvaṇāṁ stomām̐: This means "the unassailable hymn." It suggests a form of praise or worship that is invulnerable and profound, highlighting the power of words and prayers in connecting with the divine.
  • iyarmy ṛtajñā ṛtāvṛdhām: This translates to "those who know the truth and uphold righteousness." It signifies the importance of wisdom and moral integrity in achieving greatness.
  • ye apsavam arṇavaṁ: This can be interpreted as "those who have crossed the ocean of existence." It symbolizes overcoming challenges and the journey of life, akin to navigating through difficulties to reach a higher state of being.
  • citrarādhasas: This means "of varied and splendid achievements." It reflects the diversity of experiences and accomplishments that one can attain through effort and dedication.
  • te no rāsantām: This translates to "may they grant us abundance." It is a plea for blessings and prosperity, emphasizing the desire for a fruitful life.
  • mahaye sumitryāḥ: This means "to the great and benevolent." It acknowledges the presence of benevolent forces or deities that guide and support individuals in their endeavors.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking strength, wisdom, and divine support in the journey of life. It highlights the significance of righteousness and the power of invulnerable prayers in overcoming life's challenges. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in moments of struggle or decision-making, where individuals seek guidance and strength to navigate through difficulties. It serves as a reminder of the importance of moral integrity and the pursuit of greatness, encouraging individuals to strive for their goals while remaining connected to higher principles and values.
