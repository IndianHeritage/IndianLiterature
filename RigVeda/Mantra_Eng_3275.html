अक्षोदयच्छवसाक्षाम बुद्धं वार्ण न वातस्तविषीभिर इन्द्रः |दृढान्यौभ्नाद उषमान ओजोऽवाभिनत ककुभः पर्वतानाम् ||
akṣodayac chavasā kṣāma budhnaṁ vār ṇa vātas taviṣībhir indraḥ | dṛḻhāny aubhnād uśamāna ojo 'vābhinat kakubhaḥ parvatānām ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • akṣodayac: May he awaken or inspire.
    • chavasā: With strength or vigor.
    • kṣāma: Enduring or capable.
    • budhnaṁ: Of the wise or knowledgeable.
    • vār: O, the one who is powerful.
    • ṇa: A particle often used for emphasis.
    • vātas: Wind or air.
    • taviṣībhir: With great power or might.
    • indraḥ: Indra, the king of gods.
    • dṛḻhāny: Firm or strong.
    • aubhnād: From the waters or the ocean.
    • uśamāna: Of the dawn or light.
    • ojo: Energy or strength.
    • 'vābhinat: May he be inspired or motivated.
    • kakubhaḥ: Of the mountains or peaks.
    • parvatānām: Of the mountains.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes Indra, the god of rain and thunderstorms, asking for his strength and wisdom to awaken and inspire us.
    • The reference to "strength" and "endurance" suggests a need for resilience in the face of challenges, much like how a mountain stands firm against the elements.
    • The mention of "dawn" symbolizes new beginnings and the hope that comes with each new day, encouraging us to seek knowledge and power in our endeavors.
    • In real life, this can be related to personal growth, where one seeks to harness inner strength and wisdom to overcome obstacles, similar to how athletes train to build endurance and strength for competitions.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the importance of seeking strength and wisdom from higher powers, particularly in times of difficulty.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, such as during personal challenges, professional endeavors, or even in community leadership, where resilience and knowledge are crucial.
    • By invoking the qualities of Indra, individuals can find inspiration to face their challenges with courage and determination, much like how leaders inspire their teams to achieve common goals.
