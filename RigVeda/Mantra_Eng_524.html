मन्त्र: होतारं विश्ववेदसं सं हि त्वा विश इन्द्रते | स आ वह पुरुहूत प्रचेतसो 'ग्ने देवां इह द्रवत ||
hotāraṁ viśvavedasaṁ saṁ hi tvā viśa indhate | sa ā vaha puruhūta pracetaso 'gne devām̐ iha dravat ||
  • hotāraṁ: This term refers to the "invoker" or "priest" who calls upon the deities during a ritual. In real life, this can be likened to a leader or facilitator who brings people together for a common purpose.
  • viśvavedasaṁ: This means "one who knows all" or "omniscient." It signifies a person or entity that possesses comprehensive knowledge. In modern contexts, this could represent experts or scholars in various fields who guide society with their wisdom.
  • saṁ hi tvā: This phrase translates to "indeed, you." It emphasizes the importance of the addressed entity, suggesting a deep connection or reliance on them.
  • viśa indhate: This means "the community or people invoke." It indicates a collective effort or a communal approach to seeking blessings or guidance.
  • sa ā vaha: This translates to "may he bring." It expresses a desire for the invoked entity to deliver or provide something beneficial.
  • puruhūta: This term refers to "one who is invoked many times." It signifies a deity or force that is frequently called upon for assistance, akin to how people often turn to trusted friends or mentors in times of need.
  • pracetaso: This means "of the wise" or "intelligent." It highlights the importance of wisdom in decision-making and guidance.
  • 'gne: This term refers to "O fire" or "O divine light." Fire is often seen as a purifying and transformative force, representing energy and inspiration in our lives.
  • devām̐: This translates to "the divine" or "gods." It signifies the higher powers or forces that influence our lives and the universe.
  • iha dravat: This means "here, may flow." It suggests a desire for blessings or positive energies to manifest in the present moment.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the importance of invoking divine guidance and wisdom through collective efforts. It highlights the role of leaders and knowledgeable individuals in bringing communities together for a common purpose. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various contexts such as community gatherings, decision-making processes, and rituals where people seek guidance and blessings. The significance lies in recognizing the interconnectedness of individuals and the divine, fostering a sense of unity and purpose in our endeavors.
