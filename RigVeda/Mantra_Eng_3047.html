सोमो जिगाति गातुविद देवानाम् एति निष्कृतम् |ऋतस्य योनिम आसदम् ||
somo jigāti gātuvid devānām eti niṣkṛtam | ṛtasya yonim āsadam ||
  • Mantra: "somo jigāti gātuvid devānām eti niṣkṛtam | ṛtasya yonim āsadam ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • somo: Refers to 'Soma', a sacred drink in Vedic tradition, symbolizing vitality and immortality.
    • jigāti: Means 'to move' or 'to flow', indicating a dynamic process.
    • gātuvid: Translates to 'the one who knows the way' or 'the path', suggesting guidance or direction.
    • devānām: Means 'of the gods', indicating a divine or celestial context.
    • eti: Means 'to go' or 'to reach', implying movement towards a goal.
    • niṣkṛtam: Refers to 'liberation' or 'release', suggesting a state of freedom from bondage.
    • ṛtasya: Means 'of truth' or 'of order', indicating a fundamental principle of the universe.
    • yonim: Refers to 'womb' or 'source', suggesting a place of origin or creation.
    • āsadam: Means 'to sit' or 'to dwell', indicating a state of being or existence.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the divine essence of Soma, which flows and guides the gods towards liberation.
    • The reference to 'ṛtasya yonim' suggests that truth is the source from which all existence arises, emphasizing the importance of living in harmony with universal laws.
    • In real life, this can be seen in the pursuit of knowledge and wisdom, where individuals seek to understand their purpose and the truth of their existence.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, leaders who embody the principles of truth and guidance can inspire their teams to achieve greater heights, much like the gods guided by Soma.
    • In personal development, individuals who seek to understand their true selves and align with their values often find a sense of liberation and fulfillment.
    • In environmental conservation, recognizing the 'truth' of our interconnectedness with nature can lead to actions that promote sustainability and harmony.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of seeking truth and guidance in our lives, akin to the divine flow of Soma.
    • It encourages individuals to dwell in the source of truth, leading to liberation and a deeper understanding of existence.
    • In essence, it serves as a reminder to align our actions with universal principles, fostering a life of purpose and harmony.
