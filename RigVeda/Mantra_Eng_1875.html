यथा पूर्वेभ्यो जरीत्रिभ्य इन्द्र मय इवापो न तृष्यते बभूथा |ताम अनु त्वा निविदं जोहवीमि विद्यामेषं वृजनं जीरदानुम् ||
yathā pūrvebhyo jaritṛbhya indra maya ivāpo na tṛṣyate babhūtha | tām anu tvā nividaṁ johavīmi vidyāmeṣaṁ vṛjanaṁ jīradānum ||
  • yathā pūrvebhyo: This phrase translates to "as in the past" or "like those before us." It emphasizes the continuity of tradition and the wisdom passed down through generations.
  • jaritṛbhya: This word refers to "the wise" or "the learned ones." It highlights the importance of knowledge and the role of wise individuals in guiding society.
  • indra: Indra is a significant deity in Vedic literature, often associated with strength, power, and the ability to overcome obstacles. He symbolizes leadership and protection.
  • maya ivāpo: This phrase means "like the waters." It suggests fluidity, adaptability, and the nurturing aspect of nature, akin to how water sustains life.
  • na tṛṣyate babhūtha: This translates to "does not thirst" or "is not in need." It implies a state of abundance and fulfillment, where one's needs are met without struggle.
  • tām anu tvā: This means "after that" or "following you." It indicates a sense of progression and the importance of following the right path or guidance.
  • nividaṁ johavīmi: This phrase can be interpreted as "I invoke the knowledge" or "I call upon the wisdom." It emphasizes the act of seeking knowledge and understanding.
  • vidyāmeṣaṁ: This translates to "this knowledge" or "this wisdom." It signifies the value of education and learning in achieving one's goals.
  • vṛjanaṁ: This word means "the group" or "the community." It highlights the importance of collective effort and unity in achieving success.
  • jīradānum: This can be interpreted as "the essence of life" or "the vital force." It signifies the importance of life energy and vitality in all endeavors.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of wisdom, tradition, and community in achieving fulfillment and success in life. It encourages individuals to seek knowledge and guidance from the wise, much like how water nourishes and sustains life. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in educational settings, leadership roles, and community-building efforts, where the collective wisdom and support of individuals can lead to a thriving and harmonious society. By invoking the essence of life and the importance of unity, we can navigate challenges and foster a sense of abundance and fulfillment in our lives.
