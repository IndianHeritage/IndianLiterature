यया गा आकरामहे सेनयाग्ने तवोत्या |तां नो हिन्व मगत्तये ||
yayā gā ākarāmahe senayāgne tavotyā | tāṁ no hinva maghattaye ||
  • Mantra: "yayā gā ākarāmahe senayāgne tavotyā | tāṁ no hinva maghattaye ||"
  • Translation: "With which we have approached, O Agni, your army; may that bring us wealth."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • yayā: with which
    • gā: we have approached
    • ākarāmahe: we have come together
    • senayā: O Agni, your army
    • tavotyā: your power or might
    • tāṁ: that
    • no: for us
    • hinva: may it bring
    • maghattaye: wealth or abundance
  • Contextual Explanation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Agni, the fire god, who symbolizes energy, transformation, and the divine messenger.
    • The phrase "with which we have approached" indicates a sense of unity and collective effort, suggesting that the speaker is part of a community or group seeking blessings.
    • "O Agni, your army" refers to the divine forces or energies that Agni commands, emphasizing the power and protection that comes from the divine.
    • The request for wealth ("maghattaye") highlights the human desire for prosperity, abundance, and success in various aspects of life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, a team may come together to invoke the spirit of collaboration and seek success in a project, much like the collective approach in the mantra.
    • During a wedding ceremony, families may pray for the prosperity and happiness of the couple, reflecting the desire for abundance in their new life together.
    • In community gatherings, people often unite to seek blessings for their collective well-being, resonating with the mantra's theme of unity and divine support.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of unity, collective effort, and the seeking of divine blessings for prosperity.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios where individuals or groups come together to seek success, whether in personal, professional, or communal contexts.
    • By invoking the divine energies, individuals can foster a sense of hope and motivation, encouraging them to work together towards common goals.
