ससस्य यद वियुतā सस्मिन्न ऊधन्न ऋतस्य धामन् रणयन्त देवाः |महाम् अग्निर नमसā रातहव्यो वेर अध्वराय सदमिद ऋतावā ||
sasasya yad viyutā sasminn ūdhann ṛtasya dhāman raṇayanta devāḥ | mahām̐ agnir namasā rātahavyo ver adhvarāya sadam id ṛtāvā ||
  • Translation: "As the gods, who are the guardians of truth, are engaged in the great sacrifice, may the mighty Agni, the fire, be honored with our reverence, and may he lead us to the path of righteousness."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • Sasasya: Refers to the abundance or prosperity.
    • Viyutā: Indicates the act of being engaged or involved.
    • Ūdhann: Means to rise or elevate.
    • Ṛtasya: Pertains to truth or cosmic order.
    • Dhāman: Refers to the abode or dwelling place.
    • Raṇayanta: Means to engage in battle or sacrifice.
    • Devāḥ: Refers to the gods or divine beings.
    • Mahām̐ Agnir: The great fire, symbolizing energy and transformation.
    • Namasa: Means with reverence or respect.
    • Rātahavyo: Refers to the offerings made during the sacrifice.
    • Ver: Indicates strength or power.
    • Adhvarāya: Pertains to the sacrificial act.
    • Sadam: Means a place or abode.
    • Id: Refers to this or that.
    • Ṛtāvā: Means one who is established in truth.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, the mantra can be applied to the importance of integrity and truthfulness in business practices. Just as the gods are engaged in a great sacrifice, employees and leaders should work together towards a common goal with honesty.
    • In personal relationships, the emphasis on reverence and respect can be seen in how individuals treat each other. Upholding truth and righteousness can lead to stronger bonds and mutual understanding.
    • In environmental practices, the reference to Agni (fire) can symbolize the energy we use. By honoring the environment and using resources wisely, we can contribute to a sustainable future.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for unity, truth, and reverence in all aspects of life. It encourages individuals to engage in righteous actions, whether in personal, professional, or communal settings. By honoring the principles of truth and sacrifice, we can create a harmonious existence that benefits not only ourselves but also the larger community and the environment. This mantra serves as a reminder that our actions should be aligned with higher values, leading us towards a more fulfilling and meaningful life.
