आ त्व अद्य सधस्तुतिं वावातुः सख्युर आ गहि |उपस्तुतिर मघोनाम प्र त्वावत्व अधा ते वश्मि सुष्टुतिम् ||
ā tv a1dya sadhastutiṁ vāvātuḥ sakhyur ā gahi | upastutir maghonām pra tvāvatv adhā te vaśmi suṣṭutim ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā: from
    • tv: you
    • a1dya: today
    • sadhastutiṁ: good praise or commendation
    • vāvātuḥ: may it be beneficial
    • sakhyur: of friends or companions
    • ā: to
    • gahi: hold or grasp
    • upastutir: worship or praise
    • maghonām: of the wealthy or prosperous
    • pra: towards
    • tvāvatv: may you be present
    • adhā: below or beneath
    • te: your
    • vaśmi: I am devoted
    • suṣṭutim: good praise or commendation
  • Overall Meaning:
    • This mantra is a prayer for the presence and support of a divine entity or force.
    • It emphasizes the importance of friendship and community, suggesting that collective praise and devotion can lead to prosperity and well-being.
    • The speaker expresses a desire to hold onto good praise and commendation, indicating a wish for positive recognition and support in their endeavors.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace setting, this mantra can be applied when team members come together to support each other, recognizing each other's contributions and fostering a positive environment.
    • In personal relationships, it highlights the importance of friends who uplift and encourage one another, creating a network of support that leads to mutual growth.
    • In community gatherings, such as festivals or celebrations, the collective praise and recognition of individuals can enhance the sense of belonging and shared purpose.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the power of collective praise and the importance of community support in achieving prosperity and well-being.
    • It serves as a reminder to appreciate and uplift those around us, fostering an environment where everyone can thrive.
    • In real life, applying the principles of this mantra can lead to stronger relationships, enhanced teamwork, and a more supportive community.
