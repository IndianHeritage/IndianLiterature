आ वेधसां नीलपृष्ठं बृहन्तं बृहस्पतिं सदने सादयध्वम् |सादद्योनीं दमा आ दीदिवांसं हिरण्यवर्णं अरुषं सपेम ||
ā vedhasaṁ nīlapṛṣṭham bṛhantam bṛhaspatiṁ sadane sādayadhvam | sādadyoniṁ dama ā dīdivāṁsaṁ hiraṇyavarṇam aruṣaṁ sapema ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā: from
    • vedhasaṁ: the one who knows the Vedas
    • nīlapṛṣṭham: having a blue back
    • bṛhantam: the great one
    • bṛhaspatiṁ: the lord of wisdom and knowledge
    • sadane: in the abode
    • sādayadhvam: may we attain
    • sādadyoniṁ: the source of all good
    • dama: self-control
    • ā: from
    • dīdivāṁsaṁ: the one who shines brightly
    • hiraṇyavarṇam: golden in color
    • aruṣaṁ: red
    • sapema: may we be blessed
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the blessings of Bṛhaspati, the deity associated with wisdom, knowledge, and learning.
    • The phrase "nīlapṛṣṭham" suggests a connection to the vastness of the sky or the universe, indicating the expansive nature of knowledge.
    • "bṛhantam" emphasizes the greatness of Bṛhaspati, symbolizing the importance of seeking higher wisdom.
    • The mantra encourages self-control and discipline (dama), which are essential for personal growth and success.
    • The mention of "hiraṇyavarṇam" and "aruṣaṁ" signifies the duality of knowledge—both the golden (positive) and red (passionate) aspects of wisdom.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In academic settings, students often seek the blessings of Bṛhaspati for success in their studies, reflecting the pursuit of knowledge.
    • Professionals may meditate on this mantra before important meetings or presentations to enhance their confidence and clarity of thought.
    • Individuals practicing self-discipline in their daily routines, such as maintaining a healthy lifestyle or managing finances, embody the essence of dama.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of wisdom, self-control, and the pursuit of knowledge in our lives.
    • It is applicable in various scenarios, from education to personal development, emphasizing that true success is rooted in understanding and discipline.
    • By invoking the blessings of Bṛhaspati, individuals can aspire to achieve their goals while maintaining a balanced and enlightened approach to life.
