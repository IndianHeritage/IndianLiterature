नेतारा ऊषु णस तिरो वरुणो मित्रो आर्यमा |अति विश्वानि दुरिता राजानश्चरषाणीनाम् अति द्विषः ||
netāra ū ṣu ṇas tiro varuṇo mitro aryamā | ati viśvāni duritā rājānaś carṣaṇīnām ati dviṣaḥ ||
  • netāra ū ṣu ṇas: This phrase can be translated as "O leaders, who are far away." It addresses the guiding forces or deities that lead and protect us.
  • tiro varuṇo mitro aryamā: This part refers to Varuna, Mitra, and Aryama, who are deities associated with cosmic order, friendship, and moral integrity. They symbolize the importance of maintaining harmony and righteousness in our lives.
  • ati viśvāni duritā: This translates to "overcome all difficulties." It emphasizes the need to transcend challenges and obstacles that we face in life.
  • rājānaś carṣaṇīnām: This phrase means "the kings of the earth." It signifies the rulers or leaders who have the responsibility to guide their people wisely.
  • ati dviṣaḥ: This means "overcome the enemies." It highlights the importance of overcoming negativity and adversities that may hinder our progress.

The mantra as a whole can be understood as a prayer for guidance and protection from the divine forces represented by Varuna, Mitra, and Aryama. It calls upon these deities to help us navigate through life's challenges, ensuring that we remain on the path of righteousness and overcome any adversities we may encounter.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios:

  • Leadership: Leaders in any field can invoke the essence of this mantra to seek wisdom and guidance in their decision-making processes, ensuring they act in the best interest of their followers.
  • Personal Challenges: Individuals facing difficulties in their personal or professional lives can recite this mantra to find strength and resilience to overcome their struggles.
  • Conflict Resolution: In situations of conflict, this mantra serves as a reminder to seek harmony and understanding, encouraging individuals to rise above animosity and work towards peaceful resolutions.

In conclusion, this RigVeda mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the importance of divine guidance, moral integrity, and the strength to overcome challenges. It is applicable in various aspects of life, from personal struggles to leadership roles, emphasizing the need for wisdom, harmony, and resilience in our journey through life.
