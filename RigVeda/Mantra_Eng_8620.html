वयं ते अस्य वृत्रहन् वसो वस्वः पुरुष्पृहः |नि नेदिष्टतमाऽइषः स्याम सुम्नस्याध्रिगो ||
vayaṁ te asya vṛtrahan vaso vasvaḥ puruspṛhaḥ | ni nediṣṭhatamā iṣaḥ syāma sumnasyādhrigo ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • vayaṁ: We
    • te: Your
    • asya: Of this
    • vṛtrahan: Slayer of Vritra (a demon representing obstacles)
    • vaso: Of the dwelling or abode
    • vasvaḥ: All that is desirable
    • puruspṛhaḥ: With great desires or aspirations
    • ni: Not
    • nediṣṭhatamā: The most desirable or the best
    • iṣaḥ: Power or strength
    • syāma: May we become
    • sumnasyādhrigo: With auspiciousness and prosperity
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra expresses a collective aspiration for strength and prosperity.
    • It acknowledges the divine power that overcomes obstacles (symbolized by Vritra) and seeks to embody that strength in our lives.
    • The phrase "puruspṛhaḥ" indicates a yearning for greater achievements and desires, suggesting that it is natural to aspire for more in life.
    • By invoking the blessings of the divine, the mantra emphasizes the importance of community and collective strength in overcoming challenges.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a team setting, such as a workplace or sports team, members can draw on each other's strengths to overcome challenges, much like the collective invocation in the mantra.
    • During difficult times, communities often come together to support one another, reflecting the essence of seeking strength and prosperity together.
    • Individuals facing personal obstacles can use this mantra as a reminder to seek help and strength from their community, fostering a sense of unity and shared purpose.
  • Conclusion:
    • The mantra serves as a powerful reminder of the importance of collective strength and aspiration in overcoming life's challenges.
    • It encourages individuals to seek not only personal growth but also the prosperity of their community.
    • In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, from personal development to teamwork, emphasizing that together we can achieve greater heights.
