याभिः कण्वम अभिष्टिभिः प्रावतम् युवम् अश्विनाः |ताभिः स्व अ1स्मां अवतं शुभस पतिः पातं सोमं ऋतावृद्धा ||
yābhiḥ kaṇvam abhiṣṭibhiḥ prāvataṁ yuvam aśvinā | tābhiḥ ṣv a1smām̐ avataṁ śubhas patī pātaṁ somam ṛtāvṛdhā ||
  • Mantra: "yābhiḥ kaṇvam abhiṣṭibhiḥ prāvataṁ yuvam aśvinā | tābhiḥ ṣv a1smām̐ avataṁ śubhas patī pātaṁ somam ṛtāvṛdhā ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • yābhiḥ: By whom
    • kaṇvam: The young (or youthful)
    • abhiṣṭibhiḥ: With blessings or auspiciousness
    • prāvataṁ: Approaching or coming near
    • yuvam: You (the young)
    • aśvinā: O Ashvins (the divine twin horsemen)
    • tābhiḥ: By them
    • ṣv: May we
    • a1smām̐: From us
    • avataṁ: Protect or guard
    • śubhas: Auspicious or good
    • patī: Lord or master
    • pātaṁ: May you protect
    • somam: The moon or soma (nectar)
    • ṛtāvṛdhā: In accordance with truth or righteousness
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to the Ashvins, who are considered the divine physicians and bringers of health and vitality.
    • The invocation of the Ashvins signifies a request for their blessings to enhance youthfulness and vitality.
    • The phrase "by whom" suggests a connection to the divine forces that can aid in our well-being.
    • The mantra emphasizes the importance of auspiciousness and protection in our lives, particularly in the context of health and wellness.
    • In real life, this can be related to seeking guidance and support from mentors or spiritual leaders who embody wisdom and health.
  • Significance:
    • This mantra highlights the importance of divine intervention in our lives, particularly in maintaining health and vitality.
    • It serves as a reminder to seek blessings and protection from higher powers, especially during challenging times.
    • In practical terms, this can be applied in daily life by engaging in practices that promote well-being, such as meditation, yoga, and seeking knowledge from wise individuals.
    • Moreover, it encourages us to cultivate a positive and auspicious environment around us, fostering growth and health.
