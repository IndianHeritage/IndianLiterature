मन्त्र: अत्रैव वोऽपि नह्याम्य उभे आर्त्नी इव ज्ययाः | वाचस पते न षेधेमान यथा मद अधरं वदन ||
atraiva vo 'pi nahyāmy ubhe ārtnī iva jyayā | vācas pate ni ṣedhemān yathā mad adharaṁ vadān ||
  • Mantra: "atraiva vo 'pi nahyāmy ubhe ārtnī iva jyayā | vācas pate ni ṣedhemān yathā mad adharaṁ vadān ||"
  • Translation: "Here, indeed, I do not know both of you, like the two who are in distress; O Lord of Speech, may I not be hindered, as I speak sweetly."
  • Word-by-Word Explanation:
    • atraiva: Here, indeed
    • vo: of you (plural)
    • api: also
    • nahyāmy: I do not know
    • ubhe: both
    • ārtnī: those who are in distress
    • iva: like
    • jyayā: superior
    • vācas: of speech
    • pate: O Lord
    • ni: not
    • ṣedhemān: may I be hindered
    • yathā: as
    • mad: my
    • adharaṁ: sweet (or pleasant)
    • vadān: speaking
  • Contextual Meaning:
    • This mantra expresses a plea for clarity and understanding in communication.
    • The reference to "both of you" suggests a situation where there may be conflicting opinions or perspectives.
    • By likening the situation to "those who are in distress," it highlights the importance of effective communication in resolving conflicts.
    • The invocation to "O Lord of Speech" emphasizes the divine aspect of communication, suggesting that words have power and should be used wisely.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a workplace setting, when team members have differing views on a project, this mantra can serve as a reminder to communicate openly and seek understanding rather than allowing misunderstandings to escalate.
    • In personal relationships, especially during conflicts, invoking the essence of this mantra can encourage individuals to express their feelings clearly and listen to each other, fostering resolution and harmony.
    • In public speaking or debates, the mantra can inspire speakers to articulate their thoughts clearly and persuasively, ensuring that their message is received without hindrance.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its emphasis on the importance of clear and effective communication.
    • It serves as a reminder that understanding and empathy are crucial in any dialogue, especially in situations of conflict or distress.
    • In real-life scenarios, applying the principles of this mantra can lead to better relationships, improved teamwork, and more effective problem-solving.
