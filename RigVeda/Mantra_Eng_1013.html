मन्त्र: आर्चन्ति नारीर अपसो न विष्टिभिः समानेन योजनेना परावतः | इषं वहन्तीः सुखृतै सुदानवे विश्वेद अह यजमानाय सुन्वते ||
arcanti nārīr apaso na viṣṭibhiḥ samānena yojanenā parāvataḥ | iṣaṁ vahantīḥ sukṛte sudānave viśved aha yajamānāya sunvate ||
  • arcanti nārīr: This phrase translates to "the women who are engaged in worship." It signifies the role of women in spiritual practices and rituals, highlighting their importance in the community and family.
  • apaso na viṣṭibhiḥ: This means "not being obstructed by the waters." It symbolizes overcoming obstacles and challenges, suggesting that spiritual pursuits can help one navigate through life's difficulties.
  • samānena yojanenā parāvataḥ: This translates to "by the same means, they are united with the supreme." It emphasizes the idea of unity and collective effort in spiritual endeavors, suggesting that when individuals come together with a common purpose, they can achieve greater spiritual heights.
  • iṣaṁ vahantīḥ: This means "they carry the essence." It refers to the idea that those engaged in spiritual practices carry the essence of divinity and goodness, which can influence their surroundings positively.
  • sukṛte sudānave: This translates to "in the good deeds of the prosperous." It highlights the importance of good actions and their impact on one's life and the community, suggesting that prosperity is linked to virtuous living.
  • viśved aha yajamānāya sunvate: This means "all the gods are pleased with the worshipper." It signifies that sincere worship and good deeds attract divine blessings and favor, reinforcing the idea of reciprocity in spiritual practices.

Conclusion: The mantra emphasizes the significance of collective spiritual practices, the role of women in these rituals, and the importance of good deeds in achieving prosperity and divine favor. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in community gatherings, family rituals, and social initiatives where collective efforts are made towards spiritual growth and community welfare. It serves as a reminder that unity, good actions, and the involvement of all members of society, especially women, are crucial for achieving a harmonious and prosperous life.
