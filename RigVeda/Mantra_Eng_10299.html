आ त्वागमं शान्तातिभिर अथो अरिष्टतातिभिः |दक्षं ते भद्रम आभार्षम् परा यक्ष्मं सुवामि ते ||
ā tvāgamaṁ śaṁtātibhir atho ariṣṭatātibhiḥ | dakṣaṁ te bhadram ābhārṣam parā yakṣmaṁ suvāmi te ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā: from
    • tvāgamaṁ: your arrival
    • śaṁtātibhir: with great peace
    • atho: and
    • ariṣṭatātibhiḥ: with great prosperity
    • dakṣaṁ: skilled
    • te: your
    • bhadram: auspiciousness
    • ābhārṣam: may it be bestowed
    • parā: supreme
    • yakṣmaṁ: health
    • suvāmi: I bow to you
    • te: your
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the arrival of peace and prosperity.
    • It emphasizes the importance of skill and auspiciousness in life.
    • The repetition of "te" signifies a deep respect and reverence towards the divine or the higher power.
    • The mention of health indicates a holistic approach to well-being, encompassing physical, mental, and spiritual health.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, this mantra can be applied when welcoming a new team member, emphasizing the importance of their skills and the positive impact they can bring to the team.
    • During a family gathering, invoking peace and prosperity can create a harmonious environment, fostering better relationships among family members.
    • In personal health practices, focusing on holistic well-being can lead to a balanced lifestyle, integrating physical fitness, mental health, and spiritual growth.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of welcoming peace, prosperity, and health into our lives.
    • It serves as a reminder to cultivate skills and auspiciousness in our endeavors.
    • By applying the principles of this mantra in real-life scenarios, individuals can foster a more harmonious and prosperous existence.
