मक्षू हि स्मा गच्छथ īवतो द्यून इन्द्रो न शक्तिम परितक्म्यायाम् |दिव आजातā दिव्यā सुपर्णā कया शचीनाम् भवथः शचिष्ठा ||
makṣū hi ṣmā gacchatha īvato dyūn indro na śaktim paritakmyāyām | diva ājātā divyā suparṇā kayā śacīnām bhavathaḥ śaciṣṭhā ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • makṣū: "to the heavens" or "to the sky"
    • hi: "indeed" or "certainly"
    • ṣmā: "we" or "us"
    • gacchatha: "go" or "move"
    • īvato: "like" or "as if"
    • dyūn: "the divine" or "the celestial"
    • indro: "Indra," the king of gods
    • na: "not"
    • śaktim: "power" or "strength"
    • paritakmyāyām: "in the midst of" or "surrounded by"
    • diva: "heaven" or "sky"
    • ājātā: "born" or "created"
    • divyā: "divine" or "heavenly"
    • suparṇā: "well-winged" or "with good wings"
    • kayā: "by whom" or "with which"
    • śacīnām: "of the powerful" or "of the strong"
    • bhavathaḥ: "may you become" or "may you be"
    • śaciṣṭhā: "of strength" or "of power"
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the divine powers and encourages individuals to aspire towards higher realms of existence.
    • It emphasizes the importance of strength and power, both in a physical and spiritual sense.
    • Indra, as the king of gods, symbolizes leadership and the ability to overcome challenges.
    • The phrase "go to the heavens" suggests a journey towards enlightenment and higher consciousness.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In personal development, this mantra can inspire individuals to pursue their goals with determination and strength, much like a leader facing challenges.
    • In a corporate setting, it can be seen as a call for teamwork and collaboration, encouraging employees to work together towards a common goal, akin to a well-coordinated team under a strong leader.
    • In spiritual practices, it can motivate practitioners to seek higher states of awareness and connection with the divine, fostering a sense of purpose and direction in life.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the potential within each individual to rise above challenges and strive for greatness.
    • Its significance lies in the encouragement to harness one's inner strength and to seek divine guidance in the journey of life.
    • Applicable in various real-life scenarios, it inspires personal growth, teamwork, and spiritual awakening, making it a timeless piece of wisdom.
