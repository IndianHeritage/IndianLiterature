प्र वो यह्वम् पुरूणां विश्वां देवयतीनाम् | अग्निं सूक्तैर्भिर वचोभिर ईमहे यं सीमिदन्य ईळते ||
pra vo yahvam purūṇāṁ viśāṁ devayatīnām | agniṁ sūktebhir vacobhir īmahe yaṁ sīm id anya īḻate ||
  • pra vo yahvam purūṇāṁ viśāṁ devayatīnām: This phrase can be translated as "We invoke Agni, the divine being of the ancient ones, who is the lord of all beings." Here, "Agni" represents the fire god, a central figure in Vedic rituals, symbolizing purity, transformation, and the divine presence in the material world.
  • agniṁ sūktebhir vacobhir īmahe: This translates to "We meditate upon Agni with hymns and words." This highlights the importance of verbal expression and the power of mantras in invoking divine energies. In real life, this can be likened to the practice of prayer or meditation, where individuals use specific words or phrases to connect with a higher power or to focus their minds.
  • yaṁ sīm id anya īḻate: This means "He who is invoked by others." It emphasizes the universality of Agni's presence and the idea that many seek his blessings. In everyday life, this can be seen in how people from various cultures and religions seek guidance and support from their respective deities or spiritual figures.

  • Significance: This mantra encapsulates the essence of invoking divine energy through the medium of fire, which is considered a purifying force. It reflects the belief in the interconnectedness of all beings and the importance of community in spiritual practices.
  • Real-life Application: The principles behind this mantra can be applied in various aspects of life, such as in communal gatherings, rituals, or even personal meditation practices. For instance, during a family gathering or a community event, lighting a fire or a candle can symbolize unity and the shared intention of invoking positive energies.
  • Conclusion: The mantra serves as a reminder of the power of collective intention and the significance of spiritual practices in fostering a sense of community and connection with the divine. It encourages individuals to engage in rituals that honor the sacred, promoting harmony and transformation in their lives.
