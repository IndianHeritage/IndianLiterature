प्र पूर्वज पितराः नव्यसीभिर गीरभिः कृतुध्वं सदने ऋतस्य |आ नो dyāvāpṛthivī दैवेन जनेन यातं महि वां वरूथम ||
pra pūrvaje pitarā navyasībhir gīrbhiḥ kṛṇudhvaṁ sadane ṛtasya | ā no dyāvāpṛthivī daivyena janena yātam mahi vāṁ varūtham ||
  • pra pūrvaje pitarā: This phrase translates to "O ancestors of the past." It invokes the spirits of our forefathers, acknowledging their wisdom and guidance. In real life, this can be seen in practices like family gatherings during festivals where stories of ancestors are shared, fostering a sense of belonging and continuity.
  • navyasībhir gīrbhiḥ: This means "with new songs." It emphasizes the importance of innovation and creativity in our expressions. For example, in modern music, artists often blend traditional melodies with contemporary styles, creating something unique that resonates with both old and new generations.
  • kṛṇudhvaṁ sadane ṛtasya: This translates to "let us create a home of truth." It signifies the aspiration to build a life based on honesty and integrity. In everyday life, this can be reflected in the way we conduct our relationships and businesses, ensuring transparency and trustworthiness.
  • ā no dyāvāpṛthivī: This means "may the heavens and the earth support us." It highlights the interconnectedness of all elements of life. In practical terms, this can be seen in environmental movements that advocate for the protection of our planet, recognizing that our well-being is tied to the health of the earth.
  • daivyena janena yātam: This translates to "let us be guided by divine beings." It suggests seeking inspiration and guidance from higher powers or ideals. In real life, this can be observed in various spiritual practices where individuals look to their faith or philosophy for direction in challenging times.
  • mahi vāṁ varūtham: This means "may we attain great prosperity." It expresses a desire for abundance and success. In a practical context, this can relate to personal goals, such as pursuing education or career advancements, where individuals strive for growth and achievement.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a profound message about honoring our past, embracing creativity, and striving for truth and prosperity. Its significance lies in its call for unity between the spiritual and material aspects of life. In real-life scenarios, it encourages individuals to reflect on their roots, innovate in their pursuits, uphold integrity in their actions, and seek guidance from higher ideals while aspiring for success. This holistic approach can lead to a fulfilling and meaningful life, resonating with the values of community, respect, and aspiration.
