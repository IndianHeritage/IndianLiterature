गन्तारा हि स्थोऽवसे हवं विप्रस्य मावतः |धर्तारा चर्षणीनाम् ||
gantārā hi stho 'vase havaṁ viprasya māvataḥ | dhartārā carṣaṇīnām ||
  • Mantra: "gantārā hi stho 'vase havaṁ viprasya māvataḥ | dhartārā carṣaṇīnām ||"
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • gantārā: Those who are moving or traveling.
    • hi: Indeed or surely.
    • stho: One who is stable or firm.
    • 'vase: To dwell or reside.
    • havaṁ: The offering or invocation.
    • viprasya: Of the wise or learned one.
    • māvataḥ: May it come or may it be present.
    • dhartārā: Those who support or uphold.
    • carṣaṇīnām: Of the seers or sages.
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the duality of movement and stability. The "gantārā" represents those who are in motion, while "stho" signifies stability.
    • The invocation suggests that the wise (viprasya) should be present and that their wisdom should guide the actions of those who are moving.
    • The phrase "dhartārā carṣaṇīnām" emphasizes the importance of support and guidance from the sages, indicating that wisdom is essential for those who are on a journey or undertaking any endeavor.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a corporate setting, leaders (the wise) provide guidance to their teams (those in motion) to ensure that projects are executed effectively and with purpose.
    • In personal life, individuals often seek advice from mentors or elders (the sages) when making significant life decisions, ensuring they have the support needed to navigate their paths.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of wisdom and guidance in our lives. It teaches us that while we may be on a journey (moving), it is crucial to have a stable foundation of knowledge and support.
    • In real-life scenarios, whether in professional or personal contexts, seeking wisdom and guidance can lead to more informed decisions and successful outcomes.
    • Ultimately, the mantra serves as a reminder that both movement and stability are essential for growth and success, and that the presence of wise individuals can greatly enhance our journeys.
