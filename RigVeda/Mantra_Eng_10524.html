अपश्यं त्वां मनसा दीद्यानाṁ स्वायां तनू ऋत्व्ये नाधमानाम् |उप माम उच्‍चाय युवतिर बभूयाः प्र जायत्‍स्व प्रजया पुत्रकामे ||
apaśyaṁ tvā manasā dīdhyānāṁ svāyāṁ tanū ṛtvye nādhamānām | upa mām uccā yuvatir babhūyāḥ pra jāyasva prajayā putrakāme ||
  • apaśyaṁ tvā manasā dīdhyānāṁ: This phrase translates to "I have not seen you with my mind." It reflects a yearning for deeper understanding and connection. In real life, this can be likened to the pursuit of knowledge or truth in various fields, such as science or philosophy, where one seeks to comprehend concepts that are not immediately visible or understandable.
  • svāyāṁ tanū ṛtvye nādhamānām: This part means "in the sacred body of the self, may I not be diminished." It emphasizes the importance of self-worth and integrity. In practical terms, this can relate to personal development and self-care, where individuals strive to maintain their essence and not let external circumstances diminish their self-esteem.
  • upa mām uccā yuvatir babhūyāḥ: This translates to "may the young woman who is exalted come to me." This can symbolize the attraction of positive energies or influences in one's life. In everyday scenarios, this could represent the pursuit of relationships or partnerships that uplift and inspire us, whether in personal or professional contexts.
  • pra jāyasva prajayā putrakāme: This means "may you be born with progeny, desiring children." It reflects the human desire for continuity and legacy. In real life, this can be seen in the aspirations of individuals to create families, contribute to society, or leave a lasting impact through their work and relationships.

  • Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates a profound desire for connection, self-empowerment, positive influences, and legacy. Its significance lies in the universal human experiences of seeking understanding, maintaining self-worth, attracting positivity, and desiring continuity through progeny. In real life, these themes are applicable in various scenarios, such as personal growth, relationship building, and the pursuit of knowledge, reminding us of the interconnectedness of our aspirations and the importance of nurturing our inner selves while reaching out to the world around us.
