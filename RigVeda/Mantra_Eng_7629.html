प्र सु स्तोमं भारत वाजयन्त इन्द्राय सत्यं यदि सत्यं अस्ति |नेन्द्रो अस्तीति नेमा उ त्वा आह क इं ददर्श कम अभि ष्टवाम ||
pra su stomam bharata vājayanta indrāya satyaṁ yadi satyam asti | nendro astīti nema u tva āha ka īṁ dadarśa kam abhi ṣṭavāma ||
  • pra su stomam bharata vājayanta indrāya satyaṁ yadi satyam asti
    • pra su stomam: This phrase can be interpreted as "to praise or to offer a hymn." It signifies the act of devotion and reverence.
    • bharata: Refers to the people or the land of Bharata, which is often associated with India. It symbolizes unity and collective strength.
    • vājayanta: This term means "those who are victorious" or "those who bring strength." It emphasizes the importance of courage and triumph in life.
    • indrāya: Refers to Indra, the king of gods in Vedic literature, representing power, authority, and protection.
    • satyaṁ yadi satyam asti: This translates to "if there is truth, then there is truth." It highlights the significance of truthfulness and integrity in our actions.
  • nendro astīti nema u tva āha:
    • nendro astīti: This phrase can be understood as "Indra is not present." It suggests a moment of reflection on the absence of divine support or guidance.
    • nema u tva āha: This can be interpreted as "what is it that you have seen?" It invites inquiry and introspection, urging individuals to seek understanding and wisdom.
  • ka īṁ dadarśa kam abhi ṣṭavāma:
    • ka īṁ: This translates to "who is it?" It poses a question about identity and purpose.
    • dadarśa: Means "to see" or "to perceive." It emphasizes the importance of awareness and observation in our lives.
    • kam abhi ṣṭavāma: This can be interpreted as "whom do we praise?" It reflects the need for gratitude and acknowledgment of those who contribute to our success.

Conclusion:

The mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking truth, strength, and guidance in our lives. It emphasizes the importance of collective effort and the pursuit of integrity. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as:

  • In personal development, where individuals strive for truthfulness and self-awareness.
  • In community building, where unity and collective strength are essential for overcoming challenges.
  • In leadership, where the presence of guidance and support is crucial for success.

Ultimately, this mantra serves as a reminder to seek truth, acknowledge contributions, and strive for collective strength in our endeavors.
