स भूत्व यो ह प्रथमाय धायस ओजो मिमानो महिमानम् आतिरत् |शूरो यो युत्सु तन्वम् परिव्यत शीरषाणि द्यां महिना प्रत्य अमुञ्चत ||
sa bhūtu yo ha prathamāya dhāyasa ojo mimāno mahimānam ātirat | śūro yo yutsu tanvam parivyata śīrṣaṇi dyām mahinā praty amuñcata ||
  • sa bhūtu: This phrase can be interpreted as "that which exists" or "that which has come into being." It emphasizes the importance of existence and the essence of being.
  • yo ha prathamāya dhāyasa: This translates to "who first established" or "who is the originator." It signifies the role of a creator or a foundational force in the universe.
  • ojo mimāno: This means "the strength that is measured" or "the power that is recognized." It highlights the significance of strength and vitality in life.
  • mahimānam ātirat: This can be understood as "transcending greatness" or "beyond measure." It speaks to the idea of something that surpasses ordinary greatness.
  • śūro yo yutsu: This translates to "the brave one who is in action." It emphasizes courage and the importance of taking action in the face of challenges.
  • tanvam parivyata: This means "the body that is surrounded" or "the form that is enveloped." It suggests the idea of being encompassed by experiences or circumstances.
  • śīrṣaṇi dyām: This translates to "the head that reaches the sky" or "the apex that touches the heavens." It symbolizes aspiration and the pursuit of higher goals.
  • mahinā praty amuñcata: This means "with greatness, do not let go" or "do not release the greatness." It emphasizes the importance of holding onto one's strengths and virtues.

Conclusion: The mantra encapsulates the essence of existence, strength, courage, and the pursuit of greatness. It serves as a reminder of the foundational forces that shape our lives and the importance of taking action with bravery. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as personal development, leadership, and overcoming challenges. For instance, in a professional setting, an individual may draw inspiration from this mantra to lead a team with courage and strength, striving for excellence and encouraging others to reach their full potential. Ultimately, it signifies the interconnectedness of existence, action, and the pursuit of greatness in our lives.
