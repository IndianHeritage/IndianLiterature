अदाभ्येन शोचिषाग्ने रक्षस त्वं दह | गोपा ऋतस्य दीदिहि ||
adābhyena śociṣāgne rakṣas tvaṁ daha | gopā ṛtasya dīdihi ||
  • adābhyena: This term can be understood as "by the means of the offerings" or "through the rituals." It emphasizes the importance of performing rituals and offerings to invoke divine blessings.
  • śociṣāgne: This word refers to "O Agni, the radiant one." Agni is the fire god in Vedic literature, symbolizing purity, transformation, and the divine presence in rituals.
  • rakṣas: This translates to "protect" or "defend." It signifies the need for divine protection against negative forces or obstacles in life.
  • tvaṁ: This means "you," referring directly to Agni, the deity being addressed in the mantra.
  • daha: This word means "burn" or "consume." It indicates the transformative power of fire, which can purify and eliminate impurities.
  • gopā: This term means "guardian" or "protector." It highlights the role of Agni as a protector of righteousness and truth.
  • ṛtasya: This translates to "of truth" or "of order." It signifies the cosmic order and the principles of truth that govern the universe.
  • dīdihi: This means "shine" or "illuminate." It calls for the divine light to shine forth, guiding individuals towards righteousness and wisdom.

The mantra can be interpreted as a prayer to Agni, asking for protection and guidance through the power of fire. It emphasizes the importance of rituals and offerings in invoking divine blessings and the need for protection against negative influences. The invocation of Agni as a guardian of truth highlights the significance of living in accordance with cosmic order.


In real life, this mantra can be applied in various scenarios, such as:

  • Rituals and Ceremonies: During important life events like weddings or housewarming ceremonies, individuals can recite this mantra to seek blessings and protection for a harmonious and prosperous life.
  • Personal Challenges: When facing difficulties or obstacles, one can meditate on this mantra to invoke the protective energy of Agni, seeking guidance and strength to overcome challenges.
  • Environmental Awareness: The symbolism of fire can also be related to the need for environmental consciousness. Just as fire transforms and purifies, individuals can strive to transform their actions towards sustainability and respect for nature.

In conclusion, this mantra serves as a reminder of the importance of divine protection, the power of rituals, and the pursuit of truth in our lives. By invoking Agni, individuals can seek guidance and strength to navigate life's challenges while adhering to the principles of righteousness and cosmic order.
