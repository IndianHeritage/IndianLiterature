उत मे प्रायियोर वायियोः सुवास्त्वा अधि तुग्वनि |तिसृणां सप्ततीनां श्यावः प्रणेता भुवद वसुर दियानाम् पतिः ||
uta me prayiyor vayiyoḥ suvāstvā adhi tugvani | tisṝṇāṁ saptatīnāṁ śyāvaḥ praṇetā bhuvad vasur diyānām patiḥ ||
  • uta me prayiyor vayiyoḥ: This phrase can be translated to "May my prayers reach the heavens." It signifies the importance of sincere prayers and intentions in seeking divine blessings.
  • suvāstvā adhi tugvani: This means "May we be blessed with prosperity and well-being." It emphasizes the desire for a life filled with abundance and health, which is a universal aspiration.
  • tisṝṇāṁ saptatīnāṁ: This translates to "Among the three realms of existence." It refers to the physical, mental, and spiritual realms, highlighting the holistic approach to life.
  • śyāvaḥ praṇetā: This means "The one who leads us." It signifies the importance of guidance in our lives, whether it be from a mentor, a spiritual leader, or even our own inner wisdom.
  • bhuvad vasur diyānām patiḥ: This translates to "The lord of wealth and prosperity." It invokes the idea of a divine force that governs abundance and success in our lives.

Conclusion: This mantra encapsulates the essence of seeking divine guidance and blessings for prosperity and well-being across all realms of existence. In real life, it can be applied in various scenarios such as during personal prayers, rituals, or even in moments of reflection when one seeks clarity and abundance. The significance lies in the acknowledgment of a higher power that can lead us towards a fulfilling life, reminding us to remain connected to our spiritual roots while striving for success in the material world.
