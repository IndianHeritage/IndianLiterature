अव यत त्वं शतक्रतव इन्द्र विश्वानि धूनुषे |रयिं न सुन्वते सचा सहस्रीणिभिर ऊतिभिर देवी जनित्री अजीजनद भद्रा जनित्री अजीजनत ||
ava yat tvaṁ śatakratav indra viśvāni dhūnuṣe | rayiṁ na sunvate sacā sahasriṇībhir ūtibhir devī janitry ajījanad bhadrā janitry ajījanat ||
  • ava yat tvaṁ - "As you are" - This phrase acknowledges the presence and essence of Indra, the king of gods in Vedic literature.
  • śatakratav - "O Hundred-Strength" - This refers to Indra's immense power and capability, symbolizing strength and resilience.
  • indra - "Indra" - The deity representing thunder, rain, and war, often invoked for protection and prosperity.
  • viśvāni dhūnuṣe - "You who shake all things" - This signifies Indra's ability to influence and control the universe, shaking the very foundations of existence.
  • rayiṁ na sunvate - "May they not harm the wealth" - A plea for protection of resources and prosperity, emphasizing the importance of safeguarding one's wealth.
  • sacā - "And with" - This connects the previous thought to the next, indicating a continuation of the plea.
  • sahasriṇībhir ūtibhir - "With a thousand blessings" - This highlights the abundance of blessings and prosperity that one seeks from the divine.
  • devī janitry - "Goddess, the mother" - Referring to the divine feminine aspect, representing nurturing and creation.
  • ajījanad - "Who has given birth" - This signifies the act of creation and the nurturing aspect of the goddess.
  • bhadrā - "Auspicious" - This word emphasizes the positive and beneficial nature of the blessings sought.
  • janitry ajījanat - "Who has given birth" - Reiterating the theme of creation and the nurturing aspect of the divine feminine.

Conclusion: This mantra is a powerful invocation to Indra, seeking his protection and blessings for wealth and prosperity. It emphasizes the importance of divine support in achieving success and safeguarding resources. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applicable in various contexts such as during rituals for prosperity, in times of financial uncertainty, or when seeking strength and resilience in challenging situations. The significance lies in recognizing the interconnectedness of divine blessings and human efforts, encouraging individuals to seek support from higher powers while also taking responsibility for their own actions and resources.
