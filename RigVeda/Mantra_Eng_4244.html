यā धार्तारā रजसो रोचनस्योतādित्यā दिव्यā पार्थिवस्य |न वां देवā अमृतā ā मिनन्ति व्रतानि मित्रावरुणā ध्रुवाणि ||
yā dhartārā rajaso rocanasyotādityā divyā pārthivasya | na vāṁ devā amṛtā ā minanti vratāni mitrāvaruṇā dhruvāṇi ||
  • yā dhartārā rajaso rocanasyotādityā divyā pārthivasya
    • yā - "who" or "that which"
    • dhartārā - "supports" or "upholds"
    • rajaso - "of the realm" or "of the material world"
    • rocanasyotādityā - "the radiant sun" or "the divine light"
    • divyā - "divine" or "heavenly"
    • pārthivasya - "of the earthly" or "of the material existence"
  • na vāṁ devā amṛtā ā minanti vratāni mitrāvaruṇā dhruvāṇi
    • na - "not"
    • vāṁ - "you" or "your"
    • devā - "gods" or "divine beings"
    • amṛtā - "immortal" or "eternal"
    • ā minanti - "do not diminish" or "do not reduce"
    • vratāni - "vows" or "sacred commitments"
    • mitrāvaruṇā - "Mitra and Varuna" (deities representing friendship and cosmic order)
    • dhruvāṇi - "eternal" or "constant"

This mantra speaks to the divine support that upholds the material world, represented by the radiant sun, which symbolizes enlightenment and life. It emphasizes that the gods, who are immortal, do not diminish the sacred vows made by individuals, particularly those related to cosmic order and friendship, as represented by the deities Mitra and Varuna.


In real life, this can be interpreted as a reminder of the importance of maintaining our commitments and the support we receive from the universe when we align ourselves with divine principles. For example, when someone commits to a cause, such as environmental conservation, they may find that their efforts are supported by a community of like-minded individuals and even by the natural world itself, reinforcing the idea that divine forces uphold our righteous actions.


Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its affirmation of the divine support for our earthly endeavors and the importance of upholding our commitments. It encourages individuals to act with integrity and to trust that their efforts towards good will be sustained by higher powers. This principle can be applied in various aspects of life, such as personal relationships, community service, and professional ethics, reminding us that our actions resonate beyond the material realm and contribute to a greater cosmic order.
