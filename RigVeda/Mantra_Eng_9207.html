आ तेन यातं मनसो जवीयसा रथं यं वाम ऋभवश चक्रुर अश्विनाः |यस्य योगे दुहिताऽ जायते दिव उभे अहनी सुदिने विवस्वतः ||
ā tena yātam manaso javīyasā rathaṁ yaṁ vām ṛbhavaś cakrur aśvinā | yasya yoge duhitā jāyate diva ubhe ahanī sudine vivasvataḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā tena: By that (which is)
    • yātam: Moved or directed
    • manaso: Of the mind
    • javīyasā: With the greatest speed
    • rathaṁ: Chariot
    • yaṁ: Which
    • vām: You (plural)
    • ṛbhavaś: Have made or constructed
    • cakrur: They (the Aśvins) have created
    • aśvinā: O Aśvins (the divine twin horsemen)
    • yasya: Whose
    • yoge: In the union or connection
    • duhitā: Daughter
    • jāyate: Is born
    • diva: In the sky
    • ubhe: Both
    • ahanī: Days
    • sudine: On auspicious days
    • vivasvataḥ: Of the sun
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks of the divine Aśvins, who are often associated with health, medicine, and the dawn.
    • The phrase "ā tena yātam manaso javīyasā" suggests that the mind is directed with great speed, indicating the importance of mental clarity and focus.
    • The "rathaṁ" or chariot symbolizes the journey of life, which is guided by the mind's intentions and actions.
    • The Aśvins are depicted as creators, emphasizing the role of divine forces in shaping our experiences and destinies.
    • The mention of "duhitā jāyate" signifies the birth of new possibilities or ideas, which can be seen as the manifestation of creativity and innovation.
    • The reference to "sudine vivasvataḥ" highlights the auspiciousness of certain times, suggesting that there are moments in life that are particularly favorable for new beginnings.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be applied when launching a new product. The focus and clarity of the team (manaso javīyasā) can lead to a successful launch (rathaṁ) if they harness their creativity (duhitā jāyate) during an auspicious time (sudine).
    • In personal development, individuals can reflect on their mental clarity and intentions when setting goals. By aligning their thoughts and actions (yoge), they can create new opportunities in their lives.
    • In health and wellness, the Aśvins symbolize healing. Seeking medical advice or treatment during favorable times can enhance recovery and well-being.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra emphasizes the significance of mental clarity, divine guidance, and the auspiciousness of time in our lives.
    • It teaches us that our thoughts and intentions shape our reality, and by aligning them with favorable circumstances, we can create positive outcomes.
    • In essence, it encourages us to be mindful of our mental state and the timing of our actions, reminding us that we are co-creators of our destinies with the divine.
