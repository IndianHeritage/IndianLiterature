इह त्वं सूनो सहसो नो अद्य जातो जाताम् उभयाम् अन्तर अग्ने |दूत ईयसे युयुजाना ऋष्व ऋजुमुष्कान् वृषणः शुक्राम्ś च ||
iha tvaṁ sūno sahaso no adya jāto jātām̐ ubhayām̐ antar agne | dūta īyase yuyujāna ṛṣva ṛjumuṣkān vṛṣaṇaḥ śukrām̐ś ca ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • iha: here
    • tvaṁ: you
    • sūno: of the sun
    • sahaso: with strength
    • no: our
    • adya: today
    • jāto: born
    • jātām̐: of the birth
    • ubhayām̐: both
    • antar: within
    • agne: O Agni (Fire)
    • dūta: messenger
    • īyase: you move
    • yuyujāna: to unite
    • ṛṣva: strong
    • ṛjumuṣkān: with straight paths
    • vṛṣaṇaḥ: powerful
    • śukrām̐ś: bright
    • ca: and
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes Agni, the fire god, symbolizing energy, transformation, and communication.
    • The phrase "iha tvaṁ sūno sahaso" suggests the presence of divine strength and energy in our lives.
    • "Adya jāto" indicates the importance of the present moment, emphasizing that today is a new beginning.
    • The mention of "dūta" signifies the role of Agni as a messenger, bridging the gap between the divine and the earthly realms.
    • "Yuyujāna ṛṣva ṛjumuṣkān" highlights the need for unity and clarity in our actions and intentions.
    • The qualities of "vṛṣaṇaḥ" and "śukrām̐ś" reflect the power and brightness that we should aspire to embody in our lives.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a professional setting, invoking the energy of Agni can inspire teamwork and collaboration, leading to successful project outcomes.
    • In personal development, recognizing the significance of "adya" encourages individuals to seize the day and make positive changes in their lives.
    • In communication, embodying the qualities of a "dūta" can enhance relationships by fostering open and honest dialogue.
    • In spiritual practices, meditating on the qualities of Agni can help individuals find clarity and direction in their life paths.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra serves as a reminder of the divine energy present in our lives and the importance of harnessing it for personal and collective growth.
    • Its significance lies in the encouragement to embrace the present moment, seek unity, and embody strength and brightness in our actions.
    • In real-life scenarios, the teachings of this mantra can be applied to enhance relationships, foster teamwork, and inspire personal transformation.
