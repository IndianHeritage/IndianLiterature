अपो महीर अभिशस्तेर अमुñचो 'जागर आस्व अधि देव एकः | इन्द्र यास त्वं वृततूर्ये चकर्थ ताभिर विश्वायुस तन्मं पुपुष्याः ||
apo mahīr abhiśaster amuñco 'jāgar āsv adhi deva ekaḥ | indra yās tvaṁ vṛtratūrye cakartha tābhir viśvāyus tanvam pupuṣyāḥ ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • apo: waters
    • mahīr: of the earth
    • abhiśaster: of the powerful
    • amuñco: release or let go
    • 'jāgar: awaken
    • āsv: quickly
    • adhi: upon
    • deva: divine or god
    • ekaḥ: one
    • indra: Indra, the king of gods
    • yās: who
    • tvaṁ: you
    • vṛtratūrye: in the battle against Vritra
    • cakartha: you performed
    • tābhir: with those
    • viśvāyus: all beings
    • tanvam: body
    • pupuṣyāḥ: may you flourish
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra invokes the waters of the earth, asking them to release their power and awaken the divine presence.
    • It emphasizes the unity of all beings under the divine, represented by Indra, who is called upon for strength and protection.
    • The reference to Vritra signifies overcoming obstacles and challenges, suggesting that divine intervention is sought in times of struggle.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In times of drought, communities may perform rituals invoking the waters to bring rain, reflecting the plea for the release of the earth's waters.
    • During personal challenges, individuals may seek strength through meditation or prayer, similar to invoking Indra for support in overcoming life's Vritra-like obstacles.
    • In teamwork or community efforts, the idea of flourishing together can be seen in collaborative projects where everyone contributes to a common goal.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of divine support in overcoming challenges and the interconnectedness of all beings.
    • It serves as a reminder to seek strength from the divine and to work together harmoniously for collective flourishing.
    • In modern life, it can inspire individuals and communities to unite in the face of adversity, drawing on spiritual and communal resources for resilience.
