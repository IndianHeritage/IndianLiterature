एतान्य अग्ने नवतिर नवा त्वे आहुतान्य अधिरथाः सहस्राः |तेभिर वर्धस्व तन््वः शूर पूर्वीर दिवो नो वृष्टिम इषितो रिरिहि ||
etāny agne navatir nava tve āhutāny adhirathā sahasrā | tebhir vardhasva tanvaḥ śūra pūrvīr divo no vṛṣṭim iṣito rirīhi ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • etāny - these
    • agne - O Agni (the fire god)
    • navatir - new, fresh
    • nava - new
    • tve - to you
    • āhutāny - offerings
    • adhirathā - with great strength
    • sahasrā - thousands
    • tebhir - with these
    • vardhasva - may you grow
    • tanvaḥ - body
    • śūra - brave
    • pūrvīr - from the ancients
    • dīvo - of the heavens
    • no - our
    • vṛṣṭim - rain
    • iṣito - desired
    • rirīhi - may you bestow
  • Overall Meaning:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Agni, the fire god, asking for blessings and growth.
    • The speaker offers new and fresh offerings to Agni, symbolizing renewal and vitality.
    • It emphasizes the importance of strength and bravery, invoking the qualities of ancient heroes.
    • The mantra seeks the desired rains from the heavens, which are essential for prosperity and sustenance.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In agricultural societies, invoking rain is crucial for crop growth. Farmers may recite this mantra during rituals to ensure a good harvest.
    • In personal development, the idea of renewal and growth can be applied when individuals seek to improve themselves, much like the fresh offerings to Agni.
    • In team settings, the emphasis on bravery and strength can inspire individuals to work together, drawing on the qualities of past leaders to overcome challenges.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of seeking divine blessings for growth, strength, and prosperity.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from agriculture to personal development and teamwork.
    • By invoking Agni, individuals and communities can foster a sense of renewal and courage, essential for facing life's challenges.
