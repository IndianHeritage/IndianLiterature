नकीः परिष्टिर् मगवन् मगस्य ते यद् दाशुषे दाशस्यसि |अस्माकं बोध्य उचथस्य चोदिता मंहिष्ठो वाजसातये ||
nakiḥ pariṣṭir maghavan maghasya te yad dāśuṣe daśasyasi | asmākam bodhy ucathasya coditā maṁhiṣṭho vājasātaye ||
  • nakiḥ pariṣṭir: This phrase can be interpreted as "What is the limit or boundary?" It raises a question about the extent of one's capabilities or resources.
  • maghavan: Referring to "the bountiful" or "the generous one," this term often denotes a deity associated with wealth and abundance, such as Indra.
  • maghasya te: This translates to "of your wealth" or "of your greatness," emphasizing the importance of prosperity and the divine blessings that come with it.
  • yad dāśuṣe daśasyasi: This can be understood as "what you give to the one who serves." It highlights the value of generosity and the rewards of giving to those who are devoted or in need.
  • asmākam bodhy: Meaning "our understanding" or "our knowledge," this phrase suggests a collective awareness or insight that is shared among a group.
  • ucathasya coditā: This translates to "inspired by the call" or "urged by the voice," indicating a motivation or encouragement to act based on a higher calling or purpose.
  • maṁhiṣṭho vājasātaye: This phrase can be interpreted as "for the best of our efforts" or "for the attainment of strength," suggesting a focus on striving for excellence and the pursuit of strength in endeavors.

Conclusion: This mantra emphasizes the importance of understanding one's limits while seeking abundance and prosperity. It encourages generosity towards those who serve and highlights the collective knowledge that can inspire action. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various contexts, such as in leadership, community service, and personal development. For instance, a leader who recognizes the limits of their resources while striving to uplift their team embodies the essence of this mantra. Additionally, in charitable acts, giving to those in need not only fosters community but also enriches the giver's life. Ultimately, this mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of generosity, understanding, and the pursuit of excellence in all aspects of life.
