वराऽइवेन्द्रैवतासो हिरण्यैर्बहि स्वधाभिः तंवः पिपीष्रे |श्रियः श्रेयान्स तवसो रथेṣu सत्रा महांसी चक्रिरे तनूषु ||
varā ived raivatāso hiraṇyair abhi svadhābhis tanvaḥ pipiśre | śriye śreyāṁsas tavaso ratheṣu satrā mahāṁsi cakrire tanūṣu ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • varā: best, excellent
    • ived: I have known, I have realized
    • raivatāso: of the Raivata (a celestial or divine aspect)
    • hiraṇyair: with gold, golden
    • abhi: towards, in the direction of
    • svadhābhis: with offerings, with self-offerings
    • tanvaḥ: bodies, forms
    • pipiśre: have become full, have flourished
    • śriye: for prosperity, for wealth
    • śreyāṁsas: of the best, of the most excellent
    • tavaso: of your (divine) power
    • ratheṣu: in the chariots
    • satrā: in the sacrifices, in the rituals
    • mahāṁsi: great, mighty
    • cakrire: they have created, they have established
    • tanūṣu: in the bodies, in the forms
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra speaks of the realization of the best and most excellent aspects of life, symbolized by the divine Raivata.
    • The mention of "hiraṇyair" (gold) signifies wealth and prosperity, suggesting that true richness comes from spiritual and material abundance.
    • "Svadhābhis" indicates the importance of self-offering and dedication in achieving one's goals.
    • The phrase "pipiśre tanvaḥ" suggests that through these offerings and realizations, one’s life and forms flourish and become full.
    • The mantra emphasizes the connection between divine power ("tavaso") and the rituals or sacrifices ("satrā") that are performed, indicating that such practices lead to the establishment of greatness in life.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern life, this mantra can be related to the pursuit of excellence in various fields, such as education, career, and personal development. For instance, a student who dedicates themselves to their studies and offers their best efforts is likely to achieve success.
    • In business, the idea of self-offering can be seen in the commitment to ethical practices and community service, which can lead to prosperity and a positive reputation.
    • In personal relationships, the mantra encourages individuals to invest in their connections with love and care, leading to flourishing relationships.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of recognizing and realizing the best aspects of life through dedication, self-offering, and connection to divine power.
    • It teaches that true prosperity and flourishing come from a harmonious blend of spiritual and material pursuits.
    • In real life, applying the principles of this mantra can lead to personal growth, successful relationships, and a fulfilling life, making it relevant in various aspects of modern existence.
