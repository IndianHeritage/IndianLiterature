अग्निर जागार तम ऋचः कामयन्ते 'अग्निर जागार तम उ सामानि यन्ति |अग्निर जागार तम अयं सोम आह तवाहम अस्मि सख्ये न्योकाः ||
agnir jāgāra tam ṛcaḥ kāmayante 'gnir jāgāra tam u sāmāni yanti | agnir jāgāra tam ayaṁ soma āha tavāham asmi sakhye nyokāḥ ||
  • Agnir jāgāra: The term "Agnir" refers to fire, which is often seen as a symbol of energy, transformation, and purification. "Jāgāra" means to awaken or to be vigilant. Together, this phrase can be interpreted as the fire that awakens or brings awareness.
  • tam ṛcaḥ kāmayante: "Tam" means that, "ṛcaḥ" refers to the verses or hymns, and "kāmayante" means to desire or seek. This part suggests that the fire (energy) is sought after through the verses, indicating a connection between spiritual knowledge and the pursuit of enlightenment.
  • u sāmāni yanti: "U" means and, "sāmāni" refers to the harmonious or the collective, and "yanti" means to go or to lead. This implies that the energy of fire leads to harmony and unity among beings.
  • agnir jāgāra tam: This repetition emphasizes the importance of the awakening fire, reinforcing its significance in the context of the mantra.
  • ayaṁ soma āha: "Ayaṁ" means this, "soma" refers to the divine nectar or the essence of life, and "āha" means to speak or declare. This suggests that the essence of life is being declared or acknowledged.
  • tavāham asmi: "Tavāham" means I am yours, and "asmi" means I exist. This indicates a sense of belonging or connection to the divine or the universal energy.
  • sakhye nyokāḥ: "Sakhye" means in friendship or companionship, and "nyokāḥ" refers to the offerings or the gifts. This part emphasizes the importance of friendship and the offerings made in the spirit of camaraderie.

Conclusion: This mantra highlights the significance of fire as a symbol of awakening, energy, and transformation. It emphasizes the pursuit of spiritual knowledge through harmonious unity and the essence of life. In real-life scenarios, this can be applied in various contexts such as:

  • In personal development, where individuals seek to awaken their inner potential and transform their lives through knowledge and awareness.
  • In community building, where the collective energy of individuals can lead to harmony and unity, fostering friendships and collaborations.
  • In spiritual practices, where the essence of life is acknowledged and celebrated, promoting a sense of belonging to a greater whole.

Overall, the mantra serves as a reminder of the interconnectedness of all beings and the transformative power of knowledge and friendship in our lives.
