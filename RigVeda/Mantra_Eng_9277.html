मन्त्र: निपस्त्याशु तृतः स्तभूयन् परिवीतो योनौ सीदद अन्तः |अतः सङ्गृभ्याः विश्वां दामूनां विधर्मणायन्त्रैर ईयते नृन् ||
ni pastyāsu tritaḥ stabhūyan parivīto yonau sīdad antaḥ | ataḥ saṁgṛbhyā viśāṁ damūnā vidharmaṇāyantrair īyate nṝn ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ni: not
    • pastyāsu: in the past
    • tritaḥ: third
    • stabhūyan: having been established
    • parivīto: surrounded
    • yonau: in the womb
    • sīdad: sat down
    • antaḥ: within
    • ataḥ: therefore
    • saṁgṛbhyā: having gathered
    • viśāṁ: of the people
    • damūnā: with restraint
    • vidharmaṇā: with righteousness
    • yantrair: with instruments
    • īyate: is moved
    • nṝn: men
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra speaks about the importance of restraint and righteousness in guiding people.
    • The phrase "not in the past" suggests a focus on the present and future, emphasizing the need for current actions to be rooted in wisdom.
    • The idea of being "surrounded" in the womb indicates a nurturing environment where growth and development occur.
    • The mention of "gathering the people" highlights the collective responsibility of society to uphold values and principles.
    • Using "instruments" implies that there are tools or methods available to facilitate this guidance, which can be interpreted as education, laws, or moral teachings.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In modern society, educators play a crucial role in shaping the values of students, much like the "instruments" mentioned in the mantra.
    • Community leaders often gather people to promote social justice and ethical behavior, reflecting the collective responsibility emphasized in the mantra.
    • In personal relationships, practicing restraint and righteousness can lead to healthier interactions and stronger bonds, illustrating the mantra's teachings in everyday life.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra signifies the importance of nurturing environments and collective responsibility in guiding individuals towards righteousness.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, from education to community leadership, emphasizing the need for moral integrity and restraint.
    • Ultimately, it serves as a reminder that our actions today shape the future, and we must strive to create a positive impact on society.
