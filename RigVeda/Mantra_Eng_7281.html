अग्निं द्वेषो योतवै नो गृणीमास्य अग्निं शं योश च दातवे |विश्वासु विक्ष्व अवितेव हव्यो भूवद वस्तुर ऋषूणाम ||
agniṁ dveṣo yotavai no gṛṇīmasy agniṁ śaṁ yoś ca dātave | viśvāsu vikṣv aviteva havyo bhuvad vastur ṛṣūṇām ||
  • Agniṁ dveṣo yotavai no gṛṇīmasy agniṁ śaṁ yoś ca dātave:
    • Agniṁ: Refers to fire, symbolizing energy, transformation, and purity.
    • dveṣo: Means hatred or negativity, indicating the need to overcome such feelings.
    • yotavai: Suggests the act of invoking or calling upon.
    • no: Translates to 'us' or 'our', indicating a collective aspect.
    • gṛṇīmasy: Means to praise or acknowledge, emphasizing the importance of recognition.
    • śaṁ: Refers to peace or well-being, highlighting the desire for harmony.
    • yoś: Means 'who', indicating a connection to the divine or higher power.
    • dātave: Means to give or bestow, suggesting the act of sharing blessings.
  • viśvāsu vikṣv aviteva havyo bhuvad vastur ṛṣūṇām:
    • viśvāsu: Refers to all or universal, indicating inclusivity.
    • vikṣv: Means to look or observe, suggesting awareness and mindfulness.
    • aviteva: Implies protection or guardianship, indicating a need for safety.
    • havyo: Refers to offerings or sacrifices, symbolizing devotion.
    • bhuvad: Means to become or manifest, indicating transformation.
    • vastur: Refers to substance or essence, highlighting the importance of material and spiritual balance.
    • ṛṣūṇām: Refers to sages or wise individuals, indicating the value of wisdom.

Conclusion:

This mantra emphasizes the importance of invoking the divine energy of Agni (fire) while overcoming negativity and fostering peace. It highlights the need for collective recognition of blessings and the importance of mindfulness in our actions. In real life, this can be applied in various scenarios such as:

  • In personal relationships, overcoming hatred and fostering understanding can lead to harmony.
  • In community settings, recognizing and appreciating each other's contributions can strengthen bonds.
  • In spiritual practices, invoking divine energy through rituals can enhance personal growth and transformation.

Overall, the mantra serves as a reminder to seek positivity, wisdom, and transformation in our lives, encouraging us to be mindful of our actions and their impact on ourselves and others.
