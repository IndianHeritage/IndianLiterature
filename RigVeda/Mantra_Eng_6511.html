आ हि रुहतम अश्विनाऽ रथे कोशे हिरण्यये वृषण्वसू |युñजाथाम पीवरीर इषः ||
ā hi ruhatam aśvinā rathe kośe hiraṇyaye vṛṣaṇvasū | yuñjāthām pīvarīr iṣaḥ ||
  • Mantra: "ā hi ruhatam aśvinā rathe kośe hiraṇyaye vṛṣaṇvasū | yuñjāthām pīvarīr iṣaḥ ||"
  • Translation: "O Ashvins, who are the great ones, may you unite with the powerful and rich in the chariot of wealth and prosperity."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • ā hi: An invocation, calling upon the divine.
    • ruhatam: The great or powerful.
    • aśvinā: Referring to the Ashvins, the twin deities associated with health and medicine.
    • rathe: In the chariot, symbolizing movement and progress.
    • kośe: In the treasure or wealth, indicating abundance.
    • hiraṇyaye: Golden or prosperous, representing richness.
    • vṛṣaṇvasū: The powerful or strong, suggesting strength and capability.
    • yuñjāthām: May you unite or connect.
    • pīvarīr: The great or mighty.
    • iṣaḥ: The divine will or intention.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be seen as a call for collaboration and partnership. Just as the Ashvins are invoked for their strength and prosperity, businesses often seek alliances to enhance their capabilities and resources.
    • In personal life, individuals may use this mantra during times of transition, such as starting a new job or embarking on a new venture, to seek divine support for success and abundance.
    • In health and wellness, invoking the Ashvins can symbolize the pursuit of holistic well-being, where one seeks to unite physical, mental, and spiritual health.
  • Conclusion: The significance of this mantra lies in its call for divine assistance in achieving strength, prosperity, and unity. It emphasizes the importance of collaboration and the pursuit of wealth, not just in material terms but also in health and well-being. In real-life scenarios, this mantra can be applied in various contexts, such as business partnerships, personal growth, and health initiatives, reminding us that seeking support from higher powers can lead to greater success and fulfillment.
