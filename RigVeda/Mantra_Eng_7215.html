आ त्वा रथं यथोतये सुम्नाय वर्तयामसि |तुविकूर्मिम ऋतीषहम इन्द्र शविष्ठ सत्पते ||
ā tvā rathaṁ yathotaye sumnāya vartayāmasi | tuvikūrmim ṛtīṣaham indra śaviṣṭha satpate ||
  • Word-by-Word Meaning:
    • ā: from
    • tvā: you
    • rathaṁ: chariot
    • yathotaye: as you desire
    • sumnāya: for auspiciousness
    • vartayāmasi: we are moving
    • tuvikūrmim: with great strength
    • ṛtīṣaham: I uphold the truth
    • indra: O Indra (the king of gods)
    • śaviṣṭha: the most powerful
    • satpate: O lord of the good
  • Interpretation:
    • This mantra is a prayer to Indra, the king of gods, asking for guidance and strength.
    • The chariot symbolizes the journey of life, and the speaker expresses a desire to move forward in a way that is auspicious and beneficial.
    • The phrase "as you desire" indicates a submission to divine will, suggesting that one should align their actions with higher principles.
    • The invocation of strength and truth emphasizes the importance of integrity and power in achieving one's goals.
  • Real-Life Examples:
    • In a business context, this mantra can be applied when launching a new project. Leaders may seek divine guidance to ensure that their efforts are auspicious and aligned with ethical practices.
    • In personal life, individuals may recite this mantra during challenging times, asking for strength and clarity to navigate their circumstances effectively.
    • In community settings, it can be used during gatherings or ceremonies to invoke collective strength and a shared commitment to truth and goodness.
  • Conclusion:
    • This mantra highlights the significance of seeking divine guidance in our endeavors, emphasizing the importance of aligning our actions with higher values.
    • It serves as a reminder that strength and truth are essential components of a successful and meaningful life.
    • By invoking the blessings of Indra, individuals and communities can aspire to move forward with purpose and integrity, ensuring that their journeys are both auspicious and impactful.
