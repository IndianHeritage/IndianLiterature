मन्त्र: येन सूर्य ज्योतिषा बाधसे तमो जगच च विश्वम् उदियर्षि भानुना | तेनास्मद विश्वाम अनिराम अनाहुतिम अपामिवाम अप दुष्ष्वप्न्यं सुव ||
yena sūrya jyotiṣā bādhase tamo jagac ca viśvam udiyarṣi bhānunā | tenāsmad viśvām anirām anāhutim apāmīvām apa duṣṣvapnyaṁ suva ||
  • Mantra: "yena sūrya jyotiṣā bādhase tamo jagac ca viśvam udiyarṣi bhānunā | tenāsmad viśvām anirām anāhutim apāmīvām apa duṣṣvapnyaṁ suva ||"
  • Translation: "By whom the sun, with its light, dispels darkness, and the entire universe is illuminated; may that force protect us from all that is undesirable and grant us the essence of life."
  • Word Breakdown:
    • yena: By whom
    • sūrya: Sun
    • jyotiṣā: Light
    • bādhase: Dispels
    • tamo: Darkness
    • jagac: Universe
    • ca: And
    • viśvam: All
    • udiyarṣi: Illuminated
    • bhānunā: By the sun
    • tena: By that
    • asmad: Us
    • viśvām: All
    • anirām: Undesirable
    • anāhutim: Essence of life
    • apāmīvām: Protect us
    • apa: From
    • duṣṣvapnyaṁ: Undesirable things
    • suva: Good
  • Explanation:
    • This mantra emphasizes the power of light, represented by the sun, to dispel darkness, which can be interpreted as ignorance, negativity, or obstacles in life.
    • In real life, this can be seen in the way knowledge and education illuminate our understanding, helping us overcome challenges.
    • For example, a student who studies diligently can overcome the 'darkness' of ignorance and achieve success in their exams.
    • The mantra also calls for protection from undesirable elements, which can relate to personal struggles, such as mental health issues or negative influences in our lives.
    • In a practical sense, this could mean surrounding oneself with positive people and engaging in activities that promote well-being.
  • Conclusion:
    • The significance of this mantra lies in its universal message of seeking light and knowledge while striving to protect oneself from negativity.
    • It is applicable in various real-life scenarios, such as education, personal development, and mental health.
    • By invoking this mantra, individuals can remind themselves of the importance of positivity and the pursuit of knowledge in overcoming life's challenges.
